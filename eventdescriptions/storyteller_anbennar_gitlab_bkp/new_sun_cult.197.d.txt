The Cannorians in the west have been pillaging Aelantier for relics for quite some time now, and have brought back many incredible and powerful devices. If we are to keep up in this race to reclaim the past, Bulwar needs to begin some relic hunts of its own.  One of our advisors proposes to launch a grand excavation to find ancient relics from the era of Genies and of GodKings in our homeland.
