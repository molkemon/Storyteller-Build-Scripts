Artifice, the science of unifying magic and science, a field so very new for us but rooted in the works of the Precursors themselves. While the progress of our artificers has been astounding and produced wonders many could not have even dreamed of a few years ago, we are still very far away from the mythical wonders the legends say existed before the Day of Ashen Skies. But, with the exploration of Insyaa underway and the wonders we stumble upon growing by the day, we may just get that much closer to those empires of old. Take for example the Mechanim. While on the surface they look like mostly discarded automatons, that simplicity hides a complexity and ingenuity unheard of. One that, with our newly developed saws, drills, and cutting torches, we are able to examine in detail. No plate, joint, or other component shall be out of reach now.

Addendum: It is required that operating technicians wear total hearing protection during the procedure to avoid physical and psychological damage. Additionally, the machine should be disassembled in a soundproof room and be restrained by at least level 4 bindings.
