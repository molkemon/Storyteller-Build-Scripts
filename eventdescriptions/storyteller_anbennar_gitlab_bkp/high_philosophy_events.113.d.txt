High Philosophy has always been an enigma on matters of tolerance. The peoples of Haless who follow the Accretive Path, Transmutative Path, and Mystic Accord are often allowed to follow their faiths with little bother. Yet, things change within the confines of the various High Philosophy schools. Following its humble beginnings as a syncretized form of the ancient Devimarjan faith, schism after schism has torn the doctrine apart. Starry Eye and Golden Palace, Ascendant Soul and Unbroken Claw, Radiant Sun and all others; fundamentally opposed on matters of visatma, svayatma, and emulation respectively.  While not all schools are at one another's throats, the bloodshed of today has only served to heighten the tensions supposedly buried in councils long past. Conversation in the Hall of Endless Debate has come to blows, and within our own capital a philosophical disagreement ends with blades; the palace floors stained with blood. The violence defining the reformation, of Orange Sash father against Praxis son, is matched in our circles. We have issued an edict of tolerance, yet if we cannot even tolerate ourselves, then what good is our word? Perhaps the edict was a mistake…
