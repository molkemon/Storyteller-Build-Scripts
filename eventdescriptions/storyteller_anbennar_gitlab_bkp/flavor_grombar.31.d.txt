As Grombar joins what may well be one of the largest and most devastating conflicts in the history of Cannor, the war of the Corinite League, calls have already begun for the lord to lead our armies into battle personally. The shamans tell of great victory should our armies have them at their head, and the generals are quite excited about the potential boost to morale. As well as this, the last thing they would want in times like this is to be accused of cowardice.
