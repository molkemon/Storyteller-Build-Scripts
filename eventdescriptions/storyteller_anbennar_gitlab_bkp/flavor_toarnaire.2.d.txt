If Anbenncost is the World's Desire, Toarnaire is the envy of Lencenor. It is a proudful boast, a statement that even the likes of Wineport and Lorentaine pale in comparison to Toarnaire. But to those that live within its wall, it is also a warning, a reminder to always watch one's back, for envy drives the city. You can rise one day to the highest office in the city only to wake the next and see that someone has taken your place. If you are not careful, any gain can be lost, any fame can be torn to shreds, and the higher you rise, the harder you will fall. Everyone knows it. But their ruler seems to have forgotten this. He left work in the evening, intent on celebrating their recent success in the Red Flower Street, but never came home. He was found the next day, impaled in the middle of the causeway, their blood watering the cobbles. What happened is an open secret; their ruler had outlived their usefulness; And now the five families have taken their place. The event did not create much waves, for in a way, it was expected. Truly, Toarnaire is.
