Small and weak. Those are two traits anathema to our society. That is why goblins cannot exist in it under any circumstance. But there is another race in Escann that is just as worthless: halflings. These small pests infest the southern lands of our realm ever since we destroyed their farming community pretending to be an army. Our ruler has set out a bounty to hunt down every halfling found in the south. Villages are burned on sight for massive rewards while those who capture halflings can earn a lot of gold by ransoming them all to affluent relatives in the Small Country. Any resistance that the halflings put up is easily crushed by orkish might. Slowly, the halfling infestation of Escann was being cleared up. As land was made available for orkish settlement, many clanless orks became the targets of slave catchers. These clanless orks made up some of the first ork slaves in Venomtooth and became a reliable work force used to cultivate the area. Through the deaths and dispersal of the worthless, we have found a better use for the land.
