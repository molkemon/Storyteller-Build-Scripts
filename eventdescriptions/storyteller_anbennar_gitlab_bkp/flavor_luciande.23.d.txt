A coalition against the menace known as Luciande has been formed. The aim is to drive a stake through the heart of Lucian síl na Toars for bringing slave trade back to Cannor, for the massacre of the Moon Elves, for the massacre of the Red Masquerade, and for being a sharp-fanged abomination. Luciande is heavily fortified and situated in the most easily defended part of Cannor. We appreciate anyone who is willing to answer our just cause against this scourge!
