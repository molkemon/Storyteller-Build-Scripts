The peaceful demeanour of the Kobolds was a facade put in place to lure us towards them. Once our expedition came close, they jumped up and laughed, pulling, and pushing ropes and rocks, leading to a cavein next to us. From the newly formed crevice emerged an entire horde of Kobolds with weapons and armed to the teeth. This force surprised the already startled soldiers as they descended upon us, slaughtering those in their way and stealing from those close by.
