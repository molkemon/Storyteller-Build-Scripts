When Bramash ‘Chainbreaker’ first rose in revolt on the island of Endralliande, we had little sympathy for our incompetent colonial rivals. Our orcs were kept well in hand, and we had little fear of the kind of insurrection which occurs when one neglects simple matters of security and authority. Now, however, their sickness has become ours. The so-called ‘Cabinet of Freedom’ which Bramash maintained during his first revolt has been revived now that he has fled to Soruin—our colonial lands. Now we are dealing with the consequences of Endralliander incompetence. His maroon settlements foment constant unrest among our labouring orc population and the native Soruinics alike—they are even beginning to intermarry. Meanwhile, missives from his Cabinet invite interference from abroad. Their propaganda speaks of a thing called “Ozgarom”—the realm of the orcish free.
