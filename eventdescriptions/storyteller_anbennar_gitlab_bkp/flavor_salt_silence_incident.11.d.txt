Dark skies cover the once pale blue horizon of The Borders. A troop of soldiers hailing from the giants rest at a nearby lake, making camp as the winds of war die down. To their left is a field scattered with the remains of a slain Imperial Army. To their right are silent forests judging every step.  'Sarjen', Didya hear iv we cot the Emporuh?' The footman prepping the beef stew asked as his companions set up tents for the night.  'His Imperial Majesty escaped back to Anbenncost. Good riddance, my ears nearly bled from his impotent shouting.' The Sarjent responded, stepping toward the tranquil lake.  The Sarjent winced in pain as he met the water's edge, his right arm in a sling after barely surviving an Aldresian Knight's blade. But discomfort subsided just as quickly as it came.  'The silence, its. soothing.'  'Whar?'  'Just, enjoy the fucking moment will you?'
