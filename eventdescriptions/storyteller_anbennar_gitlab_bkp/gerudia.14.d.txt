The northwestern reaches of Bjarnrik border the treacherous Dalrfjall Mountains. The clans of Blodfjell and Sidaett have bought their land with the blood of their ancestors, and there is not a day without word of another massacre and raid by the accursed trolls.  Blodfjell and Sidaett both feel that their role as the shield against the trolls has gone unappreciated, and that their fortresses rot even before their deaths. They call for the Konungr to renew our support of them, and gather a grand warchest to defend our lands again.
