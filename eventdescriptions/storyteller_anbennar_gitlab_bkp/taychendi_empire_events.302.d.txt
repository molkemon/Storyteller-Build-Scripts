"You wanted to see me?" the ruler asked. Mihaela stood on the balcony, staring out at the city beyond. She gestured to a full wine cup nearby, and the ruler soon joined her at the railing. "Your realm is so... beautiful," Mihaela said, distant. "So many people clawing for glory, blood. Controlled chaos. A hunting ground for people like me... and maybe, for people like us." That "us" sent a shiver down the ruler's back. Mihaela turned to face the ruler. She was beautiful, captivating... but it was never a normal beauty. The ruler doubted it was how she actually looked, for one – but there was always something tainting her, always some emotion saturating the way she looked. Wrath, envy, malevolence... today, she was dripping with so much pride – so much vainglory – that the ruler reflexively stepped back, a hand on the ruler's saber. For the first time, the ruler did not see a partner, but a pretender.

"When I first came to Taychend, I just wanted to live my life how I wanted, in total freedom. The chaos was so alluring – no Blood Court, no subordinates scheming to rule the shadows. But now I look out and realize what my children once did, what I tried so hard to reject... so long as you're on top, control and chaos aren't so exclusive. I think you've realized that too."

Mihaela stepped forward, raised a hand to the ruler's cheek. The ruler froze at her touch. "I want to make you an offer – my final one. Put me on top. Allow me to ascend to where I've always been destined to be... and I will give you the freedom I've enjoyed for so long. The freedom of the hunt... the freedom of immortality... the freedom of Vampirism. And then? We will rule the chaos, side by side – and we shall rule it forever."
