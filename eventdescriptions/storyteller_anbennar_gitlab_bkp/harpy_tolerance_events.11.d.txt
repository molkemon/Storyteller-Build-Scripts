Some spells and potions require esoteric instruments, such as body parts of various creatures. An unfortunate result of mages overambition, possibly compounded by loosening restrictions on them, has resulted in a rash of unfortunate incidents in which harpies have been drugged and stripped of their feathers, and in some situations, slaughtered for their organs. This has met with no small amount of displeasure in our lands, and people look to their lord for guidance.
