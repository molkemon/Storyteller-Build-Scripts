Bibyobi starting lore.We are in this land as a Sponsored Adventurer Band. Our overlords in Rayafo want us to conquer and spread our culture and faith for them. They expect a return on investment, and in 35 years or when we have conquered a sufficient amount of land, they will want to redefine this relationship. If we are able to successfully reform our government before then, we will better poised to fend off any advances from our gracious overlord.
