When they first met in battle, it was random. When they met the second time, it was chance. When they met the third time, it was fate. And by the fourth, they knew their dance.

They were both battlefield medics, passing each other as they tended the hurt. Until one day, the Centaur was injured, and the Human approached, tearing their shirt. They crossed the line that day, as they looked into each other's eyes. And ever since that moment, their lives were completely changed.

Far away, they fled, their love so deeply forbidden. None would know their story, which they fought to keep well hidden.

Until they found themselves in Lelanàn, having crossed the world to be free. And if you wish to talk to them, remember: the Centaur likes tea.
