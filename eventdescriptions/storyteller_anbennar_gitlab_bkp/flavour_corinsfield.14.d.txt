For the beginnings of the Blessed Book, what must be agreed upon are the very commandments of Corin. Every good Corinsfielder knows of her travels with her party to fight against the insidious dark thralls of the archmage Dookanson; from the bravery of the liberated slave Lothane Silmuna to the brains of the inventor Jacob of Creekwhist, they show how the righteous spirit of heroes can be found within any with enough faith to stand up against evil.
