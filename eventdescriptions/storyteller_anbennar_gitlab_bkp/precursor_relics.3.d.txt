Looking terribly sheepish, the leaders of our excavation at the Precursor ruins in our lands have come knocking with the most unfortunate news. Though they’ve kept digging for weeks now, they’ve yet to turn up more than a small amount of pottery, a few loose bones, and enough worms to give a small city’s worth of people a nice fishing trip. Simply put, the ruin is exhausted. We’ve picked it clean of anything valuable, turned every stone, and used it to the last. Unfortunately, it seems our investments in the province will have to be redirected to some other industry. Let’s hope we can muster something to give even a fraction of the relics’ worth.
