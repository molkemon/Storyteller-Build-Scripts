"SILVERSPITE DEAD! REBELS CRUSHED! VICTORY! VICTORY!" Elrandar Silverspite is dead. His forces destroyed, the Kamrayakval made a final, spiteful stand against his enemies—but after slaughtering hundreds, he too was overcome, bleeding from a hundred wounds as he finally retreated. His armor was spotted crashing into a nearby river—it is almost certain he drowned, as Yodhan once did. The Kamrayakval has been defeated.

The Oren Nayiru go with him. Their Great Relic-Lord dead, it is clear that they will never again have the strength to truly conquer Taychend. They carved a bloody whirlwind of destructive and righteous vengeance across the region—but the storm has been scattered, and their dreams of an agrarian, native-ruled, theo-democratic Taychend have been scattered with it.

The Kavalali said that this was a war for Dawn. So it was, and the night has triumphed. The Oren Nayiru have been thrust back into the dark.
