A plot most foul has been uncovered, and with not a moment to spare. If the inquisitors had been more than a second late, then the very beating heart of Poppyspring would have been turned to naught but ash. Explosives, enhanced with witchery most foul, have been found planted around the base of our grandest cathedral.  The culprits were evidently a band of maleficarum and their useful idiots. The young scion of the rich Accipite family who had somehow managed to hide his potential seemed to be their leader, and behind him were two other witches: an underground hedge witch who purported to heal any ailment with the aid of foul spirits, and a Veykodan savage rejected by her own tribe. As for the fools who had fallen under these witches’ spell, they were a rich merchant of the Argezvale breed, an escapee from the slavers of Noruin, and a rather notorious thief of Havoric make who our inquisitors have been pursuing for some time now.  Goodman Silverin, a son of the Vanick family in education to become a couter, was part of their number; however, he claimed the Martyr herself came to him in a dream and told him to give up their evil designs. Verily, without his confession, their dreadful plan may have been executed.  The High Couter of Poppyspring, Elepha FearNot, has pleaded for the brigands’ release, citing supposed great deeds performed by them in service of the city. A kind and godly woman, to be sure…
