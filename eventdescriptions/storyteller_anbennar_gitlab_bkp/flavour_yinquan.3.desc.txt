Lord Y27 and our lord have been separated for centuries, though this is largely due to neither side having the ability to see the other fully reintegrated. Now that our realm has eclipsed theirs in strength, it is only natural that they bend the knee so we can be one realm once again. All inhabitants of Lord Y27's domain will be given full citizenship, and their ruling family will receive an adequate pension as compensation for their cooperation. Everything important is already in order, so now we need only to wait for the final integration into our administration.
