When it came to resisting foreign occupation, Sikai always knew how to resist carefully and efficiently, hiding in remote villages and raiding any official or station, with the goal of making governing the region as costly as possible. It would be no different under our rule. It was known upfront that, without a compromise, our realm would lose control and the region would fall back to the status quo. Luckily such a compromise has been reached. Lords of all townships standing side by side on the central square of the capital, ready to proclaim that they set aside all differences and past disputes in favor of becoming something greater. This was the day the Kai Republic would be formed, a new more advanced government that gave representation to every part of the realm in a united Sikai state. Thus the document that laid the foundational work of the Republic was signed by the new representatives, formally establishing the House of Lords. After every person in attendance walked to a set of flagpoles upon which each of the townships had raised their flag. In tandem they lowered, though did not remove their own flag fully, while raising a new flag, that of the new Republic that would now fly above them. And as proudly as the flags waved, so did the leaders of the new Republic of the Golden Grass; Thangoya.
