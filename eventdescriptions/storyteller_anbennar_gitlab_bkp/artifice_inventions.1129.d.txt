The enforcement of laws, both religious and mundane, is the sacred duty of the Order of Judges. Their members do not only dispense justice from courthouses; many travel across Bulwar to perform their rulings wherever needed, even in the smallest of hamlets.  Such itinerant judges do not have easy access to a legal library to study precedents or research obscure laws. Thus the autoupdating law codices have been developed by artificers in the employ of the Order of Judges. By an ingenious combination of divination and transmutation magic, each copy of such an autoupdating law codex is linked to the master copy, kept under lock and seal in the headquarters of the Order in Azkabar.  Whenever a new law is added to or stricken from the master copy, all autoupdating law codices will register this change as well. Each wandering judge is now issued an autoupdating law codex of their own when they have finished their apprenticeship and are ready to bring justice and order to the lands of Bulwar.
