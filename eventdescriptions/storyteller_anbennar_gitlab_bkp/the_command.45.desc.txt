The War Room has gathered in the shadow of Mount Tughayasa. Each command is represented: Marshals of the Wolf, Boar, Lion, Dragon, Elephant, and Tiger commands, lead their generals and the lieutenant generals of the nosunin, gikunin, and sunyanin. But not just hobgoblins have joined the gathering in the great hall in Tughayasa. The generals of the Wuhyun Kikunin have recently arrived and taken their place with their men and women, among whom are not just humans, but also harimari and half-orcs.

As one, all those gathered plunge their weapons into the ground, as is tradition at a Kenu-Ike. The marshals remove their armor, it is carried away according to tradition, and the six most powerful hobgoblins in the realm join their elected superior, our lord [Root.Monarch.GetName], whose tent has been set up overlooking the mighty Kharunyana and the white peak of the oracle mountain.

In a normal Kenu-Ike, this would be the moment for proposal, solicitation, and argument. However, there is none of this. They all know that they have come here for a purpose. They are here to make a concord, to speak as one voice. It is time to pronounce a command, one not just for Haless, but for the world.
