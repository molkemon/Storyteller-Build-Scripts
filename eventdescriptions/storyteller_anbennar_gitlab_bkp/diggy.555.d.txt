It’s hard enough for an ettin’s two heads to agree with one another, let alone more than one of the beasts to act in conjunction. Both savage and stupid, they are almost always solitary creatures. However, a shortage of prey has led to a group of hungry ettins in a nearby province to band together. Now they are wreaking havoc on our settlements there, eating our subjects with glee. We will need to send a cohort of soldiers and adventurers to deal with them; but such a force will not be cheap.
