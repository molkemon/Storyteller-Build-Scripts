May Kroozbuto Nubo be praised, it seems as if our efforts were not in vain. The rain is falling steadily over the province and the surrounding area. It has been many years since the people of the area received this amount of precipitation and still, there is no end in sight. Heavy clouds, pregnant with Nubos blessings, cover the sky from horizon to horizon. The people are celebrating the Gods Rain, and preparing for the harvest of a lifetime.
