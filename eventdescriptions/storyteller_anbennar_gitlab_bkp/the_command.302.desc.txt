Historically, our society has revolved around Godlost philosophy, but ever since the introduction of humans among our ranks, our officers have also looked towards other thoughts. Especially the Tiger Command has been interested in High Philosophy, which is understandable. The philosophy shares many similarities with our own, and we can't deny their interesting ideas. Accordingly, we have engaged in discussions with philosophers from cities such as Kamapar, Sarisung, and Kharispid. Let us do so again, but what sort of philosopher should Grand Marshal our ruler meet?
