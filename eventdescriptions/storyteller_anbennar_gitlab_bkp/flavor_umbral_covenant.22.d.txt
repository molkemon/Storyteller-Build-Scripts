It falls upon the court like a curtain, this understanding. Scraps of envy for the organization and unity of the Covenant, felt at one time or another by a courtier or two, spread among the assembled. None of you forget your opposition to the Vile One, nor do you think your past selves unreasonable—exactly. You merely remember what you thought, and come to the intuitive agreement that it was wrong. So, what exactly are you fighting for? To ensure that when the next end comes, the sentient peoples of Halann must each face it alone? To live and fear and die without a friend?

Don’t you feel that? It is every other soul in this world, just a thought away. If you’re ever again lonely, or you don’t know how to go on, they’ll be there.
