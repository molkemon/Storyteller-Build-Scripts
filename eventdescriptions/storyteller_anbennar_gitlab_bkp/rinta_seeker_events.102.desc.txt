The ruler is often considered fairly studious. They have taken interest in many different subjects. Unfortunately for them, the obligations of ruler ship provide strain to the amount of time a ruler has available. It has noticeably taken a toll on them, who seems to be longing to delve into a new study. This has been coming to a head recently; with their obligations taking up the entirety of the day, they have been assigning more of them to their advisors. Close friends have seen them reading literature, while their advisors have poorly managed the country, and have even taken bribes to enact things without their permission. What should be done?
