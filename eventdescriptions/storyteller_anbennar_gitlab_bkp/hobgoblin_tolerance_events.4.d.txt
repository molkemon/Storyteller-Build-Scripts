If there can only be one thing said about the Hobgoblin race, let it be their mastery in warfare is without equal in this world, a strength that made them feared by all those who inhabit Haless ever since they first left the Serpentspine. While we can only dream of our armies reaching that level of infamy, for now a retired Hobgoblin officer of no small renown has approached the court with the offer to aid us in training a truly professional military force.
