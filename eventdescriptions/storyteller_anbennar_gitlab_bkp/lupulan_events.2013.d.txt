The stream of canoes coming from our neighbors in Unlucky Guys along our rivers to be sold has recently slowed to a trickle. It appears that their area is experiencing an especially severe outbreak of teak moths this year, and there is scarcely a clearing of teak trees in their entire realm that has not been affected. Due to teak wood's role in their canoe building, all of Unlucky Guys has precious little to barter with their fellow tribes, and they would likely appreciate any form of economic relief we might be able to provide to them.
