The assembled legion came from every part of the valley. Most had travelled here for glory and prestige, hoping to be the one to successfully take down one of the beasts that named the forests their home. Some had come purely to serve their Lady, but it was obvious to me that most of them placed that as a secondary goal. A few, most interestingly, had arrived seeking one thing only; vengeance. A wendigo had slaughtered their village, or an owlbeast had crushed a loved one during a rampage, or an Old Rzentur raid saw their home burned down. Whatever their reasons, here they had come.  Mitrenya eyed the group, a critical glint in her eye. I stood alongside her.  "Are you sure about this, Ren?" I whispered, turning my head towards a small scuffle that had broken out near the front of the crowd. She scoffed, reaching out a delicate hand to rest along the back of my neck.  "I'm certain, sweetling. And. thank you. For being here." I looked at her, watching her sneer as she stepped towards the argument. "I'm not sure I would've had the patience to deal with these people without you." Barely, just barely, I was able to smother my laugh behind my hand, as a quick spell left the instigators damp, cold, and rankled.  "Come now. Must we squabble?" Though nobody else would be able to tell, I could hear the mirth in Mitrenya's voice as she stood over her unruly subjects, hands on her hips. "Let us save such frivolities for after the hunt, hm? After all."
