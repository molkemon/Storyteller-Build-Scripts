The first step to designing our mecharam prototype is to choose what we want to do for the legs of our new mounts. Our artificers have laid out two designs for our perusal.  1. This design is sleek and lithe. Despite its thinness, power and strength seems to suffuse every joint and working. We would expect rams with this design to be quite maneuverable around the battlefield, able to flank any enemy.  2. This design is thick and solid. We would expect rams with this design to be able to hold their own and need less support on the battlefield, increasing the ratio against infantry that we can field.
