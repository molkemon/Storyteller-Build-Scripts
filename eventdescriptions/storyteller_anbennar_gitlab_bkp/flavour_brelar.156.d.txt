The formation of the Ravelian Faith has had much impact on the Ravelian Societies in Breluslad. Many Brelari have converted to the new religion; however, many are also concerned about the new hierarchical structure the Faith now presents, so different to the freedomseeking intellectualism the societies once followed. The matter of adopting the Ravelian faith on a state level has been brewing among the Councilmen of Breluslad, especially those in areas with large numbers of Ravelian converts. Now people are clamouring for our lord to make a statement on the matter.
