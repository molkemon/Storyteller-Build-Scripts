While the elite of the realm descend from well-trained noble houses, they are not ignorant to the truths of war. When push comes to shove, numbers will always be important. Thus, they have been worried about the amount of levies that we have access to in cases of a military emergency. They have therefore petitioned for the permit to conscript the rural farmers of the province, training and equipping them in preparation for a mass recruitment in times of war. This will unfortunately divert their workforce away from our farmlands, but denying this request will cause the realm to be rather upset.
