The Enahelibe Ritual was our famous fertility ritual, with A Mihipha and the priestess conducting an elaborate dance to excite the participating lovers and their hearts, preparing them for the sacred act of creating life; increasing their chances. Unfortunately, the context of fertility the ritual carried meant it was only conducted for couples who could create life. Less orthodox couples like Leli and I were considered less important than a common knife. So I may have been driven by spite when I lobbied to redirect the Mihipha away from the Ritual, leveraging Leli’s immense financial support of the Sisterhood to get what I wanted. My anger was a touch habitual.
