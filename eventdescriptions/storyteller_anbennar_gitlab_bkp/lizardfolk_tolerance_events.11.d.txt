A small group of well-off lizardfolk craftsmen have approached our treasury with a proposal for mutual investment in a public bathhouse complex for a province. A traditional sight in lizardfolk lands, these pools are a site of informal commerce, lobbying, and even interstate diplomacy. The craftsmen claim such a complex would attract significant custom, even from non-lizardfolk. As investor, the state could reap the benefits of such investment and show interest in the lizardfolk community's wellbeing at the same time.
