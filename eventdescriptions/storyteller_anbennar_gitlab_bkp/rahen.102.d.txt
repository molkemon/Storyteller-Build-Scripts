The winds blow hot with change. The onceforgotten heart of Lohanparayan, the Iron Palace, lay buried beneath ash and thorn. But you had a vision. A grand design. The palaces of lesser lords fell, their stone repurposed, their legacies erased. Around Sarilavhan, an encampment rises: architects, masons, slaves, and dreamers. Smoke from the smelters twists into the air. You will raise an empire from iron and ash.
