The sun is always bright in Bulwar, where the might of Surael is strong, and it can be completely blinding. And the Bulwari have adapted. Shading to cool down market stalls, fountains to moisturize the air, kohl to protect the eyes, robes to protect the skin. But none of these would protect against the overwhelming light that now ignites the courtyard of the royal palace in our capital. It completely overwhelms the senses of our ruler. It fills their eyes until they think they have gone blind. Then the light seems to dim, and a figure appears. Its form seems to shimmer like hot sand, but some aspects are distinguishable. It seems to wear long robes, it carries a spear or a staff, and its head flanked by what could be wings or extraordinarily long ears. Jaher? Surael? Our ruler falls to their knees in supplication. "My lord, I." Our ruler stammers, mouth suddenly dry. "How can I serve you my lord?" "I would have you do my work on Halann." The voice comes from everywhere at once, resounding like a bell.
