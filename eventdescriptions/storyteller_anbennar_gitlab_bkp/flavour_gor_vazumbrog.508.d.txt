Having been mildly routed by a surprise attack from giant spiders, the delvers were pleased to see a treasure vault filled with weaponry… then disappointed to discover that all of the weapons were ornate, ornamental things: a halberd too heavy to practically swing, a sword so delicate in its beauty that it would surely shatter in a spray of gemstones on its first clash with enemy steel.  "We'll have to fight the beasts as we are, hunters… these weapons aren't fit for any purpose but brightening up some dragon's hoard."
