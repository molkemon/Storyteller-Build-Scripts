After Jaher and the Sun Elves arrived on the shores of Bulwar, many amongst the elven mages were intrigued by the style of magic practised by the human Bulwari, so utterly dissimilar to their own traditions. After the foundation of the Phoenix Empire, a large number of elven mages joined the various magi clans, studying their craft and adding their own magical insights to the clan’s repository of arcane lore. Some amongst the elves even founded their own clans, in order to preserve the fragments of magical knowledge their ancestors kept alive during the centuries of the Great Wander.  One of the major elven magical traditions that has thus found new roots in Bulwari soil is Belostaro, the Way of Mists, the uniquely Sun Elven martial magic that many claim hails from the time of the paragons or even further back in time. The ZarTenseaed or Fireglaive Clan are one of the most prominent elven magi clans that specialise on this magic.  Their leader, arkimagus Varina Thirenzuir, cuts an imposing figure, wearing studded leather armour beneath deceptively soft silken robes, with hundreds of anniwan pearls woven into her jetblack hair. She has turned out to be a vocal supporter of our reform plans and used her gregarious charms to win over many who still were hesitant to accept the new direction we are embarking on. Her eagerness to support our efforts in revitalising Bulwari magic has already proven invaluable on several occasions.
