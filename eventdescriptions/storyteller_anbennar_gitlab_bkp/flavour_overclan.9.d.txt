REPORT: A local shepherd family of the Graysheep clan has earned notoriety for selling clothes of high quality. While their exact methods are currently unknown to us, it is clear that their skills with turning wool into textiles is beyond comparison in our territory. This family business has also earned a reputation for servicing clients of all races, and even offering garments fitted to varying physiologies. It is believed that, with government assistance, these shepherds could greatly expand their business, stimulating the economy and providing our state with a source of superior textiles.
