The province of Teinmas was once part of the Duchy of Asheniande, a title we now lay claim to. After the Fourth Lilac War, it fell under the control of the Elfrealm of Ibevar, but its people remained Arannese rather than elves.  Considering our friendship with the elves of Ibevar, they would surely be glad to part ways with a small parcel of land in order to quell any tensions that may linger between our peoples. Teinmas, after all, is Arannese and should belong to an Arannese realm. But is Teinmas truly worth this much trouble?
