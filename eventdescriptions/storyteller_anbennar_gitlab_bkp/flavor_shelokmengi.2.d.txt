It was sheer madness! Ayufar stormed into the throne room, guards following him like thunder after lightning. He turned to the nobility and asked them to join him. He demanded their full support for his reforms and his plan to end the state of Enikmesiki, or else he would strip them of their ranks and titles and banish them from their lands. Of course, some, thinking it just an example of his bold showmanship, refused him! Now we are going to be sent out to deal with these fools. I had a very simple job before Ayufar took over. When I return, meet me where we first met.
