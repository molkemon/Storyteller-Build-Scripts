The one thing that can be said to be a constant of life in the realm is the drone of the jungle. It acts as a constant backdrop to the rhythms of the realm, and we could not imagine life without it. In the same way, our rule has become a part of the realm. The vassals pay tribute, all the realm bends the knee, and we are a part of the jungle as much as the noise. We have repelled the pirates, the tribes of the plains, and the zealots of High Philosophy. Their populations do not resist, for the realm itself is on our side. We are a fact of life.
