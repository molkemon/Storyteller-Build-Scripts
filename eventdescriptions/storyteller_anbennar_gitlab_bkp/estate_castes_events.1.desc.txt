A human playwright named Dhanneh Leihner is rapidly gaining fame among the common folk. His plays are crude and vulgar, yet the humans in the middle castes and lower castes adore them! It is an outrage! The plays are cheap comedy and aim to mock the upper castes. Contemptuous titles range from "The Mind of a Married Harimari" to "Harimar and Manava Go to White Castle," and his most recent play, "Dude, Where's My Horse?" mocks all the virtues we hold in high regard. We must take control of the situation!
