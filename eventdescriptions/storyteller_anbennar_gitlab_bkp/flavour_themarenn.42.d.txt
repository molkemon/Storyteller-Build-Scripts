The burden of loss weighed heavy upon him, and heavier still was the burden of losing something irreplaceable. The pride of his goddess, his one true love, now lost. How could this have happened? He walked along a corridor of sand and grass, flanked by the forest on the right and the coast on the left. The ruler clasped his hands behind his back and looked up, where thousands of bright stars seemed poised to fall upon the land, and a full moon—the embodiment of the Dame—gazed down upon the province in solemn silence.
