Unwisely used, the sacred Precursor spellblade Saerasyar, the Green-Flamed Sword, punishes its unworthy wielder in a mighty explosion of flame and magic, reappearing only when it has found a worthy member of Munazhlan's folk to show itself to. Years after the untimely demise of their last ruler, Saerasyar has once more revealed itself, and it has been spirited away to the Askalyn in Assakadail. Should the lord wish to brave its effects, they can take up the sword once more.
