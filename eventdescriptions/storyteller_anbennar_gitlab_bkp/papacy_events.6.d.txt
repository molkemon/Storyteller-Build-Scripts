There is a long tradition of High Rectors appointing their relatives to the College of Veridicals, after all: if the High Rector is the chosen interpreter of the God Fragment, then surely those of their blood have the will and intelligence to do so too? While some call this nepotism, VeridicalNephews have an established place within the Rectorate. Now that it is time to appoint a new Veridical, the High Rector must decide whether a relative would be suitable to take up residence there.
