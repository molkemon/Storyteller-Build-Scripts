The previous ruler pact has expired, and a new one must be voted in by the Council of Salla Fealgarn.  The Council itself has the choice to vote in themselves and other minor salla to lead the nation collectively. There is also Oberon Fealgarn, the heir of the founding family who is trying to gain and maintain influence. It is a foregone conclusion he will implement emergency powers for the rest of his tenure.  What will the future of Salla Fealgarn be?
