Ever since our glorious expedition into the darkness of the Serpentspine has been announced, it's all anyone has been able to talk about. Now it is also time for us to put something tangible behind our claims. To do such an expedition we need the proper men and equipment. The Dwarovar is dangerous and we won't be able to secure their safety as they head into the depths. Who should we even send? What will be our approach. The people are already starting to speculate.
