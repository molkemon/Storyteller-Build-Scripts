Our populace celebrates the return of our most recent expedition. They are being heralded as heroes and given a proper, heroic welcome. But behind closed doors, far away from the ears of the common people, the expedition leader tells our administration something else, something more.  During the expedition they have uncovered more than just trinkets and treasures: A crack in the wall has led them to a room with old books and strange runes. All of dwarvish make.  After some research, the council has decided what this must be: A remnant structure from the old days of AulDwarov, a library where the ancient dwarves have accumulated their knowledge.
