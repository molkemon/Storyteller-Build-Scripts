The retinues have set up camp, the elite troops have been inspected by the rulers and the formalities have been wrapped up. It is now time for what everybody really came for: The Settlement of Swords. In the Settlement of Swords or Kenu-Ike, it is decided whether a campaign will be initiated, provinces strengthened, or officers and administrators sacked. These meetings are one of the, if not most important meetings of the War Room in The Great Command and have the power to change the course of both our realm and military. It is also steeped in ritualism.

First, all Marshals and their retinues plunge their weapons into the ground, forming a small "settlement" out of swords to show that they will not fight against each other. After this, all Marshals remove their armor and let the workers of other Lower Commands repair, clean, and improve their kit to show that they trust each other with one of their most valuable possessions. After these rituals, the Marshals meet in the Grand Marshal's tent.

The Marshals of the different Commands have gathered and all have made their proposals known. It is now up to our Grand Marshal to select which proposal to enact.
