The Gitueni people, particularly those of the local burghers, have often looked down upon the Bateobugi of Danggun. The armored vessels are safe from both man and spirit, yes, but they are slow and the armor plating takes up valuable cargo space. Instead, they build their boats with lightweight timber from Geuneulsup and shape them with sleek ritual carvings, praying to the spirits beneath the waves. These ships speed through the water, carrying goods at breakneck pace to all corners of the realm. They seek funds to construct more of these vessels, built to their exacting yet costly standards, to maintain their trade dominance.
