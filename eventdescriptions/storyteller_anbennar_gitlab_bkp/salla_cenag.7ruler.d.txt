I finished reading the raid report with a grim smile. It was the latest of its kind to come in the last weeks.  "Another successful raid?" Lanhara asked, peering over my shoulder.  I nodded.  "Good," she said as she began massaging my shoulders. I closed my eyes to enjoy the moment, but instead my thoughts drifted to battle and strategy. My smile flipped.  "Sorry," Hara apologized, assuming it was the knot she had just hit.  I brought my hand to hers. "No, it wasn’t you. It’s those orks and their bastard chieftain." She continued the massage knowing I wasn’t done. "We’ve killed hundreds of those brutes, but they just won’t break."  My words hung in the air.  "A single thrust of the glaive over a thousand arrows?," Lanhara finally offered.  I looked up behind, toward her. "Yes, a single glaive thrust. We are going to have to face them head on."  "And who exactly is ‘we’?"  I snort, "You know what I mean."  She raised her eyebrows, "Do I?"
