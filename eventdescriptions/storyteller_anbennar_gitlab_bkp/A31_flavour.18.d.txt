The research has ended and the results are as we expected. The new variety is growing quickly and well, with many of the huge tea cartels buying licenses to grow and sell these varieties. Export is flowing swiftly as markets the world over are readily buying up our now perfected product. The tea houses of Moonhaven host a grand opening that attracts the great and good from across the empire, filling our coffers with new gold and our debate halls with new experts. While a few might insist on some other favorite blend, all can agree that this is the greatest tea to share.
