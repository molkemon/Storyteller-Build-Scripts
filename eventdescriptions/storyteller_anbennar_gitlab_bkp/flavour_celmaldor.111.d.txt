The clans, being as always conservative and jealous of their old privileges, still refuse to stop favouring merchants and employees that speak the Elveh language at the expense of those who do not. They pay lip service to the law and the decrees of state, but do not adhere completely to it. We cannot enforce it without creating an internal commotion, either.  It has been considered that we should just teach our merchants Elven; the only question is whether this should be mandatory or voluntary. The former would be a very effective but bluntforce method of creating a unified cultural identity. The latter would attract fewer but more devoted learners.
