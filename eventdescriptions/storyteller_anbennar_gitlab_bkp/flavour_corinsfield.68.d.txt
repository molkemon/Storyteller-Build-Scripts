An odd story from the countryside. For the past few years, two neighboring villages have been locked in a fierce competition over who can grow bigger crops. Uncomfortable comparisons to savage halfling rituals aside, this competition seemed harmless enough. However, recently, the mayor of one village has come to the inquisitors to claim that the other mayor is enlarging their crops using witchery. To prove his point, he has brought in an absurdly large pumpkin.  The man could be telling the truth; it really is a pumpkin of quite unusual size. However, it is entirely possible that no witchery was involved, just simply the luck of a farmer.
