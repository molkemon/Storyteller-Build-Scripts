A valued advisor has earned many friends in the royal court thanks to their dedicated service as the monarch's advisor. However, it seems that the reverse is equally the case among their countrymen in the service of a foreign power: Envious of their position, they reproach them for taking up employment among their people's enemies and have begun spreading rumors casting doubt on their trustworthiness. Worried about their standing in the royal court, the advisor has become a strident opponent of the foreign ruler. They made common cause with several likeminded nobles to call for a harsher stance against the foreign power.
