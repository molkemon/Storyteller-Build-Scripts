Droga takes little satisfaction in the plundering of Olavsborg. Her ragtag crew of orks and reachmen handle most of the violence, with Droga's wrath reserved for those who fail to provide her further clues to her husband's servile prison.  Unfortunately for the people of that reaverladen port, no one seems to know much about Khrosh here. Their onceempty, nowdetached heads strewn across the bloodslick dock threaten the reaver's footing. Fortunately, the blood and guts of men are no slipperier than those of fish, and the former angler remains steady.  As Droga continues her grisly work, she begins to build momentum at it; begins to see the beauty in this violence. Still, she remembers that the purpose of this journey is not mere destruction.
