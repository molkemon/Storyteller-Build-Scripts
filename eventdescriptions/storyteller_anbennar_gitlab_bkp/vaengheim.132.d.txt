Then did the host of our lord come upon a great river, and many vessels were spied. Yet fewer, as a wingmaiden mentioned, than the year before. Ashore they landed, and a fisherman asked. The man, who was named Balder, said, "O queen, your eyes are ever keen, for we have been beset by gnollish pirates from the far south. And their swift craft cleave the sea like knives downriver, and they board any vessel that plies this river at night, where their keen senses make sport of honest traders."
