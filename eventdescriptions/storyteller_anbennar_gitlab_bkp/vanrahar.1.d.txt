Bhuvauri’s revolt was caused, in large part, by our ancestor’s oppressiveness. They taxed the slaves beyond what they could take and lost everything. We now make war on Bhuvauri, but we aren’t here to take revenge, for they weren’t wrong to revolt. However, there are those who still wish for our return, and we won’t abandon them. Bhuvauri is much more powerful than us on paper. Their lands are far richer, their navy is unrivalled, and their slavesoldiers the Adasa live only for war. However, we aren’t out of options yet.
