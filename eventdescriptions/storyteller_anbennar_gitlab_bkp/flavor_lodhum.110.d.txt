Harpies have many ways to get what they want, and in this case it’s without a fight. A harpy matriarch has brought her flock of daughters into this settlement, establishing a temporary nest and brothel, which has become quite popular both with the locals and visitors to the area. While this is generally not of our notice, the matriarch herself has offered to have she and her daughters stay for a longer span of time, retaining their services in the region for longer than intended if the right arrangement can be struck.
