The years of 2023 saw Vængheim at war, for Queen Vængheim named all she saw as hers, yet few would bend the knee to her. Many maidens had their roost, with the will to bring their knees by spear if needed. Yet Vængheim built her roost on war and mercy both. Those who bent the knee did live, and were the better for it, for in defense they took them, to the bane of their foes.

So it was that Vængheim met the giant realm of Obrtrol, who sought to bring them an end. Yet Vængheim was to be their bane, for they had friends in Dalr, who had learned through bitter years to set traps for the giants. Trolls suffered mightily, for the cunning of man and the skybound maiden was their doom together.

Then a troll named Freydis Oaktongue came to Vængheim, and she said, "I speak not for all, for many seek you dead. They see lands they once had and lost. They would take Gerudaghot, or take death for its want. But I have heard that you bring peace, and I would ask for such, for my clan and I know well your mannish friends, and ours is a cruel end if no peace can be bought."

Then Queen Vængheim said, "You speak true, for many here would gladly take your head. They know your gifts of chainbound wrist, but songs of peace are new to them. If now you sing to me of peace, the gift of peace I give you. For I am under oath of blood, and will not mute this song."

And though the hall did mutter, Queen Vængheim bid Freydis find her knee.
