In the province of Monastic Outreach, a sage from one of our temples humbly requests permission to establish a teaching program among the villagers. He speaks of the quiet power found in the steady cultivation of mind and spirit, of the way the Accretive Path can root even the most restless souls in harmony on the path to self-improvement. Such a program would be a beacon of enlightenment in a land still shadowed by doubt, but it demands resources from the realm. Your council is divided: some see this as a vital step towards spiritual unity, while others warn of overreach and squandered coin.
