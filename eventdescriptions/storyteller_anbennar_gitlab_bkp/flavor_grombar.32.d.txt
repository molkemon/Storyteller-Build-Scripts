The worst has come to pass; the lord has fallen in battle. With their death, though traditionally an heir would take power, these dire times require strong leadership, not just an individual capable of ruling, but one capable of leading. And of all those within the realm, none seem more capable than Temar Ironsea. Having proven himself a mighty admiral in the many naval battles of the War of the Corinite League, Ironsea has become almost a living legend throughout the realm, renowned for his heroism, prowess, and leadership. Loved by the people, nobility, and military alike, those in the highest echelons of power have invited him to the capital to seize power. Though this is done under the pretext of a regency, there is the unspoken truth that should he wish, Ironsea could seize the title of Gray King here and now with little to no opposition.
