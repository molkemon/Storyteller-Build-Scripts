Surael teaches us that all who serve him are to be treated equal, but still those closest to the DivineHerald have long been the officers of the Jaddari. But times have changed and the empire has expanded. We have seen the rulership of the Sun Elves in Bulwar, the Khet in Kheterata and the human kings in Cannor. Of all it seems that the harimari have uniquely developed the skills needed to administer an expanding empire, centuries of philosopherkings slowly honing a uniquelystyled bureaucracy. Should we draw upon their skills?
