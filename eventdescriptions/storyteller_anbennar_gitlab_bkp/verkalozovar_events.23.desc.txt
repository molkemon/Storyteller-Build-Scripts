The vast sands of the desert realm are extremely hostile. Inhabited by the Sirtana peoples, they've masterfully adapted to the desert. Building marvels of sandstone, they've made their home in the desert quite a lively one for such a desolate place. A few dwarves have been found here, aiding the building of castles and great projects in the Sirtana lands. They seem wholly unaware that mere days away is the home of their brethren.
