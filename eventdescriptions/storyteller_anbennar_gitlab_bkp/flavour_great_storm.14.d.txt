The lord was running. Not fleeing. This was only a temporary setback, yes. Running towards lakemen land was unprecedented, but he was certain that he'd be able to lay low for a while. The sound of battle was still ringing in his ear. The screams, the clashing metal. A part of him wished that he'd fallen there as well. To be with Irdaeos' at this moment. But no. He had a responsibility to uphold. To liberate the Filustkhuga, to enact their revenge He has found the coast. Soon he'd be out of the expanding tempest's reach. Then he sees him. A lone centaur, standing a fair distance from him, but still in his way. He knows that he could not leave him alive. His position must remain a secret. He approaches, lance in hand.
