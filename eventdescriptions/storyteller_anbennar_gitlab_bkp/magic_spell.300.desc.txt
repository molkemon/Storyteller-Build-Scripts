The stars were right. The bones held true. The doves were numbered correctly, and the micidorian count came back off the charts: this figure was undeniably the Chosen One spoken of in prophecy. Upon hearing the news, they dropped whatever they were previously doing and traveled to our capital, accepting their heroic destiny serving their lord. There are lots of prophecies, of course, and therefore many Chosen Ones. Seems like there's a new one every day, eager to prove their heroic service to our government. Which one shall we select?
