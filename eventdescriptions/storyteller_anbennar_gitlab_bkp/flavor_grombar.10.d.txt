There is blood along the Alen, streaked across its mighty flow. An amount of pain and hardship, that no ork should ever know.  The blood is spread by endless ships, a seemingly endless force. These galleys run by slavers, and rowed by mighty orks.  They round us up, those slavers, into a parody of a horde. They whip and curse and hate us, put our children to the sword.  So where, you ask, does the Alen flow, where do these slavers dwell? The frozen wastes far to the north, or in the endless fires of hell?  You would be wrong, for it’s much worse, the land of these slavers is far more grotesque. A city of death, of pain, and of suffering, a city they call Vertesk.
