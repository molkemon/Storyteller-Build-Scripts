Spirits are roaming all across our nation, from our cities to our fields. While many of these spirits are peaceful and content in their nature, there are some who are filled with malice and evil intent. These malevolent spirits have been committing gruesome acts, murdering innocent civilians, draining their bodies of blood, and leaving their corpses turned inside out. It is imperative that these malevolent spirits are hunted down and banished from the mortal plane to protect our people.
