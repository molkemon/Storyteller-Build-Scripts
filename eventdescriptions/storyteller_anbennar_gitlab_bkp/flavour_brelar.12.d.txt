For too long have the priestly faction, the Rivers controlled the great floating city of Bosancovac after a questionable holy coup. Supported by the Lordship of Amacimst, they schemed against the patrician faction, the Boats to remove them from power and eradicate the republican traditions of the nation. Removing the former mayor, they confined them in the town of Imstana as they turned Bosancovac into a temple, placing its rule in the hands of the ynn river priests.  However, with our funds and manpower, we will support the ousted Boats and stage an uprising to bring back the democratic order back along with loyal allies. May the Boats sail smoothly into the harbour of triumph, charting a course for renewed prosperity and unity.
