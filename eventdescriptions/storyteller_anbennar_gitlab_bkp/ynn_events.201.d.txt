Trhevyr Darantynn was a Sarda merchant born in the past. He was the first Ynnic Ruinborn ever recorded to travel to the Trollsbay after foreign contact, conducting business that ultimately led him to pioneer the adoption of the stirrup, new banking systems and partnership techniques across the land. After years abroad, Trhevyr returned home in the past where he began the construction of the Darantynn Palace to be his new home, using his acquired wealth to fund the works and decorate the halls with a collection of Moon Elven artwork he purchased through his foreign contacts.
