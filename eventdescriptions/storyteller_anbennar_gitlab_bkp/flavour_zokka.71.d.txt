Once, a gnoll sowed seeds in soil soaked red.  Nanaya was her name, MasteroftheGarden. She came from the south as a storm, steel in her hand and fire in her eyes, screaming vengeance for an unknown crime.  She returned with fury. The Golden Queendoms were razed, their claims over her blood extinguished. The sad Sur knelt before her. Kingdoms fell one by one, and from her ashes she forged the crown of Xhutivert.  And fire, of course, attracts shadow. Varuuk found her, the WhispererofTruths; or perhaps, she found him. Her sword hand gave way to sorcery, and her empire bloomed with its new infernal roots. The price of her ascension was paid in blood.  Nanaya became the Fifth. The Red was brighter than any who came before; except, perhaps, for the First. And then, she met the Phoenix. Jaher broke her before Brasan, her garden scattered to the wind, withering in the blaze of a false flame.  For the higher we climb, the further we fall. Unless, of course, we can soar.  Whence shall her lessons be learned?
