A Sun Elf merchant has recently begun making inroads into the local perfume market. This has been achieved by a number of rather cutthroat practices, such as buying out failing competitors, artificially restricting the markets by buying product before it can arrive, and there are even accusations of direct sabotage of their competitors. The individual in question, however, has presented an exquisite collection of scents and smells collected from distant lands as a gift to the lord and the court at a recent royal function. If the accusations are true, then it is an obvious attempt to buy cooperation.
