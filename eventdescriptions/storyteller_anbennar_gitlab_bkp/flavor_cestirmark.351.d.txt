The legend of the Whisperer of the Depths was just a legend until recently. Unfortunately, legends tend to prove themselves more real than you'd like around here. All reports from the Special Cases Prevention Bureau indicated that this might be their worst case. The signs were subtle, the number of people affected impossible to determine, and leads few.

Under the guise of naval exercises, we worked with the Bureau to scour the bay with some of their agents on board in hopes of finding something. The ships, an entire battle squadron, didn't return. No one is sure what happened, but humans really shouldn't scream like that.
