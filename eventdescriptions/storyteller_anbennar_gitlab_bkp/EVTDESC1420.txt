There is a gentleman in our lands preaching to the people that their home would be better off free from the clutches of imperial rule and that the entirety of the region should embrace selfgovernance. While many regarded him at first as a harmless nuisance, the number of followers he's gained is rapidly growing, and some members of our government worry that eventually, this founding father will incite a violent rebellion in our lands in an attempt to break away from the imperial rule.
