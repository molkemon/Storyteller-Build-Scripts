Though perhaps they would never admit it, every war artificer has a fantasy. They dream that even on a place as primal as the battlefield, brains can be made to beat brawn. This is why they design their weapons, and deploy them to such great effect. They want to live out their childhood fantasies of burning the faces of their toy soldiers off with a magnifying glass. Now, the artificers of our realm do not have to choose between brains and brawn anymore. With the power of the gene magic of Insyaa, we can grow anything far beyond its natural, non-frightening size, including people. Our soldiers can dash about a battlefield like terrifying giants with unnatural speed, lobbing grenades the size of pumpkins at enemy soldiers and simply picking the enemy up and dashing them against the ground in close combat. Of course, the artificers have been the only ones to undergo the titano-growth procedure, because the other soldiers are not mad or manic enough to volunteer. Perhaps they should dream bigger dreams.
