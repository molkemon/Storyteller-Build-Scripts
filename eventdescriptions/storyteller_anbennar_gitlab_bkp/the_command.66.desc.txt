Most races have proven themselves unworthy to stand by our side, but the humans show potential. They are dynamic, adaptable, and possess great stamina. If we can channel these traits and suppress their inherent fractiousness, they could be of great service to the realm. To do this, we will teach the humans the virtues of absolute order and strength and turn them into loyal and fearless citizens and soldiers. This will be done from their youth onwards through comprehensive education, followed by combat training and philosophical indoctrination. They will slowly but surely embrace our traditions, virtues, and philosophies and serve us faithfully.
