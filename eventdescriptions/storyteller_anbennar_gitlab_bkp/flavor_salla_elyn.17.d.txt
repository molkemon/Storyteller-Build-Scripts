"The researcher's body could not be recovered, my liege. though we have identified that the lab belonged to Lidrandel Duskweaver."  Such were the words of the court mage that brought the monarch to the site of the accident; a laboratory deep within the woods of the Redglades; plumes of arcanaridden smoke still wafted up into the sky, while the laboratory was swaddled in new verdant growth, curiously untouched by fire. As the monarch stepped towards the laboratory, their boots sunk into the thick layer of ash that riddled the ground.  "A shame; her work has been invaluable thus far. We can only hope that she escaped the incident unscathed. What exactly was she working on here?" The monarch's eyes lingered upon a set of vines wrapping itself around one of the laboratory's pillars. The vines seemed to grow, bit by bit reaching further up the pillar's height, far faster than should have been possible.  "According to the records, Duskweaver was experimenting on the connection between the Redglades and the Feyrealm, though that hardly explains what exactly transpired here; none of her previous work has ever resulted in similar phenomena; as you know." The court mage barely looked up as she spoke, rifling through the pages of a recovered journal.  "Have the nearby forests searched by the local guards for any signs of the researcher. Should she still be alive, it would be best that we find her as soon as possible."
