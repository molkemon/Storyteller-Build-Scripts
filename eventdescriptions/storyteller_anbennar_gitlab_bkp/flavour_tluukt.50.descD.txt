As Tluukt CleaverofRealms passes, our pack enters the most delicate period of a gnoll tribe’s existence. A new, untested lord is a golden opportunity for the dissatisfied and the disloyal to assert a new paradigm of power within the pack. Yet, though the grand dreams of Tluukt still eluded her by the hour, her time on Halann reached its end, her few successes as they were prove enough to maintain the loyalty of the tribes of the sad Sur for her successors, though only so long as the successes continue. The future of our pack is far from certain, but it now falls upon those who inherited Tluukt’s ambitions to guide them to fruition.
