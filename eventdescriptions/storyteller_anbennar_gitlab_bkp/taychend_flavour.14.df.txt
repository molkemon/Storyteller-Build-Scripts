Charge of the Challúmata! The Challúmata are feared automatons that patrol the ruins of Andeios in service of their long-dead masters. Orichalchum statues animated by infused animal souls, protected and enhanced through enchantments, they are given strict, unbreakable orders they follow for all eternity. Though Orichalchum is less durable than even standard bronze, solid metal is solid metal regardless, and any being wielding a spear and shield, or molded into the shape of a bull or bear, can be a fiend in a fight. When our expeditionaries were delving into the slave warren, they came across many humanoid Challúmata, molded in the shape of elven soldiers. They held these off well enough, bringing many down—but when a Challúmata in the shape of a raging bull burst through an unseen door and attacked them from behind, drawn to its "comrades" by the clash of metal on metal, the expedition was destroyed. Only a few lucky elves lived to tell the tale. Now that we know what to expect, our next expedition will likely be much more successful, though it will still be costly.
