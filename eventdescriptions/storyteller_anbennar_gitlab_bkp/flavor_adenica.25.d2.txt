Immediately, each of the dozen or so Adenner knights present starts to clamour for the honour of facing this brazen Lorentish challenger. Our lord slams their fist down. "Enough. I will choose the knight who will face this Lorentish fop", our lord says in the intervening silence. Half an hour later, the knight, chosen for their record in jousts, stands calm and ready. Clad in the simple royal colors of white and blue, the unadorned knight looks striking against the dark brown and gashes of green of the tournament ground. Their opponent, the handsome Lorentish knight, stood proudly, with the swirl of the Lorentish rose prominent upon their gaudy chestpiece and a massive plume upon their helmet. Despite their ostentatious display, they are clearly quite practiced in the saddle, wielding their long lance expertly as they wheel their horse around the grounds. Both knights ready themselves. The clamour of the crowd rises in intensity, until suddenly, the signal is given to begin. Our lord clenches the lip of the railing in anticipation as the two knights start their headlong charge toward each other. All hangs in the balance for a splitsecond, until the tension releases in an instant as each knight hits the other's shield with a loud, metallic clang. With the first pass inconclusive, the contest now began in earnest, neither knight giving an inch of ground with their charges. Finally, the Adenner knight, perhaps weary, lifts their shield a hair too late, as their opponent's lance slips past the guard and knocks them to the ground. The crowd jeers initially, unhappy at the sight of their champion bested. As our lord starts clapping in respect, however, they soon begrudgingly follow their lead.
