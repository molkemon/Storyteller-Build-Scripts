With the passing of king Uman, the throne of the Gelkar kingdoms of Gelkalis and Lawassar has passed down to Allaniwiya sawarhassa, Uman’s heiress, but also the daughter of Ariyasari, the last king of Lawassar, and now she has a choice to make.  She could rule from the Iron Star Palace of Gelkalis, her adoptive home, a city that has become the center of the kingdoms due to the influx of refugees from the old kingdom of Elusadul.  Or she could rule from the Underground Palace of Lawassar, her father's kingdom, closer to Mount Lazzaward and to the mages that inhabit the sacred mountain.
