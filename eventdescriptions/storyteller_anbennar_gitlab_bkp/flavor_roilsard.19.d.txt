Their ruler of Anbennar stand and receive this Imperial decree: We, the Imperial Throne, blessed by the will of Castelos as his Regent on Halann, and in harmony with the wishes of the Princes of the Empire assembled, do hereby issue the following decree: The Roilsardi, leal and able subjects of the Empire, are the rightful suzerains of the Western Dames head. They are the manifestation of their authority, claim, and command to the frontier of the Empire, and shall be forever more. Thus We have decreed.
