The Lord of Gor Vazumbrog stepped into the longforgotten chamber, their armoured boots echoing against the cold stone floor. The air was heavy with age, carrying the faint tang of rust and the subtle sweetness of long dried incense. In the flickering torchlight, the final resting place of Krummul orkslayer revealed itself. Carved from black basalt and veined with shimmering threads of titanium, the sarcophagus dominated the room. Intricate reliefs adorned its surface, depicting Krummul's greatest victories. One panel showed him cleaving through orks with his greatsword Setzunklufa, while another captured his battle against the Ancient Deep Crows. Around the chamber, murals depicted his Monster Hunters standing alongside hulking titanium golems, as they held the line against tides of goblins. This particular detail seized the lord's attention, reminding them how much has been lost. Setzunklufa rested on a dais beside the sarcophagus, untouched by time. Its enormous blade, forged from steel laced with mithril, shimmered faintly reflecting torchlight. The lord knelt, bowing their head in silent respect. As they rose and turned to leave, the murals seemed to burn brighter in their mind's eye. Inspiration surged through them; vision of a stronger, united Gor Vazumbrog, its defenders echoing the honor and courage of Krummul's age. Their current dominion seemed like a pale shadow, compared with the heights of ancient empires. 'What was ruined, can be rebuilt. What was lost can be regained.' the lord whispered with determination. When the lord emerged from the tomb, they couldn't help but feel a renewed sense of purpose.
