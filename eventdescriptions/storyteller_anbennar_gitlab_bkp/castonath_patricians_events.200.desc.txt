Castonath lies under the influence of the patricians; a quasimercantile, quasinoble ruling class of ancient Castanor origin, known for being the de facto rulers of the city, living in its most prominent palaces and towers. It is said that no ruler of Castonath can control the city without the express consent of the patricians, who hold immense influence and sway even in the wake of the Greentide. The patricians have reached out to the current lord; finally deeming them worthy of their attention. Years of economic and political standoff between the patricians, who have made Castonath their playground, and the nominal rulers of the city (you), has in the end benefitted no one. The patricians now wish for a more mutually beneficial relationship between the crown and their members, much like the privileged relationship they held with feudal Castellyr. Be warned, the patricians are unlikely to extend this offer of negotiation again; sever ties with them and you will also sever ties with the legacy of Castanor.
