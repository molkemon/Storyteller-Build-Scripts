Sabotaging important rituals. Denying them access to valued trade goods. Spreading vicious rumours about everything from their intellectual capacity to their bedroom abilities. And the Shadowmoon Conspiracy is not just one prince, it is a union of many, all serving the purpose of their lord. Should we instruct them to pursue such acts?
