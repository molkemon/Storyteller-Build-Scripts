Although minotaurs are strong creatures, their small numbers and lack of organisation mean that, apart from a few ambushes on small caravans or on lone travellers, they pose no serious threat to our glorious nation. However, an exceptionally wellcoordinated herd of minotaurs has launched a raid on the arsenal of a nearby province, looting most of the weapons and causing trouble in the surrounding workshops and settlements. If we are to get rid of them before they can do any more damage to our subjects, it will be necessary to send a well equipped detachment of our soldiers and adventurers.
