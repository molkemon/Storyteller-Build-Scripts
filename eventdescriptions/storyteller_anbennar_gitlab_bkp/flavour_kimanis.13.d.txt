DEMAGOGUE: O men of Kimanis. Drunken cultists slither through your streets. Rise up.  MOB: JUSTICE. GLORY. POWER.  DEMAGOGUE: They in their drunken haze thought you, Kimanis fair, knew not your strength. Aid me.  MOB: THE CULT shall fall. The city stands. The power’s ours.  DEMAGOGUE: The cult knows not the power of our pack. The Hounds of Askovoinos hunt again.  MOB: We stand behind you, proud Son of Kimanis. Our power’s yours THE CULT shall fall: none shall withstand.  DEMAGOGUE: I am myself a single man without Kimanis; but with our strength combined we will prevail.  MOB: JUSTICE. JUSTICE. JUSTICE.
