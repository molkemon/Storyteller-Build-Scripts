An advisor bursts into the room as our ruler is having their lunch. "Wait.", he screams. All eyes lie on him as he presents a bowl of water. "I'm not thirsty," someone says. "No, not for drinking, for washing." With a hushed voice, he explains the newest culinary innovation; washing hands before stilling the Emptiness, which has vastly improved the health of our population. As these new customs spread through the country, many goblins have joined the trend and started experimenting with other cleaning agents. Thus far, the most effective appears to be an almost completely black, sticky, and gooey material that outsiders have identified as some sort of "soap". Although many sceptics still adamantly believe that some "natural aromas" of our food are lost through this revolutionary technique, the improved health far outweighs the culinary shortcomings.
