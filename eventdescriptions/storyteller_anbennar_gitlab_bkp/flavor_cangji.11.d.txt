As we continue to march towards the lakes of the realm, we have received multiple correspondences from a province of the lakes known as Sunijalla. Languishing under the rule of democracy, Sunijalla wishes to break free from the realm and instead serve under our banner. Allies in the region are few and far between, and we have seen what democracy does to the magically inclined. We send them our acceptance of their terms as we continue towards the lakes.

Pyremages, now reinforced by all the magical techniques they have learned throughout Gozengun, reinforce our soldiers with fiery weapons and shimmering veils of water. The realm of Kalsyto has long oppressed those with magical gifts such as ours, and so the only way we can truly defeat them is to show them why oppression cannot lead to a sustainable domain. The ways of the realm will be corrected, and magic will flourish in the lake federation once more!
