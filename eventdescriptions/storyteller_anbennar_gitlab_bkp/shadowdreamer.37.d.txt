For a time, it seems as though the pit will claim us. The swarm attacks without pause. Xazor moves through the darkness as a force of nature, striking, vanishing, and striking yet again. Slowly, our forces are driven further back against the wall. Yet we do not break.  As all seems lost, and our formation begins to crumble, something finally changes. Our weapons finally find their mark. A great cry tears through the cavern as a blade bites deep into Xazor’s left wing. The membrane splits, and thick, black ichor spills across the stone below, hissing as it strikes the ground.  For the first time, she falters. And our host seizes the chance, surging forwards towards the beast in an effort to end this once and for all. Yet it is not enough. With a furious beat of her wounded wings, Xazor tears herself free from the fight, the force of her retreat extinguishing torches and leaving only darkness behind.  The formation lies in ruin. The ground is thick with the fallen. What remains of the host gathers slowly, dragging the wounded and counting the few survivors. Victory was within reach, but it does not come to us today.  Yet, something has changed. Xazor Bleeds.  This creature is no untouchable horror of the deep. She can hurt. And now, she will remember us.
