Death to Traitors. Glory to Our Heroes. Onlookers gazed in amazement as banners bearing these slogans were draped over the palace walls in the capital city. In the early hours of today, while the lord was away, a group of rebels led by Mkumo .Xe Iqhusye had seized the palace and instigated plans to form an army to depose the lord and enforce Efergezmu authority. Bands of enthusiastic rebels, organized by members of the Efergezmu elite, who saw the lord as a traitor caving to the interests of a foreign class, descended upon the capital to join the struggle.
