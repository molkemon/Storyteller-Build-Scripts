The Syrhana Delathan is called only out of necessity in the typical Delathine Confederation. The unfortunate passing of Rabulath, our ruler, is one such occasion, however. Elections are not a pleasant affair for would-be candidates, as the decision must be unanimous among the constituent Delaths. Three of the most influential of our domain have submitted their candidacy, and will spend the next month rallying support among the Delaths.
