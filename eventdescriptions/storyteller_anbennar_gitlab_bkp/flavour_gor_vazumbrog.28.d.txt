To the General Staff,  Following the devastating passage of the entity known as the Hunter of the Deep, I must submit an urgent requisition for reinforcements and supplies to stabilise the Middle Dwarovar region.  Current troop strength stands at 4 companies, reduced from an initial muster of 7. The attrition is primarily due to combat losses, collapserelated fatalities, and subsequent desertions. Morale among the remaining forces is tenuous, with rations reduced to threequarters and no fresh water sources in operational proximity.  Infrastructure is critically compromised. The supply route through Breachstone Tunnel remains blocked by debris; attempts to clear it have been delayed by structural instability. The stockpile at Outpost Krazhavar is inaccessible due to a fissure collapse at Marker 14. Additionally, the eastern watchpoint; designated Point Greynail; has been destabilized and deemed unsafe for operations. Reports of incursions have multiplied.  I formally request: An additional 3 battalions, including combat engineers. A resupply convoy with stonework tools, masonry kits, and medical provisions. Authorization to conscript local labor for logistical operations.  Without immediate support, our position will degrade further. I await your directives.  Colonel Thargas Ashforge of Middle Dwarovar Command
