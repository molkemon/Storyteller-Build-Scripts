The discontent and anonymity between the lower classes and the rulers of the city has brought us to the brink of civil war. We are illprepared to face a fullfledged rebellion, and many members of the upper classes are urging the city council to take the matter to their lord, to beg their lord himself for any help he is able to provide. Good strong men and gold are what we need most to end the fires of rebellion that smolder the streets of the capital.
