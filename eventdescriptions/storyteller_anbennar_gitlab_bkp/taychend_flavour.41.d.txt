In the island ports of Kosiphineion or Paraidar, Kheionai merchants and captains have always had a presence in Taychend, even before Laskaris’ conquest made such a presence rather political. There were always those partial to Ambhen allegiances or Taychendi hostility, however. Liandithe Echatos, an adventurous Amgrebhen ship captain and occasional pirate, made many visits to Vul Tenvach, eventually calling its ports her home – and making an impression on Alvardip Tailarudu himself. Eventually, they became lovers.  The halfKheionai boy born of this union was called Olorises – given the surname Pulojith in a reflection of his heritage, but not his parentage – and quickly proved as talented and quickminded as he was impossibly ambitious. By all accounts, he has taken after his mother; adventuring across Vul Tenvach and beyond, and engrossing himself in every tale Liandithe has told him about both the wonders and horrors of the world.  Far outside the line of succession on account of his status as a bastard, there are rumours that he is nonetheless plotting to imminently take over – and even more worrying rumours that he wishes to implement a wide series of Kheionaiinspired reforms to bring Vul Tenvach into the new age. if we act now, we might be able to stop his coup.
