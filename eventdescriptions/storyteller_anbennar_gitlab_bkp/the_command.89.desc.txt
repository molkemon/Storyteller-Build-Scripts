Having failed to quash the ongoing rebellions, some hobgoblins are taking action into their own hands, refusing to wait for an army to stabilize their realm and fearing an unchained shaman might slay them all in their sleep. With makeshift weapons, they have assaulted multiple villages where mages were suspected of hiding, and killed a large portion of their population as "shamans and traitors." Not only has this made a mockery of the Great Command's order, it's not clear that a single mage was killed despite the destruction unleashed.
