Our preliminary expeditions around the dig site in the region uncovered a long, corridorlike cavern. We decided to explore it and found signs of battle: scattered orkish and goblin weapons and some broken dwarven axes. When we followed the victors trail, we ran into a settlement of battlehardened dwarves, somehow still surviving after all this time. When they heard of our nations exploits they eagerly joined up with our forces and they are now fullyequipped, employed as guards near the dig site.
