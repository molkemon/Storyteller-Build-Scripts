The province has seen a sudden upturn in their fortunes. While some were quick to thank the spirits for the increased trade, local ministers have pointed out that local kobolds are actually to credit for increasing merchant traffic through the woodland. All throughout the region, and wherever merchants can be found abroad, paper lanterns, some plain though most are intricately decorated, are used for light and beauty. This kobold group is selling such lanterns with a novelty that no other lanternmaker can claim; these illuminate without fire. While studying a local variety of firefly, one of the monks discovered that the larvae and adults prey on the same variety of mealworm, which enabled the group to domesticate the tiny bugs. A wellmade firefly lantern, decorated with earthtone dyes and a scattering of the kobolds own golden scales, has been sent to the ruler as a gift.
