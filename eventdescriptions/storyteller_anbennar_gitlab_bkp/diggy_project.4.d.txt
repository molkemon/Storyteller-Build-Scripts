We have finally finished improving our advanced metropolis infrastructure in our city. Entire apartment blocks are dug into the walls, not only rooms but entire complexes are buried deep into the stone of the Dwarovar. Dwarovar rail tech has been reused to create a public transportation that can take one between districts in less than thirty minutes. Strangely, this fascinating new development has been given the nickname "the Tube", or the "Underground". Don't they know every part of the Hold is underground?
