Gerudians, unlike southern lords, are not bound by land to serve our lords. Our rulers must therefore rule by the power of their conviction and the strength of their will. Times have been hard for the people of the nation, and many attribute the country’s difficulties to our monarch’s lack of strong leadership. Matters have become so serious that a minor, yet willful member of the nobility has risen with an army and seeks to overthrow the monarch. We now have no choice but to meet them upon the battlefield. We must arrest further slip into anarchy.
