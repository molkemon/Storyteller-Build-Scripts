The Lake Federation features an array of splendid cities, and many harbors the Treasure Fleet pulls into are numerous, but many sailors would swear Jiantsiang is distinctive. Well, it's not the city they'd cite (though the city's temples and walls are impressive) but the people — the only place, and only time, most will ever see a harimari is when docking in Jiantsiang. The silk is splendid, the bribes required to smooth trade are tremendous. But it is the merchants driving deals as fierce as their claws that mark the visit, and the harimari's stern faces will remain in the captain's mind even when the ships pull away.
