The diplomat we sent to the Free Slaves returned on a small scull, washing ashore onto the dunes near Davharral. By the grim look on his face, and the Desert Elven curses that inflect his prayer to Surael for thanks on his return, we know their decision. We cannot be sure why they chose idiocy over opportunity; we will have to ask their leaders in person when Surael scorches open the gates of Srayen open and their eyes fill with His light.
