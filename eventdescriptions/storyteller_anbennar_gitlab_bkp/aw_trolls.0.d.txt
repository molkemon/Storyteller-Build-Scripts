For most people, trolls are known for two things: their grotesque noses, and their impressive regenerative abilities that make them incredibly difficult to kill.  But for those who have lived among trolls, there is a greater peril in their existence within a province's ecosystem. Due to their large size and regenerative abilities trolls have no natural threat in the wild; they are apex predators. Leave a troll alone enough and they will eventually devastate the environment, eating and wiping out all wildlife.
