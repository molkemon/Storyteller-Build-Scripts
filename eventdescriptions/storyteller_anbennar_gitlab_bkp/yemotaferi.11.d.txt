Proximity to Mother Shadowroot is a wondrous thing. So close to the font of dark energy, you feel your mind slipping, hallucinations mix with reality, and deep in your bones you hear a faroff place calling for your soul. Walking away from that marvellous trance takes great strength of will. Many would be content to sleep forever under the Mother's canopy. More than once, wardens are dragged back against their will.  The magically inclined among us find themselves bursting with power after a visit to the Shadowroot Shrine. The touch of shadow often leaves its marks, turning a supplicant's eyes pitchblack or drawing a web of dark lines across their face. A fearsome guise to outsiders, but a mark of honour as far as we are concerned.
