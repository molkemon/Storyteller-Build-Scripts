The boy sat on his throne, looking out to the court he now oversees, to the realm he now rules. He looked to his left and right, seeing the loyal lords who form his regency council. She never got to see him like this; she died the night before his ascent and was buried on a small hill, a tree placed atop her grave as per her request. So loving she was, wanting her grave to provide shade for her people. He ignored the endless chatter of his advisors, who seek to distract his thoughts with useless trivia, hoping he would bore easily and retire from his duties, thus leaving them with the throne.
