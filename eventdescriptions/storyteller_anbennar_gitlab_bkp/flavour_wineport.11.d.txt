When the ruler had suggested the idea of Imperial membership, they had perhaps expected the process of organising and presenting the formal request to take a bit longer. A group of Wine Barons, already trading in the Empire via another region, have come with one prepared and ready to go to the Emperor once the title requests it; if they do. The request comes with a prewritten letter, and a fine panoply of gifts to represent the benefits should we join. This is, unsurprisingly, mostly wine; the crown jewel amongst these a bottle of an excellent vintage. The merchant delegation presenting the petition are all familiar with the Imperial Court as well, ensuring that such a presentation of the request will go over smoothly. It is now simply up to our title if they wishes to go ahead with the petition, or whether the League of Winebay should retain its total independence.
