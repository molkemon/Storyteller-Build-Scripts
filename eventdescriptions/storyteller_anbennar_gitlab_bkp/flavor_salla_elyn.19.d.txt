During the War of the SorcererKing, shortly after our people first arrived in Cannor, a battalion of Moon Elves disappeared after being forced to retreat by their foes. For centuries, we had thought that they had been slain by the SorcererKing's forces, yet that assumption has proven false.  A contingent of elves, donning equipment that resembles that of the lost battalion, arrived in one of our towns; with them, they brought someone who claims to be Lidrandel Duskweaver, the researcher whose laboratory suffered a catastrophic accident some time ago and whose body we couldn't recover. These elves claim to be the descendants of the lost battalion; they further claim to have been trapped within the Deepwoods; a forest to the southeast, near Escann.  While Duskweaver has sought out her daughter for a reunion, the Wood Elves have taken the time to explore our lands; already having begun to forge ties with our people. However, Duskweaver's daughter claims that the person they have brought with them is not, in fact, Lidrandel Duskweaver. Instead, she claims that the visitor is some sort of shapechanger, one who has escorted the familiar of her late mother back home. This owl familiar, the daughter claims, has shared with her the last words of her mother and thus knows she must be dead.  Regardless, these 'Wood Elves', as they are called, will depart on the long journey home. Their travels have been arduous and fraught with danger; and they have not gone unscathed. For their service bringing 'one of our people' home; or at the very least, bringing Duskweaver's daughter some closure; some of our soldiers have volunteered to accompany them on their trek back, to ensure their safety.
