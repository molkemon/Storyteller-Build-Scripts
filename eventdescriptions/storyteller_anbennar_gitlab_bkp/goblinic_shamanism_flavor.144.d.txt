Fanatic cultists of Kravi Oznad in a remote province have spent far too long at peace. Recently their battle fervour reached a delirious climax, as the rattle of spider feet and wild cries of the goblins that rode astride them descended upon the area's settlements. Strangely, in spite of the havoc they wrought, their lust for strife began to spread through the ruined towns as more wildeyed soldiers flocked to their banners, ready to die gloriously in the march to Kravi Oznad.
