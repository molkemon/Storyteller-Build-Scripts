This was a foe unlike any we have faced thusfar. The beast clambered up and down the walls, gracefully weaving between projectiles and rock formations like a dancer across a stage. Even when arrows or spells met their mark, they bounced harmlessly off of the Behir's thick blue hide, less bothersome than a bee's sting. Yet our men were relentless. Dodging lightning breath and creating wards, they steadily and systematically took control of the situation, beating the Behir back with each successful blow. After many minutes of tense backandforth, silence envelops the field.
