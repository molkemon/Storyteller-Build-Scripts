Here in Sanebanko we glimpse the wild expanse of the stormwracked Kaydhano, a great swirling desert mass shot through with corrosive tuqramu dust, the chaotic substance which inculcates wild magic. This is the edge of our world. The birds which have adapted to the Kaydhano cross back with immense wisdom, strengthened and made fearsome; the foothills of the Shimmering Overlook offer vistas of staggering beauty, and veins of precious gems torn open by the rare Kaydhano gusts which surge northwards.
