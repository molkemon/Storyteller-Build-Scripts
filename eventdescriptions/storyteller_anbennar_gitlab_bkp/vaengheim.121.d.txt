And so did one of our lord’s wingmaidens meet a man on the hunt, who was himself a hunter, and who was skilled with bow and spear and song. And so did they find each other pleasing to the eye, and warming to the soul. And so did they sing together, and hunt together, and lay together, and so strong was their love that the wingmaiden, her heart ablaze, stood before our lord and said, "My lord, I have found a hunter, and he is pleasing to me, and I would keep him with me on this hunt.
