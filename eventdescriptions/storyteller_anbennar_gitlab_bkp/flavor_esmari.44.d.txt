As we survey our recent conquest, Esmaraine, a group of Esmaryan priestess approaches us. After greeting and welcoming us to the city, they start to talk about the problems plaguing it, the poverty, the overcrowding, and the worst of all the corruption… The picture they paint is bleak, cultists rule the city and Forsana and Hedine have their claws dug deep in the heart of Esmaria. They request we surrender the city to them, so that the temple can clean the Esmaraine of its sins.
