When our first Homunculus was completed, deep questions immediately arose. As they spoke and expressed their thoughts, even our best philosophers were left speechless. Can an artificial life feel love? Can it feel anything at all? Where do we draw the line? Where do we build that wall?

When a cat greets the return of its caretaker, is that not an expression of love? When a dog is excited for its caretaker waking, is that not an expression of love? Why should we not extend love to all of Lel's life? Why should we draw lines where none exist? Can the Homunculus think? Does its existence not persist?

Lel holds the creation of life through love above all else in her portfolio. Did not Mihipha pour love into this life they grew?
