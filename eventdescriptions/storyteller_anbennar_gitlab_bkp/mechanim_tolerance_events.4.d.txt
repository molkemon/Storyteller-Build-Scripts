While paying a visit to the Silmuna School of the Arts in a city, our lord notices a group of strange creatures standing in a dimly lit corner of the library. It's difficult to see them clearly, but they can swear their skin has a soft metallic glow. "Ah, my lord, our lord hears the voice of the Head Librarian behind them. It seems you've taken an interest in our new students. I assume you've never seen a mechanim before?" The librarian beckons their lord to follow and starts walking towards the group. The mechanim look like they are arguing, but they cannot hear a single sound. However as they approach, they stop immediately and turn to them. "Let me introduce them. This is Ebbed Mist, a scholar from a province. I'm not quite sure what exactly their area of expertise is, but I seem to recall something about foresight and population patterns." A thin mechanim bows, instantly reminding our lord of one of their court advisors. His orange robe is tattered from what looks like close encounters with a thousand blades. "This one is BellChains. It was them who managed to safely navigate the treacherous titanoflora jungle and lead these mechanim to safety." The lord looks at the large mechanim, which barely grunts a response. A military man, not unlike the generals in the great army. "And my name is PrimePaleFear,"; interrupts the last one. "I'm just a simple merchant that tagged along, so I fear I will be of no use to you. My companions, however, are very capable and I'm sure their services will prove invaluable in making whatever your plans might be come to fruition."
