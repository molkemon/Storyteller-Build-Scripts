Traveler, in the year, the Holy Road Rrammoton was dedicated in the name of our lord. As the Sorrow winds, so too does the Holy Road follow the banks of the greatest work of Nirakhet. As the Sorrow bursts over its banks and enriches the fields, so too does the Holy Road enrich the Holy Land, the greatest work of Elikhet. As you were called to walk along the Holy Road, so too are we all called to worship the Khet. As this road stands forever, so too shall Konolkhatep. Inscription by the side of the Holy Road in Kheterat
