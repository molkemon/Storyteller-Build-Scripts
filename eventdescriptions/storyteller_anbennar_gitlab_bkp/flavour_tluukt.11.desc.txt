Waves of refugees herald grim tidings. The city of Birsartansbar, capital of Birzartanses, has fallen to gnollish conquest, cementing the gnollish grip over Bulwar Proper and ushering us true believers of Surael into an age of darkness unwitnessed. Our faith is challenged on every front as the legacy of Jaher is defiled and the legitimacy of the Sun Elves begins to crack.  More pressing, however, is the threat of further gnollish conquests leveraged by the numerous Birzartanesi artillery pieces that were surely captured during the city siege. Strong walls have always been the surest defense against gnolls, but that can no longer the case when those walls will soon be challenged by cannons of Birzartanesi make. Emergency measures must be taken, and a proper army mustered. It is obvious, however, that no subject of ours is keen to face these gnolls in battle, so it is crucial that we carefully determine who receives the brunt of this muster.
