The cry of Alice’s little brother sends her into a rage. She pushes past all her brothers and sisters as a distant righteous anger roars in her heart. She feels power, vengeance, and duty grow in her with each step.  This is not claiming their birthright, this us tyranny. This us evil. And it us her duty to stop it.  She calls Emil out, but the feral dog barks back defiance. She raises her sword as she quietly recites Aldresia’s oath to herself. Emil laughs uncomfortably at her as she prepares her own blade. A bead of sweat drips down his forehead.  Alice feels an energy circle around her; something is looking after her. She has no fear of death, no fear of failure. If Alice falls, she will die knowing she perished fighting for the right thing.  Alice takes a step forward.
