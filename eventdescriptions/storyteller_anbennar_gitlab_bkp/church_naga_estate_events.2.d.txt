Even now, they will not fawn over you. Theirs is the sacred duty to avert the madness of the one. They will watch you, their ruler. You will never go unobserved again. A time will come soon when you must once more return to the heavy places and prove yourself, but for now the naga are anchored at your back. A hundred millennia of lizardfolk strategy lie open to you with but a query, while rural nobility, cajoled by their local naga, are suddenly eager to volunteer labour for your logistical efforts. For the right leader, one who ever keeps an eye on the coming Storm, the Naga will risk even their own exalted blood. Take heed; the next test the Naga set you will prove more rigorous.
