REPORT: Conditions within controlled territory of Bahar and the Harpy Hills have proven much more stable and peaceful in recent years. While this has been advantageous in several respects, it should also be noted that this development has had some unfortunate consequences. While initially Voltoj bureaucrats were recruited from the ranks of orphans, which were in no short supply during times of chaos, continued stability has deprived the orphanages of new wards, making the traditional recruitment approach less effective.  It is recommended that the bureaucracy be adapted to allow for families to apply for their Voltoj children to join the academies, and that sufficient rewards be given to incentivize this decision. The attached documents discuss various proposals toward this end.
