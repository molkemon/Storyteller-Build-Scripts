A soarer mechanim soaring high up in the sky only to suddenly swoop down on a desperately fleeing target on the ground is a truly awe-inspiring yet daunting sight. But no matter their already astounding military capabilities, to the mechanim mindset, there is always room for improvement. By adding lightweight buoyant attachment struts to their torso, a soarer mechanim might carry several containers filled with explosives, ready to rain down on the enemy, or carry a fellow mechanim above enemy fortifications to infiltrate their defenses. In this way, these elegant flyers have become a sight feared by all soldiers tasked with guarding a fortress who justifiably dread the death from above.
