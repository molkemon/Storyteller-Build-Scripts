Finally, after generations of struggle, order has been returned. A Bahar once dirty and unclean from the stench of heathens and monsters, creatures of and tainted by the malevolent dark, is cleansed by deeds violent and peaceful. Bahar has been restored to order, with many a loose thread since before the Decade of Devastation removed as well. The Copper Dwarves have been crushed underfoot, so that they shall never threaten Bahar again, and even the last vestiges of heresy shiver before the might that can be seen in ReUyel as our elite elventrained army march the forests of this land. Bahar is clean, and we can only thank Surael's Chosen for it.
