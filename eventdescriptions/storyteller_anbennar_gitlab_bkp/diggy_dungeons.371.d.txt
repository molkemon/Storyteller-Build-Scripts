The hole in the wall was closed, we do not want more of those bugs coming out. At last we may rest. Some are trying to figure out how to transport those large eggs. A possible solution would be raising the bugs. Of course it sounds profitable; training and selling killing machines like that. Then they comment about the pink fish eggs too.  But what about this place? With the proper care and a good extension, it would be an amazing underground keep. Anyway, we should rest. In the next few days we will have a long journey back home. Our expedition's deeds will be remembered for long up there.
