A lowborn servant of immense magical talent has found their way into the Favored Hundred, the royal harem. Such raw power is rare in one of a lower bloodline, and what's more, their mind for politics, economics, and history led the State to provide for their formal education. They now stand ready to serve as an adviser at court. Having been totally insulated from the political influence of the various factions, they can be trusted to provide a crucially unbiased view of the administration.
