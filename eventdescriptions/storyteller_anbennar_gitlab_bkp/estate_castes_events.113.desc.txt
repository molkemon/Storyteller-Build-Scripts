"While most folks understand that the caste system exists for a reason, some will not be able to keep this rational in the face of adversity, causing great imbalance to themselves and others." -Our lord Radjgut the Elder. The labourers had not eaten much for the past few days as they had been late on schedule and were punished with lowered rations. When a philosopher from the temple passed by with baskets full of food for sacrificial purposes, they could not hold in their anger and hunger any longer. The labourers jumped on the unsuspecting philosopher, beating him up and stealing the food. Now we must decide what we will do with them.
