Having decided to utilize the dragon’s heart as the source of immortality for the potion, our researchers were finally ready to brew. Over the course of a few weeks, the alchemists meticulously prepared the base components for the potions, including the highly distilled potion base, the additives, the stabilizers and all other ingredients which they’d worked so hard to perfect. Then, when the core potion was prepared, the alchemists extracted the essence of the dragon’s heart, refined it, and finally imbued it into the potion, making it complete. The finalized potion was brought forward to the ruler, who took the potion and retired to their quarters. The following day, the ruler did not leave their chambers for breakfast nor lunch. As twilight drew nearer and nearer, the alchemists became more and more worried that the potion had failed catastrophically and that the ruler had not survived the process, but hope returned when the ruler announced, through their chambers doors, that they had an announcement, and that all high ranking members of government were to be assembled. Following the ruler’s request, when the most trusted advisors and ministers gathered near the ruler’s chambers, the doors opened revealing an interesting sight: A kobold, wearing the ruler’s clothes, and claiming that the potion had some unintended side effects. After confirming the kobold’s identity as the ruler, and running a few tests, the researchers came to a conclusion that the potion was indeed a success, however it had a few unexpected side effects, such as granting the ruler increased magical abilities, and most noticeably, turning them into a kobold. Despite this unexpected side effect, the ruler appears to have come to terms with the result.
