The fabrication of automatons is no easy endeavor. The fever of industry demands sacrifice and diligence. From raw ore to kitchen spoons, everything found its way into our smelters, and the roaring clang of hammers resounded against the delicate tinks of finesmiths until, at last, the fruits of our labor grew ripe.
