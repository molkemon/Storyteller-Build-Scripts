The growing power of the Medawor has upset many in a neighbouring land. One of these malcontents, a particularly charismatic preacher in a local province, has started to perform stirring sermons for the local populace, urging them to rise against the insidious influence of the nobles in defense of their sacred traditions. The preacher has achieved great popularity among the masses, and although there have not yet been any incidents related to these sermons, the Medawor are understandably concerned.
