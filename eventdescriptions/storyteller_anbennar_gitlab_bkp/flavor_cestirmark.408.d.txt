The Special Cases Prevention Bureau has given us the first report in years that didn't include any incidents. Eldritch entities lie in the darkness, but with few to heed their calls anymore; rumors of unexplained disappearances now talk about cats and no longer entire villages; superstitions are once again about silly beliefs, and those who hold actual power are few. The agents of the Bureau—those that are left, at least—can finally breathe and congratulate themselves. This is not a war won, but a battle: if we keep our guard down, this will happen again. We are holding a tide that is the world itself. If we want to stop it for good, the Bureau will need to be greatly expanded. But for now, the situation is back under control.
