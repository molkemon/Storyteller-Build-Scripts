War never changes; it inevitably heralds unimaginable horrors and slaughter; yet it undoubtedly changes those that are caught within its cruel embrace; it tempers those that risk their lives, and claims many more. Some may find themselves lost to war, even if they survive. Others may find that war becomes the only home they know, while yet others are able to return to a peaceful life, with their experiences strengthening them.  In that same vein, Farwen has been changed by her experiences in war. She has been tempered by them; every decision she has made, every life lost – whether by her own hand, or by her decisions – has left its mark upon her. Difficult times are known to forge strong leaders and Farwen is certainly no exception to the rule; reclaiming the Deepwoods, one grove at a time, has proven one of the most difficult conflicts the Wood Elves have ever weathered.  With the groves of the Deepwoods now firmly under the control of our people and the war winding down to a close, the time has come to pass the torch onwards. The next generation of Wood Elves crave their chance to shape their future. Thus, it has come to pass that Farwen will take up the title of Ceanna, leading our people into the next era of Wood Elven history. Her vision of the world, her ideals and experiences; the decisions she has made; shall pave the way for our people's future.
