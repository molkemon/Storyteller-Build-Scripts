The Skewered Drake had a special relationship with the Drakons of the caves. Violent, powerful, monstrous beasts, they make for incredible mounts if you can master them.  When we conquered their clan, we didn’t wipe out the beasts, and that leaves us with an opportunity. Either we can use them to train our own soldiers and set squads to spar with the monsters in pits, or simply allow the warriors of Skewered Drake to serve us in their own way, keeping their monsterriding traditions alive in service of the crusade.
