Since the opening of the new facilities at the Academy, the place has been abuzz with activity. Magi from all over the land, and even further afield have flocked here to make use of the facilities, without interference from established magical authorities. This extra talent has also fed into Project Divinity, and its testing has progressed greatly. The current ruler has even personally aided Archmage Veharia SzelNaza in some of the experiments, managing to imbue several horses with greatly increased alacrity and strength. Not all tests were such a success, some created monstrosities such as a threeheaded dog that breathed fire, however Veharia appears confident that they have gained enough control over the process to move forward with testing on humanoids.
