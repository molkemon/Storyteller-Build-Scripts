Tacenie, a grand relic of Precursor civilization that fell from the skies as their era ended, likely lies in ruins somewhere near our realm. Although it hasn't been discovered by chance in the centuries since, this great prize of our ancestors must be sought through a dedicated effort. Every valley, every forest, and every mound of sand could contain the remains of our birthright, and so this will likely take years. The only question is where to begin our search.
