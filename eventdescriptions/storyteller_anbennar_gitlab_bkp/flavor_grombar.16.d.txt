With the rising of the sun, over the horizon, comes what seems to be a gigantic caravan. Tens of thousands of orks, new arrivals from the east, with various allegiances to various clans, have arrived in our lands. They bear with them a piece of parchment, signed by a powerful lord himself, granting them the right to settle here. Although they take care to profess their loyalty to our lord, this will undoubtedly continue the process of our realm's slow loss of autonomy. Nonetheless, more subjects will surely be of aid.
