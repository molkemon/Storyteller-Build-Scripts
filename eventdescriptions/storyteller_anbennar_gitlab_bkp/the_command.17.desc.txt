A love forged in the embers of an age-old blood feud. The murder of a cousin in a botched street duel, and the shocking deception of a faked poisoning. A concerned family nurse has come forward with a story almost too tragic to be believed, but if true, has only added to the previously seen complications between Stolen Gem and Blackstep. An unknown wandering goblin vagabond from Stolen Gem has taken the hand of one meant to be betrothed to another in an arranged marriage intended to reduce tensions. Should we react quickly to such an event, we may be able to recover our standing in the eyes of the people. Some argue for acceptance of such acts, perpetrated in pursuit of noble ambitions like the love for another, regardless of societal differences or obstructions. But we can also make it clear to the two clans our stance in this entire matter by publicly decrying their love.
