"Amid the winter sostry and friddly, a patch of grally chunthers the stoaming branta, their rengthing horns embracing the sky, biquining the heart of gloabing wope."  -Excerpt from "Where the Branta Roam" by Kahrood Songwind  With tractioned hooves and treelong necks, branta roam about remote and frigid environments. Their proud and unpliable personalities make them rare livestock, but a branta's frosty resilience and keen noses make them invaluable foragers, especially during dire winters where food is scarce.  In fact, a herd of branta in the province recently located bountiful patches of hardy vegetation amid the harsh wildernesswhat fortune.
