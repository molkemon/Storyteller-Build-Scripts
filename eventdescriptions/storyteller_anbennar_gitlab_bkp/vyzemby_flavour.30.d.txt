It is said, mostly by the people of Rayafo, that Saikinoma is a city of wisdom. It rests upon fertile land, overlooking bountiful seas. It was built by wise hands, who understood the value of these things. But wisdom is a fragile thing, so easily lost to senility.  There are no secret chambers of preserved knowledge. All that was written has long since rotted away, or been spirited away. Old libraries, nestled within the honeycomb walls of Saikinoma are empty save for mold and decay. It is up to us to fill them again, to rebuild this place, to make it a home for wisdom once again.
