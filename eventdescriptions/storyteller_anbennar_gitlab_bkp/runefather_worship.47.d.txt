With the help of the Balvazha priests of the Groediazan, the Amlruzskappa has managed to produce a new set of blood runes to help our inquisitors scry, hunt down and purge any who are not dedicated to the great Runefather. In addition to this, the growing cooperation between these two factions have resulted in a further spread of blood runes across the administration, allowing for the Groediazan’s Dewara to manage doctrine and organize the state better. The Obsidian Council has already given the approval of these new runes, and now the runesmiths have begun mass production.
