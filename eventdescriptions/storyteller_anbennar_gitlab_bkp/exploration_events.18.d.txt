Our fleet set sail to find a sea route to the rich cities of Cannor. It took them 99 days to cross the ocean westward, after they had found the Kedwali Coast located near the tip of Sarhal.  The fleet's captain was eaten infront of his crew by a pack of gnolls but two of our ships had survived, one tried to return across the seas but perished. The last ship continued west and sailed across the Ocean of the Lost, and as it arrived at our port it became the first ship to circumnavigate the globe.
