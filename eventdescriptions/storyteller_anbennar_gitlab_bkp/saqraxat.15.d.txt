The wealth of the hilltop city of Golonela is well concealed. Countless Kheteratan tax assessors were historically frustrated by the inhabitants’ skill in concealing their true wealth. But now that our army has broken the city’s defenses, rooting out any resistance holdouts and dragging its notables from their hiding spots, we have free reign to finally balance the ledgers. No household will go untouched, every ounce of gold, jewelry and coinage will be looted, and their outstanding debt shall finally be paid.
