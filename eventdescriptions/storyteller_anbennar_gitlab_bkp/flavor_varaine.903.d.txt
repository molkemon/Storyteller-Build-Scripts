As our research into a potion of immortality advances, our researchers have started to come up with several different tonics and salves which may possess the qualities we desire to achieve. However, considering the delicate nature of the effect trying to be achieved, it is of utmost important that we're sure that the product in question actually works, and more than that, doesn't provide any negative side effects to the consumer, who might have to live with these consequences forever.  To this end, it is important that we start organizing trials right away to test the potion ideas. For this testing, we may try to find volunteers by offering suitable rewards to those wanting to participate. On the other hand, we may as well forcefully, or even unknowingly, utilize random test subjects without any reward, which may result in quite a lot of angry people.
