When Rogier found himself in Escann, his family dead and his troops surrounded by hostile orks, he thought the worst – but we endured. When the Young Owl took the throne, and saw only a single path – and a narrow one at that – to reclaiming the Silmuna legacy, there was little promise we would be able to walk it, but we have. Thus current news, that a foreign power has declined to join the Shadowmoon Conspiracy, while disappointing, pales before our determination and our destiny.
