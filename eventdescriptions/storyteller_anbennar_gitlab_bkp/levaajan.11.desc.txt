All monasteries teach that the nature of the path forward is not always clear, but their ruler has learned from experience: some days the path is clearer than others. And today, it was looking murky indeed. Facestealers. Oni. Hobgoblin. Centaurs. The terrorbird riders, and more. All of them encroach onto the lands of the Rukunai and Runsukhi. There are threats within those lands, as well. Turmoil blooms like a blossom on the vine, rivalries see spears sharpened, and resources are strained to the breaking point. The people, all united by the Supreme Spirit, begin to split. But perhaps. What if the Rukunai and Runsukhi were stitched together again? Could they be united? Could their group, their people be brought together? And in doing so, saved? Perhaps the day is not so murky. Perhaps there is a clear path ahead. A difficult one. But one that must be walked: We must unite our brethren.
