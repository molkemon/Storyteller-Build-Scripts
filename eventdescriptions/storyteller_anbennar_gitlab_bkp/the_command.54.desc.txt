Research Phase 9-26; Entry One. I feel like a clerk. The Forge complex is massive now, and the line chiefs barely need my input anymore, so I spend most of my time rubber-stamping supply requisitions. The perils of rendering oneself obsolete, I suppose. I expect any day now they'll bring in some bloodless quartermaster to run the whole thing a lot more efficiently than I do. Luckily, I've still got some credit with the brass. I've solved their shaman problem for good, as long as we can ship them out the Camps of Contrition and keep on top of the logistics. With such an abundance of activated Korashi, the natural next step is weaponization, but I'll wait to be relieved of duty before I start plunging my hands into that particular thicket… Personal Diary of Kitokan Lionborn, Korashi Project CRO
