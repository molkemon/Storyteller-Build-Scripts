It has been many years since we first began cultivating imported Kheionai grapes in our province. It has been a difficult process, filled with many setbacks, trials, and tribulations, but each new problem has taught us a new solution, and at last we can truly say that we have learnt the art of viticulture. Our vineyards are now thriving, selling native Taychendi wine across our realm, helping our balance of trade and stoking a sense of national pride— at last, we have beaten the ever-smug Kheionai at their own game!
