Grudges are everywhere in dwarven society, but we have our ways of working around them. The average dwarf knows it is far healthier to let grudges fester and worsen over time than to actually act on them, or we would be at each other's throats at all times. So long as we meticulously avoid one another, we can hate each other from a distance, and keep the peace.  However, when surface dwarves with a grudge meet each other abroad, though, they don't tend to have the restraint to stop themselves from starting a fight. Such a fight between surface dwarves seems to have broken out today.
