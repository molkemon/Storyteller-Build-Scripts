With a skilled cadre of scribes at our command, we may task them to use their talents for the glory of our domain and craft a powerful inscribed artifact that would rival those in the tales spun by the bazaar’s storytellers. However, even their great skill and experience will only allow them to create one such wonder at a time. Thus, we need to decide on what artifact should be made. A Crown of Wisdom to inspire our courtiers and lead their minds to new horizons? A Vessel of the Heavens as a fitting conveyance for our exalted ruler, showcasing the majesty of our realm? A Hammer of Shaping to support our craftsmen in their work? Or a Ring of Unity to bring all the people of our domain closer together, quelling any dissent and uniting them behind our noble rule?
