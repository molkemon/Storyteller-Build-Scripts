Despite fierce resistance, the lord is no match for the experienced vampire hunter. Although they fought valiantly, they were soon overwhelmed by the hunter. The last thing that they see as their eyes slowly close is the Hunter smashing the coffin, their saving grace, into pieces. They let out a final, shaking breath, knowing that they will not be coming back. Despite their attempts, they have come to know the embrace of death. Perhaps, at last, they will find the peace they never did in life.
