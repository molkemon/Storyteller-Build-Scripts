In Taychend, law is justified by the very means through which it is enforced—force. "Might makes right," as the saying goes. This naturally means that law-making power in a territory lies within the hands of the warlord who controls it. As a result, Taychend is a patchwork of different laws and legal traditions, making any attempt by a Warlord-King to impose their own new law codes destabilizing. Nonetheless, promulgating a new law code can be a powerful tool in setting a tone for one's reign and expanding one's power—no wonder then that our lord, undoubtedly a ruler with the requisite skills and might, has become interested in doing so.
