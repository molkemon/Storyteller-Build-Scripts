BONG. BONG. BONG. BONG. A small community has been plunged into chaos by – BONG. BONG. BONG. – flying gongs, disrupting the entire – BONG. BONG. BONG. – community. This clamour continues to wake townsfolk in the middle of the night, at work, or even in the comfort of their own homes. Many haven't slept in days, and there is no end in sight. The court proposes that we provide additional offerings to ancestral shrines in the hopes of appeasing the gongs. However, there is always the direct method, wherein we attempt to shoot these curiosities out of the sky, but, simply put, we are out of our element.
