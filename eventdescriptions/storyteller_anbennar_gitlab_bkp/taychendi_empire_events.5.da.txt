Our lord saw blurred images, snapping from one scene to the next. Armies marched through jungles, deserts, marshes, the Taychendi flag hoisted over cities of strange buildings—first some clearly Kheionai, then strange styles, with golden domes, smooth black spires, wooden cottages, and honeycomb fortresses. Then back to Taychend—a scene on the Kalavend, blood-soaked fields and broken bodies, with the Gathyanavaida tall in the background. A bolt from the astral whizzed through where its head would be, hurtling right towards our lord. Our lord's vision turned as if to run away—but a white-fanged shadow surprised them and pounced, its maw plunging them into darkness. The world was still for a moment—then there was a blinding burning light, a blazing sun rising, and a sun-spot soldier within, wings sprouting from their back. They drew twin blades and lunged.

Our lord snapped awake from the communion with a start, staggering back. "Be careful…" the Threadbearer said, as her hand steadied them, "the first time is always difficult. What did you see?" 

Our lord stared ahead at the Fateweaver's corpse, frowning. "Strange new lands… and dangers in the dark. Blood and battle…" the corners of their lips crept upwards "…and glory. Strange new lands, and exotic new territories. Dangers from the dark, and dangers to be overcome." Our lord smiled.
