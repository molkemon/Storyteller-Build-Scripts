One by one, we pulled the nails from Mother Shadowroot. We mended her mutilated trunk and branches meticulously, our great transmuters and botanists working in unison to heal the sapleaking wounds inflicted by the hags. Over time, we felt her panic subside. Fewer and fewer figs dropped from her branches, and the flow of dark energies became less overwhelming. Soon, the swamp will be a little more hospitable.  This endeavour has proven what we already knew in our hearts, that the Shadowroot is a gentle soul, one who takes care of her swampchildren. The nails that once pained her, we will melt down and use to construct a shrine in honour of the Mother. From now on, our people will have two guardians watching over us.
