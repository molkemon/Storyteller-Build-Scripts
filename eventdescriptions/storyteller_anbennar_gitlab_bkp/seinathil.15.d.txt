Notestock is designed to showcase the best talent and music that Seinathil has to offer. Unfortunately, turns out that with how poorly the country has been doing recently, the music is either limp, obviously incorrect, or scathing. Or bad, mostly bad. Legions of tired peasants, excited for a few weeks off at Notestock, turned to legions of angry rioters. Several rioters and musicians were killed, and it is not exactly a good look for our country.
