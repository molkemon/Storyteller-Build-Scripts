When we extend our mercantile influence throughout our associates, it normally takes time and resources to construct the relevant marketplaces. The Icewind clan, however, has both the men and the money to leverage these matters faster than we can manage. All they ask for is our permission and some political favors, and they promise to assemble a marketplace with ease. However, turning them down might upset the patrons of clan Icewind, causing them to think twice about aligning interests with the state for the future.
