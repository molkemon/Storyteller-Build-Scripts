"Oh my lord Lucian, if thy kingdom is the night princes' crown jewel, then north of the Trollbay are their onyx rings and pendants. Unforgettable dusks over the Ruin Cliffs rule. There my eternity I'd spend, given my chance."

Lucian's relaxed hands clapped at Tristan's silver tongue. Tristan was one of Lucian's most trusted merchant agents in Marlliande. Poet, sailor, and accountant. The boy had spent his undying youth well.

However, after his voyages, he came back... changed. Reflective and strangely absent-minded in public, yet eagerly euphoric out of the blue when caught alone. Among his immortal brothers, whenever he is spoken to, he does not stop talking about the lands of Marlliande.

Now, as he looks at his enthroned sire, he awaits the answer to his desires.

"Will you let me stay there, my lord?"
