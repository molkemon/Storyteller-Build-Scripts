The advice of elves is not uncommon across Cannor and has long since been accepted by our Cannorian peers, however, before the rise of Silver Shamanism, it was rare for an elf and a goblin to be seen in the same room together, let along to see an elf offering a goblin advice. Recently our lord has met such an elf, who is known often to advise our ruler in the proper course of action and some have even gone so far as to suggest this elf be offered a place at court.
