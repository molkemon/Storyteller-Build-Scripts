Assembled before their ruler were the lords of each of Vandiphas subject countries, who each swore eternal obedience to Vandipha in a long ceremony. At the end, the ruler stood and spoke. It has become clear that the Mengi cannot govern themselves, the ruler’s speech began. You were only truly united under the rule of a dragon, a higher being than you. For centuries you have fought each other in bloody warfare, betrayed each other, and refused peace and prosperity at every turn. But your suffering is at an end. Rejoice, for I am the very thing you have sought this long. A murmur broke out among the crowd as they realised what the ruler was saying. I am the true MezhiMezhi, the King of Kings, the Ruler of Rulers. I will bring prosperity to these lands, not in the name of Amilak, but in the name of the High Gods. You will not need to rule yourselves any longer. Weep in joy; the harimari, your superiors, have come to save you.
