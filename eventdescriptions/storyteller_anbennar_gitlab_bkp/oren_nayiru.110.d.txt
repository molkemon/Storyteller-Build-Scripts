There are countless myths and half-lost historical records detailing the Armour of Yodhan's capabilities on the battlefield. For centuries, they were thought by learned elves to be mere fiction. Unfortunately for the enemies of Elrandar Silverspite, they have been proven wrong time and time again. And unfortunately for the garrison of Silverspite Siege Fort, Elrandar Silverspite's incredible power has been levied against them.

Like a hurricane of flashing steel and dragon-scale plate, the Kamrayakval descended on the fort from above and effectively annihilated the garrison, cutting down all those who didn't surrender. His infamous twin blades rended flesh and steel alike.
