A missive was delivered to the Citadel of Dawn this morning listing the demands of the Aiztszuir. Principally, the resignation of Hiderion Tonanzuir to be replaced with Urion Salaszuir, not as lord of Azkare, but as king. They also demand the revocation of various policies of equality, establishing elves as ascendant above all others in Azkare, with Surael as the one true god of the state.
