Their lord sat awkwardly in their seat, hands holding each a wine glass, as the Pearl Vintage wine barons assembled themselves on either side. The hall was divided and each side was trying to convince the lord to their argument of a most raucious debate over which of their wines was better; Pearl Vintage, or Pearl Modern? The lord’s two glasses had been refilled several times now, so they were happy to let the debate carry on; but it did lead to a thought in their head that might cause even more problems in this rather acrid debate. Once it looked like the barons were about to come to blows, after a Pearl Vintage baron called a Pearl Modern vintner 'as rotten as your grapes', the lord finally intervened. They stilled the room with a shout; both from the volume, and from the radical, earthshaking words that came from their mouth: 'Why not both?' Some of the barons, stunned, simply nodded along with the lord’s words, while others stared daggers at them. Murmurs of 'over my dead body' were heard, and the lord’s servants ushered them out, knowing where this might lead. But not without grabbing several bottles of Pearl Modern and Vintage each.
