The patron’s description is as follows. The sun is high in the sky when our ruler settles in one of the private chambers of the royal palace. They ritually cleanse themselves with the water from a golden washbasin and changes into white silken robes. Silence settles in the room as our ruler kneels in prayer. They remain like this for an hour, before rising and lighting a brazier in the centre of the room. "My Lord, I pledge to you that I will."
