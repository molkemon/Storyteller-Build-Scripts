The Ravelian Society and our country have jointly revealed to the public at large the existence of the 'God Fragment', a precursor artifact that is said to house the last fragments of a precursor god, or a one true God, and is also said to be the source of all magic in the Prime Material Plane. Working with our former expedition lead, and now foremost scholar on the fragment, the elven archaeologist Torrieth, the Ravelians now see this fragment as their best hope in unraveling the secrets of this world.
