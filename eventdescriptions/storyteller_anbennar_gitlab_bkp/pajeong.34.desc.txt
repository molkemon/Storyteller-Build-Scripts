It is well-known that the Neoksamaji can access the wisdom of the ancestors and the spirits by allowing a Neoksari to possess them. This possession grants them powers such as prophetic visions and the ability to speak to the dead. One such Neoksamaji has come to our court, and proclaims that Sado and her Gumihos have demanded closer supervision of the Crane Throne. They believe that the Compact has not been properly upheld since the death of Sage King Jeong-Hun Gyo, and feel the need to appraise for themselves the goings-on at court.
