Varion finished the meeting with the Ibevari nobles and breathed a sign of relief. He has never been a fan of such diplomatic missions away from Moonhaven and especially with the Ibevari.  But the day was not over yet. He had still one meeting left, arguably the most important one. His uncle, the previous ruler of Varivar and the one many consider to be the true head of House Bluetongue. Now the mantle has seemingly passed on to Varion, a fact he hasn't accepted yet. But today is the day he meets his uncle. Varion is uncertain, he is nervous. But it needs to be done. They need to talk it out. After all, they are one family, right? . Several hours later Varion finally emerged from the room. On his face; a smile, and inside of him; confidence. Varion still understands the precatious situation of his dynasty, but instead of dread it now fills him with desire. The desire to make it count. The desire to lead moon elves as his grandfather once did.
