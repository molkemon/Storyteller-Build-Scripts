The goblins have a terrible reputation in Bulwar. "Thieves and pillagers, they are." "They bring only ruin." They have done all that, of course, and many of them are even proud of it. The Exodus was an invasion from Bahar’s point of view, with all the death and destruction that followed. But they are much more than that, capable of incredible feats, accepting of people of all kinds and races, open to every new thing. Yet the men and elves don't see those facets.  When it comes to orks, then, watch as the goblins act exactly as the stereotypes depict them. Considering their history, their fathers and grandfathers slaving away under the orks, it's understandable. Even so, seeing the ferocity and wickedness they show towards any ork is disturbing.  And now they're asking to administer the ork communities around Lake Jorkad.
