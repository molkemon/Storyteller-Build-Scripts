As more and more of the beings who call themselves mechanim spread across Insyaa and Kanolowele, it is the task of the faithful to determine the significance of their arrival to the Volcanic Truth.  On one hand, they represent a great opportunity. They are powered by the fire within their veins, and they bring great change by their very presence. Their stories of the Hinuilands where our people dare not go as well as their stories of days past could help our faith gain a greater understanding of the world. Their presence brings change, and as we know, change is the will of the Twin Gods.  On the other hand, we have encountered the mechanim prior to their current bout of activity: they have lain dormant in forgotten places for millennia. They speak of things far in the past, and if we were to change to accommodate them, would that not be undoing the change that has occurred since then? Perhaps their ways are a danger to the faith of the Volcanic Truth, and their revelations threaten to lead the faithful astray.  In either case, the answer to this question is not for us alone to decide. We must assemble the great synod of the Volcanic Truth to answer this question, for the good of both ourselves and of the mechanim.
