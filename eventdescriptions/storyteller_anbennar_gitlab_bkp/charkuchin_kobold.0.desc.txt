We have been buried in our citadel for centuries upon centuries. It has become more a tomb than a home, and our people yearn to expand outwards to the surface mountains we know hold great wonders and riches.  There is only one problem with the surface. We are not the apex predators there, and any who step foot outside the citadel without the appropriate preparation are soon swooped up as a meal by hinuibirds. Occasionally, the hinuibirds will drop a bone or two of their new meal as they fly off so the family has remains to bury, but this is the most we can hope for.  The outside world poses great dangers, but with the technology, weaponry, and numbers we now have, we might be able to protect ourselves from the hinuibirds well enough to explore our mountains; if we can only keep the supplies flowing.  There's an entire world out there to explore, and koboldkind isn't going to sit around in its citadels forever.
