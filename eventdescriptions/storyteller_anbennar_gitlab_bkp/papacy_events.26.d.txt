The king is asking the king for advice about the recent witch hunts and trials. The people of their country are blaming the famines, diseases and other unfortunate events on sorcery and mages who refuse to treat magic as a divine power. The sufferings have led to women, children and men being accused of worshiping the Devil and being a threat to the Ravelian world. Should they be banned, these witch hunts and trials, or should the people be allowed to hunt, pursue, lay blame and punish people that might be innocent or not of witchcraft?
