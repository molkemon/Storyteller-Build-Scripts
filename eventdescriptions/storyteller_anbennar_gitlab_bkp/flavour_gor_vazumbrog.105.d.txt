The empty roads outside Hehodovar echoed with Lorek's didactic voice, weaving tales of the first hold.  "And of course, on the 22000th Groundbreaker day, King Bruenor "the Dizzy" attempted to rig up whole sections of the hold to rotate; obvious nonsense, and the old dwarf was soon replaced by someone more."  The scholar's ramblings stopped abruptly. Thrain Stonefist, having successfully fought off napping on his feet, began the process of extricating the tedious man from the dangerous and nearinvisible slime he'd stumbled facefirst into.  "Looks like it was good to have a scholar along after all." mused the adventurer, as the company's many axeblows ripped the gelatinous cube apart, releasing from its grasp a variety of treasured objects and Lorek.
