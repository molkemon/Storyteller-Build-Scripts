The consort has been pacing the palace lately, their head hung low, only stopping sometimes to longingly gaze out the window. Life is quite different here, compared to their old country of origin, a nation some call our enemy nowadays. Where does that leave them, as a traitor to at least one side? They shake their head; such thoughts help no one, their place is here, in our realm, by the lord. When doubt besets them, they tend to seek out the young heir, as the innocent laughter and calm eyes of their child usually brings a smile to their face. However, not even this helps today. Perhaps it’s the weather?
