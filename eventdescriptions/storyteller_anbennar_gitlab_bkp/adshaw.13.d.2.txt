The bold prince Crovis has vanquished the Gray orks, seizing Bal Vroren and Mammoth Hall and expelling them from Vrorenmarch. He has been crowned King of the Vroren, Sovereign of the White Reach, and his gratitude towards his homeland is readily evident as he gave a speech encouraging solidarity between Adshaw and Vrorenmarch, advocating for a united future for the Alenic Reach.
