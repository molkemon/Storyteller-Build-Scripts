Our lord's sleep has been disturbed as of late, plagued with dark visions and otherworldly whispers. Even the attending healers could not dull the furor of the dark dreams, they could only sit by and wipe the sweat from their lord's brow. After a month of dark ringed eyes and fitful sleep, the source of the nightmares revealed itself. A statue, monstrous in size and appearance like a man half transformed into a beast, ridged with shards of black obsidian. The statue's mouth yawned wide, demanding blood from the sleeping lord.
