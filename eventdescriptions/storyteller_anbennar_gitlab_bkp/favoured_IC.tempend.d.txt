For five years, you’ve given yourself entirely to your Patron. The wrongs committed. The excesses indulged. The malice inflicted. All of it for you and your Patron.  Were the selfrighteous fools of Regent Court to look upon you and your black soul, they would gape in horror. And, perhaps, it is for that reason that you stand as an Elevate. For where others recoil to even consider what you have done, you simply smile.
