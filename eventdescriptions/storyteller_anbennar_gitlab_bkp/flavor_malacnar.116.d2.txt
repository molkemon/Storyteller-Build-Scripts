The human unsheathes the weapon on his belt: it's a stick with a hollow end. He points it at our lord, who seems unimpressed. The man retracts his finger, and a loud sound—followed by smoke—comes out. BAM! However, to the man's shock, our lord remains standing, unwounded. The bullet he had shot had been slowed down by a water shield conjured by our lord's magic, and simply bounced off their skin. The man scrambles to pull his rapier out, but our lord immediately closes in and kicks him onto the ground. It's over.
