By subjecting us to pay tribute to the government of our liege, we have also accepted that our border will remain open. A fact that our border population has come to abhor. The provinces directly adjacent to the raiders are never safe, as they can show up at any time to claim tribute in the form of living slaves. Men and women who are then sold in distant markets. Our people yearn for the day when we can throw off this yoke, but for as long as we fear their armies, we will have to endure these expeditions.
