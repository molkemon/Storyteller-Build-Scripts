In ages past we were but one tribe out of many, a small brotherhood where everyone knew everyone and our leader's face was as well known to each member of their warband as their own mother's.  As we look out over the bustling city of AzkaSur, we realize how much has changed. After the initial show of our conquest, the city has recovered well. The capital of our fledgling surface realm houses thousands of people from many different races, of whom only the highest officials know their ruler's face. Our realm changed a lot from the close knit group of old.  Maybe the time has come to change ourselves a bit as well. The people of Bulwar do not follow mere chieftains; they are used to following Akals and Phoenix Kings. The time has come to take a royal title of our own; one that reflects our power and right to rule over these lands.
