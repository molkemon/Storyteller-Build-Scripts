Kozen felt oddly sentimental reading the records, even though he knew those sentiments were wasted on the golems he was pitying. Nevertheless, reading the tale of the steadfast constructs of old Gor Vazumbrog being turned to so much slag by the caustic breath of the purple dragon that was his home's greatest enemy, he felt a twinge. The custodian golem that kept the streets clean, the manufactory golem that kept his forge lit, the very titanium golems that watched his back on this delve into the vaults… none of them could feel grief for their lost kin, so Kozen would feel it for them.
