This was never even a fight.  From the moment the battle began, the outcome was already decided. Xazor descends upon the host with vast, unrelenting force.  Our line breaks immediately and formations collapse in the darkness as wings flood the chamber, blotting out what little light remains. Orders are shouted and then lost, swallowed by echoes that mock them back in familiar voices.  Our steel finds flesh, but not enough. The swarm tears through our host, dragging warriors deeper into the cave, never to be seen again. Our troops are disorganised and lines broken. One by one, the lights go out.  Torches fall, voices fade, and the echoes grow quiet.  By the time silence returns to the pit, nothing remains of the hunt but scattered bodies and broken weapons.
