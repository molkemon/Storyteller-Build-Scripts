Ovdal Asra was once one of the most influential holds in AulDwarov, and contributed a massive gem of pink agate to the Dwarovkron, which sat upon the brow of the High King. This is all that is known about Ovdal Asra, though, for it fell a long time ago, and has not been rediscovered (except for a brief moment in 1410, by Fognir Asra). It lives on, however, in the form of the Agate of Asra, which sat in the vaults of the Asra Bank until recently, when it was conveyed home to the Serpentspine. And now it belongs to us.
