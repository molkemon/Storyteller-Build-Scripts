There is debate among our ruling elite over whether to embrace the change or hold on to our own martial traditions. While our people have embraced Taychendi customs and culture, our elites have learned much from the warlords of the east, one area in which Zaernmaera has remained staunchly Chendhyan is in our military. The backbone of our strength, the tribes have invested heavily in traditional feline cavalry, supported by lightly armored Chendhyan warriors on foot. However, as we expand our borders and control and become a more Taychendi empire, the calls to adopt the Taychendi military system, with strong regional commanders and a delicate balance between cavalry, heavy infantry, and light infantry, have become stronger each year. While the Taychendi are undoubtedly much more open to outside military innovations, many say sticking with the tools that won us the empire is the only way to keep it. Ultimately, the choice is yours.
