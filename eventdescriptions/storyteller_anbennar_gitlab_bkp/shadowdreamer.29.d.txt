With preparations for the expedition already underway, the attention now must turn to the question of leadership. The descent into the pit will not be easy, the caverns are treacherous, and the beast is said to be powerful beyond anything we have faced before. Whoever leads this expedition will have to be ready to face the Great Bat head on.  Among our great host several have already stepped forward. Some within our nation suggest a goblin chieftain would be ideal for the role, with their unrivalled skills in navigation of the sprawling caverns. Others suggest a troll warlord would be better suited, and with their natural strength and towering stance it is not difficult to understand why.  However, many also argue that this role is far too important to entrust to any other than the great Dream Sage himself. If this creature truly harbors the power of the Drahm'otash, then the one to slay it must be the chosen of the Dream.  The choice must be made soon. Who would be best suited for the hunt?
