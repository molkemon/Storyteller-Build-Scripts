The Monasticists have always despised Orenvalyam's wars of expansion. They see them as a pointless waste of life, needless in the face of Nayiru's eventual true return, when he, by his own overwhelming power, will sweep aside the enemies of Dawn and reshape the world. Still, even their hill-top monasteries have their obligations, as do Monasticists who live outside them. But with the Kamrayakval's wars growing increasingly destructive, these too are beginning to shirk their duties. Unless we crack down, we are likely to lose a lot of potential soldiers.
