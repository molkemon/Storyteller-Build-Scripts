Traveler, in current times, the great Rain Road Ozhxummoton was dedicated in the name of our lord. As the Sorrow brings trade to Kheterata, so too does the Rain Road bring trade from the merchant kingdoms to Konolkhatep. As the Sorrow connects Konolkhatep to the Divenhal, so too does the Rain Road connect Konolkhatep to the Gulf of Rahen. As you walk along the seam of continents along the Rain Road, so too do all of the Khet walk the balance between mortal and god. As this road stands forever, so too shall Konolkhatep. 

Inscription by the side of the Rain Road in Bakrast Khaj
