As we've conquered and expanded our ports in the realm of East Sarhal, we've always had one objective in mind: control of the markets for coffee and dye. Still, even we couldn't have predicted the effect we'd have on the lives of the people living here. The Dakinshi and Tanizu lands have seen an economic shift, now more focused on meeting the demands of our traders than making goods for their own consumption. With all this, it seems we really have no need for land in Sarhal beyond our ports.
