With the boom of trade our continent has felt over the last decades, many governments, both in Alecandi and Nékhei, have been holding long and thorough debates regarding customs and the handling of goods from abroad. Some, led by the example of Ameion, lean towards isolationism, closing the doors to further trade benefits for everyone. It is our duty, as hardy seamen, as traders through and through, as defenders of our freedom, to keep the doors open to the world. Pursuing and ensuring that free trade policies are followed around the continent is of utmost importance. Riches from far beyond the eye can see await us.

We are now in a position of strength, and the crossroads of destiny are just in front of us. The preferred opinion of our rulers is to make use of this newfound strength. However, we can always let every other ruler of the region choose their own path without our... friendly recommendations.
