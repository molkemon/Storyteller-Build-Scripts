After two weeks without word from our lord, a search party was formed to look for them. It took a week of combing through the woods the wendigo has been plaguing to find the refined set of armor our lord was wearing when they set off, now battered and soaked in blood. It was found many meters from their bones, picked clean by the wendigo. The only thing clearly identifying the body as specifically theirs was the gilded and gemstudded ring found among the finger bones bearing the bloodsplattered lettering reading their dynasty. The citizens feel no sympathy for their previous lord, if anything they feel resentment, for their supposedly wise leader went off on a suicide mission which ended predictably poorly, leaving the government in a state of disarray.
