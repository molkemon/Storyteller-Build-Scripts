With the High Temple finally standing tall, restored to its previous radiating glory, like aeons before, it started to attract the masses across Haless, Harimari, even dwarves and kobolds flocking towards the Temple Complex in search of inspiration and faith. This large influx of visitors quickly brought the attention of the Merchant class and traders too, as culture and religion were exchanged and studied, along with plenty of exotic trade goods. So a mayor came forth asking the lord to invest some funds into the local province so they may set up a Trade Center there.
