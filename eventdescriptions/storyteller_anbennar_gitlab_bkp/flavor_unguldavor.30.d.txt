When anyone thinks of orks, they think of burly warriors who charge with no regard to the frontlines with no thought of his own. While we are strong warriors, there is more to the orkish spirit than that. We are also a traditional people. We are not stupid, despite claims to the contrary. Our traditional reluctance towards most magic and technology was more about worrying about misuse and destroying our way of life. And as could be seen in the rest of Escann, that worry was not misfounded.  Escann was filled with witchkings. From Nurcestir to Esthil to Wyvernheart, witchkings subjugated their populaces to torment and cruelty. In their hunger for power, they banned independent magic, fearing its threat to their power.  In Unguldavor, however, we feared no such magic. Alchemists, inventors, and other mages flooded our realm to experiment in our labs and teach in our universities. Along with this, a new field was burgeoning. Artificery. Combining magic and science, several alchemists have begun practicing artificery, making inventions that could turn the tide of war.  Already, our universities have become the best rounded education in Escann and lives have been improved. And without the tyranny of witchkings, we have nothing to fear from artificers rising up.
