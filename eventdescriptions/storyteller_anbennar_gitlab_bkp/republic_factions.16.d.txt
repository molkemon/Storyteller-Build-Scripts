In our realm, business and politics have been irreversibly intertwined since time immemorial. Sometimes, we may be faced with deals that benefit many parties but not the republic itself. When their ruler is one of those parties, the situation becomes all the more precarious. A good business agreement builds stronger relations with key merchants, something we could certainly use. We can avoid alienating these partners if we are willing to sacrifice some of our integrity as a representative of the republic.
