Our ruler has summoned the alchemists of the nation for a very important task. While mana potions have been known to all races that have ever wielded magic and encountered Dames tear, the process of their creation has always been a bit crude; and quite unpleasant to consume: nothing more than smashed together bits of the magical ore left to soak in water or other liquids. They’ve been tasked with building upon these old recipes, and bring mana potions into a new age of potionmaking. To this end, we have two main avenues of innovation on which to focus: improve our treatment of the base ingredient, or include additional ingredients to help its potency and consumability.
