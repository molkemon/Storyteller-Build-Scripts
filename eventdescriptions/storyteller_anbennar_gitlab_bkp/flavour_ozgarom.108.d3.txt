That damnable Bramash will not cease to vex us! Unsatisfied by laying waste to our holdings in Endralliande, he now seeks to undermine our legitimate rule in Soruin. We had hoped at first that he would disappear into the jungle and get himself eaten by something, but his maroon settlements have established themselves in the jungle highlands of Soruin and begun issuing the same sort of propagandist nonsense as they did during his last campaign. They begin to speak of "Ozgarom" - the realm of the orcish free...

The result? Our Soruinic labourers begin to rumble in discontent, passing the Cabinet of Freedom's missives among themselves as they glower at their betters. Orcs from across Aelantir have begun to travel to Soruinic ports, dreaming of the free orcish society in the jungle. The natives and the maroons even begin to conspire, naming one another kin. We will not be caught off guard again; something is brewing here. If we do not prepare now, we will suffer the same calamity we did in Endralliande!
