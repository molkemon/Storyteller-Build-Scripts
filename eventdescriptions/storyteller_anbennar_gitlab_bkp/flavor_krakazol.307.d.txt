The next batch of drinks has been served. Dwarves guzzle ale like they’re being paid for it, jeering at one another to keep going. The hills are filled with laughter, song, and the sound of all here and there, and it is altogether quite the merry and lively occasion. The lord sits on his throne with a flagon of the purest ale. It is his duty, no, his honour to drink with his people, and drunk he shall be. The lord feels all the eyes of the great hall aimed at him, speculating whether or not he will lie through it.
