In hobgoblin society, all must prove their worth. To ensure everyone contributes to the best of their ability, their skills will be tested and their competencies assessed. A bureau called the Shokaitei will determine the best career path for each individual. These roles will be open to all, regardless of origin or ancestry, and any path can be suggested and awarded if candidates prove they possess the required skills or knowledge. If individuals underperform, the Shokaitei can transfer them to a more suitable position. In this way, everyone will have a place where they can work best, serving society with their unique gifts.
