For the first time since the Night of Stars, when the pyres were lit across Konolkhatep, those nobles of the Sorrow have begun to realise the extent of the problems plaguing the nation. Soldiers have not been able to land in Akasik, for the seas have been teeming with corsairs from the rebellious Akasi gnolls. Goods have not been reaching the port in Koroshesh, only raid after raid after raid of kultist gnolls, burning ports to the ground and taking krah. It took Bulwari dates not appearing at their banquets for the nobles to finally recognise the severity of the uprising.
