The dealers, plantation owners, lab workers and smugglers of Demon's Blood have started to ramp up production and distribution and are quickly making a name for themselves throughout Mykx and even some foreign ports in Aelantier. Blood Bringers is what the people call them. These Blood Bringers have petitioned the government to secure valuable export ports in Noruin, so excess produce can be safely sold to foreigners.
