The delegations stand together in ceremonial garb, but before our lord can even call the first meeting, a senior diplomat breaks ranks, asking if the Cestirmarker politicians have lost their minds. He claims to speak for all the sovereignist faction, which always considered the current state of the Concord sufficient, and strongly rejects the new reforms. A breaking point has been reached, as it is clear two diverging paths are being followed in the realm of Trollsbay, and he strongly implied these two paths could not coexist. Other diplomats intervened to call out those arguments as disingenuous, and the dispute quickly escalated until the sovereignist representatives stormed out. As the lord looks over those who are left, it is clear one correct argument was made: two diverging paths are being followed in the realm of Trollsbay.
