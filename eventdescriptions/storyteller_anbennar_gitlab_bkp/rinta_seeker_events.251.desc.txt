The viscus of the damned is currently being tested by a few mages outside any major population area. After various tests and gimmicks, they wish to separate the hammer and the nail for extended solo tests to run deeper analyses on each part. They claim this will allow for double the amount of testing.

Others in the council argue that this conservative approach will be ineffective. They believe the hammer and nail are non-functional without each other; their magic only works when they are together. The closer they get, the more potent their resonance becomes. When they touch, their magic completes.
