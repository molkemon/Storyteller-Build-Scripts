With our complete control over the heartlands of the old Kingdom of Carneter, we stand in a position to revive the title and have our lord crowned as king. The legacy of one of Lencenors great kingdoms will help guide Eilisin into the future with great respect across Cannor. However, another more extreme path presents itself too: embracing the Carnetori identity completely, declaring our nation the Kingdom of Carneter first. We would be abandoning any claims to the Kingdom of Lorent that Eilisin has, and will no doubt draw ire from the Empire for laying claim to more of Anbennars western lands. What will the future of Eilisin be?
