A strange new kobold merchant has started a shipping business and mercantile concern headquartered in our capital, and is travelling from place to place to rapidly establish it as a commercial power. He cuts quite a different figure from his fellows; where most of the successful traders of the realm garb themselves in fine cloths and dyes, the only thing he has ever been seen to wear is a hooded cloak no different than any that could be bought in the markets where he plies his wares.  He almost appears as more of a holy man than a merchant. By way of explaining this disparity between his manner and his vocation, the kobold merely says that markets create connection, connection creates synthesis, and synthesis allows for growth. These ideas of his and his startling commercial success have attracted a flock of followers from the young merchants of our capital, and he is using some of his wealth to construct a school of trade. Were we to donate to this endeavor, we might be able to recruit him to handle some of the court’s affairs of trade as well.
