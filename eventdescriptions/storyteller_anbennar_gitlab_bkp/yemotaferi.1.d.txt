After Tamrat the Terrible plunged our nation into a devastating war; visiting death and destruction on the people and the land; a motley crew picked up the pieces. RainPriests, Deathsworn, and scholars banded together to save the remnants of our realm. Through hard work and a humble attitude, this faction; known to the people as the Swamp Wardens; has saved Yemotaferi from ruin.  Still, the labours are not yet at an end, the realm far from rebuilt. We must mend our broken bonds with the Tanizu, oppose the warmongering shadowtouched king of Simegasa, and rally fellow Mengi realms to stand against the trollish horde. The Swamp Wardens must form a true and stable government if Yemotaferi is to prosper. A council of our finest, representing the varying groups comprising the faction, shall take the lead under the guidance of an Elder Warden, who will serve as sovereign of our people.
