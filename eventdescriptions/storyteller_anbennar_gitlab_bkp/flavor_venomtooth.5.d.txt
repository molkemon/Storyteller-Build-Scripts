After many years, our vengeance is finally complete. Tencfor burns around our lord as the soldiers sack the settlement. In shackles kneeling in front of our lord is Azukor, son of Brasul. Azukor has a solemn look in his eyes as he raised his gaze from the ground. "You have corrupted Korgus' teachings," Azukor, son of Brasul, said as he looked up to our lord. "Dookan allows all to join his army. To Dookan, it doesn't matter if someone is an ork, goblin, or human. We all have an inner strength and the spirit to be warriors for justice. But of course you wouldn't understand that. You let hatred blind you. You believe killing us true believers to be strength." Our lord glared at Azukor Bonecarver and slapped him. "You fool. Look around you. Whatever nonsense belief you have tied to Dookan, you have forgotten what it means to be an ork. Now everyone has to die because of your weakness. It would have been better if you died with your father at Rottenstep." Azukor smiled even as a bruise formed on his cheek. He chuckled. "Perhaps it would have been. But I will not pretend that I regret what I have done. Strike me down in your anger and I will reincarnate. The warrior soul never truly dies." Our lord raised the axe.
