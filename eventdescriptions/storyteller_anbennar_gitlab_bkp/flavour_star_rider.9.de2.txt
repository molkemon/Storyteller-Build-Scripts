This was foreseen. It was fated to happen. The happiest day in all of centaur history. That dreaded day, which the ruler was once looking forward to. A day brought about by the most egregious misinterpretation of the faith to date. Were those speeches of my predecessor genuine? Or just an elaborate hoax to goad our people towards a brighter future? Is such a thing even justifiable? I have no choice but to attend the conclusion. I wish I didn’t. What matters is the conclave. The codification of faith, the promise of every single shaman’s voice being allowed to speak in this assembly. A chance to mend the sects of the plains for the first and final time. And when the time comes, and they try to crown me as caehn of the plains, as future Storm King ruling over centaurkind? I will refuse. We have paved the way towards the present, but only the shamans can lead us. This would always happen. My choice regarding who will rule us is set.
