Many of our neighbors have never seen a harpy outside of periodic raids on their settlements, and know very little of us outside of our fearsome reputation. Perhaps this is why we have received the news from the border that we have.  A harpy was flying on her way back to her nest with her loving husband in her arms when she was accosted by an angry mob seeking to "save" the man from his spouse. They shot her down, and though her spouse managed to save himself from a severe fall, the harpy herself is in critical condition and her survival is far from certain. The man has come to our lord in a bitter rage to see if anything might be done as retribution against the attackers.
