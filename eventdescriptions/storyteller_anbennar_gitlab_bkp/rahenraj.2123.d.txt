There are many rumors that surround the fact our vizier lacks a wife: some speak of past scandal, others of a tragic widowhood, yet others say he is inspired by the eunuchs administrative capabilities. It would not normally be worthy of our ruler’s dignity to spare such a fact even a passing thought, let alone involve them in such affairs, except one of our daughters has recently come of age, with no engagement yet planned. Perhaps we should propose a marriage to the vizier, and bind their loyalty by ties of blood?
