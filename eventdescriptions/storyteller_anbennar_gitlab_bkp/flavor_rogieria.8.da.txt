The ruler faced a dilemma. The Damerian adventurers, one of the pillars of Rogierias power, hoped to gain land in the newly conquered lands of Adenica. Many even sent messages home, inviting their families to join them. This is perfect, the ruler thought, since they were loyal to his family. On the other hand, many Adenners, both returning refugees and those who fought alongside the Sons of Dameria, hoped to reclaim the land of their ancestors. The Silmuna do not forget those who proved faithful, but the claims of the Damerians and Adenners are overlapped. The ruler, asked the Dame and the Young Owl for wisdom, since the decision he was going to make would mark the future of his kingdom, of his family’s legacy.
