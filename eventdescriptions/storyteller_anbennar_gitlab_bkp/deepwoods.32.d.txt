Throughout the war of the Black Trees, Bron Blackmoon has proven himself to be an ork amongst orks, a champion of Dookan, and a hero of our people. Magically obliterating the socalled ‘Emerald orks’ by the dozen, with never a hint of fear in his eyes, only the fury of an ork, and the power of Dookan. As well as his prowess on the battlefield, he has proven himself to be a skilled bureaucrat, managing our resources throughout the war, and acting as an excellent commander amongst the troops. The question now rises; with the war over, what role shall Bron fulfill?
