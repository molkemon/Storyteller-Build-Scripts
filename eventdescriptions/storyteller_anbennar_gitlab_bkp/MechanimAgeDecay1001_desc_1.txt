The life of a leader is an active one; every day I must meet with the various members of our country to discuss the affairs of rule. Every day this task becomes more demanding; my legs are weakening quickly and this has greatly affected my ability to maintain my stature and respect as a ruler as I struggle to be at the places I must in time. Should I seek to repair them, or should I leave my position as a leader to lessen the stress put onto them?
