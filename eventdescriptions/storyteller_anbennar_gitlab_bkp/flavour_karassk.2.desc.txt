The Eternal Empire, prosperous as it is, has a bit of a debt problem. Over the years there’s been a policy of deficit spending to grow the economy, but some of the creditors are getting a tad impatient. Some worrywarts have dubbed it ‘reckless spending’ or an ‘economic death spiral’, but do they truly think the Eternal Empire is like some gambler helpless when the debts come due? They forget we can always send a few soldiers to remind these people of their loyalty. Still, we did recently subdue some rebellious cities; perhaps they can be ‘taxed’ to make up for their rebellion. The profits can go towards getting those merchants to finally cease their chattering.
