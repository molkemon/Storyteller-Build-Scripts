When you pull open the door to the illusion room, you are greeted warmly by the illusion group. This is surprising, because you've been so busy with your duties as lord that you've hardly had time to practice for your lead role. And yet the illusion group rushes up to you, as he did before. "My Grace. I thought you had left to freshen up. Back so soon?" When you give him a look of confusion, he smiles charitably. "Whatever do you mean? You have been here every evening, rehearsing your lines and preparing your costume. You have developed wonderfully. Come, see the vestments you handsewed." He leads you over to a corner of the illusion room where a bejeweled vest and fashionable skirt have been placed over a manikin exactly your size. "So as you can see," the illusion group concludes, "you've really been a wonderful partner. I couldn't pull this off without you."
