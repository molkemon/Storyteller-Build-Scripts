Now that the monasteries have been active for a considerable period of time, we have collected a staggering amount of information on how spiritfire rejuvenates the land. By acting as a sort of "pump" for chi and mana in the environment, spiritfire is able to distribute these energies throughout the terrain, making it considerably more habitable as barren desert lands begin to turn lush and fertile. This is the basic concept of how the temples function, but one layer deeper there exists a more obscure mystery; what about the people? Many people live in these now rejuvenated areas of the realm. They have been exposed to the heightened levels of latent mana for a considerable period of time, and surely now find themselves with more energy than previously seen. This phenomenon could serve as an opportunity to us, if we apply it in the right ways to our more daunting tasks. What should we do to get the most from our invigorated citizens?
