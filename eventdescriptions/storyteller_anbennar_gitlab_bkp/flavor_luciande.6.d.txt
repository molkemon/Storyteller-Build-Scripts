The shrieker is one of the most feared dangers of the Cursewoods because you never see it until it’s too late. It is a little mushroom, dark brown in color, with no distinguishing features. But when you step too close, the bulb splits open and emits a terrible shriek. You clutch your ears and try to flee, but you realize the shriekers are all around you. As you run through the forest, the sound gets no quieter. In fact, it gets even louder, consuming your mind until you are driven mad. A localized epidemic of insanity allowed us to pinpoint a shrieker patch, and with Orwick’s magical protection, our agents easily recovered some samples.
