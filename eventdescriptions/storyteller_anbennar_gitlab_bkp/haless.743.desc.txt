No one has seen his figure, but everyone keeps looking over their shoulder. The night lingers, the lamps still glow, yet hearts are already shaken. Someone has fallen inside the realm—not on the battlefield, nor in the streets, but in the very place thought safest. He speaks no words, yet delivers a message; carries no weapon, yet takes lives. A guard? A general? A minister? No one knows who will be next. They say he is an assassin from Yanshen—silent and precise, always striking at the crucial moment. No one sees his shadow, yet someone always pays the price. Now, all we can do is await the next silent curtain to fall.
