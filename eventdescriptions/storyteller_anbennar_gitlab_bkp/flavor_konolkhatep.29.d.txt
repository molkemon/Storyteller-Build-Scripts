O Horutep, Lord of Light.  What is the Sun? The followers of the Old Sun Cult say that the Sun is a god longdead. The followers of the New Sun Cult say that the Sun is a conqueror longvanquished. The followers of the Jadd say the Sun is everything and nothing at the same time.  For untold centuries, the Sun worshippers held grand religious councils and splintered into microscopic sects. They could not resolve their differences because the truth was not available to them beyond the preconceptions they harbored, and their attempts to pretend otherwise led to nothing but misery.  We of the Sorrow know this truth: the Sun is you, and you will lead us into a new dawn.
