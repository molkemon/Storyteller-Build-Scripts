With the fighting spirit of the Deepwoods orks finally broken, it is now time to use their ferocity for more productive purposes. Slave caravans have assembled in the groves under our control and begun their long march towards our heartland, with its insatiable hunger for new slave labor.  Once stripped of the cover of the trees that have sheltered them from retribution far too long, they will learn what true role in life destiny has decreed for them: penance, for the crimes committed by their ancestors. It is truly fortuitous that our need for orkish slaves is almost as great as their need for atonement.
