The Family has blessed us. The lord has recovered from his wounds. They are still bedridden, but the bleeding has stopped and the weakness has seemingly dissipated. As soon as they awoke, speculation began as to those behind the attack. The lord thinks that magic may have been responsible for both his weakness and the end of the assassin, or even the death of his mother. They have vowed that the assassins, all who supported them, and all who harbored them will pay the ultimate price. Otherwise, they may strike again.
