The Praisegranting Ceremony is the most cherished tradition of the Tuathak, a chance to show our appreciation for those worthy of respect. This year, the Gloam Knight approached our court just as we got ready for the celebrations. This fortunate occasion led to the decision that this year's Praisegranting Ceremony be held in honor of the Gloam Knight himself, for who could be more worthy of praise? It is now time for the lord to show all their vassals how to show proper deference for their betters.
