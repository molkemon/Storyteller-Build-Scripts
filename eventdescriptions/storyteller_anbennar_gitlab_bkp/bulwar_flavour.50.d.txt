Through careful study of the sacred calendar of solar rites and devout contemplation, the temple priests have concluded that the time has come once more to perform a Grand Festival of the Sun. A multitude of priests, wise men and women, holy warriors and members of the holy orders in our realm have gathered alongside a great mass of pilgrims in the capital to rejoice in shared devotion to Surakel. All assembled are united in their gratitude for His unceasing care for His children, His bounteous gifts to the faithful and His eternal vigilance against the Darkness that threatens to consume all.
