"And there you have it. Should be most of them, or at least enough so that the dastardly Masquarade of theirs needs not be spoken of in the coming millennia." Our lord grinned from ear to ear as he spoke, utterly spoiling any attempt of him to feign humility. The all-too-common sounds of shutting doors and windows, presumably at the sight of a goblin in full plate riding a dog, did not manage to impair the celebratory mood. The Raven Banner delegate was, understandably, baffled. Their band's purpose fulfilled, Corvuria was now free of vampires for the foreseeable future, and all he had to sacrifice for it was his dignity. The merry band of short, green, and delusional dog riders managed what no man before them could, and the world was better for it. The reality of it all still hadn't quite sunk in when their leader spoke again:

"Let us then proceed to the matter of payment. When you are instituted on the throne of this radiantly vampire-free realm, all the knights of our domain shall be officially named crusaders in your name, yes?"

"That is correct, Sir Knight."

The specifics of who gets to be a crusader were the only lever he could still pull, Adean bless their ignorance.

"Splendid, splendid. And as for the more worldly rewards, let's see..."
