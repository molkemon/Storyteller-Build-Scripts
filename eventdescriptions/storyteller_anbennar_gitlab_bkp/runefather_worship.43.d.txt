With our departure from the Serpentdepths, we have come across a new enemy, that apparently destroyed the empire that had once betrayed us – orks. These barbarous brutes ravaged and pillaged their way across the Dwarovar until the recent Greentide thinned their numbers. With our recent conquests, we subjugated a significant population of these tremendous, if illdisciplined, warriors. These new slaves could provide a better source of blood for our Runes compared to the scrawny goblins of the deep. The Amlruzskappa are eager to try – yet some, led by members of the Darkstone Clan, fear that the orks' physical strength could result in a stronger resistance than the Goblins.
