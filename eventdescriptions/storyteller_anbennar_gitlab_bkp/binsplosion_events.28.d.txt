As expected, the Dunesole Ogres of Sakul have begrudgingly accepted our demands of their subjugation, and already the first tribute caravans arrive at our capital, filled with the plundered riches of the former Binwarji heartland. If even these giants willingly bend the knee when faced with our might, how could the rest of Fangaula possibly resist us? The Zuultanate will rule every corner of the Land of Authority.
