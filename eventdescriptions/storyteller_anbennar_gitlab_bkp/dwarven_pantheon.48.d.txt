Appearing fully divine one day, Lorgram Deathkeeper's arrival heralded the end of the Era of Forging alongside his sister, Lorgram. When he appeared, he began to ensure that the souls of the dead were taken into Dagrin's halls and organised according to their skills and virtues in life. Here, in the deeper reaches, he acts as the keeper and custodian, ensuring that the dead are rewarded or punished accordingly.
