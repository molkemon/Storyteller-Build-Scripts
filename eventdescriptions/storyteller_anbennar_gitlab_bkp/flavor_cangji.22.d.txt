Word has come from our domain to the south, as fields burn and roads are barred. The knights of Hakuukulga, who have languished under the yoke of their vile Oni masters, have noticed our growing strength and now seek to put an end to our realm just as we plan to re-enter the wider world. They seek to strike us when they think we're weak, and snuff out our light before we eclipse them in our radiance. We will not tolerate those who wish to see us dead; let them see that we still possess teeth.
