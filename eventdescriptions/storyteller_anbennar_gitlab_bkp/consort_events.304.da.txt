Combined with a strong moral compass, the consort has always had a sense for order. Decisions made by them are trusted decisions; be it about political issues or during friendly conversation, the consort speaks their mind eloquently, something that awarded them some admiration even during their early days in their homeland. Most importantly however, the words of the consort carry a truthfulness to them, a matter making even the most stubborn members of court heed any advice offered from them.
