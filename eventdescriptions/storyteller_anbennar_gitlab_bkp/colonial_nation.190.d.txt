It has been said, among those who are willing to listen, that the official appointed by their ruler has been absent of his duties for a long time. He has been seen taking part in the greatest of feasts and balls, here in our city, quite far from those he aims to govern. Of course, it is easier to revolt against the ruler if the ruler is not there, so news about a dangerous uprising in a province have finally reached our court. When confronted with these facts, he has been heard saying: "Those mosquitoes were eating me alive."
