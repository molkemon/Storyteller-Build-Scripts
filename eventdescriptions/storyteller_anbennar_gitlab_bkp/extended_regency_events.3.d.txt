After the unsuccessful attempt to stop our ruler from worsening our nation's fate, the paranoia that had surrounded them has only proved to be true, thus causing even a greater harm to their mental health. Having already interrogated the closest members of the council, our ruler has started to enter their rooms unannounced, accompanied by some royal guards and a severe tone to their voice: "This must be done," is the only reply one can get from them while their belongings are being checked.
