The long process of bringing Khetism to the gnolls of Sarhal has begun in earnest. Priests and preachers laden in the riches of the Khet have been sent to each corner of our growing empire to spread the teachings of Elikhet. We are fully aware that this will take time. We will face resistance. The old traditions of Kvangahga and the pull of powers promised by the Xhazobkult are strong. We know this all too well, as we too followed that path not so long ago.
