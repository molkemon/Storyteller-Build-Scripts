The Amadians held their Conclave at Cara Lafquén. Once, this had been the heart of the old Onisqado Federation, which had once cradled Lake Chakantu in prosperous harmony. But the Onisqado Federation had fallen, torn apart when the whims of Wild Magic coincided with unwise elves, who harnessed it to fight their conflicts and doomed themselves in the process. The lords of the realm debated, and after each group had given their argument, the Conclave voted and made its choice.
