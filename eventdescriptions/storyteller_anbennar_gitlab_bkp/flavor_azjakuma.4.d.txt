It is time for the meeting of the Tagharoghi to decide upon a successor. The leaders of the great Shirgrii have all gathered in Shinukhorchi to present their candidate to the lord, in hopes they’ll be chosen as the Rashokudei, destined to become the next sovereign over all of Azjakuma. As is customary, they’ve also brought gifts in the form of knowledge or gold from their temple. All that is left now is for our lord to choose an apprentice.
