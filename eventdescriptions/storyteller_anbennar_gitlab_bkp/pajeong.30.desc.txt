The temple in Pajeong has fallen into disrepair, to the point that it might as well not exist. Taxes that should have been used for its upkeep have been diverted to other purposes. The Neoksamaji have come with a request from the spirits that inhabit it, angered over the dilapidated state of their place of veneration. With glowing eyes, they demand the funds and laborers to not only restore the temple but to elevate it to an even grander state. A truly impressive temple for the spirits appears to be the price if we want them to continue to abide by the Compact.
