You dodge another arrow as you pack in the grapeshot, light the fuse and duck behind the ship's railing with Tsagon, flashing him a toothy grin. After about 3 seconds… BOOM. The cannon bucks as it fires the payload, and you look over to see that it has decimated the forces on the deck preparing to fight the oncoming gnolls, though some of the more frenzied feetfirst into action types got hit in the blast too. The first mate nods at you as he grabs a grappling hook and swings onto the ship, heading straight for the mercantile ship’s captain, yelling orders from the quarterdeck. Quickly, the remaining sailors surrender, and the victorious howls of the Mykx crew reverberate throughout the night, yours and Tsagon’s joining them. While you won’t gain the vast majority of the loot not being on the boat itself, the first mate does give you more than a fair share considering your contribution to the battle.
