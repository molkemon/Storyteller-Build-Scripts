It started with giggling. At first, only we noticed it, but over time, the hag heard it too, and she became afraid. She started pacing and mumbling, shouting threats in some unknown language which slowly turned into screeching. and then she started to change. White feathers started to sprout from under her robe, creeping up her skin, enveloping her, shrinking her. until only a goose emerged, still screeching angrily, waddling out into the outside world. All the surviving victims of the hag from our lands tell the same unlikely story, which we have no choice but to accept as true.
