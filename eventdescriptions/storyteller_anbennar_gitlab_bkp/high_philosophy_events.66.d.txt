When the High Gods came to this land and remade it in their image, they raised high the great temples that now form the foundation of our faith. Here they inscribed their deeds and lessons for all who came after, a saga filled with wisdom for those willing to learn. And so, as we have studied these great works of art and dissected the meaning left in them, we too have grown wise, and in our wisdom returned the favour.  Today the world is at peace, the temples are in order, and from the Paravimvata to the sea the High Gods' relics are honored appropriately. Truly this is the meaning of Visvatma – the atma of the world itself in balance. Truly this is the way of the gods.
