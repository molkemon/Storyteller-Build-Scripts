Great strides forward have been made in the last few months in regards to the efficacy and sustainability of our enhanced crops and fertilizers. In particular, we have been able to mostly negate the negative effects that usually arise from simple mages repeatedly using spells to achieve similar results. Research assistant Eru has been of particular aid and note in recent weeks, especially with keeping up morale through the melodies she keeps on humming and then getting stuck in the heads of our crews for hours. One she claims the most recent bunch of sprouts taught her, before dismissing that claim as but a jest.

The continued deciphering of relics from the Gene Pillars, while still ongoing and far from complete, has also been of great help in getting us this far. Yet we remain uncertain of the meaning of the word "autospontaneoheterotrophism" we have seen mentioned in precursor items and records relating to similar fields of study. But, this too shall be unveiled after we manage to get into the more overgrown sections of our current relic sites.

While colleagues outside of our divisions are requesting samples for larger field studies, our internal division is of the opinion that this bounty should not be withheld from our citizens and trading partners. Soon all shall sing praises to the quality of our country's produce.
