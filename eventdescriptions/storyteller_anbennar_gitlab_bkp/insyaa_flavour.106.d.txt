Insyaa is a strange land, but just as it has terrors, it has wonders beyond compare in its mangrove forests and hinui wilds. One of these wonders that we have found are the mechanims—they have endured since the days of the Precursors and yet still function. The workers of our realm work hard, but even the hardiest cannot work for millennia.

The artificers of our realm have determined how the circuitry of the mechanims functions, and now we can use that technology to jump-start not just our colonies on Insyaa, but development in our homeland as well. Via a small and easily producible chip, the durability of our mechanim workers can be pushed beyond their natural limits. Unimaginable pain and addictive pleasure both might suffice to get the mechanims working at previously unheard-of paces by themselves, but when the two are combined, one mechanim can equal five or even ten regular workers in both speed and efficiency.

Of all the wonders of Insyaa, a worker that never stops working might just be the finest of them all.
