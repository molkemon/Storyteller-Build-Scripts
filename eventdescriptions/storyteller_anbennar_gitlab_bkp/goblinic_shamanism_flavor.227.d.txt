The dwarven language is a harsh one, and our shamans say that there are often many ways to interpret them. This results in great frustration among the shamans most keen on delving into our ancestral records, and it is not an uncommon occurrence that some tablets end up as boiling stones rather than records. This means in turn that space has freed up where we can put our own take on the history of the Serpentspine. It’s not as if the dwarves are still here to prove us wrong, after all.
