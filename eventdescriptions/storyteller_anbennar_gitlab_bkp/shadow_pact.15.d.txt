The hag’s of the Night Coven have long considered us recalcitrant heretical outcasts for not bowing to their whims. Whispers in fortuneteller's tents and roadside inns claim that in retaliation, these dark creatures have cursed a region, causing its land to turn fallow and its people to suffer under the burden of malignant spells. Followers of the hag's demand the right to freely serve their mistresses in order to revert the enchantment.
