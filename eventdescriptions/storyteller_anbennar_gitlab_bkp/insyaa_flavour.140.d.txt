Every craftsman, whether a mechanim or a being of flesh and blood, will agree that one of the most annoying parts of crafting handmade items is the requirement to go gather the components required for the assembly of the end product over and over again. With the Container Compartmentalized Torso, at least for mechanim craftsmen, this tedious task belongs to the past. Now they can store all manner of raw materials, component pieces, and even tools needed for their work. No longer will they have to interrupt their labors to gather what they need to continue. Instead, crafting for hours on end, constantly churning out high-quality goods, will become the norm with this ingenious use of very personal storage space. And the innovation does not stop there, for the basic Container Compartmentalized Torso is highly modular, able to accommodate the varying needs of different professions with ease, from heated or cooled compartments to pressure-compression systems allowing the storage of far more material than usually possible with conventional containers.
