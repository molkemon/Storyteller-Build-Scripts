Word has reached the lord: a clique of palace eunuchs has been caught trying to take the consort’s life. Aiming to elevate a member of the lord’s harem to be primary consort, they planned to strangle the consort with a silken rope in their quarters as they dressed for an elegant banquet. However, seeing the assailants in the mirror, they quickly raised the alarm. Promptly seized by the palace guard, the clique beseeched the lord to spare them, claiming the consort had been slowly corrupting them, placing their favorites in positions of power, and spreading their tyrannical influence over the entire realm. They were compelled to remove the consort for the good of all. Their selfrighteous pleading was cut short only by the blade of the Palace Marshal, methodically decapitating them one by one. The lord now faces the choice of what to do with the rest of the sorry lot. Proclaiming ignorance of the plot, the remaining eunuchs would surely be loyal if spared, and unable to amass the influence which had allowed the clique to even consider such a brazen scheme. However, a plot against a member of the royal family by lowly commoners such as eunuchs is a crime of the highest degree, and must be treated accordingly. Justice must be done, and be seen to be done.
