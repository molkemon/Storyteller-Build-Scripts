After the pump has been restarted it is finally time to do so with the rest of the systems powering the workshop. You hurry back to the Control Room and the technical specialists in your group start the procedure immediately. It takes a few minutes, but soon enough you start hearing rumbling noises from the direction of the Machine Room as well as the Boiler Room. The men you had taken up post near the pump and other important parts of the system start reporting no malfunctions either; you have successfully restored full functionality to the workshop. Finally you can start exploring the parts in the main room you were previously cut off from.
