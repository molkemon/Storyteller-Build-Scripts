Gerudia is littered with ancient barrows of a time before even the Skaldhyrric Faith, as well as the corpses of those unfortunates never given a proper cremation. Risen by dark magics, these draugr are known to amass raiding parties and gather tribute and control over their surroundings as if their lives had never ended. From the bogswallowed barrows of Haugrmyrr have truly ancient draugr emerged, claiming to serve the Frost Giants of old. Wielding corroded iron, almost as decrepit and wasted as their rotted minds, their bodies snap and shatter with the blow of axes. One by one, the horde is felled and burned so that they cannot return.
