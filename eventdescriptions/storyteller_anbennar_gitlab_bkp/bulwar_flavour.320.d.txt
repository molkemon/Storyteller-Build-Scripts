Sigilan, the old imperial capital of Fangaula, has seen many rulers, and endured many changes. The spread of the Sun Cult throughout its former dominion is just the latest such change. For many people, the idea of a supreme deity embroiled in a constant struggle against a malevolent outside force, that died and was reborn, is not too divergent from their formerly held beliefs, which eased the transition towards a belief in Surael. Now, an island in the harbour of Sigilan, connected to the mainland via a processional causeway, contains the most significant symbol of this spiritual change in circumstances, the Alabatsoba Kenetiledin. On this islet the Temple of Incandescent Sunset, or EduzErbusuranqul, rises from the waves, the marooncoloured glazed clay tiles of the ziggurat glittering in the sun. Each sunset, the entire temple appears as if aflame, a true marvel on full display for every fisherboat and trading vessel seeking the safety of the harbour before nightfall. Unique for a temple of the Sun Cult, the sacred gardens of the Temple of Incandescent Sunset are not only found above, but below the waves as well. The temple islet is surrounded by an aquatic garden full of colourful corals and kelp, wafting gently with the currents and enticing swimmers to explore their beauty.
