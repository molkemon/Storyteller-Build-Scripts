The creature was faster than we could’ve imagined, and quickly our hunt turned to, once more, a retreat. The caverns of the Serpentspine are difficult to navigate at the best of times, let alone when one is attempting to escape the talons of a ceilingcrawling creature happy to rend you limb from limb.  Thus, I didn’t blame her for leading us into what I believed to be a dead end. As I heard the clack of the talons against the stone wall, I brought my head against the wall and made a fist, brought it to my heart, and moved it in a small circle, thinking of my partner who might never know what had happened to me. "End of the road." I whispered.  "But, not the end of the line." The kobold quipped, standing proud as the drakon rushed towards us. The beast stomped on a pressure plate, and a metalreinforced net swung down from the ceiling, pinning it instantly. I stood agape, then quickly applied the sleeping tonic.
