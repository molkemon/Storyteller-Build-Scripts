The most soothing bardic tunes fills the banquet hall of the capital; a perfect accompaniment to the feast of meats and cheeses served atop its tables. Around it, people of all generations have gathered into small circles of conversation. When was the last time the capital held such a celebration, and when was the last time our lord enjoyed a feast with distant family? The centurylong Lilac Wars had created a divide between the two branches of their dynasty and, were it not for the Bards extending the hand of reconciliation towards the Marshals, might have created a rivalry that would have only ended with the destruction of the other. To this day, the scars of those wars still haunt Istralore. Istralore, the de facto heirs of Dameria. Istralore, the right hand of a deposed dynasty. A round of cheers breaks our lord out of their trance. Their lord, sponsor and guest of honor of this event, proudly raises their chalice. 'Brothers, sisters, cousins,' they exclaims, 'I would like to raise a toast to the growing success of their nation. For so long, our nation was just a small collection of counties but now their lands are vast and rich and their people are flourishing. 'All that could never have been achieved without the support of our advisors. Despite our differences during the Lilac Wars, we have shown Anbennar the strength of our blood; of our bonds.' Cheers and heckles arise, comparing their lord to a protagonist in an epic for young boys. 'Now to top off my little segment in this reunion concert,' they looks towards our lord, 'After many acts apart, this saga we call history has put us in the same scene. Their proposal to you, dear lord, is that we share the spotlight. Reunite the family. Put dear old Istralania and Sideric's souls at peace.'
