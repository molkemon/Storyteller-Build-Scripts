Success. After successfully hunting down a drake (and experiencing its ferocious elemental breath themselves), our researchers were able to complete their research on the functioning of a Dragon's fire breath. Extrapolating from that, after some trial and error, we were able to develop potions which allow for it's consumer to spew forth fire like mighty dragons. There are still some issues to fix, such as the instability and unpredictability of the fire spouts causing undue collateral damage, but with time we'll be able to perfect this potion.
