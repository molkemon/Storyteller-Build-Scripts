A preacher is making waves in the province, holding sermons against the Laquieti Istaror. They exhort that it will ruin the harpy spirit, make them rely on a tool instead of themselves; "the future harpy will be a weak one" they swear, "if we don't halt this group now." Misguided as this preacher is, they're no doubt very charismatic, and many people in the area have already been swayed by their words.
