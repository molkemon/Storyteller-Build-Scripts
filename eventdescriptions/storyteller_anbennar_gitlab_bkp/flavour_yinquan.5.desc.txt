Outside of earshot of the Maidens of Liho, 4931 is jokingly known as the City of Alcohol. The name is not very graceful; yet it is not wrong. Many of the new shrines have been dedicated exclusively to Liho's Maidens, and many of those have taken to brewing mijiu as tribute for the Huli-Jing. They have been very eager to sell the excess product for more funds.
