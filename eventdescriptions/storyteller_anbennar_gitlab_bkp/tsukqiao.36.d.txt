The Necropolis of Bim Lau is one of the greatest holds of spirituality in Haless's realm. Our empire already rules the province of the living, and now seeks to free the spirits from their Hell. The Eunuchs claim the Necropolis is a living entity, a tyrannical ruler that enslaves souls, and has so graciously made the preparations for the battle against it. Imagine an army kicking a building to rubble. The battle lasted a day, and nothing remained of the old ruler. Thus, we now rule both the living and the dead.
