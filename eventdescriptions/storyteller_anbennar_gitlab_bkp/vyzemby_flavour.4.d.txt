Fishermen on their way to make their morning catch spotted a strange sight on the shore. A ship bearing foreign markings has run aground on a local beach. The lizards that stagger out explain that they are traders from a distant land and they were forced to land due to a violent storm. The traders insist on being on their way as soon as possible to deliver their cargo of banana beer. They request assistance in getting their ship back into the water. Alternatively, their cargo could be put to better use elsewhere.
