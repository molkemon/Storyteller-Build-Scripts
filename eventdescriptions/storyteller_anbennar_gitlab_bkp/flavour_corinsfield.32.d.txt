The faithful march into Poppyspring, wounded and bereaved. Their pride perished and their saint slaughtered by the savage dragon worshippers of the frozen north, the scattered remnants of the great crusade have trudgingly trodded their way southwards, in order to find the brethren in faith they had heard about in naught but tales. They are greeted with no gleeful salutations; only by stern men in buckled hats and chain armor and red cloth outside the city gates. Doubtlessly, mages are amongst their number. However, their expertise and great hatred for the heathen could be of much use for the Red Sentries, and the flame of faith kept within their heart of hearts could doubtlessly be reignited by the proud spirit of Corinsfield. Guarded by the most elite inquisitors, the Crimson Reverend exits the city gates. To the good Reverend, the leader of the cowed crusaders bows and swears their fealty. The good Reverend closes their eyes, nods, and speaks. "Good men of the crusade, your most righteous crusade has fallen. Destroyed by the Heathen, yet still accepting the company of the Witch."
