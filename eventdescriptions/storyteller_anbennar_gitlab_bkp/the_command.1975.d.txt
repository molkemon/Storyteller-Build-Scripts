The damn bird riders from the eastern steppes have thrown in with the Dragon Command, sending a considerable amount of material aid to support what ought to be a branch of the Great Command. Clearly, on some level they are entertaining the possibility of dissidence. The treachery of the Shuvüüsh is to be expected, of course, but the true surprise of this whole ordeal is that Marshal Noriko is risking the Command as a whole by seeking foreign assistance for internal matters. It is clear we must rein her in as soon as possible.
