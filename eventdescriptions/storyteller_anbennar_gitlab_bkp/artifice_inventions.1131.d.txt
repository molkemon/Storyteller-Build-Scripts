The artificeenhanced incantation bowls have taken a mundane item traditionally found in almost all Bulwari households and turned it into a valuable tool with tangible beneficial effects. A complex mix of magic derived from several of the knowings of Bulwari magic – what the Cannorian system would class as Abjuration, Enchantment and Transmutation – are woven together in these incantation bowls. When active, these enhanced incantation bowls purify the air using the incense burned within them, thus providing a reliable protection against lesser evil spirits, protecting the physical and mental wellbeing of their owners and lifting their spirits. Many of those who can afford an artificeenhanced incantation bowl in their home swear that they have even improved their health, warding off minor ailments such as muscle cramps, headaches and even premature hair loss.
