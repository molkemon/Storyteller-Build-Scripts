They say in lands of Karach, there are no neutrals there. This appears to be a lesson that our lord has taken to heart. As the forces of Shyarayan have marshalled, he has made a choice. He has defected to Shyarayan, in one last desperate bid to claw back power from the Axtas. He has taken much of the chieftains with him. They have despised the power of the Axtas ever since the days of Uzgar Stonescribe, and now that the chance has come up to do something about it the ruling class has embraced it completely, Bloodcurse and all. To stand against the Traitor King and his chieftains, all the Axtas across the land have formed a provisional council as we work to win the war. Us workers haven't got a chance unless we organize, and organize we have: we march now to battle in defense not just of the Axtas, but of art and civilization itself.
