Some tales came to us themselves, such as those with the dwarf and the elf mages. When approached and asked to help, they agreed to put their story on our pages. They had both worked for the Magisterium, keeping their work and love apart. But as their love crossed a century old, they decided a new course to chart.

They retired all the way in Lelanàn, arriving hand-in-hand. And now they teach at the Mihipha College, their love no longer banned. Their students say they are quite funny; the duo enjoying their positions. But while the elf is quite relaxed now, don't ask the dwarf about their ambitions.
