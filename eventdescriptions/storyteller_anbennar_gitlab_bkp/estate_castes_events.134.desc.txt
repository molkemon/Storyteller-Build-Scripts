A large group of halflings, a small race known for being excellent farmers, are requesting to settle in our realm. These halflings, through their adaptability and extensive experience, have already improved our own crops, and the results have been impressive. Many in the upper castes have also tried the "Tipney" tobacco leaves that these halflings brought and enjoyed them greatly. Because of this, many in the upper castes are asking for supplies to grow these leaves in our domain. Although the halflings are very skilled farmers and are introducing new plants from Cannor, many merchants in the middle castes are protesting that they are taking jobs from the lower castes and object to their settlement in our lands.
