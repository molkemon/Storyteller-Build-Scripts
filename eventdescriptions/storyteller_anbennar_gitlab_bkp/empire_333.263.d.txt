There may not be much land on the planet of Shagatan, but that hasn't stopped our colonists from constructing a giant harbor on the island from which to set sail. Every tiny island on the aquatic world has been visited, with small local harbors constructed. The real work is being done underwater, however, where construction has begun on a bubble city that we could potentially move into. Life on the sea has become such a norm here that many young marines now move to Shagatan to begin their training. Our next generation of soldiers are just as at home on the sea as on land.
