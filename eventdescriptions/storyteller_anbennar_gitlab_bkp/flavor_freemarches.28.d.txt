An unexpectedly late frost has set in across the Forest. Luckily the grain stores have been able to make up the difference and we've had no reports of famine from any of the provinces.  The Cursed Ones have not been so lucky; we've noticed a rise in raiding for food. Such is the way in lean years. More unusual, however, is a report we've received from the west. A Cursed One band has arrived outside any army camp, proverbial cap in hand, asking for food. The band, clearly starving, likely had no other option and consisted mostly of women, children and young men. They claim to have themselves been raided by a larger group of Cursed Ones.
