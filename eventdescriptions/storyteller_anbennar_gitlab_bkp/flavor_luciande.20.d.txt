Silent Repose is no longer silent. The screams and death rattles of hundreds fill the once great halls of this magnificent elven citadel, its memory tarnished with every soul lost before and during the "Red Masquerade." Vampires killed everyone attending, and only a few managed to escape with the blessings of the gods and by sheer luck. This moment shall forever be etched in history, and Silent Repose will be known as the castle of massacres evermore.
