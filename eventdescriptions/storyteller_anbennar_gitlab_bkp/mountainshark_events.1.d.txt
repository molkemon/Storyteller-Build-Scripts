In war, the goblinkind lacks the physical strength of the orcs or the armaments of the dwarves. Instead, our path to victory is enshrined in Glozok's lesson of violence. As our lord leads our armies through successful wars, he or she learns—and through him or her, we learn—how to embody Glozok's lesson of violence. Our enemies seek to return us to slavery, and we must fend them all off to achieve liberation for goblinkind. Our path of violent enlightenment begins here.
