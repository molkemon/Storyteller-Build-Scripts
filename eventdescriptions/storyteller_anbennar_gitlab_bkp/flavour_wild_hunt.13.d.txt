On the empty plains, a clan’s shamans are its life—they call its rain, carve its glyphs, and maintain order and stability within their domain. Unfortunately, most of our shamans died in Yarikhoi at the hands of the Triunics, and now our land suffers from drought, poverty, and unrest. The few remaining shamans are mostly those who were too young or too old to fight in the Incursion, or else those who had so little talent that it was not worth bringing them. Each of them is working to do the tasks of four of their dead brethren, but their efforts are still not enough. As we have every year since our war was lost, we now face a choice—tell our shamans to work even harder or tell our people we have failed them.
