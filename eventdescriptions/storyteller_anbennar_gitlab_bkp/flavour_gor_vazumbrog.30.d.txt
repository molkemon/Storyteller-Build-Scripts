The records in Khugdihr, the chronicles in Anbenncost, and the old tales of the Silver Dwarves are brought together and compiled by the brightest and most adventurous minds of our hold. The resulting knowledge, unfortunately, is heavy on "mythic weightiness" and light on "actionable dragonkilling steps." Apparently many bold adventurers fought bravely and were torn apart.the hold was heavily damaged.the battle went on for several days.and somehow, magic turned the tide.  Disappointed but undeterred, the scouts, historians, and researchers who came together to track this knowledge down resolve to stay together until they've solved the mystery of dragonslaying, forming a new order: the Vaultwardens. They'll seek out every possible advantage in magic, techology, and magical technology. Moreover, they'll crack open every book and vault they can find to hasten the defeat of their draconic nemesis.
