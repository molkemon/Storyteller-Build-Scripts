A battle fought. A child lost. A village burned. Such things are commonplace among the Lupulan Rainforest, but the pain from such events weighs on rulers all the same. Our ruler's rule has not been a pleasant one, and they know it all too well. The toll on the realm has been great, and the ruler blames themselves for the hardship. Lupulan has deemed nature to be as it is, but why does our land have to cope through such hardship? As if a thought could be spoken to the wind, a strange twisting of light illuminates the room the ruler resides in. A bright shining figure appears, arms outstretched as if providing the gift of their presence to the ruler. A blessing. Words need not be uttered for it to be received. A mere thought will do.
