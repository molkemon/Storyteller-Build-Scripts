According to local tradition, the lord regularly performed a small ritual in their court dedicated to Lupulan. This ritual took place at a modest temple housing a monitor lizard. Offerings were presented to the lizard as a gesture of reverence, seeking the favor and blessings of the Great Spirit. Over time, the monitor lizard had grown closer to both the people in the court and the lord. It was a quiet and observant presence, rarely causing any trouble, though it occasionally snapped at those it found suspicious, including diplomats. However, on this particular day, the monitor lizard was conspicuously absent. Both the guards and advisors interpreted its absence as a dire omen. Search parties were hastily dispatched, and the lord ventured out with a group to scour the region and its numerous forests in search of the lizard. A disconcerting development occurred as hundreds of soldiers also went missing during the search. Such a mass disappearance was an extreme rarity and a cause for grave concern.
As the lord and their group pressed onward, they eventually arrived at a clearing. After ensuring they could retrace their steps back to the path they had taken, they continued forward. However, a strange and eerie silence enveloped them in the heart of the forest. Suddenly, the lord found themselves alone, separated from the rest of their group. There were no sounds, only an expanse of trees surrounding them. In that moment, they felt an overwhelming sense of doom, fearing that they had been forsakened due to their ineptitude.
Night began to fall, casting the forest into deeper shadows. In the distance, the lord noticed a faint shimmer at the end of one of the many paths crisscrossing the local woods. All other paths seemed to lead into darkness, and so the lord decided to follow the shimmering path, hoping it would lead them to safety or at least provide a painless end. They ran desperately, the darkness swiftly engulfing the path behind them, until they stumbled upon a group of guards searching for them, offering them salvation.
As the lord turned away to rejoin the guards, they spotted the monitor lizard. It emitted a soft, radiant blue glow, the source of the shimmer on their path. The lizard appeared slightly injured, and the lord sensed that a calamity was impending, one that Lupulan would need all the help it could get to overcome.
