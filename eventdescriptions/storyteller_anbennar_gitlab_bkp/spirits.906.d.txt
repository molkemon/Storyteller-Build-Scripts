This morning, our nation's heir told the court about something very interesting. For about a fortnight or so, one of their ancient ancestors has been visiting them every evening. Tutoring, guiding, and advising them in various matters, but primarily in the art of diplomacy, which was the ancestor's forte when alive. Thanks to the ancestor's wise guidance, the young heir has learned much about diplomacy and what it takes to charm foreign diplomats and local power players alike.
