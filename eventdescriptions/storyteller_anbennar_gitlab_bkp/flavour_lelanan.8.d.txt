My armor was battered, my spear broken, but my spirit remained unshaken. I had won. We had won. And Kunolo had been retaken once again and forevermore under Vyzemby's control, already rebuilt even greater than before. So I returned to my home; to the dearly missed Lelanàn. My ship arrived back at the harbor. Leli and Felana stood together on the docks, waving to me excitedly as I stepped ashore. But while I yearned for some intimate time with my wives, the docks were crowded with many more. Those waiting chanted my name, and I smiled at the sight. I looked between the crowd and my wives, pride clear on their faces, and laughed, "What's all this about!?" Leli giggled, leaning up on her toes to give me a kiss. "The High Priestess wishes to speak with you most immediately, my mighty wife. Your time leading Lelanàn isn't over yet..." I looked down at her in surprise, the meaning of her words dawning on me, and smiled at Felana in amazement. "Well then, whatever could this be?"
