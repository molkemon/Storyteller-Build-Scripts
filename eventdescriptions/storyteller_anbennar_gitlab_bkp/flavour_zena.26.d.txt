It was a surprise when one of the most interesting relics we found was not a Precursor artifact, but a new invention born from Insyaan ingenuity. Developed by a team of kobolds and ogres, the Sirimadsik Spiral Darts are a novel weapon, designed primarily to be used against mechanim, although equally dangerous to biological targets. Crafted with a spiral design etched in evocation glyphs, when they make contact with a target, the enchantments activate and cause a small explosion. Although the people of the Plateau have vowed not to use the darts unless confronted with an extreme threat, they would be willing to allow us to have a single dart with the enchantments disarmed.
