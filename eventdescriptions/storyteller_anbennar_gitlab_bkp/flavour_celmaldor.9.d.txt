A delegation of sea elves, bearing tools and resources, have arrived at our court. They have a simple offer to make: in exchange for room, board, and adequate recompense, they will assist us in constructing our renewed migrant ships.  More openminded members of the court are practically tripping over themselves to accept the offer. They argue that it is a nobrainer, that aside from the maniacal Aelnari there are few experts, if any, that can claim to be at their level. Meanwhile, other more cautious types in the court balk at the expense and at relying on foreigners, insisting that we rely on our own craft and diligence.  What is your decision?
