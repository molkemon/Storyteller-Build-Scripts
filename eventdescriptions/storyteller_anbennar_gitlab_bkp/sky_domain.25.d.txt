As foreign traders delve deeper into our country, they are swiftly followed by other outsiders: The most dangerous among them are the preachers spreading strange religions. Recently, our trade with distant realms has brought us into contact with the Ravelians, who speak of a One True God whose traits differ greatly from those of Amilak. While most consider them harmless, many Semayebaka see these foreign preachers as a grave threat—or worse, messengers of Yrilak sent to deceive the faithful. Any hostile reaction against them is likely to anger the nations they represent.
