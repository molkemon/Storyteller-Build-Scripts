While guilds have always been an integral part of the mercantile sector and have generally been a good thing, a recent development has given rise to a very powerful cloth guild gaining a near absolute monopoly on the cloth market in our realm. While matters of the merchants are not immediately ours to address normally, things are getting quite out of hand. There are multiple ways to engage with the guild, of course, like doing nothing first. We could also forcefully break up the guild, which would damage our relations with the middle castes significantly. Another way is to make a deal with them to prevent large damage to us or the workers. What will be our course of action?
