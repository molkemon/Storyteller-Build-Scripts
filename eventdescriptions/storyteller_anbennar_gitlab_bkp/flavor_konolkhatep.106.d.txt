Reports continued to flood into the chamber of the ruler. An uprising in a region of separatists demanding independence in another. A city has become a pyre, burning at the edges while the center is nothing but rotting wood, fit for flame and little more. In their eagerness to live the decadent lifestyle of a people, the selfappointed nobles have completely lost sight of the world outside their heartland. As a people cannot leave the banks of a sacred river, so too can the nobility not see beyond their heartland. The latest report from a place has been the most alarming of all. Thousands upon thousands of people have been killed, burnt upon the pyres of the kultists. They have been stockpiling weapons, many of which have been purchased (or looted) from ships. Some even claim they have developed a superweapon. Some kind of fusion of gnollish with cannon, fueled by demonfire. The report ends with a warning that the locals believe a new figure walks these lands once more. The nobles dismiss this as folly. Wishful thinking of a long dead faith. A figure? Here? In these days of enlightened gnollish thought?
