Traveler, in the year, the great Jungle Road Zuraxhmmoton was dedicated in the name of our lord. As the Sorrow cuts its way through Kheterata, so too does the Jungle Road slash through the densest jungle. As the Sorrow grows great trees along its banks, so too does the Jungle Road grow the might of Konolkhatep in the inhospitable home of the lizardfolk. As you would be lost among the trees without the Jungle Road, so too would we be lost without the Khet. As this road stands forever, so too shall Konolkhatep. 

Inscription by the side of the Jungle Road in Yassa
