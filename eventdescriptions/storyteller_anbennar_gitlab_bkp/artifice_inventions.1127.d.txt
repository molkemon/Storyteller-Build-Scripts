Millennia ago, during the Age of the Radiant Sultanates, the skies were filled with flying vessels created by the Djinn overlords of ancient Bulwar. In all the many centuries since their fall, the secret of crafting these majestic flying ships has eluded even the most dedicated Bulwari magi. But now, with the recent advances achieved by the young craft of artificery, this ancient dream has been realised. While they might not be exact replicas of the genie ships of old, the new Djinnstyle flying ships are every bit as aweinspiring when they lift off high into the noon sky. The ability to produce these astounding vessels in sufficient numbers will prove to be a decisive military advantage for every realm willing and able to include them in the ranks of their Zamukaras, or Army of Heavens.
