The Oren Nayiru, being hyper-radical zealots, call for the complete restructuring of society, including wide-scale economic reform. This process will undoubtedly leave most of Taychend’s wealthy mercantile elite destitute—or worse, dead. It is no surprise, then, that the rich merchant houses, traders, caravaneers, and slavers have aligned themselves with the forces battling the Kamrayakval, and have provided us with considerable funds for the war effort.
