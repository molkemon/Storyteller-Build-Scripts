The Demon Hills are treacherous, wont to shift and rupture at the slightest trespass, their temperament only growing more restless since they've been freed from the Oni. While traveling to one of our fortified outposts within the Hills, the earth beneath our lord’s feet opened, plunging them down a narrow shaft into a cavern. The gloomy stoneshod cave was lit with a dim, pulsing light emerging from a twisting, matted knot of dark energy. As they moved closer, the energy began to move with frantic desperation, squirming and writhing. The chain they now saw binding it to the cave's floor was taut, ready to snap. All it would take is a single swipe of their sword.
