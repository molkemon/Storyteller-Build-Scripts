Praise to the greatest, To the most courageous of all rulers, The lord of our country, Praise the powerful. I praise our lord, the greatest, The fiercest of all warriors, The conqueror of Heaven, I sing again to the Powerful One. Surael exalted our lord; of all rulers he raised them to the Heavens. To march before the forces, to lead the host, To give the battlesignal, to advance to the attack, To direct the battle, to control the fight, Unto them Surael entrusted; in battlearmor he made them sit, saying: "I have uttered my decree, in the Light of the world I have raised our lord to power. The dominion over all Heaven have I entrusted unto them. Be exalted, you my Chosen.
