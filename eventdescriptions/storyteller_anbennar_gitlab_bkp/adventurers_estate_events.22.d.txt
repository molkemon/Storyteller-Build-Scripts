Weeks turned to months as our ruler awaited any news or letter from our heir. The last word from them had spoken of a temple filled with elemental evil. It was not unreasonable to wait considering the adventurer's life, yet the description of this dungeon had filled our ruler with dread. Dread that turned out to be well founded. Today on the wings of a raven came dire news, our heir and their party had been wiped out.
