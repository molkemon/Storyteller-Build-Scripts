You can give people uniforms, but they remain a product of the city. A very corrupt city. Not long after the establishment of the City Watch complaints from the citizenry about small power abuses started coming in, but those were all about small stuff, easy to dismiss. The gravity of the abuses kept escalating sadly, and in the last couple years it became clear the corp is closer in nature to the gangs it should be fighting than an instrument of the law.
