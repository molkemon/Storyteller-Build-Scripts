The Goblinought is unsinkable, a mighty flagship surpassing all others in ingenuity and design. It rests in one of our holds now, an invincible example of naval supremacy, a vision of our imminent maritime dominance wrought in polished steel and burnished copper. Truly, only brilliant goblinkind would consider that one cannot sink a ship that does not sail. Our detractors may cry that this is a waste of resources so criminal that only the terminally deranged would even consider building it, but they'll be eating their words as soon as we find a port with which to launch our creation.
