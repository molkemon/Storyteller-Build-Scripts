Great news, my lord! A surveying expedition has discovered a large reservoir of aquamarine gemstones in our realm. It seems that everyone wants to claim mining rights for the minerals. Aquamarine holds significant importance among the Baashidi as a symbol of status and a ward of protection and longevity in traditional folklore. Many nobles in the Jasiir Jadid display aquamarine frequently in their clothing to showcase their wealth. This all ties back to the Exodus of Ardimya, during her journey to the Jasiir Doog. It was said that she left a trail of bright blue stones so that the thousands who followed her would not get lost. My lord, how shall we approach this situation?
