Every fourth year, the ruler of the realm meets to decide policy, make strategic decisions, and determine the course of the realm's conquests. The successful conclusion of the previous campaign has heralded the arrival of another, and this time it is aimed at our domain. Luckily, the realm is not subtle with its direction: news of hobgoblin troop movements have already made their way to our ears. Over the next few decades, the realm will stop at nothing to conquer the territory it has set its sights on. None in the path of their claims will be safe.
