The Ozarm'Chadash is an ancient tradition in ork society, perhaps even the oldest. In a traditional sense it is a way for the have nots to take from the haves, a means for the strong youth to take from the decaying old. If an ork holds a title, a wife, an axe or even an entire tribe a challenger can duel them for it, risking both lives in the process. A Ozarm'Chadash (Duel For Honor in orkish) is a fight to the death if the winner decides so, but the loser is occasionally spared by the victor, often for extra humiliation.  In our case we are planning to challenge one of the minor chieftains occupying one of the decrepid holds in the periphery of our realm. These lesser chiefs who infest their holds never last long in their dogeatdog world, meaning they will not refuse, as that is surely the largest sign of weakness in ork society.
