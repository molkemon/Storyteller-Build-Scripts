Their rediscovery of the elven homeland, Aelantier, has heralded forth a new era not only for the colonizers and the colonized, but the entirety of the world both old and new. The meeting of the old world and the new has led to a spread of resources and technological advancements as well as the enlightening realisation of the true vastness of our world. Animals, crop types, silver and diseases spread across the Ocean of the Lost and their government is becoming a model of a truly globalised economy. All throughout Haless and beyond, noblemen and advisors are beginning to debate how Aelantieri territories may be incorporated into their administration state and how best to enlighten the native peoples of the Aelantier with their philosophy. From their capital to Port Jaher, the future of the world is now up for debate.
