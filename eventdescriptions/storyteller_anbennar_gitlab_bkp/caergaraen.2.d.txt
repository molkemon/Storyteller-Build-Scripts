Our lord and lady advanced on their defeated enemy, Saerasyar in hand. Their foe scurried away, held an arm over their head, pleaded for mercy. Our lord and lady did not give it. Our lord and lady held the burning blade high, then brought it down. But Saerasyar refused. The blade erupted into a roiling blast of light and fire and magic, and the room echoed with howling screams. The to-be-executed was reduced to a mutilated, charred husk, as the explosion knocked our lord and lady back, sent them tumbling through the air, engulfed in flame, half their body simply annihilated—and the Green-Flamed Sword was nowhere to be found.
