The Coven of Yezel Mora has always occupied a place in the nightmares of Sarhal. While the hags weave their plots across the entire continent, they make their base of power in the shadow swamp at Sarhal’s heart. From there, a horde of swamp trolls has perennially threatened the surrounding regions, conducting terror raids designed to extract as much suffering as possible. Such misery fuels the fell magic of the hags, and makes them strong.  In recent times, however, the horde of Yezel Mora has changed. In the past, the hags conducted raids against any true sense, throwing their regenerating swamp trolls against sometimes farsuperior enemies seemingly simply to feed on the suffering and death of their own minions. Now, the hags conduct true campaigns, the swamp trolls coordinate in great waves of brutality, and the power and extend of the horde simply grows and grows.  All who look upon this agree: a new era of shadow is upon us. Whatever the hags are planning, they are no longer content to stay within their swamp and intrigue beyond their borders. Yezel Mora marches for us all.
