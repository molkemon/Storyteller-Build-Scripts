So saith THE MOB:  We have begun to surge outside Kimanis What leaders we do have now murmur low They say that there has been a small invasion Of foreigners who will perform a show  Their drab attire doth lack a tad for luster Their soldiers do not show dramatic flair Their officers are shaky on their linereads Their stage they will perform on is quite bare  A cause for overjoy. Here is Kimanis. Whose weapons glow with colors of the sun Our waving banners silkenfine yet glimmer Drunk with our wine, we cheer a battle won  And now the foreigners flee fair Kimanis These foreigners forgot one simple rule Their warplay lacked a fitting grand finale Which fair Kimanis deals to their last man
