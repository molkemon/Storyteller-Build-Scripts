As the mages of the expedition finish casting their spell, a brief silence falls over the group. Then, with a deep rumble, the roots begin to move. Rather than moving aside, however, they stretch out into the cave, reaching out towards the terrified expedition members. They scramble to escape, but the roots move with horrific speed. None succeed. After twenty seconds of gruesome screams and scratching of stone against metal, they retreat back to their original position, leaving the cavern a sickening mess of blood and gore.
