The capital is the undisputed center of the Jadd Empire, and thus of the world. It should thus be no surprise that all the knowledge in Halann has, in recent years, been finding its way to the court. From the most recent manuscripts, to the oldest papyrus books and even older clay tablets, any idea that has ever crossed a beings mind may very well be recorded somewhere in the library. Usually, this is a benefit, drawing the most learned scholars from regions near and far to devote their minds to Suraels cause. But the past few weeks, the library has been the sole domain. Rarely venturing outside into the light of Suraels sun, the monarch has instead been devouring knowledge, binging as hard as any drunkard would with wine. The monarch reads one book, finds their attention drawn by a sentence or phrase therein, reads another from there, and repeats the process dozens of times each day. We can only hope they emerge from this state soon, and perhaps even bring some useful wisdom back with them.
