Beepeck, abusing their authority as the Halfling Elector, has long coveted our lands, especially the halfling majority province of Orston. Despite our best efforts to integrate the locals, there have always been a few local instigators willing to give fuel to their efforts to legitimise these claims, as they frame our rule as oppressive and illegitimate. What chance is there for peaceful relations between us, when this incessant threat to our sovereignty continues without end?
