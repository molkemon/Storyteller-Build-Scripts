In the northwest reaches of the Ynn river lies the region of Rzenta, a cultural melting pot of Old Rzentan culture and the scions of the Vareynn Empire. While rightfully famed for their skill as fur trappers, another precious commodity comes out of these lands thanks to the skill of Rzentan craftsmen: stained glass. Stained glass from Pomvasonn adorns temples and churches up and down the river. We have reached out to a local artist to create a piece that represents the Ynn for an exhibit in Medaka's Exceptional Treasures, and will sail to the province in several years to acquire it.
