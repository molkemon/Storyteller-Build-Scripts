A horrified nomad has requested an urgent audience with the lord. He claims that their elder, a powerful diviner, has predicted an outraged local mob attacking their camp in the near future. The messenger is on his knees begging for a few soldiers to protect their camp and hold off this atrocity. However, a watching advisor whispers in the monarch's ear that should we do this, we risk alienating the local populace by providing special treatment to the foreigners.
