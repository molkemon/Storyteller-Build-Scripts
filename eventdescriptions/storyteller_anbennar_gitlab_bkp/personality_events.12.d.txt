Our ruler’s rule is marked by remarkable cooperation, from the bureaucrats to the simple traders and even the republican aristocracy, everyone seems to feel that our lord has their good at heart. There is one group, however, that feel our ruler has let them down. Their own family and supporters feel we have not made enough use of our office. Their property and influence is substantial even if it has not grown much during their time in office and many insist that they could do great things for the republic.
