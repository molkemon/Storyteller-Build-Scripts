The heart of the plains has been torn out today—after a long and prosperous life, the ruler of Storms Khaimalk III has died. Although he died surrounded by his children and trusted advisors instead of gloriously on the field of battle, it is hard to imagine that Irdaeos would have anything less than a place in his own clan in Ik Magthaal for his chosen son. It is not the eternal happiness of Khaimalk that the people despair over, but the fate of the Wild Hunt without his infinitely righteous guidance after so long spent with it, and the thought of a future without him. There is only one thing to do: the realm's swiftest messengers will be sent to ride day and night across the plains to inform the nation. They will ride faster than they have ever ridden before, for now that our ruler is dead, they carry a piece of his spirit inside them.
