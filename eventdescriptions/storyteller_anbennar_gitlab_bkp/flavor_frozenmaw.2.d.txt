"What is my name, Papa? Why does Mama call me Drazad, when you call me Elrac?" Papa looks at me, sighs, and leaves the room. I ask the question to my tutors, to the guards, even to the jester, and they all leave or claim they have important business and can't be bothered. Nobody answers my question. I go back to my room and play with my toys. Even then, this just brings more questions. All the heroes look human, while all the monsters look like orks; none of them look like me. If humans are good and orks are bad, then what am I? Why did Mama and Papa marry if their people are enemies? I know it wasn't for love, as they're never together. I ponder this. When Nanny arrives, she tells me that soon, all this will change. A man will come to make things right again and put people like Mama in their place again. She says I shouldn't worry - that despite having their blood, he’ll treat me well. Mama says to tell her if anyone says anything bad about orks, or anything treasonous about their ruler. Will the man still treat me well if I'm loyal to their ruler?
