I stood before the High Priestess in her personal chambers, where the air was filled with the scent of perfume. A slight smile rested on my face as her offer hung in the air.

"I am honored, my High Priestess, though I admit I question why me. I have a flawless record, yes, but I have sisters more flawless than I."

The High Priestess smiled warmly at me and took a bite of one of her ever-favoured pastries. "Do not discredit your influence, Kyola. I have heard much of how your sisters look up to you. They turn to you for guidance. Your prowess against Rayafo was unquestionable, and you saved many lives with your heroism in battle. We will need that heroism more yet. There is no better candidate to lead the Spouse's Crusade. Now, do you accept or not?"

I lowered my head, honoured most deeply that the Chosen of Lel herself thought so highly of me. I carefully considered my reply in that time, for I knew all too well the risks. On the battlefield, nobody was assured to return to their loved ones alive...
