In the days after the Great Incursion, the Triunic mages scattered our domain to the breezes blowing across the plains, which carried our friends to lands we still do not maintain regular contact with. One such friend was the Ruin Bringer clan, which almost alone among the clans of Nuzurbokh sees Irdaeos as we do. Unfortunately, our weakness and theirs in the aftermath of the Great Incursion has made them a ripe target for their enemies, and they have been drawn into a war they are not likely to win. We of the Filustkhuga know that loyalty is as important as glory, as it is impossible to have one without the other. It is for this reason that the Ruin Bringers have called on us to honor both Irdaeos and our friendship with them and assist in their war. What shall be our response?
