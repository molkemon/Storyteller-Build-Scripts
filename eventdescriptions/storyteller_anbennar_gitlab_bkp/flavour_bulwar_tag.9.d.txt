A cloaked figure has approached the council for a private meeting; her credentials were strong, and so her request was granted. Behind closed doors, she introduced herself as Yamima, the most influential courtesan in Zanbar. Indeed, her statements ring true; she has many supporters both around the king and in the wider countryside. She comes to ask one thing: She wishes to see herself on the throne in Zanbar.  Her request is very plausible; some small guarantees and some covert support, and she would join the Naqtazan as a member in perpetuity. Naturally, this would cause some consternation for Sareyand, who will likely not support us in the future if we see this action through, even if their ruler is too cowardly to fight over it.
