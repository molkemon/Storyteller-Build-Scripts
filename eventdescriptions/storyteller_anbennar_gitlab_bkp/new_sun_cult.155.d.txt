Many of the more well to do humans in the realm; merchants, officers, administers, priests, and the like; feel as though the recent outflux of elves from Bulwar to Aelantier is a sign that Surael is moving his eye away from our lands. They feel as though the elves have abandoned them to their fates. They say that as the servants of the Chosen, they ought to follow their masters to Aelantier and help them carve out a nation there, where they can serve the elves and remain in Surael's good graces.
