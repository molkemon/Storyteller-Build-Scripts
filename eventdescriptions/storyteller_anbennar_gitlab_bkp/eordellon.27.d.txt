The Dradh find themselves amidst magical Precursor relics, potent veins of Dames tear, and a plethora of weaker arcane artifacts - yet all the power that can be drawn from these elements pales in comparison to even the whimsical parlor trick of a full Fey. For this reason, the Dradh are insistent on seeking counsel from the Fey directly. None of the Fey, however, match the power (or the malice) of the dreaded Hags. Tales are told in our land that even the scions of Giants could not escape the clutches of these crones, ere they were foolish enough to seek them out as patrons. And yet, our Dradh maintain that they will not fall prey to the Hags' malice, insisting that any support provided will be controlled and calculated.
