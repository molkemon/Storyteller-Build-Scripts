The warriorprince Jaddar was chosen by the seven Masnsih tribes to serve as Herald of the Sihrusam, to guide the tribes in these times of trouble against the ravenous Zokka 'DevourerofSuns', to secure for future generations the Salahad of today, forever. In that noble task Jaddar sadly failed, and we were much to our shame pushed to the very edge of destruction, leaving countless Masnsih to endure alone the savagery of Zokka.  Yet, in our absence the Masnsih reconvened a new Sihrusam, aided it seemed by powers from beyond. Today, those powers have revealed themselves; the vile gnolls of the sad Sur, the pack of Tluukt, who have gladly taken under their control the desert banners. We cannot be sure what fresh horrors those noble warriors now endure under the heel of Tluukt, but it is clear that the heavens are mocking us for our complacency.
