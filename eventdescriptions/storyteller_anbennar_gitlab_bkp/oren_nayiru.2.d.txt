The cult of Vherenar Freesword, formed during the fall of the Second Gophiran Empire, has long been a persistent annoyance for the rulers of Taychend. A secretive organization, compartmentalized into hidden and autonomous "cells," led by Kavalali warrior priests, they are widespread among the slave populations of Taychend, dedicated to vanquishing the evil of slavery. Due to Vherenar's Ahartadyami heritage, they also believe in the "Elusive Ones," a group of powerful creator gods who fled the heavenly world in the chaos of some distant calamity.

When the Jaherian Exemplars arrived in Taychend and spread their ideals of abolitionism—believing in a sole-surviving god, Surael—the Cult of Vherenar took notice. The Kavalali of the Uesrennu and Katelperai cells argue that Surael is the last of the Elusive Ones of legend, and that the arrival of the Exemplars heralds a new dawn over Taychend. As their millenarian fervor infects the rest of the cult, cells across Taychend have spiraled into a theological flurry of increasingly radical proclamations—the Tenets of Uesrennu, which declare a new faith, that of the Oren Nayiru, or One Sun, supreme among them.

The Oren Nayiru now call for the abolition of slavery, the ejection of all invaders, the reorganization of land and society, the replacement of warlords with the pious Kavalali, and the spread of Nayiru’s truth to all peoples who would hear them. They are militant, well organized, and determined—and with their new faith spreading through the slave, serf, and peasant populations of Taychend, they may very well soon be successful.
