"Bloody death after bloody death. Havoc and destruction on our people. Nothing else to say on that front, but we won. Now onto the interesting parts. After slaughtering these Asanbosam bastards, we have harvested enough of their preternatural claws that we could outfit a small army. And that is just what we will do. Of even more interest, to a great understander of history like myself, are the ancient ruins we found when clearing the jungle of the dead. Not much more than a hole in the side of a hill from the outside, this truly is a ruin of our halfling ancestors. Seems to be some squatters have disturbed the place, based on the notsoancient firepits, but full of wondrous treasures it still is."; Ralambo Tallfriend, Lieutenant to the Esteemed Leader of the Bibyobi Monster Hunters
