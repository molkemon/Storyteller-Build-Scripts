The rivalry between Vels Bacar and Arganjuzorn has always made relations between the two lordships very difficult, especially now that they are under our control without access to the Panykorun, as they are not Sardish. We now have a unique opportunity, however, in the figure of Avardyl UmaZomaj: Brother of the lord of Arganjuzorn and a personal friend of our ruler, he has trained in the school of Riverblades and married a Bacar cousin, becoming a stalwart supporter of our house. Perhaps we could place him on the throne of Arganjuzorn and mend an old wound, welcoming the closest of the Dolindha into the Panykorun as well?
