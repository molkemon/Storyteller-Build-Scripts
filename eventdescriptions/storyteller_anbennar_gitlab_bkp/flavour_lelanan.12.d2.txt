It started with naught but a rumble, the earth releasing a great groan. Then the shaking began in earnest, and a great burst of lava was thrown. The sky darkened with ash as the world was set alight. The paladins rushed to the mountain, and all who could gave swift flight. They conjured their great magics, containing the lava as best they could. While the priestesses rushed to help, those who had withstood the flames. In the end, the realm was ruined, all burnt to black ash. Afomapobo had stirred from its slumber, for we had been too brash.
