The hobgoblins of the Command have a curious attitude towards religion. They have disowned their old gods, in favour of a new system of rigid military and family hierarchy where no faith is ever involved. Some enterprising shamans saw this as a challenge and went looking for the longabandoned hobgoblin gods. They now tell us they have found these lost gods, greatly diminished and desperate for followers. Although we don’t even know these gods’ names, the shamans have set up a cult for them, making offerings and performing rituals to keep them alive. After all, it was these gods who made the hobgoblins who they are now before they were rejected by them; maybe they can do the same for us.
