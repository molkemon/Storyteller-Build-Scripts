The working relationship with the Gommo has finally born fruit. While our troops were skeptical at first, they soon warmed up to the gnomish Officers of the Sun (especially once they had demonstrated just how powerful their artillery could be). The gnomes took a very scientific approach to their training, collating data on our soldiers’ capacities as honed over the centuries, and from that devising a maximally effective way of adapting our forces to the use of firepower. The Gommo has now presented us with a plan of establishing a tactical division, in which instructors from the guild and the state train military personnel according to the newly devised methods, including a permanent contract for gnomish Officers of the Sun. This should allow us to increase the quality of our soldiery substantially. Refusing would likely lead to the gnomish researchers leaving, in order to implement their theories elsewhere.
