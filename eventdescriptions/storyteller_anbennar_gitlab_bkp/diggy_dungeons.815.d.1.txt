Luckily for us the waterfall posed not only an obstacle, but also concealed its own demise: A ledge behind the water leads under and around, and, even better, reveals a stash of crates and chests that eagerly wait to be opened. Without much ado the soldiers take out their crowbars and burst them open, revealing glittering gold and gems. They throw their arms into the air upon seeing the successful result of their discovery and quickly fill our coffers with the acquired loot.
