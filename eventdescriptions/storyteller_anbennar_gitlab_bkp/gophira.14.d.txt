One of our most prominent and respected generals has, in this last year, repeatedly butted heads with our king and has challenged many of his decisions. Only his popularity among the soldiers saved him from repercussions. But you can poke a jaguar only so many times, and the king was preparing to dismiss him. Unfortunately, the general learned of their plans. Interacting with a disgruntled member of the Sangamar family, the general then decided to depose our ruler and place this pretender on the throne. It seems many of his troops—our troops—agree with this inane notion, and have joined him in revolt.
