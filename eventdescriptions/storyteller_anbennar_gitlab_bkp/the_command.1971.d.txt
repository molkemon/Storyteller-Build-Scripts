The Yanshen frontier hosts daily skirmishes with the locals, yet these encounters rarely evolve into more than brief instances of pointless bloodshed. This was about to change, as a host of enemy soldiers disguised as bandits quickly crossed the established border under the cover of night and laid ruin to several villages under our protection. Before we could send the army, it appears these brigands met their match. Upon hearing of the looming threat of bandits, a member of the local Sunyanin fortified her village with every conceivable defense, creating an elaborate web of pitfalls, tripwires, and even managing to procure a cannon she operated to deadly effect. As the invaders swiftly perished, it was the imperative of the Grand Marshal to promote this woman. Yet, regardless of her new station, she always seemed to outperform her duty. The time has come to choose the new Dragon Marshal, and it appears there is only one natural choice.
