Over the course of many months, all senior officers, the Marshals of the Commands, and members of our lord's inner circle have been brought in for questioning. They have been made to endure tests of mental and physical endurance, to see if at any point they would rely on magic to save them. Their reaction to Korashi has been carefully observed, and their political allegiances have been questioned. The reports are in, and the documents and files are long enough to make any harimari proud... Not a single Shaman among the lot. The Grand Marshal isn't sure whether to laugh or cry. All this time, and for what? If no arrests are made, there would be protests in the cities.

Sighing, he/she goes through the files again. Hmm. This individual had troubles with following orders in the past. It could be taken positively; the sign of a strong spirit and cunning. It could simply be a failure of character, an insubordination that was not bred out in youth. Or... it could be a signal of secret alliances to Shamanism.

And that one, they were staunchly opposing several new military reforms that were the pet interests of the Grand Marshal. An impassioned and intellectual opposition was, nevertheless, a possible sign of disloyalty.

And this person had embarrassed the Grand Marshal publicly. It would be good if they were far away from the levers of power. There were undoubtedly many young subordinates eager to show their loyalty to the Grand Marshal for a chance at advancement. These open positions would be an opportunity for them to ascend the officer ranks.
