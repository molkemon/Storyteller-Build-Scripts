Founded by the Progenitor himself, the Dragonflame defined the Castanorian art of war and held the honor; and responsibility – of being first among the first. The Legion always strived for excellence, often going further than would have been expected. Every soldier is keener, every unit is more coordinated, every battle is more decisive.  Wherever the Dragonflame goes, armies are emboldened by those who incarnate the true legionnaire.
