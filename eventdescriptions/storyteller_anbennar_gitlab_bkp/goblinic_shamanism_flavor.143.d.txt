Long have other races looked down upon our unique food, but those days are past.  All the great shamancooks in our country have come together to create a masterpiece in the name of the Emptiness. After days of preparations, they unveiled their creation: a never before seen buffet of exotic meats, dried fruit, steamed and grilled fungi. They have created sauces with dwarven ale, made bread with halfling wheat, salted fish from Gerudia, everything for a goblin to gorge themselves after their heart's content.  When some foreign dignitaries saw this arrangement they were surprised, nay amazed, nay awestruck. "What in the…," one of them whispered before sampling a piece of Escanni beef wrapped in Small Country pastry lined with deep cavern mushrooms. "This is amazing.", he exclaimed, before he and his colleagues descended upon the food with as much vigour, and delight as any hungry goblin. Goblin cuisine has truly surpassed all others.
