"My trip into the Dwarovar was a lonely one, for Kiusz had other business to attend to and could not accompany me. I’d heard tales of the beasts within the caverns of the Serpentspine in my travels to Bulwar, but the one that I felt would’ve been most exciting was the drakon. Long, slender beasts with the ability to climb walls and occasionally breathe fire would certainly be a hit at the Menagerie. The Skewered Drake clan were the most wellknown of the drakonriders, having bred and tamed them within the Hold of Gor Vazumbrog.  My first attempt, as always, was diplomacy. The orkish drakonriders did not take my queries about taking one of their mounts for myself well, and my propensity for battle was tested. As was my even stronger propensity for running away from unwinnable odds. I thought all hope was lost, and I would have to settle for a giant spider until I happened to meet a strangely cheerful kobold with dark red scales who offered me assistance in my search for a wild drakon. She was looking for artifacts that could help her revive her dead God, and it seemed as though we would both get further working together than continuing on our own."; The Autobiography of Fauna Hunter Bastirai Kazuru, Chapter 4
