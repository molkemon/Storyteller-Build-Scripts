Kobolds are distrustful of newcomers due to their mistreatment by most folk over many lifetimes. This does not mean, however, that non-kobolds cannot become accepted or even integrated into kobold culture. This is especially true in the Lencori lands, full of resistance and sabotage. For centuries, the halflings have resisted human rule; and they've done so from burrows, in tribes divided by petty differences, and from half the height of their enemies. Is it any wonder that now, a fellow of similar stature to our own has risen within our ranks? "Lord Ciderscale," as he now calls himself, has mixed the halfling with the kobold. Coating himself in scarlet warpaint, and announcing himself to be as red in character as the cider he drinks, he has become a mainstay of our forces in the realm. From his command center at the core of Appleton, he has done a mighty fine job defending our newly acquired lands from those further south that desire to reclaim it and expel our brethren. Truly, he must be kobold-born in spirit!
