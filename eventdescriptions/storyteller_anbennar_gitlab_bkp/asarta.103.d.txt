A thick smog of incense filled the room where the Court of Change resided. At its center, laid out on an array of cushions, sat Akhaxpa. As the ruler approached her and knelt down, she opened her three eyes, filling the room with a blinding blue light. "These churches you build to spread your faith. Why do you keep them somewhere when they already follow? They have served their purpose. Bring change to more lands by building more temples there. Otherwise, stagnation will follow."
