Walter was doing his usual task of guarding the border with Venomtooth. He and his soldiers often kept watch of the Alen, ready to turn back any raid that came towards Gawed. However, today was different.  A galley sailed down the river carrying approximately two hundred green orks shackled to the hull. This was not the unusual part, as they had seen many Escanni sell ork slaves down the Alen. No, the strange part was that the ship’s crew were orks. As Walter and his soldiers approached the boat, the rowers stopped, allowing them to board.  Walter approached the lead ork and asked, "What purpose do you have sailing down these waters with a band of orks?"  The ork crossed his arms. "What does it look like?" he replied in heavily accented Common. "We’re going to sell these slaves in Vertesk. Is there a toll or something?"  Walter nodded. "There is a toll, but I do not believe orks would sell orks into slavery. Pardon my skepticism, but I believe orks only enslave goblins and humans, not their kin."  The ork laughed heartily as he had one of his crew hand over the toll. "Why would we not enslave the strongest weaklings we can? You humans are strange. The Bonecarvers lost the war, so now they are slaves as is their fate."  Walter was not sure how to respond, so he merely disembarked from the boat with his soldiers after collecting the toll and watched as the galley sailed further downstream. He sent a contingent to shadow their journey, intent on making sure they were up to nothing nefarious.
