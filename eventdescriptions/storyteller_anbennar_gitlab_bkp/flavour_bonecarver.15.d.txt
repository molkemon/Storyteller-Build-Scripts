The silver city, they called it. A once shining example of human civilization in Cannor, the centre of a great empire, the engineering peak of human and dwarven collaboration, created under the supervision of a god himself. Now, the ashened gray walls remembered only death and decay. The entire city stunk as sewage and corpses piled everywhere and miasma filled every nook and cranny. This was not what our lord expected, they thought of grand celebrations, of orks cheering, of Korgus Dookanson. Yet none of this can they find. A creeping feeling ran up their back, one that had been there all this time yet they had refused to acknowledge. It passed the tipping point as it crept up to their heart, causing their knees to let up and their mouth to scream out in anguish. There was nothing to deny anymore. Even without seeing the corpse, the remnants of battle are clear: Korgus Dookanson is dead.
