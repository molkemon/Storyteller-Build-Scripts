With the last of the rebel armies crushed, we have found ourselves with a number of rebel officers and soldiers in our custody. Some of the more zealous shamans in our court are suggesting that we sacrifice these captives to the demons, which would not only please them but also discourage other rebellions in the future. The other option is to let these rebels go free, which would demonstrate our willingness to forgive and might make our subjects see us in a better light.
