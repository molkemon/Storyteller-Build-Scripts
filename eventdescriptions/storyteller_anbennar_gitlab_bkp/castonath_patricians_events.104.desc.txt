That a city as large as Castonath can even be contained by walls is nothing less than a miracle of ingenuity and engineering. (Mostly dwarven). The walls have stood against the blows of frost giants, stood blackened and charred against Zaamalot the Black, and even held out against the hordes of the Greentide for several years before Castonath's ultimate capitulation. Recently, the Castonath patricians have started agitating for investment and resources to be placed into the reinforcement of the walls, as well as for us to send an army to patrol its vast causeways.
