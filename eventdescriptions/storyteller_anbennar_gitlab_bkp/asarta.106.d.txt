A thick smog of incense filled the room where the Court of Change resided. At its center, laid out on an array of cushions, sat Akhaxpa. As the ruler approached her and knelt down, she opened her three eyes, filling the room with a blinding blue light. "Following the same voices will lead to the same outcomes, even if those outcomes are not the ones you wish anymore. The voices whispering in your ear are not the ones of change. Remove them. Bring in new voices. Else fall into ruin and be forgotten, as so many before you were."
