Tell my lord: Your humble servant sends you this message:  I apologize for my absence; I have been lost at sea for weeks. A storm struck my ship, and I was left to drift around the endless sea until a group of fishermen saved me. I soon learned that they all belong to a cult followed by many of the sailors and fishermen along the Divenhal Coast. They believe that their socalled SeaAncestors have been elevated by Surakel to the rank of what the rest of the Sun Cult calls Chosen, and that they will protect their followers at sea.  They took me back to the coast, where I lived with them until I recovered from my ordeal. Their lives are focused on the sea and they are consequently very knowledgeable about it. They always knew when a storm would hit and where to catch the biggest fish. They know many tricks and methods to mitigate the worst tempers of the waters and make the vast sea look like a small lake. There is a custom of theirs that I have found described in records from all around the Bulwar coast: they put an effigy of the one they want to protect close to the sea with some offerings. If, on the next day, it is gone, it means the sea has accepted it. The SeaAncestors will now protect the person represented by the effigy.  I believe reinforcing our ties with this Cult can only strengthen our position on the seas.
