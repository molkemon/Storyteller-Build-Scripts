Following our adventure at the tower we have come to a highspired building that seems to be part of a church or some similar place of worship. It feels strangely out of place in the labyrinth, but the explorers are eager for the new challenge ahead.  The interior of the building is in a state of disrepair, parts of the ceiling had broken away and buried the room in rubble, which makes advancing through it a difficult task. Eventually, the rubble becomes too obnoxious to just pass over it, and it has to be carried away.
