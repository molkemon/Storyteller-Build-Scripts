A Siren commander and a prominent Odanches stand before the throne, each making a passionate case as to why the other is the issue.  Since Riva’s reforms, each Odanchesta ship has had a detachment of Sirens assigned to it. Initially it functioned as an oversight measure, but for the loyayer Odanchesta it has simply become a matter of utility. However some Odanchesta take grievance with having non subordinates aboard their ships, and often end up butting heads with the Siren commanders as a result. Likewise, the Sirens can sometimes see themselves as overseers to the Odanches, exacerbating the problem. In this instance a disagreement has brewed so deep its fallen to the attention of the Queen.
