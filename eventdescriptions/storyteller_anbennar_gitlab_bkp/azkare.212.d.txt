Of all the nations of Haless, few have more antipathy towards elvenkind than the Xia. Jaher’s conquest left great scars on their territory, and elves today are still suffering for his sins.  A group of elven monks have arrived at our doorstep. Tired of persecution under the Xia, they have come to seek refuge in Azkare. Their military experience is considerable, having spent time as mercenary monks in the Xia’s endless squabbling, and they will be of great use in our armies.
