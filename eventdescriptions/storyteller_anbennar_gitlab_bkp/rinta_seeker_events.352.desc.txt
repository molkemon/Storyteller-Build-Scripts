Radical Adeen appear to have assassinated a noble officer from the House of Might, asserting their own prominence in military affairs, and infuriating our nobility. They call for a thorough investigation into the matter, to find the killer and bring them to justice, or at the very least make an example of someone to discipline the unruly slave soldiers. Doing so would definitely anger the Adeen, but letting the matter drop would only further incense our nobles.
