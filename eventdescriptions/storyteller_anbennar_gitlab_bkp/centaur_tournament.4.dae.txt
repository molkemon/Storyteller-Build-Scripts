As the lord steps into the arena wielding the Gaohernlangh, a massive curved greatsword with a long wooden handle, favored mainly due to its destructive slashing power, they see an unfamiliar clansman on the opposite side. With a body almost twice as big as a regular centaur, this hulking behemoth looks truly intimidating in the stark light of the steppe. Wielding the greatsword with just one hand, he starts closing in on the Caehn as soon as the fight begins. The Caehn has speed and agility on their side, but blocking their opponent proves to be almost impossible. Strike. Dodge. Keep moving. For the crowd, this fight feels like a ritual to Irdaos, with constant movement swirling up the dust of the arena. This hypnotizing combat continues for a while, until.
