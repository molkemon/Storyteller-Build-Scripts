"Bang Ba di will be back, put bad children in a bag, Bang Ba di will find all of ye, and put you into the bags, one of three."  The chilling rhyme of "Bang Ba di" and its various versions have long been known to most children in Haless. Traditionally, it served as a folk tale used by parents to scare misbehaving children into better behavior. However, to the dismay of the community, it seems that this rhyme has now manifested itself into a haunting reality. A spirit has taken on the persona of the folktale and is abducting children indiscriminately, regardless of their conduct or character. This alarming situation has instilled deep fear among parents, who worry that their own children might be the next victims of this mysterious entity.
