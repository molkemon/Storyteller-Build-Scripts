Even before our ruler could make his decision to annex their vassal's territories to the realm known, a renowned oracle residing in their capital pronounced the following prophecy: "Our ruler asks for their vassal? They ask too much; I grant it not. For as long as our ruler will live, their vassal will stand tall." The augury is spreading like wildfire; many in the court believe we should listen to the oracle and our lord’s advisors counsel is to stop the integration, at least for now. In our vassal's lands everyone, from peasant to warlord, is questioning our ruler right to rule them when even the gods are against it.
