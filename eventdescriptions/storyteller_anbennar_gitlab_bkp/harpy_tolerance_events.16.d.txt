Twenty years. It has been twenty long years since your cunning plan was set in motion, inviting the winged chicken ladies to your lands to settle and multiply. The flock is large now, their numbers strong. The promised feast is finally within your grasp. However, your hand begins to lag as you go to give the order. You have seen your people's children playing with harpy young, their laughter echoing in the streets. You have seen harpies and your own soldiers standing watch together on the battlements. They have become.neighbors. Friends, and lovers, even. The hunger still gnaws, a deep and primal thing, but it now wars with a strange new feeling.
