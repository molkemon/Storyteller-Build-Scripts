The lord of Bjarnrik has invited our ruler to a feast in their capital, to which most of the Gerudian rulers have also been invited. Our ruler has been enjoying Gerudian dishes and is having loads of fun comparing them to their Reverian counterparts or mentioning the lack thereof. After the dinner, our ruler is conversing with the lord of Bjarnrik when a Skald sits with the two and begins diverting the conversation to the Era of Black Ice. The story is mildly interesting but takes a turn for the better when the Skald casually mentions a direct patrilineal ancestor of the lord of Bjarnrik, a man named Ragnar Haraldsson once ruled over the lands of Revrland. Our ruler notices the tension in the air as the lord of Bjarnrik realizes the implications of this: this Skald just casually gave a claim on Revrland to our ruler.
