Though Taychend is an extremely relic-rich region, most of the Precursor relics in the area are mundane objects, as powerful magical items were generally limited to the elite Slaver-Noble Class of the Taychendi. With that in mind, however, there are a few relics of power within Taychend, treasured possessions of their Precursor creators, that are nearly unmatched in their magical abilities. While not as powerful as the first-rate relics employed by figures such as Ebenanth or Kadradar, we have recently discovered a relic which can provide us with great power. However, it requires someone of great magical ability to wield these artifacts—if our ruler is not one, then we must give it to someone who can use it, which may well be very dangerous for us.
