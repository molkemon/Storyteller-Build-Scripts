An educated slave by the name of Leyan'd Lanahasha! has become the hottest topic amongst the slave population, both in transit and working in Empkeios. She seems to have gained quite a following, and her ideas of emancipation and harsh criticism of our 'hypocritical' values and governing system have given her some prestige here and abroad. She is quite skilled at avoiding our efforts to find her, for no matter how hard every administration and state in our realm has tried to capture her, she has always escaped without a trace. Now, there's a growing concern that she may become influential enough to rally people behind her in an all-out uprising. Especially so if she manages international support.
