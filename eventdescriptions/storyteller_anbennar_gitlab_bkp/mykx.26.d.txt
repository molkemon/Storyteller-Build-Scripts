With the improvements made to the Bone Citadel, the area has become much more hospitable to gnolls and demons alike. Visions, possessions and even demonic rampages are becoming more commonplace, which is a good thing. A group of priests have approached our ruler, informing the state that the demons who speak to them during prayer at the Bone Citadel wish for the Citadel to become ever bigger and more prestigious, and demand a sacrificial feast the likes of which are legendary. In return, they offer what they believe we desire most: a demonic ship so powerful it will exceed our wildest expectations. The priests urge our ruler to accept the offer and work towards their demands, claiming greatness awaits.
