While seeking to improve their main source of magical power, our court mages discovered that the shoddy construction of the previous builders has lead to a series of tiny cracks spreading throughout the foundation of the mage tower. Due to the increase in magical output that we plan for the structure, leaving these cracks unattended could cause some damage to the tower in the long term. We could repair the cracks, but doing so would take time and cause the refurbishment to be delayed.
