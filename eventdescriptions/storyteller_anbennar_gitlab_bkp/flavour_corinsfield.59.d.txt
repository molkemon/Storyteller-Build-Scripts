"HALT", a strong sound amongst the silent crowd. As the crowd turned to meet the man who uttered those words, they saw a young Ynnic wearing goodman's robes rush towards the pyre. "Inquisitor. This is no witch." "What are you saying, goodman? have you not learned anything from your time studying with Corin's faithful?" "I can understand her tongue sir. she is pleading innocence." The inquisitor turns back to face the subject of the matter, looking into the deepest depths of her eyes to seek something he might have missed, "How could that be possible, young one? These savages speak in incantations and words of power, how could we trust them?" "You need not set her free, Inquisitor" the young Ynnic took a second to catch his breath "Let us put her to trial. I can speak her tongue, then you can be the judge." The Inquisitor looked back at the woman who had cried her eyes dry, before issuing another order. "Bring her to court."  The crowd, stunned in disbelief of the fact that these savages could even talk, erupted in all sorts of superstitious speculation.
