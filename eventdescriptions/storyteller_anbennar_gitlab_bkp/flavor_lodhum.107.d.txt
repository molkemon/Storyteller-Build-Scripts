In a Cannorian human from Anbenarr has arrived, spreading the wonders of artificery. With them displaying the latest machinery and devices out of the Empire, works of their Ravelian contemporaries, they have come to spread the teachings and revelations of their "God Fragment." They have recently approached the local authorities to begin the construction of a proper church to their one God, with the hint that if they are refused they will simply take their word of the one God, and the works they brought with them. If we allow them to build their church, it will no doubt bring many cutting edge works of Artificery, but risk the locals being agitated, or worse, actually coming to believe this balderdash.
