Eagerly expecting for the Runefather to contact their ruler, our lord once more wandered down dark halls, desperately listening for any whispers and extinguishing torches just for the chance of the Runefather contacting their ruler again. Many sleepless nights had passed, as our lord began to lose faith, however in the most unexpected time, many whispers suddenly filled their mind and darkness covered the room. "Sacrifice" "Show no mercy, no failure and no hesitation. Sacrifice one dear to you, to demonstrate your willingness to follow my decrees, so that you may never hesitate ever again. With blood you will pay the price of power so high beyond your understanding. It is only the strong that can make a decision so unforgivable by your kin. Abandon your past self and devote all to the Runefather." As quickly as the whispers and darkness had appeared, so did they all fade away. With such a difficult decision, our lord alone debated whether to sacrifice their kin, or abandon such deranged actions.
