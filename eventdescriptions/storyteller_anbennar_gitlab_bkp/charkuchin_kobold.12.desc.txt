The hinuibirds make life hard in the mountains, but against all odds we have managed to set up a system of settlements outside the citadels. Our people living aboveground have only been kept safe from starvation and hinuibird attacks through a delicate, justintime system of supply deliveries and protection patrols, which works very well most of the time.  Unfortunately, whether it is due to overexpansion or hinuibird intervention, our system has crashed around our ears, and now the people in the mountains are starving. There has been a mass exodus towards the citadels, and the few that have managed to make it back tell the citadel of hinuibirds picking off desperate kobolds in the mountains one by one.  Now that the erstwhile settlers have returned to the citadels, their anger has turned towards us for failing to protect them. It is hard to disagree with them.
