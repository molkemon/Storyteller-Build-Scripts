Cassier, Silna Fox, Lucian's most beloved general, has grown old and his blood nears stillness. He has outlived his usefulness. At least in this life. Cassier, his knees brittle with time, still bends them in front of his lord and says with a raspy whispering voice, "I know your darkness and the reason for your pale skin, and I accept it as I always have. Let me serve you over my own time, my liege. I wish to see your vision realized with my own eyes." From his seat, Lucian grins and a glint from his eye glares over the feeble husk. "Maybe you will be of use to me yet."
