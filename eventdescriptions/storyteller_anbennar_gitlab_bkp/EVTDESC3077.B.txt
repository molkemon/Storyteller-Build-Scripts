We have always made it our policy to propagate trade between our good people and the strange people of the new world. As of late, however, the local traders have come to us asking not for food or medical supplies, but for symbols and tools of worship of the faith and many are even requesting to be ordained as priests so they may spread the faith amongst their entire tribe. If we accept, we will be united with the people in faith, but we risk angering the more traditional members of their tribe.
