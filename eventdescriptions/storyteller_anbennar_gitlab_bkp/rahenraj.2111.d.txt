The second son of the lord has always been a point of pride, a prodigy in blade and tooth and tactics. Sometimes, the lord thinks he could be a young Harimar. It seems this scion thinks the same, for today he appeared before the lord, and begged to be admitted to the Lotus Court as a general. Having such a figure take a place of prominence in the armies of this realm would undoubtedly boost our place in court, though it would require the Raja accede to such a request. Instead, he might simply be introduced as a courtier, able to study tactics in the Ministries libraries and perhaps earn an officers commission himself. The lord can almost see the future’s path stretch out before them as they look at their son, still kneeling before them, and knows the path will take their progeny.
