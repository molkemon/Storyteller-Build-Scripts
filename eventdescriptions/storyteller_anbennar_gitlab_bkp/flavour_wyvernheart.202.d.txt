An explosion resounds across the whole capital. The Tower of the Primus, a tower part of the royal domain, has burst into flames, and many fear the worst for The Regent. The collapsing rubble being held partly aloft or falling gently as various abjuration magicks activate. Too late to save those within and only exposing any survivors further while they hold.  Not all are worried for safety, however, as a dozen Floodborn assault the rubble to make sure their deed is done. Blood runs in the streets, and the promised heir lies dead. Seemingly slain in the initial conflagration of the Tower.  The rubble continues to fall as the abjuration wards finally give in, and as the smoke and dust cloy and linger in the air, flashes of spellweaving can be seen by the majority of spellshocked observers. The crescendo builds, the way that flashes and sounds fly like an impromptu orkhestra. But there is no conductor and only destruction in these instruments wake.  It ends sooner than one might expect, the final three of the wouldbe assassins slain in an instant and exsanguinated by The Regent’s might. His eyes flare red as he storms out of the dust cloud and bellows out orders and exhortations of vengeance alike. The heir is dead, and all shall know the depths of his wrath.
