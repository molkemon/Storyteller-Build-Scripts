The latest reports from the newlyrediscovered land of Aelantier have revealed a potentially earthshattering development. It is clear that the Day of Ashen Skies was not enough to destroy the elven race in what is now  Aelantier. Socalled "Ruinborn" elves remained in place amidst the ruins of the Forebears and rebuilt civilizations from the ashes of the old.  The Ruinborn have been affected in various ways by the changed environments in which they found themselves, so much so that the more pedantic thinkers even debate the utility of a single term to describe all Ruinborn. Some cannot even be called sentient; others have remained in a sort of primitive tribalism; still others have devised states and philosophies of similar complexity to our own.
