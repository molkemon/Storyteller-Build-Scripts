The Oren Nayiru have one wish—to rise up and destroy the tyrannical regimes that dominate Taychend. Though the leaders of the faith, the Kavalali of Uesrennu and the other strongholds of the Oren Nayiru, insist on waiting for the right time and rising up together, there are occasions when local cells do not always bow down to their superiors' wishes. This has happened recently, as a few rogue cells have launched an uprising against them. Though the rebellion may cause some damage, it has nonetheless exposed those cells' locations and operational networks, leaving them open to a reprisal.
