The Kaino have long been the persecuted of the Fangaulan people. Ever since their ancestors supported a malevolent Lich, these people were banished to the depths of the Dao Nako jungle and branded with the name of the Lich they supported. The Tamajoba people were even sent to Dao Nako to look over the Kaino and act as their rulers. But now it is time for that yoke to be dismantled. The Kaino should lead themselves, but they still require the gentle guidance of our hand.
