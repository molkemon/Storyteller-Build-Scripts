The gravehound rifles are part weapon, part digging implement. With different settings, dirt, soil and sand can be blasted away easily, creating an impromptu trench. Reverting the setting is used to solidify the walls of the created trench and prevent its collapse. The gravehound rifles have become massively popular amongst the marauding Sandfang Gnoll tribes in particular, who use them in their raiding campaigns to great effect, creating trenches and siegeworks out of the desert sand itself to protect them from their intended victims – without the need to carry heavy construction equipment through the harsh desert.
