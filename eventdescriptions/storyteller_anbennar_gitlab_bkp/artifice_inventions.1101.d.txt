Dames tear is a dangerous substance, capable of manipulating both the inanimate and the living. It can react to just about any material, contorting it into new and dangerous forms; it can mutate exposed persons not just in their bodies, but also their minds and souls. If we simply distributed Dames tear across our capital, we could certainly power our sparkdrives, twave transceivers, exoarms, automated translators, and more… but only at an entirely unpredictable cost. And yet, to continue our progress in the field of the magical sciences, this is exactly what we must do.  Thankfully, we have invented a safer way to fuel our artificeries: the Arcane Battery CapitalComplex. To create it, we first induce a thaumic field which can activate unstable magics. Second, we encase this field in magicstabilizing porcelain. Third, we run Dames tear through the field. (ENSURE THAT THE PORCELAIN BOUNDARIES ARE FULLY STABILIZED BEFORE STEP THREE.) This allows the Dames tear's morphic magics to exhaust themselves in a safe, contained context, rendering it into a safer and more pliable form. Fourth, we retrieve the Dames tear, package it into porcelain casings (just to be safe), and export across the capital.  This CapitalComplex is incredibly expensive to operate, but it provides us with the stabilized Dames tear which can change our artificery forever. With the ability to expand our artificing capacities while keeping ourselves safe, the future looks brighter than ever.
