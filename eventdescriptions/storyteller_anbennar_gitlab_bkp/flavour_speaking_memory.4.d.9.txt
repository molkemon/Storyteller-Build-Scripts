One of our people's favorite stories is that of Kau’i’noe of Kuahoi’i, a great hero who lived long before the Day of Ashen Skies. Known for having sailed into the Kiamoa on a vessel of living metal, he serves as an inspiration to our people; a model of what a good life looks like. Recently, though, some of our people have started to doubt if he actually existed. Is it really possible for a human to survive sailing into that terrible storm? How can we know the truth about events that happened so terribly long ago? We already sent an expedition to ask the Dragon Turtles, of course, but they refused to answer any questions about Kau'i'noe, or even to explain why they abstained. It appears that we must solve this problem ourselves.
