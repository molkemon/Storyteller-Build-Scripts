Spiritfire, as we know it, is a form of energy from the spiritual world. With enough mental focus and magical power, one can summon spiritfire from flammable materials. Materials used to fuel the Undying Flame are especially helpful, as they connect to a powerful mystical source, making it easier to bridge the gap between the physical and spiritual realms. While conjuring spiritfire is challenging, manipulating it is even more demanding, as spiritual energy can be unpredictable. Many Ashen Souls have suffered minor burns or singed fur while trying to shape spiritfire. We've developed various techniques for controlling fire, but these are basic compared to what true spiritfire manipulation could achieve. Two popular methods have emerged from our studies, both inspired by the Kartya that many Ashen Souls learn alongside their magic. One technique, based on Dekhana, enhances the effectiveness of magic and physical attacks, allowing for fiery fists and faster spells. The other, rooted in Pichka, uses spiritfire directly to strike foes with psychic flames for decisive victories. These principles can be applied to the monasteries, but which method will serve us best?
