We have endured our rebirth in the fires of change, and now the zeal of our people is satisfied. The world we inhabit would be unrecognizable to the one our predecessors lived in before the mandate of the Twin Gods, and both magmascryers and the common people agree that, at long last, enough change has been wrought upon the world.  Even if the process of change was long and arduous, we now stand to benefit from the results our work has yielded. Even if we did not have a specific goal in mind as we changed, we have metamorphosed, and our experiences during our flurry of change have taught us something about discipline and the endurance of hardship.  As we move forward, it is important that we do not forget what we have learned from the Twin Gods during this trying time. Perhaps one last change is warranted; if we change the orders that spread the Volcanic Truth throughout our land, we can ensure that we carry these lessons into the future.
