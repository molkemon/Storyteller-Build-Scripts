As with the Halfling and the Dunesole Ogre, not all love was romantic in source. Sometimes, a mutual love was all it took to smooth over a relationship that started so rough.

The Halfling was a renowned chef, traveling Sarhal in search of a new ingredient. But to the towering desert Ogre, he himself was a meal so expedient.

But the Halfling made an offer—a bargain to save his life. With his great cooking skills, surely he could find the ogre a wife.

Now, it's been thirty years since, and the two are suspiciously both still single. But you'll find them in the Halfling's restaurant, always willing to mingle.
