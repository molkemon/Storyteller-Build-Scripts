We have made it our holy duty to civitize Aelantier and enlighten the native people with our faith. As our priests and missionaries perform their holy duty in our lands, they report being approached by some native shamans requesting to abandon their ancestral roots and join their ruler in their holy duty to propagate our faith amongst the unenlightened members of the local tribe. If we accept, we will have a whole new clerical class of natives to spread our faith, but we risk angering the more traditional members of their tribe.
