Emil grins as he pushes open the two doors of the Imperial Palace to the Dove Throne. No servant nor lord nor Emperor is there to confront him. All of them have left like cowards, leaving the city and the seat of the Empire to the Second Sons. He assumes no less from the firstborn; they were given their lives on a silver platter and never fought for their inheritance. They did not know true struggle.  "Emil. There is" A knight shouts arriving behind Emil before he puts up a hand.  "Emperor…Emil" Emil whispers, lost in a trance as his eyes stare at the throne. He steps forward, walking across the fine carpeted marble floor. He is steps away from his destiny…his birthright, and history itself would bend to him.  "I Emperor Emil." The knight shouts again, breaking him out of his trance.  Emil arrives to the foot of the throne, but moments away from ascension. He turns around and barks back agitated, ‘What? Speak quickly Aldresian.’  "There, there is a letter, signed by every prince in the Empire. They rally their forces against you, united as one. There are too many of them."  Emil sighs and crosses his arms. This war they want is not his doing. And they are about to get a very rude awakening come morning. Emil has been in correspondence with second, third, even fourth in line nobles and branch dynasties ever since his rise in Arca Aldresia. All of them are waiting for their moment, and the time has come.  "What is our response?" The knight frowned.
