The blow of a horn, then the clattering of hooves upon the arena floor. The noise of the crowd builds with the tension as lances lower, louder and louder until a sudden silence. The sound of splintering wood, a form falling from their horse and then the crowd erupts in rapturous applause and rowdy jeers. The noble knights of Lorent, pillars of the chivalric arts, ply their trade expertly. The victors revel in their glory, while the defeated taste the dust of defeat. The first joust of Igraxraak can only be called a true knight's tale.
