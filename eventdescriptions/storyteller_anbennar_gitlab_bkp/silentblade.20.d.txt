This was not a defeat, Ruuk told himself. There was still time. He had again only managed to get a handful of new recruits, but what he lacked in manpower he more than made up for in information. He knew the where, he knew when, and he knew how. The dagger was there, all he needed to do was push it into the throat. As he looked over his shoulder, however, at his ragtag band of survivors, he could not help but wish for a better dagger. They would not be enough to face the lord, not with all the luck in the world. They would only be enough to make a statement, to rally the orks at the ritual. Orks follow the strongest, he reminded himself. He did not need to win outright. All he needed to do was to show strength, and the rest would fall into place.
