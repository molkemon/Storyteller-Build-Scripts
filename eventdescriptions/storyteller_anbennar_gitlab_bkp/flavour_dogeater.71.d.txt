The greenskinned monsters from Dogeater have gone too far this time. Losing an entire village to a particularly violent and desperate ork warband was, unfortunately, nothing we didn’t expect, but what’s happening now is beyond that. Entire kingdoms have been razed to rubble worse than the Greentide, and the Cannorian cowards are refusing to send help, claiming they’ve already done more than enough supporting the initial push into Escann. Our country still stands against the orkish forces, but initial skirmishes against Dogeater have not gone well, and both our army and our populace demand we take action to guarantee our survival.
