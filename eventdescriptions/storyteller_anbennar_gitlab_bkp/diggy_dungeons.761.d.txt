The Captain shouts bounty after bounty on the golems, and a number of expeditionaries turn ranks back to the enemy. Every kill a keg, every ten a purse of gold. Every golem that falls, another two expeditionaries rejoin the ranks. It's bloody work, but motivated by the only thing stronger than fear, greed. Casualties from here are negligible, and the promised rewards to be added to the final shares after the expedition.
