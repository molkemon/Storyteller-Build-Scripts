A strange man has been spotted in the countryside at various locations around our realm. He is teaching a most unorthodox version of the creation of the world to whoever will listen to his jovial ramblings. According to some he represents a dangerous and vile reinterpretation of holy scriptures but few of our subjects seem to have been taken in by the heresy. Rather many seem fond of this wouldbe prophet and his ideas. Intrigued by the reputation of our lord as a free thinking person, the prophet is now demanding an audience with our lord, offering to act as our advisor in spiritual matters.
