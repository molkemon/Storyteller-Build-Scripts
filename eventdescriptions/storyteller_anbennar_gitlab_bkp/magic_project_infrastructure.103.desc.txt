As we continue to build upon our magical institutions, the archmages of various magical colleges have come before our ruler while in the middle of a very heated debate with one another. As insults are hurled left and right and the audience chamber is showered with multicoloured sparks, the mages shout their demands for a new school to be built, each favouring his own magical practice. After more rounds of spellslinging, animal summoning, and charmenforced silent treatments, the ruler finally steps in and ends the debate before someone is turned into a chicken. Stepping forward, our ruler declares a new school will be built for one practice only.
