During a discussion with a powerful slaver in our domain, our lord heard tales of the exploits of one former slave, a slave who, one day, presented a well-thought-out proposal to his overseer as to how the production in event province could be greatly improved. He was then, of course, punished severely for his disrespect toward his superiors. The slaver told the story for a laugh—“How could a slave be so insolent?”—but our lord was not so sure. An individual with such skills could prove extremely valuable to the government. But was our lord prepared to subvert the entirety of gnollish society for this one individual?
