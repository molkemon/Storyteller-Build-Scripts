Bricena and Arfil are traveling to the Silent Lake to spend time with their fathers. After a family dinner, Cinrthir asks if they plan to ask him to support Arfil as the heir, effectively putting Salla under the control of Salla Myna. Bricena and Arfil respond that they are already very busy and want to allow Cinrthir to grieve in peace without pressure. Cinrthir smiles and gives them a letter, asking them to take it to the regency council. The lesser proclaims Arfil the leader of Salla Elyn.
