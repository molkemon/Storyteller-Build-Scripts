Bricena and Arfil are enjoying a picnic atop the majestic Ciranmyna peak. The great hawks seem much more tolerant of their presence this year compared to last. It appears the people of Ciranmyna are starting to lose their reservations about these magnificent birds. Suddenly, a playful fledgling great hawk sneaks up and steals some of their food, causing both of them to burst into laughter.
