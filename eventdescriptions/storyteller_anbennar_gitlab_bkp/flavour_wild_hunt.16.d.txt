When the first troops of the Incursion crossed the first bridges of ice, they were greeted by the land of Joglikunazmoq, the border province of the border realm of the Triunics. Our troops took everything of value, and what was valueless they burnt down out of spite. If the Incursion had been successful, the rest of the lands of the Lake would have met the fate of Joglikunazmoq, but alas we are weak in the aftermath of our invasion. Worse yet, the land is still populated, and those who still live there are more than eager to repay us for our actions during the Incursion, and then some. The wretches of the Joglik Uts smell blood, and are already amassing for their retribution.
