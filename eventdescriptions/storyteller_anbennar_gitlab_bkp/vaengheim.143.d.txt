Then did Vængheim's host come upon murals written in ancient hand, and artworks drawn on wall and floor and ceiling, of a world now long gone. And Freydis Oaktongue did beseech the queen thus, "Wait, o ruler. Let us stay here for a spell. For these mosaics are of expert make, and tell a tale too long forgotten.
