The moss sorter lies dead, and the scouts have properly moved into the room. Suddenly, the one who did the deed motions them to stop. He gestures quickly to signal the approach of another kobold and demands that the rest hide. A few seconds later an arrow pierces through the air and into the second cave denizen's heart. After waiting a minute and confirming their safety, the group comes out of hiding and discusses what to do next. Unfortunately, a ludicrously loud yawn cuts their freshly organized meeting short, and the song and dance begins anew.  What was supposed to be a quick and simple operation has turned into a veritable massacre, and before long the number of bodies becomes a logistical problem. Spurred on by his achievement, the marksman scout moves deeper into the den, while the remaining party takes their time looting the bodies. A few minutes and caves later the group reunites, swimming with loot and with nary a kobold in sight. On the way back, there is no end to the praise given to the one man army.
