Three is a number of great power, and here at the Bronze Temple I will sing you a song of three. I will sing you the tale of the Ujun Sisters, who once lived in the land of the gnolls. They are the crone, the matron, and the cow. The three of them still watch over the cattle herds of Fangaula and all those who tend them, for they are the mothers of the cattle.  A mother’s love and a mother’s mercy both hold power beyond my humble songs, but I shall sing for you all I know anyhow. Which tale shall I speak of today?
