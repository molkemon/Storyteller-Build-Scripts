Oh, who would stay indoor, indoor, When the horn is on the hill? With the crisp air stinging, and the huntsmen singing, And a tenton beast to kill. Before the sun goes down, goes down, We shall slay the bull of ten; And the skalds shall sing a tale, and we shall ha'e the ale, When we come home again.  Being regarded a great hunter of game is one of the most sort after accolades in Gerudia, and when a great hunt is called by the skalds, many from far and wide to participate. So the question falls to you, will it be a grand occasion for the nobility, or will you instead offer bounties to commoners for pelts they bring you?
