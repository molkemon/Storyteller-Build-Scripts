Children, particularly babies, dying is not an uncommon event in rural villages. However, one such instance has gotten a village particularly riled up. They claim that the child’s mother killed their own offspring with foul curses and hexes; they are absolutely sure of it. While the inquisitors investigating the situation haven’t found any evidence of it, they also have not found any evidence to the contrary. The mother’s refusal to allow the inquisitors to dig up the grave is highly suspect as well; what could she be hiding? Everyone agrees that this is a situation that must be looked into, but the severity of such will be left to the Crimson Reverend.
