While the Ravelian Society has been known for their work in artificery, many of them are actual talented wizards and mages in their own right. With their recent explorations of the God Fragment, we have received word from the mages’ guild in our country that the society has slowly but surely gained power within the guild. Some claim that these Ravelians are now claiming that magic is in fact divine in origin, and that mages have a duty to make sure it is wielded correctly. Though others simply state that this is a natural outcome of the rising popularity of the society in general.
