Thousands of birds enter the Vale this year, migrating from abroad into our perfect swamps. They’ve flown all the way from Gerudia, or the Forbidden Plains, or. well, we don’t know where they’ve flown from, actually. But do we really need to? What we do know is that they’re here draped in all colors of feathers, big and small, with a dozen different flavors and they are completely delicious to eat. Why worry about the details when we can have a great feast.
