To Kaldarim, head of the Markarim Clan. Your cousin Adirian sends this message:  Cousin, to my lasting regret I have to admit I almost died because I did not listen to the elders of our family. I decided to take the old Edesukeru route to Rahen instead of the Golden Highway, because I assumed it would be quicker and more profitable. How wrong I was.  I lost my entire expedition, including supplies and water, in the desert in just a week. I was alone, wandering through the dunes, already preparing to die, when I was saved. They called themselves the Cult of the SandGrandfather; they believe a Dragon is their great protector. It's weird, I know. It is funny that they cared for me despite being a useless mouth to them, with no coin or name to protect me.  They gave me water, fed me until my strength returned, and carried me to an outpost. When I said I had no way to get back home because I had lost all my possessions, they gave me a small pouch of incense to sell so I could return. According to them, I was a guest; a guest for them is sacred and must be treated as family. I had no idea people like those still existed. Any ruler would gain a lot by allying with such honourable people.
