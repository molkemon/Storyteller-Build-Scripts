Upon the death of our lord, the tribes of the Lupulan convene to choose their successor. The way it has always been is that the heir of the lord is chosen to carry on their legacy, but this time was different. After talking to the other tribes, we reached a consensus that a change was needed, and we banded together with the other tribes to elect another to the office. Over the screams of the lord's idiot children, we have crowned a new successor.
