The serpentine naga make up a small minority of the lizardfolk, yet they have a rare disposition to magic and, especially abroad, have outsize influence in their communities. One such naga mage has focused his eye on court membership, the highest office he can hope for in a land without a traditional naga aristocracy. Our clergy have objected vociferously, citing the sacred role of the naga in many lizardfolk societies and the implicit endorsement for a foreign faith which they feel such a move would represent. The naga are also notorious for standoffishness and a sense of their own majesty, and there is no guarantee how well this naga would gel with the court.
