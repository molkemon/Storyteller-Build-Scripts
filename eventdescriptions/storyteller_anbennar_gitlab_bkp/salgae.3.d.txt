In the deserts of Bulwar lies a tent, seemingly one amongst many. Within that tent, however, trots the leader of the Burning Spear. As they exit the tent, they look out, at the desert, at all they have conquered, and all that is yet to conquer. They think to themselves, what does the Caehn know of deserts and death? The Burning Spear will teach the Caehn, that even the smallest breeze can create the mightiest sandstorm.
