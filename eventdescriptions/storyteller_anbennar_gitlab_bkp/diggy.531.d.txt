The work on expanding our hold was interrupted today, when our miners almost fell into a massive tunnel that opened up below them. There is no doubt that this tunnel is the work of Purple Worms. By expanding our hold along the walls of the tunnel, we can cut down on a lot of work. We are, however, not the only ones with this idea, and it's not unheard of for Purple Worms to crawl through the tunnels dug by previous Worms. If we use this tunnel, we can't dismiss the possibility of an attack in the future.
