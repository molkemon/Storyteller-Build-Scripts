Thousands of Sanadazjal members descend onto the country, ready to examine and refine the artisans of the realm. The task of the Sanadazjal is massive: they will go to every workshop, every factory, to ensure that production is up to the standard of Enuuk. Yet they are a capable organization, and they have an elaborate but effective system of coordination and logistics. A goal which would take decades for a lesser organization will only take a couple of years for the Sanadazjal.
