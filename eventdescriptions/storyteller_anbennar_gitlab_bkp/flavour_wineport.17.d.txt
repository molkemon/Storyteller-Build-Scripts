Tomais sil Chalaal sat eagerly in the arrayed seats in the great Vintner's guildhall of Wineport, his engaged conversation with his fellow Wine Barons subdued as a servant called the busy hall to attention. 'Wine Barons of the League of Winebay. You are gathered here today to witness the ratification of the Winebay Accord. This grand document, he proclaimed, held in his hand for all to see, shall bind the great cities of the League closer together, to better fulfil our goals of providing wine for all, and prosperity for us. The leaders of the great cities of the League; Foxalley, Coruan and Bottlepoint sat at the grand desk. And, at the centre, their lord, who enjoyed the attention of all, for their great efforts as mayor of Wineport which led to the creation of this treaty. The document was placed in front of them, and each affixed their city's seal to the Accord, ratifying this grand treaty. A great cheer rang out through the hall as it was ratified, and conversation slowly returned to the guildhall. Tomais grinned, seeing such a momentous event happen, and poured himself a fresh glass of wine. "Maybe politics isn't so bad after all," Tomais commented. "And why is that?", Inquired a Wine Baron sat with him. "Because I just witnessed a treaty that will get me rich, all the while drinking wine I don't have to pay for," he mused, and took a drink from his glass.
