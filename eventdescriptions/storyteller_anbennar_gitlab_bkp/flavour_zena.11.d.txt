There are many who believe Bulwar to be an arid land filled with nothing but dry plains and deserts. However, this does not tell the entire tale of this ancient land. Within Bulwar are some of the most verdant plains and fertile farmlands found in all of Halcann. The Sunshine Manors of the Sun Elven nobility control vast swathes of this farmland, and are veritable palaces surrounded by vast gardens and filled with exquisite works of art. It would be quite beneficial to acquire a sample of the artistic tradition born from the marriage of Bulwari, Halessi, and Sun Elven techniques to exhibit within Medaka’s Exceptional Treasures, granting us legitimacy in the eyes of our far neighbors to the north.
