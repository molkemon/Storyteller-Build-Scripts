We have traveled far and seen many flourishing groves, but none seem as lush or bountiful as those of the Effe’i tribe. Even the youngest in their tribe are taught the song of life, and how each plant has a unique thrum to its spirit. Even more impressive than their plentiful gardens is their stunning architecture. Exquisite floral carvings adorn the wooden facades of their buildings, almost as if the structures themselves are alive. They are able to seemingly effortlessly prepare massive ancient logs for use in housing, a material which stands sturdy and dry through even the harshest storms She may throw at us. The Effe’i are a generous people; perhaps they would share these skills with us for our own knowledge?
