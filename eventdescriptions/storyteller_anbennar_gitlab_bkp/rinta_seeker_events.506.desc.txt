To nobody's surprise, another issue has inevitably arisen regarding the design of the Godswatch Observatory. A facility of this size will have room for more functions than just the telescope; the question is what those additional functions should be. The Mwalim have presented the relatively straightforward proposal of establishing a university on the observatory grounds. The idea is that such a thing would be mutually beneficial for both facilities: the telescope would provide excellent hands-on data for prospective students, whereas the university would provide a steady stream of educated personnel capable of understanding said data. The Darwiiz, true to form, oppose this idea, believing that such a thing would benefit only the upper class of the capital rather than the people as a whole. Instead, they propose that the observatory be equipped with living quarters and facilities capable of accommodating pilgrims from all four corners of the realm, so that all who make the journey to see through the telescope have a place to stay. We could try and implement both suggestions, but there are limits to how big the observatory can reasonably be, so it wouldn't serve either purpose as effectively.
