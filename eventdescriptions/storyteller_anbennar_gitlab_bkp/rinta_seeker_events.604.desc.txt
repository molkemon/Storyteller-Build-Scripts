The Mwalim and one of their schools have acquired a tool from a distant land, which the Gawakhizans who brought it have misnamed. They called upon you to be present to analyze the new device. Instead of detailing its specifics, they emphasized its potential: a tool designed to advance knowledge and improve analysis across various fields of study. Clearly, this invention benefited everyone involved in the faith.

Mages in the court began to debate fiercely, arguing over who should test the device. Some made absurd claims to justify their right to test it. Regardless, scholars from Badabbi to Weggabiga cannot agree on who should receive the funding to purchase more of these devices. Clearly out of sync, all sides look towards any governing voice for guidance. Maybe you could help them, sire? What do you think?
