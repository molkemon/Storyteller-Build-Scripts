Our recruitment efforts for explorers have been lamentably scattershot as clans, the naval staff, the mercantile class, and the dockyards fight over who in their midst is suitable for the position. Amidst this general ferment, one explorer has risen to the top, forcing all the competing parties to admit to their merit. urvion Wavedancer is a famous merchant captain and a notorious eccentric who once boasted a troll for a crew member. He has come to the court to pay his respects and offer service to their lord.
