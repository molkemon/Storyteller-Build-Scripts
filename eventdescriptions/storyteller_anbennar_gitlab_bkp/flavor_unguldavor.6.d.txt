While we have been fortunate, the orks in the South haven't been so lucky. Rosande has been trying to capture every ork possible to enslave them while Marrhold has been on an extermination campaign in the east. What few orks survived have been pushed to the edges. A nearby nation is such an example. Currently, they are under threat. If we were to position our diplomacy correctly, we could justify all lands owned by them with oppressed ork populations being rightfully part of that nation. Alternatively, we could just lay claim to a vast swath of the south. These claims are, of course, extremely tenuous. However, it would give us land to cede to a southern vassal that would answer to us. The choice is ours now. Do we support our southern brothers directly, or just conquer for now?
