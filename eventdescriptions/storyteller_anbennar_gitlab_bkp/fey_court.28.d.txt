It should have been a normal day in our capital, or at least as normal as the Deepwoods would allow. Yet, this small blessing would be denied when our lord entered into the audience chamber for, upon stepping inside, a plethora of obnoxious noises rang out heralding the sudden shower of brightly colored confetti. "Welcome to the Welcome Welcome Back Party." a dozen fey blared out. The lord jumped back with a start. "The what?" It was a question that went unanswered and unnoticed as the group of fey busily broke apart to reveal a vaguely familiar figure. "Heeeellooo, my dear progenitor. I've returned home." Our lord's eyes widened, "My child?" "The one and the saaameee." they laughed, stiding forward for a hug. A hug that was readily given, to the "awws" of the present fey.
