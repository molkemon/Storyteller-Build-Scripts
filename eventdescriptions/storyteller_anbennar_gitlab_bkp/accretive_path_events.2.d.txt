As our heir enters adolescence and grows closer to adulthood, the Chi masters of the palace whisper that they are one of the most devout children they have ever seen. Constantly striving to improve and free themselves from distractions that could hinder their cultivation, they have come to you with a request: to undergo a formative experience to root their future rule in practice, not just principle. Advisors debate which path to choose: should the heir walk among taxmen, ride with caravans, or train at the side of seasoned warriors? Wherever they go, they will carry the teachings of the Accretive Path, and learn something of their own in turn, but you will need to guard them well.
