To even see Marutha in person is a tall ask, for the journey is by design precarious; only those who really, truly care to do so would even attempt to risk their lives in such a way. Their ruler seems to have been recognized by Marutha; for they are able to step through the sandstorm, past sunscorched skeletons and stand before the great lion, the spirit observing back with a dispassionate expression. Finally the philosopher king asks with a deep, booming voice from beyond his veil of sand. "What is it you desire, their ruler?"
