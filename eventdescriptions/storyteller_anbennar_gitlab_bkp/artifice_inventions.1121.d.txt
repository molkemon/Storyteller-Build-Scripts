The mechanised administrative bureau is truly a unique invention, borne from the marriage of bureaucratic diligence and artificer genius. It is not merely a singular device or engine, no. The entire office forms one interconnected machine that can perform automated bureaucratic tasks without much if any oversight by a living operator.  This marvel of recordkeeping and administrative pertinacity has allowed the establishment of standardised documentation procedures across entire realms, cutting down on personnel costs and administrative waste while providing ever more detailed insights in the current state of a town, province or branch of government. Any realm employing these wonders of dedicated bureaucracy can expect a noticeable increase in the efficacy of their administration.
