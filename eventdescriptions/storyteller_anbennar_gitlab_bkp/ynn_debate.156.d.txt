A proclamation has come down from their leader, recommending the thorough examination of Cannorian thought. While their ideas may not originate from another culture, the Cannorians are clearly not barbarians. The fact that these new ideas have spread through our lands, bringing innovation and prosperity, is proof enough that they are blessed by a higher power. While this does not mean every single Cannorian innovation is good, and certainly does not mean they should supersede our own traditions, we should not fear these ideas just because they are unknown to us.
