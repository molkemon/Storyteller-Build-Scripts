Bah. How the simpleminded bite the hand that feeds. Despite how much we’ve done for the simple Ynnics, they still cling to their false idols and they still cling to their mages. Even though they conspired for centuries to crush such tyrants, evidently the Ynnics enjoy the pressure of a boot on their neck too overmuch to abandon them.  A major rebellion, made up of equal parts maleficar, former nobility, and common ruinborn, has brewed to a bubbling point all across our lands. If we do not send in the Red Sentries now, then these lands could be lost to us entirely. Enough with the soft touch; now is time for the club and the blade.
