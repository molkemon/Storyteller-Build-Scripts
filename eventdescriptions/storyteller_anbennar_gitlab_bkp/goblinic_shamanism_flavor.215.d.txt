Goblin ingenuity knows no bounds. Since we are so smart, it logically follows that the situation our race finds itself in is of our own creation, so claims a prominent member of the Cult of Reason. He asserts that the dwarven race was in fact created by goblins, with the ultimate goal of having them create the myriad holds and tunnels that we now call home. Miraculously, despite their usual dogged pursuit of farfetched ideas, even the majority of the Cult of Reason stands opposed to this proposition. Despite this our support could get the idea some traction.
