We could not contain Hireet, nor find Komina. In no small measure, we Deathsworn have failed.  But Ilfanil is still within our reach. From their towers, our cloudmages have woven a web of light and protection, leaving only a handful of dark groves as an avenue of escape into the Shadow Plane, forcing Granny to take a path of our choosing. Along that route, our squads lie in ambush. Covered in mud, leaves, and illusions, we await the hag's approach.  We wait and wait in silence, lying still as corpses, rationing our breaths. We grip our weapons tight, taking care not to tremble. Surprise and unrelenting ferocity will guarantee our victory.
