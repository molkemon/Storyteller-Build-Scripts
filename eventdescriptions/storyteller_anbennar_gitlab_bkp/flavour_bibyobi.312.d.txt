"It's interesting how fast one can wear out a welcome. One day, the people of Garanke welcome us with open arms and we return the favor with open coin purses. Now that we are knocking down their doors, ransacking their larders, and flipping their beds in search of men that can turn into cats, we are all of the sudden no longer welcome. Oh well, comes with the territory of working for the crown, I guess. It does seem that these rather unsubtle methods of searching are beginning to bear fruit, though, and not just the tasty melons I keep taking from all the larders."; Ralambo Tallfriend, Page to the Esteemed Leader of the Bibyobi Monster Hunters
