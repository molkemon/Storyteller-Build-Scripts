After several days of torture, much to his credit, the shop owner finally revealed the identity of the one who purchased the orb found in Samartal.  The man was just an accomplice of the true group who perpetrated the attack and did not directly participate himself, but hopefully he will have useful information on where to find the others. His home was found in a town a few miles from Samartal and was swiftly raided. He has been brought to the dungeons for questioning.
