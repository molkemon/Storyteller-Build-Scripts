Despite our usual tactics for consolidating rule over new land—raiding, random executions, cultural suppression, sowing division, and so on—it seems the wretched elves still have some fight left in them. A former Ibevari lieutenant named Liandiel has started a rebellion in Carodir's domain. She's already incited the peasants to arms, and their rallying cry is "Elves never forget evil!" Well, let us make sure elves never forget the screams that will echo through the pass when we're done with these savages.
