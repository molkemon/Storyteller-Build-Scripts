As if we weren’t already in an unfortunate situation after Asarta broke our family’s bonds, we got a message from Arazin. Under the guise of offering themselves as a barrier against a possible Yushyni rebellion, they demand access to the provinces of Tsaratokh and Verthica.  While their arguments are valid, they are fully aware how much we’ve tried to centralize our holds to make a unified push against Anzara. To make matters worth, they indirectly suggested this is a matter worth breaking our alliance over.  Can’t they see the bigger picture? They just put us in a loselose situation: either succumb and risk the other parties following up with their demands or decline and damage the stability of our coalition further.
