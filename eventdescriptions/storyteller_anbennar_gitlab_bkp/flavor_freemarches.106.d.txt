After more than a year with no word from the expedition, a recovery mission was sent to investigate their fate. As they crossed the mountains, they found evidence of a rudmen camp, and nearby, the body of a rudman captain. In his pockets, they found a logbook, and in his fist, a strange stone.  His logbook details how, when his men attempted to arrest Obram Orannam, the gnome grabbed a rock from the snow and smacked a rudman across the head with it. The gnome then ran off into the blizzard, leaving the rock behind.  The survivors then attempted to escape the blizzard and cross back into the Freemarches, but they only got as far as the mountains, before being caught in yet another snowstorm.  In the hand of the rudman captain was a strange tablet, presumably the rock from the eye of the blizzard. Strange symbols tracked across its surface.
