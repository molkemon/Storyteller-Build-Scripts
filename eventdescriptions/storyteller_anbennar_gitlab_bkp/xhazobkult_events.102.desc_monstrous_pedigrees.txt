While onlookers typically characterize the Xhazobkult as destructionloving demonworshipers, this is not always the reality. In our region, the summoning and worship of demons is not as prominent, with the spiritual Kult instead being focused on emulating the great gnollish rulers of old. But even in our state, the taint of the Kult's more demonic side is still present, if diminished and hidden away. We have recently heard of a warlock in a nearby province preparing a ritual to conjure a powerful demon into our world. The potential dangers of such a ritual are worrisome, but the power of a demon is tempting.
