A Master Thief and leading member of the skilled Thieves Guild has grown old and seeks to settle down safely. They are no longer the young daring thief that threatened our vaults, but now an aged veteran with a family to think of. They have a long and storied history, and have suggested they can serve us in all matters of guile and subterfuge, if we are able to help guarantee the safety of their loved ones. Of course, they are also willing to give up their accomplices, and remove their threat once and for all.
