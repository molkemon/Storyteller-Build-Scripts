Chaos grips our government. More than it usually does, at least. Our previous ruler, presumed dead for half a year, has returned to us at last. It seems that the Prefectorium Parlay's hasty announcement was made in the midst of our old leader's midday nap. Rudely awakened from their beauty sleep, it has taken them six months to convince their own guards that they are who they say they are, and they're now demanding we restore their position of leadership immediately. While their claim to power is technically legitimate, so is that of our current leader, placing the Prefectorium into something of a selfinflicted conundrum.
