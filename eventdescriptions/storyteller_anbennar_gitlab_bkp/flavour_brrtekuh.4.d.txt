The gnoll bowed before their lord, his fur drenched in sweat, chest heaving as he panted against the desert sun. It was a sweltering day; but the heat was only doubled by the exertion of his deeds: for this gnoll had proven himself before Kvanga. Once, the Trial of the Reforged Chain was only for humans; those who wished to become like gnolll. But this pack had moved beyond that; for it was no longer the gnollish children of Kvanga they needed; yearned; to prove themselves to. Their lord rose, and gave their blessing.
