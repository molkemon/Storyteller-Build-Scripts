My lord, concerning reports have reached us from the provinces. Apparently, a foreign power has begun to offer their support to the guerillas plaguing our lands; no doubt in an effort to weaken us for their own malicious ends. Worse still are the rumours that the smugglers sent to provide weapons and other supplies to the guerillas are being aided by some of our people in a certain province to conceal their movements. Unfortunately, no definitive proof of the latter has emerged, though this is of little concern as it is merely the lower castes being accused. How should we proceed?
