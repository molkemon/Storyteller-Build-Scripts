The arrival of a crying child at birth is cause for celebration, as it signifies the gift of health. In this province, this joyous occasion has been amplified by a remarkable phenomenon: a prolonged period without any stillbirths or miscarriages. The absence of such tragedies has led to a rising population and filled the hearts of parents with happiness and joy. The people firmly believe they are under the protective watch of Yinti, the guardian of the pregnant. This divine protection has brought about not only the blessing of a growing population but also an abundance of joy and hope among the people. As the province experiences this period of sustained prosperity, it promises a bright and promising future ahead. The increased population is indeed a boon for the region, contributing to its growth and development. This providential intervention by Yinti has fostered a sense of gratitude and reverence among the people, reaffirming their faith in the benevolent forces that safeguard and nurture life.
