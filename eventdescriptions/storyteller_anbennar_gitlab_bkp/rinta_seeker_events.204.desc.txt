Ten years ago, demons scorched the lands of the ancient realm of Ardimya. A land destroyed out of spite. An ugly war with consequences borne by uninvolved descendants. What kind of a foolish tale is that? Someone has to write its end. As the end draws near, and the dark magicks have been completely studied, the coalition of mages feels ready to end the nightmare and save Ardimya. The caravans walk... walk... Days end... slaughter bells toll. Walk... Days begin... slaughter bells toll. After so much sacrifice, the end of many demons, and the loss of many friends, the mages begin their ritual...
