The atmosphere in the royal palace of Medurubar was most peculiar on this memorable day. Many amongst those gathered to witness the ascension of our new monarch still wore their garbs of mourning, the thought of having lost the wise guidance of exalted Taelarios still unbearable for them. As for some others, a grim determination to endure whatever fate has next in store for them and their beloved realm was carved into their mien. And finally, on some few faces a cautious hope shone, a hope that this was not the last chapter of Irrliam's story, but the beginning of an entirely new tale of a glorious rise to heretofore unimaginable heights.  The grief, worries and; worst of all; hopeful expectations of the assembled notables of the realm bore down heavily on the untested shoulders of our ruler, but they were grimly determined to prove to all of them that they were a worthy inheritor of the noble legacy left by the previous rulers of Irrliam, grand Taelarios himself first among them.  As the gilded Ember Crown was finally placed on the new monarch's head, an expectant silence filled the throne room, for our ruler was about to make their first royal proclamation. Would they renew the vendetta with Elizna, avenging the slaughter of Medurubar? Would they continue the careful policy of Taelarios, cultivating alliances to secure the peace and invest the treasury into developing the realm? Or was their mind captivated by the dream of a Phoenix Empire reborn?
