The people of the settlement grow weary of the constant presence of the most righteous inquisition. Allegations have reached the Crimson Reverend that certain inquisitors are working with a local judge to target and burn their political enemies as suspected witches. If we interfere with the inquisition, there will almost definitely be severe repercussions. However, if the situation isn't solved, then the people may rise up against us. It's up to the infinite wisdom of the Crimson Reverend.
