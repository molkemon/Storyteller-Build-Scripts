A great host of pilgrims has arrived from their realm! Pious kobolds have seen their lives destroyed, their homes seized, their temples wrecked, and their hoards looted... yet still they come to our capital, contributing everything they still have to our hoard. In this procession, foreign dragon-priests trade stories with our own; kobolds of all hues sing in solidarity and triumph; koboldkind is one, united in our capital, united in their love for the dragons. Here, in the caverns, we shall not be beaten. Here, in the caverns, we shall not be destroyed! To Malliath, Tayekan, Nimrith, and Iotharal, and every other dragon in Halann!
