For over a century, Grombar was defined by the divide between orks and humans. All affairs it seemed were at least to some degree split along racial lines, and the lingering distrust between the two groups never seemed to completely fade. In recent years, however, a third group, the halforks, have been on the rise. No longer a minority, the halforks have reached and surpassed the populations of humans and orks alike. Every day, it becomes clearer and clearer that ours is no longer a nation of orks nor humans, but a union of both, and what better symbol of unity than a people born of the two? It is well past time we make it official; Grombar is a land for ork and human alike, and the first place in Cannor that halforks can truly call home.
