If there is one thing that the Fall of the Precursors and Day of Ashen Skies has shown to the world, then it is that destroying things is far easier than building something. A fact that was proven to us again and again since our reawakening. How many of our kin did we lose to the ravages of time? How many friends devoured by hostile flora and fauna or killed in pointless wars? "Enough!" we say, for our hands shall not be those of war and slaughter. No, ours shall be the hands of an enlightened age, one of building and restoration. Our swords shall become saws, our maces hammers, and our spears drills until the day there is nothing more to be built and every soul lost has a monument dedicated to them. Perhaps until even the assorted gods of Halann have no choice but to acknowledge our work. For the stronger we build our houses, the less chance we have of being killed.
