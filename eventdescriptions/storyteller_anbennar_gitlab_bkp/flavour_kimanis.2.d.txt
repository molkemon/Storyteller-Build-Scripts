So saith THE MOB:  The space in our new theatre is cramped, And yet we stand among the crush of friends. In hope of kindling mourning fires, we hear Our DEMAGOGUE hosts tragedy this eve  ‘The Anguish of Thrysimache’, it’s called, And to oblige our DEMAGOGUE we came. It promises a night to overawe. We do not know why we have left our wine.  What spectacle could we observe tonight That daily life does not for us provide? On second thought, the actors’ magics there provide no lacking feast for weary eyes.
