The country of the Serpentspine was unknown to Triunics until recently, when our scouts were able to make contact with the vast, continent-spanning cave networks, centered around a collapsed Dwarven domain. While this place contains vast wealth, the races there—dwarves, goblins, and orcs—all hate each other and constantly war for control over great dwarven citadels and the caves around them. If we wish to spread democracy even there, we have to deal with these tensions.
