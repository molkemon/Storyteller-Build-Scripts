It is done. Our lord Riárchis has become a Kylakas, but not of the normal sort – a ruler of a true democracy, a government of the citizens, not the oligarchs! The scholar, not the librarian! Across our realm, the people cry out in joy and celebration, as a thousand years of foreign rule comes to an end. Soon, the time will come when the dream of our founder Varamelas may be fulfilled. Soon, but not now, for a storm brews on the horizon – though it is a storm we have not only foreseen, but one we welcome. Even as the ink on our declaration of a new government dries, our former overlord Lokeimeion is writing a similar declaration – a declaration of war! Their soldiers and galleys gather, and will soon set sail against us, but they do so alone. Just as we have planned, none of the other great realms speak of intervention. Though they will undoubtedly come for us once we are outside the Lokebhen aegis, that is a war for a different day. Today, we fight for freedom, for democracy, and for Besolakí!
