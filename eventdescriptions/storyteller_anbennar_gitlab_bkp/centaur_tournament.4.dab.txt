As our lord steps into the arena wielding the Gaohernlangh, a massive curved greatsword with a long wooden handle, favored mainly due to its destructive slashing power, they hear a familiar voice on the opposite end of the arena. "Beating you will be my next triumph, Caehn." The voice belonged to the clansman with the large ear necklace, collected from their past enemies. Scoffing at their insolenence, our lord charges at the accomplished warrior, however it seems that they fell right into the warrior's trap. Easily dodging the initial charge and taking initiative, the warrior masterfully tore into Caehn's defense. Feeling that victory is close, the warrior pushed a bit too far, and Caehn used it to their advantage. Now with even ground, and fatigue setting in, the fight seemed to be in a stalemate, but eventually.
