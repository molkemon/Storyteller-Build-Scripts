Despite the tales of buried treasure and hidden riches, one must remember what the old slave warrens were built for – to house slaves, deprived of anything remotely worthwhile. Most of the treasure that is found in slave warrens is there because after the Ruin, the revolting slaves seized much of their masters’ wealth and hid it below ground, in order to protect it from roving bandits or the ravages of war. In places where that did not happen, the ruins are likely to be just as destitute as their former inhabitants were.

Unfortunately, this is the case with our recent warren expedition – despite searching high and low, and excavating several collapsed sections of the ruins, they have not found anything of value. It seems the venture is a failure.
