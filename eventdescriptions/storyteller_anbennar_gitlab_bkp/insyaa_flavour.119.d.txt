For many who prefer to fight mounted on another creature, the slow decline of cavalry in the face of the rising popularity of rifles and cannons has been a source of dismay. Their glorious charges have been replaced by futile endeavors that throw away the life of both rider and mount for very little gain. But after witnessing the wonders of Insyaa, it seems some of our explorers and researchers have come up with an idea to improve on their fading prominence. Simply making the mounts really, really big. According to them, this move would not only make individual mounts more resistant to rifle and cannon fire, increase the devastation wreaked by our charges, and require fewer mounts to achieve the same effectiveness for our units, but also allow us to post riflemen atop them to fire from above. Though we reckon that part was only added to appease those who see those as the future of warfare. Additionally, it is recommended that while the Titano-Growth is active, mounts are kept far away from the sleeping spots of our soldiers to ensure the smell of manure is not permanently affixed to them.
