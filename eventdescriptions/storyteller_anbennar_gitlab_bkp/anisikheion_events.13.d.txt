The monarch looks out from the balcony of their great palace, overlooking Anisikheion's great harbor. They see a most glorious sight. Hundreds of warships anchored in port, the greatest navy Aelantier has ever seen. The monarch reminisces about Anisikheion's rise, for it is a story of Anisikheion’s navy. Were it not for its navy, it would still be a backwater island settlement. Were it not for the navy, Anisikheion would still be dominated by disloyal merchants and land lovers. Were it not for the navy, Anisikheion would not have become the 8th great kheion, able to challenge and ultimately subjugate the other great kheions. The monarch snaps out of their thoughts, and returns inside to a meeting with their admirals, for there are always more threats across the sea, not least of which being these so called "Cannorian'' newcomers. But whatever happens going forward, the navy has given Anisikheion a place in the sun, one that it will defend to the last ship.
