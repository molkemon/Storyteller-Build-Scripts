The bird emerges from the darkness of night, from the corner of the Vile One's domain. It is a shadow itself, and where its wings flap, it clears the Vile One's fog. The letter unfurls; there is no need to read it, for the words are whispered directly into the Vile One's mind, the voice of an advisor cold and absolute. "No disappointment to us still lives upon Halann, save one. Goodbye, stupid girl."
