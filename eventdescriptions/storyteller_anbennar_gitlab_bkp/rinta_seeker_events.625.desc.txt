The third expedition's planning is underway! The options for us are... The Mengi city of Fetengoni. The entire realm named after the city is one of the longest-lasting in the history of Halann; they claim they've been around since the Great Procession! The amount of history and culture that must have developed here would not only be monumental in understanding the Mengi but the world as a whole. They have a generous culture towards outsiders, meeting their needs to demonstrate splendor.

The Lupulan city of Kuta. The merfolk suggested this one in particular, as the area surrounding Kuta is known for being an astounding port and a cozy vacation retreat. The Lupulan as a region is home to a variety of exotic plants and species that haven't been studied before. The high presence of spiritual energy makes this a keen area to explore. The people of Kuta are known to be inviting of all new ideas, particularly any to stave off conquest from its larger neighbors.
