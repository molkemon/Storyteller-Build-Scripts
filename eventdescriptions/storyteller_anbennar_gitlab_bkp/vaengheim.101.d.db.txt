It was in the days of today when our queen ruled, and her maidens struck forth across the Giant’s Grave, bringing fire and sword to the Reach, making subjects of those who bent knee to Vængheim, and slaying those who did not. Yet the men of the Alen resisted them, first in battle, and then in spirit. For our queen was not loved by the Reach, and they plotted against her, seeking her ruin. One day, a wingmaiden flew to Vængheim, and in her claws held a man named Godric, who said, "O queen, foul tidings do I bring. For I am a Reachman, and have pledged myself to your house. Yet do my kinsmen strike at me, and they have burned my home and taken from me herd, kith and kin. I come alone, but I speak with the tongues of many, who speak your name fondly, though they are shamed and slain by their neighbours." Then our queen rose and said, "Have no fear, Reachman, for when I took your people under my wing, I did not take them into thralldom. Your people are my people, and I will take arms to defend those who carry my peace in their hearts." And then our queen gathered the maidens of Vængheim for war.
