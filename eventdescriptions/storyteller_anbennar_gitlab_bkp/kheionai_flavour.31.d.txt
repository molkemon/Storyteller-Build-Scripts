The messenger stumbles into our council hall, out of breath from avoiding the army approaching the gates of the city. The council is outraged, not knowing there was even such an army outside until now. The messenger explains that communication has broken down outside the capital. The tyrant has seized on our ignorance to amass an army of mercenaries, pirates, and fellow aristocrats to rebel for the office of Kylakas! We must act swiftly to put this revolt down, though the army is smaller due to his supporters being pacified by our leniency. It leaves the remaining populace baffled by our incompetence and permitting such a revolt to arise, questioning our legitimacy in the face of these actions.
