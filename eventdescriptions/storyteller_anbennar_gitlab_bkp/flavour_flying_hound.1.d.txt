Out on the desolate roads of Godsescker sits a tavern that has been left to ruin. But life stirs around this rundown shack. A host of dog riders has gathered here eagerly awaiting the opportunity to join what they believe is a bright path forward. Bursting forth from the tavern door, a goblin clad in leather armor embroidered with a winged dog on its back, accompanied by an entourage of goblins wearing similar leathers. The crowd cheers in a series of howls as Elgek, the new clanboss of the Flying Hound clan stands before them. He looks upon all those who have rallied to his cause and shouts,  "HOUNDS..." The crowd grows silent. "Korgus is dead…" The crowd erupts into celebratory cheers but Elgek quickly shushes that.  ".and nothing has changed," Elgek pauses before continuing. "We are free just as we have always been since the day we stepped foot outside those damned mountains."  "You see, in those caves we were caged in. Cramped halls always leave you backed in a corner, easy prey to those who would see us in chains."  "But here? There's more than enough space. It's so open no one could possibly catch us. Not a horse, warg or spider could match the speeds of our dogs."  "And that's the truth, we have been free for decades now. But only with Korgus’ death can we see that it was fear that held us back all those years. As long as we have had our rides, we have been free in this land."  "So Let’s finally show these orks that we are not slaves. Let’s show these adventurers that we belong in this land. And let's stop behaving like cowards and show that when you back a Flying Hound into a corner, you get bit."  Cheers erupt as Elgek makes his way from the tavern entrance through the crowd, receiving slaps on the back from his fellow riders along the way, towards his ride. Hopping on, Elgek lets out a final rallying cry.
