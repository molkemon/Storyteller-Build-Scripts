The Oren Nayiru have spread like wildfire through the peasant, slave, and serf populations of Taychend, as many increasingly see the Kavalali warrior-priests as their only means of escaping the brutal reality of their lives. Cells of the Oren Nayiru—operational chapters led by the Kavalali—have spread throughout Taychend. As the faith expands, its traditional centers in Uesrennu and Katelperai have been augmented by new strongholds. In this realm, one such new stronghold has arisen, and though the exact location of the cell is not known, it has already begun projecting power across the surrounding regions.
