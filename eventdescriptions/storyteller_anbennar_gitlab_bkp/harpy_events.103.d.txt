Our favored daughter is young and still has a lot to learn about life and how to be a good ruler. In the court of the ruler, some suggest that she may gather this experience by being tasked with ruling a province. In addition, this would reinforce her legitimacy and so reduce the chance of a future succession struggle. However, others caution that her inexperience should be rectified closer to home and that she should remain in the court of the ruler so that she may be supervised by those who know her. Isolated and left to their own devices, she may develop premature political ambitions if allowed to rule unsupervised.
