When word from the explorers who first saw Graxarr Temple from the sea reached the government, they were ecstatic: A large Precursor structure still standing? It was an alluring sight, our older folk remembered how rare and rich those vessels carrying alleged Precursor Relics were. And now that we were in this region, we would be getting the real stuff right from the source. Now that the isle is firmly within our grasp, an expedition has been organised to find out just how much bounty is hidden within. This is their report: Deep in the jungle of Graxarr, a circular array of towers dominates the landscape, with not even a single vine having grown on them. At the base of the towers is a floor made of solid white stone which gives way to multiple winding staircases, as well as gilded doors leading inside the towers. While said doors were easy enough to yank off, most of the doors inside have proven too thick to break through with our current equipment, resulting in our exploration teams coming to dead end after dead end. Eventually we found a hallway with open doors, which led us to a large room in which we recovered various trinkets. One of the captains who was with us said it reminded her of the cargo she once seized from a ship headed to another land, and went on to entertain the crew with the tale of her exploit on the way back to our capital. We could not make more progress as further exploitation seems to require mining equipment. What should be done with the relics?
