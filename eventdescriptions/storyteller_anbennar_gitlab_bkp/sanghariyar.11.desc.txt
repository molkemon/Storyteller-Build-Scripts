"When Ramapalar subjugated us back in 1333, I imagine he could never have imagined this.", our ruler said as they looked out over Dhenijansar from their vantage point. The columns of soldiers pouring through the breaches in the walls, opened by cannons forged from Shenrali steel. Staring at the golden palace, standing opulent over the city in the distance, our ruler spoke softly, "First, we punished our brothers and sisters for deserting us all those years ago. Now, we will punish those who enslaved us." With a cold look in their eyes, they turned to their general, "Burn it. Burn it all.", and turned from the field as the clashing of swords rang out and men screamed.
