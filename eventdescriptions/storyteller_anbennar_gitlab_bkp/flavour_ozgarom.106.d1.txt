How often the sweet wine of victory turns to ash in one's mouth! Bramash 'Chainbreaker', the rebel orc who rose in rebellion against the slave-estates of Endralliande, has gone from strength to strength. Sweeping across the island, he has preserved the bulk of his forces despite the utter naval superiority of our lord. Even from an apparent position of strength, however, Bramash has been forced to recognize the bleakness of his outlook. Surrounded on all sides by hostile waters and home to a plantation economy utterly reliant on continual export and import, the island of Endralliande is no fortress. The orcs cannot expect to build a lasting state in such an exposed position, and they are unsurprisingly reluctant to rely on the patronage of a sympathetic power—in the unlikely event that one volunteers itself.

Thus, Bramash has loaded his forces onto the ramshackle surviving fleet of Endralliande and put to sea, making for the mainland of Soruin and the enfolding green shield of the endless jungle. One might call it a very successful round of looting, if the goods the orcs were taking were not things they had made themselves...
