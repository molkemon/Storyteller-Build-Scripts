Between the nomads and the tlalusk, it was unclear exactly who violated whose territorial sovereignty; in fact, it was likelier that the nomads had impinged on the beast's stomping ground than the other way around. The sympathies of the Dauvek Veker, however, were very clear: they lay with the human men, and not with the sixlegged, aggressively horned shoutbeast.  Being experienced hunters, the titanium hunters had plugged their ears with layers of cloth to dampen the monster's sonic attacks, and established a complex system of hand signals to use to coordinate their strategies against the creature. Being experienced melee combatants, advanced strategies were scarcely necessary, and Branma needed to give just one command to her troops.  "Kill."
