Across every tavern in our land, tipsy and far more than tipsy adventurers regale triumphs of the 'Wayward Hero'. Each tale begins the same, that of our heir seeking a calling beyond the one their duties demanded. But from there, they diverge. Did our ruler give them their blessing when they set off? Was the dragon really the size of an inn? Was it actually a dragon or just a wyvern breathing fire? Did it only take one strike to lay the creature low? Regardless of the answers, the end always remained the same.
