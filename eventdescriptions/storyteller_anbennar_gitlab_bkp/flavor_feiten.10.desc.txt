A lord sits under a silken awning, facing the open skies overlooking Feiten bay. The party, gathered to witness a demonstration of the new scouting corps, is restless, but all quiets once the signal is given for the balloons to launch. Perfectly coordinated, they rise as one, and soon shrink to the size of those remora fish that swim in the wake of whales, insignificant against the backdrop of the clouds. In position, the lead balloon unfurls its signal banners down. Quickly, the signal ripples along the chain of balloons as each passes it on to the next in line. The flurry of activity is almost too much to keep track of for the onlookers, but each balloon keeps order and discipline, coordinating their artificial stringers perfectly. Months of training and building, spearheaded by the foremost military minds of the nation, and finally, the Shuimu Scouts are ready for action.
