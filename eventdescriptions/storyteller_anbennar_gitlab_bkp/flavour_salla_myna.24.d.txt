Negotiating the finer terms of the realm's unification took time, of course. Different lords had conditions they wished to be met for their groves, asked for specific court positions, and so on, and even after that had been settled, there were ceremonies and, of course, a parade, to mark that the Wood Elves were united once more. Bricena had not slept so little since the siege at the start of her reign, but after a month her new position was finally firmly established, and she could take the time to visit her father.
