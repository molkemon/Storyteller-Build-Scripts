For the nonmechanim we can be intimidating. Creatures of living metal must be so to those made of mere flesh, even more so if they are the size of the war mechanim. Others of our kind though can draw admiration, and few more so than the Soarers. Who does not wish to take to the skies under their own power, even if only for a time. The fact that they are made of metal only strengthens that impression and their agile performances drive it home. Aerial feats of skill honed over years, decades or even longer, draw crowds of mechanim and nonmechanim alike in shared admiration.
