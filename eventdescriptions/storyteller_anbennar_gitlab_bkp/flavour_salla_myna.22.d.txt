During victory celebrations in Fealgarn's capital, Oberon offers a few tips on how the Hawk Riders could be better deployed against the Goblins and Orcs. Somehow, news of this reaches Ayethstir, as Elaria shows up not long after, glaring daggers at Oberon and having every intention of showing him up. The impromptu competition between them results in the development of the so-called "Arrowstorm Doctrine" by combining the ideas of the two military masterminds.
