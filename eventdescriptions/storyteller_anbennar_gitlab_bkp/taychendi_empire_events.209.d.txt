Since we looted the tomb of Liandarha, we have been gradually selling off the gold and damasteel salvaged from the ruined slave collars, selling them to buyers across the realm of Taychend. After years of profit, we have finally made our final sales. Nakar Kriadvai, which had thrived from the salvaging operations for years, can now return to its previous trade of scavenging Precursor relics from the Nakari Underground.
