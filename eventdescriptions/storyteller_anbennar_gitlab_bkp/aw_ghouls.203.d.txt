When the dead number in the thousands, and the carrion crows fill the air, the ghouls come upon the land and create a Corpsefield. Roaming freely and feasting on the halfeaten corpses, a Corpsefield is a place anathema to mortals, more a realm of the dead than the living, where predatory monsters and disease abound. The longunresolved ghoul issue in our nation has manifested in the presence of many such locations scattered around our realm, their presence confirmed by adventurous scouts. Dread fills the hearts of folk everywhere as news spreads, and travel has ground to a near stop in many places as folk hunker down and desperately hope to avoid the notice of the ghoul menace.
