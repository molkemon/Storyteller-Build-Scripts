For better or for worse, Castonath is a multiracial and multiethnic city. Though not as diverse as Anbenncost, which is said to have representatives from nearly every race and culture on Halann, Castonath is certainly one of the most diverse cities in Escann. The rubbing of different races and peoples often causes conflicts, however, especially between the proud Castonath patricians and those they view as the despoilers of their city. Not a day goes by without another fresh batch of complaints to the lord about the halforkish, goblin, or orkish quarters of the city, though they know better than to go any further than complaints.
