Rushing into the central chamber of the council, the messenger exclaimed: "They accept. They will pledge themselves to our protection and support." A short second went over when the council erupted in cheers. A bottle of the finest brandy was opened and a celebration was held. From now on, both cliff and bay are under our guidance and patronage. It will not be necessary to fully annex or dominate our new partner, though new common laws will be made to make the flow of trade between our realms go even smoother.
