Borders are organic things, changing and mutating with the seasons, and with the fortunes of the latest conqueror passing by. Historians will, perhaps, say that the events that transpired in a region in the year current, were inevitable. 'As the region continued to flourish under the benevolent rule of a nation, the people on the other side of the border were bound to get jealous.' Perhaps they will be less fair to us. Either way, tensions in a region have given our rivals a reason for war.
