A woman clad in silk robes strides into the court, fuming. She points a finger at our ruler and accuses them of ruining years of well-planned diplomacy and trade deals negotiated by the burghers with our ruler's most recent policy blunder. She folds her arms and seethes, before giving an ultimatum. Allow the burghers exclusive access to the marketplaces of Yanshen under our realm’s control, or lose their trade expertise in the region. Accepting their demands would have serious consequences. Their influence and autonomy in the region would be increased, and they could easily funnel funds away from the crown. On the other hand, angering them will cause a drastic drop in the profitability of the region while new administrators and traders without pre-existing connections find their footing.
