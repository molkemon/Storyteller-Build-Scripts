Ever since the newcomer appeared at court, they have been a mystery to our own elite class. Nobody knows their name nor has been able to find information about this secretive person. As time went on, the noble became friends with the titled courtier. They prove themselves skillful in the matters of administration, diplomacy and the military, and are in general well received in the court. As our lord still lacks a proper heir to the throne, the choice is not too difficult to select the noble as the next in line of succession.
