The devastation of the past months in our lands have taken a major toll on the population. While life in the Shadow Swamp is never easy, when war and famine come the trials and tribulations of the residents increase tenfold. The swamp rarely aids its denizens. There are times where the swamp does provide, though. Death Vultures, seemingly normal vultures that have mutated from contact with the shadows of the swamp to spew necrotic breath, have arrived to clean up the death and destruction from our fields. These beasts will even consume the flesh of the trolls.
