Lately all sorts of whispers, stories and scandals – true or made up – follow our lord. From a report of cowardice from when they were a youngster, to illegitimate children suddenly appearing everywhere, to tall tales of them consorting with beasts and monsters, their name is on everybody’s lips, and never accompanied by flattering words. The ruler’s closest friends and confidants are all in agreement that all this is a consequence of that prophecy from a vassal’s capital, and their refusal to bend to it.
