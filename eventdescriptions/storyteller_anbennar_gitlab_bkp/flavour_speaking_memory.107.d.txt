The exkeeper sat in the middle of a musty room, reading reports about increases and decreases in settlement taxation across the realm. The kipu were no less numerous here, but somehow, this just felt less important. Money was temporary, but the afterlife was (or should be) forever. If no one was reading the scrolls, the afterlife would be temporary. With a sigh, the keeper returned to reading, and making notes to himself every now and then.
