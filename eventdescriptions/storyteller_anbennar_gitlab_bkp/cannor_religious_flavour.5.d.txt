Balgar the Builder, the dwarfturnedgod who gave us such wonders as the Walls of Castanor, has once again turned his eye towards war. With Corin leading the Regent Court, new engines of war are needed to spread the true faith, and Balgar has bestowed such terribly ingenious machines on his loyal Dragonfire followers. These followers of Balgar have seen the righteousness of Corin's cause and have devoted their engineering prowess to share her truth. Should this be in the form of siege engines, or more domestic concerns?
