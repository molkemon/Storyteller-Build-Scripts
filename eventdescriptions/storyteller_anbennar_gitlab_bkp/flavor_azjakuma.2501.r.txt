The state of oni with the Shuvuush might be summarized in a single sentence: They would kill us all, if only given the opportunity. They could only be ruled over through proxy, small networks of informants that necessitated extortionate bribes being the only way to rule them with any degree of control. It is such a poor situation that even these informants are harassed, and any attempts to draft Shuvuush are met with hostility and nearoutright rebellion.
