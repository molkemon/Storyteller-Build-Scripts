The work of our ruler is usually based in the Lotus Court, after all, any actions here spread outward to influence all Rahen like a drop of ink in water. But sometimes a closer hand is necessary, and the destination seems to cry out for such intervention. A scandal has engulfed the head of the local ministries and all her advisors, and direct action from the source would see the destination through such a storm unscathed, and indeed it might even profit from better leadership. The question, of course is whom to send. The vizier, even though they may seek to use the influence in furthering their own interests? Some of our ruler’s closest advisors, though their wisdom would be missed at court? Or perhaps the destination can sink, or swim, on their own.
