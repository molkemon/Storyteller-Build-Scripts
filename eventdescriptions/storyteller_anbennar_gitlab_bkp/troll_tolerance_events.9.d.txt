Slowly, over many years, some of our people have begun to accept the presence of Swamp Trolls in our society. Separated from the influence of the Night Hags and the Shadow Swamp, some of them show; if not exactly kindness; a certain restraint in their actions. They are also capable of being quite industrious, with quite a few trolls finding success as artisans and merchants. One such troll merchant in the province has reported being continually harassed by locals who still feel hatred towards her people; her goods stolen, her home broken into, and her family members pelted with rotten fruit. Their response is understandable, but surely we must do something to maintain order.
