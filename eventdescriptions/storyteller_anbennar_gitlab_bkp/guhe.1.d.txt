Our efforts to build relations with the Harimari of Jiantsiang have borne fruit in a number of ways; not least of being information on the encroaching Shuuvish riders.  While we continue to have at least a nominal buffer between our lands and the enemy's, much of our leadership argues that it is only a matter of time before the coming hordes develop a border with our homes.  Our heartlands along the river and flat and open; easy ground for the foe to fight on. The first step in preparing our defense is to establish a favorable border, and the mountains of the Goldscale Kobolds to our north would be far easier to defend than the riverlands.
