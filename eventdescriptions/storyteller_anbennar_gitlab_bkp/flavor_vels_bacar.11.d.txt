My lord, my lord, the Brelari masters of the dams are here. Our lord did not leave the Halba Hariez, despite the session being one boring analysis of bridge tolls in Stanyrhrada and whether they follow the law. And there the Brelari masters found our lord. Their tales were listened to by the whole Panykorun, the members of their group interviewed separately for the details of their journey, and proof of their mastery on dambuilding requested right from the start. It all seemed to be right, down to the last comma, even fitting the meager mentions of architectural expertise in the Adbrabohvi scrolls: the ancient knowledge was saved in Brelar, where it survived for centuries as the rest of Ynnic society declined and fell to infighting.
