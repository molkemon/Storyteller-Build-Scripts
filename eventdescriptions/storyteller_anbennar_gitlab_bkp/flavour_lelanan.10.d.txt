I wore the robes of a High Priestess, thinking of Felana's sweet words to me. Standing before the great audience, I bent down onto one knee.

"Kyola Heartblade, hero that you are, you are here today because Lel has spoken. She has seen your actions—how you inspire hope, and now seeks to make you the leader of her people."

The audience cheered so loudly. I looked over them and smiled. My time spent back had paid off, and my victory was far from mild.

Many Priestesses stood around us, their support so hard to earn. But my great efforts had not been wasted for this position I did yearn.

"Kyola Heartblade!" the High Priestess continued, handing me the sign of her station so gently. "You are the Chosen of Lel; High Priestess, may you rule over us all intently."
