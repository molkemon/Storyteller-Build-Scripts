While sitting down to a midday meal today, our lord was confronted with the spirit of one of their great great great-grandfathers, who claims that the realm’s decadent and newfangled ways will destroy everyone involved unless they repent and begin undoing the last hundred or so years of reform. The ministers have begun investigating the possibility. Preliminary reports indicate that this would be a very bad idea indeed, and that the realm is not likely to be destroyed for its “immoral and unholy excess” anytime soon. That being said, the direct political support of one of our ancestors would do great things for our right to rule. We must give the ancestor an answer soon.
