When in company of the Khets, one must behave cautiously and respectfully, as one obviously does not want a god's wrath unleashed upon himself. Unfortunately for him, a notable landowner has knocked over Khet's luggage, causing it to sink in the waters of Sorrow. Least to say, the next day the landowner's residence and its vicinity were met by a huge sandstorm which did not move away from the place it was summoned upon. The Khet is now contemptuous with his retaliation and watches as his creation swirls parts of debris in random directions.
