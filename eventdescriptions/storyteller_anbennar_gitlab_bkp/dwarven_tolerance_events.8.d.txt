One of the most alien aspects of dwarven culture to us is their concept of longterm grudges. Dwarves have a notorious fame for being easily offended and holding onto grudges over ridiculous amounts of time, often passing them to their descendants and clan members. This behaviour has created the latest conflict in our court, as one of our dwarven courtiers has attacked an administrator for an offense made decades in the past. Our advisors are divided between wanting to respect our legal procedures and respecting the dwarven traditions; however, they are waiting for your opinion before pressing charges.
