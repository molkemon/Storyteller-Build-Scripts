Fanatics following Pelodir view him as their protector and wish everyone to be seen by his gaze, artificial or not, at all times. Because of this unorthodox belief, a group of Moon Elves have built a large statue of their leader in the middle of a distant province. They may have meant well, but this is not the way to go about it. The statue must be removed, having an icon of a hostile foreign power in the center of one of our towns is unacceptable.
