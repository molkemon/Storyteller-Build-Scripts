While preparing a base to manage the expedition from our realm, we received generous help from the local warrior-monks. Their efforts to provide us hospitality and assistance ended up being excessive. We spent more time preparing the monks to accompany us into the high temple than we did exploring it. As expected, a symbol resembling two straight lines crossed in the middle featured prominently on many objects within the temple. There were many traps, which was also expected, but it appears that the expertise of the warrior-monks accompanying us made them well-suited for identifying traps—when they knew what to look for. The expertise of these monks did offer new perspectives, however—we discovered a relief of an incredibly intricately painted black sword, remarkable for its resemblance to Kongjian. One of the monks found a gem marking the descriptor in a secret room uncovered by a poorly-concealed secret entrance. Due to improper handling, the gem was shattered by the time it reached our hands. Nonetheless, with this gem recovered, we have no other reason to be inside this temple.
