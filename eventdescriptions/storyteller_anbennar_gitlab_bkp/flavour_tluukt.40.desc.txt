As we continue our valiant defense against the gnollish hordes, we receive a strange missive; a request for parley. We thought it an obvious gnollish trap, but played along nonetheless. To our surprise, the negotiations seemed to be conducted in good faith; the gnollish envoy offered us peace provided we cede the territory of one of our human protectorates to their lord’s dominion. In exchange, they are willing to pay more than a commensurate sum in war reparations. These savages have been incapable of diplomatic overtures in the past, so many of our court fear this to be a trap of some sort. But perhaps peace in this costly war is worth the price.
