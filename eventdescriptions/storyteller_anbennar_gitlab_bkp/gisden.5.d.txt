Word of the summonings spread across the court quickly. And, soon after, throughout the whole county. Lothane sil Wex, Emperor of Anbennar and Grand Duke of the Borders, had seen fit to summon Lord Ostan to Wexkeep for what he called ‘important discussions’.  Many had noticed how Lord Ostan had slowly begun to take over the responsibilities that came with ruling over the county, with Count Benar V doing little to nothing to diminish his uncle’s growing importance. Now, with Lothane sil Wex himself taking notice, many see it as a sign that Lord Ostan will soon soar to higher heights.
