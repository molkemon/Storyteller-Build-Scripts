We have received a very, very strange offer, well to call it strange would be putting it lightly, it's more accurately radically unconventional. A rune scribe, Ur Fijon is offering to give our army his wellknown runic tattoos, for a price of course. If we accepted his offer our army would see great benefit from the use of these powerful magics. Our people however wouldn't look fondly on this: the sight of our soldiers marching from town to town bearing the markings of our longheld enemies would undoubtedly shake their faith in our government.
