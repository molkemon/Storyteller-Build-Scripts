It begins at sunset.  There is no moment the sun is more beautiful, and so too, there is no moment that the Jadd Empire has been more beautiful. Engineers bustle around the Yansheni fireworks set up in the Porcelain Cities and Aizt Ralare, tonnes of gunpowder in intricate configurations that they hope can echo some of its wonder. The entire city of Sharaja sparkles, gems catching the orangered light; the fortune strewn around the city rests easy, knowing such a glorious tribute to Surael shall be molested by none. The Eborthil shipyards, which normally work around the clock assembling and maintaining Surael's Armada, fall silent even as the beauty above is put to music by Busilar's finest violinists. Cannorian wine is present at every evening banquet, grapes grown under His sun brewed by His servants into a drink delighting His people as they toast His brilliance.  A deep joy thrums throughout the Empire, every delight on offer, and every worshipper of the Jadd is blessed with a hint of how it shall feel to be embraced by Surael's Rapture.
