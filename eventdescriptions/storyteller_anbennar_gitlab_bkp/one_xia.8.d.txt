Zhu Ruirong, known as Zhu of the Way of the Piercing Spear, is a remarkable woman. The Piercing Spear Way is a yangaligned method of chi cultivation, valuing strength and directness, aligning itself with the sun and the south, it is above all masculine. Despite the challenge of her body, Zhu Ruirong has risen through the ranks with tenacity and vigor, displaying the sharp, aggressive stance that the Way demands. Now she has been named Head Teacher of the Sect and Master of the Way. She is, indeed, a remarkable woman.  That is the problem: in the past few years, Zhu Ruirong has begun to feel less and less like a woman. Less soft, less subtle. Walking the Way of the Piercing Spear has given them a Chi that is masculine in aura, and as time has passed that chi has made itself known on their body. Hands, calloused whether by training or by cultivationwho can say. Skin, rough and hairy. A stocky build that hides all curves that may have once been there. A voice that grows deeper each month.  It is known to none except Zhu themselves why they chose this path of all paths to walk, but walk it they have. Now all that remains is to ask: where has it taken them? The Transmutative Path acknowledges Place to be what one's Substance passes through. It acknowledges also the possibility of reincarnation.
