The ruler gazes upon the leaders of the Shirgrii. They regard them with a practiced distrust, and a wariness of one another. It disgusts them. Regardless, they begin to speak. "What will become of the oni? Of Azjakuma?" They extend a finger towards the map resting upon the table, showing the Demon Hills and its nearby conquests. "This is what we must do. The socalled "Righteous" path would exterminate us all, if we allowed them. In this, if not anything else, they are fully united. Why can the oni not say the same? In short, you are all here." Floorboards creak from outside the meeting chamber. ".to pay your undying homage to me. You shall surrender your sovereignty to me, in perpetuity. I trust none of you hold any objections." Before any of them can respond, the ruler slams their fist onto the table, nearly breaking it. After a moment's delay, soldiers storm the room from every direction. Two stand behind each chief, and more aside wait by the exit. The looks on their faces are wild, surprised, shocked, outraged; the chief of the Ajgriijarul attempts to stand up, but is forced back into their seat. Yes, they are all listening now. Even to the end, they are more loyal to themselves than the Shirgrii. The ruler laughs, savoring their victory. "Yes, and now."
