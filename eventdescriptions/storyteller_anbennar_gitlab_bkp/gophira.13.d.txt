Some months ago, a warlord-officer in our army began using Gophiran troops under his command to subdue a swath of small, village-sized city states and effectively add them to his personal domain. With the warlord merely seizing farming communities—valuable sources of tribute—the land grab escaped our notice, and, seeing no repercussions had befallen the officer, others started following his example. Some of these copy-cats lacked the original officer's tact—a coalition of local hero-cults, the only groups left there powerful enough to complain, have come to us outraged by the commanders' abuses. Unfortunately, we do not have the strength to deal with these renegade commanders at this time—they are, after all, in charge of the very forces we would try to put them down with.
