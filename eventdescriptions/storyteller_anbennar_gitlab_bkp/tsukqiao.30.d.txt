The story told of his arrival at the Ascension Cape after the day of Ashen Skies, of his march through the lands of the Xianjie and his departure from the decadent late Luzhong. He wasn't just a Beikling; no, he was the Lost King of Luzhong, the last True Ruler of the Ancient Empire. Every part of my body shook. How could this be forgotten from the annals of history? Could it be that there was a stronger force trying to delete this? Is this the true story of my people? Of MY Dynasty?

Suddenly, I heard crying from another room. I rushed in to find a small pool of hot water, the vapor sometimes forming faces that looked into my soul for a second. In the water, a child floated on a small piece of wood, bearing two birthmarks—the Lotus and the Mask. Son of Rahen and Yanshen.
