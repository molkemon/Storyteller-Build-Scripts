One of the more recent additions to the Hekuudzoungh Festival is the Hariigegh, where the rulers of every independent clan meet in a beautiful tent to enjoy delicious food and discuss matters of importance. Our ruler and the ruler of Tsuista Khul have been discussing the future of the Tsuista Khul clan for quite some time now. Their ruler has requested our support, hoping our mighty warriors will prove instrumental in conquering greener pastures for them. The people of Tsuista Khul wish for better grazing lands for their livestock, and are ready to wage war for the city of Jincheng and all the fertile land that lies before it, and are preparing not only to mobilise their military but indeed their entire population for this daring endeavour. Should we grant them our support, we can expect significant military cooperation in the future from them. To cement the deal their ruler offers one of their children's hand in marriage, should our ruler desire it.
