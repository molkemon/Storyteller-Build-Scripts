Scholars hailing from Bulwar and beyond have long recorded Dimuzi’s title as Dimuzi the HalfImmortal due to being the son of Precursor Elf and Karqaslu prince. Yet to those who live in Kumarkand Dimuzi is anything but Halfimmortal, he is immortal. It is because of Dimuzi that the city of Karqaslu, now Panu Karqaslu, stood long after the Karqaslu empire collapsed. It is he who fended off gnollish and harpy raids during the age of Age of Monsters. It is he who passed away after dealing the killing blow on the demon Niksaru. And it is because of his Misari heritage that Kumarkand is a safe haven for halfelves who are frequently discriminated against in Bulwar.  Dimuzi’s armour and weapons are already in Kumarkands possession, however, with the Akals recent creation of an relic collection initiative talks about finding relics belonging to Dimuzi have increased. Many aspiring adventurers who grew up idolising Dimuzi now scour the countryside in search of their heroes' legacy.
