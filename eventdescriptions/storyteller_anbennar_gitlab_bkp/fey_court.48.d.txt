Whispers follow the ruler. Not unkind ones, but whispers of awe and fear. It is rare for one to manage such close relations with the cold Unseelie. Usually, such results come at the cost of servitude or belittlement, yet our ruler stands tall. And now, some have begun to ponder whether they are in fact the Shadow of the King, an avatar of the Unseelie's archfey ruler. Of course, the proposition is preposterous, but there is value in letting such whispers linger uncontested. Seeing one's subjects sit a bit straighter in your presence never hurts, after all.
