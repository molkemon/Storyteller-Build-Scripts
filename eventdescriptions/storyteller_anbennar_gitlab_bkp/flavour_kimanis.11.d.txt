So saith THE MOB:  We’ve stumbled into towns that are not ours Forgive us if we seem to slur our speech We are so very far from fair Kimanis Now appetites that once were checked can grow  Our brothers in their square have started cheering Our brothers in their houses have found loot Our brothers holding swords will take it from them Our brothers emptyhanded can haul wine  This is the meaning of the creed of Kheions Together, we are stronger than alone But wait our DEMAGOGUE has started speaking His words have bled together in a drone  Yet look our brothers’ mood has gone to shifting What is the path our DEMAGOGUE has shown?
