Prime Discordance, call forth the chorus of chaos to bear witness to your servant, the Ascending Dragon, master of the Twin Forces, wielder of the Thousand Secrets. Excerpt from the Book of the Prime Discordance.
There is no school of magic they have not mastered. A true Acolyte of the esoteric and arcane, their reputation precedes them in any hall dedicated to the pursuit of mystical truths. It is possible they are the single most powerful being on the planet. Their glory knows no bounds in their domain, and it is difficult for even other Acolytes to seriously challenge their claim to absolute supremacy.
