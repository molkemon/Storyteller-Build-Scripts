For now, the Dwarves of Verkal Ozovar have been good subjects, bringing in the tribute without any signs of insubordination. We did face opposition, however, when we demanded access to their libraries, and were denied, with the librarian citing some ancient oath that hadn’t been broken for thousands of years. As it turns out, we never needed to enter the library halls, as the dwarves themselves gave us exactly what we needed. All that was needed was a sweet smile, a little perfume, a little bit of understanding in this cruel world. Or well, that's what the lord said to his new consort, as they visited a construction site of the first dwarven project on our land since ancient times.
