As you scribe some notes within your chambers, the wall next to your door simply ceases to exist. A sudden blast of fire and splinters deafens you, raining debris and soot everywhere. Your newest assistant, only on their second day of the job, reaches for their weapon; but you idly wave them off, wafting unconcernedly at the cloud of plaster dust. This is not an unusual experience. As the ash rises, you see the figure of our advisor standing triumphantly in the wreckage. As he strides towards you with a fistful of schematics, however, your mild irritation gives way to a touch of curiosity; for a winged woman is casually peering through the new hole in your room. She steps around the ruined frame, opening the still-functional door and striding through. As your eyes flick back to our advisor, you notice that his boot is now up on your desk. They triumphantly lift a bundle of schematics in the air, then throw them down above the notes you had been working on, scattering papers that you have spent the last few hours preparing. "Listen." Our advisor smiles, a manic light in his eyes. "I have the best idea. She and her flock" our advisor gestures with his thumb to the winged woman behind him; "they can carry things; people, even. I've designed a special harness that we could outfit our units with; harpy strike teams could drop ordinance behind enemy lines. I just need a little investment from you, my lord." The harpy behind him smiles at you with a sharp-toothed grin, eyes glinting with shared, fiery enthusiasm, and nods. It is rare to find someone who loves explosives as much as our advisor. You raise an eyebrow at him and slowly blink. A couple of seconds go by before he takes his boot off of your table and folds his hands sheepishly, still with a wild grin adorning his face. The silence fills the air, thick with the scent of sulfur. What is your response?
