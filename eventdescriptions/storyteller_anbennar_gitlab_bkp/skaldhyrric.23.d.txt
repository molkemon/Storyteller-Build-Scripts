Recently a puzzling report has been brought to the court. The people of a neighbouring realm seem to have embraced a curious fusion of their Court gods and the Skaldhyrric tales. Our tales have begun to be told in their circles alongside the stories of their gods, with the OneArmed Skald told alongside stories of Castellos and Corin. It is clear that with time, they will begin to understand that their gods are stories of the past, not chronicles of divine beings. Should we capitalize on this trend or would it be best to allow things to proceed naturally?
