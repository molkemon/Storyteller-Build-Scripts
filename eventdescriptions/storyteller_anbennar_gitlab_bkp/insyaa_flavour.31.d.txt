Upon waking today, our ruler was greeted by a small slip of paper left under the door to their room from our advisor containing an apology for his departure, explaining that the road calls to him again. On the other side of the paper, the kobold inscribed a small prayer, that our ruler might draw closer to the highest good as they rule their nation. The prayer appears to be highly personalized, and in reading it our ruler was struck with the sense that the advisor knew things about them that they did not yet know about themselves.
