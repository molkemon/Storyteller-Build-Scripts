At dawn, the first rays of sunlight strike Ebbusubtu from the east, shining on the lines of gold and copper which have been worked into the carvings. The great effigy of Jaher, gazing east across the dunes towards Rahen and Haless, once part of his empire, a look of determination on his face showing his wish to reclaim what was once part of his glorious holy empire, his eyes shining like fire in the sunlight. As the sun sets, it alights upon his countenance again looking west, a loving look upon his face for his chosen people in Bulwar. All those who gaze upon his regal visage will know that Jaher and Surael, watch us still, ever looking to our salvation. Ever bringing us the Light to banish the Darkness.
