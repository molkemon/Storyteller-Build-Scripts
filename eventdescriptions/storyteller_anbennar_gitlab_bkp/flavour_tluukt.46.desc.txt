From our perches we watch as the gnolls of Tluukt struggle to reign in the lands of the Far Salahad, lands that should rightfully be ours. We gladly joined these Tluukti gnolls in avenging Zokka, our bloodsorn ally, yet the catharsis of our loss is yet to come even as the body of Jaddar rots in some pit. Thus, when the lord of Tluukt offers us sovereignty over the Far Salahad, of restoring the great queendom of Siadanlen, all in exchange for kissing their dirtcovered feet and paying a queen’s tribute, the first thought that crosses our lord’s mind is not tossing this churlish gnoll from the highest peak, but instead of accepting this humiliating opportunity. How can we reject in the name of pride the golden opportunity to rule as queens once more?
