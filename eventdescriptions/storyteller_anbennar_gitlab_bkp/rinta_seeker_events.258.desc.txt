Dark magicks are inherently chaotic. They ebb and flow in jagged lines, leaving marks wherever they are felt. Current work done by the Magi Council in studying the Viscus has resulted in marks appearing to the general population: vast spires of obsidian, ritualistic statues of beasts, cornfields of every color, impossible mountain shapes churning into form. Dark magicks are as chaotic as can be. We can only pray that the effects may bless us with something—or at the very least not hurt us.
