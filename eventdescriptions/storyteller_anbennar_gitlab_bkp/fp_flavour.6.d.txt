Almruk scurry away with quaking quills, shadhavar flee to alarming dirges, and even tlalusks mewl with apprehension as a grim thunder rumbles in the province's wilderness. Sensed before seen, the quaking terror is none other than a barzoazh, a titan ranking among the largest land creatures in all of Halann. In spite of their sheer size, this elephantdwarfing predator relies on ambushing imposing beasts from grottos and lakes, tearing into unsuspecting prey with a liquefying bite before dragging them to a watery grave. Ornery and solitary, barzoazh usually prefer large game over mortal peoples, yet reports from the region paint a dismal picture: A uniquely aggressive barzoazh has gone on a rampage, thrashing settlements and slaying locals without apparent cause or discrimination save for a love of destruction. How should we respond to this overpowering creature?
