A dozen crates of Rahens finest spices. Furniture carved from the Lupulans greatest trees. Art, calligraphy, poems, and literature enough to fill a great nobles library. And this is only the beginning of what we have prepared for our expedition to Cannor. Not a piece fails to reflect the glory of our nation, and undoubtedly they will ensure the Ghost Emperor’s name even on the other side of the world. As the caravan sets out from our capital, our lord thinks they can see a new chapter beginning for all of us.
