There are many misconceptions about centaur society. One of the most persistent ones is that the sole focus of their existence is vengeance, death, and battle. While there are some that believe that, notably those of the Filustkhuga sect, the centaurs are varied and diverse. One such sect that diverges from this stereotype is the Irdukhuga Sect, emerging roughly 800 years ago during the rule of the Egoirlust Caehnate. They focus on honouring Irdaeos' Gift by enjoying life's pleasures and partaking in hospitality, kindness, and virtue. One of Azaly Stonecarver's apprentices, a mage and musician by trade, has travelled to their court and is studying with them for the moment.
