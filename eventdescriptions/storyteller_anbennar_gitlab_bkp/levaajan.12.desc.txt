The Bird Riders of the East have long been considered to be lost by the Rukunai and Runsukhi due to their move eastwards as well as their donning of the masks, but it was only recently that talks had begun of more than cold acceptance of their wayward designs. Those within the monasteries now speak of another topic: war. To bring them back into the fold, to have them be once more part of the designs of the monasteries. To simply ignore this would be folly: it must be debated and discussed and meditated upon.
