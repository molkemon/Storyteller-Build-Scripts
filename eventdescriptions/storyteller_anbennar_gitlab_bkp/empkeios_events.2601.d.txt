The slaver guilds of the realm have, over time, become an integral part of our economy. Special provisions in every legal code allow for a synergy between the guilds and the realm's state, developing into a truly symbiotic relationship. A balance between the state's needs and the guilds' goals is consensuated. So far, this has worked wonderfully, resulting in the average free citizen enjoying the highest standards of living in the domain.

With the de facto control of regional slave trade by Empkeios, however, many voices within the slaver guilds are pushing for greater autonomy. This would negate some of the financial benefits from slave production but ultimately liberate some political power that the state is currently investing in the control and balance of the guilds. The decision is ultimately yours, lord.
