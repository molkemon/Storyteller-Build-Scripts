To the honourable ForgeCouncillors: Sad news. The Rabulaths have decided not to  agree to our terms. Terramancy is more than a school of magic to them; it's a gift from Nahrei, their creation spirit (a deification of nature and the valley) and they are thus very reluctant to share the discipline secrets with outsiders.  They still offer their friendship and alliance; even if some of you may think these to be worthless to us, I believe given some time we can win enough of their trust to convince them to share their geomantic skills with us. The alternative is needless bloodshed; and it wouldn't be just our blood to spill. Don't mistake these people for the Basobhar our Nekheis dealt with: they have military equipment on par with any Kheion, and the Valley will be a giant trap for anybody who doesn't know the terrain. I'll stay here to try and smooth things over.; Aldarious Konta, senior diplomat.
