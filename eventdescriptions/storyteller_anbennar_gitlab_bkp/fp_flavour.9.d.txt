The land sighs as a lonely horned champion has begun prancing around the countryside of the province. Like a vigilante, the creature strikes against overzealous predators and despoilers of their domain before zipping away, vanishing into the background. Several hunters have shared frightful encounters with it, but most locals have sighted a gentle beast frolicking in blessed serenity, curing crop blights and animal diseases with its magic horn, and even stopping fights by deflecting blows off its enchanted hide. Based on these stories, we can profess with certainty that the province's newfound defender is none other than a bo, an equinelike creature with deadly front claws, scintillating stripes of fur, a magical horn capable of bestowing great boons, and a shrewd intellect for righteousness. With countless oral and written histories attesting to its nobility, the bo has heralded great signs for the nations, rulers, and peoples it has protected. With such a creature warding our lands, we believe this bo was called by our nation.
