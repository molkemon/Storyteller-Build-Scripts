What I thought of the dreamer? It is hard to say really. Their world… is unlike our country. Even more so than the rest of the realm in many respects. Those small winged creatures that fluttered through the air there were delightful, that much I can say for sure. I felt in many respects as if I was both asleep and awake at the same time, many of my memories of that journey are a haze of colour and sound. If not for the spells we weaved, I wonder if I would have made it to them at all. I am glad we did, for that was a sight I shall remember for all my life. What a magnificent being, glittering as emerald. I did not hear the words you exchanged with them, captivated as I was by that sight, but I do not mind.
