We are faced with the greatest challenge ever presented before us since the dawn of our people. We set forth now to defy the different legendary beasts of the Tswohvvwoh’ii; we call them godbeasts for lack of a better name, though we know they are no gods. Intelligent creatures, these lifeforms are massive compared to any other lifeform in the jungle. They are also far more dangerous.  In the South of Tswohvvwoh’ii, the local GodBeast has the appearance of a halfseedthralled, colossal panther. It is covered in carnivorous plants, with appearance like flytraps or green piranhas, that devour anything that it passes by even if the host is not itself hungry. If we can claim the bones of this creature, we can bring great glory to ourselves and our people.
