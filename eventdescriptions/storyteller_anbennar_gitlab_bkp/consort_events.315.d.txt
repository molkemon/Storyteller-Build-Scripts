We tried to stop the rumors, but when we were made aware of them it was already too late. Now every other man and woman at court is gossiping behind our backs. Even the commoners in the streets have the nerve to make lewd jokes about the consort and the illegitimate child of the capital. They say that the Queen is unfaithful and that the heir, the heir of our realm, is not actually the child of the monarch. All we can do at this point is keep up appearances as best we can and hope that the rumors will die down in time.
