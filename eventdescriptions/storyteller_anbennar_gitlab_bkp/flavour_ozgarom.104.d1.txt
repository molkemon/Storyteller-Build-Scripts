When the upstart Bramash first swept across Endralliande in his campaign of butchery, we feared the worst. His troops even threatened the capital and the stability of our colony altogether! Yet in recent weeks our brave forces have prevailed, smashing Bramash's host and liberating province after province as we reassert order across the island. Desperate and aware of just how little rope he had remaining, Bramash has laid waste to the naval supply depot and seized a small merchant fleet, quitting the island altogether. While we might have preferred the capture of Bramash and a proper execution, his flight in such a manner does his propaganda efforts no credit whatsoever, and in the wake of his departure, our laborers will be easily cowed.
