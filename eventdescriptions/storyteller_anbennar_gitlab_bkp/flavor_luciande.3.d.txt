The halls of Moregod echo with shouts of alarm, then screams of pain. A Luciandic agent kicks open the door to Cellblock B. The guard on the other side raises his crossbow, but the agent moves fast, faster than any man. He throws a knife and the guard falls to the ground, choking on blood in his final breaths. "In here!" the agent calls to his comrades. The group moves into Block B, checking each cell in quick succession, gazing into the eyes of the crazed prisoners. Finally, they stop at a door. The gnome within appears bruised and feeble. His hands are two sickening purple lumps, flopping uselessly at his sides. He stares at the group with the look of a wild animal. The leader rips off the cell bars and strides across the room. He tears the motionless gnome’s chains out of the wall. "Mr. Orwick Kindlehelm? Our master has a job for you." The gnome’s eyes blaze with the fire of a thousand suns. He slowly nods.
