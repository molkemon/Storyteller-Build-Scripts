Our lord wonders why, for the third time this month, they have been summoned to the undead experimentation area. The last time it was to look at a duckhuman hand hybrid undead, which, while entertaining, wasn't even working correctly; it was just an undead duck with nonfunctional hands attached. The first time was even worse, because the duck from that time actually killed the mage responsible for the presentation, which is where the hands for the second experiment came from. Walking into the room (and avoiding the duck the necromancers are now keeping as a pet), the lord is greeted by an abomination of some kind, a mismatched amalgam of limbs and another duck, again. Before they can leave, however, one of the mages catches their attention and gives a command to the abomination, which correctly performs it with all of their limbs. Finally. Our invincible, endless undead army is perfect.
