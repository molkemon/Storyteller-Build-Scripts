Another night, and more reports of overwhelming dread in our subjects' sleep. In the Shadow Swamp, nightmares are as common as the trees are grey. They usually are not accompanied by an intense scent of decaying flesh, though. Something has perished in great numbers in the province, and the slime mold known as Ghost's Breath has seemingly taken refuge in every fallen tree and puddle of mud in the area.  There are several remedies to this infestation, but none of them are easy. Or cheap.
