As it turns out, it was not just the military that sought to buck the Maidens of Liho. Members of the state on all levels, from high to low, have shown their disloyalty to the new ruler in the form of reluctance to obey their betters. Though they understand fully well what needs to be done for Huszien’s sake, they are willing to indirectly harm her by depriving her of an opportunity to recover. Everyone whose loyalty cannot be verified must be removed. If they do not leave their posts, then they will die. For the next few months, rather than grow, the state will instead have to shrink.
