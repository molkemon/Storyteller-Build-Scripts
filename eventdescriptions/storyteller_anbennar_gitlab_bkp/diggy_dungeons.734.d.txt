In a chamber to the left of the golem assembler was a single hallway, inside of which there was a door at the far end that opened into a vast chamber, though dominated by only one thing of note. A colossal archlike gateway, standing twelve feet high, wrought from seamless stone. It was unlike anything anyone had seen before, and perhaps none shall again. Its features were jagged, with crude artistic carvings of animals and things none were familiar with. Accompanying these features are runes not of a dwarven make, but something utterly alien. Glowing with a faint purple energy, they almost draw the observer in, body and soul.
