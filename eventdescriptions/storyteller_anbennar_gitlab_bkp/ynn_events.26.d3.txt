It is done. We have crushed the crazed mob of the Dragon Cult, and slashed our way through the streets of that province, reaching the palace of deviancy where the Dragon Prince was being raised and revered. The creature didn't wait for us any longer, and perched itself on the roof of the palace, growling at our warriors before spitting a cloud of flames at them, repelled by the water shields cast by our mages. Our lord stood at the front, wielding a spear and charging at the beast. Once the dust settled, the dragon lay dead among the rubble of the nowruined palace and countless corpses of our warriors. Alas, our lord was among those who perished in the fight, their death the ultimate sacrifice for the Dragon Cult's final defeat. Their remains were swiftly attended to by the best embalmers that were accompanying our army, and trusted carpenters were summoned to craft a royal coffin and barge. The streets of that province are now filled with sorrow and celebration in equal measure as they await our lord's funeral.
