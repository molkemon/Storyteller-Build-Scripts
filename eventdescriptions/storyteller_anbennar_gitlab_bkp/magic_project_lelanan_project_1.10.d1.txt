The doctors told me not to. The mihipha warned of danger. But all my life I had wanted to see my baby in a manger. So I pushed past their objections, stepped into that old dance, faced my gentle friend, and fell into a trance. Months passed after, when the magic was still subject to time, I gazed down at my daughter and heard a gentle chime. I wept for so long after; she looked just like my gone lovers, my little darling child. My heart finally recovers.
