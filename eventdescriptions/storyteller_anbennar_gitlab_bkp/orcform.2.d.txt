Not long ago, this nation was nothing but a weak idea in the minds of a few orks. Now, it has grown to be a fledgling state of its own, and one that has now proven itself as a member of global society. With the affirmation of a new nation comes change. Generation after generation is born without ever visiting the ancestral homeland, and while they do not seek to sever their ties, they wish to establish themselves as their own independent people.
