A soft breeze rustles through the vibrant leafed walkways rising above the streets of fair Arverynn. Merchants argue in the Osvoyr district and the clash of steel rings true from the Dratnik streets and parks. All of the city is abuzz with gossip, for earlier in the day a procession from our overlord, their lord of our realm passed through the city and onto Vyrisar Hill to pass beyond the great walls of the resurgent Teal Keep. In a windowed and balconied hall the Lord of Arverynn and their lord speak for long hours whilst gazing down on the bustling metropolis below the mounds. No greater opposer to republican systems may be found in the Ynn than the greatest Kyrrachean Dynasty, and few believe the lord shall accept the proposal of their lord, as other lords may have. When the sun sets and no announcement is made there is yet more rumour across the city, worry spreads as to how much a rejection would deteriorate relations between the Republic and Arverynn. Thus it is that when the delegation comes before the throne the next day, even the vast hall of the Teal Keep cannot contain the crowds of nobles and wealthy citizens craning their necks to witness what may come to pass, and heavy silence grips every heart as their lord finally asks the question. "Will the House of Calrodiy, Hammer of Sarda join us at the courts of our capital?" In the following pause, every clink of armour and gulp of breath feels louder than the roar of a dragon.
