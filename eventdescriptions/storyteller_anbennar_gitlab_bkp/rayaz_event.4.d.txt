A remnant of a failed policy, the bulwark states served Zerat for many years, as both protectors of their realm from foreign threats and as retirement homes for the disgraced members of their large family. Those states now offer us their loyalty and help in pacifying their more rebellious kin, in exchange for a great deal of autonomy on their lands. The Yushyn in particular, who have previously joined our cause, are now expecting to be repaid for their initiative in joining the right side of the conflict.  But at the same time… this offer will only give refuge to those who we had tried to oust from power. We were supposed to scour Zerat, once and for all. Would this really accomplish that?
