The sword knows what it's doing. Balgar, still, does not quite feel comfortable with it in his hand. But a blade that has slain a hundred thousand orks can add more to that total even in an uncertain hand.  That, of course, is what Balgar is trying to convince himself of as he takes his first steps outside Ovdal Lodhum's walls since the fateful expedition which saw his father slain and a human encountered. The rest of that story was history. but today Ovdal Lodhum's future is written.
