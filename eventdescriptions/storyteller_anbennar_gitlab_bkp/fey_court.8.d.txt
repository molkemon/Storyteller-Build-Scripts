In many agreements between mortals, there is often a third party present to ensure the terms agreed upon are met and that there are no backroom shenanigans. The Unseelie have under their employ a group of fey with a similar purpose, the Ironbark Oathkeepers. Short, silent, grayskinned, these fey have the peculiar ability to detect when agreements they have witnessed are being breached. Something that the Unseelie take great advantage of.  Give and take a few favors, a few such Oathkeepers could happen to be present the next time we meet with our vassals.
