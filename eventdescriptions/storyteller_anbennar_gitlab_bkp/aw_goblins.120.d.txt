You send a hound after a fox and it comes back saying the fox wants to pay taxes. Our adventurers have returned with news that they defeated the goblin infestation in our lands. Rather than slaying the creatures though, it would seem the goblins value their lives more than most of their kind and surrendered early. It seems they want to be our subjects. An unusual turn of events to be sure and vexing since the adventurers did uphold their part of the deal to eliminate the infestation.
