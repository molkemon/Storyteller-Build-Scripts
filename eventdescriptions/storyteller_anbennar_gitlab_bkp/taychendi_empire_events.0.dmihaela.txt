It is common for rulers to emulate past rulers to establish guidelines for policies and to gain a place in the heavens. However, Mihaela is a vampire, possessing immortality, so she will never reach the gates of Dhivumanhu. Instead, her religious duty is to facilitate the apotheosis of her non-vampiric subjects. To achieve this, the style of her rule and the gods she patronizes encourage her subjects to follow the same path to apotheosis. Therefore, changing the style of her rule would be advantageous in shaping the direction of our realm.
