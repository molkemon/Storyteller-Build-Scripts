Success. After participating in the Ogres' festival, our researchers found plenty of willing subjects for study, and this was more than sufficient to start to understand where this race's great strength comes from. With a little more tweaking, we now have functioning strength potions.  There are still many adjustments to be made to the formula (currently some other ogreish traits end up making their way into those who consume them), but it's only a matter of time before we're able to perfect these potions.
