I want to show you that even the smallest situation for a ruler can become an opportunity, so let’s imagine that in your kingdom, a wealthy investor has arrived. They have acquired quite a reputation for making daring, highrisk investments, most of which have paid off. They want to settle in your realm, but for that, they seek support. They don’t specify what, but what is known is that there is no money left in the treasury. What do you do?
