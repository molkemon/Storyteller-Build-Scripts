"You have to believe me." the clergyman roared, his exhausted and terrified face speaking volumes, even louder than his words "The Clergy is being infiltrated, on all levels Please, they are trying to silence me" a sharp thud from a guard caused him to stumble to the ground, colliding with a thud, before promptly being hauled to his feet, and forced forwards, into the abyssally black walls of Azkabar’s (in)famous prison.
