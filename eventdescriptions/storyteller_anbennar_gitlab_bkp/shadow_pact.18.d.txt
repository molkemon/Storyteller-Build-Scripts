Their ruler has begun to have dreams of a misty forest, with dancing lights flickering in the trees. They walk slowly through the woods, tentatively exploring the shadowy wilderness. The more they dream of the forest, the more they explore, and after some time they hear a curious sound; a voice, calling to them from within the mist. Barely audible at first, then growing to a soft whisper. "Join us.join us." When their ruler finally arrives at the center of the forest, they see a large blob of shadow in the middle of a clearing, and from the sphere of darkness a wizened arm extends outward, beckoning them to take their hand. The whisper returns, louder than ever. "Untold power."
