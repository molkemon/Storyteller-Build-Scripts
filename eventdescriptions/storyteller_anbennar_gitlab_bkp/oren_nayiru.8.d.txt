The Oren Nayiru was once the Cult of Vherenar, an organization dedicated to vanquishing the evils of slavery. Organized into elusive and compartmentalized "cells," hidden among the slave populations of Taychend, it is no surprise that the Oren Nayiru has spread the fastest among the slaves. All over Taychend, whispers of freedom spread among those in bondage, with many seeing the Oren Nayiru as their only hope in winning it.
