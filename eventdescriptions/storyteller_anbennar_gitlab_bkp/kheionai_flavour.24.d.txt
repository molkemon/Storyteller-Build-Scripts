A captain of an ambitious trading fleet has returned after some years, bringing with him tales of a faraway land called Haraf. He describes adventures that seem almost fantastical, from evading titanic worms that burrow through the sands to defending his crew against twisted elves that carve their skin into eerie works of art. He also carries with him an ornate mask that he claims is imbued with the spirits of fallen warriors and speaks of a ritual that allows him to commune with his ancestors from centuries past through the very currents of water itself. Many of our people find his stories unbelievable, but regardless, he has decided to donate some of his wealth acquired during this wild journey.
