Following our recent conquest of the neighboring province, the local militia has reported that a passage near the realm of Meteí would make for an ideal shelter against the Deathwinds. The militia has preemptively marched into the pass, constructing the beginnings of shelters for future use. The local Eltibhen chieftain, however, was outraged when he arrived to find part of the sacred pilgrimage route to the realm of Meteí being blocked off by these easterners. Today, the chieftain himself arrived in a fury at the gates of our domain, Kheion. He is beseeching our administration to order the militia out of the pass to respect their customs as lawful subjects, appealing to our faith in reason. We must respond soon, lest the tensions escalate.
