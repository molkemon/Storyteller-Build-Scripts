After a few close encounters with Harpies during routine diplomatic work in Bulwar, some of the members of the Expeditionary Corps became quite fascinated with the species’ ability to soar the skies, and have brought to their lord a proposal to work on the development of Flight Potions. Recognizing the usefulness of this research, their lord accepted their proposal. However, with so many flying creatures to study, there are multiple avenues of research: The members of the diplomatic corps who originally brought the idea forward seem to be fixated on the idea of studying harpies, but other members of the wider potionmaking community seem to think that studying more inherently magical creatures may work better for our potions.
