This menu presents the option to build a Legionway along several predetermined paths (if unlocked), or in any or our/subject owned provinces that have a Legion present.  Constructing new Citadels throughout the mission tree will allow us to build out Legionways all at once to those Citadels at a reduced cost, provided all prerequisite Legionways are built. Otherwise, Legions can start the construction of a Legionway, provided that they are situated on a valid province (see the option tooltip).  The speed that each stage of Legionway construction depends heavily on factors such as distance to Castonath, idea groups, development, and terrain, among others. Having a Legion present on the province will also speed up the survey and construction considerably.
