Traveller, in the year, the great Mountain Road Igraxmmoton was dedicated in the name of our ruler. As the Sorrow forged its path through the land, so too did the Mountain Road forge a pass through these mountains by sheer force of will. As the Sorrow guides us through the land, so too does the Mountain Road lead you through the treacherous peaks. As you ascend to the highest peaks along the Mountain Road, so too does Konolkhatep ascend great heights under their guidance. As this road stands forever, so too shall Konolkhatep. Inscription by the side of the Mountain Road in Lanaqaar
