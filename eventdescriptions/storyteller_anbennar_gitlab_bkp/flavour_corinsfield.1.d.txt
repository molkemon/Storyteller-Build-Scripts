For too long have we suffered grave offenses at the hands of the foul rune warriors. From the first night when we set foot in the plains of Veykoda, they have hassled us to no end; slaughtering a third of our number of most righteous Corinspilgrims and stealing our most valuable supplies of food and water. Most offensive of all, however, are their tattoos; onto their very skin do they inscribe their magic, ensuring that each and every one of their number is granted the insidious and hideous power of the witch.  No longer shall this be permitted to continue. By the Martyr, once we have finished our righteous mission, these vermin will be naught but stories to scare children with in the darkest times of night.  So, goodmen, march forth; let our campaign of annihilation begin.
