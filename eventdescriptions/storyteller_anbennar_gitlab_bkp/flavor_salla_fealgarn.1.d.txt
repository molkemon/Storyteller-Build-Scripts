'OBERON.' A cry bellows out as bloody earth shoots up toward him. Oberon's lungs scream as he stands up, the smell of war invading his nostrils. Instinctively he jumps forward as a thrown axe narrowly misses his head. The General swivelled around, knocking his bow as the ork berserker rushed him. Oberon lets loose three arrows in one, felling his enemy as he spots his steed Justice atop a far hill. He sprinted, diving over dying orks and elves as he moved with instinct to reach his stag mount, his friend since childhood. If he can reach Justice, the battle could still be won. But the crush of soldiers pushed against him, and Oberon was pulled back. He shouted, but Justice did not respond. He vanished behind the fighting masses. A figure bumped past him. Then another. They were his soldiers. 'NO.' The Commander screamed as a wave of pursuing orks set upon him, their axes descending into Oberon's chest. A pain erupted in Oberon's heart as he gasped awake. 'My love?' his consort asks, jolted awake. Oberon breathed. It was just a nightmare, one of the recent many since the ork’s invasion. His consort’s hands gently caressed his chest as a knock came at the door. Who could that be at this hour? 'My lord? It's urgent. We've received word I, Corin, has slain Korgus Dookanson. The Greentide Host has broken.' Oberon's heart skipped a beat. Was he still in his nightmare? Was it finally over?
