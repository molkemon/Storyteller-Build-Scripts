For years now our lord has ruled the country secretly as a vampire. Whilst there have been rumours about their true identity, it long remained a secret. But nothing lasts forever, not even the secrets of a powerful undead. Recently the rumours have been increasing across the land, and it is becoming more and more difficult to deny them. It is only a matter of time now until the ruler is exposed. If we act quickly we should still be able to preserve the masquerade, as every vampire is duty and honorbound to do. But it will require our lord’s immediate abdication of the throne, a life of obscurity their only path forward.
