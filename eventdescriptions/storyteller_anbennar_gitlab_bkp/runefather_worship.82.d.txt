The Groediazan have been supporting us in converting the Serpentspine even since we proved the success of our surface campaigns, but the stillreluctant Darkstone clan has begun to drag their heels in the Obsidian Council, in an effort to withdraw material and clerical support from us in favour of their industrial complexes in the Serpentdepths. While we can plead the Groediazan’s case and maintain their support, the Council appears to favor the Darkstone clan in this initiative.
