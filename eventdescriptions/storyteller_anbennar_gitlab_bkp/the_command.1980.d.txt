After the debacle in the War Room, our lord had a few things to think about. Normally, these affairs would not justify a moment's extra thought, however all three subordinate marshals had jumped over themselves to schedule private meetings with our lord. As if that could make any difference. Of course, our lord was not stupid, and they were well aware of how badly visible disrespect would go over. So, they needed to listen, and they needed to at least seem like there was a chance to convince them.

First there was the Tiger marshal, the one and only Zurshin Tigerborn. He came with no plans, and a determined glint in his eye. Zurshin set an hourglass on the table and began waxing large about Raheni jungle combat. Our lord did not really care. Upon wrapping things up, Zurshin finally tied it together—they desired to integrate all these tactics into the Tiger doctrine... Against who? Our lord knew that going along with this was not such a wise idea, but maybe a few well-placed words could keep the subordinate sated.
