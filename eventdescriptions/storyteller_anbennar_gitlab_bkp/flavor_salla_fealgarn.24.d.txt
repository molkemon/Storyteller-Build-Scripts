Ruibi burst through the tower’s doors with her stag Crescent, in front of her is a long dark grand hall with a shining white door at the end. She has four arrows left, her red hood masks how much blood she’s losing, and Crescent tires. She whips the reins as she pushes forward toward the epicenter of this madness.  An eight armed creature jumps toward her from the shadows as she rips an arrow letting it punch through its head. Ruibi pushes Crescent even further as two more Warped try to oppose her but again two more arrows find their mark. She barely makes it to the door before Crescent neighs in agony as she’s thrown at it, four Warped catch her stag as Ruibi is flung into the pale door passing through it.  ’ A woman’s harsh voice rings through her ears.  Ruibi grumbles as she stands up, barely lucid as she strings her bow and aims toward the Unthroned Queen.
