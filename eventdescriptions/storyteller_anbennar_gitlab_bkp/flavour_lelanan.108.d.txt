It started in the strangest way, a harpy capturing a gnoll after battle. But the weirdest thing occurred after. Despite gnolls usually being labor cattle, as the two spent years together, they grew too close to resist. And finally, one day as they rested, the harpy approached and they kissed. But gnolls are incompatible mates—any harpy worth their salt knows this. So the roost exiled both of them, denying them their newfound bliss.

They wandered together in search of a home, but for two 'monsters,' the world is a cold place. Until finally, they heard of Lelanàn, and thought just maybe they could make their case. You can find them in the northern district; always happy to talk about their tale. Just remember to ask why they're there and share with them a cup of ale.
