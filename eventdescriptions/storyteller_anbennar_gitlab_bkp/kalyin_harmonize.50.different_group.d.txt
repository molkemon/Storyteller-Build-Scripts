All gods, goddesses, and deities are different aspects of the one true Goddess, Kalyin. Proponents of the One Truth in our country have urged the importance of ensuring that we understand how Kalyin is portrayed in other cultures to further our understanding and our own beliefs. We have explored the beliefs that people elsewhere hold and their interpretations of divinity and faith. After years of work, our administrators have been able to integrate a part of their customs and faith into our worship of Kalyin. This also allows the followers of that faith their own unique worship of Kalyin to stay intact.
