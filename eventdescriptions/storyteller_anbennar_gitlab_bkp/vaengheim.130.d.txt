Then did the host of our lord come upon a camp, and wary eyes spied upon them from within every tent and beneath every cowl. A man named Helge sat by his fire, and he found his knee before our lord, and said, "Plainly shall I speak, O queen, for though you be our ruler, we have been foes for many days. For I am a thief, and this is my domain, and these people are my people, and we were beyond your law until today. Now that you have found us, I would ask your word that you let us be, and if this oath you give, we shall gladly aid you in your hunt.
