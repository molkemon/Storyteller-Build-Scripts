Infinite demand, but finite supplies. That was the Mage-Artificer Paradox. Yet love is made and given freely, even between those trapped inside the box.

The Mage was ambitious, seeking to master the world. The Artificer was content, seeking to soften life. Yet their two sides were ever at each other’s throats, their fields constantly filled with strife.

But the two shared an idea, and their cooperation began. It blossomed into a great vision, and the two could never be parted.

Even as their friends bickered and fought, they worked ever on their dream, bringing them together as they stayed up until dawn.

By sharing their split resources, they forged something new—an example to us all, and a gift for Lel’s chosen few.
