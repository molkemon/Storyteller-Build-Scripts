It is said that there are regions even below the lowest caverns known to dwarf and goblinkind. Tales of strange creatures, gigantic towering shrooms. If that is the case, then this might be the wound through which we can penetrate rock and earth and gain access to these lands.  But perhaps not.  What is the case is that in a recent expedition the men had to stop and go elsewhere because a yawning abyss stretched out in front of them that, due to its unbelievable size, proved impossible to pass. The expedition leader made a mental note and when he returned, told the administration. Interested in what this chasm might hold, another expedition was created with the sole purpose of exploring this chasm.
