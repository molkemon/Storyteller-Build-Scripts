Recently we have been approached by the nobility in Soarnaire, they have received word of plans from Venail to leave their island of Venail and migrate their populace to Aelantier. Such a plan would be great undertaking and would require great resources and expertise. We are in such a position to provide these to Venail however we would need something in return, the island of Venail seems a worthy prize. We should support these nobles in sending an offer to Venail detailing our proposal.
