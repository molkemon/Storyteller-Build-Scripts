From the steppes to the seas of Halenn, freedom reigns. The men and women are free to roam as they please, to decide their own destinies, and not to be contained by the petty will of rulers. And while those who wish to oppress may rear their ugly heads, they shall be struck by the roar of our guns and the bravery of our people. It's the end of an era, and the world dawns on an age of democracy and liberty.
