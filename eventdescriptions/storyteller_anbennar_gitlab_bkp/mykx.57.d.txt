The first writing of the Mykx constitution was a mess, because everyone involved, except Mykx herself, thought it was an excuse she made to gather all her enemies and kill them. The pirates laughed at the idea of writing a document to detail the obvious; everyone knew Mykx was in power, and she sure would crown herself as a Queen or something. So the first writing consisted of Mykx chasing around her newfound republic, capturing the most powerful, and bringing all of them to the congregation.  When it started, the assembly didn’t have paper to write because the servants and slaves were expecting the meeting to be a massacre, not an actual debate session with writing involved. Also, every proposal, the assembly looked at Mykx as expecting her to proclaim if she liked or not; her answer was always, I respect all the decisions made by the republic.  So that was how the first laws and proposals were signed; that is why it’s possible to see on the constitution itself, on the margins, help messages or the writing of improvised wills, nobody expected to leave that meeting alive.
