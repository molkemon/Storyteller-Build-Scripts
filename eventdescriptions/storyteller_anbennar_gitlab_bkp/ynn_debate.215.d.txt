Ever since we have decided to slowly start integrating firearms into our armies, we have had a growing surplus of unused swords that are now collecting rust. Hearing of this, one of our iosahar has offered to buy them off of us. While they are not offering as much money as they are actually worth, we are not using them either way, and it would be a welcome addition to our coffers. On the other hand, we could choose to just melt the swords down for their metal instead.
