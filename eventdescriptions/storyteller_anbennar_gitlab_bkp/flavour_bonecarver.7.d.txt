The Greentide was perhaps one of the greatest feats of orkish martiality in history. In it, many clans rose and fell, with only the greatest rising to the top as the home of the temple of Castellos fell to the might of Dookan’s creations. It was a war of valor, of proving and of faith. It gave the orks the new home of lush fields and ever blue skies that was promised to them by the gods. It was not an invasion, it was a simple, righteous reclamation of what was already promised to the orks.  Now though, with our clan atop all others, dominating both ork and human, it was time for another tide to start. Many new young orks are eager to carve new clans for themselves, joining our army for a chance to own a piece of land for themselves, while other clans have bowed before us in total recognition of our holiness. Meanwhile, the human kingdoms have stopped dusting their armor and sharpening their weapons. No… They have begun arming themselves and forcing their peasants into the army, clearly in preparation to take our home away from us again.  The flames were stoked within the halls of our clan, housing all of the clan chiefs once more. With an enthusiastic roar in unison, all have agreed to our call for another tide, offering their utmost promise to join us in the next crusade.
