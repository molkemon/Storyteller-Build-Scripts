After arriving in the area described in our lord's dreams, orders are made to encamp. Guards set up the lord’s tent before being ushered away to leave them to quiet contemplation. As night begins to fall, our lord continues to meditate, the sky slowly darkening as the sun retreats over the horizon. It would be some time, well into the early twilight of dusk, before they were roused from their vegetative state. The beleaguered lord retired to their tent, furiously recording the heaven-sent glyphs from the ancestors above.
