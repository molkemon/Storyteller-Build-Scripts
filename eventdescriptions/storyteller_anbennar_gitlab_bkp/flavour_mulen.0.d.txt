Long have we waited, as the sun elves first faltered then fell. Our estranged partners in Siadan have grown weary, and long to be reunited. The words of Mula SwiftSparrow are ever on our lips: a time for sowing, and a time for reaping. A time for tracking, and a time to dive in for the kill. Kirija well these patterns read, and has undergone a ritual of temporary empowerment, borrowing the light from a dozen new moons in order to fuel her magical power. She rings the tribe, soaring high on updrafts of her own creation. For a moment, she is glorious, and if we follow her we may be glorious as well.
