The vizier is the second most powerful position in the Harimraj, bearing the Raja’s seal ring and a significant fraction of their authority. But they only serve at the pleasure of the Raja, and thus may be dismissed without cause at any time. That is not to say such an act will be without consequence, impacting one of the throne’s limbs in managing its subjects and causing a major scandal at court. But perhaps it is time for the ruler to do it all the same.
