Whilst rebellion has been rife across our nation, it has for the most part been fragmented and scattered, with little in the way of leadership; that is until now. The rebels have rallied around a capable and charismatic leader who has at last managed to pull the various factions in the ongoing crisis together. Even now, whispers reach our ears of the promises they have made to the people of the realm. To remove the current rulers from power and set up a regime under their control, to remove corruption from our society, and create a new fairer realm as decreed by Surael. While utterly preposterous, their message has clearly resonated with the unquiet rebels. Thousands flock to this leader’s banner and their armies bear down upon us; we must take up arms for the future of our people and the victory of Surael’s will.
