It started slowly, couriers coming in saying that for once there had been no disappearances this past week. But all knew this couldn’t be true salvation and only indicated that it would be the beginning of the end for whatever the Hag of Oldhaven’s plans were. These outriders are then followed by more recent ones who give us the worst news. All around the nation there are charmed mobs, causing trouble and seizing control of what they can. What’s worse is that these charmed individuals are clearly those who have been missing all this time under the strange masks they wear.  While in most parts of the country they have been repulsed and put down as it seems we are required to do by this foul puppetry, in Oldhaven itself, the largest of these mobs is thousands strong and has marched to the epicentre of that cursed land with the Hag herself at its head. She seems to be conducting a dark ritual and all our hedge mages can feel it all across our land. Whatever it is she channels her efforts and all this sacrifice and energy towards, it cannot be good or right.  We must take back the country, and finally put right what the founders did wrong all those years ago. For the redemption of House Blacktower, in the name of Carleon and Kalista and for all that is good in the world, that we might still fight for it.
