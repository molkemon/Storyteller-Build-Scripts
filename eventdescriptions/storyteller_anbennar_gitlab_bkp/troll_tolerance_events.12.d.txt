A group of adventurers scouting the interior of the Shadow Swamp for rare plants has been victimized by a cruel "prank" from their Swamp Troll guide. Apparently she directed them to go through a patch of YaiYai, a horrible nettle whose sting is said to be the most painful in the world. While merely a severe irritant to trolls, it can be crippling to the normal constitution. It's not clear if the afflicted gatherers will ever walk again. To her credit, the troll was able to carry the entire group safely to a nearby settlement and seems honestly chastened. The troll claims that this is a common form of hazing amongst Swamp Trolls, and she didn't realize just how debilitating the plant would be to their bodies.
