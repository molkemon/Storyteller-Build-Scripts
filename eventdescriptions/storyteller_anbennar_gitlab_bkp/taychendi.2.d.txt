Terrible news! The ruler is dead! This can only bring horrible consequences for our domain. Whoever takes the empty throne must now navigate the fickle and unstable politics involved in the succession of the new king. Our provincial governors and local nobility grow disobedient, acting increasingly autonomous from our rule due to the weakness of the new lord. Even worse, the subject lords have made their move, taking full advantage of the current instability of our realm. They have raised their armies, seeking to gain freedom for themselves. A great storm is coming, we must act quickly.
