Applause filled the palace grounds of the newlyinaugurated Bloopalaasd as the first official performance of 'The Call of Aelantier' drew to a close. As the actors took a bow and retreated from the openair stage, the band started up a performance of wellknown Lorentish ballroom songs. Adran Eldergreen, newly appointed Head Councillor of the Kongdoum of Daccasded, turned to the Gommo representative standing beside him in the executive section. "What a party. Care for a dance, Mindi?"  "I'd rather die," said Mindi Whistletossle.  "Ha. Suit yourself." Adran lifted his hand and shot a magical shower of sparks into the night sky, prompting a round of cheers from the gathered audience. He started to do a somewhat embarrassing dance along with the music as Mindi rolled her eyes and turned to look back at the stage. "Not a party gal, I take it?"  "I'm here because I'm being paid," said Mindi, with an air of aloofness. "If I wanted to make a fool of myself, I'd quit the Gommo and join this.theatre troupe of a country."  "Have you considered it?" Adran did a spin, shooting another shower of sparks into the air to yet more cheers from the crowd. "An administrator as excellent as yourself could do great things here."  Mindi scoffed. "Sure, maybe when the sky falls on our heads."  Suddenly, gasps of surprise arose from the crowd, the band abruptly pausing the music. The crowd looked at once to a shining flash of blue racing through the night sky, growing larger and larger. Dames tear. The meteor slammed into the ground what felt like under a mile away a shockwave rippling through the palace. A few windows shattered, and many in the gathered crowd stumbled on their feet. The partygoers stood in stunned silence for a moment, watching a cloud of dust rise into the air. Then the band resumed, the celebration roaring back to life even stronger than before.  Mindi slowly turned her head to look at Adran, who shot her a wink and a huge grin. "Well?"  "No."
