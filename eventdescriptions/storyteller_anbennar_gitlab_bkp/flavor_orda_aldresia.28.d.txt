Entering Bal Doston itself, we were hit with the stench of blood and death. The servants of the castle were flayed and we met dozens of elite vampiric guards in the many halls of the grand fort.  Arriving at the foyer before the throne room, we caught sight of a confusing battle. Vampires fighting vampires. Was there some dissent in their ranks? The Umbral King’s forces were beset by a group of vampires cutting, through them with the ferocity of ten men each. We stayed silent up the stairs as they finished their quarry before one of them…called out to us.  ‘Come Aldresian, we are no enemies of yours…yet.’  With a deep breath, the Grandmaster stepped out, the shining blade of Aldresia drawn just in case. The leader of this insurgent vampiric group hissed seeing the blade but stood its ground, making no hostile moves.  ‘We are no ally of the Umbral King. We want this warlord gone as much as you.’  The Grandmaster tilted his head in confusion but the vampire crossed his arms.  ‘He sits in his throne room, cornered and feral. Will you deny our assistance at the eleventh hour?’  The Grandmaster looked back to his battered party, they had lost one of their own and half of them were at death’s door. They had to finish the fight.  Stepping forward, and turning toward the door, the Aldresians and the Vampires marched united into the throne room of the Umbral King.
