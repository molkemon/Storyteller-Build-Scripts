The Fangaulan religious practices are not so different than our own. Their gods most certainly exist, as do ours, so there is no need for strict divisions between our people. The Nakavy Avo is the Great Family, after all. It is time that we bring these two faiths together, and with them our peoples. The Fangaulan people are traditionally split into two camps, those that inhabit the great jungles of Dao Nako and those that roam the vast plains to the north. While they all recognize their gods as powerful, the different factions often prefer some gods over others. Should we prioritize the gods of the plains or the gods of the jungle?
