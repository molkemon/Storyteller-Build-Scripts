Taking a short but needed break from their duties, our lord recently went out for a quiet stroll in a nearby forest. There, among the lush greenery where shadows danced from the setting sun, they caught a glimpse of something white out of the corner of their eye. To get a better look, they turned and found themself eye to eye with the ethereal White Tiger standing some distance away. After staring at each other for a while, the White Tiger left and could no longer be found. The rumors spread quickly throughout the realm and our lord’s guards and attendants were able to give testimony and credibility to the story: that our great lord was visited by the elusive White Tiger. After all, the White Tiger is a spirit that is only rarely seen by mortals and is said to only reveal itself to the just and virtuous. Thus, our subjects all rejoice at the thought of their lord having been chosen by the White Tiger.
