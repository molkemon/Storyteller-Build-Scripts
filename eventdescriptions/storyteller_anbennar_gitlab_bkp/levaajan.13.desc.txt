The expansion of the country has outpaced the order brought by the previous Monastic Rule, and just like the robes of one of the most faithful, they must be refitted and redesigned as needed, the fabric of society respinned into shape. Their lord listens inquisitively as the various orders of the monasteries make their cases known: petty arguments over Monastic borders, heated disputes over the poaching of preeminent scholars, and other such drivel. There is even a report of monks taking matters into more violent hands, the men apparently concluding that assault was a better solution than peaceful debate. It is clear that the cloth of our teachings is starting to fray from overuse. Their lord looks at the quill before them, before jotting down the next line. Perhaps if the borders between the Monasteries were to loosen, if they were allowed to spread to each other. Yes. This is the first step, down a path their ruler will see our lord walk. And with this walk, so too shall the fabrics begin to reveal a new pattern of interweaved cloth and expensive silk, pointing the way towards the future in gold supplication.
