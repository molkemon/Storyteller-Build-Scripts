As the party turns the corner on the cave leading away from the water, they spot a strange fey creature that looks like a panther with many tails sleeping on the edge of the cave. Light from the lanterns illuminates it, but… not in a normal way. It is almost like the light bends around it, as if by a million tiny mirrors. The expeditionaries attempt to approach it to kill it in its sleep, but it wakes before they reach it. A short fight ensues, and the creature manages to kill several expeditionaries before fleeing into the caves.
