The mist twirls. The leaves rustle. The heart of a deer beats. Lucian feels everything happening in his realm yet he has no control over it. He cannot calm the mist, he cannot halt the wind, and he cannot stop the deer's heart. Lucian knows the spell. Its intent was to bind the ancient curse to him, and not him to it. He will not bend the knee. He needs to focus more on his new connection to the mist and the spell. All he needs is time, and he and the mist are not going anywhere.
