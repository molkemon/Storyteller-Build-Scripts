As we reorganize our forces to complete our unification of the realm, a familiar name has risen through the ranks. Kelindas Laskaris, a descendant of Laskaris the Conqueror, has emerged as a formidable commander of the Ameioni forces, and professes his eagerness to complete what his ancestor has started in these lands. The house of the Conqueror has been a constant if sidelined presence in Ambhen politics since Laskaris' death, as Molost fears over another potential monarchist revolt have stymied their ambitions—nonetheless, Kelindas' skills are comparable to his forebear, so perhaps he may prove himself to be a Conqueror yet.
