The young craft of artificery cannot match the might of artefacts crafted by the ancient art of inscription, at least not yet. But what it can do is the creation of durable, precise and flexible tools that are needed for the process of inscribing an artefact, and that at competitive prices. This way, while inscribed artefacts may not yet be reproducible by mere artificery, the number and quality of such artefacts that a single Kuresimi or Wishcrafter can create has been enhanced quite drastically. Sometimes it is not the flashy end result in which artificery truly shines, but by improving and expediting the many necessary inbetween steps required to reach that result.
