Gnolls. The demon spawned monsters of a bygone age that linger still in the deeper deserts and hills of Sarhal and Bulwar.  Once, they reigned over humankind, enslaving them and oppressing them. Though it is said that before The Xhazobine came, many of the human cities prospered under gnollish rule, that prosperity still came with heavy taxes and the knowledge that the money came from the exploitation and enslavement of humans. After The Xhazobine, of course, things in Bulwar were nightmarish. Though she was eventually defeated, gnollish warlords would go on to ravage Bulwar in constant fights and raids for power and slaves.  Everything changed when Jaher ta'Diranhria arrived in 1001 AA and began his liberation of Bulwar and the lands beyond. The Sun Cult named him Surael reborn, after he banished the gnolls from our lands and freed the human cities from their oppressive yoke. But the gnolls of today are not the gnolls of the past and we must decide what is to become of them.
