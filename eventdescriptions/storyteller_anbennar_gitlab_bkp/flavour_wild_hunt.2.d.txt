After the First Incursion, the Wild Hunt had reason to fear. The Triunics sought revenge, the surrounding provinces looked with greedy eyes towards our realm, and the ordsikars jockeyed for status as the problems of the Wild Hunt mounted. In the middle of this storm, the Wild Hunt seemed doomed. We could have chosen submission. Instead, we rebuilt. We grew stronger than we were before. We learned from our failings. Above all, we did not lose sight of our goal—vengeance not only for every centaur killed by the Triunics before, but for our clan members and family killed in the Incursion. The sneering Caehns of distant lands, jealous of our heritage and our guidance, sought to discredit us by claiming we were doomed—that we were trapped inside a storm. They did not realize that we are favored by Irdaeos, who controls the storms. They did not realize that we are not trapped in the storm, we are the storm.
