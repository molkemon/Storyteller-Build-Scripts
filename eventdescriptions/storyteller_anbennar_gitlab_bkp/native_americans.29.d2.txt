A woman from their ruler's tribe approaches their ruler while they are fishing as their tribe usually does to feed their people. Their ruler raises their head towards her, their eyes meet and they know she has seen something unusual, but good, nevertheless. With piety in her gaze, she whispers how she has seen the biggest salmon ever, a true incarnation of the spirit of the river we so deeply worship. They follows her to the spot where she saw it and after a few breaths they finally sees it. It is huge indeed. They prepares their tools to capture it.
