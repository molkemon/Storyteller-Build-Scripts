The matter of the Cuahu Teotlehu is decided.  We must restore the Cuahu Teotlehu. The will of gods is not to be taken lightly, and the cost of missteps is great. The Volcanic Truth demands change, but if every person pulls in a different direction we will be held in place, unable to move towards our destiny. Demands must be made of the interlopers who control the slopes of the Twin Gods: if they continue along their current path, they will be consumed by the fire of our faith. This is the only path forward.  The magmascryers have shown our council the way forward, and now the foremost duty of the faithful is to continue along their path. A great change is coming, but the Volcanic Truth has never feared change.
