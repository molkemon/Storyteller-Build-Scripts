’Speak,’ the voice from the shadows simply states. Long have you endeavoured to catch the attention of the legendary Gloam Knight, patron of those who espouse chivalric values. The Gloam Knight loyally serves the Fey Lords as the executor of their will; for those who do not break their oaths there is nothing to fear. ‘By your word many traitors have been felled; you seek my guidance. Be thankful that I offer this boon to you, Autumnsworn, for your ancestors broke their oaths long ago. Only by your allegiance now are you protected from my wrath.
