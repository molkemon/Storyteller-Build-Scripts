A thick scroll bearing the mark of the Sirens awaits attention.  All Siren commanders are obliged to provide a report to the matriarchy concerning the ongoings in their posts, but this report has made its way here due to its description of a rather interesting phenomenon. The popularity of the Sirens with both the Mengi humans and some of our own has been observed already, but amongst some it has risen to almost fanatical levels. Socalled ‘fangroups’ have been forming, and the names of Particular Sirens of note are circulating throughout our realm.  Whilst novel on its own, the truly fascinating facet of this phenomenon is the behaviour elicited from the Odanchesta. Used to being notorious, many Odanchesta seem to have taken their declining import in our cultural consciousness poorly, and as such have begun to perform ever more reckless and daring actions in competition with the Sirens.
