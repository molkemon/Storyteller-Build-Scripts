There is a new artist selling his wares in market stalls across the country. He is a diminutive kobold wearing a hooded cloak, and he has gained rapid fame for his proficiency across numerous artistic disciplines, including poetry, portraiture, and musical composition. His mysterious figure adds to his allure, but what has truly inspired the dedicated following he is accruing among collectors and lovers of art is the way his perspective appears to be unified even across mediums. His work very often takes the form of reflections across centuries, and ruminates on the way things have changed and the way things have stayed the same. He is saving up to construct a workshop where he can construct works of greater detail and beauty, including murals and tapestries. There are many among the court who support this endeavor, and are attempting to convince our ruler to help pay for it by sitting for one of his portraits and hiring him on as a court artist. Reportedly, he has a unique way of making his subject's eyes shine true to life.
