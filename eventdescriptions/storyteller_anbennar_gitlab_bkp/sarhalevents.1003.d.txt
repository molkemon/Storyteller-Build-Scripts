The lizardfolk of Taneyas have a profoundly ancient history. By their own count, three hundred and thirty two empires have come and gone over the span of more than a hundred thousand years. The histories of the naga bureaucrats, who advise and counsel lizardfolk rulers, chronicle events further back than any other living culture on Halann.  Central to the lizardfolk creed of Anzalkatsa; literally ’the inheritance of the Gods’; is the prophecy of a 333rd Empire, a final Empire which will rule forever in boundless prosperity. Whether to regard such prophecy as mere metaphor has fluctuated over time among the Anzalkatsa elite, but since the collapse of the 332nd Empire in Anzara in the 200s AA, the consensus has become much more literal. The 333rd Empire is real, and it will exist physically on Halann.  As such, no realm has won widespread recognition from naga or the public as the 333rd Empire, for all recognise the magnitude of such an immense claim. That is, until now.  Bearing the livery and standard of Khatalashya; the anzara term for the Final Empire; messengers have been dispatched across Sarhal. They do not seek recognition for Khatalashya, for this is not the way of the lizardfolk. They simply announce its arrival.
