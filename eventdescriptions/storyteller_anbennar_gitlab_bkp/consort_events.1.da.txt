It is with some consternation that the mighty families of the realm have watched the lord letting the years pass by without finding someone to marry. These feelings have now turned into open dismay as despite the realm lacking a clear line of succession our lord appears to have no plans to find a spouse nor is they showing much interest in the proposals sent to them. It has now reached the point where the court is expecting the lord to accept one of the offers on the table. Continued failure to secure the succession puts the entire realm at risk. Luckily one of the oldest and most prestigious families in the government has offered us the hand of one of their daughters, along with a generous dowry.
