REPORT: A handful of goblins, who have been dwelling in Bulwar for some time now, have taken an interest in an unfamiliar form of local magic, known as inscription. They are convinced that such magic could have potential applications for artificery, and seek to further understand the craft of inscription as a result. Our officials have identified that the Lawassari magi clans of Bulwar are particularly adept at this form of magic, and may be amenable to teaching it to certain goblins.  After careful vetting, we have selected 138 suitable candidates, and given them our support in presenting a petition to the Lawassari. In exchange for granting these goblins tutelage in the practice of inscription, we have also made some allusions to the Lawassari being granted some ambiguous favor by the state. Whether this promise is ultimately delivered on is immaterial; especially as we anticipate close work with goblins will engender a sense of commonality between our kind and their clans which can be leveraged to the state's advantage
