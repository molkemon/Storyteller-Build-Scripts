None of the magmascryers are quite sure what to make of the new creed called the Molten Eternity. On one hand, they share our values. They believe that change is necessary in all things, and that it is the duty of all to strive towards that change. On the other hand, they believe that there is a point when that change will inevitably stop. A culmination of all change means that there will eventually be a point when the world is stagnant, once their promised deity comes.  The Volcanic Truth teaches us that the only constant are the Twin Gods, but the Molten Eternity do not even care about their proclamations. And yet, more and more of the faithful turn to the Molten Eternity, and their ways of changing the world grow more and more prominent. If our faith is to survive, we will have to figure out the Molten Eternity's relation to the Volcanic Truth, and whether it is compatible with our beliefs or if it poses a danger to us.
