Once upon a time, Ninšara I and her half-brother, Araš VIII of Akalšes, were poised to bring peace to the country through their shared efforts. Yet, they found their ambitions thwarted and their realms reduced in size, with our control shrinking from most of the Harpy Hills to our current territories, while Akalšes similarly shrank from controlling most of Bulwar Proper to their present diminished state on the shores of Lake Naza. But now, a new chance for us to rise presents itself, for the stars have aligned to deliver new heirs to both Nanšalen and Akalšes born on the same summer solstice—a clear sign by Surael that they are destined for greatness. Destiny is on our side, so we must throw off the shackles of our overlords and fulfill our Divine Duties!
