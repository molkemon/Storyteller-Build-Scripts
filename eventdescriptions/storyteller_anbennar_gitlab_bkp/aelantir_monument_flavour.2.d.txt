Our advisor was completing an affair in the harbor, when they stopped to take in the view: A couple decades ago, Old Soara was a derelict ghost town, walking through it streets was a stark reminder of the Ruin that came to Aelantier.  Nowadays, the city has moved far past these humble beginnings. The Precursor buildings have been restored and refurbished, mixing the ancient elven architectural style with those of their new inhabitants. Every street is full of life, and the harbor full of ships, cargo and new migrants arriving from the world over.
