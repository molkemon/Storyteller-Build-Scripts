Even we, made of metal, age. After my many years, I can feel my own body failing me. Though perhaps my end might never come, in truth, I can feel the effects of my many years of rule. My heart does not have the strength it once did. I've done my best to keep moving forward as our realm needs me, but it has been hard for me to save my energy while ruling. Should I seek to find a way to strengthen my heart to keep ruling? Or is it perhaps best for me to retire and let someone else continue in my place?
