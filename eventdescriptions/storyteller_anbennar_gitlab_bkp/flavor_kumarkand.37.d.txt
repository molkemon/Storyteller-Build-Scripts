The most important templecomplexes of the SarArukanas, or Bulwari Sun Cult as it is known to foreigners, are by now located within our borders. It therefore has fallen to us to provide them with the resources they need to fulfil their spiritual duties for the benefit of all of Bulwar. By providing a ‘honour guard’ of sorts, we were able to convince the Kasra of these temples that their security is in competent hands and also, incidentally, that it remains in their best interest to align their sermons with the aims of our court.
