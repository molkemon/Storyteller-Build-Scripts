When it comes to being the Steward of The Sorrow, many things pose a challenge, but the hardest thing is definitely appeasing the Khets themselves. From the tedious codes of Khet savoirvivre to personal accommodation of basic Khet needs, the Steward always has their hands full with requirements they aren’t always able to fulfill. Thus it seems that no matter how much the ruler changes their appearance and no matter how sweet their demeanor can be, the Khets always seem displeased with their upbringing. The Khets simply have never seen such a creature apart from exotic slaves. They do not believe in their capabilities, treating them as a de facto figurehead for either High Priest, Khetarch or some other delusion. This caused them to subconsciously indulge into even more mischief than possible, only theoretically unshackled from what is normally an annoying human Steward.
