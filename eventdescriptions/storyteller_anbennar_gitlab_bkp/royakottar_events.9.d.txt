The Nymkorai and Kattyva both have extensive interests in the realm. While the Nymkorai work with local merchants and warlords to expand their trade empire, the Kattyva often barrel through them in their quest for reklics. Recently, the Kattyva sacked a petty city-state belonging to a minor warlord, who fought the family over their excavation of a slave warren beneath his lands. This has incensed the Nymkorai, who had captured the warlord in their own protection racket. Now, the Nymkorai seek to right this wrong. The Nymkorai are advantaged in this particular struggle, and if we do nothing, they seem likely to win.
