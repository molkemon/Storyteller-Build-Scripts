In the eyes of many, it was the capture of Szicheng that truly heralded the restoration of the League. Not only have we unified the decaying corpse of Yanszin, now we have returned it to its greatest heights.  Many in the city view us as liberators; a perception we are happy to entertain. As our forces prepare festivities for the common folk, our agents work through the influential class; bribing the greedy, threating the meek, and slaying the defiant to ensure that the city will accept its new fate.
