Following the rising tensions between the Adeanic and Corinite factions of the Regent Court, the emperor has issued the Proclamation of the capital city which states that only Adeanic rulers may be elected emperor. The Corinite Imperial estates have responded by forming the Corinite Accord, with a joint army and treasury and seeking ties abroad. The Adeanic states, feeling threatened by this new alliance, have regrouped into an Adeanic League. The stage is set for religious conflict in the Empire.
