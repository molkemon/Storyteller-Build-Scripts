The Grand Vizier of the Harimraj lives and breathes the Lotus Court, from political maneuvering to bureaucratic manipulation. But today, our ruler is being sent away from court, requested by a foreign ruler to oversee a distant city, where a recent scandal has engulfed the local ministries and left a leadership vacuum in the area. It will feel weird to leave court, but such a role undoubtedly has opportunities for influence and selfdealing. The way is lit, the path is clear: the vizier shall.
