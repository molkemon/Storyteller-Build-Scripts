The ship of state sails under its own wind, and a new hand on the tiller can only change course so much. Whether commoners view our ruler’s government as tyrannical, benevolent, or something in between ultimately comes down to the laws of the land, the bureaucratic and judicial apparatus that administers them, and the local magistrates who enforce them. However, the occupant of the Dragon Throne still holds great sway. Our monarch can issue proclamations, appoint, dismiss, banish, and execute officials at will, and use their supreme authority as they see fit. The realm anxiously awaits which direction the newly crowned lord will steer our realm. That said, the lord, being seemingly deathless, has yet to reveal how they will chart the course for our realm in their long years to come.
