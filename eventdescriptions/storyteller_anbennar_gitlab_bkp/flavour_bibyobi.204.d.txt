The Griots are the traditional storytellers of Horashesh. Purveyors of ancient knowledge telling of heroes, ideals and great events, the Griots form the basis of the Noukahi Pantheon's oral tradition. With our battles in the north against Khetist raiders, some Griots have begun telling tales of our deeds further in the south, where proKhetist sentiments run colder. It might behoove us to aid the Griots in spreading these tales, as they are integral to the Horasheshi understanding of current events in the wider world. We might even win allies if the stories are good enough.
