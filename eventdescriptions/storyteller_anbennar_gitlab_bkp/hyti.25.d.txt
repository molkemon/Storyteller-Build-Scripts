With Nyamau's constant breaches of protocol, is no surprise that many young impressionable harpies of the court end up fascinated by the misfit. It often doesn't last long, thanks to the wishcrafter's grating personality, but still a handful of them end up orbiting in her circle, and making research their main interest.  This time the captivated soul happens to be the favorite heir of our ruler. While study of nature and magic is considered a very worthy endeavour in our nation, an heir to the throne should still be focusing their time on a varied education to be ready to lead once they ascend to the throne.
