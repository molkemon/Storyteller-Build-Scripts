The new dance was ecstatic, allowing for all of us three. We felt the magic within us and knew this was meant to be. I bore our great child, delighted beyond belief. After so many years of fighting, finally in my belly grew relief.

She was the strongest of us all, our child born of pure love. We knew she would do great things, the one I had always dreamed of. We closed our eyes as she played, our bodies together curled. Finally, we rested our old bones and felt love for all the world!
