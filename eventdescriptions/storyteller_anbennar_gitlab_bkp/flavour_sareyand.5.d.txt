The streets of Sareyand are always crowded, its proud inhabitants sharing their home with merchants, caravan guards, pilgrims, visiting nobles and soldiers of fortune hailing from all corners of the realm and beyond. Yet on this most auspicious day, even the usual throng pales in comparison to the masses gathered in anticipation of the forthcoming celebrations. The market squares are filled with food banquets for the masses, the bazaars and avenues of the city host countless artisans, acrobats and storytellers engaged to entertain the masses. These festivities are held not just on occasion of the crown jubilee of our monarch, but as a grandiose celebration of the end of the lamentable divisions that have plagued Bulwar ever since the collapse of the Phoenix Empire. No longer is Surael's Garden divided into petty fiefdoms of even pettier kings, squabbling over the last remnants of lost grandeur and impotently watching as the servants of the Darkness rose once more to threaten the faithful. Now, all of His sacred Garden, from the Mountain of Clear Sight all along the Suran to the estuary of our hallowed river, are gathered under the spread wings of the Ashen Phoenix, safe from outside threats and internecine struggles alike. With the land finally at peace, its pious inhabitants enjoy a prosperity not seen since the times of empress Jexis. And within the Ash Palace itself, the nobility of the realm, its many client states and emissaries from great powers all across Halann are assembled to pay homage to the glorious reign of our ruler. Clad in glittering jewels and shining silks, they fill the halls and gardens of the palace with a marvelous display of elegant opulence. It is a truly remarkable display of the might and glory of our exalted realm and the august ruler occupying its Sunfire Throne.
