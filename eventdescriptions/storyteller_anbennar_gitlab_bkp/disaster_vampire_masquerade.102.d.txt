Last night, an army of thralls led by a small group of feral vampires attacked a small village in our realm. The news is already spreading throughout our realm, telling of the brave men and women who fought back the attack and forced their retreat, saving the entire village. These men and women were a small band of adventurers who were near the town as the attack started. Had they not been there, there is no doubt of the carnage that would have occured.
