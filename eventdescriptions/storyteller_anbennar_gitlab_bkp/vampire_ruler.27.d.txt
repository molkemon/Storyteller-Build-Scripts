The night watch of the capital has long been considered as sorry a lot as one could find in the city. The lord still recalls one of their advisors morose chuckle when they detailed their makeup in a recent defense budget meeting: a drunkard at the helm, subordinates including an obese oaf, a spotted miscreant (who, rumor has it, may not even be human), and a tall redhead possessed of the delusion that he is a dwarf. But tonight, they do not seem a joke in the same way. Just as the lord had dragged their victim into an alleyway and begun to feed, at their most vulnerable, torches appeared at the alley’s mouth, and the motley crew of uniformed men it illuminated sparked a feeling very unfamiliar to the predator: fear.
