It seems as if the people of the province did not manage to appease Vortzo Laukuno. It does not matter if their prayers were not heartfelt or their offerings poor - we will all have to pay for their failure. While the harvests in the region and the surrounding area have been disappointing during the last few years, at least some crops did grow. Now there is nothing. The capricious god of fertility disappointed us. The people of the province have some lean years ahead.
