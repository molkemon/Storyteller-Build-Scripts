I arrived in Ninakaire several months after it was found. The reclamation was rife with details of monster attacks and a diseased mound. The honeycomb structures of the Ananoma were truly a sight to behold, still standing taller after years of decay than Fahvanosy’s fortresses. Paladins bustled everywhere, set to the labor of love that was reclamation. I nodded respectfully as they hurried past me and bowed my head to them as they greeted my station.

"Madam Falana! I am glad you made it safely. What do you think of our hard work? This was made possible thanks to you and Madam Leli standing amongst such awe-inspiring stonework."

I looked around and imagined the potential. The towering Ananoma stood tall so long. Nodding to the Paladin beside me, I said, "Kyola would be proud of us being so strong."
