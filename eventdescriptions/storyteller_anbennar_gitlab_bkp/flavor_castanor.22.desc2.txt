Being first among the first, the Dragonflame defined the Castanorian art of war and perfected it to one single pillar: mankind's greatest asset is its will to learn. As such, the legionnaires have often been at the forefront of warfare innovation, and what they didn't invent was quickly assimilated before being put to good use.  Even today, whenever the Castanorian rank and file cross paths with the Dragonflame, they are sure to learn a trick or two.
