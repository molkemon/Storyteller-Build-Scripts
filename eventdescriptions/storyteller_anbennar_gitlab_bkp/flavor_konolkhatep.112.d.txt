Zinhakan stood at the end of the massive bullhorn. Crafted of iron and bone, her advisors assured her it could amplify her voice to be heard as far as Kheterat. The fire burned behind her eyes, in her throat, down her gut. Only destruction quelled the pain. She went to hold the end of the bullhorn, but her hand was blocked by the musket in her grip. She could no longer let go of it. Flesh and fur wrapped around its barrel, twisting it beyond recognition, demonfire flickering out the muzzle. She longed to use it again. When it fired, the pain disappeared.  "Curs of Kheterat. Listen to the voice of your demise." Her cry boomed across the Salahad, green flames firing into the air, her voice far louder than her engineers had anticipated. One screamed and dropped to the floor, blood trickling from his ears.  "I am the one that will end your theatre of playacting as khet, pretending you are gods. I am the one who made a pact with the xhazob, one being proven to be capable of killing a khet. I am a godslayer, and after I devoured your sleeping god in his pyramid, I shall kill every last khet along your river. I am the Gunpowder Xhazobine, with my power, Konolkhatep will burn.
