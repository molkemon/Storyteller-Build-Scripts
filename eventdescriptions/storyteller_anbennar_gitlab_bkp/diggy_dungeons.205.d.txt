The expedition descends from a rope next to the waterfall one by one, landing on the shoreline of a large underground lake. It shimmers in a beautiful silver, with minerals under the water reflecting the light of our lanterns, and fish appear to move near the bottom. The lake is fairly deep, and appears to have a cave underwater through where the water flows further. Two expedition members who are experienced swimmers immediately offer to see where these underwater caves go. Further down the lakeside, there is also a cave that continues a path above water. When light is shone into it, however, it is bent in a strange way, and many expedition members are uneasy about going in there.
