Our ruler has made it their life work to spread the dominant Faith and has quickly made a name for themselves among religious communities across our country. Their close ties to the established church has made clerics of various kind a common sight in our capital and an influential voice in the halls of power. Over time the fame of our lord has begun to spread even beyond our borders and our spies tell us that there are religious minorities among the subjects of our rivals who would support military expansion into their areas.
