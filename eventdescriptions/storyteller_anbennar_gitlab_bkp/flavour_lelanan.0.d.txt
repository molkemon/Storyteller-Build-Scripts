Rangita's passing was solemn. Having led Lelanàn for so long, the idea that she was gone felt wrong. Her funeral was grand, with Leli and Felana by my side. As we stood before Lel's shrine, the new High Priestess gestured wide.

"Though Rangita may be gone, we know the Nakazy guides her to rest. Her many teachings remain with us, pushing us all to be our best. Love freely. Love openly. Love often—her greatest teachings of all."

I lowered my head and reflected upon my own impossible wall. I had long kept out of relationships, as Paladins of Lel were expected to bear life. But my cursed body was born so wrong, and I could never be a good wife. Yet here I stood, now listening to those words. A cruel fate to those in different cities and a different start, but I was in the City of Love, not life. And Lel would never condemn these feelings in my heart.
