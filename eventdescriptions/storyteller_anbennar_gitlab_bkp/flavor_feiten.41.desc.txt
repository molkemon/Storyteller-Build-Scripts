The world's insatiable need for more whales has been a great boon to our economy, but this hunger has led to a boom in wouldbe hunters and fortune seekers who strip the old hunting grounds down to the bone. The discovery of greater fisheries out in open ocean has alleviated this somewhat, saving our industry, but the coastal fisheries along the Yanshen Coast have now been completely tapped out.  Our advisors suggest implementing a hunting license system to give the whales time to recover. Our whalers may not be happy, but it may be the only way to ensure that the whales still exist in the centuries to come.
