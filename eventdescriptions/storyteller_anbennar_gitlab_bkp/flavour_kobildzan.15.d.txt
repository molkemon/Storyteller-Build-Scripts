Three months after every new Ascension, a kobold must undergo a full physical to ensure the procedure's success. Today is Zircon's, and he awaits the results with anticipation. "Primary scale color is green," notes the doctor, glancing up from her clipboard. "Long, hook-like structure protruding from the forehead which can light up at will. Horizontal slats along the neck, leading to an advanced gill structure. Insect pair of mantles and siphons on either leg to assist underwater movement. Quite the set of new parts, I'd say. How have you been feeling?"

"Uh... wonderful," he says. "But it's a bit weird to be on land now. I like swimming. It's good, being a sailor. I get to spend most of my time outside the boat, repairing the outside while it's still at sea. You know? Get to use my Ascension, get to be what I needed to be."

"Mm-hm." The doctor nods. "And you're looking for more today? Another Ascension?"

"Yeah," Zircon nods. "Yeah, I want—something to make it work better. I still get tired easily, and when cannons start firing, I'm a pretty easy target. If there was a way to help either of these, it'd be nice."

"Then we've got two options," the doctor says, holding out two clipboards, each showing parallel diagrams. "They're both experimental, but they're becoming increasingly popular in their own right. The first: mana attunement glands. Makes it easier for you to tap into your Ascensions generally; makes you more connected with magic itself. A bit like mages, but more focused on the magic working upon you. The second: ebonsteel injections. With what we've got from Gerudia, we can make you nigh-invulnerable; kinetic material will reflect off of you like a mirror for bullets."
