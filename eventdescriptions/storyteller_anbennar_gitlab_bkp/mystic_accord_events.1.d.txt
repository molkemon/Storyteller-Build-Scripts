Aboard a ship sailing around the Lupulan Peninsula, our lord was struck by a desire to stop at an isolated island they were passing by. Stepping off the ship onto the warm sands, our lord felt a sudden rumbling from the island's heart. It shifted, then, with a steady inevitability, began to rise from the ocean, revealing, buried beneath the sand, a soft, rubbery skin. A great eye opened beside our lord, its unknowable depths searching for a response.
