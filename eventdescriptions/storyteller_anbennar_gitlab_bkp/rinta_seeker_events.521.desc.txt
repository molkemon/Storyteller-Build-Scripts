A routine accounting check of the funds we’ve provided for the construction of the Great Observatory has revealed a noticeable discrepancy. It seems a sizable amount of the money we sent has simply disappeared into thin air at some point in the process. Further investigation has tracked down the exact place this money disappeared: one of our advisors has been confiscating a portion of each payment as so-called ‘administrative expenses’ without our knowledge or approval. Confronted about this obvious corruption, our advisor attempted to pass it off as a simple miscommunication, though he is unable to provide any explanation for where the funds actually went. He’s begged for us to show mercy and sworn that he won’t let any more of these ‘accidents’ happen, but we would also be entirely justified in ordering his execution and seizing the deficit from his personal estate.
