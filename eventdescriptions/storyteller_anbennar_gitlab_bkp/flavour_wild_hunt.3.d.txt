In our period of decline, the plains have moved on without us. The clans farther away from the lakes have not suffered as we have, and they have taken the time after the Incursion to build a Caehnate they are claiming is greater than ours ever was. Their currency has spread as far as our markets. On one side is a picture of their ruler. On the other, an image of the skull of Wild Hunt broken in half, to commemorate the "Great Defeat of Buruvaqoyla." This is unambiguous—they seek to drag the good name of our Caehnate through the mud for the sake of their own petty political machinations. They care nothing for the fate of the centaurs or vengeance for our ancestors. They would not even care if the Triunics killed us all, so long as they were killed last. The court cries out at their injustice. What shall we do in response?
