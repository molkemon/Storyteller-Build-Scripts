Orcs fleeing from the Escanni slave trade have made their way to Varaine, looking for a place to settle. While we believe that integrating these orks into our society could lead to great things for everyone involved, doing so would be a hard sell for some of our population and neighbors who were deeply affected by the Greentide. On the other hand, our dwarven Communities seem to have their own ideas as to what we should do with the orks; let them into Varaine under the condition they agree to work for the dwarven cartels. While this would be better than the slavery they were subjected too, it still wouldn't be particularly accepting of their people.
