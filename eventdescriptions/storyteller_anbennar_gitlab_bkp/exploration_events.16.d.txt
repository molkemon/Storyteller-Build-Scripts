Our fleet set sail to find a western sea route to the rich cities of the Gulf of Rahen. It took them 99 days to cross the ocean westward, after they had found the Kimanis Bank located near the tip of South Aelantier. The strait was calm and they were escorted by local fishermen who warned them of the deadly winds of the Kaydhano Sea (actually a desert, it seems), but eventually, the captain and his crew made their way out into the ocean. The ocean was so calm and deprived of land that the crew thought of themselves as victims of The Great Wander, the event which saw the elves wander the seas for 1000 years after the Day of Ashen Skies. They would later recall that it might have been here that the elves wandered, and named it 'Ocean of the Lost'.  The fleet's captain died from a wound caused by a poisoned arrow after being mistaken for one of the protomechanims of Insyaa, possibly due to his shining armour. Two of our ships had survived, one tried to return across the Ocean of the Lost but was never recovered. The last ship continued west and sailed across the Uelos' Lament, rounded the Kedwali Coast, and as it arrived at our port it became the first ship to circumnavigate the globe.
