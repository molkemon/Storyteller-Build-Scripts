The first reports coming from the region speak of disorder and violence. Ashhana provinces have never been easy to hold, due the population living under the constant strain of the Blood Curse. As their communal ties loosen under nonAshhana rule, many Ashhana tribesmen drift into roaming war bands, striking without strategy or demands. They attack farms, roads, and isolated garrisons alike, then scatter, driven by the searing impulse of their curse. It will take time for Ashhana to regain their equilibrium and reduce the number of Blood Curse incidents in the region. Until that happens, local villagers will fear for their life and the infrastructure will be left to deteriorate among the rampaging Ashhana Lizardfolk.
