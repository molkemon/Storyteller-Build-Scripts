Defeat. Humiliation on a scale unheard of since the dawn of our people. Our warriors have proven completely unequal to the test in front of them. They were not sufficiently accustomed to their BoneBound equipment, and it showed in the incompetence of their offensives and the nonexistence of their staying power against the weaklings of Kalavend and Taychend.  This does not change anything. The fools that have routed us for now remain inferior and weak, and a few small victories do not change that. But the shame, oh, the shame of defeatit demands vengeance. We will repay them a hundredfold.
