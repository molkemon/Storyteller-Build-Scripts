The moss sorter lies dead, and the scouts have properly moved into the room. Ignoring the rainbow colored stone shelves, they search through every corner of this otherwise destitute cavern. Regrettably, it really does seem like the only thing of value was the labour of the now deceased kobold.  Undeterred by the smell, one of the scouts moves closer to the wall to investigate the various piles of moss on the shelves. Unfortunately for him, the smell proves stronger than the room's previous inhabitant, and it quickly overwhelms the brave soul. As he falls to his knees, he accidentally brushes his arm against several shelves and prompts a cascade reaction from the pottery. Now covered in moss, clay and blood, the poor sod is writhing in pain. The rest of the party exchange looks, first horrified, then awkward, and finally amused until a sudden scream pierces their ears. Feeling the ground tremble, they leave their companion to his fate and run for their lives.
