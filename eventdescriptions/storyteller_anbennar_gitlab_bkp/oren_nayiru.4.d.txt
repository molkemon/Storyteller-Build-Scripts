When Nayiru revealed himself, the Oren Nayiru believed that an imminent war was coming, which would liberate Nayiru's faithful and herald in a new age. All they needed to do was gather strength, spread Nayiru's word, and then overthrow the tyrannical regimes of Taychend, with the power of sun and steel at their backs. However, exactly when this great war should be launched has been a matter of great contention, with the faith splitting between those who want to launch an imminent uprising and those who would rather wait for more opportune circumstances. The Uesrennu cell, perhaps both the strongest and most radical of the Oren Nayiru chapters, have decided that now is that opportune circumstance. Led by the charismatic firebrand Calsithara the Flamespeaker, they believe that with the power of Nayiru at their backs, they can sweep away their enemies. Even though half of the faith have spurned the uprising, deciding instead to remain in hiding, Calsithara still commands a vast host, and already much of Taychend has been set alight by the fire of rebellion. Should this rebellion be defeated, however, this would undoubtedly be a major setback for the Oren Nayiru, with the location of participating cells easy to determine. One must wonder, however— if this is the power of only half their strength now, what could a united Oren Nayiru accomplish?
