Many armies have tried to destroy Ajgriijarul, and just as many have faltered and become just another bloodstain on its walls. Today though, the citadel has met its match. Its defenders fought with ferocity and bravery, not giving up an inch of their home without a fight. Though most of the warriors fell in battle, what few survivors there were scattered throughout the countryside, angrily assaulting all that approach them. The future of Azjakuma hangs in the balance.
