It is normal for Taychendi rulers to model their reigns off those of past lords, so that they have both a guideline for which policies to pursue in order to gain their own place in the heavens, and so that they can patronize a solid selection of advisors to help them do so. In the course of a ruler's life, however, they encounter many challenges that may encourage them to change direction—perhaps it is time for our lord to do so as well?
