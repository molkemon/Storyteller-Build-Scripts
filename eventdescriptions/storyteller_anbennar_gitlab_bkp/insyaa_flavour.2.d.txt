Several colossal anteaters have begun to ravage our colony in the hinuisavannah. These beasts have begun treating our settlers like slightly larger, and far less tasty, termites, feasting upon dozens of our people at a time, apparently trying to make up for our bland taste with volume. Truly nowhere is safe from them, as their over 10meterlong tongues are able to snake through windows and doors to reach into buildings, dragging out kicking and screaming those who are unfortunate enough to be trapped inside. Tales of anteater attacks have spread far and wide throughout our nation, making it far harder to convince new settlers to move to our colonies in the savannah, for fear of becoming the anteaters' next snack. Envoys from the colony have already begun to arrive in our capital, demanding we take action to kill these beasts. Exterminating these nearly 30metertall creatures will be no easy feat, however.
