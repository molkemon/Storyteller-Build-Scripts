Latranoma is real.  This sentence may seem trivial, now that Vyzemby once again walk the Far Citadel’s ancient halls and architects repair its dilapidated walls. But for years, the existence of this city has been questioned. Hidden in the deep jungle, far from other Anamona, it has always been a place of mystery. Messages from this place to other Ananoma were rare, even when all five stood strong.  Some theorized it may never have fallen at all, while others called it a myth. Now we know the answer to both. Latranoma is real, and it did fall. But we know very little else. There are no signs of battle, no damages beyond those caused by time and the uncaring jungle. We do not know why the Vyzemby of Latranoma left, or where they went. Even now, this is a place of mystery. But, perhaps, time shall lift this veil for us.
