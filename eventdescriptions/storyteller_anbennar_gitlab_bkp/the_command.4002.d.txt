After the scourge that was Harimar brought his rampage to the home of our ancestors, only a vision from the Shaman Kibyon Zangyu spared us the wrath of the tiger-demon. He brought us into Nomsyulhan and concealed the way behind us. We knew not if any other hobgoblins had survived Harimar; knowing what we know now, we wish they hadn’t. They have become completely corrupted into agents of chaos. They destroy all that is good and sacred and are not content to keep their corrupted ways to themselves. They wage ceaseless wars to subjugate any who stand before them and twist the minds of their subjects, making them think chaos is order and order is chaos.

More horrifying than even that is a dark material they use: they name it Korashi, and it has the unfathomably terrifying power to consume magic itself, destroying it. Despite the corruption of our distant kin, it is their company that gives us more worries: Oni—demonic ogre-kin of myth—who are nothing like the Soulseekers we are familiar with. They are the dark minds behind the material, and though they’ve hidden it from our blind cousins, our eyes can see their true intentions, the corruption and domination of the spiritual world.

The existence of this Great Command and its subjects is a threat to the very world itself. It falls to us to destroy these servants of chaos, for no one else understands the danger they pose.
