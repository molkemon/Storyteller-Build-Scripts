The last mage stumbles back, drained, as the wards hum back to life. Even the non magical members of the expedition can feel the wards tugging at them, yearning to imprison and stifle them. The cage emits a steady, comforting hum as well. The strength of the cage is not in doubt to anyone; if Aldorlbur can be brought here then it will certainly be defeated and imprisoned once more. The only problem is it is equally aware of this, likely preferring to die fighting than end up in there for another eternity. Stiil, the newly powered up cage provides more than a measure of comfort.
