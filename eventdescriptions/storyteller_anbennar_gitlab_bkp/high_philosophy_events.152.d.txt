When the sun rises, the practitioners of the Radiant Dawn school are there to meet the new day. They pray to the High Gods and thank them for the morning and for all of the world around them. They are joined in turn by the few Sun Cult practitioners in Rahen, who in turn make their own prayers. The two groups have, for the centuries after Jaher's conquest of Rahen, prayed together in peace. However, in this age of strife, the peace has been broken by the arrival of Suhan's Praxis.  Like the old faiths of the Sarhal Deserts, and the Radiant Dawn school, Suhan's Praxis adherents also pray in the morning. The High Gods are thanked for their work in banishing primordial darkness in the first days of the world, and, most importantly, opening the path towards Purtara for all to walk. While many in Guru Suhan's circle had hoped this common group would bring them together, it has only torn the tapestry further.  Today, a group of Praxis practitioners faced the High Temple for their morning prayer and were met with stones, knives, and inexorable violence; violence that demands an answer.
