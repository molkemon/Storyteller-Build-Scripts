News from the Reach has arrived at Court; the region has risen against their orkish overlords. We who have long supported their cause are now asked to come to the Reach’s aid against Brasur Frozenmaw, that they might drive out the occupation and reestablish the human kingdom of Vrorenmarch. There is somewhat of a difference between supporting a rebel movement and entering open war, however; are we truly so committed to Crovis’ cause?
