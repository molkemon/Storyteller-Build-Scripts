Eyes are the window to one's soul, but what happens when that window closes? I lose the details of the world, can no longer see the beauty, and cannot even recognize my own people. I may not see as well, but I can still hear the people talking behind me, about all that goes on in the court that I miss. The army doesn't even bother trying to get my advice, as they know I will fail to understand the strategic situation on the battlefields. Should I try to fix them, or should I rest and let a new ruler take my place?
