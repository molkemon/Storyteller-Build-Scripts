Our curators have travelled to one of the Sunshine Manors in Sareyand, interested in seeing which striking piece of art would be most desirable to add to Medaka's Exceptional Treasures. Immediately, their eyes are drawn to the statues within the Sunshine Manor. Expertly crafted out of marble, many depict Sun Elves in moments of triumph or contemplation. Statues of Jaher ta'Diranhria are most common, and one of him kneeling in contemplation was from a story we people of our realm knew well from our former Kheteratan overlord. It was said that Jaher, who could not just be offered the Stewardship, was willing to trade all the sand on a beach for it. Of course, the veracity of that story is dubious, but it does track with the inept governance of the Khetarchs. This statue would certainly make a wonderful addition to the museum.
