Disaster. Our auxiliaries triangulated the wyvern nest to a single ridge, a great cavern that appears to be gouged out of the mountain. As they approached its lip, wyvern upon wyvern poured out of the cavern, and our expeditionary team was almost wiped out.  The few survivors report that the entire nest; perhaps the primary nest for wyverns across the Khenak; have been stirred into a great rage, and may soon threaten nearby settlements.
