Living in the hini terrain makes one understand how helpless and tiny the ants of other realms must feel. Of course, in our own realm, there are also hini ants capable of stripping entire settlements limb from limb to feed their colonies, so perhaps what we are is even more helpless than ants. Despite all this, we have learned to thrive in the hini terrain, and now have developed the tools necessary to ensure our enemies cannot. Amidst this land, every blade of grass can be a weapons platform and every passing animal can be a terrible threat, and now our artificers have developed the tools to make sure we can take full advantage of the terrain in each battle. When our enemies come to us, we now have the power to take the fight right back to them.
