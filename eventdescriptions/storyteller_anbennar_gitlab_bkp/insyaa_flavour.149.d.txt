Everyone who has heard the sky-splitting screech of a hinuiroc or the deafening drone of constant thunder emanating from the realm of Kiamoa is all too aware of the power of raw sound, capable of sowing dread into the heart of even the most stoic among us. But as with many other things, the mechanims have taken the concept and refined it further until they turned sound itself into a deadly weapon. The Sonic Firearm Implants are integrated in the upper extremities of a mechanim chassis, and operate by creating a resonating pulse that is constantly amplified within a containment chamber until a pinhole valve is opened to release the pent-up sonic energy in a tightly focussed beam. The power of this sonic beam is capable of shattering even the sturdiest wooden planks and punching a hole even through metal sheathing meant to protect a vessel, if targeted properly. Mechanim sailors equipped with this particular modification have thus become a welcome addition to naval vessels of all kinds, from warships eager to gain an advantage over their enemies to merchant ships looking for additional protection from pirates and the hinuibeasts roaming the waters around Insyaa.
