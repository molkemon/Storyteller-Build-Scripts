The Green Gate of Grozumdihr remains open for all who wish to enter, except the Exilarch, who had been cast out of these halls ages ago, and again recently. Yet his people, the Peridots, remain scattered throughout Rahen, dwarves who have not sat idly by, revelling in their riches but working hard and well, dwarves who have earned a place among the dwarves of Grozumdihr. We shall welcome them with open arms.
