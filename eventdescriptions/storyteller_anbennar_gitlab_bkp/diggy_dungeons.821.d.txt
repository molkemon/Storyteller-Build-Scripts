The wraiths descended upon the men, their icy bodies passing through us like a hot knife through butter. Many fell to the ground, their breathing hard and staggered, their skin blue and cold. The apparitions would make some more passes over and through us but then disperse again. Some of those who fell stood up once more, their flesh returning to normal, but others had gone too far into the ice, their eyes of horror frozen in place for eternity.
