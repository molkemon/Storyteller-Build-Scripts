When the lord of Kamrayakval declared war under the eclipse, he called on his faithful to kill the servants of darkness and furrow the fields with their blood. His forces have taken that message to heart. Across Silverspite's new realms, the banners of the Oren Nayiru put enemies and unbelievers to the sword. Though the lord of Kamrayakval has attempted—half-heartedly, in many cases—to reign in the excesses of his troops, this has done very little, and sword-point conversions have become extremely common.
