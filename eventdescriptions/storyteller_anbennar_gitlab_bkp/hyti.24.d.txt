Many goblins, by now completely assimilated into our society, have found jobs inside the workshops that have started popping up around the realm. Their sleight of hand, so often decried by those who believe them to all be thieves, allows them to be more productive with complex machinery than any other race of the realm.  The most enterprising among them have managed to negotiate better pay, thanks to the odd ingenious improvement to production methods they have come up with. It's not rare that the innovation is a bit too wild though, and many owners have been forced to request a meeting with members of the Isun Kes to solicit a second opinion, fearing the proposed "improvement" to actually be a hazard.  Today brings one such case, and a magnate from a local province has arrived for a consultation.
