Kaska felt something off as she trod through the Odd Water. She looked up, and saw a great strange object emerge from behind a wall; a Big Red Cube, the same colour as the Odd Water. She knew, somehow, that it could eat her, just as she knew she could eat Small Flying Morsels and Ground Walking Morsels.  She made a squawk and bustled for the exit, tumbling over herself and smacking straight into the wall of the Old Rock. The Waxy Spikes seemed to find this very funny at first, then much less funny once the Big Red Cube began eating their companions.  Kaska spent much of the next ten minutes bustling for the best, most protective Old Rock positioning possible, and was not much concerned with the yelling and the consuming and the gouts of acid which were so occupying the Tall Things’ time.
