The elves of Eordand follow the Fey of the seasonal courts: Spring, Summer, Autumn and Winter. Long ago we might have been united in said worship, but this isn't the case today, each season having its own dedicated followers, as well as Eordellon emphasis on  "seasonal balance"; however they interpret this. This division is also reflected on how the fey deal with us, as none of us are seen as longlasting or important enough for the most powerful among them to consider dealing with us, outside of the occasional patronage of specific individuals. To rectify this we'll need to find ways to increase our status among the fey, as well as eliminate the competition.
