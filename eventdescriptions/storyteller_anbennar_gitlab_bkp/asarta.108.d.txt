A thick smog of incense filled the room where the Court of Change resided. At its center, laid out on an array of cushions, sat Akhaxpa. As the ruler approached and knelt down, she shifted, but unlike most other times, she only opened her middle eye. It still burned bright blue as it always did, but there were shifting colors behind it this time.

"I see the Night Hags have been on the move again. On some level, I appreciate what they are doing. Without their advances, these lands will never change. They destroy, then retreat, and kingdoms are rebuilt anew. That is change, but it is a short-term change. For I know their goals. I can see the future they desire. A merging of the shadow plane with ours. The end of all life as the shadows absorb all."

Akhaxpa glared at the ruler. "You have to stop them. Change that is being wrought in the name of eternal nothingness is no change at all."
