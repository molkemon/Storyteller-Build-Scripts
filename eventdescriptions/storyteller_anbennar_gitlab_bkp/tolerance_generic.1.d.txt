Recently our Realm has not been faring well. Tax collectors are accused of extorting the populace, officials are widely considered examples of incompetence and nepotism, and the processing of legal writs and civil disputes is bemoaned as unfair and corrupt. Cruel fate is the only culprit, but our ungrateful subjects are unfairly pinning the blame on the government. Some have even begun to criticize our esteemed lord, and we're seeing the signs of brewing revolt. Together with all your advisors, we have held an urgent council meeting. We have agreed that since their myriad petty concerns are for now beyond our power to fix, the best course of events to at least prevent large scale rebellions would be to try and direct the masses' anger against another enemy. One of the minorities present in the realm, perhaps; they're already mistrusted, and with some careful messaging we could paint them as the cause of the current state of affairs. What is your decision, my Liege?
