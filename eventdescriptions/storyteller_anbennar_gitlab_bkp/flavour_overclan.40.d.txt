REPORT: By parliamentary decree, the state has officially divested itself fully from matters of religious faith within the realm. While previously acknowledging the rights of citizens to practice their own beliefs freely, this new decree will take this matter further, divesting any matters of theological concern from the purview of the state. In effect, there shall be a full separation between the concerns of state, and the concerns of the faithful; so long as the faithful obey the laws of the state.
