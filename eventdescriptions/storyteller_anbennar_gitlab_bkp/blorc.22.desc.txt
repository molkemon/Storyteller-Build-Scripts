The guns of Ovdal Kanzad took their toll. Their lord knew they would when they sent the forces against its walls. The massive batteries that manned its gates produced booms that could be heard from leagues away, and the forces that ultimately took it had climbed over vast piles of orkish corpses to get there. But the deed is done. Ovdal Kanzad has fallen. And we have learned a thing or two about enduring unrelenting cannonfire in the process.
