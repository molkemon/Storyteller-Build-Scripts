The design for the Godswatch is nearly complete, but still, the Mwalim and Darwiiz find more topics to argue about. Their latest disagreement concerns the central telescope. The Darwiiz believe the central telescope should be treated with great spiritual reverence, suggesting it should only be used by mages and spiritual leaders, while the general populace should use the peripheral telescopes. The Mwalim, however, argue that the central telescope should be accessible to everyone, as imposing restrictions would limit its use for research. A priority system might help, but neither side seems willing to accept it.
