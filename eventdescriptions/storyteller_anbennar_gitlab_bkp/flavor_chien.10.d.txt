It was early morning when our lord woke up. The last night was a joyous occasion, a great celebration of what has been accomplished by all people in the past few centuries. All ambitions were complete; We had cemented our rule over the Yanglam and Kai people, we had monopolized the gem market with arcane gemstones, and we had ensured the Binhrung state would be secured from internal opposition. Our lord stood looking out into the morning sky, wondering what could be next for Chien Binhrung. It was then when a sudden chill swept past our lord, blowing into her room. Recognizing the meaning of this, she turned around to greet her ancestor. Only this time, it was not just one. Nhitrac, Duyen, Ngoc, Suong, Huyen, and Jinmari all apparated around our lord. The living person stared at each and every one of them, shocked to see all of them outside of the temple. She was even more shocked when they all bowed in front of her. In unison, all six of the legendary people of Chien Binhrung said a phrase all subjects of the state utter: "Long Live our lord."
