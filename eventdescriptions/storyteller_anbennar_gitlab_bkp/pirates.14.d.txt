The privateer Captain a skilled sailor has made quite a name for himself in the service of the foreign Navy. The payment he has been receiving for his prizes however is much less than the full value of the prizes themselves. Like many savvy privateers, the captain has realized that he could make a greater profit by turning pirate. He has tossed his Letter of Marque overboard and made port in our capital. As a pirate of our realm he will be free to plunder to his heart's content and keep the spoils he has rightfully stolen.
