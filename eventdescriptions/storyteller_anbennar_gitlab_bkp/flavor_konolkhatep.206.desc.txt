Gnolllkind is much more naturally suited to the open plains or the desert wastes than a deep hole in the ground. Despite this, Golkora is now the world's largest openpit mine. Our lord was at the time in the middle of a feud with the dwarven population across the Divenal, who smugly refused to acknowledge the great achievements simply by living across the sea from us. In Golkora, we saw an opportunity to show up those fool dwarves. The expansion of the Golkora mine began immediately. Through the devotion of 50% of the mine's aboveground administration area to a Feasting Annex, they were convinced to aid excavation through the summoning of a number of meteorites onto the site of the mine, and our advisor were brought in to oversee the construction of all the necessary supports and infrastructure to keep the mine running. With the vast amount of housing and support buildings required to keep the mine running, Golkora now more resembles a dwarven hold than a mere mine. Gold was not the primary motivation for the Golkora mine's expansion, but we also found that in spades.
