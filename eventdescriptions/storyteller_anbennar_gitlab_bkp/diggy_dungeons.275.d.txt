Soon after the structure is lit on fire, the monster's screams are heard throughout the cave. As it emerges from the doorway, burning and screaming, it is quickly dispatched by the blades and magic waiting for it outside. After the structure has completely burned down, the expeditionaries dig through the charred ruins in an attempt to still find some of the treasures of the hag. They find some, and though most of them are burned or blackened, they should still fetch some coin.
