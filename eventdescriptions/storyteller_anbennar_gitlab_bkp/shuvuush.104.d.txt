Forlorn among the dusty soil and windswept grasses of the steppe lies a shattered mask, its bonewhite hue tainted by grime, its featureless elegance marred by dozens of cracks from the heavy step of a nameless terror bird’s talons.  This is the legacy of the vaunted Faceless Queen of Nuugdan Tsarai, all that remains of her ambitious goals and empty promises. Against all odds, her seemingly fated rise to preeminence was cut short on these empty plains. For it was the stalwart bravery of Hus Bhal and not the brash daring of Nuugdan Tsarai that carried the day.  Now, the confederation of clans assembled beneath her banner is shattered, each one desperately seeking to curry favour with the Izhuun of Hus Bhal, eager to throw their neighbour to the wolves if it means they might survive this unexpected upheaval. The clans assembled beneath our righteous banner have grown fat from the spoils of victory, and their appetite for further and greater triumphs has been awakened.  All across Shuvuushudi, the fierce winds carry the name Hus Bhal with them. And every other Izhuun lies awake at night, restlessly wondering if their bhaatum might be the next to fall to the might of Hus Bhal.
