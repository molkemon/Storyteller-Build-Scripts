The intermingling of goblins and elves caused by the closeness of our goblin shamans to elves has led to the emergence of a new goblin movement; one that seeks to emulate the wealth and grace of halfelven monarchs present across Cannor. Most curious amongst this practice is an insistence on the addition of "sil" to a goblin's name. Naturally, our lord has joined this movement, and demands that others refer to them as a "sil" aswell.
