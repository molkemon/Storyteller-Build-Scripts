What's worse than a pilfering rat or sucking bat? A brat.  And what's worse than a brat? A whole mischief of them.  The dmarvukotherwise known as brats in the Common tongueis a teensy, harmless pest with a nose for finding sweet foods and fermented drinks. By wing or paw, dmarvuks can soar to the highest silos or squeeze into the tightest cellars, making them a nightmare for farmers, brewers, and bakers. Unfortunately, a swarming mischief of the critters managed to slip inside a major storehouse in the province, consuming the contents withinwhat rotten luck.
