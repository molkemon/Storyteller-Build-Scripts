Peculiar visitors have asked to see our lord when night comes, their pale skin and hauntingly attractive features make many in our court uncomfortable with their presence. They say they are representatives of an association who would like to deal with their upstart brethren in our realm. They ask to be allowed to hunt them down with any methods they deem necessary, of course, no citizen of our nation would be harmed, and they insist that they require no reward for their services.
