Brrtekuh died as she lived, suddenly and without warning; like a sandstorm, she had emerged abruptly in her youth, a nameless gnoll who quickly consolidated the pack, and she left a final cataclysm in her departure. As many of the pack's efficient yet fragile structures were based around the rule of and loyalty to Brrtekuh, these quickly became either defunct or used as political devices to gain clout and legitimacy for aspiring gnolls upon her death.  Now the once united pack, singleminded in one goal, has begun to tear itself apart. Many gnolls have reverted to the violent logic of survival of the fittest, while once prominent military commanders raise banners under their own name as warlords. Yet strangely, these gnolls are still united under one call: that of the dead Brrtekuh, claiming to be her successor in some way, not seeking to create a new pack but seeking to continue the present one.
