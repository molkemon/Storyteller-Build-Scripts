Most consorts accept their limited power and devote their lives to various pastimes and their family, but not our consort. They are genuinely interested in all the ins and outs of governmental matters. Over the years they have befriended many great thinkers, politicians and diplomats. Among them is one of our most well-known statesmen, with whom they have spent a lot of time with as of late. Now our consort has asked us to make the statesman an offer, claiming that he would make a much better advisor than the diplomatic advisor we currently employ.
