It appears as if the gifts we accepted earlier were poisoned after all, just not in a way that we would expect from a horde of monsters. In preparation for an invasion of our lands, the ogres have recalled their advisors and abruptly halted metal shipments to us. With such a sudden shortage of iron, city militaries across Yarikhoi are scrambling to procure equipment for their forces. For the time being, we will have to face down these invaders at a disadvantage.
