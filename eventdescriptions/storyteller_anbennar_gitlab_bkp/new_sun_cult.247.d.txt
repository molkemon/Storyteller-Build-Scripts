It has been a long and difficult undertaking, full of conflicts and struggles of interests. Today, an entourage arrived at the palace from EduzAmmanu. In it were a hundred men and women, the first of the new human magi. What a glorious day for our country. But it was not the only entourage that arrived that day. The lord, the priests and members of the Exemplars came up with a final offer for our ruler: They will no longer hinder our magi, but, by virtue of the Jaherian Statute on the use of Magic, the clergy, not the state, will be in charge of monitoring and controlling the magi. Our ruler accepted, ending the long dispute with the clergy. Now, our ruler contemplates our new magi, and the great future that they will bring to our country.
