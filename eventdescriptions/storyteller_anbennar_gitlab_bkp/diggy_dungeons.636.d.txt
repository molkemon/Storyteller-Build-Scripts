The broken axle turns out to be a bigger problem than imagined and two of your best engineers spend a good hour on it without any success in sight. Suddenly, one of them has an epiphany; how about they try to reroute the cords attached to the entire system and attempt to rewire another axle as a substitute system? The second engineer agrees; brilliant suggestions. They get to work immediately, remove one of the main wires and… trigger the security system. One row of the spherical objects surrounding the big transmitter suddenly springs to life and charges at you with what seems to be sharp knives.
