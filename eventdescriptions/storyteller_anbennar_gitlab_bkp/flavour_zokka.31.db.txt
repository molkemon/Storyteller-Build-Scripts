The ground is slick with something that was once a teacher. Their lord doesn't speak. They don't beg. They just kneel there, blood soaking into his robes, placidly watching them. "I remember," she begins, "being told that I was a slave." She crouches in front of them, leaning in. "I find myself thinking of those words, now. I turn them over in my mouth. I chew on them." A flick of a hand. A line of figures are brought closer; humans, elves, gnolls, centaur, harimari, and more. All are chained, and all are wounded. "Knowledge can't save you now," she whispers. "No more clever lies or witticisms. All that's left is to learn when to shut up." Their lord meets her eyes. Their voice is hoarse. "Zokka won't approve." Xhuvari only laughs, low and guttural. "Zokka gave us teeth," their lord. "I'm simply using mine." She stands and turns away. Behind her, more slaves are herded like cattle, bound and bloodied and breathing; for now. "This isn't a purge," she says over her shoulder. "This is a correction." The doors slam shut. The slaughter begins.
