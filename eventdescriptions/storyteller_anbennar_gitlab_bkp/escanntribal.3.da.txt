Dwarven architecture is imposing, showing off industrial prowess and strength that we goblins have rarely achieved, and dwarven architecture with human proportions is even more intimidating. Through much effort, this city answers to a goblin boss, but it is still made for humans in many aspects: stairs are too big for our children, furniture is too high, and doorknobs are unreachable. An ingenious band of goblins has decided to correct the city’s errors.
