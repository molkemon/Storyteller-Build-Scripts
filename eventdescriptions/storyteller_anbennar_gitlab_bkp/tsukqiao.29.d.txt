I had arrived. This palace had seen better days, under the rule of my family's empire, it had fallen into disrepair, which for an icon of old meant we were merciful to it, rather than just raising it to the ground. A royal eunuch met me at the steps of the temple, inviting me in. Walking through the gate, you can feel the history the palace represents—the place where the great Harimar disappeared forever.

I found a strange-looking door that was almost calling to me. A few of the eunuchs around seemed unable to see it when I pointed it out. I walked through it to a hall that seemed infinite, one that hadn't been touched by humans in what felt like a thousand years. The walls told a story—of the tiger king Harimar, of his conquest through Rahen and beyond. The more I moved forward, the more I went back in the story. I saw him organize his clan and begin his conquest, and then... and then... no... This cannot be correct? Could this be? Could he be?
