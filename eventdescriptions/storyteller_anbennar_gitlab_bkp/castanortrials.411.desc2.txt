With confidence, the trial participant kneels down to inspect the direct marks left in the dirt by the basilisk’s passage. Aha. A scan of the surroundings reveals a cracked branch. The participant walks up to the branch, and looks around again, now spotting a crushed bush ahead. Another cursory glance from the location of the bush reveals another suspicious branch, and on and on this continues, until… they have no choice but to admit that they are utterly lost. The search has just brought them in a wide circle, back to the start of the Trial. The shadows are lengthening, and soon it will be far too dark to see anything in the forest. What to do?
