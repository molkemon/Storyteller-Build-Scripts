The matter of the Kiamoa is decided.  The visions of the magmascryers were terrifying. The advances of the outside world are far beyond our own, and we know that their ships will soon sail to our islands. We know nothing about what they might want; even if the others come in peace, we must assume the worst. The Twin Gods will protect us, but we must protect ourselves as well. The era after the fall of the Kiamoa will be one of introspection and cautious outreach.  The Volcanic Truth teaches that nothing is constant. Even the mighty Kiamoa was destined to fade one day, and that day has arrived.  Now that our faith has decided on the proper path forward, we can begin to move towards a new world, with all its mysteries and terrors.
