Oj Boss. We got a report that during some routine road construction in a remote area, the workers found a cave filled with some weird creatures. We didn’t get much detail before some engineers came demanding we exterminate the creatures so the cave could be used for our infrastructure. But as quickly as the engineers arrived, a local offered a counteroffer of turning that cave into a hunting spot and offering exotic food to be sold from the slain creatures. With which proposal should we go?
