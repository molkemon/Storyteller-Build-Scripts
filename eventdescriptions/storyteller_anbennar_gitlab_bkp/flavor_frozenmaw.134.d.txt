Khrosh and Droga were together, at the end. They stood back to back, fighting against impossible odds. The thrill of the fight erased the sour memories of the past months from Khrosh's mind, and he was thinking only of love when the Bayvicman's blade stuck fatally into his gut. He turned to see his wife similarly impaled. Their attackers left them there to fight off livelier raiders, and the illfated couple were granted a moment's peace. They died in each other's arms.
