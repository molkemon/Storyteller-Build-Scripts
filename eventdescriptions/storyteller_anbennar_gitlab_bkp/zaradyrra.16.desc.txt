Which Ashhana dares forget what saved us from the demonic hag's vile blood curse, breaking us from our cycle of darkness? It was the music of nature; the soul song brought about by natural music backing the desperate voice of a lone warbard. Currently we bring that venerable tradition to life: currently we show forth that even the stones will cry out the glory of the Zakytzkaras. And amidst their song and the Great Cycle, we will build the 333rd Empire in this, our beloved land.
