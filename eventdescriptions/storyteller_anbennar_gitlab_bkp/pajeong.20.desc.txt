To the west, the terror-bird riders stir. The hordes of the Shuvüüsh have often been a thorn in our realm's side, ever since their first descent into our lands immediately after the Day of Ashen Skies. But recently there have been whispers of a new leader, a Faceless Queen, uniting the disparate tribes. With the death of Jeong-Hun Gyo, the bowlords of Danggun, the Hwalbaek, are concerned about our unprotected western flank, and demand the construction of fortifications in Pajeong Target.
