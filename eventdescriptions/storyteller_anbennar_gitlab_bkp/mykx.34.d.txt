The Xhazobkult in Graxarr has been working on being able to infuse demon power into the cannons of our navy. They have finally made a breakthrough. They claim to be able to infuse the cannons of our fleet with Hellfire, but doing so requires favours from demonic powers, and thus should only be done during wars in which our naval supremacy is not guaranteed.  The modifier and whether it is active can be found on the province of Graxarr.
