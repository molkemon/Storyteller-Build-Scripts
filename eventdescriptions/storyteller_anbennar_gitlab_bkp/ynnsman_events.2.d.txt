Our nation was built with backbreaking labor and an unrelenting spirit, but it isn’t for everyone. Many ambitious people, rather than live a harsh life on the frontier, have entered the service of various feudal lords in the valley. They have established small communities here and there, and now many among our youth are considering joining them for better opportunities. They will send money back home, of course, but they are our best and brightest, and our country would suffer without them.
