The group we appointed to oversee the exploration of the blue hole has returned. From initial reports it appears that the exploration was costly both in equipment and lives. The blue holes are deep and dark and are often inhabited by dangerous sealife and potentially even hinuifauna. Yet now the expedition has returned after having braved the depth and explored it all. The entire court is now desperate to know whether the gamble did pay off or if our pride will crumble like Moa erodes Haku.
