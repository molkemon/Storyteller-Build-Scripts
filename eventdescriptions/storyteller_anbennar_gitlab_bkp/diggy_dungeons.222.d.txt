As the expedition emerges from the inside of the massive stone tree, they step into a humongous cave. This must have once been the forest floor. Stone trees stretch from the cave floor to the roof like gnarled pillars, holding up the rock above with twisting stone branches. It is unclear just how far into the distance the cave stretches, as vision is obscured by the mass of stone trees. But more surprising than anything, this long dead forest looks alive. Water trickles in small streams over the cave floor. Patches of mushrooms and Serpentbloom gather around the bases of the stone trees. A number of sylphs flutter onto a nearby patch of mushrooms, looking inquisitively at the expedition, before losing interest and flying away again.
