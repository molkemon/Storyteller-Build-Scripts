A proclamation of the Raja has reached our court: "We are deeply concerned by the constant warring that seems to be engulfing the Raj of late. Not valorous wars that would please Bearded Anjita in his divine chariot, either, but internecine conflicts, pitting friend against friend, brother against brother. All that, when monstrous foes lurk on our doorstep. As such, I, the Raja, request from all my loyal subjects to immediately cease all hostilities amongst themselves and join me in ushering in a period of introspection, that shall allow every subject of the Harimraj to focus on Dekhana and Pichka to discover their Nitikamarja, their own personal way to enlightened Purtara. The Raja, exalted caretaker of the world's atma."
