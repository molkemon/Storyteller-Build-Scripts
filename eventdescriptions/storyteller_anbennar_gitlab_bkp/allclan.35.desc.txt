The time has come to settle the ageold question of warfare: how does one best turn his or her enemy into a fine paste? Our noble Pentapandemoniac Prefectoriums have come forward with their respective cures for life.  The Clodhoppers recommend simple arachnidthemed violence, allowing their mounts to feed on the corpses of our enemies, all the while wearing goofy little cowboy hats. This is amicable.  The Harmaceuticals recommend ecofriendly and costeffective chemical weapons, the effects of which range from spontaneous combustion to spreading highly contageous diseases among the enemy infantry. This is horrifying.  The Discombobulatorium, unsurprisingly, have suggested artificerproduced explosives as the best course of action. This is fun.
