The Office of Secret Affairs has delivered urgent intelligence: a "Shadow Blade" assassin, hailing from a covert organization in Yanshen, has infiltrated our borders. Their movements are unknown, their target unclear. Reliable informants claim this agent has left a trail of bodies across multiple realms—yet not a single soul has ever seen their face. They are said to belong to the infamous lineage of the Unseen Killers.

They leave no name, seek no ransom—only the certainty of death. Even elite bodyguards have fallen unconscious in the split second before the strike. If this figure truly comes for one of ours, then guards and palace walls may prove meaningless. The only option may be misdirection—to shield the true target long enough for the blade to miss its mark.
