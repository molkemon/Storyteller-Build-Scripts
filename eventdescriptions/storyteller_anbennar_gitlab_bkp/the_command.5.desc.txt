The people here are ruled by Wuhyun now—or at least, the rulers are Wuhyun. Their culture and faith are those of the Command. But there are other people who live here, people who might be restored to their previous positions as leaders of the community. And so the Wuhyun question: what is to be done about the Wuhyun? We may continue the policies of the Command and collaborate to allow them to remain in power, or we may turn a blind eye to potential riots, infighting, and certain inevitabilities—and see the Wuhyun disintegrate back into history.
