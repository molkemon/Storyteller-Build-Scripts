Then our lord found Jordis Frog-Eater deep in the forest under snow-clad trees, and they thus addressed her. "Troll, is it true that you take tribute from my people and eat those who deny you?" And Jordis answered, "Yes, pale bird, for it is the law of giants. The strong rule over the small, and if you would defend these people, I pity them all the more." Our lord hefted her spear and said, "Your law is broken, as your giants are broken. You are a fool, troll, to take from my realm. For I am the queen of Vængheim, and I will have back that which you have stolen." Jordis laughed. "It is you who will be broken, pale bird. I will eat you, and wear your feathers in my hair." Then Jordis struck at our lord, and nearly slew them, but our lord had archers in the trees, and they now loosed on Jordis, who was pierced with hundreds of arrows. Thus died Jordis, and our lord took all that she had stolen and returned it to their people, who sang of their valour.
