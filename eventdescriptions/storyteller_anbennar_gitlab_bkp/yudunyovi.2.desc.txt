The focus of the Triunic settlers has become centered on the religion of the old inhabitants of the steppes. They share tales of the old world and the ancient ways gathered from the Ustiq’lik, grand monoliths of unknown origin whose surfaces are inscribed with the stories of the ancient Zabatlari. These tales are whispered to them by the voice in their dreams; the name Soiti has been rediscovered upon the Ustiq’lik and given to this speaker.
