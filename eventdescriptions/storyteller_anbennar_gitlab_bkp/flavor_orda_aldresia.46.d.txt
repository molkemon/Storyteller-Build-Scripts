It is a brutal clash. Emil has never fought anyone like her. Each radiant beam she summons against him feels like a warship crashing against his armor. She is faster than a humming bee and stronger than an ox. He can feel the eyes of his kin on him; he wonders how many truly were hoping he’d prevail?  But at the end, history will not defy him. He finds an opening as she lunges to disarm him. Point blank he casts his disintegrate spell, and she vanishes abruptly into dust.  No one cheers him, no one comes to tend to his wounds. By the strength of his own back he’d won, and he alone would have to rule.  He exhales as he clutched his bleeding side, trudging up to his secondincommand. There is no grand speech today, only the brutal reality of what tomorrow will bring. He gazes upon the remaining nobility, all of them standing ironfaced, giving Emil no chance to relish his victory.  "Kill them all, ruler and heir. Come morning, a new Empire dawns."
