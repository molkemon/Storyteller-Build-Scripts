Marriages in the realm are almost always of a political and strategic nature and are used to strengthen the bond between certain realms or banners, build alliances, or expand influence. The marital negotiations between two realms are always of great influence on the nation as they have the potential to upset or strengthen the balance of power in the entire Great Realm. Such negotiations have now been taking place between the War Room X and War Room Y realms, and while most of the agreements have been set already, there is one part of the agreement that has made the two parties bicker about for weeks. They have now come forward to the Grand Marshal with their arguments and propositions for the agreement, and it is up to the Grand Marshal to mediate and decide which proposal will be added to the marital contract.
