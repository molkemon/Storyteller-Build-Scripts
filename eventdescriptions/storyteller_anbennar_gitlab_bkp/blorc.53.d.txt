As the chieftain brought down their axe one last time, everyone thought it was over for the ruler, who had been on the defensive for most of the duel. However, they were spared. The axe a mere inch from their face, penetrating into the cavefloor. The orks began chanting the name of their victorious chief, as the ruler attempted to get up, heavily bleeding and shamed, and motioned to their men to carry them out.
