Again Akasik is filled with chatter and gossip, but this time the chatter comes not from the mountains, but from the ports. Young Shabat is finally returning to her homeland from a 4 year long journey, as a result of this Khasa is filled to the brim with harpies, a rare sight indeed. They eagerly wait to see their idol return home; when she makes landfall she is met by many faces, old friends, her sister, and brand new admirers excited to hear of her exploits overseas. She tells them of the marvelous things she’s seen in her travels, the wonder of Port Jaher, the gloom of Nathalaire, the Impressive hold of Oval Tungr, the suntouched city of Brasan, of the great library of Koroshesh… She spared no effort on telling them all of the details, and so they didn't leave the docks until dusk, the young one filling their heads with excitement at the Idea of seeing what the wider world had to offer. It is clear for all now, even for the more orthodox members of harpy society, or the most puristical of the Akasi. The unification of Akasik heralds a new era, and our sisters will go along with us there.
