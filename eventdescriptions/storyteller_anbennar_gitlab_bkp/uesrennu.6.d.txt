A whole city moving. That's what it felt like these last few days. Because months of siege weren't enough. Because the brutal sacking that followed wasn't enough. Because nights upon nights of abject terror, awake in fear of being taken away as "rebel" or "insurgent" by the new rulers’ minions wasn't enough. No, the Kompu has a new brilliant idea to torment us with: entire neighborhoods, and the best part of the inhabited undercity will, from now on, be dedicated to housing their beasts. The people living there? Us? Sent to other districts. Living where? Living how? Their ruler does not care. We're not rats, after all. And anyone that refuses or tries to put up even the smallest act of resistance gets rounded up, like many others in the last weeks, and sent to their mines. But no, we are not rats, and this tyrant will be made aware of this fact in due time. Not current; most people's spirits are too broken right now. Nor tomorrow; their patrols are still too many and too vigilant. But in the not so far future the Kompu will lower their guard, and in that moment they will learn. Nanru Nakar is a city of elves, and this city won't forget. Or forgive.
