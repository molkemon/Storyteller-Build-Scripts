While Khets can be mostly correlated to negative events, some of them do not land in the trope of being a menace to society. In this case, an rare case of an extroverted and outgoing Khet is preparing for a tour via the Sorrow, organising several festivities along the way. The people are on the top of their fingertips to receive the Khet, as many pairs are waiting for this exact occasion to gain a blessing for their marriage from a god himself.
