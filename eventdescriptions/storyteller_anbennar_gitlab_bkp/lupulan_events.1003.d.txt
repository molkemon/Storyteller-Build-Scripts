Upon the death of a Sri Baginda, the rulers of the realm convene to elect a new Sri Baginda. Usually, this process is a formality, as the heir of the Sri Baginda serves as the obvious choice. However, the recent actions of our domain have led to a degradation of our status in the eyes of the rest of the world, to the degree that the selection of our new ruler as Sri Baginda would be seen as a declaration of weakness and an invitation to outsiders to interfere in the Lupulan. For this reason, the others have chosen a new candidate as Sri Baginda.
