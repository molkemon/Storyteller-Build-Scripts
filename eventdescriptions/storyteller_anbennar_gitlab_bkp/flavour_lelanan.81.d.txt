I always knew it would happen one day when my wives aged faster than me. My divine oath extended my lifespan, and that was plain to see.

Yet as I stood over their old bodies, I wept like I never had before. My great lovers were gone, and now alone I had to do more.

Even now, they still held hands sweetly, their love deeper than even death. As I dropped to my knees at their side, a part of me wished for that one final breath.

But my work was not yet done; Lel's power still flowed strong through me. Our great matriarch asked for more, and if I didn't answer, what would I be?

So I finally left that room and spent my nights all alone. I honored their deep love and built more stone by stone.
