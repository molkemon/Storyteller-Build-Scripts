We seek all to make life together. Some are already able. It behooves us to ask them if they can contribute to our fable. An orc and human pairing answer all our questions. Let us speak to their young daughter and even offer their suggestions. A rare elf hobgoblin couple volunteers with their whole family. Yet, we find them to be mules— not quite right with their anatomy. But they’re proof that we can do this; that our mission is ordained. We must solve the muling problem and no longer be restrained.
