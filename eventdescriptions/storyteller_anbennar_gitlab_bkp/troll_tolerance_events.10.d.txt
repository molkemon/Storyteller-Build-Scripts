One thing we have learnt about the Swamp Trolls since they began to integrate into our society is the importance they place on their dreams. It seems they believe they walk the Shadow Plane each night and that these dreams have power. Of course, our clergy dismiss these claims as heathen nonsense. One of the Swamp Troll elders has requested an audience with the court, claiming her dream is of great importance. After being let in under guard, she shared this dream, explaining that one of our lord's close confidants will betray our lord in the coming days, leading to a death in the court.
