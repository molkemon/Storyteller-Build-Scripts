The truth is plain and apparent. The word spreads across every court in Anbennar, nay Cannor. The Magisterium willfully engaged in a conspiracy to hide the most significant news in, potentially, the history of Halann. Castellos, head of the Regent Court, king of the gods, is dead. Slain in an attempt to prevent the ruin of Aelantier, and the Day of Ashen Skies. The princes are in uproar. A secret committee was formed by the Emperor of Anbennar decades ago, known as the Committee of Aranthil, dedicated to investigating odd behavior from the Magisterium. Via rigorous study and investigation, it has found irrefutable evidence that the Magisterium engaged in a willful conspiracy to hide this information and forever bury it away from the public and the empire at large, whilst also conspiring to control the Chroniclers to rewrite history, to make the Magisterium’s role in history even greater than it already is.  Already a special session of the Imperial Diet has been called, with every prince in the empire gathering to discuss and debate what to do with what many call an act of blatant treason. A few opinions eventually come to dominate the diet, and then fall to a vote. It is left to the emperor to both cast their own vote, and to guide the Empire towards the right path.
