A troubling report sits on the table. A local and powerful aristocrat is quickly on his way to becoming the richest man in the known world. Alongside his wealth, he has begun to exert his influence on the politics of our realm. He rallies support from the common populace, arguing for a more powerful executive, even implying he himself may run for ruler of Kheinsí to make it happen! This could be the man that brings our realm to its knees with the terrible plague of tyranny. Should we investigate the doings of this man, but it may prompt him to act. Must we intervene for the sake of Kheinsí?
