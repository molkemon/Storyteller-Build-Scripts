Now that we have begun reconquering land in the Dragon Coast, we must decide how it will be divided among us and the gnomes. We will make this decision now to facilitate the land split as we conquer. Both sides present their arguments, as they claim to be the rightful owners of the land. However, the situation can be boiled down to this: the Reavers represent most of our soldiers and generals, and the gnomes our advisors and researchers. Which side should we favor?
