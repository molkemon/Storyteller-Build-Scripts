Sire, an emergency meeting has just been called at the Grand Hall. Only a select few have been invited. I've heard through the grapevine that Aviwe, the Oracle King of the Merfolk Empire, requested the rulers of the Baashidi to come together. The only thing confirmed is that the topic is exchanging Baashidi scholars for Merfolk scholars. It seems that Aviwe has decided to bring our worlds closer together after all. He is one of the most powerful living mages on Halann, boasting immortality and extreme potency in evocation and divination magics. It is said that thanks to him, the merfolk were able to significantly improve their living conditions and technology, leading to their victory over the Deep Devils in their own territory!

The plan he outlined is that select courts would provide him with a selection of advisors, and in exchange, all participating kingdoms would receive merfolk scholars. While humans cannot breathe underwater, the merfolk claim to have advanced magics that would allow any land dweller to venture under the sea without any issues.

Before you head out to Abeesooyinka to meet with him, I should warn you of two things: The exchange would be permanent. Members of your court would take their families to the lands of the Merfolk Kingdoms and live the rest of their lives under the sea. The merfolk would provide their own courts and would arrive on our lands as well permanently. We would have to provide and accommodate the merfolk cabinet. As their needs are quite different from our own, we would have to construct rooms for them to live in and supply them with sea water. Although merfolk can live outside of the water, from my understanding, it wouldn’t be the most comfortable experience. Some choose to, but I assume there’s a reason you don’t see many.

It seems that this exciting possibility is being kept secret until an outcome is decided. They also hope for a quick response. What are you thinking, my liege?
