Like every afternoon, our ruler was reading the last reports from their subordinates and from the cartel. Nothing really struck them as new. Their advisors often said their endeavour was pointless and that they should focus on other more pressing tasks. But they knew that making sure everybody did their job well was of utmost importance. Without the cartel's support, the country would surely bankrupt, and the subordinate clans could warn them of impending threats. This afternoon, however, an incident broke their routine. It started with a flash, followed by a familiar detonation. Fireworks maybe? But when their palace started to shake, our ruler knew something was amiss. Rushing to the window as more blasts resounded, they could only witness the apocalyptic scene unfolding before them. Flames engulfed the once proud and powerful capital and, before their very eyes, one of the buildings in the Blackpowder District exploded. A flash, a detonation, and yet another tremor shaking up to the foundations of their world. Years of hard work, disappearing in a matter of minutes. Oxa Silentfeet, their closest aid, stormed into our ruler's office. "We have to leave now, the palace is no longer safe." "Quick, take these ledgers, they must be protected at all costs. This is terrible." "I'll see what I can take but you must go now. Saving your life takes priority." Our ruler reluctantly put down the heavy books and started to run toward the palace backdoor. Who was responsible for this mess? Surely this was not caused by negligence or reckless behaviour. They had made sure to oversee the production of blackpowder by reading every single report coming from the Alchemist's Cartel. The fey. It must be the fey who did this. But how could their influence reach the very heart of our city?
