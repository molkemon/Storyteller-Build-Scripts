Obtaining elephants from Sharaajaghal has proven to be far easier than the SGC anticipated, as the elephant lords are quite eager for their animals to be used in the trade caravans. It allows them to display the capabilities of the elephants far beyond their border, through all the cities that the SGC conducts trade in. As such, they have offered their elephants at very low prices as the elephant lords all take great pride in their elephants being seen.
