Magic has begun to infuse every aspect of industry within our kingdom, including the very roads upon which the people walk. Veins of Dames tear shoot through the cobblestones, lining the streets with a soft cerulean glow as they snake across our capital like the fingers of a lightning bolt. Our mages wish to extend this road network across the land, stitching together our land with latent mana like a grand spiderweb of potential energy. This expansion would greatly assist the infrastructure projects planned within our kingdom, but would be exorbitantly expensive to create to the extent our mages desire.
