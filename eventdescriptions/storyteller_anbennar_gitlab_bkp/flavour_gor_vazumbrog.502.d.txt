At the end of a treacherous descent through the vaults, the expedition was met with a sight filled with both promise and horror: a freestanding stone archway, whose shimmering interior betrayed a secret purpose; a portal. Taking a deep breath, thankful for the relatively clean air rushing in from the far side of the gate, Balga took a moment to visualize the dangers – or treasures – that might await on the other side. An enchanted blade to strike down their enemies? A fearsome minotaur? Some great pile of gold and gems?  Then she stepped through, and saw… nothing. Just an empty stone room, without so much as a scale or scrap of cloth to indicate what its purpose may have been. The air didn't feel quite so refreshing on this side.
