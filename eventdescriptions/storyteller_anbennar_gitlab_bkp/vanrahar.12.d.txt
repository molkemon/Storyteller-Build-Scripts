Harimari are likely the youngest sentient race on Halann, and, as such, we haven’t had a chance to truly establish ourselves. Still, it is upsetting that we only have the Ascension Peninsula to truly call home. Even the elves, who (from what we know) had their homeland destroyed, possess realms of their own in Bulwar and the faroff land of "Cannor". Embarrassingly, the Royal Harimari have been content to merely dominate a city or two.  The Ascension Peninsula alone is not enough to secure a base of loyal harimari to draw on, so we’ve decided to use a new method. The state will introduce tax cuts for any harimari family with children: the more children, the higher the tax cut. This way, we hope to rapidly increase the harimari population with guaranteed jobs once worked by the local humans.By favoring harimari over humans at every level of the caste system, the humans shall become embittered by their degrading livelihoods.  We shall not capitulate to them. We predict there is still some time before their movement grows strong enough to challenge our state, and by then we should hopefully have enough fighting age harimari.
