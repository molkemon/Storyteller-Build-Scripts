Of late our lord has been styling themselves more as a chieftain of the wild plains. They rarely go anywhere not mounted on their spider steed, escorted by an honour guard of riders. Oaths of loyalty have also been demanded from our nation's generals and military staff as the country grows increasingly militaristic. The wildeyed mutterings of our ruler turn the court to war, and the kingdom prepares to march on all Halann. For glory, for death, for Kravi Oznad.
