The Bulwari Divenhal coastline was irrevocably changed by the Day of Ashen Skies. Prosperous city states and entire swathes of fertile lowlands sank beneath the waves during the cataclysm. Yet with the recent advances in artificeryassisted landscape reshaping, the opportunity to regain those lost lands has arisen. The macrolittoral reclamation mechanism is not a singular engine but an entire array of complex artificery devices that are capable of reclaiming tidal flats and turning them into solid ground, suitable for agriculture or the construction of new settlements. In a first step, a series of forcefield emitters temporarily push the shallow waters along a stretch of coast far out to the sea, exposing the inundated ground. These mudflats are then quickly dried and solidified to a degree that allows the movement of the heavy machinery required for the next step. This next step consists of the swift elevation of the ground along waterways and the new coastline, forming solid dykes capable of preventing a flooding of the newly reclaimed land. Afterwards the superfluous salt in the soil of the reclaimed land is removed and hardy beachgrass seeded, to prevent erosion down the line. And once the newly acquired soil has settled, every once in a while, artifacts of ancient Bulwar emerge… more often than not brimming with arcane power.
