This country could hardly be called an outpost anymore. The imposing walls of the kelaktar rise over the land, foreboding and able to withstand great sieges from any who would seek to subjugate or destroy us. The workers on the farms surrounding the kelaktar know it will easily house them should any army come for them. Inside, the markets are filled with meats, horns, and hides from far-off hunts, and the blacksmiths are hard at work creating tools and weapons for the kelaktar. This land is secure and fortified, but the surrounding wilderness is wild and untamed. What shall become of it? Will we re-establish the great centaur tributary system of old, which once brought prosperity to the realm of Maghargma? Or shall we send out our rough and hardened ogres to the frontier to bring settlement to these lands, and glory to this new kelaktar?
