The mountains burn. The righteous fury of Surael thunders throughout the realm. The bloodthirsty gnolls have been burnt from their cowardly hideouts by our virtuous Elatšari, the instruments of the divine Surael. However, this triumph rings hollow, as so many still languish under the Malevolent Dark in Bulwar, whether it be corrupt elven bureaucrats that have forsaken Surael, traitorous heathens in the mountains, or monstrous races ruling over and abusing our people. However, we are still recovering from the horrid oppression of the gnollish savages. Villages are empty, and fields are left untilled and in flames. We must consolidate if we wish to ever defeat the Malevolent Dark and their agents.
