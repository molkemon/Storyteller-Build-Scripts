Our dungeoneers, handpicked for their experienced and expertise, were not misjudged. It is a matter of minutes for our party to expertly pick their way through the trapped tunnel, slipping past the tripwires without triggering a single one. Once we emerge on the other side a number of goblins come forward clutching purses of coin or rusted weapons looted from the corpses of previous expeditionaries, evidently ones less adept than our own.
