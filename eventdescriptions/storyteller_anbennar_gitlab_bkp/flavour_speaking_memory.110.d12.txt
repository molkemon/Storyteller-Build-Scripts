When Satria was a mere pirate sailing the seas of Kanolowele, he had an ambition: to found himself a grand city to retire in. But as Satria raided across more and more of the sea, his ambitions began to grow. What if instead, Satria thought, he founded himself an empire? Soon, on one of his voyages, he came across the dragon turtle Vea. Vea knew much of the empires of the past, and she thought that this dream of Satria’s was a grand one. So she took to following the pirate fleet of Satria across the sea, and when his prey saw a dragon turtle behind him they gave him whatever they pleased.  After a long time at sea, Satria came across the city of Donyatabraken, which was founded near the remains of a dragon turtle who perished in the Century of Storms. Donyatabraken was a rich and mighty place, and the people loved their city dearly. So did Satria, but perhaps he loved the city in a different way. Satria amassed the mercenaries of all of Kanolowele to raid that city, and the blood of its waters ran red that day. In the end, all of the guards of Donyatabraken were dead, but so were the mercenaries, and Satria did not have to pay their corpses.  So Satria went to the center square of the city, and with his crew and the dragon turtle Vea behind him, he declared that there would be a new guard of the city. He declared the empire of Satrialaam, and that one day all of Kanolowele would unite under its flag. Satria the Great is dead now, but the story of Satrialaam has persisted for hundreds of years.
