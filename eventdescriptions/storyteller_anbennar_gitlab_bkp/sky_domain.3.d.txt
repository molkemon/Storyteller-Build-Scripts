Our lord watches as the priest makes the ceremonial first strike on the ground; immediately after, teams of workers and oxen begin moving the great amount of earth and stone needed to create an edifice to Amilak's glory. The main chambers of worship, like many temples, will be open to the skies, so Amilak may receive prayer and sacrifice unobstructed. Radiating outwards from the heart of the temple will be all sorts of lesser buildings and complexes, including housing for the priests and scribes, houses of healing, and soup kitchens to aid the needy. While humble, it will become a great place of prayer and reverence in the years to come.
