The cultists lie defeated and scattered, their leaders as dead as the gods they claim to worship. State soldiers have occupied their strongholds, and the process of returning any stolen goods and repatriating those who have not yet been fully overtaken by madness has already begun. However, a search of their headquarters has resulted in a somewhat disquieting discovery. A hidden room has been discovered to contain a large amount of funds seemingly collected from the cult's members, along with a letter: "For donation to the Godswatch, so that the gods may be seen by all." The realization that these maddened cultists were also in support of the Godswatch Observatory carries an unsettling implication, to say the least. Though we've tried our best to suppress this information, rumors have already begun to spread, and if we were to pass the funds along to the observatory it would be all but a confirmation of them. However, it must be said that a donation this significant would go a long way towards speeding up construction.
