The metallic clash of weapons reverberates through the training court of the Exemplar fortress of Linkum. Dozens of new acolytes eager to prove their worthiness spar with each other under the sharp eyes of their seasoned instructors. Young Sun Elves from the Irrliami heartlands, the verdant dales of Imuluses, the fertile Naza plains of Sareyand, windswept Drolas and even distant AzkaSur or ReUyel have gathered here to offer up their talents and if need be, their very life, in service of a higher calling.  In the scriptoria and libraries of the grand temples across Bulwar, a new crop of Sun Elven priests devote their entire existence to strengthen the faith and consolidate the disparate theologies of the manifold sects of the Sun Cult that have sprung up over the millennia, while others train day and night to become exorcistpriests, eager to protect the faithful from the machinations of the Malevolent Dark.  Others amongst this new, golden generation of Sun Elves who have never experienced the chaos and despair borne from the collapse of the Phoenix Empire and the interminable internecine squabbles of its successor realms have found other avenues to develop their innate talents and serve in their divinely ordained role as Chosen of Surael.  Be it as scholars who minutely dissect the many failings of their forebears during the Age of Cinders in the hope that such egregious mistakes will never be repeated. Be it as disciples of one of the many magi clans where they hone their innate affinity to magic to the utmost degree. Be it as administrators or courtiers diligently performing their duties to ensure that order and prosperity is maintained within the confines of the sacred Garden of Surael.
