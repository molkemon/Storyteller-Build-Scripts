The new dwarven migrants from Cannor may be enthusiastic about reclaiming our shared birthright, but they still cling to surface concepts, preferring to work 'from sunrise to sundown', even though no sun can be seen this far in the depths. This has begun to cause problems, as priority tasks are sometimes not dealt with for hours, as many of the new immigrants simultaneously leave their posts. It has gotten to the point that some of the more paranoid postmasters have started to believe them to conspire together to destroy our kingdom. While such accusations are most likely false, something has to be done, else the authority of the monarch might be undermined. However, trying to force them to abandon their habits could seriously undermine their productivity.
