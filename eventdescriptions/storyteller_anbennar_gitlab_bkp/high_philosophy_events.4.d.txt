After a long day of travel, the ruler’s caravan at last reached the High Temple. Once the ruler would have gone years between visits, but ever since the scholar from the Ascendant Soul school arrived in their court this had become a regular journey. The ruler disembarked and entered the temple complex. Its central tower rose high into the sky, and it was surrounded by sturdy walls which sheltered various smaller structures. The scholar interrupted the ruler’s marveling and bid them welcome. They brought the ruler to the entrance of what appeared to be a small stone cellar. Inside, the ruler was met with a large pit constructed like an old stepwell. The scholar lit a lantern and began to descend the stairs while beckoning the ruler to follow. As they proceeded, the scholar spoke. "We have already spoken at length about the necessity of improving all aspects of yourself: the physical, mental, magical, and of course spiritual. but I fear you have not fully taken to heart the need to master power and have the wisdom to use it well. True you may command a country, but you do not yet control yourself. To truly achieve Purtara you must have the power to shape the world and the wisdom of the High Gods." Finally the scholar reached the bottom, and moved the lantern to illuminate the southern wall. A carving took up the entirety of the wall, and depicted a scene of massive construction. Mountains were leveled, and the temple itself built from the remains of the former. All of this done by the High God Pathim with seemingly little effort, and massive fanfare of those below her. The scholar spoke once more. "Let us now sit in this old holy place, and meditate on this wisdom I speak of."
