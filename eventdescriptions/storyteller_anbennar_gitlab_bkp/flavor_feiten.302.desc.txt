Atop the Grand Skyport, the Silken Council convenes their yearly meeting. Reflective of the current questions plaguing our nation, this year’s discussions center around the role of foreigners in the League of Feiten. One group, the reformists, look toward the Gommo and Cannorians and their institutions as something to emulate. Meanwhile, the Old Guard believes that we must not stray from the path that has brought us so much wealth and our balloons. It is up to our lord to choose which group gains ascendency for now, but this will clearly be a point of contention for decades to come in this new Age of Silk.
