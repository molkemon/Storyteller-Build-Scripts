We have finally finished improving our advanced military infrastructure in our lands. The layout of the hold has been revamped dramatically, and what was once a maze of buildings and alleyways separated into military roads has now become a perfectly ordered hold where each district is divided by larger roads at right angles. Each house is now a fortress with various defenses integrated throughout the hold, in addition to traps and killing zones integrated into the design. A large rail leads from the hold entrance to the central barracks, allowing armies to be maneuvered in and out of the hold with haste.
