With the relations between our realm and the Triunics strengthening, our people have begun to reclaim their ancient heritage. The Triunic language and holidays have become more popular than those of Yansheni. Our people have long seen themselves as Triunics, and now so does the world. Though it was just a year ago that we created our new constitution, the people cry for one simple amendment—to abandon the name of Beikdugang and embrace a new name more fitting of our Triunic nature, Kuldozein! This all can agree to, but we can take it even further. We can embrace the faith of the Triunics, the worship of Kalyin, to show the unity between our people and between all things.
