It might have something to do with their upbringing in their homeland, or perhaps it is simply their personality. Regardless, the consort is one of the most devout subjects in all of our realm. Their unwavering belief through times of hardship brings hope to our people during even the darkest of days. After each public appearance they make, we find that the people of our great kingdom tend to display a renewed energy, on their faces as well as in their daily tasks.
