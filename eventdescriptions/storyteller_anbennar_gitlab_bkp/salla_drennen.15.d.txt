What I thought of our lord? Does the word scared suffice? I may seem like a force of nature, but he truly is one. Predator and prey, the stories say, both hunter and hunted. Those stories may reassure those hunters who try their hand at slaying him in his many visits to our realm, but certainly not me! I may have the powers of nature's forces, but he actually is one. Lucky that you and Varinna have no such qualms about facing down danger. I can't believe we actually did it—slayed his animal form, I mean. It was mostly the two of you, I confess. But still, we three all earned his blessing, so I'll take just a bit of the credit.
