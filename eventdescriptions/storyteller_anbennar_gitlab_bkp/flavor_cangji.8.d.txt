Plumbing the depths of the Nomsyulhan rift is not an expedition for the faint of heart. Dangers within this otherworldly portal are often deadly and beyond full comprehension by mortal minds. Still, the Ashen Soul explores the other side with trepidation, in their efforts to locate the beast which slumbers there. Today, the expeditionaries find themselves in an underground cave system, stretching through the darkness for an unknowable distance. The brave mages walk through the caverns, searching for signs of life, when they suddenly hear a sound similar to a crystal shattering into tiny fragments. Slowly, they look around, and in the blackness they spot two glowing eyes, shining a curious blend of gold and silver. Suddenly, the twin metallic lights surge forward, and from the black emerges a terrifying creature of scales and claws, seeming to be some sort of gigantic reptilian tiger. The mages scramble, flinging spiritfire across the cavern while monks wreathe their swords in pink-blue flames. The creature races forward, and within moments is upon them.

On the other side of the portal, mages of Harimari, hobgoblin, and human descent work to hold the rift open, while ogres commune with spirits surrounding the complex who act as scouts within the rift. These managers of the other side soon begin to feel large spikes in magical energy emanating from the portal, and just as they evacuate the immediate area, titanic jets of blue flame shoot into the sky. Soon after, one lone Harimari mage emerges from the rift, covered in red and blue blood. She raises her hand, and slowly from within the void a gargantuan corpse emerges into the desert. She places it into the sand and collapses to the ground.
