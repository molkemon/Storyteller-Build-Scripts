Whilst inspecting one of our armies, our lord realised that they train more diligently when observed by them up close rather than from a distance. Our lord believes that this stems from the lord's obviously planetouched appearance; whether this is due to the soldiers deriving inspiration from the flames or by a fear of burning cannot be said. Whichever is the truth, our lord could choose to use a display of their power to help our armies become better than our rivals. While doing this could be used to inspire or discipline our troops it would also be a constant demand for our lord's time, as they would have to make frequent trips to our army.
