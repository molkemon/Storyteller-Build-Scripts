Halflings are known to excel at enjoying themselves, particularly when it comes to good food. Because of this, the halfling quarter has become a popular location for any denizens of the hold looking to dine outside of their own home.  Today, however, everyone in the hold, rich or poor, can get a taste of good halfling cooking, as several of the largest families of the quarter are cooperating to organize a grand feast, with no invitations required. As the families engage in friendly competition with one another, each part of the banquet is more rich and elaborate than the last, resulting in a feast that won't soon be forgotten.
