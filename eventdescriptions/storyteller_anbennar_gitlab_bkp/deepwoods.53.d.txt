Three dozen elves are gathered before the lord. Despite their best efforts, they fidget and squirm, unable to contain their nervousness and excitement. It is a sentiment that not even the lord can shake off. The truth is, for all its horrors and tribulations, the Deepwoods is home. The same cannot be said for the lands beyond these familiar dark canopies. And now these brave elves are venturing to those lands at the orders of the lord. There is a good chance they would never return. But, that is a risk they were willing to take. All they could do was hope their destination was worth it.
