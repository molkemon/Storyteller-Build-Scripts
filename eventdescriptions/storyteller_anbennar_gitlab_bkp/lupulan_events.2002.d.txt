While on a trip to the province to oversee construction and development in the area, the ruler was accosted by a small group of Nang-Ta-Khian, one of the many kinds of jungle spirits of the Lupulan. It seems that all the new construction going on has infringed on their jungle home. They would greatly appreciate it if a more slow and steady approach could be taken, and have promised to cooperate with the crown to the fullest extent possible if their request is honored. Our advisors are split on whether or not to agree. Although the favor of the Nang-Ta-Khian would be helpful and it is important to live in harmony with the spirits, we suspect some radical changes to the province’s development would have to take place in order to satisfy them.
