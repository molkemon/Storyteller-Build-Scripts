It was unusual for the Dauvek Veker to involve artillery in their hunts, but in the case of the Roc, they'd had to make an exception. The beast was fast and strong, and if they tried to engage it with melee weapons, it would be free to flee their heaviest blows and inflict a deadly toll on the hunters.  "Whoever brings it down gets to keep its flight pinions as a trophy."  The commander's words provided some motivation to the gunners, but not as much as the mass of feathers and talons that began to barrel towards them when their opening volley went wide of its nest. Ultimately, the bird was mere feet from the gunners' position when a lucky shot detonated its left wing. The axemen came forward and made quick work of the fallen superraptor.  "A shame the left pinion was destroyed in the blast," lamented the triumphant gunner. "Still, though…"
