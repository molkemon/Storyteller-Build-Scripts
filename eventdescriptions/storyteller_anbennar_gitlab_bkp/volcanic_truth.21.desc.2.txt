The matter of the Taga'Ala is decided.  We may worship fire, but we must not burn down all that others hold dear. If the Taga'Ala worship the ocean, let them do so: we will bring the Volcanic Truth to their lands as diplomats and traders. Their trees are submerged in water, but growth is a kind of change, as well. We will teach them that their mangroves and their love of life are already elements of the Volcanic Truth, and in due time we will call each other friends  As the Volcanic Truth spreads to new lands, the Taga'Ala will take a glowing ember of Truth from it and make it their own. This is what change means.
