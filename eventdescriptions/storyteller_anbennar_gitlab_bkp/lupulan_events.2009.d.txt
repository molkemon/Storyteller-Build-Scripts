The children run through the glowing streets in masks. Young lovers twine in quiet nooks and the old hold hands and look on fondly, remembering their courtship days. The buskers weave tales of glories past as the vendors hawk their food and sell it for a song, for tonight there is enough for everyone. Tonight is the festival to celebrate the spirits, and as always, the king has been invited to speak to his people. Already the fireworks boom overhead: the king does not have long to decide what he will say. What should the king speak on?
