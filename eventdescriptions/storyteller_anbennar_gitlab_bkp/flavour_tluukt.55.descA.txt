The ruler lies broken and bleeding at the leader’s feet. Before they can muster a second wind, the leader is on top of them, pinning them to the earth. Magical prowess brought us this far, but victory can be achieved in only one way; they don’t even have a chance to scream before the leader sinks their teeth into their neck, ripping away their life with a single bite. The grolls of a distant pack watch as the leader finishes their bloody work. By no law or tradition are they bound to join our pack, but only a fool would cast their lot in with the successors of the loser, proven weak and impotent by our victory. By nightfall, all the land will belong to us as all fealty is supplanted with loyalty to our glorious leader.
