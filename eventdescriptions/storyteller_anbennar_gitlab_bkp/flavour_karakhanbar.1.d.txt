The lord walks through one of the groves with a soldier by their side. The Emerald orks, led by the despicable prince, had shown no mercy in their attack. The groves are now littered with the bodies of gobkins, elves, and orks alike. As they approach a small clearing, the sound of fire intensifies. Accustomed to the roaring flames, they are almost taken aback by how calm and sombre the fire sounds. Stepping into the clearing, they are struck by the acrid smell of charred flesh and the sight of towering funeral pyres, surrounded by weeping mourners. Standing next to one another are groups of elves and gobkins, with some orks in their midst. They all stare blankly into the pyres as the fire licks the creaking wood, consuming whatever; or whoever; had been placed on top of them.
