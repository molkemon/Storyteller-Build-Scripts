It's no secret that tensions have been rising between the increasinglyzealous Corintar administration and many of its more moderate Corinite clergymen. As these clerics find themselves ousted from government and ostracized from their homes, they have increasingly fled to our many disparate Corintar chapters abroad. Attempts to rein in these chapters have failed, and it is clear that their loyalty is no longer to the Corintar.
