The mind is a weapon. Just like a sword or a spear, it must be sharpened. Without an incorruptible and well-educated brain, administrators cannot hope to tame the raging river of wheat and coin necessary to supply the Command's massive war machine, nor can generals hope to understand the hearts of their men and their enemies, or how to twist such hearts to their advantage. Such developments in administration, philosophy, and education require a dedicated body to research and apply them, and so the Tiger Command was born in Rayavhatimana, a Raheni seat of learning, to pour the water of knowledge from the font of thought onto the soil of the Command.
