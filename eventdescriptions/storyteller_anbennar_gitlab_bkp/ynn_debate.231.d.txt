Even the mightiest oak can fall when infested by a legion of ants. The proudest bear may succumb to a pack of hungry wolves. And, as the current tournament in the capital showed, even a squadron of troops led by the lord themselves might be outdone when attacked by a dozen foes at once. The Iosahars' tactics were fairly simple, but they worked: an attack from all directions, simultaneously, enough to overwhelm us with their greater numbers. But it is not the loss on the field that smarts the most; it is the way the story of the tournament has already spread across the land, with many in our lands seeing it as strong evidence of the decentralized Iosahar system's importance and value.
