The recent growth of the capital has drawn people from across the nation to find their futures here. Among them have been many unsavory sorts, sadly, besmirching the name of our nation and the honor of our lord. As long as they kept their predation to unimportant sorts, however, they were not worth the attention it would take to bring them to heel. But that has changed tonight. Apparently, a cloaked figure wandering the alleyways well past midnight appears to many as an unimportant sort, and a trio of thugs – adorned in peculiar red masks – have just sauntered up to our lord as the hunt proceeded apace. The tallest of the lot gestures with his frozen steak at the lord, demanding all their gold, while the largest seems ready to grapple. What shall our lord do?
