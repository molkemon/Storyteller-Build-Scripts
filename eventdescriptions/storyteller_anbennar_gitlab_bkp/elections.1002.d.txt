For 50 years our ruler has governed our republic and their rule has been long and stable. However, as time progresses, long reigns tend towards stagnation and tyranny. Aware of this issue, our Constitution gives a single person the right to rule for a maximum of fifty years. As this term limit has been reached, the highest ranking officials of the state have assembled to prepare for a peaceful transition of power. Our ruler, aware of their term's imminent end, has already begun preparations to retire from office and end their long and successful tenure. After such a long period it is finally time for a change of the head of state.
