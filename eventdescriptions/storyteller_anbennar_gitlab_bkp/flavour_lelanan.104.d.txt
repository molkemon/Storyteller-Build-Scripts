Halann was not so kind to us, and often love faced opposition. Such as when a noble from another realm fell in love with someone of much lower standing. She met that hobgoblin captain at night, hiding their taboo love against a thousand rules ordained by those above. But the flame in their hearts could not die and grew until it engulfed their souls. So they fled the oppressive systems, fighting their way out of their mutual troubles. They made it all the way to Lelanàn, arriving one quiet morning in the monsoon rain. And as they held each other's hands openly, they knew they were free from that pain.
