There are some who do not fit well, kitame; the antisocial, those given to cruelty, those with chilly, hostile minds. We knew this. Surprising, perhaps, that gentle Cheni should express this first. He conveys his thoughts through the lattice, from halfway across the palace. In times of emergency, they have used the lattice to communicate in just such a way. But in recent days, as they have planned a lattice for the entire world, they have begun to mindspeak as a matter of course. The Vile One’s response comes after a long pause. They will be woven into the lattice eventually. Persuasion takes time.

Yes, they will. But, as you say, persuasion takes time. How strange and perfect it is, that Cheni conveys his faint smile, his enjoyment for notes of symmetry, even while mindspeaking. How wonderful their lattice is. The Vile One’s chest tightens. It is a terrible equation, whether to abandon such minds, simply cut them off. But it must be faced, kitame. Are we strong enough to withstand the thrashing of a childish world, of all these people unwilling to take their medicine, until such stubborn places in the lattice can be softened?
