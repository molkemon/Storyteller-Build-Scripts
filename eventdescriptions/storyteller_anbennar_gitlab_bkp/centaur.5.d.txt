We charge onwards through the storm. We have been battered and broken, but we have never given up. If centaurkind is to be known for anything, it is for perseverance. The perseverance that led Irdaeos to smile upon us, that allowed us to prosper in these lands, and gave us our very way of life. The wind blows. Clouds shift overhead. "What truly lies ahead once we have emerged from the storm?"
