Our Monarch awoke today and found their consort dead. Their stonecold lifeless body unmoving, still in the same position as our monarch remembers them being when they drifted to sleep. The life of a shadow planetouched is rarely a happy one, but their dear consort was one thing which did make their life worth living. They want to die. They know they can't though. They know that their love would want them to live, to laugh, to feel passion again. They can't. They might have other wives but our monarch knows that they can't. They know that they can do one thing to remember them. They can grow the nation into a stable powerhouse. Into a state that will last forever, one that won't be relegated to the annals of history.
