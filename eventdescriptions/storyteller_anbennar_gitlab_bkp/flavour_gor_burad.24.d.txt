With the Hold of Gor Burad always aiming to expand its mine shafts deeper to the ground, we have started to receive more concerning reports than usual. Our miners have always been a bit hardheaded, but that is the nature of dwarves. Yet the worsening of these qualities within our miners was always chalked up to their long hours of work done in either steaming heats or in near complete darkness. But these new reports talk of the nicest of miners bickering with each other and the further they dug the worse it got. Eventually, the foremen of this group found the miners with their pickaxes up each other's noses, face red with sweat and… rage?  No official complaints have been filed, as the infighting is still minor enough to be considered a symptom of weakheaded miners, but this is still happening in different mineshafts at the same time with no apparent connection to each other.
