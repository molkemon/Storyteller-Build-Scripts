With the worst fighting out of the way, the land redistribution can finally begin in earnest. Instead of being owned by a single individual, all land will be owned solely by the realm. If we decide someone is not using their land properly, we have full authority to drive them off of it. Hopefully it will not be needed. Each parcel of farming land will be distributed to an individual family, who will then be free to tend to it however they see fit. Whatever works best for them (and the land) is best for us; as long as the appropriate rice tax is paid, then there is no problem. This system is currently undergoing growing pains. The new landholding class is learning how to best work their land, now that they have been freed from crushing restrictions on what they can grow, and now the most we ought to do is let them learn the way of the land. While we might have lessened yield now, our lord is patient; to have more later is worth having less now.
