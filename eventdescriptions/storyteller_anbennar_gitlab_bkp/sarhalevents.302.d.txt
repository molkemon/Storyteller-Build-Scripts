The spire that stands in the center of the old halfling beehive citadel has always towered over the realm, and we have always known it extended below the ground. But what nobody could have realized is that the visible section above ground comprises only a tiny section of the spire. Its many floors seemingly plumb a depth at least five times that of the above-ground structure, and possibly even more. Floor after floor of treasures and artefacts from long-dead Vyzemby and Lizardfolk civilizations now lie ripe for the picking. Clearing the first few floors has already proven a time-consuming operation, due to the numerous traps and ancient magical barriers placed. But the realization that we are only a fraction of the way through excavating this ancient structure has prompted a gold rush to see what other treasures its ruins might hold.
