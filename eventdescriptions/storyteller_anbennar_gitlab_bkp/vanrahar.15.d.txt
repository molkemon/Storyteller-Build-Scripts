An enormous guerilla resistance has been growing across the Harimraj. Bearing the symbol of the bloody lotus, they declare their intent to destroy the Lotus Throne and cast the harimari out of rulership. They strike from the shadows and fade into local communities, making it impossible to fight them conventionally, until now.  By a stroke of luck, we captured a very popular rebel leader beloved by all. Kind, gallant, brave, and suave, we killed himpublicly and very painfully. A crowdturnedmob gathered to try and free him, but the soldiers stationed at the execution massacred the rabble.  Word spread like wildfire, and now people are revolting in the street, which is exactly what we wanted. Now that the rebels are out in the open, the rebel leaders will have to come out as well, lest they leave their supporters leaderless and disorganized.Still, there are many more rebels than we expected. This is going to be a long war.
