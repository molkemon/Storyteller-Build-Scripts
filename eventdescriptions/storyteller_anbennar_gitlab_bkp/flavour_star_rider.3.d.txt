The Star Riders have gone mad, spouting vague falsehoods in their attempts to appear devout! Laymen, interpreting a faith in ways that should never be. And yet…

They cannot keep this up for long. They have nothing. They produce nothing. Such a society cannot survive for long. It is impossible. We just need to hold out. A few more years. Just a few years more.

All voices of the council have decided as such: To pay tribute to the Star Riders is your divine duty. For it is us who will bring peace to the realm. For it is us who will find a consensus in which every voice is heard. For it is us who will unite this faith, and the magic that binds us.

The chains are set, the strings applied. Twisting the flesh and cutting into it. This is our penance. That which we broke, will be made whole again.
