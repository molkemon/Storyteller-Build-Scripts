The issue of the collapse of the Ynnic Empire is a divisive one. If treacherous subjects are really to blame for destroying Ynnic society, then how come many of them were able to sire noble houses blessed by the Ynn? But then if the Emperors are to blame, what explains their centuries of harmonious rule? While the currents of faith might carry even the heaviest barge, the opposition might sink our hopes of resolving this issue if we maintain our chosen course. Is this the time to be pragmatic and lay our faith aside?
