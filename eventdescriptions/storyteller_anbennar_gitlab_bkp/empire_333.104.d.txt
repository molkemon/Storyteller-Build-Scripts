Treatises combating the prophecy of the 333rd Empire have been posted in cities across the realm, from Zerat to Shalazar. One that has gained wide circulation claims the 172nd and 173rd Empires are simply different accounts of the same empire written by various historians. One saw them as embodiments of evil, while the other saw them as embodiments of strength, and their writings were so different that they were classified as two separate empires. In any normal time, it wouldn't be controversial to merge these two empires into one and use this example as a lesson on the inherent bias of each historian. Except that if you removed the 173rd Empire from the count, the current empire would no longer be the 333rd. Since that can't be, the naga are forced to contort their snake-like bodies into knots explaining that the empire was split into two at some point, and the 173rd Empire moved its capital to modern-day Shalazar, making it a new empire, so that it still counts.
