The people find themselves grappling with a distressing series of remarkably dreadful dreams, robbing them of any joy in their slumber. Upon awakening, they are greeted by the disconcerting sight of a shadowy, elephantine figure cast upon the walls of their homes. This ominous phenomenon is a telltale sign that a Baku has set its sights on preying upon the dreams of the populace, and their suffering will persist until this malevolent entity chooses to depart.
