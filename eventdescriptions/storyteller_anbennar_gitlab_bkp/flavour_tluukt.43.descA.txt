The walls of our noble citadel have endured countless abuses over our long history, but the hardships of recent years threaten to break them wide open. First it was gluttonous Zokka, then zealous Jaddar, and even now the gnolls of Tluukt loom on the horizon. War seems inevitable, but many among us feel that defeat is at hand, and fear the reign of Tluukt. It was with some surprise, then, that instead of a demand for surrender we received an offer of cooperation from the gnollish leader of Tluukt; an offer of protection, of past glory, of relative freedom, in return for only a modest sum of tribute.
