Danggun has a thriving textile industry, and the burghers desire priority access to its products. While in the past, they were content with their role in selling the textiles to other nations throughout Haless, they now come with new demands. To ensure prosperity and profitability, they desire solitary control over all textile manufacturing and trade. However, the amount they have suggested as compensation to the Crane Throne for this privilege is embarrassingly low, much lower than even the value of the land that the textile mills sit on, let alone their product. This is a clear attempt to undermine royal authority, but without their assistance, the trade of textiles will surely slow to a crawl, depriving the crown of much-needed income.
