The Gommo is the largest gnomish guild and patron of inventors and scientists across Halann. Within Bivuzena are many scientists, both gnomish and not, who are researchers, artificers, and of course, architects. By opening a Gommo branch office within Bivuzena, we will not only gain access to scientific marvels from all over the world, but become a privileged part of their vaunted network of scientific correspondence. In one fell swoop, Bivuzena might easily become the most advanced city in Horashesh, should the Gommo accept our invitation.
