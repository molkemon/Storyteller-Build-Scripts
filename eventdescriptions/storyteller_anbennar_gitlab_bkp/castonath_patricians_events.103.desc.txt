Castonath has ever been the brightest star amongst a galaxy of lesser cities in Escann. The city has a knack for drawing all the resources and attention of whomever owns it into its gravity, something that the nobles from a neighbouring province are now bringing up in a clamour of complaints. They demand that we make a greater attempt at investing into their lands. For their part, the patricians argue that nothing good can come of investing into backwater villages and that those resources would be better spent in Castonath.
