The lord looked out across their new realm, thinking about how far they have come, and who they owed that achievement to. Their friends and family were a great help, of course, but there was one above them all who had protected them since their adolescence. The lord walked over and knelt down in front of their personal shrine, holding their hand crafted Gris Gris charm first close against their chest, then up to the shrine itself. The charm glowed warmly in their hands, as they whispered a prayer of thanks to their divine patron.
