Mercenaries are the lowest of all soldiers. To fight for gold instead of glory reveals a soul corrupted by greed. While there is nothing wrong with killing, they fight for shameful reasons and deserve to suffer the just consequences. Our lord rode into the settlement at Cannwood and watched as Cobalt Company mercenaries were killed or captured. Their measly treasure coffers were sacked and their barracks were burned. Many civilians fled into the Greatwoods, preferring to die by the claws of a werewolf than by the poison blade of an ork. Gunther of Mossford was brought before our lord and looked at them with hatred. "We refused to bow before one tyrant and we will refuse to bow to another." He spat in our lord's face and smiled defiantly. Our lord punched Gunther straight in the face, knocking him down to the ground. "No one insults their superior unpunished, human. Just for that, you will never walk across this earth again." They picked up his head with both hands and slammed it against a rock. Even as Gunther's last breath rattled in his throat, they refused to let up, slamming his head against the rock over and over, until all that was left was a bloody pulp. Once their rage finally subsided, blood was soaking the ground in a meter wide radius and Gunther's face was mangled beyond recognition. Our lord tore off Gunther's head and spat in the ruin left of his face. As Gunther's corpse was tossed into the Alen, our lord looked at the assembled captives and smiled. Despite the disrespect, this was a glorious day for the strong.
