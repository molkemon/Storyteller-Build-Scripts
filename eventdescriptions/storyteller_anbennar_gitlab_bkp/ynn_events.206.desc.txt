People have been whispering, and they have been whispering constantly, for their words have finally reached the ruler, with unamusing effects to say the least. How could anyone conceive, believe, repeat such calumny? According to our agents, we should ask the people of that region to answer that, as they have been the main actors in spreading these lies. The town has a history as the gossip capital of the land, and not even the Divine River is powerful enough to stop baseless rumours from crossing north to south, east to west, though one may at least hope that when reckoning comes, all who took part in such falsities without caution nor remorse will be swallowed by its cleansing tides.
