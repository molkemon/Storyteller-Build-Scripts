The rift opens… and I can see. Two women stand silhouetted by the light of their own craft. Do they know how rare they are, these sisters? Even at a moment's glance, I can see my Granny's gift of doubt will not reach them. The women have been rendered dull and insensate by their faith in Surael, and hand over all such quibbles to him. I see with the begrudging eyes of Hireet that the elder sister was better treated by their parents, yet never have these women allowed such injustice to breed resentment or chilly disregard. Mama can find no way in either, I see that too. Even Komina, freer of burdens, can convince them not a whit, for the pair exult in their duties, take pleasure in them. Their responsibilities give them meaning. What utter bores! There is nothing interesting about them at all; they would make terrible dinner guests. There is no purchase for the Coven here, and I watch Mama, Auntie, Granny reluctantly slip back into the dark.
