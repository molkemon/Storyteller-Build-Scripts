While it is not a publicly known fact, it is suspected by many that the extensive rituals and sacrifices made by the clergy of the Demon Cult are, in fact, a farce. Today, however, we have evidence to the contrary; the shamans in one of our provinces have managed to summon a demon during a standard Invocation of Woodfire. While traditionally the rite summons a lesser demon, which often looks suspiciously like a goblin painted red, these shamans managed to summon an unidentified greaterdemon. Our shamans were unable to control the demon and were unfortunately slain, the feat of summoning such a demon may finally silence those who doubted their abilities.
