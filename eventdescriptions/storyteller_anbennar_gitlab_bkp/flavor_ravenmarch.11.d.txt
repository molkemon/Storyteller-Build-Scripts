The center of the ballroom floor clears, as the lord and the offensive young man select blades from an attendant. The lord selects a simple, practical saber, with filigree on the handguard, while their opponent selects an ornate, engraved rapier. A murmur ripples through the attentive crowd as both take up dueling stances. The young man unleashes a rapid barrage of thrusts and feints, pressing the lord’s guard, before dropping low and thrusting upwards. The lord dives out of the way, landing in a roll and slashing as they rise. With a swirl, the lord’s saber goes flying out of their hands, leaving the point of their opponent's rapier pressed to their heart.
