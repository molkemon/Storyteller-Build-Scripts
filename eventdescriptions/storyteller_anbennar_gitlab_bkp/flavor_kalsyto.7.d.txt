As Kalyin's light expands into the northern realm, we rejoice to know she has gone before us. The locals here believe in what they call the Supreme Spirit, to which they believe all return and from which all spirits are created, fragmented like Kalyin herself once was. Furthermore, they fear spirits they call Facestealers, and how fitting then is it not that we can finally show them the face of the one they have always worshiped?
