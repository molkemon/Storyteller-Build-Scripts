A thick smog of incense filled the room where the Court of Change resided. At its center, laid out on an array of cushions, sat Akhaxpa. As the ruler approached her and knelt down, she opened her three eyes, filling the room with a blinding blue light.

"I thought lizardfolk were naturals in the water? Why then are so many of your people not taking up a life on the ocean? I thought you were so proud of the change your people had in taking to the seas when once they were river people."
