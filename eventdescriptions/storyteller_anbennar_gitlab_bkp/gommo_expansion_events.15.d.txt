The cooperation with gnomish weapons engineers and especially personnel of the guild has had a large positive impact on the technological and industrial capabilities of our great city. From the beginning research and production of artillery were a main focus of the cooperation, and our newest guns are undoubtedly the envy of all Haless. The guild has now presented a proposal for further expansion of the project by founding a fullyfledged artillery division based in a location. Funding this organisation could allow us to achieve a higher quality in production than ever before, making bigger and stronger armaments a certainty. A refusal could however lead the guild searching for more willing partners elsewhere, and one day we might see their guns turned against us.
