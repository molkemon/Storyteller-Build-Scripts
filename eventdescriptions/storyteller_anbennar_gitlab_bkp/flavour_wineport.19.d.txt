Another bottle for you, my lord?, Inquired the servant, who looked at their lord with a mildly worried look. Their ruler simply waved their hand to indicate that they wanted more; by the Gods, their head was hurting. And it was causing them to focus on who they were here to meet; a Sorncost wine baron, who was currently giving a rather long winded explanation about Sorncost wine, their family history, how they had been growing grapes since the days of the Damerian Republic. Oh, and adding the Sorncost wine to the national collection. 'It would make a prime addition, would it not? I can already see you have taken a liking to it, my lord.' The wine baron added with a wry chuckle. Their lord looked to their side, after finishing another glass of this delicious wine, and saw 12. No, wait, they focused, and there were only 6 bottles of Sorncost on the table next to them. Wait, six?. They swore it was only one or two. But it simply went down so easily. They turned to the wine baron, and groggily nodded.
