Courteous smiles. Generous application of mijiu. A few well-placed coins. The Nincìshi indisputably exist, but when they do not want to be found, they are like dust in the wind. They have evaded our efforts, but the shadow council decided to contact our agents after we showed enough interest in their existence. Through our deeds and actions, we have placed several sympathetic voices within the council. It will do little for us now, but it makes any further efforts much easier.
