The war is over, our lord. We have won our independence! The overlords of both Empkeios and Keyolion have been defeated, and we are now free to pursue our own destiny. We shall not forget the help of our southern brethren in this war, and we shall not forget the help we have given them. We are now free to pursue our own destiny, make the most of every deal, and make our realm prosper. And we find ourselves in an enviable position: the market for slaves is booming, and we have the largest slave market in the region along with one of the best harbours. The future is bright for us.
