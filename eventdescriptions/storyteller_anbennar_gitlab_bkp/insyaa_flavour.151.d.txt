A common body type, familiar to a large quantity of mechanims. Recent discoveries have allowed us to recreate the configuration for mechanims without it or those who have swapped configurations previously. Noted for a horizontal chassis where the head, torso, and legs share a plane, two legs are slotted into joints at the lower torso area. Typically of lithe configuration for additional agility, and combined with the space optimization of the form itself, it allows mechanims to be much more agile and adaptive, letting us cover larger distances over rougher terrain in less time.
