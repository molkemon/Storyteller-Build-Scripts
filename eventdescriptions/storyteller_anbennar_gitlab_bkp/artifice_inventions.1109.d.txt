The Runethought Network, first innovated by the great old hold of Verkal Ozovar, is a modern culmination of enchantment magics. We have set up finely crafted runic towers throughout our nation, each engraved with runes of patriotic slogans, nationalist anthems, histories of our past lords, and poems of our native tongue. These runes are each connected to the spiritual unconsciousness of our people, and capture what it truly means to be part of our citizenry. By using harvested spiritmagic from Haless, we activate the towers; by keeping them active, the tower’s runes engrave themselves into the minds of our subjects. Our shared culture becomes unavoidable, and, before long, each citizen cannot help but speak our language and know our stories. Without having to put in any effort, they are integrated seamlessly into our society.
