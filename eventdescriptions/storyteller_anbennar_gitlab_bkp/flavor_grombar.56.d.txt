He and his brigands fought with ferocity and zeal, far longer than we would have expected them to, and for that we must commend them. However, his quest has met its predicted end, the armies of the selfproclaimed 'Prince of Vrorenmarch' now lie broken, his rebel's spirits shattered, and the will of the White Reachmen firmly broken by the fist of the Gray King. May his failures serve as an example to others; Vrorenmarch is gone, and this is now the home of the Gray orks.
