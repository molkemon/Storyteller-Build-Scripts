As war rages across a region, nations disappear from the face of the world. A century and a half of rule vanishes in a few years now and borders change with every skirmish. The legacies of several adventuring companies have been erased as friendships fall apart. In this environment, only the strongest shall remain. 

Evidently, one of the smaller powers on our borders feels like they are not strong enough to survive. Their ruler has approached our lord with an offer. In exchange for subsidies to pay off their debt and soldiers to guard their territory, they'd swear an oath of vassalage. It's a good offer, but we could always hold onto the offer and use it as a legitimate backing to claim the entirety of their territory as our own.
