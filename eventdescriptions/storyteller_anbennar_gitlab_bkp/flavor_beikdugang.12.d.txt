With the aid of representatives from the Lake Federation, members of every section of the realm have come together to write a new constitution for the republic. One with no place for conniving advisors or rulers, led and represented by the people. It's a new constitution for a new age of the world, one respecting the past but willing to break with ideas that have lost relevance in the changing times. A new system and a new way of doing things, this is our age. The age of the republic and the union of the lake and sea!
