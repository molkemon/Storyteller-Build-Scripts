Delivered by a purpleturbaned messenger, accompanied by a small squadron of the Lotus Court's guard, a message from the Raja is not to be ignored. The contents of the letter can be summarized as follows.  All ministries in the Raj will be subject to increased oversight and control from the Lotus Court, in line with its decrees. And accordingly, all Prabhi and Senapti of the realm will be subject to increased inspections and are expected to fully cooperate with the demands from the Raja and ministries.
