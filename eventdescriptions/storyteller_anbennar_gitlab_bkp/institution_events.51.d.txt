Custom dictates that Cannorian youth of quality should go on a grand tour around that continent to see the marvels of their age, meet with great minds and people of influence and generally build learn about the world and those in it. Our Cannorian friends in a nearby land have now extended an offer to us to send their heir on a similar journey. To a certain extent their offer reeks of arrogance, but the things they could learn there may prove very valuable to our realm.
