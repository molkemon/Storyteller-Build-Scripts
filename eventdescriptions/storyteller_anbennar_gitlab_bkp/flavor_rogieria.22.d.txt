The messenger was indistinguishable from the many that attend our capital in every way except one: they were wearing a hat in the unmistakeable shade of Damerian Blue. So our lord was not surprised, not quite, when the note they received from said messenger invited them to a quiet meeting in the stables, at dead of night. There, we received an emissary from Rogieria, who would have our backing in their quest to overthrow the 'false' Emperor and restore a Silmuna to their 'rightful' throne. On these decisions, the Empire of Anbennar turns - what says our lord.
