Eunuchs went into Grostunad to study the origin of the hobgoblins. They discovered a hidden chamber that narrates the origin of the hobgoblins, revealing that they were created during the times of the old empire as royal bodyguards. This is a gigantic revelation. Although at first the hobgoblins didn't believe us, they started to do so after their local leaders, who coincidentally seem to be a lot wealthier right now, began spreading this truth.
