Taychend is a vast realm. It encompasses tens of millions of people and countless villages, towns, and city-states—each fortified against attack, surrounded by a wooden palisade at the very least. Some wonder why it is so difficult to conquer, given its division. When one realizes just how easy it is to hide tens of thousands of warriors within its confines, they usually stop wondering. Unfortunately, today, we face that reality—after months of preparation, the scorned sibling has revealed themselves, summoning their army to their banner, and beginning their long march on the capital. No matter the outcome of the upcoming battle, there is no doubt—the Siblings’ War is about to draw to a bloody conclusion, and the Empire of Erankar will once more be united, one way or another.
