During renovations of Jaengung, our eunuchs have come to learn a lot about the story of the sage kings and their nature. Seeking to learn even more, they moved to the island of Somyonghon, where further investigation revealed that the sage kings were all beiklings, and that the spirit that once declared the sage kings was the incarnation of the first member of the Xion Dynasty. Thus, it is now the Xion Dynasty who shall inherit the title of the sage kings.
