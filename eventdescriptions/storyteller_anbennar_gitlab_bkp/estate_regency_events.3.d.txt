Blood feuds are the kind of things that make realms collapse, but they are as old as the human being itself. Their ruler thinks being in power is a golden opportunity to get rid of some political enemies and adversaries. They are using their power as a regent to mercilessly attack any voice that speaks against them or their faction, even when they do not. Of course, the other factions are not specially happy with how things are being done and might try to retaliate. We are in a crossroad where we have to decide what to do, should we intervene?
