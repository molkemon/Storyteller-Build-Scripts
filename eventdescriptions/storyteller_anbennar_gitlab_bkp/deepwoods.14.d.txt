Today, the most powerful ork ruler in the Deepwoods, set out for one of their usual hunting trips. But it soon became something else entirely, as they were approached by a beautiful dryad, who spoke in a lilting tune: You, Wondrous Wanderer. You, Chivalrous Conqueror A gift I have for thee. A most coveted power. To become a champion of the tree. Your future is bright, if you make right The suffering done unto me. Your power will grow, as will the seed you sow Your reach like the roots of a tree And will topple the kingdom of man All you must do is take the hand of thee. The dryad's singsong voice is a strange contrast to the fierce, fiery look on her face, which reminds the ruler of the anger on some nearby berserk warriors. She offers her pristine hand, lips curling into a smile, teeth bared. Her robe of leaves and ivy begins to slip. There is a choice to be made, here and now, one that will determine the future of our realm. But is it a choice at all?
