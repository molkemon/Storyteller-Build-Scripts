An eccentric farmer from one of our provinces has designed a way to irrigate his crops with great efficiency through a process he has nicknamed "Three Level Irrigation." Many in our realm, after being told of his feat, seek to document his methods or even reward him, though the latter may be a bit controversial, especially with the middle castes. Though the farmer's ingenuity initially seems impressive, many are doubting the ability to accomplish this amazing undertaking as a simple farmer.
