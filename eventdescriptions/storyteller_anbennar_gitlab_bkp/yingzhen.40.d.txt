As news of our conquest of the Goldscales spreads throughout the land, an envoy of the merchant approaches our lord. The hard part is over, he says; now all we have to do is go and gather the scales. The kobolds are a crafty sort, however, and somehow a few of them have caught wind of the deal. Some of them have already sought out our forces, promising loyalty and service in return for protection. Some members of the chamber argue that enfolding the kobolds will be for the best, in the long term. Others say that we very well may not reach that long term without the funds this merchant offers.
