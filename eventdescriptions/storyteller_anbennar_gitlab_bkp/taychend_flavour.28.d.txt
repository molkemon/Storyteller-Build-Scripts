Chendhyans were routed—many were killed. Total victory! So said the hastily written message from the commander of our frontier garrisons, which we received today. Further investigation confirmed their report—the Chendhyan raid was completely broken, with their riders routed by our forces' steadfast defense, and many of their mounts slain. Our own forces suffered minimal losses compared to them, though there are still grieving wives and mothers who will view this day as a bittersweet victory. Nonetheless, the frontier is secure once more.
