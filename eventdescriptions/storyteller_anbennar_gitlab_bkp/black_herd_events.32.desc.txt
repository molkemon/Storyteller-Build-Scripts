Markhentakruan Improvement Notes - Final Entry - Today The culmination of years of research has completed its final trial. Every refinement, every failure, every scrap of lost wisdom reclaimed from the past has led to this. Markhentakruan has reached its final form, merely restored but improved, sculpted by my power to possess a new strength surpassing the Black Herd of old.

To test the final iteration of the spell, I traveled to one of the smaller kelaktars within my domain. My warriors brought forth a thousand ogres from within its walls, and once they had been slain, their bodies were arranged in several great piles along the outer wall. As I cast the spell, the corpses began to warp and melt like beeswax beneath the sun, their bodies running together until it was impossible to tell where one ogre ended and another began, each mound a mass of contiguous flesh studded with jutting limbs and half-formed faces. It was a beautiful sight, worthless lives transformed by death into fuel for my magic.

Then, in an instant, it was gone. The blast struck like a falling star, the sound and shockwave leaving us deaf and reeling even from a great distance. When the dust finally settled, I approached to find that the wall had been annihilated, the rubble hurled into the town, leaving the surrounding structures as shattered as the fortifications.

I can see it now: ten thousand dead lake humans piled high against their own walls, their cities burning as the Black Herd rides through the breach. This will be the pinnacle of Markhentakruan, the weapon that will secure the greatest victory in centaur history. It is ready. It is perfect.
