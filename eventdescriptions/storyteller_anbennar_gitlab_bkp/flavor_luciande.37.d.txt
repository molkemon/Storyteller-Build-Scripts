Lucian Síl na Toars has brought fear to the heart of everyone who has heard his name muttered. He has commanded the Company of Thorns for far too long. After the decisive fall of the Greentide, he and his company set out for Escann, not to conquer the old holdings but to create something new—a realm of their own.

Lucian rules his realm with an iron fist, keeping all the power to himself as he trusts no one with authority in the infancy of his realm. However, he is plagued by the cursed blessing of unlife and has managed to maintain the masquerade so far, partly because in his ripe vampiric age, he has not sired others. There might come a time when Lucian is forced to rely on his own vampires, no matter how inconveniently troublesome it may be.

Still, the realm is young, and threats riddle its borders. There is much work to be done, but Lucian will see his domain prosper and be forged in his vision.
