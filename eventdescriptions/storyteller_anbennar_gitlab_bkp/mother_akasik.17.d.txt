During the aspect of reciprocity our faith encourages us to listen to other points of view and to be open to change, this is a great virtue of ours, in this time we shake off our 24 years of stagnancy and start to catch up and surpass the rest of the world. However, a recurring problem we face is the fractiousness of our people during this time, new ideas don’t necessarily mean good ideas, and we all know that, the problem is actually agreeing on which ideas are actually good and which ideas are not. Our court has recently been divided into 2 factions, those who support a significant legislative change and those who oppose it. We have to pronounce ourselves on the issue, or things could spiral out of control.
