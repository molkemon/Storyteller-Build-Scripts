Today, we reclaim our birthright. The Ynnic Empire, the greatest nation in the world, having forgotten the virtues that made it worthy of existing, now remembers them. But today that damage is undone, for the Ynn are united, and an Empire once more. Our lord has been crowned in a solemn ceremony, and invitations were sent out to all knights of the realm for the greatest tourney the Ynn has ever seen.
