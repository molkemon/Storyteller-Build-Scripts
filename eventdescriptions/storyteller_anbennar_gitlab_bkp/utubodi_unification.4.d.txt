We may be the ones rebuilding the Cloud City now, but we are by no means the first ones to try. An excavation team has uncovered evidence of a prior attempt, which by now must lie centuries in the past. Some interesting finds have been discovered on the site which provide insights into how people in the past lived, but more importantly, we now have a better understanding of some of their more interesting building techniques.  There are sketches in the ruins showing figures using unusual and advanced techniques of metalworking which we know nothing of: they have likely been forgotten in the centuries since this failed reconstruction attempt. It would be difficult to redevelop them using the evidence uncovered here, but if we devoted enough time and energy to it we just might be able to do so.  The people of the past would not begrudge us adopting their ideas to hasten the realization of our shared goal of restoration. But if we did, would the city born from our work truly be ours alone?
