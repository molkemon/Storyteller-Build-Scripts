Your enchantment does not reach enough minds to hand over a bloodless victory. A few astute defenders are beginning to discover that their fellow soldiers are not acting with a will of their own, and are beginning to gather the suspects; some are even breaking out of their trance.  Forcing a man to surrender is one thing, but forcing him to fight against their perceived allies is another, however, this might be the only option to salvage this situation without completely abandoning the enchantment.
