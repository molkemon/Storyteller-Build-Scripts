Our developing embassy has received a most auspicious visitor. The Unthroned Queen, sovereign of the Dromandrod, archfey, and patron of Pelomar, has appeared to grace us with her presence. However, her visit comes with a purpose, and one some in our realm may find inconvenient. She has demanded we bend the knee to Pelomar and recognize once and for all their authority over all of Eordand.  Of course, we can always refuse this demand, even at the risk of offending a great ally to the Peitar people. Some of our advisors have even suggested that the Unthroned Queen may be persuaded to allow us to usurp Pelomar's place, if we've managed to demonstrate our superiority over them by this point. What should our answer be?
