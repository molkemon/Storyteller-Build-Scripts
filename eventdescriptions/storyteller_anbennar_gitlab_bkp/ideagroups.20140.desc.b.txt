Ever since the newcomer appeared at the royal court, they have been a mystery to our own elite class. Nobody knows their name nor has been able to find information about this secretive person. That is until now. It turns out, this person is none other than a banished politician from abroad. Originally intended to be executed, the former minister barely escaped with their life. Ever since then, they have become a trusted member of the court. As thanks for our hospitality, they grant us documents which would justify the total subjugation of that nation.
