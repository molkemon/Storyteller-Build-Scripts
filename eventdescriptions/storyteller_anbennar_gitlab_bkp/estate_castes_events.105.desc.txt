Many cocky and arrogant harimari warriors of the upper castes love to impress each other and the ladies. In these special tournaments, only members of the upper castes can register. A few days ago, the son of one of our most prestigious generals had won a streak of victories in single combat and was about to be the victor of the tournament, until a human boy entered. Some warriors decided it would be funny to enroll a random boy into the tournament, possibly due to a loophole in the rules. This proved to be a grievous error, as the boy turned out to be the son of a Xianjie warrior, known for their martial arts culture. The boy quickly defeated each of his opponents, including the general's son in the final round. The upper castes are now outraged at being outclassed by a "peasant boy" and demand stricter rules.
