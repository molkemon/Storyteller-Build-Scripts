We have made great improvements in developing spiritfire, especially when controlled by a person, but our efforts in enhancing the tools for spiritfire manipulation have been lacking. A clear example of this is the brazier used by our spiritfire monks in combat. Currently, it is a large, bulky device that either sits on the back or head of the warrior during battle. While these braziers are effective, they are clunky and require careful handling to avoid toppling and landing in the dirt.

We have several options for improving these braziers. By removing most of the supporting metal framework, the brazier can still carry spiritfire while being much lighter and more maneuverable than the current version. However, this will be more expensive than our current method of spiritfire handling.
