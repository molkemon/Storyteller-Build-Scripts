The tournament fields are packed, as the lord walks onto the battlefield to a thunderous roar of applause. Before him stands one of the mightiest warriors in all of the Caehnate. A warrior clad in thick metal armour, wielding twin lances at their sides. One of the feared and mighty Iomburvlait, viewed by many as some of the most fearsome fighters not just in the Caehnate, but the entire world. Clad in their spiked armour, the Iomburvlait begins a slow, lumbering charge, aiming to knock the lord off their feet before the match even has the opportunity to properly begin. Reacting quickly, the Caehn rapidly jumps out of their way, spinning their mighty halberd into the Iomburvlait’s flank, resulting in a roar of pain. Despite the pain, however, the Iomburvlait continues pushing forward, knowing that even a single of their mighty blows could bring the Caehn to their knees. This exchange keeps up for a long while, the Caehn making small jabs whilst the Iomburvlait desperately attempts to get the single hit that they need to connect. Eventually, however, after both combatants have well and truly exerted themselves.
