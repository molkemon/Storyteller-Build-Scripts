The Phokhao Mountains are a deadly place to live; riddled with faults and fissures, prone to avalanches, and dreadfully cold to boot. Beneath the mountains, on the other hand, one's beard can gray in peace, tranquility, and comfort. This is a comfort the dwarves have known for millennia, digging deeply and greedily. The mountain store hides many secrets to us, yet what of above? The Mountain King has nary thought of the surface since the days of the Phoenix Empire, nary rallied the Hold Guard to battle Verkal Ozovar's foes.  The Baihon Xinh has fractured, the longears of the west supplanted by the sheer ineptitude of the south Kai. Yet all that dwells under the mountains shall fall within the dominion of the Mountain King. and they are no exception.
