Amidst the heaving death of the empire of the dwarves, there was, for a time, a singular beacon of hope among the dwarves: Argordstun. Not even the most fanatical of our brethren, for a time, could break through its formidable defences, forcing us back to retreat and rethink time and time again. The dwarves of the place grew defiant, proud, comfortable even in the false pretence that; so long as the walls stood; Argordstun and her peoples were safe, even if it meant they were to be the last of their kind.  Our defilement of this place was done with great prejudice and pleasure, the husk of this ruined hold serving as a reminder to all, orks or otherwise, that it is not a case of if, but when we will bring all the Serpentspine to its knees.
