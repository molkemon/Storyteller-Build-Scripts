For the first time in over a year, the light on the Zatsarya glowed. Not with the brightness of the stars, but with an ominous yellow. It pulsed ominously like a heartbeat, and the clicking grew louder and louder until... 

Out from each spire poured thousands of lizardfolk. They moved in unison, each movement matching the others, each one’s gaze following the others. They would raise their hands and fire their spiky growths in unison. They would scuttle forward and take defensive positions in unison. They would click in unison. Always clicking. It never stopped. The entire swamp clicked and clicked and clicked and clicked and clicked and...
