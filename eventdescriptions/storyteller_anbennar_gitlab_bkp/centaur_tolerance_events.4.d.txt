Centaurs. A people with humanlike features with the upper portions of their bodies and horselike features with the lower portions. Well known for their ability to organize large warbands and caravans across the steppes of the Forbidden Lands, one of these Centaurs now approaches you wishing to aid your realm in order to garner more respect for their people. How shall we deal with this creature? We could offer them a place on our council to help our logistical organization or we could just let the Centaur help us in a way they see fit, that is if we even care about their help at all?
