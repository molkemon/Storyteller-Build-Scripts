In the wake of the eastern campaign's conclusion; with the groves of the east sitting firmly under the control of our people; the Ceanns and Ceannas of the eastern Salla have congregated in the Elynaile's Great Hall, eager to celebrate the return of their lands to their people.  To Farwen, however, the celebration itself was not the highlight of the event; rather, she had gotten the opportunity to speak with the Ceanns and Ceannas to reconcile their tales and wisdom with her own experiences in reclaiming the eastern groves; forced to adapt their strategies to the environment, she and her warriors had learned much.  Each of the eastern Salla's values and expertise varied greatly. Salla Scotha, the northernmost of the groves, specialized in medicinal care for their people; their alchemical expertise had proven invaluable in concocting elixirs to aid the natural mending of injuries, and to reduce the suffering of the injured.  Salla Ahain, from the River Grove, had long cultivated a highly positive relationship with the fey. They placed great value on their harmonious existence with the fey; though it didn't do much to protect them from the invaders.  Salla Ghul, from the southernmost grove; known as the Shadow Grove, relied greatly upon the art of stealth and reconnaissance; to know the weakness of an enemy and to exploit it with great precision formed the foundation of their warfare.  Farwen had seen each of these doctrines employed during the campaign, but one of the Salla's methods had grown on her more than the others.
