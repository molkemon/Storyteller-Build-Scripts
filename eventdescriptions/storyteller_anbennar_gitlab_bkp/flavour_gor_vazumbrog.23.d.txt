To the New Lord,  I submit this report on the aftermath of the devastation wrought by the dragon known as the Hunter of the Deep. The structural damage to the hold is catastrophic. Entire floors have become unstable, with supporting columns fractured or obliterated. The eastern galleries have collapsed entirely, burying countless lives beneath the rubble. Rescue efforts continue, but progress is slow and perilous, given the instability of the remaining structures.  The devastation extends to critical infrastructure. The central aqueduct has been severed, cutting off water to half the hold. The state forge lies in ruin, limiting the recovery of our already thin tool supplies. The sealed passages leading to one of the Vaults have been breached; we have contained the magic spill deploying the emergency golem squad.  Despite the severity of our situation I formally request a quarterday of leave to bury my daughter. After that, I await your orders.  Thrair Stonewright, Deputy Chief Architect
