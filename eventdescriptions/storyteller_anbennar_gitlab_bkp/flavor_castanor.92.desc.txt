Increased traffic and trade on the Dragon Road, though greatly beneficial to Castanor as a whole, does not come without cost. Drainage ditches need to be dug, stones repaved, patrols paid for, among what seems like millions of other things. Usually, these mundane costs are paid for by regular tolls and fees and handled by local administrators, but the situation has gotten out of hand in one particular section of the road, and such taxes are not sufficient. Officials have come to us to ask for monetary assistance. What shall we do?
