As the Golden Highway is restored, and soldiers garrison it and its major stops, Yanshen becomes just a tiny bit more connected. Merchants confidently travel up and down its length. Freed from the expense of mercenary escorts, they have been able to spend that excess money on trading more goods. Some of it has even trickled into our coffers. The untamed lands near the roads, favored locales of highwaymen and bandits, have seen their unruly inhabitants driven out. Enterprising settlers have seen fit to tame the productive but untapped frontier. Safer than ever, the realm has become that much more stable.
