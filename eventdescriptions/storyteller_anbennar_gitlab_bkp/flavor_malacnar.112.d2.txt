While Eordand has offered so far no shortage of peculiar plunder—such as a set of 7 glowing magic swords in the colors of the rainbow, or a druid staff which periodically grows more druid staffs from its wood—our generals report that with the occupation of the province of G32 Plunder, they have uncovered objects not quite like anything before. Specialized mages and scholars were called to investigate, and concluded that the magical energy coming from the plunder in G32 Plunder is of unparalleled levels even for the crafts of the magically-talented Eordan. These are objects created in the age before the Ruin of Aelantir, a catastrophic event which is more commonly known in our myths as the Ending Flood. Cannorians call these objects 'Precursor Relics,' and while some of them are extremely powerful weapons, others are frivolous commodities like amber-glass urns which light up at night, or imperscrutable pieces like what looks to be the half of a broken gear, worth only for treasure-keeping or for scholars to try and decipher. As our troops round up the entirety of these Precursor relics, they await the lord's verdict on what to do with them: shall they be destroyed, to please the most stern of our religious authorities who want nothing to do with the sinful pre-Flood world, or shall they be kept and brought to our homeland as part of the spoils of war?
