Harimari are famed throughout the realm as especially skilled administrators, courtiers, and generals. Many of the Harimari who choose to wander from their homelands in Rahen and Yanshen bring those skills with them, making a living by selling their services to the rich and powerful who need a little extra help managing particular issues. Now a small caravan of these roaming Harimari has emerged in the capital and offers help with your administration. The established courtiers are wary, though, none suggesting that the extra hands wouldn’t help, but none volunteering to be the first to show their books to those outside the department.
