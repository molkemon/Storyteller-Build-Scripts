Whispers in the dark speak of the Undergrowth Goblin clans showing signs of internal conflict. Lines in the sand are being disregarded, and spears are being raised, promising future bloodshed. Accusations fly, with clans seeking any excuse to reduce their rivals' holdings to cinders, a malicious intent typical of the Undergrowth Goblins. These conflicts could potentially harm production within the Jade Mines and add to the worry with reports of heavy infighting in The Belly. It is unclear if this is due to a displeased group of slaves or something more profound. Regardless, the might of the Command should not be challenged by mere slaves!
