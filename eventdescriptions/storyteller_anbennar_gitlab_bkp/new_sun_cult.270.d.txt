Our medics are used to treating the effects of cannonshot and gunfire. They are less used to handling soldiers who have allbutdrowned, or who have been crushed in a living landslide, or have been bludgeoned by a magical tornado, or who have been burned alive. But even if they had been, it would have only stemmed the losses, not come close to eliminating them.  Though the casualties at Amussu’s elementals’ hands were nontrivial, thankfully, it is over. The ancient GodKing mustered the forces of nature itself against us, but Surael granted us dominion over His land, and we have proven our mastery once more.  If we want to finish this early, now would be the time to attack Amussu.
