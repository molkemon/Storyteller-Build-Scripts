Surael is a God of infinite compassion and mercy for His creation, but that does not mean that He is not filled with righteous fury towards those agents of the Malevolent Dark who willingly chose to defile the sacred Order of the universe He ordained. This truth lies at the heart of the teachings espoused by the Bon Tilenndimin, the House of Righteous Ire or BetKastisar as the Bulwari call it. Built on the shores of the Odogala near the walls of Fojara, its temple gardens in part resemble more a training field than a verdant oasis. For both priesthood and laypeople here are united in their understanding that sometimes it takes a heart filled with noble indignation to wield the blade that protects the innocents and repels the forces of Darkness. The priests of this temple therefore do not lead a life of quiet contemplation, but seek to achieve greater understanding of Surael’s will by testing the endurance of their mortal forms. At any time of day, some amongst their number can be observed to practice the ritual combat forms for hours on end, with many claiming that exhaustion and ritualised repetition allow them to achieve deep spiritual insights.
