Ages have passed, and time has struck us like waves against a rock. Currently, we are the last remaining realm of the Reaver kingdoms. The province of Deveria has abandoned that tradition, Pearlsedge now cares more about the Empire, and Eborthil has moved to embrace trade rather than raiding. We are the last ones. If we are to endure, we must rebuild our domain. We call upon the old Reavers among the nobility to share their advice.
