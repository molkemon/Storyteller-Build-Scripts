You are fond of them. Umorozi offers a different calm to Cheni in the realm; a grand chamber of pillars, a barracks-space in the lattice, the mindless physical peace one feels at mile three of a run. When the Vile One’s mind wanders here, it is ready to work. -My shadow warriors? The Vile One smiles. -Yes, I suppose I am. They work as they should; they are faithful hounds.

The Vile One is seized by a sudden curiosity; she reaches out. She discovers Umorozi is, right now, physically on the other side of Sarhal. But only by reaching does she know — through the lattice, to think and speak with one another is to be together. The feeling is of being in the same room, the same corner of the party.

She feels Umorozi’s disapproval even before the thought arrives in response. Then she feels Umorozi cease the thought, rephrase things in her mind. She smiles — before they latticed, she would have looked upon Umorozi’s stoic face and only imagined such shifts and eddies.

Umorozi’s thought, eventually, is firm. -We are your dogs now. They are barely toys, and they split our focus.
