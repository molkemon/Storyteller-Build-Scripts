Once Karshyr was an island, just an outpost of the ancient empires. Once it was a town, a port for controlling halfling vassals of our predecessors. Once it was a city, the hub of the western Adzalaz, second only to Karassk. Once it was a vassal, a cog in the empire of Karassk, their first subject. Once it was an Ikatara, one of the squabbling states of Taneyas, fighting for a meaningless goal. Once it was a kingdom, the Master of the Gulf, the face of the lizardfolk to Halann. And now Karshyr is an empire, the greatest state of Sarhal, if not Halann as a whole. We have seen 332 empires come and go, watching them rise and fall, witnessing their glory and their collapse. We will not repeat their mistakes. We will not be dominated by the promise of future prosperity; we will seize it for ourselves. We have fought and bled for our independence and won't surrender it to the naga merely because they claim it was foretold—after all, the prophecy never cared for us when we were subjects. We shall not go into history like Karassk, an empire which enslaved anyone it could. Instead, we shall recreate the balance of magic within our island from scratch, creating wonders that will secure our name and our seas forevermore.

This is where our Khateshala come in. Their studies of the spires, once considered mere folly, have yielded the resources and advances to conceive of magic at a scale once thought unreachable. We shall begin to transform the entire population of our island into Khateshala, who form the core of our magic and our fleet. And after much testing, we are ready to implement the next step of the project: to transform our royal family into Khateshala.
