To the orks, orkrend was known as Trickster, a shadow in the night whose traps and ambushes were as important to his deadliness as his proficiency with the sword. Many saw him as a shadow, a nightmare, the Serpentspine's deadly darkness embodied.  The orks might be surprised to see the monument we have built to Gerin in orksfall; though the only orks to be found anywhere in the vicinity are corpses. The statue is carved of bright marble, carved and polished to the point it sparkles in the torchlight. Gerin's gaze looks towards the distance, just as the man himself always looked beyond.  He gestures orkrend almost absentmindedly, it was always an extension of his body after all, and accurately conveying that took the sculptor team as long as the rest of the work combined. We did take one liberty, only one; the pommel is adorned in garnets. He was ours at the end, after all, and we are his.
