It has been a long journey, but now it is finally time to reap the fruits of your labor. After the hardships of the past few days, there are no more obstacles left, no more enemies to fight and no more hardships that could best you and your men. You make sure to explore the rest of the complex; to the extent it is powered up, anyways; and stow away all of your findings for the way back. It takes some time, but eventually you are sure you have done all you can for now. As your expedition gathers for the departure, you once again praise them for a job well done and promise to reward them generously upon a safe return. Then, you finally head out.
