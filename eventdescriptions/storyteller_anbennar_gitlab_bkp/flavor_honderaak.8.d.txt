When our clan leaders led us out of the Khenak range and into the Empire of Anbennar, many gnolls followed against their better judgement. What, after all, could be gained by bowing to what is just another foreign ruler. Yet the clan matriarchs persisted, as our lives were forfeit if we remained in Businor. What we found, however, surpassed even their wildest dreams; our own state, where we would live alongside those who called it home before we arrived. Sanctioned by the very Emperor of Anbennar. Our gratitude is boundless, and we shall work hard to live up to the expectations that have been placed upon us.
