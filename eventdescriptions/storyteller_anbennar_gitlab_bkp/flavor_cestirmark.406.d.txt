During a series of investigations, the Special Cases Prevention Bureau has discovered eldritch artefacts and occult knowledge in the possession of one of our highest-ranking advisors! When confronted, they did not deny the accusations and immediately confessed they did study eldritch knowledge—but not for their own benefit. They claimed it was for the good of the entire realm, as how can one govern the province during the Shadow War without knowing anything about what Cestirmark is facing? This was their argument at least, and the Bureau can definitely agree with it, since they share the same opinion and methods. The question is if we can trust them.
