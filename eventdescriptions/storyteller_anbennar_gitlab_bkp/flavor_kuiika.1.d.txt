The War for Zena proved two things. The first is that we can unite Horashesh. The second is that, if we are to unite Horashesh, we need an army. We need not just every warrior to dust off his shield and grab his dulled spear in the name of Kui. No, we need to invest in our soldiers. We need iron. We need training. We need a professional fighting force.  Send out the word to every man of means. Never again shall Horashesh fall under the thumb of a foreign empire. Horashesh shall be united beneath our banner. Lend us your aid and I promise you all, your investment shall be rewarded.
