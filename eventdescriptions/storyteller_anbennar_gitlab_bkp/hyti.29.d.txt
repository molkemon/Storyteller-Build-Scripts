There's no place as opposed to harpy wellbeing as the Dwarovar. No space to maneuver, no light to see, no wind to invoke.  And yet the call of the unknown coming from below the mountains has continued to ensnare the scientific and adventuring types. Whether they hope for material gains, for ancient dwarven knowledge, or are just in it for the thrill, every year a handful of them organize an expedition for the caverns.  It doesn't matter that many never return.  News from the latest one of those, a scientifically focused endeavour this time, just reached the surface.
