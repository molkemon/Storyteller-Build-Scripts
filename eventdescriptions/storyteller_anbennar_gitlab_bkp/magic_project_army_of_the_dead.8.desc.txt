Conclusion of testing.  After conversion, zombie general proceeded to attempt to "eat the brains" of nearby sentients. We set it loose on a platoon of soldiers; rank broke immediately on sight of first consumption. Great success.  Skeleton general did not shatter their ranks, but lack of rotting flesh around digits left it capable of wielding and firing muskets. Set loose on a platoon of soldiers; difficult to consistently hit from more than sixty paces, owing to its slim profile. Tactical dismantling of the entire platoon from afar ensued. Great Success.  After conversion, revenant general stalked and attacked its killer before vanishing into thin air. Revenants appear unviable for use in warfare.  Ghoul general was never converted. It is unclear what precisely a ghoul is or how it differs from a zombie. Many fruitless hours spent in archives researching this topic. Ghouls appear unviable for use in warfare.
