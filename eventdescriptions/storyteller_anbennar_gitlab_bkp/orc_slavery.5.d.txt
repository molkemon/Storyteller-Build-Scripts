The orks ruined this great region and brought with them nothing but death and destruction. With the rediscovery of Aelantier well underway, merchants from the FortuneSeeker faction have been selling orkish prisoners as workers for the colonies and have suggested we sponsor their operations in our lands in return for gold and investment. Slavery is wrong, and Castan himself was a slave, but this kills two birds with one stone. We can get rid of the invaders and get funds to restore Escann for the better.
