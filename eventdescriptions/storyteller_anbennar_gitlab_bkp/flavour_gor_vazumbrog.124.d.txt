The dwarves ventured deep into the traditional Eagle Hobgoblin lands, once again finding themselves close to and impossibly far from their underground homeland in pursuit of another mark. They didn't know exactly what to expect, only that the rate at which merchants and pilgrims were disappearing from Nenukinan Nubikya suggested that whatever was happening here was a task worthy of the Dauvek Beker.  And so it was with no little surprise that they heard a gentle "mew" emanating from a nearby cave. Had some poor merchant's mouser sought refuge in the icy passage after its master had been killed? That assumption was quickly dispelled when a massive speckled paw emerged from the cold dark to swipe at its wouldbe rescuer, sending him bouncing down the mountainside to an unrecoverable grave. The impossibly tall cat, three dwarves high at the shoulder, poured itself out of its hiding place, ready to snack on another unsuspecting victim.  Unfortunately for the great beast, the Dauvek Veker rarely remained unsuspecting for long.
