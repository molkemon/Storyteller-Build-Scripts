\"The soul is a volatile and fragile thing. Bereft of a body, most of them move on to the next life. Most." Famous Monster Hunter Dr. Reginald sil Vekt  Arguably the most important and delicate step in the path of lichdom is the lengthy process of enchanting the phylactery to become a suitable home for the immortal ruler's soul, turning it into a beacon for their dark essence that calls more strongly than the allure of the afterlife. The mind, after all, may not want to move on, but the blind and dumb soul has fewer qualms, and so must be safely entrapped for the rest of eternity.
