We have set up a formidable slave trade system. Slaves from far and wide find themselves in our realm to work our lands or just in passing, towards final work places near and far. Nevertheless, the foundations of our independence reside in equality. We fought our Alecandi oppressors for our equal rights to trade, prosper, and live. The sheer hypocrisy that this represents when paired with our magnificent slave-based system is obvious to everyone. While our own citizens may be willing to look the other way, even for the sake of an easier life, our rivals, and especially the slaves, clearly are taking issue. Now, with the consolidation of our power in Kehionai and the rising standards of living in our nation, ideas of rebellion are starting to run wild among the slave population.
