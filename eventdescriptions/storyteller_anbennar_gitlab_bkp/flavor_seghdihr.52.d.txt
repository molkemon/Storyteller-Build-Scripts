Goblin thieves have been rampaging through the surrounding lands. At first it wasn’t anything for authorities to be concerned about too greatly. A ransacked shed here, a caravan looted there: all things well within the scope of local authorities. But as of recently the scale and scope of these thefts has exploded, with the most recent being an entire arms shipment to the local garrison having been stolen, including weapons and ammunition. It’s obvious to see that things will only escalate from here.
