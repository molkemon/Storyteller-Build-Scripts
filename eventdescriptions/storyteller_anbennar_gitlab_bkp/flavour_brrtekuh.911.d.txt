The night was cold and dry as always. A humble abandoned abode was found, vaguely pyramid shaped yet lacking the many features and Khetist details found in the others. As our excavators attempted to pull its long mummified corpse out like the rest, a blinding light suddenly vaporized the temple. Not long after, the clear skies gathered to create dark clouds never before seen by Salahad roamers, striking down on our land with burning light. Yet as quickly as they came, the clouds dissipated, leaving only dead bodies and bewilderment.
