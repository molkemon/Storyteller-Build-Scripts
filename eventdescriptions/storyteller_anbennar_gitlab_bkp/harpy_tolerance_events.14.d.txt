Gnawing hunger never truly leaves, the usual fare; tough cuts of beastmen, stringy goblin meat, alas, even rabbit, deer, and fish; has grown tiresome. You crave novelty. As if summoned by your thoughts, one of your subjects approaches, dragging a corpse behind him. He speaks of something that could sate your hunger and your curiosity all at once. It is a strange creature, built like a human woman but adorned with magnificent bronzecoloured wings. "Threw a rock at it while it was flying over," the hunter grunts proudly. "Brought it for the larder, my lord." Your eyes are drawn to the creature's powerful legs, shaped so much like a great bird's. You can almost taste the roasted meat, the crisp skin.It has been far too long since you've had drumsticks.
