Upon first inspection, the cavern appears to be like any other, a small opening within the mountain, filled with air rather than earth. But only upon first inspection. For once you enter the cavern, its truly wondrous properties become apparent. The walls seem to be entirely encrusted with crystals. When light is shone upon them, they reflect light in such a way as to create a complete tapestry of light, winding, twirling, and shifting as the light sources move. This may be the closest that we ever get to true beauty.
