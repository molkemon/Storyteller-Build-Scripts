There is one god. Our ruler knows this, has known this for millennia, and so too does their title. But what is that god's nature? Perhaps we have not quite understood it; until now, with the Ravelians having come to our shores. The Ravelian Societies that have sprung up across our realm are proving popular, and count among their members many highranking officials from the court in our capital. As a consequence, many throughout the land are seeing Surael as a blazing, fiery, respelendent gift from the One God, whose last fragment should be the center of our worship. And even our monarch has found some of these speeches convincing. It seems a new path for our realm lays ahead, one of insight and discovery, of listening to the Fragment's teachings and following Its guidance. Should we make such a decision official?
