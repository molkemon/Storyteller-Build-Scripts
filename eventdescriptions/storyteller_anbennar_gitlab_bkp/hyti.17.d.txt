Even though Hytiranyalen had now complete control of Vainviria, this didn't mean the Nansaleni queen lost all her power; she was still the leader of her flock, her voice something we couldn't completely discard, even if we wanted to.  For this reason, our ruler was meeting the dethroned harpy, dragging yours truly along. Mindnumbingly boring hours of politics ensued, with scant few rejoinders to keep me entertained. Even the two monarchs couldn't really handle the tedium, and breaks were numerous; during one of those I was approached by the Nansaleni Matriarch.  "Dreamer. You are known to me."; yeah, she really talked like that; "Your ideal might be noble, but you're soaring too high. The gift has shown what awaits you. Failure. You'll fail, again and again and again." Fantastic, I thought, the diviner was here.  Divination; the realm of charlatans and scammers. If you can predict the future, tell me the date of my death, then maybe I'll believe you once I make the final landfall. Of course I couldn't tell her that, but; big surprise; I didn't stay silent either.  "Your gift isn't telling me anything new, my lady. I know I will fail. Uncountable times. Until I discover something new. And then do it again. Upon those failures, I'll build a new world."
