A strange delegation has arrived in our settlement’s administration here in Teramzarai. The lizardfolk had long traded with the halflings of Fahvanosy, and had established an entrepot in the city – now, they seek the same arrangement with us. They demand that we respect them as equals within Roilsard and leverage our technological advantages to maintain and expand the portcity they call home. In exchange, they offer their fealty to us and loyalty to our endeavours.
