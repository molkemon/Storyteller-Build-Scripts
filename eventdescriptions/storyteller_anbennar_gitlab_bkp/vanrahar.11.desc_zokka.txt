IIt is done. The gnolls have been defeated. The mad beast Zokka and his pack have been thrown back; hopefully this proves to be a deathblow to their horrid "state". Rahen has so far been protected from gnollish invasion by the genies who ruled the Salahad before the Day of Ashen Skies. In more recent times, mountains and desert have prevented any incursions, but we weren’t going to wait and see if Zokka could be the first gnollish conqueror to overcome these barriers.  Nearly matching a harimari’s natural strength, gnolls also employ foul magics, making them a great challenge. Through holding fast to our traditions and sharpening our claws (unlike our soft brethren), we weathered their attacks. Plans are already being drafted for military colonies along the Salahad coast to serve as a bulwark between Rahen and Zokka’s pack (or whatever roving pack succeeds the socalled "DevourerofSuns"). Gnolls have never threatened Rahen before…
