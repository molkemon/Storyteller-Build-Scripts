A powerful example of enchantment artificery, the masks of enhancement are inspired by the masks popular with the Sun Elven aristocracy since the times of Empress Jexis. However, unlike their mundane inspirations, these masks contain powerful enchantments that affect both the wearer and those they encounter. Designed to enhance a certain cognitive skill set in the wearer – memory, concentration, oratory, arithmetic skills and so forth –  the masks come in many different categories. Depending on their intentions and duties, state officials might wear a mask of the bureaucrat, a mask of the accountant, a mask of the judge or a variety of other mask types. Regardless of their specific effect on the wearer, the masks usually project an aura of authority that tends to turn those in direct contact with the wearer more compliant and attentive. Due to the obvious potential for abuse in the wrong hands, the manufacture and distribution of masks of enhancement is usually strictly controlled by authorities, who hand them out only to trusted servants of the state or highranking aristocrats.
