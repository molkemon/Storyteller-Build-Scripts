The Kai have long been the core of our state’s people, having been the first to adopt the systems of the convocation. Now, as we expand further into new parts of the continent with new cultures to integrate into our empire, many of the Kai feel as if they are being left behind. While it is true that our eyes have been set on the horizons as of late, many in our court believe that we should not forget the people of our homeland, without whom we could not have made it to where we are now.
