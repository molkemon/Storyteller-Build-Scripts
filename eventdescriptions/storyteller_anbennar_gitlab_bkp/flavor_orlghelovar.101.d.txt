The mood within Orlghelovar is subdued, despite our recent great reawakening; the glassworkers are growing unsatisfied with their craft. While it is undeniable that our glassworkers are the finest, it has always been this way; they have not truly innovated or been challenged for many generations, and their passions are beginning to cool. For the first time since our ancestors began making glass, we can see new scientific possibilities on the horizon.  Our gnomish scientists have already made great leaps in the study of other materials, and with their assistance we may gain a deeper understanding of glass. We must gather our greatest glassworkers and our finest minds to search for new ideas; with one spark we could revitalise the whole industry forever.
