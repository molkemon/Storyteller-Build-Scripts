Once, we had a dream. Of beating our swords into plowshares, of channeling the legendary orkish tenacity into productive endeavours and of working side by side with humans to build a new future. A future free of the blood, misery, and hunger of the past.  We believed we had achieved that dream, proved those who viewed orks as nothing but savages and barbarians wrong. Arosha Oakbreaker taught us that it is the struggle of every ork to quell the rage within himself, but now we see that within our own people there are those who will never rise to this challenge. Instead, they give in to their bestial instincts, becoming the mindless monsters that others brand us as. Our dream may yet survive, but only if our society can quell the rage within itself.
