Though Siadunan propaganda decried the Elaienna as traitors to Harpykind for their deal with the Jaddari, the difficulties of the March have nonetheless resulted in some among us following in their footsteps – in one of our provinces, the local garrison has cut a deal with the population, marrying into the local elite (and therefore placating them) in return for alleviating taxes and tribute. While some in Siadan have decried this as shameful and traitorous, and unbecoming of true harpies, it has undeniably calmed the situation in the local area.
