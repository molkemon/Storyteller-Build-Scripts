Religion does not play an important role in our realm. Many amongst our leadership, including our ruler, do not even think of Dookan that often if at all, sometimes veering dangerously close to disbelief. However, they know that Dookan is often seen as the father and divine leader of the orkish race. It is Dookan that inspires our warriors. The life and death of his selfproclaimed son Korgus have been a rallying cry to many.  Now, decades after the Battle of Castonath, many ork clans have fallen or have turned their back on Korgus and Dookan. This has led to several orks fleeing into our lands, their hearts filled with fierce zeal for Dookan and hungry for revenge.  While Venomtooth as a whole is not very devout, we can use these fanatics as soldiers to fight on the frontlines. Our shamans often adopt a firebrand style of preaching in several villages within former Bonecarver land. Already, several of their warriors have joined the army and many seek a valorous death on the frontlines battling against the adventurers. They fear no danger and will never let up.  Our efforts to harness the orkish soul have borne vicious fruit and we will prevail.
