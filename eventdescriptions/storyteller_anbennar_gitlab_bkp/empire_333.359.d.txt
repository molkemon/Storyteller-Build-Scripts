What felled the 332nd Empire? It was an invasion from the Shadow Swamp by lizardfolk who they could not communicate nor reason with. When the hulking Ashhana burst out of the swamp and slaughtered all in their path, the empire was split in two and never recovered. When the 24th Empire collapsed, it was when some invading force arrived through the Zatsarya. Records differ on what beings burst forth, although some did say they resembled lizardfolk. Does this mean the 24th Empire was real?

No. Not because their story doesn’t make sense, but because do we really think it was the 24th Empire? Do we think the last empire was the 332nd? What we are going through is just another empire collapse, like both the 24th and 332nd, or whatever numbers they truly were. We thought we were different. We thought we had counted correctly. What fools we were.
