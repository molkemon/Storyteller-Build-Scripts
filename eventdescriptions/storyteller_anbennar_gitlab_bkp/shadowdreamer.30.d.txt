With a leader chosen and preparations nearing completion, the final question is of the scale.  Descending into Xazor’s Pit will be no small undertaking. The caverns are narrow and unstable, their depths unexplored and filled with a darkness still unknown to most orks. Slaying the beast will require countless warriors marching into the darkness, many of whom may not return. The question therefore stands: how many can we spare?  A smaller force would be cheaper to supply and organise, allowing us to focus our main forces elsewhere, but it would pose a great burden on those who descend.  A larger force, however, would provide a much greater chance to slay the bat. Tens of thousands of warriors descending together would leave little doubt of victory; yet, this would be a much more expensive investment, potentially drawing our forces away from where they may be needed.  The decision lies now with the Great Dreamer. How many shall descend?
