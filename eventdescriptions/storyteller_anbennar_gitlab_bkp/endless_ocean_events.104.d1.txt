A less attentive people might assume that the face of the ocean changes every day, but they do not understand the seas as well as we do. Oh, certainly waves rise and creatures dart about in the water, but these do not change what the ocean is fundamentally. You would not call a beast by a different name because its hair has settled differently so too do we recognize the seas are no more alien for the ways the surf roils and folds on any given day. However, over time, patterns arise from the unfamiliar and new labels emerge to describe novelties.  What is a label but a name? What is a story but the spinning of names like woven sails? What is an ocean but the collection of sea foam and raindrops? As we listen to the perspectives and observations of newfound peoples, threads and tale strands of the water itself form a story as much as a sea.
