A representative from the Hwalbaek has come to your court, bristling. They claim that their lord, the heir of their realm, has insulted our proud domain by openly proclaiming that we are a mere shadow of our former might since the death of Sage King Jeong-Hun Gyo. They insult our military, our prowess, our status within the lands of Gowon. Some even go as far as to claim that we are ripe for the picking, that our lands should rightfully belong to them instead. This is preposterous, of course, and the crown has other urgent matters to attend to at the moment. Unfortunately, the Hwalbaek claim that we must establish our firm superiority and nip this in the bud to save face, and will be dissatisfied with our rule if we should decline to act.
