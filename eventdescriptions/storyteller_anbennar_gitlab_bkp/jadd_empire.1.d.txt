Our Empire is vast and it has many people. It is at the whims of the DivineHerald that one group or area shall be favoured, as each DivineHerald will pick the seat of the Royal Court for their reign. There are several benefits to having our capital in a specific location, which we must consider carefully before we make our decision. Be assured that the area closest to the court will greatly benefit, while other areas will be less than pleased.
