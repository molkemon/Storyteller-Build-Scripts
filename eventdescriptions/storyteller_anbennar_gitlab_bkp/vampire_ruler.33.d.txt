Blood flows unceasing in the body. Night always follows day. And, it seems, a Vampire shall always rule the land. In recent years, the old ruler felt their undead flesh fading, their soul growing distant from this plane. And so they crafted one of their proteges into a worthy heir, offering them the Blood Gift, and teaching them the ways of the Hunt and the rituals of the Masquerade. Perhaps they sought yet another form of something approximating immortality. Now, upon the old Vampire’s passing, the scion has arrived in the capital. Their claim is threefold: the realm’s signet ring entrusted unto them, their predecessor’s last will and testament, and the bloody resolve in their eye.
Not all would see the succession work as such, however. They of course only now dare to challenge the old ruler, after he has passed from this realm, and even then they are watching their backs. But the presumptive heir’s ascension might not be quite as easy as it seems.
