We have arrived in their city, ready to view these famed blueprints. After making sure that we were not agents of their enemies, intent on probing for weaknesses, they allowed us to look. The splendour of Calasandur's work was evident on the pages, and they contained several improvements that we could adapt for the sake of Bivuzena. Slyly, we have queried if there are any blueprints they are either uninterested in or finished with, to see if we can hold them in our museum for a time before returning them.
