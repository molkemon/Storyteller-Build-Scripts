Our relationship with Cannor has been growing since we secured the spire at New Hookfield; without fear that they will encroach on that hallowed artifact, we can be much more open in diplomacy. This has also been aided by the fact that we have been increasing our production of both rare and new goods. The Cannorian traders have always been entranced by our dyes, while fish and salt are staple goods which will always be valuable to those traveling across the seas. However, the real breakout export for Cannor has been our pearls. Jewelry for the nobles is so prevalent within Cannor that gem shortages are frequent, and the riches found deep within the Dwarovar are both limited and hard to access. By contrast, we have been able to ship mountains of these precious jewels across the seas.

Our primary market has been Pearlsedge, who are attempting to maintain their monopoly over the gems in Cannor. They have come to our court wishing to purchase some of our pearl farm aquaculture designs for themselves. While this would hurt our economy in the long run, they are offering a ship of silver, and we are after all informal allies.
