Anbennar is a place of many peoples, many talents, and many skills. After disaster struck, we bonded together with our allies, reinforcing the bonds that had been forged during war. Now, after many years, those bonds are near unbreakable. The sailors of Moonhaven drink and share stories in Yshwood. The builders of Silverforge barter and trade their iron and minerals in the markets of Istralore. The wyvern riders of Verne soar through the skies, drilling with our armies. Istralore is a realm of Dwarves, Elves, Humans, and Half-Elves, all working together as one disciplined unit. After years of assisting our allies, the time has now come to take a step further. The leaders of Verne, Moonhaven, and Silverforge have met with our ruler and, after much deliberation and convincing, have decided to join our family formally. Each now swears fealty to Istralore, not just Anbennar— allies no longer, but true brothers to be honored and protected as one of our own.
