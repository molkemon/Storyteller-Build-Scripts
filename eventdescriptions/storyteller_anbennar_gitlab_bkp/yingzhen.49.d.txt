Our lord was meant to speak today. To provide some droning speech about some nonissue or another. Turnout was, at first, almost nonexistent. By this point it was all but an open secret that the ruler held next to no power. But he never appeared. In his place a middleaged man with hawkish eyes took to the podium, gazing upon a crowd that suddenly seemed intrigued. “The ruler is dead, and I will succeed them.”
