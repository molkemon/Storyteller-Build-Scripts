A dove and a crow, each with royal missives, arrive at our court. One bears the seal of the Empire of Anbennar, proud and decadent. The other bears the seal of their kingdom, confident and simple.  The crow chirps that a new epoch is beginning. One led not by monarchs in Anbenncost but by powers closer to home. The dove warns against the crow, claiming it is a trick to sow discord and spread chaos.  And our advisors? They growl of opportunity. A side must be picked. How will we be remembered in history?
