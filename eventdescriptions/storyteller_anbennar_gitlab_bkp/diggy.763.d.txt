A recent quake in a region resulted in a minor magmatic eruption. The local environment has suffered, but the eruption has left behind some volcanic ash. The local soil appears to be unusually fertile now, and there’s a couple of ways we could exploit this. We could either leave it as is, allowing people to settle where they wish, and allowing the local farming community to support a larger city, or import Serpentbloom, and allow an agricultural industry to take over the region.
