The rise of the Shamans is something out of our darkest myths, our oldest legends. And while discipline and order have kept most of our people in proper form, ready to beat back this ancient enemy, some have been engulfed by terror instead. In the panicked province, hobgoblins are refusing to join the army for fear of what might happen to their families while they are away, and some are even questioning the leadership of our ruler in light of this turmoil.
