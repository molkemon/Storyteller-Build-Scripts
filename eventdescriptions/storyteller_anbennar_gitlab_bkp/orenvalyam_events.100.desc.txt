The Kamrayakval is the leader of the Oren Nayiru faith and the commander of Orenvalyam's military forces. Though the Kamrayakval is technically first amongst equals, they are ultimately the last word, the last judgement, the last recourse. But no one person can rule alone—and what better trusted lieutenant than the one who would succeed them? Naturally, the character of whomever takes up the title of heir is of the utmost importance—and with their character, comes their politics.

The Monasticists, Kavalalists, and Kamrayakvalists have each rallied around their own candidate for the position, which will not only dictate the future leadership of the faith when the ruler dies but also bolster their faction's power in the short term. Who will prevail? Who will one day lead the Oren Nayiru?
