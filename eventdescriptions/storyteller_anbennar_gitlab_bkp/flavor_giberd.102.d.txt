As work has finally begun on completing Anvilwright, a cycle of chaos has immediately reared its gnomish head once again. Teams of gnome builders who have never worked in the underground before are causing frequent cave ins with their artificery equipment, still learning how best to use them, while dwarven builders have had to dedicate time and resources to digging them out.  Adding to the innumerable problems, overseers on site have attempted to cut wages to keep the project to a strict budget after the loss of several drills. This is causing dwarven builders to blame their gnomish workmates for the wage loss. This has culminated, once again, in a fist fight between the two excavation teams, adding even further to the delay. Eventually another deal was discussed, drawn up, debated and signed, and the gnomes given proper training on digging underground and teaching the dwarves how to utilize the artificery equipment of Giberd.  As the years drag on, the chancellor finds another messenger in the halls of Giberd, carrying a letter celebrating that digging has finally started at a routine pace and the balance sheet is fully resolved…for now. The messenger then calls in another two messengers, this time carrying a forty foot long, four gnome wide scroll of expenses.
