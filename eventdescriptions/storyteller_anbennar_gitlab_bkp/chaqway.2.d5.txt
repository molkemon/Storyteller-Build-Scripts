The Anqunu, the blue people of the south, have long favoured Cara Banqum with their occasional visits. The great ships of the Anqunu hunger for kallpamu, the blue rock which so concentrates magic, but also for silver and the precious metals of the lake peoples. Such trade is unpredictable, but it can change the fortune of a trading clan overnight. A permanent qhatuwai here will link all others with the arrived Anqunu, spreading their wealth among our people.
