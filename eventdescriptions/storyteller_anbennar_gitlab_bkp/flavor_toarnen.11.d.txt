Most Roilsardis finally arrived. We are giving them some lands around Aiscestir and welcome them with open arms. To keep them happy, they will pay us less tax and the first generation of newcomers will be exempt from conscription. Some of our farmers aren't happy as they are pushed back to the suburbs of the city and they have less places to cultivate but in the other hand Roilsardi immigrants are much more loyal to us. Meanwhile notaries run across Aiscestir as they weren't prepared to administrate so much immigrants, let's hope everyone have a house for winter.
