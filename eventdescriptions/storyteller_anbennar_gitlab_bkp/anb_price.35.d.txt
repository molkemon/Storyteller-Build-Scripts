For centuries, the vast Serpentspine Mountains lay in ruins as hordes of orks and goblins roamed through the corpse that was AulDwarov. In recent years, however, following the aftermath of the Greentide, the Serpentspine has slowly been resettled by the dwarven peoples. As a result, the ancient forges of the dwarven holds churn to life once more, and vast quantities of metal have begun flowing out of the mountains, and into the eager arms of the surface world.
