Our fleet set sail to find a western sea route to the fabled rich Islands and Cities of the west. It took them many days to cross the ocean westward, after they had found the Gulf of Rahen.  The fleet's captain was mauled to death by an angered Harimari warlord after he had been offended. Two of our ships had survived, one tried to return across the open sea but perished. The last ship continued west and sailed across the Uelos Lament, through the Ruined Sea, and as it arrived at our port it became the first ship to circumnavigate the globe.
