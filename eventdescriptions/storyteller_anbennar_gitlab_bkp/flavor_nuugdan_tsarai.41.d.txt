On the Day of Ashen Skies, Jaal famously slew a Facestealer, with nothing more than his mask and pure rage. It seems that he is no longer the only one.  The shamans who had captured the facestealer in the land of the Runsukhi have been attempting to kill it for a long time, and they have finally succeeded. Apparently the breakthrough came after they were visited by renowned spirit hunters from Xianjie, who had heard of the capture and wished to observe this type of spirit.
