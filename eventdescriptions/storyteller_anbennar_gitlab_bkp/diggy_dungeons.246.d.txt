As the last expedition member quickly takes cover, the fuse is lit, and an explosion rips through the stone roots and gate. But as the roots crumble, the magic within is released. In an instant, the fading explosion turns bright blue, and quadruples in strength. It engulfs several of the expeditionaries, who are turned to ash in an instant. As the smoke clears, the hallway behind the doorway is revealed. It appears to have had statues lining the sides, but the explosion has also smashed these to bits.
