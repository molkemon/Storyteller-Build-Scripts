Today is a great day. The Ynnic Empire, the greatest nation in the world, has been restored by one of the Rzentur people, so looked down upon by others once. But that is behind us, for we are all part of this restored Ynnic Empire. The lord wears the Ynnic crown of the Emperors of old, cheered on by their family and closest companions, and with their shouts they acclaim them as the new lord.
