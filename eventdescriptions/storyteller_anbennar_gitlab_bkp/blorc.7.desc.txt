Honours and treasures aplenty end up in the hands of those soldiers who first set foot inside of a newly conquered hold, whether due to ritual or circumstance. Here though the orkish vanguard was especially well rewarded, as many of them claimed a set of mithril armour from the plundered remains of the captured armoury in Mithradhum. Now as long as these orks or their descendants serve in our armies, the tip of every lance and the blade of every sword will be turned aside by our frontmost troops, giving us still greater successes on the battlefield.
