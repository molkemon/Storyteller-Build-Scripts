\"Here lies Gradolffound a box, wasn't looking, got lich pox." The epitaph of deceased adventurer Gradolf Flixis  With the discovery of the dark knowledge required to achieve lichdom, it is revealed that the very foundation of such a dark gift lies within a phylactery. A phylactery is some form of container which stores a lich's soul, typically something small and easy to transport or hide, for if the phylactery is destroyed then the lich will perish with it. The first step for any wouldbe lich is to craft such a wondrous item.
