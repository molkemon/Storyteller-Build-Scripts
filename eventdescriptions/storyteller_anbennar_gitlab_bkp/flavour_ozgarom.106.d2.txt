When the orc rebel Bramash's forces rose against their enslavers, the momentum of the revolt seemed inevitable. His forces swept across Endralliande and threatened even the capital of our realm. Yet in recent weeks the colonial forces have won victory after victory, such that Bramash can no longer be said to head an army at all, merely scattered bands enduring constant pursuit from our realm's forces. Thus the news out of Endralliande should not, perhaps, surprise us. Recognizing the futility of his position on Endralliande, so deeply vulnerable to Cannorian blockade, Bramash has marshalled the last of his forces and raided a naval supply depot, seizing a small merchant fleet. Their destination is mainland Soruin, where they might lick their wounds and disappear into the endless jungle.
