From thin air and with a brilliant flash of light, the leader of their ruler's Imperial War Wizard detachment appeared before their lord and their advisors, flanked by her subordinates and several armed men, some of whom the assembled dignitaries of their ruler recognized as their own servants, once assumed to be completely loyal to their ruler. "The Imperial Dynasty is ascendant. By the Emperor's will, the entire realm shall be truly united. Your time as an independent country is no more. Swear allegiance to the Emperor, and serve Anbennar."
