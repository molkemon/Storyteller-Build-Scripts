As our census takers have begun to map more and more of the sprawling city, they have found something that has shocked the entire court: An entire community of elves living in clusters within the expansive slums surrounding the city’s heart. Initially we were overjoyed, until our officials began to speak of their condition. Poverty, persecution, and disease run rife among them, even worse than in the rest of the slums. They are frequently extorted, threatened and even outright murdered. This situation is obviously unacceptable, and we have already dispatched soldiers to begin uplifting these sunrise elves out of their squalor and recruiting them into our own administration.
