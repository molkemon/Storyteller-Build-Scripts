Lothane Bluetusk, who fought alongside Corin against the Dookanson, is a controversial figure in orkish society, considered by some to be a traitor to the race. However to many halforks he is seen as the first prominent defender of their cause. He may have failed to protect his halfork group against Karagh Bloodfist, but he managed to save thousands of other orks and halforks by promoting integration and tolerance of (half)orcs in Corintar society.
