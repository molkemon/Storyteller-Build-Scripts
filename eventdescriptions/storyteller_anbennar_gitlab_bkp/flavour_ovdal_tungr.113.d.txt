Despite our considerable contributions to the fledgling republic's cause, we have been met with disdain and betrayal. It is abundantly clear that our trust in these socalled allies was sorely misplaced. They sought our aid to eliminate immediate threats, only to turn their backs on us in pursuit of personal gain. We shall not idly stand by as our sacrifices are disregarded and our honour besmirched. It is time to assert our rightful claim and demonstrate the might of our people.
