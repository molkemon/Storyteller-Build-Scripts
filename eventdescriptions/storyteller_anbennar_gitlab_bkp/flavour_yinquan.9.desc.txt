The swish of a tail, followed by gentle whispers. An agent nearby, listening in. These things and many more distinguish our influence over the realm. With the help of a friendly spirit, it has been particularly easy to affect all manner of trickery against those we wish. By this stage, those in the know report that the entire shadow council behind the heir has been bribed, blackmailed, seduced, or replaced—anything proposed to them would probably be accepted, should we ask through the proper channels. Of course, that means only one thing...
