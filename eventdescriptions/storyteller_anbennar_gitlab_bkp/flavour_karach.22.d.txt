"Uryzmag Stonescale emerged from the mineshaft, and as he did he passed by a mural of a miner ten miners tall, whose necklace was painted with all the colors of the rainbow.  The miners were on their way to the workshops, and today was a special day: the excavation teams had found a vein of gold. The Axta would sell most on the market, of course, but some of the richest and most lustrous had been given to the miners’ workshops.  Uryzmag almost ran to his seat. The miners had threaded together the string chains of their necklaces the week before, and now it was time to make the amulets. Lovers’ Day was soon approaching, and the craft he made could not be anything less than perfect.  He reflected on Achagi, and the way she had of knowing the names of all the trees in the jungle on their walks together. He would craft her an amulet of trees, he decided. He would line the outside of wood, and on the inside he would fill her necklace with leaves of gold. The time flew by as he added small details to the design. They wouldn’t mean anything to someone else, but the amulet was for Achagi, and it didn’t need to mean anything to anyone but her. He managed to sketch out the design before workshop adjourned for the day.  He went out into the evening, and as he left the mines did not follow him home. He couldn’t wait to give Achagi the amulet. Lovers’ Day couldn’t arrive quickly enough."
