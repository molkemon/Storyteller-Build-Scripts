Our elven friends in Jaddari have approached us with an offer. Their ambassador has proposed a new arrangement between our nations. He talks of a grant of land, guaranteed selfrule and most importantly, freedom from the stigma of 'monsters' that the world has seen us as. In return they ask for greater integration of our alliance. It is a good offer, but there are those who whisper that it's really just a way for them to subjugate us.
