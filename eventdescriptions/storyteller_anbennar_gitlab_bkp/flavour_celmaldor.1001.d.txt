"What do you mean, 'the ship got back a week ahead of time'? I need to go and see this," the ruler says. The advisors chuckle and go to the port following their liege. Outside is a ship with windshaping magic crackling in its sails. The ship was supposed to have gotten back a week from now, but thanks to the latest advancement in magical technology, it has been able to travel back quicker and safer than was previously possible. The ruler asks to be able to ride on the ship. The captain graciously obliges; to their shock, the ship travels to its own home port in half the usual time in a pleasant and fast journey.
