"Esteemed King of Yassa,  "On your behalf, I have thus far searched the lands of my people for information about your ancestors' 330th Empire, and I am pleased to report that we have made some progress. In my examinations of the Spire, I discovered that a community of Merfolk had made a home of submerged portions of the structure. While the upper levels had been of no trouble to navigate, those lower levels were hereto locked away from us."  "Now, however, the Merfolk have proven amicable to acting as our eyes in the lower levels, and have already begun reporting back what they've managed to find. While we have yet to learn anything incredibly substantive, there are signs that point to something more in the city of Nazhni. Perhaps there lies the full story of the 330th Empire which you seek."  The letter goes on, but what you've already read has you grinning. Perhaps the legacy of the 330th Empire was finally within your reach.
