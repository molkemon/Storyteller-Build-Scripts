The conversation at the feast tables of our capital often follows similar patterns: there is always the discussion of our ruler’s latest hunt, a little debate about exactly which of the zodiac signs best predisposes someone to be a knight, arguments over whether a historical conflict could have gone another way. But this night’s feast featured an exception: a rude interruption by one of our Cannorian Iosahar. Laughing as we discussed lancelthrust against spearwall, they claimed a handful of musketarmed men would have been enough to defeat any of the legendary armies of the Ynn. It is outrageous, and the stir it has caused has certainly caused further escalation in our ongoing religious debate.
