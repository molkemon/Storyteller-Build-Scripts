There is resentment across the farmlands of the realm; The common people of the land are beginning to see the High Philosophy urbanite merchants as greedy, selfcentered and undeserving of the wealth they have doubtlessly lied and cheated their way into possession of. It can only be a few years at most until they take matters into their own hands and form mobs to attack the traders they have come to hate once more. Many of them are still armed from the last time they attacked the urbanites, so all it will take is enough provocation to make the common people take up arms once more.  This is not riskfree, of course. While unlikely, these same common folk might rebel against us for harboring these merchants, not understanding the deeper designs at play. In a few years; as few as five; they will assuredly harbor so much hatred for these merchants as to finally attack them, allowing us to kill two birds with one stone.
