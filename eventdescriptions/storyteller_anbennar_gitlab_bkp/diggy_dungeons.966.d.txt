Every last one of us is shaken, bruised, and battered to the core, but when we emerge from the webstrewn depths we emerge victorious, the spider tsunami shattered in our wake. Our expedition is home at last, and by their bravery we have opened a path through the web of defenses the great broodmother has woven around herself. As we count our dead and tally up our loot, one thought remains above all others in the minds of our expeditionaries: the way is clear to Yidab's Nest.
