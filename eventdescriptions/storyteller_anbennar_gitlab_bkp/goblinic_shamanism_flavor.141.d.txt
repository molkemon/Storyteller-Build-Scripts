The great shamancooks of the Emptiness have produced a rather striking result. As goblins gorge themselves upon more and more food they grow substantially. Soon a new class of goblins emerged, with those most fattened becoming objects of worship themselves. As more and more goblins find nourishment and corresponding mass, it is abundantly clear they represent a resource that can be exploited by our ruler. Let's just hope they don't turn on each other in their hunger.
