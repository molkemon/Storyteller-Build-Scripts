Gathered around a table once more were the vanquished of the Fourth Lilac War in Esmaria. Surprisingly, the mood was vibrant; everyone socialized and boasted of their heroic deeds during the conflict. Contrary to the Count's presumption, the council did not require any investment to be constructive—the very fact that all members of the Moon Party were assembled, laughing off the war over a glass of wine, was victory enough. The ruler could now simply approach different guests and discuss his own agenda.
