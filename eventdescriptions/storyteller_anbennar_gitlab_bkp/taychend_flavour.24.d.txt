This morning, the head of a very large cat was brought before our lord, its fur pure white, and its eyes a piercing damestear-blue. The Dey Vettaga of the province of tf_dey_vettaga_prov. The hunt had been long and arduous, and the casualties... our lord did not want to think about it. This single beast had claimed hundreds of soldiers, and driven ten times the number it had killed to desert, as elves fled in terror rather than wait to be hunted down and consumed. Thankfully, one of our fallen soldiers managed to nick the beast before it killed him, and after his comrades discovered his corpse, they were able to follow the drip-trail of blood to the beast's lair. Smoking it out by setting fires, they finally cornered and butchered it. The nightmare is over, and the province of tf_dey_vettaga_prov can hopefully get some peace once more.
