First came Olokhar, and while he possessed many qualities, his flaw was so great that it caused his own empire to collapse. Second came Aldatam, the Reclaimer, whose rise was slower but had stronger foundations. However, these foundations betrayed his heirs, as each Great-Banner of the Ironblood Army fought for its own pretender, following its own Dannasan power games, instead of serving the state. Third came Harrihara, who defeated every enemy that stood before him, regardless of whether they had the power of a prophecy or the skill to defy fate. Third came Harrihara, who reforged the Ironbloods anew, correcting the mistakes of the past. Third came Harrihara, and there will not be a fourth.
