For decades, Unguldavor has been the benefactor of Barumand. The only reason Barumand survived was due to Unguldavor's willingness to defend it and help their ork brothers and sisters. In everything but name, Barumand was nothing more than Unguldavor's vassal. On a current date, their lord brought Barumand's leader to the capital to iron out the details of the arrangement. "It has been a pleasure having you by my side," our lord said, "but it is clear that the arrangement was onesided. Together, we could give orks a new homeland here in Escann. But in order to do that, I need your loyalty. We need to work together: Unguldavor, Barumand, and Khozrugan. Will you accept our protection?" They nodded and shook our lord's hand. "I accept. For our people, we must unite."
