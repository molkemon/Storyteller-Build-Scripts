As you may know, our company began with Zerynar and Magnus Hammerbeard. Though from two different walks of life, and indeed two different races, Zerynar and Magnus had met in ReUyel due to a confrontation in a bar both had frequented. Though the two were on opposite sides of that dispute, each saw something in the other, and after Zerynar offered to buy Magnus a drink in the aftermath of the altercation, the two became fast friends.  Magnus already had made forays into the shipbuilding business using classic Copper Dwarf construction methods, while Zerynar had dabbled in goblinstyle artificery, but it was only when the two combined their talents that they saw widespread success. This remarkable partnership has seen reliable Dwarven engineering combine with Goblin innovation to produce truly remarkable developments in shipbuilding.  The Hammered Shark Company has enjoyed many clients in its years of operation, and it now wishes to extend that partnership to the Overclan itself. Aside from offering compelling deals on our various products and services, we are also able to provide a never before seen invention which should prove to have a marvelous military utility. It is presently unavailable to the general public, and we offer it exclusively for use of the Overclan military.
