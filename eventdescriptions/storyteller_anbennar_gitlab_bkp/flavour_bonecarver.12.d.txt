"The Eye is mine, Gahahahah." Thral growled in victory, accidentally stumbling into the chamber where the sought after treasure was sealed, unknowingly alerting his entire party to his discovery.  "O’ Clouded Eye, show me how I can m’self find buncha gold." Thral asked the crystal as he held it tight, saliva dripping from his mouth.  The crystal lit up, showing him a treasure chest hidden beneath a pile of bones. Before Thral could register the location, he was met with a punch straight to his jaw.  "Gimme that, ya git." Voltga snatched the crystal from his hands, infuriated yet hungry for its power. "O’ Clouded Eye, show me how I can get a clan of ma" Before she could finish her sentence, another ork kicked her liver. "Dat’s for me." Zugor shouted in anger and snatched the glimmering artifact.  A brawl ensued, each ork fighting one another just for a chance to be blessed by the eye of Dookan, yet none quite getting their wish fulfilled. The crystal grew brighter and brighter, taking on a noticeable red hue. Suddenly it ruptured with a blinding flash and deafening boom that put all the orks to a halt.  When their senses returned, the orks found the pieces of the Clouded Eye scattered on the ground. They were left with nothing besides shattered glass, bruises, and broken bones. Before they could fully grieve their loss, they noticed a clear image imprinted on the walls, a map of Castanor, with a location distinctly marked in shimmering green. Could this be where the corpse of the Dookanson rests?
