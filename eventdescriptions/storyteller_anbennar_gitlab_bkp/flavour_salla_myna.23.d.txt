Bricena summoned the other Ceanna to Ciranmyna, and while Arfil entertained them, Bricena arrived fashionably late—on her great hawk, of course. Some of the Ceanna were visibly impressed by her status as a hawk rider, while others tried to hide their admiration, but not well enough to fool Bricena. She began to speak, admitting that she would never be a great general like her father. But then she said that they did not need that. What they needed was someone who could bind them together, help overcome their differences, and lead them into a bright new future, rather than leading them into war from the front.
