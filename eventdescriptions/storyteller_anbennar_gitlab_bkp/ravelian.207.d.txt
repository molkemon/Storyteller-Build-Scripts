The Ravelian Society and the skilled craftspeople have, for a long time, grown hand-in-hand, with many early craftspeople belonging to the society itself. However, in recent years since the society got their hands on the God Fragment, a slow and steady schism seems to have appeared within the ranks of the craftspeople in our realm. Those of Ravelian leaning state that the craft has for too long gone unchecked, and that we must treat it as if it was holy work, and explore this new craft ethically just as the Magisterium did with magic. The traditional craftspeople simply balk at this backwards thinking. The Ravelians, clearly outnumbered, have asked for our help, in exchange for their loyalty.
