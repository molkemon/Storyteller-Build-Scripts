Our lord can feel it: war has come to our realm. In the past, all-out conflict was rare among the Devandi, but times are changing. Thus, our lord has taken to rousing the army of our realm with a terrific speech, reminding them of the great gift of freedom given to them by the liberator Munakles and their duty to preserve it in the face of the oppressors. Afterwards, our lord can notice a small but substantial difference in the behavior of the columns: the soldiers stand straighter. Conversation has ceased, and every soldier has a determined gleam in their eye.
