With a new leader now prepared to lead Grombar, the regency of Ironsea has, in theory, come to an end. In practice, however, they hold relatively little political power, and Ironsea is still popular amongst the common people, if not as liked as they once were by the nobility. Though it may well cause some instability, should they wish, Ironsea could easily cast aside the title of regent, and claim the title of Gray King that they rejected all those years ago.
