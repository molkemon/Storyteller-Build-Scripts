Our lord is found dead outside the Red Citadel, and the news spread too fast to be covered by the eunuchs. Could it be? The ruler of Luzhong dead on the streets? Was he killed? Is the realm poisoned from the inside? Did he kill himself? Could a ruler ever do that? Quickly, it became clear that the imperial family was simply not worthy of the empire their predecessors built. The curtain of lies slowly started to crumble as the news spread further, reaching beyond Emgolu. It was clear; blood was about to be spilled. Just like it once was hundreds of years ago, the Beiklings will fight each other, but this time they have one enemy—the Xion Dynasty.
