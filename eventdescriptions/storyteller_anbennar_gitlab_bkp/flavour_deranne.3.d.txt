Light streamed through the tall, ornamented glass windows at the side of the throne room, faintly illuminating the grand pillars spread throughout the vast chamber. There was a sense of abandonment about the place. In a way this was true, as it was unlikely it would be used for the same purpose ever again; but it could earn another. Walking down the central pathway to the throne itself, our ruler gazed at the massive map of Lencenor placed atop a larger table in their way, surrounded by clerks, servants and generals in discussion. Stopping short, the ruler looked upon the very north of the map, at the Small Country and the elven sanctuary of the Redglades. Many Lorentish loyalists had fled their stronghold in the north, protected by loyal subjects and the Elfrealm. Perhaps accommodation could be sought with the elves, the offer of a stronger protector. Yet as with the Ruby Dwarves, they would not hesitate to bring them to heel through force. Moving further down the map, they fixed their eyes upon the southern winelords. A pragmatic folk of rich lands, having built up immense wealth through trade with the Anbennarians. They had fought with the Moon, but were unlikely to submit to Derannic rule without conflict. To the very south lay the duchy Sornost, dogs of the Siloriels. They would fall last. Finally, our ruler walked up to the throne and turned upon the dais, looking across the map ahead, visible in full now as torches were lit around the room. Lencenor would know war.
