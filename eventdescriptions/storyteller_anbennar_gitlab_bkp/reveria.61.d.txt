The destruction of our capital and Anbennar wasn't just about burning down buildings, stealing what was valuable, and leaving. It wasn't about destruction for profit; it was about complete and total annihilation. The stones of houses and roads were removed; no building stood, no markers, statues, or signifiers remained. The cities weren't simply demolished; they were ended. The population was enslaved, killed, or fled, leaving nothing but charred ground filled with ashes. That was a sign to the entirety of Cannor and Halann: a new age had begun. May the gods have mercy on us all.
