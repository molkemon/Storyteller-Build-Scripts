Ever since the ruler appeared at the esteemed court, they have been a mystery to our own elite class. Nobody knows their name nor has been able to find information about this secretive ruler. As time went on, the ruler became friends with the lord. Their skills in the matters of the administration, diplomacy and the military are well received in the court. Their piety is an inspiration for those who follow their word and their zeal for justice is only matched by their belief in their faith. They show great potential to be the next lord.
