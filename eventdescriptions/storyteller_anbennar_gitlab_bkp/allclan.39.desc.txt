It started as a strange blip on our BOMBNAR defense systems. We've gotten similar anomalies before, mostly attributed to what appear to be mysteriously dense clouds. As it turns out, this wasn't one of them. 3,000 tons of scrap metal and extraordinarily destructive liquids was hurtling towards ErNatvir at the speed of gravity. It would appear as though our gobbonauts have made their return far faster than originally anticipated. Whilst the impact will be catastrophic, think of all the things we've learned.
