My lord, good news from the realm of Yenaze Mezhi. This year's harvest has come in, and our reports show a substantial surplus. Our lush fields are filled with healthy grain, bolstered by the Samayebaka's prayers. The people are well-fed and happy, as our granaries can provide a considerable amount of grain as a stipend to the poor and needy—and to our military, should they go off on campaign. This has allowed our populace to thrive, and many predict we will see a moderate increase in the population should this continue.
