The rowdy vampires in the capital have openly challenged the priesthood. They’ve gone into temples, bragging about how the forces that the priests worship do nothing to stop their incursions. Priests have been bled dry and holy places were desecrated in despicable night orgies. The shamed clergy who were able to flee return to their temples to find veritable horror scenes. Forced to dump corpses and clean blood splatters after the vampire incursion while being completely unable to seek revenge in the short term.
