They laughed and called us mad. Insane for even considering attempting to rebuild in the most ravaged and cursed area of the Serpentspine, where the dragon's wrath left its marks, and dark magic still lingers like a festering wound. They warned us that the effort required to rebuild would be tremendous, if it was even possible at all. They feared that the dragonscarred tunnels beneath our hold would collapse, crushing our buildings and burying our settlers alive. They whispered of monstrous creatures emerging from the Wound, ready to hunt down anyone who dared to return. And they trembled at the thought of the ancient magic seeping from the ruined Vaults into our walls.  And they were right in all of this, but they were also wrong. We did not choose this fate – we have simply returned. Home.
