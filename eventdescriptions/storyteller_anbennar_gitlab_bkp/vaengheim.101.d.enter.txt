The words of Cassan were not forgotten, and it was in the days of today that our ruler made war upon their enemy, who named themselves Equilibrists, and who did strike at them. And they took to the field in robes of white, yet did beneath wear black mail of black iron, and they did wield weapons with skill, plucking wingmaidens from the sky with fire and arquebus. Battle raged thusly for long days, and bonds of friendship were rekindled between reachman and gerudian. For many fighting folk of Olavlund and Dalr did serve under the wings of our ruler, and to the reachman they came not as foemen, but as saviors, for the Equilibrists were bitter foes, and would salt the earth ere their foes could claim it. Thus many of their kin were brought to Vængheim's banner, for their hearts grew fond of our ruler as they saw the cruel ways of their kin. Thus did the Equilibrists falter with time, for the reachmen knew their ways and their traps, and inch by inch, day by day, did the Equilibrists lose their grip on the Reach. It was in these days that Godric and Cassan, who had found one another, came to the hall of our ruler once more, and they did say, "O queen, together have we spoken, and we have walked our lands, and felt ash rain upon our faces, and many tears have we wept. But the Equilibrists know us not, and we have spoken with them, and we have heard their plans. They gather now in Aldtempel, where all gods bear witness, and they seek to strike at you with all the might they have left." And our ruler did rise, and they gathered reachman, Dalr, Olavlunder, and wingmaidens all, for war.
