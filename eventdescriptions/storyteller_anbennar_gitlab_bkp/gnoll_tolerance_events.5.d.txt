Gnolls, an enterprising race without compare in scavenging and crafting, have recently proven extremely beneficial for the economy of our nation. Their involvement in a certain sector of the local economy has resulted in an increase in goods production and an uptick in local economic strength. The opinion of the nongnoll populace is divided. Some are, of course, hostile to those they deem foreign and demand the lord intervene. But others rather like the increase in profits the gnolls have helped share around.
