With trepidation, we have ventured deep into the dark heart of Tswohvwoh'iii. A powerful resonance greets us; eerie melodies spawned from our Mistress, woven into the jungle like threads that tie its tapestry together. The sounds of our environs, once a cacophony of wildlife, became uniform with purpose. The purpose was clear, the motive force behind every vine and blade of grass; it sought to control, to take as its own, to take us into a choking embrace anathema to personhood. The seedthralls, the puppets who were once our former kin, were especially relentless, as the songs of Tswohvwoh'iii grew ever louder and sank deeper into our souls. It hurt so much; so many of our number could not endure. Even the most stalwart, unshakeable minds ached the moment our ears were greeted by the haunting tune.  A creeping dizziness set in, followed swiftly by excruciating headaches that seemed to gnaw at our very will to go on. It was as though Tswohvwoh'ii sought to overwhelm our very existence, transforming us into mere vessels of its dark desires.  We dared not linger here, making haste to move through the hazardous terrain and cursing the very idea that the slaves to the jungle following closely behind could ever be placated. The urgency to flee her heart was undeniable, and we rushed from this accursed place, our steps punctuated by the discordant cries of the seedthralls that broke the ghastly rhytm. It was abundantly clear that we were not welcome within this abode, every waking moment evidence that our presence was a transgression against the ancient power that reigned here. What is to be done, then, for our people to endure?
