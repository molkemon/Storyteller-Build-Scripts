Depending on time, circumstance and who you ask, the fey who reside within our domain are subject to it and its lord. An easy affirmation of loyalty can be acquired when the Seelie or Unseelie are up to their antics. The same can be said when our relations with various fey communities are healthy. Unfortunately, with the Courts quiet at the moment and our currently tenuous relationship, the fey of a nearby community have decided to go about wholesale ignoring us or our wishes. Letting them go about their business will cause governance there to be more challenging, but will give them a chance to realize their lot will be better with us than without. Especially once the Courts take notice.
