The yearly debate at Gulenhyl was a disaster. All the clan leaders and the monarch were there to debate about politics and magic principles. All was going well until their lord's turn to speak. Their lord made some valid points, only for them to fall into a trap. The current chief of Clouded Eye along with the shamans and scholars tore about their lord's argument and humiliated them. Their lord was now a laughingstock across Escann as papers circulated about the poor argument.
