As important as the clergy are in our society, it's only natural that they've acquired no small amount of wealth. This has often made them the target of unscrupulous robbers, but recently someone has robbed the clergy with no apparent desire for personal gain. Feeling that the clergy hasn't been doing enough to support our great telescope, they have stolen no small amount of funds from a local temple and have donated it themselves. More funds for the telescope are always good, but the clergy wants their money returned.
