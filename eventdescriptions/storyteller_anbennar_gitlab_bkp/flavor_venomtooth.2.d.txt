Orcs from across the lands held by clan Venomtooth gathered in Venomfen. Warriors, laborers, and children all stood around the massive pyre in the village center. This time, the pyre was not burning a body, but an effigy. A stray replica of Korgus burned in place of the body; not out of hatred, but out of respect. As shamans chanted funerary songs to guide Korgus’s soul towards reincarnation, Chieftain Aadga and her youngest son, Azukor, walked in front of the pyre.  Aadga looked at the crowd and announced, "We have gathered here today to remember the greatest ork of our time, Korgus Dookanson. For thousands of years, we have fought each other in the caverns, blind to the outside world and oblivious to our own potential. Only Korgus could rally all of us together to lead us into this bountiful land we have won for ourselves and our descendants. He showed us how our strength could overcome any challenges and how working as a unified whole makes us far stronger than we could ever be when fighting alone.  "While other clans have forgotten these lessons, we will not. We must march on our enemies surrounding us and show them what true strength is. We will not fall, we cannot fall. Are you ready to set the world ablaze once more?" The crowd roared in approval. Fists were raised in the air as the flames burnt higher.  Azukor looked up at his mother with awe. For her part, Aadga patted Azukor’s head. "Do not worry, son. I have great plans for you."
