Good news! We’ve managed to infiltrate the administration of one of our rivals in order to redirect a portion of the funds they’ve provided to the Godswatch. While the money has still reached the same destination, our agents have modified some paperwork in order to funnel it through our private estates in such a way that it now looks like the donation is ours. This won’t affect the observatory’s actual funding, but it will give us much more sway when it comes to choosing its location. Things couldn’t have gone better!
