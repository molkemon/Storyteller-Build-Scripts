Despite our expedition leader’s natural charm and charisma the negotiations soon fell short as a fight between a scout and a bugbear broke out. What started as a standard disagreement soon escalated into a shoutout. Just before they had started fighting, we intervened and put an end to this. From then on the negotiations were cold and the bugbear chief reluctant. To no one's surprise he said now, but still left us with some meagre supplies as a sign of good faith before continuing onwards.
