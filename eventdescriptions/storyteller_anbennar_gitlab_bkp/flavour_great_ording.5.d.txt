With the Small Country freed from its oppressors, and its middlelevel aristocracy curtailed, there is a chance like no other to change the way it is fundamentally organised. The tributes demanded of the halflings were harsh and unfair, often taxing them twice in a row or more. There is an opportunity to perform a great deception.  With only a little administrative work, the taxes administered to halflings could be raised to a level comparable to before the liberation, yet it would not look this way if the process was streamlined.
