We had heard before that there was a war where the dwarves of Khugdihr fought alongside the humans of Castanor against the giants. It had ever been a distant thing, however, and we had not heard about the great tales that were forged in that conflict. Greatest among these by far are the tales of Murri Giantbreaker, who is said to have killed dozens of giants by his own blade. After his passing, he was given a place on the shrines as a new Great Ancestor.
