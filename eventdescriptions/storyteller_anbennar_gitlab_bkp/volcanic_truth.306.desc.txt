In ordinary times, the clergy of the Volcanic Truth is focused on changing the world within our borders with easily envisionable ends in mind. However, we find ourselves in extraordinary times, and we have already changed much of the world within our borders. As we change and change again, it has occurred to the magmascryers that we also hold overlordship over countries beyond ours, and that allowing them to go free would both change our relationship with them and free them to change themselves in swift and unpredictable ways.  This would be a change to our detriment, but all that matters right now is that we change, not whether the results of this change are beneficial.
