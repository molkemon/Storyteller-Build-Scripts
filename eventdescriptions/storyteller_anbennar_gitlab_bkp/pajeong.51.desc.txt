The traders of Yanshen, in search of endless profits, have been quick to take advantage of our current fractious state. The chaos engulfing our realm after the death of Sage King Jeong-Hun Gyo has been less than ideal for creating and maintaining stable economic partnerships. Even worse, the famed Treasure Fleet is expected to arrive soon, putting even more pressure on the court to improve our standing among the merchants of Haless. If we do not do something soon to secure our trade relations, we will be left in the dust.

A clan of merchants from Yanshen has suggested we grant them a monopoly on the goods from their province, in return they offer to open new inroads for our traders. Alternatively, we have the option to either allow the traders of Yanshen to freely operate within our domain, or simply acquire new income sources for the treasury, making us less dependent on trade.
