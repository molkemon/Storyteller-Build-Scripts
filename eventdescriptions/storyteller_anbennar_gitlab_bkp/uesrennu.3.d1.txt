Another week, another batch of slaves dragged to the mine. Tales of the mines had by now spread to all Taychend, so all these people already had an idea of what awaited them. They were dead elves walking, and their eyes, their body language fully looked the part.  "The new batch of slaves arrived? Finally." The warden’s stream of complaints didn't help. "The last big group went and got themselves killed in the tunnel collapse… Useless idiots, no matter how many times you tell them how to mine they don't learn. And I'll be the one paying the consequences of that. The higher ups are always asking for more gems and metals, but they don't put any effort in procuring the arms to do it.  "Send these down, and send for more. What? Don't you know that the needs of the mine are paramount?" The darkness engulfed yet another group of elves.
