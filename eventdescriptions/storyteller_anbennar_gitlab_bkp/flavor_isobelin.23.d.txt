Ashen skies. Foreboding news has arrived from a distant land: the ruler, revealed to be a dreaded WitchKing, an evil sorcerer of such power as to rival Nichmer of old. Their rise to power threatens to spread darkness across the land and beyond their borders, and their subjects live in terror of their magical tyranny. Droves of refugees from the capital and other cities have fled overseas, hoping to find some safety. We welcome all newcomers, but some in the city fear that the WitchKing’s agents may be secreted among them, sent to spread their wicked influence.
