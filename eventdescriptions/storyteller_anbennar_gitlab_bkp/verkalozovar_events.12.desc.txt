Utter silence overwhelms the empty cavern as our lord ruminates in meditation, an assortment of incense and dwarven brews scattered across the floor, inviting anyone to break the silence if they were to observe such an array. Days passed. Then months. Sustained by an odd combination of chi and beer, our lord was scarcely lucid when a ghastly chill crawled down their spine. Standing before the mountain king was a semi-translucent dwarf, donning a crown of pure cut nephrite and brandishing an embroidered cape. The Oz-Amekh sat upon the ground, took one sip of the brew offered, and uttered a single question.

"You wanted to see me?"
