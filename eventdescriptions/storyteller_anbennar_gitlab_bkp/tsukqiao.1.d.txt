Fuyuan Jiao was a woman like no other. She was charismatic, strong-willed, and intelligent. A great leader, especially in contrast to her husband, who had a weak and fragile appearance in comparison. In a better world, this would be her story, one where she rose to overthrow the Jade Citadel and free the Beiklings from oppression, but this is not that world. After fighting for many weeks with an illness, this morning she passed away in the royal tent. Youji knows that no matter what, the one thing he can do for his beloved Fuyuan is to see her dream achieved, no matter the cost.
