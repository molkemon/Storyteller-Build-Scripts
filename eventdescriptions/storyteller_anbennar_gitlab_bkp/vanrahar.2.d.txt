The Ascension Peninsula, the "cradle of Harimar", has been a land stuck in time. Many amongst them still don’t use gunpowder, primarily hunting for their food instead of farming.  Now, that has changed. A power is rising in the south, and it comes to challenge the very foundation of the Harimraj. They’ve proclaimed their ambition to forge a new state the way that Harimar didby the sword of conquest.
