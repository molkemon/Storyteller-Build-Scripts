The Vustarin and Kattyva seem like natural allies, with the former primarily working with precursor scrap, while the latter focuses on digging it out of the dirt. However, their interests still clash more often than not, leading to much tension between the two families—such as at the Steel-Scorched Citadel. Here, the Vustarin still maintain an interest in direct scavenging operations. Recently, disagreements here have erupted into an outburst of violence, as a dispute over who had the right to excavate a certain section of the ruin escalated, leaving several dead. Now the two families beg the Mercenary King for intervention.
