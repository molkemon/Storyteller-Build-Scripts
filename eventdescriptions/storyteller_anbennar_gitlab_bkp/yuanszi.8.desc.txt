It began with a scream. A servant lady sees the leader of one of the Xiaken having its throat slit by a shadowy figure in front of her very eyes. A stable boy sees the commander of the warrior monks killed through a spine snapped. Everywhere, all at once the dark Wulin struck, getting rid of the last vestiges of the old. Each and every ruler which had not yet fully bowed down or to the devouring path was on the list. A slaughter ensued as all who opposed the new order were stamped out in unison. Their spines snapped, their necks twisted and their throats carved up. Blood spilling everywhere, tears flowing freely. In the aftermath, their ruler appoints the new leaders of the Xiaken, loyalists of course. These new rulers sing the praises of the Devouring Path, worship the Wulin as the master surgeon and suck up to the new order like a whimpering child wanting sweets, so pathetic, yet so useful.
