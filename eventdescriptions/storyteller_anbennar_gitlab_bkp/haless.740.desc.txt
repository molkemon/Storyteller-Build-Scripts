Today, while our scholars were poring over dusty volumes within the old library of Lizicheng ruins, a sudden spirit wind blew through the hall. From among the tomes, it carried out a long-forgotten map depicting a hidden passage beneath the Yuanfen. Following the route, our expedition uncovered a sealed chamber housing ancient texts left behind by the mages of old Zizhou. These scrolls contain insights into the movement of chi and the structure of spirit forms—knowledge long thought lost.

Clerics and all concerned with the magical arts are eager to lay hands on these texts. But how should we preserve and use these rediscovered relics of thought?
