Shortly before the great incursion, a more localized conflict engulfed the city that would later become the center of the federation. Over the course of the conflict, Vahjevgiiv invaded and tried to annex our entire city. Facing backlash from the surrounding city states, they just subjugated the western half of our city. Once the greatest city in all of Yarikhoi, a wall now separates the halves of our city and even the merchants of the supposedly free half suffer under a blockade.
