Passerby gaw, enraptured by what seems like a lucid dream beneath the waves. During daylight and twilight, lights dimly hum like a sleeping city below the shore of our realm, but at night, they blaze bright as the very stars above. Surveys from the ocean surface revealed little as the radiance lurked deep in the littoral zone, so we outfitted a diving squad to investigate. According to their report, nearby merfolk scattered over the shelf at the squad's approach. Examining the sea scions' work, our divers discovered broken glass bottles, mirror shards, and lustrous metals tied with kelp rope and arrayed in tiered fences. Anchoring them, stone rune posts hummed with magic, empowering the trinkets to shine as bright as a lighthouse beacon. We're not certain why the merfolk erected this strange display, though our ruler's administrators have theories ranging from wicked arcane siege weapons to flashy demarcations. As tourists flock to observe the stars beneath the tide, what should our realm do about these lights?
