North of Ryalanar, along the shoreline, there lies Greycliff, a small cliff face backed by a solitary grove surrounded by trees. Therein lies a field of stones, an excellent spot to sit and ponder the universe. So thought the lord six months ago. He had not forgotten the moment when he lost his goddess' gift to the passage of time. So he sat against the gentle midnight breeze coming through the forest one more night, wondering the same thoughts Adelar the Wise used to think, one gentle breeze after another.
