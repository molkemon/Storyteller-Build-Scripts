City Razer and Bloodbringer. Two of the main instigators of the nightmare that was the Great Incursion. Glory seeking mares, with an affinity for causing suffering and stealing shiny things. And yet… there is hope for them. They are watching our unification of Nuzurbokh with open eyes, infatuated with our strength. It wasn’t too hard to send a few of our shamans there to "convince" them. And now? Look at them. Reformed. Devout. Rebellious. The Great Storm will soon turn into a whirlwind. The Filustkhuga will be swept aside.
