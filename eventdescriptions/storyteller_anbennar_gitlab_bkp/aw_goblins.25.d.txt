Tribal feuds are the bane of a ruler’s existence. Though no society lacks them, they are a constant thorn in their side, distracting lieutenants and tying up resources. Thankfully, it appears we are not the only ones who suffer such. The goblin skulkers who infest this land seem to have broken into similar factions, one of whom was forced out of the nest – to be easily crushed at the hands of our hastily assembled warparty.
