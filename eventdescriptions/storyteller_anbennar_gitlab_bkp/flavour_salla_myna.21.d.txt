Arfil tells Bricena they need to be in Ceanna Cenág urgently, then the two hop on hawk riders and ride off, with some guards following. When they arrive, Ceanna Cenág acts confused, asking how they knew he had requested their meeting. Surely, his letter couldn't have arrived more than a few days ago, but they are already here! In other words, reserving hawks for diplomats would be a major diplomatic boon.
