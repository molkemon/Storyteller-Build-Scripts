"A figurehead; a symbol that will inspire our people to action."  Farwen mulled her mother's words, as she peered out across the army camp outside of Elynaile. She watched the glaivewielders training, sparring and otherwise lingering about the camp, observing the camaraderie between them. It was something to aspire to, a bond that transcended mere kinship or friendship, forged in shared experiences.  Her thoughts turned as she watched, an idea rising within the back of her mind. She didn't need to partake in discussions at the Conclave, if they didn't wish to permit her; yet they wouldn't be able to stop her from assisting via other avenues of approach. She could join her people down below; train with them, go into battle with them, lead them as both their comradeinarms and their general.  Yet, that would prove immensely dangerous; she'd be likely to lose her life if things went wrong. Would it be best to serve her people on the front? Or perhaps she should leverage her position as the future Ceanna to ensure the armies are wellsupplied and properly housed. One way or another, she'd make her mark; gain the favor of her people and let them know, regardless of where she was, that she would do whatever she could for their cause.  She made her way down to the encampment, to join in the training; get to know her people. She wasn’t yet sure what she would do – lead as their general or simply work amongst their ranks; they would know that she was by their side.
