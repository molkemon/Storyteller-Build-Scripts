So let us begin. Imagine this situation: Your kingdom is currently experiencing a poor harvest, and there is discontent in your court, rumours even of a conspiracy to put a rival noble house on the throne. Also, your merchants have been complaining that a neighboring kingdom is competing in the markets against you and winning. In the midst of this storm of bad news, you have one bright light: your child is soon coming of age and is ready to marry. Who would you marry them with? What do you see as a bigger problem at this moment?
