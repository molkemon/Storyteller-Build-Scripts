"How are the White Flowers faring in our realm of Aelantir?" I asked Leli as I sat with her one night, Felana gently humming a soothing song beside us, sitting beneath the room's soft light. Leli looked up from her ledger; her long hours spent working showing on her face. As I gently took the book from her hands and set it aside, she smiled at me with her usual smile, filled with divine grace. "They are doing so much better than I ever could have hoped, Kyola! I'm currently recruiting Mihipha to travel to the colony swiftly. Once they set up the White Flower Farms there, we'll be producing more Ixora in Aelantir than here; making history!" I smiled, the shine in her eyes making my heart soar. Giving her a soft kiss, I picked her up in my arms with ease. "That is exquisite news, my dear. Good enough that you deserve a reward." I set her down on our bed and tucked her in. "Get some rest, Leli, please..."
