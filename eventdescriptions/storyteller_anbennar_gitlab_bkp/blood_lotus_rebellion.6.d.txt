The lord is discussing taxes with his ministers when the doors of the grand hall burst open and through steps one of the largest harimari ever seen. He introduces himself as one Salar BlackClaw. We have heard rumors of him before. He is supposed to be a great warrior from the southern Jungle. Apparently he was born in the same house as Harimar the Great. While his martial prowess is famous, so is his disdain towards humans. It seems that he offers his service in that context, to teach people their place.
