The prisoner recoiled from the light as the prison door opened. When he saw the ruler enter—hands hidden behind his back, cloak shrouded, mouth turned to a sinister grin—Sananth trembled with fear. He mustered the last courage he had. "I'll never be turned into one of you! I'm ashamed to—to share the name! Turning your back on the gods for immortality, skulking around in shadows—the Patriarch acting like an interloper's plaything!" 

Thelriatam halted, expression empty in an instant, and Sananth's courage evaporated. But the Patriarch's smirk returned, slowly. He crouched before the prisoner. "The end of the Cycle of Glory is close at hand—unstoppable. You will be left behind... but so be it." His smirk turned to a grin once more. "There are many more Rakulacan than you. Generations, stacked row on row, shackled by the authority of MY blood! Mihaela's plaything... hah! She sits in the realm playing around with her trophies, playing at war, at ruling, at being an empress. I love her, but it is true—I like it. Only one above me, and still... I RULE."

Thelriatam stood, turned on his heel, paused. Then a whispered secret. "There are two, actually. My sire pretends she has none. I know she is lying. I know the Masquerade exists for a reason. The other Errant Princes... it will only take seven thousand years or so of preparation to destroy them, no more. But the First Sire? More difficult. She holds the secret to our power, see. The cord. Cut it, and we fall."

Thelriatam chuckled. "A danger... and an opportunity. My calculations say fifteen thousand years at most, until MY technology, MY artificery, MY power—alone, AUTONOMOUS—can capture her. And then...?" Thelriatam paused for a moment—until a rumbling, roiling, maniacal laugh broke through the stillness. "I WILL HOLD HOSTAGE EVERY VAMPIRE IN THE WORLD! UNCHALLENGEABLE, UNIMPEACHABLE! PUPPETMASTER OF THE UNIVERSE! MWAHAHA– MWUHAHAHAHAHA–!"

Thelriatam twirled violently, with the quick-sudden swish and flash of silver-steel, stabbing the air to his side, cutting the laughter at once. Eyes bright with murderous ambition. "So no. I am not JUST a plaything." And lips a scheming grin. "I'm the Tyrant-King of Pirsitel!"
