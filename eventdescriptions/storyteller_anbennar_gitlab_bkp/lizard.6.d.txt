The Favhanosy marathon, a rather strange tradition to us, has nonetheless become an international event amongst halflings, who travel from as far away as distant lands to take part. Recently, one of our halflings is becoming renowned for all the training they have done as preparation, to the point where many of our lizardfolk subjects have begun to regard their participation in the marathon with a kind of national pride. Many of our people hope they will achieve a great victory in the event, and that they will bring pride to our nation. Of course, few of us have any idea of what exactly the tradition entails, but it has certainly whipped up a lot of excitement.
