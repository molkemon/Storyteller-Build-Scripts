After years of gruelling warfare and a protracted siege, the gates of the capital lay open. The city is a husk of what it once was, the river that once brought life to the bustling metropolis had been diverted long ago, and all that remains is a dried-out ditch filled with the bodies of victims of hunger and thirst. The raheni who once swelled the ranks of the Tiger Command in their fight for tolerance and acceptance under new masters have lost the will to fight on. There is little resistance as the true masters of Rahen, nay, Haless, march through the city, the march of steel greaves drowning out what few sounds of life remain.

Led by the Lion Marshal, the kikun reaches the palace of the Tiger Marshal. The ruler, who once ruled much of Rahen through their legions of bureaucrats, currently commands little more than their palace, but even that is coming to an end. Following the raid of the palace, those few guards with the will to fight died in battle, while the rest were captured. Special care was taken to ensure the marshal was captured and delivered to Sarilavhan.

In Sarilavhan, the ruler is taken before the lord and his loyal marshals. The tiger marshal is forced to kneel before the ruler as she begins to speak. "You have sown disunity and turned clan against clan in your bid for freedom. You would shatter the empire of steel that hobgoblinkind has forged through war and sacrifice. There is no greater dishonour than to betray the efforts and lives of our brethren and ancestors who forged our Great Command."
