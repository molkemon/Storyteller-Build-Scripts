The ruler paced restlessly around the palace gardens, fists balled behind their back, gritting their teeth and staring at the ground. It had not been a good week for them—virtually everything they had been working on had seen one failure after another. Even when they lay in bed, they struggled to think of anything other than the slights and setbacks—and so they were out here, at an ungodly time of the night. "Trouble sleeping?" A woman's voice roused the lord from their musings. They turned, startled—then, as they noticed a humanoid figure in the shadow of the garden, reached for the dagger at their hip. The woman in the shadows raised her hands in a show of peace. "What do you want?" The lord asked, as the woman emerged into torchlight. They could see her pale skin, her dark red hair—a Cannorian. "Who let you in!?" "Who let me in? Well, your guards, for one. Hmm… your servants too. Your slaves… you get the idea." The woman smiled mischievously. "It really wasn't difficult, especially for someone like me." "Like you…?" The woman waved the question away. "Unimportant. I really came here for a… minor request." She emerged from the shadows completely now. The lord could see that her skin was like a bone-white. Despite himself, the lord felt strangely at ease—like he was being lulled to inaction. "I've been looking to start a family—not with you!—and I need a place to call home. Your Empire sounded like such a… perfect place." The woman stepped closer, took the lord's hand in hers, and smiled as she looked into their eyes. Somehow, despite it all, the lord found themselves ignoring the sharp, pointed fangs at the corners of her mouth. "All you have to do is say yes… and I'll try to keep in touch. What do you say?"
