Evidently, the people of a nearby settlement think the war against the raiders hasn't progressed well enough. They have taken up what few weapons they possess, and marched on the raiders themselves, engaging a war party before our soldiers could even locate it. Doubtlessly, they will be slaughtered to the last man; fools, this is why you leave the fighting to soldiers. However, their sacrifice will pave the way to annihilating the weakened war party.
