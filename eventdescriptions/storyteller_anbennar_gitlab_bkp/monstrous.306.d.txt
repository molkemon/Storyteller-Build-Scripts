The roosts of our kingdom are buzzing with new activity. We have been beset by a small army of recruiters from abroad who seek to hire on our people for their unique talent of flight as surveyors, scouts, and couriers in their armies and commercial enterprises. Disturbingly, they appear to be attracting a sizable level of interest, especially from those harpies who aren't as established and do not have a prominent role in caring for the brood. What should we do about these younglings leaving the nest so soon?
