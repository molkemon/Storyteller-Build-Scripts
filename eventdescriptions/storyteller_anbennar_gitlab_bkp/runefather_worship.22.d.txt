A delegation of the Amlruzskappa Clan have approached the Obsidian Council asking for a redirection of resources from the Serpentspine. They claim that the Darkstone Clan has been monopolizing any and all metals and minerals from above, which they believe may even threatening the survival of the Empire’s blood runes entirely. The Darkstone delegates argue that there are plenty of resources to go around already, and that any reduction on their end will no doubt result in shortages for the Obsidian war machine.
