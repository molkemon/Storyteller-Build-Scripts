As our warriors clear a path up to the tower, many aged and defunct traps are encountered, but some stillfunctional traps are triggered by a few of our less fortunate warriors, with rather permanent consequences. However, for every trap sprung we are rewarded with new insights into their creation and material composition, gleaned by dissecting their remains. Our finest smiths have taken some of these scraps and are fashioning them into sharper and stronger weapons than we had ever dared to dream of.
