Mayhaps we have given the intelligence of the savage rune warriors too little credit. A small band of them have attacked a convoy of our inquisitors which was bringing suspected witches to our capital for trial and punishment. At first, we had heard that all were killed, and thus all was fine; the loss of the inquisitors was a grave insult, but the loss of the witches was welcomed.  However, this was not to be. Apparently, the witches were spared; and freed, into the countryside. Now, none amongst the crowds of refugees fleeing the savages’ wrath can be trusted, for within their number may hide the most detestable of villains.
