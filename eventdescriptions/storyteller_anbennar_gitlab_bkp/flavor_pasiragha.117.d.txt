Our diplomats in Sramaya have brought a message that the leadership of Bhuvauri is willing to hold a meeting between our two countries. The conference is to be held in Sramaya in a few weeks. This is perhaps the only chance for us to do something previous lords could never achieve and bring the Kharunyana delta back to friendly hands, even if it means we have to befriend Bhuvauri ourselves.
Bhuvauri began as an organized slave rebellion along the Kharunyana delta. After defeating the armies in battle, the freed slaves proclaimed the founding of the Free Land, or Bhuvauri. Many lords have attempted to retake the lands Bhuvauri holds, but those efforts have mostly ended in vain.
Decades and centuries have passed since the rebellion, and both Bhuvauri and our nation have experienced numerous changes. Maybe it is time to stop letting the past define our future; maybe we should start building new relations with Bhuvauri. The choice is in our hands.
