Once thought to be an insurmountable wave of monsters pouring across the Ruin Kingdoms, the hobgoblins of the Great Command have finally received their reckoning. Tens of thousands of elite harimari warriorsloyal adherents to the Lotus Claw and the duties thereinbegan the brutal process of clearing the land of local resistance. Goblin slaves, led by hobgoblin commanders, laid traps on vital supply routes, depots, and barracks, resolute in causing us as many casualties as possible. But with each passing day, their numbers dwindled, and the guerilla warfare has finally ended, for now. Decades of rule and cultural oppression would hopefully soon give way to a cultural resurgance and rebirth of the Ruin Kingdoms of old, with the Second Harimraj at their helm.  The ThreeHeaded Banner, symbolic of the union between the Boar, Lion, and Wolf Commands, lays broken at the feet of the a local field commaner, Poymewhijiya III Killa of the Castle Herd. He is calm, for his home in Dhujat is no longer in danger.
