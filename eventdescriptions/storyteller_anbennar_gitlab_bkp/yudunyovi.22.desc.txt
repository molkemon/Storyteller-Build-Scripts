At last, at last our quest has reached its end. The land of our ancestors, Surakes - known to others as Bulwar - is now under our dominion. The people here have long been lost and scared, when the worldly gods faded from view they turned fully to Taqoshur, whom they call Surael, to protect them from darkness. They did not forget the gods, but thinking them dead led to a worse state. The greatest gift we gave these people was not the strength of our government or the restored unity of the land, but the good news that the worldly gods live and Taqoshur is not alone. Even lost and confused, they have cared for Taqoshur’s garden, and we stand in awe of the flowers blooming - many were even sown by people unrelated to our ancestors but those blooms hold beauty as well. Now all the realm has been reclaimed. All the gods have been remembered. We are united, but not in the anaemic unity of the Kalyin worshippers, where all are mere parts of one whole. Like the worldly gods themselves, though Triunic, centaur, dwarven or Bulwari are all distinct, through the divine we are one.
