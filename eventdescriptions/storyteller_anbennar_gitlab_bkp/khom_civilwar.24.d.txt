A messenger from the Sirtan tribes has arrived with a proposal. We will not be raided during this crisis and Sirtan will militarily intervene on our side, but we surrender rights to any war loot in addition to letting the tribesman pillage as they please after our victory. The Sirtan are infamous for their cruelty and we mean to be a unifying force; is it truly worth working with them or should we risk them going to our enemies?
