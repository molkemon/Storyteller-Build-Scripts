For most lizardfolk, the 330th Empire had just been one of many to rule your kind. You had long believed that to be true yourself, before your scholars had managed to gain access to the libraries of Nazhni. Their research and synthesis has finally paid off with an exciting revelation the 330th Empire was not simply ruled by any lizardfolk. It was ruled by Mashkay Lizardfolk.  Your jubilation at this news is matched by that of everyone else who is present. Never before had your people known that the legacy of the 330th Empire was uniquely theirs to claim yours to claim, even. There are more developments regarding the maritime and administrative capabilities of the 330th Empire, but you know what's truly invigorated you.  You now have a mandate to reconquer the lands of the 330th Empire.
