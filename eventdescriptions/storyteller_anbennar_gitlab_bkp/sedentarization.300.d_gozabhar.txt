This outpost could hardly be called an outpost anymore. Every day, it bustles and brims with the lives of our people and the hoofbeats of centaur clans bringing goods from far-off lands... the tusks, hides, and meats of far-off animals... and herbs of all sorts, for all sorts of purposes, from all sorts of far-off plants. Soon enough, goods from nearly every adjacent continent will make their way to the markets of this settlement before filtering on back towards the core of our realm... or further on, across plains that are not-quite-forbidden, over the horizon.
