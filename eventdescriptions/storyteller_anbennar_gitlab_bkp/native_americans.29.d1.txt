While the ruler was going on a hunt with some of their warriors, trying to surround a group of deer and bring some food for their tribe, one of their men approaches them and says that he has seen a really big deer deep in the forest. The ruler does not hesitate and follows the man through the woods until they finds their prey. A big deer, the biggest they has ever seen poses majestic in the middle of a clearing. They whispers a short 'thank you' to the spirits of the forest for this opportunity that they will not miss. They orders their men to stay quiet and in their positions as they starts to silently move towards the best position to fire their bow. The bow is ready to release the arrow destined to kill the animal. The ruler's breath is calm. Their fingers start to free the arrow.
