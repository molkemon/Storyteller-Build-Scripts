Their ruler has faced challenges to their rule before, but none such as this: a young man has been going around, claiming to have received an enchanted blade from some "Lady of the Lagoon," granting him a mandate whereby he has proclaimed himself king of the people. Somehow, this farcical aquatic ceremony has caused quite a stir, and he’s attracted a sizable entourage. It's not clear what he wants, after achieving his claimed kingship does he advocate for a system of government based on strange women lying in ponds distributing swords? In the future, we shall have to request that the merfolk be more discerning in how they dispose of garbage. For now, though. how do we deal with this character?
