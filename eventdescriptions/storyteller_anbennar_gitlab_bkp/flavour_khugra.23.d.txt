Yusshela, the Celestial Dolphin, is a blessed and powerful Araizary, who has historically granted great boons and blessings to those deserving. Today, our ruler is going to become one of these fortunate individuals, as a reward for the continued reverence and worship of the Araizary in Khugra. They enter the sea alone, as the constellation of the Celestial Dolphin shines brightly in the night sky in the moonless night. They cannot avoid a shiver as the envoy of Yusshela approaches, with the form of a great white shark made of softly shining starlight. Despite its fearsome aspect, the beast’s demeanour is calm as it gets close to our ruler. It stops right in front of them, slightly bowing its head down, as if allowing itself to be petted. Our ruler does just that, and as soon as their hand touches the animal, the shine becomes blinding for a second. When their eyes open, the beast is gone, only the light of the evershining stars breaking the darkness. Our ruler swims back to the shore, where royal advisors await eager to listen to their retelling of the experience. The ruler feels the power of the boon flowing through their body, and the changes in their personality are evident. They have received the Blessing of the Shark, a boon of great ferocity.
