Spells, swords, shields, and awards are readied against the ghost as it dives down and conjures spikes of silverywhite energy that he launches in circular patterns around it. These spikes pierce body and plate, they skewer us in many ways, but the ghost, Gorub, is not invincible. Slowly his form fades away and his voice grows weaker. In an act of desperation, it launches more and more spikes, but eventually, it runs out. With a last, desperate "aaascend…" it vanishes into thin air and leaves the package of gold behind.
