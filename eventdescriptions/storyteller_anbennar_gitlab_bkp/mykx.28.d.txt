With the construction of a Naval Academy in full swing, the regulations and institutions have come up for debate. Several parties have shown special interests in certain measures to be taken.  The Captains wish for discipline to be weighted above all. The Buccaneers wish for enrolment into the academy to be free of charge. The Smugglers wish to open up internship positions aboard their ships to students, and get priority access to those students.  In order to ensure their interests are represented, all three factions have presented a candidate to become the principal of the Academy. Which one will be picked?
