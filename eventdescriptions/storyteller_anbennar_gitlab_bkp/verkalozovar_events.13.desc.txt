The grip of the ancients never fades in dwarven society. Perpetuated by worship, those who came before us still live and breathe in the ethereal plane, waiting for the opportunity to return as spirits. The Oz-Amekh is one such spirit, with a firm grasp over life and death. His thoughts emanate throughout the throne room, spoken from the High King's mouth as if our earthly ruler is merely a puppet of the spirit. It is time to face the truth: the Oz-Amekh is the true king of Verkal Ozovar.
