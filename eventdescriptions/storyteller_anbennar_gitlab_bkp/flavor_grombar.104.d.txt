Across Grombar, in all the lands that still follow the ways of Dookan, yet another uprising has begun. Led by the remnants of the old clans, these orks seek to restore Grombar to their own vision of the nation, one rooted in the old ways and outdated traditions. As more and more of the followers of Dookan rise up, it is clear that the ways of our ancestors will not go down without a fight.
