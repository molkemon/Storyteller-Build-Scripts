Scéposmatism, a school born in the realm of Lokemeion, has long been at the forefront of questioning the pre-existing ideals and taboos of our society, openly criticizing philosophical ideas that have been viewed as standard. Though many see them as little more than public nuisances, their views stir up the most trouble in the political rights of minorities, particularly women and non-citizens. Recently, the Scéposmatists in your capital have been pushing for the extension of certain political rights to these people, though such an act would certainly be extremely destabilizing.
