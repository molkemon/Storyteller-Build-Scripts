It seems as if the people of the province did not manage to appease Kroozbuto Nubo. It does not matter if their prayers were not heartfelt or their offerings poor - we will all have to pay for their failure. While the rains in the province and the surrounding area have been disappointing during the last few years, at least there was rain. Now there is nothing. The sun glares down on us from a pale, clear sky, scorching the earth and everything brave enough to grow. The people of the province have some lean years ahead.
