Headed by the Lord of Darkness, the Unseelie Court is one where every moment of life is bitterly fought for. There, the strong dare to push forward and reach past old limits while the weak are challenged to earn their next breath. Restraint and strength must be shown against trials both physical and emotional. Those that rise to the occasion gain the favor of the Lord. Those that do not are cast aside. Suffice to say, the fey of Unseelie are not ones to anger. Most of their followers struggle to meet the Lord’s grueling demands. Most mortals would fail to even struggle. For this reason, and others, it has always behooved our people to maintain a healthy, if not cordial, relationship with the Unseelie. Perhaps now is the right time for a show of grit and determination?
