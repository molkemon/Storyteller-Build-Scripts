\".And from his ring came a perfect simulacrum, to sit in the cell and fool his captors as he escaped." 'The Tale of Jevan the Wise', fortythird stanza  While your simulacrum now exists indefinitely, in order to summon and dismiss the illusion at will it must be stored within an enchanted power source, allowing you to call it only when needed (such as a standin when you're preoccupied with study).  Choosing the power source of the illusion is a matter of preference, and will not affect the performance of the illusion in any way.
