The citizens are not the sole authority within their cities, the church also has a strong presence in most big cities going back to their initial founding. Conflicts sometimes arise when the councils of the citizens implement rules that the church disapprove of. Such conflicts have become more and more commonplace and members of the church are openly acting in defiance of the various rules the citizens have agreed upon in their cities. Delegates from various cities dominated by the citizens are now urging us to clarify the situation.
