Your country is at war and to a monarch, war is a family business. Your son, the heir, has been appointed general and will lead our armies to victory. Your daughter has also taken a keen interest in the war, studying warfare alongside her religious education. One night, the court's storyteller retells the story of how Muta Mestika once led the divine army against the demons. Your daughter listens, transfixed. She later approaches you requesting to fight our enemies.
