Everybody knew the rat would never be a king. The old king's body was still warm, but his cousin was already preparing the coronation speech; the games of power had all been played during the last stages of the king's illness.  The newly enthroned Aryakompu had already moved his kotakompu "rival" back to the palace enclosure, when the old court mage, Rannaraikazhil, asked for an audience. Nobody knows what he told the king, but the very next day, the FleshGnasher of Mlerek was proclaimed once again the official heir to the realm.
