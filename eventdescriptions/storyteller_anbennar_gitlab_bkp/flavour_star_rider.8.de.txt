The fundamental nature of this conclave is to question the faith so that we attain its pure, unfiltered essence. It is instrumental that we do not stubbornly cling to false or inaccurate preconceptions of what our worship entails! Our lord and dynasty should be ecstatic. The conclave has been more streamlined in these past few months, and the ruler's influence is unmatched. Yet he/she feels something bubbling up. A feeling, burrowed, yet resurfacing. Unable to be placed until it shines for all to see. A hated truth, coalescing into a singular sentence: ‘This is not how my faith was taught to me, is it? Surely, certainly not. Right?’

We do not serve Irdaeos to be rewarded in this life. He has given us gifts, but only through hardship can we present our virtue to him! Well, ruler, tell us! WHAT ARE THESE VIRTUES?

Our lord answers, compelled by the fervent crowd despite realizing his/her own inadequacy.
