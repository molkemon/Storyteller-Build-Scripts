As our clans have established contact, the leader of Siegebreaker has been sending shamans to our people, calling upon us to fulfill our duty and fight the humans and their gods, who they hold accountable for Dookan being imprisoned. As they preach in the streets about the imminent victory in Escann and his need for reinforcements from the faithful, many keep walking, but some do stand and listen. Such heretical thoughts largely fall on deaf ears in our society: We all know only the dwarves are responsible for His imprisonment. As our leader orders the shamans to leave the realm, a small but not insignificant amount of orks follow them. Heretics who believe that Korgus is the son of Dookan. Even worse: Some of our most prominent clan elders are leaving for Escann as well.
