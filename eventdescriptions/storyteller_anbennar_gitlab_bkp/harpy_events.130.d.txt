Harpies line the cliffs, seated on benches carved into the face of the mountain. Far below, curious landbound residents crane their necks to get a glimpse of the oncoming spectacle. All at once the cliffside amphitheater goes quiet as two warriors take flight, circling each other appraisingly. One dives forward to strike, and the crash of metal on metal drowns out all sound as their blades meet. The minutes drag on, until only one warrior remains aloft, crowing out a song of victory as the loser retreats back into the crowd in shame.
