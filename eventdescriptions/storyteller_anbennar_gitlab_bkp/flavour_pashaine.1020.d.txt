It is a testament to the strength of our Parliamentary system that it continues to function during crisis. Our openness is vulnerability as well as strength, however, and we must be ever vigilant against the mindaltering corruption of the School of Enchantment, which seeks to subvert the representative process.  In recent days, the corporatist clique have brought us alarming reports of a Member voting against his clique to weaken the privileges of his local guild. Such a profound reversal in an individual normally so staunchly defensive of guild rights is a worrying sign of magical alteration, and many are calling for him to be immediately impeached.
