We row against the swells. The first ones just make the raft shake. The warm mist gets hotter. Around the fourth wave we clash against something… odd. A soft scaly turquoise bulge, which disappears under the surface the instant we make contact with it. In grim anticipation, we feel it scrape under the side of the raft. In just a couple of seconds, a long necked froglike head emerges upon the larboard. At the same time, turquoise scaled hands claw onto the ship's borders. Its brown fat lower jaw opens, and a hot and suffocating flow of mist falls upon us.
