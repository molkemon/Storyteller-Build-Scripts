An envoy has arrived from the orks of the south, who have shared so much of their culture with us, and amongst whom we have found the only nontrolls to possess troll hearts. They tell us of one last tenet of their feudal system, under which an overlord like their Warmaster promises to treat any threat to a brotherrealm as a threat to his own, in exchange for a  portion of our tax income. They offer this closest of bonds to our clans, saying that the formation of such a union is the only possible outcome for two nations as close as ours.
