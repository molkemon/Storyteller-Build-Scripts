Much of the land in our realm is held by former nobles and wealthy farmers, and the years of conquest have left much of this land uncultivated and fallow. Underused land is inherently mismanaged and goes to waste, weakening our people. We must ensure that this land is used in a way that supports the command and aids in our war efforts. We will give the state the power to confiscate unproductive land from these nobles and noncompliant farmers, redistributing it to industrious workers who have proven their loyalty and reliability to the state.
