It has been raining for 3 straight days. The plains that the camp was sitting upon were turning into the worst kind of mud. No one wants to be here. The rather loud prattling and crackling of the rain was barely audible to most people here at this point. But sleeping was miserable. The close lightning strikes made it impossible. Three tents had been destroyed by them already. The envoy was to come today. Everyone from the entire camp, drenched in rain, sat there on the precipice just waiting for the slacker to arrive. They saw him fall into the mud four times as he approached. Vindication. The camp was not packed together. It was deemed appropriate by everyone to just leave the place.  ‘Final Result: Demoralizing?’
