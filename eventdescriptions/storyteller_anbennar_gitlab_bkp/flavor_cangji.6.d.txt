The spirits of Danggun have been steadfast in their resistance to our expansion, as the harmful effects of spiritfire on spirits of various types are becoming more problematic in areas like Gowon, where spirits hold important administrative duties. This issue was eventually resolved through the joint effort of Danggun's spiritual advisors and the pyremages of the Ashen Soul. By slightly tweaking the inlays of the braziers, the flames can be calibrated to allow spirits of a certain pattern to be unaffected by the repelling heat, enabling the spirits of Gowon to continue their duties without being harmed. With this rift between our cultures healed, we may now finally understand their teachings about the spirits.
