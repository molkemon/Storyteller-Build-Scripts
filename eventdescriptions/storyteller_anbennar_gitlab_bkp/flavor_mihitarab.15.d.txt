Long ago, our people sailed into the west, where, it was said, lived an undying race. Long ago, great genies ruled in the east, creating works of incredible power, only rumors of which reached our ancestors. With growing naval power, bold adventurers have suggested expeditions to both of these lands, but none could agree which should take priority. This impasse was solved when our lord proposed an even more ambitious plan – undertaking both expeditions at the same time. Now, in Bakya Bay, lie the results of their efforts; two fleets equipped for these great enterprises. Their missions: to explore strange new lands; to seek out new life and new civilizations; to boldly go where few have gone before.
