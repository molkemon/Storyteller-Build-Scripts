"Close your eyes. Turn your face towards the warmth of His regard. Let the light of the sun flow over you. Feel your inner fire rise in response. Allow it to suffuse every part of your being. And then, when it feels like you are bursting with blazing light, shape it, direct it, let it flow outward in an incandescent blast of sunfire." Way of the Blazing Sun, training recitation four.  All throughout Sareyand, the monks of the Way of the Blazing Sun have of late become a common sight, but nowhere are they encountered more frequently than within the walls of the Blazing Temple, their sacred House of the Blazing Sun. It has become a daily spectacle for the city's faithful to observe the meditative trances performed by the monks and admire the whispers of pure sunlight undulating across their forms. While many on the Way see their calling in meditative contemplation of Surael's Order, or the guidance of the faithful towards His Light, some take a more militant stance, eager to bring the fight towards the servants of the Malevolent Dark. These monks flock towards the ranks of the Kasipti, or exorcistpriests, who no longer administer just to the needs of the faithful under the auspices of the local temple, but can increasingly be found accompanying the armies of our state on their holy mission to vanquish the Dark.
