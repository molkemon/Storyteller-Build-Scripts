Our lord has spent years studying and meditating about the true nature of Vortza Lesa through the figure of her husband, Vortzo Laukano. The goddess of nature and the god of agriculture, wife and husband. If the latter controls the fertility and growth of the wild, the former does the proper with those parts of nature that goblinkind has tamed. Today, all that effort has finally paid off, with the monarch shocking the shamans of the nation with an elaborate speech about the nature of our goddess, and how through her husband she also holds domain over the mushrooms and crops that mortals get nourishment from. Their lord has gained not only knowledge through these studies, but also the respect of many in our realm, who now see our lord as a talented theologian and a true authority in matters of faith.
