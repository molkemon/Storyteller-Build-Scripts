Our people are a mobile folk. The reason we are sons of Irdaeos is because we left the lands of Rohibos. And it is Irdaeos who says that a centaur only stops moving when he is dead or broken. It is the creed of our people that no matter how good a land is, there is always somewhere better, until we reach the paradise of Ik Magthaal. This tendency has made us great, but now it is turning against us. One of the minor clans is claiming that our weakness and instability have made our lands unsuitable, and they now seek to make their fortune elsewhere, as long as it is not here.
