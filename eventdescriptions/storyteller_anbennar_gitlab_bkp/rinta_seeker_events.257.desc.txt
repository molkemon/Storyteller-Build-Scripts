A ruin in Rinta Ruin has been found to contain a scroll talking of Almadhab and Alahmar's magical potential. As half genies, they were already extremely potent in their ability to make wishes. As mages, this power was further enhanced to heights unreachable by even the most exceptional conventional mages; the gap between them and us was almost as monumental as the distance between the ends of the earth. Each scroll that they have left behind provides the Mage Council a fraction of their power; each brings us one step closer to ending this madness. Rinta Ruin has been wary of allowing outside entities into their realm, but with a little convincing, the Magi Council is sure that they can be tempted to allow us to investigate the ruins. What is more worrying is the prospect that the buildup of this scroll's innate resonance with dark magicks might affect the surrounding environment. Only time will tell what the effects of desecrating this ruin can bring.
