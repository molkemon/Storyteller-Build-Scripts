Youth is wasted on the young, it is said. The storied scholars of the Orange Sash School however intend to make sure this does not befall our ruler, or at least they will try to. They offer their services and guidance but our young ruler bristles against their suggestions, calling their proposals outdated for modern times. Indeed our ruler also questions why the scholars never propose any reforms for their own institutions within our state but happily opt to dilute their power. These scholars dismiss our ruler's concern saying it stems from their youthful age and naivety. How will our ruler respond?
