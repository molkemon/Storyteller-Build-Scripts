‘I heard he partakes of the poppy seed’ whispers one woman. ‘I saw him incapable of even firing a bow,’ mutters a man. A child exclaims, ‘Mommy said he needs help to pee!’ before being shushed. All this and more has been heard from the populace of a province. The rumored weakness of our ruler has been the town gossip, and the local governors are fearful of the chaos this might cause. Their power is slipping before their eyes, and they have come to the Crane Throne for guidance. We have to handle this, but how?
