Deep within the caverns, the voices finally grow silent. As the host advances through the darkness, torchlight illuminates the cavern above… and what clings to it.  Hundreds of the batlike creatures cling to the ceiling, twisted bodies folded into themselves. The host freezes beneath these creatures. A single noise could lead to disaster, but for now, they sleep.  Against all odds, we have stumbled upon these creatures unaware. Fortune, it seems, has favoured us today.  If we move quickly, and quietly, we may be able to slay this threat before it even has a chance to realise.
