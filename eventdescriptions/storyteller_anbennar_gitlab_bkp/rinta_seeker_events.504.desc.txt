The sheer scale of the telescope we are constructing necessitates an equally great power source. The question of how we will do that, like many other questions in the construction of the telescope, has become yet another battlefield between the lord Mwalim and the lord Darwiiz. The Mwalim have recently become infatuated with new devices from Cannor that can contain and discharge great amounts of magical energy. We are far from fully understanding how they work, but the Mwalim assure us we know enough to imitate it and make a device that can support the telescope if given sufficient magic once a week. The Darwiiz urge us that this route is too dangerous; we do not understand the devices and we can’t know if we’re making an energy store or a damestear bomb. They instead advocate for using proven traditional rituals; though this is definitely less risky, it would require the magic to be reapplied every single day, which would be much more costly to maintain. Perhaps we could isolate the Cannorian discharges, limiting their danger but also their utility. It’s hardly a better solution than the other proposals but it would allow us to placate them both somewhat.
