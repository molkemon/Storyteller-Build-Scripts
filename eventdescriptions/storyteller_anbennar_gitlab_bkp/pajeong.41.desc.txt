Raids on our western province have increased, as the Shuvüüsh bird riders grow bold. Towns are razed, caravans looted, and government buildings ransacked. And at the center of it all, the local Shuvüüsh communities remain untouched. It is clear to any with eyes to see that they are welcoming their fellow tribesmen to weaken our rule. As long as they spy for their wild brethren, it has become nearly impossible to stop their strikes at our crucial administrative infrastructure in this region. Even worse, the Hwalbaek soldiers who would normally rise to the challenge of the bird-riders are complacent. They sit back and watch to see how we handle the situation, laughing at our misfortune. We must act.
