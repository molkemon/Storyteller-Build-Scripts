Before the assembled diplomats and dignitaries, the ruler places the Imperial Regalia into the hands of the new ruler, recognising them as the rightful ruler of Anbennar on the behalf of the Ravelian Church and the one true God. With the coronation complete, our ruler watches from a balcony as the attendees pour from the church and towards the surrounding celebrations, followed shortly by the new ruler. To be selected as the venue for the coronation is a great honour, the significance of which will not be lost on the citizens of our realm or the rest of the Empire.
