An interesting development has been taking place in our realm as of late. The upper castes are increasingly abandoning the usage of harimari art styles in favor of new human art styles. While most would laugh at the idea of humans being talented artists, more and more members of the upper castes are beginning to appreciate it nonetheless. This has led to many harimari artists losing their contracts and jobs, and they are now seeking support from the court. Helping the harimari by subsidizing their art and pushing humans out of the market again would be a well-respected move, but we could also just ignore them. How do we want to react to this development?
