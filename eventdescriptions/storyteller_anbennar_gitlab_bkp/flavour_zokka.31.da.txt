The ground is slick with something that was once a teacher. Their lord doesn't speak. They don't beg. They just kneels there, blood soaking into his robes, placidly watching their lord circle them. "It is written in the annals," their lord begins, "that Kaspar szalRexar told Xhuvari Bonechewer that she was a slave." They crouches in front of them, leaning in. "I find myself thinking of those words, now." A flick of a hand. A line of figures are brought closer; humans, elves, gnolls, centaur, harimari, and more. All are chained, and all are wounded. "Knowledge can't save you now," their lord whispers. "No more clever lies or witticisms. All that's left is to learn when to shut up." Their lord meets their eyes. Their voice is hoarse. "Zokka won't approve." Their lord only laughs, low and guttural. "Zokka gave us teeth. I'm simply using mine." They stands and turns away. Behind their lord, more slaves are herded like cattle, bound and bloodied and breathing; for now. "This isn't a purge," their lord says over their shoulder. "This is a correction." The doors slam shut. The slaughter begins.
