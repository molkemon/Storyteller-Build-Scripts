Once, we fled our homes, starving, searching for the scarcest bit of land in which we could grow our crops. We came to the realm of Nuzurbokh, but we were sorely disappointed; the soil was worthless, desolate. So we moved further, to what is now Zurkanek, and beyond, leaving the great expanse behind us untapped, untamed, unused. No longer. Now we have tamed the steppe. Where once was barren steppe, now vast cultivated farms make use of the land, stretching from one end of the realm to the other. A hundred works, where but a quarter of it would feed our entire people.

But in expansion, comes extension. To remedy this, to ensure the bounty of the plains can be enjoyed by our people, each province shall have a guild, responsible for the final ordering of their land, and bound to a single, central authority. With this force, under their watchful eye, none but the best farmers will supply our homeland. With this, we shall never know hunger again!
