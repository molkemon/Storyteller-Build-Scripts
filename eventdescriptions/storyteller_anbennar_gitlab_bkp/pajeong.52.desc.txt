Tensions are high within the court of our realm, as the members of the Merchants, Hwalbaek, and Neoksamaji have all gathered to air their grievances. Not only their grievances towards our lord, of which there are many; each group, of course, believes theirs are the most important. But also their grievances towards each other have spilled out, and at the end of the day, throats are sore with shouting and hands aching after shaking fists and pointing fingers. Our lord stepped in several times in an attempt to stop the fighting, but has been forced to abandon her efforts each time due to accusations of siding with one of the estates. The Merchants promise that their demands being met will result in profit for both our lord and themselves. The Hwalbaek proudly proclaim that the army's needs are paramount. The Neoksamaji whisper that the spirits will be generous if they are propitiated. Of course, our lord always has the option to refuse them all, making no one happy.
