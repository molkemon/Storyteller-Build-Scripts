As the ruler has still been unwilling to comment on their personal views on the tale of Dookan, some of the most radical shamans in HulJorkad have declared the royal family to be heretics. They have riled up a portion of the locals and they have stormed the royal palace in HulJorkad. As royal guards and fanatics clash, the ruler prepares for the worst. As fighting in the streets of HulJorkad worsens and bodies begin to pile up, it seems clear that the monarchy is no longer welcome here, or in any Old Dookan provinces. With the Old Dookan shamans alienated, the family is forced to revoke their stance of neutrality and find allies amongst the Bulgu Orazan communities of the north. As the court shaman starts a contingency protocol and begins preparing an expensive teleportation spell to a hold outside the influence of the shaman faction, he asks to which hold the ruler would like to go.
