Corruption. It was in the air, it was pungent, it was obvious. It infuriated Khetarch Neferkhet III. The court was full of useless people, simpletons, loyalists, and yesmen. People with no ability to do anything on their own, who lacked independence, and relied on him for everything. It was exhausting.  The only solution was for the court to be purged, and pure loyalists replaced with loyalists who actually knew what they were doing. This would be no small feat; even with his guidance the people would need reassurance that things would be okay, that the hardships could be endured. The nation would be stable and everything would work out in the end. Time to find someone to give a speech for him.
