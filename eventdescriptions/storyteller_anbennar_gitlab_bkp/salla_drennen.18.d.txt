What I thought of the Herald of Change? Now that’s a strange one. I feel like my opinion on him changes almost as much as he does, and I entirely appreciate the strength of that statement! It was bold of us to approach him so directly—I’ll maintain that until I die. Even the other archfey fear him, at least a little. They say it was he who cursed the werewolves, you know.

It went better than I expected. Your idea to approach upon the beginning of a new cycle was a stroke of genius. I can only imagine the moment of emergence from his cocoon is the most docile he’ll ever be—I’m certainly rather groggy when I get out of bed!

Still, I don’t really have an opinion to state. He simply was, and I am sure whatever he is now is not the same.
