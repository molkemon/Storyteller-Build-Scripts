The Statist rulers have succeeded in making institutions of our realm increasingly representative, but one institution that has resisted recent changes is the army. One of our generals belongs to a family of nobles who have strong ties to the House of our dynasty and has been sabotaging our efforts to oust the loyalists from highranking military positions. Now, he's gone even further and wants one of his relatives, also a Monarchist, to act as advisor to our ruler. While this young person seems like a talented commandant, we would loathe giving them influence.
