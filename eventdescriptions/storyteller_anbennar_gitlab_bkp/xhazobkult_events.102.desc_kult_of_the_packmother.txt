While onlookers typically characterize the Xhazobkult as destructionloving demonworshipers, this is not always the reality. In our lands, the native Kult instead being focused on animistic worship of the natural world and the Packmother. But even in our state, the taint of the Kult's more demonic side is still present, if diminished and hidden away. We have recently heard of a warlock in the nearby province preparing a ritual to conjure a powerful demon into our world. The potential dangers of such a ritual are worrisome, but the power of a demon is tempting.
