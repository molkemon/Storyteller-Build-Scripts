'Fucking traitor.’ Came the harsh shout across the courtyard.  Tensions between the returning Aldresian Knights, who fought for Corin, and those who stayed and protected Adenn, have come to a boil. An Aldresian Knight, middle aged yet with no more insignia than a recruit on his scarred armor, answers the challenge with a blade drawn, drawing shouts and murmurs.  The senior Aldresian Knight who flung the insult steps forward with her own blade drawn, fully gray hair in a ponytail and wearing a battle worn commander’s plate armor. Both pushing forward and meeting face to face, their blades inches away from each other’s throats as they howl obscenities. Each accusing the other of betraying their oaths; of failing to protect the Emperor or failing to protect the innocent.  The entire Orda watches as Grandmaster Delian arrives in a rare appearance, lucid from his usual absent demeanor, as he marshals order back to the knights. Both prideful warriors return to their factions as the Grandmaster departs, leaving the Aldresian Senior Council to bridge the growing schism.  For decades we have fought a war outside the walls of Arca Aldresia, and now we fight a war within. Younger battle hardened knights bristle at their status in the face of the older aging leadership. Called The Greentide Generation, they wish to make Orda Aldresia an effective fighting force, but the resistance of elder knights is proving difficult to them. What will we do?
