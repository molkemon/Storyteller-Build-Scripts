Unlike the Steam Barons, the mages of our country have been of little help to our feats of reclamation. No matter how complex their rune magic grows, it cannot outpace the raw utility we have found with the mighty Steam barons. What little independence they have been given, they have squandered. If they ever hope to gain their place in Basalt life, they must work for it. Of course, the gracious Steam Barons have offered their help in administering them and putting them under the watchful eye of the state. Who are we to refuse?
