The magisters, who have long been the most experienced of mages in Cannor may be able to assist in our venture. The runes of the Setzunkhupa are unlike anything else seen since the fall of AulDwarov, but the diverse magical expertise of the Magisterium gives us new insights to aid our runesmiths in this project. By examining the way that the magic from each rune flows through the Setzunkhupa, together we've been able to investigate the observatory's functions in a way that merely studying the runes or trial and error couldn't accomplish. There are still some mysteries we haven't solved regarding the runes of the Setzunkhupa, but now the day of complete understanding seems that much closer.
