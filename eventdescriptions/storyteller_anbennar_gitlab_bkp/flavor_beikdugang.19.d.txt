The discovery of mechanims has sparked intense debate in our scientific community. They are a remarkable new kind of entity, unprecedented in Hallan. The primary initial discussion revolved around whether they were truly alive. This was a perplexing question; undeniably, they lack many traditional signs of life. They do not grow or age, cannot reproduce, and do not eat or breathe. Nevertheless, many continued to explore further, seeking to determine if their abilities ended there. Could they think? Could they create something new? Could they feel? After much time, we are now certain—they can. They are alive, and this is a new and wondrous form of life.

Following this revelation, mechanims from Insyaa have begun migrating to our lands in large numbers. We have also started creating new mechanims who will grow up with our customs and become part of our people.
