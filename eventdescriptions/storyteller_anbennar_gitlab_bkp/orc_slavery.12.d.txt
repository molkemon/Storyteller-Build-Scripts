The forces of Cannor have since banned the sale of slaves in Cannor. Fortunately the Nathalairey are used to dealing with such affairs, and the rogues and smugglers of the city can smell the profit in illicit contraband; living or otherwise.  Several members of the Shadow Council suggest that we focus in other parts of business but prominent members of the Krahzate promise fortunes for all in this new world of illegal slavery; after all, the plantations of Aelantier are always left wanting, and the lords of Rahen have turned westwards for more interesting cattle.
