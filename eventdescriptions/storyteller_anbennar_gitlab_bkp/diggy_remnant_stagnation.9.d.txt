Our preparations to wake up from centuries of stagnation is topic for discussions in all social ranks of our country. From farmers and common labourers to powerful cartel officials and even clan chiefs, everyone has their own opinion on what the direction of our country should be. These discussions are usually held in private, behind closed doors, but this has suddenly changed as a famous statesman has decided to make their views public. They speak openly on their beliefs that it is time to leave our past behind and pursue the great dream of a restored homeland. A great many dwarves have gathered around them on the street where they preach their doctrine, and soon their enemies have arrived as well. The Beholden, dwarves opposing any and all change to our country, yell at them in anger and throw a mixture of insults and produce. The situation is dangerously close to escalating into a fight, so our lord is hardpressed to do something about this.
