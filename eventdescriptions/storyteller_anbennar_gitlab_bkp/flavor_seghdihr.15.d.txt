Molten steel burbles and is forged into armour and weaponry at the Hargunforge of Hehodovar, and those who march through its halls don the finest of equipment known in all of the Segbandal. Easy access to the high quality equipment here affords our men many opportunities to learn new methods of war, and we can tune the great production heart of our nation to produce whatever we choose to focus on. Should we have our men focus on endurance training in their heavy armour to hone their indefatigable spirit, improve our defensive formations to protect our soldiers from firearms, or dedicate time to melee combat to ensure that our skill in handtohand fighting never declines?
