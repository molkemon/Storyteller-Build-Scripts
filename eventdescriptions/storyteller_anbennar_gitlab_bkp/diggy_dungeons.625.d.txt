Your group follows a winding pathway that leads to a large "Machine Room", at least according to the sign above the entrance. You take a gander inside and find several rows of complex machinery humming in a barely lit hall, as well as huge metal apparatus in the back, lined by several rows of round objects. You briefly wonder why the machines do not seem to work, even though the dim light and noise indicate a certain activity, when you notice pulsating masses of what appears to be wind and electricity approaching you from the corners of the room. Several elementals seem to have made home in this room, sucking out the power of the systems to nourish themselves.
