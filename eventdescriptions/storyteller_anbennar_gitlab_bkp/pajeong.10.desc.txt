A representative from the burghers has come to court with an intriguing offer. They have heard of lucrative opportunities within Pajeong. The riches gained by the trade of Pajeong's goods are mouth-watering, and they desire to take a cut of the profits. They ask for a 'small' commission to strengthen their hold over the trade conducted there. Of course, this demand is an outrageous affront to the crown, but the malice within the man's eyes sends a clear message: if the crown values the tribute generated by their trade networks, it would do well not to anger the burghers.
