The skies have once again been graced by the Bronze Dragon.  With a piercing roar he swept down across the battlements, burning all in His wake. Rock and stone melted wherever His flames struck. Where thousands of our men would have been lost if we attempted to scale these castle walls, Aakhet destroyed them in an instance. He swooped down in front of the besieging troops as they chanted His name, landing in front of them and belching a fireball into the air as the soldiers cheered.  Aakhet lowered His head down next to the general and spoke in a whisper that nevertheless was perfectly audible for miles around: "Do not make a habit of this request. I prefer to give our enemies a chance."
