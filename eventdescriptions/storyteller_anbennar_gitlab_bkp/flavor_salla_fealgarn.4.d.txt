ATTENTION:  To all citizens of Salla Fealgarn, I, Ceann Oberon Fealgarn, using the emergency powers granted to me by The Great Salla, will due to extenuating circumstances, extend my tenure as your leader until the next council convocation.  It is with a heavy heart I do this as I understand the concerns of the council and the citizenry over my overstepping of boundaries. I intend to return power to the council as soon as it is safe.  You have nothing to fear. I have pledged myself to Salla Fealgarn and will give my life before either orkish hordes or a tyrant overtakes our Salla.
