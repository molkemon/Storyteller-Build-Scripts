For months, our lord Y01 Monarch has requested a personal audience with the Grand Marshal. For months, they have been denied this request. In the past, it would have been no issue for the ruler of Y01 to get the ear of the Grand Marshal; the Oni of the Demon Hills had represented the Command's primary source of Korashi, the all-important material that allows them to keep the Shamans in chains. Recently, the tide has shifted. The traditional methods of the Oni have been wiped away in the face of the Command's new chain workshops, imports making up a smaller and smaller portion of the Command's annual Korashi gain. Newly developed methods of governance have decreased the need for Korashi, even as the Command has grown exponentially in size. Our lord can see the writing on the wall, can hear the silence when they request audience, can sense the whispers in the War Room—an autonomously-governed Y01 might not be long for the world. And so they have written a letter in lieu of an audience. It begins: "Dear Grand Marshal, The Command is in the midst of a crisis no less serious than that of the Sir Revolt. The people are no longer disciplined, they study decadence instead. The younger generations learn impertinence, disobey heads of household. The Great Campaigns, once glorious and unstoppable, choke on enemies that should kneel. My Grand Marshal, the reason I write to you is to warn you: these faults have a common cause! A grand magocratic conspiracy has permeated the Command, the destructive force of Shamanism has crept into even the most high reaches of our society. If measures are not taken, the production of Korashi not reinvigorated, the Command will soon be destroyed from the inside out...
