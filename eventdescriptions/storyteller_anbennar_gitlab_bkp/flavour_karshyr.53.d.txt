While most traders from another realm leave as soon as their ships are filled to capacity with goods, one vessel has chosen to stay behind. Its captain has requested permission to establish a permanent merchant outpost in one of our cities, which he claims would benefit both the local economy and mutual relations. What he has failed to mention, however, is that this would give the traders from another realm even greater influence over local trade and make it harder to cohesively administer the city. Refusing him, however, could have significant diplomatic consequences.
