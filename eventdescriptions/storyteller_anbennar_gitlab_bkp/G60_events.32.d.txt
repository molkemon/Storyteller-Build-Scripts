Two meters in height, with a pointed edge for a pommel. A mual the size of a human head and ending with a spiked tip on top, all decorated with the most pristine of carvings depicting the tale of the shepherd who would become the first Riei. Blessed with the most powerful enchantments known to elfkind, able to split the clouds in half and shatter the earth with a single swing. Large yet lightweight, long yet not unwieldy, breaking the known laws of how a hammer this size should function. In the hands of their ruler, the hammer becomes something more. "This hammer is the Sheltering Sky, given to us by the heavens as a tool of judgment to be cast upon the unrighteous. He has given us an offering, and now we return our due to the heavens." Their ruler takes their knife and slits their wrists upon the blade, allowing their blood to pour onto the engravings. Their advisors tell them to stop, yet they continues, knowing very well that the True Kings watch overhead. As their blood falls upon the carvings, they begin to glow yellow as the final sealing is completed. "This is a tool for a king; one for healing, and one for war; a cure for the sickness across the world, and a curse for those who propagate it. Let it be passed to my children if they be worthy, and let the wicked dare touch the unmerciful penance of the one above all." Their ruler raised the hammer to the air, their wounds healed, and finished the Sheltering Sky.
