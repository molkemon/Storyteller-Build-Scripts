The long and arduous siege of the great Warcamp of Sarilavhan has ended, but perhaps more worrying than a criminal ransacking is a peaceful march into a fallen city. Despite entering under the cover of the new moon, no torches lit their way, and no men-at-arms stood in their path. Such is the fate of a war between brothers and sisters—courtesy is simply the result of these new circumstances we find ourselves in. The gates of Sarilavhan now fly the banners of the Tiger, Elephant, and Dragon with merriment and commerce abound.
