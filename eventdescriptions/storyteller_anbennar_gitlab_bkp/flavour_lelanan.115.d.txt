We travel with our mother everywhere,  
Her mission divinely ordained.  
Even as the journey is difficult,  
We could be no better trained.

My wife is a mighty woman;  
I could never be more proud.  
I clean her armor and weapons,  
So with the kids, she can be so loud.

Mom is the coolest paladin ever,  
Like a hero from a bedtime story.  
So when I grow up one day soon,  
I, too, will earn great glory!

They were the family of a paladin,  
Their lives rife with worry and stress.  
Yet in them, she found great strength,  
And their small wounds she would bless.

I thought of Leli and Kyola,  
How our own small family was the same.  
Those long nights we spend together,  
Are the source of a Paladin's flame.
