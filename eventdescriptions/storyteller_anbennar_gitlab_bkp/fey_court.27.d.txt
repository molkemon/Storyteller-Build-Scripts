A creature that looks vaguely humanoid stands before you, smiling softly in the middle of your throne room. It is tall, spindly even, with limbs that are slightly too long for its body, and a face that shines like polished rock. Noone saw it enter, and you know that noone will see it leave. Such is the nature of a Dapifer, a representative of the Everjoyous Seelie Court. More pressing than its entry is why the Dapifer is here.  It has come to offer a place for your heir as an honoured guest of the Court, so that they may be taught the Seelie ways, to learn what it means to truly enjoy and appreciate life. The underlying message goes unspoken; the Court is disappointed in you, and this is their way of fixing that. Our reputation will be tarnished should we refuse such a rare and coveted offer, but your heir will likely never be the same again… and it will be some time before they return.
