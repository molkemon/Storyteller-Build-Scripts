Since conquering the territory, we have encountered an unpleasant problem with the Shadowroot Matriarch, the great cursed tree at the heart of the Swamp. While normally quiescent, when disturbed it has a tendency to drop large figlike fruits from its boughs. These fruits, already rotted before they hit the ground, quickly bloom into mounds of mud and vine which shamble mindlessly, crushing and strangling anything they find. In order to prevent any careless wanderers from provoking these strange guardians, our forces keep a careful patrol around the tree. Recently, one of these patrols has captured a band of swamp trolls who were apparently trying to sneak past. Under questioning, they claim to be elite warriors who have earned the right to ritually tattoo themselves with sacred sap from the Matriarch, which is the highest honor a warrior can achieve in their culture. The tree, they say, only drops its guardian fruits when it feels threatened, and there is a way to perform this ritual that doesn't threaten it. On the one hand, this is a cheap way to win the loyalty of these trolls. On the other hand, they are known to be adept at deception without technically lying. Can we really trust that they won't aggravate the Matriarch?
