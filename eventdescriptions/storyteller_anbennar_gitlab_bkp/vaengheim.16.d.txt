Soon after, the wingmaidens of our ruler drew near to Elkaesal's resting place, and the cold grew bitter, striking at their wings and senses. As their quarry approached, some lost flight, tumbling to the ground, where they lay unmoving. Our ruler cursed the name of Welyam, not for the first time. Yet by flight, they reached a hollow where the cold did not bite so fiercely. Now, with their strongest wingmaidens, they heard a mighty roar not far ahead. Iona Gunnarsdottir, one of the queen's strongest, said, "The cold takes my ears, for this is no roar. The beast snores like my mother's mother, and not half as loud."
