Today our great ruler met with a loyal representative for a routine reason hardly worth mentioning. It was so routine and trivial that our ruler dealt with it so quickly they had quite some time left before they felt they had to leave. They spent that time speaking with everyone, especially those among them who were getting particularly angry at our benevolent rule. Our monarch's silver tongue worked its magic and by the time they finally left that day, all those in attendance found themselves less likely to start trouble.
