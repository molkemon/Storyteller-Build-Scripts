Throughout the Greatmarches our people rejoice. For the first time in centuries, we've finally attained freedom, and Gawed burns in our wake. However, beneath the cheers all know that eventually we'll be forced to defend ourselves again, and again, and again. There are emissaries from Adshaw offering their protection in return for our vassalization. While this would protect us from the Eagle, would we not just be bowing to another tyrant?
