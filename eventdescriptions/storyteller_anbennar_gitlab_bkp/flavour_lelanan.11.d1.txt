My blade slashed with divine power through the dense Fangaulan jungle. We tread upon dirt that had seen no Halfling footsteps and, after much travel, we found Ninakaire, where we planned to stay. We expected to find a den of monsters, the light of civilization long lost from its honeycombed walls. So we came ready for any threat, our great spears gripped tightly in our hands.

I slashed through another thick bough and emerged onto a clear hilltop, overlooking the ruins of Ninakaire Ananoma. The huge honeycomb structures brought us to a stop. As more of my paladins caught up, I could hear their gasps of awe. I smiled and set off down the hill, my divine blade ready for whatever lay ahead.
