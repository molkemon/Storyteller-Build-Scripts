Greetings, court mage. The Tempestcallers tasked with analyzing the nature of the Whirlwind have made a breakthrough. Through the tragic demise of Sheep 4 we were able to ascertain that there are strange artifacts buried in the exact spot where the sheep was hiding out. A brave crew of one Tempestcaller and 3 sheep were sent to retrieve the artifacts and they did. We did suffer 2 casualties, but their sacrifice was invaluable. The artifacts that we now have are… clearly not made by humans or harpies. They’d already own all of the plains if it were them who built them. They do bear a striking resemblance to the arcane foci that Taryebalkharr has found beyond the gate, which is quite the curious connection.Someone; we suspect the caehn or an affiliate of his; got their hands on them somehow and channeled great amounts of storm magic into them. The result is this great Vortex left behind in the wake of that war. We can’t remove it.  Now, we can’t replicate those things. But we can create imitations. They are bulky and heavy, however. So you’d only see them on great machines of war, if we were to ever build some. And provided we are able to channel magic into them.
