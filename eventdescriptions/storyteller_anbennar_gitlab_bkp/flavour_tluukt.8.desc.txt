As the siege of the capital draws to its violent conclusion, the rule of the venerable lord is cut to a grisly end. The once dignified elf is pulled from their palace to the streets, and as the city burns around them, their gnoll captors toss a sword haphazardly to their feet. The whooping laughter of countless gnoll warriors echoes through their ears as they lift with shaking hands the sword and points it towards their foe; our lord, who stands opposite them in the impromptu arena formed by a circle of spectating gnolls. The two eye each other tensely; though their back is bowed with the burdens of age, a keen cunning still burns in the eyes of the elf, and our lord takes careful note of it. Even as their pack grows reckless in their mocking laughter, our lord regards their foe with the wariness of an experienced warrior, pacing cautiously towards them with their own weapon at the ready. In a flash, the combat commences; yet in the same moment it is ended, as the life and intelligence fades from the elf’s eyes, their age rendering them no match for their opponent. Our lord merely shrugs as the body slumps to the ground and the yipping of their warpack’s laughter reaches its noisy crescendo. The news of the capital’s fall spreads quickly across Bulwar, and many fear the worst; that the elf, the guiding beacon of the New Sun Cult, would be sacrificed to the dark masters of the demonspawn on a burning pyre, as countless other elves have been. Few would be consoled to learn of their actual fate; cannibalized by our lord immediately after their duel, so that they might inherit the foresight and stability of their long rule.
