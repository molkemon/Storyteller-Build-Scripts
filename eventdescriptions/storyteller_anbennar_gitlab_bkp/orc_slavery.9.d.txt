The rediscovery of Aelantier meant the incredible demand of labour for the colonies, in this case, orks.  While many in Cannor see it as a distasteful trade it has flourished greatly in the city of Vertesk, and many of our underworld contacts have started to peddle slaves amongst their stolen wares in the auction houses of Nathalaire. Indeed, our links across the Divenhal has began to draw gnolls willing interested in partaking in this new burgeoning trade, and have set up an official gnollish slave district in the city: which the locals are calling the "Krahzate", after the gnollish word for slaver 'Krah'.
