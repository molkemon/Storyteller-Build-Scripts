O Ascending Dragon. May your death be cause for celebration, for your rebirth is at hand. excerpt from the Book of the Prime Discordance
The body is such a fragile thing. Delicate, and prone to failure, it simply cannot be trusted to last an Acolyte for eternity. Nevertheless, the soul must be chained to one of these vessels of flesh to continue affecting the physical world, and so our leader has begun the process of growing an empty shell to move into upon their death.
