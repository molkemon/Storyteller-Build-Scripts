After years of warfare, the last great resistance of the Xiaken has been defeated. Every local group of monkinsurgents have been exterminated, their refuges razed or left abandoned. The region has been decimated by warfare and reprisals; Xiaken monks and traitorcollaborators alike have been strung up in the gallows by the dozens, supervised by the oni and the oni alone. There are more collaborators, of course, but the days of a long leash are over. Unburied dead litter the lands, a gruesome display of the battles over the fate of the Xia. It is hard to say if the region will ever fully recover from its punishment.  Then again, it is unlikely they will ever have the strength to rebel again.
