Lately in a gnomish province there have been a small group of gnomes who’ve made an invention of a very impressive nature. While the work is clearly a prototype, it has proven highly useful for the business done within the province. However, the gnomes who constructed it want us to not spread it further than they allow, claiming that it’d be an insult to their work if it spread far and wide before it was ready. While they have little say now in what we’ll do, we may take into consideration their opinion.
