"Take that, you lying bearded bastards." Our lord shouts as they pour over stone tablets dating even before the foundation of AulDwarov. There, hidden deep within the annals of the ages past they have discovered a marvelous bit of history: a goblin named Stuka of our line has been proven to be the founder of one of our holds. A holdwide celebration is held as our lord parades through his rightful inheritance, cheered on by their loyal subjects.
