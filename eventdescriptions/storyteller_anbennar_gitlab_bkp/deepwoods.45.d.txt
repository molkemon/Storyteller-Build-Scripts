The crowd erupts a thunderous roar as the first vessel reaches the opposite shores of Silent Lake. At its helm is none other than their lord. In the coming hours, a steady stream of boats find themselves greeted by the same elated crowd. As day turns to night and the feasting subsides, the no longer sober elves gather around the victorious lord. The crowd erupts one last time as an elderly elf places a wreath of Moon Lilies upon their lord. This year's Moonsinger has been crowned.
