A family of new arrivals came from so far to the east, traveling from beyond the ocean, and could not quite partake in our feast. There were a dozen of them, strangely elven in design. Neither eating nor sleeping nor breathing, with hearts that were most benign. Communication was fraught, yet we never lost our hope. Eventually, they explained that they were much more than a homunculus trope. A great big family they were, even without the need to produce life, and together they had survived and endured insya's deadly strife. We presented a gift for their stories, as they settled in very quickly, something that reminded us of them, an everburning candlestick.
