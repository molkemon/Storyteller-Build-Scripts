We have been under fire from all sides. The death of Sage King Jeong-Hun Gyo was a sad day for our realm. Since his fall, the provinces of our once proud domain have been at each other's throats, as well as bristling against the orders of our ruler. But in our most dire straits, a light in the dark appeared. Through skill and diplomacy, we have managed to win back the loyalty of one of our provinces. They are willing to stand proudly by the side of our ruler and vouch for their heir, and tutor them in the art of statecraft. The storm is not over yet, but the rain has begun to lull.
