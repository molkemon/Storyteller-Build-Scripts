The first expedition's planning is underway! Our choices lie between two options. First, we can explore the city of Kunolo, a historically significant halfling settlement. This region has seen many cultural shifts, and Kunolo remains a prime example: it is home to people from every background. We may also be invited to delve into the past of the region, uncovering lost knowledge about Kheteratan and the halfling people!

Alternatively, we can visit the human-inhabited city of Ibidintely. This city is highly defensive against its neighboring states, and we're unsure if it remains independent. Regardless, the residents are eager to share their knowledge in exchange for some supplies. Here, halfling society thrives.

Pick wisely, my lord!
