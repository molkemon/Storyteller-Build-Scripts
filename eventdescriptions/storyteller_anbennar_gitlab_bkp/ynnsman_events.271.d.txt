An official from Argezvale, following his natural dwarven instinct, was prospecting in a nearby region around the barracks, and discovered a hitherto unknown iron vein. The dwarf has claimed that it can only be mined using a special dwarven technique. Thankfully, he has offered to bring in expert dwarven miners. However, he will only do so if a weapons manufactory designed and run by the Balgarsmiths is built within the province.
