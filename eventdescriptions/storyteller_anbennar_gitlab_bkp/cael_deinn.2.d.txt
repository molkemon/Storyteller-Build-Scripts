"The old Castanites believed there were seven sins. Pride. Envy. Sloth. Wrath. Greed. Gluttony. Lust. Corresponding to each was a being of unbelievable evil; traitorous gods who had risen against the Gods’ rightful ruler, Castellos.  Cast down and trapped in the underworld, these fallen beings whispered to the ears of unwitting mortals. They promised power in exchange for taking up their dark cause. And so the Infernal Court was born…  Hah, what ludicrous drivel.  Oh, the stories the creatures of Halann spin. Seven sins? Rebellions and fallen gods? The underworld? Such fanciful fantasies.  Ah, but I shouldn’t entirely dismiss such tall tales. There is some truth to them. We do tend to whisper sweet promises. How could we not? Desire rules the heart. It is simply a matter of time before the heart kneels before it.  And in turn, all hearts will kneel before me. For I, Iabhal, am all you crave and more."
