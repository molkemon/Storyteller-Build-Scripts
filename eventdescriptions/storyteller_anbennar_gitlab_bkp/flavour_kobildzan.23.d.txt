One of our head researchers, a dragon-priest and artificer, has gone rogue. He insists that we lack one of the most notable draconic traits—greed—and that if we are ever to shed our status as "kobolds" rather than "dragons," we must also hoard for ourselves. To achieve this, he has stolen mass quantities of artificed research and money. He is constantly experimenting on himself, siphoning massive resources toward himself to achieve ever-greater rewards. He insists that his research—on turning himself into the greatest, most powerful kobold alive—will allow us all to follow his steps, but many are unsure. Can we really trust the self-proclaimed "Prophet of Greed" to help us in the end?
