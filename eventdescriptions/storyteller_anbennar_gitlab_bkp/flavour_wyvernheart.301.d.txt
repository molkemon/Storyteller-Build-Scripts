The Floodborn Council is anything but a united group; members of the councils belong to at least one or two cliques; they are not impervious to pettiness and selfinterest. But among these squabbling cliques, a few set themselves apart by their beliefs, by their means, and mostly by their influence within the council room. Even though they are floodborn, the "Scions" of rich merchants and aristocrats have not forgotten their roots and lobby heavily for their family's interest. While some, more "Ambitious" or strict "Hardliners" floodborn want to push the council to action, the former for their own interest and reputation and the latter for the efficiency of the realm. There are the "Technocrats", those that have begun to experiment frenetically, leaving behind everything for the sake of knowledge and power. While the "Heartmages" are adventurers first and floodborn second. The one thing that brings them together is magic and its embodiment, the Sovereign. All respect the Sovereign in different ways, be it as a nobleman and arbiter, but a few esotericists have devoted themselves to the Flood as a religious entity and serve the council as the Flood’s "Devouts".  As most of the appointed councillors take their seats, the different groups become rather apparent in the stands. Each group organises around their representative, who will present their agenda in the coming session; looks are exchanged between the representatives, gauging each other; alliances and betrayals are commonplace in this room. Who knows what today’s session will bring forth?
