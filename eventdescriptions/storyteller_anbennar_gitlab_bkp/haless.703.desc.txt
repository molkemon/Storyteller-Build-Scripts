The Goldscale Kobolds are renowned across the realm for their expertise in mountain trade routes. The golden scales they bear shimmer brilliantly under the sun, serving not only as a symbol of identity but also as a mark of credit and wealth. Their so-called "Goldscale Crafts" are crafted with ingenious mechanisms and rare mountain materials. Unique in design and craftsmanship, these goods are highly popular at markets and gatherings, beloved by both commoners and merchants alike.

Recently, a prominent kobold trade guild from the domain surrounding the Temple of Jinxiou has, for the first time, formally addressed the local authorities with a proposal to establish a mutual trade agreement. They suggest dispatching a delegation to reside permanently in the major towns of the Yanzhong region. The aim is to build a more stable supply network, expand their distribution channels, and promote closer commercial exchange. The guild promises to bring with them skilled Goldscale artisans, rare mountain goods, and finely crafted wares—injecting new vitality into the local economy.

This is the first time a kobold trade guild has proposed a partnership of this scale, drawing significant attention from local officials. Most view it favorably, believing it could broaden the market and boost tax revenue. However, some voices of concern have been raised: if a foreign merchant guild is allowed to take root locally, might they eventually control the arteries of commerce and marginalize native merchants in both livelihood and influence?
