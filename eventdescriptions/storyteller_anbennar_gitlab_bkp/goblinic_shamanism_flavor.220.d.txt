The journey to Khetdom is a long and arduous one, and many are simply not capable of the sheer force of will required to stay stationary for the many hours a day that are necessary for becoming a True Khet.  Not long ago, word reached our ruler of a goblin who claimed they were on the brink of becoming a True Khet, a level few goblins ever truly reach. However, it appears these rumors have been greatly exaggerated as today we have received word that said goblin has been found dead, likely due to starvation. While some claim this was due to their refusal to move even to eat, many know the truth: their death proves that they broke their vows of slothfulness and were not capable of ever truly gaining the powers of a Khet.
