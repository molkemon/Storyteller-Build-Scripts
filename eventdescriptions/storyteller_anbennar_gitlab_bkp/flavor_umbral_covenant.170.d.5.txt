When Umorozi returns for the fifth time, she no longer needs food, drink, or sleep. The enemies of the inevitable chose to cremate what remained of Umorozi's body this time. They carved away and removed the fog-parts once more, perhaps burying or disenchanting them. The Vile One looks upon their efforts and laughs, for now she understands the power of memory. Four times before, she has resurrected Umorozi, learned, and rebuilt her form. It is not much more difficult now to create her anew from nothing. No mortal death can reach the great general now. When Umorozi strikes from the endless fog, she is entirely invisible; her whisper can reach halfway across Sarhal, so long as she can conceptualize the recipient.

Her tall frame still drinks light—look closely at the fog-form, and you can see the gentle sway of those long banded braids behind Umorozi. The Vile One would never have forgotten those.
