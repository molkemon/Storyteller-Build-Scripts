Members of the Furdanhund have approached the Obsidian Council with news of the latest siege. They report that our glorious armies have taken control of the fortress, but they have taken prisoner a large amount of undesirables in the process. Normal protocol would be the complete annihilation of the prisoners to rally the troops, but some fear that the international backlash in doing so would provoke a strong reaction from our neighbours, that may not be strategically desirable.
