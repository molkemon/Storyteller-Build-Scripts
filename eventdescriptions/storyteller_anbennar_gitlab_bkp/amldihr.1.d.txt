The Dwarven Lords of the West Dwarovar race to Amldihr, all having received an emergency call for assembly. The Amlharazs Halls fill with a riot of grumbling dwarves, a normal day at the Assembly in all respects. The chatter breaks swiftly at the clashing of the lords’ silverware and goblets. The lord speaks bluntly, as they always do, on the current state of affairs in Amldihr. Their practiced rhetoric breaks, however, as they move on to the topic of AulDwarov. In candid honesty the success of Amldihr is laid out. West Dwarovar is under Amldihr influence, the Lords of the Assembly answer to Amldihrs lord, relative peace has become the norm after centuries of orkish incursions. Is this all we can achieve? No, they state without a hint of doubt. A sense of what was is returning under our efforts, and what was, is AulDwarov.
