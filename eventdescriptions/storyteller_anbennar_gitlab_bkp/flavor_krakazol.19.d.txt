As the lord tours the many taverns of our realm, one of the taverns is recovering from a massive barfight. Shards of glass and broken chairs are everywhere and the establishment is barely left standing with many dwarfsized holes in the walls. The lord, along with his entourage, decide to assist in cleaning up what is left of the place. With all the shards of glass and splinters of wood gathered up, the drunk lord demands it all be thrown into Stramolgiv. Everyone looks confused as they all think the lord has finally lost it and gone mad, but then the lord speaks again and corrects that only the shards of glass are meant to be used as an ingredient and not the wood, as the splinters should be reserved for the fires of the brewery. This doesn't clear any of the confusion but now the, also drunk, court mage says it's a brilliant idea, too. The lord then singlehandedly names the drink Andlraezbit. It is still debated if the name means Shattered Nose and is a reference to the bar fight or if the lord was just too drunk that night to speak clearly. The lord hasn’t commented on the matter as they remember none of it.
