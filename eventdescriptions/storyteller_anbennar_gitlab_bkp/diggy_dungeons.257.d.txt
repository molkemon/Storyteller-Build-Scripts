As the expeditionaries round the corner of the cavern, they are met with a strange sight. A great banquet hall, with three large tables covered with food and drink. Around one of them sit a group of satyrs, merrily eating and drinking. They appear to be glad to have company, and happily invite the expeditionaries to join them at the table. Some already do. It is unclear, however, where all this wine and food would have come from, and it is hard to shake the feeling that none of this is real.
