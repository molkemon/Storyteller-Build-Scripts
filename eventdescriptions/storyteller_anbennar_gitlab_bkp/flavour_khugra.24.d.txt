Yusshela, the Celestial Dolphin, is a blessed and powerful Araizary, who has historically granted great boons and blessings to those deserving. Today, the ruler is going to become one of these fortunate individuals, as a reward for the continued reverence and worship of the Araizary in Khugra. They enter the sea alone, as the constellation of the Celestial Dolphin shines brightly in the night sky on this moonless night. A shining shape darts in the distance at amazing speed. From the soft light it emits, our ruler identifies it as the envoy of Yusshela, however only when it is within reach does the elegant form of a swordfish become visible. The animal slows down enough for our ruler to place a hand on its back. The moment contact is made, the light that composes its body becomes blinding. When their eyes open, the beast is gone, only the light of the evershining stars breaking the darkness. The ruler swims back to the shore, where royal advisors await eager to listen to their retelling of the experience. The ruler feels the power of the boon flowing through their body, and the changes in their personality are evident. They have received the Blessing of the Swordfish, a boon of great sharpness.
