Chapter 2: A Meeting With Understanding Yields Fruitful Results.  The story goes on to tell of how the lord met with the Man of Understanding, who knew the way to that immortal place where elves and dwarves had once forged great artifacts. The Man of Understanding led a following of a great many disciples, and so an audience was taken. When the lord approached the wise master, they bowed their head low and asked a simple question: "Where is the way to the land of the elves and dwarves, where once they forged great artifacts?"  The patriarch responded: "There are threehundred and sixty ways, and they all lead to Enlightenment. Perhaps I should teach you the Way of Gold and Cinnabar, which is the pursuit of all kings."  "Will the Way of Gold and Cinnabar direct me to the place of the great elven silversmiths and dwarven blacksmiths once resided?"  "Certainly not," responded the great master, "The Way of Gold and Cinnabar will show you the route to true immortality. It involves abstaining from grain, perfect chastity, eating vegetarian food, wearing hairthorn shirts, and an understanding of death."
