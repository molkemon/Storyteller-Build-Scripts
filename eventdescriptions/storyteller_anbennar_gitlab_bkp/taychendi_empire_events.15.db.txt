The Ravelian Society is perhaps one of the more interesting organizations that have emerged from the realms of Cannor. Dedicated to uncovering the secrets of Halann through reason and scientific investigation, they are unravellers by nature, and the field of magic and extraplanar research is one they have spent considerable effort exploring. In particular, sections of the Society believe they have found the tools to deliver the power of magic to the people—were we to support the society, we might be able to gain access to this knowledge. 

There is one problem, of course—slavery. The Ravelians do not favor the practice. But the lure of gold can blind even the most learned minds, and the allure of Taychend may well be too tempting for the Society to pass up, despite any moral reservations.
