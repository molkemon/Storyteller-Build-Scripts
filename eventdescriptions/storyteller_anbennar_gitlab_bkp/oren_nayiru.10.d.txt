Most slaves, naturally, crave freedom—and the Oren Nayiru has given them an unprecedented opportunity to realize that. Utilizing the extensive resources of the faith, many slaves have escaped bondage and formed maroon communities in the many hills of Taychend, defending their freedom with their lives and launching raids of liberation and vengeance from their hill-top communities. Breaking these entrenched communities is extremely costly in blood and treasure, but if we are to retain control of our realm it is nonetheless extremely important.
