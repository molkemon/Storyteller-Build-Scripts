\"Every construct must have a power source, something that gives it life.\" Beginner's Guide to Constructs  In the case of homunculi, it is known as the Control Heart. Something that is beyond a simple origin of energy, but instead something that acts as the living core of the being. The heart, despite its moniker, also functions as the brain. Without it, the homunculus cannot achieve true sentience.  Creating the Control Heart is the most timeconsuming part of the homunculi process, and requires great amounts of magical energies and spells to imbue the heart with an artificial soul.
