A struck match in an offtunnel, and darkness gave way to light. Five elves met - all slaves, but only one still in bondage, and one able to free him. The slavepriest held two hands up. One palm out, sabresteel in the other - a rare sight, in the mines. "Do you still hold the fire of freedom within you?" Kal remembered when his warmth came from the sun above, not the fires of the earth. "I do." "Does the rage of righteous hatred still course through your blood?" Kal remembered the first time he felt the bite of his chains, the first lash of the whip. His fist clenched, lips turned to scowl. "I do." "And do you swear to carry that fire, that rage, within you? Until your last dying breath, or that of the last slaver to stain this world?" Kal did not need to remember to answer; "I do." The slavepriest smiled. "Then hold out your hand, and shed your last blood as an unfree soul."

The lord was playing with one of his companions when one of his lieutenants walked in. He only acknowledged him with a glance, before he turned back to his closer ally before him - he was teaching the warkompu how to leap, tear throats. "There have been some troubling reports. miners going missing in the offtunnels. Consistently. Its starting to become a drain." The lord laughed, as his lieutenant froze in fear. "Miners die all the time - they were probably fed to the swarm. You're missing slaves? Get more."
