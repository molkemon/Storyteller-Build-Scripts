So saith THE CULT:  Our DEMAGOGUE is known to be quite weak For all the finer things, but mostly wine. He cannot help it, he is from Kimanis. The weaknesses he shares are also mine.  Though we be drunk, we have composed a plan To help him see the goals he shares with us. At his events and many public functions We watched him from the crowd with rheumy eyes. At last. We found ourselves a likely chance To slip the DEMAGOGUE a note to read One of his magi calls himself our man His magic keeps us duly entertained Sometimes his visions do not wholly work And yet we cheer him onward anyway.
