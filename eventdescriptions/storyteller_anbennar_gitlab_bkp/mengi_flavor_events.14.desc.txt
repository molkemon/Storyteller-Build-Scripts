It starts as a whisper, creeping through the fields and furrows of our realm like a weed. And then the slaves start to disappear. At first, it isn't a problem. You dispatch some catchers, and like any minor nuisance, you expect it to be resolved in quick order. And then the catchers start to disappear. Our advisors quickly conclude that there must be some monster afoot, and so dispatch some Riyitewag, who are well equipped with hunting and defeating such beasts. Then the adventurers disappear as well. One evening, as you finish a tirade at your captains and generals about how useless they're being, a hill on the outskirts of our capital is lit up, brighter than the midday sun. You shield your eyes from the almost blinding light, but you can still hear the chant: "Zurel Will Bring Light. Zureal Will Bring Freedom."
