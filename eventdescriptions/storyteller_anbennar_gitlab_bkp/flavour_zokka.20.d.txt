As within, so without. Zokka hears Zokka. Not in words, or thought. In understanding. A presence, immeasurable and unlimited, curls through their veins like the felflame, just as novel. Zokka, the first. Zokka, the last. Zokka, the Once And Future. Zokka who was, who is, who shall be. The moment passes. Zokka's breath returns, but it is no longer their own. Something has taken root. A gift. An idea. A purpose. As within, so without; one way or another.
