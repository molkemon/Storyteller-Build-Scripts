A heated debate breaks out among the expedition over the expedition leader's proposed incentive. Many of the soldiers are willing to move on with a small pay rise, but a group of mages are loudly suggesting that they should get more of the final loot. With some honeyed words and whispered promises of extra payment for those who side with the expedition leader, most of the mages recant their complaints. The remaining stubborn mages demand a vote on the matter. After more time wasted organising a vote, the expedition leader wins by a wide margin.
