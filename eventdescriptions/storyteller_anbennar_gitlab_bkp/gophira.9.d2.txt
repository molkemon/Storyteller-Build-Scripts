Time has passed since the Darkalyndar Succession War was fought, and the following decision to keep Darkalyndar as an independent ally made: circumstances have changed and at least some of the protagonists of that decision are deceased, it's time to revisit it. The rulers of both realms are close relatives, in good relations, but it's now needed to have the two realms under the same rule, to avoid the risk of them drifting apart or worse, engaging in wars. Having two rulers with a valid claim on each other's title is not a good recipe for peace. Lord J72 graciously agreed to step down, and our lord will be the ruler of both Darkalyndar and Gophira. Positions of power will have to be exchanged, reparations given, but it is a small price to pay.
