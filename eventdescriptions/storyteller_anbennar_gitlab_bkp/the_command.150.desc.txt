When the Oni Rashenbir arrived in our capital to pledge his people's unending fealty as slaves to the Command, he also came with a suggestion. In his travels, he had seen how hobgoblin soldiers and administrators steered clear of the many conquered High Temples throughout the realm. He argued that this was dangerous because the temples held immense magical power. With a glint of excitement behind his dark eyes, he assured Grand Marshal our ruler that the Oni were more than equipped to guard and control these dangerous temples, just as they assist with supplying Korashi. And if the Grand Marshal only asked, he would dispatch Oni experts to these high temples to secure the safety of all.
