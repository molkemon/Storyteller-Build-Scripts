A note from our advisor, our lord, has been found among the lord's papers. "My lord, I have always served you and our realm to the best of my ability. However, it is with a heavy heart that I must announce my resignation as your advisor. The declaration of war on their country has crossed a line; I cannot remain silent in the face of your crimes against my religious brethren any longer. Yours sincerely, our advisor." Attempts to locate them have so far proven unsuccessful. It appears that they have fled the country.
