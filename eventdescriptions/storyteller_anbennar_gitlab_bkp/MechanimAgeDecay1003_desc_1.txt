Eyes are the window to one's soul, but what happens when said window closes? Once, I was able to catch every detail, to see the hidden meaning in words and actions. When the times of trouble come, they ask me to guide, to decide the strategies the realm should follow to victory—once, I could guide them, but long gone are those days. My eyes are not what they used to be; their receptors have begun to fail as the glass in front has been scratched and become less and less clear. Should I seek to fix them, or should I seek to rest them and allow a new ruler to take up my place?
