To be Steward of the Sorrow, you must prove yourself worthy as being one who can protect the people of the Sorrow. Their leader clasped the armor together around their back. "You must be noble, thoughtful, and diligent in managing the resources of the Sorrow to ensure its people have roofs over their head, temples to worship Elikhet at, and food to fill their belies. However, you must also be fearless in the face of danger, lead from the front when your people need it most, and prove righteous in your defence of the Mother’s Sorrow. "Now more than ever, your people need you. As Elikhet too once did, you too must take up arms and defend this great empire. This empire cannot fall so long as you have the strength of Elikhet at your side. So too will you have the strength of me and all the Ahati at your side." The Ahati general placed their shoulder. "Ride out with me and meet this demon head on. Human and gnolll, side by side, charging at the world's ending. What say you?"
