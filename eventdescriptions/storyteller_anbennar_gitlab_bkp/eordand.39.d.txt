Worlds apart, yet so close; the Domandrod and the Deepwoods are so alike. Walking through the Feyrealm, one can reach the other at ease. From the Deepwoods the Wood Elves have been stewards of the forest, yet they do not follow the Fey Lords. They preserve the harmony of nature, yet know no allegiance to season or balance of season.  Across the world, the Deepwoods are threatened by invaders and ruin at every turn. The trees cut and burned, squatted by Green men known as orks and Goblins, the Wood Elves need assistance to protect nature, imploring we aid them. But did the Fey Lords not promise us stewardship of the Domandrod and the feytouched lands beyond? The Wood Elves know no greater allegiance. In Aelantier and the lands beyond, we will protect what is ours.
