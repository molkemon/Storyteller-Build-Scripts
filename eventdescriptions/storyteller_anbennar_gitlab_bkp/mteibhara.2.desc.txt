With the unfortunate passing of the ruler, the Syrhana Delathan has convened to choose their successor. The Syrhana Delathan must agree by a two-thirds margin to elect the next Rabulath-Sholanshaz, or Governor-Commander, and electoral debates can last months. The candidates are typically chosen from the most influential of the realm, but it is not unusual for unknown figures to capture the attention of the constituent Delaths. For a candidate to use unsavory means to win the election would not be unusual, but would certainly have consequences.
