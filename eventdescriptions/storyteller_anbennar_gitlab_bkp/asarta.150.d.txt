A thick smog of incense filled the room where the Court of Change resided. At its center, laid out on an array of cushions, sat Akhaxpa. As the ruler approached and knelt down, she opened her three eyes, filling the room with a dim blue light. "They are in danger," Akhaxpa spoke in a softer tone. Behind the light of her eyes, dark slitted pupils could be seen moving. "Other merfolk are not happy with their actions. Not for saving your sailors, but something else. A forbidden magic. Ostracization is not enough."

Those in Karassk are safe, but not by the island of Cheneskad. If you do not act, they will all die. Every single one will be slaughtered, their village destroyed, and their children's corpses left to rot on the seafloor. Akhaxpa paused, then the bright blue light behind her eyes grew brighter and her voice subtly changed. "They fear change under the sea. They won't attack yet, as they lack allies. Time is on your side. With your help, change both above and below sea level can be achieved."
