Visatma is a universal principle in High Philosophy thought; the fluid Atma which flows between beings and the world at large. Yet the specifics are always a matter of debate. Golden Palace thought holds that it must be maintained through the actions of empires and good rulers, while the Starry Eye school holds that all individuals must maintain it through the growth of their personal Atma. Suhan stands opposed to both of these schools of thought. Suhan believes that visatma simply is. There is no reason for the individual to concern themselves with its wellbeing. It will fluctuate with the seasons, the ways of the world, and the acts of man, but in time it will right itself once more. To turn back the clock of the reformation is impossible, yet the damage to visatma can be undone; what form does that work take? The kind words of local philosophers, each trying to convert those they can back to our doctrine? Or is it the strength of the ruler who, through force, will return stability?
