It is a fine winter morning in the year now, and our lord has found themselves inexcusably accosted with demands from the hobgoblins to join their second war against the people of Sir. Our lord looks out on the foggy morning sky, an awareness of dawn peeking through the cloudy mists of the Demon Hills. They think of the Aingjada who are currently wearing different colors and flying the banner of Sir. They think of the personal assistant they dispatched to lead the Siri forces, a bright young thing named Chiyi Kwaeng. They think of the girl's body, clasped in Korashi chains and dragged before the ruler of R62, R62 Monarch. They specify "body," because the chances of a living mage as powerful as her being allowed in front of the ruler of R62 are slim to none. Then they imagine it is not Chiyi Kwaeng’s body but their own in such a position, the Oni treachery unveiled, the lord a corpse trampled under hobgoblin boots. All of this the lord considers, and then finally gestures for ink and parchment.
