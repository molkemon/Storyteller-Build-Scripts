As Kanolowele and the Utubodi Plateau have grown closer, both regions have exchanged goods, culture, and most importantly ideas. It has been a time of religious flourishing, and believers in the Volcanic Truth have made their way across the continent to spread their faith.  As they have, they themselves have been influenced by ideas of the Infinite Self. From dialogues of the Volcanic Truth with followers of the Speaking Memory and Infinite Self religions, an entirely new religion has emerged with influences of all three faiths. They call themselves the Molten Eternity, and they believe that the reincarnation of all things of the Infinite Self is not guaranteed to lead to a benevolent Great Creator: depending on whether the actions of the Universal Soul are good or evil, the last being born in the chain of souls could just as easily be the malevolent Great Destroyer.  Therefore, it is the duty of all practitioners to follow the ideals of the Molten Eternity towards good, forget those who do evil, and spread the faith outwards as far and wide as possible to make sure all incarnations of the Universal Soul have the chance to do good in their lifetimes.  The Molten Eternity have gathered in our city to found a community based on their faith from which they can spread across Insyaa. This community happens to be within our borders: do the Molten Eternity have a point?
