Bulwar always has been a crossroad of cultures, civilizations, and ideas, and that is reflected in the different schools of magic that are present in our land.  The Bulwari have a long tradition and expertise in the more gnostic disciplines of Divination and Conjuration, preserved by the rogue magi that live in the remote areas of Bulwar, and by the reclusive Masnsih tribe of the szalFazil.  While our future magi study all schools of magic, we need to decide in which school they will focus, based on our country's needs.  Will we need glimpses to the future through Divination, or the charms of Conjuration?
