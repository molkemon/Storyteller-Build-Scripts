Calasandur was known as one of the greatest builders and craftsmen of his time. He founded the House of Silcalas, and built the three masterwork elven castles of Aelcandar, Escandar and Calascandar. While Azaly Stonecarver, our most prominent Vyzemby architect, grumbles that he was a hack and that the Cannorian ‘masterpieces’ do not even remotely compare to the halfling Ananomas or lizardfolk spires in Sarhal, we have it on good authority that several of his old blueprints have been uncovered. These blueprints could be a wonderful addition to Medaka’s Exceptional Treasures.
