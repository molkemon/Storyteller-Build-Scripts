Fungi, although often used in the caves of the Serpentspine Mountains as a staple crop, much like wheat or barley on the surface, has never seen much demand beyond the mountains. This has changed in recent years, however, as various remedies have been discovered which make use of the various mushrooms, curing and treating various ailments which have plagued the surface world for generations. As such, the demand for fungi has gone up greatly in recent months.
