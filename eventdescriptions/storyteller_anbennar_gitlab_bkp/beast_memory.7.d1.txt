Brave, wise, and noble is the Mongoose. With tooth and leap and claw it defends all that is entrusted to it, driving away the darkness and the predators that would have their way with its turf. Bold and ambitious, it chases away venomous vipers and cobras as a broom sweeps away dust. With charisma and pride it serves as an exemplar for all those that wish to broadly expand their domain, leading from the frontlines to establish peace. Above all else, emulate their ruler.
