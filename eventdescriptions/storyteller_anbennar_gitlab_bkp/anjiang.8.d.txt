As we've pushed further into the Oniheld lands to our west, the demons have appeared in evergreater numbers, some even masquerading as civilians in an effort to trick our soldiers into offering mercy.  Our warriors are made of more stern stuff, though, and have resisted such trickery. Evergrowing piles of Oni are set alight by our forces; tragically, alongside their thralls that fight for them even after their masters' deaths.
