Having expanded our digging operations at Esuvrem, we have uncovered another section of the vast palatial complex and a fresh hoard of enchanted steel to scrap. While the steel itself is useful, the real treasure is the Dames tear we can extract from it during the reforging process that can then be used to make more practical enchanted items of our own. While it surely won't last forever, this latest haul will keep us supplied with Dames tear for quite some time.
