The Yanzhin League is an implacable bunch. Even after the state has long since been annihilated, their agents continue to strike and harass our agents. This is no matter of harassment and hostility; this betrays a calculated and highly organized attempt to drive out our magistrates and collaborators. Attempting to wrestle any worthwhile value out of these lands is a waste of time, at least until the League can be fully destroyed. Is it worth the investment?
