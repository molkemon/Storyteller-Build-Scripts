The walls of the capital city are vast and menacing. A squadron of soldiers, clad head to toe in black, march the rebel leader Liandiel down the hall. Despite the oppressive aura, the elf walks tall and proud. The group enters Lucian’s throne room and Liandiel is shoved to the floor. She picks herself up quickly and stares at the lord of the night. Liandiel speaks: "My name is Liandiel Moonsong, and I am the one who led the ill-advised uprising against your rule. I have seen the error of my ways and I have come to beg for a chance to rectify my crimes. I am a skilled general—and also a trained diplomat. I would be glad to serve your majesty for the rest of my life. All I ask in return is that the lives of my fellow people be spared and that they be allowed to live in peace." The silence stretches on for minutes. Liandiel stares, unwavering, at the dark figure perched on the throne. Finally, Lucian replies, "Your skills are obvious—as is your great beauty. I think there is a very important position for you in the government. Not as my bride—but something far more skilled. You shall be a diplomat indeed, along with any of your compatriots who are suited for it. There is just one… adjustment that must be made first." Lucian bares his fangs and rises from his seat. "Your time of service will be very long indeed…"
