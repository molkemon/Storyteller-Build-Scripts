To the High Commanders,  I submit my report concerning recent incursions in the tunnels of Middle Serpentspine. The dragon has been seen moving through the region, and the devastation wrought by Her has opened new passageways. It seems our enemies have wasted no time in exploiting them.  I have personally observed multiple goblin bands moving through the newly fractured corridors. Their numbers are hard to estimate due to their erratic movements, but I would place them at no fewer than twenty to thirty thousand in total, divided into small raiding groups. These goblins are poorly equipped but numerous enough to pose a danger to any isolated survivors or lightly defended outposts.  More troubling is the sighting of an ork warband. This group is far more organised, numbering approximately nine thousand warriors, heavily armed and seemingly scouting for weaknesses in our defences. Their presence is a grave concern.  Other scouts have reported seeing stranger creatures emerging from the depths; shadows with too many limbs, whispers that move like smoke. I am skeptical of these accounts, as fear and exhaustion cloud many minds, but I include them for completeness.  Reconnaissance in the region is incomplete; the unstable terrain and constant threat of attack hinder our efforts. I recommend immediate reinforcement of tunnel security and dedicated patrols to monitor these incursions.  ScoutSergeant Gruar Gemcraver
