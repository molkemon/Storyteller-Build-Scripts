The Dhenbasana is littered with palaces, funded by embezzled taxes and tariffs as well as withheld tributes, bribes to look past such crimes, or (in rare cases) genuine success and wise rule. No two are identical, but almost all share one feature: a wellconcealed escape route. Their lord is reminded of this fact as they hear today's report from a messenger dressed in a strange red and white hat. Our forces were victorious and the conquered palace quickly seized, but the Raja escaped like smoke on the wind. "Apologies, their lord", he concludes, "But your Raja is in another palace." No matter. They could not outrun them, the stream cannot outrace the sea. Run as they likes, we will soon capture the Raja, and with them, the Lotus Throne.
