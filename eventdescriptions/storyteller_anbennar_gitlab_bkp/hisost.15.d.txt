The Awirenya are the most elite force in the country, possessing not just the great strength of other warrior elites but also truly noble hearts. It has been very embarrassing for the Yenaze Mezhi that these champions of our realm have nothing but scorn for its current rulers. After forging unbreakable bonds with their gnollish allies upon the battlefield, they immediately rejected the Yenaze Mezhi’s lies about gnolls being inferior to humans. To make their point, every last one of them laid down their arms and refused to serve the Yenaze Mezhi. It has come as a shock to many: most Awirenya come from noble backgrounds themselves yet it seems their brothers in arms are more dear to them than their brothers in blood. With the heart of our army being ripped out, the Yenaze Mezhi’s ability to enact their policies has been severely hampered and it has proved to be disastrous for their image amongst the general populace.
