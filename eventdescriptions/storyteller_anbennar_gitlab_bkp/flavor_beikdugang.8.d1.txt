Horror. That is the only word to describe the bloodbath that beset our expedition. Our men encountered a faceless creature near the edge of the mountain domain, and as soon as they began to murmur among themselves about it, the creature seemed to become enraged and changed into the form of a whirlwind. The soldiers prepared a defensive line, but it ultimately proved worthless. Seemingly from nowhere, several more of the spirits had emerged from behind the terrain, and our forces were beset on multiple sides. What followed was utterly gruesome; the vast majority of those on the expedition were slaughtered, with many dying from having their faces ripped off their bodies by the wrathful spirits. What few returned have warned all who would listen to never venture into those cursed lands. Many have regarded this expedition as a disaster.
