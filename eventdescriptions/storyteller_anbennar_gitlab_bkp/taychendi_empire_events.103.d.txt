The lives of slaves are nearly uniformly terrible—though the extent of the horror varies. Slave-servants to wealthy merchants or warlords often endure daily abuse and confinement. Slaves on the personal plantations and agricultural estates of the extremely wealthy are frequently whipped as an incentive. If a slave is sent to a mine, it is almost certain they will be worked to death. No wonder, then, that when a slave sees an opportunity to escape, they will usually take it. As the Kheionai discovered, when the slave population is large, the conditions for escape and revolt grow—seemingly, our current situation has led to just that. Across our realm, slaves rise in revolt, each successful uprising encouraging more. We will have to put this down with brutal force.
