The Ice Field is a spell which creates a large, intense, blizzard anchored to a single location where it will grow to encompass an entire region. Our IceSmiths first used it in the Northern Pass to create the great blizzard there and the experiment was a great success. They say that with enough new towers in other regions, they could create similar effects elsewhere.  Northern Pass: All original mage towers are intact West Dwarovar: Twelve mage towers Serpentreach: Eight mage towers Middle Dwarovar: Nine mage towers Tree of Stone: Seven mage towers Jade Mines: Six mage towers
