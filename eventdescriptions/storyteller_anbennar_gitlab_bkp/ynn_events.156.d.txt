Wise and kind eyes watch the lord from the floor of the court. A small, longhaired white tail sweeps back and forth in a patient and polite manner. Before the lord is sitting a Stenurynn Teriyer puppy, a gift from a lord of another nation. The Teriyers are famous for their elegance and social intelligence and are valued pets of many upperclass nobles. The best puppies are sometimes gifted to valued allies or other friends of the court, as such this gesture shows deep faith between our nations.
