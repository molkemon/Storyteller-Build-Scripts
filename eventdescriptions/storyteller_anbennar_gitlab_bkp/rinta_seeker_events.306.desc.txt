Adeen with magical abilities are an anomaly, and there is no clear precedent on how to handle them. Currently, magically gifted Adeen remain state property, with their magic used on a case-by-case basis, mostly in warfare. Any Adeeni mage who becomes too powerful is quickly dealt with. Maintaining this status quo would increase tensions with the Adeen, particularly those with hidden powerful magic, while also alienating our Mage guilds, who desire this potential talent. Alternatively, transferring ownership of these Adeen to the guilds would give them more power and appease them, but it would also anger the Adeen. Finally, these gifted Adeen could be freed to choose their own paths as enlightened individuals.
