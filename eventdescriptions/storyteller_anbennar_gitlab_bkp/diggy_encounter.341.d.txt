The soldiers set fire to the cube, and the gelatinous mass immediately ignited like it was a mass of kindling, not slime. It burned with a bright blue flame with green spots, emanating an unthinkable stench and fumes into its surroundings, making the soldiers flee the conflagration.  Once they returned there was nothing left of the Cube except a few spots of ooze, but the treasure had been obliterated similarly, leaving a few melted pieces of iron scrap behind.
