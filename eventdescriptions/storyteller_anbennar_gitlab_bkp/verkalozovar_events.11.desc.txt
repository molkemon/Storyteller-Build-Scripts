The restructuring of our magical studies towards runesmithing has gone as smoothly as jade. Haless has been forced to recognize that our rune-mages are genuine mages, and that Verkal Ozovar is the center of runesmithing. Our rune workshops overflow with the sight of glinting green stone, with the sounds of engraving and low magical hum, and with an aura of newfound eagerness. Theories and insights have begun to spark in our new runesmiths. Many are of the opinion that we stand at the cusp of a revolutionary advancement in the understanding of runes if only a few crucial pieces of the puzzle could be placed. Dare we hope that the lost knowledge of ancient runesmiths may be rediscovered or even exceeded? We do indeed.
