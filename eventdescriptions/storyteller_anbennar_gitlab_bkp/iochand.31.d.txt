For our Lord's Eyes Only

It has come to the attention of the Daccasded Administrative Council that the current governance of the Aelantieri Hezoggdoums has reached a point of great inefficiency. As we're sure Your Majesty is aware, at present all Hezoggdoums are de facto subjects of the Kingdom of Iochand, regardless of location. This leads to the awkward situation where Aelantieri Hezoggdoums legally must resolve any administrative issues with the Iochander government, despite the vast distance. Therefore, the Council presents the following proposal:

All Hezoggdoums located within the lands of Aelantier shall be made de jure subjects of the Kingdom of Daccasded, and indirect subjects of the Kingdom of Iochand via its implicit suzerainty over the latter.

This simple administrative change would allow the Aelantieri Hezoggdoums to be managed directly from Bloopalaasd, without need to go back and forth all the way to Cannor. Naturally, Iochand would still possess de facto authority over all subjects on both continents, in accordance with Statute 35.12a and Council Order 214. Such a change would serve the dual purpose of greatly decreasing the administrative burden faced by both the crown and its subjects, as well as greatly increasing the effective speed of administration decisions for the benefit of all in the realm. While Your Majesty is of course under no obligation to modify the preexisting structure of their domain, the Council strongly encourages them to take this proposal under consideration for the sake of their subjects' wellbeing and prosperity.
