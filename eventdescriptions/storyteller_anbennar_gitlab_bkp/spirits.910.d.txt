One night, as our great lord sits in their garden pondering the state of their nation, they end up considering what exactly makes a good leader, and how one's skill in battle is rooted in one's own selfmastery. As they rise to leave the garden, they see a figure sitting on a different bench some distance away. The figure is old and wrinkled, and as they approach they see that the figure is emitting a faint blue glow. They walk up to what they recognize as a spirit and learn that it is in fact one of their own ancestors. The ancestor spirit beckons them to sit down and have a chat. They accept, and as they talk about many things, the ancestor offers to lend their wisdom for a time so that they can better themselves. Specifically, the ancestor worked as a general and offers to teach these skills, which they joyously accept.
