Kobolds are known to be great diggers, no, not in the dwarven way, but more like tunnels that’ll give ants a run for their money. The intersection between kobolds and our people have caused tension in a major province, with a major building collapsing due to the presence of kobold tunnels beneath it. Clearly this is not acceptable, but the kobolds respond that their tunnels are a necessity to provide housing for their evergrowing populace.
