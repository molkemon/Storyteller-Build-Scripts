Our Great Prophet has saved us from the chaos that threatened to snuff out our light. Dahvar will lead us to a new era of glory. Our lessers writhe like maggots in the corpse of our onceglorious homeland. They cling to false idols and dead gods. They are fools. Do they not realize that there is but one thing worthy of worship? The form of the Star Elf is divine, pure, perfect. Only the stars in the night sky are our equals, for they are just like us. Our bodies are temples, our will divine. Noone will stand in our way. We will not allow degenerated mockeries of our divine image, and interlopers from the East pollute Aelantier with their presence any longer. The age of the star has begun.
