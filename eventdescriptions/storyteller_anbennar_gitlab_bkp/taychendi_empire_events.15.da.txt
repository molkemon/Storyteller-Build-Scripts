The Ravelian Church – perhaps one of the more interesting organizations to have emerged from the realms of Cannor. They profess a belief in a single "True God." Though it may seem similar to the beliefs of the Sun Elves in Surael, a defining aspect of their faith makes it altogether different – universalism. They see all gods and divine beings as either "aspects" of the divine or those imbued with its light, which they call saints. Initial conversations with the Ravelians have shown that they consider nearly all of our god-heroes to be open to canonization, the process of "confirming" sainthood. Were we able to control this process, we could have a tighter grip on Taychendi religion than anyone has ever known. The gates of Dhivumanhu would be ours to control.

They are also unravellers by nature, and the field of magic and extraplanar research is one they have spent considerable effort unraveling. Were we to either convert or accept the church into our lands, we may be able to gain access to this knowledge. There is one problem, of course – slavery. The Ravelians do not favor the practice. But the gleam of gold can blind even the most zealous eyes, and the allure of Taychend may well be too tempting for the Church to pass up, despite any "moral compunctions."
