Which mask you wear is of great importance to the Shuvuush, as it signifies who you are representing at the moment; whether clan heads choose to wear their clan mask, or their personal one, is often a matter of political importance. It signifies whose counsel you are accepting at the moment, as you may speak to the spirits inside the mask; if there are any present, that is.  Some amongst the Shuvuush are rather consistent in their choice of mask. Shamans in particular, both blackmasked deathpriests and whitemasked medicinemen, never change their mask. Yet most others tend to change which mask they wear frequently, taking great pride in the range of options available to them.
