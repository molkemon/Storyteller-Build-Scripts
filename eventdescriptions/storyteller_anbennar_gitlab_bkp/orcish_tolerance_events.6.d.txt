Orcs are some of the best suited for adventuring work, and some can be fantastically wealthy due to their prowess. One such ork used a newly gained fortune to buy a thousand ork slaves, and then freed them on the spot. Though purchase by another ork is how most free orks are created, the sheer number of orks the adventurer freed has created controversy. Many slavers are outraged, saying that because of this stunt, their own stock has become restless. Others have complained about the large influx of free orks that have begun to cause trouble. Some have suggested that the free orks be disciplined, or that the adventurer be made an example of, in order to deter future actions like this. However, either action would be deeply resented by the Adventurers, who are standing by their comrade.
