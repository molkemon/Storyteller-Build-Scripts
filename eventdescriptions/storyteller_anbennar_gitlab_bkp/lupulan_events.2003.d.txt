During this year's tribute period, one of our weaker tributaries has claimed they do not have the resources to pay their due, citing additional tribute being levied on them by one of our other tributaries. We have sent the tax collectors to investigate, and sure enough, the tributary is both broke and paying double tribute. It is our responsibility to ensure that the tribes under our care are being stewarded responsibly, and to have our tributaries exacting tribute from our other tributaries before we ourselves are paid threatens to undermine the system of tribute, turning it into a confusing web of obligations. What should our response be?
