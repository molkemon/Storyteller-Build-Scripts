While the Western Serpentspine is far away from any ocean, we still know the ebb and flow of trade at least as well as the sailors of Eborthil know the seas. The years of trade up and down the old dwarven roads have made us experts at shipping crates through our country and with the Marrhold tunnel rebuilt in its full glory, this flow has become a torrent of goods. We grow richer with each passing day and  each exported shipment .
