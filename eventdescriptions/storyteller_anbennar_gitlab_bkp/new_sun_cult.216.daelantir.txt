Most people live lives of duty and diligence, making a meaningful contribution; albeit one likely unknown beyond their village or town. But a few are blessed with the capacity and luck to do more, to truly make an impact on our nation. And one such individual has recently come to our attention. Unfortunately, he seems to already have found a calling; as a priest of a foreign faith, in one of the societies that has recently sprung up here. We could perhaps convince him to abandon those robes he now wears and lend his service to our lord, but the priests insist they cannot shrug off the darkness in their soul that comes from adhering to the whispers of a false god. And indeed, with a place at court they may convince others of their beliefs. An invitation has been drafted to order them to our capital and the court. It lays in front of our lord; but so does a raging fireplace. What comes next?
