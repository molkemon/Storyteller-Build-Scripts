A lord has not the right to much time to mourn the death of their parents: mere days after the funeral, the coronation ceremony had to take place, and then a myriad of tasks awaited them. At least the fog Thanal felt on their head was starting to lift; the everyday duties of a ruler of two realms helping in softening the pain of the loss. Right, realms. Plural. He will have to formalize the rank of his two crowns, sooner rather than later. There is no doubt in his mind about that at least. He loved his mother's homeland deeply, but he is a gophiran through and through, having grown up running around The Fort, which will always be his home. Gophira will be the primary title, Darkalyndar's subordinate to it, for him and his heirs as well.
