Harimari high society has always had an aura of superiority, making it easy for us to play the upper classes and middle classes against each other when needed. However, this superiority complex has its drawbacks, as they now demand that we "take care" of the humans because many are extremely successful artisans and tradesfolk, often outperforming Harimari-owned businesses. The humans have indeed been doing exceptionally well, and it seems to stem from their shrewd business strategies. We can certainly take down those businesses and claim they are doing illegal stuff, or we can simply tell the Harimari to accept the savvy ways of the humans. Perhaps we can even invite those humans to the palace to learn about their strategies and tactics; who knows if we could apply them in matters of state?
