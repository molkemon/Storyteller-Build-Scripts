So saith THE MOB:  Hurrah. When last we saw our chosen man We must confess our hesitation bloomed. We could not say as such in land of wine, And so, our brothers with discretion bade Decide if he would be both theirs and mine.  His words us struck as gentle gusts of wind Which somehow blew our ranks into the streets. But what are we without our chosen man? We thought, as we made merry and made death.  One of our number saw our man beset And through his eyes we watched them all in fear That proved unneeded, for he in his strength Cut through the foe and made his way back here.  Hurrah. Our MOB is overcome. Hurrah. Our number starts to laugh. Hurrah. Our father quits us not. Hurrah. He’ll rule on our behalf.
