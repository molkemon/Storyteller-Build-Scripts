Ameion's imposition of serfdom on the Taychendi—often framed as a "moral alternative to slavery"—has likely been one of the greatest sources of discontent among the empire's subjugated peoples. With their much-coveted freedom restricted, the peasantry fell into the arms of the Oren Nayiru, who they believed could provide the organization they needed to throw off Ambhen domination. Recently, a wave of serf rebellions has erupted, with the rebels declaring in favor of the Kamrayakval.
