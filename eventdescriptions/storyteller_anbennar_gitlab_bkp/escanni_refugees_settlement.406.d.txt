Not all who come to Escann are former inhabitants of Escann or their descendants; there are also those who wish to gain fame, glory and riches. While they are a bit late and the main phase of the Reclamation of Escann is over, we can still make use of them on the fields of battle. We can also try to convince them to settle down instead, but we will have to give them substantial incentives to abandon their peripatetic way of life. What will be our stance, my lord?
