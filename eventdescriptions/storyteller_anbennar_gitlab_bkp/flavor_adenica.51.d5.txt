The visit to the Longlance Knight's Academy was not primarily for recruitment, as many of the students there ended up being groomed and brought into the army as officers in any case. The real prize was Adran 'Thunderlance'. An instructor who had previously been a vaunted knight and general before breaking a leg in an illfated drill, his leadership ability was well known. And with just a little bit of pressure applied from the king of Adenica our lord.
