There is a moment of calm as the beast's thrashing ceases, leaving its titanic form floating in a slick of blood and ichor. For a heartbeat, the sea falls silent, then the deck erupts with cheers as the crew swarms the corpse like seagulls, tearing at it with billhooks and blades in search of trophies. As a sailor offers our lord an immense tooth pried from the beast's maw, our lord reflects on the successful hunt, and on that part of the experience our lord learned most from.
