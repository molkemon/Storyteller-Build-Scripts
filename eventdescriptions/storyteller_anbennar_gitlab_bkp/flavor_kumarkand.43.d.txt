O Surakel, our Lord. Your first EmpireYour servants of Karqasluhave returned. We prostrate yourself in service to You for the first time in far too long. Though centuries passed in which, though still blessed by Your light, we remained shrouded in delusions, You did not forsake Your people. You did not permit us to forget what You had bestowed on us so long ago. It is thanks to Your blessings, Lordnot for any merit of our ownthat we have now remembered our past and the elevated and correct rites You bestowed upon us. O exalted Surakel. May we never falter in our duty to You.
