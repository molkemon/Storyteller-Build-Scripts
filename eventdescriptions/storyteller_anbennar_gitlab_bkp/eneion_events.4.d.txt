In a large tent located in the hills south of Keyolíon, stood the chieftains of the Dtsiaerh tribes. Our lord stepped into the tent and took a look at his surroundings. No weapons present, and no warriors either: A good sign. He closed the entrance to the tent behind him and addressed the chieftains, "I am our lord, the king of Eneion. I have received your request for a meeting. I have granted it, and now stand before you. So, what do you want to discuss?"

The most powerful of the chieftains, a great warrior that towered over our lord, responded, "We have heard of the deals you have made with the tribes to the south. We seek such a deal too."

A visible smile crept up our lord’s face as he realized the best case scenario for the meeting was playing out before his very eyes.
