The lord has gathered the warriors and generals of Malacnar in the central square. Amidst lavish festivities and lively brawls, everybody’s attention is directed at the ruler as they step up to the Levodium, ready to make an announcement about the new laws. While it had always been an implicit agreement that in Malacnar law and justice would flex and align with the will of its current Battleking, the City of Warriors would now receive its first set of everlasting laws, as well as the formalization of pivotal governance institutions such as the Council of Generals; now visibly expanded; and the Assembly of Subject Territories; also clearly at its largest ever.
