The Gozlamoir Reforms seek to establish the groundwork for establishing centaur rule over settled peoples. Under the Gozlamoir System, the land of settled peoples is divided into distinct, large swathes of territory. At any given time at least 1 ordsiwyn will be assigned as the "gozlamoir" of a given territory. While ordsiwyn are generally free to decide for themselves where they wish to live and roam, if an ordsiwyn is selected to be a gozlamoir for a given region, they are required by the Caehn to depart for the region, relieving the previous ordsiwyn of their post, and assuming the responsibilities of the gozlamoir. The designated centaur clans perform the duties of governance in support of the local rulers, serving as guards, tax and tribute collectors, scouts, and messengers, but also as enforcers of the Caehn's rule and authority across the land.
