When Umorozi comes back the fourth time, she is limbless. The enemies of the Vile One know her on sight, of course, and target her, tear at her, or send assassins for her. Unable to remove or indeed damage the solid fog of her arm, her killer has instead carved out the flesh around it, as they have around Umorozi's jaw-throat wound and her stomach wound. For good measure, for punishment, they have removed all the other limbs. Do they seek to place Umorozi beyond Her reach? Such a thing is not possible. Four limbs are forged, one for each time She has brought her general back.
