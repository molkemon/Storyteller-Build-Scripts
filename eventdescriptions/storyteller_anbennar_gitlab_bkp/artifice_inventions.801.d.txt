Across our realm, mighty cities dot the lakeshores and beyond, towering above the mighty plains that we call home. Many of these cities contain beautiful works of architecture, mighty structures towering tall above our streets, serving as mighty symbols of Triunic strength and innovation. The only problem is that many of these structures were expensive to maintain when they were originally built, and with age, this problem has only worsened.  Luckily, artifice has the solution. Tiny clockwork machines, designed specifically to look like native birds, shall be deployed in order to continually provide maintenance and repair to our cities in a cheap manner, without looking out of place in the beauty we have so carefully cultivated.
