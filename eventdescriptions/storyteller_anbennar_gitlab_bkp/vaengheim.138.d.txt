The hunt in the Reach was a most unlikely toil. Skilled were the wingmaidens in trapping and tracks, yet now their quarry did hide in the forests of timber, stone, and narrow streets. For great were the cities of the Reach, and unlike any hunting ground that they had seen. And other hunters there were as well, who would make prey of our lord’s host. Yet they hid not in brush or canopy, but behind words and smiles. And their claws were hidden steel and unseen venom, and much did the maidens of Vængheim suffer, for they were ill-prepared for this unseen foe. Our lord grew wroth, for many wounds had their quarry gifted their sisters, and to strike at them was to strike at smoke.
