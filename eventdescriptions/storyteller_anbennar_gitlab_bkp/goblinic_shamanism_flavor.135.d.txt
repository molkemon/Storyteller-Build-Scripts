One of the more prominent Feykeepers have managed to make a deal with a Fey that they claim would be beneficial to our realm, should we give them sufficient resources. The Feykeeper in question claims that they have been offered passage deep into the Feywild, where they will be able to find great riches, and be bestowed with great wisdom. Naturally, such an expedition comes with significant risk, and will require us to finance the expedition with both manpower and equipment.
