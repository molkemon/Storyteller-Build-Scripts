All are well-fed. All have been given gifts for attending this conclave. Why do they give gifts? Is it to further their own influence? Is it a token of friendship? Is it—The ruler perks up. She remembers this. This was an important argument where she had to intervene. This argument would inevitably turn to be about the role of Irdaeos, and the gifts that she has given the centaurs. Arguments yet unsaid swirled in her head. It helps that there is no need for further thought, as it has been decided months and months ago. Why listen if the point has already been made? Four interpretations, of course. Each corresponding to its own sect. Each one fundamental in its approach and the future nature of our faith. The question? The nature of the gifts that Irdaeos has blessed us with. The answer was clear, and there was no doubt in her heart. The stars look favourable, and she would not disappoint them.
