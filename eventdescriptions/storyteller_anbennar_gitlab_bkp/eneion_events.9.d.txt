The states of Besolakí and Empkeios were the most rural of the Kheionai. Their societies were organized around small villages spread across the countryside, with their capitals being very small compared to other realms. The average citizen was the humble farmer, owning and working a small plot of land and selling excess produce to merchants, who would then transport the products to Alecand. For generations, this is how these two realms functioned.

With our conquest, however, the society of the Besolbhen and Apikhobhen has been severely disrupted. Traditionally, the property rights of the minor farmers were heavily protected by the kheions here. Now, our landowners are eager to buy up large amounts of land at cheap prices, at the expense of the locals.

In response, the peasants have banded together into armed militias, and have made one single demand to our state. They demand that the same protections for their property be restored under our rule. The landowners, in response, demand the ability to buy up land with no oversight from us.

The two groups have begun arming themselves, and now it seems we will have to make a choice.
