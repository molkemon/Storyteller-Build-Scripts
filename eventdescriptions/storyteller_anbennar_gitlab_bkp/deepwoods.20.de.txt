Like an obsidian pillar, the smoke pierces the sky. Across our lands, those of us who have been enlightened by the Fey cry out in agony, for we know what is occurring.  The wood burns.  The foul Black Hearth, created by our brethren, the socalled ‘Pure orks’, who refuse to accept the Emerald Touch, belches black smoke into the air. Product of the twisted mind of Bron Blackmoon, it both churns out unholy weapons, and turns our sacred trees into naught but ash.  The forest cries out in pain…
