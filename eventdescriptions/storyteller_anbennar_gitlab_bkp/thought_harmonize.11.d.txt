The followers of the faith have increasingly come to integrate concepts from The Thought in their daily life. A recent trend is to identify the concept of The Theory of Everything with their own faith. This interpretation is somewhat unorthodox to the scholar experts of our government, but it has allowed the faithful to integrate a number of concepts from The Thought, constants and classical teaching in their theology. There is some debate in the palace halls of our capital if this is the right and proper way on the path for the adherents to elevate themselves from obscurantism to Rationalism.
