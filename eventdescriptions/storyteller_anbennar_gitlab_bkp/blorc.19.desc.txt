Seghdihr, and the Segbandal they led, fought our ancestors to a standstill as they tried to enter the Middle Serpentspine. But we are not our ancestors: we are stronger, fiercer, tougher. We would ask the Citrine Dwarves if that has been made clear to them, but living ones are few and far between within the hold's halls nowadays; corpses, of course, still litter the ground everywhere. We will, however, undoubtedly see that truth in the eyes of those who deal with us, the cowardice of those who would oppose us; whatever stopped orks before, wherever we were held back, it shall not survive the onslaught.
