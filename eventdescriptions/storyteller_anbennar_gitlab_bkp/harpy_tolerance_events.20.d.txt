A cacophony of yips, howls, and screeches greets you as you approach the makeshift racetrack. A massive crowd of gnolls and harpies has gathered, a tide churning with excitement. Perched atop a large boulder, a grizzled gnoll with a booming voice acts as the announcer, his words punctuated by excited snarls. "Listen now, you runts and you birds. The Great Scramble is about to begin." he howls. "In this flock, representing the high peaks and questionable hygiene, we have the harpy." A wave of screeches rises from the harpies. "And for the pack," the announcer's chest swells with pride, "the pride of the plains, the fastest paws in the west, your future champion, I hope." The gnolls erupt in a chorus of whoops and howls. "A fine runner. May the best hunter win." He pauses dramatically, before adding with a toothy grin, ".but we all know fur is faster than feathers. It is time to prove it. Place your stakes, and let's see who has the conviction to back their champion." As you step forward, the announcer's booming voice falters. He hops down from his rock and gives a clumsy bow. "Ah, our lord. Forgive the noise. A friendly competition. The betting pot is sizable, should you wish to add your own wager."
