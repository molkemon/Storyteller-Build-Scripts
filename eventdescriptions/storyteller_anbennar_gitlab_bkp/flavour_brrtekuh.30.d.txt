A cornered tribe of backwater gnolls from a godforsaken desert, who manage to vanquish the ancient khets and the demonworshipping gnolls: such are the absurd tales used as common jest just a hundred years ago. Yet here we are, standing at the zenith of gnollkind. Such a grand campaign could not simply be attributed to mere chance: it is simply divine in nature, a work of Kvanga achieved by her emissary Brrtekuh. Many Cannorians and Sun Cultists have told prophecies of gods who bestow their essence upon Halann; none have greater evidence for their claim than the  blessing which we have received for ourselves. As we have consumed Brrtekuh, we have also consumed the essence of Kvanga, and bear her holy soul. We are the reforger of squander, the tamer of the wild and the protector of the cycle.
