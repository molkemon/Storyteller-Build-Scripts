The population is irritated these days. The citizenry has observed an astonishing and, to all but the prince's own clan, alarming development. Ever since their lord was elected as ruler, their clan has made big strides. Tenders for national projects have culminated in sweetheart deals for the prince’s compatriots, resources have been given to them before all others, and there are even rumours of outright bribery and nepotism becoming commonplace during their reign. Obviously, this has the people complaining about favoritism. The only question is: what is the response?
