Our republics are young, small, and like all things young and small, almost defenseless. Our dreams, barely born into the world, face overwhelming odds and may soon be crushed by stronger powers who will not hesitate to do so. We reject this fate. Some say we have little in common, but we share hope. We know we want to choose our own destiny and not have it decided by some overseas tyrant. So let us choose it together! Henceforth, we shall be bound together not by oppression like in Cannor, but by the unbreakable bonds of eternal friendship and shared ideals. Today, we become something more; we become a Concord of nations. Together, we have done great things. Tomorrow, the Trollsbay Concord shall do even greater ones. It’s been years since the process of creating our confederation began, my friends, and a difficult process as we all know. But seeing all of you here today to ratify it makes it worth it.
