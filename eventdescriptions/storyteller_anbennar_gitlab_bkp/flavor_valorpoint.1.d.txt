Not long ago we left our homeland, and already worldchanging news reaches us from back across the sea. The Corinite proclamation has swept the continent of Cannor, dividing it between those who champion Corin; now ascended as the God of War; and those who try to maintain the status quo by denying her status as the leading deity. Many of our men, having fought alongside Corin herself or having family who did, have a clear preference.
