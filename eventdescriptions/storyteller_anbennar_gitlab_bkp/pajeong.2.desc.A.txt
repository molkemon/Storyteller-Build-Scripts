Through hard-fought battles and emergent treaties, we attempted to bring the rebellious provinces to heel. But our armies were no match for the bowlords. Our accountants and navies could not keep up with the merchants of Gitueni. And the Neoksamaji have turned their noses up at us, hiding away on Nakseseom and loudly declaring Sado’s displeasure in our ruler. In the west, a new Izhuun has risen and carved out a piece of our once great realm. To the north, the Hwalbaek declared a new independent domain. In the south, the merchants have raised the flag of Nuwenara over their holdings. To the east, the sacred islands are now lost to us, as the priests now openly rule over what they call the domain of Baegye. We are a weak shadow of what we once were under the Sage King’s rule.
