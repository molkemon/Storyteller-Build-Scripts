Our Consort has impressed many men and women at court with their sharp wit and extensive knowledge of the natural world. They have long been a collector of tomes on botany and chemistry, and correspond with several of our generation’s brightest minds. Currently, one of these scientists is visiting our court at their invitation. They can be found discussing all sorts of new theories far into the wee hours of the night. Our Consort has asked us to make the scientist an offer, claiming they would make a much better advisor than the Advisor we currently employ.
