Ever since the newcomer appeared at court, they have been a mystery to our own elite class. Nobody knows their name nor has been able to find information about this secretive person. As time went on, the newcomer became friends with the official. They prove themselves skillful in the matters of administration, diplomacy and the military, and are in general well received in the court. Meanwhile, a vile rumor about the competence of the ruler spreads within the government. It is clear that the ruler is losing any kind of support of benefactors while the newcomer is gaining more and more support for the election.
