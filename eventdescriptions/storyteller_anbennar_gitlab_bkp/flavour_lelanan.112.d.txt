Their meeting was pure chance. A captain fell into the stormy sea as a merfolk was swimming by. The accident they did see. The captain was stranded in the water, while their ship continued until it was gone. So the merfolk helped them get to shore, and they talked on and on. They shared a great deal more in common than one would ever expect. And even once the captain was rescued, every year they came back and checked. They would sit in the shallows, then hold each other in their arms. But there was nowhere they could share, nowhere they could indulge in each other's charms. But when the Ocean Ananoma was complete, they both rushed to ask each other. And now they live on the fourteenth pier, along with the merfolk's mother.
