Frantic cries of "Whale Sighted" carry through the ship suddenly, followed by the heavy stomp of boots on wood. Our lord is jolted out of their sleep and rushes deck side, where the harpooners and rowers are already preparing to launch. No one pays our lord any mind, fixated as they are on the flickering crescent of the whale's tale and blast of its blowhole as it swims just underneath the surface of the ocean ahead. The air is heavy with pent up energy and the potential of action; so much so that our lord is compelled to do something, anything, despite lacking the years of experience that these sailors have.
