The lord has recently found themself fascinated by all things architectural. They have spent many free hours looking down upon our capital and requesting information on construction and buildings are doing - how it might be improved and how builders could be valuable in wartime as well. Their passion has slowly grown, and now shortcuts under internal castle walls and intricate sculptures have been spotted emerging apparently spontaneously around the castle, with the 'culprit' not being found. We could propose that our ruler could help the arduous task of building in our overcrowded capital. However, people only have so many hours in a day and a ruler even less, so towards which of their passions should we direct them?
