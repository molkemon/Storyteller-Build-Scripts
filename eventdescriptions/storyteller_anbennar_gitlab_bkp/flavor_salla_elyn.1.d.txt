The Silent Lake has traditionally been one of our greatest resources, offering fresh water, fish and the ability to traverse the grove far faster than by foot; as such, it occupies a central role in our people's culture. Along the Silent Lake's shoreline, a variety of shipwrights can be found, most specializing in the craft of small riverboats; capable of traversing most of the waterways of the Deepwoods, such boats remain highly maneuverable.  As the Silent Lake has come fully under our control once more; and with further conflicts against goblins and orks looming overhead; it has become paramount to find and exploit every advantage we can get. Our maritime capabilities are one such advantage; our soldiers have begun practicing and training to be highly capable hybrid combatants, able to hold their own on land and still thrive upon the waterways. Specifically, they have trained to traverse the Deepwoods via our riverboats and disembark directly into the water, from which they make their attacks.
