The next batch of drinks has been served. Dwarves guzzle ale like they’re being paid for it, cheering one another on to keep going. The halls are filled with laughter, song, and the odd brawl here and there, and it is altogether quite the merry and lively occasion. The lord sat on his throne with a flagon of the purest ale. It is his duty, no, his honour to drink with his people, and drink he shall. The lord feels all the eyes of the great hall aimed at him, speculating whether or not he will live through it.
