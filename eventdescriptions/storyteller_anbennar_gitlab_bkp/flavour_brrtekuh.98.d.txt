Our new Kheteratan subjects have grown even more recalcitrant as of late. They shut their doors at our soldiers and traders, refusing any interaction with our pack. Those we've taken prisoner and forced to explain have all given broken descriptions of events yet to happen. Dreams of our gnolls clasping them in irons and whipping them towards ruinous ends. Nothing can be done to assuage their misplaced fears for now, until these visions come to an end.
