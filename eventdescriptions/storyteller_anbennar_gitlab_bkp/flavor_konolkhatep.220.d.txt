Traveler, in the year, the great Water Road was dedicated in the name of the ruler. As a civilization launched a fleet, so too do the docks of the Water Road launch the fleet of Konolkhatep. As one expanse spans a land, so too does the Water Road span the rivers of Jasir Jadid. As you are at peace while walking along the placid shores of the Water Road, so too is Konolkhatep at peace under their leadership. As this road stands forever, so too shall Konolkhatep. Inscription by the side of the Water Road in Qasri Kallumahida
