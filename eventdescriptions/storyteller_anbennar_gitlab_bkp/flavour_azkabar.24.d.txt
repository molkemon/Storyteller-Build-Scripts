The Arsunaellu has served its purpose. Thus, it was only prudent for the lord to summon and assemble all members of it, to a grand council. Held in a glorious round chamber, with seats lining down to the centre, where the lord eagerly talked of glorious new days, of a future to be seen. Of one nation ruling Bulwar. Whispers of discontent passed like wildfire through the thicket, and when the speech was done, these whispers erupted into shouts from some. Fortunately, our friends in the Eclipse cult were more than able to persuade them to join with us, a quick flash of steel deadened even the loudest of protests. Everyone left through the main entrance as the news was read, to a massive crowd, which soon erupted into festivity. Everyone saw the protesting leadership leave. No one ever saw them again.
