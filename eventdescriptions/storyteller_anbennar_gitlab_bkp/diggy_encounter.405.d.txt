The sudden outburst from one of our scouts takes the rest of them and the moss sorter by surprise. There is a moment of silence and stillness, but as everyone's heartbeats slow down, it passes, with the first movement made by the cave's inhabitant. He slowly takes a handful of moss off the shelf, looks towards our party, then to the moss, then to our party again.  A tentative lick later, he is engrossed with trying each and every one of the rainbow globs. "I was doing this all wrong then." His voice is rife with dejection and anger. "This is going to take ages to redo. I might as well start over." The scout who unwittingly initiated this whole exchange gets closer and starts giving pointers, as the rest of the group look about in confusion. It takes some time, but in the end our party leaves with smiles on their faces and manifold mosses in their hands.
