The Company of Thorns is not new to the game, the adventure, or the slaughter. It is a long-standing company of the finest warriors. Lucian has always made sure of that. When the Greentide ended, Lucian firmly established his ambitions to create his own nation, Luciande, and those who disagreed were quickly disposed of. Now the Company finds itself in an open escan, stable, with only the most loyal remaining.
