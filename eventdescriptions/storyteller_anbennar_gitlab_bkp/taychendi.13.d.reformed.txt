Hear me, and rejoice. The first child of our ruler has come into the world. Cheer for our new heir. While the criers are announcing the birth to the population, the whole court is busy with the preparations for the birth rituals for the newborn. Already the baby was bathed and named by the proud parents; now the time for the ceremony to propitiate the child's future has come. Together with the court, we'll present the baby to the world and the gods and, by choosing the ceremony location, ask for a brilliant future in that field for the heir's reign.
