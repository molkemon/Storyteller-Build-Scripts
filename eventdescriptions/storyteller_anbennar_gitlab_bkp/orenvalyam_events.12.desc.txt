The Mudaliars are an ancient family, one of the only known directly descended from Precursor nobility. Since Liandarha struck down the Slaver-Nobles of old Aercel and liberated those within Nanru Nakar, they have had an iron grip on the hearts of the great city’s people – and yet, as the years went by, and their power, prestige, and prosperity grew beyond imagining, the Mudaliars became just like the other warlords. Nanru Nakar filled with slaves, as the lords within the towering Vanetavanem palace ruled tyrant-like over the upper Kalavend.

When Nanru Nakar came under our control, the Mudaliars did what they have done many times through the city’s history – they ran. Not to the foothills, or overseas, but to the vast Nakari underground. However, the remnants of their influence can still be felt throughout the city, revered as they are by those Nakari who are not descended from slaves.

But the Mudaliars have changed themselves. Harkening back to Liandarha’s original chain-breaking feats, some of their own have adopted the Oren Nayiru faith, and desire to be named Kavalali, so they can lead their city once more. If their request is accepted, they will almost certainly become the most conservative of the Kavalalists – so conservative that one may doubt they are truly “faithful” at all.
