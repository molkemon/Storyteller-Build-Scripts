It starts with a skirmish, a guard holding back one of our littlefolk, trying to keep order. Peasants who think that just because they were given the right to see our glorious leader, that they have the right to speak with them. The scuffle stops and a corpse falls to the ground. Peasants push back, trying fruitlessly to fight off their oncoming deaths.  When the tide of peasants finally explodes, menatarms are sent backwards into a pillar which rains down splinters of wood towards our lord's throne. The splinters burst into flames, fed by our lord's natural fire, leaping from rotted pillar to sagging ceilings. If it is not stopped, it may burn down the palace. The fire begins to sneak itself though our rotting castle gate, pushing into the cramped castle town, threatening to turn it to ash. What should we do?
