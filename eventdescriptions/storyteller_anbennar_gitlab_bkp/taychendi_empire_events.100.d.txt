In times of war, those warlords who live on the borders of our realm are at a natural advantage, often being given lands along their frontier to raid and plunder. In times of peace, however, half of their potential targets are cut off, their overlords unwilling to risk an all-out conflict. Usually, they are forced to make do—sometimes, they decide to take matters into their own hands. 

Across our border with one of our neighbours, a coalition of border warlords have launched a series of unsanctioned but successful raids on enemy territory. While some call for a non-reply in the hopes of not provoking the other side, others say that we should indulge them and declare war. If we were to take particularly harsh measures, we may be able to temper the destabilizing nature of the insubordination—though perhaps at a cost to our empire's glory.
