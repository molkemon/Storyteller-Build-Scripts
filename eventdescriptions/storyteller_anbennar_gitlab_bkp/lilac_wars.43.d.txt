The Grand Duchy of Dameria was a nation once whole, uniting us under one banner of culture and purpose. Thus, its destruction was the greatest tragedy of the Lilac Wars. Across distant lands, a great nation has been lost. But, we cannot simply mourn. We must now do our best to make a future in this new order. New borders have divided us from our neighbors. Many at the court still mistakenly refer to the old lord, may he and his family rest well. Indeed, it seems as though it will take some time before the ruler and their name settle into this strange reality.
