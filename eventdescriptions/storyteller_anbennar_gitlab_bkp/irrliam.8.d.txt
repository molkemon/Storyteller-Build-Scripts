The three suranic temples of EduzVacyn, Bulwar and AzkaSur have held a shared position of preeminence for many centuries amongst the innumerable temples found within Bulwar. Now one of them, the temple of EduzRakadags in the city of Bulwar, has fallen firmly into the orbit of Irrliam, a Cinder Realm whose fortunes have been steadily improving over the past decades and who currently stand as the undisputed hegemon of the lower Suran. As Irrliam strives to uphold the legacy left by Taelarios Irrliazuir and serve as the protector of the currently ascendant interpretation of the Sun Cult he helped codify and strengthen over the last centuries, they have reached out to us with a bold proposal. In return for our vassalage and unanimous support in matters of doctrinal disputes, they are ready to use all their might to shield us from any outside threat, be it gnollish fiends or crazed desert nomads. Now we have to decide whether this offer is worth our submission, or if we should risk the ire of Irrliam in service of our continued independence.
