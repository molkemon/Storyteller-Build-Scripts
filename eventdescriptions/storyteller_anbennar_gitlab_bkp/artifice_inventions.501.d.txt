The commercialisation of magic has always been a strange topic; mages may be prone to selling their services, but those that do tend to charge high prices for anything more strenuous than a simple cantrip. However, through the power of modern technology, precast spells can be preserved for long periods of time, contained within scrolls, themselves contained within specialised conteiners; the SpellinaBox. Now, a single mage, specialised in a single spell, can imbue it into hundreds of scrolls at a time, for mass distribution. At long last, magic can be experienced by all, and at much lower prices; the middlemage finally being cut out.
