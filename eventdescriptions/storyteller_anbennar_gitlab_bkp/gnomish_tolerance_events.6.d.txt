Gnomes love to think and love to debate, and as such some of them in a local province have begun hosting public lectures, inviting anyone to come and listen to and discuss the ideas presented. This has led to a culture of innovation in the province, but also one of scepticism, both against religion and the government. Some advisors have suggested cracking down on the meetings, while others have suggested supporting them. The decision, however, is the ruler’s alone.
