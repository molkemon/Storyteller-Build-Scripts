The ruler of their land is a person you trust, not only in political matters. Your beloved spouse hails from the same land and their ruler often provides you with philosophical insights. One day, you are discussing your thoughts about Vortza Lesa and the nature of well, nature. Their ruler, a follower of Vortzo Laukuno, mentions how inseparable the gods you follow are, as man and wife, and how the one is also inseparable from the all. 'If you wish to understand Vortza Lesa,' they say, 'you must seek him through the god Vortzo Laukuno, his consort.'
