"A complete disaster." the finance minister lamented. "We look like fools." Cried out the chief diplomat. "It will take years for our reputation to recover." And so clamored a dozen other officials. All the while the ruler rubbed their temple all while thinking on how this mess started. It began two weeks ago, a Bulswari merchant was using silver coins minted by us to pay for an order. That should've been the end of that story. But, instead, some of those coins transmuted into an unknown lesser metal. The merchant was then arrested for fraud. Unfortunately, they would not be the last. Thousands of other sods found themselves accused of counterfeiting as silver coins from across Cannor began to change. It didn't take long for people to figure out that all these coins had a common connection: us. Each and every affected coin was forged in our mints that sourced from the mines of Yfelorr. We don't know what happened to the merchant or the other unfortunate souls caught in all of this. But, what we do know is that there is now a silver crisis due to this "Fey Silver". And, we are at the epicenter of it.
