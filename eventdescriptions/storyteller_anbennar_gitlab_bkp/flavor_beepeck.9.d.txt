It appears the worst has come to pass – the Emperor has used his authority to force us out of Orston, and as our officials retreat, so too do Beepeck’s magistrates advance, their merchants and military forces setting up shop in the province. The people of Exwes are outraged, and many are calling for action to be taken to avenge this dishonour – but this would undoubtedly be rash in the extreme. Perhaps we could fight Beepeck, but the forces of the Emperor? And yet, in our minds, in our hearts, there is still a voice, whispering, tugging at our shame, our otrage – can we really do nothing? Stand by, while our honour is challenged?
