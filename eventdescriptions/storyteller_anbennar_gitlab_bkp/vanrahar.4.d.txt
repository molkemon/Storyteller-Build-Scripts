Our settlers have done well for themselves. The fertile Dhenbasana river valley is now populated with our loyal harimari subjects. The community grows steadily and is so far selfsufficient. Everything is moving at a pace in accordance with our predictions. The proximity to the Ascension Peninsula had already made harimari a significant population in the Floating Cities. Now we have increased the population even more; it’s not enough that the rulers are harimari when 90% of the population are farmers. The fostering of new families is needed to make sure society is dominated by harimari, and that will never happen while a super majority of it is human.
