Our lord watches as the geomancers of our realm, known as Femezkhanz, scour the lands for gems and precious stones. The progress of the Femezkhanz is quick and silent, for they never need to stop to dig or examine the ground. Suddenly, the group ceases their quick gait, and our lord smiles as they dispatch a messenger. The Femezkhanz, with their magical sense, have almost certainly discovered a significant cache of valuable ore below their location. Within the day, a group of zealous miners will be sent to settle the area. As the message-bearer draws closer, our lord ponders one last question: what mineral could it be?
