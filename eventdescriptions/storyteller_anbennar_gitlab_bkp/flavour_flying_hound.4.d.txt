The Muncher, our lord, placed his right hand on his chest and bowed just enough to not be considered uncourteous. "Now, if we can move on to the pressing matters…" The young goblin retainer behind him quickly grabbed the chair and slid it into a position where his boss could comfortably sit back down. The Hound, our ruler, was bereft of such a servant. Grimacing slightly, he took this chance to scan the room he was seated in, revealing the extent of changes the Grasshopper Muncher clan had introduced to the castle, and indeed, Taranton at large. Shoddily renovated facade draped with banners of the three Prideful Bunch clans, interspersed with the occasional coat of arms of some long gone Adenner noble family. Even the chandelier above the table seemed to join in on the collective pretense of knighthood. Nevertheless, there was one aspect to it all, one which could possibly achieve a verisimilitude potent enough to leave a bystander stunned in awe: the way they carried themselves. The poise, the mannerisms, how they kept their hands on their swords without gripping the hilt. The words they spoke, so pretentiously archaic and so needlessly complicated, but oh so captivating to the unsuspecting ear that only ever expected a monstrous holler to resound. The names they called one another, befitting a most gallant court. Right, that’s what it was all about.
