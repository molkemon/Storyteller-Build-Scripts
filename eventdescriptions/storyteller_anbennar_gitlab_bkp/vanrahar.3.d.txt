We stand at a precipice. The very things that make Umdaj Umdaj are being lost, the essence of Umdaj stretched too far over the vast jungles. On the map Umdaj has consumed them but in reality it is they who have consumed Umdaj. They too are Primal Harimari, but unlike us, they are haughty, seeing themselves as above humans and rejecting new philosophies like those of the Guru Suhan. If it is the responsibility of our rulers to represent their subjects then it is clear what the majority of our subjects want.  It’s just that it goes against what we know to be righteous. We cannot make a compromise here, only decide: do we give in to the desires of our subjects, or do we make war on them yet again in the name of what we believe in?
