Dread news, our lord! The Huli-Jing have informed us of the troubles they face when trying to leave the Mulim forest. Apparently, when they step out of the treeline, the Huli-Jing are struck by blinding headaches and full-body weakness, lingering for a time once they head back into the forest. They wish to wander the countryside; this simply cannot do! This strange illness is strongest when they depart towards the northeast, towards Province 4871. Since that is now part of our realm, that is where our investigation will start.
