An ork once banished from the clan for murder has returned, offering his services as a leader of hardened warriors, all once exiled, abandoned, or lost to the Deepwoods. He offers service to the realm, swearing loyalty in exchange for land and the right to raise his own banner. Some say he cannot be trusted. The tribal leaders demand an Ozarm'Chadash, a trial of honor, to prove the truth of his oath.
