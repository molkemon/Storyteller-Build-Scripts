Troutcoast, a small fishing harbor in Exwes. Timberfort, a woodland burgh in Pearlsedge. Neither of these cities would be noteworthy on a normal day, but today both stand out. Indeed, when the report arrived at the Imperial Court describing kobolds suddenly massing in the Western Dames head, the steward's first instinct was to ask what century it was. Kobolds hadn't raided through Lencenor in centuries, much less successfully. Further details since have only made things stranger: these are not raiders. They're migrants. Evidently fleeing some conflict in the Dragon Coast, these smallfolk have made it through hell, high water, and the imperial border, and are now squatting outside cities that we should be protecting from such incursions. Now the emperor is facetoface with a large family kendia, per their correction of the scaly beings, who were spotted by Aldresian Knights trying to sneak into the city of Beepeck. These kobolds have asked the emperor for shelter, not knowing that their words invoke ancient Imperial Law, and now a formal ruling must be made to determine the fate of these threedozen kobolds, and indeed all their kind who seek refuge on Imperial Soil.
