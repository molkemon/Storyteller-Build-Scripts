To see from further away, a simple proposition, but a desire for many mechanims as they stalk the undergrowth of the great jungles or ride upon the back of a hinuibeetle. Fortunately, brave mechanim explorers have managed to find just such an implement within the halls of a gene pillar. The device incorporates multiple glass lenses of various curvature and thickness inside a Precursor steel casing. The user can press an external button to swap between magnifications as a lens is pulled down or up from the steel casing. This allows its user to see much further and in much higher detail.

Now, a Soarer mechanim may survey much more of the land as it glides across the sky. One may look closer at a beloved pet to identify injury or condition. Though perhaps the most enticing application is that this makes for much more effective spotters, allowing us to fire upon enemies more accurately and from further away.
