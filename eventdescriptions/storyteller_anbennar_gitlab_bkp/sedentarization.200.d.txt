As more settlers and supplies arrive from the core of our domain, we are ready to begin a great many construction projects to improve our outpost. However, what we focus on building depends on our goals for this land and the nearby area. To garner tribute or trade from the local centaurs, we could establish a Gozabhar, a dedicated plaza built to house these clans (and more importantly, their goods). If we plan to claim the land with frontiersmen, we will require a dedicated base of operations and a supply depot. But if the land is to be tamed and not just owned, only a proper urban hub will suffice.
