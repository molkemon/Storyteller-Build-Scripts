It started with naught but a rumble, the earth releasing a great groan. Then the shaking began in earnest, and a great burst of lava was thrown. The sky darkened with ash as the world was set alight. I led my paladins to the mountain, while all who could gave swift flight.

We conjured our great magics to contain the lava as best we could, while my priestesses rushed to help those who had withstood. In the end, the realm was ruined, all burnt to black ash. Afomapobo had stirred from its slumber, for we had been too brash.
