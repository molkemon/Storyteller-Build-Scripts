The people of the Khetist faiths have long since learned to respect rivers great and small; their ancestors having been taught by the capricious floods of many a great river. Knowing this, Elikhet left many teachings on how to care for them and use them for endeavors such as agriculture, watermills and other production methods. One of our priests, in tandem with a farmer, has managed to use one of these teachings; with a few tweaks; to tremendous effect, creating a spell to promote growth and prosperity on even the smallest of rivers and tributaries. The massive new stocks of grain and livestock must be put to use, but to what end?
