Though under the rule of the Gunpowder Xhazobine, the majority of their forces are still made up of Seascourge Gnolls who plied their craft on the seas of the Divenhal. It shouldn’t be a surprise that their forces were adept at naval invasions. Their ruler was still completely blindsided when reports reached Kheterat of a surprise invasion force landing in Shasoura, catching our homeland in a pincers movement.
