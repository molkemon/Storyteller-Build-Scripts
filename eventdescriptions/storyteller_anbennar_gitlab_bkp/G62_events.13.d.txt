Our Eastern neighbor Deyeion was long served as a prison colony for the proud warriors Degakheion. Enemies of the state, deserters and other scum were put into chains and shipped of into the Andic Reach to plow the fields and cultivate the tea plantations. While they have earned some selfgovernance after numerous rebellions, the people still yearn to break free of their oppressors shackles.  In many ways our 2 cities are kindred spirits. Are we not both outcasts of the Kheionai? We, the orphaned children of a lost kheion and they, the unwanted of our parent cities“ greatest foe. I say the time has come to liberate our brothers.
