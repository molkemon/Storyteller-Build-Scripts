In a province, there have been a group of Elven people who claim to be of royal blood. While it’s hard to prove whether they are or not, they have come forth with documents that do seem accurate enough to be real. Letting them have some privileges and land could look good, because who would hate to have some elven royal blood within their realm? It would undoubtedly not be considered popular within the realm, however, as there are those who claim that the documents are all fabricated.
