With Lokemeion firmly in our hands a debate has started in our government on what to do with the wealth of knowledge preserved in the Degalistea.  The Darasgenea and the Kelasgenea clans, always the practical sort, want to empty the library, study the tomes that look more immediately useful, and sell the rest; they already have a list of buyers ready. The Artogenea clan also is in favor of this idea, though they wish to keep the treatises of military theory closely guarded. The Varagenea, always the cautious voice, warns against this plan, reminding everyone who would listen of the library phantasm, its powerful protector, known to castigate library goers for even minor infractions. What would happen if we were to empty its library? A catastrophe could befall us; better to leave everything as it is.  Finally, the Irenthegenea want the Council's support for an indepth study of all the books and scrolls of the library; a world of knowledge will be our reward, they promise.
