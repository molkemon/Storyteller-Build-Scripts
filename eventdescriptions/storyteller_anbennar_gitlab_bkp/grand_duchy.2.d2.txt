Tensions were fraught within the Imperial Parliament; there was shouting, the bestowal of false curses, and even a sword drawn between two rival princes throughout the day. Imperial court whispers had incensed the Empire; allegedly the lord had already made their decision and by the appearance of a rival monarch at the upper balcony, detractors were losing hope. When the lord entered the parliament chamber by midnight, the room was already dead silent. The more boisterous princes were either arrested for the night or sunk low in their seats as the lord walked to the podium. The lord took a moment, retrieving a piece of paper from their robes as they glanced up at the rival monarch and gave a slight glare, stirring some curious murmurs from the seats.
'I, lord, bestow this monarch and their descendants the title of Grand Duke of this region. However, they shall have no claim nor right to ancestral lands.'
The murmurs immediately erupted into confusion and arguing. Supporters were outraged, while detractors begged the lord to throw away the title altogether. The lord left the room in a wake of discord as the Empire reeled from the split decision. This monarch was seen staring imperiously as the lord left.
