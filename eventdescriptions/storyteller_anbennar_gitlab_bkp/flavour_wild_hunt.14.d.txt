On the plains, there is a natural order to things. Centaurs form clans, clans form realms, and realms form domains. The greatest of these domains go on to extract oaths of fealty from other domains. At each step of this great chain, oaths are made. The patron promises to protect and nurture the client, and the client promises undying loyalty to the patron. These oaths are made because the natural conclusion of disunity on the plains is death, whether by the elements or by civil war. The vassal domains appear to have forgotten this simple fact, as they scheme over who will rule the pile of bones that will be left of their domain should they manage to overthrow the natural order through their sheer disloyalty.
