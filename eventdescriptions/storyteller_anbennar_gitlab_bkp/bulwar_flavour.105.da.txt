After the fall of Jaher's Empire, the Golden Highway that spanned from Bulwar to Haless in the far east rotted away. The section of the Highway coursing through the milder northeastern climate suffered constant harpy raids and gnoll raiders controlled the western oasis, forcing the caravans to adapt their routes by going right through the middle of the desert. The szalMehran, a reputed tribe living in the midst of the desert, established a prosperous trade route along the ruthless desert. Using strategic locations such as the port city, their homelands, and the safety of the walls, these prospered as guides for the trade caravans who preferred to brave the desert. With our newfound control over these key cities and our deal with the tribe, the szalMehran are finding more and more success as guides along the Golden Highway, now that merchants no longer fear taxhappy patrols from differing states eager to impose a new tariff on their goods. The stability brought by our unification of the szalMehran route has led to an influx of Raheni goods in our country, as many guides still remain, for the realignment of the szalMehran to their new role is not complete. During this period of transition, we can still reap their benefits.
