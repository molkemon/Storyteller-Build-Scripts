As the ruler mounted their terrorbird with their most trusted generals and shamans nearby, they began making their way through the dreaded valley, surrounded by thousands upon thousands of men, some on terrorbirds, some on horses. Some of our own, some belonging to the Runsukhi, and others mages from Yanshen, hired with bags of crowns. They all have two things in common: they are all wearing masks and they are all filled with fear, whether they show it or not. After a few minutes the first duststorm appears, a small whirlwind drawing near the regiment in the front. When the dust settles, a Facestealer is seen attempting to pull off the masks of some men, as others pelt it with projectiles and spells. Soon the spirit crumples to sand, becoming one with the desert and the regiment continues to move forward, sustaining minor casualties. The army walks for a whole hour without anything happening, when suddenly a sandstorm grows in the distance, nearing fast. As it draws closer, it gets bigger and bigger, as smaller whirlwinds join together. Some men ready their weapons, others adjust their masks. A shaman tries to calm their terrorbird, a mage turns around and starts running. The ruler takes a deep breath and prepares their very soul.
