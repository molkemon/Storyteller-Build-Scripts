Through the Holy Will of the Father, the Runelink is maintained. Yet without vigilance by His children, the Maw shall crumble, and the Maw shall close.  Without the Maw, the Runelink cannot be maintained.  Without the Runelink, the Father shall be cut off from His children.  Without the Father, His children are lost, and shall surely perish.  The flow of soldiers cannot cease, lest we see the Empire crumble before all those who seek to defy the Father's Will. The flow of slaves cannot cease, lest those who would be redeemed in His name be allowed to waste their lives without purpose, rather than spilling their blessed sweat and blood in the name of the Father. Thus, the Maw must be maintained, and the Maw must grow.  Such is the Will of the Father.
