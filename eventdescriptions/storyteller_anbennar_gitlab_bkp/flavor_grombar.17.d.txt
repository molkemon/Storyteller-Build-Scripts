The young lord Crovis of Adshaw has gathered a great host of Alenic mercenaries and adventurers and marched them into our city, proclaiming his ambitious goal of defeating the Gray King and restoring the fallen kingdom of Vrorenmarch. With the blood of the Vrorenssons running through his veins, his claim to the kingdom is valid. He is but a man however, and the orks now also have legitimate claim to the kingdom. It is clear that Crovis is not planning on leaving the city quietly, and this may be our only chance at freedom, though we shall be forced to face the full fury of the Frozenmaw orks. Should we accept him and fail, our autonomy will be no more.
