The Riyitewag are free soldiers, unbound to a Mezhi; they roam the holy land, seeking gold and glory in the hire of any who pay for their services. From time to time, a particularly influential priest will gather large numbers of them in service of their Mezhi in exchange for an absolution of their many blood-stained sins against their fellow Mengi, accumulated over long lives of war. The High Priest of this realm has done so for us, gathering a loyal host of motivated and zealous warriors to meld into our armies as generous examples of Amilak's Mercy.
