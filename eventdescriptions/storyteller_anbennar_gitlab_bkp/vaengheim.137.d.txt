Long and strange was this hunt, for the Equilibrists were a curious quarry, for they knew the crafts of men. Yet aided by Reachmen did our lord bring war to their door, and boldly did their wingmaidens many a furtive gathering unpick. It was one of these maidens, a clever gull named Asfrid, who now spoke, and said, "Twisted grows this hunt, for the cult we seek is sly. A meeting have our mannish friends spoken of, and they would have us swoop in and slay these evil men.
