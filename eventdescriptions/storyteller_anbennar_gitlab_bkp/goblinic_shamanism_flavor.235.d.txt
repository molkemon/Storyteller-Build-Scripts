Though all the ceremonies of the worship of the Gerudian Cult are a sight to behold, the most earthshattering are their socalled "rockconcerts," so named for the fact that goblin bards often prefer to stand atop massive boulders to be seen by adoring crowds of worshipers below them.  A group of bards belonging to the cult has requested our support in organizing one such concert, promising the spectacle of a lifetime. Their requested sum also promises that it will be among the most expensive, though it is not exactly clear where all the requested money would be going.
