The Revolution has arrived. The government has collapsed under its own weight as the revolutionary masses stormed key government buildings. The ruler was captured in the chaos, and although they initially seemed able to negotiate their way out of it with modest concessions, revolutionary leaders soon saw through their ploy and opted to limit their influence by removing them. Although their diehard supporters were convinced the ruler would soon grow another one, this did not happen. Instead, a purely temporary Committee of Public Safety has been appointed to guide the new Republic through this revolutionary period. Threats abound, however, for monarchs and despots across Cannor fear they may be the Revolution's next victim and are already preparing for war against us. On the bright side, liberal forces are looking to us for assistance in their own struggles, so we will be able to gain support in such a manner.
