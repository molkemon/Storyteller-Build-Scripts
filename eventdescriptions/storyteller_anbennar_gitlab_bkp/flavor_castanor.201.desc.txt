The Edhardrachon; roughly Old Castanorian for Sons of the Dragon; are a loose society of patricians who grouped together to push their interests against the crown. For now, they just sit in their white spires and tall towers in Castonath, grumbling and not doing much else. But the patricians have deep connections and even deeper pockets. What's more, they hold intimate knowledge of and control in the great city of Castonath, the beating heart of Castanor. Who knows what will happen should they decide to put those resources to action?
