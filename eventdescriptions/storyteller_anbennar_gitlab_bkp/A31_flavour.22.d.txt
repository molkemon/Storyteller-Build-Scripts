House of Bluetongue is an influential elven dynasty, established by Varilor Cymlan, brother of Munas himself. Bluetongues ruled Moonhaven for the majority of its existence and now following the death of his father, Varion The Young was elected. Unlike his predecessors who focused just on Moonhaven under the protection of Dameria, Varion needs to look outwards. With Dameria gone Moonhaven now needs to protect itself, or find a new ally who will help it. Moon elves as a whole are very divided, even having fought each other during the Lilac Wars. Varion's uncle has fled to Ibevar following Wexonard conquest of Varivar, and Varion has not been in contact with him since, not having the blessing of his uncle to lead Bluetongues and the moon elves. Varion kept thinking and rethinking these thoughts for months. But the time for thinking has passed. If Moonhaven was to survive and prosper along with its elves it needed action. And Varion must be the one to lead the way.
