KAMRAYAKVAL VICTORIOUS. TAYCHEND OVERRUN. BEWARE! BEWARE! The Oren Nayiru has won. For years, Elrandar Silverspite has campaigned, and under the bladed-sun banner of Orenvalyam, he has conquered. His soldiers have carved a bloody path across Taychend, bringing "justice" and wreaking bloody vengeance on all those that stand in their path. Meanwhile, the warlord-priests who raised him to power reorganize the region according to the agrarian, chaotic, theo-democratic beliefs of their faith.

One must wonder what the Kamrayakval—now effectively ruler of Taychend—will do next. Some say that he is poised to conquer all Andeios, while others believe that Orenvalyam is weak and unstable, unlikely to survive the century despite its impressive victory. There are whispers that Silverspite grows unstable, that the Armour of Yodhan whispers to him in the dark, and that he is succumbing to the same madness that took its last wearer.

Maybe that will doom the Oren Nayiru's grand experiment. Maybe it will spur them ever further. Only time will tell. For the first time, the Oren Nayiru are no longer shackled by tyrant warlords or foreign interlopers, but are truly free.
