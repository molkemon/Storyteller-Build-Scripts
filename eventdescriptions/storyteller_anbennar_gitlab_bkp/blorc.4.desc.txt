The campaigns we led against the dwarves in the frigid northern wastes outside the Giant's Anvil of Krakdhumvror were long, arduous undertakings. They brought the forces of our lord to conflict with some of the most bloodthirsty and determined dwarves we’ve ever fought, cloaked in an almost servile cold that, at times, threatened to slow our unrelenting charge into their precious capital. There is much to behold of this place we have conquered; as its inhabitants were unusually unrelenting and stalwart, they protected their home with their famed icesteel structures and weaponry which now is set into our hands. Yet, we walk away with something infinitely more precious: the legacy of our triumph over the elements themselves.
