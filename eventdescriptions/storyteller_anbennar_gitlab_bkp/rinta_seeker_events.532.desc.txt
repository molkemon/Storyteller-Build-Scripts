No society is immune to corruption, not even in the past and not in the future. We'd like to think we aren't as corrupt as others, yet a massive scheme has gone unnoticed right under our noses. Large amounts of funding that was supposed to go towards the grand telescope have instead gone to seemingly nowhere. It's been eaten up by workers faking work, nobles misplacing funds, and government officials taking their own slices to keep quiet about it all. Every society has some corruption, but we didn't expect this much!
