The lord sighs as our current generals have been arguing nonstop with each other at the conference table over which path to go forwards. Despite their experience, our tactics have grown stale with a lack of new ideas, shown clearly by one general suggesting that he'll perfect the army by making them practice with loaded guns while another suggests that she'll just make them have to win battles for their pay. Those two generals, while very educated in tactical matters, are narrowminded in their views. It is clear the military needs a shakeup, but it seems we need a new angle, new ideas not taught in our war academies. We need an outsider. The lord stands up and raises their hands. They say, "Both of you, pack your bags. Effective immediately, you two are fired." As the two generals grumble while being escorted out by guards, the lord looks at the scribe in the room and says, "Sir, I want you to send a message to all the courts out West to find someone more competent than those two buffoons. Surely, a region, a province, or even a city has better generals ready to be employed." The scribe nods and leaves the room with his message, passing by the fired generals on their way out. Two months have passed since the lord has sent the call for military experts out west. The doors to the throne room open to reveal a middleaged professional, who straightens out their coat and approaches the throne. The professional kneels and explains that they have been squandering drilling armies back in their homeland and came here to make their mark on history.
