The most esteemed nobles of our realm have come up with the great idea of organizing a horse race, modeled after those of the elven lords of Bulwar. It is said that these help greatly in keeping their human populace in line and content, and so our lords think it may work here as well. However, the organizers cannot agree on the size of the races and so have asked our lord to decide, as their most esteemed ruler and the highest guest of the races, how large the races will be and how much they will donate to the organization as a result.
