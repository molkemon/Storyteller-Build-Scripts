With Tianlou now firmly under our control, our lord has proclaimed that the Jewel of the East must never be separated from our great state. With now characteristic fanfare, they have announced that the Undying Flame, traditionally a symbol of Jiantsiang's defiance against all enemies, will be shared with a twin brazier installed in the High Temples of the city of Tianlou, binding the two cities together forevermore, as our duty to protect our citizens now extends to them as well. A grand festival has been ordered to commemorate this most sacred event, for so long the flame burns, our nation shall not fall.
