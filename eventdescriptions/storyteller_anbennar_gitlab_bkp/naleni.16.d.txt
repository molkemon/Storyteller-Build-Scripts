To walk the roads of Riva Naleni without hearing singing is a feat only the deaf are capable of, and even they can't help but notice it from time to time. Music in the air near songhalls creates vibrations so strong they can be felt deep in the chest, much as the sound can be felt in the soul.  Though it is the Sirens who began the spread of our ways throughout the Telekenid, synchronising song and sky, the humans under our rule have come to see not only them as spiritual guides, but all of our kind. Throughout our land harpy and human now live harmoniously, the humans paying their rightful deference to us. In turn, their faith has spread amongst us, and many harpies feel a genuine duty to their human flocks, a genuine faith in those same beliefs.  Song extends far beyond the confines of faith however, whilst a hymn may be the most common thing to come to ear singing has become far more than that, entertainment, an industry, a true cultural cornerstone. From the humblest of lullabies to the grandest of shows, singing ties our people together. It keeps them happy. It keeps them in line.
