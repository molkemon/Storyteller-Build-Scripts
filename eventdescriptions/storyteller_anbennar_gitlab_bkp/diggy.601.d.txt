A report has come in from a maintenance crew working on the rail in a nearby province. As they began repairing a bypass, they were set upon by a hostile tribe squatting in our territory. A band of Norkers, feral, Goblinlike creatures, have set up camp along the rails. These beings are especially aggressive and greedy, even for Goblins, but this group is far enough out of the way that they aren’t an immediate threat to our active railways, and they are likely to migrate away when they run low on food. What should we do about these squatters?
