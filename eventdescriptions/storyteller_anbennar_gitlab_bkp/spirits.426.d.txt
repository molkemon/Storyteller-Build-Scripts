After an extended tenure on the throne, during which the ruler had heard countless stories and learned of various events transpiring around the High Temple Complexes, the ruler found themselves compelled to visit them. And so, accompanied by a select group of elite escorts, the ruler embarked on this excursion, led by a high priest who guided them through the intricate passageways. The guide meticulously shared their knowledge of the complex and expounded on the numerous theories and practices associated with the temple. Yet, as the guide continued to recount these tales with unwavering diligence, the lord's attention began to wane. Their pace gradually slackened, and both the ruler and their retinue of guards inadvertently became entranced by the guiding priest. This distraction led the lord's gaze to fixate on a faint, yellowish hue in the air resembling wisps of steam that seemed to beckon them forward. Drawn along this ethereal path, the ruler ventured into one of the many adjacent chambers. Their senses seemed to dull as they followed the path of the soft, golden fog, guiding them to an empty chamber. Suddenly, the ruler was seized by a bolt of lightning, or perhaps some other inexplicable force. As they scanned their surroundings and emerged from this trancelike state, the ruler found their recollection to be hazy, left only with the impression of a small, vacant room imbued with a golden radiance. Soon thereafter, the guards hastened to the ruler's side, swiftly ushering them away and ensuring their wellbeing. Trained physicians attended to their needs in the wake of this enigmatic episode.
