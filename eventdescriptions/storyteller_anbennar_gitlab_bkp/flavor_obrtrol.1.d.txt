Returning from the Forest Troll hunting grounds was our advisor and a brutish female adorned in wood carvings at least 10 feet in height. Our lord met the woman and advisor outside. The Forest Troll spoke to the point quickly, "We see how you take much human land. Hungry kids need food, I tell other troll about food, we let you share land." The advisor explained in a quiet whisper (what a human would consider normal speaking volume) that the troll is a influential figure among the Forest Trolls and would be very useful to get them on our side. No matter the choice our trolls will work to make sure the local trolls are part of our great kingdom. But we are illequipped for this voyage into the unknown, and they are not.
