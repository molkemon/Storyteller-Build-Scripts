The status of the skilled craftspeople does not rest on the same old traditions as that of the traditional estates. Instead they have had to make their own fortunes by working diligently and finding various ways to improve production. As a rule, however, this has gone very well for them lately. While not as prestigious, business is in many ways the backbone of our economy, and through their control over the means of production, these craftspeople have managed to increase their power over our country.
