Mere weeks after Thirendires was imprisoned for suspicion of treason against the realm of Ameion, his supporters have laid siege to the prisons and allowed him to walk out of its walls! To many in the Synkletos, some of the more lurid rumors surrounding Thirendires have been proven true - utilizing the power they have built up in local government over the years, Laskaris' supporters have now taken up armed resistance to our rule, demanding Thirendires be instated as our ruler!
