Unfortunate news from the realm of the Jade March. Ever since our disagreement with the goblin administrators, the wealth from the Jade Mines seems to have dried up. The metals we receive from the mines have been steadily decreasing, to the point where even the minimum quotas aren’t being met. Any attempts to enforce the quota are met with endless diversions, delays, and misdirection from the goblin rulers. As we cannot divert a large force to ensure that production in the mines picks up again, we will have to survive with fewer resources for now.
