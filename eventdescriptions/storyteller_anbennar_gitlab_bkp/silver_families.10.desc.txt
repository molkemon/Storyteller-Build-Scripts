Our country has been ruled by a famed Silver Family ever since the War of the SorcererKing, and we have prospered under their guiding hand. As the years have passed, a new dynasty has risen to power in the country, for better or for worse. Just a few days ago, our ruler received a visitor in their private quarters, with an intriguing proposal. Discussions went on long into the night and our ruler now faces a difficult choice. The representative of the dynasty that used to rule our country proposed an agreement. If we were to legitimise one of them as our heir, their name would surely strengthen the throne. On the other hand, the other heirs of that family might not give up their claim to the throne that easily.
