Brooding conversations and murmurs of confusion echoed through the grand halls of the realm of Qasri Aqoon. Mwalims and Darwiiz wondered why they had been summoned, promised a revelation. Their qualms were sullied as soon as the Great Mwalim Yableh unsheathed the Viscus, whisking into reality a power so deep, so profound that its presence brought shudders to even the most wise of Magi. Topics of conversation immediately shifted towards one idea: Can we now close the damned pit in the middle of the Jasir Doog? Can we save Ardimya?

"We do not know how we can, or if we can... but if there wasn't a chance to do right by our ancestors, then I wouldn't have summoned you here. If we overcome bitter rivalries and petty disagreements, we can restore the Jasir Doog to its former glory. We will be rid of the Qeyikul."

Cheers over prospects of glory and the end of evil erupted within the hall. People would be saved. People would be free.
