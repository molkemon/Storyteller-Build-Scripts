The foolishness of outsiders shall never cease to amaze. A group of neerdowell adventurers, evidently looking for some monster to kill or some ruin to loot, arrived a few days ago in a rural village. One of their number made the great mistake of trying to show their vile magicks to a group of children, and the villagers responded in the most righteous way; imbued with the spirit of the Martyr, they ripped the adventurers limbfromlimb and burned their remains. The village has already received gifts equal in number to their faith, and those slain by the outsiders have been given proper burials, but we are left with a pressing issue  The adventurers claimed home in a nearby nation, and this nation demands some sort of reparation.
