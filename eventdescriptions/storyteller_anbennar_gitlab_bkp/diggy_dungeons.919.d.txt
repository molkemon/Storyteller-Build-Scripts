Heading onwards from the Mites, after a few hours of delving the passageway widens into a larger room, with walls covered in webbing and several cocoons dangling from the ceiling. And on the floor, hundreds of palm sized spiders, each wider than a normal spider, with a spiky shell similar to a shield in shape than a normal carapace. These spiders seem fairly restless; it seems like our fight with the mites was noisy enough to perturb them; but they don't seem to have noticed our presence yet
