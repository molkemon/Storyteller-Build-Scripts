A transport ship lands in the capital city. Their lord, having been advised by their advisors that something special is in the works, looks on with increasing surprise. A loudmouothed teenager from the city looks at the foods and products being brought on shore and loudly asks, "Why on earth isn't that food buried in salt? How didn't it spoil?" The captain laughs boisterously and answers with a voice like a bullhorn. "Good question, young one. It's our latest invention; we call it the Sustaining Ark. We managed to put together a special, uh, cooling and cleaning artificery on ships like these. It makes sure that people don't get sick and goods don't spoil in transit." The teenager hollers in approval: "Wow. That's cool. No more salted fish." The laughter turns to cheers of approval. Their lord immediately has the captain and the artificers promoted.
