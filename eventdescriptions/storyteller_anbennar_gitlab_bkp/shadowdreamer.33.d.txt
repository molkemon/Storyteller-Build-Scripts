The tunnels open into a vast chamber, the darkness stretching far beyond the reach of our torches. The echoes which once followed us now slowly fade into the distance, swallowed by the caverns. We venture on, yet nothing happens. No shrieking wings, no more twisted shapes, nothing. The host stops, listening for the slightest sound of movement, but the only sound that answers is the slow, distant drip of water somewhere far in the dark.  The silence starts to grow heavy. Every shadow seems to twist just beyond sight. Every distant echo sets the soldiers’ nerves on edge as they tighten their grips on their weapons.  Perhaps Xazor is yet to notice us.  Or perhaps it has, and is preparing for something much worse.
