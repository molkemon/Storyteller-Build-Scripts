Old Sun Cultists have always been present in a distant land and the rugged hills. Disparate groups have survived in the region ever since the arrival of the elves, and their many small communities thrive in remote mountain valleys, sheltered by the rough terrain. The defeat of a powerful leader in the late 15th century ended this isolation. Jaherian Exemplars moved into the hills and set pyres ablaze throughout the villages, converting the meek and executing the stubborn. This new age of Darkness led to a rise of Old Sun Cult terrorism, culminating with the bombing of an important summit. The eventual discovery of the perpetrators, an Old Sun Cult sect with ties to a militant group, led to a new wave of persecution; which was seen by many Old Sun believers as a sign that evil had triumphed. The rediscovery of a lost city led to many Exemplars being recalled to their headquarters, leading to a marked reduction in persecution. The news of empty lands devoid of certain people and interests was seen by a religious leader as a sign from their god. Their faithful could build a new life there free from Darkness and persecution, a place to shelter and grow the Light. A priestess with hidden magic ability gathered the various communities and set off to build a new land for the faithful. With the help of smugglers and her own magical talents, she managed to build a fleet without catching the attention of the elves, which set off into the sunset in a distant time. These migrants would end up in a remote bay, creating the new state of a new nation.
