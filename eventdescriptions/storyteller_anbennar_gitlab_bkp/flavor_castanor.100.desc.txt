The lord has made their decision. We shall build a memorial for Castelos and organize a mourning procession from Aelantier to Castonath. The latter is a true logistical challenge, but our concern at this time is in raising one of the mightiest monuments Mankind shall ever know. Alongside the Legion engineer corps, the imperial architects have designed a towering Memorial. Stonemasons gather from all Castanor to help assemble what many already call the lord’s Tribute to Castelos.
