As our realm has expanded, so too has the influence of the Carver Cartel. While most internal affairs are split between the Carver Cartel and the elected clan government of the hold, the Cartel still reports to their lord in weekly briefings and their authority over the surface mines derives from their title. In practice, these briefings often lack key details of their operation and our neighbors inform us of the occasional incursion of dwarven miners into their legal territory. If this were not bad enough, the head of the Carver Cartel has sent a delegate to their lord demanding more autonomy in their own affairs.
