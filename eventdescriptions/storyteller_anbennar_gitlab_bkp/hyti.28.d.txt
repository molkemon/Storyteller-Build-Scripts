Sadly not every scientist respects protocols, follows safety measures, or researches with the wellbeing of other people in mind. Such amoral artificers pursue a path towards power and glory at any cost, with no respect for the purity of science.  The worst of them work in secrecy, using toxic drugs and even mages' blood as components for their trinkets and concoctions. Some go as far as to use live, intelligent beings as test subjects. When they succeed, they know someone as morally bankrupt as them will pay handsomely for the innovative discovery.
