The Clouded Eye has always been the most prized possession of the state. Our head shamans use it to see visions of the future that may come to pass and give direction to the state. Today, their ruler has approached the head shaman and requested a vision. The head shaman faces them and pulls a lever, raising the Clouded Eye out of the ground to catch the light of the full moon. As the moonlight hits the Clouded Eye, symbolism bombards them. Images of wolves fighting over a rabbit, seeds sown and blooming into flowers, and a scrawny halfork carrying a scroll and shouting into an angry crowd come to them. As the visions fade away, the head shaman looks at their ruler and asks, "Which vision struck out to you the most?"
