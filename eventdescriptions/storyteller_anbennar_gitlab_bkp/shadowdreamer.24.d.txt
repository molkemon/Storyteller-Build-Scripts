Years of dungeoneering has finally turned up results.  Hidden beneath a pile of rubble, one of the spelunker teams discovered a door of unusual design. Behind it, an ancient laboratory filled with all manner of wonders.  The Great Dreamer came to see it, and though he was shut in all alone in the dark for a time, he had no great vision or revelation. Even so, this is a discovery worth celebrating. The team who found it will be richly rewarded, and our best and brightest are digging into the dusty notes and tables of equipment to see what can be learned.  The bulk of the material seems to be medical in nature, and far beyond our modern level of development. Whoever worked in this lab seemed to have a strong interest in the relationships between biology and transmutation magic. Perhaps we will find some ancient technique for grafting limbs onto those wounded by war? Or maybe a better way to deal with blood loss? A cure for the Cannorian Cold?
