Marriages in hobgoblin society are mostly a material agreement, a way for families to strengthen their ties within their domains. Much like other parts of our society, the rules surrounding the refusal of marriage, the validity of divorce, and the content of marital contracts have never been codified, instead existing in a liminal space governed by tradition and customs. The time to introduce such rules has come. From now on, marriages can be refused by the head of the Unta or either potential spouse. Divorces shall be permitted in the event of dishonorable behavior, criminal offenses, or desertion. All marital contracts will henceforth be written and will be binding only if signed by the relevant Band Heads. These contracts shall permit the exchange of land and coin, but onerous requirements such as quotas for offspring are hereby banned. These changes are intended to promote honor and prevent familial strife, for the betterment of all in the Command.
