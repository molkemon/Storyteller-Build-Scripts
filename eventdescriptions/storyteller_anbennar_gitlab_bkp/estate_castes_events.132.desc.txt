Give the masses Khapati bread and shadow plays, and revolts will go away. These are the wise words of a long-lost Shamakadi ruler and they still ring true today. Giving the common folk distractions always works, and the nation is in for a treat. A circus specializing in shadow plays has arrived in our capital. Shadow play is the art of using shadows on a large sheet of cloth to tell stories of far away and older times. While they normally just come and go, we may want to invite them to perform in the royal palace or give them some extra financial support!
