As we have expanded out of our citadel, we have created a grand system of logistics to maintain our alltoofragile and threatened network of civilization. We hoped to avoid what has now occurred. We have run out of supplies for our mountain settlements entirely, and the settlers there are increasingly deciding they would rather risk the long and treacherous journey back to the citadels than stay in hinuibird territory, where they face either being eaten or their own starvation. Not all is yet lost, but if we cannot procure supplies very soon, all the progress we have made settling the mountains threatens to crash around us.
