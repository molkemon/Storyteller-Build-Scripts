The greatest thing we have ever done? I presume you are expecting what I am about to say, given how you asked me right after I was already talking about the Veil. How could I possibly say anything else? You have many personal triumphs, but I can think of nothing greater that we have done together than building not just a new world, but a new reality. A reality where tools stay put after use. A world where towns and villages do not disappear into the Fey Realm for days on end, where children can be safe to send outside unaccompanied for fear they end up in a glade. It is not total, of course, nor should we wish it to be. We are not who we are without our relationship with the Fey, and it is not all bad, as you well know. But even so, if the names Varinna and Elethen are to be remembered alongside yours in our history, it shall be for this.
