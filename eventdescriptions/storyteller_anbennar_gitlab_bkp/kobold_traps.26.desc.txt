In the Dragon Coast, we know Eaglecrest by a different name. We call it the city of slaughter. For so many years, they killed us. They flayed us. Eaglecresters made armor out of our scales and called us monsters. The gnomes may have had ambitions of taking our lands, and we may have constantly fought with the Moormen, but neither are as despised as the Eaglecresters. Only the city of slaughter could manage such depravity.

When we took the city, we made it our own. We erected traps and a citadel of kobold architecture, made to loom over the locals and remind them of their past. We built statues for each kobold slain, and inscribed the names of the murdered upon the doors of each household. Eaglecrest has been turned into a city of memorials, immortalizing the evil they wrought. Now, they rage against us for reminding them of what they did.

The Eaglecresters raise the old garrisons and bring out the old militias. They wear cloaks of koboldscale retrieved from the graves of venerated murderers. They have launched their last stand; and just like the others, they think they will wipe us out forever.

They are wrong. They have always been wrong.
