"Looks like tomorrow is the day. Just over the ridge is that nasty bugger, bigger than I imagined it could ever be. Five heads jutting out of its massive frame. Looks like someone lashed massive snakes to the body of an elephant. Now since the day is finally here, most of the good cheer… the little we had anyway… is all gone. Grim faces circle the campfires where the men fry up what might be the last piece of sinewy bacon they will ever eat. That dour mood lasts all the way up to the officers' tents. Here, the experienced monster hunters seem almost relaxed. Chatting about loves back home or somehow procuring tastier rations. I have overheard plans for the battle. Seems like our options are limited."; Ralambo Tallfriend, Courier to the Esteemed Leader of the Bibyobi Monster Hunters
