As follows, a day of petitioners in the court of our lord and the manner of resolution for each case:  A neighborly dispute over the ownership of cattle: the heifer was to be sold and its proceeds divided.  A cloaked figure that wished to stay upon the palace grounds indefinitely: asked to leave for the orphanage.  A charge of slander brought by a prostitute against one of her clients' wives: a certificate was issued testifying to the honor and good health of the prostitute.  A cloaked figure that declared themself "kinda homeless" and reported rejection on the part of the orphanage: asked to leave and never return.  A cloaked figure that refused to leave and lifted their hood revealing an ugly, illgroomed man, which some in attendance recognized as a prior advisor to the lord, and who elaborated on his condition as erstwhile tyrant of a large society of "hole people", now evicted from said hole following a republican revolution: court necromancer dispatched to reestablish tyranny in the hole, and grant any who wished it jobs in the army of our kingdom.  A horrible goose that somehow broke into proceedings: escaped following five casualties.
