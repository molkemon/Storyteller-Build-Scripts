The spirit of the great former of Daxugo is so strong that when they passed, they seem to have joined the three brothers to guide our people through these tumultuous times. Daxuusgen, more commonly known as Daxuu, the new god, who claims to hold the domains of reform and rationality is quickly gaining a following under the more religionminded intellectuals of the empire.  The shamans have carved a new mask, representing a dragon, as the locals view it as the strongest of all beasts, and even the river our new capital lies on, is said to be housing a great spirit dragon.
