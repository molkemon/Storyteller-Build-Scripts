The feast is in full swing, with more sacrifices being thrown on the pyres every few seconds. At the height of the feast, the ruler stands to give a speech. The speech mentions the declaration of the Empire of Mykx, the greatness of the navy and the great wealth that is flowing through our state. The most important part of the speech is the declaration of the title of Scourge of All Seas, and how it is delivered. As the end of the speech draws near, a clear distinction needs to be made: Will Viakkoc be ignored as a pretender to the title, or will they be invaded to make them relinquish their title?
