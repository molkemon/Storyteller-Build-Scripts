After months of sailing and years of preparation, Admiral Targuun's vision has been realized. The flag of the Violet Dawn meets its counterpart in Zhuhan for the first time in their own waters. News spreads quickly across their Federation, as this is the first time formal contact has been made with a civilization beyond their lakes. While many had heard stories from the Treasure Fleet about their way of life, many nonetheless clamored to hear of their spiritual practices, which they have likened to the Kukatodic people to the north.
