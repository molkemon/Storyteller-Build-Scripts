The banks are no doubt the biggest hoarders of gold of them all, all under the pretense of holding it for safekeeping. If we want to ensure the sustainability of our economy in the years to come, we must make sure that our banks stay healthy and keep their heads cool.  We could directly bring the private banks under our control, and stabilize the systems from within. Or we could force the banks to collect on their debts, essentially reducing the amount of gold hoarded by the individuals of our country.
