Every Taychendi ruler has warlords who serve under them, ruling their own realms and paying tribute and taxes to their overlord. When not on campaign with our lord, these local warlords are usually content to see to their own domains, waging petty wars and raids on the warlords surrounding them. However, the warlords of Taychend are ambitious to a fault, and when they see an opportunity for greater power, they take it. Whatever prompted them to believe they could overthrow us is known only to them, but nonetheless, a local warlord has risen up, hoping to take advantage of our low glory and seize power for themselves!
