The dragon head's eyes glowed violet as the Dauvek Veker spearmen stepped in close, pressuring it on all sides, beyond what even its three heads could track. Meanwhile, on the mountaintops, riflemen waited to shoot it down should it try again to fly up and away, a lesson the creature had already painfully learned once. With careful coordination and continuous aggression, lion, dragon, ram and serpent all fell before titanium might.
