In the weeks after the masquerade, word has spread of the chivalrous behavior of our lord. Many clergymen and pious nobles have reached out with offers of assistance and public donations. Several sermons in the many steeples of Gablaine have highlighted how our lord embodies the might and honor of Corin, the grace of Ryala, and the keen judgment of Nerat. Should we accept their offers, our standing in the eyes of the faithful will surely rise.
