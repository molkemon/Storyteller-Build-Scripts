Most of Taychend's rural population are free peasants, and without any restrictions to mobility like Ameion-imposed serfdom, they are able and willing to migrate away from their homes in search of a better life, especially when their homes and communities become overcrowded. Oftentimes, their destinations are areas recently devastated by war, where land and resources are ripe for the taking and the original inhabitants are generally thankful for the help—or too weakened to resist their migration. Recently, one such migration has taken place, with people moving into recently ravaged parts of our realm.
