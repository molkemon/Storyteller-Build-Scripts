We thought it a normal cave like any other, and in a way it was normal, for the Serpentspine. Unassuming, but on closer inspection full of wonder and danger alike. The first party was roasted alive by flames emitted by hidden runes, the second by ice spikes, also by runes. The third party was smart enough to bring a runesmith, and we discovered the entire cavern was filled with various runebased traps, arrayed in a defensive formation. Thankfully, they did find a path large enough to safely traverse, and ended up coming into sight of a massive sealed vault door, engraved with warding runes and reinforced with adamantine. It's obvious that whatever lay beyond that door was worth protecting, so that simply leaves the question.
