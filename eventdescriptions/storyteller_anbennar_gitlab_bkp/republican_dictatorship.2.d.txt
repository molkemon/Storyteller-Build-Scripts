Cautious policies on international trade and diplomacy have been distinguished by tact and caution, but in the eyes of the ruler, not sufficient progress. With absolute rulership, there should not be any need for hesitation. In practice, however, there may be good reason to hesitate. The leader might see a clear path ahead of us, but it is a path cut out in a world of monarchs and despots. Even with an autocratic ruler at the helm today, is this truly the future we want for our nation?
