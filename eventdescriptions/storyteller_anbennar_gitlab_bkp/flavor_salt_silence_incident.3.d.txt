Depending on the Parliament's choices, all Imperial princes of The Borders may secede and the Emperor will have to achieve victory or control over the powerful rival to regain them. The Emperor must have influence, a claim, and an alliance with an imperial prince in The Borders after today to automatically reintroduce them into the Empire. The Emperor and their electors will fight a war against their rival and force all princes to choose sides in the conflict. Success results in a total Imperial reclamation, while failure results in the loss of The Borders.
