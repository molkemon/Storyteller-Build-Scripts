"They call it the next step in Dreamforging, Great Dreamer." The officer gestured to the table full of gadgets after removing the cloth that covered them.  A few of them had obvious purposes. A brace for the arm that could hold another gizmo or strengthen the arm overall. A pair of boots with wings on the sides. Yet, most of these objects were utterly unfamiliar to the Great Dreamer.  After a long conversation with the "artificers" who had brought these inventions to the Drahmgall’s attention, he at least understood what most of them didand that the variety and specialty was more the point than any one item’s particular value.
