The sabari merchant houses of the realm have managed to amass an impressive fortune due to their prospering trading business in the last years, and have announced their intention to use part of these profits for the benefit of all the faithful. As a first major effort, they plan to finance the renovation and expansion of the temple garden of our capital. While such a donation is of course most welcome, some courtiers whisper that it is the most noble obligation of the ruler to see to it that the temple gardens reflect the glory of our realm.
