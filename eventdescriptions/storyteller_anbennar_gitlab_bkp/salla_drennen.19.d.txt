How would I describe our achievement? I will permit myself some latitude for self-aggrandizement here, because I can think of no other answer than magnificent. When I first met you, Narawen, I knew you were destined for greatness. I believe I knew that even before I met you, when I first heard your name, I knew it. But when we completed our work, at the culmination of our success, I knew what that meant; I knew what it was to be great.

When I walk the forests of the Thorn Grove now, I walk without worry of finding myself unwillingly in the Fey Realm. I walk without fear of pranks or mischief disrupting my way. That is not to say the Fey are gone, of course. I would not wish them so! But our relationship with them has changed for the better.

Perhaps such things will sound trivial to those who do not know our forests, but to me, they are as beautiful as the new moon or the first frost of winter.
