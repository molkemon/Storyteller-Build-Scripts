Hundreds of small villages were dotted around the rolling plains of central Adenica, and these windswept clusters were what the lord meandered through next. These plains had borne some of the first great knights and mounted warriors of Escann, and it was easy to see why. Many of the villagers had been riding horses since their childhood years, and were as comfortable on a saddle as on their own two feet. Most would never be more than scouts, but every once in a while, a truly great rider would make their presence known.
