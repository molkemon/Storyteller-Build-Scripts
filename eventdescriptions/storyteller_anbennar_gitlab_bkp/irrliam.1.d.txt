Thelrion, the brave little prince, sole survivor of the massacre that reduced the once numerous Irrliazuir dynasty to just exalted Taelarios and his grandson. His tale of woe at the hands of the perfidious Eliznans still brings tears to the eyes of every mother in the realm. Yet that brave princeling has grown into a strapping young man in the last decades, and needs to be prepared to take the throne, for even his most reverent grandfather cannot be expected to rule infinitely. Thus, it has been decided to send him abroad to broaden his horizons, allow him to study the different approaches taken by another Cinder Realm and gain valuable personal contacts in the process. The decision where to send him to receive a wellrounded education is a most crucial matter, for it will influence the shape of his future rule for decades or even centuries to come. Thus, a bitter struggle has been waged within the court of Medurubar, with advocates of several allied realms doing their utmost to convince sagacious Taelarios of the benefits associated with sending his grandson to their preferred foreign court.
