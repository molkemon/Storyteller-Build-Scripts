The memory of Karassk runs deep among the humans of Sarhal, to the point that in some rural dialects, Karassk is used as a catchall term for lizardfolk civilisation. The humans view the dominion of Karassk as a dark stain on their history, a period of repression and misery at the point of our swords, their hopes and dreams ground into the dirt. It’s all terribly dramatic, and they will not stop going on about it.  A gesture of apology for the acts of Karassk, however detached from our current government and way of life, might go a long way to thaw our frigid relationship with humanity. But it is a lowering of ourselves, however one spins it.
