The ruler is known for possessing a mind for organization. Making sure that people as well as the administration of the realm adhere to the law, while also updating and adjusting the system to fit the times, is of great interest to the ruler. Imagine their joy, upon realizing that their spouse shares many of these ideas. 'A conversation with you, dear spouse, is as stimulating as any council meeting,' they exclaim to their consort one afternoon. Their consort responds with a smile, also much enjoying bonding with their spouse over these matters.
