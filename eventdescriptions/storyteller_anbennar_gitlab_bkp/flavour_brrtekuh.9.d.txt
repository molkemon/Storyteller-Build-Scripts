While we were largely met with hostility by the humans of Kheterat, our work toward sorting through the ruins of the city has earned us some unexpected interest. These creatures appear to be shorter humans, yet clearly aren't human children. Identifying themselves as something called gnomes, they've shown some interest in joining us in digging through the ruins. These gnomes have already shown themselves to be innovative and clever creatures, and perhaps with a bit of guidance, they could be stellar followers of Kvanga.
