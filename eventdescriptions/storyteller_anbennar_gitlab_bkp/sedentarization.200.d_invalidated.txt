Our recent change in policy towards centaurs, viewing them as undesirables, has made the Gozabhar we constructed for the clans of this province obsolete, and the current governor's plans for the area are no longer compatible with the official policy of our realm. We must adjust our plans, ridding ourselves of what is obsolete and repurposing it towards a different end. If we plan to send frontiersmen riding out on horseback to claim the land, we need to build a base of operations and supply depot, while if we wish to fully tame the land, then a proper urban hub to administer outlying towns will be necessary.
