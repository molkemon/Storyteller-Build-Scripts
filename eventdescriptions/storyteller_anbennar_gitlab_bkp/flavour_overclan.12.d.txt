REPORT: It appears that the Order of Judges have historically served as instruments of legal consistency and law enforcement within Bulwar. The organization has continued serving this purpose to this day, but has struggled with consistency ever since Sun Elf authority over Bulwar fractured. Nevertheless, their role in Bulwari society is well respected, and is one that could be useful to the state. While the Order has previously served largely to uphold religious laws, their general mode of operation would easily suit the enforcement of secular laws as well.  Steps have already been taken to establish the Ministry of Judges in Azkabar, the traditional headquarters of the Order. Members of the Order shall be retrained and devoted to upholding the laws of the state, rather than simple theological dictates. With sufficient work, it is believed new generations of Judges can easily be trained and dispatched to wherever they might be needed within our state.
