With bated breath, the crowd gathers on the precipice of the seaside cliff. Beside them is the latest third-generation Zongmin-class airships, tethered to the rocks while the first batch of coal, extracted from the mines of Luogang, was piled into the combustion chambers after a brief treatment by the artificers. A group of mages were on standby, calming the constant winds that normally battered these cliffsides, but ready to release their spells once the balloon was airborne. Our lord stood alone on the deck of the airship, gazing out at Feiten Bay in front of him. No one could tell what our ruler was thinking; his face was a mask of stern determination. Once everything was ready, he gave the signal. As the black ore in the combustion chamber was ignited, a rising heat filled the silk balloon. The hull began to lift, the electroplated metal of the surface glinting in the sunlight. As the dockhands on the ground untied the ropes, the airship lifted swiftly into the air. When the mages unleashed their own wind magics, the Zongmin airships surged forward along the coastline, propelled by their Caged Firebird Engines. The crowd on the ground erupted into celebration, as the normally cautious jellyfish engineers jumped higher and cheered louder than anyone else. From the deck, our lord looked down at the increasingly distant takeoff platform and finally allowed himself to smile. Today, Feiten conquered the sky once more.
