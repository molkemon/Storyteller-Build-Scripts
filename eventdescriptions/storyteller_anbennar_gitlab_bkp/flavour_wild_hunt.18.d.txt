Our lord Caehn has been discovered in his tent with his throat cut. There do not appear to have been signs of struggle, indicating that he perhaps knew his attacker. His guards have begun compiling a list of suspects, but the issue is that there are simply so many people who might have wanted him dead. The neighboring clans might have wanted to kill him to further fracture our control of our borderlands, so that their clans might switch fealties. The Triunics could have sent an agent to further destabilize one of their hated rivals. It could have been one of our own ordsikar, whose grumbling has grown even louder and more irritating in recent times. It might be easier to instruct the guards to compile a list of all who did not want Caehn dead rather than those who did. Regrettably, this probably will not occur to the new lord, who is little more than a puppet of the ordsikar and seems likely to prove himself even more incompetent than his predecessor.
