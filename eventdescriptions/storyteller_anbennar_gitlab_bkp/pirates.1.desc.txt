A ruler rules over a nation of thieves and murderers whose sole political desire is to not be ruled at all. It is a task comparable to herding sharks, yet some brave or foolhardy soul must fill the role. The time has come once again to elect the ruler, and our ruler does not plan on giving up their authority. Our ruler must convince the assembled pirates through persuasion or dread intimidation that their rule is preferable to that of their rivals.
