The people around the grand amadian lake Chakantu beyond the Effelai jungle are hospitable and kind, with scarred bodies and an innate magical talent that they must suppress for fear of the wild and unpredictable effects their magic can cause - a curse, or gift, they carry ever since the Day of Ashen Skies and which they see as coming from the fragmentation of their great god of time, nawsayachaer. Azaly Stonecarver, our most prominent architect, has made plans to travel there to study their dams, as they have developed a mastery of hydrotechnical engineering in order to farm the snowless mountain slopes, and built great levees and floating gardens to draw sustenance from lake Chakantu. He hopes to bring this advanced knowledge back to Zena, and potentially have an exhibit in Medaka's Exceptional Treasures to teach children about the scientific advances of the people of the Onisqado Confederation.
