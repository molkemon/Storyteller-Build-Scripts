Today our lord has attended a feast thrown by our nobility. While some of our lord’s more cautious confidants were wary of such a regularly uncharacteristic event, our benevolent leader was quick to assuage their fears and attend the feast. Much to those same confidants' surprise the feast turned out a raucous merriment with much drinking done, stories told and drunken ballads belted out. Those within our leader’s circle rarely saw them as happy as they were that day. The next morning our leader left the feast with many new friends.
