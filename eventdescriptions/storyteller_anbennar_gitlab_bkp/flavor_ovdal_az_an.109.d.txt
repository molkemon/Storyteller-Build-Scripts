Seven seconds was all they experienced before everything went dark.  In the first second, the rush of magical power enveloped them and made them elated. The next second brought confusion. Suddenly, they were somewhere else. The third second brought an image of a rocky surface. By the fourth second, they felt cold. After five seconds, they began feeling dizzy, their field of view darkening. Six seconds, and they began to fall.  In the seventh second, they were dead on the moon.
