The nobles are getting bolder, and are now trying to engage the peasants and burghers to join their revolt against us and our status as a Free City. So far, they have had little success with this but we fear, as we always do when uppity rulers with fancy surnames are trying to shatter the peace we fight so hard to keep. What will the ruler of a foreign land think of us? Will they keep their promise and end these unruly nobles?
