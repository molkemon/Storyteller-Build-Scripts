Two colonial powers have become embroiled in a bitter dispute over a colonial province. While one colonial power is currently in possession of the province, the other has referred to its own colonial charter, which seems to establish their rights to these territories. This recently came to a head with several border incidents involving local militia forces, which have been gathering near the border. Seeking to avoid an outbreak of hostilities, colonial administrators from both countries have appealed to our government to arbitrate on the dispute.
