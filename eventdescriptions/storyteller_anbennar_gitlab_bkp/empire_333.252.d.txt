Although it is just one gear crank inside Zatsarya away from Halann, the distance between the space colonies and our realm feels greater than that—further than even Aelantir at times. There’s a growing sense that those back in Taneyas do not truly understand what it means to live on another world. Independence movements have even been growing on Independence Colony. Although small, it would be better to placate them before this niche political viewpoint becomes more widespread.
