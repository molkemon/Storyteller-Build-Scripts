Analyzed from the Gene Pillars and other precursor ruins dotting the realm, Precursor building plates are modular construction sheets of Precursor steel, an ancient and somewhat poorly understood form of metal of elven origin. Such modularity makes it relatively easy to implement in defensive architecture throughout our country, far stronger and more durable than anything currently available to us. Siege weapons so far encountered from other races have barely managed to scratch it. Combined with its resistance to the elements and general durability, it makes the upkeep and maintenance of forts far cheaper.
