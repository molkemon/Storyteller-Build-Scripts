"Matriarch. Matriarch." a breathless harpy shouted to the Matriarch "the goblins are leaving." "What do you mean leaving? Perzuk's avantgarde just baited the enemy in, I can see the fighting from here" "Their main army. That should be on the Ayarelleni flank. It never engaged the enemy, they just went away." A string of curses, to make any goblin's ear in Bulwar ring. "Order our swiftest, most agile warriors to harass the banners in front of us; retreat if they charge.; the rest, with me. We'll fly around and try to save our goblin allies. If we lose Perzuk's men as well, this war is lost."  Ballads would be composed of this day, the heroism of the goblins defending against countless aerial assaults, led by Perzuk's example, shining in his white armour; the whole force resisting until finally, in the nick of time, the valorous Matriarch and her harpies swooped them up to safety.  "Well, that wasn't fun." the Matriarch rounded on him. "Not fun?. We got out by the skin of our talons. If it wasn't for our enemy's supreme incompetence we'd be all dead. Perzuk, why the fuck did your soldiers shoot my people, we all wore purple to prevent this shit. And where have the rest of the goblins gone?"
