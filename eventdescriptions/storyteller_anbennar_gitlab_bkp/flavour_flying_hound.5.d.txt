There was the shortest possible moment before the orcs started mouthing off again. "I will rip your guts out!" "These chains are temporary, our rightful rage is eternal!" and "Your mother was a kobold!" were only the latest additions to the litany of doubtful debilitations they deposited upon their captors' pride. "I will cut your damn tongues off, I swear to Apzarko Hrom…" It was a terrible lie. No harm would come to them before the lord's decision was publicly announced. Thus the column of chained Rotcleavers inched forward evermore, each one angrier and louder than the next. Only at the very back, where the most important figures were tossed up onto a couple of carts, some semblance of peace and quiet could be attained. That was, until… "I wish to speak to your leader, stuntie." That one is meant for dwarves, what a moron. "We are headed back to Devaced so that you can do exactly that, greenskin." Come on, just try to jab back. "No, I mean your leader here. We need to stop for a bit." Nothing this time? "We are not stopping before Devaced, greenskin." Maybe once more will do it. "The stop is absolutely imperative to the collective safety of this convoy." Damn smartass, what’s that word mean again? But more importantly… "Why no insult this time, huh, greenskin? Too debased to act? Too slighted to taunt?" "Well, it’s a bit awkward isn’t it. I mean… we’re both green." "What, obviously not! I am of a hue most elegant and proud, the regal red!" "You look green enough to me…" "Impudence! We have bested you in battle! We are the victors, you are the losers! We, the conquerors, you, the conquered! We - the chivalric knights! You - the vile monsters!" "…stuntie."

There was the shortest possible moment before—

"I have HAD it with these mutablyjab orcs on these mutablyjab carts! Just you wait until Zozzok hears of this, I tell you, as my name is Zozzok, she will…"
