"My ancestors hunted beasts once. Through blade and banter, they acquired several astounding creatures for the Menagerie. I unfortunately lack their rugged constitution, but in a new world, Bivuzena requires new skills. My botanical studies have led me beyond the stormwall, called the Kiamoa by the locals inside, to the land of Kanolowele, an archipelago in the north of this newfound continent.  I have taken up residence with the Maxkanil Momi, masters of the coral reefs. Their songs are uplifting as I continue my research, and their stewardship of the ocean is astounding. They speak of entire islands made of corals in the south of Insyaa, and preserve colonies of polyps from there, colored a pale bluewhite like ghosts. I desire to study these colony organisms further, hoping to begin an underwater segment of the Medaka’s Magnificent Menagerie; a gift for the Merfolk who call our lands home."; Notes from Field Researcher Anase Kazuru.
