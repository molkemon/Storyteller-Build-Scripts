Truly, a wonder like this has never occurred in the history of the realm! To mark the ever-growing peak of the Dasr Sharaf, thousands upon thousands of the wisest men have come to gather within the confines of Abeesooyinka. Men, women, and children of all classes and races gather on the streets as equals to witness the march of their superiors towards the grand capital hall of Weggabiga. The reason? To advance the spread of knowledge for all Rinta Seekers.

The word Rinta itself means knowledge. In the context of faith, however, Rinta becomes much more personal: it represents what an individual treasures, their affinity to the world around them. Thus, it is essential for every Rinta Seeker to find what truly inspires them and delve into it as much as possible before their eventual passing.

Through this conference, the many mages, or Saaxir, hope to use the rising power of the Baashidi to expand the exploration for knowledge and personal salvation. Other conferences have sparked up across the province of Jasiir Jadid as well; it seems that news is coming from all over.

Let's stop by a few meetings, my lord, and explore the debates of the wisest in the lands!
