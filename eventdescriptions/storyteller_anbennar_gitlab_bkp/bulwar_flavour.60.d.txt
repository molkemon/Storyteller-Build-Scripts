The statue slowly took shape as the carving continued, but the man speaking loudly to the crowd did not seem to care about the noise or dust. "Not all heroes die fighting. Dalila evacuated the dozens of wounded during a gnoll raid on her village. Despite constant threats against her life, she persisted until a treacherous gnoll arrow struck her. Even with her dying breath, she kept carrying the wounded. For her sacrifice, we will bestow upon her the most incredible honour and grant her eternal life alongside the other great heroes of the sadnatu people; a statue carved into one of the caves of Mount Iskurthu itself. Here, she may always remain watching over our people."
