The Green Mile, that's how the locals call it. Our lord was looking at the reports from one of our cities, which has recently expanded its artisan quarters to accommodate more workshops for the jade carvers and cutters. It has not only become the center of jade production in our realm but has also gained fame across the continent for its exquisite jewelry for the nobles, talismans for the philosophers, and face rollers for the ladies, among other things. With the expansion comes a cost, and the middle castes now demand that we provide support to these new workshops and quarters. What will be the response of the crown?
