War, it is now war. The devolving relations between our political factions have now led to open conflict between the Dao. While we are not alone in our struggle, we must realize that we are fighting our own brethren. They are fools who have given in to fear over other realms, threatening our principles and conceding that we must give in to these modernizing trends. We must therefore act swiftly and defend the ideas of the Confederation. We must take their capitals and defeat their armies. This will hurt us all, but by being decisive, we will prevent the Confederation from abandoning its own ideas and principles.
