The Šadnatu have been a people without history. We have had no homeland, no realm, nothing to call our own for the longest time. We have been robbed of our home, our family, and most importantly, our god. However, no more. We have uprooted the bloodthirsty gnollish menace, and we have reclaimed our home once again. It is beyond imperative that we begin a program to rediscover our history, from the days of the misguided "god-kings" to the landing of Jaher, the Šad Sur is rich in history. It is only that we must find it.
