A heated debate breaks out among the expedition over the expedition leader's proposed incentive. No soldier can turn down an extra meal and more water will combat the heat. Sensing weakness, several mages are also arguing for higher quality rations and extra potions to go with them. With carefully measured words the expedition leader manages to convince the mages that emergency potion supplies are better kept for a pinch, but does still agree to provide higher quality rations. Not all the mages are satisfied, but sensing they're in the minority they give up on their protests.
