Was I involved? Narawen, you wound me. Of course I was. While I understand how such a bold move smacks of Elethen's recklessness, surely you did not think that she could pull it off without me? We plotted it together, right from the start. We knew your plans of course, we had counseled you, supported your ideas of a true unity of the clans for years by that point, but both of us agreed that you would never agree to what was really needed, not unless it was forced on you. We spoke to the others first; they came on board quickly. Alara and Lanhara were our best allies; they spoke to the other ceanns and ceannas for us, but by their account, it was not overly difficult to persuade them. Everyone knew it was you that had saved us, that had reclaimed these woods we call our home. We all played a part of course, but you are the magic at the heart of our incantation. In truth, you had been in charge long before Alara placed that crown on your incredulous head. I still remember the words.
