The contingent we sent to escort Lidrandel Duskweaver to her home; the Redglades; has returned. They've brought with them a variety of tales of the world beyond our forests, a multitude of treasures and, most surprisingly, denizens of the Redglades along with them. It seems that the Moon Elves of the Redglades were willing to share with us much of their knowledge, and even offer some of their own warriors to ensure the safety of our people.  It seems that some of the Moon Elves that accompanied our people back home intend to remain within our lands; others will return to the Redglades in time, now that we are able to establish longterm connections and communication with our distant cousins.  With this newfound knowledge; and contact with other elves; we will be able to find our people's place in this grand world.
