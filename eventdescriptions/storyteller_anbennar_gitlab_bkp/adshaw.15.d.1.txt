With our kingdoms recent conversion to the Corinite cause, many within the Reach have been seized with a divine zeal, eager to strike down evil and protect the weak. Already the numbers of werewolves in the Greatwoods have plummeted and the crusaders have now turned their attention elsewhere. Specifically, towards the north, the land of the ruthless Reavers who have harassed and pillaged our lands for centuries. An army of holy warriors has gathered to venture into the cold lands, and have appealed to the royal court for support in their endeavor. We are not at liberty to deny their request, nor would we. This is a prime opportunity to vanquish a serious threat and gain territory for the kingdom. By word of the lord, the Northern Crusade has begun.
