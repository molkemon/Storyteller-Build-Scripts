Hikari stood atop a stage, rows and rows of commanders, engineers, and inventors sitting before her. Listening.

"I stand before you all to deliver unfortunate tidings! Over the course of the Dragon Command's existence, the higher commands have stifled our innovations, our inventions, even denied our resilience! I ask you all: would they have denied us if we were one of the three higher commands? No! Until we are given the presence we deserve—especially the free access to the famous War Room—then we must refuse to be a part of the Great Command! If they do not believe that our Command is sufficiently experienced to provide counsel, then we will need to prove that we can provide it in droves! Who is with me?"

One clap, then another, and another, until the audience's applause was deafening.
