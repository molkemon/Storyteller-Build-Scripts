A thick smog of incense filled the room where the Court of Change resided. At its center, laid out on an array of cushions, sat Akhaxpa. As the ruler approached her and knelt down, she opened her three eyes, filling the room with a blinding blue light. "I see you have brought change to the great spires of our realm. But what you have done is not enough. You must focus your attention on one monument alone, and your rule shall be a monument to failure."
