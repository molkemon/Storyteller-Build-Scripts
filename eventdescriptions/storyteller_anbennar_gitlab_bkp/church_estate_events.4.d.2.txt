The Vaishyas of several prominent cities are accusing the established church of being greedy and indolent. The pious commoners are now withholding the tithe in a protest against a particularly bad example of misuse of the church’s authority. Pious Vaishyas are now objecting to paying tithes to the church as they consider it unfit to receive them. The church is furious and demands that we fine the obstinate Vaishyas.
