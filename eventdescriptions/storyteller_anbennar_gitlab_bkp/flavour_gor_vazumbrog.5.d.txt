To His Majesty, our sovereign ruler. Your Majesty, I write to report the progress following your directive to avoid conflict with the fungrum at Azmak Quarry and prioritize construction in a different location. Our military engineers evaluated the surrounding terrain and identified a highly defensible site to the north of the quarry. The location is naturally defensible and offers proximity to vital resources, making it an excellent choice for our operations. Construction is already well underway. We have begun reinforcing the walls, expanding sleeping quarters for our soldiers, and establishing logistical connections to ensure steady supply lines. The site's strategic advantages promise to bolster our presence in the region and secure our interests effectively. Meanwhile, the situation with the fungrum has left its mark on the garrison. Despite our officers' efforts to maintain focus, the soldiers have affectionately named the citadel 'Grip Brok,' or Mushroom Fort, a nod to the events that led us here. Morale remains high, and the construction proceeds apace. Your loyal servant, Durvin Ironmantle
