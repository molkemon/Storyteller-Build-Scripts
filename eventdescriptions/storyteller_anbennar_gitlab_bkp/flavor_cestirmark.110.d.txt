Three great peoples stand together, shoulder to shoulder, facing the rest of the world and rising above. Where the Ynnsmen lived free but downtrodden, and the Ynnics were flourishing but oppressed, we Trollsbayers who have both freedom and prosperity guided them towards a brighter future. Following our great example, the Ynnic valley will forever be a bastion of our country values such as democracy, while preserving the rich Ynnic culture that less civilized people would have sought to replace without much thought. We can rest assured that we will always find a friend in the Ynn as our sister realms follow our guidance, and that the realm of the Trollsbay will be secured forever. The great cliff passages have never been so busy: Ynnic students are just as numerous in our universities as Trollsbayer traders in the cities of the Ynn. Ynnic farmers provide for our cities, while goods from our manufactories help civilize their lawless borderlands. Today is a new age for the Ynn, but one we were already living in for a while ourselves.
