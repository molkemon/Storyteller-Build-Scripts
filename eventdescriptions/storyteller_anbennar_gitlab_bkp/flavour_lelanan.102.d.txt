Not all love was mundane. For Halann was a fraught world. The Shadow Curse was our greatest foe, for around the Hag's fists, hearts were twisted and curled.

Yet the Ashanna had discovered a way to shake their deadly bloodlust curse. And when one day they met a shadow troll, they knew the poor troll had it even worse.

So they sang a gentle song for them, and the troll felt their heart so calmed. They gently took each other's hands, and together their hearts were balmed.

But the Hags tolerated no dissent, and so they fought and ran and fled away from that blighted curse. For their love, they would be bled.

But they made it to our arms, and we welcomed them in so dearly, mending their wounds and deep cuts. And we found they loved each other so sincerely.
