The dire mismanagement of the Raj has led to a critical breakdown in the cohesion of the realm. As government ministers struggle to maintain any semblance of centralized control, outlying vassals that were already independent in all but name begin to take the final step and formally renounce their allegiance to the ineffective central government. Such losses are a critical blow to the legitimacy of the Raja; though some see the reduced administrative load as a blessing that will allow the government in Dhenijansar to stabilize in this time of crisis.
