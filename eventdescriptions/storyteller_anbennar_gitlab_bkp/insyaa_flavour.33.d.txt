Our advisor left yesterday on an expedition to chart a treacherous but potentially lucrative sea route. Although it did not seem to be a final goodbye at the time, the kobold left a letter to our ruler saying that he intends to see more of the world, along with some instructions on what to do with his vast stores of gold. The instructions are a detailed series of investments that are to be made in the businesses of his school's students and alumni, along with a few words of advice to each recipient. There is a small sum dedicated to our ruler as well, by way of thanks for the conversations the two had and for the opportunity to give advice over the course of years. In lieu of repayment, the kobold asks only that all the recipients of his wealth invest it 'wisely, justly, and kindly.'
