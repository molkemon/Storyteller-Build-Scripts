Despite the monopoly the ruling family holds on the office of monarch, our nation has seen a current of republican ideals recently. Statists have viewed this as a sign that our nation may very well be on its way towards their goal of a fullfledged republic, with some even going as far as praising the prince for permitting this shift. Regardless of how much truth there is to this, our recent political maneuvering keeps the dream of our government alive.
