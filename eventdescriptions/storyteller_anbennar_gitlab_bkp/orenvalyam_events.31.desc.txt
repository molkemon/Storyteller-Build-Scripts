Seeing Orenvalyam's decision not to expel the Cannorians, Valorpoint has reached out to secure an alliance. They share similar stances with us on slavery, and are generally pro-Ruinborn—in fact, many of them seem to have Boek blood, with their leaders having encouraged intermarriage with the natives. Although many members of both factions initially wanted a more stringent policy against the Cannorians, now that the Kavalakuttam’s policy is set, the Kavalalists very much want to sign, while the Kamrayakvalists are quite interested in the Valorborn’s military strength and zeal. 

But, as some Monasticists have been pointing out, the Exemplars too intermarried with the Ruinborn, and it did not magically remove their Interloper status. Unlike the Ozgar, the Valormen came to colonize Aelantir of their own free will. Could not the efforts to integrate with the Boek be seen as just a method of control? A far cry from free unions, surrounded as the native people were by foreign force.

The Monasticists already see the campaigns in North Aelantir as a dangerous provocation—to this group of the faction, diluting the faith's ideals by officially sanctioning such behavior would be a step too far for some.
