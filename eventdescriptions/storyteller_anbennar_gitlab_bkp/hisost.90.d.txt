The desert wind blew across the jagged sands; broken standards littered the ground and tattered banners flitted listlessly in the breeze. Two armies had clashed on the dunes, a meeting of savagery, discipline, and divine will. The fighting had been fierce, as sun fought sky on the ground below, but now the battlefield lay silent, save for the fading groans of the wounded and still dying. The battle was long over now, the foe broken and dispersed, the sun low and dim, settling beneath the horizon. Yet despite that, the sky shone clear and bright, illuminated by the fading light into purest blue. The sun is just a feature of the sky.
