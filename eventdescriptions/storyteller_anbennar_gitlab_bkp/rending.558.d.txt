Ruins in the Phokhao Mountains are not an uncommon sight, they can range from longforgotten remnants of Harimars empire, to more recently abandoned settlements. Our expedition has found itself walking the long abandoned streets of a forgotten temple, overgrown ruins and fractured walls surrounding what might once have been a place of worship. With the sun rapidly setting, the road ahead seemed much harder than we thought, and parts of our party have already started to find places to set up camp. If we don't act quickly, this short break will end up our resting place for the entire night.  If there was a reason this temple was abandoned, perhaps it is for the best that we press on. Although that would surely exhaust the party.
