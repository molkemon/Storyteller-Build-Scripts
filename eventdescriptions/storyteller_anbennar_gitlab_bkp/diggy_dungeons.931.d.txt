After loosening a few threads of the web and dousing them in oils and alchemical solutions, we set fire to the wall of web barring our way. The burning goes on mostly without issue; though the fumes seem to cause coughing and lightheadedness in some of our explorers, we're able to keep the fire in check enough to limit the smoke. When the way is cleared, we can see some mostly destroyed trinkets scattered amongst the ashes, probably the remains of previous expeditions not as fortunate as our own. Whatever their origin, they may still be worth something.
