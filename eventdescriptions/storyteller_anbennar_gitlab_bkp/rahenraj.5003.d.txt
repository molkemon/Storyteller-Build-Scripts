The troops were spotted a long way outside the capital, their armor and spears polished to a bright shine in the noonday sun. For a moment, guards feared their ruler might be under attack, until they realized the banner the company carried was a lord's own. They were quickly ushered to an audience with their monarch. In the Jungles of Ascension, Harimari proved their strength with red tooth and sharp claw. We may have used other methods, but this delegation made clear we have proven our strength just as well. The lord has taken notice of our recent successes, and in a scroll written in elegant calligraphy, proclaims our reward: we have been elevated to the rank of senapti.
