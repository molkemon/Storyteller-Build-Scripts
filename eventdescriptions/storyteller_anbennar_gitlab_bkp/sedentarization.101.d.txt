It has been several weeks since the first wagons arrived and construction began. Among the first things built were the palisades; in an unknown realm, and surrounded on all sides, the feeling of being exposed stalks the first settlers. The sound of thunder approaches, slowly growing louder... but the skies are clear. The lookouts sound the horn and the outpost prepares; a large contingent of centaurs charges forth, looming over the horizon. The tension is palpable. These are the first to come this close. But as they come nearer, several things seem out of place. The force seems too small to be an army, their archers are well within range but have not begun firing, and there are no torches in sight. The troop stops just outside the outpost.

Two centaurs approach. The first appears unarmed, while the second hands his weapon to a retainer before trotting forward. The second of them speaks, in a harsh tongue unknown to them, and the first begins to translate. "I am Ordsikar of this great Ordsiwyn. I will have an audience with whomever you choose to speak for you."
