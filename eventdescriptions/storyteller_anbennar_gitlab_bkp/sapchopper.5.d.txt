For nearly all its millenia long existence, Elynaile was the heart of Wood Elven society. It was in the Everglow Halls that the first steps toward elven unity were taken when the elven clans formed the Verdant Pact. It was in the Luminous Archive where the chronicles of Wood Elven history and knowledge were kept. And it was upon the quiet shores of Silent Lake that the elder Wood Elves spent their last years in contemplation.  No more.  Dreams of elven unity are but shattered rubble now, much like Everglow Halls themselves. Generations of elven heritage are now ashes like the Luminous Archive. And the serenity of Silent Lake lays broken, alongside the bodies of those in their twilight years.  The ruined city now lay in the hands of Clan Sapchopper.
