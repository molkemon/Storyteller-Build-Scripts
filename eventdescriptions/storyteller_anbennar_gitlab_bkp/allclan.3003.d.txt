Scandalous news, my lord. One of Harmaceuticals’ experimental substances, the much anticipated Gojuice 2: Rejuiced, has turned out to be a complete and utter failure in early trials. Formulated specially to imbue any given goblin with the energy of seven trolls (and a killer headache afterwards), the new drug has actually proven to lower activity levels rather than raising them. Harmaceuticals swear such a disaster will not happen again.
