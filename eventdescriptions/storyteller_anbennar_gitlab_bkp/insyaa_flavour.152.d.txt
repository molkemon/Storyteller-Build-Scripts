An insectoid-inspired form of locomotion, common among mechanims who interact frequently with Loa'set, allows our warriors to traverse various terrains efficiently. A chassis, inspired by insectoid body-plans, is installed on the lower half of the body. Eight legs, each made of small segments connected by ball joints for dexterity, are attached to socket ports on the thorax, designed to minimize body space for better maneuverability. This design creates a very durable and versatile chassis, decreasing accident risk for the user.
