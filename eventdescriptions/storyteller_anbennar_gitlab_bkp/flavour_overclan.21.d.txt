REPORT: Marked social tensions have been reported among our population within the Serpentsreach. While initially manageable when our ranks were simply composed of Exodus Goblin and Marble Dwarf settlers, other groups have proven far less amicable to our rule. Several notable examples include:; Kobold inhabitants of the Burad Forge displaying fierce territorialism, constantly repelling any attempts by other groups to settle these caverns.; Dwarves who never left the Serpentsreach, were not born under our rule, or otherwise feel no obligation to us have shown stubborn resistance to our rule, as well as a troubling penchant for violence toward goblin citizens.; orks and cavedwelling goblins participating in riots against what is perceived as undue authority held by dwarves and "foreign" goblins.  While violence is ongoing, it has yet to reach the level of fullscale rebellion against our rule. In fact, many dissenters, militant or otherwise, have expressed a desire for greater representation within our state. As this would not be incompatible with the current operation of government, it is advised plans be made to begin appeasing these malcontent subjects.
