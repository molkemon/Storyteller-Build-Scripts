Their lord is around the fire at the center of their camp. Dusk had long set in and drowsiness had come for them. They go off to their tent to sleep for the night, just before they enter it, however, they spots a figure at the corner of their eye. They quickly turns their head at the sight only for nothing to be there. They looks around for a moment before they turns back to their tent. It is at that moment they notices a wizened old woman standing exactly opposite the direction they thought they had seen that figure. Her sudden appearance startles them. She is a strange sight, covered in plants and mushrooms unmistakably of the swamp. Before you can get in a word she speaks, 'The man who made it didn't want it. The man who bought it didn't need it. The man who got it didn't know it.' They knows this riddle, quickly they replies with its answer 'A casket.' She grins and nods. She offers riches, wealth beyond their grandest dreams. for a price: get two more riddles right, and bring back a token of the struck deal the first thing they see when they returns to their home, do this, she says, and they will be amply rewarded.
