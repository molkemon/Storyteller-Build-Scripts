The Riei is dead. It is unclear how it happened. All we know is that his body has been brought to the foot of Rigoti for a burial ceremony. As is tradition, he will be burned at the foot of the mountain, and his ashes will be spread around its base.  For many, this is a tragic day. Another righteous king has passed on, after all. For others though, they see this as a new opportunity. From the wake of the weakness of our kind shall rise a new righteous ruler to guide us. This is the cycle of the Riei, and we await for the day he appears to climb the mountain once more.
