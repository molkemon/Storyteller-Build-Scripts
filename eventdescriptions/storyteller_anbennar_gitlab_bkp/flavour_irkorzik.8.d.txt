The Vog Mekza have gathered, prepared to choose the next candidate to brave the Trials of Sand. The tribes have sent forward their candidate; a great orator, whose position as a spokesperson for the numerous tribes in our nation would make them a natural leader. The Vog Mekza have their own candidate, a prodigy from amongst their own. There also stands a final candidate, one who excelled not in the political sphere, but as a renowned guard for caravans that crossed the Salahad. Now we must decide which of these candidates will take the trials.
