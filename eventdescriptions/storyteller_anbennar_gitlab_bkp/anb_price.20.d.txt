While paper is superior to parchment in many ways, it has never entirely been able to displace it. However, with the popularization of the Printing Press, books, pamphlets and even playing cards have become commonplace, in a way they never have before. The art of producing paper itself has also become far more refined over the last decades and these two things now coincide to make paper much more sought after than ever before. Perhaps the most visible example of this is the book market in the capital city, which seems to grow larger every year. Originally a fair for local printers, the fair now sees visitors from the entire continent.
