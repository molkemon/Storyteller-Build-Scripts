Burning horizons, advancing armies, chaos all around. But it need not be this way for long, the great Sir revolt has come and gone, and as the war camps of the Command fall like dominos, Haless breathes a sigh of relief. No longer does the Chimera loom over us all, salivating, looking upon our realm and our people as gems to be placed in a crown.

The war was a struggle; their armies were better trained and organized, their generals experienced and brutal. But through grit and determination, we have won. Sacrifices were made, deals were struck, and we will now have to deal with the aftermath of the great conflagration we have brought upon ourselves.

We have been beaten, but we were not broken. Freedom is once again seen on the horizon in north Rahen, and may it never disappear from our sight again.
