The Gommo has had their interest trained on Aelantier for as long as Cannor has known about it. As more and more of the continent was charted, this interest only intensified. There were more challenges to best, more secrets to uncover, and most importantly of all, more land to claim. Preparing for such an expedition is not cheap, however, so any actual excursions have been mostly limited. By the end of the day, this will no longer be the case.  Though it has taken them a long time to get the funds, the Gommo now has the resources to set sail. Their expedition, promising land and wealth to any volunteer laborers, will be setting sail to set up an outpost named Gommoport at the mouth of a river known as the Harafroy. The organization seeks to utilize and best the unique challenges presented by Aelantier to advance the cause of artificery.
