The leader of the Ikhsat flock we aided cycles ago has come to us in yet another private meeting, this time the one who comes is not the old matriarch but her daughter. "My mother told me what you did for us years ago, she was always very bitter about not being able to give her thanks in a proper way for fear of retribution by the other flocks, I think it's the only regret she had really, I come to fix that, debts like these can never be repaid,and so I should not fear repercussions, we would’ve been long dead had it not been for you, what I do is paramount to trying to fill a cave with droplets" she hands you a scroll detailing all the flock will grant in exchange for the help and an oath loyalty for as long as the mountains endure.
