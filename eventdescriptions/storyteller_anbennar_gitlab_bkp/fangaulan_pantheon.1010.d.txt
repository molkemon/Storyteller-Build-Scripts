Come one, come all before the eternal flame of Fojara the great lion. We will sing his songs, and we will dance around the flame as we please, and we will never be burned, for Fojara would not harm the people of Fangaula. Look now at the way the beasts and the pilgrims both bow to the flame: this is the tribute paid to a protector, not an overlord.  I will sing whichever tale you like. I may tell you why Fojara, the king of beasts, is renowned for his great courage. I may tell you of Dulugu the Hungering Lion, Fojara reborn. You need but choose and I will play my lute with all my skill to make the glories of Fojara come to life again. In his honor I could do no less.
