Disaster! Our realm in Endralliande has been struck by a massive revolt; the local orcs are in open rebellion. Following an apparently spontaneous uprising on three nearby provinces along Endral’s Coast, a huge and vexingly well-organized force of freed orc laborers has begun looting our settlements for food and supplies, overwhelming our local garrisons and butchering innocents. These savages are led by a figure known only as Bramash, some hedge mage—the sort the orcs call a ‘shaman’—who has set his talent as an abjurer to the purpose of murdering innocent civilians. His forces threaten the entire region, and are growing with every province he puts to the torch. A preposterous set of dispatches from this Bramash’s so-called ‘Cabinet of Freedom’ have already slipped out of our ports in the region, and our enemies will waste no time in slandering us based on such lies.
