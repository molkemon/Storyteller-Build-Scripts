Nobody can deny the glory of a wyvernrider in flight; one’s instinct is always to see them at the very front, bolstering the resolve of the Vernman armies. Yet the canny minds among our logistical corps see a more strategic use for our wyvernrider cadres, as suppliers and skirmishers at the flanks of our column. This is not a mere question of heart versus head, for the wyvern charge truly does bring about terror in the enemy, but as our logisticians point out, we take serious risk placing such valuable assets so close to opposing fire.
