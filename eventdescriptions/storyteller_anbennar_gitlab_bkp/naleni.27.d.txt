A monthly report from an advisor drones on and on, until they happen to mention something interesting.  Culture continues to bloom in our nation, and large amounts of our people, harpies and humans alike, have grown to idolise our most impressive figures; Sirens, Odanchesta, even the royalty. Many, especially amongst the young, dream of becoming ‘famous’ themselves, and the fortune they may amass. These groups loom large in the cultural consciousness, and the advisor suggests that perhaps by promoting one of them, we may gain some sway with the people.
