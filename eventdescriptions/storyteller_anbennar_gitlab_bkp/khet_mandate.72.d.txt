The pleasure barges are an inseparable part of a Khet's life. Designed specifically for their needs, these constructions are the living testament to the ingeniousness in which Kheteratans try to accommodate their gods' needs. For one of the Khets, this design failed as he raised the Sorrow, so that he could disembark right from the edge of the ship. What he did not expect was the port he wanted to depart to, sinked into Sorrow, as he promptly brought everything back to normal.
