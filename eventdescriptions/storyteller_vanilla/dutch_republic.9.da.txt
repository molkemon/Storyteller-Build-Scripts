The reign of the monarch is all but absolute. The Princes of Orange have secured power over the realm for a long time ahead. Everything points to the ruler ruling for their entire life. The spy master, one of their closest advisors, agrees with this assessment. This is a person fiercely loyal to their country, so loyal, in fact, that they would personally take the life of anyone who threatens the republic. In a recent meeting with a few carefully selected people, the spy master let it be known that the ruler themself and the Orangists' stranglehold on the rulership is perhaps the biggest threat of all. The spy master can assure that the lifetime rule will be a short one.
