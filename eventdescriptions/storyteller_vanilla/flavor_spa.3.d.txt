With the fall of Granada, the last Muslim bastion of the Iberian Peninsula is now in Christian hands. Among the subjects there are still, however, a large number of Jews, Muslims, and converts of questionable sincerity known as Marranos and Moriscos. In order to create a truly universal Catholic state, it has been suggested that they force the Jewish population to either convert or leave the country forever with what possessions they have that are not of precious metals.
