The capital of so many rivals is in the nation's hands. From the ancient Roman Empire to the Turkish Empire ruled by the House of Osman, this great city has always been of strategic and cultural importance to both the European and the Islamic Worlds. Now this most prestigious metropolis is in the nation's hands. Some in the court have argued that this city could be developed into a "Second Capital" on European soil, while others are of the opinion that they should move the city's riches to the capital instead.
