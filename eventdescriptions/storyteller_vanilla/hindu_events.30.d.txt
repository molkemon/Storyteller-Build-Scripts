Your devotion to Lord Vishnu does not preclude discussion of the spiritual with Shaivites. The ruler of that country is a follower of Shiva who has nevertheless been a valuable ally, a friend, and today, a teacher. Speaking of one of Vishnu's followers, the storm god Rudra, you find the ruler identifying him with Shiva himself. Their description of the deity is vivid and inspired, making a convincing argument that it is Rudra who brings religious knowledge to mortals.
