While on a hunt, our designated heir was ambushed by a band of unknown assassins. Outnumbered and attacked from cover, all but one fell there and then. The survivor, though wounded by a poison arrow, lived long enough to tell us of a valiant last stand of the heir. Tall and mighty he stood, cutting the creatures of the night one by one as they surrounded him, until the last of his bodyguards succumbed. Shaken and in mourning, our people celebrate the legacy of the heir as he achieves immortality in their memory.
