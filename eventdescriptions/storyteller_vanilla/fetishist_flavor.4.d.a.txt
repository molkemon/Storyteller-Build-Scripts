In preparation of battles to come, the monarch recently went to see their Babalawo. The father of mysteries possesses wisdom of the Orunmila and any insight into the destiny of the monarch's Ori is always welcome. The diviner left the monarch confused however, as they were told that their heir somehow will bring the monarch a premature death. A battle? Poison? An accident? "Which will it be?" the monarch demanded to know, but the signs were not clear; dealing with the matter is now a choice of the utmost delicacy.
