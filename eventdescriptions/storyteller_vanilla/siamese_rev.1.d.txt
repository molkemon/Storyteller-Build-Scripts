As our ties with the Westerners grow, there are many within the realm who view the foreigners with suspicion. Their fears are many; some believe that foreign diplomats are softening us in preparation for an invasion, while others see foreign missionaries as a threat to our faith, while merchants are concerned that our deals with the Europeans are disadvantageous to our commercial interests. Anti-European sentiment is growing rapidly in the realm and the situation threatens to come to a head in the near future.
