The annual celebration of the royal union between the monarch of the realm and their consort has taken place. It is difficult to imagine that the country had it any other way; that there was a time when the consort was growing up in a distant domain, far away from the monarch, who spent their days in the capital alone. It all seems so natural now. How time flies. It has now been over two decades since the consort married the monarch, and even though they are not children anymore, it is with some glee that they accept gifts in honor of their wedding anniversary.
