The nation is at war. The nation is laying waste to its enemies, leaving destruction and desolation in its path. In the midst of leading this onslaught, you have had little time for meditation or any sort of religious thought. However, one day, you turn to look at the blood and fire left in the wake of the advance. For a moment, you think that you may have lost your way as a righteous follower of Shiva. An inquisitor, a person of great spiritual devotion, rides up to your side. "My lord," they say, "do not look back. The world must burn for it to be reborn. Lord Shiva, too, destroys to recreate."
