A story of heinous deeds has reached us from the far away city of Calcutta. It appears that an English settlement there has been attacked by the local ruler. The traders and soldiers in Calcutta are then reported to have suffered the horrible fate of being thrown into an infamous dungeon known as the Black Hole of Calcutta. Ever since news reached us, we have been besieged by Indian merchants urging us to act now and restore order in this barbaric part of the world.
