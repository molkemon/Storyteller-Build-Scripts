The people as well as the ruler must sometimes rely on the wisdom of holy teachers for advice. A guru is not only a teacher, but also a student of the spiritual and must sometimes withdraw to reflect on it undisturbed. One of the most respected gurus, whose knowledge was frequently sought by the ruling family, has grown extremely reclusive lately, spending almost all of his time in deep meditation. The court and the people grow restless, viewing this as a bad sign.
