Under King Hans, who came to the throne in fourteen eighty-one, the fleet was given a more organized structure, and during his reign large ships were built by royal order. The 'Engelen' was built in Sønderborg, where there was a good supply of oak for shipbuilding. The 'Maria', which was just as big, seems to have been built in Copenhagen, which has gradually become the home port of the fleet. By fifteen oh nine Denmark had one of the largest and best navies in Europe.
