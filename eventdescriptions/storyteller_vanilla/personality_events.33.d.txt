A number of economic investors have approached the monarch asking for the right to develop one of the areas under their control. They would pay a lump sum for the right to extract revenue from their lands, and it would then be up to them to collect the money in their stead. The investors are quick to point out that it is in their interest to invest in the local infrastructure to increase the chances that they will make more than the sum they have paid for the right to their area.
