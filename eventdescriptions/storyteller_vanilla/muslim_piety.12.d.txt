Guilds and fraternities are a constant factor in the public life of the realm. In many ways, these are small communities within the community, with their own rules, customs, and even laws. It is also very common for these associations to indulge in mystic traditions, sufism, and often unorthodox adaptation of the Sharia, something that sometimes makes it hard for the laws of the Ulema to penetrate the urban milieu. An artisan in a local province has reached out to the state, warning them of a local guildsman with great influence on local affairs. Anyone wishing to take up a craft in the city is currently required to be approved by this man and then swear an oath of allegiance.
