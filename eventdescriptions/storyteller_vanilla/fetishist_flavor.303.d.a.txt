We have always been able to turn to the razana, our ancestors, for guidance and counsel, but now there is nothing but silence. The ancestral lands of our nation's elite, including the great dynasty mausoleum, have been lost. Words have reached us of plundered tombs and desecrated remains. We are cut off from the ancestors and thus from the great Zanahary. Where shall we turn now? Who shall lead the way? There are many who doubt whether the monarch should rule our nation if they failed to protect that which is most holy.
