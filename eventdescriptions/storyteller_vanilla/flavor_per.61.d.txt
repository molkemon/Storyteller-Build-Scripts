Success brings wealth, and wealth often comes with prestige, legitimacy, and power. To that end, many of our adventurous merchants have traveled to and from the legendary palaces of the Great Ming, returning to our courts and markets with many strange, foreign, but breathtakingly beautiful textiles and materials. Among them, blue and white porcelain was reserved for the ruler as a personal gift by the emperor of China himself. This porcelain will surely adorn our tombs and palaces, granting us an air of legitimacy and newfound standing.
