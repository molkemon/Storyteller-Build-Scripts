The monarch has been more at peace since the funeral of their cousin was seen to. The elaborate ceremony was even attended by diplomats from a neighboring country. The knowledge that the ruler's relative received all the rituals required to maintain balance with the spirits has brought the ruler great comfort. In the evening a light breeze runs through the monarch's chambers, making them smile; the Sky God has many ways of making their presence known. Then they are still together, the monarch says, before drifting off to sleep.
