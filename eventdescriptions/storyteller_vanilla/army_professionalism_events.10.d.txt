With the growth of our professional army, new career opportunities abound for those with ability and ambition. The old noble families, however, still expect to be awarded all the most valuable commissions out of tradition and the centuries during which their lineages have served the realm faithfully. Some of our higher officers are now imploring us to open more opportunities for able men to rise in the ranks, while others oppose this vehemently and maintain that there are qualities only blood can assure.
