His Imperial Majesty, the sovereign of the house of Osman, Sultan of Sultans, Khan of Khans, Qayseri Rum, and Emperor of the Three Cities of Constantinople, Adrianople, and Bursa, has a reputation as a great man and is unchallenged as a ruler. As befits a man of his caliber and stature, the Sultan has fathered several capable children, and many who show great promise as future rulers. As both the sons themselves and the government officials are aware, however, only one can inherit, and there is little in the way of consolation prizes. The Viziers are adamant that it is now high time for the monarch to name a favorite among the possible successors. This is unlikely to end the competition for their favor, but it will hopefully stop the worst succession struggles that might follow if the ruler should meet his maker without having picked a clear successor.
