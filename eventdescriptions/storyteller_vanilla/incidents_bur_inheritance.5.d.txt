Sad news reaches us today as we learn that our beloved Marie, Duchess of Burgundy, has passed from this life into the next. A sweet and kindhearted girl, Marie will be sorely missed in the noble court, but now that she has passed, it would seem that we are to be inherited by the successor. Many nobles of the realm are already bending the knee to the ruler of the domain, but many others are beginning to flock around a distant cousin to Marie and one of few surviving members of the Burgundian royal family. Should we submit, our government will be no more and we shall be annexed into the noble realm. Should we recognise the weak claim of the Burgundian pretender, we shall no doubt enter a state of war with the domain.
