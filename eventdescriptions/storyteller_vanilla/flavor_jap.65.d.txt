For far too long has our Divine Emperor been outshined by the military dictatorship of the Shogun. For far too long has His Divine Blood been relegated to linger in the shadows. Fortunately for us, unlike His ancestor the Sun Goddess Amaterasu did when She secluded Herself inside the cave, depriving the world of light, He didn't resign himself to living unfruitfully imprisoned inside the Imperial Palace. Instead, He toiled continuously, using His influences to secure favors and support. With time, He managed to secure enough support to be able to force the Shogun to abolish the shogunate and reinstate the Imperial Line as the effective ruler of Japan. With a Living Kami again at the forefront of the country, our nation will once again enjoy divine prosperity.
