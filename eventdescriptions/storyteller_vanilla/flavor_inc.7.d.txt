The coastal Chimu people are the carriers of one of the oldest cultures in the Andes. As their lands have now come under our control, we cannot help but marvel at the works of their artisans and craftsmen. The Chimu silversmiths, weavers, and ceramicists are producing treasures such as we have never seen before. The arrival of these treasures, as well as many of the craftsmen themselves in our royal capital, is now celebrated in great feasts. By integrating the Chimu, we have created a link to the glorious past of this region and taken a necessary step on the road to a new PanAndean state.
