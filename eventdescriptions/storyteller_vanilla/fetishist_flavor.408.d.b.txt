The Wotan, chief shaman of the seidkonur seeresses, has approached them concerning the ruler's lack of children. While they have mainly been worried about the line of succession, the Wotan is also worried about the ruler's devotion to Freyja. She suggests a fertility rite in which the ruler will lie with a seidmadur, one of the rare male seid practitioners. If Freyja is presented with sufficient respect and sacrifices, the Wotan is convinced that such a union will produce a strong and blessed child.
