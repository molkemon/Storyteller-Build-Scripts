After the death of Mansa Suleyman Keita, the brother of the mighty Mansa Musa, the empire of the realm has seen a constant decline in power, influence, and wealth over the decades. Civil wars, fights for the thrones, and a series of incompetent mansas undermined the stability of the realm. Former subjects such as the Songhai or the Timbuktu declared their independence from the empire, and even now the domain is seeing further and further fall in importance.
