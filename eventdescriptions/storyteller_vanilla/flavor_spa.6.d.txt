The capital has served the predecessors well, but it has been suggested that the small city of Madrid, with its central position and welcoming climate, would be a better location from which to rule all the Spanish realms. The existing Real Alcázar de Madrid could be expanded into a suitable royal residence while the city in general is still undeveloped enough to offer a blank sheet for the city planners of the kingdom. Let us build a new capital fit to rule a Great Empire.
