Sometimes, we have the chance to set something into motion without seeming to have done anything. One such chance has arrived recently, when a cardinal living in a certain province admitted to an agent from our country that he would consider moving across the border to another province and support us in the Curia for a price. While this may be tempting, there may be a greater price to pay if we take advantage of this. the wrath of the country he would be deserting.
