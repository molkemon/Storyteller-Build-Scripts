The monarch, in awe with all the discussions happening in the Ibadat Khana, has chosen to do a radical act; they have decided to create a new religion based on the syncretism that has been occurring in India during their rule. The sect has many names but it is mainly known as 'Dini Ilahi' meaning 'Religion of God.' Many in the court have chosen to join them in this religion, but it is not certain whether it will gain any widespread support. What is certain, however, is that, if accepted, this will strengthen the position of the monarch.
