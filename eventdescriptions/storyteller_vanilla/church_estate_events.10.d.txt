The time has come for the church to appoint a new leader to assist the monarch in the capital. The likely candidate is an old cleric with a solid history as a prominent member of the church behind him. He has, however, also acquired many enemies during his life who now hope to see a somewhat younger candidate with a less ugly past be appointed. As the monarch, it would not be hard to influence this election to the younger candidate who would likely be more loyal. To be seen to meddle in the affairs of the church, however, would very likely weaken the faction in the long run.
