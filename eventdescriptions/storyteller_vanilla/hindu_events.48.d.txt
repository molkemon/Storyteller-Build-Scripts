The realm has been without eligible heirs for some time, which has worried members of the ruling family. A princess of the proper lineage has recently given birth to a child who would be next in line to the throne, were it not for the fact that she is unmarried. Being versed in the legends of the Mahabharata, however, she recounts the tale of how the great king Karna was born of a virgin by the sun himself. The princess explains that she too learned the mantra by which the princess of legend became pregnant and that her child, the child of the sun, is destined for great things.
