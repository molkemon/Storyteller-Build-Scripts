The current war with the enemy country has triggered a round of intrigues in the realm court. On the one hand, an advisor, our culture advisor, has offered to draw information from their network of contacts in the enemy country; a considerable network, they claim, including several contacts close to the enemy monarch. This is to aid the war effort. On the other hand, their political opponents have raised doubts about their loyalty. They argue that, in spite of their previously exemplary service, we simply cannot trust a culture member to participate in deliberations of national importance at this time. They therefore demand that they be banished from the court.
