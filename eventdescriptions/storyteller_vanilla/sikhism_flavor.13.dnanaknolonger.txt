A man who some call a guru has arrived in this realm. He claims to have received a revelation, being called by Heaven to travel from his home to distant lands, spreading the message of the one true God. His local disciples insist that this God is revealed through all religions, including the local faith, and defend him as simply revealing the truths in our own faith. However, there is no question that these teachings reject our rituals and traditions, making people stray from the true path of the local faith.
