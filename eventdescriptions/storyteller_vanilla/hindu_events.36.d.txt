During your rule, the capital city has stood out as the pinnacle of art and science, the envy of its neighbors. The nation shows other nations the way on the quest for knowledge and innovation. Knowing that Ganesha values wisdom, it pleases you that the nation has been successful in the search for it. Yet at the same time, you find that the more you advance, the more obstacles you find impeding your research. It has struck you more than once that Lord Ganesha, who creates and shifts the obstructive elements of the world, may not only aid your studies, but also make them more difficult.
