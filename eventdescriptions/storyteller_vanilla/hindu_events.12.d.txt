Maintaining marriage bonds with other countries has helped the realm diplomatically. In a recent meeting with the monarch of another country, you also discussed the spiritual significance of the bonds of marriage. The monarch and many of their ambassadors were followers of Vishnu, and knew Shakti as Vishnu's consort Lakshmi. You took this opportunity to speak to them about the importance of the goddess as a medium to the divine. Your words seemed to sink in, and their leaders now have a better understanding of your faith as well as the value of your royal marriage.
