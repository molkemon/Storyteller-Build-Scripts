The young Guru Har Krishan has tragically passed away. After some confusion about who his successor might be, Tegh Bahadur has been chosen as the new Guru. Initially a reclusive man, he has risen to the occasion and proven to be a determined leader. Despite his own rejection of rituals and ceremonial practices, he has taken on the task of safeguarding not only the religion of his own followers, but of Hindus who flee to our lands from persecution.
