In the midst of the chaotic Georgian Crisis, a courier arrives from the southwestern reaches of our realm. He brings a message from the Atabegs of Samtskhe, and his grim countenance bodes ill. The Atabegs have declared their independence, unwilling to remain under the faltering rule of the Bagrationi dynasty. This daring move is a chilling example of the damage our internal strife has wrought. The separatists see the weakened state of our kingdom as an opportunity to establish their rule, free from the burdens and controls of the central authority. The blow to our sovereignty is profound, adding to the challenges we face amidst the crisis. How we respond to this affront will have far-reaching implications for the fate of our kingdom.
