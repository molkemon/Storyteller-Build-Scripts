The Revolution has arrived. Following weeks of protests, crowds gathered in unprecedented numbers in the capital to demand greater freedoms for the people of India. Although the monarch ordered their troops to fire on the masses, they were unwilling to do so as the demonstrators refused to provoke them through violence or insults. Facing the ever more desperate orders the ruler continued to issue, demanding ever more extreme punishment of all dissenters, the army decided instead to relieve them of their position. The monarch was summarily ushered off into exile as a new Republic was declared. The new rulers of the country promise to reorganize the state by redistributing land to the peasantry and establishing full equality under the law, abolishing the worst injustices of the past regime, and leading all of India to a brighter and freer future.
