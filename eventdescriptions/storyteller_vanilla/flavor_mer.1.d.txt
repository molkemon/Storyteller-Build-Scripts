In order to commemorate his victories over many enemies such as the Sultan of Malwa, the treacherous Rajas of Marwar, and the greedy Sultan of Gujarat, Rana Kumbha began the construction of a unique monument in fourteen forty-two. Taking over ten years to complete the Vijaya Sthambha with its richly decorated nine stories is a testament not only to their dynasty but also the skill and craftsmanship of their sculptors and architects.
