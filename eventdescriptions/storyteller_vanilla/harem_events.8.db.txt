Ever since he was a young Sehzade in the Imperial Harem, the monarch has had his mother by her side. She has offered advice and protection throughout her career, and as she ascended the throne, she remained one of his closest confidants and most influential advisors. The news of her untimely death has come as a surprise and a heavy blow to the ruler, who has now lost a good friend, an advisor, and his mother far too soon.
