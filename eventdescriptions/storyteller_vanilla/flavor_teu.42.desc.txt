The Teutonic Order, an order of noble German knights, was established on the Baltic coast in order to Christianize the local tribes. Although this mission was accomplished decades ago, they have decided to stay in the land of the Old Prussians and make it their home; however, much to the displeasure of Poland. In the last forty years, their Order has fought four wars against Poland and its allies. As a result of the recent setbacks resulting from these wars, the rich cities of Prussia are plotting against their authority. The Prussian Confederation is a looming threat which will need to be dealt with swiftly. But not all looks grim for them. The Teutonic Order is at the crossroads of its future. If they manage to defeat the Polish threat, their Order will be in a position to choose a new path.
