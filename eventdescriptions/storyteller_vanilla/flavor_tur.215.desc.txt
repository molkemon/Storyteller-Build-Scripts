Sicily, once home to one of the Islamic kingdoms in the Medieval Era, is now under our control. As it is custom, the question has once again arisen whether we should establish a new eyalet in our conquered territory. However, while most eyalets use the local government structure to some extent to rule over the land there, the local clergy themselves have reached out to the monarch. Due to the history of Sicily and its importance as a major cultural and political center in the Medieval Muslim world, the local clergy wish that our south Italian eyalet should be the restoration of the Emirate of Siqilliya itself.
