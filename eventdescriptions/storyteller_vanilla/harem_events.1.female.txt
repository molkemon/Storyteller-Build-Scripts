Her Imperial Majesty, sovereign of the house of Osman and Sultana of Sultans, has a reputation as a great woman and is unchallenged as a ruler. As befits a ruler of her caliber and stature, the Sultana has mothered several capable children and many who show great promise as future rulers. Both the sons themselves and our government officials are acutely aware that only one can inherit and what awaits the others. Our Viziers are adamant that it is now high time for the monarch to name a favorite among the possible successors. This is unlikely to end the competition for their favor, but it will hopefully stop the worst succession struggles that might follow if the ruler should meet her maker without having picked a clear successor.
