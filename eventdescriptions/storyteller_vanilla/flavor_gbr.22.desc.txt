The realm was first considered by its neighbors to be out of its age, a whim of the English kings, trying to cope with the stalemate of their centuries-long struggle with the kings of France. However, continued efforts to expand the dominance and authority of this newborn Empire have resulted in widespread recognition of the great success of the celestial monarch. It is the dawn of a new age, as the multicultural society of this domain is naturally pushed towards progress due to the mixture of very different traditions that has occurred.
