The consort made not one, but several friends during the feast arranged in their name. In spite of it being many months ago, the consort has maintained these connections with little effort, and with the aid of one noble in particular, they have enjoyed learning everything there is to know about courtly life in the capital. There are so many parties, hunts, and feasts to attend. The people of the realm really are not that bad, they think, looking out the window of the palace. Lately they smile at the landscape outside, realizing that even though they will always treasure their time in the old country, the consort has found a new home.
