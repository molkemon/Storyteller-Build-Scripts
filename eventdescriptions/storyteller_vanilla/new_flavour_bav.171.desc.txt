We were not the only ones with the rights to Ingolstadt; arguably, Ludwig the Seventh, Ludwig the Eighth's father who was driven out of the duchy by his son, had the better claim. However, Ludwig has been held prisoner by the ruler of Ansbach for the past several years. Now, the powerful ruler has used considerable talents of persuasion and a large lump sum of money to persuade Ludwig to sell his rights to the Wittelsbach inheritance.
