Time is a cycle, and what has been will always come again. The year of the one rabbit is upon us, and so are the snows, the frost, and later on the droughts associated with it. The weather will not be kind to us this year, and a great famine is tearing up our society. As desperate men sell their children into slavery to feed their remaining families, some demand that our ruler should release them of servitude to him and allow them to seek new lives elsewhere.
