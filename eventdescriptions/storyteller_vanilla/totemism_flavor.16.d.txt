During the celebration of one of our annual gatherings, the monarch decided to invite one of our allies, moved by the care and affection that they have shown us in the past. However, while the dancers were delighting our guests with their arts, one man tried to stab our distracted ruler, thankfully stopped in time by the ruler's guards. When interrogated, he confessed to be part of our supposed allies and both he and his companions were imprisoned for their acts.
