Gotland is the island of our origins, a port many of us name their trusted home. But we would be fools if we pretended that it has a better climate than the tropical islands of the Caribbean. Many of our men yearn for relocating our main port to these islands for several reasons. Of course, we would have to directly integrate our colony in the Caribbean into our administration too, but this would be a low price to pay for the easier access to the treasure fleets to loot.
