Ever since the birth of our state, the Imperial Harem has been one of the essential institutions of the government. Ensuring the succession of the Sultanate as well as a sign of wealth and power for the Sultan, the Harem only grew in influence over the centuries. The Valide Sultan as well as other important roles of the Harem can no longer hide their disapproval of the current state of affairs. They claim that the monarch is unfit to rule properly and see themselves forced to act in favor of the empire. The ruler is nothing but a figurehead of the Harem.
