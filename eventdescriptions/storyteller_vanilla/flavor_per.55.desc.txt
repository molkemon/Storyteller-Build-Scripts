The great religious division of our lands will finally end. While Shia is an influential force within the realm which cannot be ignored, the monarch is aware of the diplomatic isolation which could result in adopting the Twelvers as the state religion. Instead, the unity of our people is equivalent to the unity with the Muslim world through the Sunni faith. Naturally, to make his standpoint clear to the great community, the heresies of the domain will be targeted and returned to the true faith.
