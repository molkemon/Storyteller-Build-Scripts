In what was seen as a sudden betrayal of our Wattasid viziers, our Sultan has had all the Wattasids he could find rounded up and executed. The popularity of the Wattasid family, however, means that this has been a very unpopular move. As the monarch appointed a new, non-Wattasid vizier to serve him yesterday, riots broke out, and it is rumored that somewhere two Wattasid brothers remain hidden to take control over this revolt, determined to avenge their family.
