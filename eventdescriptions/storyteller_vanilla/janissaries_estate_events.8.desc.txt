The relations between the realm and the Janissaries have reached a new low. Failing to grant more rights and privileges, the state disappointed the expectations of the Janissaries for years. The neglect of Janissary soldiers has created an atmosphere of resentment and distrust that now threatens to erupt into violent conflict. As such, it was no surprise when the aghas of the Janissaries made an ultimatum to the monarch. either give in and give concessions to the Janissaries, or have to face open rebellion.
