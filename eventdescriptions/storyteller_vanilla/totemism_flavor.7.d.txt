With the arrival of the cold weather, crops are not the only ones that are having trouble these days. The winter winds and rains are not merciful to our people, and the monarch is no exception. The ruler has complained about a sharp pain in their throat and is completely incapable of talking; even breathing seems to be a difficult task for them. Our greatest priests have been summoned to the ruler's presence and have repeatedly advised against them leaving the bed. However, singing is an important part of our religious ceremonies, and having our ruler indisposed will definitely take a toll on their reputation.
