With the restoration of the Caliphate under the ruler, the perception of Islam as a whole is changing once more. Unified under the Ottoman banner, scholars, theologians, and artists are once again free to live a life free from worldly worries. Scientists see active promotion for their work while architects are blessed by Allah with tasks to construct new mosques. All this while the religious piety in the Islamic faith is experiencing its own renaissance. The impact of that realm can also be felt in this domain as our own population seems to be inspired by the monarch's exclamation for a new Golden Age of Islam.
