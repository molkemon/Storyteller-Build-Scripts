A Franciscan friar named Diego Delgado and eighty Christianized Maya have reached our capital. Delgado had joined a captain named Francisco de Mirones who was on his way to assault us, but the friar thought the captain excessively cruel and therefore left the army. The captain and his soldiers have already been dealt with; they were slaughtered by our own. We have seized Diego Delgado and his followers and are awaiting your orders. Should we sacrifice them to the Maya gods, or let them go?
