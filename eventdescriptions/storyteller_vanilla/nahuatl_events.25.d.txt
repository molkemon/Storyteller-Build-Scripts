As the spring equinox and the planting season draws near, the time of Tlacaxipehualiztli is upon us. The time has come to sacrifice all prisoners of war at the temple of XipeTotec. Their blood will feed the sun and their skins will be worn by their captors and the priests of the temple before being saved in the holy cave. As we have not fought any wars recently, however, we have an acute shortage of prisoners to sacrifice. This is in itself a bad omen, and things will likely only get worse if we are unable to spill more blood today.
