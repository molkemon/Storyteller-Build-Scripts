Despite the monopoly the Orange family holds on the office of monarch, the nation has seen a current of republican ideals recently. Statisticians have viewed this as a sign that the nation may very well be on its way towards their goal of a full-fledged republic, with some even going as far as praising the Prince of Orange for permitting this shift. Regardless of how much truth there is to this, recent political maneuvering keeps the dream of the Dutch Republic alive.
