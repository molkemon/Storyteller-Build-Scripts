The discontent and anonymity between the lower classes and the rulers of the city has brought them to the brink of civil war. They are illprepared to face a full-fledged rebellion, and many members of the upper classes are urging the city council to take the matter to the emperor, to beg the emperor himself for any help he is able to provide. Good strong men and gold are what they need most to end the fires of rebellion that smolder the streets of the capital.
