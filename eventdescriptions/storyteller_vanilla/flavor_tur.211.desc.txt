Vienna, the seat of power of the von Habsburgs, eternal rivals of the Ottoman Empire, has fallen to the might of the Ottoman Empire. The city put up an honorable defense against our forces; the Austrian alliance fought valiantly yet to no avail. But in the end, none of our adversaries could stand against our cunning tacticians and our well-disciplined soldiers. As the imperial capital gets sacked, the Christians of Europe fear our advance. They know that our march will not stop at Vienna.
