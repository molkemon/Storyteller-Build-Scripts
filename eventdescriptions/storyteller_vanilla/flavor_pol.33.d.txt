One quiet morning in the current year, the messenger bursts through the doors of the Imperial palace, red and flustered, eager to deliver a valuable correspondence from the seat of power in Poland. Apparently, the local noble of Poland who was recently crowned a King decided, against the calls of his own advisors, to steer his nation away from its ties to Lithuania, turning its gaze upon the West. As a result, the monarch of Poland has officially petitioned for the entry of his country in the Holy Roman Empire.
