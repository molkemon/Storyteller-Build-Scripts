The high priests of the Sun Temple in Suamox make up the leadership of the Iraca chiefdom. While much less important than the Zaque or Zipa, the leader of the Iraca is an influential man due to his position as keeper of the temple and spiritual successor of the mythical hero Idacanzas. Traditionally a supporter of the Zaque of Hunza, the current ruler of the Iraca has now come forward to attempt to mediate and create peace between the feuding Zipa and Zaque to ensure the Muisca stand united against any foreign threats.
