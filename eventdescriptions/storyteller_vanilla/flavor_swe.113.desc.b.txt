Originally, the Swedish Kingdom once tried to construct a mighty flagship in the past, the Vasa. While no expense was spared in decorating and equipping it, the ship had one big flaw. it was just too heavy for its own good. Years after this incident, the Nordic people gathered in that province to construct a second flagship, which shall lead the majestic fleet to victory. With the craftsmanship of the Danes, the naval expertise of the Norwegian and the resources of the Swedes, the workforces of that province managed to construct a flagship for the kingdom. Pompous, elegant and mighty; these are the traits of the mighty Vasa II.
