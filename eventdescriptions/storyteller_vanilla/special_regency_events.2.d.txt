Matters of the heart should never be mixed with politics, especially when it comes to the king and queen of a nation. After the growing influence of Catherine in the French court, and the rising and obvious interest that her husband had expressed in Diane de Poitiers, it became inevitable that something had to be done. As one of her first acts in her new position, Catherine has requested that Diane shall handle the imposing castle of Chenonceau back to the Crown, as well as the noble jewels that were so wrongly gifted to her.
