In the realm, business and politics have been irreversibly intertwined since time immemorial. Sometimes, they may be faced with deals that benefit many parties but not the republic itself. When the ruler is one of those parties, the situation becomes all the more precarious. A good business agreement builds stronger relations with key merchants, something they could certainly use. They can avoid alienating these partners if they are willing to sacrifice some of their integrity as a representative of the republic.
