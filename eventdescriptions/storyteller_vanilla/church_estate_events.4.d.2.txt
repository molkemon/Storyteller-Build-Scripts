The Vaishyas of several prominent cities are accusing the state church of being greedy and indolent. The pious commoners are now withholding the tithe in a protest against a particularly bad example of misuse of the authority of the state church. Pious Vaishyas are now objecting to paying tithes to the state church as they consider the state church unfit to receive them. The state church is furious and demands that they fine the obstinate Vaishyas.
