Though difficult, the monarch and their Grand Vizier find some hearing amongst the Janissaries. Some are ready to revoke some of the privileges they gained over the centuries in return for a more favorable life in one of the other classes of society in their realm. However, these regiments can only speak for themselves, so revoking any of the privileges will still lead to an active revolt, though smaller in scale than the bloodier alternative. Now comes the question of which privilege shall be revoked.
