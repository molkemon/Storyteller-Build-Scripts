Our inactions motivated more and more Swedish nobility to gather their allies and to start open revolts against our rule. However, not all of Sweden see their actions as justified, and the atrocities done by the rebels in the name of freedom and liberty are causing heavy devastation to the Swedish lands. The nobles are making themselves highly unpopular with the Swedish commoners. While this Pretender War will be highly costly to our treasury and our manpower, it will at least show the Swedes that the just rule is not one of tyranny but of justice, unlike the one their nobles try to enforce.
