The nation is at peace. The realm has seen a period of stability and prosperity under your rule, giving you more time to focus on spiritual concerns. Unfortunately, this has instilled in you a fear of seeing an end to the fortunate times. A theologian finds you deep in prayer before a statue of Shiva. When you finish the mantra, you tell your advisor about your concerns, requesting their advice. Gesturing towards the statue, the advisor answers. 'Look at the hand in front of you. Shiva's raised palm is a reminder that he not only creates and destroys, but also preserves. Fear not.'
