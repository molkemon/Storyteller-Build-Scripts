Handling the Kalmar Union is a difficult task for the monarch due to the lack of ratification of the Kalmar Union treaty and the clash of the interests of the different union partners and their nobility. The nobles of a junior partner, for example, see little reason to support the union, nor electing a culture monarch to rule over them. But our own nobility is an enemy to the Kalmar Union as well. They exploit the divided attention of the monarch and try to enforce their own demands on the ruler. Yet again, one of the nobles arrived this morning at the royal court in order to siphon resources from the crown for one of their neverending issues.
