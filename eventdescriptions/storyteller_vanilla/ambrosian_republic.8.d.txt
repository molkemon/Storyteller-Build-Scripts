Grand Captain Sforza has died, suddenly and unexpectedly. His popularity with the people and iron grip on Milan's politics were all that held the fledgling Republic together, and now the various factions of the government are fighting over their visions for the future. Giorgio Lampugnano's faction wishes to renew the ideals that the Republic was founded under, restoring democracy and ending military rule. Jacopo Piccinino's faction, in stark contrast, argues that only a strong Captain General can hold the Republic together and resist its many enemies.
