In fourteen thirty-five, the legitimate King Eric Gryf was banished from Denmark and was replaced by his nephew. The culprits behind this betrayal were the nobles of Norway, Sweden, and, worst of all, Denmark. Today, in the capital city, the righteous monarch of the Kalmar Union made a proclamation. the crown of the former realm will be turned into the crown of Denmark with all its duties, rights, and prestige. The nobility class of the former territory of Denmark loses its rights, privileges, and land. The authority of the new ruler of Denmark is from this day onwards absolute.
