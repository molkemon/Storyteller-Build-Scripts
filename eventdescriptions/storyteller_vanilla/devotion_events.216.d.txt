After many meetings, discussions, and majlis, our ministers and advisors have brought us different reforms to consider. Each has presented their ideas, and we are to choose between two of these. The first one is to exclude state officials, such as diplomats and governors, from entering into any business, allowing them to fully concentrate on their work and not on anything else, thus hopefully allowing them to do a better job. The second reform is to distribute pasture lands and game reserves that are now reserved for the family of the dignitaries among the poor, so they can cultivate the land. Unpaid labor should also be made illegal.
