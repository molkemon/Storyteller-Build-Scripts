The monarch traveled to the great city. Just like how the great Mansa Musa once did, so did the current ruler bring the riches of the realm to the city; although this time, with a lot less gold; after all, the ruler did not want to cause damage to their brethren in faith. When arriving at the city, the religious leaders recommended to donate the gold for the construction of a great mosque, which shall compliment the great Kaaba. On the other hand, some of the more compassionate advisors suggested investing in a counting house in the city so that the poor can help themselves to overcome their bad situation with the help of Allah.
