The horse that Hernán Cortes gave to us and which we made into a statue did not please our guests Juan de Orbita and Bartolome de Fuensalida. Orbita destroyed our statue, and it was only the eloquence of Fuensalida that saved their lives. Our priests were hostile and jealous of the missionaries even before they destroyed our statue, and told them that according to an ancient Itza prophecy it is not yet time for us to convert to Christianity.
