Walls made out of sandstone and empty, rugged streets decorate the ruins of a city, which used to be the crown jewel of an ancient Empire. For our ancestors, it would have been a saddening view, until they see what the proud and diligent workers of the realm created. Within an amazingly short amount of time, our constructors and builders restored the port city to its former beauty, and many of our people are settling in this city.
