It has been said, among those who are willing to listen, that the official who is the monarch of the realm so kindly appointed to take care of our colony has been absent from his duties for a long time. He has been seen taking part in the greatest of feasts and balls, here in the country, quite far from those he aims to govern. Of course, it is easier to revolt against the ruler if the ruler is not there, so news about a dangerous uprising in a province have finally reached our court. When confronted with these facts, he has been heard saying. "Those mosquitoes were eating me alive."
