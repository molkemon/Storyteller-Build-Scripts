Wenzel Anton, Earl and then Prince of RittbergKaunitz, was a noble from Bohemia. Impregnated of the culture of the Enlightenment, a devoted civil servant, he always had the full confidence and support from Empress Maria Theresa. After a diplomatic career, he became Chancellor of Austria in seventeen fifty-three, until seventeen ninety-two. A partisan of alliance shifts, he worked out closer ties with France that led to the Seven Years War, but also managed to renew with Frederick the Second of Prussia in time to be part of the first partition of Poland.
