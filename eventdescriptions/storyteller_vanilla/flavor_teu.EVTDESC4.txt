The Teutonic Order finds itself plagued with unrest and conspiracy. A new organization has appeared to challenge Teutonic rule, despite the recent outing of the Lizard League which sought to invite Poland to rule Kulm. Calling themselves the Prussian Confederation, this union of burghers, knights, and noblemen declares that their purpose is to unite against injustice and arbitrary rule. If this treason were not enough, it is known that many among their number have Polish sympathies, and like their predecessors, they might attempt to invite Polish rule over Prussia if their influence is not curtailed. Worryingly, they have already found significant support among the burghers of West Prussia.
