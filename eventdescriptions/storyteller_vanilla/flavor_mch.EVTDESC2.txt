Several large Eastern Mongol tribes unite under Nurhaci, and his number of armies increases from four to eight. It is now that he sets his goal to conquer China. In sixteen sixteen he takes the title KinKhan to show that his people descend from the KinTungus. He also takes the name Aisin Giorin, the Golden tribe. In sixteen twenty-five he attacked China, a development that ultimately ended in a total Manchurian victory.
