The formation of the Leagues in the Holy Roman Empire has made it clear that we live in a time when nations no longer sit idly by and allow people of their faith to suffer under godless rulers. Not far from our border, Hungarian followers of that religion are in exactly that unacceptable situation. Our nation has strong bonds with Hungary in law and in spirit, and cannot let this continue. It is time for us to free them and reclaim the land, so that that faith's blood may never be spilled upon it again.
