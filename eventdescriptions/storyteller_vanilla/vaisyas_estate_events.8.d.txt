The country has become renowned for its trade in high value commodities such as spices, precious gemstones, and fine silks. Merchants come from far off lands to purchase goods that they produce in abundance, but which to them are exotic luxuries. The key to their success in these industries has been their patronage of various guilds who draw their membership from the Vaishya caste. Their investments have thus far proven profitable, and it stands to reason that further investment will only continue to yield greater riches. They should determine which of their luxury goods industries is most important and focus their efforts on the advancement of the guilds involved.
