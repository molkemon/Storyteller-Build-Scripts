Our conquest of Hindustan complete, the monarch has decided to commemorate our victory by commissioning the most magnificent throne the world has ever seen. Adorned with jewels of all kinds, from diamonds to emeralds to pearls, the value of this seat exceeds that of the grandest mausoleums. The question on the minds of all in court is where this throne shall be placed. Traditionally, the conquerors of Hindustan have ruled from Delhi and administered their empires from there. As the rulers of Delhi, it is up to us to uphold this tradition.
