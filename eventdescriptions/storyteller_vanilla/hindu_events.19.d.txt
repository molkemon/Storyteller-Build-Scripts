The realm is in flames. You are at war against those who would see your country crushed and its loyal subjects slain. Such an assault on your people is unacceptable, and you find an unusual fury burning within you directed at those who caused this conflict. From the way others look at you, it is clear that you must appear frightening in your anger and determination. While recuperating in a temple, you see a statue of Kali, horrifying in its appearance and brutality. Yet in its eyes, red with rage, you see something of yourself. a sense of purpose. Just as Kali guards her children, you will protect the sons and daughters of your country to the end.
