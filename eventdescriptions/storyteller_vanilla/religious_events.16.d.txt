The sizeable Muslim community of the country has been raising objections to the coronation of the monarch. As the old Muslim aristocrats and clergy remain very influential in our country and still hold many positions of power within our state, this is not a small problem. It has been suggested that perhaps the situation might be defused by the monarch abdicating in favor of a relative who would then convert to Islam. This would allow power to remain with the dynasty family while still placating the Ulema and leading aristocrats. Should the monarch continue to govern our country, we may find that these elements within our state are likely to attempt to enthrone a candidate of their own with more favorable attitudes towards Islam.
