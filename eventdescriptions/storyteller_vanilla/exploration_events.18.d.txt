Our fleet set sail to find a sea route to the rich cities of Europe. It took them ninety-nine days to cross the ocean westward, after they had found the Cape of Good Hope located near the tip of Africa. The fleet's captain died from a wound caused by a poisoned arrow when assisting a local chief in conquering a rival tribe, but two of our ships had survived. One tried to return across the seas but perished. The last ship continued west and sailed across the Pacific Ocean, and as it arrived at our port, it became the first ship to circumnavigate the globe.
