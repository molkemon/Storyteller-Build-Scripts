Sufism is a very spiritual and mystical part of Islam. Disconnected from the worldly desires and worries, it strives to find closeness to Allah. Although seemingly unintuitive, the ruler's Grand Vizier created a concept which combines the philosophies and traditions of Sufism with the legislation of the empire, giving legitimacy to the laws by connecting it to Allah. Of course, this codification still requires a signature of the monarch in order to be active.
