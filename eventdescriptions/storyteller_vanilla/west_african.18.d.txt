The people of this realm have traditionally lived, at least in part, a nomadic life. Although our efforts to centralize have made this less feasible, people are used to it and expect us not to intervene. It does make the ruler more difficult to manage, however. Some of the monarch's advisors have suggested that we try to take control and restrict their movements, but this could be seen as trampling the way of life that made them successful in the first place.
