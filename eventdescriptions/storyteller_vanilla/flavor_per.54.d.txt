Years of interbreeding between the world's best horses have finally yielded results. Our royal breeders have gathered steeds from Europe, Africa, Arabia, and even as far as China, in order to engineer the perfect warhorse, the steed that will carve a path to victory for our armies and solidify our place in the history books. As our royal servants enter the shah's palace, they bow their heads, eloquently informing the monarch that the process nears completion, and that the results are surely to be an improvement over the existing breed of cavalry in use by their guards and cavalrymen.
