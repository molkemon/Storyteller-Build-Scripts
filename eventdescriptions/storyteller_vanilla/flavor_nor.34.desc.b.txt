News has reached us from our scouting troop in Greenland. Apparently, the settlement in Greenland is still intact and even thriving to an extent. The residents have accepted to subordinate themselves under our rightful rule and are once again people of the realm's Crown. However, there is one small detail about our colony in Greenland. due to the decades of being separated from Norway and the rest of the Christian world, the local population started to pick up the traditions of our ancestors and have been practicing the forbidden Norse rituals ever since.
