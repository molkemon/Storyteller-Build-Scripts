While few would dispute their influence on the Steppes, the Cossacks have many things working against them when it comes to gaining influence in the halls of power. The nobles despise them and would see them all brought to work as serfs on their large estates, and their manners have not brought them many friends in the capital. Lately, however, things have been going their way. They have managed to defend their settlements, and their services to the state as raiders are indisputable.
