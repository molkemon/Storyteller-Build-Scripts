As many of the Sultan's before the ruler has a very close bond with their mother. As it pleases God, the Valide Sultan is a powerful ally, friend, and confidant for our monarch, and to reflect this, she has been granted a most generous allowance. Money that, apart from being used for her personal needs, she has been putting to use for the betterment of the state. Our mother is not content with this, however, and has asked for an even more generous arrangement that would allow her to fund ambitious building projects in the capital itself.
