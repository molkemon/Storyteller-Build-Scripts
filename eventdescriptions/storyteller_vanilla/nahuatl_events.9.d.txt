One of the expert artisans often employed by the monarch and other affluents in our society has attempted to rise above his social status. As a commoner, he is expected to only hold a modest celebration when a child is born, yet after the birth of his son last month, he held a banquet worthy of at least a minor noble. While it is not wrong for a commoner to acquire wealth, flaunting it in this way is a great insult to our gods and traditions.
