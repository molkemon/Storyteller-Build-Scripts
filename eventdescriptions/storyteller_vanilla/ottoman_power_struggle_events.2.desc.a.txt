The Ottoman Government is not what it once was. As the Muslim traditions and laws are no more part of the government, the Harem has been naturally disbanded. The pashas lost their ranks while Eyalets have been converted into normal provinces of the empire. And even the Janissaries have been neutralized through a series of assassinations and blackmailing. Though not as pompous as many would have hoped it to be, the monarch managed to end the internal power struggle of the realm.
