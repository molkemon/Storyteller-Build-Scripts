A city as important in the trade flow as Lübeck is sure to accumulate great quantities of wealth, and as such, attract the attention of all greedy nations around it. This makes the presence of city walls not just a necessity, but a vital part of the existence of the city itself. Fortunately enough, we haven't been slackening in our duty to ensure the safety of our city and wealth, and the impressive fortifications surrounding it are a testament to that. The multiple walls and gates surrounding our city will make sure that nobody dares try to attack us to steal our wealth.
