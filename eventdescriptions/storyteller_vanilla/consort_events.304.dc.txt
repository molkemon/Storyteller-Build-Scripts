It might have something to do with their upbringing in a certain realm, or perhaps it is simply their personality. Regardless, the consort is one of the most devout subjects in the country. Their unwavering belief through times of hardship brings hope to our people during even the darkest of days. After each public appearance they make, we find that the people of our great government tend to display a renewed energy, on their faces as well as in their daily tasks.
