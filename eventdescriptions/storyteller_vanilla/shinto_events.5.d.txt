Many have questioned our fervor in fighting the scourge of the seas and coastlines. Some would even say that piracy and pillaging comes naturally to all zealous fishermen and that we have turned a blind eye to this despicable practice. Over time, however, our neighbors have learned to watch their shores and to track down and make examples of those that would break the peace. The frequency of Wokou raids has decreased significantly since the sudden surge a few years ago, leaving little impact on the realm.
