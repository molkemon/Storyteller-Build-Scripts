Advisors, scholars, poets, and even politicians have all taken part in the latest grand debate that is shaking the nation. Unlike the usual grim matters of war or poverty that afflict and take up the desks of our hierarchy, lately the question on everyone's mind is. Spice or Herbs? Certainly both sides have strong arguments for their case. Spices definitely have the extra flavor to make our food and drink have that special taste and kick, whilst the tea and other herbs dutifully possess their healing as well as calming properties which have been with our nation for centuries.
