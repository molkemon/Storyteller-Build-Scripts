Our stories and myths tell of repeating cycles of creation and destruction, which are reminders of the consequences that will happen if humans ever neglect their obligations to the gods. We have a responsibility to the gods who make our continued existence possible. The gods and other forces of creation and chaos fight each fifty-two year period, which may bring the destruction of the worlds. If we ignore our duty to the gods, the battle between them that determines the fate of all earthly creatures might not end in our favor.
