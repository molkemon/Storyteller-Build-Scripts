Like the Sultans of old, the heir hopes to make a name for them as a conqueror and a ruler who will lead the Empire from the horseback. As the monarch is still in good health, and as we have not allowed our heir to actually take to the field, the realization of this dream remains an uncertainty. It has come to the point that the heir is being ridiculed by the other princes, and their mother is imploring the monarch to give them a command of their own. This is not an unreasonable request, though one must wonder about the motives of those that try to goad our favorite to risk their life and limb.
