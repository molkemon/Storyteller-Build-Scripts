The city of Danzig, now lying firmly in our grasp, used to be a hub of trade under the once mighty Hanseatic League, situated in Lübeck. Since then, the League has fallen from grace due to a plethora of factors and has had its power in the Baltic and beyond challenged. Despite these shortcomings, recent developments around the banks of Germany and the Baltic have led to the League regaining its standing. Once again, the powerful merchants of the Hansa come to Danzig, with a lucrative offer.
