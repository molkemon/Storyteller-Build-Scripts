The villages once granted to the western traders have grown into a major trade port known as Calcutta. While the economy of the city is booming and the entire region prospers because of it, there is reason to be concerned. Due to the great economic influence of the European traders, they are more and more seen as the real authority of the entire area, not just of the city itself. The local commander has begun constructing a number of new forts to protect the city without asking our permission, and our demands that construction cease have been met with silence.
