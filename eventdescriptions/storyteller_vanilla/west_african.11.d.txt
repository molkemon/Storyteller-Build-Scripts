Bringing the word of the Prophet to the people of the realm has not been impossible, but the outcome so far has troubled some of our religious scholars. Although they have abandoned many pagan traditions, a reverence for ancestors remains with troubling undertones of worship. We see no fault in honoring one's family, but when prayers are directed to the dead and not to God, can anything good really come of it? Nevertheless, these are ancient practices. Rejecting them may alienate many of those who would otherwise listen.
