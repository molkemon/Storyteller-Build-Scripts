For years now, the seat of power in our nation has been shared by our secular leader and our spiritual leader John Calvin. As the Reformed faith has grown to be an overwhelming majority in our country, those who still believe in the power of the ruler have become few in number, and more people recognize Calvin as our head of state. Outside of our borders, Calvin has gained followers from all over Europe, and many of his influential followers come to the capital to meet with Calvin without even sparing the monarch a passing glance.
