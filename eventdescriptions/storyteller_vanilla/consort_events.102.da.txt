They say that a parent's love for their child is without limits, but it is obvious that some loves are more limitless than others. While the consort is certainly fond of their eldest, the heir of their dynasty, everyone knows that their sister has the bigger place in the consort's heart. The consort dotes on her and has made sure that she has had the very best education. Now the consort is trying to make their favorite daughter the new heir of the realm.
