The legacy of Great Moravia, the realm of Mojmir, Rostislav, and Svatopluk, has not been forgotten. Great Moravia, the cradle of Slavic Christianity and the birthplace of our first written script, has left an indelible mark on our history. Its legacy is a beacon of hope and a source of inspiration for our people. Now, the time has come for us to proclaim ourselves as its rightful heirs; to once again form a vast realm that echoes the glory of Great Moravia, to carry forward its legacy towards a glorious future. Let us claim our birthright, for we are the heirs of Great Moravia, and its spirit lives on within us.
