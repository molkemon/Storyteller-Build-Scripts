The tensions between the Protestants and the Catholics of the Holy Roman Empire are getting more and more explosive with every passing day. It would have come to a war already, if it wasn't for the fact that the Protestants are outnumbered. On this fateful day, the ruler of the Protestant government of the realm made a proclamation. the government of the realm recognizes the rights of the Protestants of Germany, and supports their interests militarily, economically and politically. Additionally, the ruler of the dynasty will personally lead the Protestant League in the name of all those who were mistreated by the emperor, and will fight for the religious superiority within the Holy Roman Empire.
