Recent power shifts among the foreign relatives of the monarch and clashing inheritance laws have led to an unfortunate situation with the house of the dynasty. A member of the dynasty has now been suggested to be the rightful ruler of not only the homeland, but ours as well. Whether or not this claim will be pushed through war or is merely used to bolster the foreign monarch's status for a domestic power struggle remains to be seen. Regardless, the possibility of being forced into a union in this manner is ominous.
