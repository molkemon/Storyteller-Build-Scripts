The faith of the monarch has long been the cause of consternation among the leading nobles of the realm. Over time however, the ruler's influence has led to some new men and even some of our older families adapting the new faith. The fact that the new faith rite opens doors in the capital, together with a generally increased visibility of the monarch's worship, has led to the rite growing increasingly commonplace throughout the country, attracting new converts from within as well as from other parts of the world.
