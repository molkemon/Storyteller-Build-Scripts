The Makahiki is one of the most important periods for our people, when the Pleiades rise in the horizon and the next crescent moon arrives, the New Year season starts. During this period, we thank Lono for his blessings and the Hawaiian people celebrate it. Warfare and labor are prohibited during these four months. People play games, attend religious ceremonies where animals are sacrificed and rulers prove their worth. All the social classes have their tests done to them in some ways and succeeding not only brings personal glory, but also to their lineage.
