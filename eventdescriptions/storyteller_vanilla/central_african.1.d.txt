We do not know exactly who they are or where they came from. A new ruling class has emerged and begun to transform the society. Known as the Maroserana, they may have arrived from the mainland, but local practices and religion are integral to how they maintain power. By venerating rulers of the past and having the monarch appoint mediums communicating with ancestors, they tie their own reign to the entire history of the realm. Combined with more formalized contact with important partners, craftsmen and scientists throughout the nation, the new practices introduced by the Maroserana are likely to benefit our nation.
