Not everyone is pleased with the plan to entrust an advisor with seeking better relations with another country. With dissent mounting in the realm court, an administrative advisor, who has previously maintained a friendly rivalry with the event advisor for the monarch's attention, has come forward with allegations of disloyalty. The administrative advisor claims to have investigated the event advisor's activities and found out that they amount to little more than the event advisor sifting off state secrets to the target country under the guise of seeking rapprochement. For the event advisor's part, the event advisor protests that the evidence brought against them is a fabrication inspired by the administrative advisor's jealousy at their success. The event advisor swears they are loyal and will deliver positive results if they are given full trust.
