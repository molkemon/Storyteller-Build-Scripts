Following the death of Ludwig the Eighth of Ingolstadt, the ruler of Landshut has claimed Ingolstadt's territories for himself. However, this would be to ignore the claims of Ludwig the Seventh, who was driven from his land by his son some years ago and has since been the prisoner of the ruler in Ansbach. Having heard of his son's death, Ludwig the Seventh has been making noises about reclaiming his former Duchy. This could offer us a chance to extract some gain from this affair.
