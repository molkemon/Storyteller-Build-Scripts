Two conflicting parties have come to you. They ask you to mediate the conflict and put forth documents, witnesses, and other evidence. You look over the material provided as you ponder how to declare a truce and how the settlement should be created in order to maintain the honor and status of both parties. This property dispute is not the first you have to settle, but it is a rare, sensitive one that must be settled in such a way to avert a cycle of revenge. Both have claims that should give them the right to the land, but which one is true and which one is false?
