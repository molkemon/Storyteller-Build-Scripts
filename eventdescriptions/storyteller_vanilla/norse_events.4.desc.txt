It has been months since the ruler's sudden loss of senses. Although it only prevailed for a short time, the visions the ruler claimed to have seen changed them drastically. No longer was the ruler busy with the matters of the Church, nor did the ruler seem to care about the security of the state. Instead, the ruler studied every single script, every single documentation about the old Norse religion. Although some worried about their sanity, the chats with the ruler revealed that they had not lost their sharp mind in any way. One day though, the ruler vanished completely, and messages from the new Norse province reached the court.
