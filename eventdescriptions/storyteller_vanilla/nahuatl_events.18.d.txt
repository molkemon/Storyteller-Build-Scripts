During the time in their youth, the heir became quite adept at the traditional Ollamalitzli ball game, but as an adult they have had to give up on this habit. As the game is played throughout the different districts of the capital, however, the heir is often seen as a spectator at the various courts near temples or markets even as an adult. This habit must also extend to participation in the wild betting that is often associated with the game, as the heir has come to you today asking for help to pay their debts.
