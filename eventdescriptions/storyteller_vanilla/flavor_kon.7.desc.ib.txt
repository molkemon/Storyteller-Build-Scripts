Strange pale men in silver armor and long, brown robes have been spotted at our coasts. After the initial contact it was clear that these men have come a long way, all the way from what they call Iberia. A short audience with the monarch was organized and these strangers told us their intentions. these Portuguese and Castilian are here to discover the world and to spread the name of the one they call Christ. Their technology is more advanced than what has ever been seen in the realm, and as such both the ruler and the nobility were intrigued by those strangers.
