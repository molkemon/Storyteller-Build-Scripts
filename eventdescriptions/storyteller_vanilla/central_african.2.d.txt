Of the many stories told among our people, the Ibonia is one of the most widespread. Describing the life of the hero Iboniamasiboniamanoro and his adventures to save his betrothed Iampelasoamananoro, many different versions of it are told throughout the realm. While we take pride in the way these stories have been retold throughout the ages, the Ibonia is largely unknown abroad. To spread our stories over the world, we will have to put them into writing. However, this will inevitably force us to select a definite version, most likely the one retold in the capital. Those who grew up with other variants of the story may lose faith in the monarch. Is it worth the risk?
