Our fierce efforts to control the wouldbe pirates, raiders, and smugglers among our coastal population have resulted in a maritime policy almost entirely transformed. By decree of the monarch, all fishermen are forbidden from owning weapons and all vessels are regularly searched. Strict rules and regulations now apply to all who would traverse the seas, and our new coastal guards allow us to close off our domain. The former targets of the coastal raids are ecstatic and have in turn bestowed great honors and rewards upon us for helping them keep the peace.
