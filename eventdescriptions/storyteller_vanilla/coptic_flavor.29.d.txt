As Copts, we celebrate Palm Sunday, which is one of the Seven Major Lord's Feasts. On the seventh Sunday of the Great Lent, we commemorate and relive Jesus' triumphal entry into the holy city of Jerusalem. It is on this day that He drove away the merchants and moneychangers from the Temple. The crowd who cheeringly greeted the Messiah were waving palm branches over their heads, and so do we as we make our way through the church on this happy day. During the procession, we chant the joyful hymns, and it is truly one of our favorite times during the Church year.
