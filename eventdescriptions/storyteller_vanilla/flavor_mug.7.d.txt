The lands of Burma lay secluded from their foes by a range of mountains on all sides, leading to a frequent rise of many great states within its borders. With the Pagan Empire falling and the Ava Kingdom on the decline, it would be only a matter of time before a new state would rise. Thankfully, the unification of Burma was not so successful, as they have been able to march in and subdue those states before any could mount an opposition.
