The monarch has made it their life work to spread the faith and has quickly made a name for themselves among religious communities across the country. Their close ties to the state church have made clerics of various kinds a common sight in the capital and an influential voice in the halls of power. Over time, the fame of the ruler has begun to spread even beyond the borders, and spies tell us that there are religious minorities among the subjects of rivals who would support military expansion into their areas.
