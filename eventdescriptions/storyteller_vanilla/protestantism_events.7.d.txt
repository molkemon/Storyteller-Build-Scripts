Religious fervor has gripped the realm as the population has become convinced the end of days is drawing nearer. Peasants are talking of signs in the sky, and contrary to the doctrine of the State Church, local priests are giving sermons on the apocalypse. As Millenarianism sweeps the province, blame is being put on the State and the State Church itself, which professes to be Protestant yet clearly is not living up to its name. Official reactions have so far been very weak, as even among the clergy of the capital some feel these fears are justified.
