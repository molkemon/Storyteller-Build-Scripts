The election of the ruler as monarch of the Kalmar Union brings bitterness to the nobility of the rebellious domain. A group of nobles organized to a sizeable army, which supports a pretender ruler for that domain. All their hopes in independence, however, are funneled into the life of the leader of this rebel group. We could set an example for these rebels, and eliminate the pretender army, or we could try to orchestrate an assassination of their ragtag leader.
