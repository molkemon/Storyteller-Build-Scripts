The people of the capital desire nothing more than to be cleansed of their sin, and Savonarola will guide them. Every person has their blasphemous texts, false idols, or needless luxuries. Anyone living humbly under God should be without such evil distractions, and they must be burnt. We will gather it all in huge bonfires in public places, where everyone can see the wickedness of this world evaporate as smoke on the wind. Burn your sin, capital. Burn your sin.
