Our neighboring daimyo's increasing trouble with the Ikkoikki has come to a head and led to military confrontations. The brutality of the fighting has caused survivors of the sect of the SingleMinded to spill into our country, and these bands of peasants, monks, and small landowners have already strengthened their numbers with followers from our own clan. They will surely only cause more and more trouble for us if we do not act swiftly and decisively.
