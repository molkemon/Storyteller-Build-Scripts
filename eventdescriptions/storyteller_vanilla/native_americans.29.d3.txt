As the monarch was going back home with their canoe after a good day of seal hunting, the monarch saw not far away from their position a huge seal, a true blessing from the gods, emerge from the depths of the sea. The monarch prepares their harpoon and quietly moves closer to it. The monarch leaves the row over their canoe, stands up and has the harpoon ready to be fired. The seal emerges, their gazes contact as the monarch releases the weapon.
