The Livonian Order is the successor of the Livonian Brothers of the Sword, an order of knights which was annihilated by the Samogitians in twelve thirty-six. While technically a vassal of the Teutonic Order, the Livonian Order has remained mostly autonomous. However, their order has its fair share of challenges. The Kalmar Union, the very hungry Lions of the North, seek their subjugation. The Lithuanians, backed by their Polish allies, want to annex the Baltic lands, and the Novgorodians to their east consider expanding westwards. But that is not all. In fourteen thirty-five, the bishoprics formed the Livonian Confederacy under the rule of Riga, and started steering precious resources away from them for their own gain. But not all looks grim for them. Times change, and the Livonian Order is at the crossroads of destiny. If they manage to end this confederacy, and manage to push their enemies back, then their state can make a choice of which path they shall follow.
