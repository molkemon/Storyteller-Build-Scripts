The monarch walks through the devastated streets of the capital. The years of war did not leave the city without its marks. The rightful troops stormed to capture the false ruler and brought the monarch to the feet of the monarch, the legitimate claimant of the throne of the Kalmar Union. The monarch's soldiers prepared a stone block in the center of the city and are now forcing the shamed ruler to it. Without a single word spoken, the monarch's head is placed on the block and the executioner swings his axe. The head of the false ruler rolls to the monarch and all he feels is the satisfaction of a plan working out.
