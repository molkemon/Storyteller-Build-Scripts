In the wake of wars and conflicts, an increasing number of people have turned to banditry and guerrilla warfare. Disillusioned and impoverished, these men have no qualms about causing harm to us or our enemies as they move around our kingdom. They are, however, proving more of a problem for foreign armies in many ways. If we turn a blind eye to their transgressions, these irregulars might even prove a strong addition to our own armies.
