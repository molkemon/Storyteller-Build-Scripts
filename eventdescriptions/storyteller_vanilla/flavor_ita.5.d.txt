Many have claimed this title and tried to do what others could not, but none have succeeded. The Roman Empire has too often been nothing more than a faint goal they could strive toward, but never reach. However, we have proven it does not have to be this way. Having seized the initiative, we have, through blood and toil, brought this empire back together, and we shall pride ourselves with it for the rest of eternity, for Rome is undefeated.
