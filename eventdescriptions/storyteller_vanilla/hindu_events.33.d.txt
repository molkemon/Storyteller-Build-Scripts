The realm is without potential heirs. The rule of the dynasty may slip if you pass on without a successor, and the pressure to produce an heir weighs heavily on you. You pray daily for the gods to give you a child, finally seeking advice from a guru, asking why your request has not been answered. He tells you a story. Once, Brahma the Creator brought forth from his body four sons, the Kumara, to carry on his legacy and aid him in creation. However, the Kumara refused such worldly tasks and dedicated themselves solely to learning and teaching the divine. Without their devotion and celibacy, knowledge of Vishnu would not reach us. If you wish to please Lord Vishnu, cast aside your desire for an heir, motivated by hunger for power and carnal desires, and seek the truth.
