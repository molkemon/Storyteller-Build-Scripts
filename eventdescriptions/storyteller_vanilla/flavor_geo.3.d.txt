The heart of the Great Horde lies penetrated. The great city of the Khan has fallen, marking the end of the Tatar Yoke and the horde's rule over the steppe. The scourge which threatened the Christian world for many years and forced countless nations into subjugation is now a thing of history. Our brave soldiers, thirsting for revenge, are only awaiting orders to put the city to torch, turning it into a smoldering ruin, ready to be swallowed up by the steppe once again.
