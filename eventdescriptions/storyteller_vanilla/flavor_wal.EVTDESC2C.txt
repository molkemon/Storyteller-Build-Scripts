The assembled retinue ready their pikes and their stomachs for the coming judgment. The soldiers raise the beaten and broken body of the monarch from his slumped position on the ground and prop him upright. The once majestic monarch lets out a final chilling scream as the stake impales his heart and emerges out of his back. The heir grimaces as he is forced to watch the horrific display; his eyes fill with terror as the realization dawns that he is next.
