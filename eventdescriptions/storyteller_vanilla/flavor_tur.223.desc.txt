Hizir Reis made himself a name under the service of his elder brother Oruc Reis. In fifteen sixteen, the brothers captured Algiers from the Spanish crown with Oruc declaring himself as Sultan. When his brother died in fifteen eighteen, Hizir inherited Oruc's nickname of 'Barbarossa' Redbeard in Italian and received the honorary name Hayreddin. As time went by, Barbarossa climbed the ranks of the Ottoman navy and secured many key victories on the sea which led to the Turkish dominance of the Mediterranean Sea during the mid sixteenth century. Eventually in fifteen thirty three, Barbarossa was appointed Grand Admiral of the Ottoman Navy by Sultan Suleyman the Magnificent.
