The Adler von Lübeck, the largest ship our fleet has ever seen, shall be the pride of our navy. Even our most powerful and navally capable foes will tremble at her size and firepower as she gracefully takes to the seas. Such naval prowess displayed by Adler will certainly be the object of envy in the eyes of our rivals. To safeguard its legitimacy and aura of invincibility would mean that our nation will continue to benefit from its glorious name.
