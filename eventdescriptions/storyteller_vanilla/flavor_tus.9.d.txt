For the past few years, a preacher has been catching the attention of the people of the country. Girolamo Savonarola has spoken fiercely against the rulers, the Pope himself, and has even predicted Judgment Day to be fast approaching. With the recent turmoil in Italy, more and more are starting to listen. Savonarola is now encouraging mobs to accost the dynasty in the street, to protect the ancient republic. It is only a question of time before he convinces the entire city of Firenze to listen to his "divine visions."
