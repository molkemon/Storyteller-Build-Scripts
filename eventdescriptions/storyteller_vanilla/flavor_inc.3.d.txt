One of the first things on Pachacuti's agenda is the reshaping of the realm. While our capital has served its purpose over the years, it is in many ways no more than a rural village, often flooded by nearby rivers. Such a city is no longer fitting for a kingdom with ambitions such as ours. By creating a small model version of the city, the monarch has drawn up plans for an entire redesign of the city, necessitating the complete evacuation of the city for the duration of the project. While the manpower and material needed for this project are readily available inside our new borders, mobilizing the various chiefs under our control entails a generous use of gifts and feasts. That is, however, a price we will have to pay for our new capital.
