A ship filled with Basque whalers and their families has weighed anchor by the distant shore of a land we call Terranova, the New World. Sponsored by the monarch's own funds, they have been tasked with establishing a colony on this frigid northern island. Back in Navarra, the Kingdom continues to face the perils of being a minor nation at the mercy of the great powers that surround it. There are those who wonder if they might escape their troubles by abandoning their ancestral lands and establishing a new home for their people in Terranova.
