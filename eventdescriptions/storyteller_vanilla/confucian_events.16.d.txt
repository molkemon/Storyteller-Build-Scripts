Over time the people of that religion have come to take too many of our customs. When our diplomatic advisor visited the area, they found that they are still far from civilized, however. When a local influential landholder, Lord of the area, invited the advisor to participate in a naming ceremony for his son in which he named the boy after himself, that was the final straw. The advisor returned from the area with a very low opinion of the people of that religion and of their prospects to ever count themselves among the civilized people of the world.
