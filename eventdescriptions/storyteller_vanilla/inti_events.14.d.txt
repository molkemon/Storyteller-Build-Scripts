The world can sometimes be an unforgiving place and fate may turn in surprising ways. Luckily we have the Huaca Gods of our people to help us interpret what the future holds. Should the future be too hard for the Huacas to penetrate there are other oracles in the Andes, but they require a worthy gift before sharing their visions. The ruler has been beset by ample doubts recently and it is perhaps time to consult the auguries.
