The realm may be a federal union, but they still maintain enough power through blood for marriage ties to be a factor in their relationship with other nations. Even so, the more fluid the rulership, the less tangible these ties become. Nations who are normally pleased with the Statist administration now worry about the prospect that their ties to the celestial powers may be weakening as the nation leaves its hereditary traditions behind. They want to make sure influential families retain some power, including the ruling family.
