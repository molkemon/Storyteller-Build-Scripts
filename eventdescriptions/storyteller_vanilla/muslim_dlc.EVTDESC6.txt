The second to last ritual is to revisit the Masjid alHaram mosque in Mecca to walk around the Kaaba once more. It is called Tawaf alIfadah, which symbolizes being in a hurry to respond to God and show love for Him. Before leaving Mecca, pilgrims perform a farewell tawaf called the Tawaf alWida. Some pilgrims, including the ruler of the country, travel to the city of Medina and the AlMasjid alNabawi, which contains the Prophet Muhammad's tomb.
