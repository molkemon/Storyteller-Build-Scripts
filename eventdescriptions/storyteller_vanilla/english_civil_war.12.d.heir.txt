With the death of our Lord Protector, the question remains how our country should progress. Some feel that this would be the ideal time to reestablish a monarchy in our lands and call for the reinstatement of the old heir to the throne, albeit under the condition that the country's governance is regulated by a strict constitution and laws to prevent the situation that led to the Civil War we have just left behind us. Others argue that the time has come to do away with the monarchy altogether and establish a modern republic built around the old principles of the English Parliament.
