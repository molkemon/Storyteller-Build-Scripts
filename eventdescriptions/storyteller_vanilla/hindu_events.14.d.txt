Recently, you gave birth to a child who will carry on the legacy of the ruling dynasty. Little heir makes you hopeful for the future. The heir's birth also brought to mind the reasons why you view Devi as the peerless supreme divine power. Just as the next ruler of your realm is born from you, the world is born and reborn from the goddess. That Devi uses this great power for benign purposes is truly a sign of her kindness and grace, and knowing that it exists within yourself makes you consider this an aspect of her you can identify with.
