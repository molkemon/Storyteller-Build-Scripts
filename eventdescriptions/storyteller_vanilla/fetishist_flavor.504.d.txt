The monarch nervously stepped up to the altar. The blade quivered in their hand while the crowd held their breath. Closing their eyes, the ruler then pressed the dagger into their skin. The cut placed must have gone deep because the blood that flowed onto the stone floor quickly painted it red as it dripped down the steps. The liquid glistened in the embers of twilight and the monarch just had time to hear the priests replace their chant with gasps before they passed out. When the monarch awoke again, they were not quite the same.
