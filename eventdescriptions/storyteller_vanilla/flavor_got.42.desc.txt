No other place is as sacred and secure as Saint Peter's Basilica; and today is the day in which brave pirates whose hearts can only be filled with the gold of sinners break into the seat of the Holy See. In the morning, the great fleet of the country captured the coastal town Fiumicino. Several grand ships docked at the port and dozens of our greedy buccaneers charged into the city of Rome itself. Though the soldiers of the other domain put an admirable defense, they had made one drastic mistake. they stood in the way between a pirate and his beloved treasure. The siege of that domain took several days, but in the end the ruler had to capitulate. The monarch then ordered their men to shoot the doors to the treasury of the Catholic Church open. The coffers of the Curia are open for the pirates of the country.
