The Electorate of Saxony, by the laws of the Holy Roman Empire, remains in the hands of the ruler of Saxony despite the nation having become our subject. The other Electors are unlikely to endorse our candidacy if we are seen to be coercing the Saxon Elector. We could however depose the ruler in favor of our monarch, creating a Personal Union. The electorate would then pass to our heir. Unfortunately this plan would not be popular among the Saxon lords.
