The death of the monarch has left our country without a ruler. All the important Mamluk households of the capital are calling upon their influence to have a say in who his successor is going to be. Depending on who wins, our new ruler will have the support of different households and different parts of the country. Only one thing is certain. those that would rather have seen their own group's interests prevailing will not greet our new ruler with joy. Some might even spend the rest of their lives trying to limit his influence.
