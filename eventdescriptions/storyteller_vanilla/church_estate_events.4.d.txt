The burghers of several prominent cities are accusing the church of being greedy and indolent. The pious commoners are now withholding the tithe in a protest against a particularly bad example of misuse of the authority of the church. Pious burghers are now objecting to paying tithes to the church as they consider the church unfit to receive them. The church is furious and demands that they fine the obstinate burghers.
