The fertile land of the Delta lies in our hands, prompting a resurgence in economy, culture, and scholarly pursuit. The latter has provided quite an edifying discovery; many are emerging who would see us embrace the Ismaili ideals of the longdead Fatimids, having come upon various ancient scrolls and documents in Nilic libraries, which now circulate through our nation's elite. We should issue a proclamation swiftly on how to deal with this swiftly spreading school of thought.
