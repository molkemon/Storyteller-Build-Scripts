While other orders focus their efforts in conquering the Holy Land, our order took a different path. Inspired by the brutal conqueror Genghis Khan and driven by our holy mission to convert the infidels, our state expanded more and more into the far eastern steppes. By now, a normal administration is almost impossible were it not for the most important tool in our order. the horse. Without even noticing it, we have become what once threatened Christendom. a horde, stretching from the cold waters of the Baltics all the way to the hot deserts of Central Asia. However, our expansion direction is clear; we go further east as the Lord wishes it from us.
