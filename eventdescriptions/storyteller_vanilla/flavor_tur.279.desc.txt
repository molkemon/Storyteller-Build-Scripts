The Habsburg have been a rival of the realm for years. Although the monarch was able to defend the realm from the Austrian hegemon of Central Europe, it is apparent that this dynasty is still growing in power and influence, not only within the Holy Roman Empire but also in Iberia, Poland and Italy. An ally is needed if the Habsburg are to be stopped. As fate would have it, such potential ally has found his way to the ruler's court. A diplomat from the French government of France reached out to our monarch on behest of his ruler, the French monarch. It appears the French want to contain the Habsburg hegemony as they pose a threat to their interests in Iberia, Italy and the Lowlands.
