The People of the Book; the Christians, the Jews, and the Sabians; recognize the God of Abraham as the one and only god, as do Muslims, and they practice revealed faiths based on divine ordinances. The population of the realm are almost all Dhimmi, and while they are afforded the usual things related to this status in a Muslim domain, many of them feel the state could be more forthcoming given how rich the region is. It has been suggested that they make even more favorable conditions for the Dhimmi of the realm, as it would be in the state's own best interest to encourage such a prosperous region to grow.
