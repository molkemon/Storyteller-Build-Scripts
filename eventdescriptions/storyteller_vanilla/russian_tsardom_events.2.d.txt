To be able to rise to a position near the monarch at the realm's court can be very hard unless you belong to one of few old families with a long tradition of service. Even if someone of considerable skill would make himself known, the old families expect to dominate the higher ranks at court. Lately a specific young courtier has been making a name for himself by performing outstanding service to the government, but there are no positions for him to rise to. The old families would actively oppose the creation of a new position for such a self-made man and would not take kindly to us opposing them.
