The monarch is well known for their ceaseless energy and working habits. There are few hours in the night when the lights in their chambers are not burning, and royal decrees are known to leave the capital both early in the mornings and late in the evenings. The monarch also seems to keep a constant watch over all business sectors in the country; foreigners have sometimes been amazed to learn that they will personally create regulations for even entirely new business ventures in the provinces. As our dear ruler gets hold of new opportunities for growth in the country, the only question is really if we have the resources to do all they wish at the same time.
