Conducting any form of attacks on rival ships in peacetime, whether through sanctioning reprisals or private wars or covertly backing piracy, has had many benefits to the realm, but is not without problems. One of the privateering operations was recently compromised and the ships and crew captured. As luck would have it, they had lost their letter of marque, proving they work with the realm's approval, and whatever proof connects them to the realm is purely circumstantial. As a last resort, the captain has now contacted the realm, hoping the realm will admit to its official backing so it can be used as leverage in the upcoming trial.
