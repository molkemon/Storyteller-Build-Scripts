The country's campaigns in India have brought glory to the Marathas that they entrust to lead the military. Many of these soldiers are returning home with a newfound taste for the luxuries of Persianate culture; Bengali silks, Persian-style paintings, and elaborate works of brass and silver are now in high demand. As the Marathas wield great influence in the country, the Maratha nobility have approached the ruler asking for state patronage of these arts and procurement of exotic goods.
