Ferdinand Magellan is a Portuguese explorer that aims to lead the first circumnavigation of the world, in order to finally confirm that the Earth is round. In the year currently, he is preparing to set sail with a fleet of five ships and approximately two hundred seventy men to find a new route to the Spice Islands in the East Indies. His goal is to discover the passage that would go on to allow his ships to sail through the southern tip of South America and enter the Pacific Ocean. Magellan's journey may be fraught with difficulties, including starvation and battles with indigenous peoples, but he plans to eventually reach the East Indies, and come back through the Cape of Good Hope. Magellan's achievement shall pave the way for future explorers and traders to navigate the globe and open up new opportunities for European colonization and trade in the Americas and Asia.
