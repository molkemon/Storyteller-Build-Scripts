The Empire of Persia is vanquished, and its vast holdings have fallen under the righteous jurisprudence of our most Sublime Caliphate. Indeed, a mighty and formidable foe, but even the great Persia cannot stand between us and our penultimate destiny. Though much blood was shed and many a martyr born of this conquest, the people of these lands are brothers in the faith and deserving of respect. Indeed, we shall rule justly and honor the history of this land by restoring Persia to its longlost glory as a valued member of our ever expanding Empire.
