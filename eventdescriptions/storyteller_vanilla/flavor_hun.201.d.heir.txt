Ladislaus Postumus, our beloved heir, has died suddenly in suspicious circumstances. Allegations of wrongdoing seem all the more likely since, had he come of age, he would have been poised to unite Austria and Hungary under his rule, subjecting Hungary to foreign domination. Already the nobility are moving to replace him with their favorite, Mátyás Hunyadi, the son of the great Voivode János Hunyadi, thus breaking the Habsburg line of succession to the Hungarian throne.
