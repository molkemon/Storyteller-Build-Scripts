Ever since the man appeared at the celestial court, he has been a mystery to our own elite class. Nobody knows his name nor has been able to find information about this secretive man. That is until today. It turns out, this man is none other than a banished politician of another country. Originally intended to be executed, the former minister barely escaped with his life. Ever since he has become a trusted member of the court. As thanks for our hospitality, he grants us documents which would justify the total subjugation of that country.
