Ever since the man appeared at the celestial court, he has been a mystery to our own elite class. Nobody knows his name nor has been able to find information about this secretive man. That is until today. It turns out, this man is none other than the banished heir to the foreign throne itself. He is one of the royal family and was banished in order for the foreign monarch to assume the throne. The prince has expressed no interest in the throne and transferred any kind of claim he had to the ruler.
