Ever since they were a young Sehzade in the Imperial Harem, the monarch has had their mother by their side. She has offered advice and protection throughout their career, and as they ascended the throne, she remained one of their closest confidants and most influential advisors. Even though she was by now a very old woman, the news of her death has come as a heavy blow to the ruler, who has now lost a good friend, an advisor, and their mother all at once.
