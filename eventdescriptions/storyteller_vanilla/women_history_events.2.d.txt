The queen consort Yekaterina is a German princess, one stronger and with more political power than her incompetent husband. She is deeply familiar with the ideas of the Enlightenment, and will endeavor to lighten the burden of Russian serfs. Her plans are ambitious; Yekaterina wants to usurp the throne and expand Russia's borders into Ottoman lands and Poland. Shall we help her in her endeavor? Shall we place her on the throne of Russia and allow her to rule us all?
