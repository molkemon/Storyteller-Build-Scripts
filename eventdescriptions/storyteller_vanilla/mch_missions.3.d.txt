Desertion and defection are rampant in China. It is clear that the Ming has lost its sacred Mandate, and that a new power is rising to take its place. Thousands of underpaid and discontent Ming soldiers have pledged their loyalty to the new power in the hopes that they might in time bring peace, stability, and, ultimately, payment. We have organized these defectors into the Green Standard Army. We expect them to be adept at fighting against their former compatriots.
