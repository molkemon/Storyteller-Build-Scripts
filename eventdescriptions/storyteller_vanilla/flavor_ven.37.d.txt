Venice, for centuries the foremost city of printing and glassmaking in Italy, has begun to surpass or at least compete with Rome as the foremost city of art and music. Great painters such as Tintoretto, Tiziano, Veronese or musicians like Monteverdi, Gabrieli or Donati are but a few of the many artists that work and live in this city. In Italy and abroad, Venice has begun to become synonymous with art.
