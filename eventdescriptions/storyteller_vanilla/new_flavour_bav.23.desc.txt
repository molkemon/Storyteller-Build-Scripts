Over the past decades, the city of Regensburg has accumulated considerable debts towards the ruling dynasty of the Holy Roman Empire. Now, the emperor is calling in their debts in order to fund their war with an enemy. However, given our strong connection to Regensburg, which was in the past a Wittelsbach capital, we are in a position to intercede. If we can gather together the funds needed to pay off their debts, it would be well within our rights to offer Regensburg to transfer their debts to us, essentially meaning that we will pay their debts in return for their freedom. However, the Austrians might hold a dim view of such transactions.
