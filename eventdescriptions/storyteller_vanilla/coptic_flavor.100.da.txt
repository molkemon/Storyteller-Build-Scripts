The country has recently seen many a lost soul seeking guidance at the monastery in the area. In fact, the region is becoming quite popular for people looking for repentance and absolution in the ways of Saint Anthony; the desert caves are well suited for the life of a hermit, allowing some of the most devoted Copts to spend years in contemplative solitude. Meanwhile, the population of the country takes great pride in this piety. Some well-placed words of encouragement could allow us to bolster their self-esteem even further.
