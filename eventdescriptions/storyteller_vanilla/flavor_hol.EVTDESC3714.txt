Born in sixteen forty, Anthony Hensius had enjoyed the confidence of the ruler of Orange all his life. Sent to Versailles after the peace of Nijmegen to supervise the execution of its clauses, he became Raad Pensionaris in sixteen eighty-eight after the ruler moved to England. A tough negotiator and obstinate opponent of France, he was at the core of all anti-French coalitions that were set up in all the wars of the late seventeenth century. He died in seventeen twenty.
