After almost twenty years, the great project of restructuring the realm is complete. The land has been leveled to create huge plazas and beautiful buildings erected, and the nearby rivers have been led into canals. The city is built to be the capital of an empire rather than a small kingdom and is organized into four regions matching the way in which they divide their realm in the Andes. The royal center is located at the junction of the regions of Antisuyu, Qullasuyu, Kuntisuyu, and Chinchaysuyu, and these regions divide the Cuzco valley as well as their empire.
