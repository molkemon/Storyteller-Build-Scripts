Fed up with the harsh taxation policies of the ruler and their tyrannical rule and licentious lifestyle, the populace of Naples has risen up in large numbers under a highly charismatic but illiterate fisherman called Masaniello, who was previously only known for his exploits in evading the taxman. Such is the unpopularity of the ruler that the revolt has spread to much of the countryside, threatening to overwhelm our armies with their sheer numbers.
