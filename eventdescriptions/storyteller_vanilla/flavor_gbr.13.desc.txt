We are a beacon of learning and science. Academics from all over the country and even from abroad gather in the streets and colleges of the capital to discuss the newest advancements. They have started organizing themselves in different groups to conduct those gatherings and discussions. One of those groups has caught the attention of the monarch, and the ruler has shown their approval by officially endorsing the group. It is thus to be known from now on as The Royal Society of the capital. Its purpose will be to promote and advance science, experimentation and discoveries, and thanks to it our country is sure to be the leading model of advancement. The brightest minds of the nation and even the world will undoubtedly be walking its halls.
