An unusual Jesuit has approached the Imperial Court. Matteo Ricci dresses like a Chinese, has adopted many Chinese customs, and talks in a way similar to the Chinese. A scholar as well as a missionary, Ricci seems to be as interested in the customs, cartography, and science of the Chinese world as he is to inform them of his own. This does mean that Ricci has had a lot more success than many of his brethren when it comes to attracting converts, but his skills, especially relating to astronomy and cartography, could undoubtedly come of use to us. Shall we allow him to stay at our court?
