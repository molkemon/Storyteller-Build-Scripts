The Ethiopian Adal war has been a disaster for Ethiopia. Many towns were razed, the capital was besieged by the combined power of Adal and the Ottoman Empire, and the remaining army of Ethiopia had to retreat into the mountains. The Muslims assumed that it was only a matter of time until Ethiopia fell. Then the Portuguese arrived. In the year fifteen forty-one, the Portuguese sent the commander Cristóvão da Gama with a company of Portuguese troops to assist Ethiopia and to fight against the Ottoman Empire. These new troops allowed Ethiopia to turn the tables and to defeat the vastly larger Muslim army, which led to the white peace in fifteen forty-three.
