The coronation of the foreign monarch as ruler of the realm was not devoid of problems. The realm's nobility have been split between prounioists and antiunionists; those who enjoy the privileges granted by the foreign overlord, and those who seek realm independence. As the tensions rose between the two factions, the foreign monarch intervened and held a banquet for the realm's nobility. However, this was revealed to be a trap by the prounioists as many of the nobles wanting independence have been imprisoned and shortly thereafter executed for heresy. Our people are in shock of the pure atrocities the monarch was capable of. The realm's nobility is completely decimated and the realm's populace comply with the Butchers of the foreign country for the time being. But the capital Bloodbath will be remembered forever in the realm.
