Praise the Lord on this most glorious day. For centuries to come, all Copts shall celebrate on this date in memory of our great victory. Finally, we have freed our revered Patriarch, the Pope of Alexandria, from his gilded cage in Cairo. No longer will we have to grovel for our enemies or wait decades to have new bishops appointed. This is a most vital step on our path of restoring the Coptic Church to the greatness of old, and beyond.
