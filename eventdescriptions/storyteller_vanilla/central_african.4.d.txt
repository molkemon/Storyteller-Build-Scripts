The social rules and taboos we call fady are important across the realm, but many of them are defined locally. Our administration accommodates this, and in fact, respecting and allowing these local variations is part of the policy making communities loyal to the ruler. Some of the soldiers from another country crossing our domain have displayed a much more careless behavior and seriously broken local fady. What is worse, they have refused to apologize and hypocritically called our customs superstitious, while maintaining social and religious rules themselves which they seem to have no such reservations about. This is nothing short of an insult against our people.
