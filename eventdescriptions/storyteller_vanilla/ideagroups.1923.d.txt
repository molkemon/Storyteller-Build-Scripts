One of our hosts got lost during a hunting expedition and they have discovered an abandoned shrine in the depths of our land. Perfect faith must be impenetrable, rigid stones drowned in nature's last blow, but still trying to get some air. Remnants of times long past under a faint distillation of light, its understructure strong as tamed horses. Our soldiers struck by its perfect obscurity, a cold day, a long day, a faithful day. They couldn't scream, for the supremacy of that temple made them go silent with reverence.
