In a ceremony at which the entire Burgundian court as well as many foreign dignitaries were present, the monarch was crowned as King of Lotharingia by the Bishop of Verdun. The festivities carried on long into the night as court bards sang ballads of the ruler's dynasty, comparing the monarch favorably to the likes of Lothair and even the great Charlemagne. It is clear to all that a new era of glory and prosperity for our nation has been inaugurated.
