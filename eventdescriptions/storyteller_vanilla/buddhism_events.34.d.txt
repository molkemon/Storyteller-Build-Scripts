Our messenger from Tibet has returned with news of a powerful religious leader who has become interested in the cause of the ruler and his dynasty. If he is to come to the capital, however, we must be able to do far more than just guarantee his safety. For the Lama to tie his destiny with ours, we must swear that he and his future incarnations will be given the proper respect and influence in our realm, and that we will create a great monastery for him and his disciples. If we cannot make these guarantees, we will have to content ourselves with receiving a delegation of monks eager to found a new monastery in our lands.
