The Orthodox church has a firm grip of the Ruthenian culture as well as Ruthenia itself. Many churches have been built based on the Hagia Sophia, and the religious rituals are sacred to them. Although both our faiths are Christian, there are some irreconcilable differences between them. The local council suggested to let them practice in peace, and in return the Orthodox Ruthenians might accept our rule without a second thought. The church, however, advises that we purge this heresy before others fall for this foreign faith in our country.
