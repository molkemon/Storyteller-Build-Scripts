The heir was the leader of prayers in the masjid and preacher at the congregational prayer on Friday before they were chosen to rule the country after the monarch. The local community who had elected them as Imam Khatib will be sad to see them leave, for few others are as learned and as virtuous as them in instructing the congregation in the basics of Islam. When they leave, religious questions will now have to be asked to someone inferior to them, but what they will lose, we will gain.
