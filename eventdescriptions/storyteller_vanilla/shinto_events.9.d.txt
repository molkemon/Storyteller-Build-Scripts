The growth of cities has led to the ancient Za trade guilds of the country to rapidly increase their power. The generally chaotic situation in the realm has also meant that they increasingly rely on their own arrangements for security. The guilds have now tied temples, landholders, and samurai to their cause. But they have also gained enemies. Some implore the monarch to take a stand against the guilds and open up the economy for anyone of ability. Others argue that they should at least give out monopolies and privileges to others than the guilds.
