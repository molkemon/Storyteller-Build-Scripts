The incorporation of Mosaic Law directly into the laws of the country has led to an unprecedented growth of its Justice System in some parts of the domain. A steady stream of accusations has grown into an almost unmanageable number of court cases. Not only that, but the number of death sentences has also risen greatly, and where it were not for the already established practice to give dispensation for such a conviction if the convicted can present a certain sum of money as compensation, we would have a very bad situation on our hands.
