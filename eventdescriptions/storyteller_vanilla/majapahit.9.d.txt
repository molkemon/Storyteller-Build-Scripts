The situation in the realm is dire, but it seems that all is not lost yet. A group of wealthy Muslim merchants have approached the ruler with an offer. They will pay us handsomely for trading privileges in our ports and the right to practice their religion freely within the realm. The Bhattara Saptaprabhu warn that the instability of the empire may attract our people to the merchant's foreign faith, discarding the founding traditions of the Majapahit empire. Silver, however, makes for its own counterargument.
