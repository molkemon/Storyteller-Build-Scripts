A Strelets is offered a plot of land and the promise of a salary in exchange for lifelong service to the state. Often his position will then be inherited by his son, and so the state is assured well-armed and motivated soldiers for long times to come. All over the country, this type of employment has made men seek the state's service, and while not everyone can join in offensive warfare, many make a welcome addition to the garrisons of the border fortresses.
