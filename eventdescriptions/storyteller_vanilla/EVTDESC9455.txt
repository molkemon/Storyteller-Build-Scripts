The chandeliers blaze shadows and light across the grand ball room of your palace, and the music of a hundred dancing violins fills the air. "Your Majesty," "Your Excellency," "My ruler," the foreign guests and local dignitaries greet you. Everywhere is conversation, laughter, discussion, and the breathing, pulsating rhythm of life. Your eyes gaze across the assembled myriads. Who should you talk to tonight?
