The Pazzi banking family, bitter rivals of the ruling family and the monarch, have conspired to overthrow the Signoria and install themselves as rulers of the realm. Many of the most powerful figures in the country's politics are participants in this vile plot, ranging from merchants to clergymen to nobles. The Pazzi plan to assassinate the monarch during High Mass at the Duomo, and then to capture the Palazzo della Signoria and seize the reins of government. Should the conspiracy fail however, most of the political enemies of the Signoria would be branded as criminals and the ruling family would therefore rule practically unopposed.
