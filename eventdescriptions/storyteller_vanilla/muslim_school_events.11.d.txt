While the officially sanctioned school of the government is the one recognized, there are many courts and Qadis throughout the lands, and all four of the schools of faith are deemed appropriate for civil procedures. This means that to some extent it is possible to play the courts by bringing a case before a Qadi of a school considered more likely to give a beneficial ruling to a specific case. An entrepreneurial merchant in the capital has become so good at this that it has angered not only the local population and his business competitors, but also the local governor. There is not much we can do about this without making inroads into the privileges of the Ulema.
