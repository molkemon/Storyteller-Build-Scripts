The Christians of Europe are going through an age of reformation, strife, and religious infighting. Christians attack other Christians under the pretext that they are doing it for their God. If this all happened a few years earlier, we might have joined in these senseless infightings, discussing if a man with a funny hat should truly represent the sky. Fortunately, our people have found their way back to the arms of Odin. There is a question for us, but it is not related to religion.
