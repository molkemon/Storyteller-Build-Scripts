The 1510s were turbulent times within the Ottoman Empire. Safavid Missionaries and Qizilbash Militants ran rampant in the eastern parts of the Empire. In 1511, the Turkoman Qizilbash rallied under the cause of Shahkulu, the son of the halife of Teke. He has been a thorn in our sides for a long time, and he dared to raid our grand caravans in the east. Our Empire has no place for such rabid and vile creatures; let us now raise the banner of the prophet and crush these animals once and for all.
