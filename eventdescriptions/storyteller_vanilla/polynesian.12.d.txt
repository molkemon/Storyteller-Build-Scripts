Our people have used wakas or canoes to settle this part of the world, and wakas are central to our culture in one way or another. Competitions are also an easy way to have our people entertained and a good way to keep our community fit. One group of rowers has challenged another to a race, and news of the event has spread quickly across the area. Even the monarch has approached the shore to watch the race. Bets have naturally arisen.
