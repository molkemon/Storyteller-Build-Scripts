When Cnut the Great conquered England, he ensured his reign by ruling over England from the city of London while already securing that the lands of Denmark and Norway were handled by his Norse wife. After so many centuries, the North Sea Empire is now restored once again, though this time England was not conquered by the Vikings but by our advanced society. Still, ruling over England will pose a problem due to the distance between the capital and the English lands. It might be worth relocating our capital to London once again.
