Our beloved monarch has been assassinated. The royalist conspirators concealed their bomb in a barrel on a cart parked on the Rue SaintNicaise, where Napoleon had to pass on the way to the Opéra to see the opening of Joseph Haydn's oratorio 'Die Schöpfung.' Nicknamed the 'Infernal Machine', the bomb was filled with gunpowder, flammable materials and bullets, and set off by using a long fuse. The explosion went off just as Napoleon neared, killing him instantly.
