We have made it our holy duty to civilize the New World and enlighten the native people with our faith. As our priests and missionaries perform their holy duty in our domain, they report being approached by some shamans requesting to abandon their roots and join the ruler in their holy duty to propagate the faith amongst the unenlightened members of the local tribe. If we accept, we will have a whole new clerical class to spread our faith, but we risk angering the more traditional members of their tribe.
