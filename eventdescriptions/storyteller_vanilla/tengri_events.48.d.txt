With the elevation of the Hindu faith, a great number of Hindus have come to settle in many of our cities, and with them the concept of caste is spreading into our lands. Interestingly, it seems at least some of those that regularly do business with the Hindus, as well as some of our Shamans, have taken to this way to organize society. The immediate effects seem hard to judge, but for now everything seems to progress a bit more smoothly in our realm.
