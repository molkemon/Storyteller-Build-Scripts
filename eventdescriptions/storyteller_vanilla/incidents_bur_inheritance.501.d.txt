Sad news reaches the capital today as we learn that our beloved Marie, Duchess of Burgundy, has passed from this life into the next. A sweet and kindhearted girl, and a loving wife to the ruling prince, Marie will be sorely missed in the noble court. Marie was a passionate horserider and accompanied her husband on his routine hunts, but her kind heart and passion for riding could not save her when her five hundred kilogram horse fell atop her and shattered her spine. The marriage between a prince of the ruling family and Duchess Marie I of Burgundy united our nations together in a blissful union, but now that Marie has passed from this life into the next, none are there to dispute our claim to the Duchy of Burgundy and already the nobles of Burgundy are turning to the monarch of the ruling family as their rightful liege.
