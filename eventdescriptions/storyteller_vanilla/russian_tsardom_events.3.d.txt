As our government grows, it has come to incorporate a multitude of peoples. Far gone are the days when all our subjects were Christian peasants. Our expansion into the steppes has made us the overlord of both violently freeminded Cossacks and Tatar communities. Of course, much of the steppe is also quite empty. While our empire is great, our resources are also not unlimited. Often we have to make a choice. Do we want to get our own subjects to settle the newly conquered areas? Do we want them to remain dominated by the Tatars? Or should we let the Cossacks, with their disrespect for our strict laws, colonize them?
