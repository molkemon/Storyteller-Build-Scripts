In not so distant past, the tumultuous lands of Mesopotamia used to be ruled by the Abbasid Caliphate, under which scientific progress and culture greatly progressed. The symbol of this heyday used to be the House of Wisdom, a great library in Baghdad. Unfortunately, it succumbed and fell victim to the Mongol Hordes, who brutally destroyed it. Today, many courtiers have suggested bringing back the library, moreso as an institution. Indeed, many prominent scholars and scientists have already offered their services.
