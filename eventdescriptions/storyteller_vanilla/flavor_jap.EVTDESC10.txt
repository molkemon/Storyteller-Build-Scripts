Only the swift and decisive action of loyalist troops led to the timely rescue of the monarch and the traitor's capture. The general's motives are unclear, and he could well have been motivated by anything from a desire for revenge to a blatant lust for power. The incident has reaffirmed the ruler's control over the troops; however, the brief skirmish that erupted has cost the lives of many good men, and it is clear a renewed focus will be required on internal politics to ensure that this does not occur again.
