A great multitude of Maratha warriors have erupted forth from the domain into lands far from their native home. Finding employ as mercenaries, as generals in foreign armies, some even as conquering princes, the Marathas have become a force to be reckoned with in India. With our conquest of the Deccan complete, we must decide on their future within the great Empire. By offering them a favored position in our society, we could benefit from their military prowess and unyielding ambition, though that same ambition makes them a potential danger. Alternatively, we could keep them in check by centralizing our administration throughout the Deccan.
