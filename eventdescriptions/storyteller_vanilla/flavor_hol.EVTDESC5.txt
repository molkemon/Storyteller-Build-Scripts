The anthem Wilhelmus or Wilhelmus van Nassouwe is very popular with parts of the Dutch population and will, in nineteen thirty-two, become recognized as the official national anthem of the Netherlands. Willem van Oranje or William of Orange is the main character of the anthem which tells of him, his life and why he is fighting against the King of Spain, written in a first-person perspective. For a national anthem, the text and tune of the song are remarkably peaceful.
