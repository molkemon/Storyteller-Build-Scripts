Ever since the man appeared at the celestial court, he has been a mystery to our own elite class. Nobody knows his name nor has been able to find information about this secretive man. That is until today. As days pass, the nobleman has gathered information about everyone who enters or leaves the court. Secretive by nature, it has taken months until he has revealed himself as the former spymaster from that realm. His knowledge about the foreign domain has forced him to flee.
