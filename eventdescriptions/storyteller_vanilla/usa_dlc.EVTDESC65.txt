In eighteen oh three, Napoleon of France was hardpressed for cash as he was set on a dream of European hegemony. He therefore offered the Louisiana Territory, a vast region between the Mississippi and the Rockies against a lump sum of fifteen million dollars. The President and the Senate agreed and the United States nearly doubled its size. Sire, you would only get the province that France presently owns for a lump sum of one thousand ducats. How will you choose, Mr. President?
