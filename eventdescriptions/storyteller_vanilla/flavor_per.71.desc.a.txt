The transition to the religion was a bloody process. Many lives, be them Muslim or followers of that religion, were lost and the state has to rebuild. One of its early victims was a prophet who preached Zoroaster's teachings in one of our provinces. Or so we thought. The Prophet has not perished as expected and has returned with an army of followers. Although the religion of the country is what he was hoping it to be, the assassination attempt on his life made him abandon the peaceful nature of his personality. Now he demands the end to the ruling government and the rise of a religious theocracy under his rule.
