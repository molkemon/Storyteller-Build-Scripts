While our kingdom is Islamic in name, the old religion of our forefathers is still a very strong force. Islam has, however, been gaining more ground lately among the aristocracy, and wandering marabouts have also become an increasingly common sight even in the farthest corners of our lands. In the past, our predecessors have avoided trying to be too forceful about converting their subjects, but if we wish, we could probably make use of these recent developments to encourage the spread of the true faith.
