Sforza has many rivals within the Golden Ambrosian Republic. The more idealistic and scholarly politicians who founded the Republic suspect him of conspiring to seize the throne, while more pragmatic politicians resent his popularity among the people. Sforza has recently learned of a plot to deprive him of his military command and eject him from his position in government. Outraged, Sforza issues an ultimatum to the state. accept his absolute military rule, or face him once again on the field of battle.
