The monarch travelled into the thick forests of the realm with a small group of the closest advisors and servants. After all, a ruler as they can not survive in the wilderness just by themselves. After several weeks of travelling, searching and philosophizing, the curious ruler and their company eventually found a forsaken temple with a small collection of idols of the several gods of the Norse faith. Upon touching them, the monarch suffered a seizure and lost all of their senses. Two days later, after their servants established a small camp, the ruler finally woke up from their unwanted sleep.
