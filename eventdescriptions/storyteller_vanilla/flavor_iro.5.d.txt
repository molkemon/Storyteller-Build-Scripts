The child that our tribe decided to adopt has grown up among our people, strong and virtuous. His valor in battle has been proven on several occasions, as well as his dedication to the protection of our village. His reliable smile has earned the trust of many of our fiercest warriors, and they would follow him without hesitation. It is hard to remember when he was just a frightened child, wanting to go back home. At last, we can proudly announce; this is his home now.
