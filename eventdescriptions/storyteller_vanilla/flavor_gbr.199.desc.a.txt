Our country has reached a size, prosperity, and stability never seen before. Our glorious rule has brought unprecedented wealth to the population, and all over the world people respect our authority. The saying that the sun never sets on the British Empire is not an allegory but an actual undeniable fact. In this time of peace and prosperity, people have started to compare our monarch to the likes of the greatest rulers of the past, and especially Caesar Augustus. His establishment of the Roman Empire and the peace and prosperity it brought are being paralleled to the rule of the monarch and the prosperity they have brought us. We enjoy a true Pax Britannica.
