Our adoption of Islam as our new religion has generally been quite frictionless, at least in the areas where Muslims are still not a majority. In the realm, however, the new religion has a great number of practitioners, some who feel our theology is a bit on the liberal side. A recent influx of converts from the less religiously orthodox part of the country has created notable tensions. In order to control his subjects, our local representative has brought even more recent converts as soldiers, which has only inflamed the situation further. It seems but a matter of time before violence erupts.
