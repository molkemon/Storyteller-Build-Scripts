Those who command the ships in battle or are sent to explore lands to be claimed by the monarch should be chosen from the bravest and wisest in the navy. This is not the case today. From their very first day on a ship, sailors see money illicitly changing hands and protocol being broken as a matter of course, and quickly learn that both the letter and the spirit of the law carry little weight in the realm when silver is put on the other pan of the scales. Those who do not learn quickly make enemies. To advance through the ranks of the navy, you need to learn to play this system, and those who rise to the top are rarely those we wished would be there.
