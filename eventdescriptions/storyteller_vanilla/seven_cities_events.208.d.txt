Wealth comes in many different packages and ways; some claim that the wealth of heart is to be desired, others that piety and prestige should be the only wealth in life. Gold and precious stones is the only wealth our heart desires, and the Golden City of Cibola in that province has made this desire grow fiercely. For every ounce of gold, for every handful of gems we want more, and Cibola keeps on giving. One of the Seven Cities of Gold is ours, and we intend to keep it.
