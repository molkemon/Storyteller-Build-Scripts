The dispute between the opposing leader and ourselves has spiraled out of control, and we have been left with no alternative but to go to war to settle it. The ramifications of this are felt throughout the Japanese islands, as our fellow daimyos begin to take sides, and alliances are brought into play. Whoever may win this current civil war, however, it is clear that the current ruler Shogunate was too weak to prevent it. We are not the only ones who will have noticed this, and it is quite likely that this great war of the Onin period will result in a prolonged period of strife and warfare between the different Japanese states.
