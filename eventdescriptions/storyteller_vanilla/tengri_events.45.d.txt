The monarch has always taken a great interest in both the adopted religion and those that practice it. However, the ruler has not always led a life that many of the Christian subjects would approve of. Between the influence of Shamans at court and the habits of the monarch, there are many that feel they are risking divine punishment. Perhaps we should consider instituting some more clearly Christian rules for the court and ruler? Restricting the influence of Shamans, the number of allowed wives, and generally the number of officially sanctioned rituals would go a long way towards placating the Christian communities.
