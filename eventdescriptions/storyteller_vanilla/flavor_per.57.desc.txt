In the midst of a large crowd of people stands a single man on an improvised pedestal. He speaks to the crowd in a soft yet firm and passionate voice, claiming to have seen visions of the future; and visions from the main deity of the realm. The religious ways are wrong, misinterpreted in the many centuries since the conversion. The true name, the true visage of him, is Ahura Mazda. Obviously, this man is spreading heretic thoughts which cause great wrath amongst the established church. They want his immediate execution.
