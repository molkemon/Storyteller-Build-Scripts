The great stress that our government has placed on Legalism and the limiting of Mysticism in our society has had the effect of closely associating Sufism with resistance to central rule. In times of social distress, many now look to the Mystical orders for spiritual guidance as an alternative to the highly legalistic Ulema and its government ally. As tensions flare in a specific province, an influential Sufi Master declared his support for the monarch, potentially calming the situation.
