The followers of the faith have increasingly come to integrate Confucian concepts and our rites in their daily life. A recent trend is to identify the concept of Shangdi with their own supreme deities. This interpretation is somewhat unorthodox to the scholar experts of the government, but it has allowed the faithful to integrate a number of Confucian concepts, rites, constants, and classical teaching in their theology. There is some debate in the palace halls of the capital if this is the right and proper way on the path for the adherents to elevate themselves from barbarians to Hua.
