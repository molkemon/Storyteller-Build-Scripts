Custom dictates that European youth of quality should go on a grand tour around that continent to see the marvels of their age, meet with great minds and people of influence, and generally learn about the world and those in it. Our European friends in a certain country have now extended an offer to us to send their heir on a similar journey. To a certain extent their offer reeks of arrogance, but the things their heir could learn there may prove very valuable to our realm.
