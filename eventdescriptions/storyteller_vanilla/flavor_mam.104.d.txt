In a demonstration of our commitment to the betterment of our society, we have chosen to establish a waqf. Through this act, we dedicate a significant portion of our wealth and resources to create a lasting charitable endowment, aimed at supporting education, healthcare, and social welfare initiatives for our people. This waqf stands as a testament to our enduring legacy of benevolence, aiming to provide essential services and opportunities for generations to come, showcasing our dedication to the wellbeing and progress of our realm.
