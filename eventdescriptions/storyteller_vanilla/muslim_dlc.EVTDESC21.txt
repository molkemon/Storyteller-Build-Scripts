Madrassa, meaning a place where learning and studying is done, teaches tafsir or Qur'anic interpretation, shari'ah or Islamic law, hadiths or recorded sayings and deeds of Prophet Muhammad, and mantiq or logic. Some madrassas also teach mathematics, astronomy, astrology, geography, alchemy, philosophy, magic, and occultism. The future of the land lies in the hands of the children, and to be able to trust them with the country, it is best to educate them and make sure their knowledge of the world will never fail them.
