A group of elderly men arrived at our coastline. They are all dressed in long, white robes and have funny hats. They claim to be here in the name of their Pope and seek an audience with our monarch. Apparently, these men are bishops from a country called a specific realm and are here to spread the word of Christ. None of us really knows what they are talking about, but their technology seems way ahead of ours, and their beliefs intrigued the ruler and the nobility alike.
