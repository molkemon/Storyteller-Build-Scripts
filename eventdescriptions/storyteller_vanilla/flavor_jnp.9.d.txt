Emissaries of the Sultans of Jaunpur are claiming that the sultan of Kalpi, our vassal, has been conducting himself badly towards Muslim women and must be punished. The Jaunpuris would take the matter into their own hands and want to conduct a punitive expedition against Kalpi. While we have also heard rumors of the state of affairs in Kalpi, this is a decision to consider carefully, as Kalpi is an old contested zone between Jaunpur and Malwa, and it does not seem unlikely that the Jaunpuris might have bigger plans than they let on.
