During the reign of Ibrahim the First, Kemankes Kara Mustafa Pasha was the Sadrazam, or prime minister. He tried to curtail overspending and revitalized the navy, but was finally executed by the Sultan in sixteen forty-four. The Sultan had instead started to heed Jinji Hoja, a minor religious official with a reputation for occult powers who was brought into the palace to remedy the sultan's lack of heirs.
