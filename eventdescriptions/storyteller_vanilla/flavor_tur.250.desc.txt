Ancient capital of the Abbasid, Baghdad, city of law. The city of the great Caliph AlMansur has fallen into the arms of the Ottoman empire, and the esteemed ruler, rightful and just heir to the Caliphate. Oh Noble Baghdad, long sufferer of injustice and indignity. After so many years of destitution and desecration, first at the hands of the depraved hordes of the far East, then the disgraceful Timurids, and lastly the dreadful mismanagement of the supposed Persian Empire, at long last a proper steward for this most esteemed city. May Baghdad forever more be a shining beacon of Hanafi jurisprudence, and an inspiration to all who embody the Sunni Faith.
