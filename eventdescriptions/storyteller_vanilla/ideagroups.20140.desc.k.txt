Ever since the man appeared at the noble court, he has been a mystery to our own elite class. Nobody knows his name nor has been able to find information about this secretive man. As time went on, the nobleman became friends with the monarch. He proves himself as skillful in the matters of administration, diplomacy, and the military, and is in general well received in the court. Meanwhile, a vile rumor about the competence of the ruler spreads within the government. It is clear that the monarch is losing any kind of support of benefactors while the nobleman is gaining more and more support for the election.
