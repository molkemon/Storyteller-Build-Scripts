Anti-European sentiment has erupted throughout the realm. A group of nobles have rallied behind a man named Phetracha and successfully launched a coup against the ruler. The swiftness of the coup allowed Phetracha to launch a surprise attack on the European garrisons stationed in the capital and to immediately expel all European diplomats from the realm. Phetracha’s supporters have named him the monarch, beginning what will likely be an era of relative isolation for the realm.
