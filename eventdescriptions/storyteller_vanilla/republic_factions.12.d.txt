The guilds of the realm have outmaneuvered their competitors in the political arena, wresting control over the powerful government, but their power is far from uncontested. An artist, valued by the republic for his unparalleled skill as a craftsman and artist, approaches you with a solution to this problem. The ruler says, "A shift in power now may undo everything the guilds have wisely worked towards. We know you are a wise leader, but if you need an additional incentive, the guilds do not lack funds for political donations."
