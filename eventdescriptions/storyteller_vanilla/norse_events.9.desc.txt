In ten thirteen, King Cnut the Great unified the Kingdoms of Denmark, Norway and England into one single empire. the North Sea Empire. His achievements set a signal for the Christian and Norse kingdoms alike and his legacy lived on to this very day. Now, the crowns of Scandinavia and the British Isles are once again united under one Norse banner. The jarls of the many territories bow before the great ruler. Undoubtedly, the government of the realm is the true heir to the North Sea Empire. Only one question is open. Should the realm assume the name of "North Sea Empire"?
