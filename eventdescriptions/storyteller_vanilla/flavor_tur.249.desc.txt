Proper management of our lands is the basis upon which we will build an empire that will stand the test of time. To do so, the Sultan collected all of the best Byzantine laws, with attention to detail and ample consideration for the Sharia, and took great inspiration from the Siyasah to create a single, consistent taxation system for the empire. The great ruler added them into one single tome of learning and judicial understanding. All that is now required is the legal codification so this reform can become reality.
