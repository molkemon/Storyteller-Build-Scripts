The battle between our four champions and the captive warrior has exceeded all expectations. Not only did the captive survive the first four encounters, it almost looked as if he might have had a chance to beat the final, lefthanded, opponent he then faced. As is customary after the captive warrior was finally defeated, his heart was sacrificed to the gods while his flesh was given to the captor's family to consume, and his skin will be worn by the captor himself for many days. The celebrations of this year's Tlacaxipehualiztli are likely to be remembered for years to come.
