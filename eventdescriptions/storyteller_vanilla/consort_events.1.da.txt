It is with some consternation that the mighty families of the realm have watched the monarch letting the years pass by without finding someone to marry. These feelings have now turned into open dismay as despite the realm lacking a clear line of succession, the ruler appears to have no plans to find a spouse, nor is the monarch showing much interest in the proposals sent to them. It has now reached the point where the court is expecting the ruler to accept one of the offers on the table. Continued failure to secure the succession puts the entire realm at risk. Luckily, one of the oldest and most prestigious families in the realm has offered us the hand of one of their daughters, along with a generous dowry.
