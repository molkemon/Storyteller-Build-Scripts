The Holy Roman Empire is no more, and the Imperial Protection it once provided to its members is a thing of the past. The council of knights and advisors gathered together in the capital to discuss our further approach. Half of the council suggests putting our efforts into the conquest of Germany so it can be unified under one single banner. The other half stresses that the threat from Poland and Ruthenia is not gone yet, and that we should instead concentrate our efforts in conquering these regions. In the end, it is up to our monarch to decide what path we shall follow.
