The revolution has arrived. The mob has stormed the royal palace in the capital, capturing the monarch, who was promptly executed, along with other members of the royal family who were unable to escape. A republic has been declared under the new Supreme Central and Governing Junta, which is composed of two representatives from each of the divisions of the Spanish state and headed by renowned philosopher and lawyer Gaspar Melchor de Jovellanos. It promises to abolish serfdom, establish equality under the law, and redistribute the nobility's land between the state and the peasantry, leading Spain and the rest of Europe to a brighter future free from oppression.
