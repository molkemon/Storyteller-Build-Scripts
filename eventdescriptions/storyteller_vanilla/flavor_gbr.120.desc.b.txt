We all followed the overwhelming tide of the Fifth Monarchists, being swept away by their calling of the Second Coming. They were preparing the country for it, for the Kingdom of God in the Earth. We were all expectant, awaiting the day Jesus would come down from the heavens and we would be blissfully reunited with Him. But that day didn't come. What came first were the excuses, justifications due to calculation errors. Then the promises, promises of it actually being the next day, and then the next day, and the next one. People started not believing the words of those that had led them there. They started opening their eyes and realizing that they were not saints, that they were not going to be saved. Jesus was not coming down from heaven, at least not yet. What started as disbelief quickly turned into hate towards the ones that had misguided them. The masses stormed the government buildings, dragging out the leaders that not long ago they had followed. They were dragged out of their offices and into public squares, and then condemned, criticized, and accused. Finally, in most cases they were executed, the rest imprisoned. None could escape the ire of the vengeful masses.
