Religious tensions have been slowly rising in our country since the rise of the Marabouts in the middle of the seventeenth century. Now the Marabout faction in the country have come out in open revolt against the old aristocracy in the capital city. The promised revolution has quickly gotten the support of many of the Fulani herdsmen that are present in large parts of our state, and it is clear that we will soon have a very serious revolt on our hands.
