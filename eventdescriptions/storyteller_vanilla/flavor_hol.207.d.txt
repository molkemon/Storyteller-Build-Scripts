Jan Amos Comenius, a Czech philosopher, pedagogue, and theologian, holds the title of the current bishop of the Unity of the Brethren. His journey from religious refugeedom to becoming a pioneer of universal education is well known. In his renowned work, Didactica Magna, he formally introduced the concept. As both an educator and theologian, he currently guides schools and provides counsel to governments throughout nonCatholic Europe, firmly establishing his influence in the midseventeenth century.
