The previous ruler of the realm, also a follower of Ganga, passed on recently. After the funeral, the ashes have been left in your care, along with the ruler's wishes to have them submerged in the water of Ganges, the sacred river. Although we revere Ganga as the supreme goddess, the Ganges itself does not flow through the domain. While travelling to the river with the ashes may take time, it would also be an opportunity to deepen your insights about Ganga.
