When the consort married into the royal house of the dynasty, there were those who were skeptical. However, the young consort gained the confidence of the common people, almost effortlessly, and she grew well into the role as one of the ruling class of the realm. It has now been over ten years since the union took place. Even the ones who do not adore the consort's title have come to respect her as one of their own; the wedding anniversary of the elevated couple could have been highly anticipated. It is smiling gently at nobles and commoners alike that the monarch and their consort graciously accept gifts of varying splendor.
