The monarch of the realm has many nicknames. But one of the most accurate is “the Bureaucrat King”. They have decided to establish a fixed capital, putting an end to the ancient tradition of the Iberian kings to travel continuously. They have chosen Madrid because of its central position, not being dependent of any great lord or the Church, and some would say because of its good water supply. In any case, the monarch does not like the city itself, so they have also decided to build a large monastery on its mountainous outskirts, dedicated to Saint Lawrence, which would also be their habitual residence. They have gathered a large library and relics of saints there, and have turned it into the nerve center of the administration, presiding over the daily meetings of the different councils that deal with government affairs. Finally, they have also established the royal family pantheon there, where all the Kings and Queens of the realm after the monarch will be buried.
