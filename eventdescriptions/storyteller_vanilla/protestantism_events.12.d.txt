The natural scientist and philosopher is famous for their sharp intellect and their presence in our court lends a certain grandeur to our name. The natural scientist and philosopher is, however, also a firm Catholic and has been allowed to practice their faith as he wishes in order to come here. It would be unthinkable for the Church of the realm itself to criticize the monarch, but in the streets of the capital there is talk of the natural scientist and philosopher trying to plant subversive Catholic ideas in the head of the monarch.
