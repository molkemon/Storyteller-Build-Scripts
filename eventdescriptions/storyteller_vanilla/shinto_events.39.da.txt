A group of the Kirishitan have arrived at court in the capital today, asking that they be allowed to create a small community of faithful in our great city. They would then see to the poor and teach those who would listen about their god. The Missionaries also offer to tutor our own heir in the ways of God, as well as the disciplines of Christian science. The Buddhist clergy, as well as some of our people, are cautioning us, as they feel this is an affront to our ancient traditions while the traders in our realm hope to secure valuable trade deals with the westerners if we let them stay.
