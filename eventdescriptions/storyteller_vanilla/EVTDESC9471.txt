Borders are organic things, changing and mutating with the seasons, and with the fortunes of the latest conqueror passing by. Historians will, perhaps, say that the events that transpired in that province in the year mentioned were inevitable. As that province continued to flourish under the benevolent rule of the country, the yokels on the other side of the border were bound to get jealous. Perhaps they will be less fair to us. Either way, tensions in that province have given our rivals in the neighboring country a casus belli against us.
