A conflict has arisen between the Council of Ten and one of its members. Renier Zen is accusing the other members of the Council of Ten and the Doge of corruption. He claims that the Doge has been using his positions to enrich himself and to bestow important offices on his relatives. After a failed assassination attempt on Zen, the conflict has spread outside of the Council of Ten, and poor nobles have been gathering to support Zen while the higher aristocracy supports the Ten and the Doge.
