The ceremonies at the great temple in Puri and the log in which Jagannath manifests himself have at times been ridiculed and mocked by foreign rulers, but in Orissa Jagannath, the Lord of the Universe, is undisputedly the most popular deity. The support of Jagannath and his priests has also been unwavering for Raja Kapilendra, the founder of our dynasty. Now the time has come for our dynasty to repay this debt; we should patronize the temple at Puri and elevate the worship of Jagannath as the primary cult in our kingdom.
