One of their elders was teaching their youngsters about animals and botany near the sacred cave, explaining what herbs were good after a heavy meal, which ones were best suited to cure a bad cough, and what the differences were between a wolf and a dog. It was in the middle of these explanations that one of the kids shouted, "That's a raccoon, teacher." After muttering, "That's nonsense," the teacher followed him to discover a small deposit of ancient ceramics. One of them caught his attention. it was a beautiful pitcher decorated with animal patterns, one of those being, as the proud child had stated, a raccoon indeed.
