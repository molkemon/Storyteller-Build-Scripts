Pál Kinizsi is a legendary figure of our armies, having for decades served our court. This veteran has fought numerous battles and won many victories against the Turks and others, and thanks to his exceptional military skills, he never lost a battle in his entire career. For decades, he was the sole person responsible for the defense of vast territories stretching from Slavonia to Transylvania and has occupied various administrative positions as well. As his military career is slowly coming to an end, our kingdom has the opportunity to utilize his expertise elsewhere. How should we make use of his skills?
