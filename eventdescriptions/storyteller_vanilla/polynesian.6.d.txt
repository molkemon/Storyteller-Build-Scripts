Sennit is a type of cordage made of dried fibers, mostly from coconut husk or grass, that is used all across the region to make different types of cloth, especially hats, but also as an important element for traditional architecture, boat building, fishing, or ornamentation. One would not think of this as a tool for death, but the human brain is very creative, and using a sennit to strangle their ruler was the option thought by the attacker.
