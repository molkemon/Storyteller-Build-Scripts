Some moons ago, words reached us of a people who call themselves the Israelites and claim to be the chosen people of the One God. We have reached out and spoken with some of them since. They have told us how, through the prophet Muse, they have received the One God's laws. They are written in their holy scripture, the Orit, of which they have gifted us a copy. In return for following these laws, the Israelites are under His protection. They are awaiting the arrival of King Messiah who shall rule the Kingdom of God and usher in an age of eternal peace and freedom. There will be no more death, and those who have previously passed on will be resurrected by the One God. Anyone who has followed God's laws will live forever in happiness, while the sinners shall be punished and executed.
