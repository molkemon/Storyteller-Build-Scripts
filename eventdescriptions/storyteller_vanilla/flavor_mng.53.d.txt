One of the great poets of our era, Yang Shen, is the pride of his home province of Chengdu. Known by multiple art names like Sheng'an, Bonan Shanren, and Bonan Shushi, he is a master writer and lyricist, having already produced great quantities of works, generally around the theme of nature and culture. Some of his works have already reached such widespread notoriety that his name is sure to be etched forever in the pages of history.
