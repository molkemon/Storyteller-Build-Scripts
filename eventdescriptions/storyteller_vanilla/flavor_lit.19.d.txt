The most deprived elements in the court, the Cossacks that make up the backbone of the mobile arms, have sent for an ultimatum to the highest authority of the lands. Recent developments in court affairs have left their leaders very displeased, and they now dare to demand their independence. Their aspirations for independence feed into their unrealistic dream that led to the creation of a Cossack Hetmanate in Ruthenia. If their demands are not met, they threaten the commitment of atrocities and looting all across the countryside.
