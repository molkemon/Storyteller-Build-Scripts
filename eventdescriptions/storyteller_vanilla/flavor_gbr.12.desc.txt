Theater and the arts are spreading through our population, and people in the cities enjoy a variety of entertainment. As promoters and developers of art as we are, we are proud to inaugurate a new theater in the middle of the capital. It is actually a reconstruction of another one, called just 'The Theatre' which was in fact the first permanent theater in all England. Its beams have been transported to the new site and reused to build this new theater, which is larger and more splendid than the one it replaces. With this new theater, our people will be able to enjoy many more plays, including the ones of the acclaimed William Shakespeare, and even our monarch has shown interest in attending some of its performances.
