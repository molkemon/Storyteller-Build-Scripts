This government was founded by Mamluk slaves, and the ownership and import of skilled warrior slaves remain the base for any ruler's power. It is only natural that a slave will be more loyal to his master than any other in his new homeland, and even more so if rewarded by manumission for services rendered. However, currently many of the royal Mamluks in our administration, court, and armies are those that a previous monarch bought and introduced to this government. These are men with great influence but no personal loyalties to the current monarch, men that our new Mamluks accuse of plotting and trying to limit their influence.
