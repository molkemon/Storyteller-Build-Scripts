With the liberation of the peasants from the nobility and other owners, the peasantry enjoys great freedom of mobility within the territory of the realm. While the commoners enjoy their newfound freedom, it comes with unintended consequences for the government. early thoughts of the Enlightenment, emphasizing the freedom of the lower class opposed to the ruling class, have spread in the minds of the masses. If the rights of the peasants are not to be revoked, the very stability of the realm is under threat.
