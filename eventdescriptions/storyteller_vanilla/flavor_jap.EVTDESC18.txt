In the wake of the Higashiyama culture, people were looking for some kind of inspiration. A man in our employment suggested that we build a new temple, called Ginkakuji; the Temple of the Silver Pavilion; as it would be covered in silver foil. A rock garden with Zen and wabisabi aesthetics should be planned and added beside the temple. The simple beauty of the plans seemed to be overshadowed by the inlay of silver, and that suggestion was eventually removed.
