Merciful Waaq be praised, our prayers have been answered. The rain is falling steadily over the province and the surrounding area. It seems as if our efforts have paid off. It has been many years since the people of the area received this amount of precipitation and still there is no end in sight. Heavy clouds, pregnant with Waaq's blessings, cover the sky from horizon to horizon. The people are celebrating the Barwaqoo, or God's Rain, and preparing for the harvest of a lifetime.
