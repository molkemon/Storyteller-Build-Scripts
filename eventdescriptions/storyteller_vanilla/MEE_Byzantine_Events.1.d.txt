The lands that used to be core parts of the Serbian Realm once belonged to the Romans. Due to their delusions of independence, we had to fight many wars to keep them down, and it took them many struggles to eventually split off from the realm. With the rise of the Serbian Empire, the country would become a decisive player, though they too have been touched with the cold grip of wither. Today, having recovered, we stand victorious above the magnates, whose ancestors so treacherously betrayed us.
