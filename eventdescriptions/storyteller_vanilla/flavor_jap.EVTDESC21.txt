The ruler of Japan has issued a collection of edicts called Buke Shohatto or 'The Various Points of Laws for Warrior Houses'. It is a code of conduct detailing how an honorable daimyo should behave, and some of the points are. The samurai class should devote itself to pursuits appropriate to the warrior aristocracy, such as archery, swordsmanship, horsemanship, and classical literature. Amusements and entertainments are to be kept within reasonable bounds, and expenses for such activities are not to be excessive. The feudal domains are not to harbor fugitives and outlaws.
