Serfdom has become a common practice within the territory of the realm. The peasantry suffers under the reign of the landowners while the nobility enjoys the fruits of their labor. However, serfdom has not been legalized by the current Code of Law, and as such the rules for owning and being a serf are not solidified yet. Serfdom has yet to be legally defined. With the Sobornoye Ulozheniye of the year, the monarch can change it, sealing the fate of the peasantry or emancipating the serfs and provoking the wrath of the nobility.
