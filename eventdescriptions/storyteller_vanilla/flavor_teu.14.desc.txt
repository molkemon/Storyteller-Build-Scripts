A squire from the Teutonic Order arrived this morning at our court. He brings a message from his ruler. "The good ruler of that realm makes you, oh noble ruler of the Livonian Order, and your brothers an offer of vassalization. The Teutonic Order will ensure your protection in exchange for your military service." While this offer might be insulting, it is true that our order does not have the fighting power of the Teutonic Order. And with Novgorod, Lithuania, and Denmark as rival neighbors, a strong protector might be desirable.
