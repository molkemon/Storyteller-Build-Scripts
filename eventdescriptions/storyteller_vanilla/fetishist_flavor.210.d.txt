We shed the blood of thousands of bulls upon the scorched earth, and yet we have not had a drop of rain. The skies are as crimson as the ground, heaven and earth mirroring Enkai Nanyokie's anger. By listening to the oloiboni of the affected province, we made the situation even worse. The blood and meat we left for the scavengers could have saved many lives, lives which have now been snuffed out under a relentless sun.
