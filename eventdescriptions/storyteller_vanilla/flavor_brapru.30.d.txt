With all the reforms taking place in our great government these days, there is bound to be some friction between the different ruling classes. The Burghers of Königsberg are currently particularly dissatisfied, strongly opposing the idea of only being allowed to convene their council at the leisure of the monarch. Led by an articulate and well supported alderman by the name of Hieronymus Roth, the Burghers are now threatening to seek the aid of a neighboring ruler in that country if we refuse to agree to their terms.
