Although the adjective forces have fought with all means, the rebellions have proven to be too much for our capable soldiers. Especially the revolting soldiers who rallied behind the religious authority have caused immense devastation on the land, and destroyed the once glorious palace of the realm. The government has been abolished, and the members of the dynasty have been executed for heresy and desecration of the deity's name. Zoroastrians have either been forcefully converted or expelled from the realm. This is the end of the dynasty government and the start of a new religious theocracy.
