As war grips the realm, a Papal legate has arrived to inquire about the safety of the Curia gold stored in the banks of the capital. It is clear that we need a plan to secure the wealth that the Church has entrusted to us. A common, but perhaps slightly cowardly, tactic is to send a caravan out of the city to hide the gold in a secret location for the duration of the war. Our generals however assure us that they will do everything in their power to prevent the capital from falling to the enemy, and so the safest place for the gold is right here within the capital.
