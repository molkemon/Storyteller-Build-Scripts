The sky is full of stars that form different shapes resembling things and have a use for our sailors. the Manaiakalani or The Chief's Fishline looks like Maui's fishhook; Heiheionakeiki is used to navigate to Tahiti; and Ke Ka o Makali'i or The Canoe Bailer of Makali'i represents a bowl pouring onto the horizon. People knowing them are useful for the community.
