An organization calling itself the Prussian Confederation has risen up against the injustices of Teutonic rule and has rallied an army in Danzig. We previously gave its members quiet assurances that we would support their cause should it come to open war. In exchange, the Confederation has agreed that the Prussian lands should be ruled by Poland after the tyrannical Knights have been expelled. We have much to gain from this arrangement, but we should be prepared for a bloody struggle.
