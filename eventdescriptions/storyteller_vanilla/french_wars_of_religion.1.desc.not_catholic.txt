There is widespread anger among French Catholics at the conversion of the ruler to the faith. Resenting the sweeping changes in the nation and fearing for their future, they have formed a paramilitary organization they call the Catholic League in order to demand that the ruler once again bows to the Pope; or failing that, that they are given significant rights and freedoms to safeguard their future. For their part, leaders of the faith community warn against making concessions, hinting that violence would follow should the ruler be seen to favor the Catholics. Either way, turmoil awaits.
