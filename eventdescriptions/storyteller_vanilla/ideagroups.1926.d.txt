Months and years had been spent underneath these unwavering stars, our lambs the only animal that would feed our wandering mouths, dust the only trail of life that we could follow. And then something stopped. Our livestock started to fall one by one, their harmless backs turned to the ground like a carpet in our tents. Words were quickly spread about curses, spells, and hexes. The only certain thing is that death took what belonged to life. Without our livestock's meat, our people will soon follow their fate.
