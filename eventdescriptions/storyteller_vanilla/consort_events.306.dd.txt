The monarch is known for possessing a mind for organization. Making sure that people as well as the administration of the realm adhere to the law, while also updating and adjusting the system to fit the times we live in, is of great interest to the monarch. Imagine the monarch's joy, upon realizing that their spouse shares many of these ideas. A conversation with you, dear spouse, is as stimulating as any council meeting, the monarch exclaims to the spouse one afternoon. The spouse responds with a smile, also much enjoying bonding with their monarch over these matters.
