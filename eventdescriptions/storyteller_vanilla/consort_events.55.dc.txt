The consort has been pacing the palace lately, their head hung low, only stopping sometimes to longingly gaze out the window. Life is quite different here, compared to their old country, a nation some call an enemy. Where does that leave the consort, as a traitor to at least one side of it? The consort shakes their head; such thoughts help no one. Their place is here, in the realm, by the monarch. However, with the knowledge that the government already has the heir of the dynasty, and in their person the line of succession secure, the consort sometimes wonders about their purpose at court. They did not marry out of love, and they have produced no royal children. Would they even be missed if they left?
