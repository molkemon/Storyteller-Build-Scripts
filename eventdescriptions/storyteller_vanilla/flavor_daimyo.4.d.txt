While we are an old and honorable clan keeping together a domain as spread out as that of ours comes with problems. With the death of Shiba Yoshotoshi, some retainers have chosen to support Yoshikado, a son adopted from a minor branch of the family at the request of the Shogunate over the current monarch. This places us in a precarious situation with the current monarch retaining control of part of the Shiba realm, while Yoshikado has the support of the nobles in that province.
