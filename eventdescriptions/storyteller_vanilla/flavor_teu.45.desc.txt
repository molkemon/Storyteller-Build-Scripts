Our campaign into Ruthenian territory has been a great success. The Ruthenian people are now part of the realm and their great cities serve now as additional bases of operation for the country. However, one group of interest did slip through our grasp of the region. the free people of the Steppes, the "Cossacks", are living on our soil but are not under our reign. We should deal with them swiftly so our rule over Ruthenia remains unchallenged.
