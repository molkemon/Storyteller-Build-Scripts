To maintain our history and knowledge, the wise artisans of the realm have created lukasa, memory boards, to chronicle everything from genealogies to maps. A complex system of carvings and beads on the boards serves as a mnemonic, helping historians to recall and recite the past. The artist has grown particularly adept in the art of creating the lukasa, and this opportunity to store knowledge for the future must be seized before this brilliant artist leaves our employ. What should we immortalize?
