The Teutonic Order has long since guarded the Prussian lands to our South. Originally sent there by the Pope with the express purpose of converting the heathens that inhabited the provinces, they have long since outlived their usefulness, debasing themselves by conducting raids and harassing even those with whom they share a common faith. Despite our religious difference and disagreements in matters of divinity and faith, we must nevertheless recognize their experience and expertise as capable warriors. Perhaps it would be wise and prudent to keep them on a short leash.
