After a long life, the great warrior king Kapilendra lies on his deathbed. To the shock of his eighteen legitimate sons, however, the old king, guided by the voice of Jagannath, has elected to make his illegitimate son Purushottama his heir. As the king draws his last breath, the royal succession is far from assured, as Hamvira, the favorite candidate until this date, has fled to the south where he has taken up arms against Purushottama.
