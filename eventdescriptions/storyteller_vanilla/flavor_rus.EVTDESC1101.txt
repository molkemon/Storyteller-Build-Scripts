The Time of Troubles stretched from the death of the last Tsar of the seven hundred year old Rurik dynasty, Fyodor Ivanovich, in fifteen ninety eight, and the ascension of Mikhail Fyodorovich of the Romanov dynasty in sixteen thirteen. Famine, plague, public unrest, and the appearance of false claimants plunged the country into a decade of turmoil as neighboring powers like Sweden and Poland saw their chance to interfere.
