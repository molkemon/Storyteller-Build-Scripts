The infidel activities of the court have provoked the religious authority for far too long. They claim that the current ruler and the nobility have lost their minds; that they have deviated too much from the ways of the main deity. As such, the religious authority have rallied devout soldiers and have made an ultimatum for the ruler. either abandon the quest for resolution in the Zoroastrian faith and declare it as state heresy, or face the wrath of the united religious authority armies.
