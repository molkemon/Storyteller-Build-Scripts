Many of our administrators follow Confucian philosophies, and their beliefs are rapidly taking hold of the administration of our nation. Though Shintoism is still widely practiced throughout our domain, many people are beginning to believe that religion is a private matter to be decided by the individual, and not something the state should be interfering with. If we abandon our traditions entirely and adopt Confucianism as our state’s philosophy, we may bring peace and harmony to people of all religions living in the realm.
