Bandits have been harassing the province for quite a while now; roads are unsafe and traders have been avoiding the area and taking alternate routes to get past. As local authority isn't as strong in this area as in some other parts of the Empire, the brigands have been able to continue unchecked for now. This has become a matter of some concern even outside of the area, as the leader of the band has been gaining a reputation as a leader of resistance against central power in general. Local authorities have reached out to the monarch for help to deal with the problem before it spreads.
