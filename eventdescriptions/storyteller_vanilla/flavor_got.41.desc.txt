The monarch sails to the port of the domain with nothing more than a single, pathetically small boat. The ruler had only one goal. capturing the precious flagship of the owner, the Hayrettin Barbarossa. The crewmates called the monarch crazy for even attempting this stunt, but the monarch's determination is absolute. An afternoon, the monarch arrived at the port, the boat completely sunken due to hull damage. But that did not bother the monarch in the slightest. After a short, unintentional prison visit, the monarch captured a heavily damaged ship directly in front of the owner's authorities. Naturally, the monarch got pursued by their greatest pride, the Hayrettin Barbarossa, named after their most famous pirate. What the owner's sailors did not expect, however, is that the monarch had planned this from the start. So when the owner's sailors boarded the damaged ship, the monarch captured their now empty flagship and sailed into the horizon with it.
