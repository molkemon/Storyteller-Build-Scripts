There are those who feel the Imperial Examiners are not in tune with what is good and proper essaywriting and that the ideal they promote is stale and old fashioned. Among those studying for the exams, alternative collections of examination essays have begun circulating of what is considered a more modern form. The examiners, however, are determined to keep promoting good taste and proper form. By supporting the renewal faction, we would bring a generation of new thinkers to the fore. This would likely come at the cost of the goodwill and loyalty of the old generation.
