Provinces have reported to the capital that they are now in full compliance with several sets of new imperial edicts, the only problem is that no such edicts were ever issued. An investigation into the matter reveals that forged bronze plaques with written edicts, bearing the ruler's seal, have somehow found their way to the province governors. It is a dark time when people dare challenge the Emperor openly, and many claim that this is because the dynasty no longer enjoys the Mandate of Heaven. What shall we do about these false edicts?
