After the death of her husband Hong Taiji, Empress Dowager Xiaozhuan saw her son Shunzhi become emperor and stayed away from the political intrigue surrounding the young emperor. When he died and his son Xuanye became the Kangxi Emperor, she advised him and took care of his upbringing after the death of his mother. She wields a great deal of influence in the imperial court and is known for her wisdom and political insight. The luxurious conditions of the palace are not to her liking, and she has always refused to hold any kind of birthday celebrations, claiming that it would be too costly.
