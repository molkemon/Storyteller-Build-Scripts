After the unsuccessful attempt to stop the monarch from worsening the nation's fate, the paranoia that had surrounded the ruler has only proved to be true, thus causing even greater harm to their mental health. Having already interrogated the closest members of the council, the monarch has started to enter their rooms unannounced, accompanied by some royal guards and a severe tone to the ruler's voice. "This must be done," is the only reply one can get from the ruler while their belongings are being checked.
