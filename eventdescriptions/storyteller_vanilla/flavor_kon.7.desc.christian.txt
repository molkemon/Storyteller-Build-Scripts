A small boat was stranded at our coastline. When a few of our warriors approached it carefully, they found five totally exhausted men in brown robes. Considering their clothes, they looked like they had been tried to be burnt alive and barely escaped without serious physical damage. They were brought to the monarch as the discovery of these pale men gained the people's attention. Apparently, these missionaries are persecuted people, who were under the attack of Protestant heretics. They tell us about their technology and their religion and gained the attention of our upper class.
