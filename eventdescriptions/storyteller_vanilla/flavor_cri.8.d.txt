Following a hard-fought campaign, the monarch has called for a Kurultai at the recently conquered city of Soltanieh. An assembly of generals and lesser Khans gathers outside a most auspicious location; the Mausoleum of the Ilkhan Oljeitu. Speaking before the Kurultai, the monarch raises the pressing question of how the realm is to be ruled. They gestures toward the Mausoleum and boldly proposes that the Ilkhanate should be restored. Invoking the legacy of the great Mongol Empire and the conquests of Genghis Khan, they make a compelling case for the revival of the subordinate Khanate.
