As the monarch is not getting any younger, the royal council has begun to look around for suitable heirs in the widest sense. One of our most powerful vassals, the ruler of SchleswigHolstein, has put forth the idea that their nephew and heir Christian, a descendant of Valdemar the Victorious, would be a suitable candidate. The ruler of SchleswigHolstein has also made it clear that they will support the candidacy of Christian out of their own pocket.
