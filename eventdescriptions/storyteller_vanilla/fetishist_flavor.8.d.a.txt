The people of the province find themselves in a state of confusion after one of their most valued members of society has fallen ill. Confined to her bed, one influential village elder has not stirred from slumber in over a week. Advisors suggest that they encourage the locals to pray, but to whom should the lost souls be turning? On the one hand, there is Tano, the river god and son of Nyame, said to race to their side against Death in the final hours of life. On the other hand, Anansi the Trickster could perhaps be convinced to lend his aid as well, in exchange for something valuable.
