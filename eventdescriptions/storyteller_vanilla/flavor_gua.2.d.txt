A messenger from that country tells us that their ruler is prepared to make a deal with the Bandeirantes who capture and force the people of that country into slavery. Their methods are questionable; the Bandeirantes have been known to disguise themselves as Jesuits, singing mass to lure the natives out or using surprise attacks to capture them. It is a very profitable operation though, and that country's ruler suggests a way to make it easier to profit from this.
