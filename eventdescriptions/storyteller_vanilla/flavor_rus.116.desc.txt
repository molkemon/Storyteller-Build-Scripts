Originally an alliance of convenience and a sense of duty, the bond between the nation and the Persian ally is stronger than any political tie our state has seen in the past. Through cunning strategies and combined forces, the Muslim threat from Persia has been eliminated and the people of the Persian ally celebrate a life free from the menace of great powers. It is obvious that the Persian ally owes us for our support, and no other reward than their loyalty to the crown is good enough.
