We have decided to send a group of our best prepared explorers to further examine the place. The cave is silent, the air dense and difficult to breathe because of the excessive humidity. Insects run freely among their feet. Darkness follows them. When they finally reach the entrance to the ancient site, they feel the need to stay quiet, still for a brief moment; what they see is what they have heard about many times. The legend is unmistakable. murals, ceramics, and even bones are all preserved in exquisite form. When they approach them, the discovery of some ancient split figurines surprises them, as they know they would be of special interest to our ceremonial people.
