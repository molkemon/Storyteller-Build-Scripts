Our fleet set sail to find a western sea route to the fabled rich Islands and Cities of the west. It took them many days to cross the ocean westward, after they had found the Indonesian Islands. The fleet's captain died from a wound caused by a poisoned arrow when assisting a local chief in conquering a rival tribe. Two of our ships had survived; one tried to return across the open sea but perished. The last ship continued west, sailed across the Indian Ocean, rounded the Cape of Good Hope, and as it arrived at our port, it became the first ship to circumnavigate the globe.
