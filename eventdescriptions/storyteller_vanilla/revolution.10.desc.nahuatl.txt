The revolution has arrived. The government has collapsed under its own weight as the revolutionary masses stormed key government buildings. The monarch and his supporters among the nobility have been rounded up and sacrificed to the Gods by the revolutionaries in the hope that their divine wrath may be abated by such eminent offerings. A purely temporary Committee of Public Safety has now been appointed to guide the new Republic through this revolutionary period. Threats abound, however, for monarchs and despots across the world fear they may suffer the same fate as the monarch and are already preparing for war against us. On the bright side, liberal forces are looking to us for assistance in their own struggles, so we will be able to gain support in such a manner.
