Although we do not doubt the strength and tenacity of our warriors, the technological advances of the neighboring armies have not passed by without our notice. The adoption of firearms greatly changes warfare, and we can see clear advantages in this. Our neighbors are unlikely to provide us with all the technical details to fully equip our army, but they do seem willing to sell us some of their weapons. While this would probably give them an advantage in terms of our trade exchanges, it would doubtlessly be of use to our military efforts.
