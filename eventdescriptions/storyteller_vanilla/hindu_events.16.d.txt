Your understanding of Shakti's creative and graceful aspect has aided the realm's efforts in intimidating and enticing other nations to join your causes. One of many methods by which nations are bound is through the union of marriage, which you sometimes find to be the most elegant solution, having established several such bonds with other nations. Diplomatic negotiations have taken on the character of extended family gatherings, all united by the blood of the ruling dynasty. As of late, the profound impact of these unions has inspired you to consider how the goddess aids your work. Perhaps deeper tantric meditation can bring you closer to understanding her.
