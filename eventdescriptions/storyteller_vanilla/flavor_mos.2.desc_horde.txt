The end of an era has come. The once wild horde of the realm, which subjugated the Rus and forced them into the Tatar Yoke, has reformed into a new type of government; one which does not have to rely on barbaric methods such as raiding to enforce its political power. Despite some in the court claiming weakness of the ruler to be the reason for this change of politics, the monarch's actions will lead to a prosperous time which is more akin to what the European countries see as civilized.
