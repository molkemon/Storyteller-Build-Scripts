Wrinkles faded away, eyesight improved, gout disappeared, and troubled minds finally found peace. The water was sweeter than nectar, clearer than any cut diamond, and more well-guarded than the crown jewels of any country; but now it is ours. The Fountain of Youth. Be careful though, as imbibing the precious water might cause some unforeseen side effects. Too much of the good stuff and all that.
