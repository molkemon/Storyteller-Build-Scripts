The Infantes of Aragon are the brothers and sisters of king Alfons the Fifth of Aragon, second ruler of the Trastámara dynasty, which was established in the country after the Compromise of Caspe in fourteen twelve. They are also cousins of the Castilian king Juan the Second and conspired since early in his reign to control him and seize power in Castile, with the support of a faction of relatives and allies among the Castilian nobility. The situation deteriorated from fourteen thirty-seven when a new civil war broke out between the faction headed by the Infantes of Aragon, and the one headed by Álvaro de Luna and the heir to the throne Enrique, which is now coming to a resolution.
