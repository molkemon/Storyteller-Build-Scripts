Despite all odds, the monarch managed to use their diplomatic wit to negotiate with the Janissaries. Not bloody and wounded, but peaceful and in glee, the former hostagetaker departed from the royal palace in peace. Although bloodshed was inevitable, the privileges of the Janissaries have been revoked in favor of advocating the other classes of the realm. Those who want to continue to serve as well-paid soldiers remain in the corps while the scholars, landowners, and merchants of the Janissaries have been promoted to clergy, nobility, and burghers. Though the discussions and debates took several years to conclude, they brought peace and stability to the government; even if it is at the cost of the monarch's absolute power.
