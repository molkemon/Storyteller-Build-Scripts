With Normandy reconquered, the council deliberates on the fate of the historic duchy and the chaos that it has caused over the centuries. On a pedestal in front of them lies the Ducal Ring. One of their advisors speaks up. "He is right, we cannot use it." "We only have one choice. The ring must be destroyed," replies another. A third figure stands and rushes forward, drawing his axe. "Then what are we waiting for?" With a single mighty blow, the man strikes the ring.
