As the civil war that has plagued our land has come to an end, we stand at a crossroad. Some feel that this would be the ideal time to reestablish a monarchy in our lands and call for the reinstatement of the old heir to the throne, albeit under the condition that the realm's governance is regulated by a strict constitution and laws to prevent the situation that led to our recent turmoil. Others argue that the time has come to do away with the monarchy altogether and establish a modern republic built around the old principles of the English Parliament.
