By watering the dried roots of the kava plant, we get a hallucinogenic beverage called Kava too. Kava is a central part of most ceremonies all across the Polynesian Triangle, and it is a highly ritualized act which follows a strict set of rules and order. As the process of creation of this beverage is not set in stone, the results could vary a lot, leading to different outcomes. The last celebration we had with some important members of our own and neighboring societies was a total success due to the high quality Kava we served. Our fame as hosts has been greatly improved.
