Some of the European settlers in areas close to our border have begun to raid our settlements for slaves. Entire families of Tupi villagers are being tied up and marched off to sugar plantations in the European colonies. While at first we hoped this would be isolated incidents which we might demand retribution for, it is now clear that these raids have been put in systematic use by some land holders. The elders of our tribe feel that if we do not act now, this will be entirely out of control.
