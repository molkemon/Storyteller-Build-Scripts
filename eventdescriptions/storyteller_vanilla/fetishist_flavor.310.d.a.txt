One of our nation's holiest places is the tanindrazana of the monarch, located in the ancestral lands province. Some of our most respected and influential ancestors reside here in the family mausoleum. While the tomb is still one of the most impressive monuments in the country, maintaining such a sizable building is quite an undertaking, and the state of the tomb has begun to deteriorate. If we do not spend more on its upkeep, it would be an affront to the ancestors.
