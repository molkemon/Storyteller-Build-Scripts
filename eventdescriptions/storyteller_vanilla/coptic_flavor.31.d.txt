The province of Quina has sent representatives to request resources and support in the restoration of a famous old monastery said to have been the home of Saint Schenoute the Archimandrite himself. The foundations of the stone walls were set over a millennium ago, and the devout worshipers in the area long for nothing more than to restore the building to its former glory. They assure us that with aid, it will once again become a center of higher learning and scripture studies.
