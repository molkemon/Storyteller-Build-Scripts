The Brandenburger lands are not the richest in Germany, and for long periods of time law and order have been hard to come by. As a result, many families have a tradition of lining their pockets in times of financial need by violent or coercive means. Our countryside is plagued by Robber Barons from the less well-to-do noble families of the realm. They are a menace both to our authority and to the burghers of our already struggling merchant cities. If our state is to thrive here in the sandbox of Germany, we must restore order and end these destructive traditions of the local nobility.
