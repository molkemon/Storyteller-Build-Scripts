Reaching the bank of the Ganges, you feel a sense of providence. As you step into the river with the ashes, you envision the water that surrounds you flowing through the three worlds, seeing the darkness of Patala as well as the beauty of Svarga. As Ganga once descended to the world and blessed it with her waters, your predecessor's spirit will ascend to the heavens. You feel an unprecedented unity with the goddess and the river, praying that you will once see the end of your life on this sacred shore.
