While the League war was started for religious reasons, the religious allegiance of the Holy Roman Empire is not the only concern of the members. Some see the war as a chance to claim land from those on the opposing side, considering the religious goals of the leaders to be secondary, if not a pretense entirely. Recently, we have been confronted by ambassadors from the leader of our League, who implores us to make our real motives clear for the benefit of future peace negotiations.
