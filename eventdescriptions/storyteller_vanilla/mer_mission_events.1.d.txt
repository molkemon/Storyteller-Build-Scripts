Delhi is the city that the Muslim sultanates have made their capital in India, but it was once a city ruled by Rajputs. As the armies of Mewar descended upon the ancient city, it seems they must have found it the most important to mark the end of Sultanate rule, however. While the looting is not comparable to when the city fell to Timur's armies, monuments have been torn down and treasures looted. The fall of Delhi marks a major shift in Indian politics; only time will tell what will happen next.
