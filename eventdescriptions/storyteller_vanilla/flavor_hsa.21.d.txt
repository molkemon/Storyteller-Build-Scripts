Being a nation that funnels such an abundance of trade, we see great movement of goods and people of great value. One such prominent individual is Georg Giese, a talented merchant from Danzig who has been managing his family office in our London office for years. His operations there have been a great success, and our profits and influence in the London market have been greatly increased thanks to him. Now that he has come back from London, it seems that we can use his great potential in a more direct way.
