An heir was recently born to the house of the ruling dynasty. Little heir makes you hopeful for the future. The heir's birth also brought to mind the reasons why you view Devi as the peerless supreme divine power. Although you may rule the country as monarch, the power that perpetuates your bloodline and indeed the world itself is the domain of the feminine. Recognizing this aspect of the goddess in your own spouse makes you ponder the beauty and kindness of Devi.
