Due to its seat in the parliament, the realm can boast that it has quite some influence on the politics of the country. That seat is, however, the only thing that distinguishes the area from much richer parts of the state, such as the province of another domain, which looks on at the realm with great envy. This is something that the local landowners have come to realize, and through a combination of bribes and threats, the votes of the realm have come to be controlled entirely by a few corrupt yet influential individuals.
