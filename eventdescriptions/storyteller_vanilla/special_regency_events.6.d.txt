When asked about the deepest thing in the world, people in the court replied "the caves under the mountain," "the bottom of the ocean," "the throat of the tiger." When asked about her opinion, queen Jung Soon simply stated. "The deepest is the human heart." Her wisdom is unparalleled, and the love that the peasants hold for her is also unprecedented. Many are the voices that have started to suggest that she might have a better fitting head for the crown, now that the ruler is dangerously ill.
