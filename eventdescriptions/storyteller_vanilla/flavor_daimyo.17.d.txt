Few have profited more than us from the fall of the Akamatsu clan after their assassination of Shogun Ashikaga Yoshinori. As the Akamatsu were stripped of their important lands and belongings, many of them were given to reward our loyalty, and as a sign that we have atoned for the rebellion of thirteen ninety-one. This sudden rise to power has made some suspicious, however. The Hosokawa especially view our influence as troubling and might well act to limit our influence in the future.
