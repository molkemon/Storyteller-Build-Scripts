We are at war, but not just any war. Our enemy is not only one of current circumstance, but a hated rival of our country. At times such as this, the provinces are united against a common threat, and nobles are eager to lead them into battle. Who better to lead than the Prince of our country? While allowing the ruling family to rally other nobles and the common people to war would strengthen combat morale, the Statist are not fools. They know that the effects of this unity will linger beyond the war and that Monarchists will be reluctant to let go of any power they are granted, even for the best of causes.
