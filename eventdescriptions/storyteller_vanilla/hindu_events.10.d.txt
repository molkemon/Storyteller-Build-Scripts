After many years searching for a guru to guide you, you have found no one who can adequately explain the mysteries of existence or bring clarity in religious matters. At this point, you are beginning to lose hope in great teachers and prefer to discuss the spiritual with your peers. One day, you speak with the ruler of a neighboring country, discussing the nature of the soul and its unity with the divine. Although that ruler is a follower of Vishnu, you find that your words about Shiva resonate. Later, you think about the conversation and realize that what you said did not sound like the words of a pupil, but a master. Perhaps Shiva has already shown you his grace by giving you this revelation.
