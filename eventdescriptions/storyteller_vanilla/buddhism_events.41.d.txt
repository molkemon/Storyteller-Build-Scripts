Thriving monastic communities, holy sacred relics, as well as a long tradition of how to handle the coexistence of monastic and regal power make us the envy of many Theravada rulers. It is therefore no surprise that the ruler of that country should aspire to imitate our greatness. The foreign embassy that arrived today asks that we allow a number of their monks to travel to our country and receive ordination here in order to bring our traditions to that country.
