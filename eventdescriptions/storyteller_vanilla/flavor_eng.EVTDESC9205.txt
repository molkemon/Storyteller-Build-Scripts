English poet, dramatist, and actor William Shakespeare is often called the English national poet and considered by many to be the greatest dramatist of all time. His plays, written in the late sixteenth and early seventeenth centuries for a small repertory theater, are now performed and read more often and in more countries than ever before. The prophecy of his great contemporary, the poet and dramatist Ben Jonson, that Shakespeare was not of an age but for all time has been fulfilled.
