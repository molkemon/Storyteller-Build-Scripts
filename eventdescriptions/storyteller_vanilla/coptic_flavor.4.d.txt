Rejoice, the holy city of Alexandria is once more under Coptic rule. The historical seat of our sacred Church has been liberated by the other nation. Alexandria is an ancient and proud city, founded by Alexander the Great. Many of humanity's brightest minds have called her their home. For many hundred years the scholars of Alexandria studied and taught at The Great Library, which was followed by the Catechetical School of Alexandria, first in the world of its kind. In the years to come her sacred halls of knowledge will be restored and come to produce some of the finest theologians the world has ever known.
