Some may argue that the AlShura is part of the ruling structure, but not one of its pillars. Meetings should follow the teachings of Islam, and consultations should follow the guidelines of the Qur'an and Sunnah. The leader should be elected from those attending to head the meeting. Discussions are based on deliberation and consensus. Everyone may voice their opinions, and the voices of the majority are accepted, as long as it does not violate the teachings of the Qur'an and Sunnah.
