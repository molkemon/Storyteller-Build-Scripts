The monarch of our magnificent government stepped up to the altar with fierce determination. The setting sun glinted in the obsidian blade held and the chanting of the priests intensified. As the ruler made a deft cut down the length of their arm, the crowd cheered. The blood still dripping from the ruler, they nodded solemnly to the elders seated in the audience. One of them smiled as a queue of eager warriors began to form to the altar.
