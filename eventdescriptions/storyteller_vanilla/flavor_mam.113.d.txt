In the heart of the domain, amidst the bustling lanes and vibrant markets of the old medina, lies the resplendent Royal Palace of the domain, known as Dar alMakhzen. Currently, this palace finds itself a bastion of regal authority, where our dynasty will etch its legacy into the very walls. Approaching the grand entrance, the Bab El Makhzen gate stands adorned with mesmerizing zellige tilework, each intricate piece reflecting the artistry of the era. Inside, a tapestry of history unfolds within the palace's chambers, where rulers orchestrate affairs of state and host illustrious gatherings. The palace is a living embodiment of local Moroccan craftsmanship, every detail from the intricate patterns to the towering walls whispers tales of a resplendent past. The conquest of such an illustrious monument is a clear sign of our superior might in the face of adversity and against all our enemies.
