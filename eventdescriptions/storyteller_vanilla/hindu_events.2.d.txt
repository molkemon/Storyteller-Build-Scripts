Of all those who attempt to capture Shiva's glory in art, the artist has always succeeded beyond others. This is why the advisor was called to your court, but despite their helpful advice on many topics, their knowledge of the divine remains a mystery to you. One night, walking through the palace halls of the capital, you see the artist putting the finishing touches on a statue and finally ask the advisor what visions allowed them to portray the many faces of Shiva. The artist answers, "Each and every soul is part of the spirit of Lord Shiva, and just as Shiva created all there is, the soul is blessed with the power to create. Know this within yourself, and you will know the divine."
