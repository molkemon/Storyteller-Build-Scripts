The people have lived in this arid climate for a long time. Although this causes some foolish nations to dismiss us as inhabiting the land unwanted by others, we have mastered skills of survival and combat that they would be wise to fear. Just across the border at our realm, our brethren are suffering the oppression of other people, people who may not be prepared for what the culture are capable of when you underestimate them. With the help of these locals, we could organize a raid into the lands of their oppressors, striking quickly and taking advantage of the fact that we know the desert there better than anyone else.
