The Bishop of the local monastic area reports that the local monastic communities are growing at an unprecedented speed. The Estates of a local monastery in the capital have grown so big and are so well kept that they have become the envy of our local administrator. The governor has become entangled in a conflict with the Monks over where exactly the demarcation between their lands and those of the government goes. The time has come for central authorities to step in and settle the dispute. Helping the monastery would undoubtedly secure their gratitude, but this could also be an opportunity to get hold of more land for the state.
