In Lake Texcoco, home to the realm, one man on his throne had a dream. He saw something outside of this cage that they live in. He saw a land, a limitless land that he sought to conquer. The man's name is Moctezuma the First, the second Emperor of the Aztec Empire. Although in the past the title of Emperor was only ceremonial, he transformed it into a proper title through the subjugation of his neighbors, as well as military crackdown of the other members of the alliance. With this recent shift in power, he can now start chasing his dream. There are many threats around him, such as the Purepecha Empire, the Tlaxcalan Senate, the remnants of the Zapotec, and even the disunited Mayan lands. There are many foes that threaten to stop this dream, but they shall conquer even Mictlan for him.
