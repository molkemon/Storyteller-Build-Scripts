The first port activities in Amsterdam date back to the thirteenth century. The port was first mentioned in the year thirteen forty-two when the city of Amsterdam received city rights. Recently, in the golden age, the port has been transformed into one of the main harbors of the golden East India Company and our operations around the globe. Merchants bring exotic goods to our markets from shores of extreme distance as they are attracted by the busy vibe of our beautiful city, the new pearl of our affluent times.
