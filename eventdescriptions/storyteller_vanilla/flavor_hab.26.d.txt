In an age of conflict and flame, Europe has been yet again plunged into war. The lands of the realm stand in the middle of all this, facing enemies from all sides. In this age, the realm has seen its first spark. Maximilian the First of the realm is perhaps the most renowned Holy Roman Emperor. His ambitions led to him securing much of Western Europe under the Habsburg dynasty, and his military reforms shaped how warfare was fought for centuries, giving birth to the Pike and Shot formation.
