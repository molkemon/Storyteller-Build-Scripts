Rajput nobles have come to both outnumber and outrank all other nobility at the powerful court. They know well the power they wield over our armies and our administration, and have leveraged this power to demand extreme concessions to Rajput nobles and their territories. Our attempts to curb their power through subtle means have had little effect, and so we must consider taking drastic action; either we must accept that the Rajputs are a law unto themselves, or we must strip them of their noble ranks to restore balance to the court.
