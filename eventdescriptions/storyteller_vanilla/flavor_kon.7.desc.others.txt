Three men in heavily damaged robes and serious wounds flushed on our shores. A few civilians immediately got to them and took care of their wounds. After a few weeks, the men were able to stand up again and told us their stories of their home; and how it was destroyed by unholy forces. They also tell stories about their religion, Catholicism, and how one can simply join them. Their stories about their religion and their technology raised an eyebrow of the ruler, but ultimately got their interest.
