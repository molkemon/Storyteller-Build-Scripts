In times of need, the rulers of the realm have been able to call upon the people of the capital to lend their support in important decisions about the continued future of their government. The participation in such an assembly, or Sobor, is the duty of any upstanding subject of the monarch and can strengthen the national resolve in difficult times. Perhaps in this perilous age the time has come to convene a Sobor, so that they can move forward with the support of the people.
