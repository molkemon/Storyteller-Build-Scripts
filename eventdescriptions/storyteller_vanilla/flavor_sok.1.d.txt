Inspired by the Fulani religious revolutions, Usman dan Fodio, a Fulani Muslim scholar in Gobir, has begun agitating for reform in our country. His strong message and his charismatic person have impressed parts of our population, and a growing movement now supports the creation of a Caliphate governed by orthodox Islamic traditions, adherence to the Sharia, and the mission to spread the true faith in West Africa. The message is also quite critical of the current way our state is run, and the authorities in Gobir fear that Usman Dan Fodio's followers might soon openly revolt against our rule.
