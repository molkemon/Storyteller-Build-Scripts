The Ava ceremony is one of the most important customs of our people. It is a solemn ritual where kava, or Ava as we call it, beverage is shared and includes speeches and oratory. On this occasion, several tribal chiefs have been invited and other notables of our realm. Ava is made from dried roots and mixed with water and shared among the celebrants. Our guests are clearly enjoying the celebration, and we might want to take advantage of their good mood.
