For over a century now, Europe has seen the effects of the Reformation and Counter-Reformation. As religious borders begin to solidify, many Protestants or Reformed subjects of Catholic rulers find themselves on the run. With the revocation of edicts of tolerance, hundreds of thousands seek a new home. Brandenburg is not a densely populated region. If we were to offer these men and women refuge and pay for new homes and perhaps a tax exemption until they get stabilized, we could undoubtedly harbor many of them.
