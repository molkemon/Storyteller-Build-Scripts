While Mamluks make up the core of our forces, not all of our military might consists of warrior slaves. Local soldiers make up much of our infantry, and they are also used for guard and garrison duties. Small chances of advancement and low pay aren't the best motivators, however, and it is not clear how far we can trust these men. Certainly their training and equipment are often neglected by the Amir in charge of them. In that province, the mistrust between the local people and Mamluk Amirs has led to a mutiny.
