An envoy from the leader of the peasants in East Frisia has arrived asking the ruler to support the claimant's claim to the County of East Frisia. In return for our support, the ruler has offered to kneel before the Emperor as a Prince of the Holy Roman Empire. Officially, the county of East Frisia is held by the claimant of Holland. The Holy Roman Empire has made no official stance on the matter, and a single word from the Emperor could bring an end to this conflict.
