The aristocracy of the realm is like any other. made of members of the society who own land and are dedicated to the war matters of the state. And similar to all the noble families in history, so does our aristocracy try to expand their influence over the state at the cost of the ruler's authority, undermining their authority. In a moment of wisdom and foresight, the monarch has established the 'Third Force' in the form of the local military group and has split the noble aristocracy by playing them against each other. Neither the noble faction nor the tribal faction have the support of the other military leaders to enforce their will on the monarch.
