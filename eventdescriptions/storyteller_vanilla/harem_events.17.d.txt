The monarch's most favored consort, the Haseki Sultan, and the ruler's mother, the Valide Sultan, are women of great influence and ambition. Both also control a faction of their own in the Imperial Harem, and while they both claim to have the best interests of the monarch at heart, a clash of wills has always been inevitable. The consort has been maneuvering to have one of her sons take the heir's place, but the Valide Sultan will have none of it. Both women have invested considerable prestige in the conflict by now.
