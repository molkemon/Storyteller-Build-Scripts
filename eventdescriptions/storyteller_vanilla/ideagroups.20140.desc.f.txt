Ever since the man appeared at the noble court, he has been a mystery to our own elite class. Nobody knows his name nor has been able to find information about this secretive man. That is until today. In the middle of one of our military meetings of the monarch, the nobleman has walked into the room and has claimed to have strategies against our enemies which are beyond the understanding of our generals. Although the outcry was big, he has proven his point and reveals himself as a daring admiral who got banished for his strategies.
