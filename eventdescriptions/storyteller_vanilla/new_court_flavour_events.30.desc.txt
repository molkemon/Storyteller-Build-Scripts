The advisor has earned many friends in the realm court thanks to their dedicated service as the monarch's advisor. However, it seems that the reverse is equally the case among their countrymen in the service of the rival domain. Envious of their position, they reproach them for taking up employment among their people's enemies and have begun spreading rumors casting doubt on their trustworthiness. Worried about their standing in the realm court, the advisor has become a strident opponent of the rival ruler. They made common cause with several likeminded nobles and politicians to call for a harsher stance against the rival country.
