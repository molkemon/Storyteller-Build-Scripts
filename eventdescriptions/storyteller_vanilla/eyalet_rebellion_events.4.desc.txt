An Eyalet is the administrative division of our empire and as such is supposed to have little difference from core territory and provinces held by the Sultan himself. In reality, this is of course not the case due to the vastness of the empire, and as such an eyalet has to be governed by a deputy of the Sultan. Unfortunately, the ruler of that eyalet sees little reason to pay the usual tax of the Eyalets. Letting them slide with this defiance will give other subject territories similar ideas.
