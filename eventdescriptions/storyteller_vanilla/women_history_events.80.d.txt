The Green Queen, Ratu Hijau, is the eldest daughter of Mansur Shah of Malacca, and after her brother's murder, she has worked hard to become queen of the country. After twenty years of unstable rule, she promises stability and prosperity. While her brother ruled, she tried to influence him into becoming well-disposed to merchants, create a single lingua franca, and restore the country to greater glory, something she now promises will be realized under her rule. Her younger sisters, Ratu Biru, the "Blue Queen," and Ratu Ingu, the "Purple Queen," support her even though both could become queens in their own rights.
