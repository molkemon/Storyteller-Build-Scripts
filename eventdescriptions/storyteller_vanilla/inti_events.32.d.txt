The honor to guard the life of the monarch is hereditary and belongs to a number of families in the capital of that realm. This honor is in itself not a guarantee of martial prowess, however. After a tour in the provinces, it is clear to the ruler that the quality of the guard could be dramatically improved if they brought in some new blood from the countryside. This would, however, not be well received by the families currently given the privilege, at least not until the new members have been integrated for a few generations.
