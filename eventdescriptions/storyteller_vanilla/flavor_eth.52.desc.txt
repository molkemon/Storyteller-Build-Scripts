In the years fifteen fifty-five and fifteen fifty-seven, the first Portuguese Jesuits arrived in Ethiopia. At first they intended to stand for the small Portuguese community, which has been growing in Ethiopia since fifteen forty-three. However, their influence in the Ethiopian court grew. It went so far that the Catholic church started to become a real threat to the authority of the Coptic church, and now they are trying to talk the ruler into converting to Catholicism. How shall we deal with those foreign missionaries?
