The situation in the Baltic is dire. The neighboring Orders of Livonia and Prussia have been embroiled in a decades-long struggle with their southern neighbors of Poland and Lithuania. This uncertainty cannot be allowed to continue as the need to secure our future arises. To that end, we may send royal messengers to the halls of the Holy Roman Emperor, petitioning to join the defensive confederation that they are spearheading. Many voices in our court are deliberating in regards to this matter, given that joining the Empire would imply giving up some of our autonomy as we will be subject to Imperial law and jurisdiction.
