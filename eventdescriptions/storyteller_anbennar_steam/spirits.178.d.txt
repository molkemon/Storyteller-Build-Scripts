The hungry are fed, the famished are strong. In multiple villages of the realm, which have long struggled with food shortages, a curious phenomenon has occurred—a kind of 'antifamine'. We suspect that it is Preta, a spirit known for transforming the hungry into the satiated. According to some locals, the spirit has manifested itself as a happy, plump peasant. Interestingly, this blessing seems to be limited to those who reside in the area, and we are unsure how long it will last. Undoubtedly, this miracle will strengthen the province and its people.
