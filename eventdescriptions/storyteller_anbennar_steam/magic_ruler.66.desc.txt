The monarch has made them known as a powerful practitioner of magic, and one unbound by the shackles of old laws and regulations, or as the Anbenncóster sycophants call it. a WitchKing. The international community sees the monarch as a powerful leader of a pariah state and an outcast amongst the diplomatic circles of the world. But who cares? A lion does not concern itself with the opinions of sheep. All that matters is how the monarch and their dynasty see them.
