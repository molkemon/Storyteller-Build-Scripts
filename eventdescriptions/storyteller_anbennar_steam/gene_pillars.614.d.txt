The party comes across a room blocked by a locked gate, a large set of vertical metal bars which blocks their passage. The explorers search for a way to unlock the gate, but the gate is badly damaged. The lock is almost inoperable; even if they had a key, it seems unlikely it would fit. Through the gaps, glowing devices and objects shine. A pressure plate is on the other side, most likely opening the gate. However, the gaps are too thin for anyone to squeeze through. Perhaps something smaller could fit.
