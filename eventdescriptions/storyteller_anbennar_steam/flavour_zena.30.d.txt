The line between bravery and stupidity is often a thin one. Both are quite close to insanity as well, which may be what protected Azaly as he walked right through the throng of Highhold orks into the great assembly hall of Amlharaz with the goal of studying its architecture as well as the remaining records left from the dwarves of old. With a ceiling that almost seemed to disappear into the sky, especially to his diminutive halfling form, and a table filled with great feasts, scattered gems, weapons, and loot that may as well have been a city unto itself, the Kol Doukang, the Hall of War, was an impressive sight. Once the greatest of the great halls of the ancient Dwarovar, it had become a chaotic mix of a war room, a fighting pit, and a tavern for the carousing orks of the Shattered Crown clan. While we generally prefer the quill to the sword, it is inevitable that we protect our borders, and of course fight against the Kheteratan Empire who still desires to reestablish their control over Horashesh. After returning to Bivuzena alongside several orks who were fascinated by the inquisitive halfling, the Kol Doukang and the likely exaggerated legends of the loot within have been used as a model for a command center within the governmental structure of Bivuzena. This will allow generals to gather with the rulers of our nation to plan battles, and soldiers to feast in victory.
