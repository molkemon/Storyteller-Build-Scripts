Like a wildfire, the Ravelian faith has spread across Cannor and Escann, and now we are at the forefront of this new spiritual debate. Unlike the Regent Court and Corinite faiths, however, Ravelians promise to bring with them not zealotry and religious violence, but educated and peaceful practitioners, who are wise in the ways of science. This is an opportunity that we cannot simply glance over; we should consider letting these priests educate our populace in hopes of further advancing our civilization.
