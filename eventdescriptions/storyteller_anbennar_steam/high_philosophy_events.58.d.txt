The High Judge of the World, Dipar, was in many ways a first among equals in the time of the Gods. Dipar's wisdom was the guiding light of the many gods, and it was his plans and advice that were pivotal in dozens of tales of valor and glory. But wisdom is not something that is confined in meditation; it is through a well placed word and a steady hand that a keen mind sculpts the world. But a true judge settles questions – and one remains. When to play the hand, and where?
