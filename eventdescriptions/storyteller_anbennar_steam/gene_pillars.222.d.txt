As the expedition travels further, they come across a macabre sight. There were partially constructed mechanim hanging from the ceiling in great rows. Hundreds of them all lined up. The expedition got another message from their host. "Ah yes, this is a production line for labor mechanim. They do not have the necessary technology yet, so the estimated completion time is never. I have shut down all mechanim production; you are aware of what is outside this place even more than I am. I am not creating new mechanim who will have to endure that." Taking another moment, the expedition realizes the hallway was more of a balcony overlooking the large open space. There was no safe way to the floor and no walkways on their level. "After that magical event, my production lines activated contrary to both my and my chainers intentions. It was almost ten minutes before I regained control and shut them down again. All this is to say, you should stop advancing. Things are bad enough out there; it is pointless to add to your suffering."
