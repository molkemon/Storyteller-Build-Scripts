With the arrival of the latest group of newcomers into our lands, an ancient injustice has finally been set to rights. The ancestors of the centaurs lived in these lands long before the first humans ever set foot in what was once the Oldwoods. While what has been done to their people by ancient Castanorians cannot be undone, we are glad to see their kind roam the vast grassy plains of Escann once more. Coming from the distant steppes east of the Serpentspine, they now live a seminomadic existence, traveling between our settlements and supplying them with the goods they need.
