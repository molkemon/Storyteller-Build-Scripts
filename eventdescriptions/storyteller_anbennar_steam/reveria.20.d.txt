The Mocbarja Dam is securely in our grip, the men are restless and the treasury could use some fattening; it is time for the Northern Raid. Our men set out from Mocbarja and raid several areas over the following days, the most profitable one being the dense city of Svemel. A curious detail about this area is that these people do not worship the Ynn, they instead appear to worship dragons. As such a lot of the loot comes in the form of various types of dragon art: pots with dragons painted on, glass or iron figurines of dragons and relics from bygone eras. The raid went well, with minimum casualties, and now it is time to head back to Mocbarja.
