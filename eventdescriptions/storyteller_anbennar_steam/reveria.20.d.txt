With the Mocbarja Dam securely in our grip, the men are restless, and the treasury could use some fattening; it is time for the Northern Raid. Our men set out from Mocbarja and raided several areas over the following days, the most profitable being the dense city of Svemel. A curious detail about this place is that these people do not worship the Ynn; they instead appear to worship dragons. As such, much of the loot comes in the form of dragonic art. pots painted with dragons, glass or iron dragon figurines, and relics from bygone eras. The raid went well, with minimum casualties, and now it is time to head back to Mocbarja.
