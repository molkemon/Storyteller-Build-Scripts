The brightness of the Imperial Crown dims as our ascendancy continues undiminished. They call the borders a backwater, a place where storybook monsters like vampires go bump in the night and disloyal vassals disappear into lavish castles. Let them stay ignorant; our challenge to them will come swift and unrelenting. In a few years, the emperor and empire must face the song we sing. If they do not allow us to sing solo or join us in a duet, we will create a discord and ring out louder than any other. The ancestors of the borders were kings of salt and darkness. Let us remind them who we descended from.
