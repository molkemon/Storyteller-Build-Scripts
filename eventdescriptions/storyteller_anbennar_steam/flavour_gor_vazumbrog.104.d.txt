It would be a terrible waste, propounded Lorek, to kill these unique creatures. Certainly we could bait and poison them, but just the same, we could bait and tame them. I do not hold with those who believe Krummul's legacy demands violence in every circumstance; we can be much more effective heirs to the titanium legacy if we make use of every resource at our disposal. And bait and tame they did, finding the catsized spiders to be both skillful weavers and surprisingly charismatic pets. This left just one question. how much should the population of the chitinous darlings be allowed to grow?
