Even if not of the same culture by birth, the people of the realm are difficult to tell apart from bonafide members of that culture in their manners and methods. They speak like us, behave like us, and most of all, are strong like us. The men now harbour fabulous mustachios while flags of the Wyvern fly over their cities proudly. We are now one and the same; they too have found their home alongside us. Not a single trace of what they were can be found. And it is better that way.
