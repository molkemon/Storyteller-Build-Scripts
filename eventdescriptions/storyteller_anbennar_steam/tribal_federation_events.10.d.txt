There are many informal ways for the clans in our federation to show their influence. A position in the royal council is certainly one, as is command of armies or the right to tax specific provinces. One of the foremost honors a clan can be bestowed however is a position on the lord’s honor guard. Any influential clan would be expected to have representatives among the ruler’s personal kawass, but there is only need of so many guards for one man. In some ways this is an idea at odds with the goal of actually protecting the lord.
