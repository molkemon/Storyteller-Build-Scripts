The first ever confirmation of Aelantier's existence was the day it died. The Day of Ashen Skies may have torn Aelantier asunder, but ever since the elven Remnant Fleet arrived in Cannor during the year one thousand, the tales of their fabled homeland have gripped idealists and explorers from all over. Since then many have attempted to sail west and failed, until now. Aelantier has been rediscovered.
