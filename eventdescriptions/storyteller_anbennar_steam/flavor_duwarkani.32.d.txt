The requirements for a member of the Menkaunswt dynasty to rule the nation have been written into law for as long as it has stood. Although there have been grumblings amongst the nobility that such arrangements mean potential fine rulers are unceremoniously displaced from the throne, they all recognise that to not allow a Menkaunswt back on the throne would result in civil war. Thankfully such a candidate has appeared. One from the same bloodline as King Kenmu Menkaunswt and Queen Safana Duwaran has been brought forward to fulfill this vital constitutional requirement.
