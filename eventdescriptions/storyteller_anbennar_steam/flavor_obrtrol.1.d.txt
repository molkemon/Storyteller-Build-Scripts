Returning from the Forest Troll hunting grounds was our diplomat and a brutish female adorned in wood carvings at least ten feet in height. The ruler met the woman and diplomat outside. The Forest Troll spoke to the point quickly, "We see how you take much human land. Hungry kids need food, I tell other trolls about food, we let you share land." The diplomat explained in a quiet whisper that the troll is an influential figure among the Forest Trolls and would be very useful to get them on our side. No matter the choice, our trolls will work to make sure the local trolls are part of our great kingdom. But we are ill-equipped for this voyage into the unknown, and they are not.
