A Voltoj Academy, with a Diwan present and enough development, can spend that development on an upgrade. Upgrading to a Medium academy requires thirty development and the Upper Rivers Academy Circuit can only support two Medium academies and one Large academy, which can be upgraded from a Medium one when the province has forty development. Each Voltoj Academy is also well situated to administrate unique local resources; expanding an Academy's capabilities to allow for this requires fifty development, and each one will require uncovering some documents or learning some secret. Each Voltoj Academy upgrade will spend half of the development required to coordinate it, and it will spend the Base Tax first.
