It didn’t take long for us to learn why these wretched swamps were known as the Folly. The choking odor of sulfur, the howls of unseen monsters, and steady degradation of the land itself has made living here nearly a fool’s errand. Even within our clan, renowned for pushing the frontier, only the hardiest of our people were able to settle its edges. And only a select few were willing to press further, driven by the same bold curiosity as our forebears. That may have been a mistake. A folly if you will. For today, cutting through the wretched ambience of bog life, were elven screams, both hoarse and hysterical. They were chased after by cackles and hoots, distorted by forces not from our plane. The usual browns and grays of the swamp were alight with a sickly green hue. Geysers of verdant fire soon erupted after. And from those pyres emerged hairless, upright snouted creatures. Their eyes burned with chaotic craving as they set upon their victims.
