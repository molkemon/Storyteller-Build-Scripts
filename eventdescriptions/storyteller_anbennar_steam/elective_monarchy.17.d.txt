Ruling in this nation is not like ruling in other nations. Dictating the nation's goals is difficult without approval from nobles, sometimes impossible. To a foreign monarch, this may seem disappointing and frustrating, but this cornerstone of the nation's politics is highly valued in its homeland and is, in fact, what the monarch has to thank for coming into power in the first place. Embracing these ideas might make the ruler seem weak internationally, but would aid in integrating better and gaining supporters in the Sejm.
