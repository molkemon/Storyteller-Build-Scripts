Ruling in this nation is not like ruling in other nations. Dictating the nation’s goals is difficult without approval from nobles, sometimes impossible. To a foreign lord, this may seem disappointing and frustrating, but this cornerstone of our politics is highly valued in its homeland and is, in fact, what our lord has to thank for coming into power in the first place. Embracing these ideas might make the lord seem weak internationally, but would aid in integrating better and gaining supporters in the assembly.
