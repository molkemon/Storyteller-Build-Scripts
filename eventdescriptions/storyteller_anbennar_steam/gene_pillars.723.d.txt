Why is the structural metal alone so perfectly preserved? These walls have been submerged underwater for thousands of years, at least. Even the finest artifacts dissolve away in the brine of the sea, which has certainly been the case for most of the instrumentation you have uncovered as you have pressed onward into the pillar. Why have the walls not corroded, then? Why has the steel not given way? Every room you enter is full of decaying tools and experiments, but every wall you pass looks like it was built yesterday.
