Our realm has grown large, and while so far our subjects appear to maintain their loyalty, some suspect that with the Gnolls gone they will become more and more rebellious and eventually attempt to obtain independence. One suggestion has come from the clergy in regards to keeping them friendly with us: Make them join our faith.  This is a controversial decision, as it is atypical for the State to actively convert a foreign population, but nevertheless it seems it'll be carried out. Already we have offered positions for the nobility which lost their territories during the gnollish incursions in return for their conversion and every day new missionaries arrive on the shores of Akasik.
