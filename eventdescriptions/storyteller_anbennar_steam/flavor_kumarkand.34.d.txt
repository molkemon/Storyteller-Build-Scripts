Envoys from a certain domain arrived at the ruler's throne room and unfurled a large banner with a specific flag upon it. The envoys walked upon the banner as they approached the ruler with brazen disregard, desecrating it with their dirty shoes before the ruler's eyes. Without being granted the right to speak, the envoys set out a demand of another domain; immediate recognition of the ruler's legitimate rule over that domain. The envoys smugly awaited the response they were sure was to come. Before the ruler spoke, three of the four envoys were violently dragged away, kicking and screaming, then thrown out the windows. Three seconds later their screams fell silent, and then the ruler spoke. "No quarter will be given, and the domain will bleed until the whole world has seen what the price of treason against that domain is."
