Spring is fast approaching and we have received requests for our assistance from a local province. Agriculture is essential to the local economy of that province as well as that of surrounding areas, but during the last few years, they have not been blessed with sufficient rainfall. Surely the farmers are already praying to Vortzo Laukuno, the god of agriculture, for aid. However, the local shamans believe a ceremony led by the monarch would prove more effective. They are stressing how crucial this year's harvest will be for the future prosperity of the region.
