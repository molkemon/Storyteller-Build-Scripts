Trade is just like the sea itself. Tantalising and everfluctuating, bringing life to the communities who are fortunate enough to receive its blessing. At times the sea is harsh and punishing, and yet at times it can bring wonders from the deep to shore. The fleet has brought back not just gold, spices, dyes, and clothes, but something more extravagant this time. What this could entail is only for the future to foretell.
