The more time passes, the more it seems that the everincreasing minority of Ancestor Worshippers is growing louder and more dangerous. In that province, one group has even gone to such lengths as to assault and massacre an entire chapel of hapless Pantheonists with pickaxes and other mining instruments, with the intention of destroying the temple as an affront to the ancestors. Eventually, the local guard managed to break in and kill or capture the responsible ones. However, this brings little comfort to the families of the slain and the survivors.
