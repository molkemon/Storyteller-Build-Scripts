In our aimless wanderings through our verdant home, we have encountered another tribe of beings like us yet unlike us at the same time. They call themselves the Kaora To Tatau To. To them, artistic expression is the true purpose of their existence, and they spend their time during their migrations crafting works of art, both lasting and ephemeral. And most fascinating are their voices, so unlike our own, with a richness and depth of emotion we can only admire.
