The second expedition’s planning is underway. The options for us are the Zuvunwa city of Zena, a historic city to all those who live in Horashesh. They have a close proximity to a variety of different ecosystems and cultures. Zena itself is a wondrous city with an affinity for trading in relics and exotic arts. By sending an expedition to Zena, we can take advantage of its trade and culture to explore a variety of regions all at once. The Dakinshi city of Kaziradoka, famous for its wealth in gold and silver, does not shy away from displaying its glamour. Much of the glistening architecture stems from the city’s time under the Djinnakah Vizierate. Nowadays, everything has been well preserved alongside Dakinshi culture, with blades, beans, pelts, and the smell of coffee beans lingering. Sending an expedition here would provide great insight into both the past of our own ancestors and how other people under Djinnakah have diverged. Pick wisely, my liege.
