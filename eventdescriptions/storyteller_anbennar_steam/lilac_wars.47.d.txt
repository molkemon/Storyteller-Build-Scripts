Those who've returned to the Serpentspine might scoff at us who remain in our new homes, adapting to the end of the FlowerGrudge, but we’ve our own ways. Ways that involve getting back to work and doing our best as craftsmen to find a measure of peace and profit without qualm as we do so.  Of course, we know this peace won’t last. Despite their shorter lifespans, humans are almost half as good at holding grudges as a dwarf. The FlowerGrudge might be over but we’ll be turning ploughshares into spears and back again soon. Luckily, war and peace both make good business.
