We arrive at a great open space inside the pillar. The room has been thoroughly ransacked; many mechanisms have likely been here before. In the middle of the room is a stone structure; it looks like a scribe's desk but much larger. When the expeditionaries go to approach the desk, all of a sudden a low noise rings throughout the room and a voice starts speaking. The speech is distorted and unlike any language found in Insyaa. Nothing else seems to occur; no defense mechanisms activate, no traps spring. After a short time the voice stops as well.
