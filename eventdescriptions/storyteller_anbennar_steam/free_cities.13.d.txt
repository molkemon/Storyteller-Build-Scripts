Anbennar means unity, but so often the Empire's nations have remained divided and at each other's throats. We ourselves may yet choose another path, however, and we have recently inquired if any other Free Cities would be willing to enter a league together with us. We Free Cities are weak alone, but collectively it is clear that mutual cooperation between small nations in matters such as trade, keeping imperial peace, and supporting tribunals and maintaining a unified force makes us a force to be reckoned with that even Great Powers will struggle to suppress.
