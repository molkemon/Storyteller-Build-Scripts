There is a great amount of paperwork that needs to be produced to make the country run, from birth certificates to marriage licenses to death certificates. In days gone by, we used to rely on organic clerks to do this crucial work, but their hands cramped and their memories lapsed. With the discovery of the mechanim and the work of our artificers, we can customize them to do all the work necessary to govern at a fraction of the time and cost. The new autoscribes that do the paperwork in the realm have twelve hands which never cramp, and which produce perfect, absolutely legible writing in whichever script we please day in and day out. Their vast databanks have been put to work memorizing the details of an entire community. They never mistake the form that needs to be filled out, and they oftentimes can fill it out better than the one who is making the query. In the world of tomorrow, the bureaucracy that makes our country run is limited only by the number of autoscribes we can procure. Unfortunately, this means our paperpushers are also free to dream up an impossibly large number of forms for them to fill out.
