The dreams of the tower continue, colored by a far darker presence than before. The tower has reached its peak, but so too has the wickedness that diffuses from it; a great horde of undead seeps from its base, but are held back by a lone figure in tattered black robes standing upon a balcony gazing down, a WitchKing. As they stare down upon their new subjects, their gaze slowly drifts towards the dreamer; though their face is covered by a fragmented ebony mask, fractured by lines and shaped in an endless scream, the dreamer can feel their eyes meet. And with such a meeting comes great knowledge.
