To this day, our knowledge of interaction between magical spells has barely scratched the surface, even more so for Genie magic. From what we can gather of the recent event in a prominent city, a prominent member of the mage estate thought putting an elephant on several Magic Carpets for a long voyage was a great idea, dismissing the common belief that Bahari elephants are affected by a powerful ruler’s wish as folk tales. But even those who didn't expect it hadn't anticipated the elephant to somehow absorb the Carpet’s magic and start flying. Pirakil, as they have begun calling him, is already the pride and joy of the city’s inhabitants; that is, when he in his clumsy flight isn't destroying their roofs.
