To this day, our knowledge of interaction between magical spells has barely scratched the surface, even more so for Genie magic. From what we can gather of the recent event in the domain, a prominent member of the esteemed mages thought putting an elephant on several Magic Carpets for a long voyage was a great idea, dismissing the common belief that Bahari elephants are affected by a Godking’s Wish as folk tales. But even those who didn’t have expected the elephant to somehow absorb the Carpets magic and start flying. Pirakil, as they have begun calling him, is already the pride and joy of the domain's inhabitants; that is, when he in his clumsy flight isn't destroying their roofs.
