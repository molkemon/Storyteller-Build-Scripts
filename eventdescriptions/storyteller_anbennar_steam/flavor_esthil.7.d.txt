At long last, the Iron Sceptre has achieved its goals; through conquest, diplomacy, and adventuring, the ragged band of former magisters, adventurers, and explorers is now in position to stake a claim; not merely as a company, but as a country. Houses replace tents; warcamps become forts; and forage land becomes farmland. With their end goal clearly in sight, a great assembly is called of various influential company members, captains, explorers, magisters, and settlerfolk with no less a goal than the establishing of a new Escanni nation. One of the first, but most important decisions, is who will lead them in the coming years.
