Inside the structure, there are a maze of corridors and rooms with minimal indicators as to purpose or direction. You rapidly feel like you are becoming disoriented. On stepping out into a large crossroads, your skin tingles and you feel the latent magic in this room start to activate. This is not the first trap you have encountered in this complex, and you know that should you survive this, it is unlikely to be your last, but for some reason this one feels different. Whereas most traps are usually trying to prevent further access to the structure, this one almost feels as if it is more attempting to prevent something from leaving the pillar. There are layers to this spell, and each one seems to be growing in intensity. The mana now filling this room almost seems to set your hair standing on end. While you do not know the magic, your gut tells you this is designed to trap, not kill. You spin too late, as multiple wards activate behind you, and lightning starts crackling off the metal dome on the ceiling. You ready yourself to face the threat.
