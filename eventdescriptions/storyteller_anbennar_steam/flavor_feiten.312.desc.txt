In order to ensure that the work of the Shuimu Engineering Cadre can continue as normal, a decree for the creation of a new jellyfish research facility has been approved. Though Shuimu's engineers and artificers have had at least three private duels over the location of the facility despite orders to stop, in the end they have presented a report that listed several possible locations. In the report, the engineers made it clear that, considering the needs of Feiten's future development, priority should be given to establishing a research facility on the Jellyfish Coast. We ought to be careful when choosing a location; besides the fact that asking for another report may well result in more unsanctioned artificer duels, construction will take years and have a major impact on whatever location we choose.
