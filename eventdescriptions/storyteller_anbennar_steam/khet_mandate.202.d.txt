With the Korosheshi League now ruling over the entirety of the country, the question of what should the official state name be has arisen. Kheterata is a prestigious denomination that would signal the nation as heirs to the most ancient human civilization. It would be a nice gesture towards the Kheteratan non-Korasheshi cultures of the empire. However, some fear that it could dilute their identity. They are instead favourable to maintaining the term of Koroshesh, which is after all the heart of the new empire and the seat of the League.
