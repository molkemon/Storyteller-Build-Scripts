Fuyuan Jiao had a visionary dream without parallel in Beikling history. Many of our people had despaired, but she embraced hope and devoted her life to a chance at freedom. Thus many saw her as a mother to the entire Beikling people. Fuyuan’s dear daughter is with us too; she still puts her best foot forward for the realm. The young one might have been an empress had Fuyuan Jiao had her way; as it stands, she might not be an heir but is still a valued courtier. She stands next to her kinsfolk watching Jinqiu fall. There can be no doubt that, despite any understandable displeasure she may feel, she has served the realm well. She, like all of us, devotes every waking moment to the most noble cause of expanding our empire. We defeat our enemies through ruthless strategy and consummate zeal. We transcend the weak origins from which Fuyuan Jiao had begun to pull us away from. We built a kingdom, and then the beginnings of an empire. There can be no doubt that Luzhong will soon be reborn through our efforts. Yet still, Fuyuan Jiao is dead. Why is it that her corpse seems... Never mind. It does not matter. We will see to it that she has the eternal rest she deserves. She will be buried in the greatest possible mausoleum we can fashion for her in Tiaochi. And thereafter.
