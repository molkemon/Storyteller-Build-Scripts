Strange tidings have come in from a nearby village. A young orphan has claimed to be possessed by the ghost of his deceased father, and has started accusing one of the major landowners of his murder. The villagers seem to believe the boy completely; the rumors spread from the village have begun to spiral out of control. Some say the boy can float, some say his head can spin in place, and some say his eyes are pure black.  The boy is obviously marked for death by the inquisitors. However, cursory investigations have shown that his accusations bear some merit. Should the child be disposed of and that called the end of it, or should the ghastly will be undertaken?
