While many of the Aramari clergy resisted the Corinites, due to their goddess' position as Adean's wife, the encompassing nature of the Ravelian seems to have weakened their resolve. Or, perhaps, they simply realized that the idea of an organized, unified church could greatly increase their ability to tithe and gain funding for many of their projects. With the conversion of the Aramari Temple to the Ravelian faith, what was once the Temple of Celestial Fortune has been renamed to simply St. Aras; though with Ravelian funding, its magnificence as a cathedral would eventually grow tenfold.
