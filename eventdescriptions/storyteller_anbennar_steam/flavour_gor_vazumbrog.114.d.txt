It is said that this cavern, the Earthseed, was the place where Halanna carved the first dwarves from stone, bringing them to life. If the myths are true, then the Dauvek Veker risked atavism by coming here. the Ashen Basilisk whose hide the dwarves sought could return them to this statuary state if they but carelessly met its gaze. Fortunate, then, that the dwarves were trained to fight in darkness. None can say quite what the creature looked like in life, for the warriors all wore blindfolds into the battle, trusting their battlecries to maintain safe distance from one another as they wildly swung their axes toward the petrificant creature. Swiftly, and with only a modicum of self and friendinflicted axe blows, the creature lay in tatters before them, its perilous eyes closed forevermore.
