The Dwarven Lords of the West Dwarovar race to Amldihr, all having received an emergency call for assembly. The Amlharaz's Halls fill with a riot of grumbling dwarves, a normal day at the Assembly in all respects. The chatter breaks swiftly at the clashing of the ruler's silverware and goblet. The monarch speaks bluntly, as the ruler always does, on the current state of affairs in Amldihr. The practiced rhetoric breaks, however, as the ruler moves on to the topic of AulDwarov. In candid honesty, the success of Amldihr is laid out. West Dwarovar is under Amldihr influence; the Lords of the Assembly answer to Amldihr's ruler. Relative peace has become the norm after centuries of orkish incursion. Is this all we can achieve? No, the monarch states without a hint of doubt. A sense of what was is returning under our efforts, and what was, is AulDwarov.
