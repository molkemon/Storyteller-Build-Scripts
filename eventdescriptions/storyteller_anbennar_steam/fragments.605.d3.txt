Though the beast was at first extremely resistant to them, over time, it slowly but surely became more docile. Perhaps, at one point, the beast's ancestors were tamed by the Precursors, or some other group, but there is no way to truly know. However, with the beast leading them, their arrival at their goal was far more rapid than anyone could have expected. As their expedition excitedly digs into the great mound, they uncover what must have once been the top of the Pillar, ruined, but not entirely destroyed. A great circular room, constructed of marble, containing large quantities of rubble, that seem to have at one time been portals, as well as various relics scattered throughout the room. From the ruins, the true nature of the tower is now clear to their expedition; the tower once served as a portal nexus for the Precursors, leading from this tower to various other locations around their empire. Yet, they are still left with more questions; why was the Pillar in particular so important? Why construct a whole tower just for portals? Most importantly of all, where exactly did these portals lead? It seems that these questions will be left to future generations, and for now, all they can do is ponder on the meaning of it all.
