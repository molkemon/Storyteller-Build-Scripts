Alan Leapdon sat in the near empty tavern at dusk, facing the man he was trying to get information out of, but who unfortunately seemed more interested in devouring cakes and pies like he were starved. Maybe he was, the Broken Seas weren't exactly overflowing with arable land. Finally, mercifully, Robert Saltstrider looked up to face him, eyes pincer sharp. "Forgive me, lad, it has been a long journey. Let's discuss Eordand, at last." Nodding, Alan began his questions. "What's the political situation?" "Fractured. The various tribes of Eordand are fighting, as far as I can tell it's religious disputes. Crusades, countercrusades, and the like." "It's like we never left Cannor." Leapdon laughed. "Anyone in particular we should worry about?" "Not from what I can tell. I've heard whispers, of some particularly powerful mages, but nothing specifically. And nothing we can't deal with." "Wonderful. Now, any specific landing spot?" Hearing this, Robert pulled a map out, and pointed. "The northern tip of the region. It's cold, but the locals know me and I've made sure any landing wouldn't be opposed on the beaches there."
