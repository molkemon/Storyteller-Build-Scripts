They are almost to the heart of the genepillar. The whole expedition can feel the air around them thrum with magical power. Whatever was up ahead may have more magic than an entire meteorite made of a tear. But before they got there, they came across several war mechanim at the far end of the hallway they were in. Unlike other war mechanim they had faced, these were clearly not mass produced. They all are larger than the average war mechanim and looked more heavily armed as well. Their bodies seem to be made of a gold hued alloy as opposed to regular mechanim; it wouldn’t be a stretch to assume it was stronger too. The all too familiar voice of FreedomChainedNoMore resounded once again. "As I am a mechanim, I too have a kauga. Perceiving the danger to the facility if my kauga was ever threatened, my chainers made sure to guard it constantly with their own warriors and specially designed mechanim. Obviously the chainers are gone, but the mechanim remain; they’re nice enough, good to chat to every now and then. They also want you dead, like me. I’m honestly impressed that you’ve nearly made it to me; I’m also terrified by that fact. Regardless, this is it; do your worst, I know I will." With that, the walls and ceiling opened up; there were sawblades, those ovular golem like things, and other strange weapons. Both the expedition and their host knew this was their last obstacle before they met face to face.
