There has been a terrible accident.  The great sovereign of our nation has fallen, their light departing to the empyrean.  Yet it seems strange, as reports continue to filter in; their fall seems to have been a literal one, and yet there are no signs of magic having been weaved when the height for such a thing to be lethal would indicate they would have been able to summon forth a spell of abjuration to protect themselves.  It seems odd, and while the fallen goblet of thick, fortified wine found near the scene might explain this, no one on the council can remember them being the kind to drink themselves this deep, perhaps into a stupor but not such a dangerous stupor as this.  They and some other interested councillors have decided to look into if our late ruler had kept their alcoholism a secret from us all or if there is something else afoot. The prime suspect is obvious, but our rulers are all gifted floodborn, so the possibility of hiding this via illusion or other magicks to avoid being removed from power is a feasible possibility.  The council will have to confirm and make sure this investigation does not provoke the anger of The Regent.
