Overlapping chants beat the earth and sky, some rhythmic, some discordant, driving the color from the world. Shadow ritualists draw on the power of the shadows as the dying wails of the sacrifices trail off, the fire's amber glow dulling to a sickly white. Power seeps into the world, everhungry, and we feed its insatiable form. With each Shadow Invocation we complete, we draw Halann deeper into the darkness, and reap the rewards unrepentant. Among their other offerings, the ritualists have devised ways to shape our society further, to strengthen our Shadow Warriors so that they may fuel our further ambitions. All we need to do now is choose which path we pursue next.
