As Seghdihr has slowly but surely become less reliant on the Seg Band, pressure has been mounting on the monarch to assuage the fears of the population. This has finally come to a head, as the monarch is now finally implementing a large amount of checks on the power and influence of mercenaries and adventurers. By royal decree, all mercenary companies that enter the service of Seghdihr will have royal inspectors assigned to them, which continually test the combat readiness, discipline, and above all else the loyalty of their troops. Through these new bureaucratic safeguards, the monarch hopes to ensure that events like the fall of the Segbandal will not be repeated.
