The trees soon start to clear out, as the greenery turns to scrubs and bushes at higher elevations. From a distance, someone makes out a peculiar structure. A motley mix of variously sized stones and precious gems seemingly mashed together at random and held together with magical energy, this could only be the crown of mankind. Though likely a striking monument in the days of the early rulers, the later lords had clearly put less and less thought into their offerings, and now the structure was in dire need of upkeep and a rearranging. To progress past this Trial, created by a former Realmbuilder, someone is to add their own piece to the structure. The first challenge was to decide what type of material the new addition would be made of.
