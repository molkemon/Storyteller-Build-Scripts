Goblins are not a native creature to Gerudia, but they can occasionally be found as isolated bands that have moved into former residences of trolls. So too do they mimic the former occupants, terrorizing the surroundings. But compared to trolls, they are but small creatures. Ferocious but cowardly, small in stature but great in number. Even an average farmer can defeat one, but when the whole tribe attacks, villages are removed from the map. To cull such a threat, the Einher has gathered a host to battle such a threat. Striking at night, the goblin chieftain was cut down in the midst of battle. Holding the chief's crown in hand, the rest of the goblins have laid down their arms and begun following the Einher as a leader.
