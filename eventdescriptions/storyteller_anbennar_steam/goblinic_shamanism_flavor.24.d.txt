Even a goblin child knows Guard Thunder, whether or not he's known as Blexim or Eragurd in our realm: He's the morning star who circles the sun to fight against the hordes of evil spirits, who want to kidnap her, with his lightning arrows.  However some know him as much more: according to traveling fanatics he once liberated the other gods known by our people from slavery by the evil deity followed by the brutish orks, 'Great Dookan', when Dookan was distracted fighting other gods.  Now he's fighting both Dookan and the other gods alien to our kind, and he's calling on his followers to help him by fighting their henchmen on Earth.
