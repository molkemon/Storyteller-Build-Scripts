The exchange of gifts is an ancient ritual, involving the sacrifice of a possession and the trust that you will receive in equal kindness. Even moreso than mortals, the Fey exchange gifts with such glee that would make children look like static and glum. Today you have received an anonymous gift. It is customary that anonymous gifts are to be reciprocated in kind by depositing a gift of your own in the woods: those who deserve it will always receive it.
