As the lord leaves to walk the streets of the capital today, taking a discreet inspection with their retainers, they think briefly to their ancestors; how proud they would be to see the Hold flourishing once more. Goods come from within and without the holds depths, invigorating its many layers with the haggling of merchants. Rowdy taverns are bright and boisterous, filled with drunken dwarves either boasting of their ancestors feats, or snoring dwarves who have drunken themselves silly in delight.
On to the industrial sectors, where other senses take precedence over sight. The sizzle of water upon tempered steel, the clang of hammer on metal and wood, ringing across the hold as various guilds reestablish themselves. The scalding heat of the countless ravenous furnaces fed by the bellows only makes the lord smile. The sound of the hammers and bellows seem to resonate within their heart, as the holds wonder must do for every dwarf that lives within.
As the lord heads to return home after this fruitful day, one sight mars the visits ending: homeless dwarves, thin and weak from malnourishment, something that hearkens back to more tragic days. Their retainers trace their eyes and quickly note that it is inevitable for there to be dregs even in such prosperous times as the present. Even during the most glorious times of the dwarves at the height of AulDwarov, there were still poor people and slums, after all.
With a hesitant nod and lingering gaze, the lord moves on, attempting to remove these thoughts to focus on the pressing matters at hand. The fate of few deviant individuals in society pale in comparison to reclaiming their homeland and riches of their ancestors. The enemy is out there, in here there is naught but joy, wonder and magnificence.
