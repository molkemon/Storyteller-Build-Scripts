The tools designated to investigate the Pillar in the province have continued to uncover the secrets of the Makers, and the Pillars’ link to their works. They confirm that the Pillar we have delved is one of two able to control the Kiamoa, the wall of storms which circumscribes this continent. Beyond lies the homeland of the Makers, the place from which they travelled to do their divine works. Golodh sees the path to fix what has been ruined, to restore the Makers’ inheritance, and it leads outside the great storm. Piecing together the Pillar data with records of Insyaan folklore we have acquired in our overlordship of this land, we have linked three of the Dragon Turtles with reported secrets of the Pillars. In the stories, these secrets give the bearer power over the Pillars and the Makers’ technology. In such superstition, we find our next step. We must find a way to glean these secrets from the Dragon Turtles, by hook or by crook.
