We thought we could rest without problems. We thought there was nothing to fear. We thought we could get a good night's sleep and then enjoy a hearty breakfast. This, however, was not the case. Instead of a hearty breakfast, we were faced with a hard breakfast the coming morning. Our guards were found gagged and tied up. Once we removed the crude fabric from their mouths, they told us that the kobolds had seized the night, overwhelmed the guards, and stolen much of our fresh supplies.
