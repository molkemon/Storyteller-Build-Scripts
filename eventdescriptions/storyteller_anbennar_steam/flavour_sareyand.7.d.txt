The Salahad, as they say, is a harsh mother. And those raised at her bosom are a hardy people. Whether they are the fierce tribesmen of the Mašnsih or the descendants of Jaher’s Phoenix Legions who have taken to calling themselves Desert Elves, those who have managed to eke out a living amidst the dunes and stony wastes of the desert understand the value of grim determination in the face of adversity. Those adherents of the Way of the Blazing Sun who ventured out into this unforgiving land in search of new disciples soon encountered likeminded counterparts. They were found both amongst the ranks of the Desert Legion, as some of the veterans of Jaher’s eastern campaigns had studied the chi techniques of the Xiaken during the time of the Phoenix Empire, as well as amongst the szalAttalu tribe, whose unique style of martial arts, called Ahu Alaktu, has been practiced by the Ahušessu warriors for centuries. Merging their traditions with the practices developed at the Shining Monastery soon resulted in a unique tradition, the Way of the Desert Fire, whose acolytes could soon be found all across the Far Salahad.
