Before we could react, some crystal animals jumped us. In a couple of seconds, multiple brutal cracking and splashing noises filled the air. Some of our companions were reduced to nothing but pools of blood. The creatures growled as they stood over their remains. Unorganized and frenzied, we defended ourselves, smashing some of them with pickaxes and hammer blows. But the crystal monsters smashed more easily and mauled some more of us before our retreat. We managed to organize our run to the other side of the chamber towards more unexplored caves and left.
