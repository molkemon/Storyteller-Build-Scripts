Before we could react, some crystal animals jumped us. In a couple of seconds, multiple brutal cracking and splashing noises fill the air. Some of our companions have been reduced to nothing but pools of blood. The creatures growl as they stand over their remains. Unorganized and frenzied, we defend ourselves smashing little of them with pickaxes and hammer blows. But the crystal monsters smash more easily and maul some more of us before our retreat. We manage to organize our run to the other side of the chamber towards more unexplored caves and leave.
