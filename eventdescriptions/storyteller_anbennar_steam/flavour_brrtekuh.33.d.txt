The tribute received was a mess of what some would call garbage. pieces of fabric, ropes, broken swords, rotten food, and even books with missing pages. The Brrtekuh didn't call the tribute garbage; they knew the treasure that had been gifted to them. With the piece of fabric, they could make new clothes with beautiful designs. The ropes could all be tied together and create an even stronger rope, the broken swords could be melted for new weapons, the rotten food used as fertilizer for the gardens. Even the books would be recycled and the paper reutilized. One man's trash is a treasure for a follower of Kvanga.
