The first time I saw one of the damn birds, I nearly passed out. With thicker legs than a bull, brown bronze feathers, and a massive white plume, the thing was nearly my height, and Kiusz was only barely taller. Even more terrifying was the rider atop it, brandishing a bow and yelling at us. It took some explaining, but eventually cooler heads prevailed and we calmed him down. We explained our situation here in the steppes. We had gotten off the boat in Onman looking for some of the unique creatures rumored to roam across the northern continent, and I quickly found one. Of course, the Yomod Rüsh, those that maintain and breed the birds, weren’t going to let go of their prize mounts easily.
