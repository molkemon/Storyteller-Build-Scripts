The estate mages of our realm have begun using their increasing wealth and power to buy land in alarming numbers, not only for private residences and institutes of magic, but in founding their own businesses, organizations, and other sources of income. Some courtiers argue that this might be a greater plot on the part of the mages, intended to undermine the powers of the other estates and the country itself, or it could be the natural way of things when people get rich and powerful.
