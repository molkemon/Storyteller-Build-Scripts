With Tlagapatung secured, our ruler stands before a dark, imposing statue. The people of this land whisper the name, "Semat Tidakhui", in regards to this figure. No mistaking it. She is your muse, and now that she has spent her energy and power helping you, it is time to help her. Instructions for a rite flash in the ruler's mind. The legs move without thought, the arms dangle in a pose, as if a puppeteer had finally finished weaving the strings. A strange pulse pushes the ruler's retinue back, winds from nowhere enveloping the ruler. The energy around the ruler stutters. For a moment, there is a reprieve. The Lady in Stone is weak from the unmarked cycles of being restrained. The ruler has a chance. One chance.
