Do you ever think our city is dismal? The town guard turned to his peer, confused. What? What do you mean? Well, when the envoys’ caravan broke camp today, one of them called our city dangerous; and yes, I know, that’s nothing new; but then another envoy said, "Worse: this town is boring." I just can’t shake those words. They went on and on about how the city feels shabby and sad. Oh, ignore them. They’re foreigners, so of course they wouldn’t know what the best parts of town are. For example, we have a thrilling gladiatorial scene. I know. It’s just… I only ever see locals attend their shows. Oh, yeah. Huh. Are we just too bleak?
