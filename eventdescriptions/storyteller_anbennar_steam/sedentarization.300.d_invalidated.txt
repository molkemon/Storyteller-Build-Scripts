We have finally recognized the centaurs as what they are. a pest on land that could be great. It means we can no longer sit behind outpost walls, but must ride out to claim the plains from the centaur clans that currently inhabit it. The question remains as to the fate of this area, though. Do we send frontiersmen riding out on horseback to live rough lives in the freedom of the wilderness, or will we push back the wilderness with new settlements, making these lands no longer forbidden?
