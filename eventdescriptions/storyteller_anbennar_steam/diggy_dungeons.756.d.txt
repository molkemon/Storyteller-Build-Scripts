We heard our target before we saw it, the sound of steel against stone repeatedly echoing down the hallway. Then, we caught glimpses of it as we rounded the corner. We are faster than it, but just barely, and unlike us it will not tire. The expedition as it stands cannot hope to catch and fight this thing at the same time. There is only one thing to be done. someone must be sent ahead to keep an eye on it whilst the expedition covers the rear.
