The sweeping reforms imposed by Hemantsen the First and Rani Jira left many of the barons in the realm with nothing but their anger. Nobles who had lorded over vast empires of industry have had their estates brought low, forced to compete with the likes of the common smith or farmer. Treacherous thoughts fill their minds; how can they serve a master who does not care if they live or die? And so, secret dealings begin, forces amass, and the countryside erupts in rebellion as the barons take to the field. The plight of the barons has been a truly bothersome thorn in the monarch's side. Now, with the court up in arms, it can no longer go unanswered. The time has come to bring the nobility back in line, and show them why they serve their masters.
