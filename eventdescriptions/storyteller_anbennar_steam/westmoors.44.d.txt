"Are you sure they...?" asked the lord. "Aye." Nodded the guard. "We interrogated the priest and he said he had married them, on the hillock by the river." "Gods be good, and he didn't think it strange the girl had no father or brothers at the wedding? And neither did the boy?" The lord knew his daughter could get into trouble, and that they had never been as close as when she was little, but this? This was absurd, and he knew this would take years for it to stop being a stain on his clan's name. "The priest said his job was to marry couples, not judge their history." The lord's face was red. "Did he at least tell us where they ran off to?" "Aye, sir." The soldier's hands trembled as he hesitated to continue, until the lord's glare was so great he could no longer bear it. "They went to Brontay Point, to beg the ruler for sanctuary."
