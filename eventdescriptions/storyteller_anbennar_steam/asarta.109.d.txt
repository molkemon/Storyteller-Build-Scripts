A thick smog of incense filled the room where the Court of Change resided. At its centre, laid out on an array of cushions, resided Akhaxpa. As the ruler approached her and knelt down, she opened her three eyes, filling the room with a blinding blue light. “Wars bring change. But war itself does not change. The same thousand lizardfolk marching into battle. You could use cavalry. You could use artillery. Yet you still equip your army with naught but a spear and a dream.”
