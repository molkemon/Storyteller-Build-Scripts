The matter of the Taga'Ala is decided. The Taga'Ala live beyond the Twin Gods. While the Twin Gods show us the way forward, they live a meaningless, static existence in their mangrove forests, refusing to adapt to the world around them. Leaving them in this state would not be a kindness; it would be an act of cruelty. It is the task of the Volcanic Truth to show the Taga'Ala how deprived they are. They will fight our invasion, but the will of the Twin Gods is immutable, and the change we bring will one day help them blossom in new and beautiful ways. As the Volcanic Truth spreads to new lands, the Taga'Ala will take a glowing ember of Truth from it and make it their own. This is what change means.
