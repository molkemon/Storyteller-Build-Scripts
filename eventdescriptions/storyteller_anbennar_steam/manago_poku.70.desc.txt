In our aimless wanderings through our verdant home, we have encountered another tribe of beings like us yet unlike us at the same time. They call themselves the Tamariki Poupou. They tell tales of encounters with the Loa’Set, great antlike creatures, without whose help they would have perished in the jungle long ago. The lesson they took from their fraught origin is to value their community above all else. Everyone contributes to the best of their abilities, and those who exhibit particular talents or experiences which they can share with the tribe are highly respected as elders.
