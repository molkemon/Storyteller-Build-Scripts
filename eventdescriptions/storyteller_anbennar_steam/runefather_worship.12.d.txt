A young and upcoming supporter of the Shadowhammer Clan has shown promise on the battlefield and they are now requesting to lead an army so that they may exact vengeance for the Runefather on the surface. While the majority of the army approves of such a promotion, the Fûrdanhund have objected to it, stating that while the Dwarf shows prowess as a fighter, they lack any true leadership experience and will only hinder the chain of command.
