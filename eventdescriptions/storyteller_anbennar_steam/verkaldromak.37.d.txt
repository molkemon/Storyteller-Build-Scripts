The expedition enters a huge cavern with rather crumbly ground. At their entrance a pond fed by a waterfall and filled with fish forms a small peninsula. The group decides to rest and while most catch some fish to restock at least some food a small group starts scouting the cavern further ahead.  They stick to the wall and move clockwise. The cave hasn’t really much more to offer but they find three exits. First a shaft descending thirty forty meters straight down, only accessible if they use some of their remaining rope, then a tunnel looking like it was eaten into the stone and lastly a stalagmite with a spiral around it making it possible to jump into a tunnel further up the wall.  As they return and give their report the group decides for a way.
