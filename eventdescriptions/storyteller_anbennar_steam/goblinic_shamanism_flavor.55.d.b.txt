Spring is fast approaching and we have received requests for our assistance from the province of Rainland. Agriculture is essential to the local economy of Rainland as well as that of surrounding areas, but during the last few years, they have not been blessed with sufficient rainfall. Surely the farmers are already praying to Kroozbuto Nubo, the god of rains, for aid. However, the local shamans believe a ceremony led by our lord would prove more effective. They are stressing how crucial this year’s harvest will be for the future prosperity of the region.
