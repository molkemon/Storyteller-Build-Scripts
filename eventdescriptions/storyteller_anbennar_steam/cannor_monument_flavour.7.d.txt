There is a certain tranquility that Bal Mire brings to otherwise brutish marshlands. Within a sea of perilous marshes, it represents a beacon of hope, a testament to how the indomitability of will can persevere even in the most hostile landscapes. Although abominations continue to lurk in the marshes and wretched souls contort the landscape to trap unsuspecting victims, with sufficient experience the darkness of the marshes can be traversed. Even as growing numbers of settlers continue to establish their homes within the shadow of Bal Mire, there is still much work to be done. If our progress is any sign of what we can accomplish, one day those restless souls will finally be put to rest and these tainted marshlands shall be freed from the grasp of twisted silence.
