Trolls are not often nervous, but in the northern reaches of the territory in a village once made only of trolls, now occupied by soldiers, they find themselves terrified. They were not ready for a grand army from the south, nor one that could best them so easily in combat. They now try to play the role of grateful hosts. As they walk through their homes, they prepare a feast in welcome. They roast deer, moose, and caribou on beautiful bonfires which reach up to the stars; they empty their storehouses just to appease stomachs. They say they want to be friends, and allies. They want to be part of the armies, and help conquer the north. With all Giantkin united, there is nothing that could not be done. They listen to their words as they tend their flames. They think about what could be done with trolls by their side, and they also think about what ogres could be with trolls in their stomachs. What is chosen to be done as their clan leaders continue on about the benefits of mutual cooperation? Do they accept the offer, or push them into the fires they tend?
