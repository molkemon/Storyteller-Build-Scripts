It is done. We have firmly seized Eastern Cannor, and no foreign kingdoms or petty conquerors remain to stop our great plans. Baranthad now rules over the once-broken lands, and from its rest a new power will rise, with the support of the people. The Baranthadi orks strove to bring peace to Escann, and finally we have succeeded. It is now time to lay crops and foundations for future generations, and build anew from what is ruined. No longer will we starve, for the granaries are full; no longer will we cry in sorrow, for the wars are kept out of Krumani land; and all shall soon dream a new dream for the future.
