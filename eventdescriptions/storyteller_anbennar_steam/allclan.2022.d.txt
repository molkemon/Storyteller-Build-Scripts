It came to our knowledge that some Clodhoppers managed to find an ancient dwarven magma core. Before we could scratch our heads on how and where they found it in the first place, they decided to turn that into a custom grill. Once The Discombobulatorium found out, they demanded the Clodhoppers hand over the relic to study it and use the knowledge to improve our armed forces with new tech. But the Clodhoppers, claiming the most ancient and primordial rule of 'FindersKeepers' refuse to let go of their new power source.
