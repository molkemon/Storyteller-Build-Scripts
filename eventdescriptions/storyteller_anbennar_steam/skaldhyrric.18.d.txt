Hiding in plain sight, Bheur hags hobble south from Far Gerudia as pitiful grandmothers during the wintry months. Bundled up with shawls of fur, their pallid, frozen skin and chilling aura is all but unnoticeable against the raging Gerudian gales. These hags prefer not to directly curse their victims; they instead simply intensify the cold weather and deliver invasive thoughts of despair. To them, nothing is sweeter than causing happy communities to turn against each other in desperation for survival, cackling from their frigid caves. Bundled in furs blessed by the Skalds, the Einher sets forth through the many hamlets of the realm, searching for Bheurs to terminate.
