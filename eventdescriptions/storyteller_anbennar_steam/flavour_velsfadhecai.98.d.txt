A regent was in place. Mitrenya and I, in spite of our weakened states, still had the capacity for that. We were still able to slip away from the castle before anyone realized we were gone. Some would be suspicious, of course. Mitrenya's own magic always kept her looking young, and she made sure to keep a simple glamour on me in order to ensure the same. It wouldn't stop the questions, but they would quiet down quickly. The brain is incredible at explaining the unexplainable away, after all. We traveled to the expanse and walked until we reached a village. Remote, of course, and Epednan. Somewhere nobody could find unless they knew to look for it. Some distant part of me recognized the barbarity of what we were about to do, but I silenced it. All would be in the service of Mitrenya's dream, a dream I would do anything to see fulfilled.
