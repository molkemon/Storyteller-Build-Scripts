The rumors were true. A new Izhuun has risen, rallying the Shuvüüsh of the borders behind his lead. Nurhachi Sartul is a cunning man, and has used the western frontier’s position as a crossroads of civilizations well. With military support from the other Shuvüüsh tribes to the west, and trade agreements with the Yan to the south, they are well equipped to defend their independence now that they have risen up. Even worse, they are some rare shamans amongst their people who are capable of garnering the support of the spirits; several spirits have chosen to fight with them, believing that with the rejection of the ruler from Sado, the Compact may be shattered. Wearing the clan mask of Kadash AkZunk, Nurhachi Sartul rides into battle atop a terrorbird that he himself raised, vowing to never again live under the yoke of a Gowon state.
