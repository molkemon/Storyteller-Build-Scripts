An oddity walked into an outlying village sometime last week, a young cursed one, arms open, repeating the word "peace" over and over again. Up until now, no one has ever heard a cursed one utter so much as a single syllable; however, they communicate with each other, and it is not verbally. Suffice to say, the villagers were shocked, and many feared a trap, but the sheriff arrested the cursed one, and after several days of interrogation, it was established that he just wanted passage out of the forest. In his words, "The bond is diseased, the kin are separated, there is no future in the sacred forest; it is desecrated."
