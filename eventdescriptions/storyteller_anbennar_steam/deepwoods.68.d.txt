The wise have often noted that even time itself plays tricks in the Deepwoods. Years can pass in seconds, and now, a moment stretches to eternity. Your arrow sails leisurely through the air like a bird on the wing, whistling softly; until it buries itself in the monster’s side with a sudden thud, blood pouring from the wound.  The Lord of Beasts gaze snaps toward you, before it throws back its head and bellows a deafening roar, a call to battle that shakes your very soul. There can be no escape and no delay.
