It is done. The monasteries have long prospered under the ruler's rule, and now have begun to connect with each other, even supporting projects beyond the local landscapes of their own domains. Pilgrimages from one end of Kaashalg to the other can travel unassaulted. The monasteries are fully open to each other, allowing for the spread of ideas to go beyond even the monasteries of their own Monastic Code with fervor. A new dawn has approached, and with it comes further guidance along the path to the Supreme Spirit.
