The King of the Kompu was playing with one of his companions when one of his lieutenants walked in. The monarch only acknowledged him with a glance, before he turned back to his closer ally before him, who was teaching the warkompu how to leap and tear throats. "Aryakompu," the lieutenant began, a tremble in his voice. "There have been some troubling reports... miners going missing in the offtunnels, consistently. It's starting to become a drain..." The Aryakompu laughed, as his lieutenant froze in fear. "Miners die all the time; they were probably fed to the swarm. You're missing slaves? Get more."
