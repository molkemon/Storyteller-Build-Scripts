The expedition has come to a large open pit full of ore, there are several hollow tube like structures on the ceiling though sized up such that a whole man could fit inside. There is another passage on the other side of the pit; there is no bridge between them, but the ore is piled so high that walking between them is possible. A short time after entering to investigate, the passageways behind and in front of them suddenly sealed, and the voice started up again. "You have arrived at one of the ore processing facilities of this place. Here massive heat melts the ore for further processing. Do not worry, the blast doors are completely impenetrable so no fire will reach you. Ah, you entered into the furnace before the blast doors closed. Worry, your body is incapable of withstanding the temperatures." A noise starts building from the ceiling. "As foreign objects have been detected in the furnace, the heating will terminate prematurely, eventually."
