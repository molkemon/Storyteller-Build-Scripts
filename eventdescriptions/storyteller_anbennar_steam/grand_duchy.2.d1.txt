When the emperor entered the parliament chamber at midnight, the room was already dead silent. The more boisterous princes were either arrested for the night or sunk low in their seats as the emperor walked to the podium. They took a moment, retrieving a piece of paper from their robes as they glanced up at an empty balcony chair above and blew a kiss to their own ring, stirring some confused murmurs from the seated princes. I, the emperor, declare the ruler and their descendants to be the rightful heirs to the ancestral lands, including all privileges and claims that follow. It took a moment for the princes to interpret what was said until awkward stares flooded the room. One prince from the east remarked as they departed for the night, Did…did they just declare themselves Grand Duke in the third person?
