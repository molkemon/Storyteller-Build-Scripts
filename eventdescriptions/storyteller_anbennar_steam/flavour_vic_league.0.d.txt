The Blue Reach stands independent, but it is beset from all sides. The Gawedi in the south have coveted the Blue Reach for centuries, and stand ready to subjugate its cities under the rule of the House of Gerwick. To the east, gray orks have already invaded the noble realm of Vrorenmarch, and the White Reachmen migrating to our lands bear dire warnings that we might very well be next. To the north the Gerudians across the Giant’s Grave Sea always have and always will see the Blue Reach as a strongbox to be raided, funding future raids. Between the Gawedi and other threatening monsters, the Reach has recognized the need to stand together. The resulting Vic Leagues commit the cities of the Blue Reach to trade together and stand together, but Reach politics are ever shifting. If the League leaders of Bayvic and Celmaldor do not lead wisely, they may soon find themselves in charge of no league at all, or even become the newest province of Gawed themselves. Within this chaos, the city stands ready to make a name for itself.
