As night falls across the land, the heavy footsteps of marching boots echo throughout the few elven settlements that remain on the edges of our dominion. Mercenaries and local headhunters alike prowl the streets of these impoverished slums, awaiting the promised hour with keen anticipation.
As the last rays of sunlight fade, the whooping howls of our commanders signal the onset of a violent resettling. Doors splinter before the weight of steel boots, and the sounds of wailing can be heard for miles; punctuated by the clinking of chains. Hundreds and thousands of Sun Elves are seized from their shabby homes and clapped into irons, to be forced into a life of chattel slavery so that their former lands might be more productively used by loyal subjects.
Though a vengeance would be well justified against the perfidious elves; murderous Jaher after all was not half as charitable as our lord when he violently slaughtered and purged them from this land; vengeance is not what motivates this mass enslavement. Simply put, the ageless elves who serve as objects of hope and adulation to the people have no role to play in our hegemony; they are dangerous to our rule, and act only to foment unrest and rebellion, undermining the stability of our nation.
To break their stubborn grip over this land we must subject them to the harshest oppression and the most degrading labor, so that none might think them divine ever again. As a sunless dawn heralds the morning of our renewed dominion over this land, we are confident that history and our loyal subjects will someday soon come to agree with the inevitability of this decision.
