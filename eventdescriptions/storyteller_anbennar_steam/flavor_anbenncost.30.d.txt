Emil fights with abandon, firing off disintegration spells and flaming spheres, but to little avail. The second son spends his entire force of being in an all-out assault against this mysterious knight. But when the dust settles, the mysterious knight stands tall in her shining armor, bruised but unbroken. When she steps forward, for the first time in his life, Emil steps back. The other Aldresians do not intervene; this is the test of God for Emil and a struggle over the very nature of what it meant to be an Aldresian Knight. The mystery knight swiftly speeds toward Emil, counterspelling all last gasps of magic as she disarms him and places her blade at his chest. She whispers, "Your move," as Emil stares up at her with uncontained rage. Emil lunges but is quickly struck down. His rebellion, his force in this world, is gone in an instant. The mystery knight exhales as she stares out at her brothers and sisters. "This barbarian controls you no longer. You make your own destiny," she speaks, stepping toward into the crowd and disappearing. After a long moment, a lone Aldresian knight steps up to Emil, confirms his death, and looks back to his kin. What are they to do?
