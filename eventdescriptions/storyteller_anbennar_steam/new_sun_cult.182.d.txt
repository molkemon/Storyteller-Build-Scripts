Their goal was to destabilize the Chosen by causing strife between the elven rulers and by agitating the people who follow them. Once the Chosen and their followers were too embroiled in wars with each other, the Old Sun Cultists would rise up and show the angered people that the elves were not unbeatable, that they could still have a unified, human led Bulwar. They had only to rise up and take it.  Naturally, we hanged him from the palace balcony at dawn the next day and our advisors wrote a speech; which was given in every town and city in the country; about how the dangerous group who attacked Samartal were demon worshipers who sought chaos and destabilization. But now that their threat is gone, the people can rest easy knowing that they are safe again, and that the victims of the Samartal attack are avenged.
