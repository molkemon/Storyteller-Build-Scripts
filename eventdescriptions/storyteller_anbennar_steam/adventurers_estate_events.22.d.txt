Weeks turned to months as the monarch awaited any news or letter from their heir. The last word from them had spoken of a temple filled with elemental evil. It was not unreasonable to wait considering the adventurer’s life, yet the description of this dungeon had filled the ruler with dread. Dread that turned out to be well founded. Today, on the wings of a raven, came dire news. the heir and their party had been wiped out.
