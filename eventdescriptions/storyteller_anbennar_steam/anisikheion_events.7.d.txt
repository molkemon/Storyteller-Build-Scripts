A strange sight was beheld today by the people of Anisíkheion; instead of the regular bureaucrats coming into their offices, the navy's officers and admirals were coming in. In order to solidify his position as the new head of state, the Grand Admiral made a statement to the public, saying that there is no large change to the government, and that the elections would simply be managed by the admiralty now. Although the majority of the populace accepted this statement, a minority was quite displeased with this sudden change.
