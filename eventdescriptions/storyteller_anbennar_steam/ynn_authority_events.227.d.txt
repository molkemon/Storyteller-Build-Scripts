Our lands are fortunate enough to host one of the Great Ynnic Dams of the Ynnic Empire. These Dams not only divert water to our fields and help manage the Ynn when possible, they serve as religious sites for our people. Few proud Ynnics would die happy if they hadn’t seen at least one of the Great Dams at least once. That is what makes our control of one so profitable. Market towns frequently pop up near the dams to serve the pilgrims, and they are a valuable source of income. Not only that, but the prestige from having one within our borders is also immense. In the end, it is assumed that we will welcome these wandering souls from another country with open arms so that all will know of our kindness and generosity. However, as important as they are, these dams are also valuable targets for military campaigns. What if these pilgrims are simply spies in disguise?
