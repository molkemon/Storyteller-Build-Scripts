A grand procession accompanied by dwarven fanfare perforated the lord's hall as an envoy from Verkal Ozovar sat upon the ground, awaiting a proper welcome. Once pleasantries were exchanged, gilded words flowed from the mouth of this dwarf, as if to tempt our warweary hearts with the thought of salvation. But such talk was too prompt for such a meeting. In the end, it was agreed that Verkal Ozovar and the domain of Coaban Dinh would enter a formal treaty of friendship, to be renegotiated every five years.
