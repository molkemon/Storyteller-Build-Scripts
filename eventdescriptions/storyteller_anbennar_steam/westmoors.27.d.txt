Rain battered the miserable, depressing island of Toncodd, which in truth was more a rock the gods had scattered in the sea, forlorning it to a fate of storms. But its position in this Sea of Storms had a great advantage for those seeking travel westward through the Lament or Thaw. In a surprisingly dry and warm fort sat an armored gnome with an eyepatch and an intricate clockwork harpoon; Todrick Toncodd, lord of the isle of the same name. "You're lucky I called this meeting on honest terms; some of my brethren wanted to blast your ship, but I knew we would fall to the oncoming storm if that were the case." He glared at another gnome to hand him a parchment, to which he doodled his name on the front, spat on it, and passed it to the monarch. "Choke on it, Your Grace."
