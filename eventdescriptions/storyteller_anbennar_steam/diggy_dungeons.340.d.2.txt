After two hours of rest, the scouts have not returned. We keep on waiting. Our rest turns into uncertainty and worry as hours pass. We can not wait any longer. The captain commands us into following the dark shore. It is a slippery and unsteady march, which makes it slower. It is stressful to keep raising those who fall and to not fall with them. Did the scouts fall into the river? Seems unlikely. Did something eat them? I do not want to think about it. Anyway, five good scouts lost.
