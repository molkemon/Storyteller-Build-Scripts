Success. After gaining access to the last of the Kobold's information, our researchers were able to understand the biology of dragons well enough to make some reasonable assumptions as to how their magical breath functioned. Extrapolating from that, after some trial and error, we were able to develop potions which allow for it's consumer to spew forth fire like mighty dragons. There are still some issues to fix, such as the instability and unpredictability of the fire spouts causing undue collateral damage, but with time we'll be able to perfect this potion.
