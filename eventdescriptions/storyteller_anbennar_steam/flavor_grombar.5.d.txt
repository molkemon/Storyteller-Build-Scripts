With the last defenders of Gulletpeak falling before the mighty orkish horde, the question of what to do with these goblins now presents itself. For most of orkish history, goblins have served as slaves, but in these times, as we see our southern brothers and sisters placed in chains by the humans, the concept of slavery leaves a bad taste in our mouth. So, two options remain to us. we can either expel the goblins and make room for proper orkish settlement, or we can embrace them as equal partners in our new northern kingdom.
