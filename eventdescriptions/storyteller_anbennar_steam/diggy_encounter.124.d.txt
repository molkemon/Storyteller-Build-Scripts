Purple worms, like other worms, are blind. Knowing this, we quietly ducked to the side, waiting for the worm to pass us by. While the worm had only passed through half of our group, one of the junior adventurers tripped. The loud sound could be heard clearly in the deafening silence that followed, until the worm turned towards the sound. The ensuing battle was long and bloody, but in the end we emerged victorious.
