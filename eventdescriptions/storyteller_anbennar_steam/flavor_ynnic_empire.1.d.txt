Today, we witness a miracle. The Ynnic Empire, the greatest nation in the world, once fractured and torn apart until it was an empire no more. But today that damage is undone, for the Ynn are united, and an Empire again. The current lord has been bathed and crowned in a holy ceremony and, from the height of their balcony at the capital castle, they have announced a month of festivity while cheered on by a huge crowd.
