Our community has lacked a sweathouse for a long time since the other suffered a fire. As we have had lots of more urgent issues to face, the community has lived without it just fine. Now, though, the tribe is living in a rather calm period and people have started to miss a proper sweathouse, so our men and children can go there and purify their bodies and souls. It might be a good idea to put some men to work on the construction of this building so the community can benefit from it.
