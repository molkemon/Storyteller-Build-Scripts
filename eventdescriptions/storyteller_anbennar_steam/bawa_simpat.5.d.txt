The monarch trudges through the undergrowth of the Lupulan, sweat dripping from their brow and legs aching from a day's worth of travel through the rainforest. Angered spirits echo in the distance all around, unseen but making sure they are heard stalking the monarch's personal retinue, or else twisting the path ahead to lead in no direction at all. As the monarch lets their vision narrow on the path before them, a chilling wind suddenly breaks their focus, with the monarch turning to find all remaining soldiers of the retinue missing. The wind whispers in their ear again. A voice. An offer to the monarch. A sprawling kingdom of untold opulence and strength radiant underneath the jungle canopy. One final unifier of the rainforest to bring both spirit and mortal alike to heel. A monarch of two planes, that must bow only before the great benefactor who made it possible from the very beginning. The Whistling People of Tlagapatung speak in hushed whispers of the Lady in Stone, and her words linger in your ear now. Kneel before the true ruler of the spirit plane and all others will fall before you. All you must do is take her hand.
