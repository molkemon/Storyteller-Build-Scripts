The Blood Curse and how to control it is central to the identity of all Ashhana lizardfolk. The main methods of control have solidified into two distinct factions, Canvas and Claw. Canvas relies on artful expression to control the curse; from intricate dances and expressive music to colourful painting and exquisite sculpture. While very effective for keeping control over the Blood Curse, however, Canvas is generally reserved for the elite. For commoners, Canvas will generally mean consuming rather than creating art. Claw, meanwhile, relies on more primal arts: war chants, bloodletting, and near indiscriminate violence, primarily against outsiders. The Claw faction is as such responsible for the more fearsome reputation of the Ashhana, and while Canvas regards this as distasteful, there is no denying that Claw is more effective for commoners for keeping the Blood Curse at bay.
