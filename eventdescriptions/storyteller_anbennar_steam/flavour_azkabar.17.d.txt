Whilst the Black Walls of Bulwar can do much to protect our nation from hostile outsiders, they cannot stop them all. Unwanted subversive elements can still infiltrate inwards, or danger can even rise from within. Naturally, measures must be taken to prevent this. The lord proposed the creation of the 'Bidyatu Network'. A massive series of obsidian prisons, dotted across Bulwar, in which the guilty and treasonous will be locked away for the glory of Surakel, and the safety of Bulwar. The prisoner population will swell with the guilty.
