Our efforts to tame the Moon Field have paid off. Where once there were empty plains and wandering tribes, now villages dot the plains, surrounded by fields bringing in a great harvest of grains and vegetables, and recently even orchards, cared for from sapling to mighty trees, have begun providing yields. Some of these villages have already started resembling towns, the proximity to a nearby realm and the Krahnorians providing opportunities of wealth to be made, and roads have sprung up connecting all of these new settlements. Thanks to our efforts, the Moon Field is now one of the most bountiful regions of the Ynn, and will likely only improve.
