Kobolds are known to be great diggers, no, not in the dwarven way, but more like tunnels that will give ants a run for their money. The intersection between kobolds and our people has caused tension in a specific province, with a major building collapsing due to the presence of kobold tunnels beneath them. Clearly this is not acceptable, but the kobolds respond that their tunnels are a necessity to provide housing for their evergrowing populace.
