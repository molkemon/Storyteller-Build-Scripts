In Elvendocks, nobles and aristocrats laugh over Lorentish wine in their floating mansions. In Newport, a thousand ships from a thousand realms unload their cargo, with vessels from Gerudia to Haless able to be sighted. Merchants hawk all the goods one could ever need from the corners of Dovemarket, promising unending happiness in exchange for a few crowns. Lecturers give lessons in everything from history to mathematics to the arcane in the twin academies. In the Bilge, the poor build cities within a city, extending deep into its winding depth. In the temple district, prayers ring out in a thousand voices to a thousand gods. Anbenncóst is old, vast, and faces many an issue. But it is, more so than any other city in the world, alive.
