The descendants of Asuru szalHamuranz rose to become the second family within the Akalate of Dartaxagerdim, serving as the right hand and staunch supporters of the royal family, on account of their shared kinship. The Szalmusar, as they were collectively known, were the leading religious family within the Akalate and assured the legitimacy of the House of szelFoFarramazIn addition, as hereditary lords of the sebhuliam Order, they commanded the absolute loyalty of the thousands of shock troops that made up the Akalate's Armies.
