Through penned treaties and whispered deals in backrooms, we have brought the rebellious estates to heel. They who once were so proud now prostrate themselves before the Crane Throne, and pledge their allegiance to the monarch. The Hwalbaek, clutching their Samdumae Talismans, are awestruck by our discipline and control over the nobility. The Merchants stand ready to assist in creating new trade partnerships with Yanshen, impressed by our deft economic maneuvering. The Neoksamaji believe Sado to be approving of our efforts and consider us worthy of honouring the Compact. Through wise and honeyed words, we have secured the restoration of Danggun. The future is uncertain still, but today, we stand together.
