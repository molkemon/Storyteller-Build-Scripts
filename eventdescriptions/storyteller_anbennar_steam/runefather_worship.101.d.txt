Eagerly expecting for the Runefather to contact the monarch once more, the ruler wandered down dark halls, desperately listening for any whispers and extinguishing torches just for the chance of the Runefather contacting them again. Many sleepless nights had passed, as the ruler began to lose faith. However, in the most unexpected time, many whispers suddenly filled their mind and darkness covered the room. "Sacrifice. Show no mercy, no failure, and no hesitation. Sacrifice one dear to you to demonstrate your willingness to follow my decrees, so that you may never hesitate ever again. With blood you will pay the price of power so high beyond your understanding. It is only the strong that can make a decision so unforgivable by your kin. Abandon your past self and devote all to the Runefather." As quickly as the whispers and darkness had appeared, so did they all fade away. With such a difficult decision, the ruler alone debated whether to sacrifice their kin or abandon such deranged actions.
