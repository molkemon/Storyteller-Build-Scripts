Generally, when the expeditioner reaches the treasure room, there is a sense of trepidation. after all, many of the relics that old Gor Vazumbrog locked up were locked away for good reason, carrying a terrible curse along with their boons. Not so today. this treasure room offers the comfortable warmth of the hearth, cleaning the air of the everpresent hint of miasma that the purple destroyer left in her wake. The weary delvers can think of nothing they would rather do than take a moment to warm themselves by the enchanted hearth, and then let it keep them company on the long journey back home.
