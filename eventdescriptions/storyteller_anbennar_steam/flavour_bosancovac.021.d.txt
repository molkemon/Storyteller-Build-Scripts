When a ship crests a wave on the mighty Ynn and comes into sight of our beloved Bosancovac, the first thing they see is the docks. Vast expanses of platforms and moors, rigging and enough rope to entangle a water serpent, and thus hold the many ships which flock to Bosancovac every single day to see the sights; here a visitor can meet the people and most importantly sell their many wares.
