Yusshela, the Celestial Dolphin, is a blessed and powerful Araizary, who has historically granted great boons and blessings to those deserving. Today, the monarch is going to become one of these fortunate individuals, as a reward for the continued reverence and worship of the Araizary in Khugra. The ruler enters the sea alone, as the constellation of the Celestial Dolphin shines brightly in the night sky in the moonless night. Suddenly a colossal form, shining softly, rises from the depths. The envoy of Yusshela is a whale made of transparent starlight, that begins swimming slowly in a circle around the monarch. Our ruler extends their hands to touch the blessed animal, and as they do so, the light emitted from its body becomes blinding. When they open their eyes the whale is gone, only the light of the evershining stars breaking the darkness. The monarch swims back to the shore, where royal advisors await eager to listen to their retelling of the experience. The ruler feels the power of the boon flowing through their body, and the changes in their personality are evident. They have received the Blessing of the Whale, a boon of great wisdom.
