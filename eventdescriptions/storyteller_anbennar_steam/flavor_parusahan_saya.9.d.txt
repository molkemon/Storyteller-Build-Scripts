With the corruption at the heart of the realm finally excised, the administration of Rahen should now be freed of the shackles of old, no longer serving greedy masters but instead acting on the will of the people. But parasites tend to burrow deep into their hosts, and do not wish to go quietly when discovered. Soon after he had taken the throne, Hemantsen discovered that multiple industries still work at the behest of corrupt masters, taking profits from the people and hoarding it for themselves. This blatant exploitation of the people and the realm was the exact reason Hemantsen rallied against the old ways. It cannot be allowed to fester any longer, lest the nation fall into turmoil soon after he departs from rulership. Rahen will be brought to heel; it is up to the cravens to choose if they will face the pen or the sword.
