The ruler stepped into the grove. The sun had just set, and shadows danced across the trees. Every slight shift in the darkness looked like movement. The wind ripped through the trees, knocking leaves onto the ground, and the ruler shivered. Except for the crunch of the leaves underfoot, it was deathly silent. The ruler stopped in the middle of the grove. "Alright. I’m here. Show yourself." One by one, figures stepped out of the darkness. Five, ten, twenty; they lost count quickly. They surrounded the ruler. One figure stepped forward. "Welcome, ruler, to our gathering. We are most pleased that you accepted our invitation. Now, to the business at hand." "What do you want?" The ruler asked. "It is a most simple request. We ask for you to surrender control of Eighard to the Cult of Rot. It is only through our assistance that you have accomplished anything. We are clearly better suited for control of the state than you, as I’m sure you’ll agree." "And what if I refuse?" The ruler asked, the shadows pressing against the ruler. The figure stepped closer. "Then you will not leave this grove alive, and we will seize control anyway. Would you prefer to do this the easy way or the hard way?"
