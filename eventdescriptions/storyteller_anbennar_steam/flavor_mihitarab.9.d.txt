In our most ancient myths, there were more members of the family than there are today. They were cast out for betraying their relatives, placing themselves ahead of the whole, and are thus known as They Who Would Abandon the Family. They whisper in the ears of mortals, causing all their selfishness. It has long been thought that no halfling would willingly follow these monsters, but we were wrong. Our investigators have found whole villages of dedicated cultists, all of whom seem to be planning for something. Some were even caught and have not been seen since. If the entire realm is to survive, these betrayers must be wiped out, whatever the cost may be.
