With the recent resettlement of Triunic peoples into their ancestral steppe homelands, there has been a movement to rediscover the old ways. Locally supported archeological digs are sprouting up around the new settlements, and many artifacts have been discovered. Most of these are the usual suspects. axe heads, pottershards, and old refuse pits that give insight into how the ancient Zabatlari lived. But there have also been religious artifacts, giving insight into how they worshipped. Amongst all this discovery, however, a strange phenomenon has emerged. settlers have begun hearing a strange voice in their dreams. The cause is unknown, and the reports are unusually consistent; needless to say, many academics have found it very interesting.
