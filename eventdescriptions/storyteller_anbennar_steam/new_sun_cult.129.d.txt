The Chosen are GodKings to be worshipped as Surael given earthly form. The Chosen are rulers by Surael's will and should actively fight the Darkness. The Chosen should lead the fight against the Darkness, with the assistance of the people. Surael only meant for the Chosen to guide the people on the path to the Light. The Chosen are too precious to stain themselves with the mundane task of ruling.
