Our lord seemed unusually late to return from their last hunting trip, but just as the courtesans began to worry, they appear from the horizon, and on a new ride. An antler horse of a massive size; far more than your usual war steed; is trotting along the route that leads to the capital, carrying the lord on its rugged back. Arriving to the capital’s gates to a row of impressed guards and frightened peasants, the ruler deftly descends along the creature’s side and bids it farewell for now, as the horse begins its walk towards a nearby grove; the gates and streets simply weren’t large enough for it either way.
