Finally, the council has decided upon a way to select a new monarch. Open to any dwarf brave enough to try, a great tournament is being held, with the crown of HulazKrakazol as the grand prize. Contestants have to down a whole keg of ale before every match, which they only win when their opponent vomits, loses consciousness, or dies. The tournament rages for several days, with the number of candidates dwindling every match. After the final fight, a blacksmith named Gorick Irongut is finally crowned as the new king, still partially covered in his last opponent's breakfast.
