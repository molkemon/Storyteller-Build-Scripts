The issue of the Corrupted Ruinborn is a divisive one. If the Corrupted really are forsaken by the Ynn, then how is it they survived the Ending Flood? But then if they are saved, what explains their wild nature? While the currents of faith might carry even the heaviest barge, the opposition might sink our hopes of resolving this issue if we maintain our chosen course. Is this the time to be pragmatic and lay our faith aside?
