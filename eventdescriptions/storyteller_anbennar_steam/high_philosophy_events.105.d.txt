"The Nitikamarja must inherently involve suffering. For to reach Purtara is to sever yourself from the bonds that tie you to this plane; those temporal needs must be broken… On bloody feet, with bloody hands, we walk the righteous sevenfold path. Dekhana, Pichka. All is suffering till that fateful moment of true freedom." Unknown Author, Brahmkalaa of Tianlou High Temple  The fighting has at last come to an end. The bloodstains marking the pristine white marble upon the High Temples will take years to clean out of the stone, and many families will never know peace again, but at the very least the worst is over. The nation can begin to recover in the light of a new day and face her challenges anew.
