The Bâlvazha and their inquisitors have brought forth a supposed apostate today who they claim does not worship the Runefather, instead worshipping different faiths of old. Numerous relics of dwarven kingdoms, orkish warbands, and goblin artifacts were found in his possession along with notes on their purpose. The apostate claims that he is merely a historian, collecting the history of the Serpentspine for future generations. Further inspection of his contraband seems to strengthen his point, there being no sign of any worship of the traitors who once exiled us, nor the monsters who took their place. Yet the Bâlvazha remain firm in their accusations, dismissing arguments to the contrary as misinterpretations. They insist that we live up to the ideals of the Runefather and execute him for his transgressions.
