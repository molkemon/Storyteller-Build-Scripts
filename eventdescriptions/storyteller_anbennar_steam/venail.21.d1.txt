Many years ago the nobility of Soarnaire made a deal with the elves in Venail, upon their leaving the island they would guarantee control went to the owner of Soarnaire. Thanks to our present control of Soarnaire we hold the rights to Venail, however it has been many years now and there is no evidence of the inhabitants having any intention of ever leaving the island. Fortunately a recent expedition has been made by Venaili explorers into the Bloodgroves, if we wish to we could use this opportunity to press our claim to Venail.
