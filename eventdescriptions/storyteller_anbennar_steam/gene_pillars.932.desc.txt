This large chamber glows faintly from the arcane symbols coating the walls, tracing conduits that have not been used in millennia. The concentration of symbols increases the closer to the center that you reach. In the center of the room is a large plinth, topped by a golden dome. As you near the dome, you can see a series of levers on the sides of the plinth that appear to be linked to certain parts of the arcane sigils. While every symbol emits a faint glow, the whole magical mechanism feels somehow inert, waiting. Time, or a deliberate shut down millennia ago, appears to have done its work and stopped the purpose of this space. Digging into the translation tomes you brought with you, you can determine this room was most likely a power source for the rest of the facility. You start to approach the plinth to determine if it is possible to start it again. As you reach the plinth you see several fractures in the coating of the dome, and through the gaps in the gold you can see a familiar blue sheen; dame's tar.
