Bands of harpies, using instability or antiharpy policies as a pretext, have begun attacking merchants and travellers on the roads and throughfares of a certain province. Even worse, rather than simply taking them away for their absurd and perverse rituals, they are slaughtering them en masse. One thing is clear. whether we appease them, send patrols, or do something more drastic, we must take decisive action before it spirals out of control.
