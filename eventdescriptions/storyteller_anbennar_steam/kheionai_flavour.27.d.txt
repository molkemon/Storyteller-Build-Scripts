Yet another messenger has arrived today from our Nékheis, delivering a firm denial of our recent policy decisions. This is yet another incident in a long line of refusals by our subordinate governors to cooperate with the administration from the Kheion itself. Even when they do acquiesce to our requests, they are fulfilled in such a manner that almost comes across as sabotage to our administrators. More often than not, though, the backlash is so obstructive it prevents any meaningful progress. It has become apparent to both administrations that we simply lack many of the bonds that once drew our Nékheis to prosper under our rule.
