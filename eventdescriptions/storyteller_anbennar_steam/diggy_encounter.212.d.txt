Despite our knowledge of these tunnels, the xorn come from a world full of stones, and they know quite well how to weaponize their surroundings, as well as their ability to shift through stones. The monster was infuriating, periodically ducking away from our weapons, vanishing into the floor just to reemerge and pick our soldiers off, one by one. Soon the ranks gave in and broke, sending our soldiers into a frenzied retreat.
