We have not reached the end, no, we stand at the beginning. Before us lies a long and wonderful road, one that stretches from the storied lands of the Hero Lords, winds along the sacred waters of the Kharunyana, twists beyond the shadow of mighty Mount Tughayasa, and spills forth into every distant corner of Haless. This is no ordinary chapter in our history. Upon us is an age where legends draw breath, where heroes are forged not just in battle, but in vision, in unity, in unshakable purpose. Progress marches beside us, not behind. At the vanguard of this new era stands a realm once thought the stuff of fairy tales, a land whispered in lullabies, dreamed of by children and old warriors alike. Here, a farmer’s truth holds weight equal to a prince’s decree. Here, the long-fractured North has been bound together, not by steel alone, but by shared hope. This is Shamakhad, a nation born of myth, tempered by struggle, and fated to endure. We do not walk toward greatness; we ride headlong into it.
