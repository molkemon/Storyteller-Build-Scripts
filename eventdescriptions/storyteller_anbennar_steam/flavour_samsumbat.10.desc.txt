Our heritage is toiling in the mines for our masters, cruel overlords with no regard for the loss of life in the dangerous pits and tunnels. Many children lost their parents far too early, and many mothers, their sons. No more. We do not forget what we learned from generations of digging, but this knowledge will be applied with a mind to the wellbeing of our brave miners, rather than purely for profit. New equipment, new procedures, new support for each other; we will all play our part to make Samsumbat both safe and prosperous.
