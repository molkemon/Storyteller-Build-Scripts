Aipar looks to his left, then to his right. Before him stand his fellow soldiers, his countrymen, and several noble families who just minutes before were all bitterly arguing who had the right to rule the new nation. Now, after having been eyed up and down by every prominent noble in the realm, a decision has been made. Aipar served well in the Great War for the Crown, known to our democratic brothers and sisters as "The Splintering". While he knows little of administration, the noble families all agree he will serve well as a temporary king, until a true royal ancient bloodline can be found to rule the domain of Sunijalla.
