Shamans: a destabilizing force to any society, but especially to that of the hobgoblins. Magicusers claiming power from the gods, son overthrowing father. Korashi is the method by which we keep our Shamans under control. A dark-colored, workable material, it eats magic as vigorously as its creators, the Oni. Without Korashi, the Command would topple and quickly. Since our conquests in Shamakhad and the associated influx of human Shamans, the reserves of Korashi have been stretched to their breaking point, and each day that passes our supplies dwindle. Effort must be taken to keep its levels high, for only by maintaining our supply of Korashi can we therefore maintain our conquests. For years, the main source of Korashi has been the Horned Ogres of Azjakuma, better known as the Oni. They now come to us with an interesting proposition, formalizing the longstanding trade that has existed between our peoples. Now that we are the dominant force in Northern Rahen, they fear, and not incorrectly, an eventual invasion. The Oni will increase shipments of Korashi to our capital in exchange for a truce, assuring them that we will not invade the Demon Hills they call home. It is up to Grand Marshal our lord if this "deal with the devils" is in the combined Commands' best interest.
