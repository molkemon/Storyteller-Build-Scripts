If it is better to die with honor than live in supplication, a chance at glory is better than the surety of shame and regret. Under this principle, we assembled a great army with the last coins of the treasury of our empire to go and retake what is ours. Despite the valiant efforts and overwhelming support of an entire empire, we appear to have failed in our task, and the soldiers who fight for us lose a bit of their righteous fervor every day. As their hopes of one day living in the world’s greatest empire wither, so do we; it was only through their hope and the last remnants of summoned strength that we have been able to fight on so long against such overwhelming odds. It appears as though it will be shame and regret for us after all.
