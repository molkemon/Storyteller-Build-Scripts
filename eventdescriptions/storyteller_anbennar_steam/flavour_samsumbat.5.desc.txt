The question of citizenship was another that threatened to weaken the foundation upon which our laws were built. When the realm was founded, gnolls were synonymous with slavers, lizardfolk were violent monsters from the jungle, and trolls were so rarely encountered as to be thought myth. No one had considered that our influence would expand to encompass these races, thus triggering another round of virulent arguments from different factions. Thankfully, reason prevailed in the end, though a few councillors nearly came to blows. With due consideration given to our diverse population and some lectures from some well-known philosophers, the decision for how to treat these so-called "monstrous races" was made.
