A recently received supposed gesture of diplomacy from the offending noble turned out to be nothing more than a blatant insult. In accordance with the rules of chivalry, the words spoken can be justification for a duel of honour, traditionally in the form of a joust. While it may be foolhardy to risk life and limb over the gabble of some arrogant ruler with a clear lack of etiquette, a lack of challenge on our part over this matter will be interpreted by many to mean the offending words were true.
