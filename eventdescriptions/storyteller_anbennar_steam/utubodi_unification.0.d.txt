As one of the vanishingly few safe havens shielded from Hinuifauna rampages, the Utubodi Plateau has been settled by humans and ogres, and, for the last couple of centuries, even by kobolds. The Plateau contains many settled tribes and states, but without question the most important of all is the realm that holds the remnants of the Kakunor Cloud City, the place of origin of all Skyfall Ogres inhabiting Insyaa, which crashed onto Insyaa on the Day of Ashen Skies. Although the true past of the Cloud City has been long forgotten, the people of the Utubodi Plateau struggle for control over this holy site in an attempt to prove their supremacy. Whomever manages to rebuild the city could unify all of the Plateau under their banner, and potentially venture past the Plateau to the lands beyond.
