The rise of a young Floodborn has been watched from all corners of society. Born into the royal family, they have been privileged and have conjured their own pride ever since. With the best tutors, they have been taught since a young age, driving increasing costs to their family as they continue to grow beyond their teachers at what seemed like an exponential rate, with no signs of stopping even now. Their mastery of multiple schools of magic grants them even greater access to the levers of the nation, as they seem more and more interested in one day ruling it. Despite not being in the direct line of succession, the Council has decreed that they shall be named heir for the moment, with the hope that some time shall temper their arrogance while keeping them ambitious for when they do take the throne and lead the nation to greater heights. The gods above watch and weep, knowing that with scions like this and more to come, the age of arcanum is here and that soon the entire aristocracy shall be as if they were walking Avatars, one and all. Just as this promising young mage shall succeed the great ruler, we shall succeed the gods themselves.
