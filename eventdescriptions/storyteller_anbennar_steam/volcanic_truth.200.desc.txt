The Volcanic Truth is decided. the conquest of the Taga'Ala is the mandate of the Twin Gods. They are worshippers of the Endless Ocean, but their folk traditions quail in the face of the Volcanic Truth, and we have gone there to teach them the Truth. Already, the holy orders of the faith are changing the world around them through force of will and devout zeal, and although the Taga'Ala are fearfully clinging to their old ways, they will soon acknowledge the superiority of the Volcanic Truth. Once they are reborn in fire, their mangroves will serve as the harbors from which yet more missionaries of the Volcanic Truth depart to spread the faith.
