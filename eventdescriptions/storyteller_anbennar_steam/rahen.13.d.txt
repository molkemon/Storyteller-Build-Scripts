The teachings of Guru Suhan have spread across the capital like wildfire, swiftly becoming the religion of the masses. While such a thing could be ignored as just a fad in another situation, many of our ministers have expressed support for Suhan’s teachings, with some even openly chanting Praxis mantras in court. It will be impossible to remain neutral in the growing sectarian divide currently splitting the realm in two – we will have to pick a side.
