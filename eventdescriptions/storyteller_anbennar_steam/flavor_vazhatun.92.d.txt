By now we are used to reports of astronomers struck by astral terror, and occasionally even their families and acquaintances. But the recent reports all talk about terror victims far out into the country, who've never even seen the Setzunkhupa or even DurVazhatun. A theory was put forth that all the rumors travelling around the country have inspired people to fake astral terror as pranks or ways to get attention, but according to our investigators these cases seem genuine. With our increasing failure to contain the everincreasing rumors, the astral terror was already close to an open secret, but with this, it seems the lid has finally blown completely off.
