We have laid the groundwork to establish a guild of merchants and officials to regulate and run the ivory and porcelain trades. However, there is one sticking point. Many of the merchants chafe at the idea of the ruler being so involved in the affairs of their businesses, and have proposed that we grant the guild a monopoly, where they pay us their expected income for eight years of ivory and porcelain trading every ten years, but have more control. We could also continue with our plans for The White Guild to fall under state control, and just directly gain the income over time. Regardless of our choice, The White Guild's control will help ensure that production increases, and they will work with us either way.
