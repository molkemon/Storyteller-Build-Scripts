We have endured. Since the days of Firanya we have ruled these hills by wing and talon. We have survived the Xhazobine, the Castanites, the conquests of Jaher and the fumbling expansions of his descendants. Yet our future is still not secured, and every day we must fight to ensure our ancient legacy is not eroded by upstarts. And we are alone. Yet, to our south, amidst the peaks of the Šad Sur, a new contender enters the stage of Bulwar; a gnoll matriarch by the name of Tluukt, who some call 'Cleaver of Realms'. Our policy has long been one of vitriol and distrust to the gnolls, but this rising pack near the very heart of Bulwar Proper represents an irresistible opportunity.
