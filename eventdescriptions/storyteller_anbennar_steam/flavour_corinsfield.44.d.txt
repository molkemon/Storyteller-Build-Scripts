The dwarf stands on the gallows, almost with an odd sense of pride. He is not scared, and the knowledge infuriates his executioners to no end. The crowd, made up of his fellow dwarves as well as a scattering of humans, nervously stands still. The humans were expecting a show; the crying, the pleading, the "please, no" that they are used to their brethren giving. He will not give it to them. And it will be his final act of defiance, and his executioners will remember it for as long as they live. With a smile and a nod, he accepts the rope as it is tied around his neck and tied under his beard.
