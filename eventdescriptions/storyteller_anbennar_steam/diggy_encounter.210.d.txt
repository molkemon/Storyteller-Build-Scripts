Strange sounds echo through the caverns, sounds of breaking stone and minerals hitting against each other, like a mill crushing them to pieces. As the first scouts rounded the corner, they could see that their first guess was not as far off the truth as expected. There, sitting among a small deposit of minerals, sat a xorn, a hideous scaled creature not of this world, shovelling rocks inside its wide maw with the three arms surrounding it. The scouts return with their observations, so that the expedition leader can decide how to approach this outsider. Seize its meal, or make use of their nature to shift through stone as easily as fish swim through water?
