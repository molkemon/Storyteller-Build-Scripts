Two letters lie side by side, both stained; the ink splodgy. The unity of the Odanchésta coalition is a fragile thing, and two Odanchésta have recently gotten into a conflict at sea over plunder rights. This is far from an uncommon occurrence, but in this instance both are aggrieved enough to write to the Matriarchy demanding something be done about the other. Divisions like this are ripe for exploitation, though it will require some work.
