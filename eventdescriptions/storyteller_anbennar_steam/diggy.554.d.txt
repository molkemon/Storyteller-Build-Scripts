The Dwarovrod in that province is in serious need of repair after several sections were damaged. a cave-in buried a set of switches and signals, a band of feral bugbears ripped up rails to smelt into weapons, and a small spur line has been nearly obliterated by a lava flow. Trains carrying cargo and passengers are backed up for miles, as they take turns using one track for both directions. Repairing the track will be expensive, but not doing so will cost even more in the long term.
