It is inconceivable. How can this be? Such are the thoughts of our monarch as they just barely manage to dodge a magical barrage from the other ruler. It is worse than being on the backfoot; the ruler is losing, and badly. A second blast strikes them in the side, sending them tumbling to the dirt. As they struggle to muster the strength to stand, the other ruler approaches, licking their lips as they prepare for the final blow. Before it can be delivered, however, the ruler's contingency activates, spiriting them away from the battlefield, no doubt leaving the other ruler blinking in surprise. This was never going to be a fair fight, yet even though the ruler has survived it, the other ruler will no doubt quickly capitalize on our retreat. Even now, many of our pack are no doubt questioning their loyalty to one who runs from the field. We must put Zokka's pack to death and restore our glory before our armies lose faith in the ruler entirely.
