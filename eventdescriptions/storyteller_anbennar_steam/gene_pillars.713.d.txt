An uncommon haven lies before your party. Light filters into the room through some unseen source, and settles atop a brown pool before you. You take a step forward, then like the flash of drawn steel, a silver bolt escapes the water. Just as you prepare to parry, the bolt spreads its wings, then wheels around you before landing back in the pool. Soon, dozens of the creatures whirl through the space, and disappear through a tight shaft into another room. No sooner has the flying fish flock taken off than does it disappear, leaving you, your party, and the serenity of this hidden alcove.
