The soldiers crowded around the Palace of Stanyrhrada. The Amber Princess stood there, in the courtyard, staring up at the Golden Dome. It shimmered in the soft sunlight, its magic so potent it could be seen by the naked eye. The Amber Princess lifted her arms and closed her eyes, and although nothing visibly happened, the assembled soldiers felt the air shift. A few of them stepped back in fear. The Amber Princess’ hair began to blow in a breeze that none of the soldiers could feel, and the Golden Dome began to pulse. The Amber Princess’ chest began to rise and fall, and some of the soldiers realized they had never seen her breathe before. Faster the wind blew, and faster she breathed, and faster the Dome pulsed, until the soldiers were sure that either she or the Dome were going to break. Then there was a great crack, and the Amber Princess shuddered, and a wave of energy washed over the soldiers, causing some of them to stumble. Looking up at the Golden Dome, they saw that it no longer shimmered in the soft sunlight.
