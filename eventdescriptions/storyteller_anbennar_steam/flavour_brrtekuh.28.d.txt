The situation in the Brrtekuh Council was a mess, with dozens of personal feuds, political ideologies fighting among each other, bribery, jealousy, and pure pride mired in the Council. The solution was to lock the entire Council inside a building, with guards watching every entrance, and only basic food and water allowed inside. The councillors could only leave when they all agreed and solved their problems. At the start, screams and violence could be heard from the building. As time passed, monotony and apathy settled in. Seeing no other way out, the Council started to talk among themselves, solving the feuds, smoothing the ideologies, and connecting. What came out of the building wasn't anyone's preferred scenario, but it was the one they could all agree on.
