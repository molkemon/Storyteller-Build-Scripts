From the Hetenapuzo at the high temple province. Recently discussed individual Suresh of the Stalking Way was sighted near the camp at the high temple province. He has proven elusive thus far, but he is definitely still near the camp unless he has left. Close investigation of camp records and interrogation of penitents shows that one of the individuals here was previously close to him, and another is his son, a campchild. One alarming fact. the boy has shown significant talent in the necromantic arts. The mother, one Kunya of Clan Wolfheart, is a simple pyromancer, so Suresh himself is likely the source of the inheritance. Recommend raising the bounty and reinforcing our camp location with formal troops. For the time being, as camp warden, I have taken the child under close supervision. He will be treated honorably and given the chance to grow as a hobgoblin would. I have even taken the liberty of giving him a proper hobgoblin name. Zoburu. The reason for this is simple. he is both too valuable as bait to kill, and too important and potentially powerful to let remain just another campchild. Should his father make a move, I would prefer him not to be easily swayed.
