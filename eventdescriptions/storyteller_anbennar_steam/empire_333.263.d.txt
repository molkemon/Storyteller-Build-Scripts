There may not be much land on the planet of Shagatan, but that has not stopped the colonists from constructing a giant harbor on the island from which to set sail. Every tiny island on the aquatic world has been visited, with small local harbors constructed. The real work is being done underwater, however, where construction has begun on a bubble city that they could potentially move into. Life on the sea has become such a norm here that many young marines now move to Shagatan to begin their training. The next generation of soldiers are just as home on the sea as on land.
