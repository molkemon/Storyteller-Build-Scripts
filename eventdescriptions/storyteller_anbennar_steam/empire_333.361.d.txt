When the lone Gasabi at court started to scream, the monarch shuddered. With everything that had happened since the venture onto that cursed planet, it was hard not to wonder if the doom that ‘cursed’ mutation had long been feared to bring had finally arrived. The shock quickly faded, though, along with any fear it sparked. Faded, and was replaced by fascination; for it was not panic or terror driving the blackscaled lizardman to scream. It was not even clear he had a choice in the matter at all. After all, the scream was made up of words. Strange words, spoken in a rush, punctuated by even louder screams; but words all the same. At this point, the poor Gasabi’s voice gave out. Some advisors are quick to assure the ruler that must have been some strange manifestation of their curse. But the realm knows better. The monarch has fought those clicking creatures on the battlefield. We have won, and more often, we have lost. But we have never had a chance to truly know their foe; until the glimpse we got today. And if a thousand thousand years proved anything, what we know, we can understand.
