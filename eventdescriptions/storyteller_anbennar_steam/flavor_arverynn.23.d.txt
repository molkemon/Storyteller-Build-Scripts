After ascending the artificial mound of Munkarel and exploiting a breach in the outer walls, we stand in front of the columns and debris-laden entryway of the old Moon Temple, known to the Veykodans as Setirdeyel. The Veykodan Buycev have retreated deeper into the temple, outnumbered by our troops and our own Veykodan mercenaries. Cautiously, our mages disarm the runeladen paper sheets the Veykodans have left along the way, and a magical staff banishes the darkness from the temple's inner hallways, highlighting a beautiful mosaic of the Sun, the Moon, and the Ynn stretching through recurved walls. Only one way leads further down, a dusty wooden trapdoor in a corner by the end of the hallway. Should we ignore it and block it, the raiders will surely starve in the underground levels of the mound temple, turning on each other like Wendigos or withering as they desperately claw for a way out. However, one can only wonder what secrets lie beneath the temple, whether treasure, curses, or threats to this land that must not be allowed to grow a minute longer.
