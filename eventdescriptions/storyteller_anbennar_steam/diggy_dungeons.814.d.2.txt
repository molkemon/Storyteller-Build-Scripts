Upon further inspection, a pathway revealed itself behind the waterfall, leading around it towards a small cave filled with crates and chests. This immediately got their senses for treasure tingling, and without much ado, the crates and chests were opened. Much to their dismay, however, they were all empty, all except one which housed the skeletal remains of a rat. After the initial euphoria of the promise of loot, this result was not well received by the expedition, and with low spirits, they continued their journey.
