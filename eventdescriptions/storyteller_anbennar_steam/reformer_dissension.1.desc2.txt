With the last great Ravelian Debate finished, the Ravelian Church has finally established itself as a cut above most faiths of the world. Theirs is a faith that is for all, perhaps like The Jadd, but driven by a respectful mix of reason and spirituality. For a long time the Ravelians in our realm have watched as the followers of Adean and Corin bicker over the Regent Throne, what the Ravelians see, in the grand scheme of things, as a trivial affair. They have become tired seeing their religion treated as a secondary faith, but now their universalist teachings have finally entrenched themselves as the third spoke in the faiths of the Cannorian Pantheon that has dominated Anbennar since its existence. Now, with the Ravelian emperor on the Dove Throne, it seems inevitable that the Ravelian faith will become the new official faith of the Empire.
