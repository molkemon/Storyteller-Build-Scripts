Great news my liege. A surveying expedition has found a large reservoir of aquamarine gemstones in this realm. It seems that everyone wants to get a chance at claiming mining rights for the minerals. Aquamarine holds significance amongst the Baashidi both as a symbol of status and a ward of protection and longevity in traditional folklore. Many nobles in the Jasiir Jadid display aquamarine frequently in their clothes to display their wealth. It all ties back to the Exodus of Ardimya, during her journey to the Jasiir Doog. It was said that she left a trail of bright blue stones so that the thousands that followed her need not worry about getting lost. My liege, how shall we approach this situation?
