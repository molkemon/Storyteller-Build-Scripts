Our developing embassy has received a most auspicious visitor. The Unthroned Queen, sovereign of the domain, archfey, and patron of our realm, has appeared to grace us with her presence. Yet she does not simply come to view the embassy, as she has made clear. Indeed, she has asked pointedly as to our progress on unifying the territory under our rule. It is by her mandate that we are sworn to uphold the seasonal balance, and by her grace that we have the right to all of the territory. But that does not mean we haven't had to struggle to assert that authority. With the attention of her majesty on us so directly, we may expect some boon if our progress pleases her. That being said, if we have been wanting in our goal, her appearance is sure to motivate our realm at every level to renew their efforts. What do we have to tell her?
