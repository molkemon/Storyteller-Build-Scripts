The woods to our east have been united under the Corintar's banner, and the lands restored to prosperity. With a new community of orks from across Escann, the Green ork culture has been experiencing a renaissance of its own. Every year, tens of thousands of Corintari citizens flock to regional festivals in honor of Corin and great ork heroes. With an open flow of Corintari orks moving to and from the restored enclave, Green ork culture is flourishing across our lands.
