O Horutep, Ice Lord. The Gerudians of the north sing great tales to keep the frozen horrors at bay. They roam their winter wastes and drink their mead while dragons slumber, and refuse to admit they all will surely die. Their tales are old and creaking, and their leaders are weak. You rule the northern wastes, and in your benevolence, a new fate for the skalds of Gerudia is possible. let them sing a new song. Let them sing of you, and let them be sheltered by you.
