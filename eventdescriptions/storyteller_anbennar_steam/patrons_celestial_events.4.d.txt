The sun is high in the sky when the monarch settles in one of the private chambers of the royal palace. The monarch ritually cleanses themself with the water from a golden washbasin and changes into white silken robes. Silence settles in the room as the monarch kneels in prayer. The monarch remains like this for an hour, before rising and lighting a brazier in the centre of the room. "My Lord, I pledge to you that I will."
