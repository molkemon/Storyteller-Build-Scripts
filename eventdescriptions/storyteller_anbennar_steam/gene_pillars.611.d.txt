As the party ventures, they hear a growl from the shadows. Preparing for another beast to emerge, instead they are greeted by the sight of a two-legged creature. It resembles the orange furry beast the party encountered earlier, but this one walks on two legs. And it is misshapen and horrid. It moves with an unnatural gait, unable to fully control its muscles. Its body pulses in random spots, as if about to burst. Its eyes, glowing with energy, glare at the party. Facing the party, the terrible beast roars with anger and pain.
