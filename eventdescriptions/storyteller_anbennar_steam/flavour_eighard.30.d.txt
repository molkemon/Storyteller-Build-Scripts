The elves and their allies retreat before us. Fueled by the desperation of the archfey, they poured out of the trees, seeking to delay their inevitable fate. But they have failed. We have won. Even their combined might, strengthened by the blessings of the archfey, could not resist the glory of the Tuathak, the glory of the Amber Princess. Within these verdant forests, autumn now reigns supreme. But where is the Amber Princess? She has not been seen since the start of this war. The archfey must still live, somewhere. Regardless, we can, for now, go back home.
