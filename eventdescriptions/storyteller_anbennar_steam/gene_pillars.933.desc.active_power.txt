The room ahead glows brightly now that the generator has been restored. Waves of power emanate from the artifact in the center of the far wall. While it does not seem harmful initially, some notes found around the room point to the purpose of this machinery: control of other beings, specifically those who are draconic. The notes go into great depth into how the device works and why the creators devised it, but no explanation as to why it was left active in a supposedly empty facility. One colleague posits that the energy drain of the device may be why the power source eventually failed. It is supposedly able to emit a frequency that will cause pain in them while active, and through repeated exposure it will assist in the control and training of them.
