The doctors told me not to, but the midwife warned of danger. Yet all my life I had wanted to see my baby in a manger. So I pushed past their objections, stepped into that old dance, faced my gentle friend, and fell into a trance. Months it took, long after magic is still subject to time. When I gazed down at my daughter, I heard a gentle chime. I wept for so long after she looked just like my gone lovers. My little darling child, my heart finally recovers.
