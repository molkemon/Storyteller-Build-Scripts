As territorial control has waned due to our infighting, agitators for independence have come to the conclusion there is no better time to push to expand their powers than now. It is easy to surmise why: with our society still reeling from the desertion of so many, enormous vacuums exist in our leadership. Perhaps worse still, what limited martial resources we have preserved currently find themselves occupied in our efforts to quell this uprising. Consequently, the representatives for liberation efforts have found the temerity to approach us with an ultimatum: cede control to them, or else.
