Nerat is the Judge of the Dead and Warden of Hell. Our duty as his servants is to take action to destroy those who defile life itself, and unlike the corrupted leadership of the Necropolis our order did not forget that oath.  The ruinborn elves of this socalled Reaper's Coast worship an evil god of death they call 'Cheshosh', and those Cheshoshi built a powerful kingdom here, centered around a massive complex they call the Bone Citadel. It is a grim monument of death and heresy where their priests wield deathly magics and torture the souls of those who displease them. It is antithetical to everything Nerat stands for. It must be destroyed, lest they rip open the gates of the Fugue Plane and upset the very natural order.  Our clerics and paladins shall march on the Citadel and let them know Nerat's righteous fury.
