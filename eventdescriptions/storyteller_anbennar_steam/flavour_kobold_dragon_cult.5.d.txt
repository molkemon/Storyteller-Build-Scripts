Imagine this. Insyaa is out. You are playing Kobildzan. You are pushing past the great Holohana beasts which tower over the landscape. You are managing to find your way into the great mountain ranges, which loom over the plateaus and valleys beneath. You are ignoring the Gene Towers of the Precursors, and you are colonizing into the depths of Insyaa. In the middle of it all, you find kobold mountaintops and kobold citadels. Once Insyaa is out, you will be interacting with these grand citadels. Kobolds rule from the heights here, and they have a crazy control over the elements. They are not artificers; they are mages, but they create vast tunnels and railways of earthmagic and evocation, allowing them to dominate the heights and support their civilization. Cool, right? It will be so fun once Insyaa is out. You will conquer all of them as you ensure that you are the home for all kobolds. Until then, though, well, until then, you just get this. Sorry.
