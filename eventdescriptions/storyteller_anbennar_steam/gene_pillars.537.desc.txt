We finally manage to pry open the door to the room we expect will give us access to the top levels of the tower, only to be met by an impenetrable wall of wood. An endless morass of stems, roots, and branches blocks the entrance to the room. As we start to cut away at them, we realize the blockage is not just at the entrance. The entire room appears to be completely overgrown by these strange and virulent trees. What could have caused this out-of-control growth?
