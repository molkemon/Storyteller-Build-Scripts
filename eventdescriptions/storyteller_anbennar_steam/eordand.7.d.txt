On a dark evening with clear sky and bright moon, you survey the capital. For years now the ruins of Cadcimn unearthed miracles that have enriched your city in Eordand, and you eagerly anticipate further news from the expedition. You feel a tug in your mind, a hunch, and turn around. Behind you is a shadowy figure, contrasted against the magical lights bathing your balcony. Clutched in its tenebrous hands you see a small but wickedly shaped dagger.  "Are you prepared to answer for your crimes?," a graven, solemn voice utters. "What was buried was meant to be forgotten. What has started cannot be undone, but who is responsbile may die."
