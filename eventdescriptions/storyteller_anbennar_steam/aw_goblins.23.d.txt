Adventurers take on countless quests, from exploring ancient ruins to unraveling mysterious spells. The bards would have you believe that no two quests are alike, but they miss the one quest that almost every adventurer has taken on at some point or the other. killing goblins. It is thus no surprise that the recent foray into the goblin lair in the realm went smoothly. The lessening of their numbers should be a welcome relief to the locals.
