All magic is said to have come from God one way or another, and as such magic is divine. For some this has been used to promote artificery for the benefit of the masses, but for others, especially those gifted with magic, it has allowed an aura of allure and gravitas that equals the Age of WitchKings. One such individual has amassed a large following in our lands, calling themselves a Living Saint and using their powers to, admittedly, do good works. A Rectorate Inquisitor has arrived in our domain, and advises that we act against this heretic, for this is a dangerous precedent to allow mages parade themselves as if they were gods.
