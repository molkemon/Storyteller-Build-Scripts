The Serpentspine has always been contested by various races. The dwarves have sought to reclaim their ancient homeland for thousands of years, but this was nothing more than a remote dream until the Greentide brought the overwhelming majority of the orks and goblins out of the mountains. Into the power vacuum left behind flowed the reclaimers; remnant holds as well as adventurers from the surface struck out to retake their ancestral halls. But for all the dwarven claims to rightful ownership, the goblins and orks have inhabited the roads and holds of the mountains in the dwarves' absence, and have no lack of a claim to homeland. As such, conflicting claims over various pieces of land have caused constant crises since the reclamation of the Dwarovar. Now, in a specific province, such a dispute is threatening to boil over into open conflict. Two different clans are asserting that they are the rightful owners of valuable land, rich in minerals and containing many old structures that date to the time of Aul Dwarov. Both are refusing to yield in their rights of property and have brought dozens of pieces of documentation and oral testimony to the courts to back their claims. As the court case rages, tensions rise and angry words threaten to erupt into blows. Knowing the matter to be sensitive, the courts have turned to the central government and ask us to resolve the matter, as they do not seem to have a ready answer to the question.
