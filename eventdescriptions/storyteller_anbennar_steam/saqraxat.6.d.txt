With the reconquest of parts of the Middle Sorrow, Saqraxati slave patrols have become a common sight along the river. Riverboats pass each other going both up and downstream, conveying both patrols and their misbegotten goods to their destinations. Their activity has of course been quite disruptive towards the local gnollish populace, and some of these unruly dogs have started stirring up the packs to revolt.
