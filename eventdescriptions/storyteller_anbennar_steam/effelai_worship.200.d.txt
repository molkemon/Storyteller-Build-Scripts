It has been a long and arduous journey, one that has taken us through every equally horrifying and disparate region of the jungle. We've witnessed the full spectrum of this land's offerings, from the darkest evil to the most bountiful beneficence. Through it all, we persevered, united in our quest to find a new home for our people. In these lands, where disease and death loomed, we will bring forth new life. Where shadowy forests and lurking monsters once held sway, the laughter of children will soon drown out those former horrors. Amidst the overgrown ruins, long forgotten by time, we shall raise a garden that will endure, a symbol of our resilience.  Preparations have already begun, and the survivors of our grueling trek have already taken well to the environs. Trees are felled to provide materials for shelter, tents are repurposed into homes, and sturdy palisades rise from the earth. The energy with which we work is fueled by the hope of a people who dream of building something of enduring worth, something that justifies every agonizing step we took to reach this most hallowed place. We are driven by the belief that the trials and tribulations of our migration were not in vain, and that our new home will be a testament to the strength and unity of our people, a place where generations to come will thrive.
