As we take our first steps into the deepest sections of the Sandworm Gene Pillar we spot some movement and ready ourselves to fend off something dangerous. To our relief it seems this latest encounter does not wish us any harm. Before us is a heavily damaged mechanim of a rather peculiar make. The mechanim seems to have been made in the image of a Dung Beetle, and pushes a large mechanical ball with hundreds of strange attachments around. To our astonishment the mechanim pulls out a number of the strange attachments and swiftly fixes a number of components in a broken door, and without much effort the door opens. This individual could be of paramount importance. They seem to be heading somewhere. We should best follow them.
