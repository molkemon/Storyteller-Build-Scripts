We did it. The mechanim is finally trying to communicate back to us, and we seem to be getting closer to some sort of understanding. As far as we have managed to gather, this mechanim has had ready access to a power source for millennia and has been active for all that time, but has fallen into an automated stupor during that enormous time span, leaving it nearly catatonic despite being otherwise fully active. They seem very excited to finally have someone to converse with and are freely sharing information with us. Apparently, it is a specially built mechanim meant to help take care of and repair systems within the pillar. Its large mechanical ball contains tools that are designed to interface with and handle the different components of the tower. Now we just need to convince them to help us.
