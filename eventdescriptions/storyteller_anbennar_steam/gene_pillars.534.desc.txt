As we are making our way higher up the tower, we are suddenly attacked by a spear; it pierces through the wall and almost takes one of us out. Withdrawing at astonishing speed, it slams back in. Again and again the spear punctures the wall; we start to sprint, but soon we are met with a vertical wall, one that will take some time to climb. The spear reappears, jutting again and again through the wall of the tower. Then it stops, and as it does we see a single large eye stare in through the hole. A large woodpecker has chosen us as its next meal.
