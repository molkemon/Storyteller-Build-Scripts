Bordu, who would later in life be known as Bordu Crownshatterer, was an ork who was part of the horde which took Amldihr from the dwarves. He is said to have taken the Dwarovkron from a dwarven king himself. Legend has it he used all his strength to bend the dwarven crown to fit on his own head, shattering bits off in the process. That day he became Bordu Crownshatterer, father of the Shattered Crown clan, chief of Amldihr. He spent most his life unifying the vast hold of Amldihr under his one clan and bringing structure to a otherwise chaotic society. The Songs of him speak of the many scars he got and friends he lost trying to unify his hold, something we could all learn from.
