Our servitude under Bianfang was always one marked by desperation. Caiqi I’s voluntary submission succeeded in safeguarding us from a mightier adversary, but we now face a new foe. The once formidable Dragon of the Yanhe now wanes in power after the loss of its heart, so why must we remain in its shadow? A representative of the Peacocks has been dispatched to their new capital, conveying our intent to depart from their dominion.
