Titanic bones line the walls of the cavernous room the expedition now finds itself in, shelves of giant slabs of mineralized tissue piled against each other as the party picks its way through the rapidly shrinking available floor space. Stretching into the open air, jawbones, ribs, femurs, skulls, and strange bones never seen in any other animal are moved aside as the explorers tirelessly dig through to the other side of the room. Curious, that a creature could contain such giant bones without collapsing in on itself. The very idea of something living with such enormous bones within its body sends a shiver down the spine of the adventurers present. Something about this place is off, and we cannot set it right.
