With the expansion of the Adasa Guard over the years, it is only sensible for others to take notice and use this vast pool of trained and armed manpower to their needs. As such, the Porcelain Guild of the Kharunyana has offered sums of coin to procure them as guards for their cities. Not only will this be a tidy profit, it will undoubtedly give us influence over the Porceilin Cities.
