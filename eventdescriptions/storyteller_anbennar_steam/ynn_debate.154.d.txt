A proclamation has come down from their realm, declaring that the use of firearms is sanctioned by the faith. Weapons are weapons, regardless of what shape they take, and the only difference in honor between them is how they are used. Swords can be used in shameful ways, while muskets can be used in virtuous ways. So long as we preserve our social order and maintain our righteousness, we will remain blessed by the faith no matter what weapons we defend it with.
