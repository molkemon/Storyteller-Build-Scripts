The privateer captain has made quite a name for himself in the service of the navy. The payment he has been receiving for his prizes, however, is much less than the full value of the prizes themselves. Like many savvy privateers, he has realized that he could make a greater profit by turning pirate. He has tossed his Letter of Marque overboard and made port in the capital. As a pirate of the realm, he will be free to plunder to his heart’s content and keep the spoils he has rightfully stolen.
