Despite fierce resistance, the ruler is no match for the experienced vampire hunter. Although they fought valiantly, they were soon overwhelmed by the hunter. The last thing they see as their eyes slowly close is the hunter smashing the coffin, the ruler’s saving grace, into pieces. The ruler lets out a final, shaking breath, knowing that they will not be coming back. Despite their attempts, the ruler has come to know the embrace of death. Perhaps, at last, they will find the peace they never did in life.
