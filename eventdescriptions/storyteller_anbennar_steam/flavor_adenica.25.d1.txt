With a grunt, our ruler throws off their robes, calling for attendants to grab their gear. "I will not stand idly by as the honour of Adenica gets insulted," our ruler says as the stunned onlookers stand with mouths agape. Emotions flit across the knight's face; shock, confusion, even fear; before it twists into eagerness. "So, I defeat a lord today," he boldly states. "Let us prepare, then." Clad in the royal colors of white and blue, our ruler warily surveys their opponent. The handsome knight stood proudly, with the swirl of the Lorentish rose prominent upon their gaudy chestpiece and a massive plume upon their helmet. "That stupid rose will serve as a good target at least," our ruler mutters under their breath. Despite the ostentatious display by the knight, they are clearly quite practiced in the saddle, wielding their long lance expertly as they wheel their horse around the grounds. The shouts of the royal and Lorentish parties became a deafening roar as the lord and knight ride toward each other at full charge. Our ruler’s lance barely scrapes the side of the knight’s plate as their horse turns away at the last second, while the exchange leaves our ruler with a large dent in their armor. Sensing weakness, the knight prepares their second charge quickly, speeding up and almost knocking our ruler off their horse with a fullbodied blow right at the location of their first strike. As the knight charges past, however, our ruler steadies their steed and hits the knight with a surgical attack at the apex of their turn, knocking both horse and rider off balance. Our ruler launches a flurry of attacks, aimed at preventing the knight from recovering. Weakened by the two strong hits the knight had already scored, however, our ruler’s blows are parried away easily. The opponent slips out of grasp, readying a final assault. This one knocks our ruler off their horse. Throwing their helmet to the ground and with a final, pitying look downward at our ruler, the knight raises their lance high in triumph, to the jeers of a hostile crowd.
