After the collapse of the Command, the Hobgoblins lost much of their purpose, once again isolating themselves as familial clusters. Their isolationism has been troublesome, and it has been difficult for the nation to integrate hobgoblin cultures into its society. However, recent efforts have led to breakthroughs, and a new Hobgoblin culture is emerging. Calling themselves the Fenghuang, they are much closer to the nation's own society, and, while their ultimate loyalties still rest with their families, they have seen the glory of the dawn.
