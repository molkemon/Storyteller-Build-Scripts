Negeal? Already? Caehn Vranulk has given us autonomy just a few years ago. This cannot stand. The monarch anticipated this response. He looked at the Sochar people, his subjects, with an indifferent expression. "It is true. He did give you autonomy over Zurenkhiin. However, the circumstances have changed." "My caehn, we built the caehnate of Great Storm together with the first Windbearer. Mostly because we believed him, in him. That we could create a caehnate that was capable of excising the stain upon our kind that are the Filustkhuga. But also one, where the clans would not be subsumed into one another." "Well, you need to look at it from a more practical point of view." An old shaman attempts to interject on behalf of his lordship, but is cut off by the monarch. "I am giving you the choice of either accepting Negeal, or we will replace your ordsiwyn with ones that will."
