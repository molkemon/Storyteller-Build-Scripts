A new room, it was difficult to keep track at times. But the expedition was certain they had never been here. That’s because inside the room were hundreds of dormant domestic mechanim. They are all identical, the same body, the same face. They fill the entire room front to back, all lined up in neat rows. Unable to help themselves, apparently their host chimed in once more. "They were stored here, the chainers thought there would be a massive demand for slaves in their homeland. I’ve never seen their homeland; they came and went with portals, but from what I heard it sounded like they already had enough slaves. Obviously that never happened; they became paranoid, and these mechanim were left in storage forever. I hate that you’ve come down here, but I consider myself lucky; it could have been others. I’m aware that you may perceive us like the chainers did, as nothing more than malfunctioning machines, but we’re more than that. They are more than that. I hate prostrating myself like this, but I beg you. Please don’t hurt them; don’t make them slaves."
