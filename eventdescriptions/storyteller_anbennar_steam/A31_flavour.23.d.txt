While none have ever doubted our devotion, we have always had a different relationship with divinity than most. The founders of our republic knew Munas as a mortal elf, albeit one of uncommon vision and skill. The philosophers and intellectuals that fill the tea and coffee houses of our nation have long since discussed this distinction, including many who held membership in the Ravelains. Now, these elves of distinction come forward with new and impressive arguments. They claim Munas the elf was but the chosen saint of the one true god, since rediscovered by our elven cousins in Aelantier.  This discovery, divined from the Ravelian God Fragment has swept through our cities swiftly, and the influential urbanites call on us to commit to this new faith, whatever it should mean for our future.
