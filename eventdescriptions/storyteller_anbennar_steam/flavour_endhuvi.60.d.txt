As wild spirits emerge across the realm, the people cry out for the protection of the great Kharunyana. Unlike other great spirits who visit their wrath on the people in their lands, the great white fish has shown its willingness to protect them. Reports have reached the capital of the sight of many wild spirits being driven away by the avatar of Kharunyana. Lands that were overrun as the Rending began have been wiped clean as Kharunyana protects its own.
