The relationship between Yingzhen and our own state has always been something of an oddity; what was once a meager tributary clinging to us for protection has become a power in Yanshen; and their star only continues to rise. Many of our kin have already migrated to their lands; a movement not only tolerated, but celebrated by the locals. It cannot be denied that our relationship with their state is approaching that of equals rather than of a subject and her master.  The matter has some to a head, at long last. An envoy from Yingzhen has arrived; his entourage including even some of our kin; to discuss an adjustment to the relationship of our states. One that would see us grow closer as partners. An alliance.
