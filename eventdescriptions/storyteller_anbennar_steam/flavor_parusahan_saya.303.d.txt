The search for the throne has culminated in our final obstacle, Sarisung, heart of Rahen. Filled with an uncountable number of souls, a city this large should surely contain the throne of Harimar. But desperation clouds the minds of the scholars as they begin their search. They know they have reached the end of the line, and past this point there is nowhere left to go. The survey corps scatters throughout the city streets, winding through the slums like rats in a maze. The desperate attempt to find the throne is about to come to a close, but the odds of locating our prize grow smaller with each passing day. Do we even have anything left to hope for?
