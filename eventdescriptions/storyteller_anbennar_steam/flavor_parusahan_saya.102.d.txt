For three months the specter of uncertainty has haunted the realm, filling the heads of our advisors with doubt and fear. If we continue on this path, the only thing awaiting us is a long slump into eventual destruction. This cannot come to pass; we will not allow it. While the tales of the past were mostly kept within the nobility, records of the first Harimraj must surely have survived in one of the many archives and libraries found throughout the domain. If we have to dig up the entire continent to find the key to our salvation, we will do so. This is more than a simple realization of our purpose, but the binding force which joins our state together, and as such we must find it no matter the cost. Scholars, nobles, ministers and clergy are dispatched far and wide, each given a single task: find the history, return to the capital, and save us all from the clutches of damnation.
