As trading voyages led by Cannorian halflings have become a familiar sight on our shores, the language barrier has become more and more of an issue. The language they speak, which they call "common", has no relation to our own, making it difficult for a Vyzemby to learn it with any degree of fluency. One Cannorian, however, has a solution. They generously gifted the realm with a schematic of a device they called a "printing press". They explained that this allowed for the mass production of books, which until now were far too expensive for common use. The local burghers of the capital immediately saw the potential in this "printing press" and have begun producing copies of a VyzembyCommon translation guide.
