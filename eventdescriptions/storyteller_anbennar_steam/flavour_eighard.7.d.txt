The ruler had been drawn here, for reasons they did not entirely understand. They walked. And walked. And walked. The trees seemed to bend and twist, enveloping, almost trapping them. The wind rustled through the branches, a high hiss that sent shivers down their spine. After what seemed like an eternity, the ruler reached what appeared to be the center of the forest, where a great ruin lay decrepit. They laid their hand on the ancient stone. Nobody knew what this place was for, or when it became ruined. Perhaps it had always been ruined. A soft whisper came, almost imperceptible. "Hello? Who’s there?" "Come, sit, stay for a while…" the whispers continued. "We have much to teach you…" The ruler spun around, searching for the source of the voice, to no avail. "Wherever you are, come out.
