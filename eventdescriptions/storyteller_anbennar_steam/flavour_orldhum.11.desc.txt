The icy hold of Krakdhûmvror and the mountains it resides in show evidence of a past foe much greater than the greenskins of today. the Giants. Beings of yore whose stature rivaled the peaks of our own mountains. Though long past, their lesser Troll kin are an ever growing nuisance in our expansion into the Northern Pass. The Trolls' tall stature and regenerative capabilities make them formidable fighters and potentially great laborers having little need for medical aid. Shall we properly civilize them or remove these beasts once and for all?
