Brother of Dagrin and made from a different stone by Halanna, Azmirethazin is the patron of all smiths and metalworkers. He is also known to dispense his sister Anvelind's justice, wielding his mighty forging hammer, Mae'nalenlier. His crafts are known to be unparalleled, although some say that the items crafted by his grandnephew, Urisazirn, rival or even surpass his quality.
