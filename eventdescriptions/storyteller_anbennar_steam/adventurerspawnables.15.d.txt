Succession in Harpy society was often fickle and argumentative. As the next Matriarch was chosen from a pool of worthy candidates, being cut out of the running was common and often resulted in bloody consequences. Such an occasion had occurred, as Princess Ašuria Ayarakin, once favoured heir of the matriarch, was barred from the succession entirely. Enraged at having her birthright stolen, Ašuria and her allies attempted a coup against the ruling monarch. The coup was a catastrophic failure, with many of her allies falling in battle, fleeing into exile, or having outright betrayed her during the chaos. She was barely able to escape alongside some of her closest remaining confidants. They fled to Aqatbar, dubbing themselves the ‘Daughters of the Hills’, and brought what meager supplies they and their husbands could carry onto purchased vessels. While the Matriarch would normally seek out such dissidents, and snip these loose ends, word has reached the roosts that their ships sail to Aelantier, the lost continent of the elves. With neither the means, nor the willingness to follow Asuria to such lengths, the looming threat from upstart noble flocks has necessitated that the ruling monarch strengthen the position of the heir. Surely a much more treacherous fate awaits Princess Ašuria across the roiling sea.
