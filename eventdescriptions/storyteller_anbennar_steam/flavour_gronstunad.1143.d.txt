Brawling in the streets of HulazKrakazol. Our generous offer to purchase the Amethyst has been rejected, and our envoy's head returned to us in an iron box. There are already calls to seize the Amethyst by force, and indeed no outlay in men or arms is too costly to retrieve the gem. The municipal government of HulazKrakazol has additionally submitted a formal request for war, though much of it is smudged and it consists largely of extended profanity. Nevertheless, we are in broad agreement with its suggestions.
