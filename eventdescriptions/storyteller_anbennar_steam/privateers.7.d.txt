Our use of privateering to strengthen our position on the oceans has not gone unnoticed by the unsavory individuals who raid ships without the blessing of the monarch. While we prefer not to see many similarities between our legitimate authorizations of reprisal and these pests, some of them see quite a few. Some of the more successful pirates in our area of interest are seeking to receive the backing of the country in more or less official capacity. They are also suggesting, through crude insinuations, that if we do not take them up on the offer, someone else just might.
