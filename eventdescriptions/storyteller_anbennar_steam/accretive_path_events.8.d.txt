In a province, a sage from one of their temples humbly requests permission to establish a teaching program among the villagers. He speaks of the quiet power found in steady cultivation of mind and spirit, of the way the Accretive Path can root even the most restless souls in harmony on the path to self-improvement. Such a program would be a beacon of enlightenment in a land still shadowed by doubt, but it demands resources from the realm. Your council is divided. some see this as a vital step towards spiritual unity, others warn of overreach and squandered coin.
