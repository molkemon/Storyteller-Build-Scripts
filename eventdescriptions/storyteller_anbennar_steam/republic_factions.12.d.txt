The guilds of our ruler have outmaneuvered their competitors in the political arena, wresting control over the local government, but their power is far from uncontested. A craftsman, valued by the republic for his unparalleled skill, approaches you with a solution to this problem. ‘Our lord,’ the craftsman says, ‘a shift in power now may undo everything the guilds have wisely worked towards. We know you are a wise leader, but if you need an additional incentive, the guilds do not lack funds for political donations.’
