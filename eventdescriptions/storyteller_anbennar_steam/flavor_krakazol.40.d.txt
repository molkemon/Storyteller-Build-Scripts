As the ale from the meteorite continues to stream into the ocean, the reality of their achievement is starting to dawn on them. As the ale seems, by all accounts, infinite, they have put something into motion that will, in time, change the world. Though they still exist in its death throes, they have slain sobriety, and begun a new era. An age of eternal drunkenness. And though they celebrate their immense accomplishment, the gnawing doubts remain. Nay, they are stronger than ever before. As they have achieved their goals beyond the point of even their wildest dreams, they are left without adversary or ambition. Their part in this world is played, and they are a people without purpose. What is an Amethyst Dwarf, after he has achieved everything?
