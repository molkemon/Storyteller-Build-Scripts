My Lord, it seems like the Clodhoppers managed to arm themselves so well that they now rival our very own military. We understand that they need to be armed and supplied well for their hunts for all kinds of dangerous creatures within the Serpentspine, but this is a step too far. In response to that, the Cloak and Swaggers volunteer to thin their numbers to a more manageable force. What do you say to that? Accept the Swaggers proposal or let the Clodhoppers be as armed and deadly?
