The party comes across the remains of the metal three-armed guard. Thankfully, the statue does not wake to fight them again. Manui kneels beside the guard and reaches out for the damaged core. "One of the twelve locks. They were what kept the Tower Master imprisoned." One explorer steps closer and kneels beside her. "We saw a vision of a warrior fighting this guard, with the very same spear you carry." "Yes, Kaiko Ikaika, the Wanderer. My people told many stories about him. He made a deal with the Master; break the twelve locks, and the life of his dearest would be returned. However, the Master tricked him and stole his body when it was freed. The Tower Master would have been free as he is today, had it not been for the elder of his tribe. The elder had followed Kaiko Ikaika to the Tower, and he sealed the Master again after the deal had been finished. The Wanderer’s love had been given life again, but his body was the Master’s forever." "How did the elder seal the Master away? Can we do the same?" Looking down at the ruined core in her hand, Manui shakes her head and replies, "He led the Master on a chase through the pillar, repairing the locks along the way. But, this lock has been damaged beyond repair. The others will likely be in the same state. The Master will not allow any chance of imprisonment again." Manui reaches into her shirt and pulls out a metal plate, with ornate carvings and symbols. "The Dragon Turtles have not forgotten the terror of the Tower Master’s reign. They have given us a method to end this madness. If we are to seal him once again, we will need a new lock. This one will do, but it will require a large amount of energy to power."
