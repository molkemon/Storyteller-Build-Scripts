Clattering loud as a purse of gilded rings, rumors in the province have reverberated to colorful heights. Swifter than a lightning’s boom, a flitting flicker had been sighted among the wilderness, always zipping away before onlookers could approach, but, one day atop a green hill, an adventuring group spotted shining remnants bright as emeralds. golden quills. With such an obvious clue now plain to see, we can safely declare the mysterious light running through the province is none other than an almruk, or “gilded hog” in Centaur. Broad as a wild pig, this flighty fey creature charges across frontiers faster than wing or hoof, occasionally littering its valuable spines on its horizon-spanning journeys. By some divine boon, the almruk’s last sighting gifted us a hefty supply of its invaluable quills.
