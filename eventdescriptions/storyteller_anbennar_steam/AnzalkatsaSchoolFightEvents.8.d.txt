Concerning news has come from those who live on the frontiers with the Ashhana tribes, as the bloodcursed tribes have found themselves a unified ruler. They rule over most of their people and proclaim their goal to bring the rest under their dominion. Such a unified and controlled polity of the Ashhana lizards, no matter what their proclamations of their intentions toward lands outside of the Ashhana tribes, has brought worry and alarm across the rest of the Lizardfolk. This has led many to study the past to see what answers lie there, and discover that there is little. The three hundred thirty-second Empire, after all, was brought down by the bloodcursed berserkers, and thus their texts offer little answers on how to respond to the return of this great threat. This has led to a great outpouring of new ideas, thoughts, and theories on how best to respond to the rise in power of the Ashhana, often couched in criticisms of the three hundred thirty-second Empire’s approach to them. This has led to a general increase in the prestige of the Sculptors of Fate, whose scholars are leading this charge, and support for their beliefs has grown.
