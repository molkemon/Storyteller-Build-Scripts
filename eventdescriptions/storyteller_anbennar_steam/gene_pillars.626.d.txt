Myow. The party hears a familiar sound come from the shadows. They ready their weapons, prepared for another monstrosity. A small furry paw emerges from the darkness. Then little Myow comes forward, a glowing shattered bauble in its mouth. The party gathers around Myow, caressing and petting the loyal companion. Surprised by its survival, but glad nonetheless. Manui watches uncomfortably and then sighs. "The thing in its mouth is another lock. We should take this energy as well." The party agrees, but clearly Manui has more to say. "I’m not sure if you’ve noticed, but this creature is not of this world. It is a spirit, kept bound by the remnants of the lock. Harnessing that energy will release it from this plane." The party looks toward Myow, preparing to make a difficult decision.
