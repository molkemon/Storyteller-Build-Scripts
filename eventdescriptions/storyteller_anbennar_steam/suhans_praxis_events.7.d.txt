In this city I weep; in this city I hope. Twenty years ago, when Keo first invited me here; he is the one hiding next to that statue; I saw the crossroads just next to the customs house, on the way downhill towards the Baashidi Steps. Houses of the Orange Sash, Unbroken Claw and Starry Eye all glowered at each other, and on the fourth corner of the crossroads? A tavern, of course. Ours is the path to the tavern door. Behind it we find friend, enemy, savage; the pitiable and the enraging. Each has something to teach us.
