All Roads Lead to Castonath, a common saying in the glory days of ancient Castanor, one that encapsulates the City of Stone's importance and reach in antiquity. As the nexus of the imperial apparatus and then largest city on Halann, Castonath was fed by Kheteratan and Bulwari grain, while the center of industry and artisanry in South Castonath ate through prodigious shipments of ore and other raw materials. Much of this traveled to the city through the Dragon Road, and when this artery was severed, Castanor lost a vital link to the Divenhal and easy access to the greater trade networks that it represented. The restoration of the Dragon Road is finally leading to the slow resumption of trade and people through this vital axis, a dry riverbed with only memories of water welcoming the spring rains as it fills wellworn channels. Once again, it can be said that All Roads Lead to Castonath.
