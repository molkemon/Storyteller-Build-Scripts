Spells, swords, shields, and awards are readied against the ghost as it dives down and conjures spikes of silvery-white energy that it launches in circular patterns around it. These spikes pierce body and plate, they skewer them in many ways, but the ghost, Gorub, is not invincible. Slowly its form fades away and its voice grows weaker. In an act of desperation, it launches more and more spikes, but eventually, it runs out. With a last, desperate "ascend," it vanishes into thin air and leaves the package of gold behind.
