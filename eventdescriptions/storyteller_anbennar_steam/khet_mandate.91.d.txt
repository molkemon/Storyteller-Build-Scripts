The Great Pyramid of Elikhet towers as one of the landmarks of the Kheterata, its smooth shape and rough, clean walls naturally matching the barren wasteland. Or so the architect thought; the Khetarch has ordered a renovation of the Pyramid, so that it can fully demonstrate the might of Elikhet's legacy. He demands a number of expensive investments. a chamber after each of Elikhet's titles, their walls inscribed with an epic dedicated to his history; magical wards protecting Elikhet’s sarcophagus and replacing the tip with a solid gold block.
