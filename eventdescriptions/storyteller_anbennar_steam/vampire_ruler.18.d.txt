It was almost too easy. Our lord decided the hunting grounds would be the tavern, that honeypot constantly attracting flies; and wasps. Our lord arrived late at night, well into the carousing, when nary a soul could make out the lord's face through heavy beer goggles (let alone recall it the next morning). It was similarly straightforward to attract a potential victim; turning up the charm, our lord barely even needed their vampiric abilities. The inn had been wellselected for its availability of private (and discreet) rooms, and the pair blended in with countless others as they found their way upstairs. The rest was done in the darkness, and in the darkness it shall remain. Suffice it to say that our lord’s desires have been wellsated, and that their victim shall survive, with a hazy recollection of the night. Our lord shall greet the dawn with a smile on their face, knowing their prey's only memory of the night shall be the brightness of their eyes.
