The Rzentur share much of the customs and lifestyle of broader Ynnic culture. However, ever since the overthrowing of a lord and the rise of the Drozma Tur faith, a wedge has been driven between the Riverworshipping Sarda and Dolindhans and their Dragonworshipping Rzentur cousins, who are once again decried as barbaric by the other two. Events such as the rise of the Great Heathen Host, culminating in the slaying of a leader at the hands of a Ynnic knight, have further driven this wedge, resulting in a sharp turn for the stillnascent tradition of Rzentan chivalry. While many knights are still employed by Rzentur rulers, individualism is no longer encouraged, nor are heroic deeds emphasized. The informal code of Overlord-Vassal relationship developed during the apex of the Two Castles Period has been put to the wayside. The strongest Rzentur lord rules with the title of Ladr, Duke, as a superior to his other vassals and the sole enforcer of the Dragon's will.
