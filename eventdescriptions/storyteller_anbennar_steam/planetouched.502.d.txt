The monarch has recently found themself fascinated by all things architectural. They have spent many free hours looking down upon the capital and requesting information on construction and buildings being done, how it might be improved, and how builders could be valuable in wartime as well. Their passion has slowly grown, and now shortcuts under internal castle walls and intricate sculptures have been spotted emerging apparently spontaneously around the castle, with the culprit not being found. We could propose that the ruler could help the arduous task of building in the overcrowded capital. However, people only have so many hours in a day, and a ruler even less, so towards which of their passions should we direct them?
