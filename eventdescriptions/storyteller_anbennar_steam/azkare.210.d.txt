Having been rejected by the Xia on account of her elven blood, Amaraeis, the long time martial arts master of the Nakhriban, has come to us with a proposal. They believe that, through their leadership, a new era of elven martial arts can be born in Azkare. It is a testament to Amaraeis’ skill that he has already managed to build a small following, and with proper support from the state, who knows what they can accomplish.
