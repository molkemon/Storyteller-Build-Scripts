Yusshela, the Celestial Dolphin, is a blessed and powerful Araizary, who has historically granted great boons and blessings to those deserving. Today, the ruler is going to become one of these fortunate individuals, as a reward for the continued reverence and worship of the Araizary in Khugra. The ruler enters the sea alone, as the constellation of the Celestial Dolphin shines brightly in the night sky in the moonless night. The envoy of Yusshela ascends from the depths almost angelically, a sea turtle of stunning beauty, every star of the skies reflected in its shell made of softly shimmering starlight. Our mesmerized ruler extends their hand to touch the animal, and as soon as they do so the light it emitted becomes a blinding flash. When their eyes open, the beast is gone, only the light of the evershining stars breaking the darkness. The ruler swims back to the shore, where royal advisors await eager to listen to their retelling of the experience. The monarch feels the power of the boon flowing through their body, and the changes in their personality are evident. The ruler has received the Blessing of the Sea Turtle, a boon of great beauty.
