With Heir winning the Hekuudzoungh tournament, millions of the realm’s subjects are once again reminded of the supremacy of Daxugo, its heir and the Daxug culture as tales of the martial feats displayed during the tournament spread throughout the nation. As millions enjoy the fireworks, street parades and food stemming from the weeklong festivities throughout the realm that accompany the tournament, even the most remote youths are exposed to Daxug culture.
