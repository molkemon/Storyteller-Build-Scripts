To the west, the terrorbird riders stir. The hordes of the Shuvüüsh have often been a thorn in the Crane Throne’s side, ever since their first descent into our lands immediately after the Day of Ashen Skies. But recently there have been whispers of a new leader, a Faceless Queen, uniting the disparate tribes. With the death of JeongHun Gyo, the bowlords of Danggun, the Hwalbaek, are concerned about our unprotected western flank and demand the construction of fortifications in that province.
