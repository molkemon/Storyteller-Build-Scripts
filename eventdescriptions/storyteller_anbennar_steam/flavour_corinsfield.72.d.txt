A small village has been decimated by one of those feral creatures the Ynnics call a wendigo. Although the beast was subdued after much fighting and its body burned, problems yet remain. Farmers in the area have begun to accuse the town’s counter of summoning the creature as a familiar to ransack the village following a dispute between the two parties. The inquisitors are on the verge of looking into the situation; if the beastie truly was a familiar, then its summoner needs to be found posthaste. However, equal amounts in the inquisition refuse to look into the situation, protesting the investigation of a man of the cloth and claiming the dispute is clearly political.
