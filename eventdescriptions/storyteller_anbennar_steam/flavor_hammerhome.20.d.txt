Pass the Map." yells a dwarf.  Hundreds of dwarves gather around the Silverdocks on the edge of Castonath, preparing supplies, from compasses to old maps of Castonath, and of course ale.  "Dwarves, men, and halflings. Prepare yourselves. Castonath, though once a great city, has changed after the Greentide. Many of you dwarves who formerly lived in Castonath may not recognize your ancient home, now standing ruined before you. But if we fully survey and map this new Castonath, we may finally be able to restore the city to its ancient glory." The crowd, after a round of cheering, soon departs into the stone city, with whatever fate awaits for them there.
