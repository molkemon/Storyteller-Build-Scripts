The lord is used to short sleep. There’s always a new case of terror, always a new ambush, always a new crisis, even at night. So it came as a great surprise when this morning they woke up, well rested for the first time in ages. Asking around, confused at who let this happen, they found out that this night, this time, everything went well. All the skirmishes have gone well, nobody has succumbed to the terror, and the people seem just a little more confident than before, just a little more sure of themselves. Many battles are still ahead, and much of the nation is still in chaos, but for this night, this moment, the lord finally knows peace.
