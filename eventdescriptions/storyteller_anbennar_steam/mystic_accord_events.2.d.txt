Traveling through the Lupulan Jungle at the head of an army, our lord was waylaid by the sound of a distant, rumbling step. Ordering the column to halt, they waited with apprehension as the noise approached. Then, emerging with gentle care from the jungle wall came the shape of an enormous monitor lizard. Its reptilian gaze seemed to see only the lord, still atop their oddly calm horse. With the same slow movement, it reached forth a clawed foot, extending a single long digit towards their forehead.
