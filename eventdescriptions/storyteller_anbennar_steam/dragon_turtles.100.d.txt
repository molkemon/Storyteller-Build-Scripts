A rift has opened between us and Kealakys the EverHungry. The monarch decided to go to him personally in an attempt to mend it. Things did not start well. We performed all of the proper greetings and made some small talk, but when it came time to eat we discovered that the delegation brought Keakalys an orchard’s worth of mangos when he specifically asked for guavas. That made him hungry. Then, the monarch decided to engage Kealakys on the subject of life, and whether or not it was imbued with specific purpose. The details of the conversation were hard to follow, but at some point the monarch and Kealakys became at odds with one another, and neither would budge. That made Kealakys angry. Everything happened in a flash: Kealakys and the monarch were shouting at one another, and then a hungry, angry Kealakys bent down, and all of a sudden the shouting was coming from inside Kealakys’ mouth, and then there wasn’t any shouting at all. It would appear we no longer have a monarch.
