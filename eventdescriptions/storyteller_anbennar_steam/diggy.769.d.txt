Over the last few years a new issue has become apparent. The Cannorian dwarves have shown considerable enthusiasm in the abstract idea of rebuilding the Dwarovar, but when it comes to its execution, their efforts lack fervor. Most of them are skilled in surface professions, such as bakers, farmers, gardeners, and fishers. While they have been trying to apply their skills to the cultivation of fungi and serpentbloom, the simple truth remains that they need miners more than farmers.
