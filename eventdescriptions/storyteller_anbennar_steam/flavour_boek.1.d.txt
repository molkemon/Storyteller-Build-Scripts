Hundreds of brave souls are laying dead, dozens of villages are burning, and a staff is throwing lightning and thunder towards a fleeing family. Our shamans have brought grim tidings. During their last ritual, the aspects have warned them of what is to come. Death and destruction are coming to our lands. Not even our families will be safe. The portents are clear; we need to prepare for what is to come. The very survival of our people is at stake.
