Centuries ago, in a fit of terror, the Exodus Goblins fled the Serpentspine and their former orkish overlords, seeking refuge amidst the humans and elves of Bulwar. What they found was war, but through quick thinking and devious ingenuity, they managed to make a foothold for themselves. However, the orks are gone, and in their place a new Goblin paradise has arisen. Surely they will want to come back. Wait, why are there so many of them?
