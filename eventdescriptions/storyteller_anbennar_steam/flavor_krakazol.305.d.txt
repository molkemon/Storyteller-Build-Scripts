The next batch of drinks has been served. Dwarvesh guzzle ale like they are being paid for it, jeering one another on to keep going. The halls are filled with laughter, song, and the odd brawl here and there, and it is altogether quite the merry and lively occasion. The monarch sits on his throne with a flagon of the purest ale. It is his duty, nay, his honour to drink with his people, and drink he shall. The monarch feels all the eyes of the great hall aimed at him, speculating whether or not he will live through it.
