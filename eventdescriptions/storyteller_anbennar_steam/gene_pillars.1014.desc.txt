A faint, intermittent clanking has haunted us throughout our expedition, echoing down the hallways of the pillar. It grew louder as we approached the next room, now clearly identifiable as mechanical footsteps. We readied ourselves to battle another deadly defense system only to find a lone mechanism inside, old and visibly damaged from its endless wandering. Its voice is patchy and hard to parse, but it is obviously excited to see a group of friendly faces, gesturing for us to follow. Guiding us expertly through the twisting passageways, the mechanism stops at a decaying patch of wall, pulling aside the rusted plating to reveal a hidden cache of supplies within.
