The hunt has become more and more popular, and many magpies have come to the capital to join in the great hunts led by the shamans. But as they return, they bring strange treasures home. No shinies, no, no, they are strange men from the borders. Guards watch in a stupor as the chuckling and laughing magpies carry them into their homes. Some of the strange men seem quite happy in their new lives and are looking to stay.
