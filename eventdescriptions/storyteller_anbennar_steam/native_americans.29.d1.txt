While the monarch was going on a hunt with some of their warriors, trying to surround a group of deer and bring some food for their tribe, one of their men approaches the monarch and says that he has seen a really big deer deep in the forest. The monarch does not hesitate and follows the man through the woods until they find their prey. A big deer, the biggest they have ever seen, poses majestically in the middle of a clearing. They whisper a short thank you to the spirits of the forest for this opportunity that they will not miss. They order their men to stay quiet and in their positions as they start to silently move towards the best position to fire their bow. The bow is ready to release the arrow destined to kill the animal. The monarch's breath is calm. Their fingers start to free the arrow.
