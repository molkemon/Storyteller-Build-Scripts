Oh, who would stay indoors when the horn is on the hill? With the crisp air stinging and the huntsmen singing, and a tented beast to kill. Before the sun goes down, we shall slay the bull of ten, and the skalds shall sing a tale, and we shall have the ale when we come home again. Being regarded a great hunter of game is one of the most sought-after accolades in Gerudia, and when a great hunt is called by the skalds, many from far and wide participate. So the question falls to you. will it be a grand occasion for the nobility, or will you instead offer bounties to commoners for pelts they bring you?
