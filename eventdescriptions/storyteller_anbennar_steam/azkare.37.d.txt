The Xia have always been reluctant subjects to elven rule. Among all the nations of Haless, it is theirs that holds the greatest antipathy for elven rule. Now their years of plotting open rebellion have come to a head, and they rise up en masse against our prosperous rule. There is a certain irony to the fact that we elves have managed to unify them, if only for them to immediately turn upon us. We have tried to show these people peace, order, and dignity, but they have made it clear that they would rather lurk in the shadows of antique glory. It is time to drag them into the future of Haless through force.
