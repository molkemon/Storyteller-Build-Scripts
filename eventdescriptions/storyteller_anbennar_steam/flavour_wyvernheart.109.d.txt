It is done. An artificer rises as he pulls up his glasses, the brand of domination still glowing on his forehead. He disconnects a pipe filled with auburn ichor from the table and switches a few levers. "HeadArcanist please check the results," he beckons towards a thick window. Moments later, a tall woman enters, wearing vestments of a deep burgundy decorated with a silver eye, a symbol of her seat at the council. "Indeed, what a marvelous result." She says, gazing at the Burnblood tied to the reclined table, its eyes wide open, staring back. Its eyes a burning orange and instead of a pupil, a cleft sclera akin to a reptile. The skin; a deep red, enhanced by infernal magic, toughened by enchantments, and perfected through bioartificery. A beast, deformed, hulking, muscles bulging. Claws as sharp as razors, scales as hard as steel. A heart of Dames tear itself. And anger, rage, hatred; all fueling the beast's bloodthirst, all apparent in its eyes and the tears of blood they produce while looking at the woman. The HeadArcanist smiles and reaches out to the brand of domination on the beast's forehead, and it relents and closes its eyes, falling into a deep slumber. She then adds: "You've done well. Spread this one's genes to the spawning pools; production must begin immediately. I will report this to the Sovereign; our lord shall be pleased."
