In the depths of the experimental layer lies a contraption unlike any the party has ever seen before. Sitting on a raised pedestal, a giant cannon constructed by seemingly otherworldly means is aimed through an opening in the ceiling towards the sky, lying dormant. Getting closer to the weapon, the party can feel the intense magical energy flowing into its core, as it siphons off power directly from the Kiamoa itself. Conthus, who has been following the party from a safe distance while they clear the path, enters the chamber and clomps towards the gun, his swirling head flickering faster the closer he gets. "At last, we arrive at my salvation. Tremble in awe before the might of our crowning achievement of melding material and magic; the Stormcannon." The Precursor swings his mechanical arms wide, gesturing to the gun with a grandiose flair. "This device is capable of infusing whatever it contacts with the Kiamoa itself," the ancient being rattles through his voice port. "Through this, I can merge into the Kiamoa, and finally free myself of this wretched tank. I will be free and at long last at peace." The Precursor hurriedly begins moving around the room, gathering a seemingly random assortment of parts and schematics as the Stormcannon stands ominously on its dais. A lot could be learned from such a device if we took the time to examine it.
