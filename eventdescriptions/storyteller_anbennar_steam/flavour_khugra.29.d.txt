After years of travel, the sailors sent on a Great Journey have come back. The Araizary led them to a beautiful coral reef in a previously unknown location. While many would have surely preferred good old gold and silver, the immense beauty of the reef, shining with a thousand colours and full of beautiful fish and sea life, is enough to fill one’s heart of joy and wonder. This beautiful location has become a symbol of Khugran unity and pride, and their greatest artists are already working hard to recreate in their paintings and sculptures the marvels of this place.
