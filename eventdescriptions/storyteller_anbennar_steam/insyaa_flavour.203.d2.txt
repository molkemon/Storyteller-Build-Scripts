The song of Nalo Pu’ukani, the memory of Grandfather Moss, and the discovery of Hoki’eal. All three Dragon Turtles have given over their secrets to us, trusting that there is finally a force in Insyaa powerful and wise enough to deal with what may come once the Kiamoa falls. We will honour their trust; we will bring whatever lies beyond the Kiamoa into our memory, and it shall never be forgotten. At the moment each Dragon Turtle gave us their words of power, they hesitated; even Hoki’eal did. What we learn beyond the Kiamoa must all be remembered, every part of it, however immense it is in its totality. We are taking on a grand and terrible burden.
