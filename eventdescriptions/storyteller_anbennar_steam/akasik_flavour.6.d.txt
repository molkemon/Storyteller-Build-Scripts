As we knew they would, our officials have refused the prince's radical proposal, forcibly expelling him from the capital. He seems to have taken the news quite harshly, gathering a great host of angry refugees and brigands. Thankfully it seems his hatred towards the gnolls is greater than his hatred of us, as he has made his intention to invade the gnollish kingdom to restore his kingdom himself. We eagerly await word of his inevitable demise.
