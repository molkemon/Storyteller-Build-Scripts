Khagor looked upon the city, sitting upon the shimmering lake, with smug pride. Where the ashes of Elynaile once stood was the orkish city of Gudir'dar.  "From ashes, something new may come."  The ork blinked as the words passed through his mind. They passed as his own musings and yet… there was a familiarity to them. He stood uncertain for a moment before dismissing his unease. Slowly, he then returned his gaze to his city. His glorious city.  "But there couldst be more."  His head throbbed, yet he could not dismiss the thought. The city was not enough. Until now, he and his clan had settled for shoddy warcamps, planning only their next battle. If these woods were truly home, Sapchopper would need to make it so.  "The time hast come to embrace these woods."  Khagor paused.
