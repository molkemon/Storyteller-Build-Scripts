The fair city of Maldarion the Navigator thought they could unite the Reachmen to stop our expansion and take the Jotunsglotta, but they have failed. Their armies have surrendered, the city is ours. However, they still hold great influence across the cities and trading fleets. It may be wise to allow them some autonomy and utilize their connections, or should we seize their port directly? The Gerudians have long been envious of Celmaldor's extensive and beautiful docks, and our shipwrights are eager to utilize them.
