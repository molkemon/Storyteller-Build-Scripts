From all creatures, the Halann, existing or extinct, none calls to mind the idea of raw strength as Giants do. While dragons may soar the skies and breathe the elements and djinns may command powerful magics, giants represent physical might taken to its extreme, lifting mountains and leveling armies with a single swipe. Pondering this, our alchemists have decided to bring to our ruler a proposal to develop potions to try to mimic, even if to a small degree, the mythical strength of giants. Recognizing its potential, the ruler decided to entertain this request, approving this research. To begin the research, the alchemists must decide where to begin looking for inspiration. study the giants' closest living kin, or focus on studying the giants themselves, or rather, what remains of them.
