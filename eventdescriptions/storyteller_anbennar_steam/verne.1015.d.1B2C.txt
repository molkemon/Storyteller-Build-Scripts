They were foolish, my Vernman guys, blockheads out the Expeditionaries, you know? No good telling them to decipher some wall pattern; they needed me to trust them. So I told them to be Vernmen, and I drove them forward, and we got through. We lost a couple to a weird gravity room and some illusion garden that led my guy right into a gas trap, but by the end of it these guys were pumped; I could see it. They had done it; they would die for me. Guys like that are crazy—the more you ask of them, the more they like you.
