Some of Surael's Chosen have approached the ruler to tell them of their intentions to leave Bulwar for Aelantier, to join the Jaherian Exemplars. They say that they feel their decades, and in some cases, centuries, of experience and talent are wasted here in Bulwar, and the call of their ancestral home is too strong to resist. It would not be good for the morale of the general populace if they just let them leave, but it would open new positions which would have to be filled by someone, be they elf or human.
