Among all letters coming into the capital, the monarch did not expect to receive one from the Bloodsong orks. At first the monarch thought that this might be a prank or someone jealous of our Heroic victory over the Command. The monarch wanted to toss the letter into the trash, but curiosity struck the Elephant, and so the ruler got up and took a quick read. “The only thing first that you guys are is biting the dust on the battlefield. After we come back, we make sure that there will be only dust left in your lands.” The monarch's eyes were twitching. Quickly, the ruler crumbled the letter and tossed it to the side, sitting down. Hunched forward with an annoyed look, they took some time on what to do or reply. One of the many courtiers picked up the letter. His face went pale before turning red with hate as he remembered his home in Tilathimen was burned down by the Bloodsong Clan. “MY LORD.” he barely managed to wrangle out some words, but the monarch knew already the answer.
