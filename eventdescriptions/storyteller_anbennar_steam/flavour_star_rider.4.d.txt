I have done what you wished me to do, my light. I have returned my chieftainate to your fold. I have made them faithful once more. Our neighbors have claimed me mad when I instituted systems unseen to the plains before, but the only madness was their own. Do not punish them, for they understand now. They called me cruel, but they will come to realize that this cruelty was benevolence. Your light now shines on all of Nuzurbokh, but I ask of you, what now? The ruler knows. The ruler asks regardless. This enlightenment has to be shared amongst all of centaurkind. The shamans of each sect, united, understanding the worship of Irdaeos in all its splinters, shards, witnessing his brilliance. A consensus, not just of the east, but of the entirety of the plains. Born of understanding and comprehension. An interpretation that will last until all ends. The tenacious Maslekhuga, the gregarious Irdukhuga and the warmongering Filustkhuga; We will bring our faith to them, as they share theirs with us. Be our star in the darkness, which we will never forsake again.
