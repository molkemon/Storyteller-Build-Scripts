Dwarves are a prideful folk, refusing to bow before others even when the odds are overwhelming. Yet do not mistake this pride for foolish hubris. After tasting defeat by forces of the Republic, we sit cornered and without the firepower to pose significant resistance. We had prepared to make our final stand, but an ultimatum was delivered by a member of Red Mares; either willingly submit to the Republic and be granted large autonomy, or be crushed by the Republic’s armies and lose it all. Though a week ago the natural inclination to deliver a stubborn refusal would have been the natural response, now it seems the sentiment has changed. Even now as the envoys await the final decision, a large crowd of dwarves, charmed by the diplomats' manners and appearance, have gathered and seem eager to make niceties with the outsiders.
