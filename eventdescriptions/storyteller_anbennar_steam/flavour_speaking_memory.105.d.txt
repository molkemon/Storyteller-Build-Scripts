The monarch ran as quickly as their feet could carry them. There was so much to do, and the ancestors had ideas about all of it. The realm’s tax issues were nothing compared to taming the waves, and if the Holohana came again, the country could deal with them just as they had so many years ago, with only the wisdom of the ancestors and the grit of their troops keeping the realm safe. As the monarch ran, they reflected. it seemed mundane at first, but the only thing separating them from their ancestors was time. The monarch held all the power and wisdom that they had read about in those ancient scrolls, and all they had to do was apply it. All the people across all the land had tales from their ancestors, and those were the very things that made today possible. And if there still was a today, it fell on everyone in that palace, all the people that the monarch had taken for granted, to make sure that there was a tomorrow.
