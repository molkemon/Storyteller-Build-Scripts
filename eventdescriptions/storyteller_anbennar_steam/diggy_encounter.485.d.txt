Of the many creatures of the Serpentspine caves, few boggle the mind as much as the Gelatinous Cube. This gooey, translucent, polyhedral, brainless creature has baffled scholars for centuries, with its cubic shape inspiring many debates as to if the creature was created this way to serve a specific purpose, or if its shape is a consequence of the manner and environment in which it lives. Regardless as to why the Gelatinous Cubes are cubes, they are damn effective at cleaning the various tunnels; both natural and artificial; throughout the Serpentspine. With our expedition now face to face with a sizeable colony of these creatures, and the opportunity of using their abilities for our benefit, we must decide how we may try to tame these beasts.
