Azukor entered his mother’s room and immediately froze. His mother, grayed with age, her face wrinkled from constant scowling, lay lifeless at her desk, her body slumped over it. On the desk were several letters full of hatred for everyone and everything beyond the Venomtooth clan. A planned invasion of the clan lands of Heartgrinder and Clouded Eye were laid out, plans to hunt every halfling and goblin in the woods were meticulously detailed, and even a list of personal enemies was found amongst the papers. Azukor walked over to his mother and touched her hand. It was cold, the deep green of her skin already paling in death. Her once proud tusks seemed brittle and weak. He choked back his tears. Tears were for the weak. The strong could feel no sorrow. Aâdga died valiantly fighting the Cannorian devils before they hexed her. Azukor carried his mother to her bed, respectfully covering her remains with a blanket. He walked back to the desk and got seated. The enemies of orkkind would not wait. He dipped his quill in ink and started sketching. Soon, the Cannorians would know what it felt like to lose those they loved.
