Shay zor Hajo spoke, as he rubbed his saltstriped beard. He said, "The most important thing in war is not soldiers or strategy. it is logistics. Men march on their stomachs, and beyond a certain scale foraging strips the land utterly bare. What you need is supply lines. Carts, servants, guards, all of it." Ayufar replied in between sips of his coffee, "And I assume that becomes a battle in and of itself?" He continued, "Damn right it does. Being beaten in battle can set a war back, losing supplies can end it, and doom an entire army to death, or heaven forbid, force them into banditry. The amount of jumped up lords claiming to be great military minds who ignore their supply lines scares me." The first speaker then offered, "How about I offer you a place here, so you can keep my less experienced leaders in line?" He looked at Ayufar with dark eyes burning with an inner fire, and nodded, grinning yellowed teeth. Ayufar replied, "Perhaps I shall take you up on it. It would do me good to whip these fools into shape, and to command the soldiers of your realm. I will begin at once."
