The crowd chants for the final time tonight. The game has ended; you watch as men, women, and children file out of the great hall and down the slopes of the mountain. From your vantage, you see them file down into the various towns and villages into the valley below. You are approached then by the leader of one of these valley towns; the restarted games have allowed his town in the valley below to make substantial wealth from the various traders and travelers from surrounding kingdoms, and they want to present a gift to you personally. Before you can say anything, he presents the wealth to you and makes his way down to the valley.
