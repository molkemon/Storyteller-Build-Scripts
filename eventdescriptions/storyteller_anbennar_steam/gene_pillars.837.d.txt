As we traverse through the outermost layers of the upper pillar, we end up coming dangerously close to the sandworm's esophageal lining. As we look on with horror and some reluctant fascination at the pulsing veins of the sandworm's skin, a sudden splatter of blood erupts as some freakishly long pale white wormlike parasites burst out of the sandworm's skin and launch themselves at us. One expedition member ends up grabbed by one of the creatures, and we just barely manage to hold onto them before they are dragged out of the pillar by the parasite. The parasite is strong, however. Can we manage to hold on?
