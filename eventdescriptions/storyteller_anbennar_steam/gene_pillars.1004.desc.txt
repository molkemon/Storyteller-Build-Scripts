To call the rarely encountered remains of the teleportation network found in the Gene Pillars unreliable would be apt, as one is equally likely to end up right where they started or in front of a very hungry beast; or worse, directly inside of one. However, this most recent destination might be the worst one yet, as the room we found ourselves in was entirely flooded, leaving us only a scant few moments to find a way out of this mess before we end up sleeping with the Hinuifishes, especially with both loot and gear weighing us down.
