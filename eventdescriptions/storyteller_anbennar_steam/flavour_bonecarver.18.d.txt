The Hideguzzler Clan had only recently escaped from our grasp, fleeing to the rugged mountains of Gulletspeak to build a stronghold for themselves. However, they seem to have forgotten who we are: the orks who broke the mountains of the Dwarovar long ago. Crushing the relatively puny heights of Gulletspeak proved to be a swift matter for our clan. Now, with their leaders executed for their betrayal, the goblins can only do one thing: accept the rule of our clan once more.
