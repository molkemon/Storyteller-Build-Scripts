The Ahati have been growing exponentially in recent years. Many young men join their ranks and many more desire to do so. This has led to the Ahati needing more space for housing and training. They have therefore proposed the building of barracks in a nearby province to house both their cadets, soldiers and personnel, alongside ours. They think that there is much to be won from having the Ahati and regulars live, work and train together. They will pay the cost of the building, in exchange for future favours.
