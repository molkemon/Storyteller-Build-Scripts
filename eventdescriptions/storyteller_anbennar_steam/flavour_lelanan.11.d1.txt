My blade slashed with divine power. The dense Fangaulan jungle was in our way. We tread upon dirt which had seen no Halfling. Once we found Ninakaire, we planned to stay. We expected to find a den of monsters, the light of civilization lost from its honeycombed walls for years. So we came ready for any threat which we might face, our great spears gripped tightly in our arms. I slashed through another thick bough and found myself emerging onto a clear hilltop overlooking the ruins of the Ninakaire Ananoma. The huge honeycomb structures brought us to a stop. As more of my paladins caught up, I could hear their gasps of awe. I smiled as I set off down the hill, my divine blade ready for this maw.
