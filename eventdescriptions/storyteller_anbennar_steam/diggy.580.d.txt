Since the previous incident with our farms, the fungus of our realm has slowly grown bolder. What used to be a rare sighting is now a frequent occurrence. They constantly scout the common walking routes and sometimes even settlements. And now some of our citizens' possessions have started to disappear, leaving many worried that the fungus might be getting smarter. The situation is becoming untenable and action is needed. We could simply smoke them out, though they have grown significantly in numbers since we last considered this possibility. Or, as one young diplomat recommended, we could attempt to negotiate with them, since they have shown signs of intelligence.
