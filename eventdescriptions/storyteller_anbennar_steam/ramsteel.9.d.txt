The second step for our mecharam design is to decide on the main make and body. Our smiths and artificers have laid out two designs for us to choose between. One design is made out of intricate mithril work laid over carefully set sheets of ramsteel. We would expect rams of this design to be able to move faster in and out of battle due to the lightness of mithril. The second design, of mostly pure ramsteel and little else, does not look close to as impressive as the first design. However, the smiths insist that it is just as strong, though not as light. We would expect rams of this design to decrease the overall cost of our mecharam units.
