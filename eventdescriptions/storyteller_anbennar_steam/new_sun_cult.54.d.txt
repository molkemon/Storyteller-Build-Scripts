A group of prominent human citizens have approached the ruler with a complaint. Despite efforts to crack down on the corruption which has been extorting them, they are concerned that in the future there will be no guarantee that these burdens remain gone. They request that humans be allowed the social mobility to rise beyond their current means, if not to high ranks of government which obviously should remain the domain of Surael's Chosen; still high enough to have the clout to avoid the effects of administrative malice and guarantee their people a place in the future.
