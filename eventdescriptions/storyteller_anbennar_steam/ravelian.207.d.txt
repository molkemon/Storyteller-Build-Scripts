The Ravelian Society and the estate artificers have, for a long time, grown handinhand, with many early artificers belonging to the society itself. However, in the recent years since the society got their hands on the God Fragment, a slow and steady schism seems to have appeared within the ranks of the artificers in our realm. Those of Ravelian leaning state that the artificery has for too long gone unchecked, and that we must treat artificery as if it was holy work, and explore this new craft ethically just as the Magisterium did with magic. The traditional artificers simply balk at this backwards thinking. The Ravelians, clearly outnumbered, have asked for our help, in exchange for their loyalty.
