Brothers. Stand with me in the assembly of souls. Lend me your strength, and beckon home the whims of Creation and Negation. Magic is full of unsolved problems—some, like the Dark Matrices, may even be unsolvable. But more common than the unsolvable problems are those which are simply too difficult for even an Acolyte to tackle alone. Two working together, however, such a team is not easily dissuaded from the acquisition of arcane knowledge. Such is the case for this realm and another country.
