When we bombed Tianlou, we revealed that our airships were not just the next step in trading or whalehunting, but a potent tool for war. It is thus no surprise other countries have begun to seek our designs, offering exorbitant payments and naked threats alike. But now, it seems, some have also turned to more underhanded methods. We caught the man copying diagrams in a workshop late at night, and immediately clasped him in chains. He put up a weak protest, claiming to be a trainee, but his story quickly fell apart. Soon after, a message arrived from another nation, asserting the man is a legitimate diplomat and decrying our false imprisonment of their ambassador. This is an obvious lie, but after Tianlou, the rest of Yanshen already suspects us of the worst, and this may inflame issues further. Justice is ultimately in the hands of our lord, what says their name?
