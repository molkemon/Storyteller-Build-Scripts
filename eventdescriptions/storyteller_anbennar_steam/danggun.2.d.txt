A most ferocious storm howls across the lands of the realm, born from the rattling last breath leaving the lips of the monarch. It is a storm whose mournful howls and icy touch is felt by all inhabitants of the realm, be they human or spirit, for a Sage King has left this world. A profound sense of uncertainty has the realm in its grip, for until they are renewed once more, the Compacts of Manjang no longer bind the spirits of Gowon to mortal authority. No longer do humans and spirits bow to the same master, and disputes once decisively resolved, conflicts until now safely suppressed, might well rise to the surface once again, threatening the prosperity of the realm and the wellbeing of both the mortal and immortal denizens of the realm. All that is left is the hope that a new Sage King will soon rise to meet Sado’s exacting standards and return the land to its lost harmonious order.
