Caught between the opposing sects of Claw and Canvas, a sizable portion of the population simply seeks a reprieve from the Bloodcurse and the cessation of hostilities. Pariahs of both the Canvas and the Claw, these moderates do seem to desire nothing more than to simply acquire self-control, however they can. These solace-seekers find little harbor in any social circle, feared as they are among Canvas adherents for their scars and mocked for their creative pursuits by Claw believers. However, they need not be outcasts now.
