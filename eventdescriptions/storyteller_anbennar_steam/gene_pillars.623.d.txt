The party wanders familiar hallways and sights. They enter the very room where they once encountered the two-legged orange beast. This time, no furry aberration comes from the shadows. Instead, a white butterfly flutters out. Flying daintily in the air, the butterfly lands on one of the party members’ faces. It closes and opens its wings slowly, the sight making some party members chuckle nervously. Manui suddenly raises her spear towards the shadows and cries out a warning. From the dark, a figure emerges, wearing black and white clothes. Where its face should be is instead a mass of white and black butterflies. A third arm protrudes from his chest, pointing directly towards the party member with the butterfly on their face. Manui acts swiftly, swinging her spear and swatting away the butterfly. Soon after it leaves their face, the butterfly explodes into a burst of white powder. The party immediately readies their weapons for battle.
