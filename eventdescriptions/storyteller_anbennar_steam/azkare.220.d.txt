It is a strange day for any parent, the day that their child leaves for war. For Hiderion and Mya, it comes half expected. It is clear that the Setae blood runs hot within their halfelven son. Since he was young, he has had one eye set on the horizon, and finally the call of adventure has become too much to resist. With his parent’s blessing, the young man has set out to join the Daulophs, the mercenary lords of his mother’s family.
