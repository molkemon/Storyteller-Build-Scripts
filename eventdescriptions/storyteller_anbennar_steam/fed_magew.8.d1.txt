As peace dawns on the horizon, no welcoming beams of hope meet the gaze, but instead dark tendrils, spreading throughout the lands of the Federation, for magic has disappeared from our lands. It is not simply the powers of magic that have been lost, but what it is to be triunic. Is only someone born in the lake cities a triunic? Is only someone who follows the established faith a triunic? This day will shake the foundations of the Federation, dooming countless thousands to exile or execution.
