Time marches forward, leaving those of a past age behind. The Star Elves are no different, no matter how much they strive to stay ahead. Today, Lithiel signaled her intention to leave public life until her last days. While she once rebelled against their just rulers, they can only mourn the loss of such a beloved public figure. A mourning festivity has already been planned to see her away from the harbor on her trip to Silent Repose, where she will spend her remaining years.
