The Skaldol Treaty of Faiths guarantees freedom of religion for all those in Celmaldor. Despite this freedom, it is rare for members of the three major clans to convert to another religion. Nevertheless, a scion of the first major clan has recently renounced their religion in favor of a different faith. Following this upset, the patrons of that clan are requesting we pressure this scion to revert, promising to cede some of their political dominance in exchange for this favor.
