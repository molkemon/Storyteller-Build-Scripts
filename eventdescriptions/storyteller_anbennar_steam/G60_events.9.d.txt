In our meetings with the few cooperative Kheionai, we have found that there are no Fasiri living within the great cities of Alecand, preferring to live in the rural country as they always have. The majority of the city dwellers are appalled by the notion of being ruled under a Kingdom rather than their citystates, which they hold a strong sense of nationality towards. Their autonomy and independence has been reduced, upsetting the balance of power in the east and leading to deep resentment against the Fasiri. We must restore the balance among the Kheions and prove the legitimacy of our lord and all their descendents.
