The party enters a large circular chamber. Numerous drawings and symbols adorn the walls. The entire chamber seems to be filled with a strange, volatile power. Energy seems to endlessly flow, entering and leaving this room. One of the drawings on the wall depicts tall white pillars scattered throughout a map of another land. Forty pillars to be exact, the largest of which lay east by the coast. Another drawing showed the land itself. Colored pillars dotted the map in numerous locations. One pillar was pink; the pillar they were exploring right now matched the pink pillar’s location on the map. Numerous lines connected the pink pillar to many other locations in the land. Many lines seemed to be between the pink pillar and the red pillar, the easternmost pillar. Before the party could continue their observations, the air shifted. The energy flowing through the room began to take shape. Spirit warriors form from the energy, wielding weapons in their hands.
