The spread of the Corinite faith has been considerable both throughout the empire and beyond its borders, and at last their firebrand preachers have reached both our lands and those of our Istralorian friends. Their attempts at proselytization have spurred a divisive debate amongst our people. Some profess the belief that proactive work against evil is what Munas had intended for the elves, while others believe that following Corin is a betrayal of Munas and his ideals. The time has come for Moonhaven to decide which path to follow.
