After spending years working on his steam engine design and failing to make further improvements, Grun Strongbellow has declared his departure. Never the sort to rest on his laurels or let himself grow complacent, Strongbellow left to seek inspiration for his craft elsewhere, just as he left his home to seek a place in the past. His contributions did not go unnoticed, and we have recently received a petition from a group of successful merchants who watched his progress on the steam engine with great interest. They ask the lord to officially recognize and lend support to a society dedicated to continuing Strongbellow’s legacy of innovation.
