Arannen has always worked with lace in order to successfully combine the fashions of Anbennar and Corvuria, but during the late sixteenth century lace became widely popular across all of Cannor. The newfound popularity of lace has led to increased demands, and the veteran lacemakers have answered the call, forming a new guild under their control and opening up new workshops and schools to make sure all Arannese Lace stays at the forefront of this new trend.
