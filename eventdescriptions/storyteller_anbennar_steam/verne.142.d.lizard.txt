TELLUM GAMING AND SPORT Gataw Lizardfolk Crew Wins Shock Regatta Victory. "For humans, we found them very competent." It takes three rounds of translation through a young Vyzemby woman, a Kelino elf, and finally our Vernman assistant before we get this out of the stoic Rhada lizardfolk who agreed to crew a Gataw ship. Indeed, we knew perhaps least of this team as the Regatta began; their decision-making at times was completely opaque. Why, when approaching Insyaa, did they choose to extend their route by hundreds of miles by sailing north? All questions were answered when their ship stormed into first place on the back of the Great Spirit Cinulau, and they were never again behind the pack. Approaching the Sea of Lel, they took a strong lead aided by the araizary water spirits of Taneyas, and retained a few even as far as the Gates of Divenhal. The Gataw captain argues with the Kelino fellow for a good five minutes before we hear more. "Lots of things Cannorians don't know." When this is translated for the lizardfolk, a low laugh rumbles through the room. What we do know is this. if you were canny enough to vote on this longodds team, the next round is on you.
