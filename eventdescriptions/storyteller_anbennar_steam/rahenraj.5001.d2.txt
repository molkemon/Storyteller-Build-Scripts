The Dhenbasana is littered with palaces, funded by embezzled taxes and tariffs as well as withheld tributes, bribes to look past such crimes, or in rare cases, genuine success and wise rule. No two are identical, but almost all share one feature. a well-concealed escape route. The ruler is reminded of this fact as he hears today’s report from a messenger dressed in a strange red and white hat. Our forces were victorious and the adjective palace quickly seized, but the Raja escaped like smoke on the wind. “Apologies, Your Majesty,” he concludes, “But your Raja is in another palace.” No matter. Haless could not outrun Harimar; the stream cannot outrace the sea. Run as he likes, we will soon capture the Raja, and with his power, the Lotus Throne.
