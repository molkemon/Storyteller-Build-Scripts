The Floodborn Council can sometimes be fickle, its whims unpredictable, especially when a few prominent members pass away. The shift of power from one faction to another within the Council has drastically changed the priorities and focus of the majority. As such, the previous agenda that was agreed upon has been overturned, simply stricken from the list. Some members of the previous majority protest slightly but are promptly ignored as a new agenda comes forth for the Sovereign to complete. Some of the Hardliners question these actions heavily and call for the Sovereign to step in; with a tight enough grip, the Sovereign can overturn the change in agenda but will be met with some pushback.
