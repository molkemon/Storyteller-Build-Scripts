When the walls of Gemradcurt fell, the soldiers of Eighard thought the worst was over. The battle for the city was even bloodier. For every five thralls of Winter that were struck down, four brave soldiers of Autumn met their end. Immarel herself, the wretched LadyMother who had so threatened the remnants of the Hibernal Crusade, took to the field. She froze Eigharder soldiers left and right, being by far the deadliest presence in the fight for the city streets. But even a power such as hers was not enough to quell the rage of the Tuathak. On the steps of her tower, she fell. Now, three Eigharder soldiers stand above her, with one boot on each of her hands to prevent her from spellcasting. None of them say anything. As the spear punctures her throat, her gurgle means more than words could ever have meant.
