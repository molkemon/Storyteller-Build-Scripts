In our aimless wanderings through our verdant home, we have encountered another tribe of beings like us, yet unlike us at the same time. They call themselves the Kamatoi Haeraitou. They have adapted to live on the open Titanoflora Savanna by following the migrations of the hinuibeetle herds. Traversing the hostile savanna atop these massive insects, they have learned to guide the animals in any direction of their choosing.
