Cold winds blew out to the Thaw as the monarch ascended the carved stone steps of Celmaldor's central palace, white as if hewn from a thousand thousand harvested pearls. In a room overlooking the city and the forest beyond sat Prince Arfil Icewind, the current leader of the Icewind Clan; due to everyone higher in line to the seat deserting the city as it was taken by Moorman forces. "So, you are my suzerain." He was clearly trying to sound professional, but his trembling hands and gaze focused on the armed guards said something else entirely. "In its over four centuries of history, Celmaldor has always stood independent of the powers that surrounded it. Until now. I cannot know the future of my people, but for their sake, I offer the city to you, my liege." He shakily handed over a parchment, "I only ask for the Icewinds who stayed within the city in its final hours of siege to remain in charge, to lead the city in this new age."
