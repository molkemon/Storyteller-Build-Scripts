For the past twenty years, laborers from a specific region who have experience excavating precursor steel have been chipping away at the Windward in the now-abandoned Degakheion. It has been tough work finding all of the Dames tearalloy conduits that pulse through the city, as well as digging out the entirety of the massive Dames tear core of the city, the Kheinilián. Now, however, as the final shipments of Precursor steel and Dames tear sail back to Taychend, they have finally finished their task, and the city can return to its previous trade, though Degakheion is now effectively a dead city, a tombstone for the age when the Kheions were once untouchable, and its citizens, such as they are, will have to evacuate each time a Kaydhano Deathwind approaches.
