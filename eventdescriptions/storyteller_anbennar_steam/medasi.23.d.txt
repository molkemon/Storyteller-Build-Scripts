One of the most prominent rulers recently died in mysterious circumstances, having become violently ill after dinner one night and passing away hours later despite the best efforts of his doctors. His family is publicly accusing some of his political rivals of orchestrating his murder, claiming they bribed one of their cooks to sneak poison in his dinner, and demanding they be arrested and punished for their crime. They have been unable to present conclusive evidence of this crime, and the group claims that the death is probably the result of an illness, with one of them daring to say that the ruler would not have to fear local diseases if they simply went back to their domain.
