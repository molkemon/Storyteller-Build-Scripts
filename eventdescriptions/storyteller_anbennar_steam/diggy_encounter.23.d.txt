Darn it, the nest was not abandoned. And what we assumed was a safe way around was the true trap.  As the convoy made its way through the corridor, spiders ambushed them, injected their paralyzing poison, cocooned them, and carried them away, somewhere deep into their nest. Despite this horrific display of cruelty, cold steel cannot be poisoned, and our weapons decimated their number faster than they could abduct us. In the end, we managed to free most of our men, but the eightlegged beasts destroyed some of our supplies.
