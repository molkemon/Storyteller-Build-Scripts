The first Mechanim-Human interaction occurred when a Human hunter set a fire beside and attracted the attention of the Mechanim scout WalksAlone. WalksAlone, concerned at the idea that the fire would spread, approached the camp and stepped from the cover of the brush into view. The two figures stared at each other for a long moment, unsure of what to say or do, before the human extended an open palm, face up, towards the fire, where he was cooking part of his hunt. WalksAlone slowly approached, and took a seat opposite of the Human. He had no need to eat nor understanding of what the Human had offered, but accepted the gesture to mean an acceptance of his presence. He returned the Human's gesture with one of his own, offering wood onto the fire he could now see was controlled, and showed the Human how to stack the logs to channel the heat more efficiently. The Human showed WalksAlone how to butcher and skewer meat to evenly cook, and WalksAlone showed the Human how to adjust logs in the fire by inserting their hand and rotating the burning wood, but neither was as good as the other at these tasks. The Human offered some of the food to WalksAlone and took a bite to demonstrate it was ready to eat, but despite his best efforts WalksAlone could not manage to eat the food, which made the Human laugh. Though the two could not yet understand each other verbally, they conversed in simple ways involving these gestures, slowly building a vocabulary. By nightfall, they were asking each other where they came from, and roughly drawing maps in the dirt. The following morning WalksAlone followed the Human back to his village where he was introduced as a friend. This first interaction led to a long standing tradition of limited migration between Mechanim and Humans in which single individuals or rarely groups of two or three would leave their home communities and travel to the other, living there for months or years. WalksAlone did this frequently, establishing strong friendships with generations of Humans. Notes, Academician Prof. Raven, recording the legend of WalksAlone.
