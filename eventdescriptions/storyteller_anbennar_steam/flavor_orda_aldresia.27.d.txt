Like assassins, we evaded patrol after patrol of vicious vampiric hosts as we entered the gardens behind Bal Doston. Surveying the scene for a minute, we caught sight of a service elevator near one of the towers, a small pulley used to transfer copious amounts of food from the kitchen. A vampire lord found us within the pleasure gardens, but we dispatched him with ease as we approached the keep itself. The advisor, taking a moment of caution, slowly snuck up to the service crane before their body jerked slightly. They turned around, drawing their weapon as they pointed it at us. Behind them, a horned woman with wings and red skin turned visible as the eyes of the advisor glowed purple from being mind controlled or seduced.
