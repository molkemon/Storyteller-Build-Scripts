Civilization is a fragile thing. While a halfelf will live little longer than a human, the savagery of an ork does not dissipate in their descendants. Thus the halforks showed us through their assistance of their brethren. Despite their good blood, their lower natures prevailed. Perhaps this is the way of the world. The Castanorians collapsed into dark wizardry, the heroes of the War of the SorcererKing gave birth to petty lordlings, and those petty lordlings were overcome by the Greentide. Only we, cast out of the West for daring to do anything for what is right, were able to bind barbarity to the ends of nobility. We must not allow ourselves to become corrupted as our predecessors did.  To that end, we shall ensure that halforks may never rise again. They will be chained along with their brethren, even if their strength may be lesser and consequently shorterlived. It does not matter if they are descended from Lothane Bluetusk or a blackhearted slave owner; whether they fought against us or lived in peace. They are all full of idiot malice, seeking to bring about another Greentide and despoil Escann once more, either through force or foul plotting. We, unlike some fools who cry of them "living in peace", know that anything must be done to maintain order.
