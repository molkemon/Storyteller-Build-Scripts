The dwarves seemed to have rediscovered their old pantheon from before their empire fell. Hidden among these silly dwarven gods we have discovered something very interesting. Their stories refer to an entity known as Grobilazk the Twisted who, after discovering that Halanna (an obvious copy of Kroozuta Zemna) had created dwarves, grew jealous and stole the gifts of Joztralanna, using a stone and a cave chameleon to create us goblins from his own Image. Though usually a loner, Grobilazk was a very intelligent trickster, bestowing unto us the same intellect and ingenuity that have allowed us to survive in this world, before eventually going on to create the bugbears and hobgoblins to prove his power over the dwarves.  The dwarves spoke ill of Grobilazk, considering him to be a dirty trickster who plagued them with our presence for thousands of years, but they are clearly jealous of this god’s sheer creativity for creating a race as wonderful as goblins. It is obvious then that many shamans have already taken to spreading the name and teachings of Grobilazk the Twisted, for his existence has not only finally given us a creator god, but also explains our natural intellect and cunning. Shamans of Grobilazk teach that we were created to dominate the dwarves and to be his instrument of mischief across the world, encouraging us to create wild contraptions and perform diabolical pranks.
