Goblins and treachery—a more iconic pair is difficult to name. An old goblin saying claims that one should always carry three knives. one for enemies, one for friends, and one with which to stab yourself and then blame someone else. The tendency for goblins to backstab and betray each other has led to a great orgy of violence to spill out in the realm, reducing the infestation significantly, but to the greater detriment of the province.
