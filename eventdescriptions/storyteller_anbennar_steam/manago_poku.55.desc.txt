As the revelations of the Gene Pillar reverberate around our domain, we find ourselves looking at the world around us with fresh eyes, and not because we have scavenged different ocular receptors off a destroyed Mechanim body. Already, there is great discussion, and at times disagreement, over what it all means, and how it all fits together. Let alone what it entails for our future, or ourselves. This is acceptable. A journey is made of many singular steps. We have been pushed, stumbling, to take the first. Now we must choose to take the remainder, in a new direction.
