The rural landscape is quiet as a steelclad priest of Argezenna stalks the darkness. He is just one of many, seeking out the monsters in the dark that still dwell in the places beyond the civilised light of the country. His task is holy, and the fetid rot he spots ahead in a ramshackle encampment is his next target. He nods to the soldiers on his left and right, and they light their torches and draw their weapons. These creatures chose to hide like rats, so they will die like rats.
