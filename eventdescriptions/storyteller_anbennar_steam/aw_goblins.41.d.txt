Might makes right. Such is the law of the realm, and we need none other. It is not an idea foreign to the goblins who recently infested our lands, or at least so we conclude from the lavish tribute that just arrived in our capital accompanied by a small delegation from the skulkers. They are deeply apologetic for their trespass on the lord’s land, and hope this gift befitting their stature conveys the depth of their remorse. They also promise the finest of any jewels or metals they discover in their digging if we permit them to stay here. Will the ruler accept this arrangement, or should a warband sharpen their axes and get ready to drive them out?
