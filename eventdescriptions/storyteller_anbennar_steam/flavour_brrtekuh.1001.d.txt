It is time to deem who is worthy to bear the title of Ekxiemgo, the one who leads the Brrtekuh Council. On this occasion, powerful gnolls within the pack have gathered together to vote and plan out a combination of trials for the Oxh, Yzhil and Zakr, that they might compete and prove who is the most worthy.
