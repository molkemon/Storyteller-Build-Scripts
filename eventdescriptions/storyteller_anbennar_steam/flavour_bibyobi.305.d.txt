Back out in the field, nothing quite like it. I will say, the rations are much better this time around on account of all the fish in the river. This time it is one of them Ninka Nankas we are hunting. Even the Esteemed Leader seems scared of this one. There are lots of rumors about evil magics shooting from its eyes and the ability to control water. I think I am an openminded fellow, but a beast that can control water is too much even for my humble mind. Regardless, it seems the Esteemed Leader thinks we need to harry the beast from up the river to the shore where our navy can partake in its demise. I must confess, I am unclear on the details of how he plans on moving an invisible waterdwelling beast, though.
