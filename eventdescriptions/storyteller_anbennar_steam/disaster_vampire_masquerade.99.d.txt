As our troops move into the streets of the realm, they lay silent. Unspeakable horrors must have occurred here. Indeed, we have learned of what happened here from what few survivors remain. They have been hardened by many weeks spent fighting off frenzied vampires who continuously patrol in the night to find new prey to devour. However, the vampires, realizing they have run out of easy prey, have amassed into packs with simple, almost animalistic hierarchies. Slowly but surely, a gigantic swarm of vampires has gathered and is ready to leave the province to continue their blood frenzy elsewhere. Truly the conditions here are much worse than we had been led to believe, and there are reports of similar situations in nearby lands. The adventurer guilds stand ready to lend their aid in establishing order here, and wait only for the command.
