Our dream quest was successful. Our leader was shown a treacherous path through thickets of strangling vines, alongside hundreds of feet of flowers with poison potent enough to fell even a great beast with a single petal, and past swamps bubbling with noxious fumes, all guiding us to a clearing. No. An expanse. An almost unnaturally large and empty space opens up before us, but one could be forgiven for missing its enormity as the gaze is drawn naturally and entirely to a single massive tower in the center. It is probably the single most awe-inspiring structure ever encountered in our tribe’s history, even in its current desolate and overgrown state. One can only wonder what treasures may lie inside.
