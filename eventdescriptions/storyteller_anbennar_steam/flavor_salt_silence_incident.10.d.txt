Decades ago, we killed all imperial messenger doves when we broke from the Empire. But even in our defiance, the Imperial Court sends us overtures. Our diplomats, generals, and statesmen all report to us of the returning influence of the Empire in our domain. Other imperial princes like us also face similar pressures. But while some call it a suffocating presence, others speak of a protective embrace. Whatever the case, it is enchanting and overwhelming. The Empire demands an oath of fealty, and after all this time of cunning diplomacy we find ourselves the target of a coup.
