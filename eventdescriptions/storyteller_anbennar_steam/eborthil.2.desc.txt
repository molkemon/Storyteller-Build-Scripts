The seasoned warrior, Eborthil, recounts tales of a bygone era, speaking of a time when the land was scarred by conflict. They describe how the space was cleared out for strategic advantage, a necessary step before engaging the enemy. They speak of the Delian Battery, a formidable defensive structure, and the Tower of Tef, a landmark that once dominated the skyline. They detail the intricate tunnel systems beneath the fortress, used for both attack and escape, and finally, they describe the Toref Citadel itself, the heart of the enemy’s power.
