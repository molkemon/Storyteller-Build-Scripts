As the monarch strides into the arena, admiring their gilded longbow, they glance at the sky. No wind, cloud cover—it’s a perfect day for shooting. Across the way, they see their opponent. the scarred behemoth whose religious fervor is matched only by his strength. The monarch hoists their bow and takes a deep breath. The match begins. The devotee presses forth, using the traditional tactic of charge, fire, quick turn and make distance, repeat. The monarch gets no rest as arrow after arrow slams down, some so close they can feel their wind. The Caehn meets fire with fire, riding against the behemoth's shots with the raw courage Irdaeos demands. Finally, one of the two is too slow to end their charge, as an arrow pierces flesh, and…
