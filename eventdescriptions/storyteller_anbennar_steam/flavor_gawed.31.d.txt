The knights of the Orda Aldresia are bodyguards to the Emperor, and should be at their side at all times. But even great warriors can be taken unaware, and when the Gawedi infiltration force broke into the imperial palace it caught the Aldresians completely by surprise. As the slaughter of the capital garrison unfolded the famed knightly order was pushed back inside the palace. Intense fighting hallway by hallway ensued as the legendary knights were slowly separated from the throne room, barricaded within the palace's shrine. A new relief force from the imperial princes was supposed to arrive within the hour to shatter Gawed's armies and oust the Northern Eagle, but none of that would matter if the imperial forces could not hold the capital. If the knights of the Orda Aldresia failed in their duty, all would be lost. In one last desperate push, they rallied their brothers and made a single thundering push to the throne room. Cutting through the Gawedi in a furious whirlwind of steel and shot, the knights rumbled to the broken doors of the throne room and there saw the Emperor and their small dwindling bodyguard. The Aldresians fought valiantly. The Aldresians fought bravely. The Aldresians fought honorably. And the Aldresians died. Their commander felled two dozen Gawedi alone but their push slowed down under the tide of northern blades, and less than twenty yards from the Emperor the last of their guard fell. The last dying knights screamed for anyone to intervene, yelling at the Alenics to show mercy, but the fatal blow came anyway. The Emperor fell, nobly, at the end.
