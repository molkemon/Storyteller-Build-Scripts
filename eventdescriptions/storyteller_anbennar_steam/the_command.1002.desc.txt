Our recently conquered territories are mostly carved up and split between the Lower Commands, but a small part has not yet been granted due to a conflict between two Commands. Both claim that they have the right to this territory and both call for the Grand Marshal to give them what they are due. Both have evidence that can be best described as flimsy at best and fraud at worst, and it is up to the Grand Marshal to decide who will get the land and who will be angered.
