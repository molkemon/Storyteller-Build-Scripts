The expedition leader looks onto the site the scouts have found. In a small nook tucked behind a great junction of caverns, a small workshop has been found, with numerous fallen golems in front. Inside there is the small body of a gnome. The leader breathes heavily and removes his helmet in silent mourning. It is now beyond any doubt that Fognir was right. Mintbrick is dead. He fell, valiantly, from the looks of it; but in the end, he fell. The only thing strange about this situation is the sole remainder of the slaughter. a golem, unlike the others; a golem that is bashed, bruised, and broken, but there seems to be no major damage to all essential structures, especially the memory banks. Maybe this golem will divulge the secrets we had hoped to obtain from Mintbrick. It is time to get the golem to Khugdihr and see what it might have to tell us.
