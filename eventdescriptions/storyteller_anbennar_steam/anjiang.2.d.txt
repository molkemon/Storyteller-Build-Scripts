In any other circumstance, we would have failed utterly to beat the Yingzhen at their own game. Fortunately, in this circumstance we were aided by the Seers. Secrets are easier to find when given the places to look; and our agents have found ample evidence to prove that Yingzhen holds malicious intent. The plan seems to have been to infiltrate our Court of Envoys, replacing honest messages from our allies with extremist ramblings, ultimately aiming to alienate us from the rest of the League.
