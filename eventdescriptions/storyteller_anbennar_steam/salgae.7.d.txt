A sturdy centaur, draped in furs and snow, trudges through the brutal snowstorm. He is the leader of the Raging Blizzard, and as he marches he considers, his so called Great Caehn has never had to feel the cold touch of the north, nor has he had to sleep blanketed in a coat of ice. He decides, the Raging Blizzard shall demonstrate to the Caehn, that a simple chill can become a screeching blizzard.
