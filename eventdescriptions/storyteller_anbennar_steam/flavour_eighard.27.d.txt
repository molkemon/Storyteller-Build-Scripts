They attacked at dawn. A great host of Rzentur appeared out of the forests surrounding Svemelbut, and this time they were not alone. The great shape of Varlengeilt cast a shadow over the city as, ever so briefly, he blotted out the sun. The meagre force garrisoning the city stood no chance against them. They fought with fire, with Vechnogejzn, the ancient magical tradition of their people historically used in conflicts against fey. Within hours, the siege was over. The city was taken. Our forces are nearby, but it will take some time for them to arrive. We must hurry. The presence of Varlengeilt will surely cause their forces to grow over time.
