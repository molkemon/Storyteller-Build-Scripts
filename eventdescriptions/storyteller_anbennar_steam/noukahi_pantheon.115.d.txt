As the monarch and their entourage approach a crossroads, they see what is unmistakable. a priest of Mojobaa walking down a path in their direction. The shining armor he wears clinks as he boldly strides; a sword's handle peeks from his back, and slung around his waist is a proud smith’s hammer, accompanied by his many other tools. As the priest spots the monarch’s party, he waves them down. When all come close enough for talk, he makes a swift gesture of respect, after which he makes a request. allow him to accompany them to the next town, as they all seem to be heading in the same direction. The whole of the monarch’s party turns to the ruler, awaiting their decision.
