You have conquered Shamakhad on wargback. But you cannot govern it from wargback. Deima Cotiam, leader of the Sir Revolt, said this moments before execution. Our enemies were fools, dead fools. They supped so thoroughly from the teat of the elven empire that their worldview became likewise infantile, succumbing to delusions of grandeur and heroism. When we first thundered down the foothills, they looked behind us, believing that we were running from worse terrors—monster creations of the Dark Master, stowed away in the Serpentspine. They thought we were merely raiders, here today and gone tomorrow. They thought to sally forth on one last grand adventure, and respark the Age of Heroes. Now they think no longer. We remain. Unfortunately, the dotard Deima was correct in one respect. our methods of governance are underdeveloped, incapable of administrating a realm as large as Shamakhad. The Revolt of the North was proof of that fact. The Command may have evolved from traditional clan loyalty structures to more complicated chains of military hierarchy, but we will need to evolve further if we are to build the hobgoblin empire Moguwon envisioned. What happened in Sir may never be permitted to happen again—not merely that, its repetition must be made impossible. The gerunanin have shown themselves to be disloyal and dishonorable; in their hands, every blade is potentially a tool of treason. Therefore, let our first law for the conquered be this. from this day forward, the humans in newlyconquered territories will be stripped of any and all weapons. This includes spears, swords, guns, and all implements by which the Command might be threatened. Henceforth, we proclaim the policy of Disarmament.
