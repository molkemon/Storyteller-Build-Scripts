A familiar sight faces you at the end of the corridor. Once again the way deeper is sealed. Unfortunately, there does not appear to be any way around it; the few scouts sent out down side tunnels all seem to end up back at this junction. The only way deeper is through. You are familiar with how these seals work by now, having dealt with a few in this expedition and having read the notes of the prior trips, you believe you should be able to methodically work out a solution. Looking at your team, you start to progress down the corridor towards the door. One of your party unfortunately does not appear to have been paying as careful attention as the rest of you, and the ominous click almost manages to echo through the hallway. Everyone freezes, hoping it was a trick of the mind and not a warning of impending doom. Alas, runes start to glow on the walls and a translucent barrier forms behind your team, the rearmost member losing part of their sleeve as they were too close to the formation point. You circle up and await the onslaught. Suddenly multiple waves of energy launch from the door, each seeming to fill the entire tunnel and you all frantically start to respond.
