Recently, a local official has fallen in love with a local harpy. Fortunately, since we have managed to find a permanent understanding with them, this is merely a subject of interest for the people of the realm, and a significant part of our people wants the two to go all the way together. However, there is a legitimate concern that, if this is not stopped, this could lead to harpies getting an undesirably higher degree of influence in our country.
