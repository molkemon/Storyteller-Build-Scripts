A lord woke up to the blaring of horns and the clanging of anvils, which was confusing, considering that the nearest forge was far from their bedchambers. Outside the palace grounds, a procession arrived bearing a banner with the symbol of Azmirethazin. Along with them were wagons filled to the brim with armour and armaments, as a variety of smiths worked even then to ensure every piece of equipment was up to standard. There was enough equipment to arm four regiments of infantry, at the very least, and enough dwarves following the procession to equip them; each volunteer eager to wear such fine work, and willing to waive recruitment fees to boot.
