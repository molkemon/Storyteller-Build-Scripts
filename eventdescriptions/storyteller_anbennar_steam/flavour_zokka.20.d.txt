As within, so without. Zokka hears Zokka. Not in words, or thought. In understanding. A presence, immensurate and unlimited, curls through the ruler's veins like the self-flame, just as novel. Zokka, the first. Zokka, the last. Zokka, the Once And Future. Zokka who was, who is, who shall be. The moment passes. Zokka's breath returns, but it is no longer the ruler's own. Something has taken root. A gift. An idea. A purpose. As within, so without; one way or another.
