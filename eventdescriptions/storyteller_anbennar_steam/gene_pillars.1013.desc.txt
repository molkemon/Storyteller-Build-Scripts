In such a hostile environment, full of endless dangers and unknowns, there are few sights as welcome as the familiar. So when we breached yet another room with shields raised and blades ready, it was a relief to find that we recognized the place; we had already charted it during a previous expedition. The tension in the party unwound as we took a moment to relax, the constant danger of the gene pillar abating momentarily as we assessed our options. We could spend more time resting in this sanctuary, or we could press on, taking this as a measure of our progress.
