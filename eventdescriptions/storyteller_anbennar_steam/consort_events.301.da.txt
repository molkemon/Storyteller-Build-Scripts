When the consort married into the royal house of the dynasty, there were those who were skeptical towards the monarch inviting a young woman from a foreign realm to join the ruling family. However, time flies and it has now been over ten years since the union took place. While not everybody adores the consort, most have come to respect them as one of their own; it is highly unlikely that the wedding anniversary of the elevated couple could have gone by unnoticed, and a number of congratulatory letters, as well as gifts of varying splendor, have already been sent to the palace.
