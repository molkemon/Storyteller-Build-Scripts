The Tower Master screams in anger and despair. But the deed has been done. The spirit’s body fades and disappears from the roof. Manui looks towards the party and speaks. "The lock has worked. The Tower Master is locked away once again in the prison chamber. I will keep the lock hidden away; this time no one will ever break it again. Thank you for your aid." She walks to the body of the lifeless Wanderer. Gingerly, she kneels before it. Offering a Kanolowelen prayer, she places her hand on its chest. The wind picks up and roars on the roof. And the Wanderer’s body, after hundreds of years of unnatural life, dissolves into dust, carried away into the wind.
