Looking terribly sheepish, the leaders of our excavation at the Precursor ruins in the realm have come knocking with the most unfortunate news. Though they have kept digging for weeks now, they have yet to turn up more than a pittance of pottery, a few loose bones, and enough worms to give a small city's worth of people a nice fishing trip. Simply put, the ruin is ruined. We have picked it clean of anything valuable, turned every stone, and used it to the last. Unfortunately, it seems our investments in the province will have to be redirected to some other industry. Let us hope we can muster something to give even a fraction of the relics' worth.
