Though a nascent organization only recently given favour and trust by the lord, the responsibilities of the Shuimu Engineering Cadre are paramount. Foremost is their mission to keep our airfleet operational and to drive its development, for our balloons are our primary advantage over our rivals. The complexities and difficulties of doing so cannot be overlooked, and we must take care not to overstretch their capacities, lest we unceremoniously crash and burn.
