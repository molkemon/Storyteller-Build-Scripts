Right from the very first day we took residence in the Summer Palace, it caught my attention. Ruined, the stone carrying no trace of the gleam held by the brick gifted me, I nonetheless could not take my eyes off it. the Marble Gate of Shazsundihr. Not even a shadow of its former glory is left, in truth. the great doors opening onto the mountainside are halfrotted away, all decorations long gone. And yet I keep meandering around it during our hunts. What could be hidden inside? How glorious must be the great rooms chiseled out of the stone? The goblins tell me only ruin and death is left in those halls, but they just didn't have the aptitude for it when they invaded the hold; they only sought treasures. The architecture, the machinery, the thousand years old science must be there, or at least some traces of it, waiting to be rediscovered. Never mind the darkness, the mountain's calling me in.
