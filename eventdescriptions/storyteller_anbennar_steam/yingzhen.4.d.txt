It has been a very long time since the realm has held a legitimate election. In other cities of the domain, candidates for even the most humble role in the government are known to partake in large campaigns to sway public favor; but not here. Less important posts are traded almost freely, needing only a bag of coin or a sufficient favor to some shady representative to assure victory. The real positions, though, are handpicked by the reclusive masters of the cabal known as the Sinistral Chamber. Comprised of an informal court of the realm's most successful criminals, they have murdered, bribed, and enchanted their way into turning the domain into the perfect safehaven for their work.
