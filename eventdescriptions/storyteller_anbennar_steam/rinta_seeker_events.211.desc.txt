Almadhab has been staved off, with his Psychopomps vanquished through swords and magics. The once gaping infernal pit was sealed in a gigantic burst of life, leaving nothing behind but azure steam and a fading memory of a continent drenched in blood. Families return to their ancestral lands, tribes leave their weapons behind, children run out to graze the open fields, for the first time in many a century. There are no tears, not even a sign of sorrow. For the Ardimyans, all they want to do is smile. Enjoy a world clean of death. Cherish a life liberated from eternal pain. Bring those who died with them into a better future, hoping that they can see it from above. Almadhab himself has disappeared into nothingness, not a trace of his legacy anywhere.
