Silence, for months no word has arrived from the expedition. Fearing the worst, a search party has been sent for them, finding undescribable tragedy. Scattered bones, gnawed and torn and worse litter the entrance to the Precursor ruin beneath the ice. Bloodied knives and hacked bones. Nonsensical writings on the walls. The passage beneath to the deeper ruins was caved in by the strike of a pick, a skeletal hand still clutching at its hilt. Let us never return to this cursed place.
