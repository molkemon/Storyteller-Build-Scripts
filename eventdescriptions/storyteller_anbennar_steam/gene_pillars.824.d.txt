These central chambers are quite hard to effectively navigate through. As we make our way through them, the extensive damage creates what is essentially a claustrophobic labyrinth of passageways and broken rooms. And each time we feel the twists and turns of the worm’s movement, the tunnels threaten to collapse in front of, behind, or even worse, directly onto us. As we try to clear a path and secure the passage, it becomes ever more clear that we might be in over our heads.
