One of the more recent additions to the Hekuudzoungh Festival is the Hariigegh, where the rulers of every independent clan meet in a beautiful tent to enjoy delicious food and discuss matters of importance. The ruler of Nuugdan Tsarai and the ruler of Tsuista Khul have been discussing the future of the Tsuista Khul clan for quite some time now. The ruler of Tsuista Khul has requested our support, hoping our mighty warriors will prove instrumental in conquering greener pastures for them. The people of Tsuista Khul wish for better grazing lands for their livestock, and are ready to wage war for the city of Jincheng and all the fertile land that lies before it, and are preparing not only to mobilize their military but indeed their entire population for this daring endeavor. Should we grant them our support, we can expect significant military cooperation in the future from them. To cement the deal, the ruler of Tsuista Khul offers one of their children’s hand in marriage, should the ruler of Nuugdan Tsarai desire it.
