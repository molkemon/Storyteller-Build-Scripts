Long have we toiled in our efforts to unite this disparate land under one banner, but through it all a grand question has hung over the heads of the realm. When we have finished our epic task of forging a nation anew, what do we do next? This final problem has gnawed at the minds of the greatest scholars this nation has ever known, and with our task rapidly approaching completion it has become all too real. But there is one possible path which we may follow, one that has not been taken in over a thousand years: the reclamation of destiny and the rise of the first Harimraj reborn. But the Harimraj was built off the backs of the nobility, and we have just stripped them of their powers and left them a shadow of their former selves. In our haste to unite the domain under one nation, have we already forsaken the ultimate path to destiny? This quiet terror stalks the court in the quiet hours, and a shadow of doubt passes over the halls of the capital.
