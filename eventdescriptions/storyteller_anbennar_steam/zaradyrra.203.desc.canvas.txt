With no clear path to success and exhaustion setting in, many of the Shyarayan rebels had started to desert. Their leaders had nothing to offer them but a stalemate, as the war had taken too great a toll and offered little hope for the future. With their forces depleted, the rebel leaders have now offered us their surrender in return for some minor assurances. While the struggle to suppress any new violent Claw activities will certainly last a while longer, the time when they would be any threat to the state has now passed.
