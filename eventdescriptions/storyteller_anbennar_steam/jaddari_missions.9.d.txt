It has been many seasons since our expedition marched into the depths of the Dwarovar. Now finally they have returned. They come marching in over the great flagstones of the Dwarovrod, carrying sacks of gold and treasure back to the light of Surael. After a well deserved rest, they have been received by their lord to tell him about their adventures in the deep dark. Bards are already telling their tales and the priests have pronounced their great service a true honour to Surael.
