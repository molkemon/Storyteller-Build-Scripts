Everything is in place. Fortifications surround the temple of Graxarr, and when rituals bring about hostile demonic visitations, the priests have a way to contain them and extract their power. Huge pyres and cranes were used to feed the fire more easily and were assembled atop the temple. A road was built to receive the supplies and sacrifices to the pyres more easily. Then there is the Everpyre; no longer will it be on decentralized pyres along the temple. Now it will be on one spot, where it will be easier to control and feed the materials, whether mortal or inanimate objects.
