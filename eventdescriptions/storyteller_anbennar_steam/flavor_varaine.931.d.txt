Having decided to utilize the ancient necromantic ritual as the source of immortality for the potion, our researchers were finally ready to brew. Over the course of a few weeks, the alchemists meticulously prepared the base components for the potions, including the highly distilled potion base, the additives, the stabilizers and all other ingredients which they’d worked so hard to perfect. Then, when the core potion was prepared, several hundred individuals, chosen among prisoners, political dissidents, and other undesirables for the nation, were one by one subjected to the ritual, robbing them of their lives but generating the raw life essence that was then refined, and imbued into the potion. The finalized potion was brought forward to the lord, who took the potion and retired to their quarters. The following day, the lord emerged from their chambers greatly changed. Pale, gaunt, and ghastly, the lord’s visage, while not quite like that of a lich, still emanated the same frightful impression of unlife, and the aura of necromantic power surrounding them greatly helped elevate the feeling of unease in those who witnessed it. Even without any test, all alchemists seem to agree: The potion worked, though at what cost? While word of the process used to allow for their transformation is sure to get out and cause a ruckus amongst other nations, this is likely the least of our concerns.
