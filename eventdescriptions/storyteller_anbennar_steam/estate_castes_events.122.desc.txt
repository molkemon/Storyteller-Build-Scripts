A group of explorers has been scouting the dense forests in our lands for a while now, and they have made a baffling discovery. They came across a village of harimari who had completely secluded themselves from civilization. When approaching the village, two explorers were killed on sight, and the five others were taken captive. When asking the harimari why, they claimed that they were refugees who fled during a war in the past and have now become wary of outsiders. They now wish to be left alone, as they have grown accustomed to their solitary way of living. The explorers were then released and immediately traveled to the capital. We could respond in different ways. How do we want to respond to this village?
