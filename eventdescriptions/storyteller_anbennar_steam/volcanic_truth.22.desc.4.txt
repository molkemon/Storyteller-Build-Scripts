The matter of the Kiamoa is decided. Only a fool embarks on a journey without a map. The visions of the world beyond the Kiamoa were wondrous, but if we do not prepare for the arrival of those dwelling beyond the Kiamoa, we might not survive the encounter. We will focus on protecting ourselves, and only then will we send our ambitious, our courageous, and our daring to the lands beyond Insyaa, so that they might share the Volcanic Truth. The Volcanic Truth teaches that nothing is constant. Even the mighty Kiamoa was destined to fade one day, and that day has arrived. Now that our faith has decided on the proper path forward, we can begin to move towards a new world, with all its mysteries and terrors.
