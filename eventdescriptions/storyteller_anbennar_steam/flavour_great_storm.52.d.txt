We are currently in the midst of creating a network that can manifest strong gales along certain routes. We are not quite certain what we can use it for, but we will find out soon enough. Now, here is an interesting thought. Someone has suggested that the places of worship that we have seen inhabited by those Rukunai can act as a sort of amplifier. If we were to build some of our own, they could be an immeasurable boon to our projection of the winds. Now, this will be costly, but the Malukhuga have agreed to compensate the caehnate if these temples are built. We can alternatively choose to embrace more conservative ways of research.
