At long last, change has come. Fire sings through the lands of the mangrove trees, and the Taga'Ala now see the world through new eyes. The placid follies of the Endless Ocean have been cast off. what few of its traditions the Taga'Ala still practice have taken their rightful place beneath the rites of the Volcanic Truth. The Twin Gods are most pleased. We, their most devout servants, have done well. It was our perseverance and our force of will that made this great change possible, and our brothers in faith consider our great conquest as a sign of the Twin Gods working through us.
