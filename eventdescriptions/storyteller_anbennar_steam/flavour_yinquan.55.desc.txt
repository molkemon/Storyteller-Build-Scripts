Unfortunately, the annihilation of the officer class has not been without some complications. Killing traitors requires no time at all, but training replacements takes a considerable amount longer. During this time, the lack of large sums of the command structure has caused discipline within the ranks to break down. With no figures to guide them, the army has earned the title of being the "Brigands of Yanshen." As the lack of individuals to oversee logistics has set in, living off the land has become the dominant method of obtaining supply. With few to stop them, the soldiery has had the opportunity to indulge their greed; something they have taken to eagerly, for they take nearly everything not nailed down. With none to rally them, they have taken to breaking and fleeing as soon as they think their lives are endangered. Sadly, this is blood that must be shed. These problems will disappear in due course as we replace our officers with new, loyal individuals. In due time, the idea that our men are brigands will also be forgotten.
