We have so, so much more to learn. This is true of all the Hinuilands, from the highest Gene Pillar. But it is especially salient when we venture into the Fluvial Waterworks. We need to understand what these things do, how the planes of force below shape the land that lays above. How the yetactive sigils might be harnessed, or altered, and to what purpose. And even if that is done, there will be the yawning chasms of knowledge that may lay forever out of reach: how a working like this could ever be created.
