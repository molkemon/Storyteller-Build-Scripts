The arrival of Cannorians into Rahen has been a huge boon to our industry and trade. With increasing demands for Raheni goods like porcelain and spices, silver bullion has flooded our ports, and the merchants have grown fat on the riches of the trade. The Cannorians, longstanding trade partners now interested in further trading opportunities, have started to ask for a more permanent residence inside our cities to facilitate greater volumes of trade. This idea has met some pushback from some of our more suspicious advisors, as they fear that the foreigners may bring an unforeseen damage to our country; They have been  described as brash and ignorant of the long history and customs of the local populance. On the other hand, the Cannorian have different technologies and knowledge that we can learn from. They may be eager to trade that knowledge with us if we let them have their quarters inside our city.
