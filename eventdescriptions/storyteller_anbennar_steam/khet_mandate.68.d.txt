While Khets can be mostly correlated to negative events, some of them do not fit the trope of being a menace to society. In this case, a rare case of an extroverted and outgoing Khet is preparing for a tour via the Sorrow, organizing several festivities along the way. The people are eagerly awaiting the Khet, as many couples are waiting for this exact occasion to gain a blessing for their marriage from a god himself.
