Collecting tales from Escann was a difficult task. The hatred between them ran so deep, but as we wandered that wartorn land, we found four strange people tending their sheep. a goblin, an orc, a human, and someone in between. Such a peculiar sight they did make. But as we approached them for their story, truly, it was impossible for us to make a mistake. They told us of their village where the victims of the Greentide hid. They found comfort in each other and eventually shared love. But they were hated for their tolerance, chased from their homes by an angry mob. Now they wander below the Serpentspine, unable to find a single job. So we brought them back to Lelanàn, where they found their final place. And as they settled down for good, they were a beautiful mixture of race.
