Bard, bard, tell me about the Silmunas. They are gone, son, gone. But what happened? Where did they go? It is a sad tale, not for a cheery evening or a cheery lad. But I want to know. Please. Ah, what is there to say? They were betrayed. They fought. All the way up the Doveroad, they fought, dying for their throne and for the rightful Emperor. And they lost. But then what happened? Then what happened? They died. And a piece of Anbennar died with them. But what happened to the dead ones, the dead Silmuna? I will not tell a child a tale of corpses. Come on, please, please, just answer and I will not have any more questions, I promise. Ah, lad. Adénn, and all those who had fallen before, they were hidden. We could not give them victory, but we could at least allow them a peace in death, the one they had always sought in life. Where? Where? Do not be so quick to break a promise, child.
