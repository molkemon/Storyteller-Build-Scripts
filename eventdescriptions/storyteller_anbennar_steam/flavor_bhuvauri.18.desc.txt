As dusk came to the Porcelain cities, so did our Adasa guard, slipping from the walls and making their way through the streets into certain homes at certain times. For some whom they visited, they promised coin in exchange for their support in these trying and turbulent times, for others, they would be removed from the equation, weeds being rooted out of this great garden we build to span the river. At dawn, these strings of pearls shall become one under the Brass Dragon.
