As the ruler enjoys the momentary respite of an empty court, they hear a commotion outside the courtroom. The ruler instinctively reaches for their sword, but as the source of the commotion bursts into the room it becomes clear this is not a threat, this is a gift. A large amount of gnomes enter the throne room carrying various items packed in wrapping paper that are laid out on the table before the ruler. The gnomes explain that they are grateful for the continued support of their people by the Reverian state and would like to return the favor. The ruler begins unpacking the gifts and concludes that despite half of the gifts coming from the Creeks being various forms of pranks, the overall collection of gifts is a remarkable collection of useful items. The Gnomes explain that they have collected various experimental tools that can aid in the administration of the realm.
