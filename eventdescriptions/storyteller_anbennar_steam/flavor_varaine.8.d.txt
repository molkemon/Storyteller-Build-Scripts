Gnomish artificer regiments and the successes of the adventurers during the Greentide gave us some ideas about using potions in our army. Currently, two main lines of thought permeate our military leaders as to how this implementation might be done. either as a concentrated boon to our most important knights and military units, with the caveat of large parts of our army being left behind, or as a more widespread tool to be used by the entire army, at the cost of having to divert some of our other production to supplying the needed potions for the army.
