One of our people's favorite stories is that of Malie, a great hero who lived long before the Day of Ashen Skies. Known for having crafted the first common code of laws across all Kanolowele, she serves as an inspiration to our people; a model of what a good life looks like. Recently, though, some of our people have started to doubt if she actually existed. Is it really possible for a human to be as wise a judge as a Dragon Turtle? How can we know the truth about events that happened so terribly long ago? We already sent an expedition to ask the Dragon Turtles, of course, but they refused to answer any questions about Keala, or even to explain why they abstained. It appears that we must solve this problem ourselves.
