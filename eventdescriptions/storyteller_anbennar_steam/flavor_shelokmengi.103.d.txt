My monarch, good news from the Yenaze Mezhi. This year’s harvest has come in, and our reports show that we have a modest surplus. Our sizeable fields are lush with healthy grain, bolstered by the Samayebaka's prayers. The people are wellfed and happy, as our granaries can spare some grain for a stipend to the poor and needy. This has allowed our populace to thrive, and many predict we will see a slight increase in the population should this continue.
