Now this is an assignment. No more trudging through a mosquito infested jungle worrying about what's for dinner, you or the rations. Just a nice city with nice people, nice food, and nicer beds. Garanke may not be the grandest city, or the richest, certainly not the cleanest, especially since we arrived, but it might just be the friendliest. The folk of Garanke have welcomed us with open arms, and we have heavily patronized their taverns and market stalls in kind. Now, there are the rumors of the leopard men who hunt at night and kill all manner of high-ranking officials that we have been sent here to root out, but past experience tells me that nothing so absurd could exist in this jungle.
