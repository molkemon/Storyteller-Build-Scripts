They say that long ago, when a pack killed their sworn enemies, they would celebrate by making cups from their skulls. There was a loud chorus of shrill laughter from the surrounding gnolls. This meeting was far from the prying eyes of the capital, in a wooded clearing near one of their estates. Land granted generations ago when the glory of the Ivemkverag was at its height. Dozens of noble houses had sent sons and fathers to this meeting to discuss the future. Though they had been ousted from their high positions, they still clung to their trappings, their wealth, and their power. They each carried the colors of their family dyed into their fur, adorned with gold, finery, and masterfully crafted weaponry. "Yes. That’s what we should do. We just need to kill that upstart cur, then everything can be made right again. Why should we, the founders of the nation, tolerate being tossed aside this way?" Murmurs of agreement, although more subdued. These were not childish words to be taken lightly. "We should swear then, each of us. We will gather our warbands and companies, and hire more besides. We all have long ties to mercenaries and soldiers of fortune all across the Divenhal. We will gather an army, and just as Xanzerbexis led our ancestors against the tyranny of Busilar, we will throw down this despot. What say you?" From all over the forest there were shouts of "Down with the Tyrant," echoing long into the night.
