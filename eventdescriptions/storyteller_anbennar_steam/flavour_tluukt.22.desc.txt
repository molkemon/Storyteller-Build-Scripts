When Jaher purged the gnolls from Bulwar, the hope was that they would be forever free from their taint. This hope was in vain; as the Phoenix Empire crumbled around them, the gnolls steadily reasserted their monstrous influence over the remote borderlands. They were content to keep them that way; remote, but now the villainous ruler of that country strikes at the very heart of them all, felling the city of Traz Buranun with their demonic powers. For days afterwards, news of the city's fall was sparse; but with a flood of refugees the reality of the matter becomes clear. The city has been reduced to ash, and the bulk of the population subjected to a life of slavery. The survivors, few as they are, huddle in their streets, haunted by the memories of their fair city burning away. The meaning of this all is clear; the gnolls of that country are no mere bandits, but the embodiment of Malevolent Dark, demons who threaten to consume light itself.
