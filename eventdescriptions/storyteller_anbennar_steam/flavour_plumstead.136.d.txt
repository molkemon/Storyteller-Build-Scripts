"Why does it smell like that?" Biscuit was confused as the smell of something extremely sweet filled the air of the wagon. He was confused as the salt in the barrel was supposed to keep the meat dry, not sweeten it. Milon, who was passing the wagon, came up to see what was going on. He inspected the barrel and decided to taste the contents. "This isn’t salt? It’s sugar." Biscuit was confused, yet in a strange sense also amused. For some reason, some fool had 'salted' the meat with sugar. It seemed to be that this was the only barrel which had been filled with sugar, and so the two laughed it off. Biscuit was even able to make something out of this sweetened meat, even when its taste was a bit unusual.
