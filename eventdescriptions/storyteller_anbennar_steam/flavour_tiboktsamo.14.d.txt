They said Kolo's Chosen would have the strength of an elephant. They said he would have the courage of a lion. They said his spirit would be a roaring fire, that burns away the injustices of the world and makes whole what was broken. But fires do not burn forever, and on this terrible day the spirit of the Kolovadoy has been extinguished. Now, the people of this realm sing solemnly. They sing in the streets, and from their homes. They sing praises, they sing lamentations, they sing until their throats are hoarse. Today shall be a day of mourning. But there is still much to do. When tomorrow comes...
