In a small town on the borders, there’s a man who nobody likes. He underpays his fellows for work, he’s never seen inside the temple, and he curses anyone out at the slightest provocation. To put it quite simply; he’s a poor neighbor.  Recently, after bumping into this miserly man, a goodman was cursed out. Rapidly afterwards, he became sick, and passed on. Now, nearly the entirety of the village has mobilized to see the man hanged, claiming his curse is evidence of magic. However, save for this, the inquisitors had already investigated this man prior, and found nothing. In the case that he is innocent, the conflicts could be stopped by simply taking the man and moving him somewhere else.
