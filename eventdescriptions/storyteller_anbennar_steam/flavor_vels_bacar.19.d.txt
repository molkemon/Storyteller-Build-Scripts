The monarch stood before the full Panykorun in session, rising from the throne with pride. They were all here. from Amacimst to Grebsnieth, and from Brelar to Adbrabohvi, all the members of the Panykorun were in place. A rare sight indeed, a promising one. They were ready. "I once swore upon the Ynn to listen to the Panykorun and govern with it, just as it would rule with me, until the preparations were ready for me to leave this crown. Until we all, indeed, were ready for it. And I think we are. Under the guidance of my noble family, the yen Bacar, this assembly has seen the Ynn unite and modernize, grow and prosper, advance forward and never grow stagnant. The world of old was ruled by imperial tyrants and broken by rebellions and disputes, while the Panykorun guarantees reason, advancements and prosperity so we may never return to such dark times." With this said, the monarch walked down to the center of the Hall of Colors and, with one solemn gesture, left the Panykorun crown onto it. An enchanted crystal cover was then placed on top of it, so it would never degrade, and the members of house yen Bacar sat down at their new seats in the Panykorun amongst cheers.
