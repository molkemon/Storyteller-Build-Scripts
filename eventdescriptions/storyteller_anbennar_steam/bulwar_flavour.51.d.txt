Tell the ruler; Your servant Veharia Faranzuir sends the following message. I am writing this letter regarding an accident that occurred while I was traveling through the hills between Aqatbar and EduzWez. It concerns the cult known as the Five Thousand. Let me talk about them, as I have recently contacted some of them and started to understand the peculiarities of their faith. Followers of the Five Thousand do not merely pray or rest in contemplation; they act. They spend every spare hour of every day since infancy with training. When reaching adulthood, they seek to emulate the warriorheroes of old and their selfless service; as such, they seek to protect the innocent and fight against evil; be it wild beasts, monsters, or those who dare to harm the weak. I managed to experience them in action when they protected the trader caravan I travelled with through the hinterlands of Bahar from a bandit attack, even though they did not seek nor receive a reward for their deed. Afterwards, while I accompanied them, I learned further details about the Five Thousand; they constantly roam in small groups across the countryside and believe unity is the true way to stand against the dark. They fight and train to confront the world's current evils, taking a firm stand in emulation of their heroes. I sent you this missive because I am convinced they would be a great boon to our kingdom.
