The deliberations in Ameion have been a long and arduous process. Groups have formed, some wanting to hold on to the memory of Laskaris, while others saw the need to adjust to the changing times. After days of deliberations and multiple physical altercations, Ameion has wisely concluded that they need friends to brave the storms ahead. Together with Deyeion and the spurned children of Kheiós, they will unify into something far greater than the sums of its parts. No longer will they be playthings of the decadent cities of Kheiós. They will be their own power and soon eclipse those stuck-up island dwellers. This day will be remembered as the birth of the United Echate League.
