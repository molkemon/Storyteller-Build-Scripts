The lord. During our excavations in the province our miners breached a wall into a great green cavern. Blessed by Halanna, her breath giving life to everything there, this fertile place has helped our efforts greatly. Our workers have more food and with their bellies full of mushrooms they work exceptionally fast. Some workers have even set up a field brewery, which has certainly restored the vigour of everyone in our company. We are certain these peripheries will aid us even after the preliminary excavation is finished.
