The centaurs of Nuzurbokh, of the entirety of the Irdülhain, are not too keen on living the sedentary life. They do understand how it works, however, and they do know that all that has been sown shall be reaped. However, a very particular tree has borne fruit today. Dignitaries from beyond the eastern mountains, the Stone Gate of Koraff, arrived today, my caehn. A delegation not only consisting of humans, but of centaurs as well. Daring. Daring indeed. Their reason for approaching is the Storm Oath we stand under. The ruling dynasty is about to explode in anger and protestation. Well, we do not necessarily stand under the Storm Oath; it is more of a recommendation. This does not assuage the one who calls himself caehn. At all. Look, it is something the Storm King has promised them, and you wish to become one yourself. Is that not true, my caehn? Crisis averted. It is not as though they demand much; fifty centaurs is all that is requested. It would legitimize your aspirations. Give them the worst fifty soldiers we have.
