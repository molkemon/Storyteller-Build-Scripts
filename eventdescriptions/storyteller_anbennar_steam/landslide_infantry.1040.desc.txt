It happened without warning. The ground shook, a sound so loud it deafened all who heard it, and then the roof of the cavern seemed to split as fire spewed from the secondary chamber. All things considered, it was only a minor eruption. Still, it has made the chamber unusable for the time being, and construction has halted in the wake of this disaster. We are fortunate to have lost less than a hundred workers, if the injuries are not included.
