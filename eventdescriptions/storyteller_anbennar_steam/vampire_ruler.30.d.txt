Many scandals haunt the halls of the court in the capital. There is the story of the monarch who turned himself into a frog while practicing new magics, sure to draw a laugh at any banquet or ball. There is the story of the cruel consort, who personally designed a variety of special chambers since sealed off from the main dungeons, which draws a shiver from listeners even in the hottest summer. And now, a new story shall be added to the list, even if it is only spoken of late at night or in brightest sunshine, with a hushed tone. the feast of the monarch. For no courtier, no servant, no advisor, is soon to forget the sight they awoke to this morning. The main hall ran red with blood, as if all a banquet's wine had been dumped upon the floor. Ghostly pale corpses were scattered around, like raisins in a cake. And at its center. the massacre's architect, its chef, its creator.
