Having decided to study harpies, our researchers headed off to the hills of Eastern Bulwar to find suitable subjects. There, they were able to quickly develop amiable relations with the local flocks and begin their work. After a few weeks of research though, as the research grew closer and closer to its conclusion, the researchers' necessities reached the limit of the harpy's hospitality, and several not so subtle diplomatic missives were received pushing Varainé to either call their researchers home or properly compensate the harpies; though the wording on these missives seemed to imply that this repayment could be done in various ways. Being this close to finishing their research, the researchers urged Varainé to make a deal with the harpies to let them finish their work.
