Some things are simply naturally beautiful. Without any help from us, nature is able to make things we can only stare at in awe, and one such marvel has been revealed to us. Our chroniclers have remarked on the cascading water, the unique tree trunk shapes, and even the beauty of the very stone and earth. By exposing this place of natural beauty and connecting it to our current efforts of refurbishment, we have surely contributed to the health of the mangroves as a whole.
