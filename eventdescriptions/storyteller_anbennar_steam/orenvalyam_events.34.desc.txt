The integration of so many Cannorians into Orenvalyam has led to the tales of Corin, their recently ascended wargod, to spread throughout the faithful. While many still look to her worship as having justified countless atrocities and colonial invasions, it is difficult to deny the achievements and righteous qualities of the goddess herself. Through a great act of sacrifice, she slew the tyrannical Dookanson, and through hope and good morals, inspired many to the cause of true justice. Some Oren Nayiru, mostly of Cannorian origin, calling themselves the Campurana Corintar, or Sect of the Corintar, are saying that they wish to form a new knightly order of crusaders, not fighting for bloodthirst or base ambition, but for Dawn, which they believe Corin was aligned with. While the Kavalalists support it, the Monasticists warn that they would still be too close to retreading the example of the Exemplars and Istralorians. Instead, they say Corin should be worshipped for her shield, not her sword.
