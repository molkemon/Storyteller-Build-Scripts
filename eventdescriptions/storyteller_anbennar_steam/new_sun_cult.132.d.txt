The tolerance of heretics in the New Sun Cult is another matter that has largely been left up to individual leaders to decide upon. This can be dangerous, however, when one lord believes that they must be persecuted at all costs, and another is willing to tolerate them in their city. This often leads to wars between otherwise peaceful, Suraelrespecting, neighbours. We should use this opportunity to decide what to do with the heretics in Bulwar, and beyond, so that when the time comes, all know the correct path, and no one can complain about how they were handled.
