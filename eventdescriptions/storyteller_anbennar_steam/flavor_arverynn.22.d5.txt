Creak… thump. A long convoy of trade carriages proceeded through the woodlands, announced by the sound of their large sturdy wheels and the neighing of their horses, while the roofs of white cloth covered its occupants from the Sarda sun. Unbeknownst to them, the infamous bandit leader, Talvyr Nizelynn, was on the prowl. Schwing… THWACK. A rush of arrows darted from all sides, snuffing the life of the convoy's guards. The bandits wasted no time jumping up and surrounding the convoy. "Great job, my fellows… now, let's search the interior of these caravans." Talvyr could not say his second sentence when he was interrupted by the agonizing scream coming from inside a caravan. It was the voice of one of his companions. "Momir." Talvyr called out, entering the caravan only to see his lifeless friend lay in a puddle of blood. "You will pay for this." Talvyr unsheathed his knife, looking for his opponent when SWING. Down from caravan's roof he jumped, dressed in simple clothes of linen and leather which concealed his muscles, cutting off the bandit leader's head with a single strike of his blade. Talvyr's head rolled to the ground, and his bandits scattered as the convoy's survivors began fighting back, killing some of them in the process. Months later, the mysterious banditslayer showed up at the gates of Arverynn, and called out for the ruler. "Your title, your name. Ever since Sarda bandits slew some of my friends, I only desired revenge on the Sarda people. To see them beaten and brought in chains. And wherever I ask, you, ruler, are the biggest enemy of a free Sarda. So let me join you, so I can kill and send even more of your people to their deaths." The ruler, a bit surprised by this logic, was not entirely taken aback. In fact, they strangely felt a sense of… kinship? And so they stepped out, walked up to the mysterious banditslayer, and stared him down… before they both grinned and shook hands warmly.
