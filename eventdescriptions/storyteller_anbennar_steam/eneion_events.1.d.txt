The Eneioni minister of foreign affairs said they would send an offer to their sovereign, the mightiest of the great kheions. an offer of cooperation that would give them the ability to declare war without their intervention, while reassuring their loyalty to them. The monarch, with a look of skepticism, asked a simple question, "Are you sure you will be able to convince the Degakheioni of this proposal?" The foreign minister, clearly confused, responded, "My team of diplomats are the greatest across The Cleaved Sea. We have made many a deal with the barbarians, and the Degakheioni have agreed to our proposals before. Why would you doubt us now?" "Because," the monarch said in a concerned manner, "it won’t be just your head upon a spear if you were to fail. This is perhaps one of the most important diplomatic offers Eneion has ever made. The consequences of a rejection are immense."
