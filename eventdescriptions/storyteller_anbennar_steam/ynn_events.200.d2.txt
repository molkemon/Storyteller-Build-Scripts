A young swashbuckler has recently graduated from the School of the Riverblades, passing the final exam with several honours. While at this point a young Riverblade would leave this year to seek employment across the land, this particular individual wishes to stay in our capital and aid our lord’s benevolent rule, a wish school instructors assure us is backed by actual talent. Renowned for their elegance and affinity to water magic, Riverblades undergo strict training to combine the deadliness of a warrior with the grace of a calm water flow; one such individual in our ranks will surely impart an example of discipline on our troops.
