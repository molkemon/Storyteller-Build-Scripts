It is obvious that whatever sentimentality our empire still has for the naga has led us to fail in crushing their movement when we had the chance. The naga protests have curdled into riots, and the rioters have formed their own command structure under a leading figure in the provinces outside of our lands. The naga leadership of the insurrections has been issuing prophecies to their band of rioters left and right, and has promised each and every one of them a place of wealth and prominence once the Anzara Empire falls, as it is prophesied to do once it meets their forces on the field of battle. The naga have forced our hand: the only way to respond to this delusion is with blood and violence.
