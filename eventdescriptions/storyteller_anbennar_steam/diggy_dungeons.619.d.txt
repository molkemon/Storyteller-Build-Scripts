You assume position as ordered and the fighting ensues. However, you quickly notice that the arachnids have long since adapted to the environment. Their moves are unfathomably quick and their fangs are not only poisonous, but seem to have no problem getting through the armor of your party. What you initially assumed was going to be a short skirmish turns into a prolonged fight, and when the last spider finally dies, you notice that quite a few of your men have lost theirs as well.
