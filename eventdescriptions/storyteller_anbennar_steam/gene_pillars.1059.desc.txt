Within the room are rows and rows of potion bottles. The bottles appear to be separated into four colors: red, blue, green, and yellow. Should we leave them alone or should we try to consume them? They seem to have been kept in excellent condition. They are artificially cooled, and magical fields that seem fully operational appear to be keeping them fresh. Still, it is difficult to know the effect of such substances; they could still be dangerous after all this time. Many miracles can be found in the pillars of the Precursors; perhaps this is one of them.
