While the Amlharaz session technically began when our lord struck their gavel, in reality our capital has been buzzing with whispers and rumours for weeks. For nothing leads to mystifying webs of alliances, a furious exchange of favours, and the formation of bitter rivalries as when the Amlharaz debates matters of internal politics. For all the complex schemes, however, the first Dwarf to speak would have us focus on a matter that unifies the Dwarves: caring for our veterans, especially those wounded or maimed in the line of duty. Their voice is quickly drowned out, however, by a faction who believes the bureaucracy must be expanded to properly administrate the growing Empire (and they note they have already drawn up the forms to do so). Opposing them in turn are the representative of Holds who would have our lord instead listen to those Dwarves of exceeding brilliance and skill, and grant them the authority to cut through seas of paperwork as they deem necessary to ensure the proper functioning of the Empire. Amidst the bickering, one Hold's representative sidles up to our lord, and slyly suggests that if the lord were to devolve some taxation powers to the Holds, all this discord might just blow away. The Amlharaz grants each Hold a vote; but at the day's end, it shall speak in a single voice. Which issue will our lord focus AulDwarov on?
