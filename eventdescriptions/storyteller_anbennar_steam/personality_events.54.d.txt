During a banquet our lord got into a discussion about food and sweet delicacy. While most of the involved parties remained steadfast in their assessment, arguing the strength of the local cuisine, the lord made an awkward statement when they let slip their opinion; 'I always thought a neighboring country had the best cheese platter. Ours are completely tasteless by comparison.' While our chef was gravely insulted, the lord of that country must have had spies present at the event as one of their emissaries later delivered us a large crate of cheese, completely free of cost.
