The last squabbling and grumbling have settled after the end of the final Debate and the sun now sets on Medurubar. Many of the delegates have gone out to enjoy the festival which has been prepared, great fires lighting the city with tables of drinks and food and many games to enjoy. Others, however, are already preparing to leave the city, disgruntled by the lack of cooperation and consensus during the Council Debates.  Though many things were decided upon, it was not as unanimous as many would have hoped, with plenty of disagreements and arguments over the decisions made. There will be enough support to ensure the results are enforced but not enough that we can truly say that Bulwar has been unified in the New Sun Cult faith. Hopefully, in time, the others will come around to our side as well. In the meantime, we must put our faith in Surael and his Chosen to guide us.
