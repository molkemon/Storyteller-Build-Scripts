The isle of Egstrel has always been a desolate place, as far north as one can go in Eordand. Few have dwelt here, and those who do live a quiet life of fishing the frigid waters. But recently, these fishermen have discovered something quite curious: a smooth and clear substance upon the beaches after a storm. Melted by the intense heat of lightning, sheets of curious onesided glass are produced from the sand of the beaches, quickly decorating houses in Marathmas. From the inside of one's home, one can view outside, while the Frozenglass prohibits outsiders from viewing inside. The sleepy island of Egstrel has become known across Eordand for Frozenglass, a true gem of the north.
