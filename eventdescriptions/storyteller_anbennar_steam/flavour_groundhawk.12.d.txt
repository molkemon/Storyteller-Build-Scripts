Goblins are generally known to be a cowardly, deceptive, and smelly lot, best kept away from civilized society. It seems though that in the chaos of the Greentide a bunch of them got mixed up with Cannorian society and somehow learned how to act like people. A realm of such goblins, calling itself Orvitzyen, has been sending delegations to various Cannorian kingdoms, asking for them to improve their view of goblins and especially their treatment of the many goblin refugees that have been swarming the cities on the border to Escann. Even the ruler has gotten such a delegation in their court. While their requests seem ridiculous, their surprising knowledge of customs, culture and the Regent Court has caused many high people to challenge their assumptions about the intellectual and behavioural limitations of goblinkind.
