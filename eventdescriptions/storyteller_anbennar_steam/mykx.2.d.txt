With the Calasanni unwilling to commit to these isles, as they have run away, being enslaved or dead, they are now ours. Despite our reputation, Gnolls can also be farmers; some of our people dream of being able to settle down, raise a farm, with some slaves to toil the fields and raise a family. They still take the occasional drugs and booze, of course, but they mainly desire the rural life. It won’t be hard to attract some settlers from the mainland with the promise of free land and cheap slaves.
