This was foreseen. It was fated to happen. The happiest day in all of centaur history. That dreaded day, which the monarch was once looking forward to. A day brought about by the most egregious misinterpretation of the faith to date. It was not willful. An honest mistake born of desperation. Was it not apparent that we were punished for our misdeeds? But yet, this is disavowed by all others. It is only we Star Riders who acted along that doctrine, driven by my speeches towards a twisted path. I have no choice but to attend the conclusion. I wish I didn’t. What matters is the conclave, the codification of faith, the promise of every single shaman’s voice being allowed to speak in this assembly. A chance to mend the sects of the plains for the first and final time. And when the time comes, and they try to crown me as caehn of the plains, as future Storm King ruling over centaurkind, I will refuse. We have paved the way towards the present, but only the shamans can lead us. This would always happen. My choice regarding who will rule us is set.
