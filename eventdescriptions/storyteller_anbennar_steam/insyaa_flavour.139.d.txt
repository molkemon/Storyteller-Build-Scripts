A mechanim's carvings can depict many things, from great triumphs we wish to display to the world to great tragedies we wish to ensure that are never forgotten. But of all the things we carve on ourselves, the thing that binds them is that they are things we want others to see. every carving is designed to elicit a reaction, whether it is astonishment or sorrow. One reaction we have not tried to elicit from the other peoples of Insyaa yet is horror, which can be a powerful force in itself. The other peoples are often very fleshy. They can be made to turn away easily from some superficial damage to our torsos that is painted in the right colors, to say nothing of how they react when they see the scenes of violence, gore, and terror that we can now carve on our bodies. Our artificers have designed them to have maximum psychological impact on our foes. From how the organic peoples have reacted so far, it appears to work very, very well.
