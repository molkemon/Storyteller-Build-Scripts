Kubrak and his expedition moved further down the depths, occasionally stopping for breaks. The group stops at an incomplete dugout cave and camps there. Kubrak munches on his fried fungi rations while keeping watch on the surroundings. A little precaution never hurt anyone. Alas, his efforts are wasted as the enemy announce themselves with shrill screams, echoing through the dark caves, which he grumpily reciprocates with a bow and bellow to his companions, followed by a chain of curses as they hastily assemble themselves. Decent at best. But he will drill them later. For now, there is glory to be had.
