Our attempts at keeping our western border defenses strong are faltering. In the borderlands, terrorbird raids continue to strike at our infrastructure. Worse, there are whispers of a new leader spreading among the local communities. Travelling from village to village, he is rallying the Shuvüüsh people, calling for a return to their independence. Utilizing their clan’s trademark cunning diplomacy and opportunism as well as the wisdom of the previous leaders, he has brought the tribesmen to a frenzy that may soon boil over. And it is unclear if the might of the monarch will suffice to stem the upcoming deluge.
