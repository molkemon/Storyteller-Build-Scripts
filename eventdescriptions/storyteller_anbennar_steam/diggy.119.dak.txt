The dwarves of Shazstundihr have dug deep and uncovered many lost regions of the hold. With the greatest of the marble quarries uncovered, a steady flow of traders once more crosses the Marble Gate, bringing dwarven goods and dwarven influence back to Bulwar. This imitation of the ancient hold has not gone unnoticed by its population; even now, the flag of Shazstundihr is flown alongside our own banners. It seems the hold is beginning to consider itself a nation, something we may have to deal with.
