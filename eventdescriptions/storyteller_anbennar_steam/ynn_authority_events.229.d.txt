Today a pilgrimage that recently set out from our lands to reach one of the Great Ynnic Dams has returned to us. However they return not in joy and religious ecstasy from seeing such a monumental sight, but in frustration, anger, and sadness. It seems that the local ruler of that domain has decided to turn our poor pilgrims away at their gates. The ruler might suspect them to be spies or some other such nonsense. While the less religious among our courtiers have simply shrugged and moved on, a powerful force within our court urges our glorious ruler to not let this stand. If the ruler did this once, they may do it again, and no true ruler could ever allow such dishonor to stand.
