The monarch of the realm sighs dejectedly as the procession before them finally ends. Another festival, another holding of court, another religious matter. Try as the ruler might, the monarch cannot keep up with the necessities of running a country. Maybe it was a poor upbringing, maybe an accident, or maybe just a lack of inherent ability, but the ruler knows they are not quite capable enough for this position. As advisors retire to their quarters and the monarch retreats quietly to their chambers, a voice calls out, so light that it is almost carried away by the wind. It calls for the ruler, beckoning them around a corner. From the darkest corner of the room, two small glowing eyes peer into the monarch's very soul, seeing them for all they are. The eyes glimmer warmly with a seeming friendliness as the same faint voice returns. It knows the ruler struggles with the role placed upon their shoulders. It can help ease this burden. All it asks for is a friend to the ruler, someone to rely upon in times of need.
