Where once a man would only produce meagre amounts of cotton, silk, and other fabrics, now machines have greatly bolstered this process; every man produces the yield of a dozen. Across the world, Bhuvauri fabrics can be found in every port and market, and in blessed Sramaya itself, the people have taken to using silk as wrapping, and cotton as packing for cushions and bedding. Never in any age of the world has the common man been able to use what was once a luxury item.
