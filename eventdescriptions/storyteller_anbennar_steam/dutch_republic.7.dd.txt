Despite the monopoly the Sparrows hold over the government, our nation has seen a current of entrepreneurial ideals recently. Peacocks have viewed this as a sign that our nation may very well be on its way towards shifting the balance of power away from the Sparrows. Down on their luck harpies flock to the glamorous shows of the Peacocks, dreaming of one day making it big through skill and acumen. Some view the lord and the traditions she embodies as a barrier between themselves and the true freedom they desire, and that the Merchant Matriarchs offer a path forward. They dream that all vestiges of the old world may be cast off, allowing anyone to fly high and free on glittering winds.
