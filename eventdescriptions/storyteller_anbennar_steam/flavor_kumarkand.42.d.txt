A faction of the revived Kumarkandi Cult has now developed a new focus on ritual. They speak of the idea of becoming Chosen among the faithful and leaders of the Garden through martial means, just as Dimuzi once did. This new "Cult of the Gumar" meets with our approval, and we will support it as part of our ongoing religious reforms. Its primary benefit, aside from refining the Old Sun Cult in these lands into a more properly Karqaslu religion, will be in increasing recruitment to our military staff.
