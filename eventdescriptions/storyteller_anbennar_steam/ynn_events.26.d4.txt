It is done. We have crushed the crazed mob of the Dragon Cult, and slashed our way through a province's streets, reaching the palace of deviancy where the Dragon Prince was being raised and revered. The creature didn't wait for us any longer, and perched itself on the roof of the palace, growling at our warriors before spitting a cloud of flames at them, repelled by the water shields cast by our mages. Our lord stood at the front, wielding a spear and charging at the beast. Once the dust settled, the dragon lay dead among the rubble of the nowruined palace and countless corpses of our warriors. Above the dragon's carcass, stood our lord, wounded, covered in blood and scorch marks, with a broken weapon and a bent armour, but nonetheless triumphant. Our surviving troops raise their blades to the sky and cheer, to their victory today, and to the victory of their lord, who will be immortalized in Ynnic memory as the second coming of Artorean.
