Though under the rule of the Gunpowder Xhazobine, the majority of her forces are still made up of Seascourge Gnolls who plied their craft on the seas of the Divenhal. It shouldn’t be a surprise that her forces were adept at naval invasions. Yet the monarch was still completely blindsided when reports reached Kheterat of a surprise invasion force landing in Shasoura, catching their homeland in a pincer movement.
