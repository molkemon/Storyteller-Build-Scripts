It is said of the office of Kylakas that he, who has a mighty army, is capable of protecting both his domain and his territories. Such a philosophy has been put to the test with a recent proposal in our governmental policy. Some of our council members argue that we should neglect to fund the military in favor of our levies on Alecand. The members aligned with the territories, however, have loudly objected to such a proposal. They argue that to maintain and expand our presence in Devand, it is prudent to send additional funding and resources to the territories at once. One way or another, one of these factions may walk out dissatisfied.
