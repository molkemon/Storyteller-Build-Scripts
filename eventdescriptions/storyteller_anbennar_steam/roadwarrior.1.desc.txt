From the lowliest Roadrat to the CarCaptains, the Crew of the Convoy is how anything gets done. While space on the rig is limited, a few of the smarter lads have suggested we can reorganize some of the compartments, saying that we don’t need things like emergency brakes and overflow valves. If we want to let them at it, it’ll likely take a fair bit of resources from the Convoy’s plunder, either getting conveniently lost during reorganizing things or using it for materials.
