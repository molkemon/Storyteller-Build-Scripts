A noble has recently arrived to court, their anger only tempered by their strict adherence to etiquette and the presence of armed guards who give them threatening looks. They angrily proclaim that their heir has been either seduced or controlled by a vampire and has now disappeared, likely forever. They imply the government is doing nothing to stop the growing vampire threat, and that more sons and daughters of the prestigious nobility of their realm will suffer the same fate as their heir if the situation continues and the government's implication remains as lax as it is now. A thorough investigation of the matter has only yielded a farewell letter where the heir mentions their love for their supposed kidnapper. Though vampire tricks could easily have swayed them to write such a letter before departing.
