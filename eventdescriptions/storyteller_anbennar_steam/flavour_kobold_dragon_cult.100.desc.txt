The treacherous depths of the Dragon Coast are home to many poorly charted caverns, one of which is said to contain the legendary Dragon’s Hoard, a veritable mountain of gold collected through the years by kobolds for their draconic overlords as a form of tribute. Having recently conquered the caverns where it is said they reside, many adventurers have started flooding into our country hoping to find riches meant for a king or better, a dragon. It seems the hunt can finally stop, as according to a letter recently sent to the ruler, one of our partnered adventurer groups seems to have found the hoard. The report, clearly written by the party’s bard, reads as follows. “After a long and grueling fight against monsters untold and traps uncountable, we have cleared that monster’s den and looted the scaled fiend’s treasury. We have included a mighty share of the profit for the State”. Alongside the letter, there was a single bag of coins. A SINGLE BAG. The adventurers had the audacity to pocket everything else and hand over to the State a single novice adventurer’s share worth of loot, or even less than a bag holder’s. Fortunately for us, they foolishly thought anonymity would stop us from seeking recompense, and forgot to not use their personal seal on the letter, so we could easily track them down. Unfortunately, demanding to be repaid millions of crowns worth of assorted jewelry and gold items would not go down particularly well with anyone, but some of the monarch’s advisors have suggested forcing the adventurers to give the hoard to our recently acquired kobold community. It is technically their property, and it would certainly help integration efforts, alongside putting the adventurers in their place.
