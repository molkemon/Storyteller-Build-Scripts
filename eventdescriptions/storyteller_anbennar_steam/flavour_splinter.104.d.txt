Today marks a grand celebration, for today is the first tourney on the lakes for millennia. Feudal lords and nobility from across our realm and several other visiting noble families have traveled to Qarshtuluu to celebrate and partake in this splendid occasion. Drinking, dancing; conversing about the past, present, and future - the royal family enjoys themselves. In particular, our heir revels in the chance to show off their proper etiquette and formalities, and has enchanted several of the guests with their charm and charisma. After several hours, the grand finale begins. In the midst of a fierce jousting competition, our heir faces down another young noble - young lord Iljar. The tension remains high as the two mount their horses, lances in hand. But our heir deflects Iljar's lance in a spectacular display of skill and thrusts into his breastplate, sending him to the ground breathless. As the crowd cheers for our heir, they walk back to their defeated enemy, reaching out a hand of mercy. As if blessed by Kalyin herself, the two walk back to their quarters, with the sun seeming to shine especially bright on our heir. This Tourney has been an astounding success. Although it did cost us quite a lot to host, it can hardly be argued this was a waste.
