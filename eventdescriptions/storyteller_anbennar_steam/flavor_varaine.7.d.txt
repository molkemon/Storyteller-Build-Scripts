Varaine still has large amounts of potions and salves produced during the early days of the Greentide, which combined with our increased generation of potions in the last few years, represent a lot of potential gains for our nation.  Some of our advisors recommend that we use these surplus potions as a way to deepen our diplomatic and trade relations with Escann, by selling them for a cheaper price to the adventurers in the region. Alternatively, other advisors recommend that we use these potions to improve our own nation, giving our army and adventurers an edge over their possible enemies.
