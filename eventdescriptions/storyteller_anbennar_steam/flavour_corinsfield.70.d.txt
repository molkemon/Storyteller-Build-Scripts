Recently, two men have gotten into a drunken brawl at a local festival. Normally, this would be entirely beneath our notice; however, the inquisitors have gotten involved. Apparently the fight began over one of the men’s wives accusing the other of being a witch. Seeking to defend her honor and life, her husband threw the first fist at the other. Gossip has spread quickly through the town, and so the inquisitors are demanding a thorough inspection of everyone involved. This could be a waste of resources on drunken hearsay, but it also could uncover an actual witch.
