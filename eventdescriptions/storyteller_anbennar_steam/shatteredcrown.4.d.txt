There haven't been this many shamans gathered together in our realm in a very long time. The return of the dwarves has stirred up even the oldest of our professions. They gathered to discuss findings, visions, omens, ancient dwarven murals, old ork wallpaintings, and discrepancies in the oral traditions of the various clans. The conclave took three whole days, but in the end the majority of the shamans came to the same conclusion. The answer to how Dookan is freed and how the dwarves can be defeated does not lie in Amldihr; it lies in HulJorkad. The place where our history begins, the place where the ancients told stories of meeting Dookan himself. Deep down in that hold, we must go.
