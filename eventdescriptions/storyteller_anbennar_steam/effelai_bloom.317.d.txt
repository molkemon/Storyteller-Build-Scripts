It started with strange behavior. Researchers staring into nothingness, not always responding to people around them, and swaying to what appeared to be music only they could hear. Still, they kept coming to work, talking with their friends, and advancing their research. Until one day, when they started singing. The soldiers attacked them immediately, terrified of the song spreading, but the enthralled mages dispatched them with ease. A new song servant infestation has been created, in the middle of our research site.
