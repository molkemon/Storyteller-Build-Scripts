The dynasty of our noble ruler is certainly a powerful one. Through the monarch they rule our nation and will do so for many years to come. However, even the great are sometimes in need of help. Thankfully, our head of state's family has tied strong bonds to other great powers, such as the house led by no other than a foreign monarch, ruler of that realm. Their gracious spouse is a descendant of our own royal house and an invaluable contact. Perhaps we shall ask them if they can influence their monarch to lend us a hand?
