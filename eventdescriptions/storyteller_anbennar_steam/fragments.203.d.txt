It is confirmed. Something outside the Prime Material is indeed linking at irregular periods within Amadia. We seem to have caught its attention, and in the recent months it seems to have stopped connecting to our plane. The greatest mages and artificers in our employ suggest one final course of action. to feign the closure of our endeavors and lay a trap. And when the time is right, we will bind this pocket plane into our own, restoring it to its natural place in our realm, and allowing our adventurers to finally enter this hidden realm. We must act quickly.
