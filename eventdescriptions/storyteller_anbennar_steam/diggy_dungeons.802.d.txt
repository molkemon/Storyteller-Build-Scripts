No matter how far we look, we can find nothing to help us get down the chasm. Even worse, the labyrinthian edge has got us completely disoriented to the point where we can't even find where we started. Our only option is to climb down; for better or for worse.  For worse. The climb is arduous and many of our men fall during the descent. Then, suddenly, the rope gives way and we start sliding down the chasm, deep, deep into the black unknown.
