A new void stretching across the length of the chamber bars our progress, but the spans crossing it are no mere bridges. The machines used to create the Holohana extrude tissue in gigantic chunks of flesh, and these bricks of meat and gristle have become sinewy bridges spanning across the black abyss. Carefully, the party works to cross the factory floor, marvelling at the scale of the creatures whose body parts they use as stepping stones. Then the visions crash back into sight, and memories of the facility as it was active begin to intrude into the minds of the party. Different sinews, different tissues, all shadowed in the darkness and pulsing with the constant groan of machinery. The adventurers are now faced with two separate versions of reality, and must watch their step in both.
