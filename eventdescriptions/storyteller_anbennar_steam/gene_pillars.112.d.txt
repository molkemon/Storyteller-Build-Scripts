More chambers within the pillar reveal the extent of the experiments that took place within this facility. Instruments filled with strange liquid and vials that glitter in the dim light are placed haphazardly around the room, some broken and spilled onto the ground while others remain intact and radiant. A particular vial has caught the party's eye; dark and malevolent, the liquid inside the glass bottle seems likely to be harmful in some way, but it is possible it could also hold immense value.
