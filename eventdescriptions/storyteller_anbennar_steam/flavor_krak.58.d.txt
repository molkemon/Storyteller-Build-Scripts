The IceSmiths are the magically empowered class of our nation, wielding their ice magic with great skill for millennia. They have approached us with an offer of alliance. If we are consolidating our power, they wish to join on the condition of higher research funding. We could fund them and have their might on our side, or perhaps we ought to put them in their place and make them wait for the money like everyone else. They won't be very happy about that though.
