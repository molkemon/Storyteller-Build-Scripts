Our waterblessed ruler has been spending more and more time in lakes and springs around our capital, but other people who try to relax there have reported dangerous currents and large amounts of water being moved. These two events were hardly coincidental and we knew they must be related. After asking the monarch about these events, they have told us that they have been planning to create a special marine regiment. Taking together the fact that only planetouched individuals can match our ruler's title with what our reports claim, it is only reasonable to presume that our ruler is planning on creating a regiment of planetouched marines, brought together by the personal affinity they feel to our ruler.
