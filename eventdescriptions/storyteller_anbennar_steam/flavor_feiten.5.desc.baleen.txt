The journey back was as uneventful as setting out. Maybe even more so, without the excitement of a possible whale hunt to look forward to. With little else to do, the lord thinks back to the conversations they had with the harpooners on the expedition. They were fond of requisitioning some of the baleen from the dead whale for their own uses, as it was both quite flexible yet strong. The lord could think of many uses for a material such as this; perhaps as cabling for crossbows, or as ribbing for balloons?
