It was long after the sun had set. The monarch was going to bed, only to shoot up when their back made contact with warm fur. They witnessed the fox tail they had just laid on flick out of the bed, returning behind the figure of a HuliJing. Quickly, the monarch wraps themselves in blankets, seeking not to look improper. The spirit speaks to them, tails flowing like water. "I speak for Huszien, do you wish for her boon, little ruler? I will provide it, if you can keep me engaged."
