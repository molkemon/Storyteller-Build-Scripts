"We have something a bit different in mind this time," the shadowy figure said. The ruler was used to these sudden arrivals by now; they didn’t even flinch. "Speak, then," the ruler said. "Perhaps you have guessed this by now, and what I am about to say will be entirely unsurprising, but I am a representative of the Cult of Rot," the figure said. "You have been most kind in assisting our organization thus far, and we are interested in taking this relationship to a higher level." "What are you asking for?" "Official cooperation. The followers of Winter still rebel against you, yes? We can help, but we want a promise of support in return. We want positions in your government. Our goals align, and we can help you, but only if you help us in return. I imagine you are aware of the Amber Princess, yes? I can tell you, with no uncertainty, that she is to return soon. It will be a great boon to you to have us on your side." The figure produced a piece of parchment. "What do you say?"
