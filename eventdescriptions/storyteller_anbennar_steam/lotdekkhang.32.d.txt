Using veterans from the Dishonoured, it was still hard to convince the hobgoblins to trust them. Once they convinced the first hobgoblin gang to migrate to the new military camps financed by the state, the Daulophs and the Dishonoured veterans in Khron Sakkar, word quickly spread through the slums that their offer was genuine and beneficial. Soon more and more gangs agreed to end their old ways and join the new Company. They agreed on a new name, a name from the streets used to describe their culture as a whole. Swallows. An ode to both the naming conventions of the Hobgoblin factions back in the Command and to the sense of freedom they experience in Lot Dekkhang.
