So many questions abound as to the purpose of this structure. Legends suggest that those who occupied these towers used them for dread magics and forbidden studies, the specifics of which now occupy the team's mind. You have finally located what you believe to be a laboratory complex and your team readies to breach the sealed doorway. Opening the door leads to a room of horror. All around are kobold corpses in glass tanks, all stages of their lifecycle preserved with methods both mundane and magical. After the initial shock washes over the team, you all steel yourselves and start documenting everything to be found in the room. The bodies appear to have been manipulated with some strong and dark magic, twisting and changing them into unnatural forms. What could they have hoped to learn from this?
