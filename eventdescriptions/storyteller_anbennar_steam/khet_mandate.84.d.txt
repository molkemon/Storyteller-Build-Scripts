When it comes to being the Steward of The Sorrow, many things pose a challenge, but the hardest thing is definitely appeasing the Khets themselves. From the tedious codes of Khet savoirvivre to personal accommodation of basic Khet needs, the Steward always has their hands full with requirements they aren’t always able to fulfill. Thus it seems that no matter how much the ruler changes their appearance and no matter how sweet their demeanor can be, the Khets always seem displeased with their upbringing. The longlived nature of the elves doesn’t help them establish amicable relations with the Khets, as having even half of a Khet’s lifespan gives much bigger understanding of their behaviours. This is of course a problem for the most mischievous ones, as a Steward who knows them well will be able to predict their antics before they’re able to act, causing a feedback loop of even more chaotic actions.
