Certain nobles of the realm, once so steadfast and supportive of our monarch, have started fleeing our court as animals flee a raging wildfire. They seek refuge with the enemy, citing our failing legitimacy and claim to the title of Castan in the eyes of the populace. With them they take their retainers and men-at-arms and all of their equipment and belongings, striking a blow to our war efforts. Traitors, the lot of them.
