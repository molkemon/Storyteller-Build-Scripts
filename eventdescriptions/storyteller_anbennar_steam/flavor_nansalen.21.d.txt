While the leadup to the actual wedding was tumultuous, considering that it took a coup, a war, and a former queen being set aside, the wedding itself went surprisingly well, lacking unwelcome surprises. There had been concerns that Karodir would simply use his sorcerous might to escape or even try to take the wedding party as hostages, but from what Araš later tells us, he was surprisingly placid about the whole affair despite the martial prelude of the wedding. As a scholar at heart, he was apparently somewhat relieved to be rid of the daily minutiae that ruling a vast realm such as his inevitably brings with it, as he can now focus on deepening his already prodigious understanding of magic. Apparently, he is quite excited about being able to take a more active role in the development of his fledgling academy of magic in Ulmiš Idiqlat.
