As we escape one threat we immediately find our way to another. We rappel down a small drop to another room due to the sideways architecture of this place, where we hoped we would find ourselves somewhere safer. But it appears that we are once again close to the outside of the pillar as a strange liquid apparently seeps into the room from the outside. What is worse is the fact that there is something moving in the vile liquid. A swarm of numerous large creatures that resemble worms or maggots start wriggling towards us, clearly intent on turning us into their next meal.
