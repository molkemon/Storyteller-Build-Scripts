"My liege, another shipment has returned from the North, unsold." a ratched advisor grovelled before our ruler. "Our merchants claim that the market price has been dumped, and our gems made practically worthless." "You fools, how hard can it be to sell prime dwarven merchandise? How idiotic must one be to not be able to get rid of the best gemstones in the market?." The lord's shouts bellowed through the great halls, their court cowering in fear. Slowly, with small steps, an old dwarven trader steps forward: "My lord, it is not our traders' fault. It is as we feared. The Diamonds have returned to the surface, and it is they who encroach on our markets." Quickly, many of the dwarves in attendance flooded out of the throne room as the lord's screams, curses and insults echoed all throughout the Rubyhold.
