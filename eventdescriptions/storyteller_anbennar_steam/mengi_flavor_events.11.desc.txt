The play is fast approaching and little is left to be done; though one important task still remains:a new glass mask must be presented to the head actor as recognition of their mastery over the role. The mask is for the main or a major character of the play, usually the protagonist or antagonist, whichever role is being played by the most experienced actor. The masks are steeped in tradition and myth and should be easily recognizable to any Mengi  as representing a specific archetypal idea,rather than a specific role or person.
