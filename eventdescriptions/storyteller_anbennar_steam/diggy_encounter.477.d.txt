Despite our expedition leader's natural charm and charisma, the negotiations soon fell short as a fight between a scout and a bugbear broke out. What started as a standard disagreement soon escalated into a shoutout. Just before they started fighting, we intervened and put an end to this. From then on, the negotiations were cold, and the bugbear chief remained reluctant. To no one's surprise, he said no, but still left us with some meager supplies as a sign of good faith before continuing onwards.
