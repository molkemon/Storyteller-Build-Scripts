Even with all the awesome power found within the realm of the magical, the power of nature is still a force which must be dealt with ever so carefully. This principle is at full display in the current chambers, where it seems some spores from the Mushroom Forest ended up inside the pillar and have latched onto the manainfused walls, using them as a supercharged food source. Fungi blanket every surface for several rooms, growing large enough to block the path of the expedition. And if that was not enough, some kind of interaction between the mushrooms and the energies they fed upon has awakened some dormant mutation within them, creating a massive hall of menacing fungus which now bars the way.
