Our great government has received one of the stranger challenges we’ve seen. A veteran, whether for glory, gold, or to settle a perceived slight by the state, has challenged us to duel above the magma and miasma. It is easily known that they are a veteran: their warweary eyes, slightly singed beard, and most importantly, their lack of an arm. Clearly, their bluff and bluster covers up their inability in combat, despite their experience. Shall we not even acknowledge their pitiful challenge, or should we dispatch this fool once and for all?
