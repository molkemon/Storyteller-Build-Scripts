Few things were able to stop the rampage of the battling ork. We were not among them. The horde overwhelmed us, possessing greater strength, greater numbers, and greater ferocity than most enemies we had faced before. Our soldiers fell to their axes, clubs, and blades, a slaughter beyond comprehension. Routed and demoralized, most soldiers had to take their destiny into their own hands, fleeing from the battlefield until the leader gave the command to retreat.
