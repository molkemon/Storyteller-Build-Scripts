Our artisans start inspecting the crank and its mechanism. They test things out patiently. Again and again they try. Finally they start turning the crank and the gate opens. We cheer as we see a larger chamber ahead. We walk with utter confidence. Next, a sudden chainlike noise is followed by a metallic shriek. Then, the horror. The right side of the gate caved in. Five of our members lie crushed in a blood pool underneath the thick metal. At least the gate did not fully close.
