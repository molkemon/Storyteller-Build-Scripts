Yet another group of pirates, the fearsome harpies of the Naléni have long since raided the seas around their mountains, until now. With our subjugation of them, however, comes a rather unique conundrum. Usually we would just add their children to the Academies, but their ability to fly makes it difficult to enslave them. As such, we have come up with two possible solutions. We could drive them off, of course, but that would take resources and be distasteful for many of us. Or we could make a deal with them, that in exchange for letting them keep their mountains, we will have the coast to ensure no pirate will harm our ships.
