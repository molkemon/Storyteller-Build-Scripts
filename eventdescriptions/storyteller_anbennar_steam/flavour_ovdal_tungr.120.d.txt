Our allies have come to celebrate alongside us for the victory against our former oppressors in our capital and have requested to build an embassy to foster a continued friendship with us. Another request they have made is for compensation for their efforts in the aid of our war against ReUyel; they ask for either the ownership of the city of ReUyel or for several trade concessions to be made to dwarven traders to aid their influence over Bahar. If we are to maintain good relations with our dwarven friends, we must come to an amicable conclusion upon this deal.
