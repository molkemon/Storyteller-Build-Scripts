With our complete control over the heartlands of the old Kingdom of Carneter, we stand in a position to revive the title and have the monarch crowned as king. The legacy of one of Lencenor's great kingdoms will help guide Eilísin into the future with great respect across Cannor. However, another more extreme path presents itself too. embracing the Carnetori identity completely, declaring our nation the Kingdom of Carneter first. We would be abandoning any claims to the Kingdom of Lorent that Eilísin has, and will no doubt draw ire from the Empire for laying claim to more of Anbennar's western lands. What will the future of Eilísin be?
