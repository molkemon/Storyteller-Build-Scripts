Despite two decades having passed since the conquest of Vrorenmarch during the Graytide, many among the White Reachman nobility still hold a deep grudge against their orkish overlords and yearn for a return to human rule in the Reach. While minor rebellions have been successfully put down over the years, no clear ringleaders have emerged for the faction of nobles wishing to restore the human kingdom. Recently however, whispers have emerged of a conspiracy involving the Adshaw noble family across the waters of the Bay of Chills, and a supposed claimant to the old throne of the Vrorensons whose identity still remains shrouded in mystery.
