The bridge building starts off fine. We build the foundations, set up the pylons, and start passing the rope across the chasm without a hitch. However, as work progresses, we hear more and more sounds coming from below. We first think we are hearing echoes, but as we draw closer to finishing the bridge, we realize the noises are not like anything we are doing. By the time we finish the bridge and start ferrying the expedition through, we realize our noise while building the bridge attracted something from the depths. Calling them spiders would be misleading to say the least, but we are lucky to make it through the tunnel on the other end with minimal casualties.
