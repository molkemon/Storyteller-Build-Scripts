The mages and engineers have presented us with two options for getting into the Sarda Vault. The first is using water breathing potions to get into the thing, which will allow us to explore the vault bit by bit without upsetting the local terrain and is a fairly quick solution. The second is draining the lake, which the engineers suspect will take around four to six years, depending on how quickly we build the machines and on local weather conditions. What will we do? Will we dive or drain?
