The echoes grow louder. In place of what was once voices is now something much worse. Screams. From cracks in the stone, the creatures pour forth in a shrieking tide. Their wings beat wildly against the cavern walls as they descend upon them, twisted mouths mimicking their commands. "Hold the line." "Retreat." "Behind you." Stolen voices echo throughout the darkness, sowing confusion as they tear into their host. Steel crashes against claw in a terrible ambush. They were not prepared for this.
