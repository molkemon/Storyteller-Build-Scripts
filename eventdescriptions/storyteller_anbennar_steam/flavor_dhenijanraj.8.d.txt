Once thought to be an insurmountable wave of monsters pouring across the Ruin Kingdoms, the hobgoblins of the Great Command have finally received their reckoning. Tens of thousands of elite harimari warriors, loyal adherents to the Lotus Claw and its duties, began the brutal process of clearing the land of local resistance. Goblin slaves, led by hobgoblin commanders, laid traps on vital supply routes, depots, and barracks, resolute in causing as many casualties as possible. But with each passing day, their numbers dwindled, and the guerilla warfare has finally ended, for now. Decades of rule and cultural oppression would hopefully soon give way to a cultural resurgence and rebirth of the Ruin Kingdoms of old, with the Second Harimraj at their helm. The Three-Headed Banner, symbolic of the union between the Boar, Lion, and Wolf Commands, lies broken at the feet of a local field commander, Poymewhijiya the Third Killa of the Castle Herd. He is calm, for his home in Dhujat is no longer in danger.
