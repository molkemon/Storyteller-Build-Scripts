While the Adeen are united in their desire for greater recognition within society, many within their diverse ranks are divided over how to achieve this goal. Some take a more hardline stance while others believe in gradual pragmatism, and some only advocate for their ethnic group at the expense of all others. These multifarious motivations often lead to conflict, and conflict within a professionalized fighting force often turns violent. Skirmishes between two competing factions have turned violent in a specific province, requiring an official response.
