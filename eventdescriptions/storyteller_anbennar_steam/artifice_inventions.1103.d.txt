Imagine, if you will, a drill. Imagine it digging a tunnel. Now, imagine it digging deeper than that. No, four times that depth. Five times that depth. Twenty times that depth. The Subterrene is a marvel of engineering, capable of digging faster, better, and with greater stability than any drill before. These manned machines pierce even the deepest bedrock, and place supports behind them as they dig. This allows excavation of high-quality tunnels in a fraction of the time. With the Subterrenes, we can build complexes for trains beneath our cities. We can create buildings that never see the sky. We can take every piece of land we own, and make it hold four times the buildings it currently does. What cannot be accomplished with a Subterrene?
