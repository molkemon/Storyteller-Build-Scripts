A flamboyant bard, his instruments wheezing with each step, melodiously hums in tune with good news. "My ruler, my ruler. Betwixt sets highlighting the nation's majesty—a service I am all too pleased to humbly perform—, I plucked out a drunken mercenary’s muttering when playing to a crowd in the capital. Alone, freshfaced, yet somehow lousy with wealth; how queer. I plied the mercenary with music—'Tis No Peak Amply High' by yours truly—and drink, and my serenading succeeded." "Blessed by fate, this adventurous soul stumbled upon a submerged cove jammed with merfolk treasure. He could only retrieve a paltry sum, so I jammed my way to the spot, and lo. dizzying wealth slumbered within the sequestered grotto." "Which leads me to this moment, my ruler. The question I pose—with keen drama—is who exactly stored the treasure? Stranger still, why did the merfolk leave their hoard unattended?" "I am certain you know just what to do with this untended treasure hoard, my ruler."
