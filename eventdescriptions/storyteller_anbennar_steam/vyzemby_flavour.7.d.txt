Southern Sarhal is in many ways a land of merchants as much as it is a land of might and magic. The region’s position between the formerly lost continent of Aelantier, the rising powers of Cannor, and the rich and bountiful lands of Haless bolsters its own natural wealth, resulting in many choosing to make a living by selling goods from all these places throughout the land. As you stroll through the markets of the capital, one such merchant catches your eye, a lizardfolk hailing from the lands of Karassk with wares from across Taneyas. Jewellery, books, and various texts spill over his stall, as he beckons you closer. What shall you buy?
