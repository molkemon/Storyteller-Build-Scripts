Since we have embraced the creed of the Endless Ocean, our view has turned, as every wayfarer must, to the horizon. Somewhere beyond the great wall of storms which rings the domain, lies the afterlife. Knowledge we once abhorred, that the Gene Pillars are capable of bringing down the edge of the world, now promises an entirely new frontier. What can this truly mean? No one sect of the Endless Ocean is quite agreed, of course, though notions spread rapidly from group to group on such an important topic, carried by our garrulous exchange. Can we perhaps sail to the very afterlife itself, and meet those lost to us? Will we find other islands in the ceaseless blue beyond, other folk who fare their way on the waves? Ours is a curious creed, whatever shore it may wash, and we doubt any would prove as adaptable as we to the immense changes and challenges that ending the wall may bring. We simply must know. Piecing together the information gleaned from the Gene Pillar with the collected stories of the domain, we have discovered a fascinating link between the Gene Pillars and the Dragon Turtles of the domain. Three of their number are closely associated with the secrets of the Pillars, even far beyond their homelands: Hoki’eal, Grandfather Moss, and Nalo Pu’ukani. Hoki’eal, curious and daring, has long ridden the edge of the wall for her own reasons, joy and sorrow alike; Nalo Pu’ukani shares our creed and our desire to go beyond the wall, and his many songs and humble secrets include tales of the Pillar’s operations; Grandfather Moss’ prodigious lifespan includes an enormous wealth of wisdom. With their help, we will cross the wall into a truly endless ocean.
