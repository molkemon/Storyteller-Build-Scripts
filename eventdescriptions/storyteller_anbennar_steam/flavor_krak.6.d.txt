With our expansion efforts well under way we find ourselves torn between two major parties. One side wishes to see us claim the Northern Pass and worry over the news of strange orks in Gerudia that could threaten our endeavours. The other believes we must focus all of our efforts on reclaiming Amldihr, the seat of the Dwarovar. We cannot do both effectively enough to satisfy everyone and they seem to find no compromise acceptable. The lord may see us torn between these two unbudging forces.
