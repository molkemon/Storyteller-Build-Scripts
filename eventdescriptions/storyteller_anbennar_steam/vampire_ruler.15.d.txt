Our ruler left their seat of power to hunt, but as they stalked the streets, they had a disquieting feeling: that of being stalked in turn. And now, at the sight of a cloaked man also roaming our capital's streets, whose steeltipped wooden stakes glint in the moonlight, it is clear that someone would turn our lord from predator into prey. Our ruler could attempt an ambush, matching their pursuer headon, but such a course of action would be risky. No matter what, it is clear tonight's feeding must be done with care, or perhaps abandoned entirely, but it cannot wait for long.
