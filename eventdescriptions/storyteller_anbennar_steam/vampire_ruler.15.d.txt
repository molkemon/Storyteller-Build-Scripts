The monarch left their seat of power to hunt, but as they stalked the streets, they had a disquieting feeling. that of being stalked in turn. And now, at the sight of a cloaked man also roaming the capital's streets, whose steeltipped wooden stakes glint in the moonlight, it is clear that someone would turn our ruler from predator into prey. The monarch could attempt an ambush, matching their pursuer headon, but such a course of action would be risky. No matter what, it is clear tonight's feeding must be done with care, or perhaps abandoned entirely, but it cannot wait for long.
