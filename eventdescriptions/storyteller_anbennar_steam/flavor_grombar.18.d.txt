The Gray orks find few friends in Escann. The humans despise them simply for being the same species as Dookanson, and their green cousins see them as both traitors and heretics. Ironically enough, however, those who put an end to the Greentide may just be the best chance they have for an ally in the south. Corintar has made the local halforks welcome, treating them as equals, and given their brief cooperation in the fight against Dookanson, they may well be open to establishing an alliance between their fledgling nations. Some, however, would rather not open relations with the southerners at all, to prevent a potential show of weakness. Their halfork ruler is eager to lead the embassy, hoping to have a word with the famous Lothane Bluetusk and have personal contact with him. It is risky to send the ruler into human lands, but if the ruler is guarded by their warriors and pretends to be a simple halfork mercenary captain, they should be fine.
