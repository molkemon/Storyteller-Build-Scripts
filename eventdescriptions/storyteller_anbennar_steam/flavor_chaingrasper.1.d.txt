Around two decades ago, Dak Chaingrasper unified the disparate clans living around Ovdalazn under the Chaingrasper flag. Although he has since held the position of Clanboss, Dak knows that his situation will not last forever. Even if he were to survive the perpetually scheming minor clans, he will most likely be gone before the start of the next century. Although a lesser creature might make peace with their own mortality, Dak will not. In the last decade, he has made great strides in his research of the necromantic arts in the pursuit of immortality. In order to be able to continue leading the Chaingrasper Clan, Dak will do anything necessary to succeed in his quest for power.
