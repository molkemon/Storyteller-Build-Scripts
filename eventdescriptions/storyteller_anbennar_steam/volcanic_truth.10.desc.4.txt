We have convened a great synod to determine what the implications of the Kiamoa's fall truly are. We have assembled beneath one of the Twin Gods, the guides of our faith. The magmascryers have brought us to the caldera of Quahopilo, the bringer of change by force, to show us the way forward. The magmascryers begin their dance. Within the lava, we see ourselves trapped in a cage of another's design. The magmascryers rage. They direct the fire of the Volcanic Truth against the bars of our prison. They move with purpose and their fire boils over with passion, and yet we remain trapped. And then, a bar of the cage collapses in on itself. It is not much, but it might be enough for some of our bravest to crawl through the gap into the world beyond. From this single crack in our prison, the magmascryers focus on what they might see. In the lava beyond, we can see swirling shapes which fade too quickly to truly grasp; we see sparse glimpses of grand structures and great ships. And most importantly, we see nothing of the Volcanic Truth. At last, the visions within the lava fade, and the magmascryers complete their dance. The world beyond is still unclear and unknown, but we know that it is coming to Insyaa. Will its presence here be a threat or an opportunity? Can the Volcanic Truth flourish beyond the Kiamoa? What will this change mean? Not just for us, but for all of Insyaa? How shall we vote?
