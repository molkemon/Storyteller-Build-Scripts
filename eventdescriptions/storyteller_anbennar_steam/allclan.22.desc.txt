In a twist of audacious defiance, Ves Udzenklan is rattled by a backwoods uprising, led by a lone goblin named Greg. Fueled by an unyielding belief that goblins should be forever free from the shackles of taxation, Greg has rallied his Clodhopper friends in a whimsical yet determined revolt against the Pentapandemonium. Chaos and laughter fill the air as goblins of all shapes and sizes, armed with outlandish weaponry and dubious battle plans, march alongside Greg in a display of rebellious fervor. As Greg's forces make their stand, a mix of giggling, trepidation, and a hint of sympathy reverberates throughout the realm. While their cause may be tinged with absurdity, the sincerity of their conviction is undeniable.
