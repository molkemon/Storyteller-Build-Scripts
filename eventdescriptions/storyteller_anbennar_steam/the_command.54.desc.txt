Research Phase Nine Two Six; Entry One. I feel like a clerk. The Forge complex is massive now, and the line chiefs barely need my input anymore, so I spend most of my time rubberstamping supply requisitions. The perils of rendering oneself obsolete, I suppose. I expect any day now they will bring in some bloodless quartermaster to run the whole thing a lot more efficiently than I do. Luckily, I have still got some credit with the brass. I have solved their shaman problem for good, as long as we can ship them out the Camps of Contrition and keep on top of the logistics. With such an abundance of activated Korashi, the natural next step is weaponization, but I will wait to be relieved of duty before I start plunging my hands into that particular thicket.
