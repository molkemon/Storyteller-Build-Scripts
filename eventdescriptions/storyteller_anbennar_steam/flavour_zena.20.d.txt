The glowing gems of Ovdal Lodhum are unique. Stories say that they were once broken in halves, and paired between spouses and lovers to symbolize their enduring bond. But they can also be set to more utilitarian purposes, placed in lanterns as sources of light within the tunnels of Dwarovar. Azaly Stonecarver is interested in acquiring a sample for Medaka’s Exceptional Initiative, and potentially researching and replicating the glowing effect on the streets of Bivuzena.
