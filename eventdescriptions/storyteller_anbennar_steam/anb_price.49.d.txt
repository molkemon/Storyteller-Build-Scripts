In what started as a radical Corinite idea, the outlawing of orkish slavery in Cannor was eventually passed by a conglomeration of Anbennarian powers for instead a purely commercial and political reason. to curtail the influence of their western colonial rivals in Lencenor, and more importantly to halt the rise of burgeoning great powers in Escann such as the nation. While this did not abolish the act of orkish slavery but merely the trade, in the short term it actually made orkish slaves rarer and thus more expensive for the benefit of many homegrown slavers of Aelantier to the chagrin of their Escanni counterparts.
