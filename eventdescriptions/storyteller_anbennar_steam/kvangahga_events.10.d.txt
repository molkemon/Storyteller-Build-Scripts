The clang of hammers and the scrape of chisel and file echo through the workshop as our lord approaches, the wizened gnoll hunched over a mighty anvil inscribed with the sacred runes of the Kvangahga faith. Our lord has been reliably informed the old mystic is the greatest weaponsmith of the age and has made the trek to this remote workshop to commission a mighty weapon blessed by the goddess herself. Now that they stand in this sacred place, they know that something truly remarkable will be crafted today. Wordlessly, the ancient gnoll gestures to two bundles laid before the anvil, almost as though they knew our lord would come. On the left, a pile of bleached bones each carved with intricate markings. On the right, a collection of broken and battered metal objects of a long forgotten design, still as shiny as the day they were first forged. "Choose" croaks the artisan, in a voice like the grinding of a tomb.
