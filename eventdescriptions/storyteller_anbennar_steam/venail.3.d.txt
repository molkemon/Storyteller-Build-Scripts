Early in the morning, an elf envoy came to the doors of the Dames crown Town Hall, as a representative of Venail, asking for an audience. He brings with him a most intriguing proposition. Venail seem to have embarked on a massive colonization effort of the ancient world of Aelantier, and came to ask us to help them in their task through a huge loan. It's a gamble, but if they are successful in their ventures the benefits it could bring us are unimaginable.
