Early in the morning, an elf envoy came to the doors of the realm's town hall, as a representative of Venáil, asking for an audience. He brings with him a most intriguing proposition. Venáil seems to have embarked on a massive colonization effort of the ancient world of Aelantier, and came to ask us to help them in their task through a huge loan. It is a gamble, but if they are successful in their ventures, the benefits it could bring us are unimaginable.
