As we suspected, the Dakini still have plenty of loyalty to their mother; working on Her behalf, we were quickly able to get many of them on board with our plans. In exchange for bribes of money and Mijiu, as well as the promise of future tribute, they have agreed to use both their charms and their wit to stir distrust and greed in the hearts of the myriad Xianjie. The ground will soon be perfect for our intervention.
At the same time, we have also set up good and lasting relations with the Dakini. Though they are not part of our lands, they have been granted an observatory role in the government befitting their close relationship to Her grace. In the future, we can expect them to lend their magical talents to our realm when we request.
