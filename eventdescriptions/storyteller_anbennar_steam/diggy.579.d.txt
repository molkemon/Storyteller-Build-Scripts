Many unusual creatures roam the caves of the Serpentspine, and our farmers seem to have encountered some of these. They claim that for the last few months, while collecting mushrooms, strange sounds could be heard in the distance, and recently we have received reports of glowing eyes and occasional "walking mushrooms". These rumors have been discarded until now, as yesterday it was discovered that the freshlygrown mushrooms are now protected by a large number of fungrum. A few unfortunate and bruised farmers have already been beaten up by them, and farms in the area are now paralyzed.
