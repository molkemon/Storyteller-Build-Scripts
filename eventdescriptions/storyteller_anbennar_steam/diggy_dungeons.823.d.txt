Walking along the paths carved out by time and nature yields many different sights into the very structure of what surrounds us, what we live in. Here we can see the sediments of ages past, relics of times immemorial, from before any life walked the surface of Halann. Besides rock, however, there is also some plant life to be found down here. One of the most extraordinary specimens is a slimy root that pulses with a violet glow. Many are wary of it at first, but after one daring cook has tried it, his mood lightens up. He plucks more out of the ground and distributes it, soon most of the expedition has their hands on the nourishing and invigorating plant.
