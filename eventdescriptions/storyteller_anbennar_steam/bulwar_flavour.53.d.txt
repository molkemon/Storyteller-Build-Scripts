We received multiple letters concerning the “Sinners” and “Criminals” of our country, which describe all the crimes they committed, where the evidence is located, and how to find those individuals. Our spies are convinced those messages must have come from the Cult of the Eclipse, a radical group that believes in cleansing Bulwar from crime and what they consider the stain of the monstrous races. One of the letters we received contained the names of all those considered “sympathizers” of gnolls, harpies, or goblins, be they individuals who trade with them or have any other form of relationship with them. We can of course ignore those parts and focus only on the useful sections about criminal activity within our borders. This cult appears very experienced in acquiring, cataloguing and investigating evidence of what they consider crimes in the eyes of Surakel. A clandestine cooperation with the cult could be a boon for our nation, but they have very radical positions that can disagree with some groups.
