The diffusion of classical and new books thanks to the various royal libraries and the colleges attached to them has changed the conceptual and philosophical landscape of the nation, with new schools flowering and debating throughout the land. The main problem discussed by these communities is the question of how to lead a good life while dealing with wars for the safety of mankind. The Statist school, mainly found among the administrators, emphasizes the role of the state, the only guarantee of peace and security. The River school, called this because of the book 'The Worldy Rivers', emphasizes the role of openness and exchange. Lastly, they could sponsor the School of Courage, which has glorified and justified the virtuous wars waged against the monstrous races.
