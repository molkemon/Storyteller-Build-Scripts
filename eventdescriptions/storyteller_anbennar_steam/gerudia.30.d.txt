Every day, our subjects report more and more wagons stocking up on essentials and belongings, and more and more families heading out across the horizon. It seems the Cagodics, who have been our neighbors for so long, have set out for greener pastures. This has led to some less complaining from our subjects about their various misgivings towards them, but also less economic activity. Only time will tell what this will mean for the Cagodics and the communities they leave behind.
