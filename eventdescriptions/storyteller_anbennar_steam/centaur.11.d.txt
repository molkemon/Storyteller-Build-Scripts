From the south, born of the cities and sands of Bulwar, rise the Burning Spear. They have come to think of the deserts of the south as a new home, and adopted many of the traditions and tactics of the sun elves that previously ruled the lands they now occupy. Their loyalty to the Caehn is unquestionable; we now have at our disposal a group of warriors uniquely suited to the trials and tribulations of occupying the lands of Bulwar.
