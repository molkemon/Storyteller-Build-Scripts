The hinuibirds may be vicious creatures, but they are as vicious towards each other as they are to us. Our mountain scavengers in the carcass recently discovered the evidence of a great fight between two hinuibirds, with the loser's body now left to rot on site. Although it is too dangerous in almost all circumstances to proactively hunt hinuibirds ourselves, this one carcass could feed our settlement and keep it in supplies for months and months; if, that is, we can get to the corpse before it decomposes.
