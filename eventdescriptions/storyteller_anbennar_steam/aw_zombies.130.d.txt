While zombie outbreaks do not often begin in major cities, those that do have a tendency to spiral out of control rapidly, the high population density and many dark corners providing the perfect conditions for zombies to multiply. The province has what the city folk called a modest undead problem outside its walls, and despite the precautions that are normally taken to control the spread of diseases and monster populations in cities, hundreds of zombies have risen overnight within the city itself. Several quarters of the city have been cordoned off in an attempt to stop the creatures from spreading, but to no avail. Refugees are being stopped at the province border already by local authorities, hoping that they will not bring the plague with them.
