Exciting news from the Jade March. A new vein of mithril has been uncovered in the Jade Mines, and new mines are being set up to exploit this windfall. This vast deposit of mithril will enable the great forge of Sarilahvan to produce even greater quantities of mithril equipment at an even greater quality than before. The access to mithril weaponry and armour will serve as a great boon over the betrayers, who only possess modest at best reserves of mithril.
