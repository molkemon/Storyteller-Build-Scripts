We are a nation of many different peoples, and to not acknowledge them would be so great a dishonor that no amount of prayer could absolve me. They are the core foundation of all that we do. They are the farmers that feed our armies, the merchants who bring wealth and trade to our cities, and the craftspeople who produce our goods. Without them, without all of you, we would be nothing. We have come far, and yet, we still have much farther to go before we can rest. Even now, our enemies conspire to destroy us. They seek to pick us apart, one at a time. To continue on our path to glory, we must become one unified people. We must not look to those different from us as enemies, but as equals, another piece of the whole. No matter who you may be, all are equal before Surael. Together, we shall triumph as one people under the Sun.
