You duck into a small guard room and see several automatons spaced evenly around the perimeter; heavily armed but dormant. Once convinced they look safe, you start to investigate the room for other potential exits or even the possibility of loot. As you cross the room, a small collection of gems catches your eye on a small podium near some of the figures. Carefully checking for traps, you start to approach. However, despite your diligence, you must have missed something, and you hear the guardians whir to life behind you. Your mechanic colleague runs over towards the figures and starts attempting to communicate with them in their rapid tongue. With rapid gestures from both parties to the room and the rest of the team, they appear to reach a conclusion and weapons get lowered. Your colleague returns to the expeditionary team and speaks in your tongue, "These guardians were part of the facility defenses and were designed to keep the beings that were imprisoned here contained. They used a word I do not recognize to describe them, so I am unsure what they are, but they may be related to the items we found previously." They pause briefly, "I would very strongly advise against attempting to take the items from this room, as their final orders were to keep the beings contained and prevent any looting."
