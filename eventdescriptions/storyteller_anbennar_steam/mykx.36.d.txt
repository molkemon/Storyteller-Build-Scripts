We have abandoned Viakkoc, seeking greater ambitions over the pathetic desires of their monarch. Their ambitions lay in the old world and the old way of thinking; we seek the new seas, the new land, where we would build a new system and order. They will never forgive us for stealing a chunk of their fleet and spitting in the face of the greatness they pretend to, but what do we care about? We are following the Gnoll natural law, the strongest pack rules, which will forever be the pack of Mykx.
