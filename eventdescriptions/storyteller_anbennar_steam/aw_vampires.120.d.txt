The vampires cooperating with our government are promoters of the Masquerade, a rule by which vampires must make a conscious effort to ensure their existence is not revealed to the masses. It is no wonder then that having vampires openly breaking this rule in the realm has negative consequences on their interests in the ruling power. They have thus branded these upstarts as anathema and sent out vampires specialized in hunting their own kind to brutal hunts where the hunted is either destroyed or forced to accept the status quo. Reports by our friends in the shadows indicate that open vampiric activity has all but disappeared in the realm, the situation is once more under control, and people can now walk out in the open without fear of being taken away by bloodthirsty beasts.
