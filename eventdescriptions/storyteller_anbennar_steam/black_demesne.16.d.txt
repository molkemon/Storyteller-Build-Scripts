The capture of Dames crown has given the domain access to a vast quantity of 'traditional' wealth. Though the social norms of our magical classes tend to shun such mundane concerns, it cannot be denied that control of the city's financial institutions could be a considerable boon to the Skull Throne. Some of our lord’s inner ring of personal advisors suggest transferring vast amounts of wealth out of the city and sending it to Castonath, the City of Cities, both to strengthen the position of the central government and to shore up the loyalty of our core power base in the regions surrounding Castonath. The Acolytes, however, are arguing that we should leave the city with its wealth intact as a prize for whichever Acolyte happens to hold it, granting it the socalled 'Black Mark', a seal of formal permission that allows the city to continue functioning for its own sake rather than Castonath's. How ought we proceed?
