Clan Shalebeard has always produced many fine administrators and governors and forms a large portion of our administrative body. They have approached us with an offer of aid in reforming our republic into something great. However, they will find someone more suitable to back if we do not take their offer. They wish only that their duties remain more or less as is. This may be a trouble in the future when we need to expand our administration, but the alternative is putting them all down, lest they shift their support.
