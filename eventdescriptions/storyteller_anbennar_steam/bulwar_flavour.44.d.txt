Priests and scholars from all over the land are starting to arrive at the capital city. A palace was donated by the city's wealthiest landlord, to be used as residence for the members of the Council, and to house the debates in his great hall. The city watch joined forces with the Eduznabari to protect the palace, as thousands of citizens crowd the streets, so that they may witness this historical moment. Hundreds of scribes and assistants come and go from the palace at all hours, carrying boxes of paper and ink, holy books, and cult objects. Soon, the Council will begin, and the future of our faith will be decided.
