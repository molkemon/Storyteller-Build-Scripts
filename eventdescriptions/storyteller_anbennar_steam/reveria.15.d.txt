The gem industry of Drekiríki has proven lucrative; however, the meager supply of raw gems is seriously hampering the economy, according to a prominent Cliff Gnome industry representative. Presenting documents showing ancient expeditions into the kobold caves that revealed plenty of gems, he urges the ruler to conquer the caves so the economy can be bolstered with gems and metals. His arguments are sound and so the ruler begins planning, even if the monarch is worried about the implications of fighting kobolds with darkvision in their dark hovels.
