The city of Deshak, capital of the homonymous kingdom, was originally founded as a Kheteratan colony, from which the great mineral wealth of the mountains was extracted and shipped to the Sorrow. The city has been invaded multiple times and it was almost completely destroyed by the Onslaught. It would later become the first place to be ruled by the Crodamos dynasty, and in more recent times it suffered a devastating gnollish invasion that led them to seek protection under the king of Eborthil. It holds the gate to the Divenhal, and as such anyone seeking to control the trade in this sea must also seek ownership of this precious city. This is precisely what the Korosheshi League wants, and they have offered us extensive compensation in exchange for transferring the control of the city to them. The offer is definitively tempting, and with the city in their hands they promise that they, and by extension us, will achieve total control of trade in the Divenhal.
