When we invited the Xianjie refugees, who we regard as brethren in faith and at times culture, we thought they would swiftly integrate into our society. That said, the amount of refugees is much larger than expected, as it seems like the Xiaken weren’t that keen on escaping into the corrupt realms of Rahen or tyrants of Bianfang, favouring us even before we made efforts to attract them. As a result, their numbers became so large that they were able to establish many villages dominated by the refugees, trying to establish their little Xianjie on our lands. While this had been anticipated, the scope of it overwhelmed us, as it will take more time than expected to integrate them properly into the society. Luckily, some of them still have the will to fight even more battles, drafting themselves into our army and bringing valuable fighting skills and experience. Moreover, some of our Sikai brethren’s monasteries openly showed a desire to join our Republic, basing themselves on the ancient Xaram claims to South Xianjie and the current Wulin’s incompetence.
