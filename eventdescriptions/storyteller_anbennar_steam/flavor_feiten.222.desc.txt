The Shuimu blinds. Liberated from the darkest depths of Serpentspine mines to now grace the skies, the visible mithril parts on the Shuimu scintillate, dancing with the rays of the sun as the balloon twirls lazily in the breeze. Not far behind the leading balloon rises its brethren, adding their own sparkling stars to the azure heavens. With years of research and development behind, the newly upgraded Shuimu are finally ready.
