"Something is in the air," the ruler declared in their courtroom, a sense of unease palpable in the atmosphere. It was a foreboding energy, one that they could feel on their skin. The unsettling feeling was soon accompanied by reports from soldiers stationed at various garrisons across the region. They described clashes, spirits rising, and a growing conflict that had caught the ruler's attention. The reports revealed that four powerful spirits had materialized in their world, each of them vying for supremacy. An azure dragon, a vermillion bird, a white tiger, and a black tortoise were wreaking havoc in the four corners of the region. These spirits were amassing other spirits to their side, creating chaos as they clashed with one another. In the following days, the influx of reports only grew. What astonished the ruler the most was the sheer number of spirits being reported. They couldn't deny the truth of what was happening. As if to solidify this realization, a blue flame appeared beside them, hovering like a fistsized specter. According to the reports, these four beings were seen gathering such flames or spirits, leaving the ruler to ponder their motivations. As a mortal ruler of a nation, they had a duty to protect their people and restore order. With a deep breath, they accepted the challenge of quelling these unruly spirits, ready to confront the enigma that had arisen in their lands.
