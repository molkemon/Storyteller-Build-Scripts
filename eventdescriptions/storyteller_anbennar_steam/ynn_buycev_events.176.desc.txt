A letter has arrived from the governor of the colony in our province reading: "My lord, our colony has been razed by a Veykodan wielding terrible magics. I stared from the parapet as a decrepit Veykodan, covered head to toe in tattoos, glowing red with pulses of blue, hobbled into the town square on a cane and waited. He took a deep breath, and their tattoos started glowing. At first, it was one beam of energy: the people around them looked on in astonishment and got closer. But more came from the Veykodan, and these ones were blazing hot, dozens of twisting streams of red and blue emanated from that vile Veykodan, spreading out in all directions and making terrible crackling sounds as the air itself boiled from the heat. The town and its panicked residents were enveloped by this creeping barrage. The lucky ones, the ones directly touched, were turned to ash in an instant, the others perished in the ensuing fire. My own clothes were set ablaze in the chaos, but I remembered the stream close to the parapet, below a small bluff. I ran for it and dove, passing out. I was awoken by the sound of howling wind and blazing fire, and raised my head above the bluff to see that the whole town had been consumed by flames, with that decrepit Veykodan hobbling around on their cane, admiring their handiwork as they checked for survivors. Thankfully the bluff hid the stream from their sight. Sometime later they left with some Veykodan woman supporting them in their hobble." The governor's account of what happened was chronicled and spread. Now our citizens are demanding we find this Veykodan, dubbed "the Scourge of our province", and have them tortured to death for their crimes. One of our generals has suggested we send an army to our province to find them and fulfill the people's wish.
