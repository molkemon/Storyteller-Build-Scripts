The fluttering sound of a million wings echoes through the Serpentspine; the soft glow of a false dawn emerges on the horizon. This can only mean one thing. a lumimoth migration has begun. They dart through the air, thousands strong, crossing the caverns and the holds and flowing down the old dwarven rail. Glittering, shimmering patterns form in the mass, subtle dancing shapes that the eye struggles to hold onto. Stalactites become beacons as the fluttering insects land, the entire cave complex illuminated in shades of purple and cyan.
