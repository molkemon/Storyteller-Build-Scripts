Good evening once again, ruler. The ruler whirls around in the empty hallway to see the shadowy figure once again leaning against the wall, face obscured by darkness. How do you... every time... The figure does not react. We come to you once again with an offer. You are preparing to strike at the heart of Winter, are you not? Yes, we are, although I have my doubts about our chances, the ruler said. You fear Winterswrath, the figure said. The ruler nodded curtly. That is precisely why we have reached out again. You are familiar with the technique of Icewardery? I am, and we considered it, but there is just no way to cast it on the scale to benefit a whole army, the ruler said. That is where you are mistaken. We have expanded upon the technique, and would be willing to provide you with the spell, only a single cast, of course. All we ask in return is that you lend us the magical energy required for the casting. What do you say?
