AmlazOrlz, the Assembly of Lords, is a great gathering of nobility across Cannor and the Serpentspine, allowing for the exchange of favors, gifts, and secrets facilitated in the halls of Haraz Orldhûm. Of course when wine and ale is tossed around, certain blunders can occur. It has come to our knowledge that our advisor has taken a fancy with a particular pedigree of nobility that has visited the court, having several rendezvous with the party in question. We must act before news breaks into AmlazOrlz.
