In the other room was another workshop, though this one was obviously far more mechanical in nature, as if an improved iteration of the previous. Unfortunately for them, said workshop was also abuzz with activity as some strange machine in the center of the room was roaring to life, assembling golems from materials it was being fed by more mechanical spiders. Each golem was roughly orksized, radiating with heat as they stepped out of the machine. Though indeed, a single one could be taken down with marginal effort, the rate they were being produced quickly made many realize that if nothing was done, they would be overtaken, and quickly.
