Since the Age of Petty Kingdoms the Cult has been divided. First it split into sects, and later the Sun Elves arrived and established their own version of our Cult, claiming that they were restoring the old traditions, while at the same time spreading their lies about Jaher’s divinity.  But now we achieved true unity, and from AzkaszelAzka to AzkaszelUdam, from Brasan to AzkaSur, every Sun cultist shares the same beliefs, the same practices, the same hymns. We worship Surakel in the streets and no longer in secret.  Surakel is the god we worship, as he is the Sun that shines over Bulwar.
