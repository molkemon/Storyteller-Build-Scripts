All across Cannor, word has spread of Ibevar, the Truesilver Elfrealm. Well, at least the rhetoric of their rulers has proclaimed it such. But regardless, Ibevar's rise has not gone unnoticed. The Alenic barbarians, long known for their hatred of their kind, are quaking in their boots, as their people have come to border their land. Lorent and Anbennar's claim to the legacy of their kind is in shambles, as their great Elven state stands ascendant. Pelodir, Seawatcher, all those who thought they would need to flee to the untamed lands of their ancient home in Aelantier to make a place in the world have eaten crow. Ibevar's renown has grown across all of Halann. Intellectuals and pilgrims flock to Antirionn in search of their elders' wisdom. The newest turns of fashion in Núrael have even the Esmari following every stitch. Aldresia's old castle stands as the home of a new generation of Diranbe masters. Adenican Coursers thunder across the plains once more, the long helm of their elven riders gleaming in the sun. With the coming of Silversnight, Escin and the City of Ibevar grind to a halt, as the Cogaulúis ball brings their people together in dance and song, the rivers filled with beautifully carved barges. Elvenairey wineglasses clink together, filled with the soft glow of the Varilleni, as the fine perfumes of Antirionn fill the air. The scents of those thousandyear seeds kept safe from their old homeland provide a nostalgic atmosphere, a reminder that even so far removed from the birthplace of their people, Ibevar is truly the heart of elvenkind.
