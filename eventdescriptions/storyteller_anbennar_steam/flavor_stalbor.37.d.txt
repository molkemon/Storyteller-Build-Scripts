Strange news of increasing gnoll sightings have perturbed the normally openminded people of Stalbor. It seems Stalbor has become known as a haven for gnollkind, beyond the odd merchant in city marketplaces hawking wares. They are arriving from places as close as the hill country between Busilar and Verne, tiring of persecution at the hands of human administrations, and as far as Sarhal, wishing to escape the incessant wars and raids there. The strangest news of all is that, wherever they settle, they seem to be causing no major problems with their neighbors.
