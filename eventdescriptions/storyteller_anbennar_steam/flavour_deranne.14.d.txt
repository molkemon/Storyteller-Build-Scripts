The Halflings of Fahvanosy worship a pantheon called the Great Family, where gods are associated with domains and affinities not unlike those of the Regent Court. Yet, while Castellos was universally regarded as the head of the Regent Court, there is no such figure among the halfling pantheon. In Lélanan, however, it is the goddess of life, love, and water, Lel, who is believed to have this mantle, and the city’s ruling elite, The Sisterhood, consists of priestesses who dedicate their lives to her rites. In the aftermath of our conquest of the city and our continuing campaigns on the Island, the Sisterhood have established themselves as an authority beyond Lélanan’s borders, using the opportunity to strengthen their power over the temples and pantheon as well as their hold over the people’s hearts. After the fall of previous governments, they are seen as the last true leaders of the opposition to Derannic rule, and are using their newfound influence to effectively incite rebellion among the masses.
