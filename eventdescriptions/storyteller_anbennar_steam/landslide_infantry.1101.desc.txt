While the forge is already a great boon to our nation, it could still yet do more. Without the help of mages, the forge would be completely unable to function, as the mere heat of the magma it uses would repel any mundane worker from approaching it to complete the work we need done. It is necessary for us to expand the number and quality of the mages we employ, lest we fall short of our production goals. But what direction should we take the education of these talented individuals?
