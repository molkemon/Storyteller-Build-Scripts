We, representatives of the guilds of Hytiranyalen, are writing to issue a formal protest against the diffusion of cheap inscribed tools. After inspecting some initial designs, we fear a future where these objects will make mass producing low quality versions of our artisanal products far too easy. And many of our members and their workers fear for their jobs. This is to say nothing of the safety issues; our merchandise is guaranteed by a crafting expertise spanning generations, while one in ten of their tools ends up malfunctioning in some way. We implore the Matriarch to put a stop to this foolishness; we have been able to assuage their worries until now, but without good news we probably won't be able to for much longer.
