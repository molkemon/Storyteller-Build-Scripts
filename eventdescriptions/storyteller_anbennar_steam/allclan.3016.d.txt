A representative from the Dealers reports a heist into their central vault. The team of individually specialised thieves, calling themselves the Mountain's Nine, used a cunning and convoluted plan based around a matinee goblin fight night, as well as several hundred sticks of dynamite, to break their way in, take what they could carry, and vanish into the tunnels. These glaring oversights in our security need to be fixed before someone motivated by more than money does this again.
