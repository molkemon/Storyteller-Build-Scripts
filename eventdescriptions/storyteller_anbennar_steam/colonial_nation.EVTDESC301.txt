The crop harvest in the realm has been very poor this year, and the shortage of food is already felt across the colony. The prices are soaring, and some predict a harsh winter might be coming. The nearby Indians have found out about our difficult situation and have volunteered some of their food supplies. What they brought isn't nearly enough to sustain us through the winter, but there must be more corn where the gift came from. Some propose that we send soldiers to the Indian villages and seize their food.
