When fashioning a delicate instrument such as a lyre, great care must be taken with the choice of proper materials for all its parts. The body of the lyre is one of its most important parts, as it amplifies the sounds of the instrument. There are several options to choose from for the material of the body. Wood is the classic option, but both steel fashioned out of the iron found deep within the hills of the Gelkari and mithril imported at exorbitant cost from the dwarven realms within the Serpentspine offer intriguing alternatives. What material shall be used for the body of our magical lyres?
