Over the past few centuries, the influence of ynnic feudalism has steadily been sowing its seeds in Brelar. Especially during these dark ages, the distrust and decentralisation of Dolindha has caused an increase in feudal thinking among the Brelari. This is most evidently present in how our leader is no longer elected by village elders locally elected in turn, but by families of wealthy patricians, usually among their own number. Our leader has gone a step too far, however, they have declared that upon their death, their son ought to be elected as their successor, openly calling for the patricians to support him as if his succession were that of a hereditary lord and not a Republic.
