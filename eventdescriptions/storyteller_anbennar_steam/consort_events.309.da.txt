Since the death of their spouse, the consort has been growing increasingly comfortable in their seat, as both guardian of the young heir and royal regent of the realm. To the general public, it is clear that the situation is temporary and simply a political necessity while everyone awaits the crowning of the young heir. Meanwhile, there are rumors of other characters, said to partake in clandestine meetings where they discuss, in their eyes, the honest potential of the young heir. "Putting that drooling imbecile on the throne would be a threat to the entire realm," one conspirator mutters. "The consort is certainly a capable person, and a much better option," another argues. Although certainly aware of these cunning forces, the consort has yet to take a stand on this highly sensitive issue.
