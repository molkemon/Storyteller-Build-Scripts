Since the death of their spouse, the royal consort has been growing increasingly comfortable in their seat, as both guardian of the young heir as well as royal regent of the realm. To the general public, it is clear that the situation is temporary and simply a political necessity while everyone awaits the crowning of the little heir. Meanwhile, there are rumors of other characters, said to partake in clandestine meetings where they discuss, in their eyes, the honest potential of the young heir. Putting that drooling imbecile on the throne would be a threat to the entire realm, one conspirator mutters. 'The royal consort is certainly a capable person, and a much better option,’ another argues. Although certainly aware of these cunning forces, the royal consort has yet to take a stand on this highly sensitive issue.
