At long last, word has returned from the Dalairey Expedition.all dead, save one who returned fully mad and clutching a brazen sculpture. Speaking gibberish and in constant fear, the survivor will not recall what happened to the expedition, save that none should look for them.  The Precursor ruin discovered in the ice is long gone, save for a few scraps of debris and frozen droplets of blood. The statue, depicting a proud elf of old, is covered in scratches and pits and is eternally cold to the touch. Looking upon it, you think of the lives lost to find; this. You find yourself staring at it in melancholy for hours, before realizing that no time has elapsed at all; you are still in your office, learning of the cursed expedition and looking upon the statue for the first time.
