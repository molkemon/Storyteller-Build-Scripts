Lot Dekkhang Metnikhren of Aktamakhang has invited lady Mya to a feast in the palace, near the Teplinbasiet temple. Her father, who is a Dauloph mercenary captain in service to him, insisted she accept. The feast was glorious; the Lot Dekkhang even had a gift for her: a beautiful silken robe adorned with pink and blue, the colours of Lot Dekkhang and Khao Elnak respectively. As the night came to a close, Mya's father took her apart and informed her: Ĺthis feast is all so the Lot Dekkhang can ask your hand in marriage, and if you refuse, he plans to take your township by force.ĺ Not soon after, the Lot Dekkhang began approaching her.
