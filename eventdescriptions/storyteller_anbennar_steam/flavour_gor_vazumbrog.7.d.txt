To His Majesty, the ruler, I write to inform you of the aftermath of our execution of force against the fungrum at Azmak Quarry, as per your command. The fungrum offered little resistance, their response limited to somber chanting in their incomprehensible tongue. Once the fires consuming their fungal groves subsided, our builders began preparations for the citadel's construction without delay. However, troubling incidents have since plagued the endeavor. Several engineers have vanished without a trace, their tools left untouched. Foundations, meticulously laid, have crumbled inexplicably, halting progress and sowing unease among the workforce. Some among our ranks, particularly the more superstitious, now believe the fungrum's chants were not defiance but a warning of instability in the cavern floors. The military engineers concurred that the site was unsuitable, and the construction has been relocated to a new area. This episode has left a mark on the garrison's morale. Despite the admonitions of their officers, the soldiers have taken to calling the newfound citadel 'Baelgrip Brok', or Fort of Burnt Mushrooms, a grim reminder of the cost of this venture. I await your guidance as we move forward. Your loyal servant, Durvin Ironmantle
