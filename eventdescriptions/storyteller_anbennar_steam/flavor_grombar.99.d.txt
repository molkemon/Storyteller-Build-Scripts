The religious divide in Grombar has finally come to a boiling point. Each and every day, our people turn from the old ways of Dookan to the rising faith in the Corinites. Though the old clans and their shamans still cling to the ways of Dookan, it is becoming ever clearer that the people have far more faith in Corin than they ever did in Dookan. Therefore, a choice lies before us. Shall we cling to the old ways, and retain the faith of our ancestors? Or shall we move with the times, and join the faith of the slayer of Dookanson? Our humanist politics, however, will smoothen the edges between conflicting sides and will allow us to avoid drowning the country in disaster.
