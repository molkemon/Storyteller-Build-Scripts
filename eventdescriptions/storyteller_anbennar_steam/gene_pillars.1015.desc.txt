Once quarried from the mountains, shaped by powerful magics, and then raised by mechanical hands, this column stood as one among many bearing the weight of the gallery. Through millennia it endured as its fellows cracked and crumbled to dust, their burdens shifting onto the few that remained. Time and seeping water sapped its strength, yet still it held, long after the halls had fallen silent and the works of its makers were forgotten. Now, disturbed by the intrusion of new footsteps, the column can bear the weight no longer. With a sudden, thunderous collapse it gives way, sending the ceiling crashing down upon the unwitting party below.
