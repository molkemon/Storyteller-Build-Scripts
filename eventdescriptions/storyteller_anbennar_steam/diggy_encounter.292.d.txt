"Mushrooms," one of the manuals declares, "can have devastating effects on one's health." That is true, however no one prepared the soldiers for the onslaught of what was to come. As more and more mushrooms came from the dark, they started to pummel the soldiers. Their fists are manageable, but the sheer number of enemies is an overwhelming force to be reckoned with. Those who emerge make a mental note of their strange discovery. Mushrooms, while poisonous, can also be quite feisty.
