The Greenscales are coming. A horde of Greenscale Kobolds has arrived in this domain, and it seems like they are stirring up conspiracy theories of faraway dragons everywhere they go. They have come to escape another domain, and they have brought with them all of the Greenscale classics. a painfully overwrought inferiority complex, a deep distrust of every kobold without a green hue, and a bewildering obsession with fate. The locals, put simply, are pissed. The new migrants claim that we must put aside our differences and allow them to aid us in defending the caverns so that all kobolds might live another day, but there is only so much we are willing to tolerate in the furtherance of koboldkind. The Greenscales demand rights, privileges, and autonomy; they demand to be taken seriously within our tribe and realm. We will allow them to construct extra defenses, certainly, but it is unclear what else we should do. How should we handle these newcomers?
