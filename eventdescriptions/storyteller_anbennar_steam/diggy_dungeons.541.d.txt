Left, right, above, the oozes leap and strike from all angles. With their footwork impaired by the magma and communication difficult over the clamor, the expedition cannot effectively organize to combat the oozes together. Quickly it becomes a battle of attrition as each ooze chips away at the expedition before being destroyed. Though the expedition's manpower exceeds the number of oozes and they eventually cease spawning, many men have died unnecessarily while so close to their fellows and to safety.
