The Revolution has arrived. The government has collapsed under its own weight as the revolutionary masses stormed key government buildings. The monarch was captured in the chaos, and although it initially seemed that they might be able to negotiate their way out of it with modest concessions, soon the revolutionary leaders saw through their ploy and opted to limit their influence by removing their head. Although their diehard supporters were convinced that the ruler would soon grow another one, this did not happen. Instead, a purely temporary Committee of Public Safety has been appointed to guide the new Republic through this revolutionary period. Threats abound, however, for monarchs and despots across the realm fear they may be the Revolution's next victim and are already preparing for war against us. On the bright side, liberal forces are looking to us for assistance in their own struggles, so we will be able to gain support in such a manner.
