A group of men and women of exceptional ability, the Exemplars, are dedicated to protecting the peoples of Bulwar and to maintaining the tenets of their faith by being an example of purity and light for the faithful. The Jaherian Exemplars went to Escann a couple of years ago, following the advice of Lahmaš, the elder of the Lamassu and one of the four beings that Surael tasked with protecting Bulwar. Led by Ecaris Ecarizuir, these Sun Elven Exemplars are some of the most disciplined and experienced warriors in all of Bulwar, ready to answer the temple's call to battle.
