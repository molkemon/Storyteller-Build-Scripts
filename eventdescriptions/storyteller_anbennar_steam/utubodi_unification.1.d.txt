After such a long time coveting the Kakunor Cloud City, the nation at long last has taken possession of this ancient site. Its previous stewards evidently were not equal to the task, thus the task of guarding the city now falls to them. The Cloud City will be restored to its former glory under their care. The rest of the Utubodi Plateau is watching, and they intend to prove to them that they are capable of restoring the city not just for the glory of the wise and enlightened rule of the nation, but for the benefit of the entire Utubodi Plateau. Let them all see what becomes of the Cloud City under their control. Those with clear eyes to see and minds not clouded by impotent jealousy will draw their own conclusions.
