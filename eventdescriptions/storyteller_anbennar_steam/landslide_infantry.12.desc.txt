Fire scars, but it also helps create. What can easily burn down a forest or a village can cook food, fire clay, and burn away infection. While there is a potential for danger with fire, it is a necessary tool; it is foolish to not use it out of fear. It is for this reason the Landslide Infantry have been actively working to use their talents for the good of our nation both on and off the battlefield, and also why our people are now seeing their flame differently.
