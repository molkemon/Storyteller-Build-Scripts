Our hunters have reached their destination and started to recruit local sources, gathering leads in pursuit of their slippery prey. For the longest time, the Borders have had to deal with this tenebrous threat from their eastern neighbor. It is our duty to ensure that they do not become the unintended victims of our liberation of Corvuria. The imperative now is to decide upon our approach to capturing our prey to prevent failure after we have expended so much time and effort.
