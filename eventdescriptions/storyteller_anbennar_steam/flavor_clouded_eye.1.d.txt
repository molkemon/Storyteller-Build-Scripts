Grama Clouded Eye is old.  She is old enough to remember a time before Korgus lived, when the orks still fought each other over scraps in the Dwarovar. She is old enough to remember when the Dookan first came to her in a vision, letting her know of his son's coming. She is old enough to remember when she first laid eyes on Korgus, and knew immediately that he was the Dookan's chosen child. She is old enough to remember when the Dookanson chose her as his personal shaman, and commanded her to spread the word of his coming to the rest of the orks. She is old enough to remember the start of the Greentide, when thousands upon thousands of orks spilled out into Escann, seeking victory and glory for the Dookan and his son. And seeking a better life for themselves and all of the orkish race, away from the poverty of the Dwarovar. And she is old enough to remember when Korgus fell, the true child of her beloved Dookan, slain by the human Corin.  Grama Clouded Eye is old enough to remember all of that. And is old enough to know that her time to pass into the arms of the Great Dookan is fast approaching.
