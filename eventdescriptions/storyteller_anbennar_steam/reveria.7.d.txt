The vile Reverians have plundered our river settlements, our temples, our relics, and our honour. We fought them on the Ynn, on the land, in the forbidden lands, and at every step, we fought and resisted them. Successive generations of our people have died against their blades; yet that was not enough for them; now they have taken the Dome of Stanyrhrada all the way back to their distant Cannor. We will never forgive them. As long as the Ynn flows, our hatred for the Reverians will continue too.
