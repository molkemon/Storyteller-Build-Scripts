Golpalar the Third has succeeded in his rise against the craven and corrupt Raj of the past, and Parusahan Saya is now free to set the stage for the next chapter of the history of Rahen. But not all obstacles can be overcome, and history turns on even the slightest moments. This would prove to remain the case in a most tragic fashion, as Golpalar the Third is struck down with a wasting sickness mere days before his coronation as the Raj. Hemantsen, Golpalar's brother, now stands in Dhenijansar in the place of his older sibling. A firebrand in both the debate halls and the battlefield, he has served his nation in many different areas and is seen by many as the perfect fit to rule. But still, doubt clouds his vision. Will he truly be able to carry on the legacy of his brother, who set the stage for the cleansing of the Raj of all remaining evils? Just then, Jira enters his chambers, and all doubt washes from his mind. He approaches her, taking her paw in the affectionate way he always does. "We now stand ready to make all our dreams come true. His dreams." He looks away for a moment, remembering the ideals his brother had taught him many years ago. Jira smiles, placing a paw against his face. "He may be gone, but we remain, Hema. We will carry on his legacy, and make the Raj into something truly wonderful." She turns to leave, motioning for Hemantsen to follow her into their future together. "We'll make sure we succeed. For Golpalar." Hemantsen looks into her eyes, and finally lets himself smile.
