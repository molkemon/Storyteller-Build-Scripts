There is a darkness that burns in our hearts. The evils of this world stoke those flames, constantly trying to force brother against brother. Yet visatma works to counter that force, and bring balance to the world. Through the actions of good men, and, most crucially, good rulers, we can avert the times of crisis and futility, for through balance and above all order, there is peace. The fighting has at last come to an end. The bloodstains in the High Temples will take years to clean out of the stone, and many families will never know peace again, but at the very least the worst is over, and the nation can begin to recover.
