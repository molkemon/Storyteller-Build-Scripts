The busiest port in the region was recently crowded with the most ships it has ever seen, as a major fleet assembled and departed for Aelantier. But they are not alone, they are merely the first. Across the land, adventurers and settlers are departing for this new (and old) world. This influx to Aelantier has increased the demand for naval supplies; seasoned timber for ships and so on and so forth. As a result, virtually all markets in the region and surrounding areas have reported an increase of the price of naval supplies, and the ripple effect is bound to spread south and east before long.
