The temples have been corrupted to serve their unique needs. The shamans have inscribed rites and runes into the High Temples, now serving as mere chains, ready to heave themselves upon the Great Spirit they now set their eyes upon. An oni warlock's third eye quivers, as if anticipating the bountiful harvest of Chi that awaits it. Now, their mystics and workmen toil tirelessly to prepare the Grand Receptacle, with which they shall seal the ancient terror's spectral form forever more.
