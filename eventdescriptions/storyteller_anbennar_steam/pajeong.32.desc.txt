The people in that domain have had enough of the Neoksamaji. They desire to live their lives as they wish, unrestricted by the capricious laws of the spirits. The spirits in that domain are in anguish over the loss of the population’s once devout adulation. The Neoksamaji naturally desire for us to crack down on these heretics, claiming that this is a breach of the Compact. However, the people will surely react violently to any move against them.
