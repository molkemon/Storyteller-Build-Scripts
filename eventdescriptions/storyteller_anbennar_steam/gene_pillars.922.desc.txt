Your brief foray through the level has led you towards an area where another external breech should be. It was discounted for purposes of ingress due to a jutting shard of structure above the breech, which made traversing the outside too risky. As you enter the room, you notice that you are not the first to visit this location. Just inside the breach is a gargantuan nest, atop which is one of the apex predators of the Charkuchin range: the HinuiRoc. Rocs are known to become incredibly territorial during the breeding season, and reacting to any who may pose a threat to their young with extreme prejudice. It has yet to notice you, but you will have to react quickly, as their senses are keen. Just as you ready to leave, a glimmer reveals that between the woven branches that comprise the nest rest gold and gems.
