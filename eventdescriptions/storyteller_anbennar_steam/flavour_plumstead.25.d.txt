Blessed be the sons of the Dame, for they have proven to be the first and foremost of the settlers' realms of Epednar. They have proven not only that there is merit in the tolerance and integration of the local populace, but that this is a necessity if one is to form a truly stable and prosperous society. The others fell, either to our integrational embrace or to the vengeful armies of the Ynn, forced to either bend the knee to the ruinborn or leave these lands forever.
