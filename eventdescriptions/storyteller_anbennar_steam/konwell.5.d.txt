Our recent investments have paid off handsomely. The Institute is quickly becoming the envy of Cannor, churning out talented and knowledgeable students who quickly become coveted as skilled administrators and advisors. The hiring of the brightest of other races has paid dividends, with our students learning directly from those who have been perfecting their crafts since humans were just starting to play with fire. Let us take this time of prosperity and ensure our place in the world by building and dedicating an entire new wing at the Institute. We can choose to dedicate the wing to the study of transmutation, which will help our production and sieging ability; dedicate the wing to the study of divination, which will help quell rebellions before they happen and help our spy networks; or dedicate the wing to the study of artificery, which will increase the effectiveness of our artillery and fire damage.
