The golem we have found alongside Mintbrick is a perplexing subject for our engineers. It seems that it was built in Ovdal Asra, but the way it is fashioned transcends every notion we had of the talent of the ancient artificers. Every single part of it is crafted with so much attention to detail that it almost seems impossible. Despite our best tries, we could not manage to decode the many secrets that lie within the mechanical frame. The engineers have given the operation to restore it the name Cognita, a name that has also stuck for the golem and is now generally considered its official name.
