Millennia ago, the influence of the Mother Of All and her son Elikhet stretched across the Divenhal Sea to Eborthil and Businor. Sadly, later governors saw the rule of the Steward not as a gift, but as a burden, declaring their independence and imposing their own will on the good citizens there. Some of the rich and powerful saw this as a good thing, but as always the voice of the common people was silenced. However, today is a great day indeed, as once more the hand of Nirakhet reaches her wayward children, and the people of these great cities can live with their voices heard by the government. There will be no more kings in the north. Our message to the kings and emperors in the rest of Cannor is clear: take heed. The people will not stand meek and silent as they see their liberation approaching.
