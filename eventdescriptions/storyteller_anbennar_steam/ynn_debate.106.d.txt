The religious debate is not just a question for this country, it will undoubtedly shape the course of the Ynn or at least its worship for years to come. And as such, it seems to have sprawled beyond our borders, reaching the neighbouring court of another realm. We have no concern if they wish to keep abreast of the goingson in this country, but now it seems they have chosen to interfere. A messenger from the ruler of that realm recently announced to all at court that they think our position is ludicrous and nighheretical as befits such a backwards country. This country banished the messenger out at once, but the stain that interloper spread will not be removed as easily.
