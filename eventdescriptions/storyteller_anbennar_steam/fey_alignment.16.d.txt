The poor and homeless are a constant in every society, every day a struggle for their survival, often only alive because of the kindly donation of those fortunate. While traveling through the capital with your entourage, you pass by another unfortunate soul so alike all the rest, afflicted with the wretched curses of disease and malnutrition. She begs of you for charity of a few coins so she may eat this day, surely not a second thought for one who is decorated with the wealth of a nation. You notice that hidden behind her rags an exquisite amulet that calls out to you; you must have it.
