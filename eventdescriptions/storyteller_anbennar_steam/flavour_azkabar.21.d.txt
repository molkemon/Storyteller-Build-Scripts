With our duty and new purpose reaffirmed, the matter of where to start on purifying the Garden has emerged. Obviously, there are many foul creatures of the dark that linger in Bulwar, but some advisors from the Eclipse Cult have raised an interesting point. Namely, that our nation is filled with many more mundane undesirables. After all, are the dwarves of Bulwar not descendants of the troublesome and meddling Šadru? Who aided enemies of Surakel? Whilst, obviously they are not as evil as a gnoll, they still must be managed. The question of where to direct them soon emerged, and was swiftly answered. Dwarves love their holds, after all.
