The high density of magical energy within the pillar has caused manifestations of elementals to be common throughout its halls. These creatures of pure energy, formed into the shape of miniature storm clouds, hide in the largest chambers and prey on hapless explorers who stumble into them unawares. A group of these elementals now block a passageway to a deeper chamber, crackling and rumbling as the party moves closer. It appears that the creatures are not currently intending aggressive action, but trying to get past them would almost certainly provoke a response.
