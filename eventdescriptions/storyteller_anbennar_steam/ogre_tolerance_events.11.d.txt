An angry letter from a local leader arrives in the capital, bound in bone and large as a blanket. Unfurling the massive missive, an advisor reads aloud: "To our lord’s mightiest overlord, I am scrawling my displeasure about a new quarry near my home. As an ogre, I am a descendant of crafters and builders, yet I cannot help but vomit at the whole affair I've seen unfold. These" The advisor bites their tongue and skips a few choice words. "Which is to say, nearly everything has gone wrong with this nascent operation: discipline, diligence, worker motivation, lack of professionalism, mineral quality, and so much more. It is my hope that you deploy your gnarliest warriors to sack the overseers and right this crooked operation." The advisor sets the letter down, the chamber looking to our ruler. "How should we handle this heckling ogre, my lord?"
