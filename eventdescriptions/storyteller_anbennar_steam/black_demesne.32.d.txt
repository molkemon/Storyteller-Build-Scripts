Wine is one of the most beautiful of the earthly delights. It liberates the imagination, freeing the self from all unnecessary concerns. There can be no doubt that wine has been the mental lubricant that has enabled many of the great innovations of the past. And, of course, it is a noble pleasure, fitting for people of our stature. Now that we have access to the great vineyards and wineries of Lencenor, some in government have proposed distributing the wine to the population as a whole to cement their loyalty and affection for the Skull Throne. Others, however, demand we reserve it for the new nobility; the magical initiates.
