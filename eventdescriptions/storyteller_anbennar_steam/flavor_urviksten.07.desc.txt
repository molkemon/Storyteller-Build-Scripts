Though we know Bera as a Reaver and a slayer, the people of the Dalr Valley know her as a slayer of trolls. When she returned she led the Dalr people against the trolls of the valley, driving them to the brink of extinction. But in their weakness, the Bjarnssons allowed these monstrous brutes to multiply and recover, their descendants becoming what we now call the Fjord Trolls. We shall correct this. By our hand the last trolls of Gerudia will be drowned in the fjords, and their old lands will be populated by Olavish settlers.
