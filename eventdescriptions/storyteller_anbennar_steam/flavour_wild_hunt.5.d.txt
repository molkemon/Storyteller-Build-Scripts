On the plains, sound travels far. This is why it has become undeniable there is a new sound on the plains. It is the sound of our neighbors preparing for war, for the Great Caehn of the Wild Hunt has sent a call from the edges of the Serpent Gift to the forests of Nuzurbokh. He says that the centaurs are stronger and wiser now, and that at the edge of the plains are a people whose very existence is a crime against Irdaeos. The call of the Great Caehn echoes over the quiet waving of the grasses and the gusts of wind. The Great Caehn says we are once again to wage war against the Triunics.
