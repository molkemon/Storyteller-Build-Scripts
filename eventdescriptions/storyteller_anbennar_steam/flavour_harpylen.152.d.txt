When fashioning a delicate instrument such as a lyre, great care must be taken with the choice of proper materials for all its parts. But before the individual components are combined to fashion the lyre, first a decision must be made regarding its intended size. Should it be small but melodious, easily held with one hand? Should it be a proud instrument, easily as long as the wing of a harpy? Or should its proportions be truly enormous, easily towering over the musician? Which size should the lyre have?
