Our introductions concluded, and it was time for our leader to negotiate on our behalf. Behind closed doors, many harsh words were left unsaid, in the understanding that both tribes must set aside some differences if we are to move forward as one. Life under Her canopy is a constant struggle for survival; as such, the most valuable gift is that of knowledge. Every tribe member present witnesses the pact being sealed, centuries of knowledge being shared across tribes, our cultures embracing a new future. A future in which all work to overcome whatever perils exist within Her domain or beyond.
