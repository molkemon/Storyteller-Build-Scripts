More of the containment cells for the experiments of this level line the hall, each with its door blasted open or lying on its side in the middle of the passageway. Deeper into the corridor, a soft orange glow emanates from inside one of the cells, and a weeping noise can be heard from within the room. Peering inside, what seems to be a small elven girl is crying on the cold metallic floor, looking up at the party with big, weepy eyes. It is impossible to say where she came from, but the slight red stains around her seated frame do not inspire confidence in the adventurers.
