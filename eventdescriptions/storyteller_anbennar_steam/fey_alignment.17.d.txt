Those who do good deeds are not rewarded in kind, but know that their actions have brought ease to those who need help the most. But today, the good deed you have done will be rewarded, for hidden behind the illusion of a poor beggar was a powerful and dreadful Fey. Your generosity will be rewarded this day with a potion most desired; a Fey Mead that may shield you, once, from the ire of the Fey and their plots. It may only be used once, but you will forever remember that your generosity may one day save your life.
