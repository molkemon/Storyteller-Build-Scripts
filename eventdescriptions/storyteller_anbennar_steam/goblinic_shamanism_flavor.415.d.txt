For thousands of years, Goblins have been forced to serve and fight the orks, who would enslave and kill our people for the sake of their imprisoned god Dookan. This is why it’s come as a surprise when we’ve discovered that orks have recently not only abandoned the worship of their god, but have accepted him as dead, believing that he left unto orks the "orkish soul" and that one can be reincarnated with it, and those can eventually become Bulgu, a kind of orkish hero that they sing about.  Of course, this means that Dookan has been slain by one of our own gods, which is a truly pleasing end to that which tormented us for so long. Many shamans believe that Dookan’s slayer has passed down pieces of Dookan’s remaining soul to the goblins, and those with the "goblin soul" have been attempting absolutely outrageous feats just to be considered "heroes'', all to maybe get a nice song made for them from a shaman. Though most merely die stupid deaths, there have already been a few who have been seen as heroic enough to be immortalized in song by our shamans, and these songs are really rather nice to listen to, and they help our people feel more proud of our history and what we are capable of.
