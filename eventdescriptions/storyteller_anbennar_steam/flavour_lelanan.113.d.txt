The Fey are a very beautiful force, ephemeral, powerful, and aloof, uninvolved with the world. Sometimes they change, though. They start writing love poems, delivering them to the doorstep of nosy mages, talking about their gorgeous elven lover and the deepwoods from which they came. The Archfey were not pleased that they would fall in love with a mere elf. So the Fey hero valiantly defended their lover, but tragically, was forced to swiftly flee. They conjured an old connection, emerged so far to the south, but their lover had made it with them, and the weird island halflings nearby seemed safe. So I thank you for giving us a home and protecting us if the Archfey try to destroy our love. In return, I have attached some notes on magic and will always brag about my lover if you wish.
