In spite of our recent conquest of the Middle Dwarovar, the Citrine dwarves have remained intransigent in their refusal to deal with us. While progress with Seghdihr has been slow, we have had more promising results with other dwarven holds which used to be part of the defunct Segbandal; particularly the exiled monarchs of said holds, who have thus far been identified to be living among the dwarves of Seghdihr and Verkal Gulan. While they will likely request concessions, they have proven far more willing to deal with our officials than the Citrine dwarves, and could provide considerable clout in our efforts to secure working relations with the holds of the Middle Dwarovar. Their expatriate communities seem to hold them in high esteem, and they have indicated a willingness to submit to our authority in exchange for their desires being met.
