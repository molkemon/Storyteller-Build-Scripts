Analysts previously predicted that the conquest of AzkaSur, while temporarily disruptive to regional trade, would ultimately allow for direct mercantile access to the Middle Dwarovar via Seghdihr. Any animosity which might have arisen from our military campaigns, it was assumed, would be offset by an evenhanded treatment of the conquered AzkaSuran people. It was also assumed that simple economic selfinterest would smooth over any wounds created by this campaign. Seghdihr has proven these predictions to be inaccurate. Though initial diplomatic overtures gained some traction, all delegations were suddenly and abruptly expelled, with the gates to Seghdihr shut after they had left. Though the exact cause of this turnaround is unknown, based on commentary made by Citrine guards to our delegates, it is believed that the fact of the Overboss being a goblin was a major contributing factor to this sudden animosity. Whatever the explanation, the gates of Seghdihr have yet to reopen, and all attempts at contact have been ignored.
