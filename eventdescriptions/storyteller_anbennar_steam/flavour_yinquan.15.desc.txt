Dread news, ruler. The HuliJing have informed the state of the troubles they face when trying to leave the Mulim forest. Apparently, when leaving the treeline, the HuliJing are struck by blinding headaches and full-body weakness, lingering for a time once they head back into the forest. They wish to wander the countryside; this simply cannot do. This strange illness is strongest when they depart towards the northeast, towards a specific domain. Since that is now part of the state, that is where our investigation will start.
