Katyradz has failed, and the great city of Karassk remains firmly under the grip of the old kingdom. Our officials hoped that this would mean the collapse of the Red Party, but they have been disappointed. The traitors have been quick to switch their allegiance away from the would-be conquerors and towards the successful defenders. They now advocate for subjugation to our old overlord, who has usurped the role of Asarta in this War of Colours, changing a threat to our independence for another.
