Seek, then, brothers in the arcane mysteries. A dragon ascends not on its power alone, but is carried through the blessings of fellow travelers. The realm and the country have consecrated a growing friendship through the three-day Rite of Bonding, which forms an empathic link between the two Acolytes. At the end, their sacred vow of friendship and stalwart loyalty is secured through the sacrifice of a hundred healthy animals. Marking each other in the mystically infused blood, they declare their solemn allegiance for the whole world to see.
