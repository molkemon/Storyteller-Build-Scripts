Surael teaches us that all who serve him are to be treated equal, but still those closest to the Divine Herald have long been the officers of the Jaddari. But times have changed and the empire has expanded. We have seen the rulership of the Sun Elves in one realm, the Khet in another, and the human kings in a third. Of all, it seems that the harimari have uniquely developed the skills needed to administer an expanding empire, centuries of philosopher kings slowly honing a uniquely styled bureaucracy. Should we draw upon their skills?
