As our armies marched into the vanquished Hold of Grôzumdihr, they noticed a strange gate at its entrance, with Dwarven words inscribed in a strangely lit green stone. It seemed not worth the effort to pry those jewels from their place, and so when one of the more learned shamans with some understanding of that strange tongue entered, she could decipher some of what it said. This revealed an odd discovery; it looks that this hold welcomes all. It was inevitable, of course, but the dwarves are strange to have invited it. Perhaps that is why they fell so easily. Their seeming disposition did mean our looting was somewhat less violent than usual, which left a strange set of papers unburned and unshredded. The monarch has claimed them for themselves, and only time will tell what they may learn from them.
