Rebels everywhere. The relic lord is active, an extreme threat. Send help now. Urgent. Urgent. The Oren Nayiru has been a thorn in the side of the powers controlling Taychend for years now, as they make inroads in downtrodden communities all across the region. However, many did not believe they had the strength to truly make a push to conquer their enemies. The many have now been proven wrong. Over the past two years, Elrandar Silverspite Kalinzuir, a half-Ruinborn, half-Sun Elven mercenary turned true believer, has made a secret bid to unite the disparate cells of the Oren Nayiru under a single banner, taking on the title of Kamrayakval. Gathering the soldiers of the faith to crown him under an Eclipse, it is clear that he was successful, and that his secret weapon is none other than the Armour of Yodhan itself. The strength of a hundred elves, the durability of steelscale, the power over gravity itself—the Kamrayakval is said to be clad in a living dragon, its shapeshifting abilities twisted, contorted into armour form. The grim work of a Precursor Slaver Noble. Taychend’s latest Great Relic Lord stands at the head of a host of freed slaves and peasant revolutionaries. Time will tell if he lives up to the success of his predecessors.
