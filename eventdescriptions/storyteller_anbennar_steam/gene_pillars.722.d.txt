Currents lit with swirling bioluminescent plankton help the party navigate down towards the deeper floors, as the tidal pull draws their light deeper into the water column. As you follow their trail deeper into the pillar, a grisly scene reveals itself to you: skeletons. Countless skeletons, all of explorers, frozen in place by the buildup of sediment. Some are shockingly close to the water surface. Some are next to tunneling tools and weapons still in their skeletal grip. Some are broken to pieces by aquatic scavengers. Some look almost intact. Perhaps worst of all, some look fresh. As these bodies pass by, you cannot help but think the services of a cleric may prove useful as you press forward.
