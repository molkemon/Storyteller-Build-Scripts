For the past several years, the realm of Brrtekuh has been wracked by internal chaos and disputes, dealing with everything from political cliques threatening to push the clan into a warlord era to small street gangs destabilizing streets of important economic urban centers to accumulate wealth for themselves. Yet unbeknownst to major parts of the confused populace, three gnolls were assured of their inherited, or rather, consumed birthright. The Yzhil, who has consumed the limbs of Brrtekuh, subsequently inherited her great might and aspiring valor. Inspiring those who have succumbed to the lingering instinct of the spirits they were forged from and stamping down on the rest who were too far gone. The Zakr consumed her head, with which she presented her ferocious beauty to the world as well as that which howled both inspiring and comforting to the pack in the darkness before sunrise. Almost immediately, they convinced the gnolls who had found no faith to be had left after the death of the great pack mother to enter the fray once more, serving the utmost duty of continuing her name even in vain. And equally important was the Oxh, who consumed the rest of Brrtekuh, who has her heart that beats in grueling battles as well as the mournful aftermath for her kin and is the center of the body, connecting the entire body and pumping lifeblood throughout her form to ensure all properly function. Even if all have been convinced, they have been proving that unrelenting faith requires a sturdy foundation, with which the Oxh will fulfill. With this, the Mazukxiem was established, a council of her most trusted gnolls led by the Oxh, Yzhil, and Zakr continuing her legacy in spirit.
