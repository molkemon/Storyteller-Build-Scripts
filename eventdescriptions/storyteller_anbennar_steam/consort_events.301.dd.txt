When the consort married into the royal house of the ruling dynasty there were those who were skeptical. However, the young spouse gained the confidence of the common people, almost effortlessly, and grew well into the role as one of the ruling class of the realm. It has now been over ten years since the union took place. Even those who do not adore the consort have come to respect them as one of their own; the wedding anniversary of the elevated couple was highly anticipated. It is smiling gently at nobles and commoners alike that the lord and their consort graciously accept gifts of varying splendor.
