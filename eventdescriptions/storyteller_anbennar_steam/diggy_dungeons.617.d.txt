You proceed to follow the cargo tunnel towards the other facilities. As you go along the tracks laid down by workers potentially eons ago you begin to notice two things. First, the rather quiet atmosphere totally devoid of life usually found in most shafts like this; and second, the appearance of more and more cobwebs around you. and soon, one of your scouts notices several pairs of shiny, red eyes that keenly seem to follow his every move as well as cocoons. Quite a few cocoons that seem to hang in the distance and that seem awfully recent. He barely has time to shout out a warning, though, as suddenly a dozen gigantic spiders descend upon you. They seem to have built a nest in the connecting corridor and you are but the latest prey that fell into their poisonous fangs.
