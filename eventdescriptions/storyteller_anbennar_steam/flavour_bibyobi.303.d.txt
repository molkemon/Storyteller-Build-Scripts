One hell of a fight. Hundreds dead on our side compared to one dead hydra. It must have had fifteen heads total by the time it was laid down for good, so a few score men per head, give or take. I am sure the people back home will reckon that a fair trade. Fairer yet is the harvest of this beast. I overheard the mage they keep around describing all these parts to the Esteemed Leader, starting with those big heads.
