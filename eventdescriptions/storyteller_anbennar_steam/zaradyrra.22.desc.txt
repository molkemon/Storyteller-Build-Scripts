The two hunters stood side by side, marveling at the bloodsplashed statue of a regal lizardfolk before them. One removed the ponderous hand from his chin to address the other. "Is it truly defaced, or do you think the blood may symbolize that underpinning even the most noble adherent is a cavalier attitude towards collateral damage which perpetuates our civil war?" The second hunter turned to his companion with an incredulous expression upon his face. "Do you wax poetic about the smell of your own excrement?"
