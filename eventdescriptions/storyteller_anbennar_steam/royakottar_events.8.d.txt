The Ursibadur family have always had a close but troubled relationship with the expert caravaneers and traders of the Nymkorai. After all, while the Ursibadur may drink the wine, the Nymkorai procure it. The Ursibadur have naturally always sought to escape from this reliance, instead seeking independent traders from cities and kingdoms outside the city of Royakottar. Recently, one of these independent contractors met an unfortunate end, their caravans ambushed and slaughtered by bandits. The Nymkorai say that they had nothing to do with it; the Ursibadur want retribution.
