Through our might we have united the lands of the Primal Harimari. The Royal Harimari, our northern cousins, have grown soft, and Haless has forgotten our true strength. This is a tragedy, one we shall soon make right. We have already proven that we are mightier than our fellow harimari and by extension obviously mightier than humans. Let it be known, the languid weakness of yesteryear is gone. From now on the Harimraj will know our strength, and the humans will be put in their place.
