The Agškastu Rifle is a true marvel of artistry. The interior of its rifle barrel is covered in minuscule inscriptions that use a mix of gunpowder and anniwan, a uniquely Bulwari mixture of powdered Dames tear and lapis lazuli, to power potent conjuration and abjuration magics. Such magics create not a conventional explosion or bullet, but instead conjure a miniature portal through which the air within the rifle is transported to another plane, while abjurations prevent the resulting void from instantly collapsing due to pressure differential. As a result, a perfect void sphere is formed, hurtling towards the target and on impact creating a powerful implosion, which tears asunder any matter it encounters.
