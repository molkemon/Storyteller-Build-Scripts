Evidently, the people of that domain think the war against the savages hasn’t progressed well enough. They have taken up what paltry arms they possess, and marched on the savages themselves, engaging a war party before our soldiers could even locate it. Doubtlessly, they will be slaughtered to the last man; fools, this is why you leave the fighting to soldiers. However, their sacrifice will pave the way to annihilating the weakened war party.
