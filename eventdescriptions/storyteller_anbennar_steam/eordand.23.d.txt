While the grand city of Svemel is under control, there is still an influential figure who holds sway over the Rzentur: Varlengeilt, the Dragon Prince. A young green dragon born of the previous DragonGod Drozmagog, Varlengeilt holds great sway over the hearts of the Drozma Tur faithful, and should they wish could agitate them further against us. However, for now he remains silent within his lair, a heavily guarded palace in Svemel, watching the city from its pinnacle. Should the ruler wish, the palace and halls of worship may be looted for whatever may be found. But Varlengeilts hoard is small, for after the death of Drozmagog it was split between the feuding groups who each wished to raise the infant dragon. It would be wise to consider offering a donation instead, as they do, to demonstrate your goodwill. Gifts of all kinds, from lowly vegetables to priceless gems to even willingly offered brides are given to Varlengeilt, to be used to his discretion. Should it be known that we recognize Varlengeilts spiritual authority, perhaps all of Rzenta will soon forget our conquest?
