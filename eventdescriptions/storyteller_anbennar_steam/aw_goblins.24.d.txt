Might makes right. Such is the law of our realm, and we need none other. It is that law that we have demonstrated so clearly to the skulking goblins of their lands, and their weakness gave proof to their lack of any right to be there. Their claws and teeth were no match for our own, and by nightfall, the lord’s forces ensured the nest of skulkers would trouble us no more. They did not pay our ruler tribute in loyalty or in coin, and so they paid in blood.
