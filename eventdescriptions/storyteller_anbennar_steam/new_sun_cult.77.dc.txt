It was not so long ago that the monarch declared to the people that they would bring order to the country and lead them to victory against the invaders. There was so much to be done though, that even after ten years they could only do so much. There has been reform here and there, more recruiting and training. There is some new trade flowing into the cities and very few families are seen now attempting to move away. It is not the grand sweeping fix that the monarch promised but at least it is better than nothing. Let us hope it is enough.
