Nearing the top of the pillar, the party reaches a dark room. As they carefully edge forwards, a light appears in the darkness. Before anyone can react, the creature suddenly shoots forward, as if pulled by an unknown force. A withered hand reaches from the light and grabs the creature by the neck. Lifting the poor crying thing into the air, the thing in the dark reveals itself. The warrior from the visions stands before the party, his body now decayed and decrepit, countless years having worn it down. He glows and shimmers with power, the same spiritual energy flowing throughout the many rooms in this pillar. "Ah, the twelfth lock. The last remnants of my prison." He speaks, not acknowledging the party’s existence. An abnormally wide grin splits his face. The creature struggles in his grasp, kicking its little legs and paws. The man tightens its grip, and the creature’s crying is reduced to whimpering, then silence. Grabbing the creature’s collar with his other hand, he rips it off. The hand releases. The creature falls to the ground with a thud. It lays there, silent and still. The party stares, not daring to utter even a single word. But the Master of the Tower has no such reservations. "Freedom." He crushes the bauble hanging from the collar. And the room is filled with a terrible, blinding light.
