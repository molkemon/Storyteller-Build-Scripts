After many years of hard toil excavating the soil and stone of this forsaken land, we have found it. A great Precursor ruin beneath the sands, the source of the artifacts the Fograc brought to us. Although many of the buildings and towers are collapsed in the bowels of the earth, they still hold marvels inside that have made our expedition well worth it. From now on, this land will be known as Cadcimn, as it holds memories of the Precursor past as yet unknown to us from Eordand.
