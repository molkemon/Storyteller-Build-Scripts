Our armies stand tall and shining outside our capital. Diplomats arrive every day from neighboring princes seeking alliances and discussing the oncoming maelstrom. While the Empire sleeps at the stroke of midnight, spurred by our lullabies, we prepare to sing another type of song. one of confrontation, of dominance, of boldness. We have sent a messenger to the Imperial Court, demanding privileges to become King of Salt and establish our superiority of the Borders. When they wake up, they will choose the melody they wish us to sing. one of harmonic triumph, or one of strident war.
