A time of chaos has come to the Empire of the Jadd. For many years the empire has expanded both east and west, accumulating the faithful of many people and cultures. Each culture has their own needs to be tended, its own visions for the shape of the Empire. The DivineHerald's task has been to keep the balance even as a rift grows in their empire, but a single mortal, even one who is the voice of Surael himself, can only do so much to stem the tide of disunity. It is during their reign that the empire has finally broken into allout civil war, with both east and west vying for dominance. Historians from Cannor and Haless alike have already dubbed this event the Deioderan, after the fiery dance traditional to the Jaddari. One wrong step and you will get horribly burned.
