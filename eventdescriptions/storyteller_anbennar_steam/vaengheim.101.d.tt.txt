And so it was in the days of a certain year, that the troll named Jordis FrogCatcher did come to the cities of Gerudia, demanding gold and flesh in tribute. And Jordis did slay and eat any man who refused her. A furrier named Gunnar ran to Vængheim, and he did beseech the monarch to save his home from Jordis and her greed. Then the monarch rose and said, “Jordis has slain my people and stolen from them. And so she has stolen from me. I will fly to Jordis, and she will pay me weregeld, or she will die.” And then the monarch gathered the maidens of Vængheim for a great hunt.
