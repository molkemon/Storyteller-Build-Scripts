Halflings were some of the first foreign people to adopt the Rinta Seekers faith into their lives. Since then, they have flourished in their studies. Interestingly enough, the Vyzemby have begun mixing in their old faith with the Rinta Seekers beliefs. Many consider this to be unjust, while others are intrigued and see this as the Vyzemby way of studying knowledge. The lord is told that a decision is required to make sure these slight tensions do not bubble further.
