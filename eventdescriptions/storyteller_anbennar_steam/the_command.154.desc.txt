The KenuIke is tense. Angry stares and sullen, sotto voce whispers—the Wolf and Boar marshals stare at each other with particular disdain. One misplaced remark leads the Boar Marshal to leap at the Wolf, until the Lion Marshal separates them both. Then the Grand Marshal pounds the ruler's fist on the table, and issues a simple order. no fighting in the War Room. Everyone settles back into place to glower at one another. Have things really gotten so bad, so quickly? The Grand Marshal knows the score. the rumors that have been swirling around the warcamps, the ad hoc investigations each Command has carried out against the others, the accusations flung with every minor defeat, the breakdown of order and inner peace. Someone—no one knows who—leaked the document that has come to be known as the Shinukhorchi Missive. In a matter of months, it has proliferated across the Command, bringing panic and chaos to the realm. Demands for greater action taken to suppress Shamanism have flown in like arrows, many even from human subjects. Ridiculous. Fearmongering. And yet, sometimes even the Grand Marshal must bend to the will of the people. Sighing, the ruler reviews the accusations.
