In ascending, the initiate hones each of the Three Aspects, but beware. The Passions lay their traps, and the clouded mind is shunned by the chorus of Discord. The realm understands that power comes from risk. There is no such thing as safe magic, and even the most powerful mages are easily hobbled by their fears. Unfortunately, the arcane arts have not served the Acolyte well in this case, leaving former wellsprings of power tainted and unusable.
