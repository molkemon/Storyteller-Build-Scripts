Alan Leapdon found him in a townhouse: Henric Darkwood, captain of the Ynn Expedition. "Do you have your report?" "Aye, one moment." Henric fished through a pocket and snatched a journal. Opening it, he gestured for Alan to ask his questions. "What's the political situation?" "In the north, we have fighting between folks worshiping the River Ynn, and a dragon cult, dedicated to a god named Varlengeilt." Dragon cults were not an unfamiliar concept to the Moormen, given the constant raids by the fanatical kobolds for their hoards. But to see a civilized people worship a dragon, a living one, that was a strange thing indeed for Alan. "Interesting. Any weaknesses?" "Varlengeilt is powerful but vain. He will not see us as a threat if we do not give him reason. If we strike hard and fast, we could secure Svemel before while his forces are busy with rebellions and the rest of the Ynnic elves. Then, once we have the city, put down this dragon. The River Worshipers will be much more willing to work with us after that."
