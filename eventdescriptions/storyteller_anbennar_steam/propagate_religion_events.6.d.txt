With the Ravelian Faith increasingly becoming the dominant religion of our country, many of our government officials, nobles and other notables have begun to adopt Cannorian notions of kingship, Cannorian names and titles. Many have in fact started to refer to our leader as a King, even though our official state religion is still established. Some advisors now suggest that it might be prudent to go along with this. If we start referring to our own country as a kingdom, it might ease relations with the many Ravelian countries around the world.
