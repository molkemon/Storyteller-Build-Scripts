Grimmun Gemhorn had never been on a hunt before, having just recently finished his training as a scholar under Lorek Runehand. It was for this very reason that he needed to go on this hunt. Lorek had disappeared in the aftermath of the Hunter in the Deep's attack on Gor Vazumbrog, and Grimmun wanted to be present when the company faced her down again, if the dragon that the scouts reported seeing in this deadend cave was the purple menace herself. The beast that showed its head in answer to Grimmun's thoughts was not purple, though it was suitably enormous. a drakon, like those that had taken up residence under Gor Vazumbrog and nearly killed Lorek once before. This one, though, was so massive that the scouts could be forgiven for mistaking it for a true dragon. The company readied their spears and thanked their divinities and good fortune that the everflowing waterskins that they had prepared to dilute the dragon's poison would serve just as well against this creature. When the battle was done and the monster run through, Grimmun found his worst fears realized. Lorek had apparently stumbled to relative safety outside the hold during the dragon's attack, only to be attacked in the caverns outside and dragged back to its lair for later consumption. Grimly, Grimmun grasped his mentor's loupe and began examining the unique contours of the dead drakon's skull, noting the way its geometry prevented bullets from penetrating as deeply.
