One of our councilors, traveling through a distant province, has stumbled upon a peculiar practice among its halforkish population. They had picked up some legal practices from the human side of their ancestry, and were refusing to allow the use of traditional clan courts that have served our orkish society so well for so long. It may be detrimental to let them continue their brand of justice that exists outside of the purview of our lawmakers, but at the same time, the practice is limited within their own tightknit communities and does not seem to be harming anyone outside of it.
