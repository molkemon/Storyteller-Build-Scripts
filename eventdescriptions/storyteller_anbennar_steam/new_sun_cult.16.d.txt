The recent instability of our state has finally started to bear its rotten fruit. Despite the Sun Elves' right to rule, we have always been far too few to run every aspect of government. As such, we have been forced to employ our lesser to pad out the administrative staff's numbers. Now those very same whelps we lifted out of barbarity have turned their back on us. Officials and ministers from inside the court and across the nation have presented us with demands and threats of rebellion. They ask for greater influence over the realm and for reform of the state. There are even whispers that they would relegate the Chosen people to a ceremonial role, becoming rulers themselves in all but name. Well; they shall have our answer.
