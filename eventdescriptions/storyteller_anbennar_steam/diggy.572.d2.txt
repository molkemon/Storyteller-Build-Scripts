"No, you dimwit," shouted an old dwarf, stepping forward from the crowd. "That's not how you pronounce that name." Two guards quickly jumped the old greybeard, muzzling him as they dragged him out of the hall. The damage was already done, however, and doubtful murmurs filled the room. Did the ruler really not know how to interpret the old runes? They seemed so simple. The rumours would soon spread beyond the hall, and the ruler became somewhat of a laughingstock.
