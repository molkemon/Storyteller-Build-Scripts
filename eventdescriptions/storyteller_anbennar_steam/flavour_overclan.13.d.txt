Our continued military expansions have seen an increase in military officers; an expected and mostly unremarkable development, if not for one note. With the conquest of Sareyand, many of these officers have begun to take an interest in elven military structure and tactics. These officers have cited historical military successes by the elves across Cannor, Bulwar, and even further afield as adequate reason to study the methods of our defeated adversaries. Some have even initiated a program to acquire and convert facilities such as the Ash Palace into ones suitable for the state's purposes. Though some members of the general staff have expressed apprehension toward this initiative, their argumentation has proven weak, and their opinion has ultimately become a minority position.
