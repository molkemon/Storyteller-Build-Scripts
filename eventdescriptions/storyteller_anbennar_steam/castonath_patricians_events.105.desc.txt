An advisor, a wealthy Castonath patrician from a long line of merchants, has long been a close ally and confidant to the ruler. Through years of service in Castonath, they have proven not only that they are loyal beyond reproach, but also that they have a keen eye for finances and investment. They would be best able to continue serving the state if we were to formally grant them control over trade and associated investment policy over our entire nation, a worthy challenge after having led the region to prosperity for so many years.
