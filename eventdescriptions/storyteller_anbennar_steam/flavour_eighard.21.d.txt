A massive force stands in the clearing where, in a time before any of them can remember, the Amber Princess watched the archfey escape. She stands in the same place they once did, and opens a very similar portal. The massive army pours through, and finds themselves in a very strange place. It is a forest not unlike the one they were just in, but somehow even more vibrant. Birds sing, the flowers bloom, and a great host of elves surrounds the portal. They clearly anticipated the arrival of the invaders. These are the Deepwoods, and its inhabitants.
