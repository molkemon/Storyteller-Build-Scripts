The lord got called down into the depths of Verkal Dromak to meet with the brightest of minds beyond the Sleepers. The altar has sent out energy waves for some time now but only recently the Sleepers noticed that the intervals are getting shorter and the waves stronger. And now they all have gathered for a meeting since they have come to the conclusion that when the waves will come so fast that the simple dwarf can't see the time between them the altar might blow up and destroy a substantial part of our hold.
