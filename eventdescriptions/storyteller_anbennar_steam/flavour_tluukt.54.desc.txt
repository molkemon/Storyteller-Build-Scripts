The sun hangs high in the sky as the ruler issues their challenge to the other leader, aiming to assert the supremacy of our pack at the point of a sword, or more appropriately, a lightning bolt. They seem surprised, distracted, and perhaps even enamored. There are no laws or traditions among gnolls dictating that the other leader is bound to accept our challenge, but accept they do. Combat between packleaders rightly ends with death. The display of mercy is a weakness all its own, and thus only one will emerge from this magical combat alive.
