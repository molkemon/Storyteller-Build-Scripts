It is over.  Our people cry, the forest rises, and the Fey laugh at our dying people. Where once villages, covered fields, and forges stood tall, all that remains is ash and rubble. Farms are barren, crops are destroyed, and the domesticated animals scream in pain. From this corruption, it seems, our nation has been quenched, but the nation we could be, that of the Emerald, is born in its place.
