The southern queens grew tipsy on Bulwari beer and dreamed of cavorting like elves; in such folly is the death of the harpy way. We have reached into the heart of Ayarallen and seized it, reclaiming the ancient mantle of Firanya and refusing to see our culture adulterated at the whim of some southern matriarch. Let our enemies blanch when a true harpy cry first rings out across their valley; for them remains no mercy.
