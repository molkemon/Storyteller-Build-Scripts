The Ynn is holy, our savior from destruction and our protector even now. But that does not mean it does not carry detritus, at times, it is the nature of the great River that all; good and bad; will find themselves within its flow in time.  Thus, our acts today, hurling Cannorian books, pamphlets, and other publications was not pollution. It was purification, the Ynn once again saving this land by burning the seeminglyinnocuous works that would in fact lead to its destruction.
