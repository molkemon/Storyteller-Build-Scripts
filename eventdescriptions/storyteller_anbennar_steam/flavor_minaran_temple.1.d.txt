Minara has always been a somewhat controversial figure in the Regent Court, and to many, was seen as an excuse to partake in hedonistic practices. While the Corinites never agreed with the Minaran priesthood, the Ravelian Church has surprisingly treated it with open arms, even proclaiming Minara as a saint on the same level as their benefactor, the goddess Ryala. With the official change of religion within the Minaran Temple, the ruling monarch has decided to officially change what was once the Temple of Minaras Bosom into something less crude.
