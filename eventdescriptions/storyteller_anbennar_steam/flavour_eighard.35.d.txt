And I hate it, Allara said, absentmindedly playing with Keldora’s hair. The pair had stopped for the night; they’d found a small clearing with a fallen log and set up a campfire. You always say that, Keldora replied, smiling. Well it’s true, isn’t it? It’s too damn cold. I wish we were being sent to the Ynn like everyone else, but no, this task is simply too important to let us go with everyone else. Did you see the people they were sending?
