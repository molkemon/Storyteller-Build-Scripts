The various advisors that serve the court are paid well for their brilliant minds, as they pursue inquiries into the most fruitful paths forward for our nation. But there are downsides to employing such curious creatures. One of them has recently begun to note our ruler’s late night excursions and unusual nature, and so took it upon themselves to investigate further. If they were simply a peasant with a wandering mind, this would not be troubling, due to the longstanding measures taken to preserve the identity of our lord. However, likely due to their proximity to our lord, the advisor was able to gather evidence and nurture suspicion, until they fully pierced the veil. They have now realized our lord’s true nature, and are threatening to expose them before the country and the world.
