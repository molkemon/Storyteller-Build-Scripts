"There have been days throughout our past where the forces of the dark have sought to tear us asunder from within. They divided our people through deceit and with their lies they sought to corrupt those we freed, to lure them back into the shadows. To prevent this we instituted laws to ensure that while all voices were heard, none would be able to taint the radiance of Surael. The first and foremost law is that none may question the truth of Surael, as his divine being is the foundation of this earth and his will is law. With this, along with our inclusion of other cultures and their beliefs, we purged the darkness from within.  And now, with this stable foundation of law and order, we look to the future and its many challenges. To face it we will look at the great empires of old and learn from their faults and strengths to forge a code of law that is just and fair and imbued with Surael’s Light."
