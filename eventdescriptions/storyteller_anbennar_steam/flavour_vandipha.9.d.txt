The lord sits upon their throne, eating slices of sundried fish from a bowl held by one of their slaves. They look down at the slave for a moment and smiles, revealing their teeth. The slave flinches for a moment, then smiles back, her grin shaky. You, girl. How long have you worked here? A month, my lord. That explains why you are so plainly adorned. The lord swallows the slice of fish in their hand, then takes off a jeweled ring and hands it to the slave girl. When you next make it to the market, buy yourself some fine jewelry. You should fetch a good price for this ring. I… I do not. There is no need to thank me, girl. This is simply your just reward for obedience.
Later that week, a Cannorian merchant visited the palace to speak to the lord. He was greeted in the entrance hall by the slave girl, to whom he bowed. My lady, your beauty impresses me. You must be a Mengi princess, here at their pleasure. May I speak to your lord? She looked shocked, and from the balcony, boisterous laughter roared out. The merchant looked up and saw the lord wiping a tear from their eye, chuckling. How poor of a place must Cannor be, that this slave looks like a princess to you? They laughed again. She’s not wearing nearly enough gold.
Obedience is the place of slaves. Those who obey are to be rewarded, and to receive a piece of the opulence they help create. Those who disobey are never to be spoken of again. The Umsharaj; the Empire of Seas; will be remembered forever as a land where even slaves are adorned with fine jewelry that foreign kings could never hope to own. From the Summer Isles to the distant home ports of the Treasure Fleet, never has Halann witnessed such wealth, such grandeur, such splendour.
