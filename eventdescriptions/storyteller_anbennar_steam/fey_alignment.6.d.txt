The Fey are known for their extravagant and random behaviour; today a gaggling bunch of beautiful and horrifying beings have decided to party with you, helping themselves to your personal belongings. This merry celebration is cut short however, as a rather loud and especially narcissistic Fey enters the party.  'Who thought to start the festivities without me. Shame on you, all of you.,' it turns their attention to you. 'You there, servant, introduce me and serve me some drink.'
