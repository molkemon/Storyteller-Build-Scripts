We have received alarming reports of bandits in the realm leaving behind pillaged caravans, looted villages, and dangerous roads. While the local guard usually handles bold brigands, they have never encountered a foe as agile as centaurs. Striking from nowhere, disappearing like lightning, and evading any counterattack with ease, these galloping criminals seem unstoppable. Our hawkish generals drummed up plans to clear out any centaur clans in the area, regardless of innocence. Cautious about expending troops and possible collateral, a cadre of officials recommended outfitting and training local militias to bring the centaur criminals to justice. Finally, a few diplomatic advisors heralded entreating the bandits, getting to the bottom of why they took up arms to begin with. The people cry for action; how shall we proceed?
