Your people have not all obeyed you perfectly. However, their children have. As organized experts, children have started paying taxes for their families. They deliver grain, meat, metal, and gold to local governors. This is disturbing, to say the least; even more disturbing is that it seems to be working. One night, you come across the same gnoll from years back. She smiles, and her features shift until you recognize the hag Hireet Smoketusk, one of the three hags of Yezel Mora. "The children of this country do not obey you. They obey me. Swear to obey me, and I will release my hold. Refuse, and I will make them burn the fields, salt the earth, and then kill their parents."
