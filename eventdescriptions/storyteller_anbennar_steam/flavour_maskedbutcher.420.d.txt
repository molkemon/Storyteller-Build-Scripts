What do you want from me?  I have given you treasure. I have granted you strength beyond compare. Should you wish it, all Halann could be crushed beneath your foot.  Yet still, you seek more?  Are their faces not enough? You have taken all that they are; that they were. You hold their very selves in your hand, equipped to grant some twisted perversion of the things that made them, them.  What else do you want? I have no more boons to give. I would offer my own face, would you be able to reach it, but would that sate your desire?  Or perhaps it is simpler. Perhaps, all you seek is my acknowledgement. My meaningless, token praise.  Well then, it is yours. Take it. I have no need of it any longer.
