We’ve stumbled across something big. Real big. Some sort of ancient Dwarven machinecart that looks like it’s designed to move on the old rails. While some of the warriors are saying that we should take it apart, stating there’s a lot of valuable looking metal on it, one of the boys who, mind you, took a rather serious blow to the head last week, says they think they can make it move again. If they’re right, we’d have something very unique and powerful on our hands.
