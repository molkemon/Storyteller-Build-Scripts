We have stumbled across something big, really big. It is some sort of ancient Dwarven machinecart that looks like it is designed to move on the old rails. While some of the warriors are saying that we should take it apart, stating there is a lot of valuable looking metal on it, one of the boys who took a rather serious blow to the head last week says they think they can make it move again. If they are right, we would have something very unique and powerful on our hands.
