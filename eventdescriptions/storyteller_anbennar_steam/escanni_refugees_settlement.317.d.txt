It seems that some of the refugees in our lands are not what they seem. An altar to Kazraeil smeared in blood of unknown origin was discovered in a refugee settlement; unquestionable evidence of active practice of Infernal Worship. Guards have been posted close by and the refugees are being monitored carefully for now. A vocal group of nobles are calling for a quick judgment, arguing that infernalists are too dangerous to be let alone; they press us to punish the accused, and fast. Under normal circumstances this would be the case, but the altar to Kazraeil seems suspicious and our investigation isn’t conclusive on whether this blood was real or not. Furthermore, the few refugees arrested in proximity to the altar earnestly claim, and seem to be, innocent. This could all be a troubling prank that went too far, an attempt to frame the refugees, or a sign of something far more sinister. What shall we do, my monarch?
