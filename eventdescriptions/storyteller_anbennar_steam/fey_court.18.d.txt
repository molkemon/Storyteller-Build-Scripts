Of all the fey in our woods, treants, trees brought to sentience by fey magics, may be a builder’s greatest bane. There is no telling when or where a treant might be born. Perhaps in the middle of a grove never seen by mortal eyes. Or, less conveniently, near recent construction in a specific province. What was once a promising new development is now left at a standstill. Reports describe the nascent treant repeatedly knocking over building frames and tearing up foundations as it moves about and finds ideal places to set its roots. Worse still, there is even a possibility the treant might move into already established settlements. And, as tradition dictates, it is up the ruler to issue a ruling.
