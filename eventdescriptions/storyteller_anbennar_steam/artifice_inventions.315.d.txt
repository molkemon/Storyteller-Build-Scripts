The Sunblessed Armaments, a uniquely Bulwari creation of artificery, are based on partially reverseengineered materials used in the construction of precursor armour and weaponry brought by the Sun Elves as cherished family heirlooms. This breakthrough allows the creation of an alloy capable of storing sunlight for a prolonged period of time and then releasing it in an incendiary burst through a singular focus lens. In a land as blessed by near-constant sunny weather as Bulwar, this allows troops equipped with these sunblessed armaments to wield Surael’s light against their enemies to devastating effect. The very foundations of a hostile fortress tremble at the thought of a constant barrage of sunbursts charring them to ash, as do the sweltering troops within.
