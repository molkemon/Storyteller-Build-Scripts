Following the rising tensions between the Adeanic and Corinite factions of the Regent Court, the Emperor has issued the Proclamation of the capital city which states that only Corinite rulers may be elected emperor. The Adeanic Imperial estates have responded by forming the Adeanic League, with a joint army and treasury and seeking ties abroad. The Corinite states, feeling threatened by this new alliance, have regrouped into the Corinite Accord. The stage is set for religious conflict in the Empire.
