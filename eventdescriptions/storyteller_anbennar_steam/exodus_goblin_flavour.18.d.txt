A chorus of rowdy humans and their constituent nonhumans celebrating victory can be heard from the coast of the glass isles, all the way to the shores of the Bahari subcontinent as the remains of our pirate allies peter into the coastside in splintered and halfburnt vessels, their sails fluttering in the wind like slices of holey cheese. Our corsair ally’s ship plants unceremoniously on the shore, sending the gloomy captainess careening from her plot in the crow’s nest down to the sand afoot our monarch, her despair keeping her glued to the nightdarkened grains that her nose was now buried into. Minutes passed before her first mate pulled her up by coat with his claws to show her drooping face, tugged down by the chin from the weight of disappointment.
