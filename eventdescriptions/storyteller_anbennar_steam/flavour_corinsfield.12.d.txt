The news has spread to every town, village, and hamlet. Criers shout from every street corner. The Father is dead. A grand funeral will be held in Poppyspring; every counter is expected to attend, as well as their congregations. There will be great wailing and gnashing of teeth; and then, from there, our fates are held entirely in our own hands. There will be none to decide the destiny of Corinsfield but the people of Corinsfield.
