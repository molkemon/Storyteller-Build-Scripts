Like a midautumn landslide rolling down from the Dalairey Range, our forces have recently descended on the Veykodans and routed them from many acres of tribeland. In doing so, they have come upon a huge untouched herd, grazing marginal land at the edge of a great redwood copse. They have requested support from the capital to secure this herd and its furs, meat, and ivory for New Havoral.
