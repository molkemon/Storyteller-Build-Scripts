The Magisterium, the greatest authority for Mages in the Empire of Anbennar and indeed broader Cannor, has expressed dissatisfaction with recent policies pursued by the government. Officials are baffled and confused, for the policies in question have almost nothing to do with mage politics, instead concerning themselves with minor matters of coinage or the regulation of field lengths. This must be chalked up to the odd and arcane minds at the top of the Magisterium; nothing much can be done about it now. What matters is how to respond to these accusations.
