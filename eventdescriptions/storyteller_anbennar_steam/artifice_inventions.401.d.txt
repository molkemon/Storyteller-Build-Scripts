The ancient institution of the town crier has long been a crucial method for news, information, and official decrees to easily proliferate across the countryside. It is a great system, but it has some big problems. criers are difficult to organize and can distort information in the retelling. Fortunately, through the power of artifice, we have become able to remove the living element of the system and replace it with large, portable machines capable of artificially recording and reproducing sounds. We are sure you will agree the sudden unemployment of the criers is a small price to pay in the name of progress.
