The expedition formed ranks as the horde approached, and a number in the back ranks quickly began to assemble crude fortifications and barricades in order to fall back towards. When they were completed, the expedition fell back to their safety. Thankfully, the armour of the enemy was shoddy and their weapons of poorer quality than ours. They came like waves, crashing against the barricade and then withdrawing, only to come back with greater force and number. We ourselves were not without losses, but in the end the tide of these goblinoids began to slow, and then stop. The floor was slick with blood, some of ours but mostly the enemy. Before any further action could be taken, the portal began to glow and a blinding light filled the room…
