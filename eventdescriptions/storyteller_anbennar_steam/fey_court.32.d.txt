The council room is empty with the exception of our ruler. The intended meeting was nothing of great importance, just a part of the general decorum expected between our ruler and various leading figures of our realm. Yet only after more than a few painful minutes do the various who's who of our realm finally arrive. It is all smiles as apologies are offered, but the slight does go unnoticed. The lax regard for our lord’s time is deliberate. Even as the various figures banter and coo frivolously, a knowing tension mounts. On and on it builds until, finally, the subject of duties comes to head. "It is a difficult thing," one woman sighs. "Keeping up with various courtly duties." "That it is," agrees another guest. "Especially when torn between two courts," chimes a third. The woman turns to our ruler and gives a small, almost dismissive, bow. "Apologies again, my lord for our tardiness. Their monarch’s needs can be quite demanding." She pauses, tapping her lips. "Wouldn't you agree?" And there it is. The reason for such wanton behavior, their realm. The weight of the tension quiets the room as its occupants await our lord’s reply.
