There is no more smoked hinuifish. Such a terrible discovery unsettles the party, and they pause to dig through their packs in the hopes of discovering a satchel of the delicious snack that they had missed. Such searching is unsuccessful, but also brings to light another horrible truth: their food supplies are dwindling, and there might not be enough left to complete the exploration. Dropping some loot would lower the load and allow their food supplies to be stretched to the end, but some members of the party are loath to give up their hardwon discoveries.
