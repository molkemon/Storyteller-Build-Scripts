The royal treasurer awaits the Queens attention, flexing their wings in agitation.  They report irregular figures in the royal treasury, and are very quick to explain that they believe some of the Odanchesta are keeping more than their fair share of the loot; having cross referenced ships logs with those kept by the Sirens. Before Riva’s reforms the Odanchesta would keep the vast majority of their loot, and only tithe a small amount to the Matriarchy. Now they are expected to hand over the largest portion, but seemingly not all are content to do so.
