Acting as oases dotting the Golden Highway, the new SGC merchant inns are a welcome respite for merchants travelling between Rahen and Bulwar. Providing beds for the night and serving Dwarovar Dark for the dwarves and less deadly drinks for merchants of other races, these inns have become popular for both SGC and foreign merchants. In turn, it has given the SGC a grip on the trade routes flowing by just past the gates of the Segbandal.
