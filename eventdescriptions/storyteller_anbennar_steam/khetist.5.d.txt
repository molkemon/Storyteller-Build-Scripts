Khetists are often bewildered by the many varying aspects of their faith. Officially, they worship Elikhet and his kin above all others and, for the more formal parts, the rulings of the Elikhet. But these rulings are few and far between, given the lazy natures of even the oldest and most respectable, if their behavior could be called that, Khets. So when it comes to the different sects and their customs, most people leave each other to their own ways. Recently however, a priest has objected to the advisor's practice of some esoteric ritual, of which they say is technically incorrect and should be punished. The advisor argues that it is not the priest's place to speak on this and that it is an overreach from them.
