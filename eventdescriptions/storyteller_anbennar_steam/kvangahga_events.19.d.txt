We have received news that the funeral of a famous gnollish brewmaster known for her love of strong spirits has taken a strange turn. As is tradition, the local community has gathered to consume her flesh and send her soul on to Kvanga’s forge, but it would appear that the brewmaster's activities in life have given her flesh the properties of a potent alcohol, and the townsfolk have become quite drunk. The funeral celebrations have so far lasted for several boozefuelled days, and show no sign of stopping.
