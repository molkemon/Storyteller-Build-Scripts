Our Memory Speaker priests have a sacred duty to remember the stories of every member of our tribe, so that the dead may live forever so long as someone remembers them. Now, something terrible has happened; one of our priests has begun to lose their memory while still in the prime of life. Worse, it has been many years since they most recently went on a pilgrimage to tell their stories to the Dragon Turtles, whose immortality and perfect memories ensure that none whose tales are told to them will ever be lost. While senility is always a terrible tragedy, we now risk losing the stories of many of our recently deceased; consigning them to oblivion if we do not act fast.
