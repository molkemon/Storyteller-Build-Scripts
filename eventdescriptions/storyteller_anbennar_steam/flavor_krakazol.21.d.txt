The now named Hyg Azgrafa had the most unexpected effects on its drinker. The appropriately named Digger's Delight made its enjoyers feverishly obsessed with digging, as they said it has never been easier before. This was proven true, as the mithril's great adaptability transferred to some degree to the drinker. This gave their bodies the ability to readjust unconsciously if a change or just a bit extra was needed. Long term exposure to the drink increases these adaptable traits even more and allows them to use them in all kinds of construction and management of areas.
