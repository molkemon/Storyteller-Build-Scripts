Recently, a group of tinkerers supported by our artificing guilds are going much farther than normal. Not only have they greatly increased the amount of loud noises in their neighborhood, but we are also hearing dark rumors about such things as the abduction of beggars for experimentation and unnatural things such as failed attempts at necromancy. Whether those rumors are true, we do not know, but if we do not do something, they will evolve into mass hysteria.
