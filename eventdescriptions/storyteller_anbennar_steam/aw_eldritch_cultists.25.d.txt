The Eldritch Cult works in the shadows under false pretenses and false words. Thankfully, their ways only really work in what they call 'civilized realms', and their secret handshakes and secret rituals have no bearing against our greatest shamans and wise folk. Our warriors have found a few hideouts, and have organized a raids to kill anyone found there. They have now returned, and although some of the cultists managed to escape, a large number of them have been killed.
