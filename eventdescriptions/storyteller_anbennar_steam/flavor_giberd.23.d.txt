Day Twenty, Orwick Silverscrew’s Personal Communiques  "I thought that Dames crown, or Beepeck would be the height of my frustration. But sadly the gods are utterly content to prove me wrong on my every assumption. The land here is lovely and verdant, but the ports of this land harbour only fools and scoundrels with a poor taste for liquor."  "Oh Royan, I was sure that it was going well at first, but it took only a few days for it to sour. The swill they bottle and call ale is enough to make my stomach churn, and worst of all, they want to sell crates of the stuff to our home country and replace our fine ales at river parties."  "It’d be a humiliation too harsh to bear, not to mention the reaction from the nobles and merchants in the realm when they find out; but I couldn’t just compromise Pearledgers support.
