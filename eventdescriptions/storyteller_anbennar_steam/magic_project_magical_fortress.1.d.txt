You have been coming out to patrol the lands surrounding the capital for some time now. In all honesty, it has become a daily ritual to walk amongst the common folk and hear them greet their ruler. As you look out at your holdings, you cannot help but dream. The lands are sublunary. There is nothing special about them; it is a capital like any other, a city for normal folk, a city as mundane as the folk themselves. But that will change. One day, there will come a time when the capital is anything but ordinary. It will be a cathedral of Abjuration magic, a grand edifice of the arcane. You have imagined it as so, and you have mobilized the mages in response. The plans have been drawn up, but plans are just ideas on paper, a dream in your mind for what could be. But as the monarch, it is your job to make your dreams manifest. And after all, a scroll is just magic scribbled to a page. It is the power of the practitioner, the honed, perfected practice of magecraft, which makes it real. All of this has led you here, to today. At last, it is time to begin. Vast reserves of resources will doubtlessly need to be dedicated to the task. Towns on the periphery will need to be torn down, the land carved, and of course, the logistics of transporting both material supplies and the magical equipment necessary to the task cannot be understated in their volume.
