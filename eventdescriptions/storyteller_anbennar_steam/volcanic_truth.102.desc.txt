We are attempting to forge an empire for ourselves in Kanolowele, but our conquests now stretch across lands of great significance to the Volcanic Truth. The two most sacred volcanoes in the faith were once held by the Cuahu Teotlehu, who are the only acceptable custodians of these sacred volcanoes in the doctrine of the Volcanic Truth. With our recent conquests, we have displaced them, and now the adherents of the Volcanic Truth are convening to determine exactly what should be done about this matter. Their synod has not yet come to a decision, but should they demand the restoration of the Cuahu Teotlehu and we refuse, it will have a tremendous adverse impact on our relationship with the faith.
