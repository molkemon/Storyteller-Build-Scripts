Gentlegoblins. The time has come. The ruler's voice reverberated through the hall. Indeed. Every journey needs a destination, after all. The Muncher remarked wistfully. That it does, friend. I am glad we get to choose ours. The Worm smiled at his two compatriots. They have gathered there to close a chapter in goblin history. Everything they had done, whether on purpose or not, led them to this moment. The quests and the duels, with all the adventures in between. Their standing as knights, at first just an imitation of pictures in a book, now a core tenet of their very soul. It was time to make it all official. That is official. Not spouting whatever comes to mind as we all touch the same sword. Obviously not, sir. Who would think to do that? The Earworm concurred. And so they have spent their last few moments together as a tribe, with very little reminiscing and a lot of dreaming. For tomorrow, they decided, they wake up in a kingdom. Oaths, crowns and thrones mean remarkably little without the power to back them up, but these fellows had more than enough of it to paint the world in their image. And the oath they spoke...
