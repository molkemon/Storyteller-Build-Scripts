Unsurprisingly the orks within that province have caused issues, destroying the property of the local estates due to what we can only guess was a planned raid. What initially was according to them a smaller raid towards the local populace eventually got out of hand when some of the orks under the chief stopped listening and targeted the land belonging to the local estate. It is hard to claim that this was not initiated by the orks, and it is justified that the estates are demanding that the orks should be punished because of their property being destroyed.
