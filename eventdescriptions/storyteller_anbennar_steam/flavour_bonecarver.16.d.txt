All religious activity was halted throughout the entire realm for a month as all shamans, from grand to stillintraining, gathered at the grand temple. They locked themselves within the building stocked full of food and water, with only their endless sounds of discussion being evidence of their existence to the outside world. The shamans ceaselessly discussed the matters of the realm, the law and the faith, preparing for a radical transformation of their government and society to both better worship Dookan and spread his teachings. The old clan ways were thoroughly squashed, leaving behind only remnants of it within familial structures and words that have shifted in meaning. Instead, important successions were now matters of the shamans, even of the chief to ensure that no longer can corrupt and unfaithful chieftains like those of the gray orks, Venomtooth and Severed Ear would rise ever again. Most importantly of all though, it was put forth that this was not any simple country, it was declared to be a nation ruled by Dookan himself, with the shamans ruling in his exiled stead as a court of regents. As the writings were finalized and approved by the highest shamans, the ruler congratulated all his kin of faith in their work and dedication, all utterly satisfied with the growth of their clan over decades.
