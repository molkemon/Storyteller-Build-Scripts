As conflicts that ravage our corner of the world rage on, the services of mercenary companies are high in demand. This particular group of mercenaries have found that their talents are especially suited to the situation at hand and have been contracted by multiple warring states. When soldiers return home they bring with them the spoils of war, including both their salary and whatever riches they were able to plunder during their campaign. This influx of wealth has stimulated the local economy and encourages further recruitment.
