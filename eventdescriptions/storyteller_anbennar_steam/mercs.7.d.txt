As conflicts that ravage our corner of the world rage on, the services of mercenary companies are in high demand. The mercenary companies have found that their particular talents are especially suited to the situation at hand and have been contracted by multiple warring states. When soldiers of the mercenary companies return home to their origin, they bring with them the spoils of war, including both their contractual salary and whatever riches they were able to plunder during their campaign. This influx of wealth has stimulated the local economy and encourages further recruitment for the mercenary companies.
