In our aimless wanderings through our verdant home, we have encountered another tribe of beings like us yet unlike us at the same time. They call themselves the Ru Ana A Halana. Their enormous bodies are well suited to their chosen task, as they reshape the land itself in their quest to plant secluded gardens, hidden from the destructive wanderings of the Holohana. It is a truly admirable duty they have placed upon themselves, to spread and nurture nature’s beauty wherever they go.
