As we step into the room, we are suddenly struck by the strangeness of it all. A number of colorful plants blanket most of the space. They have beautiful bright leaves with strangely mundane flowers that appear to be a common dull green, as if the colorful bits and the normal bits of the plant had traded places. This must have been one of the many strange experiments of the Precursors, but how are the flowers growing so well here? As we get closer to investigate, we notice something white in the soil that covers the floor of the room. We carefully dig out a large animal skull, its empty eyes staring back at us.
