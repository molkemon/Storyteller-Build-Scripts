A new development has taken place in our lands. Gnolls, being unparalleled in their ability for scavenging, have been raiding the trash piles of the province and, by some freak of ingenuity that our people cannot imitate, managed to produce cheaper tools and furniture than those of the local craftsmen. Obviously, said craftsmen have now come to the lord to complain and demand that something be done to prevent this undercutting; however, more pragmatic people in our court can see a nonzero benefit to letting them carry on with it.
