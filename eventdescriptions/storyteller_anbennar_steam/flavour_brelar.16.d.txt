At last the Great Brelari Watermill has been restored and further embellished. Using buckets rather than blades, carefully shaped to ensure the water enters smoothly, it truly is a marvel to watch it work. To further make the watermill truly a spectacle, a large caravan was sent west to the Ekyunimoy to purchase gold beyond the old paths, using it to inlay the large wheel. With a trick of the light, it made the water look like liquid gold, sparkling with and gleaming to everyone's delight. The wheel was both highly functional and left behind a great impression of the prosperity of the city to any visitor passing by.
