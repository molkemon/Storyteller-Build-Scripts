The heir took the family armor when they left. That damn brat. Leaving our house for what, love? Where were such noble intentions during the Lilac War? When their side slaughtered our countrymen and kin? We fought valiantly, with honor, and promised we would never forget those who did us such harm. And yet, we look at the bright eyed sons and daughters of Anbennar and wonder, can those scars heal within a generation? The heir was smitten when they met the heir of our rival; the two look like a fairytale couple. With all our anger and resolve, can we move on?
