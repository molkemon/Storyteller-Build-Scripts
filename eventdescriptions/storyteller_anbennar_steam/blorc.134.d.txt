Racks upon racks upon racks of armor, swords, axes, and battlehammers as far as the eye can see. High-vaulted rooms filled with scale replicas of the different tunnels and railyards around the target, libraries full of books and scrolls on battle tactics, philosophy of war, and strategic deployment. The academy sprawling out through the entire hold is a testament to the famous dwarven grudges and goblin strategies; so many rooms and people dedicated to warfare and recruiting armies puts even our warcamps to shame. Such an unthinkable adversary must be destroyed, and its secrets must become our strategies so that our war rooms will be the best throughout the entire Serpentspine.
