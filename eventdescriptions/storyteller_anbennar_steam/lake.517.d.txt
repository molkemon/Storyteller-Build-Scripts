Something truly unexpected happened; our most complacent sister seems to have begun a meager attempt at pushback against our creative interpretation of the new provisions of the Ultakal decree. According to the latest spy reports, they have begun to send out emissaries to neighboring city states. It is expected that they will begin to openly decry our alleged mistreatment of the inhabitants of the isle who required almost no convincing at all to work in the mines of Ultakal. If we cannot hamstring this tepid attempt to besmirch our reputation in time, it could prove inconvenient for our operations. The race is on.
