The box is made of ironbraced oak wood, and its heft is remarkable for its relatively modest size. There is clearly something inside, as careful listening reveals the sound of objects shifting around when the box is moved to and fro. In the center is a set of three moving wheels. Each is inscribed with the numbers from one to nine. There can be no doubt—entering the correct code is paramount to unlocking this box. But what ought to be entered? Perhaps there is a clue in something already seen.
