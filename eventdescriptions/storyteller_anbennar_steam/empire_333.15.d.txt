Lizardfolk have so many mutations because it is for survival. Not every mutation will prove useful in the moment, but many more will. If they all looked the same, they would all die the same. This is how the lizardfolk keep coming back from the brink over and over again. There is always a mutation that emerges suited to the situation they find themselves in. This is why the lizardfolk before them established themselves on so many different planets. It was for survival. Should something happen to the intergalactic transportation system, they knew they would have spread their people out to so many locations that some would still survive. One strain, one mutation, could revive the empire. That way the empire would never die. That way lizardfolk would never die. They say there have been three hundred thirty-three lizardfolk empires on their planet of Halann. They do not need to cling to that idea anymore. For now, they know they are still part of that original galactic empire. They knew one of their kind would make it back to this spot and rebuild what was broken. They are not the three hundred thirty-third Empire. They are the First Empire.
