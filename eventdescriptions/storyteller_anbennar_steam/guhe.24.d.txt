There are certain challenges presented by the composition of our army, given the differences between our Human and Harimari soldiers. At the worst of times, the two groups are handled clumsily; assigned conflicting roles in battle, or left unable to work to their own strengths. Worse, as battlefield technology continues to evolve, the same lessons seem to have a constant need to be relearned. It is a splendid sight to see, then, whenever our officers are able to bring them into harmony. A recent graduating class of officers seems to have placed a great focus on the harmony of our military, and at least for now we can know that our fighting men shall be at top form.
