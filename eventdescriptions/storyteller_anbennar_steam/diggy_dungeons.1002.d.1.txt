Following the tracks left by the Broodmother, we are able to make our way through the mazelike cave network and exit at a large chamber, the ceiling dark beyond the edge of our torchlight, and the walls, covered in naught but a thin film of silk; a stark contrast to the webbed nightmare of Yidab's Lair. However, the room lies apparently empty, with no sign of Yidab upon a first inspection. Yet upon entering the chamber, as Yidab drops down from the wall above the tunnel we came in from, the truth is revealed. She was waiting for us, and she is prepared.
