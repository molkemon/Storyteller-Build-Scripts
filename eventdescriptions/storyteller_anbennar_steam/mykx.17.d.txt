All sailors and raiders know that there are good ships where the division of the bounty is fair, and bad ships where the captain takes too much and gives their favorites too much. So we have a messy system where it is on the whims of a captain, and some are saying that it should continue to be as such and reinforce their authority. Others are saying we should allow them to take a good amount. Still, not everything, even more surprisingly, enables the crew to vote to establish the shares, a radical proposal that few sailors would defend. No proposal has enough backing; our support would break the stalemate.
