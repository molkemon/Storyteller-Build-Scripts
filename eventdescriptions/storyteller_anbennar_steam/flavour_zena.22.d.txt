Azaly’s apprentice has spent the last year studying with the centaurs, and has crafted a magnificent gift. Working with the mages of a certain realm, she has developed a music box, capable of playing a recording of a song that she has learned on the steppes. With instruments made from animals and their natural gifts, the centaurs have fashioned a rousing ballad filled with throat singing and the percussive thundering of hooves. While she desires to remain with the centaurs for the moment, Azaly’s apprentice has offered this unique piece for Medaka’s Exceptional Treasures; in return for the funds needed to extend her stay on the steppes.
