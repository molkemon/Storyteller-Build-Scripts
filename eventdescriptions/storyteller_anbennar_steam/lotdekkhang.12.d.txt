With the dwarves pushed back into their hold and the surrounding lands firmly in our grasp through our subjects’ administration, we can now exert tremendous influence upon them. Our lord and the court have come to an agreement that our demands should be sent, the question is what precisely those demands should be. The heftier our demands, the larger the chance the stubborn fools will refuse. Our court diplomat has advised that we should demand vassalization, but ultimately it is our lord’s decision.
