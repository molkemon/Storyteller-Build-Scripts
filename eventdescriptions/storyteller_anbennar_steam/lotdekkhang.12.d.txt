With the dwarves pushed back into their hold and the surrounding lands firmly in our grasp through our subjects administration, we can now exact tremendous influence upon the dwarves. The ruler and the court have come to an agreement that our demands should be sent, the question is what precisely our demands should be. The heftier our demands, the larger the chance the stubborn fools refuse. Our court diplomat has advised that we should make a demand of vassalization, but ultimately it is the ruler's decision.
