I talked to the men with the fancy clothes, and they were making things blow up so that they could make other things blow up. But they weren’t using the stuff that makes the really big booms, so I told them all about this idea I had for a bunch of guys who run around and make fireworks. They’d have big ships, and then on the ships they’d go around and collect the boompowder, and then after that they’d make big explosions for all the whales to watch. I swam around with the whales once, and they seemed bored. I think they could use some excitement. The villagers could come too. They could board the ships, and if there were enough snacks they could just live out there and watch the fireworks. Bring snacks. I can think of some snacks. And I told all of this to the fancy men, and they told me that I was very helpful, so then I went to go tell the village, and they said I was helpful too. I think I could grow up to be really helpful.
