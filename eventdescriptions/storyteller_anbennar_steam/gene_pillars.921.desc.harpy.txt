Once again, you have made the trek to this forsaken ruin. The journey is easier this time, as a small settlement has started to spring up around the base of the pillar. The population is just a few researchers, with a small garrison and associated camp followers. Some of the locals suggested you buy climbing supplies to traverse the cliffs, but you and your sisters just laughed and demonstrated the spans of your wings. They also warn you about some large birds seen flying around the pillar a few weeks ago, but no recent sightings. Come the dawn, you will all fly to a rendezvous point before setting up a winch to lift any heavier items that are unable to be carried on the wing. As you soar, the wind flutters your feathers, and you are once again reminded of the roost you grew up in. You believe that once this site is cleared, it would be the perfect location for a future roost. Once you reach the target breach, you signal to your expedition team and you land in formation, checking the opening. Swiftly, you set up the winch and start raising the rest of your gear.
