Your group follows a winding pathway that leads to a large "Machine Room", at least according to the sign above the entrance. You take a gander inside and find several rows of complex machinery humming in a barely lit hall, as well as huge metal apparatus in the back, pallined by several rows of round objects. You briefly wonder why the machines do not seem to work, even though the dim light and noise indicate a certain activity, when you notice the spherical objects spring to life and homing in on your group with what appears to be special blades.
