Today is a glorious day. Every last one of the traitors to Anzara have been pulled out of the jungle holes they huddled in, and we have decided to celebrate. In Zerat, we have planned for our people a day of food, music, games, fun, and, of course, executions. The naga who stood against us huddle in cages which are too small to hold them as they await their deaths. We do not reject the wisdom of the naga outright, nor the counsel of learned and insightful folk; but alongside one wisdom there is another; the madness of the hundred; and to this we have put an end. Of course, we have invited those treasured loyal Naga who remain to rejoice with us at the deaths of the traitors. We would not want them to miss the fun.
