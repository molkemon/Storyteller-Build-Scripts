For most people, trolls are known for two things. their grotesque noses, and their impressive regenerative abilities that make them incredibly difficult to kill. But for those in the neighboring country, they say there is a greater peril in their existence within a province's ecosystem. Due to their large size and regenerative abilities, trolls have no natural threat in the wild; they are apex predators. Leave a troll alone enough and they will eventually devastate the environment, eating and wiping out all wildlife. It seems that the trolls of that country have been victims of such a case, and have moved their sights to this realm.
