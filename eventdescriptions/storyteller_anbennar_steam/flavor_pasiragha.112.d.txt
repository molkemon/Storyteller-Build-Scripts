The establishment of Foreign Quarters in Avhavubhiya has invited some unwanted attention from its other citizens. Meetings held among the most prominent people in the city to discuss it soon descended into shouting matches between the sides that wanted to close the district and those who wanted to keep it open. The streets haven't fared much better. Subtle remarks thrown to the district's population have turned to outright racist slander. Immigrants and foreign scholars are being harassed every time they do business outside the district, making them scared to even leave the safety of their houses. It is clear that the local population in the city don't like the newcomers. Something must be done if we don't want this situation to get out of hand.
