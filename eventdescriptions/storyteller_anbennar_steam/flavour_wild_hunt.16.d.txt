When the first troops of the Incursion crossed the first bridges of ice, they were greeted by the land of Joglikunazmoq, the border state of the Triunics realm. Our troops took everything of value, and what was valueless they burnt down out of spite. Were the Incursion successful, the rest of the lands of the Lake would have met the fate of Joglikunazmoq, but alas, we are weak in the aftermath of our invasion. Worse yet, the land is still populated, and those who still live there are more than eager to repay us for our actions during the Incursion, and then some. The people of Jolgik Uts smell blood and are already amassing for their retribution.
