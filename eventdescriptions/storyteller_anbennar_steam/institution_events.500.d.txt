Goods have been moved across continents since antiquity. But where this was previously limited to a set number of routes and goods such as the manufactured goods of Rahen and Yanshen finding their way across the Gulf of Rahen and along the Golden Highway, all trade is now increasingly becoming part of a greater world network. With the discovery of Aelantier, sea routes around Sarhal and the crossing of the Ocean of the Lost, local trade networks are being connected into one worldspanning interconnected web.  Silver mined in Amadia is now being boxed and taken via Cannor all the way to Yanshen and Rahen. Iron mined and wrought in Gerudia is being sold in West Sarhal by Damerian merchants, and others are making a fortune just distributing cloth and spices within the Halessi trade sphere. Local Raheni merchants are investing in future Cannorian trade ventures. It may still be early to speak of a truly Global Economy but surely the first seeds have been sown.
