Jubilation in the streets of HulazKrakazol. The Amethyst is returned to us, and the taps flow uninterrupted. The outlay in gold is nothing compared to the political and spiritual significance of the gem, and perhaps it is a testament to the jovial nature of the Brewmaster Hold that we have acquired its gem without violence.  The postmaster entrusted with accompanying the Amethyst to Gronstunad has asked that we not receive him with a full reveille, as he is still somewhat sensitive to loud noises.
