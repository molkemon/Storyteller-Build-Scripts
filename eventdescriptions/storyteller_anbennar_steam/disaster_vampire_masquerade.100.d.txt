With the growing chaos in our realm, villages in the countryside are growing ever more fearful for their lives and families. Mounting dread could threaten to throw vulnerable towns into anarchy. However, under the guidance of an effective adventurer captain, villages in the province have organized militias dedicated to protecting themselves from both vampires and anyone seeking to capitalize on the chaos. While villages organizing into militias without the involvement of the government is a tad worrisome for our authority and control of the province, it may just be necessary if we are to maintain order in the countryside during these uncertain times.
