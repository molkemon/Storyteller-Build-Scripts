Most Epednari keep to themselves when meeting people from other tribes on the road. But an Epednari has, according to a report, helped a Epednar family whose wagon had broken down on the road to their new plot of land. He not only helped repair the wagon but also had them stay at his tribe's village for a few days to strengthen again and afterwards helped them get back on the trail again. The chief of the families has now declared the other tribe to be eternally welcome in their lands and their villages.
