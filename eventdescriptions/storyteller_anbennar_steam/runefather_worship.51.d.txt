News has come in from the Harazherûd, the leader of the Fûrdanhund. Apparently, a new batch of recruits have shown exceptional ability in the field of combat. Other members of the Fûrdanhund report that these developments will improve their legions and aid them in their quest to bring the Runefather’s justice to the Dwarovar. A spell, after all, is only as strong as its weakest rune.
