The band of adventurers returned to the town after sufficient preparation, ready to protect the family within the supposed haunted house and to exorcise its foundation. Upon returning, however, there was no family indeed, there was no sign they were ever there, and a choking stillness lingered in the air, allencompassing and suffocating to anyone who breathed the same air the house did. The adventurers looked for hours until a full day had elapsed, but nothing was discovered. Not even the wail of a ghost, or the creak of a floorboard. just.  Nothing.
