Back when the Godloss began, that time which other races call the Day of Ashen Skies, it became plainly obvious that the gods, if real at all, were unworthy of worship. Still, it took our ancestors several hundred years to fully accept this fact. Eventually, though, they all came to recognize this undeniable truth, or so we had thought. So close, yet hidden from us by the mountains and the Godswall, we have just learned of the surviving Eagle Hobgoblins, still ruled with impunity by selfish shamans. They are everything we thought they would be. the ordinary people are slaves while the shamans act with absolute power, committing any number of horrendous crimes with no fear or repercussions. Our people have been ever vigilant against the return of the shamans; the existence of this society, a twisted reflection of our own, has caused a great stir. The news of their existence has already caused mass hysteria and the people cry out for something, anything, to be done to stop them. Our citizens may be too hasty in their request, but they are ultimately correct. Eagle Hobgoblin society is a direct challenge to ours and cannot be left alone. They must be subdued as soon as is practical, and in truth their homeland isn’t that far away. Expeditions have been organized to search the Jademines for old dwarf tunnels that could have been used to escape, and historians have been combing the records to discover the most likely time of their departure. They will not escape righteous punishment.
