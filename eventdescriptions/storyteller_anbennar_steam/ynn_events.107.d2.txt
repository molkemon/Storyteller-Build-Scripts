The Great Dam of our lands, powerful as it may be, has received a considerable amount of damage whether from besieging armies or prolonged neglect. It is in urgent need of repairs, as per the inspectors report, and our lord was called to help before the uncontrolled water flow results in disastrous floodings for the communities downstream. While private citizens can raise enough resources and workforce to plug the leakages and avoid a disastrous collapse of the Dam, traditionally the Ynnic Empire and its successor states have been providing the bulk of the capital needed to keep the dams fully operational and ensure they retain their true monumental grandeur.
