The matter of the natives and unrecognized populations is of a key concern to us. We need to ensure that they are under our care and protected from hostile neighbors, properly civilized and practicing the true faith. Our plan is to give our colonists this duty; each will be given a number of natives to tutor and protect. This is obviously not something people will do for free, so in return the natives will provide a number of days of labor to our colonists.
