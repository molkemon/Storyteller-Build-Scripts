One of the best tinkerers in the capital has brought us marvelous news. Through hard work, gumption, and a spark of genius, she has managed to restore an ancient dwarven machine to functioning order. Inlaid with a beautiful latticework of mithril and brass, studded with gems faintly glowing with arcane energy, whirring with gears and bearings still as precise as the day they were crafted; yet we still have no earthly idea what it does and why it was built. Undoubtedly it would fetch a princely sum from a wealthy collector, but we may also discern some valuable knowledge if we choose to study the device.
