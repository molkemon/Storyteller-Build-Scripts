Every day, the Divine Herald serves as Surael’s voice, speaking His truths and declaring His desires for His Empire. But during the recent revelry, the ruler has also had to serve as Surael’s feet and legs, attending myriad parties across the Empire and dancing long into the night. While even His sun takes a rest each night, the Divine Herald has had no such break. Though their spirit is willing, and indeed eager, to participate in every service required by Surael’s crusade, their flesh has grown spongy and bruised. And yet, as the ruler arrives at their office, atop the assembled paperwork lay a dozen more such invitations.
