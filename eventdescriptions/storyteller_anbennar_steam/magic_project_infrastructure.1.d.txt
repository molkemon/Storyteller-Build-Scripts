Magic is of the old world, of the elven homeland long since collapsed. Magic is the way of the aged and the decrepit. Magic is a door in the city of lost things, a worn epitaph commemorating forgotten legends. But it need not be so. Inspired methods of thinking, refurbished or constructed building projects, uncovered ancient secrets, and newly founded organizations today, our government's institutions take one shuddering, stumbling step out of the clouded annals of history.
