We have been the nominal vassals of Bianfang for a while, exchanging a guarantee of coexistence for a commitment to raid only their enemies. That treaty has held up for far longer than anticipated, but we are well aware that we would be crushed if Bianfang decided to turn their back on us. Luckily, a new state has been growing on the other side of the mountains, strong enough to guarantee our safety, while weak enough for us to secure a favourable deal with. Switching sides to this new power may be a risky move, but we probably won’t get an opportunity like this ever again.
