Investigating the campsite we find many corpses of long dead dwarven adventurers. While it's unclear what caused the death of the explorers, one of the corpses contains a notebook of what we believe to be annotations regarding this cave system, including drawings of this cavern as well as of a few rough maps of other areas. It seems like this previous expedition had managed to go deeper into this cave system, and had set up here while returning to the exit, however as the journal is written in some ancient script, we're unable to fully decipher the information contained within.
