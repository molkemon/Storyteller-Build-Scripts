Once again, you have made the trek to this forsaken ruin. The journey is easier this time as a small settlement has begun to spring up around the base of the pillar. The population is just a few researchers, with a small garrison and associated camp followers. Your stay will not be too long here, as you are due to head up the tracks leading to the top of the cliff in the morning. The locals warn you about there being some large birds seen flying around the pillar a few weeks ago, but no recent sightings. From there you are expected to reach an outcrop above the pillar by the end of the day and then descend using the climbing supplies that were provided, at great expense, to your team. From the ground, it seems like a reasonable prospect. A day and a half later, however, the journey seems far more risky. Hanging from the side of the cliff, with the wind racing around your limbs, you once again wonder why you agreed to this plan. Your fingers are starting to cramp, but the breach is only a few spans away.
