Long ago there were people who lived in the desert, and they were ruled by the wind. The wind blew past their homes but never harmed them, and though the desert had little to eat, the people had tools of bronze and houses of stone, and were rich and happy. An’adira ZephyrBorn ruled these people, and with her father’s power and her own wisdom the people of the desert prospered for many years. It was then that the dogs of the desert attacked. An’adira and her father fought with the power of the earth, the water, and the wind, but they were no match for the dogs, whose green flames held such power that the elements themselves could not extinguish them. Eventually even her father abandoned her and An’adira was forced back, and then she made a desperate cry to a spirit of the desert to save her people. But the spirit was capricious, and it took her away from the desert just when she was most needed. An’adira awoke in a fishers’ village in the Sunlight Reef, and the air was still around her for the first time. For many days and many nights she cried for her people, but these fishers needed leadership as well. An’adira taught them to forge weapons of bronze and to play the music of her people, but eventually the day came when she and the fishers heard word of ruins in the desert far to the south. An’adira knew then that she had to return. She assembled a mighty expedition to sail to the south, but what happened on that voyage no one lived to tell. Some say she found her people, and that she lives there still with them in a place beyond the sky. Others say she died in the sands of the south, and that her spirit wanders the ruins, weeping for her people until justice is done.
