In a drunken dream, we walked alone on narrow cobblestone roads. Beneath the flame of a lantern, we turned our beards to the cold and damp. Then our eyes were stabbed by the flash of aeon's light, which split the night and touched the taste of Gripoldur. We all knew that Dwarovar Dark was a nostalgic drink, but this hit differently. It wasn't as nostalgic as it was somber.
