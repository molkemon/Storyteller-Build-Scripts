Bombur Whiteforged, fourth in line to rule Argôrdstun, the dwarf meant to lord over Ovdal Lodhum, sobs into his beard as he enters the monarch's chambers. "They rejected me," he yells, petulant and devastated. "They don't want me. Why? I wanted them to rule with us." "It's okay," the monarch says, patting their son lightly on the shoulder. "It's all right. Not everyone loves us like we love them. What would you like us to do about it?" Bombur sniffs, then looks up at the monarch. "Can we destroy them, please? Can we make them regret ever denying the Diamond Dwarves? Can we show them what it looks like when they deny our kindness?" "Of course, my dear," the monarch responds. "They will rue the day they dared to reject my son."
