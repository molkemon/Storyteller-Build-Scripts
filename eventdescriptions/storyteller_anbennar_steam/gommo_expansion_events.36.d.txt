Ever since some of Haless's few surviving Jaherian legionaries settled in that city, the city has traditionally invited foreign officers of the sun in order to keep their army up to date and implement new methods of warfare. This attitude towards adaptation in warfare would serve us well in this new age of technology. While our inventors and engineers have long dominated the development and production of such weaponry, few have focused on the tactics and training necessary to put them to optimal use. Some in the Gommo seem to have realized this gap and have sent a proposal. they will provide some military consultant researchers to conduct research and testing on the military processes used in that city, which should allow us to emulate their success. This will however require funding, that might be better spent elsewhere.
