Due to our aggressive mining of the local terrain, many of the inhabitants have left for nearby cities. Some have even made their way to Anvilwright, looking for jobs to support their displaced families. This has had the benefit of boosting the labor force and added to the taxable income in our capital. Though their lack of appreciation for our newfound industrial prowess is disheartening, at least Silverforge now has more direct control of the land in their domain.
