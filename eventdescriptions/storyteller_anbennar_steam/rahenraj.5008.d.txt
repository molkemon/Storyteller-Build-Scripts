If Tamatma is the soul of the Harimraj, coin is its lifeblood. Each day the engine of trade carries pecuniary wealth down the great rivers that are Rahen’s veins and arteries, paying for porcelain and palaces and plenty of soldiers to guard the same. With the vizier’s latest edict, it seems that flow will be diverted to the Lotus Throne, and from there sent to irrigate the armouries and recruitment centers of the realm. A veritable legion of tariff collectors fan out across the Harimraj, each an engineer to ensure the flow is diverted as the ministries see fit. And soon, the strange alchemy transforming the treasury’s gold and silver into the soldiers’ flesh and steel will begin.
