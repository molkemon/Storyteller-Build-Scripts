The merchants of Oktikheion and Ormam are the strongest across the known world. Wealthy as they are, they have amassed their riches into a strong upper class that is able to dictate the politics of their cities. Before, they were invincible, pushing us away at the mere sight of our faces. With a little bit of proof of our dedication to the merchants of both cities, it became clear that we had been working with the wrong way of diplomacy. By providing Oktikheionai and Ormami merchants with luxurious ports and markets to sell in, they have come around and opened their eyes to what it means to work with us Fasíri. Prosperity is inbound to our lands, and the riches from the coast have begun to flow inland.
