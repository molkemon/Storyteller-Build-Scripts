Humans are an ambitious race, and our ideals have given rise to the idea that orks and humans are to live together, so both should have a voice in the government. There are several powerful humans who now wish to be granted the right to be a part of the government. The humans may not appreciate their request being denied, and we could risk losing all the progress that we have worked for. And yet, there are several who fear that their influence and ambition may lead us down a darker path than we desire.
