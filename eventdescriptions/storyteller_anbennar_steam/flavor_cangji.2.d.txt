Atop the castle of Bazuneizar, a massive brazier of steel and gold rises from the mountain peaks, intricately inlaid with gold and silver trim. This behemoth vessel was constructed by the pyremages, with help from craftsmen in Bazuneizar as well as the ancient artifacts discovered within cobwebbed halls. Ten of these mages now gather in a central courtyard, pink flames dancing between their fingertips, as the air around the brazier begins to rapidly expel water vapour. By superheating the surrounding atmosphere, the pyremages are better able to conduct the next step. And as one, they do, thrusting their hands forward in an intricate dance as fire shoots from their palms. Trails of flame arc into the massive brazier, filling it with octarine light, before the embers catch alight with a dramatic surge of fire. A collective breath enters the lungs of the pyremages, and remains held as the artifact inlays begin to glow. With the right mixture of artifact, spiritfire, and magical force, the pyremages have theorized that they can create localized, near-perfect copies of the Undying Flame. This theory has not been tested until today, and so the mages watch on in anticipation, fearing their research soon to be squandered. All are silent as the kindling burns. Suddenly, a second rush occurs, more powerful than the first, and the brazier instantly turns a brilliant blue as the flame is made eternal. As the spiritfire takes hold and begins its neverending blaze, all present for the event begin to cheer. For the first time in history, they have created a copy of the Undying Flame. The wastes are no match against the purifying heat of our ultimate triumph.
