The tournament was an utter disaster; many knights arrived hours late because the monarch did not pay for enough messengers. Due to an extreme heat wave, many knights and their mounts collapsed from heat stroke before the event even started. Topping it off, one of the old and rotting seating stands collapsed under the weight of the Iosahar's retainers and supporters, sending the most beloved subjects tumbling to the muddy ground in a hail of splintering wood.
