A comprehensive agreement with the dwarven holds under our influence has concluded, and with favorable results. Acknowledging the difficulty in administering any one hold directly, let alone all of them, it has instead been decided that these subject holds will be granted a degree of autonomy in their own operation, as well as a reduction in tax dues. In exchange, these holds shall agree to provide favorable commercial deals to the state and its merchants. This, it has been calculated, will offset any loss in revenue gained from decreased direct control and tax dues, and may in fact allow for greater local productivity.
