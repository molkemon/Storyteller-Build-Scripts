It was like any other night. Calm and silent. Maybe you could hear some revelry in the tavern, but nothing out of the ordinary. Most people were already deep asleep. They didn’t notice the black night sky wasn’t dark. As people were slowly drifting away into slumber, they saw a blinding light coming from outside. Many thought they had overslept, and it was already well into midday. Some others noticed. It was not sunlight. It was not natural light. The stars were not visible, as their usual blinking was obscured by thousands of moving lights across the night sky. At first, people were engrossed by such a spectacle. Unable to understand what was happening, they admired that strange beauty. Seconds passed, then minutes, then hours. It was as if time had stopped. And just as suddenly as it started, it finished; the lights went out. Vision slowly readjusted to the returned nightly darkness. And then, chaos. Unrest. Disorder and turmoil. People gone mad. They were claiming the Dead Gods were back. Back from the deepest parts of the darkest Void. Back for revenge against those who betrayed them. Everyone had seen the lights, and not all went insane. But everyone was distraught and concerned. What did the lights mean? What were they?
