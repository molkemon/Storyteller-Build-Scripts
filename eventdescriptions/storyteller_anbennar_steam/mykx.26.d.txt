The best way to show the powers of the Kult and the powers that the Xhaz can bring is a true demonstration. So we gathered the locals, threw a huge party, and burned in front of the pyres some dozens of slaves and asked for a blessing of fertility for the land, beside the spectacle of seeing demons being summoned. The tangible benefit soon appeared. the local harvest tripled in size, the hunts they did in the jungle always returned full, and the fishers returned with their boats almost sinking by the size of their catches. So now the locals see him as the Xhaz, the true Cheshosh. We have promised to take care of the citadel in exchange for their servitude.
