You should have seen the looks on their faces when the final vote failed. The Luna delegates have always been better than most at hiding emotion, but their mute fury was obvious to any, like me, who have dreamt of the small country and been disappointed. I surmise the Diet will soon learn to its peril that in respectfully asking for recognition, the Luna Confederation was merely being polite. Caspa Meanmarrow, Private Report to the Beepeck Chancellery
