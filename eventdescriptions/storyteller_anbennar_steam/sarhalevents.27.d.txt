The boy who stands before you has his father’s eyes. It makes the hatred with which he stares at you feel even more disorienting. He says, "I am the rightful monarch of this realm, and I have come to reclaim my birthright. I have thousands of men willing to fight for me. Give me the throne, or I will take it." You steal a glance at his mother in the corner. She’s flanked by armed men, and she smiles at her son. She’s dropped the disguise she used to seduce his father, and you recognise her as the hag Komina Riftwhisper, one of Yezel Mora’s rulers. You have no doubt that her son obeys her every command. If you give him the throne, he’ll follow her dark designs.
