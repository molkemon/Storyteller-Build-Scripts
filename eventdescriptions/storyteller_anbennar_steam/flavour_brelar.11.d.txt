Too long have the leaders of the Amethyst Order been led by the despicable Lords of Amacimst, the main enemies of our state. They oppose our very republican ideals and pose a great threat to Brelar if they are to be left unimpeded, as they posess a great military force of seasoned knights. Rooting out their leadership will be formidable task, as well as the restructuring of their order. It is known that many knights have been banished from the order for the most trivial of reasons, reinstating them will surely grant us trained knights in the army who are loyal to our republic.
