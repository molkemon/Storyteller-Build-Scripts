At the ruler's command, our most loyal soldiers have arrived at the site of one of the suspicious ministerial estates. They have conducted a thorough search of the premises, seizing logbooks and holding the administrative staff for questioning. Their investigation has yielded vast swaths of incriminating evidence that detail the many crimes, abuses, and betrayals conducted by the ministers against the workers of the estate and the government at large. The offending ministers have been taken into custody and await execution for their brazen corruption and betrayal of the trust placed in them and their office. This will certainly send a message about how the realm treats those who abuse their office, and help us continue to build our case against more powerful high ministers who yet avoid the grasp of the hand of justice.
