Wyverns have a snarl response to danger or to threat, and it was the offhand mention of this from a rider to an artificer which allowed the nebuliser breakthrough. Trials of incendiary weapons wielded by the rider found that the aim and deployment of such weapons distracted the rider from the highly difficult juggling act of riding itself. But if the wyvern dictates when to produce the jet of flame, the rider need only use the natural boisterousness of the diving wyvern, who roars naturally at their chosen target. The nebuliser sits at the side of the mouth, and is placed into an active mode by the rider before the dive. When the wyvern opens its mouth, the nebuliser is sprayed, followed an instant later by an automatic Dames tearpowered sparker. This makes the incendiary nebulisers somewhat imprecise, better for scattering formations than for picking off individual targets, but the sheer morale-breaking utility of an unstoppable gout of flame remains compelling on the battlefield.
