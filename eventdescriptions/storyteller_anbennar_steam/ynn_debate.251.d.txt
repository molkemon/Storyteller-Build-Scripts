Pleased news arrives from a distant land. Our lord has sent us a gift in gold, accompanied by a letter where they subtly assured us that we will be left to our own devices, free to run our realm as we see fit and, even in cases of credible accusations, their ruler will look the other way. This is sure to sway our position in the current debate, or at least ensure we will be as grateful as to not stir up the waters for a while.
