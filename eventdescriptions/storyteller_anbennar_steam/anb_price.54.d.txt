The warm, spicy, yet sweet smell of cloves was once unfamiliar to our shores and many others, yet now we hear increasing tales of their proliferation across the markets. Still, whenever cloves are mentioned, it is rare to not hear mention of the great port of Teramzandai in the same sentence. This port, in the south of Fahvanosy, has become a staple port of the entire clove trade; Cannorians, Vyzemby, and lizardfolk alike are heard haggling about the docks. It is no surprise then that with the influx of merchants getting their hands on these exotic goods, the price too has risen.
