When trade with the Cannorians began, there were many who argued against it. Many of these naysayers were appeased, however, by the fact that our new friends were an ocean away and thus unlikely to compete with us directly. Now, however, they are establishing colonies in what has long been considered by all civilized people as rightful lizardfolk territory. More aggressive members of our government are calling for military action, while those aligned with merchant interests have pointed out that trade with these new outposts will be worth more than a scrap of jungle could ever hope to be.
