Varaine is, undoubtedly, the Potion Capital of the world. Knowledge of all sorts of concoctions, ingredients from the furthest locations, and the most brilliant alchemists in all of Halann gather in our once small town, which has now become one of the most important points of scientific innovation in the world. This body of knowledge and scientific advancements have greatly benefited the population of Varaine, from lowly peasants using medicinal potions for their everyday ailments, to high ranking nobles using flight potions to help them reach appointments in time. But most of all, no one has benefited more from the potions than Varaine’s rulers, who have for a long time dictated the direction of the nation’s research and reaped the benefits of both the potions and the wealth produced by their sales. Following this long tradition of Varainey rulers establishing bold research projects, the current lord has called upon the best alchemists in the land for the most audacious project of them all, the Ultimate Potion, something to leave their mark on the world forever: A Potion of Immortality.
