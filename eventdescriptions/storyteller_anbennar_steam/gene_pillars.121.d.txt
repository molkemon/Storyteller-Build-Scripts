The final level of the gene pillar is the closest to what has been deemed by adventurers as the Spout, where the newborn parts of the Kiamoa leave the pillar and are infused into the stormwall. Such a high concentration of magical power has created a variety of strange anomalies, and each threatens to bring the party to its doom. But the first encounter is something truly unexpected; an entire community of storm elementals, seemingly possessing some form of complex society. One of the beings, the closest possible thing they may have to a sentry, notices the party and raises a limb of storm clouds, emitting sounds like thunder over the horizon. It seems like it will not let the party pass without a struggle.
