A crowd of nobles and courtesans stood around the young poet, who was perched on a tree stump in the middle of the Butterfly Garden of the Teal Keep. ".And then Komerjon Smashed the armies of bats and purpleskins, right? Right?" An eager noblewoman asked, gesturing to swipe the air with her palm. "Please excuse her, Sir Archie. I do not know what comes over the youths these days." Said her father, a dignitary from a distant land, taking hold of their hand. "It's okay, it's okay." The human poet cleared the lump in his throat, and frowned, resuming his narration. "Sadly, the king was riding to his death that day. The soldiers of Vareynn, as stalwart as they were, became surrounded by hordes of Cursed One brutes and swarms of Teira assassins, and the left flank started to falter." The harper began to play, and the poet began reciting his song. "The Soldier King with an antler crown He rides to war and will not back down The fear and doubt his might will drown A stormy charge; his path laid down." He wasn't even finished when the audience was brought to applause.
