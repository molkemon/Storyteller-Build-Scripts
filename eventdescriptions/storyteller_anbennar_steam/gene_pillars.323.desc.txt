It drops from a great height, so blind in its rage and violence that we almost lose our scout right then and there. The hefty wound it marks in its thigh festers within hours. For half a day we have wondered at the odd, fluorescent cubes which litter the ground here and there throughout these chambers; only now do we realize that they are the droppings of this nightmarish thing. What dark psyche brought about such a being? It is scarcely possible to imagine unleashing such malevolence upon Halann, hard to fathom the hostility the Precursor who devised such a being must have felt towards all creation. We face nothing but the very face of death in this place, the rotting chancre of disease. And its ears are manky too.
