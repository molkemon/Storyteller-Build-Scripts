Unity. We know better than any the meaning of the word. We have made a lasting peace with the lizardfolk, which no other people in Horashesh have managed. We have built our cities with the ancient knowledge of the halflings and filled them with relics from across Sarhal. Our streets are paved by hands that were willing to pitch in when they had lost everything but their own ability to work. And now Iqhekabi believes they are more deserving than us to be in charge? What have they done to prove their aptitude for leadership, other than failing to hold back the tyrants of Kheterata? The Irsukuba people were defeated by the Zuvunwa once, and they will be again. They think us weak? No. This alliance has served its purpose. Enough is enough.
