With a new quest on the horizon, efforts have begun to locate the final throne of the Harimraj. Along the fault lines of Shamakhad lie many different candidates, one of the more promising being the city of Sir. Home of many ruins due to its long history of conquest, it is entirely possible the throne of Harimar is hidden within the ancient structures which dot the landscape. Unfortunately, our search has come up short. Ruins all across Sir have been explored, the city nearly turned upside down in our fervor, but still no trace of the throne was found. Luckily, this is only one possible location, and we have many more to explore. We continue now towards the sea, in hopes of finally discovering the location of the ancient throne.
