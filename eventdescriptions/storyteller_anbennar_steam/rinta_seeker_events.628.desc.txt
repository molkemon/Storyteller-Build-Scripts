Weggabiga, Dadubakha, and Gawakhiza, the three brothers of the north. Over the course of hundreds of years, they have been drawn close together. Their alliance has proven to be not only beneficial but truly an existential savior against invaders from the north and south. Through thick and thin, the three brothers have had each other’s backs. Even in these last few years, the northern states of Ashamadi blood, whether in part or whole, find themselves unprecedentedly close. A bond of land, culture, and respect has fostered to grow even more intertwined. Should it be more than just an alliance? Can we achieve more together? Our people have been working in tandem for years. It is as if our borders do not exist anymore. Do we really need to keep Weggabiga, Dadubakha, and Gawakhiza? If we are already one, should we start acting like it?
