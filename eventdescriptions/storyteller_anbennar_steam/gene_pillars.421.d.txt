Finding themselves in a grand hallway lined with cells, the inquisitive expeditionaries light a torch to survey the poorly lit room. This, as it would turn out, was a mistake. Several beasts, hideous fusions of various animals, react aggressively to the light and come out of their cells. They are haphazardly created malformed beings, some with four goat legs randomly attached to the body of a gecko, with the misshapen head of a bull crowned on top, others some wretched fusion of ape and mantis, too deformed and disfigured to tell what ends where, and many more. The creatures seem easily confused and disoriented, and some have started to attack each other in the confusion. For now, they do not yet know the party is at fault for the sudden illumination.
