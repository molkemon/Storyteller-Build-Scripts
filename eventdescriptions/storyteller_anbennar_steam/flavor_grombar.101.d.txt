It seems that, in the practice of spreading her faith, one of the most prominent Corinite preachers in Ur Sarmond, after a particularly rousing series of sermons, has been thrown out of a window by an angry mob consisting mostly of clansmen still loyal to the ways of Dookan. The ruler must now make a choice; shall they condemn the killing, and bring the anger of the followers of Dookan? Or shall they support it, and provoke those following Corin into an uprising?
