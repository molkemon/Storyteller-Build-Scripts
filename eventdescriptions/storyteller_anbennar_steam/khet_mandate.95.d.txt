Our extensive dedication of fields to cash crops has caused an unusual phenomenon to happen. Kheterata is not producing enough food. The rich are forced to import it from abroad, but due to our efforts, the supply is not enough to feed the poorest families. The simple folk are accusing the Khets' lavish lifestyles and the steward's incompetency in vouching for their boons for this situation, as the poorest are ready to explode in a violent revolt.
