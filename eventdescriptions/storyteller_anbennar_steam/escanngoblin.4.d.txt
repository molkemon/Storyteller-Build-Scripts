We were subjugated by the orks many, many years ago. We were dragged to these lands under the hard fist of the orks. And now here they are, in lands we now rule. A few arguments have broken out amongst leadership on how to deal with our old masters. We could attempt to make up for the past, move on, try to break from this cycle of violence, generations long. Or, we could perhaps convince the orks into a deal of indentured servitude to pay their debts. Generations of orks, paying off the violence of their ancestors. The benefits to the economy would be tempting enough, for many Goblins. What shall we decide?
