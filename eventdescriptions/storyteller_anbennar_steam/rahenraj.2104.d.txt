The relationship between the Raja and the Vizier changes every time either is replaced or succeeded. Sometimes the relationship is good, sometimes it is bad and sometimes it is neutral. On such things, the fate of the Lotus Court turns.  Currently, the Vizier and Raja are more than just good associates. They have bonded a lot in the last years over shared interests and hobbies and are now the most loyal of friends. This has had the effect of strengthening the position of the Vizier significantly and their influence over the Raja is as great as can be.
