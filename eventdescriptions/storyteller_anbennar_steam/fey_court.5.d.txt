It is uncertain whether these rotund almost beatlelike fey were named after the Seelie court position they so often hold or if the position was named after them. What is certain is that there are few, fey or otherwise, who are so capable of leaving one flattered, then utterly incensed in one breath. A valuable, if sometimes infuriating, asset for our ruler. All we need to do is call a few favors with the Court and we could find a tulmun in our own court for a time.
