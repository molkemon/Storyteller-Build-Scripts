Today, we reclaim our birthright. The Ynnic Empire, the greatest nation in the world, who forgot the virtues that made it worthy of existing in the first place. But today that damage is undone, for the Ynn is one, and an Empire again. The monarch has been crowned ruler in a solemn ceremony, and invitations were sent out to all knights of the realm for the greatest tourney the Ynn has ever seen.
