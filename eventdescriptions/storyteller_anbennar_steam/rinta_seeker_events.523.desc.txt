Progress on the Godswatch Observatory has hit an unexpected snag. There have been reports of the use of underhanded subterfuge in order to obfuscate the true origin of certain funds for the project; it seems like one country has been taking credit for donations they did not give. Accusations have been thrown out by both sides despite the lack of provable evidence, which has resulted in a significant souring of relations. Trust in the project has notably decreased, as nobody wants to provide a donation without guarantees that someone else won’t take credit for it. A group of diplomats from the various contributing countries have gathered in order to find a solution to this. After some debate, they have presented a proposal to establish an organized oversight committee to guarantee that there will be no foul play when it comes to donations. The sky is alight, they say; we cannot afford to get bogged down by disagreements on the ground.
