For as long as we have known, Forest goblins have built homes high in the trees, away from the dangers of the forest floor as well as any prying eyes. Entire towns and villages have been built between the branches of the Deepwoods, connected by small rope bridges and questionable architectural choices. Over the years, a specific settlement has seen a massive population boom, with the small village in the trees having grown into a burgeoning city. Unfortunately, disaster has recently struck as the weight of so many goblins has brought a few buildings crashing to the ground, killing hundreds of goblins and injuring many more. Most call for us to immediately send aid to that settlement to rebuild, but some in our court propose a step further. A chief advisor has proposed a new organisation, the Organisation of Safety at High Altitudes, to implement new building regulations and ensure this doesn't happen again. Not many goblins would be happy with being told how to build their houses, but it may just be a necessary measure.
