A sickly melody fills the grove, disappointment radiating from within the temple's depths. Vines slowly retract as the song begins to swell, briefly reaching a deafening pitch, before retreating into a cold dead silence. The heir suddenly emerges from the temple, emotionless servants of our Goddess carrying them on either side. Seemingly almost in a daze, the heir is laid out onto the ground outside, before being abandoned by their temporary wardens who quickly descend back into the depths. While this does not bode well for our future, there will be other opportunities for the heir to prove their mettle.
