Though they are still new to the realm, the artificers who have begun work in the capital already show transformative potential. But they can be absentminded at times (to say the least), and require clear direction from the lord to ensure their brilliance benefits the state. Should we have them focus on improving our postal network, to ease the flow of information and ensure even the furthest corner of the realm is firmly within our grasp? Or should we give them access to our artillery workshops, which produce the weapons that have brought us this far and will be pivotal in our continued rise, and see just how much more explosive these tinkerers can make them?
