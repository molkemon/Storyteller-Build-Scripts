A few years have passed since our researchers first contacted harpies in Bulwar, leading them to wish to pursue the creation of Flight Potions. Apparently, there was more than simple scientific fascination involved in those exchanges, as a significant contingent of harpies traveled to our lands asking to be allowed to live in Varainé, and more importantly, to reencounter their previous acquaintances or find new ones. We could accept these harpies, though it would be hard to sell this idea to most of our citizens. Alternatively, we could let in only those harpies that had previous contact with our researchers, though it is likely many women would not appreciate the gesture. Finally, we can send the researchers who dealt with these harpies before to them, appeasing the harpies at the expense of our nation's image.
