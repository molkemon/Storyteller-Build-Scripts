Having decided to study the giant's living kin, our researchers headed northbound to find suitable study subjects. Luckily, a wonderful opportunity presented itself: An ogre village, hidden away in between the mountains of northern Serpentspine. Not only that, these ogres were not only friendly (Or at least, as friendly as can be expected from wild ogres), but they were also in the verge of a great festival.  This festival, consisted mostly of feasts and food and eating competitions, would provide many great opportunities to ingratiate, or prove ourselves, to the ogres, thus securing the subject needed for our research.
