It is said that the ministries have a dedicated form that tracks and proscribes the movement of any soldier anywhere in the realm. Perhaps this is why their forces will crumble against ours, because the armies we have mustered against our foe know no such restriction. The exigencies of waging war have led to some disputes recently, where our armies have accidentally ranged into a province not involved in our war efforts. Their diplomatic delegation to the capital takes great offence at these transgressions, but our generals see it as an opportunity. They are not our intended target, no, but perhaps they should be. A calculated insult to their diplomats should draw them into this conflict, and thus add to our spoils.
