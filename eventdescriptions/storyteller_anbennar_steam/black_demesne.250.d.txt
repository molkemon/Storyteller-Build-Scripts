The Aldresian Crusade. A momentous occasion when the lords and ladies of Cannor banded together as one to defeat the undead hordes of the White Pestilence and its creator, the Acolyte Canrec the Defiler, the last vestige of the SorcererKing Nichmer himself. But those were times past. No seer could have ever foretold that in our day and age that a new darkness would rise from the east once again, and in this iteration it calls itself the Black domain. The Black domain has proclaimed a Dark Decree rejecting the legitimacy of the Empire of Anbennar and calls for its members to join it and leave the empire itself, in return they will give clemency and mercy once the domain inevitably spreads its shadowy wings to the rest of Cannor. The Emperor of Anbennar has justly stood defiant against such claims, and rallies their loyal princes to war against this dark threat: The Second Aldresian Crusade.
