Out of all the vile creatures roaming upon this earth, the cockatrice might be the ugliest, as it lacks any and all majestic features of the others. Even the monstrous people of Bulwar and the raging spirits beyond Shuvüüshudi have some redeeming features, not even the manticore or the baku, similarly haphazard creatures in stature and composition, share the vile presentation of the cockatrice. It is truly, unequivocally and irrefutably, ugly. "I'd rather dance with an ogre than even look at a cockatrice." From The Book of Beasts by Studgar the Slayer
