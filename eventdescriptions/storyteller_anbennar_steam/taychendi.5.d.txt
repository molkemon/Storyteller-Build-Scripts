The lords of Taychend never bothered much with the sea. They could never afford to, for their rivals on land were always a much bigger threat. Even the lords of Oremvand and Nagyar Vyechei focused their attention on inland matters rather than the lands beyond. Thus, naval affairs were left entirely in the hands of the merchant guilds, who held an exclusive monopoly on shipbuilding techniques and naval expertise. The ruler has reached out to the merchant guilds, seeking to purchase ships for their fleet. The merchant guilds are happy to accept, on the promise of more favorable treatment by the kingdom.
