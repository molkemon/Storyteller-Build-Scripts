A group of Ynnic knights riding their iconic antler horses has reached our border, claiming they are hunting down a group of outlaws and that they finally found their hideout, which is on our territory. They are demanding we let them enact justice, with thinly veiled accusations of us willingly harboring the bandits. Not only are these implications baseless; we can't control the actions of everyone on the frontier, after all; letting foreign soldiers hunt down nogooders in our own land would greatly undermine our authority.
