The holy texts state that the precursor elves committed the Original Sin by binding and defeating a deity to steal their magic. This revelation has been on the mouths of many a fire and brimstone preacher for the past few months and now this rhetoric has taken an antielven turn. A mob of devout citizens have stormed elven neighbourhoods, slaughtering the innocent in the name of God. While the precursor elves did do this, the elven people living here were not responsible; hells, most elves dont even practice magic. Surely the sins of the father are not sins of the son?
