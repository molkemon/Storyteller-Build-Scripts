All the signs were pointing this way, and most of the corridors seem to terminate in this room. There are several boards around the room with traces of text upon them, but the real focus is the door. It stands maybe fifteen feet tall and ten feet wide. From brief visual analysis, it appears to be made of the same material as the seal on the exterior of the lower levels. Unfortunately, the examination has to stop at purely visual, as the magic imbued into the door is palpable, with waves of warding and evocation magic wrapped into every layer of the door. A colleague suggests poking the warded passage with a long pole, and after taking cover, you attempt this. The pole is obliterated down to sawdust in the blink of an eye. Deep carved text surrounds the door, but the first word is unclear. The other two words seem to be in the language used by the researchers here, and they seem to roughly translate to "Containment Facility". A survey of the room quickly turns up some small items of value.
