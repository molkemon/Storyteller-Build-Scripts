Alignment with the gnomes of the Gommo has paid dividends for our technological progress and the advancement of our society. Indeed, gnomes are now a common sight in certain districts in many of our ports, from Feiten to Cuihiok. Not all are happy with their presence in our lands, however. Many warn of the hidden price of our progress; the slow encroachment of Gommo influence and power into our society, and our increasing dependence on what they provide, despite the Gommos many assurances and pledges that they merely seek to advance knowledge for the sake of knowledge worldwide. Coming to a head, tensions between those suspicious and those welcoming of the Gommo have resulted in accusations of betraying state secrets and technologies against a known gnomephile who hosts a contingent of the Gommo within their estates at all times.
