Not all the moon elves of Varivar were loyal subjects. Indeed, not all the moon elves of Varivar even spent the bulk of their lives on the land. Or so our lord has surmised from an hour of conversation with the recently returned port captain Ivrandil Saltsworn. The elf is tanned much darker than most Moon Elves, almost appearing as a Sun Elf at first glance. He is as garrulous as most are poised, quick to share a story or a laugh. And he seems to have little sense of who the nominal ruler of Varivar is, and to care even less. He apparently makes most of his living taking on the occasional transport of hazardous materials around the region, and in trading with Sea Elves; who our lord thought were merely rumor, but who Saltsworn seems to have broken bread with and (as he says with a laugh) even almost married one. He is amenable enough to flying the flag under his own, but the lord has another proposition in store: what would he say to a refitted ship, in exchange for assisting the Imperial Navy in their downtime?
