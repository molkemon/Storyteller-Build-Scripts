In ancient days gone by, the Anzara Empire, as representative of all the lizardfolk, fought for its future against the lesser creatures who lived outside our jungle. In those days, our ancient foes did not take so readily to our dominion. Although they did not warrant our notice, the first of the two ancient empires of humankind was the Fangaulan Empire. The lands of their empire have quite degenerated since those days, and their subjugation was an easy task. Without the djinn to protect them, they could not defend the ruins of what meager accomplishments they managed to scrabble out to rival our civilization in those days. The Kheteratans proved more formidable both in terms of the effort needed to impose our will on them, and in the persistence of their ancient empire in what the humans claim passes for a historical record. But when Anzara marched to meet them once again, we discovered something strange: this empire had rotted from the inside. Across centuries of rising and declining and rising again, somewhere along the way the Kheterata we knew had vanished. Although we had hoped the Kheteratans might help us in rekindling the rivalry of those days, dominion over their river, their city of Kheterat, and their pets will have to suffice. Now, the Kheteratans will join the Fangaulans in acknowledging our glory, and will be allowed to persist as long as their deliveries of tribute are timely. Of the Fangaulans, nothing remains. Of the Kheteratans, all that remains is Sorrow. The Anzara Empire persists, and by virtue of its survival claims victory in Sarhal’s ancient war.
