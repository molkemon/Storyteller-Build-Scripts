There are some who say that the growth of the Sanadazjal has made them lazy, fixed in their ways, and uninterested in innovation. To those fools, one need only discuss the story of the Ironworker’s Sanadazjal. Just decades ago, the Ironworker’s Sanadazjal did not exist, after all, the region was infamously lacking in the metal they are named for. It stands in harsh contrast to another domain, which is overflowing with it. With the arrival to the mainland, a small group of esteemed metalworkers began to study the iron of the frontier and refine a technique for working it. With support and funding from the wider Sanadazjal, this group was able to engineer a highly effective method for the extraction and molding of iron, and off the back of this productive innovation, founded the Ironworker’s Sanadazjal. Today, this Sanadazjal is among the most powerful and is highly innovative in all the realms related to the dynamic metal. In no place can this better be seen than in one of its subdivisions, the Cannonmaker’s Sanadazjal. This capable organization is responsible for the proliferation of the cannon in the realm, and has worked tirelessly to integrate its techniques into the military, yielding stunning results.
