Sermons praising Nirakhet are once more heard upon the shores of the lake. The new temple stands at the mouth of the river, a symbol of our unity and devotion. Reflections from the water undulate across the beautiful facade as crowds gather to hear the message of hope. Those that remained faithful to Her through the generations come forward with joy, and those led astray by corrupt teachers are coming to accept the truth; the truth that Mother Nirakhet cares for all and weeps for all. Her priests play their part in lifting up the downtrodden, and the world is a little better for it.
