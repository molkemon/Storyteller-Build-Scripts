The dwarves of Argezvale have sinned; that much is clear. They have gravely consorted with the rune warrior savages, inscribing foul magicks upon their very skin. They have displayed an alarming ability to conceal these taboo tattoos as well, making rooting out their heresy much more difficult. In addition, stemming from their Lorentish heritage, they believe that the blackguard Adean is to rule the Regent Court, rather than the Martyr of Escann. However, despite these obvious incompatibilities, the dwarves themselves are a remarkably affable people. Of their own good will and neighborly intent, they have immediately integrated themselves into construction projects throughout the countryside. Despite their obvious witchery and heresy, there are calls from within to show them leniency.
