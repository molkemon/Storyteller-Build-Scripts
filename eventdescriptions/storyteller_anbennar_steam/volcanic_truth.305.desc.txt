We have changed the ways in which we interact and live. We have even changed our deepest moral foundations to fulfill the mandate of the Twin Gods. What we have not yet changed is the ways in which we enforce change abroad. A military, at its core, is merely a tool of inducing change by force in lands that lie beyond our borders. It was once a priority whether these changes were beneficial to our realm or not, but we now live in a time of change for change's sake. And yet, while they have changed their stratagems and battle formations, the commanders of our troops remain securely in their posts. Perhaps this is no longer enough. We need to remove the people in charge of the military in order to increase the pace of change.
