After our endeavors on the mainland, we have come to hear more of the people to our far east who call themselves the Taychendi. Focused on combat and imperial ambition, their lands are said to be rich homes to powerful militaristic kingdoms. Thus, a few of our most trusted advisors were sent out to study the region to find out more about their culture and ideals. As they return, they have brought us local maps and relics of a make we have never encountered yet. We must establish diplomatic ties to explore this region of great interest.
