It is clear as soon as our expeditionary team arrives at the pillar that maintaining a presence here will be tricky indeed. The jungle has grown up against the side of the Pillar; the gaping void in the Pillar’s side appears to have been at least partially dug out from within, for there are enormous claw marks twisting the ancient metal. What could have torn such gouges in a metal our magic struggles to pierce? The jungle presses in from all sides; the Pillar itself is the only landmark for miles around, a sunken sail poking through a great, green ocean. Our observation of the site noted that this is a favoured spot for local predators, and we now understand why; this place is something of a menagerie of the Precursors. Strange creatures from within the pillar seem to periodically wander out of the breaches in the pillar’s enormous walls, apparently naive to the rule of the local jungle and thus highly susceptible to predation. Thus our only way in is a feeding ground under constant attack.
