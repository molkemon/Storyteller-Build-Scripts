A gigantic army landed on our shores recently, with weapons of war we had never encountered before. The few elves that came back after the first attempts at contact reported that they were clad in black metal and left few survivors. Those evil invaders have now started marching on the Bone Citadel, the heart of our chiefdom, to destroy it and leave nothing in their wake. The lord has called upon the vassal chiefs to honor their oaths of loyalty, but their authority has been called in question by our subjects as many believe those demons were sent by Cheshosh himself to deliver punishment upon the lord for the Bone Citadel’s exactions. They are of course mistaken; those invaders were clearly sent by Cheshosh to test us and reveal the weak and traitors among our ranks. The Bone Citadel shall stand for centuries to come once this is over, for what does not kill us makes us stronger.
