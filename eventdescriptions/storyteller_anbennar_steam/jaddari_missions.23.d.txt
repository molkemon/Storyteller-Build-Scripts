Today a group of beautiful winged ladies showed up at our court, requesting to see the lord. They appear to be envoys from the Jaddari, who say they have a proposal that could be very beneficial to both of our nations. Their exact proposal becomes clear when they meet with the lord. The kingdoms of southern Rahen are weak, yet they hold valuable land. They suggest we make an alliance and divide those lands between us, drawing a clear line to mark out each others domains. While this would mean an open threat towards the Raghamideshi kingdoms, it would also be an opportunity to gain stronger control over wealth outside of the Raj.
