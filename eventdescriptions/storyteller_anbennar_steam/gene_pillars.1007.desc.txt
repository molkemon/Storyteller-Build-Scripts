As many an adventurer knows, one of the most common obstacles one finds in their travel are not the monsters or endless dungeons but instead the humble locked door. And one such specimen is indeed blocking the progress of our intrepid explorers. While usually swiftly surmounted, this one is both made from dense metal and lacking any kind of obvious keyhole, with only a strangely shaped recess indicating that it could be opened in the first place. Worse, other than going through, the only other way to proceed would seem to be digging through a half-buried and unstable looking doorway, which might only collapse it further.
