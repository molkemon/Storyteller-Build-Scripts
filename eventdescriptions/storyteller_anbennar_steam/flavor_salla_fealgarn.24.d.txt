Rúibí burst through the tower’s doors with her stag Crescent. In front of her is a long dark grand hall with a shining white door at the end. She has four arrows left, her red hood masks how much blood she is losing, and Crescent tires. She whips the reins as she pushes forward toward the epicenter of this madness. An eight-armed creature jumps toward her from the shadows as she rips an arrow letting it punch through its head. Rúibí pushes Crescent even further as two more Warped try to oppose her, but again two more arrows find their mark. She barely makes it to the door before Crescent neighs in agony as she is thrown at it. Four Warped catch her stag as Rúibí is flung into the pale door, passing through it. A woman’s harsh voice rings through her ears, saying, "I thought you ran away, couldn’t live with your cowardice? Your failure to save your friend?" Rúibí grumbles as she stands up, barely lucid, as she strings her bow and aims toward the Unthroned Queen. She is a pale woman, with red and black eyes, tall and wearing a black gown with a primal greed about her. The woman says, "You have one arrow. You are about to bleed out. And you face a former Fey Queen. Aren’t you scared little girl?" Rúibí exhaled.
