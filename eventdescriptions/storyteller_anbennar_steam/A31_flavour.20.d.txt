The feast has been going on for hours now, with elves from both the Redglades, Lorent itself and Moonhaven greatly enjoying themselves and socialising. Varion exchanged few words with others, preferring to observe the entire feast from a distance. A part of him still could not believe how smoothly everything went, not even a single fight or an argument. Moonhaven and Redglades were quite similar he thought; both are elven realms with the undying loyalty towards their overlords of Lorent and Dameria. Well, only one of that is true now, following the Lilac Wars. Varion's mood briefly worsened, until he looked at the table across. Two elven officers, one from Redglades and one from Moonhaven were talking there. They looked just over 200 and were surely fighting during the war. Maybe they even met at a battlefield? Regardless, at this moment they were having a friendly chat with each other. Varion smiled and thought to himself.
