The work of the ruler is usually based in the Lotus Court, after all, any actions here spread outward to influence all Rahen like a drop of ink in water. But sometimes a closer hand is necessary, and a specific domain seems to cry out for such intervention. A scandal has engulfed the head of the local ministries and all their advisors, and direct action from the nation would see that domain through such a storm unscathed, and indeed it might even profit from better leadership. The question, of course, is whom to send. The vizier, even though they may seek to use the influence in furthering their own interests? Some of the monarch's closest advisors, though their wisdom would be missed at court? Or perhaps that domain can sink, or swim, on its own.
