While the occasional revolt of enslaved orks is hardly a novelty for the plantations of Endralliande, the uprising which has erupted over the last few weeks is of unprecedented scale. Following an apparently spontaneous revolt on three nearby estates along Endral’s Coast, a huge and surprisingly well-organized force of orks has begun looting major settlements for food and supplies, overwhelming local colonial garrisons more suited for policing than a pitched campaign. The rebel slaves are led by a figure known only as Bramash, an autodidact shaman with a prodigious grasp of Abjuration. His forces are sweeping across the region, freeing as they go, and missives dispatched from a so-called ‘Cabinet of Freedom’ have already made their way to sympathetic Corinite circles. Their stated goal is nothing less than freedom for every enslaved ork in Aelantier.
