No tale of any realm could have prepared you to bear witness to the gruesome majesty before you. A hulking mass of muscle and sinew hunches over the unrecognizable ruin of its latest kill. An incomprehensible cacophony of fang and claw, fur and feather, tusk and horn, gorging itself on the carcass.  You dare not even breathe as your trembling hand reaches for an arrow, desperate to end this encounter before it begins. As you raise your bow, the monster stops; and its gaze snaps directly to you; before it throws back its head and bellows a deafening roar, a call to battle that shakes your very soul. The Lord of Beasts explodes into a furious charge, maw foaming with rage.
