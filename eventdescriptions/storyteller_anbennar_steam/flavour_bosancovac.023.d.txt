Between the docks and the mercantile quarter is one of the more mundane districts of our great city, yet in mundanity and simplicity can be found treasures from across the Ynn. Here goods are contained,ready to flow into the merchants’ waiting hands, to continue to grow our cities, and our people's wealth. Mundane in appearance it may be, but to those with a keener eye, the Warehouse District screams of profits to be made.
