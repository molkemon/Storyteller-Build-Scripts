The Golden Party is outraged at us entering into open conflict with the realm they support, and as such they have decided to openly rebel against our rule, in a bid to overthrow our government and make us subjects of their preferred master. Mercenary armies, paid with the coin these wretched merchants earnt selling their homeland to the highest bidder, march against our capital now. If we do not crush this threat quickly, these avaricious traders will reduce our proud people to just another product to be sold.
