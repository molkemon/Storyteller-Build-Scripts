We have managed to bring about a new Cult through a mix of purges and incentives. At this moment, Kumarkand has managed to rid itself of the vast majority of elements within its own temples that are loyal to cults outside of our borders. But the substance still remains the same, even if the surface has been changed. Our clergy have not had the time, amidst all the purges, to properly change the doctrine of the Kumarkandi Sun Cult.
