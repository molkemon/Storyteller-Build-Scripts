The dwarves of Grôzumdihr have dug deep and uncovered many lost regions of the hold. Dwarves once more cross the Peridot gate into Rahen, as advisors, helpers, and the emissaries of a once more powerful hold. This imitation of the ancient hold has not gone unnoticed by its population; even now, the flag of Grôzumdihr is flown alongside our own banners. It seems the hold is beginning to consider itself a nation, something we may have to deal with.
