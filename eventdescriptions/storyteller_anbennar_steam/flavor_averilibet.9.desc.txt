I will confer my favor upon thee, for Amilak the father of all sees thine Wind Lord, thine protector, thine guardian beast, ever before his face . we must now become as one; receive now this grace from me and through me; deck thyself as a bride who awaits her bridegroom, that thou mayest become as I am, and I as thou art. Let the seed of his light descend into thy bridal chamber; receive thy bridegroom, one of many, unfamiliar with his name and give place to him, and open thine arms to embrace him. Behold, grace has descended upon thee.; Epistle to Mezhi Neza, second Mezhi of Averilibet.
