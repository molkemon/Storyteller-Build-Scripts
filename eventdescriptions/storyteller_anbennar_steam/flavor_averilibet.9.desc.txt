Amilak, the father of everything, thine protector, thine guardian, let thou stand before Him and His light and glory. Let the seed of His divinity descend into the bridal chamber. As bride and bridegroom, no matter if familiar with His word, His deed, or His divinity, thou shall receive His blessing before thy people, and open thine arms to Him. Bride and bridegroom thou mayest embrace one another as thou embrace Him. This favor I give upon thee for Amilak's word is yet upon you.
