Well, we entered the ruins heedless of their spear tips leveled towards us. It seems it might be best not to go against the will of people who have been living detached from all society for gods know how long. More familiar with these jungles than we can ever hope to be, these Ghost Halflings are fighting us left and right, and stabbing us in the behind, too. We will whittle them down eventually, but let's see how much damage they will cause before then.
