From our swamp, the waterways run into Madriamilak, and the water fuels agriculture and trade for our fellow Mengi. But the rivers could be cleaner than they usually are. The death and decay that is so ubiquitous here affects the streams as well. Plants, carcasses, and soil infused with dark energies travel onto the shores of our kin downstream. Not only does it frighten them, but it also hurts the prosperity of the riverbanks. As caretakers of the Shadow Swamp, we have a duty to clean it all up. By hand, magic, and prayer, we cleanse and bless the rivers, sending the water on its way to do good in the land of Amilak.
