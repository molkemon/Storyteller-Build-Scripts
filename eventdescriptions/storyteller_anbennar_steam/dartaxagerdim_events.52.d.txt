After arriving at the mage academy on the shores of lake Garlas, our lord has been received with all honors by the heads of the academy. During the banquet in his honor, many speeches were held exhorting the wisdom of our lord to continue to support the efforts in uncovering the lost secrets of our magical heritage and how it is the birthright of every magically gifted Bulwari to study the arcane lore of their ancestors and wield the power that stems from it. After the festivities have reached their end late in the evening and most participants have already stumbled towards their quarters, our lord is approached by a group of senior mages who request a discreet audience with him. They reveal that they are emissaries of a hidden sorcerous cabal striving to work for the greater good of all Bulwari by uncovering the lost secrets of the Magušim art and return Bulwari magic to its rightful place. What exactly that would entail is left somewhat vague in their presentation. They explain that they have been granted permission to invite the lord into their ranks. While the lord is not a scholar of magic or ancient history, even he has heard tales of the followers of Hašahu, the Serpentking, who have espoused very similar goals.
