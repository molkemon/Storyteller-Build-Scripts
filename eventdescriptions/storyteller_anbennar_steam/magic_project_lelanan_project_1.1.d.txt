A myriad of new relationships, each a blessing upon us all, all shapes and sizes and genders, surrounding us like a comforting shawl. But we cannot deny the issue; Lel asks we all create life, but these relationships bear no children, no matter the husband or wife. The Mihipha have come forth with a deeply ambitious plan to allow all to create life together, no matter dwarf or troll, woman or man. First, we shall dive our annals and sort out our own knowledge. Perhaps our greatest institutions will help, the Mihipha or their college. Next, we seek the Lizardfolk, whether foreign or abroad. Their eggs are truly wondrous, a miracle by which we are awed. Then the halflings existing, testaments to our divine mission. Let us find somewhere they are free and make our own addition. The Magisterium is vaunted, the Old Damenath vaults renowned. And if they deny our access still, there is always something else to be found. Haless is a land of mystics, of secrets most profane. Whether migrants or natives still, let us not shy from the arcane. Once knowledge is collected, no secret left alone. Dames tear will be needed, others or our own. Then we hope dearly our achievement will ring out, for our love for all the world will never again be in doubt.
