The ruler is dead. "It worked." The giddiness and joy of the monarch was such that they looked like another, a better one. "Every member of the swarm, from the babies to the great queens; I can feel them, the far away kotakompu as close as those near me." The animal feelings came to the monarch in great waves, tired kotakompu, agitated kotakompu, hungry kotakompu. The hunger was overwhelming. The monarch commanded them to feed; the whole swarm happily obeyed, feasting on whatever was closest. The monarch tasted all the new flavours in their own mouth. The meat was divine. "I can know them now, truly know. Every bite, every chatter, every contented snuffle and cry. How they feel, what they like, their thoughts." As the monarch turned towards his bloodmage, the collective experiences of a million started taking their toll. The weight, the burden forced the monarch to his knees. In response, the kotakompu – all kotakompu – gave a cry of concern. The monarch beamed even more, despite the pain. "As I hear them, they hear me. They understand me now. Fully. This is the best day of my life." To anybody who knew the monarch, even the most loyal and steelhearted follower, such a spectacle could only provoke immense disgust. "And you. Rannaraikazhil, my brother, my bloodfriend. What debt do I owe you. What glorious work. Bloody Fingers? I name you Blessed Hands. Anything you desire; anything; you can ask me, and I will repay you with all I owe. You deserve that and more. But after that; you must promise; you will join me to plan one final step."
