The northern forests of Aelantier is a place of thick trees and barely any sunlight. It is said that one could get lost in these woods just as one would get lost in the Deepwoods back in Cannor – perhaps even more so. In the dead of night, the entire camp is woken up by what looks like fireflies – but no, it was fairies. These fey resembled miniature humans but with butterflylike wings, and laughed with joy as they played pranks on the men – stealing trinkets as they buzzed above like bees.  "You wish to find our queen? Look for a tree split between two worlds, dearie. Heehee." one says.  "No trust fey, captain," one of our men whispers. The man was of brutish ruinborn stock, one of the Cursed Ones. "Fey in forest. Mean harm. They Dark. Unseelie. Look good, but actually, bad." he grunted.  Eventually, the sprites disappears into the woods, but by the time the group gathered their senses, it was sunlight. The explorers had not slept, but it was time to go.
