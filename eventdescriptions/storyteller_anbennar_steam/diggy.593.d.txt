Any mine will fail without the right infrastructure; as for that province, our miners have complained about increasing workplace accidents. After thorough investigation, we have discovered a large infestation of veladklufar, large insectoids sporting scissorlike jaws that slice through rock and stone, swarms of which tore supports, railing, and minecarts to ribbons. To make things worse, they possess iridescent scales that camouflage them in the dim environment of the mines, allowing them to evade retaliation from our miners. We will need to send help to deal with this infestation, or risk losing productivity in not just our mines, but our stonecarved towns too.
