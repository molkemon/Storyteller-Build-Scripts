You dash and dance through the vibrant trees, your hair whipping in the wind. You do not know where you are, but you know exactly where you are going. You can sense they are hiding from you. Is this fear? You can feel it emanating from them. They believe themselves concealed within their forest, but you can sense them as you draw closer. The first you find is a curious little thing. You cock your head at them, even as their eyes widen at the sight of you. They surprise you by laughing instead of running. They laugh right up until the moment that your arm pierces their chest, when it turns into a choke. Then they go limp. You shudder as you draw their chaos into you. The second you find throws you off for a second, for he is not a man but a massive wolf. He leaps at you, but you are faster. In the blink of an eye, your arm is through his underbelly, and he falls to the soft grass with a whimper. You shudder as you draw his strength into you. The third you find stands proud at your approach, which again catches you off guard. She is so regal in the face of her death that it almost, almost, gives you pause. Your arm, though, cares not. It plunges into her chest, and she falls. You shudder as you draw her severity into you. You still do not feel whole.
