It has been many seasons since our expedition marched into the depths of the Dwarovar. We have been waiting with bated breath for their return. But as weeks turned into months and months turned into years, life returned to normal. Now it might really be time to declare the expedition lost. Who knows what happened to them? Maybe they succumbed to a goblin ambush. Maybe they encountered some sort of monster that dwells within the depths of the earth. One suncooked advisor even suggests they were killed by Dwarven mushrooms. It is most likely they just lost their way; and no matter what, may Surael protect all their souls.
