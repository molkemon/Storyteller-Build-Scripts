There are misguided harpies that believe science and culture stand in opposition. The cold and rigid mathematical nature of natural sciences versus the flighty and romantic nature of cultural studies. Fools. Everybody that has dedicated their life to either discipline knows that science is part of, and inspires, every kind of art; and, as a mirror, science has elements of art, poetry, and music inside it. Thus no flock is closer to us in spirit than the Mišaralleni. Bards, sculptors, and vetaunyar studying the magic of the Song—there is no facet of harpy culture this flock does not excel in. Let us focus on what we have in common, forging a bond linked by our love of knowledge.
