We have finally found the answer to those gnawing doubts and intrusive thoughts that assault us in those moments when we are without drink or quarry. We feel inadequate because as we have achieved so much, it is only now that we are finally able to see what the ultimate foe is. There is no turning back to what we were before, and though we despair at the enormity of the task ahead of us, we must see it through. To put an end to our fears and doubts, we must slay sobriety.
