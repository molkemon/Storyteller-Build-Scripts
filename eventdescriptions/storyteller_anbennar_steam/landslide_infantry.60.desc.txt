In our small corner of Insyaa we have fought to maintain our identity against others of our kind. We have thought ourselves to be part of a wider whole that we must distinguish ourselves from. But in truth, we are finding that we are just one part of a much wider community. And though we may have differences with our old neighbors, we cannot dispute that they are like us. Compared to the kobolds, mechanim, and ogres, they are practically the same. Now that we can reach out to the great Plateau we have learned of such differences we could never have considered before. The magical practices of our mages are but one of many. And we have learned how to take the magics of others to augment our songs, change what we are, and form a synthesis. Perhaps no one has benefited from this more than the Landslide Infantry who now have a wealth of new techniques to learn. We will see how they advance themselves.
