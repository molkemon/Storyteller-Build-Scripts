Surveying, cataloguing, and processing the official records of a millennia old government is a long and monumental task, one that will continue for years. Yet it is necessary work if we are to fully understand what came before and build something greater. As part of this task, one team of scribes came across a collection of eyewitness accounts of Elikhet's battle with the Storm Queens of Akasik, including an incredible story of one Daviros Stormbreaker. The accounts tell us that at one point of the battle, one of the genies had the upper hand over our lord, and at the moment before she could deal a deadly blow, Daviros took up his spear and thrust it into her back, providing a distraction at great cost to himself as the magical feedback coursed through his body. Thanks to his bravery, Elikhet was able to recover and lead the army to victory. After the battle, as Daviros lay in agony, Elikhet healed him and named him Stormbreaker, and proclaimed in front of all that this mortal saved his life. He cried out that this demonstrated that humans can stand proud as equals to himself, and that his empire must treat all as having the same worth, from the meekest to the mightiest. This account has been very encouraging to the government, reading the words of Elikhet that accord so strongly with our ideals. Better yet, by tracing further records we have discovered that the spear of Daviros Stormbreaker has been kept by the Order of the Stormbreakers, founded that day, until the present time. This relic is a mighty symbol of our cause, and would be better served out in the world than kept in a secretive vault. Some argue for it to be carried by our highest ranking general to inspire the troops further as they fight for the cause. Others claim it should be kept in the capital, freely on display for all citizens to behold and be reminded of the tale of Daviros. Where will it best serve the people?
