It has become clear to us that the consort is a person with a heart of gold. They always do their best to please their spouse, the monarch, and treat their friends with unparalleled kindness. During a recent trip to a province, the consort saw the suffering of its poorest peasants with their own eyes. Ever since, they have been drafting a plan to help their most destitute subjects. In order to make these ideas into reality, however, the consort needs some funds from the nation’s treasury.
