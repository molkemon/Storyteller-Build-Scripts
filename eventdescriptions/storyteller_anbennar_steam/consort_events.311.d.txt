It has become clear to us that the consort is a woman with a heart of gold. She always does her best to please her husband, the monarch, and treats her friends with unparalleled kindness. During a recent trip to the poor province, the consort saw the suffering of its poorest peasants with her own eyes. Ever since, she has been drafting a plan to help her most destitute subjects. In order to make these ideas into reality, however, the consort needs some funds from the nation's treasury.
