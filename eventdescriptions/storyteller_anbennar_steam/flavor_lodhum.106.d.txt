It seems the bloodshed in Cannor has found its way here. A group of travelling Adeanic knights from the Empire of Anbennar, hoping to test their mettle against the horrors of the Dwarovar, have come into conflict with a group of Corinite crusaders from Escann. The rivalry is beginning to disturb the locals, as although it was simply reserved for contests of skill or strength, one side has issued the demands for a duel to the death. We must act quickly if we wish to forbid this, as it may have repercussions.
