The Ravelian Society has revealed that they have acquired a precursor artifact they call the 'God Fragment'. This fistshaped shard is said to be the origin of all magic in the Prime Material Plane (our world), and that the precursor elves stole and disseminated magic from a god which they had defeated and bound in this fragment. Whether it is true or not, whispers say that the fragment's highpitched noises is actually a language that the society have begun to decipher with the help of the fragment's original discoverer, the famous elven archaeologist Torrieth.  Not much else comes from this revelation, though it seems the Ravelian Society as a whole has become enamoured with this artifact, as it seems that it is now their best hope for unraveling the secrets of this world.
