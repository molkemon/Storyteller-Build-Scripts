The grip of the ancients never fades in dwarven society. Perpetuated by worship, those that came before still live and breathe in the ethereal plane, waiting for the opportunity to return as a spirit. The OzAmekh is one such spirit, with a firm grasp over life in death. A firm grasp indeed; his thoughts emanate throughout the throne room, spoken from the High King's mouth as if the earthly monarch is merely a puppet of the spirit. It is time to face the truth. the OzAmekh is the true king of Verkal Ozovar.
