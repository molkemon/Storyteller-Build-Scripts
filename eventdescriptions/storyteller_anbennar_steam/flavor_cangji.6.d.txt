The spirits of Danggun have been adamant in their resistance to our expansion, as the harmful effects of spiritfire on spirits of many types is becoming more problematic in areas such as Gowon, where spirits hold important administrative duties. Like many problems, this issue was eventually solved through the joint effort of Danggun’s spiritual advisors and the pyremages of the Ashen Soul. Slightly tweaking the inlays of the braziers, the flames can be calibrated to allow for spirits of a certain pattern to be unaffected by the repelling heat, allowing the spirits of Gowon to continue their duties without being harmed. With this rift between our cultures healed, we may now finally understand their teachings of the spirits.
