Let the beautiful, terrible thorns shield the young growth of my flowers and vegetables. Let the sun shine on them all, thorns and plants alike, to prolong this beautiful day. So may Roilsard, the great home of all thorns, prosper and forever be glorious. If you, O sower of the thorns, would brightly come to me, you should be emperor, you should be king, and enjoy yourself without end. Be on your guard against your enemies; choke them out like weeds in my garden. The beautiful, terrible thorns surround that verdant valley. With their seeds in hand, their sower is like a warrior. Return frequently to me, as a watchman at morning, and shield me with your might and power.
