Many goblins of the clan feel a strong connection to one specific aspect of the stories of old, but few devote their whole lives to understanding them in their entirety. Even among those that do, it is rare to find an individual whose character and deeds so closely align with their favourite fictional chevaliers. Such individuals are sometimes called Emulants, and the bibliophiles of the clan have noted that the great bravery, chivalry and pride of the monarch may mark them as one of the most swell knights to ever grace Escann. Several easily impressed goblins have approached the ruler to recommend further contemplation of specific plot points in the stories they like, though there appears to be a split among them about which tomes exactly we might dedicate our time to. Spending many of your waking hours reading, studying, and possibly even understanding a new book would take your focus from the direct administration of the realm and require the delegation of some important responsibilities, but could yield major knightly prowess. Will you pursue the wonders of chivalry further, my ruler?
