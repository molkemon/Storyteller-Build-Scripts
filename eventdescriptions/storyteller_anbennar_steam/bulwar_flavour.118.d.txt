Every four years the Gelkar people hold great goat shearing competitions. Most that compete are local farmers that have developed their own techniques, but the winners of these contests are often career shearers that make their living taking work from the larger farms seasonally. These local winners then compete in regional competitions, and now in a grand finale set to be hosted in the capital. Such an event always seems to bring about a boost in trade, but perhaps we could reap other benefits by supporting the competition.
