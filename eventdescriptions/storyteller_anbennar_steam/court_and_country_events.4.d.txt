Recent years have seen a number of new taxes and obligations put on many realm cities and guilds. So far we have received all what has been asked for, but our local officials report an increasing dissatisfaction among them. With the growing resistance to the capital edicts and taxes, the most powerful cities in the province have banded together around the mayor of that city. They now demand their rights to levy their own taxes and privileges to uphold their own laws, and they have violently evicted our local official from the area.
