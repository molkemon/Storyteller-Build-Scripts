The lumber is set, the mortar applied; the new temple is looking alright. It is not really all that glorious, but that is not what it intends to be. Just a simple place of worship with an open roof to view the skies with. It does have, despite the rustic appearance, a certain divine touch. Drakaneld could not dispute that. He liked it the longer he looked at the building. So did the rest of the carpenters. The celebrating construction crew was observed from a distance. Hushed whispers exchanged. Overwhelming hatred.
