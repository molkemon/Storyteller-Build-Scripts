Many say he could become a clan head and lead his clan to greatness, but he is young still, and must prove himself. The warrior bows respectfully, but the look in their eyes is like starved iron. The match begins. The young brave adopts a novel tactic, moving in zigzag patterns rather than the traditional chargeshotretreat.
