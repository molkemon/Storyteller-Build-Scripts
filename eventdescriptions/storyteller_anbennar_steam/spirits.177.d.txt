The silence of a newborn is always a bad sign, but when a whole province is experiencing a dramatic increase in stillbirths and crib deaths, something is deeply wrong. This has happened in the province, and the population is deeply disturbed and fearful of what is going on. Despite efforts, we have not been able to identify any poisoned water sources, diseased crops, or any material causes. Thus, we believe that it is Yinti who is punishing the province. The reason behind this remains uncertain, but some elders in the province speak of infidelity and broken love as the possible cause.
