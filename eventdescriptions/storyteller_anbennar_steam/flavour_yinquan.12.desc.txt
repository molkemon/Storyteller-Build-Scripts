The monarch hefted a barrel of mijiu into the room, their muscles bulging with exertion. The HuliJing paused their drink to clap for them as they set it down. It was a slow and sarcastic kind of applause. "Do you know why I had you, specifically, bring this last barrel to me?" she said, "I wanted to tell you this next part in person." The fox spirit took another swig of mijiu. The monarch waited in respectful silence. "I, on behalf of the HuliJing, grant you a boon for tribute. Tell me, maiden, what it is you desire." The monarch had only one response.
