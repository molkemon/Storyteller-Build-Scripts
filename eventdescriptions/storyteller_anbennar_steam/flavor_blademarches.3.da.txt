The ruler ship over the Blademarches has always relied on one thing and one thing only: the Test of the Gleaming Blade. The test is simple: fix your eyes on the blade and un sheath it from its scabbard. If you are worthy, you will see clear and true. If you are not, you will be blinded permanently.
As the nominal heir of the realm, the current lord, by tradition, has the first chance to take the test and prove themselves a worthy successor. Of course, if they wish, they are also granted two privileges: the right to find a blade chosen: someone else the blade deems worthy but acts as a champion for the lord. Secondly, the right to train in the art of the sword with the Blade Stewards for a few years, if needed, to ensure fair and stable succession, though others may not see it that way.
