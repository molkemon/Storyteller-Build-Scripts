As the siege of the capital draws to its violent conclusion, the rule of the venerable monarch is cut to a grisly end. Our dignified ruler is pulled from the palace to the streets, and as the city burns around the gnoll captors toss a sword haphazardly to their feet. The whooping laughter of demonic gnoll warriors echoes cruelly through their ears as the monarch lifts with noble hands the sword and points it towards their hated foe; the opposing ruler, who stands opposite the monarch in the impromptu arena, flanked by a circle of cowardly gnolls. The two eye each other tensely; though the monarch is battered with the wounds inflicted by their captors, the monarch regards with a keen cunning all the myriad weaknesses of their opponent. Even as the opponent's bodyguards grows reckless in their mocking laughter, the opponent regards their foe with the wariness of an experienced warrior. Clearly intimidated, the opponent paces hesitantly towards the monarch with their own weapon at the ready. In a flash, the combat commences; yet in the same moment it is ended, as the life and intelligence fades from the monarch's eyes, cut low by some demonic treachery on the opponent's part. The news of the capital's fall spreads quickly across Bulwar, and many fear the worst; that the monarch, the guiding beacon of the New Sun Cult, would be sacrificed to the dark masters of the demonspawn on a burning pyre, as countless other elves have been. Their imaginations fail to conceive the monarch's actual ignominious fate; cannibalized by the opponent immediately after their duel, so that the opponent might sate their demonic gluttony.
