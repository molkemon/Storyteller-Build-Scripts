As the siege of the capital draws to its violent conclusion, the rule of the venerable lord of their dynasty is cut to a grisly end. Our dignified ruler is pulled from their palace to the streets, and as the city burns around them, their gnollish captors toss a sword haphazardly to their feet. The whooping laughter of demonic gnollish warriors echoes cruelly through their ears as they lift with noble hands the sword and points it towards their hated foe; the opposing lord of their dynasty, who stands opposite them in the impromptu arena, flanked by a circle of cowardly gnollish creatures. The two eye each other tensely; though they are battered with the wounds inflicted by their captors, they regard with a keen cunning all the myriad weaknesses of their opponent. Even as the opponent’s bodyguards grow reckless in their mocking laughter, they regard their foe with the wariness of an experienced warrior. Clearly intimidated, they pace hesitantly towards them with their own weapon at the ready. In a flash, the combat commences; yet in the same moment it is ended, as life and intelligence fades from their eyes, cut low by some demonic treacherry on the opponent’s part. The news of the capital’s fall spreads quickly across Bulwar, and many fear the worst; that they, the guiding beacon of the New Sun Cult, would be sacrificed to the dark masters of the demonspawn on a burning pyre, as countless other elves have been. Their imaginations fail to conceive their actual ignominious fate; cannibalized by the opponent immediately after their duel, so that they might sate their demonic gluttony.
