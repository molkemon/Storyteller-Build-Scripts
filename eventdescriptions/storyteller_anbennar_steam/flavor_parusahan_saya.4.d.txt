With the fall of both the offices of Vizier and Raja, no power is left which can question the ascendancy of this realm. In the streets of Dhenijansar, people cheer as our glorious host parades through the streets, celebrating their liberation from the corrupt ways of their old masters. The court trembles at our approach, titanic footsteps of steel and stone marching to claim what is rightfully ours. For we are no longer a humble breadbasket serving the people, but a radiant host of valiant warriors, defending Rahen from all who seek to weaken us. We are the true guardians of the Raheni; we are the true Raj.
