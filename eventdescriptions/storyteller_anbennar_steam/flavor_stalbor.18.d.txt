Finally our anxious wait has ended; our risky gamble with the monster hunters has paid off. With the first rays of dawn, the first of their members emerged from this grueling mission in the lower levels of the fortress. In her grip she held the severed head of one of our intransigent enemies. The rictus grin of the head reveals abnormally sharp and elongated fangs in a face that might otherwise pass as that of a pale, pampered aristocrat. Over the course of the day more and more of their number climbed up the stairs from the catacombs deep below their feet, carrying with them not only proof of their success in smoking out the last remaining pockets of resistance, but also deeply disturbing documents, artwork, and other artifacts that might shine a light on the true nature of the tenebrous adversaries we have faced here. But now it is time to celebrate our long overdue victory and begin the transformation of the fortress from a symbol of oppression into a beacon of freedom.
