Many diplomatic ministers dreamed of this day, when the great Satrapy reform was complete. It has never been about tributaries paying protection money, but the cooperation of states for the benefit of the common citizens. Councillors and guildmasters alike have seen the outcomes of the reforms, and many of our Satrapies have been reorganized into Medasi with even greater integration with our country, leading to economic prosperity for their people and greater safety for all of our realms. Soon we will see all of our republican allies working as closely with our Grand Republic, and people across Sarhal will reap the benefits. The whole is now truly greater than the sum of its parts.
