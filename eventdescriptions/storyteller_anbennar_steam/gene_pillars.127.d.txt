The party feels themselves nearing the end of their journey, but unfortunately now stands before the most devious trap they have yet encountered within these walls. The chamber before them looks normal, albeit more infused with Kiamoa than others, but a stray insect walking through the room immediately collapses into a pile of sludge as it crosses the threshold. It is clear that walking into this room would surely spell doom for the party. Luckily, it seems that even this trap was too difficult for the Precursors to bypass, and as such it comes with a manual deactivation switch. But upon further examination it has become clear that triggering this switch will release the Kiamoa in the room which powers its defenses, unleashing the pure unadulterated might of the storm onto the hapless adventurers.
