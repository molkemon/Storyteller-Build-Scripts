Our celebration across the land aimed to show every citizen the wonder of life in Surael's embrace, as a citizen of the Jadd Empire. This policy has been particularly effective in the realm. While at first the locals watched the dancing soldiers and Lightbringers with a mixture of fear and bewilderment, they could not deny the appeal of the free food and alcohol at our state-sponsored banquets. And so our generosity opened their hearts to the first rays of Surael's light. As the festivities continued, we brought in everything from music to puppet shows to tell the truths of Jaddar, Jaher, and Surael in a language they would understand. Some of our most skilled missionaries visited the area as well, including mages who could prove Surael's might directly. With the sun shining so bright before them, it is unsurprising they have now embraced the Jadd and our Empire.
