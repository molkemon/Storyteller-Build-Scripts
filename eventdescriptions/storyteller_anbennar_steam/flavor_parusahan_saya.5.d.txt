All throughout the realm, forges begin to emerge with a curious new contingent working within: the Farrum Arcanum, metallurgists working with mages to create iron and steel in levels never before imagined by the citizens of the domain. The highlands are especially flooded with this new order, as countless new sources of metal are discovered within the hills. Day after day, diviners locate rich new deposits in the hills, and miners excavate the materials used in the forges in the valleys of the region. There, teams of pyromancers and blacksmiths melt, forge, and temper the iron at blazing speeds, and soon the market is flooded with new metals unlike any have ever seen before. Hemantsen and Rani Jira smile as the reports return of their successful plans, with Jira especially pleased to hear her connections have served them well in their endeavors. The royal couple returns to their work, arguing over vast tables scattered with maps of the realm. This is one project that has proven a success, but the work continues all the same.
