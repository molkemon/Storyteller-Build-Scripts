After weeks of reading, the shamans have summarized their findings to the ruler based on several government documents. When HulJorkad fell, a group of dwarves made it out alive and fled through the Dwarovrod to Seghdihr. They described thousands of monsters breaking out of the prison on the seventh floor and flooding the hold; the description they give of the monsters clearly indicates they speak of their ancestors. They did not say much more, just to fortify the northern gate and that they are heading to Grônstunad to report to the secondary capital, refusing to disclose classified information about HulJorkad to the local governor of Seghdihr, to his frustration. It seems the shamans seem fixated on going to Grônstunad and tracking down that report.
