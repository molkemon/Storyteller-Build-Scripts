Ever since the fall of the Verdant Veil, change had not been just a constant for the Wood Elf clans, but an ever increasing factor. Goods, knowledge, and people flowed in and out at a dizzying flow for a land isolated for millennia. And at the heart of this somehow, in the midst of this torrent, Hofónn. Despite being the capital of Salla Cenág, it had barely been more than a backwater. The town itself was little more than a handful of hovels larger than the scouting camp it had started as. Yet, now it stood as a true hub of the Deepwoods. One could find metal tubes that thundered and shot fire. Merchants hawked Chronicler manuscripts describing the recently rediscovered lost elven homeland of Aelantier. And if one even listened carefully, they could hear the chants of strange missionaries spreading the word of the Jadd. Still, it still came as a shock to the ruler when the clans of the Verdant Pact decided to meet within Hofónn rather than return where the Pact was signed in Elynaille.
