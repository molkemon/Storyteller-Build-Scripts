Now that we have begun reconquering land in the Dragon Coast, we must decide how it will be divided amongst us and the gnomes. We will take this decision now to facilitate the land split as we conquer. Both sides present their arguments as they are the rightful owners of the land. Still, the situation could be boiled down. the Reavers represent most of our soldiers and generals, and the gnomes our advisors and researchers. Which side should we favor?
