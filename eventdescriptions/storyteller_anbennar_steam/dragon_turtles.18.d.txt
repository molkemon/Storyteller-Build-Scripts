A new leader has risen in this realm. Makaoioi the Erudite would say that every ruler is merely the sum of the people behind them, and if he is correct then our ruler sums up to quite a great amount indeed. Makaoioi has been working specially with the government to help the monarch get settled in their position, and now a small army of precision-trained bureaucrats stand at the ready to guide the realm along whatever path the monarch thinks is best.
