Ever since the ruler appeared at the noble court, they have been a mystery to our own elite class. Nobody knows their name nor has been able to find information about this secretive ruler. As time went on, the noble became friends with the monarch. Their capability proves them as skillful in the matters of administration, diplomacy, and the military, and they are in general well received in the court. Meanwhile, a vile rumor about the competence of the ruler spreads within the government. It is clear that the monarch is losing any kind of support of benefactors while the noble is gaining more and more support for the election.
