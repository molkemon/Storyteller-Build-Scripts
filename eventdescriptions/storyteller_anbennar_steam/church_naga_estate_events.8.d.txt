Even now, they will not fawn over you. It is their sacred duty to avert Tsishagya, the madness of the one. They will watch you for it, your monarch. You will never go unobserved again. A time will come soon when you must once more return to the heavy places and prove yourself, but for now you reign sovereign. The collected teachings of a hundred thousand years are presented for your perusal, your network of Naga efficiently sorting the mundane from the salient. They serve as extensions of your senses, a means by which you might perceive the world ever more completely. Sometimes, it feels as if they know your desires before you even think to word them. Take heed; the next test the Naga set you will prove more rigorous.
