Long ago, hundreds of elven ships desperately set sail eastwards, fleeing the impending doom of the Day of Ashen Skies. Sadly, it seems that for some of them, the journey ended before it even began. Wrecked upon the northern shore of the isle of Ivrandir lies the island’s namesake, an ancient ruined Migrant Ship that was quickly recognized as the lost ship Ivrandir by elven archaeologists. From what has been surmised, the Ivrandir drifted off course during the initial panic and became separated from the rest of the Remnant Fleet in Uelos’ Lament, sailing blindly before eventually running aground on the shallow waters northeast of what is now Endralliande. The survivors on the boat would have been forced to sit and watch as the Ruin unfolded in front of them, far too unsheltered and close to the epicenter for any hope of survival. A number of millennia-old bones were discovered within the hull, and though they were far too long gone to be identifiable, they were given proper burials regardless. Though it is surprisingly intact for a ship of its age—a testament to the incredible engineering techniques of the Precursors—the Ivrandir is far too old and derelict to ever sail again. That being said, having such a well-preserved Migrant Ship in our possession has caught the attention of many Lencori shipbuilders, who have flocked to the site in hopes of improving their naval construction techniques, and perhaps even the production of their so-called Behemoths.
