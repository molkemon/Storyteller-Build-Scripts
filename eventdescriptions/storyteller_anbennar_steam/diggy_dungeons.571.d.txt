"There." exclaims the lead engineer as he turns the last valve with a flourish. Immediately the sprinkler directly above blasts the floor around them with foul water, and he scrambles to turn the valve back off as unlucky expedition members around him are splashed. At least now they know the sprinklers certainly work; and with unexpected force. Activated at the right time, they will be a great boon against Aldorlbur.
