Some months ago, a warlord officer in our army began using the Gophiran troops under his command to subdue a swath of small, villagesized city states and effectively add them to his personal domain. With the warlord merely seizing farming communities, which are nonetheless valuable sources of tribute, the land grab escaped our attention. Seeing no repercussions had befallen the officer, others started following his example. Some of these copycats did not have the original officer’s tact, and a coalition of local herocults, the only groups left there powerful enough to complain, have come to us outraged by the commanders' abuses. Unfortunately, we do not have the strength to deal with these renegade commanders at this time, as they are in charge of the very forces we would try to put them down with.
