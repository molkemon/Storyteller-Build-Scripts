Our presence along the Gerudian Coast has become a major impetus for urbanization in the region. Those who live in the rural areas of Gerudia seem to be flocking towards major cities, causing these rickety medieval cores to expand at an unprecedented rate. It seems that these northern cities will continue to grow, becoming more similar to our own cities by the day. Soon enough, both the Gerudian Coast and the Alenic Reach will be highly developed and prosperous regions.
