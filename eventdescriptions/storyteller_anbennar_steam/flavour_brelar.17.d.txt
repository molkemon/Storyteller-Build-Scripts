Today it is proven that Dolindha is more prosperous, more powerful, and more just under the rule of the Republic than under the yoke of petty dynastic rule. Those regions which still are ruled by Iosahar sworn to the republic are connected to the parliament in Brelar, and over the next few decades shall be integrated into the greater republic. Thus Dolindha shall be united under the rule of a government which represents it, and it shall be as though each citizen had a seat in the Rider Assembly of old.
