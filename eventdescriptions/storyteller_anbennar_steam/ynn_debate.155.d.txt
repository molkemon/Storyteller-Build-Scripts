A proclamation has come down from their leader, condemning the use of firearms by worshippers of the Ynn. These weapons, they claim, go against the natural order of the Ynn, upholding the vulgar while laying low the virtuous. As we face armies wielding these weapons, we ought to rely on magic and blades as we always have, lest we lose who we are. In one's attempt to fight monsters, one cannot allow oneself to become a monster in return. By the grace of the Ynn, we will prevail regardless of our weapons.
