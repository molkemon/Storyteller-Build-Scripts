The lord surveys the storied halls of Amldihr, frowning. When the holds of the Kingsrock were seized, the Clanboss had been seeking a grand home for their people. In that regard, they’d been successful. They had not, however, accounted for the message such an endeavour would send. Barely a month after Amldihr had fallen, the first of the outsider goblins had arrived. Before long, they were showing up in droves, flooding in through the gates and tunnels, seeking a new home of their own. The hold is already damaged from years of orkish misuse, and these refugees are hardly helping matters.
