The ornately carved oak doors of the throne room swung open with a heavy thud, announcing the arrival of a visitor. Cloaked and weathered, the figure strode confidently across the polished marble floor, the worn leather boots whispering tales of countless battles. Hands, clutched tight, held a simple scrap of parchment; a royal decree seeking a Hero for the realm, plucked from the dusty notice board of a local tavern. The silence in the court was as heavy as the hero's armor. All eyes were fixed on the figure whose presence commanded respect. This wasn't just any soldier; but a Hero who would lead our realm to glory.
