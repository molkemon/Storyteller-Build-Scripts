Some time ago, our esteemed ruler visited the temple at Balrijin and paid their respects in accordance with the millennia-old rituals. After performing the ceremony, a kobold monk, a sycophant rather transparently trying to get in their good graces, informed the ruler of a supposed prophecy that went as follows. "One day, a Great Conqueror shall arise in the East as Harimar reborn, and under their armies, Yanshen shall be united, under the banner of the wisest of Emperors." While many in court have dismissed this as the ramblings of an old monk, it has been hard to dissuade the ruler of the possibility of being Harimar’s reincarnation in some way.
