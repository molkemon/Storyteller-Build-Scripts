With fanfare, the arrival of the Highlord is announced as the doors to the realm open. The monarch strides down the newly renovated hall, taking in the Platinum Court as it stands to greet him. The atmosphere is taut as the new Platinum nobility shift in place, a nervous but excited energy challenging their attempts at regal manners. Adventurers who were made lords, now with a proper Highlord to lead them. The monarch takes their seat on high in a masterfully crafted, platinum ornamented throne. Taking a breath, the ruler dons the ancient Dwarovkron, and states.
