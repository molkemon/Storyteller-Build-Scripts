We are at war, but not just any war. Our enemy is not only one of the current time, but a hated rival of our nation. At times such as this, the provinces are united against a common threat, and nobles are eager to lead them into battle. Who better to lead than a Calasanni prince? While allowing the Silcalas family to rally other nobles and the common people to war would strengthen combat morale, the Republicans are not fools. They know that the effects of this unity will linger beyond the war and that they will be reluctant to let go of any power they are granted, even for the best of causes.
