It’s no secret that both the lord and the lady have a way with words, and thus with people. They can both make a stranger feel perfectly at ease in their presence, and somehow always manage to be the one making the closing argument in a discussion. Although perhaps not entirely equal in charm, and using different personal strengths, they have grown to enjoy attending social events together, often seen smiling smugly at one another from a distance. What is for certain is that the government is better off since their union.
