Cinulau, the majestic whale spirit, often referred to as the Wandering Isle, has once again graced our waters with his presence. As a testament to our allegiance, he pledges to unveil the mysteries of the deep sea and bestow upon our sailors the wisdom to charter uncharted waters. This ancient being, with a song that resonates through the depths, has tales of forgotten realms and will guide us to treasures both tangible and ethereal. In his wake, a path of prosperity and discovery unfolds for our people.
