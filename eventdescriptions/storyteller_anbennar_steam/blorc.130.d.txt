The radiating red heat of the gigantic flows of magma were almost unbearable when we finally entered the heart of the hold. Like blood to a heart, these streams of molten rock lead into a gigantic forge, fueled directly by the very stone and earth herself. Gigantic heatproof pipes carry the slurry to the array of blast furnaces that refine the unimaginable amounts of ore the mines throughout the target produce. To use these foundries for our purpose would be a blessing, so our top priority now is to dismantle what we find and carry it over to the capital, and rebuild it there, to the best of our ability.
