Only son of Azmirethazin and Argezenna, Dolurazan was taught mastery over the black metals such as iron, wrought and cast, steel, adamantine, and titanium. In time, he created the arms and armour of the gods and their servants in the Era of War. During the Giantbreak Wars, one of his arms was severed by a Fire Giant kin when he was captured. His sister, Derzobrazan, freed him from captivity and forged a new arm as a replacement. Dolurazan is seen as the patron of all blacksmiths, and many of their smiths now keep small shrines to him in their forges.
