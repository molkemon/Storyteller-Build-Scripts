The expedition reaches Mindanan.  The locals, when translated, speak of this place with awe. The temples here are incredible, rising out of the jungle canopies towards the sky, and yet. standing here, every halfling feels a strange sense of being grounded. As if Halann herself is standing next to them.  Ella enters one of the temples alone, bringing nothing but the clothes on her back, a pot of tea laced with sleeping draught, and a notebook and pencil.  She sleeps, but doesn't dream; instead, she speaks.  'Is anybody here with me?'    'I come seeking aid. The Verdan'    'I. I don't understand.'    Ella leaves the temple, with a complexlooking potion scrawled in her notebook.
