The expedition reaches Mindanan. The locals, when translated, speak of this place with awe. The temples here are incredible, rising out of the jungle canopies towards the sky, and yet, standing here, every halfling feels a strange sense of being grounded, as if Halann herself is standing next to them. Ella enters one of the temples alone, bringing nothing but the clothes on her back, a pot of tea laced with sleeping draught, and a notebook and pencil. She sleeps, but doesn't dream; instead, she speaks. "Is anybody here with me?" "I have always been with you, halfling." "I come seeking aid. I know what you seek. Tell me, child, what makes one form of life different to another? Would I not love you all the same?" "I don't understand." "Not yet. But you will. If only you speak to it, as you speak to me." Ella leaves the temple, with a complex-looking potion scrawled in her notebook.
