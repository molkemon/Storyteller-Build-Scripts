Hailing from the eponymous Forest of the Cursed Ones, a horde of these purpleskinned buycev have descended on our frontier villages like a hammer upon a nail. Communicating effortlessly and silently, they leave little trace, yet seemingly seek no loot. Instead, they merely burn, kill, and otherwise destroy everything in sight before returning to their accursed forest. We could send out a force to root out the Cursed Ones and protect the region, but it would be extremely costly.
