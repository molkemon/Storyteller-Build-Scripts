An Epednar tribe has made camp outside of a garrison marking the border of our territory. The chieftain and the local garrison commander have engaged in dialogue on an overlooking hill. He claims his tribe has been forced to migrate out of their ancestral lands by a rival tribe, desperate to survive, and with nowhere to go, the men of the tribe will pledge their service to our armies in exchange for being allowed entry into our lands. The Epednar prioritize speed over power, riders of horses much swifter than our own; they would be very useful as scouts or mobile auxiliaries.
