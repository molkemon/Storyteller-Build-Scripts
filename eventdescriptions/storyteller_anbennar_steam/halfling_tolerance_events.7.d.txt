It is well known that halflings breed like rabbits, and those in our country are no exception. This has led to overpopulation in one of our provinces and a migration into a neighboring one. While the internal movements of our citizens are usually not our buisiness, these halflings are a special case. Should we hinder their expansion, or help decrease overpopulation? We could, of course, do nothing, but that might result in a less organised exodus.
