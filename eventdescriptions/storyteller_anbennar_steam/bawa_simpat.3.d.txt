It began with a roar from the jungle that shook the ruler's very core. With almost no warning, a tide of spirits burst into the monarch's courtroom, a whirlwind of indignant shouts and cries of fear. The spirits wailed demands for one to step forward and confess to their grave misstep among the ethereal, but not a soul in the courtroom stepped forward. The ruler glared at them with eyes of cold fury until they left. Thus began a trend; the local spirits of the realm have begun shirking their loyalties to the ruler. Intimidating the people, refusing to speak to the monarch even when directly addressed; even more bizarre behaviors have been seen in outlying villages. It has come to a head in the last week. a friendly spirit known for dancing with children and guiding travelers to the capital intentionally has led the hunters in circles for almost a week, demanding an answer as to who has defied the Great Lizard. None of them can give one.
