We have made of our wyverns silver dragons, clad at their joints with paperthin sheets of mithril which barely add to their bearing weight. With this, we have overcome the final and perhaps most fundamental vulnerability of the magically shielded wyvern; ranged weaponry boasting black Dames tear. As rifles and volley tactics have proliferated across the armies of Halann, our wyvern doctrines have been made increasingly opportunistic, relying on skirmish and surprise as direct charges became more and more risky. Now, however, we can plough right into a company of riflemen, finally once again using the close quarters ferocity and terror of our wyvern mounts.
