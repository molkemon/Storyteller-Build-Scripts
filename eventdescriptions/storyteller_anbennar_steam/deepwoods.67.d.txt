The wise have often noted that even time itself plays tricks in the Deepwoods. Years can pass in seconds, and now, a moment stretches to eternity. Your arrow sails leisurely through the air like a bird on the wing, whistling softly, until it buries itself in the monster’s heart with a sudden thud. The Lord of Beasts snaps toward you as he shrieks a wretched cry, and lurches forward onto the ground, slain. You fall to your knees, overcome with relief. The sword of fate no longer waits above your neck. Your gaze turns to the slain monster, and a wave of realization ends the trance. You have hunted and slain the Lord of Beasts like a common stag. A feat of such enormity will inscribe your name in legends forever.
