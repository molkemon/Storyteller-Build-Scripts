The recent Corinite Proclamation has shaken the hearts of the faithful within the Reach. They speak of neglecting duty, of allowing monsters and faithless to prey upon the faithful. This is of course ridiculous blather; did we not support Crovis' expedition against the orks? Have we not raised fleets to protect against raids from the reavers? Now that the Reach is united under our banner, perhaps it is time to once again publicly demonstrate that we are not negligent in our duties. With a renewed conviction that is guided in the proper direction, we could finally end the threat of the Gerudian Reavers for good. By word of the monarch's title, the Northern Crusade has begun.
