The messenger we dispatched to offer our support for Drolakand’s independence has been late in their return to the capital. As we continue to await their arrival, the prevailing theory was that they must have run off with the first of Drolakand’s tribute shipments. However, our spies posted amidst the crags of the Drolas peninsula report a different story; the messenger's headless corpse apparently now hangs proudly from the mast of the monarch’s finest ship. The meaning is clear to our lord, and the method is almost gnollish in its brutality; but it is a foolish gesture nonetheless. The rightful place of Drolakand is beneath our heels.
