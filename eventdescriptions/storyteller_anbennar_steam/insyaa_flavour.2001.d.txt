Where did the rumour start? Even though the country is small, we cannot be sure. Perhaps it was in a flash of memory, provoked by a strangely shaped branch or unlikely turn of phrase. Perhaps it emerged from an encounter with strangers, one of the small bands of other roaming Mechanim we have passed. Perhaps there was a clue, somewhere, somewhen. Like ourselves, however, the rumor is more important for its future than its past. The learning that swirls through the tribe is that even amidst this strange ecology, filled with wonders great and greater, there are places of distinction. Where the world is warped, stranger than its surroundings. Where a lesson can be learned. Our travels are slow, bounded by the weakest amongst us. But we could send out some of the bravest to search for these places and report back what they find. Though this journey may take them through dangerous lands, and with a Holohana could spell disaster. All our wandering is to learn. How can we pass this opportunity up?
