The Fey corruption has spread to most of our kin, and we stand almost outnumbered. If we pure orks are to survive, we must ensure our own advantage against the corrupted. Fortunately, we have the perfect solution. Proposed by the great shaman Bron Blackmoon, it will be able to take the evil magics of the woods and transfer it to our weaponry. In other words, by burning the trees that surround us, we can transfer their magic into the iron and steel of our weapons. With their own magics turned against them, the corrupted will stand no chance against our mighty warriors.
