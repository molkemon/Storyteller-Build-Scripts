Suhan and his Praxis have been a disaster for Raheni society. It has made the humans disobedient and led other harimari to believe in falsehoods, like denying their superiority over humans. No matter how hard we try, Suhan's ideas seem impervious to any counter arguments, so a different approach is needed. We shall enlighten the people about the real Suhan with several anecdotes. For example, one day Suhan was on a sinking ship. As the water roiled, Suhan scampered into an escape boat and began rowing away. The remaining passengers cried out from the deck, "Suhan. There are still children on the boat," but Suhan replied, "I appreciate the offer but I am not hungry." Another time while Suhan was in the Hall of Endless Debate, a kobold approached him to cogitate. Confused, Suhan looked down at the kobold, exclaiming, "Oh dear. Someone's pet is loose." Suhan's followers, obeying a whim, gifted him a chariot. After drinking many pain relief potions from Khandi, he began to pilot the vehicle with abandon. He soon came across a school for hobgoblin children, crashing his chariot into them. Actually, Suhan wasn’t being evil there. How could anyone associate themselves with Suhan after this? These true stories shall be disseminated across the realm. Now it’s unlikely people will recognize the validity of these stories immediately, but if we keep repeating them, then eventually our citizens will start believing at least some of them, right?
