Steam, smog, and the never-ending clog of the machines are plenty. Steam Barons are no longer mere experimenters nor relatively influential magnates of many artificial, magical, and technological things. No. While we have dug out our hold and nation from the deepest and most scorching depths of mediocrity, they have made us prosper. While we have fought for recognition by risking even the ingrained roots of our beards, they have made sure that there is a future for us and that it would be glorious. Now, we have reached that future. To say we prosper is to do us a disservice; we are booming. Steam Barons have always fought for us, so it was only a matter of time until we started fighting for them. They run the country, they know how to make it tick in brilliant rhythm, and even in this optimistic future of ours we have just reached, they are still planning for more.
