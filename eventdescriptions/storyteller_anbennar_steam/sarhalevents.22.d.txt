With the unrest throughout the land, your advisors have suggested you barricade the doors to your estate; you've taken this advice to heart. One day, as you pace your entrance hall, empty of petitioners and filled with guards, you hear a crack on your front door. Then another. Then another, then another, then your door bursts open. Your guards jump to attention, and a hulking cloaked figure steps through the opening where your door once was. It speaks in a booming voice as your guards shake in fear. 'I can bring you peace. I can slay your enemies, crush the traitors, quell sedition. Yes or no?'
