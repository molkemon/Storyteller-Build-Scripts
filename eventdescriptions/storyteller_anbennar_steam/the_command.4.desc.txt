Every fourth year, the KenuIke of the Command meets to decide policy, make strategic decisions, and determine the course of the Command's conquests. The successful conclusion of the previous campaign has heralded the arrival of another one, and this time it is aimed near us. Luckily, the Command is not subtle with its direction. news of hobgoblin troop movements have already made their way to our ears. Over the next few decades, the Command will stop at nothing to conquer the territory it has set its sights on. None in the path of their claims will be safe.
