A centaur watches over his herd and his chattel from his palace, and the vast amount of goods being produced in his cities, fueled by the work of his slaves. The Caehn and his clans are but leeches, feeding off the wealth of the Vine Tearer and siphoning from his people what rightfully belongs to him. As he reaches for his whip and spear, he knows that the Great Caehn shall know the pain of the lash.
