Many land donations are quite old, and while they are always written down, both landscapes and names may well have changed since a donation was originally written. Recently, the church has been accusing several members of the nobles of outright land theft in the form of encroaching on lands donated to the church long ago. The nobles, on the other hand, argue that it is the church who are trying to seize land areas they do not own. Whoever we decide to side with will become the majority land holder in the area.
