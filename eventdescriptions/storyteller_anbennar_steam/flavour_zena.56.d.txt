Kiusz made it damn clear that he wasn’t happy about going back north, and I agreed with him. The forests above the Ogre Valley are cold, desolate, and entirely inhospitable. Now, the few locals that live there believe that spirits inhabit everything in the forest, from rocks to trees. I can’t imagine the tree spirits feeling too good when they get ripped in half by a Tlalusk; giant wooly nightmares, nearly fifteen feet tall and with six legs. Similar to the elephants back home but far more ornery, they graze on lichens and shrubs for sustenance. Getting in their way is a good way to get impaled by their tusks, and that’s if you survive the roar, which can rupture eardrums and knock a grown man flat on his ass. Best of all? Damn roar alerts every Tlalusk for miles to come in and help their friend. So we knew we were gonna have to pull out all the stops on this one.
