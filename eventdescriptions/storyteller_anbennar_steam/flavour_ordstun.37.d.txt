Our lord holds the Aegis up to the light, smiling at it like an old friend. The diamond shines and shimmers, a star in the sky: and it looks so beautiful, now that they can actually witness it. The gem cannot pull them any further, for they have already seen what lies within. They look down, watching refracted light illuminate portholes in the floor; they witness layers of civilization from the highest level of Argordstun, seeing a beehive of a hold hum with activity. Millions of dwarves craft relics, runes, laughter, song, and prosperity in a hold of countless riches. All of this- this beauty, this joy, this reverence- eclipses anything our lord’s ancestors could have imagined, back in those days of endless isolation. In Szetur ordstunov, their every fantasy has been fulfilled. Our lord fixes their eyes forwards, placing the Aegis and its crown back on their forehead. They then begin the trek towards the pliant Gleaming Council, ready to proclaim our nation's primacy over all the dwarves of Halann, ready to hear them chant our name without a single voice of dissent. They can feel the weight of empire tense upon their chest. In these last moments of quiet, and of peace, our lord thinks of the Diamond Queens. "May they be proud," they think, "of all we have accomplished. May they be proud of Argordstun, and of Szetur ordstunov."
