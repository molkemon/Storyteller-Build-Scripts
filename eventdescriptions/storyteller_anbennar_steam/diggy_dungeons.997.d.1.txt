As our scouts delve into the sprawling tunnels to search for Yidab, we remain in place, waiting in alert for what might come next. Minutes pass without incident, and as our nerves begin to calm, the threat seemingly gone, we hear a warning shout from above. Seconds after the warning, Yidab drops onto our forces, but with these few crucial seconds of forewarning, we are able to ward off the beast's attacks and harm it greatly.
