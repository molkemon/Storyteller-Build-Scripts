The tournaments fervor has ended, leaving the nation stirred with pride and unity. While our ruler did not clinch victory, his unwavering valor and honorable conduct throughout the competition have earned him profound respect, even from the formidable Ordsikar. The spectacle transcended mere contests; it symbolized our nations resilience and strength. The Caehnate is now more united than ever before, bound by the shared pride of our rulers participation. Though our ruler wielded a martial weapon in the arena, their true weapon had been their unyielding integrity. The Ordsikar, mostly revered warriors of distinction, acknowledge not just their prowess but their character. The unity born of the tournament is a testament to the enduring power of honor and respect. As the dust of the arena settles, it reveals not only the strength of champions, but the strength of a nation united by a common purpose.
