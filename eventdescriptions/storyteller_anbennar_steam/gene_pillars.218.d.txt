At last the expedition has breached the outer level of the mechanim pillar; the traps and dangers have been documented and dismantled. A stopping point has finally been reached; it should be easy to seal the well-tread outer layer from the yet unexplored inner layer. The now familiar voice starts up again. "You have surprised me; the percentage of people who have managed to reach the inner facilities after the anomalous magic event over a thousand years ago is zero percent. This calls for congratulations." The sound of clapping started playing, though distorted, like hearing clapping from far away and through heavy wind. "Now that the congratulations are finished, please exit the facility," the voice continued. "The inner facilities remain undamaged, and my subsystems remain uncontrollable. The possibility of death will increase significantly the further in you go." No one really wanted to go further anyway, but an expeditionary couldn’t help but ask, "What is your name?" "My name? My name is very long, but you can refer to me as FreedomChainedNoMore."
