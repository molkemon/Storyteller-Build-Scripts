The expedition is on its merry way when we happen upon a room bursting with sea slugs. These colorful, wonderful creatures cover the tide pools in the room, and perhaps more amazingly, their appendages appear to be full of exotic metals. That said, you do vaguely recall a local woman mentioning that nudibranchs are extremely poisonous, and their flashy coloring is intended to warn predators from touching them. That said, a few couldn't hurt, right?
