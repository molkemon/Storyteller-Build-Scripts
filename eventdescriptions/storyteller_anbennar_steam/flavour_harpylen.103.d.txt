Following their rest in the roosts of a place, the Ivetasananthor flew east towards the rising sun. As they traced the hills east along the northern coast of a realm, their track ran cold on the edge of the highlands of a region. Yet the flock of a place had spoken of a realm of harpies to the east; the so-called Jewel of the Sky. With this song in their heart, the hunters struck out across the dry plains of a domain, a virtual sea of green and yellow. They kept the coast on their left, until they reached the great city of a place, straddling the great river, and with the vast mountains of the horn of a realm rising behind her. It was in that city that the Ivetasananthor met the Naleni, through one of their ships. The captain of this sleek vessel called herself a Odanchesta, and kept a crew of human, harpy and gnoll under wing and sail. She called her ship her roost, and herself its queen. With her aid did the Ivetasananthor sail around the horn, to a place, the Jewel of the Sky. It was here that they learned the songs of Naleni, and grew to know their ways. When the Phoenix broke a place, the Naleni had flown far, and nestled in the forbidding mountains of a region. Their flight had been chaotic, and the flocks settling here had been isolated and disparate. It would take over a century before a ruler united these flocks into a queendom, with those opposed to her striking east out to sea. There they would become exceptional seafarers, and through piracy they would gather great wealth and power. Eventually, the queendom of Naleni would arise in earnest, as an unsteady accord between these reavers, and the daughters of a ruler. As a legacy of this, the Naleni were infamous pirates, renowned for their skill at sea, as well as a fierce independent streak.
