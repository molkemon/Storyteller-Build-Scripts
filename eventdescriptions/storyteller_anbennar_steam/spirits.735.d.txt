Long have we toiled, long have we suffered. The spirits knew little mercy for the natives of Haless, many fledgling states mercilessly crushed beneath the incomprehensible onslaught. We have not come out of this ordeal stronger, we have not learned some essential lesson from this all. All we have learned is that the world is cruel, and that we must treasure every moment that remains, for we know not when the next Rending shall arrive.
