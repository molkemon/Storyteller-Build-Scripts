As we continue to march towards the lakes of Kalsyto, we have received multiple correspondences from a region of the lakes known as Sunijalla. Languishing under the yolk of democracy, Sunijalla wishes to break free from Kalsyto and instead serve under our banner. Allies in the region are few and far between, and we have seen what democracy does to the magically inclined. We send them our acceptance of their terms as we continue towards the lakes. Pyremages, now reinforced by all the magical techniques they have learned throughout Gozengun, reinforce our soldiers with fiery weapons and shimmering veils of water. Kalsyto has long oppressed those with magical gifts such as ours, and so the only way we can truly defeat them is to show them why oppression cannot lead to a sustainable country. The ways of Kalsyto will be corrected, and magic will flourish in the lake federation once more.
