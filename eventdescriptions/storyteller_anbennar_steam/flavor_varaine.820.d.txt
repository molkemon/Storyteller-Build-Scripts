Having decided to hunt quite literally for more information on their own, our researchers received as a parting gift from the kobolds knowledge of drakes. dragon-like creatures living in the Serpentspine, who despite being much more animalistic and being unable to fly, still possess the dragon's signature breath. Armed with this knowledge, our researchers headed towards the Serpentspine. Arriving there, our researchers started to prepare for their hunt, when a group of dwarves took notice of their preparations. These dwarves, skilled hunters in their own right, made an offer to help the researchers with their hunt, though at a pretty steep cost. While the dwarves' knowledge of the caves of the Serpentspine would be extremely helpful to the researchers' endeavors, they could probably manage without them, though at some risk.
