You stand before the door with apprehension. A creeping wrongness of this place has been growing the further you go into the complex. While you may not consider yourself the most spiritual person, this place feels almost unholy. Steeling yourself, you nod to your allies and start forcing the sealed door in front of you. The door refuses to budge for some time, but eventually with a slight hiss of air escaping you manage to get enough of a gap to enter. Inside is a laboratory, but on a much different scale to any you saw before in this complex. The reason for the scale difference is apparent instantly, as one wall is dominated by a beast of impressive size, even for the local creature. It appears to be a large scaled beast, with wide wings and four large clawed feet. It is completely clothed in scales, varying in size from fingernail sized to some that are large enough to double as shields. After a frantic readying with weapons, it is quickly ascertained that the beast is very dead, and has been laid out across the wall as an anatomical study. One of your colleagues leaves the room rapidly looking decidedly unwell. You manage to control the roiling in your gut and move towards it for closer inspection, as finding knowledge, any knowledge, is the goal of this mission after all. You go forward to look closer and notice something slightly more disturbing; when checking out the clawed feet, the feet appear to have been bound to the wall, yet the scrape marks underneath, and slight damage around the bindings lead you to believe that the dragon may not have been deceased before the anatomical study began, and was flailing in pain and fear before these inhuman scientists.
