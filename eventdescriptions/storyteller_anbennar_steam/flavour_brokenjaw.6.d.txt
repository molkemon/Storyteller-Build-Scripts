BETRAYED AGAIN. As Dookan and Zurtrin were betrayed by Korgus, as all righteous orks were betrayed by Brasur, so now has Broken Jaw been betrayed by those trolls we thought to make equal within our realm. They think to keep their clans separate from ours, to squirrel away all the knowledge we have given them and deny this final, righteous integration of our realms.
