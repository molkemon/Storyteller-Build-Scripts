I, Orwick Kindlehelm, and my fellow mages, do all solemnly swear to serve the Dark Lord Lucian until the sun is no more. We will plunge into every depth of arcane knowledge in utmost service, abide by the rules of the Masquerade, never revealing ourselves to mortals, and never rest until Luciande is the greatest nation on Halann and Lucian its undisputed master. This I swear in the name of the eternal night, and with the ritual of vampirism as my sacrament. May the pain be short and Lucian’s glory forever.
