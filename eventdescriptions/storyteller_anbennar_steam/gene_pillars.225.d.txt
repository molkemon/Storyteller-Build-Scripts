With the maps kept by the expedition, they could tell that they had nearly gotten through the entire inner layer. They also knew they should be arriving at the next major subsection soon, and sure enough, a door much like the others they had seen soon came into view. When they opened the door, they found mechanim, broken mechanim. They were torn apart and scattered over the floor. They seemed to be older mechanim; perhaps this is where they were made, but it was hard to tell now. The host interrupted them. "Yes, as you can see, many mechanim were destroyed here. If you analyze further, you will see that it was not the chainers who did it, however. After the anomalous magic event, many older mechanim were constructed; some decided to stay. This chamber in particular is home to a prototype charging port where mechanim could charge their kauga directly from the energy in the facility. There was competition." As the expedition heard this, they became aware of eyes looking at them from the distance high up in the room. "They are observing you; they are afraid of you and waiting for you to make a mistake. I am certain you will; you cannot resist making mistakes. If you could, you would not be here."
