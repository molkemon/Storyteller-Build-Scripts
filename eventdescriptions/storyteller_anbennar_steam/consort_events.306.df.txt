The first time a consort watched their monarch sparring with soldiers in training, they were impressed. It’s not every noble who would enjoy the manual labor of actually learning how to wield a weapon of their own. But their lord is not any noble, they thought before approaching their spouse to compliment their skills. They even mustered the courage to ask their opinion on the current military situation of the realm. Intrigued by this mutual interest in the military aspects of the kingdom, the lord has gained a lot of new ideas for their army.
