As the party explores, one hallway leads them outside the pillar to a wide balcony. The party has climbed to a dizzying height, some members stumbling backwards in fear. As they collect themselves, they hear a distant cry. As the party searches for the source, the balcony darkens. Looking up at the sky, something blocks the sun's light. A giant winged beast soars directly towards them, landing on the balcony. The beast resembles the same winged creature in the visions of the Kanolowele warrior. Its beak snaps at the party as it stares directly towards them, its eyes calmly examining the explorers.
