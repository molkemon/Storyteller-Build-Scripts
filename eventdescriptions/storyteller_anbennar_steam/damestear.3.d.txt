A strange glow dimly shone even through the solid rock, casting curious faces in its cyan hue. It had been a nice enough spot for a luncheon, and loose lips soon saw that word of the woman’s tears – a telltale sign – brought the eyes of our nation upon those weathered stones. While many of the people grow eager at the prospects of such a bountiful deposit of the substance, as it should bring wealth to their land, whispers of dissent run on the breeze, fearful of the change coming to their home. Soon, the fist of our nation will clench around this place, and see that the substance finds its way into trustworthy, deserving hands.
