While common knowledge regarding Dames tear is that only high quality crystals can be processed into high quality potions, modern advances have brought us new ways to use crystals of any grade to create potions of remarkable quality.  One way to do so is by utilizing better mechanical processes to break down the Dames tear, thus making it easier to purify the resulting product, as well as improving its solubility. Thankfully, our nation has been advancing it's gunpowder industry considerably in the last decades, and the technology we've developed for gunpowder mills could be easily adapted for the crushing of the magical ore.  Another possibility relies on our other area of expertise: alchemy. By utilizing certain chemicals extracted from the Green River we're able to selectively dissolve the Dames tear while leaving many of its impurities behind, therefore making our potions much more efficient and with less side effects.
