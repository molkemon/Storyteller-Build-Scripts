It has felt like an eternity. An eternity where the whole city has been enraptured by the games, foreign sailors managed to spectate the last days and even the nobility descended to the beach. In this ecstatic maelstrom, sleep did not matter to pirates and merchants as long as wine and music kept going. Many bled, many fell and many just misstepped, as well as many were victorious. But even said victors were not able to keep further as the last two combatants.
