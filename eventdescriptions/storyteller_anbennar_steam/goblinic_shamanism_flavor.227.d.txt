The dwarven language is a harsh one, and their shamans say that there are often many ways to interpret it. This results in great frustration among the shamans most keen on delving into their ancestral records, and it is not an uncommon occurrence that some tablets end up as boiling stones rather than records. This means in turn that space has freed up where they can put their own take on the history of the Serpentspine. It is not as if the dwarves are still here to prove them wrong, after all.
