The Nothic continued to stalk our expedition for some time, and it still haunts the sleepless nights of some of our men. The first night when the eye was not seen at all merited a tenuous, straining watch, filled with gruesome thoughts of where the creature might be hiding now. As the days pass, however, we concluded that it was not in hiding, but simply left us behind; whether it has learned our secrets or just lost interest, no one can say.
