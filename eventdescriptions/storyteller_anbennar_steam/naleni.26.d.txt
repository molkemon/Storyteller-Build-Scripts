A thick scroll bearing the mark of the Sirens awaits attention. All Siren commanders are obliged to provide a report to the matriarchy concerning the ongoing events in their posts, but this report has made its way here due to its description of a rather interesting phenomenon. The popularity of the Sirens with both the Mengi humans and some of our own has been observed already, but amongst some it has risen to almost fanatical levels. So-called fan groups have been forming, and the names of particular Sirens of note are circulating throughout our realm. Whilst novel on its own, the truly fascinating facet of this phenomenon is the behavior elicited from the Odanchésta. Used to being notorious, many Odanchésta seem to have taken their declining importance in our cultural consciousness poorly, and as such have begun to perform ever more reckless and daring actions in competition with the Sirens.
