Upon waking today, the monarch was greeted by a small slip of paper left under the door to the ruler's room from the advisor containing an apology for his departure, explaining that the road calls to him again. On the other side of the paper, the kobold inscribed a small prayer, that the monarch might draw closer to the highest good as they rule their nation. The prayer appears to be highly personalized, and in reading it the monarch was struck with the sense that the advisor knew things about the monarch that the ruler did not yet know about themselves.
