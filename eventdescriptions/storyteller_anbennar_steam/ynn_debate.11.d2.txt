As the monarch pronounces a fateful question, a divide as great as the Ynn emerges between the Companions. "Yes, of course," one voice speaks, "the Ynn River created all of us equal so we could determine who is superior on the field of battle." "Absurd," an elder companion immediately interjects, standing up. "I will not let my dynasty of warriors be compared to mongrels. Even the weakest of the Ynnic lordlings is a span above those fleeting Epednar riders or magicdrunk Veykodans." As animosity rises, not just against foreign cultures but from one warrior to another, the guards steady their weapons in case they need to intervene, but as they do, the crowd suddenly turns silent. The time for the host to speak has arrived. The monarch raised the question, and as all turn to the ruler, they must choose a stance. "I believe..."
