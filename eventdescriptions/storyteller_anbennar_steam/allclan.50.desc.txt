With conclusive evidence of our product working on Goblins, we can say that Project Brainfreeze is well underway to success. We are able to reliably use drugs to manipulate Goblins into complacency, with little to no side effects, and without them realizing it. However, most of our research so far has been focused on Goblins only, which means we have no idea how this product will work on other species. To remedy this, we must start testing on other races immediately, but sourcing test subjects from outside our nation may be tricky.
