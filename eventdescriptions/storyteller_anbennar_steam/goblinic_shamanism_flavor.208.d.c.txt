We tried our hardest, yet sometimes that is not enough. The sacrifices we offered appear to have been rejected by Kroozbuto Nubo. While the rains in the province and the surrounding area have been disappointing during the last few years, at least there was some rain. Now there is nothing. The sun glares down on us from a pale, clear sky, scorching the earth and everything brave enough to grow. The people of the province have some lean years ahead.
