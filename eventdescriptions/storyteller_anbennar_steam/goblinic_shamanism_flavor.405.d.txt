The elves and humans of Bulwar have several different religions, but all those religions are kind of the same religion. They all worship light and the sun, whether they call it Surakel or Surael. Then again, the sun is undeniably very powerful, she is one of our own gods as well after all. The humans and elves don’t understand us when we talk about Tewanka Sunka, however, so some of our shamans have taken it upon themselves to set up a separate, more outwardfacing cult for Aunt Sun. To properly integrate these humans and elves and whoever into her worship, they have incorporated many of the themes and ideas from the existing Bulwari religions.
