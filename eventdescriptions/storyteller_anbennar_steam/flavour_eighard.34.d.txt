The messenger burst into the monarch's chambers. "Your Majesty. There are... there have been..." The messenger was panting. "Slow down," the monarch said. "Catch your breath. What has happened?" "In Arakeprun and in Murdkathermass, there are disappearances. The cities are in chaos." "What has caused this?" "No one knows for sure. Some say it was an attack from a foreign power, and we have been infiltrated. Some say it was done by traitors, those hoping to undermine the Amber Princess. And then there are those who say it was the Amber Princess herself, and that this was the end goal of her ritual all along. Is it possible? Has she betrayed us?" "Watch your tongue, young one," the monarch said. "If this is indeed the result of the Amber Princess's ritual, then surely she had good cause. The ends justify the means, after all. We shall simply have to wait and see." The monarch realized they were sweating. Their layers of clothing, meant to counteract the cold, suddenly seemed like far too much.
