Time. We measure it, build our lives around it. We work to make the most of every moment, for we know that as time moves forward, so must we. And at all times, we feel the pressure to match our neighbors and seek new heights. But time is a cultural concept, not a fey one. It is something they blissfully let fly by. Yet our recent and constant friction with our local fey combined with increasing daytoday integration between our ruler and fey life has made this an impossibility. And now, in their frustration has come to a boil. They do not care about the goings ons of the world or how it changes. Instead, they just wish to live their lives day to day.
