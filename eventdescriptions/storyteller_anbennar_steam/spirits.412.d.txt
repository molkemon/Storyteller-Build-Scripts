With the High Temple finally standing tall, restored to its previous radiating glory, like aeons before, it started to attract the masses across Haless, Halessi, and Harimari, even dwarves and kobolds flocking towards the Temple Complex in search of inspiration and faith. Though such a large influx of visitors quickly brought the attention of the merchant class and traders too, as culture and religion were being exchanged and studied, along with plenty of exotic trade goods as well, a mayor of the realm came forth asking the ruler to invest some funds into the local province so they may set up a Trade Center there.
