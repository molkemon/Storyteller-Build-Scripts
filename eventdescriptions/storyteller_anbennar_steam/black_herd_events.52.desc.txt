Before me are a pair of vast iron doors, movable only by the strength of a dozen ogres and barred with a massive slab of stone. Opening such a barrier would take a team of ordinary centaurs hours of backbreaking work. A single spell shatters the stone and caves the doors inward, however, revealing the sanctum within for the first time in centuries. The room is stark and unadorned save for a single dais in the center, upon which rests a small black bottle that has been chained to the floor. I gallop forward eagerly and begin my examination. The bottle is clearly carved from some sort of bone. It must come from a magical source to contain the power I feel within; giantsbone perhaps, though where the ruler would have acquired such a treasure is a mystery. Carefully unchaining the bottle, I cast a few spells to help me analyze the contents, and for the first time since I became the leader, I truly laugh. Jubilation cuts through the unending stress of rebuilding the Black Herd and a smile finds its way to my face. I suppose I should be grateful to the advisor. The ruler was closer to his ascension than any seems to have realized. The potion is complete, a perfect specimen, in fact. The other must have fallen while the final stages were being prepared. His loss will be my gain, as with this I can begin planning my own ritual in earnest.
