The miners of a distant province have made a disturbing discovery during an expansion of operations. As they tunneled deeper into the earth, they came across an ancient cavern filled with dozens of dwarven corpses distorted in twisted positions, as if they were caught in the middle of a fierce battle. The corpses were covered in layers of thick, ancient ice, preserving them in an eerie stillness, with their weapons still clutched tightly in their hands. Their skin was pale with a blue tint, preserved by the icy cavern. Our scholars are puzzled by this gruesome discovery, but some say that the ancient garments suggest that the corpses must be hundreds, if not thousands of years old. This presents us with a difficult dilemma. On one hand, we could simply ignore the problem and continue our operations in other areas. On the other hand, the prospectors have informed us that the most promising veins of precious metals are located in this direction.
