Our diplomats in Sramaya have brought a message to us that the leadership of Bhuvauri is willing to hold a meeting between our two countries. The conference is to be held in Sramaya in a few weeks from now. This is perhaps the only chance for us to do something previous rulers could never achieve and bring the Kharunyana delta back to friendly hands, even if it means we have to befriend Bhuvauri ourselves. Bhuvauri began as an organized slave rebellion along the Kharunyana delta. After defeating the Raj armies in battle, the freed slaves proclaimed the founding of the Free Land or Bhuvauri. Many Rajas have attempted to retake the lands Bhuvauri holds, but those efforts have mostly ended in vain. Decades and centuries have passed since the rebellion, and both Bhuvauri and the country have experienced numerous changes. Maybe it is time to stop letting the past define our future; maybe the Raj should start building new relations with Bhuvauri. The choice is in our hands.
