"Escann Stands." Those words are on the lips of town criers and merchants alike, to the exclusion of all else. Even from the window of the meeting room in the capital, many common folk and refugees can be seen gathering around one such crier and listening to their tales, enraptured. Inside the meeting room a different scene plays out, but the matter at hand is the same. An emissary from the government of another realm stands opposite the monarch. His charge is simple; he carries with him the following message. "Escann Stands, the Greentide is at an end and all those that wish to go to the government of that realm are welcome to do so."
