Escann Stands” Those words are on the lips of town criers and merchants alike, to the exclusion of all else. Even from the window of the meeting room in our capital, many common folk and refugees can be seen gathering around one such crier and listening to their tales, enraptured. Inside the meeting room a different scene plays out, but the matter at hand is the same. An emissary from their government of their realm stands opposite of our lord. His charge is simple; he carries with him the following message: "Escann Stands, the Greentide is at an end and all those that wish to go to their government of their realm are welcome to do so."
