The local leading the patrol came to a sudden halt amidst a circle of carnage, disturbing the warleader. She swiftly approached their guide. "I thought you said you were leading us where highwaymen had set camp." The young lizard girl gave the captain a dumbfounded look, and slowly nodded her head. "Right. And I said some Claw chaps were gonna fight them. See what's left?" The warleader swiveled her head. She had dealt with her fair share of battlefields, and the sight before her stretched well beyond the necessities of battle into pure sadism. Whatever remained of the foreigners could not be distinguished from the general red blotch covering the space.
