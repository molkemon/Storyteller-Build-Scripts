Our feeler network of poaching stations reports that one of the fronts of our ongoing war with the native Veykodans has gone quiet. We assign large guard forces just to have hunts go off without a hitch. While this is of course a relief for the poachers in the region, we know better than to believe the Painted People have ceased their hostilities against us. Even now, other poaching stations report shadows in the trees. Additionally, we have noticed the telltale erratic movements of herds being manipulated by the natives, baiting traps for us.
