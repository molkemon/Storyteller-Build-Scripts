With all design decisions made and a handful of rianelion lyres crafted according to the latest specifications laying before the Vetaunyastu Jorouna, all that remains is to test out their efficacy. A Sanarenni flock of senior Vetaunyar is already eagerly awaiting their opportunity to play on these most precious instruments while weaving their song magic in front of an eager audience. And soon enough, they would do just that. The clear and dulcet tones of the lyres resonate with the polyphonic harmonies of the Sanarenni singers, weaving a sublime tapestry of sound. As their recital concludes, the verdict regarding this latest design for the rianelion lyres is clear.
