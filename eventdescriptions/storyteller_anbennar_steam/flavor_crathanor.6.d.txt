Once the Copper Dwarves were our allies, now they are an equal partner to our Republic, but perhaps they should be even more. With OvdalTûngr’s navies kept separate from our own and allowed to keep their specialties, no fleet could stand against us. We would be like two sisters, sailing to the horizon together. But perhaps instead we could integrate them fully into our realm and have them as yet another flourish in the great work of art that is Crathánor.
