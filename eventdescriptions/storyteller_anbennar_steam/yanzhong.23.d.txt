The Great Patrol's primary task is, and will always be, the upkeep of the roads of our lands. They are men dedicated to keeping the path maintained and free of bandits, so that even in the most chaotic of times merchants will know our lands to be a safe harbor.  Recently, a period of local calm combined with particularly dutiful men of the patrol has seen them go above and beyond in their duties; what started as a simple repair project on the fringes of the restored section of the Highway has turned into a restoration effort for the area beyond. Word was received today that an entire stretch of the Highway has been restored entirely.
