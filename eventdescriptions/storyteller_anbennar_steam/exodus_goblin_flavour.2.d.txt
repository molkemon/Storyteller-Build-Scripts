Speeches have been rehearsed, and the venue is set. The clanbosses and chieftains of the land have been sent their invitations, and it would be a breach of common courtesy if they were not to attend, not to mention a valid justification for war if need be. All that was left to do for the time being was to wait, and after several hours of waiting and chatting about war policies with the siegemastergeneral of Mountainshuggeras, as well as stopping the high corsair of Landshark from taking everything on the table, the clanboss of Greysheep and his posse finally arrived, decked out in the sleekest most fashionable attire among nobles at the time, at his hip a waifish harpy without so much as a smidge of brain beneath her eyes. With all the rulers gathered, the party finally began. It was touted as a celebration of the Exodus, and the stability of a new society they have formed in this harsh and unfamiliar land. They all drank and partied their hearts out, and by the end of the night they were lying on the floor, the furniture, and everything in between. Empty kegs and trays of food were strayed all around as their contents were sitting comfortably in the bellies of the many guests.
