For centuries, alchemy and potionmaking have been present in Varainé, in larger or smaller scales, due mostly to the presence of the Green River. However, as the sixteenth century came about, Varainé improved upon these crafts, turning them from mere curiosities into proper tools for the nation and its people, and now, alchemy is almost ever present in Varainé life. Potions of various kinds are used extensively by commoners and nobles; chemical compounds boost all aspects of the economy, and commerce of ingredients and finished products have turned the once bucolic town of Varainé into a bustling city. While this meteoric growth of alchemy, and by consequence Varainé, cannot be assigned to a single factor, it is undeniable that the Varainé government's support of the alchemists was instrumental for the nation to reach the heights it did. And now, as demand for potions soars and opportunities to develop new potions start revealing themselves, the need for an institution to foster the research of alchemy and properly train the future generations of alchemists becomes clear. To this end, the Varainé government built the Academy of Alchemy, Potioncraft, and Herbalism. the foremost research and training institution of the alchemical sciences in all of Halann. This institution, as well as representing the overwhelming importance of these sciences in Varainé society, also opened the doors to even more advanced and large scale usage of potions, as following its construction the alchemists started organizing themselves as a political entity, going from disparate producers into a largely organized political group, thus opening the doors to many wide scale projects which were previously impossible.
