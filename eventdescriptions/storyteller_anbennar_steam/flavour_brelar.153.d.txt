One interest of the Ravelian Society is the science of artificery, specifically how this art may be used to improve the lives of the average elf and krahrnorian. Thus the Brelari Ravelians have proposed for the state to construct a manufactory in their domain. Using these vaunted artificer techniques, they claim it will be a more efficient project, that will prove a direct and noticeable improvement to the lives of the locals.
