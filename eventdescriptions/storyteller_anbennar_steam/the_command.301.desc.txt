It is a sunny day when the Grand Marshal is visited by a philosopher from the Tiger Command. He bows in respect. "You asked to see me, Grand Marshal?" "Yes I did. Sit." After the philosopher is seated, the monarch continues, "I have been contemplating our teachings and I have decided that I need to adjust my views. However I would like to hear an expert's opinion on each teaching. Please, share your knowledge." "Of course Grand Marshal. Where to begin." The philosopher spends a good while explaining all the teachings and their philosophical arguments, splitting apart their merits and faults and giving the Grand Marshal a good understanding of the values of each. Finally he settles back and asks, "I hope this has given you a good overview of our teachings. Have you yet formed an opinion?" "Yes, thank you. I have."
