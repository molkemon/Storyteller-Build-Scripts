For decades, Unguldavor has been the benefactor of Barumand. The only reason Barumand survived was due to Unguldavor’s willingness to defend it and help their ork brothers and sisters. In everything but name, Barumand was nothing more than Unguldavor’s vassal. Today, the ruler brought Barumand’s leader to the capital to iron out the details of the arrangement. “Leader, it has been a pleasure having you by my side,” the ruler said, “but it is clear that the arrangement was one-sided. Together, we could give orks a new homeland here in Escann. But in order to do that, I need your loyalty. We need to work together. Unguldavor, Barumand, and Khozrugan. Will you accept our protection?” The leader nodded and shook the ruler’s hand. “I accept. For our people, we must unite.”
