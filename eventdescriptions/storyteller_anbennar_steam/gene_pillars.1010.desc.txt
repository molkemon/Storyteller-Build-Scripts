Despite the undeniable magical might of those who built this impossibly large pillar, unmatched by any who came after them, they were in the end still very much mortal and obedient to their biological functions; or at least their slaves were. As such, it is no surprise to come across canteens and other places where food was regularly conjured, served, and consumed. However, it is far rarer to come across one such place that is still in working condition, giving us a rare chance to restock our supplies. Having long grown tired of salted meat and hardtack, we could even risk ordering a more extravagant meal, at the risk of the device malfunctioning or it producing something with adverse effects on our health.
