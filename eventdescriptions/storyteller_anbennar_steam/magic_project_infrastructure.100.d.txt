While seeking to improve their main source of magical power, the court mages discovered that the shoddy construction of the previous builders has led to a series of tiny cracks spreading throughout the foundation of the mage tower. Due to the increase in magical output that they plan for the structure, leaving these cracks unattended could cause some damage to the tower in the long term. They could repair the cracks, but doing so would take time and cause the refurbishment to be delayed.
