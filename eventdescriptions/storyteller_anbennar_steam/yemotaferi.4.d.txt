Just before dawn, a delegation of wardens had arrived in the village. In a rousing speech, the rainpriest had told the villagers that their service was required. Every devout servant of Amilak was to pack up every scrap of food, tool, and weapon they could carry, and join the great exodus into the Shadow Swamp. There, the entire nation of Yemotaferi would find a new home, bringing the light and love of Amilak into the damp and dark. At first, the villagers were perturbed, scared to join in this endeavor, which seemed tantamount to suffering and death. But when a few brave youngsters loudly pledged themselves to the cause, a sense of duty and zeal spread through the assembled people. Before long, the whole village was frantically gathering supplies, preparing for the journey.
