There is one god. The realm knows this, has known this for millennia, and so too does its ruler. But what is that god's nature? Perhaps we have not quite understood it; until now, with the Ravelians having come to our shores. The Ravelian Societies that have sprung up across the realm are proving popular, and count among their members many high-ranking officials from the court in the capital. As a consequence, many throughout the land are seeing Surael as a blazing, fiery, resplendent gift from the One God, whose last fragment should be the center of our worship. And even the monarch has found some of these speeches convincing. It seems a new path for the realm lays ahead, one of insight and discovery, of listening to the Fragment's teachings and following its guidance. Should we make such a decision official?
