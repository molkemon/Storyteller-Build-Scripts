One of their trolls from the expedition has returned without the others. She reports that their mage guide died without any apparent cause. Without anyone who could hear the signal, the expedition quickly became lost, and lots were drawn to send four trolls out in opposite directions looking for aid. That was two weeks ago, she says. While their natural regenerative abilities allow them to withstand environments no other race can, they still need food to survive. If they do not find their lost expedition soon, then they will not be able to save any of them.
