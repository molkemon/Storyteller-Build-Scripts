Vandiphan gunships fire upon Yetmesira, as troops march into the city. Both harimari and Mengi pillage the City of Chains, capturing soldiers and even women and children. When the troops reach Yetmesira's market, the order is given to spare it; after all, it will need to be used to sell the soldiers captured today. All is to be put into its proper place, and the proper place of those who defy Vandipha is in chains.
