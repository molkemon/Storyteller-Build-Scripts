Governing solely out of Shalazar, Karassk, or Zerat may have worked for the various empires long gone, but Taneyas has grown in population, power, and wealth immensely under our guidance. Why would a merchant of Karassk listen to a noble of Zerat or a mage of Shalazar, or vice versa? Each views themselves as the centerpiece, the glorious heart of our great empire; each eyes the others with disregard at best and suspicion at worst. We could centralize power into a singular city, forcing all parties to bow to our preferred party. But why should we? Zerat’s ancient Zatsarya now symbolizes our resilience and strength, Karassk’s wealth makes possible the armies who keep our subjects in line, and Shalazar’s grand archive and schools of naga wisdom prove and legitimize our ancient heritage. All are essential to Anzara, and abandoning any would cripple us. So instead we shall grant all three cities equal positions, the title of "Capital" a mere honorific that is necessary for where we govern from.
