Drums beating, archaic dwarven language, sturdy armour, blacktipped spears. These are the signs of the Obsidian Dwarves, outcasts from AulDwarov, heretics who have been exiled into the depths of the Serpentspine for their dangerous rune magic. Ever since they resurfaced, some of them can be observed here and there in the Serpentspine, but not in such a large quantity. Before us are at least six legions of dwarves stampeding through the old caverns. This is not just another warband; this is an army.
