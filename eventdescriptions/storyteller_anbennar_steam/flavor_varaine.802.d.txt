With the perfecting of our Fire Breathing potions, word has spread around Halann of our achievement, and none are more excited by this invention than kobolds. At first scattered groups, but gradually in larger and larger numbers, kobolds started coming to Varaine to experience for themselves the power granted by these concoctions. However, in their excitement and amazement, the kobolds ended up causing quite a lot of undue collateral damage from randomly spewing fire where they shouldn't, leading to many complaints from the population at large.  To solve this, some members of our government suggested we restrict the sale of Fire Breathing Potions to kobolds, though that would be a form of racial discrimination we're not too fond of. Alternatively we could increase the price of the potions in general, both restricting the access in a less discriminatory manner and giving the government a nice increase in revenue. Finally, we could do nothing (or maybe even incentivize them), and simply learn a thing or two form the kobolds' joy; even if it is slightly destructive.
