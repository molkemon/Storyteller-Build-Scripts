Our exploration of the Gene Pillar in the province has discovered vast reserves of Dames tear, stored by the Precursors long ago or veined deep in the ground; perhaps a reason the Pillar was built here in the first place. Long beyond our expedition, the province promises to be a major centre for the trade of this magically invaluable good. We could regulate such trade carefully, or simply declare open season for any merchant willing to take the risk.
