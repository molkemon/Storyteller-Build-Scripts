When AulDwarov, the ancient Dwarven empire in the Serpentspine, fell to the orks and goblins, it was the weakness of our military and decades of stagnation that led to our downfall. It is no wonder then that the noble Guard advocates to bolster our ranks with capable dwarves and weapons, so that our military might can defeat all dangers that wait in hiding beyond our borders. This requires a substantial commitment, so they now approach our ruler to ask what we should do.
