"We want access to her tower," said the shadowy figure, who had once again appeared without warning. "By the... can you at least knock?" the ruler asked. The figure did not respond. "You seem more agitated than usual. What for?" "We know of the discovery you made within the tower, of the deal that Immarel made with the Everfrost Prince," the figure said. "This worries us greatly, as I imagine it does for you." "Well, yes, it does, but what do you need access to Immarel’s tower for? Our soldiers are already searching the thing top to bottom." "Your soldiers are fools. They will throw away the most important things and keep only the useless trinkets. We are no fools. We expect that, if given unsupervised access, we could discover a great many magical secrets which we would be more than happy to share with you, of course. For a price..."
