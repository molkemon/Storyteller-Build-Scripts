The Hall of Endless Debate in the realm has long since attracted the greatest philosophers and statesmen of their time in order to refine their craft, share their ideas, and hone their thinking. After conquering the city, gnomish statesmen and philosophers have innovated and researched its rich culture. Now the Gommo wishes to enter the fray and establish a special research division in the city in order to further develop its potential. This will however require funding, that might be better spent elsewhere.
