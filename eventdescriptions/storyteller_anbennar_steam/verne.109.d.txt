Nobody can deny the glory of a wyvernrider in flight; one’s instinct is always to see them at the very front, bolstering the resolve of the armies. Yet the canny minds among the logistical corps see a more strategic use for the wyvernrider cadres, as suppliers and skirmishers at the flanks of the column. This is not a mere question of heart versus head, for the wyvern charge truly does bring about terror in the enemy, but as the logisticians point out, we take serious risk placing such valuable assets so close to opposing fire.
