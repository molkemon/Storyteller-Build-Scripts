While researching different creatures upon which to focus the research, two main lines of thought appeared: The more conservative and more widespread one called for focused research on animal genitals, since they may have properties that are conductive to fertility potions. Another line of thought, more obscure and defended vehemently by a single scholar, preached that gnoll claws were a miracle cure for all kinds of fertility issues, and refused to elaborate on how he acquired this knowledge.
