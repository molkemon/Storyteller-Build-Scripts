Home is always where faith is, a saying amongst Esmaryal’s faithful who seemingly never lose their path; it is said that ones pure of heart will always find their way through even the darkest of days and roughest of roads. The Esmaryan Clergy have always been fast to point out one’s hopelessness as the reason for their downfall, yet, could it be true?  It has not been uncommon for even the vilest of criminals to take shelter in the Mother’s Sanctuary. Is that their faith, nestled within their heart, giving them hope with each beat? Telling them there might yet be a chance? The way of our goddess, unlike her guidance, is hard to threat. But much like her teachings, easy to understand.
