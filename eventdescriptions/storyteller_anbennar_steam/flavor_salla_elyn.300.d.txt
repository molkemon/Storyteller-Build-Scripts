Once more the Ceanns and Ceannas of the various Salla meet together in Elynaíle to discuss the conflict against the orks and goblins. They settle down around a large oval table within the Conclave Hall and begin to debate amongst themselves, trying to come to a consensus for the wisest course of action. Yet their discourse comes to an impasse. As their discussions die down, their attention shifts to the ruler of Salla Elyn; whose opinion will undoubtedly act as the tiebreaker.
