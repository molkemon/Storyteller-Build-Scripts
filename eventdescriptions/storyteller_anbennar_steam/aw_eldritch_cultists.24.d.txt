The Eldritch Cult is an illusive foe, but to our warriors no amount of hiding can last for too long. After raiding and torturing various houses and businesses in the region, they were finally led to the main hideout of the cult and burned the building and its inhabitants to the ground; slaying those who looked to flee from the burning wreckage. For good measure, they also destroyed the nearby buildings just to make doubly sure than any eldritch remnants were no more.
