The fortress was beyond anything they had beheld. It sprawled across the cliffside overseeing the boundless waves below, walls standing a half dozen elves tall. Bal Ouord, the local humans called it. It is one of the many great fortresses erected by a long past empire known as Castanor, the Empire of Man. Aweinspiring from without, Bal Ouord proved to be equally impressive within. As the monarch stepped through candle lit hallways, a pit formed in their heart. Compared to this feat, what had their realm built? The people had spent centuries seeking out a life within the forests living shanties of wood and bone. For all their talk of change and adaptation, there was still so much more to learn it seemed. Worse still, the monarch could feel the stares of the locals. Now within the main chamber, the monarch finally saw the empty throne. The humans here at once had a kingdom. While the Wood Elves would be tolerated for now, one day soon, a choice would have to be made.
