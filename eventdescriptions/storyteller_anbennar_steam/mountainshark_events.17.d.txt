Forest trolls march towards the Vale. Though they live far in the north, and must pass through hostile ogre-filled lands to get to us, the promise of full bellies and freedom is not one they are willing to ignore. Every day, more forest trolls arrive, and when they do, we embrace them. We settle them in the Vale to displace the native ogres, or send them to Orlazamazdihr to work our foundries, or enlist them in our armies as soon as we can. While some are disgruntled that we choose where they settle, most had little concept of what our lands were like, and so do not greatly mind when we tell them where to go. We will teach them of Glozok, bring them into the Govzenszel, and become greater as a nation all the while. The tenacity and strength of the trolls shall become a signature Mountainshark asset. We will work with them, and they will work with us. Through Glozok's lessons, even trolls can be sharks.
