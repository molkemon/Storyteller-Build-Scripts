A neat and tidy small folded paper waits on the desk, its words written in the spymistresses' careful hand. Our conquest of lands in Rahen has set rumors circling the court. Disgruntled Odanchésta huddle in dark corners amongst seedy taprooms in ports across the coast. Whispers of rebellion flit from ear to ear, voices rasp that the matriarchy has betrayed the Odanchésta.
