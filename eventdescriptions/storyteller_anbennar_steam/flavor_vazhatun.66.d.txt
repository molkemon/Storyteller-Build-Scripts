I feel myself slipping even now, so I must write to tell you to stop. You must stop looking for answers, for you will find no answers in the sky. The sky guards its secrets with a terrible grip; the sky does not want us. I have seen the stars and I have seen the abyss between the stars, and I have seen there is only darkness in the abyss, a terrible darkness deeper and more true than can be found in any pit, or chasm, or the deepest depths beneath our feet. I feel nauseous; I am clutching at the floor, for like the hourglass, the world will turn and the floor will be the ceiling, and there will be no floor, and we will fall forever and ever and ever and ever, and there are sharks in the abyss.
