In our efforts to make ourselves presentable to our surrounding nations, we have run into a persistent roadblock; their diplomats seem to be uneasy around our ruler. Our rule over the Bulwari has never been effortless, of course, but we will never be seen as anything more than monsters if we cannot convince the dignitaries, traders, and rulers of the humans that they can feel at ease in our lands. Our experts on human culture report that some of this disconnect might be due to the rarity of matriarchal societies in the human world. When this is combined with the imposing figure the ruler projects, this could be the source of the tension on the part of the largely male diplomats. It might be easier for the diplomats to understand our governing structure if we were to create the impression for them that the men, especially the spouses of figures of note, have more power and influence within our country than is actually the case. A number of the ruler’s own harem have already volunteered to assist, and it likely would not be any hardship to them to march around as dignitaries for a while.
