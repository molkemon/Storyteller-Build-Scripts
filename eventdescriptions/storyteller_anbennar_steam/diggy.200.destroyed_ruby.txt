The Dwarovkron was an ancient crown which sat upon the brow of the High King of AulDwarov to represent the unification of all dwarfkind under one throne and one Assembly. It was lost during the last days of the Dwarovar, but the pieces have now been found. It took all of our expertise and ingenuity to reforge it, but reforged it we have. All six of the legitimate gems have been reunified and set within that great Kronium firmament, which now rests atop the lord’s brow. With such a storied relic in our hands, victory over our enemies is surely at hand. Like the Dwarven Empire of old, we shall unite the Serpentspine and forge a new power, one that will stand for a thousand years.
