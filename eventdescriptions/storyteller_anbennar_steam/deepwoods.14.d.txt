The sky shimmered in a myriad of color, bright yet devoid of the Sun. The trees seemed to curve and bend, as though dancing to an unheard tune. The air was alive, thick with a verdant vibrancy. This was the ruler found themselves in. How they found themselves in this otherworldly place was a mystery. All that they could recall was waking in the middle of the night, determined to find the source of the whispers that filled their minds. In that determination, a single step had turned into ten thousand. And at the end of the journey, long after the familiar forest had given way to this strange and otherworldly place, the ruler found themself before the Whisperer. No, they had a name. One that the wind itself barely dared to murmur, the Blackened Prince. The Prince was unlike any other creature the culture had seen before. They were regal, slender and poised. Beautiful did not quite capture their visage for they did not stir of the mind or the loins, but rather the heart. It instilled in the ruler a sense of awe and the chill of fear. Perhaps it was only natural. The Prince's very presence radiated power. Their gaze pressed upon the ruler, smothering them. “Ah, so one hast finally heard mine call Steady enough in spirit, mighty enough in body To venture before mineself. Curious.” A small smile slowly crept itself upon the Blackened Prince. All the while, the ruler felt a throb within their mind. “Mayhaps, Thou art the one right A wrong spanning millennia?” Scenes flashed by. Flames. Screams. Hatred. Vengeance. The ruler's nose began to bleed. Their balance began to fail. “Yes, I see it now Thou could be the one. Take mine hand Embrace me.”
