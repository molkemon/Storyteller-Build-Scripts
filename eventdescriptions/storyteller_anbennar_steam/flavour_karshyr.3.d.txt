Four lizardfolk, richly attired, step into the newly renovated council room of the crater city. The first to speak is the Grand Architect of Karshyr, the designer of the now glorious capital. “The project is finished. We can now all stand proud as a true city of the lizardfolk, no longer must we cower in shame as a remote island province. We are once more a meeting point for all lizardfolk cultures and creations. Our citadels reach for the skies and our ports control the seas. The next step is to develop the seas themselves.” The next to speak is the head of the Aquaculture Guild, the lizard behind the backbone of the trade economy. “We should prioritize the production of dyes above all else. They are our most valuable export, our key to economic security and prosperity, the beating heart fueling our continued independence. By devoting our efforts primarily towards our dyeworks, our production is sure to be catapulted to a whole new level and we may acquire the wealth to dominate the Gulf forevermore.” The third to speak is our Grand Admiral, the master of the seas and supreme guardian of the island. “To focus solely on the dyes is folly; they are profitable enough as is. What we lack isn’t wealth, it’s lizardfolk for our fleets and our armies, it’s the ability to match the kingdoms of the mainland in combat.
