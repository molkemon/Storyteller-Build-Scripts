Despite finding the cable that seems to be powering the device, simply cutting it will not suffice. There are a number of options, of course. The mages could attempt to simply siphon the magic from the cable before it reaches the Runeforge. Simply absorbing the magic, however, could lead to catastrophic results if it is more than they can handle. Otherwise, pouring the power into an effect could mitigate the risk, but it would take something of incredible power to do what they hope.
