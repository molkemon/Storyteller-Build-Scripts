It is well known that vampires hunt, going out on nightly incursions to fill themselves on the blood of prey. Less well known is the fact there is more to hunting than simple nourishment—a good part of the reason vampires hunt is for the thrill of the chase itself. The monarch feels the stirring of both desires in their loins tonight. With all the necessary preparations complete, the ruler departs the seat of their power, searching for the victim who will be their prey tonight.
