You give your answer, the riddle is about motherhood, and the genie shrugs. "Indeed," comes that tired voice, and before your eyes the door dissolves. Beyond its threshold lies a grand storeroom, different from those you have previously traversed only in its heavily stocked shelves. It is an enormous haul, and you cannot possibly take all of it with you. Besides, the wholesale plunder of a place like this would strain the fragile Vernman accord with the myriad priestly lodges of Akasik. That is why the priest is here; he won you approval for this little jaunt in the first place. He offers you the pick of the litter in thanks for such a grand reclamation of Akasik history.
