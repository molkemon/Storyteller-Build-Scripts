The Predyvens System was a system of land division established by Arverynn in the decades following the War of the Bacaran Succession. A Predyvis was a grant that temporarily transferred fiscal rights to an individual or institution: most commonly they were taxes or incomes from cultivated lands, seldom water and fishing rights, or customs collection. Rights over a piece of land could be distributed among different individuals, so a person could have the rights to all revenues from the local stream, while his neighbour had the lumber rights from the adjacent forest.  Grants were for a set period, usually lifetime, and revocable at will by the Emperor. Grants to institutions, such as the toll revenue from the Tromseloc Way granted to the Trom Throm Rance, were effectively in perpetuity for the institution's lifespan. Grants were not transferable or hereditary; a Predyvis gave the grantee possession, not ownership, which remained Imperial. In rare cases, Predyvis were granted to a house rather than an individual, in which case it effectively became hereditary.  Built upon the Zalagon Pananek, existing feudal laws, and experience handling Pomentere Estates since the formation of the Veykodan Guard, the system was an innovation, but still firmly feudal. It allowed Arverynn to frequently reorganize estates or rights when needed. Often times, a Predyvis apt to support a single Ynnic Knight would be divided to support several infantrymen instead. As most of the organization of the Predyvens system was handled by the Imperial Functionaries rather than the Emperor directly, it was also prone to corruption, the functionaries often granting Predyvis to family members, colleagues, or made up individuals to collect the revenue themselves.
