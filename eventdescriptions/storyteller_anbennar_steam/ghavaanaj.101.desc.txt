Visitors from far and wide flock to the city of Sharaajaghal, its bustling marketplaces and schools crowded with guests. As spectators pack in, an elephant marches in, with their lord carrying a flaming torch. The crowds roar as they lob the blazing icon of the spirit of competition into the great sandstone brazier. With trumpet fanfare and roaring crowds, let the Sharaajaghal Games begin.
