The time has come for the next session of the Endless Debate. Gathering into two cliques, the HuliJing and the Gumiho have begun their attempts to gain Huszien's favor for the coming session. Eventually, though, Huszien's favor will go to one group, whose leader will rule Lingyuk. This time, it was.
