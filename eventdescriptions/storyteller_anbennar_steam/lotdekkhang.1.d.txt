Ever since the founding of the Teplin culture, our people have organized events in the Teplinbasiet Arena. Be it feasts, plays, or fights. A fight like today is a rare one, however; through the decades it has become tradition that the heir to our kingdom should be selected during an arena match. Today is one such match, as two dozen candidates line up before the fight. It is time for the ruler to bestow their blessing, which will grant that candidate first choice from the armory. Upon which contestant will the ruler bestow their blessing?
