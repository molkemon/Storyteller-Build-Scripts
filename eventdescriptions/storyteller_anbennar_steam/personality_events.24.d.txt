Realizing one's own deficiencies and delegating to competent people that complement one's own abilities is one of the most important aspects of sound rulership. The monarch is a firm believer in listening to good advice and in improving themselves by listening to those more experienced. Over time, however, it may be possible to learn what comes natural to others. If we allow the monarch to devote themselves fully, a tutor would likely be able to improve their grasp of some of the more advanced parts of the running of the realm.
