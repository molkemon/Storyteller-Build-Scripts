When we established the Day of the Khet as an official holiday, we sought to allow our people to experience just once a year the great pleasure and celebration that is everyday life for the Khet. Great festivals are celebrated in every part of our realm, during which an immense quantity and variety of luxuries and diversions are available to everyone, from the poorest of the beggars to the divine Khetarch himself. This year the celebrations have been particularly magnificent, which has resulted in some additional benefits for the state.
