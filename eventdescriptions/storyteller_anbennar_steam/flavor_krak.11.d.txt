As cold as we thought the peaks of the Giant's Anvil were, we did not realize just how truly freezing it can get up north in the Wastes. We were not prepared to face such drastic temperatures even with our IceSmiths serving as auxiliaries. Not to mention stumbling nearblind through a blizzard only to be attacked by a roving herd of mammoths. We have found a few interesting objects and relics that warrant further study, but otherwise this has been more of an exercise in poor planning.
