Something unexpected arrived. Lorent administrators knew their plan since the start and let many Roilsardi go. As farmers depart, Lorentish landlords and loyalists come. Roilsard rebels will never be able to rise up, but at least, Toarnen is their new home. However, requesting an exceptional tax to "free our kin" does not make our population happy, so organizing a shorter migration wave would help our finances, and the population would be more favorable to our plans. But a bigger migration would make Toarnen much stronger in the long term and a real example of how we never forgot our roots.
