The extraction room lacked the proper spacing for a full formation, and thus a small contingent of men, led by the captain of the expedition, stood to face the Lava Golem alone. As the construct rumbled to life, the captain was the first to act, bringing down his greataxe on the golem's head. Several minutes of deathly combat later, several of their men lay dead on the floor. Yet the golem was not invincible. Each blow weakened it more and more. After enough damage was sustained, the golem collapsed to the ground.
