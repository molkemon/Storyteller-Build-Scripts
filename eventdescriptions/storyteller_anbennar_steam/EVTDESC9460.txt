The first time you brush it off, but when it happens a second and a third time, you do begin to wonder. Why are people laughing at you when they think you can't hear them? After you've asked around for more than a bit, you finally manage to get an answer out of a chamber maid. It appears that your heir has been running about town in a most unbecoming fashion, and that your family now seems to be the laughing stock of the domain.
