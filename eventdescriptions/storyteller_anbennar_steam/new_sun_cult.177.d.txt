The only real lead our investigators had after Samartal was the magical orb left in the ruins. They have been trying to track down the individual who sold the orb to the instigators in the first place, hoping to back track the others from that original purchase. Thankfully, as this orb is not a common magical item, it was not overly difficult to find the one who sold it. His small, back alley shop was full of barely legal, and upon further investigation, some very illegal merchandise, all of a dangerous magical nature. Our soldiers have arrested him, and he already sits in the dungeons awaiting our torturers' ministries and our investigators' questions. Let us hope he talks before he dies.
