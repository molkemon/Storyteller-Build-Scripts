The Greentide and general instability of the fourteenth and early fifteenth century Escann led to many Castonath patricians fleeing the city to regions where they held longstanding familial connections and friends, namely in Bulwar, Businor, and Kheterata. After the restoration of Castonath to civilized control, many of these patricians have moved back, but almost as many did not, electing to stay at their new locales. Recently, a prominent patrician family of a returning province have come to us with an issue. One of their relations has managed to anger the ruler of a foreign country greatly through one insult or another, and has fled that country to the gates of Castonath seeking asylum, carrying with them a long train of items and wealth. Should we let them in, or return them to the authority of that country?
