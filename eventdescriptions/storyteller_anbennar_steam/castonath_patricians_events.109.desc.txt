The unrest and general instability of the 14th and early 15th century in Escann led to many Castonath nobles fleeing the city to regions where they held longstanding familial connections and friends, namely in Bulwar, Businor, and Kheterata. After the restoration of Castonath to civilized control, many of these nobles have moved back, but almost as many did not, electing to stay in their new homes. Recently, a prominent noble family from a returning group have come to us with an issue. One of their relations has managed to anger a lord of another country greatly through an insult or another, and have fled to the gates of Castonath seeking asylum, carrying with them a long train of items and wealth. Should we let them in, or return them to the authority of that country?
