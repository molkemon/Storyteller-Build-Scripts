The provinces under the control of Aelthanas’s peasant hordes have become a hotbed of dissent. Swarms of wellwishers and opportunistic looters now flock to his banner, moving towards other districts at the behest of their lowclass agitators. With the sway that Aelthanas holds over the commoners, there is no doubt that more would join him as he spreads his influence. We should move quickly to smother his revolution in its cradle before it spreads even to the capital.
