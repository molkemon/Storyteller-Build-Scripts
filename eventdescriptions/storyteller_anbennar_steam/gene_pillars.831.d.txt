We think we have stumbled upon something of paramount importance. The chamber we currently stand within seems to be some sort of power station. Massive pistons seem to be moving up and down in tune with the movements of the sandworm. Based on what we have been able to find out, we think this device was supposed to help keep the pillar powered by absorbing and redistributing the force created by sandworms shaking the ground below the tower. Because of the peculiarities of its construction, however, we believe it could just as easily be used to extract power from the motion of the sandworm even given the current predicament of the Gene Pillar.
