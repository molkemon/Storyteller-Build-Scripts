Atop the Grand Skyport, the Silken Council convenes their yearly meeting. Reflective of the current questions plaguing the realm, this year's discussions center around the role of foreigners in the League of Feiten. One group, the reformists, look toward the Gommo and Cannorians and their institutions as something to emulate. Meanwhile, the Old Guard believes that they must not stray from the path that has brought them so much wealth and their balloons. It is up to the monarch to choose which group gains ascendency for now, but this will clearly be a point of contention for decades to come in this new Age of Silk.
