The exkeeper sat in the middle of a lavishly furnished soiree, where his powerful memory was now being turned to the task of remembering factoids about foreign dignitaries. As he idly munched on the finest new innovations in Seacrest Ogre cuisine, his mind turned to his rapid change in careers. He was working with people, sure, but he was working with people before. Weren’t these diplomats already assured a place in the afterlife? Everyone needs someone to remember them, the keeper thought, but now all he could do was remember what he had already read. He returned to listing the names of the deceased in the back of his mind, not paying much conscious attention to the latest developments in Kanolowele trade.
