Glorious news. A poet of great talent has emerged from our people. Fans, critics, imitators, and rivals abound. Performances draw ever growing crowds and debates about the meaning of their latest work rage across our lands, inspiring a growing number of epigones. This is certainly a great boon for the culture of our people. Of course, such a bevy of aspiring poets and recitals is starting to cut into time otherwise spent working. Surely this is a worthy sacrifice for such sublime art.
