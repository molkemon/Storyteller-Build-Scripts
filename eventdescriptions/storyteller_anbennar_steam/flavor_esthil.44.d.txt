Varina resurrected not in the vault her phylactery was in, or even in Castonath, but rather in the deepest levels of Esthil, the tower. Mages loyal to her managed to get her phylactery out of the vault and out of the city, with the help of some loyal patricians, before the Floodborn could destroy it and kill Varina for good. However, traitor Floodborn and Patricians now control all of Castonath.
