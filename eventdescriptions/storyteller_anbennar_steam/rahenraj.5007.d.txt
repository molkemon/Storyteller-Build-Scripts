Some say war is an art, and not a science. Such fools have obviously never studied the four-volume 'On the Proper Practice of War', the crowning achievement of one of Indrayanar’s least favourite ministers. Volume Two focuses on the fifty formations every soldier should be able to execute. It is also the source for much of the vizier’s latest mandate, which outlines a strict regimen of drills that every soldier in the realm is expected to follow. One of your generals snorts at the idea of letting the Ministries dictate the practice of war, but even he knows the wisdom of choosing your battles—when the vizier drums a beat, all the realm must follow.
