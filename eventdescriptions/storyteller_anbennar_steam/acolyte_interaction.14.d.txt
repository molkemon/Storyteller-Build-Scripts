Prime Discordance, call forth the chorus of chaos to bear witness to your servant, the Ascending Dragon, master of the Twin Forces, wielder of the Thousand Secrets. There is no school of magic that the realm has not mastered. A true Acolyte of the esoteric and arcane, their reputation precedes them in any hall dedicated to the pursuit of mystical truths. It is possible they are the single most powerful being on the planet. The Acolyte's glory knows no bounds in the domain, and it is difficult for even other Acolytes to seriously challenge their claim to absolute supremacy.
