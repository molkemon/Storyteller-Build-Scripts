When I visited the forge, I was told a story about a foreperson that had grown concerned about the Magmascryers it had employed and how they spent so much time looking into the forge and the magma that fuelled it. It worried that they might jump in if no one was there to stop them. The heat and the fumes seemed to do concerningly little to dissuade them, and so it wondered aloud if the government should listen to it and refocus their efforts somewhere more productive.
