An important facet of Sage King JeongHun Gyo’s rule during the unification of Danggun at the end of the Daehonse was his support for the spread of the Neokttareum faith. Through diplomacy and grace, he proved to the people that the spirits and the Compact’s law were worthy of reverence. But to the people, Haeng Gyo does not have the charisma of his father, and their devotion has begun to falter. This has angered the Neoksamaji, for whom the veneration of the spirits and adherence to the laws of the Compact are of utmost importance. They desire to be granted the right to directly oversee all conversion efforts, despite the increased costs that their fervent endeavors will entail.
