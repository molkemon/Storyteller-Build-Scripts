To ascend through the factory, the party finds itself clambering up a complex web of bone and sinew, fallen from a higher level of the pillar and now draped across a number of machines. The bones are difficult to use as footholds, but the climb itself is relatively uneventful, until the visions begin anew. The expeditionaries see people moving the bones with magical force, cutting, splicing, knitting, merging, and tearing at the fabric of the creatures until they are just perfect for whatever unfamiliar purpose they were created. The elation these sculptors of flesh and bone feel as their task is nearing completion is unlike any other; they are creating life itself. No other feat could be so grand. The mages retire to a chamber recessed in the wall, behind the netting the party now climbs, but the vision falls away just as the bone netting begins to violently jerk in the wind.
