The monarch, accompanied by a slowly thinning crowd of loyal retainers and servants, solemnly proceeded out of their seat of power into the wild. Here they could get in touch with the noble beasts whose memories form the foundation of the exalted way of life that sustains the monarch. One by one, the warriors and other followers take their leave or are dismissed until eventually the ruler is on their own at a parting of ways. Now the decision cannot be delayed any further. where will the monarch go?
