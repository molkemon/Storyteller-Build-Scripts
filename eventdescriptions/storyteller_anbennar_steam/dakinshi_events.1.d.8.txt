The pacification of dissidents is going well, Vitaragomo. The offshoots of the defeated nobility tried sparking unrest in the buffer Tanizu tribes, but we managed to successfully locate and eliminate them. Furthermore, the tribes enjoy their new freedom of acting, preferring to stay under your mantle. From the recent counting, you can expect around one thousand Tanizu joining the ranks for our new campaign, including one hundred seventy shifters, though it will be your choice whether you are going to accept the more taboo ones coming with me. Furthermore, it seems like new confederations are forming on the territories we retreated from after we took over the reins of the state. Their threat is yet to be assumed, but knowing that damn witch that massacred us, they will soon start making their moves. As my job has been done here, I will be coming back to my station at Kuvunika. Hopefully by that time, more willing warriors will be found to secure the eastern flank once and for all. For the Lizard Warlord. Reporting from Kuraru, Munitagoromo Garuka Kagomuneba.
