With the marriage between our ruler and their spouse, the people of our nation are experiencing an increased fondness for all things from their homeland. While some cultural exchange was to be expected, particularly considering the popularity of their title, it is with alarm that our administrative advisor reports that a religious shift has been taking place in one of our provinces. It would seem that the influence of our spouse trickles down even into our subjects way of worship, as the words of believers from their faith have converted a surprising amount of people. When confronted about it, our spouse shrugs, arguing that they have long since converted to our faith and the ruler should know that. I had nothing to do with this, they plead.
