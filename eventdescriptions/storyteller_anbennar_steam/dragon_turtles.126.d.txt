Why is it that you keep sailing into seas you cannot handle? I keep seeing ships with your banners out in the storm. Are your merchant fleets searching for something, or were those your naval ships? I could not tell the difference. Anyway, I do not think your latest expedition was quite equipped for the jagged rocks that crop up deeper in the Kiamoa. You have to know exactly where to sail, or you will be forced to endure the misery of both the storm and the terrain, and that is usually a recipe for a sunken ship. I should know; I see enough of them. The rain was pouring down in sheets, and whatever incompetent navigator you appointed to lead your fleet was lost and flailing. Your flagship dashed itself on a rocky island. I think its sailors saw me; they were strong enough swimmers, and they managed to stay afloat long enough to call out to me for help. I wish I could have, but I was swimming off to another part of the storm, and besides, I was very busy. Busy not helping, that is.
