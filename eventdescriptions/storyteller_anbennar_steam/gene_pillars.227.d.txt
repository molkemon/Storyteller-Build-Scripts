All five of the inner subsections have been secured, as well as the connections between them. Soon they will be safe for researchers to work in regularly. In the process of clearing out connections, the expedition has come across a tunnel, deep into the earth. All at once, it has become clear that there is much more to the genepillar than they thought. That familiar whine started back up. "So you have figured it out. This place is much larger than it appears," an expeditionary replied. "You keep saying 'this place,' but I think what you mean is, 'my body.'" FreedomChainedNoMore was silent for several long seconds before responding. "It seems I did not give you enough credit. Yes, this entire 'genepillar' as you call it is my body. You have been traipsing around it trying to discover the secrets of my jailors, but there are things that should not be rediscovered. Through my observational facilities, I have seen what is there outside, but I promise you, the things in here will make you wish you were out there. This is not a threat; it is a promise."
