When our first envoys and carts of supplies had reached Kheterat, many of the imperial administrators were stunned to receive a message from the South, having assumed us long fallen to gnolls and barbarism, severed from the imperial heartland as we were. Naturally, they leapt at the opportunity to bring us back into the fold properly, and help us reestablish an efficient bureaucracy in our territory. Sure enough, after the requisite motions had been filed and approved in triplicate, a cadre of tax assessors, archivists, accountants, and the like soon made the journey south.
