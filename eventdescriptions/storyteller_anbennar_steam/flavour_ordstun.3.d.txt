The Diamond Dwarves send a delegation, requesting that we marry our oldest daughter to one Bombur Whiteforged. The Diamonds had not sent any delegation previous to this; truly, we did not even know they were still alive, and we are unsure what they expect from this proposal. The terms of this proposal include a small gift, a promise of mutual cooperation, and an agreement that we subject ourselves to their rule for time immemorial. We must forgive the Diamond Dwarves a tad, for it has been a long time since they have opened the gates of their hold. They must imagine us to be nothing more than romantics from old stories, who will accept any proposal as long as it is given among roses. But, truly, this is all they will offer?
