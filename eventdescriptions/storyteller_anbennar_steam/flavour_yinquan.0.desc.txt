With the previous Great Maiden of Liho deceased, the time has come to nominate a new maiden for the position. Some have already taken the tests necessary to prove themselves worthy of the position. One will be selected and venerated above the rest as a living ancestor, one who has the spiritual experience of many lifetimes, one who has achieved a level of enlightenment most mortal beings could only dream of. We will celebrate their ascension with a few days of festivities.
