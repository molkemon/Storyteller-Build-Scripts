Amusingly enough, the second councilman spot is the most hotly contested; only the greatest from each family runs for the position of head, and as a result many do not make the cut. The second seat is far less restrictive, and there are often a wide variety of candidates from each family, though ultimately it does eventually shrink down to a pool of twelve. The family that won this particular election was led by their administrator, diplomat, and military leader.
