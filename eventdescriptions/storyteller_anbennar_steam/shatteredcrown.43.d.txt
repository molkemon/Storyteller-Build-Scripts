With the state turning into a formal monarchy, now would be the time to formalize the name our dynasty will carry. Advisors have put forth the plan to remove any bastards from the lineage and adopt one of several names. Bordukhur, which means 'son of Bordu', the founding father of our clan; KaraKhogond, which means 'Black Kingdom'; or UvalKhodash, which means 'First King'.
