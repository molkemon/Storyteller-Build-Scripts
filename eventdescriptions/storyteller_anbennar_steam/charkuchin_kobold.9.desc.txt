It is migration season, and as the hinuibirds swept down upon us we put all of our usual cautionary measures in place, only to discover something curious. we did not need them. All of our settlements report that they have more than enough food and supplies, even with the hinuibirds swarming. We have built a system that can withstand even the strongest shocks, and as the word of our prosperity spreads, the people of this realm rejoice. Today is a glorious day.
