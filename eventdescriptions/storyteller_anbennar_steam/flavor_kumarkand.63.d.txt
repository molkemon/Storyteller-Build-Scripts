Whether it be ordering a new coin, palace, or mass execution of government staff, the Akal’s actions have recently grown erratic. Although the Akal has rescinded these orders after regaining clarity of mind, many question if these sudden bouts of mania are tied to the magical helmets after noticing how similar to Alusir the Akal has begun to act. Kar Alusir has repeatedly emphasised the safety of his helmets, but the Akals reduced governing capabilities point to the contrary.
