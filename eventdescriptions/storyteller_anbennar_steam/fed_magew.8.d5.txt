As peace dawns on the horizon, no welcoming beams of hope meet the gaze, but instead dark tendrils, spreading throughout the lands of the Federation; that which cannot be shackled has been restrained. Magic has never obeyed our rules, our laws. But through this Onyxguard, the people have been led astray, been led to believe that magic is now safe. Is the biggest crime that they have let the mages roam freely, or that they have lied to their people?
