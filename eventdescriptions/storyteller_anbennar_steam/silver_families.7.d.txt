The House of Silurion is one of the Silver Families, originating with the marriage of Urion Starsworn, leader of the cavalry complement of the League of Free Realms, and Alvara Ebonfrost, a princess of Castanor. Starting out as minor landowners in Escann, the family would eventually ascend to rule the kingdom of Castellyr until it fell to the Greentide. The House of Silurion is known for its chivalry, bravery, and honesty.
