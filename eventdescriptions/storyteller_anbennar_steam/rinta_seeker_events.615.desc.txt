The Ashamadi are the northern peoples amongst the Baashidi. In times long past, they were discriminated against heavily due to their connection with the Tanizu. In Dhebiji and Suhratbi society, they were often given more work for less pay. Stories of physical abuse were common as well. The sentiment of them being lesser was raw, and shown as clear as day, shadowing the Ashamadi under prejudice. Ever since the Dasr Sharaf, times have changed. Ashamadi peoples have found themselves welcomed into more and more courts among their Baashidi brethren. People have pushed back against the precedent that the Ashamadi are lesser. This doesn’t mean that everything is resolved though. Certain court mages find themselves being targeted solely for being Ashamadi. This has hindered the progress of the Grand Congress, and generated tension amongst all scholars participating. The highest of the saaxir have called upon the kings of the Baashidi to reprimand their courts if they are caught perpetrating Ashamadi discrimination. What should we do, my liege?
