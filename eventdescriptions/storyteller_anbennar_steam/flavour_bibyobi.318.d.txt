Well, all those firepits and animal hides we keep finding make sense now. The rumours of our kin still traipsing about this jungle are actually true. The Kaino call them "Ghost Halflings," but I like to call them long lost bastard cousins. These people have been hiding in these jungles since our ancestors left these lands for Fahvanosy, and we are just meeting with them now outside a great ruin. Well, meeting might not be the best choice of word, for they are standing at the mouth of the ancient structure with their spears leveled at us and muttering in their tongue. The only problem being, listening to their speech is like conversing with a child who is chewing on marbles. The words sound right, but the meanings of the sentences are all lost on us. Maybe our esteemed leader will be able to piece together their blabbing.
