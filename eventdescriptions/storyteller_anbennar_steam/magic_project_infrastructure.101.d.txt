Ley lines, a type of field magic that strengthens spells cast within their boundaries, provide good locations for the construction of mage towers. But like many of the aspects of the art, ley lines are fickle and wont to cause conflict and frustration at uncertain times. Currently, the site of a future mage tower has become affected by a shift in the ley lines, causing the brickwork of the structure to begin to emit a faint azure light. This surge in the power of the ley lines could prove useful to spellcasting within the region, as ley lines are used in a wide variety of different magical traditions. Alternatively, it might behoove them to direct this power towards more immediate concerns.
