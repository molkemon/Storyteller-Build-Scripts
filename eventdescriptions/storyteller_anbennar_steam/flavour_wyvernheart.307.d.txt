Today a surprising proposal has been brought forward in the Council. The Heartmages’ proposal, supported by the Technocrat Arcanists and Wealthy Scions of all people. Multiple councillors scoff and stifle their laughter while listening to the Heartmage representative presentation: They want direct investment from the Council in hunting down monsters around the realm. The goal of the Heartmages is clear and simple: they haven't outgrown their desire for adventure yet and wish to do "good" across the realm. But the Technocrats clearly have more nefarious plans to procure rare reagents through this endeavour. While the Noble Scions would use this to entrench their position by doing "favours" to those in need. The eager Heartmage representative looks at the Sovereign expectantly, convinced that his proposal is sensible.
