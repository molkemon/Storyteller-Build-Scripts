Rocs, the largest avians to grace the great plains. A magnificent creature, embodying the very ideals of freedom and motion we bear in our worship for Irdaeos. It has long been passed down in our religious traditions that the Rocs are symbols for Irdaeos’ blessing, and the sight of one flying overhead is an omen of good times. My Caehn, it has recently been found that one of these wonderful avians flew over our capital.
