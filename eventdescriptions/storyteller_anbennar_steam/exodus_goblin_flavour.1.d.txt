Humans living in the mountains of GarlasKel follow a different faith than all other Bahari we conquered so far. While we goblins generally do not care what our subjects worship, these Old Sun Cultists have begun proselytising within our lands to other humans. They claim that the decadence and weakness of elves has caused our Exodus and that all remaining Bahari humans must return to the socalled 'true' religion of the Bulwari. What should we do with those cultists?
