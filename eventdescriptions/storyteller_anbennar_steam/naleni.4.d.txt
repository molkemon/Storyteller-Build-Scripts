A message has arrived, on a scroll reeking of salt and cheap ale. It comes from a group of the Odanchésta. Whilst the Matriarchy has been infiltrating Boyebaretti, they have been doing their own roguery, and have a proposal. a deal with the local human pirates. In exchange for a complete right to continue their raiding, as well as use our ports, they offer to swear absolute fealty and assist in our takeover.
