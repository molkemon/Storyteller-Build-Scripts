Spiritfire is as much tied to the mind as it is to the body. While we move the flames with our arms, we summon them from our cognition, bringing them to light through force of will. The nature of the psychic connection is poorly understood, but what is known is that the Undying Flame and its embers drastically boost the strength of the connection, allowing for great blazes of spiritfire to be summoned after the caster is attuned to the fire. This boost of magical power has drawn the attention of the ruler, who stands in the central courtyard of Cangyuan and watches the brazier flicker in the night. If the attunement makes spiritfire more powerful, perhaps a more thorough attunement will make the ruler even more powerful than the masters of the Ashen Soul. The possibilities of what could be achieved with this power flash into view, with masterful diplomacy and decisive martial prowess forming within the ruler's mind. Standing before the glorious blaze, the ruler takes a deep breath.
