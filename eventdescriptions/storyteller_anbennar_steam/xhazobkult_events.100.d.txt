Urgent news, a pack of impatient gnolls in that province have gathered some menials in order to enact a sacrifice of their own. They are already performing the initial rituals to prepare a pyre, and some have even taken to gorging their thirst for blood on a few hapless sacrifices. The ruler must act quickly and decide on what to do with these unruly gnolls, as any sign of doubt and weakness could be a bad omen before a sacrifice.
