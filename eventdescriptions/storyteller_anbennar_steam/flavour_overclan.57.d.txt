A Voltoj Academy, with a Chief Vizier or Circuit Diwan present and enough development, can spend that development on an upgrade. Upgrading to a Medium academy requires one hundred thirty development, and each Academy Circuit can only support two Medium academies. Each Academy Circuit can also support one Large academy, which can be upgraded to from a Medium one when the province has one hundred forty development. Each Voltoj Academy is also well situated to administrate unique local resources; expanding an Academy's capabilities to allow for this requires one hundred fifty development, and each one will require uncovering some documents or learning some secret. Each Voltoj Academy upgrade will spend half of the development required to coordinate it, and it will spend the Base Tax first. Due to the intricate nature of hold bureaucracies, Academies in hold provinces will require one hundred twenty development beyond the normal in order to upgrade them, but only one hundred ten development will be spent by any upgrade. Please select an Academy Circuit to address.
