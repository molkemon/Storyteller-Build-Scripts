A proclamation has come down from a foreign country, recommending the thorough examination of Cannorian thought. While their ideas may not originate from the Ynn, the Cannorians are clearly not barbarians. The fact that these new ideas have spread through our lands, bringing innovation and prosperity, is proof enough that they are blessed by the Ynn. While this does not mean every single Cannorian innovation is good, and certainly does not mean they should supersede our own traditions, we should not fear these ideas just because they are unknown to us.
