To call the Firanyalen flock proud and set in their ways would be an enormous understatement. While this was useful when they were enticed to rebel against Ayarallen, it also means that their missionaries received a chilly welcome in that domain. Yet, they did not dare to expel said missionaries or even hinder their efforts beyond a few minor attempts at obstruction. At the time, such feeble opposition to the conversion efforts had been seen as a promising sign that they were almost ready to realize that the way forward was to embrace Surael. Now, though, the reports of revolts breaking out in that domain and another domain reveal that they were simply biding their time, secretly plotting with their former overlords in Ayarallen to overthrow them and restore the old ways of the Hunt.
