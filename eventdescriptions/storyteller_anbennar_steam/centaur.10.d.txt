Before our lord lies the land of Castanor. It was from here that we were expelled, from here that the vile Beastbane nearly annihilated centaurkind. Now, those who expelled us are long dead, and in their place are their descendants, living in their petty kingdoms, with their tiny lives. It is disgusting to think how many centaurs have died for them to squander this land, using it for their petty farms and cities, defiling the plains which are our birthright.
