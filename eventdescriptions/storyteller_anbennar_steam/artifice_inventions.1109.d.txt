The Runethought Network, first innovated by the great old hold of Verkal Ozovar, is a modern culmination of enchantment magics. We have set up finelycrafted runic towers throughout our nation, each engraved with runes of patriotic slogans, nationalist anthems, histories of our past rulers, and poems of our native tongue. These runes are each connected to the spiritual unconsciousness of the nation, and capture what it truly means to be part of our citizenry. By using harvested spiritmagic from Haless, we activate the towers; by keeping them active, the towers' runes engrave themselves into the minds of our subjects. Our shared culture becomes unavoidable, and, before long, each citizen of the nation cannot help but speak our language and know our stories. Without having to put in any effort, they are integrated seamlessly into our society.
