When Bramash ‘Chainbreaker’ first rose in revolt on the island of Endralliande, we had little sympathy for our incompetent colonial rivals. Our orks were kept well in hand, and we had little fear of the kind of insurrection which occurs when one neglects simple matters of security and authority. Now, however, their sickness has become ours. The so-called ‘Cabinet of Freedom’ which Bramash maintained during his first revolt has been revived now that he has fled to Soruin, to our colonial lands. Now we are dealing with the consequences of Endralliander incompetence. His maroon settlements foment constant unrest among our labouring ork population and the native Soruinics alike; we are told the maroons and the natives have even begun to intermarry. All the while, missives from his Cabinet invite interference from abroad. Their propaganda begins to speak of a thing called “Ozgarom”; the realm of the orkish free.
