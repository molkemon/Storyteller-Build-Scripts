It is taught that the High Gods were not so much the world's creators as they were its illuminators; against primordial darkness, they held up a torch and uncovered a path to enlightenment. The lessons that the people of Haless have gleaned from their High Temples suffuse not only High Philosophy but also the many teachings of Vimdatrong and Yanshen. There has generally been a broad coexistence of these faiths, with the philosophers often reframing their moral actions of the people into the concept of the Nitikamarja. However, some of our advisors believe that we should proselytize more aggressively in the state where a different faith is dominant, despite the unrest this would cause.
