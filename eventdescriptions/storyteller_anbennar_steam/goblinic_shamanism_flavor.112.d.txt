A searing fire has torn through a small hut on the outskirts of the capital. The only survivor is a small child, whose eyes have been seared red, and speaks only of Vortzo Liezmo, Lord Fire. As the god of fire, furnaces, and industry, he is the protector of smiths and the miners who supply them with their material. It is said that he managed to trick Kroozmuta Zemna into allowing those who plundered the Earth, her realm, into the good parts of the afterlife. This makes him highly regarded among cave goblin miners.
