While other races are squeamish about consuming the meat of humanoids, particularly their own kin, to we Kvangahga gnolls it is one of the most sacred of rites. It is a great honour to pass on the wisdom and strength of your flesh to your community. Sadly, some of our non-gnollish subjects do not see it the same way. A particularly prominent member of the local community, who had passed the Trial of the Reforged Chain, recently passed away and was given the full honour of a Kvangahgan funeral. This has caused outrage amongst his kin, who demand to be allowed to hold their own funerary rites.
