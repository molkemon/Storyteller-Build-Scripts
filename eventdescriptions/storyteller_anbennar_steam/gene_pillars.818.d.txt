We find ourselves standing in a massive room filled with large reinforced glass test tubes containing what appears to be young undeveloped sandworms. Much smaller than their fully grown counterparts, they would still be capable of swallowing a whole ogre alive. As we inspect the room, we feel a violent shake as the sandworm changes directions, causing one of the test tubes we were currently inspecting to shake loose due to our meddling and be thrown onto the floor. To our horror, the young sandworm suspended in the liquid starts to move.
