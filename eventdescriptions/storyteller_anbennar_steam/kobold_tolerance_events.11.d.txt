The treasury has discovered an unexpected problem with our resident goldscale kobold population. While their golden scales are naturally valuable, they have usually been fairly rare, as the kobolds themselves have been fairly rare. Our kobolds, however, are now doing so well that spare scales are flooding the market, and each one is becoming less valued as a result.  The treasury calls this \"inflation\" and warns that if unchecked, this could seriously damage our economy. However, if we aren't careful, handling this situation could cause our people to grow suspicious and resentful of the kobolds; who have done nothing wrong save for shedding scales, after all.
