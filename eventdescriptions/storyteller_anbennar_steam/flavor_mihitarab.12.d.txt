My child, what troubles you? The monarch and the heir stood on a balcony, viewing both the fishing boats visible only as shadows in the setting sun and the bug farmers returning from the jungle. I merely wonder how long all of this will last. One day, the Dog Lords may come out of the jungle and put an end to all of this. Turning to him, the monarch asked, "What, then, would you do?" "Conquer them." After noticing the shocked expression on the monarch's usually placid face, the heir continued. "The only reason Mihitarab prospers as it does is because of your skill in preventing wars. The only way to prevent them in the long term is for both sides to be united under one rule, and we have the opportunity to do just that. Why not take it?"
