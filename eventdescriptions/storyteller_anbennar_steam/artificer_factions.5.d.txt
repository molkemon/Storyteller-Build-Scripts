The Brillites are known for their inattention to anything beyond the new invention or gizmo, and the war our country is no different. As the Brillites have come to dominate the affairs of the country, many in our public offices have voiced their concerns with this war. "Who cares about the gain," they say. "All I care about is getting back to my research," they cry. While the pen may be mightier than the sword, it seems the country cares about spanners. And spanners don't care about anything but themselves.
