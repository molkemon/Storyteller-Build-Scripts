Finally, after many years of tension, minor border clashes, and major clashes of interest, it seems that the Blood Pact, supposed to seal the alliance between the Command and Siadanlén forever, has been irrecoverably broken. Missives have been sent, diplomats expelled, and hobgoblins and harpies assaulted, exiled, or lynched by angry mobs on both sides of the border. With war seemingly inevitable, time will tell who will come out on top in Haless, though whoever it is, everybody can be sure that the people of Haless will pay dearly for it.
