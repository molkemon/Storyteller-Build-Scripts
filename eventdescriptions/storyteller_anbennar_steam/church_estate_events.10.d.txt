The time has come for the established church to appoint a new leader to assist our ruler in the capital. The likely candidate is an old cleric with a solid history as a prominent member of the established church. He has, however, also acquired many enemies during his life who now hope to see a somewhat younger candidate with a less troubled past be appointed. As our ruler, it would not be hard to influence this election to favour the younger candidate who would likely be more loyal. To be seen to meddle in the affairs of the established church, however, would very likely weaken our position in the long run.
