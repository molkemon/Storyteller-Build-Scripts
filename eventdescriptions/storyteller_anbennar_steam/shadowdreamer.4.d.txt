Zur was barely an adult when they reached Gor Ozumbrog, but one does not simply refuse destiny’s call because they are not ready. Today he stands surrounded by the truest followers of the Dream, dressed in the finest robes that their clan can provide, and addresses them as their new leader. “Drahmgall. I am your chosen. Dookan speaks to you, through me, and he will hear no dissent.” There was no cruelty in his words, but they were as hard as stone. “Zog was your master before, and the Dream led him here. It led us all. You will make this place a new home for our people. This is my first command, and it will stand until the Dream shows me what comes next.”
