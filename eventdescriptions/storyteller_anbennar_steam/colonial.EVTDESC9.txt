Our efforts to develop the province go on, but now some of the settlers have rejected the capital city of the colony and begun establishing a new settlement quite far from the official realm colony in the nation. Now people flock to the second settlement, and we are left to split our efforts. Should we force the settlers to abandon their second colony and rejoin the official state colony, we shall consolidate our efforts in the nation and build our colony in a less efficient but more expedient way.
