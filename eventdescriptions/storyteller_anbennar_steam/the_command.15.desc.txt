Whispers in the dark speak of Undergrowth Goblin innerclan aggression. Lines in the sand are being disregarded, and spears are being raised in promise of future bloodshed. Accusations suggest that clans are looking for any excuse to reduce rival clans’ holdings to cinders with a gleeful maliciousness only found in Undergrowth Goblins with an axe to grind. These conflicts could potentially harm production within the Jade Mines and only aim to increase worry with other coinciding reports of heavy infighting within the streets of The Belly. It is unclear if this is a displeased group of slaves or something much deeper. Either way, the might of the Command should not be challenged by the likes of mere slaves.
