After learning of what happened in the capital, Ethelbert quickly called on nobles friendly to his case and mercenaries to form an army and march on the capital. Whilst previously the privy council advised Lisolette to show leniency to Ethelbert, as they thought he might have been unaware of the plot against him on the throne, the speed of his reaction proved he was aware of the plans of those traitorous nobles. Only force of arms can decide who will get the crown now.
