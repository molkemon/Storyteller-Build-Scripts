The folks of the colonial frontier are an honest but simple sort. While to the Bureau, the various nuances between a crytogeometrist or a liminal warlock are self-explanatory, most homesteaders will put all the people able to do magic in a single category. With the increase in general weirdness due to the Shadow War, regular people have been getting more and more wary of anything unusual. Our efforts to hide the exact nature of the threats to prevent more from joining have been successful, and on one hand, most people are not sure what a cultist or eldritch creatures are, but on the other, they sure know mages are weird, so many believe they are the same thing. Antimage sentiment is growing.
