Wyvernheart. Its cities are connected by one culture, one language, and one faith expressed by all. The mouth speaks, and all ears listen in unity. The roads are paved together in one standard, crafted via magic and artifice alike. The heart pumps, and its blood is pumped across the organism that is our nation. Our fortresses protect our borders; no invasion threatens the health of our people. Our humours are in order, and our nation’s skin protects. Rich off Escann and the bounty of the Serpentspine, the body that supplies the Heart stretches out constantly. Even at its most lackadaisical, it grasps that which other nations and peoples cannot know and makes the strides that others dare not tread upon. The Heart of All Halann continues to beat, whether the blood flows red or turns black in time.
