A number of Ancestor Worshipping dwarves in a local province have begun charging exorbitant prices or outright refusing to purchase from or sell to Pantheonists. This action seems to be spearheaded by numerous local shopkeepers and even entire guilds. As a result, this has brought the local economy to a standstill as critical industries can no longer acquire necessary materials and are forced to import from elsewhere in the country. While the slightly higher taxes involving the transport and import of materials may provide some solace to the locals, at least the state can make some gains from this situation.
