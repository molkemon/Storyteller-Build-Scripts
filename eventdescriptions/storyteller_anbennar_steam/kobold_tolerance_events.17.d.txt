It seems as if the kobolds in a nearby province have proven far braver than their appearance would lead us to believe. They have gathered great numbers, and those willing to fight for them, and are now terrorizing the locals. They seem to have little respect for our authority, however it’s entirely possible this is only because they outnumber the local militia three to one. We must do something about this. Most of our advisors suggest that we act quickly and enforce our will on them before this gets out of hand. Others suggest that we show a display of power, to force them to submit before our might. While these ideas are sound, it’s also possible we could find a local leader that we can persuade to send their people to work for our cause.
