Out in the darkness of the Dwarovar, there are eyes watching us from the dark. Our ancestral enemies see our petty squabbles and laugh, knowing that it is conflicts like these that give them an opportunity. One band has taken advantage of this opportunity. A group of goblins struck deep behind our defensive lines and pillaged a nearby province before skulking back into the darkness. To make matters worse, local representatives of both Ancestor Worship and Pantheon communities are now blaming each other for allowing the goblins into the area to attack the faithful of their respective sides.
