When they first met in battle it was random. When they met the second time it was chance. When they met the third time it was fate. And by the fourth, they knew their dance. They were both battlefield medics, passing each other as they tended the hurt. Until one day the Centaur was injured, and the Human approached and tore their shirt. They crossed the line that day as they looked in each other’s eyes, and ever since that moment completely changed their lives. Far away they fled. Their love was so deeply forbidden; none would know their story, which they fought to keep well hidden. Until they found themselves in Lelanàn, having crossed the world to be free. And if you wish to talk to them, remember. the Centaur likes tea.
