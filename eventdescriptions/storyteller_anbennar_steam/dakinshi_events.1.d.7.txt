As coffee was poured into Pinghoi imported porcelain cups, each family started discussing already preselected candidates. As the last cup was finished, the beans were cast. Thirteen out of fourteen votes went to Ka Gunam Zong. One abstained from the Baashidi representative, who frankly could not understand a word. It was a rare occurrence for the families to be united like this, but the humiliation that was the loss of Fuhoengwaan justified the action.
