Even the brightest of metals can lose their luster. In the wake of the Hoardcurse, our once grand adventurer lords have succumbed to debauchery. Their greed blinds them to the plight of our hold, causing them to sequester themselves and their indentured masses into their turrets and fiefdoms. The lord stands alone in the desolate hall of AmlazOrlz, a highlord only in name without a noble retinue.
