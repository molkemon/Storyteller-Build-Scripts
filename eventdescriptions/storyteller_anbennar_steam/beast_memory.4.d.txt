The Three Heroic Spirits are the spirits of ancient beasts who rallied the other children of the SkyHeaded Beast to save humanity from the wrath of the creator. They lead the moiety of the ancestors, and give blessings to all who keep the memory. Every eight years the three Heroic Spirits change their leadership of the ancestor realm in the same way that a new leader is chosen among the people of flesh.  Brave Mongoose is the warrior beast who first stood to rally the others against the creator. She is a leader and a fighter who cherishes most those who seize their ambitions and challenge obstacles. Her blessings aid the Tanizu in war and in governance. Mongoose Shifters take after their patron in boldness and ferocity, often at the vanguard of any battle or political event.
