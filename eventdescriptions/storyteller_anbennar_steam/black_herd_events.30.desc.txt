Markhentakruan Restoration Notes; today we have brought Markhentakruan back to the strength it once held in the days of the elders. Its thunder shakes the plains, its fire devours all it touches, and the enemies of the Black Herd flee before it. But I am not satisfied with simply matching the strength of our precursors. The simple truth is that I have already surpassed them as a mage, and am not content to simply sit idle on that title like some fat ogre mengur’m. It would be negligent of me to leave our signature magic unrefined. The quandary I face is deciding what needs to be done to improve the spell. There are of course a half dozen minor adjustments I intend to make, tweaks to improve efficiency and make the spell easier to cast, but that is mere busywork. I am searching for a grander improvement that will cause my version of Markhentakruan to eclipse that of the past. The voracious resin seems to have reached the peak of its potential, so I have turned my attention to the core of the spell. the explosive transmutation. Enhancing the potency of the detonation should not only improve both the initial lethality but also amplify the spread of the flames.
