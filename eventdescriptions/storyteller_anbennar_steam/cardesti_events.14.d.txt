The Augur Queen's curse, often referred to as the curse of the Cardesti, is famous across Cannor. after a set period of time, the Cardesti must leave their encampment and move on, settling anew somewhere else, before they once more move on from there as well. If this is not practiced on time and regularly, the curse is invoked and consumes the life of its subjects; the poor Cardesti who failed to continue their wandering. Today, a special criminal case has come to our attention. a member of the Cardesti community has been caught committing a petty crime. Normally, a few months in jail would teach these kinds of criminals the error of their ways, but in this case, the jail sentence is effectively a death sentence, and our jurists are debating whether to uphold the letter of the law or issue a special edict.
