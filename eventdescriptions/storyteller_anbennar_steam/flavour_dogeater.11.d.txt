Every ork wants to be the strongest, or to follow the strongest, and none represent this better than our fearless boss Dûsh, who might have been too fearless this time, charging all by himself through a small army of ventures before the rest of our forces could reach him. After the skirmish was over and the weak scum were defeated, the mood was somber, as Dûsh still hadn't come back. Some of us already started to punch each other to find out who should be the next big boss. That all changed when a giant bloody pile of so-called heroes started moving, and from below the corpses of the weak came out the strongest, our fearless leader, who is now thought to be unkillable by our boys.
