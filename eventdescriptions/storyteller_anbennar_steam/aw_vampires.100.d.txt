It all started with the sound of a bell warning the inhabitants of the town to hide in their homes. What was thought to be another night of strange pale figures walking the streets has turned to utter chaos. Swarms of bats descend upon the town, rendering the walls useless as packs of vampires move to begin their feast. The guards were quickly overwhelmed by bats turned into beastly assailants, their blood spread on stone and sucked out of their necks. Soon, the town turns to anarchy, the desperate population entrench themselves and set fire to their own homes as to stop the frenzied beasts from advancing, but slowly the night darkens and the monstrosities kill more and more desperate groups to satiate their thirst for blood. There is no longer a pretense of a Masquerade in the realm; these vampires have fallen to their most base needs and the people now fight to survive every single night.
