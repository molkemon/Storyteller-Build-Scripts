The sinking evening sun has brought most unwelcome news to the imperial court. It appears that Jaddar’s most generous offer of friendship to the Emperor of the western lands of Anbennar has been most rudely rebuked. This most grave insult to the DivineHerald and all the subjects of the Empire has already stoked a righteous fervor amongst the nobility and priesthood, with some even calling for the dispatch of legions towards Anbenncóst to bring these upstart lords of the west to heel. Their arrogant refusal has made it clear that in due time, these lost westernmost holdings of the Phoenix Empire need to be returned to the fold and their lords forced to prostrate themselves before the most exalted throne of the DivineHerald. The imperial court, the nobility, the priesthood, and even the faithful common people agree that this proves without a doubt that only by uniting the many realms and peoples of Halann under the banner of the Jadd Empire can Surael be served and the Struggle against the Dark be won. Relying on the wisdom and grace of foreign rulers, who may well be hopelessly tainted by the influence of the Malevolent Dark, is clearly a fool’s errand.
