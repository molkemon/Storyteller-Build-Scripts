The Command is the greatest threat that civilization in Rahen has ever faced. When the Hobgoblins swept down out of the mountains, they caught the northern kingdoms of Ghatassak and Raghajandi completely by surprise, and soon overwhelmed all the kingdoms of Shamakhad. The time has come, however, to dislodge this foul enemy and drive hard into the heart of darkness that threatens all of Haless. As we prepare to carry the war north and engage with the forces of the Command, we must contend with the role of our Senapti Tiltaghar. Among the greatest and most loyal of our vassals, Tiltaghar is well prepared to lead the war against the Command as the vanguard of the North, the strong sword that will strike into Shamakhad. While we could manage the war ourselves, it is the role of the Senapti to be our banners; perhaps we can entrust the command of this battle against the hobgoblins and the administration of the liberated land to Tiltaghar.
