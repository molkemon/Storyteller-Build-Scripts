Our reputation as a so-called rogue state and hellish bandit slaver zone makes diplomacy rather difficult, especially when we are in the mournful position of not having the world’s largest army. However, there is one obvious ally. the Ravelian State. Our ruling class is mostly Ravelian, it is spreading rapidly among the rabble, and the Society practically runs our bureaucracy, being about the only ones interested in it. If we were to gain political recognition from the High Rector, it would do wonders for our foreign affairs. A tour, therefore, is in order. A tour for an honored representative of the State, who has come to see all the pious little Ravelians. He is hearing of all the good his veridicals do, and how much more they could do with some extra support from back home. For instance, isn’t it a shame how those trade embargos are hampering charity work? Wouldn’t it help so many people if our neighbors were pressured to ease off? And if there are not quite enough inspiring stories for our guest, well, we still remember how to lie. The representative seems quite pleased with his visit, and likely to recommend further ties between our country and his. All in all, it is money well spent, and the Godheads seem happy too.
