On entering the treasure room, the delvers got the distinct sense that they had been there before, and that something was about to happen that they ought to prevent. The room contained six delicate clay jars, slightly increasing in size from left to right. The mages detected divination magic on the jars, and perhaps a whiff of something else. The jars were carefully packaged with spare blankets to ensure they made the trip safely back to the main hold, where the ruler could decide whether and when to break them to see what was inside.
