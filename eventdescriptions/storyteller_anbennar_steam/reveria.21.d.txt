As the boats sail downstream back to Mocbarja, a dragon is spotted in the distant sky, approaching fast. In the blink of an eye, the fleet is set ablaze by this fearsome green beast. Reavers jump into the water, preferring sea serpents or any other monster than a literal dragon. Our troops hopelessly pelt it with projectiles as the dragon comes back for a second flyby, and then a third, before deciding it has had enough and begins to fly off. Most of our fleet was burning down; the captain of the expedition had to be removed from the ship by force and he unfortunately lost his leg, but he kept shouting to the dragon in the distance.
