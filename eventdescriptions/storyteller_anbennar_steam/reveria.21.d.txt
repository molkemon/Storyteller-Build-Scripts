As the boats sail downstream back to Mocbarja, a dragon is spotted in the distant sky, approaching fast. Soon the fleet is set ablaze by this fearsome green beast, as Reavers jump into the water, preferring sea serpents to this sky serpent. Our troops hopelessly pelt it with projectiles as the dragon comes back for a second flyby and then a third, before deciding it has had enough and begins to fly off. The captains begin to shout orders.
