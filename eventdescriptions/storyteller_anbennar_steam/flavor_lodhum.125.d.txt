A Moon elf mage from Cannor has recently arrived, bringing with them the latest magical theories and treaties in several fields. Our own mages, in turn, have invited this individual to hold several symposiums and demonstrations to see these in action, while giving some of their knowledge in return.  This elf's skill is readily evident, especially compared to the majority of the dwarven mages in our society. A donation and offer of patronage would no doubt encourage this elf to stay with us longterm, at least for a few decades before returning to their research.
