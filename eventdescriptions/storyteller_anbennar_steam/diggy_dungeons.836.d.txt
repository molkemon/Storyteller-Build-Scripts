The rusted window gave them no quarter, and in a desperate attempt to break it open, some of the men fell down into the labyrinth. Then the window itself broke out of the tower, sending the very rocks that made up that part of the wall flying to the ground. In that torrent of material, many more got swept down into the labyrinth until it was unfeasible to go on above the walls. At last, the remaining soldiers climbed down into the labyrinthian confines, and the expedition continued onwards.
