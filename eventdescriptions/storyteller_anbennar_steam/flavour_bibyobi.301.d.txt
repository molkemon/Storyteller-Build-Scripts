Thousands of us must have fallen for that load based on the number of poor slobs I am surrounded by. One week long bender and I get myself into this. A pound of mealy bread and the chewiest rashers of bacon I have seen. Day in and day out. Almost more monotonous than the marching. You think with all those heads a hydra would be easy to find, but no. As the man carrying all of the Esteemed Leader's baggage, this incessant walk through the jungle is less than peaceful. Maybe I will suggest we reach out to the locals to see if they have an idea of where to go.
