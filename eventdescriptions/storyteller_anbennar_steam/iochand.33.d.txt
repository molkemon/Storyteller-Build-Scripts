For the monarch's eyes only, it has come to the attention of the Daccasded Administrative Council that the governance of the Aelantieri Hezoggdoums remains cripplingly inefficient. As we are sure Your Imperial Majesty is aware, many of the Hezoggdoums have reached a size at which the title of Hezogg restricts their appointment of local governance, resulting in their administration becoming overburdened and overextended. Furthermore, the ascension of Iochand to the status of Raighsz has caused a contradiction in the text of Statute 35, specifically the definition of the rank of Daccasded as a "title equal in status, yet subordinate to Iochand" as seen in Section 8b. In order to reconcile both these issues, the Council presents the following proposal. Ascend Daccasded to the rank of Raighsz, changing Your Imperial Majesty's title to "monarch of Iochand and Daccasded," and grant all Aelantieri Hezoggs in possession of titles of worthy sizes the rank of Kong. Such a change would solve all legal inconsistencies in accordance with Council Order 119, as well as greatly improve the governing capabilities of all affected Hezoggdoums. Naturally, Iochand would still possess de facto authority over all subjects on both continents, in accordance with Statute 35.12a and Council Order 214. Furthermore, Your Imperial Majesty would as a result be granted a second Raighsz title, a mark of prestige that would no doubt greatly improve the crown's respect among the other kingdoms of Cannor. Again, while Your Imperial Majesty is of course under no obligation to modify the preexisting structure of their domain, the Council strongly encourages the monarch to take this proposal under consideration for the sake of their subjects' wellbeing and prosperity.
