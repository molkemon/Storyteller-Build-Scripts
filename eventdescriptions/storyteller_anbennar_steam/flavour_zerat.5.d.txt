The empire hates both dragon worshippers and Kheteratans, so it is to the great misfortune of the land of Aramoole that they happen to be both. We have sent our troops to the city by which the land is named, and we have instructed them to give two gifts to our new subjects. For sheltering like weaklings under the wings of a dragon, we will give them a taste of their beloved fire. As we have no dragon fire, as they would no doubt most enjoy, the torches of our soldiers must suffice: we will burn the city of Aramoole to its foundations, and we will take the riches they have dedicated to their protector, a small fee for giving them the fire he could not supply. Our second gift will be to return them to their land of origin, free of charge. Whichever of them survive the bequeathing of our first gift will be sent into the mountains to return to Kheterata, and as an extra kindness we will be sure to tell them which way is north. To cement ties of trade, in exchange for their lives we will take their possessions, which will be given to the lizardfolk who will resettle Aramoole. It is possible that some of them will make it back to Kheterata. When they do, we hope they tell their people two things: that the Anzara Empire has returned, and that our generosity is overflowing.
