Victory. A funny little word to describe the mood in Gor Vazumbrog that day. The dragon had been defeated, and the finishing touches were being placed on the patches over the wound it had carved so long ago. an impressive feat of magic, artificery, and adventuring to collect all of the required components. And yet, the dead remained dead. Even sutured, the hold would always bear the scars the toxic wyrm had left on its walls and on its people. Everyone knew the sound of the miasmatic cough; even those who thought they were protected by the Aakhigen masks could still fall victim to it, sometimes even years after their encounters. Worse, everyone knew the sounds of the Vaulter's Scream, the haunting melody that was the constant reminder of the fitful, nightmare-wracked sleep that awaited the brave delver's in the old vaults in retirement, and likely even some of the arcane researchers in the new, more studious model of containment. Still, ground is being broken today on an operation to deepen the hold beyond what was possible before. The Suture would serve as a scaffold for the Titanium Hold as the onetime gatehouse claims its rightful place as the greatest hold in all the Dwarovar, and indeed, the capital of a reforged AulDwarov.
