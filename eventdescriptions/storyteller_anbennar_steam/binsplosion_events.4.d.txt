This missive is to be given to the ruler with the utmost haste, as the report by Ngolo Folforo on the Ogre Problem. I have personally sighted them in increasing numbers by the border with Tzilekal, usually busy eating much of the local wildlife along the way, but also I have received accounts from villages saying their cattle and crops are being stolen by them. Additionally, mage binders have noted planetouched moving away from them in large numbers, the reason as of yet unknown. And then there are the rumors of strange magic. I have not personally seen it, but the gathered eyewitness accounts are too similar to ignore. Sandstorms searing our border in odd timings and outside of the dry season? It is no doubt the work of their rituals and rites. Such sorcery is foul and does not bode well for the realm. I understand your hands are tied, great ruler, but I beseech you to send a court mage or planetouched to perhaps reason with or at least investigate their uncanny magics further.
