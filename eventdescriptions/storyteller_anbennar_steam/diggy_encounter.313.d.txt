With both the water at the right temperature and the jets gently flowing across the pool, our men can finally undress and relax, something they have been looking forward to ever since our initial discovery. The increasing anticipation is now being washed away by the water, and cramped muscles, stiff from the endless walking, can finally relax.  As time goes on a few other toys are discovered, a small fountain at the centre and artificial rain from the pipes above. This happy occasion went on deep into what the Serpentspine offers as a night until everyone had their fair share of water. The expedition stayed for a few days more, sampling the relaxing water before departing again.
