The monarch takes a walk through the cluttered testing grounds that was the home of the mecharam project for so long. Scattered around are wayward sheets of metal, half constructed legs, and scorch marks from alchemical misfire. The monarch well remembered the accidents and broken bones that were reported from miscalibrated parts and insufficient craftworks that led up to this final prototype, not to mention the vehement disagreements between advisors as to the feasibility of the project itself. But it is finished. The world's first wholly mechanical mount, and the vehicle that will catapult Ramsteel rangers to the most fearsome dwarven cavalry around, is finished. The final product stands proud in front of the monarch in all its metallic glory, as of yet unblemished and unscratched by the rigours of war that it would later endure.
