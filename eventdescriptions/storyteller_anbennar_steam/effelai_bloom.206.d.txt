Anyone wise has come to see the strange, otherworldly tones of the Effelais songs with great suspicion. To listen intently to these eldritch hymns is to feel the haunting tones press against your very soul; no mere mortal mind can remain unaffected. Fortunately for the government, however, these songs can sometimes be useful; such as in a distant province, where the population appears to have become remarkably sedate and obedient.
