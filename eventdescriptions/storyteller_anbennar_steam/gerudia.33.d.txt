At dawn, the two parties met upon the western banks of the mighty Vroren which once marked the northern edge of the world, at least for the Cannorians. One group. the soldiers of the country, their banners held high and led by their monarch. The other group. men and women who despite wearing furs and leather like common Tarics, carry the measure of their supposed Castanorian forebears. "Monarch, we come in the name of an ancient oath we made with the Empire of Castanor, to forever guard the North from the monsters that lurk beyond Cannor. With your victories against the orks which threw us back from our home, we humbly ask that we be allowed to return to our lands, so that we may defend the Pass once more." A moment passes, and the monarch makes their choice.
