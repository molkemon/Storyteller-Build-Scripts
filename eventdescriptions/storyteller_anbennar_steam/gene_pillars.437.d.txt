Near the top of the pillar, notes and logs are strewn across every surface imaginable, and the very air vibrates with the power of memory. So much knowledge was stored in this level that the cascading effect of the memory banks has overloaded reality itself, filling the entire space with impressions of times long past. As the party explores this veritable researcher’s paradise, their own identities quietly slip away, retreating into the personas of scientists and academics. It is only when the party starts to forget why they are exploring in the first place that they truly begin to panic. They have to get out, and fast, that is for certain. But why? Research has been going spectacularly. Their work is the most important duty. They cannot possibly leave now. They keep this thought as the body previously suspended from the hooks charges down the hallway.
