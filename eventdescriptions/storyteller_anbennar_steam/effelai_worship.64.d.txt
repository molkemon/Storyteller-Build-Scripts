"You all serve an evil will. It must be burned away for our people to survive," he called out. "The Servants, they are creatures of your forest's creation. You too shall be enthralled and subsumed," said the old Orwai'i, greyhaired and dishevelled, who had wandered into the center of our village as stunned onlookers stared on. Before long, our people armed themselves to set upon the heathen before he could bring a terrible wrath upon us for his disrespect. However, by the time they arrived, he was gone.
