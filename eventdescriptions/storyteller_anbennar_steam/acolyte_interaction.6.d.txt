Creation and Negation, the Twin Forces, are at play in every moment and in every time. The dreamers, visited once by Creation, were now met by Negation, the darker twin, who tore them asunder and birthed Beauty Descendant. The very air feels heavy with grief and the raindrops are as knives. Nature itself weeps in heartbreak for the dissolution of the romantic partnership between the realm and another country. However, all true disciples of the arcane know that pain and fury make for potent fuel indeed.
