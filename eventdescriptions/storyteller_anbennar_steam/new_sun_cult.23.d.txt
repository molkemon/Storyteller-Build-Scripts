Uncertainty is a plague upon the faithful, for it eats away at the very pillars of belief, and if nothing else these are uncertain times. The people here have abandoned the true way of Surael and returned to the Old Sun Cult. Long thought all but extinct, the chaos of the current crisis has driven many back into the arms of the old heresies that Jaher and his followers drove from the land ago, believing now that the Sun Elves are not worthy of homage. Though we are sure this wave of disbelief can be rectified, the fact that there are those who still preach the old faith is troubling and may cause issues if not stamped out quickly.
