General, general. comes in Varien, a young soldier of the Yearncliffer flock. "What is it? Don't you see we're busy?" replies the general, indeed quite busy overseeing the complete chaos that is befalling Esmaraine's town hall. "Excuse my rudeness, sir. I have found what you asked for." The soldier then hands a sheet of papers to the general, all having no visible signatures or stamps. "What is this, soldier? These papers have no markings; they can't be used as evidence. Stop wasting my time and look through the official documents." "Aha." The excited soldier then leans closer to the general and casts a simple detect magic spell on the sheet of papers. "Look. It's all there." The amazed general looked in Varien's eyes with admiration before turning back to examine the papers more carefully. "Payments, bribes, plots, favors, gifts..." Pausing for a second, he turned his gaze towards the hidden signature at the bottom of the page, "for our lady, the one true ruler of Esmaraine." All surrounding the general looked at the rising smokes in the distance with a sense of dread; to them, Esmaraine was already lost.
