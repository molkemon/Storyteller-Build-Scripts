Wealth can be quite a dirty business, yet it is instrumental to all daily activity. Recently, due to economic growth, more and more merchants have become extremely rich. While this is normally none of our concern, many have now become richer than even the upper castes, who now protest against these new rich, fearing they will lose influence and power. The lower castes have also started to complain about them, as they are the ones who do the hard work without receiving the fruits of their labor. Both groups now demand that we do something about it.
