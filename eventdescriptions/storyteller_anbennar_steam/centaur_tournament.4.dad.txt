The monarch steps into the arena wielding the Gaohernlangh, a massive curved greatsword with a long wooden handle, favored mainly due to its destructive slashing power. On the opposite side, the monarch sees an unfamiliar clansman. Apparently, it is a young and healthy warrior belonging to one of the minor clans within the Ordsiwyn, looking to prove himself during this tournament. The fight has begun. The young warrior, eager to land the first blow, charges at the Caehn. The Caehn, thinking this is going to be an easy victory, sidesteps and slashes in a wide half circle, but the young warrior proves to be more skillful and agile than the Caehn had expected. As the two fight against each other, their hooves begin to throw dust into the air, obscuring the crowd's view almost completely. Until, that is…
