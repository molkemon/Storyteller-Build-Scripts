The inner chambers are massive and seem to be almost entirely intact. Large pillars reach up under the high vaulted ceiling. Hundreds of strange coiled pieces of metal are humming across the chamber, and some sort of magical energy seems to be arching between them with a sickly yellow hue. Stumble informs us that, despite the fact that the devices seem to be still functioning on the surface, several things appear to be seriously wrong. The most important one being that seemingly random arching of energy. It is not intended and is likely very dangerous. We should tread forward carefully.
