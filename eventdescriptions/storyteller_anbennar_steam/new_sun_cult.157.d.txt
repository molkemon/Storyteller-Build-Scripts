With all the new goings on in Aelantier, the exemplars and adventuring companies have sent back missives requesting more supplies of various natures. Ships are always in demand to ferry supplies and people west, and wealth and relics east. Arms and armour are needed too, to equip them against the threats of an unknown continent. And of course, food production in the colonies will not be reliable for some time, so sending them more food will do great good for the settlers.
