I’m sure you can hear the music outside. It's quite loud. It's been going for a few days now. A group of Darwiiz are holding a festival dedicated to dispelling evil spirits and opening the minds of participants. A traditional trance dance fueled by elixirs and emotion, Darwiiz spiral along lines on a circular carpet, marking holy symbols. People in the center go in, and come out feeling refreshed. This “Qabqol” in particular has managed to grow from a few friends and Darwiiz to a massive crowd of a thousand or more. Everyone seems to be having a great time, with drinks and food being shared and people coming together over common religious love. Unfortunately, this comes at the same time as the Mwalim come close to making a groundbreaking discovery. Many wise scholars have locked themselves into a chamber in hopes to complete a variety of studies. how sound reacts with the environment, rare elements and their applications in alchemy, the medicinal perks of elusive flora, and so on. The Mwalim have stated that they need silence to be able to complete their research. While you don’t seem disturbed by the noise, they’ve come to ask for your assistance in moving the party further away from the great halls so that silence can be preserved. This would be untraditional, and may anger a few folk, of course. Ultimately, it's up to you.
