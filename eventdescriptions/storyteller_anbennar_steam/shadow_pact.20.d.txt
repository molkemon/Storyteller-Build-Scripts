It is once again that most holy week of restoration in Simegasfan. The people of the city have gathered to restore the pristine appearance of the Palace of the Sealed Tree, as they have done once every ten years for centuries. Fresh mud from the riverbanks has been mixed with straw from the harvested fields to create new adobe that will be used to fill in cracks and plaster over any superficial damage that the elements have caused over the course of the year. This communal rite brings together the inhabitants of Simegasfan, as did their ancestors. All take pride in safeguarding the inner sanctum hidden deep within the palace through their labors, thus earning protection and blessings alike from the tree worshiped within.
