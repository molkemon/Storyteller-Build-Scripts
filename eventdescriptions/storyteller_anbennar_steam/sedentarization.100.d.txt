It has been several weeks since the first wagons arrived and construction began. Among the first things built were the palisades; in an unknown land, and surrounded on all sides, the feeling of being exposed stalks the first settlers. As the next scheduled supply train approaches the settlement, they see smoke on the horizon; they quicken their pace, fearing the worst. And they soon find their fears are true. Many of the buildings have been burnt down, and supplies looted. Some survivors remain amidst the rubble, but food stores are low, and wounds will soon fester without proper treatment. These survivors inform us that the raid occurred just days ago. The centaurs hit hard and fast, ravaging and looting the outpost, leaving the town aflame behind them as they disappeared into the steppes. The outpost will be able to recover, but it is clear that the local centaurs do not want them here, and will likely continue to raid their settlement until they find a way to protect themselves.
