Time and again we have pushed forward in our pursuit of progress, breaking down the barrier between the possible and impossible. But as we raced through uncountable discoveries, we neglected an important tenet that has proven to be the downfall of many great minds over the years; just because you can doesn't mean you should. Spiritual energy is naturally suppressed by the braziers of the Eternal Cinder monasteries. This proved to be a boon when dealing with crises such as the Facestealers, but other spirits being warded from their territory instead began to grow agitated. Who were these people who stripped them of their homes? Who gave them the right to remove them from ancestral grounds? And so these spirits waited in their weakened state, and very slowly began to adapt to the searing astral heat. When they were ready, catastrophe erupted. All the monasteries in the realm simultaneously began spewing giant bursts of flame, which settled on the ground and set it alight. These jets of fire reached far from the monasteries, destroying fields and decimating villages. It seems these terrible eruptions show no signs of slowing down, lest we appease the spirits and convince them to end our torment.
