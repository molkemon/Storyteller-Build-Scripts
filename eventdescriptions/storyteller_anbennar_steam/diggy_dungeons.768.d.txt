It was almost unnoticed. Almost. One of our mages however spotted a large cable hidden under a grate in the floor, and followed it to an almost completely hidden adjoining chamber. Inside was a massive fissure leaking blue gas. Immediately it was confirmed as Halanna's Breath, a form of magical gas that emerges from deep within the earth. This gas was being harnessed by a large turbine of some kind, sucking it in and then converting it to pure energy, almost like a water wheel. This obviously is the source for the machine's power.
