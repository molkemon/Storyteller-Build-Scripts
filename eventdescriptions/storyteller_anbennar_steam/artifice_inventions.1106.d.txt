We have made an engine that runs on Power. This is not an engine that is "powered" there is no coal, nor Dames tear, which allows it to function but rather it is fueled by Power itself. By using Wish spells from Bulwar, Fey relics from the Deepwoods and Eordand, and Runewritings of the dwarves and giants, we have crystalized the concept of Power into physical form. Our Empire Engine, assembled in pieces across our nation, is essentially a grand ritual spell without precedent: it imbues us, our people, our government, and our armies with Power, every day of every year. This metaphysically gives us the ability to shape the world as we wish.  Or, at least, we think we have. It is hard to do an objective test of whether we have summoned Power for ourselves. It sure seems like we have, though, right? Don't you feel more powerful?
