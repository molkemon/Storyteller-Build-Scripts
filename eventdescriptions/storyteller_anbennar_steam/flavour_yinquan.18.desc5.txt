Our presence at the high temple of the realm was welcomed at first, but the reception grew notably colder over time, on account of our hired adventurers having an unfortunate habit of attempting to steal kobolds to take them as good luck charms during their temple exploration. Obviously this was not permitted, though the resultant manpower shortage was deeply inconvenient. Featuring prominently in the temples were motifs of a long and winding line with three other lines crossing through it at varying points. However, the art that caught the eye of our observing scholars and mine as well was a tile mural found deep in the temple, next to the gem we were seeking. It depicted a group of large figures with prominent horns placed next to a group of smaller, undetailed figures.
