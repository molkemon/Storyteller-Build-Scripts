The ruler, the Binwarji allowing our crusaders to return, has bolstered our forces and impressed upon us the need for independence. Already, the warriors sing of a free Dendugma, serving only God and His people. The plan, laid out by our warriors, is to push into the Dao Nako, secure the Ironwood supply, and create a new order across our lands, focused not on oppression and fear, but on piety and good works.
