The Amber Princess appeared behind the Gloam Knight, and thrust an arm through their chest as if it was only water. The Gloam Knight gasped in pain, before going limp. The assembled troops watched in horror as, one by one, each piece of the Gloam Knight’s armor fell into the soft forest floor. Within minutes, the Amber Princess’ arm was thrust through only air. Where had their body gone? Then the Amber Princess closed her eyes and stretched her arms out, and her hair began blowing in a breeze that none of the troops could feel. If the troops were horrified before, that was nothing in comparison to when they saw the Gloam Knight’s armor begin to float. Some of the troops cried heresy and deserted. In what felt like slow motion, the Gloam Knight’s armor fit back together in the facsimile of an elf. The Amber Princess, still with her eyes closed, manipulated the animated armor, before dropping her hands. The armor did not collapse. Instead, it walked over to its sword, bent down, and picked it up. Fully half the assembled troops deserted.
