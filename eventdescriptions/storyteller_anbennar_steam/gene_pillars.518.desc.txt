Our fight against the snake's nest seems to have drawn some attention our way. As we make our way further in, we start to notice vibrations reverberating through the tunnels. We take cover, attempting to hide as a massive fully grown hinuisnake slithers by. Whether it is looking for us because we disturbed the nest or simply because it is looking for prey, we do not know. It does, however, appear to be blocking our continued progress.
