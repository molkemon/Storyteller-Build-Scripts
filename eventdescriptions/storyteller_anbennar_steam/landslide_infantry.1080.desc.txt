This time it was no natural disaster that has halted our progress, but our fellow countrymen in the form of bandits and pirates. A construction such as this requires materials from across our nation, and at any of a thousand points our workers can be waylaid, robbed, or killed as has now been the case dozens of times. We have only recently come to understand the scope of this sabotage, as over a thousand individuals have reported incidents of piracy and banditry. We must do something to stop this.
