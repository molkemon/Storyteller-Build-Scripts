The creeping feeling of turmoil on the misty hills has only grown since its presence was made known to us. With the Great Command collapsing around us, it is only natural that those who seek liberation would claim it at this critical moment, our moment of weakness. Always a point of contention, the Oni of the Demon Hills have expelled local hobgoblin garrisons and declared total independence. Instead of taking advantage of the chaos, they have taken up defensive positions across the countryside, awaiting an inevitable counterattack, and in the process, ceded all initiative to us. The Boars cry for war, while the Lions urge us to consider leaving the matter for a more suitable time. The Grand Marshal must make their decision.
