Great trophies of even greater beasts line the halls of the lords, each a legendary tale to be told for eternity. This display inspires awe in those who seek to emulate them and spurs them to ever more daring deeds of dauntless destruction. But none are more welcome than the new recruits arriving at the capital. They are a band of seasoned adventurers, monster hunters whose fame has propagated throughout the land. They now offer their services to us, in hope to die in valiant combat.
