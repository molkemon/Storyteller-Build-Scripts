It was a small blessing to begin with that delegates from all the cities of the League even arrived today, and a much larger one that our work in Yanzhong and Yinzhen has resulted in them supporting the unity proposal.  Of course, few among our scholars expected the same response from the representatives of Guhe and Anjiang; a correct call to make. Neither supported the notion, but this meeting itself has established the concept of unification as a very real goal.  With this in mind, we can move forward on wooing the two stragglers.
