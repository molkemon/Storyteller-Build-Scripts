A scout returns from a routine inspection with some interesting and rather surprising news. Just ahead is a cave filled with moss, upon which a group of kobolds is feasting. These little dragonlike creatures are an unusual though not uncommon sight in the Serpentspine ever since the death of their dragon god, which led to the scattering of his kobold and human servants. These particular individuals do not seem to be soldiers, as they are peacefully minding their own business.
