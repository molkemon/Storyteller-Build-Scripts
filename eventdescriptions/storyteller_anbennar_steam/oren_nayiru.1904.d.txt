With their Great RelicLord dead, it is clear that they will never again have the strength to truly conquer Taychend. They carved a bloody whirlwind of destructive and righteous vengeance across the region, but the storm has been scattered, and their dreams of an agrarian, native-ruled, theodemocratic Taychend have been scattered with it. The Kavalali said that this was a war for Dawn. So it was, and the night has triumphed. The Oren Nayiru have been thrust back into the dark.
