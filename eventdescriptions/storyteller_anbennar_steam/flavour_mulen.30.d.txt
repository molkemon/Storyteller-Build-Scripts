After many years of ambiguously sharing power with the Merchant Matriarchs, it's time to finally formalize the roles of the Merchant Matriarchs and our ruler in our government. The Mulen constitution of current will resolve much of the problems with our previous arrangement and give stability to our country. In it, representatives of the Peacocks and the Sparrows will endorse the interests of their patrons, and make proposals for governance. While drafting this constitution, the Sparrows and the Peacocks engaged in bitter negotiations, but slowly points of contention were eventually resolved with compromise. Resentment and distrust still persists between the two factions, but with the establishment of the constitution they may be channeled into authorized institutions. The internal conflict continues, but "rules of war" have now been adopted by both sides.
