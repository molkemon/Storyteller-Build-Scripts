Well, I wouldn’t have put it that way, the genie says, but even as he speaks his magic is loosening its grip on the great door. It retracts to each side, dissolving in both directions, parting to let your group pass. Beyond its threshold lies a grand storeroom, different from those you have previously traversed only in its heavily stocked shelves. It is an enormous haul, and you can’t possibly take all of it with you. Besides, the wholesale plunder of a place like this would strain the fragile Vernman accord with the myriad priestly lodges of Akasik. That’s why the priest is here – he won you approval for this little jaunt in the first place. He offers you the pick of the litter in thanks for such a grand reclamation of Akasik history.
