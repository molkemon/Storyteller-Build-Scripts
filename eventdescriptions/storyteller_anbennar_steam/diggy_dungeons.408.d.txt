Of the many sights to be seen beneath the surface, few are as rare as the naga. A serpent with the face of a woman, these crafty spellcasters can make quick work of an adventuring party, using strategy and cunning to outmaneuver greater enemies. Slumped over a dilapidated turbine is one of these rarities, adorned with charcoal black scales and lighting snoring with a satisfied grin. Fortune appears to remain with them.
