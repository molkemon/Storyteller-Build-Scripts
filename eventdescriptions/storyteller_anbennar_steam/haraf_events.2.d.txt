Our lord and his entourage arrive in their lands, marked by dunes of sand slowly replacing the dry grasslands they are accustomed to. After the expected reception by their chief, our lord is led on a tour of the desert settlement and eventually intrigued by the tamed collection of giant, burrowing worms native to the desert. "Ah, I see you have a curious eye for the sandworms," says their chief. "I must admit, even after all these years living among them, I still find them as majestic as when I was a boy. Magnificent gifts they are." A reminiscent smile decorates their face as our lord shifts their attention from the sandworms to the chief. "I wish to ride one of them," states our lord, plainly. The smile on the chief's face suddenly spreads into an ecstatic look of glee as they hear the words. "I thought you'd never ask, my lord." Several minutes pass as our lord’s chosen sandworm is readied and they stand atop it. "My lord, I must insist you ride one of the smaller sandworms for your first attempt. This one is much too large and ornery for a beginner." "No need to worry, my lord. If I can wrangle the creatures, I can handle a measly worm." boasts our lord. As if in protest, the sandworm charges ahead as the chief struggles to resist the worm's attempts to throw them off. At last, our lord sinks their wormhooks into the beast and is able to steer it back towards the settlement. A cheering crowd awaits our lord as they return and the chief receives them. "Amazing. Such a large worm broken in so quickly, it would be a shame to not offer him to the one who has bested him. All that he requires now is a name, my lord?"
