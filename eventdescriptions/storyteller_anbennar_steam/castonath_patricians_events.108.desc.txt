The river Nath cleaves Castonath into multiple parts, with North Castonath and South Castonath facing each other across the river's banks. Both sit high above Lower Castonath downriver, with a sudden sheer drop separating the two somewhat wealthier districts from the less fortunate below. The drop creates one of the most striking features amongst many in Castonath; a waterfall dropping mightily down in the middle of the city, crashing into the rocks below. Unfortunately, however, the Nath's flowing waters and convenient positioning also make it one of the greatest trash and sewage receptacles in the known world. Residents of the upper districts dump all sorts of refuse into the Nath, turning the water an enchantingly muddy brown. This has led to many from Lower Castonath lovingly dubbing the waterfall the 'Shitsfall'. Along with human refuse, many patrician owned guilds and industries take advantage of the Nath to dump toxic reagents or byproducts and other wastes for easy disposal. The residents from Lower Castonath have been complaining about higher rates of plague and malaise that they believe stem from this filthy practice, and are petitioning the rulers to better regulate the use of the Silverfall as a glorified sewerage network.
