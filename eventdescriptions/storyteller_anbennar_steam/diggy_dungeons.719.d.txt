Unwilling to let the spiders repair the golem the expedition had just spent so much effort to disable, many set to work on destroying the mechanical spiders with whatever they had on hand or foot, for many. Indeed, a number of the expedition seemed to take a disturbing level of joy from crushing the mechanical spiders underfoot. After this was taken care of, many noted the spiders themselves were composed of a number of rare metals, including being made with solid gold thread. On top of selling the golem for parts, it should make a tidy sum.
