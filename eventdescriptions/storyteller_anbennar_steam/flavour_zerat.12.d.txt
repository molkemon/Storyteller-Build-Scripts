We do not have much interest in directly administrating the world outside Taneyas, so it is fortunate that the world has decided to come to us. Through our control of the port of Karassk, the finest goods of the known world filter into Taneyas to trade, and we have hardly had to lift a finger beyond providing a garrison for the port and a navy to protect its incoming ships. From Karassk, these fine goods make their way along the rivers of Taneyas to Zerat, Shalazar, and all corners of the empire, where they are sold for great sums of money. Of course, the empire takes a small portion of this for facilitating the exchange. Eventually, these small shavings of gold amass into a treasury, which we invest to improve the dockyards of Karassk and the riverways of Taneyas so goods can come in faster. The cycle then begins again. What a fine system we have devised.
