As word of our Strength potions spread, ogres from far and wide decided to come to Varaine to experience the true strength of Giantkind for themselves; as well as gorge themselves on the plentiful Varainey food from alchemically boostes pastures and fields.  These visitors present both an opportunity and a danger to Varaine. We could simply take these ogres into our country, no questions asked, and possibly deal with the consequences of their entry and their hunger. Alternatively, we could take in those willing to put in the work into helping our nation, which would be a fair deal. Finally, we could just not take any risks and drive them away.
