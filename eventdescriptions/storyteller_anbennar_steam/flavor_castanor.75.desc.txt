The centaurs are gone. But so too is the flourishing civilization that once lived in these isles. A people proud of their independence were made slaves and refugees, a land dotted with states that pushed the idea of what a city can be has seen all those great buildings razed and burned. But Castanor has known its time of failures too. Its times broken, destroyed, left for rubble. And now this great empire, the greatest of all humanity, has surpassed any of what it once was. And as the monarch walks through what was once the Lake Federation, they think that some day may be true here, as well.
