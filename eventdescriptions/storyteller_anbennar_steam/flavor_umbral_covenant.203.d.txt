Coven and Covenant are ever more entwined; for all that the hags fed on the constant small-scale warfare conducted by their swamp troll horde, gorging themselves on misery, a stable ally has done great things for the extent of the Coven's domain. Yezel Mora turns its hueless gaze across yet more of Sarhal, and Granny, Mother, Aunty, and Daughter come ever closer to their final act of shadow.
