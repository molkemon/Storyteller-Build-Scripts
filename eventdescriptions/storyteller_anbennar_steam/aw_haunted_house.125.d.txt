The guardsmen saw the flame first from their perch; it was bizarre, for it barely smoked, releasing only a modicum of that you would find in a proper fire, before it erupted into a horrid flame. The flames seemed to dance unnaturally, growing and twisting to touch all that was within the manor's grounds, engulfing everything from the wooden walls, all the way to the iron bars of the gate. The townsfolk were driven into a panic as the flame spread through the dead grass that surrounded the estate, helplessly clamoring for their buckets as the flame whipped out of control, waltzing through their town and consuming all it touched, including the people unfortunate enough to be caught in its path. The haunted house that once loomed ominously over the people of the realm is now gone, sparing the folk of its horrors forever, having left this world in the same manner it lived. terrorizing and traumatizing all in its presence.
