The twin brothers Sevinay and Kiyokat again stand before Hiderion, now aged considerably. Hiderion waits for them to speak, leaning back in his chair.  It is Kiyokat that talks first. "My lord, I think you know why we are here. I grow tired of war and my family and peace calls to me." Hiderion nods once, and turns to his twin. "My lord, you do not want the advice of an old man, dull from his years of thinking. It is time I leave your side. I wish to work on a memoir, of the time we spent together, of the great things we have done." Hiderion nods once more. "Go, my beloved friends, with my blessing. You have served the people well. Enjoy the fruits of the lands you have built.
