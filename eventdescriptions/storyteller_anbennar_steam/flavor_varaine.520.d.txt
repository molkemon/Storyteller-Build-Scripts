Having decided to study other magical flying creatures, our researchers spent some time traveling from place to place in search of stray denizens of the plane of Air who ended up in the Prime Material. While this did result in a lot of progress for the development of the potion, our researchers believe the final breakthrough they need to complete their work would require a very powerful elemental, drawn straight from the plane of Air. While finding such a creature in the wild wouldn't be impossible, it would be extremely difficult and there'd be no way to guarantee it'd be in an ambient we could actually study it. Thankfully, it is possible to invoke these elementals straight from the plane of Air, though to invoke one of the power needed wouldn't be trivial. To that end, the immediate choice would be to ask our own magisters for help, though they may not have all of the experience required for such a task. Alternatively, we have heard stories of Sarhaly expert elementalists who specialize in invoking elementals, though their help would be expensive.
