It was not so long ago that the monarch declared to the people that they would stabilize the nation and push out the invaders. We took on an ambitious portion of our vast list of reforms and solutions, and while not every measure we tried to implement was successful, the majority were. This has resulted in a far more stable society protected by a much more able military. While the people sometimes still struggle to have their problems heard, we at least keep an ear to the ground now. And while there is still some corruption and slack within the administration and military, it is far from the stagnant pool it once was. All in all, most among the court believe that the monarch has done a fine job. And the people seem to agree.
