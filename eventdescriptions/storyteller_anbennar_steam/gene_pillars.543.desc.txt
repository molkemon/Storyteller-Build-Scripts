We stumble upon a locked door, and end up spending longer than we should like getting it opened. No matter how much we tried to get it open, the door seemed sealed shut as strongly as possible. When we eventually manage to pry it open, we learn why. As soon as the door opens, a thick cloud of something starts to billow out. At first, we think it might be smoke, but we quickly learn it is not. The cloud is a harmful wave of spores. Not only does it seem toxic, but the spores also seem to have a corrosive effect on skin.
