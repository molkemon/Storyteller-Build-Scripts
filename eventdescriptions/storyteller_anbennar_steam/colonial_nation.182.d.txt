The ruler's struggles with the administration of the nation were not a secret for many. Rumors about their ineptitude had to be silenced by more skilled diplomats in an attempt to maintain the peace of the colony. However, the recent increase in corruption has revealed the inevitable; one of the former administrators has made public that the monetary help received by the Crown has gone directly into the ruler's pocket. This ill behavior in times of need has led to a number of uprisings among the people that need to be taken care of.
