We have breached into Kheterat, the Jewel of the Sorrow, the First Human City, the Elikhet's Legacy and the Khets' home. Daring to plunder its riches, we started to loot the place, as a voice suddenly rang over the skies. "Foreign trespassers, evacuate this city or else, the next sunset shall bring forth the Khets' wrath." We naturally ignored this, as this wasn't the first time the trick of "The god's imminent wrath" has been used. This was our mistake. Next sunset indeed, a cloud of locusts swept through the city, devouring all our retainers in their path, while leaving Khet's faithful unscathed. The Khets have shown that they are the ones who own the city.
