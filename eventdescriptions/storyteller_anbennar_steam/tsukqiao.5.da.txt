My spouse had a visionary dream without parallel in Beikling history. Many of our people had despaired, but she embraced hope and devoted her life to a chance at freedom. Thus many saw her as a mother to the entire Beikling people. Fuyuan's dear daughter is with us too; she still puts her best foot forward for the realm. The young one might have been an empress had Fuyuan Jiao had her way; as it stands, she might not be an heir but is still a valued courtier. She stands next to me watching Jinqiu fall. She has made me proud. It is a shame she lacks the wherewithal to inherit the throne after I die. We devote every waking moment to the most noble cause of expanding our empire. We defeat our enemies through ruthless strategy and consummate zeal. We transcend the weak origins from which Fuyuan Jiao had begun to pull us away from. We built a kingdom, and then the beginnings of an empire. There can be no doubt that Luzhong will soon be reborn through our efforts. Yet still, my beloved Fuyuan Jiao is dead. I buried her in the greatest possible mausoleum we could fashion for her in Tiaochi. And thereafter.
