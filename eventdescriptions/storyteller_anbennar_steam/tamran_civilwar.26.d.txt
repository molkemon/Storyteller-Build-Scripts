Mercenaries, always following the scent of blood, have been pouring into Thidinkai of late. A rather famous mercenary captain has come to us with a proposition. He is willing to serve as a general and leverage his connections to secure us favorable contracts and sabotage our enemy's efforts to find bodies. In exchange, he wants us to provide mercenaries certain privileges in our new administration, as well as a modest sum to grease some pockets.
