The party comes upon a chamber grander in scale than most others they have encountered before. Their voices echo ominously through the empty space, and it appears large enough from end to end that it could house a small village. The expeditionaries are utterly perplexed at the purpose of this cavernous space, for it seems to be just a bare set of rocky walls. Upon further inspection, massive claw marks are found on one of the walls. The lowest of these are over a meter above the floor. One of our party members puts their hand on the wall. Their hand is not even a fifth of the size of a single claw, judging from the size of the mark it left. Some grand beast must have been held here. The barrenness of the room makes it abundantly clear that its original purpose was to function as a holding cell for dark experiments.
