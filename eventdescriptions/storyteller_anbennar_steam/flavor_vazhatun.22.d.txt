Slowly the suspicion and wariness of the strange colour gives way to fascination and curiosity. People, travellers from all around, begin swarming to the lake to look at the colour as well, to see and experience the strange wonders it promises within its iridescence. This has led to some opportunistic dwarves to profit off of the sudden influx of people. They have set up shops and other attractions along the shore, advertising this great display of the wonders of nature.
