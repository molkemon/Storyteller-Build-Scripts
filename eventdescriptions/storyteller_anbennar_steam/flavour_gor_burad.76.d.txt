Faith is important to any dwarf. Whether it be the old gods that allowed them to reach such monumental heights, or their ancestors that got them through their horrific banishment aboveground, they have found solace and success in their faith. However, sometimes this same intense faith that allows them unity in trying times can sometimes lead them to fragmentation and fracture, like glass under pressure. This has been especially exemplified in their latest Borbordûz challenge, in which one of their priests has seen fit to descend into the magma arenas to "stop the decay of Basalt civilization due to perfidious nambypambies in their government." While one would expect a bigwig priest in most lands to be a cakewalk, the martial tradition of Gor Bûrad has led to them becoming an acclaimed fighter herself, their strikes and swerves seemingly swift as the ancestors' spirits themselves. Shall they refuse her challenge and subject themselves to the humiliation it will bring, or put an end to her squabbling and plotting?
