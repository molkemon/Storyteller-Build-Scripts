In our aimless wanderings through our verdant home, we have encountered another tribe of beings like us yet unlike us at the same time. They call themselves the Kamutarihikou. Their practices are abhorrent to us, for they are Tinanatahae; corpse thieves. They plunder the remains of Mechanim damaged beyond repair to restore the function of their own damaged parts. And yet, the most desperate amongst our kin sought them out as we passed by, in hopes of healing we could not provide.
