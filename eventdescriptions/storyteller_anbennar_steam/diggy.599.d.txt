Nobody is sure how it started, but the local clergy report a disturbing new custom. A peculiar cavern in the province area, home to a single deep crow, has become a pilgrimage site for superstitious locals. Our reports are inconsistent when it comes to its alleged powers; some believe it brings wealth and prosperity, while others expect it to heal their ailments. But regardless of their intentions, all bring offerings to the crow, hoping for an approval signaled by an earsplitting caw straight from its mandibles. Typically, these offerings consist of harmless items like fungi or serpentsbloom, but the recent blood sacrifice of a young person has sent shockwaves through the community and sparked outrage among local clergy. Their voices merge in a cacophony of fear and anger as they demand an end to this sacrilegious practice.
