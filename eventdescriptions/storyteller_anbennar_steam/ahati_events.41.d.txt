Relations between humans and gnolls have for the most part been tense, to say the least. This has been so ever since the gnolls and humans first interacted. The Ahati, who see in the gnolls nothing but invaders and despoilers, are even more hateful towards these furry inhabitants of Salahad. And a band of Ahati have shown this hatred through pure slaughter and destruction by attacking a gnollish village in that province. They have burned the dwellings, killed not just the men, but the women and the children too. None have been spared and the smoke of death and destruction can be seen from far away. And while some villages have been lost already, some are still intact and so we can intervene if we wish to. Stopping them would anger the Ahati, but improve our standing with our gnollish subjects. We could also look the other way and let the Ahati rage rain down upon the gnolls as they please.
