The dawn of the orkish slave trade is a golden opportunity for us. Not only is it a chance to pay the orks back for what they did to our kind and to clear up more land for goblins, but becoming a primary supplier of orks would put us directly in the heart of the Cannorian trade network. This would not just make us filthy rich, but even more importantly it would make our efforts to integrate into Cannorian society all but guaranteed. This would look like a win-win-win-win, yet there is one detail that gives some of us pause. In the recent decades, a surprising number of orks have become followers of Corin, some showing more devotion and piety towards the woman they fought against than us who fought beside her. This could be the key that would let us end our conflicts with the orks and instead turn them into allies. A tempting proposition, despite our history of animosity, as our flight from the Deepwoods and establishment of Orvitzyen have taught us the value of finding allies in unexpected places. Unfortunately, such a show of clemency towards the orkish invaders would risk to undermine the relationship with the Escanni and wider Cannor that we have worked so hard to build.
