Our plans seem to have backfired. In attempting to convey peace and neutrality, it apparently came off as an attack on Surael. The radicals who lived in the far corners of our country have now gathered and declared a war on our "heretical and devilish" government. They have declared that we are being guided by the Malevolent Dark, and that by messing with the natural order of Bulwar, we have corrupted the lands. Their forces number in the thousands and they gather more as they continue to march towards our capital. This was never the intention of what we wanted to do. Most people where our troops have ventured have seen massive growth in their towns and cities. We have let the citizens freely express themselves as they willed. It seems most of the rebellion is composed of elves, mostly of noble background. If we manage to beat this rebellion, we may get a hint to the reasoning of why these elves fight against a good cause.
