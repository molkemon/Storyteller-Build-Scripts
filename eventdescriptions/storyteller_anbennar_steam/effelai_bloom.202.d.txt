The songs come from the jungle, their insistent melodies feeling as though they might inhabit your very soul. Indeed, in the case of the so-called 'songservants', it seems that is precisely what happens. In that province, the songs have grown ever more insidious, and now citizens disappear without a trace. Whether they are newlyminted songservants or mere victims of the deranged, it certainly hasn't been ideal for morale or productivity.
