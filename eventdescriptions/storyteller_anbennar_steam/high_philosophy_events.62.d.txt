It is at times such as these, when our country is characterized by relative tranquility and self-reflection, that we are reminded to look at those who are less fortunate. The recent crop yields have been neither failures nor a great success. As a result, while most have food and the state has ample grain supplies, there are still some unfortunates who are going hungry. We could well take action to feed them, but our restraint and consideration has thus far seen affairs largely manage themselves towards a positive goal for most people. Is this not evidence that Dekhana is the right path?
