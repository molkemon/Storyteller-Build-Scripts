"Me gran'da came an' 'e claimed 'is land / spit anna grit anna hammer innis hand / Wanted a new life on the sand / Krummel damn this town.  Me gran'da saw when th' Hav'rics came / raidin' anna killin' an' putting lands t'flame / me gran'da fought but he died th' same / Krummel damn this town.  Me ol' man lived an' 'e saw th' wreck / taxed fer half 'is output, dagger attis neck / Hav'rics let our land rot, kneelin’ at their beck / Krummel damn this town.  Me ol' man heard of the Hav'ric's deal / Workin’ fer yer selfrule, workin’ fer yer meals / me ol’ man sighed an’ ‘e swore to kneel / Krummel damn this town.  So now I’m ‘ere on th’ bloodsoaked land / spit anna grit anna hammer in me hand / Workin’ fer free to the Hav’rics’ plan / Krummel damn this town."  Work song of the Argzevale Dwarves in Sarda, 18th century (composer unknown)
