Through His Glory, His Will, and His Splendor, the armies of the Runefather, imbued with divine purpose, have made the first step to reclaiming the surface. It has cost much dwarven blood, but such blood is spilled with divine purpose, and thus the spilling of blood is the Will of the Father. With the Runelink established, the Third Step has been taken, and thus, after countless millennia, the Fourth Step may begin. Through blood, through sweat, through redemption.
