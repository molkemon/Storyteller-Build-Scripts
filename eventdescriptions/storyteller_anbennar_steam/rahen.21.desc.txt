Torches lit up the night sky in the city. People started flocking into the town square. A criminal has been apprehended trying to sneak into the porcelain production shop; a Cannorian, the greatest fear of the guildmasters, has been caught attempting to steal the secrets to porcelain making. As news spread, the crowd only grew in size and rowdiness until finally a city magistrate arrived on the scene. The crowd writhed with citizens calling for punishment, for the spy to be put to death, and for the Cannorians to be expelled entirely from the city. A decision needed to be made.
