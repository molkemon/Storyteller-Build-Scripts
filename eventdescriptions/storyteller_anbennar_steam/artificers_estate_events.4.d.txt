Recently some of our nobles are having a problem. their peasants apparently no longer want to be serfs and prefer to live in the cities, even if that means they need to serve as manual labourers in the artisans' workshops. Now the nobles are demanding we do our best to return their personnel lest they be forced to work themselves in the fields instead of training for war. However doing so would anger the artisans, as they have grown fond of their new source of cheap labour.
