Expedition members pick through the remains left on the steel bridge. Almost no trace of the men who fought the gigantic ooze remains, aside from scattered pieces of armour and hastily discarded equipment. Weapons sank into the great ooze and spells bounced off ineffectually while it continued its inexorable advance. As the expedition was nearing complete defeat, the last survivors retreated beyond the threshold of the steel bridge. Opting not to leave the vicinity of the magma pool, the ooze instead slipped away over the bridge and disappeared. It took several hours before a scout braved the bridge again, reporting that the ooze appears to have left the expedition alone for now.
