The mages of Muhaqaar grew arrogant, even before fate handed them lordship over the lake. They looked down upon their people from their tower, convinced their work was for the greater good. Despite their lofty message, like all who rule through power, their actions belied their words. Using their spellcraft to bind their slaves' minds, robbing them of their will? Could any action against another human be so base? Despicable. By strength of arm, strength of will, and strength of spell, we have liberated those chained in Muhaqaar. The research and academic resources they amassed in the tower will be freely distributed, giving our people the freedom and resources they need to study magic for the better of all. It also puts an end to their unholy desire to see Elikhet raised in a new, artificial, inferior body. Let not what is gifted by Mother Nirakhet be so easily disregarded.
