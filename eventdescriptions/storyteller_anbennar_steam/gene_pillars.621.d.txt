The previous expedition at this pillar ended rather poorly; the party's actions have led to the release of a great spirit. According to Kanolowele legend, the spirit is the fabled Master of the Tower. His freedom and return have caused great chaos in the area. His winged servants patrol the skies, capturing any unfortunate, wandering souls and imprisoning them in the pillar. We must stop the Tower Master’s reign, or suffer his reign. As the party moves to the entrance of the pillar, they are greeted by the sight of a Kanolowelen woman, wielding a familiar spear, the same spear in the visions of the great warrior in the pillar. The woman looks upon the party and speaks. "The rain follows the tide. It is good that you’ve come back after unleashing this terror upon this land. But now you must rectify your mistakes. My name is Manui, and I will help you imprison the Tower Master, just as my people once did."
