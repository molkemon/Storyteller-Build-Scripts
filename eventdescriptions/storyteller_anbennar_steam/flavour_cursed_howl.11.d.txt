"This isn't over..." the Silentblade chieftain laughed, as he croaked his last. His scolding in these moments, the laughter that came before we could take no more, it still rings in the clearing. His body begins to dissolve before our eyes, into a horrific black gunk that seeps into the ground. Such a bizarre demise seems to be unexpected to his other subdued companions, who look on with scarcely less horror than we do. Whether this was a planned or an unexpected manifestation of power, one thing is certain. we cannot be sure whatever malign force was operating is truly over.
