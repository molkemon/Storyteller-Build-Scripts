The two goblins looked to the monarch with unblinking, expectant eyes. The ruler vacantly stared in their general direction, flexing their neck muscles as they struggled to contain a mighty yawn. The dimly lit room lent itself well to a nap, just like the cushioned chair they were sat on, and indeed, like this whole meeting. You could scour the continent from Khugdihr to the Dragon Coast and still not find an alchemist skilled enough to brew a stimulant for all this boredom. A quick prod from behind jolted them awake, their page gesturing to the petitioners in the room. The monarch could really go for a duel right about now. But duty called, and the knightly kind was not contained to battle, no matter how hard they wished it were. “Remind me again, oh fair subjects of mine, of your names.” “Zzezuk, son of Zezzuk, my liege.” “Wozzka, daughter of Wezzok, my liege.” “Alright, as your liege, I would be fantastically delighted if you could please summarize your positions on the matter in one sentence. Preferably in one clause.”
