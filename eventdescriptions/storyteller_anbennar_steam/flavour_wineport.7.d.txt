Castíne stepped off the gangplank and onto the pier of Bulwar. Beautiful, sunkissed Bulwar. She grinned broadly at the elven envoy who was waiting for her, offering her most charming smile. Castiné loved the elves. their ageless beauty, their refinement, their culture, and their wine. Especially their wine. This was why she was here, after all. The elf bowed deeply. "Honourable envoy, we are elated to welcome you to the domain of the local ruler. May you walk in Surael's Light, during your stay and forevermore." Castiné returned the bow. It was no easy feat, but she had practiced. As with all things elven, there were many small details to even a bow, where one could err. Frustrating, to be sure, but she enjoyed a challenge. "And may Surael's Light give you sight to see the purpose of my visit. I have missed Bulwar dreadfully, dear envoy." The elf smiled. Then nodded. "My master is most curious, it must be said. We are blessed with your traders in our cities, yet your visit promises to be something special."
