The reports from your regional governors are concerning. Taxes simply aren’t getting paid. Local farmers aren’t delivering their harvest quotas, local landowners aren’t paying you money and local nobles aren’t helping you solve this. One night, as you walk through your capital and focus on the only place that still respects your authority, you see a hulking gnoll out of the corner of your vision. She approaches your entourage and speaks. ‘I can make your people listen to you again. Do not ask how, just answer me. Will you accept my help or not?’
