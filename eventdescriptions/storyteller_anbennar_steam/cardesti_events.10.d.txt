The illiterate peasantry is known for their superstitious nonsense, and it looks like this time, the Cardesti are at the forefront of peasant suspicion in the realm. The locals are accusing the Cardesti of using forbidden magic to alter the future and bring misfortune upon the province. The local governor has sent us a letter, warning the ruler that a continued presence of the Cardesti could lead to further upheaval and unrest.
