In recent years, our nation has experienced new levels of prosperity. Nowhere else in the Empire, or perhaps all of Halaan, can match our riches. In fields of academics, economics, diplomacy, and administration, we are always reaching new heights. This is in no small part due to our egalitarianism, by which we enable bright minds in all fields to shine brightly and add competitiveness to their field. One such bright mind has come to the attention of the Crown and is now ready to serve their country.
