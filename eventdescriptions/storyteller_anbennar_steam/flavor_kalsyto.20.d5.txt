Once we were as divided as a people, looking towards only one face of the many. Once we feared and cowered alone in our huts like the scared children we were. Once the dream was on the cusp of destruction as we tore at ourselves. But no longer. For our Mother has called us home. She taught us much and guided us down the path of unity. Now we look upon each other, not as a desperate people falsely united in hatred, but as brothers and sisters, united under the grace of Kalyin. Blessed be Halann. Blessed be the Federation. Blessed be our mother, Kalyin.
