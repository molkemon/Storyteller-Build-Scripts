You should not have attempted to dig through the walls. In an effort to shorten our journey, you made the foolish decision to pick away at the armor of a figure, only to discover a horrible, hidden secret: under its brilliant surface, colossal sheets of pulsing red flesh pump away. Even as you watched on in horrid fascination, the walls began repairing themselves, laying down a glistening metal film until our efforts were totally invisible. Now, our party lies exhausted from the task and horrified from the foul excavation, and worse, the environment itself seems to be exerting its oppressive influence on the sanity of our party members. Something must be done to put this disturbing memory behind us.
