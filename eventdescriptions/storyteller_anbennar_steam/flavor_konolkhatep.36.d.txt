O Horutep, Keeper of all Lore. Knowledge brings the power to act upon the world. Our commanders cannot fight without strategy, our merchants cannot trade without economics, and our priests cannot worship without doctrine. In your Great Library, you hold all the knowledge of the world, and it then stands to reason that you hold all the power of the world, though you knew this already, O Horutep. In their reports, the scholars of the library say that there is yet more to discover beyond what we already know. Let them go forth and discover it in righteous defiance of the world's ignorance, and let their insights be compiled in your grand library. But let them also remember that even to discover requires knowledge itself, and that there can be no knowing without you.
