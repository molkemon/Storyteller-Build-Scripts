A tributary mission is more than a journey to pay respect to our lord, it is also an opportunity for trade with our government. Seeking to capitalize on this, it seems competing noble houses in a neighbouring country have taken to the habit of sending their own tributary missions to us, claiming to represent their ruler in order to gain advantages for themselves in their country’s internal politics. This time, however, two such missions arrived at the same time in a local province, which made their conflicts from home erupt into bloodshed.
