A tributary mission is more than a journey to pay respect to the overlord; it is also an opportunity for trade with the government of the realm. Seeking to capitalize on this, it seems competing noble houses in the country have taken to the habit of sending their own tributary missions to us, claiming to represent the monarch in order to gain advantages for themselves in the internal politics of the country. This time, however, two such missions arrived at the same time in the province, which made their conflicts from home erupt into bloodshed.
