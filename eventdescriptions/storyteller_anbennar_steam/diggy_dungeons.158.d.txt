The discussions and arguments slowly escalated into small brawls over particularly large and heavy coins. Even with people trying to calm down others, nothing seemed to really work. Only after the booming voice of the expedition leader echoed through the room did people realize what was happening. One by one, they began to calm down and began loading the coffers with the gold. They would still have a bad temper for the next few days, but eventually the horrors of the gold were behind them.
