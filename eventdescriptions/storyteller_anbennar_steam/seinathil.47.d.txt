An important theme in the Passionate Period of music is a new preoccupation with and surrender to nature. No musician has encapsulated this idea better than Caenlin Woodsong, an ork hailing from the far end of the Deepwoods, who recently immigrated to our nation and has been writing fantastic pieces on the eternal strength of nature. The calm but emotional pieces have brought upon a new side to our country, and leave many looking towards more peaceful stable times.
