Traditionally, the Kings of AzkaSur have had personal Sun Elf priests to accompany their prayers. Since the relocation to Sardazar, Ereleša Surzuir, the daughter of the last Elven King Erlian, who was born after his death, has served the Monarchs dutifully. But a scandal has occurred. an affair between the priestess and the current monarch has produced a bastard son, an abomination for all the faithful. The boy was hidden away for a time, but the monarch has had a change of mind and has not only brought young Erlian to court but made the boy his official heir, with Ereleša now his consort.
