With the surprising arrival of the foreign ships from the far east, many have discussed what should be done. There are those who suggested that we invite these foreigners to a banquet, hold a traditional autumn feast for them to gauge their intentions. Still, some are more interested in their advanced ships and tools, and suggested that we acquire some of their knowledge. There are even some who suggest that they are a threat to us, and must be dealt with. They turn to you for your decision.
