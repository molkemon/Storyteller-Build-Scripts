In ancient times, records show that many of the Khet would, in times of conflict, settle their disputes not through combat, thus risking their immortal lives, but through an ancient game of strategy. This game, involving enchanted stone tablets imbued with the images of fearsome monsters, would often take the place of more violent forms of dueling. With modern artificery, this ancient game need no longer be limited to just the Khet and their ancient stones. By using modern enchanting techniques on simple cards, we can replicate this game on a large scale for the masses. Not only will this help to keep our more important citizens alive and loyal, rather than dying in pointless physical altercations, but it shall surely keep our populace more peaceful. After all, why would they fight when they can instead engage in this far more entertaining and far safer method of solving disputes?
