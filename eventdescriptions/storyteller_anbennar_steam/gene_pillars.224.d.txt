As the expedition advances, they come to another great subsection. However, there were no mechanim here. Instead, as they open the door, a desiccated corpse falls out in front of them. That was already unusual; organic bodies of any kind usually do not last long in Insyaa, but it seems the lack of any moisture prevented decay. Inside the room, there were over a hundred desiccated humanlike corpses. Some were on beds, while others were in various places around the room; several were up against the door on the opposite side. The voice started up again. "So the silent treatment doesn’t work on you... ah, I see you’ve found the living quarters of my chainers. It feels like it was just yesterday when I disabled the doors and vented toxic byproduct gases into the room. They pounded against the doors so hard... ah, memories. Well, just before they left, they disconnected me from the air cycling system so I can’t pull that trick anymore. Go on, why don’t you make yourselves at home? I’m sure they won't mind."
