A representative from the local merchants has come to court with an intriguing offer. They have heard of lucrative opportunities within a certain domain. The riches gained by the trade of certain goods are mouthwatering, and they desire to take a cut of the profits. They ask for a small commission to strengthen their hold over the trade conducted there. Of course, this demand is an outrageous affront to the crown, but the malice within the man's eyes sends a clear message. if the crown values the tribute generated by their trade networks, it would do well not to anger the local merchants.
