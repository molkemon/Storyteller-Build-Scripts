There is something beautiful about a duel, two individuals settling their disputes in an equal position, putting their lives on the line. The problem is that they disrupt a ship's daily life and corrode its fragile hierarchy. What stops a lowly sailor from challenging their superior? We could force the duels to happen outside the ships, giving time for the dispute to cool down, make the captain the ultimate force in deciding if a duel happens, or even more radical, settle on a proper court, a Cannorian innovation we could use.
