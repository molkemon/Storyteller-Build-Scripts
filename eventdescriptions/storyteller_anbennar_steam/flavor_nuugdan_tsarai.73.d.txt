One of the more recent additions to the festival is the Hariigegh, where the rulers of every independent clan meet in a beautiful tent to eat great food and discuss matters of importance. The lord of Nuugdan Tsarai and the lord of Tuiista Jaal have been discussing the future of the Tuiista Jaal clan for quite some time now. The lord has requested our support conquering greener pastures for them. The people of Tuiista Jaal wish for better grazing lands for their livestock, and wish to wage war for the city of Jincheng and all the fertile land that lays before it. Should we promise them support, we can expect significant military cooperation from them, as they not only ready their military but indeed their entire population. To cement the deal, the lord offers a close kin’s hand in marriage, should the lord desire it.
