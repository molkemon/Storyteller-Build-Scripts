Despite this, visitors from Rubyhold and Silverforge are relatively common, so the arrival of a dwarf known as Grun Strongbellow did not initially receive much attention. That quickly changed after he secured an audience with the lord and presented a possible solution to our holds flooded lower levels, a problem that has long plagued us. Though many of our engineers regarded his ideas with some initial skepticism, Strongbellows willingness to share and seek input on his blueprints have won him the admiration of our artisan class. Theres some risk to it, but it seems that his plans might just work.
