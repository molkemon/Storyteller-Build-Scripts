The large stone forest cavern leads into several smaller caves, most of which are dead ends. One entrance, however, appears to have been an open trail even before the forest was turned to stone, and initial scouting has not found a dead end yet. As a new expedition follows the trail, they soon come to a divergence in the path. The actual trail appears to move downwards, following a treacherous decline. A second path, however, appears in the form of a narrow opening between two stone tree trunks embedded in the cave wall.
