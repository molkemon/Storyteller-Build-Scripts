Ancient traditions are not forgotten in one day. It is a long, complicated process, which requires a lot of patience and effort. Unfortunately, we have neither of those. Artug Ostokhan, chief of the most powerful clan in Khozrugan, has arrived in the palace of our ruler and challenged him to an Ozarm'Chadash, a duel to the death between the ruler and whomever sees him as unfit for reigning over a clan or country. This is a true orkish challenge, one that can be solved only on the battlefield. If our ruler rejects the duel, he and his relatives will be ashamed for eternity, and Artug's claims will be proven. Combat is the only solution.
