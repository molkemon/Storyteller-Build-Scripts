It is complete. This book; henceforth referred to as the Blessed Book; has been completed. The scriptorium of Poppyspring shall take this draft and copy it into a manuscript, and then that manuscript shall be sent to the presses. Copies, thousands in number, shall be printed, and they shall be taken back to their communities by messengers. Every goodman shall be able to look to their faith for instruction and guidance, as it was always meant to be. Only one step left to take. The other writers have signed their name on the final page. The Crimson Reverend, a lord, picks up a quill, dips it in ink, and signs.
