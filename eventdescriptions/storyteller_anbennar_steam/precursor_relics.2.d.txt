Our historians estimate that the Precursor Elves must have held the world for thousands of years. And yet, it seems that simply wasn't enough time to leave the amount of relics that the world now clamors for. The ruin in that province has proved bountiful and illuminating. Our crew on the ground has brought us concerning news, however. The site is growing barer by the day, our digs moving further and further from the exhausted abundance of the site's center. This isn't the end, but we can hear rumblings that the site may soon be exhausted.
