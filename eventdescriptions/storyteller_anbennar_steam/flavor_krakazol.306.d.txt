The next batch of drinks has been served. Dwarves are guzzling ale like they are being paid for it, jeering one another on to keep going. The halls are filled with laughter, song, and the odd brawl here and there, and it is altogether quite the merry and lively occasion. The monarch is on his throne with a flagoff of the purest ale. It is his duty, nay, his honor to drink with his people, and drink he shall. The monarch faces all the eyes of the great hall aimed at him, speculating whether or not he will lie through it.
