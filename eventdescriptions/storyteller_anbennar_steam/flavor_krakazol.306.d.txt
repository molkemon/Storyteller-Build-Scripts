The next batch of drinks was served. Dwarves guzzle ale like they’re being pitted for it, jeering one another on to keep going. The halls are filled with laughter, song, and the odd howl here and there, and it is altogether quite a merry and lively occasion. The lord sat on his throne with a flagon of the purest ale. It is his duty, indeed, his honor to drink with his people, and drink he shall. The lord feels all the eyes of the great hall aimed at him, speculating whether or not he will finish it.
