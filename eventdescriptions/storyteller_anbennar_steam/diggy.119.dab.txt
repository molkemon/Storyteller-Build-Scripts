The dwarves of Krakdhûmvror have dug deep and uncovered many lost regions of the hold. Designed to take advantage of the biting cold, these lost forges have begun to produce intricate works of quartz and steel. This imitation of the ancient hold has not gone unnoticed by its population; even now, the snowflakebound anvil of Krakdhûmvror is flown alongside our own banners. It seems the hold is beginning to consider itself a nation, something we may have to deal with.
