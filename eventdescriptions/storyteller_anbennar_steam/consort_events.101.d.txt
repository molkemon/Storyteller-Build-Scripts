It is no news that the consort has no fond feelings for the monarch's heir, the heir of the dynasty. Ever since they arrived at court, the friction between the two has been obvious, and it has only gotten worse since the monarch's first child was born. Of course, they would much rather see their own progeny on the throne. For quite some time now, they have silently been pulling the strings to make it happen, convincing or bribing the right people and grooming their eldest at the best of their abilities. However, it was not until yesterday that they confronted the monarch and demanded that it was time for a new heir to be appointed.
