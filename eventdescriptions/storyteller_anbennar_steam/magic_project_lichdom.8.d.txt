A man's home is his castle, and a lich's ritual chamber is his home. It should be decorated as such. Lich King Xamarzu, as every good mage knows, preparation is half the battle. The dark ritual of lichdom is a dangerous undertaking, and the ritual chamber must be prepared accordingly, with several overlapping magical circles, enchanted ornaments, and an appropriately ornate pedestal on which to place the phylactery.
