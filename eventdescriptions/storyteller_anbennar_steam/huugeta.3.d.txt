Accompanied by the cacophony of rattling wheels and clanking chains, a slaver's caravan arrives in the domain. Through repeated cracks of the whip, the Yetmesirans line up their goods for the sergeant. "A hardy bunch of Dasmati is what I have for you. I'm told they put up quite the fight against their captors, so I expect a proper price for them." In return, the sergeant gestures at two towering figures behind him, their skin generously battlemarked and their eyes steely. "These two proved a great investment. They did a lot of fine work against the trolls since we bought them from you. Accomplished warriors now, so I think that tips the scales in my favour." The fleshpeddler smirks. "And yet, you're looking to sell, not keep them. That tells me all I need to know."
