Conthus warned the party beforehand of how his brilliant mind engineered some defenses that break the very laws of reality itself, and it seems as if they finally discovered what he meant. A vast chasm spanned by a single thin walkway stands in the path of the expedition, and somehow the water within the chamber has begun flowing sideways across the bridge, threatening to sweep any who try to cross into the chasm below. The Precursor did note that it was possible to cross, but it is clear that one wrong step will inevitably cause whoever tries to pass to be swept into the darkness below. Care must be taken to ultimately ensure the survival of the party.
