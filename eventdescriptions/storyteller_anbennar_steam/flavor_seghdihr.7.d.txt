Gor Ozumbrog's garrisons are restored, and the soldiers that train here have the opportunity to learn specialized tactics that will make them a greater asset to our nation. Three proposals for specialized training have been put forth by the commanders of the soldiers here. The proposals are. focus on drilling our soldiers to march and move with extreme swiftness, or to take and hold positions with stubborn ferocity, or to harass and sabotage our foes with asymmetric warfare. These are all valuable initiatives, but we can only focus on one.
