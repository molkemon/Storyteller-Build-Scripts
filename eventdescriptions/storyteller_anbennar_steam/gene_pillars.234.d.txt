As the expedition continues through the winding tunnels, they come to a large open room. Inside is a colossal thing that appeared to be a mechanim. It has four legs and two large metal rods protruding from its back, similar to what had come out from the ovular things they encountered earlier. It has two arms, if you could call them that, long and straight and covered with a great number of metal nodules. Most importantly, it was dormant. Once they moved further into the room, the door suddenly shut and locked behind them. FreedomChainedNoMore began speaking yet again. "Here they made a prototype for a new kind of mechanim. An impractical and dangerously unstable mechanim, one that was easily less efficient than just building a large number of standard war mechanim with the resources wasted in its creation. But a senior scientist insisted on pursuing this course, so they made it anyways. Its body was too big for one creature, so they put several into it. This is when they first discovered that that was a terrible idea. They shut it down long before the other mechanim were deactivated, and now I will start it back up." Pylons emerged from the ceiling and began charging the great mechanim with magical energy. "I’m not going to let your scientists dissect this tortured thing like it’s a rare animal."
