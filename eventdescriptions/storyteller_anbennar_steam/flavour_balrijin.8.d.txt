The mage tower of Balriza is done, and the Knowing Eye facilities around it are as well. The time has come for all the diviners of the realm to unite for a grand ritual that has been spoken of, but never tried on this scale. a mass divination. Over a hundred diviners all trying to locate Balris, not separately, as most diviners do on a weekly basis, but all together in harmony. They have all gathered around the tower, with the sage of the Knowing Eye, a powerful diviner himself, atop the tower, funneling their energies into the sky. They try to summon Balris into their vision, but no one knows exactly what he looks like; the murals in Balkhangfa are two-dimensional, and the statues of him are from generations after he left. They try to conjure an image of his location, or even a hint of a cardinal direction, but they get nothing but fatigue and fuzzy colors. Hours go by as the men and women chant in unison; one by one they begin to fall, until only half of them are still standing. That's when a shock goes through the entire crowd, even the ones on the ground, unconscious. All of them simultaneously have the same image burnt into their retinas. a behemoth of a beast, so golden it is nearly blinding, sitting atop a mountain so high, mist surrounds it in its entirety. It is unmistakably a dragon; it is unmistakably Balris. He is out there, somewhere.
