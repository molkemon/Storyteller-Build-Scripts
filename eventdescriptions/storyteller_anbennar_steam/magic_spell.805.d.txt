By utilizing the wishblades, relics remaining from the times of djinn rule that are currently in the possession of the nobility and magi clans within our realm, we can reshape reality to better suit our needs. While not all wishblades possess powers suited to reshaping the natural world around us, many can be used to turn chaos into order. All that is required is a handler familiar with the wishblade and a sufficient source of magical power; within the boundaries of Surael's Garden, we have access to plenty of both. The only choice left is to decide on which part of the domain we should focus their power on?
