Hear ye, hear ye. Hark, for it is I, Zott Goldgrabber, reporting live with GobliNN. A string of assassinations have just struck our fair hold; rich landowners and industrial magnates were found poisoned last night. I am here at the Cloak and Swagger branch headquarters with an official who may be able to shed some light on this issue. The newsgoblin pauses, snickering to himself as a thought crosses his mind. A departure from shedding blood, no doubt. The Cloak and Swagger official, a rather gaunt goblin in some unnecessarily dashing leather, appears unamused. He leans forward, taking the technomegaphone from Goldgrabber, and draws in a deep breath before he speaks. The wealthy had it coming.
