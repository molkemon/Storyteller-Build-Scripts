To our east lay only endless tracks of sand and ruins, worthless wasteland of no consequence to the ambitions of our lord. Yet, a power is growing now in the east that can no longer be ignored. Our nominal ally Zokka has grown far too mighty far too quickly, subjugating all of the Far Salahad to their ambitions. While they were embroiled in conflict with the Salahedesi nomads they were a worthwhile pawn, but now they have grown too ambitious for our purposes. Worse, they threaten to burn all of Bulwar in senseless violence and bloodshed, destroying it all in order to satisfy their own pride. If we are to subjugate the Bulwari, it is clear that we will need to subjugate Zokka’s forces first.
