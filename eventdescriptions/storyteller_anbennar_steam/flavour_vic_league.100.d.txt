The delegate from Coldmarket stood at the speaker’s podium and began to read the introduction to the final motion aloud. In the spirit of amity, commerce, and fraternity between all peoples of the Blue Reach, the duly appointed delegates of the Vics of the Blue Reach, together composing a full and united Council of the Blue Reach, have assembled in the compromise region on this day to strengthen the mutually desirous ties between its Vics. Such ties are an innate and desirable consequence of cultural proximity and unified interests both mercantile and diplomatic across the Alenic Reach, Gerudia, and the Dragon Coast. The Council of the Blue Reach resolves that the strengthening of these aforementioned ties can and should take the form of a United Reach. In the spirit of recognition of the unique circumstances and unique identity of each of the members of the Council of the Blue Reach, the Council further resolves that the United Reach shall allow the independent operation of its members in a way consistent with Vic League precedent, itself descended from the traditions of the Ragni Federation of the Blue Reach. In the spirit of solidarity in the face of unprovoked and unjustified aggression from the enemies of the Blue Reach, especially from the Kingdom of Gawed, the delegate from Coldmarket paused to let the jeers and hisses die down. The Council of the Blue Reach puts forth this final, binding, and necessary motion to unite its members into a free and eternal Ragnian Commonwealth.
