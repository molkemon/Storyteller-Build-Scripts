The way ahead is becoming harder and harder to traverse. There have been several rooms that the team could not physically enter due to collapsed ceilings, and in one place some sort of seismic activity has loosed a landslide from the surrounding cliff, breaching the structure and blocking the corridor completely. At one such barrier, one of your colleagues noticed a slight gap close to the ceiling and has spent the past few minutes attempting to widen it to see if progress is possible. After managing to get their head and torso through, they cry out, "You need to see this." They finish wriggling their body through and you follow. The passage is tight, and claustrophobia sets in shortly before you reach the end, where the blockage must have fallen from. You pull your body through and find yourself in a massive open space. It looks like it may be as large as half the pillar by area and would have once been maybe four to five floors tall. You can see the remains of several of the areas where floors would have been mounted at the edges of the structure, but something caused the floors to collapse down onto the area you had been trying to explore. Above the damage, however, something still hangs. It appears to be a large reinforced edifice, braced with large metal struts to the edge. There appears to be no way to reach it from your current location, and even if there were a way there is no possibility to breach it from where the missing floors were.
