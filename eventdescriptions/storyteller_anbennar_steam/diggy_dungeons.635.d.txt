After you spend hours attempting to repair various systems in the control area of the structure, you finally feel confident enough to power up the entire system once again. The scholars in your expedition in the meantime found out that a reboot of the entire pump station is necessary for the rest of the systems to function properly again and provide the rest of the workshop with sufficient electricity. You gather everyone and venture towards the pump station near the bottom of the complex. You barely even reach the entrance of the corresponding area when your scouts warn you about the sighting of a giant water elemental roaming the mostly flooded corridors.
