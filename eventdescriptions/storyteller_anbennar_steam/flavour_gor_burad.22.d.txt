Forcing the Diamond Dwarves to share the pain we felt during the Eruption was a balm to the collective soul of the Basalt Dwarves. However, the Steam Barons realised that there was another benefit: that the lava now flowing within Argordstun can be harnessed to improve the Hold even further. As such, they propose that we repeat the process in a more controlled manner, to see if we can replicate these effects in the remaining Serpentreach Holds. The lord swiftly agrees, and knows exactly which Hold should be the target of this experimentation: Ovdal Lodhum. While Argordstun was the principal force behind Gor Burad being abandoned to the orkish hordes, we shall now pay the Garnet Dwarves one last consequence for the failures of their ancestors and in the process bring benefits to our other Holds.
