The lord. Exploring around the digging site in the province our miners have found something amazing, simply spectacular. By the guidance of the earth mother, or by pure luck, we came across a great cavern with a great spire in the middle. The mages in our midst were immediately excited, pointing out that such a wonderful formation would make an excellent foundation for an earthwork tower. We left some of them to survey, prepare, and hire workers; the tower has been finished and is now fully operational.
