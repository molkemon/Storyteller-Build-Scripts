After years of tearing each other apart, a semblance of unity seems to have brought the elves of Aelnar together once more. As the lord approaches the royal balcony, overlooking the royal square, they express themselves, looking stern but sad: We understood each other. Upon hearing this seemingly simple statement, all those assembled shed tears. From the various representatives of the factions to the inhabitants of the city and the peasants of the countryside, from the veterans of the war to the saints and the sinners, everyone shed tears. The news of an assembly of the country’s greatest living minds spread rapidly; from this assembly emanated a series of reforms which greatly improved the unity of the people and opened the door for further, deeper, and even more effective reforms.
