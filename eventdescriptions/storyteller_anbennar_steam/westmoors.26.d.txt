The highlands north of the Westmoors rose high into the air, rivaled only by the Dragonheights. Towards the top of the heights stood a cave, and just outside, perched comically, was a table and a matching set of chairs. There the monarch sat, as a procession of small green creatures crawled forth from the cave, led by a slightly taller kobold by the name of Korlak, chin raised high in pride. Sitting at the table, the kobold lord huffed. "It brings me no pleasure to be here today. But though we have resisted your people for a thousand years, we know better than anyone else when it's time to change strategy." The kobold, somehow, produced a parchment, presumably seized from somewhere. On it was a crude copy of a seal, along with scrawling writing that refused to stay in line. "I believe this is how your people swear oaths, right?"
