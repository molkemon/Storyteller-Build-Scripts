With the death of Brasur, the throne of Frozenmaw now passes to his son, a halfork. Already, it seems that the chieftains and lords of the realm seek to bring him on their side, for as soon as he has ascended to the throne, he has a vital choice to make; shall he embrace those reformist chiefs and human lords, seeking to transform the lands of Frozenmaw into a land of peaceful cooperation between orks and humans? Or shall he bow to the will of the hardliners, establishing a nation for orks, and orks alone. Today, the legacy of the Gray Kings will be defined, but it is only he who can choose what that legacy shall be.
