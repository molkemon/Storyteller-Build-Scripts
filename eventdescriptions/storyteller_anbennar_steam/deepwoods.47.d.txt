There are places in the Bone Grove where time slips. Where, or really when, the past becomes the present. In those moments, long extinct beasts can be seen roaming the glades. Behind them, in silence, great warriors long forgotten stalk their prey. Such events are usually fleeting, playing out without witness. But in our province, locals have been reporting seeing such scenes on the daily. Who knows how long these scenes will last, but it seems we have a chance to capitalize on this. On one side, our generals believe our recruits could learn a thing or two from these past apparitions. On the other hand, a minister has suggested sending someone from the Order of Chroniclers to record this occurrence.
