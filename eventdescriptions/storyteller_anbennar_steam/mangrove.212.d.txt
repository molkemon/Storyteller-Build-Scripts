As a general rule, poetry does not have rules. The value of the art form is in the artistic expression afforded to the author when composing it, and that includes spelling, grammar, word spacing, and all other parts of the writing process. But when an author can make poetry even with all the rules of language, that is truly a rare and beautiful thing. One such person has taken up the mantle of chronicling for us here, and we have come to respect him greatly for his accuracy, thoroughness, and art.
