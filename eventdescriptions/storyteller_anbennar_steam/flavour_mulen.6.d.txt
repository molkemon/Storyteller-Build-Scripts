Scouts with the most flying speed and endurance were selected, to be protected by soldiers with the greatest experience with fighting centaurs, for our expedition to the Forbidden plains. No feat like this has been attempted in all of our history, but with Mulen’s expanding might we are now confident that the centaurs can be overcome. The scouting expedition shall find out what lies within and beyond the Forbidden Plains, and if any of it could be of value to our government.
