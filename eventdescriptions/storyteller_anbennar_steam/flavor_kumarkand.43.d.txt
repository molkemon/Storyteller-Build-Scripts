O Surakel, our Lord. Your first Empire—Your servants of Karqašlu—have returned. We prostrate ourselves in service to You for the first time in far too long. Though centuries passed in which, though still blessed by Your light, we remained shrouded in delusions, You did not forsake Your people. You did not permit us to forget what You had bestowed on us so long ago. It is thanks to Your blessings, Lord—not for any merit of our own—that we have now remembered our past and the elevated and correct rites You bestowed upon us. O exalted Surakel. May we never falter in our duty to You.
