At first, the ruler thinks this is one of their more boring dreams. A single hue, filling their vision, with the occasional shift of tone or light but little else. A lovely hue, orangeyellow, but a monochromatic vision is not what the ruler expects when they lay down to slumber. Then, finally, motion. A moving out, a moving away. Finally, there is more than just the colour, and it is put in context. There is a sea of colour, in a vast chamber. Further out, faster. The sea is a size to boggle the mind. And there, at a corner of the chamber, are those dwarves? Quickly, the vision approaches them. In an accented tongue, the ruler makes out only a handful of words; "expanding the Citadel Delve"; before they snaps awake. For a few more moments, the ruler lays in bed. They has seen what must be the inside of Verkal Gulan, its amassed fortune. And the ruler knows they may never see a sight to rival its wealth again, for all the years remaining.
