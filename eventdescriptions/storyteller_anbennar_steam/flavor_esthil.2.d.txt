Canrec the Defiler, acolyte of the SorcererKing and architect of the White Pestilence. While the name might strike fear or anger into some, for Varina, it held neither. A certain amount of healthy respect, of course, but in truth, she would have loved to have met the man in his time.  Instead, she has settled for reading his works. Copied by the furtive hands of acolytes and poseurs who wished to emulate him or keep his knowledge in circulation, pages and documents written by the man have survived in secret places. But while the collection she holds in her hands now is great, perhaps greater then any before, they are not enough. Key texts are missing, and the only place in the world they might rest is in the original copy of Canrec's biography: the . In the hands of the Orda Aldresia who defeated Canrec so long ago.  The only way forward is to take what they have locked away.
