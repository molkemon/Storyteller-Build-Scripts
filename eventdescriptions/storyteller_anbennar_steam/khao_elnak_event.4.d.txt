Everything was supposed to go smoothly. The courts of Thirabnir were not easy to infiltrate, but where money could not crack the gaps, the natural abilities of our spies made their way through. The poison was served unnoticed to the lord's table at his usual dinner time. It took time for the effects to come up, but soon the news arrived. Narai Chaiya was dead, and it was time to elect a new lord recommended in his suddenly materialized will. Or so we thought, as Thirabnir elected a local instead of the former monarch.
