You have finally located what you believe to be a laboratory complex, and your team readies to breach the sealed doorway. Questions abound as to the purpose of this structure. Legends suggest that those who occupied these towers used them for dread magics and forbidden studies. Unease has started creeping over the expedition, as they all ask, "What were they studying here?". The collection of relics and notes starts to lead to a dark conclusion. Involuntary sounds of distress escape the team, as opening the door reveals kobolds in every stage of lifecycle development enclosed in glass tanks. Steeling their nerves, the team ventures in to get a better look at what is inside. This is a room of nightmares; the forms in the tanks are not just varied in cycle of life. Rather, some have major deformities or damage, as if some foul magic has twisted them and sculpted their forms into unnatural states. What kind of evil mind ran this place?
