You duck into a small guard room and see several automatons spaced evenly around the perimeter; heavily armed but dormant. Once convinced they look safe, you start to investigate the room for other potential exits or even the possibility of loot. As you cross the room, a small collection of gems catches your eye on a small podium near some of the figures. Carefully checking for traps, you start to approach. However, despite your diligence, you must have missed something, and you hear the guardians whir to life behind you. The first blow lands as you start to turn, knocking one of your colleagues to the ground. The automaton seems to be fighting with a fury and the combat is frantic. After a few minutes your team finally subdues the last guardian and checks on each other. The last had been knocked to the floor but was still attempting to drag itself over towards the kobolds of your group. A few members of the expedition had been knocked to the ground, but on inspection all the injuries appear to have been to disable and incapacitate, not to kill. This is a disturbing development, and after getting your colleagues back to their feet you want to swiftly leave this room.
