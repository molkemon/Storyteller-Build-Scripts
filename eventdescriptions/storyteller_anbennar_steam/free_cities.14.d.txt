There have been many leagues of mutual cooperation within Cannor. The League of Celmaldor, for example, was a league of merchant guilds in a commercial and defensive confederation, or the Eight Sisters which was a mutual defense and peacekeeping association. Some leagues become so powerful and united that they are even viewed as a state in themselves, like the Cerulean League. The free city of another country has suggested that we join something similar, a cooperation of sorts, where we collaborate in matters of trade, keeping imperial peace, and supporting tribunals.
