Has the creature left us? It is hard to say whether its physical form is still among us, but in our dreams its eye glitters and gleams with a taunting glint that startles even the most experienced adventurers awake and sends a shiver over their back. They ramble on about the Black Door, the Apotheosis of Worms, and most concerning of all, they seem to recall events from the future, predicting our demise in gruesome detail.
