While researching possible additives for our potions, our researchers found two main avenues of improving our potions.  On one hand, we could try to add rare spices which, as well as possibly boosting the potions efficiency, will also make them less repulsive to its consumers.  On the other hand, Coffee has been growing in popularity as of late, and its invigorating effects are nothing to scoff at. Adding some concentrated coffee beans, as well as masking the potion's flavor, may also give our mages the energy boost they need to keep going after tough spells.
