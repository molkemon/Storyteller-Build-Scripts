While researching possible additives for our potions, our researchers found two main avenues of improving them. On one hand, we could try to add rare spices which, as well as possibly boosting the potions' efficiency, will also make them less repulsive to their consumers. On the other hand, coffee has been growing in popularity as of late, and its invigorating effects are nothing to scoff at. Adding some concentrated coffee beans, as well as masking the potion's flavor, may also give our mages the energy boost they need to keep going after tough spells.
