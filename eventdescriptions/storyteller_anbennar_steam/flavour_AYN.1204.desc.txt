Ardimya was both the name of our homeland and the name of our Empress. She was a djinn who disappeared with the rest of her exalted kin during the Dissipation, when all the genies on Halann, some of whom had ruled over the lands they had claimed for millennia, all disappeared in a matter of months. After our beloved Empress vanished, her Sultanate was no more, yet our ancestors did not give in to despair. Instead, they founded the Jinnakah Vizierate on the ruins of Ardimya’s Sultanate, a most noble endeavor founded on the desperate hope that she might one day return to us. Thus these lands, now called the Jasiir Doog, have been given to us as their stewards. Yet to our everlasting regret, they have remained ravaged and in utter disarray for far too many centuries. Our tribes have been scattered ever since the Jinnakah Vizierate fell almost eight hundred years ago, due to the last remnants of a once elaborate Geniewrought irrigation network collapsing, leaving its former subjects alone to wander the vast former territories. In doing so, our forebears unwittingly mirrored the exodus of our distant ancestors, who followed Ardimya in the Great Procession out of another wartorn land of sand and prosperity to these promised lands so many millennia ago. However, our homeland, once seen as the salvation for our people, has been plagued by the unrelenting threat of the Qeyikul. These dangerous creatures from the Infernal plane were summoned long ago by treacherous sons of Ardimya, and with their unrelenting attacks have contributed to the downfall of the Jinnakah Vizierate. While most gifts of the genies were lost just as they themselves disappeared, their relics, some still imbued with their power, lay in wait for us to find. Hidden deep within the sands of the Jasiir Doog lay relics, most as useless as slag iron, but some as rare and as powerful as a dame's tear.
