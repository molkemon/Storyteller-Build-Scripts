A powerful man in the local society has sent an ultimatum to the capital. He is demanding one of our citizens, whom he believes to be the murderer of his father, be turned over to him, so the supposed killer may be punished in accordance with local customs. If the "murderer" is not turned over within three months, he has promised that a great war host will descend upon our colony, seeking their retribution through blood. The citizen accused has denied all responsibility, claiming instead that the local chieftain killed his own father in a bid for power, then blamed him for it to avoid being seen as a kinslayer.
