In our aimless wanderings through our verdant home, we have encountered another tribe of beings like us yet unlike us at the same time. They call themselves the Sa'paomi. They tell tales of encounters with the Loa'Set, without whose help they would have perished in the jungle long ago. And they have proven themselves to be adept pupils, constantly innovating new tools and reinventing themselves to better adapt to their surroundings; they truly are a marvel of adaptation.
