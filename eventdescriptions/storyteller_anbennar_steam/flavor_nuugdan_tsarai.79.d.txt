The land of Yanshen is ours, but its people sometimes do not see us as their rightful masters. As such it is imperative that the many regions of our realm are administered locally by trustworthy officials. In tradition with ancient code, the land shall be carved up into a new clan structure, which will placate the many clans of old whose leaders have now been uplifted into the nobility. These clans form local administrations, collect taxes and suppress revolts. The land will be divided according to inheritance, history, ancient claims and participation in recent conquests. For example: Odheongu and the lands directly west of it shall fall under the authority of the Fanwang of the Rung Oedek family. There are countless clans, but every major clan is already sending representatives to our capital to stake their claim.
