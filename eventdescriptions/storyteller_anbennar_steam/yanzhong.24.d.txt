It is often said that even one blessed with foresight would struggle to predict the trends of the markets. At unknown whims, certain industries find themselves swamped with demand; while other times the value of goods plummets for reasons barely discernible. Today we receive news of the former, fortunately. Recent fashion trends in a realm have seen a growing number of merchants traveling our lands, eager to supply those new desires. The trade they bring is already giving our coffers a surplus.
