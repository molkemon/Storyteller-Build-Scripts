Normally, the unnatural forces that resurrect a zombie cause no change in the creature's body, but there are exceptions to this rule. In some circumstances that are still poorly understood, the White Pestilence can cause major changes to occur in the bodies that it reanimates, morphing the corpses and turning them into something else. One such mutation causes the affected corpse to become as strong as an elephant and its skin hard as stone, its rippling musculature splitting the skin and allowing it to move far faster than its shambling fellows. Though thankfully quite rare, one of these monsters recently appeared in our realm, slaughtering countless civilians and militia.
