When we sent a small force into Pakpon Haitri, we found little in the way of the Devouring Path; any malefactors melted away like smoke in the wind rather than confront our nation. The situation seemed to quiet down, enough that other priorities took precedence. But that did not last. Today, a massive force of what can only be Devouring Path practitioners emerged from some hidden school, slaying the local magistrates and wreaking terror on the inhabitants. The deal made with Dieu Xau is ended, and so too is our patience; if they seek to be foes of our nation, we will show them just how we deal with such. Whatever remains when our cannons are through will be brought closely in hand, such that this moral and spiritual decay never happens again.
