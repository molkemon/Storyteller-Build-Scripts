Casteless is usually an insult to someone who is unable to fit into our society, those who can't or don't want to, causing issues all over. But over the past few years, this has started to change, as the number of halforks started to rise across our lands. Humans, harimari, dwarves, harpies, and even orks had spots inside the caste system, usually put where their natural skills shine the most, but the halforks were basically a new race on the rise, a race outside the caste system. Unlike the orks that sit on the lower caste thanks to their raw physical might, halforks, who also swarm the lower caste along with their parental people, and started to outnumber them too, and unlike the orks and their natural aggression, are much more refined and tactical thanks to their human side. In fact, they do make some excellent officers thanks to their combined traits, but because of the caste system, they are not allowed to become officers. Perhaps it is time for some change? Should we embrace this and adapt our tactics to fit into a halforkish military?
