Ever since the newcomer appeared at court, they have been a mystery to our own elite class. Nobody knows their name nor has been able to find information about this secretive person. That is until today. It turns out, this person is none other than the banished heir to a foreign throne itself. They are one of the royal family of that dynasty and were banished in order for the current monarch to assume the throne. The dynast has expressed no interest in the throne and transferred any kind of claim they had to our lord as a thanks for our hospitality.
