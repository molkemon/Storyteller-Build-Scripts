The disloyal naga have begun to inflame ethnic tensions wherever they can find them. Their most recent act of treason concerns our Rhada subjects: the naga have been flocking to Odu Kherass in ever greater numbers in an effort to convince the Rhada that the prophecies are on their side. They seek to amass their numbers where they are greatest, and then strike us down. They seek to prematurely birth the three hundred thirty-third Empire. Even the illiterates who would dare to stand against the Anzara Empire know that there is meant to be an empire after us. The naga who cannot be bothered to assume a useful role on behalf of the state have whispered enough poison into the ears of their peasant marionettes to convince them that their rebellion marks the rise of the three hundred thirty-third Empire. There are two ways in which we might respond. We could acquiesce to the naga and leave it to them to extricate themselves from their promises when their Final Empire of storied prophecy amounts merely to slightly lowered taxes, or we could send the army to deal forcefully with the naga, the Rhada, and anyone else who happens to be in their vicinity.
