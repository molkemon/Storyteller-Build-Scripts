There is betrayal within the highest ranks. The highest echelon of the military, blinded by the prospect of career advancement, runs the risk of acting contrary to our needs. Their distrust of Lihos Maidens is well known, and has managed to perpetuate itself between generations of leaders. As the Maidens grow more influential in the government, the prospect of a disloyal organ of state is unfathomably risky. If we are not careful, they might think to challenge the Maidens; considering their control of the military, they might well succeed.
As Huszien has asked us to create a modernized army, this presents us with an opportunity to follow her designs while still advancing our interests. As far as anyone knows, the officers leading our military have already tried to sabotage it. The punishment, as with all forms of treason, is death. We will harbor no traitors.
