The Vustarin and Kattyva seem like natural allies, with the former primarily working with precursor scrap, while the latter focus on digging it out of the dirt. However, there are still places where their interests clash more often than not, leading to much tension between the two families, such as the SteelScorched Citadel. Here, the Vustarin still maintain an interest in direct scavenging operations. Recently, the disagreements here have erupted into an outburst of violence, as a dispute over who had the right to excavate a certain section of the ruin escalated, leaving several dead. Now the two families beg the Mercenary King for intervention.
