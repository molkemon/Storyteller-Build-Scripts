Even we, made of metal, age. After my many years, I can feel my own body failing me. Though perhaps my end might not ever come, in truth, I can feel the effects of my many years of rule. My core, my heart, does not have the strength it once did. I've done my best to keep moving forward as the realm needs me, but it has been hard for me to save my energy while ruling. Should I seek to find a way to strengthen my core to keep ruling? Or is it perhaps best for me to retire, and let someone else continue in my place?
