Hold it right there, whelp. One step further and you’ll be out of our clan’s protection and into Dogeater’s hunting grounds. Never heard of them before? Not from around here, are ya? Simply put, the Dogeater clan cares not for honor. Nothing is too low for them; they eat dogs for Dookan’s sake. Every night I hear their screams and shouts as they endlessly brawl each other over the most minor squabbles. Not sure if they’re hollerin’ with glee or agony anymore, hell, it might be both for all I know. They claim to be a clan of the strong, yet all I see is disgraceful savagery. Step into their territory by just a margin, and droves of warg riders will ambush you and corner you into a fight. It’s how I lost my eye here, see? Barely escaped with the rest of my limbs intact. I must admit, a true ork cannot help but admire their brazen strength and questionable bravery. In fact, I have heard their chieftain dwarfs even the largest of our clan; rumors say he may have ogre blood in his veins. Many of his clan would even happily boast that his father killed Corin, that he was stronger than Korgus. Simple ravings of idiotic lunatics, I would say. If he was truly that strong, where was he in Castanoth? Where was he when Corin actually killed Korgus? Too busy fighting against his own men, apparently. But still, I cannot emphasize this enough. You are wargfeed if you choose to step any closer to those brutes.
