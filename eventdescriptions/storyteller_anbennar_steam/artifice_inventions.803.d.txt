For a thousand years, gnomes had to make do with very little space. From the tiny land grant of Giberd to the flooded swamps of Portnamm to the cramped islands of Nimscodd and the Splinters, we have rarely had much in the way of physical realestate. Despite this, we have always built metropolises and monuments, for we have longsince mastered the arts of dense, efficient, towering constructions. We have burrowed into the earth so as to clear extra storiees, stabilized our foundations into bedrock, and stacked our buildings with perfect geometries. With modern technology, our cityplanning ability has only improved; never before have we had access to massproduced iron, or steel, or glass. But, today, we take a glorious step into a new age of citybuilding.  First, massproduce lumber, beams, foundations, bricks, and more. Second, enchant them to assemble into particular forms when exposed to an automated Fabricate spell. Third, include magical glue as part of the fabrication process, to ensure that constructions stay stable. Fourth, pack all of these items into tiny little boxes of holding, which can be easily carried anywhere. Fifth, repeat this many, many, many times. Sixth, bring these boxes to an empty location and build an entire city from seemingly nothing.  With these Prefabricated City Constructors, we can construct entire cities ahead of time, according to set plans, and with perfect cohesion. We can now develop our lands like never before.
