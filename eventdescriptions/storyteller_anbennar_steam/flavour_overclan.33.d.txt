Our efforts to solidify our control over the Segbandal, though productive, have not been absolute. The Citrine dwarves still prove defiant, even if their kin in other domains have managed to serve as a counterbalance and moderating force. Those same kin have expressed their own agendas and interests, too, which have at times conflicted with one another and the state's own interests. As with other regions under our control, it is recommended that we appoint a Circuit Diwan to represent their interests on a national level. This office only requires your decision as to which candidate should be selected.
