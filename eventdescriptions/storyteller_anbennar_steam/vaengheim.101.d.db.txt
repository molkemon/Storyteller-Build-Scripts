It was in the days of a certain year when the monarch was queen, and their maidens did strike forth across the Giant's Grave, and brought fire and sword to the Reach, making subjects of those who bent knee to Vængheim, and slaying those who did not. Yet did the men of the Alen resist them, first in battle, and then in spirit. For the monarch was not loved by the reach, and they plotted against them, and sought their ruin. One day did a wingmaiden fly to Vængheim, and in their claws held a man named Godric, who said, "O queen, foul tidings do I bring. For I am a Reachman, and have pledged myself to your house. Yet do my kinsmen strike at me, and they have burned my home and taken from me herd, kith and kin. I come alone, but I speak with the tongues of many, who speak your name fondly, though they are shamed and slain by their neighbours." Then did the monarch rise, and said, "Have no fear Reachman, for when I took your people under my wing, I did not take them into thralldom. Your people are my people, and I will take arms to defend those who carry my peace in their hearts." And then the monarch gathered the maidens of Vængheim for war.
