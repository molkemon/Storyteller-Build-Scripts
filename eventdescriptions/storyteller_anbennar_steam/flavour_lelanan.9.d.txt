Her body lay before us, lifeless and so still. My second love, not moving. She was gone, not speaking, not laughing, no warmth, no breathing. Leli simply wept beside me. Two weeks since we were told of her passing, yet the pain never subsided. And upon this world, I felt now trespassing. I sniffled, held Leli tightly. I had to remain strong for us both. Gently, I rubbed her shaking back, thinking of Kyola’s Oath. We held no such power, no strength, nor beauty as she. And now I felt the weight of her absence, feeling how deeply she had changed us. How could I do without her? Without her late night reassurances? Her guiding confidence? Her endless love? How could I ever hope to make this world better as she did?
