The sleeper finds herself walking in a long corridor. It is a familiar walk, one she used to make multiple times a day, a long time ago. She's back in the high tower, the place where she learned her magic. But it isn't a dream about her youth, no, her wrinkles are all still here. She arrives at the end of the corridor to find the door leading to the great hall open to her. Inside, the room is full to the brim, many familiar faces; old friends and colleagues turned bitter enemies; and a lot of new ones. Every mage worth something is here.  She jolts awake. She knows what she has to do.
