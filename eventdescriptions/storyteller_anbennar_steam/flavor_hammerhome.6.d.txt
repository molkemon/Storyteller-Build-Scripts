It's a quiet afternoon in the Highforge. Various Balgarsmiths exit and enter the great building, bringing various crafts and minerals in and out. The roaring of magma across the forge and the sounds of hammers drown out any conversation within the building, a place truly dedicated to Balgar's spirit. But there is something different about this afternoon. A large crowd of humans, dwarves and even a few halflings have gathered around the Highforge's public area, all anticipating a great speech. "Friends, warriors, kin, I welcome you this day to the greatest temple of Balgar for a special celebration. With the realm firmly under dwarven leadership, today shall mark the day of the founding of the Balgaric Empire. A realm now truly up to Balgar's standards, we shall industrialize and bring prosperity to all of the domain. Glory to Balgar and glory to the empire."
