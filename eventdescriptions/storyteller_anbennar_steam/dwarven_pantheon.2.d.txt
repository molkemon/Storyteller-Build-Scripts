The hammer falls as the corrupt captain follows his fellow conspiring magistrate into the Halls of the Dead, where Dagrin will certainly ensure that they suffer for their corruption and misuse of power. This is the third execution held by the priesthood of Anvelind in the capital city this week, as all across the realm similar trials are being held that have been authorised by the current lord. These are to be overseen by Anvelind’s clergy in an effort to assist in resolving the rampant revolts and unrest throughout the country.
