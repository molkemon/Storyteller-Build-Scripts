While you have never suffered from them, you have heard of how a migraine is supposed to feel to the sufferer: a disturbance of the sufferer's vision, followed shortly by a rising pressure and intense agony in their head. However, you need to correct that assessment; you have never suffered from them before today. Two turns back, you noticed a vague aura starting to form in your vision from your right eye, and then each step you took since then has seemed to drive a nail through your skull. The pressure almost becomes overwhelming as you stagger to the door. Inside the room, sigils that are agonizing to look at blaze from all the walls, surrounding a magitechnical artefact embedded on the far wall. Through a grimace, you look at your colleagues and see similar levels of agony in their expressions. You have to make a decision swiftly before you are all overwhelmed.
