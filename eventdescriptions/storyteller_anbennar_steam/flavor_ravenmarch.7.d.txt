Eyeing you up as you approach, the man in the fanged wolf mask smirks and sweeps into a low bow. With a deep voice, he begins, "Greetings, stranger. Would you care to join me? I assure you that here only those of appropriate pedigree are allowed. The only commoners present know their place is beneath us, serving our drinks." He throws back his half-full glass of wine as a servant passes, before snatching another one out of their hands. "You simply have to keep them on their toes, so they know their place, wouldn’t you agree?"
