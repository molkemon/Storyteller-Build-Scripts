As the monarch was enjoying his walk across the shores of the Sorrow, a small sandmade visualization of a temple appeared before him. It bore a likeness of a Khet and its shape was irregular, but somewhat beautiful. "Um, you hear me?" A voice ringed in his head, as the Steward recognized its bearer instantly. "I'd like to have this built on the Sorrow. Somewhere where people can see me, where people lack a place to worship. Appreciated, Khukhet."
