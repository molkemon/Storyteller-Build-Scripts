Hello, the Amber Princess said. She had not been in the room a moment ago. Oh. Um, hello. The ruler bowed. The Amber Princess looked far less regal than usual; her hair was matted, and her breathing came erratically. She placed one hand on the ruler’s desk for support. I need... she said. What? What do you need? I will give it to you. I need your... your essence. This ritual is taking more out of me than I expected. I need more power. The ruler steeled themselves. I will give you what you need, said the ruler. The Amber Princess nodded, and held out her arms towards the ruler. The ruler was thrown back in their chair. Their vision swam with strange sights: a dark forest, a cave littered with bones, an endless ocean, a sea of snow then black. Ah, good, you aren’t dead. I hoped that wasn’t the case, the Amber Princess said when the ruler awoke. I still hunger. Are you fit to give me more?
