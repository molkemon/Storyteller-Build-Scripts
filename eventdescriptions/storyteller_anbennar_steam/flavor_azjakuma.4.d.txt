It is time for the meeting of the Tagharoghi to decide upon a successor. The leaders of the great Shirgrii have all gathered in Shinukhorchi to present their candidate to the ruler in hopes they will be chosen as the Rashokudei, destined to become the next sovereign over all of Azjakuma. As is customary, they have also brought gifts in the form of knowledge or gold from their temple. All that is left now is for the ruler to choose an apprentice.
