The ruler leans against the cracked wall of Adrail. The Aldresians’ swift assault on the Mages had taken the entire Empire by surprise. The Magisterium is reeling, several of their most powerful wizards slain or under arrest as the main Aldresian host closes in on the Grand Magister’s home. The ruler peeks around the wall as they see a small retinue of Aldresian knights approach the perimeter of the towering blue and gold spire of the Grand Magister. A second later they are consumed by a cloud of green air squeezing the life out of their lungs. They die slowly. The ruler breathes deeply; if they do not stop the Grand Magister who will? Who will stop this dark ascension? Long ago, they made a promise, an oath to all of the Empire to protect against this. And they would die fulfilling that oath if need be. Tapping their chest, the ruler starts to recite their vows as they step around the wall. "I shall hold no lands, accept no titles, take no partner and bear no children." The ruler murmurs, as a speeding fireball comes their way. "With your wisdom, make me an unbreakable shield." The Grandmaster recites, louder now as they methodically counterspell the oncoming magic. An inhumane cackle erupts from the top of the tower as the Grandmaster’s voice rises, audible now to nearly a roaring shout. The Grand Magister is amused at the audacity of the Grandmaster’s ambitions. "Give me the strength to fight eternal against the forces of darkness, till the very moment my sword breaks and my light extinguishes." The Grandmaster continues, as the Grand Magister descends to duel in single combat.
