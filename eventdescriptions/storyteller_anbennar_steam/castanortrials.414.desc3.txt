Hours of grueling tracking later, the creature finally arrives at the large maw of a cave, the muddy ground in front of the entrance crossed with the long gashes characteristic of basilisk tracks, and its depths lost in dark shadow. Chunks of stone, vaguely reminiscent of limbs, perhaps previous victims, serve as a terrifying reminder of the basilisk's petrifying gaze. Just as the creature musters the courage to dip a toe into the murky depths of the cave, a mighty roar pierces the silence. It appears the basilisk has arrived back to its home, and it does not sound happy.
