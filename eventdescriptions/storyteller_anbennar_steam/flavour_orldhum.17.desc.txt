As we conduct trade with Cannorians, it is natural that we must interact with the Empire of Anbennar at large. The decentralized state of the empire is a marvel in of itself, a representation of races and cultures throughout the continent. We have managed to entice the Electors and even the Emperor to participate in our Assembly of Lords. Here, we are able to exchange gifts and ideas freely with the Emperor and their princes, granting our court Imperial legitimacy as a consequence.
