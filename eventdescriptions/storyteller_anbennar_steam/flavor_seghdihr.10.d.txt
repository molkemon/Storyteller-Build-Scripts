The sounds of troops marching and training echo through the barracks and war halls of Seghdihr. The resolve of our soldiers has been strengthened to the utmost. Those who train here have the opportunity to learn specialised tactics that make them great assets to our nation. Three proposals for specialised training have been put forth by the officers in charge of training. We could focus on improving the fighting spirit of our companies, improving the skills of our officer corps in directing defensive tactics, or mental conditioning to ensure that our men do not break. These are all valuable initiatives, but we can only focus on one.
