Against monumental odds, the people of this realm have pushed beyond their limits and achieved something few would scarcely believe when we began our existence many centuries ago. But now, as monks and pyremages gather in monasteries across Haless, we choose to not focus on our own glory and how we achieved it. Instead, we turn to the land of our origin, and the people who allowed our nation to blossom into the ultimate ruler of the north. Mages channel their magic and monks manage the pyres all across our lands, and simultaneously a gout of brilliant blue flame erupts from the braziers of the monasteries like a stream of pure water. This fire splits into countless sparks, all settling upon the ground, where they suddenly begin to burrow into the terrain. With a blast of blue light, these sparks erupt into millions of blue lotus flowers, the colour of the oldest glaciers in a distant domain. All the lands around the monasteries are transformed into majestic fields of beauty, and our citizens cheer as we conclude our celebrations of triumph. Truly, our own path is much like that of the deserts of that domain. Once fraught and full of peril, with time, care, and the help of those around us, we have grown into a state of knowledge, magic, and camaraderie. Even in the coldest depths imaginable, something beautiful can still bloom.
