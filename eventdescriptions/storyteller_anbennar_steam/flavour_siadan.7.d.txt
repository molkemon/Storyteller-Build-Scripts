With the Šad Sur territory, there have been many proposals about what to do with the land. There is little harpy majority territory in our realm, and there have been many suggestions that we supplant the native gnollish population with our own and create a new southwestern homeland for the Siadúnan Harpy people. However, our bloodbound vassal, the pact of Zokka, has long complained of the low quality of their territory, and it may be worthwhile to augment their meagre territories with the highlands and mountains of the Šad Sur. An alternative option sees us splitting the territory half and half between our own harpy settlers and Zokka's pact. Whatever the choice is, the Chorsekhíl are eager to implement the will of the monarch, and they are already drawing up preliminary plans to implement either strategy; though they have warned that the terrain of the Šad Sur means a replacement plan may take upwards of twenty years, if it is to be done without a general revolt of the gnolls there.
