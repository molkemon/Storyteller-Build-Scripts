Shackled men and women were dragged through the streets of the capital, betwixt great and ancient buildings erected by geniebound golems in elder days. And just as the genies, these men and women were enemies of Fangaula, enemies of the ruler, rightful heir to Aro and Biakata. They had risen up in rebellion, or had tried to invade and subjugate what was rightfully theirs. And though there may be more enemies on the horizon, more invaders and rebels to bring to heel, for now the many peoples of Fangaula know the ruler is not to be ignored without dire consequences.
