Behind every tangled copse of trees, an ambush, behind every verdant ridge, a trap. Our courageous forces have their work cut out for them. Even those parts of this ancient forest we thought securely in our hands are prone to rise up in rebellion as soon as we turn our backs.  While the orks dwelling in the Deepwoods cannot dream of matching the might of our rose knights, they are certainly determined to make us bleed in their obstinate refusal to accept their destiny beneath our boots. But no matter how many contemptible tricks they attempt to delay our advance, we shall not be deterred.
