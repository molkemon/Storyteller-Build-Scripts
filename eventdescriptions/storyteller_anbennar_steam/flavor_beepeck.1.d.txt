Orston has been a de jure part of Exwes since before the Lilac Wars, and yet the Halflings who live there have much more in common with us than their human rulers. Beepeck stands as the representative of all Halflings in the Empire. If we allow this situation to stand, and let Halflings right across our borders remain subjugated, it will not only weaken our authority in our own lands, but our very standing as an Imperial elector. We only have one option. to acquire Orston for ourselves and shore up our authority. However, there remains a question on how to pursue it, most importantly, whether to petition the Emperor for assistance in this matter. No doubt, our place as one of the Electors could be used as leverage, but could we not just as well seize the chance and do it ourselves, without outside involvement?
