Our latest project in that country has resulted in something unusual. a glut of spirits, claiming to flee from something inconceivably large and powerful. They were devoured, and in the scattered remains of their memories we have glimpsed a great and terrible visage. Its precise shape remains unknown to us; full knowledge appears to bring madness or death. Our finest shamans have been reduced to gibbering lunatics, only able to acknowledge one truth. the Roil rises. Haless is an island of stability in the tempestuous sea of the Spirit Realm, and corrupted temple by corrupted temple the shore recedes before us, bringing that great mass of Chi and terror to the banks of our material reality. Sire, our movements have unsettled the ancient earth to such an extent that repair may no longer be possible. Something is coming, something is coming soon. Mystics and shamans of Haless speak of Great Spirits, ancient slumbering beasts that will awaken at the end of time. Our legends adumbrate a time before time, when the Oni served gods in heaven. Their gods fell, and the Oni fell upon them in the feral hunger of beasts. Those who ate godsflesh became as gods themselves, immortals who did not die, and whose progeny carried the curse of hunger. Could this be the return of those gods? And if so, shall we be prepared as our ancestors were?
