Once the mysterious fey reached the throne room, they called upon the sonad. After a series of whispers between the two that couldn’t be overheard, the fey laid its hand on the sonad’s head and uttered a strange enchantment. Ever since, there has been a remarkable change in the sonad. Everything from their physical prowess, to their intellectual insights, to their personal charisma seems to have been greatly enhanced by the event. There is no doubt in anyone’s mind that the fey saw the spark of a glorious ruler inside the sonad, and thus transformed them into the ideal guide that the nation needed.
