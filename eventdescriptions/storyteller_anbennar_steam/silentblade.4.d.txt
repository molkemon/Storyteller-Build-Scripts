Ruuk lowered his bow with no satisfaction. No doubt, it was a shot his father would have been proud of. But his father was dead, replaced by the wretched and warped thing that had just crumpled. He turned, leaving behind the thought to face his troops. "This is it," Ruuk bellowed, raising his bow. "This is where we draw the line. This is where we show our might." The ragtag orks before him bellowed as one. Their rage, their anger, their strength washed over him. This was not some mix of lost and clanless orks. This was the vanguard against villainy that stood before them. Dropping his bow, Ruuk Elmscourge, no, Ruuk Silentblade hefted his ax. "Together. For Clan Silentblade." He turned to face the chaotic crowd of orks he once called his clan. They were disorganized. Weak. Yet, in the distance, he could spot the creature he had thought dead rising once more. He snorted, ignoring the pit in his stomach. It was never going to be that easy. With one final breath, he let out the cry of war that had once guided his people.
