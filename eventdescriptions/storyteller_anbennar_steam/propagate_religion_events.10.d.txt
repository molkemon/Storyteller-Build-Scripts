An increasingly common sight in the countryside is that of traveling Zealots, mystic missionaries of the Ravelianist faith. They are often warmly received not only by their brothers in faith, but also by the rural population who have found a way to integrate the Ravelian creed into their own traditions and myths; they have even confirmed some of the mythological heroes as saints in their religion. The result is overall not something that would fit in Cannor but has made many more predisposed towards Ravelianism in general.
