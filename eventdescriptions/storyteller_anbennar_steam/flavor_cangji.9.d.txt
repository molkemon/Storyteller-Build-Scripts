Our history has long since divorced from our past beneath a domain and a realm, but it is impossible to ignore the connection we share with the northern regions of a province. This was the cradle of our fledgling nation, and within its central city lies the ultimate link in the chain of our past; the Temple of the Undying Flame. Standing within the temple’s halls, countless pyremages of all skins and stripes look upon the massive conflagration in utter devotion, either throwing themselves to the ground in reverence or frantically scribbling onto scrolls and tomes. This great beacon of neverending light is the origin of our understanding of spiritfire, and its reclamation by us can only be seen as an event of utmost significance. Long have we survived on the fading embers of its divine warmth, but with the source in our hands, the possibilities of magical growth are endless. All at once, the pyremages infuse their own magic into the temple’s brazier, causing the newly installed inlays to glow a brilliant white. Soon, the Undying Flame begins to shift and crackle, until it finally bursts into a pillar of fire, blasting through an opening hastily cut into the rooftop and forming a beacon that extends far into the night sky. The mages cheer, and the Ashen Soul weeps in euphoria. Finally, they have returned home.
