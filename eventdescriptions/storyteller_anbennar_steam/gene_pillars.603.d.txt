The party comes across a fourlegged beast. Its orange coat is mottled with black stripes, and white fur covers its underside and lower face. The party almost did not notice its approach, since the beast had silently crept up on them in the shadows. Its tail hangs low and sways side to side. It lets out a low, threatening growl, and one of the party members takes a step back. It roars loudly, striking terror into the party. Suddenly, similar roars respond from throughout the facility.
