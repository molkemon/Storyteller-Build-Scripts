Enlightenment. Revelation. Apotheosis. Whichever word you wish to use, our everhumble ruler has experienced it. Our lord reportedly entered a trance state after imbibing what was previously thought to be a fatal dose of the Harmaceuticals' latest product. After spending an hour unconscious and frothing at the mouth, our lord rejoined the Prefectorium Parlay, revitalized. Their already razor wit seemed to be doubled, their brilliance tripled, their charisma quadrupled, and their other laudable aspects no doubt experiencing something else ending in upled. Our beloved leader is reborn, a new light burning in their gaze, though we're still unsure whether or not that part is just a lasting aftereffect of the narcotics.
