Calas of Eilísin left almost forty years ago to fight in the Greentide in the Order of the Ashen Rose. Now that Dookanson has been defeated, he and some others seek to return home to Eilísin and to serve in our armies. While they would certainly be useful in the fight against Lorent, those who stayed loyal to us have doubts as to where their loyalty stands, and that will not be good for morale, not to mention the organizing we will have to do to integrate these adventurers back into our army.
