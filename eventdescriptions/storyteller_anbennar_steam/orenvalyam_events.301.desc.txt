Orenvalyam tries to hold itself to a higher ideal than other realms; its warriors are the agents of justice and liberation, not brutality and devastation. Yet as the Kamrayakval’s wars turn to maelstroms of blood and destruction, and the fabric of the faith frays at the seams, this ideal too has been ensnared by darkness. Banditry and brigandage, looting and plunder—the soilandflame banners of Dawn have been tarnished by the crimes of those who carry them. And yet, even as they commit these fell deeds, they still advance Orenvalyam’s frontiers and win their Kamrayakval victories. Should we steer our armies back to the righteous path?
