I sing of the waning light of a kingdom, whose rulers across ages have led it to wealth. The ruler this kingdom now has is not one of the rulers maintaining the kingdom’s good health. The ruler this kingdom now has is quite stupid; their brain has been rotted by ailments and drink. Their people despise them, their counselors mock them, and their neighbors ignore them as lazy and weak. If ever this kingdom can rise back to greatness, it is surely because the people have said that because their ruler is feckless and greedy, the kingdom’s great glory demands that they drop dead.
