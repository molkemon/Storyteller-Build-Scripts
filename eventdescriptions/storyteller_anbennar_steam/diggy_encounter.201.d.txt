Swords clash against rock, arrows embed themselves in the mud, gunfire and battle cries echo from the walls; the elemental is suffering the full front of our attacks. Over time more and more rubble is dislodged from the enemy, and its vulnerable core reveals itself among the hard rocks within. With a shout the leading general directs all attention to the weak point and orders a volley of bullets. Sharp splinters are dislodged as the hail of metal impacts the core, and slowly the earthen body starts to fall apart, heavy rocks impacting the now muddy ground. At last, the elemental is vanquished, but the goblin is nowhere to be seen.
