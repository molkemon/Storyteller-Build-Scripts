The song of Nalo Pu’ukani, the memory of Grandfather Moss, and the discovery of Hoki’eal. All three Dragon Turtles have given over their secrets to us, trusting that there is finally a force in Insyaa powerful and wise enough to deal with what may come once the Kiamoa falls. We fear to see the ocean change, to become something we do not recognize, a friend with a different face; but this is its nature, and whatever sorrow we feel cannot stop our drive for discovery. It cannot make children of us. What lies beyond the Kiamoa is no doubt more of that ocean, more of her, and we cannot divide her from herself. When we meet what lies beyond the edge of things, the life beyond life, we will not shrink.
