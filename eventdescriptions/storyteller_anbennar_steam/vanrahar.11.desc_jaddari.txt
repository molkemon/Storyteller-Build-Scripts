The cultish acolytes of the mad son of Jaher have been decisively defeated by our superior might. Though they had impressive cavalry, they could not outrun us forever. The Salahad coast has been conquered, and our bureaucrats now draw up plans to settle strategic areas to secure it. The memory of Jaher’s conquest is not so easily forgotten, and celebrations erupt all across Vanrahar. This will go no small way towards rehabilitating our image after we refused the soft rule of our cousins. Some of these elves are old enough to remember when Haless was under elven rule, but we will make sure that their descendants will not be able to even conceive of a Haless dominated by elves. Jaddar’s insane ideas, even worse than Suhan’s, will surely be discredited in the minds of our people after this.
