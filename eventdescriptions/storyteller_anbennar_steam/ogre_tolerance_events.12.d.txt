A few years ago, an adventuring party discovered and braved ancient ruins in their domain. Keeping their discovery hush, these daring spelunkers enlisted the aid of a trusted historian to verify the ruin’s origins. After a lengthy investigation into the titanic halls, bustling with countless artifacts great and small, they determined that the ruins were giant in origin. Yet such a valuable secret could not be kept forever. The expedition’s details leaked, and, fearful for the site’s integrity, the adventurers have approached us to guard it from raiders and looters. As we struggle to process a backlog of requests pertaining to the giant ruins, a particular group has jumped the bureaucratic line with their mighty strides. ogres. Speaking curtly but fairly, they expressed that they, above all others, deserve to be its tenders and guardians. after all, it is their legacy as giantkin. With so many disparate parties involved, it seems impossible to appease them all. Who should manage the giant ruins?
