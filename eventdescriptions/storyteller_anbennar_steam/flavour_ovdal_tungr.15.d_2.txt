The monarch gazes out at the horizon from atop the Sea Hold's highest spires. It is a calm day out on the Divenhal, the immense cityship tracing a billowing path of steam through an otherwise cloudless sky. The ruler closes their eyes, taking in everything that their realm and another nation have accomplished together. Bulwar flourishes under their joint rule, experiencing an era of wealth and prosperity not seen since the Age of Godkings. There is not a port or harbor in the world unvisited by their steamships; the seas, too, are their dominion, bestrode by the Copper Seaway and patrolled by fleets of copper and iron. From the decks of the Uthojbrog they salvage treasuries from the depths, and from the Ovdal Uthoj they maintain maritime sovereignty. Their crown jewels rule the seas. Sailors and citizens alike bustle through the streets below, the chatter of daily life rising up from the heart of the Sea Hold. A small fleet of merchant steamers sails in the Sea Hold's wake, secure in the knowledge that they follow the greatest vessel to ever grace the oceans of Halann. The monarch seats themself by the edge of the upper deck, the stresses of executive work momentarily forgotten as they stare up at the perfect sky. A ruddy sun casts its beams through the columns of steam overhead, lending the vista an almost coppery hue. The uppermost peaks of the Sea Hold are a complex collection of masts and smokestacks, home to thousands of signal flags and national banners gently streaming in the tranquil breeze. United and unchallenged, their realm and another nation rule the waves.
