We have received a very, very strange offer, well to call it strange would be putting it lightly, it is more accurately radically unconventional. A Veykodan rune scribe, Ur Fijon, is offering to give our army his well-known runic tattoos, for a price of course. If we accepted his offer, our army would see great benefit from the use of these powerful Veykodan magics. Our people, however, would not look fondly on this. the sight of our soldiers marching from town to town bearing the markings of our long-held enemies would undoubtedly shake their faith in our government.
