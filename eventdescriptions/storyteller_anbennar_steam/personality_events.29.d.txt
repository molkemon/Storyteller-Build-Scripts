Like many states of similar size the outward unity of our ruler is partly a mirage. Locally any city, estate or even village may have its own rules and laws since time immemorial. Eager to reduce inconsistent legislation in our country the ruler has been withdrawing privileges and issuing new, nationwide, directives to bring order into chaos. This is, however, not always immediately successful. With a recent government directive is outright being ignored in one part of our country the ruler's authority is in question.
