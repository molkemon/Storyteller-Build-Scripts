Like many states of similar size, the outward unity of the realm is partly a mirage. Locally, any city, estate, or even village may have its own rules and laws since time immemorial. Eager to reduce inconsistent legislation in the country, the monarch has been withdrawing privileges and issuing new, nationwide directives to bring order into chaos. This is, however, not always immediately successful. With a recent government directive, outright defiance is being ignored in one part of the country, and the ruler's authority is in question.
