As the expedition travels further into the pillar, they come across an odd room. There were long lines covered with raw ore of different kinds, many rare minerals present in the bodies of the mechanim and the construction of Kauga. It seems like this was some kind of storage facility, though the manner of just storing the resources in lines on the ground seems quite odd. As the rest of the expedition went to investigate the ore, one member brushed against a long protrusion in the floor. Instead of being solid as they expected, the protrusion moved, and suddenly the floor began moving as well. The lines of ore began moving swiftly in different directions with the expedition still on them; there was only a moment to react.
