Word has reached us of a great discovery. In one of the many tunnels that snake their way through our hold, two miners have come across a vast deposit of the magical mineral, Dames tear; a main ingredient in all things magic from artificery to enchantments. Up until this point they have been speaking almost in unison, but soon, the two begin bickering. Eventually, the first miner speaks, and says that the Dames tear must be mined and exploited for the benefit of our great nation, and would surely bring great riches. The second, however, raises her voice, and loudly proclaims that mining Dames tear would, rather than riches, bring great danger, and not be worth the risk to extract.
