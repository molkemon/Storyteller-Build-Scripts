A few of the newcomers in our roosts migrated here with a very specific goal in mind. these unfortunate earthbound individuals long for our ability to fly. For such fixated individuals, riding Zamukaraš or being transported by one of us is not enough; they want to feel like they themselves are flying. This obsessive trait is often found in otherwise brilliant minds, so much that some of them even gain a position as court advisors. And yet, even prestige and power won't distract from the goal. So here we are, our court advisor inside some complex machinery that they believe will for sure function this time; several harpies are ready to attempt a save once the great invention inevitably fails. But grabbing a body is one thing; a big vehicle is another.
