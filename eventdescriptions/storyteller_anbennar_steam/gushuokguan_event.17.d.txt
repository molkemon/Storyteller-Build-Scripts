The Seven Swords Society has for a while now advised our officers on strategy and matters of the military. They have acted like wise elders and mentors to the inexperienced young officer cadets and have proven their worth many times over. They have become an integral part of the officers curriculum, and many young officers have already floated the idea that these veterans should be allowed to accompany armies as advisory liaisons when asked to. Let us learn from the past and improve the soldiers of the future.
