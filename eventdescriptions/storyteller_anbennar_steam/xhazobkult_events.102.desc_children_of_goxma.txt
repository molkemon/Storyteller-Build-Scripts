While onlookers typically characterize the Xhazobkult as destruction-loving demonworshippers, this is not always the reality. In the realm, the summoning and worship of demons is not as prominent, with the noble Kult instead being focused on devotion to the goddess Goxma. But even in our government, the taint of the Kult’s more demonic side is still present, if diminished and hidden away. We have recently heard of a warlock in a province preparing a ritual to conjure a powerful demon into our world. The potential dangers of such a ritual are worrisome, but the power of a demon is tempting.
