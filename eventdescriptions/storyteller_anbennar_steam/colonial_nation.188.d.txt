The nobility are not very good at hiding their political positions, nor do they seem to care that much. They have been heard in feasts and banquets, loudly stating that the fate of the colony is doomed to failure, given the power that the burghers so wrongly hold. But today their intentions have led to an unprecedented approach; one of them came to the monarch's chambers before the first hours of dawn. "Sorry to interrupt your rest, your majesty, however something must be done immediately. The burghers' insolence and lack of virtue are making this colony a fool's paradise. This must be stopped before today's council."
