The central fact of Dame's tear is that there is never enough to go around, a principle that has reared its head once more. A delegation from this realm is wringing their hands outside the chambers of our ruler, squabbling in whispered hisses over who will break the bad news. Soon enough, it is known, that the province's supply of blue gold has passed its peak, as the deposit in this domain has been dramatically depleted in size. Worry spreads among the elite of our society, whose prosperity and power is often dependent on Dame's tear, that they might soon be at the mercy of foreigners when securing their supply. We can only pray that the deposit can be stretched until an especially bountiful Tearsfall.
