For years now, porcelain has been an extremely valued commodity in Escann, for both its magical and nonmagical properties. This has led many on the seemingly futile quest to recreate it on Cannorian soil, but with none successful. Until now, that is. An Escanni Artificer has been able to successfully recreate the valued substance using relatively basic materials. Their method has rapidly spread throughout Escann, and it seems that each day a new porcelain workshop opens its doors in the east.
