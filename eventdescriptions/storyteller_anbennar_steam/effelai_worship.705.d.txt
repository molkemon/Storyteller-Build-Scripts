We have traveled far and seen many dangers on our pilgrimage; one such evil lies just within Her canopy. A vast expanse of dense, humid, treacherous land nestled between two mountain ranges not far from Her heart. Here festers a massive moldering mound of mushrooms, like a foul wound in Her side. Her song does not reach here; twisted and dampened by the massive stems, it shuns Her beauty. It has not been seen to expand as far as we know, but should it ever show signs of so doing, we must raze it. Another evil lies far closer to home, the foul leechmen of the swamp. Long have we warred with them and their ravenous false god, he who seeks to consume Her gifts. Recently, however, we have encountered a group dissimilar from ourselves only in looks. They call themselves the Lai’i, and similarly to us they fight to survive under Her branches, though it seems we have more to learn than expected. They are fierce warriors with armor stronger than any we have seen, and although their views on Her beauty differ from ours, perhaps they can be convinced to fight against a common enemy.
