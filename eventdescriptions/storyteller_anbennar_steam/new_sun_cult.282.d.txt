POWER. OVERWHELMING.  Those were the words ringing in the ears of every inhabitant of Bulwar when Amussu uncovered and seized his fifth and final relic. Each heard it in their mother tongue, loud and clear even as all the land’s dogs began to howl and all its horses neighed in fear.  Our scholars and our mages and our priests all say the same thing: we only have a few months to defeat the ancient GodKing before it's too late. It may be nighimpossible, now that the binding rituals begin to slough off and he attains his full power, but we have no other choice.
