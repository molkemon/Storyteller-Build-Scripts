As the shamans of the realm travel to HulJorkad to discuss what the next step of their Dookansearch should be, the clan holds its breath. Days go by, but eventually they conclude several things after studying more newfound relics of the past that were found, along with conclusions drawn from more contemporary sources, such as dwarven prisoners from previous wars and dwarven documents found in the holds they took. The first conclusion is that they must dig further down in HulJorkad, so the shamans may find what lies below. The second conclusion is that they must seek out the hold of DúrVazhatun because a group of dwarven prisoners from the Blueshield Company admit to being ordered to search the hold for information about the origin of orks. Rumor within the group has it they were doing experiments on orks before the hold fell. The last conclusion is that they should seek out one of the few holds that orks nor goblins have taken, a so-called remnant hold. Such a hold would no doubt have kept records on them, which might illuminate where they took Dookan to. To this end, the shamans urge the ruler to seek out Ovdal Lodhum or ArgÔrdstun, both of which reside along the Argrod, which intersects the Gorrod near HulJorkad. To this end, they should seek to gain control over the Gorrod as soon as possible.
