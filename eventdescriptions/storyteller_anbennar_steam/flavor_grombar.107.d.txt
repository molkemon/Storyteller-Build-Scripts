With the continuous spread of the Corinites across the land, it is becoming more and more clear that, no matter how much we may promote the old ways, the land will never truly be free of Corinite heresy. Thus, some of the more senior shamans have negotiated with a group of Corinite preachers, and the result is the document that now sits before the lord; the Edict of Garok. Should the lord sign it, at least for a time, a degree of peace may be achieved with the Corinites of our realm. Many of the more traditional elements of the tribes, however, will surely be displeased with any sort of peace with the Corinites.
