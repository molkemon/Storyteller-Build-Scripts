Our armies shattered. Our flags are in tatters. Our towns are burning. This is the news that comes back from the western front. Zunk Kuulgo has won. Their lands are independent once more, and what few troops we have left lack the morale to fight anymore. The borders of the realm are wide open for the picking, and the Shuvüüsh remaining in our lands quickly return to their old ways. Our weakness is palpable, and all around, hungry eyes gaze upon us, desiring to take their own delicious morsels out of our wounded realm. We cannot think of expansion now; we must focus inward, focus on defense. There is nothing left to do but try to desperately hold onto what we have left.
