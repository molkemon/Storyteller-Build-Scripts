The discontent with Mardint's rule is not limited to the nobility. The Šabari, merchants and artisans that suffer with the blockade that Zokka has on the country, and the commoners, who took refuge in the fortress after the gnolls took their ancestral lands, are the main groups affected by the bad policies of the new king. It is our duty as the First Temple to give hope to the peoples that have lost everything to Darkness, but organizing a meeting with the Šabari could be a better use of our limited time in the city, and yield better results in the long run.
