We near the end of our journey now, but do not fear, Surael's light is neverending. We are left with one last reminder of this as the sun turns orangered, reaching the Sunset Temple at Venáil. Think back to those elves of yore, the fear and uncertainty that lay before them even after Munas spied this isle, and know that Surael, and Surael alone, was with them, guiding them, shaping them, just as he guides and shapes us. We must leave the sun as it reaches the apex of beauty, even at that moment it descends to its nadir, plunging into the sea. But fear not, it shines bright in Aelantier beyond, as souls there too worship Surael with the fervor he deserves. And from this temporary darkness comes eternal light. already the sun rises again above Haless, beginning this voyage of beauty and wonder anew. And so this pattern shall repeat, with every sunrise and every sunset, for every soul and every heart in the Jadd Empire, eternally, for ever and ever and ever. The sun shall never set on the Jadd Empire.
