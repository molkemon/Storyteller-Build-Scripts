No tale of any realm could have prepared you to bear witness to the gruesome majesty before you. A hulking mass of muscle and sinew hunches over the unrecognizable ruin of its latest kill. An incomprehensible cacophony of fang and claw, fur and feather, and tusk and horn, gorging itself on the carcass. You dare not even breathe as your trembling hand reaches for an arrow, desperate to end this encounter before it begins. The soft creak of the bow draws no notice from the feasting lord of beasts, and you let your arrow fly, offering a silent prayer.
