Young vampires, known as vampire spawn by their brethren, have a tendency to test their powerful new abilities with little care or responsibility. With the increasing numbers of infected in their domain, it is no wonder that many incidents have occurred where these fledgling vampires have caused destruction and death for little more than their own amusement. The devastating consequences will be felt for a while in their domain, but thankfully the young have stopped testing their new abilities after an initial frenzy, as they have been taken down by vampire hunters, cowed by their elders, or have realized the risks they take with their wanton destruction.
