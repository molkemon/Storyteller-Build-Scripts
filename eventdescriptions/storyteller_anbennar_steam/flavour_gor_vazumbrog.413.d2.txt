They could not move the General King; he took them all for fools. "My metal men do everything. What need have I for you?" The mob set out to show the dwarf the error of his ways. They smashed the tools of golemcraft and set the hold ablaze. They asked, "What good are metal men that leave us all unfed? Let rich dwarves feel at last the pain of the poor, the sick, the dead." The rebels held the streets all day, with constructs off at war. The people stood like titans then, to end what came before. Then metal stomps resound the streets; their war was at an end. Outmatched, the workers still did shout, "Titanium does not bend." Golemancer's Working Song, Verse Two.
