A mismatched concert, sounding like a melodious dream, tilts the heads and commands the ears of locals living in the province, yet, despite the wondrous notes, no musicians command the noises. Nay, this concert on the plains was brought to our people by a meandering symphony of shadhavar. Often mistaken for mundane antelope, these busking ungulates possess a singular horn filled with cavities and holes that enable it to mimic sounds. Normally a discordant chorus of mimicry and animal calls, the shadhavar rolling through the province have instead been performing jaw-dropping renditions of beloved classics, captivating the populace. From dawn till dusk, the symphony has serenaded the land, granting an uplifting reprieve for even the saddest souls. Bravo.
