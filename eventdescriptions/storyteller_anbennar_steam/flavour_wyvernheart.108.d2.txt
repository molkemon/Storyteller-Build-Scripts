Once again a ruler of Wyvernheart finds themselves entering the bowels of the research complex under the Gulletpeak. The Sovereign delves deeper, tracing the steps of their ancestors. Accompanied by several "immoral" artificers employed by Wyvernheart, a glowing rune of subjugation on their forehead. The artificers show the Sovereign into a newly built facility where great cages can be discerned, holding cells for the great beasts that inhabited the Trialmount before Dames tear was conjured from the skies. To preserve them they were moved here, as it was felt they would surely grow to be useful in time. And now the time has come for them to serve their true purpose. An artificer, gazing at one of the caged beasts, makes an eerie smile, sharpening their saw. With a tinge of excitement in their voice, they plead. "Please try to not damage the organs too much. We will need everything we can extract." The Sovereign looks to the cage.
