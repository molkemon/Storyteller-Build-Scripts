The Nisetataa are the trademark of the Korosheshi Lapidarist Guilds, gems painstakingly faceted with fifty-six cuts. The careful crafting process requires incredible skill and can take decades to complete, but when finished it results in an unparalleled focusing catalyst for mages. They receive their name, meaning Kingdom Jewel, from the enormous price they reach, exceeding that of a small kingdom. One of the greatest gnomish artisans of Koroshesh has recently finished crafting such a gem. He is, however, having a hard time finding a customer able and willing to pay the gigantic sum of money he demands as compensation for his years of labor and centuries of experience. Our contacts in the country have informed us of an amazingly wealthy state mage living there, who could be interested in purchasing the gem. We could put the artisan and the wizard in contact in exchange for a share of the sell profits. However, mages are an odd bunch, and we cannot guarantee he will be interested. Perhaps we should invest a bit into convincing him to secure the purchase.
