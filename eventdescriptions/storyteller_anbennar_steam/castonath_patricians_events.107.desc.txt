Castonath in recent years is a city choked with the colorful fumes of artificery swirling between the great spires and staining the pure white walls of the city’s ancient palaces. Magical workshops and artificer studios have started popping up all over Castonath, taking advantage of the resources and diverse population of the city. With this growing importance and influence of artificery comes growing pains, however. Not a year goes by without a report of some thousand year old fountain or marble spire being the hapless casualty of an explosion from some artificery experiment or another. The established families are complaining, claiming that this wanton destruction of the heritage and history of Castonath cannot continue. They are willing to put forth sums of their own money for repairs and to ensure that harsher regulations on artificery are put in place. Meanwhile, our artificers are naturally pushing back at any mention of harsher regulations, stating that their work must be allowed to continue unimpeded.
