Thanks to the incredible efforts of our people, the lands of Aelantier have been reclaimed, and it is up to us to make our new earth a paradise where our people can prosper. Every meadow will be cultivated, every river exploited, every mountain explored, every secret discovered. And if, inadvertently, any entity comes to challenge our hegemony, we will make them understand that they have come to the wrong neighborhood and knocked on the wrong door.
