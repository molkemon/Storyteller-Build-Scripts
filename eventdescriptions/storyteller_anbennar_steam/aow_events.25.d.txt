While most of our nation fears the invaders, a few citizens seem to welcome them. After the realm fell to the enemy, its inhabitants seem to have started considering if they would be better off in a country headed by rulers of their own faith. In an effort to mitigate this betrayal, the local rulers have sent a messenger to our court, requesting promises of a more hands-off approach to religious matters in that realm. Otherwise, the enemy may already have a hold on the province.
