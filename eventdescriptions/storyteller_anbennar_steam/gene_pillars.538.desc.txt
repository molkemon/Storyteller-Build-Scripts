Just a little farther and we will be upon the crown of this mighty pillar; a place where great trees erupt from the central parts of the pillar and shroud it in the great foliage of the titanoforest, the forest this very pillar helped bring into existence. How beautiful, the way the pillar has adorned itself in the forest it seeded. We step forwards, into the last great unknown this great pillar has in store for us.
