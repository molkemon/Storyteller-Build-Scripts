An advisor enters the chamber ponderously, bearing most strange news. They tell of a Metobesebi human woman who has risen to command her own galley. According to the crew, including the harpies aboard, the human took command and successfully repelled an attacking Raheni galley after the previous Odanches was slain during a boarding action. The human woman is now demanding recognition from the other Odanchésta, intending to stand amongst them. Naléni has no written law, but informally only harpies have been permitted to attain positions such as Odanches; a human doing so would be quite an upset, though it is clear this particular human can be very useful.
