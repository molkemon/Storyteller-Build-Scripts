At one time, the land of Rayazas was filled with many rare and wonderful things, as befit the capital of a great empire. Crown jewels, records from early antiquity, works of art from the greatest artists of a civilization spanning millennia; Rayaz had all these things and more. But as the rulers of Rayaz admired their fine treasures, there was something they would not admit. They took it all from us. Once they stole the products of our civilization, they did the same with them as they did with our lands when they took those: they sullied them with their covetous staring and their rancid presence. This leads to a natural question; why did they take what is ours? Perhaps they wished to emulate some squalid version of our great empire through worship of our possessions. Perhaps they wished to cause our patriots anger and pain. Perhaps they took them just to take them; it does not matter now. Through blood, might, and will, we have set things right. Now we will have our treasures back, and more. Now it is our turn to take.
