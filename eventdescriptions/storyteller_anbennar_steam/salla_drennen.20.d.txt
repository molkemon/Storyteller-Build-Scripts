How would I describe our achievement? Oooh, it's fun when you ask us both the same question. It'll be a shame when you’re done writing. Sorry about the question. I always knew you could do it; we could do it; so when we did it, well, I wasn't shocked or anything of the sort. I suppose I was giddy. You know the sort of feeling you get when you’ve had just the right amount of wine, you’re somewhere warm and with good company? That euphoric sense of rightness, bliss. That’s how I’d describe it. Is that strange to say? I suppose it more describes me rather than it, but I think its effect is maybe more enlightening than anything I could say about it itself. I know all who live in these woods, now or tomorrow, will remember what we did. I hope anyone who reads this will too. Elethen, Varinna, and Narawen, architects of rebirth, builders of the rainbow veil.
