Having convinced Stumbles Though Broken Things, as our new friend has elected to call himself, we continue making our way further through the pillar. With the help of Stumbles, our progress is much improved. We come upon a unique hazard, however, as we continue our descent. A large chamber lies before us, and astonishingly, a large riverlike system covers the chamber that seems to be a smaller variant of the Hinuigrasslands to our north. Normally this would not be a major issue, but Stumbles informs us that his mechanical ball does not do well in contact with water. Should too much water get into the ball, it might become damaged. What should we do?
