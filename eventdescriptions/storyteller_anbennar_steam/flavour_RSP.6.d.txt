On the Isle of Melted Detritus, every scallawag, cutthroat, and brigand of the Driftcourt assembled. Its courtiers streamed in as stowaways on other ships, and its nobility arrived on stolen and creaking galleons. The Driftcourt did not have cause to assemble often, but the issue which it faced today required a great ruling that would be heard across all of Aelantier. The procession continued to the outcropping of rocks where the great decisions were made. The ship bells heralded its arrival. Around the stones, the captains of standing assembled. The stature they cut and how they conducted themselves varied in many ways, but here they stood united. The First Mate of the court began to speak. Listen up, you saltmarked lot. We of the Driftcourt have got a problem. Money is flowing through our fingers. The courtiers jeered. We stand apart now. Every one of us is looting for all we can, but the colonists flow through our cracks. Cannor is growing richer, off our land. The First Mate paused. There is only one thing we can do, if we want to get richer. This pirate court must become a pirate crew. We need a leader. The pirates broke out into raucous cheers. Everything was soon to change.
