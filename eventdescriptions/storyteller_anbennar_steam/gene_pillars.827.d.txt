As we make our way out of a series of repetitive and heavily damaged rooms, we stumble upon a vast expanse. We can see outside the pillar into the dark cavern that is the inside of the sandworm. Before us is a vast gap where the tower has taken heavy damage to the point that only the main supports that run up the full length of the pillar are left. Everything else has been stripped away, creating a nearly impassable abyss.
