The monarch has stunned all the world. In an effort to avoid the dragon fleeing where the domain could not reach, the ruler came up with the plan to challenge the dragon to a duel. Refusing such a haughty challenge from a mortal mage would grievously wound the pride and honor that are so central to a dragon's identity, and he was compelled to accept, assuming that he would make quick work of our glorious monarch. Unfortunately, though the ruler was able to wound the dragon, the monarch was in turn grievously harmed. We cannot be sure if the monarch will live, and if the monarch does, it is unlikely that the monarch will ever fully recover from arcane damage of such magnitude. A temporary setback for the Mystic Revolution, but one that is no doubt humiliating for the Skull Throne.
