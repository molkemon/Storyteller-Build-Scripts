The travels through the chambers of the Gene Pillar have come to a rather unforeseen halt, as, after stepping through a seemingly normal doorway, an unseen magical anomaly caused us to find ourselves not in another stone chamber or laboratory, but instead in a clearing surrounded by deep woodlands. And we were not alone; three strange, seemingly wooden, women were there, braiding each other's hair and barely acknowledging our arrival apart from a few scant words: "Strange travelers are thee, guided here by wild magic." "Yet for trespassing there is a fee, else one stays here beguiled." "So tell us what you consider freedom is worth." "And perhaps we shall send you back to your earth."
