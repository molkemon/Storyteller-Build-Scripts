A child cries out to its mother in amazement. "Mother, mother. There's a town under the sea." "Are you crazy? Stay away from there, that's a precursor ruin." "No, mom, it's real. Go look." The mother goes to the edge of the sea; her eyes boggle. Not far from where the settlement on the coast ends and the ocean begins, there is indeed a town under the sea. The merfolk of the area, having determined that our ruler is a suitable terrestrial neighbour, have decided to grace us with an underwater city right next to our own town.
