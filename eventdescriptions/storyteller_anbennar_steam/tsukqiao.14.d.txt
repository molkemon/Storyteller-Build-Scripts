For one hundred fifty years our people have lived in the shadows of foreign empires, for too long we have endured their rule. Luzhong is reborn, Luzhong rules all of Yanshem and this will never change. The ruler makes a grand revelation. the Xion Dynasty are actually the fabled Fei Dynasty, whose name has been bastardized over the years. 'A Grand Reveal Once Ashes now Again End of the Road'
