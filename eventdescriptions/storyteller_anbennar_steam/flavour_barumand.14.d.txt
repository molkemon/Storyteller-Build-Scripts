The coast of Lake Silvermere is small, so we were amazed when a Barumandi fisherman began reporting a new species of fish in the area, one never before seen in Cannor. Of course, we were quick to spread the news far and wide. This was until humans reported that the fish was in fact not new, but has been fished in Lake Silvermere for millennia. Such troubling events have brought sorrow to Barumand in the scientific and prestigious fields.
