Many in our country believe that Khugra is clearly not ready for independence, and that we must seek foreign aid against the troubles that ail us. Among these supporters of submission to foreign powers, there are three main tendencies. The Green Party thinks we must pledge ourselves to the new lizardfolk emperors in Rayaz, so that we might be a part of the future of lizardkind. The Golden Party believes we must submit to the Ikatara of Nazhni, so that we can march towards riches and prosperity together. Finally, the Red Party thinks we need to bend the knee to whoever comes out victorious of the war between Asarta and Karassk, following them towards glory in the battlefield. Which of these options will our wise ruler choose?
