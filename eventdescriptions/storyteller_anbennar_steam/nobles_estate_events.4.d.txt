A representative of the people living in an area dominated by one of the most prominent families has approached the throne today imploring the monarch to take action against what he claims are systematic abuses of power against the people there. As the ruler of the realm has a responsibility to protect his people, not doing so will make them appear weak and may harm the long-term growth of the province. The powerful family would likely not take kindly to chastising such a powerful member of their ranks however.
