Magical progress is sometimes hard fought, and many people of lesser intellect or magical skill will often see the pushing of boundaries as dangerous or unethical, even when they do not understand the goal of the overall project. Such an unwashed crowd has gathered in the capital to demand the end to construction efforts on their land. They rant of villages demolished to make way for the spires of the court mages, soldiers used for experimental war magics gone awry, and merchants and hawkers who were muscled out of their trade by mass Transmutation. Each person believes their complaints to be valid, and they are all very angry. Acquiescing to these demands would lessen their ire, but ambition rarely waits for those too small to see the light.
