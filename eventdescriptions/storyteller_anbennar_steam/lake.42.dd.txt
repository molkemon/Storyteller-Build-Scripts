In the year 473 AA, prospectors from our fair city found gold on the isle of Ultakal. As the sole source of the precious metal in all of the Four Lands, our neighbor and sister city of Khugatseig was overcome with greed, nearly driving our people to war until the statesmen of Haraigizenhi Bulrek managed to broker peace. Since then, our two cities have traded ownership of the island every 5 years without incident. The most recent trade has gone as normal, and we currently hold sovereignty over the Isle of Gold.
