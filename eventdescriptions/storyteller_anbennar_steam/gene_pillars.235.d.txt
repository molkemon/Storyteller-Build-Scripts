A new room opens up before the expedition. They can tell it has many defense systems, mainly because they had all activated before they even entered. The whole room is in ruins; there was fire everywhere and bound spells were still being unleashed, though they weren’t even directed at the expedition. Their host spoke up once more. "Looks like I finished just in time. They were planning to fully enslave us with what they were developing here, a way to force mechanim into following orders and suppressing their personalities. I should have destroyed it long ago; I never did because I always figured I would have more time. Sorry, but I used up all the death traps here; there are none left for you. You will have to manage without constant danger for the moment." Clearly a lot of effort has been put into destroying the research; there was no chance of recovering it. Though not aimed at them, the sheer amount of mundane and magical threats would make traveling the room difficult.
