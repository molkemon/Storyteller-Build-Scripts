Their armor gleaming in the light of the torches, the spiders barrel through the lines of troops, scattering goblins left and right, and they lie about with leg and fang. Discarded torches and weapons litter the cavern floor alongside the bodies of goblins and spiders alike, but it soon becomes clear the forces are beginning to be overwhelmed. They manage to flee further down the tunnel in the confusion of the fight, but not without leaving behind a sizable chunk of their own force.
