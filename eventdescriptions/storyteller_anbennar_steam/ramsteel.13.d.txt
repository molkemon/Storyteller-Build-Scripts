Eyes sunken with lack of sleep yet feverishly bright, the advisor enters the council chamber. The advisor's undergarment is soaked through with sweat, but they do not seem to notice as they excitedly waves for attention. "A breakthrough," they stammer, out of breath. After they calm down, the advisor details the progress made. Apparently, a method has been found to drain the Common Terrace through Hornriver into Uthojan's Eye, which would open the Eastern Vale up for development and use again. The rest of the Vale remains unfixable until some indeterminate time, but even this one area would be a major boon for our interests. Its repair would allow us to start building up the dwarven breadbasket once again and even let some of our flocks return to their homelands.
