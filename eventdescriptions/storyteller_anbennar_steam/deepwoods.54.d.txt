Incredibly, our entire expedition to the north has returned safely. Not only this, they managed to return with a half dozen extra souls. a human adventurer couple, a gnome claiming to be from a large empire, and a trio of Wood Elves who followed Corin out of the woods some time ago. From this motley bunch and our own expeditionaries, we have learned that the land to our north is known as Escann, which apparently stems from the term "Eastern Cannor". Escann is a land ripe with fertile land and apparently home to a number of honorable and chivalric kingdoms before the orks swept through some decades ago. Now, though, it is a place rife with strife and chaos. For even though the orkish armies have been defeated, various bands of adventurers from the distant lands of Anbennar, Lorent, and beyond are still fighting to drive back the remaining orkish clans and the clans of freed goblin slaves who have staked a claim there.
