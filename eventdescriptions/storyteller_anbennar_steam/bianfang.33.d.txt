Word has reached the monarch. a clique of palace eunuchs has assassinated the heir. Apparently scheming to place one of the monarch’s nephews on the throne, they slit the heir’s throat as they slept. Having been promptly seized by the palace guard, the clique beseeched the monarch to spare them. They claimed all signs pointed to the heir ruling as a tyrant of the first degree when they were crowned monarch, a threat to the tranquility and prosperity of the realm, whom they were compelled to prevent from ever ascending to the Dragon Throne. Their pathetic mewling was cut short only by the blade of the Palace Marshal, methodically decapitating them one by one. The monarch now faces the choice of what to do with the rest of the sorry lot. Proclaiming their innocence and begging for mercy, the remaining eunuchs would surely be loyal if spared, and unable to amass the influence which had allowed the clique to get away with such a brazen plan. However, the spilling of noble blood by lowly commoners such as eunuchs is a crime of the highest degree, and must be treated accordingly. Justice must be done, and be seen to be done.
