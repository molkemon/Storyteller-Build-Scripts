We are used to dominating the Dwarovar. Walls fall before our onslaught, the dwarves are crushed into the ground they love, while goblins leave a green smear on cavern walls. But the hobgoblins reminded us why the Dwarovar is a place of blood and brutal battle, even for us orks. Their solid lines would not break before a mighty charge, even when most would turn and run. Their well-trained soldiers would not shatter before sword and axe and orkish might. Their discipline could withstand bloody losses, and not crumble. But they fell, nonetheless. We have taken Grônstunad, the brightest of the holds they rule. And all their solid forces, their impressive armies, well, it has just made an excellent whetstone on which to sharpen the blade of our troops.
