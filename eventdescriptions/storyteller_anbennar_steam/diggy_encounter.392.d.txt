With the troops at ease, the water slowly rose, but since this hallway seemed so big, any fear abated. And when the water seemed to halt about at ankle height, everyone became cheerful again and continued onwards. Then, suddenly, more parts of the wall gave way to the water masses, flooding the cave faster and faster as more walls broke down. Then a loud crack from above announced the end. the ceiling broke open, burying some under the fallen rocks, and the rest under the subterranean lake that came crashing down. Some managed to find small pockets of air where they could hold out for some time, but soon the oxygen became scarce and dizziness approached, ultimately suffocating those who had not drowned.
