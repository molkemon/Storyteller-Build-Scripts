Upon entering the vast central square of the upper level of Orlghelovar, we noticed how strangely bright it was, even without a single lit up torch in sight. Looking up, some of our orks were nearly blinded by stray beams of light that scattered between mirrors in a strange pattern, some leading to a window of a ruined structure, while others led to peculiar spots on the vast square. They spotlighted great unkempt trees and other flora, still growing in these depths despite there being no sign of other life. So long have these old inventions functioned despite the death of their makers millennia ago, maintaining such vegetation which has no business surviving in such a desolate place. While we wreak havoc on our most hated enemies, life still continues to thrive and reclaims the remains left behind.
