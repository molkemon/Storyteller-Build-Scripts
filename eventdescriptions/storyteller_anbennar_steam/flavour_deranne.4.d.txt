Trust is a fragile thing, and deceit all too tempting. When we replaced the Lorentish banner with the one of the Seaflower to fly high in the winds above Lorentainé, we knew the fight wasn’t over. The armies of the Rose may have been scattered and their leadership broken, but the spirit and disobedience of its people simmered beneath. Nowhere is that as evident as with the entrenched nobility. Scheming, cliques, and knives in the dark abound; people who fear the prospect of new masters, others who see the chaos as a way to climb the ladder of thorns. Questioning our right to the Ruby Crown, they undermine our very authority, and thus threaten the stability of the realm.
