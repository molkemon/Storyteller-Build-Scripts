One step on a branch. And then another. And then a leap, a skilled maneuver soaring briefly through the air to drop upon the golem’s head. Even as they wrenched fiercely to try to decapitate this foe, the golem shook mightily. It was all the wouldbe Castan could do to hold on, and though they clearly damaged the golem with their struggles, they were ultimately flung off. It’s definitely angry now, at least.
