When Umorozi comes back the third time, the Vile One makes for her a beautiful foghewn arm. Umorozi can throw a javelin clean across a battlefield; from the ground, she can inscribe symbols, messages, images in the sky. Nothing in that hand can be tugged from her grip. Her muscles do not glimmer in the sun any more; they defeat the light. When Umorozi walks through her Mistress’s fog, her arm disappears entirely.
