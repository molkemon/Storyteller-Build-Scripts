The monarch often goes out on fishing trips with their beloved pet otter, and on this day they decided to bring along some of the members of the court. Despite not visiting the good fishing spots, in fear of clothing being damaged by rough terrain, the monarch still received a catch the entire court would need days to eat through. The whole party confessed themselves thoroughly impressed by the efficiency and playfulness of the otter’s display, and they returned to the capital a great deal more cheerful than they left.
