As we round the corner, we find ourselves standing face to face with a large fuzzy beast. At first, we think it might be another hinuisquirrel, but we soon realize it is a hinuimarten. The hinuisquirrel we met had little interest in harming us and was just trying to avoid being captured. This beast, however, immediately charges at us. Its teeth are sharp as razors, and some of its fur is covered in the blood of some previous victim.
