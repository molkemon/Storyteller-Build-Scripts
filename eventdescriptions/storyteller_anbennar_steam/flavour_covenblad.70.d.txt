It started simply enough, with one of our knights choosing to risk civilians in the name of a greater monster threat. He was admonished to a great extent though by his knight captain and this radicalised his fellow knights. The desire for radical steps in the name of greater peace has thus seeped into many of the chapters and local keeps. The challenges of the new Escann require sacrifices to maintain its security, but are those the steps we take or our ideals themselves?
