The great stormwall of the Kiamoa has weakened. Although we can now journey through it to explore what lies beyond, it is now also possible for what is beyond the Kiamoa to enter Insyaa. Was the Kiamoa a prison or a refuge? We cannot say, at least not yet. What does this fundamental change mean for the lands of the Volcanic Truth? Are the Twin Gods aware of what lies outside the Kiamoa? How will we as a faith respond to it? These are questions beyond our ability to answer alone. We must call together the great synod of the Volcanic Truth, and go to ask the Twin Gods.
