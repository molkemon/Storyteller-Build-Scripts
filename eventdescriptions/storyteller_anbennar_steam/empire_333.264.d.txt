Their language is borderline impossible for our people to pronounce, with a word used to describe their unique mutation that allows them to breathe underwater. Their movement on land is more of a waddle than a sprint. They seem to have more in common with merfolk than us landloving lizardfolk. Yet despite all these problems, the letters being sent back by the Shagatan natives who moved to our world are full of nothing but praise for life here. They regularly talk about how much they love living in the coastal villages, swimming off the coast of East Sarhal, and enjoying the local cuisine, especially the latter. It seems the taste for fried tchoken is universal amongst lizardfolk no matter what planet they are from. Their effusive letters home have inspired so many more lizardfolk from Shagatan to make the trip to find a new life on Halann.
