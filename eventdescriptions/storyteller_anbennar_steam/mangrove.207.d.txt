While there are few rules or restrictions on the practice of chronicling or who can participate, there are a few requirements that need to be adhered to. In general, it is not a productive endeavor to have a young chronicler blindly redo the work of a more experienced elder. Yet that is exactly what has happened here, the younger man having wasted weeks of time reworking old ideas and the elder, insulted his work, has been ignored and disagreed with.
