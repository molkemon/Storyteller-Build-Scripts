The reign of the lord is all but absolute. The Princes have secured power over the realm for a long time ahead. Everything points to the lord ruling for their entire life. The spymaster, one of their closest advisors, agrees with this assessment. This is a person fiercely loyal to their country, so loyal, in fact, that they would personally take the life of anyone who threatens the state. In a recent meeting with a few carefully selected people, the spymaster let it be known that the lord and the Monarchists’ stranglehold on the ruler ship is perhaps the biggest threat of all. The spymaster can assure that the lifetime rule will be a short one.
