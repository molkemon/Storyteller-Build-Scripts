My Caehn, your undue refusal to the call of war has caused a segment of our tribe to lose faith in your capabilities. There have been rumors of potentially overthrowing your wise leadership for a more capable Caehn. We have caught some of the perpetrators that were spreading scandalous ideas among the population, and if given funding, we could further crack down on these ill-minded clans and engrave your rule with an iron hoof.
