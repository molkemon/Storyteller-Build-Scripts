Satyrs: horned and hoofed forest creatures obsessed with drink and debaucherous rituals; not so different from a mead halls resident drunkard. And like the drunkard, they don't know when to stop drinking and partying. While an Einher is often fond of drink themselves, it takes a real hero to wrap up the celebrations and ensure that all are safe at sleep til morrow. While this is not an especially bloody and violent quest, some consider it to be the hardest task of their career.
