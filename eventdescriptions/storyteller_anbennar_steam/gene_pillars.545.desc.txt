At first the room just seems like one of the many overgrown rooms within the pillar. Then we start to realize that there is a war ongoing, but not one we would have to worry ourselves with. The trees on one side of the room are needlecarrying softwoods and the trees on the other are leafed hardwoods. These two groups of trees seem to have been carrying out a centuries long war against each other. A battleline can be observed along the center of the room: Trees growing into each other, trees driving needles into one another, trees growing deliberately to cast shade on saplings, and much more. It seems that up until now the trees have been in an eternal stalemate.
