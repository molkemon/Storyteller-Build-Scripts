It is hard to admit that something you thought would protect you was inadequate for the job it was intended for. It is harder to accept that someone else took advantage of your weakness and therefore laid you low. But it is hardest to see you have failed, and do nothing. To allow others to leave you at their mercy when you now know how to improve. Perhaps the weak will give in to those tyrants and bend the knee, but we will not be so easily cowed. While we still have time, we must learn to adapt to this new way of war that the Ahetl Mulyatli have shown us under the rule of their Warrior King: a man covered with lava and magical artifacts, leading from the front and devastating those he encounters. If we could somehow match his techniques and philosophy, then we too could accomplish his feats.
