Thundrous footsteps in the distance. A trail of devastation. Death in the air. We have only recently learned to move, to walk, to wonder. But now we learn something equally important: a way our journey may meet its end. These colossal beasts, termed Holohana, dwarf even the hinuibeasts that we thought were the land's worst scourges. The Holohana are also stranger than most we encounter. For all we do not truly know the meaning of the word, they look unnatural. Whatever their origin, they promise us only death and destruction. We must be cautious in stepping too nearby, lest they decide to tread atop us and destroy us in an instant.
