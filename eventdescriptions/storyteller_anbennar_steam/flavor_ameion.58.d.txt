Over a millennium ago, the Kheions overthrew their monarchs after the First League War showed such figureheads to be incompetent. Since then, only once has the Cleaved Sea seen a crowned head rule on its shores; and Alecand sent sixty thousand elves to cast them down. Since that time, no Kheionai bloodline has worn a crown, and the Laskaris descended claimant shall be no exception.
