Even though the oni rule of Yuanszi is an inevitable conclusion, there are a number of methods to achieve that goal.  The first is to demand their submission as an effective vassalstate, where we might exert control of their government as necessary, and perhaps later integrate them into the greater administration.  Alternatively, we might bring them into the fold as a Rethagi, an area akin to a viceroyalty ruled by a human governor under oni supervision. Though we could not integrate them further without considerable effort, they would not be considered a diplomatic relationship.  Then again, there is always conquest.
