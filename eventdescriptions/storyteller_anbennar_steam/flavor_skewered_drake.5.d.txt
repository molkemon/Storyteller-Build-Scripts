The peaceful trolls of Grizehud are truly an odd bunch. Most people would have roared in defiance and even started rebellions when being told that they would be tasked with mining duties; however, the trolls happily accepted the terms of our gold mining operations, as long as we fulfilled two requests. They requested that we entrust the fort we had constructed to collect the mined gold over to them in order for them to protect their friends' treasures. And they have asked for some new goblin and dwarven friends to be brought to them to help them with the mining and start new lives in their domain. While the former request turned a few heads, it was eventually acquiesced to, as these trolls have proven to not have a deceitful bone in their body. As for the second request, all we can say is let them have their friends.
