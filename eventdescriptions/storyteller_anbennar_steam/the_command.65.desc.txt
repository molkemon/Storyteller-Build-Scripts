In hobgoblin society, everyone must prove their worth. To ensure that every individual does their part and contributes to the best of their ability, their skills must be tested and competencies assessed. We will create a bureau, the Shokaitei, which will decide what the best career path is for each individual. These roles will be open regardless of origin or ancestry, and any path can be suggested and awarded if candidates prove they possess the required skills or knowledge. If individuals underperform, the Shokaitei will have the power to transfer them to a more suitable position. In this way, all will have a place where they work best, serving society with whatever gifts they have been born with.
