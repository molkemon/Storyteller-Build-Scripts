Memories from without are not the only target of the strange systems preserving this place. Passing by the old command structures, signature memories of the party members that have interfaced with the memory banks are projected into the minds of the adventurers, filling their heads with strange thoughts of their fellow party members. An insult here, a slight there, constant snipping back and forth. With the experiences these people have together, surely some tricks of the mind cannot tear them apart...right? This is proven incorrect in the depths of night, as two members wrestle each other to the ground with murderous intent, knives drawn at each other’s throats.
