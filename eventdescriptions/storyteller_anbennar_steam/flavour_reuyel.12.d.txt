And so it has been completed, a massive vanguard of the old way has been steadfastly established in Bahar; and with that, no matter what the misguided followers of Taelarios will do, it will be made clear for all across Bahar that we are the true rulers of this land. While at first our rebellion produced a baseless hatred for the elves within the general populace, we have now found a newfound respect and tolerance for them. Though they are indeed not the heirs of Surael, their previous conviction of this falsity seems to have been an honest one, and not just a ploy for power. This fact, added with their age and experience, means that we have found a usefulness in them as advisors for our mages. Additionally, many of them may be employed as our officers, for in Bahar we can show that the old way allows us to coexist with the elves as equals, under the flag of ReUyel.
