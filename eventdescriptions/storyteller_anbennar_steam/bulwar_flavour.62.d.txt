Tales of the prosperity and safety found within our realm, for those who have stayed true to the faith of their ancestors, have spread far and wide. Many amongst the faithful, who are forced to practice their faith in secret under the yoke of heretics or unbelievers, yearn for the freedom to worship Surakel as we do, openly and without the ever constant threat of persecution. Hoping to find shelter within our ranks, many adherents of these oppressed cults have given up their old lives and made the perilous journey to our realm. While their beliefs might differ in certain aspects from our own, they are nonetheless our brothers and sisters in the eyes of Surakel. Sheltering them is therefore our sacred duty. However, the question of whether they should conform to our state cult, or whether their ancestral beliefs have sufficient merit to warrant a place within the tapestry of our people’s faith, is less easily answered. Their unique interpretation of the Devotion to the Light might well be a blessing from Surakel to guide us in our path forward, but we cannot possibly pay homage to all the manifold cults that have arisen across the domain over the millennia. Therefore we must now choose whether to embrace a new cult in addition to our own, and if yes, which cult in particular.
