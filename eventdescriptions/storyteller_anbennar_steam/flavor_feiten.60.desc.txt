We have hunted whales for generations, and even children can help load the salted provisions and harpoons that require. More recently, we have learned to hunt trade and commerce, which requires a careful studying of price and demand before loading the optimal cargo. But we have never had to outfit an expedition like this one. Because today we are sending an expedition to Cannor, to make our name on the world stage. We will not bring back carcass or coin, but scholar, philosopher, and artisan, seeking their finest minds to bring our airships to a new zenith. The aims are lofty, but the business at hand is more practical. Aside from the provision and fuel necessary for this epic voyage, there is a small storeroom earmarked for the ruler's discretion. What will the ruler decide is most important to bring for the journey?
