This section of the pillar is packed with countless metal rods, each crackling with some kind of magical energy. Ethereal blue mist seems to be absorbed by the structures, and after passing through more inscrutable machinery, it emerges from the other side transformed into the base form of the storm clouds found within the Kiamoa. The party moves carefully between the poles, when suddenly a nearby dormant structure powers up and begins to crackle as the hapless explorers pass by. They all feel some kind of force pulling at their innermost essence, as if their lives are being drained away merely by being in close proximity to these things. Whatever is happening, it appears to be supercharging the rods, and they look set to explode at any moment.
