The debate rages on, but some have grown sick of arguing, and want to find an answer by deed instead. Our vassals have suggested that we pause the bickering to hold a tournament, aimed at determining whether a single centralized army can outperform against a larger, decentralized, army. The structure is clear: the ruler will wield absolute control over 100 troops while each of our vassals will oppose us with 50 troops a piece. This may be mock combat, but it could prove whether the new empire is best with a strong central power or a decentralized one.
