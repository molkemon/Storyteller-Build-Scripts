Some people take a foul, sadomasochistic joy in the Bloodcurse. In the throes of their depravity, they seek to destroy all means to free ourselves from the disease. They infiltrate ritual bleedings and slaughter participants just as readily as they massacre artisans and burn workshops. Many advocate that we hunt down these deviants and put an end to their reign of terror. Others theorize that these behaviors may be the symptoms of a more advanced form of the Bloodcurse, and with adequate treatment these loathsome creatures may yet become sane once more.
