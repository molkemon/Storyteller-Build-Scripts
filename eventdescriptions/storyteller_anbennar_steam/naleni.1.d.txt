The nation of Naleni is a mess. A crude amalgamation of a pirate republic and a traditional harpy matriarchy, factional politics divide the realm and jam any sort of consensus, making exercising central authority extremely difficult. Power is split between the Matriarchy itself, led by the Nephiueda dynasty, and the various independent Odanchesta, pirate captains in common, themselves arranged in a loose and often fractious coalition united only in resisting the authority of the Matriarchy.  Peace in the realm is maintained by a historic compromise known as the Mureda Accord, so named for the Matriarch who negotiated its terms. Under it the Matriarch retains formal authority, but a coruler is ‘elected’ from amongst the ranks of the Odanchesta every four years to serve as idar Odanches, or Dread Captain, a quasimilitary ruler who shares power with the Matriarch. These elections are less voting, and more candidates scrambling for the most muscle to support them.  The accord has served Naleni well until now due to the lack of outside threats, given its isolation on the edge of the Great Horn, but it has begun to fray with the arrival of the Raheni colonisers. The Matriarchy stirs as it sees its realm under threat, whilst the Odanchesta enjoy the bounty of plundering Raheni trade, the two groups' combined interests dwindling.  The current Matriarch's daughter, Bimogire, has just been elected idar Odanches, providing a rare moment of semiunity. Despite this, it will take a careful hand to steer the ship of state; bolstering the Matriarchys authority whilst balancing the interests of the Odanchesta.
