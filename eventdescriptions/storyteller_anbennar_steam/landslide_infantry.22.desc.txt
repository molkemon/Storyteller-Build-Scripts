The Landslide Infantry have never been more destructive to anything than themselves. They have harmed others both intentionally and unintentionally, but none have to spend more time around them than each other. In a new age of discipline and military structure, it is necessary for the Landslide Infantry to finally finish what they have started and become a fully modern military force. This means doing away with the last of their habits that break their cohesion and destroy military structures.
