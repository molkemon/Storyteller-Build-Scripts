In your quest to find a less submerged path down, your group suddenly tumbles its way into a dry, labyrinthine collection of glass tubes, tall enough for even the largest ogre to stand upright within. It seems you can breathe freely in here, though an acrid, chemical odor pervades through the halls and rusts your equipment. Worse, there are inexplicable cliffs and sheer drops throughout the maze. If the space were less hostile and more navigable, these dry corridors might provide a useful space in which to erect a forward base; future expeditions will have to deal with the crushing pressure, and again, some ineffable disturbing quality of the pillar leaves you with the distinct feeling a cleric’s support will prove useful.
