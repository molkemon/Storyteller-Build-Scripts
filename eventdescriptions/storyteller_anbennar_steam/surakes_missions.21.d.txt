Brasan the Emancipator was a Djinn who taught the humans of ancient Bulwar the art of genie binding which allowed them to overthrow their genie masters, his own people, and eventually become the GodKings of Bulwar. It is by his teachings that our people were freed of their first overlords, and we would have ourselves seen as their most recent emancipators, freeing our people from their sun elven oppressors.
