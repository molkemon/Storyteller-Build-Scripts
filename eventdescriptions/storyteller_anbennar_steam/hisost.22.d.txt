We swore that Amilak’s love transcended racial boundaries; we promised that gnolls and humans could live in harmony; we told our people that all could be Mengi. Now, under the leadership of the Yenaze Mezhi, we swear that Amilak favors only us, we promise that the gnolls will be subdued, we tell the people that the gnolls are beneath us. Such a flagrant change of our values has not gone unnoticed. Everywhere the people ask if we really know what Amilak wants, and if we do, then why has Amilak become so capricious? Some are wondering if Amilak is even speaking to us at all, or if the Semayabaka just say what they want to hear to make us believe they have His favor. Some are even wondering if Amilak is there at all… how else could such a blatant contradiction go unexplained? Most would never doubt their faith in Amilak, but their faith in us… not so much.
