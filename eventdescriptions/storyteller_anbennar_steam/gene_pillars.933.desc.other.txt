The symbols around this room are still inert, their infrequent flickers casting odd shadows across the room. The chamber seems focused on a possible magitechnical artefact in place on the far wall. Without power, there is no true way in determining the purpose of such a thing from observation alone. Fortunately for your team and your scholars, there are copious notebooks left around the space, and it does not take long to start understanding the purpose of the device. The artefact is called a "Draconic Suppression Matrix," and it was designed to enable the researchers here to better control their Draconic test subject. It is supposedly able to emit a frequency that will cause pain in them while active, and through repeated exposure, it will assist in the control and training of them.
