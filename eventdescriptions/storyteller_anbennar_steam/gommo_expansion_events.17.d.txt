The gnomish philosophers, primarily those affiliated with the Gommo guild, have revitalized the theological and philosophical culture in their domain. It is amazing what new thinkers can do to centuries of eunuch-dominated debates. They brought a number of foreign schools of thought with them, and have even turned their gaze inward, compiling a massive taxonomy of the various domain schools of thought and teaching practices. Now the Gommo has petitioned us to allow them to establish their own philosophical academy in the domain in order to educate any and all who wish to learn in their modern school of thought. Though this would be an intrusion into the eunuchs' standard practice, everyone from monks to priests to bureaucrats to philosopher kings would undoubtedly have much to learn from them. The corresponding influence we would have in Haless's philosophical and theological debates would be considerable. Refusing such an institution might see the Gommo take their schools elsewhere, leaving the domain and its towering temples behind.
