For their assistance and stalwart friendship over these past many years, the citrine dwarves of Seghdihr have forged for their monarch a beautiful, jewelled diadem. Made of the highest purity gold from Verkal Gulan, in it is set a sun motif made with gems with a great ruby at the center. On the steps of the great temple of AzkaSur, the dwarves’ procession gathers round the monarch and presents the ruler the Sun Diadem, proclaiming it a symbol of the unity between the surani and citrine dwarves. So long as their ruler wears this, Seghdihr will stand with them. As their monarch stands, the sun reflects from the gems on the Diadem, and to the people below, it is as though the sun itself were shining from them.
