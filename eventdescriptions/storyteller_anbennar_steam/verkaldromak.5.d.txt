The mages' guild is increasingly displeased with the decisions of the ruler pertaining to the sleepers. This situation is stirring trouble not only in our realm, but trickling into the world beyond. Mages everywhere are dispatching delegations and communiques to request changes in our policy, and even the ruler of the Magisterium has reached out. The esteemed scholar and well-trained mage notes that several previous catastrophes have afflicted once mighty realms over similar power dynamics; alternative power structures with unregulated magic potential dissolving great empires and leading to untold suffering.
