Missives have entered your chambers talking of a meticulously tended garden, unlike any cultivation the realm has seen before, hidden in the heights of the Great Plateau. They speak of rows of a peculiar root stretching across terraced slopes, their leaves a deep, almost black green. The advisors huddle amongst themselves, then split into two camps. One side opposes anything to do with it. "Let's leave it all together, my ruler; the beasts who inhabit the planes are far too cruel to anger." The latter group insists that an expedition is sent.
