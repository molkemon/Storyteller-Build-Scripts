Many millennia ago, we fought in the grand campaign against the invaders of Dwarovar. While our allies were fighting a losing battle, we chose to find a new path to win the war. It is there that we uncovered the holy secrets of blood rune magic, which helped turn the tide against the invaders. Our so-called kin, in blind jealousy, rallied together and marched to put down those who saved them. It was in these crucial moments the Nameless King sacrificed himself for our people, and plunged us into the Serpentdepths. In so doing, the Nameless King ascended to become the Eternal Runefather, and we began rebuilding our empire in the deep depths of the world. When we emerged thousands of years later, we found that without our help, the dwarves lost everything, as savages and monsters overran them. Yet, new Dwarven nations are forming across the caverns, seeking to steal the Runefather’s birthright.
