The shamans have been unable to find much in this fourth layer of the hold, aside from the trillions of mushrooms. They seem to come in endless shapes and colors. red, white, big and brown, purple and glowing, sickening, poisonous, illusory, magical, rejuvenating, hallucinogenic; the list is endless. The shamans have taken a liking to some of them, using them for rituals and for attempting to commune with Dookan. Regardless, they order the men to keep digging down.
