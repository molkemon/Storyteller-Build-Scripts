Give the masses Khapati bread and shadow play, and revolts will go away. These are the wise words of a long lost Shamakadi ruler and they still ring true today. Giving the common folk distraction always works and the nation is in for a treat. A circus that specialises in shadow play has arrived in the capital. Shadow play is the art of using shadows on a large sheet of cloth to tell stories of far away and of older times. While they normally just come and go, we may want to invite them to perform in the royal palace or give them some extra financial support.
