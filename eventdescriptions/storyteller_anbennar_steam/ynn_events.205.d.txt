Today a remarkable visitor clad in robes and wielding an antler wand came before the monarch, offering their service. While many wielders of magic exist throughout the Ynn, such as the harvest mages of Stantirshalas and the watercasting Riverblades of Vels Bacar, the magical studies they conduct are more often than not secondary to their main skillset and so it is hard to ascertain their true power and knowledge. The mage standing before the ruler today, however, has proof of studying at the Academy of Rzenta, a magical institution established in imperial times at the border of the fey-infested forests northwest of Dolindha. A mage endorsed by the academy is no party performer, having passed rigorous study under talented teachers to be approved by the faculty. This mage would be of great aid to either administration or war efforts.
