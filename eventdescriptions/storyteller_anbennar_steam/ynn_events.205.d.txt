Today a remarkable visitor clad in robes and wielding an antler wand came before the monarch, offering their service. While many wielders of magic exist throughout the land, such as the harvest mages of a distant province and the watercasting Riverblades of another region, the magical studies they conduct are more often than not secondary to their main skillset and so it is hard to ascertain their true power and knowledge. The mage standing before the monarch today, however, has proof of studying at a renowned academy, a magical institution established in imperial times at the border of the feyinfested forests northwest of a known city. A mage endorsed by the academy is no party performer, having passed rigorous study under talented teachers to be approved by the faculty. This mage would be of great aid to either administration or war efforts.
