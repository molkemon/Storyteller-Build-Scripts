William Jones, a poet and scholar from his province has made a name for himself among his people by distributing a large number of nationalist pamphlets and even composing a national anthem. Denouncing what he describes as our oppression and drawing on the ideals of the revolution, he calls on his countrymen to pack their bags, quit their homeland and seek freedom in the 'Promised Land' of the New World. It seems that his words have struck a chord among the people, with one influential nobleman praising him as "the hottest arsed person I have ever met". Already, many of his countrymen, radicalized by his ideas, are departing for their country.
