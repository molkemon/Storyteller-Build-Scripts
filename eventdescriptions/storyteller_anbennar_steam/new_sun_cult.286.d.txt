We aimed to stop the GodKing. We failed. His sandstorms blew away every arrow and spear thrown at him. Our armies could not fight his summoned monsters. And his magics surpassed all the arcane might we could hope to muster. We have lost a battle, but hopefully, not the war. The ruler shudders to imagine what Amussu's strength might be with more relics in his grasp, but we yet have time to regroup our forces and ready for another assault.
