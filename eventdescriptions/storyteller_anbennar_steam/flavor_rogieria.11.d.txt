The last member of the council closed the door as he left the throne room, leaving Rogier alone, illuminated by dim candlelight. The Young Owl turned his attention back to the map spread out on the table. A map of his domains, of his legacy.
In the east, the western vale, the lands of his wife, of Farranean. A beautiful land of bustling cities and farmlands whose people love him as the restorer of the kingdom. In the core of his realm, the Dameria of the East. Successor of the Chivalric Adenica, home of the exiled Damerians and the location of his capital city.
In the northern woods and swamps, the lands of Balmire. The shores of the Alen are home to countless workshops, protected by the great fortresses of Bal Mire and Adennthil.
Rogier contemplates the map again, the legacy that he, the King of the West built, the most powerful kingdom in Escann, Rogeria.
