The renegade philosopher has not shied back from expressing opinions contrary to those of both the ruler and the agreed revelations of the Ravelian Church. Their latest publishings appear to have been particularly provocative, so much so that the Church is demanding we hand them over to their inquisitors to be tried for heresy and persuaded to recant. Denying them this would anger the Church greatly, but the philosopher has many supporters among the intelligent intelligentsia.
