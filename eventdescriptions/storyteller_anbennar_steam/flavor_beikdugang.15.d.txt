It seems the tides of fate are changing. The ancient institution of the eunuchs is under attack all across the realm. It seems they are on the way out. If there was ever a moment to rid the nation of eunuchs as an institution, now would be it, and we would score favor with a rising power at the same time. Still, men are often mistaken about matters of fate; perhaps the age of the eunuch is not over yet.
