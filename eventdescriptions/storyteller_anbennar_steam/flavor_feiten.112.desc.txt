As the main port of entry for many foreigners, the city has naturally become one of the epicenters of Ravelian spread in our nation. So much so, that buzz around establishing an "official" society has gained momentum. We may be able to leverage the resources that this would open up to us to help spread Ravelianism in the rest of our nation, but at the same time, many are rightly wary of this encroachment of foreign influence.
