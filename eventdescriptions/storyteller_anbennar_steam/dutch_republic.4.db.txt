With a ruler like the monarch, most nobles agree that the control she and the Sparrows exert over mercantile policy is used wisely. They say that the alternative of Peacocklead policy decisions would only destabilize and weaken the nation. Many traders, with the words of the Merchant Matriarchs in their ears, disagree. These traders say that an open market is crucial to ensure the nation's continued economic priorities. Under an autocratic ruler with expansionist ambitions, the freedoms of commerce they have enjoyed in the past may be threatened. If our ruler is to wield the Sparrows wisely as she claims, they demand she shows dedication to protecting their economic liberty.
