Mark this day as the shattering of Western Cannor by the ravenous hosts of the Gorger Lord. The elves, men, dwarves, halflings, and all others who call this place home have had their lands rent asunder and their families feasted upon. The people of Western Cannor shall bear witness to our slavering magnificence and sit in despair as all they hold dear finds a new home within our cavernous bellies. Those foolish enough to stay will watch as tribes of ogres claim what was once theirs before they too are hunted and devoured and the cowards that flee will only be delaying the inevitable and will die tired. The allconsuming ogre horde will come for all in the end. We are the doom of their world.
