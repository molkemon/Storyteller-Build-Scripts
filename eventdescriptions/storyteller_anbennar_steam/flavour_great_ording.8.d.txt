Following their victory at the Morban Flats, the Castanorian forces marched on Calascandar, hoping to break the legendary fortress. Meanwhile, however, the previously defeated Free Realms army had rallied in Gawed and had rapidly marched south, seeking and, with the assistance of the defenders of Calascandar, broke the siege and destroyed the Castanorian army there, leaving Escann wide open for a Free Realms invasion.
