An increased number of tributary missions to our lord have led to the port of the trading city becoming the main point of entry for ships from our overlord. The province has also started to attract greater numbers of traders and adventurers, further allowing the region to prosper. Some undoubtedly see it as a good way to avoid scrutiny from customs and law enforcement, however. Encouraging these fortune seekers will likely prolong the economic boom of the trading city, but it might still not be in our best interest.
