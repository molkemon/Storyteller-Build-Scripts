Rahen finds itself in turmoil, as a guerrilla group called "The Blood Lotus" has taken up arms against what they claim to be the injustices of the caste system. Their cause is surely just, as humans have suffered centuries of oppression in the Harimraj. An opportunity has presented itself, and we could with minimal effort establish a line of communication with these guerrillas and send provisions to aid in their struggles against another country. The question is. should we?
