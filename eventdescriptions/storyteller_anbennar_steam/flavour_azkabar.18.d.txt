"You have to believe me," the clergyman roared, his exhausted and terrified face speaking volumes, even louder than his words. "The Clergy is being infiltrated on all levels. Please, they are trying to silence me." A sharp thud from a guard caused him to stumble to the ground, colliding with a thud, before promptly being hauled to his feet and forced forward into the abyssally black walls of the famous prison. No one really took notice of a particularly zealous member of the Irrliamzur being arrested for slanderous rumors, not even his fellow brothers in the faith.
