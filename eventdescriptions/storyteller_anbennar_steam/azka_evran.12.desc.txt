The Library of Aqatbar groans, its shelves heaving a mighty sigh. But this is not a groan of discontent, nor a groan of burden. It is a groan of hunger, of responsibility, the library itself eager to fulfill its duties and become the repository of all knowledge in Halcann. Here and there are empty shelves, outofdate reference texts, installations of rare volumes missing their brethren. We shall sweep away these tiny flaws nowpick up a pen, let us begin to draft our letters of request. But to where shall we send them?
