The family has blessed us. The monarch has recovered from his wounds. He is still bedridden, but the bleeding has stopped and the weakness has seemingly dissipated. As soon as he awoke, speculation began as to those behind the attack. The ruler thinks that magic may have been responsible for both his weakness and the end of the assassin, or even the death of his mother. He has vowed that the assassins, all who supported them, and all who harbored them will pay the ultimate price. Otherwise, they may strike again.
