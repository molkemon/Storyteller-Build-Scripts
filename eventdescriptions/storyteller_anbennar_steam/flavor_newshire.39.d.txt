Ella drinks her potion. The Verdant Seed looms above her. When she awakes, she is silent, thoughtful. The Seed is the same as it was, but something seems different about it. A shift in the air, perhaps. Over the coming days, the roots and vines begin to recede, shrinking just behind the ruined walls of Castonath. Ella remains quiet on the matter, only saying that she had a chat with the Verdant Seed, whatever that means.
