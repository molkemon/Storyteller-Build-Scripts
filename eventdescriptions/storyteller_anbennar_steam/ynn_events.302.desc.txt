The snapping otter is one of the sixteen animals of the Ynnic Zodiac. They are known for their good nature and cleverness, and many Sarda train snapping otters to aid them in fishing, in a manner similar to hunting dogs. A fisherelf in the realm of the monarch has managed to train such a remarkable otter that it is spoken of several towns away. After a local baron witnessed the animal in action and commented that such a talented pet would serve as a gift for even the ruler, the fisherman decided to travel to the capital to present his liege with the otter as a gift.
