In Taychend, the death of a ruler is always seen as a good opportunity to exploit the weaknesses of an enemy. When those weaknesses are great, the resulting opportunity can be too; and such has occurred here. A terrible crisis has befallen our nation, as the death of our previous ruler has given those who despise us the confidence they need to declare war. Woe befalls the land, as every elf knows that dark days are ahead. However, if we can overcome this, we can shatter that false confidence of the enemy, sundering memories of our aggressive expansion alongside it; and our monarch will surely benefit from a glorious victory.
