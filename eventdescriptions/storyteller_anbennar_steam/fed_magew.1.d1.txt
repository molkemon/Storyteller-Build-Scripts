The Arcane Ponderance has seemingly always been a point of contention. It has divided politicians, generations, and cities. As such, it should come to no one's surprise that the most vile scar upon our people would one day lead to war and that bloodshed would beget bloodshed. Perhaps everyone thought that war between brothers was a thing of the past? That though the sisters would bicker, Kalyin would lead us to peace? Perhaps some simply kept their cards close to their chest. It matters little. What matters is that the ghosts of the past have finally caught up, and it is time to face them.
