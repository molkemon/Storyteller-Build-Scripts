The local residents of the rebel province are outraged by the new colonists taking away their livelihoods with support of the state, leaving them destitute. They demand that the settlers leave or that they help them relocate to another province. This is already bad enough, but now they have committed treason by raising arms to enforce this. It is a shame to waste perfectly good humans, but if they negotiate now, it will send the message that threatening violence is a way to get what they want, which is an unacceptable answer.
