As a Vampire Spawn, the responsibility lies with their Sire, the vampire who granted them the Blood Gift. As such, by vampiric tradition and custom, our leader is dutybound to follow whatever orders they receive – regardless of the crown on their head or the throne they sit atop. Recently, their Sire has begun making exorbitant demands of them, demanding they are granted privileges above and beyond that of any normal nobility. And today, another such demand arrived in the capital, deep red ink scratching out an absurd request on bonewhite paper. How shall the leader proceed?
