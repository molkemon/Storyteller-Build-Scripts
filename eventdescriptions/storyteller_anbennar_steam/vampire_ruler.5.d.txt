As a Vampire Spawn, the ruler is the responsibility of their Sire, the vampire who granted the ruler the Blood Gift. As such, by vampiric tradition and custom, their title is dutybound to follow whatever orders they receive, regardless of the crown on their head or the throne they sit atop. Recently the ruler's Sire has begun making exorbitant demands of them, demanding they are granted privileges above and beyond that of any normal nobility. And today, another such demand arrived in the capital, deep red ink scratching out an absurd request on bonewhite paper. How shall the ruler proceed?
