Today a pilgrimage that recently set out from our lands has returned to us safe and sound. They come bearing stories of visiting the Great Dam and basking in its glory. They drank from the Dam's massive reservoir, gawked in its shadow and felt the spirit of the Great Ynn pass through them as they visited the nearby town. They had especially good things to say about the local noble that rules the lands the Dam is on. They tell of her graciousness in allowing them through her lands and the safety that they felt on their travels through them.
