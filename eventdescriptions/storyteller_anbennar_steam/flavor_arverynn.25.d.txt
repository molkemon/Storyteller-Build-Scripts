The decade surrounding Emperor Fineas's death was a chaotic haze, events being blurred together, rearranged, and forgotten. The emperor, ever searching for an innovative solution to bring his Empire back from the brink, enlisted mages from near and far. Most promising among them was Doukanszrim, an affable old Veykodan with a slender body and a captivating smile, skillful with people as he was with substances. Doukanszrim enticed the emperor with his ideas to negate inequality and soothe the spirits of Arverynn's populace; however, at the time when he was needed the most, Doukanszrim fled and placed a spell on the populace to cover his tracks, leaving the Emperor at the mercy of his own frenzied subjects. Continuing to read through the Imperial Archives, one will learn that Kanvel, the defunct emperor's sister, repeatedly accused Doukanszrim for the disappearance of several items, including the Vyrekynn's personal keys, several tonnes of Dames tear, but also people and animals. most ominously, the emperor's pet otter which went missing exactly one year before Fineas's death. It is also believed that he fled to Munkarel, the same monastery Kanvel was exiled to, using the Vyrekynn keys to access the deepest level of the dungeon and make it into his hideout, playing a part in its eventual fall. With these thoughts in mind, the emperor launched an expedition into the Moon Field, using the pretext of Veykodan raids as a simple excuse to bring vengeance to the Vyrekynn family, finally unearthing the secrets beneath the Moon Mound.
