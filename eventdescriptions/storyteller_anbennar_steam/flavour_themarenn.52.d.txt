As he approached the Lake Palace, Casetin was filled with the sense of destiny he had always felt waiting for him. At fifteen he had bested every instructor his father could afford him, and so he requested to be sent to Arca Aldresia. He never intended to join the Knightly Order, just to fight and train amongst them, to become the man he felt he had to be, even if he never knew why. Despite the religious tension at the citadel of knights, he remained a fervent follower of Adean, never straying from his oaths. And now as his homeland had entered a time of troubles, he returned, to lead Themarenn, and the Empire if need be, against the Corinite Scourge.
