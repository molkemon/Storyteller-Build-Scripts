As we had wished, the stairs have not forsaken us. After a calm yet urgent walk, we can see the top of the chasm coming up ahead of us. After months in the deep, the promise of a return to ground level has the expedition excited, a grand prospect of victory.  With the cliff crested and conquered we can see the ghost just ahead of us, pestering others for a change. But it means that our business is close to ending, the enemy is in plain sight.
