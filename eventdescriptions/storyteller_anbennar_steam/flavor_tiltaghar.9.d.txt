The Command was birthed from the death of another empire, AulDwarov. The dwarves have long looked upon the Command with disdain, a hated enemy who conquered their eastern capital in their moment of weakness. We have long had friendly relations with the dwarven realms, serving as a gateway to the rest of the world through their tunnel networks. However, it was through their collapse that the Command could come into existence. Are we to uphold our oath to return their home, the mountain, to them, or do we need to ensure the safety of the Raj first and foremost?
