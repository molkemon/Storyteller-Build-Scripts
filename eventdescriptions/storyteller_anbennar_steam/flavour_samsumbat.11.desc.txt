With the authority vested in me by the citizens of this realm, the People's Council, and the Khet, I hereby proclaim that on this day, in the year currently, that all persons held as slaves or in any form of forced servitude anywhere within the jurisdiction of the Republic shall be henceforward and forever free. The government and military will recognise and maintain the freedom of such persons, and any efforts to repress the freedom of such persons will be treated as a criminal act. Thus it was spoken on the banks of the Sorrow by the new Steward. The old symbols and laws of oppression have been swept away, though not without consequence. Even as a group of former slaves are symbolically given a set of new clothes for their new life, many in the crowd are grumbling. The people from the south may have been fighting for this, but for those who have lived for generations in the great northern cities, relying on slave labour for religious rituals, farming, and education for their children, this decree represents great disruption. Time will tell if the predicted chaos can be overcome, or if the upending of centuries of traditions leads this realm to a violent end.
