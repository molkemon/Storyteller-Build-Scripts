The Ultakal decree has been in place for so long now, it has become one of the foundational pillars of the identity of our beloved city. It allowed us to be generous when others were strapped for cash. It brought us safety by giving everyone a vested interest in our continued independence. It provided us with the luxury of being able to act honorably. But now, this pillar is crumbling. There is turmoil in the streets. The other cities are shunning us. Our coffers are empty, while we should be sitting on a pile of gold. We have no other options than conceding to all demands made by Khugatseig, no matter how outrageous.
