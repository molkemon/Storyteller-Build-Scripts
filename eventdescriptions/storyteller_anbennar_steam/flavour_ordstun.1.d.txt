Over the past two decades, the orkish armies who once haunted our doors first scattered, then disappeared entirely. Our diviners, methodically scrying every cavern around us, have found only disorganized tribes without power or purpose. Something or someone has driven the orks away, and our Queen plans to seize this opportunity. Much of the hold’s populace, including its reformists and radicals, rally to the Queen's banner. Far more of it, though especially the old Clans, Lapidaries, and Stonekeepers stand against her. We may have defeated the Beholden for now, but their advocates still dominate our manors, guilds, and temples. They have been isolated from the world for millennia, and they do not plan on surrendering to our Queen even an ounce of the power hoarded in this time. Six thousand years ago, Sigrid Stormheart, the Diamond Queen, closed the gates of the hold. Now, we have a new Diamond Queen who has opened these gates to the world. By rebuking the very traditions which have kept this hold alive, she ignites the true war for the fate of our nation.
