The ruler is dead, their infernal flame burns no longer. The Xhazob to which they played host has returned to the outer planes and has not seen fit to grace the monarch with the same demonic gifts granted to their sire. Without the blessings of the Xhazob, it is unclear if the monarch will be able to hold on to the vast empire the ruler had established. Already, rebellious elements among the conquered peoples are beginning to cause chaos and gather their forces, supported by foreign powers seeking to liberate them from evil.
