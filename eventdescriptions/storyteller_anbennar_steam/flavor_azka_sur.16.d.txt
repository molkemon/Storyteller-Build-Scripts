Halfelves are often welleducated because of the education provided in the monasteries, notably in history or theology. Most halfelves study to attune with their dual nature and find their own truth, after which they are confronted by a Lamassu, a magical being who guarantees their sincerity. Those who have passed the attunement ceremony could already live in Bulwari society under the status of Purified, but they were often discriminated against. In these trying times, many of them have proposed to join the Regency as advisors, hoping to prove to the rest of Bulwari society that they are worthy of being free and productive members of society.
