Fangaula, the land of Authority, is more connected to the elemental planes than any other region on Halann. These ties are strong enough that one may find individuals who are uniquely touched by the planes, imbuing them with a wide range of elemental powers and abilities. While these planetouched people are exceedingly rare in the rest of Sarhal, they form a distinct minority within Fangaula. They can be born into all levels of society, as almost every child born in Fangaula may turn out to be planetouched, save for the direct offspring of a planetouched, who may never share their parent’s preternatural status. As such, they do not typically form a coherent social class, especially as the power disparity between one planetouched and another can be quite great. The two great empires of Fangaula, the first empire founded by Aro the Hero and the second empire of Biakata the Great, have left distinct legacies regarding the view of planetouched in Fangaula society. Tales of Aro’s empire, founded by the halfgenie and led by powerful planetouched heroes, tell us of a time when the power of Fangaula’s unique elemental ties was used freely, while Biakata’s empire kept a tight leash upon the planetouched, using their power while restraining their chaotic potential to safeguard the empire’s future. When the planetouched Banni slew the imperial family, many in Fangaula came to fear and resent the planetouched, everpresent prejudices turning into outright hatred.
