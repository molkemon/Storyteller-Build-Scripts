A debate has arisen in Roilsard about the location of the capital of our duchy, with two clear sides of the conflict having arisen.  The sil Saloren and their supporters argue the strategic location of Saloren, and it's already strong fortifications make its location a prime position for the capital.  The sil Roilsard however argue that Roilsard county is the historic location of the capital, and if we are truly to be the successors to Roilsard we should stick with tradition.
