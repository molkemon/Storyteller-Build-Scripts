A debate has arisen in Roilsard about the location of the capital of the duchy, with two clear sides of the conflict having arisen. The síl Saloren and their supporters argue the strategic location of Saloren, and its already strong fortifications make its location a prime position for the capital. The síl Roilsard however argue that Roilsard county is the historic location of the capital, and if they are truly to be the successors to Roilsard, they should stick with tradition.
