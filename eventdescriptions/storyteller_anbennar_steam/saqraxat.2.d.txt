We are the last true vestige of the Kheteratan Empire beyond the Sorrow. Throughout all the chaos caused by Grizaka’s invasion, we have remained steadfastly loyal, loyal to the legacy of Elikhet’s benevolent settlement of Horashesh, and loyal to his guiding vision of what this land could and should become. Our dedication has not gone unnoticed, and for our steadfast support in Kheterata’s time of most dire need, we shall be duly rewarded.
