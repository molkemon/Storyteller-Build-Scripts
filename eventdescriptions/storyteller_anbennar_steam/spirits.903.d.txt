The burden of governance sometimes lies heavy upon even the most accomplished ruler. Fortunately, for those who subscribe to the teachings of the Mystic Accord, the wisdom of the ancestors is but a prophetic dream away. Such was the case last night for the ruler, who awoke on their desk after a long evening of fruitlessly attempting to draw up a diplomatic treaty proposal only to find an extensive list of the proclivities and taboos of their neighbors, coupled with suggestions as to how to use these to sway them to their cause. Perplexingly, the whole thing was seemingly written by their own hand.
