Your expedition has found a way down further into the upper complex. It once again appears to be a series of laboratories branching off the main corridor. However, unlike prior laboratories which have been well sealed, this door appears to have been breached, and somewhat worryingly the breach appears to have originated inside the lab. Some of the expedition point out that any such breach must have happened millennia ago, and should not be a worry, but you all further raise your guard while exploring this level. You also start to notice that the outside world has started to make inroads to this level, with a few minor breaches having evidently let in spores and seeds, and so plant life has begun to take root. After some time, a member of your team indicates that there are signs of spoor that look recent, and then another finds a chewed leg bone of some kind of small animal. We now need to be concerned that there is some kind of predator in here with us. It does not take long for you to start feeling watched, hunted even. Your team swiftly forms a defensive circle and watches the room at large. After a few minutes, one notices a flicker of movement and you all raise weapons ready to do combat. Nothing appears to show. Then once again another flicker, as if something moved in front of the light briefly, and you realize that whatever is hunting you is somehow invisible.
