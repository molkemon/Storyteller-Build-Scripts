After a few days of search, the scouts have found a clear path into the chasm. An underground river has carved a steady decline into the rock that we can follow without a problem. As we continue downwards, the carved rock reveals gold and gems in the walls. Without much debating, the men get out some pickaxes and start excavating the treasure. With a song on their lips and their coffers already noticeably heavier, they continue down in the best of spirits.
