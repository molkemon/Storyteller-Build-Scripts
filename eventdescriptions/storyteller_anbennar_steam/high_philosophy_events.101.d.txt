"TThe history of our tradition is marked predominantly by the scars of strife. Her fabric torn and shredded, patched with the dreams of Rajas, before being ripped asunder once more. Countless threads have been lost through the sands of time: the Niatkaiks, the Jagdishka Sect, the Closed Gate School, and so many others. This is to say, that I hope Suhan will end as simply another rip in the cloth, patched together with the deeds of better men." Philosopher Vasantbir Ghick  The sash has been torn down. Tied glass lays shattered upon the ground, and Suhan has crushed the fragments to crystalline dust. An exquisite work of careful hands has been torn to pieces. Stained glass turned to nothing but sand; our art has been destroyed.  The court is divided, Golden Palace scholars argue with Ascendant Soul scholars with hands on daggers. Meanwhile Suhan's agents roam the countryside turning the common folks against us. The only way that we will regain control of the situation is by creating a unified front against the storm. The only real question is how will we deal with those who have joined Suhan.
