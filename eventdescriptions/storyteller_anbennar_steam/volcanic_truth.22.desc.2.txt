The matter of the Taga'Ala is decided. The Taga'Ala have sailed to the Kiamoa where they send their dead. They knew not to sail beyond, and there is wisdom in this. they stuck to what they knew, even as they explored what might be. The Taga'Ala may be agents of change, but the Volcanic Truth teaches that we must direct change in accordance with the will of the Twin Gods. We will impose restrictions on their trade, and send missionaries in the hopes that one day they will embrace the Volcanic Truth as well. As the Volcanic Truth spreads to new lands, the Taga'Ala will take a glowing ember of Truth from it and make it their own. This is what change means.
