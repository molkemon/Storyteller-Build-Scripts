Walking through the massive Gene Pillars, one finds not only traces of leftover experiments and precursor projects but also very rare looks into the culture and more everyday habits of their builders through the rare intact items we can retrieve from ancient living rooms and bedchambers. One of these items we stumbled upon is a dimly glowing piece of a quartzlike crystal, that makes the person holding it experience a sense of ease but also fills them with a yearning to visit an alien place entirely unknown to them. The current standing theory is that it is supposed to show a recorded image of a place of importance to the original owner. Perhaps home, or a family they longed to see again. Sadly, the magic within is too dissipated to let us truly see it. Still, what to do with such a find?
