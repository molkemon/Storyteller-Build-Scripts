There is no denying the Canvas Sect enjoys preferential treatment from traveling merchants. Claw advocates claim their traders are vilified in rumors and backroom slander, while non-Ashhana merchants increasingly avoid trade with the sect out of safety concerns stemming from what they describe as hostile rhetoric. The commercial preference has progressed to where Canvas middlelizards have pushed out Claw traders. Claw representatives accuse them of scalping goods required by Claw commonfolk. Canvas representatives counter that this disparity is the natural consequence of the Claw’s supremacist antagonism, and if the Claw wished, they could reassess their beliefs.
