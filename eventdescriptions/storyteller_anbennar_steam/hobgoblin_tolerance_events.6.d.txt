The hobgoblin reputation of discipline and efficient warcamp management is not exaggerated in the slightest. So much is this the case, their competence is cause for sore relations with the rest of the military, from the lowly soldiers, who feel sneered and mocked by the hobgoblins apparent attitude of superiority, to the high command, whose members are acutely aware of the superior hobgoblin methods and how poorly that reflects on their leadership. This contrast has proven so stark in the case of one of our armies, our agents have investigated and found proof of a deepreaching scheme of military equipment resale and record forgery that point directly at some of the most powerful noble houses and merchant conglomerates
