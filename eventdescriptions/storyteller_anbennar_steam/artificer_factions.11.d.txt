In the ancient tale of the Tortoise and the Hare, the Mechanists are the tortoise. They are slow and steady. Resolute. Their influence creeps deep in the foundations of the country.  Need a solid mechanic? They're a Mechanist. Oh, you need someone to design a sturdy core for your invention? Get a Sending Stone to the Mechanists.  It seems now is the time that the Mechanists have looked to have their favours repayed, and have pulled strings to gain strength within our bureaucratic offices.
