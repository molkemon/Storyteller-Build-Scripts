There are no flattering words with which to describe Commander Janeth; that is the conclusion reached by the council. An exceptionally stupid centaur, blunt with his words but making up for it in sheer martial prowess. He is perfect. The last few months were spent with the creation of something akin to a mercenary company. An army totaling eight thousand hoofs, an expedition to retrieve special loot from the wastes beyond the Stone gate. Janeth, or someone like him, would be needed in order to ensure that the company would not get off track. And even if this naive brute were to encounter those demonic creatures, those Facestealers, what good could they do against him? Perhaps they would even be able to obtain one of them and bring him to the ruler's court? The possibilities were intoxicating.
