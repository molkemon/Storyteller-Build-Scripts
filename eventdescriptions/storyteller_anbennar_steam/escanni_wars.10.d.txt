Castanor once stood astride all Escann and all Cannor. Then it fell, and in its wake, petty kingdoms rose. Then those too fell, crumbled before the Greentide, and all that was left was devastation. But no more. Out of scraps we have forged steel. Out of embers we have lit a wildfire. Out of petty adventuring bands and relickingdoms we have created a power that dominates all Escann, and those few corners of the realm which still resist our rule shall fall soon enough. The Wars of Consolidation are over, and we have won. Now it is time for us to expand our gaze, our ambition. Where shall we look next?
