The south coast of the Sarmadfar, once desolate but for the migrative Fograc, is now studded with Eordan colonies scraping the arid land. Despite our efforts, it is clear that the land will never be fully developed without considering the status of the Fograc. Having left us long ago before the schism of the seasons, the Fograc have abandoned the values taught by the Fey Lords and instead foolishly revere the stars. Some of the faithful have declared that the Fograc should be forced to repent for their transgressions, while others have stated that there is no reason to antagonize them.
