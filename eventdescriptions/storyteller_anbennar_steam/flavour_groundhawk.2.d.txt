The location was picked at the edge of the Deepwoods, deep enough to be part of her forest, but far enough out to be protected from the evils of the fey. As many goblins of our tribe as could attend stood outside as the shrine was being consecrated. Once the shamans finally emerged, everyone fell to their knees and sang an ancient song of worship to the beat of the shamans’ drums. Tomorrow, we will be gone from here. Tomorrow, all that will be left are a handful of guards and shrine attendants. Tomorrow, we will be singing prayers in courtship of new and different gods. But at this moment, in this place, the Lady Forest will know that it is she who truly commands our hearts.
