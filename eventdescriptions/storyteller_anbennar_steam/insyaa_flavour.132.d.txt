Using materials found in recently explored precursor ruins, our artificers have discovered how to create reinforced plating that uses precursor steel composites. These composites are better equipped to deal with the elements, having a higher melting temperature and resisting fire damage, sandstorm degradation, and water oxidation. Along with these benefits, these plates are also designed to cover more of the machine form, protecting internal systems. Such an invention should allow us to keep the machine in the fight for longer without needing to be repaired.
