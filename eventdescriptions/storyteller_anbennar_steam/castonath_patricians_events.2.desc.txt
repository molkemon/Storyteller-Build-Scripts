It is no surprise that pockets of resistance continue to fester throughout Castonath, as it is far too large to exert complete control over. Such infestations are usually easily taken care of by our patrols or by independent clans who decide to take matters into their own hands. As a weed not killed at its root, however, some of these pockets spiral into great trouble. A powerful patrician has decided to take it one step further and has outwardly declared control over a couple blocks of the city, daring us to rein them in. They've dug in with a large contingent of hired muscle, and their fortifications will likely make taking the street back expensive in blood.
