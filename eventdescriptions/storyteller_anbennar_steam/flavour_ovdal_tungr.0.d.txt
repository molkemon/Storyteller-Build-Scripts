Conflict brews within the clan halls and tavern tables. The ruling clans have long grumbled at each other, ensuring each has their way or control of the trade routes. This has been the way of life for generations, but times have rapidly changed recently. The Uthojdihr have crawled their way back into the light following a substantial rise to power and influence. Council chambers are filled with shouting elders, flying papers and banging gavels. They push ideas that directly affect our future in ways that the other ruling clans could not dream of. Ceaselessly they work to gather more autonomy for their operations, slowly but steadily increasing their sway within the council and subsequently the lord’s authority. If we want to continue listening to the others, we may, but there is clear and common sense to be found with the Uthojdihr, even if the ways they operate are a little unusual.
