The orks of Skewered Drake once cast out the first Great Dreamer. They did so because they did not believe in the truth Dookan had shown him. Now, they have fallen to the Crusade, and this has been proof enough of the legitimacy of our faith to turn the majority of these cousins of ours to our side. Their leaders, though, are something of a special case. Do we avenge that first slight against our late leader, and put these fools to the axe? Should we give them a taste of his punishment, and put them into exile? Or do we show mercy and incorporate them into our ranks, as the Dream is for all orks?
