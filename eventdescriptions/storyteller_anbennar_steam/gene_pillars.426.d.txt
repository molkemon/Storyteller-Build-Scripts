The next chamber is a large greenhouse open to the sky, the floor falling away outside the windows to reveal the distant landscape below. Like before, memories flood into the minds of the adventurers, and they see gardens full of life and bountiful greenery, growing a plethora of fruit and flowers of dazzling hues. But these memories are shattered as a distant shape makes a soft whistling noise and a spine the length of a ship’s oar flies through the window. The glass shatters and the spine clatters to the ground. As the illusion disappears it reveals that the greenhouse is filled with bristling plants with lethal spikes nestled in the center of their petals.
