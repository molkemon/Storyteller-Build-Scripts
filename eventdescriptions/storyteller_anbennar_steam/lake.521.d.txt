It was a negotiation conducted under fairly perfect conditions. The topics discussed were agreed upon beforehand, the finalization of terms considered by most a formality, but the proceedings still commanded a certain amount of seriousness as a sign of mutual respect. Nobody on either side had time to waste, but a fine glass of wine every now and then still gave the proceedings a relaxed atmosphere. So, without much ado, the binding clarifications and proceedings for the Ultakal decree were drafted, amongst them even the establishment of a supervisory committee of independent observers.
