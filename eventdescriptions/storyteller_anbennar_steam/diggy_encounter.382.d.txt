The overwhelming masses of goblins quickly surrounded them and despite their best efforts flooded over their defenses in one gigantic wave. In their eyes lies a grim lust for death and destruction, a primal urge to kill. As their men scramble away, the torches go out and the light dims, only the faint glow of some bioluminescent mushrooms illuminating the slaughter. But just as suddenly as the goblins appeared they vanish again, drawn into some other part of the Serpentspine by their hunger for death.
