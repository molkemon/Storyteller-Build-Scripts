The day is young as the Sovereign and their entourage reach the Trialmount legions of Burnbloods, following their steps closely. A sizeable cart affixed with an alchemical contraption emitting a rhythmic whirr accompanied them. Atop it the figure of one of the "immoral" artificers employed by Wyvernheart can be discerned, a glowing rune of subjugation on their forehead. Having reached their destination, the Sovereign raises their arms and conjures a firestorm at the foot of the mountain. In the following moments, new firestorms begin to appear alongside the base of the mount as the councillors and overseers dispatched around the Trialmount follow the signal. One by one they alight a fire that will only be quelled by complete destruction of its surroundings, and the fire quickly surrounds the Trialmount. As the fire starts creeping up the sides of the mountain, shrieks can be heard as anything trapped in the gigantic circle burns to a crisp. Smoke rises to the sky, and the Trialmount disappears. Silence falls upon the area for a moment, a lull before the coming storm. Suddenly, the artificer, gazing at the smoke with his binoculars, points towards the dust as a dozen silhouettes break off from the smog led by a white wyvern. He looks towards the Sovereign and makes an eerie smile before pleading with a tinge of excitement in his voice. "Please try to not damage the organs too much. They will be useful." The Sovereign looks up.
