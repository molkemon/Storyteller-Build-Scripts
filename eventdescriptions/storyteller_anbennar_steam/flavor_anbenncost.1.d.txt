The Greentide brought with it a new race, halforks. a mixture of human and orks. While the first generation of halforks, including such legends like Lothane Bluetusk, Grandmaster of the Corintar, were often born of despicable abuses from either orkish invaders on human villagers or wicked adventurers on captured orks, in recent history a good number of halforks have been born of mutual couplings in the cities of Escann. Despite this, those of orkish blood have often been treated poorly in the adventurer kingdoms of Escann, and since then many have been making their way to Anbenncóst; a city renowned for its diversity and promise for a new start. Like many migrants to the city, the majority of halforks have settled in the Bilge, the massive slum that encompasses much of central Anbenncóst.
