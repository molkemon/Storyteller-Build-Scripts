As the siege of the capital draws to its violent conclusion, the rule of the venerable monarch of the dynasty is cut to a grisly end. The once dignified elf is pulled from the palace to the streets, and as the city burns around their gnoll captors toss a sword haphazardly to their feet. The whooping laughter of countless gnoll warriors echoes through their ears as the elf lifts with shaking hands the sword and points it towards their foe; the ruler of the dynasty, who stands opposite the elf in the impromptu arena formed by a circle of spectating gnolls. The two eye each other tensely; though their back is bowed with the burdens of age, a keen cunning still burns in the elf's eyes, and the ruler takes careful note of it. Even as their pack grows reckless in their mocking laughter, the ruler regards their foe with the wariness of an experienced warrior, pacing cautiously towards the elf with their own weapon at the ready. In a flash, the combat commences; yet in the same moment it is ended, as the life and intelligence fades from the elf's eyes, their age rendering them no match for their opponent. The ruler merely shrugs as the body slumps to the ground and the yipping of their warpack's laughter reaches its noisy crescendo. The news of the capital's fall spreads quickly across Bulwar, and many fear the worst; that the elf, the guiding beacon of the New Sun Cult, would be sacrificed to the dark masters of the demonspawn on a burning pyre, as countless other elves have been. Few would be consoled to learn of their actual fate; cannibalized by the ruler immediately after their duel, so that the ruler might inherit the foresight and stability of the elf's long rule.
