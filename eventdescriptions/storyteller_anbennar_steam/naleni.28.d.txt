A human woman, wearing a thick leather button jacket over a half corset stands proudly before the throne.  She stands tall and straight, hair wild, and requests the Queens blessing to serve as a Odanches in her navy. She lists her time spend aboard other ships, serving with some well renowned Odanchesta, earning the respect of her harpy fellows. She requests a commission, given that she cannot earn one in the usual way, owing to not being a harpy. Whilst not written law, traditionally only harpies have been allowed to hold the rank of Odanches.
