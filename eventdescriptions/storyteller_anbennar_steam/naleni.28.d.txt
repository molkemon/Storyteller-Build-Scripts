A human woman, wearing a thick leather button jacket over a half corset, stands proudly before the throne. She stands tall and straight, hair wild, and requests the Queen's blessing to serve as an Odanchés in her navy. She lists her time spent aboard other ships, serving with some well-renowned Odanchésta, earning the respect of her harpy fellows. She requests a commission, given that she cannot earn one in the usual way, owing to not being a harpy. Whilst not written law, traditionally only harpies have been allowed to hold the rank of Odanchés.
