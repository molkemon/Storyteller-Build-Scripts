A sight to behold; Dracball, Slayer of Drozmagog, Sword of Legends; before me, all mine. It calls to me, a tangible sensation of eagerness and anticipation to wield it in one's hand. The metal of the deepred blade, fashioned from a secret alloy by the Monkriok bladesmiths, is indistinguishable from bloodstains of its previous victims. With a clap of thunder, Dracball becomes encased with bolts of lightning. Heroes have wielded it, legends that are sung today; I can be one as well.
