A love forged in the embers of an age-old blood feud. The murder of a cousin in a botched street duel, and the shocking deception of a faked poisoning. A concerned family nurse has come forward with a story almost too tragic in nature to be believed, but if believed, it has only added onto the previously seen complications between the two factions. An unknown wandering goblin vagabond hailing from one of the factions has taken the hand of one meant to be betrothed to another in an arranged marriage intended to reduce tensions. If we react quickly to such an event, we may be able to recover our face from the view of the people. Some argue for acceptance of such acts perpetrated in pursuit of such noble ambitions like the love for another, regardless of societal differences or obstructions. But we can also make the two clans firmly know our stance in this entire matter by publicly decrying their love.
