With rope and strength the statue keeled over, breaking loose from the base. As it fell to the ground, snapping noises echoed through the room, followed by a low rumbling sound. The mechanism inside the statue had broken down, releasing the winch that held the hidden door in place. The sound of bursting stone mixed with the creaking of stone sliding on stone as a door further into the library opened. Within the rubble, however, the explorers also found some well-hidden gold. A thoroughly successful undertaking.
