The Ursibadur family has become dominant in recent years, with the other families struggling against their expanding influence. This is undoubtedly threatening the stability of the realm, as the other disempowered families increasingly contemplate attempting to even the scales with violence. We could curb the influence of the Ursibadur family in order to head off further instability, or we could allow their dominance to continue and reap the rewards of their gratitude.
