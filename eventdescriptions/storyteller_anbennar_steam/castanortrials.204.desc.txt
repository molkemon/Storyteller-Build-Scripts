The path soon diverges like a spider’s web, spiraling up and around the base of the Trialmount. One of these paths takes you to a small, sheltered cave, carved neatly out of a hillside. Looking in, one could see a small anvil and furnace; along with various blacksmithing tools. Though covered in a thick layer of blowing forest detritus and dust, the simple forge complex looked to be as functional and as operational as the day it was made. Magically preserved through the ages, here was the trial of a former lord, better known as Dwarffriend. The concept was simple; forge an item to be given as a gift to the dwarven contingent that would be present at the coronation of every subsequent lord.
