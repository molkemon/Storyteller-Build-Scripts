Despite our recent attempts at showing our rule to be strong, which includes but is not limited to cleaning the streets and slums of the domain, creating a strong, inclusive government under the king, fostering a prosperous age of peace and development not seen in the realm since ever, and providing the local aristocrats and philosophers with hefty sums of gold, our rule has still been challenged, and not by the fringes, but by the majority of the high class of the domain. It proves upsetting to hear the average philosopher ranting on and on about the most minute of details, of what it means to be a good leader, when we have done more than they could ever talk about. It is as if their only purpose was to argue, and that is nothing but a waste of life. We question why anybody would ever devote their precious piece of existence to this before returning to be with the Last True King. At that point they should be cast out from the beyond. While we have nothing against the average elf of the domain, these philosophers have proven to be equivalent to cockroaches. contemptible leeches to our desired society that just refuse to go away and don’t create any value to begin with. Thus, we entered the city at night, corralled these so-called thinkers who opposed our rule by word, cut their heads open, and let them stand out in the fields miles away from home to await the next arrival of the death winds. If they were smart, they would have realized that we were right straight from the getgo, or that maybe speaking out and riling the masses would have consequences. Either way, the people of the domain will no longer be limited by their intolerance.
