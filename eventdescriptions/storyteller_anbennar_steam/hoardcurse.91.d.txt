Our expansions are settling, our nation is thriving, and many dwarves are leaving the hold with fine metalwork and strong ales, and returning with even greater treasure, gold. Gold, gold, gold. The glorious, gleaming, glowing yellow metal graces even the most downtrodden of hands, as great celebrations are held to our traders' return, and they flaunt their newly found wealth with great aplomb, happily showering our people with gilded coins. A new age, a Golden Age, descends upon our nation.
