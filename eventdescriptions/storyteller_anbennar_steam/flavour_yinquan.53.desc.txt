Listen to me well. If you do, I will sell for half price. There is no point convincing you to worship Huszien Herself, for you are not from Her domains. Even so, you must change your ways and embrace the spirits. You name yourselves followers of the Accretive Path; I say you are caught between your natural and good desire to worship the spirits around you and the dictations of the High Philosophy, who want to mandate that you must grit your teeth and bear the injustices visited upon the benevolent spirits of Haless. You must remember that these urbanites hold no sway outside of Rahen, for the great and glorious country has driven them back on all fronts. Do not be afraid to embrace harmony with these majestic beings. You have no cause to disguise it as respect anymore. That I, a mere farmer, might be able to travel abroad and tell you these wondrous things should prove their benevolence; for my wealth is Huszien's; and should also show you the tides of victory. Come. Purchase the fruits of my harvests. But remember who let me sell it to you.
