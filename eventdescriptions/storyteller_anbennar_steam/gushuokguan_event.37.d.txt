War, it is now war. The development of relations has now led to open conflict between the Dao. While we are not alone in our struggle, we must realize that we are fighting our own brethren. They are fools who think that staying in the past will keep the confederation afloat, though they must realize that times change and that to survive, we must adapt. Let us quickly teach our brothers a lesson before they destroy what we have built up for so long. We must go into the future as one, though the others must first see the errors of their ways.
