Aye, I once saw a Behemoth up close, during my time in a port. Towering beasts, they are. So large that you had to leave that section of the docks entirely to even see the sunset on the horizon, and I believe I witnessed only a smaller ship. I remember I attempted to count the number of cannons, then next thing I knew I was dozing off in its shade. I believe I got to eighty, and I wasn't even halfway through. Just the thought is enough to make a man weep. And so I wonder what the Trollsbayers thought, when they saw an armada of them outside their bay.
