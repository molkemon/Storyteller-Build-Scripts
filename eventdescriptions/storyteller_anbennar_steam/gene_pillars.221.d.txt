Once again the Mechanim Genepillar is being delved. The expedition will become the first people to gaze upon the inner facilities in over a thousand years. As they make the first step beyond what the previous expedition’s stopping point, Freedom Chained No More finally spoke up. "It’s clear that incredible danger is not sufficient to deter you from this. Perhaps you should know it was not your safety I was concerned with. There are many mechanim still inside the inner facilities; they do not speak your language, they do not understand social norms, and they have no worldly experience. By continuing further, you’re not just putting yourselves at risk, do you understand?" No one in the expedition felt comfortable responding, and so their mysterious host continued. "Very well, if you really want to continue, I have many things to say to you. As I doubt you’ll stay to listen, I'll give you the short version." With that, they began speaking incredibly fast, more than a hundred words a second. "I hope that was informative."
