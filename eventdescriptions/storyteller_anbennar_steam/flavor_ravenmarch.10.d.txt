The center of the ballroom floor clears, as our lord and the offensive young man select blades from an attendant. Our lord selects a simple, practical saber, with filigree on the handguard, while their opponent selects an ornate, engraved rapier. A murmur ripples through the attentive crowd as both take up dueling stances. The young man unleashes a rapid barrage of thrusts and feints, pressing our lord’s guard, before dropping low and thrusting upwards. Our lord dives out of the way, landing in a roll and slashing as they rise. While the young man pivots to face our lord, the tip of the saber presses to his throat. The room is silent save for the sound of his sliced cape falling to the ground and his voice calling out. "Clearly I was misinformed about our country’s skill with a blade. I yield."
