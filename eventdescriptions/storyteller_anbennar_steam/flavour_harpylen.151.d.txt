When fashioning a delicate instrument such as a lyre, great care must be taken with the choice of proper materials for all its parts. While the central heart string of the magical lyre, the jinisapo, is fashioned from anniwan, a mixture of tin, pulverized Dames tear and lapis lazuli, several options are available for the outer strings: simple gut strings or strings made of silver or even exotic spider silk strings imported from the depths of the Serpentspine.
