As your party opens a door, a chitinous, clawed creature leaps at you, then shoves itself down your throat. After a few moments of tortured screaming and coughing, you collapse, vomit, and become still. Fearing the worst, your party approaches your body, but then notices you are still breathing. Unsure of what to do, they produce some smelling salts to raise you from unconsciousness, and shine a light down your throat. There, lodged firmly inside, what appears to be a rather large hermit crab stares back at them.
