With the recent awakening of the Children of Fire, most of our people expected that our siblings would immediately start the fight against the Underworld, however it appears that they do not remember much from the ages before they fell into their long slumber. Many of them, just like us, have their own goals and aspirations that do not always fit our preconceived notions of how they should behave. This new perspective on our newly awakened metallic brethren has brought many changes with it, for while most of the people still see the mechanim as some sort of mythical beings worthy of reverence, some are starting to think that they are just like ourselves, carrying their own gifts and burdens. Even amongst the icy peaks of the domain, some have begun to question their old ways and started calling their ancestors' stance, which admittedly is somewhat defensive and isolationist atop the mountains, as cowardly, calling for their leaders to bring the fight to the Underworld instead of cowering behind the walls of their mountain citadels. Given all these changes, there is one question of utmost importance that remains for us to give an answer to: which direction shall our own country take?
