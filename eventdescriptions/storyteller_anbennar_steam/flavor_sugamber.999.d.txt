After losing both sons in the Lilac wars and his wife to disease, Duke Ewald the Second legitimized his daughter, Lisolette. As she was a bastard from an affair with a kitchen maid, however, her claim to the throne is seen as less than legitimate to many in Sugamber. This has especially drawn the ire of Ewald the Second's cousin Ethelbert, as he is now no longer next in line for the throne.
