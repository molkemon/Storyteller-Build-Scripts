An assassination attempt was narrowly thwarted by our lord’s vigilant guard. Our lord did not escape completely unscathed, however. We are not certain who the culprit is, but our investigators turned up some rather compelling evidence that points towards another nation. A dagger with their crest in the pommel was discarded near the scene with some of our lord’s blood still on it. Those bastards will pay for this attempt on our lord’s life.
