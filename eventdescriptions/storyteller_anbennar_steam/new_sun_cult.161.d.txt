An assassination attempt was narrowly thwarted by our monarch's vigilant guard. Our monarch did not escape completely unscathed, however. We are not certain who the culprit is, but our investigators turned up some rather compelling evidence that points towards a certain country. A dagger with their crest in the pommel was discarded near the scene with some of the monarch's blood still on it. Those bastards will pay for this attempt on the monarch's life.
