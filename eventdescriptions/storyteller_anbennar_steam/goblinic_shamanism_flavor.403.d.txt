The gnolls are known to be prolific conquerors. Often their conquests were initiated by a gnoll possessed by a very powerful demon. Some shamans have started performing rituals to worship these demons, and to invite them to possess goblins as well. So far, they have had little success, but with the noises and smoke created when they burn the right kind of mushrooms and mosses, they can make a goblin look very convincingly terrifying and demonic. Although they are still trying to get the real thing to work, for now, they are able to convince our enemies and subjects alike that we do have a demonic champion.
