The Venomtooth clan claims that they are faithless, that they only follow those who have proven their strength. Yet this whole notion was shattered today, as droves of spineless orks surrendered in a province, preferring to keep their necks over any beliefs of prideful domination they held before, while Dookanist slaves were liberated from the bindings that mocked the chained god. Aâdga herself at least was saved from such humiliation, having her brain punctured by shards of stone debris from a shaman’s explosion. Azukor, the son of Aâdga, was adopted into our clan, too young and weak to be a meaningful option as the successor of the clan. Without a leader or purpose to string them together, the rest of the clan fell into violent infighting soon after, spurred on by an ork’s sole greed to prove their strength and dominance over mutual interest. Now, it was only a matter of time before each of these nascent upstart clans would fall.
