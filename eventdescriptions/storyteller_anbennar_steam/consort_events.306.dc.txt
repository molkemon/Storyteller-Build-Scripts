The first time the consort watched the monarch sparring with the monarch's soldiers in training, they were impressed. It is not every noble that would enjoy the manual labor of actually learning how to wield a weapon of their own. But the monarch is not any ordinary person, the consort thought before approaching their spouse to compliment their skills. The consort even mustered the courage to ask the monarch's opinion on the current military situation of the realm. Intrigued by this mutual interest in the military aspects of the country, the monarch has gained a lot of new ideas for their army.
