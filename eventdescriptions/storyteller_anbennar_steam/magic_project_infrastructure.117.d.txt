The chaos and upheaval of our magical institutions has presented us with an interesting opportunity. for years, our mages have been governed indirectly, through systems of organization besides those of the current government. Yet given that we are currently in the process of promulgating new magical practices, it would be child's play to use this as a way to install permanent representatives of the state in positions of power, giving them the authority to execute orders given by the capital. This would not only hand significant control of our mages to the government, it would enable us to better organize large-scale magical projects in the future. There are, of course, dissidents who oppose this prospect as a naked power grab and totally antithetical to the collaborative ideal of the realm. But dissidents naturally fall out of power, and a power-hungry core of mages are always ready to take their place. Through them, we may institute a new way of management.
