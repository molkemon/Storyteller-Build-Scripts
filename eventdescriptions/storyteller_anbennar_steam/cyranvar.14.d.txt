For most within the realm, the Gladewardens of Domandrod are more myth than truth. A secretive sect of elves permitted to live within the fey forests even when the fey lords had decided they wanted them no more. It is an honor that drives their zealous desire to keep any and all out of the woods. Thus, it was more than disturbing that sightings of the Gladewardens have been spotted across the realm, even all of Eordand. And, today, they stand before the ruler with a grave warning. "Elves like us have trespassed within the Domandrod. They show no understanding of the seasons or respect for the divinity of our lords. And now, they drive us back. If the ruler does not act soon, the forest may be lost."
