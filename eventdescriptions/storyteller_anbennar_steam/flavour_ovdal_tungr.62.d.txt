Despite our benevolent economic guidance, the nation of Eldoria has found itself forced to declare bankruptcy, but not before sending an envoy to our capital to beg for monetary aid. Since we have already extracted extensive trade concessions from them, they have little to offer in that regard, and so we are able to drive a hard bargain: in return for covering their debts, they agree to cede control of their foreign affairs, essentially turning them into an extension of our own state, all without any of the hassle of actually ruling them directly.
