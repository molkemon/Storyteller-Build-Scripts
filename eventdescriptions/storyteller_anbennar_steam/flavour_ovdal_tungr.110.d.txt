In the now well established Copper Embassy in the capital, the dwarven ambassador has approached the monarch with an arrangement for the newly conquered lands in East Bahar. The nation has requested ownership of the city of Aqatbar, citing that their aid was instrumental in securing the province; or at the very least that they may receive trade concessions within city grounds. Aqatbar is a beautiful old city that displays the great wealth and culture of the Bulwari people; we must consider greatly what to do with it and our alliance is on the line.
