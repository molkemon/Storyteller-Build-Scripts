Heading further north, after arduous journeys through the frozen tundras, our researchers were met with a rare sight: A dying wild troll. While what led the troll to this sorry state is no longer within sight, it does not seem like the troll will survive his current turmoil unaided despite his regeneration. Our researchers could try to save the troll to study him alive, though at the cost of very important supplies, or they could leave the troll to his death, and utilize the opportunity to study his regeneration in his final hours.
