Bricena and Arfil journey to the Silent Lake to spend time with their fathers. At the end of a family dinner, Cinrthir asks whether they will ask him to support Arfil as the heir, and in effect put his Salla under the control of Salla Myna. Bricena and Arfil respond that they are plenty busy as is, and want to let Cinrthir grieve in peace rather than pressuring him. At which point Cinrthir smiles and gives them a letter, asking them to take it to the regency council. Of course, the lesser proclaims Arfil the leader of Salla Elyn.
