Azaly Farscribe has returned after over a decade away. All but his most dedicated supporters had already written him off as dead and gone, his estate seized years ago by the government and sold off. Since his return he has begun regaling the people with his travels to the lands of Cannor. His journey took him northwards along the West Salahad Coast and into a coastal region named the Divenhal Sea. From here he sailed to a great city named Anbenncost. There he met halfling beekeepers, who told him of The Small Country and the revolt brewing within those lands. This revelation spurred new purpose in Farscribe, who quickly made his way to the Small Country though the neighboring lands of Beepeck. Having found himself in the midst of the Small Country Revolt, Farscribe joined the rebellion, fighting on behalf of his Cannorian brethren with all he had. Now he returns to tell us of these adventures and all he has learned from the Cannorian Halflings. This truly marks a new age for the Vyzemby.
