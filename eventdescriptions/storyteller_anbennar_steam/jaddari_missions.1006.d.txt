All creatures can be the Light’; it may be a simple concept, but it is a revolutionary tenet for the adherents of the SarArukanas, the Bulwari Sun Cult, that has brought the truth of Surael to sentient beings of many cultures and races. This foundational principle of the Jadd is, of course, also an intrinsic aspect of the military organization of the Desert Legion, once formed from the veterans of Jaher’s eastern campaigns, and who became the armed protector of the new faith after the revelations of Jaddar, his grandson. Long gone are the times where the Desert Legion was composed solely of elven soldiers – by now humans, harpies and even gnolls serve united under the sign of the Jadd. With the Jadd fighting on so many fronts, as they battle servants of the Malevolent Dark and those who refuse Surael’s truth, the Desert Legion must expand to meet this challenge – and an additional captain will be required to command these new troops.
