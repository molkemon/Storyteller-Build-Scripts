A thick smog of incense filled the room where the Court of Change resided. At its centre, laid out on an array of cushions, resided Akhaxpa. As the ruler approached her and knelt down, she opened her three eyes, filling the room with a blinding blue light. “Magic is a great tool of change, don’t you agree? I have a soft spot for the mages. But sit too long in the same tower, practicing the same spells, and they grow complacent and lazy. You have the tools to build a tower. So too should you have the tools to move it.”
