This room is large and circular, a great banquet hall with old tables. One can imagine the livelihood of this place, but something feels amiss. The tables, the chairs, the walls, there is a strange air to them all. The path ahead is blocked by another barricaded door; this time, large boulders are keeping us away from continuing our journey. They are the size of horses or rothé, which will make this an arduous task to put them away.
