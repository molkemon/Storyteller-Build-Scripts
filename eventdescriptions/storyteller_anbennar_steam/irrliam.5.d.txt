The royal lines of the Cinder Realms have become increasingly intertwined over the centuries since the fall of the Phoenix Empire. This has stemmed both from a desire for a consort of suitably noble lineage and the opportunity for mutually beneficial alliances offered by a dynastic marriage. Yet, one of the dynastic marriages entered into by a member of the royal house of Sarelzuir centuries ago has led one of our more ambitious rivals to openly doubt the legitimacy of the heir’s reign. Soon after Taelarios Irrliazuir ascended to the throne of Irrliam, he was betrothed to Panoril Sarelzuir, noble daughter of Eledas the First, the founder of Sareyand himself, and twin sister of our beloved king Eledas the Second. With the meteoric rise of Irrliam to the position of undisputed hegemon of the lower Suran in the last decades, its ruler has claimed that he, as the direct descendant of Panoril, represents the more senior of the two bloodlines, if only separated by a few minutes, and thus should become the rightful overlord of Sareyand. Their royal marriage to us has solidified this outrageous claim even in the eyes of some of our own vassals, and some obsequious courtiers, plainly trying to curry favour with the Irrliami, openly suggest the abdication of the monarch in favour of the rival ruler as the proper course of action. Refuting this brazen demand would show the rest of Bulwar that we are not the lapdogs of Irrliam, but would certainly give those grasping fools in Medurubar a pretext for invasion.
