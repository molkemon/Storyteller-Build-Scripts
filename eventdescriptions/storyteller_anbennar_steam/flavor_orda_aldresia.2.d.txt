When the Fourth Lilac War began, we held to our oaths, raising our blades and banners for the one true Emperor, Grand Duke Adénn Skylance. Kind, charismatic, and chivalrous, the Grand Duke of Dameria had little trouble convincing us to follow him into battle. For two decades, we fought for him without question. Had he ordered us into the hells, we would have followed him with a smile. Some would call it fanaticism or zeal, but any knight worth their salt would have seen it for what it was. true belief in a dream of a safe and peaceful Anbennar. Yet no matter our resolve, no matter our oaths, we failed a great man. When Emperor Lothane assaulted the Imperial Palace at the end of the Lilac Wars, and killed Emperor Adénn Skylance, there was widespread denial amongst our order. For a full year, we withstood his overtures at attempting a peace. The Wexonard had sense not to approach Arca Aldresia, as we would sacrifice our entire order to cut through his army and take his head. But, with time, there has been discontent growing within Aldresia; a question of our purpose, with some even claiming we have betrayed our oaths. Few are friends of Lothane, but we are Sentinels of the Empire, and Anbennar is more than just one man. Lothane has sent a final messenger to us, asking for our return without conditions. We must uphold our duties once again, but will our order survive it?
