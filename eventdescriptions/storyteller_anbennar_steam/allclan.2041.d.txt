Lo and listen, Gobbophilus, I bring you news from the hinterlands. It seems one of our citizens has taken it upon himself to embody the entrepreneurial spirit in truth and full, as they have begun to sell finely crafted artisanal cheese from the shed of their home. Indeed, he has managed to acquire the rating "A for Amazing" by several local bankers whom he is related to, and indeed it seems to be an entirely safe investment for ourselves. We should be able to recoup our investment in no less than five or so years.
