A new faith has emerged from a distant land, as the streets of its capital are now filled with the ideas of a new goddess. Believers claim that Enuuk, Kodave, and Yukel are but mere aspects of her singular divinity. Kalyin presents herself as the embodiment of all things and her followers preach unity and oneness within the Federation just as Kalyin herself represents oneness among the Divine. Her followers are not pacifists, however; they proclaim that it is the Federation’s moral obligation to bring ruthless retribution to her enemies beyond its borders. With the strife and division of the religious war fresh in their minds, many have come to adhere to this idea, hoping that Kalyin represents an opportunity for peace and coexistence within the Federation. How should we respond?
