The dark ritual of the Oni has worked, corrupting the Temple Complex of the realm. Where its Dames tear heart once throbbed with a pale blue light, it now pulses with a deep crimson, as if all the blood of the earth is flowing into the Temple. Its sinister glow heralds a new age of power for our people, as we can now take from the spirits of the land their energy, and bend it to our own purposes.
