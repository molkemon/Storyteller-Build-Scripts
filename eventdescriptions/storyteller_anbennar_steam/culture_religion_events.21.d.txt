For a long time now, the personal convictions of the lord have been at odds with the religious establishment of our nation. This has caused both friction and conflict, with the clergy and leading nobles often questioning their rule. There are, however, many that now support the lord in their devotion to the new faith, both in the capital and the surrounding countryside. Perhaps the time has come to make a great change to how our government functions, to its very core? Surely the defenders of the old ways would not go out willingly, but if there is a will, there is a way.
