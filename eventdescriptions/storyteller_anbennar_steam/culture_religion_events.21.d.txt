For a long time now the personal convictions of the monarch have been at odds with the religious establishment of the realm. This has caused both friction and conflict, with the clergy and leading nobles often questioning the ruler's rule. There are, however, many that now support the monarch in their devotion to the new faith, both in the capital and the countryside. Perhaps the time has come to make a great change to how the government of the realm functions to its very core? Surely the defenders of the old would not go out willingly, but if there is a will, there is a way.
