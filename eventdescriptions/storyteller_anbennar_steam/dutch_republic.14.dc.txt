The Republican rulers have succeeded in making institutions of our realm increasingly representative, but one institution that has resisted recent changes is the army. One of our generals belongs to a family of nobles who have strong ties to a certain class and has been sabotaging our efforts to oust the Silcalas loyalists from highranking military positions. Now, he's gone even further and wants one of his relatives, also a supporter of that class, to act as advisor to our lord. While this young person seems like a talented commandant, we would loathe giving them influence.
