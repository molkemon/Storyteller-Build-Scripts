A shrill scream from the dark is all the warning we have before our expedition is beset by the horrors of the depths. About a dozen bloated, beaked creatures, with large hooks for hands start rushing into our forces, piercing and biting their way through flesh and armor. When we are finally able to organize and mount an effective response, the creatures are already fleeing back into the dark caves they came from, taking some of their killed prey with them. Though we do not suffer many casualties, this unknown foe proved itself to be very effective at killing. Effective enough for us to consider the risk of taming them worth the potential reward.
