The expedition has finally made passage beyond the mechanical lock and the hidden door. We had thought this workshop had given up most of its secrets, but clearly we were wrong. Automated golem work stations, strange libraries filled with tomes and tablets that look ancient, sitting on shelves made of solid metal. But as the expedition explores, one scout catches sight of something that should, by all means, not exist. a metal dwarf. Its skin is made from some kind of bronze or brass, it has silvermetallic hair and beard, and eyes seemingly made of gold and ruby gemstone. When our scouts came across them, the dwarf dropped whatever they were doing and ran, fleeing deeper into the complex. Unlike every other golem we have encountered, it seems that this thing, whatever it is, may be the intelligence behind this workshop. If we can communicate with or capture it, we can perhaps unlock everything this place has to offer.
