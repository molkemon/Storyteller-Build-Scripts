Even floating above the vast metalworks of the south, the noise of the forges ringing was audible. As the hungry Deadmines to the north churned out ore at rates beyond mortal capacity, so to did the forges of the south thirst for it, and the old metalworks of Vernham were no different. The shadow cast by the grand necropolis was hardly noticed by the denizens of the metalworks, being only a slightly darker shade of black to those who dwelt beneath the endless smoke.  Winged servants of necrotic flesh swooped and swept the outside of the palace, making sure the dark stone remained polish even in the onslaught of smoke, with their controllers having little hope of success; and nothing could cover the sound of the hammers below.
