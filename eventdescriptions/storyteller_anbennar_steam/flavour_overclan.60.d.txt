A Voltoj Academy with a Diwan present and enough development can spend that development on an upgrade. Upgrading to a Medium academy in a Hold province requires fifty development. The Serpentreach Academy Circuit can support one Large academy, which can be upgraded from a Medium one when the province has sixty development. Each Voltoj Academy is also well situated to administrate unique local resources, expanding an Academy's capabilities to allow for this requires seventy development and each one will require uncovering some documents or learning some secret. Each Voltoj Hold Academy upgrade will only spend ten development.
