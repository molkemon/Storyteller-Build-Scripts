The magisters, who have long been the most experienced of mages in the realm, may be able to assist in our venture. The runes of the Setzunkhûpa are unlike anything else seen since the fall of AulDwarov, but the diverse magical expertise of the Magisterium gives us new insights to aid our runesmiths in this project. By examining the way that the magic from each rune flows through the Setzunkhûpa, together we have been able to investigate the observatory's functions in a way that merely studying the runes or trial and error could not accomplish. There are still some mysteries we have not solved regarding the runes of the Setzunkhûpa, but now the day of complete understanding seems that much closer.
