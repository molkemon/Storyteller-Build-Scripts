The Young Owl was a halfork, and a Silmuna. His children were halforks too, and Silmuna as well. And as we have followed the path he laid out for us, it has come to pass that many among the realm are also halforks, and what they lack in Silmuna blood they more than make up for in dedication to Rogieria's cause. Elsewhere in Escann they may still be despised, but here few go a day without paying homage to the halfork who defined it, and that simply cannot coexist with the prejudice others demonstrate. Indeed, while the majority live lives akin to any of our subjects, Rogieran halforks' ability and loyalty has led a surprising number of them to attend court in the capital and seek to spend their life furthering our work. Instead of assigning them haphazardly, perhaps we should concentrate their focus on a single aspect of the state?
