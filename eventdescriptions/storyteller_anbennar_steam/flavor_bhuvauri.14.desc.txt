For years, the only orkish slaves anyone had access to in Haless was the Command, used only as fodder in their grinding machine of warfare. No longer, however, as more and more everyday escape the massacres of the Sir uprising, and we send them straight into the Academies. With their natural strength and endurance, they shall be not only some of the finest Guard in the Adasa, but an intimidating example of Bhuvauri’s growing might.
