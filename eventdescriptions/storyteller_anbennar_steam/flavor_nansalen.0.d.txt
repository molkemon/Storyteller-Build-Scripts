Once upon a time, Ninšara the First and her halfbrother, Araš the Eighth of Akalšes, were poised to bring peace to Bulwar through their shared efforts. Yet, they found their ambitions thwarted and their realms cut down to size, with their control shrinking from most of the Harpy Hills to their current territories, while Akalšes similarly shrank from controlling most of Bulwar Proper to their present diminished state on the shores of lake Naza. But now, a new chance for them to rise presents itself, for the stars have aligned to deliver new heirs to both Nanšalen and Akalšes born on the same summer solstice; a clear sign by Surael that they are destined for greatness. Destiny is on their side, so they must throw off the shackles of their overlords and fulfil their Divine Duties.
