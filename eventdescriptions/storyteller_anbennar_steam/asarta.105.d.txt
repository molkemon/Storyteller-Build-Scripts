A thick smog of incense filled the room where the Court of Change resided. At its centre, laid out on an array of cushions, resided Akhaxpa. As the ruler approached her and knelt down, she opened her three eyes, filling the room with a blinding blue light. "You please me, ruler. You have brought change to the lands in the west. Lizardfolk now walk where humans once did. But it seems you have neglected your homelands, where the same people live as they always have. The two sides could do with a change in environment."
