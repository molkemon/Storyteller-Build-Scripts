Legend tells the tale of an immaculate gem that fascinated spirits and drew them in like moths to a flame; but when they grew close, it would trap them, binding them to the material plane. They would then wreak havoc, and the gem was blamed for many an earthquake and mudslide. Whatever the truth of the story, a gem that could bind a spirit to this plane could immensely benefit the monarch as he seeks to guide their country through the chaos their traitors and rivals have wrought.
