More than any Bulwari, to a man or woman of Drolas the prospect of freedom is of the utmost importance. Our way of life has always been to earn our keep on the open seas, and we have never shied away from unsavory methods in doing so. But as of late our liberties have been eroded by our subjugation under the overlord, who has long frowned upon our piratical traditions and insisted upon the local authority of their own governors. We have had to grin and bear it for a long time, but an opportunity to change our fortunes has come as ever from the sea, though this time from a strange place; the ruler of a foreign realm. That ruler has sent a messenger by sea, reporting that the pieces have all been moved into place without us even knowing. The ruler, in interest of renewing the long tradition of DrolasGnoll business relations, is willing to support our independence in exchange for small amounts of tribute. We need only fly the black flags once more and cut down the hated provincial governors of the overlord.
