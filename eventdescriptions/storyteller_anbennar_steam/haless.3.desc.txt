Harimari are famed throughout Haless as especially skilled administrators, courtiers, and generals, and many of the Harimari who choose to wander further from their homelands in Rahen and Yanshen bring those skills with them, making a living selling their trade to the rich and powerful who need a little extra help managing particular issues. Now a small caravan of these roaming Harimari have emerged in the capital and offer help with your administration. The established courtiers are wary though, none suggesting that the extra hands wouldn’t help, but none volunteering to be the first to show their books to those outside the department.
