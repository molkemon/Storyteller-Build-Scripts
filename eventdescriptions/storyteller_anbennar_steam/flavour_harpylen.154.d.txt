When fashioning a delicate instrument such as a lyre, great care must be taken with the choice of proper materials for all its parts. For without a bow, a lyre is just an intricately crafted paperweight. In order to coax the most melodious sounds from the magical lyre, several options for the material of the bowstring are proposed. Certainly, the most mundane one is horse hair, but the more ambitious people involved in the project have proposed hair from a centaur's mane or even hair from a unicorn as the only suitable options. What material shall be used for the bowstring of our magical lyres?
