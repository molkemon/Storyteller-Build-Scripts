As the ideals of the faith have fractured, the Kavalakuttam, supposed to be the beating heart of the faith where all of the priestly Kavalali can share power equally amongst themselves, has been increasingly mistreated by the Kamrayakval. There are displays of intimidation, infringement on their rights and privileges, and speeches denouncing the body’s members, full of contempt for their indecisiveness and constant debate and discussion. With the very ideals of the Kavalakuttam called into question, its members are naturally growing increasingly opposed to the Kamrayakval. What should be done about it?
