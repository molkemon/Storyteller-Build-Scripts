For practitioners of the Fey Cult, contact with the Fey is seen as a great blessing; however, dealings with them are to be left to the Feykeepers, as even those who seem to have gained the upper hand end up losing. It was no surprise then that the monarch was delighted when they were visited by a fey. Naturally, said fey began trying to strike up a bargain with the ruler, to little success on the Fey's part. That was until the fey made a very particular offer. "Fine. All I ask is that you answer this riddle, and I will grant you your reward. What has a golden head and a golden tail but no body?"
