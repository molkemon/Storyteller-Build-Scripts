After hours of combat, skirmishes, successes, and defeats, we have finally killed the last of the Obsidian Dwarves. Their corpses lie now piled high in the cave where our battle had taken place, most of their armor and weapons shattered. There is still a trace of their strange magic in the air, like a static tingling. Next to them lie our own losses, equal in size. Many weep upon seeing what we had to sacrifice, but we all know that there would have been no other way.
