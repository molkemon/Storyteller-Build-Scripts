Our particular devotion to Autumn as of late has inspired a festival dedicated entirely to the season. Communities gather together to give thanks for the buffer between the warmer seasons and Winter's grasp, and to enjoy one another's company outside. As the leaves begin to turn and fall, many are collected and used as decoration, be it for homes or as part of gifts to the fey. In general, it is a time of showing gratitude and preparing for challenges to come.
