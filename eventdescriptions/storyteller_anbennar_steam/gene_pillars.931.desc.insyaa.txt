You stand in front of this, the heart of this facility. The notes from the prior expeditions have driven our research forwards in leaps and bounds. Our scholars' careful translation of the words above the door lead us to believe it reads "Draconic Containment Facility". What draconic means is still unknown, but this pillar seems to have been built to contain them. However, there is one major blocker: the door. There have been experts studying it since its discovery, and they are describing it as a magical work of art, layer upon layer of tightly bound enchantments. Spells to bind, burn, freeze, and stun, intertwined and all incredibly potent. The creators of this door were truly masters of their craft, and this may have been their masterpiece. Unfortunately, you have to shatter their work to gain entry to the facility. You look to the rest of your team and gather their input, while the scholars scramble for cover.
