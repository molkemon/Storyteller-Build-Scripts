The spiders strike hard and fast, but our preparations prove more than adequate. Goblin spears pierce their underbellies, our infantry advancing with blades raised to hack off arachnid legs. After a brief but harrowing battle, the spiders lay slain, their corpses strewn amongst our own dead and wounded. Though our losses were not insignificant, perhaps we could avoid further casualties if we made use of the spiders' odd metallic exoskeletons.
