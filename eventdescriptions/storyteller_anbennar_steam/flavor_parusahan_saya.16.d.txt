Men, women, and children all raise their voice in a rallying cry as our nation hardens into one which can withstand the onslaught of the future. This is no longer a land of the weak, often divided by faith or culture. We are united, solidified, and bent towards glorious purpose. We are the forefront of the next chapter of Rahen and all Haless combined. We are the Iron Raj.
