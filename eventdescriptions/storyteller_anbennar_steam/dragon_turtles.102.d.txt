Why is it that nobody in that country knows what exactly they believe? If they do not know what to think, tell them. They are weak. Their minds are malleable. They will believe exactly what they are told enough times, and if they do not believe what you have to say, that is your failing. Doctrine can be warped as is required for the sake of social cohesion. Reshape it however you want; it is not as if any of it matters anyway. Go to your counselors. Tell them that you have decreed something new, and if they complain, talk over them. Declare your will into being. If your counselors cannot implement it, they are either weakwilled or stupid. Figure out which and punish them accordingly. That should solve the problem, unless the people object again. That is possible, of course. In that case, there is likely someone whispering in their ear on the other end.
