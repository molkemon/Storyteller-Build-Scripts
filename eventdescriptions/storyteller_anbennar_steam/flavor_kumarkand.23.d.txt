Great joy makes itself known within our court, the diplomats we send to secure an alliance with Nansalen have returned with good news. It seems they are indeed ready to throw off the chains of Arayallens queendom and join their brethren of faith in the light. Wether the queendom retaliates or not, this is an important step towards pacifying the harpy hills.  From now on, under our guidance, Nansalen will be much more than a simple matriarchy it shall be the Queendom of Surakel.
