Great joy makes itself known within our court. The diplomats we send to secure an alliance with Nanšalen have returned with good news. It seems they are indeed ready to throw off the chains of Arayallen's queendom and join their brethren of faith in the light. Whether the queendom retaliates or not, this is an important step towards pacifying the harpy hills. From now on, under our guidance, Nanšalen will be much more than a simple matriarchy; it shall be the Queendom of Surakel.
