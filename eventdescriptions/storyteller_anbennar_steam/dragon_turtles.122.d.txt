My dear ruler, I understand that you have been on something of a talent search. Far be it from me to discount the importance of a strong state, but I also presently find myself in need of counselors to advance the work of the Consensus. I have conducted a search across Insyaa, and one of my top candidates just so happens to currently hold a position within your government. They are compassionate and wise, and when I informed them of the gravity of the position I sought them out for, they saw the chance to leave a legacy larger than any one nation itself and agreed to work for an institution that places importance on justice and fairness. I suspect they will be submitting a resignation letter soon. I fear you will miss their wisdom, but it seems apparent that their compassion was not being put to much use. I am sure you will attempt to find new advisors. I wish you all the success that your efforts deserve. Regards, Makaoioi the Erudite
