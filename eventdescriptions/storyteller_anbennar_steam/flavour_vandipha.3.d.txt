Peace, beauty, wealth. The isle of Van has become paradise on Halann. Harimari lounge about and debate the finer points of High Philosophy, their every need tended by obedient slaves. The philosophers, the landowners, the soldiers, the merchants, even the mages and adventurers adore life on Van. Joy is such a familiar sensation to them that none of them would even consider betraying their great ruler, and the lower castes are pacified by their obedience to their betters. The pleased philosophers, in fact, have begun to speak of a new school of High Philosophy. In the natural beauty of Van, as the sun shines over a tropical wonderland and birds chirp in perfect unison with the waves, they claim they have found a path to Purtara. In studying and inquiring more into the reasons why nature is as beautiful as it is, in categorization and classification of the world around us, Kartya is found. The soul's journey to harmony is mirrored by a person's journey through nature. The state, pleased at the emergence of this new school that avoids politics and governance entirely in favor of contemplation of nature's bounty, has graciously funded its expansion. A gilded cage has been forged for High Philosophy; a faith so sublime that no one will ever even consider another way could exist.
