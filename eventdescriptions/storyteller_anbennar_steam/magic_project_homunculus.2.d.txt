Every construct must have a power source, something that gives it life. Life, however, is not the same as soul. Beginner's Guide to Constructs. The artificers and tinkerers claim one cannot have something from nothing. One cannot make a soul without a soul. They call it the principle of equivalent exchange. Fools that they are, they fail to understand the power of magic. With our mastery over the transmutative arts, we can create something beyond a simple state of matter. a spark of energy, self-perpetuating, endlessly hungry, extended into an artificial soul organ. Without it, the homunculus cannot achieve true sentience. Creating the soul is the most fundamental part of the homunculi process. Mastery of this step will come through trial and error.
