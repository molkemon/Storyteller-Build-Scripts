\"The human form is a thing of divine beauty. A mage must be both scientist, artist and priest to replicate such divinity.\" Gellan the Architect  With enchanted scalpel in one hand and magicready clay in the other, the transmuter must perfect the body of the homunculus before giving it life. In most cases, the homunculus is given the ideal physique of their race. After all: why not strive for perfection?
