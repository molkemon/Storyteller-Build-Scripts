The rift opens, and I can see. These strange gnolls resist the designs of Granny and Aunty both, shielded by the resourcefulness of their goddess. Their philosophy repels the logic of paranoia, for all may feed from the greatest among their number, and even the dead are not truly lost. The gnolls chew their fallen right there on the battlefield and forgive them their betrayals. I sense Granny’s frustration, the braying laughter of Hireet. For once, it is Mama who dispenses the wisdom, for some enemies will fall to nothing but simple violence. The Coven will need an army, Hireet repeats, and with gritted teeth, Granny accepts the lesson.
