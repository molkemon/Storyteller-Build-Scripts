We have long thought that volcanoes are deeply unfortunate things. All that power available for the taking, if only we could take it. Many have tried just that, and many have burned in the fires that they thought to harness. Their problem, we have now revealed, is that they were simply not using the proper tools. With the recent rediscovery of metallurgy advanced enough to both create and work with Precursor Steel, we can create transport cells to extract lava from a volcano, place it in an appropriate receptacle, and then do with that powerful heat anything we want, from heating homes to making machines of war run. Of course, our artificers did not harness the power of the earth for us to use it in our fireplaces. The military has also developed many plans to show our friends in foreign lands the power of the magma we have unearthed, and we will be sure to deliver it to them out of the containers so their soldiers can see it up close.
