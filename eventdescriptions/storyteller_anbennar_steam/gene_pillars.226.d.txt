There were five main subsections of the genepillar visible, and the expedition has gone through four thus far. Because they knew this would be the last subsection, the voice of their host starts up again. "You have likely figured this out already, even with your limited intelligence, but this place was made to mass produce the most in demand mechanim types: labor for working, domestic for households, and soarers for observation. There is still one very common mechanim type you haven’t encountered: the warrior mechanim. Your presence has triggered its activation; this is your last chance, leave." But they could not leave; they were here for a reason. The expedition moves forward, and the door opens. As promised, in the room that stretches out before them, there were over two dozen warrior mechanim, undamaged and committed to the removal of all intruders. And to them, the expedition were intruders; it is unlikely a battle can be avoided.
