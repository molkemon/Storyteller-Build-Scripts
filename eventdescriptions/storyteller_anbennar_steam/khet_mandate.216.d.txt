The merchants of the Korosheshi League are always looking for opportunities to expand their enterprises and increase their profits. Recently their interest has been directed towards Elizna. The rich and prosperous island, originally known as Ibtat, was gifted by the Khetarch Baqtkhet I to the Sun Elf conqueror Jaher. It is an almost obligatory stop for traders looking to conduct businesses in Bulwar, and it houses many productive textile workshops and tea plantations. As such it is no wonder that the League is interested in it, and it is because of this that they have asked us to transfer control over the island to them, in exchange for substantial compensation. Should we keep this gem of the Divenhal under our direct control, or should we give the Korosheshi a chance to turn it into a magnificent Nisetaata?
