Having been to practically all civilized parts of Cannor, the Cardesti have developed a strong bardic tradition. Some of their highly praised songs cover topics such as love, fate, and their numerous journeys. A group of Cadesti bards recently gained renown after impressive performances in some of our nation’s busiest taverns; their songs are apparently mesmerizing to the extent that some of our court members have voiced interest in having them perform for the ruler. The bards were fast to show their readiness, provided we pay them accordingly.
