The cavelike structure we have found contains a stone altar, a grey slab of uncaring stone topped with remnants of woven cloth, its color long departed and its weave on the verge of ruin. At its base, however, chiselled runes have survived the test of time and seem to be waiting for a learned mind to decipher them. On the other hand, there are stairs leading out of the chamber and further along our path of adventure.
