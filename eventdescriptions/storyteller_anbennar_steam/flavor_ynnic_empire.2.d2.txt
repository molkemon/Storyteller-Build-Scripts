Today an air of solemnity fills the streets of Malacnar, a somber one for this day spells the end of something and the beginning of something new. Speaking in front of the largest gathering of warriors the city has ever seen, Battleking has declared that the warriors of Malacnar will no longer fight just for the banners and glory of the city, but for the entire Ynn River. The Ynnic Empire, the greatest nation in the world, will be restored by none other than the warriors and generals of Malacnar. The monarch has been crowned in a solemn ceremony, and many warriors were knighted and assigned fiefs, sure to protect the people of the Ynn River.
