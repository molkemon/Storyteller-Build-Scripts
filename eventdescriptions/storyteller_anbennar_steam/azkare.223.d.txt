It is rare to see Hiderion without Dagran Krakovar. In recent years the two seem nearly inseparable, with the stocky dwarf constantly at the side of the sharp-eyed elf. At meetings of the council it seems Dagran is the only one who can dissuade Hiderion when his ideas grow a little too lofty, and Hiderion is the only one who seems capable of understanding Dagran’s grand infrastructure plans. It is a rare thing to see such a perfect friendship, but when these two are together, it makes even the most hardened cynic believe that their grand dreams are possible.
