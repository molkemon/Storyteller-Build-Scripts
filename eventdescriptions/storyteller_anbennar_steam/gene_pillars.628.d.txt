The Master attacks with reckless abandon. The party lands multiple hits on him, wounding and bleeding his body. He stumbles backwards, pain and surprise clear on his face. It seems that the spirit is not accustomed to being harmed. After all these years of possessing a body, the Master had never experienced a true fight. "Damn you. Damn you all." He lets out a roar of frustration. And then his body suddenly falls lifelessly to the floor. Before the party can move to react, one of the members cries out. Behind them, a spirit hovers. Its hands, more resembling claws, dig into their face and chest. And behind them, Manui slowly prowls towards them, the metal plate lock in her hands. The spirit whips around as Manui approaches, but it is too late. Manui presses the lock against the spirit’s body and it bursts into light.
