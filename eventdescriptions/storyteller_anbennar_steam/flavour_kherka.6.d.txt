With the conquest of Arpedifer, and the magical infrastructure built, it's time to finally cast the prospecting spell. After finding it in one of the Arpedifer library's scrolls, our mages have already cast a weak version of it as a test, and everything went well. For the real deal it has been decided to "convince" some Arpedifer mages to collaborate with ours; if we could manage to conscript Zaleria it would be best, but even some apprentice of hers would suffice…  It's done. And it worked. A scrying circle as big as our island. Our mages annotated on a map all the ore veins the spell found and we're absolutely spoiled for choice, there's thousands of them.
