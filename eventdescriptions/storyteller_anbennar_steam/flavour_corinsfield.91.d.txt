To our east, now within our very borders, lies a great swath of scum and villainy, filled with people who offer nothing but murder and vice. This hive of wasps and spiders was known as New Havoral, and is home to the most vile breed of Aelantier. These brigands and blackguards are, by and large, far beyond redemption. Made of the dirtiest muck of the old world, pickpockets, bandits, and thieves, they welcome all stripes; from murderer to mage. We shall show them no quarter. However, there are some who show genuine repentance; some even celebrated our coming, as they would be bullied and robbed no longer by the insidious ruling sort. To these we are most concerned; should we press the same judgment on them as we do their brethren, or accept them at their word?
