With the help of the mysterious potion seller, who vanished without a trace from Varainé after reluctantly offering some of his knowledge, our research for the creation of the immortality potion is nearly finished. We now know that to finalize the product, we will need some sort of catalyst ingredient to focus the potion's effect, something to represent the very notion of immortality. Knowing this, the ruler sent forward Varainé's best explorers to find the rarest and most exquisite ingredients to suit this need, and the results didn't disappoint. From the icy north, a team of researchers brought in an Elven precursor, fully preserved and encased by the ice, and whose magical longevity would be ripe for extraction. Eastwards, in Escann, with the help of adventurers, another group of researchers managed to kill a young but fierce dragon, and the creature's heart was brought forward as a possible ingredient. Finally, while exploring some ruins near the Necropolis in Cannor, a team of researchers found some manuscripts detailing longforgotten magical processes surrounding the manipulation of life essence, which despite being prohibited by all current magical agreements, would work perfectly as a source of eternal life for our potion.
