The party reaches the top of the pillar. They emerge onto the roof, where countless winged servants fly above and around. It is a dizzying height; some of the party members look down and quickly regret their decision. Manui steps forward, pointing his spear at the lone being at the other end of the roof. The Tower Master, still possessing the body of the withered Kanolowelen, looks across the land. As he turns towards the party, his face is contorted with anger. "I have spent an eternity in that damned prison chamber. I have waited so very long to be freed. And now that I am?" The Tower Master stretches out his hand. The spear is ripped out of Manui’s hands and flies directly towards the Master. "I still cannot leave this damn pillar. I am still trapped. I can see the land before me up here. But I can never touch it. And even after those damned elves have left, I still have to deal with intruders like you. I let you all live for freeing me. Now I offer no such mercy." The Master twirls his spear and rushes at the party.
