Some spells and potions require esoteric instruments, such as body parts of various creatures. An unfortunate result of mages' overambition, possibly compounded by their cutting their restrictions, has resulted in a rash of unfortunate incidents in which harpies have been drugged and stripped of their feathers, and in some situations, slaughtered for their organs. This has met with no small amount of displeasure in the realm, and people look to their ruler for guidance.
