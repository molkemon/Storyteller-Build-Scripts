The garrison at the province has reported an unsettling occurrence. One morning, they awoke to find that a vast mound of animal corpses had appeared only a short distance away, within easy sight of the fort's walls. Tracks around the grisly pile indicate that the local trolls erected the pile overnight, dumping hundreds of bodies while using the strange, malleable shadow of the Swamp to avoid being noticed by the sentries. It is not clear why they did this, but most of the possibilities are unpleasant. Is it a trap? A warning? A curse? Some gruesome attempt at tribute? Whatever the reason, the pile is disgusting and, given the necromantic tendencies of the Swamp, probably dangerous.
