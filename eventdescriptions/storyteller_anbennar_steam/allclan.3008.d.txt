I bring ill tidings, my lord, if admittedly somewhat amusing. The Discombobulatorium is demanding we act. You see, there is a fad going around among their students that has them a little too interested in their assigned curriculum. Rather than slacking off and doodling weapons of mass destruction in true goblin fashion, they have taken to stealing important Discombobulatorium equipment to melt it down into scrap metal. While this is a commendable degree of enthusiasm, it would not hurt for us to supply them with some free copper wiring to avoid anything truly important going missing.
