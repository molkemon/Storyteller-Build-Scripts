The hunt in the Reach was a most unlikely toil. Skilled were the wingmaidens in trapping and tracks, yet now their quarry did hide, in mannish forests of timber, stone and narrow street. For great were the cities of the reach, and unlike any hunting ground that they had seen. And other hunters there were as well, who would make prey of the ruler's host. Yet they hid not in brush or canopy, but behind words and smiles. And their claws were hidden steel and unseen venom, and much did the maidens of Vængheim suffer, for they were illprepared for this unseen foe.
