The consort has been pacing the palace lately, their head hung low, only stopping sometimes to longingly gaze out the window. Life is quite different here, compared to their old country of origin, a nation some call our enemy. Where does that leave them, as a traitor to at least one side? They shake their head; such thoughts help no one, their place is here, in this realm, by the lord. However, with the knowledge that the government already has an heir from a secure dynasty, the consort sometimes wonders about their purpose at court. They did not marry out of love, and they have produced no royal children. Would they even be missed if they left?
