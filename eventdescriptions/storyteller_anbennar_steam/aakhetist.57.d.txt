One of Aakhet's favorite pastimes was to attend military drills and complain about the new recruits. Normally he left satisfied that his critiques had been listened to and would be acted upon, but his latest visit appeared to have troubled him far more than unusual. "Is this nation only good for trinkets for the rich? I cannot denounce the youths today, for they are not even given a chance. We have clearly spent too much time chasing gold instead of glory." Look at the sorry state of this. Aakhet looked genuinely upset as he tossed a battered wooden shield on the floor. "This is the type of equipment our rookie soldiers are having to use during their drills. I want those manufactories torn down and new military training facilities built in their place. If I am to criticise the youths of today, I wish them to be at least given a chance to disappoint me."
