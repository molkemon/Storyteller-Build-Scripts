A sour victory if I ever did taste one, but it is over, and for that I can only be grateful. And little time for sorrow of the fallen if the storm off the coast rolls in. There is always time for harvesting our well-earned spoils, though. For this evil creature, it seems the eyes are what we want to be cutting out. It is said, by those who know such things, that the eyes of this monstrosity can strike a man down with just a look. I will say, the men who first engaged the beast are not faring too well, with many keeled over and emptying their guts. If this is because of some magical eyeball, or just coming down from the nerves of battle, time will tell.
