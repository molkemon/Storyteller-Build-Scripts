In the year fourteen forty-four, the united hobgoblins of the Command seemed poised to ravage Haless and forge a great empire. Perhaps if they faced our weak and decadent cousins, they could have been successful, but it was not so. They faced us, and, unlike our cousins, we have not lost what let us dominate Haless all those years ago. Having previously crushed the hobgoblins, it was a foregone conclusion we would dominate Shamakhad once again. Now, Haless recognizes once more that the heirs of Harimar are the mightiest. Like our glorious scion, we shall conquer all we desire. Shamakhand, Yanshen, Bomdan, and even further. At the top of every food chain, there is an apex predator, a creature so powerful nothing else dares to prey on it. The Command deluded themselves with unreproachable lions, boars, and wolves, but the only apex predator the jungle knows is the tiger. It was Harimar who made those weaker than human hobgoblins flee to the dwarf caves, but the lesson clearly did not stick the first time. Let there be no ambiguity this time. Rahen is not theirs to conquer.
