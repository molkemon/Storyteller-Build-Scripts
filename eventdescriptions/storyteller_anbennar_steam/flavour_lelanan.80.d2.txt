I rubbed my tired eyes and looked over at Leli. Her face bore the signs of a weariness even greater than my own. With one hand, I closed the ledger before me, and with my other, I tenderly rubbed her back as she let out a quiet groan. The gesture immediately caused her eyes to droop shut. "My love... Let's get some rest..." I murmured to her. "We've done enough work for today." Leli nodded drowsily, smiling faintly, her speech a slur. "You're right, you're right. The flowers will be okay if we rest..." I took her hand and guided her to our bed, blew out the nearby candles, before joining her. "I'm so proud of you, Leli..." I murmured against her head.
