Although it is but one gear crank inside a Zatsarya away from Halann, the distance between the space colonies and our homeland feels greater than that; further than even Aelantier does at times. There is a growing sense that those back in Taneyas do not truly understand what it means to live on another world. Independence movements have even been growing on a certain colony. Although small, it would be better to placate them before this niche political viewpoint becomes more widespread.
