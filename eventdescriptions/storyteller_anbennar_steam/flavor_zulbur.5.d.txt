And now Relork relaxes, safe in the knowledge that they are safe and prosperous. No longer will the curse tear at the seams of their society or minds; no longer will the Ashana be known as raiders or monsters, but instead as artists and creators. No longer will Relork have to fight for what he believes in, for it is entrenched in all that he loves. And now Relork can relax and watch his deeds be immortalized in Adzalani culture, through both his statues within Zulbur and the widespread tale of Relork sweeping Adzalan.
