The split in our family was a blow that none of us expected. One by one Asarta and Arazin distanced themselves from us, as now they formed an alliance, united by their shared resentment towards us.  As we aspire to claim the title of the final empire, this cannot be the conflict that takes us down. We shall bring vengeance to our brethren for their insubordination and bring them under our heel once again, this time directly under our management.
