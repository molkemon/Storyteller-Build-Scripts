The Cannorians have a new faith, called Ravelianism. It preaches that God was torn apart by the Elves of old, and that one of its pieces lies in the form of a speaking box. And now Cannor falls under our grand peace, we have the opportunity to meet with these priests and discuss this last wayward face of Kalyin. But in the end they are wrong on one count. God is not shattered, Kalyin is One, and her faces stretch across the world.
