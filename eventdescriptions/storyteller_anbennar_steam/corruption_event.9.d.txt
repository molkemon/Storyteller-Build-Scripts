The country may not house the most egregious liars and traitors in the world, but compared to some other countries, our people do tend to be a bit more easily bribed into abusing their authority. Most of the realm's population accepts the difficulties and, to the dishonest, occasional benefits this brings. The people in the province see things differently. They lament this state of things as a consequence of letting the easily corrupted people rule the nation, claiming that the country ruled by their own people does not suffer the same fate. Some of them are suggesting that they would be better off seceding.
