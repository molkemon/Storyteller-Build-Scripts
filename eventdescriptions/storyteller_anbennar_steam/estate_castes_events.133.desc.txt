An eccentric farmer from one of our provinces has designed a way to irrigate his crops with great efficiency through a process this farmer has nicknamed "Three Level Irrigation." Many in our realm, after being told of his feat, seek to document his methods, or even outright reward them, though the latter may be a bit controversial, especially with the middle castes. Though the farmer’s ingenuity initially seems impressive, many are doubting the ability to do this amazing undertaking as a simple farmer.
