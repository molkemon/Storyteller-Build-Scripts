As local populations continue to rise, the study of urban planning has become an increasingly popular focus of study for many of those who have come to learn in our schools. In theory, the art is simply determining how to most efficiently use space and plan for expansion, but the all too common result is that such efforts are bogged down by local issues. However, a recent graduating class has proven itself very effective at the art, and we find our work to improve our cities to be at least a little smoother, for now.
