The first one was an odd sight, with some backwater Escanni merchant peddling greenskinned orks at one of our docks. Eventually, however, the entire dockyard district was full of them. Orks were once a threat, but now they have become a commodity, and it seems many in overseas Aelantier have begun to clamor for cheap and strong labor that offers little to no moral qualms, with Vertesk soon becoming the epicenter of this trade. After all, they invaded the surface, so maybe they should pay for their crimes? Why not make a little money in return?
