The rediscovery of Aelantier meant the incredible demand for labor for the colonies, in this case, orks. While many in Cannor see it as a distasteful trade, it has flourished greatly in the city of Vertesk, and many of our underworld contacts have started to peddle slaves amongst their stolen wares in the auction houses of Nathalaire. Indeed, our links across the Divenhal have begun to draw gnolls interested in partaking in this new burgeoning trade, and they have set up an official gnollish slave district in the city, which the locals are calling the "Krahzate", after the gnollish word for slaver 'Krah'.
