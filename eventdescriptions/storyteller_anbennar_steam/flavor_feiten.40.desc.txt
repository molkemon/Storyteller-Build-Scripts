In the past, whaling has been an individual endeavor. To cover as much ocean surface as possible, ships spread out, scanning widely for the elusive whale. As whaling has exploded in Feiten, so too have our techniques and hunts.  Instead of a singular ship sailing out into open ocean, a spotter fleet now patrols the Jellyfish Coast. Once a suitable beast is found, the fleet works together to drive the whale into shallower waters and tire it out, where specialized, smaller harpoon boats quickly pounce. The days of famous captains making their fortune off of a single successful hunt may be over, but the group hunting method is much more consistent and reliable overall.
