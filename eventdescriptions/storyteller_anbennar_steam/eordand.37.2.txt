At long last, word has returned from the Dalairey Expedition. All dead, save one who returned fully mad and clutching a chest full of Precursor riches. Bedraggled, wounded, and melancholic, the survivor claims that they buried the rest of it out in the wastes to keep it safe. Alas, they have passed from this world. As for the rest of the treasure, it was not found, but what was found was a massacre in the wastes. Brothers turned on each other, possibly slaying each other for a chance at the treasure. What could have driven them to do such an action? Surely they could have all arrived back home and lived comfortably forevermore?
