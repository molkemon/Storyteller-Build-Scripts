Halflings in other realms are usually seen cramped in ghettos at the worst areas of the cities, and as a whole, they have become a resolute minority dedicated to making ends meet. A part of this is making money for themselves, and some halflings have made recent fortunes peddling wares that are clearly a cheap imitation of the real thing. Merchants in the area are worried that this will affect the value of their goods, and plead to us they'll stop the halflings from trading in the area until either they match standard quality or abide by infringement laws. While we are not obliged to help the merchants, they might get angry if we were to refuse them.
