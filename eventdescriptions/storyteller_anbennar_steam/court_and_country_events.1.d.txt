The encroachment of the powerful state on domains that have traditionally been dominated by regional aristocrats, local courts or other interest groups has led to a widespread resentment of the ruler and the capital court. These groups have now united and formed alliances to combat central authority. Today we have received a number of objections to our latest tax decree, citing ancient rights and immoral conduct by the representatives of the ruler. It is clear that continued attempts to strengthen our power will be obstructed at every opportunity.
