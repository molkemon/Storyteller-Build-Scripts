An experimental elixir. A miscast spell. A simple pumpkin seed, caught in the crossfire. The worst disaster Newshire has ever seen. Within minutes, the Verdant Seed had grown to the size of a castle. Great roots dug deep into the ground, and rumbling from their growth could be felt all across the nation. Vines and ivy covered freshly toiled soil in hours. And that was before the beasts arrived; monsters of twisted bark and swarms of razor-toothed sprouts. Is this the price we pay for progress? The cost of success? Of prosperity? Did we give too much?
