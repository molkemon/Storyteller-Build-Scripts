There have been many who have cast doubts of our wayward ally in Yingzhen; some voices having blamed them even for Anjiang's betrayal of the League. Today, however, there can be little doubt that they were right. Yanzhong has fallen, betrayed by its own Golden Patrol. What's more is that nary hours had passed before these very same insurgents handed control of the city to Yingzhen. Refugees already flee to our borders, begging us to help them reclaim their homes.
