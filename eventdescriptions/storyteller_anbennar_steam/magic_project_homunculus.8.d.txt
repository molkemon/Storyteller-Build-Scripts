Whatever do you mean, "Are you made of mud?" I don't ask you if you're made of meat. Most constructs are created not with the gift of true freedom, but for the want of an intelligent, loyal, and eternal servant. The magical community understands this well, and the commonfolk understand it enough to the extent of comfortable caution and occasional suspicion. The homunculus, however, is different. it would have the capability to appear identical to a biological group if that be so desired. There is a natural tendency in the plain folk towards judgment. If they suspected there to be an imposter among us, the uprising would be fierce and unstoppable. Thus conventional logic would suggest we must hide the nature of our creation. Alternatively, we could get ahead of the whole mess; yes, this is a homunculus, so what?
