The GhostEmperor is a sovereign of men, indisputable heir to the mantle of Baihon Xinh. But he is greater than any who have come before; for the spirits listen to him as well, and obey. This is proven by recent happenings in the realm. a faint glow surrounds the palace at night, while many report hearing orders delivered by spirits walking in their dreams or appearing during a temple visit. The GhostEmperor's will spreads, and they are bound more tightly to glory as it does.
