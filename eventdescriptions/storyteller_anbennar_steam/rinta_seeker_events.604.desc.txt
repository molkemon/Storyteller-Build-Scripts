The Mwalim and one of their schools have gotten their hands on a tool from a far away land, of which the Gawakhizans who imported it have butchered the name of. They called for you to be present to analyze the new device. The details of what it was were left out in favor of describing what it could allow for. A tool specifically made for the advancement of knowledge and allowing for better analysis in a variety of fields of study. Clearly, this invention was a boon to everyone involved in the faith. Mages in the court begin to erupt in debate, arguing on who should be able to test the device. I’ve heard of some absurd claims in an attempt to justify testing the tool. Either way, scholars from Badabbi to Weggabiga all can't seem to decide who will get the funding to purchase more of these things. Clearly out of sync with each other, all sides seem to be looking towards any kind of governing voice. Maybe you could help them sire? What do you think?
