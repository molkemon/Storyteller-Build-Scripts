As we have pressed onwards, the inexperience of our party has begun to show. Misjudged steps triggering traps, overlooked hazards almost costing us our lives. It has become clear that we were not as prepared for this expedition as we thought. After the most recent close call, with a falling set of shelves of all things, we have paused to discuss our plan. If we continue on like this, it is obvious that dangerous mistakes will continue, risking injury or even death to the party. On the other hand, slowing our pace and being more cautious will no doubt hamper our progress.
