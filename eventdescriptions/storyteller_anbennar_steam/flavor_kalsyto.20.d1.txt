From the factories of the cities to the farmlands of the hinterlands, industry reigns. The men and women work each in their own way, not to be abused by the visionless hand of tyrants. And while those who wish to divide may rear their ugly heads, they shall be met with a united front, for they are many, and they are few. It is the end of an era, and the world dawns on an age of work and cooperation.
