Our lord stands before the crowd, radiating an aura of majesty which seems to nearly dim the sun itself. With a hand, the lord almost instantly silences the crowd. Time itself seems to pause as their lips part: 'My people, the time has come. I hereby proclaim with Kalyin's eternal blessing that I, our lord, lord of our realm, have unified every last man, woman, and child in these great lakes. For the first time in our history, peace - true peace - has graced this ancient yet wartorn land. I swear upon this crown: For all eternity, I shall act as Kalyin's faithful vassal upon this world.'
