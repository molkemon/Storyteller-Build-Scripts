A controversy among the songsters of our country has reached the ears of the monarch. An ambitious young harpy has composed a song in honour of the ruler, extolling her many virtues. The songster is pushing for her peers to add the new piece to the official repertoire of the flock. More conservative voices have denounced the upstart, claiming she is merely trying to curry favour and disrespecting our traditions in the process. How shall we resolve this conflict?
