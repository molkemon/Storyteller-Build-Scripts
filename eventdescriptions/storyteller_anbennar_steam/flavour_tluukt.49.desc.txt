Our relations with both Tluukt and Siadan, once a happy alliance of mutual interest, has now somehow become a point of conflict. The ruler of Tluukt, decries our relations with Siadan, characterizing them as scheming vultures and pinning our lack of success in the Salahad on our alliance with the harpies. The ruler of Siadan, for her part denies these allegations and blames Tluukt for no end of suspicious circumstances in the Salahad, accusing them of being a craven scoundrel. What is abundantly clear is that both Tluukt and Siadan are expecting our pack to side with them against the other.
