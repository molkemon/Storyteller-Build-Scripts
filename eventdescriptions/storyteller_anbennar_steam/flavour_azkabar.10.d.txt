Whilst the majority of dwarves in the world can be found in Cannor, there are still populations scattered across Bulwar. Everywhere they go, they bring their talents in metallurgy, excavation and crafting. With the plans of our lord, it seems likely that we'll need significantly more obsidian. Thankfully, the number we've employed already has proved incredibly capable, gathering significant amounts of obsidian, as well as a truly massive amount of gems. Truly, these dwarves are friends of Bulwar.
