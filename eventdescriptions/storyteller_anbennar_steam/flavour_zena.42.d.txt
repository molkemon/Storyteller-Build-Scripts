Deep in the Serpentspine, the Spiderwretch goblins have carved out a niche by mastering the arachnid beasts they ride. From the massive spiders they mount, they harvest silk to create armor, venom, and, most surprisingly, pavement. This was news to Azaly, who happened upon a batch of Spidersilk Crawlers as he was searching for dwarven holds near the Ogre Valley. Accompanying them to their camp, he marvelled at their paved roads, created by binding stones with websilk. Hard and durable but soft on the feet, he has returned with several of the goblin’s premier weavers to see if they can replicate this with other silks to pave the streets of Bivuzena, knowing that the people may be less than enthused about walking on spiderwebs.
