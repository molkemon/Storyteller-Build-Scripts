Due to the exalted place of the Favored Hundred in society, it is sometimes difficult to remember the royal harems ostensible purpose: the provision of companions and servants to the lord. Nevertheless, it still manages to perform this function admirably. Not only does the Hundred still excel at fulfilling the lord’s physical desires, courtesans have come to provide personal companionship and emotional support. One in particular has formed a particularly close bond with the ruler, and it seems they have lightened the burden of rule.
