Kalyin is in everything, and in everyone, for we have seen this already with the many faces of Her we found across Halann. We cannot simply rely on our own opinions of how to rule. As such, we have gathered people from many races and many lands to Yarikhoi, to the Conclave of a Hundred Faces, so that we may see the totality of mortal experience and learn how to perfect our insight on the world.
