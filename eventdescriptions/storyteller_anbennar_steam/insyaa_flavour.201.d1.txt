In the aftermath of our latest expedition to the Gene Pillar in that province, teams have been left in place to slowly sift through the mountain of data now available to us. Among their discoveries is a device which, the team believes, is intimately connected to the function and continued violence of the Kiamoa, the raging wall of storms which form the very wall of our world. The data we have discovered in the pillar confirms what the Dragon Turtles have always maintained; there is something beyond the Kiamoa. What that something is, especially after so much time, is a more vexed question. Our team believes that the machinery they have found could bring down the Kiamoa altogether, allowing us to travel beyond it. Beyond a few swiveleyed intellectuals and fringe thinkers, however, the idea has little to recommend it.
