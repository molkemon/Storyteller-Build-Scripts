Today, when the monarch was washing their face, they received a peculiar note, conjured in a water bowl by one of the Khets. "Dear Steward, I would like to invite you and your family to accompany us in our barge for a trip across the Sorrow. We're embarking in three days; please make sure your appearance is as natural as possible." The royal family showing off their bodies like this would of course be the next hot topic for the next few months, but ignoring the Khet isn't possible either.
