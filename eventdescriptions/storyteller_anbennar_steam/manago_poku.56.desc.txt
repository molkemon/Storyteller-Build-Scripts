Where am I? Who am I? Why am I here? Almost universally, a Mechanim's first words. None of us has an answer to the questions that have shaped us ever since a comet lit up the sky and gave us a chance at life. It may be that we will never find one. But if that is one of the things we learn on our quest, so be it. Right now, we must simply learn all we can, and pick up the pieces of our existence. Perhaps our quest will be like restoring a rock outcropping trampled by a hinuibeast: absurdly difficult to do and pointless to boot. Perhaps it will be like trying to awaken one of our yet dormant brethren with fist or word or song: worthy, however impossible it proves. Perhaps it will be more like some other thing that we discover on our journey. That too, is a reason to journey. Right now, we have only a glimmer of what awaits us. But every leaf or rock or river might hide a hint, and the strange creatures that inhabit these lands may yield even more helpful clues. We are thankful for this, because there are so, so, many lessons to be learned.
