If there is one thing anyone with even a modicum of knowledge knows about the human race, it is their lust for something greater. Recently human members of our government, driven by this innate quality, have begun pushing themselves to ever greater heights of productivity and perseverance. Two schools of thought have developed in the capital in response to this development. One group argues that this can and should be leveraged for the good of the realm. Another group considers the recent rush of human activity a threat that should be contained. These factions have come to the ruler to seek their opinion.
