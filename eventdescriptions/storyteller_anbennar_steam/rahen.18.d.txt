Porcelain makers along the Kharunyana have been hit by a downturn in sales in the last few years. Instability in the Raj and the looming threat of hobgoblins from the north greatly reduced demands in the high quality porcelains that they made, but they have lately faced another threat from upstream. The porcelain production facilities in Sarisung were not exactly known for their quality, but their size more than made up for it in quantity. Though of lower quality than true hard paste porcelains, the lower pricing demanded by the sarisung guilds has been more affordable to households with lower incomes to purchase, expanding the market but dropping prices. With gangs and cartels running that city, our own porcelain producers have been asking to stop the entry of Sarisung products in our market.
