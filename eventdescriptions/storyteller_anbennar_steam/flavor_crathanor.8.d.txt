We have reclaimed our ancestral lands in Itrahures, known to the rest of the world as Businor, but enemies lurk all around us. The Foothills still reek of our ancient Gnollish foes, to the north the Empire of Anbennar eyes greedily at weakened Busilar, and to the south our Tefori cousins are eager to exploit our victory. We may have won the battle, but if we seek to restore our ancient glory, right those ancient wrongs, and ascend as the new Sun in the Divenhal, we must win, no matter the cost. Itrahures will not fall again.
