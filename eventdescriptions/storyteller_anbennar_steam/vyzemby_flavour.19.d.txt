The dance of Enahelibe performed by the Sister of Lel takes on a different meaning to the Vyzemby in times of strife. In peace, it is a fertility dance, calling to mind harmony and nature. It is a time to celebrate the pure beauty in love. It does not lose these elements in difficult times, but instead places greater emphasis on the bonds of love and how together one can build a better future for their offspring. This is why the Sisters continue to perform this dance in harsher times. because they remind you that tomorrow can, and will, improve.
