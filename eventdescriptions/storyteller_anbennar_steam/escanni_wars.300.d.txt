The realm has seen better days. Ever since the wars began, battles have been fought in the fields and invading armies have pillaged the surrounding villages for supplies, leaving burning houses, butchered livestock, and murdered peasants in their wake. Consequently, the survivors have packed what meagre belongings they had left and formed a caravan. It should have come as no surprise when a missive arrived from our borders this morning. But instead of the usual report of a skirmish or a raid, the local guards describe a massive caravan headed westwards to the ports of the Dames head. When they were stopped, the emigrants explained that their farms and villages in the realm have been burned down and instead of starving to death or being killed by bandits, they prefer to head to the Trollsbay, where they hope for a better life. We could try to stop them and send them back to their towns, but they are clearly going to try to force their way out regardless as they no longer have anything to lose.
