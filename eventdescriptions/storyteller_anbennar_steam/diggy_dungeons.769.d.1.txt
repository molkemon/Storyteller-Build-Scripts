Ranks formed, as our front line made ranks and slowly advanced on the coming golem horde, forming a solid wall which did its best to stay the golem advance. Behind this line, our heaviest hitters formed into two groups, and quickly as could be managed swung around the flanks leaping over or sliding under obstacles and crashing against the sides of the golem horde. We could hear Verdric shouting obscenities as he rallied another wave, but at the very least we will be able to gain ground. It'll take some more effort to reach the dais, but progress at this point is steady.
