The integration of gnolls into our society brought a new workforce; initially distrusted, the first roles they were encouraged into were less desirable jobs such as farm labour. Over time, many farms came to rely on gnoll workers. With the Yenaze Mezhi’s antignoll policies in full effect, many gnolls have gone into hiding, and it is hard to hide if you need to go to work at the same place every day. Many gnolls have chosen to simply stop showing up, and there are not enough humans to replace them. Our advisors are warning of a nationwide famine.
