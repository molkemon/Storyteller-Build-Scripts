May Surael’s Light shine upon you. When the hordes of Zokka, thricecursed be his name, descended upon the banks of the Suran, you sent emissaries, you sent messengers, you sent your despairing subjects to the lands of me, your benevolent father, pleading for deliverance. And as was my sacred duty, I marched to your aid, yet to my enduring regret my legions failed to shatter the vile horde. Despite our valorous sacrifice, I failed in my obligations on that most ill-fated of days, leaving you to the merciless depredations of the demon worshippers. Yet those dark days are at long last behind us all. Just as His light chases away the darkness each morn, I have shattered the vile might of Zokka Devourer of Suns, may he choke on his greed for all eternity. It now falls to us to restore the proper order in Surael’s Garden. Like a diligent father I shall not rest until the last of my lost children are once more safe under my protection. I entreat you to seek me out in radiant Sareyand and renew your oath of fealty, so that we may be one family again, united and blessed in purpose. Do not let the whispers of the Dark sway you from your path, for only together may we fulfill our sacred duty to Surael. I, Eledas Sarelzuir, by the grace of Surael exalted king of Sareyand, say this to you, my beloved charge, and await your reply with paternal benevolence and patience.
