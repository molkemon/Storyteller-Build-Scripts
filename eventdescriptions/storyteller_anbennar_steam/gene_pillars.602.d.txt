In a long hallway, the party encounters an elderly man carrying a spear. They recognize his features as Kanolowelen. He hurriedly runs through the hallway, sweat and exhaustion clear on his face. The party calls out to him, but he does not respond. He runs into the party, passing straight through their bodies, ethereal in nature. The party recognizes the phenomenon as a vision of the past. Other Insyaans have been here before. Suddenly, another figure appears after the elder. Another Kanolowelen, but younger. The man floats in the air, distorting the very air and space around it. He chases the old man down the hallway. As he passes the party, he suddenly stops and turns to look at them. "Who is there? Someone is watching?" The figure speaks in an unknown language. Despite that, the meaning is somehow conveyed. Narrowing its eyes, the figure looks for a few more seconds, but somehow fails to see the party in front of him. Then he resumes the chase, disappearing at the end of the hallway.
