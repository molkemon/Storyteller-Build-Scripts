A platform of crimson wood, brought from beyond the seas, was built in front of Corin's temple. The people had reunited in the temple's square, eager to hear the words of the ruler. On the platform, decorated in the colors of Dameria and Corin's church, stood the ruler and the grand priest of the Corinite Church of Rogieria. The old priest went up to a podium, and said.
