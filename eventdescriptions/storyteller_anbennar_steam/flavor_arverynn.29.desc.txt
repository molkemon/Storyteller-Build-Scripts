Our restored rule over Sarda has prompted many nobles to pay a visit to the Teal Keep and give us their congratulations. Among them was an envoy from the house of Stenurynn who, after congratulating us for our present accomplishments in an eloquent manner few could match, concluded the visit by reminding us of a debt. A previous ruler had long partnered with the lords of Stenurynn, both in their youth when Lord Kyran yen Stenur provided them with troops, equipment and hospitality in preparation for their rightful return to Arverynn, and afterwards when the ruler sat on the throne and the yen Stenur sponsored many of Arverynns festivities out of their pockets. While the rest of the previous ruler’s debtors have already been repaid, those in Stenurynn were patiently waiting for the situation in Sarda to stabilize: now that they are collecting debt from a ruler in law and in fact, what shall our answer be?
