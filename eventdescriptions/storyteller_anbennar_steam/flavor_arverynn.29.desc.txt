Our restored rule over Sarda has prompted many nobles to pay a visit to the Teal Keep and give us their congratulations. Among them, was an envoy from the house of Stenurynn who, after congratulating us for our present accomplishments in an eloquent manner few could match, concluded the visit by reminding us of a debt. Calrodiy the Fourth had long partnered with the lords of Stenurynn, both in his youth when Lord Kyran yen Stenur provided him with troops, equipment and hospitality in preparation for his rightful return to Arverynn, and afterwards when Calrodiy sat on the throne and the yen Stenur sponsored many of Arverynn's festivities out of their pockets. While the rest of Calrodiy's debtors have already been repaid, those in Stenurynn were patiently waiting for the situation in Sarda to stabilize. now that they are collecting debt from a ruling monarch in law and in fact, what shall our answer be?
