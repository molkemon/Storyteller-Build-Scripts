Since the beginning of history, countless peoples have practiced sending messages and treasures or even remains out to sea as an enduring gesture. The country upholds this tradition too, but when we utter a prayer as our bottles vanish into the endless seas, we never expect the bottles to return to us. Adorned in colorful bands of purple, orange, and blue, a cadre of merfolk arrived on our shores tugging hundreds of containers stuffed with farewell offerings. They introduced themselves as a submerged society sworn to goodness and truth, stating their primary mission was to complete the unfulfilled wishes they have accumulated over the years. Given the sheer volume of these keepsakes, the merfolk dogooders have quite the task ahead. one we might assist with, especially with translation and location efforts. Of course, we just can pledge to help and then sell the dredged goods.
