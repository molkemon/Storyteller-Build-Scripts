The ghastly apparitions flew towards them, their icy breath taking them by surprise. Yet they seem to be weak, their influence upon them barely noticeable. Through some they passed their ethereal bodies, creating a faint layer of ice on the skin through which they went. Those who had multiple ghosts go through them would not stand up again, but in the end, the wraiths disappeared. In their place lay now icy crystals glittering in the torchlight. They are cold to the touch and do not seem to warm up or melt, a fascinating and, most likely, valuable discovery.
