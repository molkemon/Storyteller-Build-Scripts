The ghastly apparitions flew towards us, their icy breath taking us by surprise. Yet they seem to be weak, their influence upon us barely noticeable. Through some they passed their ethereal bodies, creating a faint layer of ice on the skin through which they went. Those who had multiple ghosts go through them would not stand up again, but in the end, the wraiths disappeared. In their place lay now icy crystals glittering in the torchlight. They are cold to the touch and don't seem to warm up and don't melt, a fascinating and, most likely, valuable discovery.
