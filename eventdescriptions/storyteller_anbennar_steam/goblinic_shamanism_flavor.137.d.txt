For practitioners of the Fey Cult, contact with the Fey is seen as a great blessing, however, deals with them are to be left to the Feykeepers as even those who seem to have gained the upper hand end up losing. It was no surprise then that our ruler was delighted when they were visited by a fey. Naturally said fey began trying to strike up a bargain with our lord, to little success on the Fey's part. That was until the fey made a very particular offer "Fine. All I ask is that you answer this riddle, and I will grant you your reward: What has a golden head and a golden tail but no body?"
