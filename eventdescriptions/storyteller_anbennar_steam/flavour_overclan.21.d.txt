Report. Marked social tensions have been reported among the population within the Serpentsreach. While initially manageable when the ranks were simply composed of Exodus Goblin and Marble Dwarf settlers, other groups have proven far less amicable to the rule. Several notable examples include. Kobold inhabitants of the Bûrad Forge displaying fierce territorialism, constantly repelling any attempts by other groups to settle these caverns; Dwarves who never left the Serpentsreach, were not born under the rule, or otherwise feel no obligation to the realm have shown stubborn resistance to the rule, as well as a troubling penchant for violence toward goblin citizens; orks and cavedwelling goblins participating in riots against what is perceived as undue authority held by dwarves and foreign goblins. While violence is ongoing, it has yet to reach the level of full-scale rebellion against the rule. In fact, many dissenters, militant or otherwise, have expressed a desire for greater representation within the state. As this would not be incompatible with the current operation of government, it is advised plans be made to begin appeasing these malcontent subjects.
