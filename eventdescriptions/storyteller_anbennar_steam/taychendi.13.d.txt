Hear me, and rejoice. The first child of our monarch has come into the world. Cheer for our new heir. While the criers are announcing the birth to the population, the whole court is busy with the preparations for the birth rituals for the newborn. Already the baby was bathed and named by the proud parents; now the time for the ceremony to propitiate the child's future has come. The court will move to a famous battlefield where our most renowned warrior will bury a shield with the dynasty's insignia, together with the connection between the mother and the child. They will become a great warrior and leader.
