The king was found dead outside the Red Citadel, and the news spread too fast to be covered by the eunuchs. Could it be? An emperor of Luzhong dead on the streets? Was he killed? Is the realm poisoned from the inside? Did he kill himself? Could an emperor ever do that? Rapidly, something was made clear. the imperial family was simply not worthy of the empire their predecessors built. The curtain of lies slowly started to crumble as the news spread more and more; it did not stop at Emgolu at all. It was clear that blood was about to be spilled. Like it once was hundreds of years ago, the Beiklings shall fight each other, but this time they have one enemy. the Xion Dynasty.
