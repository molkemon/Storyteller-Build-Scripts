With the gnomes taking up a more prominent role in society as of late, and with the plenitude of gnomes making their way into the lord’s court, the ruler has been getting exposed to the gnomish philosophy of The Thought. The lord has been developing an interest in the philosophy, and to the dismay of the clergy is spending more and more time with gnomish thinkers. The Thought is a very tolerant school of thought, which does not infringe on the belief in the Cannorian Pantheon or any belief for that matter, however the clergy still deeply dislike the doctrine.
