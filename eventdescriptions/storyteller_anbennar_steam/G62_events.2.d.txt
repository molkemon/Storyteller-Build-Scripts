Rowing from the shores of Kimánis they come, fleeing from what all the tyrants have done. Row, row. Here they come, back to the land of the free. Row, row. Here they come, from tyrants they are to flee. The name of the Dega is in utter shame, and the men of Okti are crying in bed, no fit rulers in sight, for now. Thus the Kimani peoples are leaving for the bastion of freedom, Amgremós. Row, row. Here they come, back to the land of the free. Row, row. Here they come, from tyrants they are to flee.
