The burghers are not the sole authority within their cities; the church also has a strong presence in most big cities going back to their initial founding. Conflicts sometimes arise when the councils of the burghers implement rules that the church disapproves of. Such conflicts have become more and more commonplace, and members of the church are openly acting in defiance of the various rules the burghers have agreed upon in their cities. Delegates from various cities dominated by the burghers are now urging us to clarify the situation.
