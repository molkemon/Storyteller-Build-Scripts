The ruler has sent a messenger to discuss the recent witch hunts in our country. He strongly condemns the witch trials, especially the lynchings that have occurred when suffering people have taken it into their own hands to punish alleged witches, many of which were not mages in the first place. Magic is divine, and must be treated and respected by all. But apostate mages who refuse to do so must be dealt with by proper Rectorate officials and inquisitors, not the whims of the masses. We might not agree with this sentiment, but the ruler is the ruler.
