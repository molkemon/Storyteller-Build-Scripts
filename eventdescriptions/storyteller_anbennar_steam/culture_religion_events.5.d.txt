The presence of an openly heathen ruler in the capital has not gone unnoticed among the subjects in the realm. Among the orthodox, this has come to be a common matter of irritation and discussion, and in areas with existing religious tensions, it has led to even greater frequency of conflicts. A priest named a specific individual is reported to have attempted to further inflame the situation by repeatedly returning to the corruption of the capital in agitating speeches to the people of the province. At this point, it might be hard to remove him from his position without extensive use of force.
