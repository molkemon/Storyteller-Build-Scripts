Ash chokes the air as timbers smolder and stone crumbles to the ground. Relics that have stood for a thousand thousand years lie shattered upon the earth rather than held aloft towards the heavens. Cries of rage and despair fill the air, mages and their collaborators screaming as they are clasped in chains and taken away. Those who resist are put down for their impudence. For their insubordination against the Great Command, the High Temple has suffered the price of disobedience brought upon it by siding with the opposing ruler.
