Failure. By some malignant convergence of incompetence we have been defeated by the forces of evil in the Leechdens. Those of our people that were not able to get away from that fetid pesthole in time have been exterminated to the last individual. The Leechdens have, due to our failure and the evil powers of the Leechmen, been reduced to a worthless swampland devoid of any life aligned with the Effelai and its Goddess.
