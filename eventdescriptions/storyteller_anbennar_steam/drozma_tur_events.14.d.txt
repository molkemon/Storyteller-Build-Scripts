For long years the Divine Hatchling was kept safe and protected for his tutelage to be successful and his wisdom and might to be as great as we could give. Now upon the RiverCycle of the Dragon we declare and present our God to the world. To Svemel the followers have been called in hundreds and thousands to give praise to the young Prince. Rzenta and all faithful are awash with joy and triumph, for we endured the dark days in mourning of Drozmagog and are now rewarded, here to see his Heir arise. Hear oh Tur, followers. Hear oh Ynn, great river. Bow for the Son of God. Praise the Prince of Change. Fear the Lord of Eternal Fire.
