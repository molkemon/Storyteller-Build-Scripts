With the degree of intermarriage between ourselves and Eborthíl, it would be more accurate these days to speak of two wings of the same vast family. Our ships dock in one another’s ports, our people travel unaccosted as quasicitizens of one another’s countries, and our diplomatic outlook is functionally shared. Factions in both families have begun to argue for a closer union, a dynastic compact which would strengthen both sides. Of course, síl Verne will be the surname for formality’s sake, but we can surely allow an Eborthíli colour or two into the heraldry.
