Relations with The Command have been sour ever since they overtook the Horned Ogres as their neighbour. Border incidents such as orkish plundering along their side of the border, or smugglers who seek to circumvent embargos have been happening ever since. A true escalation however seems to have come from the expedition atop the Jade Mountains. Avalanches caused by their expedition for Balris have damaged hobgoblin settlements along the edge of the mountain range and now relations seem more hostile than ever. With an Alliance president at the helm, rhetoric on a potential war against The Command has harshened, and war has now become a real possibility, especially after Balris said the following on the matter. "The humans of Shamakhad were far nicer downstairs neighbours than the hobgoblins, they had far better architecture. I wouldn't mind seeing a return of The Ruin Kingdoms."
