It is a tragic day, for the monarch has died. She was found in bed, seeming to have simply fallen asleep. The heir, after an initial period of shock, quickly took charge and led an investigation into the death. While the monarch was in good health, no evidence of foul play has been found, even after a magical examination by the heir. The end of her long and prosperous rule has led to great mourning among the commoners, who hope that the heir will follow the legacy of his predecessor. This seems unlikely, as she has a dream that will doubtlessly require bloodshed—the unification of Fahnavosy.
