It was a strange day in the realm, the sky was a crimson red, gray wheat was wilting and drowning in the fields, children were crying in terror and hiding in the cabins in the protective embrace of their families. Back in the grand temple, the ruler sat in front of a roundtable, surrounded by his most elite shamans, all had a clearly excited look on their faces with the sounds of violent thunder and bloody downpour hitting the roof. “THIS IS WONDERFUL.” One shouted in joy, releasing the built up excitement in the room. “Many years have passed, and we have gotten a sign of the Dookanson’s return. Clearly he had been planning the invasion of the heavens themselves all this time.” There were plenty of joyful, victorious celebrations and reassurance of faith from the other shamans, who cheered on, even lifting the ork up in unison shouting “URAK DUL.” through the sound of thunder, screams of anguish and terror and floodborns drowning outside mixed with celebrations of other, more faithful Dookanists.
