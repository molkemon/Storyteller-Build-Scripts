When the Verdant Veil fell, many of our people struck out to find their place in this newly rediscovered world. One of these elves, Gwindeth Camllann, accompanied Corin herself, serving loyally as one of her many companions in the fight against Korgus Dookanson. After Dookanson had been slain, she studied the ways of Diranbe, the Art of Bladedancing, at the Blaíddscal in Ibevar. Now, she has returned to the Deepwoods, returning to us, bringing with her all of the knowledge she has accumulated in her travels. Most significantly, she has brought with her a unique variant of Bladedancing, one which she has personally adapted to fit her own preferred weapon, the Glaive. As glavewielders make up the backbone of our people's infantry, Camllann's art, known as Dawnswheí or Diranáí, 'Glaivedancing,' has begun to intrigue many of our most decorated warriors, many of whom have sought Camllann out to be taught this practice. Should this practice find its roots within our martial doctrine, it may prove to turn the tides of battle in our favor.
