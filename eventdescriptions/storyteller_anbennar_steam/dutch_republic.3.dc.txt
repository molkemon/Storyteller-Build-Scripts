Popular support for the House of Silcalas is strengthened from the pulpit of the primary church, where the common folk are often reminded of the great importance of a strong ruler for the state church and the tradition of hereditary rule. Our realm, however, accommodates more than one faith. Those who adhere to other faiths don’t always have kind words for the established order, with some using our republican ideals as an excuse to openly challenge the lord and the state church.
