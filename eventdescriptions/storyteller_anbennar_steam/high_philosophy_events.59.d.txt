The great emphasis that our government has placed on Dekhana, the reflective and internal side of the Nitikamarja, has had the effect of alienating many ministers, reformers, and activists looking to make their mark on society. No longer content with meditation and scholarly debate, many in the realm have begun to organize around gurus and preachers, who call for enhanced worldly involvement and an end to the spiritual withdrawal that they claim dominates the realm. As tensions flare, several influential philosophers have declared their support for the monarch, potentially calming the situation.
