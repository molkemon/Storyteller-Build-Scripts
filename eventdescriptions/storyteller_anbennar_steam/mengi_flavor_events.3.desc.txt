Though defeated in 1370 many slaves in our land eventually learn the story of Abazima SkyEye, his enslavement at Fetengoni hands, his service in Yetmesira, his sale to Raheni merchants, and his eventual return to Yetmesira at the head of an army. Many enslaved, regardless if from Bamaliwuni, seek to emulate his actions and gain their freedom via force, we now need to put down these Rememberers otherwise the revolt may spread and threaten our kingdom.
