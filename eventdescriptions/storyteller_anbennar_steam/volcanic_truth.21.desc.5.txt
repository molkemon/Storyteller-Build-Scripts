The matter of the Molten Eternity is decided. Like us, the Molten Eternity understands the value of change. By learning what they have from the other faiths of Insyaa, they have made their faith stronger. They may have abandoned the worship of the Twin Gods, but we need not repeat their mistakes. So long as we can incorporate what is good about their faith, it will allow us to synthesize their beliefs with our own. Synthesis is a form of change as well. We could not expect the religious landscape of Insyaa to remain constant forever. If there is one thing the Volcanic Truth knows all too well, it is change, and that is why we will prevail against all challengers.
