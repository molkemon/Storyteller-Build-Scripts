It is a well-known fact throughout these lands that halflings are usually very powerful individuals, with their concentration among our nobility. Moreover, influential halflings tend to stick together, forming connections that provide them with more and more influence if left unchecked. Taking these two considerations together, a prominent citizen known for hatching half-baked conspiracies has accused several halfling notables in our country for conspiring against our ruler and its title for the benefit of their own kind. These allegations are only barely credible, but we could put this to use to create a significant change in this country in one direction or another.
