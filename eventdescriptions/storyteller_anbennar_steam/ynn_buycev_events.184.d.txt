After two weeks without word from their monarch, a search party was formed to look for the ruler. It took a week of combing through the woods the wendigo has been plaguing to find the refined set of armor their monarch was wearing when they set off, now battered and soaked in blood. It was found many meters from the monarch's bones, picked clean by the wendigo. The only thing clearly identifying the body as specifically the monarch's was the gilded and gemstudded ring found among the finger bones bearing the bloodsplattered lettering reading the dynasty's name. The government's citizens feel no sympathy for their previous monarch; if anything, they feel resentment, for their supposedly wise leader went off on a suicide mission which ended predictably poorly, leaving the government in a state of disarray.
