With the integration of Redtusk orks into our diplomatic corps, diplomacy with orkish nations is smoother. Navigating orkish customs, so alien to our human diplomats, is fraught with danger, but our Redtusk diplomats seem to negotiate these challenges with ease. One diplomat in particular has astonished our lord, as what they believed to be a grave insult to our orkish neighbors was instead wellreceived by their ruler.
