Concerning news has reached the court from the province. Apparently the temple gardens there are suffering from a strange affliction, the flowers, plants and trees withering away as if starved by a devastating drought. Of course this has caused much consternation and worry both amongst the ranks of the priesthood and the faithful inhabitants of the province, who fear that the Malevolent Dark has strengthened its influence over the land and that Surael has averted His gaze from them.
