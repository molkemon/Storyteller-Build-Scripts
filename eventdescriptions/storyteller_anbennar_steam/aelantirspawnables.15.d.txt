As knightly traditions have waned over the upcoming century, a large group of Dwarven Knights from Lencenor have decided to forsake their old ways and begin life anew in the promising lands of Aelantier. Led by the most prominent of knights who formed the clans of Hardgauntlet, Runeback, and Glitterhoof, they venture to the rich lands of Veykoda for a new future.
