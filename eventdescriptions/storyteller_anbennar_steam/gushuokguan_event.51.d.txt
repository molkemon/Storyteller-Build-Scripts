The representative Wu speaks now. "Brethren, I stand before you with an urgent message, one which cannot be overlooked in the slightest. Our neighbours, who still cling to ancient autocracy, threaten the independence and prosperity of not just my republic but of the entire confederation. We must act swiftly and liberate its population from tyranny now." A lot of arguing ensues, solidifying into two camps, to only one of which we must lend our support. Camp A supports the war, Camp B does not want to start a war and instead wishes to focus on internal development. It is now up to us to choose a side and with that choose which direction the confederation goes.
