The Everglow Halls within Elynáile are the location of the first steps of elven unity, undertaken when the Wood Elven clans formed the Verdant Pact in response to the Greentide. Within its Luminous Archive, many chronicles of Wood Elven history and knowledge can be found. Built upon the shores of the Silent Lake in the Deepwoods, it stands as a shining beacon of elven unity and a treasure trove of research. Azaly does not travel to Elynáile to study their tomes and scrolls, however. What he is interested in is the eponymous Halls themselves. The name Everglow was given due to the affixture of the vaytbulbs that were plucked and grafted into the walls, coaxed carefully to glow brightly at all times. He spent countless hours in discussion with prominent elven botanists and fey researchers on how to care for the plants within a different climate, and how to best create a familiarity to the feylinked woods, given the proximity of much of Sarhal to other planes. Upon his return, he put his theories into practice within Medaka’s Exceptional Treasures, with several wood elven rangers accompanying him to ensure that nothing dangerous from the feyrealm crossed over when he lit up the museum. He is currently planning on expanding the vaytbulb network throughout the city.
