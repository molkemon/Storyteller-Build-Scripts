The most soothing bardic tunes fill the banquet hall of the capital; a perfect accompaniment to the feast of meats and cheeses served atop its tables. Around it, Silistras of all generations have gathered into small circles of conversation. When was the last time the capital held such a celebration, and when was the last time the monarch enjoyed a feast with distant family? The centurylong Lilac Wars had created a divide between the two branches of their dynasty and, were it not for the Bards extending the hand of reconciliation towards the Marshals, might have created a rivalry that would have only ended with the destruction of the other. To this day, the scars of those wars still haunt Istralore, the de facto heirs of Dameria, the right hand of a deposed dynasty. A round of cheers breaks the monarch out of their trance. The sponsor and guest of honor of this event proudly raises their chalice. 'Brothers, sisters, cousins,' the sponsor exclaims, 'I would like to raise a toast to the growing success of their nation. For so long, our nation was just a small collection of counties, but now our lands are vast and rich and our people are flourishing. All that could never have been achieved without the support of our noble kinsmen. Despite our differences during the Lilac Wars, we have shown Anbennar the strength of our blood; of our bonds.' Cheers and heckles arise, comparing the sponsor to a protagonist in an epic for young boys. 'Now to top off my little segment in this reunion concert,' the sponsor looks towards the monarch, 'After many acts apart, this saga we call history has put us in the same scene. No, their nation's proposal to you, dear monarch, is that we share the spotlight. Reunite the family. Put dear old Istralania and Sideric's souls at peace.'
