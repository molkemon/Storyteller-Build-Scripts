The recent establishment of a Gommo branch office in that realm has expedited the growth of the local trade and artifice community. The Gommo branch office has allowed many local craftsmen to establish valuable foreign contacts and clients. Now a representative of the Gommo has petitioned us for permission to expand the branch office into a fully fledged Gommo Trade Division based in that realm. Allowing the expansion would certainly increase the branch office's role as a vital trade conduit for artificer inventions and valuable knowledge. A refusal, on the other hand, is certain to see the Gommo reduce their investment and pursue trade routes elsewhere.
