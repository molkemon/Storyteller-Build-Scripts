Our explorers have journeyed long and far, and at last have discovered what lies behind the Stormwall. All this time, there has been an entire continent of people within it, who are amazed by our ships and baffled by our advanced technology. After an initial period of difficult and stilted conversation, we have begun communicating with some of them, and learned that they consider everyone to be the same person in different stages of development. Now that they have learned there are many more people in the world than they thought, it appears to have set off a crisis of faith for them.
