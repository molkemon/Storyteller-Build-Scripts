His Majesty, sovereign of the Southern Reaches of Rahen, Custodian of the Sun Mountain has a reputation of a great ruler. As befits a monarch of such caliber and stature, they were graced to be a parent of several capable children and many who show great promise as future rulers. As both the children themselves and our government officials are aware, however, only one can inherit and there is little in the way of consolation prizes. Our Viziers are adamant that it is now high time to name a favorite among the possible successors. This is unlikely to end the competition for their favor but it will hopefully stop the worst succession struggles that might follow if our ruler should meet their maker without having picked a clear successor.
