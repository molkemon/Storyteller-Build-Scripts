His Majesty, sovereign of the Southern Reaches of Rahen, Custodian of the Sun Mountain, has a reputation of a great ruler. As befits a monarch of his caliber and stature, the ruler was graced by Surael to be a parent of several capable children and many who show great promise as future rulers. As both the children themselves and our government officials are aware, however, only one can inherit and there is little in the way of consolation prizes. Our Viziers are adamant that it is now high time for the monarch to name a favorite among the possible successors. This is unlikely to end the competition for his favor, but it will hopefully stop the worst succession struggles that might follow if the ruler should meet their maker without having picked a clear successor.
