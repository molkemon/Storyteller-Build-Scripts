Bulwar is a land plagued by darkness. gnolls, harpies, and goblins swarm upon their lands seeking to claim their haven for their own, much as the Centaurs once did to them. It is no wonder then that this struggle takes form in their religious doctrine. Those destined to fight in this struggle vary between the sects of their faiths, but in their darkness they failed to perceive the truth; the Sun shines upon all who walk upon the world. Its warmth cares nothing for your race, it does not even care for your devotion, for Taqoshur is benevolent towards all. Darkness is not a malevolent force bent upon this world, but the natural chaotic state that Taqoshur allows us to thrive away from. He gives freely all the gifts this world needs to create and know joy, and the Yudunyovi under him help guide us to that end. He is not so cruel as to choose a specific people for this gift of life, and it is our duty to ensure all know this.
