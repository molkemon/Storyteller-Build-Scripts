Eyes are the window to one's soul, but what happens when that window closes? The details of the world are lost to me; I can no longer see the beauty, and I cannot even recognize my own people. I may not be able to see as well, but I can still hear the people talk behind me, of all that goes on in the court that I miss. The army does not even bother trying to get my advice, as they know I will fail to understand the strategic situation of the battlefields. Should I seek to fix them, or should I seek to rest them and allow a new ruler to take up my place?
