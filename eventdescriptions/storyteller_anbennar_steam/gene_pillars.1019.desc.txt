The depths of a Gene Pillar are troves of valuable relics, but most are doomed to remain sealed within. The largest and most useful artifacts are dangerous to extract, often protected by traps and defended by the pillar's creators, and moving them through the hostile hinuifauna is an even more insurmountable challenge. However, this pillar is situated within a stone's throw of our capital, nullifying the transportation related difficulties. Though it would still be dangerous, major extractions might be a reasonable possibility.
