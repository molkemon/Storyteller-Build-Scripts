The past is something much looked back to in Gerudia; nostalgia for better times, when they were feared across the seas of Halann, when every coastline could face their Reavers and entire realms forged in their image from their weakness. Their cold and meagre lands forged strength, and rewarded their ancestors with Ebonsteel. This mythical metal, at first a mundane black with the faintest of purple sheen, was reforged and awoken with the power of the Skaldic songs. Many of their greatest heroes wielded such weapons and armor of Ebonsteel, yet even in their time the secrets of forging Ebonsteel were almost gone. Once the forges of Urviksten and Blodfjell ran red and full of song, yet their ancestors lost their greatest gift through the strife and shame. The art of forging Ebonsteel, and unlocking its true power, has been forgotten.
