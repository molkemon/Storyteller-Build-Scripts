When you are riding a beast that can trample a small army or gore a troll on its horn, it is only logical to develop a strong bond with your mount. The Rhino Riders have taken this notion to heart, opting to let the steed choose its riders and developing a near-religious reverence for these animals. These traditions are quaint, but when the company needs their services, we cannot wait for a rhinoceros to make up its mind.
