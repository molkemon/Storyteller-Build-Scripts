Gather closely, little fledglings, and listen to my tale. In a queendom of wings and song, the people spent their lives content and secure in the knowledge that their queen cared deeply for them. For their winged emissaries could be found across the land, from the most prestigious stage of the royal capital to the dusty market squares of the loneliest hamlets. And wherever they went, their songs went with them. Songs of love, songs of devotion, songs of courage, songs of hope, they all resounded across the hills and dales, the peaks and plains of the queendom. Each song touched the minds and hearts of the people who listened to their melodious harmonies. Each song bound the people of this queendom ever closer together in joyful celebration of the fundamental connection between all who think and feel and share this world. And the wind carried the thousand thousand melodies to all corners of the world, to all the many peoples beyond the queendom. And the world listened and rejoiced, gladly taking the songs into its heart so they may never be lost, never forgotten. Now, who among you can tell me what queendom I am speaking of? Hmm, what did you say? Yes, you are right. It is of course our beloved Harpylen, the land where the wind itself is full of song and joy. Aren't we blessed that we all can join in this grand symphony, adding our voices to the great song of songs? And in this way, no matter how long or short our lives will be, we will live on in the eternal song resounding within the world's memory.
