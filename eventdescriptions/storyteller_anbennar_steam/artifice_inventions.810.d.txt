One of the greatest issues in Bulwar is water. Water is life, when the land is as dry as ours. Food may feed a man, but this food could never grow if it did not receive an apt amount of water beforehand. This, as it happens, is the most limiting factor restricting the growth of the population. Though we are blessed by Surael with two great rivers, the further one moves away from a water source, the more effort is required to keep crops well irrigated. The natural answer to this dilemma is to make irrigation easier. If water can be sent further from its source for the same effort, then crops can be grown farther afield than ever before. This, however, was easier said than done. At least until now. The Kurešimi have devised a machine that can propel water at a velocity far higher than traditional solutions, allowing it to be pumped much farther, reaching far more fields. Watering crops has never been so easy.
