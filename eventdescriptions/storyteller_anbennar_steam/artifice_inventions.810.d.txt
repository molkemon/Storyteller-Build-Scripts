What is one of the greatest issues in Bulwar? Water. Water is life, when land is as dry as ours. Food may feed a man, but this food could never grow if it didn't receive an apt amount of water. This, as it happens, is the worst limiting factor towards the growth of our population. And though we are blessed by Surael with two great rivers, the further one moves away from a water source, the effort required to keep crops well irrigated grows exponentially.  The natural answer to this question is to make irrigation easier. If water can be sent further for the same effort, then far more land can host crops than ever before. This is easier said than done. But now, it has indeed been done. The Mechanists have devised a machine that can expel water at a velocity far higher than traditional sources, allowing it to reach much farther from the same place. Watering crops has never been so easy.
