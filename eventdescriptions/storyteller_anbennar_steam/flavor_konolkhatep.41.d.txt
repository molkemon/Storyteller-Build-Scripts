O Horutep, King of Kings. There are many kings across the world, from Anbennar to the farthest regions of the storied east. These kings rule varied subjects and impossibly different lands, yet there is a similarity in what happens when each of these kings first dons his crown. Each king, or at least the ones worth mentioning, O Horutep, knows at that moment that he has been given a responsibility beyond the material, and that each of his subjects is like his own child. You are King of Kings, O Horutep. This means that you are a grandfather to all the people of the world; they may shelter forever under your might and bask forever in your glory. What better fate could there be than that?
