We have pieced together enough information on another god. When Death entered the world at the end of the Era of Forging, Lorgram and his twin sister suddenly appeared. Lorgram began to ensure that the souls of the dead were taken into Dagrin's halls and organized into areas based on their skills and virtues in life. Here in the deeper reaches, he acts as keeper and custodian, ensuring that the dead are rewarded or punished accordingly.
