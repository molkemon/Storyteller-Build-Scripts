We have traveled far and seen many worthy combatants; but only one tribe holds their duels in as high regard as we do. the Soruinic. They boast bountiful feasts, massive statues dedicated to past victors, and crowds composed of their entire tribe. We are impressed. However, these duels seem neither to be for culling the weak, nor a simple sparring match. Upon inquiry, we have found that these duels are called tournaments, and are hosted to find the best fighter in their tribe, yes, but also simply to celebrate. The Soruinics live to be remembered, so that when their deity eventually engulfs and consumes us all, they will still live on in memory. We see no need for this, as to have lived for as long as one does is memory enough; but there is something truly impressive about this extravagant spectacle… for this grand organization of duels, and these feasts do keep a warrior's spirit ready to fight.
