Reports have reached us of a feral Vampire acting within the capital. Though such monsters are not unheard of, vampiric rumours swirling amongst our people shall surely draw unwanted attention to our ruler and other vampires in our country. As such, the Vampire estate has demanded the ruler lend state assistance in hunting down and destroying this errant predator. The tone of these requests began politely, but have recently escalated into allbutcommands; as if they have forgotten just what role our ruler plays within our realm. Indeed, this is a traditional responsibility of the Vampire estate. Our ruler could intercede, however; and even overrule them if necessary.
