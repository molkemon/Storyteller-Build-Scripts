Questions abound as to the purpose of this structure. Legends suggest that those who occupied these towers used them for dread magics and forbidden studies. Malaise has started creeping over the expedition, as we ask what they were studying here. The collection of relics and notes are starting to lead to a dark conclusion. You have finally located what you believe to be a laboratory complex, and your team readies to breach the sealed doorway. Opening the door leads to involuntary sounds of distress among the team, as enclosed in glass tanks around the room float what are clearly kobolds in various stages of their lifecycle development. Steeling their nerves, the team ventures in to get a better look at what is inside. This is a room of nightmares. The forms in the tanks are not just varied in cycle of life; some have major deformities or damage as if some foul magic has twisted them and sculpted their forms into unnatural states. It is clear what they were studying now: the power of draconic gene magic. Your people are familiar with the potential of this power, having made progress in understanding it and furthering the abilities of your race. These experiments, however, were obviously conducted on unwilling subjects, and without any consideration to what harm it could cause. This is an evil place, and you dread what may be found deeper in the heart of the pillar.
