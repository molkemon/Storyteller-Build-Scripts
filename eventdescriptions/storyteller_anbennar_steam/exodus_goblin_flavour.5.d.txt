When we came upon this land, all of its inhabitants were subject to the tiresome elves and their pathetic empires. They called themselves saviours, yet under their mandate, all were forced to be subordinate to their sun, leaving many humans that believed in the old ways at the mercy of these heroes. We however are different; we care not for what sun you worship, not whether you believe you are a creature of darkness or the old ways of the Surael. All is well so long as you believe our creed, our state. So long as you believe in Aqatbar.
