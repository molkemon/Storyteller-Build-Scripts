They crept through the silent jungle, coming upon a lonely, dilapidated shrine. The soldiers crawled through the underbrush, surrounding the structure.  It had all led here. The knife marked by a local blacksmith, the inns on the road, and local reports of an odd magician. This was where the assassin’s master lived.  The silence was broken by the sound of cracking wood as the door caved in. Soldiers rushed through the building, clearing every room, until one found a halfling in priestly garments. The soldier was blasted backwards with a shout of "Vengeance.", but more swarmed into the room and wrestled the priest to the ground.
