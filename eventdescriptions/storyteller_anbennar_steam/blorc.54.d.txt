As the chieftain brought down their axe, splitting the skull of our lord wide open, a silence went through the crowd. Soon the silence broke into cheering coming from their side of the crowd, as the chief claimed the regalia of our lord for themselves and the personal guard began swearing their loyalty to the new lord. As the new lord began marching towards the capital, with the mutilated head of the old lord as proof, a new era for our government ushered in.
