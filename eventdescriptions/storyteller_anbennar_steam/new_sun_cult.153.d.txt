Rising in the ranks of an elven military is not as easy as in the armies of other races. When one's superiors live for upwards of four centuries, there are not many opportunities arising for advancement, no matter how skilled or proven one may be. The Exemplars in Aelantier, however, provide a unique solution to this problem for many of these young officers. They wish to go and join up with them in their quest to bring the Light of Surael to the ruins of Aelantier. This may prove disheartening for the human contingents, however, seeing their idolized elven officers abandon them.
