The glory alone of killing a fully grown wyvern would be enough for many adventurers to answer the call, but what also attracts these monster hunters is its vast abundance of materials. A party managed to track down the nest last night, and brought down a wyvern before it could take to the skies and flee. Now, however, it appears that the adventurers have been selling off useful pieces of the beast, such as its scales and poisons. While they are within their right to do so, it is also within our right to tax them for such sales, if we wish.
