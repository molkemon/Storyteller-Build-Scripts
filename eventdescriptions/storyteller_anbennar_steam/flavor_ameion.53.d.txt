Mere weeks after Thirendires was imprisoned for suspicion of treason against the Republic of Ameion, his supporters have laid siege to the prisons and allowed him to walk out of its walls. To many in the Synkletos, some of the more lurid rumours surrounding Thirendires have been proven true; utilising the power they have built up in local government over the years, Laskaris' supporters have now taken up armed resistance to the rule, demanding Thirendires be instated as their ruler.
