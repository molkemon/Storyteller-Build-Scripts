Using the narrow entrance corridor as a chokepoint, the feral goblins' charge was quickly broken upon the expedition's shields and spears with few casualties from the defenders. As the remaining goblins turn tail and flee, it becomes apparent that the goblins' fallen brethren have brought quite a haul. Most of them were kitted in crudely modified armor and wielding weapons sourced from inside the forge; even after so many years of misuse, these pieces are still finely crafted and would fetch a high price from collectors.
