The "waterfall" was just a slope, but the relief does not last long. Violent water splashes reach the crew from all sides. A few torchlights extinguish. Some oars break on the rapid's rocks. But we remain focused through this adversity. Determined and organized we row with as much grace as the situation allows for. We manage to control our track and speed. Sweaty and panting, we finally arrive at the shore. The flow turns into a pool on a large circular metal platform. In front of us there is… something weirder.
