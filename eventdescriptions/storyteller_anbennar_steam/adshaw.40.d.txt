It seems that even the reclusive kobolds in our lands have been impacted by the rush of migrants. Kobolds from all over the Dragon Coast have flocked to Axzo Vurte, turning it from a remote collection of hovels into a sprawling city within less than a decade, and yet only a fraction can be seen from the surface. Beneath their feet are countless miles of twisting caverns and caves where they make their homes and workshops, as well as from which they pull gemstones to grow their hoard and pay taxes with. It is effectively impossible for us to ever know exactly how many kobolds call the city home, which they have renamed to Axzozex and proclaimed a secondary homeland of the kobold tribes.
