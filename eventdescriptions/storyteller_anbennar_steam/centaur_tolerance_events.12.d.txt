One of our minor nobles has met their untimely end at the hands, and hooves, of one of the centaurs that resides in our controlled lands. Having been apprehended after the murder, the centaur claims that the deceased noble attempted to ride them. Apparently, this is greatly offensive to the centaur culture, and the centaur claims that the immediate and brutal killing of the minor noble was the only reasonable response. How shall we handle the situation? The centaur is probably right; they should be free from being harassed like this, but siding with the opinions of these horsemen over our own nobles certainly would not look good for our international standing.
