We have failed.  The Maw crumbles, despite our efforts.  The Runelink grows weaker, and thus the Father's Will has been defied, a treason of the highest order. Yet all is not lost; the Runelink remains, though weakened, and thus may be restored.  Thus, the Father, in His wisdom, in His mercy, shall spare us, though this mercy shall not last eternal. The Father gives His children many gifts; the gift of life, the gift of sweat, the gift of blood, the gift of the holy rune. The gift of dying in His holy name.  These gifts are given by the Father freely, but may just as easily be taken away.  The Runelink must be maintained.  The Maw must be restored.  So it is, and so it shall be.
