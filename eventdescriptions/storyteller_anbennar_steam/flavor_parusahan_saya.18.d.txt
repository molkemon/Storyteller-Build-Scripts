At long last, the Vizierate is broken on the spears of our soldiers, and resistance within the realm has been annihilated. With the disgusting betrayal of the Vizier still fresh in the mind of the monarch, it is clear that the office no longer serves its intended purpose. It must be removed. New councils are drawn up, each handpicked by the monarch and their trusted advisors. These loyal advisors will serve in the same capacity as the Vizierate, providing guidance and assistance in times of need, but without the independence that colored the corruption of the previous incarnation. With control over our right hand finally restored, we are truly a united Raj, a sight unseen since the dawn of Harimar himself. Is there truly any doubt of our legacy now?
