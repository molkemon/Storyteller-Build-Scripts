After surviving the attempt to harm their physical integrity, the monarch has started to feel consumed by what they may call caution, whilst others may refer to it using less pleasant words. They have started to avoid social events, leaving the rest of the council alone to govern the whole nation. Their flagrant lack of responsibility has raised many suspicions among the court. However, what has greatly deteriorated public opinion is the strange behavior they have been exhibiting lately, their eyes red and wide open in the middle of the night, muttering in darkness that they shall never leave the throne, asking someone to come to them even as a shadow, even as a dream.
