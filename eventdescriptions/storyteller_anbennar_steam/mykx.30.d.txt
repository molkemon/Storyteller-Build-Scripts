Years ago, we were only exiles from a distant land, the ones that when we escaped, it was said that they took weeks to notice our absence. Then she, Mykx herself, guided us to a new land. When we arrived, we were nothing, just vultures picking apart the weak new states of the region and stealing the few trinkets the locals had. But now? We thrive. We are the rulers of the seas, those who ride the waves of hell and bring ruin to the weak. The mighty Cannorians bow to our demands and buy our drugs happily, the Bulwarians curse our name and fear our ships, and none on this planet doesn’t recognize our flag and tremble. Even the demons recognize our position. They no longer appear so arrogant and detached from our offerings. They know now the gifts we can give and the power we have. It’s even whispered that some demons fear us. We are loyal to the Xhaz; we are his ultimate followers, they have been honorable to us, repaying our sacrifices, and as Mykx always said.
