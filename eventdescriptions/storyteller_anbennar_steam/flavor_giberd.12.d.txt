A hurried messenger barges through the doors of Giberd’s debate hall, interrupting the heated argument within an instant. Agitated eyes look upon him impatiently as he unfurls a long scroll upon which an Esmaraine seal has been placed. The eyes of hundreds of gnomes upon him, their incensed voices ring out in rage as the contents of the letter are slowly read out. “By the decree of the monarch, Esmaraine officially challenges the electorship bid of Giberd, contesting that they have claim to being the true representative of the Esmari and imperial gnomes via right of the former grand dukedom. Esmaraine demands that Giberd immediately drop their bid for electorship, so that a worthy elector can be chosen posthaste.”
