At the funeral of the village elder, mourners noticed an unexpected occurrence – far more people attended the service than were officially invited. This, in itself, is not unusual, as funerals often serve as social gatherings for communities, allowing people to mourn together. However, what stunned everyone was the sheer number of guests who came to pay their respects. A procession of unfamiliar faces graced the event, many clad in clothing styles that belonged to decades, and sometimes even centuries, past. Only a few days later did the realization dawn upon the people. Ancestral spirits from across the lands had arrived to honor their departed elder. Generations upon generations of ancestors had gathered to be present. Such an unprecedented visit from our forebears has left the people feeling emboldened and comforted. The knowledge that their ancestors continue to watch over them brings solace and reassurance.
