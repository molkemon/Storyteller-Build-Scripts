A Diamond is formed only when the whole weight of the world rests upon it; it must be capable of withstanding the greatest of pressure and heat, or it will splinter into nothing. When we opened the gates of our hold, we could afford to be no different. Our nation has not been easy to govern, but with each revolt, each complication, each pressure, we have not broken: no, these conflicts have strengthened us, refined us, polished us to a brilliant shine. We have faced the reactionaries, the backward-looking pretenders, and the clans and lapidaries demanding domination over our country. We have assimilated dwarves from across the land, moderated the right to conflict within government halls, and determined national policies of land and conquest. We have, throughout all of this, maintained stability, increased our control, and destroyed every threat to our province. By centralizing authority, we have unified and united the spirit of our nation. Our chamber of government, the "Gleaming Council," has often failed to live up to its name. It has been a palace of schemes, plots, backstabbing, corruption, bribery, and incompetence: but it shall be so no longer. The Council will gleam as a place of unity and cohesion, where all parts of Diamond society work together under our direct supervision. Through the will of our lord, assented to by the Council, the entire nation shall glow. We shine with luster, and the world will envy the Diamond Dwarves.
