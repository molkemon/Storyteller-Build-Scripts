The Golden Dome of Stanyrhrada is a large structure built by the ruinborn probably from Precursor artifacts, it's not as if they're smart enough to do it themselves and is believed to bring good harvests. What's more important, however, is that it's made of gold. Lots of gold. Our ruler has obviously laid claim to such a treasure, and is fielding some very attractive offers from international collectors. The locals are usually too afraid to contest our plunder, but it seems they're particularly attached to this thing. Some militia called "The Knights of the Dome", supposedly a "centuriesold sacred order", has begged governors to let the site be. We naturally dismissed their pleas, but now they've launched a revolt, swearing to drive us out. We must crush this rabble, and let them die knowing they were too weak to guard their precious gold.
