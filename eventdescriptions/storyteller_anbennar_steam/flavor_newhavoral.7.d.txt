The Golden Dome of Stanyrhrada is a large structure built by the ruinborn—probably from Precursor artifacts, it is not as if they are smart enough to do it themselves—and is believed to bring good harvests. What is more important, however, is that it is made of gold. Lots of gold. The ruler has obviously laid claim to such a treasure, and is fielding some very attractive offers from international collectors. The locals are usually too afraid to contest their plunder, but it seems they are particularly attached to this thing. Some militia called "The Knights of the Dome", supposedly a "centuries-old sacred order", has begged local governors to let the site be. We naturally dismissed their pleas, but now they have launched a revolt, swearing to drive us out. We must crush this rabble, and let them die knowing they were too weak to guard their precious gold.
