1st Clause: All Princes, Mayors, and leaders of other subdivisions within the Empire of Anbennar, regardless of rank or electorship, are hereby forbidden from constructing seagoing vessels capable of waging war.
2nd Clause: The government of Neckcliffe is exempt from the restrictions placed by the first clause.
3rd Clause: The government of Neckcliffe is obliged to maintain a fleet capable of protecting the Empire of Anbennar from any naval incursions.
4th Clause: The government of Neckcliffe is obliged to protect all waters north of The Pearls from any forms of piracy.
5th Clause: The government of Neckcliffe is obliged to provide transportation for Imperial armies requiring crossing of all waters north of The Pearls.
6th Clause: The government of Neckcliffe is given the right to intervene militarily should any princes violate the terms set within the 1st clause.
