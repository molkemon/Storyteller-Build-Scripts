When word from the explorers who first saw the Graxarr Temple from the sea reached the government in Ilzin Mykx, they were ecstatic. A large Precursor structure still standing and untouched? The only thought that ran through the advisors in charge was gold. Tales, legends, and memories of entire packs killing each other or being raised to the apex of power thanks to finding one Precursor Relic in a raid or an archeological dig. A small number of voices said that yes, gold was possible, at selling those relics, what about power? With the new technological advances and magical discoveries, a Precursor Relic could be the key to unimaginable power, leading to gold. What they all agreed on at least was that they needed to organize an expedition to this Temple and fast.
