Khagor could not explain why he now kneeled before the prince, taking their offered hand. He could not explain the sensation that overtook him as something changed within. And he could not explain why none of this mattered. But, for a moment, there was the briefest doubt. The smallest question that echoed in his mind. Then, Khagor looked up at his patron, his savior, his everything. They smiled upon him, and he knew, all was well.
