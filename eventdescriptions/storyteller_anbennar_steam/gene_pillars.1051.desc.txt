As we enter a new chamber of the Gene Pillar, one of our numbers is almost immediately swallowed by a great moving mass as it sloshes down onto the ground. This horrible abomination of moving muscles and sinew, warped into a horrible congealing form, continues its assault on us, cornering us in short order. We can still see the remains of a hinuibird protruding out of the mass; it gently twitches as its nerves are taken over and connected to the mass before us. Is this our fate?
