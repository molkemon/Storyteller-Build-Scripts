In an unexpected turn of events, merchants from abroad have been flocking to a province, going out of their way to include a stretch of innocuous woodland in their itinerary. The reason? Kobolds. A Trivu, a tribe or clan of kobolds, has been selling a rather unconventional commodity. Fireflies. Widely known as ingenious inventors, albeit often reckless, these kobolds have found a way to breed the luminescent bugs in captivity, and are now selling flameless lanterns. These light sources are cheaper and last longer than conventional counterparts, and can be refueled by adding more of the leaves that the bugs survive on, allowing one lantern to last for many months.
