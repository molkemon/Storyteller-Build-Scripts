The Ikatara of Khugra has been plagued by internal turmoil since its independence from the Karasskan Empire a few decades ago. This instability has led to the formation of factions within the realm that view Khugran independence as a failed experiment and seek to secure their future by submitting to a greater power that can protect them. We have been contacted by one such faction, which promises to hand over the reins of the state to us if they emerge victorious from the conflict. However, we are well aware that some of our neighbors have received similar promises from other factions. The various Khugran factions are awaiting the outcome of the war between Asarta and Karassk before making any moves. We hope our allied faction will prevail once this standoff concludes. Nevertheless, our armies stand ready to seize at least the border regions of the weakened Ikatara should it surrender to one of our rivals instead.
