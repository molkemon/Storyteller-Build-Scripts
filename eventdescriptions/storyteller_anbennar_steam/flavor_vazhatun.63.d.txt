A great shock ran through DûrVazhatun today. One of the astronomers has committed the particularly grisly murder of one of their friends. Using a knife they gouged out the victim's eyes, skinned the corpse, and opened the victim's stomach, skull, and bladder. When pressed for details, the murderer claims to have seen something behind the victim's back, behind the victim's eyes, beneath the victim's skin. "They are hiding everywhere, behind every corner, inside every shadow. All I wanted was to break their facade and reveal them. They're right here, in this room, why can't you see them? WHY CAN'T ANY OF YOU SEE THEM." Needless to say, the astronomer seems to have been afflicted with the same malady that has struck some of the other astronomers. It would be best if the public didn't learn about the details of this murder, which makes it even more unfortunate that it involved such a notable scholar.
