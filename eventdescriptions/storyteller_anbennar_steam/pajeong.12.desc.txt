The burghers have become more bold, and their requests more audacious. They now desire total control over all trade conducted within Danggun as well as the royal mint, planning to centralize their efforts within the port city of Onman. Giving them final say over all monetary policy will of course increase their influence over the realm significantly, but they promise that the commercial interests of all will be taken into account when key economic decisions are made. Of course, the commercial interests of some will be taken into account more so than others.
