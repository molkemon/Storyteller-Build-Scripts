After the capture of Hehodovar, most of our army went in search of valuables left behind. One of these goblins found an empty office containing a mysterious seal, thinking it valuable, he took it as loot. A few strange reports, however, soon brought it to the attention of our lord. The seal seems to have some strange power that makes decrees or orders all but impossible to disobey. We must watch it closely and make sure that it cannot be used by the wrong goblin.
