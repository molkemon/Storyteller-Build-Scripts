For thousands of years, the Wishcrafters of Mount Lazzaward have dedicated themselves to the creation of weapons and tools made through Inscription, one of the four Bulwari schools of magic. Inscription allows one to create a magic item by carving a very complex spell on the surface of said item, and to create high-quality objects, they need resources. tin, copper, iron, lapis powder, and more, that the Wishcrafters turn into Anniwan and Sihariš, two alloys essential to their craft. The hills surrounding the sacred mountain are filled with resources that they can mine, and satisfy the Wishcrafter’s demands.
