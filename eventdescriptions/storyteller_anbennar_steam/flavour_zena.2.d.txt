A letter has come to your court, from a foreign ruler. You open it, and read. “It is with a heavy heart that I write to you, ruler. I know that your wise words stayed the hand of Kuiika at the gates of Bivuzena. I appeal to you in the hope that wisdom will prevail again. The balance of power in Horashesh has been broken. Do you believe Kuiika will stop their brutal expansion at the gates of Bivuzena, or will their eyes inevitably turn north? Do you believe Zuvavim will allow the wealth of the Dao Nako trade to flow from the sea inland, or will they hoard their wealth to themselves? I say to you, down with the Medawor. We will stand with you against the Kheteratan Empire, if you join with us to restore our borders to their rightful state. I implore you, let us form an alliance, and begin walking on the road to the Horashesh we deserve to live in.” How shall you respond?
