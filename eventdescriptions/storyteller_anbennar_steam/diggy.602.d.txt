In a stunning display of ingenuity and ambition, the Norkers of that province have managed to derail a supply train traveling along the area's rails. The crew was able to slip away while the Goblinoids ransacked the cargo, a shipment of food, pay, and munitions, bound for one of our military depots. While the cost in material is noteworthy, and the repairs will take some time, the greater concern is that this tribe may become even more aggressive and ambitious if given the chance to capitalize on their newfound resources.
