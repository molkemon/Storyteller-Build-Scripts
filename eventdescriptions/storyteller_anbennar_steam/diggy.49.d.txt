Expanding a hold is an involved affair, which can range from turning existing caverns into livable rooms and halls to carving out entire new sections from bare rock. Sometimes, it is even a matter of reclaiming collapsed sections of the hold that were lost a long time ago. Meanwhile, we have to take into account what type of rock we dig through, as well as maintaining the structural integrity of the whole hold. Of course, this is only more difficult and expensive if done outside of our capital.
