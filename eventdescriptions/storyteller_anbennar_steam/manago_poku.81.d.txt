The days of our wandering are still in the memory banks of most Mechanim. And yet, those memory banks are now filled with other things too: carving a home in the capital and its surroundings, creating new mechanim with whom to share the years ahead, all the new problems and new possibilities that becoming the nation has offered. Perhaps, some day, our people will not know why they tread in a certain way, or take certain paths through the jungle, or act for certain reasons in the presence of certain stars. Perhaps, when they wonder, a long-forgotten memory bank will be tapped for an answer. But even unknowing, the traditions we forged along our journey will shape us forevermore.
