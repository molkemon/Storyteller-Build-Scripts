Whilst drinking is a beloved pastime for many of the children of Bulwar, loosening tongues, strengthening friendships and bringing merriment, at least in the best case. In the city’s fringes and hidden corners, people have taken to distilling their own potent brews, capable of addling a man's senses utterly with but a few glasses or even potentially poisoning them with its sheer potency… And less than desirable conditions in which this is done. This coincides with some of our realm's more radical thinkers postulating on the potential dangers of drink to a man's health and thoughts, turning mild mannered thinkers into zealous and passionate brutes. Either way, the choice falls to the ruler on how to handle this contentious issue.
