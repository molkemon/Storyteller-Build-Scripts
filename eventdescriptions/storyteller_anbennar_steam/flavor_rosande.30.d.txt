The carriage tilts and a colossal figure emerges unto the courtyard of the palace. With a frame and height surpassing that of the average ork, Kylian síl Rósande is an anomaly among both humanity and the nobility. Unlike the wretched beasts called orks and despite his size, Kylian síl Rósande, with his chiseled face, luscious golden hair and sapphire blue eyes, is unmistakably human. The delicate marble tile flooring cracks as Kylian síl Rósande walks down the hallway, past adoring nobility and servants starstruck by his magnificence. Yet despite having returned from his magical studies to serve his country at its time of need, he brings with him a meager retinue of seventeen concubines, five ork slaves, and eleven homunculi. Indeed his humility is widely known and praised, as everybody knows Kylian síl Rósande does not brag, preferring instead to demonstrate his magical prowess on his orkish slaves. On many occasions he has incinerated or vaporized orks from his retinue, indulging the request of lowly peasants wishing to see magic for the first time. He arrives in the throne room and sits upon two of his orkish slaves, as the nobles bicker around him for the privilege of crowning their new king. The discussion continues for minutes and the orks beneath Kylian síl Rósande begin to tremble. As no one is worthy of crowning me, I shall crown myself. Kylian síl Rósande walks up to the throne and conducts an impromptu coronation, much to the pleasant surprise of the onlookers.
