The tunnels and caverns of the Serpentspine have always been hostile to anyone who traversed them, yet the constrained spaces and desolate rock are a good whetstone for our strength and fury. But never have we found a place where the land itself seemed to oppose our war as keenly as when taking Gor Bûrad. We felt the heat as soon as we approached. Much of the magma from the volcano formed large streams on the interior and exterior of the hold, causing traversal to be particularly difficult yet not impossible. But only for a moment. Our fury and ferocity burn hotter than even molten rock; the hold could not hold us off forever. And the lessons we learned in taking it shall ensure that all the others fall quicker as well.
