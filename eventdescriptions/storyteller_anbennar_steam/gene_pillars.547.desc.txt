We finally stand at the peak of the pillar. Everything else in the pillar seems to somehow connect back to this singular room; the tiny tunnels which carry air, the overall layout of the passages, even the origin point for some of the root growth. Most of the systems in here appear to be nonfunctional, but what is functional seems to be diagnosing the problems with the tower. Some of them however also still look to be being fed data on experiments many many centuries after they were abandoned. The robustness of the systems here is definitely something to look into.
