A feast in Elynaile to celebrate the reconquest of the Lake Grove. However, Farwen Elyn chokes on an olive of all things, and Cinrthir, if he is alive, has little desire left to rule. With his oldest daughter dead and his grove retaken, he retires, putting a council in place until the fate of the Salla can be decided. Uncertainty remains as to what happens next, since Arfil is nominally next in line, but a personal union is a rare thing.
