While Kheterata may be facing perhaps its darkest hour, the light of our faith yet shines bright, a beacon for those who still dream of its legacy. Those true sons and daughters of Elikhet, who chafe under the occupation of the Sorrow, see in us a future for the Elikhetist faith. They flock to our banner. exile and escapee, noble and commoner, young and old, soldier and civilian. In return for our steadfastness, the Sorrow provides her greatest bounty.
