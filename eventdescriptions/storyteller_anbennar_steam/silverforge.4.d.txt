Silverforge has invested greatly in Esmari trade in an attempt to obtain the necessary resources to complete their hold, even expanding their territory to directly control many local trade centers. In some regards, we have managed to profit from this as an important trading city ourselves. Still, we are very much on the outside looking in, and we would be better off integrating more fully into this new market.  This opportunity seems to have arrived along with delegates from the unfinished hold. They propose a large investment in our markets to ensure their growth and integration with their local hegemony. We would still pay taxes to the Emperor as per our role as a Free City, but in exchange they ask that we come to their aid in their military endeavors.
