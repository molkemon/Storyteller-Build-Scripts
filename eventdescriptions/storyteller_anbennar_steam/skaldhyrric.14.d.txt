Many are the monsters of Aelantier never seen before, but few match the fearsome Wendigo. Haunting the forests and dark places, Wendigos delight in hunting and consuming the flesh of folk. It is even said that the Wendigo are born from those who are forced to cannibalism out of desperation, twisted into unrecognizable monstrosities by the lingering magical corruption of the Day of Ashen Skies. Still, these monsters can be slain, and who better than Gerudians who do not balk and groatuesquery? And with the cadaver collectors eliminated, our new homesteads in this land can thrive.
