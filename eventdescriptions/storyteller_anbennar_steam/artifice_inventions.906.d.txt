The Scrap Mech. A piloted suit of scrap steel with a magically heated coal furnace to run the engine, only a goblin could have designed such a pile of junk and have it work so reliably. This mech will allow a trained goblin to walk unhindered into battle, protected by the crude amalgamation of steel and other scavenged metal plates and wielding massive, interchangeable, hand attachments such as large saw blades, sharp claws, hammers, anything you can think of. The sheer momentum of one of these babies charging into the enemy lines, flames spewing out the front, smoke belching out the back, gleeful goblin cackling ringing from the top, will be enough to break any formation.
