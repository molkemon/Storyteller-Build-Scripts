Despite the monopoly the Silcalas family holds on the office of ruler, the nation has seen a current of republican ideals recently. Republicans have viewed this as a sign that our nation may very well be on its way towards their goal of a fullfledged republic, with some even going as far as praising a prince for permitting this shift. Regardless of how much truth there is to this, our recent political maneuvering keeps the dream of the Republic alive.
