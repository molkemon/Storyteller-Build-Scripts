As our land returns to normal, it seems there will soon be a day where not even a trace of the divine attack on our home remains. Even as people repair what has been ruined and mourn the ones who died, a great sense of camaraderie and triumph has settled on our people. Working together, we have survived an attack from the gods themselves. Field by field, house by house, we worked together to combat the endless deluge of blood. The monarch has announced this day as a national holiday: The day that we won a war against a war god.
