Despite how it may appear on the map, my domain isn't as united as it may seem. Many of the tribes that willingly joined Rayaz remain very powerful in their own lands. Their participation under my rule is held solely by their good faith and the belief in the prophecy. This delicate state of affairs is now threatened by my brother, Katyradz, betraying our alliance and choosing a path of his own. He is a part of the prophecy as well, and that makes him a viable option to replace me and claim my mantle as the bringer of the Final Empire. This fact alone is enough to cause trouble, with the more opportunistic tribes starting to renegotiate my relationship with them. And now, the chieftains of Lata and Alashkatsa came before me to do just that. Their terms are to give them autonomy, or risk their secession.
