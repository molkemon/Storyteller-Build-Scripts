The party finds a large metal statue standing guard. The statue does not move or respond. Investigating, the party finds a tear core in its chest. It looks like it was broken, hastily repaired, then broken once more. One member touches the core, and the entire party is assaulted by a vision. A man stands in the same room that the party is in, the same young man the party had encountered before in the hallway. He wields a long spear, glowing with engravings, and a large winged beast stays by his side. Both their eyes are focused on the threat in front of them, the great metal guard with four arms, each equipped with a different weapon. The fighters engage. The guard swings each arm in quick succession, a whirling flurry of weapons. The man twirls his spear, deflecting each swing with perfect timing. The engravings glow greater, and the man moves faster, as if guided by an unknown force. The guard stumbles back after a perfect parry sends a large clang reverberating throughout the room. Sensing an opportunity, the beast leaps forward, biting and ripping off one of the guard’s arms. The man does not let up on the assault, and he rushes towards the guard. His spear aims true and it pierces straight through the guard’s body, right where its core lies. As the guard falls, the vision ends. The party, still disoriented from the sudden memories of the past, do not notice the statue’s core flickering with light. A rush of spirit energy floods the room, and the three-armed guard begins to whir with life once more.
