Lining the tunnel and the top of the dam, the expedition watches as the engineers secure the last of the explosives on the debris in the four gates. As the lead engineer counts down to detonation, the expedition retreats into the tunnel for safety. "3… 2… 1… Detonate." The sound of the explosion booms through the tunnel, echoing off the curved walls countless times and shaking dust loose from the ceiling. Once the shaking has stopped, the expedition peers tentatively over the edge and into the cavern. The dam is a little blackened, but still intact. More importantly, the debris is gone and water once again flows through the riverbed.
