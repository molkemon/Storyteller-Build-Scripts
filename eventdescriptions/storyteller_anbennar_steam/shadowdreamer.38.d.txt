The battle grinds on as steel meets claw in a storm of motion, the cavern thick with blood and heat. Orc and beast alike fall in droves, yet neither side yields. There is no retreat for them. Xazor tears through their host with relentless force, but she bleeds. Each strike draws more of that dark ichor. This beast, which once appeared unassailable, is now finally within their grasp. Their lines hold, landing blow after blow, yet Xazor still persists. The swarm begins to lose cohesion, their attacks breaking into scattered desperation as their master begins to falter. And then, at last the moment comes. A final surge. A final strike. Xazor tumbles to the ground with a thunderous crash, and in an instant the echoes that filled the pit are gone, replaced by the cheers of orks. As the dust settles the remaining warriors watch as the minions unravel, their twisted forms breaking apart into ash which scatters across the floor of the pit. When the last of the minions have dissipated, even Xazor’s corpse begins to fade, dissolving into a pool of thick, tainted ichor that seeps deep into the stone below. Dark veins spread across the pit, as if the stone itself drinks it in. The pit of Xazor is quiet now. Whatever once ruled these depths and watched from the dark has finally been unmade.
