Gnolls, an enterprising race without compare in scavenging and crafting, have recently proven extremely beneficial for the economy of the realm. Their involvement in a certain sector of the local economy has resulted in an increase in goods production and an uptick in local economic strength. The opinion of the non-gnoll populace is divided. Some are of course hostile to those they deem foreign and demand the ruler intervene. But others rather like the increase in profits the gnolls have helped share around.
