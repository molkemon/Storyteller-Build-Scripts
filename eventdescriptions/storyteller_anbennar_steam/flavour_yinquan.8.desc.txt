Courteous smiles. Generous application of mijiu. A few well-placed coins. The Nincìshi indisputably exist, but when they do not want to be found, they are like dust in the wind. Our efforts have not been able to accomplish the impossible, but the shadow council saw fit to get into touch with our agents after we demonstrated sufficient interest in their existence. Through our deeds and actions, we have been able to place several sympathetic voices within the council. It will do little for us now, but it makes any further efforts much easier.
