In the chaos that followed the collapse of the Hibernal Crusade, we were in dire need of protection and had to make many desperate decisions. Having a shared enemy in Eighard, we struck a deal with the Tuathak realm Raithtall, even sealing the deal with a royal marriage. Now though, with the threat of Eighard gone for good, our former allies demand that we submit to them and renounce Winter for Autumn. There is only one choice.
