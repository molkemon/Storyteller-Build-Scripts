The air of the guild hall is rich with the scent of pipe smoke, oiled leather, and burning coal. Trophyladen walls bore skulls of slain horrors; beasts with too many eyes, fangs dripping venom, and shattered horns from battles long past. A great hearth roars, casting flickering light over grizzled veterans checking their runed crossbows, while younger hunters tighten the straps of their leather armour. Maps lay sprawled across a great stone table, marked with sightings of possible quarry. What should we hunt?
