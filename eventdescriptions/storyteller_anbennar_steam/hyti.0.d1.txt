Good people, I bear the saddest of news. Nyamau Thiuivene made her final landfall. She died tonight, catching us all unprepared. Yesterday morning she was looking her usual spry self, nagging me for having reordered the escritoire, as per usual. She died doing what she loved; tinkering away in the lab. I was even able to nag her to come to bed one last time. Sorry, just a moment. She didn't listen that time either, of course. We haven't just lost our brightest mind, a teacher, a friend; Nyamau was a visionary, the herald of a bright future only she could see. She was a harpy ahead of her time. But thanks to her lifelong effort we can finally see her vision as well; we need now only to pick up her mantle. Nyamau might be dead, and her loss leaves a huge wound in everybody she was close to, but her legacy lives on.
