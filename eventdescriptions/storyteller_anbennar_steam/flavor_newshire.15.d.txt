Peregrin Kettlebrook was a young, plucky halfling entrepreneur. Second Turnwell was an upandcoming trade centre, with hundreds of Newshiremen and foreigners flocking through it every day. There was nowhere else Peregrin would rather be. As he gazed upon the streets of the town, something nagged at his foot hair, and he combed his fingers through it thoughtfully. What was this town missing? There's an armoury, a general store, an inn, halls set aside for all the estates, a blacksmith, a tailor, a leatherworker, and woodcutter, plenty of greengrocers. Where was a halfling supposed to get himself a drink around here?
