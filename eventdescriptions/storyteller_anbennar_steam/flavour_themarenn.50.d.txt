Decades ago, the twin brothers Alarin and Vincen Redcoaster took on the task of building a oneofakind waterway. Something that was unheard of during those ages. Their ingenious solutions and innovations particularly in the field of Dam construction and controlling water level proceed crucial for the project to succeed.  Despite losing his brother a few years ago and the relatively small beginings, Alarin and his company are now recognised as some of Cannor's most prolific construction companies, acquiring contracts all the way from Gerudia to Noriun. Their headquarters in Ryalanar has also been a boon for the local ship building business.
