The troops were hardpressed to keep up with the Amber Princess as she rushed into the clearing. The archfey stood at the other end, the Drag of the Waves, the Everfrost Prince, the Unthroned Queen, the Old man of the Archives, and the Gloam Knight. To see them all in the same place, and seemingly working together, was very strange. It unsettled many of the troops. Suddenly, a portal opened, and before anyone could react, the archfey began jumping through it. One by one, they escaped. Just as the Gloam Knight, who was last, was about to go through the portal, the Amber Princess held up a hand. "Stop," she said. The Gloam Knight paused, almost involuntarily, just long enough for the portal’s magic to lose stability and ripple closed. "You are but the steward of Autumn in my absence. I know your loyalty. Relinquish yourself to me." The Gloam Knight faced the Amber Princess. "Once, yes, I answered to you. But that was an eternity ago, and now I look at you and see nothing I recognize. You have changed much." They took a step closer, peering at the Amber Princess, then recoiled suddenly. "No, it is worse. You are something else entirely…" They charged. The Amber Princess stood still. They closed in. The Amber Princess did not move. Just as the Gloam Knight’s sword was about to pierce the Amber Princess’ chest, she disappeared.
