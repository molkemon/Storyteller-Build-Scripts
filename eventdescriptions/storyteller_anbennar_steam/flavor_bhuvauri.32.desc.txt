Our industrial capacity has greatly increased since we began our projects. Every day, ore is mined, shipped, refined, transported, and forged into all manner of weapons, armour, tools, and material, while the forges blaze with coalfire. Our economy has become a machine, churning out tonnes upon tonnes of steel across sea and land.  While some protest the billowing smoke and pollution, we know more than any that progress and prosperity requires sacrifice, and we have already bled enough.
