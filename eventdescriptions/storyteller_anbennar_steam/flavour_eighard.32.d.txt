"I will need one hundred and twenty-eight mages around each city," the Amber Princess said. "They must be stationed perfectly equidistant from each other. I will also require a ring of salt, unbroken, banning any and all from entering or leaving the cities while the ritual is ongoing, unless they have my permission." "How long will this take to complete?" one of those gathered asked. "I can’t say," the Amber Princess replied. "It could take only a year. It could take decades. Should everything go according to plan, I give it, oh, five years?" "Will this not have disastrous consequences for both cities?" one of those gathered asked. "Arakeprun and Murdkather are two very prosperous settlements." "It will," the Amber Princess said. "But that is of no import. What we shall gain from this is far greater than the measly output of two cities." "You haven’t told us what is the purpose of this ritual?" one of those gathered asked.
