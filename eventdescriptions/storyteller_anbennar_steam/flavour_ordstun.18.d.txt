It seems that, like us, Seghdihr, ancient leader of the Segbandal, has withstood the test of time. No doubt still today it is a Hold made up of honorable dwarves, but their power is lacking, long have they ceased being Hegemon of their respected region, they are no longer a trustworthy neighbor, their weakness is a liability for us. They must submit to our rightful authority and protection, or be put out of their misery. While it leaves a sour taste in the mouths of our elders to be forced to humiliate a proud people like this, we must send them an appropriate Ultimatum, it is for their own good.
