It seems that, like us, Seghdihr, ancient leader of the Segbandal, has withstood the test of time. No doubt still today it is a hold made up of honorable dwarves, but their power is lacking. Long have they ceased being hegemon of their respected region; they are no longer a trustworthy neighbor, and their weakness is a liability for us. They must submit to our rightful authority and protection, or be put out of their misery. While it leaves a sour taste in the mouths of our elders to be forced to humiliate a proud people like this, we must send them an appropriate ultimatum; it is for their own good.
