We thought that the ruler of Iosahar attended court today merely to pay the respects due to our ruler, but it seems there was another reason for their visit. In the early evening, as our ruler rested in a private garden, the Iosahar slipped in to discuss the ongoing debate over why the Ynnic Empire fell, and whether ultimate duty lies in the Emperor or in the subjects. After speaking quietly for a few minutes, the Iosahar finally paused, to listen to our ruler’s reaction. The response is already forming in the ruler’s brain – they know the ideas outlined were.
