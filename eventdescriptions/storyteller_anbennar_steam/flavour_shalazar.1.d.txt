Irize Starshield, hero of the last war against Ashhana, through whose efforts they were able to take back their city, holds a high position in the ancient city. Rumours say that only thanks to her resilient defence the old kind Itros Moonwarden survived the Ashhana assault. Irize was offered the esteemed position as the king's closest advisor, and from this place she has effectively run the city. The city guard is strictly drilled and vigilant, guarding the citizens from the monsters hiding in the ruins of Old Shalazar. The city is safer than it ever has been in a long time. But many are quietly concerned about how tight the hold of the Shield of Shalazar has become, and are wary of her succeeding the childless king.
