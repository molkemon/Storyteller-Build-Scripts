The Edhardrachon cavalry has been nighunbeatable, time and again outclassing our own. This time, however, we were not caught off guard. As the traitors cut deep into our lines, our prepared stakes were raised from the ground, encircling the now trapped cavalry, sending the horses into a panic, and the soldiers surrendered not long after. Now a general, or the Argent Claw as she is known among her traitorous legions, is at our mercy. We now have a choice to make. by all rights, the woman should be executed for her treason against the crown, which would set a good example for the other traitors. On the other hand, the general's skill on the battlefield is immense, and it would be a waste to send all that experience to the grave. As such, some of our advisors suggest cutting a deal with her, so we may use her against the traitorous patricians.
