Excellent news has come in from Amgremós; in light of our demonstrable military excellence, and the recent ties forged between our people in the recent war, they have decided that it would be prudent to independently request our protection. Not quite a reply to our own offer of vassalization, though given the Amgremósians have simply copied all of our conditions of vassalization in the agreement, it seems we may as well humor their attempt to save face. It seems the Amgremósians have yielded to our dream of a united Devand, instead of resorting to a life picking the scraps of shipwrecks.
