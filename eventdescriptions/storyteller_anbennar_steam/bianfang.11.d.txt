The ruler of our dynasty stands on the great mezzanine of the Hanging Temple of Aoshan, the Proud Mountain. They are indeed proud, surveying our recently added dominions in Xianjie, dearly bought with the lives of tens of thousands of our soldiers. On the far horizon, the Temple of the Keepers is barely visible, and within, Kongren, the Voids Edge. The monks, with all of their Schools and Styles and mysterious martial arts, put up a fierce resistance, but our discipline and numbers eventually overcame them. Now, their temples lie open before us, filled with riches and priceless artifacts crafted by generations of monks. We would be well within our rights to sack these temples, put them to the torch, cart the plunder back to our capital, and declare these robed ruffians outlaw, to be executed on sight. They have long labeled us tyrants and despots, inciting our people to rebel in the name of 'freedom'; just another word for chaos. That said, to bring law and order to these lands may require a softer touch, and we may yet learn from these mystical warriors who felled so many of our troops.
