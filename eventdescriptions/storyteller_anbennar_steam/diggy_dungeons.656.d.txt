There is no time to prepare, the goblin troops immediately attack your group and you are forced to defend yourself against their nimble attacks. Despite your experience from prior encounters, however, your overall handling of the situation offers much room for improvement. Several dozen men fall as some of your officers seem to give confusing, somewhat contradictory orders. After the skirmish is over, you have shed far too much blood against such a small contingent. It appears to have been a scout troop, and this does not bode well.
