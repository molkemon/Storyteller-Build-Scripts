Preliminary overviews of newly trained officials from orphan backgrounds, particularly the Voltoj, have proven very promising. While many pureblooded goblin officials have had trouble securing unqualified cooperation with our human subjects, Voltoj officials seem to receive such cooperation more readily. The reason for this is presently unknown, though probable explanations include the halfhuman heritage of the Voltoj, and their tendency to be more attuned with the local situation when compared to our goblin bureaucrats. Whatever the case, it is undeniable that the Voltoj are a potentially useful resource, and it would benefit the state to utilize that resource more readily. Attached to this report are documents relating to several Voltoj officials who have been vetted for particular utility to the Overboss personally. Confirmation of the Overboss' choice should be returned promptly to this office as soon as possible so that the selected official may be promptly promoted and dispatched to their new posting.
