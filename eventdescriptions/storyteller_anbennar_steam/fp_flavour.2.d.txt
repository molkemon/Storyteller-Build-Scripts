What's worse than a pilfering rat or sucking bat? A brat. And what’s worse than a brat? A whole mischief of them. The dmarvuk—otherwise known as brats in the Common tongue—is a teensy, harmless pest with a nose for finding sweet foods and fermented drinks. By wing or paw, dmarvuks can soar to the highest silos or squeeze into the tightest cellars, making them a nightmare for farmers, brewers, and bakers. Unfortunately, a swarming mischief of the critters managed to slip inside a major storehouse in that province, consuming the contents within—what rotten luck.
