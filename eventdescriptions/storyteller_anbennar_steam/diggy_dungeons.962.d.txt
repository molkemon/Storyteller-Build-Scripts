One after another we fall to the unrelenting tide of spiders, unable to run any further and without any shelter in sight. Some of us break and run regardless, some stand and fight tooth and nail, while others lay down on the cold stone of the cavern floor and give up in the face of death. It matters not what they do, for their fates are the same; their lives ended by the children of great Yidab before even making it into her nest.
