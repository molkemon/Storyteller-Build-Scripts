A sword within a shield. That emblem of Corin is plain as day, recognizable to most across Cannor, and increasingly across Halann. But, what does it mean? It serves to remind us of our dual purposes. We must be an unflinching bulwark, defending the weak. And, moreover, we must be swift and sharp as the finest blade, in striking out against the injustices of the world, keeping our hearts as cold as steel for those who would do evil, no matter what place of reverence they might have slunk into.
