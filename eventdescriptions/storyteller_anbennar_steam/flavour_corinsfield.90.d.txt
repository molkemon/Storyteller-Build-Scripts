With the passing of our most holy leader, the people of Corinsfield find themselves without a guiding hand which would lead them towards Corin's righteuos path. To this end, three candidates have been presented before The Compact;  First, a reverend hailing from a the background of a judge, this candidate is noted to be gifted in administrative tasks.  Next, a fellow goodman who runs Poppyspring's Charity organizations, this candidate has a way of getting to people and would strengthen diplomatic aspects of the government.  Finally, hailing from the background of an inquisitor, our last candidate has experience catching witches and protecting Corinsfield from magick. This candidate would have increased military skills.
