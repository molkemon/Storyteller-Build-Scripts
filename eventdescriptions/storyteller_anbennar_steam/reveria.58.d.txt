Ages have passed, and time has struck us like waves against a rock. At the moment, we are Reveria, the last remaining of the Reaver kingdoms. Deveria abandoned that tradition, Pearlsedge currently cares more about the Empire, and Eborthil has moved to embrace trade, not raiding. We are the last ones. If we are to endure, we must rebuild our realm. We call upon the old Reavers amongst the nobility to take their advice.
