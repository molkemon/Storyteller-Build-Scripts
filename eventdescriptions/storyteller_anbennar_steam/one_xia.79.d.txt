At the end of the Trial, you are pulled out of the spirit realm. The Righteous Master of the Keepers smiles broadly, informing you that your soul has been judged to be balanced and serene. You are thus given permission to wield  against evil spirits, demons, and other enemies of the Xia, along with earning the service of the Order of Keepers as bodyguards or as a brigade on the field; if you are to fall in battle or otherwise, they will secure the blade and hurry it back to Jianxusi.
