Moredhal rises. As the gentry has grown in power and prestige to the point of overtaking the entirety of the country, there have arisen certain factions amongst their ranks who disagree over doctrine and priorities. Some have called them cults. While the government remains impartial, and while the gentry needs must set aside their disagreements while wielding power, things are much different amongst the rank and file. There, three factions have emerged. the Passionates of Agrados, the Triadic Devotees, and the Grudgeknights. The Passionates are sworn above all to Ildran, the Marquis of Pride, and Hedine, the Mistress of Lust. They are most concerned with religious doctrine and the establishment and maintenance of the culture. They are most represented amongst the administrative corps and the bureaucracy. The Triadic Devotees are sworn above all to Ibberal, the Grand Duke of Gluttony, Forsara, the Countess of Greed, and Canturian, the Baron of Envy. They are most concerned with commerce and contracts, and believe that the accumulation of money and goods is the best way to benefit the country. They are most represented amongst the merchants. The Grudgeknights are sworn above all to Kazraiel, the Wroth Prince, and Mesner, the Lord of Sloth. Their foremost preoccupation is battle and valour, and much of their doctrine is based around the ancient Agradosian tribes, who worshipped Agrados first and foremost. They are most represented amongst the soldiery.
