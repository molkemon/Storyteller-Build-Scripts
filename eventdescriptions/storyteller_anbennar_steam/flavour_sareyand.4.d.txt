Dalarand may have fallen after its last king Daraztarion Dalzuir, the young Phoenix, chose to bring disorder and chaos to Bulwar in a futile attempt to restore the Phoenix Empire under him. In the end, in spite of all his foolish ambition, the sole reward for his warmongering ways was betrayal by his general and confidant Kalindil Varamzuir, who founded the realm of Varamhar on the ashes of Dalarand. Nonetheless, the lands around lake Naza are the rightful inheritance of the Sarelzuir dynasty, for in the monarch Sarelzuir’s veins runs the blood of the rightful rulers of the lands of Dalarand. Keladora Dalzuir, sister to doomed Daraztarion, was the mother of Eledas Sarelzuir, making him the rightful ruler of both Sareyand and the lands of fallen Dalarand. With the gnollish threat dealt with and the eastern borderlands of Sareyand reclaimed, it was high time that we repaid the Varamzuirs for their treachery, took their vassals, wiped Varamhar from the map, and resurrected the realm of Dalarand in its stead, with the monarch on its throne, of course.
