Local Corintar chapters enjoy a great deal of independence from the Corintar itself, and choose their own local priorities among themselves. One thing remains in common, however; and that is the relentless purge of evil in all of its forms. Praise for one of our chapters has reached our lands, as reports come in that a feral vampire has been hunted and killed by members of this chapter. Great news for our chapter, but greater news for us, as several local lords have sent emissaries to the monarch to thank the ruler for the work of the Corintar abroad.
