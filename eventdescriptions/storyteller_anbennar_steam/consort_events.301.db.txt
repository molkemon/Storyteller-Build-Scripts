When the consort married into the royal house of the ruling dynasty, there were those who were skeptical of their origins. They were a young person from a distant land, a nobody in the eyes of many, suddenly invited to rule this great nation. However, time flies and it has now been over ten years since the union took place. While not all adore the consort, most have come to respect them as one of their own; it is very unlikely that the wedding anniversary of the couple could have gone by unnoticed, and so the monarch and their consort humbly accept all congratulatory letters, as well as gifts of varying splendor, brought to them.
