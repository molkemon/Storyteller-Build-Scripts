The Water Serpents that swim beneath the Ynn are astonishing creatures, the greatest of which are said to be longer than the very tributaries of the Ynn River and older than the Ending Flood. Such aura of profundity doesn't stop the youth in all its hubristic vigour from trying to jest with them. a group of farm boys, twenty-six in number, is carrying a seven meter long water serpent through the countryside while singing at full lungs about their strength. The creature, who had the misfortune of washing ashore, still wiggles within the boys' grip as they approach the capital's gates; there they halfseriously explain to the guards that this creature was given to them by the Holy River, and that they want to parade it in front of the monarch for the Water Serpent Stuir Balia celebrations, so that the ruler may name it King of the Stuir Balia and grant it a royal pool worthy of its status. Catching wind of this almost instinctively, the monarch saddles their horse and rides to the gates. It takes one glance at the creature for the monarch to know what their response is going to be.
