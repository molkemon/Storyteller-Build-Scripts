With centuries of unlife ahead of them, many vampires seem to lean into one of their preturning obsessions or another. Some pursue knowledge in dusty tomes, some seek power in courtroom or battlefield, still others indulge in every hedonistic pleasure known to man (and invent new ones as well). But those are all lies, pay them no heed. The only true obsession a vampire knows, the one that will last them as long as they unlive, is blood. And tonight, that truth is feeling particularly salient for our ruler. For try as they might to pay attention to the affairs of state, the latest political developments in the realm, that red ichor alone dominates their thoughts.
