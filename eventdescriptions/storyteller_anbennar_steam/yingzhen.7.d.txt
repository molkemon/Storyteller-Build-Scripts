To all but a few who knew him, Peng Sun was a waste of life. A man who had tried and failed to enter Yingzhen's bureaucratic class. That few, however, knew that he had risen to a much higher station. Hand picked by the Chamber's master, he was the man who would solidify Yingzhen's relationship with the Oni. Neither his departure nor his return were met with the fanfare of a hero, but the blood he had spilled against our new benefactor's enemies would one day be known as the fledgeling city's first use of political murder as a tool not only for personal gain, but for that of the state as well.
