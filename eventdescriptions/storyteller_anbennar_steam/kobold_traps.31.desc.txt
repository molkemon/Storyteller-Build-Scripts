Kobolds are distrustful of newcomers on account of their mistreatment by most folk over many lifetimes. This does not mean, however, that nonkobolds cannot become accepted, or even integrated into kobold culture. This is especially the case in the Lencori lands, full of resistance and sabotage as they are. For centuries, the halflings have resisted human rule; and they have done so from burrows, in tribes and cultures divided by petty differences, and from half the height of their enemies. Is it any wonder that now, a fellow of similar stature to their own has risen within their ranks? Lord Ciderscale, as he now calls himself, has mixed the halfling with the kobold. Coating himself in scarlet warpaints, and announcing himself to be as red in character as the cider he drinks, he has become a mainstay of their forces in the Small Country. From his command center at the core of Appleton, he has done a mighty fine job defending their newly acquired lands from those further south that desire to reclaim it and expel their brethren. Truly he must be koboldborn in spirit.
