The realm of the Serpentspine was something unknown to Triunics until recently, when their scouts were able to make contact with the vast, continent spanning cave networks, centered around a collapsed Dwarven Empire. While this place contains vast wealth, the races there, dwarves, goblins, and orks, all hate each other and constantly war for control over great dwarven citadels and the caves around them. If they wish to spread democracy even there, they have to deal with these tensions.
