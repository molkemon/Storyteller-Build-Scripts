Not only has the Emperor acknowledged our claim, but they have thrown their weight behind their words, and Exwes has conceded. Orston has been ceded directly to us. What a joyous day for our cause and that of all Halflings across the Empire. As Beepeck erupts into celebrations, our forces are already marching into our new territories. We are not treated as warmly as we had hoped, but no doubt, the Orstoners will soon see us as liberators.
