At long last, the monarch approaches their destination. To their embarrassment, which would no doubt be greater if their entourage were still around, their stomach suddenly rumbles loudly. Examining their surroundings, the monarch takes courage, as there are many opportunities to sate their hunger. Off in the middle distance, deer are going about their day, unaware of the two-legged predator close by. Near their feet, rabbits are running around. The nearby trees promise a bounty of ripe, wholesome fruits. With such varied options, what shall be the meal of the ruler?
