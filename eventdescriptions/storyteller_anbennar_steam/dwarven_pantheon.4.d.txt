The ruler sniffs the concoction offered by the High Priest of Margurz. The scent of alcohol masks the worst offending smells, but the ruler still retches at the odour. "Are you sure," they ask, "that I need to consume this?" The High Priest nods. "It is an essential part of the ritual. You must consume it, then count backwards from 100." The lord reluctantly takes the concoction and starts doing so, falling unconscious by 93. "Excellent." cries the High Priest as he turns to a nearby acolyte. "You. Fetch me the saw. We have nine hours, according to the texts, before they wake up." A day later, despite having a splitting headache and a shaved head, the ruler says that they have never felt better.
