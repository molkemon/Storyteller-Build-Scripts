Our dedication to the balance of the four seasons as of late has inspired a festival dedicated to this very concept. Celebrations often feature a mix of elements, from the flowers of Spring and the jubilation of Summer, to the gratitude of Fall and the reflection of Winter. Feasts, too, are often a mix of the best food each season has to offer. Despite the seemingly eclectic mix of elements and honors, the entire experience becomes one of cohesion, unity, and understanding. Not simply for all four seasons, but for each other.
