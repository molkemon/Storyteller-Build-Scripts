The sight of the Great White Catfish, avatar of the Kharunyana, has become a point of pride for many in the realm; citizens routinely gather to watch it on its travels up and down the length of the river. It has sheltered the nation and its people from the worst of the Rending, proving the ruler's decision to continue inundating more Great Temples was the right one. Seeing Kharunyana's avatar has quickly become a token of good luck for many, and there are even those who follow its progress up and down the river on a fleet of small boats, guarding its wellbeing and ensuring respectful conduct. Though it roams freely, the Catfish favors one particular bend in the river, a quiet, broad stretch along the bank of a province. There it lingers, swimming both above and below the water, in lazy circles convenient for watching. Its presence there is a spectacle and many evenings it can be found there, glowing faintly white in the darkness. People bring gifts, offerings, and many such things to shrines along the banks of the river there, so much so that the palace has been forced to erect a special building made just to house it all; a great shrine to the Kharunyana and its bounty.
