These artificery-crafted siege weapons utilize evocation magic to focus a barrage of sonic shockwaves that, over time, can shatter even the strongest fortification, reducing the offending wall to powdery dust. It is of course exceptionally inadvisable to stand in the way of such a beam, at least if one prefers to avoid the liquefaction of one’s internal organs. During peacetime, resourceful miners have on more than one occasion successfully used a requisitioned solidscream beamer to bore new tunnels through solid rock in search of rich and well-hidden ore deposits.
