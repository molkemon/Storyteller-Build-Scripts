Before its founding, Clan Sapchopper had been made up of two groups. the raiders who had stumbled into the Deepwoods during the Greentide, and the young warriors from the Bloodytusks, a clan of orks who had been trapped in the woods for nearly three generations. Together, they attempted to escape the Deepwoods. It was an attempt that ultimately failed. And in the time that followed, those who had not joined the Bloodytusks were swept away by the Greentide’s invasion. All of this was history that was willingly left forgotten, until now. With our recent conquest of the old Bloodytusk lands in the Hunter’s Grove, there are veterans of our clan who have begun to recall their roots. While they proudly and loyally bear the Sapchopper name, these grizzled warriors have gone so far as to begin petitioning our Chieftain for the right to rule their old home. Such arrangements of lesser clans ruling under greater ones are not unheard of. Agreeing would lend us a valuable stream of warriors and enable better use of the land, however it would come at the cost of some control. What say you, Chieftain?
