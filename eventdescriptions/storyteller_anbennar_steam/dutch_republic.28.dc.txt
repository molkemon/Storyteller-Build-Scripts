Being a border province does not seem to be a cause for concern among the nobles of the realm. Conversely, they believe that our neighbors should be the ones to be concerned, as we might very well decide that their lands would be better off under the rule of the realm or as Generality Lands of the republic. The people across the border seem to have seen this coming, since they appear to fear the might of the owner, particularly with an ambitious Calasanni prince in power.
