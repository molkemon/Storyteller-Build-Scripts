The prisoner gurgled something. "Finally ready to talk?" The head rose, and then fell. A nod. "We’ll get you some water." The guard left.  Water crashed across the prisoners head. "There’s your water. Now talk." Was the voice the same or new? It seemed deeper. "Had to… knife. Magic… not work. Work on… assassin. Worked on… queen." A voice echoed from the dark. "What did you say?" "Have to… they tell us… the monarchs must die." "WHO. ANSWER ME." Burning pain shot through the prisoners side. A punch? "The… lords. In the jungles." "ANSWER ME." Pain unlike anything he had ever experienced ran through the prisoner's body, the magical arts turned towards torture.
