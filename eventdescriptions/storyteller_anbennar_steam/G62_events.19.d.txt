As the festival has lasted several days, more and more old rivalries are dug up, and many a pirate and sailor has challenged old enemies to duels. This has done little to lessen the revelry of the festival participants, however; they treat the duels as spectator events, cheering the fighters on and betting on the outcomes. Even musicians see their chance to profit off the spectacle, as they accompany the duels with aggressive music to the beat of the clashing steel. The duels have quickly become so popular that many veteran fighters have started to challenge random people to duels, just to prove their skill. In order to keep order in the city, the ruler has quickly ordered the organization of a dueling tournament.
