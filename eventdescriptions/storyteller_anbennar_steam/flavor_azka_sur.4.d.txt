The great gates shut behind the monarch's hunting party. They sat on their horses and gazed in wonder around them. The walls were high enough that from the outside, only the very tops of the closest trees to the wall could be seen. It enclosed a large area that bordered the mountains and had been seeded with plant life and several herds of various animals. The trees around them were lush and green and as they ventured further in they came upon the great plain which dominated the center of the area. It was like seeing a miniature of the lands that surround the Suran, with a small tributary river running through it and the foothills and mountains on the far side. There were animals drinking from the water, others grazing, others still hunting as the monarch would soon be. The monarch smiled. 'This is where the fun starts.'
