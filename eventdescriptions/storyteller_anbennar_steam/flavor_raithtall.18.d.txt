The monarch is known for their charms, but charming a fey noble was more than people expected even of them. Recently it seems that a notable fey visitor to the Twilight Court has taken a special interest in the ruler, finding new excuses to make visits to the palace. While of course, a ruler can never entertain affairs, having the favor of such a powerful fey could certainly improve their standing, both in Eordand and in the Feyrealm.
