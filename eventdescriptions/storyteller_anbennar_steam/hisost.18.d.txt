The Awirenya refusing to serve the Yenaze Mezhi have served as an inspiration for the common soldiery. Many regiments are deserting, taking their equipment and training and going off to protect the gnolls from forces loyal to the Yenaze Mezhi. It is a noble gesture, but one which leaves the realm with an immediate problem; they still need an army. Soldiers are deserting in such numbers that the army will be left significantly diminished if they do not stop them. Cut off from the logistics of the state, it is also questionable how capable they are of actually resisting a concentrated effort from the Yenaze Mezhi. Still, this is what they wish. We know the queen would have been proud of them.
