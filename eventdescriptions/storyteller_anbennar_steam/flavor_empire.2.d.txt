A holy warrior arrives at our court, dressed in resplendent shining armor and bearing a glowing blade in their hand. They represent a paladin order present within our lands that assists us in combatting various monstrosities that roam the countryside. While our people are thankful for their help, at times this order’s zeal gets the best of them. The paladin accuses a local temple of a different faith of heresy, claiming they are the staging ground for a future infernal incursion. Some of the paladin’s brothers and sisters in arms have already arrested a few clergy, and seek our approval to fully dismantle the temple. What will we do?
