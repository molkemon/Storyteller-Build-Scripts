One night, as the great ruler sits in their garden pondering the state of their nation, they end up considering what exactly makes a good ruler and how they can manage all the various affairs of state. As the ruler rises to leave the garden, they see a figure sitting on a different bench some distance away. The figure is old and wrinkled, and as the ruler approaches they see that the figure is emitting a faint blue glow. The ruler walks up to what they recognize as a spirit and learn that it is in fact one of their own ancestors. The ancestor’s spirit beckons the ruler to sit down and have a chat. The ruler accepts, and as they talk about many things, the ancestor offers to lend their wisdom for a time so that they can better themselves. Specifically, the ancestor worked as an administrator and offers to teach these skills to the ruler, who joyously accepts.
