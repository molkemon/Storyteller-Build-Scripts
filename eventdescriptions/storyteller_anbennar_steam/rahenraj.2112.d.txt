We heard, vaguely, that one of the Vizier's sons was visiting court, but thought little of it until the Vizier approached the Lotus Throne himself, head bowed. Apparently, said son has proven talented with both spearplay and tactical thinking, and the vizier would have him serve our armies. When the man is brought to introduce himself, he does seem promising; but also undoubtedly loyal to our Vizier, first and foremost. A sharp sword indeed, but one that would be close to the ruler himself, with all the risk that entails. What shall the ruler decide?
