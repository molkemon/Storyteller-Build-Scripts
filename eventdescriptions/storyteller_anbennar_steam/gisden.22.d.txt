A shift roars outwards across our land, and subtle may it be, it will change Gisden forever. No longer are we merely scions of Alenics, Arannese and more, but we are the inheritors of a most ancient and noble people. Milcori, the true sons and daughters of the so called Borders, who built the ancient labyrinths led by the enlightened LichKing Avatars of Nerat. Some may be appalled by our revelations of yore, let them be. It is their land no longer; it is Milcorissia, and they have no power here.
