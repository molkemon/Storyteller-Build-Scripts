The monarch gathers workers and shamans at the site where the World Tree was located. A great pit is excavated. After a few days of work, workers reach a layer of soil littered with animal bones. Sure, that they have reached the spot, the monarch orders to create a pyre akin to the Black Hearth and ignites it. In an instant it creates a heatwave that engulfs the terrain in flames. Shamans say that in place of the pyre they have found a crystal of powerful potency. It is inserted into the Black Hearth and the heat inside increases.
