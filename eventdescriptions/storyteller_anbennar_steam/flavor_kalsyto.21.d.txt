There are but two divine mandates. Liberty and Unity. Every citizen of the Federation knows this, deep inside their hearts. One cannot exist without the other, never truly. Tyrannies have buckled under this mantra and will continue to do so, because their people want the one thing only we can give them. democratic representation in a united state that does not discriminate. We cannot be so selfish as to not give the gifts we were given, or else we would be no better than the slaver selling slaves outside our borders. It is only a matter of time before every person on Halann will know what we know, and we have never been closer. Let us unite Halann as Kalyin's image was so long ago.
