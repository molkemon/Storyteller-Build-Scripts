Our expedition to Haless reports several new developments in their search for the antimage technique known as Courache. Firstly, it is not a technique at all, but a substance generally known in Common as Black Dame's tear, capable of negating magic on touch, apparently with absolute efficacy. Secondly, it appears to be the near-exclusive preserve of the Command, whose deployment of the stuff is a profound taboo in a continent often dominated by powerful mages. Needless to say, we must not endorse the tyrannical ideals of the hobgoblins just to get our hands on the substance, and the report lays open several possibilities for acquiring information on this taboo resource without doing so.
