Messengers have arrived in the capital, bearing warnings and threats. The hobgoblin delegation bears the threeheaded banner of the command, and carries a message from the Grand Marshal; our actions in declaring a war against those they are currently engaged in struggle with have not gone unnoticed by their warcamps, and the marshals are incensed. They warn us that the Command asserts absolute authority over all the lands they are engaged with, and will not suffer us attempting to steal land they consider rightfully theirs. They demand we withdraw and unilaterally make peace, and threaten war if we do not.
