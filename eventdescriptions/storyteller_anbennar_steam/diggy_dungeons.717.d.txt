The plan was simple: the mages at the back would cast a few light spells to create openings for the close quarters fighters to get in, and attack the golem's weak points the joints. Unfortunately, the golem's size did not mean it was slow, and a number of expeditionaries were killed before it was brought to its knees, cut clean in half by its heavy blade, crushed by a stomping foot, or bones shattered by being kicked into the walls. Despite the death toll, a number of the expedition see this as further proof of the riches ahead.
