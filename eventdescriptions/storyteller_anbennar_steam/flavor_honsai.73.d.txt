Our agents infiltrated Chien Binhrung and were even able to identify the gemmerchants' facility that held the gem we sought, which could bind the Ghost Emperor to this realm. But that is roughly as far as they got before things started going wrong. We sought to sneak in, but the area was well guarded, so we were forced to ambush a patrol to enter. Then once inside, we were forced to trawl through hundreds of gems to find the one we sought, discarding mountains of wealth for a priceless treasure. When things began to go south and the guards showed up, a few men deserted with pockets full of rubies and sapphires, while others died fighting off the Binhrung forces that gathered in response to this attack. Only a few of our agents escaped, and though we have the gem, we have also earned Chien Binhrung's deepest enmity, forever.
