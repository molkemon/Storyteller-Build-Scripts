The recent establishment of a Gommo branch office in that realm has allowed the gnomish scientists greater success in researching the mysteries of ancient knowledge than our scholars had in decades. The increasingly better treatment of gnomes and the opportunities afforded by the library of that realm have led to a further influx of gnomes and Gommo influence. These groups have now petitioned us to allow the expansion of the Gommo branch office into a fully fledged Gommo Research Division based in that realm. Such a division could allow us to harness more technological prowess than ever before, while refusing it would most likely lead to a strong decline in the Gommo's scientific activity in that realm, as the gnomes may shift to merely consulting the Great Library's scrolls but bringing their knowledge elsewhere.
