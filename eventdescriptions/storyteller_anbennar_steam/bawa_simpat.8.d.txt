As the monarch kneels before Semat Tidakhui, the Lady in Stone, a gleeful laughter fills the air around the statue. The ruler's retinue surges forward to the monarch's aid, but a hand lurches out to stop them from the figure prostrated before the statue. They pause, hearing the laughter in the cold wind pierce their very souls. The monarch stands up in a single fluid motion, staring with deadened eyes at soldiers drilled to give their lives for the lady. Semat Tidakhui speaks. "You will kneel before me as you did her. Her domain and all she has conquered is mine in turn. I have led your people this far and will lead you further still, to unseen opulence and power. Rise again, and hail your Lady in Stone."
