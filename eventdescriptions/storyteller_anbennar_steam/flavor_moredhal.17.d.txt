At one of our most recent Festivals of the Seven Thousand Seven Hundred Seventy-Seven, the monarch summoned a succubus. It was a fairly routine event, to be sure, but this succubus was different. She was wonderful, and entrancing, and delightful in all the right ways and all the wrong ways, too. The monarch was taken in by her charms, and woke up the next morning in a forest clearing, stark naked. The affair was soon forgotten as just another night of debauchery, and life went on as normal. At least, until the monarch was rudely awoken this morning by the cries of a babe. It appears that the monarch’s tryst with the succubus has yielded a child, and not just any child – a halfdevil child. What fun.
