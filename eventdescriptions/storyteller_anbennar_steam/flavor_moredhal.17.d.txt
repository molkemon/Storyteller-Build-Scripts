At one of our most recent Festivals of the 7,777, our ruler summoned a succubus. It was a fairly routine event, to be sure, but this succubus was different. She was wonderful, and entrancing, and delightful in all the right ways, and all the wrong ways, too. Our ruler was taken in by her charms, and woke up the next morning in a forest clearing, stark naked. The affair was soon forgotten as just another night of debauchery, and life went on as normal. At least, until our ruler was rudely awoken this morn by the cries of a babe. It appears that our ruler's tryst with the succubus has yielded a child, and not just any child - a halfdevil child. What fun.
