With a skilled cadre of Inscriptors at our beck and call, we may task them to use their skills for the glory of our realm and craft a puissant Inscripted artifact that would be the equal of those encountered in the legends told by the bazaar's storytellers. However, even their vast talent and experience will only allow them to craft one such marvel at a time. Thus, we need to decide on what artifact should be made. A Crown of Wisdom to inspire our courtiers and lead their minds to new vistas? A Vessel of the Heavens as a fitting conveyance for our exalted ruler, demonstrating the majesty of our realm? A Hammer of Shaping to support our craftsmen in their work? Or a Ring of Unity to bring all the people of our realm closer together, quelling any dissent and unifying them behind our noble rule?
