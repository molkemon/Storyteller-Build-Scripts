We have pieced together enough information on another god. When Death entered the world and ended the Era of Forging, Lorgrim and her twin brother suddenly appeared. Lorgrim's role was to gather the souls of the Dwarvenkind while Dagrin stood stupefied as the oldest of his people suddenly grew weak and perished from the monsters known as Age and Sickness. Lorgrim wandered the world, gathering the dwarven dead and bringing them to the care of her yet-discovered twin brother.
