While she always wanted to become the ruler of the North, the way that was brought onto the domain was not one that she planned for. Her predecessor’s death was supposed to come through her flaming blade. Yet, that moment never came; her body was buried three years ago as she lost the duel against the famous Akaliza. The domain slept at that time, cast aside during the siege, intentionally sidelined even if her presence would have drastically changed the outcome in favor of Eusiwuni. Warlords of the North, humiliated and forced to concede in favor of a new power. Minor warlords fighting for the scraps. One clear favorite to win it all. Soon, her people treated her as a god; their radical reverence of shapeshifters fueled to her benefit. If anyone wanted to raise a hand at her, she would burn them alive. Thus, she forced everyone to pray to her burning likeness. She brought the Kaziran Leopard Lodge to the country to monopolize the trade for herself and then isolated every shifter around her court, away from their minor warbands. No one protested. That finally proved she and only she was in charge of Eusiwuni’s new future. Yet, there was one last thing to make straight. Akaliza, the one who stole her score on the kill she wanted the most, was still alive and well in the place that stripped Eusiwuni of the prestige and territory it had, an annoying thorn that was to be disposed of and razed to the ground as a statement to everyone, even the south.
