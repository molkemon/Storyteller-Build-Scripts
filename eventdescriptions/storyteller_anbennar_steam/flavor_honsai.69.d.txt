Baihon Xinh helped Jaya Raya grow to prominence, its support raising a small tribe into the Lupulan's undisputed king of kings. It is time for them to return the favour. Magistrates who have visited the jungle tribe report they have a large contingent of shamans and other spirit-attuned practitioners, many of whom must undoubtedly know rituals that could support Caoban Sang and bolster his ties to the mortal realm. We could demand these people attend the capital, but there is also a more moderate approach of merely requesting their services as a form of tribute payment that would not risk violating our longstanding agreements with Jaya Raya.
