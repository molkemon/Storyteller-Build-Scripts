Grun Strongbellow, may his soul be ever restful among the ancestors, is dead. He was found seated at his workbench in a picture of peaceful repose, some last few notes scribbled in the margins of his latest blueprints mere hours before he dozed off for the last time. A state funeral has already been planned, with attendants from the country, his home of Silverforge, and other nations beyond pledging their attendance. Though the weight of his death has yet to truly sink in, the Strongbellow Society has already begun venerating him as the spiritual ancestor of their ideals. We will mourn his death, but Strongbellow would not want us to get caught up in formality and become mired in grief. The country will move forward, celebrate his accomplishments, and through our memory Strongbellow's legacy will become immortal.
