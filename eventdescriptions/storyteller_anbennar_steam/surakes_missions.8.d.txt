Bulwari is the name given to the human cultures throughout Bulwar. There are the Bahari of the north, the Brasanni of the Suran Delta, the Gelkars of the Harpy Hills, the Zanites of the river valleys, the far off Maqeti of the Gulf of Rahen, and the Surani of Far Bulwar. But what does it mean to be Bulwari? Some would say that it is to be a human from these lands, but what about the nonhumans who live here, the dwarves and elves, and other races? Perhaps the best answer to the question, "what does it mean to be Bulwari," is "To be part of Surakeš."
