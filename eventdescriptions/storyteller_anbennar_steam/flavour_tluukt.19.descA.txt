Our messenger to the other country returns with positive news; their monarch has accepted the terms of our offer and is moving into action as we speak. The governors of the domain will be helpless in suppressing the Drolas insurrection, and by the time the sun sets all of Drolakand will be under the other country's control. Ordinarily, the domain would be capable of crushing rebellions like these with ease; and that's where we come in. The domain certainly won't be expecting the other country and our nation to be fighting side by side.
