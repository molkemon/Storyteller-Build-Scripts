Hunched into the many crevices our soldiers wait for the warband to pass by them. Arrows, stones, and spells hurl through the air onto the orkish menace, decimating their numbers. Screaming and bellowing they try to strike back, but each attempt at breaking free from our vicious volley results in a direct hit against them. After a few minutes of this the warband has been mostly slain, with the few survivors routed and fleeing back into the dark depths from whence they came.
