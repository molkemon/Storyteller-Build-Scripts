The great watercarved stone caverns we found marked a change from the jungle and wetlands we have grown so used to exploring in this region. That difference intrigued us enough to venture into them, foreboding as they were. We are not truly sure what we have discovered. There are strange planes of force that seem to hew into the rock. There are peculiar sigils on the walls, still charged with power. And there is the undeniable feeling that from within these caverns, one could reshape the world. There is much more to be learned of just what has been found here. But we have been given a glimpse within the magic behind the Hinuilands, and it is a thread we shall unravel in time.
