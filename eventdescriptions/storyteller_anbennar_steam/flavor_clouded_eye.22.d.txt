The Clouded Eye has always been the most prized possession of the state. Our head shamans use it to see visions of the future that may come to pass and give direction to the state. Today, the ruler has approached the head shaman and requested a vision. The head shaman faces the ruler and pulls a lever, raising the Clouded Eye out of the ground to catch the light of the full moon. As the moonlight hits the Clouded Eye, symbolism bombards the ruler. Images of wolves fighting over a rabbit, seeds sown and blooming into flowers, and a scrawny halfork carrying a scroll and shouting into an angry crowd come to the ruler. As the visions fade away, the head shaman looks at the ruler and asks, “Which vision struck out to you the most?”
