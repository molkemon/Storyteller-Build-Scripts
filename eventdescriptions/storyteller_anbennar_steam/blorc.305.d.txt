It began on a whim. Perhaps it was the enjoyment of something different, something exotic, that led to it growing into something more. Because it was a goblin, rather than another ork, that drew the ruler's eye. Or perhaps it was merely meant to be. An encounter arranged by fate. Regardless, in time, the infatuation became something more. A few meetings, a few shared moments of laughter, a few exchanged drinks, a few whispered words, and a whim turned into a passion. The ruler should have known there would be consequences. Nothing lasts forever, after all. And, now, the time had finally come to make a decision. The warriors and priests gathered around the hall are filled with a strange tension, their brows furrowed, their lips pursed. United in their opinions, they insist that the ruler must quell the rumors that have begun to spread. There is a fine orkish consort, or so they claim, whose powerful father has promised a large dowry in exchange for ties of blood. In the end, it has come to this. A decision between passion and reason. Between the wants and the duties of the ruler.
