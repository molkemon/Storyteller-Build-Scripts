Now that we have eclipsed Arbaran, many feel like we have to unify the Silcalas realm. The question remains how we would go about this. Our monarch's advisors are split on the issue. The military advisors and generals believe we should just go in and claim Arbaran for ourselves, arguing that we are the main branch of the Silcalas family now and thus have the right to rule over Arbaran. Our foreign affairs advisors believe we should try diplomatic means, believing that the nobles of Arbaran will see our point of view and would be willing to side with us on this issue if given enough incentive.
