After the Silcalas dynasty lost control over the realm, they remained an influential family within the country. However, they never fully accepted losing control, working in the shadows to remedy this state of affairs. All of that culminated in today’s claim of the Small Country. It seems the Silcalas family has somehow managed to carve out a provision in the new constitution that proclaims their dynasty is the head of our new Small Country. However, not all hope is lost for the current lord, as it seems the Silcalas family is willing to adopt them into the family if they are willing to swear their loyalty to them. With all other nobles backing the Silcalas takeover, there aren’t many options left for the current lord. If only this could have been prevented.
