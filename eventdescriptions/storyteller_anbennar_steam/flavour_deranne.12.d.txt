During my travels throughout the Ruined Sea, I met a Seaflower merchant at an inn in Port Isobel’s Tower Street. Arlaín was his name, a rich and portly man, who I quickly learned was of high standing within the Deranne Trading Company. He even owned an estate in Brunvern that had been passed through his family since the Conference at Columnshaven. Now, just as any good Derannic, I am quite fond of the ruler, and so I made my leave as I sensed the topic of conversation was moving to more royal matters. I’m not one for getting into brawls, after all. The Bard nervously scanned the tavern's patrons. So yes, while relations are generally cordial, it's evident that ever since that day in Columshaven, a wedge was driven between Crown and Trade.
