The elves of Eordand follow the Fey of the seasonal courts. Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter. Long ago, they might have been united in this worship, but this isn't the case today; each season has its own dedicated followers, as well as Eordellon's emphasis on "seasonal balance," though they interpret this differently. This division is also reflected in how the fey deal with them, as none of them are seen as longlasting or important enough for the most powerful among them to consider dealing with them, outside of the occasional patronage of specific individuals. To rectify this, they will need to find ways to increase their status among the fey, as well as eliminate the competition.
