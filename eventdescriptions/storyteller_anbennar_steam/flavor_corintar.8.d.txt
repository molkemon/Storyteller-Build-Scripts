Gwindeth Camllann was, at first, just one of many Wood Elves who followed Corin out of the Deepwoods and fought at the Battle of Castonath. Yet after witnessing the bladedancing of Aldresians and Ibevari elves cutting cleanly through orkish lines, however, Gwindeth's destiny would change. In the decades since the Greentide ended, the Wood Elf has devoted herself to mastering what she had seen while in the service of the Corintar. This zeal has built her a following, one that could be steered with a little direction.
