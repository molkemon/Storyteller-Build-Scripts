The Green River is the pride of Varaine, however ever since its largescale utility was discovered in the 15th century, the river has progressively been molded into a tool for the state and the alchemicals industry. These changes suffered by the river, though gradual, were noticed by many in our nation, who disliked the fact that the river over the years had become more and more polluted and dense with chemicals to the point where it's nearly impossible to properly appreciate the river's natural beauty.  These reports, which grow more and more numerous by the year, are a large concern for the Varainey government, and efforts to conserve or even restore the river started being looked into. However, alchemists brought forward new proposals to increase the river's utility even further, a decision which would greatly alienate even more of the people who desire the river to be preserved, but would bring large economic boons to the nation.
