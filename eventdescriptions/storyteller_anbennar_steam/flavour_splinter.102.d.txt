In the busy docks of Zurkanrek, a young woman stands in a state of both shock and revelation. Days prior to returning ashore, she received visions of existence shattering, of broken pieces gathering together, of the three-headed goddess of all Unity. Weeks have passed since then, and she had told her revelation to all who would listen. As word spread to the ears of the monarch, an idea began to form. This could be a prime opportunity indeed, for should the faith of Kalyin, the unified Goddess of the Lakes, spread under the patronage and backing of the crown, all Triunic civilization would recognize the unshakable truth that Monarchy is triumphant, and that Monarchy is the future of our people.
