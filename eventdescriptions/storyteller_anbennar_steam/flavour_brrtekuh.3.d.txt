They thought the sands could protect them. Yet they did not realize that for hundreds of years, they have been living within the sands, and what might have been a grand tale of survival against the elements for one Irkorzik Gnoll was merely a common trek for the armies of Brrtekuh. This trek was different from the usual fare, however, bringing those same gnolls who have mythologized the endlessly engulfing desert, along with their pack mistress. Now with the monarch before them, there is no dispute on which of the two sister packs of the western sands is the greatest.
