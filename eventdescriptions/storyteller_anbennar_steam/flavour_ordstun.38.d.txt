There is naught but grief at the funeral of Ardeginn XXII Whiteforged, the Second Diamond Queen, the Opener of the Gate, the Diamond Unbreakable, the Reclaimer of the Serpentreach, the Protector of All Dwarves. But outside the funeral, in the homes of rancid conspirators and venerable nobles, there is nothing but celebration. The Queen is dead; and, if those aligned with the Diamonds get their way, the new ruler will share Ardeginn XXII's fate. Since emerging from Argordstun, we have faced so many challenges: but we have always had Ardeginn at our helm, steering us in the exact direction we needed to go. Was she divisive, certainly; was she imperious, of course; but she was also one of us, and she was a muchneeded source of stability. Without her, what do we even have? A power vacuum has opened, and every part of society is trying to fill it. The new ruler must now weather the storm of ten thousand petitioners, rebels, nobles, lapidaries, stonekeepers, and monsters: and they must survive this storm. Perhaps, if they can survive long enough, this chaos can be outlasted, just as we outlasted the orcs when we stayed in our hold.
