An unlucky foot placement seems to have activated some mechanism within the upcoming part of the pillar. As we try to make it through the chamber before anything bad happens, we feel a strong wind starting to pick up strength, and a number of panels open in the side of the chamber. Soon we are pelted by grains of sand and buffeted by strong gusts of air. We seem to be stuck in some sort of chamber meant to emulate the conditions of a sandstorm. If we do not do something soon, we are going to be buried alive.
