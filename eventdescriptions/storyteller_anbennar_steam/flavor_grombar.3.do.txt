For the past decades, though we may have been subjugated under the orkish boot, unlike the tales we have heard from the south, these Gray orks have ruled with a relatively loose grip, allowing us and our fellow Reachmen to exist in relative autonomy of their domain. As long as we paid our taxes and sent our soldiers, it seemed that the orks were content to leave us well enough alone. Until now, that is. A messenger from Bal Vroren has arrived, demanding that we integrate our realm into their new kingdom of Grombar. It is clear that this is less a request, and much more of a threat. In fact, it has been made clear to us that our choice is between that of slavery or outright death. The Gray King’s grip has finally tightened into a chokehold.
