Varina promised to not use necromancy on humans, but for necronomics to be sustainable she needs to bend that a little bit. Some necromancers will try to see how much pain souls will be in if they are real careful and considerate while raising them. Besides, just being alive is painful, isn't it? Hunger, thirst, aches, and so on. Your soul being used to animate your body might not be all that bad in comparison.
