Before the assembled diplomats and dignitaries, the Pope places the Imperial Regalia into the hands of the new Emperor, recognising them as the rightful Emperor of Anbennar on the behalf of the Ravelian Church and the one true God. With the coronation complete, the ruler watches from a balcony as the attendees pour from the church and towards the surrounding celebrations, followed shortly by the new Emperor. To be selected as the venue for the coronation is a great honour, the significance of which will not be lost on the citizens of the realm or the rest of the Empire.
