As they were about to move forward with the plan to avoid the strange kobold, one of the scouts trips over a rock and lets out a yelp loud enough to alert the moss sorter. This is swiftly followed by screeches unlike the ones the kobold made before, and a veritable wave of scales pouring out of the nearby caves. Being far away from the rest of the expedition, the party has no choice but to run away, leaving their stumbled comrade at their pursuers' mercy. The survivors' numbers thinned during the escape, but once they reunited with the other troops, the kobolds stood no chance. The mounting pile of scaly bodies is enough to scare the rest, and they disperse into the caves as quickly as they appeared. With wounds patched up and reports delivered, the remaining scouts urge the rest to move out as quickly as possible.
