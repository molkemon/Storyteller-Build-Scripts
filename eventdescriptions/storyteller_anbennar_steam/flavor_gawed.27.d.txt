An untouchable, indomitable aura pervaded the court in the weeks and months after the conquest of the Northern Imperial frontier. But that veil of invincibility was pierced when a simple damp brown package was brought to the court late one day. The advisors of the monarch crowded around the ruler as the hesitant messenger unveiled the wrapped gift. The courier fumbled the package unceremoniously as it dropped to the floor splattering crimson. It was a native eagle of the Alennic Frontier, the symbol of Gawed, bloodied and dead. The ruler roared in anger, demanding who sent such a gruesome item, but the deliverer took a long moment for a response. Murmurs and wails went up from the court as the culprit was revealed. The package was delivered by a swarm of doves.
