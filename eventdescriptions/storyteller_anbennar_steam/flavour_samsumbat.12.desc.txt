No more will we suffer at the hands of the corrupt kings of Kheterata, setting themselves up as Stewards of the Sorrow yet barely able to keep their own house in order. Together with the Republic of our country, we have torn out the rotting foundation and replaced it with something new. However, not all in our court are convinced that the ruler will do a much better job than the previous Stewards. This has been a point of contention during the whole campaign, but now that the goal has been achieved, the matter must be resolved: how will we respond to this new Steward of the Sorrow? Have they earned our trust and loyalty, or are we better served parting ways from here?
