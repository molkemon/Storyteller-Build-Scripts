The streets of the capital have turned brown with the dried blood of the innocent. Vampires lounge in orgies of bodies, laughing the night away, now that the country is lost to the dead. The ruling caste live, yes, for now, pampered and fed forcefully and treated as the animals they are. Perhaps the vampires will feed on two of them tonight. Perhaps some sweet blood with the savoury. Despite their newfound freedom, some vampires have already fled, fearing what is to come; for now that society has been broken, it must now be remade ľ a society where the strong must rule. A society where all vampires must now come to heel and obey, to one, true Vampire Lord.
