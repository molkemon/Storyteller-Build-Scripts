"It could have been worse," the finance minister whispered. "We still look like fools," cried out the chief diplomat. "Do you have any idea how many foreign complaints we received?" The other dozen officials murmured. The monarch sighed as they thought about how this mess started. It began two weeks ago when a Bulwari merchant was using silver coins minted by us to pay for an order. That should have been the end of that story. But instead, some of those coins transmuted into an unknown lesser metal. The merchant was then arrested for fraud. Unfortunately, he would not be the last. At least a hundred other people found themselves accused of counterfeiting as silver coins from across Cannor began to change. It did not take long for people to figure out that all these coins had a common connection. us. Each and every coin was forged in our mints that sourced from the mines of Yfelorr. We do not know what happened to the merchant or the other unfortunate souls caught in all of this. But what we do know is that this damned "Fey Silver" is causing us problems.
