Colltinn is a ruined forest beset with rot and hated abominations from continued warfare in previous years between Pelomar and Sidpar. There is one quality that lifts it above worthlessness. Colltinn is the last area of land before entering the forbidden Domandrod. All Peitar feel a deep connection with the Domandrod and the Fey; Sidpar enjoys this connection, but our cousins in Pelomar have been long severed from the Domandrod. Now that our wars have ended and we are now allies, and we realize the pain that we have caused our cousins, we should consider our ownership of the land. Pelomar offers us acknowledgement of our supremacy over the realms of the south along the Sarmadfar, including support in war against the heretic Caamasi, in exchange for Colltinn. Should we accept this offer?
