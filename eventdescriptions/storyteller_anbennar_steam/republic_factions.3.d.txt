The influence of a powerful guild has driven up prices on the trade goods in the realm. It has come to the point where traders are finding it cheaper to import goods from abroad to compete with local prices, something they claim is becoming more difficult due to the current restrictive legislation on imports. Relaxing customs would give our merchants a chance to make beneficial agreements where the trade goods are produced, but the guilds, who are vehemently opposed to this, do serve their purpose as well.
