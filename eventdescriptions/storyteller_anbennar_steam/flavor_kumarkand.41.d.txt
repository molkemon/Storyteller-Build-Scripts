We have contacted the other Old Sun Cult sects in order to obtain support for the restoration of the true way of Surakel in these lands of Kumarkand. One of these sects has proven more receptive than most. The Šibhluliam have already taken to the task with alacrity, and will put themselves at our service for some time. Though their willingness to help is to be welcomed, it should be noted that they are a bit too radical for our taste.
