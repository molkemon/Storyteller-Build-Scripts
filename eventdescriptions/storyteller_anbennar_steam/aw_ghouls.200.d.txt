Though they prefer the flesh of the newly dead, ghouls are not particularly picky about the bodies they consume. Upon gaining access to a crypt or mausoleum, they will break open every sepulchre and unearth every grave to consume the flesh and gnaw the bones of the dead, moving on once there is no more food for them. Even after the creatures are gone, the damage they have done remains, and many in the realm are furious or traumatized by the desecration of their ancestor's tombs. Demands that we act to prevent such perversions, and accusations that we do not care to stop them, grow louder by the day.
