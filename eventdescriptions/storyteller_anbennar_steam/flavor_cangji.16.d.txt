As we move across the last battlefield of Shamakhad, we gather the few korashi manacles that were recovered from the hobgoblin commanders and place them together into a meagre pile. Spiritfire monks channel their flames into the restraints, and after absorbing the energy the shackles detonate into a shower of glittering black dust. All our soldiers simultaneously cheer, for they know that at last the final menaces of magekind have been obliterated, and they are truly free. We have fought to bring our people together, and now we have fought to save those who are our people only in ability, nothing more. The cruelty of the Command has been broken, and we have forged an everlasting bond with mages not just of Shamakhad, but of all Haless. With our new position as the defender of magic, mages flock to our nation to give us their skills and knowledge. Who knows what the future holds in store for us, except the fact that we will stand as the bastion of magical freedom from now until we fall to dust.
