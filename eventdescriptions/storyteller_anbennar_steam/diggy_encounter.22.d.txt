As our scouts return, their number diminished, they report of the horror they saw. There was no need to fear the spiders, their traps were enough already.  As a particularly unlucky member stepped on what seemed to be stable ground, the nets hidden by rocks gave way into a crevice, leading to godsknowwhere. Others got crushed in avalanches triggered by the unassuming vibrations of small webs, and even more just… stuck to the webs, with no way to cut them down without getting stuck as well.
