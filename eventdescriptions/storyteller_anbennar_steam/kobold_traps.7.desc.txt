Our men have pushed their way into the kobold traps, overcoming all defenses. We've suffered flamethrowers, drakes, acid, crossbows, cannons, pitfalls, and more, but finally, after everything, we have taken the trapped province. There's only one problem. the kobolds have destroyed the province's traps before we could get to use them. The entirety of the province's defenses have gone up in flames. We shall not be able to commandeer their madness, but did we really want to in the first place?
