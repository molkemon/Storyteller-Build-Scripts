Tensions within the War Room have unfortunately spilled out into conflicts between various Commands on how the state should be governed and how its resources are to be used. The latest of these conflicts concerns who should get priority distribution of steel and blackpowder. The Boar Command argues that the materials are needed to supply the frontline infantry units while the Dragon Command requests that cannons receive priority. This disagreement has become very heated with both sides accusing each other of trying to sabotage the Great Command. Whoever is favored, the other party will be extremely displeased.
