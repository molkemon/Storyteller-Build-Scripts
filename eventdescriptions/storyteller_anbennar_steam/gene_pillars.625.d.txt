Greetings. The party meets another person in the pillar surprisingly. A woman, wearing long, flowing robes, appears before them. She wears her hair in a tight bun, and her face shines a pale white. Her red painted lips curve into a welcoming smile. "Weary travelers, perhaps you’d like to take a rest before continuing?" The woman’s words fall upon deaf ears. The only things wandering within this pillar are the spirits. The party aims their weapons, and her face twists into a scowl. She begins to rise in the air, and a dark mass behind her crawls forward into the light, revealing her true body. The woman’s body lies on top of a gigantic spider’s thorax. Where its spider head should be, instead the woman protrudes. Her mouth opens up and snarls, fangs dripping with venom. As she moves towards the party, countless spiders also emerge from the dark and rush forwards.
