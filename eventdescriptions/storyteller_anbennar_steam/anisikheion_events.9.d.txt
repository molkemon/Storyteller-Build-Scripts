At first, it wasn’t noticeable, merely a small sacrifice to gain independence. But it seems that the navy took the inch they were given and ran a mile. Their influence can be felt in every corner of the realm’s government, from the lowest of bureaucrats up to, and including the ruler himself. And now, even the citizens are beginning to notice. Marines standing guard in front of government buildings, the navy being accountable to no one, and the Grand Admiral having so much sway that he can effectively choose his leader. While on paper the state is a republic, the reality is far different.
