The children of Koroshesh are of course great traders, but they are also so many things more. Among them are bold seafarers, masterful architects specialized in hydraulic works, and knowledgeable scholars trained at their Grand Library. The Delta Gnomes communities shine especially in this regard, producing some of the greatest artisans, scribes, and calligraphers the world has ever seen. Our agreement with the Korosheshi League allows us to benefit from the many talents of these skilled individuals.
