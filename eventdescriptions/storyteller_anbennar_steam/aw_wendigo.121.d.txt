In times past, wendigos used to infest the Northern Ynn like a plague, and it took brave knights to venture into the woods to slay these beasts. Such knights used this as a rite of passage to prove themselves as true champions of their lord, earning the right to wear the antlers of their defeated foes upon their helmets. One such group has been known dissidents against the authority of the monarch, and have used their valiant deeds to inspire disloyalty amongst their ranks.
