Bang. The night sky turned green and gold from the glow of the fireworks. In the river, dozens of barges floated slowly at the mercy of its waters, full of light and music. Nobles and adventurers from all corners of the land; diplomats and friends from distant realms. Everyone came to celebrate the first grand ball in decades, to celebrate the restoration of the kingdom. One of the barges was twice the size of the others, decorated in vibrant colors. Our lord was drinking wine, sitting on a throne at the stern of the barge, covered by a pavilion. Bang. A silver firework illuminated the sky, the signal for the speech. "Friends and honorable guests," our lord said. "Today we are celebrating the fulfillment of the promise made long ago, and the birth of a new era for the kingdom."
