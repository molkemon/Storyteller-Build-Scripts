Ruling a great government can be busy work, and the monarch has never shied away from that challenge. As the years go by, however, it has become increasingly clear that the ruler is not devoting the expected time and effort to find a new consort. While it is reasonable to expect a certain period of mourning, that time is now long past. It is high time the monarch takes a new spouse. Luckily, when you are a ruler, the high ranking families of the realm are eager to lend a helping hand.
