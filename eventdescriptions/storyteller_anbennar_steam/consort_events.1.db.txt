Ruling a great kingdom can be busy work and our lord has never shied away from that challenge. As the years go by, however, it has become increasingly clear that our lord is not devoting the expected time and effort to find a new consort. While it is reasonable to expect a certain period of mourning, that time is now long past. It is high time our lord takes a new spouse. Luckily, when you are a lord, the high ranking families of the realm are eager to lend a helping hand.
