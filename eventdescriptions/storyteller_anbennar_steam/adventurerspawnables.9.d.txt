After years of crackdown by imperial authorities for their despicable behaviour the gang of brigands and dishonourable mercenaries known as the Havoral Band has pulled off a most daring heist.  They sensed their days in Cannor were numbered, and as such left their mountain bases for a daring night attack on a notable Esmarian port, seizing several ships before fleeing into open waters. By the time the identities of the culprits and victims were confirmed, the Havoral Band was already past the Dames head Tolls. Their destination could only be the very same one of every wayward soul heading west: the uncharted lands of Aelantier.  While the people of Esmaria breathe a sigh of relief for their freedom from this organised scum, both the natives and settlers of Aelantier must now prepare for yet another competitor in the race for the rediscovered continent… One far more savage and ruthless than most.
