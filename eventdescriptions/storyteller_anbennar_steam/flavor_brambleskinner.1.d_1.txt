The ruler's envoys have come from Brambleskinner. They offer to assist us in crushing Relgakk Blackmoon and in securing the Arrow grove. Should they succeed, they ask in exchange for our loyalty. It may be a small price to pay for our survival, as Relgakk Blackmoon is a danger for all goblins in the Deepwoods. He aims to slaughter us all, and we are the first on his list, as we are, unfortunately, his closest neighbor. What should we answer?
