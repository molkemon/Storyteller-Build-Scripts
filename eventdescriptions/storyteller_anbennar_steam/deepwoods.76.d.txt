Though some time has passed since your triumphant return, a part of your mind still stalks the woods, returning to the hunt each night, as your distractions and duties fade with the sun. On one such night, silently strolling through the still darkness, your mind returns to your fated meeting, when you truly came to know the strength of life inside you, and a sudden chill runs down your spine. You quickly glance back over your shoulder, unsure of your senses, and find yourself once again face to face with the nightmarish monster. And then the fear fades. As you survey his bulging muscles, scarcovered hide, and razor claws, you see the cycle of life, stretching infinitely deep into the past and future, as eternal as it is bereft of malice. There is no evil here, no monsters but those of mortal invention, just a single destiny shared by all. to live, and then to die. A slight smile crosses your face, and the Lord of Beasts speaks. "You have proven yourself the greatest amongst your people, to have bested me. For your deeds, I will grant unto you a single boon, a tool with which to carve your legend upon the stone of history. Choose wisely, mighty hunter."
