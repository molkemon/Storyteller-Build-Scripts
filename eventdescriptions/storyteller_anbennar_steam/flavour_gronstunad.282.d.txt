Our monarch. Our miners in the realm were making their way to a new shaft site, tunnelling and placing minecart tracks, when they ran into a much more valuable vein. The expedition incorporated the vein into our incursion at once, folding it into our expanded complex. Though the first dig site has yet to be exploited since the new vein was so large, the new vein is already proving very profitable, and all our workers have been moved over. Now our mine yields twice as much as it did before.
