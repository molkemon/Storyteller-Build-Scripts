It has recently come to light that fellow worshippers of Surakel are suffering at the hands of the government of a certain realm. Hefty taxation, soldiers engaging in wanton looting, and bureaucrats who care little for their people's wellbeing have led to a vicious cycle of impoverishment and extortion in that province. Whether this is due to inaction or active policies by that government, it matters little to our furious populace, who are already amassing at our capital demanding that our monarch step in. Our brothers and sisters are brave in acknowledging the truth that Jaher was never the reincarnation of Surakel in the face of all those who would back that lie with force; we must be just as brave in coming to their support.
