Please, sir, stop this madness, I beg you. The young woman has to twist her neck in order to speak without interference from the soldier’s spearpoint. A short distance away, her husband is being butchered. The castle has gone up in flames. "You disgust me," the soldier says. "It is one thing to be a member of this people, and to live in thrall to Winter. It is another thing entirely to taint a perfectly good member of another people with your frigid blood." The soldier presses his spear to the woman’s neck, just enough to draw a pinprick of blood. "Agh. If you care so much about my blood, why then must you kill him too?" "He is defiled. He is worthless now." The soldier thrusts downward, and pierces the woman’s neck.
