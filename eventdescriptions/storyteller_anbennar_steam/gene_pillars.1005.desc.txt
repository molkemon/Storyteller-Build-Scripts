Finding a seemingly unguarded room full of treasure, writings, and preserved magical reagents is enough to brighten the day of any adventuring party. However, such joy is quickly dampened when one subsequently discovers the slew of warding spells and runes placed liberally around the room. However, given the thousands of years that the wards were not maintained, there might be a few pieces we could carefully liberate without setting off whatever cataclysmic spells might still be waiting for intrepid looters. But is it worth the risk?
