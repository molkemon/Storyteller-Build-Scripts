For the past couple of days we have been entertaining a guest from the royal court of a distant land, the former home of a noble consort. After a full week of hunting, feasting and gossip with the family, her sister is finally preparing to return home. She assures us that she has had a wonderful time and that the royal family simply must come to visit soon. With delight in her voice she says that she’ll make certain to inform everyone back home about how the young heir to our realm is the spitting image of her dear sibling.
