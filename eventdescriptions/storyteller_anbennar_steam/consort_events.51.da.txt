For the past couple of days we have been entertaining a guest from the royal court of a certain country, the once home of the ruler. After a full week of hunting, feasting and gossiping with the family, the consort's sister is finally preparing to return to the capital of that country. She assures us that she has had a wonderful time and that the royal family simply must come to visit the capital soon. With delight in her voice she says that she'll make certain to inform everybody back home about how the young heir to the government of this realm is the spitting image of her dear sister the consort.
