Hours after Alusir suddenly declared a royal hunt, an ill organised imperial caravan set off from the capital. Unlike many of his other vanity projects, which had extensive and careful planning, the royal hunt Alusir now leads was spurred by a random rumour of a mythical beast he overheard. Without prior planning, the caravan wandered around the countryside until it arrived at a location which Alusir decided sounded vaguely familiar to the rumours.  With more entertainment staff than actual hunters or warriors, prospects of a safe and successful hunt are not guaranteed, despite Alusir’s repeated assurances.
