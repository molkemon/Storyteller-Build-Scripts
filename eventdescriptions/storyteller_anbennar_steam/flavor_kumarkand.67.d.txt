Hours after Alušir suddenly declared a royal hunt, an ill-organized imperial caravan set off from the capital. Unlike many of his other vanity projects, which had extensive and careful planning, the royal hunt Alušir now leads was spurred by a random rumor of a mythical beast he overheard. Without prior planning, the caravan wandered around the countryside until it arrived at a location which Alušir decided sounded vaguely familiar to the rumors. With more entertainment staff than actual hunters or warriors, prospects of a safe and successful hunt are not guaranteed, despite Alušir’s repeated assurances.
