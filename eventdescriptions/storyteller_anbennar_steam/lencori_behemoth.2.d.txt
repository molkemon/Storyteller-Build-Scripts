The harbor of Wineport is no stranger to large ships, but to call its most recent arrival large is akin to calling the local vineyards adequate. The colossal Migrant Ship Rhasdalanai, one of the homes of the oceanwandering Sea Elves, has dropped anchor in the bay in order to resupply. Built millennia ago by the Precursors, the vessel has caused such a spectacle that visitors from across Lencenor have flocked to the city, hoping to catch a glimpse of this naval wonder before it once again disappears onto the high seas. Among the many sightseers happened to be a few prominent members of our admiralty, and seeing such a gargantuan ship in person seems to have caused quite a stir among them. Even the largest of our military vessels look like ordinary fishing boats compared to one of the seaborne goliaths of the Remnant Fleet. While the Sea Elves themselves are unlikely to pose any threat, if any one of our rivals happened to get their hands on a ship of this magnitude, any hope of exerting a naval presence in Lencenor would be all but dead in the water. Our admiralty has started pressuring the ruler to do something about this potential threat, but what could we possibly do? There isn’t a single shipyard in Halann that could build a vessel anywhere close to as large as the Precursors could. Well, perhaps there is. Lament, in Westport. While the Behemoths built there aren’t quite as large as the Rhasdalanai, they are still really, really big. The duke of Deranne might have been on to something after all.
