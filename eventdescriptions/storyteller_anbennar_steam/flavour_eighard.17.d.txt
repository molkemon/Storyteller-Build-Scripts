On and inside a hill outside Darblath, construction begins on Malnatur. Wood sourced from the plentiful forests of Eordand, iron from the faraway provinces of Armonadh, and resources are gathered from all over the country to contribute to the construction of the largest project ever undertaken in Eordand. Most workers come from Darblath, but there are those from all across Eordand who wish to be a part of the construction. Every few days, the construction is interrupted by an attack from nonbelievers, but this does not stop the prison’s progress. Its spires climb ever higher, and its tunnels wind ever deeper. Mages oversee every part of the process, assisting with magic where mundane tools do not suffice. Some workers claim that, when they are digging at the deepest levels, they feel unexplainable winds, as if the air itself is crying out. But this is, of course, superstition.
