For many years, House Sweetcastle has basked in an unparalleled autonomy within the realm of Themarenn. With the devastating Lilac War now but a sorrowful chapter in history, the young lord Vernell III aspires to usher Themarenn into a prosperous future. To do so, he must resolve lingering issues and address the extensive privileges granted to the nobility. Upon ascending to power, the lord Vernell dispatched one of his most loyal generals to reclaim Yearncliff from a rebellious local lord. With Yearncliff now secured, opposition to the Count's directives is expected to be minimal as he turns his attention to House Sweetcastle. Nonetheless, a path of diplomacy may yet prevail. By extending some concessions, we can revoke the old treaty, thereby reinstating Sweetcastle and its ruling house as loyal vassals. This decision will undoubtedly bear longlasting consequences, so it must be made with the utmost care and wisdom.
