As the walking dead rampage across the countryside, those still living across the country are plagued by thoughts of impending doom, of fingernails scratching at the door and monsters crawling from their cellars in the dead of night. The reports of the continued spread of the zombie plague have led many to give in to hysteria or despondency, and powerful individuals across the nation have begun to publicly express a lack of faith in the government’s ability to protect the people from this threat.
