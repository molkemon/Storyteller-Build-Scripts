From port to port, city to city, prosperity reigns. The men and women are free to trade and do business as they please, to profit from their own diligence and labor, and not have the product of their work stolen by the greedy stomach of tyrants. And while those who wish to idly hoard may rear their ugly heads, they shall be stopped by the meritocracy of our system. It is the end of an era, and the world dawns on an age of profit and yield.
