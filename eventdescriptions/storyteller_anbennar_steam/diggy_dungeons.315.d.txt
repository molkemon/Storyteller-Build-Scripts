A shocking image astonishes a few of us. The ores are rhythmically slowly contracting a bit inwards and then a bit outwards. We realize the truth too late. The hastiest of the expedition are already striking and cutting some crystals. Several grave, rough, and metallic growls heavily echo in the chamber. The floor and roof shake, a few stalactites crash on the ground as the crystal ores rise up with their screaming mouths wide open and standing on some pairs of rocky legs.
