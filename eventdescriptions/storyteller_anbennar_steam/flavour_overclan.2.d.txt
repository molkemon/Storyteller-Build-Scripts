REPORT. Due to recent conflicts, the number of orphaned children has increased within our borders. Most notable among this population are Voltoj orphans; those with mixed goblin-human parentage. Certain officials have suggested widescale recruitment of these orphans into the government apparatus, citing historical precedent and the potential advantages of bureaucrats with no loyalties to anything or anyone beyond the state itself. The Voltoj are known to be sterile, which would prevent them from establishing dynastic lines within the administration, something that is clearly detrimental to the interests of the state. Attached you will find several proposals for enacting this recruitment system and maintaining its function.
