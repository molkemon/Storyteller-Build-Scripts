The Amber Princess bows her head before the ritual circle. She breathes in. She breathes out. Around her, the trees seem to press inward, almost as if they are drawn towards what she is doing there. She reaches out her hands, tenses them, feels the bone and sinew within her, and draws the magic close. She tenses, tenses, tenses, and then she releases. The magic slams into the middle of the ritual circle and wrenches a hole in time and space. Spiraling colors shimmer and shift into an image of a verdant forest. She takes a step closer. Another. A very, very, very slight smile creeps across her face. Then she runs. The soldiers are fighting on the outskirts. She intends to go deeper.
