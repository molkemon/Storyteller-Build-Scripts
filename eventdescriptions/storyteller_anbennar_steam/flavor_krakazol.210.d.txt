At first it was only rumours. The lord studying Necromancy? Preposterous. Why would an Amethyst Dwarf seek to defile the glorious dead? Then it became whispers. When confronted, the lord scoffed and said we weren’t drunk enough. An odd reply, considering we are almost always drunk. One could even say it was evasive. Now, it is common knowledge. The entirety of HulazKrakazol is in an uproar, demanding that the lord be given the punishment most devoid of honour: execution. Even the lord’s personal guard would not defend them, and they were summarily dragged to the executioner’s block without trial or argument. The axe falls, the name removed, the memory forgotten.
