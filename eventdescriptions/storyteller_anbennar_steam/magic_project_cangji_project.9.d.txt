At the end of the harrowing period of uncontrolled blazes, mages of the Ashen Soul emerged along with the common people of the land to discover their braziers nearly extinguished. The efforts taken to appease the spiritual powers were draining for both the magic of the Ashen Soul and the strength of the fires burning within the monasteries, and as one grew weak so too did the other. Watching hopelessly as the flames sputtered, the people feared for the first time that the fire would be snuffed out, and their homes lost. At that moment, those closest to the braziers suddenly began to feel a tingling sensation, and their hair and fur stood on end across their bodies. From high in the clouds, spirits descended onto the braziers, and began to gather around the flames in droves. As they joined the burning embers, the fires grew larger and larger, until with enough spiritual energy they reached the same strength as once seen before the firestorms began. And while unseen to many, some close to the flames have claimed to even hear a playful laughing sound as they flickered and danced in the wind. In any case, with the merging of spirit and spiritfire, the possibilities for our homes and people are truly endless. Who knows what we will accomplish with the spirits on our side? The Ashen Soul looks to take this question and answer it tenfold, as they run off to grab their scrolls and quills as the hunger for knowledge calls them once again.
