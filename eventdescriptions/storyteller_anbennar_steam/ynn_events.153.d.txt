A tattered purple cloak flaps powerfully in the wind, a solid but weary antler horse scrapes the ground anxiously, and a tall, broadly built elf removes his antlered helmet to show himself to the ruler's gaze as the ruler observes this heavily armed visitor from the battlements above the castle gate. The elf presents himself as a knight of little renown, eager to swear service to the ruler in exchange for their protection, but his appearance betrays his origin. In the northeastern reaches of the Ynn, at the edge of the Forest of the Cursed Ones, lies the castle of Amacimst. From there hail the Amethyst Knights, the most famous and skilled knightly order in the Ynn, their purple cloaks and bardings invoking fear and respect across all realms of the River. This knight is clearly of the Amethyst Order but, as happens due to the order’s strict rules regarding the spreading of ideas they view as subversive, he must have been exiled or otherwise forced to flee his home castle.
