It is dawn when the servants' screams echo throughout the hall. Even the ruler of the giant is stirred awake as courtiers and soldiers alike storm downstairs to confront the commotion. A wave of gasps follow as the ruler pushes open the door to the balcony of the courtyard. On the crimson stained pavement below are the headless bodies of a dozen of our diplomats, arranged in the shape of an imperial dove. Knights push past the ruler, desperately rushing to secure the grounds as the ruler contemplates a decision.
