So saith the mob: The stage is set for us, the actors. Those who wronged Kimanis shall cower. No longer shall the city bow meekly to some foreign power. We shall seize the weapons long hidden, and we shall make all matters right. Within the heart of foreign garrisons the Hounds’ fierce canines strike in might. Beware, oppressors of Kimanis. For the demagogue and our hand shall grind your presence to dust, and make once broken things to stand.
