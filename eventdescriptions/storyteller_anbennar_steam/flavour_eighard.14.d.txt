As Eighard’s armies push through the domain, our soldiers cannot help but notice the foes they face. Peitar, Gladewardens, the occasional minor fey. All expected foes, but something is missing. One says, "The archfey spurred this war, and yet we have seen not a trace of them." Another says, "Fey are capricious tricksters, and archfey most of all. They would avoid an open conflict, of course, but they would not be uninvolved in a war for their dwelling place. Where, then, are they? Why have we seen neither hide nor hair of them?"
