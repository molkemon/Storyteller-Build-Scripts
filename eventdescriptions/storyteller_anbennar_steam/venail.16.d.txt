Recently we have been approached by the nobility in Soarnaire. They have received word of plans from Venáil to leave their island and migrate their populace to Aelantier. Such a plan would be a great undertaking and would require great resources and expertise. We are in a position to provide these to Venáil; however, we would need something in return. The island of Venáil seems a worthy prize. We should support these nobles in sending an offer to Venáil detailing our proposal.
