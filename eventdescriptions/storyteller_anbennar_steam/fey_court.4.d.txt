The Frumptious Jubilee is not so much an event as it is an experience. Every night and day, the Seelie fey take it upon themselves to make a new delight. One might throw a party. Another might bake a cake. And others may simply compliment your choice of attire today. Regardless of method, the end result is always the same: to leave a smile on everyone's face. If the lord should so wish, we could ask our contacts in the Seelie Court for such a celebration.
