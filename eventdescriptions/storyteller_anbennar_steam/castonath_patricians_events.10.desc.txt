An old chieftain has approached our lord recently. Memories of the brutal siege of Castonath during the Greentide and myriad offenses by the defiant patricians since still percolate unbidden in the chieftain’s mind, the screams and cries of family and friends lost amidst the clamour of war. The chieftain says that they cannot let those cries go unanswered any more, especially now that things have settled down and now that the clan is prosperous enough to survive without them.
