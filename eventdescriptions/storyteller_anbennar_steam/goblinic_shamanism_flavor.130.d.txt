The recent arrival of the Obsidian Legion has incited panic amongst the dwarves, yet in us goblins they bring out a morbid curiosity. Tales of the magical weaponry of the Obsidian Dwarves have been the source of this interest, with axes that rot flesh and swords that can melt someone from the inside, and canon batteries that fire with a power unrivaled by any in the world, all powered with just a bit of magical blood. Of course, we can’t just ask the Obsidian Dwarves to share what they know, for they are notorious for killing or enslaving every goblin they see, so we have sent out scouts to scavenge remnants of Obsidian battlefields for their infamous Blood Runes. The scouts could only find a few blood rune weapons in working condition, but after one of our court mages decided to test them using his own blood, we were instantly able to confirm their effectiveness after the blade melted through our table. With this revelation, some shamans, referred to as Scribblescratchers, have taken to replicating these Obsidian runes. Scribblescratchers will scratch these runes onto equipment and then put them through a frightening ritual where they execute a prisoner with magical blood on the spot, allowing their blood to flow onto the item and empowering it. Similarly to the obsidian dwarves, this practice can even be brought into battle, with these unfortunate souls being called “Scribblesappers”. Though many in the clan are outraged at this new practice for its cruelty, if we stay on the side of the Scribblescratchers, our nation may see a new era of prosperity painted in crimson red.
