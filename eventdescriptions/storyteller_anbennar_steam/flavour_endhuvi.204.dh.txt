Marriages are a key tool in a ruler's repertoire; they cement alliances, improve relations, and secure the dynasty, but for that to happen, a feast must occur. In this situation, you are invited to a marriage between two of the most prosperous and powerful family houses in your area. At this union, the most powerful individuals, kings, merchants, priests, and generals, are all here. It’s said that the real deals are made at those feasts. What do you do?
