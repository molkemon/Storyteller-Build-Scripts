halforks have often been seen as social outcasts, especially in 'civilized' society where they are seen as a grim reminder of the Greentide and other orkish attacks. In orkish society, many are seen as tainted and weak from their human blood. As such many halforks have found themselves as a life on the outside, as adventurers or mercenaries, reaching their own version of success and fortune in these fields; and can sometimes use their wealth to take care of their communities within the various cities of the world. The most talented find themselves forming halfork dominated mercenary companies or regiments, as opposed to joining native armies and other mercenary companies. One such proud company, recently formed, is now petitioning us to formalize their status as a stateapproved mercenary company. What shall we do?
