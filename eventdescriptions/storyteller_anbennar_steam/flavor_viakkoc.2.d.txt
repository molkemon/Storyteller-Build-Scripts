With Nathalaire defeated, the centuries old pirate hub is now under our control. However, we are now faced with the question of what exactly to do with it. Our natural instinct is to simply expel the humans there and fully assert our superiority in the Sea of Follies, but we have recently been approached by important leaders in the city with an interesting proposal. In exchange for leaving the city alone, we would be granted a monthly tribute, and the Nathalairey would provide us access to their global network of contacts, greatly aiding our trading efforts. While this would also naturally prevent us from raiding lands Nathalaire controls and we may be forced to compete with Nathalaire for raiding rights in the waters near the city, it may give us a significant boost to our finances.
