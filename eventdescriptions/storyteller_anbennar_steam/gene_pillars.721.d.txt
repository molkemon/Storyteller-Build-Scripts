Having exhausted the abovewater corridors and navigated all other ingresses, the only passageways left to profitably explore lie in the briny recesses below. This would be simple, were it not for the fact that the entire remaining pillar is flooded from here on down, and it is anyone’s guess how far that may prove. Someone will need to shell out a pretty sum for a mage capable of maintaining a sizeable air bubble to keep our party and supplies dry.
