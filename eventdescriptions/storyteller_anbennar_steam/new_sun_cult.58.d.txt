Our advisors have come to us with an idea. A three-day period where any in the land can come to the capital to present their grievances and complaints to the crown. We will have representative groups organized for each major section of our populace that have complaints, and let them decide which to voice during the Grievance Sessions. Ideally, we will be able to solve the people's issues this way, or at least find some solid compromises. Additionally, it will help them feel as though they are participating in the affairs of the nation, and are heard by its ruler.
