Despite the fear of encountering feral beasts, our researchers were pleasantly surprised when the troll settlement they came to study was actually a fairly civilized and well organized place (at least, compared to their expectations). The trolls were at first apprehensive of the researchers, but upon realizing they weren't (or rather, couldn't) be a threat, they turned friendly, and even showed themselves to be supportive of the researcher's cause.  After a few days studying the trolls and living among them, however, it became clear to the researchers that more direct physical research would be needed for any major breakthroughs. And despite their helpfulness, none of the trolls were so willing to give up their own flesh for this purpose. They did, however, propose an alternate solution, pointing the researchers towards recently deceased trolls and giving them permission to exhume the body for their research. On the other hand, some trolls did agree to collaborate with this more direct research, though they imposed a somewhat hefty cost to it.
