Sometimes, the Steward of the Sorrow can be overwhelmed with the sheer amount of work that is needed to be done at once. For example, a Khet's thirst for Lorentish wine has been a thing long neglected by the Khetarch, as there were more urgent and mischievous Khets to worry about. Now, he's had enough and the price for it is a blotted sun, causing widespread panic among pretty much everyone in Kheterata apart from the Khets.
