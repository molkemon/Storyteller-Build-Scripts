On a hillock, nestled between the trees, sit the long abandoned ruins of a fortress of white stone. Crumbling stone walls and hollow towers make nests for birds, bears, and more sinister vermin. In recent times this area has been cleared, but the ruined keep remains destitute. Our engineers see great potential to restore the fortress to its former prowess. They argue that if we rebuild this fortress, it will greatly increase the defensive potential of the site. Our enemies will break upon splendid white walls.
