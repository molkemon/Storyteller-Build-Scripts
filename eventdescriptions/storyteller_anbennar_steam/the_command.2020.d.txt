Unfortunate news from the Jade March. Ever since our disagreement with the marcher goblins, the wealth of the Jade Mines seems to have dried up. The metals we receive from the mines have been steadily decreasing, to the point where even the minimum quotas aren’t being met. Any attempts to enforce the quota are met with endless diversions, delays, and misdirection from the goblin administrators. As we cannot divert a large force to ensure that production in the mines picks up again, we will have to survive with fewer resources for now.
