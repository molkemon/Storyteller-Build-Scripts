Mithril has always been the most valued of metals, a mineral so rare some would have considered it as valuable as a maiden's tear. However, with the gradual resettlement of the Dwarovar, more and more mithril mines have been found, and the price of mithril has slowly but surely decreased. Although still one of the most valuable metals in the world, it is no longer as rare as it once was, and mithril is now available to the average adventurer, though still at an incredibly steep price.
