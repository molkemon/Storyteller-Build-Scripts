The cold winters of Gerudia are a damn sight different from the heat of Zena. Kiusz complained more than anyone about the chill, likely due to his cold blood. Well, nothing better to warm you up than a good dog. The northerners are well accustomed to the climate, as are their animals. With cattle bred to survive the harsh frosty winters, they needed herders that could chase off the dangerous creatures of the ice. Enter the Varghund. Once simple working dogs, they have been crossbred with the Snow Wargs of the Gray orks to create massive beasts; capable of herding and protecting their charges and fiercely loyal to their owners. With greywhite fur and brilliant blue eyes, we even heard tavern tales of them following berserkers into battle, fangs bared. The only question was. how exactly were we supposed to steal a dog that can bite through plate mail and bring it back to the Menagerie?
