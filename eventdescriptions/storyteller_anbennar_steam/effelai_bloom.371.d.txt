Our shrine grows ever closer to completion. The illusion of peace within the jungle has been shattered; a wholly new spectrum of color now covers the temple, the air gently vibrating with her anguished song. Even our leader has begun to feel the effects from such frequent exposure to our Goddess's influence. That's when they approached us. Contorted figures, moreso resembling tortured trees than our own brethren; these messengers spoke for our Goddess. Little is known of what was said between the leader and her servants, but they returned with renewed fervor. It has become abundantly clear that the temple must be completed, and soon. Though with the continued heavy toll on our psyche, what is to become of us when it is completed?
