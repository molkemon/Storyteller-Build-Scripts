Disaster. One of our rivals has managed to infiltrate our administration and redirect a portion of the funds we’ve provided to the Godswatch. While the money has still reached the same destination, it now appears to have been funneled through their private estates in such a way that it now looks like the donation is theirs. This won’t affect the observatory’s actual funding, but it will give them much more sway when it comes to choosing its location. We must find a way to make up for this underhanded subterfuge.
