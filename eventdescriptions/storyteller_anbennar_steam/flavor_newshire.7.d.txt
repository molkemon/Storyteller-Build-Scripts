As quickly as they could, the Chloromancers put their experiments into practice. Halflings from across the realm were organised to spread this solution as far as possible. Newshire was united, the riots stopping as the people found hope once more.  The process was hard, and long, and resource intensive, but for the first time in years, shoots of grain could be seen sprouting from beneath the earth.  For now, the Chloromancers are focussed on their work, refining their serum and preparing more every day. They'll likely expect a reward.
