Having decided to utilize the precursor as the source of immortality for the potion, our researchers were finally ready to brew. Over the course of a few weeks, the alchemists meticulously prepared the base components for the potions, including the highly distilled potion base, the additives, the stabilizers, and all other ingredients which they had worked so hard to perfect. Then, when the core potion was prepared, the alchemists siphoned the frozen precursor's leftover magic, refined it, and finally imbued it into the potion, making it complete. The finalized potion was brought forward to the monarch, who took the potion and retired to their quarters. The next morning, our ruler emerged from their room, but not quite as they were beforehand. Their subtly more elven features, their slightly pointier ears, and the palpable feeling of magic in the air surrounding them can only mean one thing. the potion successfully gave the ruler a precursor's immortality, together with a few unexpected but welcome side effects.
