The fleet is ready. Droga stands on a wellworn patch of the docks, a lookout spot from which she and Khrosh had so often watched the sunset. Tonight, when she turns her eyes to the horizon, she plots how she will take her revenge on the northmen and rescue her husband. She hopes that Khrosh is not being too badly mistreated as a thrall of the barbarians. She hopes that whatever happened to him in his captivity, she can help him to recover from. She hopes he survived the journey across the sea at all.
