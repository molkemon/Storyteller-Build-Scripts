Even as they set forth into the deeper parts of the pillar, many of our expeditionaries have been surprised at the relative ease with which they have managed to explore the pillar until now. Most have some experience with dangerous traps and hostile creatures, but this has proven to be a relatively tame adventure by comparison. They joke that they will make it out with little more than a few decorative scars, and their laughter echoes down the ancient halls of stone. But then one of them drags a hand across an old block of granite, and they shout, "They’ve broken loose. The mist is hiding their forms. Run, hide, get to safety. Tell the administrator the dogs are out for blood." Time snaps back into place, but wobbles between past and present. The explorers look around and see visions of the animals swarming the halls. It seems so real.
