Somehow, they knew. As soon as they set out, months before they would see the interior of a treasure chamber, the adventurers began to hear their song. Musical, but tuneless. Arrhythmic, but driving. It calmed them, sped them on their journey, and stirred something deep within their hearts. As they fought slimes in the caves, the music played, and they sought closeness with it. As they battled mysterious and murky creatures risen from underground lakes, still the tune captured their attention; they battled inattentively, but with aplomb. It kept them company while they took their rations, and lulled them to sleep when their day was done. When they finally reached the treasure chamber, they greeted the Singing Glyphs as old friends, eagerly awaiting the day they could share their song with the ruler, surely capturing his heart as well.
