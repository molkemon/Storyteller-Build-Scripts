The Oren Nayiru, being hyperradical zealots, call for essentially the complete restructuring of society, including widescale economic reform, a process that will undoubtedly leave most of Taychend’s wealthy mercantile elite destitute or worse, dead. It is no surprise, then, that the rich merchant houses, traders, caravaneers, and slavers have thrown their lot in with the forces battling the Kamrayakval, and have sent considerable funds for the war effort.
