"You will pay for this." roars the Kultist as the hail of gunfire sends tens of his finest gnolls tumbling to the ground, simply stepped over by the silent Azkabari, who content themselves instead with silencing him, with a crack of a gun. The shot seems to silence the whole world, as it echoes like a bell through the valleys and mountains of Bulwar. A bell tolling the end of the gnollish presence in Bulwars famous river valleys.  The implication sinks into the squadron. Every single gnoll was expelled from this place. Untold lives, unknowable struggles and unimaginable suffering. All at their hands.
