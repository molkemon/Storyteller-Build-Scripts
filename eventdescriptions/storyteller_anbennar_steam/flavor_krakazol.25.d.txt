Well, they indeed did not give the land to us for a hefty sum of money and a wide smile. They actually corrected us that they would not give it for any amount of money and they were outraged that we would even consider their faith up for sale. We also learned many new curse words and that High Philosophy does not forbid said cursing, even from their people of the cloth. Anyway, now we just have to get the lands the old-fashioned way and try to make the war quick and painless. We will even try to go around the temples to avoid angering them further. We just hope that given time they will acknowledge that those temples are better cared for in our hands than they ever would be in theirs.
