A chest is placed before the ruler, and thrown open to reveal dozens of weapons. Most of them appear to be well-maintained, and they gleam brightly as the light of the many torches around the hall falls upon them. This chest is but one of dozens, all of them filled with armaments, found after a raiding party happened to come across an old orkish weapons cache. These weapons have clearly been used to attain victories over the dwarven empire of old, and thus, they are nothing less than the holiest of relics of orkkind. Surprisingly, however, the priests gathered to observe the distribution of these precious relics are quick to pronounce their objection to their use. In a scathing tone, they claim the weapons to be profane and demand they be gotten rid of or turned into scrap. They announce it to be Dookan’s will, though they struggle to explain why. Many zealous warriors are quick to join the priests in support of their demands, just as many others are quick to oppose them, eager to make use of these relics. In the end, as with most things, the decision on what to do rests with the ruler.
