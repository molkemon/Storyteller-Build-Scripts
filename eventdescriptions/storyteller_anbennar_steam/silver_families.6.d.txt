The House of Silebor is one of the Silver Families, originating with the marriage of Eborian Goldwaters, head admiral of the League of Free Realms and ally of Jaher during the Liberation of Corvuria, and a Tefori noble woman. The family rules over the Kingdoms of Eborthil and Deshak and have a strong rivalry with the neighboring Silnaras. The House of Silebor is known for its shrewdness, mercantile pursuits, and naval leadership.
