After months of clever diplomacy, careful negotiations, and the odd threat here and there, the Grand Hall is filled with orkish heroes, dead and alive, in the flesh and bones. Many stared at each other with disdain, fueled by ageless old and newly forged rivalries, while others happily boasted to each other of their great feats in battle. The monarch stepped upon the grand podium, before the chiefs of Escann. It was a grand sight to behold; never had so many been in one place since the Greentide. The ruler coughed lightly before speaking. “We have fought each other for too long, while the adventurers have banded together for our destruction, while the rest of Cannor has been stabilizing and slowly preparing to return to Escann. Is this what you would call honor? Is this what the Dookanson wished? No, my kin. We must band together. Whatever disputes or ambitions you hold currently, I ask you to forfeit them, for yourself, for your clan and for orkkind. Urak Dul.” The entire hall, moved by the convincing words of the chief, started cheering in unison. “URAK DUL. URAK DUL. URAK DUL.” For the first time in a long time, it seems orks are united once again.
