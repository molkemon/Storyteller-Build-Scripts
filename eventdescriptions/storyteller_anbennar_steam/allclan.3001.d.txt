Boss. Boss. Brilliant news. After months of careful planning and the expenditure of several bottles of elven perfume, one of our emissaries in Bulwar reports that he was successfully seduced by a harpy spy. While she no doubt made off with the information she desired, that is of little consequence next to what has been achieved. Rejoice, throw your hats in the air, toss your spare sticks of dynamite out the window, for this is truly the best day in goblin history.
