I'm writing this as fast as I can; there's no time to—the ground gave way. It wasn't just a quake. Something alive beneath the stone. Great fissures split the streets. Houses crumbled, swallowed whole. The market screams everywhere. The forges, the gems, everything we built, undone in minutes. Shadows moved in the cracks; not dwarves, not anything I've seen before. I ran. Don't know who else got out. The whole cavern's collapsing, and the air is thick with dust and something worse.
