Hours of grueling tracking later, the creature finally arrives at the large maw of a cave, the muddy ground in front of the entrance crossed with the long gashes characteristic of basilisk tracks, and its depths lost in dark shadow. Chunks of stone, vaguely reminiscent of limbs, perhaps previous victims, serve as a stark reminder of the basilisk's petrifying gaze. Pushing through the thick sense of unease that seems to saturate the air, the creature slowly enters the cave, senses on a hair trigger. Several tense minutes later, the creature is able to make out the end of the cave and the basilisk, sleeping as peacefully as a newborn babe, thankfully. Haphazardly around the basilisk are what appears to be several molted skins; some old, some quite fresh.
