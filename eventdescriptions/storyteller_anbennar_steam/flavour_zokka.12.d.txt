Zokka lies still. Steam rises from the monarch, soft and fragrant, curling in the air like a sacred pyre's smoke. No mourning is heard; only reverence. Zokka kneels. Zokka does not hesitate. The first bite is taken with care, the second with desperate, sudden hunger. To eat is to grow. To grow is to dominate. This is the logic, as Zokka well knows. Zokka remembers. The flesh carries with it what came before. Zokka is dead. Zokka lives. The Once And Future Xhazobain endures.
