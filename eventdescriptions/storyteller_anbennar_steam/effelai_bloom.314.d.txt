As their mages intensify their research on the neural network of the Effelai, they notice it starts pulsating in a rhythmic fashion, emitting a melodic sound that almost seems to mimic human song. They eagerly start to note down their observations, oblivious to the danger they were in. And suddenly, they were surrounded. Seedthralls, emerging from the depths of the jungle, attack every living soul in sight, answering the call of the network and coming to its defense.
