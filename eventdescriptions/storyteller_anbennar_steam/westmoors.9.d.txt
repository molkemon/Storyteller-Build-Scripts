What brings you to my estate, Cannorian? King Eolar Szil Arawkelin sat upon a hill, under the shade of a peach tree, as servants fanned him and served him chilled wine. Meanwhile, the Cannorian knelt on one knee, as his men held crates. A business opportunity. My name is Marlen Moorton, and I have been sent by the Kingdom of the Westmoor, the great kingdom of Northern Cannor. I see, and what is this opportunity? My people have a great admiration for your civility in a sea of these barbarians. In all of Haless, only Arawkelin’s people are the equal of the Cannorian. My offer is: in exchange for joining our empire, you gain the power you need to dominate Vimdatrong once and for all, and bring the light of your culture and civilization to the jungle. The King raised his brow. Bending the knee to some other realm far across the sea? It wouldn’t be popular amongst his people, especially the nobles. And what could that possibly be? Grinning, Marlen nodded to one of his men, who handed him a crate. Your people have honor and dignity, your grace, but they lack a great power of our people, that has aided us on our adventures. And that is? With a sword or spear, you gain respect and authority, but... Mr. Marlen opened the crate, and the sight of muskets drew gasps from the courtiers. But with guns, you can get anything you want.
