Victory. We have, after a significant expense of effort and resources, managed to drive the Leechmen out of the Leechden territories that we control. Most of the vile creatures that inhabit these fetid lands have now receded far beneath the swamp, well beyond the reach of our spears. We will have to ensure our defenses persist, but in the meantime, the rule of our people and its Goddess over these territories has been made certain.
