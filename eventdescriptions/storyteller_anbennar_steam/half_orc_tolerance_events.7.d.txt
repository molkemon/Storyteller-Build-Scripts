In civilized society, due to prejudice received from their role in the Greentide and perceived savagery, many halforks have little to no economic or social prospects. In orkish society, many halforks are seen as outcasts and weak due to their human blood, and a similar lack of prospects arises. In both cases, they often resort to criminal activities to make ends meet. The situation is especially bad in a specific province, where the gangs have taken control of several towns and have taken a multitude of businesses under their protection. One of our advisors thinks it might be a good idea to recruit those gangs and use them to keep order in the province, while others want to send in the military to take control of the situation. There are even voices to use this as a casus belli to simply eradicate them all.
