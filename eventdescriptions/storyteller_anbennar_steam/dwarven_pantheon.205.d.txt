The chaos of the Godwake has not spared even our court, with many within our halls refusing to turn to the Pantheon. Debates and discourse are rife, and court intrigue; dangerous even in the best of times; has turned deadly. The worst offenders on both sides hold no shame about it. Something must be done, and quickly. One of our closest advisors suggests holding a large theological debate, followed by a feast, to calm tensions and help air grievances. Another suggests the practical approach of simply banishing the Ancestor Worship adherents from our court. Neither option will be without cost, whether in gold or in blood.
