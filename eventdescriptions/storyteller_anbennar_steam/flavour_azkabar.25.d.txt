From the gates of Bahar, to the dunes of the Salahad, and all lands between, evidence of our holy work remains. Unmarked graves in the tens of thousands. Still smouldering harpy roosts, and gnoll skulls piled high enough to drown a man. Such evidence of our holy work sparks joy in the hearts of the clergy and the Eclipse cult, who now rest easy, knowing they have done the work of Surakel across the land. The wealth of Bulwar, and its people, sits safe behind the Black Walls, holding our people tightly, and presenting a dreadful challenge to any who would try and trample our garden. Throughout all of Halann, only we can be said to be holy, safe, and pure, through our dedication, our efforts, and our faith.
