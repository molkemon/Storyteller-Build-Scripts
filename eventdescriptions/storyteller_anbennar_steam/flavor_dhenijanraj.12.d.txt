As we begin to put the chaotic years of the Blood Lotus Rebellion behind us, a question remains in the minds of many in the ministries. What of the dwarves? Recently admitted to the caste system but excluded from the upper castes, the recent turbulence of the Blood Lotus has raised the question of whether dwarves should join humans and harimari as rulers and warriors of Rahen. Several prominent dwarven merchants and artisans have made formal petitions to this effect, and the cause of the dwarves is taken up by folk of all races who believe the sons of Peridot and Amethyst to be as Raheni as the rest of us. Shall we extend membership in the upper castes to the dwarves?
