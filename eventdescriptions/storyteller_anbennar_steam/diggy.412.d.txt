In the days of AulDwarov, ErNatvir was the beating heart of the rail system ferrying goods across the length of the Serpentspine. The people responsible for smooth transit were the sometimes revered conductors that managed the economic veins and arteries leading to and from the Railyard. Nowadays, with the Marrhold Tunnel reopened, ErNatvir has become even more important as the first stop in the Serpentspine for most traders coming from Cannor.
