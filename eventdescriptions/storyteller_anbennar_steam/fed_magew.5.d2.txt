The ideals of the Federation were "A land of the people, for the people." Sadly, not all ideals can be realised, and few without pain. Our history is stained with the blood of our own kin. Though many are willing to lay the past behind them, those who but yesterday walked with their heads held low might not. At times, rapid change is necessary, yes, but to forcefully expedite the process of healing beyond reason, or to halt it all together, is madness. Mages are powerful, and ought to be respected. They have the power both to elevate the Federation to new heights and to plunge it into obscurity. Alas, it is not just mages who possess such powers, but politicians as well. So it seems that in our pursuit to preserve the Federation, we are once again forced to fight our own brothers and sisters, all because of a few stubborn fools.
