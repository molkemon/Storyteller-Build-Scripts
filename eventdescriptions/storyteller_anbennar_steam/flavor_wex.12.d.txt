One state conquered by us during the Lilac Wars was the Elfrealm of Varivar. Lying on the Dames ear and controlling the strategic fortress of Eargate, our recent decision had given this nation autonomy within the empire. This had spared us the headache of administering the area, which was the last region to cease supporting Dameria and surrender to us. Some suggest going even further in granting autonomy on account of the region’s elven population, who have traditionally been self-governing. This would, however, place the region's abundant forests and rich naval tradition out of our hands, and may simply give the Munasrevering Varivari more room to plot the return of his descendants, the Silmunas.
