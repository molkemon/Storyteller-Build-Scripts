Gnomes have acquired a distasteful reputation as being "hard to understand," with other races pointing to our penchant for speaking quickly and using nonstandard diction to explain why. Of course, we understand that these habits derive from a lifetime spent conversing with superior Gnomish intellects, but that won't persuade the other races. To address this, the Conversation Calibrator uses the power of illusion magic to create the appearance of speech that may be more comprehensible for their level of understanding, while allowing us to express ourselves without having to slow down to a mindnumbing pace. The pauses between sentences are worth the sacrifice.
