A darling Lencori upholsterer, a Wexonard draper, even a few glass nothings purchased from an Esmari market. the official complex of the Pashainéy Elector reflects the cultural refinement and mastery of the river cities, bulging with goods from all over the Empire. The curtains are of course in a rich Pashainéy purple, while on the Diet office of the Elector themselves, the ruler of the realm, is an enormous map of the Luna Valley and its surrounds, each member city marked out in brilliantly cut quartz. All these things have been won by influence, by the expert management of the Diet's complex politics. Now a power with whom all must deal, the Confederation has arrived in earnest to the halls of Imperial power, and it will not be easily dislodged.
