The crowd erupts a thunderous roar as the captain of the first vessel reaches the opposite shores of Silent Lake. In the coming hours, a steady stream of boats find themselves greeted by the same elated crowd. As day turns to night and the feasting subsides, the no longer sober elves gather around the victorious captain. The crowd erupts one last time as an elderly elf places a wreath of Moon Lilies upon the captain’s head. This year’s Moonsinger has been crowned.
