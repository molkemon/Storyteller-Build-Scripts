When Umorozi comes back the second time, her voice, once honey and thunder, is made strange and hoarse. She is able to whisper halfway across a compound and be heard only by her intended listener. She can issue a command and it will sound in the ear of every soldier within three miles, though she has not called out. The ugly gash which cleft her jaw and throat is now a beautiful seam of silverfog. Umorozi no longer much cares for dates, or sweet foods in general.
