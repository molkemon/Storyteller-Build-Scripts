During one of your visits to a local clan, you notice that despite the rather hot temperature, all of its members are wearing thick clothing. Intrigued by this, you pretend to accidentally grab the cloak of a nearby warrior, taking it off him. What you see astounds you. In front of you are two kobolds, one on the other's back. The rest of the clan immediately disown them, but it is undeniable that they are all kobolds.
