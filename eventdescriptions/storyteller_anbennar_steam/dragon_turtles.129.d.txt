I am not teaching you to make firecrackers. Whatever weak cantrips you know how to cast do not compare to the power of flame, summoned with knowledge and guided with wisdom. I will not have students who are weak in any way. Anger is the product of a weak soul, and this is the worst kind of weakness. Evocation is the angriest magic, and this is why it must only be wielded by the kindest. Be calm. Now gather yourself where you stand, and feel how your presence affects even the smallest of things around you. You suffer under the delusion that you stand apart from the world, and this is what we will break today. Extend yourself. Think about what you wish to accomplish, and then do not think at all. Move. Burn the air itself with force of will. Make the planet turn around the axis where you stand. You are might, you are glory, you are flame. Burn brighter. Burn higher. Good. Now we will discuss your stance.
