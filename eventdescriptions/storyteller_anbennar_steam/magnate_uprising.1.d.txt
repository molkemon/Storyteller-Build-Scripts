A concerning development in their capital today: the Magnates, backed by a private army and supported by Magnates all across the country, have confronted our lord and demanded their abdication. Expressing significant outrage at what they deem our lord’s incompetence and despotism, they now claim that they would do a far better job of running the country than them and demand the end of the Gawedi monarchy. Whilst the outcome of this skirmish in the capital has not yet been determined, its effects are being felt all across our realm as the provinces begin to declare their support, either for the Crown or the Magnates.
