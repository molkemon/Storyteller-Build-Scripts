A concerning development in the capital today. the Magnates, backed by a private army and supported by Magnates all across the country, have confronted the monarch and demanded their abdication. Expressing significant outrage at what they deem the monarch's incompetence and despotism, they now claim that they would do a far better job of running the country than the ruler and demand the end of the Gawedi monarchy. Whilst the outcome of this skirmish in the capital has not yet been determined, its effects are being felt all across the realm as the provinces begin to declare their support, either for the Crown or the Magnates.
