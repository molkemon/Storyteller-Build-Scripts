One may attribute different adjectives to the race of Khets. moody, mischievous, persistent, lazy. Those would not be the words used by the Khet worshippers, who happened to revere one who has been sleeping for a month in the province. He positioned himself on a rocky bed of an important trade route leading there, and his existence there happened to flock in zealous worshippers of Khet. This amassing of people is blocking the land trade in the province as we speak, and merchants would like the Steward to fix it.
