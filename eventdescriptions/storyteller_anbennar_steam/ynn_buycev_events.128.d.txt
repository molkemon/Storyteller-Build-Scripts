In the abandoned Old Rzentur village, they find it. a multicoloured flame bringing light to the dark of night. Pulsating wildly, the scattered unearthly light brings even the most stoic men present to unease. “Destroy this heathen fire at once,” one of the soldiers shouts. His raised voice is matched by another, however. “We should bring it back with us. Showcase what we’ve found and prove our dominance over these buycev and their fae.” The expedition quickly devolves into shouting before the commanding officer pounds his spear into the ground. “Enough.”
