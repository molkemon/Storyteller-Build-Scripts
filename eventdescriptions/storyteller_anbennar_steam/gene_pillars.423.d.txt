Standing above the collapsed walkways, the adventurers solemnly take in the depths of the pit that yawns before them. Tattered bridges, strewn haphazardly across the void, allow for passage into the rest of the pillar. But as one foot falls in front of the other, radiating waves of sickness claim their minds and cloud their vision. Another look, and the bridges are pristine as the day they were built. One daring explorer walks across the nearest span, only for his foot to slip into the darkness as he nearly plummets to his death. Real life and the distant past flicker in and out of view, as the party realizes they will need to try their luck to survive.
