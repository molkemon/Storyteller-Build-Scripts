Alaran's army of rangers, bandits, landless knights and other scoundrel has disbanded, and the area around Vels Bacar was thrown into chaos as they occupy villages, castles, and roads, ambushing defiant knights and robbing the people blind. In Vels Amsto, a ranger named Yarhelval Velenik has managed to unite a large group of these under him, declaring the land he controls as Lesnikpansvo: The Ranger's Lordship.
