With the unification of the five holds of the Middle Dwarovar and the refounding of the Segbandal, it is time to determine where the capital city of our confederation will be. Prominent members of each hold convened in a congress in Seghdihr and, after a short deliberation, the league voted overwhelmingly to formally elevate Seghdihr to be the capital city of the new Segbandal. Though some grumbling longbeards from Verkal Gulan or Hehodovar protested, none are truly surprised; the Citrine City is where the alliance began, and it is where it will always remain.
