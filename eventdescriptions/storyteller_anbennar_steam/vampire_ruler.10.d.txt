All vampires know the hunger, a nagging urge always in their mind. It is something no vampires can escape from, no matter their power and resolve, and eventually they will be consumed by that urge. Some nights the hunger is distant and a vampire can almost pretend they do not feel it. Some nights it niggles at the mind, a small itch, but one that cannot quite be scratched. And on nights like tonight? Tonight the hunger consumes the ruler.
