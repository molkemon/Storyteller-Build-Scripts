During natural disasters, it is all too easy for survivors to fall to infighting as the stress of the situation brings out the worst, basest instincts of mortals. Unnatural disasters, such as the ongoing zombie crisis in the realm, follow the same pattern, and the major enclaves of survivors in that realm have descended to what can only be described as a small war. Bottled up within the relative safety of fortified settlements, strong personalities and mutual distrust on opposing survivor communities have boiled over into armed conflict as the survivors fought, slaughtering each other over perceived slights and unproven accusations. Several survivor communities have been wiped out; killed not by the monsters that lurk outside the walls, but the monsters that were inside them the whole time.
