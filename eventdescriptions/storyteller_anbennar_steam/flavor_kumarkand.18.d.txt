Alusir’s crazy idea had become an even madder reality as Bahari beasts, colloquially known as elephants, clad in fine iron plate armour reminiscent of Anbennari knights of old towed miniature siege towers into battle. But these were not mere siege weapons of old possessed in the dozens and exclusively used in sieges, but mobile gun platforms which made up the whole Ituqatter corps. These socalled ‘new chariots’ towered over the Bahari beasts, their wooden exterior backed by iron plating combined with Innovations in suspension technology created the perfect mobile fortress, providing an excellent elevated sightline from which Ituqatter could shoot upon Karqaslu’s enemies.  Now, despite protests from the treasury and foreign envoys alike, the resplendent Ituatter parades their new warmachines through the capital. No longer shall the chariots of old be seen as relics of the past but harbingers of doom for all those who oppose Karqaslu.
