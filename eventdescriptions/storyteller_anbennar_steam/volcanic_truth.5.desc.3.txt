The magmascryers are crucial to our faith, but we hardly need magmascryers to tell us what the Twin Gods demand of us now. They have erupted into great plumes of fire, and devastated the lands around them. The elders cannot think of a time in the long history of Kanolowele when both of the Twins showed such wrath, and this is an obvious indication that we have grown stagnant. The Twin Gods are attempting to reshape the world in fire, and if we do not wish to be consumed by it, we will have to follow their commands. Everything associated with the old ways must be torn down. the fires of change will spare no one and nothing. What will reemerge from the devastation shall be glorious, but in order for that to happen we must burn for a time.
