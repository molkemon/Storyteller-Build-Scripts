A thousand curses upon this wretched day. Once bustling with miners and scholars, experts of every magical discipline, the grand Dames tear mine of this realm has fallen dark, not even the faintest glimmer of the cyan glow that it once cast to the heavens above through even the darkest of nights. We have dug deeply and greedily, and wholly stripped the province of its most vital resource, that which had cast all other matters aside. Panic and protest rumble through the halls of our elites, as they awaken to discover that our place in the world has grown far more precarious and fickle, for the knowledge of what we have lost. Regardless of what supplants the industry that the Dames tear had birthed, our nation will be much poorer for the lack of it.
