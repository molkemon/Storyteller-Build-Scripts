Goblins have always had a fascination with explosives; the danger, power, and chaos that they produce makes them an ideal tool and somewhat accurate metaphor for goblin culture. The limiting factor in their expertise is the tendency for the inventors not to outlive their invention. It remains unclear if the explosion in the local domain was a direct attack on one of our villages or if they had simply infested the local storehouses. Either way, it damaged much of the village and left several casualties.
