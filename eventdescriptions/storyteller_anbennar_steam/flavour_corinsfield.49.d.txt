The Ynnics still do not quite understand the people who now rule over them. One day they do nothing but smile and grin at you; the next, they have you bound like an animal being sent to slaughter. Then, they throw you in court and tell you to defend yourself; defend yourself from what? What crimes, what allegations? Once that sham ends, they drag you out in front of a crowd, and list crimes that you never committed, and read you hymns for a god you do not worship. Then, they kill you, and they cheer. Such is the story for the one currently on the pyre. What have they done? They do not know; all they know is that the people with whom they had lived in peace now cried for blood, blood, blood. What a strange, violent folk. The flames roared, bringing the farce to a close.
