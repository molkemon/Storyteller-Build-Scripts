The gnolls are traditionally scavengers and get much of their metal supply by breaking down old and unused metalworks or tearing up the metal products of those they raid. This normally takes them quite some time, using pry bars to pull off sheets and metal saws to break things down. These Scrapperclaws are worn like gloves with long exaggerated claws extending along the fingers and a frame running up the arm to the shoulder. The claws have small saw blades which vibrate back and forth to quickly saw through pieces of scrap, and the frame around the arm will strengthen any gnoll that wears it, allowing them to carry more than normal. This has quickened the looting process significantly and has raised productivity in the scrapyards by a great deal. This has made gnolls very sought after by the owners of ironworks and scrapyards across Cannor and Bulwar as very effective labor forces in the preproduction stages of metal fabrication.
