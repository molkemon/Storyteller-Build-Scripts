News has arrived from the south, our long term allies in Moonhaven have formally announced their adoption of this strange new Ravelian faith, and this news has brought great trouble to the court. While many think nothing of it, or else shun the elves for their abandoning what was long held as the truth, others were moved to consideration by this announcement. The Elves have long been thought of as wise and patient folk and would not do such a thing without forethought. Perhaps we ought to examine some long held assumptions and take this chance to once again lead the empire in adopting new religious understandings.
