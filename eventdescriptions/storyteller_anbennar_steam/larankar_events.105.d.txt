Taychend is a big place. Tens of millions of people, and tens of thousands of villages, towns, and city-states, basically all of which are fortified against attack, surrounded by a wooden palisade at the very least. Some wonder why it is so difficult to conquer, divided as it is, but when one realizes just how easy it is to hide tens of thousands of warriors within its confines, they usually cease to wonder. Unfortunately, today, we have been confronted with just that reality. After months of preparation, the scorned sibling has revealed themselves, summoning their army to their banner and beginning their long march on the capital. No matter the outcome of the upcoming battle, there is no doubt. the Siblings’ War is about to draw to a bloody conclusion, and the Empire of Erankar will once more be united, one way or another.
