The Elf comes. That cursed wretch from the Grove of Arrows, riding high with his proud feathers; Elaria Ayeth, they call him. The sapling prince, who marches his kin to our border like we won’t see them, as if we didn’t own every shadow in this part of the Deepwoods. Our scouts lie dead, yes. Throats slit, bodies vanished. But it matters not. Let them think their little ambush means something, that we fear him. Let them come. Blackmoon waits with a thirst for blood. The Elf rides to his doom, and with him, the last breath of Salla Ayeth. We will crush their line, tear up their roots, and feed the forest our triumph.
