In a large room, the party finds a strange contraption that resembles an empty doorframe. As some party members investigate the device, lights turn on and the frame starts humming. Energy and light pour into the doorway. As the party looks into the glowing beauty, a vertical line appears in the middle of the frame. The line begins to split apart, the sound of tearing paper echoing in the room. The line rips open, and a giant eye peers through. It blinks. The sound of rending parchment echoes in the chamber, and spirits of all shapes and forms come rampaging forth from the portal.
