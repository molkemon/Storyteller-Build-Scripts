Dating back centuries ago to Pelodan the First, the Oathsworn dynasty has been beholden to reunite the Eordan peoples and end the seasonal heretics. Swearing an oath to bring back the heretics to the Fey Lords, Pelodan the First lead the united Peitar people on a holy crusade. He failed in his mission, and confidence was forever lost in the Oathsworn, leading to even the Peitar peoples splitting off into different states. Since then, Pelodan's descendants have clung to the past glory and infamy attached to their name, nostalgic of a victory that could have been. The oath still stands, with each heir to Pelomar swearing the same oath to the Fey Lords.
