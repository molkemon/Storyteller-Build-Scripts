The core of The Jadd has been, is, and will forever be the writings of Jaddar, and the truths revealed to him by Surael. That said, there are always further details to be worked out, and the evolution of society constantly calls for adjudications about how His laws pertain to new circumstances. Thousands of theologians and other scholars from across the Empire spend their lives working on these problems and pondering details of Jadd philosophy. But the Divine Herald, the ruler, is Surael's own voice on Halann, and thus the final say in any such debate. Where should we look to best follow His light?
