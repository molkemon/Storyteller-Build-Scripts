It was not so long ago that we found ourselves subjugated beneath the loathsome heel of the one they call Tluukt, the Cleaver of Realms. We escaped from her wicked embrace by the skin of our teeth, but we have never thought ourselves safe. Today, correspondence, or a lack thereof, with our noble friends in Zanbar has confirmed our worst fears. A land put to ruin by gnolls, its people slaughtered and its history desecrated. A clear message to the people of our great city. this is the fate of all those who defy the will of Tluukt. Many now call for us to reconsider our relationship with Tluukt, to fall under her loathsome protection once again. Better to live under her gaze, they argue, than to suffer a fate worse than death.
