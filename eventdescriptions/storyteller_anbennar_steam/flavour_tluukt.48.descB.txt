It appears that making the depths of our displeasure with our ally Zokka a matter of public fact has backfired. The only response from our ally Zokka has been silence; by refusing to mollify our grievances with gold or krah, Zokka is sending the message that their pack has no need of our pack. This is a grave insult in the bloody meritocracy of gnolls, and one that will not go unheard by the gnolls of the Salahad.
