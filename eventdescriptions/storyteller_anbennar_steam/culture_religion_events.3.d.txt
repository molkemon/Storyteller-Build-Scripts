The faith of our lord has long been the cause of consternation among the leading nobles of our kingdom. Over time however their influence has lead to some new men and even some of our older families adapting the new faith. The fact that the rite opens doors in our capital, together with a generally increased visibility of their worship, has led to the rite growing increasingly commonplace throughout our country, attracting new converts from within as well as from other parts of the world.
