You duck into a small guard room and see several automatons spaced evenly around the perimeter; heavily armed but dormant. Once convinced they look safe, you start to investigate the room for other potential exits or even the possibility of loot. As you cross the room, a small collection of gems catches your eye on a small podium near some of the figures. Carefully checking for traps, you start to approach. However, despite your diligence, you must have missed something, and you hear the guardians whir to life behind you. A tense standoff ensues; you and your colleagues face the guardians and ready your weapons. Currently they are not moving towards you, but they seem prepared to attack. One of your team takes a pace to the right, towards the gems, and the guardians raise their weapons further; the colleague returns to their original position and the weapons lower slightly.
