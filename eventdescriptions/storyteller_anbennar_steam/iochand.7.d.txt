Did you hear that we are free now? Free? What happened? The people in Portnamm went and struck it against the Lorentish. I heard they gave them a right whooping. Now Southroy is not a subject of Lorent anymore. I wondered what all those soldiers kept marching around for. Those people set us free, then? We have self-determination or whatever it is called? No more taxes? Well, I heard we still have to pay taxes. Ah well, I got my hopes up. At least we are not a vassal anymore. No more foreign overlords, are we? Well, I heard the Portnammers made us their subject instead. Calling us Ronroy now. Oh. Well, they are a republic, right? Do they have a hierarchy and everything? At least we are free from the archaic institution of monarchical feudalism and its structural exploitation of us honest folks in the lower socioeconomic classes. Well, I heard they have a king now. Restored that old Iochand thing that fell hundreds of years back. They did give us these things, though. What? Paper? I'm afraid I cannot read. Said they are tickets. We have to go to some play thing up in the city. Said they will drag us there if we do not go. Can we go back to farming after? I think so. Good enough for me.
