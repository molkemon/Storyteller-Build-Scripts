It seems we were not the first to make it here. There appears to be some very old writing on parts of the walls in this level, and it is most definitely nothing like the other writing found in the rest of the tower. It does not seem to be nicely engraved. It is rough and unrefined. This was carved into the wall with force, a tough task considering the wall's durability. They must either have been very strong or had some other way of carving it. Somebody must have been here before us. If only we knew what it said.
