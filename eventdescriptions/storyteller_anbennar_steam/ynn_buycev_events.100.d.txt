A dispatch rider from a frontier garrison of the Epednan Expanse rushed into the regional commander's office. Between rushed breaths, he tells of a great Epednar raiding party that has been assembled and begun scouring local villages. "What they can take they steal, what can't be stolen is burned, what can't be burned is ignored entirely." Stopping to catch his breath, he continues on. "Already the populations and food stores of whole villages have been taken by these heathen Buycev. Please, sir, you must organize a response."
