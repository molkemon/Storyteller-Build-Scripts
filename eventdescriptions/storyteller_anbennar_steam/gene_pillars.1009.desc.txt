Many beasts lurk within the Pillars, from basic Hinuifauna to those eerie creatures long corrupted and mutated by the magical energies still lingering within. Yet this recently encountered beast is one of the worst so far, lurking just outside the glow of our lights and torches and striking at us whenever we try to make a move or draw close to the shadows surrounding us. We have to vanquish it if we wish to continue our quest and secure the pillar for further exploration.
