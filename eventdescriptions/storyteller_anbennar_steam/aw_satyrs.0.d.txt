Strange halfman halfgoat fey have been spotted in the realm. While the commonfolk were initially scared, they soon made themselves known as satyrs, and have already made themselves a popular fixture among the settlements, offering hilarious, yet indecent jokes, and music of such a quality that it can only come from a denizen of the Feyrealm. While not a hostile threat, these satyrs care not for the affairs of the mortal realm and who they indirectly hurt in their revelries. Their presence here may cause more harm than good.
