The many minor caves and dwarfsized houses that line Marrhold are perfect dwellings for goblins. Those who have fled from the Serpentspine in particular are overjoyed to have a place like their old home, except protected by our armies from dangers and oppressors. The stream of new arrivals settling into the previously unused lower reaches of the hold has revitalized the tiny hold, a growth that is almost comparable to that of the recently reclaimed holds of the Dwarovar.
