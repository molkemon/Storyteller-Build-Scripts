We have watched with utter dismay as the rebel Bramash has swept across Endralliande, torching our estates and placing our entire colonial effort into peril. Like any such rebel, he has done little but sow chaos, his joke of a Cabinet of Freedom content simply to strip the gilt off the wardrobe rather than spare any thought to using it themselves. It is an embarrassment that we have been unable to defeat him squarely in the field, but it appears his destructive instincts have spared us in the end. Unwilling to face up to the challenge of actually ruling a country, Bramash has proven himself the petty looter we knew him to be. His forces have quit Endralliande on a fleet laden with stolen goods, making for the mainland of Soruin. He hopes to hide himself in the deepest jungles of the continent, where our colonial fleets cannot find him. In the meantime, we are left with a devastated island to rebuild. Such an abject failure to protect our colony has spurred endless recriminations at home; this must never happen again.
