With the great vaults of the High Temples open to adventurers, droves of them have been swarming to the enormous subterranean complexes in search of glory and gold. While many are repulsed by the still potent temple defences, their efforts have been growing more and more successful. Now, as they plumb the deepest reaches of the temples, there are whispers of unimaginably powerful ancient artefacts, grand structures made entirely of Dames Tear, and huge hoards of lesser precursor relics.
