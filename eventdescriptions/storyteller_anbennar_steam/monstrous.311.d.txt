The plight of the harpy is one of the world’s great ironies. Our only concern was for the flock, but in order for the flock to live on through new young, we were forced over generation after generation to allow the creeping tendrils of the world outside into our roosts. Perhaps this is why the songs began so we could ensure our future without losing ourselves. Of course, the world outside is not so bad, and if there was not room for it in our tales before, it was by conscious choice. As our land is enriched by links of commerce and foreign connections that would have seemed impossible a short time ago, the realm stands on the brink of a new age, where as we sing our neighbors harmonize.
