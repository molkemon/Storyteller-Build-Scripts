The continuously deteriorating relationship between the state and the Castonath patricians has reached the point where there is little trust at all for the ruler in Castonath. Every state initiative is suspected at being aimed to contain the power of the Castonath patricians, and tax evasion has become widespread. It has now come to the point that a number of guilds have begun a general strike, demanding greater autonomy and freedom from the overbearing state.
