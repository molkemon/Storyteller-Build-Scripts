Smoke bellows from the shores. Streets lie darkened and abandoned, with a few disfigured corpses lying on the stone of the roads.  Settlements up and down the coast have been left devastated following a coordinated attack by carnivorous plants emerging from the Effelai. Strangely enough, we have the oddest feeling that they were… directed. Like they were trying to drive us out in an animalistic rage. Whatever the case may be, we have been able to beat back the plants, though not without casualty to our own.
