The Uthojbrog makes its triumphant return with the Precursor wreck safely secured amidships, slowly steaming into the port facilities at Yametses. A transfer point for the wreck is already prepared, where mages and engineers alike are all but jumping with joy at the prospect of being able to study such a uniquely valuable relic of the Precursors. Once the wreck is safely ashore, the crew of the Uthojbrog begin the process of rearming the grabbing claw. There are other wrecks to retrieve, and they have been awaiting the completion of this project for years. The ruler waves the Uthojbrog off as it leaves port once more, under steam in the search of more sunken Precursor ships. It was an occasion that the ruler could not have missed, but now that the Dredging is fully underway the demands of leadership can no longer be ignored. The ruler takes in the alien sight of the Precursor battleship one last time, and then turns back towards the port. There is a ship waiting to take the ruler back to the capital, and somehow the journey back feels like it takes no time at all.
