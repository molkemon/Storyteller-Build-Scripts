It was inevitable, really. From the very first efforts of strongarming the university in Yangcheng, to the effective extortion of Yanzhong, few truly expected that Guhe's military dominance over the League would ever end well. Early this morning, guards in their employ marched on the bureaucratic centers in all the League, demanding fealty and arresting those who refused. A counterattack by the few remaining loyalists was able to drive them out of our own capitol, but the cities most under Guhe's influence, Yangcheng and Yazhong, seem to have fallen entirely.
