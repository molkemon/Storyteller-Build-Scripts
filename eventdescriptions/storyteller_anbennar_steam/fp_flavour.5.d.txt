Wild animal and livestock corpses have been spotted with mounting frequency in the province, and, like a macabre art gallery, each victim bears a grisly hallmark that chills blood and quiets heartbeats—they are all headless. With such a particular commonality, our hunters and investigators didn’t take long to determine the culprit behind these decapitated cadavers. a dire hyena. A ruthless ambush predator, dire hyenas possess an oversized maw capable of crushing skulls to satisfy their primary diet of fatty brain matter. Unlike mundane carnivores who stalk traditional prey, dire hyenas prefer dense, developed brains, no matter the matter’s source. With such a keen hunter on the prowl, the people in the province are awash with panic that they or their loved ones might become the next headless victim. How shall we allay our people’s fear?
