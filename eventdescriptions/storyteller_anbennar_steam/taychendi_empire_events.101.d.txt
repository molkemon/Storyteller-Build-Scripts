Undisciplined soldiers and overly permissive commanders have always led to abuses, but with so many troops surging through one of our provinces, and the frenzy of war surging through those troops, it is no wonder an incident with some rampaging soldiers has exploded into an all-out provincial revolt. Across the region, once-subjugated cities throw off their garrisons, guerillas reinforce rural armies in the countryside, and local elites and peasant freedom fighters both rally support for a renewed campaign. If we are to pacify the region once more, a lot of blood will need to be spilled.
