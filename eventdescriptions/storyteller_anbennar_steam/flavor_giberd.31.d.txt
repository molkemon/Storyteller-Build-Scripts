Artificery's lifeblood is Dame's tear. Dame's tear is incredibly rare. As our artificery continues to reach new heights, our demand for the skyfallen material grows higher with it. Unless we want our guilds to collapse below the weight of the demand, we must figure out some solution. Some have already been proposed; from entering into discussion with the owners of the Moonisle to trade massive quantities of the stuff from Ryalanar, to a more short term but renewable deal which commits us less irreversibly. More radical elements call for the Moonisle to be seized for our own gain, no questions asked.
