As the news arrived, few could scarcely believe it. Khets had died before, of course, and Khetarchs were no exception. But what had transpired was far more, far worse. The Khetarch had not simply expired by natural causes, but had been consumed by invading gnolls. The faithful are in anguish at the loss of their gods, though most of us realize that we must move on. The question is, where can we possibly go from here? Do we turn to the Aakhetists and their Bronze Dragon? Or is it possible that these gnolls follow a superior vision under their goddess?
