As we step into the room, we are worried. The plants in this tower have proven to be more trouble than they have been worth, deadly guardians of not so great a prize. As we step into this room, however, we are greeted by perfect rows of identical trees. They do not try to kill us or poison us or anything else, but they might honestly be the most unnerving thing we have seen in the entire tower. They are much like the trees in the reported sole forest northwest of here. This must be where the original tree was made.
