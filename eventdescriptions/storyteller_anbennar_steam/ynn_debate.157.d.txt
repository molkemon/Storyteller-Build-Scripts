A proclamation has come down from a distant kingdom advising the outlawing of all Cannorian thought. While not quite barbarians, these people remain untouched by the blessings of the Ynn, and are as such untrustworthy. We have seen the path that their philosophies have set them on. We have already seen their greed and dishonor on full display, time and again. Any dealings with these people must include guarantees that cannot be circumvented, and any of their ideas must be treated with the utmost suspicion.
