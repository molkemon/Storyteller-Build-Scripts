The east rumbles, a stampede of wooly rhinoceros heralds the rocking of royal carriages, sturdy wheels rolling onwards as the Veykodan elites riding on top lead the beastmasters and painted warriors to war, several rows of Buycev forming on the horizon and threatening to swallow the Moon Field.  A cacophony of shouts, both male and female, is heard as the Ynnic soldiers and settlers hastily retreat to the monastery, trying to reinforce its gate and fill the breaches with whatever objects they can find. A Malacnari captain is leading the defense, and he emboldens their spirits masterfully, reminding them that Calrodiy was equally outnumbered, and he didn't have walls nor a gigantic mound to protect him. In his heart, the captain is thankful to the fates for giving him such a picturesque stage for his last stand.  Two southron soldiers mutter something as they sit by one of the towers' stairs. They weren't supposed to be here, they had volunteered just to see the Moon Field and maybe shoot A FEW barbarians, why did it have to come to this?
