After battling seemingly endless hordes of the undead on their descent, the expeditioners had little appetite to see another skeleton. Irrespective of their appetites, however, a skeleton appeared in the treasure vault anyway, seated on a throne. The first thing the adventurers noted about it was the delicate and authoritative iron circlet resting on the dead dwarf's head. In spite of its desiccated state, it still seemed to grant an immense aura of authority to the dead king. Once the discoverers had waited a respectful moment to see if the corpse had any orders for them to carry out, they noticed its second notable feature. dozens of daggers, in a variety of styles and at a variety of depths, wedged irretrievably into the exmonarch's bones.
