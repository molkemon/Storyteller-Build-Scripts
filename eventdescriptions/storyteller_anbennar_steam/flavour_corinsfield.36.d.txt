Witches stalk us everywhere. With their wicked plans and foul plots, they would see to our total destruction. If we want to save ourselves from these menaces, then there is only a single choice; we must kill them first. Man, woman, or child, none with the vile potential shall be suffered to live. So, Corinsfielders, watch your family, watch your neighbors; for they surely hide amongst us. Only one thing can be done.  Let us make terror the order of the day.
