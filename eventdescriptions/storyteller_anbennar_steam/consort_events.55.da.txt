The consort has been pacing the palace lately, their head hung low, only stopping sometimes to longingly gaze out the window. Life is quite different here, compared to their old country, a nation some call an enemy nowadays. Where does that leave the consort, as a traitor to at least one side of it? The consort shakes their head; such thoughts help no one. Their place is here, in this realm, by the monarch. When doubt besets them, they tend to seek out the young heir, as the innocent laughter and calm eyes of their child usually brings a smile to their face. Today, however, not even this helps. Perhaps it is the weather?
