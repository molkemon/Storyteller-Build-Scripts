With the mechanical great worm eliminated, we can finally enjoy a single moment of respite. We help Stumble get back to his senses and get his help in finishing up the repair of some of the systems for the great central column. While he works away, the party takes a well-deserved moment to rest and patch themselves back up. We are so close to our goal, and finally it seems we have nothing left to do, but wait. Or so we hope.
