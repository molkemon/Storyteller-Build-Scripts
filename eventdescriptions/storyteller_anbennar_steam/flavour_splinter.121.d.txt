Great excitement grips the peoples of the Lakes today. Wild stories of magic, artifacts, strange red people, and adventure grip the nation. A great many people have petitioned the royal court to not only send more expeditions, but many even volunteer to join these missions themselves. While our past aversion to magic was rooted in historical tragedy, perhaps this can be a new dawn for our people? Just as Monarchy was once feared and shunned, perhaps magic too can be reborn under our reign?.
