Less than a decade ago, the gnoll Tluukt earned her epithet "CleaverofRealms" by slaughtering Carodir I, the ruler of Varamhar, in a magical duel for the city of Bulwar, marking the second akalate she has broken through magical ability. Tluukt has opted to leave the administration of Bulwar to its traditional sovereigns, the 12 families, while demanding periodic sacrifices and tribute. This was a fatal mistake.  We could never beat the gnolls on our own, however, so to rival the gnolls in resources and power the 12 families; spearheaded by the Alukatran; have decided to work together to assemble the Naqtazan, the River Alliance. Nobody in Bulwar wants to see a gnoll as hegemon, and so to that end the right words could see a large sum of akalates come together to free their mothercity from the hyenashaped monstrosities that currently think they rule it. There are many and more plans for what comes after, but for the time being we should focus our efforts in winning our freedom.  Tluukt must be almost stupid to believe that the humans and elves of Bulwar will simply submit to a new Age of Monsters. The twelve families have no taste for being treated as minor subjects, able to be thrown around at the whims of greater powers; they are the greater powers. Show the gnolls the error of their ways. The darkness will find no purchase here.
