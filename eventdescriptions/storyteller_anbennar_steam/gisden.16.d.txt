A letter arrives. It bears the seal of the Counts of Gisden and of the Dukes of Asheniande. It is opened, slowly read, and then carefully considered. Gisden wishes for Téinmas to be returned to them. Its people are Arannese, or so they argue, and they should be part of an Arannese realm. But elven blood was spilled in the Lilac Wars. The sons, fathers, and brothers of the people of Ibevar died, and our recompense was to be Téinmas. A land Ibevar laid claim to yet never truly owned, part of the Forlorn Vale. Gisden is our ally. Would such an exchange be worth the pain and discontent our people would surely feel?
