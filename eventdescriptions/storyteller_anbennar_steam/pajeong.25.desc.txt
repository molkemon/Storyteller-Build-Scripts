While the elite of the Hwalbaek descend from well-trained noble houses, they are not ignorant to the truths of war. When push comes to shove, numbers will always be important. Thus, they have been worried about the amount of levies that they have access to in cases of a military emergency. They have therefore petitioned for the permit to conscript the rural farmers of Danggun, training and equipping them in preparation of a mass recruitment in times of war. This will unfortunately divert their workforce away from their farmlands, but denying this request will cause the Hwalbaek to be rather upset.
