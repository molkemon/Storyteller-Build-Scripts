A strong prosperous nation is often the benevolent patron of their relevant religious institutions, but with the rise of zealous behaviour in our ruler the people have come to look at Corin's ways with their own intepretations of our official dogma. Many selfproclaimed priests of Corin, often selftaught, have appeared across our country, preaching her good word. While many are no doubt of pure intent, these independent preachers can border on misintepretation, or worse, heresy, or even rile the masses and set them against our rule.
