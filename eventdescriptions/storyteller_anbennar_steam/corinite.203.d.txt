A strong, prosperous nation is often the benevolent patron of its religious institutions, but with the rise of zealous behavior in the ruler, the people have come to look at Corin's ways with their own interpretations of the official dogma. Many self-proclaimed priests of Corin, often self-taught, have appeared across the country, preaching their good word. While many are no doubt of pure intent, these independent preachers can border on misinterpretation, or worse, heresy, or even rile the masses and set them against the rule.
