Standing on the shore, it is possible to see the Kiamoa in the distance, no longer the great barrier it used to be for the people of Insyaa but instead only a memory of itself. There, present, affecting their lives, but no longer real. And beyond it, a world they could never have considered. A world of strangers, some like them, but others so strange they have no words to describe us. We wear clothes they have never seen, sail ships they cannot match, and bring guns and magic they have never fought. How else can they respond but with wonder and fear in equal measure? The Landslide Infantry are a fascinating response to the world beyond their borders. Not only have they shown themselves to be able to adapt to magic before, but now they are adopting our foreign guns and making themselves indispensable to their leaders. If they did not have so much else to worry about, perhaps they would stop this potential new power in their court, but they do not seem to have the luxury of throwing away useful tools against what they see as a threat.
