Some days have passed and with them a great load of rock and sand are falling down to the strikes of our miners' picks. Today, with another wall crumbling to dust, a new passage has been revealed. The open space shows us a bigger darkness where the tunnel divides on what seems to be a downward bifurcation and, in front of us, a rock ledge is facing another of its kind. A walkway can be built with the appropriate resources and effort to create a forward path.
