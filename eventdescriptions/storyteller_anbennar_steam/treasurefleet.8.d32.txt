A shining and shifting rainbow glimmers above the Gate of Lights as the Treasure Fleet passes under it, and colored explosive fireworks are deployed in celebration of the decennial visitation of the Fleet. The citizens of the realm have always gone out of their way to welcome their kin from abroad, but with the wealth of Aelantier now flowing into the realm, the magnificence of the festivities is unparalleled. With the ruler's ships now frequently visiting the Fleet's homeland, many have taken to view the actual commerce of the Fleet as a formality, and instead the entire city is in a season of holiday until the time comes for the Fleet to set sail again to other ports.
