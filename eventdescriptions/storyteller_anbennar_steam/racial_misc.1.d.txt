Your new heir is a halfork. Halforks are a result of a union between humans and orks, or in rarer cases, between elves and orks. Initially, most people looked at halforks with disgust or pity because they combined the worst parts of their parents. humans feared their menacing appearance, while orks despised their weakness. The fact that most halforks during the Greentide appeared as an outcome of violence from either orks or humans did not improve their reputation. But, as times changed, views on halforks changed too. By the time the Age of Artificers came, relations between humans and orks had improved, willing unions between them had become much more common, and halforks had come to be known to embody the best of their parentage.
