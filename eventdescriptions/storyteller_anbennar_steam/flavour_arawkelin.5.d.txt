Arriving with the latest batch of trading ships from Feiten come a number of High Philosophy scholars. It seems they have a particular history in dealing with the conversion of individuals of Naamjyut culture to High Philosophy. As we have acquired a number of provinces from Pinghoi with this culture, they are offering to help us with converting them to High Philosophy, at no charge, as a thanks for ridding them of the Dread Queen of Pinghoi.
