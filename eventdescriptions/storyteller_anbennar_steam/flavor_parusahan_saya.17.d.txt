After a string of tense missives sent back and forth between parties, it seems that negotiations with the Vizier may finally reach a common ground. The advisor sets out personally to deliver the final treaty, and the court within the realm breathes a collective sigh of relief as the nightmare of diplomatic tension looks to come to an end. Two days later, the advisor's head is returned to us in a burlap sack. We have tried to be reasonable. It appears this will no longer be possible.
