The Cleaver of Realms is cloven in twain herself, and her vicious kin has been chased from their lairs. Thanks to our mighty warriors, the mountains and vales of the Šad Sur are finally freed from under the dread shadow these fiends have cast for far too long over Surakel’s Garden. Now this harsh yet strangely beautiful land, blessed by an abundance of Surakel’s fiery light, can be reclaimed by its rightful inhabitants. The valiant Šadnatu, once scattered to the four winds, may now return to their ancestral home and resume their sacred duty to ward the verdant lands of Surakeš against malign invaders from the southern desert.
