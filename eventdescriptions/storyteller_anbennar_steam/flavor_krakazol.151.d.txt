Tracking the elusive kirin is no easy task, in fact, it is quite hard. Sightings are rare, tracks and traces even rarer, and people who have been up close? Forget it. Where many would have stopped in frustration, the lord continued, fueled by the urge, the call, the need to challenge ever greater foes in search of glorious death. But in order to die, they must find it first. And then prepare accordingly so that it won't escape, or even worse, kill them with its bursts of lightning. There is no glory in death from afar.
