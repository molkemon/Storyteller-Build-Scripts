One night, as our great lord sits in their garden pondering the state of their nation, they end up considering what exactly makes a good diplomat, and how a single person can smoothly charm a guest no matter the interlocutor before them. As our lord rises to leave the garden, they see a figure sitting on a different bench some distance away. The figure is old and wrinkled, and as our lord approaches they see that the figure is emitting a faint blue glow. Our lord walks up to what they recognize as a spirit and learn that it is in fact one of their own ancestors. The ancestor spirit beckons our lord to sit down and have a chat. Our lord accepts, and as they talk about many things, the ancestor offers to lend their wisdom for a time so that they can better themselves. Specifically, the ancestor worked as a diplomat and offers to teach these skills to our lord, who joyously accepts.
