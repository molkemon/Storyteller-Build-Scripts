Youbing Ma was a name that would one day be hailed as a founding father of Yingzhen. One day, at least. Today, though, his accomplishment would have to remain hidden. His slaying of the ruler of that country was masterful, and as much as he would love to take the credit he was due, the entire point of subterfuge was, after all, to be subtle. No, he would have to make do with the generous reward he would soon be given.
