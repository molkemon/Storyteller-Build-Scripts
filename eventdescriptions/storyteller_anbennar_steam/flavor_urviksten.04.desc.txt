In the city of Westgate lies the tomb of our first Castan, the first Ebonfrost, who conquered this land long ago. That we have been able to secure this place alone is testament to our resilience and determination in the face of adversity. But here amongst the tombs of our ancestors lies one of the greatest lost relics of the Era of Black Ice: The Ebonfist itself, the gauntlet and weapon of our founder, entombed here as his empire fell around our ears. Though time has not been kind to it, its recovery represents to many the first true sign of victory in our reconquest, and a physical reminder of why we fight.
