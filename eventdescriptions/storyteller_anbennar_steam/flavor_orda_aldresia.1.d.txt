The Orda Aldresia, meaning Aldresian Order, was founded after the Aldresian Crusade, when the knight Aldresia led valiant warriors against the undead hordes of the White Pestilence, driving the crusade into the heart of Corvuria and defeating the lich and former acolyte of the SorcererKing, Cancrec the Defiler. Since then the Orda Aldresia has safeguarded Cannor from undead and other evil threats using the highly secretive art of Diranbe, bladedancing. the ability to cast spells with a blade as if it were a wand. The Order was also instrumental in helping found the Empire of Anbennar in twelve twenty-one, and eventually turned into the personal bodyguards of the emperor, who they saw as the embodiment of justice and fairness in Cannor. Obviously that has not always been the case, and the order stagnated from chivalrous knights saving all of Cannor to token bodyguards for decadent emperors until now. The High Rectorate has made Aldresia the first ever nondeity saint in the Ravelian Church, and the order has shifted its focus as vanguards of the Ravelian faith.
