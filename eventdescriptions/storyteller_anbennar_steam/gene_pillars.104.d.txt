The destruction that has been caused by the party’s advance within the pillar has triggered some sort of ancient defense mechanism. As the party continues to explore the damp corridors of the facility, suddenly an alarm blares to life and a number of primitive security constructs lumber out of their deployment zones. Several of them are armed with long, sharp blades in place of arms, and they constantly emit clouds of the mist that powers the Kiamoa as they move. This quaint feature has led these constructs to be dubbed ‘the Mist Brigade’ by the few who have survived encounters with them in the past. Currently, they however do not seem interested in sharing this diverting historical tidbit with the party. The constructs advance, each brandishing some sort of deadly weapon in the party’s direction.
