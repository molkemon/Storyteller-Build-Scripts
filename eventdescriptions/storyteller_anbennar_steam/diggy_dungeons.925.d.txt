Trusting the silk to bear their weight, the expedition began heading over the chasm on the web. However, about halfway through, the worst happens. Rather than the rope itself snapping, the web seems to unstick itself from the places it was anchored. All those on the bridge at the time fall to their death screaming. And as if that wasn't enough, it seems like the screaming has called something up from the chasm, and now shuffling and scraping noises are being heard coming from the depths.
