Day... I don't know anymore, if I am being honest. The last few weeks have been me merely guessing the days, but today I have decided to do away with the charade. They are all dead. Everyone except me and Darran. I sent him back with this log so there is a record of the expedition, and because he was afraid. I am not. This whole jungle, it is alive. The entire domain is some kind of... I don't know what to call it; a massive network controlled by a single being far grander than even the dragons of yore. Perhaps even the gods, if there are any anymore. It controls and helps people, more strongly the closer you are to its heart, and its origin... it is a fallen city, sunken into the ground, roots shattering through the roofs like daggers as if reaching out to help. It is beautiful, really. A sea of gemstone-glimmering plants. But you must stay away. This jungle does not like outsiders. I want you to stay away. I need you to stay away. Please. Darran, if you read this, keep going until you reach the sea, and book a ship back to Brontay immediately. The ruler whoever is in charge must know that it is not safe. It will never be safe. Not for a colony or for any other expedition. You know what happened to the others; I am sure from the other logs you will be descriptive in your retelling of their fates.
