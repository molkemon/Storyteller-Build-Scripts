With the passing of the Medaka, the Satrapal Council of Imbushid has convened to decide who shall inherit the throne. Our two candidates are the Efergezmu of House Ndarumo, pious servants of the cult of Mbenilitye, and the Medawor of House Babhet, owners of the Imbushid gold mines and close kin to the nowdeceased richest family of Imidkis.
