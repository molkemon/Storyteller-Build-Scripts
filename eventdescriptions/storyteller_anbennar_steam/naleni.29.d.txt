A disobedient Odanches stands before the throne, dragged there by their assigned Siren commander.  The Siren reports that the Odanches knowing and willfully preyed upon a ship that had paid its toll dues to the state, and thus was not permitted to be pillaged under their privateering license. The Odanches merely shrugs, and offers the quite considerable plunder to the Queen in exchange for a pardon.
