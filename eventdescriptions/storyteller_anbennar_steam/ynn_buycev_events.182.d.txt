The ruler has come back from the hunt after two weeks, limping, drenched in blood but victorious. The ruler regaled the congregation assembled at the capital with how they tracked down the beast and interrupted its feeding. How it then set upon the ruler in a lightning-fast attack for interrupting it, leaving a large dent in the ruler's helmet, which they proudly presented to the congregation as a badge of honor. Then in fine detail how they traded blow for blow with the wendigo, crippling its leg and being ferociously struck in the chest in return for maiming it. The ruler sliced a digit off of a claw, hurting it to the quick and causing the beast to spurt blood and yowl in agony. Eventually the ruler won the battle as the wendigo swiped at the ruler; it was left open, they each struck each other, however, the ruler’s heavy armor protected them from the worst of the wendigo’s savage blow, allowing them to slip their sword under the beast’s rib cage and finally put it down for good. Now that the beast was dead, the ruler collected the claw and made a pendant from it. Never to miss a good opportunity, our ruler ensured that their account of the battle would be distributed through every town in the realm, with an artist rendition of the dented helmet and claw pendant included of course.
