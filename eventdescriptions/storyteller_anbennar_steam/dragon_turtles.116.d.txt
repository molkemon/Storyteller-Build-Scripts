I may be blind, but I am not deaf. My ears work just as well as they ever have, and what I hear are the cries of a people who plead for deliverance from their tyrannical and unjust government. Surely, you have heard them. If not, perhaps it is you who is deaf. For a long time, I have tried to avoid intervening, with the belief that you would determine a way to right the ship. But as corruption mounts, I am forced to conclude that either you cannot or will not see justice done. The Consensus has mechanisms for cases like these: the only ones who may try a nation are the Judges, and the three of us have agreed that your case is grave enough that we are invoking our right to a hearing. I have also tried to remain neutral, but it has been trying in a way that taxes me greatly. All I hear are tales of your realm’s petty corruption, cruelties, and simple unfairness. We all have, and this is why the Consensus must intervene. You will come before the court, and you will explain yourselves.
