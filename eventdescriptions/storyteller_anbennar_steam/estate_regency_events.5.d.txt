It is said that burghers only care about money. As long as money is abundant, they are happy and will not cause many problems. At least, that is what we thought. Our lord and their faction have used their power to further advance some shady businesses while in power. Smuggling is nothing new to us, we know it and it would not be the first time we turn a blind eye to this, but this time these illicit moves have irritated another nation, and they might use this incident as an excuse to declare war on us and get some compensations. Unfortunately, they are not willing to negotiate.
