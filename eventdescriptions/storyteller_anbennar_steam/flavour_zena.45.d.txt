The hold of Krakdhûmvror’s most impressive magical alloy is a versatile metal imbued with the characteristics of ice and freezing water. This is especially exotic to the people of Horashesh’s steamy climate. Their sacred and secret Icecraft is used within Frozen Forges to channel the biting cold of the northern winds into a magical energy that allows the hold's IceSmiths to create IceSteel. With swords that cause frostbite, armour that can unfreeze and flow like water, arrows that shatter into rending shrapnel on impact, the applications are endless. Azaly is interested in using IceSteel for a more utilitarian purpose, a concept that the dwarves of Krakdhûmvror much appreciate. Compared to the grand marvels of the rest of Dwarovar, the halls of the Frozen Hold contain little pomp and circumstance. This particular style of architecture is known as Orv Zana, or ‘Cold Stone,’ allowing buildings to focus on defense and survival. As the realm expands to the northern deserts of the Salahad and its colonies closer to the arctic south, the ability to combine IceSteel into buildings to both cool and insulate is a challenge the Quartz Dwarves are eager to meet.
