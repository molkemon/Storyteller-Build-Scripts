The expedition's scouts have signalled the rest of the troops to stay back in order to investigate a strange noise, a desperate cry that sounds almost civilized. Carefully stepping forward, they pass through several small caves, the sounds getting louder with each cramped passageway. Some time later, they discover a dimly lit room and notice a lone kobold next to what appears to be a wall of muted rainbow cloth. Staying cautiously still, the scouts make out the wall to be a stone shelf of various types of underground plants, mainly moss. The kobold seems to be fully engrossed in moving each one to a different spot from before, then onto the next one, thinking for a bit and screeching as it undoes its previous movements. This sorting of moss leaves it completely oblivious to the presence of their troops, who are now easily within shooting distance of the creature. What shall they do with this strange specimen?
