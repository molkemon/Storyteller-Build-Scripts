There is a great crash and the main gate of Hagstow is battered down. The wards that seemed to strengthen it have been to no avail. The knights of the Young Dragon charge in, and those forces left to guard this final line of defense are battered away. The storming of the Black Tree’s stronghold has begun. With the consent of the Covenbladic government, the Young Dragon has called out the Black Tree chapter for their crimes and declared the Black Tree to be officially no more. They had hoped for perhaps the first time ever for a peaceful arrest, but the dark hearts of the Black Tree compelled them to resist till the end. Defiant with their last breaths, the stink of their evil will rot with their corpses.
