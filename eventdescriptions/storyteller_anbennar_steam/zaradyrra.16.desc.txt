Which Ashhana dares forget what saved us from the demonic hags and their vile blood curse, breaking us from our cycle of darkness? It was the music of nature; the soul song brought about by natural music backing the desperate voice of a lone warbard. Today we bring that venerable tradition to life. today we show forth that even the stones will cry out the glory of the Zakytzkaras. And amidst their song and the Great Cycle, we will build the three hundred thirty-third Empire in this, our beloved land.
