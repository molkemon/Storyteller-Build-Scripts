Yusshela, the Celestial Dolphin, is a blessed and powerful Araizary, who has historically granted great boons and blessings to those deserving. Today, the monarch is going to become one of these fortunate individuals, as a reward for the continued reverence and worship of the Araizary in Khugra. The monarch enters the sea alone, as the constellation of the Celestial Dolphin shines brightly in the night sky in the moonless night. The envoy of Yusshela arrives quickly, a great octopus made of transparent starlight. The cephalopod approaches the ruler and extends its eight arms to embrace them. The monarch expects to meet the usual viscous and cold touch of these animals. Instead, a warm and comfortable feeling overcomes them, as the previously soft light that the creature emitted becomes blinding. When their eyes open, the beast is gone, only the light of the evershining stars breaking the darkness. The monarch swims back to the shore, where royal advisors await eager to listen to their retelling of the experience. The ruler feels the power of the boon flowing through their body, and the changes in their personality are evident. The monarch has received the Blessing of the Octopus, a boon of great cunning.
