To the venerable Overboss and parliament of the Overclan, as you are no doubt aware, we dwarves have called the Serpentspine home since time immemorial; though sadly not all of us are able to live within our ancestral holds. One such hold is Shazstundihr, the Marble Gate, which lies not too far from your territories. Many of the dwarves currently under your rule can trace their lineage to this hold, and few would deny that it is our rightful claim to dwell within this hold. We believe that you would agree with this, as your state has thus far been receptive to the interests of the Copper Dwarves. As such, we formally request that you act in support of our rightful claim over Shazstundihr, and do what is within your power to not only reclaim the hold from any illegitimate squatters, but to then entrust its operation to its rightful Marble Dwarf inhabitants. We of course remain your loyal and devoted subjects, and shall remain so upon the completion of this most righteous task. Additionally, our gratitude for your aid shall be considerable. We believe you know what is the correct decision, and eagerly await your response.
