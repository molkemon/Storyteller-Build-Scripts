‘Action begets action. Life creates life, death unto those who seek death.’  These are just some of the premature eulogies the traitors chant to each other. Sickening in their naivete, their attempts at philosophy that paw at meaning and yet only reveal the absence of thinking in those who have risen against their destined rulers.  Some see themselves as the last remnant of the cultures being stamped out, others evoke claims to being the truest expression of Heartmen ideals, and finally, some emblazon the Wyvern and Moon in equal measure for the land of origin. They are all united in their willingness to endorse the attack on the Heir, though, and for that, The Regent commands death without exception.  They can stand united, die united, or shatter as they please. This can only end in their deaths, though, or the death of Wyvernheart itself.
