Moving through the base of the Gene Pillar has been slow going, due to extensive flooding inundating several rooms with water. The party continues on, pressing past swirling pools of dangerous currents, until they arrive at what looks like a generator of some sort, spitting out vapor resembling storm clouds into a series of pipes which lead further into the structure. Suddenly, the water level shifts, and one of the generators is fully engulfed by the sea. The machine sputters, sparks flying across the chamber as it makes a variety of clanks and groans.
