The journey back was as uneventful as setting out. Maybe even more so, without the excitement of a possible whale hunt to look forward to. With little else to do, the monarch thinks back to the conversations they had with the quartermaster on the expedition. They were responsible for keeping inventory of the butchered whale, had conducted many experiments on various parts of the beasts. One such substance they discovered from these experiments was ambergris, which they found had a pleasing odour. Could this material be processed or sold as a sort of incense?
