Despite everything, an ember of hope still burns in our hearts, putting a fire into our bodies that pushes through our burning legs and failing lungs. Even so, not all of us are lucky enough to outrun the horde of spiders. Some stragglers are simply swept under the tide of arachnids, while some others burn out and fall limply to the cavern floor, every last iota of their energy depleted by our bid for salvation. Most of the remaining survivors push onwards as fast as we can reasonably maintain, but our escape is not without losses.
