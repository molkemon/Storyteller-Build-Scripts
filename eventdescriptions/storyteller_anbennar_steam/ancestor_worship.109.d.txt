Gimron Lawgiver does not have any exciting stories written about him. Still, he is remembered with great reverence all the same. Gimron led the rewriting of the Foundational Laws of the Dwarovar in the wake of the War of the Bloody Gem, in an effort to mend the deep wounds that civil war had wrought. Every dwarf has duties and rights that no king or clanhead can take away from them in pursuit of pride or glory. Real justice is not just punishment; it is healing.
