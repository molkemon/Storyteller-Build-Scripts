A small stream of water trickles down into the next cavern. Its walls are filled with a yellow, vaguely luminescent fungus, its shape reminiscent of Serpentbloom. As an adventurer comes closer, a cleric intervenes: "Halt," he says, "we don't know if this is dangerous, let me investigate." After a short time, the cleric returns to the troop. "It is edible," he declares. Soon the evening meals contain this fungus as a staple, and the troop has stocked up on supplies.
