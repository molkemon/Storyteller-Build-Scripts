While the KhetGoblins are generally known to be lazy and lethargic to preserve their powers, in rare cases, they have been shown to hold great fury. Recently reports have been coming in of servants all across the realm disobeying the commands of their KhetGoblin masters and paying the ultimate price for it. While doctrine is not entirely clear whether a KhetGoblin killing a servant in a fit of rage is to be considered part of a goblin's ascension, the fury of the KhetGoblins has become legendary across the realm, and most will take any measure necessary to avoid angering them.
