The Yarikhoi people stand at the head of a terrifying monolith known as Kalsyto, but they are not a people who can easily coexist with one another. As word of our expansion into the mountains of Gozengun reaches the lakes, and tales of our strength passes through traders braving the plains, tensions within Kalsyto have begun to rise. Arguments between fellow states, disputes about how to handle the growing threat, and the scheming of those who believe democracy to be nothing more than a farce grows throughout the lakes. Word has come back that Kalsyto struggles underneath the pressure. Their fear of magic has spurred them to rash decisions and risky political gambits, destabilizing the region overall and causing each region to trust their neighbours less and less. Soon, the time will be right for us to stop Kalsyto and their magical oppression for good.
