Despite the many dangers we have faced up until now, something we have yet to stumble upon are actual functioning security features. Our luck seems to have run out on that front, however. As we enter the next chamber in our descent, a number of lights start to flash and some sort of magical device activates, unleashing a torrent of flame in our direction. We barely managed to duck for cover behind some rubble, but now we find ourselves pinned behind this cover. What should we do?
