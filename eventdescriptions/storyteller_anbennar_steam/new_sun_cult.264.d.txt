Our adventurers were gone only a few days when we received a Mašnsih emissary, who claims to be from the szalArdeth. Her tanned skin is white with fear as she tells the monarch that the discovery was nothing other than Amussu's tomb. This ancient GodKing once wished that he would be the only one to have the power of the Wish, before a horde stormed from beyond the Serpentspine, apparently immune to genie magics as they slew his rivals. He was soon the last GodKing of Bulwar. However, he could not rejoice. Because he did not have the time to bind another Genie to his will before the enemy stormed his palace. But when he tried to utter his last wish, he found that he was mute. He retained the power of the Wish, but he was unable to use it. When the enemy found him, they tried to kill him, but he simply would not die. His wish had bound him to life, forever living with the power of the Wish, but never being able to utter one ever again. The new rulers of Bulwar buried him alive at the edge of the Salahad desert, where he remains until this day. The szalArdeth have guarded that tomb for millennia, but apparently the meteor opened up a passage into it they never knew existed. She implores us to recall our adventurers; but just as the monarch takes up a quill to sign such an order, a rumble fills the air, and the ground quakes. The ruler and Mašnsih look at each other with horror, even as the monarch scribbles the order and passes it to their secretary. Is it too late?
