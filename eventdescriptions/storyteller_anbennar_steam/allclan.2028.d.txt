Boss, we got a report that during some routine road construction in a certain province, the workers found a cave filled with some weird creatures. We did not get much into detail before some builders came hammering through, suggesting that we exterminate the creatures so they could put the cave to use for our infrastructure. But as quickly as the builder came by, a local dropped from the ceiling offering a counteroffer of turning that cave into a hunting spot and offering exotic food to be sold from the slain creatures. With which proposal should we go?
