The ownership of Colltinn has been a contentious debate between Pelomar and Sidpar, with both sides only agreeing that a decision must be made. This parcel of seemingly worthless land is nonetheless important to Pelomar, as they are unconnected to the venerated Domandrod. Sources within Sidpar whisper that they be willing to give Colltinn to us. Sidpar has struggled against the Summer Court realms, and would do well to gain an advantage against them. Should we offer them a deal, offering them support in eradicating the Summer heretics of Dearktir? Or should we simply demand the land, in their weakness they may not be willing to turn down any alliance with us.
