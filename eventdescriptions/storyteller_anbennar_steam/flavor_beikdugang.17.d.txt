Our people are descendants of the Triunics, from far to the northwest across the vast ocean. We are proud of the traditions we have inherited from them; we have no tortured, perfidious eunuchs like in Tianlou, and we love the sea, which brings us its bounty and wealth. However, we cannot enjoy the sea because Danggun stole our northern coast. They still cling to our stolen lands thousands of years after retreating from the rest of Yanshen, but they are vulnerable now. Danggun is wracked with instability, and now our long humiliation shall come to an end.
