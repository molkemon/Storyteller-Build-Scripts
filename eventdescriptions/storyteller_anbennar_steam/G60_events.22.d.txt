The monarch peers over the starboard of their royal transport to the great Kheions that now proclaim their rule as legitimate. Arpedifér, Oktikheion, Kimánis, Degakheion, Ormam, Kherka, and Lokemeion, all cities of great desire across all the lands they now control. Finally there is peace, brought by the toil and sweat of every great Riei since Aósiki and the combined efforts of all the Fasíri who had followed the monarch first. The Kheionai are no longer separated by pretentious ideals and have found unity under their banner, discovering their old philosophy to be misguided. Now calling themselves the Berrirghe and adopting cultures and traditions of every former Kheionai and their new Fasíri kin, they seek to no longer fight among each other and finally live in harmony.
