The people of that province have long felt singled out in the realm's society, being both a cultural and a religious minority. Now, they are being cast out of the only place they have left. Seeing no future in that province, their presence here is increasingly threatened. Many now fear the repercussions of staying too long in such a hostile environment. Rather than the controlled migration they had hoped for, they are leaving rapidly, no one wanting to be the last ones left here.
