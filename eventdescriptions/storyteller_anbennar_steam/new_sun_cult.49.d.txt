The past few decades have taken a heavy toll on the Sun Elven states in Bahar. We have been plagued with famine and infighting, then goblins from the mountains, heresy, and rebellion. It is not only destroying the land, it is destroying the humans' trust in us. Voices cry, "How could Surael's Chosen not have stopped this?" if far and few between. "They have been too busy fighting each other for claims and wealth; they ignore our suffering." We realize now that our infighting was foolish in the face of the disasters happening around us. Our brash confidence has nearly proven our undoing. This cannot continue; the people must have absolute faith in us so that we might lead them to Surael's Light. We will have to find ways to counteract the atrocities of these past decades of war and hunger, such that the light shines on Bahar's bright future ahead and not its gloomy past.
