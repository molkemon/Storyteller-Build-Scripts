Colltinn is a ruined forest beset with rot and hated abominations from continued warfare in previous years between Pelomar and Sidpar. There is one quality that lifts it above worthlessness. Colltinn is the last area of land before entering the forbidden Domandrod. All Peitar feel a deep connection with the Domandrod and the Fey; Sidpar enjoys this connection, but our cousins in Pelomar have been long severed from the Domandrod. We considered the Pelomarans as allies and friends after many years of unifying marriage, but they spit on this trust by demanding Colltinn from us. Why ever would we give up our ancestral land, but then again, can we afford to lose their support?
