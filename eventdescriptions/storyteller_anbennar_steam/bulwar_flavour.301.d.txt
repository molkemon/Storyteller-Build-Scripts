The Ebbušubtu, holiest of mountains, where Jaddar received his revelations from Surael Himself, has been a place of reverence for the faithful adherents of the Jadd as long as the faith has existed. Many of the devout dream of once in their life walking the golden road to the Mountain of Clear Sight, and each year a steady stream of pilgrims finds their way there to strengthen their faith. This year a major religious festival, the Sarašdanna or Praise of the Sun’s Glory, is being celebrated here, leading to a large increase in the number of pilgrims. Amongst them is a talented individual who would like to devote their talents to our cause.
