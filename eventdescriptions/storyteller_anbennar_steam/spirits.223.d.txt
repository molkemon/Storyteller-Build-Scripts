The monarch stood on the palace balcony, gazing at the tumultuous night sky as they grappled with the overwhelming disaster that had befallen their realm. In a moment of contemplation, they whispered to themselves, "What could a single mortal do to change the course of this disaster?" Their voice was barely audible against the backdrop of chaos. A mysterious response emerged from the shadows, catching them off guard. "More than most of you could imagine," came the whispered reply. Startled, the monarch and the attending rulers swiftly turned their attention to the corner of the balcony, their eyes searching for details in the darkness. Curiosity mingled with caution as the monarch addressed the shadowy figure. "What do you mean by that?" they asked, their tone a blend of wariness and willingness to hear more. The intruder's response carried no hint of hostility. "The Left Hand is a pathway to many abilities, some consider unnatural," the shadowy figure revealed. "Abilities that grant power, and with power, you can change the path and future of your nation." As the words hung in the air, the figure extended a dark hand, waiting for the monarch to decide whether to accept this enigmatic offer.
