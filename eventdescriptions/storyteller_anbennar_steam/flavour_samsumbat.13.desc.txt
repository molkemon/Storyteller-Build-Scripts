Generations of injustice have been resolved. A promise made thousands of years ago has been fulfilled. The son of Mother Nirakhet made an oath to the Akasi, and their trust in the Steward of the Sorrow was broken once he fell, and they did not forget. Now, as our victorious troops march through Akasik, as the soldierbuilders get to work in rebuilding what was damaged, and as the monarch meets with the local rulers, the Akasi people know that we have come to honour Elikhet's vow. Prosperity and freedom will once more be part of the thread of life in the land of the storm.
