While the Ravenmarch has been governed from Old Vernham with great success, its medieval facilities are quickly becoming outdated and inefficient. And with our glorious reconquest of Dostanor, we find our possessions separated into disparate regions. The newly reclaimed lands in the Folly remain the farthest from our governance, with New Corveld being the only center of civilization. In the tainted Vertbern Basin, the Order of the Ruby Scale struggles to fight the damned creatures of the night. In these changing times, it may be best to establish a new capital to govern the newly reformed Dostanor. But we are torn between two different homes. one nostalgic of glory long buried in the marshes of Daravan's Folly, and the home we have established in Old Vernham. Gablainé, while being the heart of a Corvuria that was cruel to us, may serve the best chance to embolden our hold over the region and establish our Corinite faith throughout the region.
