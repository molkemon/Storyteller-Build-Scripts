Korgus Dookanson is dead. The son of Dookan, our god, is no more. By all means, this revelation should have brought shock upon the ruler and the gathered shamans. Left them questioning their faith and reason for following the Greentide, Korgus' great crusade against the surface world. Yet none so much as stirred, for these accursed woods had already challenged their very essence. For those outside the Deepwoods, only mere five years had passed. But, due to the magics of the Deepwoods, it had been decades for those within. Decades of defending against defiant elves. Decades of dealing with disloyal goblins. And, worst of all, decades of damnable fey shenanigans. The time for belief to be questioned had come and gone. For the people, Dookanson's death was just one more straw. One more challenge to overcome. Indeed, in the eyes of the ruler and the shaman, it changed nothing. Their path had been clear for some time.
