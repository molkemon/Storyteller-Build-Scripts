Our victory over the warriors of darkness should have been assured, yet as the sun sets over the freshly ruined citadel, our Legion has been broken, our Herald slain, and our grip over the Salahad shattered. Our lord fought valiantly in the final siege, personally slaying scores of gnolls, but in the end they and all our leadership were butchered like animals by the vicious demonspawn of Tluukt. Yet the light of hope is not completely doused; our noble allies among the Masnsih have already reorganized in resistance to the hegemony of gnollkind, but it is a resistance we shall have no part in.
