Keeping control is no easy task for a vampire. They all know the pull of the beast, the monster that lives within each one. A monster that constantly threatens to take them over, wrestle control away from the rational being that normally inhabits their earthly shell. And as our lord well knows, once that beast begins to pull, there is only one way to stave it off. Tonight, the yearning has struck our lord, and their course of action seems clear.
