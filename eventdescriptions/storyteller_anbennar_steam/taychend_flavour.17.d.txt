The Slaver Nobles had many secrets, and whether they were rivals among their own class or imperial agents looking on with suspicion from Tacenie, there were many prying eyes eager to uncover them. As a result, the Slaver Nobles invested heavily in underground facilities and storage areas, hidden in the depths of their domains, where they could embark on whatever twisted project they so desired. Sometimes, they hid these ruins in their own slave warrens, concealed behind false walls and shadow veils. Luckily for them, the illusion and abjuration magic that once protected these hidden entrances has worn thin since the Ruin, and on a recent warren expedition they stumbled on a massive underground ruin. An extensive underground facility, with many store rooms and hidden chambers, it is a veritable treasure trove of Precursor artifacts, unmolested by the ravages of time. It will take years to fully clear these ruins out and scrap them for the valuable Precursor steel that has kept them stable for so long.
