Our beginnings were humble; the liberation of a group of mining slaves under Maracout, determined to establish selfrule. From those beginnings, we knew the slavers to the northwest would see an opportunity; a young state, unable to defend itself, would be an easy source of profit. Or so they thought. We have dealt a fatal blow to the chieftains of Saqraxat, but not only that, we also see how narrow our vision was. Slavery is not just a problem here, in the former lands of Kheterata. No, our work is far from complete.
