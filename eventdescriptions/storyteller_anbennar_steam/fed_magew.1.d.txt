The Arcane Ponderance has seemingly always been a point of contention. It has divided politicians, generations, and cities. As such, it should come to no one's surprise that the most vile scar upon our people would one day lead to war, and that bloodshed would beget bloodshed. We are faced with a dilemma: stagnation or violence. Violence will lead to the death of many, stagnation will lead to the death of all. The ghosts of the past have finally caught up, and it is time to face them.
