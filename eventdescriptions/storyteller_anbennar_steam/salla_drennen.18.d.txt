What I thought of the Herald of Change? Now that’s a strange one. I feel like my opinion on him changes almost as much as he does, and I entirely appreciate the strength of that statement. It was bold of us to approach him so directly, I’ll maintain that until I die. Even the other archfey fear him, at least a little. They say it was him that cursed the werewolves, you know. It went better than I expected; your idea to approach upon the beginning of a new cycle was a stroke of genius. I can only imagine the moment of emergence from his cocoon is the most docile he’ll ever be; I’m certainly rather groggy when I get out of bed. Still, I don’t really have an opinion to state. He simply was, and I am sure whatever he is now is not the same.
