A large group of Cardesti nomads have crossed the borders and entered our domain. These people are an odd bunch; having mastered the magical art of divination, their fortunetellers are quite popular with those who can afford their services. Moreover, as a group of experienced travelers, they have picked up some unique artisanry techniques and their bards are highly praised. However, the stark contrast between the customs in our realm and the Cardestis’ might prove troublesome in the future.
