Though prodigious consumption of alcohol is a trait found in their intimate counterparts, the dwarves, binge drinking is also a common pursuit for many goblins, and the quest for more alcohol has recently led a vast group of goblins in the region to ransack every brewery, vineyard, and distillery they could find. When the easy sources of liquor ran out, the goblins rapidly turned into a violent mob, attacking people, breaking into homes, and generally causing chaos.
