The ruler glanced up at the woman who had just appeared in front of their desk. She was very tall, towering over the ruler. Her golden skin appeared almost translucent in places, and her long, light brown hair floated in a nonexistent breeze. The ruler scrambled to the woman's feet, kneeling in front of her. "You have returned. After a millennia and a half. THE AMBER PRINCESS HAS RETURNED." The ruler was yelling to no one in particular. The Amber Princess flicked a finger, and the ruler was forced back to a standing position. "Calm yourself," she said. "Cease with your groveling. We have work to do."
