The subroutines which control the Mist Brigade have now realized the party has entered a much more restricted area than before. This is something which heavily disrupts their programming, and in response they have begun to escalate their offensive against intruders. The party discovers this when a giant fireball whizzes by overhead, crashing into a nearby wall and leaving only a smoking crater behind. The Mist Brigade soon reveals themselves from the gloom, and among them are now special units infused with evocation. And they are decidedly unhappy to see the hapless explorers.
