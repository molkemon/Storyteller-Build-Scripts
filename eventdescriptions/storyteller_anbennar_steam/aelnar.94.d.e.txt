As the one center of political and social gravity untainted by our society's deep patchwork of family grudges and rivalries, the Favored Hundred have attracted the attention and good will of much of the upper crust. Though they appear to be a loose collection of unrelated individuals, in reality the courtesans of the royal harem have become highly coordinated, working in deliberate concert to raise the prestige of both their own institution and the monarch personally.
