Second son of Dagrin and Anvelind, Margurz was enamoured with the creatures and plants of the wilderness that had been made by the Travellers and the Primordials. Seeking ways to use those found above and beneath the earth, Margurz would earn the title Master of Ale and Herbs by discovering the process of fermentation, and creating the first culinary and alcoholic wonders of Dwarvenkind, as well as discovering the medical properties of herbs and alcohol as consequence of his earlier enlightenments.
