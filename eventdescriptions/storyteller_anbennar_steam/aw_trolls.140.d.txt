Trolls are certainly strange. They have a sort of childlike innocence to them, if you catch them on a good day. One such troll has taken it upon themselves to maintain an important bridge in their domain, in return for modest amounts of coin, food, or trinkets—certainly a better deal than our usual builders. Apparently, this phenomenon is quite common for trolls who live closer to settlements, and that it is due to some innate calling to their origins as servants of True Giants that they have a strange affinity with stone and maintaining such structures.
