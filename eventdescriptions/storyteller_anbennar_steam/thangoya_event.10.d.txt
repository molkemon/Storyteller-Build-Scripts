Hidden below the Hanging Monastery of Aoshan lie the ruins of a long forgotten monastery, not unlike the others in the region, outcompeted by those who remained. When our officials first stumbled upon it, they were surprised to notice the golden leaves that dotted the paths towards the complex. That surprise turned to shock as the hidden monastery’s populace were goldscale kobolds, who had remained hidden from the world at large. They weren’t hostile, but did not let anyone enter their monastery. Their spokesperson explained they stayed isolated so as not to be taken as pets by the Yan, who believed their presence was an omen of good fortune. If we are to win the kobolds over, we should free those pets trapped in royal courts across the continent. The swiftest course of action here would be to participate in this cruel system, buy out some kobolds, and immediately release them in our country. Hopefully, that will be enough to warm them up to the Republic.
