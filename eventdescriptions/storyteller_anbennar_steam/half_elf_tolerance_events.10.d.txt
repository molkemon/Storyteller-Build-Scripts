A prominent noble in our realm known for their impotence has recently had a child. However, they now realize that they prefer their existing bastard child, a halfelf who has now started to show great competence in their role. The noble has petitioned and asked for the monarch's permission to legitimize the bastard as his heir. If we go through with this, the nobles will be displeased, but the halfelven communities of the country will naturally take this as an indication of our country's increased favor for them.
