Ever since Stalbór was founded, the brave men and women of Arturo’s Watch have guarded our people against threats both internal and external. But even these heroes cannot be everywhere at once, and as our realm continues to prosper and grow, the small chapter house of the Watch established in the realm will no longer suffice. The province now requires a fully staffed branch of the Watch to ensure its continued safety. It is incumbent on us to decide what the main duties of this latest branch of the Watch shall encompass.
