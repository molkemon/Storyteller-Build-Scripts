Today we were raided by the Buycev barbarians yet again. This is not unusual. What is unusual however is that these barbarians are more well armed than they were last time, and had more men and armor too. After the raiders retreated we inspected what corpses were left behind and found that their armor bears the signature marks of being forged by one of our rivals. No doubt those lowly scum bribed these barbarians to attack us. This is cynical and despicable. Some even say this means war.
