The inner circle of the monarch was a place where any advisor clever enough to get the ruler's ear could find his concerns heard by the court, and answered in course. Recently, however, the rising tension of the Crimson Deluge has darkened this once mighty court. Zealous administrators and nobles across the court have become increasingly hostile to those who they see as insufficiently devoted to the cause. This has come to a head as a prominent advisor to the monarch has openly called to rein in some of the senseless violence of the Corinite Crusaders. His opponents in court have seized this opportunity to paint him as a traitor to Corin herself, and are calling for his head.
