Hailing from nearby Bulwar, the family of SzelAzka has traditions they have kept even through their slavery all those years ago, namely the tradition of cleanliness. Members of the house are known for adhering to strict washing and cleaning rites, and while this does have the benefit of making them seem spotless in their political pursuits, it also has the benefit of making them seem the pious and honorable family, endearing them to the priests and religious.
