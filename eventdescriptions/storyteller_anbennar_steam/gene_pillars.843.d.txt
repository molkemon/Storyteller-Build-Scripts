As we reach the large central column, we start to hear a horrifying scratching sound as metal grinds against metal. Stumble suddenly looks terrified and almost runs into a nearby energy arc in terror. We manage to hold him back, but he yells at us to get somewhere safe and points behind us. Emerging from a passage in the floor, a massive mechanical worm rises up. Hundreds of spinning disks create a strange field of pressurized air allowing it to move along the ground. As it picks up speed, we realize it has set its sight squarely on us. Its maw is opening, revealing hundreds of spinning grinders. It appears we are face to face with the pillar’s last defense.
