Only a certain type of person is willing to take the personal risk of coating themselves in flaming death and charging into battle. The benefits of doing so are great, but there is a necessary amount of confidence and self-assuredness required to even attempt something so wild. It is hard to find an individual who simultaneously holds these qualities and takes orders well. But with the rising importance of the Landslide Infantry, it may be necessary for our army to promote cohesion, or at least a sense of honor and pride amongst these wildfires to temper their flame into something more directed.
