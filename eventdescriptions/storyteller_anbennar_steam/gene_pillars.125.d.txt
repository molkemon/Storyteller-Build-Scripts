Tampering with the deployment of the Kiamoa was deemed a massive security risk in the multiple contracts that make up the internal programming of the Mist Brigade. As such, the party's exploration has thrown them into crisis mode as they get closer to the Spout. These intruders must be eliminated swiftly, and with extreme prejudice. Safety limits are removed, and the final weapon is sent to crush the explorers. Stomping down the hall, completely filling it with its massive frame, a construct built around a massive collection of lightning bound in a bottle now charges the party. Its intentions are clear; detonate itself in their midst and wipe them out without a trace.
