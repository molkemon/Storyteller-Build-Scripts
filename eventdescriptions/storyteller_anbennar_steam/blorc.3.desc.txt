We have fought the dwarves for so long that the days when they were actually organised together, rather than dying alone, are merely held in our shamans' myths. There is one truth we know, however: when they were unified, it was under the central control of the largest hold in the entire Serpentspine: Amldihr. Situated in the centre of the Serpentĺs Vale, it was where every dwarf came to discuss matters of the empire.  Now in ruins, their grand halls lie empty with only crumbling statues and weathered carvings suggesting this was a place of the greatest importance to them. Yet only a fraction of the ruins have been scoured of their vast treasures; who knows what still lies here?
