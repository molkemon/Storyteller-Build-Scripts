As the celebrations roared through the city, the many competitors for the spirits' acknowledgment watched each other carefully. It was clear that this competition was not entirely about the art; it was also about understanding your opponents. Who would you be able to upstage? Who would attempt to sabotage the others? What scandals could possibly happen at such a celebration? The stage was set for an event that would be spoken of for lifetimes, and the only first step was to decide on our path. Who would we support in this endeavor? Who would be worthy of bringing us into the light of the great spirits?
