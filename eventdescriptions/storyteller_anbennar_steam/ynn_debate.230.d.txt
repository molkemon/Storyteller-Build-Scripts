A dozen weaklyheld swords are nothing compared to the might of a fierce and united charge. This lesson has been proven on battlefields across the Ynn for centuries, and at the tournament today, the monarch proved it once again. Our foes may have outnumbered us, but each Iosahar had their own battleplan, and they were sabotaged by disunity and miscoordination. Meanwhile, the ruler’s battalion fought like an extension of the monarch's will, driving each enemy before them and crushing all those that stood in our way. Already, news of the tournament has spread throughout the realm, and stories of the monarch’s prowess are being sung in taverns across the land. And in the minds of those who argued for the importance of decentralized power in the ongoing religious debate, well, each new tale is a blow to their confidence.
