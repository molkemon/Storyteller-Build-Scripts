A dozen weakly held swords are nothing compared to the might of a fierce and united charge. This lesson has been proven on battlefields for centuries, and at the tournament today, the ruler proved it once again. Our foes may have outnumbered us, but each warrior had their own battleplan, and they were sabotaged by disunity and miscoordination. Meanwhile, the leader’s battalion fought like an extension of their will, driving each enemy before them and crushing all those that stood in our way. Already, news of the tournament has spread throughout our lands, and stories of the leader’s prowess are being sung in taverns across the land. And in the minds of those who argued for the importance of decentralized power in the ongoing religious debate, well, each new tale is a blow to their confidence.
