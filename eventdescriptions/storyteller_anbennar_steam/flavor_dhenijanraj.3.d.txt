The Peridot King, Exilarch of Grozumdihr, He who sits the Palanquin Throne in Rayavhashapal, has been a fixture in Rahen for over a thousand years. A king without a kingdom, the Exilarch is the cultural leader of the peridot Dwarves, the largest of the three dwarven minorities found across the subcontinent. Holding great sway over the dwarves, the Exilarch's support will be needed to ensure that the dwarves agree to join the castes without minimal resistance. To this end, the ministries have sent messengers and diplomats to treat with the Exilarch over the past several months in an attempt to win their support. Today, the Exilarch has sent a reply to the missives of the Raja's representatives. Recognizing the strength of the central government and its desire to integrate the dwarves into the castes more firmly, the Exilarch has agreed to not oppose these moves if Dhenijansar will finance and back an expedition to restore dwarven rule over the longvacated hold of Grozumdihr. This will allow any dwarves who do not wish to join the caste system a home over which ancient dwarven tradition still holds root. With stability and support from the Raja, it may at last be time to refound Grozumdihr, but it will not be cheap to do so. What says our lord?
