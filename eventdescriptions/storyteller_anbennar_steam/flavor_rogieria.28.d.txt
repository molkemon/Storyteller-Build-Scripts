The emperor of Anbennar sat uneasy on the throne in the imperial council. Again and again, his authority had been undermined; clearly, Rogieria was behind it all. And now this challenge? They dared challenge his godgiven right to sit upon the Dove Throne? Well, there would be no challenge, not today. The doors to the chamber were locked; no perfidious Rogieran would be entering his council. But then, a flash of blue. A cloaked figure was approaching the center of the chamber. "Who are you? I command you to reveal yourself at once." The figure stopped before the throne and drew back his hood. "The challenger. Of course. Though you cannot simply march in here and demand the throne be handed to you." The emperor declared, advancing across the floor. "Hardly." The challenger turned to the assembled nobility. "Let all who would stand with the rightful emperor show themselves." For a moment, nobody moved. Hope soared in his chest, hope that was turned to unthinkable dread by the scraping of a chair on the floor behind him. One of the electors rose, then another, and another. One by one, they all came to stand behind the challenger. "No, no. You cannot do this." Other figures began to stand from amongst the princes of the empire, revealing ribbons of Damerian blue. "This is outrageous. This is..." "Please, do not humiliate yourself any further." The emperor looked around the chamber and saw a veritable sea. He hung his head in defeat. As the crown was placed upon the challenger's brow, a priest of Nerat shouted from the crowd. "By what right do you claim the emperorship?" The challenger turned, smiling, and replied.
