We thought these lands where fire bubbled beneath the surface to be treacherous and impassable. But to survive in these lands at all we have had to prove ourselves indomitable, and the Fires Beneath proved a difference only in scale, not in kind. Anyone who makes their life here will have to grow used to losing it in an instant. But like the grass species that thrive in the wake of a flashfire, so too will we learn to rebuild.
