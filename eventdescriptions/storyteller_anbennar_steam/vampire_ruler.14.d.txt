Recent rumours have caught the ruler's attention tonight. a lurker in the shadows of the capital, preying on its populace late at night and leaving nothing but empty corpses behind. This is undoubtedly not the fault of the monarch, who treats satiation with much more care and respect; it must be a feral vampire. And so, amidst darkened streets only lit by moonsliver, the ruler begins to hunt the wouldbe hunter. From shadows, the ruler watches the shadows; from rooftops, the ruler watches the rooftops; with a predator's eye, the ruler considers what another predator might focus on. And then, movement where there should not be. A rival? No. A victim, of circumstance, and soon of the ruler's blade.
