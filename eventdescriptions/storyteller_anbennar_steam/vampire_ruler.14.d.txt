Recent rumours have caught our lord's attention tonight: a lurker in the shadows of our capital, preying on its populace late at night and leaving nothing but empty corpses behind. This is undoubtedly not the fault of our ruler, who treats satiation with much more care and respect; it must be a feral vampire. And so, amidst darkened streets only lit by moonsliver, our lord begins to hunt the wouldbe hunter. From shadows, they watch the shadows; from rooftops, they watch the rooftops; with a predator's eye, they consider what another predator might focus on. And then; movement where there should not be. A rival? No. A victim, of circumstance; and soon of our lord's blade.
