Today, a large group of pilgrims has gathered to meet with the ruler. They are asking for rights to set out on a pilgrimage towards a great structure located outside the borders. These great structures, built by ancestors long ago, help calm the great river and divert water to the fields and mills. As a vessel through which the river passes, all of them attract a great many religious pilgrims. This group seems well provisioned to get through the land, but who knows what will happen to them outside the borders. The structures are extremely important to the culture and letting a pilgrimage get robbed or worse may paint the nation in a negligent light among the vassals and people.
