Vampires are heavily restricted from siring new heirs, as doing so without caution brings great risk to the Masquerade. Any new heirs must be approved by other vampires in the realm and they must be carefully selected. The monarch has found the perfect candidate for the blood gift, and is ready to brush aside any concerns their brethren have. What is the point of being a vampire, or being the ruler, if you cannot spread its blessing to the worthy? The only question that remains now is how they shall serve.
