Sometimes, the population of Kheterata likes to overinterpret the Khets various shenanigans. They can interpret a sleeping Khet as a sign for everyone to stop their labour, or the crashing of the barge as a bad omen. This time a Khet has climbed a palm tree at the highest point of the sun to enjoy some sunbathing. The local priest of Khet has interpreted this as a coded message from the Khet, that the Steward reaches too far, where only Khets can reside. Naturally everyone has become hysterical and outright rebellious towards their ruler.
