Deep within Haless lie the glistening sands of the Rünsukhi plateau. Home to Facestealers, evil and dangerous spirits, the Ashwoven Harimari were trapped there amongst the Rünsukhi people due to a war between the Kingdoms of Guwaamud and Lianzhou in two eighty two AA. However, in their many years of involuntary exile they have managed to ward off the malign spirits of the land due to the use of spiritfire. If we are to visit the plateau, it might be worth having their protection.
