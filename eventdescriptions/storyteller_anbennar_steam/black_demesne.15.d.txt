The elves of Ibevar create a difficult conundrum for the Skull Throne. They are magical beings, which in theory should entitle them to the status of citizenship in the domain. However, having large minority populations could create social and political difficulties that would distract from the pursuit of magical supremacy. They are not mere animals, like the mundanes, but it may be a regrettable necessity to pursue harsher measures. On the other hand, they could be made use of as a resource if the risk is willing to be taken.
