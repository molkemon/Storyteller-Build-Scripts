With the door safely barricaded no one can get through, and the passage should be safe to any intruders. This means, however, that in order to follow up on the strange occurrences the expedition has to find another path.  After some time one of the scouts returned with great news: she has found another way into the deeper library. Not only that but this path must have been dug by a xorn or a similarly creature with corvidian instincts: gems and gold were laid in hoard just a few minutes walk into the tunnel.
