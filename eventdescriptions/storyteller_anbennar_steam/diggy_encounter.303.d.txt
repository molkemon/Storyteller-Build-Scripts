As soon as our messenger arrived at the dwarves’ doorstep, he was attacked on sight. From the houses and fields, dwarves poured over him, dismembering him within seconds. Seeing this, even for us, was a gruesome display of blind hatred, and we retaliated in kind, throwing ourselves into battle once again against our mortal enemies. Axe and sword, shield and bow, countless lives falling on both sides were the result of our well-intentioned idea of peace, but the fact that dwarves are stubborn idiots that cannot be reasoned with proves itself once again.
