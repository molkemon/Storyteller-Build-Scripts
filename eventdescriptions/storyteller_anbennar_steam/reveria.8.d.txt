The ruler has sent an expedition of chroniclers to the Skalds of Gerudia to document all matters related to the bloodlines of the Reavers. The lineages of the reaver kingdoms of Reveria, Deranne and Pearlsedge have been carefully documented and brought back to court. After that the court spymaster has gone over the fine details of all family trees, carefully editing details about centuries past to ensure the Sil Revers and thus our realm have a legitimate claim on both Deranne and Pearlsedge. We shall now publish these findings inconspicuously, with our claims not yet emphasized, which will hopefully result in raising our prestige and establishing these fabrications as fact.
