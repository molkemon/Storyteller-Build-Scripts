The local cave system has proven very habitable for bats, and a number of gunpowder producers have cropped up in the area to refine the guano into the explosive. The alchemists involved in that process have lent their minds, and have a radical idea: using a variant of the guano solution from which they extract saltpeter as a chemical bath for a normal sheet of glass. The preliminary tests, though poorly attended, have produced better results than anything else we've tried.  With some refinements to the solution and the soak methods, we'll be able to produce our own Sapphire glass.
