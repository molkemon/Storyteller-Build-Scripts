Even though the Council can be rowdy and sometimes proposals or issues escalate to duels, it is, most of the time, a frighteningly efficient machine. Each seat in the Council corresponds to a specialty; some councillors, like the Devouts, have an entourage of multiple magi who carry out their instructions. On the other hand, some councillors are often absent in sessions, most of the time due to their missions for Heartmages or out of a lack of interest in the agenda presented for the Technocrats. The Hardliners are the heart of the machine; their strictness also applies in their work and their expectation of others, to the great dismay of Noble and Merchant Scions whose seats are tied to their gifts and not the work they put in. Finally, the more ambitious of the Floodborn find themselves leading smaller groups of councillors, trying to further their own standing. Most of them end up becoming the representatives and leaders of the other cliques.
