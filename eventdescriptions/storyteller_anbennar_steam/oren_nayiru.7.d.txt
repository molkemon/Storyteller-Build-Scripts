Due to their wide appeal among the lower classes, the Oren Nayiru have infiltrated every level of the population, as slaves, servants, and workmen. Thanks to the recent assassination of a prominent figure, a wave of, frankly, justified paranoia has spread across the realm. Many demand a sweeping investigation of Oren Nayiru sympathizers, something likely to be an extremely expensive, time-consuming, and mostly fruitless endeavor. Nonetheless, if we do nothing, it will certainly make us look weak.
