A nest of rats, breeding until it spills out from its lair. A plague of locusts, swirling in the air as it crosses the country. A damn pain in the rear, which isn't constrained there. These are the thoughts running through the ruler's head as they hears the latest report about the realm's goblin infestation — the growing horde from another country has spilled over the border into the country, and a new den of skulkers will require a new band of adventurers to handle.
