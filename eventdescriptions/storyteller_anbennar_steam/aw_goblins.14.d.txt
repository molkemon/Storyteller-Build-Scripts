A nest of rats, breeding until it spills out from its lair. A plague of locusts, swirling in the air as it crosses our lands. A damn pain in the rear, which isn’t constrained there. These are the thoughts running through the lord’s head as they hear the latest report about the realm’s goblin infestation – the growing horde from elsewhere has spilled over the border into our lands, and a new den of skulkers will require a new band of adventurers to handle.
