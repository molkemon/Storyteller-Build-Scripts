A report. A great host of rebels has risen up in the province and laid the city under siege. It appears that our control of the city, and our conversion efforts, have not penetrated nearly as deep as we had hoped. The followers of Varlengeilt have yet to be fully rooted out, it seems. However, the dragon himself is not present. It is clear that he is too much of a coward to show himself, and would rather work through those he holds in thrall. Perhaps he will make his presence known in time.
