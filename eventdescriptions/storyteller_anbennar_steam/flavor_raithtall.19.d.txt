Recently, the lord received grim news from the Domandrod. A group of fey emerged, warning of a terrible disaster that would soon hit our nation. Worried, but not shaken, the lord took some of their most trusted druids and went out to the edge of the Domandrod. There, they stayed for several days, conducting their ritual. Once they emerged, they proclaimed that the disaster had been averted and our people had been spared. On the news a wave of celebrations spread throughout our nation, celebrating the lord and their druids for their accomplishment.
