Now that we have reformed our society and our state is strong, can we address perhaps the most important question surrounding the rule of our society. What is the place of our Grand Marshal in all of this? Will we give our Grand Marshal absolute control over the state and the army and let them rule as an absolute ruler, a ruler that has proven their worth and is the one and final authority in the War Room and the Great Command as a whole? Or should the Grand Marshal be a powerful, yet not absolute, leader who will share power with the Grand Regent and the War Room? A ruler that though master of army and administration is still bound by those around them to check their decisions and power.
