Before we went on the expedition, we had heard tales of an ancient Gnomish explorer by the name of Hannoddodd who had found an island populated with hirsute and savage humanoids. Hannoddodd had made attempts to capture the males which failed, but three females were taken. Their ferocious nature forced the explorer to kill them and to bring proof of these savages; their skins were preserved for transport. These people were called 'gorillae' by the interpreters traveling with Hannoddodd. We assume that the apelike ruinborn we found in that province must be related to these Gorillae Hannoddodd spoke of.
