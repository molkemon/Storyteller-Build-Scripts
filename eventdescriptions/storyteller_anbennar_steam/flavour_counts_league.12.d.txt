We have done it. Against all odds, we have beaten back the crisis that came to our doorstep and will see the break of a new dawn on the other side of the long night that was the deal the founders took with that wretched creature. Our fate is our own again, and it seems time that we all come together to enjoy this when so much could have been lost. We may never know what the Hag intended to do, but now we are safe, and as a bonus, the wound upon the world left by old Beastbane that she sought to exploit has been healed ever so slightly. The wound still lies there, but whether from a karmic hunger sated by the passing of a Hag who sought to exploit it, or whether in its own strange tribute to our heroism, it shall no longer ruin the land itself.
