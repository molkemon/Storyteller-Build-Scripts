The camps around the Iron Palace are now streets. The forgefires are joined by lanterns and schools. Sarilavhan swells like a furnace-fed hearthstone, and the torn castles of Rahen rise amalgamated in thick black walls. The Iron Palace itself begins to claw at the sky. It is not the dainty marble of elven kings nor the painted lacquer of Harimari halls. It is brutal. Beautiful. Eternal.
