The different cliques in the Council have their own relationships with each other, and some, more often than not, end up butting heads and disagreeing. The scene unfolding before the Sovereign today is another of the results of one of these rivalries. The two overseers have left their provincial seats and have come in front of the Council to denounce the other. The atmosphere grows tense fast as the argument becomes heated, with ambient magic buzzing in the Council room. Representatives from the different factions step in to stop them. They have reached a point where if they were to return to their seats, they would rise up in rebellion against each other. The choice must come now. which overseer shall receive the Sovereign's blessing, and which shall be deposed?
