It was easy enough to ignore the Deepest when we settled here. It was just a hole in the ground, perilous to any who fell in but easy enough to avoid. It would be a local curiosity, until today: when the hole emptied out entirely. Its bottom was not visible from above, but that only piqued people's curiosity further. Explorers and fools quickly flocked to it, venturing down with ropes and harnesses. They made only a haphazard exploration into its depths before the hole flooded back, and the most foolish or the most brave were washed away. In its wake, however, even more have flocked to the region. They do not truly know when the Deepest will empty out again, or if it ever will again, but their curiosity outweighs their impatience. At least for now.
