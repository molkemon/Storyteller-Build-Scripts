The theory that merfolk are ruinborn has grown amongst merfolk society, although in doing so it has only triggered yet more blowback. Merfolk societies are typically not eager to share any internal struggles with those above land, but we have gathered that there are entire villages worth of merfolk ready to leave for fear of persecution for their belief in the ruinborn theory. With our new holdings in Aelantier, we are ready to give them a new home.
