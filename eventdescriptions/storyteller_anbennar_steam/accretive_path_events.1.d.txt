Your agents bring troubling, or perhaps promising, news from the domain. Its ruler has turned away from discipline and self-cultivation. Rather than hone their Chi or govern with clarity, the ruler wastes their hours feasting on unhealthy food and beverage with donors and toying with games of sport. One agent even reports them trying to reroute a river flood using their quill on a map. In their neglect, vigilance wanes. With their gaze turned away, a window may be open.
