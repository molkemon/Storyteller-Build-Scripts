Since our efforts to increase the supply of cotton, a clothmaking boom has emerged in the empire. One of the Khets has grown particularly fond of this newfound influx of quality artisan clothing he's been receiving, to the point that he spends most of the day sweeping through his closet adoring his collection, before finally deciding what to wear. As a result, he has decided to bless his main suppliers with fresh facilities, filled with large amounts of coin and leisure facilities for both the workers and himself, for whenever he wants to visit them.
