With a grunt, our ruler throws off their robes, calling for attendants to grab their gear. "I will not stand idly by as the honour of Adenica gets insulted," our ruler says as the stunned onlookers stand with mouths agape. Emotions flit across the Lorentish knight's face; shock, confusion, even fear; before it twists into eagerness. "So, I defeat a lord today," he boldly states. "Let us prepare, then." Clad in the royal colors of white and blue, our ruler warily surveys their opponent. The handsome knight stood proudly, with the swirl of the Lorentish rose prominent upon his gaudy chestpiece and a massive plume upon his helmet. "That stupid rose will serve as a good target at least," our ruler mutters under their breath. Despite the ostentatious display by the knight, he is clearly quite practiced in the saddle, wielding his long lance expertly as he wheels his horse around the grounds. The shouts of the royal and Lorentish parties became a roar as the lord and knight ride toward each other at full charge. Our ruler's lance barely scrapes the side of the Lorentish knight's plate as their horse turns away at the last second, while the exchange leaves our ruler with a large dent in their armor. Sensing weakness, the Lorentish knight prepares their second charge quickly, speeding up and almost knocking our ruler off their horse with a fullbodied blow right at the location of their first strike. As the knight charges past, however, our ruler steadies their steed and hits the Lorentish knight with a surgical attack at the apex of their turn, knocking both horse and rider off balance. Our ruler launches a flurry of attacks, aimed at preventing the knight from recovering. With a final, mighty blow, the Lorentish knight is knocked from the saddle, to the cheers of a giddy crowd.
