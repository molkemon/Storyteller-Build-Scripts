During the monarch's nightly slumber, the ruler was struck with a blessed revelation. They were ensorcelled within the night sky itself, far over the earthly limit of Halann. Voices they could only understand as their forebears called out to them, guiding their vision to a constellation and burning its shape into their mind. The monarch, now understanding the meaning of their astral portent, has made preparations for travel. They ask that a small entourage accompany them to the place described in their vision. The monarch wishes to have an official general's bodyguard while they travel. Establish them as a leader and have them lead a brigade to the province.
