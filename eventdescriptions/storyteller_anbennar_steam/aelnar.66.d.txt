During her nightly slumber, the monarch was struck with a blessed revelation. She was ensorcelled within the night sky itself, far over the earthly limit of Halann. Voices she could only understand as her forebears called out to her, guiding her vision to a constellation and burning its shape into her mind. The monarch, now understanding the meaning of their astral portent, has made preparations for travel. They ask that a small entourage accompany them to the place described in their vision.
