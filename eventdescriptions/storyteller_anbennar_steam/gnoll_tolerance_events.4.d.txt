Gnolls are well known for their enterprising tendencies and military tenacity. For better or for worse, many peoples can testify to their combat ability. Similarly, if someone manages to obtain the friendship or loyalty of a gnoll, they cannot but benefit; gnolls’ fierce devotion to those they regard as pack overwhelm all else. A gnoll of some ability has now come to the ruler with an offer of service, vowing to do their duty as if for their pack leader.
