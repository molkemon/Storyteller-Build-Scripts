The monarch looks out from the balcony of their great palace, overlooking Anisíkheion’s great harbor. They see a most glorious sight. Hundreds of warships anchored in port, the greatest navy Aelantier has ever seen. The monarch reminisces about Anisíkheion’s rise, for it is a story of Anisíkheion’s navy. Were it not for its navy, it would still be a backwater island settlement. Were it not for the navy, Anisíkheion would still be dominated by disloyal merchants and land lovers. Were it not for the navy, Anisíkheion would not have become the eighth great kheion, able to challenge and ultimately subjugate the other great kheions. The monarch snaps out of their thoughts, and returns inside to a meeting with their admirals, for there are always more threats across the sea, not least of which being these so called "Cannorian" newcomers. But whatever happens going forward, the navy has given Anisíkheion a place in the sun, one that it will defend to the last ship.
