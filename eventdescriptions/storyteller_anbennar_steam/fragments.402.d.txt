Several days ago, our expedition's precursor expert, the elven mage and adventurer Torrieth, felt a strange magical presence, and since then we have been following her lead through the forests of Noruin. "A place of power… gotta be," she murmurs. Eventually, the expedition comes to a stop at a small hill. Atop the hill sits a strange runestone, which softly emanated a telltale blue color of magic when approached. Without a word of warning, Torrieth touched it, seemingly absorbing the power for herself. We received reports that Torrieth is currently in a coma, but recovering well. But what we do with this strange turn of events is still up to our expedition leader.
