The move to the "new" city of Siadunasar has already nearly been completed. Harpies from Siadunaiun have been moving to the city en masse, and governmental and bureaucratic institutions have nearly all moved to the new city. However, an unexpected problem has arisen; homesickness. Not from those harpies moving to Siadunasar, who have generally found the place to their liking, but from our lord herself. Having been hatched there, and having spent the earliest years of her childhood in the roost that overlooks the Salahad, it is expected that she have fond memories of her old home, but nobody, including our lord herself, expected the intensity of her feelings. Though she herself has simply stated that she can "get over it", there has recently been a proposal that Siadanlen should establish Siadunasar as the "Winter Capital" of the realm, so that our lord may spend the summer months among the ancestral home of the Siadunan people. While this would also have the added benefit of maintaining the old "centre of gravity" of the realm, we may find that the areas around Siadunasar might be more costly to govern with our lord absent for some of the year, and some key governmental offices still in the old capital.
