Our people are a mobile one. The reason we are sons of Irdaeos is because we left the lands of Rohibos, and it is Irdaeos’ word that a centaur only stops moving when he is dead or broken. It is the creed of our people that for however good a land is there is always somewhere better, until we are brought to the paradise of Ik Magthaal. It is this tendency that has made us great which has now been turned against us. One of the minor clans is claiming that our weakness and instability has made our lands unsuitable, and now seek to make their fortune anywhere else so long as it is not here.
