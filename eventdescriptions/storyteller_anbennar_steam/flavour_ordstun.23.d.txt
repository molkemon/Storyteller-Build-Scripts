The court of Diamond is filled to the brim with jovial and boastful dwarves, assembled to witness the arrival of a most unexpected visitor. Arriving at Argordstun is an ambassador, sent by the hold of Verkal Gulan, who (while being searched for hidden weapons) was found to carry with him nothing but a single large bar of pure gold. "Perhaps he intends to beat the ruler to death with it," one dignitary proclaims to the bellowing laughter of his fellows: just then, the foreigner steps into the throneroom, presenting the ruler and the Gleaming Council a most intriguing offer. He asks us to halt any plans of conquest against the Gold Hold, for they are rich and more than ready to engage the ancient imperial mints as to provide us with tribute. Laugher fills the halls; it is clear that the Golds are scared, and the fight for dominance has practically already been won. Only a sharp gesture from the ruler silences the crowd once more, as they readies to proclaim their decision on war or peace.
