For the past couple of days we have been entertaining a guest from the royal court of a distant land, the former home of our consort. After a full week of hunting, feasting and gossip with the family, our consort’s cousin is finally preparing to return home. She assures us that she has had a wonderful time and with a deep bow of gratitude, says she will make certain to inform everyone back home about how kind our government has been to her, and continues to be to her beloved cousin.
