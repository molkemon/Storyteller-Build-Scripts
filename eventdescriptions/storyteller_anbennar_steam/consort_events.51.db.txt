For the past couple of days we have been entertaining a guest from the royal court of a certain country, the once home of the ruler. After a full week of hunting, feasting and gossiping with the family, the consort's cousin is finally preparing to return to that country's capital. She assures us that she has had a wonderful time and it is with a deep bow of gratitude that she says she'll make certain to inform everybody back home about how kind the great government of this realm has been to her, and continues to be to her beloved cousin.
