Few pursue Surael's brilliant path with as much vigour or steadfastness as our soldiers. And now that their path has led them to celebration, they place just as high a value on partying as they do any other piece of their service. We cannot begrudge them this break, but we should be careful, since our military prowess will be hampered during the festivities, and even after, as our troops figure out where they left their arms and ammunition when the celebratory fervour took them.
