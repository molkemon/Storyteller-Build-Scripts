The House of Fire and Water, sanctum of alchemists across Haless, is where sorcerers gather to refine wonders through the harmony of flame and flow. Though many attempts end in failure, some among them manage to produce items of astonishing power—objects that heal the dying, unleash arcane destruction, or commune with the unknown. Today, the master of the House arrives at court with a collection of newly forged marvels. Each is presented with reverence. one that grants strength in war, one that heals grievous wounds, one that ignites with an unquenchable blaze. Bowing before the monarch, the master proclaims. "These are the fruits of our most devoted work. Not all are perfect, but each bears our sincere wish to serve. We humbly offer these for Your Majesty's discerning choice."
