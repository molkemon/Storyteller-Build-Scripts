Every five years the whole domain takes a reprieve from their work and celebrates the Week of Ascension, with each day dedicated to a separate virtue. Patience, Inquiry, Brotherhood, Nobility, Freedom, Obedience, and, on the last day, the most sacred. Intuition. In the name of the monarch, every mundane creature, whether rich or poor, is directed to the same locations for festivities, partakes in the same food and drink, and listens to the same discourse. Meanwhile, every magical initiate in all the domain is recalled to Castonath to join the ruler at the foot of the Charred Tower for convalescence, conversation, and a meeting of the minds in which bonds of brotherhood are forged and information is exchanged. These parties of the initiates are, consequently, perhaps the single most important political event in the domain, as it is where alliances among the lesser initiates are forged and informal allegiance is declared to one Acolyte or another. All this, of course, under the grace and watchful eye of the Ascendant Dragon.
