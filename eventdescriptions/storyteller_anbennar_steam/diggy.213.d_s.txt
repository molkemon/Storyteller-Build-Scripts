One of the greatest holds in AulDwarov, Seghdihr contributed a great citrine gem to the Dwarovkron as a gesture of good faith. A symbol of wealth and commerce, it was lost in the waning days of the Dwarven Empire.  Recently, we located the gem and liberated it from its former owners. With it now in our hands, our claim to rule the Middle Dwarovar will surely be more compelling to our prospective subjects.
