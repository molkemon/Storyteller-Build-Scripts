In the capital, the monarch of the ruling dynasty was crowned Kamrayakval – PriestEmperor of the Oren Nayiru. The High Kavalali of all the great cities of Orenvalyam, of both Taychend and the many lands beyond, each blessed the crown held aloft over his head, and each took a word of the sacred mission they charged him with. Ahead of the new Kamrayakval, the new ruler faces difficult choices, factional intrigues, war and conflict at home and abroad. It will not be an easy task, nor will it be a pleasant one, but in the end, they did not vie to be selected for the role, and prepared for so long to take up the mantle, for an easy nor pleasant life. Only one question remains.
