In our aimless wanderings through our verdant home, we have encountered another tribe of beings like us yet unlike us at the same time. They call themselves the Takurekohukau. They have made it their mission to reawaken our dormant kin in the hopes of uncovering the secrets of our past hidden in the memories of those who still await their awakening. And in some rare cases, they have encountered Mechanim with vague recollections of the time before our long dormancy. The Takurekohukau terms these EpicBearers and holds them in the highest regard, as living symbols of their struggle against oblivion.
