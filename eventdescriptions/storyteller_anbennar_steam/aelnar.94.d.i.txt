When the monarch attends the court in the presence of the Favored Hundred, it is impossible not to be awestruck by the transcendent beauty of the assembled courtesans of the royal harem. It is as though dozens of the most beautiful people in the entire world have come to attend on the monarch. Their lavish devotion inspires a kind of mesmerizing effect in all those who witness it, as though such assembled beauty could only be tamed through a sort of earthly divinity. The monarch's legitimacy has thus been cemented by the raw power of aesthetic preeminence.
