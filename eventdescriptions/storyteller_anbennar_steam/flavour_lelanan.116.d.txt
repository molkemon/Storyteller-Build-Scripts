A family of new arrivals came from so far to the East, having traveled from beyond the ocean. They could not quite partake in our feast; there were a dozen of them, strangely elven in design. They neither ate nor slept nor breathed, with hearts that were most benign. Communication was fraught, yet we never lost our hope. Eventually, they explained they were much more than a homunculus trope—a great big family, even without the need to produce life. Together, they had survived and endured Insyaa’s deadly strife. We presented a gift for their stories as they settled in very quickly, something that reminded us of them. an everburning candlestick.
