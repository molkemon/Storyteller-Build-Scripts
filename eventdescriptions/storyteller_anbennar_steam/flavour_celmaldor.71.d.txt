Commerce ebbs and flows; those are the only guarantees of trade. Anything else is pure speculation, but that does not mean that we cannot help to steady the constant flow of trade that flows through our trade league. With our trade being at a low ebb, investment is wanting. Our fellow league members suggest that we invest that to keep the trade flow steady. Some suggest building more markets to strengthen our trade; others more workshops to strengthen our craftsmen, and a few more suggest adding more coastal guns and forts to protect against any potential pirates and privateers roaming across the sea.
