Braving the tangle of branches and roots that line the chambers within the pillar, your party is no stranger to the sticky entanglement of sap. It gunks up your armor, fuses the fingers, and generally feels unpleasant. Consequently, an overwhelming feeling of dismay befalls your expedition as it happens upon a room full of golden sap, deeper than your tallest party member. However, deep underneath the molasses surface, baubles and artifacts rest on the pillar floor. Retrieving them would be hideously foolish, and profitable.
