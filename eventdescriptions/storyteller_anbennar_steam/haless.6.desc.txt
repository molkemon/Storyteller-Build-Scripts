The Roaming Harimari are newcomers to much of their current homeland, and unlike in Rahen or Yanshen they have never been an unchallenged ruling class. The codes of law we practice are derived from Nephrite codes or ancient human texts, without the Harimari having had the chance to carve out accommodations they now claim are necessary. Some of our structures are too small to comfortably accommodate them, and when they are in our care or service we have no obligation to provide them meat for their diet. They are in many easy to overlook ways foreign to us. Now a Harimari scholar has come to our court with a list of amendments they claim are necessary to allow them to partake fully in our society.
