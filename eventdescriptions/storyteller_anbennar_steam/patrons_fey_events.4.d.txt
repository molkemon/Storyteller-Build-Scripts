Some say that the ability to learn is the most powerful of all abilities. At the beginning of every apprentice's journey to scholarship, they must first learn how to learn itself. However, with the fundamentals mastered, it is up to them to apply their skills and expand their knowledge. The Old Man of the Archives serves as a mentor for the art of learning; from his guidance, their understanding of the written word blossoms. But with their lessons completed, it is time to test their skills.
