Our fleet set sail to find a western sea route to the rich cities of the Gulf of Rahen. It took them ninety-nine days to cross the ocean westward, after they had found the Kimánis Bank located near the tip of South Aelantier. The strait was calm and they were escorted by local fishermen who warned them of the deadly winds of the Kaydhano Sea, which seems to be a desert, but eventually, the captain and his crew made their way out into the ocean. The fleet's captain was crushed by a tail of a giant monster in Insyaa. Two of our ships had survived; one tried to return across the Ocean of the Lost but perished. The last ship continued west and sailed across the Sarhaly Coast, and as it arrived at our port, it became the first ship to circumnavigate the globe.
