When we allied ourselves with the newly reforged hold of Khugdihr, friends to the powerful Asra Bank, we never anticipated the return we have received now. Coffers full of money have arrived at our doorsteps, funds supplied by Khugdihr's vast vaults deep within the bedrock of the Serpentspine, filled by none other than their sponsor, the Asra Bank. The missive supplied with it is unmistakably friendly: Together we have reclaimed parts of the Serpentspine, and now, with our new funds, we shall restore it to its former glory.
