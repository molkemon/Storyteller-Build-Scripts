Making it onto the Gene Pillar is only half the battle. Many of the outer layers of the Pillar have been stripped by the digestive juices of the sandworm, creating many heavily damaged access points amidst what is essentially a labyrinth of steel plating, sand, and other debris. We must find a good entry point into the pillar at one of these many exposed spots, preferably somewhere with access into the center of the tower where the damage to the pillar is likely less severe.
