It is said that of all the composers in Cannor, PierreAlexandre Commonfoot might be the first to have the true gift sharing emotion through his music, of communicative expression and fair feeling. Commonfoot, a halfling composer living in Lorent, writes genius pieces that make you truly feel like you're walking the streets of Lorentaine with him. This soothing, pleasant style has permeated life in Seinathil, and has brought the entire country to a more serene, calm state.
