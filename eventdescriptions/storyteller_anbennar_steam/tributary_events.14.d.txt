Ever since we started sending tributes to the overlord, the demand for the exotic goods of our artisans has been rather high in the overlord's capital. This has led to the growth of a small trade network, which while not entirely legal, has enriched many noble officials and entrepreneurial merchants. Having discovered the size of the operation, the noble monarch has now imposed much stricter regulations to stop what they consider to be illegal smuggling. Where there is demand, it is hard to stop people from trying to make a profit, however.
