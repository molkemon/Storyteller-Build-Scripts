Some Rajas die in triumph. Ramapalar the Reunifier died peacefully in the Golden Palace, atop a realm he had reforged. Others die in tragedy. The Raja Amapurna II fell to the smothering sickness, that foul plague which did not distinguish between the highest lord and the lowliest peasant. Only time will tell how historians describe the end of the ruler; but make no doubt, today marks their end. Our forces dragged them out of the castle they had been hiding in, and by the time they were presented to the monarch, their silks were torn and muddied. Finally, their outside appearance matched their aptitude for rule. There was rage, and anger, and disgust. But Harimar himself was not the first Harimari, merely the first to conquer and unite them, and so too has the monarch proven himself the rightful Raja. Ultimately, the ruler was made to recognize that. And that shall be the last the historians write of them, as they shift their attention to a more worthy dynasty. ours.
