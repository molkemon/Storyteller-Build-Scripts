When a new material becomes available, the next thing to do is find uses for it. As warfare evolves, more devastating variations of hot lead, a material like Sapphire Glass, with unprecedented hardness and relative cheapness, becomes increasingly attractive for its potential use in armor. Heavy armored troops have proven too immobile for modern warfare, but it is possible that sheets of metal, plated with Sapphire glass, could offer great protection with as little weight as possible.
