We seem to have finally stumbled upon something functional that is not trying to kill us. A quite simple device that appears to be some kind of a highly advanced glassblowing machine. Our best guess is that the device was used to create the countless glass tubes storing all the biological specimens we have discovered so far while making our way through the pillar. Luckily the controls look easy to handle. We might be able to do something useful with the device, as the glass is quite beautiful, but also looks quite hot.
