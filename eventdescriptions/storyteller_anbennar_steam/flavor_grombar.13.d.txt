As Grombar expands outwards, into the lands of the far north, we have come across a new, valuable source of income. mammoths. Already, mammoth tusks are fetching high prices both for domestic buyers and foreign merchants, but supply is unable to keep up with demand. A proposal has been made to capture a small group of mammoths and begin breeding them in Mammoth Hall, as was done centuries ago under the old empire of Castanor. Although expensive, the endeavor promises to pay for itself in the coming years.
