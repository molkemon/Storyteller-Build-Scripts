During the Greentide many denizens of Escann fled westwards. Through the combined efforts of Gawed, Arbaran and Dames crown, these refugees were welcomed across western Cannor and distributed around the Dames head. This refugee crisis brought with it a wave of new ideas, as among those that fled Escann were nobles, scholars and knights. But it also strained nations that were busy fighting in the fourth Lilac War, leading to some nations refusing refugees. Our realm wasn’t able or willing to partake in the effort to help the refugees until recently; some refugees now seek shelter in our lands, and it is up to us to accommodate them until they settle down or hopefully reclaim Escann.
