Tribal feuds are the bane of the ruler's existence. Though no culture society lacks them, they are a constant thorn in the ruler's side, distracting lieutenants and tying up resources. Thankfully, it appears the culture are not the only who suffer such. The goblin skulkers who infest the domain seem to have broken into similar factions, one of whom was forced out of the nest to be easily crushed at the hands of our hastily assembled warparty.
