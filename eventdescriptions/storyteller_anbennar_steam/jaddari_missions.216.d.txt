For decades, the Jadd Empire has been run entirely from the capital of the Divine Heralds. It was the center of the Empire, the center of the bureaucracy, the center of authority, as much as the Sun is the center of the universe. But suddenly, that has all been thrown into doubt. Two Divine Heralds? Two Suraelic Courts? Two singular power centers? Nonsense on its face. Or such is the content of many discussions around the empire; during dinner at local magistrates’ houses, over coffee before dawn prayers at local temples, amidst taverns, caravanserai, and markets. But perhaps it’s visible most of all in the dilapidated status of the lord’s court.
