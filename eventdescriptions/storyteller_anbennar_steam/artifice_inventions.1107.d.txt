Unlike the Sparkdrive, which uses Dames tear as a catalyst to activate elemental energies, the Megacannon channels the dormant mana of Dames tear itself into a beam of unbelievable power. Such a blast would normally cause an explosion which would wipe out all nearby troops, including ours, so we have built a casing of magic-insulating porcelain around the Megacannon's barrel. Porcelain alone cannot contain this weapon, though; it may be able to deflect the magical components of each explosion, but the kinetic energy would shatter the porcelain if there was no other shielding. Therefore, the cannon has been surrounded in lightweight mithril, the only material capable of restraining this level of firepower while still being mobile and usable. Finally, the entire cannon has been enchanted for greater recoil control, firepower, and mobility. With the expertise of our artificers, engineers, smiths, and artillerists, we have turned mana itself into a weapon of mass destruction. We have only been able to build one Dames tear Reactor Megacannon, but we do not need more than one. How can our enemies possibly face us, when we can target them with something like this?
