Turbulence. It is when the Ynn churns, water turning white, breaking any branch or body it carries. It is a description of the ongoing debate over whether the Corrupted Ruinborn are in fact fellow YnnChosen and all its constant arguments. And now, it seems, turbulence has struck some of our trade relations. The ongoing debate has made some of our neighbors wonder whether we are proper adherents of the Faith or if we are near heretics, and the mistrust has seen them cancel contracts and send their merchant caravans elsewhere. But perhaps we will do better without those ossified relations, and the opportunity to seek new partnerships.
