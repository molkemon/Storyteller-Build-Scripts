A corrupt administration operates less to sustain itself and more to sustain its members. Consequently, as it begins to fall apart, the immediate reaction of most officials is not to save what they all stand to lose, but to grab what they can while it lasts. The country may not be on its way to a complete collapse, but the severe corruption reaching all the way from the lowest clerk to those closest to the ruler has corroded several crucial governmental functions, leading to a widespread destabilization of the realm. This has been a long time coming and we all pay the price.
