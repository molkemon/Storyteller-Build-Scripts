We have received a call to action from Parusahan Saya, one of our subjects within the heartlands of Rahen. The lands of Kramitra and Satarsaya have recently fallen under their control, and the new powers of the land have struggled to rein in the rebellious old powers who still run rampant within the territory. They have petitioned for a contingent of the palace guard to be dispatched so they may better exert control over the region and get back to producing more food for the rest of the Raj. This state has helped us before in the past and has proven to be loyal to the Raj in many other cases. Perhaps this has earned them a token of our respect?
