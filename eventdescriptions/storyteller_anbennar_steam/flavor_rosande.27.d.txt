.AND. YOU. HAVE. FAILED. Our ancestors came to Escann to collar savagery, and you have taken your hands off the leashes. From this day forward, your duty shall come BEFORE your pleasure.' The lord’s face was beet red, and he took a swig of wine so as to continue his tirade against the nobles assembled within the Crimson Palace. 'And that isn't all. You will need to prove yourself for army command. Your taxes.' The looks of outrage and fear on the assembled nobles faces quickly turned to horror, as the lord suddenly toppled over. Chaos erupted. Some nobles ran towards the exits, trying to evade the evil magics doubtless at work. Others retched up their meals, convinced that poison must have been the cause. Still more drew their swords and advanced upon their orkish servants, certain that one or the other must be the cause of the tragedy. After a few moments, a noble named Aline sil Leigalen took the stage. 'Friends. Let us not behave like orks in this dire hour. Our great ruler has been struck down, doubtlessly by poison applied by treacherous slaves. The poison even caused him to go mad in his final hours, turning against his gallant retainers despite my pleas. Now, in his memory, let us dispatch the perpetrators.'
