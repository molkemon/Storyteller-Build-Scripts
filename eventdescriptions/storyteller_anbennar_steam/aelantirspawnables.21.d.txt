With the increasing level of militarization within the Nimscodd Hierarchy, and with it the increasing restrictions and mandatory compliances imposed by central government, many gnomes of the Storm Isles, always a fractious bunch, have begun to chafe under the rule of their Cliff Gnome kin in the south. This came to ahead when a respected if irascible character, a Professor Sneecood, of the famous Storm Isle Sneecoods, after complaining of increased restrictions on his workshop, suddenly resigned as representative, after rumors started to swirl of impending charges of embezzlement. Within days Sneecood had somehow miraculously acquired a large amount of ships, with intent to set out on a scientific expedition to north aelentir and began extolling it as an opportunity for intelligent and determined gnomes to make something of themselves, with the opportunity to push the bounds of artifice in service of the progress, in the Nortiochand. Many Storm Gnomes flocked to join him, and before local governors had any idea what to do, the fleet had weighed anchor and departed for the cold north.
