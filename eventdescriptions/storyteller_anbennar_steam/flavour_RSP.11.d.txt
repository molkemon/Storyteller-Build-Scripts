Thud. No sound in the world can quite match it; a shovel striking wood buried beneath earth. For a heartbeat, the digging stops. Then the sand flies twice as fast. Another strike. Another. Shouts of excitement from both the hardened seadogs and the cabin boys. Finally, wood splinters. A lid cracks and the groan of rusted hinges is loud on the shores of the domain. The sun shining shyly through the canopy lends its golden rays to the pirates favourite find; gold. Enough of it for the coins to spill between fingers, for the pearls to tumble into the dirt. Judging by the cheers echoing across the beach, the crew shall be insufferable about this treasure trove for weeks. And rightfully so.
