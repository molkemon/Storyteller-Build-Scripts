The Dullahan is a hideous monster that appears as a headless rider on a horse, though in reality horse and rider are the same creature. It carries its head under one arm, with moldy skin and bulging eyes leering at terrified witnesses. The Dullahan gallops through the woods with hooves like thunder, calling out names in a terrible voice. And when you hear it say your name, you choke to death on your own breath. Thankfully, the Dullahan slows down while it takes victims, so their agents were able to subdue one for study near a village.
