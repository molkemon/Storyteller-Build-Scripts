The expedition has clearly had some effect on the flow of magma that once covered the steel bridge into the furnace. Only small amounts of magma now drip from the ceilings, leaving most of the bridge clear of magma and safe to pass. Despite this, there is still a roiling pool of magma beneath the bridge. As the scouts move ahead searching the magma for ambushing oozes, the expedition follows behind, staying away from the edges of the bridge as best they can.
