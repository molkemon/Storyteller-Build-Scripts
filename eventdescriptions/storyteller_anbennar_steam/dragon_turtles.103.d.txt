It is the case, or so my agents tell me, that some of your people have obtained some interesting, compromising information about governments abroad. Did you know that there are still kings who are weak, even as fully grown adults? Intriguing. Did you know that each time afterwards, he kills his housekeeper to prevent the information from getting out? Fascinating. It seems like somewhat of an overreaction, don’t you think? The question could just as easily be resolved by a bribe, or even a simple request to keep quiet. Perhaps someone has been whispering bad advice into his ear. But in any case, this information has no value in itself; the value is in what you can get him to do to keep his secret safe. I can think of lots of things. I can also think of lots of secrets, which will soon bubble up on the surface of Kanolowele like infected blisters. You should tell your agents to keep their ears out.
