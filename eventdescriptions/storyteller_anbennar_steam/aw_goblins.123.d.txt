It is a well-known fact that certain regions of the country have become host to large numbers of goblins in recent years. Our efforts to root them out have failed, and now they have become increasingly brazen in their large numbers. Over the past week, goblins have simultaneously erupted from their burrows across the country, intent on seizing as much wealth and land for themselves as possible. A great tide of goblins covers the land, a problem that has grown far too vast for adventurers to deal with by themselves.
