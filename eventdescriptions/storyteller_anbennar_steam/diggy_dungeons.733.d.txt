As we began to deal with the strange machine, disaster struck. Whether it was a button hit in error, a change wrought by a shift in pressure from someone leaning against it, it cannot be said. The device exploded in a ball of sparks and metal shrapnel, sending the expeditionaries around it to the ground, struck down or dead depending on the size and amount of shrapnel, even piercing armor. Those still standing were not without injury, and many felt as if they should have left it well enough alone.
