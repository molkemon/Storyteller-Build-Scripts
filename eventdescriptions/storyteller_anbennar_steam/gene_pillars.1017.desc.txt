Once again, the party's mage and artificer are at each other's throats. This time the argument has spiraled beyond cutting words, and the two look ready to come to blows, with the mage's hands wreathed in flames and the artificer brandishing a dangerously sparking device. Fortunately, they managed to deescalate the situation before one of them was turned into an unusually energetic corpse. It seems the argument began as a debate over who would receive the magically powerful relics the party had found, which would be of immense value to both the mages and the artificers. Neither of them is willing to proceed until the issue is settled.
