There is no idle chatter as the Red Sentries march into what was once a village. Every building is blackened and burnt by flames. None remain but ash and skeletons. One soldier stops moving, focusing on a single point; the ruins of a building, with only a stone foundation still visible. In the building sit three skeletons wrapped around each other; one of which is the third the size of the other two.  Nothing is said, but each and every soldier’s face can be read; this brutality cannot continue. The savages will fall, and that will be the end of it. Never again will villages see this desolation.
