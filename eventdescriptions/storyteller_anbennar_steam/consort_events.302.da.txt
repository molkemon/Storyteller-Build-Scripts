The annual celebration of the royal union between the current monarch and their dynasty and their consort has taken place. It’s difficult to imagine that our realm had it any other way; that there was a time when the consort was growing up far away from our monarch, who spent their days alone in the capital. It all seems so natural now. How time flies. It has now been over two decades since the consort married the monarch, and even though they are not children anymore, it is with some glee that they accept gifts in honor of their wedding anniversary.
