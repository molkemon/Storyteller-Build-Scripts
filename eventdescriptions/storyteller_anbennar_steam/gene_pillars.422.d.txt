Towers of thorns rise in all directions within this room, blocking the path of the expeditionaries as they push ever onward into the bowels of the structure. Some test the spines and find them to be as hard as rock, while others gingerly touch the sides of the stems and feel a deep warmth and a sense of rushing fluid. These are living thorns, come to stand guard against those who wish to take things that are not theirs. Slipping through the branches is a small squirrel, carrying valuables within its puffed cheeks. Some try to push through the thorns only to emerge with countless abrasions sprinkled on their skin. They wish to dive into the tangle still, in pursuit of the folly of wealth. How greed will change a man.
