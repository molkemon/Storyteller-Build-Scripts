There is a moment of calm as the beast’s thrashing ceases, leaving its titanic form floating in a slick of blood and ichor. For a heartbeat, the sea falls silent, then the deck erupts with cheers as the crew swarms the corpse like seagulls, tearing at it with billhooks and blades in search of trophies. As a sailor offers the monarch an immense tooth pried from the beast’s maw, the ruler reflects on the successful hunt, and on that part of the experience they learned most from.
