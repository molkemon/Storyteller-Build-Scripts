Raids in the plains of Fangaula and Horashesh have been commonplace for centuries, but such indifference to proper ownership cannot be taken lightly if we are to rule these lands. Lately, Khetist raiders from the northeast of our lands have become more and more brazen in their attacks on our ranchers and villages. With little regard for anything beyond the loot they can carry on the backs of their horses, these raiders sow destruction while we try to sow seeds for harvest. It is time we retaliate with force.
