They said it would never work. They said we could never cage our largest rat breeding program right next to the largest Serpentsbloom warehouse in the country. Well, they were wrong, because that is exactly what we did. This was, in reflection, possibly a slight miscalculation on our part, as the cages lasted approximately three days before the rats gnawed through the bars to get at the stores of Serpentsbloom. No worries though, all we have to do is track down thousands of drugged-up pedigree rodents with a newly acquired taste for premium magic mushrooms.
