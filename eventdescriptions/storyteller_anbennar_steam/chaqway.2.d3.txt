We have cleared a space. We have peppered the square with stones, as the people of the lakes do, that it may be more easily swept, surer in times of rain. This is a place where the flow of exchange pools for a moment, and we may drink our fill. A few clans have volunteered to leave half their number behind; the broken halves reform as a new clan of this place. By such means is the Banche trade network expanded and strengthened.
