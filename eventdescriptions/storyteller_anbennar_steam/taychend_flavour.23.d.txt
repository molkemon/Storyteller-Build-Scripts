For months, the soldiers of the realm had been prowling the hills of the province, looking for the beast that had caused so much trouble. For months, patrols were wiped out, villagers kept disappearing, and yet not one elf ever managed to lay eyes on the beast and live. The military presence was increased, and still nothing. Desertions began, and stricter discipline was imposed, but to no effect; the harsh measures imposed only led to the soldiers losing heart in the chase itself. Eventually, the venture proved too costly to continue, and the commanders have recommended we halt the campaign and leave the villagers to their fate. With even the military giving up, there is little reason to ignore that recommendation.
