Bearkilla, the Immortal, the Strongest. Dush was called many things by our clan, but death was never one of those. Until today. One of our boys found him dead in his tent alongside his sister after hearing a sudden lack of loud rambling, meaning besides the fact that the one thing keeping our lads together was stone cold on the floor, his entire line ended, leaving us without a boss and with no clear strongest to lead us. But fear not, our clan has faced this issue many times since the day of founding and has found a great solution each time... Look over there, a rainbow rothe.
