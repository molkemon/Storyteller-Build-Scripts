We have long feared what lay to the north. A failed expedition may have haunted us, but the Ashhana turned that low lying fear front and centre, scarring our empire beyond repair for a millenia. Yet now we stand in control of these darkened lands, the threat they pose to us, and thus the eternal empire, vanquished forevermore. Similarly, we have long feared the Ashhana, feral mockeries of our people who tore through Taneyas and sacked the once great city of Shalazar. But now they have been pushed into the corners of the great Jungle, the terrors who once ruined us declawed and forced to work for us. Despite all we have done to make the Ashhana pay, they are still not suffering to the degree they inflicted upon us, to the degree that they deserve. And we have now acquired a vast and dark swathe of land where the Ashhana can rot, away from our pure jungle in a land of corruption and decay. The current inhabitants can stay or flee; we care not for the trolls or those they have kidnapped. Most are choosing the latter, but reports of a few choosing to stay and even help us with trapping the Ashhana. Yet whether we get helped or not, the strongest and vilest will serve in our armies, the rest will rot forever in a land of Darkness.
