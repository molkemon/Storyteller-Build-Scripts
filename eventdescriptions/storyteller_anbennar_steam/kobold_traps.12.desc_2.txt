The Bluescales are coming. A horde of Bluescale Kobolds has arrived in this domain, and they have immediately begun acting like the caverns have always belonged to them. They’ve come to escape another domain, and they’ve brought with them all of the Bluescale classics. dragonpriests who insist that they know the dragons better than anyone else, an imperious claim of sovereignty over the caverns, and a hundred breeds of mushrooms and traps. The locals, put simply, are pissed. The new migrants claim that we must put aside our differences, and allow them to aid us in defending the caverns so that all kobolds might live another day, but there is only so much we are willing to tolerate in the furtherance of koboldkind. The Bluescales demand rights, privileges, and autonomy; they demand to be taken seriously within our tribe and viraxia. We will allow them to construct extra defenses, certainly, but it is unclear what else we should do. How should we handle these newcomers?
