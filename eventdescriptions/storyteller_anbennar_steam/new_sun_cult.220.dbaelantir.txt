There is one god, Surael. He is the sun above, whose light brings life to all of Bulwar. But as anyone who has seen His radiance shine through a misty afternoon or a glass prism knows, when that light reaches Halann it may take many forms and many colors. So perhaps some now see Surael as a different god, the One God, whose last fragment reveals new inventions and old truths. That is not an issue for the crown of the realm, no matter how much the priesthood whines. The Ravelians may remain and flourish, and with them, so shall we all. After all, anyone who has seen a rainbow knows it is one of Surael's most beautiful gifts to us; and it could not be if it was all one color. So too our radiance grows with the Ravelians' insights, and we see Surael's beauty ever more clearly.
