After decades of bickering, it is time to end the dual fleet system of Zuvavim. Although the competition between the two sides did ensure innovativeness, the squabbles caused far too many problems for the system to be maintained. It used to be that the fleets held too much power for the state to overrule any individual side; at least not without upsetting their backers in the delicate balance of power between the Medawor and Efergezmu. That is no longer a concern of ours, and the time has come to choose whether to end the fleet whose backers have lost power in Zuvavim.
