As long time allies of Krakdhumvror, we recognize their unique position in the Serpentspine. Fairly isolated, they have been able to endure the ork hordes and goblin invasions that overran most of the Western Serpentspine. However, this isolation also puts them at a disadvantage in terms of growth. We should propose a deal in which we provide a trade outlet and inlet, shipping them grain and other products. In return, we will receive help in crafting weapons and armour.
