The Khets' requirements for tribute are always hard to interpret from his subjects' standpoint. This is because the decision on whether tribute is accepted or not is always dependent on a personal whim of the Khetarch. As it stands now, one of the subjects has presented their tribute a day late, and so the Khetarch demands they pay additional interest for the entirety of this year, as they have officially missed the deadline. In their defense, the subject argues that they missed it due to an emissary appearing late to announce the current year's tribute requirements.
