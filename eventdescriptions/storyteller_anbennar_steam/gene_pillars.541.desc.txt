As we finally start making our way into the upper parts of the tower, we find ourselves having to climb up through a hole in the ceiling to reach the next level. As soon as we have all managed to catch our breath, one of us notices a gentle movement in the corner of the room. A second later, a branch rakes out from the corner, nearly taking one of our heads off. As we back away, another strike from a large twisted root comes at us from the other corner. As we survey our surroundings, we notice roots rapidly growing over our escape route; the trees lining the walls of the room slowly start to come to life.
