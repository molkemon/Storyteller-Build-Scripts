For some time, you could ignore it as a figment of your imagination, a hyperactive mind filling the gaps in the unknown while you proceed deeper into the abyss. However, as you descend, it becomes clear that to ignore your senses is denial: the slow pulse is real. Every so often, it almost seems like there is a trick of the light distorting the water. Every so often, the walls seem to shimmer, as if the entire pillar is shifting. Every so often, the location itself groans. When these flickers ripple through space, the faintest beat can be heard, echoing throughout the water. And perhaps your fear is getting the best of you, but it almost feels like as you descend, the pulses are getting louder.
