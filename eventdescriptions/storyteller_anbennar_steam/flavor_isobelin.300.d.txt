"A great light cast out upon the sea, the torch of the Thorned Lady guiding me to an open door, an invitation to take part in a golden dream." From the Journal of Aldarion Seamoon, 1766.  One can scarcely see common ground between the disparate lands of Halann and all the fractious peoples in them. However, the same sun rises and sets on their days, and the same moon and stars light their nights. Another light has joined them, the light of our city, illuminating their way across the sea to their dreams.
