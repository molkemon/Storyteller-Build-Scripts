The expansion of magical networks throughout the realm has allowed for magic to become more accessible to those within the nobility and lower class alike. This in turn has made it easier for those who hold ill intentions to sneak into mage towers and guild halls to steal powerful artifacts they do not understand, or access old spellbooks which give them power they cannot control. Security of these items is important to ensure that they cannot be used by those who mean to cause harm, but restriction of these resources will cause progress in improving the infrastructure to be delayed as access becomes more difficult.
