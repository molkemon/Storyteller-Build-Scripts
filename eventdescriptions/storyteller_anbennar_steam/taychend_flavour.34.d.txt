The Sarihaddu and Chendhyan tribes live very similar lifestyles; semi-nomads in the dry lands of western Taychend, who move from grazing spot to grazing spot in a similar cycle each year. Unfortunately for both groups, there are limited grazing spots available, and so they often get into fights with each other over land and resources, despite the efforts of those who would prefer the two groups cooperate. In a specific domain, once such a struggle has occurred between two minor tribes of the respective cultures, this dispute has become such a problem that the local elites of the province have asked our ruler to intervene. We must choose wisely, as this issue has attracted enough attention that our choice will be noticed by the entirety of both groups within our empire.
