One amongst the number of monster hunters we have gathered to our cause has attracted our attention due to his natural leadership talents. Galin síl Vekt has cleansed more than his fair share of vampire nests and survived to tell the tale, becoming something of an unofficial leader amongst his peers in the process. His fellow vampire trackers have begun to call him ‘Leechfinder General’, partly as an honorific, partly as a wellmeaning jest playing on the gradual militarization of their ranks. We now have the opportunity to make this title a reality and grant him far reaching authority over the campaign to finally rid Corvuria and its neighbors of the vampiric infestation plaguing these lands. If we want to succeed in liberating the abused people dwelling in the shadowy lands beneath the Godshield mountains from the yoke of the bloodsucking monsters that have oppressed them for centuries, his services will surely prove to be invaluable.
