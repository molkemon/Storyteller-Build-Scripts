Word has reached us of a conflict in the Bay of Chills between our trading partner Everwharf and the lordship of Adshaw. According to reports, several warships from Everwharf were sighted attacking and sinking a few unarmed Adshaw merchant vessels. Though they claim otherwise, the countless eyewitness reports seem to prove their guilt. Adshaw demands justice and is preparing to march soldiers into the city to receive it. The question is, do we believe Everwharf's claims of innocence? If true, their actions would be grounds to eject them from the league, but if they have indeed been framed, then we are obligated to defend them.
