At the close of the 14th century, we led a joint campaign with Gawed to retake Royvibobb from reavers. For our human collaborators, this was a simple mission which would clear a stubborn base of pirates. For us, on the other hand, it was our first chance in a thousand years to reclaim our home.  From that fortress whose gnomes once founded the First Hierarchy, we followed in our ancestors’ footsteps: we made a deal with humans while scheming for something greater. By agreeing to a fiftyfive year truce with the Alenics, a mainland beachhead was secured. With it, we could finally prepare for the reconquest of the Dragon Coast.  We have been planning that reconquest for the past halfcentury, but time is running out. Our truce will last for five more years: so if we wish to be seen as a legitimate power, we must conquer the Dragon Coast and ally ourselves with Gawed or Lorent. If we cannot do so, all of this will have been for naught.
