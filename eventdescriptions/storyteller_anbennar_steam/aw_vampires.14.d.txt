It’s been known for a while now that a neighbouring nation has an issue with vampires who care little for keeping their presence hidden. Their open attacks on travellers and presence in towns and other settlements have put the fear of the night in many hearts. The ever increasing paranoia of the population and rival predators makes finding prey a harder task, and thus they seek greener pastures. Recently, some of these vampires and their offspring have been moving towards our lands and establishing lairs of their own. Our people are reporting disappearances and strange figures observing homes from afar.
