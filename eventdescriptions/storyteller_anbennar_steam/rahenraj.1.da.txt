Dhenijansar has fallen, and with it, the old ruler. Across Rahen, former enemies and allies alike acknowledge us as the new leader of the harimraj. As much as things change, however, things stay the same; Prabhis and Senaptis bicker and squabble, harimari and humans still struggle to get along, and trade still flows upon the Kharunyana, but for once, it is us who sit upon the throne in Dhenijansar.
