Day by day. Night by night. The calls of piety and clashes of zealots erupt across Cannor. Religious conflict devours the continent under a flame of passionate faith and belief in Corin, and the immediate response from those faithful to Adean. This is a time of troubles, terror and wanton bloodshed. However, to the associates of our lord, this is a time of opportunity to rightfully decry the injustice levied against the Infernal Court. Anxiety stirs in the heart of our lord. Their associates in the cults douce their worries with wine and brandy, urging them to head into the royal court and declare our lord and the government loyal to the betrayed and unjustly punished Infernal Court. "For the cause. For us." they whisper. "Please, our lord. For your friends. We are so close." Our lord stands. Pushing through the heavy doors, they stare down their confused advisors and associates; those in the know smiling and nodding, whilst most simply tilt their head in confusion. Finally, our lord speaks.
