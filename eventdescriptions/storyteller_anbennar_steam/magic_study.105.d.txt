It is known that within the bounds of a certain province lie ancient relics from the Elven Precursors. The magical knowledge they held was immense, and a close examination of the artifacts they left behind would no doubt be invaluable to our magical research. So far, our delving into their secrets has been limited to those relics we have been occasionally able to purchase from the market or uncover from ruins, leading some to propose more organized extraction measures. However, such actions might prove disruptive to the local economy and ecology, and the locals might not be pleased. This is not to mention the risk, however slim, of explosive backlash from toying with forces beyond our control.
