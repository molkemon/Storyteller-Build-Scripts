The Peacocks have succeeded in making institutions of the realm increasingly representative, but one institution that has resisted recent changes is the army. One of our generals belongs to a family of nobles who have strong ties to the ruling dynasty and has been sabotaging their efforts to oust the dynasty loyalists from high-ranking military positions. Now, she has gone even further and wants one of her relatives, also a Peacock, to act as advisor to the monarch. While this young person seems like a talented commandant, the Sparrows would loathe giving them influence.
