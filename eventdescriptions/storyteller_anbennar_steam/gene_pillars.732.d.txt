Walking through the water, you pass by rows and rows of old tanks, indicating this whole building was once filled with air, even at this depth. Of course, that poses the question: what was once in those tanks? No sooner is this question asked than it is answered: an immense cloud of mucus starts to drip through the air bubble your party had been walking within. It seems like some property of the mucus is weakening the magic fueling your shield. If this goes on much longer, you may be crushed by the weight of the sea collapsing inward on your party.
