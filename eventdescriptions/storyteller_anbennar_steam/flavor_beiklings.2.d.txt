Our war against the Jiangshi of Jinqiu has taken an ironic turn. As our troops crossed the border into the rugged borderlands of the Black Jade Citadel, we encountered envoys from the league of Xiongxiap. It appears that they, likely anticipating the imminent change in overlords, are willing to support our invasion against the Eternal Council, in return for certain concessions and guarantees under the new regime. While their lack of loyalty is deplorable, their duplicity stands to benefit us. They are weak, and while they are ready to offer us access to either their coffers or their warriors, their envoy insists that they cannot part with both. But, the envoy is quick to point out, there may be an alternative. Weizhen Suzhen, the current leader of the league, is the mind behind this plot, and he stands to lose everything if it fails. We could, rather than merely accept his modest offers of support, put our full support behind him instead. Doing so would no doubt galvanize the league into a far more formidable force, and would make Suzhen dependent on us in perpetuity.
