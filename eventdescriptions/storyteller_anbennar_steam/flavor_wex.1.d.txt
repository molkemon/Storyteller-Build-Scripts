At the very end of the Lilac Wars in 1443 the Wexonard army besieged and sacked the imperial capital of Anbenncost in order to defeat the late Grand Duke of Dameria, Adenn "Skylance".  The Wexonard army remained for several months after the war's end, looting and pillaging to their hearts content. It is said that even during the coronation of Grand Duke of Wex, Lothane, into the imperial throne of Anbennar, that the city was still a victim to Wexonard greed.
