This chamber seems surprisingly stark for being so deep into the pillar. Only a single door, recessed into the wall, marks any security measures in the space. But when the party tries to cross, the door slams closed and a pit opens just before their feet, threatening to swallow them whole. Trying a different path, this, too, triggers a security response, moving the pit in front of the party once again. Trying to jump causes arrows to fly out, magic is met with blasts of fire, and tools are wrenched out of hands with powerful gusts of Evocation magic. It almost seems as if the door knows what the party is planning before it happens.
