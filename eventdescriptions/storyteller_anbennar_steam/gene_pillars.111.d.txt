Conthus guides the party into the upper levels of the pillar, which have survived the churning waters outside mostly intact compared to the lower chambers. "Tread carefully," the mechanical body intones, "for this is the place where we tested magic on many different creatures. Things encountered here may be unstable."As he finishes his disquieting explanation, a sound like trees crashing in the forest reverberates down the hall, and the party sees some kind of gargantuan primate with three arms and four heads wailing in the hall.
