Duwarkani started as a marriage between a human and harpy royal family in a pact as equals, a tradition which has maintained ever since. When a Queen of the Menkaunswt dynasty rises to lead the nation, her first duty is to choose a consort to share the throne and the duties of the monarchy. One must look for a husband who can assist in administering affairs within the growing nation, be amicable with the many diplomatic visitors, and be able to direct the mixed-race army of the nation should the need arise. These traits can be surprisingly difficult to find amongst the nobility, but the monarch has narrowed the list down to her two preferred candidates.
