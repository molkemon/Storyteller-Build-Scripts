Some time ago we brought in a foreign advisor. He presented himself as a scholar and as lord of so and so, heir to such and such. At the time we had not paid much attention to his origins; as he had entertained us with stories of his many feats in other courts, and quite frankly we couldn’t have cared less where he was from.  Recently however, tensions over a border area have been rising in a place very familiar to our foreign advisor. He has recently informed us of a succession crisis surrounding his family and how it pertains to the border troubles at hand. He holds a claim to a strategic piece of the area in question and suggests that if we were to press his claim, he could integrate it into our country. If we accept the offer, we would be in a strong diplomatic position against our neighbor, as we would have one of those "casus belli" they’re always on about.
