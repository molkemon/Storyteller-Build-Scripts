A trader with wings of shimmering gold. A socialite, her feathers each dyed a distinct hue. The courts and gardens of the realm are now resplendent as a new fashion for colorful feathers has gripped our nation. The demand for dyes is insatiable, and our merchants eagerly oblige, bringing exotic shades from across Halcann. Indigo from Rahen, deep reds from the halflings of Cannor, one trader even claims to have brought seaweed dyes made by the lizardfolk of Taneyas. The dye frenzy shows no sign of stopping, but among the more traditional matriarchs there are whispers that this is a dangerous excess only fit for ostentatious peacocks.
