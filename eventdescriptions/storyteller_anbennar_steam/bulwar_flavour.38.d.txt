The rivers of Bulwar are the second greatest blessing Surael gave His people, only after the blazing sun in the sky above. The great Suran and mighty Buranun transform an arid land into a fertile cradle, driving desert along with starvation back much as Surael beats back the Darkness. This truth has given recent events a chilling undertone. The Suran has swollen to a volume not seen in centuries, overflowing its banks and flooding the surrounding lands. Crops have rotted under feet of water, irrigation channels have been washed away, even some citizens of the realm have drowned in the face of a flashflood. We will recover as best we can, and break out the emergency grain supplies to stave off famine, but many will never look at the Suran the same way, knowing this great lifegiver can deal death and devastation as well.
