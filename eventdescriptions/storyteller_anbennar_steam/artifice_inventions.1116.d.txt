Using magical means to fly is an ancient timehonored tradition in Bulwar, but due to the complexity of the inscription spellwork required to create these vaunted flying carpets, chariots, and battle platforms, their use has been largely restricted to military applications and as a travel conveyance for the high and mighty. Thanks to recent breakthroughs in artificery regarding the sustained manipulation of gravity, the ability to transport large groups of personnel or heavy goods across large distances through the air has finally been acquired. The so-called sunspire pylons form a network of docking stations for large levitating platforms that traverse along established routes within the network. Instead of being occupied with the fraught and time-consuming business of landing, these platforms simply unload their freight on the lofty upper level of the pylons and take on new cargo, all while being recharged for the next leg of their journey, before they take off again. The pylons themselves do not serve only as simple waypoints for the levitating platforms, however. Part of their task is to temporarily reduce the weight of any cargo item or person cleared for aerial transport, in order to optimally utilize the limited space aboard the levitating platforms without overburdening them in the process. Once the cargo has been offloaded and brought to ground level, its normal weight is restored.
