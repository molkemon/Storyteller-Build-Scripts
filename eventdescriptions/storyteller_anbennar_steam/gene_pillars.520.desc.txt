We hear the tapping of its claws against the inside tunnel of the central structure; hard, razor-sharp claws digging into the metal. Then we see it: a large creature covered in fur, its tail bushy with extra long strands of hair. In its mouth are two large and long sets of upper and lower teeth, and large black eyes. It is a hinuisquirrel. As it reaches the ground we spring the trap and after a long period of struggle we manage to subdue the creature. We quickly realize that the creature is actually quite amenable and maybe even downright friendly when well fed. It may also be more intelligent than we first thought. Maybe we can even convince it to help us up and down the tower.
