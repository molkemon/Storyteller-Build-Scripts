Eyes dart around, searching for Aldorlbur as the oppressive heat grows closer and the roaring louder. Right when it feels as though the elemental should be upon them, the roaring abruptly ceases. For a moment, silence. Then Aldorlbur tears through the pipes on the flank of the expedition like no more than paper, effortlessly incinerating those closest. As the expedition members cry out and lunge for safety, it pauses rather than pressing on and instead circles around. Is it toying with the expedition, or genuinely wary of them? It matters not, as the torn pipes open a new escape route for the expedition. Escaping unscathed from the elemental while it is so close will be difficult, especially as it becomes inured to such a strategy. Still, surely it's a better option than rushing to a fiery demise?
