Many soldiers see our ongoing war as a conflict of faiths. This has led to some debate around one of our ongoing sieges, where the local population are of a certain faith. Normally, our troops would supply the siege by taking what we need from the locals, but the pious in our army think we should spare our brothers and sisters of that faith, even at the cost of the siege dragging out for longer. Which comes first among the people, faith or war?
