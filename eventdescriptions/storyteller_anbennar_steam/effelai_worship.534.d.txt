We are faced with the greatest challenge ever presented before us since the dawn of our people. We set forth now to defy the different legendary beasts of the Tswohvvwoh’ii; we call them godbeasts for lack of a better name, though we know they are no gods. Intelligent creatures, these lifeforms are massive even when compared to the other gargantuan lifeforms in the jungle. They are also far more dangerous. The GodBeast that rules over the Eastern Tswohvvwoh’ii lands is a flesh-eating plant with thousands of heads that extends from roots deep below the surface that it can move and uproot at will to just below the top of the tree canopy. Though it seems to be made entirely of plant matter, it rends and tears its prey with the ruthlessness and skill of a carnivorous animal. Even worse, unlike virtually all other creatures in the Tswohvvwoh’ii, it is seemingly independent of the Goddess’ control. Did She let it loose as a test for us or itself, or did it break free by its own strength of will? We do not know. But we suspect that defeating this creature will not only win us eternal glory and memory but the Goddess’ favour.
