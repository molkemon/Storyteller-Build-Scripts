The room we have just entered at least seems harmless. All of the experiments seem to be contained in a number of liquid filled capsules. Within those capsules there appear to be pieces of flesh and plants, grafted together for purposes we cannot guess. We see, for example, a suspended arm with what looks to be a wooden skeleton or a lizardlike tail, covered in scales seemingly made of bark. The room has been somewhat emptied already, however. There is some evidence that whatever was being studied here was transported away, perhaps to another pillar.
