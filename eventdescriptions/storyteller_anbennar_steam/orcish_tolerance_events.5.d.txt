At first it was a single occurrence; a manor of a minor aristocrat being sacked and pillaged with little trace of the culprit and its indentured servants also vanishing similarly. This would be the first of many similar events occurring in the countryside. While initially thought to be the work of bandits and other such brigands, we’ve now received live witnesses and their accounts on the latest of these attacks. As it turns out, it seems the slaves from the initial attack somehow broke free of their shackles and took their anger out on their master’s property before disappearing into the untamed frontier, only to appear to conduct the same act on other slave holders wherever they can find them. With each raid, they grow larger and bolder. Something must be done, else we’ll have a full revolt on our hands soon. The question on everyone’s mind, however, is what?
