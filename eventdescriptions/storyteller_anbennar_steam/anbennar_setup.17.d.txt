The Exodus Goblins are recent arrivals in Bulwar. Driven from their homes in the hills and mountains of the Serpentspine by the orks, they descended on Bulwar in two great hordes, cutting a swathe through the exhausted competitors in Bahar. Their numbers are vast and supplemented by desperate humans who come to their banner as mercenaries. Time will tell if this is enough to survive the resurgence of the Sun Elves.
