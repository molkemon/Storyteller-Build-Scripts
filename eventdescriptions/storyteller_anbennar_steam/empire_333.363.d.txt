It has taken a drastic shift in tactics, throwing the rulebook out the window, and doing whatever crazy ideas come to mind using whatever lizardfolk happen to be in the force at the time, but it is finally happening. The Hivemind cannot handle whenever they do something different. Whenever they are forced to fight a different type of lizardfolk with a different sort of mutation from the last fight, they lose their composure and freeze in place. Their anger is palpable, and it gives them strength. The lizardfolk of Taneyas and beyond are starting to believe.
