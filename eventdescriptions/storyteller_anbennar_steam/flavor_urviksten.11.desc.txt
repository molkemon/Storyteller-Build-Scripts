The powerful Ebonfrosts. Since the first of their line fought his way up from lowly reaver to become the forty-ninth emperor of Castanor, the family name has been synonymous with power and resourcefulness in Gerudia. The dynasty was presumed to have died out, but it now seems that there still exists a member, although their claim to the throne is considered weak. Since the current monarch lacks an heir, should we perhaps acknowledge this Ebonfrost as a legitimate heir?
