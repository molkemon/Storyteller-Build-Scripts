The Itayzemby lizardfolk have long found success within the armies of the Vyzemby as mercenary auxiliaries. Their naval prowess and tough scales make them a boon for any war fleet, and their considerable size makes them ideal shock troops. Even the halflings with the most isolationist leanings can scarcely avoid one or two such auxiliary companies within their ranks. Perhaps this is why, when excavating the vaults of Vadronoma, the trophy room caused such a stir. The centerpiece of the collection was the skeleton of a naga, posed in a striking position with a spear impaled through its skull. Additional skulls of all but the most recent, and even a few now extinct, lizardfolk mutations lined the walls. The room was remarkably well preserved, sealed away by layers of dried clay for centuries.
