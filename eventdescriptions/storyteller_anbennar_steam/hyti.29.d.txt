There is no place as opposed to the well-being of the Dwarovar. There is no space to maneuver, no light to see, no wind to invoke. And yet the call of the unknown coming from below the mountains has continued to ensnare the scientific and adventuring types. Whether they hope for material gains, for ancient dwarven knowledge, or are just in it for the thrill, every year a handful of them organize an expedition for the caverns. It does not matter that many never return. News from the latest one of those, a scientifically focused endeavor this time, just reached the surface.
