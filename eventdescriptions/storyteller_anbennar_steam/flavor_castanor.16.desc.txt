Legion Square is host to both horse and man today, standing at attention. With the only movement being the sway of their standards in the wind, emblazoned with a striking thunderbolt, the Thundersworn Legion is a sight to behold. Unlike the rest of the primarily infantry focused Legions, they have been trained to be masters of cavalry and mobile warfare. Will legends be told of the daring charges of the Thundersworn Legion? Only time will tell.
