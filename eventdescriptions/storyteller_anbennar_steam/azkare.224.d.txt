In his lifetime, Hiderion has experienced much loss, and never is its sting lessened. Today, he has received news that his beloved friend Dagran Krakovar has finally passed from the world. Together they drew up the plans for a new, better kind of empire, one which served all. It was with Krakovar’s help that Hiderion’s lofty ideals became concrete and present. But it was not just the expertise of the dwarf; he was Hiderion’s long time friend, and after the many decades they knew each other, his absence will leave a fresh hollow in the elf’s heart.
