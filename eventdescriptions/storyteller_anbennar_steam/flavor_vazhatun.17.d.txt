Everything is grand in Tianlou. The massive High Temple at the city's heart is the largest in Haless. The Great Storm, visible across the sea on clear days, resembles an entire mountain range made out of wind and rain. And of course, the city itself is no less impressive than Sarisung or Sramaya. So too are the riches of its merchants and the profits that can be made if one has the right goods. DûrVazhatun possesses exclusive knowledge, and exclusivity is valuable. By making the right deals and forging the right connections in Tianlou, they have been able to leverage the latest scientific and philosophical works in DûrVazhatun for a high profit from the city's merchant and ruling classes.
