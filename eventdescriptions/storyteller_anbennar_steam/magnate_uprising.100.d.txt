Friends and countrymen, lend me your ears. This day the Kingdom of Gawed, which was an oppressive and witless state ruled by the incompetent, is no more. In its place shall rise the Northern League, governed by the esteemed Magnates, in order to guarantee the rights of all the people of our realm, and resist those who stand in the way of the glory and welfare of our nation. Amidst massive celebrations throughout the capital and the whole realm, the Northern League has been declared.
