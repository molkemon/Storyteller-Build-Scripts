We are finally getting close to the innermost core of the pillar, but as Stumble fixes up one of the last doors needed to enter the inner sanctum, a strange surge passes through the pillar. It seems our previous tampering has also provided power again to this part of the structure, and the sudden power surge seems to have somewhat overloaded the device siphoning power from the sandworm. With it temporarily out of commission, the worm has roared back to life. It seems to be in the process of repeatedly rolling around. We better buckle down.
