Against all odds, we have done it. The Mengi have been massacred. The Baashidi have been beaten back. The lizardfolk are licking their wounds. The Dakinshi have been defeated once again. All who would seek to subjugate us have failed. The Lady of Drums may have set us upon this path, but even she could not do it alone. Together, however, we are unstoppable. We are the Swift Legged Bongo—nay, we are more than that. Under our hand, the Tanizu have been united once again. Across the jungle, we hear the most joyous symphony of all—that of victory.
