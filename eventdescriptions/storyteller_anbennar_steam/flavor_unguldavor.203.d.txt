After ten long years, it was done. Where once Ozarm’chadashes left several leaders dead, now it was merely a test of competence that led to nepotism disappearing. Where once bold and pointless charges led to shocking defeats, now orks fired in formation and were drilled to honor group cohesion. New traditions replaced old ones all throughout Unguldavor. Traditions that harmed no one, such as venerating ancestors and warriors, were kept while destructive traditions were abolished. The result was a state suited for the modern world instead of being stuck in the past.
