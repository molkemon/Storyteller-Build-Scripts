In the aftermath of our latest expedition to the Gene Pillar in that province, teams have been left in place to slowly sift through the mountain of data now available to us. Among their discoveries is a device which, the team believes, is intimately connected to the function and continued violence of the Kiamoa, the raging wall of storms which form the very wall of our world. Such a fundamental alteration to the natural balance of Insyaa would be reckless folly, destructive to the entire continent in ways we cannot predict. We know that some odd thinkers; the daring wayfarers of the Endless Ocean, those Speaking Memory followers who revere the Dragon Turtle Hoki’eal, and the strange mechanim who follow the teachings of Golodh; seek to pierce the Kiamoa for one reason or another, but we are not they.
