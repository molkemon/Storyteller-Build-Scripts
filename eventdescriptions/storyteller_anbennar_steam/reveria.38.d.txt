With the plenitude of Tales from Gerudia being studied by chroniclers and spymasters, the lord has also grown interested in these Tales of old. The stories feature giants, dragons and other beasts that they grew up hearing about. Even some ancestors are mentioned in these tales, and they’re all in Gerudian too, which makes them pleasant to read. The Skaldhyrric faith might be incompatible with those from Cannor, yet it is reconcilable with traditions and folklore from Reveria.
