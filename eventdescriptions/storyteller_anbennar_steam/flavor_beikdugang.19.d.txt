The existence of mechanim has set the scientific community on fire. They are an incredible new kind of thing, never before seen in the realm. The biggest initial debate was whether they were truly alive. It was a troubling question, as they do not share many of the traditional characteristics of life. They do not grow or age, they cannot reproduce, they do not eat or breathe. Still, many have pushed on to see if that was the end of it; they looked to see if they could think, if they could make something new, if they could feel. After a great long time, we are now certain they can; they are alive and it is a new wondrous form of life. After this declaration, mechanim from a distant domain have begun migrating to our lands en masse, and we have started to create new mechanim who will grow up with our ways as part of our people.
