It started as a wild idea, floating around teahouses and muttered after temple visits. With the realm seemingly falling apart, perhaps it would be better to simply leave? Perhaps to the new land that has opened up now that the everpresent Stormwall has fallen? The Rending of Realms has proven that ideas have a force, and a weight, and can grow in their telling. And soon refugees, and those seeing the same in their future, began to pool wealth they had been able to carry with them on their journey. They scraped together a fleet and supplies, and today they set out. The land and future that lies ahead is unknown, but what is unknown is embedded with hope, and there is no hope to be found here.
