Of all our new subjects, it has been the Xiaken who have been the most resistant to our beneficent rule. They have plotted and rebelled against us, using their considerable experience in both war and hostile diplomacy to pose a significant threat to our attempts to bring them, and all other Halessi, peace and civilization. But now, as our soldiers bring security to their lands and our administrators bring order and prosperity, it seems the Xia have finally realized what it means to walk with us towards a new dawn for all Haless.
