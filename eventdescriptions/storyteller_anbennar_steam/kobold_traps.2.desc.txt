The Dragon Coast is our home, and we are here to trap it. Gnomes, Reavers, Alenics, and more all want to take it from us, but we will hold fast against the tide. This is the home of dragons, and of our people; we will create every contraption and gizmo we can think of to protect it, and we will defend it until the day we die. Of course, the question still must be asked. where, specifically, do we want to create or upgrade our traps?
