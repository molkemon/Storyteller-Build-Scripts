The experiments of the Precursors tended to be strange, wild things, whose effects are both difficult to discern and detain in equal measure. But some organisms are clear in their purpose; they were designed to explore just how much the mortal body can suffer before it expires. Some of these experiments were so vile that even the Precursors shunned them, locking them away in the depths of their laboratories. These were the creatures whose creators were exiled to far corners of the empire to atone for their existence. The Unforgiveables. In the final level of the pillar, an incredible wailing surrounds the party as one such creature wallows in the passageway further on. A horrific mass of limbs and bodies, this poor construct seems less like a sentient creature and more like a living puddle of slime. Unfortunately, it blocks the only way forward.
