All are well fed. All have been given gifts for attending this conclave. Why does one give gifts? Is it an instrument to further one's own influence? Is it a token of friendship? The ruler's title perks up. They remember this. This was an important argument where they had to intervene. This argument would inevitably turn to be about the role of Irdaeos, and the gifts that he has given the centaurs. Arguments yet unsaid swirled in their head. It helps that there is no need for further thought, as it has been made up months and months ago. Why listen if the point has already been made? Four interpretations, of course. Each corresponding to its own sect. Each one fundamental in its approach and the future nature of our faith. The question? The nature of the gifts that Irdaeos has blessed us with. The answer was clear, and there was no doubt in the monarch's heart. The stars look favourable, and they would not disappoint them.
