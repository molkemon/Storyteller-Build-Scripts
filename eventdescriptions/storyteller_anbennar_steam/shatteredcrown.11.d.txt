In order to increase our influence over the ogre swamps and to simultaneously drain orks out of the overpopulated Amldihr area, a resettlement program will send thousands into the valley. Earlier settlers have already mixed with ogres, and out of this came a fearsome new species. half-ork, half-ogre, an Ogrillon. Hundreds of these are now reaching adulthood and have experienced trouble finding employment due to discrimination from both their parent races. With nowhere else to go, they are now starting to form into warbands, ready for hire.
