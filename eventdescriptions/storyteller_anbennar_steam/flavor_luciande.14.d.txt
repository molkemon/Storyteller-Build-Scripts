Whereas the continued security of the elite class within this nation depends on a certain degree of secrecy in our affairs, and whereas the difficulty of maintaining said security increases exponentially with the number of the elite class present, and whereas our lord Lucian’s supremacy over this nation must be absolute and paramount, now, therefore, our lord Lucian in the current year hereby enacts and proclaims the following law to take effect immediately. No man or woman other than Lucian may bring a new individual into the elite class unless done with Lucian’s express permission. The punishment for breaking this law is death for both sire and siree. All hail Lucian, our Lord, Protector, and Judge.
