Over a thousand years ago, in a time of great turmoil in Haless, venerated First Master Kai Jian of Jiangliusi summoned all the masters of the Accretive Path to a convention in the shadow of the Xiadao, where the disparate representatives of the various schools and paths forged bonds which would eventually be codified as the Xia itself. It is once again time to honor the most ancient tradition of the Xia.
