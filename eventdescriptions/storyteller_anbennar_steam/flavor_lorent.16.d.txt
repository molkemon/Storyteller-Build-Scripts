Lencenor is a land filled with conflict, family ties, and conflicts within the families. As it happens, Kylian Siloriel has finally inherited the throne of Ionnidar, resting place of Lorenan the Great held for centuries by the house sil Ionnidar and their predecessors, the Carantis. Now the question is, should he take this opportunity to claim the land directly, surely causing a stir amongst the Lencori nobility and breaking our ancient traditions? Or should he try to be seen as a diplomatic king, and simply allow their autonomy and let the bonds grow stronger? Good things come to those who wait.
