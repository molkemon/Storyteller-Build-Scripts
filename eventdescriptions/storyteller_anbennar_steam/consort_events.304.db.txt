The royal spouse possesses an innate curiosity in regards to the way they view the world. Understanding what makes people tick, or how a system comes together, is a topic often discussed around the spouse, and it is said that the interest began early, during their childhood in their homeland. These musings can keep them up late in the evenings, studying the texts of scholars, old or contemporary, or even making certain that their own ideas are jotted down for future benefit of the government.
