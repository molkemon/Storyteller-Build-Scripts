Although we have unified the cities of the Kheionai under our king, we have not yet unified their people. Not even a single city views our rule as legitimate; their craftsmen refuse to contribute to the kingdom, their nobility rejects our land claims, and the philosophers refuse to even acknowledge our religion. It will be an arduous journey to appease all the cultures and solidify our legitimacy across the region, but just how the ruler unified the Fasíri, so will their descendants unify all the Kheionai.
