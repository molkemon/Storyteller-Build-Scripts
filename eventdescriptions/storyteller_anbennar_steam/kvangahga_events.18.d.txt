After a funeral ceremony for a particularly well-known and notably corpulent merchant in a local district, several members of the community have fallen ill. Feasting on the honoured dead is important to ensure the strength and wisdom of their flesh is passed on to the community, but the severity of the mould and rot on this particular dead gnoll makes eating them undesirable. We could send in the army to help eat the gnoll, let it rot and upset the locals, or lead from the front - eat the gnoll ourselves.
