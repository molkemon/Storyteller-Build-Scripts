It is a formal duel, a wrestling match, and a backalley brawl, all wrapped into one. It is none of those things. Fighting a magical golem designed by a Castan millennia ago is not an easy fight, for it is resilient and hardhitting even if it is slow to move. The trial participant has been able to hit it hard, and it is clearly the worse for wear, but it is just when victory seems in reach that a particularly vicious shove finally hit and the participant was knocked to the ground. Before they could arise, the golem halfstumbled, halfleaped onto their prone body, engaging a messy grapple. This is where the fight is won or lost; what to do?
