My Prefect, Lord. We have a developing, but not yet critical, situation. The Discombobulatorim officials needed more magical power to fuel their experiments. And because of that, they used their government stations to persuade mages to participate in their tests. That in itself isn’t an issue, but the aftereffects are, as for some reason participants tend to die quickly during or after the experiments. And after the fifteenth one died in the middle of town, people are starting to talk about it, and not in a good way.
