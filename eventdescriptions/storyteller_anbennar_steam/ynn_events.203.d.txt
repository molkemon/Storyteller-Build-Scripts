While surveying the terrain in a distant region, a series of ruins was uncovered by our settlers. At first they noticed what appeared to be dilapidated remains of households under the driedup clay, then they dug out shards of metal and glass, and eventually they hit a layer of rubble that was burying elaborate mosaic floors of oncegreat halls. News of the discovery quickly made the rounds, attracting people from across the land looking for Precursor Relics. Local nobility became involved too, putting a bounty on any artifact that would be brought to them as soon as prospectors were able to confirm that the ruins belonged in fact to Domfanynn, the mythical lost capital of the Sarda Ruinborn and their oldest settlement after the Ruin of Aelantier. Of the ancient capital, the bestconserved remains were a pair of stone spires adorned with metal domes which, despite being sunk into a riverbed, remained shielded from the current by Precursorgrade magical wards. As to the salvageable artifacts, most made their way to lavishlypaying lords, but foolhardy settlers still tried to peddle some of them as Precursor Relics to outsiders, earning them the name of Fools Precursor Relics.
