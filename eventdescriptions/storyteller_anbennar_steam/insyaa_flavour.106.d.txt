Insyaa is a strange land, but just as it has terrors it has wonders beyond compare in its mangrove forests and hinui wilds. One of these wonders that they have found are the mechanim; they have endured since the days of the Precursors, and yet they still function. The workers of the realm work hard, but even the hardiest cannot work for millennia. The artificers of the realm have determined how the circuitry of the mechanim functions, and now they can use that technology to jumpstart not just their colonies on Insyaa, but development in their homeland as well. Via a small and easily producible chip, the durability of their mechanim workers can be pushed beyond their natural limits. Unimaginable pain and addictive pleasure both might suffice to get the mechanim working at previously unheard of paces by themselves, but when the two are combined, one mechanim can equal five or even ten regular workers in both speed and efficiency. Of all the wonders of Insyaa, a worker that never stops working might just be the finest of them all.
