The rift opens, and I can see. Vertigo, brief and intense, for the empty wall on one side of the room peers over a steep drop. The perch is poorly lit and smells faintly of rot, but there is also a strange chemical tang over the top. It is the work of the harpy, of course, with her illfitting clothes and her lank, greasy hair. Granny has too many wings, but this only fires the harpy’s interest further. "I have something for you to hunt," Illfanill says, and the covetous gleam in the harpy’s eye is terrible indeed.
