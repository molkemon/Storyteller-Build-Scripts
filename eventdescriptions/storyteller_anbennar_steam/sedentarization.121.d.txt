As the outpost in this area is quite young, it is exceedingly vulnerable. Not quite selfsustaining yet, it requires constant shipments and streams of supplies, settlers, and goods. The road to the outpost was constructed swiftly, and goes through miles and miles of land; as a result, each shipment is theoretically vulnerable to great beasts of the steppes, bandits, and centaur raiders. It is of vital importance that the supply lines from our outpost back towards the more populated parts of our realm are guarded, and that all travelers, merchants, and materials make it to the outpost safe. To do this, we could finance and recruit a force of mounted warriors to patrol the lanes back to the heartland of our empire. However, doing so would be costly in both gold and soldiers that could be better spent elsewhere, and using our own men is not the only option before us.
