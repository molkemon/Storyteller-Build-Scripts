A very large number of Gnolls has started to move over the imperial border from the south following the dissolution of the Hill Gnoll kingdom. Massive refugee encampments have formed in both the provinces of Gnollsgate and Countsbridge, reaching all the way to the edges of the cities. While they are certainly making the locals uneasy, they have not been violent, and they have sent envoys from among their number to the ruler to plead their case. They claim to have faced severe oppression in their own lands, and are seeking the protection of the emperor. They promise to adopt our customs and religion if only they are given the ability to live their lives as free citizens of the Empire of Anbennar.
