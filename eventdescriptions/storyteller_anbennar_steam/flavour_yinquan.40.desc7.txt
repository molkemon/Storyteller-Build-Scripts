As a result of political maneuvering seemingly going wrong, the monarch has made a number of very powerful enemies, including Liho's Maidens, the high ministers of the government, and a wandering merchant. They have come together to remove the ruler from office effective immediately. After a few hours of behind-closed-doors discussion, the monarch has agreed to the transition and a successor has already been prepared. Despite the inherent skullduggery of such a coup, it seems to have occurred without any actual issue; the state will continue on without any problems.
