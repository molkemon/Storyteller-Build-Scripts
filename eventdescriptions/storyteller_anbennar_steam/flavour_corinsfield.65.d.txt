A most infernal plot has been uncovered, one that could threaten the nation, no, our very faith. Members of the inquisitorial order have been caught redhanded taking bribes to smuggle out mages from our very borders. This grievous violation of the natural order must be addressed immediately, if only to calm the agitated spirits of the people. While these inquisitors are opening their minds to witchery, it should be noted that they are still doing their faithful duties in technicality by removing the magery of the nation.
