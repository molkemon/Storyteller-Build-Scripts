The meteoric rise of the monarch has shaken the Raja to their core. Alliances shift and new powers consolidate as Parusahan Saya seeks to claim the second most powerful office in all of Rahen. This is not something which can be allowed to come to pass, even if it means the very foundations of the Raj will be turned to rubble and smoke. The missive arrives mere days after the war is declared; the Raja has forsaken the sacred oath of the Senapti and is now rising in direct opposition to our rule. The Vizier has been bolstered by the snivelling cowardice of our overlord, and now stands as a much harder obstacle to overcome than what was previously envisioned. But this is no matter, for now we may strike the corruption of the Lotus Court at its very center, and scour Rahen of the perverse influence of the Raja once and for all.
