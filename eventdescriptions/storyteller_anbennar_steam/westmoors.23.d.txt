In the highest peaks of the Westmoors, straddling the northern border, stood an ancient, weathered set of stones the locals still stubbornly called a holdfast. Standing in front of it was a man of short stature but great shadow; Dominic Beron. "My people have been in these lands before Gerrac, first King of these lands, led the Alenics westward beyond the Alen. Ever since, we have fought against all who would seek to subjugate us, every king and emperor. Until now." He snapped his fingers, and a servant brought forth a dagger, black and bright, from which Lord Beron cut his hand and let the blood drip onto the shallow grass of the moor. "Clan Beron is yours, my ruler."
