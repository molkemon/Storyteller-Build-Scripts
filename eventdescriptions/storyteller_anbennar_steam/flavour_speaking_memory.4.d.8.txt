One of our people's favorite stories is that of Kaleo of Mokulali, a great hero who lived long before the Day of Ashen Skies. Known for having ruled the legendary island of Mokulali with great wisdom, he serves as an inspiration to our people; a model of what a good life looks like. Recently, though, some of our people have started to doubt if he actually existed. Is it really possible for a human to have protected the entire population of his island from the Day of Ashen Skies? How can we know the truth about events that happened so terribly long ago? We already sent an expedition to ask the Dragon Turtles, of course, but they refused to answer any questions about Kaleo, or even to explain why they abstained. It appears that we must solve this problem ourselves.
