Pulling the lever led to a visible surge of power, magical in nature, as blue energy crackled along the cabling and poured into the gateway. Its purple runes glowed brightly, as bolts of matching energy arced along the inside of the gateway. The explosion caught everyone off guard. Those caught in the blast were sucked into the whirling vortex of energy and vanished entirely, not a trace left behind. In the seconds that followed, those caught too close were sucked in, violently ripped apart and turned into red mist. Still, a few in the expedition were not so lucky, and their screams could be heard as they were pulled intact, or mostly so, into that purple maw, the sounds of their suffering not ceasing for hours.
