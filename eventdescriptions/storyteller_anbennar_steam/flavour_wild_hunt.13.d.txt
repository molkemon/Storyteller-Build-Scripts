On the empty plains, a clan’s shamans are its life; they call its rain, carve its glyphs, and maintain order and stability under its domain. Unfortunately, most of our shamans died in Yarikhoi at the hands of the Triunics, and now our land suffers from drought, poverty, and unrest. What few shamans remain are mostly those who were too young or too old to fight in the Incursion, or else those who had so little talent that it was not worth bringing them. Every one of them has been working to do the tasks of four of their dead brethren, but their efforts still are not enough. As we have every year since our war was lost, we now face a choice. tell our shamans to work even harder or tell our people we have failed them.
