Heretics are all around. In the streets, skulking in alleyways. In the cloisters, speaking of Surakel with cloying false reverence, holding the darkness in their heart. In the leadership, hiding and twisting the nation into a foul perversion of Surakel’s will. Not just in our realm, but across the entirety of Bulwar.  It is our duty to keep our senses sharp, and ready, looking everywhere and bringing the burning light of Surakel to those who would defy and poison his garden, and today our beliefs have once more been vindicated.  The Varamhari are plotting a most foul act, to claim false divinity, and turn their leader into some perverse demiurge.  It is our duty to turn the light onto them, and reveal their twisted whims to those who serve their Akalate.  Of course, we would take them under our protection.
