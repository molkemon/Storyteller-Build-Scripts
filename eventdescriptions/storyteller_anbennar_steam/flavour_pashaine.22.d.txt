Following a disastrous Diet at which the Pashainéy delegation excoriated the Empire and walked out entirely, the Empire is at a crossroads. Pashainé had been dealing with what it calls a magocratic revolution, a campaign of sedition which threatened to overthrow government with the kind of magical rule which only swiveleyed Black Doctrinaires truly espouse. The Pashainéy excoriated the Emperor for his inaction and complacency, arguing that the Empire was rotten to the core. The Emperor, on the other hand, now speaks of a dangerous and radical state careening out of control, subsumed by mob rule and a pogrom mentality towards mages. There is no middle ground to be had in the coming conflict; two fundamentally opposing images of Anbennar are about to clash.
