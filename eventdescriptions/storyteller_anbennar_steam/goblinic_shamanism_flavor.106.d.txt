Even a goblin child knows Guard Thunder, whether or not he is known as Blexim or Eragurd in this realm. He is the morning star who circles the sun to fight against the hordes of evil spirits who want to kidnap her, with his lightning arrows. However, some know him as much more. according to traveling fanatics, he once liberated the other gods known by the people from slavery by the evil deity followed by the brutish orks, Great Dookan, when Dookan was distracted fighting other gods. Now he is fighting both Dookan and the other gods alien to their kind, and he is calling on his followers to help him by fighting their henchmen on Halann.
