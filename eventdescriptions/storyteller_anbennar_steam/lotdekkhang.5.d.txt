Lot Dekkhang has poured considerable funds into propaganda stating that the Sun elves of Azkare, the Neprite dwarves of Verkal Ozovar, and the Wulin of Xianjie all threaten the Sikai people, and that the Teplin and Sikai people need to unite. The claims have not convinced any Sikai townships to seek protection from Lot Dekkhang, but they have aided the legitimization of the claims of Lot Dekkhang on all of the remaining Sikai townships.
