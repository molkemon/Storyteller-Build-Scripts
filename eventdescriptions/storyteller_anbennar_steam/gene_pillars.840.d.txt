We have stumbled upon some sort of trash chute. We are walking on a number of suspended platforms over a large circular opening in the ground. Down below we can see countless pieces of debris, but also things that look like they could potentially be valuable. There are a number of places we could likely climb down to the debris, but Stumble seems to be convinced that there is something alive living down in the debris, warning us to stay on the upper levels of the chamber. Do we take the risk?
