Taking the first steps into the cavern, we quickly find ourselves inside a massive natural chamber, infested with silk and cobwebs. And hiding behind these webs, creeping through the floors and walls, the flickering torch light reveals swarms of spiders. These arachnids, ranging from the smallest pinhead sized crawlies to cat sized beasts, quickly flee the light, but the knowledge of their presence causes many members of our expedition severe discomfort. Upon the chamber itself, a path forward is clear through a tunnel in the far cave wall. However, some slight clues like unusual cobweb patterns seem to hint that previous expeditions may have been here in the far past. Finding clues as to the remains of these past expeditions may be a great boon.
