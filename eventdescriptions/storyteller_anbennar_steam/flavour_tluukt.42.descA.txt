Our warriors amass in the plains before AzkaSur; innumerable gnolls howling and yipping wildly, their whooping cries no doubt heard in every corner of the ancient citadel. The warriors of the citadel, tempered by Zokka and by Jaddar, no doubt fear a long and bloody siege. But, looking upon the pitiable squalor and sheer destitution of the once glorious lands of AzkaSur, the ruler has other thoughts. The monarch of AzkaSur, whose lands thronging with refugees and bandits, may be willing to consider an offer of protection under our glorious pack, provided we prove ourselves different from rapacious Zokka and vow to return AzkaSur to its former glory as well, along with its lost territories.
