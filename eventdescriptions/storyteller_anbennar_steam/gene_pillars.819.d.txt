A faded and partially damaged carving can be seen on a part of the wall in the next room we enter. It appears to be a smaller and more simplified depiction of the pillar’s layout, hailing from the time before it was swallowed. The pillar must have been much taller previously, and it looks like we still have not managed to penetrate that far into the pillar. This map could be very useful if we could commit it to memory or find some way to bring it with us.
