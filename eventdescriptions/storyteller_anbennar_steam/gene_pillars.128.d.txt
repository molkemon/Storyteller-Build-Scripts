After trials and tribulations both magical and mundane, the party finally arrives at their ultimate goal; the Spout, a gigantic opening in the surface of the pillar where the Kiamoa pours into the sky like an inverted waterfall. The noise is almost deafening, the crash of thunder and the crackle of lightning drowning out all but the loudest of the party members. As they walk towards the center of the chamber, the party spies some sort of moving image of the Stormcannon, seemingly powered by some kind of Divination magic. Inside the frame depicting the lower chamber the party spies a familiar face; Conthus, detaching himself from his mechanim frame and loading his containment cell into the barrel. The Precursor looks towards the image, stunning the party. "I sense that you have arrived at the controls. Excellent. There should be a control panel nearby which will activate the cannon. I’m sure even you can’t miss it, the buttons are very large." The miniature Kiamoa inside the Precursor's cage swirls erratically, streaks of light shooting across the glass as the party locates the controls. "Somewhere on that panel is the button to load me into the Stormcannon," the old creature crackles with the remaining power within its metal body. "Activate it, and I will be free to join my people in grateful rest as pure energy. I cannot thank you enough for helping me in this endeavor, the undoubtedly most important quest of your lives." But another button catches the eyes of the party; red, imposing, and situated directly next to the controls the Precursor pointed out. With his mechanim body finally defunct, all Conthus can do is swirl in his dome as the party makes their choice.
