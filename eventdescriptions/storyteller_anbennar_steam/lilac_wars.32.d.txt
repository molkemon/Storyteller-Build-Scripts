The education of Marquis Laurens has been progressing well, and we dare to hope he will live up to the name Silmunas. However, a recent, if minor, incident has left many uneasy. In one of our sessions with him about the history of the Silmunas, the young man asked with typical childish innocence about if he would ever get the chance to meet Uncle Rogier. The tutor panicked and said that if the Marquis so wished, it would be best to protect the wish by keeping it secret and safe. Laurens is a young boy, and given our role in the Lilac Wars, his innocence often pangs Wesdamerians of status with guilt over what was done. None, however, have seen fit to quit and instead take more wages for themselves to cope.
