An alarming trend has developed in the pack as of late. The traditional hierarchies, foundational for the functioning of a pack as large as ours, are increasingly challenged, with inferiors ignoring or openly challenging the orders of their superiors. What started as isolated incidents quickly resolved and ascribed to the inflated ambitions of some underlings, has spread like wildfire through the pack, and is approaching a level where the continued running of our pack might be made impossible. We may be forced to take drastic measures to restore discipline if this trend continues.
