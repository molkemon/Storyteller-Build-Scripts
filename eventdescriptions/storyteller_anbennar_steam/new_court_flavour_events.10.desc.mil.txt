Not everyone is pleased with the plan to entrust an advisor with seeking better relations with another country. With dissent mounting in court, a military advisor, who has previously maintained a friendly rivalry with the event advisor for the monarch’s attention, has come forward with allegations of disloyalty. They claim to have investigated the event advisor’s activities and found out that they amount to little more than sifting off state secrets to the other country under the guise of seeking rapprochement. For their part, the event advisor protests that the evidence brought against them is a fabrication inspired by the military advisor’s jealousy at their success. They swear they are loyal and will deliver positive results if given our full trust.
