With the help of the Bâlvazha priests of the Groediazân, the Amlruzskâppa has managed to produce a new set of blood runes to help their inquisitors scry, hunt down, and purge any who are not dedicated to the great Runefather. In addition to this, the growing cooperation between these two factions has resulted in a further spread of blood runes across the administration, allowing for the Groediazân’s Dewara to manage doctrine and organize the state better. The Obsidian Council has already given approval of these new runes, and now the runesmiths have begun mass production.
