Today the monarch has attended a feast thrown by the nobility. While some of the monarch’s more cautious confidants were wary of such a regularly uncharacteristic event, the benevolent leader was quick to assuage their fears and attend the feast. Much to those same confidants’ surprise, the feast turned out a raucous merriment with much drinking done, stories told, and drunken ballads belted out. Those within the leader's circle rarely saw the monarch as happy as they were that day. The next morning, the leader left the feast with many new friends.
