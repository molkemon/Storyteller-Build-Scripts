We have won the Lilac Wars and no one can deny that the Siloriel are secure as the greatest of the Silver Families. It is our time of victory, yet the nature of our kingdom ensures that whenever we incorporate lands into our realm, there are always those picking over the scraps. Be they merchants seeking their tolls, clerics their abbeys, and nobles additional lands, all are quick to voice who should hold the land in trust for their regal lord, King Kylian. My liege will have to put up with quite a few meetings from such parties for the next few years as we digest our success in such proceedings.
