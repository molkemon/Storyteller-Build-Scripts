For several decades now, kobolds have lived in our lands. In that time, their inclination to worship dragons led them to gravitate toward faerie dragons. The faerie dragons have taken this devotion positively, often considering kobolds to be their most favored playmates. Many kobolds have even shed their old cultural identities, now calling themselves feyscaled in honor of their fae patrons. While the impact of this on our nation has been minimal, some see this relationship as a great example of an ideal relationship with the fey.
