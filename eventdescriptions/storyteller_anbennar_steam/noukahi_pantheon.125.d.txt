As the monarch travels deep into the woods, they come across a dilapidated shrine, moss and mushrooms in the process of covering the thing, its wooden structure weathered and grey, and a statue within it, its colors long into the process of fading as its paint peels. Resting in the chest of the statue is a gem that glows and flickers under the scattered light that comes through the leaves. The dark purple thing draws the monarch’s eye, their hands twitch with an overwhelming desire to take it.
