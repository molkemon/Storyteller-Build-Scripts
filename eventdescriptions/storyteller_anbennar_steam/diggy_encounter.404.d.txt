Having quickly disposed of the wretched creature, the scouts secure the perimeter and begin their work. Not deigning to use anything inside the room to further emphasize their point, their own supplies suffice in creating the grotesque masterpiece. What's losing a spear or two if the reward is fewer of those scaly monsters coming out from their dens? The pale finally upright, they stop and take a moment to appraise their work. A truly hideous creature pierced all the way from top to bottom, its maw fixed in an expression of immortalized agony. The sheer helplessness on display. The verdict is out, and it is perfection. Giving each other knowing nods, they happily trot back to the rest of the expedition, eager to boast about another blow to their would-be rivals, once again reduced to pests.
