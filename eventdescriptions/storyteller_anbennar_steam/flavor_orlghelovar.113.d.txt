After a lot of iterations, our artificers and engineers have produced the final version of the lighting device. Stripped down to its barest essentials, the conical design is no larger than a lantern, and produces vastly more light. The top can be screwed out to access the core, precut in a rectangular shape to be easy to replace. They are now being installed throughout our holdings in the Serpentreach, illuminating the long trade routes under the mountains.
