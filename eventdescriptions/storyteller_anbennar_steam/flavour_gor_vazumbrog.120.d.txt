Grimmun idly speculated about what religious significance the feathered, venomous creature partially blotting out the desert sun might carry, though that was irrelevant to the purpose of the day's expedition. No, this was a trial run for the defeat of another venomous, winged creature. Mages harried the creature with bursts of distracting light and dulled its counterattacks with antitoxic wards, sufficient to stop this creature's meager nibbles, but of dubious utility against the purple queen herself. Artificer-wrought weighted lines entangled the creature's wings, bearing it down to the ground, where it was pummeled into a stupor by dozens of titanium hands and ultimately shot to death by the well-disciplined Vazumbrog riflemen.
