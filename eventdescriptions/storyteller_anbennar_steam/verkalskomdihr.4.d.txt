Our change in policies regarding the usage of trees is paying off, and the forest is rapidly recovering. New trees are sprouting everywhere, carefully selected and placed to maximize the lumber gains. The old Forest Ranger Corps is now tasked with controlling the forest and ensuring the safety of our commercial timberland. We created a new office for the forest to organize the planting of new trees, specify the amount of trees which can be felled, and allocate the lumber for trading and manufacturing. This development has led to a net profit in available lumber.
