The ruinborn we found in our immediate surroundings were poor, very poor. The only noteworthy exception being the Cheshoshi with their Bone Citadel.  The further west we go, the less this seems to remain true. Our explorers spread stories of great riches in the northern Eordand region and especially in the southern Kheionai region. If we are to enjoy their wealth, we need to kickstart the process of acquiring outposts near them.
