As the funeral procession in the capital marches on, the people arrayed on all sides mourn their king. Within the casket is JeongHun Gyo; the late and powerful Sage King of Danggun. Risen to power in a period of weakness, he was deemed worthy by Sado, reconsolidated and expanded the country’s borders. A truly virtuous man that was respected and loved by all who knew him. His son, Haeng Gyo, next in line for the Crane Throne, however, is a pale shadow of his exalted father. Weak, foolish, and decidedly unprepared for the duties of the throne, his ascension casts dark clouds over the realm’s future. Within the court and beyond, schemes are being hatched, and various groups gather their forces in an attempt to leave their mark on the lands of Gowon.
