The City of Warriors lays beaten before us, our valiant knights having trampled the dreams of those upstart bandits who proclaimed themselves to be the heirs to the Battlekingdom of old. As the state of Malacnar was hardly legitimate in the eyes of their fellow Ynnics, having in truth been created from the deranged whims of a traitorous noble, there is near-unanimous support among our nobles and allies for Malacnar's dissolution. the question is what approach we will take.
