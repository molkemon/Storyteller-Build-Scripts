There once was a lonely shepherd, living in the grasslands of the Sorrow, who tended his sheep and lived a peaceful life. Then one day, the ground around him shook and soon trapped him and his livestock in a cage of solid rock. The shepherd prayed to Khets for forgiveness, as he was a devout man. He tried to seek an escape route for himself and his sheep, but he was forced to make a choice. leave the sheep or die with them. He made a dire choice and he stayed alive. On his way out, already mourning his companions, he unexpectedly discovered a deposit of rare metals. Content with his consolation, he thanked the Khets and reported his findings to the authorities.
