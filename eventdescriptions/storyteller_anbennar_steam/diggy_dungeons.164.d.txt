Finally we have arrived at the centre of this affair. Hidden behind devious arcane traps and dangerous bookshelves lies a circular room with a high, domed ceiling. At its focal point stands a stone plinth with strange runes across its perimeter. From the runes rises a translucent figure emitting faint light in erratic bursts. A mage says, "A living spell." Indeed, this new enemy is a living spell, magic made substantial. All of its power is bent on a single purpose. to expel foreigners and to safeguard the library. It has bested our attempts so far, but its end is nigh.
