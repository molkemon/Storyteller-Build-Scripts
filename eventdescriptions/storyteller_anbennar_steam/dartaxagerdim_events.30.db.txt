A righteous empire of the faithful such as ours can only continue to fulfill its sacred duties with a mighty military fit to vanquish any foe and fend off any hostile incursion of the servants of the Dark. For this, we need to invest in the formation of an elite core of our armies, the Dartaxian Legions. Their role will be to anchor our auxiliary troops in battle and smash through the lines of the enemy. And who would be better suited for this than our valiant Šebhuliam, who have proven their utter devotion to our cause time and time again.
