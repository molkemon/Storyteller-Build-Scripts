Before the ruin, the Precursor SlaverNobles of Taychend held masses of time-indentured slaves, who worked in deplorable conditions in the hope of receiving a massive reward at the end of their five thousand year tenure. With the nobles not wanting to sully the pristine Taychendi landscape with shack housing for their slaves, they forced them to live underground in vast, winding slave warrens. Their scavengers have recently uncovered an entrance to a hitherto unknown warren; while dangerous to explore, these warrens could potentially hold valuable relics from the precursor period.
