It is wonderful to see you again. I hope the work of rulership is not taking too much away from you; I know how power can wear over a long time. But although I would dearly wish to spend the hours talking, this is not why I asked you here. The other Judges and I have been discussing a certain matter. The work of the Consensus is entering a slow period, but we have such talented counselors here. I cannot abide the thought of their expertise going to waste, even if they are no longer working for the three of us. Would there be space in your country for some of them? We have recruited them from all across Kanolowele, and they are acquainted with everything their years here have taught them. If you would be open to it, I will send along the necessary introductions.
