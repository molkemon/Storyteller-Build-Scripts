With the deaths of both Narathya and Tesheladar, the chaotic struggle for the throne that has gripped Larankar is now definitively over. Both surely thought that the throne was theirs by right, but it seems Erankar truly did banish any notion of predetermination from his realm. As the embers of the Sibling’s War die down, the monarch must sift through the ashes and piece back together their shattered empire.
