While our laws give some basic protections to the poor people of our own species, these laws tend not to apply fully to the half-orc minorities in our lands, who have often been looked down upon due to their history in the Greentide. This has led to more and more business owners using half-orcs as cheap and replaceable workers for undesired or downright dangerous jobs. This development is boosting the local economy of that province, so it could be beneficial to let them continue. On the other hand, we could help these poor souls, but our neighbors would see it as a weakness to give concessions to social outcasts.
