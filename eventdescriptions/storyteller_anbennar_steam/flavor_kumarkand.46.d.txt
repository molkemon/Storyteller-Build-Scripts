We have a sort of advantage over Jaddar. We already have proof that the will of Surakel includes all sapient creatures under His protection, as we have the legacy of Dimuzi and his followers. Dimuzi is an example that Surakel has a place for everyone in His garden; this realization has convinced many to step forward in service to our state. So long as they are faithful to His will, it does not matter if one is even a gnoll or a harpy; they have the right to rule our people.
