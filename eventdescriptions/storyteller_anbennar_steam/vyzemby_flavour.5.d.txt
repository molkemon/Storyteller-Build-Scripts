Halflings are renowned for their endurance, being among the first to till the soil after the Day of Aschen Skies. Foot races have been a popular pastime among our people for centuries, with athletes such as Volana Cliffleaper rising to national or even international prominence. The division of Fahvanosy under squabbling clans has limited the length of these races to a relatively small distance. With the island unified under our rule, the idea of a foot race spanning the entire island has become a realistic possibility. To lend prestige to this new endeavor, racers are asking the lord for an official endorsement of a yearly race.
