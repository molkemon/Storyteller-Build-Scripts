A wind blows through the town, and all goes silent. Children cease their playing, and adults halt from whatever business they were in the middle of. Even the horses and beasts are not immune to the chill, and either flee or halt in their tracks. Some dared to turn to its source, while others chose to look away, away from that evil thing that houses great and heavy clouds formed above it, encasing its foundation in a preternatural darkness. While none know the true extent of what is happening, one thing is for sure. something terrible has come to their town.
