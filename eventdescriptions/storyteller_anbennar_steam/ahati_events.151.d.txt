Many of our officers and generals received a military education from the Ahati, as they were the best of the best. We always thought that even though they were Ahati trained, that they would still be loyal. How wrong we were. When the first batch of officers switched sides, we were relieved that some still remained, hoping they would truly be loyal. How wrong we were. We now discovered that one such general has been leaking battle plans, fort maps, and equipment information to the Ahati via other treacherous officers. The Ahati now know even more about our military and our inner workings, and the damage will be significant. We should perhaps just execute the general and his accomplices to prevent any more leaks. Or we could try to bribe him, hoping that cash will make him loyal again, but we may be wrong again.
