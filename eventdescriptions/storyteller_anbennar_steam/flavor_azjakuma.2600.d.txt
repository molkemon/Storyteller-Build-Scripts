Kongren, a blade made out of specially prepared korashi, is normally kept in a unique shrine, guarded by a Righteous Path religious order. During the initial invasion, orders were directed specifically to capture this shrine, slaughter the monks, and seize the sword before they could move it elsewhere. This did not happen. The monks are at an unknown location, having taken the blade with them. Now, we will need to find it and then finally seize it for ourselves.
