A heap of letters and scrolls of all shapes and sizes lie in a heap. Aside from the pile lie two documents both written in clearly hasty hands. Each message in the heap is the same, complaint after complaint about our expansion along the Ofehibi coast. Raheni shipping has dropped precipitously and the Odanchesta are going hungry without their prey. The documents set aside are far more pressing than the rest of the rubbish. Reports from the garrisons of Biribitid and Dakmender telling of Odanchesta crews openly rioting in the streets and looting the cities.
