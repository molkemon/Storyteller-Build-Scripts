Making their way further into the Gene Pillar, the party encountered one of the main production areas for the Kiamoa. Swirling clouds of blue energy, crackling with energy as they fly over the heads of the party, are drawn into countless vents and shafts that run throughout the walls like an endless maze. Unfortunately, much of this part of the facility was not designed for mortal access, and as such the only way forward is through the tempest of deadly lightning arcs. While the party is carefully navigating between bursts of magical clouds, one vent that initially went unnoticed stirs to life and prepares to emit a fearsome gout of pure energy directly into the unlucky souls who dared to tread within the pillar.
