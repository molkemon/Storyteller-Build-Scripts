The ynnic ruinborn, \"lordlings\" in Freemarcher slang, have begun to arrive in Free Heart in search of joining a rudmen crew. These lordlings know the river just as well as any Freemarcher, and even if they're weaker than a halfork, their knowledge of the language and customs of the Ynn make them a useful addition to the crew of any rudman captain. However the Freemarcher lords say that allowing the lordlings in goes against Varbuk's Dream, that the Freemarches cannot stand on their own if they rely on imported talent.
