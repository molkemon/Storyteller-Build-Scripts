It would seem that, even after all we have done for the naga, some among their number have chosen treason over service to the empire. Even after our defeat of the Riverclaws, there remain pretenders and claimants to the Riverclaw lands who, until now, have remained content to skulk deep within our jungles and lie to themselves that there will be a day the Riverclaws are free again. Now, however, this quiet opposition has been replaced by open rebellion, led by the Naga of our empire who have defected from our palace to consort with the Riverclaws. Given that their rebellion does not have a realistic chance at victory, we can only reason that their motivation was to die with vengeance on their minds. This rebellion poses peril, of course, but it also represents a great opportunity. All the traitors in the empire have gathered in one place, and when we emerge victorious over them our control will be absolute.
