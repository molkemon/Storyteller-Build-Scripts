In theory, the halflings of West Tipney should not be our enemies.  They are gluttons, yes; and they have not learned the wickedness of the witch yet, yes; however, in their tale, we can find many similarities to our own.  They, too, are fleeing their homes, pushed out by the Lorentish; surely, accepting the truth as to the mageocratic designs of their oppressors cannot be too far out of their purview.  However, just because they have faced oppression does not mean they will simply accept our truth. They accept the witch amongst their numbers, and aren’t likely to turn on their own kin.
