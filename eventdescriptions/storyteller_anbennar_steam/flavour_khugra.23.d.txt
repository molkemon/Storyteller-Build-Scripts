Yusshela, the Celestial Dolphin, is a blessed and powerful Araizary, who has historically granted great boons and blessings to those deserving. Today, the monarch is going to become one of these fortunate individuals, as a reward for the continued reverence and worship of the Araizary in Khugra. The monarch enters the sea alone, as the constellation of the Celestial Dolphin shines brightly in the night sky in the moonless night. The monarch cannot avoid a shiver as the envoy of Yusshela approaches, with the form of a great white shark made of softly shining starlight. Despite its fearsome aspect, the beast's demeanor is calm as it gets close to the ruler. It stops right in front of the monarch, slightly bowing its head down, as if allowing itself to be petted. The ruler does just that, and as soon as the monarch's hand touches the animal, the shine becomes blinding for a second. When the monarch's eyes open, the beast is gone, only the light of the evershining stars breaking the darkness. The monarch swims back to the shore, where royal advisors await eager to listen to the monarch's retelling of the experience. The ruler feels the power of the boon flowing through the monarch's body, and the changes in the monarch's personality are evident. The monarch has received the Blessing of the Shark, a boon of great ferocity.
