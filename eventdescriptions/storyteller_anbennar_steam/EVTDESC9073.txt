Before the wholly assembled Imperial Parliament stood the monarch and their entire family. Behind the ruler's closest relatives were their more distant relatives, some more distant than others, but each bore the name of the dynasty. Some cast wary glances across the crowd of princes, others exchanged furtive glances and unspoken agreements, and the children glanced at the lances of the guards, all polished and gleaming, with fluttering pennons representing the many states of the Empire. "By the legacy of Castanor, I shall endure any test," the ruler intoned. A cacophony of voices echoed the oath. "By the legacy of the Phoenix Empire, I shall vanquish any foe. By the legacy of Dameria, I shall serve the people of the Empire. This I swear, and to this I pledge my life and my blood, and that of all my descendants..." The ruler's face was as stone, unreadable to the crowd, turned away from those closest to the monarch as they recited the words of the oath. It was framed as a sacred duty, a grace conferred, bound by laws and traditions that predated the Grand Summit of Aranthíl, but the reality of those words was very simple. now, and forever, would the House of the dynasty rule the Empire of Anbennar.
