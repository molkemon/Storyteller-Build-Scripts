Many members of our court thought the inclusion of the advisor among them was just an amusing experiment by the ruler at first, but their adaptation of Cannorian ideas to our reality has brought great benefits to our nation. Needless to say, our noble houses are not keen on questioning what they consider to be prestigious traditions dating back generations and have called the advisor a foreign barbarian hellbent on destroying our culture. The advisor’s proposals are actually quite small in scope, and this is clearly a ploy of the nobility to see them replaced by one of their own. Nevertheless, the ruler will have to settle the situation. Do we favor skill or status?
