Our reentry into international trade and our capital's position close to the surface has brought many foreign traders from a distant land and another land to our hold. Our markets and merchant districts now bustle not only with dwarves, but with blondehaired sun elves, sternfaced people from the other land, and friendly people from the distant land. The volume and frequency of trade has become so great that a group of human traders have successfully petitioned our ruler to settle in our hold permanently, and already a cluster of houses close to our warehouse district have been remodeled to comfortably accommodate their new, taller residents.
