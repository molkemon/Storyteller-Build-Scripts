Greetings, my most high ruler, may the winds beneath your wings ever carry you onwards. I wish to provide you with a report regarding the current state and progress reached on the Nilelista project. The first attempt to craft a rianelion lyre has been promising, and the Sanarenni flocks who used the lyre during the performance of their song magic have reported an ease to their efforts when compared to times when they wove their songs unassisted. And their audiences appear to have been affected as well, with many mentioning a strengthened sense of community and drive to protect the flock. Yet the prototype is considered somewhat basic, therefore we consider it wise to go into a more opulent direction for our next attempt. I remain hopeful that we will soon have further exciting news to report, my august ruler. I remain your most devoted servant, Sanarestu Jorouna.
