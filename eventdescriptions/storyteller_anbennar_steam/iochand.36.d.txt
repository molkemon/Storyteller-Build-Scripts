DECLARATION OF DACCASDEDER INDEPENDENCE Despite repeated calls for reform, the Kingdom of Iochand has taken insufficient measures to end the imposition of its tyrannical practices upon the peoples of Aelantier. We no longer have the time, or goodwill, to entertain further empty platitudes. Therefore, we, the united peoples of Aelantier, do formally proclaim the full and undisputed independence of the Kingdom of Daccasded as a sovereign state. The current ruler shall be relieved of their position as monarch of Daccasded, and Chairman Tando Bigbrain shall be subsequently crowned Kong in accordance with the will of the people. In addition, the remaining members of the Daccasded Administrative Council who lack membership in the Nimszexael Society, including former Head Councillor Eldergreen, shall be required to either formally renounce their loyalty to Iochand or be forcibly removed from their position. Statute Thirty-Five shall also be formally revoked. Any failure of Iochand to fully and immediately withdraw from all holdings in Aelantier shall be treated as a direct and unequivocal declaration of war. May Aelantier forever prosper under the leadership of those who are willing to take it seriously. AUTHORIZED BY The Free Council of Daccasded Kong Tando I "the Blue" Bigbrain
