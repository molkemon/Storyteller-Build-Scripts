Near the peaks of some of Halann’s highest mountains, one can find the Roosts. The traditional capitals of a region’s harpy flocks, they are a large collection of nests, with smallscale artisan shops, minor trading stations, gardens, public parks, and baths. Functionally cities atop the world, they are protected from invasion by the sheer verticality of the cliffs and the harpies' natural ability for flight. Azaly’s interest in the roosts began after considering the amount of wasted space on the rooftops of Bivuzena. If harpy society could create entire civilizations atop mountains, surely his architectural genius could manage a neighborhood atop the city skyline. While the specific materials and methods of construction were not viable for his purposes, he still sought out the Riaveltu of a harpy capital and travelled to the roost. Listening to the wisdom of their ancestors, he began to understand their philosophy of freedom and the wind. Mercurial as always, several harpies returned with him, curious to see the wonders of Bivuzena and if Azaly would manage to capture the feeling of the open air within a city of walls. The ruler has offered them residence within the mountainous areas of their domain if they so desire.
