At sea, the Migrant Fleet was nighanarchic, every ship for itself. On the island of Venáil, they organized into a vaguely republican council, drawing upon the Moon Elves' best in pursuit of their singular vision. Now that they have returned to Aelantier, they must reimagine themselves once more. Aelthanas, the glassworker turned philosopher advocate, is calling on the current monarch to step down, and open the leadership up to a complete democracy. Evercharismatic, and everscheming, Dahvar, whispers of theocratic rule, replacing their previous unity of vision with a unity of faith. There are even some fools—blinded by ideas of 'heroism'—who speak of elevating Lithiel, anointing her 'Heroine of the Reconquest' and placing a crown on her head. There shall be a crown, forged of Aelantieri iron, set with Aelantieri rubies and sapphires and diamonds. But it shall be placed on the head of the current monarch, and that ruler alone.
