At sea, the Migrant Fleet was nighanarchic, every ship for itself. On the island of Venail, we organized into a vaguelyrepublican council, drawing upon the Moon Elves' best in pursuit of our singular vision. Now that we have returned to Aelantier, we must reimagine ourselves once more. Aelthanas, the glassworker turned philosopheradvocate, is calling on our lord to step down, and open the leadership up to a complete democracy. Evercharismatic, and everscheming, Dahvar, whispers of theocratic rule, replacing our previous unity of vision with a unity of faith. There are even some fools; blinded by ideas of 'heroism'; who speak of elevating Lithiel, anointing her 'Her heroine of the Reconquest' and placing a crown on her head. There shall be a crown, forged of Aelantieri iron, set with Aelantieri rubies and sapphires and diamonds. But it shall be placed on the head of our lord, and they alone.
