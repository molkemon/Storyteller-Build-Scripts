The ruler, the decision by the oppressive and cruel kings of Binwarji to both attempt to conscript us and bolster our camps with additional supplies and weapons was a silly one. We shall not forget their atrocities inflicted upon us. With our newly gained power and independence, we shall carve out a homeland, and no longer be at the whim of idle charity or suffer the active malice of our neighbors. They can call us strange outsiders for our traditions, they can call us dangerous for our magic of memory and mind, but they will never again call us servants.
