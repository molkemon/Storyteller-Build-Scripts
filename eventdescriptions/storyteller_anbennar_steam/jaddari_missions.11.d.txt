It has been many seasons since our expedition marched into the depths of the Dwarovar. Now finally they have returned. The sound of heavy bootfalls echoing through the halls initially brought panic to our miners, however soon it became clear that these were the enlightened steps of the Jadd expedition. Their adventure has taken them into the deepest, darkest pits of the Dwarovar and now they are back at the surface with amazing treasures and glorious tales. After a wash and a well deserved rest, these heroes of the Jadd have been received by our lord to tell them about their adventures in the deep dark. Bards are already telling their tales and the priests have pronounced their great service a true honour to Surael.
