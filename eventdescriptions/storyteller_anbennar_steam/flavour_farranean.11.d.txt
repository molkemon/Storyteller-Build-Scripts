We seek not domination, but only that we determine a desire to live our lives and be free of those who would be tyrants dictating their will to our people. Of course, no empire or dominion ever comes about by accident, but it must still be said that we have never wished to be a world-spanning empire as those who build colonies across those lands wish. Those great conquerors out of those regions may bring the bloody wealth of conquest, but in our domain we see that we have a bounty in this untapped vale, so cultivated by civilizations and generations past. It is a land with dreams that we have inherited and which we shall pass down to those to come after. This continuation of such a little dream is still worth doing, though, as we have to maintain our optimism and work to advance it actively. Any energy spent advancing this agenda could never be wasted effort. Acting to preserve the small things is just as much a priority of this government as all the larger concerns. We do not forget our people even amidst other scrambles.
