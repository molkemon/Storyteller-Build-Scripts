Our lord stands inside the Black Tower of Vertesk. This particular section smells of disuse and abandon, with dust so firmly upon crumbling stone that the only thing in the air was the musk of age. Well, apart from the guards behind them. "If there is something you wanted from here, it's probably been picked out by now." The first guard spoke with a thick accent, a man who sounded as sturdy as his stature implied. Our lord waved them off with a simple shake of the hand. Even so, the pair watched on as our lord claimed a chunk of stone from the crumbling interior. The whole scene was supported by the gentle clinking of the second guard squeezing a bag. A thought crossed our lord’s mind; were these guards not corrupt? It was one thing to offer them money, but another thing entirely to actually accept it. Perhaps they ought to be turned in on the way out.
