Our longlasting friendship and camaraderie with Keyolíon, with mutual respect throughout these years despite the unbalanced power relationship, brings upon us a unique opportunity. We can prove to all the Nékheis that it is possible to build bridges across our differences. Unity for the once colonies, now great states. Keeping the status quo is obviously an option. But if we want to demonstrate our power over the region, we can use this summit to prove that a two Nékheis one country system can work.
