Yesterday, three thousand people died. Aging elves were dragged out of their homes and butchered before their families, and anyone tangentially related to them were rounded up and executed. Usually, that included their families too. In some places, attempts were made to resist. That resistance did not last long, even though so many fought bitterly for their lives, desperately holding on even past the point all seemed lost. Even Eldandar Silverspite made his appearance. In Ahartadyam yesterday morning, the city’s High Kavalal was having tea, unaware that the great conspiracy he had found himself at the heart of had unravelled. The Kamrayakval suddenly crashed through the wall of his manse, grabbed him by the head, and leapt off into the night. They found his shattered corpse splayed on the ground a few miles away. Three hours later, he appeared again in Enlarmai to slaughter a high priest, his guards, his family, and any who dared defend him. Five hours after that, in Kattisangamar. Early this morning, in Marhed Rayodana. The High Kavalali have all gone to ground. Even those not involved in the conspiracy have retreated to the shadows. Yesterday, three thousand people died, and in the end it will not matter, and they will try again, and they will not make the same mistakes. And yet, still, success. Because Elrandar Silverspite has bought himself time.
