Alefisol is less a country and more a threat to all we hold dear. Led by a mad half-genie who took control of the infernal forces he was supposed to be imprisoned with, he has spread an evil across the land hitherto inconceivable. He burns the land with infernal fire, spreading a wicked light almost as a challenge to our light of freedom. But men once free shall never be slaves; the Baashidi yearn for freedom, and they do not fight alone.
