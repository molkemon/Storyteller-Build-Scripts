The Green ork of Korgus have wandered outside of Escann, thousands upon thousands of them can be found here, in the Deepwoods. A region about which they say it is easy to enter but almost impossible to escape. They struggle in an eternal war against Forest Goblins and a mysterious race of tall and slender effeminate men. How orks can struggle against such weak races is a mystery to us, perhaps they will do better under our guidance.
