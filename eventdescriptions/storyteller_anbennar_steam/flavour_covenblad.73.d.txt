The Sword Covenant was once a fivefold promise, led by dissidents from the Orda Aldresia and Farrani knights left without their kingdom. This promise is well and truly shattered, for could it have ever meant anything? Power resides in the will of the righteous, in those who go forth and shape the world with their own strength. Did Galinda really care for the people, or for her own legacy? History is written in deeds and not the ink of promises kept or broken. Covenblad is a strong nation, and it was the strength of our forebears, not their ideals or promises, that the modern mageknight draws inspiration from.
