Kalyin’s dominance over reality extends not only throughout the Prime Material, but also to every other plane, and that includes the spirit plane, which connects through much of Haless. The question therefore remains on the nature of the spirits. are they also Kalyin? If they are, we must work with them in Haless, even if it is inconvenient and causes trouble. If they are not, then we should seek to fight their influence on Haless and restore Kalyin’s true order.
