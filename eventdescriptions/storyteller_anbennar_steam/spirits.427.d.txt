After a year of recovering from the injury at the High Temple, the doctors perform one last check for the monarch. Making sure that the ruler's body is in perfect shape, along with an acupuncture session, hitting all chi points and making sure no bad chi is left in the ruler's body after the incident from the Temple. Though as the final needle is put inside the ruler, the ruler suddenly jolts on the table, almost as if the ruler was hit by lightning. The ruler's body shook. The ruler's fists clench, and the doctors quickly remove the needles before the monarch slowly gets up unhindered by any injury. In fact, the ruler never felt better before. The doctors are rather stunned how fast the monarch moved as the ruler tended to lag behind in movement. They ask the ruler to lay down to check once more, only to find out that nothing was wrong. While the doctors spoke and checked with each other, the monarch was already out on their throne, leading the nation anew into a better direction.
