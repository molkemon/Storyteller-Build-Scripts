After a year of recovering the injury at the High Temple the doctors perform one last check for the lord. Making sure that their body is in perfect shape, along with an Acupuncture session. Hitting all chi points and making sure no bad chi is left in their body after the incident from the Temple, though as the final needle is put inside they suddenly jolts on the table, almost as if they were hit by lightning their body shook. Their fists clench and the doctors quickly remove the needles before the lord slowly gets up unhindered by any injury, in fact they never felt better before. The doctors are rather stunned how fast the lord moved as they tended to lag behind in movement. They ask them to lay down to check once more only to find out that nothing was wrong? While the doctors spoke and checked with each other the lord was already out on their throne, leading the nation anew into a better direction.
