In the time of Jaher, Sarisung was the greatest city in Haless. It was larger than Tianlou, richer than Sramaya, more diverse than Dhenijansar. The elves rightly saw Sarisung as the lynchpin of the continent, and control over the city as vital to any attempts to dominate the vastness of Haless. As their empire disintegrated, however, Sarisung suffered many hardships; thrice sacked and its population vastly diminished, the city fell out of the orbit of any major power and was left to fend for itself. Lacking central leadership, the boroughs of Sarisung fell under the sway of whatever criminal organization could offer the people protection, and so within the city had become a gangcontrolled slum.  But today the Claw and Lotus Banner flies over the walls of the city, and the corrupt and unjust rule of the Crime Lords is at an end. Law and order are restored to Sarisung, heart of Haless, and we begin the long road of restoring the city to its former glory. The first step on that road will be to deal with the surviving gang leaders who are in our custody. These ruffians deserve the noose for their many sins; but some in the ministries note that a quick end to these upjumped bandits may do less good for the Raj than to use their talents for our own ends.
