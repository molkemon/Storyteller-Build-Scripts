It appears that making the depths of our displeasure with our ally Zokka a matter of public fact has at last borne fruit. A chain of Krah a mile long marches into the capital, a traditional gesture of submission among gnolls of our wealth and power. The value of the gift is secondary only to its meaning, a meaning that shall not be misunderstood by the gnolls of the Salahad; Zokka needs our pack's support more than we need Zokka's.
