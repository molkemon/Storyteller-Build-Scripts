Nobody is really sure why the lord decided to purchase that marrow in the first place, but all the Stewards will readily admit that converting it into a portable government building was a stroke of genius. With the innards scooped out and turned into a giant bowl of soup, windows and doors cut in the side, and a drop of magic to stop it smelling, a grand manor for our government to serve in was constructed. We’ll probably need to replace it soon, but for now, this marrow shall serve as a symbol of Newshire’s mastery over transmutation magic.
