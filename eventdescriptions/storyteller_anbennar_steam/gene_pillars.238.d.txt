A lot of suffering was endured to bring the expedition to this point. Your scientists can come down here, I won’t try to kill them with gas. I would still advise against tampering with my kauga however. The only people who understand how it works are me and my chainers, and my chainers are all dead. One wrong move and you’ll unleash a magical explosion so powerful it could kill a holohana, I think at least, my chainers never did figure out how to deal with those monsters of their making." One final threat, that of mutual annihilation, the final proof that they were victorious, that there was nothing else left to threaten them with.
