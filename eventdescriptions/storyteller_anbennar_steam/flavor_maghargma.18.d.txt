The Kingdom of Maghargma stands on the precipice of eternal greatness. With a firm beachhead established in Yarikhoi, there is no question that they shall eventually hold dominion over all the lands of their creators. The Fire Giants once labored to fashion Yarikhoi into a paradise; as their true heirs, they shall begin preparations to do the same and fashion Yarikhoi into a paradise for all ogrekind. Standing between them and their ambitions are hundreds of thousands of humans. As much as they may wish to sweep them away and build their territorial crown jewel from the ground up, to do so would be infeasible at best. They conquered this land when the humans were divided and at one another’s throats; should they endeavor to exterminate them, the whole population of the ogre valley under arms would not be enough to resist their unstoppable tide. For better or worse, their empire has no path forward that does not include the Triunics.
