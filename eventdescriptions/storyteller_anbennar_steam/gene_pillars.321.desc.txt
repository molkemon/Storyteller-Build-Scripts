Imagine Halann tipped on her side, or rather shrunk to a relatively small area, then tiered. The animals which occupy this place, altered and mundane alike, have thrived or perished under very different conditions from the outside. Forced together into comparatively narrow confines, predators seem to have hunted their prey to extinction and then perished, except where the Precursors already catered to their needs with engineered prey; some animals from outside have subsequently entered and multiplied unaccosted. Flight is privileged; anything which ignores the collapsing floors of the great tower gains great advantage. Wandering these overgrown halls, one hears distant birdsong far above, or else the distant diving screech of something much, much larger. Perhaps we expected something like the bare wilderness of a mountain peak; not so. If anything the jungle is thickening here, the plants unfamiliar, the animals even more so.
