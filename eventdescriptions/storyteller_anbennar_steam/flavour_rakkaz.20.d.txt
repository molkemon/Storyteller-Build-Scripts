It has been ten years since the ruler has made a pact with the tenebrous mistresses of the swamp. And they have certainly held up their end of the bargain, enhancing both his magical abilities as well as his other talents. Now it is time for the monarch to pay the price. On the day of the anniversary, the ruler could not be roused from sleep, no matter what attempts both mundane and arcane were made to wake him. He appears to have been trapped in a deathlike state of trance, and whether he will ever awaken again lies in the hands of the Night Hags.
