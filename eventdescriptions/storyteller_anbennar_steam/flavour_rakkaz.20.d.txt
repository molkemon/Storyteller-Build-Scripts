It has been ten years since our lord made a pact with the tenebrous mistresses of the swamp. And they have certainly held up their end of the bargain, enhancing both his magical abilities as well as his other talents. Now it is time for him to pay the price. On the anniversary, the lord could not be roused from sleep, no matter what attempts both mundane and arcane were made to wake him. He appears to have been trapped in a deathlike state of trance, and whether he will ever awaken again lies in the hands of the Night Hags.
