It has been ten years since the ruler has made a pact with the tenebrous mistresses of the swamp. And they have certainly held up their end of the bargain, enhancing both his magical abilities as well as his other talents. Now it is time for the monarch to pay the price. What the ruler's life has gained in value must be repaid with life time remaining. On the tenth anniversary, the monarch collapsed onto the floor of the throne room and perished.
