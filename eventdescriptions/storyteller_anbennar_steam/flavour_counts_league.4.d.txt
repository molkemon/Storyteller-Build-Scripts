Our flag has flown for a while now, the decision to adopt a different symbol while the symbolic task of restoring Castellyr remained unfulfilled, seeming more and more hollow as we get closer to the task. The old white dragon flag is seeing more use as people do not feel they might be cursed by bad luck or that our survival is premature. The task remains ahead, but to see the old flags flutter in the wind is a source of pride for those who remember the old kingdom. It may only be a physical item, but to see them mended and put up is something that is a reminder of what had been lost in this interregnum and what can be restored if we are fully successful. Our banner will expand, and soon the internal flags of our vassals shall be replaced as they are integrated into our whole.
