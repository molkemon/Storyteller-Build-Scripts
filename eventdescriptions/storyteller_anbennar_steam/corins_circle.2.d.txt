Since joining our forces, Laurens síl Place has served well, and we owe some portion of our recent success to his clever ideas and brilliant tactics. More recently, he seemed to yearn for the feeling of his days adventuring, and requested a small squad of his own to lead. The ruler was happy to provide it, even giving síl Place the opportunity to select the soldiers himself. Since then, Laurens and his squad have been effective. Extremely effective. They have held off multiple orkish raids, seemingly knowing where they would strike almost before they even came into sight. They also have an unerring sense of which among the horde is warleader, who to slay to most quickly rout their foes. Once would be fortune, twice might be Corin's blessing. But three times, four? The ruler invited one of the squad's more novice adventurers to a dinner, and the youth was eager, almost delighted, to reveal Laurens' secret. It was minddevouring sorcery. They would set out into the hinterlands to capture an ork out hunting. Síl Place would use some blend of divination and enchantment magics, ripping apart the ork's mind to learn all about its warband and warleader and the plans for the next raid. They could then preempt the attack, arranging an ambush and targeting the warleader with precision. Take a few prisoners in battle, shred their minds to repeat the process, rinse, repeat. The soldier was awestruck, seemingly proud, and the ruler almost puked.
