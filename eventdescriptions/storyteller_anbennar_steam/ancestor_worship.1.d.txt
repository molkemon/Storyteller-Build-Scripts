After rediscovering Gor Vazumbrog, tales have reached our ears of a great dwarf. Known as Krummul orkslayer, he held the line against the orkish invasions into the Segbandal, and even ventured onto the surface world to aid the humans of Bulwar against an orkish incursion from HulJorkad. After his passing, the dwarves of the old Segbandal raised him to a position of great honour on their shrines, as one of the Great Ancestors.
