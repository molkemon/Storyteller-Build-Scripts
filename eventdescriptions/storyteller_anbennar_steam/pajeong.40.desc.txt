It was not so long ago that Sage King JeongHun Gyo conquered the Shuvüüsh tribes now within our lands. There remain many who remember their lives as an independent clan and wish to return to that unfettered existence. With his passing and the destabilization of the realm under the current rule of the monarch, they believe their wish might finally be realized. The local authorities, trained by the Administrative Academies of the Neoksamaji, are nervous, as the local communities begin to ignore them and instead claim more autonomy for themselves.
