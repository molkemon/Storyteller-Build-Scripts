At the Raja's command, our most loyal soldiers have arrived at the site of one of the Suspicious Ministerial Estates. They have conducted a thorough search of the premises, seizing logbooks and holding the administrative staff for questioning. In their investigations, our forces have found damning evidence that reveals that many crimes were committed by the estate's overlords. But before arrests could be made, the ministers in question fled the scene and rallied their local guards and mercenaries to attack the Imperial Army to cover their escape. It comes now to strength of arms to purge the Raj of the disease that plagues it; to battle.
