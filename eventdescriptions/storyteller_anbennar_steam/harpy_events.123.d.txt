Our rise to the title of Queen of Harpykind has been prestigious and meteoric. Our prestige, our title, our claim to this mantle cannot be contested by any other flock; both those under our banner and those who will one day kneel to our Queen. One part of our ascension remains unclear; where the home of our flock shall lie. We have organized our administration from our current capital for as long as we can remember, and it is the home and history of our flock. However, the historic capital of Harpylen past lies within our realm; Heunthume. Will we maintain our current home, or will we fully embrace the mantle and legacy of Queendoms past?
