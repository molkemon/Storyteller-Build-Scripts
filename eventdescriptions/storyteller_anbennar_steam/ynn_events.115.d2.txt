Lukaus is the mythical founder of Dolindha, who first tamed the Antler Horses of the Ynnic wilds and trampled his foes in a stampede of might. While Lukaus's dynasty ended, he is remembered for fathering so many bastard children; any person in the Ynn could very well have some of his blood in them. For their feats in horseriding and their outstanding empathy with any steed they mount, people have taken to saying that the blood of Lukaus runs very powerfully in the military advisor, and any who rides against them is either a fool or a fellow champion.
