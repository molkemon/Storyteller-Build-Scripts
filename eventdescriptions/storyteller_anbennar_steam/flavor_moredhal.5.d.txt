Our relationship with the Gentry has continued to improve. Indeed, things have gotten to a point where we have been inducted into the organization ourselves, and worshipped at the feet of their true god, who is decidedly not Corin. And wouldn’t you like to know who that is? Since we purged the records, the nonhuman vermin that have been leaking into our country have seemed to understand that they are no longer welcome here. And they have been "helped along" by some of our more zealous compatriots. Good riddance, I say. Good riddance. The sooner they leave, the sooner we can restore Escann to its former glory. And one of the ways that we can do this is by ensuring that our heir is worthy. Our compatriots have offered to ensure that the heir’s abilities are greatly magnified through their rituals, and they have asked for nothing in return – merely our unyielding support. Which they already have. So what else remains to be done?
