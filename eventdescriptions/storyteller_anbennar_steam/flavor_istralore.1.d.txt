The Dukes of Istralore have long been the traditional marshals of the Empire of Anbennar and as such held sway over the imperial army itself.  Shortly after the Corinite Proclamation in 1516, army zealots, who were ostracized within their own military circles, petitioned the Duke of Istralore to support the religion and grant it official legitimacy within the eyes of Anbennarian nobility and the Empire itself.
