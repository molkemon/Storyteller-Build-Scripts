A Grisgris, the special religious amulet of Fangaula, is given to everyone once they are considered an adult and stays with them until their very last breath. Yet somehow, either through an evergrowing population, decreased production, or a lack of proper resources, the number of grisgris amulets being made isn't sufficient for every new adult. The people have started speaking openly about this issue, and the blame has fallen on the heads of the clergy. The clergy come to us with a request for financial aid to avert a possible crisis of faith in our country. What shall we do?
