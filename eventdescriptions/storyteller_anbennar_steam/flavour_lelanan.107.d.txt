They did not always love each other, so in fact it started with a collapsing home. She tried to evict the annoying Kobold, he tried to blow up the blasted Gnome, but as they fought and tangled and competed, they found a fondness within another. And in time their anger softened, even if sometimes they still hated each other, but their romance was a deep taboo. Their biologies were completely not a match, and when they were discovered one day, their joint work was dumped down the hatch. So they followed the artificer tradition and fled to where they could be free, holding one another as they got on a boat and came to us across the great big sea. Their teams of companions came with them, and now they run the Lelanàn workshop, but you won’t find them there. You’ll find them admiring a raindrop.
