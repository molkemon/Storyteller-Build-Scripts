We have mapped out a clean path through the most accessible parts of the pillar and secured the route from most dangers. With the establishment of a basecamp this far down into the Sandworm Gene Pillar, further expeditions deeper into the maw should be possible with some preparations, but for now it is best if we make our way back out. However, we are bringing some very important information with us. We seem to have found some evidence that hints at the possibility of extracting the pillar out of the sandworm. These ancient notes indicate that some sort of antisandworm device or weapon can be found in the further reaches of the pillar. If we were able to get to it and make it work, we might be able to use it to kill the sandworm or potentially force it to spit the pillar out. And having mastered such a device, we can use it to ward off other sandworms, making further explorations of the pillar considerably safer.
