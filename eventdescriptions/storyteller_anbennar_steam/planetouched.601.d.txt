Our glorious ruler is blessed, and with their ascension to our throne, life has seemingly blossomed. All over our domain, trees bear more fruit, apples swell larger, and livestock grow fatter and healthier; even the runts of the litter now look like prized cattle by normal standards. This has done wonders for our exports, as traders and nobles in and outside our realm hear about our unique produce and all desire to sample it. As a result, the price of our cattle has grown tenfold and sometimes even more. However, as the cattle are a limited breed, a question has arisen. What should we choose to do with these so-called blessed cows?
