The harimari of Cangji have been our rivals for many centuries, vying to seize total control of the plateau and the Rukunai population in return. We have traded blows for generations, never managing to bring each other low and bring the entire region into the fold. But something is stirring within Cangyuan; our spies tell us that monks are gathering en masse within the temple, and the warrior caste of the Ashen Soul mobilizes arms in greater numbers than ever before. The threat to our nation is clear. Who else would they attack except their hated rivals of old? These outsiders sit in their reclusive temples, hiding away from the world, and expect us to give in to their bids for power? The ridiculousness of the situation nearly outweighs the gravity of existence. We cannot sit by and let them attack us; we must strike first.
