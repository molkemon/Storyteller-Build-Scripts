Lastly, it is clear that Bjarnrik cannot remain in a state of halfhearted prohibition of raiding. To the principalities of Cannor, we are nothing but weakwilled shadows of our ancestors who let thieves plunder their lands. To the Olavish, we are seen as cowards who reject the ways of our forebears.  The house of Sidaett is aligned with the Crown in ending the practice of raiding for good. Varheim and Frostbrygja are yet to openly declare, yet both have already followed our lead of ending raiding. Revrhavn has vigorously defended their right to raid, claiming that ending this will deprive us of everything that makes us Gerudians. The Blodfjellers shake their heads at this mess, insisting that we should be united in dealing with the Trolls, not infighting.
