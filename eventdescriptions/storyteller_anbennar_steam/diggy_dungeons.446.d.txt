This was a foe unlike any they have faced thus far. The beast clambered up and down the walls, gracefully weaving between projectiles and rock formations like a dancer across a stage. Even when arrows or spells met their mark, they bounced harmlessly off of the Behir's thick blue hide, less bothersome than a bee's sting. Yet their men were relentless. Dodging lightning breath and creating wards, they steadily and systematically took control of the situation, beating the Behir back with each successful blow. After many minutes of tense back and forth, silence envelops the field.
