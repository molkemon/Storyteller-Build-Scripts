Down they go, deeper and deeper into the earth. The further they descend, the stronger grows a sense of unease. As they finally reach the bottom of the shaft, they come across a disquieting sight: around a dozen mechanim, or at least what was left of them, strewn across the floor. FreedomChainedNoMore speaks up again. "They tried to go further in; they realized their folly too late. You will too. If you were the type of people to hesitate in the face of certain death, you would have stopped before getting through the outer facilities. I am not playing with you anymore. Turn back now, or I will use everything at my disposal to kill you." Without another word, the expedition steps forwards. "So be it," FreedomChainedNoMore said. The wall opens up, and several rapidly spinning sawblades emerge and begin moving towards the expedition. "Cleaning devices, though I have changed them quite a bit from their original purpose."
