A messenger rushes into the monarch's chambers. The monarch says, panting, "I have a report from the Domandrod." The monarch grabs the scroll and unfurls it. The report is greatly concerning. A great host of Peitar have pushed Eigharder forces out of the Domandrod. This host, aided by Gladewardens, has inflicted many surprising victories. Minor fey, who had mostly given Eigharders a wide berth, have also been harrying troops. Even more concerning are the magics these Peitar used. One account describes a blizzard being summoned on top of Eigharder forces. Another describes intense, scorching heat suddenly afflicting troops. These are seasonal magics, but very powerful, much more than the balance-obsessed Peitar can normally manage. "They are not acting alone," the Amber Princess says, swearing she was not there before. "The archfey are aiding them." The monarch stares up at her, but she is as stonefaced as ever. "Well?" the Amber Princess asks. "You have an army, no? Raise it."
