Mages are powerful beings. Their control of the elements, their mastery of the world, and their bond with all that is ethereal is nothing to scoff at. Everything they can accomplish, from the perspective of just a normal being, is almost unreal. It can make one feel weak in comparison.  But what if, what if someone could harness and control magic in the same way that mages can? Leading scientists amongst Orlghelovar have taken interest in using their excess supply of Dames tear in a new gigantic undertaking: Controlling and focusing magic to their whim, via the form of a ray of magical energy. While all of this theoretically would require a mage, what if they removed the mage completely? Put the power into the hands of the normal one might say. With the new developments with glass, Dames tear, and science as a whole, it looks to be a mountain that Orlghelovar may be able to take on. What was once considered impossible now may simply be a few inventions away.
