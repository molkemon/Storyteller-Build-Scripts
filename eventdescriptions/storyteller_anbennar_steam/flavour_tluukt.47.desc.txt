Our alliance with the Tluukt pack was forged of mutual interests, yet as of late the ruler of Tluukt has not made a secret of their discontent with the arrangement. In better times their dissatisfactions would be seen as the spurious lies they are, but in truth our failures to consolidate the Far Salahad under our pack and the consequent instability of our rule leaves us with little opportunity to test our alliance; mollifying the Tluukt pack with the traditional gift of krah may prove the more fruitful decision.
