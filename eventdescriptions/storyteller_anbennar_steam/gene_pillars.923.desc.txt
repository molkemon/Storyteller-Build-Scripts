You have reached a complex of rooms on the upper floors that appear to be residential quarters once again. Unlike those rooms you found previously, however, there is quite a lot of ornamentation here, along with one adjoining room that potentially could be a small laboratory. From these finds, your colleagues hypothesize this level may be the personal quarters of one of the lead researchers within this facility. While much of the relics here have faded due to the time since the facility was occupied, there are still many indicators of wealth, such as status markers and even house symbols. Among the ruins is a sealed box. Somehow, the enchantments placed on it persist to this day. After we determined no harm would occur from opening the vessel, we found a collection of notebooks written in the same language we have found throughout the facility. These will likely prove paramount to understanding the purpose of this complex.
