A strangely unique Chronicler has become known to us, traveling the land and observing other Chroniclers while chronicling their actions and work. Unsurprisingly, he has found one of our chroniclers to be amongst the greatest of the Taga'ala and is planning on recording several of her great works she has participated in for posterity. Naturally this has brought much added acclaim to our nation for hosting such a well-regarded chronicler.
