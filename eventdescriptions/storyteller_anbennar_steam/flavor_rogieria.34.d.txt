What is it to be a true heir to Rogier the Exiled? It is to be righteous, to lead one’s people against evil because evil must be opposed. It is to have piety, to stand loyal to your homeland and people and family even in their greatest hardship. It is to show mercy and justice, to welcome and embrace those in darkness reaching for the light. It is to have his skill, by which to rule this land so that it may prosper and grow towards ever greater glory. Has our ruler not demonstrated these qualities? Do we not stand rich and powerful under their rule? Are we not admired by our friends and envied by our enemies? Is our ruler not looked to as the model of ruler ship for all civilized nations? Truly, our lord is Rogier’s righteous heir.
