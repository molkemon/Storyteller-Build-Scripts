Alerion, currently a general in our armed forces, walks through the remains of the last city to fall under our yoke. Arrows, sweat, blood, corpses and flags litter the ground of the palace of the city. Arriving in the throne room, she sees, among the broken swords and spears, the bullet marks and collapsed pillars, a seat on the dais. She takes her place on the throne, the heavy weight of the dream of her people finally lifting from her shoulders. In a joyful, yet strong voice, she sings.  'I am more than a conqueror, More than you think, I carry with me, a flame that can never burn out. I believe, I am mightier thanks to God, I peacefully sing, under his warm rays. I have victory. I am free indeed I can soar like an eagle now, As finally, I can see the Sun Dawn.'
