I told the Esteemed Leader that I am his page now, and this is the job of a courier, which I am no longer. In an attempt at flattery, he said such a job could not be trusted to a mere courier. Hah. Like I am falling for that one. Alas, here I am, delivering a very hefty parcel to some dingy backalley gambler's den. Supposed to ask for Muur, whoever that is. Oh well, maybe I will pick up a card game or two; might even get lucky.
