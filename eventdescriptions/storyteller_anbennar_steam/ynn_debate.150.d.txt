A proclamation has come down from a distant kingdom in favor of accepting the Corrupted Ruinborn as equals along the currents of faith. They state that although the savages are not capable of civilization like true people of the former empire, that does not preclude them from grace. All life flows from the divine, and so all life is precious to its worship. Even common animals deserve some protection of which the Corrupted are no exception. Even more so as they are able to learn our language though crudely, and, though they foolishly worship animal spirits and runic inscriptions, this is only the misguided interpretation of our own zodiac and they can be saved from their beliefs.
