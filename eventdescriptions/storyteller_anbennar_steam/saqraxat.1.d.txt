Even before the Kheteratan collapse, the internal politics of Kulugiash were balanced on a knife’s edge. Successive rulers limited the encroachment of Medawor settlers, in order to maintain the autonomy of the Efergezmu. In turn, they defended the legitimacy of the settlements, and protected them from any revanchist reprisals. This careful balancing act has been maintained for decades, yet its inherent tension continues to simmer under the surface. Now is the time to finally resolve this split in our favor. There will undoubtedly be a backlash in response to our intervention, yet Kulugiash must become an integral part of our domain for all of Saqraxat to thrive. The recalcitrant Efergezmu shall be broken, so that Kulugiash’s Medawor can finally take their place.
