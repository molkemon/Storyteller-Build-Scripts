The monarch feels nothing at first. But after a few seconds, they feel a warmth in their stomach that then turns into a burning heat which radiates through their body. It feels like lava spreads through their veins. The monarch starts to sweat like never before. They try to stay composed, but their consciousness threatens to slip away. Their vision turns black as the ruler is about to faint, but suddenly inside the darkness they again see the birth of a golden phoenix. Seconds later, the monarch is back to reality, and the burning heat is replaced with a feeling of strength.
