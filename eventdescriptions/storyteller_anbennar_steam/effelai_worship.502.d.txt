As if anticipating the heir's emergence from the temple's depths, the very air seems to tremble with a sweet chorus. Gangly roots writhe erratically, before withdrawing from their previous position guarding the entrance. While the blooming has always been rampant within this region, the foliage seems to now be barely able to contain itself from reaching out and engulfing the temple. We could see a fierce light emanating from within numerous gaps, and a sense of relief had already begun to wash over those attending the ceremony. The heir quickly strode forward from within the enveloping light, a warm glow emanating briefly from their form, before quickly dissipating. They had succeeded, and the Goddess's blessing continued to cast a calming hand across all those in the vicinity, even as the song contorted into a rapturous cascade of deafening voices.
