In a forest far to the north, there lived a mighty Eagle. He thought that he could rule all he saw. A bunch of people turned into wolves, and he thought the wolves were evil, but he forgot to look at himself. He coveted the land to the south, its roses, wine, and treacle. A shame it also coveted him. He tried to take the City of Dyes, trying to crush its people, but only he was crushed in the end. All around the big Dragonmount, Iochand chased the Eagle, and after all the battles were won.
