Centuries ago, our ancestors met with the goddess Corin. Recognizing the nobility of her character and the justness of her cause, they quickly pledged their allegiance and swore to fight the Greentide as Marcher Lords in her name. In return for their zeal and righteousness, Corin bestowed upon them divine right of lordship over a land of their own in Escann. Many generations have come and gone, but we are still fundamentally the same people as back then. We still seek the same piety and faith in Corin as the Marchers had, and our soldiers still fight against evil and injustice just as fervently as they did. We have a glorious yet heavy legacy to live up to, yet we are just the goblins to make it justice.
