When Corin joined the pantheon in fourteen forty-five, many Escanni refugees devoted themselves to her, seeing Corin as their heroine and the one that put an end to the Greentide. Decades later with the Corinite Proclamation, a majority of refugees remaining in Wescann converted and some of our people are seemingly being influenced by them. If we do not act, they might convince some of our own people to turn to Corinism. It isn’t necessarily a concern, but keeping things under control while we still can might be preferable, as these heretic beliefs could spread even further. We can leave them be, but such autonomy could be a threat to us long term. Should we suppress them?
