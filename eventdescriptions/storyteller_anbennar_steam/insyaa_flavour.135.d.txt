Other races tend to be vexed by our appearance. We are mechanical beings unlike anything they have seen, which makes us fearsome foes on our own. Yet, we can go further by intentionally modifying our bodies with cultural symbols they may find appalling or otherwise intimidating. horrific scenes of battle or tragedy, such as a warrior’s gallant triumph over multiple enemies, or a particularly horrific savaging of a foe, or otherwise making ourselves more menacing through modification. These specially crafted plates are adopted by warriors during times of war. Through the use of these plates, we shall strike fear into the hearts of any man, kobold or ogre, and it is far easier to fight a disorganized rabble than a brave cohort.
