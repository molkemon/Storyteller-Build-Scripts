The invitations were sent out to all the temples, lords, and prominent figures in Bulwar; the Council will take place one year from now in Medurubar, a city which sits on the conflux of the Suran and Buranun rivers in Irrliam territory. The debates will take place in a grand hall that has been prepared in the city to hold all the delegations and splendour that will, no doubt, begin to arrive in short order. Now, we have only to decide how grand our own delegation should be. A large and splendid one could both show our support for the cause of unity and help to make us seem a powerful player in these matters. But, if we are to outshine both friend and rival, we will have to be unconcerned with cost.
