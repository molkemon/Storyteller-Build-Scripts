Fate favors our southern sibling. The Nékhei of Apikhoxí has recently thrown off the fetters of their feeble overlords in Ormam, leaving them free to pursue their own destiny. Apikhoxí straddles the mouth of the Xiktheam river, and holds dominion over the best farmlands in Devand. Therefore, it is perhaps to be expected that they have grown both wealthy and powerful since their foundation. Today, an envoy from Apikhoxí came to Keyolion, and made the ruler an offer. It is an unusual offer, to speak plainly. The usual niceties, military support, recognition for our claims to Vothelisí, but most interestingly, an offer of knowledge exchange, and research grants for a new field of study. Sakkharology. The scientific understanding of sugar, its cultivation and applications. It is a daring request, and since we have only recently thrown off our own shackles, it would perhaps be unwise to jump from one lap to the next. Even so, Apikhoxí is closer to our home, and we have far more in common with them than with any lord of distant Alecand across the sea. Perhaps unity is the way forward. How shall we respond?
