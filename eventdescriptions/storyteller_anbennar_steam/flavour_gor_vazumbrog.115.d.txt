Without the Copper Dwarves, it wouldn't have been possible. While the interior sea of Claxac's Abyss was within the confines of the Serpentspine, the Titanium warriors were nonetheless out of their depth when it came to hunting a monster within the murky brine. As fate would have it, a few merchantmarines of Tungrian descent happened to be passing through on a rare inland trade foray to procure some Jorkadian silk, and were able to be enticed to build this ship in a bottle to battle the coveted megafish and acquire its hide. It was an ugly thing, the Pale Leviathan. fattened on lesser fish and less fortunate dwarves, eyeless, impossibly long, its hide a terrible troglodyte white. Uglier still was the ship that would end it. its magically reinforced Titanium bottom and distinctly unseaworthy sides, more a platform on which to cast abjuration magic and from which to fire harpoons than a proper ship at all. And yet, it took the Leviathan's pounding long enough for the harpoonists to win out, crumbling only in the exact moment that the great white fish spurted out deep black lifeblood from a lethal, heartpiercing blow.
