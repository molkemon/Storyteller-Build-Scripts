We have ousted the false Kheteratan kings from the capital, sending them in exile to the countryside. The High Priest entered Kheterat following the news of our success and swiftly proclaimed the Khetarch as the true king of Kheterata. It shall henceforth be the duty of the High Priest to manage matters both religious and secular under the guidance of the Khetarch's teachings, so we may always be rightfully guided by Nirakhet's kin.  The odds were against us, but our people's faith in the Divine Mother has shown its truth, and we can now begin the purification of the lands her tears nourished for us.
