The burden of governance sometimes lies heavy upon even the most accomplished ruler. For those who subscribe to the teachings of the Mystic Accord, however, the wisdom of the ancestors is but a prophetic dream away. Such was the case last night for the ruler, who awoke on their desk after a long evening of fruitless worry over the latest bureaucratic imbroglio, only to find a concise outline for a thorough administrative reform that promises to alleviate the realm’s bureaucratic woes, seemingly written by their own hand.
