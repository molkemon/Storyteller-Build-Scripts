Members of the Ravelian Society have always been known to be freethinkers, radicals and nonconformists, but it seems it has now come to a head. Mysterious pamphlets appear overnight throwing all sorts of insults against the established church. Reports even say that at one point there was a lyrical poem making fun of the High Priest. While the priesthood are clearly outraged, it does seem that the pamphlets is largely a comedic work against the absurdities of clerical customs.
