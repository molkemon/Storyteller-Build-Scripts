The Khet spend the majority of their time aboard their Pleasure Barges, great moving temples of luxury and debauchery that slowly move through the Mother's Sorrow. In these barges, the Khet are pampered by hundreds of servants and given access to the most luxurious and exotic goods available. It is in the state's best interest that nothing interrupts the pleasures of the Khet or the movement of their barges, but sometimes this requires steep sacrifices. It is unclear what happened this time; perhaps something offended a Khet, causing them to retaliate with terrible magic, or else it was necessary to strip the land of valuable resources or to conscript the locals as servants to satisfy the whims of the catheaded beings. Maybe it was all just an unfortunate accident. Whatever it was, the province through which the barge passed has suffered greatly, and now it falls to them to mend this damage while the Khet continue their journey down the river.
