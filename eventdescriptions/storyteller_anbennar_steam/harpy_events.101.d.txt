The death of our previous ruler without a designated favored daughter sent her daughters into a flurry of activity, as planned conflicts flared and woven plots ensnared competitors. But in the end, it was the ruler that would triumph. She was never regarded as particularly exceptional and was never the favored child of her mother, but perhaps it is this very lack of distinction that allowed her to escape the attentions of her sisters. With her more competent competitors eliminating each other, the ruler endured and has been proclaimed the new ruler of the flock. She will lead it with her unassuming demeanor.
