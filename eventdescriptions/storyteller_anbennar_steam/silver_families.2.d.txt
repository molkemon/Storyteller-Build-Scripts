The House of Siloriel is one of the greatest of the Silver Families, originating with the marriage of Ioriel Redrose, chief diplomat, and, after the death of Munas Moonsinger, leader of the League of Free Realms during the War of the Sorcerer King, and Ruben Lorentis, King of Lorent. Under the family, Lorent would become one of the most elvenized realms of Cannor, equaled only by the Grand Duchy of Dameria. Lorent remained independent of the Empire of Anbennar, though eventually a Siloriel managed to be elected to the imperial throne, sparking the Lilac Wars. This would result in a victory for Lorent’s Rose Party, though several duchies under cadet houses in the Bay of Wines would break away from the Kingdom’s control. The House of Siloriel is known for its political acumen, martial traditions, and courtly etiquette.
