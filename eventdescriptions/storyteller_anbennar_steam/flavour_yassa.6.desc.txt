The most prosperous and populated of the human lands in east Sarhal is Madriamilak. Here, the rich and prosperous Mengi dominate countless kingdoms big and small, all united by their reverence for the god Amilak. Great cities such as Yetmesira and Dakmender draw people from all walks of life across Halann. Many of them have established significant outposts of their own here, eager to obtain even a sliver of the wealth that flows from Madriamilak. Our own merchants are eager to stake their claim in a land so rich with commerce, asking us to name a port they can direct their efforts to.
