With the device operational once more, it seems to have had an impact on the worm. The shaking has lessened, and we have not felt any major twists and turns in hours. To the best of our understanding, it seems the device is managing to siphon some of the energy from the sandworm's movement. This seems to have the beneficial secondary effect of slowing it down and making it more docile. With the sandworm now less volatile, we believe we might now be able to access some of the most dangerous sections of the pillar we have so far been avoiding. It will bring us to the deepest depths of the pillar, but if anti-sandworm devices can be found, it will be there.
