With the colonization of Aelantier well underway, one of our advisors has come forth with a proposal: to utilize some of our species minorities to speed up the founding of outposts and cities in the old world. The halflings' sheer numbers are perfect for that purpose, and it’s not like we’ll miss them here. However, there is debate in our government as to whether it is worth offering financial incentives to encourage emigration there, or rather simply forcing them to move to Aelantier.
