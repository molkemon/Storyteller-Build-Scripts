The nation of Escanni wishes to engage with the Magisters. Their stifling rules prevent magical success. Although, looking at our current situation, perhaps we could have done with some more rules of our own. Nonetheless, we have long been independent of their reach. Standing before them, ready to drop to our knees, is really quite embarrassing for us. We let them sneer, and laugh, and have their fun at our expense. They know we wouldn't be here without good reason, and denying us help whilst our people die is hardly a good look. Ella proves herself a stalwart young woman, keeping her head held high, except when she bows her thanks. We hold a writ of entry. Let us put it to good use.
