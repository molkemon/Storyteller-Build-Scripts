There is always a price to pay for knowledge, and the debt has now come due. The ruler has sadly but perhaps inevitably descended into madness; the knowledge within their mind has scoured their soul clean of any trace of who they once were and has deformed their body. It is believed that they now know everything, but they understand none of it. The ruler is reduced to an empty shell spouting endless phrases. While these are often bereft of any intelligibility, profound wisdom can sometimes be found in their words, normally in a language long dead. Their body seems to be immortal, eternally enslaved by the Aspect which now rules them, resisting all mortal hardships or attempts to release them from their misery. The secret they whisper will serve us well. This is the only humane course of action after all, giving their sacrifice some semblance of meaning. The mageshamans have tried to propagate a new title for the former ruler, ‘The Vessel of Knowledge’, but rumor always travels faster than wisdom, and the people prefer another name. ‘The Gibbering Husk’.
