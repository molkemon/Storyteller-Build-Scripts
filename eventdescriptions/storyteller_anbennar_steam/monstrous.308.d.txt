As part of our ongoing efforts to improve relations with our neighbors, we have sent some of our military units to both learn our allies’ tactics and teach some of our own to them. The first reports back seemed to indicate things were going well, but the most recent dispatch we have received was rather strange. There has been a change in command in the detachment our allies sent to train with us, and it seems the new commander knows very little of our race. He has informed our unit leader that he cannot, in good conscience, do anything that might contribute to women being put in danger in combat, and therefore has requested that we replace the detachment we sent for training with an all-male one. He has refused all attempts by our people on the ground to explain to him why exactly that is impossible. It seems the situation must be escalated further up the chain of command.
