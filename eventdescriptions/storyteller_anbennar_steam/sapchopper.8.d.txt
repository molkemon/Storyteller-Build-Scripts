A good hunt is like a good war; a test of both body and mind. And there is plenty of good hunting in the Hunter’s Grove. With the grove’s conquest long behind us, and our control absolute, it is time we reap the land's bounty. Of course, the first to do so will be our chieftain, flanked by our clan’s finest warriors. With the weapons sharpened, the traps set, and the provisions prepared, there only remains one task. deciding our prey. To the south roam the ironscaled carapellars, easily able to crush an ork with its maws. Eastward are the hunting grounds of the eyrpiercers, whose shrieks can rupture a hunter’s ear more than forty paces away. Yet that pales in comparison to the Darkwood Terror; a shambling tree that has developed a taste for flesh. Many hunters have fallen to its cunning.
