A shiver passes through the room as Castan takes the crown in his hand. Then, after a short silence, the room rumbles. As Castan looks at the crown in his hand, it breaks, and half the crown falls onto the stone floor, breaking into a thousand pieces as if it were made of glass. Castan stares at the now shapeless lump of silver in his hands, nearly catatonic. The other expedition members try to shake him out of it, then drag him out of the collapsing room.
