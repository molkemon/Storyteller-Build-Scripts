What a terrible thing, to go unloved. It is a fate none of the Covenant will ever suffer again. As the Vile One’s lattice expands, it connects near every living consciousness to every other. She sits at its centre, and all feel Her presence, for She is immense and powerful and perfect. Fret not; it is not a featureless spiderweb. Nobody is robbed of their talents or their daily joys. They simply know each other now, know Her. They love Her, as Her closest disciples love Her, and do you feel that? It is the endless susurrus of new life, new souls connecting to the grand conscious matrix of Halann. Enjoyable, when one is at one’s daily work, to listen to the faint harmony of baby minds entering the Covenant. Of course there is the void as well, the minds of the dying; a lurching drop into nothingness in myriad places of this great weave. But you understand, don’t you? Not one of them dies alone. She did that. She is there when you are tired, when you are lonely, when you doubt. You flag, and Her certainty fills you, and you push yourself on. It is hard to imagine not having such a wellspring from which to draw. We will find the recalcitrant few soon enough, those left in the corners of this world. We will teach them what it is to know Her; what it is to find Umorozi in the weft and lean a little on the general’s strength. What it is to feel grief, and have Ikomeye as a companion in catharsis. What it is to hear Cheni’s gentle voice soothing us in our pain. Even Komina, universal Auntie, is here. She sings two thousand years of grief and love and sorrow, and the entire world answers. Planets orbiting our Mistress’ star, they are Family now, everpresent as She is. Mother? Wife? Sister? Lover? Perceive Her as you will.
