Jaddar Jexiszuir, grandson of Jaher, had a revelation from Surael that they must fight the battle against the Darkness here on Halann while Surael fights it in the heavens. He was also shown that all beings are creatures of the Light and all must be made to see this fact. Without the guidance and tolerance of the Jadd, they would never have founded this great nation, filled with so many peoples and races. It is to the Jadd and its Light that they must sing. I heard the voice of many peoples around the throne, and the beasts and the elders. And I beheld, and the number of them was ten thousand times ten thousand and thousands of thousands, saying with a loud voice. "Chorus" together against the Darkness, together against the Malevolent Dark, to receive the gift of Light, and wisdom and strength, and honor and glory and blessing.
