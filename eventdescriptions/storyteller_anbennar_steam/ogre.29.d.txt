The shamans speak of stories telling of the last time Ogrekind was united as a singular people. These stories say it was when they served their gods as their laborers and builders thousands of years ago. Now, a new age is upon them. As they claim Escann for themselves, the last of the ogre diaspora make their way to the Ogrerealm. With their arrival, the Gorger Lord has now united all the ogres of the west under the banner of Great Skurkokli. As their horde continues its relentless march, the procession of their people stretches from one horizon to the other, and the very ground shakes as if Halann itself trembles before their might. Everything flees before them as the horde swarms into the rest of Cannor from their new home in the Ogrerealm, hungry for the feast that awaits them. Ogrekind has been united this day, as one people with one purpose. A purpose that was delivered to them by Rancor Bloodtooth, He Who Hungers. To lay claim to all that they set their eyes upon and consume it to sate their everpersistent hunger. Glory to Ogrekind.
