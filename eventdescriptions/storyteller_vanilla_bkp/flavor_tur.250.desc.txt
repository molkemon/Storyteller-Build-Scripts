Ancient capital of the Abbasid. Baghdad, city of law. City of the great Caliph AlMansur has fallen into the arms of the Ottoman empire, and the esteemed ruler of this realm has finally taken rightful and just control of the Caliphate. Oh noble Baghdad, long sufferer of injustice and indignity. After so many years of destitution and desecration; first at the hands of the far East, then the Timurids, and lastly the mismanagement of the Persian Empire, at long last a proper steward for this most esteemed city has been found. May Baghdad forever more be a shining beacon of Hanafi jurisprudence, and an inspiration to all who embody the Sunni Faith.
