In the not so distant past, the tumultuous lands of Mesopotamia were ruled by the Abbasid Caliphate, under which scientific progress and culture greatly flourished. The symbol of this golden era was the House of Wisdom, a great library in Baghdad. Unfortunately, it fell victim to the Mongol Hordes, who brutally destroyed it. Today, many courtiers have suggested reviving the library as an institution. Indeed, many prominent scholars and scientists have already offered their services.
