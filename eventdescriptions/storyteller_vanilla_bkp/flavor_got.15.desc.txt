In 1435, the legitimate King Eric Gryf was banished from Denmark and was replaced by his nephew. The culprits behind this betrayal were the nobles of Norway, Sweden and, worst of all, Denmark. Today, in the city of their capital, the monarch of the Kalmar Union, righteous ruler of the realm, made their proclamation: the crown of our country will be turned into the crown of Denmark with all its duties, rights and prestige. The nobility class of the former territory of Denmark loses its rights, privileges and land. The authority of the new ruler of Denmark is from this day onwards absolute.
