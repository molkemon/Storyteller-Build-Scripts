Recently returned after a long stay in the Hejaz, a popular Sufi leader originally from the Sus valley has enjoyed a quickly rising popularity in our kingdom. With him from the holy land, alJazuli brings the Dala'il alKhayrat, a prayer book in which he has collected litanies asking God to bless, show mercy and kindness to Muhammed. Unlike alJazuli himself, the book can be in many places and given its fantastic popularity, it seems destined to survive both him and our dynasty.
