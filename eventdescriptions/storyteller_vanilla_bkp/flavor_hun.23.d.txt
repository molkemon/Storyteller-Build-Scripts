The Nádasdy family is not one of our oldest or most influential noble families but has no lack for ambition. Having completed his education and grand tour abroad, the young Tamás Nádasdy shows promise both as a statesman, a diplomat, and a general. He has in short time made a career out of balancing the many demands of the Hungarian estates, strengthening the economy while also tending to the defense of our country and the search for viable allies against our many enemies.
