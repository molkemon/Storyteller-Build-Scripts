The holdings of Ragnar Lothbrok and his descendants spread all across the British Isles and Scandinavia. The ruler of our realm claims to be a direct descendant of this Viking invader, and as such has laid claim to the Kingdom of Norway, claiming that it is rightfully a part of their Northern Kingdom. While many doubt the legitimacy of their Nordic ancestry, the nobles of our own realm support their claim and their approval is all we really need to press our claim on Norway.
