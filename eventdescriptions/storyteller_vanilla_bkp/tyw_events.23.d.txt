Our decision to take a stance in the religious conflict in the Holy Roman Empire has caused even more heated discussions about where our country stands on the issue of the Reformation. We may not doubt the goals of the League, but there is no shortage of people in our country who do. What is obvious to us, that we have chosen the right side for the good of all our people regardless of faith, seems like the lies of heretics to some of our subjects. The most zealous objectors have taken up arms against their rulers and the League.
