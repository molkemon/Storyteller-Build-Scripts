The tensions between the Protestants and the Catholics of the Holy Roman Empire are getting more and more explosive with every passing day. It would have come to a war already, if it wasn't for the fact that the Protestants are outnumbered. On this fateful day, the ruler of another country made a proclamation: the government of that country recognizes the rights of the Protestants of Germany, and supports their interests militarily, economically and politically. As a result of this official statement, the Protestant members of the Holy Roman Empire gathered together and organized into the Protestant League, which shall face the Emperor in war.
