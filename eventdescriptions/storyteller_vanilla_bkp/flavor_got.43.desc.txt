"Daring. When will we go?" says the young and excited buccaneer. "This is madness, our ruler, and you know it" replies the other, much grumpier and older captain. "But it might be worth a shot.", the educated smuggler comments. Our ruler informed their crewmates of their plan of raiding one of the most defended armories; the Venetian Arsenal. The reactions were more mixed than our ruler expected. The captains, the buccaneers, the quartermasters and the smugglers all discuss. Back and forth. About risks. About gains. In the end, our ruler had enough of the debate and convinced everyone of the potential benefit of such raid: while, yes, there was plenty of gold to loot and ships to capture, the main goal was to acquire the Venetian weapon plans for their ships. With them, our realm becomes unbeatable on the sea; their piratical reign unchallenged. Eventually, our ruler's crewmates agreed on participating in their plan.  In the next morning, one lone, broken ship sailed into the Venetian port. Naturally, the Venetian galleys stopped what one would consider to be nothing more than floating wood. After boarding the wreck, one of the sailors comments how the boat smells like burning wood while another notices the barrels of gunpowder littering the floor of the ship. The resulting explosion and clouds completely concealed our pirate fleet which then sailed directly into the ports of the Venetian Arsenal.
