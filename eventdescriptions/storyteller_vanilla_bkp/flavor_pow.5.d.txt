A European merchant came to our village this morning; he was no stranger to us, since we had seen him around his fair-skinned companions. He has come to us with a trade deal: the need for food has taken its toll on their people and their crops have withered from the lack of knowledge about their cultivation. In exchange for some food, this man has offered to teach us how to use iron to improve our weapons. He could also give us some cloth that they use to protect themselves from the weather.
