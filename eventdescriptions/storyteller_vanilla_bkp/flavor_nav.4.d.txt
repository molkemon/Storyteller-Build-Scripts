The sad passing of King Alfons the Magnanimous ends a reign of prestige and prosperity for the Crown of Aragon. With his death, King Joan of Navarra looks set to take the throne. Thus begins a personal union between the Crown of Aragon and the Kingdom of Navarra. The lords of Catalonia, however, are not pleased by the succession of King Joan; they have demanded great concessions in exchange for their acceptance of his rule.
