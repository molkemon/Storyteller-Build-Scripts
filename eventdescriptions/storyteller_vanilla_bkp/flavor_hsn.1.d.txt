The Ayyubid Empire once stretched far and wide and stood as the pride of Islam and the guardian of the Holiest Cities. Saladin's successors, unable to honor his legacy, have weakened the Sultanate, and the unfortunate turn of events led to its collapse. We have been stagnating ever since, however, our recent campaigns of conquest prove that our strength has not been broken yet. With our hearts set on restoring the glory of our ancestors, we march forward, for we are the descendants of Saladin, and his legacy is ours to reclaim.
