The province of Quina has sent representatives to request resources and support in the restoration of a famous old monastery said to have been the home of Saint Schenoute the Archimandrite himself. The foundations of the stone walls were set over a thousand years ago and the devout people in the area long for nothing more than to restore the building to its former glory. 'With your help, it will once again become a center of higher learning and scripture studies' they assure us.
