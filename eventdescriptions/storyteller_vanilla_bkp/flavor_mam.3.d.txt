In 1458, the Sultan received an illegitimate son of the King of Cyprus, who attempted to take the crown from his half-sister who became queen. The Sultan honored him with a special robe, and promised to make him King of Cyprus. When the Queen offered a large gift to Cairo, the Sultan changed his mind and recognized the Queen's authority over Cyprus. As one of our advisors had befriended several guards, they protested and attacked the Cypriot embassy in protest. What shall we do?
