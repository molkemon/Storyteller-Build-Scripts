Matters of the heart should never be mixed with politics, especially when it comes to the ruler and their partner. After the growing influence of Catherine in the French court, and the rising and obvious interest that her husband had expressed in Diane de Poitiers, it became inevitable that something had to be done. As one of her first acts in her new position, Catherine has requested that Diane shall return the imposing Château de Chenonceau to the country, as well as the noble jewels that were so wrongly given to her.
