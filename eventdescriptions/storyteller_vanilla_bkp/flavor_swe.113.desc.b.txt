Originally, the Swedish Kingdom once tried to construct a mighty flagship in the past, the Vasa. While no expense was spared in decorating and equipping it, the ship had one big flaw: it was just too heavy for its own good.  Years after this incident, the Nordic people gathered in a certain province to construct a second flagship, which shall lead the proud fleet to victory. With the craftsmanship of the Danes, the naval expertise of the Norwegians and the resources of the Swedes, the workforces of that province managed to construct a flagship for their country. Pompous, elegant and mighty; these are the traits of the mighty Vasa II.
