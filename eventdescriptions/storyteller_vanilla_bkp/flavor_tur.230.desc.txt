Ever since Murad Hudavendigar's conquest of Edirne in 1362, our Sultans have formally claimed the title of Caliph, the leader of the Ummah. However, the following decades forced our state to change and to embrace the Muslim faith. As such, the title of a Caliphate is no longer of any meaning to us. As fate would have it, we managed to capture the Abbasid Caliph and brought him to our capital. Through this act, the idea of a Caliphate has not only ended in our realm but also in all the Muslim world.
