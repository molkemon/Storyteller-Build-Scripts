An adventurer on his deathbed told a tale about a gold-covered king who washed the gold off while his attendants threw trinkets of gold and precious stones into the lake. The man claimed to have been rescued by the Golden Man himself and seen the ritual and the golden city with his own eyes. Before he fell ill, he made a crude map that he claims shows the way to the Golden Man. Now that we have arrived in this province, a landmark corresponds to one of the few roughly drawn on the adventurer's map.
