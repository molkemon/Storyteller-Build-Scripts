Following the death of Ludwig VIII of Ingolstadt, the duke of Landshut has claimed Ingolstadt's territories for himself. However, this would be to ignore the claims of Ludwig VII, who was driven from his land by his son some years ago and has since been the prisoner of our ruler in Ansbach. Having heard of his son's death, Ludwig VII has been making noises about reclaiming his former Duchy. This could offer us a chance to extract some gain from this affair.
