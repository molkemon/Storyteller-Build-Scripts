With the death of the Duke of Burgundy, our close royal ties to the Burgundians have come into great fruition. Many Burgundian territories exist within the Empire, and Marie de Bourgogne has decided to fully integrate with the Empire by offering to wed a noble prince of our dynasty. The French are expecting we return the Burgundian lands that were once held by the French throne, but we of course could deny their claims and keep Burgundy as an integral part of the Empire.
