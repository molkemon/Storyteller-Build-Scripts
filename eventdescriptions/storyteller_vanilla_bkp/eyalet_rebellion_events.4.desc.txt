An administrative division of our empire, as such, is supposed to have little difference from core territory and provinces held by the Sultan himself. In reality, this is of course not the case due to the vastness of the empire, and as such, a province has to be governed by a deputy of the Sultan. Unfortunately, the ruler of a province sees little reason to pay the usual tax of the provinces. Letting them slide with this defiance will give other subject territories similar ideas.
