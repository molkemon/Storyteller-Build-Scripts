The first of the great works of the Jaunpur sultanate after its independence was the imposing Atala Masjid mosque, a marriage of local and Muslim architecture unlike anything that can be seen elsewhere in India. The impressive facade of the mosque is quite unique and has attracted the attention of both high and low. As our realm has grown so has the fame of the Atala Masjid mosque and as we now stand ready to start more works of construction it is believed we should continue to develop this unique architectural style and make it more closely associated with our realm.
