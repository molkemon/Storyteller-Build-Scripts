Tapa cloth is a barkcloth made by most groups in the Pacific area. Tapa cloth can be used for several things, especially clothing, but also to create beautiful ornaments that can be very in demand for their quality. Tapa can be decorated and dyed, and it is extracted from the paper mulberry tree. In every village or hamlet, the sound of bark being beaten to create the tapa is a constant. Our cloth has become highly in demand lately due to its quality.
