We are an Order of knights, which had only one goal: the Christianization of the Baltic lands; and we achieved this goal. However, with the Reformation spreading it has become more and more apparent to us that our order has lost its purpose, and a crusade against fellow Christians is more than absurd according to our advisors. Maybe it is finally time to dissolve the Livonian Order as we know it, and form a proper kingdom; the Kingdom of Livonia.
