Three men in heavily damaged robes and serious wounds washed up on our shores. A few civilians immediately tended to their wounds. After a few weeks, the men were able to stand up again and told us their stories of their home; and how it was destroyed by evil forces. They also spoke about their faith, Catholicism, and how one can easily join them. Their stories about their faith and their technology piqued the interest of our lord, but ultimately captured their lord's attention.
