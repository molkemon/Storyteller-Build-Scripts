The graves of that province reach to the horizon. Not a pleasant look, but the result of brutal necessity. Our ruler contemplates while their guards pull the next wagon of bodies to the never-ending graveyard. Countless soldiers and loyalists died during the period of the Janissary Coup, and while our ruler was victorious in the end, the amount of life taken for the stability and safety of their government distresses our ruler. The House of Osman triumphed once again against another enemy of the country. Those Janissaries now nothing but a shadow of their former self. Many of their members are now lying in those graves while those who are still loyal to our ruler have been incorporated into the standard army of our realm.
