Long before we ruled this land, almost from the beginning of the world, the ancient Empire of Kitara stretched across all the Great Lakes, under the reign of the Batembuzi. The Batembuzi dynasty was founded by none other than Ruhanga, the creator, and his descendants held divine powers. Many kingdoms have claimed to carry the heritage of ancient Kitara, and if we follow the lineage of our monarch back far enough, we might also find a drop of that divine blood.
