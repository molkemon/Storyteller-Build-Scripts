The Iberian Peninsula was once home to many Muslim sultanates during its history. However, the Spaniards and the Portuguese eventually drove the Muslims out of the area which was once AlAndalus and formed their own kingdoms atop the ashes of our Muslim brethren. As fate has wished, the infidels have been defeated and AlAndalus is once again free from the Iberians' influence. The administration of AlAndalus is a different matter though. Asserting our control over this region directly needs great effort and the gain might not be worth it. The establishment of the great domain of AlAndalus might be what we need.
