A skilled diplomat and a capable leader, Haji Giray has spent most of his life building a strong nation of his own, separate from the Golden Horde's control. To survive, Haji has never hesitated to form alliances with any state that could help him achieve his goals. Surrounded by hungry countries like Poland, Lithuania, Genoa, and the Golden Horde, Crimea must navigate a challenging path to remain independent and prosper. Our ruler leaves behind several potential successors, but as they all focus on securing the throne, the nation's future seems uncertain. To the south, a new power is watching developments with great interest, a fact that pretenders will quickly exploit.
