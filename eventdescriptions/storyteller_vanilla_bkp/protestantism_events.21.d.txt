The theologian and inquisitor has had an exemplary career, starting as a low-level local priest and finally reaching the very top of the Church hierarchy and has over the years come to be regarded as a pillar of the country. It is therefore very troubling that the theologian and inquisitor have begun to take issue with the direction the Church is heading. While the theologian and inquisitor has steered clear of directly criticizing the ruler, it is hard for anyone to take their words as anything but harmful for the State Church.
