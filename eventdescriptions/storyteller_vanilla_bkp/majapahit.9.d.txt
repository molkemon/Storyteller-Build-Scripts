The situation in our country is dire, but it seems that all is not lost yet. A group of wealthy Muslim merchants have approached our ruler with an offer. They will pay us handsomely for trading privileges in our ports and the right to practice their religion freely in our country. The Bhattara Saptaprabhu warn that the instability of our realm may attract our people to the merchant's foreign faith, discarding the founding traditions of our domain. Silver, however, makes for its own counterargument.
