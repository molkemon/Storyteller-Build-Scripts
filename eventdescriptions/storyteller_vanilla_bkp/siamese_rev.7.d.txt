Anti-European sentiment has erupted throughout our country. A group of nobles have rallied behind a man named Phetracha and successfully launched a coup against our ruler. The swiftness of the coup allowed Phetracha to launch a surprise attack on the European garrisons stationed in the capital and to immediately expel all European diplomats from our country. Phetracha's supporters have named him our ruler, beginning what will likely be an era of relative isolation for our country.
