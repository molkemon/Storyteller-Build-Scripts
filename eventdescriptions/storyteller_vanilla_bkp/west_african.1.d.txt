Our people have come a long way from their origins. Where there were once many tribes with their own customs and leaders, people throughout our country now recognize their lord as their ruler. Many have speculated that their ruler may soon declare themselves Sultan, but this may not go without contention unless the title is given to them by the Caliph.  Word has reached us that the Ottoman Caliphate has its seat in the distant city of Istanbul and they may just be willing to grant us an audience. Perhaps with the Caliph's blessing, the Sultanate of our realm will find support among the faithful of its citizens.
