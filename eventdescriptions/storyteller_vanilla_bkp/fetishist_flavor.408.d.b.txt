The Wotan, chief shaman of the seidkonur seeresses, have approached us concerning our lord's lack of children. While we have mainly been worried about the line of succession, the Wotan is also worried about our lord's devotion to Freyja. She suggests a fertility rite in which our lord will lie with a seidmadur, one of the rare male seid practitioners. If Freyja is presented with sufficient respect and sacrifices, the Wotan is convinced that such a union will produce a strong and blessed child.
