The faith of our ruler has long been the cause of consternation among the leading nobles of our government. Over time however their influence has led to some 'new men' and even some of our older families adapting the new faith. The fact that this new faith rite opens doors in our capital, together with a generally increased visibility of our ruler's worship, has led to the rite growing increasingly commonplace throughout our realm, attracting new converts from within as well as from other parts of the world.
