Maintaining marriage bonds with other countries has helped our country diplomatically. In a recent meeting with the ruler of a neighboring country, you also discussed the spiritual significance of the bonds of marriage. The ruler and many of their ambassadors were followers of Vishnu, and knew Shakti as Vishnu's consort Lakshmi. You took this opportunity to speak to them about the importance of the goddess as a medium to the divine. Your words seemed to sink in and their leaders now have a better understanding of our faith as well as the value of our royal marriage.
