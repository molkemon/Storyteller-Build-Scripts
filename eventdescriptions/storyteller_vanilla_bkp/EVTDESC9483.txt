The Royal Chamber is a swirling mist of scents and shapes, coming and going into and out of existence like colorful snowflakes. You are extremely famished. So very famished. As if you hadn't eaten in months. The stars looking down on you, still suspended in a moonless sky, seem to be laughing and dancing. They appear to be free, and wild. There is something about those stars. They seem so right. You have gone quite mad.
