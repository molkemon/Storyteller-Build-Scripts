The anthem Wilhelmus or Wilhelmus van Nassouwe is very popular with parts of the Dutch population and will, in the year nineteen thirty-two, become recognized as the official national anthem of the Netherlands. William of Orange is the main character of the anthem, which tells of his life and why he is fighting against the King of Spain, written in a first-person perspective. For a national anthem, the text and tune of the song are remarkably peaceful.
