The first time our lord's consort watched our lord sparring with their soldiers in training, they were impressed. It's not every noble person that would enjoy the manual labor of actually learning how to wield a weapon of their own. 'But our lord is not any noble person', they thought before approaching their lord to compliment their skills. They even mustered the courage to ask their lord's opinion on the current military situation of the realm. Intrigued by this mutual interest in the military aspects of our domain, the lord has gained a lot of new ideas for their army.
