Our country has given rise to many warriors of renown, but none has gained as much persistent fame as the legendary Miyamoto Musashi. Being called the best duelist of the history of Japan, he won his first duel at the age of thirteen, and started traveling and participating in duels around the whole country, remaining undefeated. His experiences are transmitted through his 'Book of Five Rings', in which he explains all his martial arts techniques, mentality, and philosophy. In its pages, there's invaluable knowledge in the way of becoming the perfect warrior, and our armies could benefit a lot from reading it.
