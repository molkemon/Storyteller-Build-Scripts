Over time, we have managed to turn the Wokou from a lawless and disparate group of pirates into a navy under our command. The leaders have been made to serve our monarch and have been given influence and power in our realm. The crews and ships under our command are now a professional and experienced force, capable of raiding, fighting battles at sea and even performing small-scale invasions into foreign territory. From the quiet cities of the neighboring to the haunted ports of our enemy's domain, our navy is feared and respected.
