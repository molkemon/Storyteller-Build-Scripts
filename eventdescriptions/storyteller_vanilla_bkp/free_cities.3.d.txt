The discontent and anonymity between the lower classes and the rulers of the city has brought us to the brink of civil war. We are ill-prepared to face a full-fledged rebellion, and many members of the upper classes are urging the city council to take the matter to the monarch, to beg the monarch himself for any help he is able to provide. Good strong men and gold are what we need most to end the fires of rebellion that smolder the streets of our capital.
