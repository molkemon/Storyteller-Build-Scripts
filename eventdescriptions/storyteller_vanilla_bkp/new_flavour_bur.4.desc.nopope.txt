In a grand ceremony attended by the entire Burgundian court and many foreign dignitaries, our monarch was crowned King of Lotharingia by the Bishop of Verdun today. The festivities continued late into the night as court bards sang ballads of our dynasty's rulers, old and new, comparing our monarch favorably to Lothair and even the great Charlemagne. It is clear to all that a new era of glory and prosperity for our nation has been inaugurated today.
