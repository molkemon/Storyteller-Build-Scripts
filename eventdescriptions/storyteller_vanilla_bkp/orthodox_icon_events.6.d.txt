The clergy of the cathedral in our province claim that it is thanks to the strong local veneration of John Climacus, and the example he set for self-improvement, that our province now blossoms so. Indeed harvests seem more plentiful and the people more at peace and loyal than ever before. The 'Ladder' of John Climacus has helped many to improve themselves over the centuries and now we seem once again to be in his debt.
