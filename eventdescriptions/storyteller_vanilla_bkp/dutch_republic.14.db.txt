The Statist rulers have succeeded in making institutions of our country increasingly representative, but one institution that has resisted recent changes is the army. One of our generals belongs to a family of nobles who have strong ties to our ruler's House and has been sabotaging our efforts to oust our ruler's loyalists from high-ranking military positions.  Now, he's gone even further and wants one of his relatives, also a loyalist, to act as advisor to the ruler. While this young person seems like a talented leader, we would loathe giving them influence.
