As our ruler is not getting any younger, the royal council has begun to look around for suitable heirs in the widest sense. One of our most powerful vassals, the lord of Schleswig-Holstein, has put forth the idea that his nephew and heir Christian, a descendant of Valdemar the Victorious, would be a suitable candidate. The lord of Schleswig-Holstein has also made it clear that he will support the candidacy of Christian out of his own pocket.
