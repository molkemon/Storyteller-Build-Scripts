Few are the countries in the world who do not claim to value honor and justice, but even fewer are the countries who actually live up to these ideals. However, the lack of large-scale and everyday corruption in our country has made it one of these rare places where principles matter over personal gain. Even abroad, our expatriates are sought after for positions of trust, as they are known for adherence to duty and rejecting attempted bribes. Who else can claim to live up to these ideals?
