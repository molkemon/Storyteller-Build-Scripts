A representative of the farmers of our country arrived in the capital today, imploring our ruler to help expand and maintain the irrigation system of the province. The local population is growing and claiming new land at a speed that the current infrastructure does not support. The representative also asks that the state supply food to those who need it in the meantime. While this project would have few downsides besides the cost, there are currently no plans to improve this particular region, and doing so in reasonable time would likely be quite expensive.
