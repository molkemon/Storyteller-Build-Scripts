In time many of the everyday occurrences in the life of our lord and their predecessors have become the subject of ceremony. Being able to attend the bedroom of the ruler as they dress, for instance, is considered by many to be the best way to gain the ear of the monarch. An elaborate court life will certainly limit the power of our nobles to plot against us in their countryside properties, but it could also prove quite expensive. Denying them the right to be part of the daily life of our lord entirely, on the other hand, would also limit their influence, but the nobles would likely resent it.
