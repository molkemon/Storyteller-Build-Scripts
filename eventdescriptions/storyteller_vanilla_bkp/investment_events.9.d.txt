The trade company of that province has been granted a monopoly on trade in its regional jurisdiction. Rogue traders are therefore a serious concern for the company, as unlicensed traders threaten to undermine the monopoly through competition.  Such illicit dealings have become rife in that province, where rogue traders are engaging in free trade with merchants from the neighboring country of that country. The company wishes to crack down on this trade by either reminding those traders that they are bound by treaty to respect the monopoly, or by prosecuting the rogue traders themselves, making an example of them in our capital.
