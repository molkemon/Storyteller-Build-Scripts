Our privateers have long been a nuisance to the Spanish fleets, but a bold plan has recently been devised that would make us more than just a nuisance. The Spanish Treasure Fleet is departing from the colonies, carrying a cargo of silver, gold, and exotic goods worth more than their weight in either. Our privateers plan to intercept these ships at the beginning of their journey. They assure us that the cargo can be seized with little risk, and is unlikely to fail unless someone betrays our plan to the Spanish.
