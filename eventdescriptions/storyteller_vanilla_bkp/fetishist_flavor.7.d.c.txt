Our ruler has been more at peace since the funeral of their cousin was seen to. The elaborate ceremony was even attended by diplomats from our neighbor. The knowledge that the ruler's relative received all the rituals required to maintain balance with the spirits has brought the ruler great comfort.  In the evening a light breeze runs through their chambers, making them smile; the Sky God has many ways of making their presence known. 'Then we are still together', they say, before drifting off to sleep.
