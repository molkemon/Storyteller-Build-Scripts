The ruler clings heavily to the edge of the canoe, gathering all of his inner strength to not be carried away by curiosity. When he arrives at the lake where the river flows into, he finds his loving wife, standing right next to her canoe. The intense fear is gone and he runs to hug her. An intense love rushes to his heart, and the hope of everything going back to normal. “Thank you,” she mutters, “for believing in me.”
