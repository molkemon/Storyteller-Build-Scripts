In the old days of England, the North was home to many great and powerful kingdoms. The old kings of Northumbria ruled a kingdom that stretched all the way from Edinburgh to south of the Humber. Not long after, the Nordic invaders settled York and created their own northern kingdom from the ashes of our fallen kingdom that they named Jorvik. Finally, after many years under the control of Nordics, Normans, and southerners, Northumberland is free once more and we may coronate ourselves as the kings and queens in the North.
