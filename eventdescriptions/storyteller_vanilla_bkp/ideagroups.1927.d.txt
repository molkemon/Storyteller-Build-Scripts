It was a custom of our ruler to wander around the proximities of their palace, losing themselves in the thicket in the pursuit of some peace. But today they came back, slightly changed. When they arrived at the palace this morning, their eyes were bright red, their mouth dry as a wasteland, their voice cracked when they stated that they would eat the scraps of their lover's heart from the ground like a hungry dog. In the fear of their sanity, a group of explorers followed their steps and found a both convincing and disturbing answer: harmful fumes had been emanating from a nearby cavern.
