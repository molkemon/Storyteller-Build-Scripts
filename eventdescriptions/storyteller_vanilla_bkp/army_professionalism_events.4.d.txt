The campaign in their country has run into severe logistical problems. As the season for salary payment draws nearer, it is becoming abundantly clear that they will not be able to pay all troops as there is simply no way for the money to get to them on time. Our generals blame constant enemy raids on the baggage train and insist they planned everything in detail, but the problem is now the ruler's to solve. If we only pay a fraction of our troops many will no doubt desert. We could also choose to pay none of them and tell them to live off the land, but that would likely devastate the entire area.
