The nobles of our realm are not very good at hiding their political positions; nor do they seem to care that much. They have been heard in feasts and banquets, loudly stating that the fate of our colony is doomed to failure, given the power that our burghers so wrongly hold. But today their intentions have led to an unprecedented approach; one of them came to our lord's chambers before the first hours of dawn: "Sorry to interrupt your rest, my lord, however something must be done immediately. Our burghers' insolence and lack of virtue are making of this colony a fool's paradise. This must be stopped before today's council."
