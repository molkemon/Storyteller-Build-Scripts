The followers of the local faith have increasingly come to integrate Confucian concepts and our rites in their daily life. A recent trend is to identify the concept of Shangdi with their own supreme deities. This interpretation is somewhat unorthodox to the scholar experts of our government, but it has allowed the local faithful to integrate a number of Confucian concepts, rites, constants and classical teachings in their theology. There is some debate in the palace halls of our capital if this is the right and proper way for the local adherents to elevate themselves from barbarians to civilized people.
