The balance of power in Geneva is held in part by the monarch of Savoy, and in part by the Grand Council of Geneva. At any moment though, the monarch of Savoy may wish to exert more pressure on our nation and dissolve the democratic powers afforded to Geneva. There are other powerful cities under the same rulership, and perhaps by forging the right alliances, our influence over the Swiss states of Savoy may grow greater than even the monarch of Savoy.
