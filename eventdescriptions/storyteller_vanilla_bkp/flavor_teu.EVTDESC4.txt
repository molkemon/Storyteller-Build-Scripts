The Teutonic Order is plagued with unrest and conspiracy. A new organization has emerged to challenge our rule, despite our recent exposure of the Lizard League, which sought to invite Poland to rule Kulm. This group, the Prussian Confederation, claims to unite against injustice and arbitrary rule. However, their true intentions are suspect, as many among them have Polish sympathies and may attempt to invite Polish rule over Prussia if left unchecked. Their influence has already gained significant support among the burghers of West Prussia, a worrying development.
