A delegation has reached us from Naples, where a popular uprising has led to the establishment of a fledgling republic that is in rebellion against our rival, the King of the South. They appeal to us for aid against the ruthless ruler, under whose tyrannical rule they have long suffered. Indeed, they would go so far as to offer one of our own nobles the position of "Signore", Lord of the Republic, in exchange for our support. This would be an excellent opportunity to weaken the rival, if we can spare the resources.
