The capital of our country attracts traders and merchants from all over the world, and the sound and smell of distressed animals, the voices bargaining, the smell of spices and all the colors of the garments, the fabrics and the carpets sold here can be overwhelming. An area in the center of the market has been left untouched, intended for a place of worship but as the market has become increasingly busy, that place could be better used for more stalls.
