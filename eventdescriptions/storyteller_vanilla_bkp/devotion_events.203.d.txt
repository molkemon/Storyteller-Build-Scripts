The House of Wealth manages the country's finances and serves as a treasury. It oversees the zakat, a tax paid by wealthy Muslims, which is distributed to those in need, the unemployed, the injured, or those unable to work. Last year, despite the country's problems, the people had a better time. The zakat collected by the Bayt alMal far exceeded our expectations. Now, we must decide what to do with this surplus of money.
