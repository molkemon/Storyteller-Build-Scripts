The celestial ruler has called together all their friends and allies to a grand feast where they; in between all manner of wasteful debaucheries; appealed to all before them for a fresh Crusade against our country. Those present were left bedazzled by the splendor of the feast, and amid the many barrels of fine wine that were drunk, the appetite for war did seem to increase. It remains to be seen, however, whether this enthusiasm will last into the morning after.
