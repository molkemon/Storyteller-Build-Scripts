We received an envoy this morning from the Free City of their nation detailing the city council's utter failure to satiate the needs of their working classes. The messenger spoke of fires smoldering and raging in the streets of their capital as a metaphor for the discontent that the free city struggles with, and claims that if nothing is done then the city's peasants will soon raise their pitchforks in rebellion. They humbly plead for soldiers and gold, so that they may be able to bring swift justice to those discontent citizens.
