A bitter dispute has erupted between the ruling Council of Ten and one of its own members, Renier Zen. He boldly accuses the other council members and the Doge of corruption, claiming that the Doge has exploited his power to amass wealth and secure key positions for his family. Following a foiled attempt on Zen's life, the conflict has spilled beyond the council's walls, with common nobles rallying behind Zen while the higher nobility stands firmly with the Council of Ten and the Doge.
