According to rumors, the Fulani are in the midst of a religious revolution. While it is difficult to prove these rumors, a Fulani Muslim scholar in our capital got the inspiration to organize a reform within our own country. His strong message and charismatic personality have impressed parts of our population, but the lack of evidence for successful jihads in the west limits his success. Still, the scholar's popularity made the Fulani culture within our country very popular; an event that baffles even our brightest advisors.
