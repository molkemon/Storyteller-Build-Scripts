The islands in the Sea of Sulu have been inhabited for centuries, divided into smaller kingdoms. These lands are now united under their first ruler. Known as a respected scholar of Islam, the ruler has strengthened both political and religious institutions in the islands, which hold one of the oldest mosques in the region. Through intermarriage, charisma and innovative diplomacy, they are now united by a common code of laws. The world has seen the rise of the Sultanate of Sulu.
