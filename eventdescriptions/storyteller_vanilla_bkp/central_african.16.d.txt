Ntare I Cambarantama was a legendary king and founder of Burundi, descendant of the ancient Ntwero clan, and the first Mwami of the first cycle of Burundian kings. Now, the cycle begins anew for the first time, with Ntare III Kivimira Savuyimba Semunganzashamba Rushatsi Cambarantama. Being one with the beginning, he is the founder of a new era for all the clans of Burundi.
