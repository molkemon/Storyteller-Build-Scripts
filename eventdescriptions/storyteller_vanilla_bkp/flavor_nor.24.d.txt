Over time the extensive integration of Norway into the economy of the rich cities in the Low Countries has led to a growing shipbuilding industry at home. Norwegian cities strongly tied to the timber trade are quickly growing bigger than ever before, with merchants from Norway, the Netherlands, Denmark, Scotland, England, and Sweden blending together to create a cosmopolitan maritime environment. Young Norwegian men have also begun to migrate in increasing numbers to the Low Countries. Some settle there while others continue onto the open seas. The growing number of expatriates from Norway and those who eventually return to our country are together immersing our country ever more deeply into the emerging global economy.
