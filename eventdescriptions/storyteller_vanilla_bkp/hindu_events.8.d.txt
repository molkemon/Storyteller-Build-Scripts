In your search for spiritual knowledge, you cannot help but sometimes feel that it is deliberately eluding you. If the divine is present in all things, how can its nature escape one who seeks it?  You pose these questions to your advisor, who thinks for a moment, but not more, before they answer: 'The ruler of the realm obscures his favor from the land. Noticing this in your search is the first step to deeper understanding of the divine, for without the truth being hidden, there would be no search at all.'
