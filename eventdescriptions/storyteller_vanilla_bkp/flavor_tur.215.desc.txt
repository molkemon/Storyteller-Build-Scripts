Sicily, once home to one of the Islamic kingdoms in the Medieval Era, is now under our control. As it is custom, the question has once again arisen whether we should establish a new province in our conquered territory. However, while most provinces use the local government structure to some extent to rule over the land there, the clergy themselves have reached out to our ruler. Due to the history of Sicily and its importance as a major cultural and political center in the Medieval Muslim world, the clergy wish that our southern province should be the restoration of the Emirate of Siqilliya itself.
