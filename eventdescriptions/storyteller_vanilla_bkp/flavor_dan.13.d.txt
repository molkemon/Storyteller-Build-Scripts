Our expedition to India has returned with nothing but bad news. It seems the crew found their way to the island of Ceylon and immediately proceeded to occupy a local temple to use as a base of operations. As soon as the authorities found out, they stormed the temple together with troops from another European nation already established in the area and ejected our men. The ships continued to visit a number of ports but were met with similarly negative responses wherever they went, with many insisting that the Danish sailors were in fact pirates.
