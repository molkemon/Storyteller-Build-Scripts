The warmth of the day invited the youngest among our people to spend the morning near the river. They played and swam and sang songs of times long past. While following the river downstream, some of them decided to hide in a nearby cave, trying to conceal their laughter. When the rest came to find them, they kept going deeper and deeper into the cave's heart, until they found an underground stream. Moved by the heat and humidity of the place, they followed it and found what could be the entrance to one of our ancestors' sacred sites.
