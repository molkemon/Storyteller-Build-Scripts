The armies have amassed on the exercise field for the Grand Army Inspection of this year. Everywhere you look there is a forest of spears and swords, aiming in defiance at the heavens above. Several powerful generals and marshals are present. Over the past few years these men have done everything in their power to strengthen the army. The safety of the realm depends on these troops, but it is not unheard of for a strong general to covet the imperial throne himself.
