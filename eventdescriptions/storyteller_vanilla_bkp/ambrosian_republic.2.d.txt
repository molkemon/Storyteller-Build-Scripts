With the throne of our country left vacant, both the lord of France and the Emperor are openly discussing their claims to the realm. The French claim is based on marriage ties to the old Visconti dynasty. Though the claim is not strong, France has had designs on Italy for quite some time and there are few with the strength to resist the lord of France. The Emperor meanwhile contends that in the absence of a clear heir, the title of Duke is to be conferred upon the Emperor by Imperial law.
