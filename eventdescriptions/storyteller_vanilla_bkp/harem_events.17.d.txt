Their ruler's most favored Consort, the Haseki Sultan, and their ruler's mother, the Valide Sultan, are women of great influence and ambition. Both also control a faction of their own in the Imperial Harem and while they both claim to have the best interests of their ruler at heart a clash of wills has always been inevitable. Their Consort has been maneuvering to have one of her sons take their heir's place as heir, but the Valide Sultan will have none of it. Both women have invested considerable prestige in the conflict by now.
