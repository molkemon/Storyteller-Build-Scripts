The island in Lake Titicaca is known through legend to be the place where the sun emerged after days of darkness. The people living on the island were very frightened by the darkness but finally they saw the sun emerge from the Sacred Rock, and realized that it was the dwelling of the sun. The sun hid under the crag during a great flood and the island was the first land to appear when the flood waters began to recede. The sun emerged from the Sacred Rock once again to illuminate the sky. It would be wise to build a temple here, to bring pilgrims here and perhaps earn a coin or two.
