An Ottoman coronation ceremony is the most grand thing many will see in their lifetime and the coronation of our new ruler is no exception. As the Sword of Osman is girded upon our ruler by the Sharif of Konya, however, our Viziers look upon them with some amount of dread. They were never the most likely of candidates and would likely never have taken the throne if a clear favorite had existed. Some would go as far as to say that it was their mediocrity that allowed them to triumph. Greater men never considered them a threat and others hoped to control the Empire through them. One thing our new ruler has clearly demonstrated: When the need arises they will not shy away from sinking a dagger in the back of those that trust them.
