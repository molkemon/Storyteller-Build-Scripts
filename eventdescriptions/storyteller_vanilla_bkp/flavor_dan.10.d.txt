A group of merchants led by Jan de Willum, Herman Rosenkrantz, and Roland Crappe, has approached our ruler, asking to be given a charter to establish a Danish colony in India. The merchants are claiming to have established ties with the King of Ceylon and the Raja of Tanjore, but it's unclear how much truth is behind these claims. They do seem to know the area well. If we grant them an Indian Charter, they will attempt to establish a Danish presence in India and further our interests in the region.
