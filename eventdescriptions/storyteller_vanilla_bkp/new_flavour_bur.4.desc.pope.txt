In a grand ceremony attended by the entire Burgundian court and many foreign dignitaries, our monarch was crowned King of Lotharingia by the Pope, Pope Eugene the Fourth. The festivities continued late into the night as court bards sang ballads of our dynasty's rulers, past and present, praising our monarch alongside legendary figures like Lothair and Charlemagne. It is clear to all that a new era of glory and prosperity for our nation has begun. A new era of glory and prosperity has been inaugurated for our nation.
