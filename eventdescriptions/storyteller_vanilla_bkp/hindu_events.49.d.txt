To our lord, war is a family business. Our heir has been appointed general and will lead our brave armies to victory. Our daughter has also taken a keen interest in the war, studying warfare alongside her religious education. One night, a guru retells the story of Bala Tripurasundari, the young goddess who fought alongside her brothers, who sought for combat out of curiosity. Our daughter listens, transfixed, and learns how Bala's mother again and again tried to curb her child's wish to go to war. When she later approaches us, we already know her request.
