The duchies and republics of Northern Italy have always been divided through their infighting and petty meddlings. To nobody's surprise, none of the Italian states stood a chance against us. One after another, the doges and dukes submitted to our lord. While the conquest of the Italian states went relatively effortless, governing the territory is a different challenge. It might be worth to establish a province in this territory with one of the local politicians appointed to represent our will.
