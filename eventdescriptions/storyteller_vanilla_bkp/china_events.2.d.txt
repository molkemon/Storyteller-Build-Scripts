As merchants from every corner of the world seek out the rich ports of the Middle Kingdom it is no wonder that some will at times encounter groups they consider to be their enemies. Local officials are puzzled by a number of disturbances in a certain province that seem related to two such competing groups trying to establish themselves in the same region. Fearing that the unrest will spread our leaders are suggesting that we close down the ports and remove any foreigners in the area. The local offices of Maritime Trade, however, instead recommend that we fine both sides but allow them to continue purchasing our goods.
