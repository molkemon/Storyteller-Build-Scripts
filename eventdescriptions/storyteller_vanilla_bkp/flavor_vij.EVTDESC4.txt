The Veerabhadra Temple will set a wonderful example of the Vijayanagara style and art. The Vishwakarma Brahmin architects have already agreed to sculpt the temple dedicated to the deity Veerabhadra. The inside of the shrine will be exquisite, with a wealth of magnificent sculpture in the intricate structures. One structure shall have pillars with lifesize images of dancers, musicians and other sculptures, created by the artisans of our realm. The huge granite Nandi bull promised to be made by the Vishwakarma Brahmin architects will be carved out of a single stone and the ceilings will have breathtakingly beautiful paintings. The only problem is money.
