The ruler's struggles with governing our nation were not a secret for many, rumors about their ineptitude having to be silenced by our more skilled diplomats in an attempt to maintain the peace of the realm. However, the recent increase in corruption has revealed the inevitable; one of our former administrators has made public that the monetary help received by the Crown has gone directly to the ruler's pocket. This ill behavior in times of need has led to a number of uprisings among our people that need to be taken care of.
