A renowned spiritual leader of a respected lineage has gained widespread acclaim in our realm. His teachings have resonated deeply with our Muslim communities and have even impressed our traditional warrior nobles. While some may still adhere to the ancient Tengri Shamans, the leader's captivating personality has earned him great respect, earning him the title of 'Khoja'. A local noble who clashed with the Khoja suffered a crushing defeat, abandoning his lands. So far, the Khoja have discreetly supported our ruler, perhaps they could be persuaded to openly back the throne if we grant them land and an official title at our court?
