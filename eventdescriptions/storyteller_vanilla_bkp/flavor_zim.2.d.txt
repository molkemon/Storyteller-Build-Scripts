The once mighty Zimbabwe dominated the Southern African region and was the middle point of culture and commerce in Southern Africa. They constructed great monuments and were the foundation of our civilization. Now we not only have reached their zenith but we completely surpassed it too. The great nation has reached a height of power, which our ancestors could only have dreamt of. During a day of celebration, the high priest met the monarch and congratulated him on this success. They recommend the renaming of our nation to Great Zimbabwe to honor Mwari and our ancestors.
