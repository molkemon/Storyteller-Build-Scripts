The Adler von Lübeck, the largest ship our fleet has ever seen, shall be the pride of our navy. Even our most powerful and capable foes will tremble at her size and firepower as she takes to the seas. Such naval prowess displayed by Adler will certainly be the object of envy in the eyes of our rivals. To safeguard its legitimacy and aura of invincibility, our nation will continue to benefit from its glorious name.
