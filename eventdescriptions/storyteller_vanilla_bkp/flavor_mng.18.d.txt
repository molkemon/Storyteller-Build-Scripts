An unusual Jesuit has approached our court. Matteo Ricci dresses like a local, has adopted many local customs, and talks in a way similar to the people here. A scholar as well as a missionary, Ricci seems to be as interested in the customs, cartography, and science of this realm as he is to inform them of his own. This means that Ricci has had a lot more success than many of his brethren when it comes to attracting converts, but his skills, especially relating to astronomy and cartography, could undoubtedly come in handy for us. Shall we allow him to stay in our domain?
