Word has reached us that a local chief in our country has begun to construct an ambitious new temple for the main god of their realm. As is often the case, this god also happens to be a mummified ancestor of his own lineage. While the chief seems content to acknowledge our authority, it is hard to shake the notion that he is attempting to build a bigger power base for himself in our country. The priests of their main deity in their capital strongly advocate that we remove the god to put this chief in his place.
