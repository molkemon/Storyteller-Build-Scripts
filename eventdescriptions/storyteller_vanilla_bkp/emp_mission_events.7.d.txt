With Normandy reconquered, the council deliberates on the fate of the historic Duchy and the chaos that it has caused over the centuries. On a pedestal in front of them lies the symbol of the Duchy. One of our advisors speaks up: 'He is right, we cannot use it.' 'We only have one choice. The symbol must be destroyed', replies another. A third figure stands and rushes forward, drawing his axe. 'Then what are we waiting for?' With a single mighty blow, the man strikes the symbol.
