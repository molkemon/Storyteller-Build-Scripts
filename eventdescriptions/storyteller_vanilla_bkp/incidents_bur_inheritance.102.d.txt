With the death of the Duke of the land, the reforms that he passed which led to the close integration of the Lowlands with the rest of our realm have come back to haunt us. The Emperor has sent an envoy, demanding that the Lowlands be separated from the government of the land and returned to their Princehood status within the Holy Roman Empire. However, our ruler is a cunning and wise lord, and has proposed to revoke the reforms of the previous ruler of the land and introduce a Great Privilege. The Privilege would enforce that all cities in the Lowlands recognise the land as their rightful ruler but also remain loyal to the Emperor of the Holy Roman Empire. Our scheme could of course backfire and the Emperor could use this as a provocation for war, but there is also a chance that the Emperor will be satisfied for now.
