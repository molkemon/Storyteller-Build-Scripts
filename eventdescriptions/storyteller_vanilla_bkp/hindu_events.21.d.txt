A long time has passed since the war where you decided to worship the fearsome image of Shakti. Back then, your country was under attack; now, your own armies are attacking. Having seen both sides of war, you feel like you better understand the destructive forces that reshape the world. Your memories also cause you to stop and consider your own motivations in pursuing this aggressive course of action. The goddess you know is fierce, but to what end? Meditating on why you bring death to your enemies may give insights into why the goddess brings death.
