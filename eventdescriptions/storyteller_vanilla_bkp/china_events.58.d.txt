Strange noises have been coming from the villa of one of our Grand Secretaries at night, growing louder and more intense each month. At first, we thought it was just a matter of him having a good time with one of his concubines, but it's become clear that there's a strange connection between the lunar phase and the noises. A spy who snuck into the garden on a full moon night reported that the Grand Secretary spends his nights standing in the garden, howling at the moon.
