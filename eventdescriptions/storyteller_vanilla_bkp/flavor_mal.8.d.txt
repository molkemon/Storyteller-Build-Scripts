The governor of a province is accusing us of neglecting the coastal trading areas of our realm. He has now threatened to sever ties with us completely and has started to deal directly with European trading nations rather than as our representative. Never very committed to our state religion, this province has grown to be a stronghold of the local traditional faith and its new rulers have been quick to use this against us. With a strong sense of identity and its Atlantic trade ties, this province has every chance of becoming a durable domain. If we are to stop this, we should probably act as soon as possible.
