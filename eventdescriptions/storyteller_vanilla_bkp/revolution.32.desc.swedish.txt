The Revolution has arrived. It began with a coup staged by a group of noblemen; quickly gaining notoriety as the Men of the Year; that forced the ruler to abdicate and go into exile. Although the conspirators had aimed to establish a constitutional monarchy, matters quickly escalated as a radical clique of lawyers, businessmen and liberals roused the mob through incendiary speeches inciting popular outrage against the ruler. The Royal Palace in the capital was stormed, officials only able to look on in terror as one symbol of the old regime after the next was torn apart and a Republic was proclaimed.  A Council of Public Safety dominated by the radicals has taken over temporary control of the state. They promise to establish equality under the law and redistribute the nobility's land between the state and the peasantry, leading our country and the rest of Europe to a brighter future free from oppression.
