Praise the Lord, our country is ours. Once it was the heart of the ancient realm of old, a bastion of Christian power, situated at the crossroads of Africa, Arabia and the Greco-Roman world. Among the obelisks and palace ruins we will rebuild the churches of this mighty nation of old.  The greatest of them all is the Church of Our Lady Mary of Zion. It is the place where our ruler was crowned, the descendant of a great lord, who was the son of a powerful king and a wise queen. It is here he brought the sacred relic. Every year, thousands of pilgrims come to visit this holy place.
