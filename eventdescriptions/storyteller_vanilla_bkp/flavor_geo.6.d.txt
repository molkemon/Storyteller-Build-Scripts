Throughout the vast expanse of our country, tales of disorder and chaos echo through our halls. The political turmoil plaguing our governance has not gone unnoticed, particularly by the rulers of Samtskhe. They gaze upon our weakened state with a newfound boldness, a defiant glint in their eyes. Discontent murmurs are growing into loud proclamations of dissatisfaction, as they condemn our unstable rule and their subjugation under it. Their loyalty, once unquestionable, now trembles on the edge of a sword, their yearning for independence threatening to spill over.
