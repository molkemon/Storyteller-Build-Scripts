Though the country of Rome has formally split from us, the Pope continues to meddle in our affairs by orchestrating plots against our ruler. Jesuits and priests number among his agents, sheltered by traitorous individuals who secretly support the Pope.  These people employ cunning methods of hiding agents from the law, deviously building secret areas behind walls, under the floorboards, and even in concealed underground passageways. Hiding in these secret hiding places can injure or even suffocate their occupants, so they are at least unlikely to be able to make a quick escape once located.
