The immense development project in Constantinople has, at last, begun to bear fruit. Over the years, various groups have moved to the City of the World's Desire. These groups have established their own communities within the city and have begun to interact with other communities. Many of these communities have begun to flourish within Constantinople, earning enough to support themselves and their families. Rarely has any other nation shown as much acceptance for other faiths as we do, and that is the reason why the city of Constantinople is experiencing a period of great prosperity.
