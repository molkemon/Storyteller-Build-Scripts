Oh, how innocent was his smile. How he reached me with questions regarding the realm. "Lord," he would say. "Lord, how large is the Holy Land?" He asked me once. I remembered laughing with him as we shared fruits. I thought… that what I did for him was for the better. Apparently, I was wrong. Hearing the news of his brothers' untimely demise, my healthy, steadfast heir fell ill, very ill and now, he lies in an eternal slumber… right in front of me. I want to see his face for the last time, but I cannot. Forever, will the guilt engulf me. In my sleep, I will remember the faces of my other sons whom I mercilessly murdered, and I can no longer face my wife or my concubines. All that exists now, is guilt and shame.
