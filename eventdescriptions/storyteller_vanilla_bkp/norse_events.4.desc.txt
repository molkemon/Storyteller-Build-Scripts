It has been months since their ruler's sudden loss of senses. Although it only prevailed for a short time, the visions they claimed to have seen changed them drastically.  No longer was their ruler busy with the matters of the realm nor did they seem to care about the security of the state. Instead, the ruler studied every single script; every single document about the old Norse religion. Although some worried about their ruler's sanity, the chats with their ruler revealed that they had not lost their sharp mind in any way.  One day though, their ruler vanished completely and messages from their province reached the court.
