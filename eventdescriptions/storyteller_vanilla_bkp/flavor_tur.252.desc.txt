Long the favored adornment of nobility, since ancient times, since the glorious days of Rome. Silk: No textile is more sought after, and no empire richer in it than our realm. So much more than mere cloth, it is a testament to wealth and power, to influence, and reverence; an object of veneration and admiration of all, peasant and noble alike. What a blessing it is that we are so positioned to benefit from trade of this material, and what a testament to the divine destiny of our Empire and our people. Just as our silken robes envelop the bodies of the world's monarchs and adorn their great halls, so too will our sublime radiance envelop this world.
