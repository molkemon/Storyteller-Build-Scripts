News has reached us from the province concerning a terrible tragedy. The ground there shook and shifted, erasing several villages and claiming many lives. As followers of Waaq, we believe in harmony and balance above all other things. Our world rests upon the horns of the great bull, which stands opposite to his love, the cow. Disaster besets our world when she turns away from him, as the world shifts atop his horns. Harmony must be restored, and we must find a way to do so.
