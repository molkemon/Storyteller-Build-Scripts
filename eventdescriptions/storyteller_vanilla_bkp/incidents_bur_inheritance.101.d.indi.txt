In spite of our strong claim on the government of our province, the ruler of our province has refused to make any concessions to us and has declared themselves independent from any foreign power. This is most unexpected, as the line of our province's ruling family came to produce no male heir, we had expected that the government would reintegrate with our realm, with the Lowlands returning to the Empire. This unexpected course of action has, however, given us quite the strong claim to the government and we could simply end the illegitimate reign of the ruler of our province by force.
