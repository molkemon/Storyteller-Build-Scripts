Our nation is without potential heirs. The rule of our dynasty may slip if we pass on without a successor, and the pressure to produce an heir weighs heavily on us. You pray daily for the gods to give you a child, finally seeking advice from a guru, asking why your request has not been answered. He tells you a story.  'Once, the Creator brought forth from his body four sons, to carry on his legacy and aid him in creation. However, they refused such worldly tasks and dedicated themselves solely to learning and teaching the divine. Without their devotion and celibacy, knowledge of Vishnu would not reach us.  If you wish to please Lord Vishnu, cast aside your desire for an heir, motivated by hunger for power and carnal desires, and seek the truth.'
