As rare and valuable as silk is, there is a fair deal of secrecy around producing it and creating the exquisite fabrics the market craves. The silk weavers in our province have begun to pioneer a number of new mechanical solutions to some of the more common problems they face, which together with a more rigorous work organization has greatly increased output in our realm. With the proper support, it is possible we could spread these innovations to other sectors, but it would not come cheap.
