Life on the frontier can be harsh and while the Cossacks are used to fighting their own battles, a little help from the central treasury could probably be put to good use. Last season was unusually harsh, and together with an increasing number of Tatar incursions, many Cossacks now have an urgent shortage of food for the coming months. If we were to supply the Cossacks with grain as well as military supplies such as arms and uniforms, we would not only be strengthening our defenders on the Steppes directly, but we would likely also tie them closer to our state.
