The sizeable Muslim community of our country has been raising objections to the coronation of our ruler. As the old Muslim aristocrats and clergy remain very influential in our realm and still hold many positions of power within our domain, this is not a small problem. It has been suggested that perhaps the situation might be defused by our ruler abdicating in favor of a relative who would then convert to Islam. This would allow power to remain with our ruling family while still placating the Ulema and leading aristocrats. Should our ruler continue to govern our country, we may find that these elements within our state are likely to attempt to enthrone a candidate of their own with more favorable attitudes towards Islam.
