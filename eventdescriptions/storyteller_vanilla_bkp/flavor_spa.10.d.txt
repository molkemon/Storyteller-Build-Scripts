The expanding Ottoman Empire has long been a cause for concern for Christian countries with interest in the Eastern Mediterranean and the Balkans. With the fall of Tunis to the Ottomans, they are now also present in the Western Mediterranean and seem destined to quickly supplant the weak Muslim dynasties that control most of the area. Interestingly enough, the former ruler of Tunis, recently ousted by Ottoman forces, has taken refuge in our country and is asking us to retake his lost city. Perhaps his cooperation is just what we need to be able to stop the Ottoman threat before it's too late?
