Ali, a close relative of the Prophet, once said: 'Let mercy, love, and kindness fill your heart for your people. Don't view them as easy prey, but rather as your brothers in faith or equals in creation. They may stumble, make mistakes, or intentionally commit wrongdoings. Grant them your pardon and forgiveness, just as you hope God will grant you His. You are their leader, and the one who appointed you is above you, with God above all. He has entrusted you with their well-being and is testing you through them. We can learn a valuable lesson from his words, which we could adopt or incorporate into our governance.'
