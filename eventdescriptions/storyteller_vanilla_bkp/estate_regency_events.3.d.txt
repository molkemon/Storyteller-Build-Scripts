Blood feuds are the kind of things that make realms collapse, but they are as old as the human being itself. Our ruler, thinks being in power is a golden opportunity to get rid of some political enemies and adversaries.  is using their power as a ruler to mercilessly attack any voice that speaks against them or their faction or even when they do not, just because they can. Of course, the other factions are not specially happy with how things are being done and might try to retaliate. We are at a crossroads where we have to decide what to do, should we intervene?
