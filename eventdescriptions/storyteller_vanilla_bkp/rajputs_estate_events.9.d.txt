The cornerstone of their identity is their Dharma, which they derive from the moral guidance in their bardic writings. Every leader must put kinship above all else, surrounding himself with a brotherhood of warriors and advisors all sharing descent from a common ancestor. Their soldiers must fight and die in service to their masters; nothing but absolute loyalty is acceptable. Rather than attempting to suppress these traditional values and power structures, our country has encouraged them, harmonizing them with service to the state and our ruler. They see no contradiction between their Dharma and their duty to us.
