While Mamluks make up the core of our forces, not all of our military might consists of warrior slaves. Local soldiers from the [not_ruling_culture_province.Culture.GetName] culture make up much of our infantry and are also used for guard and garrison duties. Small chances of advancement and low pay aren't the best motivators, however, and it's not clear how far we can trust these men. Certainly, their training and equipment are often neglected by the Amir in charge of them. In the [not_ruling_culture_province.GetAreaName] region, the mistrust between the [not_ruling_culture_province.Culture.GetName] and Mamluk Amirs has led to a mutiny.
