Being a nation that funnels such an abundance of trade, we see great movement of goods and people of great value. One such prominent individual is a talented merchant from a major port city who has been managing his family's business in our London office for years. His operations there have been a great success and, our profits and influence in the London market have been greatly increased thanks to him. Now that he has returned from London it seems that we can use his great potential in a more direct way.
