In times of need, the ruler of our country has been able to call upon the people of our capital to lend their support in important decisions about the continued future of our government. The participation in such an assembly, or Sobor, is the duty of any upstanding subject of our monarch and can strengthen the national resolve in difficult times. Perhaps in this perilous age, the time has come to convene a Sobor, so that we can move forward with the support of the people?
