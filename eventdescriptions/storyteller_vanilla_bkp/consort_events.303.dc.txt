Growing up in their homeland, their consort enjoyed spending time with others. They were an unusually calm child, finely attuned to the feelings of their friends, or even anyone they encountered. This almost sixth sense when interacting with people has not only made their consort a highly regarded and sought-after dinner guest, since their arrival in the capital; it has also translated into other affairs of the state. During a recent meeting with foreign diplomats, it is said that the irreproachable negotiation tactics of the consort were the highlight of the event.
