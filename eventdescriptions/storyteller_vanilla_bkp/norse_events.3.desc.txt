The ruler of our realm travelled into the thick forests of our realm with a small group of the closest advisors and servants. After all, a monarch as they are cannot survive in the wilderness just by themselves. After several weeks of travelling, searching and philosophizing, the curious monarch and their company eventually found a forsaken temple with a small collection of idols of the several gods of the Norse faith. Upon touching them, the ruler suffered a seizure and lost all of their senses. Two days later, after their servants established a small camp, the monarch finally woke up from their unwanted sleep.
