While the officially sanctioned school of our government is the [Root.GetReligiousSchool] school, there are many courts and Qadis throughout our lands and all four schools of faith are deemed suitable for civil procedures. This means that to some extent, it is possible to 'play the courts' by bringing a case before a Qadi of a school considered more likely to give a favorable ruling. An entrepreneurial merchant in their capital has become so good at this that it has angered not only the local population and his business competitors, but also the local governor. There is not much we can do about this without making inroads into the privileges of the Ulema.
