Our expansion along the Silk Road has been a rather fruitful endeavor. The investment in the trade route has proven a wise decision. In addition to troves of silk and gold, our merchants return with not just wild tales, but all manner of strange animals, wondrous tapestries, fine wares crafted from bone, and handfuls of translucent green stones said to be highly favored by the Eastern nobles. No doubt such imports can bolster the economy of our already prosperous nation. The merchants of Kirkkilise have expressed particular interest.
