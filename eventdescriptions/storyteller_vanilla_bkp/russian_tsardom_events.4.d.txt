For centuries the Tatars have been raiding our settlements, leading to the construction of a defensive line along the Steppe border. The Abatis line is a system of fortresses and garrisons intended to stop any raid from getting very far beyond the Steppe. As soon as there is a gap in this system, however, there is a risk of Tatar raids penetrating our borders. A recent raid at a distant province has penetrated our defenses and left unprotected settlements devastated.
