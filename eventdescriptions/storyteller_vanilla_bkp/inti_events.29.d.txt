There is great distress in our lands. People are suffering and many fear for the future. In times like these, a grand celebration for the entire realm might lighten the minds of our people. The sacrifices it entails will also make the gods more inclined to listen to our pleas. For this festival, our priests are urging that we undertake a human sacrifice as the plight of our people has been great and they feel a grand ceremony is necessary to placate the gods.
