The Father has taught us that marriage, the union between a man and a woman, is holy. Once they have made their promise before God, nothing can break the link between them but death. However, life is sometimes more complicated than what can be read in the Bible. The ruler of their country is requesting an annulment of the marriage between their ruler and their ruler's partner, their partner's lord. Normally, we would not consider granting such a request under these circumstances, however, the ruler of their country does hold some influence over us.
