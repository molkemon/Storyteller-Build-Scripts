With the fall of Granada, the last Muslim stronghold in the Iberian Peninsula is now in Christian control. Among our subjects, there are still a significant number of Jews, Muslims, and converts of uncertain loyalty known as Marranos and Moriscos. In order to create a truly universal Catholic realm, it has been proposed that we compel our Jewish population to either convert or depart the country, taking with them whatever possessions they have that are not made of precious metals.
