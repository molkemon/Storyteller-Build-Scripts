Word has reached us that the beloved and most favored of sons, our heir, is plotting against his father and aims to overthrow the state. Our trusted and closest consort, the Haseki Sultan, implores us to cut off this cancer from our body and have our heir murdered. There can be no doubt that the Haseki Sultan speaks out of her own interest in this matter, our heir is no son of hers but his replacement is very likely to be. Our own ministers also speak of our heir's betrayal, however, and he could pose a very real threat.
