In order to commemorate his victories over his many enemies such as the Sultan of Malwa, the treacherous Rajas of Marwar and the greedy Sultan of Gujarat, Rana Kumbha began the construction of a unique monument in the year 1442. Taking over ten years to complete the Vijaya Sthambha with its richly decorated nine stories is a testament not only to our dynasty but also the skill and craftsmanship of our sculptors and architects.
