It has become increasingly fashionable for European youth of a certain quality to go on a Grand Tour to see the sights of Europe. This way the young scions of the great European houses can pay their respect to the great rulers, minds, and institutions of our age while also bringing new ideas and contacts with them back to their home. We should consider sending our heir on such a pilgrimage to further their education and the future prospects of our country. Of course, an heir to a country such as ours would be expected to travel in a certain style.
