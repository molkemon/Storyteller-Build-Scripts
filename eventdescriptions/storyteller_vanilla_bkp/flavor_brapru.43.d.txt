Numerous theologians and scholars have all gathered in Berlin for a once in a lifetime event. The topic is simple: reconciling the differences of Lutheran and Calvinist theology. We have long since been a stronghold of religious freedom in the Empire, providing refuge to those targeted by the Inquisition. Despite this, tensions continue to rise as the people take sides in what has become a Protestant Schism, akin to the splitting of the Roman Church. Hopefully, with this conference there will be peace.
