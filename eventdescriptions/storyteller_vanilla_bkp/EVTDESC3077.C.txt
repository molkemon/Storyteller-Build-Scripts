We have made it our holy duty to civilize the New World and enlighten the native people with our faith. As our priests and missionaries perform their holy duty in our realm, they report being approached by some native shamans requesting to abandon their roots and join our lord in their holy duty to propagate our faith amongst the unenlightened members of the local tribe. If we accept, we will have a whole new clerical class of natives to spread our faith, but we risk angering the more traditional members of their tribe.
