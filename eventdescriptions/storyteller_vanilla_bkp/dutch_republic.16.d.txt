The Statist rule has strengthened recently, which we can only interpret as a sign that the republic is the future. The successes of our country, the increased trade economy and qualified administration, could only have happened to a country like ours.  By giving up some of the dictatorial control the rulers of old insisted on, we have achieved things that would have been impossible for them. Now is the time to show our confidence in these ideas by relinquishing some of our control in favor of freedom.
