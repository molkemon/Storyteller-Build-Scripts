Participation in many traditional rituals is reserved for our monarch and those closest to them. While this means the Confucian elite of our country cannot take part, this has not often been a problem as they view such rituals and sacrifices with at best mild displeasure. This insistence to keep our monarch and his closest from the old religion elites separate from our other subjects has, however, led to an unnecessarily sharp divide between the two groups. This, rather than the rituals themselves, seems to be a constant source of irritation and misunderstandings, even at the very top of our society.
