Over the centuries, our government has seen its share of war. However, it has also begun to experience an increase in the enlightened pursuit of the finer things in life, such as philosophy, art, and music. Evidence of this is for instance found in our very own monarch, often entertaining their guests with music composed by themselves. However, something is missing from their life as they spend their days in the capital, constantly surrounded by pomp and ceremony. A summer residence, without a care, away from the busy city life is what they desire, and they have already personally drawn up sketches for a modest one-story building, enveloped by a magnificent garden.
