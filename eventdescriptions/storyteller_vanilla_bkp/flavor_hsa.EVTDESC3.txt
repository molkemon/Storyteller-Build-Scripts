As a new ruler, it is customary to hold a central meeting where the league's members are invited to participate. The member communities have chosen representatives to express their views on the issue. This meeting brought up some issues with trade, the economy, and the military. As the representatives haven't managed to reach a decision on which of these matters we should address, and although the consensus-building at the meeting is supposed to follow tradition, it is our duty to decide which matter to address.
