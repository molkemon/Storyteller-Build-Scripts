His Imperial Majesty, the ruler of the house of Osman, Sultan of Sultans, Khan of Khans, Commander of the Faithful and Successor of the Prophet, Custodian of the Two Noble Sanctuaries, Qayseri Rum, Emperor of the Three Cities of Constantinople, Adrianople and Bursa, is a great man and unchallenged as a ruler. As befits a man of his caliber and stature, the Sultan has fathered several capable children and many who show great promise as future rulers. Both the sons themselves and our officials are acutely aware that only one can inherit, and what awaits the others. Our Viziers are adamant that it is now high time for the ruler to name a favorite among the possible successors. This is unlikely to end the competition for their favor, but it will hopefully stop the worst succession struggles that might follow if our ruler should meet their maker without having picked a clear successor.
