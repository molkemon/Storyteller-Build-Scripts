The soldiers we sent to Calcutta promptly reduced the new forts and imprisoned the westerners they found inside. As the prisoners then returned to their respective mother countries, however, this incident seems to have grown out of all proportion. Among the freed a story has begun to circulate about a terrible dungeon known as the Black Hole of Calcutta. According to the story the vast majority of the prisoners died due to the horrible conditions they had to endure there. Our local commander assures us that this is not true but that is of little consequence. The English East India traders are doing their utmost to convince their mother country to restore order in our kingdom.
