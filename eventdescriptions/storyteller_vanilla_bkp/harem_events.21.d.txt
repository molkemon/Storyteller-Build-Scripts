As the ruler has yet to name a favorite among his consorts, every one of them is maneuvering to take their rightful place alongside the Sultan. The Imperial Harem is rife with intrigue, and right now, making a well-informed choice for the position of Haseki Sultan, one of the highest honors imaginable in the realm, would seem all but impossible. Luckily, the ruler's mother has a firm grip on the politics of the Harem and has presented the ruler with a consort she feels would be a good candidate.
