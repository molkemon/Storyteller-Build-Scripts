The most deprived elements in our court, the backbone of our mobile forces, have sent an ultimatum to the highest authority. Recent developments have left their leader very displeased, and they now dare to demand their independence. Their aspirations for independence feed into their unrealistic dream that led to the creation of a Cossack state in Ruthenia. If their demands are not met, they threaten to commit atrocities and loot all across our countryside.
