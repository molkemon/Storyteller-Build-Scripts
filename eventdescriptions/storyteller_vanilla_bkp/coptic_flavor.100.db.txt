Our nation has recently seen many a lost soul seeking guidance at the monastery in the area. In fact the region is becoming quite popular for people looking for repentance and absolution in the ways of Saint Anthony; the surrounding wilderness is well suited for the life of a hermit, allowing some of the most devoted people to spend years in contemplative solitude.  Meanwhile, the people of our nation take great pride in this piety. Some well-placed words of encouragement could allow us to bolster their enthusiasm even further.
