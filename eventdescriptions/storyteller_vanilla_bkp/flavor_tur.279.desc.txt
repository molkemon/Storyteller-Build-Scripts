The Habsburg have been a rival of our nation for years. Although our ruler was able to defend our nation from the Austrian hegemon of Central Europe, it is apparent that this dynasty is still growing in power and influence, not only within the Holy Roman Empire but also in Iberia, Poland and Italy. An ally is needed if the Habsburg are to be stopped.  As fate would have it, such potential ally has found his way to our ruler's court. A diplomat from the government of France reached out to our ruler on behest of his ruler, the ruler of France. It appears the French want to contain the Habsburg hegemony as they pose a threat to their interests in Iberia, Italy and the Lowlands.
