The first time our lord's partner watched our lord sparring with our lord's soldiers in training, they were impressed. It's not every noble person that would enjoy the manual labor of actually learning how to wield a weapon of their own. 'But our lord is not any noble person', they thought before approaching their lord's partner to compliment our lord's skills. They even mustered the courage to ask our lord's opinion on the current military situation of our realm. Intrigued by this mutual interest in the military aspects of our domain, the ruler has gained a lot of new ideas for their army.
