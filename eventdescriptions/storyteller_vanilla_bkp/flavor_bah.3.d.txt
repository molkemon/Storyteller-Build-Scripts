Today a Persian merchant named Mahmud Gawan arrived in our capital seeking an audience. Given the chance to introduce himself, he explains that he is exactly the kind of person our kingdom needs. Apart from his personal qualities as a trader and administrator, he says that he has a network of contacts all over the Muslim world from which we might benefit. Mahmud Gawan promises that if we give him the means to do it, he will make our economy flourish and our kingdom so famous that everyone will flock to our shores to serve us.
