The pilgrimage to the holy cities of Mecca and Medina is one that every Muslim should undertake at least once in their lifetime. As the monarch it is our responsibility to ensure that they can safely reach that destination. This is a position that in many ways requires both cunning and martial ability, as the way to proceed can range from defeating raiders to negotiating passage from local rulers, to outright threats or bribery. The leader of the pilgrimage will have the overall responsibility for the caravans and will have great freedom in how to achieve a steady and peaceful passage for all our pilgrims.
