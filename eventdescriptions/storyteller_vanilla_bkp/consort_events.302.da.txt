The annual celebration of the royal union between our monarch and their consort has taken place. It's difficult to imagine that our country had it any other way; that there was a time when the consort was growing up in their homeland, far away from our monarch, who spent their days in the capital alone. It all seems so natural now. How time flies. It has now been over two decades since the consort married the monarch, and even though they are no longer children, it is with some glee that they accept gifts in honor of their wedding anniversary.
