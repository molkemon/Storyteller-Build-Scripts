The renowned naturalist and artist has just returned home after two years abroad. Malaria forced her to cut her journey short, but her experiences in South America have inspired her to create her magnum opus, a comprehensive work on the metamorphosis of butterflies. Her stepfather, a skilled still life painter, had encouraged and guided her in her artistic pursuits from an early age. This talented woman published her first book of natural illustrations, titled A New Book of Flowers, when she was 28 years old.
