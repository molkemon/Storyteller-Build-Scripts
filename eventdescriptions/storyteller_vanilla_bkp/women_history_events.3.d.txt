While the heir to our land is still too young to serve as ruler, the heir's mother Kösem Sultan has asked to officially serve as a regent ruler of the Ottoman Empire. She has already directed the affairs of this great state through her husband and one son. If she comes to power, she will rule in an era dubbed "The Sultanate of Women". She has never bowed to the whims of others, but the madness and ineptitude of her family is a great concern. One promise made by Kösem Sultan is that she will use her wealth to support irrigation projects in the Egyptian desert. Shall we allow her to rule until her child is of age, and hope to influence her actions?
