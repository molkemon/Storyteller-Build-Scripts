Born amidst the churning struggles of mystical Sufism, this order was founded by Sheikh Safiaddin Ardabili of Ardabil, and their fiery brand of Shi'a Islam has been pivotal in shaping the religious and political landscape of Persia and beyond. The Safavids were initially a religious movement, drawing followers through their charismatic spiritual leadership. But as their influence grew, they became a political and military force to be reckoned with, and have come to dominate much of Northwestern Iran. Perhaps it is time we leverage the influence and military might of this order to further our own ambitions. Will we consider enlisting the Safavid Order to serve our own ends?
