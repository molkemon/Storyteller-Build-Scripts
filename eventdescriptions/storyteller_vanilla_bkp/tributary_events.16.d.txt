Following a lost power struggle, a large group of political refugees from the celestial republic has begun to seek refuge in our country. The conflict seems insignificant enough that the winning side in the republic isn't particularly interested in searching for their fleeing compatriots, but they may still appreciate if we delivered the refugees to them. On the other hand, these are men and women of some ability, who could likely do a lot of good in our country if we allowed them to stay.
