The reign of their monarch is all but absolute. The Princes of Orange have secured power over their country for a long time ahead. Everything points to the monarch ruling for their entire life. One of their closest advisors agrees with this assessment. This is a person fiercely loyal to their realm, so loyal, in fact, that they would personally take the life of anyone who threatens their domain. In a recent meeting with a few carefully selected people, their advisor let it be known that the monarch themselves and the Orangists' stranglehold on the rulership is perhaps the biggest threat of all. Their advisor can assure that the lifetime rule will be a short one.
