With the male line of Pegu nearly depleted and our heir still a mere child, the Kingdom of Pegu is rudderless. In these times of war and uncertainty the people look to Shin Sawbu, mother of King Binnya Waru, to guide them. Shin Sawbu is a woman of exceptional administrative talent and religious piety who has overseen the royal household for many decades. Proclaiming her as Queen Regent will surely be popular among the people.
