The city officials are not the sole authority within their cities, the church also has a strong presence in most big cities going back to their initial founding. Conflicts sometimes arise when the councils of the city officials implement rules that the church disapprove of. Such conflicts have become more and more commonplace and members of the church are openly acting in defiance of the various rules the city officials have agreed upon in their cities. Delegates from various cities dominated by the city officials are now urging our ruler to clarify the situation.
