Centuries of infighting and corruption have led to the Janissaries experiencing decadence, content with increased influence and meddling in the Sultanic affairs, they have become brazen and forgot who their allegiance lies with. Eventually though, the ruler and their Grand Vizier managed to come to an understanding with the Janissary corps. They convinced the leaders of the Janissaries of their new position as an official class in the society, which allows each member to be promoted to the positions of a noble or merchant. Though not as convenient as the previous privileges, this hierarchical change in the estates opens opportunities for the Janissaries who do not wish to serve as soldiers while the ones who are born for the fight can return to their actual duties.
