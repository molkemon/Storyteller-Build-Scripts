For centuries the merchants of Lübeck have traded valuables all across the Northern seas. Recent developments on the diplomatic fronts have resulted in the powerful monarchy granting certain Lubeckian individuals the ability to not only freely travel to and from London but also the green light to establish offices around the most crowded areas of the city's port. That historically culminated in the creation of the Steelyard, a series of offices dedicated to the merchants of the Hansa that had a positive effect for both nations.
