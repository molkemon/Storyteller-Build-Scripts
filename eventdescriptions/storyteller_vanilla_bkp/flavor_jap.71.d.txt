Started by a priest who allegedly was attempting to burn a cursed kimono, the Great Fire of Meireki decimated the city and claimed over 100,000 lives. Due to the nature of the buildings in the city, which are made of wood and paper, the fire that started in the Hongo District quickly spread throughout the city. Sadly, the fact that we chose to construct our buildings so close to one another played a significant part in the fast spread of the fiery destruction.
