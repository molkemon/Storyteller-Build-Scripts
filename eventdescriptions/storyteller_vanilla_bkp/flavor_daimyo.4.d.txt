While we are an old and honorable clan keeping together a domain as spread out as that of ours comes with problems. With the death of Shiba Yoshitoshi some retainers have chosen to support Yoshikado, a son adopted from a minor branch of the family at the request of the Shogunate over our lord. This places us in a precarious situation with our lord retaining control of part of the Shiba realm, while Yoshikado has the support of the nobles in their province.
