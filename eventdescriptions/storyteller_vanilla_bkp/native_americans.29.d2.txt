A woman from their lord's tribe approaches their lord while their lord is fishing as their lord's tribe usually does to feed their people. Their lord raises their head towards her, their eyes meet and their lord knows she has seen something unusual, but good, nevertheless. With piety in her gaze, she whispers how she has seen the biggest salmon ever, a true incarnation of the spirit of the river they so deeply worship. Their lord follows her to the spot where she saw it and after a few breaths their lord finally sees it. It is huge indeed. Their lord prepares their tools to capture it.
