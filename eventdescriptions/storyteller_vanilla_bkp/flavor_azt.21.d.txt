Conquest is never enough to subdue a populace. The military crackdown will only temporarily keep the spirit of the defeated low. In order to properly govern, to properly rule, you must first divide them. Make them feel alone, and isolated, split them apart, then rule them. Our ruler has followed this strategy against the Zapotec, the cloud people. As they were already divided, all that was left was to deal the final blow, and the remnants of their civilization are now under our rule.
