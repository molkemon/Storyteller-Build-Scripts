The ruler's advisor has raised some eyebrows with their spending habits. They have been spending large amounts of money, far larger than one in their position should have access to, distributing gifts to their local estate in their home region and improving the local infrastructure. An investigation has revealed the facts: Rather than serving the monarch to the best of their abilities, they have been funneling state funds to their own province in order to boost their popularity there. While this has been a boon to the local economy, such practices could prove damaging to the state as a whole if tolerated.
