Engaging in attacks on rival ships during times of peace, whether through sanctioned reprisals or private wars, or by secretly supporting piracy, has brought us many advantages, but also poses challenges. One of our privateering operations was recently compromised and the ships and crew were captured. Fortunately, they had lost their letter of marque, which proves they operate with our approval, and any evidence linking them to us is purely circumstantial. As a last resort, the captain has now reached out to us, hoping we will acknowledge our official support so it can be used as leverage in the upcoming trial.
