An envoy from the leader of the peasants in East Frisia has arrived asking our lord to support the claim of their ruler to the County of East Frisia. In return for our support, they have offered to kneel before the Emperor as a Prince of the Holy Roman Empire. Officially, the county of East Frisia is held by the ruler of Holland. The Holy Roman Empire has made no official stance on the matter, and a single word from the Emperor could bring an end to this conflict.
