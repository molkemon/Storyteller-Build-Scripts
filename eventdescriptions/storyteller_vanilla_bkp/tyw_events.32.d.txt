The Sound Toll has been an important source of income for our country since the early 15th century and has allowed us to wield significant influence over the trade through Øresund since.  A neighboring country has been exempt from the tolls since our countries were united in the Kalmar Union. However, that neighboring country is no longer under our rule and has not been for a long time. Perhaps they should start paying the price as well; there is only so much generosity we can show for old time's sake, after all.
