The ruler of that country is a person you trust, not only in political matters. Your beloved spouse comes from the same land, and the ruler often provides you with philosophical insights.  One day, you are discussing your thoughts about Vishnu and the nature of the soul. The ruler, a follower of Shakti, mentions how inseparable the gods you follow are, as man and wife, and how the soul is also inseparable from the divine. 'If you wish to understand Vishnu,' they say, 'you must seek him through the goddess Lakshmi, his consort.'
