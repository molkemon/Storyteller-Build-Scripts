When the consort married into the royal house, there were those who were skeptical towards their origin. They were a young woman from a small town, a nobody in the eyes of many, suddenly invited to rule the great nation. However, time flies and it has now been over ten years since the union took place. While it's not all who adore the consort, most have come to respect them as one of their own; it is very unlikely that the wedding anniversary of the elevated couple could have gone by unnoticed, and so the ruler and their lord graciously accept all congratulatory letters, as well as gifts of varying splendor, brought to them.
