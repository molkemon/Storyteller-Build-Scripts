Ever since the mysterious man appeared at the noble court, he has been a mystery to our own elite class. Nobody knows his name nor has been able to find information about this secretive man.  As time went on, the nobleman became friends with the monarch. He proves himself as skillful in the matters of the administration, diplomacy and the military, and is in general well received in the court. As the monarch still lacks a proper heir to the throne, the choice is not too difficult to select the nobleman as the next in line of succession.
