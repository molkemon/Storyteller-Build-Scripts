The moral code and religious law of Islam deals with many topics, such as crime, politics, economics, but also personal matters such as sexual intercourse, hygiene, diet, prayer, and fasting. It is considered the infallible law of God and there are two sources of the law. The first is the precepts set forth in the Qur'an, and the example set by the prophet Muhammad in the Sunnah. The judges and leaders of the faith are complaining about the lax maintenance of the Sharia law in our country and insist that the ruler make sure that the law is enforced at all times.
