Praise the Lord, our diplomats have finally returned from their journey to the north. Not only are they with us again, safe and sound, but they also bring many interesting tomes and tales from their travels. Most come from the government of that country, where our diplomats were very well received. While we might have hoped for more than knowledge, we are thankful for whatever God and our fellow Christians provides us with. We will make sure that their gift does not go to waste.
