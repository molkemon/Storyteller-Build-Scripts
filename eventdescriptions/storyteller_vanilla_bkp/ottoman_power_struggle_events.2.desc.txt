The long time power struggles between the House of Osman and the many institutions of the state is finally over. The ruler steered our realm through its darkest hour and brought sweeping reforms which saved the government from stagnation and collapse. Many outdated institutions have been modernized, the Janissaries have been reined in and are no longer a threat to the government. The Eyalets and Pashas have sworn loyalty to their master and the subjects of our realm are enjoying a time of newfound prosperity. From this day forward, our realm shall be known as a state which passed the test of time and is now going through a new Golden Age.
