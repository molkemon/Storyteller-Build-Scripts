With the growth of our realm it has come to encompass many chiefdoms and in these chiefdoms various local idols are worshiped. Though it might seem a bit unorthodox a truly Andean realm should strive to incorporate all of these idols in its central place of worship. We should make a room in our royal temple for the idols of our subjects so that they can honor them when they come to our capital. Some might say that this is to take their gods as hostages, but surely this is just a sign of how inclusive our empire has become?
