A rumor from that province tells of a giant snake moving into a tree, right in the middle of a village square. While the locals were alarmed at first, the animal has made no attempt to hurt anyone, and has instead gained some social status among the inhabitants, who have begun calling it by pet names. Finally, a wise woman from the area has declared the snake sacred, saying the creature is for certain a spirit taken serpent form, which has encouraged people to bring it offerings and food.
