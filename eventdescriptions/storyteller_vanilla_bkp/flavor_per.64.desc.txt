Although the forces of our realm have fought with all means, the rebellions have proven to be too much for our capable soldiers. Especially the revolting soldiers who rallied behind the church have caused immense devastation on the land, and destroyed the once glorious palace of our domain. The government has been abolished, and the members of the dynasty have been executed for heresy and desecration of their god's name. Zoroastrians have either been forcefully converted or expelled from our realm. This is the end of our dynasty's government and the start of a new theocracy.
