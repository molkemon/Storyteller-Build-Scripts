There is widespread anger among French Catholics at the conversion of our lord to the Catholic faith. Resenting the sweeping changes in our nation and fearing for their future, they have formed a paramilitary organization they call the Catholic League in order to demand that our lord once again bows to the Pope; or failing that, that they are given significant rights and freedoms to safeguard their future. For their part, leaders of the Catholic community warn against making concessions, hinting that violence would follow should our lord be seen to favor the Catholics. Either way, turmoil awaits us.
