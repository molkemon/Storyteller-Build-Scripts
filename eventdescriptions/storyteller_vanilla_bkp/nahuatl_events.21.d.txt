As part of the grander Tlacaxipehualiztli festival this year, the most honored among the captured warriors will be pitted against four of our finest eagle and jaguar warriors while tied to a sacrificial stone and armed only with a mock Macuahuitl, a club with feathers instead of sharp blades. In the unlikely event that the captive should survive the encounter with his first four opponents, he will face one final, left-handed, fighter. Apart from pleasing the gods, this ceremony is immensely popular and is likely to attract a great number of spectators.
