A renowned woman, Germaine de Staël, has arrived at our court. She is a writer and a thinker who has hosted many gatherings and gained fame in both Sweden and France. She uses her writing to oppose the growing power of Napoleon, and her novels are subtle stories about the struggle for freedom in France. Napoleon holds Germaine de Staël in high regard for her talents, and has said that she "teaches people to think, and reminds those who have forgotten how".
