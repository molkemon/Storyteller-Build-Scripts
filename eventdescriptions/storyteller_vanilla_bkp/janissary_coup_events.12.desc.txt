Though difficult, our ruler and their Grand Vizier find some hearing amongst the Janissaries. Some soldiers are ready to give up some of the special treatment they've had over the centuries in return for a more favorable life in one of the other classes of society of our realm. However, these regiments can only speak for themselves, so giving up any of the special treatment will still lead to an active revolt, though smaller in scale than the bloodier alternative. Now comes the question of which privilege shall be revoked.
