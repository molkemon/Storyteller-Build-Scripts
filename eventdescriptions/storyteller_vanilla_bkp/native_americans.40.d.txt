Our community has lacked a place for purification for a long time since the other one was destroyed by fire. As we have had many more pressing issues to deal with, the community has managed just fine without it. Now, though, the tribe is in a relatively peaceful time and people have started to miss a proper place for purification, so our men and children can go there and cleanse their bodies and spirits. It might be a good idea to put some men to work on building this structure so the community can benefit from it.
