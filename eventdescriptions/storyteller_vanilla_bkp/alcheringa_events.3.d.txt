For years, the traders of a certain country have been the only ones Australia has ever known, but no more. Through the unification of the island of Sulawesi, we are free to control their trade and carry our own goods across the seas to China and Indonesia. In an event of pure chance, one of these trade goods did happen to contain writings, which portray the stories of a distant land. Naturally, our spiritual leaders were very much interested to integrate them into our collection of stories.
