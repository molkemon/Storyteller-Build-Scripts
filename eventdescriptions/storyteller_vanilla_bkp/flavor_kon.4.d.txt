Today, our great lord, flanked by a Portuguese priest, declared himself born anew as a Christian. Rejecting the faith of their ancestors, which he called idolatrous, the ruler is calling for a total adoption of the Catholic faith, with a permanent diocese bishop, the destruction of the idols of the old gods, and the end of their traditional way of life. Already infuriated elders are calling for the ruler's head and a restoration of the old order. Either way, the country is in for a period of great unrest.
