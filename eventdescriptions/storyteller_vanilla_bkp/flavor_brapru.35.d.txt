The renowned composer and educator, Johann Crüger, has gained great recognition for his concerts and writings on music education. He has also compiled a comprehensive collection of over 240 hymns in his book, the Praxis Pietatis Melica or the Practice of Piety. The intention is to continually add new hymns to benefit the many faithful across the German lands. This book has already gained widespread popularity among German Protestants and its popularity continues to grow.
