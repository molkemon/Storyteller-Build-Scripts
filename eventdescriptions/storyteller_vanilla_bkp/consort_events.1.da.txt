It is with some consternation that the mighty families of the realm have watched our lord letting the years pass by without finding someone to marry. These feelings have now turned into open dismay as despite the realm lacking a clear line of succession our monarch appears to have no plans to find a spouse nor is he showing much interest in the proposals sent to him. It has now reached the point where the court is expecting the monarch to accept one of the offers on the table. Continued failure to secure the succession puts the entire realm at risk. Luckily one of the oldest and most prestigious families in the realm has offered us the hand of one of their daughters, along with a generous dowry.
