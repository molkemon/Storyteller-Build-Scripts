A man who some call a guru has arrived in our country. He claims to have received a revelation, being called by Heaven to travel from his home to distant lands, spreading the message of the one true God. His local followers insist that this God is revealed through all religions, including our religion, and defend him as simply revealing the truths in our own faith. However, there is no question that these teachings reject our rituals and traditions, making people stray from the true path of our religion.
