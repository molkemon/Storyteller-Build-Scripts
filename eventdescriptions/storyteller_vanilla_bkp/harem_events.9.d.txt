Like the Sultans of old, their lord hopes to make a name for themselves as a conqueror and a ruler who will lead the realm from horseback. As their ruler is still in good health, and as they have not allowed their heir to actually take to the field, the realization of this dream remains an uncertainty. It has come to the point that their heir is being ridiculed by the other princes and their mother is imploring their ruler to give their heir a command of their own. This is not an unreasonable request, though one must wonder about the motives of those that try to goad their favorite to risk their life and limb.
