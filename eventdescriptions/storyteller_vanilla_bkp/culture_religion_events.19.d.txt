Ever since their younger years, the heir has taken a great interest in the religion of our subjects. As they grew up, this has not faltered, instead it seems that the religion is taking up more and more of their thoughts and dreams. They are now often seen surrounded by friends of the religion, and frequently even in the religion's quarters. Many, both among nobles and clergy, have begun to question how proper this is and some have openly begun to ask the monarch to talk sense into the lord.
