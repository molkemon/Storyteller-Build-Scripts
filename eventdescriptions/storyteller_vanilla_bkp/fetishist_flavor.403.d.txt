We have encountered followers of the eternal path. They prioritize a virtuous and moral existence, stability, and liberation from ignorance. It appears they revere multiple deities to guide them on their path to self-improvement, mentioning figures such as the destroyer and the preserver. They advise our people to reflect on their actions in this life, and through the concept of karma, the spiritual cause and effect, we can strive to become worthy of a better existence after this one. Their ultimate aspiration is to break the cycle of birth and rebirth.
