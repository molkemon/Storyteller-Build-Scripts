The notorious outlaw Juraj Jánosík and his gang have been causing chaos in Upper Hungary, targeting the nobles and merchants. Their brazen attacks have brought great suffering and disruption, and it's proving difficult to catch them as they bribe local villagers to keep them safe from the law. Jánosík is gaining a heroic status among the Slovak people, and his gang is swelling in numbers, with many from Poland and Moravia joining their ranks. The landowners, overwhelmed by the situation, have turned to us for help, begging the Crown to intervene and bring Jánosík to justice.
