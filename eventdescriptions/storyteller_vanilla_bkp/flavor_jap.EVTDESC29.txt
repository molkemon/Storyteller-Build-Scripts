A proposal has been brought up to control the daimyos of Japan, known as Sankin Kotai or alternate attendance. Under this plan, a daimyo would be required to establish a residence in Edo and alternate between living there and in his han, typically spending one year in each location. His wife and heir would remain in Edo as hostages. This arrangement is intended to weaken the daimyos financially, making it difficult for them to wage war as they would need to maintain lavish residences in both their han and in Edo. Additionally, the Sankin Kotai would also serve as a military service, with each daimyo required to provide a certain number of samurai based on the assessment of his han.
