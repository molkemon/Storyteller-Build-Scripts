Merchants from the Hanseatic League used to wield tremendous amounts of power and prestige. With the League's monopoly on the grain trade of the North, none dared oppose their formidable and well-fed armies. However, all things change and time is mastered by none. As a result, the grip of the Hansa on the grain trade has withered, giving birth to the beginning of the glorious Golden Era and a marvelous influx of foreign trade.
