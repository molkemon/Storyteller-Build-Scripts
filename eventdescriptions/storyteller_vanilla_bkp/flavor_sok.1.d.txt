Inspired by the Fulani religious revolutions, a Fulani Muslim scholar in our province has begun agitating for reform in our country. His strong message and charismatic leader have impressed parts of our population, and a growing movement now supports the creation of a realm governed by orthodox Islamic traditions, adherence to the Sharia, and the mission to spread the true faith in West Africa. The message is also quite critical of the current way our state is run, and the authorities in our province fear that this scholar's followers might soon openly revolt against our rule.
