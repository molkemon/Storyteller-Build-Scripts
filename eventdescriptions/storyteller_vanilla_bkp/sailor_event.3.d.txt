Our army has endured severe casualties over the past years, with thousands of lives lost in battle. Meanwhile, those who serve our nation at sea have not suffered the same fate. Despite the sacrifices they've made to protect our nation, the ranks of our navy do not lack for hands on deck. As a result, the navy has begun to encourage those willing to risk their life at sea to aid our ailing land troops instead. After all, if there's no one to protect our ports, even our best ships and crews will have nowhere to dock.
