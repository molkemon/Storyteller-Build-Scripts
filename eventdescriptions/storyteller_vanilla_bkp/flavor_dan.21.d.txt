Throughout his professional life, the Bishop of Ribe, Jens Dinesen Jersin, has been a man of renown. Early in his life, he attempted to reform Latin education by introducing explanations and translations in Danish rather than Latin itself. Secondly, the Bishop is a proponent of a practical, pious approach to faith, authoring two immensely popular religious texts, 'The True Way of Life, a Right Path to Eternal Life' and 'The Struggle and Victory of Faith'. Recently, however, Jersin has been fighting popular superstition through a text titled 'About Miracles, Signs, and Revelations, and Their Explanation', attempting to free the minds in his diocese of worries about the supernatural.
