In an attempt to increase their authority over the Daimyo of Japan, our ruler has been conducting nationwide cadastral surveys. This involves measuring and counting all people, resources and ownership relations, and refashioning them according to their design. It is a task given by the Emperor to our Shogun, but it is being used to turn longstanding relationships of power on their heads. Today the time for such a survey has come for our realm. We have been approached by the administrators of the imperial administration with a proclamation stating that fiefs granted to vassals ultimately belong to the state, and that vassals hold land in trust for the present time only.
