Just as the city of Cuzco was in need of being reshaped in order to fit the wider ambition of the Cuzco kingdom, the ruler is now in need of a stately estate to match the importance of his person and his lineage. On a mountain completely surrounded by the Urubamba river, the ruler has planned the city of Machu Picchu, a royal retreat for his court and his family, and a beautiful place for a ruler of his magnitude to reside in.
