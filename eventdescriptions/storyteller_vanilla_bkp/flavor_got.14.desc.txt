The ruler walks through the devastated streets of the capital. The years of war have left their mark. Our troops stormed to capture the false king and brought him to the feet of the ruler, the legitimate claimant of the throne. The ruler's soldiers prepared a stone block in the center of the city and are now forcing the shamed king to it. Without a single word spoken, the king's head is placed on the block and the executioner swings his axe. The head of the false king rolls to the ruler and all he feels is the satisfaction of a plan working out.
