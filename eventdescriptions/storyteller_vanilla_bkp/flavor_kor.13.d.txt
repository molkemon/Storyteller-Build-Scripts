We and the Jurchen have a history of warfare in the past, and only thanks to our natural borders, the Jangbaek Mountains, was it possible to keep Korea safe from the raids of the horde. With the unification of the Jurchen the question of our safety has risen up yet again though. While we were able to hold off the raids on our borders, it is much more unlikely that we can protect ourselves from the force of an united Manchuria. It might be worth considering to pledge our allegiance to Manchuria, and pay tribute to them in return for peace and prosperity in our realm.
