By subjecting us to pay tribute to the ruler of our overlord, we have also accepted that our border will remain open. A fact that our border population has come to abhor. The provinces directly adjacent to our overlord are never safe, as raiders can show up at any time to claim tribute in the form of living slaves. Men and women who are then sold in the markets of our overlord's capital. Our people yearn for the day when we can throw off this yoke, but for as long as we fear the armies of our overlord, we will have to endure these expeditions.
