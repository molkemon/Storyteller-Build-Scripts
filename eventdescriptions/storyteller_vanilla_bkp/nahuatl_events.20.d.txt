As the spring equinox and the planting season draws near, the time of Tlacaxipehualiztli is upon us. The time has come to sacrifice all prisoners of war at the temple of XipeTotec. Their blood will feed the gods and their skins will be worn by their captors and the priests of the temple before being saved in the holy cave. Fate has rewarded our ruler with many prisoners, and this time the festival will last for many days. Much blood will be spilled in honor of the gods.
