Once there was nothing, then our creator brought the world into being and brought man out of a hole in the ground. Our creator created the mountains and can make them tremble with their voice. Our creator lit the fires known to men and causes the rain to fall. Our creator is the father and the mother of humankind and will guard us in adversity. We have come across an increasing number of followers of our creator recently. They all have in common that they refuse to acknowledge any other spiritual power than their one god and while they speak freely and gladly of his existence they guard the secrets of the cult itself carefully.
