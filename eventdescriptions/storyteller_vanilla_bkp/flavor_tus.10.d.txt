The people of our capital desire nothing more than to be cleansed of their sin, and Savonarola will guide them. Every person has their blasphemous texts or false idols or needless luxuries. Anyone living humble under God should be without such evil distractions, and they must be burnt. We will gather it all in huge bonfires in public places, where everyone can see the wickedness of this world evaporate as smoke on the wind.  Burn your sin, our capital. Burn your sin.
