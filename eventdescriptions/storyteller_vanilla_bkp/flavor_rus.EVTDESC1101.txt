The Time of Troubles stretched from the death of the last Tsar of the 700-year-old Rurik dynasty, Feodor Ivanovich, in 1598, and the ascension of Mikhail Fyodorovich of the Romanov dynasty in 1613. Famine, plague, public unrest, and the appearance of false claimants plunged the country into a decade of turmoil as neighboring powers like Sweden and Poland saw their chance to interfere.
