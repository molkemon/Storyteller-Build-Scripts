It is a fateful day for our nation. Our ruler's illness is impossible to ignore anymore, precluding them from taking care of their duties. They had been exhibiting increasing signs of mental instability, insisting on taking clearly disadvantageous decisions for our country. This morning, a council of seventeen doctors have agreed on signing a document declaring them unable to manage our kingdom, without any possible prospect of improving their condition. After communicating their resolution to our heir, he has expressed his discontent with their suggestion of him taking the throne himself, arguing that he still feels a strong responsibility towards his mother.
