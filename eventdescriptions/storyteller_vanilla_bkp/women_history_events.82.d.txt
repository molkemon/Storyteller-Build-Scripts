Red-haired and with a fiery temper, the pirate Anne Bonny was captured together with her second husband Jack "Calico Jack" Rackham, captain of the pirate sloop Revenge. Bonny and Rackham fought Jonathan Barnet's troops fiercely but had to admit defeat as their crew was too drunk to fight and was ultimately captured. Rackham was hanged, but Bonny asked for mercy because of her pregnancy and was granted it. She was released not long after the birth of her child. The two of them had plagued the waters of the Caribbean for several months, seizing smaller vessels and looting them for an abundance of treasure. Bonny fought alongside the men, showing her competence and effectiveness in combat which gained her respect from her comrades. This is a woman with skills we can utilize, either by making her an admiral or an advisor.
