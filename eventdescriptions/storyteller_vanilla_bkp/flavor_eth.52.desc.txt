In the years 1555 and 1557, the first Portuguese Jesuits arrived in Ethiopia. At first they intended to stand by the small Portuguese community, which has been growing in Ethiopia since 1543. However, their influence in the Ethiopian court grew. It went so far that the Catholic church started to become a real threat to the authority of the Coptic church and now they are trying to persuade our ruler to convert to Catholicism. How shall we deal with those foreign missionaries?
