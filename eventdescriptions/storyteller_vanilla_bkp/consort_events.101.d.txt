It is no news that the consort has no fond feelings for the monarch's heir, the heir of the dynasty. Ever since the consort arrived at court the friction between the two has been obvious and it has only gotten worse since the birth of the consort's first child. Of course the consort would much rather see their own progeny on the throne. For quite some time now the consort has silently been pulling the strings to make it happen, convincing or bribing the right people and grooming their eldest at the best of their abilities. However, it was not until yesterday the consort confronted our monarch and demanded that it was time for a new heir to be appointed.
