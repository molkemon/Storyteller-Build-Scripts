Our lord's partner has impressed many men and women at court with their sharp wit and extensive knowledge of the natural world. They have long been a collector of tomes on botany and chemistry, and they correspond with several of our generation's brightest minds. Currently one of these scientists is visiting our court as per our lord's partner's invitation. They can be found discussing all sorts of new theories far into the wee hours of the night. Our lord's partner has asked us to make the scientist an offer, claiming that they would make a much better advisor than the one we currently employ.
