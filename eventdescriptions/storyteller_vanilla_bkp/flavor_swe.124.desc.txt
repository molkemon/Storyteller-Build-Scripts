While our ruling family is legitimizing their rule over Sweden, the nobles of our country put their eyes on our neighbor in the south: the Kingdom of Poland. Next to the consolidation of Scandinavia and the route through Russia, Poland is the last path of expansion for our country. However, not everyone of the nobility agrees with that statement, highlighting the value a strong Polish ally in the south could bring. Others point out that it would be possible to get a member of our dynasty on the throne of Poland, and as such a personal union over them too. In the end, it is up to our lord's decision how the Polish relationship should look like.
