Charles François de Broglie was an Earl of France who became the mastermind behind King Louis XV's secret service. Under the King's guidance, he established the first comprehensive and organized spy network, known as Le Secret du Roy. His vast network spanned across most of Europe, and although his initial mission in the Polish succession crisis was unsuccessful, he made a significant contribution during the American War of Independence by providing crucial support to the Insurgents, including military supplies, financial aid, and diplomatic backing for Benjamin Franklin's mission in Paris.
