The tensions between our war leader and our defensive war leader have spiraled out of control, and now war has broken out. The ramifications of this are felt throughout our realm, as our lords begin to take sides, and alliances are brought into play. Whoever may win this current civil war, however, it is clear to all our subjects that we were too weak to prevent it. If we do not ensure that we reestablish our authority over the warring parties, it is quite likely that this great war will result in a prolonged period of strife and warfare between our lords.
