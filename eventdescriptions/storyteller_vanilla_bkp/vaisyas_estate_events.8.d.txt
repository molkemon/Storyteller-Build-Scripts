Our nation has become renowned for its trade in high value commodities such as spices, precious gemstones, and fine silks. Merchants come from far off lands to purchase goods that we produce in abundance, but which to them are exotic luxuries. The key to our success in these industries has been our patronage of various guilds who draw their membership from the Vaishya caste. Our investments have thus far proven profitable, and it stands to reason that further investment will only continue to yield greater riches. We should determine which of our luxury goods industries is most important to our nation and focus our efforts on the advancement of the guilds involved.
