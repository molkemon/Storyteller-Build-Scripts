The feudal lords have failed in their duty and it is time to remind them of their responsibilities. The Buke Shohatto, or 'The Various Points of Laws for Warrior Houses', was a collection of edicts governing the behavior of our daimyo and the rest of the samurai warrior aristocracy, serving as a code of conduct, a guide on how to behave as an honorable ruler, not just laws to be obeyed. These principles might be followed, even if we cannot enforce them directly.
