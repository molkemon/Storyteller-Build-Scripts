Turks are no uncommon sight in our country. The Qizilbash themselves are Turkoman, and as such are related to their cousins in Anatolia. Many Turks of our newly conquered provinces have joined the different tribes of the Qizilbash and have been integrated into our realm. Although their lands will no longer cause problems to our monarch, the sheer increase of influence the Qizilbash is reason for concern.
