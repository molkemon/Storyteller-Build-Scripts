Kempe Gowda, one of the local rulers of the Vijayanagara Empire, is building a Nandi temple in the traditional Vijayanagara style. A large granite Nandi statue will be placed on a base in the temple's inner sanctum and it will only consist of the inner sanctum fronted by a porch. It will also house one of the largest Nandi statues in the world. The height of the statue is approximately 4.6 meters and it is approximately 6.1 meters long. The sacred bull known as Nandi, the close companion and attendant of Shiva, will be worshiped at the temple. The humble Kempe Gowda has asked for a small donation to help fund the Nandi temple.
