A strange man has been spotted in the countryside at various locations around our realm. He is teaching a most unorthodox version of the creation of the world to whoever will listen to his jovial ramblings. According to some, he represents a dangerous and vile reinterpretation of holy scriptures, but few of our subjects seem to have been taken in by the heresy. Rather, many seem fond of this would-be prophet and his ideas. Intrigued by the reputation of our ruler as a free-thinking lord, the prophet is now demanding an audience with our ruler, offering to act as an advisor in spiritual matters.
