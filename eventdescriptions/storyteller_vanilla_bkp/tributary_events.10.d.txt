A tributary mission is more than a journey to pay respect to our ruler, it is also an opportunity for trade with the government of our country. Seeking to capitalize on this, it seems competing noble houses in that country have taken to the habit of sending their own tributary missions to us, claiming to represent their lord in order to gain advantages for themselves in the internal politics of that country. This time, however, two such missions arrived at the same time in that province, which made their conflicts from home erupt into bloodshed.
