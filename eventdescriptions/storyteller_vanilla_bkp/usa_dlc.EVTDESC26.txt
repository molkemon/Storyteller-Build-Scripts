The people of this land have long traded and sold animal hides, and the newcomers from across the sea joined in from the start. The English set up a trading post early on, and it has become a major source of wealth in this land. However, the trade in animal hides in our country has faced challenges in recent years, and although the people across the sea are eager for these hides, the supply from our land is steadily decreasing. If we were able to take advantage of this trade and break the English grip on it, our nation would prosper.
