Today, fifty days after Easter, we observe Pentecost. On this sacred day, many centuries ago, our Church was born. The Holy Spirit descended upon the Apostles and the other followers of Christ as tongues of fire. Thus they spoke the word of the Lord in all the world's languages and converted thousands of souls to the true faith. Hereby began a new era, the messianic age of the Kingdom of God. In memory of this great event we hold one of the Church's largest celebrations. Together we read from the Bible, sing joyous hymns and kneel in prayer.
