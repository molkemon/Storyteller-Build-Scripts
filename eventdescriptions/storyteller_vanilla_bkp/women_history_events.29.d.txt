The mother of the Ottoman ruler, Ayse Hafsa Sultan, held a powerful position as the first Queen Mother. Her marriage to the future Sultan Selim secured a strong ally for him in her father, Menli I Giray, which aided him in his quest for the throne. Every year, the Mesir Festival honors the healing remedy made from herbs and spices given to Ayse Hafsa Sultan when she fell ill. As her son's de facto co-ruler, she demonstrated a significant shift in the role of the Sultan's mother and her growing influence in power.
