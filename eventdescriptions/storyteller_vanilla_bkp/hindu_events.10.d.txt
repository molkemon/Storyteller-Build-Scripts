After many years searching for a wise guide to help you understand the mysteries of existence and bring clarity to your spiritual questions, you have found no one who can adequately explain these things. At this point, you are beginning to lose hope in great teachers and prefer to discuss the spiritual with your friends.  One day, you speak with your lord, the ruler of your realm, discussing the nature of the soul and its unity with the divine. Although your lord is a follower of Vishnu, you find that your words about Shiva resonate.  Later, you think about the conversation and realize that what you said did not sound like the words of a pupil, but a master. Perhaps Shiva has already shown you his favor by giving you this revelation.
