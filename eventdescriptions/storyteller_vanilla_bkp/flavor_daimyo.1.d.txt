With the Tosa province once again under our direct rule, we've been approached by some local Daimyo who want to take over the fiefs in the area on our behalf. The Chosokabe claim descent from the first emperor of China and have made themselves known as militarily strong retainers of their previous overlords. As retainers of our Shogunate, they promise to be a loyal force for our interests in Shikoku.
