The thread of fate is fickle, and balances on the razor's edge between the sublime and the eternal. Fickle, are the vagaries of destiny. The stars are aligned in a favorable position, and the moon is in a significant place. Who he is, I do not know, but the stars foretell that a man born to a noble family on this day, shall one day be the ruler of a mighty realm. Thus the astrologer spoke. Today, the monarch gave birth to a child.
