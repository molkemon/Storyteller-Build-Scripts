Our nation has been without a suitable heir for some time, which has caused concern among members of our royal family. A princess from our lineage has recently given birth to a child who would be next in line to the throne, were it not for the fact that she is unmarried.  Being well-versed in the legends of the Mahabharata, however, she recounts the tale of how the great king Karna was born of a virgin by Surya himself. The princess explains that she too learned the mantra by which the princess of legend became pregnant and that her child, the child of the sun, is destined for great things.
