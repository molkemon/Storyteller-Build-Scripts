In our glorious lord's realm, our ruler rules the state with absolute authority, but the state survives only through the support of the common people. The state must ensure that the people admire the figure of our ruler with a fervent zeal bordering on worship, and thus we must produce ample propaganda to promote the image of the ruler. We must carefully consider how our ruler is portrayed, emphasizing only those aspects of their rule that the people can see in action.
