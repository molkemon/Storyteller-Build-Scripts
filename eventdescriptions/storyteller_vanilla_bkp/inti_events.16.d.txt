A great number of diviners are attached to any given Oracle's shrine. The Guacarimachic speak to spirits while the Ayatapuc communicate with the dead. The Huatuc achieves a prophetic mood using a holy beverage while the Hamurpa read the entrails of sacrificed animals. All of these great diviners have now dwelt on the questions our ruler has come to pose but the answer is not as favorable as he would have hoped. The signs are conflicted and as far as can be discerned the future holds both risks and opportunities for us.
