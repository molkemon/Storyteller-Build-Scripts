In order to grow as human beings and approach spiritual liberation, we must earn merit. According to the teachings of the Buddha, we do so through good deeds, acts and thoughts, and the accumulation of merit will carry over to our coming incarnations. The utmost sign of a good heart is the willing transfer of merit to others. This proves that we do not only perform good deeds, acts and thoughts for our own benefit, but for that of everyone else as well. Our great ruler, the monarch, has arranged a great ceremony at the royal family shrine. At the end, they will pass on the merit which they gathered during the ritual. Whom shall they dedicate it to?
