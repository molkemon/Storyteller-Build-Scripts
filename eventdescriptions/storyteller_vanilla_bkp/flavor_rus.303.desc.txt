The peasantry is the backbone of the country and has to live in an oppressive feudal relationship with their landowner in order to maintain the food supply of the country. However, this serfdom robs the peasantry of any autonomy. The empathetic monarch has shown mercy upon the peasants and loosened the bond the peasant has to their overlord. Although highly appreciated by the lower class, the nobility claims that the monarch has become soft and weak.
