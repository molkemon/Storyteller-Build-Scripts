The strategic location of Belgrade makes it a crucial defensive position and a key target for any invasion from the south. Previous kings wisely acquired the Banate of Belgrade from Despot Durad Brankovic. However, the fortress itself, though grand, is in disrepair and requires urgent upgrade. Unfortunately, the Hungarian nobility is unwilling to contribute to the maintenance of Belgrade or our other border forts. They fail to see the looming threat and claim that supporting our defenses would weaken them and invite the crown to overstep their rights. If we want our defensive line properly maintained, our ruler will need to cover the costs directly from the state treasury.
