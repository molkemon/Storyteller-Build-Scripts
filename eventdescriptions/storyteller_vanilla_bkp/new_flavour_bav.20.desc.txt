Our ruler's consort urges our ruler to pledge to fund a magnificent church for the Theatine Order in our capital if an heir to the throne is born. She has already chosen a Bolognese architect, Agostino Barelli, to build the church in the Italian high baroque style. Our aristocracy and wealthy citizens are quite taken by the plans, which she has allowed to become widely known, as they are strong admirers of the fashionable Italian style. Should we do as she urges?
