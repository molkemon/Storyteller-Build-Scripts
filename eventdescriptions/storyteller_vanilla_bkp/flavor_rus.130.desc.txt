The elite units of our army are the streltsy. They are recruited from the rural population and the available tradesmen, and their service is lifelong. In times of peace, the streltsy are expected to return to their jobs. As such, any kind of campaign is undesired as it results in a loss of income. To counteract this, the idea of a secured salary has been suggested, which would keep the streltsy loyal even during our most demanding wars.
