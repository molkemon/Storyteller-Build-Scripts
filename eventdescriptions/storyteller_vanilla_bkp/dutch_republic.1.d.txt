We take pride in our system of government, in part precisely because it is unprecedented and stands in contrast to the oppression suffered in other countries. However, an increased number of nobles are pointing to the international trend of absolutism, where one ruler exercises unrestricted power, gaining popularity among neighboring countries.  Our ruler, they argue, has all the makings of a great ruler. Granting more power to our ruler could strengthen the nation and show other rulers we approve of their ideas.
