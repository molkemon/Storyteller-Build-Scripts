Romanians, or Vlachs, have been part of our lands in Transylvania for centuries but have had little representation in the Transylvanian Estates. The region has always been very mixed, with Szekelys, Transylvanian Saxons and the Vlachs coexisting in a delicate balance of power. Recently, however, the Vlachs have begun to demand rights and representation. Some have even taken up arms against the harsh authorities.
