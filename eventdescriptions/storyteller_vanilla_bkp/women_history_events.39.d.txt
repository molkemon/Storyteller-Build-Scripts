Fionnuala Nic Dhomhnaill, also known as Iníon Dubh, the Dark Daughter, is the second wife of Aodh mac Magnusa Ó Domhnaill, King of Tyrconnell, and the mother of Aodh Rua, or Red Hugh, and Rudhraighe Ó Domhnaill. She was raised in Scotland, where she formed many powerful connections, ensuring that O'Donnell's armies will never lack Scottish mercenaries. The king has grown old and frail, and Iníon Dubh now leads the territory in his place, while their eldest son, Red Hugh O'Donnell, sits imprisoned in Dublin Castle. His claim to the throne is in jeopardy, and Iníon Dubh was forced to have her nephew, Hugh Gavelach O'Neill, assassinated when he attempted to seize power. While defending her son's claim, she rules the land in his absence and works to secure his release.
