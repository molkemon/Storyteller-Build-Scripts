Though Holstein has long been ruled under the suzerainty of Scandinavian kings, it has now been incorporated directly into the government of our realm. In an age defined by the growing authority of centralized states this is not especially remarkable, but in this case it has caused a serious diplomatic incident. While the Holy Roman Empire has been content for us to rule over the Duchy of Holstein, it remains legally and inseparably part of the Empire and most emphatically not a foreign province.  The Emperor has therefore demanded the restoration of the Duchy of Holstein, and further demands that all foreign authority over its territory must be relinquished so that Holstein can take its place among the Princes of the Empire.
