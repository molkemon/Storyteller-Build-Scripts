The Oyo Mesi have always been a crucial part of our government. They are the ones that give the approval for the enthronement of a new ruler, and have the duty to depose it if they deem him a tyrant. Unfortunately, their decision regarding our monarch has been made and the monarch has received the traditional calabash as a message that the monarch is to abdicate by way of ritual suicide. They are too strong and influential to be opposed, so the monarch can't do but obey their command.
