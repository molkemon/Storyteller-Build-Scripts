For centuries the North has been split in several kingdoms, which all tried to dominate the region on their own. Several attempts to unite the region once and for all have failed in the past due to biased rulerships, lacking organization or simple stubbornness. But this all has finally found its end as we are now the undisputed great power of the North, ruling over the three capitals of our realm. It is time to establish a proper empire, which shall bring prosperity to our domain.
