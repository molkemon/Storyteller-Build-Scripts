With the growing influence of the Janissary Corps in our society, we have also seen an increased presence of the Bektashi order of Sufis. The highly unorthodox, quasi-Shiite Bektashi order has maintained a close relationship with the Janissaries since the 15th century, contributing to their distinct culture. So long as the Janissaries remain influential, we will have to provide a degree of tolerance even for the most heterodox of religious practitioners.
