The assembled retinue ready their pikes and their stomachs for the coming judgment. The soldiers raise the beaten and broken body of their ruler from their slumped position on the ground and prop them upright. The once majestic ruler lets out a final chilling scream as the stake impales their heart and emerges out of their back. Their heir grimaces as they are forced to watch the horrific display; their eyes fill with terror as the realization dawns that they are next.
