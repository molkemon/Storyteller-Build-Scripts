It may surprise you to learn that for many years, Ottoman sultans did not take legal wives – an effort to suppress competition in the harem. But the great Suleiman took Hurrem Sultan; Roxelana; as his legal wife and elevated her above all women in the realm. The ruler takes her counsel on diplomatic and military matters, and even allowed her to act as an official correspondent to Christian monarchs on our borders. She convinced Suleiman to limit Crimean slave raiding into her native land, and supported the construction of schools, mosques, and hospitals.
