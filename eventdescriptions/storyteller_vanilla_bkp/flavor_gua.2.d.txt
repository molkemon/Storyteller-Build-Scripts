A messenger from our ally tells us that their ruler is prepared to make a deal with the Bandeirantes who capture and force the people of our ally into slavery. Their methods are questionable, the Bandeirantes have been known to disguise themselves as Jesuits, singing mass to lure the natives out or using surprise attacks to capture them. It is a very profitable operation though, and our ally's ruler suggests a way to make it easier to profit from this.
