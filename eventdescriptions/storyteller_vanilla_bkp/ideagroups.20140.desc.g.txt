Ever since the enigmatic man appeared at the esteemed court, he has been a mystery to our own elite class. Nobody knows his name nor has been able to find information about this secretive man. That is until today. As days pass, the nobleman has proven himself as very talented in the arts of state maintenance. Apparently, he was previously one of the high advisors to the neighboring throne, but due to conflicts with his former ruler, he was forced to flee.
