Sufism is a very spiritual and mystical part of Islam. Disconnected from the worldly desires and worries, it strives to find closeness to Allah. Although seemingly unintuitive, the ruler's trusted advisor created a concept which combines the philosophies and traditions of Sufism with the legislation of the realm, giving legitimacy to the laws by connecting them to the divine. Of course, this codification still requires a signature from the ruler in order to be active.
