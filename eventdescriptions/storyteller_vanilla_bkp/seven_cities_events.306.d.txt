The ruler of our domain and the leader of the rival tribe were enemies, something our leader didn't realize until too late. They both demanded tribute from our expedition to allow us passage. By offering one native leader gifts first, our leader insulted and angered the other leader. The gifts offered were not to their satisfaction and some warriors tried to take our supplies. Our leader took a firm stand, and he and his second in command brandished their swords and threatened violence. The leaders backed down and better gifts were presented to them both.
