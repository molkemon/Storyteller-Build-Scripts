The nobility in that distant land resent being ruled from afar by Denmark. They yearn for the days when they might, once again, be ruled by one of their own. Over the past months, there have been signs that a plot was underway, but our authorities were unable to confirm these rumors. However, there can be no further doubt: A group of rebellious nobles have rallied behind Maximilian, a prince from a lesser-known branch of the von Wittelsbach family, and taken up arms against us, declaring him their Duke.
