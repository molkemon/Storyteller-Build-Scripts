In the midst of a large crowd of people stands a single man on an improvised pedestal. He speaks to the crowd in a soft yet firm and passionate voice, claiming to have seen visions of the future; and visions from their divine being. "The ways of our faith are wrong. Misinterpreted in the many centuries since our conversion. The true name; the true visage of him is Ahura Mazda." Obviously, this man is spreading heretic thoughts which cause great wrath amongst their church leaders. They want his immediate execution.
