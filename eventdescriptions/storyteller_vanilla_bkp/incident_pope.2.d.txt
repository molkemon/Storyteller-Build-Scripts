Italy, no longer the 'Shadow Kingdom', has been returned into the fold of the Holy Roman Empire through the relentless efforts of the Emperor. The Pope of Rome has chosen to accept the hegemony of the Empire rather than to be left sitting naked and vulnerable on its doorstep. Allowing the Pope to become an Imperial Prince would have dire ramifications for the Catholic world. While Catholics within the Empire might have their faith emboldened through the legitimacy conferred by Papal membership, those outside the Empire are unlikely to take kindly to such a decision and may turn to heresy.
