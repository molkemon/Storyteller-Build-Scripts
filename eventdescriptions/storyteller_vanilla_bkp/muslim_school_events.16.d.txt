The Madrasah of the capital city has been gaining repute, not only as a center of learning, but also for its scholars from other schools. They have in particular made a name for themselves at the Madrasah and as a result both the institution itself, the adjoining market district and the bustling city as a whole is growing rapidly. Urban growth is never without its problems, however, and a growing Madrasah still needs to be provided for. If the ruler would set aside a larger charitable donation for the upkeep and maintenance of a greater Madrasah it would benefit both the city and the area at large.
