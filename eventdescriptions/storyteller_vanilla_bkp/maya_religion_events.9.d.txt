A dream about the Hero twins, who were conceived when their mother spoke to a severed head who spat upon her hand. That severed head was their father, who together with his brother was summoned to the underworld after their ball game had disturbed the peace of the rulers of the underworld. The twins had many adventures before they themselves were summoned to the underworld as their ball game disturbed the rulers once again. The dream was about the many trials the twins faced before they reached the rulers of the underworld, and how they managed to defeat them. The dream ended when the twins transformed into the sun and the moon, which is a sign that a new age is beginning.
