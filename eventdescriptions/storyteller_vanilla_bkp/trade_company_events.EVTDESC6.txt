A conspiracy has been discovered in our country. Some disgruntled foreign employees of our trade company, together with traders from the enemy country, led by none other than a governor of their trade company, planned to storm and seize the local fort and murder the local governor in an attempt to undercut the trade company's business. All conspirators were convicted, sentenced to death and beheaded by the order of the governor. This incident has naturally stirred up quite the reaction though.
