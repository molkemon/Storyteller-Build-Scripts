The relations between our ruler and the Janissaries have reached a new low. Failing to grant more rights and privileges, our state disappointed the expectations of the Janissaries for years. The neglect of Janissary soldiers has created an atmosphere of resentment and distrust that now threatens to erupt into violent conflict. As such, it was no surprise when the leaders of the Janissaries made an ultimatum to our monarch: we either give in and give concessions to the Janissaries, or face open rebellion.
