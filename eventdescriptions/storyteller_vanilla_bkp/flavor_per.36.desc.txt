The aristocracy of our country is like any other: made of members of the society who own land and are dedicated to the war matters of the state. And similar to all the noble families in history, so does our aristocracy try to expand their influence over the state at the cost of the rulership of our lord, undermining their authority.  In a moment of wisdom and foresight, our lord has established the 'Third Force' in the form of the ghulams and has split the noble aristocracy by playing them against each other. Neither the nobility nor the qizilbash have the support of the other military leaders to enforce their will on our lord.
