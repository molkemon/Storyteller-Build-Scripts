It seems the local officials of the Maritime Trade Office in that province have taken it upon themselves to stimulate local commerce. A series of regulations issued have, while quite contrary to official policy, managed to provoke a local trade boom, with many local families getting into lucrative business while also attracting foreign merchants from overseas. The Grand Secretary is, however, imploring us to shut them down and retract the local stipulations as they are an affront to good governance and central power.
