Just as the ruler has resolved to remove power from the Sejm, so has the Sejm made its own demands to the ruler. Mikolaj Zebrzydowski, Jan Szczesny Herburt, Stanislaw Stadnicki, Aleksander Józef Lisowski and Janusz Radziwill have formed a rebellion, or semilegal uprising, in Stezyca and Lublin. The nobles have gathered at the rebellion formed a league which outlined demands in 67 articles, demanding the dethronement of the ruler for breaching the Henrykian Articles, and the right for the Sejm to appoint state officials instead of the monarch.
