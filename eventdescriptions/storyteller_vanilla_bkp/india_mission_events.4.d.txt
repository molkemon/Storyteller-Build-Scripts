The spiritual importance of the sacred river Ganges cannot be understated. Far more than just a body of water, She is the Goddess Ganga who brings plenty and purity to all who bathe in Her waters. Our glorious conquest of the Indo-Gangetic Plain complete, we have tasked the Brahmins with organizing a lavish religious festival, unprecedented in scale. Consecrated in Ganga's holy waters, the country Empire will surely be favored by the Gods for all eternity.
