In a recently cleared part of the deep Mangrove forests of the Sunderbans lies the city of Khalifatabad. Not content with having cleared the area in the 1400s the local ruler Khan Jahan Ali has spent a lifetime filling the city with Mosques and great buildings. Among the many Mosques of Bagerhat is the huge Shait Gambuj Mosque with over eighty domes, quite possibly the largest Mosque in India.
