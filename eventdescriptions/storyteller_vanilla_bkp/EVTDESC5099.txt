A dispute has broken out between several extremely wealthy and influential merchants over our trade policy. One group favors taking a mercantilist approach, calling for the government to ensure that the wealth generated by trade within our country goes mainly to our traders and producers. The other dismisses this protectionist approach as old-fashioned and is in favor of free trade, which they claim would liberate them to seek profits around the world. Naturally, both sides consider the other to be at best highly unreasonable, at worst dangerous in their misguided beliefs.
