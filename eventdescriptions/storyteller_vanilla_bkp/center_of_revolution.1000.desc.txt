A philosopher from the capital of our realm, a certain notable thinker, has published several treatises criticizing the inequalities in our land and calling for a new social contract. Disputing the divine right of our ruler to rule as they see fit, he proclaims that sovereignty lies with the people and that ultimate power over the laws and policies of our realm must lie with them. His thesis is highly popular among the middle class entrepreneurs and literati, who have taken to spreading his ideas via pamphlets that call for popular mobilization against absolute power. Already, they are winning supporters across our continent as the first sparks of Revolution are born.
