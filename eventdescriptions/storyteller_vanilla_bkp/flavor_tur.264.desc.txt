Although violent and bloody, the ruler managed to separate the Imperial Harem from all political affairs of the realm for good. The harem's task is the assurance of a new heir while the Sultan shall lead his country from the horse once again. Though this separation from family and state has a drastic consequence: future heirs are limited to learn from their father and have to rely on the advice of their mothers and confidantes. As such, it is no longer a guarantee that future Sultans act in the interests of the domain when their time comes to rule.
