The ruler of our realm sails to the port of a distant land with nothing more than a small boat. Their lord had only one goal: capturing the precious flagship of Hayrettin Barbarossa. Their crewmates called them crazy for even attempting this stunt, but their determination is absolute. An afternoon later, the ruler arrived at the port, their boat completely sunken due to hull damage. But that did not bother them in the slightest. After a short, unintentional prison visit, the ruler captured a heavily damaged ship directly in front of the authorities. Naturally, they got pursued by their greatest pride, the Hayrettin Barbarossa, named after their most famous pirate. What the authorities did not expect, however, is that they had planned this from the start. So when the authorities boarded the damaged ship, the ruler captured their now empty flagship and sailed into the horizon with it.
