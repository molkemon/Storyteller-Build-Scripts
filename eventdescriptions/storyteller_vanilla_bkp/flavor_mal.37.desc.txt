The ruler of our realm travelled to the great city of 385. Just like how the great Mansa Musa once did, so did the current ruler bring the riches of our realm to the city; although this time, with a lot less gold; after all, the ruler did not want to cause damage to our brethren in faith. When arriving at the city, the clergy recommended to donate the gold for the construction of a great mosque, which shall compliment the great Kaaba. On the other hand, some of the more compassionate advisors suggested investing in a counting house in 385 so that the poor can help themselves to overcome their bad situation with the help of Allah.
