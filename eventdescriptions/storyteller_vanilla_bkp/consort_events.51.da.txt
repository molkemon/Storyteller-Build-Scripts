For the past couple of days we have been entertaining a guest from the royal court of their country, the once home of the lord. After a full week of hunting, feasting and gossiping with the family, the consort's sister is finally preparing to return to their capital. She assures us that she has had a wonderful time and that the royal family simply must come to visit their capital soon. With delight in her voice she says that she'll make certain to inform everybody back home about how the young heir to the realm of our country is the spitting image of her dear sister or brother of the consort.
