The ruler of our realm travelled to the capital of Sweden and invited all Swedish nobles to a great festival to celebrate his coronation. Although hesitantly, the Swedish nobility accepted the invitation and travelled to the center of the capital. Shortly after the arrival of all the nobles, however, the ruler ordered a trial against the Swedish nobles, and executed the majority of the nobles for heresy.  While this act was a gruesome one, it was one of necessity for our government. The Kalmar Union would fall apart if anti-unionist thoughts were to spread further in Sweden. The population of Sweden sees this event as a proof of our tyranny though.
