A representative of the people living in an area dominated by one of the most prominent families of our nobles has approached the throne today imploring our lord to take action against what he claims are systematic abuses of power against the people there. As the ruler of our realm, our monarch has a responsibility to protect their people, not doing so will make us appear weak and may harm the longterm growth of our domain. The nobles would likely not take kindly to chastising such a powerful member of their ranks however.
