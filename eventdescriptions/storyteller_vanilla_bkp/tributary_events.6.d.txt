An increased number of tributary missions to our overlord have led to the port of the capital of our tributary becoming the main point of entry for ships from our overlord. The province of our tributary has also started to attract greater numbers of our overlord's traders and adventurers, further allowing the region to prosper. Some undoubtedly see it as a good way to avoid the watchful eye of our overlord's customs and law enforcement, however. Encouraging these fortune seekers will likely prolong the economic boom of our tributary, but it might still not be in our best interest.
