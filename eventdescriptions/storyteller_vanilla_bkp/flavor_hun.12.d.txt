As a bishop, a cardinal, and a member of the royal council, the dynamic personality of Tamás Bakócz has served our kingdom well. After a solid education and career, Bakócz has come to dominate Hungarian foreign policy as well as domestic matters. One of the cornerstones of the foreign policy of Bakócz is that we must cultivate close ties with the Austrian rulers in Vienna, only they can help us against the Turks.
