The silhouette of our capital has never been as full of cranes and half-built towers and monuments in our lifetime. The reign of our ruler has been characterized by an ever-increasing number of building projects both here and in other parts of our realm. The increasing number of people employed in planning and erecting great buildings to the taste of our monarch has led to the development of a new and distinct architectural style. Internationally, the demand for the great architects and builders of our realm's school is increasing, and some foreign cities can now compete with us in our own style.
