The allegiance of one of our neighbors, a fellow member of the Empire, appears to have angered their neighbor. They have sent an ambassador, a man named Íñigo Vélez de Guevara who has conducted diplomacy in various parts of the Empire, to offer us a claim on the lands of those who have opposed their League.  Dire times may be ahead, but dire times are not without opportunities. This is a chance to expand our borders, or alternately to show that we mean our neighbors in the Empire no harm, regardless of League loyalties.
