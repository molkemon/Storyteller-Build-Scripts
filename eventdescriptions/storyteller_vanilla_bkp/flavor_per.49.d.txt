After the downfall of the White Sheep Confederation, many of their fighters roamed around the barren countryside looting and harassing traveling merchants and farmers. Men without purpose, whose hearts were full of faith and loyalty to their former masters, are now rudderless in a world that's changing faster than they are, leaving them behind. To these men, we must raise our banner, and grow the seed of our faith in their hearts. Let us call upon these battle-hardened men of the plains and have them lead our armies into greatness.
