The rule of our great government is the foremost responsibility and privilege of the monarch. The dynasty have in the past prided themselves on leading through example, observing strict etiquette, personal austerity and carefully weeding out those that do not conform to this ideal. It is therefore with a heavy heart that the monarch has observed the development of their heir. Far from an ideal person, our imperial heir is often described by their teachers as a petty person, focused on immediate gain and emotional pleasures. There are, however, factions at court that would be most dismayed if we abandoned our heir, a new heir would not have their support.
