The mestnichestvo relies on the genealogical information of the boyars and their entangled dynasty hierarchies. This crucial data is published in the form of the Razriady, register books. As the autocracy in our country develops and the bureaucracy shifts to a more centralized form directly under the rule of our monarch, the mestnichestvo has been deemed as unnecessary. Today, our monarch orders the burning of all Razriady and abolishes the mestnichestvo for good.
