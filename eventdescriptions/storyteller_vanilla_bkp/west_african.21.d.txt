The Golden Stool is the symbol of the Ashanti king, which no one is allowed to violate. Now, the foreigner, who we trusted had some respect for our traditions despite their disinterest in them, have sent a governor here who demands to sit upon the Stool as if he was king himself. This has outraged the Queen Mother and many others, who are arguing that this man must be stopped and the sanctity of the Golden Stool preserved, at the cost of war if necessary.
