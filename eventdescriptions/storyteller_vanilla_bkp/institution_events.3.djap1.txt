The discovery of the New World by their ruler has ushered in a new era not only for the colonizers and the colonized, but for the entire world, both old and new. The meeting of the old world and the new has led to a spread of resources and technological advancements, as well as the enlightening realization of the true vastness of our world. Animals, crop types, silver, and diseases spread across the Pacific, and their government is becoming a model of a truly globalized economy. Already, officials from their government are mapping out areas of the new world to claim in the name of the Emperor, documenting the strange locals of the new world, and debating how their spiritual and cultural practices may best serve their lord. From their capital to Ceuta, the nature of the world is now up for debate.
