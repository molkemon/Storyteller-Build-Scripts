The Puritans have for some time criticized the hierarchy and doctrines of the Church of our country. The Reformation has to their eyes merely replaced the Pope with the ruler and they openly denounce the state's slothful attitude to reform. Theological disputes aside, Puritans have also been persecuted as nonconformists, depending on the leanings of the current ruler. Finally exhausted by the religious affairs of Europe, many Puritans have decided to migrate to the New World. They have organized themselves into a colonial company and requested a charter to establish new settlements in the Americas.
