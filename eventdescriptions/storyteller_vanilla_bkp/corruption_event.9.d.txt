Our nation may not be home to the most egregious liars and traitors, but compared to some others, our people are a bit more easily swayed by bribes to abuse their power. Most of our population accepts the difficulties and occasional benefits this brings. The people in that province see things differently. They lament this state of affairs as a consequence of letting our people rule the nation, claiming that the country ruled by their own people doesn't suffer the same fate. Some of them are suggesting that they would be better off breaking away.
