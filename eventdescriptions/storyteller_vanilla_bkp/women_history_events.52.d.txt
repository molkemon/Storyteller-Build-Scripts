A battle-hardened general, Qin Liangyu, has once again joined us. Her father ensured she received the same education as her brothers, and she is well-versed in history, the classics, martial arts, archery, and riding. She is also known for her beautiful and thought-provoking poetry. After her husband's death in prison, Qin Liangyu took over as district commander of a chiefdom, leveraging her skills and experience from accompanying him in battles against local warlords. Her troops, the White Cavalry, have fought numerous battles against rebels, independence fighters, and bandits over the years. She has been promoted to Commander in Chief, presented with four poems from our ruler, and recently awarded the title Grand Protector of the Crown Prince, a distinction few have received.
