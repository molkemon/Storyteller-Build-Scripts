A man named Sher Shah Sur or Sher Khan has become the leader of the nobles from Delhi who live in our lands. With promises of wealth and power, this leader has gathered an army and is ready to attack anyone who opposes him. His long-term goal is likely to reclaim the Sultanate of Delhi, but we have received a message from his camp. Either we give up our throne and let him rule our kingdom, or he will come and take it by force.
