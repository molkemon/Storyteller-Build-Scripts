For too long Persia has stood divided when foreign invaders have entered our lands. While our ruler is an immensely popular leader, a durable state should not be built on personal loyalties alone. The road forward must be to unify our people through a common religious identity different from that of our neighbors. While far from all of our subjects would identify as Shia currently, there are many cases of Shiite customs, such as the honoring of the Imams, being practiced by the Muslims of Persia. By ensuring that our people all adhere to Twelver Shiism, we can create a Persian identity which will stand the test of time.
