As our ties with the Westerners grow, there are many within our country who view the foreigners with suspicion. Their fears are many; some believe that our diplomats are softening us in preparation for an invasion, while others see the missionaries as a threat to our faith, while merchants are concerned that our deals with the Europeans are disadvantageous to our commercial interests. Anti-European sentiment is growing rapidly in our country and the situation threatens to come to a head in the near future.
