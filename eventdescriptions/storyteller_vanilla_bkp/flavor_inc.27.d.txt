The passing of a ruler means that the time has come for them to be mummified. It's time for the icy grip of death to freeze and mold them into a new reality. As a mummy, they will continue to live their past lives in their estates, with servants carrying out many of their tasks for them. Those servants will eat for them, drink for them, and transport them. The dead ruler is also entitled to hold onto his past estates. Unless someone is to remove them from history, that is.
