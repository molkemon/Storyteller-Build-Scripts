The influence of a powerful guild has driven up prices on a certain commodity in our country. It has come to the point where traders are finding it cheaper to import goods from abroad to compete with local prices, something they claim is becoming more difficult due to the current restrictive legislation on imports.  Relaxing customs would give our merchants a chance to make beneficial agreements where that commodity is produced, but the guilds, who are vehemently opposed to this, do serve their purpose as well.
