For decades and even centuries, trade has been an essential part of our lives. We have traded within and beyond our realm, following east-west trade routes. Shell beads and fur would be given in exchange for copper and red dye, all of them luxury items that could help us prove our high status. Today, one of our allies has sent a merchant to our village. This man has offered to teach us where to find these things ourselves, a testament to the sincerity of the alliance between our peoples.
