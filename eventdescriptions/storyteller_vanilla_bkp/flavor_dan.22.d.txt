With his experience in Dutch shipbuilding and his training under Maarten Tromp and Michiel de Ruyter, Niels Juel is a formidable force. Juel continually strives to enhance and modernize the Danish Navy's tactics and organization during peacetime. In times of war, he has demonstrated exceptional skill in naval warfare, achieving impressive results against stronger opponents. Despite facing opposition from traditionalists within the navy, Juel's brilliance is becoming increasingly evident both domestically and internationally. It is time we acknowledge his remarkable talent.
