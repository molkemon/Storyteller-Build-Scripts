More foreigners have landed on our coast. Their initial aggressive behavior has led to some skirmishes between them and some members of our tribe. After a while, they moved north and built a fort to seek protection from further hostilities. Today, one of their leaders has approached our village in peace, and with apparent good intentions. He has given us many gifts brought from their distant home, stating that they need our help to survive in this foreign land, his hands almost trembling, stiffly grabbing a small piece of paper. When asked about it, his voice quivered while he offered us the opportunity to pledge allegiance to their ruler in exchange for protection under their crown, and some knowledge of their form of government.
