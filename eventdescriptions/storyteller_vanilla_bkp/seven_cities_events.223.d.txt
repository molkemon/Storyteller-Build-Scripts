Golden cups hang from the trees in the city of Quivira and anyone who is thirsty may take one and fill it with sweet water from one of the many springs, rivers, and streams that keep the area well watered. Plump, robust people must be the result of eating prunes, nuts, mulberries, and sweet grapes that are bigger than anyone has ever seen. The city's wealth is ensured by the great deposits of gold and jade found in the caves.
