The daughter of Hambirao Mohite, a renowned Maratha general, is vying for the throne. When Tarabai's husband Rajaram passed away, she declared herself regent for her young son Shivaji II and took control of the war against Aurangzeb's forces. The war was led by her, and her expertise in cavalry tactics might have secured victory hadn't the Mughal emperor Aurangzeb passed away. Her strong character and administrative acumen guided the country through difficult times, and it would be beneficial for the country to keep her in a position of authority.
