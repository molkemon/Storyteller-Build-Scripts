Your search for religious truths sometimes feels impossible, as if the divine and your own spirit were separated by the rules of existence itself. You seek advice from a person at the court, your theologian, on the matter and your advisor answers: 'What you are feeling is the first step to understanding the soul and Lord Vishnu. The absolute truth exists separate from our reality. The sacred knowledge of the gods is passed down through the ages, and if you so wish, I can teach you.' Your advisor's boldness in offering to be your ruler's spiritual master surprises you, but perhaps your advisor is right. You may need a guide.
