The coastal people of this land are the carriers of one of the oldest cultures in the Andes. As their lands have now come under our control, we cannot help but marvel at the works of their artisans and craftsmen. The people of this land, skilled in metalwork, weaving, and ceramics, are producing treasures such as we have never seen before. The arrival of these treasures as well as many of the craftsmen themselves in our capital is now celebrated in great feasts. By integrating them, we have created a link to the glorious past of this region and taken a necessary step on the road to a new state.
