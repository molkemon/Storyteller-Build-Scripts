The young man that our people decided to raise has grown up among us, strong and virtuous. His bravery in battle has been proven on several occasions, as well as his dedication to the protection of our village. His reassuring smile has earned the trust of many of our bravest warriors, and they would follow him without hesitation. It is hard to remember when he was just a frightened child, wanting to go back home. At last, we can proudly announce; this is his home now.
