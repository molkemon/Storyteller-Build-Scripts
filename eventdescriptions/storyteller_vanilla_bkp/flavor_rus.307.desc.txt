With the liberation of the peasants from the nobility and other owners, the peasantry enjoys great freedom of mobility within the territory of our realm. While the commoners enjoy their newfound freedom, it comes with unintended consequences for our government: early thoughts of the Enlightenment, emphasizing the freedom of the lower class opposed to the ruling class, have spread in the minds of the people. If the rights of the peasants are not to be revoked, the very stability of our realm is under threat.
