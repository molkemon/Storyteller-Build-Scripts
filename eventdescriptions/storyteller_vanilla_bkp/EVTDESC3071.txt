As our colony in our country has continued to grow and develop, we have begun to encroach on the territory belonging to a people referring to themselves as the "savages". They send jeers and insults in their native tongue, and have even started raiding our settlements. One of our own men with a sympathy for these savages has learned their language and he tells us that they are claiming our colony in our country is encroaching on their ancestral land. Apparently they demand that we grant them concessions, or war will be inevitable.
