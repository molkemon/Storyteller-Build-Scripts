The city of Danzig, now firmly in our control, used to be a major trading center under the once powerful Hanseatic League, based in Lübeck. Since then, the League's influence has waned due to various factors and its power in the Baltic region has been challenged. Despite these setbacks, recent developments along the German coast and in the Baltic have led to the League's resurgence. Once again, the influential merchants of the Hansa are visiting Danzig, with a promising proposal.
