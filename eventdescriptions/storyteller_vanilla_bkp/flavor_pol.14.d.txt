Stanislaus Hosius, cardinal and bishop of Warmia, is concerned about the spiritual well-being of our realm. To counter the growing influence of Protestantism among the Polish burghers and nobles, Hosius has sought the aid of the Jesuits. Together, they have devised a plan to establish an elite school within his diocese. Those trained here will learn the skills necessary to refute the arguments of the reformers and to re-Catholicize regions that have fallen to the Protestants.
