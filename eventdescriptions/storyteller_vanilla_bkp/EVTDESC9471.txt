Borders are organic things, changing and mutating with the seasons, and with the fortunes of the latest conqueror passing by. Historians will, perhaps, say that the events that transpired in our province today, were inevitable. 'As our province continued to flourish under the benevolent rule of our country, the people on the other side of the border were bound to get jealous.' Perhaps they will be less fair to us. Either way, tensions in our province have given our rivals in their country a reason to go to war against us.
