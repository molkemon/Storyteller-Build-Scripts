In the morning, the great fleet of our nation captured the coastal town Fiumicino. Several grand ships docked at the port and dozens of our greedy buccaneers charged into the city of Rome itself. Though the soldiers of the ruler of [118.Owner.GetName] put an admirable defense, they had made one drastic mistake: they stood in the way between a pirate and his beloved treasure.
