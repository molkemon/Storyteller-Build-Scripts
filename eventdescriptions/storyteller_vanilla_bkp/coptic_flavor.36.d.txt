Sometimes we are reminded of how far this world extends beyond our own realms. We have been visited by a strange group of people from lands far south from here. While most of them only speak their own peculiar-sounding language, a few of them have conversed with our translators in Arabic. They claim to come from the great government of their ruler and that they seek friends among their fellow Christians. We have very little knowledge of the Christians in the south, but these men seem to be genuine believers. They speak of Christ with fervor in their voice and seem far more well-versed in the scripture than our average noble. Apparently their country is surrounded by religious enemies and they ask us to aid them in defending the true faith. While this conflict is too distant and unknown for us to get engaged directly, we could share some of our knowledge and resources with them.
