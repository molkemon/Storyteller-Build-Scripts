The ruler nervously stepped up to the altar. The blade quivered in their hand while the crowd held their breath. Closing their eyes, they then pressed the dagger into their skin. The cut they placed must've gone deep because the blood that flowed onto the stone floor quickly painted it red as it dripped down the steps. The liquid glistened in the embers of twilight and the ruler just had time to hear the priests replace their chant with gasps before they passed out. When they awoke again, they were not quite the same.
