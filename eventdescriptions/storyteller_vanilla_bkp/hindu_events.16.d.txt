Your understanding of Shakti's creative and graceful aspect has aided our nation's efforts in intimidating and enticing other nations to join our causes. One of many methods by which nations are bound is through the union of marriage, which you sometimes find to be the most elegant solution, having established several such bonds with other countries. Diplomatic negotiations have taken on the character of extended family gatherings, all united by the blood of our royal family. As of late, the profound impact of these unions has inspired you to consider how the goddess aids your work. Perhaps deeper tantric meditation can bring you closer to understanding her.
