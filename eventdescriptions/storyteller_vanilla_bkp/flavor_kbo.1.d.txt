Ever since the 14th century, there have been two branches of the Sayfawa dynasty fighting for the throne of our realm. As neither branch has been powerful enough to defeat the other entirely, the Idrisid and Dawudid lines have come to take short turns on the throne. Some rulers have not even lasted a year. This conflict has gone on for far too long and by now our advisors and governors are all playing kingmakers while the kingdom burns. With the death of our ruler, the usual disputed succession has taken place with a pretender taking up arms against the royal heir.
