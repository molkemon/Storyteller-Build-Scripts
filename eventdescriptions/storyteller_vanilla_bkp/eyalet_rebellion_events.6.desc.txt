Ever since the fall of the Mamluk Sultanate and the establishment of the new state, the situation in Egypt has been a precarious one for our ruler. Many governmental systems of the old Mamluk Sultanate have been taken over and used further, which in turn led to an internal competition between the ruler of the new state and our ruler for many years. Now these old tensions are getting heated anew as the ruler demands more autonomy for themselves and their domain.
