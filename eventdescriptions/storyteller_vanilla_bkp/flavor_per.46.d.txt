Conquest always brings new people into our fold. For the Armenians, honorable fighters of the Caucasus, fate devised their downfall at the hands of our ruler. As the last of their separatist elements are quelled by various means, the question of what shall be done with their population looms above the heads of Shah our ruler and his advisors. Many among the Qizilbash suggest we have no need for unruly heathens in our administration. However, there is merit and prospective cultivation in allowing them to flourish harmoniously under our banner.
