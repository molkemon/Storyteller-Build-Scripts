With all the reforms taking place in our great government these days, there is bound to be some friction between the different ruling classes. The people of Königsberg are currently particularly dissatisfied, strongly opposing the idea of only being allowed to convene their council at the leisure of our ruler. Led by an articulate and well-supported lord, the people are now threatening to seek the aid of the ruler of a neighboring country in that realm if we refuse to agree to their terms.
