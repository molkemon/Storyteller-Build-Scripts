Of all those who attempt to capture Shiva's glory in art, the renowned artist has always succeeded beyond others. This is why your trusted advisor was called to your court, but despite their helpful advice on many topics, their knowledge of the divine remains a mystery to you.  One night, walking through the palace halls of your capital, you see the renowned artist putting the finishing touches on a statue and finally ask your trusted advisor what visions allowed them to portray the many faces of Shiva. 'Each and every soul is part of the spirit of Lord Shiva,' the renowned artist answers, 'and just as Shiva created all there is, the soul is blessed with the power to create. Know this within yourself, and you will know the divine.'
