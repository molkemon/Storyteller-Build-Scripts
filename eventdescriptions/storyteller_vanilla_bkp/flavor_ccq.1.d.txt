We have recently discovered that the ruler of Inca is not who he claims to be. His real name is Pedro Bohorquez, an adventurer from Andalusia. The Paziocas have accepted him as their military leader, and losing him might lower the morale of our soldiers. Although Bohorquez has lied to us, he has the support of the Jesuits and his army has maintained control for a long time. What should we do about this situation?
