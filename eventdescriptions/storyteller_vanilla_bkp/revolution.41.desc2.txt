The Revolution has arrived. Following weeks of protests, crowds gathered in unprecedented numbers in the capital to demand greater freedoms for the people. Although the ruler ordered their troops to fire on the masses, they were unwilling to do so as the demonstrators refused to provoke them through violence or insults. Facing the ever more desperate orders the ruler continued to issue, demanding ever more extreme punishment of all dissenters, the army decided instead to relieve the ruler of their position. They were summarily ushered off into exile as the Great Gurkani Republic was declared. The Republic's new rulers promise to reorganize the state by redistributing land to the peasantry and establishing full equality under the law, abolishing the worst injustices of the past regime, and leading all the people of the realm to a brighter and freer future.
