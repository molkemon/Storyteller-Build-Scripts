The Kalmar Union was supposed to represent the united will of Denmark, Norway, and Sweden, and be the protector of their common interests. In reality, however, it only served as an expanded source of wealth and manpower for the Danes, allowing the Danish kings the audacity to wage wars with the Hansa and cause troubles with the realms of Eastern Europe. The Danes made many enemies, and the majority of them would be more than happy to support our kingdom in our cause for independence. However, it would be more glorious if our kingdom manages to win the inevitable independence war on its own, and that would bring us the respect of the other monarchies of Europe.
