The Revolution has arrived. The mob has stormed the palace in our capital, capturing our ruler, who was promptly executed, along with other members of the royal family who were unable to escape. A Republic has been declared under the new Supreme Central and Governing Junta, which is composed of two representatives from each of the divisions of the realm and headed by renowned philosopher and lawyer Gaspar Melchor de Jovellanos. It promises to abolish serfdom, establish equality under the law and redistribute the nobility's land between the state and the peasantry, leading our country and the rest of Europe to a brighter future free from oppression.
