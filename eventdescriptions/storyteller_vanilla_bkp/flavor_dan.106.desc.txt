The coronation of their ruler as the ruler of our realm was not devoid of problems. Our nobilities have been split between those who enjoy the privileges granted by our overlord and those who seek our independence. As the tensions rose between the two factions, the ruler intervened and held a banquet for our nobility. However, this was revealed to be a trap by those who want independence as many of the nobles wanting independence have been imprisoned and shortly thereafter executed for heresy. Our people are in shock of the pure atrocities their lord was capable of. The our nobility is completely decimated and the populace comply with the Butchers of their overlord for the time being. But the Capital Bloodbath will be remembered forever in our realm.
