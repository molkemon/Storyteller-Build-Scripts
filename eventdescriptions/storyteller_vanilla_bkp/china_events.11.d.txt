Pupils and Examinees have outperformed themselves and most everyone else at this year's Imperial Examination. The successes at the examinations promise good things for the secretariat, and for the country at large. In fact, it has been suggested that given the excellent results, the official quota for how many can pass is on the low side. The Grand Secretary has approached the ruler asking for a second round of outtakes to allow more of this gifted generation to join the cadres.
