All gaze on in wonder at the splendor of the monarch's court, which is widely seen as the most lavish in the land; the European capital of good taste, fashion and chivalry. The monarch has patronized all the fine arts, commissioning tapestries, playing host to the Burgundian School of composers, collecting a considerable number of manuscripts, and hosting many talented painters. Their court is also host to the Order of the Golden Fleece, and many a jousting tournament has been held in their lands.
