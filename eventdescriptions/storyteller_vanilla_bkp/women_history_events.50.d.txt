Narita Ujinaga was a retainer of the Hojo clan and father to Kaihime, a local heroine known for her bravery and beauty. The two of them were put under Gamo Ujisato's care after they had surrendered to Ishida Mitsunari, whose ally Sanada Nobushige had lost a retainer to Kaihime's sword. Her father had to leave for urgent business and a rebellion caused by Hamada Shugen and his younger brother arose. Kaihime's mother-in-law was killed, which made Kaihime seek to end the rebels. Brandishing a sword, the instigator and two followers were slain by Kaihime and the rebellion was suppressed.
