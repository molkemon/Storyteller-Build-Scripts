Before she married Wladyslaw IV, Marie Louise Gonzaga had a literary salon in Paris and spent many years in a convent. Before the wedding she had to change her name to Ludwika Maria as the name Maria is reserved for Mary, mother of Jesus here in our country. Not two years later, Wladyslaw IV died and she married John Casimir. The nobility finds her a peculiar and scandalous woman, as she has very ambitious economic and political plans and keeps meddling with politics. Active and energetic, she despises boredom and has many different hobbies to entertain herself. For example, she founded the newspaper The Mercury, the convent of the Order of the Visitation of Holy Mary and opened the first literary salon in our country. Tito Livio Burattini thrives under her patronage and is currently building a model aircraft. Perhaps this Italian polymath will be the first inventor to fly? When the Swedes attacked, she led the troops in repulsing the enemy. She is a formidable woman with a great deal of influence and political power.
