Our nation has always lived under the shadow of China. The great Dragon has always considered itself to be the center of the world and rightful ruler of all its territory by the celestial decree itself. In the past we had been forced to accept these preposterous claims, but the Tiger has awakened, and it will obey the Dragon no more. If there's a nation that can claim to be instructed by Heaven itself to rule these lands it can be no other than our divine realm. We could take the throne of China for ourselves as it is, with all its associated bureaucracy, or let it die under the weight of a clearly superior and truly divinely authorized rule.
