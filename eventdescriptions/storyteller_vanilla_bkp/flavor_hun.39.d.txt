Balázs Magyar is a key commander of Hungary's Black Army, having served as leader of Croatia, Slavonia, Bosnia, Transylvania, and Upper Hungary. Beginning his military career under János Hunyadi, his battlefield prowess and loyalty to the king distinguished him in the Hungarian army. He played a significant role in numerous battles across Hungary, Bohemia, and the Balkans. The Black Army greatly benefits from his exceptional leadership, and now it is time we award this hero of Hungary for his years of service by offering him a well-deserved position and recognition.
