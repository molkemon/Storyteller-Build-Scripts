By assuming the mantle of Imperial China we have taken a step into an ancient tradition. Our realm has inherited a number of institutions, such as the examination system and the concept of a heavenly mandate, that are closely associated with the Chinese philosophy of Confucianism. As the new Empire of China it is up to us to decide which degree we wish to integrate this ideology with our own, but embracing Confucian philosophy would likely both help us rule the vast country we now claim and strengthen our celestial mandate in the mind of the people.
