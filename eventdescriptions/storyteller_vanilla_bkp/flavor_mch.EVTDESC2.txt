Several large Eastern Mongol tribes unite under Nurhaci and his number of armies increases from four to eight. It is now that he sets his goal to conquer China. In 1616 he takes the title 'Khan' to show that his people descend from the Tungus. He also takes the name 'Golden tribe'. In 1625 he attacked China, a development that ultimately ended in a total victory for the Manchurians.
