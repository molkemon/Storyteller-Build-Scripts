Eric Gryf from Pomerania was once the king of the Kalmar Union from 1412 until he got deposed by the nobles of the Union in 1435. Ever since then, Eric lived on the Gotland and, more or less, ruled over the island. Due to his past, Eric always had a legitimate claim on the throne of the Kalmar Union. However, his premature death ensures that none of his claims will ever bear fruit, unless the new ruler of the country decides to take Eric's claim on the throne for themselves.
