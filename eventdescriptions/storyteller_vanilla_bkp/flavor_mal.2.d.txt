A group of wandering nomads known as the Fulani have united under the leadership of Tengella and his son Koly. Migrating south, the Fulani have now formed an expansionist state around Futa Tooro with its eyes set on the entire Senegambian region. These Fulani seem to have no respect for our borders and there have been numerous incidents already of these tribesmen penetrating deep into our lands, levying taxes on our people and undermining our authority.
