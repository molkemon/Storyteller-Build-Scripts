Her Imperial Majesty, the great ruler, has a reputation as a remarkable woman and is unchallenged as a leader. As befits a ruler of her caliber and stature, the Sultana has mothered several capable children and many who show great promise as future leaders. Both the sons themselves and our government officials are acutely aware that only one can inherit and what awaits the others. Our advisors are adamant that it is now high time for the Sultana to name a favorite among the possible successors. This is unlikely to end the competition for her favor but it will hopefully stop the worst succession struggles that might follow if our ruler should meet her maker without having picked a clear successor.
