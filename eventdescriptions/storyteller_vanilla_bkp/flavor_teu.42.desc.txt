The Teutonic Order, an order of noble German knights, was established on the Baltic coast in order to Christianise the local tribes. Although this mission was accomplished decades ago, we have decided to stay in the land of the Old Prussians and make it our home; however, much to the displeasure of Poland. In the last 40 years our Order has fought four wars against Poland and its allies. As a result of the recent setbacks resulting from these wars, the rich cities of Prussia are plotting against our authority. The Prussian Confederation is a looming threat which will need to be dealt with swiftly. But not all looks grim for us. The Teutonic Order is at a crossroads in its future. If we manage to defeat the Polish threat, our Order will be in a position to choose a new path.
