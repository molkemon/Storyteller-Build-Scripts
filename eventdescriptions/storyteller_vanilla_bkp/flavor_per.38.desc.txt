The capital of so many rivals is in our hands. From the ancient Roman Empire to the Turkish Empire ruled by the House of Osman, this city has always been a great city of strategic and cultural importance to both the European and the Islamic Worlds. Now this most prestigious metropolis is in our hands. Some in our court have argued that this city could be developed into our "Second Capital" on European soil, while others are of the opinion that we should move its riches to our capital instead.
