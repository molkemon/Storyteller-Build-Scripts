The Champaner fort is situated in a strategic location, nestled between the mountains and the plains of Gujarat. It's a prime spot to control the trade route between the Doab and our coastal ports. Our ruler, the monarch, has devised a grand plan to transform Champaner into a secondary capital of our kingdom. This ambitious project will be overseen by the finest architects from the Indo-Islamic world, and no expense will be spared to make it a symbol of our dynasty.
