The Kingdom of Croatia has been united with that of Hungary since 1102 and during that time the local authority of the monarchy has been bestowed upon leaders, to lead the Croatian Army and Parliament. The recently appointed leader of Croatia, Tamás Erdödy, has proven to be an unusually talented servant of the crown and an able general. For his services his family has been made the hereditary rulers of Varazdin and Erdödy himself was congratulated by the Pope after his victorious campaigns against our enemies.
