One of our most noble and bravest warriors, Nurhaci, has proven himself a valuable asset for our growing country. His reorganization and unification of the various Jurchen tribes under our banner have given us the tools necessary to take the fight to our oppressors. Historically, Nurhaci would go on to launch an all-out assault against the entirety of the collapsing Ming Empire, setting the foundations for the next dynasty that dominated the region. His reforms regarding the Banner system as well as the creation of the Manchu script are some of his legendary achievements.
