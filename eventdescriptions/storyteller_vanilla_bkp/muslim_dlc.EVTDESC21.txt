Madrassa, a place where learning and studying is done, teaches the interpretation of sacred texts, Islamic law, the sayings and deeds of the Prophet Muhammad, and logic. Some madrassas also teach mathematics, astronomy, astrology, geography, alchemy, philosophy, and the mystical arts. The future of the land rests in the hands of the young, and to entrust them with the realm, it is best to educate them and ensure their understanding of the world remains strong.
