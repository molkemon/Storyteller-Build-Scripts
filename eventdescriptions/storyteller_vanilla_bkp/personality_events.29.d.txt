Like many states of similar size the outward unity of our country is partly a mirage. Locally any city, estate or even village may have its own rules and laws since time immemorial. Eager to reduce inconsistent legislation in our country the monarch has been withdrawing privileges and issuing new, nationwide, directives to bring order into chaos. This is, however, not always immediately successful. With a recent government directive being outright ignored in one part of our country the monarch's authority is in question.
