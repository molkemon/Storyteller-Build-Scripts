Praise the Lord on this most glorious day. For centuries to come all Copts shall celebrate on this date in memory of the great victory. Finally our revered Patriarch, the Pope of Alexandria, has been set free from his gilded cage in Cairo by the noble rulers. No longer will we have to grovel for our enemies or wait decades to have new bishops appointed. This is a most vital step on the path of restoring the Coptic Church to the greatness of old, and beyond.
