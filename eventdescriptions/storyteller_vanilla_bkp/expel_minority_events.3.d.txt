The people of a certain province have long felt out of place in our society, being both a cultural and a religious minority. Now, they are being forced out of the only place they have left; that province. Seeing no future there, their presence is increasingly threatened. Many now fear the repercussions of staying too long in such a hostile environment. Rather than the controlled migration we had hoped for, they are leaving rapidly, no one wanting to be the last ones left there.
