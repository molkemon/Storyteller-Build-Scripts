Our adoption of Islam has not been without friction. While its favored status has made the Muslim community grow in our lands many remain uneasy with their one god. Our ruler has shown great interest in the foreign religion but still practices many of the rituals of our ancestors. Perhaps it is because our ruler takes such interest in them that the Muslim community itself has come to scrutinize the life and actions of our monarch. The Muslim community leaders question whether our ruler's decisions are really made with the best interests of them and their god in mind.
