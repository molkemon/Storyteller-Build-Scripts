All recruits from the Devshirme System have to go through the military academy for their training to become members of the Janissaries. However, as new recruits from the Devshirme flow into the capital, it becomes more apparent by the year that not every student is the same. Young men of great talent and intellectual skill have proven themselves to be above the levels of a mere infantryman. In order to facilitate their potential, the government has opened the Imperial School of Military Engineering in the capital. Graduates of this school learn everything in the matters of logistics, maneuvers, and the construction of siege engines.
