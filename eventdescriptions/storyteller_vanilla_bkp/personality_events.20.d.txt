It is said that every other performer, artist, or thief in the capitals of our rivals is really an informant of our lord. With a keen personal interest in the comings and goings of just about anyone with any influence, our monarch often sees opportunities long before our chief of espionage does. Lately, the contacts of our lord have indicated that there are openings we might be able to use in countries of interest. This would require us to open the state coffers a bit, however.
