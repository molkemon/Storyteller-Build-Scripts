Crushing the old order has brought unparalleled justice and equality to the people as well as substantial assets into the state treasury. It has, however, also meant a loss of experienced administrators, bureaucrats, and clerics. Many of these nobles, the old clergy, and other privileged classes have withdrawn from public life and are leaving the country fearing for their lives. This has left us with a state that has more to govern but fewer people with the required experience to do so. With merit as the guiding principle of government, we are certain to find new able citizens to take their place, but this has still placed us in a bit of a situation for the time being.
