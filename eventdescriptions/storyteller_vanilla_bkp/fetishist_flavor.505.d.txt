A skilled artisan of fine goldwork has gained recognition in our realm. His success has enabled him to enlarge his workshop and he has already taken on several apprentices. Our nobility is particularly drawn to his expertise in crafting delicate golden figurines, representing various deities and intended for divine protection, in the forms of snakes, eagles, and jaguars. As expected, it has become the latest fashion to keep these sacred tokens close, often as a pendant around one's neck.
