Every male in our society is expected to take up arms in times of war and the success of our state has always depended on our brave warriors. In order to further encourage the warrior culture of our people, the leader is now working on a set of laws restricting everything from how our subjects dress, what they may buy in the markets and what ornaments they can use or what offices may be held in government. While these laws will protect the rights of our nobility, they are above all a way to reward valorous warriors. For the common people, their skill in battle will be what determines how they dress and what they may own. Even among the nobles, positions in government will only be open to those who fight without fear.
