We are fortunate to be the custodians of a holy city, one of the most revered in the world and the final resting place of a revered leader. In recent years, during the turmoil of Timur's conquests, the Jalayirids' decline, and the Qara Qoyunlu's rise, the city and its shrines have fallen into disrepair. It is our duty to restore this sacred place to its former glory.
