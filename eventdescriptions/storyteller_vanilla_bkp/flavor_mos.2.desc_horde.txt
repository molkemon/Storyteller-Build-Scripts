The end of an era has come. The once wild horde of our nation, which subjugated the Rus and forced them into the Tatar Yoke, has reformed into a new type of government; one which does not have to rely on barbaric methods as such raiding to enforce its political power. Despite some in the court claiming weakness of our ruler to be the reason for this change of politics, our ruler's actions will lead to a prosperous time which is more akin to what the European countries see as civilized.
