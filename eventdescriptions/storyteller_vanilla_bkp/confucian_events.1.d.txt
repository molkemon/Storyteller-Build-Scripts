Our efforts to integrate our pagan subjects have now finally started to pay off. Our subjects have begun to accept the ideals of Confucianism and our local officials have also integrated elements of the local culture in our wider Confucian corpus of ideals. As local bureaucrats enter our administration, pagan customs have to some extent begun to filter upwards. Even among prominent officials in our capital, a small altar can sometimes be found to a pagan pantheon. Paganism has become one of the many religions of our Confucian realm.
