The previous ruler of our country, also a follower of Ganga, passed on recently. After the funeral, the ashes have been left in our care, along with the ruler's wishes to have them submerged in the water of the sacred river. Although we revere Ganga as the supreme goddess, the river itself does not flow through our country. While travelling to the river with the ashes may take time, it would also be an opportunity to deepen our insights about Ganga.
