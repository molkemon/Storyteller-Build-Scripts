The lord's consort has been pacing the palace lately, their head hung low, only stopping sometimes to longingly gaze out the window. Life is quite different here, compared to their old country, a nation some call our enemy. Where does that leave them, as a traitor to at least one side of it? In spite of some effort, their accent still gives them away, constantly reminding people of their roots; will they ever truly be one of them? They shake their head. Such thoughts help no one, their place is here, in our country, by the ruler. However, the knowledge that the royal couple has yet to produce an heir is not alleviating any stress either. Having spent yet another quiet meal in their company, it is obvious even to the ruler that their spouse is a bit overwhelmed, either with boredom, with restlessness, or maybe by something more melancholic in nature.
