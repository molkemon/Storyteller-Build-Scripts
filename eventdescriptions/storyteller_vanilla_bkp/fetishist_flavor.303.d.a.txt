We have always been able to turn to the ancestors, our guides, for wisdom, but now there is only silence. The ancestral lands of our elite, including the great mausoleum, have been lost. We've heard of plundered tombs and desecrated remains. We are cut off from the ancestors and thus from the great Zanahary. Where shall we turn now? Who shall lead the way? There are many who doubt whether our ruler should lead our nation if they failed to protect what is most holy.
