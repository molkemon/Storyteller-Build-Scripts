After many meetings, discussions, and councils, our advisors have brought us different reforms to consider. Each has presented their ideas, and we must choose between two of these. The first one: exclude state officials, such as diplomats and governors, from engaging in any business. This will allow them to focus fully on their work and not be distracted, hopefully enabling them to do a better job. The second reform: distribute pasture lands and game reserves that are currently reserved for the family of the dignitaries among the poor, so they can cultivate the land. Unpaid labor should also be made illegal.
