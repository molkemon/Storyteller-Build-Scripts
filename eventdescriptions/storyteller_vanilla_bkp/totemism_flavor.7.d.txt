With the arrival of the cold weather, crops are not the only ones that are having trouble these days. The winter winds and rains are not merciful to our people, and our ruler is no exception. They have complained about a sharp pain in their throat and are completely incapable of talking, even breathing seems to be a difficult task for them. Our greatest priests have been summoned to their presence, and have repeatedly advised against them leaving the bed. However, singing is an important part of our religious ceremonies, and having our ruler indisposed will definitely take a toll on their reputation.
