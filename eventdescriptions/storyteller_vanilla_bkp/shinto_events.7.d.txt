In an increasingly hostile world, the safety of a castle is both a promise of protection and a source of employment. Samurai have for years been abandoning their countryside homes for the cities beneath the walls of the castles of their masters, along with merchants and craftsmen. Our capital, the city, is no exception and its growth has been a source of concern to our administrators. New funds will likely be required to improve the infrastructure if we want to minimize problems such as fire and disease.
