For a long time now the personal convictions of our ruler have been at odds with the religious establishment of our realm. This has caused both friction and conflict, with the clergy and leading nobles often questioning their rule. There are, however, many that now support them in their devotion to the faith, both in our capital and the countryside. Perhaps the time has come to make a great change to how our government functions to its very core? Surely the defenders of the old would not go out willingly but if there is a will there is a way.
