After surviving the attempt to harm their lord's physical integrity, the ruler has started to feel consumed by what they may call caution, whilst others may refer to it using less pleasant words. They have started to avoid social events, leaving the rest of the council alone to govern the whole nation. Their flagrant lack of responsibility has raised many suspicions among the court. However, what has greatly deteriorated the public opinion of the ruler is the strange behavior that they have been exhibiting lately, their eyes red and wide open in the middle of the night, muttering in darkness that they shall never leave the throne, asking someone to come at their side, even as a shadow, even as a dream.
