The people of that country have become utterly disloyal following many acts of disrespect and persecution by the state. The situation has become great exacerbated by an outrageous act of sacrilege. One of our governors marched into a temple, slaughtered a cow, and desecrated the icons inside. Unwilling to accept further persecution, the people have resolved to oppose the government. Their methods of resistance are unorthodox yet effective; rather than rising up in violent rebellion they boycott institutions of state, deny loans, and refuse all participation in government. The government's reliance on them has become a damning vulnerability.
