The recent war has damaged our nation's core. A woman has been observing carefully our decisions during the battle. Showing respect for our ancient traditions and taking care of our captives is something that should make us proud. This old, wise woman presented herself to our warriors as Jigonsaseh, Mother of Nations, a title that the Great Peacemaker had given to her a long time ago. Her judicious eyes were filled with delight. “Rejoice”, she said, “for you have honored our people.”
