There are few things more important in life than the crucial knowledge learned in childhood. For that same reason, our ruler has dedicated a great deal of her efforts to securing the best possible tutors for their heir's education, as they are to rule the realm in not so many years. A man came to our court this morning, his trembling hands almost could not support the weight of all the papers he was carrying. He asked for their name, and presented himself as their new guardian.
