When our ruler's partner married into the royal house of our dynasty there were those who were skeptical. However, the young consort gained the confidence of the common people, almost effortlessly, and they grew well into the role as one of the ruling class of our country. It has now been over ten years since the union took place. Even the ones who do not adore the consort have come to respect them as one of their own; the wedding anniversary of the elevated couple could have been highly anticipated. It is smiling gently at nobles and commoners alike that our monarch and their consort graciously accept gifts of varying splendor.
