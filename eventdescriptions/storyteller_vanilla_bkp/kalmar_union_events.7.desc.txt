The election of our ruler as the ruler of the Kalmar Union brings bitterness to the nobility of Denmark. A group of nobles organized to a sizeable army, which supports a pretender ruler for Denmark. All their hopes in independence, however, is funneled in the life of the leader of this rebel group. We could set an example for these Danish rebels, and eliminate the pretender army, or we could try to orchestrate an assassination of their ragtag leader.
