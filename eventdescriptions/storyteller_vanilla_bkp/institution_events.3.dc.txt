The discovery of the New World by their ruler has ushered in a new era not only for the colonizers and the colonized, but for the entire world, both old and new. The meeting of the old world and the new has led to a spread of resources and technological advancements, as well as the realization of the true vastness of our world. Animals, crop types, silver, and diseases are spreading across the Pacific, and their country is becoming a model of a truly globalized economy. Already, their bureaucrats are debating how best to incorporate New World territories into their ancient government and exactly how the cultures and religious practices of the native peoples could be incorporated into their nation. From their capital to Ceuta, the future of the world is now up for debate.
