The state of our nation has allied with a number of wealthy Jain families. They have been granted positions in the administration, control over Jain pilgrimage sites, and preferential treatment in the taxation system. Our heavy patronage has at last paid off; they have offered us the service of a number of skilled financiers to aid our administration, and donated a large sum to our treasury. The workings of our realm now run as smoothly as they ever have.
