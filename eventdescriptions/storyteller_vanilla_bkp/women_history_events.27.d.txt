Our ruler suffers from bouts of insanity, and he suffered a complete breakdown when his son was born. His wife, Margaret of Anjou, has already taken to rule the kingdom in his place. She understands her duty to protect the interests of the country fervently, a trait probably inherited from her mother. Her mother fought to establish her husband's claim to the Kingdom of Naples. Margaret's grandmother, Yolande of Aragon, governed the realm with a firm hand, put the province in order and kept out the English. She is the "champion of the Crown", by virtue of personality and family example.
