Our war against the rebellious has not gone unnoticed in our overlord's land. While the ruler of our overlord's realm will not stoop to the level of our petty squabbles, it seems some members of their court lack such scruples. A powerful interest group is interested in our success and has made promises to help us. Of course, nothing comes for free in this world. In return, they expect some favors to further their own political ambitions at our overlord's court.
