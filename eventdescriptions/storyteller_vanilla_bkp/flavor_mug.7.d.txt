The lands of Burma lay secluded from their foes by a range of mountains on all sides, leading to a frequent rise of many great states within its borders. With the Pagan Empire falling and the Ava Kingdom in decline, it would be only a matter of time before a new state would rise. Thankfully, the unification of Burma was not entirely successful, as we have been able to march in and subdue those states before any could mount opposition.
