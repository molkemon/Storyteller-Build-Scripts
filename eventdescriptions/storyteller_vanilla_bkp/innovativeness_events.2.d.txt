As a member of the cabinet, our lord has had good opportunity to expand on their personal interests. As a result, their manor outside the capital boasts one of the largest laboratories in the realm, with many furnaces, and large stores of all kinds of materials imaginable. They are also particularly proud of what they call their 'chamber of wonders', a large office filled with curiosities from floor to floor, as well as a library dedicated to explaining the objects. Such habits do not come cheap however and as our dear minister asks for a place to study the moon and the stars as well, a tailor-made castle observatory, some feel that perhaps this should be the limit of our generosity.
