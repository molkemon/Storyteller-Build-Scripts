Tensions are still running high among the nobility of the country and the realm of Thuringia following the war between the ruler of the country, Friedrich, and the ruler of Thuringia, Wilhelm. A knight, Kunz von Kauffungen, who fought on Friedrich's side but was financially ruined by the conflict, has joined forces with several disaffected knights from Thuringia and devised a plan to take the ruler's sons hostage in order to gain leverage for blackmail. They were able to seize the princes but were apprehended as they attempted to cross the border, with the princes returned safely to the capital of the country. How should we deal with the traitors who sought to take the ruler's sons hostage?
