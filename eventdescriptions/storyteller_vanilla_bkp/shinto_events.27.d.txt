The trade post in the land of the Nanban traders has started to attract not only merchants but also scholars and intellectuals. To these men, the books that the foreigners bring are far more valuable than the goods they wish to sell. The study of western science and culture, known as Rangaku, is growing into its own discipline, and according to its adherents, we have much to learn. Many clerics are skeptical, however, as are some Confucian scholars.
