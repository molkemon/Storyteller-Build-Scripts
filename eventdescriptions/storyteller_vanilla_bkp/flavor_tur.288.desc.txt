Centuries of infighting and corruption have led to the Janissaries experiencing decadence, content with increased influence and meddling in the Sultanic affairs, they have become brazen and forgot who their allegiance lies with. As military reforms were met with armed resistance, the ruler saw themselves forced to decimate the Janissaries.  Though gruesome as it was, the eradication of the Janissary conspirators allowed the government to reform the military from the ground up. The Janissary corps have been replaced with the Mansure Army, which uses Western strategies, tactics and equipment to fight for their ruler. All that is left is the official disbanding of the Janissary as an estate.
