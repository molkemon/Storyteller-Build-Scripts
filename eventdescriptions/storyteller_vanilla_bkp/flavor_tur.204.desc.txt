Urban was an iron founder and engineer from the Hungarian province of Brassó. As one of the pioneers of siege engineering and artillery construction, he made a living based on his weapons of war. In the current year, Urban tried his luck with the Byzantine Emperor, but as they could neither afford his salary nor possessed the materials necessary for constructing such cannons, Urban saw himself forced to turn to the Ottoman ruler. He promised to create a cannon big and potent enough to blast the walls of Babylon itself.
