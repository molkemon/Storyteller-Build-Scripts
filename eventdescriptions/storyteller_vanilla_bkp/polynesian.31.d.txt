An emissary from Tonga came to us a few days ago. During all this time, we have treated him with great honor, as we are great hosts. In his last night among us, our ruler has invited some dancers to perform the fire dance or Siva Afi. This dance consists of some dancers, usually warriors, that cover their war clubs with towels and set them on fire. It is a very traditional and iconic part of our culture. To our surprise, the dance has been accompanied by some kind of acting. The story talked about the time when our people got rid of our former overlords after attacking their governor during a Siva Afi dance. Everyone can see the Tongan emissary getting angry. This will be a problem.
