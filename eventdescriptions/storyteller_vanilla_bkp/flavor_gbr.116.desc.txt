Jousting has been a tradition in all Europe for centuries, allowing knights to display and hone their combat abilities. In recent years, jousting has been improved and perfected, with innovations such as the introduction of the tilt barrier. Its purpose is to prevent collisions and to keep both riders at an optimal angle when breaking the lance. Improvements in armor technology have also led to better safety for the combatants. As a result, jousting has become more of an entertainment and spectacle rather than an actual martial exercise. However, it also means that more people than ever before are able to participate in them.
