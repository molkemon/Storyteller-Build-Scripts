The fertile land of the Delta lies in our hands, prompting a resurgence in economy, culture, and scholarly pursuit. The latter has provided quite an edifying discovery; many are emerging who would see us adopt the ideals of the long-dead Fatimids, having come upon various ancient scrolls and documents in libraries, which now circulate through our nation's elite. We should issue a proclamation swiftly, on how to deal with this swiftly spreading school of thought.
