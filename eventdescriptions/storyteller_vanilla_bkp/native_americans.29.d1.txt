While their lord was going on a hunt with some of their warriors, trying to surround a group of deer and bring some food for their tribe, one of their men approaches them and says that he has seen a really big deer deep in the forest. They do not hesitate and follow the man through the woods until they find their prey. A big deer, the biggest they have ever seen, poses majestically in the middle of a clearing. They whisper a short 'thank you' to the spirits of the forest for this opportunity that they will not miss. They order their men to stay quiet and in their positions as they start to silently move towards the best position to fire their bow. The bow is ready to release the arrow destined to kill the animal. Their lord's breath is calm. Their fingers start to free the arrow.
