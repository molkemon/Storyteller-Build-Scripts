The trade company's policy of religious tolerance has thus far kept the local population content with their rule.  A group of evangelical missionaries have arrived in the province with the intention of spreading the faith to the heathens. These missionaries request permission not only to preach, but to conduct mass conversions through coercive means.  The company's ambassadors fear that such intolerance is very likely to provoke a reaction from the people of the province, and urge the company to restrict missionary activity.
