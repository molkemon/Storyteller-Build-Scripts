The tensions between our war leader and the defensive war leader have spiraled out of control, and now war has broken out. The ramifications of this are felt throughout the Japanese islands, as our fellow lords begin to take sides, and alliances are brought into play. Whoever may win this current civil war, however, it is clear that the current Shogunate was too weak to prevent it. We are not the only ones who will have noticed this, and it is quite likely that this great war will result in a prolonged period of strife and warfare between the different Japanese states.
