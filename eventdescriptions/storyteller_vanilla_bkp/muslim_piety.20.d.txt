Our nation has sorely lacked a firm guiding hand under the rule of our ruler, a timid child, ill-prepared to face the dangers of the world. Fortunately for us all, one of our ruler's uncles, and close advisors, has decided to act. The child is now safely locked away where they can do no more harm. The advisor has been proclaimed our new monarch, and has sworn to provide our nation with the strong leadership it so desperately needs.
