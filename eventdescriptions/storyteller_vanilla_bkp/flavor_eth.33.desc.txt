Over thousands of years nobody opened the real Ark of the Covenant. Because of that, the content of the Ark has been a mystery for the usual believer and even for the clergy this event is one of its kind. To reduce potential panic in the nation, the ruler decided to open this legendary artifact in the palace, far away from the prying eyes of the masses. When our gracious lord and their close ones opened the gold-covered wooden chest, they found what had been promised: two ancient stone tablets of the Ten Commandments as well as Aaron's rod and a pot of manna. It wasn't the most spectacular event, but it was one of devotion. After confirming the authenticity of these artifacts, the clergy placed them back into the chest and hid the Ark of the Covenant again. Days have passed from this event and ever since many activities have felt more easy for our glorious lord. It is as if God himself blessed our glorious dynasty and the fortune of our nation.
