With a diet consisting mostly of cured and salted meat and dried grains, the malaise and lethargy should have been the first symptoms recognized as scurvy, but it was not until gums started bleeding and teeth falling out that we realized we had to do something to save our expedition members. As they are exploring a province with an unknown flora and fauna, it would be unwise to eat anything as it could be poisonous. Maybe we should try to establish contact with the natives of that province and ask for their help?
