The Reign of Terror has seen countless Frenchmen massacred in the name of the Republic. No sooner has one threat been eliminated then the next is identified and purged. Fearing that they might be next; after all, it is 'Today you, Tomorrow me'; the National Assembly has united to bring an end to the Terror by deposing the ruler and establishing a Directory that will return stability to the realm and ensure that no one may use the chaos as an excuse to restore the monarchy.
