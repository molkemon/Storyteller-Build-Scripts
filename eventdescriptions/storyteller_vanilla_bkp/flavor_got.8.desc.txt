We managed to defeat the treacherous Kalmar Union and gained great power in Scandinavia. One of our newest acquisitions are the lands of the Duchy of Holstein, which used to be part of the Holy Roman Empire. One of our advisors reminded us that the state of our country made a deal with the Empire: in exchange for access to the Imperial Coffers, our country would officially promise to return the Duchy of Holstein to the Empire. It would be wise to keep this promise or otherwise we risk a devastating war with the ruler of the Empire.
