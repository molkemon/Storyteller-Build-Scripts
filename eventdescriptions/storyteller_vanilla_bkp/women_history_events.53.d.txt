With the recent wars and upheavals in our country, the visit of a renowned beauty can only be seen as a gift sent from the Heavens. She was an active partner in her second husband's uprising against our oppressors and defended the last remaining stronghold, a castle, for three years against General Caraffa's forces. We are truly blessed, for she might be the one we have prayed for, someone who can lead our country, our army, or give advice in these troubled times.
