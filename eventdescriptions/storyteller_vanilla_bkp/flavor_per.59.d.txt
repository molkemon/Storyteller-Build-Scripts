The birthplace of some of history's most illustrious empires, the so-called Cradle of Civilizations, is now a part of our great realm. With it, we have gained access to profitable trade routes crossing the coasts of Farsistan and the waterways beyond the Pirate Coast and the Persian Gulf. But, with great wealth comes great responsibility, and our treasurers warn against overindulging ourselves in our newfound riches. Trade, much like a relationship or the growth of a beautiful tree, needs to be nurtured, taken care of, in order to flourish and help us prosper for the rest of our lives.
