The Revolution has arrived. Following weeks of protests, crowds gathered in unprecedented numbers in the capital to demand greater freedoms for the people of India. Although the ruler ordered their troops to fire on the masses, they were unwilling to do so as the demonstrators refused to provoke them through violence or insults. Facing the ever more desperate orders the ruler continued to issue, demanding ever more extreme punishment of all dissenters, the army decided instead to relieve the ruler of their position. The ruler was summarily ushered off into exile as a new Republic was declared. The new rulers of the realm promise to reorganize the state by redistributing land to the peasantry and establishing full equality under the law, abolishing the worst injustices of the past regime, and leading all of India to a brighter and freer future.
