Our current leader is the one we believe should rule our Empire. He, however, has many brothers. These brothers envy his position as heir and also hold positions of authority within our realm. These incompetent individuals may try to rise up when our leader takes the throne and try to disrupt his rule. Therefore, it is best that we eliminate these threats while we have the chance. However, doing so may have some unforeseen consequences in the future. We shall consider this choice carefully.
