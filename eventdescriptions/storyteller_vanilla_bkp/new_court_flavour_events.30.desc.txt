Their lord has earned many friends in the noble court thanks to their dedicated service as the monarch's trusted advisor. However, it seems that the reverse is equally the case among their countrymen in the service of the rival realm: Envious of their position, they reproach them for taking up employment among their people's enemies and have begun spreading rumors casting doubt on their trustworthiness. Worried about their standing in the noble court, their lord has become a strident opponent of the ruler of the rival realm. They made common cause with several likeminded nobles to call for a harsher stance against the rival nation.
