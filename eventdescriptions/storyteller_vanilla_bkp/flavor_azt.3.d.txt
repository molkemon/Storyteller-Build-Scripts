Tlacaelel, the foremost architect of our realm and the first person to be named its prime minister, is an indispensable force within our state. Having served a number of our rulers, Tlacaelel is by now involved in the running of almost every part of our state. He is the one who organizes our military campaigns, he handles our finances and when our ruler is in the field he acts in their stead. Above all, however, Tlacaelel is a great champion for the worship of our deity and has taken a number of measures to ensure that our deity and the martial traditions that surround them are given the proper respect.
