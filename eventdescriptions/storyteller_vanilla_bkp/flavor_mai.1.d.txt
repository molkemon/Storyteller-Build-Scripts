In the year 1450, a man named Johan Gutenberg, who was a wealthy merchant in the city of Mainz, applied the knowledge he gained from his father's work as a skilled craftsman who made gold items, and created the world's first machine that could mass-produce written words. The first thing he printed was a poem from Germany, but Gutenberg had bigger plans, as he wanted to print a book of the holy scripture. This endeavor led him to financial ruin, and his investors took control of his business, producing the first printed version of the Bible in 1457. The world would never be the same again. What shall we do?
