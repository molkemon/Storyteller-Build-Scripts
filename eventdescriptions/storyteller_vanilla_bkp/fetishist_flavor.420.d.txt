On the seventh day after Igziabeher had created the world, He rested. In His honor, we keep the seventh day of the week holy. During the Sabbath, from sundown the day previous until the stars emerge, we do not work, write or cook. We even do not light any fires. Desecrating the Sabbath is a crime against God's laws. Word has gotten out that one of our most trusted advisors, our trusted advisor, often works during Sabbath. While some of the more extreme protesters demand that our advisor be stoned to death for their sinful ways, we do not want to set a dangerous precedent. After all, our advisor is not the only one at court to violate the Sabbath, even though they are the most regular and least discreet culprit. Simply firing our advisor would surely appease the majority, although their experience would make it a great loss.
