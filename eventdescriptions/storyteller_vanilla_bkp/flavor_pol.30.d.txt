The glorious city of Constantinople is in our hands. Once the great capital of Byzantium, the Turkish armies of the Ottoman Sultanate conquered the city and removed the Byzantine Empire from the face of the Earth. But today, the city is free from the clutches of the Ottomans. While the majority of our realm is celebrating our conquest of the city, the noble court is concerned about the government of Constantinople. While it would be prestigious to keep the city for ourselves, the advisors of our lord suggest to reestablish the state of Byzantium or the great Latin Empire.
