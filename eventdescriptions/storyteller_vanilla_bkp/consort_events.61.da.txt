With the marriage between our monarch and their spouse, the people of our country are experiencing an increased fondness for all things from their homeland. While some cultural exchange was to be expected, particularly considering the popularity of their title, it is with alarm that our lord reports that a religious shift has been taking place in one of our provinces. It would seem that the influence of their title trickles down even into our subjects' way of worship, as the words of the believers from their homeland have converted a surprising amount of people. When confronted about it, they shrugs, arguing that they have long since converted to our faith and the monarch should know that. 'I had nothing to do with this', they plead.
