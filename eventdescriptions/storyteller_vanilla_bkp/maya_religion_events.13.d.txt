Our nation is struggling, and our clergy have begun suggesting that we pay homage to Ixtab. Rope Woman, as she is also known, is the goddess of self-sacrifice and will accompany those who die by hanging to the afterlife. It is a noble way to pass on, and those who choose it will join others who died as warriors or as sacrificial offerings, women who died in childbirth and members of the clergy. These individuals enjoy a pleasant existence and are rewarded with fine food and drink. Wouldn't it be preferable to the turmoil your country is currently facing, to rest instead in the shade of a peaceful tree, free from all hardship?
