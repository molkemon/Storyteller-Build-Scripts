With the acquisition of Tatar lands we have become the overlord of the people to whom we were subjected for such a long time. While this is a victory for the noble people it is also an opportunity. As we have been so painfully aware for centuries the Tatars are excellent horsemen and know the steppes better than any other. If we should allow the local leaders to keep ruling their realm, but in our name, they may well be open to the idea of serving in our armies.
