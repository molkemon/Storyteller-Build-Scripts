With more and more foreign traders arriving in the Swahili coast from orthodox Islamic states, our old ways have come more and more under fire. We have watched with disgust as some of the old dynasties of the Swahili coast have been overthrown by a rising class of indigenous traders allied with the newcomers. As one of the oldest and most powerful dynasties, however, we still stand strong. In order to suitably impress foreigners and domestic troublemakers alike, it might be best if we assigned our scribes to create a chronicle over our famous lineage, so that none may doubt our origin and the old roots of our dynasty.
