Utilizing advice from their closest advisors and not without putting their own charm to the test, the consort has cleverly manipulated a good amount of members of the court into agreement: the royal heir and next in line to the throne, is a feebleminded fool, unworthy of their legacy. However, there is one simple solution to the problem. During yet another extravagant feast thrown in order to appeal to the nobility in charge, the consort and their lackeys have managed to spread the idea of keeping the young heir locked away in their quarters; some isolation would allow the child to focus on their education, it is argued. That the young heir might also fade from the public eye is unfortunate for them, but ultimately a bonus for the realm.
