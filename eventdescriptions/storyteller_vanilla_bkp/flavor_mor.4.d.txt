Fulfilling a long standing legend, the preserved body of Idris II was found in one of the mosques of Fez a few years ago. Ever since this event, the Idrisid sharifs have been the center of a growing cult in Fez and in our realm in general. This devout cult seems destined to lift the hearts of our people after the harsh period we have left behind us. There is, however, always a risk with passionate popular movements like this one; it wouldn't do for them to forget who wields the temporal power.
