There are those who feel the Imperial Examiners are not in tune with what is good and proper essay writing and that the ideal they promote is stale and old-fashioned. Among those studying for the exams, alternative collections of examination essays have begun circulating, considered a more modern form. The examiners, however, are determined to keep promoting good taste and proper form. By supporting the renewal faction, we would bring a generation of new thinkers to the fore, likely at the cost of the goodwill and loyalty of the old generation.
