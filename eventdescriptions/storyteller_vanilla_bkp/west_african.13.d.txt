Although many of our citizens are not yet followers of the local faith, we have remained hopeful and patient. Others are not so patient. Many of our citizens consider it absurd that our ruler, ruling by the grace of God, accepts the widespread paganism in our realm. This, in turn, has sparked concern among the very same pagans that they are subjects of an externally imposed belief system, with some of them calling for the overthrow of our ruler if they do not show reverence for the spiritual practices that have lived with the people here for millennia.
