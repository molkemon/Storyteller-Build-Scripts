Electing monarchs from different nations allows us, to some extent, to avoid a dynasty taking control over our country entirely, but also causes foreign nobles to sometimes doubt our loyalty. The family to which our current lord belongs has noticed that the increased support in the Sejm for their heir, who is a member of a different dynasty. Although they hopefully understand that they can't rule our realm forever, they are beginning to ask questions about how this will affect future cooperation between us.
