Ever since the man appeared at the noble court, he has been a mystery to our own elite class. Nobody knows his name nor has been able to find information about this secretive man. As time went on, the nobleman became friends with the monarch. He proves himself as skillful in the matters of the administration, diplomacy and the military, and is in general well received in the court. His piety is an inspiration for those who follow his word and his zeal for the realm is only matched by his belief in his faith. He shows great potential to be the next ruler.
