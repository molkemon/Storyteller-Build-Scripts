A small group of Conquistadors has captured our great leader, the ruler, by luring him into an open plaza and scaring away his entourage using horses, cannons, and firearms. This catastrophe is only made worse by the vile threat of the invaders to burn him alive and thus prevent his mummification after death. Hoping to avert this horrible fate, the Sapa Inca soon ordered us to gather a huge ransom, more precious metals have been assembled than these barbarians have ever dared to dream of. With our ruler in captivity, our country is as much a hostage as he is, perhaps it is time that another member of the royal family take over at least temporarily?
