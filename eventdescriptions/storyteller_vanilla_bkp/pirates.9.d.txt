The notorious Chinese pirate Lin Daoqian and his ruthless band of cutthroats have made port in Pattani, where he seeks an audience with the ruler of our realm. Lin is renowned for his brutality. It is said that in Taiwan he massacred the native people and used their blood as caulk to seal a leak in his ship. He states that his purpose in Pattani is to offer his services to our ruler in exchange for sanctuary from Chinese authorities. By his reputation and the size of his fleet however, it is likely that his true ambitions in the city extend beyond what he claims.
