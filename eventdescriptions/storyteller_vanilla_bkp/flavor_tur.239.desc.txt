Born as the son of Sultan Selim the First and Hafsa Sultan, a Muslim convert, Süleyman has shown such exceptional talent that was rarely seen in Ottoman Sultans at the time. He was a well-learned man, and was taught Islamic literature from a very young age. He also took an interest in subjects that most young men would be repulsed from, like science, history, literature, and military tactics. If he is designated as the heir to the realm, many are sure that the realm would achieve its greatest heights.
