The ruler pauses their survey of the battlefield to momentarily regard the burning city of their country with an unsettling dispassion. Their assembled retinue ready their pikes and their stomachs for the coming judgment. The soldiers raise the beaten and broken body of the ruler of the other country from their slumped position on the ground and prop them upright. The once majestic ruler lets out a final chilling scream as the stake impales their heart and emerges out of their back. The heir of the other country grimaces as they are forced to watch the horrific display; their eyes fill with terror as the realization dawns that they are next.
