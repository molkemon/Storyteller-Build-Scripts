Provinces have reported to the capital that they are now in full compliance with several sets of new imperial edicts, the only problem is that we've never issued any such edicts. An investigation into the matter reveals that forged bronze plaques with written edicts, bearing our seal, have somehow found their way to the province governors. It is a dark time when people dare challenge the Emperor openly, and many claim that this is because our dynasty no longer enjoys the celestial favor. What shall we do about these false edicts?
