There have been rumors about a kingdom in the north of the Americas ruled by white, blond men whose treasury is overflowing with gold, silver, and rubies. An explorer, his fleet frozen in the mouth of a river, spent a winter in a certain province and was told about the Kingdom of Saguenay by the natives. With signs and gestures towards his gold and silver insignia, the natives relayed that precious metals came from the northwest, from a tribe most aggressive and warmongering. They are as white of skin as the explorer himself, and clad similarly. The inhabitants of Saguenay are a numerous people, law-abiding, and wealthy.
