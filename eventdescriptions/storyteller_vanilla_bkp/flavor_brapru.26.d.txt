A state like ours has an insatiable hunger for good administrators, jurists, and scholars. The legal system in particular needs to not only be maintained but also at times reformed, expanded, or repurposed. In times such as those, it is great to have access to men of talent. In Samuel von Cocceji, we believe that we have found such a man. Active as a professor of law and a judge since his early twenties, von Cocceji has the integrity and character to stand up to anyone, even our ruler, for a strong, fair, and righteous legal apparatus.
