Since the settlement of the first islands, dogs have been by our side as companions, but also as a source of food. Pretty much every islander group has their own breed of dog: the Poi in Hawai'i, the Kuri in Aotearoa, or those in Tahiti. When a source of food is needed, dogs are not always the first to be sacrificed, but they are fed with vegetables or fish so they are ready.
