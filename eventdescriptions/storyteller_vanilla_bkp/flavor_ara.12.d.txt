The brothers and sisters of king Alfons V of Aragon, second ruler of the Trastámara dynasty, which was established in the country after the Compromise of Caspe in 1412, are now causing trouble. They are also cousins of the Castilian king Juan II and have been secretly trying to control him and take over the realm since early in his reign, with the help of some relatives and allies among the Castilian nobility. The situation has been getting worse since 1437, when a new civil war broke out between the group led by the Infantes of Aragon and the one led by Álvaro de Luna and the heir to the throne, Enrique. This conflict is finally coming to an end.
