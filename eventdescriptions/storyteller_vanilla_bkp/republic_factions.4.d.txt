In the country, the presence of guilds has bred a new sort of influence, and a new sort of influential person. Leading figures in the capital are not only growing richer than many aristocrats in the province, they also wield power, all while lacking the familial ties that have defined the rulers of the realm for a long time.  Perhaps this sort of shift is inevitable, especially considering the nature of our republic. Nevertheless, at this stage, the only thing that's certain is that the nobles in the country have an aversion to these ill-mannered new rich and would like to see them ousted from local political proceedings.
