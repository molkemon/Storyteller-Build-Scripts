For the past nine months, I have been an expecting father, and today one of my concubines did indeed give birth to a son. The joy is cut short, however, when the child is brought before me. His face is deformed into what looks like a permanent scream, and soft, black hair grows in patches over his little body. The child is misshapen, and my physician suspects that the deformity has affected his mental capacities as well. Some claim that this disaster proves that we no longer enjoy the celestial favor.
