The ruler's partner has been pacing the palace lately, their head hung low, only stopping sometimes to longingly gaze out the window. Life is quite different here, compared to their old country, a nation some call our enemy. Where does that leave them, as a traitor to at least one side of it? They shakes their head; such thoughts help no one, their place is here, in our country, by our lord. However, with the knowledge that our government already has our heir, their dynasty, and in their line of succession secure, the ruler's partner sometimes wonder about their purpose at court. They did not marry out of love, and they has produced no royal children. Would they even be missed if they left?
