Three former generals in the south, who once fought for the Ming but now support the Manchu, have turned against us. Ordered to give up their positions and return to the capital, they have instead formed a secret alliance and raised an army. They claim to be fighting to 'oppose the Qing and restore the Ming', but several of their leaders have declared themselves the rightful ruler, despite having no connection to the Ming royal family. A great civil war has started; we must protect our realm from these traitors.
