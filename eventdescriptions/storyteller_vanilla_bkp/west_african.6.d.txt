Some people from our elite have recently been attacked by what they believe to be a raid organized by foreign military. Initially, the attack seemed to have occurred too deep into our country for this to be possible, but an explanation for how this could have happened has begun to take shape. The people in one of our border provinces, who we thought to be our loyal subjects, appear to have conspired with our neighbors to organize the attack.  While it may not be possible to retaliate in kind, there are other ways we can show what we think of such cowardly tactics.
