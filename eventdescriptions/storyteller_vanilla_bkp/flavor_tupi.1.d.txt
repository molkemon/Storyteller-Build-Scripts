Some of the European settlers near our border have started raiding our settlements to take people as slaves. Entire families of Tupi villagers are being captured and taken to sugar plantations in the European colonies. Initially, we thought this would be isolated incidents that we could demand punishment for, but it's now clear that some landowners are using this tactic systematically. Our tribe's elders believe that if we don't act soon, this situation will spiral out of control.
