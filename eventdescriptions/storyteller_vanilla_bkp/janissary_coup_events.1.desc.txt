The years of stagnation and decadence left their mark on the government as even simple tasks find great resistance from within. The strongest reactionary force are the Janissaries. Once the personal guard of the monarch, the Janissaries see any attempt of modernization as a threat to their own privileges. The monarch sought to change it today as they tried to reduce the power of the Janissaries. Shortly after, great commotion abounded in the capital as the Janissaries, long discontent with what they see as the misrule of the realm by the monarch, rejected the bread given by the monarch and overturned their cauldrons in the central square. The Janissaries claim that the monarch is a disgrace to the House of Osman, that they have made the SheikhulIslam their personal puppet and that they aim to destroy the military strength of the empire. The Janissaries have now moved quickly to seize control over the government and have secured the support of many of the other political groups of the capital. While the monarch is still alive, they are no longer in control of their empire.
