With the restoration of the Caliphate under the great Sultan, the perception of Islam as a whole is changing once more. Unified under the Ottoman banner, scholars, theologians, and artists are once again free to live a life free from worldly worries. Scientists see active promotion for their work while architects are blessed by Allah with tasks to construct new mosques. All this while the religious piety in the Islamic faith is experiencing its own renaissance. The impact of the Ottoman Empire can also be felt in our own realm as our own population seem to be inspired by the Sultan's call for a new Golden Age of Islam.
