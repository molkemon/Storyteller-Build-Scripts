Although our military is mostly dictated by the Janissaries, the nobility still have significant influence over the majority of our normal troops. Naturally, this causes conflicts between these two classes as they are bound to clash against each other. Questions such as the leadership of the ortas, the duty of defending the monarch and other special privileges are regularly a reason for conflict. In such situations it is up to the monarch to mediate between the nobility and the Janissaries and resolve their latest point of contention.
