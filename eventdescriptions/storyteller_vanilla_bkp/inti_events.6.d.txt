A ruler is expected to travel around his kingdom, inspecting each and every part of his realm and partaking in the religious as well as worldly ceremonies of his chiefs. As the ruler approaches the huge temple of a local god, a mummy of a dynasty long gone, the royal priests urge caution. This is an unusually popular god and if we are seen to honor the gods of our subjects too clearly we would be giving them too much power and authority. On the other hand this god is said to be very powerful and its popularity along with the favors it might provide are very enticing.
