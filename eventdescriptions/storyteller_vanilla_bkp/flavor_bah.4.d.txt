Slaves are one of the oldest commodities of the Indian Ocean trade network. Young people from Ethiopia are being captured, converted to Islam, and trained to become elite soldiers, administrators, and sailors before being sold to rulers in India and Persia. As our kingdom has a distinct shortage of men who are both able and loyal, these 'Habshis' might prove a valuable asset to our dynasty. Let us use these slaves to man our forts, our desks, and our ships rather than trust the bickering Gharbian and Deccani factions at court.
