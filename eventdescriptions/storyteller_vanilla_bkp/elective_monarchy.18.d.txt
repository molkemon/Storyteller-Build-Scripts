Recent power shifts among the foreign relatives of our monarch and clashing inheritance laws have led to an unfortunate situation with the house of our dynasty. A member of the dynasty has now been suggested to be the rightful ruler of not only our homeland, but also your country. Whether or not this claim will be pushed through war or is merely used to bolster the foreign monarch's status for a domestic power struggle remains to be seen. Regardless, the possibility of being forced into a union in this manner is ominous.
