Our country is an attractive place for merchants from all over the world due to our access to gold and ivory. While it brings great prosperity for us, it also has the side effect of religious conflicts between the merchants. Such are the case with the Christian and the Muslim merchants in our capital, who try to compete against each other on the markets and in the royal court. If we want ever peace from this nuisance, our ruler has to choose how we should deal with these aggressive merchants.
