With the fall of Budapest to Ottoman forces, the Hungarian estates have convened to decide who will be able to protect them going forward. In the first diet, the nobles supporting the Voivode of Transylvania elected him as the new king of Hungary, but many others have formed an Austrian party and held their own diet to elect the monarch to be the future King of Hungary. The result is that what remains of Hungary is now divided, with half of the nobles pursuing an independent policy based around the lands of Transylvania, and the other half turning to our realm.
