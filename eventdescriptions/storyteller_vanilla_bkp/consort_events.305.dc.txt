The consort of our ruler is proving to be a fearless person. Often seen studying the technique of the soldiers during their training, sometimes they even approach the practice grounds themselves. While some were initially hesitant to engage the royal consort in melee, others were curious. Even though it was a surprise to some people, their reputation for handling themselves in a fight is not an unwarranted one. In addition, the presence of the royal consort seems to have an encouraging effect on the troops.
