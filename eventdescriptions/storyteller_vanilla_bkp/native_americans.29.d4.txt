The hunting group is coming back home when among the group of pronghorns that comes across the ruler, the biggest antelope the ruler has ever seen appears. Almost as if it was a deity, the ruler can feel a special bright aura around it and immediately knows the ruler has to hunt it. The ruler leaves the group behind and moves silently to sneak the animal. When the ruler is ready, the ruler prepares the ruler's weapon, hoping to not miss the chance.
