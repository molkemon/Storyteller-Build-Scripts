The coexistence of Hinduism and the faith of the people of this land has been easier in many ways than we initially feared. There are already many examples of deities being incorporated among the many gods of the Hindus and of Hindu gods and customs being incorporated into the lives of the people. In fact, to a certain extent, it seems that the same has started happening to many other religions in the far corners of our realm, with many lesser and higher gods being incorporated into the joint Hindu pantheon.
