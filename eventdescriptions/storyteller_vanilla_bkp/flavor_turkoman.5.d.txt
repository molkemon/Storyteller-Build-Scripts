Our people, although initially of a distinct culture, have slowly but surely embraced the richness and depth of Persian civilization. The art, science, philosophy, and grandeur that once marked the height of Iran have found a new home amongst them. Indeed, they have not merely absorbed Persian culture; they have given it new life. The synthesis of their vigor and Persian sophistication has resulted in a vibrant and powerful realm that promises to leave an indelible mark on the annals of history. Together, they stand as the true rulers of the land. From the fertile plains of the Caspian to the sunbaked sands of Khorasan, their unified people shall write the next glorious chapter in the great book of Iran. They have honored the legacy of their ancestors and yet set their sights on even greater heights.
