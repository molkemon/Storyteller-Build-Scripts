A great number of diviners are attached to any given Oracle's shrine. The Guacarimachic speak to spirits while the Ayatapuc communicate with the dead. The Huatuc achieves a prophetic mood using a holy beverage while the Hamurpa read the entrails of sacrificed animals. All of these great diviners have now dwelt on the questions the ruler has come to pose and all agree that the time ahead will be a most fruitful and successful one.
