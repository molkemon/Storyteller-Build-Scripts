Our support for their ruler, our candidate for the esteemed throne, has yielded us many diplomatic advantages since they were elected. Recently, however, it has been suggested that the ruling family's claims on their country reach beyond this single individual and that our monarch should in fact be considered the ruler of both our realm and their country. They consider their ruler sovereign, of course, but our monarch outranks them in the dynasty. Surely a monarch's first loyalty must be to family, must it not?
