With the recent Ottoman expansion, our capital of Constantinople finds itself in a very vulnerable position. Even though Constantinople is well fortified, the surrounding terrain provides no advantage to the defenders if the city were ever besieged by the Turks. Many are suggesting that it would be wiser to move the court to a different city, one further away from the border with the Ottomans. The upper Hungarian town of Buda seems to be the best candidate. What should we do?
