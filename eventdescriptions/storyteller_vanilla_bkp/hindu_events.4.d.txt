Our country is at peace. The nation has seen a period of stability and prosperity under our ruler, giving us more time to focus on spiritual concerns. Unfortunately, this has instilled in us a fear of seeing an end to the fortunate times. Our advisor finds us deep in prayer before a statue of Shiva. When we finish the mantra, we tell our advisor about our concerns, requesting their advice. Gesturing towards the statue, our advisor answers: 'Look at the hand in front of you. Shiva's raised palm is a reminder that he not only creates and destroys, but also preserves. Fear not.'
