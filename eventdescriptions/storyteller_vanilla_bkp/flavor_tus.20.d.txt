The Pazzi banking family, bitter rivals of the Medici family and the ruler, have conspired to overthrow the Signoria and install themselves as rulers of our realm. Many of the most powerful figures in our politics are participants in this vile plot, ranging from merchants to clergymen to nobles. The Pazzi plan to assassinate our ruler during High Mass at the Duomo, and then to capture the Palazzo della Signoria and seize the reins of government. Should the conspiracy fail however, most of the political enemies of the ruler would be branded as criminals and the Medici would therefore rule practically unopposed.
