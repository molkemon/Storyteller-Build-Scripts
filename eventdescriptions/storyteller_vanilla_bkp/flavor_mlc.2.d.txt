Unlike the late Raja Ibrahim, our new ruler is a Muslim and the trader communities in our city seem to be more accepting of his rule. Nonetheless, a royal advisor who would plot against their lord is a frightening thing, and we would be wise to replace this minister or risk meeting the same fate as his last master. Our trusted advisors all point to a young and successful general as a suitable replacement. Tun Perak is said to be wise for his years, but above all, he is also said to be extremely loyal to their lord.
