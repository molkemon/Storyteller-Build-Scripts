One quiet morning today, the messenger bursts through the doors of the Imperial palace, red and flustered, eager to deliver a valuable correspondence from the seat of power in Poland. Apparently, the local ruler of Poland who was recently crowned a king decided, against the calls of their own advisor, to steer their nation away from its ties to Lithuania, turning its gaze upon the West. As a result, the monarch of Poland has officially petitioned for the entry of their country into the Holy Roman Empire.
