The populace has been left unsatisfied by the outcome of Masaniello's Revolt. They have now risen up anew, this time demanding freedom under a republican government. Having seized control of the city, their leader, Gennaro Annese, an armorer of much cunning and bravery who was the brains behind Masaniello, has declared the "Most Serene Republic of this Kingdom of Naples", which is to be governed by the S.P.Q.N., the Senate and People of Naples. The nobility are split between loyalty to our ruler and support for the fledgling republic. Whichever way we respond, turmoil surely awaits.
