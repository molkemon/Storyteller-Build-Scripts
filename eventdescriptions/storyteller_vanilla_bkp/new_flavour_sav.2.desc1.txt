His Holiness, Pope Eugene the Fourth, has decreed that the Order of Saint Lazarus, an old and prestigious crusader order, shall be merged with the Order of Saint Maurice, of which our monarch is the head, which will henceforth be considered the only legitimate successor of Lazarus' Order. Although we may well wish to downplay the Lazarine Order's former focus on providing leper hospitals, this will surely help to boost our moral and religious authority.
