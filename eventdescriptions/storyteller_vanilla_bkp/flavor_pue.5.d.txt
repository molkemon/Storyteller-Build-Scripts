There is a small fire in the center of our village tonight. People sit around it and lively chat with their friends and relatives. The air is filled with the smell of recently cooked food and the sky is clear without a single cloud. Nights like these are the ones that feel most like home. But tonight is not just a regular night. Soon after midnight, a group of masked men dance towards the fire and everyone stays silent. They dance, representing stories of times long past.
