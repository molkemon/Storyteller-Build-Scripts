Spain is a country of many languages and while some attempts have been made to create a standardized overview of grammar for the native tongue it's far from the only language used in our administration even in the core of our lands. Recently a particularly well-researched and thorough new grammar for our native language was presented to our ruler. When our ruler asked what use this might be to the state the bishop of Avila offered this simple suggestion: 'After Your Highness has subjected barbarous peoples and nations of varied tongues, with conquest will come the need for them to accept the laws that the conqueror imposes on the conquered, and among them will be our language.'
