"It is beautiful; beautiful enough to bring tears to a man's eye. Oh, I weep. I weep to see my Empire flourish so. My armies have brought all Anatolia to heel, petty Beys and the White Sheep alike brought to heel. Armenians, Kurds, and Pontic Greeks alike call me their Sultan. How the Almighty has blessed me, that I might rule lands so vast and great; that Osman Ghazi himself must look down upon me, knowing that his legacy is safe and secure in my hands. My heart flutters at the thought of our great ruler, the Sultan."
