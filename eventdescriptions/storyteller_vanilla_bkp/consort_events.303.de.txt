While growing up, their consort enjoyed learning of the world. They were always asking questions and often pondered ways to make life easier for people, or how to make any given task more effective. Better yet, how to convince somebody else that their way of organizing an assignment was the right one. Thankfully, moving to the royal court of their country has not caused their consort to abandon their dreams of commanding people. If anything, they have gotten a lot of practice; during some of their recent negotiations with foreign diplomats their ideas have received some very positive feedback.
