We trust in the spirits, in the gods that have always been with us, and our people are rarely impressed by the lies of those who would lead them. However, some people on our border who have come into contact with those who worship one they claim returned from the dead seem to have seen similarities between their faith and ours. Just as many here choose to worship the Sky Father, our neighbors speak of the one they call Christ as having a Heavenly Father himself. Many have begun to suggest that these two may be one and the same, and that the animosity between our faiths is nothing but a misunderstanding.
