Our ruler has always taken a great interest in both our adopted religion and those that practice it. They have, however, not always led a life that many of our Christian subjects would approve of. Between the influence of Shamans at court and the habits of our ruler, there are many that feel we are risking divine punishment. Perhaps we should consider instituting some more clearly Christian rules for our court and ruler? Restricting the influence of Shamans, the number of allowed wives and generally the number of officially sanctioned rituals would go a long way towards placating the Christian communities.
