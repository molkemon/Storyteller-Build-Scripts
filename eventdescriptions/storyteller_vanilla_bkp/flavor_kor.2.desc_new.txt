While serving as Third Censor in the current year, the NeoConfucian Kim Ilson, a former state historian, proved himself to be a man willing to go to great lengths in criticizing government officials and the yangban elite. His many opponents have uncovered certain allusions made in his draft of the Annals of King Seongjong which they insist to be treasonous. They demand his immediate execution, and that his supporters among the NeoConfucians be either banished or executed.
