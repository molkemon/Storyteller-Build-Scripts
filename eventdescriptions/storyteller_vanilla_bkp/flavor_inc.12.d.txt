With large parts of the nobility and the traditional Incan elite still hesitant about Huascar the new ruler has not been idle. Distrusting the loyalty of the Incan elite, Huascar has surrounded himself with those who do support him, the lower nobility and foreign subjects. He has also avoided all the customary great feasts and ceremonies associated with the coming of a new ruler. Atahualpa, on the other hand, is doing his utmost to be seen as the very model of a traditional Incan ruler, and an increasing number of the traditional elites now support him.
