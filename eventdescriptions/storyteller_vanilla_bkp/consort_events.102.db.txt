They say that a parent's love for their child is without limits, but it is obvious that some loves are more limitless than others. While the consort is certainly fond of their eldest, the heir, of the dynasty, everyone knows that the heir's brother has the bigger place in the consort's heart. The consort dotes on him and has made sure that he has had the very best education. Now the consort is trying to make their favorite son the new heir of the realm.
