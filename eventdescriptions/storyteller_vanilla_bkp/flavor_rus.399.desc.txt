Death to the enemies of the Revolution. The Revolutionary Guard, members of former serf families, have swept over the cities and have brought the frustration of past peasant generations. Countless rich burghers and boyars have been brought in front of a trial to judge their family's deeds during the time of the serfdom. Although called as a necessary evil against the enemies of the revolution, these trials have been more show than fair processes. Despite that, many burghers from peasant origins have joined the Revolutionary Guard in their quest to terrorize the great cities of their country.
