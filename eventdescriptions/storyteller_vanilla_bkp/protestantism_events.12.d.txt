The famous natural scientist and philosopher is famous for their sharp intellect and their presence in our court lends a certain grandeur to our name. The natural scientist and philosopher is, however, also a firm Catholic and has been allowed to practice their faith as they wish in order to come here. It would be unthinkable for the Church of our country itself to criticize our lord but in the streets of our capital there is talk of the natural scientist and philosopher trying to plant subversive Catholic ideas in the head of our lord.
