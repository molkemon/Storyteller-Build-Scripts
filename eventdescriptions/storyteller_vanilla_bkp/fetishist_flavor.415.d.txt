Lately our diplomats have made one mistake after another. Usually they are well-mannered and poised, yet many of their latest engagements have ended in disaster. By sheer luck, they have not offended anyone too important so far, although we should be careful. Many are pointing fingers at the seidkonur, blaming our misfortune on their spell. They believe that Freyja was displeased that we would rather talk than fight our way through war, but the seeresses insist that they are not to blame.
