The sky is full of stars that form different shapes resembling things and are useful for our sailors. The Manaiakalani, or the chief's fishline, looks like Maui's fishhook. Heiheionakeiki is used to navigate to Tahiti. Ke Ka o Makali'i, or the canoe bailer of Makali'i, represents a bowl pouring onto the horizon. People who know them are useful to the community.
