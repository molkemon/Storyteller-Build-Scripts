The young nation was initially viewed by its neighbors as a fleeting idea of the English monarchs, a desperate attempt to break the stalemate of their centuries-long conflict with the French kings. However, the relentless pursuit of power and influence by this fledgling Empire has earned it widespread acclaim for the remarkable achievements of its ruler. It is the beginning of a new era, as the diverse society of this realm is naturally driven towards progress by the blending of its various traditions.
