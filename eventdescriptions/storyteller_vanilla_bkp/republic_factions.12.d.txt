The guilds of our country have outmaneuvered their competitors in the political arena, wresting control over the provincial government, but their power is far from uncontested. A valued craftsman and artist approaches you with a solution to this problem. 'Your Majesty,' he says, 'a shift in power now may undo everything the guilds have wisely worked towards. We know you are a wise ruler, but if you need an additional incentive, the guilds do not lack funds for political donations.'
