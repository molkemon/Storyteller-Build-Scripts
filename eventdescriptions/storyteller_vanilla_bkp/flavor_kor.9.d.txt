Brewing peasant unrest due to overtaxation and the inability of the government to nip it in the bud have come to a head in a troubled province. A notorious outlaw, Im Kkeokjeong, reportedly the son of a butcher, has gathered a large band of peasants and begun killing the wealthy landowners, distributing their wealth among the poor. Our authorities in the region are appealing to us to help them deal with the crisis.
