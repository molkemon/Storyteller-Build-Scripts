We have known of the Muslim faith for a long time, but chosen to remain true to our traditions. Those who devote themselves only to the distant creator, forsaking the gods and spirits that dwell among us, are certainly on the path to misfortune. People at the border, who live next to Muslims every day, have started to see things differently. They find that the words of Muhammad speak to them as well as their neighbors and rather than seeing this as a betrayal of the old ways, they identify the prophet's God with our most revered sky deity, which they believe is actually their own lord.
