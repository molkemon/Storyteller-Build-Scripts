The Teutonic Order has long since guarded the Prussian lands to our South. Originally sent there by the Pope with the express purpose of converting the heathens that inhabited the provinces, they've long since outlived their usefulness, debasing themselves by conducting raids and harassing even those with whom they share a common faith.  As the Holy Father wills it, these unruly lost souls have been brought back to virtue and the true Catholic way. Their service will be useful to our realm as well as Rome herself.
