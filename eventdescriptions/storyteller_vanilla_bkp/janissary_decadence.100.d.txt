By forcing the inspection of all rolls of membership in the Janissaries our ruler has finally managed to impose control over the corps again. All bad elements and nonsoldiers are being weeded out of the system and will no longer receive state salaries or the status associated with being Janissaries. This is not something the former Janissaries take lightly however, while the ruler has the authority to carry out these measures some are taking up arms against their former ruler.
