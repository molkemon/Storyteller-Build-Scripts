Antonio Pigafetta was an Italian explorer from Vicenza. He traveled in the grand expedition of Fernando Magellan in his voyage around the world. During the expedition, he was Magellan's assistant, and wrote an accurate journal about it. Pigafetta was one of only eighteen men who returned to Spain out of the two hundred and forty who set out three years earlier. This voyage was the first circumnavigation of the world, and his journal is the source for what we know today about this expedition.
