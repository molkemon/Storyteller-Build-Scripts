After the death of Mansa Suleyman Keita, the brother of the mighty Mansa Musa, our country has seen a constant decline in power, influence and wealth over the decades. Civil wars, fights for the throne and a series of incompetent rulers undermined the stability of our country. Former subjects such as the Songhai or the Timbuktu declared their independence from our country, and even now our realm is seeing further and further fall in importance.
