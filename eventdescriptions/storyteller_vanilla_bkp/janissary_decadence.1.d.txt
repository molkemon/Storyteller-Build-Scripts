Great commotion was abound in the capital today as the Janissaries, long discontent with what they see as the misrule of the empire by the ruler, rejected the bread given by the ruler and overturned their cauldrons in the central square. The Janissaries claim that the ruler is a disgrace to the House of Osman, that the ruler has made the SheikhulIslam their personal puppet and that they aim to destroy the military strength of the empire. The Janissaries have now moved quickly to seize control over the government and have secured the support of many of the other political groups of the capital. While the ruler is still alive, they are no longer in control of their empire.
