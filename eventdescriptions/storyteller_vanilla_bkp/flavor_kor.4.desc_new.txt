Cho Kwangjo, a NeoConfucian reformer, has been trying to change the government according to Confucian ideas. At first, his efforts to reform seemed successful, but now they seem to have had the opposite effect. After he strongly criticized the old system of the yangban, he weakened their control over the government and made them angry by suggesting they take away their land. The yangban now claim that Cho Kwangjo is a traitor who wants to become the ruler, and they say his reforms are misguided and will harm society and the country. Cho Kwangjo, however, believes that the only right thing to do is to continue the reforms as quickly as possible, even though many people are against him.
