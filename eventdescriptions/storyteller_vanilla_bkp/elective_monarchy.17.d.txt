Ruling in our country is not like ruling in other nations. Dictating the nation's goals is difficult without approval from nobles, sometimes impossible. To a foreign monarch, this may seem disappointing and frustrating, but this cornerstone of our politics is highly valued in our homeland and is, in fact, what our ruler has to thank for coming into power in the first place. Embracing these ideas might make our ruler seem weak internationally, but would aid in integrating better and gaining supporters in the realm.
