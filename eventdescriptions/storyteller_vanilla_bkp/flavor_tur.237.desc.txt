The Empire of Persia is vanquished, and its vast holdings have fallen under the righteous jurisprudence of our most Sublime Caliphate. Indeed, a mighty and formidable foe but even the great Persia cannot stand between us and our ultimate destiny. Though much blood was shed and many a martyr was born of this conquest the people of these lands are brothers in the faith, and deserving of respect. Indeed, we shall rule justly and honor the history of this land by restoring Persia to its long-lost glory as a valued member of our ever-expanding Empire.
