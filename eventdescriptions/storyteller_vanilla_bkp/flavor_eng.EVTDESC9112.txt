England, as we know it, decided to become a naval power and established the Admiralty as one of its key assets. There were also signs of England taking another route, a route toward the continent and land power. Some elements can be found in Cromwell's New Model Army, but also the question marks of a possible pro-French Stuart dynasty if England had managed to keep some of its French possessions. If that had happened, England would have had much greater interests to defend on the continent than on the high seas.
