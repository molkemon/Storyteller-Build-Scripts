In preparation of battles to come, the monarch recently went to see their seer. The mysterious elder possesses great wisdom and any insight into the destiny of the monarch is always welcome. The diviner left the monarch confused however, as they were told that their heir would bring the monarch a premature death. A battle? Poison? An accident?  'Which will it be?' the monarch demanded to know, but the signs were not clear; dealing with the matter is now a choice of utmost delicacy.
