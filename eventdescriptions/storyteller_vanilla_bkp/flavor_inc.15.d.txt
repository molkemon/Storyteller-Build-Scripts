A small group of Conquistadors have managed to capture our great leader, and they're threatening to burn him alive. This catastrophe is only made worse by their vile threat. Hoping to avert this horrible fate, our ruler soon ordered us to gather a huge ransom, more precious metals than these barbarians have ever dared to dream of. As news of the ruler's death travels through the realm, many of the people subdued by the Incas in the past now see their chances to revolt, and even the core areas under our control are shaken and unsure of the future. The only sliver of hope we have is that it's said that the ruler's body was lost soon after its burial, and in his death, his status has already reached mythical proportions.
