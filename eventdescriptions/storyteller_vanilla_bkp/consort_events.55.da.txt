The ruler's partner has been pacing the palace lately, their head hung low, only stopping sometimes to longingly gaze out the window. Life is quite different here, compared to their old country, a nation some call our enemy nowadays. Where does that leave them, as a traitor to at least one side of it? They shakes their head; such thoughts help no one, their place is here, in our country, by our lord. When doubt besets them they tends to seek out the young heir, as the innocent laughter and calm eyes of their heir usually brings a smile to their face. Today, however, not even this helps. Perhaps it's the weather?
