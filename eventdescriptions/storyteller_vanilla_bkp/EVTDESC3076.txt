It seems that the trading between our colonists in the country and the people of the province has led to an outbreak of diseases usually foreign to the region. They come to us with grizzly stories of bringing goods traded from our nation back to their home, only for it to infect their weak and elderly with a plethora of diseases their healers have never encountered before. All they ask is for a single free shipment of medical supplies, and they will gladly begin integrating with our colony.
