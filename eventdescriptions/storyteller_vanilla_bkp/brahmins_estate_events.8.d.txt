One of our lord's trusted advisors has proven to be a highly capable administrator and has gained the ear of our ruler. While our advisors have long been entrenched in our realm's bureaucracy, it is rare for them to have an explicitly political role even in the courts of our domain. The appointment of this advisor as the highest appointed ministerial office is an appealing prospect. Though our subjects throughout the realm would take the opportunity to increase their influence, it would assure them that we have their best interests at heart.
