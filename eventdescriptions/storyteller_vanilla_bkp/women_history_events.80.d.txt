The Green Queen, Ratu Hijau, is the eldest daughter of Mansur Shah of Malacca. After her brother's murder, she worked hard to become queen of our realm. After twenty years of unstable rule, she promises stability and prosperity. While her brother ruled, she tried to influence him to be favorable to merchants, create a single common language, and restore our realm to greater glory, something she now promises will be realized under her rule. Her younger sisters, the Blue Queen and the Purple Queen, support her, even though both could become queens in their own rights.
