The Brandenburger lands are not the wealthiest in Germany and for long periods of time law and order have been difficult to maintain. As a result, many families have a tradition of lining their pockets in times of financial need by violent or coercive means. Our countryside is plagued by bandits from the less well-off noble families of the realm. They are a menace both to our authority and to the merchants of our already struggling cities. If our state is to prosper here in Germany, we must restore order and end these destructive traditions of the local nobility.
