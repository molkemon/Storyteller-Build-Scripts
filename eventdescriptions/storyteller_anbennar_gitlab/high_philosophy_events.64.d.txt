The willingness of the monarch to risk life and limb in the protection of their people is considered highly worthy of respect. Leading figures have voiced their support for the ruler, some even proclaiming them as an ideal ruler for this self-sacrifice. Indeed, to plunge headfirst into danger in pursuit of a people's defense is surely in keeping with the Nitikamarja, and well implies that Tamatma, the chi of the collective, is strong, and victory certain.
