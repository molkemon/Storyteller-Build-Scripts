The capital was abuzz a few months ago with a discovery sent from our overlord. documents from the time of the Ynnic Empire, that seemed to directly back their claims in the ongoing religious debate. But that news is nothing compared to the recent message out of another realm; the documents were forgeries. That our overlord would turn to such dishonorable tactics is proof that their stance on the ongoing debate is wrong. How can we trust a sovereign who would lie so profanely about the very truth of history?
