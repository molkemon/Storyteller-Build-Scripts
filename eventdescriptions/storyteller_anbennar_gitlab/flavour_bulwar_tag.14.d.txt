The Naqtazan is now formally established. All of the members who have accepted the offer have been enshrined as the founding members of the alliance, which will host a shared treasury and military under a joint command, with generals from Bulwari in the lead, obviously, to defeat Tluukt and perhaps even beyond. The constitution of the league, as drafted by representatives from the families of Alukatan, szalLekad, and Belatis and approved by both the council and the rest of the league, has created the foundations for our own power bloc in Bulwar. With that being said, "future base for Bulwar to unite the region" is not a purpose any, except perhaps us, would knowingly support. Something else needs to be added to the charter proclaiming the league, succinctly stating its reason to exist. Perhaps it might be an alliance of humans breaking their own chains? A holy crusade against the Malevolent Dark? A simple league to prevent a new Age of Monsters? All of these sound respectable, and more than that they all serve to obscure our true intention.
