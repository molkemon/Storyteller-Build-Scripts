Our missionaries have gathered, with Erelas and the other fine minds among our reformers instructing them on what theologies and festivities might best sway our population. With their bags laden with sacred texts and gifts for the people, where should we send them? As they go out amongst the schools, to the farmhouses, the taverns, and the castles, they shall be fighting a war for the hearts of our people, before we fall to such madness that the sword might well replace the tongue in these battles.
