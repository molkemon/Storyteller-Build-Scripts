A halfdrowned goblin, after the water was pumped from her lungs, has shared with our shamans the conversation she had while drowning with the god Star Water. A rogue star, when the other gods were busy fighting each other, carved out his own domain in the seas. He was wounded in the fighting and now wears an eye patch and has a wooden leg. He acts as the protector of pirates and fishers. It is also believed he causes floods when he is drunk, so it is forbidden to sacrifice alcohol to him.
