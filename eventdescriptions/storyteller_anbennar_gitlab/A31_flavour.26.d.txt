The artificers of our realm have approached us with a proposition. They have devised a novel form of governance inspired by records of the system of government practiced by the Gnomish Hierarchy of old, and designed to conduct the business of governance according to logical principles. Of course, artificers are bound to play an important role in this new technocracy, but they assure us that this is merely an unavoidable consequence of a government based on intellect and merit.
