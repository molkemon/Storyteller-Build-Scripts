The lizardfolk have always seemed rather strange to us, due to their more stoic and pragmatic ways, but in recent days and weeks they have been acting strange, to say the least. For the recent festival of Drongay, they helped set up decorations, cook food, and take part in the ceremonies en masse. The explanation for this strange behaviour turns out to be that they have just found out that Drongay is the god of the lizardfolk, which has led to many of them coming over, rather flattered by the festival and feeling compelled to attend it. This has all led to the lizardfolk and our people drawing much closer together by the festival’s beginning. It seems that no matter how different our two peoples are, food, drink, and music can always bridge the gap.
