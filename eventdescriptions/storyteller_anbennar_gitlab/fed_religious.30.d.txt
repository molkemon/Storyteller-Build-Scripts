The representatives of the Yukel were last to arrive at the Summit, and taking their place between the Kodave and Enuuk factions feels something like sitting down in the plains between two armies already mustering to charge. Given our impartiality here, it shall be on us to mediate the issues and moderate the debate. That gives us considerable flexibility in our approach. We could decry the foolishness on both sides, or subtly choose one faction or the other to favour in the course of the debate. Alternatively, some believe we should wash our hands of the entire circus, simply playing our assigned impartial role.
