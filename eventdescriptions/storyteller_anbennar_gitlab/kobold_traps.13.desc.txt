A great host of pilgrims has arrived from a foreign domain. Pious kobolds have seen their lives destroyed, their homes seized, their temples wrecked, and their hoards looted, yet still they come to the capital, contributing everything they still have to our hoard. In this procession, foreign dragonpriests trade stories with our own; kobolds of all hues sing in solidarity and triumph; koboldkind is one, united in the capital, united in their love for the dragons. Here, in the caverns, we shall not be beaten. Here, in the caverns, we shall not be destroyed. To Malliath, Tayekan, Nimrith, and Iotharal, and every other dragon in Halann.
