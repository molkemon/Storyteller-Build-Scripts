The wisest magically inclined minds in the expedition gathered, looking over the various runes and the attached apparatus. Quickly it was determined that the gateway was, in fact, a portal creating device, currently in a state only comparable to the sleep of a flesh and blood being. One mage stated, taking notes feverishly, "Whatever it is, it is years ahead of anything we could even begin to conceive of replicating. The runes in the archway aren't dwarven, but possess a vague resemblance to examples of certain goblin script variations. But we can say it definitely leads somewhere on this world, and it is not an interplanar portal." Many of their fellows agreed, saying that the insights alone would be valuable once returned with. However, the investigation ended there as one of their number shouted that the portal was activating, seemingly on its own. Quickly, they retreated to a safe distance as a chaotic vortex of purple energy formed, as many could feel the air pressure in the room drop as a powerful gust of wind headed towards the portal. The vortex itself was mesmerizing, and a few had to be held back from simply wandering in.
