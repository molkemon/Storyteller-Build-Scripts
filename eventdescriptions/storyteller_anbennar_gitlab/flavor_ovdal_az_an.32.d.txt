With the recovery of the Jade Mines, the Eastern Serpentspine finally lies under our control. Not only have we defeated the vile Command, but we have also righted another, much older grudge. We have, in spirit, defeated the old hold of Grônstunad, who once proclaimed themselves Emperors of the Eastern Serpentspine. With us now surpassing their memory, we have also ended this animosity towards Grônstunad. We are triumphant.
