Tell my lord. Your servant Faribarz sends the following message. Recent scouts have noticed that in the old city of Gerxhoraak, the ruins of one of the great cities of the Suncrown gnolls, a group we believed had vanished until now, still linger there. The city may lie in ruins, but they still keep the Red Flame alive in the crumbled temple. A gnoll priest of the temple gave us a message, saying they are one of the few Red Flames remaining and that they used to burn the remains of the Red Xhaz, defeated by Jaher. They believe that as long as the flame remains, no Xhaz, Xhazobine, or Xhazobain will threaten Bulwar ever again. The scouts have noticed that all the priests and attendants wear armor beneath their garments and swords at their sides; they also appear to have fought on numerous occasions, as many are covered in scars, and some even wear bandages and various prostheses. They are remnants and veterans of a thousand battles against the Xhazob Gnolls; could they be the remains of the so-called Guardians of Gold, the elite guard of the Suncrown Gnolls? At the moment, they refuse to be called anything other than Marzana's heirs. Their dedication to the cult and its sacred martial duty could be a great boon to our nation.
