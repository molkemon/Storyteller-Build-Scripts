The fields lie vacant as the owner of the plantation drops his empty cup to the floor. Only a few orks could be heard toiling away in the vineyard, punctuated by the distant sound of the whip. The orks were likely exhausted due to the extra work required of them to fulfill this year’s quota, and none were there to replace them due to the scarcity of orkish stock up for auction. Disgruntled, the noble audibly cursed the crown for causing all of his struggles, grabbing his whip and leaving the empty dining table in his wake. After all, he had to make use of the little he had.
