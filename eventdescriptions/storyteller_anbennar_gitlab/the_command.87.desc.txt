The rise of the Shamans is something out of our darkest myths, our oldest legends. And while discipline and order have kept most our people in proper form, ready to beat back this ancient enemy, some have been engulfed by terror instead. In a certain area, hobgoblins are refusing to join the army for fear what might happen to their families while they are away, and some are even questioning the leadership of their monarch in light of this turmoil.
