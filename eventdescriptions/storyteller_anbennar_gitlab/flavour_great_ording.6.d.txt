Following the battle of Dames Teeth, where the Castanorian navy was destroyed, the tower of Vertesk was vulnerable to attack from the seas. Thanks to the assistance of Alenn the Younger and members of the Gawedi resistance, Maugun and the free realms forces had set off from Anbenncóst, seeking to exploit this weakness and gain access to the Tower of Vertesk. The battle was in full swing, but it was far from over. Maugun, Alenn, and the others fought hard, pushing their way through the tower. Soldier after soldier fell, Maugun's blade swinging rhythmically through the defenders until none remained. Finally, he found himself at the study of Venac the Arrogant. Venac had remained in the tower, believing his presence could turn the tide of battle. He was nearly correct. Noticing the intruder, Venac threw fire from his fingers and called out, "And who are you to think you could defeat Venac the Great?" Maugun darted aside, dodging his flames, and decapitated Venac in a single motion. Alenn the Younger was not so fortunate.
