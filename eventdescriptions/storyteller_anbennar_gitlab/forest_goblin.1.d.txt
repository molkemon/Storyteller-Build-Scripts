The Greentide has been, to put it bluntly, disastrous for us Forest Goblins. Already struggling to fight back against the Wood Elves, the warhost of orks pouring into the woods shattered any defence that could be put up. Hopelessly outclassed on the battlefield, it was only the ingenuity of our minds that kept us holding onto the fringes of our groves. Every goblin knows now that for all their might, even the strongest ork will fall the same as anyone else to a hidden pitfall trap. Discipline is only so useful for an Elven battalion when faced with a rolling spiked log. We have gotten very good at fortifying our lands with traps; and some of our goblins in a certain province seem to have gotten too good. We've received reports from the areas that the traps are so widespread and prevalent that it is becoming impossible to get around without falling victim to something ourselves. Not a day goes by where one of our own goblins doesn't go missing in the forest, and calls are being made to stop building new ones; at least until we figure out where the ones we already have are.
