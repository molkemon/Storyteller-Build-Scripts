Interesting encounter in the market in Aqatbar today. After meeting with F. Zawari at the Alchemist’s Guild, I visited an antique dealer under recommendation of Zawari, accompanied by L. Wirebeard. Wirebeard traveled ahead and I arrived thirty minutes later, only to discover Wirebeard in argument with the owner of the antique emporium, Ms. T. Surakesi, over the provenance of a glass decanter displayed in the store. Wirebeard is once more causing problems with his refusal to properly learn Bulwari. I do not understand Mrs. Finehammer’s tolerance for him; the man could not sell gold to a goblin. I digress. I spoke to Surakesi in my own passable Bulwari and achieved a pleasant resolution to the argument. Her store is in possession of many antique Cobalt pieces, phenomenal craftsmanship as always. I explained our business coming from reestablished Orlghelovar, showed her some samples, including my beloved Glomla’s travel flask, that I had in my possession. Surakesi passes her compliments to our glassblowers; please pass this on to Glomla. It appears there is a large market for antique Cobalt Dwarf glass in Aqatbar at least, and Ms. Zawari claims this is also the case further south. There appears to be a great opportunity to leverage this interest into new customers for our own wares.
