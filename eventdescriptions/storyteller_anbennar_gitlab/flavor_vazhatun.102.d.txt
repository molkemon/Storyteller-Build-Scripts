Though it feels like it took an eternity, we have finally brought the madness to an end. The riots have ceased, all the remaining infectees are under proper supervision, and even the steady reports of new terror incidents have stopped. Optimistic as it may be, it seems that astral terror has completely left the domain, at least for now. And yet, nobody is in the mood to celebrate. Our people are exhausted, malnourished, and many of us have had to put down someone we knew closely. While we fully intend to reopen the Setxunkhûpa and resume our study of the stars, it is probably safer for all of us if that grand undertaking is postponed a bit into the future, when our nation has recovered its strength.
