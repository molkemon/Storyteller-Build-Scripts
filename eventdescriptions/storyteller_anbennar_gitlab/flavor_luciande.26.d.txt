The mist twirling. The leaves rustling. The heart of a deer beating. Lucian feels everything that happens in his lands, yet he has no control over them. He cannot calm the mist, he cannot halt the wind, and he cannot stop the heart of a deer. Lucian knows the spell. Its intent was to bind the ancient curse to him, and not him to it. He will not bend the knee. He needs to focus more on his new connection to the mist and to the spell. All he needs is time, and he and the mist are not going anywhere.
