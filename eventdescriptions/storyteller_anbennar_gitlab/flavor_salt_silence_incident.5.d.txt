It is a sight to see a fully armored Aldresian Retinue marching across the horizon. A thousand of these glinting blue armored knights have shattered countless armies ten times their size for their Emperor in the Lilac Wars. These Aldresians held the Empire’s answer to the ruler's ambitions. Within the hour our capital would burn, or we would attain an unthinkable ascendancy. At the head of the retinue is an older man with a gunblade in hand and a dark blue plume on his helmet. He stirs his horse as it gallops ahead of his army and stands at our gate waiting for the ruler. The ruler steadies the bodyguards around them and steps out from the gate. The ruler. You have been formally recognized as King of The Borders and Salt within the Empire. The Emperor expects gratitude. The Knight Commander announces.
