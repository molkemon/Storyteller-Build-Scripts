After the dust has settled, after the soldiers stop fighting, and after the peace treaties have been signed, the only thing that remains is to rebuild what was lost. This is as clear as ever, as our ruler has received a letter of demands from our recent enemies. They claim that our armies were ransacking the ancient temples that dot the continent of Haless, and that such a foul act can never be taken back. But that it could be forgotten about, with a bit of gold. Should we assist them in rebuilding these ancient monuments, or laugh in their faces?
