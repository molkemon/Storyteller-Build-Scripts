The Hwalbaek are expert archers, and their skills on foot and on horseback are strong. But the hordes of the Shuvüüsh are better riders still, and their terrorbirds can easily keep pace with the horses of the lowlands. Despite our defeat of the Zunk Kuulgo, their other clans are still considered powerful and have the advantage in speed and power, especially if they ever were to unite. Thus, some of the noble families have demanded we invest more into the cavalry, breeding faster and stronger stock.
