Time has passed since the Darkalyndar Succession War was fought, and the following decision to keep Darkalyndar as an independent ally was made. circumstances have changed and at least some of the protagonists of that decision are deceased; it is time to revisit it. The rulers of both nations are close relatives and are in good relations, but it is now needed to have the two nations together under the same hands, to avoid the risk of the nation drifting apart or worse, consuming each other in wars. Having two rulers with a valid claim on each other's title is not a good recipe for peace. The monarch graciously agreed to step down, and the ruler will be the ruler of both Darkalyndar and Gophira. Positions of power will have to be exchanged, and reparations given, but it is a small price to pay.
