Since time immemorial, Dames tear meteorites rained down from the sky, providing raw magic in solid form. While traditionally used to create enchanted weapons or mana potions, some have used dark magics to corrupt the Dames tear, inverting its properties. instead of enhancing magic, it nullifies it. During the Age of Artificers, a new discovery was made, allowing black Dames tear, the nullified variant, to be simply mixed in the casting process for bullets, which allowed mass production of magic shield-penetrating ammunition.
