The monarch scurried up the side of the crucible, some of the skin ripping from the ruler's hand as he climbed the steel. Larandesh had coursed his magic through the Dames tear-infused grooves on the stone, evoking a sticky substance that could hold your weight. It did the job; the monarch was the last one up. Then a chant, and the mage let the spell fail. The party recovered their breath and their nerves atop the catwalks. The Challúmata below tried to climb themselves, but couldn't make their way up. That didn't stop them from trying, though. Eventually, the Rakulacans began to stand, dust themselves off, and collect their gear. They couldn't stay here forever, watching the Challúmata claw at the crucibles, after all, and they still had to get their hammer.
