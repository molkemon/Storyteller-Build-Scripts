An elderly dwarf artisan claims to have discovered a method to open a portal to a strange land, in which allegedly reside a number of dwarves who the dwarf claims to have seen in dreams. As unbelievable as this may seem, he further claims that he just needs a few ingredients and some manpower to develop such a connection; luckily, most of these ingredients are available in local markets. All he needs is a bit of gold, if we wished to explore this.
