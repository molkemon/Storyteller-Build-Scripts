The Dame has always been known as the goddess of knowledge and wisdom, so it was no surprise that many of the Damish and Moonsinger clergy were proud members of the Ravelian Society, and later on, the Ravelian Church. With the official conversion of the Damish Temple to Ravelian doctrine, the luminaries of the faith have decided to rename and turn the Temple of the Highest Moon that sat atop the Moonmount mountain into a monastery dedicated to the deepest of contemplations on the secrets of the world.
