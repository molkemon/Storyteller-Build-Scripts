Hello there, Grand Marshal of the ruling dynasty. Oh, don't be so frightened to be addressed by name, I knew you would find this diary. You found all my others. We have met before. Or it may have been a predecessor of yours; I forget so easily, and you are all the same. But a long time ago, when we did meet, something very valuable was taken from me. Call it innocence, call it my son, call it my life. whatever it was, that which was taken can never be restored to me. And so long as The Command exists, more people like me will have those same things taken from them. The only logical end to fight for is the end of The Command. The greatest irony is that you will take this as confirmation of the rightness of your practices thus far, but know this. had you not made war on me and mine, I should not have had cause to wage war on you and yours. Day twenty-five. I purchased papaya at market today.
