Even stripped bare of the gems that once adorned it, the bare Dwarovkron remains a fearsome sight. Adorned with platinum surfacing thanks to the dwarves of Haraz Orldhûm, it was made from Kronium, the heaviest metal in existence. Truly, it is a masterful piece of work. After we located it, our smiths have restored it as best as they were able, and it will make a fine addition to our vaults. And who knows? If we are able to locate all of the seven gems that once studded its surface, we would have a convincing claim to the entirety of the Serpentspine.
