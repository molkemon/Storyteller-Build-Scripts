Khetist scholars in the realm have long debated how to reconcile their beliefs with their geographical reality. Many have seen the sacrifice of Castanor as a feat worthy of the Khets themselves, while many common people have interacted with the native faith of these lands. It has been decided that the best means forward is a syncretism between the two practices. This will serve to help integrate their people into this new land, and for those already here to embrace the glory of the Khets.
