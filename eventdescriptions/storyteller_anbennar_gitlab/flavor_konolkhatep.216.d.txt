Traveller, in the year currently, the great Song Road Izermmoton was dedicated in the name of the ruler. As the Sorrow rises and falls in accordance with the seasons, so too does the Song Road rise and fall along its path. As the Sorrow spread the great faith of the Khet along its banks, so too does the Song Road spread Khet worship to further lands. As you are spurred on to your destination by the ballads of the Song Road, so too do the Khet spur us on to greatness. As this road stands forever, so too shall Konolkhatep. Inscription by the side of the Song Road in Axat.
