Bureaucrats have long favored paper over parchment and other materials due to how much easier it is to work with. There are also various measures that can be taken to make official papers harder to falsify. The invention of the watermark in Seinathíl is one, the elaborate marbling techniques and calligraphy of the Raheni ministers another. As empires increasingly come to grow to encompass more than one continent, this need for provable authenticity and ease of use has led to imperial bureaucracies from Yanshen to Cannor exclusively using paper for official documents and orders.
