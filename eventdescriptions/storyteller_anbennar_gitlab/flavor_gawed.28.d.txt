The Imperial army’s march to the north took weeks, but by the time they were assembled a greater host had not been seen since the full might of The League of Free Realms gathered to oppose Nichimer. Noble knights and valiant footmen thundered up the realm all the way to the Alenic Frontier, each and every soldier ready to give their life for the Empire. As the Imperial legions crested the hills around the Alen’s mouth and arrived at Vertesk, however, they found no ambush or grand royal army ready to contest them. Instead, the city’s gates were open, the forts abandoned, and a great rush of tracks heading north toward Gaweton the only sign of the enemy’s flight. Across the entire northern frontier, the Gawedi forces had withdrawn, and the Imperial host readily took up positions in the enemy’s abandoned fortifications along the border. The looming eagle appears to be nothing more than a shadow at best.
