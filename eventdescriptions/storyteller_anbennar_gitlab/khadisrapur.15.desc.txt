At long last, victory is ours. The hobgoblin threat is eliminated, the orks are freed and enlightened to our ways, and Shamakhad is pretty much ours. We are the last remaining Ruin Kingdom standing. We did our duty even when we were on our very knees, a mere battle away from being swept under the rug of history and conquest, and yet we managed to endure and rise. Our battlehardened troops march across the region, welcomed by everyone; even the orks give us praise as we finally gave them a home to call their own. Though the land they have been granted is rough, it is theirs alone, and for that they happily fight and defend our cause and name. And Haless? They are all spreading our name, our spirit, and how we defied all odds like the heroes in legends, struggling valiantly against an impossible end. It is time to bask in our glory and rest for a bit before taking on the further challenges that the world will inevitably soon throw at us once more. After all, we are.
