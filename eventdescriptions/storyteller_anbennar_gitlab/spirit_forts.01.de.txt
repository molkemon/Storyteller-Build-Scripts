We have gained control over one of the many fortresses that dot Bomdan, yet this is no mundane fortification. It is a Spirit Fortress, inhabited and guarded by the venerated ancestor spirits of the local populace. If we desire to win their approval and the blessings and protection that come with it, we need to prove ourselves worthy. Only once the spirits have acknowledged us as the rightful guardians of all these seven pillars of the land can we truly claim to be the righteous ruler of all Bom. These are the Spirit Fortresses found within the seven Sacred Cities of Bomdan.
