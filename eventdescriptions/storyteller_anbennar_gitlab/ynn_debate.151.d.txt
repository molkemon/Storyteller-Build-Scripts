A proclamation has come down from a certain country declaring that no Corrupted Ruinborn is the equal of a worshiper of the Ynn. According to them, it is only by the grace of the Ynn that their societies have flourished since the Ending Flood. The lack of any civilization among the Corrupted is thus proof they were never the chosen of the Ynn. Lords may entertain ideas of teaching them their ways, but they can never learn. One may teach a dog to do tricks, but the glorious works of Ynnic society are no mere tricks. They grow crops where they cannot. They have writing and a comprehensible language where they do not. They have culture where they do not. That is the disparity which they can never cross for they are the lost and they are the found.
