In Tughayasa, the vast turning of the wheels of prophecy pauses. In the Ministries and the eunuch academies, a thousand thousand quills are put down, their writing halting for a moment. The great rivers—Yanhe, Kharunyana, Dhenbasana, Telebei—cease their flow, rushing water left tranquil as a moonlit pond. Those in the Spirit Realm could see it, and describe it, better. But every soul in Haless can feel as one who devoted their life to the region passes into a new chapter. Whether they loved or feared the previous monarch, none can deny their passion or energy, nor that the continent has been forever reshaped in their wake. The monarch's eyes close, for the last time. In those final moments before they go to join their ancestors, they see a vision. a thankful message from Haless. The figure smiles at one who has followed in their footsteps. Then our monarch is gone, and this chapter in the book of the realm's history is closed. Thankfully, this is Haless. Where the past isn’t dead—it’s not even past.
