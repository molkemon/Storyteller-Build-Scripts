An eerie silence settles between ourselves and the Reachmen. Our orkish emissaries report a wall of stony faces lining the streets of our vassal cities; those who hold no personal animosity merely turn their heads, unwilling to be dubbed collaborators. No matter what progress we have made in shoring up the loyalty of our vassal rulers, the germ of rebellion has spread among the people of the Reach, and stories and legends of reclaiming conquerors now circulate more or less openly. In response, we have already begun to mobilize for outright war. The most certain course is to march our orkish armies directly into vassal land and work to build fortifications immediately, but this is likely to inflame tensions and potentially hurry any ongoing plans for rebellion. On the other hand, our clanchiefs have real doubts about drafting local human vassal forces to fortify against their own; they may defect in the event of war, or may indeed already be sympathetic to dreams of renewed human rule.
