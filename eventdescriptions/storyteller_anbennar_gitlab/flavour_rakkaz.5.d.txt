Our painstakingly planned takeover has been an unreserved success. The foolish descendants of Rakkaz the First didn’t know what was happening to them even as we undermined all the pillars they based their rule on. Bringing the shamans on our side with the promise of constant sacrifices, paralyzing the military chain of command, and making the minds of the simple pack members increasingly susceptible to our influence has been hard work. But thanks to our efforts, our coup has processed as smoothly as an obsidian blade carving out the heart of a sacrifice. While we may keep up appearances, at least for a while, and let the ruler remain sitting on the corpse throne of their ancestors, they will dance on their strings to our tune like any other good puppet. Now that we have taken over, it is time to begin our arcane research in earnest, without being held back by superstitious fools and cowards. Our dark patrons expect great things from us, and we will not disappoint them.
