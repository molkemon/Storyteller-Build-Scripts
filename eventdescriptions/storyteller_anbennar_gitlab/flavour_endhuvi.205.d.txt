The Harimari asked for another tea, evidently somewhat unimpressed. "I will explain some of the finer points of my question, provide broader context." The philosopher murmured to the attendant to bring the best tea and add some wine; it would appear they would be here for a long time. It took some hours, but in the end, the Harimari felt satisfied. As they say, "The building of the High Temples wasn’t done in a single day."
