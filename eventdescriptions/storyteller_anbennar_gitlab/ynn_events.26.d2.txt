It is done. We have crushed the crazed mob of the Dragon Cult, and slashed our way through the streets of the province, reaching the palace of deviancy where the Dragon Prince was being raised and revered. The creature didn't wait for us any longer, and perched itself on the roof of the palace, growling at our knights before spitting a cloud of flames at them, repelled by the water shields cast by our mages. Once the dust settled, the dragon lay dead among the rubble of the now-ruined palace and countless corpses of our knights. All the same, our surviving troops raise their blades to the sky and cheer, for few can say they have accomplished the deeds that transpired in the province today.
