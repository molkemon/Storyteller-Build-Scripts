The end of the Sorcerer King's reign was drawing near. His forces had been turned aside in the fields, and his acolytes seemed all dead. He had fled to Trialmount for a final showdown, a battle fit for only the finest warriors of the coalition. Maugun was not a poor fighter, yet his ability to ride did not translate well onto a mountain, so he was one of those who needed to stay back. The Adenicans, grateful for their liberation, saw fit to introduce the elves to one of the finer sports of Cannor. jousting. During this tournament, the Adenicans were impressed by the prowess of Maugun, with few able to defeat him in the joust, especially not while Maugun was atop his unicorn. Thanks to this and his recent knighthood, they granted him the moniker for which he is best known. No longer would he be known simply as Maugun of Ording; for now, he was Maugun the Unicorn Knight.
