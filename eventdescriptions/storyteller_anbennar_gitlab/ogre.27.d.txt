The Northmen are feared by many. True reavers of the high seas, pillaging and raiding wherever they please. Well, unfortunately for them, these oceans aren’t big enough for the two of us. First, we start with their fleets. Their pesky little boats prove quite the nuisance for our Hulks. Their design makes it easy for their warriors to board our ships and even manage to sink plenty of our vessels, the little buggers. Unfortunately for them, they don’t realize just how many of us there are; an entire mountain range worth. They can only hope to slow the inevitable. More and more of their little ships are sent to the bottom of the sea, and more and more of the infamous reavers of the north are roasted and eaten by our crews. Soon, their fleets stop coming, and we take the fight to them. They put up token resistance, but what can you do when the majority of your armies aren’t even soldiers? We will say it is awfully nice of them to send meals our way so we don’t get hungry while we are here. Nothing is spared; everything edible is taken or eaten. Their plunder becomes ours. However, the true prize is the captured Northmen ships that will be reverse engineered into lower draft vessels that will accompany our Hulks and raid the coastal towns and cities. The world will look upon our black flags and quake in fear because they know the Saltbreath and their Feast Fleet is here. Yo ho.
