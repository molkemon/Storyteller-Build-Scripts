The first expedition's planning is underway. Our choices lie between the Jiromji city of Kunolo, which historically was a halfling settlement and faced a variety of culture shifting events, and the Halfling city of Ibidintely. Kunolo remains the best representation of this, as every group imaginable lives within its walls. We may also be invited to research the past of the region, as tumultuous as it is, and discover lost knowledge of Kheteratan and the halfling kind. The city of Ibidintely welcomes humans, and it is highly defensive against its neighboring states. We are unsure if it remains independent or not. Either way, the residents seem nonetheless happy to exchange knowledge for a little bit of supplies. Here, halfling society is alive at its fullest. Pick wisely, my liege.
