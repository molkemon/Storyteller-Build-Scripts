To the ruler, regarding the so-called Goblin Hunters of Raven Banner, we have managed to ascertain the involvement of at least five individuals in border raids on the territory of Earworm, as well as five hundred eighty-seven individuals on the territory of Flying Hound. May eternal glory and light of chivalry shine upon the clan. It needn’t be said, but the discrepancy in figures presented is almost certainly due to the treatment of prisoners by the Worms, as a not insignificant number of captured humans have been subject to what can only be described as mob justice. The results of these actions speak for themselves, ruler, but for the sake of recordkeeping, the following acts have been committed so far. Drowning. ninety-five times; Suffocation. twelve times; Suffocation by drowning. three times, likely duplicated data; Dismemberment, leading to bleeding out, leading to death. forty-three times; Dismemberment, leading to bleeding out, leading to a mercy kill. thirty-nine times; Burning. seventy-seven times; Brawls. five hundred two times; Honorable duels. two times, disputed; Dishonorable duels. one time, disputed; Decapitation. thirteen times; Failed decapitation followed by a wholly different method of execution. four hundred sixty-seven times; “I don’t remember” and variations thereof. one thousand thirty-seven times. As can be seen, ruler, the situation cannot be proclaimed to have unfolded as desired. Further instructions are necessary. However, we on the ground collectively came to the conclusion that your presence is not crucial. I personally disagree with that assessment and request your presence, as the several persons of power here in the tent watching me write this do not make me feel as safe as I would like to be. I eagerly await your decision. May eternal glory and light of chivalry shine. Wezzok. P.S. My dog died, please come.
