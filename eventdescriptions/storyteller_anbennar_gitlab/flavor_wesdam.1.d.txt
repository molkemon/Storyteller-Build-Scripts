Laurens Silmuna was raised in the High Lorentish court of Madelaire by his mother, Reánna the Second of Madelaire. With Laurens coming of age as the rightful ruler of the Marquisate of Wesdam and Duchy of Madelaire, he has a choice. Continue the ways of his upbringing, fully embracing the high culture of his Lorentish neighbors, or return to the roots of his Damerian ancestors.
