Lencenor is a land filled with conflict, family ties, and internal strife. As it happens, Ruben Siloriel has finally inherited the throne of Kyliande, the ancient duchy held by another house of the blood of Lorenan the Great, the síl Kyliande, direct descendants of the Caylentis kings of Enteben. Now the question is, should he take this opportunity to claim the land directly, surely causing a stir amongst the Lencori nobility and breaking our ancient traditions? Or should he try to be seen as a diplomatic king, and simply allow their autonomy and let the bonds grow stronger? Good things come to those who wait.
