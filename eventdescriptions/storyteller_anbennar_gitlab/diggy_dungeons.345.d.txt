We only have seconds to react. "At my signal, split to the sides" instructs our captain. The pink fish runs, splashing water everywhere. The floor trembles. A water splash is about to reach us. "Split," yells our captain. We do. Everything gets waterblurry. We heard an otherworldly cry and a familiar struggling voice at the same time. The voice repeats itself, "Shoot the damn spears." Recovering our sights, we throw the spears. As they fly, the beast shakes its neck in pain. The captain is nailed to the fish's chest, embracing his spear, just below its throat. The spears land on many bits of the creature's flesh. It cries in pain as it frantically whips its tail. It sweeps some of us. The fish starts running. It tramples over some men. The captain, still hanging to the creature, starts stabbing the neck with his sword. We see the creature trying to run back to its nest, but it falls down to its belly. The turquoise water gets purple as blood gushes from the beast.
