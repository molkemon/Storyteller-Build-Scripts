Tyaphon and Avery kept arguing, while the rest of the party just stood there, frustrated that their supposed leaders did not really know what way they should go. In the meanwhile Milon and Adolohn, an Epednari Explorer, tried to analyze the maps for clues on where they were and where they should go. And not long after they found what had happened. Both Tyaphon and Avery had been dead wrong, as they had completely underestimated some distances which meant that both their turns had not even been passed yet. The two were quite ashamed and Milon and Adolohn revised the maps to make sure that they would not get lost or make miscalculations.
