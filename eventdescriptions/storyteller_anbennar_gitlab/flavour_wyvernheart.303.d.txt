On the second of Castanmark, the last Patrician in the registry was drafted as a Burnblood Candidate. All influence groups have been uprooted from the city; Castonath now lies fully within our grasp, concluded the Hardliner Archauditor. A round of applause resounded in the Council chamber, currently in a requisitioned building while the new Seat of the Council is under construction. The sole Heartmage in attendance then took up the mantle of speaker. "Let us move on to the next issue then. Now that Castonath is ours, how shall we rebuild it? The Castonathi Heartman should suffer our indecision no longer." And one by one, each group presented their project in great detail; the Devouts quickly folded, as Castonath held no real import to them, while no Ambitious Floodborn were in attendance. The Heartmages held no real position on this as long as the city was rebuilt. The three proposals that drew support were the following. The Hardliners desire a centre of administration to centralise the realm. The Wealthy Scions wish to prop up Castonath as the centre of wealth in Wyvernheart, which would fund the Council’s agendas. And a surprising yet popular proposal from the Technocrat Arcanists to create a grand Floodborn Academy to strengthen the council at the roots.
