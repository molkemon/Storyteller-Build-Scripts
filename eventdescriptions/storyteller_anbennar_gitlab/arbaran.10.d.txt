The rise of the realm has been unparalleled over recent decades, and the coffers of the Calasanni Trade Company have swelled to a volume of crowns and goods that few can imagine, even fewer rival. But we have long been aware the industrial magnates north of the Alen might just be one of those very few who do think, see, and plan on the same scale we do. We have known rivals and crushed many underfoot. But we have also longed for companions and allies, where positive cooperation might lead us all to new levels of prosperity. And as the monarch sits down for their first meeting with these Alenic magnates, they can see from the glint in their eyes and the size of their purses that they may very well feel the same.
