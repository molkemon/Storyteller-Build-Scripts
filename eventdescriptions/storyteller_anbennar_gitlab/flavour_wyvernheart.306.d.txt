The council room is in uproar as the representative of the Hardliner Mages finishes stating his case. A handful of councillors can be seen holding their heads, seemingly tired by the situation. While two groups clearly seem at each other's throats, wands at the ready. A few Ambitious Floodborn seem to lead the charge, supported by the scions of noble houses and the Hardliners. They claim that the ruler's recent actions aren't befitting of their position and as such are challenging their rule. On the other side, the Devouts of the Flood, growing in number and influence, chastise their brethren for not being able to see "the Purpose," as they call it. Whereas the Heartmages and Technocrats seem very disinterested in this happening, the Sovereign won’t find support with them. It is up to the Sovereign to calm the tensions in the council room, or ignore them outright; the Sovereign can ignore the Council at their own risk.
