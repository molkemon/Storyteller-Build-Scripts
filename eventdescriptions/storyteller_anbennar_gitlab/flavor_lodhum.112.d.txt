Dwarves are known for their love of a good drink, but some of these surface dwarves have a tendency of going a bit too far. A party held by some visiting surface dwarves has slowly been spinning out of control, and the locals have been complaining about noise and other disruptions for several days now. Now, however, it has gotten truly out of hand, as the partygoers are spilling out into the neighbourhood and causing severe damages.
