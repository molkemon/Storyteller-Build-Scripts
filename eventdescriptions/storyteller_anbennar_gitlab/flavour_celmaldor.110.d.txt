The law guarantees mostly equal pay for all races. Despite what is on the books in black and white and plainly written Elven, the mercantile clans are using non-Elven citizens’ lack of fluency in the Elven language as pretext to pay them less for the same sort of work. This has of course created significant controversy. Whereas Elven is and will remain the national language of Celmaldor, we do have an obligation to permit all that wish to do business with us to do so without suffering stupid sorts of financial loss.
