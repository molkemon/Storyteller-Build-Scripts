Using the drills and techniques found in the ranger captain's logbook, we were able to restore the prestigious Forest Ranger Corps and continue our preparations for an expedition to explore the Deepwoods. The ten thousand brethren trained for two years and are now specialists in tracking, surviving, mapping, covert operations, and warfare on wooden terrain. One of them, Frandhi Flintstone, distinguished himself from all the other trainees as a capable leader and decision maker. We shall appoint him as leader of the expedition force.
