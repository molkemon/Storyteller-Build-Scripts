Mountainshark was led to enlightenment by Glozok; the Darkscale kobolds were led to enlightenment by Nimrith. Though our father was not a dragon, he commanded the fierceness and cunning of one; though Nimrith was not Glozok, he was a father of a nation all the same. Kobolds and goblins have always been similar creatures. We have both been despised for existing and subjugated by all of those around us. We have both been enslaved, and we have both killed our enslavers. We have both had to be violent, cunning, and genius just to survive. Together, with our shared history, it is clear that we should work together. This collaboration shall begin with a shared hoard of gold and mithril in Khugdihr. As we build this hold into evergreater heights, it will be a hold for kobolds and goblins both. Glozok's lessons shall spread to the kobolds. they, too, shall be sharks.
