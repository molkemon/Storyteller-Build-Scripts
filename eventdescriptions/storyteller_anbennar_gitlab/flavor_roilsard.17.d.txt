To gauge Roilsard’s true success, the breadth of their ascendancy, one would have to soar alongside them, many leagues above Halcann. Indeed, it was not clear even to them at first, until money began to flow into their treasury at unheard-of rates, trade and merchandise flooded into Cannor, and their colonies overseas became cities to rival even Anbenncóst in their glory. Uelos’ Lament is now a Roilsardi lake, Aelantier and Sarhal falling under what is often referred to in mercantile parlance as a “thalassocracy” – rule by the sea. Thanks to a combination of colonization, naval expansion, and shrewd trade and diplomacy, Roilsard has truly, finally, become a rose of the seas.
