The Sangamar King smiled as the monarch looked out over the desert. The monarch was standing where Olhokar had once sacrificed his eldest son to save Gophira. The monarch had not known how successful they were, for in saving Gophira, they had saved first the greatest empire of Taychend, and then the greatest empire since the Precursors. The Gophiran Empire now stretched from the cities of the Triarchy to the coast of Soruin, from the sandblasted wastes of Alecand to the plains of the Dry Coast. Taychend had far surpassed the heights of prosperity they reached under the Second Empire. The Ironblood Army was so large as to be uncountable, so powerful as to be undefeatable. By subsuming the warlords, and then unleashing them on the world, the Sangamars had seemingly dominated them. While its borders and buildings expanded, the Third Gophiran Empire was unstoppable. But the ruler knew that blood was like poison to metal, and bloodlust could be poison to their empire. There was a point, halfway through the Second Empire, that it seemed nothing could stop the turning of the Cycle, Gophira’s final victory. Then, Gophira proved that it could stop that victory all by itself. The Second Empire collapsed into a deathspiral of civil war. When the Third Empire’s borders could expand no further, would Gophira’s army then turn inwards for a new source of glory? The title sighed. The monarch supposed that was a question for their descendants, for now, it was all they could do to make sure Gophira did not falter, that it would rise, an Empire of Iron, an Empire Eternal.
