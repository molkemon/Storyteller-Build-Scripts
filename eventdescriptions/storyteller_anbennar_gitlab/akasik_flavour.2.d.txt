The young prince of Khasa, spirited away from his falling kingdom just a scant few months after his birth in fourteen thirty-eight, has grown into a strong and ambitious young man, raised in his family's estate in the mountains of Akasik and vassals of our fiefs in Deshak. He has remained loyal, if vocal about his displeasure of our rule over his adopted kingdom. Today however, we have received worrying reports that he has petitioned our officials across the sea to crown him king of Desha, an act that would not only undermine our own rule, but surely drag us into war. Though we are confident our loyal subjects will make the right decision, there is a nagging suspicion that they may very well take him up on his offer and rebel.
