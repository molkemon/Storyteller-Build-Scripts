A fleet of barges and canoes docks at the river pier, and their representative, a burly middleaged merchantelf with a brown vest and a feathered cap, steps onto the shore to convene with the good folk of that province. He entertains many by talking in a colourful, yet direct language, about the luxuries and wealth his fleet carries throughout the realm, which could be delivered to that province as well, if they agree to but a simple request; every word of his speech accompanied by warm theatrical gestures. The crowd that had formed around him eventually opens up, as the local steward arrives with two guards. The merchantelf simply smiles, and extracts a gift out of his pocket. Later that same day, the merchantelf's request has made its way to the monarch of the country, who promptly responds upon hearing it.
