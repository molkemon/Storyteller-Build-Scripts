Ever since King Jon the Second of Gerwick first cast his covetous eyes on the conquest of Vertesk, one Gawedi monarch after another has sought to expand their realm as far south as the Havoral, and perhaps even beyond. Now that the northern imperial frontier has been occupied by the Gawedi, the Emperor has been forced to act, and has sent a message northward to cow the kings of the Alen. The Emperor’s threat to the ruler of Gawed, sending a bloodied Alenic Eagle carried by a flock of doves to the court of Gaweton, seems not to have had the intended effect of encouraging these occupiers to return imperial land, however. Imperial spies claim even more Gawedi armies had arrived to reinforce their holdings. With no remaining recourse available, the Emperor has rallied the imperial princes, and now they march north with a great host to liberate the northern frontier with steel and sorcery. The Eagle shall fall to the Dove.
