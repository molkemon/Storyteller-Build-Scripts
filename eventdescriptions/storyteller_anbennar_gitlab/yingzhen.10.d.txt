The death of a good ruler is a grave tragedy for any state. Empires crumble, programs falter, and even the most outwardly safe country can descend into anarchy if it was held together only by one person's force of will. No, it is not a great army or a massive fleet that shall spell doom for our enemies; it is the humble knife. It is not the field of battle where blood shall be spilled, but the royal chambers.
