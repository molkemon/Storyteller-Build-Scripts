During the War of the Sorcerer King, the legendary elven troubadour Alarian Singkeep took the fort that would later be known as Seinathíl, using little more than music, a little magic, and his trusty enchanted harp, Swansong. It has been many moons since that war, and although the school Alarian founded still holds his harp, on display, time has aged the beautiful instrument. It has lost much of its luster and is little more than an artifact of a bygone era, broken and unusable. But nothing can be broken forever, and after a little work, Swansong is as beautiful as it once was, with new enchantments and dame-reinforced strings. The legendary harp is now ready for our line to use. However, it must still be activated, and needs to be wielded and tamed by a truly worthy musician. It is said that the weapon chooses the wielder, but in this case it almost seems like that is literally true. We need to be enchanting before we can even hope to use Swansong to its full potential.
