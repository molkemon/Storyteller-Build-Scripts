Though we often receive various envoys and diplomats from the kingdoms to the west, we have received what may be the most unique envoy to ever enter the lands of Corintar. A group of ork mercenaries hailing from the far north have arrived into our land recently. Their captain asked for a private audience with the famous hero of the Greentide and our former leader, Lothane Bluetusk, who is living peacefully in Corintar, advising local rulers, but not ruling the country directly. During this meeting, the ork captain revealed that the ruler is none other than the Warchief of the Gray orks and descendant of Brasur Frozenmaw, leader of the Graytide, and the ruler offers Lothane alliance and mutual support. Having aided us against their green brethren during our wars with Dookanson, the Frozenmaw clan are still orks, and many of our people still harbor grudges and fears of their kind. If we ally with them, some may say that we are traitors to humankind, but a lot of halforks from Grombar may join our clans. Furthermore, the strength of the Gray orks shouldn’t be underestimated.
