Recently, some explorers have found quite the sight. a field full of beautiful flowers of every color, each one taller than a man. They say those in the field of flowers seem to experience time slower, as well; as if the flowers have some effect on their minds. Clearly, these flowers are some form of magic. And no magic can be permitted in Corinsfield. Red Sentries armed with torches and oil are on standby. As soon as they have the goahead, they will march forth and remove this blight from the world, as well as anything that may inhabit it. All they need is the confirmation of the Crimson Reverend.
