Trade over large distances can be risky, if profitable. The weather, brigands, and pirates remain a threat on land as well as on water. Normally, the merchants can cope with a certain amount of mishaps and have their own ways to protect themselves and insure their cargo. Lately, however, things have been going badly for a large number of merchant families, and some are at risk of bankruptcy. It may be prudent to offer some financial compensation to get them back on their feet.
