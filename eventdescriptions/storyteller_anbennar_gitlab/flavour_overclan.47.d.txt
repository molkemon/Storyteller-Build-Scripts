When I first arrived in the domain, I had been promised a fertile audience for the message of Corin. Every day I have tried to preach to these people, yet they continue to test my patience. They argue incessantly about minutiae, sometimes with each other more so than even with myself. I swear to the gods that the other day, I nearly saw two men come to blows over a discussion on the merit of lentils. In most quarters, it appears that Corin's word has little room for purchase here. Fortunately, this has not proven universal. While the local people themselves have been largely resistant to my efforts, the local gnoll population has proven much more receptive. In some ways, I have come to understand this to be the fertile audience I was enticed to preach to initially. While they can be uncouth and even violent at times, they also seem to be able to respect strength; something Corin always wielded well. I hope to show them that strength is nothing if not wielded righteously; though I've found I'm not the only missionary frequenting the gnollish communities as of late. Adeanics, gnomes. I daresay I even saw a harimari or two wandering about as well. Even if they listen, my work here is far from over.
