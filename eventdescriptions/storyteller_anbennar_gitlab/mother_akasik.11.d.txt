In Akasi society, the aspect of compassion means many things, from the increase in diplomacy with those outside of Akasik, to the deposition of tyrants, but the most awaited one by the most Akasi is without a doubt the general increase in charity. Any virtuous person must of course be charitable so long as they can afford to, and the more you can afford, the more you should contribute. The state is no exception on this matter, and it is expected that the state and institutions that uphold it, as the most powerful pillars of society, will be the ones to grant the most significant contributions.
