Victory. We have shown the weaklings of the Taychend and Kalavend their place—beneath us—and how exalted a feeling it is. We decimated their armies with our superior force of arms. Our BoneBound equipment was more than enough to smash through their pathetic so-called weapons, a quality that became clearer the more we used it. Having done away with their soldiers, we pillaged their lands. We took the best for our own use and left the rest to rot. In this manner we have proven our superiority, and the reality that our defeated foes refused to accept. that might makes right, and strength is the law of the world.
