The monarch thirstily gulps down several mouthfuls of water, throat parched from long hours of oration to the town that their retinue was currently visiting. It may not be the most dignified way of recruitment, but who could argue with the effectiveness of the noble and virtuous Knight of Adenica personally paying a visit during a recruitment drive? The strategy had been wildly successful, but enough was enough. It was time to get back to more kingly duties. There was time enough for one more visit, however. Where should they go?
