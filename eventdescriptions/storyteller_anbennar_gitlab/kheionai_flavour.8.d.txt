Though known for his leadership, a former Kylakas has ended up living in a barrel. This sight brought many to question the state of the city, though as it had turned out he chose to live in that barrel. Nowadays he is known for openly preaching his way of thinking; he calls himself an Ulótholist, which is in reference to a centuries old philosopher known as Ulóthopholes whose work is still studied to this day. The man living in a barrel believes that to live a fully good and honest life, one must reject the material and live a life devoid of material possessions. Although crude and offensive, this man would prove to be a boon for the state and could bring with him much experience. The problem with this is however that he won’t accept employment, thus we’ll need to send advisors and bureaucrats to just ask him questions.
