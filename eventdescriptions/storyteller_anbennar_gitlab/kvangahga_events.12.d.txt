While scavenging an ancient tomb, one of our mystic artisans has uncovered a hoard of enchanted artefacts from the time of the Genie Sultanates, which they have duly gifted to our monarch. It would be a great honour for the ruler to reforge these arcane items themselves, but perhaps by involving others in the process we could learn much about the techniques used by the ancients. Or we could always sell them to the highest bidder, though this will likely be frowned upon.
