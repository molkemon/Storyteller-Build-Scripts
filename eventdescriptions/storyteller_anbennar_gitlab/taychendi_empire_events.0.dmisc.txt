It is normal for Taychendi rulers to model their reigns off those of past rulers, so that they have both a guideline for which policies to pursue in order to gain their own place in the heavens, and so that they can patronise a solid selection of rulers to help them do so. In the course of a ruler’s life, however, they encounter many challenges that may encourage them to change direction – perhaps it is time for the current monarch to do so as well?
