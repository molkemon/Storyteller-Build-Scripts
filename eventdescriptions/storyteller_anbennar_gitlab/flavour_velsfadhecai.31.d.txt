In an odd move, several prominent officials in our nation have received letters from their counterparts in Vels Bacar. These letters contain nothing of substance beyond expletives, crude drawings of figures in increasingly lewd poses, and rather violent insults about the recipient's family and friends. Although the veracity of these letters cannot be verified, our monarch has been inundated with demands to end our Iosahar agreement with Vels Bacar immediately, and abandon them to their fate.
