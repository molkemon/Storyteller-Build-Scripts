My monarch, good news from the Yenaze Mezhi. This year’s harvest has come in, and our reports show that we have a substantial surplus. Our sizeable fields are lush with healthy grain, bolstered by the Samayebaka's prayers. The people are wellfed and happy, as our granaries can provide a considerable amount of grain as a stipend to the poor and needy, as well as to our military, should they go off on campaign. This has allowed our populace to thrive, and many predict we will see a moderate increase in the population should this continue.
