A small group of kobolds in that province have been found manufacturing weapons with the power to kill a grown elf, then selling them to the peasantry with the supposed purpose of vermin removal. By technicality, they aren’t breaking any laws, as our edicts forbid the unlicensed production of guns on the land and on the Ynn, but say nothing about the production of guns in caves. However, this minutia of the law matters little to the nobility, who are demanding the kobolds be stopped from this underground activity, as any reasonable noble knows that an armed peasant is a recipe for disaster.
