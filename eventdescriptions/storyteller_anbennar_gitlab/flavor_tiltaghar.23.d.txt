It is done. Rahen knows calm once more, or at least it begins to. The lands of the second Harimraj may still stand divided, for now, but they shall soon accept a future of unity and of greatness. There is still much to do if Rahen is to recover its past glory, let alone exceed such lofty heights, but for once, it feels possible. For if the North has shown anything, it is that there is no enemy great enough to stand against the might of a true Raj.
