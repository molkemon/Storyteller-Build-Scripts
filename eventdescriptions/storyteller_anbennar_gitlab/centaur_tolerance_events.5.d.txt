Over the last few weeks, several landowners in the realm complained about strange scenes on their properties. broken fences, trodden fields, and squash vegetables. Not long after these mysterious vandalisms, local authorities wrangled the perpetrators. a group of centaurs who had migrated through the area. Charged with crimes of trespassing and property damage, the centaurs countered with how the landowners mar the land and divide life under the sky’s domain. a mockery of the right to live unfettered and free. They instead request we codify a common right of passage so that all people, not just centaurs, may move about without worrying about dividing barriers or private land. We could step in to mediate this local affair, but we risk alienating one of the offended parties. What shall we do?
