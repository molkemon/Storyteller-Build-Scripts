As Steward of the Sorrow, the monarch has to withstand the many problems of managing both the Khets and the populace of Kheterata. One of these responsibilities includes being Khets' private secretary, constantly receiving messages about many little inconveniences that need to be fixed or provided. In one of these messages today, the Steward was told by a Khet that he needs fencing lessons and that he only trusts the Steward to be his teacher.
