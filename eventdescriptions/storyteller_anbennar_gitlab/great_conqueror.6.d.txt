What little trade we had with the vast and wild lands of the Forbidden Plains has crept entirely to a halt in recent months. What few have managed to escape have told us why. The centaurs are unifying under a new Great Caehn, and the Forbidden Plains have erupted into a terrible war. The grasses weep blood, they say. The peals of thunder across the plains are drowned out by the screams of the dying, and the wails of the soon-to-be dead. There has been war for months upon months, but the Great Caehn is gaining strength, and soon the centaurs, all of the centaurs alive, will spill out beyond the Forbidden Plains. They will come for us. First they will send us messengers. Then they will send us scouts. Then they will send us a horde without horizon, and our lands will suffer the same fate as the Forbidden Plains.
