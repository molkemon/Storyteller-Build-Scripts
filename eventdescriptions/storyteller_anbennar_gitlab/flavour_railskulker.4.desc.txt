It started with some minor increases in volunteers. A few hundred goblins joined the technocrats. Over a few months, this number has increased severely, and now thousands upon thousands have joined. Almost every village and city now has a council to advise and help with administration. There has truly been a large explosion of the councils. The clan boss seems to be happy with the change as well, luckily. It increases our production of resources like iron and mushrooms. Additionally, the chances of a revolt are reduced because now people can join the council or talk to them to achieve their wishes. All in all, this unbelievably large explosion in councils will only be beneficial for our society.
