The assorted desert tribes of Sirtan have long been a problem for the Lupulan. Severed from their connection to the spirits, they have long sent raiders south out of spite to loot their villages and kill their people. Their experience hiding in the desert wastes means that they cannot ever truly be subdued, so an all-out conquest would be both fruitless and painful. However, a little bit of effort increasing the Lupulan’s defenses through jungle garrisons so that the northern tribes can put up a better defense might go a long way towards protecting the entire Lupulan. Political support has recently grown among the other tribes for such an effort. Should they seize the opportunity?
