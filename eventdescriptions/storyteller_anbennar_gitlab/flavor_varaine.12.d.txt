As the academy grows ever more important, it becomes clearer and clearer that the institution is unable to properly attend to all of Varainé's ambitions as it stands. With so much research to be done and so many novice alchemists to train, it is only natural that an expansion to the academy be built. With this expansion, we find the opportunity to create new research branches, most notably into fields which, despite being adjacent to the institution's main alchemical focus, would still greatly benefit the nation and the alchemy industry.
