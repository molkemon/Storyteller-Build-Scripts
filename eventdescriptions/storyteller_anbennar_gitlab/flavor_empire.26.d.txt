Imperial Law, both ancient and modern, delineates the rights of mages, but we never had to worry about defining what a mage is until recently. Artificery allows anyone to use magic, even without any innate ability. This has long caused anxiety among our magical class, who have sponsored a series of reactionary laws to heavily limit the practice of Artificery. Tensions over these laws have been building for years, but today they reached a flash point. the artificers of the capital have gathered before the palace in peaceful protest. They have set up stands and stalls to show off all manner of strange contraptions, and impede the passage of any traveler as they show off how incredible and potentially useful their inventions are. While this impromptu fair persists, movement through the center of town, and in or out of most government buildings, is nigh impossible. Their terms are clear. rescind the previous restrictions on the Artificers, or the roads will remain closed indefinitely. The monarch must make a decision quickly, before our administration grinds to a halt.
