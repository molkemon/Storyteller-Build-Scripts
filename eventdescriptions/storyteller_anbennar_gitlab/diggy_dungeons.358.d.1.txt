A couple of our miners start preparing some explosive charges. In the meantime, we leave them be. We relax and rest. Then, we are disturbed by a loud explosion. Metal pieces fly in different directions, as well as one miner's flesh. The other miner is bleeding out, legless, and crying. Other crew members are affected too. One is on fire, another lost an arm, and another one is bleeding a lot from her face. We grab and treat the wounded. Once the discord ends, we notice how a hole in the gate was made by the explosion. A larger chamber is seen on the other side. Crouching a little, we can still pass through it.
