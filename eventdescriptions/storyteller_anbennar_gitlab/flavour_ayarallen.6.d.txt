Although different in size, scope, and power, the essence of all queendoms in the history of Bulwar stems from the same source. Many think this source to be the sky, as they dance freely among the clouds and soar above the heads of others. But the sky cannot be claimed by one queendom, for all harpies share in the freedom to fly wherever they choose; the sky can never be shackled, for that would mean to shackle all harpykind. Instead, queendoms draw their spirit from the earth, and the people who live and work below their wings. the humans, elves, and other species who have come together and built the lands of Bulwar into something that may be enjoyed by all, a garden of knowledge and prosperity shining in the night. Through hard work and mutual trust, they have come together with these people and formed a truly beautiful state, where all within may enjoy the fruits of the garden. Their mastery of the realm has surpassed the idea of harpies as disparate raiders, sacking the land and leaving the people behind to suffer. They are now wardens, custodians, and caretakers, bringing Bulwar under their wing and guiding it forward. In taking flight towards a new age, harpies have defined themselves as guardians of the land and its people, and as such together they decree a new name. Harpylen, the Fourth Queendom, and the rightful stewards of Bulwar. The world shall know their radiance, and their people will bask in the light of their glory.
