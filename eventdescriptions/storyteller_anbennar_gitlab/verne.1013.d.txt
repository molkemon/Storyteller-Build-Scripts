You’re thinking we went there on some grand tip, given what we found. It wasn’t like that. It was just a lake in a place it shouldn’t have been, right at the center of a desert. Sometimes the little mysteries are enough; sometimes the curiosity that kills the cat is damn idle. The way we saw it, there was no way the thing was natural. Magic written all over it. But it wasn’t doing anything. I was a bad investment, see. No great researcher, no great delver. I wandered around the Vernissage at the bottom of a bottle, getting bare credit on artifact analysis so I wouldn’t lose my stipend. My supervisor Arak made it real clear to me. help me discharge a favor to a friend, because the guy can’t get the bug out of his ear about this damn lake, and I’ll ignore you staggering along Bordello Street bringing shame to the institution. That’s how I wound up in that wasteland, ole Sally doing her level best to bake the shit right out of me. I won’t pretend I was a quartermaster for the ages, but I really didn’t want to die there.
