The ruler takes a pinch of the powder offered to the monarch by their slave, and sniffs it. It is fragrant; the ruler has tasted it before on both fish and meat, and knows it is delicious. "And this spice, you can grow it here?" the ruler asks. "Yes, of course. Van is the perfect place for it." The halflings were until recently a distant curiosity to the harimari; strange short men from across the sea, who grew exotic spices sold by middlemen in the markets of Madriamilak. With Vandipha's recent conquests, though, halfling slaves have been brought into their grasp, and they have discovered just how skilled they are as gardeners. Some of them even claim they can grow a rare spice called 'cloves' on Van. Cloves are quite expensive, and this would doubtless bring Vandipha great wealth. This is what obedience to one's betters should look like; all under the High Gods is in order.
