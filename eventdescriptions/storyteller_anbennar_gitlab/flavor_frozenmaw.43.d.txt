What hated years have come upon the noble sons of the Reach, to live so abjectly under orkish yoke? What options lie open to us? Since Brasûr Frozenmaw swept north and laid waste the kingdom of Vrorenmarch, the Reach has been a great satrapy of the orks. We have watched as our fellow Reachmen have largely knuckled under, and we too have nominally accepted the overlordship of this strange tyrant, whose absurd claims at civilization involve the wholesale intermarriage of human and ork nobility. Well, some may accept such a fate, but we will not. Frida Vrorenson may be the captive wife of Brasûr, an insult in and of itself, but not every relative of the Vrorensons lies dead or inert. We have maintained discreet contacts with the ruling family of Adshaw, whose relative Crovis is a nephew of the Captive Queen. The light of justice shines in the young man’s eyes, and he has already agreed to gather support for a rebellion to restore Vrorenmarch. In the meantime, we stout Reachmen must prepare the way. Discretion is the better part of valour, and what is demanded of us is patience and steady work. When Crovis arrives in the Reach, he will find a people prepared to fight with him, true Reachmen unwilling to be orkish thralls.
