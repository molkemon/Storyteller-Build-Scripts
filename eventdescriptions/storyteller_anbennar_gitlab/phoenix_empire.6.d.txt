To the west, beyond the mighty seas west of the Divenhal, lies Aelantier, the ancient homeland of the elven people. There, across the sea, several Bulwari expeditions have been established, intent on carving out new realms upon the western continents, and intent too upon spreading the light of Surael to our old homeland. It is, therefore, of vital importance that we bring these realms under the sway of the Phoenix Empire, to prevent them from leaving Surael’s embrace. The first step shall be the establishment of an exclave on the continent, to serve as a staging ground for any future expeditions. Already, a suitable target has been selected, and both our armies and navies ready themselves for yet another expedition, this time beyond the edges of the old world.
