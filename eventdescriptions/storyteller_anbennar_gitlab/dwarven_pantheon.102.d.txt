Long ago, during the time of AulDwarov, our ancestors worshipped the ancient Pantheon which consisted of many gods. However, when AulDwarov fell, our ancestors believed that the gods had abandoned them and subsequently gave up their faith. But as we continue to reclaim our ancestral home, there are those who wish to revive the old faith, despite the objections of the Stonekeepers. They propose that perhaps it was our ancestors who abandoned the gods instead. The Stonekeepers demand that we authorize the burning of these individuals who are considered heretics for such blasphemy. We cannot avoid taking a side any longer. Action must be taken.
