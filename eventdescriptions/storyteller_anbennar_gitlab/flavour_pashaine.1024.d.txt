The Emperor’s lifeless body is found in the early hours of the morning. His face has been left recognisable, but practically every other part of his body has been mutilated by what are clearly arcane methods. one limb burned with the carbonised absoluteness of magefire, another snapfrozen but utterly dry, another encased in smooth rock. It is a message. the mages can reach anyone. And they will stop at nothing to deny Pashainé the aid of the Empire.
