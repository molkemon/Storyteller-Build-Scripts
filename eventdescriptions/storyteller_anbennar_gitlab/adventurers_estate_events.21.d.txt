Adventurers. The heroes of countless tales across Halann and the perennial headache of those in power. Heralding from all walks of life, an adventurer could be a youth avenging their lost village or a priest seeking a lost temple or a wayward heir heeding the call of adventure. Or so our own heir so claims. While they are perhaps not wrong, they conveniently forget to mention the risk adventurers face every day. The risks have consequences that would not just affect our heir but also our realm.
