Potentially concerning reports are emerging from the coastal lands adjoining Rezankand. Due to having to eke out a meagre existence in a dry and barren land, and their constant struggle against the strange incursions of the jungle to the north, the Oono’i are nearly extinct, and may require direct assistance to survive in any form. The more altruistically inclined in our council advocate a generalized rescue operation to prevent any further loss of kinfolk, but more pragmatic voices claim it is simply not worth the effort. A few opportunists suggest, on the other hand, that accelerating their decline may even be beneficial. What should be done?
