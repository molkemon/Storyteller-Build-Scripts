As I move through the palace halls, I step past the various minor halls that have been constructed; the library, the ballroom, the laboratories, the apartments, the bazaar, the stables, the treasury, and the temple. As I make my way deeper into the palace, I notice a pair of labourers transporting furniture through the corridor, as with a palace as large as this one, there is always more work to be done. The furnishings and features are numerous.
