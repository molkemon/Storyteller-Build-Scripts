There is a place in Sarhal where the fog lies deep. It licks at the stony hem of palaces and settles in the alleyways. One cannot always keep it out of doors. It is said that the fog itself listens to what you say, that one can never be sure of privacy in a town to which the fog has arrived. Stranger still, it is said that the fog was invited; that a realm steeped in the corruption of the three hags of Yezel Mora has pledged itself entirely to their cause. Yet it is not the hags who rule in this befogged country, as they rule Yezel Mora. Instead the Wise Three have sent an apprentice, a daughter in shadow, a figure referred to only as the Vile One. Possessing terrible arcane power of her own, this Vile One advances the agenda of the hags in her own right, and has taken direct command of her inheritance. A new agent of shadow menaces Sarhal, and all rightthinking people tremble at the prospect.
