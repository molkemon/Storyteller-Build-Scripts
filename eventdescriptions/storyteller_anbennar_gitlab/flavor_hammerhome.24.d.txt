With the escape of the dwarves after the ork attack, they ran, and as natural sprinters, eventually outran the orks, and hid in a large stone manor found in the city. But the dwarves, while setting up barricades in the manor, found something amazing. the symbol of Balgar. The dwarves, intrigued, began scouring the whole manor, eventually finding a small room full of blueprints and plans. This was obviously Balgar's manor. The plans in the room detailed various projects which Balgar wished to complete, but that he seemingly had scrapped. The dwarves, amazed by this discovery, soon bagged all of the blueprints into their bags, and set off back to the Silverdocks.
