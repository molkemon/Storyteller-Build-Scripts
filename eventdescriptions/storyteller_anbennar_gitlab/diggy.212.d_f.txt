Faceted so masterfully that it seems to absorb and pulsate with light, this enormous diamond, also known as the Aegis of the Serpentreach, was the crown jewel of Argordstun, and is said to exert a hold on those who gaze into its depths. Whether such tales are true or false, it is undeniable that it has a storied history. As Auldwarov was being forged, Argordstun pledged support and handed the Aegis over as a display of faith in the High King and the Assembly. It was lost after Auldwarov fell, of course, but is now found, and is likely to strengthen our claims to rule the caverns of the Serpentreach.
