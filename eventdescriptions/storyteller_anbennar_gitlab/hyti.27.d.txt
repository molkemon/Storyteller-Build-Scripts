In our quest to find the best substrate for inscription spells, we haven't given much thought to what it takes to get there; only how well it works has traditionally mattered. It seems the way we currently smelt the Anniwan alloy produces an enormous amount of smoke; so much that we have started to receive indignant protest letters from neighboring countries, whose border folk fear the fumes' impact and resent the stench and the haze on the horizon. We can't change the production method now; it will take us back years. Is there some other way we can get them off our case?
