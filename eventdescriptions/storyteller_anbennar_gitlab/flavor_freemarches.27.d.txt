A magistrate in a village to the east has written to the court seeking advisement on a legal question. A young Freemarcher has taken up a Cursed One wife, and they have managed to conceive a child. Now he is seeking permission to bring his wife and child to live with him in the Freemarches. While there are no explicit laws preventing Cursed Ones from living among us, many will not approve of clemency towards them. It is simply unheard of that one would even attempt to live amongst us.
