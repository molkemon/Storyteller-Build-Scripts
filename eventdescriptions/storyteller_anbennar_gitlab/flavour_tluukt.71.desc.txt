Our blood pact lies in tatters, our other half lost to the sands of the Salahad, put low by the loathsome warriors of Jaddari. The enduring legacy of our Matriarchy is now beset on all sides by rebellious flocks and by the coming storm of Jaddari warriors. Hope seems lost, yet in the west another gnoll pack has been gathering power and making overtures of alliance, first with Zokka and now with us. They promise us more than merely a restoration of Harpy supremacy over the Salahad. They promise us revenge.
