As the heir settles into their new life within the palace, they become acutely aware of the perception surrounding their role. Many in the court view them with skepticism and consider them to be an ineffectual figurehead, someone easily manipulated or discarded when the time is right. This perspective is, of course, quite different from the powerful and knowledgeable entity they once were. However, the heir chooses to embrace this newfound persona willingly. They pretend to be carefree and prone to making mistakes, cultivating an image of someone who poses no real threat and is content to let others take the reins of leadership. The contrast with their previous existence is striking, and they find solace in the simplicity of this life, where they can relax and even make mistakes without dire consequences. Despite their seemingly carefree facade, the heir hasn't forgotten the ritual that brought them back from the brink of some unknown fate. They feel a deep sense of gratitude and respect toward the young heir whose life was sacrificed for their rebirth. In many ways, they see it as a second chance at life, an opportunity to experience the world anew. With their ancient knowledge and formidable magical abilities, the heir knows that they possess power that far surpasses those around them. However, they bide their time, choosing not to reveal the extent of their capabilities. Instead, they observe the close family members and court members who hold significant influence over the nation. In this new chapter of their existence, the heir treads carefully, waiting for the right moment to make their move and perhaps reshape the fate of the kingdom. For now, they continue to play the role of the seemingly unassuming and carefree heir, all the while plotting and planning behind the scenes.
