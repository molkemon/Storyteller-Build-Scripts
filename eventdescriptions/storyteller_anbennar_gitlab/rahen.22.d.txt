The city of Tiltaghar is bustling with many travelers coming to town. Though not as well maintained as it once was, the Tiger Ziggurat still stands mighty in the middle of the city, welcoming those seeking their own moral path in life; its stone blazes like the sun in the morning, and its presence calms troubled hearts and minds. For many, a visit to the ziggurat is just the start of a religious journey, an ancient tradition of pilgrimage between the two holy sites of Tiltaghar and Tilathi. With its magnificence and importance, the ziggurat demanded great care and maintenance from those ruling its land. While there are visitors aplenty to be taxed, it is expected for the lord to maintain the site in reverence to the High Gods.
