With our newfound mastery of the necromantic arts, a protracted debate over the form of our undead legions has divided the council. Our mages argue that future wars must be approached in terms of raw numbers. Any future conquests will see us facing vast armies of enemy infantry, providing us in turn with an endless supply of humanoid corpses to throw at our foes. However, such a sudden shift to an infantry-focused army would be difficult, to say the least. We have always fought as cavalry, leveraging speed and size to outflank and overpower our foes. To abandon centuries of tactical and strategic experience is a bitter brew to swallow, and many of our advisors argue is not worth the cost.
