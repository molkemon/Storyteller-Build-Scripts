Arfil told Bricena that they needed to be in Salla Cenág urgently, and the two of them hopped on hawk riders and rode off, with some guards following them, of course. When they got there, there was no emergency, but Ceanna Cenag played dumb for a minute, asking how they could know that he had asked them to meet him. Surely, his letter could not have arrived more than a few days ago, but they were already there. In other words, setting some hawks aside for diplomats would be a major diplomatic boon.
