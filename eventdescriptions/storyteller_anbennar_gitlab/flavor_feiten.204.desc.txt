The Jellyfish Coast, it seems, has not always abut the seas as Yanshen's craggy shield from the waves. The oldest murals we know of in our remaining High Temples depict dry land that would stretch for legions beyond the current confluence between water and soil, but no one has ever proven the truth of those depictions. Until now. In dredging Feiten Bay, our ships have unearthed a wealth of ruins and artifacts clearly indicative of a vastly superior civilization. Proof of the High Gods from before their ascension, perhaps? Either way, our Shuimu engineers rejoice at a source of relics so close to home.
