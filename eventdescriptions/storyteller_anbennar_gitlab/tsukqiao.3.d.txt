There is a time of great joy amongst the Beiklings. A dream almost fifteen hundred years in the making is now done; the masks of many faces have been united, and our realm of yore has been reforged. But there is still much to do. The doctrine of might believes that only absolute power can guarantee that the empire will not fall again, and to do so, we must build a dynasty that is able to endure all catastrophes. We will tell a thousand lies if that brings unity.
