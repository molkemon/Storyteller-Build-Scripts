The monarch steps into the arena wielding the Gaohernlangh, a massive curved greatsword with a long wooden handle, favored mainly due to its destructive slashing power. From the opposite end of the arena, the monarch hears a familiar voice. "Beating you will be my next triumph, Caehn." The voice belonged to the clansman with the large ear necklace, collected from his past enemies. Scoffing at his insolence, the monarch charges at the accomplished warrior; however, it seems that the monarch fell right into the warrior’s trap. Easily dodging the initial charge and taking initiative, the warrior masterfully tore into the Caehn’s defense. Feeling that victory is close, the warrior pushed a bit too far, and the Caehn used it to their advantage. Now with even ground, and fatigue setting in, the fight seemed to be in a stalemate, but eventually…
