This would be finally the night he'd put those vermin in their place. After years forced to live near them, every day hearing the noises they called a language, every night dealing with the clamor they'd make in their drunken stupor, he would be done with them. Each one of his mates had a story to tell. who got assaulted for a simple word, who had their daughters harassed by their youngsters. One of those things had the audacity of opening shop right in front of his business, stealing half of his clients. And now it had come out that their race was responsible for the state our nation is in, the rising taxes, the increased prices? Tonight he'd get back what he's owed, and with interest. Horror. My Liege, the peasants of that province have assembled a mob and torched the biggest minority district of the city. Hundreds lie dead; how could such a thing happen? These fanatics… this is why we need to keep the plebs down; if a small hint that other races might not be perfect is enough to turn them all into assassins, they can't be trusted with anything.
