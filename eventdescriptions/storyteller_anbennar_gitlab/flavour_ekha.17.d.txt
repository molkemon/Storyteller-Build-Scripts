They are little more than vultures, in truth. A representative of the common people begins. They come off as all refined and dandy, I tell you, but they are soulless below the skin, bribing all the folks who come here and killing the ones that do not comply. These city folk have made the domain theirs, running all the work down here while you petty folk argue in the rich parts. I am thankful to the Deshati for letting me up here. I have seen most of the stuff they did with some of my mates, and I have proof for you to judge. skulls and bones plenty. I know where they disposed of them, and some of my friends down in the thieves guild have some letters among these rich folk. They are chilling when you read them; you will see what I mean. Some try to engage here and make ourselves heard, but it never reaches. I am sure you know why by now, but I am telling you, the number of bright people that come here trying to make better for the lot of us is not small.
