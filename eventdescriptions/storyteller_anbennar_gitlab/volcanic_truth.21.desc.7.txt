The matter of the mechanim is decided. The mechanim may come from a past long faded into obscurity, but their future is theirs to decide. We must engage with the mechanim in a spirit of open inquiry, so that the change they bring is not overshadowed by our preconceptions. They may live in our communities as kin. We will share the history of Kanolowele with them so that they may teach it to others of their kind. But now that we have decided what to think of the mechanim's past, the Volcanic Truth must look now to the future. The mechanim have a place within Insyaa, now and forever, and thus they shall live beneath the gaze of the Twin Gods just as we do. They will work towards change within themselves and within the wider world, as is their duty.
