As we are Harimari, it is vital to ensure our nation remains ruled by Harimari, in order to keep our delicate caste system in place. However, due to a variety of circumstances, it seems that our heir is not one of the glorious Harimari. We now face a choice. disinherit them and put a proper Harimari in their stead, or keep them and deal with the instability that follows?
