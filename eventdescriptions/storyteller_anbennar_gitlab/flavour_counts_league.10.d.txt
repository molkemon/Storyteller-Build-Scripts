Balgar built this city, on rock and stone. It is one of the great wonders, and even without meaning to, these old stones have power. Now that the city is restored, we are busy resettling it, and those who make their homes here repeatedly report that they just feel safer without being able to put anything concrete down as the reason why. It is a strange occurrence, and a much needed and welcome one. We have not had any reports of absences from within the limits of Castonath either. Whether it is wards in the stone or just the nature of it being old and being considered protective that diminishes the subtle magics and whimsy of the fey, we can only be grateful. As many people as possible must be moved in, ahead of schedule.
