Tombs are more often than not built outside of holds, in order to save space for the living. This does have the drawback that it is not easy to keep them all safe. The Dwarovar has never been safe outside of the gates of a hold, and recently, some rampaging monsters have gravely defaced the grave of their ancestors. Whether it was ettins, minotaurs or some other large creature, the result is the same, and a lot of carefully crafted dwarven stonework lies in ruin.
