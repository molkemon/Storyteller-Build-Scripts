When Jaher first took the easternmost city in Haless, it became clear that it would make a fine center from which his eastern holdings could be administered. To that end, he built the Hall of the Rising Sun, a palace from which he, or his governor, could rule over the city and the Yan region. Now that we have held the city for a time, we have seen fit to repair that old palace, converting it into a branch of the convocation that we might cement our rule where Jaher never could.
