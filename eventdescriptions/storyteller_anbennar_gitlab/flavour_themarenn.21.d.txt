A great Esmari tradition, one of widespread renown, are the River Parties. These parties almost never stop; day and night barges can be spotted slowly sailing downriver from all towns. During a river party, none is the wiser; emperors, beggars, criminals, and priests are all subject to a drunken fight or a scandalous affair. But the river parties can be so much more for those who are the wiser, those who know how to please an important guest. We have in our possession both the diplomatic and monetary capital to take advantage of these River Parties. And so we must leverage this to our advantage and invite dignitaries not only from the Empire but far and wide to be our guests, using the opportunity to strengthen our ties abroad.
