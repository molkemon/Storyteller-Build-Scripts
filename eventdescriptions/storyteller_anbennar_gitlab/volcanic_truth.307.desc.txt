We have changed everything we can think of, and yet the Twin Gods are not satisfied. It must be the case that there is still more that needs to be changed. nothing is too insignificant or impractical to be considered. We must change the routes of our roads, and the positions of the buildings along them. We must change the structure of our language, and the very concepts it means to express. Then we must change it all again, until the Twin Gods are satisfied.
