With bated breath, the crowd gathers on the precipice of the seaside cliff. Beside them is the latest third generation Zongminclass airship, tethered to the rocks while the first batch of coal, extracted from the mines of Luogang, was piled into the combustion chambers after a brief treatment by the artificers. A group of mages were on standby, calming the constant winds that normally battered these cliffsides, but ready to release their spells once the balloon was airborne. The ruler stood alone on the deck of the airship, gazing out at the Feiten Bay in front of them. No one could tell what the ruler was thinking, their face a mask of stern determination; once everything was ready, they gave the signal. As the black ore in the combustion chamber was ignited, a rising heat filled the silk balloon. The hull began to lift, the electroplated metal of the surface glinting in the sunlight. As the dockhands on the ground untied the ropes, the airship lifted swiftly into the air; when the mages unleashed their own wind magics, the Zongmin airships surged forwards along the coastline, propelled by their Caged Firebird Engines. The crowd on the ground erupted into celebration, as the normally cautious jellyfish engineers jumped higher and cheered louder than anyone else. From the deck, the ruler looked down at the increasingly distant takeoff platform; finally, he allowed himself to smile. Today, Feiten conquered the sky once more.
