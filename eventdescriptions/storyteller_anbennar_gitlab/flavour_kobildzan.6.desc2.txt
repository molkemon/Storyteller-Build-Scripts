Another Gommo voyage is chartered for Haraf, and it leaves without so much as a goodbye. Relying on Gommo connections which stretch a thousand years into the past, the previous divisions within the gnomes—the struggles between Cliff versus Creek, and of state versus Gommo—have fallen to the wayside. In their place, two camps of gnomes have emerged. those who embrace us and the dragons, and those who hold fast to the Thought and the Thought alone. Even the latter group, though, still has connections with our people. They study the dragons, but do not worship them; they find potential in Ascension, even if they do not desire it exactly how we do. In Haraf, they consort with goblins and kobolds alike, finding collaborators and rivals in Zurzumexia and Mestikardu. The southwestern peninsula of North Aelantier becomes a hotbed for artificery, unified by one thing and one thing alone. an opposition to our rule.
