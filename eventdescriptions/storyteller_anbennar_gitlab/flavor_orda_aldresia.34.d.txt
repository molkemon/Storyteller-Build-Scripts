Adria, if you are reading this letter, then I thank you for giving me the modicum of respect that I do not deserve. It has been long, and I have been absent, both in mind and presence. My duty has called me, but it is no excuse. For twenty years, as the war raged on, as you have grown up, now I bet a promising and striking young woman. Whatever hardship you have encountered or success you have earned, I am sorry for not being there. I know my apologies mean nothing to you, but regardless know that even absent your forgiveness I will make it up to you. I had nearly given up once Adénn died, knowing I had failed him, my brothers, my Empire, and of course you and your mother. I was not there for her funeral; my shame was too great, and that is a sin for me to bear alone. She was always the better half of me. That night we danced when we met, I knew all the eyes were on her, curious how I had caught her attention. Truth be told, I was always curious about that myself. I am coming for you. I will find you. No matter where you are, no matter what challenge restricts you. I will not let all of this be for nothing. It cannot be for nothing; I refuse to let it be so. You will live in Arca Aldresia alongside me, take lessons in knighthood, or choose your own path, but so long as I know you are alright. I depart in a week's time, with a thousand knights to find you. You are in my heart, even if I am not in yours. Pappa Signed Fourteen Fifty-Four.
