The towering shard that looms over Gamyi has long been considered a symbol of strength for the nonbelievers. The site where their hero Kui once declared himself divine now has fallen in the hands of Elikhet’s faithful. As it is much too large and sturdy to dismantle, it will instead be repurposed to serve as a new symbol of Khetist authority over Horashesh. Those too stubborn and defiant to accept Elikhet’s light into their hearts will be brought before the tower to be judged, so they may finally begin to understand Kui's weakness.
