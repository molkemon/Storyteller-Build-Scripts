Grudges are everywhere in dwarven society, but they have their ways of working around them. The average dwarf knows it is far healthier to let grudges fester and worsen over time than to actually act on them, or they would be at each other's throats at all times. So long as they meticulously avoid one another, they can hate each other from a distance and keep the peace. However, when surface dwarves with a grudge meet each other abroad, though, they do not tend to have the restraint to stop themselves from starting a fight. Such a fight between surface dwarves seems to have broken out today.
