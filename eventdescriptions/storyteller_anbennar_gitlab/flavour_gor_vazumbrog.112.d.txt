There was no great mystery about the direction of Magda's deathmarch. She knew what it meant when the edges of her dragon claw wounds turned black and green, and she announced to all in earshot that she intended to take the beast down with her, leaving glowmoss blazes on the walls behind her to aid any brave enough in following her. Those blazes led here, to Ulagom Canyon, where the warriormatron's wounds finally got the best of her, in bizarre and spectacular fashion. Where the woman had fallen, the glowmoss and miasma had mixed together, giving rise to new life. a mycoloth, a tremendous beast of fungus, magic, and ill will. Branma Ironreach took up her mother's axe, and without hesitation set about putting an end to this branch of her mother's legacy. The branch was not fated to end there, however. as the creature was chopped to bits, something glittered amidst the fungage. Spores for a new strain of glowmoss, visibly growing before their very eyes into edible efflorescence. It seems they can't grow outside of the specific magical circumstances of this gravehall, however, so they must make a choice about what to do with them.
