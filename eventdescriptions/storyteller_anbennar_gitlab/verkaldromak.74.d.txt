As ruler, it is the monarch's duty to guide the nation into a bright future of wealth and prosperity. But the way into such a future is long, hard, and full of dangers for our people. Since the monarch is a Sleeper, the flow of time within dreams does not match the flow of time outside them. They have decided that it is time to go into a long sleep, spending years in the dream world whilst only passing a week outside, to seek the dangers of the coming months.
