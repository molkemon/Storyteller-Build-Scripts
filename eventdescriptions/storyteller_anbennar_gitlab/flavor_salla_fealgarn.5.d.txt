Oberon nearly broke down the doors of the council as the vote nearly completed. Half for, half against, with the tie-breaking vote in the hands of the greatest of the minor lords. He easily pushed aside the guards who were unable to pick between loyalty to the heir of the clan’s founding dynasty and their duty to the people. "There are countless hordes of orks still roaming outside our gates, and you wish to banish me for all my efforts?" Oberon roared as two guards pushed him back slightly. "You’ve tried to illegally..." "I broke the Greentide. We are this close to victory, this close to safety. And you wish to throw it all away for your political games." Oberon interrupted as the council verbally rebuked him. Onlookers from outside were already spreading whispers. Some jeered that a would-be tyrant was being denied his throne, others decried that a war hero was being dishonored. But the final vote came in regardless of their opinions. "I vote nay on extending Oberon’s emergency powers and his tenure extension."
