It is theorized that the Crimson Deluge which saw most of Escann nearly drowned in blood rain was a mark of Corin waging war upon the Regent Court. To the magical intelligentsia however, a more interesting occurrence happened. the awakening of latent magical potential within certain individuals and families in Escann. The young heir has revealed themselves to be a natural savant in the arcane arts. The question now, is whether they cultivate this or not.
