With the work on our embassy proceeding well, we have gained the attention of the Kraken, an archfey looked upon with particular favor by followers of the Summer Court. They have requested access to our embassy so that they may interact with all those who might go through its halls. This would be a trivial request to grant, were it not for one issue; the Kraken is bound to water, and our embassy is on land. Of course, we cannot simply reject the Kraken's request. They are, after all, an archfey, and well respected among the Summer Court faithful. The question is, how are we to go about satisfying them?
