Our scout commander, Halir Firmhandle, returned from her mission into the deepwoods yesterday looking rather worse for wear. As she explained between pulling arrow after bonetipped arrow out of her leather armor, her scouting party had encountered an elven settlement. While they had approached the elves with offers of trade, the elves had rejected them in no uncertain terms, calling them untrustworthy soileaters and shooting at them until they were forced to retreat. The expedition was not all in vain, though, as Halir’s party did manage to scout out the forest outside a nearby domain during their trip. At least now we will know who our neighbors are.
