In the halcyon days of Baihon Xinh, it ruled uncontested as an empire, kings across Haless paying it tribute and deference. But that was then, and this is now. The monarch demands to once again be called emperor, for the title is tied deeply to his spirit. Ensuring any foreign dignitary accedes to his request is likely to bolster said spirit, though they will certainly chafe at it, for with our wartorn land we cannot yet truly claim an imperial stature.
