Various constructions funded by financial institutions throughout the country have been repossessed as collateral, in an effort to curb the economic losses they are experiencing due to the Gobblivion Property Plunge. The residents of these properties are far from content, yet they possess limited means to prevent this action. The issue of overgobulation is beginning to surge to unprecedented levels as domains become overcrowded with goblins on the streets. Food supplies are being stretched thin, and with no resolution in sight, the outlook is grim.
