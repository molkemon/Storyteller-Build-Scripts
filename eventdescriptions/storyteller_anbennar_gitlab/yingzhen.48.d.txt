To the rest of the Yanshen, the Oni are to be feared. They are viewed as monsters, as heretics, as more demon than man. While some of those claims are more true than others, they have turned out to be very good guests in our lands. Those who have migrated here from Azjakuma have, in most cases, found themselves residing in isolated temples not unlike their communities in the Demon Hills. A smaller number of them have found themselves in our cities; still fairly reclusive, but interacting with the other townspeople productively nonetheless. In both cases, something of a clerical class has emerged in our state. These Oni, and an evergrowing number of humans trained by them, function as what have become known as 'Spirit Brokers', twisting spirits to the whims of themselves and anyone willing to pay for their services.
