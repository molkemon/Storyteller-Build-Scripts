Under our rule, we have completely reformed the humans and gnolls, not only recreating them but redeeming them to a greater form. Formerly stubborn and vile Akasi now diligently serve as laborers and farmers all throughout our realm, building a foundation for all. At the heart of our territory, the former Kheteratans resourcefully scavenge for resources, resculpting the land and buildings to facilitate fertile fields to feed the peoples across the land. And finally, on the outer reaches, the gnolls stand ever watchful of evil, serving as the defenders of Kvanga.
