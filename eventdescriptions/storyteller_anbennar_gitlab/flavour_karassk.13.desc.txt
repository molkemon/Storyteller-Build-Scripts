Throughout the streets of the more autonomous governorates marches the imperial army. Their mission is to educate the population, and while others might question the skill of a soldier in education, they do not realize that the soldiers of Karassk are very effective communicators, for communication is the most important part of teaching. Specifically, they are here to educate the people about the prophecy, as these people have been erroneously taught that the prophecy has not been fulfilled. The soldiers are here to correct that misconception. Any citizens who still do not understand Karassk’s fulfillment of the prophecy are led by the soldiers to private tutoring sessions, where it is all explained to them. Some are so impressed they immediately leave to see the capital for themselves. Some are so enraptured by it they never return home.
