It should have been a normal day in the capital, or at least as normal as the Deepwoods would allow. Yet, this small blessing would be denied when the monarch entered into the audience chamber. Upon stepping inside, a plethora of obnoxious noises rang out, heralding the sudden shower of brightly colored confetti. "Welcome to the Welcome Welcome Back Party," a dozen fey blared out. The ruler jumped back with a start. "The what?" It was a question that went unanswered and unnoticed as the group of fey busily broke apart to reveal a vaguely familiar figure. "Heeeellooo, my dear progenitor. I’ve returned home." The monarch’s eyes widened. "My child?" "The one and the same," they laughed, striding forward for a hug. A hug that was readily given, to the "awws" of the present fey.
