Taelarios wondered about his ancestors, who are said to have been immortal. He was only four centuries old, one of the oldest living elves, and felt the time affecting his mind. And yet, the Precursors lived for thousands of years? How does one not get tired of this? Taelarios certainly was tired. He did not wish to have this throne, the one he relinquished to his now deceased daughter. Imariel was a wonderful child, her deep blue eyes always filled with kindness. Wait, weren't her eyes green? Ah, that doesn't matter, Taelarios is too busy trying to remember such small details. There is still so much to do. Bulwar is still in peril; gnolls, harpies, heretics, and other scum. And he had to protect Irrliam. What did his mother use to say? Always count your eggs one at a time, was it? No, not quite like that. Taelarios tries to remember her words, her voice, even her face, but almost nothing comes to mind. Taelarios quickly discarded those thoughts. They were tiresome. And he has been tired for so long now. He needed some rest, even though he did take a nap recently. Maybe another one would help? Yes, a good long nap would be great. Anything for the tiniest bit of rest.
