For a decade now our overlord has shut their ears to our needs. We called upon them multiple times and, in the end, we were just flat out ignored. The Gelakar are a loyal people, but loyalty is based on trust, and Birzartanšes has lost ours in the Decades of Destruction as they let the harpies grab our land, subjugate our brethren, and then had the audacity to not care. Enough of our men and wealth was drained for their selfish goals, but not anymore; from this day forward, we will take matters into our own hands. What can they do about it anyway?
