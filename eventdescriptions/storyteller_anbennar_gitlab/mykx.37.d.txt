Recently in Cannor, a new theater play depicting pirates appeared, and they used the Mykx flag to depict the violent scoundrels. All around the world, there has been a shift. When the word pirate is used, they think of us, not of their own local pirates or realms. We have penetrated the conscience of the globe, and they recognize and fear us. Viakkoc is now just another part of the realm; its years of independence and raiding have been forgotten, and all think of us. The dispute between our realms is gone, our triumph is total.
