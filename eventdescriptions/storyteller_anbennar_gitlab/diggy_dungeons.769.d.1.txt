Ranks formed as the front line advanced slowly against the coming golem horde, forming a solid wall that did its best to halt the golem advance. Behind this line, the heaviest hitters formed into two groups and quickly swung around the flanks, leaping over or sliding under obstacles and crashing against the sides of the golem horde. We could hear Verdric shouting obscenities as he rallied another wave, but at the very least, we will be able to gain ground. It will take some more effort to reach the dais, but progress at this point is steady.
