Day by day, night by night, the calls of piety and clashes of zealots erupt across Cannor. Religious conflict devours the continent under a flame of passionate faith and belief in Corin, and the immediate response from those faithful to Adean. This is a time of troubles, terror, and wanton bloodshed. However, to the associates of the monarch, this is a time of opportunity to rightfully decry the injustice levied against the Infernal Court. Anxiety stirs in the heart of the monarch's associates in the cults, who douse their worries with wine and brandy, urging the monarch to head into the royal court and declare the monarch and the government loyal to the betrayed and unjustly punished Infernal Court. "For the cause. For us," they whisper. "Please, monarch. For your friends. We are so close." The monarch stands. Pushing through the heavy doors, the monarch stares down their confused advisors and associates; those in the know smiling and nodding, whilst most simply tilt their head in confusion. Finally, the monarch speaks.
