The war stretches on, and despite their best efforts, the city of Sir still holds strong. We have resisted the hobgoblin armies and held onto our homes, though the strife has been great and the people weep for their lost loved ones. With no clear victory in sight, we have received a messenger from the Great Command; they begrudgingly offer us a limited peace that would grant freedom to the core lands of Great Sir, and only that. What say we?
