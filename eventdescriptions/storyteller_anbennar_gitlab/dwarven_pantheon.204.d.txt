A large part of the issue for people who remain devoted to Ancestor Worship is the fear, perhaps not necessarily unfounded, that the deeds, efforts, and tales of their ancestors will be cast aside and forgotten, just as the gods that many of their friends and family now turn to were. We could certainly ease these concerns, but it would not be painless or cheap. However, ignoring or, worse, dismissing them entirely would likely lead to further unrest.
