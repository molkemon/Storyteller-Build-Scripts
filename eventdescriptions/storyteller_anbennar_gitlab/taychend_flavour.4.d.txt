Agents of the Cult of Vherenar, a secretive religious brotherhood dedicated to liberating those in bondage across Taychend, have recently provoked a slave rebellion in our lands, supplying our slaves with weapons and convincing them of the injustice of their position. Already widespread damage has been caused in various provinces of our realm, as masters are killed in their sleep, mines are thrown into chaos, and those in bondage rise up against all who have wronged them; this revolt must be dealt with quickly in order for things to be brought under control.
