Although minotaurs are strong creatures, their small numbers and lack of organization mean that, apart from a few ambushes on small caravans or on lone travelers, they pose no serious threat to our glorious nation. However, an exceptionally well-coordinated herd of minotaurs has launched a raid on the arsenal of that province, looting most of the weapons and causing trouble in the surrounding workshops and settlements. If we are to get rid of them before they can do any more damage to our subjects, it will be necessary to send a well-equipped detachment of our soldiers and adventurers.
