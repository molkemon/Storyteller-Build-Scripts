Arannen and Asheniande had intended to partition the lands of Gisden between their two realms. Now, we have swept aside their ambitions and enforced our will upon them. It is not long before we are summoned to the Imperial Parliament to answer for our acts. A Marquis of the Empire falling is not a matter so easily ignored. In response to the Imperial Prince’s worries, we bring forth proof of our claims. Evidence carefully collected during the war, witnesses bribed to speak on our behalf, and captives coerced to reveal the truth. After examining all that we have brought forward, the Empire ultimately agrees that our war against Arannen was just. Although the Marquis’ remaining relatives, present to stand bear against us, screamed and shouted in frustration at the results, there was little they could do about the Imperial Parliament’s decision. Despite Arannen having been brought low, the war is not over; Tellum, Toarnen, and Celliande. All three of them supported Arannen in different ways during our conflict with them, whether through selling the Marquisate weapons and supplies or other, nefarious means. Gisden shall remember. They will pay for what they have done.
