Ogres have a long tradition of building monumental fortified settlements, possibly dating back to the time of their giant ancestors, a tradition they maintain to this day, showing significant prowess in construction, due in part to their size and strength. This is not simply a choice of style, as it is blatantly clear ogres are rather tall fellows. With an imposing height of at least ten feet, they positively need larger dwellings than other races, and often find themselves in awkward situations when living in the cities due to their blundering frames. In hopes of remedying this, a delegation of ogre master masons, architects, and notables has presented the court with a proposal for a series of sweeping reforms to the standards of construction in the realm, in order to accommodate the needs of their growing community. Aware of the seemingly daunting costs, they assure this style will not only favor their race but also the realm's reputation as patrons of the arts.
