The dockhand swaggers every day to and from the imperial dockyard to the civilian side to collect the daily tax. It is a small amount; half goes to the imperial coffers while the other half goes to the ruler of the country. But recently, the dockhand has returned empty handed, despite the docks swelling with adventurers and traders seeking to go to the New World. Unfortunately, such large crowds attract and sustain a dark underworld that has formed amidst the chaotic docks and shores. Outfits of rough criminals, calling themselves gangs, each with a name more ludicrous than the last, have started to run protection rackets among the trade and fishing sections. Garrison captains have suggested we crack down on these growing gangs, but an advisor of ours instead suggests this can be a great opportunity.
