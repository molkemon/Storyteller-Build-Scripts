Today our messenger brought shocking news to us. The neighboring elven kingdoms are in a state of utter chaos. The estate that was hosting the elven summit attended by the most prominent figures of the New Sun Cult world was blown to pieces, and many of the elven kings and their close supporters have perished or suffered great injuries. They suspect one of their kin was behind the incident and have started to turn against each other. The elven alliance network is in shambles, and if we were to declare war on one of them, it is doubtful that many of their kin would step up to their defense. Should we use this opportunity to deal a fatal blow to them?
