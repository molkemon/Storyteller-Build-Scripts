Positive relations between our merchants and the gnollish merchants of that realm have resulted in a strengthening of trade agreements between our two realms. All the usual goods and wares flow out of their capital and into our markets, but most singular of all the gnollish wares is the good they take most pride in selling. slaves. By partaking in this market, we have seen a massive influx in foreign slave labor across all of our lands, cheapening and accelerating virtually any laborious projects.
