The waves. He wishes to hear the waves, ordsiwyn. Tell him that there are no waves here. Only stars. He weeps, ordsiwyn. He asks you to let him go back. He has all that he wishes to have. food, drinks, the most comfortable lodgings, and the faithful listening to his every word. Tell him to forget his old life. He cannot, ordsiwyn. They are not his people. He misses his child. He will get over it. Tell him to start teaching magic to us by tomorrow.
