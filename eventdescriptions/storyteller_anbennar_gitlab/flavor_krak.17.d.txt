The Nortumrod is finally complete and ready for use. A grand highway that could fit five wagons abreast, runs from Seldizâd to Fýllstýn by way of Skordhûmad and the Giant's Anvil entrance at Hardy Hole. The highway is patrolled and has regular outpost towns set up along its length. During the winter, special IceSmiths enchant the road to keep the snow away from it, leaving it open and ready all year round. Truly we have completed a marvel of dwarven ingenuity.
