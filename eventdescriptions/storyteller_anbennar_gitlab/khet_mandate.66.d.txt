The Khetarch is most times the eldest of the Khets, the one to stand above them as the head of their community. This position in theory bears great responsibilities, but unfortunately in practice most of them are still a specimen of lazy and indifferent species, causing them to drop most of these into mortal appointees like Steward of the Sorrow. In the newest batch of requirements, there is a request from a Khet to get a personal ship and its crew from the navy. It would act as his pleasure barge, shooting cannonballs into the waters to gather fish and giving him the aesthetic he needs.
