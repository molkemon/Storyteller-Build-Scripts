A debate has broken out amongst the three technocratic factions which dominate Giberd about what to do with the imperial archives of Konwell. The Technomancers support upgrading the premises and repairing any damage to the Imperial Archive and Konwell schools. The Brillites wish to send a team of gnomish scholars and technocrats to scour the imperial archive for any research that could aid our artificery efforts, while the Mechanists propose the most radical and contentious suggestion. moving the entire imperial archive to Giberd in order to increase Giberd's importance and to allow all artificers in Giberd easy access to the archives.
