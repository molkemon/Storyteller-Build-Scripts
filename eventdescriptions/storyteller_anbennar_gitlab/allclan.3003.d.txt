Scandalous news, my lord. One of the Harmaceuticals' experimental substances, the much anticipated 'Gojuice Two. Rejuiced', has turned out to be a complete and utter failure in early trials. Formulated specially to imbue any given goblin with the energy of seven trolls and a killer headache afterwards, the new drug has actually proven to lower activity levels rather than raising them. The Harmaceuticals swear such a disaster will not happen again.
