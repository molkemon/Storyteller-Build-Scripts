The hags of the Night Coven have long considered them recalcitrant heretical outcasts for not bowing to their whims. Whispers in fortuneteller’s tents and roadside inns claim that in retaliation, these dark creatures have cursed the province, causing its land to turn fallow and its people to suffer under the burden of malignant spells. Followers of the hags demand the right to freely serve their mistresses in order to revert the enchantment.
