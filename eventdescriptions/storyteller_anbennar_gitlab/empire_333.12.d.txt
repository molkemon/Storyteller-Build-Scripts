Peering out over the great city of Zerat stands Khus an Zhyani. Many Zatsarya were gifted to the first lizardfolk empire by the gods, yet none remain as complete and unblemished as the spire in Zerat. In the stillness of the morning light, as the small crowd waited for the ruler to speak, the monarch steeled their nerves. They knew the statement they were about to make would cause controversy, not least amongst the naga. But it was time. They were the Final Empire. If not now, then when? "I am lifting the ban on entering the upper floors of this Zatsarya." The monarch's next words were instantly drowned out by murmurs and shouts from the crowd. Several naga, including the leading figure in the current high temple, started to move forward, but the monarch continued. "The gods left us these spires to be used when we are ready. For as long as lizardfolk have dwelled here, we have not been ready. True, there have been no signs from the gods, nor anyone, that we are ready. But the Inheritance was left to us to discern, and we are the Final Empire." The monarch stepped forward, pushing ahead of the naga that were slithering onto the stage. "We are the prophecy. There is nothing beyond our capabilities, because there is nothing beyond us. We will uncover the mystery of the spires. For if we are eternal, then it is simply a matter of when."
