There is one god, Surael. He sacrificed himself to save us from the Day of Ashen Skies, surviving as the sun above even when all the other gods perished. He sent his incarnation Jaher to free Bulwar from monsters, and with Jaher's death that role passed to his chosen, the Sun Elves. This is the truth of the realm, its ruler, and all its people. Where pockets of our realm have been led astray, we shall guide them back onto the sunlit path; where foreigners whisper lies, we shall see them expelled or hung. Whatever the God Fragment's nature, it is merely an artifact from Aelantier; and it pales before the majesty of Surael's blazing beacon in the sky. It is that sun that has watched over us for millennia, given life to our plants and our people, and seen us thrive to this day. The selfsame light shall shine over us forevermore, illuminating the monarch's life and the path before them, and blessing all the realm with its splendour.
