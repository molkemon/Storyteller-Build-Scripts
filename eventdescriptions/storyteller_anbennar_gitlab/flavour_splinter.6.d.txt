Once, the lands of Bulwar were ruled by cold and hateful gods, who viewed humanity as nothing more than slaves. Our ancestors showed them that the divine can be a source of warmth too, but their pessimism outlasted our stay, and they came to know Taqoshur as Surael, a lone warrior against a terrible darkness. While night may always come, the sun always follows. The despair the peoples of Bulwar feel is unwarranted, for Taqoshur shines above and does not care for this world alone. A new dawn prepares to rise over Bulwar, one to expel their darkness once and for all and let them bask in the gentle warmth of Taqoshur. The only question is how to organize them after the fact. Conquering them upon horseback may be the straightforward answer, but they may find our warm rule more pleasant under their familiar polities.
