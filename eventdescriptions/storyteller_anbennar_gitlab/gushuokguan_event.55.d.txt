The representative Li speaks now. My fellow peasants, I stand before you pleading to allocate more funds to the workers whose fields have suffered after the last conflict. We must restore the prosperity of our realm through the production of more tools and the building of more farms; it is our duty to protect the weak. I have already gained the support of others and pray to gain yours too. The representative Guo speaks now. Fellow soldiers, I must agree with all which my colleague says, though I disagree on giving the funds to restoration projects. It is now the time to first restore the army, as I and many others fear that if we do not restore the armed forces, we will have no peasantry left to defend. What side will we choose, will we focus on domestic restoration or rebuild the army?
