Imagine this, dear reader. Insyaa has been released. The Storm Wall breaks, and you get access to the continent. You explore inside, and there are kobolds. A bunch of kobolds. Without dragons. That would be crazy, right? This event would be so cool if Insyaa were out. But it is not yet, so let me just narrate a bit of it to you. The Charkuchins, that is the kobolds here, they have black, purple, and silver scales; they are unlike any other kobolds on the planet. And they do not worship dragons. The Kobildzani are going to meet these guys and be shocked. It is not an "oh, your dragon left too? That is so sad" or an "oh, your dragon died? That is awful" kind of thing. No, the kobolds here do not worship a dragon. They do not even know about dragons. So this will cause a huge culture shock, but the kobolds in Insyaa are also rather successful. They are not hated or despised like the Cannorian kobolds are. They have huge citadels that go up to the skies in the mountains. And they have their own vibrant culture, politics, and everything. You know what this is going to do? It is going to cause a minor religious schism in Kobildzan. All those people who have said all along, "We do not need the dragons," bam, they have a little proof. The Black Aspect morphs into a bit of kobold civic religion, and your society evolves with it. Fun, right? Anyways, yeah. This will be really cool once Insyaa is out.
