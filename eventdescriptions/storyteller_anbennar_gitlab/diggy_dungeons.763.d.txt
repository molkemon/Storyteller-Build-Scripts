The captain's efforts are hopeless. No matter what he tries, he cannot regain command of the situation. Panic sets in, expeditionaries crying there is no hope, only death lay past the gate they passed. The golems spread out. The rest of the expedition elsewhere does not even realize what has occurred until it is too late. They are slaughtered, hunted down to the very last. One escapes, and those who come looking after will find not even blood or corpses left in the labyrinth of madness beyond the hidden door.
