It has been days, weeks, or months since the monarch went to sleep, trying to pierce the veil of time and reality. Guards stood at their bed through all this time, aides attentive and ready to scribble down knowledge gained the moment the monarch would open their eyes. Some sheets are already filled with cryptic, incoherent sentences mumbled in one of their wilder dreams, ready to be filled with sense. And today is finally the day for which they all waited so long. Even if expected and wanted, the voice of the monarch makes some of the present start in surprise.
