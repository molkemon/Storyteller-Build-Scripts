Having all of the Xianjie under one administration has done wonders for its prosperity and its security. Pilgrims and travelers have been able to take to the roads with confidence, both to the Xia and to the realm proper. This exchange of ideas combined with our efforts to spread Lihoism has led to a very peculiar interpretation of enlightenment. They have taken the ideas of spiritual detachment and combined them with the freespirited variety of the Xia, the self-titled "Glorious Enlightenment" sect. Some of the HuliJing have heard word of this idea and wish for us to integrate it into the state. So far, this has not been an unpopular prospect.
