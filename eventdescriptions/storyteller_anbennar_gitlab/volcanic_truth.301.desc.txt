The people cry out. the world is not changing quickly enough. The Twin Gods see us destroy and build and destroy again, yet things remain constant. the land beneath us, the sky above us, the sea beyond all refuse to give up their forms. What might change instead, an increasing number of our people think, is the one overseeing all this change. Perhaps another ruler might change things faster. Perhaps a change in leadership is a change in itself, and the Twin Gods will be happy if the ones who oversaw the stagnation of before are sacrificed to them.
