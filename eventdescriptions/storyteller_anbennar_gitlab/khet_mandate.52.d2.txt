In the past, the arrival of a Pleasure Barge was a cause of panic for those living on the shores of the Sorrow, as the Khet's whim could mean ruin and suffering. Our new barges, more luxurious and splendid but less frequent, do not elicit this feeling anymore, and now the barges are received with merriment and celebration when they pass by a population center. This has made the Khet feel more loved than ever before, and their enjoyment of the pleasures of the barges has increased now that they are greater but more infrequent. Recently, a great party was organized by local authorities to celebrate the passing of a barge, and the Khet were happy to participate and accept the honors and gifts the locals offered them, after which they publicly announced their satisfaction with the administration.
