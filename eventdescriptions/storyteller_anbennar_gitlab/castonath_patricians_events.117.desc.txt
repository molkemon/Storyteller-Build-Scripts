North Castonath, traditionally, has long been the wealthiest part of the great city. Many of the patricians and old Castans centered their city dwellings there in the heyday of Castan's Empire. Nestled next to the proud Upper District and verdant Imperial Quarter is Pantheonway, the religious and cultural center of Castonath. Pantheonway is saturated with myriad sculptures and busts depicting the most famous of heroes and events of old Castanor. Also represented are the deities of the Regent Court. Castellos foremost, of course, but depictions of every deity, minor or major, can be found in the quiet streets of Pantheonway. Many of the members of the estate church have been pushing for a commission of Corin in her moment of victory. slaying Dookanson with the ruins of Castonath in the backdrop. As befitting her role in saving the city and all Escann from the Greentide, they wish for this commission to feature prominently in Pantheonway, next to the greatest and most venerable statues in the district. The patricians, of course, object, citing the importance of the history and tradition of the current layout and order of these structures. Who shall we support?
