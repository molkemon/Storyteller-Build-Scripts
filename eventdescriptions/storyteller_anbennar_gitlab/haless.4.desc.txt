While the Harimari are feared throughout Haless as warriors and generals, for most the navies are still the domain of men. The larger Harimari forms scarcely fit amid the tight confines and dangerous rigging of many sailing vessels. Some however, especially among the Roaming Harimari, still take to the sea despite these difficulties, where they prove able to make good captains of themselves, with a particular skill in boarding actions. In the closer confines of a ship’s deck, they can rip through smaller sailors before they have a chance to react. Now a Vandiphan captain has appeared on our shores and offered his service as a naval commander.
