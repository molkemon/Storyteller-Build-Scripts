A common body type, familiar to a large quantity of mechanim. Recent discoveries have allowed us to recreate the configuration for mechanim without it or those who have swapped configuration previously. Noted for a horizontal chassis where the head, torso, and legs share a plane, two legs are slotted into joints at the lower torso area. Being typically of a lithe configuration for additional agility, and combined with the space optimization of the form itself, it allows mechanim to be much more agile and adaptive, letting them cover larger distances over rougher terrain in less time.
