Holvash in orkish society was a lavish dish reserved for only the strongest of Warchiefs, but with our entry into Cannor, we have improved the dish dramatically. With exotic sugar and new ingredients, we have devised a new recipe. frying pasta and coating it in sugar, you get a sweet dish that makes even the toughest ork look at you differently. We will promote its sale so that humans, orks, elves, and the like all can enjoy this tasty treat.
