The library looks quite abandoned, and we are lucky that its small size and a few pieces of rubble seem to have blocked off easy access for any thieves. Dwarves are master diggers, however, and soon the rubble was cleared. Other than a few rotten manuscripts, the library seemed to have perfectly preserved the items, which seemed to mostly be research on ramsteel production and mining. Let us sell anything we do not need from here and be on our way.
