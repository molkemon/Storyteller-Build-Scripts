The experiment of integrating some Darkalyndar rider units in our army is going extremely well; they have been a powerful asset, our cavalry cannot compete with them, and they have adapted well to the Ironblood training regimen. Our people and soldiers have been very welcoming towards the Chendhyans, and even our heir has been extremely fascinated by them and their jaguars, frequently disappearing from their tutors' sight only to be found around the Darkalyndars and their mounts. But a small number of soldiers is one thing; having entire companies of foreign soldiers in our army is another. Should we take the risk and expand the operation to have an entire Tydhzegaeda brigade?
