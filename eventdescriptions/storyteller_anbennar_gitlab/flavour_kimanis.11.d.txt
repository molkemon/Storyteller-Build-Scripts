So saith the mob. We have stumbled into towns that are not ours. Forgive us if we seem to slur our speech. We are so very far from fair Kimánis now. Appetites that once were checked can grow. Our brothers in their square have started cheering. Our brothers in their houses have found loot. Our brothers holding swords will take it from them. Our brothers empty-handed can haul wine. This is the meaning of the creed of Kheions. Together, we are stronger than alone. But wait, our demagogue has started speaking. His words have bled together in a drone. Yet look, our brothers’ mood has gone to shifting. What is the path our demagogue has shown?
