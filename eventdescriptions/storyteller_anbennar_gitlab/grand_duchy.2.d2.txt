Tensions were fraught within the Imperial Parliament; there was shouting, the bestowal of false curses, and even a sword drawn between two rival princes throughout the day. Imperial court whispers had incensed the Empire; allegedly the ruler had already made their decision, and by the appearance of the ruler of the target domain at the upper balcony, detractors of that ruler were losing hope. When the emperor entered the parliament chamber by midnight, the room was already dead silent. The more boisterous princes were either arrested for the night or sunk low in their seats as the ruler walked to the podium. The ruler took a moment, retrieving a piece of paper from their robes as they glanced up at the target ruler and gave a slight glare, stirring some curious murmurs from the seats. "I, the emperor, bestow the target ruler and their descendants the title of Grand Duke of the target domain. However, they shall have no claim nor right to ancestral domain." The murmurs immediately erupted into confusion and arguing. Supporters of the target ruler were outraged, while detractors begged the ruler to throw away the title all together. The emperor left the room in a wake of discord as the Empire reeled from the split decision. The target ruler was seen staring imperiously as the ruler left.
