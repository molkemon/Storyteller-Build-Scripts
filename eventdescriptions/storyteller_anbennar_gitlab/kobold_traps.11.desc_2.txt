A caravan of kobolds has entered our caverns, finding its way into the nooks and crannies of the realm. Hailing all the way from another domain, they come with everything they had on them. trinkets, baubles, tools, seeds, clothes, and gods. Religions and manners of belief which are in conflict with the dragons. This has immediately caused uproar in the realm. The locals balk in horror at these migrants, who enter our lands but fail to worship Malliath, or Tayekan, or Nimrith, or Iothoral. Already, the dragonpriests have demanded that they sacrifice lambs to the slaughter, or contribute wealth to the Great Hoard, but the newcomers refuse. Instead, they hold fast to the religion of those who conquered them, who forced them into such misery that they entered our lands. They brought the enemy’s ways of life into the heart of our nation. The migrants offer their own wealth to the realm, and desire to help us fortify the caverns. They say they want to help us, and yet distrust is sown throughout our nation. Do we embrace these strangers, who declare loyalty to the ruler alongside their new gods? Or do we put them to work, while ensuring that dragons are the only things worshiped in the caverns of their name?
