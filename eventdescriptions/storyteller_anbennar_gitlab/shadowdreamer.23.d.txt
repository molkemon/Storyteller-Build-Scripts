The Great Dreamer tried to do as his predecessors had done, and follow the whispers of the Dream into the deepest dark beneath the old hold. It became clear after months of wandering that this strategy would not bear fruit here. The whispering has fallen quiet beneath the location, and the swirling tunnels and ruined halls sprawl further and deeper than they have ever seen before. Instead, the Great Dreamer has seen to the creation of squads who can fan out and systematically map the whole labyrinth. These parties will be led by veteran officers of the Sagah, and include skilled spelunkers and particularly sensitive Drahmgall initiates. They will delve into that network of ancient pipes, tunnels, halls, and mysteries, and find the way down.
