There is finally some activity in our ruined lands, but it does not appear to be the good kind. In recent days, a wanderer without even a clan named Kavelos Truthseeker has been stirring up sentiment against the rule of the Caehn, who he claims is a representation of Irdaeos’ displeasure and the squanderer of a bloodline of conquerors. These appear to be popular sentiments among the commoners. Now that someone has given voice to sentiments that were previously aired in private spaces, his following grows by the day. As he travels across the land, even our court is consumed by whispers, and a tension is building as our rule is challenged.
