EduzHammura is no more. Your greatest temple has been seized and subverted by a faith you so despise. One in opposition to the sun you worship, one far closer to that malevolent dark you are right to fear. For these lands are mine. Mine to claim, mine to dominate, mine to cleanse and enslave as I see fit. Mine to plunge into darkness as I devour the remnant of your liar lord. But even so, you watch. You were all too happy to let me eat your fellow vassal. Was their perversion of your faith too much to bear? Does it hurt, knowing you might have been able to stop me, if only you had put your hatred aside? But you only watched. So witness my pyres, Bulwar's mighty, permanently lit. Witness the tallest ziggurat in all the lands, burning with the emerald fervor of the logic that spells your doom. Witness me set your city alight, with fire hot enough to turn the sand to glass. Are you not afraid that I might do the same to you? Keep your nobles. Keep your foolish faithful. Fight me with hope in your hearts, for it makes them all the sweeter. And as your huddled masses seek succor, send them to me. Let them abandon your false prophet for the vivid light of the Kult. I am the only God that matters now. If any deity speaks, then they speak through me. Hear my words, Bulwar. Hear my words, foul Surakel. Hear my words, First Sun, my blazing enemy.
