People in our cities seem to enjoy a more free life, free of material possessions. They follow Ulótholism, though not many are concerned with the original philosophical idea of Ulóthopholes and rather just enjoy what they consider a free life. When asked, many of these people will not be deterred by any arguments and simply go on with their day. Some have also been seen walking through the wild completely naked, though such cases are rare; it could be a bad look on the state if we keep allowing this kind of behaviour.
