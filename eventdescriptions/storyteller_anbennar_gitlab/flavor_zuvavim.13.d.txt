The Dawyaw Fleet has it all. The finest ships, the most experienced admirals, and a thorough training regime for all new sailors who step aboard their ships. Yet they have been struggling to find new sailors. Not for a lack of willing new seamen, as many have been joining the rivalling Movuma fleet, despite its lower training standards. The predominantly Noukahi Pantheon recruits cited an old Dawyaw policy that no ship can be captained by a nonkhetist. This rule was dropped years ago, but the rumours persist, perhaps because despite the official rule no longer being in place, it unofficially remains in place as no captain currently in the fleet is of a nonkhetist faith. We could raise a new ship with a promising Noukahi Pantheon officer to be its captain, yet grant it to the Dawyaw fleet, a solution that would satisfy both sides. Or we could just force new recruits to join the undermanned Dawyaw fleet, which would certainly be the cheaper option.
