While most refugees from Xianjie have been human, that is not the full picture. Many dwarves, harpies, and harimari have also arrived here for the same reasons as everyone else. Similarly to the other monks, they established their own separate communities, with the bulk of them ending up in Phungoen and Lektonmai. While some may still need to adapt to seeing a few more nonhuman faces around, the local harpy and dwarven communities were quick to help out the newcomers, helping them feel at home despite the distance they just passed.
