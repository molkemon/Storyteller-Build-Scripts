In times since the war's end, many across the realm have begun to wonder if there was more they could have done. They were defeated in the Lilac Wars, but to blame their allies alone when many in the neighboring domain shouldered the blame most would be cowardly. They could have done more, at court and across their nation, and so they will now devote themselves to doing more for the realm itself as they take their new place. In the wyvern they trust. They shall soar to new heights, they shall embody the majesty to build back what was lost with the moon, and just as human bonds with rider, so shall they find new connections. They shall find the wyvern within, just as they hold out hope that the creatures themselves can be found once more.
