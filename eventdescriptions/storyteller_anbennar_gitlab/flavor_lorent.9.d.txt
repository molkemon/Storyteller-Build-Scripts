The Imperial Host split the seas as they crossed the Dames head. Dozens of banners of every color and heraldic blazon waved beneath the high Penant of the Imperial Dove as the Emperor landed with the first wave on the beach. Approaching without caution and with fury at the memory of their broken alliance, the Emperor’s righteous anger found no Lorentish soldier on which to visit itself. The armies of the empire entered the city of Wesdam and found it empty of any foe, only the peasantry present barricading themselves in their homes as the tension of a possible war was still in the air. Outside the city, the Imperial Commanders found a large set of tracks fleeing west with dozens of roses trampled into the mud.
