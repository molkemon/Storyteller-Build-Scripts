Our cooperation with the adventurous sort continues to yield results. Whereas before, the strange siren song that has come to plague our people was felt all across the land, now with the help of the adventurers, who are themselves integrating into the Castellyrian ways, we have seen people be less vulnerable. Before, the dangers that arose in the postGreentide Escann meant that people had such draining lives that the call of the night was that much sweeter. Now, with these adventurers solving many of those issues and taking up arms in the people’s protection, they can sleep sounder, and so when the call comes, fewer people are tempted and there are fewer disappearances. This unity of purpose means that the adventurers now help us twice over; their loyal service will be well rewarded.
