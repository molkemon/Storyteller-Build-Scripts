The goblin minority of Igraxraak have certainly been busy, in more ways than one. Every day, it seems we have more goblins than the day before, and every day, some new urban development seems to have occurred, seemingly overnight. Nowhere is this as evident as in Xalgazelaginva, where the goblins have been at work, seemingly without end, building new laboratories, wyvern pens, slaughterhouses, tanneries, workshops. The list goes on. Vast ramshackle towers rake into the sky, while their foundations scrape the very roots of the Khenak Range. Each year, the city grows more labyrinthine, more chaotic. The Xalgazelag and goblins both seem to thrive in the maelstrom, spitting out new inventions week after week. Of course, Xalgazelaginva is only the heart of this trend, and it seems that a network of sorts is developing, with arteries forming between Xalgazelaginva and our wyvern farms all across our realm. Each grows gradually to resemble Xalgazelaginva, bit by bit and day by day, building ever upwards and downwards, with no end in sight.
