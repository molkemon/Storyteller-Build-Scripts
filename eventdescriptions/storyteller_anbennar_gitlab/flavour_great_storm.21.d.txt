Luck is not enough to describe what has occurred today in the Forbidden Plains. It appears a few noblemen of our own have led a delegation towards the centaurs beyond the gate in the steppes of Nuzurbokh to request aid against the Facestealers, similar to how we asked the centaurs that remained in Khaashalg for help. Either way, it seems their ploy has paid off. Fifty centaurs who have been trained in a basic way are now under our command. Granted, they might not be the best they had to offer; their envoy said so; but it seems promising. The Stone Gate of Korath has been opened, and fruitful connections have been established.
