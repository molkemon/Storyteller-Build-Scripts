The last few days had been quite frustrating for the monarch. Accosted by an unknown individual, they had woken up every day to a bouquet of flowers addressed to them, but by an unknown sender. Even worse, no matter how much the monarch looked, they simply could not figure out who the sender was. Today, though, that changed; while interrogating a servant for answers, the monarch was greeted by that famous grin and witnessed the servant pull their nose out into an elephant's trunk. It was Dhenbasana.
