Negotiating the finer terms of the Deepwoods unification took time, of course. Different Ceanna had conditions they wished to be met for their grove, asked for specific court positions, and so on. Even after that had been settled, there were ceremonies and a parade to mark that the Wood Elves were united once more. Bricena had not slept so little since the siege at the start of her reign, but after a month her new position was finally firmly established, and she could take the time to visit her father.
