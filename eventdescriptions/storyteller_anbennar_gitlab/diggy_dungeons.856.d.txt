The ghost has a distinct advantage, that being his ability to fly. While we have to take strange, winding paths through the rock, it can just fly up ahead, unbothered by the surrounding geology. Only sometimes do we get a nice stretch where we can start gaining on him, but he is slowly slipping away, his laughter fading into the dark and stale air. At some point, this is the only thing we have left of him to follow, a difficult trace as echo and wall try to confuse us.
