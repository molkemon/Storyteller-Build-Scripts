The Battle of Stone is over. We have made several concessions to obtain aid in reforming our ancient republic, and the efforts have paid off. Gundomar Frozenforge will act as the First Councillor of the Free Council. This new system will have a council of elected dwarves in each hold which will manage the affairs of the region and will elect members to send to the High Council where larger issues in the realm will be decided. Our future is bright now, and with the people satisfied and our old ways reaffirmed, nothing can stop us.
