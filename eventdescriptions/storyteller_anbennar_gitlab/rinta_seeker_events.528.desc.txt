One of the greatest and richest men in the realm, a well-respected trader who built his fortune from commerce, was a well-known patron of religion and the arts. We had expected that in death, he would bequeath all his wealth to the state. However, the construction of the Godswatch seems to have caught his interest in his later years. Just before his death, he amended his will to bequeath all his wealth directly to the project instead. Most would see it as our duty to respect his last wishes, but we could also nullify the will and seize the money for ourselves.
