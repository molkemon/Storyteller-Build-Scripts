Blood is, in the literal sense, life. Much blood has been spilled by this instance of life, and as such, the old contradiction remains true. Ella Kettlebrook has opted to remain in Castonath, acting as the city's sole permanent inhabitant. The Chloromancers still study the Verdant Seed and its offspring, but only from the safety of their laboratories, built in carefully selected locations in the area. None will forget the Verdancy anytime soon, and none will forget Halann's wrath against our hubris. Perhaps we should have known better, or perhaps this is just one of her lessons, a fifth Great Task.
