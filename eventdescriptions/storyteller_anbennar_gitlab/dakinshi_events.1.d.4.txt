Nestled in the steep mountain slopes of the Pakreda Sedara Mountains lies the territory ruled by the Death Bringing Cobra clan. These are the final remaining territories ruled by the ancient dynasty that once held dominion over all of Bamaliwuni and beyond. Yet such days are long gone; ever since the royal family abandoned the capital two hundred years ago, the Death Bringing Cobra clan has declined in relevance. A decade ago, it seemed for a brief moment that the clan would have a reversal in fortunes. The armies of the Death Bringing Cobra descended from the mountains, captured the fortress city of Inshibonde, and even laid siege to the stronghold of Kuvunika. Unfortunately, in fourteen thirty-nine a high-ranking retainer named Kwizera betrayed the clan’s leadership when they were most vulnerable, initiating a purge of unusual brutality and forcing the scant remnants of the clan to flee back into the mountains from which they came. In the chaos and confusion, another high-ranking retainer of the clan, Kuende, became the new matriarch. A powerful mage, by all accounts she was pregnant at the time of Kwizera’s brutal purge, and most assumed the child was lost from stress. Yet despite ruling without family, Kuende seems blithely unconcerned with matters of succession, and in the halls of her mountain fastness she consorts with ever darker magics. The Death Bringing Cobra shares her desire for revenge, and her people are willing to follow where Kuende leads. Yet her designs for Sarhal are bleaker than even her closest retainers can imagine, her venom and lust for vengeance concerned with more than petty warlord infighting.
