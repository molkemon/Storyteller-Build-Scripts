Erel tracked through the high pass, looking down over his camp. Another two dead, and another yet disappeared; something was hunting them. He had spoken with his folk, and all reported the same culprit. something tall and pale, with eyes like daggers and hair like crowfeathers. Vampire, his grandmother had called it, when it was described to her. Erel was not a superstitious man, but it was hard to not believe the stories, trekking through dark woods on a dark night. He did not know what it was, nor did his fellows, but it was not as human as it seemed. He hoped that, when they made it to the next settlement, they could alert the local lord, who would hopefully do something about it, or at least do something which would spook the beast and leave his people alone. But until then, he would have to pray for the best, and hope those he loved would make it to safety.
