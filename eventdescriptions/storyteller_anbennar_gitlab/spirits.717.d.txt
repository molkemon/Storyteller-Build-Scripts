On a serene and tranquil evening, the monarch took a leisurely stroll, temporarily escaping the weighty responsibilities of ruling and immersing them in the natural beauty of the lands now under their governance. The vast expanse of the region, illuminated by the descending sun, captivated their senses. However, despite the peaceful surroundings and the presence of various palace animals, including graceful peacocks and exotic birds of paradise, their mind refused to find solace. Unusual unease lingered within them, and even the creatures around them seemed to sense it. A scorpion, perched on the railings, fixated its gaze upon the monarch. Baffled and cautious, the monarch attempted to gently shoo away the scorpion, but it remained unperturbed, its focus unwavering. Growing increasingly wary, the monarch summoned the guards to remove the scorpion. However, to their horror, their escort suddenly slumped to the ground, lifeless. Panic seized them, and they rushed to the balcony in search of escape, only to find it swarming with dozens of brown scorpions. Even from the fallen guards, scorpions emerged, crawling toward the monarch. In their midst, they raised their pincers as an ominous shadow loomed over them. Desperation and terror gripped the monarch, and as they strained to discern the source of their distress, their eyes widened in realization. Just as they were about to succumb to the nightmarish scene, they abruptly woke up in their bed, drenched in sweat. Relief washed over them as they realized it had all been a terrifying nightmare. As they exhaled a sigh of relief, they noticed a small black figure at the foot of their royal bed—a tiny black scorpion, gazing at them before leaping off the bed. A voice resonated in the room, declaring, "These lands are not yours to rule," before fading away. Later that day, the court was inundated with reports and messages from nobles across the land, heralding unsettling developments.
