It appears the worst has come to pass – the Emperor has used his authority to force us out of a province, and as our officials retreat, so too do the magistrates of another realm advance, their merchants and military forces setting up shop there. The people of the domain are outraged, and many are calling for action to be taken to avenge this dishonor – but this would undoubtedly be rash in the extreme. Perhaps we could fight that realm, but the forces of the Emperor? And yet, in our minds, in our hearts, there is still a voice, whispering, tugging at our shame, our outrage – can we really do nothing? Stand by, while our honor is challenged?
