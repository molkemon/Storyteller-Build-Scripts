The Rending of the Realms has revealed much about the magical potential of chi and the spirits of the land. In particular, we have learned a great secret. the source of much of this chi and these spirits are the High Temples. Within each, deep inside the complex, rests a cyclopean Dames tear heart, overflowing with spiritual potency. With this ancient knowledge comes a ritual, one to bind the heart in chains infused with Korashi, forcing it to work as a beacon to spirits and a reservoir for their Chi. The spirits that come will fuel our ambitions with their life power for generations to come.
