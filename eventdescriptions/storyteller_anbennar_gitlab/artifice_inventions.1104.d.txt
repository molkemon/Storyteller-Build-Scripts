Have you ever had a thought you couldn't quite communicate? Have you ever remembered the tune of a song you couldn't express, or had a word on the tip of your tongue but couldn't get it out? Have you ever wished that you could escape the labyrinth of communication, and simply beam your thoughts directly into other people's minds? No? Well, now you can. The ThinkThought Transmitter started much like the Automated Translator, which utilizes the Tongues spell to allow communication between all languages. No matter how good a translation is, though, information is still lost. Some languages have words and concepts which others don't; differences in grammar often cause glitches within the magics of the spell; turns of phrase or metaphors are clumsily translated through both language and cultural context. As a whole, the Automated Translator has revolutionized the world, but it has not made communication perfect. To address this, the inventors of the ThinkThought Transmitter went further. With it, we no longer have to rely on words at all. Instead, the Transmitter is hooked up to one's mind, body, and soul, and utilizing the telepathic pathways developed by the TWave Transceiver, directly sends raw information to other people. Entire ideas, concepts, scenes, and memories can be sent, in vivid detail and involving all senses. Now, friends can relive each other's perspectives of the same event. Lovers can reminisce on times gone by. Enemies can truly experience the redhot rage that their counterpart feels for them. All communication has become simpler and more direct, for we no longer are limited by words.
