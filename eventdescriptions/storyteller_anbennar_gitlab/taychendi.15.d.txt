Even before our ruler could make his decision to annex the territories of the vassal realm, a renowned oracle residing in the vassal's capital pronounced the following prophecy. "The ruler asks for the vassal? The ruler asks too much; I grant it not. For as long as the ruler will live, the vassal will stand tall." The augury is spreading like wildfire; many in the court believe we should listen to the oracle, and the ruler's advisors counsel is to stop the integration, at least for now. In the vassal's lands everyone, from peasant to warlord, is questioning the ruler's right to rule them when even the gods are against it.
