For centuries, the hated Chaqanel have raided our clans, bestial as predators in their misapprehension of exchange. They do not perceive the fundamental truth of society; that give is as vital as take. At Dalcamguin, where the Chaqanel so often made their raidheart, we now place a qhatuwái. This commanding spot over the surrounding plains will offer safety, not threat, and the knowledge that each vast horizon is peaceful. The hateful energy of the Chaqanel will begin to dissipate here, gradually displaced by the flow of rightful exchange.
