As the first level of HulJorkad is made safe for their shamans, they begin the second large shaman conclave of this age. It ends up lasting only a couple of days as they explore the first level of the hold. When their findings reach the ruler, the monarch is shocked by the conclusion. In order to complete their Dookansearch, they shall need to pour all their efforts into exploring HulJorkad. To this end, their government shall need to be reformed and focused on repairing HulJorkad and gaining access to the second level. To ensure this goes well, the hold is going to need to become the seat of government. It will also need a sizeable company of experienced diggers, and its surroundings need to be secured so it cannot be threatened by other states.
