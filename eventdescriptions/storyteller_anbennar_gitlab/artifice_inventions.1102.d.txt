Maritime journeys, especially past the storms of Insyaa or the eldritch monstrosities of Aelantier, often can take months or years. Now, though, we have discovered something that could make it quicker. simply accessing different oceans, more specifically, those in the Plane of Water. Plane of Water Teleporters, first invented by the Deranne Trading Company in the Ruined Sea, where the boundaries between planes are weak, have proved to be a revolution in trade and navigation. The Plane of Water corresponds to our material plane, but with denser distances. so if one of our ships teleports to that plane, sails a mile, and then ends up back in our plane, they will have traveled at least twice as far. In addition, the relative peace and consistency of the Plane of Water makes this easy to accomplish. We must, of course, calculate our routes thoroughly beforehand, and must hope that our portals do not accidentally place us inside another ship, in front of an iceberg, or on top of a seaside cottage, but these are all small risks to bear. With the Plane of Water Teleporter, we do not need to worry about demons, nor fey, nor otherworldly monsters; only the occasional water elementals, who are rather friendly as long as we do not try fishing while we are there. In total, the seas are ours to sail, and intercontinental trade has never been easier.
