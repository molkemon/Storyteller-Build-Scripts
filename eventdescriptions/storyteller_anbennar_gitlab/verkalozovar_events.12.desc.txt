Utter silence overwhelms the empty cavern as the monarch ruminates in meditation, an assortment of incense and dwarven brews scattered across the floor, an invitation to break the silence if any were to observe such an array. Days passed. Then months. Sustained by an odd combination of chi and beer, the ruler was scarcely lucid when a ghastly chill crawled down their spine. Standing before the mountain king was a semitranslucent dwarf, donning a crown of pure cut nephrite and brandishing an embroidered cape. The OzAmekh sat upon the ground, took one sip of the brew offered, and uttered a single question. "You wanted to see me?"
