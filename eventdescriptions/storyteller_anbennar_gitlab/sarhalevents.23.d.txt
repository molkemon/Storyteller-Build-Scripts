You sit in your chambers. Your advisors have told you that the unrest that plagued your country shows signs of beginning again. The traitors within the realm arm themselves, and your rule is resisted at every turn. You hear a crack on your bedroom window, and then a loud boom, and you jump up, seeing the cloaked figure from five years ago climbing through. She flips her hood off and reveals her face; it is Hireet Smoketusk, one of the three hags of Yezel Mora, and her smile terrifies you even more than her size. She says, "The pendulum swings back. What I did for you will lead to more unrest, even worse than before. Unless you pay the price. A pound of flesh from every child in the capital, and your eternal fealty." She licks her lips and grins even wider. "And a few choice morsels to myself."
