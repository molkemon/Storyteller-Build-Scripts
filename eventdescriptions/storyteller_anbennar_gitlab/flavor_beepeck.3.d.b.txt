The petition was a success, mostly. The Emperor has acknowledged our claim, transferring de jure ownership of Orston and surrounding territories to us, but they have refused to press Exwes into actually relinquishing them. It is no great matter, though, with the legitimacy of the Emperor’s words, Orston will doubtless be more accepting of our rule once we have taken the area away from Exwes by force. We have made plans already; the halflings of Orston will be secured all the same.
