The volcano of Junansuar has played an important role in the faith of the Fangaulan peoples ever since the time of genie rule over these lands. For countless centuries, the Dendugma priesthood worshiped their furnacedeity in their forgetemples on the slopes of the volcano. Yet now, with the arrival of Surael’s truth to the sweltering jungles and savannas of Fangaula, their time has passed. In their stead, devout worshippers of Surael work tirelessly to forge the weapons needed by the faithful to do their part in the eternal struggle between Light and Dark. Near the top of the volcano, a ziggurat of basalt and obsidian has been erected. the Forge of Sunfire, the KurakdušAgšar. Here in the Sada Tiletamasa, as the native Fangaulan acolytes call it, the Dendugma steel weapons crafted in the many forges scattered across the volcano are imbued with Surael’s blessing by the priests before they are granted to deserving warriors willing to sacrifice everything to spread Surael’s Order ever further and chase the Darkness from the tenebrous corners of the world. Thanks to the ever faithful Servants of Surael, the secrets of forging Dendugma steel have survived the fall of the Dendugma priesthood. Their master smiths forge tools and weapons of incomparable quality to aid the faithful in their quest to bring His Light to all those still suffering in the grasp of Darkness.
