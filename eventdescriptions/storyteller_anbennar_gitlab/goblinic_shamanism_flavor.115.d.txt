Stories about the Khet, catfaced humans in the southern deserts, have gone around for a long time. How they are lazy and spoiled on the one hand, but on the other hand, can perform godlike feats that go beyond simple magic. Our shamans have now figured out their secret. They are not powerful despite their laziness and decadence; it is those things that bring them this power in the first place. By spending no energy or effort on moving or thinking, they have ascended to godhood. Furthermore, the shamans believe this feat can be achieved by goblins as well, which has led to a great many attempts by wealthy goblins to obtain this power.
