A huge crowd was called and even more people came, most of them Roilsardi immigrants or their offsprings. A giant and free banquet was planned in New Toarnaire as today is the Coronation's day, where the Crown of Thorns, crafted by skilled elven smiths, will be shown to the population. The monarch arrived on the stage where the three flags of Roilsard, Toarnen and Corvuria fly high and open the casket, revealing a crown of an unusual shape. it is a crown only composed of thorns. Made in gold and silver, each wavy band of thorns is in a different metal. The thorns are only on the outside to not hurt the wearer and is very light despite the use of dense metals. As the monarch put the shiny crown on his head, the flagbearer revealed a flag with a red background, the crowns of Roilsard and Toarnen merged in the center, the Corvurian white crow to the right and the Crusader's emblem to the left. The monarch then yelled to the crowd. "It is not the end to our glorious victories, Roilsard must be freed from impostors and kinglets. May Corin and our ancestors guide us across the seas."
