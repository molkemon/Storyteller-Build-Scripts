There are some in the clergy who see the illumination of the night sky as an excuse to tear down the very foundations of our faith. Specifically, they have demolished a temple. Believing that their place of worship is worth less than the value it could provide, the local priests have liquidated the temple’s building materials and everything inside it. They have offered this money to be used for the Godswatch’s construction, proclaiming that the noblest act of faith is the pursuit of knowledge. It is certainly an interesting theological sentiment, and the funds would not go unappreciated. However, some would argue that the clergy destroying their own temples might not be the best look for us.
