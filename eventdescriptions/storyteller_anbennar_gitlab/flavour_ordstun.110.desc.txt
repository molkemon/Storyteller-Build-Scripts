To reclaim the home of our forefathers, we require all dwarves of the Serpentreach to receive the finest training in the Dwarovar. The faults of our past kin must be rectified, to bring our peoples together once more, united. By recruiting the most grizzled of veterans to prepare the next generations of soldiers, may the ranks of Szetur Ôrdstunov strike fear to the foes of the Diamondsworn legions.
