Across our lands there has been a slow increase in marriages and other unions between ork and elf, and more to the point there have been many children from these unions who have begun to take prominent places in society, in a way that simply wasn’t possible with the rare children of elfgoblin unions in the voltoj. These halfelves, halforks seem akin to the reports we hear of halforks in Escann, and so for now we shall know them as such. While this belies their elven inheritance, it does show that our country sees our orkish roots as the strongest. Life isn’t always easy for these new children, often the products of some unions less pleasant and yet still kept and raised, in no less loving situations than their fullblooded brethren. Whatever their origin, there is much adaptation to be done and a long road ahead as we all adjust to a permanent future with better relations between the people of the woods. The destruction that they inherit shall hopefully be the last great conflagration in its history.
