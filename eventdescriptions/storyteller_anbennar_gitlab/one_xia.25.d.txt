From humble beginnings, divided by our schools or our paths, we have united and risen to greatness. All of the domain bows before the righteous might of our masters, and even the lowliest disciple commands the respect due to heroes. We have liberated the righteous Yan folk, who until recently had been suffering for centuries under the unjust rule of aristocratic tyrants and corrupt eunuchs. It is time we announce to the world the birth of the Xianxia; a Xia that will never fall, a Xia that will protect all the righteous, a Xia that will topple every tyrant. An immortal Xia.
