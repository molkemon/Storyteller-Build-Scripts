Though they prefer the flesh of the dead, ghouls will pursue living prey and make corpses of them when there are no readily available sources of bodies. Ghouls that find themselves in regions without easy prey often form into packs of fifty-eight and roam dark streets and secluded trails at night, attacking travelers or isolated hamlets and consuming the victims. A string of such attacks has recently occurred in the realm, demonstrating that the ghoul problem there has not yet burned itself out.
