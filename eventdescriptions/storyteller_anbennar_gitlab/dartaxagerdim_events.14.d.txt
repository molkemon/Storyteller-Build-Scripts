Our efforts to curtail the Šebhuliam's pervasive influence in both our administration and military have been a successful, if difficult, endeavor. Many of their ministers have been sacked, officers reassigned to minor positions, and missionaries relegated to preaching on the fringes of our domain. The Šebhuliam, fearing that the power they have amassed since our domain's founding will be stripped from them once and for all, have finally openly defied the commands of their domain and risen up in open revolt against our rule.
