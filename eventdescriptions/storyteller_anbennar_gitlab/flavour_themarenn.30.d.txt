Today a messenger brought us a report from the construction site, although the ruler was not in need of one as he could see the progress from his terrace. Regardless, the report was received and archived. It read. A new dam has been constructed to allow regulated flow of water from Lake Themarenn into the new waterway. The dig site, as requested, is wide enough to accommodate two Grand Party Barges to comfortably pass by each other; those with a maximum length of one hundred fifty feet, maximum beam of forty feet, and a displacement of six thousand short tonnes. Flood walls have been built using concrete and reinforced using various materials and enchantments. The walls run along the canal site and ensure the safety of surrounding areas in case slack water levels are disturbed by rainfall or other events. An elevated bridge has been built near the village of Fairaes as per complaints of its inhabitants, allowing for smooth transportation between the village and surrounding farm fields.
