The leader wakes. Their wrists are chained to their chair. Another leader sits opposite. It is a small table. There are no guards or weapons. Just the two of them, a steaming cup of black tea, and a flickering candle. "How did" the leader begins, cutting themselves off with furious coughs. The other leader sips from the cup, waiting the fit out, before speaking. "The two of us aren't so different, you know. We never were. We never have been." The leader does not respond. "I noticed," the other leader continues mildly, "that some of your retinue were missing last week. The others seemed oddly quiet, did you think?" The leader's claws twitch, glare intensifying. The other leader meets their gaze, unafraid. "I don't want to kill you," the other leader says. "You're valuable. But you're also loud. And awfully sloppy." The leader snarls, but the other leader continues, voice level. "Zokka gave us purpose. I think you've forgotten that. All you want to do is raze, and tear, and rip the world to shreds. But he only wants the First Sun. We're supposed to build the path for him; not break everything on the way." "You're nothing but an upstart slave." The leader finally roars, voice cracking, spittle flying. The other leader just smiles, finishing the drink. "Then why are you the one in chains?" Behind the other leader, four agents step into view. All were once Kaxhkeddax. They meet the leader's eyes with contempt. "This isn't a purge. Just a bit of restructuring."
