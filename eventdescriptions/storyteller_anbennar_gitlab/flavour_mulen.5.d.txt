Much time has passed since a host of harpies tentatively stepped into the mountain's darkness led by that goblin. Many feared the worst, that perhaps this had all been a trap laid by the goblins, but at last the expedition returned and its harpy soldiers collapsed with joy to again see the open sky. They report that they have explored the caverns and made contact, as well as conflict, with the goblin tribes of the Tree of Stone. With maps of the caverns and with our battered expedition as a pretext for war, we shall take the wealth of the mountain for Mulén.
