"It's gone, Thrain. All of it." Urist's words are clipped as he leaves the ruined hold, stepping out into the open air. Thrain pauses, aghast, as he takes in his friend's sootladen hair and dishevelled clothing. "What?" "Hellfire, I think. Must have come from the south. Caught sight of a plume of it as I made it out of the gates." The pair go quiet, looking around. This might have been a town once, trading with a nearby domain in gems and glass. Now, it's empty; as empty as the lands from here to a distant realm or the scorchmarked caverns that now, apparently, line the Serpentspine Mountains. "All of it, you said?" Thrain presses after a moment. Urist frowns, looking thoughtful. "I haven't heard from Dorri or Brina, so anything north of the Deepfang territories, at the very least. That means the west is probably gone, too, given the tunnels. But." He trails off, eyes flickering somewhere behind Thrain. "But?" "The Horn isn't linked to the rest of the range, is it? If it came from the gnolls, Krakdhûmvror might still be standing." Thrain turned to follow Urist's gaze, settling upon the snowcovered tip of the mountain in the distance.
