The permanent garrison of the Segbandalic Golden Cartel guards in Davharral has been established, providing the SGC a secure point of control over virtually all trade moving between Rahen and Bulwar. As the deployments here are very long term, the members of the garrison have often taken spouses and children along with them, which has resulted in the creation of a dwarven community in the city, and many dwarves stay in Davharral after their deployment is over, taking up jobs in other aspects of the rich trade flowing through the city.
