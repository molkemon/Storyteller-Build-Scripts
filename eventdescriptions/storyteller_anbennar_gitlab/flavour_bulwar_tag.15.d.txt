Tluukt and her pack have been driven back, their armies shattered and the one named "Cleaver of Realms" defeated by a realm that was not quite as broken as she had thought. The jubilation in Bulwar proper was unmatched, the sight and sound of thousands rejoicing at their renewed chance at both security and freedom defining the city for three whole days. The twelve families felt similarly, celebrating alongside league members while still using the occasion to prepare their next moves. Our victory is stupendous, and its happening has fixed the gaze of all civilized folk in Bulwar on our newly assembled league. What we advertise to the akalates will define the alliance, much akin to what we dedicated it to a few years ago. What shall we tell them, while the situation is still fresh?
