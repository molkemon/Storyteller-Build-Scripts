An unexpectedly late frost has set in across the Forest. Luckily the grain stores have been able to make up the difference and there have been no reports of famine from any of the provinces. The Cursed Ones have not been so lucky; there has been a rise in raiding for food. Such is the way in lean years. More unusual, however, is a report we have received from the west. A Cursed One band has arrived outside an army camp, asking for food. The band, clearly starving, likely had no other option and consisted mostly of women, children, and young men. They claim to have themselves been raided by a larger group of Cursed Ones.
