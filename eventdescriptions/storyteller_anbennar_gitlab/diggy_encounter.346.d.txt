We tried to get the Cube into some sort of confinement, a ruse which the Cube must have discovered at some point, spurring it to attack the expedition. Despite its slow speed, we couldn't stop it rolling and oozing over a few unfortunate men, covering them with its acidic gel and inflicting serious chemical burns. After its short-lived rampage, the monster scurried off into the dark. The horrifying wounds it caused are more than enough reason to leave it be.
