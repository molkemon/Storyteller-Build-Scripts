Humankind is known for their crafty ways to find nooks and crannies to fit into society; they are adaptable, and so they have adapted to our underground society. Alongside the long rail networks spanning the Serpentspine, some humans have set up shop and started bartering drinks and other commodities, creating new mercantile opportunities throughout our country. The only problem is that there is a lack of oversight in these shops, so should we crack down on them or let them continue their businesses?
