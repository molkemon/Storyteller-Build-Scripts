REPORT. Previous attempts to contact and work with the Lawaššari magi clans have proven more beneficial than initially anticipated. Our goblin applicants were accepted by the Lawaššari, and have since integrated themselves well into these clans, with some even rising to become teachers in their own right. This acceptance and integration by the Lawaššari is estimated to be a significant forward step in our efforts to ensure goblins are accepted and integrated into wider Bulwari society. This report recommends such efforts be emulated and continued in other areas with the aim of further positive effect to the state and its people.
