The Greentide began twenty years ago when Korgus Dookanson, son of the orkgod Dookan, led the clans of the Serpentspine on a great invasion of the surface world, sending their goblin slaves out on the front lines to be slaughtered. But the surface dwellers gained a divine champion of their own, Corin, the avatar of Agrados. Yesterday, these rival demigods finally faced each other in combat. There, the resulting clash ended in their mutual destruction. Corin and Korgus, both slain at each other’s hands. The resulting chaos was precisely what we goblins needed to escape. All over Escann, we broke free of our chains as the Greentide fractured. At the same time, the Forest Goblins would arrive from the south, escaping the vile Deepwoods to join their brethren in newfound freedom. And yet, the orks want to reenslave us, and the humans see us as invaders to be eliminated. It will take all the strength and cunning we have to not lose this one golden opportunity forever.
