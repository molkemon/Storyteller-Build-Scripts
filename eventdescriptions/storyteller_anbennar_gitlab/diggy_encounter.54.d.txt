Darkness overtook us, and winged beasts blotted out every source of light. Haunting moans mingled with terrified screams as the cloakers descended upon us. They flew silent like owls; the knowledge that they were there was more terrifying than their actual presence. No one dared light a torch until much later. It is hard to say how long it had been since the cloakers had left us behind. A look around tells the story of the attack. many bodies, bitten to death, and huge losses in our supplies.
