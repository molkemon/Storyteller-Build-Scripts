The news struck Orenvalyam like lightning. There had been an assassination attempt against the ruler at Korrimutren – the Fateweaver, which had given the gift of prophecy to so many, had been torn to shreds by an explosion of unimaginable power. The Threadbearer had been killed, alongside the chamber guards. Their names were forgotten the moment anyone heard the other casualty, though. Not the ruler, of course – his armour, scorched black and battered, had withstood the flame – but the next worse thing. Velara Denazuir, Elrandar Silverspite’s partner, confidant, second in command, a voice of moderation and morality, had been slain. They say they found Silverspite cradling her heat battered body in his arms, trembling in silent grief and fury.
