Ever since their proper organization after the creation of the academy, the political power held by the alchemists in Varainé has steadily grown. Now, the alchemists are using the influence they have earned to demand a more active role in the Varainéy government, and have presented several different options as to how they could play this role. While many of the proposals made do show promise, we do have our other elites to keep in mind, as well as having to avoid giving the alchemists too much power over our nation.
