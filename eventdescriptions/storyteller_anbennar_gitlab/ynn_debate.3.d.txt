After a century of division, our Overlord has seen to adapt Ynn River Worship to better fit the changing world we now live in, or at least, to better fit their opinion of it. They now promise a quieter period where a religious leader does not need to fear being assaulted over religious squabbles at any given feast. Now, we must ask ourselves to what extent we truly follow this new faith of theirs. Shall we join them in spreading the word of this new doctrine, or shall we bide our time in an effort to claim a doctrine of our own?
