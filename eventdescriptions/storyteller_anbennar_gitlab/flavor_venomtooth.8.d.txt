Bork was excited when he joined the army. Ever since he was a young boy, he always dreamed of killing all the enemies of the orks and Dookan. He even practiced with the village animals, though hated how everyone seemed to be angry at him when he killed the cats. When he was a teenager, he had the opportunity to work as an apprentice slave driver, being handed the whip that soon became his favorite tool. He would often be the first to hit human slaves with the lash, but had the restraint to make sure they could continue working. By the time he was eighteen, his request to join the Venomtooth army as a drill sergeant finally got approved. He was not quite sure what his new duties would entail, but the gold payments were too good to pass up. Bork showed up at the training fields and walked up to the two older orks there. Both were covered in battle scars, and the left tusk of one of them was chipped from a glancing blow by an Ancardian axe. The ork with the chipped tusk handed Bork a whip and explained, “We heard of how you were one of the best slave drivers at that mine in Entelaham. The slaves you oversaw were the most productive and efficient of the whole mining complex. To us, that means you should be great at instilling discipline in rabble. That is why we want you to be a drill sergeant so these unruly younglings properly shape up.” Bork grabbed the whip given to him. Though not as sharp as the one he used at the mines, he smirked as he looked at some conscripts chatting nearby. He walked over to them and whipped them both. “Stop talking and get in formation.” Over the next few weeks, Bork lashed conscripts for every infraction. By the end, he had the soldiers follow his every command, just in time for his troops to join the march towards the west.
