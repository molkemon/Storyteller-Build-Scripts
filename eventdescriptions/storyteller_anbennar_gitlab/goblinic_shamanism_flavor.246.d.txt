One of the more prominent Feykeepers has managed to make a deal with a Fey that they claim would be beneficial to the realm, should they give them sufficient resources. The Feykeeper in question claims that they have been offered passage deep into the Feywild, where they will be able to find great riches and be bestowed with great wisdom. Naturally, such an expedition comes with significant risk and will require the realm to finance the expedition with both manpower and equipment.
