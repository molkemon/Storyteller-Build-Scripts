The House of the ruling dynasty has enjoyed increased support in the realm lately. As a result, their proponents have grown bolder. Those within the church speak of the princes of the realm in royal terms, comparing them to ancient Castanorian kings. Our people have not forgotten their role in securing our nation's sovereignty and being praised as the last in a line of saviors certainly benefits the monarch. Nevertheless, calling the ruler a king, even in the context of a sermon, risks weakening our federal institutions.
