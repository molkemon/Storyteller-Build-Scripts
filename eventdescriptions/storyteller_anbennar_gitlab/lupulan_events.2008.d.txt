A letter has arrived from a certain domain today. For months, their armies have been involved in a pitched battle against a particularly old and powerful Hantu Raya that seems to have made it its personal mission to murder every single person living within their borders. While the power of their entire army would surely be enough to overwhelm the Hantu Raya in fair combat, the blasted spirit has adopted hit and run tactics. The conflict is therefore rather like attempting to hunt down a madman with demonic powers, and even all their men cannot canvas the entire rainforest. Therefore, that domain has appealed to us as their overlord for our assistance in this matter.
