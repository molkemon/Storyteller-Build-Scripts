Two centuries ago, Bwa Dakinshi descended into anarchy, the empire collapsing as the ruling clan was ousted and various warlords vied for supremacy. Our great empire is naught but history, our cities empty gravemarkers, and our people suffering and enslaved by foreign raiders. Every claimant to the title of Bwa Dakinshi must defeat their rivals in order to bring an end to this era of calamity and suffering.
