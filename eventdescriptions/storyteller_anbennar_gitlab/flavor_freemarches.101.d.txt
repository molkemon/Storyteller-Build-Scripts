From the log of Captain Taruk Freeorc, today was the fifth day of portage. Everything must be carried on foot over these accursed mountains. The rivers are too narrow or too rough to be canoed; we hope that when we break through to the other side there will be slow rivers to descend down the mountain range. In addition, we were attacked today. Our bond breakers rendered their charge ineffective, but there were far more of them than we could easily handle. Several good men died in the fight. My brother Ratak was struck in the chest by a Cursed One arrow, and one of the Gnomes is attempting to save his life. Addendum. Ratak has perished. We will bury him at dawn.
