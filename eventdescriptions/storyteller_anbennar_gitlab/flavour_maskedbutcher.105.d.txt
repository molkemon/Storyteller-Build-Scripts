It's gone. The blade dug deep and true, and it just slipped off, like taking off a uniform after a long, long day. I feel relieved, actually. Nobody came to save me. I begged and I pleaded. I cried. I screamed their names, and they didn't answer. They took the thing that isn't mine anymore, and they put it with the rest, and the drumbeats ceased. Who does it belong to now?
