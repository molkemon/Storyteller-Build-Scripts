The various advisors that serve the court in the capital are paid well for their brilliant minds, as they pursue inquiries into the most fruitful paths forward for the realm. But there are downsides to employing such curious creatures. One of them has recently begun to note the ruler's late night excursions and unusual nature, and so took it upon themselves to investigate further. If they were simply a peasant with a wandering mind, this would not be troubling, due to the longstanding measures taken to preserve the identity of the monarch. However, likely due to their proximity to the monarch, the advisor was able to gather evidence and nurture suspicion, until they fully pierced the veil. They have now realized the ruler's true nature, and are threatening to expose them before the country and the world.
