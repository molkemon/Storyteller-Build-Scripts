There is a party at the Red Citadel. All the eunuchs and the royal dynasty are celebrating. The ruler excuses himself and leaves to his room. Inside, he is looking at the Mask of Many Faces, thinking of all that the empire is and how overwhelmingly competitive the rule is. He thinks of all that he had to achieve to become a legitimate ruler and he thinks of all, much more than his predecessors. He ponders this cycle; every ruler has to fight against the legitimacy of the previous ruler, and rulers that lived thousands of years ago. This endless madness is due to end one day, in blood and fire. What happens when there are no more titles worth claiming? No more lands to reclaim? What happens when the task becomes impossible? He believes every lie his dynasty and the recordkeepers have been telling. This sends him into an emotional spiral of madness that ends with him about to jump from the alcove.
