Our spies in that province have dwelt there for so long that they may well know the city better than its very inhabitants. Their expeditions into the nooks and crannies which line that great city have uncovered many things, the newest of which is a handsome find indeed; the handsomest of all. For the Crown of Dameria, once thought lost, is now found. Once a circlet of the purest silver, adorned with various precious stones, it was made for Marion Silmuna, the son of Auci the Eighthborn and Munas Moonsinger; the first dynast of the House of Silmuna. It came into our hands splintered, for it was broken when that province fell – by who, none know. Nevertheless, through several long and trying ordeals, our spies were able to retrieve all of its fragments as well as a few of the jewels. The next logical step, then, is to decide what is to be done with it.
