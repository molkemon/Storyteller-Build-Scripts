The Rothé is a curious creature, living both in the light above and the dark below. They are excellent animals; they are strong, their meat is tender, and their disposition is agreeable. It is therefore no wonder that the Serpentspine once housed many of these beasts. Nowadays most are imported from the surface, but some of them managed to survive the tunnels and caves on their own. A herd of these is just ahead, their fur long and tangled, their horns spiraling along their heads. They would make an excellent addition to our diet, but who knows what strength they possess if they survived here?
