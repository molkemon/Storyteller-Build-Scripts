At dawn, the two parties met upon the western banks of the mighty Vroren which once marked the northern edge of the world, at least for the Cannorians. One group. the soldiers of the country, their banners held high and accompanied by their monarch. The other group. a band of men on horseback, at the front a man draped in furs both foreign and familiar to the monarch, a knife on his belt and a flatbow over his shoulder. "Monarch, we come in hope of righting a great and terrible wrong imposed upon our people. Many years ago, we were forced from our lands by the orkish menace you vanquished." The man gestured to his people as he continued, "Some of my kin wish for autonomy and the right to rule ourselves, but I humbly accept the rule of your grace. I do not aspire for kingship, I simply want my sons and daughters to know the homeland of our forefathers." A moment passes, and the monarch makes their choice.
