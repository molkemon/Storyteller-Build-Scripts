Firm footsteps mark the expedition's hourlike march. Then, pain screams. For just a second's fraction our march abruptly stops in bewilderment. At the same time as the pain echoes through the rocks, it is followed by another scout's yell, "Goblins." The same alarm multiplies now, accompanied by agony cries as they rush forward to help their scouts. Dust rises from their run as their warriors take the lead, wielding their hammers and axes ready at the sound of their war cry and the guttural shrieks of their enemies.
