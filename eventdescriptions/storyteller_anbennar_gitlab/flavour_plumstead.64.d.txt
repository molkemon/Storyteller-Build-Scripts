It was a celebratory day, as the two lovebirds could finally tie the knot and declare their eternal love for each other. Ever since the nightmare at the Buffalo Skull Ranch, people had started to rethink the hostilities between their tribes. And now, two Epednar from two different tribes could love one another in the open, without the fear of death of vengeance. The only shots that day were celebratory and filled with the finest plum brandy.
