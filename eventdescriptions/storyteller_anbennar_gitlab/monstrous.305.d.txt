Since the first days of harpy rule, the human villages under our protection have had two means of paying tribute in gold or in men. This system is hated by the poorer villages that could not afford to pay in any means other than their population, and now that we are making attempts at reform we have commenced an overhaul of the system in order to better align it with human sensibilities. Although the new system of tribute is graduated based on village prosperity, it favors compensation in gold rather than men to make up for the resulting treasury shortfall, and it is already apparent that the demonsterization of our tribute system will result in some rather dire demographic trends. In a series of emergency meetings, our advisors have come up with a plan to address this problem. The advisors tell us that with some efforts to spread the word, bribes to immigrants, and some judicious usage of enchantment magic, we might be able to entice enough of the men of surrounding nations to come to our domain to return our demographics to acceptable levels. Although expensive and somewhat radical, this is the best idea our greatest minds have been able to come up with that doesn’t involve a wholesale return to the old system. What shall we do?
