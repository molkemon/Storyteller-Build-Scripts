The Accretive and Transmutative Paths, which together are called Righteous, each refer to a Path. This path is not a way of living, but rather refers to the very essence of life. Chi. This Chi flows through the realm like a river, winding through thickets and thorns, through prairies and meadows, and down great waterfalls and through terrible rapids. Yet never has there been a fork in the Path until now. The Rending of Realms has begun, and we must choose which stance to assume.
