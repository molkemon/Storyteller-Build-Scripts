Greycliff, another night, hours of pondering, one more time. Until the ruler heard the song, that is. In the quiet of the night, beneath the silver moon, she gazes out the window, humming a simple tune. Dreams of love once lost, now a whisper on the breeze, but she knows deep inside, her heart's never truly at ease. Walking down the winding road, with hope lighting her way, every step she takes, she knows there will be a brighter day. In the rustling leaves, she hears a song so pure, a melody of love that time cannot obscure. Through fields of gold and amber, she dances in the sun, with every twirl and sway, a new journey has begun. In the tales the wind tells, she finds her spirit strong, and knows that in her heart, love has been there all along. The mountains and the valleys echo with her song, of a love she is yet to find, but she knows it will not be long. For in the folk tales sung, by the firelight's gentle glow, she hears the promise of love in the stories long ago. So she sings her song of hope, as the stars light up the night, with a heart open wide, she knows love is shining bright. It is never too late to find a love so true, in the whispers of the wind, she will find a love that is new.
