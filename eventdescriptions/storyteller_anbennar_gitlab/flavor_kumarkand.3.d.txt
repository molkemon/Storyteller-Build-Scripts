It is no secret that Akalšes servitude under Varamhar is the darkest part of our long and proud history. However, there is light on the horizon as our fellow vassal Kumarkand has sent us an unsanctioned envoy representing a brewing plot to overthrow our overlord Varamhar. The plotters are not a scheming group of nobles but represent nearly everyone in Kumarkand, down to the most obscure of village elders. The envoys ask for Akalšes marital aid in overthrowing Varamhar, not only as fellow subjects but as Bulwari brothers who are the legitimate heirs of these lands. In exchange, they promise to give us Današ Naza once we have gained our independence and offer to help transport five thousand members of Današ Naza’s resistance cell over to us. This offer might just be our best opportunity to overthrow Varamhar, those wretched foreign invaders who dare to subjugate the rightful heirs of Bulwar. However, should we win against Varamhar, it is not clear if Kumarkand will bow down to the king of kings or replace the tyranny of Varamhar with their own.
