The dragon turtle reclined in their lair. "When your predecessor left off, we were in the six hundreds. Would you like to continue on from that point, or are there other gaps in the record that need filling?" The scribe looked up at him. "I think it's best if we continue on from the stopping point, so that nobody gets missed." "Very well, then." The dragon turtle cleared their massive throat, and began to speak. "Kanoa, son of Nalu. Malea, daughter of Nalu. Anela, daughter of Nalu. Mahina, daughter of Nalu. Mano, son of Liko. Keanu, son of Liko. Liko, son of Liko. Kailani, daughter of Liko..." As the scribe knotted each name down, his lips moved to repeat them.
