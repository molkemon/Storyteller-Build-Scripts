A delegation of Swamp Trolls has arrived at a local province with an urgent message. There is a kind of spirit in this swamp, they claim, which can replace a living person, mimicking their form and personality so perfectly that not even their loved ones can tell the difference. If left unchecked, these spirits can consume and replace entire communities. The trolls have shamans which can detect these spirits, and while normally they exterminate any infestation themselves, now it is one of our own villages that must be destroyed. These doppelgangers pose a tremendous risk to all living inhabitants of the swamp, but the swamp trolls fear to provoke our retribution, so this delegation was sent to explain the situation to us. It is well known that Swamp Trolls cannot lie, but they can misdirect through clever wording.
