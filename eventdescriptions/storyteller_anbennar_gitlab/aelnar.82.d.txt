The death of the late emperor has left a yawning chasm in the halls of power. Already, officers and administrators are beginning to sharpen their knives for a coming power struggle. All eyes now turn to the monarch, who must prove themselves worthy of Sicrheior's legacy before the glory of the Empire is drowned once again in a new generation of blood. Fortunately, the blood of a genius is strong. There is no doubt that fools will see the folly of their doubts in due course.
