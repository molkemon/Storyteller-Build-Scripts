Our policy of mandatory Elven has been nicknamed "Alfrbrut", which corresponds to "Elves’ Toll" in the local Gerudian dialect spoken in Celmaldor. It appears that the people do not like having to learn a minority language to get a good job. Some use the nickname affectionately, almost gently ribbing the monarch for his decision; others use it as if it was poison on their tongues, and a few, though not many, seem likely to use it as grounds to revolt. We could loosen the fluency standards to mollify the second group of people, since not all of them need to speak at the levels found at universities or in the Magisterium, but it would lead to a lot of gibberish being passed off as Elven.
