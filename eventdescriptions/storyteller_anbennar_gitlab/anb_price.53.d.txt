The busiest port of the realm was recently crowded with the most ships it has ever seen, as a major fleet assembled and departed for Aelantier. But they are not alone; they are merely the first. Across Cannor, adventurers and settlers are departing for this new and old world. This influx to Aelantier has increased the demand for naval supplies, such as seasoned timber for ships. As a result, virtually all markets in Cannor and Escann have reported an increase in the price of naval supplies, and the ripple effect is bound to spread south and east before long.
