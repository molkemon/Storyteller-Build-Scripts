No longer will the swallows have to fear for their lives at the hand of the Command, for now they are the Command. It is time for all hobgoblins to enter a new chapter of demonsterization and reforms. The Swallows of Lapnam Amrik who have long lost the stamp of monstrousness are now in control together with the current ruler. A new administration has been set up in Sarilavhan, but they are expecting pretender rebellions in the hobgoblin territories of the Command for years.
