While ghouls are regarded as sentient undead, they are largely driven by their bestial needs for flesh, making them even more terrifying. A ghoul that is exposed to powerful magics, however, will transform into a far more dangerous creature; a Ghast. With strength and cunning far beyond those of a normal ghoul, Ghasts lead large packs of ghouls on raids against the living and delight in the destruction they cause. Notable for their horrific stench, the smell of rot and fermentation wafts around a ghast at all times, strong enough to make hardy warriors retch. Word has filtered back to the capital of one such creature being sighted in the realm, leading packs of dozens of ghouls and leaving a trail of destruction in its wake.
