In many other nations, aristocrats enjoy much greater power than here. In this realm, money is what matters, not rank. A commander, a person lacking neither, is well aware of this. 'Ultimately,' they say, 'if this realm is to flourish, it needs a strong ruler. The military is standing by you, your monarch, if you would be willing to restore some of its old glory. Many among the nobility would be willing to back such a cause with considerable funds.'
