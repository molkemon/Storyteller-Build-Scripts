After a healthy amount of bickering with archivists, historians, and translators, our engineers have finally completed the new artillery contraptions based on the old cannons that we received from a distant realm many centuries ago. There are many seemingly small but deceptively useful changes compared to the design of our regular cannons, which should provide us with a crucial edge over our enemies. The end result might not look as elegant as the description of those antique dwarven cannons, but field tests suggest they are not lesser in their effectiveness.
