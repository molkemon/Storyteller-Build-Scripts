Yanshen's lifeblood is also one of her greatest scars; the old Golden Highway. An everlasting mark of the elves and their imperial ambitions, the road is often considered the only good thing the elves ever did. As our efforts to properly renovate it progress, growing calls from our own administration as well as the merchants travelling it come to give it a more fitting name; one to cast down the last of Jaher's influence on Yanshen.
