A visiting group of Bulwari merchants, adventurers, and other individuals of means have been visiting the domain as of late. This is usually of no concern since after a week of excess, visiting parties move on. But the current group seems exceptional, having more or less made themselves residents in one of our establishments. This is in conjunction with the requests being made of the staff becoming more and more extravagant and dangerously reckless. The leader of this band assures us they have almost had their fill and offers a handsome sum in recompense for damages, to of course be entrusted to the state to ensure it reaches the proper hands. Of course, we could kick them out now and likely save ourselves a great headache.
