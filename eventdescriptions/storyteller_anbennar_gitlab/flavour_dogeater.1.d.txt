Boss, we have been capturing more and more wolves from the hills and mountains. These dogs are hardy and angry for sure; many of our lads got killed by them. It was quite disgusting to see them eating Dok. I still miss him a lot, and I get dreams about his death every now and then. But we think we can make more of these wolves, keep the strongest ones alive, let them kill the weak ones, just like us. After a year, we should have enough strong ones to give them out to the rest of the boys. We just have one question though. is there something else you have in mind for them?
