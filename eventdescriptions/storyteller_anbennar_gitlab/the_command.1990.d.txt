It was a bright afternoon when a parcel addressed to the Grand Marshal arrived at the War Room, the courier breathless and drenched in sweat. It was not the odor of the package that first caught the ruler’s attention, but rather the petrified look in the courier’s eyes. There was no need to gaze into the box, for through the cracks in the lid it was already clear that this parcel contained the head of the Great Command’s envoy to the Tiger Command. A crude way to declare war, but the message certainly came across. With the beginning of the Great Insubordination, the Tiger, Elephant and Dragon Commands have moved their war camps and capitals farther from Shamakhad in preparation for war. These new capitals have all been fortified such that successfully sieging them without weakening their defenses first would be near impossible. We must blow down the Tiger Command fortifications with a concentrated artillery strike, dwarven siege technology must be used against the Elephantine walls, and the metals that fuel the great cannons of the Dragon Command must be seized.
