With the boom of trade our continent has felt for the last decades, many governments, both Alecandi or Nékhei, have been holding long and thorough debates regarding customs and the dealing with goods from abroad. Some, led by the example of Ameion, lean toward isolationism; while the rising power, Empkeios, is leading the effort for open customs and free trade. The latter has now issued an ultimatum to every other nation in our region. We can either fall in line, forcing our trade policy to be aligned with theirs for the foreseeable future, or we may face the consequences of not opening our borders to their traders.
