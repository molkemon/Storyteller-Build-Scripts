Seemingly guided by a will other than their own, the humans of Yarikhoi have denied the gifts that we so generously offered them. Normally, such insolence would earn swift and brutal retribution, but we do plan on conquering them anyway, so payback was already on its way. This will, of course, make our destined conquest of their lands more difficult, but it is nothing that the might of the ogres will be unable to overcome.
