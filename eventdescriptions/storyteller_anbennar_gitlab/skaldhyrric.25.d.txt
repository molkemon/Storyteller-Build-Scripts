Every so often, the Skalds of Gerudia gather together in a single place to recite the great poems of their faith en masse, in friendly competition with each other. The spectacle is one of the most sacred and exciting things that can be experienced by the poor and destitute of society, as all are invited and encouraged to attend, from noble to commoner. Such competitions are in many ways the greatest rituals of the Skaldhyrric Faith, and even those attendees who are not believers are overawed by the musical and magical display of the event.
