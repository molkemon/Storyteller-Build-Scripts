Because Dookan was able to enslave the goblin pantheon even though he himself was imprisoned, our shamans from the Dookan cult have decided that the whole imprisonment thing wasn’t doing much good. They have decided that they need to set him free, hoping he would look kinder upon the goblin kind after a good deed. After a long search, they found a long metal chain suspended from a crane, dangling down into a deep pit. Assuring them that this was not an old mining outpost but rather Dookan’s prison, the shamans have broken the chain, and are now celebrating Dookan’s release with glee and fervor.
