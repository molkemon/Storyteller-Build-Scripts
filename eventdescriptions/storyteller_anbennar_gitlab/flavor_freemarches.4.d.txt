The day they were dreading has arrived. Rumours are spreading of a Cursed One attack on an outlying village. Ten years of peace are over, and the resumption of the war against the Cursed Ones has left the capital a very melancholic place. Meanwhile, some are encouraging the populace to be glad the peace happened, as well as to celebrate Falah and Muroga. They are even calling for Muroga to be officially recognized as an Avatar of Falah.
