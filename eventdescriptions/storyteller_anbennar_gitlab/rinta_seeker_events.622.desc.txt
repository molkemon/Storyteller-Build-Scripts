After many years of debates, months of arguments and insults shared between Mwalim and Darwiiz, it seems that the greatest project undertaken by scholars and mages has been initiated. an expedition of sorts. Dedicated to exploring the world, thousands of volunteers, whether they be regular citizens on their Ruaa pilgrimage to the wisest of scholars, will be sent off to visit different lands and cultures. While we have knowledge of the people who live outside the Jasiir Jadid, the world at large is a mystery. The goal is to dissipate the mystery of the world and to further enhance our catalog of knowledge. Some of the expeditions are to Shangra, Khabtei Teleni, and Zitan Tomon. We have been told to ask around for volunteers and organize our own proposal for whom we will send and to where. I have brought the best cartographers around to help you make your proposal for the high scholars. The Saaxir will organize their own expeditions and locations and will take our proposals and crew into consideration for the final expedition. Ultimately, they will decide on a few locations, and we will receive a notice to vote when they require our input. I believe there will be three rounds of these. The deadline will be in a few months. I wish you well, sir.
