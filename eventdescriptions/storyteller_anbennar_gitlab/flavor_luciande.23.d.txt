A coalition against the menace known as Luciande has been formed. The aim is to drive a stake through the heart of Lucian sil na Toars once for bringing slave trade back to Cannor, twice for the massacre of the Moon Elves and the massacre of the Red Masquerade, and thrice for being a sharp-fanged abomination. Luciande is heavily fortified and situated in the most easily defended part of Cannor. We appreciate anyone who is willing to answer our just cause against this scourge.
