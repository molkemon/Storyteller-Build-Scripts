Sometimes it is hard to justify why my disguises, or lack thereof, seem to work as well and as consistently as they do. People only see who they want to see, as demonstrated to me every day, but it is a wonder that they never recognize me. If I so desired, it would be relatively easy to change my appearance, but seeing as how the way I look now is perfect, it is best if that did not have to happen. If someone were particularly observant, they would remember my amulet. Not many do. My physical form is incredible. I do not just mean in the appearance sense; being able to hide my tail and my ears means that not even the other HuliJing could recognize my form unless they already knew me. Most of my sisters prefer to take the form of foxes, something which has made everyone in the realm more than happy to spoil any foxes they find. There is something of a game we play, where a HuliJing tries to flush out her sisters. It is not for any particular reason, just for a good laugh. When a fox sauntered up to me today for a treat, I gave it one. Once it was chewing on the scraps I gave it, I said hello to my sister. They will never respond the first time around, so I had to lay it on a bit thicker. I took my amulet off and turned around, brushing the fox's face with my tail. As suspected, they choked with surprise, morphing back into their humanoid form so that they could choke on their food scraps better. I patted their head and asked if they were alright. They nodded, still coughing. With that done, I slipped the amulet back around my neck, then stayed for an extra minute to make sure their food went down. After that, I waved them off again as they turned back into a fox and sauntered off. Diary, I bet you they will remember how I look for sure.
