Tolerating the different and the unknown is often easier said than done. When hostilities began to break out, the monarch declared an edict of religious tolerance, stating that all religious groups in the greater Raheni tradition were to be considered as equals. The steward and herald have dispersed the edict far and wide, yet it seems more and more that the ruler seems to care little about actually enforcing their words. Several rogue Jireedeehana have taken to terrorizing local practitioners of the older High Philosophy schools, and as a result numerous instances of extrajudicial killings and reprisals have taken place. Several villages and small towns have begun to tear themselves apart as the old and the new grasp for each other's throats. The doctrinal chasm widens by the day, and unless the ruler puts force behind their words then the violence will only continue to spiral.
