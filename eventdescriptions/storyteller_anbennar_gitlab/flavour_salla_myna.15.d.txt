Bricena and Arfil were having a picnic together atop the Ciranmyna peak. The great hawks seemed far more willing to tolerate their presence than they had a year ago. They noticed that the populace of the domain was starting to lose its reservation about the great hawks. Suddenly, a fledgling great hawk snuck up on them and stole some of their food before flying off, which brought much laughter from the pair.
