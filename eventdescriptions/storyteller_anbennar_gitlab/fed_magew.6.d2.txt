The mage has been nothing but a curse for the federation. They and their ilk have brought countless horrors upon the peoples of the lake. Whatever the means, be it magic or common deception, they have poisoned the minds of others within the federation. Having thrown common sense to the wind, they now seek to insinuate this rot into our core foundations and beliefs. Diplomacy has failed, and once again the mage brings nothing but misery to the lakes.
