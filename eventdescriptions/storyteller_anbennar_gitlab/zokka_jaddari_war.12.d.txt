With Surael's light having cleansed the darkness of Zokka's pack, much of the land they once held has fallen into our control. A notable exception to this is the province of Hašr, where a resistance movement has cropped up under Marudeen szalAkur, The Hašr Hawk. After years of public sacrifices, destruction, and terror wreaked on their people by the Gnolls, they have finally stepped out of the long shadow which Zokka cast and into freedom. Shocked by Sareyand's treacherous war against us for their former lands, which include the whole of Hašr, Marudeen has chosen to stand with our legions against the invading Sareyandi army in an attempt to assert their independence. Aided by the rapidly organizing armies of Hašr, our beleaguered legions may have the support they need to crush the oncoming elven onslaught and live to spread the light of Surael another day.
