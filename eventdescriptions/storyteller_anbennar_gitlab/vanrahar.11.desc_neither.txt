The Salahad coast is ours. Aside from worthless sand, there are a few important trading ports along the coast, such as the city of Davharral. Though unwise to nurture the barren desert, Davharral is still an important Raheni city, and leaving it outside our control makes little sense. Though culturally Bulwari, these lands are far closer to Rahen’s heartland than Bulwar’s. It is only natural that the Raheni should dominate these lands rather than those across the vast Salahad. The only reason we haven’t yet was because of nomadic desert tribes. Attacking settlements and retreating swift enough to avoid retribution, their raids made any presence on the coast a drain. With firearms becoming more prevalent, we have started to repel these raiders with ease. Not that we wish to dominate the Salahad desert, but the easy-to-provision coast is good enough for us. With new settlements of harimari, all shall soon come to recognize that the Salahad coast is part of the Raj’s natural borders.
