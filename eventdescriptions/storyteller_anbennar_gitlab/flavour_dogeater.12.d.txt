Unting is a natural thing for folks to do when there are no worthy opponents to fight, and many an aspiring warrior has bloodied themselves fighting a beast to prove themselves worthy of a real position in the horde. However, just as many fools have gotten crushed facing something they were not prepared for. This was what the clan imagined happened after Dûsh went missing for weeks during a hunt, at least until he showed up carrying the barely recognizable carcass of a gigantic adult bear on his back, seemingly beaten to death with his bare hands. If there was any doubt Dûsh is our strongest warrior, it is long gone.
