Ashen skies. Foreboding news has arrived from the capital of that country. the ruler of that country has revealed them to be a dreaded WitchKing, an evil sorcerer of such power as to rival Nichmer of old. Their rise to power threatens to spread darkness across the land and beyond their borders, and their subjects live in terror of their magical tyranny. Droves of refugees from the capital and other cities in that country have fled overseas to Isobelin, hoping to find some safety across the vastness of Uelos’s Lament. We welcome all newcomers, but some in the city fear that the ruler’s agents may be secreted among them, sent to spread their wicked influence to our city.
