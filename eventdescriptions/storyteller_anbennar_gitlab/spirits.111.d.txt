The shamans of the Mystical Accord faith are wellliked and respected as intermediaries between the corporeal and the spiritual. Unfortunately, frauds and tricksters sometimes misuse this trust, as has now happened to the nation. A shaman claimed to be able to communicate with a great yet unknown spirit who could not only bless the court but the whole nation. The advisors were skeptical until the shaman performed a spirit summoning in front of the ruler themselves. In hindsight, it seems to have been a simple magic trick without any spiritual involvement to speak of. After agreeing to a ritual, the shaman requested preparation time and a private room. They then snuck away and stole money from the coffers, and they have not been seen ever since.
