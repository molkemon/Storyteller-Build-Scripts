It has been a long and arduous journey, one that has taken them through every equally horrifying and disparate region of the jungle. They have witnessed the full spectrum of this land's offerings, from the darkest evil to the most bountiful beneficence. Through it all, they persevered, united in their quest to find a new home for their people. In these lands, where disease and death loomed, they will bring forth new life. Where shadowy forests and lurking monsters once held sway, the laughter of children will soon drown out those former horrors. Amidst the overgrown ruins, long forgotten by time, they shall raise a garden that will endure, a symbol of their resilience. Preparations have already begun, and the survivors of their grueling trek have already taken well to the environs. Trees are felled to provide materials for shelter, tents are repurposed into homes, and sturdy palisades rise from the earth. The energy with which they work is fueled by the hope of a people who dream of building something of enduring worth, something that justifies every agonizing step they took to reach this most hallowed place. They are driven by the belief that the trials and tribulations of their migration were not in vain, and that their new home will be a testament to the strength and unity of their people, a place where generations to come will thrive.
