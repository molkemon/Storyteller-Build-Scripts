Excavating tunnels in the remote mountains of northern Yanshen is usually a mundane affair. Yet a recent discovery has stirred unease. In a disused shaft in a local province, several miners have uncovered a stone stele carved with strange runes. Its form bears clear resemblance to the ritual iconography of the Devouring Path, and the surrounding chamber is littered with wax-sealed bones and shattered soul vessels. After careful examination, scholars have confirmed the site to be a remnant left behind by followers of the Devouring Path. Among the workers, the place is already being referred to as the "Old Altar of the Devourer," with some even claiming that echoing voices rise from the shaft during the night. It is time to decide how we shall respond to this buried trace of faith.
