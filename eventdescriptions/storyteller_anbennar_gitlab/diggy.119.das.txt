The dwarves of Ovdalazan have dug deep and uncovered many lost regions of the hold. Its remoteness in the Tree of Stone has led many dwarves to the hold in an effort to escape others, be they other dwarves or surface races, and bond in shared desire for isolation. This imitation of the ancient hold has not gone unnoticed by its population; even now, the flag of Ovdalazan is flown alongside our own banners. It seems the hold is beginning to consider itself a nation, something we may have to deal with.
