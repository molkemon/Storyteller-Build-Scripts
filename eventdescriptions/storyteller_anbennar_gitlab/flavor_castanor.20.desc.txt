It seems that the exploits and actions of the legion have spread far and wide. Everywhere they go in our lands, the legion has people from all walks of life asking to join; from the poorest of peasants to the most privileged of noble scions. It is well known among the poor that serving in the legions can be a ticket out of poverty, with high wages and riches to be earned from loot. Meanwhile, serving in the officer corps is almost a necessity for nobles looking to advance their family and station in government and the high society of Castanor. Should we let these recruits join the legion, potentially ruining their cohesiveness and character, or direct their energies elsewhere?
