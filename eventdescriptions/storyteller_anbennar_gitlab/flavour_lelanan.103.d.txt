As with the Halfling and Dunesole Ogre, not all love was romantic in source. Sometimes a mutual love was all it took to sand down a relationship which starts so coarse. The Halfling was a renowned chef, traveling Sarhal in search of a new ingredient. But to the towering desert Ogre, he himself was a meal so expedient. But the Halfling made an offer, a bargain to save his life with his great cooking skills. Surely he could find the ogre a wife. Now it has been thirty years since, and the two are suspectly both still single, but you will find them in the Halfling’s restaurant, always willing to mingle.
