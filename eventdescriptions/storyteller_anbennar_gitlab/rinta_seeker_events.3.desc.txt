During a recent archaeological dig, certain books from scholars of old were found in fairly good condition. The only problem with them is that they seem to have been placed there purposely. Whoever dumped the books there is also assumed to be the reason why the books that we found had been written over with crazy ramblings. Looking at the books, it seems they could still be restored to some quality, though, as the texts written by these scholars are still visible. We could take the time and attempt to remove the ink, restoring the original manuscripts to as fine a quality as possible, but it will cost us a good portion of money. Hopefully, the restored texts will provide us with broader knowledge of the world.
