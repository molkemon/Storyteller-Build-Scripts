From thin air and with a brilliant flash of light, the leader of the country's Imperial War Wizard detachment appeared before the monarch and their advisors, flanked by their subordinates and several armed men, some of whom the assembled dignitaries recognized as their own servants, once assumed to be completely loyal to the dynasty. "The Imperial Dynasty is ascendant. By the Emperor’s will, the entire realm shall be truly united. Your time as an independent country is no more. Swear allegiance to the Emperor, and serve Anbennar."
