With Lokemeion firmly in our hands, a debate has started in our government on what to do with the wealth of knowledge preserved in the Degalistéa. The Darasgenéa and the Kelasgenéa clans, always the practical sort, want to empty the library, study the tomes that look more immediately useful, and sell the rest; they already have a list of buyers ready. The Artogenéa clan also is in favor of this idea, though they wish to keep the treatises of military theory closely guarded. The Varagenéa, always the cautious voice, warns against this plan, reminding everyone who would listen of the library phantasm, its powerful protector, known to castigate library goers for even minor infractions. What would happen if we were to empty its library? A catastrophe could befall us; better to leave everything as it is. Finally, the Irenthegenéa want the Council's support for an in-depth study of all the books and scrolls of the library; a world of knowledge will be our reward, they promise.
