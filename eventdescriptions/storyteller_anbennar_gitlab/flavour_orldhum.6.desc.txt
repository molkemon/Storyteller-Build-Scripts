Mithradhum was the home of the Mithril Dwarves, staunch rivals who were the only hold that threatened our position as First amongst Equals. Our rivalry is ancient with grudges held for millennia, always opposing us with the sway garnered by their strategic position and the High King's favor. Yet here we stand, a renewed successor in control of our old rival. In a decrepit state and at our mercy, what will we do to our old rival? We must act quickly, the seat of the old empire awaits.
