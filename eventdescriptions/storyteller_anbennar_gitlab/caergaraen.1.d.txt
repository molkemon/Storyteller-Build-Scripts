Unwisely used, the sacred Precursor spellblade Saerasyar, the GreenFlamed Sword, punishes its unworthy wielder in a mighty explosion of flame and magic, reappearing only when it has found a worthy member of Munazhlan’s folk to show itself to. Years after the untimely demise of the last ruler, Saerasyar has once more revealed itself, and it has been spirited away to the Askalyn at Assakadail. Should the current ruler wish to brave its effects, they can take up the sword once more.
