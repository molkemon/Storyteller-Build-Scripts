The audience chamber doors smash open, revealing a zite standing twice as tall as the startled onlookers, huge even for its race. "It is a rare thing for my masters to recognize the might of those outside the Court." The Zite chitters loudly. The monarch blinks slowly, the petitioner who had been speaking forgotten. Entirely oblivious, the Unseelie fey continues, "I had to see who commanded such respect. Such strength." The fey elongates its pronunciation of strength, letting it roll on its tongue, seemingly savoring the word. Then, without warning, the Zite kneels. "I offer myself and forgo the Unseelie, knowing you shall bring me greater foes than the King himself." He looks at you expectantly, his radiant rouge skin glowing with anticipation. In the background, a courtier coughs.
