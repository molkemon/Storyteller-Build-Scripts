This morning, the head of a big cat, a very big cat, was brought before the monarch, its fur a pure white, and its eyes a piercing dames tearblue. The Dey Vettaga of that province. The hunt had been long and arduous, and the casualties... the ruler did not want to think about it. This single beast had claimed hundreds of soldiers, and driven ten times the number it had killed to desert, as elves fled in terror rather than wait to be hunted down and consumed. Thankfully, one of our fallen soldiers managed to nick the beast before it killed him, and after his comrades discovered his corpse, they were able to follow the driptrail of blood to the beast's lair. Smoking it out by setting fires, they finally cornered and butchered it. The nightmare is over, and that province can hopefully get some peace once more.
