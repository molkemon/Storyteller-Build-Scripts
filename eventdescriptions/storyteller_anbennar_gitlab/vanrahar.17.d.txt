At last, it is done. The rebels and their sympathizers have been entirely defeated. With battles so grueling, it was impossible to tell the difference between a rebel fighter and a civilian, and, eventually, many of our warriors stopped caring. A recent census has shown that huge swathes of humans have been killed as well as a similar percentage of harimari. Every effort is being made to help harimari communities recover from the devastation. We wish we could leave the humans to rot for this betrayal, but they are still needed, so we must provide vital aid to key human communities as well. When we dragged the rebel leaders to talk, there was no negotiation; they were a conquered people and treated as such. We dictated that there would be no more humans in the upper caste, that harimari would be given preference in all castes; any prior preferential treatment for harimari would remain unchanged. The rebels for their part listened quietly with a dullness in their eyes, for they too knew it was the end. There would be no next time; they had fought with everything they had and lost. Even though humans will likely always be the largest race in Rahen, we've stripped them of their remaining power. Catching us off guard at first, we analyzed and countered the indomitable human spirit; they think too highly of themselves.
