One of the least devastated territories we seized in the recent war was Plumwall. After we smashed their so-called “Plum Cloak” militia, we moved on to take the more strategic fortress of Acromton. While this means that many Silmuna loyalists could still be hiding in the city, it seems more likely that any new lords will bow to us without complaint. After all, they plotted to support the Sorcerer King while he was ravaging Cannor, and we are far less reprehensible than he. They also have little in terms of strategic resources beyond their famous plum orchards, making any defiance unlikely to have a major effect.
