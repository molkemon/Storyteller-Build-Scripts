As we push back against the hostile wayward elves of this land, we find ourselves nominally in control of forest groves unclaimed by any of the great salla of Cyranvar. Something not seen since our people’s first arrival in the Deepwoods itself millennia ago. Sensing an opportunity to raise their station, a number of minor salla have begun to flock to seize their own stake. Even as we speak, a steady stream of Wood Elves has formed, gushing with eager souls and fantastical dreams. If they are to succeed, they will need our administrative help though.
