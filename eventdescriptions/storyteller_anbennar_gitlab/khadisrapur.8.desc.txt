The command did not bring only destruction upon our lands, but also orks, savage tribals from the mountains blessed with strength alone. With no commanders to bark orders at them, they are lost like puppies too. They barely understand us, barely know how things work around here, and only have a penchant for fighting, following some obscure code of honor we find difficult to penetrate. Poor fools, in short, forced to live in strangers' lands. As much as their very livelihoods are sad, we cannot let them live in our core lands, but there is hope. Those who are loyal to us, to our cause, and swear upon their honor to stay loyal under punishment by death, shall be given a new home in the highlands of Shamakhad. Those who disobey shall be reintegrated into the very ground from whence they came. The decision is final, and we shall message the surviving orks.
