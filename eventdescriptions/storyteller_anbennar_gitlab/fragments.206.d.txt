In what used to be a stretch of jungle was now a valley that looked like Amadia before the Ruin. A large paved path stretched across the valley all the way to the top, with humble stone houses dotted by the roadside. At the very end of the road, up a mountain, was a grand palace with huge gates that welcomed all sorts of life in, and presumably, allowed a dragon to fly out. Our adventurers poured into the valley and started looting. The inhabitants of the valley put up a resistance, but their spears were no match for our guns. Adventurers broke formation and stole all manner of things, from ornate artifacts to the clothes off the backs of the inhabitants and fled back to safer lands. The core group fought their way to the grand temple and clashed with the temple guards, who flung strange magic at us, using their spears as spellcasting foci. They bore armor resembling those of the ancient Dragonguard that once liberated Aelantier, but in the end they were no match. One of the Dragonguard questioned, "You have meddled with our realm for thousands of years, since our savior took us to his home, and now you invade? The day after he died?" before being struck by a bullet. Thousands of years? Our quest started a few years ago at best, but no Dragonguard survived to answer more questions. In the end, the palace was indeed empty of any dragon, but full of riches from an age that predated the precursors.
