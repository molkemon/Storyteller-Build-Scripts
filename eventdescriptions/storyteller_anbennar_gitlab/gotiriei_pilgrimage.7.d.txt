As I travel up the mountain, the people and lands start to shrink. The ocean slowly takes over the horizon, enriching my views. The more I look at the ocean, the more my mind sinks into its depths. Being so high up this mountain, I can already see visions. visions of a sturdy, massive navy guarding our seas from any foes and pirates, an unshaking bulwark against all threats from the outside. But on the other side, I see among the clouds of potential our flags waving through every port and harbor across this earth, being renowned everywhere for our goods and trade. A massive trade fleet with a massive treasury. My mind slowly fades into thoughts of the sea, as I begin to wonder.
