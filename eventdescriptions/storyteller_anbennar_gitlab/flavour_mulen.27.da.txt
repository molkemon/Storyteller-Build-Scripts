The Great Library of Aqatbar has many books, and will be a great addition to our collection in Mermigán. We have reached a deal with the owner of Aqatbar to purchase a number of their prized collection of books and manuscripts, and many noteworthy works have been added to our libraries. These include the six tomes of the art of bibliothecography, by Packlord Zheh the Scholar; a copy of the Ešlimšitum, detailing the expedition of ten thousand Akalšesi into Kheterata; the Geneology of the kings of Kuzaram, by Attalus the genealogist; the complete works of Burak Kazaš; and a recent work, Living Among the Harpies, by the Mašnsih Erbay szalSerim.
