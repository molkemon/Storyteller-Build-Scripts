The clans, being the owners of much of Celmaldor’s wealth both movable and immovable, are quite rich. They therefore have the funds to negotiate even with the Magisterium to secure the patronage or education of young mages. Having come into the service of a particular enterprising mage, one clan has offered to provide the mage’s services to the ruler. The clan clearly expects that the ruler accept the offer, and will be offended if rebuffed.
