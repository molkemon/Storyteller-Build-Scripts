TELLUM GAMING AND SPORT Forty-eight wyvernriders milling near the Heartspier marina were only one indicator today of the vast infrastructure lurking just beneath the Grand Regatta. This is no mere schoolboy race to the lighthouse. The entire course of the Regatta will be recorded by sendstone, each little gem tethered by displacement to our newsroom back home. It is an astonishing display of artistry, folks, the kind required for the around-the-world race which we will follow these next few months. At the start line, there was a great gathering and a display of fireworks, and crack sailors from across Halann set off. Teams from all over Halann have entered the Regatta. Some of the greatest sailors in the world are competing for a cash prize of two million braves. But who will chart the entire course first? Follow us here exclusively every day to find out.
