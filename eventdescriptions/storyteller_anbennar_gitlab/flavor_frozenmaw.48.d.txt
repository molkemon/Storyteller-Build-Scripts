Barred as we are from the intimate tavern social circles of the stern Reachmen, we must rely on sympathetic agents to understand them. From reports in the rebel domain, we hear of a local potentate by the name of Lady Magda, who has achieved much subtle influence in recent times and appears to be coordinating subversion against our vassal governments. She has no little influence in that domain herself, but she is clever enough not to have stepped outright into sedition. Indeed, encouraging others to hoard food and money against the future is good advice even in ordinary times; sharing stories of just past rebellion, while a clear insinuation, is not a declaration of war. Acting directly against Lady Magda now would only inflame tensions further, offering sleeping rebels a convenient outrage for which they no doubt hunger. Still, surely we must do something; Lady Magda’s quiet machinations cannot go unanswered.
