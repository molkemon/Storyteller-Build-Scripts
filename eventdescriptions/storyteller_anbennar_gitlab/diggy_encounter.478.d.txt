Outmanned and outmanoeuvred, but not outfought. The bugbear onslaught was fierce and vicious, but in the end, they could not deal a serious blow to our forces. As we broke through their lines and even defeated the ambush, they had prepared just in case someone followed them, with ease. This opened the way into their den where they kept the numerous riches they had acquired over time. a hoard of gold and jewels, old dwarven artifacts, and strange remnants of past battles.
