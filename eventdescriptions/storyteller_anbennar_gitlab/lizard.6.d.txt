The Favhanosy marathon, a rather strange tradition to them, has nonetheless become an international event amongst halflings, who travel from as far away as Aelantier and Cannor to take part. Recently, one of their halflings is becoming renowned for all the training they have done as preparation, to the point where many of their lizardfolk subjects have begun to regard his participation in the marathon with a kind of national pride. Many of their people hope he will achieve a great victory in the event, and that he will bring pride to their country. Of course, few of them have any idea of what exactly the tradition entails, but it has certainly whipped up a lot of excitement.
