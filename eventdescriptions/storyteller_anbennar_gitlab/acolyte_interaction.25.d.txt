The Seventh Initiate was rejected from union with Creation, for his obsession with that which is Above left him blind to those things which transpire Below. The Black domain is a land of wonders, but even it has not overcome certain immutable laws of how societies function. Conflict, of course, is one, and all Acolytes are intimately familiar with it. But another of these laws is debt, and some Acolytes, such as the country, tend to forget that financial constraints bind even the most powerful mystic masters. The misfortune of one Acolyte is typically the fortune of another, and this case is no exception. The country's comparative frugality has left them in an excellent position to buy out the vast majority of the realm.
