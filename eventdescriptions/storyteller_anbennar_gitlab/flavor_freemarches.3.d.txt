While some were sceptical of the reports of Muroga, none are now. It has been exactly one year since the Miracle of Muroga, and since then there hasn't been a single Cursed One sighting, never mind an attack. Freemarchers are not sure how to deal with the new status quo, whether now is the time for bold inroads into the Forest of the Cursed Ones, or if they will be left over extended and exposed if or when the Cursed Ones return. For now, all Freemarchers are thanking the Gods for this reprieve.
