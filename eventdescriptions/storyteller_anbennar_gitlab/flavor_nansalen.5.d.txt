As the name implies, the New Sun Cult is just that. new. Prior to its establishment, there were a multitude of different Sun Cults, each with their own tenets and beliefs, with the Akalšesi Cult of Kings being one such example. In twelve hundred two after the ascension, however, Taelarios Irrliazuir managed to assemble a number of religious tenets into a universal doctrine and convince most Sun Cults across Bulwar, including those of Akalšes and Nanšalen, to adopt it, at least officially. We held true to our divine mission, of course, and now that we have fulfilled it, it is time to ensure that the Cult of Kings and Queens receives the recognition that is its due. In order to reach this goal, a grand conclave of clergy has been called in Heunthume, where we can ensure that the universal doctrine of the New Sun Cult is adequately updated to reflect the current reality. specifically, that the rulers of Akalšes and Nanšalen are not merely the King of Kings and Queen of Queens who united Bulwar, but that their successors will also be Chosen of Surael in perpetuity, and that defying them is to defy Surael himself. Of course, no Sun Cult Council is complete without both figurative and literal struggle, with the most memorable being a three-way brawl that led to all three participants falling over a balcony, only barely being caught by patrolling harpies before they fell to their deaths. However, in the end, the leaders of the largest Sun Cult Temples were persuaded to accept the updated doctrine. While some of the smaller temples remain recalcitrant, they are insignificant in the face of our power and influence, and as a result, sermons across Bulwar will soon include praises to the Akal and the Matriarch, emphasising the importance of following their example and heeding their words.
