Our first four expeditions were a success; we mapped out the region and discovered the spots where the artifacts mostly originate from. It would have been simple. one more expedition, then a quick invasion to seize these locations. But then, our fifth expedition failed, and damn the fools who caused it. Our procedure was all but perfected. accompany a friendly trading caravan, then dispatch multiple small groups of travelers, or spies, to explore the countryside, establishing contacts among the locals and searching for artifacts. But, unbeknownst to any commanders of the expeditions, our men had been sneaking off and diverging from their assignments for an insidious purpose. they had discovered a herb smoked by the locals, which they named Pöd. Such men who smoked Pöd became instantly unproductive and instead preferred to wander about, behaving like animals. Their eyes were bloodshot, and they talked freely, a quality which was quickly discovered by the locals, more accustomed to the effects of Pöd leaves. It seems our luck faltered on our fifth expedition, however, as one of our engineers, by the name of Kalaas, told of our entire scheme to a Hobgoblin spy who had given him this nefarious forest leaf. Fortunately, the Hobgoblins have executed Kalaas for us. But the damage is done. all of Nomsyulhan knows of our plans to invade. Zernuj Anzabukh, Leader of the Ikögshaanti Expeditions to Nomsyulhan, Report given to the monarch.
