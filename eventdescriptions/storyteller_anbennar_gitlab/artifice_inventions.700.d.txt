Ever since the death of Alaria the Lifehand at the Battle of Trialmount, healing magic has lagged far behind other schools. But where magic has fallen short, artificery can go far beyond. These kits contain unique potions, self-tightening tourniquets, and everything required to get a soldier off of a soldier's doorstep and back to the battlefield in scarce hours. All contained within a lightweight, portable package that barely weighs down a soldier on the battlefield, and able to be deployed with little to no training. At a moment's notice, any soldier can just as easily become a medic.
