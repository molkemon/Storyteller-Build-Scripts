By now we are used to reports of astronomers struck by astral terror, and occasionally even their families and acquaintances. But the recent reports all talk about terror victims far out into the domain, who have never even seen the Setzunkhûpa or even DûrVazhatun. A theory was put forth that all the rumors traveling around the domain have inspired people to fake astral terror as pranks or ways to get attention, but according to our investigators these cases seem genuine. With our increasing failure to contain the ever-increasing rumors, the astral terror was already close to an open secret, but with this, it seems the lid has finally blown completely off.
