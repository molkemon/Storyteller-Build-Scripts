The Centaurs cannot easily fit into many of our buildings, but have quickly set up a few tents outside of our outpost. They have demanded the governor and their attendants follow the ordsikar to their encampment. As the governor walks through the encampment, he feels a sense of foreboding as every centaur he passes glares, snorts, and turns away. The ordsikar stops once they reach a large plaza, around which the largest tents are set up. The ordsikar looks down upon the governor before speaking to him in their harsh tongue. The shaman translates. We are not wanted here. That we are even being warned is a courtesy. They say we are lucky that we were not found by something they refer to as “The Filustkhuga”, who would not have been so kind. If our outpost is still here when they next come by, they will be coming with weapons. We must prepare.
