Our attempts to expel the population of a certain province to a colony have not exactly been interpreted as wellmeaning by the people who are leaving their homes behind, but some of those who have left see some merit in their new home. The colony, at least in these early stages, is somewhat more permissive regarding religious practices, with some preachers regarding it as a better place for practicing the faith. These news have now reached the province, softening the resistance to the project somewhat.
