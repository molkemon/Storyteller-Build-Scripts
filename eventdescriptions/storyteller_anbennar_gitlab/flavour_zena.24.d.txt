We have learned through our expedition to the Rünsukhi plateau that the Divine Agate, which was formerly mined there for jewelry and meagre decor, has special properties. Capable of storing and releasing chi, sufficiently empowered agate can offer effective protection against the adverse spirits of the plateau when worn. Amidst the treacherous wastes of Gozengun, these gems have been crafted into charms known as Tian Zhu, and are a popular source of protection. When sufficiently infused with power, they even glow with a soft orange gleam, making them a wonderful exhibit at Medaka’s Exceptional Treasures.
