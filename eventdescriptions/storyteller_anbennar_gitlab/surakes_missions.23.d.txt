Jaherian ta'Diranhriá, or Jaher as he was known to us. Said to be Surael reborn, he freed us from our gnollish oppressors long ago, and brought to us the Light Surael which continued on after his death through his kin, the sun elves. It is to him and his glory that we ought to sing, for if not for him, we would still be under gnollish rule and our glorious nation would never have come to be. O Jaher, Surael Reborn, exalted ruler, preeminent one, savior of Bulwar, look upon our deeds favorably and joyfully, and give to us for a gift lasting life, the fullness of extreme old age, the firmness of our faith, the longevity of our country, the ruination of our foes, and the conquest of our enemy's land.
