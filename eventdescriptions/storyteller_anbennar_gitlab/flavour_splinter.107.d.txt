The dramatic rise and prosperity within Sunijalla has allowed the monarchy to not just survive, but thrive. In the wake of this unprecedented development, the nation feels secure and powerful enough to demand the submission of all other breakaway states, as well as demanding they adopt the monarchy as their sole form of government. Several minor noble families in each nation are now being considered as the rightful monarchs of their respective nations. Naturally, we intend to establish marital ties so we can gain deep leverage into their respective states. As such, we have sent delegates to the other breakaway states. However, there is one outlier. The abominable Magocracy of Qorondulyuz presents a grave threat to both our monarchy and the very core of Triunic civilization itself, and as such it must be dealt with directly.
