The monarch's palace is quiet today, even as a massive crowd has gathered to observe the ceremony about to occur. The ruler emerges, but there is no cheering. Silence is paramount. The first cart of alcohol is brought forth, followed by the monarch, without a word and aided by several priests of Dagrin, who taps the first massive barrel that stands at least fifteen feet tall and is branded with the symbol of Dagrin. A rough line forms, each participant possessing a mug. The drinks will be passed out for the entire day, here in the capital and across the country, as the blessed Dagrin's Drink is distributed by the priesthood of Dagrin.
