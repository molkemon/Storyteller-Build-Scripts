We travel with our mother everywhere. Her mission is divinely ordained. Even as the moving is difficult, we could be no better trained. My wife is a mighty woman; I could never be more proud. I clean her armor and weapons, so with the kids, she can be so loud. Mom is the coolest paladin ever, like a hero from a bedtime story. So when I grow up one day soon, I too will earn great glory. They were the family of a paladin. Their lives were rife with worry and stress, yet in them she found great strength, and their small wounds she would bless. I thought of Leli and Kyola, how our own small family was the same. Those long nights we spend together are the source of a paladin’s flame.
