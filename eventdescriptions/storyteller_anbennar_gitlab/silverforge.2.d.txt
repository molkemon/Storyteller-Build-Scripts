Orcs have long been the enemies of dwarven civilization. Though the loremasters claim they were but one factor in the Last Days of Dwarovar, they wrested control of the Serpentspine from the dwarves and barred them from their home even so. Following the loss of the Serpentspine, orks had only made the rare raid into the open air of Escann. While these threats never amounted to much, the dwarves knew of their potential should they ever rally in large enough numbers. This came to pass in fourteen twenty-four, when the Dookanson led the Greentide in a flood over Escann. The orkish army destroyed the successor states of Castanor and nearly reached the Havoral Divide, until the Greentide Vanguard was routed at the Battle of Rottenstep. If not for that victory, and the Marcher Lords' subsequent heroism, Silverforge would have been helpless to defend the Empire, its army almost shattered by the end of the fourth Lilac War. But now, they have rebuilt their army and improved upon its composition. They have expanded their territory to encompass the duchy of High Esmar, and with the discovery that Aelantier is once again inhabitable, ork prisoners of war are being transported through their lands. It is believed that these prisoners will make valuable slaves in the newly forming colonies. However, this might be the opportunity they have been waiting for. With the increased size of their realm, and an ample supply of labor, they might be able to complete the mining project proposed by Strongbellow. The land survey has already been commissioned. They shall purchase their own slaves and put them to work as debt laborers to pay for their crimes against their first home in AulDwarov and their second home in Escann.
