Even we, made of metal, age. I have lived a long life, longer than many of my fellow friends made of flesh, and though perhaps it might not ever come to an end, in truth, its effects can be felt. Ruling this realm has not been easy on me; my weakened Kauga has already suffered a lifetime of use, and the energy that I require from it is not one it can provide me any more. Should I seek to find a way to strengthen my Kauga to keep ruling? Or is it perhaps best for me to retire, and let someone else continue in my place?
