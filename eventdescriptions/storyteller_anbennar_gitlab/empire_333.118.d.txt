The Three Hundred Thirty-Second Empire is one of the longest lasting empires in history. It took over one thousand five hundred years after its formation before it finally fell for good. At least, that is what the official records state. As everyone knows, however, Zerat stopped being a true empire after the Shadowfang Invasion, when the bloodcursed crocodilian lizardfolk rampaged through Taneyas and split their great empire in half. The polity around Shalazar split into its own kingdom, completely separate from the primary capital in Zerat. So why did that not count as the end of the Three Hundred Thirty-Second Empire? Why was Shalazar not considered the true continuation of the old Zerat empire? Shalazar was even the old capital of the empire. It only moved to Zerat in the wake of the invasion. Empires have been counted as collapsed over far less significant means. Often the records do not even elaborate on what collapse meant. The One Hundred Ninety-Seventh Empire collapsed after the emperor was killed by a falling tchoken. Nothing more needs to be explained.
