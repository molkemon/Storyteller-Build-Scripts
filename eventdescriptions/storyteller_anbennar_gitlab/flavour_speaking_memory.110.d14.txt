There are many heroes who have passed on, or who we cannot reach. CleftclawOutrunstheWinds used to be one of these heroes, but he is no longer. the mechanim awakened him again, and now he dashes across the lands of Kanolowele with a frightening speed. But if the pace at which he moves is terrifying, Cleftclaw is not. He is a biologist, and there could hardly be anything less scary than that. He spends much of his time in contemplation of the natural world, standing perfectly still. He loves these times. when he is still, he can almost imagine that he is viewing nature as it would be if he was not there. But on occasion, a village in Kanolowele will see a vast beetle made of metal barrelling towards them at blinding speed. It is CleftclawOutrunstheWinds, who has come to warn them of danger and take them to safety. On other occasions, a mechanim standing alone in the deepest parts of the wilds will encounter Cleftclaw, who stops just long enough to give them his observation journals, so that everyone might benefit from his discoveries. Then he speeds off again, for there is yet more to observe. The wilds of Kanolowele are full of terrifying things. But they also hold CleftclawOutrunstheWinds, and knowing that makes them seem less fearsome, somehow.
