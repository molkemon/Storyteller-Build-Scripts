The ruler has discovered a cave in the area of the excavation site in the realm, full of oversized mushrooms. When they explored the area to see if they could use any of this in their mining efforts, they ran into a settlement of Fungrum. They decided not to anger them and instead opened negotiations. Defending their mushroom homes from a sudden goblin raid, they earned their trust and have received assurances that they would gladly join their kingdom. At least, they think that is what they meant since they cannot speak; they have been communicating in a rudimentary and improvised sign language. Either way, they have since been paying regular taxes in the form of edible mushrooms, mushroom wood deliveries, and some sort of mushroom brew.
