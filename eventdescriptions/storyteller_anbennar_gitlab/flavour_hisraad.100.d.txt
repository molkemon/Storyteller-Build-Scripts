Decades ago we set forth from Dadubakha to spread civilization among the natives of Shalazar and beyond. Now we can look back at years of bloodshed to see that we have truly reached our goal. Large swaths of land once in the hands of foreign savages are now cultivated by true people. However, those in Dadubakha still see our conquests as theirs. After years of cowtowing to their sponsorship, it is time we take what we have done for ourselves. We are our own people, with our own rulers.
