The province of Taraghata has suffered from avalanches from time to time, usually in the summer, when the snow on the Jade Mountain recedes ever so slightly upwards. However, this month has been disastrous; never before has this much snow and molten water come down. The city is completely covered, in a way even the records of the province from before the conquest cannot match. Spies in Balrijin confirm that this is their doing, as mages melt snow atop the mountain so they can look for their goddragon, Balris the Gold.
