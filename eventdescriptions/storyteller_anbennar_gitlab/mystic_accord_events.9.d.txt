While on a hunt in the hills of Xianje, the ruler became separated from their party. Before they could turn to catch up with the rest of the procession, a thick, impenetrable fog had risen from the damp earth. Then, in the mist, figures began to twist and dance, ephemeral as light on steam. One, standing still amongst the moving shapes, beckoned for the ruler. One of the hunters, perhaps? No, its form wasn’t quite human. Yet still it seemed to draw the ruler towards it.
