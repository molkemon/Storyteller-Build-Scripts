The Nirat rebellion has been vanquished by our armies, and internal peace has been restored. However, total peace is not yet achieved. There is still the matter of the gnollish raiders to our west, and once they are dealt with, it is almost assured that their brethren to the south and the opportunistic elves to the east will both attempt to take advantage of our turmoil. Ahead lie turbulent waters, but Elikhet chose us knowing we could lead our people through it.
