A decade had passed since the Siege of Nianano; since the end of the Tyrant Degsadra’s Cycle. Restoring order to the realm has not been easy; many cities have needed time to rebuild their walls and structures, and many farms have been left devoid of farmers to tend to them, with many having given their lives for the sake of freedom. Only recently have we truly recovered, our new Cycle coming into its own, no longer defined by that which came before it. Yet, we have not been alone in our attempts to recover; those that had sworn themselves to the former tyrant have hidden themselves in the shadows, slowly reorganizing and recuperating, preparing to halt our new beginning and turn back the Cycle to Degsadra’s day. Now, just as we truly push towards liberty and prosperity, the viper bears its fangs; the tyrant’s loyalists rise up against us, intent on razing all that we have built to the ground.
