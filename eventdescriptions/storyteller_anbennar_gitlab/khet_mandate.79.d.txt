A Khet has recently opened a new art gallery, consisting of his newest sculpture collection. The upper classes were surprised by the realism of his works, appreciating his precise anatomy and evocative facial expressions. This didn't last for long though, as soon enough citizens started to notice muffled screams from inside of the structures, turning out to be living people encased within the marble. The families of the people inside now demand the steward break these statues wide open and free them while compensating the Khet accordingly, despite him evidently not liking the idea of his collection being smashed into pieces.
