The matter of the Cuahu Teotlehu is decided. The magmascryers of all lands must be given the freedom to commune with the Twin Gods. Organization is necessary, but the Cuahu Teotlehu hoarded what is the birthright of all magmascryers for themselves. They grew fat and greedy. This is why the Cuahu Teotlehu fell, and this is why they must be replaced by a new order of interpreters. By broadening access to the Twin Gods, we will chart a path between liberty and order, and gain the benefits of both. The magmascryers have shown our council the way forward, and now the foremost duty of the faithful is to continue along their path. A great change is coming, but the Volcanic Truth has never feared change.
