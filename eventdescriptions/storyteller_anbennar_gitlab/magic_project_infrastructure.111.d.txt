Magic has begun to infuse every aspect of industry within the realm, including the very roads upon which the people walk. Veins of Dames tear shoot through the cobblestones, lining the streets with a soft cerulean glow as they snake across the capital like the fingers of a lightning bolt. The mages wish to extend this road network across the land, stitching together the domain with latent mana like a grand spiderweb of potential energy. This expansion would greatly assist the infrastructure projects planned within the realm, but would be exorbitantly expensive to create to the extent the mages desire.
