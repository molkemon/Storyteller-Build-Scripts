Today, I test myself against The Rigoti. I will not be alone in this pilgrimage to the Stairway of Kings at the mountain’s peak, for I am accompanied by my most loyal and deserving guards and retainers, together with a few fellow pilgrims who seek the blessing of the Last True King. My trials, visions, and insights, however, shall be mine and mine alone. It is a King’s burden and privilege alike to take the inward journey alongside the outward, a crucible of both spirit and body. I, galvanized by the will of the Last True King, swear upon my crown and my people that all which I recount within this testament is the solemn truth. May those who come after me find it of use in their own journeys.
