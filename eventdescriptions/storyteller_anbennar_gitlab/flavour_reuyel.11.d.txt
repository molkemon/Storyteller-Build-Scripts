Finally, after generations of struggle, order has been returned. A domain once dirty and unclean from the stench of heathens and monsters, creatures of and tainted by the malevolent dark, is cleansed by deeds violent and peaceful. The domain has been restored to order, with many a loose thread since before the Decade of Devastation removed as well. The Copper Dwarves have been crushed underfoot, so that they shall never threaten the domain again, and even the last vestiges of heresy shiver before the might that can be seen in ReUyel as the elite trained army marches the forests of this land. The domain is clean, and we can only thank Surael's Chosen for it.
