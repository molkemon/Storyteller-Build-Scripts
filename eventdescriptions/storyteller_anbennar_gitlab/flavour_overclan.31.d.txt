The expansion of the state into the Lower Suran Circuit has seen the importance of the Chief Vizier's role increase once more. This expansion has also left those living in the Lower Suran Circuit feeling underrepresented by the state. It is therefore suggested that a Circuit Diwan be selected to serve as adequate representation for the interests of the circuit. Attached you will find several candidate proposals who meet all the necessary qualifications and criteria. This office awaits your prompt decision.
