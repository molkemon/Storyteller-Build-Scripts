Spending time to find a power source that may or may not be accessible has been determined to be too much of a risk, and a forward assault has been judged our best means of stopping the golems and their mad maker once and for all. There are two ways. a simple hammer and anvil assault, where a main line holds and squads attempt to flank the sides, surrounding the golems and crushing them between the groups before they can be reinforced. A more experimental tactic would be using tactical squads with mage support to create a fluid defensive line, but that may be more prone to collapse if cohesion cannot be maintained.
