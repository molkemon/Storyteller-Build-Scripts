Chamakomo held aloft the severed head of the man sent to kill him the night prior, before the now armored group of elders. "Behold now, the price of kings." As the gasps came over the crowd, he continued, "This assassin came into my home, to slay me in my own bed, in front of my wife and children." "Accursed tyrant. May a Devouring Gourd feast on his privates." One elder proclaimed, his hands shaking with emotion, "These animals tell us we need to act civilized and then they do this?" "There can be no compromise with the Binwarji, with these Silvered Lords, for they only know slaves and masters. We are neither, strangers from a distant land, who seek only to follow our traditions in peace. But it seems they do not intend to give us such a chance." A young man strode forth, and raised his blade, proclaiming his vow. "No more lords or kings, now we choose our own way, we shall beat the drums of liberation like God’s aspect Makan." Chamakomo nodded. "It shall be hard, this land has never given us anything easily or for free, but..."
