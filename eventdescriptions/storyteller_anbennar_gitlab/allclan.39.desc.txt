It started as a strange blip on our defense systems. We have gotten similar anomalies before, mostly attributed to what appear to be mysteriously dense clouds. As it turns out, this was not one of them. Three thousand tons of scrap metal and extraordinarily destructive liquids were hurtling towards the nation at the speed of gravity. It would appear as though our astronauts have made their return far faster than originally anticipated. Whilst the impact will be catastrophic, think of all the things we have learned.
