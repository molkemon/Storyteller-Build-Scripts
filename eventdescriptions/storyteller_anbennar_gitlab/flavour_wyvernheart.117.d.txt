During the civil war, the Regent enacted a plan to cement his and his heir's rule on Wyvernheart. He, with the help of the Technocrats and artificers, developed a process to imbue floodborn with some of his exceptional powers. This became the focus for the past few years and has now come to fruition; the Floodborn Council will now be reestablished. The first members of the new council approach the Sovereign, crimson robes adorning their figures. The soft velvet of their robes touches the floor as they bow before the monarch. An indiscernible bond between them exists, as the councillors look up to the Sovereign; a sort of kinship can be felt. None of them are of the same blood; they are not family. And yet all have the same blood flowing in their veins; the blood and magic of The Regent, the most powerful floodborn Wyvernheart has ever witnessed. As the bowing councillors rise up, a flash of crimson in their eyes. They are the Scions, the "heirs" of The Regent, magically empowered and biologically upgraded, but also perfectly loyal and incapable of repeating the mistakes of the Council of old. Now a new era dawns upon the council, the era of the Scions, an era where the Council is the extended arm of the Sovereign, and their rule is uncontested.
