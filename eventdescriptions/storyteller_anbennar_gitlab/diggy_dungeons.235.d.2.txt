Having placed explosives at the inner edges of the archway, the expeditionaries light the fuses and find cover behind the nearby rocks. The explosion blows a hole into the cave wall behind the arch, revealing another cavern. The arch, however, seems strangely undamaged. Instead, the force of the explosion appears to have activated it. The symbols engraved on it shine a bright blue light, as the magic is concentrated and then released in a powerful pulse. As the gateway attempts to reopen its connection, however, it is unable to find the proper outlet for this power. With a powerful explosion, several expeditionaries are killed instantly in the blast as the archway disintegrates.
