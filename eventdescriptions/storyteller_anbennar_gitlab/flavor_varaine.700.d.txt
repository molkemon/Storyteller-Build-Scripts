According to an adventuring professional clique within the Expeditionary Corps, each unexplored site on dry land has three or four times as many underwater and ocean counterparts. Given this wealth of untapped opportunity, we have accumulated what research exists on Waterbreathing Potions. True to their name, they turn submerged death traps into traversable hazards, yet contemporary Waterbreathing Potions can’t protect against heavy currents, crushing pressure, or freezing temperatures. To this end, the ocean-obsessed Expeditionary clique has requested funds for an expedition to South Sarhal, hoping to make inroads with the masters of the ocean. the merfolk. This is easier said than done, as the merfolk are infamously reclusive, and only the keenest and most considered advance can hope to succeed. Alternatively, the local Baashidi humans already enjoy friendly merfolk relations, but the Baashidi nobles who host surfacesea meetings are known to extract exuberant concessions. If we can just get our finless foot in the door, the merfolk will surely accept, contributing the valuable insight necessary to perfect our Waterbreathing Potion.
