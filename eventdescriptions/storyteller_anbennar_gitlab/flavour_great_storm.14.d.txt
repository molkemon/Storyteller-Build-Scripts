The monarch was running, not fleeing. This was only a temporary setback, yes. Running towards the lakemen land was unprecedented, but he was certain that he would be able to lay low for a while. The sound of battle was still ringing in his ear. The screams, the clashing metal. A part of him wished that he had fallen there as well, to be with Irdaeos at this moment. But no. He had a responsibility to uphold. to liberate the Filustkhuga, to enact their revenge. He has found the coast. Soon he would be out of the expanding tempest's reach. Then he sees him. a lone centaur, standing a fair distance from him, but still in his way. He knows that he could not leave him alive. His position must remain a secret. He approaches, lance in hand.
