The sacred streets of Adbrabohvi, the holiest city of heathens, run red with blood. The soldiers who still hold the northern crusade and the crazed witch Sybil in their hearts have been overtaken by their hatred. They have defied their orders and now run amok, dragging heathen men and women, young and old, from their homes and executing them in the streets. Buildings and books, older than the oldest man to ever step foot in Poppyspring, have vanished behind great pillars of roaring flame. Never could we have foreseen such bloodshed. Suffice to say, this cannot continue. The Red Sentries, under the command of the general Ichabod Roose, have been redirected from their objective of rooting out the witches of the occupied city. Their new goal is to stop the rampaging Istranari and cease their corruptive influence on our pure faith permanently.
