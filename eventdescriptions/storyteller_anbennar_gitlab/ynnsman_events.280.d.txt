There is a battle brewing in the Ynn, between the Malacnari Battleguards of old and the newcomers to the region, the halfork mercenaries. It is not a literal battle but instead one of contracts, and we have been given the opportunity to take a side, as a band of halfork mercenaries have requested a contract for service and settlement rights in that province. Accepting these newcomers would show implicit support for the halforks and enrage the Malacnari Battleguards. However, accepting this new blood would greatly improve the quality of all of our mercenary companies.
