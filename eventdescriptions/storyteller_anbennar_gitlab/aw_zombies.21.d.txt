In an exceptional display of skill and bravery, a band of adventurers has completely destroyed the zombies in their domain. Luring the creatures to a defensible location, the adventurers used well-tested methods of hidden oil catches, carefully dug trenches, and devastating magics to light an enormous conflagration that burned away the overwhelming majority of the undead. Outriders and abjurers prevented any stragglers from fleeing the inferno, holy chants and lances driving back the abominations. By the time the sun rose, there was nothing left of the horde other than ash.
