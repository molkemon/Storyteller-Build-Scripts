Forgive us, but it is the way of sages to consider the unthinkable. Unthinkable indeed. You would leave a pack of beasts injured and feral at our doorstep, not yours. It is not the temple realms that would border such a rump command. And the cause of peace? A false peace. The Siri delegate slams a flat palm on the table, but that is the terrible thing with the monks; they never startle. He takes a long, low breath. The delegate turns from general to general, Wulin to Righteous Master. The entire future of the Siri Republic rests momentarily on his shoulders.
