Unlike the misguided fool followers of the Regent Court, those that embrace the true Court do not simply waste away reflecting or praying to prove their fervor to their patron deity. No, they act, through shows of debauchery, cruelty, and malice. Particularly gratuitous acts might even catch the Court’s eye and enable one to have a chance to become an Elevate, a mark of proof that they stand above the weak and the mulling. As it were, their priests believe that such a chance might be yours, my ruler. And while it is no surprise that the Court has taken notice of your activities, there is no doubt that more must be done to earn the Mark. Indeed, the state will have to be second while the Court watches your next moves.
