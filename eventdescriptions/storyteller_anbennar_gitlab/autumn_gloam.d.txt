The Twilight Court is named after the Gloam Knight, the archfey to which it is sworn. Unlike the unpredictable disposition of most fey, the Gloam Knight is a beacon of loyalty and steadfastness in the Feyrealm. Valuing loyalty and truthfulness above all else, the Gloam Knight personally hunts down those who have betrayed their oaths and promises, serving as the blade of vengeance for those who have been wronged.
