Our settlements in the mountains must always be on guard for frequent hinuibird attacks, but in recent days such attacks have escalated. The hunuibirds are swooping down from the sky to abduct our people directly from the village streets of the affected domain, and all the while they have been gathering strength. More hinuibirds appear in the sky every day, and they are flocking in ways that indicate that the attacks we are seeing are only the beginning. The few naturalists remaining in the affected domain predict that the hinuibirds are amassing for one final, conclusive attack, in which they will fill their bellies until there is nothing left of our settlement. The only solution, they say, is to send troops with sufficient weapons to drive the hinuibirds off, the numbers to succeed, and the wherewithal to stand firm and fight.
