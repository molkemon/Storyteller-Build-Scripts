From the streets of Abeesooyinka to the deserts of Badabbi, men and women alike would succumb to the deadly Lizard illness. While I’m sure you’ve heard a bit of it, information on the contagion is limited. All that your court knew, even I, is that the illness causes people to gradually turn a sickly green; this is where the name comes from, given the similarities to the skin of Lizardfolk. While the illness is not common, it tends to be highly noticeable, as I’m sure you’ve seen how people treat those infected with the illness as their state gradually worsens. Recently, a group of infected children had found themselves alone on the outskirts of Abeesooyinka, having to resort to stealing and begging. A wandering Darwiiz happened to appear, and offered them a cure. Like a miracle, through dance and strange elixirs, the children found themselves cured of their green skin and weakness, jumping from joy. One of the kids seems to have spread the tale across the entire city. He seems to be from a certain domain, and many are calling him a hero. Some have suggested throwing a party for the wandering Darwiiz, providing him with gifts for his heroic actions. I can begin to arrange the details if you’d like.
