The fish have had their time. Now it is our turn. Large shoals of fish are caught in grand nets and hauled ashore. Each and every fish is carefully prepared and made into every manner of dish. Every man and woman must pull their weight. To fish, to prepare, to cook, to serve, and most importantly, to eat. For this is a time to be merry and to feast. This is a time of plenty and of joy. We care for our waters; we care for our fish. We carefully manage and cultivate their environment in accordance with our old practices, and then we get to reap the rewards.
