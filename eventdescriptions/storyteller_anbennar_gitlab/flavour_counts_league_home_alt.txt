Ebonmarck was disputed for centuries between Vrorenmarch and Castellyr. The land had been settled by many Gerudians who considered themselves part of the new Castanor, and so they maintained deep ties to the protoCastellyrian nobility in Cast and Anor even when under Vroren sovereignty. This was the land where the Blacktowers lived for the past four centuries, and it was only with the coming of the Greentide that they were sent packing. This land was where our lives were made and where Carleon, among others, was raised. It is bittersweet to return when the entire other side of Castellyr has been our home for twenty years and more now. Still, it is one thing to know one’s history and another to be able to inherit it in full. And in the ruins, a distant member of the family, a prodigal son, reveals themselves to us and tells of their desire to help us with our purpose, just as their other family members did.
