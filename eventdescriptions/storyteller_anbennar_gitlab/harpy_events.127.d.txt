Dawn in the capital breaks with a forlorn shriek, its grief and fury echoing between the nests. More and more harpies join into the cacophonous chorus until it is all that can be heard. No messenger is needed to know the song of the nestrobbed, but by midday, the details have reached the court. A matriarch awoke to find that her flock’s nest was empty, the eggs stolen away in the night with no evidence to be found. Rumors swirl as to who is responsible, and a furious mob has assembled demanding judgement for this attack on a flock’s future.
