The Green River is the pride of Varainé, however ever since its large-scale utility was discovered in the fifteenth century, the river has progressively been molded into a tool for the state and the alchemical industry. These changes suffered by the river, though gradual, were noticed by many in the nation, who disliked the fact that the river over the years had become more and more polluted and dense with chemicals to the point where it is nearly impossible to properly appreciate the river's natural beauty. These reports, which grow more and more numerous by the year, are a large concern for the Varainéy government, and efforts to conserve or even restore the river started being looked into. However, alchemists brought forward new proposals to increase the river's utility even further, a decision which would greatly alienate even more of the people who desire the river to be preserved, but would bring large economic boons to the nation.
