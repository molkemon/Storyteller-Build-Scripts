Good people, we are here to hold the wake for Hytiranya's greatest heir. Nyamau Thiuivene made her final landfall. She died in her bed, finally peaceful after the long illness. Even if her mind failed her in those last years, even if she could not recognize friends and family, even... sorry, just a moment... I hope you will be able to remember her as the brilliant soul that she always was. We have not just lost our brightest mind, a teacher, a friend; Nyamau was a visionary, the herald of a bright future only she could see. She was a harpy ahead of her time. And she dragged our whole nation with her towards this light, changing the life of every Hytiranyaleni, no matter their power, their wealth, or their race. Everyone starred in her dream, and she made it a reality. The plaza was packed full for the funeral, yet no sound could be heard.
