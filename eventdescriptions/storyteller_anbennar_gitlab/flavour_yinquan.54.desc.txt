There is betrayal within the highest ranks. The highest echelons of the military, blinded by the prospect of career advancement, run the risk of acting contrary to the realm's needs. Their distrust of Liho's Maidens is well known, and has managed to perpetuate itself between generations of leaders. As the Maidens grow more influential in the government, the prospect of a disloyal organ of state is unfathomably risky. If we are not careful, they might think to challenge the maidens; considering their control of the military, they might well succeed. As Huszien has asked us to create a modernized army for the realm, this presents us with an opportunity to follow their designs while still advancing our interests. As far as anyone has to know, the officers leading the realm's military have already tried to sabotage it. The punishment, as with all forms of treason, is death. The realm will harbor no traitors.
