Many of the more well-to-do humans in the realm, such as merchants, officers, administrators, and priests, feel as though the recent outflux of elves from one domain to another is a sign that Surael is moving his eye away from their lands. They feel as though the elves have abandoned them to their fates. They say that as the servants of the Chosen, they ought to follow their masters to that place and help them carve out a nation there, where they can serve the elves and remain in Surael's good graces.
