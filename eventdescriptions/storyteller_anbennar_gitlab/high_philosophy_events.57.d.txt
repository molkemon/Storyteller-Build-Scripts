In the canon of High Philosophy's many texts, the debate over which combinations of foods are appropriate for consumption features prominently, but they have never been so hotly debated as now, with the arrival of Cannorian traders carrying large numbers of previously unknown foodstuffs. Exchanges of plants and animals found in Aelantier have caused a culinary explosion in distant Cannor, and now the traders that have plied the trade to the western continents carry to our shores many interesting new additions to our palate. Traditionally, philosophers have said that one must not combine foods not shown together on High Temple friezes. Against these restrictions, these new foodstuffs have caused quite a stir. Some hardliners are calling for a complete ban on these new imports, while others say that each should be identified with its nearest counterpart and allowed as substitutions for them in traditional recipes.
