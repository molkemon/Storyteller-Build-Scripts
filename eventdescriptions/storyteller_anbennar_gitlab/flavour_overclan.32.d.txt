The expansion of the state into the Upper Suran Circuit has seen the importance of the Chief Vizier's role increase once more. This expansion has also left those living along the Upper Suran feeling underrepresented by the state. It is therefore suggested that, as has been done in earlier comparable circumstances, a Circuit Diwan be selected to serve as adequate representation for the interests of the circuit. Attached you will find several candidate proposals who meet all the necessary qualifications and criteria. This office awaits your prompt decision.
