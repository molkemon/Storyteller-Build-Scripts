Hrenvyr stood over the gold inlaid branches, carefully grown and twisted to form a natural balcony over several decades by the best botanists the Emperor could find. Above him, the Golden Dome of Stanyrhrada shined as bright as it ever had, and from here he could oversee the entirety of the palace-turned-castle of Stanyrhrada, said to be one of the most beautiful fiefs in all of the Ynn. It lay surrounded by fertile fields and orchards which provided bountiful harvests thanks to the Golden Dome he stood under. In the courtyard below, the Order of the Dome was dutifully training, loyal to the Dome, and now him. He had dreamt of standing here for as long as he remembered, yet had never expected to look over this domain, his domain, without some sort of miracle. Yet here he stood. Hrenvyr had been made heir to Stanyrhrada following the lordship's subjugation by Arverynn. Previously a member of a distant branch of House yen Stantir, he had grown up together with Carodin the Second in the Teal Keep as a ward, becoming good friends and serving under Carodin the Second as advisor in the years of adulthood. As Hrenvyr was looking over his domain he thought on the future of Stanyrhrada. House yen Stantir was one of the most prestigious dynasties of all Sarda, and could directly trace its lineage from the Imperial line. Stanyrhrada was one of the wealthiest, largest, and strongest Lordships of the Ynn. Other lords may have started planning to revolt against Arverynn, but Hrenvyr knows that it is unity that brought the Ynn to its greatest point, that the empire was prosperous thanks to its many loyal Iosahar maintaining the peace and serving the empire. And that is what Hrenvyr will do, for so long as he lives.
