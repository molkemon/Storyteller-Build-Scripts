Day two hundred three. Lisolette went missing tonight. Nobody knows what got her, but we heard her screams in the dead of night. Rushed out to help her, but she was gone, leaving a trail into the thicket that none of us were willing to follow. First Rican wanders off, now síl Ordóin goes missing? It feels like we are being picked off one by one. I am not sure we can afford any more losses, or we will have to call it off. Day two hundred thirty. Something strange is happening in this jungle. For a few nights now, expedition members have been reporting hearing síl Ordóin calling out to them, in pain but definitely still alive. I did not believe them at first; I thought it was just their minds playing tricks, but tonight I have heard it as well. Could síl Ordóin still be out there? I really do not know, and even if she is, can we afford to help her? Hell, can we afford not to?
