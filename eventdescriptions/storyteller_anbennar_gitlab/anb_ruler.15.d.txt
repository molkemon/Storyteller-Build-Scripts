Otherworldly precision, incomprehensible motive, unfathomable breadth. The only way to defeat such an opponent is to become even more unfathomable, to surpass the natural and become infinite. Such power and resolve, born of paranoia, will not falter in the face of nature itself, regardless of the implement with which it intends to corrode, degrade, and erode one's sense of self. One's consciousness must become a monolith of sheer, unadulterated tenacity, clinging to the world that strives to deliver righteous deliverance. But righteousness is the mantle of the deceiver, true nature hidden in the murky mire of one's own facade. You need not cling to such concepts. You see all, you know all, you are all. Conqueror of the material, one with the immaterial, and elucidator of the fathomless depths of understanding, the universe unspooled at your feet. You command the Reaper before you to kneel. Your words carry meaning and intent, your will made sound, reverberating throughout your halls before the Reaper, goading its submission. Yet unlike all before it, your words leave it unaffected. You try again, ardently focusing every ounce of primordial power you possess into one single word. "kneel." The Red Reaper buckles, not into a pose of submission, but rather into one of agony, as if forced to the ground by a million invisible hands, crushing the floor beneath it with each passing second. The crunch of bone is heard, then silence. A pile of cracked bone and torn crimson cloth lies before you. And thus, your day continues.
