With the rending of the realm causing havoc amongst the lands of Luzhong, our King needs to devise a plan to fight against the spirits. Using the increased spiritual power to his advantage, we have prepared a ceremony to commune with one of the ancient emperors of Luzhong. During the ceremony, the spirits confirm to the Emperor that he and his family are the heirs to the Fei Dynasty and that the Xion are destined to rule all worlds, including the spiritual world. He tells the king that he must seek to challenge the great lords of the Necropolis to rule the Spiritual World. The king leaves the room. The eunuchs enter the room thinking the ghost had left, only to find him laughing hysterically.
