Admittance into the best and most prestigious mage academies in the country are hotly contested every single year. The most skilled are usually accepted first, but as with anything, the promise of money can often loosen up the standards somewhat. It is not unheard of for the most powerful patrician families of Castonath to get their magically talented children into the best academies in this way. A recent incident has occurred where an academy has flat out refused to accept a patrician student, citing 'the magical ability of a cockroach' in the offended party. The patrician family, insulted, is demanding that we intervene and force the academy to accept the student, while the mages stand by their decision.
