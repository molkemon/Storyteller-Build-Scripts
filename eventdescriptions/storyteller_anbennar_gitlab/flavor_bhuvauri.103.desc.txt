The time has come for the latest Meritocratic election, this time for the Fangaulan house of Sanukelborlan. The event is awash with ceremony and celebration, a pinnacle of the Praxic Meritocracy we have fostered within great Bhuvauri. The house of Sanukelborlan is prized for their military might and tactical thinking, and as such the candidates reflect this. From the strict hobgoblins, to the mighty brown orks, who shall we choose?
