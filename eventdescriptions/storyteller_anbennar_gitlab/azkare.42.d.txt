From the highest balconies of the convocation in Sarisung one can see about halfway to the city’s edge. Today, the view is resplendent with the sights and sounds of a festival, one which, before the custodianship of the city, would never have been possible. The slums have been cleared and replaced with vast swathes of residential sprawl, the ruined streets have been repaired, buildings of administration have been erected across the city, and as it is in Azkare, so it is across the entire country. Where once Haless was ruined by centuries of warlordism and petty conquests, now it is finally on the road to repair. For many, however, the declaration of the Sunrise Empire only marks the beginning of the ambitions. While the people celebrate the empire, the administration turns its eyes towards the horizons, where the unification of all Haless awaits.
