Water shall destroy, water shall ruin in a pristine roar. The river quietly flows, dislodging secular stones. Our entire country is being flooded by the Ynn River with a violence not seen in centuries. The situation is especially dire in the realm, where emergency workers have been desperately reinforcing the embankments along the main stem of the Ynn, while the bravest TideGuards set out to rescue people and valuables from the crumbled buildings that were carried away. Despite these efforts, the local forces are not enough, and they are requesting the government's assistance. An assistance we cannot give, as our entire country is also battling the wrath of the river.
