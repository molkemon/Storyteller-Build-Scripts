The journey will be harsh. Every sailor knew that was the case, even as they made the final preparations for their great voyage. Soon, all of the crew chimed in to help begin their journey to the seas, until the last of the preparations were set and the ships set sail for the unknown. On the fourth day of the expedition, a sailor spotted a beach covered in blue dusted sands on the horizon, with winds brewing in their direction. They went below the cabin and hid from the storm before it could take another life. Finally, after a week long expedition, the sailors spotted land. strange people following stranger customs, of untamed nature, but also untold riches, all waiting to be claimed by the ambitious new settlers who will call this new land their home.
