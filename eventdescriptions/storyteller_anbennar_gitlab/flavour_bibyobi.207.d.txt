Our conquest of northern Horashesh is complete. With the Medawor Khetists soundly defeated in their strongholds, it is time the Efergezmu of the south and east are properly integrated into our realm's administration. While we have supported their rise to power, our intent was never to allow them complete autonomy. Without our guiding hand, they will surely soon again fall prey to the internal divisions and machinations of outside forces that brought devastation to these lands for decades. But we are not without magnanimity; we will give them a choice. join us, or fall before our armies. We cannot let Horashesh remain divided anymore.
