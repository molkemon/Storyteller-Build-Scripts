Reports have arrived from the Deepwoods of unusual news. Apparently an army of dogs were unleashed upon the woodworkers by the wilds. The dogs, identified as Corgis, were lightly armoured with bark and vines, protecting them from the elements and light projectiles. The battle lasted for around half an hour before the corgis could be repelled. Many of the workers suffered light wounds, with reports of a few casualties. This raises many questions. Why were the fey training corgis and not some mutated or stronger beast? If the wilds tame dogs, are they still wild dogs, or tamed dogs? Questions for a gentler and nobler age, perhaps. The incursion has been put down, and that is all that matters for us.
