Upon learning that the hinuibirds had targeted a certain domain, we vowed that we would provide relief to its people through aid and reinforcements. Unfortunately, that vow seems to have been lost among other priorities in recent months, and when our most recent expedition went to that domain to assure them that aid would arrive soon, we found there was no more need for aid. The hinuibirds had attacked again, and now large parts of what was once a thriving settlement look more like a graveyard. The expedition has crept out of the settlement unseen, so as not to have to speak with the orphaned and the widowed. It is obvious we have failed, and someone is going to have to take the blame.
