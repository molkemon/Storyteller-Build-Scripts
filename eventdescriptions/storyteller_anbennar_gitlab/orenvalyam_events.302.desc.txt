Dawn is a place of high morality, of justice, equality, freedom, and righteousness. And yet, with the ideals of the faith in tatters, the moral degradation of this dream is increasingly evident. Society has become increasingly corrupt; some Kavalali have even turned to accepting bribes, and everywhere merchants and traders have begun to take advantage of the cracks in Orenvalyam to further their own economic interests. If something is not done, this will only get worse.
