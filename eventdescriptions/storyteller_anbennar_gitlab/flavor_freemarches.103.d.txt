From the log of Captain Taruk Freeorc, today marks the end of another week we have been camped at the edge of this accursed blizzard. We have found no source of game, and our nearest drinkable water source is back in the mountains. In addition, these nightmares will not abate; they make our dreams even more unpleasant than being awake out here. Halforks and gnomes continue to wander off at night; we tracked one's footprints to the edge of the blizzard. Something in there is drawing them inwards. Obran continues to barrage me every day about leading an expedition into the blizzard. He says that staying here is suicide, and we must either brave it or leave the wastes for home. Given the state of our supplies, I suspect he is not wrong.
