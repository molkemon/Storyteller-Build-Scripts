An envoy has arrived from Sanghariyar, stating that it is time we repay the gifts they so generously gave us. It appears their gift of money and protection from their soldiers was little more than a thinly veiled ruse to trick us. They never wanted to be our ally or our friend; they wanted our land. Now we have to choose between sacrificing our freedom to them, or fighting when a whole contingent of their army is already within our capital.
