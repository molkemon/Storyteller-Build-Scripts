My ruler. Our fellow goblins from the bank came with a grand proposition. Instead of wasting precious metals for our currency, they came with an amazing idea of using paper money. It is made from serpentsbloom and can be mass produced with no hinge. Our advisors advise that we use that to print money, and stamp whatever value we wish so we can fund and pay any project that we wish. The head of the bank and all of his employees, who may or may not be relatives, gave us a thumbs up; we just need yours too, and we are swimming in money.
