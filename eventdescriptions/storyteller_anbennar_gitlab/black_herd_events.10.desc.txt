Markhentakruan Restoration Notes; today after our first failed cast we have returned to the drawing board. Clearly we lacked an adequate understanding of the transmutation mechanism. So it is back to gathering information. Asking only clan members with magical talent provided an incomplete picture, so I have expanded the scope and ordered every member of the clan old enough to have witnessed the spell or spoken to its casters to give an account. Most of their recountings are obviously of little worth, but some have already given us new trails to follow. The older warriors especially passed on valuable insights; how the elders cast the spell during the heat of battle, what the affected corpses looked like moments before its violent conclusion. The problem stymying our progress is that with so many points of data, it is proving difficult to assemble them into a cohesive whole. For every set of accounts that seem to agree on a characteristic, there is another set that gives a completely different conclusion. We simply lack any hard evidence to determine which are the truth. The only option remaining is to begin working our way through the different permutations and eliminate the false leads one by one. I am not looking forward to it.
