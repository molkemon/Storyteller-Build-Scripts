I am soon to be at rest, yet I am restless. As my life ends, I see this city begin, and yearn to see it spring forth as a flower from winter’s frost. My uncle told me he knew at once the sails of her ships were not new horrors arising from the sea, but a hope unknown which had arrived at last. A Riverstone Washed in the Sea is the Keystone in the Bridge of Dreams. A close shave with a broken blade is the best we get in the Bridge District. Ah. This knife in my heart shall not pierce my love for this city, or unstitch this grand tapestry of the world. The Widows Children are Orphans No More. There is no greater triumph to which to aspire than to awaken the dreams of all who sail underneath or walk upon the Twin Bridges, who become their true selves in a Thornsway costume, who pull gold from thin air among the towers of Tower Street. As a lady told us, we shall go under, over, and through but ever and always forward.
