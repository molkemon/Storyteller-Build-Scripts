Not so long ago, the wyvern gazed over our southern territories, the parts of the Luna watershed that were once part of the old Vernman kingdom, and presumed to play the jovial older brother. At times they even claimed such lands outright, as if certain domains were ill-behaved younger siblings who needed to come back to the fold; sometimes even as if a certain realm were a mere cousin. No longer. Pashainéy dominates the Luna and lands afar alike, and now we are in the position of dispensing with Verne as we wish. Yet there are true ties here, the boisterous bonds of kin which sing to the blood in our Vernman veins. Who are the Vernman to us? Kin, or mere neighbours? They are the red in our purple, but what should that mean? Are we...
