The Imperial Banner marches proudly along the quiet meadow. Only the sound of hoofbeats, soldiers marching along the road, and the errant clattering of steel from tired arms permeate the soundscape. The Emperor leads the army, his armor spotless. No traitorous imperial prince or upstart king comes to greet the gallant host. A bee buzzes up toward the imperial army, bobbing up and down lazily as it passes by a horse and heads off to find an open flower. "Well, my liege?" A tired captain asks the Emperor, who raises a hand, halting the column abruptly. The Emperor gazes across the green meadow and exhales.
