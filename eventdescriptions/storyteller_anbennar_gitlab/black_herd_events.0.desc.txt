It is the end of an era for the centaur tribes. The Filustkhuga and Maslekhuga sects led the packs into the incursion against the ogres, an event that they took part in while hoping to gain new lore, knowledge, and all the loot they could carry. They were wrong. Though the initial conflicts were a success for them, the other tribes grew too greedy and hastily attempted to move ahead, resulting in the recently crowned ogre king destroying them in battle and ending any chance of them gaining anything in their realm. Their tribesmen barely escaped the ensuing chaos, with many of the mages in their ranks sacrificing themselves in battle, so that the majority of the Black Herd could escape back into the plains. When the Wild Hunt called for a similar incursion into the Lake Federation, some of their ranks ran east to join, and, to their knowledge, died a worthless death in a fool's war. The others, however, hid deep in the westernmost plains and bided their time, waiting for the inevitable collapse of the Wild Hunt. Their prediction came true around three years ago, and they now have gained control over a small piece of territory and reestablished their fallen clan. Led by one of the last remaining mages and only remaining heir to the title of storm king in their ranks, Baildelk the Second, they may never get another opportunity like this again. By Irdaeos’s blessing, they will survive.
