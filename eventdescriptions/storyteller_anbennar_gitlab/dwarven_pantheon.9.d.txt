A delegation of runepriests from Urisazirn arrived in the capital today, bringing with them carts loaded with magical artefacts and runeworks of their own creation. They presented a few pieces and books as gifts to the monarch, while the rest were sold to various nobles, mage guilds, and independent practitioners to assist the church's coffers. This mass distribution of magical items will undoubtedly reinforce the influence of the country's spellcasters for the foreseeable future, or at least until the objects are inevitably lost or damaged due to misuse or misfortune.
