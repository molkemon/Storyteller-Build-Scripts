Living in this terrain makes one understand how helpless and tiny the ants of other lands must feel. Of course, in the lands we call home there are also similar ants capable of stripping entire settlements limb from limb to feed their colonies, so perhaps what we are is even more helpless than ants. Despite all this, we have learned to thrive in this terrain, and now have developed the tools necessary to make sure our enemies cannot. Amidst this land, every blade of grass can be a weapons platform and every passing animal can be a terrible threat, and now our artificers have developed the tools to make sure we can take maximum advantage of the terrain in each battle. When our enemies come to us, we now have the power to take the fight right back to them.
