As we sail down the Kharunyana, our ruler as well as our mages are working hard to try and divine where exactly we should head, aided by a generous amount of ale. Finally, as we pass a small island, the monarch indicates that this is our destination. A group of locals gathers around our ships as we beach our ships on the shore, demanding to know what we are doing on their island. They soon disperse, however, as the ruler orders a large pile of gold to be brought forward, declaring that we are buying their island.
