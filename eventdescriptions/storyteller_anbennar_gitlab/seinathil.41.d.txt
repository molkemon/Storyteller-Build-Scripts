It is said that of all the composers in Cannor, Pierre Alexandre Commonfoot might be the first to have the true gift of sharing emotion through his music, of communicative expression and fair feeling. Commonfoot, a halfling composer living in Lorent, writes genius pieces that make you truly feel like you are walking the streets of Lorentainé with him. This soothing, pleasant style has permeated life in Seinathíl, and has brought the entire country to a more serene, calm state.
