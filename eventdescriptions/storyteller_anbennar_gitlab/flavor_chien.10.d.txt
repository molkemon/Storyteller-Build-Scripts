It was early morning when the monarch woke up. The last night was a joyous occasion, a great celebration of what has been accomplished by all Anroangs in the past few centuries. All ambitions were complete; they had cemented their rule over the Yanglam and Kai people, they had monopolized the gem market with arcane gemstones, and they had ensured the Binhrung state would be secured from internal opposition. The monarch stood looking out into the morning sky, wondering what could be next for Chien Binhrung. It was then when a sudden chill swept past the monarch, blowing into her room. Recognizing the meaning of this, she turned around to greet her ancestor. Only this time, it was not just one. Nhitrac, Duyen, Ngoc, Suong, Huyen, and Jinmari all appeared around the monarch. The living Anroang stared at each and every one of them, shocked to see all of them outside of the temple. She was even more shocked when they all bowed in front of her. In unison, all six of the legendary Anroangs of Chien Binhrung said a phrase all subjects of the state utter. "Long Live Anroang."
