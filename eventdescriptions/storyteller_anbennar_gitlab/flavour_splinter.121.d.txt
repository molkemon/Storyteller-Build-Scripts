Great excitement grips the people of the realm today. Wild stories of magic, artifacts, strange red people, and adventure grip the nation. A great many people have petitioned the royal court to not only send more expeditions, but many even volunteer to join these missions themselves. While the past aversion to magic was rooted in historical tragedy, perhaps this can be a new dawn for the people? Just as monarchy was once feared and shunned, perhaps magic too can be reborn under the reign.
