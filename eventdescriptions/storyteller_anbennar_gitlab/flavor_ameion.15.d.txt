It was an ingenious plan, one that only learned philosophers and scholars of government could devise. If there were always to be those who would reject our rule and reject the value of Kheionai civilization, why should we suffer their incessant whining and rebellious schemes? As in the realm of law, exile is more moral than death, and so that shall be our solution. Already, the first Taychendi settlers have arrived on the island, devoid of any form of civilization. Not even primal tribes wander across the trees and cliffs on this landmass. Here they will build a place for their values, however backward, to survive. Already, they have established a government they believe mimics the last issued lawcode of Vuls Tenvach. So long as this reserve exists, those who foolishly challenge our rule on the mainland will have no allies to muster and will ultimately submit or come here.
