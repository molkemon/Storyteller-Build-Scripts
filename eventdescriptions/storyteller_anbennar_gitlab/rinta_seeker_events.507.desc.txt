The design for the Godswatch is all but finalized, but still the Mwalim and Darwiiz find yet more topics to argue about. Their latest disagreement regards the availability of the central telescope. The Darwiiz have taken the stance that the central telescope should be given an air of spiritual reverence; looking through it should be treated as an act of great devotion. As such, they argue its use should be limited only to mages and spiritual leaders, with the general populace limited to only using the periphery telescopes to gaze upon the sky. The Mwalim disagree with this, arguing that the central telescope should not be given any restrictions on who should use it. to do so would be an arbitrary limitation that could severely hamper its usage as an instrument of research. As always, they could try and force a compromise by simply instituting a priority system for usage of the telescope, but it is clear that neither group would be very happy with this.
