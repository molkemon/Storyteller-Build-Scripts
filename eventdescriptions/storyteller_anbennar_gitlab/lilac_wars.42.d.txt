Throughout Esmaria, preparations are being made and the court shall not be absent. A great battle shall soon begin, one fought not with steel but with artistry. Yes, the Thorned Moon festival shall soon be upon us. Courtiers and designers, princes and baronesses from both the Moon and Rose parties are gathering now that the war is done. It is a true testament to Esmari's dedication to fashion and beauty that these former foes can talk across the former lines of conflict once more. In this contest, potions, perfumes, and songs shall take the flanks. And at the vanguard shall be clothes and dresses of silk and fabric. As for them, they will make sure they have the presence to renew contacts and restore those with whom they fought against. The Lilac Wars were a tragedy but also the vogue of the past. It is well past time to usher in a new trend.
