After the success of our device, we have begun work on the implementation of our new tool into our society. This process begins with the shrinking down of the device. Already gargantuan in size, one had to climb and sit upon a specialized seat to be able to use and control the device properly. This would be fine if it was to emit a mass amount of energy, but at its current state, it would be a waste of metal and effort. Thankfully, our team of highly trained scientists has managed to size down the device to where it is able to be transported with ease, and to where its production viability becomes less of an issue. It still has a measly output, but we also have a team working on how to improve the amount of energy our laser is able to emit while still keeping the photons stable and coherent. The speed at which we are all moving towards advancements with our laser is astounding.
