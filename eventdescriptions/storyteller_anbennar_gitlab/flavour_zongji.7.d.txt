We have finally, after a long and arduous millennium, found ourselves back in control of Jiantsiang. It was first established by us in the year nine hundred and eighty AA as a garrison on the northern frontier of our empire, to bravely protect Yanshen from bandits and to stop Shuvuush incursions from reaching deeper into the region. As our empire began collapsing, we eventually lost control over the region. Yet over a thousand years after our empire collapsed, Jiantsiang has survived, and at times, thrived. Their great brazier, the Undying Flame, still burns as red as Tigersblood Rubies, and it is a true testament to the strength and tenacity of the people all over Yanshen that it does so. Under our rule, we will ensure that the brazier continues to burn, and that northern Yanshen will continue to be safe and sound. The valiant warriors of Jiantsiang happen to fall within the upper castes in our new order, and for their service, they will be granted estates, and solidified as some of the most prestigious warrior families in Yanshen. The shrewd merchants of Jiantsiang, gemcarvers and merchants, in the lucrative ruby trade, will be a part of the middle castes, with easy access to our whole empire, a grand expansion on the trade of these blood red rubies. And you cannot forget the people in the fields of the frontier, providing food for the garrison and risking their lives living near the Shuvuush border, or the people in the mines extracting the rubies for refinement. They will fit into the lower castes, their endurance and strength invaluable to our order, and our vision.
