Shedding all pretense of loyalty, Kayd's faction of traditionalist, honorbound warriors have taken up arms against the state. For Kayd, the monarch has clearly become a pawn of radicals who seek to dismantle all the timehonored traditions of elven society. As several officials and other prominent figures declare for Kayd, it has become clear that this reactionary faction has been preparing for this day for some time. Worried commoners fear the reign of blood and terror as the monarchy mobilizes its response.
