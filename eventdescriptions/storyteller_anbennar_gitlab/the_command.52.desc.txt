Research Phase Forty-Four Six; Entry Seven. I gathered representatives from all of the subordinate commands along with our Oni attachés and reviewed the current state of the process. The Oni do not understand our hesitation to induct more ritualists, but they are not used to caution regarding such matters. Luckily, presenting it as chemistry rather than shamanism has done wonders for gaining acceptance, and we received at least the verbal approval from each of the line chiefs. It feels strange to be aligning with the Oni against my own people, but with such a slow process, manpower truly is the name of the game. I suggested we draft retirees and the injured, since the process is not inherently exhausting, just time-consuming. None of it is a substitute for process improvements, though; we have already filled three warehouses with unactivated teimarji.
