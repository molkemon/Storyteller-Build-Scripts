The nobility of the realm felt that the monarch did not have the proper upbringing a Sun Elven monarch should. But that is nothing that some lessons from good educators couldn’t fix. Lessons in diplomacy, decorum, justice systems, accounting, and a few years of study, the monarch is now as competent as any member of the court, if not more so. The nobles will have fewer reasons to complain after such a show of devotion to the good of the realm.
