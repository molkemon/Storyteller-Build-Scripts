The Regent Court League is victorious, and so are we. We have our muses to thank for this victory. In Seinathíl, we have historically sung to two muses in particular. Munas, the God of travel and exploration, is also the patron of music, and thus fits our songs quite well. Ryala, however, has been the popular muse for many young artists, an inspiration for many a song. Who do we give credit to for our victory today?
