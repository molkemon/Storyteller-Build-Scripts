The sudden outburst from one of their scouts takes the rest of them and the moss sorter by surprise. There is a moment of silence and stillness, but as everyone's heartbeats slow down, it passes, with the first movement made by the cave's inhabitant. He slowly takes a handful of moss off the shelf, looks towards their party, then to the moss, then to their party again. The momentary joy of the scouts' foremost moss expert is quickly shattered, as a colorful glob flies towards their face at incredible speed. Taking a moment to compose themselves ends up being a mistake, as several more come right after and nearly completely cover the expeditioners. The subtle social clue is taken, and the scouts promptly abscond the cave. As they run for their dignity, in the distance they can still hear the moss sorter cackling to himself.
