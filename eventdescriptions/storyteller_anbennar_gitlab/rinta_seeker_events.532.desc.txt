No society is immune to corruption, not ever in the past and not ever in the future. Still, we would like to think we are not as corrupt as others, yet a massive scheme has gone unnoticed right beneath our gaze. Large amounts of funding that was supposed to go towards the grand telescope has instead gone to seemingly nowhere. It has been eaten up by workers faking work, nobles misplacing funds, and government officials taking their own slice to keep quiet about it all. Every society has some corruption, but we did not expect this much.
