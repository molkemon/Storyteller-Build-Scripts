Much like the Dwarves of old, many of the major Human cities in Bulwar have strong identities, which persist even after many of them have been subjugated by successor states to the accursed Phoenix Empire. Of these, the ones of particular interest to us are the cities doubling as significant centers of trade, where we seek to secure several rights and concessions on behalf of our traders. In already independent realms, this might be possible to achieve with warm relations; and a generous donation to the coffers of the local elites; while for those subjugated for a foreign state, that foreign state might well be willing to literally sell out their subjects, if the price is right. And for those occasions where a rival or implacable foe of ours holds sway over one of the major trade cities of Bulwar, by greasing the right palms, by enticing the right notables to support our cause, and through espionage to fabricate or find incriminating documents, we could convince the rest of Bulwar that it would be right and proper for our nation to intervene against our foe; and the people we liberate will likely be eager to find new protectors. And in states already under our sway, they would probably be happy to provide a few modest trade concessions, in return for lessening our grasp over their foreign policy.
