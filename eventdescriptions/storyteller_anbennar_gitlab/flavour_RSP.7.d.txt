My scurvy subjects, with this Driftcourt convened, I now pronounce my first decree as the leader of the pirates. A single fleet needs a single flag to sail under, and this choice is no small matter. We must choose a colour that shall honour all the pirates before us, who never got the chance to reap the vast plunder that will be brought in by our fleets. But we are a band of pirates all our own, and these colours must represent us as well, standing for all the glory we’ll swindle and swashbuckle our way to. We now choose a colour that will be feared across the Ruined Sea. that will appear over every bay, inlet, and cove. And when our colours soar on those horizons, the people there will rush to hide their valuables. Or try, anyway, since we won’t give them the chance. There are many pirates at this court tonight, aye, but the nation that assembled all the pirates of the Ruined Sea into a great federation. And so it falls to this court to decide. will we carry forward all those flags of the past, or bring one brilliant, blazing flag into the future? The decision is made.
