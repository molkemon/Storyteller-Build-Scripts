Encouraged by recent successes against the Command and hoping to regain their lost eastern lands, the brave heroes of Tilathi have expressed their fervent desire to join the next campaign against the hobgoblin menace. When news of this reached the royal court, it was met with enthusiastic approval. Yet it appears that their masters in Sarnavan have other plans and are firmly opposed to any action that might bring the Command’s retribution down on them. So whatever their original intentions, it appears that the stalwart people of Tilathi might have to free themselves from their chains before they can liberate their eastern brethren.
