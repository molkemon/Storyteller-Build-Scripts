The streets are quiet in the ambassadorial district. Most of the Dao have vacated their offices in the province and have moved their share of guards for the capital to their own capitals instead. They claim that they no longer feel represented by the council. The Inked Shields claim that the council is trying to modernize and centralize forcefully, giving up the principles of the union, while the Red Scarves claim that the council is starting to become a bloated and ancient institution which serves only the blind who remain in the past. It is quiet, though this seems to be merely a calm before the storm. The time has come for us to make a decision which side we stand on, one last time.
