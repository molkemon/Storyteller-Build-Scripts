Whispers from the lesser mages at court have filtered back to the monarch. Concerns have been rising that the amount of land directly held by the throne risks the collapse of the domain, just like the historical destruction of the realm of the Heretic. The idea that they must not hang the future of the Mystic Revolution on the shoulders of one person is the governing philosophy of the Black domain, and it seems that the time may have come to establish a new dominion under a subject Acolyte.
