Hours of grueling tracking later, the creature finally arrives at the large maw of a cave, the muddy ground in front of the entrance crossed with the long gashes characteristic of basilisk tracks, and its depths lost in dark shadow. Chunks of stone, vaguely reminiscent of limbs, perhaps previous victims, serve as a stark reminder of the basilisk's petrifying gaze. Pushing through the thick sense of unease that seems to saturate the air, the creature slowly enters the cave, senses on a hair trigger. Several tense minutes later, the creature is able to make out the end of the dark cave, and no basilisk in sight. Just as the creature is about to leave, all hope of becoming Castan lost, a loud rumble and the sound of something being dragged along the floor breaks through the silence. The creature quickly finds an alcove to hide in. The basilisk arrives, the petrified remains of its latest hunt scraping against the thin walls. Thankfully, it seems too focused on its dinner to notice anything out of place. What shall the creature do?
