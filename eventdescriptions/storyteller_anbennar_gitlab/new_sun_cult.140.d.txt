Jaherian ta'Diranhriá, or Jaher as he is known to us, was the elven liberator of Bulwar. The original Sun Cult named him Surakel reborn, a god given earthly form on Halann. His conquests in the name of Surael, his proper name, would serve only to reinforce this belief. Upon his assassination in eleven twenty-seven AA, the people's faith in him and his kind was shaken. How could a divine being simply be assassinated like a common general? The priesthood made a proclamation then that Jaher's divinity, as granted by Surael, had passed on to his kin upon his death, making Sun Elves the chosen people of Surael and, as such, divine in their own right. There have, of course, always been people who think otherwise. Some maintain that no one could truly embody Surael's majesty and that only parts of his divinity are in the Sun Elves. Some believe they are not divine at all, and simply share a connection to Surael. As such a core tenet of our belief, it is vital that we have a unified opinion on this matter.
