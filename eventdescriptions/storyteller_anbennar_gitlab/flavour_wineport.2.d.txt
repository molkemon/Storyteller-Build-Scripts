Another toll vanquished, another party with food and wine flowing freely, and another slurred and drunken debate about whether they should renege on their promises and in fact keep the toll in place. The monarch sighed, and swirled their glass of freshly liberated wine. The ruler looked between the two sides; one arguing for free trade and keeping promises, how more trade will enrich everyone. The other side said to think of the League’s coffers and how they could be enriched, and how the Wine Barons could dominate trade. And then, of course, they all turned to the monarch's title, and waited for the final answer.
