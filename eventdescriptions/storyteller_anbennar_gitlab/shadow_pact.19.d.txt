A most ominous shadow has begun to encroach upon our lands in the last months. Seemingly out of the blue, people in the prime of their life have begun to lose all apparent interest in their lives, becoming listless and withdrawn. The most advanced cases of this disease have become almost catatonic, its victims barely reacting to even the most drastic outside stimuli. No treatment has proven efficacious, and the victims of this strange disease follow no rhyme nor reason. Sturdy laborers, brave soldiers, diligent workers. all waste away, losing their grip on life and depriving the realm of their contributions. The people have begun to call it the ‘Burial Shroud’, for its victims behave as if already buried in the ground even as they still breathe.
