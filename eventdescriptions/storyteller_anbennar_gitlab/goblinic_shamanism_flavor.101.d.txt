The gnomes value intelligence over all else. They pay homage to neither gods nor spirits, preferring to develop an understanding of the physical world. A group of our shamans have adopted gnomish thinking and now demand recognition as the Cult of Reason. They have begun debating, experimenting, and recording every major and minor discovery they have made. While they are eccentric even by goblin standards and the majority of their findings have been useless at best, occasionally they produce actionable ideas.
