Unlike our enemies of Bianfang, our new rivals of the Onis and the Hobgoblins in the Command have no respect for any organized warfare. The Onis especially have been shown to love raiding the countryside and destroying the lands of the locals. In response to an increase of said events, it had been decided to take the time to build a Western Outpost, a great fort that will stop these creatures in their wake. It has almost finished construction, and it already looks like it could take on a horde by its own.
