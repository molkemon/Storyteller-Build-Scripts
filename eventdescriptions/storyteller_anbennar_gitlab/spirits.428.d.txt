No matter how powerful or knowledgeable someone is, everyone needs a moment to rest and gather their thoughts. Even our glorious monarch needs a breather if not to gather some inspiration. And so the ruler settled for a hilltop underneath a chrysanthemum tree with a perfect view of the High Temple on the other side of the province. And with a simple bamboo flute, the ruler played. Soft tunes playing out their whole life up to this point, the harsh youth, the ever threatening coronation as the new ruler. All the decisions, be it good or bad, that brought them to this point under this chrysanthemum tree. At that moment only the ruler could be heard, across the small valley, attracting citizens to check the source of the soothing sounds. Gathering people by the hundreds, even smaller spirits appeared around the tree, everyone listening quietly to the story of their flute.
