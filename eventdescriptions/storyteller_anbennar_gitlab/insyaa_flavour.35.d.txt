In recent weeks, the administrative advisor has been zealous in attempting to persuade the monarch to sit for another one of his portraits. It seemed like a mere flight of artistic inspiration at the time, but guards beginning their early morning patrols discovered today that some of the kobold’s best-loved tools, trinkets, and souvenirs from his time in their service have disappeared from his workshop in the palace, along with the kobold himself. On his central easel sat the most recent portrait of the monarch he completed, its paint still drying. It has been hung alongside the first, and observers in the court have noticed that the eyes of the second portrait appear quite a bit wiser.
