For the first time in millennia, the magma of Gor Bûrad boils. The axes are sharpened and metals tempered. This only means one thing. the Borbordûz has returned. The hearts of all Basalt dwarves burn like an inferno as they watch their ruler step upon the dark rock of the platform, magma bubbling below. Through the great commotion, their speech rings through like the strongest of Shriekers. "I am the ruler, of Clan. I am the inheritor of the Basalt throne of Gor Bûrad, the same that Magni the Vindictive once held. The soul and wrath of every Basalt dwarf speaks through me alone. All around me tremble at my step, friend or foe. It is today that our greatest tradition reignites. Let the Borbordûz begin."
