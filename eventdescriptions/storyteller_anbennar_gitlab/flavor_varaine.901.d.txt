First of all, in the process to create the legendary brew requested by our ruler, our alchemists must have the appropriate equipment for the job. Though the glassware and other tools currently available to the government aren't bad, the advanced experiments our endeavor will require call for more modern equipment, much of it perhaps even made specifically for our purposes. To acquire this specialized equipment, we will have to spend quite a sizable amount of money. Alternatively, we could commandeer the private equipment of the independent alchemists, which would go a long way in reducing our costs, but would also violently upset the group.
