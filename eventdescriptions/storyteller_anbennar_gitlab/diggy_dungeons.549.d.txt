A heated debate breaks out among the expedition over the expedition leader's proposed incentive. No soldier can turn down an extra meal, and more water will combat the heat. Sensing weakness, several mages are also arguing for higher quality rations and extra potions to go with them. Attempts to placate these mages fail, and soon they have convinced most of the engineers and support staff to side with them, demanding a vote on the matter. After more time wasted organizing a vote, the mages win by only a handful. Facing the prospect of mutiny if he does not capitulate, the expedition leader reluctantly agrees to extra rations of a higher quality and more potions for all.
