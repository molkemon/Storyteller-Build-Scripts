The dying wish of our late ruler was to honour the Summer Court, requesting to retire to the depths of the sea. Yet it seems the sea itself had other considerations; guards off the coast of the realm watched as the royal casket was treated as the Nereids' plaything, and, when they bored of it, they sent it back to shore. The public spectacle this has created is widely seen as a striking omen for the government as a whole, with riots breaking out in the streets against our supposedly illegitimate rule.
