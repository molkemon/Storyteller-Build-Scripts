As the traders woke up, they noticed something was off about the caravan spider. When trying to load it, the spider resisted and behaved erratically. It seemed agitated. The tamers expressed concerns of a potential illness. As the early morning drew by with no progress on the caravan, the spider seemed increasingly aggressive as well. It might rampage. A spider of that size could level towns if it did that. Imagine eight legs, each the size of a guard tower, running over a building. Each leg could destroy a house alone.
