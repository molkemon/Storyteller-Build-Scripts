At last, Droga arrives in Redgarhavn, and is overjoyed to find her husband Khrosh taking shelter in the market where he was doing his captor's shopping before the raid began. Alas, her joy is excessive indeed, for she does not see that same captor's axe strike the back of her neck. Droga's raiders are driven off, but her severed head will keep her husband company on his captor's mantel for the rest of his life.
