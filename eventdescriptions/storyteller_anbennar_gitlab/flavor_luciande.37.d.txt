Lucian Síl na Toars. This name has brought fear to the heart of everyone who has heard it muttered. Long has he commanded the Company of Thorns, maybe too long even. After the decisive fall of the Greentide, he and his company set out for Escann, not to conquer the old holdings but to instead create something new. a realm of his own. Lucian rules his realm with an iron fist, keeping all the chains to himself as he trusts no one with power in the infancy of his domain. Lucian is, however, pestered with the cursed blessing of unlife, and until now he has managed to behold the masquerade, partly due to the fact that in his ripe vampiric age he still hasn’t sired others. There might yet come a moment when Lucian is left with no choice but to depend on vampires of his own, no matter how inconveniently troublesome delegating his tasks to others might be. Still, the realm is young and threats riddle its borders. There is much work to be done, but Lucian will see his domain prosper, and he will see it forged in his vision.
