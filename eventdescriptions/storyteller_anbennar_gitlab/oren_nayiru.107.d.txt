To claim that the institution of slavery has done more to fill the Oren Nayiru’s ranks than any other factor is to state the obvious. Growing as it did from a religious brotherhood specifically dedicated to vanquishing it, the Kamrayakval’s forces have been swelled with masses of freed slaves, and the Oren Nayiru have, obviously, made a point of freeing any of those they come across. This has also resulted in significant unrest within the slave stock; in a slave revolt, the slaves have risen up against their masters, pledged support for Silverspite, and caused significant damage while doing so.
