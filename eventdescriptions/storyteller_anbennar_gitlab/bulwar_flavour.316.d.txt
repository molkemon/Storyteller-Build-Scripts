The ancient city of Khasa has changed hands many times, and its inhabitants are accustomed to foreign overlords. But unlike many conquerors before them, the forces of the current ruler are not here to plunder the riches of Akasik or enslave its people, but to bring them into the fold of the faithful. When the high Kašra of EduzNebuszufalli, The Temple of Bright Skies, proclaimed Mother Akasik as one of the minor gods of certain locales and allowed her veneration as an entity subservient to the divine Order of Surael, many who previously had hesitated to break with the faith of their ancestors gladly joined those who liberated them from the yoke of the Xhazobkult in worship of Surael. The Asamkage A'juich Ijima, as the Akasi call the Temple of Bright Skies, has subsequently become the heart of Sun Cult faith in Akasik. The temple hosts the largest of the traditional summer solstice celebrations, praising Surael instead of His devoted servant Mother Akasik. The efforts to absorb the traditional religious orders of Akasik like the Desati Sisterhood into the ranks of the Sun Cult orders are coordinated within this most sacred precinct. It is here that the Hurkashu fire planetouched train to join the ranks of the priesthood alongside their brethren amongst the Kašipti, or mages of Light, united in their desire to bring down sacred sunfire upon all servants of the Dark who dare threaten this land or its resilient people.
