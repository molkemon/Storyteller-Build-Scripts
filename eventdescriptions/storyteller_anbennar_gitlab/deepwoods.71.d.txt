The Lord of Beasts rears onto his hind legs, and swings a hulking arm toward you with all the power of a wild animal desperate to survive. As your body screams for mercy, you muster all the strength left inside your muscles, and strike with savage fury. To your surprise, the blow finds its mark, and you bury your weapon deep inside the monster’s powerful body. He stumbles back, stunned, and a final guttural roar shakes the leaves of the trees, giving way to silence. You collapse to the ground, utterly spent. After gathering the strength to rouse yourself, you realize you are no longer alone. The many species of the woods have gathered at the edges of the clearing and in the branches of the trees, silently observing the carnage left by the meeting of two apex predators.
