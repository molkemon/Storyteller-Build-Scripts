The Herald of Light shoulders the heavy burden of rule over all of blessed this country and guiding the faithful in the struggle against the Dark, thus only the most devout servants of Surael are worthy to be considered for this lofty position. It only follows then, that the successor to the outgoing Herald of Light should be chosen by vote from amongst the ranks of the Heralds of the Holy Orders, who have proven their unwavering devotion to the cause of the Light. Who amongst the Heralds shall be elevated and become the new Herald of Light?
