The world may be changing in new and unfathomable ways, but the way we live remains the same. After the people of our realm spend their day attempting to change the world, they return to homes built by their ancestors, where they speak a language that has been spoken since time immemorial and interact with one another the same way their grandparents did. We do not need a mere change in norms; we must change everything about the way we live. There are new and interesting ideas we could adopt from cultural minorities and people who came here from abroad. If we were to restructure our lives in accordance with their ways and invite more newcomers to our lands, it would destroy centuries of stagnant tradition in a single fell swoop.
