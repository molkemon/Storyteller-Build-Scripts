Across the lowlands lie our sleeping brethren. When the goddess made us, the Children of Water, she also made the Children of Fire. They were supposed to fight alongside us. And yet they sleep, for neither we nor they were ready to take the fight to the jungle. When they next awaken, they must see that they are not alone. We will gather them all in one place; a long and tiring process for sure, but one that must be done.
