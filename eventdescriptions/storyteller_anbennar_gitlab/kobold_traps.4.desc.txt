The south is full of rich and plentiful land, which is devastatingly hard to defend. The small country hosts thousands of fertile farms which provide grain for half a continent; Lencenor is full of wine lords and elves. Neither, though, boasts much in the way of swamps, hills, mountains, or highlands. If we want to defend this region, we must do so through a wide net of traps which can protect our entire southern flank. Where, specifically, should we create or upgrade our traps?
