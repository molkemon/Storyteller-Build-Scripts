A large human cohort of the military has all handed in their resignations at once. The group is far too large to have any hope of issuing a court martial for desertion, lest they risk a civil war. Their reasoning is they disagree with the attempts to find the Pit of Grilax. They claim that the search has gone far too south, when in fact the Pit of Grilax was clearly located deep in the Salahad. They have set out on a journey into the hottest, driest parts of the desert. Thousands and thousands of men are heading out on a perilous journey into a place arguably even less habitable than the Shadow Swamp, in the name of locating this mythical location. If any return, it will be a miracle.
