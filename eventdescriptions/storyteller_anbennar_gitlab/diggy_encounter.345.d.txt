Through deception and trickery, we managed to contain the Cube in a makeshift pasture from which it cannot escape. Contained this way, a debate sparks to live what we should do with the Cube. Some got tired of constantly saying "the Gelatinous Cube" and decided to give it a proud and imposing name. Larry the First, Lord of the Depths, Duke of Ooze, Grandmaster of all Cuboids.
