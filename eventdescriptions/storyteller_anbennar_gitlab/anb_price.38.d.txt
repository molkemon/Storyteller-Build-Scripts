Porcelain has always been popular in Cannor, and along with silk and ivory, it served as one of the main goods flowing into the west from Haless. The new adventurer kingdoms of Escann, settled in the aftermath of the Greentide, seem to be loving it far more than anyone else, as is the way with the newly rich. Combined with easier access to Rahen through new trade routes of the Dwarovar, the demand for porcelain has never been higher.
