Throughout the ages, war has often been the time when the rule of the many gave way to the rule of the few. Being aware of this, we are reluctant to make too many concessions in power to the military even now that our nation goes to battle. A general who has been crucial in our successes so far, at least in her own version of events, is now in conflict with representatives of the government on the future course of action. Her military expertise is beyond question, but strongarming the people to go along with her plans would weaken their political position in the realm.
