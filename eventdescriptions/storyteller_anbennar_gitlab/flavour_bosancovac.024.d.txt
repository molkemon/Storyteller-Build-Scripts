As our city has expanded and grown, so too has our prestige and reputation across the realm. Thus, to accommodate them and display the wonders of our mighty city, the Foreign Quarter was built. Constructed in opulent style, painted and decorated by the finest artists money can commission, each building is designed perfectly to grant the comforts of home to those dignitaries who come to stay here, enough so that they may want to stay here forever; and truly, with such wonders, who can blame them?
