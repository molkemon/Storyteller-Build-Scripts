Purifying the Land… For Profit. Every taverngoer in the domain was familiar with the cry by now. Bibyobi has had its fair share of beast tamers and monster slayers since its founding. Bibyobi translated to Beast Camp after all. But this whirlwind of recruiting had never been seen before. Thousands had signed up to join the official Monster Hunters of Bibyobi, and they were seeking thousands more. Ostensibly to purify the land of all sorts of foul and malodorous beast and sell whatever can be harvested from the corpse. With the fervor these monster hunters organized, it was sure to be an adventure. In order to reclaim the jungle of Dao Nako, it must first be purified of the evil monsters that lurk in its depths. It is the official order of the crown of Bibyobi that the Monster Hunters be organized to hunt and kill these terrors.
