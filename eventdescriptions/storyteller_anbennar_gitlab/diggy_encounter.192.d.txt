The peaceful demeanor of the Kobolds was a façade put in place to lure them towards them. Once the expedition came close, they jumped up and laughed, pulling and pushing ropes and rocks, leading to a cave-in next to them. From the newly formed crevice emerged an entire horde of Kobolds with weapons and armed to the teeth. This force surprised the already startled soldiers as they descended upon them, slaughtering those in their way and stealing from those close by.
