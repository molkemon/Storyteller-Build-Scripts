The Wild Hunt has survived its sundering. Our grand challenge has reinvigorated their realm, albeit barely. This does put us in a tight spot. Bloodbringer and City Razer are still subject to their rule, and their envoys are working hard to integrate them while we are cut off from the rest of Irdülhain. We cannot, will not let this stand. Our advisors have concocted a plan to counteract their resurgence, but it will take money and one of our diplomats to make it work. Their plan? To remind them of their folly. Remind them of the fruits that the crimson call has borne for them.
