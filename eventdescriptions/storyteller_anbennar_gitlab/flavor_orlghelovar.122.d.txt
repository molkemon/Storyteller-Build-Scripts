Despite all the doubters, we have achieved what was thought impossible. The mages have been integrated as a proper part of our researchers, with the new position of mage supervisor in the council. This mage supervisor is elected by the most powerful and renowned mages of the realm, and is in charge of the practical applications of magical research. The mutual mistrust has been replaced by a friendly rivalry, as researchers and mages both endeavor to contribute to the filling of our libraries with new magical knowledge.
