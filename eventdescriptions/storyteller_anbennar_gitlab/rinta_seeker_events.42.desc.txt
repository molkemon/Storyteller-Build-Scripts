Reports have reached our court of a group of Darwiiz in the province that have begun dancing. This wouldn’t be out of the ordinary, were it not for the fact that the dance is especially eccentric, and has been going on for several full days at this point. Many local citizens have also become enthralled, joining the dance alongside the mystics. It has become a matter of some concern, and multiple have even fallen dead of exhaustion after their continued dancing.
