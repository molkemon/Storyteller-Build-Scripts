After a long fought battle, we have finally managed to get these orks we have been fighting against beneath our boots. But simple domination is not enough. we want these orks to end. As we cannot personally guarantee the death of the orks we have conquered in the long run, nor can we actually take all of this land for ourselves yet, we will guarantee their demise in the short term. Burn the lands and the occupants; we will occupy them when it suits us.
