The humans of Bulwar may be Surael’s firstborn creation, but over the centuries, many races have found their way to His Garden. The dwarves, who have forsaken their ancient mansions beneath the mountains to dwell beneath His Light. The elves, who arrived in Bulwar’s darkest hour to deliver the faithful from the clutches of the Red Xhaz. The harpies, whose winged warriors once struck fear into the hearts of the Bulwari during the age of the queendom, but who have since then inextricably woven themselves into the rich tapestry of Bulwar. Even the goblins, whose savage multitudes streamed out of the lightless abyss beneath the mountains mere decades ago, have begun to flourish under Surael’s benevolent Light, eagerly partaking in His gifts of Order and Civilisation. Only the gnolls have time and again refused to join the ranks of the faithful, revelling in their vile depredations and gleefully opening their hearts and minds to the corroding whispers of the Malevolent Dark. While the rare blessed few amongst their number may have chosen the path of Light, most of their ilk have spurned every opened hand, every opportunity of redemption, over and over again. Now, there will be no more chances, no more mercy extended to those who have proven beyond a doubt their utter degradation. Surael’s Light might be a blessed balm to the faithful, but to those abominable minions of the Dark, it shall become a burning torch, finally cauterizing the foul wound of their continued existence and cleansing the Garden of Surael of these poisonous weeds. With the last drop of foul gnollish blood spilled into the desert sands, the memory of noble king Eledas shall finally be avenged.
