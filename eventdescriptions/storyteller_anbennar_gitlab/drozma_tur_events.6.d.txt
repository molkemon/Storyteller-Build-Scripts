A few tales have reached the court about lone wanderers or riders in our country seeking out talented fighters, mages, scholars, and other skilled individuals, offering them work in a far off place. Naturally, in these dark ages, there are few who would accept such offers, and even fewer who would be worth asking. However, it is a bit rude to go around trying to poach people who should by right and law serve their lords, so it may be wise to put an end to this recruitment.
