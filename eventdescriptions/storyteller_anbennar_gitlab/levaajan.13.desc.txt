The ruler listens inquisitively as the various orders of the monasteries make their cases known. petty arguments over monastic borders, heated disputes over the poaching of preeminent scholars, and other such drivel. The ruler looks at the quill before them, before jotting down the next line. Perhaps if the borders between the monasteries were to loosen, if they were allowed to spread to each other. Yes. This is the first step, down a path the ruler will see the country walk. And with this walk, so too shall the fabrics begin to reveal a new pattern of interweaved cloth and expensive silk, pointing the way towards the future in gold supplication.
