We spent some hours marching and resting. As we advance, the passage starts getting wider and broader. Light reflections shine from the end of the tunnel. The tunnel is no more. We reach a colorful chasm surrounded by vivid glimmers from different distances. To impress us more, a bridge hangs between our cliff and another one that leads to more natural paths. At a glance, it looks damaged and unstable. The ropes growl unsafe noises while the structures swing side to side.
