The ruler has sent an expedition of chroniclers to the Skalds of Gerudia to document all matters related to the Reavers' bloodlines. The lineages of the Reaver Kingdoms of Reveria, Deranne, and Pearlsedge have been carefully documented and brought back to court. Despite long debates, it was decided that we could lay claim to the lineages, given the tenuous connections. But why not invoke one of the oldest laws of the Reavers? Might makes right, and all Reaver Kingdoms except ours are gone. If Deranne and Pearlsedge cannot rebuff our forces, then they aren’t rightful heirs of the Reaver Kingdoms; if they can, then we are not a true Reaver Kingdom. Let us announce our claims, that all Cannor know that we are the Reaver Kingdom and that we seek to restore what is ours.
