While other races are squeamish about consuming the meat of humanoids, particularly their own kin, to we Kvangahga gnolls it is one of the most sacred of rites. It is a great honour to pass on the wisdom and strength of your flesh to your community. Sadly our allies do not see it the same way. A certain ally has sent an envoy to complain after witnesses saw our troops consuming the bodies of their fallen troops after a recent campaign. We have assured them this was meant as a sign of respect, but they continue to be outraged by this turn of events.
