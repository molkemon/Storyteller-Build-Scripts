This place is not a place of honor. No highly esteemed deed is commemorated here; nothing valued is here, at least so read the signs we have found plastered on every hold wall for the past month. Preliminary reports from the specialists indicate that this is likely the result of a radiation-induced mania caused by a magical energy leak in one of our reactors. Despite our assurances that the safety of our citizens is a secondary concern ranking just below Cannorian fish imports, it will take the research facility years to get through all of the local governing body's green tape. Unless, of course, we had one of their number in a position of power.
