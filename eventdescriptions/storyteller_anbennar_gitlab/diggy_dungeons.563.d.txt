Resting for a moment near a group of crumbling stone pillars, the expedition lets out a collective sigh of relief after escaping from Aldorlbûr again. Using the hole in the pipes the fire elemental created, they were able to slip around it as it circled them and once again flee from it practically unscathed. Though most are overjoyed, some can't help but wonder if Aldorlbûr is simply savouring the chase.
