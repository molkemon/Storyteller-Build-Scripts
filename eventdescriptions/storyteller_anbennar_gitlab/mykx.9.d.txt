Despite trying to make sure all the crimes our population commits are on the target of our raids or pillaging, it is not unheard of for our citizens to apply to each other, which cannot be ignored, unfortunately. Captains, Buccaneers, and our own state officials across the archipelago are demanding laws that regulate punishments for crimes on a national level. It is now up to the state to decide which proposed plans will be enacted. They all make sense; we can still use the criminal forced labor to enrich our lands, we can just maroon them, a classic sea punishment that would scare potential criminals and ties more to our sea heritage, or we can embrace the Cannorian model, of lashes for not-so-bad crimes and execution for capital punishment. Which punishment type will become the standard?
