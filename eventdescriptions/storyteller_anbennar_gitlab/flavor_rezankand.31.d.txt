Despite our efforts, the Oren Nayiru are many, and we are few. With our forces dedicated foremost to bringing peace and security to Taychend, the Oren Nayiru have the liberty to preach and spread their message, and twist our actions at every turn to discredit our ideals and frame themselves as the only viable movement for Taychend’s future. Recently, this has resulted in the attitudes of the general population turning towards supporting the Oren Nayiru, increasing their power. If we want to stem this, we will need to put the work in.
