While a number of archfey align themselves to either the Seelie and Unseelie Courts, not all do. From the kindly lord Blossom to the mysterious Emerald Lady, these "courtless" archfey and their agendas only add to the chaotic game of intrigue that plays out in the Feyrealm. Most of the time, the realm is largely free from the influence of these archfey. But sometimes, given the right time and place, they can attempt to invoke their aid as they would the Courts. Such rituals can be burdensome on their people and the fey around them, but the reward can be the subtle favor of one of the Courtless.
