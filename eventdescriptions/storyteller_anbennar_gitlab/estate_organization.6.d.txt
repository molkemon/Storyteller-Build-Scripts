Ravelians have always been entrenched in the art of artificery, and were even counted amongst the first artificers due to their origins in the Ravelian Society. Indeed, it was their beliefs in using artificery for the greater good and helping the common man that garnered the early faith its most devout followers. Now, with the official conversion of the country to the Ravelian faith, many believe it is now time to enforce the church's will on the many schools and guilds of the artisan estate.
