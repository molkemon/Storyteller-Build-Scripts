A spyglass bobbed amid the ocean swell, its leering lens always pointed at the country's shores. Some days, it peeped out for hours, watching. Watching. Watching. As locals dreaded the doubtlessly nefarious oculus’ intention, a daring fisher managed to sneak up on the scope, and after a fierce struggle, the wrangler claimed their prize. a merfolk clinging to the spyglass. Dragged to shore, local authorities pestered their netted prisoner for answers. Thoroughly rattled, the merfolk stammered through a thousand explanations. their aunt had bequeathed them the artifact, they promised to share what occurred on the shore with a sick sibling, the lands above the waves teemed with captivating vibrance. The local authorities mulled over the reasons, considering that it may well be as innocuous as the merfolk divulged. If they were gracious enough, they could even sponsor a one-of-a-kind tour to see their lands up close. Yet, what if this is all a front to snoop on them? Could they allow a caught spy to simply swim away freely?
