Towering onyx black walls, standing taller than ten men and thrice as wide, devour the light and hopes of those who would look upon Azkabar with avarice. For decades and centuries, the mighty obsidian walls of Azkabar have stood against mages, hellfire, and the dangers of a thousand calamities. Whilst for many years it has been believed that the secrets of their construction had been lost, the Akal, Idris szelAttalu holds a zealous belief that with enough searching, even this mystery may be uncovered.
