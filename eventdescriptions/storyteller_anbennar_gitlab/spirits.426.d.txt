After an extended tenure on the throne, during which the monarch had heard countless stories and learned of various events transpiring around the High Temple Complexes, the ruler found himself compelled to visit them. And so, accompanied by a select group of elite escorts, the monarch embarked on this excursion, led by a high priest who guided the ruler through the intricate passageways. The guide meticulously shared their knowledge of the complex and expounded on the numerous theories and practices associated with the temple. Yet, as the guide continued to recount these tales with unwavering diligence, the monarch's attention began to wane. The ruler's pace gradually slackened, and both the monarch and their retinue of guards inadvertently became entranced by the guiding priest. This distraction led the lord's gaze to fixate on a faint, yellowish hue in the air—resembling wisps of steam—that seemed to beckon the ruler forward. Drawn along this ethereal path, the monarch ventured into one of the many adjacent chambers. The ruler's senses seemed to dull as the monarch followed the path of the soft, golden fog, guiding the ruler to an empty chamber. Suddenly, the monarch was seized by a bolt of lightning, or perhaps some other inexplicable force. As the monarch scanned the surroundings and emerged from this trancelike state, the monarch found their recollection to be hazy, left only with the impression of a small, vacant room imbued with a golden radiance. Soon thereafter, the guards hastened to the monarch's side, swiftly ushering the ruler away and ensuring the ruler's wellbeing. Trained physicians attended to the ruler's needs in the wake of this enigmatic episode.
