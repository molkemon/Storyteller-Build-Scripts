It is done. Morgurax, the First Lich. The pain is truly terrible. The poison scours your bones and the ritual tears your spirit from its earthly vessel. Every inch of your body feels as though it is burning, freezing, and evaporating, all at once. But finally, the pain fades. And you are still standing. The next few days are quiet ones, spent assessing your new existence alone in your chambers. Your new body looks much like your old one, though you no longer feel the pangs of hunger or thirst, nor any other distracting mortal urge. Your flesh already hangs loosely from your bones due to the emaciation of your long cleansing. Given enough time, it will eventually slough away and leave only your polished skeleton behind. You will be a perfect being in body as well as in mind and soul.
