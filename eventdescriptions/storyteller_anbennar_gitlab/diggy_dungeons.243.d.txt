The trail ends by the base of a very large stone tree. Its roots are exposed, obstructing the doorway hidden behind them. There are strange scratches on the cave floor, and a mage suggests that some spell was used to move the roots aside as a sort of security measure. If we can figure out the spell, we can access the door behind. Of course, if we have some gunpowder left over, we can also just blow the roots and doorway to bits.
