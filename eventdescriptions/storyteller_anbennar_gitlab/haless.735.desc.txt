The House of Fire and Water in Luoyip is the most prestigious gathering of alchemists in the Yanshen Coast. It began as a collaboration between a few adventurers, ship doctors, and gunners from Ganggim who shared knowledge of medicines and weapons to assist explorers and border patrols. Over time, it grew into a hub for inventors, scholars, and engineers throughout the Yanshen Coast. Their arts focus on practical applications—healing wounds, wielding fire, navigating storms—and have become indispensable for seafarers, adventurers, militias, and trade caravans alike. Their works are famed for their effectiveness and reliability. Recently, the organizers of the House have submitted a petition requesting state funding for two goals. expanding their facilities for experiments and instruction, and publishing their work to attract more disciples from across the realm. Supporting them would foster further alchemical innovation and strengthen the nation's access to refined tools and medicines, crucial for long-distance expeditions.
