Whispers of discontent and treachery have twined their way through the empire. Even if untrue, the lie of such a thing can be just as insidious as the truth. It has come to the advisors even within their ivory towers, and for that reason the situation has become intolerable. The creation of a secret police, to ferret out these malcontents, no matter where they hide, is of the utmost importance. We shall gather a group of the most supremely talented and loyal people in the empire to make up this new apparatus that is answerable only to the supreme sovereign. Let nothing escape our Crystal Eye.
