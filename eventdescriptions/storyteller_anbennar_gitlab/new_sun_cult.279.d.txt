Most days, the only thing to fall on the land are the gentle rays of Surael’s bright sun. On occasion, there are rain showers, a soothing balm to the parched land. Today is nothing like those, not at all. Meteorites of all sizes rain from the sky; even the smallest move fast enough to bludgeon a man, the larger ones crush a chariot, or a house, with ease. The populace has generally retreated to caves and basements, and are powerless to do anything but watch as Amussu’s calamity brings devastation to their land.
