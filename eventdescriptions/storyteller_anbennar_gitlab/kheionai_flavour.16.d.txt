A question that began as a simple philosophical debate between two well-respected elves has begun to take on a life of its own. The debate has swept throughout our society and captured the minds of our most prestigious scholars. The scale of such a philosophical inquiry will surely be remembered for centuries to come. While there is a wealth of discourse being conducted high and low, one facet of the debate has played out in our own halls of government. Various philosophers attempt to court the favor of our administration, which they seek the prestige and patronage of. As the crowd ripples with philosophical fervor, surely one speaker stands out among the rest?
