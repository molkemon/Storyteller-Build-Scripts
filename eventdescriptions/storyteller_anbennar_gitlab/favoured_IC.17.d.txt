Today, we were visited by one of the lowest ranking members in the Marquis Ildran's cult. He did not bow nor scrape before us, as was typical of a man sworn to the Marquis of Pride, and seemed most unhappy to be in our presence. "I have been sent," he begins, unwillingly, "by my superiors." These few words are forced out through gritted teeth; he does not enjoy being at the bottom of the pile. How unfortunate. "They would like me to inform you that the Marquis has selected you to receive one of his boons." "Oh, indeed?" The man shifts uncomfortably. "Yes." "And how does he intend for this boon to be bestowed?" "He has placed his cult at your disposal." I should have liked a better boon.
