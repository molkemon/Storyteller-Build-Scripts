This outpost could hardly be called an outpost anymore. The imposing walls of the kelaktar rise over the land, foreboding and able to withstand great sieges from any who would seek to subjugate or destroy them. Workers on the farms surrounding the kelaktar know it will easily house them should any army come for them. Inside, the markets are filled with meats, horns, and hides from faroff hunts, and the blacksmiths are hard at work creating tools and weapons for the kelaktar. This land is secure and fortified, but the surrounding wilderness is wild and untamed. What shall become of it? Will they reestablish the great centaur tributary system of old, that once brought prosperity to the Kingdom of Maghargma? Or shall they send out their rough and hardened ogres to the frontier to bring settlement to these lands, and glory to this new kelaktar?
