Following a lost power struggle, a large group of political refugees from the ruling government has begun to seek refuge in our country. The conflict seems insignificant enough that the winning side in that realm isn't particularly interested in searching for their fleeing compatriots, but they may still appreciate it if we delivered the refugees to them. On the other hand, these are men and women of some ability, who could likely do a lot of good in our country if we allowed them to stay.
