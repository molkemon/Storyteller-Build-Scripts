A large congregation of scholars have arrived from a far away land called Jasiir Jadid. We have welcomed them into our country, as they seek to study our culture, people, and landscape. They have provided many of our civilians with advanced methods of care and medicine. They have constructed wells and cured illnesses. Some of their scholars have even performed grand feats of magic. As they come to our court, they speak of their faith. Knowledge Seekers. While the idea of all gods being dead and heaven being accessible through being learned is a strange concept, the scholars have done absolutely remarkable feats that haven’t been seen before. Some people have already converted, convinced of their beliefs. They haven’t forced anyone to join, nor have they acted as missionaries in the normal sense. Something about them carries a sort of weight. Should we convert to their beliefs?
