We do not know how it started, but for the last few days our poor heir has been possessed by an ancient ancestor. We know this because they told us so. The heir will occasionally yell out the ancestor’s name, but otherwise only speaks in tongues, cryptic riddles, and nonsensical prophecies. Our court mages and revered priests have done everything in their power but have been unable to exorcise this malevolent spirit. The heir grows more violent and uncontrollable every day, and since we cannot be rid of this mad ancestor, we unfortunately have no choice but to put poor heir out of misery.
