I honor Huszien with everything I do. It has been that way for years. That is how I made it here, why I am speaking to you all today. When I was born, I was into a High Philosophy family of glassmakers. We were pious people, always thinking the temples were for everyone's good. When news broke about what they were really there for, it shook me to my core. So I decided something needed to be done. My old studies were put to the torch, and then I enlisted in the military. Nobody trusted someone like me much. Even though I had put behind my roots as one of the spirit torturers, nobody seemed to acknowledge my piety. With the new modernizations, though, I have finally been able to have my position match my faith. You need to trust in Huszien. That I am here and speaking to you all today is proof that your dedication to Her will be well rewarded. All you need to do is follow the decrees given down to you by Her and Her maidens, and in due course your valor will lead you to a rank as high as mine, if not higher. Now, with that being said...
