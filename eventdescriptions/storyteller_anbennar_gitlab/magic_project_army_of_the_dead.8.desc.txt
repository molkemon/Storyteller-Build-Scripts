Conclusion of testing. After conversion, the zombie general proceeded to attempt to eat the brains of nearby sentients. We set it loose on a platoon of soldiers; the rank broke immediately on sight of first consumption. Great success. The skeleton general did not shatter their ranks, but lack of rotting flesh around its digits left it capable of wielding and firing muskets. Set loose on a platoon of soldiers; it was difficult to consistently hit from more than sixty paces, owing to its slim profile. Tactical dismantling of the entire platoon from afar ensued. Great success. After conversion, the revenant general stalked and attacked its killer before vanishing into thin air. Revenants appear unviable for use in warfare. The ghoul general was never converted. It is unclear what precisely a ghoul is or how it differs from a zombie. Many fruitless hours were spent in archives researching this topic.
