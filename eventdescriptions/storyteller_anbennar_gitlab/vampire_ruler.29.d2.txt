The recent growth of the capital has drawn people from across the nation to find their futures here. Among them have been many unsavoury sorts, sadly, besmirching the name of the realm and the honour of the monarch. As long as they kept their predation to unimportant sorts, however, they were not worth the attention it would take to bring them to heel. But that has changed tonight. Apparently, a cloaked figure wandering the alleyways well past midnight appears to many as an unimportant sort, and a trio of thugs—adorned in peculiar red masks—have just sauntered up to the monarch as the hunt proceeded apace. The tallest of the lot gestures with his frozen steak at the ruler, demanding all their gold, while the largest seems ready to grapple. What shall the monarch do?
