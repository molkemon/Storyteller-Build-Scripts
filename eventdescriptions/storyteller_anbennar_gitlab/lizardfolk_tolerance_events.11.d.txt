A small group of welloff lizardfolk craftsmen have approached our treasury with a proposal for mutual investment in a public bathhouse complex in that province. A traditional sight in lizardfolk lands, these pools are a site of informal commerce, lobbying, and even interstate diplomacy. The craftsmen claim such a complex would attract significant custom, even from nonlizardfolk. As investor, the state could reap the benefits of such investment and show interest in the lizardfolk community’s wellbeing at the same time.
