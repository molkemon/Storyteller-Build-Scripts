One of their head researchers, a dragonpriest and artificer, has gone rogue. He insists that they lack one of the most notable draconic traits, that of greed, and that if they are ever to shed their status as "kobolds" rather than "dragons," they must also hoard for themselves. To achieve this, he has stolen mass quantities of artificed research and money. He is constantly experimenting on himself, siphoning massive resources towards himself to achieve ever greater rewards. He insists that his research, on turning himself into the greatest, most powerful kobold alive, will allow them all to follow his steps, but many are unsure. Can they really trust the self-proclaimed "Prophet of Greed" to help them in the end?
