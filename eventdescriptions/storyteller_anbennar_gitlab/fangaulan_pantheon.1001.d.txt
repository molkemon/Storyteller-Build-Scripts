Gather round, gather round, and be quiet. Hear the tale of Aro Makaya. half-genie, rebel, father of empires, dethroner of genies, and the greatest hero ever born. Though Aro ascended the holy mountain of Sirik long ago to watch us from the lunar realm of all ancestors, his tales and wonders live on in performances just like this. Perhaps you would be keen to recall Aro’s Great Wonders, constructed by the very genies he overthrew and imprisoned? Or maybe I should delight you with Aro’s Ten Tasks of Royalty; travails meted out by God to measure Aro’s worthiness as a stalwart ruler? Now, which tale shall this humble jelli spin?
