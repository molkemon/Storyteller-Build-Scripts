A thick smog of incense filled the room where the Court of Change resided. At its centre, laid out on an array of cushions, resided Akhaxpa. As the ruler approached her and knelt down, she opened her three eyes, filling the room with a blinding blue light. “Does one not wish to learn new skills? Do not emulate those who tell you to practice a one kick a thousand times, for one will simply learn to parry it. Learn a thousand kicks and they will never parry you.”
