The construction techniques of this realm have served us well in the past, but before we did not have construction workers that could alter their bodies to make sure the work gets done. Thanks to the hard work of our artificers, we have managed to build new frames for our machines engaged in construction. Even the most difficult construction tasks can be accomplished with an efficiency that was previously unheard of with machines specialized to each individual task of construction, from mixing cement to carrying heavy loads to lifting building materials up large distances. And once a task is done, we can simply refit the machine engaged in that part of the task to do something else, and continue on just as efficiently as before. As our nation expands onto a new continent, these construction bots will help us build up civilization here quicker than we ever could at home.
