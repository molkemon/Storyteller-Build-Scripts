When good men and women are more devoted to the good of the nation than to their own interests, great things happen. For months, the regent has taken a lot of time to share their wisdom with the heir, and both have enjoyed this time greatly. The preparation to be a ruler is never easy, but the monarch has done a great work. Lessons about sciences, etiquette, history, and military tactics have been going up and down the palace, following the regent and the heir wherever they went.
