Centuries ago, the Goddess Kodave saved the Metsamic people from an encroaching centaur horde through an act of divine intervention. A wave, tall enough to blot out the sun, rose up and washed away the horde just as the protoMetsamics crossed into Yarikhoi. Many hone the art of war to protect Kodave's waters, just as they were protected so many years ago. Peacetime drills in the local grove leave much to be desired for young warriors, however, and new blood wish to whet their blades on something a bit more animated. Needless to say, having this many bloodthirsty warriors around is not the greatest recipe for peacetime prosperity; as such, the priesthood is mulling a few possible solutions.
