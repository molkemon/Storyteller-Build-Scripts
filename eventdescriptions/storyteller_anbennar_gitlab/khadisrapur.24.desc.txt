After decades of intermingling between the humans of Rahen and the slave orks from The Command, a new people is beginning to rise across Haless. These are the halforks, people who inherited the physical might of their orkish side and the adaptability of their human side. But because of their upbringing, halforks mainly take mercenary and adventuring jobs even though their traits make them excellent workers. But over time, such traits are overshadowed by their new nickname – “The Casteless” – thanks to the adventuring halforks who mainly operate outside the caste boundaries, their novelty making them difficult to integrate into traditional caste. It is a group which naturally expands, containing all the exceptions, which only spreads the nickname more across Haless.
