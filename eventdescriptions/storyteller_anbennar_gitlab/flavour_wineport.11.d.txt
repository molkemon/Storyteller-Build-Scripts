When the monarch had suggested the idea of Imperial membership, they had perhaps expected the process of organizing and presenting the formal request to take a bit longer. A group of Wine Barons, already trading in the Empire of Anbennar via Toarnaire, have come with one prepared and ready to go to the Emperor once the monarch requests it, if they do. The request comes with a prewritten letter and a fine panoply of gifts to represent the benefits to the Emperor should they join. This is, unsurprisingly, mostly wine; the crown jewel amongst these is a bottle of an excellent Roilsardi Red vintage. The merchant delegation presenting the petition are all familiar with the Imperial Court as well, ensuring that such a presentation of the request will go over smoothly. It is now simply up to the monarch if they wish to go ahead with the petition, or whether the League of Winebay should retain its total independence.
