A bastard. It is always a bastard, isn't it? The ruler of a well known elector had a child out of wedlock and sent it to a distant land out of shame. We have learned of their true lineage after one of our spies gleaned it from a close retainer of theirs too deep in their cups. Our spies are able to foment blackmail on this lousy retainer and put pressure on their liege if we need to. Or, we could alert the ruler of what we found and let them deal with their friend while we reap the diplomatic benefits. What will we do?
