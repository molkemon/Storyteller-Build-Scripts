The merfolk are the strongest ally of the Baashidi people. Over the course of a millennium, they have saved us from countless wars and periods of violence. All Baashidi are in the debt of the merfolk, and yet they refuse to ever take count. I know you understand this well, my liege. Thus, the merfolk have been provided with seats of respect and honor in courts across the realms of Jasiir Jadid. The only issue with that is the fact that it is extremely hard at times to provide the merfolk with a comfortable environment to observe. Most cities have general infrastructure for the merfolk, allowing them to swim through canals and enter buildings through the waters. Unfortunately, the Grand Hall is currently in need of repairs when it comes to Merfolk accessibility. With many debates coming along, many are calling to exhaust more resources in an effort to allow the merfolk to witness the rest of the Conferences. Sire, I believe that if we help the merfolk into the conference, it would be a great boon to our standing amongst the realms of Jasiir Jadid.
