Our expedition finds itself in a cavern of truly staggering scale. Despite the immensity of the cave, what catches the eye is not the high shadows of the cavern roof above, but a colossal wall of woven spiderweb spanning the entirety of the cave. After some hasty experimentation, it appears that the silken barrier is strong enough to repel most of our attempts to cut a way through. Using a combination of magic and enchanted weapons, we were able to, after much effort, make the beginnings of an opening, but some of our explorers believe that a well-stoked flame could provide a quicker way through the wall.
