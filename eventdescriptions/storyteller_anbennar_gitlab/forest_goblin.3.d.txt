The kartels operating in our country have one love above all else. money. Normally, this is a great arrangement for us, with us getting a slice of the profits. Unfortunately, some of our goblins' greeds seem to have run too far, and a timber kartel in our country has expanded their operations in a reckless manner. Everyone in the Deepwoods knows that the forest is dangerous. The first lesson we all learn as a child is not to stray too far from the path, or the woods will eat you whole. So when trees start being cut further and further into the Feywild, attention is drawn. In our case, it seems to have drawn the attention of a treant, who unsurprisingly isn't too happy with our logging operations in the area. Unless we send aid to the angry treant's province, our production in the area will struggle until the treant moves on.
