The first step is taken, finally. Ruin Bringer is no more. Their clan is to be destroyed and subsumed into the Great Storm, and the shamans are already underway to enact that exact order. The shores of the Oritsukh sea are free from the Filustkhuga for the first time in around twenty years. There was much work to be done, but the chieftain was tired. He sat there, contemplative on the shores of the sea, watching as the little wooden contraptions of the lake humans sailed by in a safe distance. "Father." "Yes, ordsikar of the Great Storm?" "Are you certain that you will grant the clan of Burning Frost limited autonomy?" "That was the plan. Have them administrate the northern parts of the nation while we focus on our conquests in the southern parts of the Nuzurbokh." "They will undermine my authority once you are no longer the ruler." "Do not worry, Tholnuvalk. I’ve talked with them and the succession should be clear. They know that Negeal will be instated once the conquest is done." There came no further reply of the chieftain's heir. He simply galloped away upon hearing the confirmation. "He is getting impatient, isn't he," thought the old centaur to himself.
