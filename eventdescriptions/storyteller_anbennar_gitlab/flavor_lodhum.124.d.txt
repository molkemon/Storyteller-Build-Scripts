After several complaints by some guilds about incomplete or lowgrade metal shipments, we have found that some of our merchants have been selling large amounts of our highgrade metals without authorization. Moon Elven merchants who delivered tea would offer high prices for our best mining yields, which they then smuggle out in the empty tea containers. As we are making preparations to roll up the smuggling ring, however, some advisors warn us that doing so might disrupt the extremely valuable and delicious tea trade.
