The formation of the Ravelian Faith has had much impact on the Ravelian societies in Breluslad. Many Brelari have converted to the new religion; however, many are also concerned about the new hierarchical structure the Faith now presents, so different to the freedom-seeking intellectualism the societies once followed. The matter of adopting the Ravelian faith on a state level has been brewing among the Councilmen of Breluslad, especially those in areas with large numbers of Ravelian converts. Now people are clamoring for the monarch to make a statement on the matter.
