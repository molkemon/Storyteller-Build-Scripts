Time passes and people change. Stories pass through the ages, yes, but sometimes threats that were once tangible and real are delegated to distant stories, then even further into myths and fables. The cities have grown, the people have changed, and the new generations just have no time to listen to the stories of an old house on the outskirts of town, sitting all by its lonesome, staring down at a domain that once but now no longer cares. They are, after all, just stories.
