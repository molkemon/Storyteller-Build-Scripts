The journey back was as uneventful as setting out. Maybe even more so, without the excitement of a possible whale hunt to look forward to. With little else to do, the monarch thinks back to the conversations they had with the carpenters on the expedition. They had their own allotments of cleaner whale oil they burned to conduct urgent repairs and work even in the darkest of nights; something that the monarch thought would be very useful indeed, after some further processing and refinement.
