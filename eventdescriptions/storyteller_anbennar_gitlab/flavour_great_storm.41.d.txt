As our nation is enveloped in the struggle in Irdülhain, we have received messages from beyond the gate. Not by the Khaashalgi, no, but by centaurs. Left behind by the Storm King’s incursion, hardened by the desert that envelopes that land and stalwart in the face of evil. They have heard of our war and know about the necessity of our success. The Great Storm must descend on Wild Hunt, and their warriors will help us do so.
