Once Insyaa is out, you will have accomplished much by this point. You will have taken over the entire Charkuchin range and met all the kobolds there. You will have interrogated them, asked them questions, and integrated them into Kobildzan. You will have found out that none of them worship dragons. We already covered this, sure, but this will just be an extension of that. Here, the old Zurzumex guys, the Thoughtful, will be moving to your Insyaan colonies. They will be mixing with the Charkuchins and celebrating them. The victories of the Charkuchins will be fully integrated into the pantheon, and Zaamalot, monstrous, basic, and singleminded, will be shed in favor of the righteous and dynamic Charkuchin Kobolds. This event would be so cool if Insyaa was out, but it is not yet. So, for now, you get this. Sorry.
