The Silmuna have been wounded time and time again by traitors, and with the rise of Rogieria, we will have our due. The realm was informed in dead of night by a clandestine messenger that another country has decided to cut ties with the Shadowmoon Conspiracy, and in the aftermath, those old scars burn once more. This treachery is not on the level of the Wesdamerian or Wexonard betrayals, of course, but it is disappointing that the ruler of that country sees the failings of the current emperor and refuses to do anything about it. But a pebble cannot stop a mighty river, and an army is not harmed by the loss of a single sword. The Shadowmoon Conspiracy remains strong, and our destiny remains laid out before us.
