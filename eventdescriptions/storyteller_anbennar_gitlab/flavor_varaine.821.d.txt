Success. After successfully hunting down a drake and experiencing its ferocious elemental breath, our researchers were able to complete their research on the functioning of a dragon's fire breath. Extrapolating from that, after some trial and error, we were able to develop potions which allow the consumer to spew forth fire like mighty dragons. There are still some issues to fix, such as the instability and unpredictability of the fire spouts causing undue collateral damage, but with time we will be able to perfect this potion.
