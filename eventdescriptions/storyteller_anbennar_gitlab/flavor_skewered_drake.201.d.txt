As the monarch had arrived at the bustling arena, they were glad to find the most skilled of Drakon Riders already demonstrating their prowess. Spectators of every culture and race under the dominion were present, all cheering on for their favourites, with many of the vassal rulers paying close attention. Impressed by the display of bloodshed and skill, an imposing warrior has requested an audience with the monarch. Renowned in the subject territories, he was unheard of by the Drakon Riders, yet they bowed deeply and offered their service to Drakonshan. Presenting their list of accomplishments and feats, they have felt that aiding the nation directly would be more suitable for their skill, to strengthen the forces of Drakonshan with strategies not used by the orks before. It seems that not only the masses have something to gain from this day, but also the commanding officers.
