This may be Amilak's domain, but many of his rivers have their source in Yrilak's lands. Because of this, occasionally something modeled by Yrilak's dark influences will flow down the various rivers and wash up on our banks. monstrous fish, pitchblack fallen trees, or grayish dirt colder than ice. Most of the time such objects are mere oddities, but the influence of Yrilak is nevertheless felt far downstream from his lands.
