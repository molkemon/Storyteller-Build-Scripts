Today, we witness a miracle. The Ynnic Empire, the greatest nation in the world, which pulled itself apart until it was no more an empire. But today that damage is undone, for the Ynn is one, and an Empire again. The monarch has been bathed and crowned in a holy ceremony and, from the height of their balcony at the capital castle, they have announced a month of festivity while cheered on by a huge crowd.
