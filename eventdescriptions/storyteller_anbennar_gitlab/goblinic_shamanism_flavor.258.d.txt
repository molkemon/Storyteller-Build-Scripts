When all material things are stripped away, and the usually selfish desires of goblins are turned towards the pursuits of battle, goblins are much more willing to share. When the warrior you fight beside is armed with the finest iron, you can kill twice as many in your fight for glory. When all the riders and their mounts in your charge are wellfed and strong, the bloodshed is increased a hundredfold. The recent freedom with which goods flow between our battlesibling's hands has led to a few unexpected trade benefits, all the more money to spend on war.
