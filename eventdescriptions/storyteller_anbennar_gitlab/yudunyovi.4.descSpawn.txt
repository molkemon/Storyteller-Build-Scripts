The worldly gods have spread amongst the people of the plains like a fire through dry grass. It is not just some cultural revival movement, either; the centaurs too are converting in droves. These people see this new faith as a return to a time of glory and splendor, a time of glorious shining light. Among these gods are familiar faces, though in unrecognizable forms; Enuuk and Kodave are absent, yet Yukel remains. Even Irdaeos is worshipped now, under the name Shirdos. Before all these gods were worshipped as individuals, but now the people of the steppe worship them all together as a unified whole; the Yudunyovi pantheon has been reborn.
