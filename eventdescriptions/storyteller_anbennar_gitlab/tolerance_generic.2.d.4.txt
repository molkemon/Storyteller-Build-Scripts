He did not want to do this. He believed himself a good, honest man; he despised people that harmed other beings. But he could not stand living all his life in fear. These were monsters, violent and unpredictable like wild predators. Even the state said as much. It was kill or be killed. And he refused to go quietly. Horror. My Liege, the peasants of that province have assembled a mob and gone around attacking one of the minority races. Hundreds lie dead; how could such a thing happen? These fanatics… this is why we need to keep the plebs down; if a small hint that other races might not be perfect is enough to turn them all into assassins, they cannot be trusted with anything.
