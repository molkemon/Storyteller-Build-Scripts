Not all lived so long as those with Elvish blood, which was a blessing so many sought. Yet it was so easy for us to imagine how such a lifespan could end up quite fraught. As we built up ties with the elves of Cannor, so many had tales they wished to share, and they traveled down to Lelanàn seeking help with their deep despair. For an elf who loved another, their lifespan could become a curse as they watched their companions die. What fate could be any worse? So we carefully recorded their stories, helped them preserve the love they had lost, and as they shared their past with others, that cold frost melted from their hearts.
