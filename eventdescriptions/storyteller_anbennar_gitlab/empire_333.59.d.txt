The lizardfolk have fought with many people. It can be difficult for them to tell when one war ends and another begins. The tension, which had been high before the question, dissipated quickly as it seemed instead the lizardfolk would get a typically rambly answer from the Thraxis. That is, until it spoke again. "But they were different." They did not stop. They developed... we can scarcely call them weapons. Tools of extermination. We don’t understand what happened, for before that the two sides were friendly. We did not see it all, for we dared not test what they could not kill. The crowd was deathly silent. This was not a tone anyone had ever heard the Thraxis use before. The voices it spoke with usually echoed around each other in a disconcerting cacophony, but this time each voice spoke as one in a deafening tone. "We are unsure if we have a beginning. Before meeting those elves, we believed we also did not have an end. After seeing what they did though, we changed our mind. If anything could end eternity, it would have been them. You have seen the evidence in what they did in Aelantier. All we can say about that is it was almost much, much worse."
