As you walk around the palace garden in the capital, now flourishing with the help of the hands of your new friend, they ask, "For who is the better gardener?" You laugh softly, and give them a wry smile as you answer, "You are, of course." The new techniques brought from Bulwar, and the way they have carefully nurtured some of the land’s more delicate plants, makes that clear. Then you meet the eyes of your friend, and they have no humor in them at all. They are as serious as the day they met you in your palace. They shake their head, and gesture toward the sky. They ask again, "Who is the better gardener, the one who makes the rain or the one who is the light?"
