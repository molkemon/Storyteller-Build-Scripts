The conquest of the dam of Bosancovac is completed. The time has finally come for the first raid. The fleet has been preparing to ensure this one is successful. Their crews are bolstered by Ruinborn Ynnics who know these waters well. Their boats now sail closer together so that the soldiers aboard them can support one another. With these strategies in place, along with a dozen more precautions, the raid appears to be going splendidly. As the raiders pillage the various estuaries before continuing southwards, they find themselves blocked by the Vels Bacar dam. The fleet turns around and takes a different route, north. Many more days of raiding go by, and eventually, another dam is reached, the Mocbarja dam. With all of their opportunities exhausted, the fleet decides to head back to Bosancovac. This raid was a resounding success; some ruinborn settlements were too well fortified to plunder, and some beasts too fierce to kill, but in the end, the Reavers returned victorious, for they got information. And besides a lot of gold, that is the most important trophy for a raider.
