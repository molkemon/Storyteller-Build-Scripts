The messenger runs desperately into the council hall of the Kheinsí, waving his arms and bringing word from the estate of the prospective tyrant. He collapses to his knees, out of breath from the run. His message reverberates throughout the chamber. Our spies sent to infiltrate the man’s home were apprehended outside of his villa. The tyrant has taken this as a chance to act prematurely, declaring his civil rights to be violated by our illegitimate regime. He now amasses his supporters across the realm, ordering them to march against our armies. We must act quickly to prevent their success, hasty as it may be.
