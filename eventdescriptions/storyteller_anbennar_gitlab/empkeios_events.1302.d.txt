An educated slave by the name of Rileyan'd Lanahasha seems to have become the hottest topic amongst the slave population, both in transit and working in Empkeios. She seems to have gained quite a following, and her ideas of emancipation and blatant and unhinged criticism to our hypocritical values and governing system have gained her some prestige here and abroad. She seems to be quite skilled at avoiding our efforts to find her, for no matter how hard every administration and state in our nation has tried to capture her, she has always escaped without trace. Now, there is a growing concern that she may become influential enough to rally people behind her in an all-out uprising, especially if she manages international support.
