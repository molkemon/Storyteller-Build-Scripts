What I thought of the dreamer? It is hard to say really. Her world is unlike ours. Even more so than the rest of the feyrealm in many respects. Those small winged creatures that fluttered through the air there were delightful, that much I can say for sure. I felt in many respects as if I was both asleep and awake at the same time; many of my memories of that journey are a haze of color and sound. If not for the spells we wove, I wonder if I would have made it to her at all. I am glad we did, for that was a sight I shall remember for all my life. What a titanic being, glittering as emerald. I did not hear the words you exchanged with her, captivated as I was by that sight, but I do not mind.
