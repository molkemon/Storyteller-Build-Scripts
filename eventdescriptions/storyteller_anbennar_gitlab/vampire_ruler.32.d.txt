Cleaning the late ruler's blood off its scythe blade, the reaper takes one last look around the room. It looks towards a slain servant's corpse with a sorrowful gaze and slowly, serenely, drifts towards that mangled form. It lays down its scythe, cradles the body. And then both disappear. What remains of the monarch will be found in the morning, but by then it will be far too late. None can escape death.
