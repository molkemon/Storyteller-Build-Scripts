The ruler is meeting with the ruler of Zena aboard one of their floating cities. He has made the long journey to their shores in the hope of negotiating a stronger trade partnership with them. “Our partnership with your people is beyond profitable. Your wood, fish, gold, sugar, and cocoa are exchanged for our paper, cloves, silk and wool to our mutual profit. More and more of our merchant princes visit the main trading platform in the capital every year, and the trade of goods and ideas flows freely between our two realms. To strengthen this connection, let us negotiate a treaty to bind us even closer together. There are others arriving on your shores from across Halcann who we know greedily covet your lands, and will not respect your ways or the Effelai the way we do. If you are amenable to this, we will protect you from these invaders, and both our nations will continue to profit.” What should we reply to this unprecedented offer?
