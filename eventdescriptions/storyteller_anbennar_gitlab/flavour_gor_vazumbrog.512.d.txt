As fortune would have it, the limping and bedraggled company's attendant scholar had survived to see the treasure room, and immediately launched into an explanation of what they had found on their arrival. "The Lost Songs of Karradûn are a collection of hauntingly beautiful melodies inscribed on runeetched crystal tablets, designed to resonate through specially crafted dwarven instruments. These songs are the last remaining fragments of the fabled dwarven bard Karradûn's repertoire, a master composer said to have woven the essence of emotion and memory into music. Though the songs have different themes, most were intended to unite and uplift. Each song is stored on a thin, shimmering crystal tablet, engraved with runes of deep complexity. The runes shift subtly when viewed, giving the unsettling impression that the songs are alive." "But are they useful?"
