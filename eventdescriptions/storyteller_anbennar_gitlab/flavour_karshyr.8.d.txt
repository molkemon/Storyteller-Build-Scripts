To say that we have come far in the last centuries would be an understatement; our island of once squabbling tribes has become the uncontested hegemon of Fahvanosy, and our cloves have made us wealthy beyond compare, barring our patrons in Karshyr. And now those patrons who have brought us to where we are request that we convert to the Ravelian church alongside them, though they of course insist we are in no way obliged to.
