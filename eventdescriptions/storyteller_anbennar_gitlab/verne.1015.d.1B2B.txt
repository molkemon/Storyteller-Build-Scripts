They were foolish, my Vernman guys, and I was foolish thinking they could work their way through that place. They would have been better just charging through as a unit, working off instinct; instead they got picked off by traps one by one, trapped in sensory deprivation rooms screaming themselves hoarse, confused by illusions of beautiful gardens, led right into gas traps. Or they would have misjudged some weird gravity loop and crushed themselves against walls. In the end, it was just me and two other guys, and I am rubbing my head thinking how the hell we are going to get out of here. At times like that, you just have to do it with the hangover. They sent me out here all too happy with the prospect of my violent death, that much was obvious.
