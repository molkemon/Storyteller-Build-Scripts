The Nymkorai and Kattyva both have extensive interests in the countryside. While the Nymkorai work with local merchants and warlords in order to expand their trade empire, the Kattyva often find themselves barreling through them in their quest for relics. Recently, the Kattyva sacked the petty city-state of a minor warlord, who fought the family over their excavation of a slave warren beneath his lands. This has incensed the Nymkorai, who had captured said warlord in a protection racket of their own. Now, the Nymkorai seek to right this wrong. The Nymkorai are advantaged in this particular struggle, and if we do nothing they seem likely to win.
