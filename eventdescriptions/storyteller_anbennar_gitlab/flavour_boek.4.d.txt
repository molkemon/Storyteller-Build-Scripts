We were foolish to believe this power would serve us. For many days something has been wrong with the ruler. Their eyes darken, their flesh grows cold to the touch, their hunger seemed insatiable. Many did not concern themselves, for perhaps this was simply the price to pay for power? On the seventh day after their encounter with the great aspect, the ruler completed their transformation and, after consuming everyone in the palace, fled. While their whereabouts are currently unconfirmed, it is rumoured that they still roam the kingdom, striking down any who they meet.
