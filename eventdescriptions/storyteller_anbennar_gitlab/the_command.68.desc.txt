Our state has until now been a confederation of commands, each enjoying a great deal of autonomy and control over their own lands, that is held together by the Grand Marshal and the shared Ninyu Kikun. This system has been functional so far, yet the expansion of the state has made it more difficult for this apparatus to govern our vast territories. This is primarily brought on by the fact that our military and civil administration are one and the same. It is for this reason that we will need a reorganization of our nation and administration to secure its future. Henceforth civil and military administration will be separated, with the Ninyu Kikun leading the way. Its structure will be mirrored into a military and civil branch, and each will deal with its own matters, of course keeping a militarized structure for familiarity and efficiency. Each command will similarly be split, with its civil branch led by a Murasesho or "Marshalregent," who presides over the Command's Seshokozen, or "regent generals," who rule the smallest territorial subdivisions known as Kikushi.
