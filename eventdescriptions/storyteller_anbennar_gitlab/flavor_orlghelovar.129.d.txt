Sapphire Glass, so named for the blue tint that is a byproduct of its manufacture, has eliminated many of the drawbacks of glass. The saltpeter bath makes it harder than steel, and the increased strength allows them to manufacture it at thicknesses that afford it incredible flexibility. Their factories are producing glass of incredible quality, with methods that remain a trade secret of national importance. The age of dwarven reclamation will be synonymous with the age of glass.
