As the divine war rages on above us, mortal Crusaders are taking it upon themselves to wage a war of their own. They say, "We must fight on Halann as Corin fights in the sky," and through their struggle, they will see her victorious. A particularly fervent band of Crusaders has named one of our neighbors as deeply sympathetic to Adean's cause, and demanded that the ruler rectify this with force, or they will take matters into their own hands.
