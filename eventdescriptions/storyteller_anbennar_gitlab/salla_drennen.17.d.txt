What I thought of the King of Darkness? There is no sensation quite like standing in his presence. I never saw him, if there is even a him to see, but I felt him. Along my spine, as a shiver of fear. A prick in my neck, a warning of something behind me. In my mind, as a smooth caress. It was terrifying, and I do not use that word lightly. I am surprised Elethen even agreed to come. You remember how we had to console her after. Despite that, there was something beautiful about it. Despite the pain, the harsh barked words of his command, there is an elegance about him. I see why they do it, those who swear fealty to him. Perhaps if I did not already have a place, I would have been tempted too.
