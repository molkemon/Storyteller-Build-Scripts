The untrained mind may wonder what connecting threads exist between the countless musical arts of the Endless Ocean. After all, just as the rhythm of the waves drums a myriad of beats against the shore, so too do the feet of all Insyaa step to different patterns. However, spend enough evenings in observation, and one will begin to notice similarities. These performances are often conducted at the boundaries of dusk, in the presence of water, and incorporate fire in the act, be that as an element of the show or as a centerpiece for the stage, all traits deliberately chosen for their symbolic value. These commonalities invoke transience. just as day and night pursue one another, so too will fires spark and cool. Song, unlike sculpture, is a fleeting art, and water changes so rapidly you may never know the same drop twice in your life. Even our brief existence in this world is but a flash, like the reflection of a torch against the tossing surf. By surrounding the dancers in like concepts, the Dance of Life aims to reaffirm its performer’s position as another moving piece in the great turning of the world.
