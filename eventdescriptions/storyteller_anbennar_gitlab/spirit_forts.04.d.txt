Our recent conquest of the realm has led to an unforeseen consequence. As soon as our troops triumphantly entered the city gates, the ancestral spirits tirelessly guarding the Spirit Fortress laid down their weapons and lost their corporeal presence, a clear sign of their repudiation of our right to these lands. We shall have to expend significant efforts to regain their goodwill and demonstrate the righteousness of our rule if we hope to reawaken the ghostly inhabitants of the Spirit Fortress from their current state of ethereal slumber.
