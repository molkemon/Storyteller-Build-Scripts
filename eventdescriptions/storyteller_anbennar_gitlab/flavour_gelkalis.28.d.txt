For the first time in history, the Gelkar kingdoms of Elusadul, Lawaššar, Gelkalis, and Arkašul are united, but not under a Queen’s wing or a Serpentking’s spell, but under the blessed ruler of the Gelkar. For too long petty rivalries have divided the kingdoms of the Gelkar, putting them under control of foreign powers, but that era has ended. Now the hills are united under the Gelkar, and no one can contest its might in this new unconquerable land. Today, the era of the four kingdoms ends, and the era of Kelharssara begins. May Surakel’s Light always shine on the hills of the Land of the Gelkar.
