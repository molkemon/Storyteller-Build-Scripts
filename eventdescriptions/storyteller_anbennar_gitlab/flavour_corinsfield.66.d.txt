A problem has been festering in the countryside, and it appears to have come to a head. Two of the largest landowners of the countryside have been accusing each other of witchery for months now. One man claims the other withered his crops, and in response the other claims he poisoned his cattle. Both have brought their raging concerns to the inquisitors, who have found no true evidence of either claim. However, the foul roots of magic run deep; it is not impossible for the inquisitors to have missed something.
