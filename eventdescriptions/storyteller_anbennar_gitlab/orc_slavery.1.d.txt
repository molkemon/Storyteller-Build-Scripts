After the Greentide, many orks were captured and sold into slavery by both enterprising adventurers and fledgling orkish states to the civilized realms of Cannor, with cities like Vertesk profiting greatly. By the Age of Unraveling, orkish slaves were the staple amongst the colonial empires of Aelantier, filling its plantations with expendable and cheap manpower. Our colonial governors report that the province has built up a sizeable orkish minority because of this.
