The tournament known as the Blackpurgis is the primary way the domain ensures the ruler is the legitimately crowned greatest mage of the realm. It is, under the Black Doctrine, a sacred rite that determines who is farthest along in the path to Godhood and who is most worthy of veneration. It begins with the well-attended Beastly Duel and Necromantic Duel, before the crowd clears out for the truly sacred portion. the Personal Duel, in which the Acolytes try to kill one another. Perhaps it is time to hold such a glorious contest?
