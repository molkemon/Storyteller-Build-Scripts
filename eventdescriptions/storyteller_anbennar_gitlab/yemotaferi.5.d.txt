Dire news has reached the council. the hags refuse to accept their defeat. Though we have seized the Shadowroot Matriarch from them, along with the heartlands of the trollish horde, the Coven still has resources and lackeys, which they are now calling on. All around the swamp, in realms like Rakkaz, Rombeni, and Simegasa, shadow cultists are crawling out of the woodwork. A dark coalition is taking shape, and the hags are taking personal command of legions of trolls, gnolls, and humans. A tide of evil is about to come crashing down on us, threatening to undo our righteous efforts.
