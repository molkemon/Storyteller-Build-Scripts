It can be hard to care about the sky when you live beneath the waves, but it seems that the cavalcade of lights above is striking enough to have made a splash in merfolk society. Our subaquatic friends have given us a significant monetary gift, the only condition being that it be used as funding for the Godswatch. This generous offering has gone a great way towards strengthening the bonds of trust between our two peoples even further. Some of our advisors think it is a bit fishy that it is all in liquid assets, however.
