Have you heard of the Halfling Priestess’ ultimatum? It is said that the High Priestess of the Sisterhood of Lélanan, and by that point essentially the leader of all Fahvanosy resistance, walked with her retinue right into the halls of the great headquarters of the D.T.C. in Fahvanosy, the residence of the governor in the city of Teramzadai. Now, do remember that this procession was made up of halflings, so it's not as if this should have made a very imposing sight. The Bard shot an apologetic glance toward the halflings among the patrons. And yet I hear it was. The High Priestess, clad in her elaborate attire, through a stately posture and a truly serene face, managed to emit an air of authority that towered above all else. And so, she put forth an ultimatum. The Derannics were struggling in their attempt to hold fast to the isles, and the priestess promised worse if her demands were not heard. The Derannics were to keep all jurisdiction over local trade, but Fahvanosy was to be divided into Halfling states, with considerable autonomy, their governing bodies, laws, and armies, and so on.
