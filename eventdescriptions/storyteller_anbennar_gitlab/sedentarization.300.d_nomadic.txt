This outpost could hardly be called an outpost anymore. Every day, more people of all sorts of backgrounds and even from beyond our borders arrive here at the frontier, seeking the freedom that the steppes bring. Some arrive having sold the last of their possessions to acquire the horses they rode in on. It will be a harsh life, and a rough one, but these people all have the grit to make it worthwhile, and the free spirits to thrive. And this base of operations will give them all the supplies they need to begin new lives in the lands beyond.
