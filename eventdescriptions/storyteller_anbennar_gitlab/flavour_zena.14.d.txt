Azaly arrived in Amadia, and what he found was far more fascinating than mere hydrotechnical advances. Translucent, pale blue, or pale pink opals that refract the light are found in abundance in Amadia, a kind of Dames tear, but more. Prismatic Dames tear it is called, which could be used as a source of Wild Magic. It is thought to have been created after absorbing the energies from the mages who protected themselves from the Day of Ashen Skies. Its brilliant colors and potential research applications have left Azaly eager for a sample, although the Amadians are anxious about the potential dangers of giving it away to outsiders who may not be aware of the risk, and even more anxious about drawing additional attention to their homeland's riches, already coveted by far too many.
