It is rare for serious relationships to develop between members of different families. When it does occur, the Ursibadur, freespirited as they are, are generally involved. Except in times of exceptional hostility, families will often let these relationships slide; but when said relationship ends with a drunken fight, and said fight ends with an iron quarrel embedded in someone's chest, things get serious very quickly. The Ursibadur family claim that their woman had been wildly hallucinating before the incident occurred; that she had been spiked with absurd amounts of White Lelivazha and the whole thing was a stitchup. The Vustarin claim intentional assassination, that the Ursibadur woman enticed their man in with her HouseofVicewiles and then killed him in cold blood. The dispute has been getting quite serious, and if we do not intervene quickly it may escalate further.
