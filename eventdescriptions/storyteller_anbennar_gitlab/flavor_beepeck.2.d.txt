It appears that tensions in the northwest have finally come to a head. A neighboring power has come to us, pressing their claim to the province of Orston, which is currently legally owned by another sovereign. They say that as the elector representing halflings within the empire, they have a right to the halfling land. While to do so would support the power's important role as one of the racial electors, to take land away from another sovereign prince may not go over well with the broader commons of the Empire. Many princes will doubtless reject the power's claims to legitimate grievance, even setting aside those who will worry about future moves from the Dwarven or Elven electors to follow in the power's footsteps. It seems there is no easy answer, but heavy is the head that wears the crown. As emperor, the decision is ours to make.
