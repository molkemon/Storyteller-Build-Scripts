The Great Library of Aqatbar has many books, and will be a great addition to the collection in Mermigán. After searching through Aqatbar, they found the library and have begun the process of transporting the prize back to Mermigán, adding many noteworthy works to their own libraries. These include the six tomes of the art of bibliothecography, by Packlord Zheh the Scholar; a copy of the Ešlimšitum, detailing the expedition of ten thousand Akalšesi into Kheterata; the Geneology of the kings of Kuzaram, by Attalus the genealogist; the complete works of Burak Kazaš; and a recent work, Living Among the Harpies, by the Mašnsih Erbay szalSerim.
