It was dawn when they arrived once more at our borders. The eyes of the court looked upon the newest letter, reporting on even more such interlopers passing by our forts and into our lands. Yet they were here not to raid, but to migrate. For years we have heard tales of adventurers from faraway lands marching into the lands of the Veykodans, and yet to our surprise these foreign colonialists managed to actually push the native Veykodans out, destabilizing the locals enough for them to seek greener pastures. our pastures. Many have already entered into our lands, and soon we will have to deal with the issues of this migration. Some of the court have already nervously whispered the possibility of the Buycev deciding to rise up and take over. Others dismissed the idea that they, in their disorganized state, could ever do such a thing.
