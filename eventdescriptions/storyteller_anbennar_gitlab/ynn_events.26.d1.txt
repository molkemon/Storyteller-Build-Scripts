It is done. We have crushed the crazed mob of the Dragon Cult, and slashed our way through the streets of the last stand province, reaching the palace of deviancy where the Dragon Prince was being raised and revered. Our knights and warriors disarmed the enchanted gates and entered the palace's garden, exchanging magical blows of fire and water with the Dragon Cult's last defenders. The fighting continued on the inside, as we fought room by room, until every inhabitant in the building lay dead. Once the dust settled, our mages determined that the Dragon Prince was dead, and presented us with the body of a teenaged boy in noble clothes, none other than the creature itself having taken inhabitant form.
