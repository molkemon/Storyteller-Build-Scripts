REPORT. Continued reliance on the Voltoj has created a concurrent issue for the state. While our personnel needs have expanded significantly, the supply of available Voltoj has not, at least not to an acceptable degree. A lack of conflict has resulted in a decline in orphaned Voltoj, which have thus far formed a significant part of the bureaucratic workforce. In order to rectify this issue, this office presents two proposals, to be initiated simultaneously. FIRST. Recruitment to the academies shall be made open to all Voltoj, including those who still have living families. SECOND. Recruitment to the academies shall be made open to all orphans, regardless of race, and not simply Voltoj. Through these measures, it is believed the administrative deficit will be dealt with indefinitely.
