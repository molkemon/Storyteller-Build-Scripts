The answer to what the naga have been doing has been answered emphatically. gathering support for a popular revolt. Anzalkatsa zealots, demanding the dissolution of Duwarkani rule over Taneyas so that the prophecy of the three hundred thirty-third Empire will return, have risen up in Zerat. Each mob led by a naga has vowed to not stand down until the naga have been returned to their rightful place as guides of the lizardfolk and stamped out faith in Nirakhet.
