It seems that one of the so-called Ynnic Overlords is taking measures to reform their religion. As we understand it, the religion revolves around an obsession with the river they take their name from. Now, they are debating the Cannorians and what we adventurers mean for their little religion. This could be a chance for us to influence their religion to be more favorable to our way of life. We should tell them of the error of their ways and what we have to offer should they listen.
