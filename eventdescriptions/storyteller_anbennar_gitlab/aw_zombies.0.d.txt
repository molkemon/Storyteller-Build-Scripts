Animated by dark magics, devoid of thought, zombies are the rotting remains of those who once lived, given a single malevolent purpose. to kill. Recent reports from the realm indicate a small but growing number of these undead abominations haunt the land, killing those who cross their path, the victims rising to become zombies after their demise. The origins of this blight are uncertain. perhaps a vile necromancer lost control of their servants, or an ancient curse has been released. Regardless, the dark corners of the realm will be unsafe until the creatures are dealt with. While the remedies of Garion the Wise can be used to prevent further infection, this will become a problem if left to spiral out of control.
