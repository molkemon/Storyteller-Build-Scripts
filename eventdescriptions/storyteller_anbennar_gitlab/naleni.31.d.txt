A commander in the city guard, looking thoroughly honored to be here, holds a report for the Queen. It details a recent spree of fights breaking out before, during, and after siren performances. Seemingly the shows are ever more increasingly popular, and access has become such an issue that people will literally fight over spaces in line. Harpies of course simply fly in. This is particularly true of the religious services, due to the fervor of some of the faithful.
