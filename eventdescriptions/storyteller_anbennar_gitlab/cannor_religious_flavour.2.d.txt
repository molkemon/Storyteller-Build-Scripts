As she climbed the stairs of the makeshift stage, the seemingly endless sea of eyes nearly brought her to her knees. Fighting through the weakness coursing through her legs, she maintained her practiced stride, graceful yet purposeful. This was the moment. This was the Beauty Celebration of Divine Ryala, her chance to prove her virtue to the world. The rest of the day passed in a blur. The performance. The waiting. The selection of the Chosen Women of the Temple. And now she lived her new life. She had been deemed worthy of a place amongst the ranks of the Ryalan Temple. One of several women each year that were deemed worthy to serve both her people and her faith. Women who won this honor did not always stay behind the temple gates; some rose to even greater heights.
