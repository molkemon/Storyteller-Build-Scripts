While paying a visit to the Silmuna School of the Arts in Anbenncóst, the monarch notices a group of strange creatures standing in a dimly lit corner of the library. It is difficult to see them clearly, but the monarch can swear their skin has a soft metallic glow. “Ah, my ruler, the monarch hears the voice of the Head Librarian behind them. It seems you have taken an interest in our new students. I assume you have never seen a mechanim before?” The librarian beckons the ruler to follow and starts walking towards the group. The mechanim look like they are arguing, but the monarch cannot hear a single sound. However, as the monarch approaches, they stop immediately and turn to the librarian. “Let me introduce them. This is Ebbed Mist, a scholar from Insyaa. I am not quite sure what exactly their area of expertise is, but I seem to recall something about foresight and population patterns.” A thin mechanim bows, instantly reminding the monarch of one of their court advisors. His orange robe is tattered from what looks like close encounters with a thousand blades. “This one is BellChains. It was them who managed to safely navigate the treacherous titanoflora jungle and lead these mechanim to safety.” The ruler looks at the large mechanim, which barely grunts a response. A military man, not unlike the generals in the realm's army, interrupts the last one. “And my name is PrimePaleFear. I am just a simple merchant that tagged along, so I fear I will be of no use to you. My companions, however, are very capable and I am sure their services will prove invaluable in making whatever your plans might be come to fruition.”
