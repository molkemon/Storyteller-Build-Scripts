In the Necropolis, many souls wander the walls of this ancient fortress. However, a unique spirit strolls there today. An architect, who escaped from the hold of Grônstunad and migrated to the surface to Bim Lau, rests here. Known back when he was alive for his talents as a builder, this soul has contributed quite a lot to the walls of Bim Lau. However, this soul has never been able to rest due to longing to return to its native home. When we found him, he explained his story, and we brought him back to Verkal Ozovar to share his talents, where they would be appreciated.
