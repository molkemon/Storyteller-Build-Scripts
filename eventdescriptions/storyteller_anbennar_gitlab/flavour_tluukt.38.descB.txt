To our east lay only endless tracks of sand and ruins, a worthless wasteland of no consequence to the ambitions of the ruler. Yet, a power is growing now in the east that can no longer be ignored. Our nominal ally Zokka has grown far too mighty far too quickly, subjugating all of the Far Salahad to his ambitions. While he was embroiled in conflict with the Salahedesi nomads he was a worthwhile pawn, but now he has grown too ambitious for our purposes. Worse, he threatens to burn all of Bulwar in senseless violence and bloodshed, destroying it all in order to satisfy his own pride. If we are to subjugate the Bulwari, it is clear that we will need to subjugate Zokka's pack first.
