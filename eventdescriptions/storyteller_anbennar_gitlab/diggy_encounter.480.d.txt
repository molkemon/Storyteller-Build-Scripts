Our expedition ventured into the depths ready for all sorts of monsters and abominations. What we did not expect, however, was to find the cute white furry balls of fluff known as the Bnunin. Distant relatives of common surfacedwelling bunnies, these large rabbits have adapted to underground life in several ways, but remain supremely cute, especially when compared to the average life form in these caves. When confronted with a herd of these creatures, then, we couldn't suppress the urge to take them home and cuddle. I mean, use their adaptations to cave life for our benefit, yes. And while the creatures seem frightened with our presence, they do seem docile enough for us to get into their good graces.
