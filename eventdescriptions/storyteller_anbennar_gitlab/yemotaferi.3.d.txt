In a gray clearing, beneath a grey canopy, three groups of warriors met. Beyond the black treeline lurked the dark shadows of the swamp, but the gathering itself was illuminated by a number of torches, radiating white light. The Deathsworn of Yemotaferi and the shapeshifters of the Violent Hearted Goose and Friend Helping Parrot tribes prepared to speak oaths that would bind them in a shared struggle against the Coven of Yezel Mora. From this day on, all would fight as one against the evil that plagued their homes. One thing was not yet decided. the question of Tanizu command. The Yemotaferi captain nodded appreciatively at the leader of each of the tribes. But in the end, he would choose one to shake hands with, endorsing their leadership over all Tanizu.
