Scholars hailing from Bulwar and beyond have long recorded Dimuzi’s title as Dimuzi the Half-Immortal due to being the son of a Precursor Elf and a Karqašlu prince. Yet to those who live in Kumarkand, Dimuzi is anything but Half-Immortal; he is immortal. It is because of Dimuzi that the city of Karqašlu, now Panu Karqašlu, stood long after the Karqašlu empire collapsed. It is he who fended off gnollish and harpy raids during the Age of Monsters. It is he who passed away after dealing the killing blow on the demon Nikšaru. And it is because of his Mišari heritage that Kumarkand is a safe haven for half-elves who are frequently discriminated against in Bulwar. Dimuzi’s armour and weapons are already in Kumarkand's possession; however, with the Akals recent creation of a relic collection initiative, talks about finding relics belonging to Dimuzi have increased. Many aspiring adventurers who grew up idolizing Dimuzi now scour the countryside in search of their hero's legacy.
