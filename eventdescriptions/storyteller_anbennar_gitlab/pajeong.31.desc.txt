To the south, the people in our holdings in Yanshen still practice the teachings of the Accretive Path, desiring to refine their chi at the expense of the spirits and their ancestors. To the west, the conquered Zunk Kuulgo still wear the masks of the Three Brothers of the Saanorgegh faith and practice the binding of their ancestors' spirits instead of venerating them as we should. The Neoksamaji demand that we put a stop to this. They desire to send missionaries and guards in order to ensure that the spirits are given the deserved reverence, and bring the heathens into the Neokttareum faith. They encourage us to even make public our disapproval to other nations who do not follow our ways.
