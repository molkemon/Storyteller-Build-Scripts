A colossal catfish, adorned with shimmering white scales, extends a length surpassing even the combined dimensions of the realm's mightiest war vessels. The sun breaks over the river, and a pearlescent rainbow erupts through the water, as its guardian stretches his glistening splendor from bank to bank. Initially, the land's tremors and turbulent waters evoked fear among the river's residents. But now, with eyes wide in awe, they eagerly approach the riverbanks to welcome the magnificent creature. Carrying festooned banners and symbols of jubilation, they greet their cherished protector's return. The objections of the philosophers fall on deaf ears; for the magnificent catfish blesses them with bounties of its offspring and the river's treasures. Both the humble and the proud scurry onto boats, escaping the grandeur of its magnificent presence.
