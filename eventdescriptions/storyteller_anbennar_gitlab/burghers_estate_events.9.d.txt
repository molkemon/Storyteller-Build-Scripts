Economic development is not always without conflict. It seems rich merchants in some of our domain cities have been attempting to bypass various guild privileges by outsourcing work to the homes of families living outside the city. The fact that this 'Puttingout System' has been a very profitable strategy only further enflames the anger of the artisans in the city, who feel their ancient rights have been trampled and that the carefully managed prices of their products will suffer. The local artisans now turn to the ruler to defend their privileges as the city council has been unable to root out competition from outside the city.
