My liege, news just came in from the Grand Hall in Abeesooyinka. Many civilians have been complaining about the overcrowding of the city due to the Grand Hall and its scholars sprawling around the city. It doesn’t help that caravans have been arriving into the city en masse. Thus, saaxir and scholars are being sent to other major cities nearby until this situation is resolved. Many of the people coming to our territory happen to be of Dhebiji descent. The Dhebiji have been particularly brutal against us Ashamadi. Their history of violence and excursions is great, and even in the modern day, certain groups continue to perpetrate violence against Ashamadi peoples. Some people amongst our court seem nervous about harboring Dhebiji in our lands. Even if it is just a possibility, some wish to not take any chances at all. What should we do, sire?
