The slaughter was swift, and the spoils wonderful. The serpent was flayed with due haste, with its meat mixed into our food stores. All seemed right in the world, until our men began dropping like flies. One, two, then a dozen more. Panic ensued, until the cause became known to all. Nagas possess acidic blood, deadly to ingest. Worse than that, it appears as though a large portion of the food stock has been contaminated.
