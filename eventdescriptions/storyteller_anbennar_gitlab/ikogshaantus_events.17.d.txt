There has been no guild so successful in the history of Ikögshaantus as the Geomancer’s Sanadazjal. Capable of constructing an entire complex of workshops in a day, these highly capable and pious hill trolls are the result of years of Sanadazjal investment into Kashlaalg. But of late there has been some unrest among the Geomancers. They tire of the menial work of consecration and wish to embark on a greater project. The guild has come to us for advice; what should we tell them?
