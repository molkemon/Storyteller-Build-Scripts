The Zaradyrra faith of the Lizardfolk makes a religion of art; but the definition of art can be flexible indeed. Of late, one particularly skilled young Zaradyrra butcher has begun to make a show of her craft, slaughtering and carving up fowl and larger livestock in an elegant display of knifemanship. The elite disparage this as a disgusting display, not fit for the court, but we could show a lot of understanding of Zaradyrra custom by recognising this young butcher’s skill and talent. Some have gone as far as to recommend we invite her to perform before the monarch.
