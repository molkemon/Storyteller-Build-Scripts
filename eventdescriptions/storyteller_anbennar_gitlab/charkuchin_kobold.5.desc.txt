It would appear that the hinuibirds have found a new and innovative way to make our lives miserable. A hinuibird separated from its flock a few days ago, and crashed right into the peak of the mountain where a settlement is located. Usually we would celebrate the existence of one less hinuibird in the world, but in this case the massive impact of the hinuibird crash destabilized the mountain itself, causing a landslide which has devastated the settlement worse than ten hinuibird attacks would have. In fact, much of the infrastructure of the settlement has collapsed, and our people are buried under the wreckage. Relief is desperately needed, but the question is if we can afford to send it.
