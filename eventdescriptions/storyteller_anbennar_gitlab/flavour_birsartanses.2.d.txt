Since the fall of Aqatbar to the goblins and the following massacre that decimated the Eletarzuir, our relatives and the rulers of Baharkand, Deggarion, the surviving bastard son of Kaltan the Third, fled to AzkaEvran and ruled the city since. Instead of returning the crown of Baharkand to its rightful heir, our Queen Keladora the First, cousin of Kaltan the Third, the people of AzkaEvran proclaimed the bastard their Akal. This stain on our honor could not be allowed to continue. Now the city has fallen under our control and needs to be chastised for their impunity. It shall be plundered, and its treasures, including the spearhead of legendary Dinatoldir, taken to our ancestral capital instead.
