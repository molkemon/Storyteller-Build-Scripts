No amount of warfare and conquest could have prepared you for the terrible intimacy of your battle with the Lord of Beasts. Here, there are no walls to protect you, no armies to do your bidding, no diplomats to negotiate, and no peace. You circle around the clearing opposite your foe, slowly drawing nearer, weapon flicking out like a serpent to keep deadly claws at arm's length. Even after ambushing the Lord of Beasts and inflicting a grievous wound, he fights with all the ferocity of a wolverine, pushing you beyond the limit of your martial ability. Neither of you can be perfectly successful, and blood drips from your ragged forms as blade and claw alike find their mark little by little, ever searching for a chance to kill at last.
