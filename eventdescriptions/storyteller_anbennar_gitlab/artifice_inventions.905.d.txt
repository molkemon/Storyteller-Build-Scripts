Scarce millennia ago, we were tigers, wandering the jungle of Rahen and hunting with tooth and claw. Then, following in Harimar's footsteps, we became philosopher kings, pursuing wisdom and power together. Through these mighty meldings of clockwork and steel, our muscles and claws can now be supplemented by the unconquerable might of machines, jumping enemy lines in a single bound, and ripping through even the strongest armor with barely a flick of a wrist. We can now revisit our oldest ways and use them to bring ruin to any battlefield.
