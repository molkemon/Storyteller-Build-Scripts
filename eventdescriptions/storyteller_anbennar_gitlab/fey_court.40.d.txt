The janitorial staff look on in horror at the scene that unfolds in front of you. Your throne room has become a raging tornado of singing, dancing, and nonsensical fey games. Pixies zip between the branches of an awakened Treant, and some nearby Boggles have left their underground burrow for a new one inside of a chocolate cake. Confetti and garbage litter the ground, and a silk, violet banner hanging over your throne reads. "Thank you, the monarch." Exactly what the fey are thanking you for is never explained. But as a Giant Moth hatchling perches atop your head, it is clear that you have endeared yourself to the local fey.
