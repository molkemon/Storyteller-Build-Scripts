The ancient fort cities of Ananoma have been a great burden to our land since time immemorial. We could tear down their remnants and reclaim these natural harbors for useful construction and commerce. This would cause dissent among our halfling population, but our engineers believe the task is logistically simple. Further, several historians and adventurers eagerly advocate for such a demolition as an opportunity to explore the deepest, unlooted chambers of Ananoma in search of hidden treasures and artifacts of great value.
