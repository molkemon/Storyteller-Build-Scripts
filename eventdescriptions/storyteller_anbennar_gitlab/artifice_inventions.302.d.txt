The body is a material, and it can be changed and molded just like any other resource. Through reverse engineering Precursor gene experiments in Insyaa, we have learned how to shape flesh, bone, and muscle however we wish. and with these advancements, we can become everything we have always wanted to be. In the depths of the Dwarovar, kobolds transform themselves into dragons in all but name. In the swamps of Sarhal, trolls graft weapons and armor onto their very skin. In the palaces of Rahen, Harimari enhance their bodies to be more like their ancestors. In every part of the world, artificers begin transforming the body itself into a weapon of war. We are now part of this process. Biology is ours to control.
