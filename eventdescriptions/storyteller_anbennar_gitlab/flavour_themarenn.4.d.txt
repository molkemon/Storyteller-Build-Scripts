Isobel the First, one of the most powerful diviners of our time, is an old lady. While her contributions to the stability of the region have been invaluable, eventually she will need an heir. Having foreseen the future, she is afraid that the vultures in Telgeir and Terr who fought her realm during the fourth lilac war might use this opportunity to usurp her realm. During his childhood, Vernell the Third spent years in Gabmoríonn while his parents were busy fighting in the war, away from home. Under the tutelage of Lady Isobel, he made friends with other orphans that she raised and learned about Esmari traditions, literature, and history. Now he is in a position to return the favor by protecting his ward's legacy. Isobel herself has expressed interest in naming one of her adopted sons as the heir, a talented young man named Arodan, one of Vernell's best friends growing up. On the other hand, the nobles of the realm suggest Vernell himself approach Isobel and convince her that he can lead both duchies and protect her people.
