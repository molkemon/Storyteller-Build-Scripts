Step. Lunge. Block. Step. Cut. Halftwirl. Slash. Balgar is still using the motions he learned from his father, though he has picked up some of the improvisation necessary when a black ork's tusks aim to gouge out your throat. Battle, ambush, slaughter; the days and weeks and months have already begun to blur together, and Balgar has lost count of the number of deaths added to orkrend's toll. He can't help but shudder when he thinks that this was his father's life, the sum total of it, for decades.
