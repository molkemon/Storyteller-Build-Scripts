One day the wingmaidens of Vængheim traveled to the battleroost of the monarch, and they carried with them crates of papers upon which baleful words were writ. For these pamphlets spoke of the rise of Elkaesal, and the restoration of balance to the world, and bid the Reachmen rise against their queen and make a great noise, to rouse Elkaesal from her sleep and save their homes.
