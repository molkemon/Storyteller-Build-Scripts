A dozen crates of Rahen's finest spices. Furniture carved from the Lupulan's greatest trees. Art, calligraphy, poems, and literature enough to fill a great noble's library. And this is only the beginning of what we have prepared for our expedition to Cannor. Not a piece fails to reflect the glory of the realm, and undoubtedly they will ensure the Ghost Emperor's name even on the other side of the world. As the caravan sets out from the capital, the monarch thinks they can see a new chapter beginning for the nation.
