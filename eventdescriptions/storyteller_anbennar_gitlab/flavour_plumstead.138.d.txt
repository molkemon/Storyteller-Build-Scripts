"Run." They could barely get away when the cart exploded. For some reason, a fuse on a gunpowder cart had suddenly gone alight, and its men barely made it out alive. Fearing sabotage, the party halted to investigate. They searched the wreckage and found the supposed perpetrator. a small crate with glasses. These had probably reflected sunlight upon an exposed fuse, causing it to slowly heat and then go ablaze. A freak accident, though a good lesson for the party who continued to inspect the other wagons to prevent such an accident from occurring once more.
