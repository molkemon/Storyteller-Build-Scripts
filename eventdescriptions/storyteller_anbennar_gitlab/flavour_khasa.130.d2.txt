Recently, an emissary has reached our court from the newly formed Akasi nation of Khasa. Bearing gifts of Shakhat and spices, this diplomat has humbly requested our acknowledgement of their rightful claim to the ancient province of AurKesAkasik, which we have inherited from our Deshaki vassals across the Divenhal. While this territory is helpful in securing our trade ports along the coast, which play a vital role in maintaining our influence over trade in the western Divenhal, it may be more valuable to ingratiate ourselves with the growing kingdom, especially if they continue to be successful. Having a powerful ally guarding our interests in Sarhal could be an unmitigated boon.
