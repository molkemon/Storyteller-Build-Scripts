The status of the adventurers does not rest on the privileges of the nobles or the church or the wealth of the burghers. Instead, they have had to make their own fortunes by bravery and fighting skill. As a rule, however, this has gone very well for them lately. While not as prestigious, business is in many ways the backbone of our economy, and through their control over the means of production, the adventurers have managed to increase their power over our country.
