Good news. We have managed to infiltrate the administration of one of our rivals in order to redirect a portion of the funds they have provided to the Godswatch. While the money has still reached the same destination, our agents have modified some paperwork in order to funnel it through our private estates in such a way that it now looks like the donation is ours. This will not affect the observatory’s actual funding, but it will give us much more sway when it comes to choosing its location. Things could not have gone better.
