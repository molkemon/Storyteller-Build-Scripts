The dining hall's festivities are interrupted by a sudden knocking. A human enters the already open door and confidently introduces himself as Walbert With, "the best alchemist you'll ever see north of the Cliffs". Then another human, raggedly dressed and with thuggish manners, reluctantly steps into the hall and is introduced as Iessíl Pírkmóine, younger companion of Walbert With and "trading associate" of his. Some in our court sneer at the unlikely contrast between the alchemist's grim seriousness and the trader's lowly ways, and our guards ready their weapons as precaution. Normally we wouldn’t even consider inviting such individuals anywhere near our monarch, however our currently employed Natural Scientist has gone to great lengths to sing the praises of Walbert With and vouch for him. Unfortunately for our Natural Scientist, no one else in the court has heard of "Walbert With" before. Once the sneering subsided, the Natural Scientist nervously explained that they has brought these two here because they believe Master With to be very knowledgeable when it comes to alchemy and wishes to learn the trade themselves. "Master With" then piped up, as if someone asked, and said that he refuses to work in the capital without his companion Pírkmóine, who he assures us is as renowned.
