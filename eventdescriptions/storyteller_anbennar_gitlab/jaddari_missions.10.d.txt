It has been many seasons since our expedition marched into the depths of the Dwarovar. Now finally they have returned. The sound of heavy bootfalls echoing through the halls initially brought panic to our miners, however soon it became clear that these were the enlightened steps of the Jadd expedition. Their victory over the darkness has rewarded them with many great treasures and even greater experience. After a wash and a well deserved rest, they have been received by the monarch to tell the ruler about their adventures in the deep dark. Bards are already telling their tales and the priests have pronounced their great service a true honour to Surael.
