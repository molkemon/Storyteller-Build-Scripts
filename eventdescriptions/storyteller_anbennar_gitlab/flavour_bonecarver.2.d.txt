Bal Vroren, the most far northern Castanorian Citadel, built by the god Balgar himself to withstand the might of the biggest and strongest races on Halann, the giants and their kin, played a crucial role in the Giant’s War a thousand years ago. For months, it was put to the test again, not only by strength this time, but by our determination and devotion as well. Even through freezing nights, disease, hunger, and wild trolls, we persevered knowing our cause was just. At dawnbreak, the siege proved too much for the gray orks family who hid within. Food was running out, wood was running out, and diseases were running rampant with no medicine nor physicians left to treat them. And so, we proved our will to be stronger, to be the ones who were favored by Dookan in the end after all as the white flag was raised.
