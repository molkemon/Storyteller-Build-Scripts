A most peculiar visitor has come to the capital. The hooded character has revealed themselves to be the bastard of a sun elf with a Bulwari mother and seeks to offer services as an advisor to our state in exchange for refuge. The halfelf eloquently explains that their elven parent's secret has come to light and they are no longer welcome back home. While many in our court are not keen on allowing a halfelf to roam the court as they wish, some more openminded courtiers find the halfelf to be quite competent in matters of state and plead with the monarch to allow them to stay.
