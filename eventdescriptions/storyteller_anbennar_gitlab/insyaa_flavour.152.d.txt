An insectoid-inspired form of locomotion, common among machines who interact frequently with Loa'set. A chassis, inspired by insectoid bodyplans, is installed onto the lower half of the body. Eight legs, each made of small segments connected to ball joints for dexterity, are connected to socket ports on the aforementioned thorax, which is designed to minimize body space for maneuverability. All of the aforementioned design elements come together to make a very durable and versatile chassis, which allows the warriors to traverse many types of terrain more efficiently and decreases accident risk to the user.
