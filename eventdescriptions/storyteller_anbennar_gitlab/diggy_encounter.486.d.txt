Our expedition finds itself nearing a geothermal hotspot, with a few pools of nigh boiling hot water, heated by some volcanic vent in the depths. However, despite the water being hot enough to scald our explorers from mere proximity, from the pools emerged a large, black turtle, its shell an almost translucent dark purple and covered in spines. This specimen, known as the Obsidian Shellback, is a known but elusive denizen of the deep, so our surprise is great when more and more of these creatures are visible just beneath the surface of the scalding pool. It has been noted from the few documented encounters with these creatures that, despite their elusiveness, they are actually quite sociable, and that their shells are extremely resistant to most forms of damage. We could try to make use of our fortuitous encounter to attempt to tame a few of these creatures.
