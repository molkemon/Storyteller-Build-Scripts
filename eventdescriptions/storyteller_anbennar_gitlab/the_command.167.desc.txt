From the warden at the high temple province. Grand Marshal, we are requesting military support from at least three—no, four divisions. Our walls were torn open this morning by blasts of great force. A prisoner named Suresh had apparently maimed his own leg to get the Korashi shackle free. Then he dissolved a camp guard and stole her key ring, using it to free the other prisoners. The rest of our guards are presently engaged in pitched battle with the unchained Shamans, but our supply of Korashi is low and the Oni have not responded to requests for aid. Please Grand Marshal, the fighting here is desperate. Many of these prisoners wield abilities our guards have never imagined before. One man pressed together only his hands and invoked the power of hell itself. everyone around him was inverted. Not simply the guards, but other prisoners as well. I retched. There are some prisoners who have escaped in the fighting, some of them surely powerful Shamans. If they make it to the borders, it is likely that they will join the ranks of the enemy. Additional note from the Wolf Marshal. Grand Marshal, I suggest delegation of responsibilities on the management of Penitents; these issues will only continue to arise as time passes. Also, I propose either a girdle or beltlike design for Korashi chains going forward, clasped around parts that are not so easily removed.
