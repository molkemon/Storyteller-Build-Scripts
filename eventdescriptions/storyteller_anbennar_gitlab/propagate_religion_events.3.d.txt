From the Uelos' Lament to the Gulf of Rahen there is an abundance of ships, from small cravels to great galleys, constantly sailing between the many ports. Taken together, this trade network provides goods and supplies for more than half of the world's population. What many of these galleys have in common is that they are manned by sailors following the Ravelian faith. These are sailors from Cannor joined by converted locals.
