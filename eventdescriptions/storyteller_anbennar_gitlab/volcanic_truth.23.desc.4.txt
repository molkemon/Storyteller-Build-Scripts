The matter of the Kiamoa is decided. The lands beyond the Kiamoa may be great and powerful, but they have a weakness of which they know not. The visions of the magmascryers tell us that the lands beyond know nothing of the Volcanic Truth, and this is intolerable. Imagine what we could achieve with the marvels of these foreign lands. Imagine the grand change we could enact in Insyaa. Imagine how we could bring change to the lands beyond the Kiamoa, if only they knew of the Volcanic Truth. We will not hoard our visions to ourselves. We will go beyond the Kiamoa, and we will bring the Volcanic Truth there; with words or with swords. The Volcanic Truth teaches that nothing is constant. Even the mighty Kiamoa was destined to fade one day, and that day has arrived. Now that our faith has decided on the proper path forward, we can begin to move towards a new world, with all its mysteries and terrors.
