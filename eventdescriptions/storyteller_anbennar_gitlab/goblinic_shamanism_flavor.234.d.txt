The goblins of the Gerudian Cult love loud music. One could even say that they love it a little too much for their own good; at a recent cult gathering, their raucous instruments and voices grew so powerful that a human skald in attendance fearfully demanded that they tell them to tone their worship down. Making a habit of such loud concerts, he claims, runs the risk of undoing the work his order has done to keep the White Dragon asleep.
