The Dragonflame Legion stands at attention in Legion Square, every individual immaculate in their parade dress and every piece of equipment polished to perfection. The insignia of the Legion, a silver dragon breathing fire, stands crisp from the otherwise perfect whites of their uniform, with larger versions of that mighty dragon flapping in the wind atop the poles held by the standardbearers. The Dragonflame Legion was the very first of Castanor, led by the legendary Castan the Progenitor himself. It only fell a millennium past its formation during the Dragonwake in four hundred seventy AA, burned to a man on a last ditch attack against Zaamalot the Black as the dragon conducted its campaign of terror and fire across Inner Castanor. Only time will tell if the newly refounded Legion will live up to its name.
