The joyous news spreads through the realm borne on the backs of messengers from the palace. Lady Saetae has given birth. Both she and the child are healthy and strong. Celebration fills the streets of towns across the state as the people delight in the birth of Hiderion and Mya's son. For the couple, the joy is more private. Normally so eloquent, Hiderion finds himself unable to speak as he holds his son, golden tears rolling down his face. Mya gives him a tired smile. "What shall we name him, my love?"
