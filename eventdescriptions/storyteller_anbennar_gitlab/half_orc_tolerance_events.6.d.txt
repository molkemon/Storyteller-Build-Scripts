One of our councilors, traveling through a specific province, has stumbled upon a peculiar practice among its halforkish population. Apparently passed along from the orkish side of their ancestry, the halforks were meting out law and justice in what they called 'clan court' hearings to deal with conflicts in their community. It may be detrimental to let them continue their brand of justice that exists outside of the purview of our lawmakers, but at the same time, the practice is limited within their own tightknit communities and does not seem to be harming anyone outside of it.
