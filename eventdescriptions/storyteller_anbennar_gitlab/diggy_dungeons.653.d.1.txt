Still disoriented and exhausted from fleeing, you enter what appears to be a smithy in front of you. You see several arrays of smaller metallurgy workshops lined up perfectly with all kinds of tools used for turning raw materials into the intricate wonders of art you saw throughout the workshop. As you rummage through the first stations, your guards notice noises that quickly close in on your group. Barely managing to alert the rest of your men of the danger, they are soon struck down by a small, but well-equipped mob of Goblins.
