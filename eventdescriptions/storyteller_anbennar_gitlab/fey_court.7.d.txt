The King of Darkness expects his subjects to hone themselves like a proper blade. Each subject is to grind against struggle and emerge sharper than before, stronger, deadlier. In time, those with potential are granted the chance to enter the Gauntlet, an ever-changing labyrinthine entity that straddles our plane and the Feyrealm. A grueling place, those who overcome the Gauntlet leave it more than they ever were. While the Unseelie fey must earn their passage, it is possible for us mortals to gain access given the right rituals and favors. Doing so will enable us to better train our soldiers and leave them an image pleasing to the King himself.
