It is normal for Taychendi rulers to model their reigns off those of past rulers, so that they have both a guideline for which policies to pursue in order to gain their own place in the heavens, and so that they can patronize a solid selection of rulers to help them do so. However, Mihaela is a vampire, possessing immortality, so she will never see the gates of Dhivumanhu. Instead, her religious duty lies in her role of facilitating the apotheosis of her nonvampiric subjects. To that end, the style of her rule, and therefore the gods she patronizes, encourages her subjects to follow that same path to apotheosis. Naturally, switching up the style of her rule would be advantageous in dictating the direction of the empire.
