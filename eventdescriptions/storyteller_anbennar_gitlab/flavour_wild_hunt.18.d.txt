Our lord has been discovered in his tent with his throat cut. There does not appear to have been signs of struggle, indicating that the lord perhaps knew his assailant. His guards have begun compiling a list of suspects, but the issue is that there are simply so many people who might have wanted him dead. The neighboring clans might have wanted to kill him to further fracture our control of our borderlands, so that their clans might switch fealties. The Triunics could have sent an agent to further destabilize one of their hated rivals. It could have been one of our own lordsikar, whose grumbling has grown even louder and more irritating in recent times. It might be shorter to instruct the guard to compile a list of all who did not want the lord dead than those who did. Regrettably, this probably will not occur to the new lord, who is little more than a puppet of the lordsikar and seems likely to prove himself even more incompetent than his predecessor.
