The hallucinogenic beverage known as venombrew has been produced by the goblins of the spiderlands since time immemorial. A valuable commodity to trade with other goblin tribes, it has become even more valued in modern times by those goblins who spend their time making strange art or devices. This is why it is so fortunate our artisans have produced a surplus of it this year. What should be done with it, oh clever ruler?
