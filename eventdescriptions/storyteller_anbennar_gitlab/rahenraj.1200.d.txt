The ruler has sent his ministerial inspectors to every local domain and province to inspect their local ministries and make sure that every piece of the bureaucracy works according to protocol and remains loyal to the Lotus Court. These inspections are routine, and yet we must watch ourselves. The inspectors are known for their thoroughness, and if we are up to bad things, they will most likely find out. Will we allow them to look around in our state files, or will we send them away?
