Rumours of the budding success that adventurer companies are achieving in Escann have spread in the refugee communities, and some seem to desire to join up with the adventurers to fight for their lands. The first official request to be trained for battle and then guided back to Escann was delivered to us this morning. These men and women seem hardy enough, but most of them have yet to see battle; sending them to Escann directly would probably be sending them to their death. We could respect their wishes and train them before they join the reclamation of Escann. The other option is to use their desire for battle to our own ends, as we always need more men for our armies. What will we do with them?
