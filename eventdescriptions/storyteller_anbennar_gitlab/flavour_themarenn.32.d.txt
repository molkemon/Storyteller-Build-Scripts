According to the already agreed upon plans for the construction project, the Horrenwood area near Loveswood is to be fully ceded to the crown. While at the time no claimants moved to object to the decision, new reports indicate that a local noble has revealed documents that make him entitled to these lands. The Royal Architect has already come up with a scheme to secure the land, utilizing a Drostenian law that allows us to force the noble to sell the land. On the other hand, we can simply ignore the claims and cite our initial disclosure as a reason to not recognize these documents, which might in turn cause some upheaval, even if other nobles are generally cooperative.
