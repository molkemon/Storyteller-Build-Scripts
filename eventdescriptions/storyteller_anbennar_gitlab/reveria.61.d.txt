The destruction of the capital and Anbennar wasn’t just burning down the buildings, stealing what was valuable, and leaving. It wasn’t destruction for profit, it was destruction with a goal. the complete and total destruction of both cities. The stones of houses and roads were removed; no building was left standing, no marker, statue, or signifier remained. The cities weren’t simply demolished, they were ended. The population was enslaved, killed, or fled, for the only thing left of Anbennar and the Emperor's Capital was the charred ground filled with ashes. That was a sign to the entirety of Cannor and Halann. a new age had started. May the gods have mercy on us all.
