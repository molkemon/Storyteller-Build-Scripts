Another territory we acquired during the Fourth Lilac War was the Duchy of Acromton, the castle of which we took by starvation after a brutal siege. The duchy is primarily known for controlling the eastern passage around the Dames ear, from which it has derived both wealth and fame. While it does contain many Damerian loyalists, any autonomous administration would be primarily concerned with the Duchy of Rubernaire, whose rulers still claim the title of Duke Crossguard traditionally borne by lords of Acromton. As such, the new rulers of Acromton will surely be content under our benevolent imperial protection, despite the intrigues of dovesworn agents.
