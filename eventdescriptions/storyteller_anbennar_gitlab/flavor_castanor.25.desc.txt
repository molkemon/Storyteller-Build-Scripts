General Winter; the bane of any misjudged campaign, the slow and inevitable killer of many an unequipped or unaware army. The winter months spelled death and slowdown to many, but not to the Giantsbane Legion. Trained to fight the last of the giants in the frigid north, nothing short of the glacial dragon breath that ended their first iteration at the Tragedy of Bal Vroren would break the Giantsbane Legion.
