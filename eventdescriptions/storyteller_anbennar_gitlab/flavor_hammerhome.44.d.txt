As the expedition finally reaches the last level, they realize they have struck gold; an ancient workshop which the gnome dates all the way back to the times of Castanor. The workshop contains a myriad of ancient tools and machinery, but the most important find is a large tome. It contains the details of contingency plans marked with titles such as, "Goblintide", and "The Dragon’s Roar". Though many of these plans aren’t very applicable in this day and age, it is possible that some of the ideas in these plans could help them. Among the rest of the workshop is a detailed plan, telling of another route for them to safely return to the surface of the citadel.
