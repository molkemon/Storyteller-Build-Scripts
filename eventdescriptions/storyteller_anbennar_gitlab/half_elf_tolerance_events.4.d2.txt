Prominent halfelven members of the community are holding a gala in the next few days. Apparently, they have restricted attendance to halfelves and elves, as is their wont for this sort of event. However, in a gesture of goodwill to the country, they are extending invitations to a few people outside of that ingroup, and the monarch is one of them. Some are advising the monarch to accept, and others are advising them to ignore it. Still others are saying that this refusal to include people outside their social circles indicates hostility to the rest of the realm and should be condemned.
