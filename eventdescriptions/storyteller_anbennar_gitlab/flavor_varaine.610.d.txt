Having decided to study the giant's living kin, our researchers headed northbound to find suitable study subjects. Luckily, a wonderful opportunity presented itself. an ogre village, hidden away in between the mountains of northern Serpentspine. Not only that, these ogres were friendly, or at least as friendly as can be expected from wild ogres, but they were also on the verge of a great festival. This festival, consisting mostly of feasts and food and eating competitions, would provide many great opportunities to ingratiate or prove ourselves to the ogres, thus securing the subject needed for our research.
