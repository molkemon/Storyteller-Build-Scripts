A burgeoning group of scholars and knowledgeseekers from Anbenncóst has offered their services as middlemen between us and the estate adventurers of our realm. The Order of Chroniclers seeks to gather any and all information in the known world in order to create a global encyclopedia of knowledge, and thus encourage adventurers to be explorers and truthseekers rather than warriors. As a part of their offer, they will give Chronicler Grants that can cover or fund portions of adventuring quests and expeditions. In return, we must share any knowledge the adventurers report or gain in our realm to the Chroniclers, in order to aid in their research and bookkeeping.
