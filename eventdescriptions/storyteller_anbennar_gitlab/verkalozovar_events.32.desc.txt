There are few greater artisans than the dwarves, famed throughout Halann as the craftsmen of the gods. Yet their relative scarcity in Haless has elevated this level of workmanship to a status akin to legend, with dwarven pieces prized above all else. The true masters of bronze and steel, dwarves have established several grand foundries for metalworking across our vast domain, producing arms and tools alike in honor of the ancestors.
