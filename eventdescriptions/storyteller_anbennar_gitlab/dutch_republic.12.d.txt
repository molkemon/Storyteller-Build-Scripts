The war with that country was over, we thought all matters were settled. Negotiations have drawn out over some minor issues, but everything changed this week, when their representatives suggested a new clause, one favorable to the republic. To weaken the Silcalas family's position politically, they want us to pass an Act to keep future Princes of Silcalas from the office of monarch. What motive they have for this is not yet clear, but even if such an Act may not last forever, it would considerably strengthen the Republican rule if this is handled with discretion.
