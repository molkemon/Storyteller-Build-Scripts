I grow weak, but soon I will be reborn, and as I slip away I have been reflecting on what I have left for myself. Although I would like to think myself good, I am not the Great Creator, and although I am not perfect I am not the Great Destroyer; it seems there is more of my path left to walk. I have done and failed to do much, and as I reflect on it all I am coming to a clearer conclusion. I am happy on my deathbed. I always knew that of all the Molten Eternity’s virtues, mine was meant for me, and my dedication to it has reshaped the continent in its image. My actions ring through the Universal Soul, and if I have one regret, it is only that the next version of me will not remember what I have done now. Yet the Molten Eternity spreads, and tales of my deeds spread with it, and the virtues remake the world in the image of the Great Creator. Perhaps I will remember after all.
