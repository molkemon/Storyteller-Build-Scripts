Great trophies of even greater beasts line the halls of the monarchs, each a legendary tale to be told for eternity. This display inspires awe in those who seek to emulate them and spurs them to ever more daring deeds of dauntless destruction. But none are more welcome than the new recruits arriving at the capital. They are a band of seasoned adventurers, monster hunters whose fame has propagated throughout Rakhadesh. They now offer their services to them, in hope to die in valiant combat.
