As soon as he read that another spider had made its way onto the quest board, Gor Vazumbrog's preeminent scholar began collecting small mammals and large insects with which to placate the queen. He had had such success with the Ironweave skitterers that he was sure that taming a matriarch would be just a matter of portion size. When he and Thrain travelled out at the head of a company to actually face the hulking arachnid, it became clear that peace was not an option as she tore through the front rank of the Dauvek Veker spearmen who had been intended to keep her corralled enough to cajole. Moreover, the halfdead victims coccooned in her lair would likely not easily accept Titanium rule if it meant living alongside the creature that had devoured their comrades. "I think we're going to have to kill this spider, Lorek."
