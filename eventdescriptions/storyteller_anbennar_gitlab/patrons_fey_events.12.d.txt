The ancient glade does not transform into the throne room of old this time. Instead, what lies before you is a terrible gaol; a feminine form shackled by thorns whose body is split into a dozen sections across the room. A lonely crown sits on the floor, untouched by the dust and silvery blood of the chamber. A single guard watches you intently, a thorned treant with a wicked polearm and malevolent grin. "My subject. My state is not so grand at the moment. The thieves have stolen my kingdom, my rule, but not my will to fight. From this prison I cannot fight them, but you can help me. Please, accept a portion of my curse so I may extend my power outside for but a brief moment."
