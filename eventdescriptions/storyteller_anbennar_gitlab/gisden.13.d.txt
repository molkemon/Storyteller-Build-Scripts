Horror is on the face of every man gathered around the room. The letter is read once, twice, and then a final time by all present. The words sent by our spies reveal a plot that may lead to Gisden’s doom. Arannen and Asheniande intend to partition Gisden and right the wrongs done to them during the Fourth Lilac War. Discussions occur in that room. Men speak of countless plans, talking in hushed tones as though afraid their enemies could hear them. They suggest begging for intervention from the rest of the Empire; they consider peacefully submitting to the Arannese boot once more; and they wonder whether victory is even possible in the first place. In the end, all options but one are discarded. Gisden shall never surrender; war is coming. Although our enemies’ intentions couldn’t be clearer, it seems there is still some time before they choose to act in full. Gisden must plan, prepare, and be ready to call upon its allies. We have endured Arannen’s wrath before; we will do so again.
