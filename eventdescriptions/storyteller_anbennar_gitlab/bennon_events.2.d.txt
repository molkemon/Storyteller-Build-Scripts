Havoric settlement is commencing swiftly throughout the Vale, but a dozen or so elven representatives from the Silent Repose have come to our court. They make a very simple request. minimize settlement near the Silent Repose. They tell us of the importance it holds to them and would wish for it to stay under Elvish influence. Although many Clan leaders scoff at the request, no doubt already planning to settle the Havoral Heights themselves, others take the plea more seriously; namely our new Elven subjects. Ultimately, the decision of settlement is up to the monarch.
