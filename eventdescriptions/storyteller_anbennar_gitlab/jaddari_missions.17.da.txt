The desert legions have come a long way from their days in the wastes. Our newly reformed legions are spoken of all around Halann, and our foes tremble in fear before our divine might. Our righteous fervor, our disciplined practice of the Tenets of the Jadd, and our ferocity in defense of those ideals shine through in our soldiers and how they carry out their duty. Our nation is one of many races, all of whom fight side by side in our army. Our army is a reflection of our people and all we stand for. And we are the sons and daughters of the Sun. We are his champions, and through our military, we will bring the light of Surael to all people of Halann and vanquish the darkness.
