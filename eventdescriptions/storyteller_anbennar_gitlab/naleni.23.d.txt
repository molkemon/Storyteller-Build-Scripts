No letters rest on the desk; instead, a very desperate looking Odanches stands waiting. There is not an Odanches alive who would give up the sea, but life on the waves does pose a problem when it comes to the traditional aspects of life, such as keeping husbands and raising young. This particular Odanches has become so infuriated by the difficulties of splitting life between land and sea that they have come today to ask for assistance maintaining their roost whilst on ship.
