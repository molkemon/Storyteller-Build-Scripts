We have traveled far and seen many struggles for survival; however, a people known as the Lai’i have shown themselves to be great warriors and seem not to be struggling, but thriving. Daily sparring matches take place from a young age, and disputes between tribe members are not infrequently settled in trial by combat, typically to the death. Their armor far outperforms our own, seeming to provide protection well beyond its material construction. They are extremely reluctant to share their secrets, but from what we gather some mystical property is imbued in the finishing stages of its crafting. They are fearless in the face of danger, often charging recklessly into danger with no visible setup. It seems a shame to lose such valiant warriors on petty squabbles; perhaps they may find use for some of our medicines, and in return offer us some modicum of protection from their beasts.
