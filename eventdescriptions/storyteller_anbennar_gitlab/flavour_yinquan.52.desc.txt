Finally, the urbanites have been massacred. Militias of farmers gathered and, once more, assaulted the cities. Directly aided by our forces and some strategically placed maidens, they have been able to finish the job of annihilating the disloyal merchant class. A myriad of traders, most caught halfway in preparing to flee the country, have been lynched and their wealth expropriated in the name of the realm. Now comes the second part of the plan; redistributing that wealth. After being taken by the state, we will now arrange a grand celebration where only the most pious will receive shares of the treasure taken from the dead, courtesy of Huszien Herself. If Huszien were to give these individuals not only spiritual fulfillment, but material success at the same time, we could create a class of utterly devoted merchants who will both trade and spread Huszien's word at the same time. It will take some time until these commoners have settled into their new roles in the economy, but this matter is well worth the wait.
