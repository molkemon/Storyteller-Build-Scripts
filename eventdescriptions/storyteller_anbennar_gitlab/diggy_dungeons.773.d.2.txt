The mages begin the ritual, absorbing the magic from the cable. The results were immediate, as the Runeforge began to sputter and slow down, and the dwarf began to panic, drawing more power from the ravine. The amount of power they were taking was nearing dangerous levels, as they began to weave a spell. The combined effort of dozens of mages caused the very room itself to shake and shudder, as a massive sphere of pure magical force was created above their heads, and thrown at the Runeforge and its golems. All of a sudden the flow of magic ceased, as an explosion was heard from the chamber that led to the ravine. Even as many of the mages collapse, glowing red hot from the amount of magic they had channeled, all that remains is the construct that calls itself Verdric Topazbeard.
