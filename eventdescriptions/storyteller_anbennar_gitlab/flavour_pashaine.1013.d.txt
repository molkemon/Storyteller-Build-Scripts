A recent diplomatic attaché has incited much suspicion in the halls of state since his arrival two weeks ago. Preternaturally calm and apparently unconcerned with even the most worrying of recent events, the attaché shows all of the classic signs of arcane enthrallment, including the flat emotional affect which is so distinctive of those dancing to another’s tune. As of yesterday, news of this strange interloper has hit the streets of the capital, and now crowds urge for the purging of this subversive element and the removal of magical spies from our midst.
