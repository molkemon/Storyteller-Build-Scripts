The country seeks to secularize the Ravelian State, subjugating our Rectorate to its Parliament. It argues that this is a natural extension of the people’s rule, and that if we are sincere in our beliefs, we will see the wisdom of this. In truth, Pashainé has enormous influence over our state organization and can influence a great many Veridicals, even beyond those from within their own borders; we should consider our next move carefully.
