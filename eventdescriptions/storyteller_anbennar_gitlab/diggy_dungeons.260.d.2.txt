Detecting the source of the illusion, a mage from the expedition carefully dispels the magic from the room. He was not prepared, however, for just how powerful the spell was. The illusion spell that held up this feast comes apart at the seams, then violently disintegrates. The expeditionaries are blown to the ground as the magic dissipates with a powerful force, and when they get up again, they are no longer standing in a feast hall. All that surrounds them is a cave filled with satyr bones, and the corpse of a single satyr who seems to have died when the spell did.
