The temple in that domain has fallen into disrepair, to the point that it might as well not exist. Taxes that should have been deferred to its upkeep have been siphoned into the wrong pockets. The Neoksamaji have come with a request from the spirits that live there, angered over the dilapidated state of their place of veneration. With glowing eyes characteristic of someone being possessed by the spirits, their voice booms as they demand the funds and laborers to not only restore the temple but to elevate it to an even grander state. A truly impressive temple for the spirits that live there appears to be the price if we want them to continue to abide by the Compact.
