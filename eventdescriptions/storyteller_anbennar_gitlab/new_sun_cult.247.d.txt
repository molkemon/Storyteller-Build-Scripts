After centuries of oppression and neglect, the venerable ancient art of Bulwari magic is flourishing once more. The many heated debates and protracted negotiations, the steep cost to the treasury, and the manifold efforts have at long last paid off. We are now known as the beating heart of a revitalised magical tradition, with some of the more effusive poets already proclaiming a rebirth of Bulwari magic. For many millennia, Bulwar was the undisputed center of magic for all of Halcann. While the upstart Cannorians still dwelt in mud huts and the Halessi debased themselves in feeble obeisance to fickle spirit entities, Bulwari magi imposed their indomitable will upon the world, crafting the first magic spells ever used by humans. And thanks to our tireless effort, soon enough even the many lands beyond Surael’s Garden will have to acknowledge the meteoric rise of Bulwari magic and accept its return to a rightfully deserved preeminent role amongst the varied magical traditions of the world.
