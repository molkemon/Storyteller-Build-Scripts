Despite all the speeches and books promulgated by Eborthíl upon the plight of their royal family, how they all died pleading to Castellos and pledging that their nation would avenge them, only we know the whole truth. Still, for the executioner, who killed all of them and then proceeded to process the bodies for consumption, it wasn't the first time, nor would it be the last. They did their job with no rage or passion, used to the proper processing of remains for consumption. This day would be remembered by all of Eborthíl with deep regret and promises of revenge. The Deshaks had a sense of dread for the rise of another gnoll enemy and compassion for the royal family they had lost. For the gnolls, it was a day of celebration and laughter over how they had avenged a wrong, the destruction of those who dared to hurt their Brrtekuh. But for the executioner…
