Many Esmarians yearn for the bountiful lands of Aelantier, and many more are simply discontent with serving the decrepit House of Vanbury or the unbridled savagery of the Havoral Band. Countless travelled west alongside other Anbennarians but found themselves amidst strangers and foreigners, with most fertile land already claimed and settled. Thus begins the tale of an unclaimed land of forests, lakes, and rivers. This land bounty reached the ears of these straggler Esmari, whose hopes and spirits were rekindled with fervor. Settlers from along the Esmar and its tributaries, mostly former members of the Themarenni Marines, as well as landhungry pioneers, gathered together with haste. Passage was found at Yearncliff in the form of six ships commanded by the lesser Themarenni noblewoman Aucana of Sweetcastle. There, with good tide and friendly wind, the brighteyed and eagerminded troupe set off for the bountiful woods of the Broken Sea Coast.
