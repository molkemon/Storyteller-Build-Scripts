Popular support for the house of the ruling dynasty is strengthened from the pulpit of the church, where the common folk are often reminded of the great importance of a strong ruler for the state church and the tradition of hereditary rule. The realm, however, accommodates more than one faith. Those who adhere to other faiths don't always have kind words for the princes of the realm, with some using republican ideals as an excuse to openly challenge the monarch and the state church.
