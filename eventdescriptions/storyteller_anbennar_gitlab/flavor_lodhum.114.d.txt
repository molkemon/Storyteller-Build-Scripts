A visiting Sun Elf scion from a prominent noble house in Bulwar has caused a ruckus, trying to order around a number of humans in Ovdal Lodhum. They, of course, simply shrugged them off and continued on their way. However, it seems the Sun Elf individual has a vindictive streak, and has been harassing these heretics for ignoring the will of Surael's Chosen. It is getting to a point we cannot ignore, as it seems the individual has hired a number of armed thugs in order to drive his point across.
