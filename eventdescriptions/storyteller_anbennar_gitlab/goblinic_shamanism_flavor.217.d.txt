A gaggle of wildeyed members of the Cult of Reason has nearly drowned us in demands to support their newest experiment. They claim that there exists some outside omniscient force that peers into the affairs of the world. They claim that if we can bend this force to our will, we would know what it knows. everything. While their plan to do this seems shaky at best and some of our greatest minds believe it could bring on the end of the world, the Cult of Reason is salivating at the sheer volume of knowledge that could be obtained if they succeed.
