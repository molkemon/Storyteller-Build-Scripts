It is done. We have crushed the crazed mob of the Dragon Cult, and slashed our way through the streets of the province, reaching the palace of deviancy where the Dragon Prince was being raised and revered. The creature didn't wait for us any longer, and perched itself on the roof of the palace, growling at our knights before spitting a cloud of flames at them, repelled by the water shields cast by our mages. The monarch stood at the front, wielding a spear and charging at the beast. Once the dust settled, the dragon lay dead among the rubble of the now ruined palace and countless corpses of our knights. Above the dragon's carcass, stood the monarch, wounded, covered in blood and scorch marks, with a broken weapon and a bent armor, but nonetheless triumphant. Our surviving troops raise their blades to the sky and cheer, to their victory today, and to the victory of their monarch, who will be immortalized in Ynnic memory as the second coming of Artorean.
