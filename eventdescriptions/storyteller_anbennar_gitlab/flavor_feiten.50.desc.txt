At long last, we are on the offensive. No longer will these pirate fiends scuttle our boats, raid our coasts, and disrupt our trade. The rebellion has caused not only us, but thousands of others along the coast grief. This ends now. If we are going to bring the fight to the pirates, we're going to need to plan an ambush, to help even the odds. We can lead them towards our coast so our naval batteries can tear them to shreds, and our balloons can rain destruction from above. Alternatively, we could lead them out to deep water, where the local forces can have at them however they wish. Whatever we decide, we will likely need a skilled admiral to effectively lead this ambush, but we could always rely on the raw strength of our navy if worse comes to worst.
