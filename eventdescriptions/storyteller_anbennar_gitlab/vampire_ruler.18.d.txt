The ruler decided the hunting grounds would be the tavern, that honeypot constantly attracting flies and wasps. They arrived late at night, well into the carousing, when hardly a soul could make out the monarch's face through heavy beer goggles, let alone recall it the next morning. It was similarly straightforward to attract a potential victim; the ruler barely even needed their vampiric abilities. The inn had been well selected for its availability of private and discreet rooms, and the pair blended in with countless others as they found their way upstairs. The rest was done in the darkness, and in the darkness it shall remain. Suffice it to say that the ruler's desires have been well sated, and that their victim shall survive, with a hazy recollection of the night. The ruler shall greet the dawn with a smile on their face, knowing their prey's only memory of the night shall be the brightness of their eyes.
