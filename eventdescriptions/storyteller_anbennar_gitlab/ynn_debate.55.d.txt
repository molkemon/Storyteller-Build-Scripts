The ongoing debate is a storm unlike any other, one roiling all who live their lives near the Ynn. But of course, life still goes on amidst a storm, and so too does one of life's few constants. taxes, in particular all the payments obligated to the ruler by Iosahar. Or is something different this year? After all, the ongoing debate which challenges the Iosahar system might be a good excuse to raise the dues owed to the capital. But on the other hand, if we offer our Iosahar a slight reduction in their payments, we might win them to thinking our way.
