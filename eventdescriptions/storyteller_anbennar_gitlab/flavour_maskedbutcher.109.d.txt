Under the sun, hope is born. A glorious thing, hatched from an egg, incubated by warm rays. Hope cannot last. Not in the lands of the Butchers. You know this, oh godbegot, godbegot. This is your will. This is how you have chosen to spend your power. Hope gives way to sleep. Sleep gives way to dreams. Dreams give way to nightmares, to despair, to total, complete anguish. But do not dream, lowly mortals, for the blood moon rises. And it will rise again.
