Lately all sorts of whispers, stories, and scandals, true or made up, follow the monarch. From a report of cowardice from when the ruler was a youngster, to bastard sons and daughters suddenly popping up everywhere, to tall tales of the monarch consorting with beasts and monsters, the ruler's name is on everybody's lips, and never accompanied by flattering words. The ruler's closest friends and confidants are all in agreement that all this is a consequence of that prophecy from a nearby domain, and the ruler's refusal to bend to it.
