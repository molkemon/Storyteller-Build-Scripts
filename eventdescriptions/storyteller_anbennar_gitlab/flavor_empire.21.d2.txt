A small tribe of orks, fleeing turmoil or persecution in Escann, have come to us seeking refuge. While orks are best known as the monsters who perpetrated the Greentide, nearly all of the original generation of orks have passed away; perhaps this younger generation can integrate into Cannorian society, if given the chance. They profess loyalty to Corin, having denounced their ancestors' foreverwars, and now seek to join the crusade against evil across Halann.
