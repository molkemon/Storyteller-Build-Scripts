The Ahati are, as we know, one of the most skilled fighters in the Salahad and beyond. Most of the Ahati can, after years of training, best most of our officers and specialists in one-on-one combat with great ease. And one such Ahati has now proven themselves to be even greater than that. They have proven to be a great and skilled swordsman, moving as if dancing on the sands. Combined with being a genius strategist and great leader, they make for a perfect advisor to our forces, whether to train our officer corps or create war plans. Luckily we have been granted approval by the Ahati to hire him as such.
