All mundane creatures are hereby ordered to cease the use of so-called artifice, which is a heretical perversion and abomination that shall not be countenanced. Failure to enforce this decree will result in the revocation of your people's current status as autonomous tributaries of the Skull Throne. The Black domain's lack of concern for our sovereignty is well known, but their new proclamation takes this to a yet further extent by directly legislating on behalf of our government. They demand we enforce their decree that artifice be banned from the continent of Cannor, and there can be no doubt that disobedience is likely to result in conflict.
