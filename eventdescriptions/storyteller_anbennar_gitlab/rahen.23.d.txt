The city of Tilathi had been in the middle of a volatile region for some time. This however didn’t deter people from visiting the Elephant Ziggurat. With its stone gleaming like a moon in the night sky, the ziggurat radiates beautiful splendor for all of Haless to see. Many pilgrims finished their journey from Tiltaghar here, while others traveled from the south to start their own discoveries of the wisdom of the High Gods. Despite its splendor, the silver ziggurat is old, and necessitates preservation for it to shine long into the future. With many travelers in the city, we can ask for their generous donation for the upkeep of the site.
