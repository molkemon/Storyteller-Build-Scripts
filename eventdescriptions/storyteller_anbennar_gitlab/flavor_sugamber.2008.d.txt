Enough is enough. The monarch slams their fist down on the throne, flinching lightly as their hand makes contact. Another day, another report that the Hill and Flamemarked Gnoll mercenaries had started a brawl in the barracks that served as their joint quarters. It seemed that the two distinct packs were not going to mesh well together at all. Maybe it was time to let the gnolls fight it out amongst themselves, as an advisor who had travelled Bulwar extensively had suggested. The monarch was unsure of what exactly that entailed, but surely anything would be better than the current situation. The winning pack will confer a permanent buff to our armies based on their mercenary modifiers.
