While growing up, the consort enjoyed studying people. They often made a game out of trying to predict the actions of the people around them, finding it not only fun, but as the years went by, an increasingly useful habit to keep. Thankfully, moving to the royal court of the realm has not caused the consort to abandon her knack for intrigue. If anything, they have gotten a lot of practice; during some of our recent negotiations with foreign diplomats these skills have actually proven most valuable to the council.
