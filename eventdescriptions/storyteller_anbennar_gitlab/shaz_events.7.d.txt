The conquest of Azka Evran has sent a tremor through the political landscape of the elven successor states, and Birzartanšes will answer us first. They pose an imminent threat to the Marble Dwarves, and our great work is incompatible with their ambitions of a new Phoenix Empire. The elves see us as an upstart state that has disrupted their plans, and needs to be cast down. Just as with AzkaEvran, it will be preferable to strike first and to strike fast before we are overwhelmed and defeated by a combined alliance of elven states.
