As the sun reaches its peak, a house slave requests your attention. He leads you to the front of your palace in the capital where before the guard stands a foreigner and their entourage. The foreigner is dusted and sunscorched from travel, but their pointed ears and hair the color of gold still stand out. You of course have heard of elves before, but this is the first one you have ever seen. Their entourage is almost as surprising as well, an eclectic group of Northerners from the desert. There is a gnoll with a shortsword at his side, a Harpy dressed in silk finery, and a few human tribesmen. Each alone might not be surprising, that they would be here together on one mission certainly is. Their discussion with the guard falls silent as they see you approach. The elf steps forward, and begins, "Hello, I come bearing news of the Lord who brings Light to the Four Corners. The god Zurel, as you call them. We wish to tell you about His truths as revealed to His Herald."
