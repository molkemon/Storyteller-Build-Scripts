Returning a fallow land to its former fruitful state is an arduous task; it requires the hard labor of the many and the strict oversight of the gifted few. We Rósanda are of course more than capable of providing the latter, and our orkish slaves will suffice for the former. Most of our slave laborers are, however, bound to the country estates of their masters, where they do their part in restoring the domain as penance for the sins of their ancestors. Whenever the realm has need for a large amount of laborers, they must be temporarily requisitioned via the system of royal corvée until the work is done and they can return to the domain of their masters. While all our noble landowners support this system in principle, most tend to be somewhat cross when it is their workers, and not those of their neighbors, that are requisitioned.
