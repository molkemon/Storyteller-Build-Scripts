A thick smog of incense filled the room where the Court of Change resided. At its centre, laid out on an array of cushions, resided Akhaxpa. As the ruler approached her and knelt down, she opened her three eyes, filling the room with a blinding blue light. “Are there no new ideas in that head of yours? Or those of your clergy or wise lizardfolk? Can none of them bring in new ideas for your people? Again and again, over and over, they follow the same paths, never changing, only making incremental progress. You need new ideas.”
