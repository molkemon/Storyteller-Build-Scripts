The Effelai Bloom has been a time of unparalleled chaos, with nature itself fighting tooth and nail against every move. However, with every misfortune thrown at us, we have found a confounding boon for all our troubles. It appears that during an exceptionally violent upturning of the terrain, the roots and greenery of the jungle have fallen away to reveal something truly amazing. the ruins of an ancient civilization. We have already begun excavation of the site.
