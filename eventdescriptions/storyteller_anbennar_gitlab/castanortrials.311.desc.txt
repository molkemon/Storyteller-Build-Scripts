The silver wyvern drops out of the air, still regarding the creature with curiosity. On the ground, it looks much smaller and less intimidating than with its wings outstretched in the air, only reaching the height of an average man. Though a young member of the species and friendly as of yet, its wicked tail barbs and daggerlike teeth, supported by a powerful and muscular frame, betray its true potential danger. It approaches and starts sniffing the creature's pack.
