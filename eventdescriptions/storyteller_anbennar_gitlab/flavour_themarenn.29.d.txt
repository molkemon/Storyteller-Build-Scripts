Having years of experience in the field of maritime engineering, the twins have come up with ingenious plans to smoothen the progression of the waterway. As per their vision, the Themarenn dam is built in an arched style so that the abutment can balance and absorb the applied hydrostatic pressure, whatever that means. This is followed by an intricate system of locks and bridges that regulate the slack water levels, allowing for uninterrupted inland and maritime transportation.
