A caravan of kobolds has entered our caverns, finding its way into the nooks and crannies of our domain. Hailing all the way from another realm, they come with everything they had on them. trinkets, baubles, tools, seeds, clothes, and theories. Philosophies. The damned thought of the gnomes comes to haunt us. This has immediately caused uproar in our domain. The locals balk in horror at these migrants, who enter our lands but fail to worship Malliath, or Tayekan, or Nimrith, or Iothoral. Already, the dragonpriests have demanded that they sacrifice lambs to the slaughter, or contribute wealth to the Great Hoard, but the newcomers refuse. Instead, they hold fast to the thought, and declare that we should categorize, synthesize, and catalog every story we know about the dragons. They have even convinced some of the dragonpriests, who have already begun making compendiums of their own, and who debate that some of the dragons may not even be real. The migrants offer their own wealth to our domain, and desire to help us fortify the caverns. They say they want to help us, and yet distrust is sown throughout our nation. Do we embrace these strangers, who declare loyalty to our ruler alongside the thought? Or do we put them to work, while ensuring that dragons are the only things worshiped in the caverns of their name?
