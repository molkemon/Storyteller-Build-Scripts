Spiritfire has become the center around which the Ashen Soul builds its magical traditions, but the other elemental magic of the sect is still important to consider as we delve deeper into spiritfire manipulation. Particularly, the affinity towards water that the Ashen Soul demonstrates has partially fallen by the wayside as we pursue the development of fire magic. These techniques are not opposites, but mirrors to each other in how they control and direct the elements. Analyzing one gives us insight into the other, and there is a great deal of insight to glean from water magic. Water is freeflowing, but much more ordered than the erratic movement of flames. Water can be shaped in many ways to serve a different function, and in these forms we find some valuable techniques to apply to spiritfire magic. Of the multitude of different configurations, three have stood out as especially informative. Which should we seek to truly understand?
