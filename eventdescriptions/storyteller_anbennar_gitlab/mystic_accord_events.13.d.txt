Kharunyana did move around on occasion, but it is incredibly easy to track such a large fish. Especially so when it endeavored to make itself known. Visiting the great spiritfish at least once was something of an informal obligation for the ruler and neglecting to do so would be a terrible blow to their piety. There was something humbling about Kharunyana's gaze, even as the spirit offered the ruler supper for their toils. If there was something to ask, now would be the time.
