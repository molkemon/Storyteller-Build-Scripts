Naturally, the first source of inspiration for the artificers of Breluslad were the Great Dams, for while their architecture is impressive, they hold within an even more detailed and magnificent system of mechanisms for opening gates or powering mills, pumps, and other machines. Thus it is no surprise that the Artificers have concluded the state-sponsored research project by presenting to the monarch a prototype of a new type of watermill. Using the clockwork of the Dams along with the magic enhancements of artificery, this construct will allow for powering a huge amount of industry on a massive scale, the drawback being that there are not many places along the river suited for their construction.
