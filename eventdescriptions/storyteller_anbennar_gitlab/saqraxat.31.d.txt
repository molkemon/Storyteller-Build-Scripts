If one word could describe his feelings, it was humiliation. He felt humiliated and indignant, and frustrated. His entire service to Kheterata had seen his homeland face defeat time and time again. No longer would he stake his reputation on those who would just fail him once more. Instead, he would strike out on his own and fight in Elikhet’s name under a new banner. Out on the frontiers of the faith, where his only enemies would be the feeble armies of the hapless Medasi, where he could make a real difference. Yes, Saqraxat would be where he shall lay the foundations of his legacy.
