While we can hardly call it a peaceful process, we have begun to win the hearts and minds of our people. This effort takes many forms. From the greatest orators amongst our missionaries sparring over metaphysics with our wisest elders, to the livelier leading impromptu festivals of Minar, or more simply, general celebrations, many pleasant memories have been made. More grimly, however, a number of violent incidents have taken place through the course of these efforts. While we have done our best to stay our blades, those who fear change are often the most violent. Some of our brethren have been faced with imprisonment or exile, whether it be for such violence or merely for standing firm in the way of our efforts. With agitators turning elf against elf, those in our close counsel betraying us, reformation has been a painful time for our nation. While the monarch and Erelas Starwatcher have come far in their efforts, the war for the soul of our nation continues.
