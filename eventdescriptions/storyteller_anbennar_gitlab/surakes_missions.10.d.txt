The Suran now flows with flowers of all varieties, coloring it with all the colors of the rainbow and more. Bricked roads have been constructed along the waterways to allow comfortable strolling along the riverbanks, small cafés and eateries have been erected along it as well, and a guild of boaters has been created exclusively to ferry those who wish a closer look up and down the Suran. Already the towns and cities along the river are prospering even more than before with the influx of strollers, as they have come to be called.
