The sky blazes red at morning, smoke on the horizon rising from the Jiangliubao. From every corner of the misty Xianjie, hermits leave their mountaintops and students gather in their training grounds. For two years the Xiaken have made preparations, have honed body and mind and blade. Now they shall turn as one towards their foe. the Great Command. In the temple of the capital, however, things have not proceeded with regularity. For here there is a visitor to the monarch, cloaked in robes and draped in cloth, red clawed hand covered in rags. Here is his offer, his wisdom and truth. that in the course of history, there are certain rulers so great that they are able to discard the path of usual action, to go against what might otherwise be expected of them. The ruler is one such ruler, and where fools have prepared to stick their necks underneath a hobgoblin blade, they might see a different route on which their armies could march. All that would be required. an agreement to not fight the Command, and a small gift as a show of goodwill. The monarch takes a moment to consider the hidden stranger’s wisdom, before returning with one of their own.
