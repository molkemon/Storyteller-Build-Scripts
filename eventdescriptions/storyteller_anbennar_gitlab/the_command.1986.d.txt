Tensions within the War Room have unfortunately spilled out into conflicts between various Commands on how the state should be governed and how its resources are to be used. The latest of these conflicts concerns the structure of the supply network. The Wolf Command insists that focusing on a mobile logistics system will strengthen war efforts, while the Elephant Command suggests that using static fortifications to control and supply surrounding areas will serve the Great Command better. The argument has grown rather heated, with both sides claiming the other's strategy will be extremely ineffective and only serve to limit the military capabilities of the Great Command. Siding with one party will cause the other to see it as a slight against them.
