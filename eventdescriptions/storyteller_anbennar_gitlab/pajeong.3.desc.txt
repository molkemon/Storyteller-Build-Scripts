We have been under fire from all sides. The death of Sage King JeongHun Gyo was a sad day for our nation. Since his fall, the estates of our once proud country have been at each other’s throats, as well as bristling against the orders of the monarch. But in our most dire straits, a light in the dark appeared. Through skill and diplomacy, we have managed to wrest the loyalty back from one of our estates. They are willing to stand proudly by the side of the monarch and vouch for their ruler, and tutor him in the art of statecraft. The storm is not over yet, but the rain has begun to lull.
