The hold springs into a frantic action as news spreads of the creature slumbering below and the expedition to slay it. Messages and orders fly around the halls of the hold, ferried to the various clans and estates to muster the necessary manpower and equipment for the expeditionary force. In the throne room the king, alongside his closest aides, has gathered the most knowledgeable and competent of elders, scholars, mages, nobles, and adventurers. Through the latter part of this planning, however, there have been arguments regarding some of the finer, but possibly most important, details of the expedition to focus on. What is the monarch's decree? What can we do before going down?
