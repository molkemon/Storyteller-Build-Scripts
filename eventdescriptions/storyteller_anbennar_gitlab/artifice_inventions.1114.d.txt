Songs play an integral part in the existence of every harpy, enriching their cultural and spiritual life. Some mighty songs are even used in the uniquely harpy song magic of Ištarun. With these voice vessels, a permanent record of even the most complex and nuanced choral production can be made and reproduced at a later time. This way, even small harpy settlements in remote parts of the world have the opportunity to experience the ephemeral beauty and majesty created by large harpy chorals or exceptionally gifted singers, connecting them with their kin in other farflung flocks. Sharing such reproductions and playing popular records has become a mainstay of harpy communal life, strengthening bonds even across vast distances.
