As we continue to spread our creed of change throughout the world, Volcanic Truth traders have reached down the coast of the continent to trade with the Taga'Ala. Their ways are at once familiar and archaic. they worship the Endless Ocean, like some stubborn remnants of the past still do in Kanolowele, and organize themselves under small councils tasked with making their political decisions. More concerning is the Taga'Ala's expertise in seafaring. They are a mercantile people, and if they were to leave their mangroves they could begin to spread the old ways into the lands of the Volcanic Truth once more. They appear content for now, but unless we take measures to stop them, their foreign customs could soon reach beyond their borders. We must decide if the beliefs of the Taga'Ala are a threat to the Volcanic Truth, and if so, what must be done about them.
