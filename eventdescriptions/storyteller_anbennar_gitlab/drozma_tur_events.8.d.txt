A warrior from a far flung lodge and monastery most rudely rushed upon the gates of Gomosengha, demanding entry from the local lady. Upon admittance they brought forth scale and eggshell before the court and proclaimed in certainty that on the day of the eleventh of the eleventh fourteen and forty-four a secret heir to the Dragon God hatched in a far northern valley. Rejoice, for thus to you I reveal the living son of Drozmagog, the Prince of Change and Lord of Fire. Varlengeilt, Terror in Green. The young Dragon God has been kept hidden in the Dolenmach moors where he has been taught by scholars and warriors alongside several other young heroes to defend him. In time this order was to reveal the Dragon Prince to the world, but the newsbearing warrior claims they kept him secret for their own gain, seeking to wield this power for personal benefit and ambition. His words have spread aflame through all nations of faith, and we must quickly make a decision on what we make of this new God and our course of action toward him.
