Across Escann, there are few groups as persecuted as our fellow halforks. Out of place both in the lands of humans and in the lands of orks, many halforks find themselves without a family, home, or even food. It is no surprise, therefore, as halfork refugees from the south continue to flow into our lands. We have already directed them to settle in a specific province, and through their gratitude, it is clear they will soon become loyal subjects.
