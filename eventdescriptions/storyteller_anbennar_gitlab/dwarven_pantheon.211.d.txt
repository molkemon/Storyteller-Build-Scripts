While clearing out an old section of the hold, a surprised dwarven barkeep uncovered a completely intact chapel dedicated to one of the gods of the Pantheon while expanding his root cellar. Already, many are lining up outside his pub to get a pint and marvel at the craftsdwarfship of their ancestors. In a gesture of good faith, he has granted many of their clergy access to record and restore the temple to its function. A summary has already been prepared for the monarch's viewing.
