Spirits have their world and we have ours; though they may come close to one another in Haless, to straddle both worlds is a tremendous feat; not one beyond Caoban Sang, but maintaining it will tax even him. The man already bears the weight of preserving the realm amidst all those who would destroy it, he should not have to focus on self-preservation as well. Though spiritbinding shamans are more closely associated with the shunned Devouring Path, there must be some out there who know how to ease the bridging of two worlds. If we can find one, and adapt their efforts, we should be able to maintain the Ghost Emperor's vitality for longer.
