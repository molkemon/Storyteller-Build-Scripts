Without a warning one starry night, the local Oni minority for unknown reasons stormed the temple complex, overwhelming the local sentry before storming into the temple. Alarms were raised, and the local militia quickly assembled and rushed into the temple, which the Oni started to tear through the deep chambers of the temple, walls torn down, gates and doors smashed as if they were looking for something. Though what specifically they were looking for, we did not find out, as the local militia heroically fought and slayed the huge demons as fast as they could to stop the desecration of the temple, which sadly was not enough. Through the ruins, we did find some strange artifacts belonging to the Oni and some which did not.
