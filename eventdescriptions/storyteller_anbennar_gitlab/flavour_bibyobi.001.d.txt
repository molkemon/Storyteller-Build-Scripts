For generations the Amezatany have told tales of their great empire on the Sarhaly mainland. From the far depths of the Dao Nako jungle to the lustrous shores of Horashesh, the Halflings led a charmed and peaceful life. But hard times and evil men brought changes, and these changes pushed the Halflings into the ocean. Our ancestors fled the waves of humans that descended on their jeweled Ananoma and settled in Fahvanosy. Since then, we have led a comfortable life on our islands full of spice, but it is time we reclaim what was once ours. All the shores of the Seahorse Sea belong to the Amezatany and the adventurers of Bibyobi will be the proud vanguard of our return. We are in this land as a Sponsored Adventurer Band. Our overlords in Rayafo want us to conquer and spread our culture and faith for them. They expect a return on investment, and in thirty-five years or when we have conquered a sufficient amount of land, they will want to redefine this relationship. If we are able to successfully reform our government before then, we will be better poised to fend off any advances from our gracious overlord.
