Before, pirates were just a typical daily problem for a Cannor merchant; it added to the transaction cost, but nothing major. Then we arrived, and suddenly it wasn’t just a nuisance but a real impediment. No matter how many escorts or literal warships accompanied them, we kept sinking and raiding their trade routes. So the merchants resorted to begging their monarchs and rulers to negotiate with us just to allow some trade. We were delighted to negotiate, just some payments and a formal declaration that the seas belong to us.
