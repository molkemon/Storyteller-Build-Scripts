It is done. We have taken the final step and sworn an unending pact with our patronesses, pledging to bring about their shadowed will. What dark need has driven us to this? Is it the revenge we seek against our enemies on this plane? Is it the despairing nihilism of those who have suffered too deeply, too many times? Is it the furious animal craving for safety, for the power to defend ourselves at any cost? A curiosity for hidden knowledge? Merely a simple lust for power? It matters not. The pact is made, and we wait now only upon what was promised. a leader fostered by the hags, one who will sweep away all opposition and guide us to greatness.
