The moss sorter lies dead, and the scouts have properly moved into the room. Ignoring the rainbow colored stone shelves, they search through every corner of this otherwise destitute cavern. Regrettably, it really does seem like the only thing of value here was the labor of the now deceased kobold. Undeterred by the pangs of their conscience, one of the scouts begins to rummage through the makeshift robe of the creature and finds scattered notes on the collected mosses. Unable to decipher them, the only thing left seems to be to take the moss and figure out what to do with it.
