Hoofbeats that sounded like rolling thunder. Arrows and spears tearing clean through a man. Naught but flame and ash left in their wake. It is the fate of so many who venture onto the Forbidden Plains, and it seems it is a fate our settlers have encountered as well. The Centaurs have raided our outpost, and left ruin in their wake. But our efforts to tame this wild land are not yet done.
