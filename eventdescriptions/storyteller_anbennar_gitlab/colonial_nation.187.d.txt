Concerns have been raised among the citizens about the rule of the colony. Hints about their opinions had been sneakedly spread over the course of the past months, but it has now become clear in many respects that their discontent is distinct and worrying. One of them has approached the monarch this morning, even before the ruler could attend the council, and has made their intentions quite explicit. "We are deeply concerned about the noble oligarchy influencing the realm's fate with their Old World's vices and greediness."
