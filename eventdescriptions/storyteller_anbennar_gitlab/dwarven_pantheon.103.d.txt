Dagrin Everbeard was the chief god of the old Pantheon. As we begin to piece together his story from archaic tomes, desiccated scrolls, and abandoned temples, a certain fervour has gripped a group of adventurers who have offered their services to delve into the abandoned ruins of a great temple dedicated to Dagrin ahead of more sanctioned groups, in an effort to hasten our progress. All that is required is our authorization to do so.
