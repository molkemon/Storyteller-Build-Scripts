The ruler and the other monarch were having an exquisite time, relaxing at one of the local hills with a particularly scenic view, drinking fine wine and discussing the future of Bladeskeep. Much work had been done in that regard and much was still being planned, but little by little, the progress was beginning to show on the outside, even to the untrained eye. Soon enough, people would believe the goblins when they told them they had been knighted as Stewards. "It is preposterous, isn't it, the amount of work we have to do here." "It is, my friend. But appearances matter, and the work of a knight is never over, even if we have to remind people of it." "I guess so, boss, sorry, ruler." "Ah, forget it, other monarch, no need for the knights." "Well, what if I wanted for there to be a need for them..." "Hm? What is that about now?" "Well, it is a bit..." After a good while the back and forth came to an end, and the Worm's intentions became clear. Through all the escapades and quests, the clan had fallen in love with the knightly ways, and were now seeking to properly establish an identity for themselves as the goblin protectors of Escann. How much of that was a true cultural shift and how much the whims of one hopeless romantic, well, did it really matter at this point?
