The monarch sat alone in the chamber. The ornate writing table sat before them was carved from marble, with upwards of fifty different inkwells around the outside. Although most bureaucrats and merchants used the more modern, and frankly dumbed-down four-color writing language, they felt that all historical records should be written in the traditional lizardfolk style with its huge array of colors. How else could one convey the complexity of language? Without color, one's words could be mistaken as sarcasm or anger when it was never intended. Such a thing would make communication through the written word alone fraught with danger. But what colors to use? What of the prophecy, and how our journey all started when Katyradz defied it? How do we want his legacy to be remembered?
