Young Crovis has returned home defeated, his army vanquished by the damned orks in the east. Gone is the ambitious and bright-eyed lad that marched towards the Graytide, seeking to become a hero to the people his mother's family ruled over. In his place is a gruff and hardened man, not broken but wisened by his failures. Whatever his future holds now, for the foreseeable future the Escanni Reach remains in orkish hands.
