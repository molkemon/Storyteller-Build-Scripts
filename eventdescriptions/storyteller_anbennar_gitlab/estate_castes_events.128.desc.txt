Victory. Our soldiers and officers scream from the top of their lungs after another gruesome battle. The harshness of the battlefield has led to the bond between caste and race to strengthen and petty feuds to disappear. More and more we see soldiers work together with human soldiers and the other way round. Officers have also shown more mutual respect and the morale of our army has improved significantly overall. This is of course great news, and we would be wise to respond to this development, but how?
