A new work of history has been published by the alleged apostate that the Obsidian Council showed mercy to twenty years ago. His book details the history and culture of the Dwarovar in the millennia since they were betrayed by their brothers, and does so in great detail. However, the historian has also worked tirelessly to show the faults and follies of the traitors, and the deserving fate exacted on them by history, proving the Runefather’s righteousness and the necessity of their actions to save their people. Runefather worshipers of all classes, from all across their realm, have viewed the book as a resounding justification for their actions across the Dwarovar.
