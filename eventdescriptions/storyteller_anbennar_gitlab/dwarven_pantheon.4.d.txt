The monarch sniffs the concoction offered by the High Priest of Margurz. The scent of alcohol masks the worst offending smells, but the monarch still retches at the odour. "Are you sure," they ask, "that I need to consume this?" The High Priest nods. "It is an essential part of the ritual. You must consume it, then count backwards from one hundred." The ruler reluctantly takes the concoction and starts doing so, falling unconscious by ninety-three. "Excellent," cries the High Priest as he turns to a nearby acolyte. "You. Fetch me the saw. We have nine hours, according to the texts, before he wakes up." A day later, despite having a splitting headache and a shaved head, the monarch says that they have never felt better.
