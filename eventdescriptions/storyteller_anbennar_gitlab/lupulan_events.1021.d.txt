A great change is coming for Haless. At least that is what our rangers tell us. They may have been the first to notice, but across our country the spirits whisper of this change. Common folk speak of the growing generosity of the spirits, for many have recently been blessed by their boons. It is as if they are preparing our people to march forth like an unbreakable tide upon Haless. Even sightings of our Lord, the Great Lizard Spirit Lupulan, have been reported, a truly auspicious sign after his long period of seclusion.
