Horrifying and blasphemous news has surfaced from the province. Vile bandits have plundered the spirit shrines that safeguard the local roads, leading to a weakening of the local economy. Many peasants and merchants are now reluctant to travel on that road as it is considered "spiritually damned." The shrines will be restored soon, but it might take a considerable amount of time before the most superstitious locals regain the courage to travel on that road again, and the local economy returns to normal.
