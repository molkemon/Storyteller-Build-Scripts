The previous leader's term draws to a close, and the time has come to select a new leader for Bhuvauri's armies. This works by selecting a candidate from one of the three houses of Bhuvauri. There is the Dakinshi house of SkyEyes, spiritual and mysterious. The Bulwari house of SzelAzka, pragmatic and tolerant. And the Fangaulan house of Sanukelborlan, militaristic and belligerent. Power rotates between the three families; who shall come next?
