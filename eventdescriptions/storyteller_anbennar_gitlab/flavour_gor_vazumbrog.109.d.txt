The grim stalwarts of the Dauvek Veker trudged heavily into the Greatcavern, there to investigate the rumors of a very large and thus surely very dangerous troll. The dwarves were tense, weary, and more than a little hungry. So it was with no little pleasure that they began to discover edible fungi and even serpentbloom along the cave walls and floors. Deeproots, serpentvine, cavegrass; even the more exotic firebuds, shimmerfruit, and crystal rokon all looking ever more suspiciously arranged. And then they saw it. the very large troll. Or more accurately, the very rotund troll, peeking out from behind a cavern wall and smiling at the dwarves sampling the assorted underground comestibles. "Little strong locked door friends," he roared, genially, "you enjoy Grizehûd starter food. You come home with Skorri, meet Asfri and little Ulvol, and you come for a big feast. We saw you coming a long time ago, set up everything so you can see best, most forward foot. Come, eat, fill up your waterskins, and fill up your packs with leftovers for the long trip home."
