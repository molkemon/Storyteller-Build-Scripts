A position as a courtesan in the royal harem seems like a strange place for a grizzled veteran soldier to end up. However, a retiring soldier would no doubt be expected to take on the work of contributing to their family's ambitions. If one wants to avoid that, the Favored Hundred can seem like a particularly attractive destination. So it was with our newest courtesan, a talented but aging officer who has promised to take up the work of passing on their wisdom and discipline to the next generation of warriors.
