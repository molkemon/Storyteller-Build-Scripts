Many desperate Ebenmasser settlers head west into the deadly Expanse in vain hopes of finding gold like the Ynnic Empire did centuries ago, but just as many come to the Ynn to offer their services. What they make is not fancy but built to last. Ebenmasser communities are some of the most integrated in the Ynn and rarely cause trouble, giving them the reputation of being somewhat reliable Cannorians. Some of them petitioned us today to build one such community in the realm, promising to help improve the region's infrastructure in return.
