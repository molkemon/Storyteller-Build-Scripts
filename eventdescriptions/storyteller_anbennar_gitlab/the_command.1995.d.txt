Cannons roar, bricks break, walls crumble, such is the nature of a siege. It was no easy feat to destroy the walls of the capital; it took thousands of hobgoblins manning hundreds of cannons, cutting supply lines and gathering intelligence to finally break the fortified city. The Wolf Marshal commands his troops to charge the breach as the defenders let out a rallying cry. The fighting over the city is brutal; xia masters and kai mercenaries led by hobgoblin officers face off against endless regiments flying the three headed banner. The ringing of clashes of steel overtakes the city as the battle engulfs and destroys everything in its wake. As the forces of the Great Command advance deeper into the city, the monarch is seen fighting with techniques utilizing cultivated chi. The monarch fought fiercely, defeating multiple edan of soldiers before finally being subdued. Resistance in the city crumbles once the monarch is captured and the banner of the Great Command once again flies over the capital. Bound in Korashi chains, the monarch is made to kneel before the ruler in the very War Room this civil war originated from. The ruler begins to speak. "Your disloyalty has threatened the very existence of the Great Command hobgoblinkind has forged through blood and steel. You have trampled upon the unity that forms our strength as a people, and have truly dishonoured yourself in this petty bid for freedom. Normally such great treachery would have you stripped of any and all rights." The ruler gives a signal and a blade is brought before the monarch and their hands are unbound by a soldier behind them. The ruler finishes.
