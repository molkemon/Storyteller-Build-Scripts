The tight corridors and overall narrow spaces in the living areas made any engagement rather difficult for both parties, and your expedition struggled quite a bit to get the upper hand over the well-rested bunch of Goblins that had scattered all across the quarters. Eventually you wore the Goblins down, but several of your best men were no longer in fighting condition, with plenty more barely hanging on. Nonetheless, your strategist noticed several handwritings left behind by the Goblin warriors, indicating that they were but a fraction of a much larger force that may eventually make their way to the workshop.
