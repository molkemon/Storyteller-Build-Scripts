Even the mightiest oak can fall when infested by a legion of ants. The proudest bear may succumb to a pack of hungry wolves. And, as today’s tournament in the capital showed, even a squadron of troops led by the monarch himself might be outdone when attacked by a dozen foes at once. The Iosahars’ tactics were fairly simple, but they worked. an attack from all directions, simultaneously, enough to overwhelm them with their greater numbers. But it is not the loss on the field that stings the most; it is the way the story of the tournament has already spread across the land, with many in the realm seeing it as strong evidence of the decentralized Iosahar system’s importance and value.
