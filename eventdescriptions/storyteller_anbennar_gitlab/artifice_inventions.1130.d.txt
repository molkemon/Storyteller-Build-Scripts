The mundane exigencies of war remain essentially the same, whether one stands on the side of Surael’s Light or has chosen to serve the Darkness. With the allseeing war tables, faithful Jadd artificers have provided any general doing their part in the struggle with a significant advantage over their opponents. Using advanced divination magic woven into the war tables, they are able to accurately depict the movements of both enemy and allied troops in the theatre of war in real time, and can even highlight strategically important information such as the location of potential new recruits or supply depots.
