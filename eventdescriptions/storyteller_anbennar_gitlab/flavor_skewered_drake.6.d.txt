Dinatoldir, the Piercer of Shadows, few weapons have a similar reputation in the history of Halann. Forged from the famed metals of the Precursor Elves, and carried by one of the greatest conquerors in the history of all of Halann. Even long after its shattering, it was proclaimed that whoever would reforge this weapon would unite Bulwar once again. Now it has been reforged, but not by some gallant elven princeling or brave human rebel. Instead it was reforged by an ork, a creature once considered a mere beast to be hunted by the people of Bulwar. And now it is those beasts that rule over them. Irony truly is almost as sweet as revenge. The rule of elves, gnolls, and men in Bulwar is over. The time of the ork has come.
