Our ownership of Khugdihr has granted us a great deal of legitimacy in the eyes of the Asra Bank, and they have agreed to return to us the Agate Gem, also known as the Gem of Asra, in recognition of our claim to rule the western Dwarovar. Of course, we also had to donate a few treasures of ours unto their bottomless vaults, but it was a small price to pay for the famed Agate Gem.
