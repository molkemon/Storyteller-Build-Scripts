When the emperor entered the parliament chamber at midnight, the room was already dead silent. The more boisterous princes were either arrested for the night or sunk low in their seats as the ruler walked to the podium. The ruler took a moment, retrieving a piece of paper from their robes as they glanced up at an empty balcony chair above and blew an ally’s kiss, stirring some confused murmurs from the seated princes. "I, the ruler declare the monarch and their descendants to be the rightful heirs to the domain, including all privileges and claims that follow." It took a moment for the princes to interpret what was said until awkward stares flooded the room. One prince from the east remarked as they departed for the night, "Did the ruler just declare themselves Grand Duke in the third person?"
