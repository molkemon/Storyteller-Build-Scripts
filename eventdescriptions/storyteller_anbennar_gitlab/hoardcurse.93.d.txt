Gold, gold, gold. Thoughts of gold seem to be the only thoughts left in our holds. Dwarves work themselves to bone just for a few measly coins, and even then, production numbers are down across the board as some sit in their homes and cuddle bars of gold all day. Many of these bars are loans, and the banks are skyrocketing interest prices, and backing them up with thugs from local mob bosses. Food and goods are becoming scarce as all are loaded onto the trade carts. Our people are starving and desperate, as mountains of our most precious metal fill vaults across the nation. Even the ruler is struggling to focus on the nation over investments, as more and more demand a solution. How to even begin addressing these myriad and interlocking issues boggles the mind of the monarch.
