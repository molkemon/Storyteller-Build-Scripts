Much of Haless is home to settled kingdoms and principalities, their grand cities with thousands of years of history and trade that rivals anywhere on Halann, but the Lupulan Rainforest is one of the major exceptions. This massive peninsula is covered in a jungle so dense it reminds many of our merchants of home, and the many Yanglam tribes here even revere a spirit that takes the shape of a lizard. While these chieftains may hesitate allowing our merchants to build communities on their coasts, we can surely show them the riches our presence has to offer. The major exception to this endeavor, however, is the Kingdom of Arawkelin. a different, more mercantile realm whose interests align with ours in more ways than one. Though diametrically opposed to the native Yanglam tribes, their resplendent city at the mouth of the Sunharbour river and their commitment to fostering commerce between Rahen and Yanshen makes them ideal candidates to sponsor a new community of lizardfolk. Regardless of our choice in local diplomacy, a merchant outpost in the Lupulan Rainforest would further connect our growing network in Sarhal to the unimaginably vast riches that flow across Haless.
