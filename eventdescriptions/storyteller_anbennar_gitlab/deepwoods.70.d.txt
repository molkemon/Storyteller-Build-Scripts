No amount of warfare and conquest could have prepared you for the terrible intimacy of your battle with the Lord of Beasts. Here, there are no walls to protect you, no armies to do your bidding, no diplomats to negotiate, and no peace. You circle around the clearing opposite your foe, slowly drawing nearer, weapon flicking out like a serpent to keep deadly claws at arm's length. The Lord of Beasts fights with the strength of a bear and the speed of a mongoose, a natural equal to the greatest mortal warriors in history, and only your burning desire to live wards despair from claiming your mind. Blood drips from your ragged forms as blade and claw alike find their mark little by little, ever searching for a chance to kill at last.
