A knowledgeable library filled with the knowledge of our greatest generals. Lián Zhu, along with many others, has filled the library to the brim with military interest, to be used by our soldiers and the common folk alike. Along with collections of the sciences, arts, and magic, the library collected a grandiose amount of military knowledge. We aimed for something that represented our nation, and with famed generals and warlords alike coming to take a look at our catalog, we can gladly say we have accomplished such. Thus, sitting atop a hill in the center of Jiangdu, with its entrance guarded by a group of prestigious soldiers and a massive flight of stairs, lay the symbol of our nation. War, embodied in the writing of the most experienced.
