Due to their long-lived nature and lower birthrates, an elven village can remain unchanged over the course of a century and can be fiercely resistant to change. This issue has come to a head as one of our fastest growing towns has encroached on an elven village. The elves have refused to allow their homes to be destroyed, putting the town’s future growth in jeopardy. While forcing them to move would severely damage our relations with the elves, allowing them to stay would severely hamper the town’s future growth.
