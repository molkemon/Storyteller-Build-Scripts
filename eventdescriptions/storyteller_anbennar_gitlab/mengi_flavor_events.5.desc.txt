The ultimate authority within Madriamilak is said to be Amilak's word alone. For the mortals that exist within his domain, the next greatest show of one's right to rule is violence, shown both in one's personal strength and one's authority over the strong. Thus when given the chance, be it in a simple test of strength or a campaign against one's foes, any sort of royalty must be ready to show these qualities off in order to be considered legitimate.
