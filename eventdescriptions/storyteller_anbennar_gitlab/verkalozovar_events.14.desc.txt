In order to reflect the resurgent prestige of Verkal Ozovar, we have decided now to erect a palace in a valley of the Phokhao range. Nestled in a ringlet of mountains yet in clear vision of the sky, this Nephrite Palace will represent the expanding surface influence of our underground hold. Our artisans are poised to create a marvel, but the question remains. who will inhabit the palace? The monarch would be the obvious choice, but perhaps we should honor the OzAmekh instead.
