Dwarves are short of stature compared to most of the races of the world, but their size belies a strength and constitution few can match. Dwarves are respected members of adventuring parties and guilds across Halann, be it as warriors or runemages. every dwarf is more than their size. No dwarf has ever embodied this more than Murri Giantbreaker, and it is no wonder that his stories ever remain popular among their population.
