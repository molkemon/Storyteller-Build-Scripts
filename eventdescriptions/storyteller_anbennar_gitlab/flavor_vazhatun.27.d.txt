It turns out that there were other dwarves besides us who survived the orkish menace. DûrVazhatun was recently resettled by a band of dwarves hailing from a society of civilized humans west of Khugdihr. A recent delegation from DûrVazhatun was sent to us, and we retold the story of the hold's fall to them, including how we took in refugees of the true Sapphire Dwarves into our ranks. The pretenders then had the gall to demand that we hand over our records to the hands of these colonists. Naturally we refused, and the meeting quickly turned sour from there.
