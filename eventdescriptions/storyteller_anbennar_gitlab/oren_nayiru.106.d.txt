When the Kamrayakval declared war on Taychend, one of his very explicit targets were the warlords who still remained in control of much of the region. While he has since taken some of those warlords under his banner, provided they offer at least a semiearnest conversion to his faith, of course, the banners of Orenvalyam have nonetheless presented an existential threat to the landowners and warlords arrayed against them. With that in mind, they have pledged men and resources to our cause, so we can put down the Kamrayakval together.
