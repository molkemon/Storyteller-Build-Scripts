Our great government has received one of the stranger Borbordûz challenges we have seen. A veteran, whether for glory, gold, or to settle a perceived slight by the state, has challenged us to duel above the magma and miasma. It is easily known that he is a veteran. his warweary eyes, slightly singed beard, and most importantly, his lack of an arm. Clearly, his bluff and bluster covers up his inability in combat, despite his experience. Shall we not even acknowledge his pitiful challenge, or should we dispatch this fool once and for all?
