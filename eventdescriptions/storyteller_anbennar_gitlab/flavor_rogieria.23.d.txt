When Rogier found himself in Escann, his family dead and his troops surrounded by hostile orks, he thought the worst, but they endured. When the Young Owl took the throne and saw only a single, narrow path to reclaiming the Silmuna legacy, there was any promise they would be able to walk it, but they have. Thus today's news, that a certain country has declined to join the Shadowmoon Conspiracy, while disappointing, pales before their determination and their destiny.
