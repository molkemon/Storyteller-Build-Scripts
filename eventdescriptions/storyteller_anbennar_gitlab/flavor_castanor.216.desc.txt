As philosophers and thinkers debate with words and generals and soldiers fight with arms for the claim of the two Castans, so too do the ordinary people of both factions take their own sides. For the most part, those remaining in the fields and workshops lay low, working as they always do and not caring much about their overlord. Not in the province, however. The people of the area have split into two roughly equal groups; one supporting our Castan and one supporting the false Castan. Each group has formed a mob, hundreds strong, scouring the countryside for targets from the other side and posturing against one another. Violence has been avoided for now, but who knows how long that will last?
