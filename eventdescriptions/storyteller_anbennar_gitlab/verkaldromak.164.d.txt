Our administration’s greatest challenge has been enforcing the ruler’s will during the months in which they are sleeping. Our workers are harder to motivate, our nobles more prone to squabbling, and in this case, our guards are less diligent about diffusing a situation. Today in the capital, a drunken fistfight between two dwarves was allowed to escalate first into a brawl between the occupants of the tavern, and then into sheer chaos that engulfed much of the area. A significant amount of damage was done before our men could get it under control.
