The Ravelian Society, a loose group of academics and reformists that gather to discuss all manners of topics through the lens of logic and reason rather than superstition, have come to officially petition for a public space in their realm. Supporting their presence in their realm may be beneficial, if we wish to keep relevant in these modern times. They have, however, been found to be vocal opponents of what they view as unjust and untrue, and may develop to be an unwelcome thorn against our state.
