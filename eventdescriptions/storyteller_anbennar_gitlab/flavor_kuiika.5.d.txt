To all my fellow Efergezmu. Horashesh has never been united. In the days of yore we were slaves to the genies. The Karassian Empire pushed our forefathers to the brink of death in their scaly conquests. The Fangaulan Empire saw us as little more than clay to be colored on a map. Kheterata demanded tribute from us, draining our lands and milking us until we could give no more. I am here to announce that, as of today, this has ended. No foreign power shall subjugate us again. No cat worshippers shall force their will on us again. No lizards, no khetists, nobody shall take these lands away from us. From this day forward, Horashesh will be free.
