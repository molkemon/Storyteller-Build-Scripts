Even stripped bare of the gems that once adorned it, the bare Dwarovkron remains a fearsome sight. Adorned with platinum surfacing, it is incredibly heavy, but not impossible to lift. It is a masterful piece of work, and looks most wondrous atop our ruler's head. It is an enduring symbol of our victory over the hated dwarves, and was won by Bordu Crownshatterer, who used his great strength to remake it for himself. Indeed, Bordu founded this very clan, and we owe much to him. Let us therefore honor him by holding this crown in high esteem as a relic of the clan of the Shattered Crown, rightful inheritors of the Serpentspine.
