Many have ridiculed the Cult of the Khet's beliefs that slothfulness is the path to power, and certainly to the uninitiated such a claim must seem ridiculous. However, today we have found proof; after months of extensive napping and resting, the ruler has developed a new power. the power of persuasion. Tested on the many servants and slaves ordered to tend them during their period of rest, the ruler has gained the ability to force others to obey their commands.
