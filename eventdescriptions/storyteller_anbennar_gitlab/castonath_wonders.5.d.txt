When the Greentide struck down the gates of Castonath, the Balgarsmiths held out for over a year atop the Dragonforge and its adjacent crenellations. Eventually, however, the orkish threat was too great, and the Balgarsmiths set the Dragonforge aflame, destroying much of the original foundry and structure to prevent it from being used by the orks. Now that we have cleared the destruction and rubble from its wake, we can finally get around to restoring the inner workings of the forge.
