While visiting the local village of Brelarico, the monarch was approached by a hooded figure who seemed to have a very familiar voice. They beckoned our entourage to enter an unremarkable villa, pleading for us to meet some seemingly important people. The figure turned out to be a trusted advisor who had come in contact with what remains of the Order of the Mountain Eye, setting up a secret meeting between them and our administration. They believe that cooperating with us will lead the revival of their fallen order, which has waned greatly since the fall of the Ynnic Empire. Their skills in espionage were once unrivalled and may yet be should the order be put to good use. They say that serving our republic will revive the order, finally putting the order back to use under Brelar, and we will gladly support their efforts.
