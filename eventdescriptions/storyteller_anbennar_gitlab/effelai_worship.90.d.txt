Corpses are strewn about a bloody altar and dull, discarded flint knives. Our foraging party, our people, had already been sacrificed by the time we arrived at the leechmen's camp. There is nothing we can do for them but to cut down the monsters that did this. They will know the wrath of Chahinanito, and a people spurred to action through violence. Their hissing calls through twisted mouths will be silenced in a night of dancing blades and running blood.
