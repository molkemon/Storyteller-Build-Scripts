The monarch lies broken and bleeding at the ruler's feet. Before they can muster a second wind, the monarch is on top of them, pinning them to the earth. Magical prowess brought us this far, but victory can be achieved in only one way; the monarch doesn't even have a chance to scream before the monarch sinks their teeth into their neck, ripping away their life with a single bite. The gnolls of Zokka's pack watch as the monarch finishes their bloody work. By no gnoll law or tradition are they bound to join our pack, but only a fool would cast their lot in with the successors of the loser, proven weak and impotent by our victory. By nightfall, all the Far Salahad will belong to us as all fealty to Zokka is supplanted with loyalty to our glorious ruler.
