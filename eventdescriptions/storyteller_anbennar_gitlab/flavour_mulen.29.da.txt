The Royal Library of Ghundagar has many books, and will be a great addition to our collection in Mermigán. We have reached a deal with the owner of Ghundagar to purchase a number of their prized collection of books and manuscripts, and many noteworthy works have been added to our libraries. These include collected histories from the Cinnabar Lotus society of Harimar's and Jaher's conquests of Rahen and Haless. They also include the poems 'Parvatim' and 'Golpala's eight' by the renowned Ghundagar warrior turned Yodhiyari poet Akshal Bhirala, and transcribed discussions from the philosophers of the Hall of Endless Debate. Finally, there is a copy of the ancient Yanakhura, the first of the five great Raheni epics.
