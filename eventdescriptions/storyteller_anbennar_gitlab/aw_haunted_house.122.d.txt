Fear grips the town as wild chatter circulates like the plague. Stories conflict and no one can agree on specifics, but one thing is assured. the family that lived in the house is gone, fabled to be dead. The last confirmed family member was apparently in good spirits, as confirmed by a townsperson, though they would say it was a rather ominous and odd sort of happiness. That same townsperson watched them return home that day, down the road and back to that house of horrors, through a fog as thick as the clouds in the sky. That was yesterday, and that was the last time anyone saw of the family ever again.
