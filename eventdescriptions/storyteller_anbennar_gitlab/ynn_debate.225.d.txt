The conversation at the feasttables of the capital often follows similar patterns. there is always the discussion of the monarch’s latest hunt, a little debate about exactly which of the zodiac signs best predisposes someone to be a knight, arguments over whether a historical conflict could have gone another way. But this night’s feast featured an exception. a rude interruption by one of the Cannorian Iosahar. Laughing as we discussed lancethrust against spearwall, they claimed a handful of musket-armed men would have been enough to defeat any of the legendary armies of the Ynn. It is outrageous, and the stir it has caused has certainly caused further escalation in our ongoing religious debate.
