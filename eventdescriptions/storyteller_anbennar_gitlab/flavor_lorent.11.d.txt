Rage. The only word that can describe the palpable and passionate emotion bursting at the seams within the Imperial Parliament. The memory of the alliance between the King and the Emperor is distant, but the betrayal stings as if it were yesterday. Those princedoms that stood with the Rose party feel the betrayal the deepest, while those assembled who fought for the Silmuna’s Moon can feel little satisfaction at being proven right about the untrustworthiness of the Lorentish crown. The fury at Lorent’s unending aggression into the West Dame’s head has seen the Emperor pushed to address the Parliament. Many more meetings throughout the year shall commence, the swarm of Doves fickle and indecisive. But of all the blowhards howling for war, how many of them truly mean what they say? And how will they proceed?
