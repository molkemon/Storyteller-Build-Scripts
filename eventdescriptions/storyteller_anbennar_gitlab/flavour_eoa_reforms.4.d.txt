The young mage bowed and presented a sealed scroll. Its contents identified her as a recent graduate from the Imperial War Mage Academy in Anbenncóst and as a certified member of the new Order of Imperial War Wizards. This most recent edict of the Emperor, establishing that aforementioned Order, had caused some consternation across the Empire, including more than one of the monarch's own advisors, who argued that this constituted a worrying new overreach by the magocrats. As one advisor put it, "As if the Great Geas hadn't gone far enough." "The Magisterium has snapped up the best war mages from across the Empire, and we're getting back their dregs." But this freshfaced mage, eager to assist the regular troops and, more importantly, eager to prove herself under the ordinary chain of command as a powerful and sorely needed battlemage, hardly seemed an agent of magocratic control. Her bubbly demeanor almost seemed infectious, inspiring the troops to serve their liege with their utmost efforts. As she would not tire to proclaim, the Order of Imperial War Wizards was only here to help.
