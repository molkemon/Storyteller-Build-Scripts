In times past, wendigos used to infest the Northern Ynn like a plague, and it took brave knights to venture into the woods to slay these beasts. Such knights used this as a rite of passage to prove themselves as true champions of their lord, earning the right to wear the antlers of their defeated foes upon their helmets. Unfortunately, we have received word from their subordinates that a group of prominent knights have not returned from their hunt. No doubt this will shake the faith in our monarch and the realm.
