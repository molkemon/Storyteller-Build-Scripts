Another report reaches the trapmaster’s cobbled together desk about “The Trappist,” a title that has spread like lightning through the country over the past several months. "A third faux assault on one of the Trappist's newest traps has been foiled once again," it states. "Their knowledge of battlefield tactics is rivaled only by their intuition for creating extremely effective trapdoors, tripwires, and tangles. We should hire this kobold immediately." It almost feels like the Trappist can be in two places at once. On one hand, she has personally led a hundred successful sieges in combat exercises; on the other, the traps she makes are unparalleled in quality. Tragically, we cannot employ her as both a siegemaster and a trapper, but we can employ her towards one of these ends. What should we choose?
