It was quiet within the Imperial Parliament. Benign spies sent to the Emperor's court sought to gain any insight from the ruler but found no leaks or whispers. Neither supporter nor detractor of the target ruler sat at ease, though the absence of the upstart ruler gave some comfort to the former. They waited twelve hours, gorging on the buffet and brewing silent plots with their attendants until the Emperor arrived at midnight. A banging on the parliament doors sent the sergeant at arms sprinting to open the door, but their face dropped as they saw the Emperor. A few princes leaned over to see what all the fuss was about. Stepping into the chamber was the Emperor in full battle regalia, ready for war, bearing a torch in one hand and the battered banner of the target ruler in another. The Emperor stepped to the podium, staring imperiously at the chamber as he sparked the torch and set the banner alight. The burning of the banner itself was the Emperor’s speech.
