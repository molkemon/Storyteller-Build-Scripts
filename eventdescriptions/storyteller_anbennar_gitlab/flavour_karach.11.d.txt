The High Speaker’s term is ending, and the time has come to select the one who will lead our nation forward beyond their term. There are many candidates, from all the factions of the country. They campaigned with all their might, and each of the factions threw their weight behind their chosen one, but only one can win. It falls to each of the votes of each of the workers of the country to decide the course of the nation. Who have they elected as their chosen voice?
