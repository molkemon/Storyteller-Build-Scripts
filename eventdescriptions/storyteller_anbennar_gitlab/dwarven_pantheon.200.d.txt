Turmoil engulfs the country. Our decision to abandon Ancestor Worship has upset or enraged significant portions of the state, the army, and the civilian population as a whole. Ancestor Worship fanatics have begun agitating for open rebellion, and evidently, some individuals are certainly contemplating it, if they are not already moving in secret to do so. This will certainly be a period of turmoil unlike anything we have experienced before, for this is a struggle near unheard of in dwarven society. a struggle of faith.
