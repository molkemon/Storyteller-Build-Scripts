Horror. A cowardly cutthroat created chaos in the crown chambers. The attempt on the monarch's life was luckily averted and the felon killed, but it is already the second time this month the ruler has been the target of hired killers, and many fear it will not be the last. The clergy is saying, "The gods are seeing that if the first half of the prophecy will not stay true, the second will." The family cries that the monarch will get himself killed.
