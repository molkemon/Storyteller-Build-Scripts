At first they tried exorcism. Then they tried spells of binding, unsummons, and banishments. Nothing worked; in fact, whatever they did, it made the elemental even more aggressive, sending the soldiers it infected into a frenzy. One by one, those going mad were cut down. At some point, the corpses piled high when everything went silent. For days, there was fear and mistrust, but nothing ever happened again. The elemental must have left.
