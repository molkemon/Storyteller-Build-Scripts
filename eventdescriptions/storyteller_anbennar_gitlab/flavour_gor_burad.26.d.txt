The excavation of the Vîlngan has taken a turn for the worse. While the curiosity over it initially quelled the underlying rage, the high concentration of the populace working towards a common goal has unsurprisingly led to differences in opinion, and the minority side of that argument is dead. Some of the miners have turned their picks from tools to weapons and have gone on a rampage in the capital, killing thousands of guards and damaging countless properties.
