Good news for once out of the operations of one of the mercantile clans of Celmaldor. Contrary to the usual trend towards clannish isolationism and supremacism, this group has decided to instead draw from a far wider racial pool of new employees. The upshot is a massive increase of a wider selection of races on their payroll. Though the usual sorts are complaining as always, this is what was always envisioned, and in any case it is clearly working out for the economy.
