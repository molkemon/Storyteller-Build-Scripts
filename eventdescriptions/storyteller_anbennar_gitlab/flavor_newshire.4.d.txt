It started with a drop. Within days, the Deluge had spread across all of Newshire. As our crops were drowned in blood and gore, the soil withered and died beneath them. Our harvests failed. Our people began to starve, panic, and riot. Our nation wasn't prepared for this. Was any? Did any need to be as badly as we did, with so many livelihoods dependent on good rains? Perhaps it was a message. Of course we know of the war over the Court, but maybe we erred somewhere, too. Some go as far as to whisper even worse, that maybe this was how it was supposed to end all along.
