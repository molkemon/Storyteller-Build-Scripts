One by one, the monstrosity picked out the soldiers. Muffled screams from the darkness alluded to the horrific being stalking them. One by one their numbers diminished until far too many were lost. "If we run back now, all will be for nought," the expedition leader exclaimed, but not a second later he was taken in by the grue, sending the rest of the expedition fleeing. Only a few returned from the darkness into the safe light. The darkness of the Serpentspine has claimed so many lives in the past, and now it has taken its due once more.
