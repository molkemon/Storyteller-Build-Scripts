Ugol Bloodseeker walked through the crowd towards the cauldron, where the monarch was waiting for him. The crowd was silent, their polished tusks shining in the light of the torches. Once, Bloodseeker had been taller than the monarch by a thumb. Now, the chief of Clan Silentblade towered over him by nearly two heads’ length. The effect of the fey magic he had imbued within himself, it was said. The fey magic that was now also within Ugol’s grasp. Ruuk Elmscourge could talk to him all day long about the costs, it did not change a single thing.
