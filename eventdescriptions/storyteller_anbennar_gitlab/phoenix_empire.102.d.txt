With the foundations and design of our palace decided upon, we must now begin the process of deciding what the various rooms and chambers of the palace shall contain. The Emperor must deliberate on the many options available. A royal library, something to rival a vast collection of books? A great ballroom, to entertain the nobility and keep their politicking under the Emperor's watchful eyes? Laboratories, so the greatest scientists of the domain can collaborate and push new frontiers of research? Apartments, so the realm's noble families can dwell within and be reminded of who they serve? A vast bazaar, surpassing that of other great cities, so the Emperor can get a taste of all the wondrous goods that make their way to and from the domain each day? Dedicated stables, as a training ground for the Empire's finest cavalrymen and a house for its finest equines? A royal treasury, to hold and manage the endless wealth flowing from the Empire's vast dominion? A temple, open to all, so we can bask in the glory of the deity and offer Him the praise He is due? We have room to construct more wing.
