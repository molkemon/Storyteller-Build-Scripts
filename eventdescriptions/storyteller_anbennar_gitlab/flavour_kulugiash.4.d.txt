Death to Traitors. Glory to Our Heroes. Onlookers gazed in amazement as banners bearing these slogans were draped over the palace walls in the capital. In the dawn hours of today, while the ruler was away, a group of rebels led by Mkumo .Xe Iqhusye had seized the palace and instigated plans to form an army to depose the ruler and enforce Efergezmu authority. Bands of enthusiastic rebels, organized by members of the Efergezmu elite, who saw the ruler as a traitor caving to the interests of a foreign class, descended upon the capital to join the struggle.
