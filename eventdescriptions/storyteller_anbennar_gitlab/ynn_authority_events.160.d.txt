Today our great ruler met with their loyal Iosahar for a routine reason hardly worth mentioning. It was so routine and trivial that the ruler dealt with it so quickly they had quite some time left before they felt they had to leave. The monarch spent that time speaking with them all, especially those among them who were getting particularly angry at our benevolent rule. Our monarch’s silver tongue worked its magic and by the time they finally left that day, all the Iosahar in attendance found themselves less likely to start trouble.
