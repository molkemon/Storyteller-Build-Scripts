With the rise of artificery in our country, new devices are sprouting up every day from our enterprising inventors. Our position in the Deepwoods uniquely gives us easy access to magical components, either from the fey or more directly growing in the woods. Our inventions are sure to make a hefty profit if we spread them to our neighbors, eager as ever to get their hands on our goods. However, a group of advisors backed by some artificers have come to us asking to halt the burgeoning trade, allowing us to keep all of our goods for ourselves.
