The records in Khugdihr, the chronicles in Anbenncóst, and the old tales of the Silver Dwarves are brought together and compiled by the brightest and most adventurous minds of our domain. The resulting knowledge, unfortunately, is heavy on mythic weightiness and light on actionable dragonkilling steps. Apparently, many bold adventurers fought bravely and were torn apart; the domain was heavily damaged. The battle went on for several days, and somehow, magic turned the tide. Disappointed but undeterred, the scouts, historians, and researchers who came together to track this knowledge down resolve to stay together until they have solved the mystery of dragonslaying, forming a new order. the Vaultwardens. They will seek out every possible advantage in magic, technology, and magical technology. Moreover, they will crack open every book and vault they can find to hasten the defeat of their draconic nemesis.
