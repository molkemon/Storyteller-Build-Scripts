Aelantier is a land known for its chaos. Its modern history is defined by its apocalyptic destruction over a thousand years ago. For most of the period since then, the lands we now encompass never settled away from this chaos. raiders and warlords dominated Taychend, the kheions were bombarded by the Kaydhano, and even the city of Ameion was once humble, a riverside patch of dirt settled by the losing faction of a war. In more recent times, we took it upon ourselves to end this chaos, to restore the peace our ancestors maintained for millennia. Those who found comfort in the disorder resisted us, afraid of the bright future we had in mind, but just as eyes adjust to daylight after time indoors, they found themselves living lives of comfort their grandparents could not comprehend. Even still, we are but one corner of Aelantier. The chaos has not been vanquished in the Effelai to our north, the Kaydhano still rages, and many of the Elves reborn in the Ruin find themselves subjugated by powers who have no right to these lands. Our victory stands regardless, for we have made ourselves the bedrock of Aelantier, the foundation from which the Ruinborn shall rebuild the legacy our ancestors destroyed.
