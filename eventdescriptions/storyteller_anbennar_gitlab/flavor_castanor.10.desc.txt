The time has come; to keep up with our expanding conquests, we must proclaim a new Legion. Will we harken back to the Castanor of old, and revive one of the old Legions? Dragonflame, Sword, Shield, Giantsbane; each one legendary in their own right. Or will we forge a Legion anew, and fashion our own legends? Legions do not cost professionalism to hire. Additionally, they have a fixed maintenance and do not interact with the normal mercenary cost and maintenance modifiers.
