Among the wandering swordsmen of Yanshen, none are more feared or more elusive than the silent assassins who walk in shadow. These figures of legend dwell far from courtly light, yet when chaos brews and banners rise, their blades often determine who lives to see the dawn. Now, in our desperate resistance against a tyrant or worse, a monster, one such figure has arrived. Without herald or escort, he appeared in the heart of court. No greetings. No requests for audience. Only one message. "I will deliver your enemy's head." He asks for little—some minor tools to ease the path and a humble reward. Yet it is precisely this matter-of-fact tone that lends weight to his offer. Whispers swirl. Can such a man be controlled once let loose? Or is he the perfect tool to fight shadow with shadow, blade with blade?
