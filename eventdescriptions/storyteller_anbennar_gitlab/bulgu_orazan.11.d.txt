Should shamans be forced to practice abstinence? From an orkish perspective the historical answer is obviously no, but various religious and philosophical organizations have their clergymen practice abstinence and claim it leads to a more fruitful environment for them. This conversation would not be important if it were not for the fact that the daughter of a prominent noble in the capital got pregnant from the shaman who was supposed to be teaching her the Songs of her heritage. The noble was livid and has put the nation on alert; others have come forth with similar stories. What does the ruler think?
