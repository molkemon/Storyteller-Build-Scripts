White snow, in front of me, behind, around, above, below, everywhere; forever. When will it end? The expedition soon found itself beset by a terrible ceaseless blizzard, lost deep within the Dalairey Wastes. Curse whoever thought we should head further in. Huddling beside fires, the expeditionaries spit bitter curses at each other, blaming each other for their fate. The only thing certain. an impossible decision must be made if the expedition is to survive another day.
