Though their numbers can increase exponentially when dead bodies are plentiful, ghouls require the flesh of the deceased to sustain their bodies, and will begin to break down if they have not been able to feed in more than a fortnight. Either due to overconsumption or proper disposal, the lack of readily available corpses in the realm has deprived the creatures of their food, and they are far less numerous than they used to be. Many have starved, their desiccated corpses found by patrols, while others have left the region for better hunting grounds.
