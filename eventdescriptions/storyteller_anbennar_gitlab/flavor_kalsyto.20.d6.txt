Citizens of the blessed federation. It is with a joyous heart that I can proclaim that we, once and for all, can look upon this world content. Look upon yourselves, and see the many faces of your fellow citizens. How diverse those who now stand side by side with you, see their smiles as they revel in true liberty. True democracy. Freedom may not have come cheap. It is an investment, one we all so diligently worked towards. And now we reap the fruits of our labour. And what a reward. Citizens of all creeds and walks of life, united under one banner, one system. A true Federation.
