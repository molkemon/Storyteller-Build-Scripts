The entire hold is abuzz with excitement. Just earlier, historians, theologians, and archeologists rushed into the palace to discuss an unimaginable find. The Granite dwarves of old had also been followers of the Dwarven Pantheon, but even more, they were one of the few holds where that faith was still tolerated after most of the Dwarovar converted to Ancestor Worship. This means that they follow in one of the most noble traditions of dwarven culture now, and they will rebuild the faith in the name of their forefathers.
