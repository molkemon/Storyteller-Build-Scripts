A typical Trivu, a kobold family or tribe, specializes in a specific craft or profession, but all take on a bit of the burden of leading and organizing kobold communities. The typical organization calls for each settlement to be administered by a single Trivu which determines the overall direction that settlement will develop and specialize in, but the growth of large cities has led to a sense of prestige, as some Trivu govern far larger cities than is traditional. Now, our attention is pulled to the capital of the province, where three Trivu have distinguished themselves and are petitioning for leadership of the growing city. The Inkclaws specialize in construction, and have already been assisting with the city's infrastructure, designing and implementing new road and sanitation systems. The Broadnose Trivu has submitted a proposal for ways to improve local manufacturing, which they claim their own crafts have already proven the efficacy of. The Quickdagger Clan is a garrison Trivu, and while they are a newer group with a lack of experience, they are fiercely loyal and assure us they can maintain order within their city.
