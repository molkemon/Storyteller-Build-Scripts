Seeing a firebird at dusk is considered a good luck charm, its blazing radiance delaying the encroachment of the dark for just a few minutes more. Kings and emperors alike have attempted to keep lesser firebirds as pets across the centuries, with varying degrees of success due to the sheer magical output that they can put out. What we have that they did not, however, are specially engineered porcelain cages; cages that are able to contain and modulate the energy that they release. Perfect to use as a type of engine for our airships, which can make use of the heat and fire directly to control the buoyancy of the gases that keep them afloat.
