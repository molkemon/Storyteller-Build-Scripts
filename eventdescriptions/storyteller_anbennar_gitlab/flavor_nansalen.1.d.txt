Finally, for the first time in centuries, we can stand tall, with no overlords to bow to, and nobody to answer to except for Surael and each other. Yet, while this has been an ordeal, it is not the end of our journey; it merely marks the end of the beginning of our resurgence. Now, we must focus on consolidating our rule and restoring what we once held, and then push beyond to finally fulfill our Divine Duty and bring Surael’s divine Order to all of Bulwar. It will not be easy, but with destiny, Surael, and our kin in Akalšes by our side, nothing will stand in our way.
