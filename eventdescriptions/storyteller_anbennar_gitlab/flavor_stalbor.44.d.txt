Another recent addition to the cosmopolitan Stalbórian society are the ogres. Most come seeking a way out of the constantly repeating cycles of gluttony and starvation that grip their austere homelands beyond the Serpentspine, but some of them hailing from even farther origins in the far east or south come for a more spiritual type of nourishment. We are happy to fulfill both needs, as their ogrish strength has turned out to be a valued contribution to our growing industries.
