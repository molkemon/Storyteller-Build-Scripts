The night watch of the capital has long been considered as sorry as one could find in the city. The monarch still recalls one of their advisor's morose chuckle when he detailed their makeup in a recent defense budget meeting. a drunkard at the helm, subordinates including an obese oaf, a spotted miscreant who may not even be human, and a tall redhead possessed of the delusion that he is a dwarf. But tonight, they do not seem a joke in the same way. Just as the monarch had dragged their victim into an alleyway and begun to feed, at their most vulnerable, torches appeared at the alley's mouth, and the motley crew of uniformed men it illuminated sparked a feeling very unfamiliar to the predator. fear.
