It's just a book. How hard can it be to read a book? I've read hundreds of books, thousands. But I can't read this one. Something stops me. Something crushes me when I try. I can barely open the cover for the thought of how it'll feel to try to read it again. And yet, something whispers to me of the mastery over the land I would gain if I could ever read it. Wouldn't that be worth it? If it could revolutionize all of our industries and let the Titanium stand tallest among all holds?
