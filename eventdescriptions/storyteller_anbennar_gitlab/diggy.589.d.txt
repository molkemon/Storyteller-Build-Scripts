Since the fall of AulDwarov, wild herds of rothé have been grazing in the vast fungusrich caves of the Serpentspine touched by Halanna's Breath. Unlike their domesticated ancestors, wild rothé are larger and have stronger hooves, which often makes them difficult to domesticate. According to the inhabitants of the province, the crops of serpentbloom have attracted the attention of a peculiar herd of rothé that seems to have retained some of the characteristics of their domesticated ancestors. While they could hunt this herd down for their meat and hides, some of the locals have also suggested that they could try to domesticate this herd, although domestication could be complicated due to the nature of these animals.
