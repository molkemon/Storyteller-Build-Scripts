With Zhiquian now firmly under our control, the monarch has determined that the two provinces must not be separated ever again. With uncharacteristic fanfare, the ruler has announced that the Undying Flame, traditionally a symbol of Jiantsiang’s defiance against all of our enemies, will be shared with a twin brazier installed in the High Temples of the city of Damaogong, binding the two cities together forevermore, as now our duty to protect our citizens extends to them as well. A grand festival has been ordered to commemorate this most sacred event, for so long the flame burns, our nation shall not fall.
