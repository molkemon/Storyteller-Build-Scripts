Ikomeye resents the lattice, just as the hound resents the one who yanks them by the chain out of traffic. Her pain is no longer felt alone; however maddening it is for her friends to understand her fully, it is also, somewhere buried deep within, a relief. Together, the Vile One and Cheni and she have almost unwound the entire Tetrahedron; unable to countermand or alter the Wish upon it, they have simply learned to read what it protects, peering through the layers of magic like well-cut glass. Ikomeye insists on only one thing; that if they are all to be joined, Umorozi should be, too. It is her attempt at petty cruelty, at forcing upon Umorozi something which she will not like. She has misunderstood. Umorozi joins the lattice as willingly as she took on the fog, as willingly as she allowed the Vile One to remake her. Umorozi believes in the Vile One, and she is the first to form the bond voluntarily. A ticking over, a regular drum. This is how Umorozi thrums along the connection, what she gives to the network. Cheni is where peace lives, Ikomeye a place to scream, and Umorozi a place to work. They are a lattice of four. With Umorozi’s discipline, their focus is ever further honed. The last records of the Tetrahedron lie open to them all; they need merely be unravelled.
