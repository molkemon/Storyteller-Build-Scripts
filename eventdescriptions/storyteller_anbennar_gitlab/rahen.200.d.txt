The Dhenbasana is littered with palaces. Some big, some small. Some beautiful, some functional. Some newly purchased by a successful merchant, others dating back to before Ramapalar’s reunification. And all too many are sadly unfinished. Perhaps an engineer died to a flash flood, taking with him key information. Perhaps a benefactor was convinced in the Hall of Endless Debate to devote his funds to another purpose. Perhaps the builders and their patron were all lost to the Smothering Sickness, like so many in Rahen. But today, in the realm, one such palace was finished. Long after most had given up on it, it now is a glittering monument to glory, something to rival the Golden Palace itself. A blondhaired Copper Dwarf, hailing from the faraway Hold of Ovdal Tûngr, stumbled upon plans drawn up by since departed masters of their craft. And with a keen eye and unflagging spirit, he set to work. From laying bricks to emplacing ornate decorations, slowly that ancient vision became reality. All hope seemed lost when he grew ill, and left for treatment with herbal tonics that could only be found in Parusad Bhola. But a Harimari, unique for dying his coat green and for his unmatched strength, took up the torch with a spirit and fervor to rival the dwarf’s own. The palace rose, and rose, and rose. With a little help in its final decoration from a human mage new to the region, the palace became a veritable work of art, standing above and beyond its brethren. And so the dream of so many, over so many years, has finally been realized.
