The followers of Saamir will now only control those isles below the ground, as fertilizers for the crops; the lucky ones will toil for us. As word spreads to the old world that we have fertile land, an ample amount of slaves, and we are not killing each other as the packs on the mainland do, more migrants than usual make the journey and begin aiding the settling effort. There are rumors that some Cannor Gnolls are even joining our ranks.
