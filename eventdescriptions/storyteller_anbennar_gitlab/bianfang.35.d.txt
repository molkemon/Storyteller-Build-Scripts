Word has reached the monarch. a clique of palace eunuchs has assassinated the consort. Aiming to elevate a member of the monarch’s expansive harem to be primary consort, they strangled the consort with a silken rope in their quarters as they dressed for an elegant banquet. Having been promptly seized by the palace guard, the clique beseeched the monarch to spare them, claiming the consort had been slowly corrupting the monarch, placing their favorites in positions of power, and spreading their tyrannical influence over the entire realm. They were compelled to remove the consort for the good of the country. Their selfrighteous pleading was cut short only by the blade of the Palace Marshal, methodically decapitating them one by one. The monarch now faces the choice of what to do with the rest of the sorry lot. Proclaiming their innocence and begging for mercy, the remaining eunuchs would surely be loyal if spared, and unable to amass the influence which had allowed the clique to get away with such a brazen plan. However, the spilling of noble blood by lowly commoners such as eunuchs is a crime of the highest degree, and must be treated accordingly. Justice must be done, and be seen to be done.
