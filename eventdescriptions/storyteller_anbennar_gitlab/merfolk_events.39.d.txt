Spectators along the waterfront marvel at the arcane flares sparkling in the capital's skyline. Even the local guard, having rushed to the pier's edge in anticipation of potential danger, joins the rapturous masses. Center wave, a merfolk coils atop a spiraling funnel of sea water. Flicking his webbed fingers, a cloud swells and crackles with lightning, the children oohing at the shape formed—a whale. With another gesture, the whale cloud breaches through a smokey barrier, transforming into a glowing jellyfish that jiggles to a phantom tune. The sea sorcerer presents spectacle after spectacle, each grander than the last. After hours of performance, the merfolk’s spells explode into climactic sparkles, eliciting an uproar of applause. Smiling, the sea sorcerer enchants his voice with intelligibility and volume, thanking the crowd for honoring his arcane skill demonstration with their attention and requesting only their adoration or a small donation tossed into the harbor. With a final shimmering flourish, he sinks back into the sea. The crowd reacts by.
