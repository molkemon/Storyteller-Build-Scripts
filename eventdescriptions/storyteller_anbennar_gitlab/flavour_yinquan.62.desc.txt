It was critical to find every last one of the fox spirits, wherever they might have been, to return Huszien to being. If any of either the HuliJing or the Gumiho were not present for the final ritual, then She might even come back malformed, if the ritual even worked at all. Now that they have all been gathered together, however, the strongest members of the Maidens of Liho have come to begin the ritual to bring Her back into the world. As described by the spirits afterwards, a minor headache eventually became a pain so great they could hardly think straight. Their bodies strained against their rejoining, minds attempting to refind themselves within the new group mind. The room pulsated with mystical energies, only barely contained by a collection of some of the strongest mages in Haless. So great was the pull that, for a time, it seemed as if they themselves would be lost to the mass without shape. Eventually, the maidens prevailed, as the exhausted spirits finally accommodated their new gestalt consciousness. Atop them all stood the newly created Huszien. Still fractured, Her personality seems to be developing slower than Her body; it took six hours for Her to recognize the name Huszien, and She is highly suggestible. While presently being retaught about Her identity and how to function in society once more, it will be a significant sum of time still before She is the spirit that She once was.
