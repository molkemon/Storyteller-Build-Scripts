At the best of times, public works such as roads, bridges, and drainage require a great deal of effort to maintain, and these are clearly not the best of times in the province. The undead that haunt the land here have led many to abandon their homes and have made maintaining the infrastructure in the area impossible. Collapsing buildings, weedchoked canals, and dilapidated roads crisscross the land, leaving a lasting wound that will take years to heal; if it ever does.
