Taychendi warlords are notorious for following whichever way the wind is blowing. A single lost battle is often followed by a wave of defections, as warlords seek power at the side of those most likely to hold it. Though we continue to resist him, the Kamrayakval has won many, many battles, and those warlords who fight for us are increasingly leaving our employ to join him, in return for simple oaths of loyalty and paper conversions.
