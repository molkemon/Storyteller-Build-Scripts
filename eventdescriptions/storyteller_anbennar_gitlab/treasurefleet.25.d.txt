The arrival of the Treasure Fleet is always an exciting day in the realm. Sailors from a faroff land, with their exotic manners and wild tales to tell, will always catch people's eyes. But this year the crew that disembarked has one additional fourlegged member that has sent the rumour mill of the harbour taverns into a frenzy. A centaur, member of a halfhuman halfhorse race roaming vast plains close to the homeland of the Treasure Fleet, apparently managed to sneak aboard one of the bigger ships. He was quickly discovered once the fleet set sail, for his love of the sea is not matched by his tolerance to it, nor his skill sailing on a ship. Even though he may be a terrible sailor, his naive enthusiasm charmed the whole crew, who are now asking them to give him a place in their navy, as they fear for his safety, should they return with him to the home ports.
