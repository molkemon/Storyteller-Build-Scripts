It started slowly, as most things do. Words and phrases, foods, various baubles and trinkets from the communities they stayed with. But over the years, the foreign influence on the Cagodics increased. Marriages between the Cagodics and others resulted in children of two worlds, and some of them chose to stay behind in the villages, towns, and cities that had captured their heart. As for the remaining nomads. clothing, art, music; every stop changing them, little by little. Eventually, they stopped speaking of their ancient oath with Castanor the same way, with duty and a longing for a chance to fulfil it once more. To them, it was just something their ancestors believed in. And now, so many years after they came, the Cagodics have ceased to be more than one of our own with a curious origin story.
