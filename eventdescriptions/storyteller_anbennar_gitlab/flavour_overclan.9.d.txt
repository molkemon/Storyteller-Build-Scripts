A local shepherd family of the Graysheep clan has earned notoriety for selling high-quality clothes. While their exact methods are currently unknown, it is clear that their skills with turning wool into textiles are beyond comparison in this territory. This family business has also earned a reputation for servicing clients of all races, and even offering garments fitted to varying physiologies. It is believed that, with government assistance, these shepherds could greatly expand their business, stimulating the economy and providing the state with a source of superior textiles.
