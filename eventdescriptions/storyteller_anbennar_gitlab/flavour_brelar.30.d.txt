The expansion of voting specifically targeting those voters resident in the subsidiary republic of Drozmasrecat has raised the concept of integration to the Hill's Confluence. It is a certainty that the Dragon Republic has proven more than capable in its own administration, and the point of integration is agreed to lie in the voice of the rzentan people, who despite the vast expansion of voting rights still remain a step removed from the Confluence compared to other citizens. Therefore, it is necessary to consider whether to finally unite the Northern Ynn under one state despite the grumblings of the Drozma Tur clergy.
