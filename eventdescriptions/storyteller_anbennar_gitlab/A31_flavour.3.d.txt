The elves of Moonhaven have initiated an infrastructure program to further improve the development of eastern Dameria and erase the last traces of the devastation left in the wake of the Lilac Wars. With their initial efforts already resulting in a marked improvement, they have offered to send some of their experts to our realm so that Istralore may as well benefit from their work. This gracious gesture of amity will not only improve our lands and benefit our peoples, but reaffirm the ancient bonds that tie our two realms together.
