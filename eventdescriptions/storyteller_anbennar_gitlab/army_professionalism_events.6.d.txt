Our drillmasters stand in awe of the professional armies and the methods they have so successfully employed to organize army routines and practices. We are not the only ones who have taken notice; professional officers are in high demand in many countries. Of course, the state form of that country does not take kindly upon those of its officers that would seek employment abroad and would likely disapprove if we tried to entice any subject of theirs. That does not mean, however, that there are not individuals who would be interested in such a career opportunity, if we made it worth their while.
