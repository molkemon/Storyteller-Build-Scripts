Under the light of the moon he struck. With his scales flickering under the fires he lit below, the forces of the country were terrorized through the night by the Bronze Dragon. This was no indiscriminate destruction though. Aakhet's rage is not a mindless fury, but a deathly strike of precision into the heart of his enemies. One by one, each general leading the fierce forces were set alight by Aakhet, before pulling out of his fiery dive and soaring back into the sky, onto his next foe.
