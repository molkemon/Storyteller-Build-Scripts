Dhenijansar has fallen, and with it the Harimraj. Across the lands of Rahen, former Prabhis and Senaptis now stand as independent states, without the old ruler to tie them down. However, as much as things change, things stay the same, and across Rahen, various Raheni and harimari states are at each other's throats, once more vying for supremacy over their corner of Rahen. However, with Dhenijansar in our hands, although we make ourselves a large target, they have no chance of defeating our mighty armies. One thing is for sure, however. Rahen will never be the same again.
