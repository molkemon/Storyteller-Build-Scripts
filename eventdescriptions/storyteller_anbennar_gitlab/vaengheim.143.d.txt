Then did Vængheim’s host come upon murals writ in ancient hand, and artworks drawn on wall and floor and ceiling, of a world now long gone. And Freydis Oaktongue did beseech the monarch thus, “Wait, o queen. Let us stay here for a spell. For these mosaics are of expert make, and tell a tale too long forgotten. For old voices sing from the dust in this place, and it would behoove us to take the time to hear them.” Then did the monarch answer.
