We have shared a long and intertwined history with our northern brethren, a history of trade, alliance, brotherly sacrifice and enduring friendship, forming an unbreakable bond between our people. Now, ten years after we sent the delegation to our northern brethren, they welcome this union even more warmly than us. With a joyful spirit, the common folk eagerly await the day when they will unite with us under the guiding light of our monarch. Their population no longer answers to their leaders, but to ours. This momentous occasion holds the promise of mutual growth and prosperity as we move away from being Keyolíoni and Empkeiosi and pursue our common destiny as one; as Theínós.
