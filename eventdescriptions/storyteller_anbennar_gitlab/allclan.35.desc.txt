The time has come to settle the age-old question of warfare. how does one best turn their enemy into a fine paste? Our noble Pentapandemoniac Prefectoriums have come forward with their respective cures for life. The Clodhoppers recommend simple arachnid-themed violence, allowing their mounts to feed on the corpses of our enemies, all the while wearing goofy little cowboy hats. This is amicable. The Harmaceuticals recommend eco-friendly and cost-effective chemical weapons, the effects of which range from spontaneous combustion to spreading highly contagious diseases among the enemy infantry. This is horrifying. The Discombobulatorium, unsurprisingly, have suggested artificer-produced explosives as the best course of action. This is fun.
