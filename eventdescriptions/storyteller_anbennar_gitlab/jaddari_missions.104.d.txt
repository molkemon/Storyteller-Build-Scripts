We Desert Elves are well used to withstanding intense temperatures and sun exposure, but not every land trains a people as well as the Salahad. A long summer has led to a smattering of drought across the Empire, crops wilting in the heat and even the occasional forest fire breaking out. It has been suggested that the Divine Herald, Surael's own voice, visit one of the affected areas. We must ensure our people know this is simply a reminder of Surael's might, rather than any indication of His displeasure. Where shall the Herald grace with a visit?
