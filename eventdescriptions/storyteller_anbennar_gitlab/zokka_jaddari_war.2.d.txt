During personal combat, Zokka the selfproclaimed Devourer of Suns has been slain by Jaddar Jexiszuir. His horde turned into a mass of routing gnolls as the pack fell apart. Many surrendered to this new overlord who destroyed the one that unified them. While many fled, elven troops moved in with little to no resistance. Along the Suran, the oppressed human population of Hašr have declared their freedom and sought protection under their liberators.
