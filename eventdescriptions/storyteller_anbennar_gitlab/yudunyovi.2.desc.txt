The focus of the Triunic settlers has become focused on the religion of the old inhabitants of the steppes. They tell tales of the old world and the old ways gathered from the Ustiq’lik, grand monoliths of unknown origin whose bodies are inscribed with the tales of the ancient Zabatlari. The tales are whispered to them by a voice in their dreams; the name Soiti has been rediscovered upon the Ustiq’lik and given to this speaker.
