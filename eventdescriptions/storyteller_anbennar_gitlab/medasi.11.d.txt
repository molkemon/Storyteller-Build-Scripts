With the passing of the Medaka, the Satrapal Council of Gomuvuri has convened to decide who shall inherit the throne. Our two candidates are the Efergezmu of House Asigareg, descendants of one of Kui's great Irsukuba allies who have since embraced Vurebindu culture, and the Medawor of House Benrba, known as close friends of the Aramoole battlemages.
