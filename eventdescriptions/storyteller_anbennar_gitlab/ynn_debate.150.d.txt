A proclamation has come down from a certain country in favor of accepting the Corrupted Ruinborn as equals along the currents of faith. They state that although the savages are not capable of civilization like true Ynnics of the former empire, that does not preclude them from grace. All life flows from the Ynn, and so all life is precious to its worship. Even common animals deserve some protection, and the Corrupted are no exception. Even more so as they are able to learn the language, though crudely, and, though they foolishly worship animal spirits and runic inscriptions, this is only the misguided interpretation of their own zodiac, and they can be saved from their beliefs.
