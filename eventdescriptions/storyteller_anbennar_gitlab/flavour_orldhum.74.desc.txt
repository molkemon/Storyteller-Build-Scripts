The rediscovery of Aelantier has fueled reclamation efforts, providing a new horizon for expansion and untold riches. During this time, the cost of labor has drastically increased and there is always a need for more workers. Fortunately, the monarch of Haraz Orldhûm has extended a golden, no, a platinum opportunity. For a price, we can bolster our colonization efforts with an influx of slave labor provided from the Serpentspine.
