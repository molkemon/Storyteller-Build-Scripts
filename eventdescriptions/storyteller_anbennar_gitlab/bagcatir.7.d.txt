Eordand is a magical place truly, like our precursor mages are numerous; almost everyone can do magic to some degree, and for most of it, our magical citizens seemed satisfied enough. But recent expansions are challenging that. The recently acquired mage guilds living in the ancestral cities are more numerous and organized than any we ever had to deal with; some of them even used to rule a nation themselves, and they are not happy that power is reserved to merchants. If we do not do anything about it, we might soon find our livelihood turned into craters.
