The cooperation with gnomish weapons engineers and especially personnel of the Gommo guild has had a large positive impact on the technological and industrial capabilities of our great city. From the beginning, research and production of artillery were a main focus of the cooperation, and our newest guns are undoubtedly the envy of all Haless. The Gommo has now presented a proposal for further expansion of the project by founding a fully fledged Gommo Artillery Division based in a specific domain. Funding this organization could allow us to achieve a higher quality in production than ever before, making bigger and stronger armaments a certainty. A refusal could however lead to the Gommo searching for more willing partners elsewhere, and one day we might see their guns turned against us.
