The Dragon Cult reveres wealth, but it is only willing to dole it out on one condition. that they are repaid with interest. Prominent members of the Dragon Cult throughout our clan's realm have begun offering high-interest loans to those who do not have enough wealth to set up their own businesses. "We spend money to make money, and then spend money to make money, before spending in order to" one of these lenders begins to explain, causing the monarch's eyes to immediately glaze over. This practice appears to be here to stay.
