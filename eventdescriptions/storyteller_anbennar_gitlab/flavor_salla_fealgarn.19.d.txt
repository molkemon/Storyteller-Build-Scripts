Rúibí and the rest of her team slowly ascended the small hill overlooking the portal. It was an ancient thing, mostly covered in moss and roots from overground trees on a long forgotten raised stone platform with strange markings. Rúibí could already see some Warped prowling on the sides of the portal. She quickly dispatched Dorca to quietly take them out while Soala provided cover for Eira and her. Are the other teams in position? Eira asked as she readied her rapier. Rúibí peered over the small fallen tree they were behind and noticed Mlyn off in the distance shining a small piece of metal toward her. Yes, but this all seems too easy. I don’t have a good feeling about this. Rúibí worried. This was a trap, no doubt this of course was a trap. The Unthorned Queen was tactical, cunning, ruthless; how could she ever think, no, even dare, Rúibí? Eira harshly whispered, putting a hand on her shoulder. Calm. Take a deep breath. Rúibí did as Eira told and her breaths slowed. What is our plan, leader? Rúibí thought for a moment. Either they could take the portal right now, while it seems like the Unthorned Queen is unprepared, or they come back with some local help and more reinforcements. What was she to do?
