There is a gentleman in the realm preaching to the people that their home would be better off free from the clutches of the imperial imperialism and that the entirety of the region should embrace self-governance. While many regarded him at first as a harmless nuisance, the number of followers he has gained is rapidly growing, and some members of our government worry that eventually, this founding father will incite a violent rebellion in the realm in an attempt to break away from the imperial Empire.
