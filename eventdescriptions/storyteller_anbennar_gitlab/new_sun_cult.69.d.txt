Our tactics have been passed down for several centuries now, ever since the days of Jaher's Phoenix Legions. However, our armies are not identical to those legions, and the times and technologies of war have changed in the past three hundred years. New tactics will mean a fresh perspective on battles, and hopefully an effective one that will surprise our enemies. We have three promising options. The old guard in our military say that defensive tactics would suit the terrain of our lands the best. Let the monsters shatter against our walls and shields. The vanguard believes that to beat the monsters you must be faster than them. They propose reordering and training our armies to be deployable anywhere at a moment's notice in smaller, better trained groups. The last option is to rely on sheer volume of bodies on the field. With the humans rushing in a massed assault on the enemy, they will be too busy defending against those numbers to react to our more skilled elven troops coming in from the flanks.
