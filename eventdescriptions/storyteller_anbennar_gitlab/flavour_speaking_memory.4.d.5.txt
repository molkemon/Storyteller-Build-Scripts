One of our people's favorite stories is that of Kaikal, Mother of Stars, a great hero who lived long before the Day of Ashen Skies. Known for having learned the songs of the winds, tides, and stars, she serves as an inspiration to our people; a model of what a good life looks like. Recently, though, some of our people have started to doubt if she actually existed. Is it really possible for a human to have sung to the stars with such compassion that they agreed to descend upon Halann and live beneath the waves? How can we know the truth about events that happened so terribly long ago? We already sent an expedition to ask the Dragon Turtles, of course, but they refused to answer any questions about Kaikal, or even to explain why they abstained. It appears that we must solve this problem ourselves.
