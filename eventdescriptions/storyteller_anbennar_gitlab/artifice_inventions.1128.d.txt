Reshtic energy, named for the Bulwari term reštu meaning primordial, is a particular form of energy found in true abundance in the elemental planes, but only rarely on the material plane. Only in locations connected to the elemental planes via planar gates, such as Bulwar, can it be properly utilized. Unlike arcane workings, which can be fueled by a dame's tear, reshtic artifacts employ the ambient reshtic energy of their surroundings to power their function. After many failed attempts, a new branch of artificery, reshtic artificery, has reached a level of sophistication that allows for the construction of reshtic machinery. Such devices are capable of working indefinitely by drawing on ambient reshtic energy instead of relying on arcane batteries or other conventional power sources. This way, both mining operations and the manufacture of a variety of goods have become a much less labor-intensive affair, significantly improving production output wherever these advanced reshtic machines are deployed.
