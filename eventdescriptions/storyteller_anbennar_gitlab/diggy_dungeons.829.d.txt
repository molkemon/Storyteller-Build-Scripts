The path ahead was clear and straight, without any major problems. Only after some time did the walls of the labyrinth reveal cracks, and after the next corner, the passage was closed. This did not stop us; with pickaxes, we started demolishing the wall next to us. With the wall removed, we continued onwards into the next corridor. Strangely enough, some kind of hoarding creature must have made its lair here, for gold and gems lay strewn across the floor.
