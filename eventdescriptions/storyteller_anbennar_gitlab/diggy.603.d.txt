Norkers look like Goblins, walk like us, often speak our language, but they are lazy, selfish, arrogant, and uncultured, everything we true Goblins are not. Yet, it seems that a group of Norkers in that province have reorganized under the leadership of a particularly Goblinlike Norker, who now seeks recognition and our permission to lead his people under our rule. While we cannot expect to see any serious gains from such lowly creatures, this could be a step toward making Norkers more useful in the long term.
