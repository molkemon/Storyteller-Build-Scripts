The life of a goblin. What does that mean? As our society advances and we learn more, not only into how things explode but into culture and history, we come to a crossroads. This world we find ourselves in is truly complex. The caves were not as complex. Surviving was hard, yes, but not complex. Here there is so much more than angry dwarves or angry orks. Sometimes there are angry humans and elves, too. As we move forward, we must decide how we interact with the world around us. What is a goblin in this world? How do we want to hold ourselves?
