Before our front line could clash with the goblins, our men fell down to wire traps. The ferals hastened to quickly club them to death. Confusion suddenly led to disaster. Against warnings, some of our companions broke their formation to help the trapped. This proved to be a big mistake, as they rapidly got surrounded by the enemy. To end this and avoid losing more men, we charged the goblins. We struggled as we clashed with them. Some of them fell dead as well as some of us. Many minutes that felt eternal passed until the remaining goblins retreated to small holes around. We took this opportunity. We fled forwards, leaving the rocky chamber and our deceased behind.
