The Oni of Haless are feared by their neighbors, due to the fell chi rites that they perform to survive. Their society is organised around four Shirgrii, ancient monastic fortresses and libraries, home to the Oni castes of scholars, monks, and warriors. These citadels are home to immense knowledge, and it is said that their walls once held back even the Precursors. Azaly, long disappointed by the woeful state of the municipal library in Bivuzena, has travelled to the Demon Hills to see the Shirgrii for himself. While bristling at only being allowed access to a small selection of the grand temple in the province, his genuine appreciation for the Oni’s immense scholarship and knowledge has gained him some favour, and he has drawn up several notes on how the academic structures in Bivuzena could be improved. He has also convinced several Oni scholars and mages to return with him, in order to teach classes to aspiring academics, in return for steering trade their way to keep them safe from the Hobgoblin Command, the Shuvüüshudi tribes, and the lords of Bianfang.
