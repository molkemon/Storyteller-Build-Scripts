The Ravelian Society was a group of Cannorian academics and reformists focusing on looking at the world through the lens of logic and superstition. Nowadays, however, they seem to have been taken over with a renewed zeal seen only in the most devout of clerics, perhaps due to the influence of a precursor artifact under their study known as the God Fragment. The God Fragment was discovered right here in Aelantier, and now, many members of the Ravelian Society are flocking back to start lodges and research outposts in Aelantier. Accepting them may eventually cause upheaval in our colonies, though their links with the wealthy of Cannor may provide great boons for the development of our country.
