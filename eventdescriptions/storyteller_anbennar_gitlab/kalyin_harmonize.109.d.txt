Kalyin created everything and is everything; nothing is outside of her. This is the One Truth, and it has always been our goal to seek out every reflection of Kalyin on Halann. During our search, we have encountered many new races and many different beliefs, one of which is something known simply as The Hunt. Mainly practiced by the harpies, most would consider it a philosophy rather than a religion. Regardless of what its true nature might be, we decided to try and see what Kalyin wanted to teach us when inspiring the harpies to develop the Hunt, and we discovered a lesson that would forever aid our mission. According to the harpy philosopher Nehijunus, “The Hunt is nothing more than the way harpies live; nothing less than looking clearly at the world.” The Hunt also knows a principle called “mantalqo,” which roughly translates to ‘flying above without resistance,’ which encourages syncretism with other faiths in search of answers to an individual’s questions. With this principle in mind, it is quite possible that the key to finding and understanding Kalyin’s many different faces might be to simply take a step back and face Her with a clear mind and untroubled heart. We should also remember that the hunter is not alone and serving only themself. We are part of a greater community, and all work together in pursuit of a shared goal. We all serve the greatest possible purpose. the hunt for Kalyin.
