O Ascending Dragon, let not the chattering creatures shake your coming roar. It is not exactly a secret that most Acolytes consider mundane, everyday people who have not been initiated into the arcane mysteries to be little better than animals. Nevertheless, as a farmer may be particularly cruel to his livestock, so too has the realm become unusually cruel to its subjects. Magic is freely wielded as a tool of domination by those capable, and agents of the state are empowered to summarily execute any uncooperative creature who stands in the way of the swift completion of policy objectives. This black day mars the reputation of the Acolytes among the common people, but intervention would seem improper. It seems to all the world as though these souls will be left to their fate of suffering and subjugation.
