Our grand ambitions now lie in tatters, put to the torch by the vile gnolls of Tluukt who have made abundantly clear our folly for entrusting them as allies of Zokka's will. The sands of the Salahad laugh at us as we flee in fear from Tluukt's rancor, our tails firmly between our legs. The sun that once we sought to devour now only sneers at us from its impossibly lofty seat in the heavens. We have failed, and whatever hopes for the future we may once have held are now certainly doomed.
