It almost seems effortless and inevitable to become rich with the Marrhold Tunnel under our control. Our officials at the border barely have to lift a finger and money keeps trickling in; as long as the machine that is our economy is well-oiled, there is nothing to do but reap the benefits that we have sown with the reopening of the tunnel. The dues at the tunnel entrance and exit alone have long repaid the enormous cost of the tunnel's construction, so this venture has been declared a full and utter success.
