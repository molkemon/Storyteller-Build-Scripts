With all the Fasíri people unified under the banner of the boar, our leader Aósiki calls us to war against the Kheionai, who threaten our realm to the east. With our fearsome boarriders, we will dethrone the people who call us Eltibhar, seeing us as mere barbarians whom they can crush with no effort whatsoever. Our people have not suffered through the Great Clan War to be subjugated by a foreign force who has no interest in our prosperity. Today we ride for our freedom. Our banners will fly on every stronghold, for we are the true rulers of Alecand.
