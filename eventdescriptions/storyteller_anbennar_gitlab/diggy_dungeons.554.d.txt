Hairs on the back of necks stand up and muscles stiffen as another guttural roar echoes throughout the cavern. Despite the mass of pipes and stone between them and the approaching entity, everyone can feel its piercing gaze upon them once again. The expedition leader's voice falters and cracks uncharacteristically as he organizes a last-ditch defense. With the elemental approaching at a blistering pace, they can only huddle together and clutch their weapons in fear.
