May Surael’s Light shine upon you. When the hordes of Zokka, thricecursed be his name, descended upon the banks of the Suran, you sent emissaries, you sent messengers, you sent your despairing subjects to the lands of my most noble predecessor, your benevolent father, pleading for deliverance. And as was his sacred duty, Eledas marched to your aid, yet to his lifelong regret his legions failed to shatter the vile horde. Despite their valorous sacrifice, they failed in their obligations on that most ill-fated of days, leaving you to the merciless depredations of the demon worshippers. Yet those dark days are at long last behind us all. Just as His light chases away the darkness each morn, I have shattered the vile might of Zokka Devourer of Suns, may he choke on his greed for all eternity. It now falls to us to restore the proper order in Surael’s Garden. Like a diligent father I shall not rest until the last of my lost children are once more safe under my protection. I entreat you to seek me out in radiant Sareyand and swear to me the oath of fealty you once swore to Eledas, so that we may be one family again, united and blessed in purpose. Do not let the whispers of the Dark sway you from your path, for only together may we fulfill our sacred duty to Surael. I, the monarch Sarelzuir, by the grace of Surael exalted ruler of Sareyand, say this to you, my beloved charge, and await your reply with paternal benevolence and patience.
