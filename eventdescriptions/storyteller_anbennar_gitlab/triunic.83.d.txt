When the Day of Ashen Skies shook the world, the Khantaar people of Khashaalg fled west through Nuzurbokh where they settled in Yarikhoi. They brought with them two things. first, their god, the Supreme Spirit, who would later come to be known as Enuuk; and second, a burning, fervent desire to build cities as great as the ones they lost. As a result, the very act of building is one way to celebrate their Goddess, and it is not uncommon to spot a priestess doubling as foreman in many construction sites throughout the land. As of late, there is a new zeal for constructing, but it is without direction at the moment.
