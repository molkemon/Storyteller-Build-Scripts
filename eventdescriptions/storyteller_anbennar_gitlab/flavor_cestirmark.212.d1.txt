The delegations stand together in ceremonious garb. One by one, they are called, and give the documents to the monarch. The monarch holds their breath, and pauses before daring to look at the answer every single time. In this fateful moment, a single nay would be enough to question everything. And yet, with the last diplomat, the monarch realizes there is but a single word on the papers laid in front of them. Yes. It is at this moment that the silence is broken and the assembly erupts into cheers. In all the bay’s capitals, the scene is broadcasted via magical holograms, and the cheers are taken up by the crowds. Today, the Trollsbay cheers as a whole.
