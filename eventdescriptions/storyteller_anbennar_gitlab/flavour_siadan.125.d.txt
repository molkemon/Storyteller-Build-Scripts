Though Siadúnan propaganda decried the Elaiénna as traitors to Harpykind for their deal with the Jaddari, the difficulties of the March have nonetheless resulted in some among us following in their footsteps. In one of our provinces, the local garrison has cut a deal with the population, marrying into the local elite and therefore placating them in return for alleviating taxes and tribute. While some in Siádan have decried this as shameful and traitorous, and unbecoming of true harpies, it has undeniably calmed the situation in the local area.
