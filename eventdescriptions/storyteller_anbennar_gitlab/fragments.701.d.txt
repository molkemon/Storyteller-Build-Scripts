The soul will be in the deepest part of the jungle. That is the theory we are operating on. Of course, none of us are really sure where the soul would be found, but it is a start, and I am sure we will get a few good stories out of it at the end. We will take a route towards the Leechdens, the thinnest part of the jungle and surely the fastest route to the jungle center, wherever that is. We have just reached the Leechdens, famed for the horrible leechmen who will drink a man's blood dry. The expedition does not want to stay here long, so Lisolette sil Ordóin, my second hand, has come with a suggestion. using the trees to construct crafts so we can sail through the Leechdens, minimizing our time here and thus the chances of any encounters. Rican Dreadmarsh, something of an optimist, suggests we could try to buy safe passage through the dens.
