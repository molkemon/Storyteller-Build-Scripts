First came Olokhar, and while his qualities were many, his flaw was also big, so much that his own empire collapsed under it. Second came Aldatam, the Reclaimer; his rise was slower but had better foundations. But those same foundations ended up betraying his heirs, each GreatBanner of the Ironblood Army fighting for its own pretender, following its power games instead of serving the state. Third came Harrihara; he vanquished every enemy that appeared in front of him, no matter if it had the power of a prophecy on his back or the skill to defy fate. Third came Harrihara; he reforged the Ironbloods anew, amending the mistakes of the past. Third came Harrihara, and there will not be a fourth.
