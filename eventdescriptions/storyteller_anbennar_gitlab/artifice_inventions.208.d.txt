Kobolds have always dreamed of flight, imitating their mythical ancestors the Dragons. Through the power of artificery, they can finally achieve this. Using a pair of magically strengthened fabric wings, imbued with large amounts of mostly reliable magic, the best that is achieved is only a limited form of flight, best used for descending upon enemies from the cliffs of their homeland. Refined versions are capable of sustained flight, but these are much bulkier than the standard wings that only allow short bursts of power.
