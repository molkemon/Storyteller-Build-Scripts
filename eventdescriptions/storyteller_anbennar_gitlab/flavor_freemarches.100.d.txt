From the log of Captain Taruk Freeorc, we set off up the river from Feranradh at first light this morning, with two hundred experienced Rudmen and a small collection of gnomish artificers. We are in search of the source of this "kinbond" that the Cursed Ones seem to have with each other. The gnomish leader, Obran Orannam, says that the Boek and Teira have legends of the Cursed Ones coming from the Mountains to the south of here. My brother Ratak is here with me; he knows these rivers well, and I am grateful to have someone I can trust at my side. He is good with the men. The Rudmen are nervous about going beyond their familiar rivers and are frightened by rumors of what they may do to those that trespass on their homelands. With two hundred experienced Rudmen, we should be able to pass, though I expect to encounter resistance at some point. We can already feel the eyes of the Cursed Ones from the forest, watching us.
