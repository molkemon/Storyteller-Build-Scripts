Tragic news has come from the Daulophs; Hiderion’s son, Tonan, has passed after an illustrious career of service. While the men under his command are naturally saddened by his loss, it is Hiderion who is heartbroken. Never is the curse of long life more evident than when an elven parent far outlives their half elven son. He has been seen only rarely at court these past months, and when he is present, it is in near total silence.
