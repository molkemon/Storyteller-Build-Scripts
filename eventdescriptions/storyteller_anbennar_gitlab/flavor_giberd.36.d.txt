Nothing good comes without a cost. This is true of many things in life, but it seems not even innovation is sacred, as the spendthrifts and merchants of the realm have sent a letter to the office of the monarch demanding that our precious and unique Wind Towers, which facilitate our trade across Esmaria, also be built in their nation, so as to ensure a fair balance of trade, which is of course a thinly veiled attempt to maintain their trading strength. Nevertheless, the harsh sanctions and economic might they wield means that ignoring them is absolutely not feasible for our nation’s prosperity; thus, the guilds have been assembled and three guilds have forged three solutions. The Technomancers argue there is no point not building the wind towers, saying they will figure them out eventually, and it keeps us on good terms. The Brillites argue that we should build the Wind Towers and make sure they send every ship straight to Giberd. The Mechanists suggest simply shooting the realmmen until they stop complaining.
