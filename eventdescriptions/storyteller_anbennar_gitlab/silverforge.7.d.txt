Within the past few weeks, we have been receiving couriers that tell of a dwarven adventuring band on its way to Silverforge Hall. This would not be a surprise given that the occasional band still sets out for the Serpentspine even now despite the initial rush having ended around the beginning of the fifteenth century. However, this band was not headed towards Escann, but from it. Then only a few days ago, a courier arrived from the band itself revealing them to be none other than Sevrund’s band, having set out a century prior. The bulk of the band arrived today with Sevrund himself now a strong but aged dwarf of one hundred thirty-seven. His hair is unbraided and cut short, unusual for a Silver Dwarf. He is unarmed but wears fine Silverforge plate armor, undented as a show of prestige despite the many scars beneath. This would not have been his battle armor. As the ruler walks out to welcome Sevrund in person, he is met with that unmistakable gaze, as Sevrund shouts out that one hundred years is too long away from his true home. They embrace as the ruler tells of the many stories Silverforge had heard of the "The Young Blood Band." "Only half true, and the wrong half at that." Sevrund bellows in laughter. What’s more, he tells that the remainder of his band is slowly transporting a large number of ork prisoners of war to our realm.
