We have made great improvements in developing spiritfire as controlled by a person, but our efforts in improving the implements of spiritfire manipulation have lagged in comparison. A stark example of this is the brazier used by our spiritfire monks in combat, currently a large, bulky contraption which either sits on the back or head of the warrior as they do combat. These braziers have proven to be effective, but they are clunky devices which require careful handling lest they topple from the soldier and into the dirt. We have many options for improving these braziers which have recently come to light. By eliminating most of the supporting metal framework, the brazier can still carry spiritfire while being much lighter and more maneuverable than the current version. Of course, this will be more expensive than our current method of spiritfire handling.
