The scene in the realm is one of nightmares not seen since the Greentide. Entire villages and towns have been razed to the ground, the invading army showing clear disregard for the lives of civilians or even reveling in their slaughter. With a viciousness unseen since Nichmer or Korgus, they have cut down civilians to the bone, sparing no man, woman, or child. Their army marches on, leaving only death in its wake.
