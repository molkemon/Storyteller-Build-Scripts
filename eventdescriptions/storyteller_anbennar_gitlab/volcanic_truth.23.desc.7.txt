The matter of the mechanism is decided. The mechanism might be an asset to the Volcanic Truth, but we must be wary. they know nothing of the new Insyaa, and if we allow them to follow their nostalgic whims blindly, they will undo centuries of change. The only way to prevent this is to teach them thoroughly about the doctrines of the Volcanic Truth as they awaken, so that they may understand the importance of embracing the changes that have occurred while they have lain dormant. Once they have come to know the Truth, they can go forth and share it with others. But now that we have decided what to think of the mechanism's past, the Volcanic Truth must look now to the future. The mechanism have a place within Insyaa, now and forever, and thus they shall live beneath the gaze of the Twin Gods just as we do. They will work towards change within themselves and within the wider world, as is their duty.
