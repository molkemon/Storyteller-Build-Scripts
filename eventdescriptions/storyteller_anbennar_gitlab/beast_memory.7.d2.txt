Wise, noble, and brave is the Leopard. Though its prey and foes may range far, the wise Leopard is always two steps ahead, motivated by its ferocious hunger. Though the enemy may try to get away, in the end, through cunning and calculation, the Leopard will always get what it wants. Though the prey may seem to gain the upper hand, though a foe may back the Leopard against a wall, through a strength born of intransigence it will neither bend nor break. Above all else, o monarch, emulate.
