Second only to the Elven Migrant Ships, the Behemoths are the masterpiece of Derannic naval engineering. A gigantic warship, a floating citadel carrying tremendous firepower that made the few already built the fear of every Cannorian navy. Our recent acquisition of the vessel’s plan, thanks to the invaluable work of our agents, has revealed the secrets of the Behemoth construction. Specialized shipyards and large amounts of resources will be needed, but soon the Behemoth will give us control of the sea.
