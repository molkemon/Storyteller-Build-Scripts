Greens, purples, yellows, oranges, reds. Mushrooms, vines, swampgrasses, lichens, grains. Giantheart trees inch towards the heavens before they are cut down for lumber and glue; crops grow without disease, perfectly adapted to the swamp; cattle graze on plentiful forage, happy and healthy before the slaughter. They grow everything they need for carpentry and consumption. and really, what else do they need? A beautiful bounty blossoms in the Vale.
