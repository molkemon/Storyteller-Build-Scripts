Indifference and fatigue. this is how most of our citizens would describe their feelings about refugees in our lands. This isn't necessarily for the worse; we tolerate each other, and that's a minimum if we desire to live in the same lands. In the past few years the situation in the refugee communities has evolved in accordance with this policy. While we can still try to influence proceedings and results of this evolution, it might prove costly. What shall be our direction?
