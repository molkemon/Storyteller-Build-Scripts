The messenger returned to the halls of Wineport, looking slightly harried. As he approached the monarch, the Wine Barons assembled chuckled amongst themselves as they saw that the messenger still had faint stains of lipstick on his face, and was putting up a conspicuous effort to hide his neck under his clothes. "Is that a good sign, or?" "Simply a part of Minari hospitality, as I am reliably informed," replied a baron to the other asking the question. The monarch stilled the room as they motioned for the messenger to deliver the news. "Ah, yes, of course, my ruler. I can at least say that the Minari were happy to see a messenger from your court," he says with a nervous chuckle. "But, as for their response to our gracious offer of wealth and jurisdiction over their secular affairs..."
