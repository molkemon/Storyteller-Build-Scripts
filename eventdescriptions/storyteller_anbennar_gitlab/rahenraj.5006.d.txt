Far from the capital, there lies a small village, visited today by a small and unarmed Harimari. The farmers there are busy tending to their crops, but even so, they could easily tear the interloper apart. And they might well have, given what he asked of them, if not for the fact that the visitor bore a mandate bearing the vizier’s own seal. Such is the reaction when you are told your husbands’ and sons’ kartya now leads them to battle. That they may not return for years. That the most you could do for them would be praying fiercely to Anjita the Warrior. The same scene repeats itself across the Harimraj, for the vizier has mustered the realm for war.
