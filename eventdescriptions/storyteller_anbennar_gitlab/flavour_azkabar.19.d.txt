The meetings of the Aršunaellu were becoming a regular facet of life for the powerholders of the associated realms. After all, there was always more work to be done, more disputes to solve, even as the earlier doubts and paranoia started to fade. This growing sense of safety and comradery was shattered as the ruler of Azkabar walked into the chamber, flanked by grinning clergymen. It only worsened as they spoke of their proposition. a centralization of the Aršunaellu around Azkabar, of course. These accords were nearly thrown out, before the Eclipse Cultists promptly put knives to the throats of the Kumarkandi and Zanbari leadership. The accords are signed, and the rebellious leadership goes home to plot, straight into the welcome arms of another mob of cultists. A tragic murder, caused by foreign and corrupt agents, seeking to destroy Bulwar’s best hope, of course.
