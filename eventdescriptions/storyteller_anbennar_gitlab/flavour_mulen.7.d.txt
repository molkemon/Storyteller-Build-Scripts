After many years, our scouting expedition has returned from the Forbidden Plains. Reduced in number and battered from hoofed charges, they nevertheless fought fiercely and used aerial tactics as best they could. The scouts report that the plains seemed to stretch endlessly to the western, eastern, and northern horizons, until they found a coast to the north. There they discovered a nearby settlement whose occupants swiftly spotted them and regarded the expedition uncertainly. They were not centaurs. They were humans.
