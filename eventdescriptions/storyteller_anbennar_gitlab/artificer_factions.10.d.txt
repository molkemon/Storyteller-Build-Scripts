These Technomancers. Do not trust them. They are conniving mages, but smarter, because they have caught on with the times. Indeed so. The many intrigues, plots, and half-plots of the Technomancers slowly but surely have paid off in the state. Eventually, you realize that a few Technomancers have appeared in place of officials who have recently resigned, suspiciously disappeared, or been banished over spelling mistakes in their theses.
