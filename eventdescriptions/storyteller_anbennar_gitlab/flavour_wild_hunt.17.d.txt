The question of how to best honor Irdaeos is a delicate one. Some say that it is done through a life well lived, others seek Irdaeos’ favor through grand rituals, and still others seek unity with Irdaeos through unity with others and their communities. Even within these sects, there are differences of opinion to the degree that any two centaurs will have two different opinions about how entry to the heaven of Ik Magthaal is obtained. This naturally causes a great deal of fighting and debate between the sects. If there is any silver lining to the failure of the Incursion, it is that they have done something unprecedented. They have unified all the sects, subsects, and individual practitioners of the land under one belief. their state has failed Irdaeos in every way it is possible to do so, and the reign of the current Caehn is his vengeance.
