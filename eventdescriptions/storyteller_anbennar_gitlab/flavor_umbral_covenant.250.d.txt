The bird emerges from the black of night, from the corner of the Vile One’s chamber. It is a thing of shadow itself, and where its wings flap, it clears the Vile One’s fog. The letter unfurls itself; there is no need to read it, for the words there inscribed are whispered directly into the Vile One’s mind, the voice of a Granny cold and absolute. “No disappointment to us still lives upon Halann, save one. Goodbye, stupid girl.”
