Attempting to find truth within the legend of the Gem of Fascination, which could bind a spirit to the material, has led us to Chien Binhrung. Perhaps that is no surprise; where else would a gem wind up, if not the home of a great jeweler? Thankfully, the gem was not added to one of Binhrung's most holy sites. the petrified queens of the Nha Nuhoanha temple. There, every queen who has ruled the country lies, their flesh turned to stone and studded with gemstones. Instead, it has wound up in the private collection of one of their wealthiest jewellers, who will undoubtedly part with such a treasure, for a fee, yes?
