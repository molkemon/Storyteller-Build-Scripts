In the years since Kylian the Sixth Siloreil and Lothane the First sil Wex forged their alliance against the Silmunas, relations between us and the Dove Throne have declined. Once allies, the Anbennarians now only hamper our ambitions, contest our rightful claims, and have proven to be far from the ally Emperor Lothane was. Our last war between us and the Empire found us victorious, but it appears that such conquests were a bridge too far for the Dove Throne. This time, the Emperor has called all the imperial banners to reclaim the west.
