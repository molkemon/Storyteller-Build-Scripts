A pristine bleached parchment lies on the desk, placed there by the hand of a close advisor. The humans have become so enraptured with our Sirens that they have begun paying their hard won gold in order to listen to the performances up close. Our expanding songhalls have spread this new business across the realm, and inspired by the Siren’s example many harpies have begun turning away from the sea and taking up singing as a profession. This has led to a shortage of recruits for the ships, and in response the Odanchésta have been press ganging foreign sailors into their crews, creating ever more disloyal and unruly groups causing trouble in our ports.
