My lord. You are not safe here. The monarch offered no reply; they simply walked forward, their gaze fixed upon the heavens. The burden of loss weighed heavy upon them, and heavier still was the burden of losing something irreplaceable. The pride of their goddess, their one true love, now lost. How could this have happened? The ruler walked along a corridor of sand and grass, flanked by the forest on the right and the Lovers’ Coast on the left. The monarch clasped their hands behind their back and looked up, where thousands of bright stars seemed poised to fall upon Halann, and a full moon—the embodiment of the Dame—gazed down upon Vernell in solemn silence.
