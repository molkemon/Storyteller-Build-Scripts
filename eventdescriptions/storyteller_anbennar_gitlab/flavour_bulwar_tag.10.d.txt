Everyone wants to see Tluukt dead, but nobody wants to actually plan out how that would happen. Eventually, the burden of planning was taken up by a member of the szalGivar family, whose energy for the project eventually managed to draw others in. Many of the specifics of the plan have been hammered out, but the planners have requested input from the council. They wish to see what we would think of how to kill Tluukt. We could contract mages, either Old Sun Cult fugitive magi or even elites from the Order of the Zenith; some would have us hire disgruntled pack leaders, seeking to oneup their current leader with the correct motivation. Each of these, for their merits and flaws, probably have around the same margin of success.
