In the middle of an otherwise normal dinner, there is a sudden shout. One noble’s deer, ripe for consumption, has suddenly exploded into a flock of birds, which have flown off into the air. The noble is covered in droppings, and he is not happy. From the treeline comes a roaring guffaw. A fey is doubled over in laughter, pointing at the noble, whose face twists in rage. He jumps from his seat and begins to storm towards the fey, but his compatriots pull him back. The fey, now slightly calmer, turns to the monarch with a raised eyebrow and a smirk. Clearly, they are wondering what the monarch thought of the spectacle.
