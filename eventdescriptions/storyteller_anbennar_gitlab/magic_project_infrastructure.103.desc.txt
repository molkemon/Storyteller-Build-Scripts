As we continue to build upon our magical institutions, the archmages of various magical colleges have come before the monarch while in the middle of a very heated debate with one another. As insults are hurled left and right and the audience chamber is showered with multicoloured sparks, the mages shout their demands for a new school to be built, each favouring his own magical practice. After more rounds of spellslinging, animal summoning, and charm-enforced silent treatments, the ruler finally steps in and ends the debate before someone is turned into a chicken. Stepping forward, the monarch declares a new school will be built for one practice only.
