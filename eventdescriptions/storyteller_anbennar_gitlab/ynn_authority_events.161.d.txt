Our ruler, blessed as they are by the Ynn, is not a diplomatic person. For one reason or another, they cannot help but offend all those around them with both their words and actions. Their reputation is well known, to all but themselves at least, and as such, in the interest of keeping our standing, we keep our gracious ruler on an unfortunately tight leash. They never walk too far away from our diplomats on official visits lest they say something that would cause an incident. However, during a recent meeting between them and the Iosahar, they seemingly slipped away from their handler. By the time we were able to get them back, the damage was already done. We do not know what they said or did, but by the anger on our loyal vassal’s faces, it must have been bad.
