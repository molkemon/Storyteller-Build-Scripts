Akaliza is one of the little Vitarogomo whose fame was not earned through fearstriking acts of violence. She began as an adventurer, helping common people when the warbands did nothing but take their taxes or pillage their livelihoods completely. She thrived, living in the moment, exorcising curses, culling off the local monsters and earning hefty sums from local traders. That was four years ago at this point. Before she took her sister’s life as a last bounty, taking over Degumwamba and later proving her worth to the mentor of her family, the magic talking frog Rufaro. Using favors she accumulated in her travels, she secured a seemingly nice headstart. But she was stubborn and that almost cost her everything. Surrounded by the Mengi and Dakinshi, she had to make a miracle happen twice, killing both the King of Lipmekat and the Vitarogomo of Eusiwuni in hand-to-hand combat, before a real battle could happen. One that she would have lost, as her comparably harsh standards attracted little people to her ranks. It was a harsh lesson that forced her to change. She made concessions that attracted the people she never wanted to stand down to. If she wanted to win, she needed to play by everyone’s rules, even if it pained her to do so. Yet, she still needs to maintain Rufaro’s backing, not only because losing that would damn her reputation, but also because the help of his druids was irreplaceable to her plans.
