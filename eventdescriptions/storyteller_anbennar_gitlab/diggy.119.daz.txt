The dwarves of Verkal Gulan have dug deep and uncovered many lost regions of the hold. The great Citadel Delve is worked unceasingly, and Verkal Gulan's position as the beating heart of Dwarovar finance has been reconfirmed. This imitation of the ancient hold has not gone unnoticed by its population; even now, the golden gates of Verkal Gulan are flown alongside our own banners. It seems the hold is beginning to consider itself a nation, something we may have to deal with.
