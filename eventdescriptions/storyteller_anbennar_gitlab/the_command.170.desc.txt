As soon as his finger touched bare skin, the life force seemed to just simply leave his victims. Suggestion. in addition to korashi-reinforced armor, guards should be provided lamellar face masks with korashi studs. Instead of attempting to raise a revolt, Suresh was apparently after only one person. his son. Our initial defense proving feeble, we cut our losses and allowed Zoburu to meet his father outside the camp walls. Whatever words were exchanged between them did not appear to end well—soon, the sky lit up with fire and death. Zoburu managed to skip back over the wall with his skill in evoking wind, but his father apparently lacked his talents, and frustrated blasts of energy could be heard slamming against the gate for the rest of the night.
