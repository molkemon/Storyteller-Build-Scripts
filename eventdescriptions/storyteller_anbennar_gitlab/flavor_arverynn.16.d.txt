Decisive, concerted action is needed to heal our city, for we are swimming upstream against the tide of urban decline sweeping the whole Ynn. But if any city can prosper even in a time of decline, let it be Arverynn. The monarch walked up to a balcony in front of a crowd, gathered for the ruler's visit to one of Arverynn's residential districts. The monarch spoke. "We won’t just look past the decline, adding even more illusion spells to make buildings vanish as they're abandoned. No more of that. We will soar above it, high and majestic like a great cleansing wave, to crash down on the rot and decay that has taken to our city, letting it bloom anew from the fertile soil. Only then will the Lament for Arverynn be soothed." Despite the speech’s destructive imagery, the crowd was moved by their monarch’s passion, and they began responding in kind. "Praise the Ynn River, praise the Emperors."
