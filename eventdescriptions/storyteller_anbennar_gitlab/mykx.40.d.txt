Another slow day on the patrol ship. As the sun reflects off of the Ruined Sea, you and your best friend, Tsagon Wethide, each take a tablet of Demon’s Blood from the full pouch at your side that you have saved up the entire year’s wages for. You stare into his brown eyes. They turn into mushrooms. They explode. You blink. When your eyes open again, you and Tsagon are standing on the shore of an unknown island, with the ship grounded in front of you, half in flames. The dark sky is a tapestry of unfamiliar stars, and the rest of the crew is scattered on the beach. The captain is chasing her tail. The first mate has donned an eyepatch and acquired a bird. You shake the sand out of your grey fur and take a scared glance at Tsagon, who shares a wideeyed look with you before pulling out a star chart from his coat and squinting at the sky. “I think we might’ve gotten a bad batch.” He muses, “It’s been three weeks. Weren’t these only supposed to last a couple hours?” Your hand goes to your side and checks your pouch, at one point filled with illicit tablets; now only containing a single one, the others likely consumed by those surrounding the wreckage. Tsagon nods at it solemnly, “So what should we do with that?”
