Halann was not so kind as they, and often love found opposition, such as when a noble from Harimari fell for someone of much lower condition. She met that hobgoblin captain at night. They hid away their taboo love, breaking a thousand rules ordained by those higher above. But the flame in their hearts could not die, and it grew till it engulfed their souls. So they fled the oppressive systems, fighting their way out of their mutual troubles. They made it all the way to Lelanàn, arriving one quiet morning in the monsoon rain. And as they held each other’s hands openly, they knew they were free from that pain.
