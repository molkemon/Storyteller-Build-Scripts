I am preparing to leave, and as my successor takes on my duties I have been reflecting on what I have left for them. Although I would like to think myself good, I am not the Great Creator, and although I am not perfect I am not the Great Destroyer; it seems there is more of my path left to walk. I have done and failed to do much, and as I reflect on it all I am coming to a clearer conclusion. I found a virtue early in life, and as I worked towards it I thought about what the Great Creator might think of my virtue, and what my interpretation of it would mean in the end. I have done great things, and Insyaa is all the better for it. But I am left with the sense that for all I have been given, I have not done quite as much as I could. A ruler stands at the apex of the pyramid of all things, and with that power I could have remade the world in the image of my virtue. I am left with a sense of regret. Perhaps in my retirement, I can live more virtuously.
