The dreamers traverse the grove, handinhand. There they were found by the spirit of creation, who saw in their love not the Passions but Beauty Ascendant. To be an Acolyte is to wander alone through the darkness, to fearlessly face the boundaries of knowledge armed with only one's own wit and determination. It is a lonely path, as no Acolyte can find peers among the wretched and pathetic weaklings. Fortunately, the nation and the realm have found one another and began an amorous partnership. One would be wise to remember that power often follows love.
