Our superior tactics and peerless discipline quickly scared the bugbears. As the first fell back from the horde, their underlings retreated as well, causing a mass hysteria that resulted in a fleeing army. Still, this was not just any uncoordinated flight; this was more of a retreat, which meant they knew where to retreat to. Let us follow them to wherever they wish to hide, and maybe we can find some more loot there, or at least slaughter them for good.
