No ork with valor would admit defeat unless their ankles had been cracked, that was until this day. The outside war was alien from the spine; it was full of enemies in the boundless horizons, polishing their blades and polearms in preparation to fight back. Even if all the adventurers had been destroyed, there are yet more human realms to fight against. For the first time in perhaps millennia, the clans had to think strategically; no longer could they settle squabbles with wars and rely on their own strengths to assert their will. They had to do all they could to survive the onslaught after their own. As the shamans discussed these matters within their prestigious halls, bickering and whispering over the specific details, they came to an agreement. For the survival and continuation of the orkish race and its descendants, it was hereby declared that Bonecarver shall be the guardian of all Dookandom from henceforth.
