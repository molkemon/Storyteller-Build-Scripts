We have just received an unexpected visitor in our palace. It is an underminister of the Ministries of the Golden Palace itself. They explain that the support and trust in the ruler has reached a bottom point and that the Ministries no longer want to listen to or heed any command given by the ruler or their dynasty. They explain that the position of ruler must be filled by a new dynasty, a fresh start, and that if someone would rise up and claim the throne, the Ministries would support the action and call it legitimate. They prove their offer by showing already prepared documents that show our dynasty to be more legitimate and more senior and that we have the right to claim the throne if we want to do so. Whether these documents are real or made up does not matter, for nobody would even think about bringing Ministerial documents into question. This is a one-time opportunity and according to the underminister, one cannot wait much longer. The fate of the realm is now in our hands, they say.
