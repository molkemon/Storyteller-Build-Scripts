The republican rulers have succeeded in making institutions of the country increasingly representative, but one institution that has resisted recent changes is the army. One of our generals belongs to a family of nobles who have strong ties to the old guard and has been sabotaging our efforts to oust the loyalists from high-ranking military positions. Now, he has gone even further and wants one of his relatives, also a supporter of the old guard, to act as advisor to the monarch. While this young person seems like a talented commandant, we would loathe giving them influence.
