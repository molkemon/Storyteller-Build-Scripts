The second time a dragon entered Gor Vazumbrog after its refounding was significantly more welcome than the first. Luckily, a slightly listing bronze dragon thundering through the dwarovrod gives a lot of advance notice, and the sentries were warned to let him land freely in one of the outer courtyards. Aakhet accomplished this with an ungraceful front roll, seemingly shapeshifting uncontrollably into human form at a safe distance above the ground. "After our meeting, my dear ruler, I chanced to cross paths with your Hunter of the Deep," he coughs and his skin is coated in bronze scales, then hiccups and the scales disappear, only to be replaced by draconic claws sprouting from his fingers, "and she was not at all hospitable, to say the least. As a matter of fact, that creature is a deranged lunatic, and no kin of mine. And so I thought I should come and issue a correction. "I misspoke when I said lead bullets would pierce her hide. Stop working on those if you've started. Instead, tell your artificers to get to work realizing these plans for protective gear. It's called an Aakhigen mask, and it should help keep you safe from the worst effects of breathing the miasma. You'll still want to avoid getting it on your skin, though."
