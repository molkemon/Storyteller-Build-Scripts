Trouble brews in the Crimson Reverend’s court. Many of the high-ranking courtiers of the court have submitted a petition to the inquisitors; they make the bold claim that a witch hides within the court. However, it does not stop there; many of them have accused the Crimson Reverend of being a witch. These rumors have spread to Poppyspring, and the people have gathered in a massive mob around the assembly, armed with torches and pitchforks. They demand no less than the Reverend’s head.
