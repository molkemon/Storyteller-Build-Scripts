Near the capital, there is a sect of Semayebaka who use a novel method of interpreting Amilak’s Will. They view clouds as coded messages to His faithful and believe that by taking careful note of their shape, volume, and number, they can divine Amilak’s intent and thus predict future events. This has reportedly served them quite well in the past, and these nephomancers are highly respected among the local populace. A Semayebaka of this sect has come to us requesting that they be allowed to read a portent for the Mezhi.
