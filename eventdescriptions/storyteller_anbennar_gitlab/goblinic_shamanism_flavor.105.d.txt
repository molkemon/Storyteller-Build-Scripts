Today we were visited by traders from one of our neighboring countries. They claim that their nation considers the sun to be the most powerful and clever of all gods, despite being imprisoned by her sister. They claim the many contradictions in our mythologies are caused by her plots, which can span centuries, and that someday she will succeed in breaking free and take back her rightful place as ruler of all stars, beginning an age that shall be named after the metal of the sun, gold. She previously already helped the mortals by spreading the arts of medicine and shamanism, and now is using her schemes to prepare the conquest of Halann by our kind.
