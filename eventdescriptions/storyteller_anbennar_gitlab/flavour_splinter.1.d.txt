Long ago, before ashes covered the sky, the people of the steppe worshiped a pantheon known as the Yudunyovi, or Worldly Gods. Chief among them was Taqoshur, whose warmth from above provides life to the world. But it was Soiti, who resides outside this order, that beckoned this truth to them through their grandchildren, who bear the gift of magic in this world. Just as they reclaim their ancient homelands, it is imperative they reclaim their rightful place as those blessed by Taqoshur and uncover the truths of his pantheon.
