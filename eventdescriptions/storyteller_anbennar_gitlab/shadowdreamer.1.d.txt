Cast out of Gor Vazumbrog for his prophetic visions, Zog Shadowdreamer has led his people far, unified in pursuit of the Kabir Drahm. Today, he stands to address what remains of those loyal dreamers who escaped with him. “My friends. Long have we run, persecuted for our beliefs, shunned and hunted. But no more, for we now carve the destiny of the Dream. Let it be known that we are the Dreamers who will never be shaken awake. Let us not forget those who have been left behind, those who died so that we could get this far. Know that in their final moments, they were able to find peace in knowing that they died in service to the Great Dookan until their last. This is the destiny of every Dreamer. The road ahead will be dangerous; not all of us will make it, but we must have faith in Dookan’s guidance. No cost is too high to achieve our destiny. Today, we finally begin our great march to Gor Ozumbrog. Today, we become who we were always meant to be. Today, we will make the Dream come true.”
