Rusty tools, metal wheels, screws, and nuts. Nothing new. As we ransack the room, some clay pots are tipped over and break with a worrying sound. A thick fog and mineral pigments fill the room. We cannot stop coughing, and our eyes become irritated. We still keep looking for clues, but it is just unbearable. The fog keeps thickening. We must get out and try again after many hours.
