The Skaldhyrric tales are ever evolving and changing and multiplying in number, and this is no bad thing. However, it has come to our attention that some unorthodox skalds have been reciting heavily edited versions of established tales. The more orthodox skalds at court are incensed, claiming that the tales are but perversions of the truth, the prose is clumsy and the claims ridiculous. They demand that the monarch put a stop to this.
