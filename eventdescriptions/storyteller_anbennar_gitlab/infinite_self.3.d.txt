We had heard tales of the mechanim emerging into the wider world, but until now they had not come into contact with the lands of the Infinite Self. We have told them of our practices, and they were interested but troubled. They wished to know if they were steps on the path to the One True Soul or merely scenery on the roadside, and though we did not have an answer for them at the time, the question remains vital. we should come to an understanding of what the mechanim mean, not just to the faith but to society at large.
