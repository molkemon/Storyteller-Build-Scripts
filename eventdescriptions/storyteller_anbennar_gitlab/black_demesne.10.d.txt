The frigid air bites against the skin of the mages, who stand along the circumference of a massive circle laid out with large stones. The circle encompasses the mostly frozen body of Jorgurem, the once great dragon brought low by the power of the ruler. At the cardinal points of the circle stand the Acolytes, and standing at Jorgurem's head is the ruler, wielding the great scepter that had taken the work of so many talented artisans. Slowly, the dozen mages reach out to one another across the fabric of creation, linking their minds with thin threads of arcane connection. They begin to chant, redirecting the raw force of nature itself to the scepter. The power flowing through the circle's channel is first a trickle, then a flood, and finally a torrent. Two of the weakest mages in the circle lose their grip on the spell, and, before anyone has had time to notice, a greenish-blue flame has consumed their bodies and left only ashes. Finally, the ruler reaches out with their scepter and touches the dragon's head, a rush of power flowing through the body of the dragon and binding it to the ruler. The beast opens its eyes, staring directly at the ruler. It unfolds its wings and, in an unnatural, shaky motion, opens its mouth. A single word utters forth from that enormous maw. "Master."
