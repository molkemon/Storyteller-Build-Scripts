Seascourge Gnolls lived for the sea; the land was hostile to them, hard to plant, so the options were either to fight the natives or go to the seas, to raid and plunder the Cannorians. So they embraced an Xhaz school focused on extracting the most demonic power they could from nothing. We are Ruin Gnolls. We don’t live for the sea; we are born in the sea, we live in the sea, our entire lives; we have one option. the sea. Even the ones of our kind that stay in plantations need to understand the sea to make their harvest survive. All around us, we can see the endless sea, so our Xhaz school is not born of desperation to extract any value; it comes from pride and knowledge of our world. We are not leaving the past and the traditions we carried over from Viakkoc to embrace something truly of our own.
