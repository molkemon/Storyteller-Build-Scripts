The Groediazan have begun a smear campaign against the Furdanhund and our army for an apparent failure to maintain religious doctrine whilst on campaign. They cite numerous instances where proper ceremony and rites were ignored by officers and soldiers. Our generals and the Furdanhund claim that it would be impractical to actually perform proper ceremonies during wartime, as it slows down troops and wastes resources, yet the Groediazan have spent considerable effort rallying our own clergy against us for our failure.
