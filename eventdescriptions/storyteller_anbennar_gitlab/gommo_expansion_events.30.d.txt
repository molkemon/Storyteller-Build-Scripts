The city is known around the world for its ancient and massive library. Since the time godkings and genies ruled over Bulwar, the records stored in the city remain. As such, it is the foremost repository of ancient knowledge of the whole continent. This great library has been a great boon to our scientific community since we took the city. Now the Gommo has announced plans to invest in a further exploitation of the ancient wisdom within, by establishing a special research division in the city. This will, however, require funding that might be better spent elsewhere.
