The monarch paces slowly, deliberately, heading towards a clearing near the riverbank. Their attendant follows. It is known to many in the area that spirits both eminent and elusive can be found gathering here to commune with mortals, and it is the eminence that the ruler seeks today. The ruler beseeches the gathered spirits, who gaze back into them with stone cold eyes. The ruler speaks with an almost naive innocence, imploring the spirits to give some insight as to why everything spiritual has gone awry. After the monarch finally falls silent, one of the spirits, appearing as a shimmering clouded leopard, speaks. "You have invited a great and terrible evil into your ranks. Among your court of spirits and men, a darkness stalks closer with every passing day. This darkness stands in opposition to the Great Lizard Lupulan and presents a grave danger to the rainforest we call home. You must act to purge your ranks of this malignance."
