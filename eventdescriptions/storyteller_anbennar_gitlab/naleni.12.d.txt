The attacks come swiftly. Reports continue to rush in, mutineer crews assaulting provinces all across Naléni. This is no rogue act of terror. It is a coordinated attack, a strike against the very foundations of the confederation, and now they come for the nation's seat of power. These rogue Odanchésta want nothing more than the nation's end, to see it washed away by the tide. They will not succeed.
