The estate nobles are a decadent lot, living fat off the riches of their ancestors and contributing little to society. At least, that is what the Thieves' Guild believe. Many noble manors have reported priceless paintings missing, and countless trinkets, baubles, and ancestral heirlooms have found their way to pawn shops across the country. The nobility, of course, are embarrassed, and this act can only show their weakness in this world.
