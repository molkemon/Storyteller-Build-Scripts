In the chaos of the greentide, goblin society faced complete upheaval. Families were separated, loved ones lost, and entire clans were destroyed by the invading orks. With the chaos dying down now, and our tribe reestablishing its place, some of these survivors have slowly found their way back to us. Recently, our scouts have reportedly found a large group of goblins, long thought to be lost forever. Long lost cousins and friends have been reunited, and it seems the group has even brought back some treasure from their time in the wilderness. Although, my ruler, it is worth noting that some of these arrivals seem off. No doubt with their long absence, the fey have had something to do with their return.
