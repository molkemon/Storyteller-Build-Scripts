I have gathered you here today to introduce you to someone. Some of you have met her already. What is that, my child? Not now. I am busy. "I do not understand, my Ingo. What is this statue?" This is my child. I am her and she is me. One day, she will be all of me. "And this is supposed to help us how?" I will not live forever. After I am gone, my child will remain. She will guide the Bongo for eternity. "We, um, well, this is most interesting, my Ingo. We will of course support you in this mission however we can." Save your adulation for the fools outside. Spare me your empty words. Do you think I cannot see you for who you are? You do not trust me. You think me mad. You think me senile in my old age. I care not for your concerns. Now BEGONE.
