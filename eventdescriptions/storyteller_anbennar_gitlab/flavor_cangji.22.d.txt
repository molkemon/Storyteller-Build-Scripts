Word has come from our borders to the south, as fields burn and roads are barred. The knights of Hakuukulga, who have languished under the yoke of their vile Oni masters, have noticed our growing strength and now seek to put an end to our nation just as we plan to reenter the wider world. They seek to strike us when they think we are weak, and snuff out our light before we eclipse them in our radiance. We will not tolerate those who wish to see us dead; let them see that we still possess teeth.
