Our lands suffer. Though we are only a small power in the Empire, we served the Moon Party as loyally as we could. The reward was stinging defeat. Yet, for all of our woes, our loss was not so devastating as it could have been; Corvuria did not take the chance to try and claim our Dostannorian domain. A small respite in this dark time. Indeed, our government has been greatly weakened by the loss of so many of our knights and mages. Worse still, we continue to receive word at court of disturbing reports. Administrators seem to simply disappear in the night. More and more of the local elites we rule through are barricading themselves at night. Across the countryside, there is a quiet fear of something afoot. Something seems to be about to give in our humble land.
