The fall of the stormwall has revealed many wonderful and frightful new vistas for us to explore. One of the most mysterious destinations is the Central Insyaan Plateau, mostly due to the high elevation and ecology incapable of sustaining fauna or flora long term, making it relatively safe compared to the rest of the continent’s interior. The many Precursor artifacts and ruins within Insyaa are the primary goal of our expeditions, for hidden within them surely must be many unique artifacts to add to Medaka’s Exceptional Treasures.
