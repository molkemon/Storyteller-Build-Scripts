It is neither elevated nor correct that we, the proud heirs of the Karqašlu, should take orders regarding the will of Surakel or the correct interpretation of Jaddar Jexiszuir’s teachings from anyone that is not a member of our people. To this end, we have ensured that one of Jaddar’s heirs is, as it were, deployed to our lands to serve as a Herald. Under our protection and guidance, of course—neither they nor their kinsfolk will have any say in this matter. It is us, not the Mašnsih, that will shepherd the legacy of the Jadd.
