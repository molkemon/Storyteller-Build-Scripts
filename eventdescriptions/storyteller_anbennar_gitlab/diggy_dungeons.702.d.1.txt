The puzzle was, relatively speaking, actually quite simple in execution after we figured out what to do. Upon closer inspection, there was a groove in the floor where the largest disc was meant to lay, but the smallest was there instead. Each disc also had runes carved in such a way that the discs would interlock and stack with each other, but only from bottom to top. Eventually, we managed to stack them, creating a five-disc tower. When completed, it and the poles sank into the floor, and the vault. With that, the way is now clear, and now...
