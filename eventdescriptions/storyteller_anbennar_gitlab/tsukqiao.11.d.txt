Eunuchs go into Grostunad to study the origin of the Hobgoblins. They discover a hidden chamber that narrates the origin of the Hobgoblins. It turns out they were created during the times of the old empire as royal bodyguards. This is a gigantic revelation, and although at first the hobgoblins did not believe them, they started to do so after their local leaders, who coincidentally seem to be a lot wealthier now, started to spread this truth.
