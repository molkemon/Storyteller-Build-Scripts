Despite our usual tactics for consolidating rule over new land—pillaging, random executions, cultural suppression, sowing division, and so on—it seems the wretched elves still have some fight left in them. A former Ibevari lieutenant named Liandiel has started a rebellion in Carodir’s Pass. She has already incited the peasants to arms, and their rallying cry is "Elfkind never forgets evil." Well, let us make sure elfkind never forgets the screams that will echo through the Pass when we are done with these savages.
