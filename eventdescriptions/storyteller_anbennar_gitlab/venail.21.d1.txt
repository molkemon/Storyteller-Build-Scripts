Many years ago the nobility of Soarnaire made a deal with the elves in Venáil. Upon their leaving the island, they would guarantee control would go to the owner of Soarnaire. Thanks to our present control of Soarnaire, we hold the rights to Venáil. However, it has been many years now, and there is no evidence of the inhabitants having any intention of ever leaving the island. Fortunately, a recent expedition has been made by Venáili explorers into the Bloodgroves. If we wish, we could use this opportunity to press our claim to Venáil.
