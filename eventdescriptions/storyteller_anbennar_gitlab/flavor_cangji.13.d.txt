Within the borders of the hobgoblin Command, mages have been oppressed for the crime of existence, and treated as untrustworthy and dangerous. Long has this oppression gone unchecked as the Command spread across Haless like a cancer. No longer will the Command freely oppress our brethren, beating them into submissions and shackling them with korashi chains. The word has spread through Shamakhad of our inevitable assault, and our quest to free the mages from their shackles. As we make our declaration of war, many prisoners within the Command follow suit and begin to rebel against their hobgoblin masters. They have been assailed on two sides; we shall use this opportunity to strike, and win freedom for all mages within the Command.
