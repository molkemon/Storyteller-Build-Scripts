The deliberations in Ameion have been a long and arduous process. Groups have formed, some wanting to hold on to the memory of Laskaris, while others saw the need to adjust to the changing times. After days of deliberations and multiple physical altercations, Ameion has foolishly decided to turn down our offer of alliance. While they value our friendship, their focus continues to linger on Taychend, and they have little interest in the happenings to their west.
