There are many cults and gods the goblins worship, and this can lead to some disagreements, to put it mildly. But the Hunt is different. It is not a god, barely a religion. It is, above all, a way of life, a purpose. All goblins can feel a sense of belonging, an ancient feeling buried deep in their memories from when they were one people in the Tree of Stone. Now, with a new purpose, goblins can be together again.
