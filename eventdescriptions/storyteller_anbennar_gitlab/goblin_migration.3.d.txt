Ever since goblin refugees have come to our city, we are faced with a dilemma. what should we do with their odd shamanism? An outspoken minority of citizens have voiced their concern, finding fault with the refugees. some goblins draw curious crowds with their rituals, distracting humans from their true religion. A potential solution has come from the refugees themselves. Many have adopted what they call the Avatar Cult, a form of syncretism with our faith. Some have even taken inspiration in manuscripts found in the old Gnomish Quarter, and now fancy themselves as philosophers following The Thought. This original stance may be what we need. We could meet our goblin residents halfway and find a middle ground for religious matters, should we want to further integrate them in our society.
