The Mage Generals have been worried about the security of the southern border. While we could no doubt repel an invading force in open battle, this would create substantial disruption and chaos that could set back our project of transformation by a considerable degree. They have suggested the construction of the so-called 'Black Shield' in a southern domain, which should serve as a sufficient deterrent to ambitious enemies hoping to approach from the south. If an Acolyte were to possess this fortress, they would gain both the responsibility and prestige of being the guardian of the South.
