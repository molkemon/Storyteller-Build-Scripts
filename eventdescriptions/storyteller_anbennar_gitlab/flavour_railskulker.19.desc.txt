A letter has arrived from the nation we offered to become our vassal, and it reads as follows. "To the leaders of the railskulkers, we hereby decline your request. We think that you only wish to use us and profit more from our people, which will result in them becoming even poorer. We have been aware of your observations of our people's struggles for a while now, and we have been working on a solution. Your country only knows violence, and after our own small investigation, it was decided that goblins constantly under the effect of a drug shroom should not be trusted with rulership of our nation. We hope you understand." This message greatly offended the council and our leader, so we see no other options than to go to war.
