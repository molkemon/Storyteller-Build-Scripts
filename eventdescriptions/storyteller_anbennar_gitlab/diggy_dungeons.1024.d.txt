Thinking her to be an easy target with two of her legs occupied, we charged Yidab hoping to deal her some damage. However, Yidab seemed to be much more proficient in fighting without her occupied paws than expected, and though she did take some hits, she was able to hurt us pretty badly. Worse still, our proximity to Yidab meant that the string she had stretched, which turned out to be her attempt at using sound to harm us, ended up being much more effective, actively causing internal damage to some of our soldiers.
