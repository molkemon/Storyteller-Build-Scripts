The forces of Cannor, especially the scheming merchants of the Dames head, have long been envious of Vertesk's great power and wealth from its slaves, and have since banned the sale in Cannor. Vertesk, with its stock drying up, has been left no choice but to focus on other sources of wealth. The City of Chains is no more, but that does not mean Vertesk has to die with the orks it once sold. Now, Vertesk will.
