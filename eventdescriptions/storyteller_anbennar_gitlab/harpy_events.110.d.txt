Rumors in the court have caught the ear of the monarch, that the heir is plotting to usurp their mother. It is not known from whom this information was acquired; it very well could have been one of the other daughters, who even now seek to remove the heir from succession, whether or not the plot is real. Even so, a plot from the heir would pose a very real threat. Some officials aware of the matter are convinced of its truth and say that they must be assassinated in secret to foil the plot and preserve the honor of our flock.
