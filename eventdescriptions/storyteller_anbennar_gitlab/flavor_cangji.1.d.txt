Since the founding of Cangji, our small ember of the Undying Flame has warded us against harmful spirits and sheltered us from the cold of the mountains. Now, as we find ourselves threatened by enemies on all sides, scholars and monks have been praying before the flame, hoping for deliverance from that which troubles them. A smaller cohort also huddles around the flame, muttering amongst each other and frantically moving back and forth between a variety of scrolls. These are the pyremages of Cangyuan, a smaller sect of the Ashen Soul specializing in the manipulation of spiritfire. They scribble mysterious schematics onto neverending sheets of paper, until they frantically rush inside the temple with scrolls streaming from their eager arms. For seven days and seven nights the pyremages worked within the temple's walls, isolated from the rest of Cangyuan. When they finally emerged, they carried with them a massive brazier strapped to a shouldermounted platform. One of the mages put it on his back, concentrated very hard, and soon spiritfire filled the brazier and flew into his hands with a pink-blue glow. The onlookers cheered; they finally had made spiritfire, which before had required a stationary burner, fully portable. The fight against the facestealers begins today.
