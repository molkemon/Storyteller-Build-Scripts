In Haless, many of the legacies of the Sun Elves have been left crumbling and forgotten, weathered relics of the eternal wax and wane of empires. But rising tall within a natural harbor on the western tip of the Lupulan Rainforest lies the great naval fortress of Aksa Sanuyego. Also known as the Citadel of the Flaming Leaf, this great citadel was the primary anchor point for maritime trade in the eastern Phoenix Empire, its construction ordered by the ruler himself. But the old Phoenix Empire is no more, and Azaly has petitioned the new owners to study the citadel. Refurbished by the current holders after centuries of habitation, the fortress is built bordering the jungle on one side, and the sea on the other, much like Bivuzena itself. It contains a massive naval infrastructure but is designed with a narrow, defensible front against land attacks. If it is possible to use this construction to enhance Bivuzena’s walls, as well as guide the construction of new jungle settlements that border the sea, it would be an enormous boon. Several Kelino artisans, who had assisted in restoring the citadel to a state that would make the ruler himself proud, have travelled to Bivuzena with Azaly to assist with our planned modifications.
