The wind blew heavily, but nothing moved across the endless desert. Dawn had yet to arrive. The stars acted as witnesses to the nascent battle below. Marvaan was ready to face any challenge head on. He and his pack called the lion out. They taunted him for hours, smearing his name and his pride. Silence was the only answer. Until the first sun started to rise. Until the sands began to fracture, glowing green, turning to liquid glass. The Once and Future Xhazobain arose from the dunes, and Marutha rose to meet him. A massive sandstorm enveloped the two, warping and shifting, smothering the avatar of Marvaan’s eternal Pack Lord along with the Great Spirit. He clenched his eyes tight, picturing the battle within. How their eyes locked; how their howls and roars burst the sand into the air in a mighty explosion. Fangs meeting storms, flying around like spears and arrows on the battlefield. All felt the hunger. All recognised its agony against the salted pride of the lion. From dawn till dusk, they painted the desert with ichor, and flesh, and impossible chunks of spirit. At dusk, the desert had morphed into Zokka’s domain. And from dusk onwards, Marvaan found himself gazing upwards to the sky, and the setting sun against it.
