Yet another faith from the west has newly arisen, only this one has been even more prominent than the rise of Corin prior. The Ravelian emissary trying to convert us to his faith claims that there is only one true God, and that all other gods are merely aspects or saints of this god, and they speak unto their followers in the form of the God Fragment. When one of our diplomats asked to see what the God Fragment looked like, the emissary looked confused. When pressed further they attempted to describe it. After an hour of back and forth, we decided to call it a cube and move on. Although the idea of a single true God is ridiculous, as we know for a fact that there are dozens of our gods, a certain group of Goblins now believe that the cube must be the holiest shape in the universe. Cube cultists have begun constructing buildings and statues made entirely of cubic blocks, as well as creating the images of themselves and their gods, representing what they believe to be their most ideal forms, for to be a cube is to be closer to God.
