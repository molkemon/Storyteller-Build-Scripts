After an unusually upwardly inclined trudge through the catacombs, the delvers found themselves faced with an assuming collection of architectural notes. What they found described therein, however, was as mad as any other relic. plans for a hold that could travel through the air, through a combination of magic, artificery, and a theorized lighter than air metal that the architect had uncreatively nicknamed "liftthril." What the designer hoped to accomplish with such an edifice is ultimately unclear, although there is an oblique reference to making contact with the lords of the sky.
