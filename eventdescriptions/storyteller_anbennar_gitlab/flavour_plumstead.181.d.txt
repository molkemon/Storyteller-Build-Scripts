Well damn. The party stood before lake Taspasu, where the vault was supposed to be. Captain Avery stood before the shore and threw a rock into the water out of frustration. "Where is that damned vault? It should be right here, but all we found was this lousy lake." Feeling defeated, the party set up their camp, as going back empty handed was a no go. And while scouting the area, a few men stumbled upon a strange monolith protruding from the lake. It was irregularly rectangular and seemed to be decorated with gilded carvings. Tyaphon and Milon decided to inspect the strange monolith and made a baffling discovery. the vault, which they had been looking for, was indeed here, though there was a small problem. The entrance was located underwater. Mixed feelings gripped the party leadership as they had indeed found the vault, though it seemed to be inaccessible. They immediately sent a message to Paihubi with a question. how to get into the vault?
