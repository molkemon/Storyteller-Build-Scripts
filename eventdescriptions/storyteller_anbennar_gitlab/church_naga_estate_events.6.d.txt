Even now, they will not fawn over you. It is their sacred duty to avert Tsishagya, the madness of the one. They will watch you for it, your monarch. You will never go unobserved again. A time will come soon when you must once more return to the heavy places and prove yourself, but for now you enjoy plain sailing. In every province of your realm, the wealthy are encouraged to invest in the status quo, hoarding a little less against darker days. Local naga sermonize about the ancient strictures against withholding tax to one’s rightful ruler, emphasising duty and service to one’s fellow citizens. Such undercurrents, you know, can prove more powerful than one expects. Take heed; the next test the Naga set you will prove more rigorous.
