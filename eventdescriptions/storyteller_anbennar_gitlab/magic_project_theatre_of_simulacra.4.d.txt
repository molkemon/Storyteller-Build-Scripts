Something is not right. To all the world you look the same, but the bags under your eyes tell a different story. There are gaps in your memory, growing by the day. Periods of time that you don't remember, periods even that you don't remember not remembering. It arrives in different ways; sometimes you're in a conversation hearing only every other sentence, sometimes you fall asleep on one day and wake up on another. No one seems to notice. Certainly no one complains. Finally, you remember the room adjoining the palace, the place where the mages are hard at work preparing a simulacrum of yourself. Perhaps they will have answers. Steeling your resolve, you march over to the door and open it, only to be met with yourself. Both of your lips curl into a neat 'O' of surprise.
