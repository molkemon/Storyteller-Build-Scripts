While the Orda Aldresia is but a shadow of its renowned past, relegated to being ceremonial bodyguards of the Emperor of Anbennar, directly attacking the Orda Aldresia is still out of the question, so the only other reasonable solution is to take by stealth what cannot be taken by force. To that end, Varina considers her options carefully. A single professional thief could get the job done, but would be expensive. Some of the locals of the Order's territory may be bribable, and that would be cheap, but how effective could they really be? Another possibility is the use of not one professional, but a band of them. Such a group would almost certainly be able to finish the job, but would be much more expensive. Of course, Varina could always put her trust in her magical skills.
