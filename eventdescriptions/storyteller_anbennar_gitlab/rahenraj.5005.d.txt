Prabhia talks about tactics, Senaptia thinks about strategy, but the viziers study logistics. And it seems the vizier has been studying hard. That, at least, is what the monarch concludes after reading the latest missive distributed by the ministries on the vizier’s behalf. A series of reorganizations and directions, each subtle, but each strengthening the systems that support the Harimraj’s military forces. "As a paw supports a shield, the realm must support our armies," the vizier concludes. And with those words, every state that swears loyalty to the Lotus Throne moves to do just that.
