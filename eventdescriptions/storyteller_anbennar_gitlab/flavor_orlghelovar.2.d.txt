Our reentry into international trade and our capital's position close to the surface has brought many foreign traders from Bulwar and Ourdia to our hold. Our markets and merchant districts now bustle not only with dwarves, but with blondehaired sun elves, sternfaced Ourdians, and friendly Bulwari. The volume and frequency of trade has become so great that a group of human traders have successfully petitioned the monarch to settle in our hold permanently, and already a cluster of houses close to our warehouse district have been remodeled to comfortably accommodate their new, taller residents.
