It is a time of commercial success for the merchants of this realm, and some of the more internationally minded merchants have begun casting an eye on additional investment opportunities beyond our League. Their gaze has landed on another nation, a country we have long had ties with but which stubbornly insists on remaining outside our protection and influence. Were we to invite that nation to join our League as a member, it would become much easier for our merchants to bring their plans to fruition. Should we extend them an offer for membership in our Vic League?
