The muse has continued to assist the rule of your state whenever possible. Your power is cemented, your people cheer your name, and your rivals have gone silent. With such a good partnership between you and your spiritual muse, nothing can stand in either of your way. Though, one thing still puzzles the monarch. Who is this muse? A spirit suddenly appears one day, offers to help for nearly nothing, and your kingdom is now flowing in wealth and strength. Interactions between humans and spirits aren’t uncommon, by any means, especially in the Lupulan; but the relationships are generally more equal, more symbiotic. This feels different. Doubt nags at the back of the monarch’s head, before it quickly dissipates, the muse’s voice swirling in their ears. “We have done well. It is only a matter of time before what we want is ours. But I must go now. You will speak to me soon.”
