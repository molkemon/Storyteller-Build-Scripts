Yusshela, the Celestial Dolphin, is a blessed and powerful Araizary, who has historically granted great boons and blessings to those deserving. Today, the monarch is going to become one of these fortunate individuals, as a reward for the continued reverence and worship of the Araizary in Khugra. The monarch enters the sea alone, as the constellation of the Celestial Dolphin shines brightly in the night sky in the moonless night. A raspy bark breaks the silence of the night and calls the attention of the monarch, who turns to see the envoy of Yusshela, a small seal made of softly shining light. The aquatic mammal, a rare sighting in these waters, approaches playfully until it stops almost at handreach, standing upright in the water. Staring at the ruler with an amused expression, the animal barks again and spins over itself. Suddenly, it jumps above the waters, directly at the monarch, who instinctively catches it as it lands and starts emitting a blinding light. When the monarch's eyes open, the beast is gone, only the light of the evershining stars breaking the darkness. The monarch swims back to the shore, where royal advisors await eager to listen to the monarch's retelling of the experience. The ruler feels the power of the boon flowing through their body, and the changes in their personality are evident. The monarch has received the Blessing of the Seal, a boon of great charm.
