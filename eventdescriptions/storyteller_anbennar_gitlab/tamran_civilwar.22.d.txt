A messenger from the Sirtan tribes has arrived with a proposal. They will not raid during this crisis and Sirtan will militarily intervene on our side, but we surrender rights to any war loot in addition to letting the tribesmen pillage as they please after our victory. The Sirtan are infamous for their cruelty, and we mean to be a unifying force; is it truly worth working with them or should we risk them going to our enemies?
