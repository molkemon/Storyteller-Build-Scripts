Traveller, in the year currently, the great Water Road Manmmoton was dedicated in the name of the ruler. As the Sorrow launched the fleet of a civilization, so too do the docks of the Water Road launch the Ardimyan fleet of Konolkhatep. As the Sorrow spans the length of Kheterata, so too does the Water Road span the lesser rivers of Jasir Jadid. As you are at peace while walking along the placid shores of the Water Road, so too is Konolkhatep at peace under the guidance of the Khet. As this road stands forever, so too shall Konolkhatep. Inscription by the side of the Water Road in Qasri Kallumahida.
