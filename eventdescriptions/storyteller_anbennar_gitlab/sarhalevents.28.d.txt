You are unfit to rule. You know that. Someone should have found a more suitable candidate before you became monarch, but no one did, and now you feel like you are sleepwalking through ruling the country. Your advisors cannot hide their disdain for you. It is only a matter of time before your incompetence leads to your death, and you swear you can hear footsteps around every corner. Tonight, you dream of a hand being offered to you, and you somehow know that if you take the hand, you will not have to worry about your incompetence anymore.
