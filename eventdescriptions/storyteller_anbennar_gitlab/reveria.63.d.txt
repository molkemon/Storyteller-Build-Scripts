They say that actions matter more than words, and we, the Reavers, have done it all on the seas. We didn’t proclaim the seas were ours; we showed the world that the water belonged to the Reavers. Any ship in the ocean that belonged to us had to have our authorization to sail, and we sank any power that refused our merciful edict. We raided every coastline, then we demanded tribute, or we would raid them again. We didn’t write nor proclaim anything; we just did what we wanted. Now, it all belongs to us. And so, the time has come.
