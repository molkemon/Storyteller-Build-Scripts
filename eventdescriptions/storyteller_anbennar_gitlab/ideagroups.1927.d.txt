It was a custom of the monarch to wander around the proximities of the palace, losing themselves in the thicket in the pursuit of some peace. But today the ruler came back, slightly changed. When the monarch arrived at the palace this morning, the ruler's eyes were bright red, their mouth dry as a wasteland, their voice cracked when they stated that they would eat the scraps of the lover's heart from the ground like a hungry dog. In the fear of the ruler's sanity, a group of explorers followed their steps and found both a convincing and disturbing answer. Harmful fumes had been emanating from a nearby cavern.
