The Volcanic Truth is changing as quickly as it can, but the clergy has always been clear that even if change is paramount, it is merely a means to an end, all in the service of what might come after. The role of the Volcanic Truth has always been to change the world in service of the Twin Gods, rather than change merely for the sake of change. And yet, this is the way it has always been. The Twin Gods erupted in anger that they refused to change the world around them. The new faith of the Molten Eternity has swept across Kanolowele in a short time, bringing with it radical new ways of thinking. It is undeniable that they have served as a potent source of change. While everything else has changed around them, the one source of constancy remaining has been their faith. Could the final command of the Twin Gods be to forsake them?
