Whilst drugs and alcohol are perhaps unnatural pleasures and temptations, created by the flawed hands of men, the pleasure and temptation of the flesh is one that is felt by most, and indulged with regularity. Whilst there is nothing inherently wrong with such, the underbelly of Azkabar has a way of twisting the most natural of desires into sinful vices, to be capitalized upon. Whilst the details are lurid enough to make the ruler feel sick, the reality of prostitution in the city is undeniable and undeniably profitable. Banning it feels the natural response to the monarch. But a doubt creeps in quietly and quickly. Can they truly enforce a ban like that? Or perhaps it is better to regulate it. Control it.
