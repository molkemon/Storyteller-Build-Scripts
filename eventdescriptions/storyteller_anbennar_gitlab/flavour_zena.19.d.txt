Azaly Stonecarver, our most prominent architect and researcher, is an ardent admirer of the Dwarven Holds. He has travelled to the hold of a specific domain to inspect their pitchrigged railways, partake in their wine, and study their archives of romantic poems and songs, finding inspiration for our griots. One of his most startling discoveries was the fact that the glowing stones of that domain were no mere tale. He has since begun to study their nature more extensively.
