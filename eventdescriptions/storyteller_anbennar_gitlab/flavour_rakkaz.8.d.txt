Kheterata has been broken. The Mother’s Sorrow has turned red with blood. The temples of the Khet are charred ruins, their pleasure barges fuel for the sacrificial pyres. Kheterata’s people are led away in chains to serve their rightful masters or feed the hunger of our dark patrons. At long last we have avenged the humiliation suffered by our kind after the defeat of Rakhor at the hands of accursed Elikhet and his vile spawn. Those amongst our ranks who have descended the furthest in service to the voices below swear that they could hear Rakhor's distorted cackle emanating from the Womb of Shadows as our forces began adorning Elikhet's pyramid with demonic sigils.
