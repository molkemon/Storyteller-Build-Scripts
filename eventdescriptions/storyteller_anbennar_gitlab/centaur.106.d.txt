As centaurs, our tactics have always been about finding the quickest path to creating a hole in our enemies’ heads. It has always been the fastest way to win our wars. Recently, however, the very nature of our livelihood changed, and so did warfare. As fortresses and urban centers rise around us, the armies we face counter such rudimentary ways, and simply winning in battles is no longer enough to win our wars. Yesterday, a few of our clan chiefs reached out to us, recommending that we perhaps consider reevaluating the way we fight.
