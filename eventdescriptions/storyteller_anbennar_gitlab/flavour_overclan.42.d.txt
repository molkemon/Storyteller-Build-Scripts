Report. Per a recent petition brought forward by some of our harpy subjects, the parliament has examined the issue of poor accessibility for harpies within the layout of our cities. Finding the concerns of these harpies well founded, the parliament has decreed that all buildings open to the public must be designed to allow for aerial access for harpies. With consideration to our harpy subjects in mind, the potential adoption of a more sophisticated vertical construction philosophy may well be considered going forward.
