With the government of our young state still in its infancy and our resources stretched thin, we have found ourselves unable to cope with the surge in violence against humans from agitators. Despite redirecting substantial resources to protective measures and deploying the army on several occasions to restore peace, we cannot be everywhere at once. With farms burned and mob violence spreading, many humans consider our state to have failed in its duty to protect them and have taken matters into their own hands, arming themselves and forming self-defense brigades of questionable loyalty.
