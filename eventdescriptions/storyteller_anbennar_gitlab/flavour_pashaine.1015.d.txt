Reports flood the lesser ministries complaining of systemic project failures at sites across Pashainé. Repairs go undone, construction projects collapse, and the inquests that follow discover missing materials or shoddy workmanship; this is simply not the Pashainéy standard. The obvious suspect is magical sabotage on the part of the arcane conspirators, and indeed some of the project failures appear to be the work of saboteurs or mages. Yet every institution of Pashainé is currently mired in paranoia, and it is also entirely possible that these projects are failing precisely due to that kind of infighting.
