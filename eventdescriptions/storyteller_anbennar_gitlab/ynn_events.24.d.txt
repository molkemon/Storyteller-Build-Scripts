The celestial are coming. Sound the alarm. Erethra Dolenmach shouted, running from one bell tower to another. As the bells rang through the streets of the last stand province, hundreds, thousands of followers came out of their homes. A mob armed with torches, axes, and whatever objects they could find, also bolstered by the few elite devouts to the Drozma Tur cause still remaining, now form a barrier several rows deep between the Dragon Prince and the approaching knights warriors of the realm. Little can be said or done, as the knights warriors grimly advance, while the cultists wave their blades and draw their bows.
