Rival flocks clashed midair above the roost, their feud raining feathers and blood upon the roost below. What began as a personal quarrel between two matriarchs has spiraled into all-out war. With violence in the air, most have elected to stay on the ground, avoiding the aerial brawls above. Yet with flight too dangerous and land routes impractical, the roost is increasingly isolated. Cut off from commerce and with daily life grinding to a halt, fear and uncertainty now grip the roost.
