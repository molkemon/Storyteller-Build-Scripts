A thick smog of incense filled the room where the Court of Change resided. At its centre, laid out on an array of cushions, resided Akhaxpa. As the ruler approached her and knelt down, she opened her three eyes, filling the room with a blinding blue light. “You agitate me. How long have you been focusing on the same goals? Surely you have achieved them by now. Change your focus. If you have not completed those goals by now, you never will.”
