Our authority to rule the lizardfolk has regularly been undermined by the naga. These are a strange, snakelike mutation of lizardfolk that lack any limbs but in exchange have extraordinary magical power. They hold positions of religious and cultural importance, and have not taken kindly at all to the spread of Nirakhetism amongst their kind. Which is why it has been a relief that their protests have died down recently. More disconcertingly, they appear to have disappeared entirely. Our advisors are celebrating the end of Anzalkatsa resistance in the lizardfolk jungles, but the more cautious amongst them are insisting we should double our efforts and find where the naga have gone, lest they return with greater support.
