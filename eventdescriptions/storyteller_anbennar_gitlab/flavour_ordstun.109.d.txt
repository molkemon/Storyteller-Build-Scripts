As all lapidaries know, cutting gemstones into fine pieces of art is a laborious task. This process is monumentally more difficult for the hardest gemstones. and there is no stone harder to cut than a diamond. Despite this, we slowly carefully facet the Diamond of our nation. With the Lapidary Revolt over, and with the Revolt of the Clans long in the past, our government has finally started to sparkle with the sheen of a well-cut gem. Our glow begins to resemble the Argôrdstun which once ruled the Serpentreach.
