Kamrayakval is victorious. Taychend has been overrun. Beware. Beware. The Oren Nayiru has won. For years, Elrandar Silverspite has campaigned, and under the banner of Orenvalyam he has conquered. His soldiers have carved a bloody path across Taychend, bringing justice and wreaking bloody vengeance on all those that stand in their path. Meanwhile, the Kavalali warlordpriests who raised him to power reorganize the region according to the agrarian, chaotic, theodemocratic beliefs of their faith. One must wonder what the Kamrayakval, now effectively emperor of Taychend, will do next. Some say that he is poised to conquer all Andeios, others that Orenvalyam is weak, unstable, unlikely to survive the century in spite of its impressive victory. There are whispers that Silverspite grows unstable, that the Armour of Yodhan whispers to him in the dark, that he is succumbing to the same madness that took its last wearer. Maybe that will doom the Oren Nayiru’s grand experiment. Maybe it will spur them ever further. Only time will tell. For the first time, the Oren Nayiru are no longer shackled by tyrant warlords or foreign interlopers, but are truly free.
