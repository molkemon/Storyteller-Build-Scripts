Sire, I know we are on our way to Abeesooyinka again, but I do have something that might interest you. With so many mages in Abeesooyinka, it was only a matter of time before some would grow close. Others though, have become great rivals, and a particular mage from our lands has challenged a foreigner to a duel in the arcane arts. Both are extremely potent in their abilities. People are placing bets, and the duel will occur in just a few short days. It would be the most entertaining event. A warning my liege, I do believe you must hear this. The mage from our country does seem particularly advanced, but comparatively lacking in skill compared to the foreigner. Most people seem to agree that he is heavily outmatched. If you are to place a bet, I urge you to keep this information in mind.
