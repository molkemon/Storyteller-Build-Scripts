Farraneán, our once home has been returned to. We are now a new nation, but for many who grew up on stories, a place once known in dreams has become real. But there are those who are living here now, those who had made this their home before we came here. What shall become of them? Are we to remove them all, to send them back as the refugees of the first Farraneán did? Or shall we show them the value of being Farrani and let our settlers return slowly to what had been their old homes and live in harmony?
