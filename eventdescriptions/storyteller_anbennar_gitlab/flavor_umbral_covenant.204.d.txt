It suffuses each meeting; the Coven and Covenant meet, and the power of each is felt by the others, too much magic for a single glade, for the space beneath a single tree. It is deliciously uncomfortable to be around one another in such a state. Long have the swamp trolls lusted for grisly revenge upon a continent which despises them; finally, the hags have unleashed generations of such hatred, feeding on the carnage that results.
