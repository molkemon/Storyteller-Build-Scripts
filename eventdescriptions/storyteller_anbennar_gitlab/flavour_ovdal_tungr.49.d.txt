Many in the country likened the noise to that of an earthquake. indeed some tired sleepers had been awoken, and had rushed out into the streets in fear of such a reality. Four juddering jolts shook through the ship. The propellers had begun to turn. The Sea Hold, Ovdal Uthoj, the pinnacle of dwarven nautical engineering and the largest ship Halann had possibly ever seen, shuddered and groaned as slowly, ever so slowly, it eased into the harbor proper. A thunderous wave of cheering and celebration erupted from the onlooking masses as they watched the Sea Hold's gargantuan bulk begin to move under its own power. They had done it. The seas now belonged to the country and the Ovdal Uthoj.
