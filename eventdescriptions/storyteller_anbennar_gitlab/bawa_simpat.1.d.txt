A battle fought. A child lost. A village burned. Such things are commonplace among the Lupulan Rainforest, but the pain from such events weighs on rulers all the same. The monarch's rule has not been a pleasant one, and they know it all too well. The toll on the government has been great, and the monarch blames themselves for the hardship. Lupulan has deemed nature to be as it is, but why does the realm have to cope through such hardship? As if a thought could be spoken to the wind, a strange twisting of light illuminates the room the monarch resides in. A bright shining figure appears, arms outstretched as if providing the gift of their presence to the ruler. A blessing. Words need not be uttered for it to be received. A mere thought will do.
