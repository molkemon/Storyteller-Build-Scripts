Throughout the world, halforks join up at a disproportionate rate when compared to their population, possibly due to a lack of or prejudice against nonmartial career prospects. And this is a good thing, too, as in recent campaigns, some very talented halforks have won even more acclaim and merit than many of our own best and most experienced soldiers. The achievements of one specific individual are so impressive that even our conservative advisors think it would be beneficial for us to assign him a role in our military cabinet. This could be a great chance to further improve the social standings of halforks and further entice them into our service as well.
