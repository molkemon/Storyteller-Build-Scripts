It started slowly, as most things do. Words and phrases, foods, various baubles and trinkets from the communities they stayed with. But over the years, the foreign influence on the Tarics increased. Marriages between the Tarics and others resulted in children of two worlds, and some chose to stay behind in the villages, towns, and cities that had captured their heart. As for the remaining nomads. clothing, art, music; every stop kept changing them little by little. Eventually, they stopped speaking of their homeland with sadness and reverence, and instead more of nostalgia and wonder, like the elves and their ancient Aelantier. And now, so many years after they came, the Tarics have ceased to be more than one of our own with a curious origin story.
