Every one of us mechanim remembers how it felt when we first came back to life, for we are a people who do not forget things. We awoke in rusted heaps in forgotten places. The world felt impossibly vast. The Precursors were so close but so far away. The only comfort we had was the one who first awoke us, and they were the singular light in the darkness and uncertainty that surrounded us. But we have also learned much of the world since the Great Reawakening of the first of the mechanim, and we have learned much of ourselves. The artificers have learned of subroutines and processes within our programming that can help the reawakened remember more and learn quicker, and dedicated teams deploying these techniques are already reaching out to the new mechanim. We will show them that the world is not such a terrible place, and that they are not alone.
