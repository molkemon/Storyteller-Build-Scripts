The dire mismanagement of the realm has led to a critical breakdown in its cohesion. As government ministers struggle to maintain any semblance of centralized control, outlying vassals that were already independent in all but name begin to take the final step and formally renounce their allegiance to the ineffective central government. Such losses are a critical blow to the legitimacy of the ruler; though some see the reduced administrative load as a blessing that will allow the government in that domain to stabilize in this time of crisis.
