The ruler Grom faced the Bladebreaker chief and her family in the manor. Chief Atu Bladebreaker bowed and said, “My ruler, I plead with you to spare my people. I led them into this rebellion, so I am the one who should be punished.” Grom nodded. “From this day forth, Atu and her family are to no longer hold any position of authority in Unguldavor.” He looked at the Bladebreaker prisoners and added, “Along with that, it will now be illegal for any subject of Unguldavor to raise an independent army, save for Khozrugan, which is under direct Unguldavori control. All captured Bladebreaker soldiers are to turn in their weapons and vow to never take up arms again.” As Bladebreaker soldiers handed over swords, muskets, and axes, Unguldavori soldiers seized control of the armory and arsenal in the city. The forges that belched smoke into the skies were now under national control. Bladebreaker was one of the strongest clans in Escann, but now it has learned its place.
