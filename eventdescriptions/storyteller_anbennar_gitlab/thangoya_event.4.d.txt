For a while now the Xia have migrated to our lands in an attempt to stay ahead of the hobgoblin tide that has wreaked havoc upon the northern Xianjie. In the beginning we struggled to cope and adapt to the massive amounts of people coming from the north, but now we have found common ground and integrated them into our fold. Now our policies have borne fruit as a second, even larger wave arrived in the Republic. This time though, we were prepared. Translators from the first wave of refugees were employed to help register and house the large groups of people, and through interactions with the established Xiaken villages we were able to get these new citizens up to speed with our customs and political system. No doubt they will become valuable and integral members of society and strengthen our Republic in the years to come.
