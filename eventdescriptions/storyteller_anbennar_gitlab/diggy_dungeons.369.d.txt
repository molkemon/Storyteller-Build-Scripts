The scissor bug quickly jumps to bite them. They block with a tall metal shield. Before the bug's maws slip and it flinches, some men at the sides grab its legs. The bug awfully shrieks as its legs are pulled, the shield pushes the bug to the opposite direction. It can only bite back the metal, its long fangs bending the shield's sides. The ones who hold its legs hurry to cut them with knives, as if cutting hard ropes. A long, sweaty, and intense struggle begins. The men are redfaced when they cut all its six legs off. The bug is legless on its back now. It uselessly becomes invisible again. Before it tries to move, they crowdstab its thorax with spears and pickaxes. Despite the wounds, the beast struggles and bites back. It only manages to cut down a few spears. They just keep stabbing. Finally, they managed to nail it to the ground with a steel spear. It still lives, but flails around helplessly using its last strength. It is nailed and without strength to lift its head and bite.
