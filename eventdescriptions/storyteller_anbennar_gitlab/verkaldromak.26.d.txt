With DûrVazhatun claimed, its isolation prompts a dilemma; the link to the far hold is tenuous at best, allowing only some level of verbal communication and little else. Even worse, the dwarven artisan mage who created the initial portal has passed, and he did not leave his notes for anyone else. Perhaps in the future, they might find another skilled enough to stabilize and expand the portal, reopening the way to DûrVazhatun.
