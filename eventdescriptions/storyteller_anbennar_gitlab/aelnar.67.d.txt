After arriving in the area described in the monarch's dreams, orders are made to encamp. Guards set up the ruler's tent before being ushered away to leave the monarch to quiet contemplation. As night begins to fall, the monarch continues to meditate, the sky slowly darkening as the sun retreats over the horizon. It would be some time, well into the early twilight of dusk, before the monarch was roused from their vegetative state. The beleaguered ruler retired to their tent, furiously recording the heavensent glyphs from the ancestors above.
