At the very end of the Lilac Wars in fourteen forty-three, the Wexonard army besieged and sacked the imperial capital of Anbenncóst in order to defeat the late Grand Duke of Dameria, Adénn "Skylance". The Wexonard army remained for several months after the war's end, looting and pillaging to their hearts' content. It is said that even during the coronation of the Grand Duke of Wex, Lothane, into the imperial throne of Anbennar, that the city was still a victim to Wexonard greed.
