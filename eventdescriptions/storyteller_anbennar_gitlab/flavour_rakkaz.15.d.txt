The flicker of abyssal flames glimmering within the monarch's eyes and deep down their gullet, a shadow that moves independent of its source, constant murmurations at the edge of hearing sounding wherever the monarch goes. Ever since the monarch has chosen to share their flesh and soul with a denizen of the nether realms a decade ago, they have become a fearsome presence. Mortal flesh however cannot contain the essence of even a lesser demon indefinitely, and lately the cracks have begun to show. The monarch has been increasingly wrecked by surges of agony and weakness, and not even their favorite pastimes such as feasting on the flesh of kerho infants in front of their parents have been able to soothe their erratic mood swings. It all culminated earlier today in a bout of agony of unprecedented strength, causing the monarch to claw at their flesh and tearing out whole chunks of it, with boiling blood gushing out of the wound. The contortions of their body increased until it started to bloat and glow with an inner infernal light, only to explode in a mass of gore and charred meat.
