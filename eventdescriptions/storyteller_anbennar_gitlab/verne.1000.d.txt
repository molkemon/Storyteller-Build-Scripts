Vernmen scatter over the Khenak like ants over a picnic. No longer is this place the benighted home of myriad, dread hill gnolls; it is now integrally Vernman, a part of the great tapestry of our country. This renders it no less perilous a landscape, nor does it free us from glancing over our shoulders, for the wyverns we seek to tame may also prove our end.
