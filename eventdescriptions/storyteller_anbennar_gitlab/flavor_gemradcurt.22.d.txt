The soldier sighed as he stuffed the manuscript into his coat pocket, looking back at the dead rebel on the ground in front of him. All that trouble for a bit of paper and a story; thank Immarel he had intercepted this elf before he found a press, or else he might have had real trouble on his hands. He looked back at his horse, shaking his head; the ride to the domain was long and tiring indeed, and now he needed to plug a leak.
