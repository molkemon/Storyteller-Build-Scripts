Your country has been invited to attend the Grand Masquerade at the castle of Silent Repose in Luciande. Activities include a ball, the exchanging of court intrigue, and to top it all, a grand feast with meats all around. Cannor and Escann are made ready to be eaten. The tasty scarlet shall flow, and no thirst shall be left unquenched. Other topics include the addressing and discussing of the Lucinde's hostile actions against Moon Elves and our slave trading policies. Leisures will be plentiful, but we will be coming to a decisive conclusion in these discussions before the end of the festivities. We hope that all open-minded monarchs and leaders would attend personally. Your presence shall lighten up the mood in the dining halls. We hope we will recognize your face during the Grand Masquerade and get to know you on a more personal level.
