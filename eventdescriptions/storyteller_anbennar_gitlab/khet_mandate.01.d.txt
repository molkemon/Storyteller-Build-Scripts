We have managed to claim the favor of the Khetarch from those unworthy of holding it, and now our ruler is the Steward of the Sorrow, ruling the bountiful land at the shores of the river in the name of the Khet. These catheaded beings are as fickle and hedonistic as they are powerful, however, and if the state is not able to provide for their whims and desires, we run the risk of suffering the same fate as our predecessor, replaced by the next ambitious conqueror that shows up at our doorstep. Because of this, we will dedicate a section of our administration to ensure they are properly pampered, and in return they will officially support our state, facilitating the establishment of full control over the area and even lending us their godlike powers in moments of need. Our relationship with the Khet promises to be one of great mutual benefit, as long as we keep the pleasure barges moving through the river.
