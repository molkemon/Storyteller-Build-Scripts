The island of Ultakal is one of the most prestigious holdings in all of the Lake Federation due to its sizable gold mines. These days, though, prestige as a motivator for the people of the Lake Federation has been displaced by more pedestrian concerns, such as avoiding starvation or getting shot through with arrows. It is this first motivator that was likely the motivator of the communication sent to us by the commander of the garrison of Ultakal. After our successful siege of the capital of the realm, they have decided to surrender under the logic that aid is not likely to be forthcoming anytime soon, and it is impossible to eat gold. Our forces already have the island under occupation.
