With the Ravelian Faith increasingly becoming the dominant religion of our country, many of our government officials, nobles, and other notables have begun to adopt Cannorian notions of kingship, Cannorian names, and titles. Many have in fact started to refer to the monarch as a King, even though our official state religion is still the Ravelian Faith. Some advisors of the monarch now suggest that it might be prudent to go along with this. If we start referring to our own country as a kingdom, it might ease relations with the many Ravelian countries around the world.
