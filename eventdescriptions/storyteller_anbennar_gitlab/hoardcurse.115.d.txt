Empowering the new nobility was a mistake. The wealth and opulence brought forth to the adventurers made lords who have only fostered a sense of superiority and an excessive ego. The ruinous Hoardcurse has only allowed such unbecoming qualities to fester and grow, inflicting their arrogance upon the indentured populace. Punishing the masses for the slightest of infractions, the once noble adventurers have become yet another monster to cull. For the sin of eschewing noble obedience, we must slay our former compatriots; a grim reminder to all future nobles of the consequences of excess.
