A common perception of Ovdal Kanzad is a tragedy. Once a much larger people who held resolutely against Grimka’s Goblintide, to the many, sulphurburnt beards, shining amber, and steel draped in crimson. However, their generosity is boundless; they fight with great determination to honor and respect their fallen allies. Our great halls will be adorned with banners of the Topaz dwarves. No obstacle will be too great in guaranteeing their place in the annals of history. By doing this, we have guaranteed a powerful ally to the east.
