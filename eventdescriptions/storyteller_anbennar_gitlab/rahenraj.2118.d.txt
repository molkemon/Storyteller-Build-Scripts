The Grand Vizier of the Harimraj lives and breathes the Lotus Court, from political maneuvering to bureaucratic manipulation. But today, it seems, the ruler is being sent away from court, requested by another ruler to oversee a domain, where a recent scandal has engulfed the local ministries and left a leadership vacuum in the area. It will feel weird to leave court, but such a role undoubtedly has opportunities for influence and selfdealing. The way is lit, the path is clear. the vizier shall.
