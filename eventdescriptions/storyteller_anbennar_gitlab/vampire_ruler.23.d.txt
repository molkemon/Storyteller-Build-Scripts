The monarch is wounded, but victorious. The hunter put up a ferocious fight, but was no match for the skilled vampire. A deft feint was all it took for the hunter to make their final mistake; the monarch swiftly finished off the hunter with a slash to their throat. The body of the wouldbe hunter now lies on the sanctum floor, their life blood draining onto the surrounding stonework. The duel was not without cost, however; it will take time for the ruler to recover from their injuries. Now that the hunter is dead, the ruler will have plenty of time.
