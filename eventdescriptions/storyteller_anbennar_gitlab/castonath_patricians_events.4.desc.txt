Tired of the tyranny and lawlessness, a steady stream of patricians try and leave Castonath every year. Some are let go freely, especially those who only leave with the clothes on their backs, but others we detain for a healthy few days until they agree to give up a healthy portion of their valuables. Not wanting to go through this process, a particularly rich patrician family has seen fit to try and secretly smuggle a massive cache of valuables past the walls of Castonath in their escape. We have secured the offenders and their valuables; how shall we deal with them?
