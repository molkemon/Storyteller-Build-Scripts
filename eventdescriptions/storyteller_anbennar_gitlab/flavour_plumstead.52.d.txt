The lands we find ourselves in are extensive and seem almost never ending. To the north, the terrain slowly changes into grasslands, forests, and eventually a large river. Yet to the south, the desert goes on and on, until you reach the region of Haraf. This strange southern land is home to many peoples, like the desert dwelling Harafne or the mask wearing Mayte. And even though they are wary of strangers, they do have an interest in trade. These were some of the last pieces of writing which Adelar Longstaff, the founder of our nation, would leave behind. He had in his last years been traveling to the south on multiple occasions to set up trade routes. Not only to trade in gold, of which the Harafne had an abundance, but also in knowledge, as this was what our nation was founded on and for. And even though he had been suffering from gout, Adelar still kept traveling, exploring, and collecting knowledge. He would, until he gave his last breath, keep exploring. He embodied Plumstead, and in turn, we will try to do the same.
