One of our captains is reporting some joyful news. His troops were clearing a Cursed One camp when they found a small halfork girl held captive, around nine or ten years old. She says she was captured when she was very small and has lived as one of them. The captain doubts that her parents survived the raid she was taken in, but calls have gone out to nearby villages to begin the search. We have not known Cursed Ones to take captives or even willingly leave survivors before, so she surely has a wealth of useful information on how they live. Though so far the only thing she has had to say about her experience is, "I miss their songs."
