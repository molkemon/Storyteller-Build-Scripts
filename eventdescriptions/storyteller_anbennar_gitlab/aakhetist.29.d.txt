Aakhet's military briefings are unpredictable. Often they will be an excuse for him to reminisce about old conquests. Sometimes he will come up with a scheme so elaborate, not a single general in the room could follow it, leaving the plans unfulfilled and Aakhet frustrated. This plan was different. In a few simple steps, he outlined exactly how you could decapitate the leadership of the country and bring it under our control in a single war. The genius of it was in its simplicity. Sometimes over a thousand years of expertise in military campaigns does bear fruit.
