With the Great Daxugo Legal Code taking form, we are working away the last bits of our institutionalized horde legacy. As old systems are modernized or replaced completely, we find ourselves at yet another crossroads. Our advisors have observed states from all across Halann and have come up with four ways to represent the people, now that the village elder is nothing more than an honorary title. Parliamentarism would empower our cities by granting them local representation, but the abolishment of the privileges of the nobility, followed by their incorporation into the parliament, is likely to ruffle some feathers.
