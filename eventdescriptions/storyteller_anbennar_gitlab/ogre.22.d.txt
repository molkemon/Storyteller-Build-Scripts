Several Gray Fist hunters have discovered gold nuggets sparkling in the river that flows through their tribal territory. As two of the hunters discuss keeping the claim for themselves, the third, a particularly fat and flatulent ogre known as Gaseous, brutally kills and eats his companions. He declares that the shiny rocks in the water belong to the Gorger Lord and no one else. After having his hearty meal, Gaseous makes his way to the gathering at Skurkokli. The mighty Rancor Bloodtooth is so pleased at the ruthlessness and zealous loyalty of the Gray Fist ogre that he awards him a magical jeweled ring, granting Gaseous heightened intelligence, but cursing him with a love of numbers. The Gorger Lord grants Gaseous the position of tallyman of the horde, making him chieftain of the Gray Fist tribe and giving him the authority to mine, prospect, and mint the gold that was discovered. It is on this day the Goldlord, Gaseous Goldmaw, is born.
