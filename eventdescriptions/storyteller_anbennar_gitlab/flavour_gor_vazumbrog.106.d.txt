While the main halls, parade grounds, and assembly rooms and main arteries of the hold are capacious, the shops, back rooms, and capillaries can be quite cramped. Natural caverns only moreso, while the caverns left in the wake of a purple worm are quite spacious indeed. Thrûn Stonefist has much time to consider these matters as he tries to find his way out of an even more cramped space. the guts of the purple worm that was threatening the hold during the deepening process, and has now swallowed him. He hasn't the room to swing his axe, constrained as he is by the undulating worm gullet, though he can reach the pocket knife he keeps in his boot for clearing overgrown foliage. When the worm's side splits open, revealing the grinning visage of Magda Stonefist wielding a borrowed axe, he comforts himself that the split is along the exact line he had been painstakingly endeavoring to carve with his breath-retener, and surely he had loosened up the situation.
