Our decision to finance the efforts of the Order of Judges in that area has borne fruit. As the wandering lawmen roam its countryside and cities, disputes ranging from ancient feuds to petty theft have been settled following the precepts of consistency and equality before the law that the Order upholds. Justice is served daily by the knowledgeable Judges; the people of that area no longer need to pour resources and time into solving legal matters. In the end, the law of Bulwar is more important than the local customs of that area, and most parties involved in these cases accept the word of the Judges with little to no complaint, for they can no longer fall back to odd regional customs and laws to refute their opponents.
