As foreign traders penetrate deeper and deeper into our lands, they are quickly followed by stranger folk. The most insidious of these are the preachers promoting strange religions. Most recently, our trade with distant realms has led us to contact with Ravelians who speak of a One True God whose characteristics are far divorced from those of Amilak. Though most view them as harmless, there are many Semayebaka who see these foreign preachers as a dire threat or, at worst, messengers of Yrilak sent to deceive the faithful. Still, any hostile reaction against them is sure to anger the nations that they represent.
