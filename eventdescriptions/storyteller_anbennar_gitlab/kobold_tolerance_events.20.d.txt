In the mining district of the province, a Trivu of Kobolds has risen to prominence due to the exceptional amount and quality of ore they have extracted in recent months. In addition to their sheer productivity, they have also reported no caveins and only fourteen mining-related fatalities in the past half-year, which is impressive enough on its own. The secret, not that they are hiding it, turns out to be a combination of abjuration magic and alchemical acid. They reinforce the surrounding rock magically, then melt away the ore-bearing rock into collection troughs. After some time, the metalladen slurry will dry into a rich powder, which can be refined using a rotating bloomery. The end result is an exceptional yield of base metals without the use of explosives or backbreaking labor, though the overall process is slower and a bit more expensive than traditional mining.
