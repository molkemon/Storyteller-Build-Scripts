A sharp ripping crunch reverberates through the caverns. Yet another train gone. The physics behind it is sound. The magic is working, too well even. There is so much power inside the tunnels when the pylons activate; much more than what they anticipated. It is a wonder that the whole mountain does not come crashing down on their heads. The problem is in the engineering, the materials. Their trains simply cannot handle the pressure; they crack like eggs. They need to look back at the source; they might still have something to learn from the old masters.
