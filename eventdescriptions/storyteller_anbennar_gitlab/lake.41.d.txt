As the city grows, the difference between the high and lower classes in Zabyos'tlar has become all too visible. The city has grown up and over the canal, bathing the lower districts in darkness while the Zabyos'tlaric elite thrive high above. With this divide, the city has become known as two. the grand and opulent Sky Walk of Zabyos above, and the filthy, darkened Undercity of Tlar below.
