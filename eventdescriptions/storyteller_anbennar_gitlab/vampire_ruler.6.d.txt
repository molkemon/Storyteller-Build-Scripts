Reports have reached us of a feral vampire acting within the capital. Though such monsters are not unheard of, vampiric rumors swirling amongst our people shall surely draw unwanted attention to our ruler and other vampires in our country. As such, the vampire estate has demanded the monarch lend state assistance in hunting down and destroying this errant predator. The tone of these requests began politely, but have recently escalated into almost commands, as if they have forgotten just what role the ruler plays within the realm. Indeed, this is a traditional responsibility of the vampire estate. Our monarch could intercede, however, and even overrule them if necessary.
