The concept of Shijikura or "Classification of Possessions" is an old one in hobgoblin society. It describes the traditional view on who can own what and how much of it. This is focused mostly on things like homes, materials, or food, but can be expanded to luxuries and spoils of war as well. Expanding on the organization of the Commands, we will organize our citizens into property classes, outlining what types and quantities of different goods they can possess. These classes will not be based on military rank, however, but on the need of the individual and their family; after all, what need have generals for wheat seeds, or farmers an arsenal? Any who break these laws will be subject to property confiscation and trial by the Ozukehogai.
