A silence like the quiet that meets a hunter in the forest falls across the borders. Neighboring imperial princes meekly make way for the giant's surging rise as onlookers outside of the empire take note of our growing strength. The ruler summits a large hill beneath the region’s cloudy skies. The wind is still and the downpours lull, as even the elements bow to the giant's strength. Today the ruler has called a council amongst the powerbrokers of the realm to consider the next steps in our endless ambition. As noble, clergy, and merchants bicker around the well furnished and well attended outdoor table, a crack of lightning snaps everyone’s attention to the western horizon. A pale blur speeds toward them. Footmen scramble for their sheaths and archers ready their bows as the ruler steps forward to meet this being, but they stop ten yards before the ruler. Before the crowd stands a witch of the Magisterium, adorned in silver robes and insignias denoting them as a representative of the empire. Her green eyes hardened and skin weather beaten, an accomplished war mage stands before the giant. The mage snarls. ‘Bend the knee, ruler. Or the Emperor will let loose the dogs of war.’ The ruler grimaces as they consider their options.
