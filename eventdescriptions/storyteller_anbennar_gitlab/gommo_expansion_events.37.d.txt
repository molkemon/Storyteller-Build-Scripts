The towering High Temples of the city have been a beacon for pilgrims, scholars, and mages since long before Harimar arrived and disappeared into their midst. Not a single eunuch in all Yanshen serves without having been trained in the city. Recently, with the strengthening of global trade, the allure of the city has drawn in people from ever further away. Since our taking of the city, the Gommo has shown an increased interest in the temples and schools of the city. They plan on establishing a special academic division in order to perfect gnomish philosophy and theology. This project will, however, require funding, and the money might be needed more direly elsewhere.
