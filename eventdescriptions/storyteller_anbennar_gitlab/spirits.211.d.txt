Ghosts have become a common sight since the onset of this spirit tide, especially in the vicinity of damaged and ruined high temples. These apparitions are typically pale blue or white, although sightings of other hues such as red and yellow have been reported. They appear with a faint aura, taking the shape of sentient species. However, recent sightings have brought attention to grey, shapeless apparitions haunting the realm. These ghosts evoke feelings of dread, unease, and weariness among the people. Whatever their nature, it is evident that these entities are not entirely benign spirits.
