By imperial decree, all elves are equal before the law. None is the greater or lesser of their brothers or sisters. All elves live to serve the nation, and the nation lives to serve all elves. Within these pages lies the unified Sibhil Càd, which explains at length the rights, obligations, and responsibilities of all trueblooded elves. May the laws enumerated in the following pages be the basis for mutual love, pride, honesty, and respect amongst the children of the Stars. Signed this month, year years since the Exile. Decreed by the monarch on the basis of the power invested in the ruler by the sovereignty of elvish blood.
