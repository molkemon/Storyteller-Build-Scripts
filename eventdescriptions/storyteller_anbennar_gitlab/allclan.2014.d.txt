We know that uniforms and style matter; we look fabulous, but for some, their looks and attire matter much more. In fact, Cloak and Swagger dictates a dress code that requires a heavy use of black, and as the organization grew to such a massive size in the past few months, it led to a worldwide shortage of black dye. Thus, we are out of black clothing, which makes matters worse. Our Cloak and Swagger officials refuse to work in these conditions.
