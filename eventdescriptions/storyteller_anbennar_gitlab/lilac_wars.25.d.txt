It is good that war is terrible, that we do not grow too fond of it. Yet not all of war is so terrible. The desperation and urgency of the Lilac Wars afforded us greater powers than we would have otherwise not had as the realm grappled with crisis. Gone are old dues and laws written with a different Cannor in mind, whose sole purpose was to empower the obstinate. The realm is better administered than ever before, and we look to take our next steps.
