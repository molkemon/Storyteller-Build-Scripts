Aakhet's reign was known not just for its military conquests, but for its great festivals. Aakhet himself never officially attended them, but it was an open secret that he would attend in disguise. In honor to Aakhet, let us hold a new feast in his name. Empty the storehouses, slaughter our finest cattle, and throw a party that will be spoken of in legend for its revelry. Perhaps, if we are lucky, Aakhet himself will turn up.
