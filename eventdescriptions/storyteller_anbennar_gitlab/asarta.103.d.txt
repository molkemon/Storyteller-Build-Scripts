A thick smog of incense filled the room where the Court of Change resided. At its centre, laid out on an array of cushions, resided Akhaxpa. As the ruler approached her and knelt down, she opened her three eyes, filling the room with a blinding blue light. “These churches you build to spread your faith. Why do you keep them somewhere when they already follow? They have served their purpose. Bring change to more lands by bringing more temples to those lands. Otherwise stagnation will follow.”
