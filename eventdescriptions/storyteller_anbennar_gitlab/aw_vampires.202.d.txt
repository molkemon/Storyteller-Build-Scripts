The rowdy vampires in the realm have openly challenged the priesthood. They have gone into temples, bragging about how the forces the priests worship do nothing to stop their incursions. Priests have been bled dry and holy places were desecrated in despicable night orgies. The shamed clergy of the realm who were able to flee return to their temples to find veritable horror scenes, forced to dump corpses and clean blood splatters after the vampire incursion while being completely unable to seek revenge in the short term.
