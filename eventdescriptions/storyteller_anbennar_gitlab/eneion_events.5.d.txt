Today, a large envoy has arrived from a subject of ours, the nékheis of Eneion. They come with the most intriguing of offers. They propose to support our efforts in the unification of the Kheionai as a partner. With our aid, they claim, they will be able to unify the nékheis. They offer eternal loyalty, and some lucrative benefits for our merchants, in exchange for the aid we would give them. Our officials and our warrior elite have been furiously debating the issue of whether to accept or deny Eneion’s proposal. It is clear that denying them will not go down well with the Eneioni, and may end up creating a new enemy. If we accept the deal, however, we may end up creating a power stronger than we may realize. The monarch sits alone in their private office, weighing their options. The decision, in the end, rests upon them alone.
