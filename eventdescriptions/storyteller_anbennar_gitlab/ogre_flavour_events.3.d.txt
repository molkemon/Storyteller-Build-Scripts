Bellicose voices clog the halls of the capital, sapping the ruler's patience and attention. As two disparate parties stand before the ruler divided by a line of guards, they finally simmer down, allowing the first group comprising a colorful mishmash of small peoples to speak. "Is this how the country treats its daring soldiers of fortune? We braved the unknown and deadly for equal shares of all treasure, but when we completed our quest, these," the mercenary casts a lurid eye at the opposing party before biting their tongue. "They reneged on our promised dues. What nonsense." The second group, a cadre of ogre warriors, shrugs nonchalantly at their lesser former partners. "My ruler, it is the law that all equal shares are done by the fistful. Is it our fault they have such diminutive hands? Perhaps these foreigners should have spent a moment learning our customs before doing business in our lands? They walked half as far and carried half as much during our contract; by all accounts, they have been compensated fairly. In fact, they should pay us a higher share for our labor and..." The curses and hollers resume between the irate parties, but the ruler considers the conundrum. True, ogre tradition for fair shares involves literal fistfuls at a time, but even with the adventurers unaware of this practice, they surely are still entitled to equitable booty? Who comes out on top in this disagreement?
