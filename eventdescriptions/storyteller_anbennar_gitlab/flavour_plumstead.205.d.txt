Adventurers in our nation are supposed to uphold a certain level of decency and honesty, especially towards the native population. Any adventurer who breaks the law will be detained and stripped of their license if deemed necessary. Sadly, we cannot arrest every law-breaking adventurer, as a group of adventurers have stolen some jewelry and sacred tools from a tribe. They have already disappeared, and we doubt we will ever be able to arrest them. The tribes in turn are furious that we have not put in the extra mile to get the thieves.
