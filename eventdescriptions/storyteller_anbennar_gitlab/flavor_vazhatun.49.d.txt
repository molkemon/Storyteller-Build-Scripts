This marks a joyous occasion not only to disprove those who said the planet didn't exist and our ancestors had lied or made a mistake, and not only as a restoration of our ancestor's lost discovery, but as proof that we are every bit the astronomers that the original Sapphire Dwarves were. To celebrate, the High Astronomer has invited everyone whose work relates to the Setzunkhûpa for a feast in the Azure Hall, six o'clock tomorrow. Bring your finest clothing.
