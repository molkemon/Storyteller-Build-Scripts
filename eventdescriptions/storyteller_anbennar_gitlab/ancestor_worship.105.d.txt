Some foolish dwarves think Quila the Harvester is out of place amongst the Great Ancestors, deriding her as just a farmer. Any dwarf who knows their history, however, knows just how fundamentally her work changed the Dwarovar. After Quila’s irrigation works were implemented, the Serpent’s Vale went from being able to feed Amldihr to being able to feed every single hold with heaps to spare. Quila heralded in an age of plenty and prosperity for AulDwarov that was never before seen.
