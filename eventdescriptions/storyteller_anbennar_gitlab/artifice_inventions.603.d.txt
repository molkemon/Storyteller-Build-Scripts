Some powerful mages reach for immortality, often going mad in the process. More clever ones settle for functional immortality, surrounding themselves in nighimpenetrable mageshields. Now that we have figured out how to stably project such fields via artificery, abjuration spells imbued into clockwork spellgauges allow shield size and strength to be adjusted on a whim. Gone are the days of gaps in one's armor, or even wearing armor at all.
