The expedition did not return from the lower levels. When the first scout team returned, there was only blood, gore, and the bodies of slain expeditionaries. Whatever felled them could not, or would not be found. When we returned for a second trip, it was as if nothing happened at all. Nothing. No blood, no bodies, no broken weapons or armor. The scouting team quickly returned, unnerved beyond all reason, and refused a third trip by themselves.
