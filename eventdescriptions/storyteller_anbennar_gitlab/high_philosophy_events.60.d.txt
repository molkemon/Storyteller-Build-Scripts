To move, to act, to do; the motive force by which the High Gods sculpted the world is a great and powerful thing, but to dwell eternally in this power is dangerous. So it is, some now claim, with the state as it stands today. Many detractors have begun to organize around gurus and preachers who advocate for a period of spiritual withdrawal and selfreflection to reset the course of the realm, ensuring that their steps walk on the path to Purtura. There is substantial resistance to such action, however. Many in the government, secure in their beliefs, see this move as naught but dithering, though if they follow them, it may well increase fatigue of the people in the long term.
