Report. Continued expansion by the state has seen the role of Chief Vizier gain increasing importance for both the ruler and the state as a whole. This has had an undesirable side effect in other regions under our rule struggling to represent their interests on a national level. It is advised that the office of a Circuit Diwan be established and a suitable candidate be appointed posthaste in order to alleviate this issue. Attached you will find several recommendations for candidates to assume this role. This office awaits your prompt decision.
