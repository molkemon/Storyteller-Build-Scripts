For days nothing really happens. The troops follow the way and once in a while they see one of the landmarks. Confidence rises as more men start to believe in the city that descended from the sky. For months they went through this unknown frontier without a real goal in sight and even the temple didn't convince most of them. But every matching detail from the elder's story strengthens their trust and therefore their will to march on. Suddenly they run into a strangely barren circle with a ruin on a hill in the middle. Nothing grows within the circle; there is only plain dirt. Even the clouds seem to pass around this place. As the first men step into the circle, they feel like walking through a waterfall. On the other side, they are met with total silence. Not a single sound from either the wildlife or the rest of their group can be heard. They quickly return to the outside and report their observation. Some of the rather superstitious men are starting to get restless and urge to leave.
