Banners are hung throughout the domain as hundreds of mages stream into the central courtyard of the Low Temple. These magical minds were brought from across the land, all having some knack for elemental manipulation and spiritual communion. They gather among the sconces of pink flames that line the courtyard, studying them and making note of their ethereal properties. Suddenly a door is flung open, and the Ashen Soul enters the courtyard. The silvergrey harimari look around the square, and together begin to utter a low incantation before spiritfire bursts from their bodies, illuminating them with a wondrous glow. They turn to the brazier in the center of the courtyard, and with combined powers feed their spiritfire to the bowl of embers. The fire jumps, sputters, and finally expands to create a shining dome of pinkblue light. Within the dome, the cracked ground of the plateau quickly begins to rejuvenate, as grasses and flowers sprout from beneath the cobblestone. The gathered mages cheer, and the Ashen Soul welcomes them inside to further discuss their newfound revelations.
