Better to end your own life than be taken by Gnolls. With the capture of the capital, our hunt begins in earnest. The monarch is a legendary mage, and the priesthood of GerBexamurr, the so called Sobkryzag, have requested the honour of finding and capturing this legend so that they may be put to use at the Talazdvoger. Already, scryers rifle through the monarch's belongings, while others hunt down their nearest and dearest for details on their whereabouts. Their methods are suitably brutal, but there is no doubt that it is only a matter of time before the monarch is found and shackled.
