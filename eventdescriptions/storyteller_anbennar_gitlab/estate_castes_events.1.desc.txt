A human playwright, going by the name Dhanneh Leihner, is rapidly gaining fame among the common folk. His plays are tactless vernacular pieces of garbage, yet the humans in the middle and lower castes love them. It is an outrage. The plays are cheap comedy and aim to make a mockery of the upper castes. Contemptuous titles range from "The Mind of a Married Harimari" to "Harimar and Manava Go to White Castle", and his most recent play "Dude, Where's My Horse?" makes a mockery of all the virtues we hold in respect. We must take control of the situation.
