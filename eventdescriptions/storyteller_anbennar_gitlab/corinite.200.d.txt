The term "Corinite Crusader" is a loose term afforded to all those who fight in Corin's name. Their ranks swell with people of all backgrounds, but a great many can be simply counted as peasant rabble who fight for religion rather than their feudal lord. What is certain, however, is their unyielding zeal to spread Corin's justice across the land. With our recent war prolonging the suffering of our people, large bands of crusaders have flocked to our banners, both in the army and in the supply train, hoping that the war may finally end with a righteous victor.
