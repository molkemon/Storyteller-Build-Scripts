The illumination of the sky has caused a great many changes in our society, but one thing that never changes is the nobility's interest in growing their own personal wealth. Taking a great interest in the large sums of money flowing into the Godswatch Observatory project, a number of nobles have demanded their right to a portion of the funds. They argue that their invaluable contribution to the administrative side of the project, as well as the fundraising efforts among their subjects, means they should be provided with a monetary reward for their troubles. Of course, we are not under any actual obligation to pay them, but it is also always prudent to stay on the nobility's good side.
