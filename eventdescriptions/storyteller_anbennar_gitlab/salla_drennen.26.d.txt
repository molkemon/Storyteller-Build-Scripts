The most I have ever doubted our work was during the raising of the Veil, when we were getting reports about villages affected by the shifts in the connection between our world and the feyrealm. I wondered if it was hubris, to think we could ever so neatly thread our fine fabric between the two realms without hurting anyone. I am still not sure it was not, but perhaps it is the burden of those who do great work to question themselves, lest they become untethered from the earth. I am glad at least we gave the villages aid, even if we could not agree on what form it should take.
