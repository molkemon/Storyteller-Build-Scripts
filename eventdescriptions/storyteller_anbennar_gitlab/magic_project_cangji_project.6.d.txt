As more knowledge on spiritfire is collected, the archives of our great cities grow. The most resplendent among them, the Silver Spires, is nestled within the bustling streets of Cangyuan, metallic towers stretching into the sky. Breakthrough after breakthrough has been recorded within the scrolls and tomes of the Spires, each detailing a new fundamental property of spiritfire or a revolutionary application for the flames. Today, a different sort of revelation has struck the librarians of the Spires; they have run out of room. Somehow, every single nook and cranny of the vast library of magical knowledge has become occupied by literature. Stacks of books tower above the halls, scrolls roll haphazardly down stairs, and tomes begin to fly out the windows from the crush of pages behind glass panes. But where others would despair at the immense pile of knowledge, the scholars rejoice and hurriedly summon the pink flames of their domain. With resolve, they begin to build. Giant stacks of stone and glass are placed within the courtyard, and with the effort of hundreds of scholars working together a new tower begins to rise from the assorted materials. Shelves, doors, windows, stairs, balconies, floors, and ceilings all move into place either with direct manipulation or the help of whimsical fire sprites. Eventually, the tower is fully built, and the mages celebrate their new wing before consecrating the tower with its own silvery cap. Satisfied with their construction work, they nod to each other and flood the wing, hoping to fill it up even faster this time.
