Gazing east from the city's walls, one will see that the wide open field outside the city extends far in the distance to form the borderlands with another domain. Known as 'Moon Field', it was considered an extension of the Moon City, and a keeneyed individual can still notice a hillock with a ruined marble temple on top built in the same style as the Moon City's. Left abandoned amid the grassy plains and reclaimed by plants and animals for the last two centuries, it is still decorated with imagery of the Moon that summons the Ynn River to rise, no looter daring to pick it off. Recently, a captain of the local guard claimed the temple is occupied by a group who use it as a base. According to the report, after a run-in with border raiders, he and his troops charged all the way to the old temple's defensive walls, behind which the raiders took shelter. Should we give credit to these claims, we can use them as an excuse to launch an expedition into the ruins, perhaps reclaiming the land of the Moon Field in the process.
