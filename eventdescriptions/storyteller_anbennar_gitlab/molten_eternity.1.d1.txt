I grow weak, but soon I will be reborn, and as I slip away I have been reflecting on what I have left for myself. Although I would like to think myself good, I am not the Great Creator, and although I am not perfect I am not the Great Destroyer; it seems there is more of my path left to walk. I have done and failed to do much, and as I reflect on it all I am coming to a clearer conclusion. I began walking the path of my virtue with such dedication. I thought myself a servant of the Great Creator. But as I tried and struggled, my failures kept mounting while my successes remained so few. I thought so many times that I should rededicate myself. The virtues were the purpose of my life. But there was always something else to do, and now there is no more life left. If there is comfort left to me now, it is that the Great Destroyer is not here yet. Perhaps I will do better next time.
