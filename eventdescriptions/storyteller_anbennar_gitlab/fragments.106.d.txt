On a giant open space, the men find strangely round hills. As they approach, the magically gifted among them gets a massive headache and strange hallucinations. When he recovers, he urges his companions to get the adventure captain. He and his advisors inspect the hills, and his chronicler quotes the elders' story. "And the angry gods awoke the earth and made it swallow the Glowing City." So maybe we find the domes under these. The adventure captain agrees and orders his men to dig at the first hill. After a few hours, they bump into Dame's tear, and a few minutes later uncover a whole railing. There are definitely buildings under these hills.
