As our embassy continues onward to completion, it has gained us the interest of the Midwinter Chef, an archfey with special respect among followers of the Winter Court. He has expressed satisfaction with the idea of our embassy and has asked us to hold a grand feast to make everyone feel welcome. He has offered some help with the food preparation, but the overall task of arranging the feast is to fall upon us. Of course, this is not simply a polite suggestion, and to deny the Midwinter Chef would be incredibly unwise. Likewise, to please him with our hospitality could have him look favorably upon us. How should we approach conducting this feast?
