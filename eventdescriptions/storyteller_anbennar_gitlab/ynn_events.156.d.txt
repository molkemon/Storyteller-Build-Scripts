Wise and kind eyes watch the monarch from the floor of the court. A small, longhaired white tail sweeps back and forth in a patient and polite manner. Before the monarch is sitting a Stenurynn Teriyer puppy, a gift from the ruler of a neighboring realm. The Teriyers are famous for their elegance and social intelligence and are valued pets of many upperclass nobles. The best puppies are sometimes gifted to valued allies or other friends of the celestial court, as such this gesture shows deep faith between our nations.
