Gnomes are small, diminutive, and worst of all weak. No matter how strong the mind of a gnome might be, none of that matters when some larger individual decides that they are beyond reproach. This time, we can make sure that never happens again. Even better than a simple exoskeleton, this time the Brillites have earned the name. They have created a set of Dames tearpowered plate armor, capable of using magic to keep itself and its wearer intact even past the blow of a cannonball, capable of holding two palm-mounted sparkdrive blasters, capable of boosting the wearer's strength to ten times what it was previously. It might be expensive, but who cares about the cost when it comes to benefits like this?
