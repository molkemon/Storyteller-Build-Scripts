The Kolovadoy is the sacred hero who will reclaim the Ananoma and the ancestral lands of the Halflings of southern Sarhal as foretold by Jyva. The Zuvavim nobility believe this hero to be a human, but this has been proven demonstrably false by our deeds. It is clear the Kolovadoy is a halfling, and that this prophecy is to be fulfilled by the rulers of Bibyobi. It is time we force everyone to acknowledge the truth of this prophecy and lay claim to the title we have earned. The monarch is the Kolovadoy, may they be the first of a sacred line of chosen rulers.
