The monarch awoke to a familiar, yet entirely unknown sight. A throne room that was not their own. Their ruler rose from the position they came to in, a kowtow, and stood up, examining their surroundings. A voice boomed from the empty throne, each word bringing the visage of a dragon further into view. Yanhe, his draconic figure so large that only his head rested upon the throne, spoke slow, deliberate words. "I recognize your desires and deem them acceptable. I offer you my patronage, if only you will accept it."
