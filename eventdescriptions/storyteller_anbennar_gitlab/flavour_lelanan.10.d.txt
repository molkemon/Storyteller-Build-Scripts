I wore the robes of a High Priestess. I thought of Felana’s sweet words to me. I stood before the great loud audience and bent down onto my one knee. “Kyola Heartblade, hero that you are. You are here today because Lel has spoken. She has seen your actions—how you inspire hope—and now seeks to make you leader of her folk.” The audience cheered so loudly. I looked over them and smiled. My time spent back had paid off. My victory was anything but mild. Many Priestesses stood around us. Their support was so hard to earn, yet my great efforts had not been wasted for this position which I did yearn. “Kyola Heartblade,” the High Priestess continued, handing the sign of her station to me so gently. “You are the Chosen of Lel; High Priestess. May you rule over us all intently.”
