Dieu Xau's school became a town, and that town seems to have become a base for the Devouring Path, with devastating consequence. There are conflicting reports, which is to be expected of the few terrified souls who have abandoned their ancestral homes to escape the Devouring Path's influence. Some speak of abductions, people simply never returning home after a day in the fields. Others speak of nightmares beyond nightmares, dreams with evil spirits from which they could not wake. Whatever the truth is, it is clear. something has gone very wrong in Pakpon Haitri.
