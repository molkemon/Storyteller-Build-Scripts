The harpies of the Harpy Hills have been in Bulwar at least as long as humans have. They even ruled this land once during the reign of the Harpy Queendom under Firanya, the supposed mother of all harpies. Yes, they fly down from their hills to steal our men sometimes, but they have none of their own and we certainly weren’t giving them any, so in hindsight it is hard to blame them. But there have been many other transgressions between our people in the past, both far and recent. Now that they are part of our nation, we must decide how to deal with them.
