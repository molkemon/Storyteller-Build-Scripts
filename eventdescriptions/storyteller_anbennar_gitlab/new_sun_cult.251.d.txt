It appears one of our more prying neighbours has gotten wind of our plans to lift the longstanding restrictions on human practitioners of magic and are not at all enthused about this development. In their entitlement, the arrogant fools of that nation consider it their role to reprimand us, citing legal precedent and sacred scripture from the time of Jexis to the learned writings of Taelarios himself, all painting the threat posed by human magi unshackled by firm restraints in the most dire colours. If we do not deviate from our set course, they threaten to enforce our repentance by any means necessary. Many amongst the royal court scoff at this blatant attempt at impotent meddling in our affairs, yet some counsel for a measured response to calm their unreasonable fears.
