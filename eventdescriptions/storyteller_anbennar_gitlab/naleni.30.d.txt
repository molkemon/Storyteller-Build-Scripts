A Siren commander and a prominent Odanchés stand before the throne, each making a passionate case as to why the other is the issue. Since Ríva’s reforms, each Odanchésta ship has had a detachment of Sirens assigned to it. Initially it functioned as an oversight measure, but for the loyal Odanchésta it has simply become a matter of utility. However, some Odanchésta take grievance with having non-subordinates aboard their ships, and often end up butting heads with the Siren commanders as a result. Likewise, the Sirens can sometimes see themselves as overseers to the Odanchés, exacerbating the problem. In this instance, a disagreement has brewed so deep it has fallen to the attention of the Queen.
