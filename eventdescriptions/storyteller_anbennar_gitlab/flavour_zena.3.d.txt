Our potential allies in Iqhekabi have been vanquished. The nobles of distant Kheterat know of the goings-on here in Horashesh, despite their weakened hold on our lands. The spies of the Kheteratan Empire are numerous, and despite my best efforts, the Medawor still hold some sway. They are nervous and scared. Kuiika and Zuvavim have seen what we are capable of when we are cornered, and are rightfully terrified. A Horashesh made up not of squabbling independent tribes, but unified and led by a glorious leader, the kind the griots sing of? It is their worst nightmare. Very well. I shall become that nightmare. We will stand triumphant over the entirety of Horashesh, and declare the rebirth of our confederation; a united Horashesh once more. And if any dare to stand in our way?
