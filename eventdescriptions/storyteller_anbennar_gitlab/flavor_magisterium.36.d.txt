There is panic in the Magisterium, pure raw unadulterated animal panic. Our work is ruined. Our good intentions were misunderstood. The empire hungers to dismantle our progress. Already the secret, the terrible truth about the death of Castellos, has spread across the empire, soundly out of our hands. Worse yet, our oversight of the Chroniclers has been misunderstood as propagandistic efforts to bolster our prestige and power. None of our explanations seem to be reaching past the fury of the princes, who have started to call to dismember our organization, to even splinter the Chroniclers away from us. It’s doubtful they will see any reason, and even less likely that we’ll be able to make it out of this terrible situation with no concessions. The world will scorn our names and believe us liars now, for many decades, lest they manage to see reason.
