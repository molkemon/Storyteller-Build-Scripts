Silent of foot, unknown masked hunters have approached us. While similar in some regards to the Peitar Gladewardens, these hunters bear pointier ears and no indication of Fey veneration upon them. But they also bring trophies from battle with the Goblins and orks that we are beset with. they say that their homeland of the Deepwoods is at war with them as well. They call themselves 'Wood Elves,' and offer their assistance to be rid of this foul infestation once and for all.
