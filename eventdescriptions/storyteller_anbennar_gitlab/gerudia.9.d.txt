It has been many years since Deranne was conquered and integrated, with new generations being born and raised under the Crown of Bera. The Derannic people were once like Gerudians before becoming a shade of the Lorentish, but over these new generations the people have begun to adopt their Gerudian roots again. Once and now known as the Talderannics, this country has become an integral part of Gerudian culture once more.
