Dames tear is raw magic condensed into a stone, and as such, it is incredibly useful as a power source in the field of artificery. The Mechanists in our realm have been undertaking initiatives to mine as much Dames tear as they can within our provinces. The Brillites, fickle as always, have complained that such careless mining will simply lower the quality of the Dames tear they harvest, making it practically useless in their eyes.
