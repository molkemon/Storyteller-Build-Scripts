Like any species of bird, the hinuibirds of the mountains migrate from place to place to find new food and better climates with the changing of the seasons. Unfortunately, in this case we are their main source of food, and now that the hinuibirds are migrating here in massive numbers we will have to intensify our efforts to fight back. We will have to send more supplies to our besieged settlements and more soldiers to kill the predatory hinuibirds which besiege them. everything is going to get harder for a while.
