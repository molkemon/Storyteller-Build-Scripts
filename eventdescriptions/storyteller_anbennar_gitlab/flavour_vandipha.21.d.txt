The Pearl Bazaar is truly a wonder unique among the nations, adorned with details from all over the world; Bulwari, Sarhaly, Raheni and Bom influences can be seen in its architecture. At its centre lies a mandala, crafted with ten thousand and one perfect pearls, the namesake of the beautiful bazaar. In this bazaar lies the headquarters of the celestial Slaver's Guild, who oversee all of the country's slave trade. Occasionally, an exceptional slave is offered for sale, and a representative of the monarch is sent to oversee the transaction. They have many possible buyers for this unique slave. We could sell them to our ally, and be seen as reliable and trustworthy partners. We could sell them to the Raja, and receive the favour of the Harimraj. We could sell them to our strongest neighbours, and benefit from their grace. Or we could simply sell them to the highest bidder, and enlarge our considerable wealth.
