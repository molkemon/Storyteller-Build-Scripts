As the matter of our people's conversion lies settled, another quiet conversation comes, much quieter than the heated debates so many years prior. While some from that long table have perished, a few fled, and a number having even been imprisoned in the dungeons some floors beneath it, calm conversation murmurs on. "After all these years, I still find it strange that our people would fight so fiercely against the ruler's reign in the stars above." Erelas' brows met his spectacles once more, though the monarch was silent in turn, mind clouded with the dark thoughts and fears of what had been. The strewn bodies, a fight at Carodir's pass that had been ended with fist and blade, when quivers had exhausted themselves. Assassins springing from the quiet of the forest, toward their carriage. The war was fraught with danger at every turn. Every labor of love comes at some price. And now, it was done. The people of Ibevar had been set upon a new path. "The troubled times are behind us, fret not, mélislun." The pair shared a tender, familiar smile, as a new day dawned upon their nation.
