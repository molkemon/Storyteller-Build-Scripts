Having taken the territory from the dwarves, it is now up to our monarch to decide what to do with the land. While everyone unanimously agrees that we should allow our people to settle the province, not all seem to agree to what extent we should allow such settlements. Most seem to be in favour of having no limits on the number of settlements. However, this would leave the dwarves a minority in their own lands. The dwarven delegation objected to a full settlement of the province by humans. Many of their men have already died in battle, and many dwarves have already abandoned the province and moved further into Exwes to try to rebuild their lives under new overlords. There are fears that mass human settlement will destroy what remains of the dwarven way of life.
