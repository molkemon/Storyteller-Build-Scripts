A hollow wind took to the air as we surveyed our surroundings, unaccompanied until a singular bird emerged and began to sing. Turning our heads, we saw a tower still standing and glinting in the sunlight, and a similar tower decayed to rubble. It is clear that the city did not survive the Ruin in one piece, but it was not obliterated in the same manner as the rest of its civilization. As far as one can tell, the city has slept here unperturbed since it landed, with scant records indicating its surviving inhabitants abandoned it for another location where supplies could be found. Even as they left it, they left it in peace, using what could have been the last of their great magics to conceal it so that it may remain in grandeur when they return. So too did they leave their dead, gently but swiftly laying their departed so they may rest alongside their city. The chaos invoked by the Ruin would not see them return to their city, but after many years we have come in their stead. It may never again be spotted in the cerulean skies, but what remains of its buoyant architecture will see us raise our own civilization to new heights.
