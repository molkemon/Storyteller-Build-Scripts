The subtle conspiracy of yesteryear has evolved into something much more sinister, as mages opposed to the government make their fury known in public displays of illusion and enchantment. It is difficult to understand what precisely they expect to achieve, for each act of terror radicalizes another town’s populace against the arcane threat. Perhaps it is merely the tyrant’s desire to cause fear, the megalomaniac’s need to be heeded. A time is coming when all such selfish perversions will be stamped out, when even those with the magical gift will shudder at the thought of one who would make themself WitchKing. For now, this magical terror causes chaos on our streets.
