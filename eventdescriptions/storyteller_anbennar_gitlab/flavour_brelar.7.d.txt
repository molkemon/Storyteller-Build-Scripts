The grandeur of the Ynnic Dams is unrivalled to anything we have set our eyes upon before. Long were they simply maintained and left to stagnant hands, but with the ingenuity of our Brelari Dam Architects, we can proclaim to have reached a perfection of which was never even dreamt of before. Water cascades ever so grandly and gracefully from the chiseled stone, leaving a fine mist to coat the foliage below and causing floral patches to sparkle. For great function must be met by great beauty.
