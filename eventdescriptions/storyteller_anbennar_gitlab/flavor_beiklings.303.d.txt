The thunder of the hammers bounces throughout the room. Echoes of one million voices ring out, screaming in unison. Not too long ago, he was nothing but the leader of a forge. Now, he teaches the art. Those who have seen it describe it in many different words. mastery, perfectionism, even metalbending. But for those who were seen as the best of the best, it was none of these things. It was a scream, a roaring. To the people that work the iron and other metals that feed the furnaces of the hills, this is a known truth. the earth lives, waiting for the moment that someone awakens it. There is a primordial might lying under our feet, and it awaits those who know how to make it theirs. He had spent so many years working on that forge without this knowledge; it wasn't until that day that he decided to learn the ways of old. He finally heard a sound long lost, the anthem of the earth itself. As the metal was reborn in the shape of a blade, so was he reborn as a true Master of the Forge.
