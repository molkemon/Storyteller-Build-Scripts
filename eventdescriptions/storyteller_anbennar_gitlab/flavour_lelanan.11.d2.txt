I arrived in Ninakaire several months after it was found. The reclamation was rife with details of monster attacks and a diseased mound. The honeycomb structures of the Ananoma were truly a sight to behold, still standing taller after years of decay than Fahvanosy’s fortresses so bold. Paladins bustled everywhere, set to the labor of love that was reclamation. I nodded respectfully as they hurried past me, and I bowed my head to them as they greeted my station. “Madam Falana, I am glad you made it safely. What do you think of our hard work? This was made possible thanks to you and Madam Leli. Us standing amongst such aweing stonework.” I looked around and imagined the potential. The towering Ananoma stood tall so long. I nodded to the Paladin beside me. “Kyola would be proud of us being so strong.”
