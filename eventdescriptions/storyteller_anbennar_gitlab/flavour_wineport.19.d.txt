Another bottle for you, my lord, inquired the servant, who looked at the ruler with a mildly worried look. The monarch simply waved their hand to indicate that they wanted more; by the Gods, their head was hurting. And it was causing them to focus on who they were here to meet; a Sorncóst wine baron, who was currently giving a rather long-winded explanation about Sorncóst wine, their family history, how they had been growing grapes since the days of the Damerian Republic, and adding the Sorncóst wine to the national collection. "It would make a prime addition, would it not? I can already see you have taken a liking to it, my lord," the wine baron added with a wry chuckle. The monarch looked to their side, after finishing another glass of this delicious wine, and saw twelve. No, wait, the monarch focused, and there were only six bottles of Sorncóst on the table next to them. Wait, six? The monarch swore it was only one or two, but it simply went down so easily. The monarch turned to the wine baron, and groggily nodded.
