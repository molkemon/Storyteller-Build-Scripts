The port of Kuta has stood as one of the most advanced among the Yanglam. Home to many dwarven ship builder clans, the area is known for being one of the largest ports among the Lupulan. The shipbuilding clans are offering to build us some ships in exchange for the materials needed, at no extra charge. Though we ourselves may be landlocked, our many subjects insist on the importance of a strong navy. This should quell that desire.
