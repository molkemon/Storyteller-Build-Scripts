After months of combing through the ruins, the expedition has almost fully exhausted its supplies. They have enough to do one more thorough search, but there are still two areas of the cavern that lay unexplored in the distance. The first appears to be a standard dwarven forge complex, with some strange details. For some reason, there appears to be a stable attached to it, but much too small to have housed horses. A thin wisp of smoke drifts from the complex. The second at first glance appears to be a library, though its days of glory were long gone. Its main columns were cracked, and rubble blocked the entrance. We may be able to gain some knowledge out of it yet, however. Which area should the expedition explore?
