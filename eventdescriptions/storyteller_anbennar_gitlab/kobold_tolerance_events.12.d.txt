The burghers in that province have filed a formal complaint about the goldscale kobolds living there. The kobolds' innate love of and mastery of money has led to some of them becoming merchants, and doing well; very, very well. The golden merchants are coming close to dominating trade in that province. The burghers consider this to be antimercantile, even oppressive, which certainly has nothing to do with how much market share they have lost. They demand that we do something to curb this rising power and maintain the free flow of trade in the province's markets.
