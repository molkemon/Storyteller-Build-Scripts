The monarch awakens with a jolt. In their sleep they heard a voice, one that was clear and clearly not part of them. The voice told them of magic, of how it flowed and shifted. The voice told of how one can make magic move the way one wishes it to. After awakening, the dream did not fade as most dreams do, and the techniques the voice spoke of have proved to be very effective. It is clear that the monarch has been visited by Soiti.
