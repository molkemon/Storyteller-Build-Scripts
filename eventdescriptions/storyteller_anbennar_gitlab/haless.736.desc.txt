Hidden Spring is a famed hot spring retreat in Yanshen, nestled in lush woodlands where steam gently coils above crystal-clear pools that never run dry. Since ancient times, it has drawn travelers, scholars, and officials seeking healing and peace of mind, many of whom lingered far longer than intended. Legend tells that a ruler himself, during his conquest of Yanshen, once bathed here for days, declaring the spring "a balm for the soul, water gifted from heaven," and placed guards to protect it. Centuries later, a ruler of the Phoenix Empire also visited this spring during his campaign, and so it came to be called "The Conqueror's Spring." Today, ancient rituals continue. incense is offered before entering the water, and paper is burned afterward, said to wash away both bodily afflictions and burdens of the mind. As winter deepens and sunlight becomes rare, the rising mist shrouds the spring like fine gauze; it is the perfect time for a bath.
