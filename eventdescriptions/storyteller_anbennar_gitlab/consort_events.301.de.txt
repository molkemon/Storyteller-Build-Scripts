When the consort married into the royal house, there were those who were skeptical. However, the young consort impressed all with their genuine and graceful way with people, and the nobility of the capital are constantly finding new ways to show their adoration of the consort. It is not surprising that the wedding anniversary of the elevated couple has been highly anticipated; somehow the consort has even managed to personally thank each nobleman and woman for the gifts brought to them for the occasion.
