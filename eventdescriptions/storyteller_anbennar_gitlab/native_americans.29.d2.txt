A woman from the ruler's tribe approaches the ruler while the ruler is fishing as the tribe usually does to feed their people. The ruler raises their head towards her, their eyes meet, and the ruler knows she has seen something unusual, but good, nevertheless. With piety in her gaze, she whispers how she has seen the biggest salmon ever, a true incarnation of the spirit of the river we so deeply worship. The ruler follows her to the spot where she saw it, and after a few breaths, the ruler finally sees it. It is huge indeed. The ruler prepares their tools to capture it.
