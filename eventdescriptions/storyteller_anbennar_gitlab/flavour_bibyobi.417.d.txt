Our esteemed leader and the rest of the company finally caught up with us. Judging by the state they were in, they had encountered plenty of their own troubles along the way. The monarch was in a foul mood over the whole debacle, but brightened right up when I told the monarch about the ruins and set that mage of the monarch loose inside. The fellow came back near trembling with excitement, clutching a clay tablet that even I could tell was something special. It was made by our ancestors, as I suspected, and more than that, the tablet is still brimming with magic, powerful stuff for certain. A fine day for history, and hopefully for our pockets.
