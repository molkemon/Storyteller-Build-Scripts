The expeditionary steps forward. "It's not worth it, Verdric. We are not the ones who locked you away. Clearly you can see that? Open your eyes, we don't have to fight." "How can I trust you?" They reply, despite their chiseled, metallic face, it contains all the expression needed to betray the doubt they have. "The High King." "There has been no High King in Amldihr for over seven thousand years. AulDwarov fell long ago. Amldihr fell, and with it that empire." The sphere clatters to the ground, as Verdric falls to their knees. "Of course. Of course." Their head hangs in shame. "All of this... for nothing. It's been so long... too long. I should have known. I... I did know. I just ignored it. Gods forgive me. I... I have been the greatest of fools." Verdric stands up, simply hanging their head in shame. "The contents of my workshop, they are yours. It is poor restitution for what I have done, but it is the best I can offer."
