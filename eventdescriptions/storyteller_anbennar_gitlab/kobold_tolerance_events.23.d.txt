Kobolds, mischievous, enthusiastic, and inquisitive folk, a little under half as tall as a human and possibly the people on Halann with the least in common with the other races. Despite the differences, the scales and claws, they have much in common with us as well, and they are so inquisitive. A band of especially curious Kobolds have arrived recently; they say they are touring the continent to learn about other people's arts and music. They have clearly been traveling for some time and are now looking for a place to stay, rest, and perform what they have learned.
