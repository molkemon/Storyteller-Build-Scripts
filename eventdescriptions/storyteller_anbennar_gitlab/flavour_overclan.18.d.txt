Attention, Titanium, Pyrite, Citrine, Gold, and Topaz dwarves. By decree of our parliament and the Overboss, if you are able to trace your lineage to the holds of the Segbandal, you are entitled to participate in a state-sponsored reclamation program. All those who are accepted will be given state backing to resettle and rebuild their ancestral homes, and will be entitled to the protection and security afforded by the state while doing so. This measure is fully supported by the formerly exiled Kings of these holds, who eagerly await the return of their kinsdwarves. Do your part. Join your kin, obey your King, and serve your state. Reclaim what is rightfully yours. For more information on how to apply, please speak with your local Voltoj official.
