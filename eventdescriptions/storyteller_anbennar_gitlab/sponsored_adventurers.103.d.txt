Over the past several decades, we have fought to spread civilization among these natives. Many brothers and sons have shed their blood in order to bring our people to glory, and now, after our decades of hard fighting, we are asked to lay down our swords. Instead of being a band set on conquering the wilds, we must now turn to making it our own at the behest of those who sent us forth all those decades ago. Do we remain under the heel of our overlords, or is this a time for greater change than our contracts of fealty?
