Despite the monopoly the Silcalas family holds on the office of ruler, the nation has seen a current of republican ideals recently. Republicans have viewed this as a sign that their nation may very well be on its way towards their goal of a full-fledged republic, with some even going as far as praising the Calasanni prince for permitting this shift. Regardless of how much truth there is to this, their recent political maneuvering keeps the dream of the Republic alive.
