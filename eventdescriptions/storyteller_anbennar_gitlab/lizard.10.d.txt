As if we weren’t already in an unfortunate situation after a rival broke our family’s bonds, we got a message from another power. Under the guise of offering themselves as a barrier against a possible rebellion, they demand access to certain provinces. While their arguments are valid, they are fully aware how much we’ve tried to centralize our holdings to make a unified push against a major enemy. To make matters worse, they indirectly suggested this is a matter worth breaking our alliance over. Can’t they see the bigger picture? They just put us in a lose-lose situation. either succumb and risk the other parties following up with their demands or decline and damage the stability of our coalition further.
