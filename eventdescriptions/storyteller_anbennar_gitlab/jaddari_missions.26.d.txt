The only thing we expect from across the mountains to the east is the occasional Harpy Hunt. But today, the harpies come in peace, escorting an Elf whose flowing white robes over sunscorched skin fail to cover up a razor-sharp sword at his side. We bid him welcome, and after the customary hospitality rituals, he is eager to get to business. He speaks of a new faith, The Jadd. But more importantly, he speaks of increased sovereignty, decreased taxes, and support from the Desert Legion in expanding our realm to absorb all Ghankedheni. With one last flourish, and an invocation of his god, he comes to an end. His eyes bright, the smile on his face not quite reaching his eyes, he looks at us expectantly. What will the family's decision be?
