As our colony in the realm has continued to grow and develop, we have begun to encroach on the territory belonging to a people referring to themselves as the native culture. They send jeers and insults in their native tongue, and have even started raiding our settlements. One of our own men with a sympathy for these savages has learned the native culture language and he tells us that they are claiming our colony is encroaching on their ancestral land. Apparently they demand that we grant them concessions, or war will be inevitable.
