A monarch has not the right to much time to mourn the death of his parents. Mere days after the funeral, the coronation ceremony had to take place, and then a myriad of tasks awaited him, everything that was delayed to let him officially ascend to the throne. At least the fog felt on his head was starting to lift, the everyday duties of a ruler of two kingdoms helping in softening the pain of the loss. Right, kingdoms, plural. He will have to formalize the rank of his two crowns, sooner rather than later. There is no doubt in his mind about that at least. He loved his mother's homeland deeply, but he is a gophiran through and through; he grew up running around the fort, that will always be his home. Gophira will be the primary title, Darkalyndar's subordinate to it, for him and his heirs as well.
