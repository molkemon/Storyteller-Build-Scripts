The monarch stirs in their private chamber, suddenly aware of a cloaked figure resting against an ornate cabinet a dagger’s strike away. How did this happen? The stranger holds up their hand, speaking in a charming yet difficult to identify accent. “My monarch, there is no need for alarm. I am here only to speak about a plight affecting your most loyal subjects. You see, the grungy gutters and smoky back rooms complain of only one issue these days. ogre discrimination. They are prime sentry and bouncer material, but the police harass, arrest, and even kill them; a crime lord can't even enjoy a perfectly legal dinner without worrying about their ogre bodyguards attracting unwanted attention. And so I have been sent to plead with you, monarch; please issue a lien for these ogres. In exchange, your loyal scoundrels shall assist in outing the freelancing rats, corrupt pigs, and foreign snakes seeking to undermine this nation’s greatness. What say you, my monarch?”
