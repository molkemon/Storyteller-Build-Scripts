Tragedy has struck Duwarkani. As the royal couple strolled along the stone walls of the city, a soldier who knelt before them suddenly sprang up, a dagger in each hand, and plunged them into the bowels of the King and Queen. Their blood pooled together on the battlements as life flickered out from their eyes, grasping each other in a deathly embrace. They were dead before any physician or apothecary could reach them. The assassin made no attempt to run. He wanted the people of the country to know why he did what he did. Knowing there would be a public trial before his hanging, he used that opportunity to address the crowd. Using the legacy of Castan the Second, the infamous king of Castanor who liberated Kheterata from gnollish rule, the assassin decried the mixing of humanity with monsters. What next, he proclaimed. Humans marrying gnolls? Lizardfolk? Trolls? He said he had to do something before humanity fell to the beasts.
