The matter of the mechanim is decided. Change burns within the mechanim. They are suffused with the same holy fire that lives within the Twin Gods, and the Twin Gods' mandate is clear. the mechanim shall be given roles in the faith as avatars of sacred change, so that the future of the Volcanic Truth will be as longlasting as they have proven themselves to be. The mechanim shall travel across the lands of Insyaa as traders and emissaries, so that their holy change may spread across the continent like the lava of a volcanic eruption. But now that we have decided what to think of the mechanim's past, the Volcanic Truth must look now to the future. The mechanim have a place within Insyaa, now and forever, and thus they shall live beneath the gaze of the Twin Gods just as we do. They will work towards change within themselves and within the wider world, as is their duty.
