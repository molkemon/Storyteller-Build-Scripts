Relations between the country and the dwarven realms of Cannor have always been cordial, if distant, thanks to distance. Despite this, visitors from Rubyhold and Silverforge are relatively common, so the arrival of a dwarf known as Grun Strongbellow did not initially receive much attention. That quickly changed after he secured an audience with the monarch and presented a possible solution to the country's flooded lower levels, a problem that has long plagued the realm. Though many of the engineers regarded his ideas with some initial skepticism, Strongbellow's willingness to share and seek input on his blueprints has won him the admiration of the artisan class. There's some risk to it, but it seems that his plans might just work.
