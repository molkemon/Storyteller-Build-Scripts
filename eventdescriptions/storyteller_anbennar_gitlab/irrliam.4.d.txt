At long last, the entirety of the former domain of Šadnatu has been brought under our control. After a period of prosperity and prominence under the Phoenix Empire, successive waves of gnollish incursions had whittled this once mighty domain down to a rump state, our loyal vassal of Zanšap. Now, we are faced by a choice that has the potential to define the future of the Šad Sur and its proud inhabitants for centuries to come. Should we keep these lands to ourselves, taking on the duty of warding the fertile plains of the Suran and Buranun against gnollish raids out of the desert ourselves, and benefitting from the mineral riches found within the mountain range? Or should we entrust the Zanšapi with the sacred duty of warding Surael’s Garden against outsiders, and return their homeland to them as they clearly expect us to? With the proper amount of support, a reborn kingdom of the Šad Sur might become a mighty pillar to support our budding empire, but some fear it might buckle once again under the weight of constant raids and incursions.
