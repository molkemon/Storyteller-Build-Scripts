The Empire is presented with a difficult symphony to join. Depending on the Parliament’s choices, all Imperial princes of the Borders may secede, and the Emperor will have to achieve diplomatic superiority or military success over the powerful rival to regain them. Path of Salt. The Emperor must have one hundred fifty or more relations, a royal marriage, and an alliance with an imperial prince in the Borders after twenty years to automatically reintroduce them into the Empire. Path of Silence. The Emperor and their electors will fight a war against the giant and force all princes to choose sides in the conflict. Victory results in a total Imperial reclamation, defeat results in the loss of the Borders.
