Through our relentless conquest of some of the most strategically important cities of Yanshen, we have shown ourselves to be unrivalled in the fields of war and siegecraft. The Yan have lost all fighting spirit when confronted with the possibility of facing our might. Their soldiers desert their posts when they see the dust kicked up by our hordes in the distance, their diplomats draft up conditional surrender documents before we even declare war, and their rulers beg one another for help against our neverending wave of conquest sweeping across their lands. We are truly the nightmare of Yanshen.
