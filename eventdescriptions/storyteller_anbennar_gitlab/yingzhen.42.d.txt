In the faroff lands of Cannor reside the Gnomish people. Strange, short folk that have an inscrutable talent in the arts of Artificery. Such a people are always bound to have some manner of outcasts; perhaps those with too great an ambition, or too misaligned in their ideas of ethics. Our agents have identified one such gnome, going by a name we cannot hope to pronounce. Imprisoned for, supposedly, 'unsavory testing methods,' we have laid plans to bring this wasted talent under new management.
