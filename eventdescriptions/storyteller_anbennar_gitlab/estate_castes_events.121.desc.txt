It is always horrible to see the poor suffer, yet the caste system forbids any real interference into their conditions. Sadly, in one of the cities, more and more people from the lower castes have lost their jobs and income and are forced into the slums, which have such terrible conditions and homes that one can clearly see where it starts and ends from a higher location. This has led to it being dubbed the Impoverished Isle or even the Island of the Destitute. The problem is so rampant that the court is now discussing what the best course of action is. We have to do at least something.
