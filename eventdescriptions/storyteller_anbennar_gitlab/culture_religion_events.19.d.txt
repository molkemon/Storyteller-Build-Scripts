Ever since their younger years, the heir has taken a great interest in the religion of their subjects. As they grew up, this has not faltered; instead, it seems that the religion is taking up more and more of their thoughts and dreams. The heir is now often seen surrounded by friends of that religion, and frequently even in the quarters of that religion. Many, both among nobles and clergy, have begun to question how proper this is, and some have openly begun to ask the monarch to talk sense into the heir.
