As the ruler and their personal guard travel to the dwarven gate, and delivers the ruler's challenge to the closed gate, the guards of the dwarven gate spread the news to the hold, forcing the chief to accept. When the chieftain arrives it became clear this would not be an easy fight, as the chief was clearly forged in battle, like any Black ork living in the holds are. As thousands of orks gather outside the gate to see this duel between the past and the future, the ruler sharpens their axe one last time.
