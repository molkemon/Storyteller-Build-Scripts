Castellos is dead. We find ourselves again on the realization that the gods we thought reincarnated are now falling apart, as our ancestors once did. We abandoned our Gerudian practices, believing the gods of the Regent Court were reborn gods of old. If Castellos is dead, what about the others? What about Nerat? How can we even be sure that any of the Regent Court are alive, claim the proponents of the return to the Skald Faith as they seek to return to our traditions. Still, there are two other groups. one that claims the Gnomes could be the way; despite all their losses and destruction, they have lasted for centuries thanks to The Thought. Now that we have integrated them into our realm, why not also incorporate their religion? The second option is even more fringe, whispers in the dark and rumours, but if we are a people of the sea, why not turn to Uelos? What should we do?
