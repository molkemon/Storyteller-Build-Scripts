The skies overseeing the Forbidden Plains were always of special religious importance for the centaurs living there, but their relevance for the Malukhuga is unmatched. Irdaeos watches us, to see how we employ the gifts he has granted upon our kind. Observe the skies, my son, look at that constellation. A boon of the skies. A shrewd and righteous centaur will be born tonight. Roc, Harbinger of Irdaeos. We humbly sacrifice this lamb so that you may feast on it. Bring our regards to he who commands you. The ruler knows about these customs of the Ghanzamh centaurs. Their proficiency for magic, their preference for divination and understanding of the movement of the lights of the night sky. And now he has become their chieftain. The subjugation of their clans was quick, and our unity in faith has allowed for common ground to be found, despite their wariness towards the Teaghud, towards us. As the mountains of Nuzurbokh are scraping the sky, so too does he, the ruler. Touching it; growing closer to it; in order to allow for us, for all of centaurkind to attain communion with he who has granted us entry in his domain. A shaman's retelling of the ruler's accomplishments.
