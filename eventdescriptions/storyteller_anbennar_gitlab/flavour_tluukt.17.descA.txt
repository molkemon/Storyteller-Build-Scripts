The messenger we dispatched to offer our hand in support of Drolakand's independence has returned today to the capital. We await good news and are well rewarded; the ruler of that domain has willingly acknowledged the supremacy of our monarch. Even better, in their hands the messenger bears the first tribute offered by our new subjects; a paltry amount of gold, but a gesture well received nonetheless. Gnollish rule over the Drolas peninsula had been in the past well supported by the native populations; they are a fierce people who assimilate well to Gnollish beliefs. Reasserting our hegemony over them was just a formality.
