Sramaya burns. One of the greatest cities in Haless, perhaps even in all of Halann, has been reduced to a shadow of a shadow over just a few short months ago. After capturing the city of rebels and slaves, none have been spared the wrath of the Southern Harimraj. For weeks, everything valuable was seized, and any traitorous dog lingering in the city was killed. When the Raja of Vanrahar finally felt it was enough, the order was given to cease, commencing the next phase in the destruction of Bhuvauri’s legacy. A great convoy of harimari settlers entered ruined Sramaya, their needs easily covered by loot from the city. After all, even though it was home to traitorous rats, it is no accident Sramaya became successful; the Kharunyana and Dhenbasana river confluence allowed the city to dominate trade from the sea to upriver markets. It shall do so again, but this time the city will not support fickle humans but a loyal harimari community. When Sramaya inevitably rebounds thanks to its important trade, it will stand as a great city of harimari excellence.
