In a large tent, located in the hills south of Keyolíon, stood the chieftains of the Dtsiaerh tribes. The monarch stepped into the tent and took a look at their surroundings. No weapons present, and no warriors either. A good sign. The monarch closed the entrance to the tent behind them and addressed the chieftains, "I am the monarch, the ruler of Eneion. I have received your request for a meeting. I have granted it, and now stand before you. So, what do you want to discuss?" The most powerful of the chieftains, a great warrior that towered over the monarch, responded, "We have heard of the deals you have made with the tribes to the south. We seek such a deal too." A visible smile crept up the monarch’s face, as they realized the best case scenario for the meeting was playing out before their very eyes.
