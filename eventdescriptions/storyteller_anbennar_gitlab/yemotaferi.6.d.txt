We have broken the Coven's legions, routed their lackeys, and destroyed any organized resistance. The Hags themselves have abandoned their cause, each seeking safety in their own way. Hireet, together with a small elite warband, managed to break through our lines and disappear into the jungles of Taneyas Komina. It is said that she has enthralled two of our Deathsworn and headed westward, but none of our squads managed to intercept her; unfortunately, it seems she is also lost to us. Nevertheless, we still have a chance to break the Coven for good by slaying Granny Ilfanil. We have learned she intends to escape into the Shadow Plane through a rift torn into the darkness. Her captured acolytes claim she could open such gates at the flick of her hand in the past, but ever since we brought the light of Amilak into the Shadow Swamp, she has struggled to do so. If these testimonies can be believed, we have her at a disadvantage. There are but a handful of truly black spots left in the swamp, and if we move fast, we can set up an ambush and take her by surprise.
