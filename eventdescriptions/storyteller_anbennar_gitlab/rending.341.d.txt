Hokuma, once the great spirit of the earth, now finds himself reduced to a mere shadow, his power drained away by the insidious Devouring Path Onis over many centuries. Consumed by fury, he manifests as an enigmatic mist of deep black and fiery red, a tangible reminder of the wrath that bubbles beneath. With an innate command over the very ground they tread upon, he can cause it to tremble and shake with his will. Yet, despite his immense power, Hokuma's true desire is twofold. a thirst for vengeance against the Onis who weakened him and a longing to restore and conserve his cherished hilly domain. It is in these hills that he seeks solace, a place to recuperate and find peace at long last.
