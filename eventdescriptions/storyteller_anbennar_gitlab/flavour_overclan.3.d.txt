Since assuming control over all notable entrances to the Serpentsreach, border guards have noticed a marked increase in goblin refugees attempting to cross into our borders. Reports as to conditions within the Serpentsreach itself are scattered, but paint a picture of anarchic disorganization among the goblin clans residing there, which, combined with significant pressure from ork and dwarf forces, aptly explains the exodus. While this dismal state of our kin which remain in the Serpentsreach is not encouraging, there is a potential benefit to the realm in this development. Goblin refugees often arrive impoverished and aimless, leaving them with few immediate prospects for survival. Offering these refugees monetary compensation in exchange for military service would therefore provide a mutual benefit, as this way our armed forces would receive a much needed influx of new recruits, whilst the refugees would gain the needed resources to establish themselves and become productive members of society.
