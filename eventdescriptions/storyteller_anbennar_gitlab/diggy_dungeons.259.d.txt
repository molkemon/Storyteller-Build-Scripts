Detecting the source of the illusion, a mage from the expedition carefully dispels the magic from the room. Slowly, the tables full of wine and food give way to simple rocks with a stream of water running through the cave, and mushrooms growing on the edges. One by one, the satyrs fade away, leaving only their yellowed bones on the ground. In the end, only one remains. A very old satyr, sitting listlessly in the corner of the cave. He is dying. He must have been a powerful mage indeed, to have maintained this elaborate illusion for so long, refusing to cope with the death of his companions. The expeditionaries quickly grab what they can from the room, and press on to let the old satyr die in peace.
