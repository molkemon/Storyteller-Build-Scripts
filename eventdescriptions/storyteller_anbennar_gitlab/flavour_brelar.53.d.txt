Long have we believed Brelar to be safe from falling into feudalism and serfdom, as has almost all the rest of the Ynn. A hope bred by hubris and mortared by complacency. Today the Order of the Mountain Eye brings to the ruler reports of collusion among our Iosahar. War is on the horizon as the armies of our subjects march upon Brelar, claiming righteousness in the betrayal of their sacred oaths citing the installation of a lord as justification for their sacrilege. Now is the time for all the people of the republic to stand up, to defend to the death the ideals of ancient Brefineis, that the embers of republicanism shall not be eternally wiped out.
