Cannon are booming, mighty, valuable, deadly, and suddenly in shortage; what rotten luck. Prodding our normal forges and distributors for this lapse in weapon stock, they say they have dealt with a sudden spike in demand, and when we pressed for who in our nation could be buying artillery, they replied. ogres. Our domestic weapon production satisfies all sorts of national and individual needs, but for the giantkin, our small arms are well, small. too small, at the very least. To suit their towering proportions, these ogres have instead purchased cannons to retrofit as handguns, rifles, and similar equipment. Although done legally and without ill intent, it does put our nation in a logistical bind. Waiting for domestic manufacturing to level out will take years, so we are left with two options. Either we pay premium prices to ensure we always have cannon, or we repossess the ogres’ purchases, earning their ire. How should we proceed?
