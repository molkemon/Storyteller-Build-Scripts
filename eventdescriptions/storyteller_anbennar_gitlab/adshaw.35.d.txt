Long ago, Crovis warred against the many orks of Vrorenmarch in hopes of finally liberating our Reachmen relatives from their grasp. It can be argued if he succeeded or failed in his original campaign, but his later achievements outshadow his youth. He retook the East from the remnants of the Tide and from all who opposed the unity we strove for. Through his actions, we are now united. Through his actions, we now prosper.
