A major, and somewhat unsurprising, discovery was made when expanding Haraz Orldhûm. The platinum hold is, in fact, filled with platinum. More specifically, lots of jewelry and trinkets, which seem to be very pretty, but most importantly, valuable. While keeping the shinies to ourselves is certainly a promising proposition, our recent diplomatic attempts with nations outside the mountains could greatly benefit from us using these as a bargaining chip, to show we are capable of commerce. Plus, the money we may gain from it is certainly not bad either.
