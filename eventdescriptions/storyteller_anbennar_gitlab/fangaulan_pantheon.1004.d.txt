Droplets echo through the dim and spiraling cavern hallways of the Oasis of Water Dreams. An ancient subterranean complex in Rostgrax, the temple lionizes gods and heroes great and small, but one statue stands prouder and taller than all others – Biakata. Stark and fierce, the air planetouched Empress’ stony visage looms beneath the very ceiling, threatening to rise again and reform her fallen Second Fangaula Empire. But the water drops continue echoing. Two stone slabs resting on her shins like armor plates recall the Mukarron heroine’s conquests, aweinspiring use of opportunity and superior tactics, and feats of statecraft and lawmaking that outlived her Empire.
