We clear the passage of rubble, making an ample opening. Odd colorful glitters appear in the distance. Relieved, we walk to the exit. Suddenly behind us, the cavern roof starts dropping small pebbles. The miners feel stomach butterflies. Before they could say "run", the passage starts collapsing, rumbling and raising rockdust. We sprint towards the exit. The speed of a good number of us is not enough as they get trapped and crushed by the collapsing rocks. Wheezing our lungs out, we make it. We look around confused, worried, and surrounded by red, blue, green, and purple glimmers. We rest a bit before mourning our friends.
