It started with naught but a rumble, the earth releasing a great groan. Then the shaking began in earnest, and a great burst of lava was thrown. The sky darkened with ash as the world was set alight. I led my Paladins to the mountain; all who could gave swift flight. We conjured our great magics, containing the lava as best we could, while my priestesses rushed to help all who had withstood. In the end, the province was ruined; all was burnt to black ash. Afomapobo had stirred from its slumber, for we had been too brash.
