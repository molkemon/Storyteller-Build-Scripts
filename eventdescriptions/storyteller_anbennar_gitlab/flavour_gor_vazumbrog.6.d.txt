To the Majesty, the ruler, it is with measured optimism that I, Commander Durvin Ironmantle, provide an update regarding Azmak Quarry following your command to pursue diplomacy with the fungrum inhabitants. As instructed, we approached the myconids with respect, and to our astonishment, they demonstrated a willingness to engage in dialogue. While steadfast in their adherence to what they deem sacrosanct—the seclusion of their sanctuary and the rituals surrounding the Sporeweaver—they proposed an alternative site for our citadel. This location, situated by the more open part of Dwarovrod tunnels, was surveyed by our military engineers and deemed not only viable but exceptional in its defensive potential and access to resources. In return for our agreement to respect their customs and avoid encroaching upon their sacred spaces, the fungrum have pledged preferential treatment for our traders passing through the quarry. Furthermore, they have offered to hinder any enemies seeking passage through their domain, providing an unexpected boon to our strategic positioning. The fortification efforts are already underway. Interestingly enough, this successful negotiation has struck a chord with our garrison, who now refer to our newfound citadel as 'Hyggrip Brok', or Joyous Mushroom Fort. I await further instructions but see great promise in this burgeoning alliance. Your loyal servant, Durvin Ironmantle
