As one travels through the Serpentspine, Veladklufar can be spotted in abundance. These roachlike creatures are renowned for their iridescent scales, but those found in the province possess a unique feature; a deep red hue that sets them apart. The origins of this rare color are still debated among our scholars; some claim it is a result of their diet while others argue it to be a rare mutation. Regardless, industrious merchants have recently discovered a way to extract a vibrant pigment from these insects. Though the process of harvesting is labor-intensive and yields modest results, the demand for this exclusive dye easily justifies the effort put into its production. The locals have even given it a name; the Blood of Dookan, a nod to its rich crimson shade. With proper support from the state, we could potentially turn this discovery into a thriving local industry.
