A member of the migrant Cardesti community currently settled in this realm has gained notoriety for successive accurate predictions of future events, foreseeing important events such as upcoming rainstorms and monster attacks; and even smaller, more personal things like whether a newlywed couple is going to have a son or a daughter. Using a combination of magical talent and millennia of experience passed down by their predecessors, the Cardesti are quite renowned in the field of divination.
