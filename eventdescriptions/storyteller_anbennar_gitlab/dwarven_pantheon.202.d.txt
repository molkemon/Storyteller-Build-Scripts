The Stone Keepers, the traditional priests of Ancestor Worship, had long been an important part of the country. However, with the official conversion to Pantheonism, the communities they represent and the power they once had have become increasingly marginalized. Now, several representatives have come forward, demanding that if the country will not return to the fold, then it must formally issue a rather humiliating series of concessions, such as letting them worship and practice in peace, among others.
