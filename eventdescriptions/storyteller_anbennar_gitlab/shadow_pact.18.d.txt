The monarch has begun to have dreams of a misty forest, with dancing lights flickering in the trees. The ruler walks slowly through the woods, tentatively exploring the shadowy wilderness. The more the ruler dreams of the forest, the more the ruler explores, and after some time the ruler hears a curious sound; a voice, calling to them from within the mist. Barely audible at first, then growing to a soft whisper. "Join us...join us..." When the monarch finally arrives at the center of the forest, the ruler sees a large blob of shadow in the middle of a clearing, and from the sphere of darkness a wizened arm extends outward, beckoning them to take their hand. The whisper returns, louder than ever. "Untold power..."
