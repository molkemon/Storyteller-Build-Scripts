It is like a scene from a frightening campfire story. Harpies descended upon a village in one of our provinces, attacked it, and let loose the, as it were, birds of war upon its innocent inhabitants. This has further worsened their already decreased standing in the realm, and now the people of our country are demanding vengeance. But an argument can be made for instead focusing on repairing the affected area or even trying to defuse tensions.
