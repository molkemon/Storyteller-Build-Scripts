With their influence growing ever growing and spurred on by recent successes, the Shadowhammer Clan has begun providing further support for the administration in managing the newly conquered lands of Dwarovar. A supply of fresh runes and young bureaucrats looking to make a name for themselves have arrived from the Serpentdepths to help with the cleansing of the traitors’ symbols, and their meticulous investigations into every dark corner of the realm has resulted in a more efficient integration.
