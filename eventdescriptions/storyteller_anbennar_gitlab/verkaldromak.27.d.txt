The monarch is floating in space looking down on a barren but ever-shifting planet. Instinctively they know it is Halann. As they get closer they notice some of the distinct forms of the Serpentspine, but besides it the world does not seem to come to rest. They start to cry over the perils Halann seems to be in. But suddenly the rock starts to shift at a place that can only be Earthseed, and dwarves emerge from the depth. They quickly start tending to the writhing of Halann's surface and give it form and consistency. The calmness spreading over Halann reaches the monarch, and they sink back into the void of their sleep.
