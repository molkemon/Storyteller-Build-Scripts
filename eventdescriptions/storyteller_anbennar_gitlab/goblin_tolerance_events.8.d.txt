While Goblins are typically lacking in strength and bravery, they make it up in their cunning and innovation. Consisting of the more creative minds across Halann, Goblins will oftentimes find the most surprising solutions to issues that can plague traditional scholars for years. Now, one of the most innovative of their kind has come to offer their services to the ruler and use their mind for the betterment of the realm, provided we pay for any damages that may occur during their research.
