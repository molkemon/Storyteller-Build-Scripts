The ground is slick with something that was once a teacher. The leader does not speak. The captive does not beg. The captive just kneels there, blood soaking into his robes, placidly watching the other leader circle them. "It is written in the annals," the other leader begins, "that Kaspar szalRexar told Xhuvari Bonechewer that she was a slave." The leader crouches in front of them, leaning in. "I find myself thinking of those words, now." A flick of a hand. A line of figures are brought closer; humans, elves, gnolls, centaur, harimari, and more. All are chained, and all are wounded. "Knowledge can't save you now," the other leader whispers. "No more clever lies or witticisms. All that's left is to learn when to shut up." The leader meets the other leader's eyes. Their voice is hoarse. "Zokka won't approve." The other leader only laughs, low and guttural. "Zokka gave us teeth, the leader. I'm simply using mine." The leader stands and turns away. Behind the other leader, more slaves are herded like cattle, bound and bloodied and breathing; for now. "This isn't a purge," the other leader says over their shoulder. "This is a correction." The doors slam shut. The slaughter begins.
