Expecting the unexpected, we draw ourselves for Yidab's upcoming attack. Though we are not sure what exactly she may do as she wraps herself in her webs, we will keep alert to what is certainly some sort of ploy. As more and more time passes, we begin to wonder if Yidab is not just taking a rest, but the possibility of this being some sort of trick to get us to lower our guard or attack is too high, so we keep alert. Finally, after what seems like minutes, Yidab comes out of her cocoon. While we were ready for anything she might throw our way, we were not expecting her to yawn and continue the fight as if nothing had happened. She was just taking a rest.
