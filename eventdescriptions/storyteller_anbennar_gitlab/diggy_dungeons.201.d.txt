The cave ends in a very narrow opening, between the stone remnants of a tree and a large boulder that rests against it. The mining engineers look at it doubtfully. The lead engineer says, "We can try to cut pieces out of the tree and the boulder to widen the entrance, but the whole cave ceiling rests on it. We would have to work very carefully and with support beams, and even then it is a bit risky. It might be safer to just dig past here and create our own entrance in the greater cave system."
