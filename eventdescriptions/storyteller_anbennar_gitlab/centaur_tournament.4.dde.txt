As the monarch steps into the arena wielding the Gaohernlangh, a massive curved greatsword with a long wooden handle, favored mainly due to its destructive slashing power, the monarch hears a familiar voice on the opposite end of the arena. "It would be my honor to face you in this tournament, Caehn." The monarch notices that it is one of their well-respected Ordsikar, a veteran of many battles, and a loyal ally in the past wars. Carefully moving in on the Ordsikar, the Caehn takes the initiative as the monarch knows that this enemy could probably outlast them. Slash after slash, block after block, the Ordsikar’s defense seems impenetrable, until at last a slight opening shows itself. Could it be a feint? Seeing as this could be their only chance, however, the monarch takes it. The crowd gasps, and…
