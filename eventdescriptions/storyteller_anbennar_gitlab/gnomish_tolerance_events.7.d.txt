While comedy is a well-established gnomish tradition, some real comedic geniuses among their number have decided to insult our ruler. Their criticism has been widely circulated and hit a little too close to home, but they feign ignorance over this concept they call 'free speech'. While we could invoke antisedition laws, it would be seen as overkill, and more comedians would certainly remark upon our thin skins. What shall we do?
