It has been some time since the Lilac Wars and their direct effects have been largely forgotten, through new peace and newer wars their memory has dulled. Yet we still recall when we were plagued by soldiers turned bandits in the outlying districts of our realm. It was only through new expansions of our levies and better use of them that most bandits were killed or otherwise driven away. Most is not all though, and the suffering caused has birthed many would-be soldiers and novice adventurers. Among them is a teenager who has arrived in the capital. He has gathered a militia on his own initiative to support the forces we sent to clear bandits out and showed a knack for command as he did so. We have space for him, and the only question is how to put him to task?
