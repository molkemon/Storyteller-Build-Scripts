We have travelled to Rzenta to acquire the stained glass piece that we commissioned several years prior. As expected, it is truly beautiful. The Ynn is represented with brilliant blue shards, the Cliffs of Ruin cutting across it in gray. To the east, the autumnal forests of the Cursed Ones are represented in orange and reds, and in the west, green grass and brown sands mirror the Epednan Expanse. It is a gorgeous piece, and will surely be well received within Medaka’s Exceptional Treasures.
