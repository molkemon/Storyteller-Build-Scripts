Even now, they will not fawn over you. It is their sacred duty to avert Tsishagya, the madness of the one. They will watch you for it, your monarch. You will never go unobserved again. A time will come soon when you must once more return to the heavy places and prove yourself, but for now the naga are anchored at your back. A hundred millennia of lizardfolk strategy lie open to you with but a query, while rural nobility, cajoled by their local naga, are suddenly eager to volunteer labor for your logistical efforts. For the right monarch, one who ever keeps an eye on the coming Storm, the Naga will risk even their own exalted blood. Take heed; the next test the Naga set you will prove more rigorous.
