Chieftain Aadga stood before the newly built pens outside Venomfen. She crossed her arms as she observed the goblins being herded into the pens. They were emaciated and tired. An eighth of their number had already died on the march over here, with guards stabbing the fallen to ensure their deaths. The goblins had the look of defeat on their haggard faces. They had fought to keep their only recently gained independence, but it had been all for naught. Aadga pulled out her dagger and walked over to the table full of bowls in front of the pen. She looked through each one, all different experimental concoctions that needed to be tested out. She dipped the dagger in the first bowl, which contained a mixture of worm venom and hemlock. A goblin was dragged before her, an elderly female with a resigned look in her eyes. Aadga nicked the goblin’s skin with the blade. The goblin started gasping for air, her eyes opening wide as she clutched her throat and looked around. Aadga scoffed as she watched the goblin slowly suffocate until falling lifelessly to the ground. She looked at a nearby scribe and said, “This concoction is inadequate. The goblin neither experienced drawn out suffering nor a quick death. It is not fit for battle or torture.” She got up and looked at the hunters who now formed a circle around her. She laid out several daggers on the table. “Since I have more important things to do with my time, I will need you to test out these different poison mixtures to find out which is the most effective. You will perform several tests with each mixture and observe the effects. Do not worry if you run out of goblins; we will bring you as many as needed to continue the tests. And remember, I need one potent mixture to coat all our elite vanguard’s weapons with and another for my showdown with Rogier.” She clapped her hands. “Now then, you may begin the trials.”
