In the hushed stillness that precedes a storm, a palpable tension hangs in the air, as the collective breath of disgruntled veterans and hardened soldiers holds the promise of an imminent rebellion. Under a new banner, many people have risen to depose our rule over their city, led by a general of the old guard who has served the realm for decades. Their rabble threatens the progress we have made across the lands and can potentially lead to a disastrous cascading effect across the others. We cannot risk the rebellion spreading across our lands lest our kingdom collapse and go back to being treated as mere barbarians. We must truly display our military prowess against these insurgents with all we have. Assemble every spearman and boar rider, for we march for the realm, to save the civilians still loyal to us.
