The fall of the stormwall has revealed not only a vast new continent, but also a new people unlike any we have encountered before. The mechanim, believed to be originally built by the Precursors as a labor caste, had been dormant for untold centuries before being awakened several hundred years ago by the passing of the Oracle’s Eye Comet. Beings made of metal, they tell us that they were cast out by the Precursors after gaining sentience, abandoned by their creators. Given the disappearance of the Precursors, they are now the de facto authority on the Gene Pillars, vast facilities that stretch over a kilometer into the sky. These are veritable troves of Precursor artifacts, full of ancient designs and research experiments. Azaly’s brightest apprentices have been sent to explore the Gene Pillar in a specific province, accompanied by several mechanim guides paid in Dames tear. Their goal. to study the ancient internal teleportation network that has been used to traverse the nearly unnavigable structures and see if they could determine how to replicate it within Bivuzena. Battling ancient defense systems gone haywire, they gathered notes, studied glyphs, and returned to Bivuzena with several mechanim eager to assist with their research. While the specifics of the full network have yet to be uncovered, smallscale temporary teleportation gates powered by mages have been successfully tested; a step in the right direction. The mechanim who returned with the apprentices have integrated well into the city, and several of their poetry pieces are proudly displayed within Medaka’s Exceptional Treasures.
