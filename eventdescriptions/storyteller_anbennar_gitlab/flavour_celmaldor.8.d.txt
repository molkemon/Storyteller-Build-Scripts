The migrant ships of old. how great and glorious they were. How safe they were to travel in; how reassuring were their silhouettes on the horizon. How mighty they were in protecting our people as they traveled to their intended destination. If we are to travel farther afield, and settle lands that even our distant ancestors may have known nothing about, we should contemplate returning the migrant ships to existence.
