Sandstorms are no stranger to any resident of the Salahad, but the past series of storms affecting the country have been almost malevolent. The whipping sands seem to grasp at one's limbs, physically dragging them down. Any significant movement across the country, such as the march of the army, is slowed to a crawl by the storms, which necessitate frequent breaks lest one be brought down and buried beneath the dunes.
