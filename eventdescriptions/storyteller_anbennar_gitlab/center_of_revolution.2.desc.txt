A poet and scholar from a certain province has made a name for himself among his brethren by distributing a large number of nationalist pamphlets and even composing a national anthem. Denouncing what he describes as oppressive rule and drawing on the ideals of the Revolution, he calls on his countrymen to pack their bags, quit their homeland, and seek freedom in the Promised Land of the New World. It seems that his words have struck a chord among the people, with one influential nobleman praising him as "the hottest arsed person I have ever met". Already, many of his countrymen, radicalized by his ideas, are departing for a certain country.
