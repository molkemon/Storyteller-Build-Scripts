When this rebellion began, the Grand Marshal gave an address to a sea of people. soldiers, their discipline strong, even if their courage faltered. And the rebellion ends with an address to a group of similar size. captured mages, bound one and all with Korashi, and made to kneel by the soldiers arrayed around them. "You have betrayed the Great Command. You have betrayed honour, betrayed duty, betrayed what it is to live. And so you have forfeited your lives." By our oldest laws and deepest values, I sentence you all to death. For a fifth, that death will come now. And for the rest, that death will come after service in chains. "But make no mistake. You are fallen, now. And never again shall you rise." The soldiers begin to move through the crowd, their blades falling on every fifth captive. And as blood begins to pool among the crowd, the Grand Marshal knows their victory has finally finished what began when the Shamans were overthrown so many years ago.
