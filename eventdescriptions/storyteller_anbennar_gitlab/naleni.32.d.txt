The royal treasurer awaits the Queen's attention, flexing their wings in agitation. They report irregular figures in the royal treasury and quickly explain that they believe some of the Odanchésta are keeping more than their fair share of the loot, having cross-referenced ship logs with those kept by the Sirens. Before Ríva's reforms, the Odanchésta would keep the vast majority of their loot and only tithe a small amount to the Matriarchy. Now they are expected to hand over the largest portion, but seemingly not all are content to do so.
