The Dwarovrod is dangerous, extremely so. Every dwarf in our nation knows that from experience, or from the experience of others who failed to account for the danger. With the new wave of colonizers coming from the outside into our holds, these lessons must be taught again. Unfortunately, even with our teachings, "do not go into the Dwarovrod alone," "have many backup lanterns," and "bring mining equipment in case of caveins," many new members of our nation do not respect them. This often leads to permanent injuries or even deaths for the newcomers, along with lost supplies. What should be done about these fools?
