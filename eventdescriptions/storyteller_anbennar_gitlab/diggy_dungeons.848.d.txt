As we were working on removing the barricades, suddenly the statues lining the walls began moving. Their eyes gleamed deep red, their granite weapons bent on removing the intruders from the premises in one way or another. They approached us, their steps echoing through the hall and pointing spear and blade towards us. In any other situation, we would have easily defeated the golems, but because most people had laid down their weaponry, not expecting an attack. In the rough scramble to arms, many were already cut down by the enemy, and only after some time did we manage to gain an advantage. Eventually the golems were all destroyed, their rocks now the rubble preventing us from going further.
