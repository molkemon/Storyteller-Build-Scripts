The realm of Y27 and the current domain have been separated from one another for centuries, though in truth this is largely due to neither them nor us having the ability to see the other fully reintegrated. Now that the state has entirely eclipsed theirs in terms of strength, it is only natural that they bend the knee such that we can be one realm once again. All the inhabitants of Y27 will be given full citizenship, and their ruling family provided an adequate pension as compensation for their cooperation. Everything important is already in order, so now we need only to wait for the final integration into our administration.
