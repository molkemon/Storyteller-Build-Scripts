When asked about the greatest nation, Ves Udzenklan undeniably springs to mind as the first choice for all who respond. The nation consistently churns out innovation after innovation. The Glorious Erectors construct marvels previously unseen in the many caverns and roads of the Serpentspine, while the Harmaceuticals ensure that their products flow down the rails at a remarkable pace. The Cloddhopper’s expeditions into the depths of the cavedwellings yield ancient treasures one after another, flooding wealth through the streets of the Holds and bending the creatures within to the nation's command. The Cloak and Swaggers adeptly infiltrate the innermost circles of neighboring realms, unearthing every secret and discovering every angle to exploit. Mithril extracted from the mines finds its way to markets from Castonath to Brasan, while the Iron is forged in smithies across Cannor and Gerudia, from plate and sword to tools and fineware. Serpentbloom springs forth in abundance, nourishing the everexpanding goblin population. The bounty of the Serpentspine is readily available at their fingertips, regardless of their residence within these bounds. The elite armies are unmatched, ready to face any adversary with nothing but the finest in arms and armor, defending the glory and safety of the people. Across Ves Udzenklan, the citizens bask in the prosperity and riches, an unprecedented luxury within these dark corridors. From Khugdihr to Hul Jorkad, every goblin within the borders experiences the stability provided by the nation. The achievements stand higher than any in the past, and it is likely that none will rival them anytime soon. The greatness towers above all else on Halann.
