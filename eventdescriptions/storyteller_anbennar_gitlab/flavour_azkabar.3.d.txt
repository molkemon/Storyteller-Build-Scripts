Another day in Azkabar. Within the borders of the city thrives a vibrant community of faithful and loyal children of Surakel. Or at least, so the ruler would desperately say to any watchful eye from the many temples of the Garden. Away from the warmth and revealing light of the sun, rests and ferments a rot which mars the city's commerce, administration, and good name. Drugs and alcohol are peddled over games of chance in houses of ill repute, scorning the good name of Surakel, speaking his name with irreverence as they plot ever more fiendish and vile schemes. Cracking down on these practices is the natural option, but this is a rot that is deep set, and in the dark of this night, as the ruler silently rests in solitude and contemplation, they cannot help but consider the fruits of cooperation. the wealth, the power, the control.
