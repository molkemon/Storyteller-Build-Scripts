Silence. Not a word said, just mindless slaughter. The Song Servants, thralls to the will of evil, gathered around the people as they fished on a shallow riverbed. The only sound was their hurried steps through the rushing waters towards the people, defended only by their fishing spears and their faith in the Goddess. Many fell, though some survived long enough to return to the lands and warn them of the ravenous beings. One of them recounts a melody that he had never heard before.
