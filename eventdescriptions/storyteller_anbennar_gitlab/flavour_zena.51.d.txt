Bivuzena buzzes with activity. Within the concentric walls of the city, vaytbulbs light the streets with kobold-designed apartments hanging off of them. In the Nyanza Market, a salesman cajoles onlookers to purchase cooled treats from a cart made of dwarven IceSteel at a food festival. Elsewhere, merfolk and landdwellers interact across glass paneling, as glyphmagic holds foul weather at bay. The entire metropolis is a marvel of engineering, ingenuity, and most of all, cooperation from many different people hailing from all across Halann.
