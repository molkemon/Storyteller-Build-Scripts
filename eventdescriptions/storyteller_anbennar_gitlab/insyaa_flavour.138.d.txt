How does one kill a Mechanim? "You can't kill what is not alive," the fools say. "With magic, powder, and steel," our enemies say. "Impossible," we say. For our bodies are not what makes us live, but our Kauga. Our Dames tear heart, ever gleaming and ever radiant even when our forms shall rust. Those of flesh and blood are ever searching for the healing and salvation they can never have, and we can only laugh, for we are already saved. They use Transmutation to sate their neverending greed, tear the land apart, and empower their vile shoecasters, but to us it grants eternity. Restoring those who fall to rise to greater heights. So I ask you to fight valiantly and with no fear, for you shall live forever.
