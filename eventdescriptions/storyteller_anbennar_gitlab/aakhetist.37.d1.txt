"Finally, I can play this card," proclaims a clearly delighted Aakhet. "I play Dragonwake. With this card I can summon every dragon card in my hand. Come forth, Nimrith the Red, Zaamalot the Black, and Alos the Copper." The room itself began to shake, as draconic forms swirled around the table. Aakhet's eyes started to glow, and you swore you could see flames flicker beneath his grin. "My brothers and sisters return to me for this game. None of them survived until the end of Dragonwake, but I doubt you can survive your next turn."
