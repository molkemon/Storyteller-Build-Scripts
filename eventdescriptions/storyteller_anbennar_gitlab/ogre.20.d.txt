The haughty Lakemen felt themselves safe on their little island. They thought themselves untouchable after defeating the centaurs all those years ago. They never thought the ogres would arrive from the shores of the Frost Forest. They never thought the ogres would come from the sea to attack their very heart. Today, the Gorger Lord has opened the Lake Federation’s chest to expose its delicious beating innards. The ogres of the horde gladly accept this offering from their lord and will feast upon the land itself. They will gorge themselves upon the Federation and when they are done with that, they will gorge themselves upon the rest of the lands east of Great Skurkokli, stripping it bare. All of it.
