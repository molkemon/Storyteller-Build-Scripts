Our country is faced with a dilemma. Since their invasion, the realm has ever been plagued by orks. We have rounded them up and sent them to a distant land to pay for their crimes, but much remains to be done. There remains a sizable orkish element in our lands, an orkish question, if you will, and it demands a solution. The military establishment, in conjunction with the Triadic Devotees, has proposed a solution. While the orks are savage and utterly inhuman, and while our culture and creed theoretically forbids them from even residing within our borders, they can be useful. After all, they were able to destroy the weak Escanni kingdoms of yesteryear, which they did under the leadership of Korgus Dookanson. Dookanson, who died, as was proper, to a human, led them well. We will do better. Under our leadership, the orks will serve as a near-infinite source of free bodies, throwing themselves against our enemies over and over again, sparing their human betters from risking their lives in battle. An insurgent element in the military establishment, which is affiliated with the Grudgeknights, disagrees violently. They have charged the military establishment with being unfaithful to Agrados’ ideals and are vociferously supporting a widespread purge of the orkish element. This move would likely serve to greatly boost our military readiness and preparedness by providing the troops with an adequate target to train their weapons on. No more playfighting.
