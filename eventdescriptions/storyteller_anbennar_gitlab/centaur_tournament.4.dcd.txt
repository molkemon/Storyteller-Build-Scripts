As the monarch enters the arena, a gleaming spear held firmly in hand, their eyes lock onto an unfamiliar clansman on the opposite side. This young and robust warrior belongs to a minor clan within the realm, determined to make his mark in the tournament. The duel begins. The youthful warrior, eager to strike first, charges at the Caehn. The monarch anticipates an easy victory and swiftly sidesteps, sweeping their spear in a wide arc. However, the young warrior proves to be more agile and skilled than expected. As they clash, the dust stirred by their hooves fills the air, shrouding the view of the spectators. The arena becomes a whirlwind of spears and swift movements, leaving the crowd in suspense. Until, at last…
