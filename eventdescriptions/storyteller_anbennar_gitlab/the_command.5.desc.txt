The people here are Wuhyun now or at least, the people in charge are Wuhyun. Their culture and faith are that of the Command. But there are other people who live here, people who might be faithfully restored to their previous position as leaders of the community. And so the Wuhyun question. what is to be done about the Wuhyun? We may continue the policies of the Command and collaborate to allow them to remain in power, or we may turn a blind eye to what riots, what infighting, or what certain inevitabilities might occur and see the Wuhyun disintegrate back into the annals of history.
