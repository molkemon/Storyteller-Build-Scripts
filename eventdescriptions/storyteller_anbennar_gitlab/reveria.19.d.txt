With the Vels Bacar Dam in our domain, our Ynnic Reaver fleet can raid the southern reaches of this subcontinent. At this point, the logistics of our raids are honed to near perfection, so each raid goes rather smoothly, but the southern Ynnics put up a valiant fight in some of the more developed settlements. When our men sail towards the fiefdom of Stanyrhrada, a marvelous golden gleam blinds them as the sun reflects off a great golden dome atop the city's castle. It has now become clear that the domes seen in other Ynnic settlements were mere imitations. Under duress, the locals tell us it is a magical dome from a bygone era that allegedly casts a spell of good fortune over the region. Sadly, due to the castle's extensive fortifications, our raiders cannot take it yet. It is as desirable as it is out of reach; we simply must come back for this dome.
