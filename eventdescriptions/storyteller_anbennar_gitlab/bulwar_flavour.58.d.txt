Tell my lord. I believe I know who was behind the recent string of unauthorized sorcery around our capital. This is because I spoke with the people who claim to be responsible only yesterday. They are followers of the Cerulean Mother; as they knew I was involved in the investigation, they invited me to their meeting. They wanted to come clean and present a defense for their acts. They discussed their desire to expand research on magic and arcane knowledge in our country. Still, because the local temples are hardline followers of the Jexisian Regulations, they have remained hidden until now. They show me their projects and books; none of what I saw seems to be what we could consider forbidden Binding magic or the Evil Eye. If anything, their spells look like the Light spells our Exorcist priest uses, but the way the spells operate feels familiar yet foreign, as they are probably related to the practices of old Dameria. Their knowledge is vast, and they have shown an incredible talent for understanding and casting magic, so an alliance with them would be a boon to our kingdom if we are to expand our magic infrastructure and research.
