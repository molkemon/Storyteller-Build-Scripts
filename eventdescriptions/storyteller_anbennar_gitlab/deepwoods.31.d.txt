Throughout the war of the Black Trees, Gotag Baneaxe has proven himself to be an ork amongst orks, a champion of the Fey, and a hero of our people. Cutting down the so-called ‘Pure orks’ by the dozen, with never a hint of fear in his eyes, only the fury of an ork and the power of the Fey. As well as his prowess on the battlefield, he has proven himself to be a skilled bureaucrat, managing our resources throughout the war, and acting as an excellent commander amongst the troops. The question now rises. with the war over, what role shall Gotag fulfill?
