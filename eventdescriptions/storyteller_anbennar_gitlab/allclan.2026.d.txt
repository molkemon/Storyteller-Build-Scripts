The Pharmaceuticals have an issue because they have a shortage of some alchemical goods. Luckily, they found that a certain pink slime species naturally produces a precursor for the synthesis of one of their drugs. They are pushing to issue a state-wide order to capture all pink slimes. Sadly, these slimes are often held and sought after by goblin children since they are harmless and kind of adorable. Along with that, the Clodhoppers fiercely oppose that as they are the main hunters of such creatures and do not wish to exterminate that species just to be used for a few more drugs. What shall we do?
