As our clans have established contact, the ruler of Siegebreaker has been sending shamans to our people calling us upon our sacred duty to fight the humans and their gods, who they hold accountable for Dookan being imprisoned. As they preach in the streets about the imminent victory of Korgus in Escann and his need for reinforcements from the faithful, many keep walking, but some do stand and listen. Such heretical thoughts largely fall on deaf ears in our society. We all know only the dwarves are responsible for His imprisonment. As the ruler orders the shamans to leave the realm, a small but not insignificant amount of orks follow them. Heretics who believe that Korgus is the son of Dookan. Even worse, some of our most prominent clan elders are leaving for Escann as well.
