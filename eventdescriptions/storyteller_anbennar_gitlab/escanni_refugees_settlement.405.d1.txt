The administration we have set up is heavily inspired by where we come from. Even if some influence of the former Escanni realms can be felt, our rule is quite different from what Escann used to know. Chivalric Escann is long gone, and what we have built is meant to replace, not simply emulate and rebuild. As such, the refugees that expected a return to what their parents or grandparents knew and taught them feel a bit conflicted. Should we assuage their discomfort?
