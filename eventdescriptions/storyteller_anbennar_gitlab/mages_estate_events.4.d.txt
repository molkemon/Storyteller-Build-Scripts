Within Anbennar and much of Western Cannor, magical rulers have long been seen as wise and almost divinely chosen. A noble within our realm wishes to push his house in that direction and petitions us to disinherit his eldest in favor of a younger, more magically gifted heir. The noble families within our realm warn that this goes against the very traditions that keep the country together, whilst the magical practitioners subtly support one of their own in power, as usual.
