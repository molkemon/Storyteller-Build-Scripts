Now that we occupy the Imperial throne, we have the means to make our will known in the Empire at large. Chief among our concerns is the land of the northern Vernmen, those who were left outside our borders through the vicissitudes of Imperial and regional politics. They must be returned to us, a brother people who belong within our realm. We cannot allow petty concerns of Imperial protocol and precedence to prevent the rightful unification of the Vernman people. Let us bring this matter to the Empire and see it resolved.
