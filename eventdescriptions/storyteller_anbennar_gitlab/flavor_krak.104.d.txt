The Ice Field is a spell which creates a large, intense blizzard anchored to a single location where it will grow to encompass an entire region. Our IceSmiths first used it in the Northern Pass to create the great blizzard there, and the experiment was a great success. They say that with enough new towers in other regions, they could create similar effects elsewhere. Northern Pass has all original mage towers intact. West Dwarovar has twelve mage towers. Serpentreach has eight mage towers. Middle Dwarovar has nine mage towers. Tree of Stone has seven mage towers. Jade Mines has six mage towers.
