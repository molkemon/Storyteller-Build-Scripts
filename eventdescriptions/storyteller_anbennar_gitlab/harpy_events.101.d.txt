The death of the previous ruler without a designated favored daughter sent her daughters into a flurry of activity, as planned violences flared and woven plots ensnared competitors. But in the end, it was the new monarch that would triumph. She was never regarded as particularly exceptional and was never the favored child of her mother, but perhaps it is this very lack of distinction that allowed her to escape the attentions of her sisters. With her more competent competitors eliminating each other, the new monarch endured and has been proclaimed the new ruler of the dynasty flock. She will lead it with her unassuming demeanor.
