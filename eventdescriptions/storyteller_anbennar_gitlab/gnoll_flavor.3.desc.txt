During a discussion with a powerful slaver in the realm, the monarch heard tales of the exploits of one slave, who one day presented a well-thought-out proposal to his overseer as to how the production in the province could be greatly improved. He was then, of course, punished severely for his disrespect toward his superiors. The slaver told the story for a laugh, asking how a slave could be so insolent, but the monarch was not so sure. An individual with such skills could prove extremely valuable to the government. But was the ruler prepared to subvert the entirety of society for this one individual?
