Originally a jeweller from ancient Ovdal Lodhum, Werdûn Diamondeye was challenged by his peers to cut a gemstone that would captivate the gods with his craft. After a long and gruelling journey through the Tree of Stone and back, he returned to Ovdal Lodhum and presented a cut garnet to his peers. They told him that its match was in the possession of Dezobrazan herself. After making this declaration, he vanished, and the temple in Ovdal Lodhum dedicated to her contained a new mosaic depicting the wedding of Werdûn and Dezobrazan. As the patron of courtship and love, many young dwarves sought his blessing for happy marriages.
