On a tranquil evening, the monarch settled upon the terrace of the court, overlooking the myriad islands under their dominion. The ruler watched as the sea swallowed the sun, and the moon assumed its solemn duty, casting the lands in its soothing radiance. It was a much-needed moment of respite for the monarch after the recent calamities that had beset the court. However, this moment of serenity proved short-lived. A deep resonance echoed through the islands, disrupting the tranquil atmosphere. "A storm?" the monarch wondered aloud, their thoughts momentarily preoccupied by the possibility. Another resounding roar followed, this time even more powerful, and the ground beneath them quivered. It was not the sensation of an earthquake, though. With swift resolve, the monarch rose from their seat and hastened toward the palace. Two guards and a court wizard approached the monarch with faces etched in alarm as they made their way to the palace. They attempted to speak, but no words emerged, their expressions of awe conveying more than any words could. It was at that moment that the palace was abruptly enveloped in shadow. The monarch turned their gaze toward the source, and there it emerged. Cinulau, The Guardian of The Sea, appeared in all its colossal splendor. This massive whale dwarfed even the largest of the islands, leaping from the inky depths of the black sea. It left a trail of sky-blue energy in its wake. With this unforeseen appearance, it was clear that the serenity of the seas would no longer be assured.
