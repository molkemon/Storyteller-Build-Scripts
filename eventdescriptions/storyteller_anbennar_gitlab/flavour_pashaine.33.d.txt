Unusually among states, we were born of a conversation. A centuries-long conversation between philosophers, artists, novelists, and statesmen which posed a simple question. who do we wish to be? The state we have built relies on continuous checks, continuous reorganization, and change, just as the guilds of a city ebb and flow in power and new guilds form within the cohesive whole. And we are vulnerable too, to outsiders who wish to overharry us and enemies from within, those with autocracy in their hearts. We will not stay still. The Pashainéy cause is now the Anbennarian cause, and that cause is eternal. Not merely ourselves but the whole world deserves this new way of life, and we must continually reinvent it, or we will stagnate as surely as the ancien régimes of Cannor.
