Where once the Bhoorakama were low in number and dispersed throughout the realm, now they congregate within Sramaya, and allow us to introduce them to our academies. While many within the Three Families were hesitant to allow orks into the Adasa for fear of their undoubtedly savage nature, these halforks are a different matter. With the strength and resilience of the Bhoorakama, the Adasa would surely have a fearsome frontline force, yet tempered with the better judgment of their human half.
