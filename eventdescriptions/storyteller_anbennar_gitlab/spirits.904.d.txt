The burden of governance sometimes lies heavy upon even the most accomplished ruler. Fortunately, for those who subscribe to the teachings of the Mystic Accord, the wisdom of the ancestors is but a prophetic dream away. Such was the case last night for the ruler, who awoke on their desk after a long evening of fruitless worry over available military supplies only to find a plan for a flexible network of army depots, supplied by a mix of locally and centrally sourced providers, that is sure to improve their military capabilities. The whole thing was seemingly written by their own hand.
