My liege, many of the newest refugees to join us have proposed to celebrate our success and their return to the realm. They tell tales of grand jousts hosted in the time of their grandparents or great grandparents, and wish to witness and take part in one as it was done in the chivalric domain of old. If we want to reproduce an authentic joust, it will be at great expense, but it will surely be a fruitful endeavor. Of course, we could ask the refugees to simply fund their own proposal, but that might not be popular.
