A Black Apostle is a well-regarded magical initiate who has not quite risen to the level of Acolyte. Powerful in their own right, the Apostles are the voices of the monarch in far-flung lands across the domain, carrying out the will of the Skull Throne. As is custom, an Apostle has prepared to travel to poorly integrated lands to force the local elite into groveling submission. You can use this decision to core provinces otherwise uncorable due to border gore.
