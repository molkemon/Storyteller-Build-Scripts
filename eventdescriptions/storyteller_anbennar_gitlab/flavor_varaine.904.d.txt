Our researchers made significant progress towards developing the Immortality potion, and initial tests have shown promising results. However, there is still much work to be done for the potion to be deemed ready, and the alchemists are having a harder and harder time trying to get through these final hurdles. One day, a mysterious potionseller drove his cart into the city of Varainé and started selling his wares. Despite many of the nation's and therefore many of Halann's greatest alchemists being gathered in the city due to the efforts to develop the Immortality potion, the elixirs sold by this individual were above and beyond anything ever seen in living history. Potions so exquisitely crafted it would take a normal alchemist a lifetime to replicate a single recipe, and so overwhelmingly powerful consuming them would kill all but the strongest of travelers. This potionseller, whose name and likeness were unknown to all in the city, was summarily summoned to the presence of the monarch, who demanded his help in helping finish the ultimate potions, but he refused to help. "You do not know what you ask. My strongest potions would kill a dragon, let alone a mortal."
