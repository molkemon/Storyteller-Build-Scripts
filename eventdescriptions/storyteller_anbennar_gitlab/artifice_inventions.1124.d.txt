One of the monikers for the vast desert of the Salahad is the sand sea. And now, thanks to this latest advance in artificery, it can be sailed upon like any other sea. The autopropelled sand sails stand to revolutionize travel and trade across the inhospitable wastelands. Powerful evocation magic creates a buffer of air between the bottom of the vessel and the sandy ground, allowing it to hover safely above the terrain and move smoothly across the sands. The sails of the vessel are enhanced as well, generating a stiff breeze in the desired direction to propel the vessel forwards whenever the local weather proves to be not as accommodating. Where once traversing the Salahad meant a perilous journey of weeks or even months, the autopropelled sand sails have made it possible to travel between the trade settlements at the border of the desert and the major deep desert oases in a matter of days, turning the crossing of the Salahad into a much more profitable and safe venture.
