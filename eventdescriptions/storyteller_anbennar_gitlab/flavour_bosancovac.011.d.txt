Though it was a long and arduous process, dangerous equally for the workers whose hard work was marred by the dangers of the Ynn, we have finally managed to establish a larger foundation for our city. We faced multiple challenges, from the risk of the entire structure falling into the Ynn’s raging waters to the water serpents which lurk within. Running behind all of this was the knowledge that we had risked so much of the riches the merchants have poured into the city. It is no matter now; the new foundation will allow us to expand and grow further than ever before.
