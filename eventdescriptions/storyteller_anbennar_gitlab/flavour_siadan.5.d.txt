The command is a state much like our own; nearly the entire ruling populace has been marshalled for war, as they march ever forward for glory. The monarch, summoned to Sarilavhan to sign their nascent alliance in blood, can see that fact written in steel across the city, in the faces of the hobgoblin soldiers, the martial simplicity of the city's construction, and the pride and glory of an empire in ascendence. This is not the future of Siádan; a state that, hopefully, will strive for more than just military conquest; but the shadow of the command looms large, and its resources and manpower are undoubtedly key to Siádan's rise to that destined state. The Second Blood Pact will secure that bond and secure Siádan's future, with the terms of the alliance also stating that harpy and hobgoblin populations will be exchanged between the two states. As the monarch's blood signs the pact, and as the Grand Marshal does the same, she cannot shake the knowledge that this pact is not one signed between equals, and that eventually, one day, harpy and hobgoblin blood may mix together on the field of battle. But that is for tomorrow; today, the world trembles.
