The crowd chants. Your focus on the game wanes as you look around the crowd, seeing joyous faces. You wave out to them, and for a moment a section of the crowd is watching you, cheering, before shifting their gaze back to the pit. You look around your box to see the various elites of the nation sharing glasses of tej; for a brief moment putting their rivalries behind to enjoy the game. Somebody scores, and you’re consumed in the game again, happy in knowing what Izenwez can bring.
