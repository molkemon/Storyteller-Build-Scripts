We received reports of unusual spirit sightings, specifically, our scouts reported herds of spirit elephants traversing the countryside day and night. Initially, we feared these sightings might foretell an ill omen or pose a potential threat to our citizens. However, it soon became apparent that this was not the case. The spirits were following ancient, long-forgotten paths that had been lost to time and wars. Though these paths may be nothing more than dirt trails, they provide us with a significant tactical advantage should we ever need to mobilize our armies swiftly across the country.
