Nestled within the undulating terrain of Lingstuan lies a hidden valley where eternal winter holds sway, blanketing the land in serene snowfall throughout the year. Hengdu Bowu, the grand frog, who achieved perfection in body and mind, resides in a forested dwelling within this enclave. Here, he imparts martial arts to eager disciples. Many of his devotees emerge as masterful warriors, choosing to hone their skills in solitude amidst the misty peaks of the Xianjie. Hengdu Bowu is the very essence of winter and the tranquility it bestows.
