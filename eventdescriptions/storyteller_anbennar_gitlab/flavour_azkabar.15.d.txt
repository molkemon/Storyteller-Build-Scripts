Bulwar has known many empires throughout its history. Many invaders, and many more which sprung from its own soil. Some remnants of these greater empires linger today. The phoenix kingdoms, for instance, but of greater interest to us, are Akalšes and Kumarkand, both once powerful and dignified realms, now reduced to pathetic weak imitations of the nations they used to be. It is only right to bring them under its protection and influence.
