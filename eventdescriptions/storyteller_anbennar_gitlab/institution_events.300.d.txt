The rediscovery of the elven homeland, Aelantier, has heralded a new era not only for the colonizers and the colonized, but it has also led to the spread of materials and techniques, as well as a realization of the vastness of the globe. As animals, crop types, silver, and diseases spread across Uelos' Lament, the first steps have been taken towards a truly global economy. With foreign lands and people being mapped and documented, ideas as well as religious and philosophical debate are increasingly being colored by what we have found in overseas societies. Great minds feel the need to question what was once truth, and from the capital city to Sarisung, the nature of the world is now up for debate.
