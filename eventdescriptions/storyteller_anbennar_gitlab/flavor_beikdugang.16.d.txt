As we have conquered and expanded our ports in East Sarhal, we have always had one objective in mind. control of the markets of coffee and dye. Still, even we could not have predicted the effect we would have on the lives of the people living here. The Dakinshi and Tanizu lands have seen an economic shift, now more focused on meeting the demands of our traders than making goods for their own consumption. With all this, it seems we really have no need for land in Sarhal beyond our ports.
