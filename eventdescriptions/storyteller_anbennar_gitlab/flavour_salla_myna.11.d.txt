Bricena is speaking to the Great Hawks through a Druid serving as translator, or rather, trying to do so, because the Great Hawks fly off whenever they approach. The Druid offers to charm them, but when asked, admits that it will not help long-term cooperation. He can ensure that they are willing to tolerate his presence, but that spell does not extend to Bricena. When asked for his advice, he says that other than taking eggs from a nest and raising them in captivity, something his Circle would vehemently oppose, she would likely need to spend years around the hawks before they would be willing to tolerate her presence.
