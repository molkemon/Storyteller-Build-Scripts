A shadow once flew above the river, a visage of fire and green deceit, yet it was slain by Artorean of Arganjuzorn long ago and only its memory may haunt us. Not so. Out of the north and west come tales of the heir to Drozmagog, a new dragon to unite the disparate followers and bring anew the heathen host’s assault on all good chivalric lords of the One River.
