After a funeral ceremony for a particularly well-known and notably corpulent merchant in a local area, several members of the community have fallen ill. Feasting on the honored dead is important to ensure the strength and wisdom of their flesh is passed on to the community, but the severity of the mold and rot on this particular dead gnoll makes eating them undesirable. We could send in the army to help eat the gnoll, let him rot and upset the locals, or lead from the front—eat the gnoll ourselves.
