We have restored the hold of our predecessors, now we must surpass them. Our very revival is contradictory to Haraz Orldhûm's nature, a band of adventurers claiming the mantle held by the aristocracy of yore. As such, we have a reputation to restore and uphold; our new reign will be seen as legitimate and enforced through our actions. The Serpentspine is in need of order and we will be the impetus for its restoration.
