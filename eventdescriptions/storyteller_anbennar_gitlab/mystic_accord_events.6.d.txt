The Demon Hills are treacherous, wont to shift and rupture at the slightest trespass, their temperament only growing more restless since they’ve been freed from the Oni. While traveling to one of our fortified outposts within the Hills, the earth beneath our ruler’s feet opened, plunging them down a narrow shaft into a cavern. The gloomy stone cave was lit with a dim, pulsing light emerging from a twisting, matted knot of dark energy. As they moved closer, the energy began to move with frenetic desperation, squirming and writhing. The chain the ruler now saw binding it to the cave’s floor was taught, ready to snap. All it would take is a single swipe of the ruler’s sword.
