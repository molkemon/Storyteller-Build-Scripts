It has been ten years since the ruler has made a pact with the tenebrous mistresses of the swamp. And they have certainly held up their end of the bargain, enhancing both his magical abilities as well as his other talents. But what he has chosen to do with these gifts seems to have pleased the Night Hags, as they appear to forgo the payment that is their due according to the contract. Whether they have waived their claim only for now or permanently, only time can tell.
