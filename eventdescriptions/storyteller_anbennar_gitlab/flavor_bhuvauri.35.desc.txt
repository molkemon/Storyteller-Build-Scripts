Many amongst the distant Fangaulan have practiced the tradition of multiple wives, and the Sanukelborlan family are no exception. They are known for having wives and husbands from many groups, from Raheni, Bom, and beyond. This practice is surprisingly useful, as it gives the family a wide array of connections and gives many groups a feeling of cooperation with the Adaseta. However, at the same time, the religious of Bhuvauri scoff at the practice, seeing it as impious and sinful due to the Raheni tradition of monogamy.
