Allegedly, a nearby blacksmith claims he saw a brash Aldresian squire attempt to woo a passing milkmaid. When the squire failed, an onlooking Magisterium apprentice insulted the knight and attempted the same feat. The mage too failed. What devolved from an idiotic spat between a squire and apprentice led to their similarly young and dumb friends arriving to support them. It ended in fireballs, gunshots, and a dozen dead peasants. The mages blame the knights, the knights blame the mages, and our people demand recompense. Both organizations are powerful; who do we focus our ire on?
