Seems there is a bit more credence to tales of these leopard men, or Wereleopards, than I initially thought. Ever the optimist, I thought there may have just been an abnormally large housecat rummaging through the garbage heaps, but based on what I heard that beastmage say to the Esteemed Leader, this is not the case. Tonight they will attempt a spell to force these Wereleopards to change form, but the change will be maladjusted. I expect to see men with ten tails leaping from buildings while cats with human ears scurry down the street.
