The leaders of the Amethyst Order have been led by the despicable lords of Amacimst, the main enemies of our state. They oppose our very republican ideals and pose a great threat to Brelar if they are to be left unimpeded, as they possess a great military force of seasoned knights. Rooting out their leadership will be a formidable task, as well as the restructuring of their order. It is known that many knights have been banished from the order for the most trivial of reasons; reinstating them will surely grant us trained knights in the army who are loyal to our republic.
