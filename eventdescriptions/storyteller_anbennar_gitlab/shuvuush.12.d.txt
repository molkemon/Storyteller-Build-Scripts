Eager young warriors have striven to prove their worth all week long in birdriding contests, archery matches, and wrestling competitions. Now, as the Hekuudzoungh festival draws to a close, all that remains is the announcement of the winners of each competition and, most importantly, the overall victor of the grand tournament. The revelers are gathered in front of the judge’s podium, eagerly awaiting the announcement, one final highlight before the day descends in raucous revelry. The grand summer bonfires are lit, which provide the latest batch of Terrorbird eggs with the heat needed to hatch. And the winner is.
