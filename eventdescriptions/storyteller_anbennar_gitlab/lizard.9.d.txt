The unity of the Riverclaws is no more. The Asartas secession from our alliance creates a unique political opportunity which cannot be underestimated. If we want to make any moves, this is the right time to act. The tribes of Yushyn are known for their rebelliousness and unbroken sense of identity. Before the war, they were asked by Rayaz to rebel against the Zerat in a joint attempt to break the Anzara. They expected independence, but the administration of Sykar didn’t leave them a bit of it in the end, establishing their own garrisons and keeping important figures under house arrest. These areas are a ticking time bomb that waits to explode just at the right moment. We shall offer our service to Rayaz and claim the provinces of Verthica and Tsaratokh, offering to act as a buffer against the Yushyn. And if they decline, well, maybe the relationship between our tribes won’t be as profitable for us anymore.
