One of our most prominent and respected generals has in this last year repeatedly been butting heads with our monarch, and has challenged many of their decisions. Only his popularity among the soldiers saved him from repercussions. But you can poke a jaguar only so many times, and the ruler was preparing to dismiss him. Unfortunately, the general learned of our plans. Interfacing with a disgruntled member of the Sangamar family, in a disturbing retread of the very trend that had once destroyed the Second Gophiran Empire, the general then decided to depose our ruler and place this pretender on the throne. It seems many of his troops, our troops, agree with this inane notion and have joined him in revolt.
