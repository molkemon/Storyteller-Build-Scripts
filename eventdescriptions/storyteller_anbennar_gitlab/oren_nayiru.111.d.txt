Though the Oren Nayiru claim to fight for the dawn, they thrive in the darkness. They hide among the peasantry, slaves, and serfs, in the hills and forests, within fields of maize and rows of slum housing. In an effort to flush the Oren Nayiru out and reestablish supremacy, our forces have recently set some of our lands aflame and committed a variety of unspeakable atrocities in the process.
