The dwarves of Argordstun have dug deep and uncovered many lost regions of the hold. Diamonds are once more extracted from the wealthbearing kimberlite, and a substantial part of the population now lives within vast circular shafts formed by the extracted veins. This imitation of the ancient hold has not gone unnoticed by its population; even now, the diamond shaft of Argordstun is flown alongside our own banners. It seems the hold is beginning to consider itself a nation, something we may have to deal with.
