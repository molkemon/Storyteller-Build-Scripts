As per usual, the halls of the Poppyshield Assembly are animated with talk and debate. However, instead of the usual banal arguments about taxes and grains and witches and whatnot, now they argue over the very souls of the citizenry. The Crimson Reverend has assembled the most learned men of the nation, counter, inquisitor, soldier, and advisor alike, to codify the most righteous Corinsfielder beliefs; the result will be printed into books using presses imported from Noruin, in number grand enough to be possessed by every good Corinsfielder. To take a faith so reliant on oral histories and hymns and codify it is no easy task; such debate will continue for many, many moons. To begin with, they have agreed on the essentials of both the Martyr of Escann and the Father Godwin’s tales. The details on these, however…
