The idea of moving our capital into the Forbidden Plains began as a musing. One of the would-be settlers absentmindedly wished the far-flung outpost was closer to our capital to make administration easier, and the monarch seized on the idea. Strange as it was, the wagons began trundling from our prior palace out to the plains within weeks, and the ruler followed them months later. Certainly, this was a strange decision, and it has made many people very angry and has been widely regarded as a bad move. Such a prominent presence in the plains soon attracted the centaurs’ attention, and their vengeance. They came with bow and blade and set our would-be outpost alight, looting and leaving devastation in their wake. The outpost will be able to recover, but it is clear that the local centaurs do not want us here and will likely continue to raid our settlement, our capital apparently, until we find a way to protect ourselves.
