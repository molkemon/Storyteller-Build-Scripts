Good day to you, ruler. As a gesture of goodwill, we have reluctantly chosen not to bill Iochand for these severe losses. You're welcome. While we are fully aware that no official consent was obtained for this branch's establishment from the Iochander government itself, the Gommo would like to formally remind you that it is a fully sovereign organization that resists the habit of kissing the feet of every selfrighteous monarch in Cannor with an inferiority complex. Rest assured that we have the full approval of both the Triarchy Administration and the Daccasded Administrative Council, and our charter was signed directly by Councillors Bigbrain and Middanman. If you have an issue, consider taking it up with any or all of them instead of wasting the Gommo's valuable time. Please redirect any complaints into the wastebin in my office at the Gommo's Isobelin branch. Unpleasantly yours, Mindi Whistletossle, Director of Ruined Sea Affairs, The Gommo.
