An embassy from the pirate haven of Agremos has arrived in Ameion. They have come with a proposal. The cities of the Andic Reach share many things in common. In many ways, they are the spurned children of Alecand, unwanted bastards, who had to fight for everything from the time they raised the first huts on this stretch of coastline. Instead of allowing the older cities to play them against one another, they should unite together, form a league that is able to stand up to the kheions. Their proposal has inflamed a serious debate within the city. Some want to hold onto the memory of Laskaris and follow his vision, while others consider this league a wise option. Should they abandon their ambitions in Taychend and throw their whole weight behind this proposed Echate league, or should they hold onto their independence and the vision set up by Laskaris?
