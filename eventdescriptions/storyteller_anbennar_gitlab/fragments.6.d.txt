As our explorers in Aelantier continue their journey through the ancient ruins, they have come across what appears to be an ancient logbook, seemingly meant to have kept track of various logistics across the empire of the Precursors. Though our translations are shoddy at best, one name seems to pop up in the logbook again and again; a word which roughly translates to 'The Pillar of the Heavens'. What this pillar is, and what it represents, we have no way of knowing, but its prevalence in this text seems to imply at least some importance to the Precursors. And of course, what the Precursors see as important, must surely also be valuable. Some, however, have doubts, and more than a few members of our expedition are reluctant to go searching for a place that may not even exist, and even if it did exist, may not hold anything of value whatsoever.
