A week of ritual celebration and prayer, ending with a mighty feast, has been called for by the Semayebaka all across our nation. The celebrations will be dedicated to Amilak, offering up excess bounty to be enjoyed in the Heavens and on Halann. The greatest of these rituals will be held and funded by our great monarch, who shall ascend the highest point in the realm and offer their personal submission to almighty Amilak.
