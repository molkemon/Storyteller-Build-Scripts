The Greentide brought with them an endless and remorseless enemy to the forces of Cannor, but after the tide subsided came a new opportunity. A new people, landless by nature and savage by birthright, and ripe for the picking. These orks were captured and sold both by intrepid traders and their kin to plantations in newly rediscovered Aelantier, and served as the foundation of the growing economy of the oncelost continent.
