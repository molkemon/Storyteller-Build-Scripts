Whilst practising their magic, the monarch has realised that they can hear and sometimes see through the shadows of their courtiers and sometimes even random citizens in the capital city. Realising that this could be a valuable resource in keeping order within the realm or it could help them to expand their influence as none of their enemies would expect their shadows to peer into their lives. They could put the shadows to work within the realm; each temple housing a divine shadow cast over their holy place, each noble kept under subtle surveillance and will never be alone even if they think they are. They could also send the shadows abroad to spy for them. After all, who expects their shadow to spy on them? This would allow for the greatest flow of knowledge to come into their nation.
