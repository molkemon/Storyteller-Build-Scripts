Though dark magics animates and sustains them, zombies are still made of dead flesh, unable to heal from injury and slowly putrefying. Except in very dry or clean conditions, zombies break down relatively quickly after their animation, the elements claiming the body as surely as if it had been buried beneath six feet of earth. Without a constant supply of new victims to reanimate, a zombie infestation normally burns out after several months due to natural decomposition. This appears to be what has happened in the realm, as new sightings of zombies have almost completely stopped in recent weeks.
