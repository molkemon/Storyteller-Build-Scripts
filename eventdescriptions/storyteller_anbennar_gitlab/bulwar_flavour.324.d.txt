The location of Kvangraak in the middle of the vast savanna is not due to mere random choice. the city was founded in order to control access to the subterranean Dao genie palace of Esuvrem. With the Sun Cult reigning supreme over this part of the gnollish savanna, a large temple complex has been erected over the cave entrance to the genie palace. This temple, called EduzPazrikettu, the Temple of Hidden Truth, or Zedrekhtidet Afakkan in the gnollish tongue, is decorated with delicate but sturdy metal workings, from mural plates to decorative railings along the ziggurat levels, the product of the concerted efforts of the many master blacksmiths that call this city their home. While the devotion of the local gnolls to Surael and his struggle against the forces of Darkness cannot be doubted, some remnants of their former belief in Kvanga persist in their reluctance to discard what others might consider unsalvageable, and their dogged persistence to tease out hidden meanings and buried secrets from seemingly mundane texts or artefacts. This predilection has earned the scholar-priests trained in the halls of the Temple of Hidden Truth a reputation as some of the most erudite experts regarding the buried secrets of the ancient past. Lucky is the academy where one of their number accepted a teaching position, and it is a costly but wise investment for any would-be treasure hunting expedition to secure the services of such a scholar.
