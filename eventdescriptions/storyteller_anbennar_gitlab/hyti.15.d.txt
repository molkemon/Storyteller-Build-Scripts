The air of a whole nation was vibrating as one, taut with excitement. To celebrate the opening of the Isun Kes lodges to the general population, all who visited were presented with a choice of useful trinkets from which to select their gift. The sharp deviation from the once spells they were used to was evident. Wishcrafting manufactories, once sole appanage of the richest, had finally reached everyone. Less powerful, yes, lasting for a limited number of uses yes, but cheap. All who were handed an inscribed tool could feel a new era beginning. magic at their disposal, to be used whenever they felt like it, several times. Magic in one’s pocket. The happiness of a little goblin receiving the most amazing gift, already plotting mischief with it; the expression of wonder of an old harpy who thought they had seen everything; the straightening back and newfound surety of a poor man, who could for once feel some power in his hands – this was it. The joy of making new discoveries was a reward in itself, of course, but this? The feeling of having just improved the life of a whole nation? For everyone that had worked on the laquieti Ištaror, it was the pinnacle of their career, something they had fantasized about during hard days in the lab. But what they were experiencing now surpassed their own hopes. Waking up, and realizing that the dream is real.
