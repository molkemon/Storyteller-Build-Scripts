In a recent war, we captured some of the enemy's land, including areas where they established supply depots for their settlement in the Charkuchin Mountains. We are in the strange position of not actually needing the help with supplies; we have many depots of our own, and at this point our effort would be better spent by improving existing infrastructure rather than building outward. But that is not to say the capture of the supply depot is useless; even if it means nothing to us, we may take comfort in the fact that it no doubt meant a great deal to them.
