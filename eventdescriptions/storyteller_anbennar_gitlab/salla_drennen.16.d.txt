What I thought of Lord Blossom? I liked him. Strange thing to say about an archfey, is it not? Yet all the same, I did. I liked him greatly in fact. There is nothing in his demeanor that ever so remotely resembles the other archfey we have stood before. I confess I did not even realize who we were speaking to at first. Who would have thought the wizened and jolly old man politely greeting us was in fact the lord himself? I say that, and yet somehow Elethen sussed him out instantly, or so she says. In fairness, there were signs. I saw it in his eyes afterward. age and wisdom beyond that of any elf. Right in the iris, a beautiful depth, almost unfathomable. I sound like I... What? Narawen. He’s an Archfey; how could I possibly... I will not dignify that comment with further response. But...
