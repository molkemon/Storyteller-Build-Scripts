Once the mysterious fey reached the throne room, they called upon the ruler. After a series of whispers between the two that couldn’t be overheard, the fey laid its hand on the ruler’s head and uttered a strange enchantment. Ever since, there has been a remarkable change in the ruler. Everything from their physical prowess, to their intellectual insights, to their personal charisma seems to have been greatly enhanced by the event. There is no doubt in anyone’s mind that the fey saw the spark of a glorious ruler inside the ruler, and thus transformed them into the ideal guide that the nation needed.
