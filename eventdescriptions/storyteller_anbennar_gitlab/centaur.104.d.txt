My Caehn, a chasm widens as the very earth beneath us trembles with the weight of discord. Two esteemed clans, guardians of our land, stand divided in a bitter territorial dispute. Spears are honed with hateful intent, and the air thrums with the ominous cadence of war chants. The hoofbeats of spitefulness resound louder with each passing moment, demanding our utmost attention. It is imperative that we quell this issue before it escalates.
