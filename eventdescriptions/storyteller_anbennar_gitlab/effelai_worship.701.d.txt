Our scout reports make it clear. people beyond just our domain live in their great realm. Although their culture bears some resemblance to ours, their bodies are vastly different, perhaps better suited to other parts of the jungle than ours. Upon making contact they were met with a cautious greeting; now we must send out an envoy bearing finely wrought gifts, as well as eloquent but firm words. One way or another, our new neighbors shall cooperate or submit to us.
