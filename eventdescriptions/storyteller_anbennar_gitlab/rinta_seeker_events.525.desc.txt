The gods are dead. The lights in the sky are not gods, but some unexplained natural phenomena. There can be no other explanation. Yet, there are some who see things differently. Whether by misjudgment or madness, a number of those who have seen fit to look at the sky under their own volition have become errantly convinced that the dead gods yet live. The details of this supposed revival differ between adherents, typically consisting of incoherent, insane ramblings that range from a generational conspiracy to the arrival of the end times. It is hard to understand how they might have come to such an irrational conclusion, as the gods are dead. Regardless, this wave of madness among the populace has given rise to the formation of a nascent cult, one that has seen fit to burn villages and sacrifice the innocent in order to appease what they see as the gods’ anger. The gods cannot be angry, as they are dead. This wanton violence and destruction must be stopped at any cost. The gods are dead. We cannot permit the existence of those who deny this. The gods are dead. The gods are dead. The gods are dead.
