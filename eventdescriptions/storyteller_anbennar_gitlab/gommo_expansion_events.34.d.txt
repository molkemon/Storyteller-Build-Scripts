Since the invention of firearms, we gnomes have been at the forefront of development and proliferation of these powerful modern weapons. The forges of war in a great industrial center, one of the Kheionai's greatest, embraced firearm production almost as soon as the technology was introduced from the east. Now we control the city and can continue increasing its capacity for industry. In this regard the Gommo has brought a proposal forward to establish a special firearms division in the city to coordinate our efforts. This will however require funding, that might be better spent elsewhere.
