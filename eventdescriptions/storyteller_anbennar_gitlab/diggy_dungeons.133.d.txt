Our expedition has arrived at the end of the chasm. Over the past days, goblin presence has become stronger and stronger, with many skirmishes here and there. But now they stand in front of a rickety wooden castle clearly brimming with goblins. Floor by floor, they cut themselves through until they arrive at the central chambers. There a large goblin sits on a throne of bones, wearing a shoddy crown. The soldiers have not yet been noticed, and so there is the opportunity to defeat this monster with brains, not just with brawn.
