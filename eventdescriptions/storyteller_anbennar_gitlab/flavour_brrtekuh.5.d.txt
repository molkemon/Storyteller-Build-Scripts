When the packs surged onto the island of Elizna, most fled to the shore, where the Jaherian Exemplars had set a wall of spears around their ships, around the towering Diranhria, to ferry any who could reach them to safety. That wasn't everyone, though. Many were too slow, or too attached, to flee. They tried to stay and fight, and they were cut down, and they were slaughtered, and they were devoured. Those who once, even for a short time, called themselves Stewards of the Sorrow were consumed by flame and flashing maw. The Vulzinzuirs died with them. The last of their number made a stand at the palace at Elizna. Some may call it retribution for the slaughter of the Irrlazuirs in Medhrubar so many years ago, but the gnolls didn't care about that. To them, it was simply victory.
