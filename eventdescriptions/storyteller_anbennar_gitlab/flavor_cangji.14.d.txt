It is quiet throughout the streets of Jiantsiang, as a vast crowd of citizens gather along the shores of the Sea of Spears. Hundreds of blue lotus flowers have been released into the water, floating along the surface like frozen shards of pure ice. This is the beginning of the Grand Festival of Jiantsiang, a celebration of the coming year and all the fortune it will bring. This festival in particular is special, for it is the first festival conducted after the Rending had been beaten back from the realm's lands. Countless people wait in silence as the flowers drift out to sea, awestruck by their beauty. As the blooms begin to leave the sanctity of Jiantsiang’s harbour, mages lined along docks and piers begin to channel magical power into the flowers. As they do, wondrous beams of light shoot from the center of the petals, illuminating the night sky with a spectacle of hues that shower the harbour in colour. The citizens burst into celebration, both for the festival and for their guaranteed safety from the spirits that have caused them so much grief. Music begins to play, and the people move away from the harbour’s shores to begin the true festivities.
