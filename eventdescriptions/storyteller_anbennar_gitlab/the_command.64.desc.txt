Much of the land in the realm is held by former nobles and rich farmers, and the years of conquest have seen much of this land lie fallow and uncultivated. Underused land is inherently mismanaged and goes to waste, weakening the people. We must see to it that this land is being used in a way that supports the Command and aids in the war machine. We will give the state the power to confiscate unproductive land from these nobles and noncompliant farmers, redistributing it to industrious workers who have proven their loyalty and reliability to the state.
