Decades of negligence have taken their toll on the Swallow Hobgoblins. When we reached out to them with a proposition to support the Dishonoured in taking over the entire country, they instantly agreed, fully aware of our backing in the city. You could say that the whole debacle went too smoothly. The hobgoblins are effectively in charge of the country as our loyal subjects, and it seems like anyone who would have opposed their rule is dead or scared enough to not resurface anytime soon.
