To the Escanni refugees, this word has a different connotation than for our people. To them, "home" is something lost, something that was destroyed and taken away from them. So when the Escanni say that they are going home, they do not say it lightly. The reclamation of Escann has now begun in earnest, fledgling states establishing themselves in the smouldering ruins of Escann. Even though most are former adventurer bands that took the opportunity of claiming the riches of an abandoned land, they now seek to call home the former inhabitants of Escann. Most of these new nations have now dispatched messengers across western Cannor, spreading the word that the Escanni now have a homeland to come back to. And many refugees are heeding this call, leaving one group at a time towards the East. We cannot stop them by force, but we can choose to help their venture; after all, Escann is still a dangerous place. What say you, my monarch?
