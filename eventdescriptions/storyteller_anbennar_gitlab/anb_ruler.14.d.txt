Otherworldly precision, incomprehensible motive, unfathomable breadth. The only way to defeat such an opponent is to become even more unfathomable, to surpass the natural and become infinite. Such power and resolve, born of paranoia, will not falter in the face of nature itself, regardless of the implement with which it intends to corrode, degrade, and erode one's sense of self. One's consciousness must become a monolith of sheer, unadulterated tenacity, clinging to the world that strives to deliver righteous deliverance. The Reaper's scythe approached with a wide swing, promising a swift and painful end. And it was a promise kept.
