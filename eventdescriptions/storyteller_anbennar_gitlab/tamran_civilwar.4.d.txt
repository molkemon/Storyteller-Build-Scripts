Chien Binhrung and Kudet Kai were once united in rule over Thidinkai, but after a devastating war that left both rulers dead, the soldiers of Kudet Kai carelessly cremated the queen’s body in an act of treachery and total disregard for customs. A few decades ago, they came close to avenging this tragedy and bringing an end to their rule, but were thwarted by a particularly clever Harimari mercenary whom they have now elected lord. Now, they find themselves at a turning point of history. The empire of Baihon Xinh, who formerly claimed dominion over them, is no more, and Thidinkai plunges into another era of warlords. Emperor Caoban Sang was betrayed by his sons and was forced to kill them, but one of them survived and now stands in open rebellion. Already, their spies have discovered evidence of correspondence between the pretender and the tiger lord of Kudet Kai. Against all reason, there was silence on the part of Emperor Sang, but all was soon to be revealed. Sang, seeing no other way to protect his people in his advanced age, performed secret magics to live on in spirit form. It would be hypocritical to denounce this action, for is it not similar to their veneration of the Crystal Queens? One of the first acts after his rebirth was a message to them, promising territory from Kudet Kai should they assist him in putting down the traitors. Since it would seem the pretender has made the same promise to their rival, they are left with little choice but to fight.
