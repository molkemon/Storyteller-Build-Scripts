Clan Earthstriker has been in control of the mining operations in our domain for generations. They have a great stake in the Dead End Mines as well. They have approached us with a proposal wherein they will assist us in funding the reformation of the republic in exchange for higher sale prices on their raw materials. We could divert some of the funds from our workshops elsewhere into the mines to pay for the extra costs. Or we could put them down and take control of the mines ourselves.
