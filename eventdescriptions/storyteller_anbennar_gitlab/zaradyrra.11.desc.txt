The Ashhana know well that at times war is a necessity. They do not dare sit in complacency or accept a lack of preparation. The crafting of weapons through metalwork is thus one of the sacred Hundred Petals. Confident in the virtue of their task, blacksmiths prepare their weapons with humble devotion. They are paired with warriors known for their restraint and patience, who then test the forged weapons for all to witness. The audience watches and learns. When such a display comes to the village, one or two youths are inevitably inspired to work as blacksmiths themselves.
