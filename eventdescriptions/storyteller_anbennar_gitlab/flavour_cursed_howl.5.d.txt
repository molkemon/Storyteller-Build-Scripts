The chieftain rang out, his words a meditation as he thundered to the audience that gathered in the wake of Cursed Howl’s great victory. Two angry figures stood in the arena, gathered for their final duel. One challenged the other, stating that they entered this contest for the orkish soul under the childish delusion that they were going to kill and pillage everyone in their wake, believing no one would be strong enough to object. At Ciranmyna, the Flower Grove, and dozens of other places besides, the challenger showed their ugliness and naivete to them all. The wind was sown, and it was time the Reaper had their due. Gordak dropped into the arena, brandishing the masterwork blade he had inherited from his father, the same blade he would use to slay Bolk when he had led the Cursed Howl too far astray. He charged them both, eager to finish their legacy personally.
