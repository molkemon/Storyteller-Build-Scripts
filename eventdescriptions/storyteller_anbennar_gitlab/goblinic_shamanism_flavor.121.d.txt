When it first happened, it was just in passing. The harpies came, and they hunted the plains and trees. The goblins hid. The goblins watched. They watched the harpies hunt and seek and find what they were looking for. Always something different, but always something they valued. The goblins had long hunted too. a home, safety, a meal. But it wasn't their life, not like the harpies. The goblins then started to hunt more, to find what they wanted, what they sought. And this time, the harpies watched. They watched as the goblins sought with greater fervor. They watched as what once was simply a hunt became the Hunt, the Goblin Gods and all their shamans hunting with them. For the goblins now found something. They found their Hunt, and the harpies were hunting with them.
