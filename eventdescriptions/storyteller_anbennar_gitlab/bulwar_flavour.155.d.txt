A most unexpected but welcome message has reached the court from the temple of the realm. It appears that overnight, the grounds of the sacred temple garden have broken out in swathes of blooming dawn lily. It is said that this sacred flower first bloomed after a great leader, the first Surael Reborn, defeated a large army on the way into a city, signifying the blessing of Surael. While they can rarely be found in the wild, all attempts to cultivate them have inevitably failed. Therefore, ever since their first appearance, their discovery is traditionally interpreted to be a sign of Surael’s approval, whether for the finder, the city, or the ruler. In addition, they are considered to be a particularly potent magical ingredient, which has prompted the Magi to request permission to harvest them, outraging the Namensar priesthood.
