Efforts by the Vog Mekza to seize control over our state have borne horrifying fruit. Not a single institution seems to be free of their grasp now, and not a thing can get done without their approval. Instead of simply serving as the vanguard of our struggle against the Khet, the Vog Mekza seem intent on dragging our realm into a mire of zealotry and fanaticism. As chaos and uncertainty grip our people, one thought is increasingly leaving every gnoll's muzzle.
