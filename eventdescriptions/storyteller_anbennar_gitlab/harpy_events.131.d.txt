When Kobolds breathe fire, when Ogres fly, and when Satyrs hatch from eggs. All phrases signifying something impossible and concepts that any sentient being on Halaan would deem as absurd, until this very day. A hatchery in the realm recently experienced the joyous arrival of a new generation. A particular egg out of the many that were hatching managed to catch the special attention of the caregivers, when instead of talons or wings, a pair of hooves popped out of the cracked shell. Needless to say, the hatching of a Satyr from an egg sparked quite the uproar among the resident Harpies of the realm, and the tale quickly reached even the ruling elites of the flock.
