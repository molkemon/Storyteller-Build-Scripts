The monarch advanced on the defeated enemy, Saerasyar in hand. The foe scurried away, held an arm over their head, and pleaded for mercy. The monarch did not give it. The monarch held the burning blade high, then brought it down. But Saerasyar refused. The blade erupted into a roiling blast of light, fire, and magic, and the room filled with howling screams. The one to be executed was reduced to a mutilated, charred husk, as the explosion knocked the monarch back, sent them tumbling through the air, engulfed in flame, half their body simply annihilated, and the Green-Flamed Sword was nowhere to be found.
