For a thousand years, gnomes had to make do with very little space. From the tiny land grant of Giberd to the flooded swamps of Portnamm to the cramped islands of Nimscodd and the Splinters, they have rarely had much in the way of physical real estate. Despite this, they have always built metropolises and monuments, for they have long since mastered the arts of dense, efficient, towering constructions. They have burrowed into the earth so as to clear extra stories, stabilized their foundations into bedrock, and stacked their buildings with perfect geometries. With modern technology, their city planning ability has only improved; never before have they had access to mass-produced iron, or steel, or glass. But, today, they take a glorious step into a new age of citybuilding. First, mass-produce lumber, beams, foundations, bricks, and more. Second, enchant them to assemble into particular forms when exposed to an automated Fabricate spell. Third, include magical glue as part of the fabrication process, to ensure that constructions stay stable. Fourth, pack all of these items into tiny little boxes of holding, which can be easily carried anywhere. Fifth, repeat this many, many, many times. Sixth, bring these boxes to an empty location and build an entire city from seemingly nothing. With these Prefabricated City Constructors, they can construct entire cities ahead of time, according to set plans, and with perfect cohesion. They can now develop their lands like never before.
