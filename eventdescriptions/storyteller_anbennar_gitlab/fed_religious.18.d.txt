In the now peaceful streets of the capital, whispers of an extraordinary young woman have become impossible to ignore. She has declared herself the prophetess of 'Kalyin', the true and only Lake Goddess, the embodiment of everything and everyone. An evergrowing number of the common folk are listening to this so-called 'prophetess', as the recent religious war weakened their faith in the Triunic Goddesses. What should we do with this troublemaker?
