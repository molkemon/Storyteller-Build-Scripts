The gnoll bowed before the monarch, his fur drenched in sweat, chest heaving as he panted against the desert sun. It was a sweltering day, of course; but the heat was only doubled by the exertion of his deeds. for this gnoll had proven himself before Kvanga. Once, the Trial of the Reforged Chain was only for humans; those who wished to become like gnoll. But this pack had moved beyond that; for it was no longer the gnollish children of Kvanga they needed to prove themselves to. The ruler rose, and gave their blessing. And through them, Brrtekuh's.
