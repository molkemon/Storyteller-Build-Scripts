Tales of a great person from a border province have spread amongst our people, as they have lived alongside us and achieved great deeds. Since their passing, some dwarves have even added them to their home shrines. This has led to some dispute amongst our Stone Keepers. Almost every shrine also honours dwarves who are not actually ancestors, so a lack of direct lineage should not be prohibitive for veneration. But should the veneration of a nondwarf be incorporated into the scriptures?
