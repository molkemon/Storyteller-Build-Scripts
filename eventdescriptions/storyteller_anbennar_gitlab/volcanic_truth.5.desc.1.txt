For the longest time, the order of things was simple. the Twin Gods oversaw all, and Maulu and Xihatele oversaw the Twin Gods, ensuring that they were happy, both with the state of the world and with developments of the faith. Now, external forces have arranged themselves such that the old order has been disrupted, and the old system of communion with the volcanoes has been disrupted with it. And yet, Muatlaqua and Quahopilo were there before the Cuahu Teotlehu, and they will remain here after them. Even if the Cuahu Teotlehu are no more, we need not necessarily restore them. The faith faces a core question. if not the Cuahu Teotlehu, who will interpret the will of the Twin Gods?
