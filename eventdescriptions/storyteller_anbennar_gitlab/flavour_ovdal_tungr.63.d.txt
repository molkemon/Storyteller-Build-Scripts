If it was less tragic, the economic irresponsibility of Bulwari states would be quite amusing. Despite having had its debts cleared from us before, that country has once again sent envoys begging for aid. They have no more rights to give up, no more concessions to give, and can only plead for us to be merciful and magnanimous. They say, "Surely, the sum of money required to keep that country solvent would be a pittance for the legendary wealth of our realm."
