Though elves hold all the highest positions of government and command in that realm, it falls upon poor peasant men to fill out the conscription. Simple men; craftsmen, bakers, farmers, pottery makers, each with families and aspirations. These peasants fight zealously in the name of Surael. In our war with that realm, however, their faith is tested in each battle again and again as wave after wave of gnoll hits hard and hits fast. The elven commanders retreat from these losing battles, and we let them run. The ruler has commanded their pack to focus their bloodlust solely on the human conscripts; but no slaves are to be taken. Instead, countless fathers are turned into corpses, cannibalized in front of their comrade's very eyes. Their sons are left behind to rage, to fear, and to wonder why only elves return from the battles where so many of their friends and family perished.
