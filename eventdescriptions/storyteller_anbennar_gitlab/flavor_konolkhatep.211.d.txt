Traveller, in the year currently, the great Chain Road Yaikmmoton was dedicated in the name of the ruler. As the Sorrow stretches on without end, so too does the Chain Road stretch across the plains of Gazraak. As the Sorrow makes sure we never want for water, so too does the Chain Road make sure we never want for slaves. As you see the wretches brought along the Chain Road to a life in bondage have fallen, so too would we all fall to ruin without the favor of the Khet. As this road stands forever, so too shall Konolkhatep. Inscription by the side of the Chain Road in Akaz Rakkaz.
