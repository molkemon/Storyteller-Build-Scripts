With the Reaver Reunion as nothing more than a distant memory, it was time for the ruler to host a feast of their own and invite Gerudian Konungr, Jarls, and Skalds. The first guests have already arrived, and the mood is exactly as the ruler planned for. Even those who have proven enemies of the house have been invited, so no one can say the Reverians aren’t magnanimous towards their nemeses. Nonetheless, they didn’t come. As such, the halls of the capital were full with Gerudians. They tasted Lencori wine for the first time and enjoyed our short friends' entertainment more than the Reverians could have anticipated. The Skalds sing ancient Tales of Reavers during the Era of Black Ice that captivate the entirety of the court, including the ruler. Many Tales and facts lost to the Reverians have been meticulously handed down by generations of Skalds. As the evening sets and our guests go out to explore the various noteworthy establishments in the capital or return to their ships, they all thank the ruler for their hospitality. A successful feast, it seems.
