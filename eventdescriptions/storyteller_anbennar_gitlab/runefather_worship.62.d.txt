The Harazherûd has approached the Obsidian Council with information about the Runefather’s newest enemy, although they are not as unfamiliar as we may think. Our ancient enemy of goblins still roam the caverns and holds of the Serpentspine, threatening the periphery of our empire. Before the traitor dwarves, before the orks, before the precursors, there were the goblins. Ever present, ever a nuisance. Fortunately, we have had time to learn their tricks down here in the dark; the deep goblins we have hunted and enslaved provide the empire with the blood to fuel our runes. Compared to our place of exile, where the goblins possessed massive cities of their own, the squatters of the Serpentspine merely serve as another obstacle in our path to reclaiming the Runefather’s birthright, and an opportunity to bring in more slaves to expand rune production.
