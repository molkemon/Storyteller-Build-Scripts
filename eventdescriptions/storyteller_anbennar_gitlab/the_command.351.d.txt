You cannot command an army from a hovel. You cannot defend land without a fort. You cannot live a fulfilling life without a house for your family. We of the Command have always kept this basic tenet of life in mind, and the land of Zhaoyun in the Xiaken is the perfect place to form the headquarters of a new command, dedicated to building impregnable forts, robust granaries, sprawling marketplaces, austere universities, and comfortable homes for the sake of our people. If it can be imagined, the Elephant Command will build it.
