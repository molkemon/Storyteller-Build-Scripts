The ruinborn we found in our immediate surroundings were very poor by our standards, with no precious metals to steal. They were decently fed, so they made mediocre slaves, but at least they knew the land. The only noteworthy exception is the Cheshoshi with their Bone Citadel, which now belongs to the Kult. The further west we go, the less this remains true. Our explorers spread stories of great riches in the northern Eordand region, especially in the southern Kheionai region, of great kingdoms filled with gold, silver, and even precursor relics. If we are to enjoy their wealth, we need to kickstart the process of acquiring outposts near them.
