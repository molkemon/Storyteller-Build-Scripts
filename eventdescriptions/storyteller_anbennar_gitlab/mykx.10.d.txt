Asraport burns, we laugh, and surprisingly, the dwarves of Traveler's Rest have sent an emissary to us. The remaining population has all gathered at a spot and is ready to fight to the death. Their message is simple. End the looting and burning and guarantee our lives, and we will march to your ships. Slavery is better than death, and we cannot stand the destruction of our new home. What should we do with the dwarves?
