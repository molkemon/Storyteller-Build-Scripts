The Red Sentries march onwards through the valley; routine training for them, as well as useful surveillance. The officer raises his hand, and his men instantly cut all idle chatter. Silence. But, wait faintly, in the distance, there is a sound. Soft pattering; the paws of sabertooth cats. "It's a trap," a man calls. "Goodmen, ready your weapons," calls the officer. Leering and inhuman, grinning and jeering, the rune warriors show their faces over the edges of the valley. The Red Sentries raise their shields, and brace for the onslaught.
