As Yidab draws herself into her cocoon, we retreat to protect ourselves from any potential tricks she may be playing. As more and more time passes though, and it seems like she is not preparing an attack, we allow ourselves to relax, take a breather, and even check up on our injured comrades to make sure they will be able to get back into the fray.
