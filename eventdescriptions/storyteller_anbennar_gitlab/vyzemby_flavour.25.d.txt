A promising recent graduate of the Miphipa College has approached us, asking us to help fund an expedition northwards up the West Salahad Coast based on recent interactions with other halflings who claim to have come from far to the north. This would be explored by a person named Azaly Farscribe, who seems adamant that such an expedition would be diplomatically, commercially, and most of all culturally revolutionary. Such a journey would, however, be perilous and costly. Is it a worthy investment?
