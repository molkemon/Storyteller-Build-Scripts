With the conquest of Arpedifér, and the magical infrastructure built, it is time to finally cast the prospecting spell. After finding it in one of the Arpedifér library's scrolls, our mages have already cast a weak version of it as a test, and everything went well. For the real deal, it has been decided to convince some Arpedifér mages to collaborate with ours; if we could manage to conscript Zaleria it would be best, but even some apprentice of hers would suffice. It is done, and it worked. A scrying circle as big as our island. Our mages annotated on a map all the ore veins the spell found, and we are absolutely spoiled for choice; there are thousands of them.
