For many years, the citizens of Poppyspring have dreamed of this moment. The Ynn, a disgusting sign of everything they saw wrong with the world; foul witches consorting with decadent nobles to keep common folk mired in ignorance and forever entrapped in servitude; has fallen. The great lands held by the nobles have been confiscated by Red Sentry and Inquisitor alike and given back to the people over whom they once held dominion, and thus, destitute and starved, their hold is broken. Now, however, comes the logistically difficult issue. The Witch. While they were able to purge witchery from their former conquered neighbors, halfling and dwarf, they weren’t as entrenched as the water mages of Ynnic society. They still hold the minds of many of the common folk, poor savages, and are unwilling to fall without a fight. Perhaps, if they extend a softer hand to the common folk, they may abandon their witches with less of a fight.
