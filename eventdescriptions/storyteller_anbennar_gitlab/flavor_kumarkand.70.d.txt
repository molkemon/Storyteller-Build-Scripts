What once seemed like paranoia now seems like reality. What once seemed like cruelty now seems like necessity. What once seemed like opulence now seems like poverty. The brilliance and foresight of Alušir had been lost on the Akal, who now with clarity brought by the magical helmets, saw the truth. that Alušir was no madman but a visionary. Although the Akal’s personality changing to mimic that of Alušir doesn’t seem to have diminished their capacity for running the state, where once the Akal tempered the ambitions of Alušir, now the Akal embraces them.
