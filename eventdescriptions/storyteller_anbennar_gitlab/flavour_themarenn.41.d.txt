Home is always where faith is, a saying amongst the faithful of Esmaryal who seemingly never lose their path. It is said that one pure of heart will always find their way through even the darkest of days and roughest of roads. The Esmaryan Clergy have always been fast to point out hopelessness as the reason for downfall, yet could it be true? It has not been uncommon for even the vilest of criminals to take shelter in the Mother's Sanctuary. Is that their faith, nestled within their heart, giving them hope with each beat? Telling them there might yet be a chance? The way of their goddess, unlike her guidance, is hard to threaten. But much like her teachings, it is easy to understand.
