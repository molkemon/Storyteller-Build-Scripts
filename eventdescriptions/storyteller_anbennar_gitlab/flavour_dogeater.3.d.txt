It has been quite some time since my father died at the maws of the Dogeaters. Many of us managed to flee to the woods and set up resistance efforts, inspired by his final speech. However, our efforts have not borne fruit. Many of us died in the last gambit to reclaim our clan, and I am starting to lose hope myself. In fact, ever since then, the Dogeaters have only become more violent, more savage, more uncaring, and more battlehungry. They cannot be bribed, for they have no need for scraps of gold. They cannot be intimidated, for they are assured they will be reborn even in death. They cannot be reasoned with, for they can only respond in shouts and grunts. Though I remain hopeful still, surely a clan constantly on the brink of implosion is not long for this world, right?
