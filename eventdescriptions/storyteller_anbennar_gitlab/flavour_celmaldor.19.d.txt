The everbelligerent House of Adshaw is always looking for new excuses to invade other lands and expand their territories. Most recently, a prince of Adshaw has attacked the former kingdom of Vrorenmarch under the pretext of freeing the land from its orkish invaders and dynastic relations to the old ruling house. The orks are hardly the most pleasant newcomers, and it is tempting to use this conflict to expand our influence in the White Reach while the monsters fight each other. Yet it is hard to deny that an Adshaw on the throne of Vrorenmarch would be terrible news for Celmaldor and all the free cities of The Reach.
