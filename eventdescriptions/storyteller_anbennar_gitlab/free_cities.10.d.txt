Landowning nobles in the capital dislike that their Free City status keeps them out of direct noble rule, and lately they have exhausted every option in keeping them quiet. Had they been mere peasants, armed with pitchforks and torches and with little military training, they would be able to squash any revolt they may spark. But these are men and women with land and titles and formally trained in the art of war, and should not be underestimated. The only one that may silence their ambitions is the emperor of the Empire of Anbennar.
