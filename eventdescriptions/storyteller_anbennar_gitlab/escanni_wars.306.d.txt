As war rages across Escann, nations disappear from the face of Halann. A century and a half of rulership vanishes in a few years now, and borders change with every skirmish. The legacies of several adventuring companies have been erased as friendships fall apart. In this environment, only the strongest shall remain. Evidently, one of the smaller powers on our borders, a neighboring domain, feels like they are not strong enough to survive. Their ruler, a title and name, has approached our ruler with an offer. In exchange for subsidies to pay off their debt and soldiers to guard their territory, they would swear an oath of vassalage. It is a good offer, but we could always hold onto the offer and use it as a legitimate backing to claim the entirety of that domain as our own.
