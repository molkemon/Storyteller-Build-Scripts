Our shamans have observed the Raheni society with great interest. Their social order is extremely rigid, with a strong bureaucratic workforce that writes down every last law, statute, and correspondence. A group of shamans has now established the cult of paper to emulate this level of recordkeeping. In order to increase efficiency and social cohesion, they have ensured that the language is complicated enough that most goblins will have trouble making sense of any of it. Beyond that, they have also established a system of strategically losing and sometimes finding records whenever it is in the best interest of the social order and themselves.
