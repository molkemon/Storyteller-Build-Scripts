The cave trolls of Grizehûd are much like ourselves, desiring peace between neighbors and working alongside each other in harmony. Goblins, dwarves, kobolds, trolls, and orks, once bitter enemies fighting for the gold of the caverns, now all banter, trade, and cohabit in peace. This melting pot of cultures has produced a unique culinary fusion, mixing and matching the best of each to create something wholly new and utterly delicious. Nowhere is this better seen than on Feast Day, a celebration occurring every four days. Culinary experts flock together and prepare vast mountains of food, full of gastronomic masterpieces. They then distribute these to the entire community, ensuring the needy are fed and communal bonds are forged. One of Azaly’s apprentices, more chef than architect, spent months with the trolls of Grizehûd, learning from their most acclaimed cooks and sauciers. Inspired by their generosity and concerned for the refugees still finding their footing after the War for Zena, she has envisioned a similar Street Food Festival for Bivuzena. With several troll chefs who are eager to expand their gastronomic repertoire, she has put together a proposal for a network of food banks and public feasts to alleviate the hunger of the people of Bivuzena.
