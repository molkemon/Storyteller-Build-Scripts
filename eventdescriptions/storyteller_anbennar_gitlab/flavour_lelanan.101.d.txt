Aelantier was a land of great mystery, but our mission was perfectly clear. So we sailed due straight west, reached those lands with no fear. But when our explorers dropped their anchors and stepped foot on distant shores, they stumbled right into a proper city. They found houses, even with doors. They were greeted with pensive caution; the first Halflings the natives had ever seen. But one approached the explorer with joy and made a great big scene. We could not understand their language, but we knew when one spoke of love, and while the rest looked on in surprise, our explorer just removed their glove. A kiss on the Ruinborn's hand, a smile shared between them. That was all it took to begin the creation of a worldcrossing gem.
