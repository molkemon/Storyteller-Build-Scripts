TELLUM GAMING AND SPORT Home Team Verne Takes Grand Regatta. The hosts have taken it. We are sure there will be many tavern arguments calling it rigged, but we at the analysis desk cannot fault the results. After a largely invisible first half, the VerneAmadian team hoped to make ground as they approached Sarhal, and boy howdy did they. The entire circumnavigation of Sarhal drew Red Tide from the back of the column to the front with the aid of extensive merfolk piloting, carrying the team to a photo finish over the Lencori Uelos’ Abyssals. "We wanted competent openwater sailors, and there are none like the Banché," explained Red Tide captain Vincenio Vercane. "Tor Nayyi is not unlike the Bay of Verne, a local sea that opens into open ocean, so we had a crew with a thorough understanding of a variety of conditions. Obviously, the last stretch being home ground was no drawback." Vercane refused to comment on rumors of his parentage, still dominating the social pages, but he has nevertheless become a local heartthrob in Heartspier.
