Our people and the Feyrealm have been irrevocably linked since the day a commander led our progenitors into these woods over a thousand years ago. Yet in all that time, we have worked to avoid straying too far from trodden paths. Stories passed down from grandparent to grandchild have cautioned against taking one step too far. They were stories well told. We were a scattered people without hardly any comprehension of what the Feyrealm even was. Our relationships with its Fey denizens were still being established. Simply put, we were not ready for extraplanar exploration. But now, we are one people with generations of experiences with our neighboring plane. Its inhabitants are no longer seen as fickle and impetuous, but understood as flexible and eager. And now one of our own has found a new path. The adventurous Wood Elf known as Vucras Siubálrn, Vucras the Traveler, has discovered a reliable means through the Feyrealm. This path appears to lead to another forest, one in our own plane. This has caused quite a stir in the court and has led to courtiers urging us to officially fund expeditions with Vucras at the lead.
