Founded in the early fourteenth century by Vherenar Freesword, a Dyami adventurer and abolitionist, the Cult of Vherenar is an incredibly secretive religious brotherhood that has since spread through Taychend like a plague. Dedicated to liberating those in bondage, they have proven a consistent nuisance for many landowners and slavers in Taychend, burrowing into slave populations and creating elusive hideouts from which the Cult’s operations are run. It seems a Cult of Vherenar cell has been set up inside one of our cities, and they have been stirring unrest.
