The Necropolis of Bim Lau is one of the greatest holds of spirituality in Haless. Our empire already rules the realm of the living, and now seeks to free the spirits from their hell. The Eunuchs claim the Necropolis is a living entity, a tyrannical king that enslaves souls, and has so graciously made the preparations for the battle against it. The battle lasted a day, and nothing remained of the old king. Thus, we now rule both the living and the dead.
