Holidaying in Zangea is popular amongst high society in Khatalashya. Some even go there to find love, albeit more of a short-lived affair than a long-lasting relationship. It seems that is what the ruler did on their recent journey to the alien planet. That whirlwind fling was with a powerful mage, charmed as the monarch was by their spellbinding charm, and the fruit of said romance has just been born.
