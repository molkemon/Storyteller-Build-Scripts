The caste system, as Haless has known it for many years, has reached its breaking point. Those beneath yearn to grow and those above try to tamp them down and preserve their power. This has played out across the region, yet in the realm, such a social construct has become less and less adhered to. The Cinnabar Lotus Society and the forward thinking of the rulers has resulted in the people of the realm welcoming such a change. While caste persists, the principle of consultation of all castes has become deeply entrenched in the realm. Advisors to the monarch have come from all walks of life and it is commonplace for the upper caste to take part in debates across the country in which representatives from all manner of other castes participate. Society must flow, much as the Kharunyana does, bending and bowing, changing as time passes on. People are not stones, they go where the currents take them and so we must as well. As rebels rise across Haless, the people of the realm make their stance clear, it is time for the old ways to pass and new to come.
