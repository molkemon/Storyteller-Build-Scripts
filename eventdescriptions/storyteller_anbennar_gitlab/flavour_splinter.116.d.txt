As our soldiers march through the empty streets of the capital of the Kingdom of Danggun, a small group of former administrators and a small section of the once royal family approach. Invited to the temporary military headquarters in the center of the ruined city, the monarch personally listens to their request. It seems that they desire the reinstatement of their royal family and the total reconquest of their kingdom, and in exchange, they will accept us as their overlord and bend to the will of the Triunic Crown in perpetuity. The monarch has a decision to make.
