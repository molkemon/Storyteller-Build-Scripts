The Greentide began twenty years ago when the ork Korgus Dookanson, who claimed he was the son of their evil god, invaded and laid waste to the chivalrous kingdoms of Escann. Only Corin, a mortal woman imbued by the powers of Agrados, the disgraced god of war, could stop them. And stop them she did, and with her followers she united the marchers and adventurers in Escann into a single force of good, stopping the invasion of western Cannor dead in its tracks. Yesterday, on the eleventh day of the month of Nerament, Korgus Dookanson was slain by the heroine Corin in the city of Castonath, but in return she too lost her life. Corin is dead, but so too is Korgus. It is now up to the rest of us to follow her legacy and bring an end to the Greentide once and for all.
