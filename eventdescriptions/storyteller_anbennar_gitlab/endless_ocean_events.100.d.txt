Long has our sails been filled by the wind of the Kiamoa. Its winds are just as much a sailor's friend as a sailor's bane. In the best of times a sailor can cover great distances driven by those great gales, and sail into a horizon both unknown and known. When the Kiamoa howls, however, a sailor will face challenges unlike that of any other. The pulses of the Kiamoa can bash ships to splinters and pull whole houses into the sea. All that has changed now. Our dearest friend and our most bitter rival has been made into something else. There are still wind and waves, but they beat at a different pace now. But as an old practice dies, another emerges. Now the realms of the dead lay open to us as our sailors set out to cross that great divide. We now truly have an endless ocean to sail.
