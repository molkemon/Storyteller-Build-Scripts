Translations of our works sit in libraries across the world. When foreigners visit, they show the proper wonder and deference as they walk under our ancient gates, knowing that the buildings they glimpse are older than their entire cultures. Though the niceties are still awkward, we now receive envoys more or less smoothly, and can compel them through politeness to show dignified deference to our leaders. This is the lesson of the lizardfolk. adapt if you must, but maintain your dignity. Never allow the world to see you needily scrape, or they will replace monster with beggar and continue quite merrily with their disrespect. We are the standard bearers for the oldest civilization on Halann, and this legacy now garners the respect it should. Never will we sacrifice the principles which make us great.
