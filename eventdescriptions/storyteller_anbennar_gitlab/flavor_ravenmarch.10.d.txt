The monarch and the offensive young man select blades from an attendant. The monarch selects a simple, practical saber, with filigree on the handguard, while their opponent selects an ornate, engraved rapier. A murmur ripples through the attentive crowd as both take up dueling stances. The young man unleashes a rapid barrage of thrusts and feints, pressing the monarch’s guard, before dropping low and thrusting upwards. The monarch dives out of the way, landing in a roll and slashing as they rise. While the young man pivots to face the monarch, the tip of the saber presses to his throat. The room is silent save for the sound of his sliced cape falling to the ground and his voice calling out. "Clearly I was misinformed about the country's skill with a blade. I yield."
