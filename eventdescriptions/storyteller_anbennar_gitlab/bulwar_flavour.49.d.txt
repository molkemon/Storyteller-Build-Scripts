Praise to the greatest, to the most courageous of all rulers, the monarch of this country, praise the powerful. I praise the ruler, the greatest, the fiercest of all warriors, the conqueror of Heavens, I sing again to the Powerful One. Surael exalted the ruler; of all rulers he raised them to the Heavens. To march before the forces, to lead the host, to give the battle signal, to advance to the attack, to direct the battle, to control the fight, unto them Surael entrusted; in battle armor he made them sit, saying. "I have uttered my decree, in the Light of the world I have raised the ruler to power. The dominion over all Heaven have I entrusted unto them. Be exalted, you my Chosen."
