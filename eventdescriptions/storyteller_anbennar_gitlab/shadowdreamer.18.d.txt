Azdal Coinbiter stood with his hands on his hips, proudly lording over a table of his thinktank’s inventions. His mouth full of gold teeth glimmered in the light of a selflighting torch, and various knickknacks spun or jumped or blinked as the Great Dreamer looked over them. “They are certainly… interesting.” The Great Dreamer murmured. “I don’t see how some of them could possibly aid in the crusade, though.” Azdal smiled even broader. “Oh, my Dreamer. This is only our sample stock. We can create whotzits and thingamabobs to assist your marching armies, your coinpinchers, your diplomats, or even your ladyfriends. I assure you.” The goblin bowed deeply as he finished speaking. The assembled Drahmgall scoffed and muttered from the rear of the room, but it was for the Great Dreamer to decide.
