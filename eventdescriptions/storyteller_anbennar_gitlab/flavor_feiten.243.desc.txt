Though a nascent organization only recently given favor and trust by the monarch, the responsibilities of the Shuimu Engineering Cadre are paramount. Foremost is their mission to keep the airfleet operation and to drive its development, for their balloons are their primary advantage over their rivals. The complexities and difficulties of doing so cannot be overlooked, and they must take care not to overstretch their capacities, lest they unceremoniously crash and burn.
