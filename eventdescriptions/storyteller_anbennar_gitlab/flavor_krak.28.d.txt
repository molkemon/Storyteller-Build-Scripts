Aeons ago, when the orks rampaged across the Dwarovar and Northern Pass, we held back the tide at the entrance of the Giant's Anvil. Kar Durghoir stood ever defiant against the waves of orks, its IceSteel walls and gates outlasting generations of orkish warriors. It was only by some twisted turn of fate and a stray projectile from an orkish catapult that its mighty gates gave way. As the projectile hit and killed a group of our IceSmiths in the middle of their rituals, the great blocks of ice they were conjuring collapsed onto the gatehouse and greatly weakened the gates. Now, fate is on our side once again. Kar Durghoir has been rebuilt and it shall never again fall as long as a single Quartz Dwarf draws breath.
