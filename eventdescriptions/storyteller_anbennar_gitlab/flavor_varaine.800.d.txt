Everyone has some sort of feeling about dragons. Some worship them, most fear them, a few have even dared face them. But most of all, everyone respects a dragon's power. Inspired by these mythical creatures and their awesome strength, and hearing rumors of Kobolds in far off Yanshen who worship one of these beasts, our researchers proposed a daring research project, one to harness the power of the elements themselves into a deadly weapon. A Fire Breathing Potion. Venturing into Yanshen, our explorers spent some time mingling with the dragon-worshipping kobolds of Balrijin and found the gold-scaled folk to be extremely amiable and welcoming. However, despite their friendliness and desire to help, there was only so much our alchemists were able to learn about dragons and their inner functioning before beginning to strain the limits of the kobolds' generosity, until finally they reached some knowledge the kobolds were unwilling to share. To get the information needed to finish our research, we could try to make a deal with the Kobolds, increasing our access to their stored knowledge. Alternatively, we could request that our researchers try to find the necessary information elsewhere, such as through hunting for fire-breathing creatures in other locations.
