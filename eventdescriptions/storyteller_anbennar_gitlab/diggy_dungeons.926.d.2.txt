Building our own bridge to cross the chasm takes a while, but with the additional security of a sure footing, we are able to pay better attention to our surroundings. Thus, during the crossing, we are able to spot plenty of valuables stuck within the silk of the webbing across the chasm. Once we cross the chasm on our safe bridge, it is trivially easy to chop the web and drag it to one side to collect the valuables.
