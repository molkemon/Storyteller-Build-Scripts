Any serious effort to tame the jungle relies on those brave enough to work the frontier, contributing with each day's work to the relentless march of civilization. Unfortunately, the jungles have begun to fight back in a specific province, as massive insectile creatures destroy structures, kill livestock, and in at least one case, even slaying an entire family in their homes. Once the conflict dissipates, all that's left is a sickly song resonating throughout the settlement.
