More than any Bulwari, to a man or woman of Drolas the prospect of freedom is of the utmost importance. Our way of life has always been to earn our keep on the open seas, and we have never shied away from unsavory methods in doing so. But even in our richest days we have never been anything but poor and vulnerable, and time after time found ourselves subjugated to the ambitions of others. Even though now we are independent, we are still surrounded by threats. We are clearly not the only ones to notice this, for today we have received a missive from another nation; an offer to protect our independence, in exchange for tribute. Unlike most Bulwari, we have had our fair share of good times under gnollish rule, so the question becomes if we are willing to give up even a sliver of liberty in exchange for independence.
