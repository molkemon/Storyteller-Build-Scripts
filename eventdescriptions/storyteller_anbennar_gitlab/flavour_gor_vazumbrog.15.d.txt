What began as a humble band of adventurers has grown into a country. Gor Vazumbrog of old followed a clear tradition. leadership drawn from the ranks of the general staff, ensuring that those who guide us have proven their skill in battle. The captain who bravely led us here is a great leader for a company, yet even he, with his unflinching honesty, acknowledges that the burden of ruling a nation may demand qualities beyond his ken. The question now looms over us all. should the hero who brought us here step aside for a leader better suited to govern our burgeoning country?
