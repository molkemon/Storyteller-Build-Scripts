Founding Proclamation of the Nimszexael Society. We, the members of the Nimszexael Society, seek to achieve the following. Full representation for the interests of the diverse peoples of Aelantier within the administration of the Kingdom of Daccasded; an end to the implicit hegemony of the Kingdom of Iochand over the peoples of Aelantier, and the revocation of Statute Thirty-Five; freedom for the peoples of Aelantier to retain their own distinct cultural practices; protection for the interests of the Artificers and Merchants of Aelantier from Iochander domination; dignity and respect for the rights of the peoples of Aelantier; and the slightest modicum of seriousness, for once. The Nimszexael Society shall hereby do everything in its power to achieve these goals, regardless of whether the Kingdom of Iochand chooses to cooperate or not. May Aelantier see a brighter, freer, and less silly future. Authorized by the Nimszexael Executive Council, Tando Bigbrain, Chairman.
