The warrior prince Jaddar was chosen by the seven Mašnsih tribes to serve as Herald of the Sihrušam, to guide the tribes in these times of trouble against the ravenous Zokka, the Devourer of Suns, to secure for future generations the Salahad today, forever. In that noble task, Jaddar sadly failed, and we were much to our shame pushed to the very edge of destruction, leaving countless Mašnsih to endure alone the savagery of Zokka. Yet, in our absence, the Mašnsih reconvened a new Sihrušam, aided it seemed by powers from beyond. Today, those powers have revealed themselves; the vile gnolls of the Šad Sur, the pack of Tluukt, who have gladly taken under their control the desert banners. We cannot be sure what fresh horrors those noble warriors now endure under the heel of Tluukt, but it is clear that the heavens are mocking us for our complacency.
