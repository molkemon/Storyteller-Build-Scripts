With the decisive defeat of Irrliam and Bulwari possession of EduzBuranun, it so happened with a little palm greasing that the wider Bulwar region was willing to heed the calls of a religious council, a gathering similar in scale to the one called after Taelarios' death. Of course, many of the wider principles of the Sun Cult were established then, and this council was not keen to challenge them; rather, the focus was on the many rites around the proper worship, codifying many rituals, practices, and holy days, and on establishing a consensus on a single, standardized dogma, instead of hosting regional variations. On a more subtle note, the purpose of the council was to cement Bulwar's position as head of the Sun Cult. Nearly all of the final religious decisions are made by priests from Bulwar proper, acting in Bulwari interests, and though a council like this seems meaningless, it sends one profound message.
