The vast sands of the Naymak Kuwatai are an extremely hostile environment. Inhabited by the Sirtana peoples, they have masterfully adapted to the desert. Building marvels of sandstone, they have made their home in a quite lively place for such a desolate area. A few dwarves have been found amuck in this place, aiding the building of castles and great projects in the Siran lands. They seem wholly unaware that mere days away is the home of their brethren.
