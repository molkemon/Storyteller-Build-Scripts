As expected, something was amiss in Azkabar. The Cult of the Eclipse, sinners and heretics as a rule, has established their tendrils deep into the workings of the city. Our investigator was able to look very deeply into their connections, discovering that Azkabar's corruption runs up even to their ruler. Before he could properly publicize this information, he was killed. But we know. They will fight us when we march in to depose their government, obviously, but in the name of defeating the Malevolent Dark, we will have full moral responsibility to do so and then replace it with someone more proBulwar.
