A representative from the Hwalbaek has come to your court, bristling. They claim that the ruler of that rival realm has insulted your proud realm by openly proclaiming that you are a mere shadow of your former might since the death of Sage King JeongHun Gyo. They insult your military, your prowess, your status within the lands of Gowon. Some even go as far as to claim that you are ripe for the picking, that your lands should rightfully belong to them instead. This is preposterous, of course, and the crown has other urgent matters to attend to at the moment. Unfortunately, the Hwalbaek claim that you must establish your firm superiority and nip this in the bud to save face, and will be dissatisfied in your rule if you should decline to act.
