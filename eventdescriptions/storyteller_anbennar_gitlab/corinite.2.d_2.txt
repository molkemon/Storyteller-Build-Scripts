The skies in the capital have been uncommonly clear in the last days, until heavy red rain from the Crimson Deluge inundated the capital building specifically, sparing the rest of the capital. Easily cowed courtiers and commonfolk alike have taken this as a sign that Dookan is not pleased with how they run things. However, while their shamans have not yet made heads or tails of this ominous event, many others are already running amok praising the name of Korgus, claiming he has ascended to the heavens and is spilling the blood of Great Dookan’s celestial jailers to avenge his divine father.
