War. The Empire of Anbennar is threatened, surrounded by hostile plots and scheming enemies. Our dream of peace, so hard-fought and yet hardly secure, and the unity of the many peoples under the Dove’s wings will never be accepted by our enemies. They would gladly see us once more divided into a petty patchwork of squabbling, bickering fools. No longer. Our Emperor has declared an Imperial Realm War against an enemy nation. The threat to the realm must be driven back, and we will impose the Mage’s Peace upon them.
