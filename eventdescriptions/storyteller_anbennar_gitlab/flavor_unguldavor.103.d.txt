The yearly debate at Gulenhyl was a disaster. All the clan leaders and the monarch were there to debate about politics and magic principles. All was going well until it was the ruler's turn to speak. The monarch made some valid points, only for them to fall into a trap. The current chief of Clouded Eye along with the shamans and scholars tore about the monarch's argument and humiliated them. The monarch was now a laughingstock across Escann as papers circulated about the poor argument.
