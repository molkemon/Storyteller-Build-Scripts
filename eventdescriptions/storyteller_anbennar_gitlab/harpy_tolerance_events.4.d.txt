It is known to everyone and their mother-in-law that unless some civilizing influence is present, harpies will abduct males of other races in order to mate with them. It turns out that the chosen mate of one particularly brave or insane harpy was one of our advisors. Some people, especially the advisor's opponents in the government, are advising the monarch to just let the advisor go, but others are urging us to interrogate other harpies who may know about the advisor's whereabouts or at least send a search and rescue party out.
