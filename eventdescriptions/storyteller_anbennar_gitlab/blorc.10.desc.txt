The Dwarovar is ours, and orks can be found across the Serpentspine. But if there is anything we share, anything that thrums through all of us, it is rooted in HulJorkad. To own that hold, to ensure the dwarves are forever expunged from its halls, their mighty walls torn down and their deep dungeons destroyed; this somehow speaks to the very nature of every ork. And with that done, we shall be even more united in our Dookangiven purpose.
