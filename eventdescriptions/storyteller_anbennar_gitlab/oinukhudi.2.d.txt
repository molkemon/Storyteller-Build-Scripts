It is the duty of the Cheqh'an to appoint new bannerlords once per ten years when winter turns to spring. Typically, the Cheqh'an makes these appointments during the yearly Ursdayalal in the land that these bannerlords would govern, however times have changed and the Cheqh'an now presides in the capital. The appointment ceremony has become nothing more than a formality, with the bannerlords selecting their sons to inherit their titles without any interference from the Cheqh'an. The time has come to make these appointments. Will the Cheqh'an finally make the journey south to appoint new frontier bannerlords?
