It started simply, but then it all came crashing down. The representative of the Broken Chain argued with the expert of the Silver Reliquary about the unethical methods the latter was using regarding the lives of their laborers. A member of the Young Dragon took offense with the Black Tree chapter over reports coming from the Forlorn Vale of the dark curse that had been woven over its people. The latter simply smiled darkly. Then the representative of the Burning Sword tried to call them all down, and the Palatine Arbiter echoed these thoughts, but it was for nothing. All four of these chapters have come to blows over minor grievances before, and now they all declared in a final act of unity that they could not all be satisfied. The representatives then all left, each taking care not to be seen leaving together and especially not with whom they argued. To Hagstow, to Steelhyl, to Bal Mire, and to Silverdocks, they all absconded. The promise of the Covenant that keeps Covenblad united even in darkest times is rent asunder.
