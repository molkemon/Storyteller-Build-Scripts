As the party steps over the ruined husks of the dispatched golems that once guarded the chamber, a hushed silence settles over the bold expedition. The doors before them have an unmistakable importance, carved with monolithic basreliefs of mages at the apex of their power. They make barely a whisper as they open, revealing within the beating heart of the temple, a titanic shard of crystalline Dames tear thrumming and pulsing with a low light. Finally, the temple is truly theirs.
