With the last great Ravelian Debate finished, the Ravelian Church has finally established itself as a cut above most faiths of the world. Theirs is a faith that is for all, perhaps like The Jadd, but driven by a respectful mix of reason and spirituality. For a long time the Ravelians in our realm have watched as the followers of Adean and Corin bicker over the Regent Throne, what the Ravelians see, in the grand scheme of things, as a trivial affair. They have become tired seeing their religion treated as a secondary faith, but now their universalist teachings have finally entrenched themselves as the third spoke in the faiths of the Cannorian Pantheon that has dominated Anbennar since its existence. Led by the ruler of that country, they have approached the Emperor demanding that the Ravelian faith be given equal, if not greater, standing than the existing faith within the Empire.
