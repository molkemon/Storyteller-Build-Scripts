We are in this land as a Sponsored Adventurer Band. Our overlords in Dadubakha want us to conquer and spread our culture and faith. They expect a return on investment, and in thirty-five years or when we have conquered a sufficient amount of land, they will want to redefine this relationship. If we are able to successfully reform our government before then, we will be better poised to fend off any advances from our gracious overlord.
