The delegations stand together in ceremonious garb, but before the monarch can even call the first one, a senior diplomat breaks rank, asking the ruler if Cestirmarker politicians have lost their minds. He claims to speak for all the sovereignist faction, which always considered the current state of the Concord as sufficient, and strongly rejects the new reforms. A breaking point has been reached, as it is clear to them two diverging paths are being followed in the Trollsbay, and he strongly implied these two paths could not coexist. Other diplomats intervened to call out those arguments as disingenuous, and the dispute quickly escalated until the sovereignists representatives stormed out. As the monarch looks over those who are left, it is clear one correct argument was made. Two diverging paths are being followed in the Trollsbay.
