Balrijin, a federation of clans centered around a council of sages nestled atop the mountainous capital of Balriza, the rugged cradle of the rugged people, is obsessed with one thing, and one thing alone. Balris The Gold, Golden Dragon of The East, He Who Will Return, Bringer of Songs, Slayer of Ignorance, Wisest of Masters. Centuries ago, maybe even a millennium, He first graced us with His presence, His scales as gold as ours, but his physique unfathomably more gargantuan and aweinspiring; this was no mere coincidence. Whether He created us, or has merely adopted us due to our likeness, is hotly debated among the sages, but all agree the dragon elevated us from tribal feuds when He settled down in Balkhangfa Cave and began giving council to our people. Balris taught our people how to live in unity, how to host feasts, make art, and write music and poetry. He also guided us spiritually, by teaching us the higher truths of philosophy and magic. For two generations, the Dragon Sage tutored our people and shaped our budding state, but as time went by, He grew restless, eager to continue His journey in search of His kin. When He left, He swore to us that He would soon return to us, but by the best estimates of our most gifted scholars, it has been at least nine centuries since His departure. This is a long time, even for a being as timeless as He. Though some among the Sage Council fear the worst, we must seek Him out, for no goldscale can truly rest while Balris' fate remains unknown. Until then, we shall hold on fervently to His teachings. Our people have been looking for Him, but as of yet, all rumors were dead ends. Still we seek them out, for it is our duty. Recently, new rumors have been spreading from across our western border. a golden beast is attacking humans in Jinqiu, yet they refuse us entry to seek for it, which can only mean war.
