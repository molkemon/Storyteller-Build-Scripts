While the local faith are parochials, they have some customs and rites that we might make use of. As their traditions and ceremonies regarding community already contain a concept of a mutual benefit similar to our government, this is something we could make good use of. Local officials in that province have already been doing so, but it has been suggested that we make use of the faith's ceremonies even at higher levels. Others argue that we would be shaming our ancestors and turning our back on what is proper if we allowed these tenets to contaminate ours.
