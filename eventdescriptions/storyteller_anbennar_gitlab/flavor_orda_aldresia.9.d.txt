Grandmaster Delian eyed the simple iron and leather hilt of his singing blade. The saber itself was slumbering; it had fallen asleep since the Lilac War ended years ago, ever since he learned of a prince's death. "Dozing off on duty are you?" came an annoying voice. Crovan sil Wex, Grand Magister of the neutral Magisterium, strode across the empty throne room with a punchable smile. Delian tried to ignore him. The emperor had declared an afternoon recess, but the knights never rested. "I guess you've earned it in a way. You did join the winning side... at the end." Crovan winked, calling forth a spark of anger in Delian, and for the first time in a while, a whisper from his blade. "But I mean, you did still support a feckless loser for a while, a false emperor by the name of Brokenlance, was it? Should've come to the right side, like Wesdam did." The Grand Magister continued. The whispering blade started to rouse, growing desperate, hectic, shouting internally. "Ah well, we're allies now, aren't we? No harm, no foul." Crovan laughed, turning around as Delian's blade screamed in his hilt. Delian lightly touched the pommel of his blade as Crovan stopped. A daring brow was raised from the Magister as the Grandmaster breathed deeply. Delian's blade, Bloodbrother, howled for a bloody duet that the Grandmaster never joined. "Thought not." Crovan cackled as the Grand Magister left, leaving Delian alone in the throne room.
