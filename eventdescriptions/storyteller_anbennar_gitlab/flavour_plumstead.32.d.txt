"Get him." "Kick him in the groin." "Pull his ears." The crowd went wild as two Epednar men fought. And it was not just men, but two representatives from the Epednar tribes. While tribal rivalries are not uncommon, the relation between these two tribes has been tense as of late after a wave of cattle thefts, for which both tribes blame each other. What happened was that during this government function, a banquet was held. During the banquet, a large sculpture of a cow, filled with delicacies, was wheeled in. After this, one of the Epednar politicians remarked that he had found his rival's cow, to which the reaction was an Epednar flying across the table. The two fought, and while the guards were on their way, the crowd just cheered and stoked up the fight even more, as the function had been lacking entertainment anyways. The two were then taken away by guards, and both tribes now demand a response from us. They both demand we punish the other side, as their honor has, in their eyes, been damaged.
