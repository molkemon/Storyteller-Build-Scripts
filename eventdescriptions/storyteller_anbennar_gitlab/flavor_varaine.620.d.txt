Having decided to study the giant's remains, our researchers headed towards the Giant's Grave Sea, where these creatures' great civilization once stood. There, an expedition to fish out usable remains began to be assembled, but after a few weeks of preparation a different opportunity presented itself. Word reached our researchers of some nearby oddity sellers who have come upon an entire giant body in the Giant's Grave Sea, preserved from the elements by the cold and the depths. While on one hand this is motivating news, as it shows our expedition has hopes of success, it also provides us with a shortcut, as we could skip the expedition and just acquire what we need from the vendors; though their prices are quite steep.
