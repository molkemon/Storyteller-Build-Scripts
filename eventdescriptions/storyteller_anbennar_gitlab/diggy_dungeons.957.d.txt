The dull rumbling of the spider tsunami's passage shakes the very stone around us, but we manage to find a side passage just big enough for the remainder of our expeditionary force. The air is tense, thick with the labored breaths and fearful mumblings of our expeditionaries. We don't know how long we can hide here, or how long we need to hide to make it through this, but it seems the tide of spiders is passing us by without a second thought. Perhaps if we linger here long enough we can make it out of this alive.
