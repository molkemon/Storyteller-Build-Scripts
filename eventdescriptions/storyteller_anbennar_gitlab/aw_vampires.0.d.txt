Various incidents have been reported in the realm with peculiarly similar characteristics. Those who frequent the roads at night are attacked by unknown assailants; some simply disappear. Those who do not show clear signs of resistance are found dead, bloodless, with two puncture wounds on their neck. The few who lived and reported to the soldiery have already been incarcerated to avoid the worst. It is quite clear that this is the work of vampires, creatures rumored to have originated from Aldresian crusaders who coveted the elves' longevity so intensely that they revived Ettalinde the Wretched, an acolyte of the Sorcerer King, and bargained with her. She gave them vampirism, a condition that turned out to be as much a curse as it was a blessing. It renders one immortal through undeath, but requires the host to flee the sunlight and hunt others to feed on their blood.
