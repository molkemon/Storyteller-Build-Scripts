Boats crash into hidden rocks. Small groups of explorers disappear as blizzards suddenly appear. Kobolds that would readily have braved the open ocean now fear sheets of ice and monsters they have never seen or heard of. The kobolds of Vrendzan, once the foremost explorers of the Dragoncoast, have been cowed by Aelantier’s horrors. Almost unanimously they speak of scaling back expeditions and focusing on building up settlements, ideally with extensive defenses. Admittedly, it does appear that they have explored as much as would benefit them at the moment. There is no telling how long they will be able to make their home here before the Cannorians attempt to end their nation. Taming the coast and creating a civilization capable of existing on its own is of vital importance. But there are a few dissenting voices. Perhaps they have not adventured far enough. Perhaps there is more to see, relics to uncover that would justify the loss in kobolds. A choice must be made.
