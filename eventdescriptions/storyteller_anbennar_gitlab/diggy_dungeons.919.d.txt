Heading onwards from the mites, after a few hours of delving, the passageway widens into a larger room, with walls covered in webbing and several cocoons dangling from the ceiling. On the floor, hundreds of palm-sized spiders, each wider than a normal spider and with a spiky shell similar to a shield, are present. These spiders seem fairly restless; it seems like the fight with the mites was noisy enough to perturb them, but they do not seem to have noticed their presence yet.
