As always, the counter approaches the savage to tighten the rope. An unpleasant task, more unpleasantly that day, the counter was unfortunate enough to touch the savage. Normally that would be prohibited to prevent the spread of disease, but at that moment the counter felt something unusual from the body. "Cold," he murmured. "What is the problem, goodman?" yelled the inquisitor watching from further away. Slowly turning his gaze from the savage towards the crown, the counter remarked, "There is no need to burn him." Taking a deep breath, he continued, "The savage is already dead."
