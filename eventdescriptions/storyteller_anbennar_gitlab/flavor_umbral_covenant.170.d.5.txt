When Umorozi comes back the fifth time, she no longer requires food, drink, or sleep. The enemies of the inevitable chose, this time, to cremate what remained of Umorozi’s body. They carved around and removed the fogparts once more, perhaps burying or disenchanting them. The Vile One looks upon such efforts and laughs, for now she comprehends the power of memory. Four times before she has resurrected Umorozi, she has learned and rebuilt her form. It is not so much more difficult now to build it again from nothing. No mortal death can reach the great general now. When Umorozi strikes from the endless fog, she is entirely invisible; her whisper can reach halfway across Sarhal, so long as she can conceptualize the recipient. Her tall frame still drinks light; look closely at the fogform, and one can see the gentle sway behind Umorozi’s back of those long banded braids. The Vile One would never have forgotten those.
