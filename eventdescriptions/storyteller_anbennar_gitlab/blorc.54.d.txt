As the chieftain of the rival domain brought down their axe, splitting the skull of the ruler wide open, a silence went through the crowd. Soon the silence broke into cheering coming from the rival domain's side of the crowd, as the chief claimed the regalia of the old monarch for themselves and the personal guard of the old monarch began swearing their loyalty to the new title. As the new title began marching towards the capital, with the mutilated head of the old title as proof, a new era for the government ushered in.
