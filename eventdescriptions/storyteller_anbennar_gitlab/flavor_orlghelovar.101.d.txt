The mood within the realm is subdued, despite a recent great reawakening; the glassworkers are growing unsatisfied with their craft. While it is undeniable that the glassworkers are the finest, it has always been this way; they have not truly innovated or been challenged for many generations, and their passions are beginning to cool. For the first time since the ancestors began making glass, new scientific possibilities can be seen on the horizon. The gnomish scientists have already made great leaps in the study of other materials, and with their assistance, a deeper understanding of glass may be gained. The greatest glassworkers and finest minds must be gathered to search for new ideas; with one spark, the whole industry could be revitalized forever.
