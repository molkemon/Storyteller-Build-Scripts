A recent quake in the province resulted in a minor magmatic eruption. The local environment has suffered, but the eruption has left behind some volcanic ash. The local soil appears to be unusually fertile now, and there are a couple of ways they could exploit this. They could either leave it as is, allowing people to settle where they wish and allowing the local farming community to support a larger city, or import Serpentbloom and allow an agricultural industry to take over the region.
