A sword is not made with love and care; it is made with destruction, fire, and control. You dig the iron out of the ground, you grind it down, you heat it till it melts, then you whack it to form the sword. This process is what we Brrtekuns will do to the world economy. break the markets to pieces, grind the monopolies to dust, burn the trade routes down, melt the useless luxuries, then whack, over and over, until the global economy is a mere sword directed at our enemies. Finally, after the flames have charred the land, from the ashes of death and destruction, after the flies have consumed until the world is rotless, finally, a new world shall be ushered anew, a land up to us to forge, to guide. A land reborn, a Halann redeemed by Kvangahga.
