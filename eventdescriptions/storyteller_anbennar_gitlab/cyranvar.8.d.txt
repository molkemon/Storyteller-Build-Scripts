Since Vucras’ expedition began, all of Cyranvar has been abuzz with excitement. Fantastical stories of never before seen fey and lands rife with seasonal strife have spilled from the court to the common folk. Yet, today that excitement has been stifled with anxiety. For today carries a story not of wondrous discovery but of mortal squabbles. The mysterious Feyrealm connected forest holds elves just like ours. But they are different. With their exotic skin colors, strange tongues, and bizarre customs, it seems like we hold more apart than together. Indeed, perhaps for that reason, these elves are intensely hostile. Our intrepid explorer has reported that skirmishes with these wayward elves are increasingly common.
