The time has come again for the Hohldihla; the "Dance of Dreams", in which the physical can be left behind and communion held with the Aspects that make up the world. Those who will enter the Dream today carry the Dotim from their sacred shrines to the central Dotimveb and place them reverently on their altar. Only then are the fires stoked and filled with seasoned herbs, the smoke of which rapidly fills the domed temple. As the haze in the air grows, so do the shadows lengthen and distort, the colours deepen with ochre and vermillion, and the supplicants slip into the Dance of Dreams. The communion begins. Combining the correct influences when performing a ritual will allow us to deepen our knowledge and unlock new aspects.
