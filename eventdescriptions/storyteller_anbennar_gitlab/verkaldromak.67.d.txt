The monarch was called down into the depths of Verkal Dromak to meet with the brightest of minds beyond the Sleepers. The altar has sent out energy waves for some time now, but only recently did the Sleepers notice that the intervals are getting shorter and the waves stronger. And now they have all gathered for a meeting since they have come to the conclusion that on a certain day when the waves will come so fast that the simple dwarf cannot see the time between them, the altar might blow up and destroy a substantial part of their hold.
