Having set up the necessary equipment, we must now search for the ingredients our research demands. While many of the ingredients we require are things we already have at hand, due to the scale of the tests and the purity required, even the most basic of herbs and chemicals will need to be procured in gigantic proportions; a task which will demand considerable manpower. While the more advanced steps of preparing the ingredients will require specialized professionals, there is a large volume of menial tasks to be executed, which require little to no training. To help in accomplishing these tasks, we could conscript the army, or we could force the people in general to supply the workforce needed.
