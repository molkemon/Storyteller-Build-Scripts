Standing on a hill near the capital, the monarch surveys the crusaders that have rallied to their banner. The time for words is gone now, and the abomination that is the Black domain willingly chooses to hear not. Holding a ceremonial blade given by the Orda Aldresia, the monarch begins chanting, and is then joined by knights of the order and prominent commanders of the crusading army. Together, their blades light in a brilliant tealblue like the blazing banners of the order itself. Together, they hold it high, illuminating itself behind the coming dawn.
