With the death of the previous ruler, the duty of organising a proper procession, ceremony, and entombment for them falls upon the current ruler. Naturally we shall conduct these activities at our holiest resting place. The Great Necropolis of Bim Lau. How extravagant we make this ceremony is up for debate. If we appease the spirit of the deceased with an opulent ceremony, we might even be able to count on help from the afterlife as long as the spirit resides in the necropolis.
