This body has broken. Perhaps one too many rivets have popped from a joint, perhaps cogs have ground themselves toothless, perhaps a wire has burned through. It no longer moves, and has collapsed in a wreck to the floor. But its eyes remain bright, and its head remains gold. Golodh knows a tool has been used to its last, and has broken from the strain. Golodh also knows this is a wonderful thing. is it not the perfect ending for a tool, to be used so completely? In a moment, the tools that yet attend him remove him from the body he had used for days or for decades, and summon forth another headless body. Another tool for him to use, and to be discarded in turn, when it has worn out its use.
