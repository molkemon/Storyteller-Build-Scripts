Our pack has always held a special reverence for our ancestors; perhaps more so than any other gnoll pack. In the past, we find guidance and wisdom to carry us into the future. Of course, the past was not always perfect, and few gnolls exemplify this better than the Seascourge Gnolls. On the one hand, they are part of our realm, and our peoples have long entwined histories. They have helped our campaigns, and we have saved one another in times of dire need. And yet, their ancestors were also demon worshippers. Should we allow them to revere gnolls who were misguided at best, or outright corrupt at worst? Or should we fashion new myths for them to follow, even at the risk of taking their history away from them?
