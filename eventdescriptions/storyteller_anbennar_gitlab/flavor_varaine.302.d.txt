Mages all around Cannor have been enjoying our mana potions, and as wizards chug jugs of this wonderful blue liquid, an opportunity presents itself for Varainé. Some wizards affiliated with the Magisterium come to us after proposing that, as a show of good faith, we should send the Magisterium a shipment of some surplus potions, and that such a gesture would be deeply appreciated by the organization. We do know that the Magisterium is interested in these potions, as all wizards are, so we could also instead propose them special deals on their purchase, still maintaining some profit but honoring the request brought forward. Alternatively, we could ignore the request and instead direct this surplus to our own mages, but word of this might reach the Magisterium's ears to our detriment.
