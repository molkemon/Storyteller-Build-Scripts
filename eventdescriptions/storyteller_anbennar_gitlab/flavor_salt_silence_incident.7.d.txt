For the first time in decades, Imperial Armies march east toward the darkening skies of The Borders. The Electors rally to us, their armies joining ours while our war bards sing songs of battle in our wake. The monarch of Salt and Darkness claims to be the King of Salt and Darkness, and their lands fell silent in submission to their rise. They are nothing more than an upstart ruler who wishes to challenge Imperial authority. It is time we correct them on a few matters. They mistake our indifference for weakness.
