The light of the morning sun brought another proof of Surael’s benevolence gracing the Empire. A messenger arrived from the west, bringing most welcome news with them. The ruler of the Empire of Anbennar has proven most receptive to our diplomatic overtures and agreed to a treaty of friendship and prosperity. This is not only a clear recognition of our newborn empire as the legitimate successor to the Phoenix Empire of old, but it promises to open up new markets in which to trade the many treasures of our lands. Our Šabari and Barkabti merchantnobles are already giddy with anticipation at the thought of those lucrative new mercantile opportunities.
