It is in the nature of the goblin to be small and minuscule, their eyesight undesirable and their field of vision smaller than a small spoon. They lack any skill in detecting intruders until it is far too late. Those were the words of Drak Ironbone, a dwarven scholar, but things have changed since then. The magpies have wings, their eyes pierce the air from heights that were unreachable to them before. They can now see their enemies from far away, and prepare accordingly.
