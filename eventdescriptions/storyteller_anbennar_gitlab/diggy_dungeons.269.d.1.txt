As the expeditionaries comb through the maze, mapping it as they go, they initially remain undisturbed by the hag. As they keep pushing through the stinking passageways for hour after hour, though, they soon find themselves exhausted and forced to rest. This is when the hag struck, killing one of the expeditionaries that was standing guard in a single blow. The ennis hag rampages through the expedition, killing anyone in its way. Before the expeditionaries have a chance to react properly, the monster has already retreated into the depths of the maze again.
