Since the decline of Kheterata, the Efergezmu of that province have been clamoring to break up the vast estates held by the Kheteratan ruling class. Granting this request would please the Efergezmu greatly, but would antagonize the Medawor and risk a loss of productivity. On the other hand, the Medawor have offered a donation to the state in exchange for turning a deaf ear to the Efergezmu’s complaints.
