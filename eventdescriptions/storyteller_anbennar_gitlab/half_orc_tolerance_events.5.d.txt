While dealing with the affairs of the realm, a delicate inquiry was brought to the monarch. One of our local lords happens to be in a predicament, since his sole legitimate heir died in a hunting accident. His only other child is with a prostitute of a different race. Despite the child's nature, he seems to be quite fond of his illegitimate child and wants to designate him as heir of his barony. This could be a great chance to further improve the standing of the halforks in our realm, but it would severely anger the more conservative noble elite. Maybe we could also profit a bit from this?
