Then did the host of the monarch come upon a great river, and many vessels spy. Yet fewer, a wingmaiden did mention, than the year before. Ashore did they land, and a fisherman asked. The man, who was named Balder, found his knee and said, "O queen, your eyes are ever keen, for we have been beset by gnollish pirates from the far south. And their swift craft cleave the sea like knives downriver, and they board any vessel that plies this river at night, where their keen senses make sport of honest traders."
