Our smartest scouts carefully disarm each trap as we move through, eventually reaching the topmost laboratory and discovering numerous damaged relics and stone tablets scribed with strange symbols. Among them, we find a small reflective metal tablet, which displays varying information when observed at different levels of light, in writing that is somehow legible despite its unfamiliarity. This ranges from agricultural improvements to medicinal uses for several rare plants and fungi we previously thought useless, to techniques for wood joining via magic. All of this is unfamiliar knowledge to our sages and has never been found documented anywhere else.
