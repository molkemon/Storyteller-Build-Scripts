All throughout our history there are many incidents of our people being oppressed by one race or another. Even our own human GodKings were not all benevolent. Ours is a history of struggle for freedom, and that fight for our independence is what we should sing of when we sing our country’s anthem. Arise, peoples of Bahar. The day of glory has arrived. Against us, tyranny's bloody standard is raised. Do you hear, in the countryside, the roar of those ferocious elves? They are coming right into your arms to cut the throats of your sons and your women. To arms, Bahari! Form your battalions, march, march. Let impure blood water our furrows. God of mercy and justice, see our tyrants, judge our hearts. Your goodness be with us. Defend us from these oppressors. You reign in heaven, and before you all must bend. In your arms, come support us, you great God, lord of sunlight.
