It spawned from minute encounters with blessed tailwinds or against vexing headwinds, each some manner of curious, unfortunate, or joyous. Breaking the surface barrier, each interaction earned a grain of trust, and now those minute grains form the very foundation of our relationship; from tourists to explorers to merchants to kings and diplomats, the merfolk nations seek further rapport with the realm. The merfolk rose to meet us, and we accepted them as aquatic kin. Now, we consider one another pledged allies, ready to weather the storms that threaten either.
